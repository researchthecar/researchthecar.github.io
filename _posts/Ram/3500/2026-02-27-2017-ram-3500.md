# 2017 Ram 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ram 3500 is a heavy-duty workhorse capable of handling immense towing and hauling demands. However, used-car buyers should thoroughly review ownership records and safety campaigns before purchasing, as owners have reported critical issues regarding braking systems, steering components, and heavy-duty powertrain hardware.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L V8 Hemi Gasoline Engine

The 6.4-liter Hemi V8 gasoline engine produces around 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, offering strong gas-powered hauling capabilities for heavy-duty applications.

### 6.7L Turbo Diesel I6 Engine

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six turbo diesel engine is available in multiple output levels, peaking up to 385 horsepower and a massive 900 lb-ft of torque when paired with the Aisin transmission.

**Grid Heater Bolt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The internal grid heater bolt can break off and drop into the cylinders while driving or towing, causing catastrophic engine failure.
- **Solution:** Replace the factory grid heater intake horn with an upgraded aftermarket grid heater relay kit to prevent internal engine damage.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A heavy-duty Mercedes-backed 6-speed manual transmission was offered exclusively with the standard-output Cummins diesel for drivers preferring a traditional three-pedal setup.

### 66RFE 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 66RFE 6-speed automatic transmission is standard behind the 6.4L V8 gas engine and standard-output diesel, designed to balance heavy towing with everyday drivability.

### Aisin 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

The commercial-grade Aisin 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with the high-output Cummins diesel to handle maximum towing capacities and strenuous fleet use.

## Brakes

### Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) Hydraulic Control Unit

The hydraulic braking system relies on the ABS module to maintain control under heavy loads, though owners frequently report failures associated with safety recalls.

**ABS Module and Hydraulic Control Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights, loss of anti-lock braking function, disabled electronic stability control, and spongy or failing brake pedal response while towing.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer inspect the braking system and perform necessary recall repairs or hydraulic control unit replacements as parts become available.

## Steering

### Front Suspension and Steering Linkage

Heavy-duty solid front axle steering components endure significant stress, occasionally leading to severe steering oscillation if wear goes unaddressed.

**Front-End Death Wobble**
- **Symptoms:** Violent side-to-side shaking of the steering wheel and front end when traveling at highway speeds, making the vehicle difficult to control.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace worn steering dampers, track bars, drag links, and ball joints with heavy-duty aftermarket alternatives.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The central electrical architecture controls complex powertrain modules, trailer brakes, and cabin electronics, with occasional reports of ground or fuse box anomalies.

**Intermittent Lighting and Cluster Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior lights, turn signals, and interior dash functions failing intermittently or going out simultaneously despite functional bulbs.
- **Solution:** Inspect the main power distribution box, wiring harnesses, and ground connections for corrosion or loose terminal pins.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V021000: Steering:Linkages:Drag:Link:Connection](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A drag link separation can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will check the steering linkage jam nut tightness, replacing the drag link assembly or welding the nut to the adjuster sleeve, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18E096000: Steering:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The weakened connection may result in a fractured steering drag link, causing a loss of steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Streetcar will notify owners and dealers will inspect and install a new steering knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V347000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Tailgate:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate opens while the vehicle is being driven, cargo may fall out the vehicle and create a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the tailgate latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V486000: Structure:Body:Tailgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate latch releases and the tailgate opens while driving, cargo may fall out, creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V398000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect information in the Service and Owner's Manuals may cause the flanged lug nuts to be over-tightened, which could result in a broken wheel stud and possible wheel separation. Wheel separation can cause a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the torque specification in the owner's manual and other published service documents. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, inspect the BTSI, and, as necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V499000: Wheels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the wheel fractures, a loss of vehicle control can occur, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** AEV will notify owners, and dealers will replace the wheels with wheels of similar value, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ram 3500 is an extremely capable heavy-duty truck, but prospective buyers should approach with careful inspection. Pay close attention to outstanding safety recalls concerning the ABS hydraulic control unit, listen for signs of front-end death wobble during test drives, and verify the status of diesel engine grid heaters to ensure a reliable purchase.
