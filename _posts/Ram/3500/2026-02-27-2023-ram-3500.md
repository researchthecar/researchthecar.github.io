# 2023 Ram 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Ram 3500, buyers are looking at a heavy-duty powerhouse designed for serious towing and commercial applications. Known for its robust capability and high-torque diesel options, this truck generally handles demanding workloads with ease. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues and maintain awareness of specific electrical quirks and safety system components when evaluating individual listings.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8 Gasoline Engine

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 pushes out approximately 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, providing strong standard gas-powered capability. It offers dependable performance for everyday heavy-duty tasks without the added complexity of a diesel setup.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel Inline-6

The standard-output 6.7-liter Cummins turbo diesel delivers robust torque for heavy towing. For maximum capability, the high-output variant generates up to 1,075 lb-ft of torque, making it a favorite among heavy haulers seeking long-term pulling power.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

Paired with the 6.4L gas engine, this 8-speed automatic provides responsive shifting and optimized gear ratios. It is generally well-suited for both empty cruising and heavy towing applications.

### 6-Speed AISIN Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

Available exclusively with the high-output Cummins diesel, this heavy-duty 6-speed AISIN transmission is built to manage massive torque outputs under severe loads. It offers commercial-grade durability for frequent heavy haulers.

## Steering

### Lighting and Steering Column Control Module

Electrical complaints center on lighting controls and module interactions, requiring careful testing of switches and exterior lights during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Unintended High Beam Activation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that activating the left turn signal inadvertently triggers the high beam headlights due to steering column control module malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Dealers may need to inspect and service the steering column control module under applicable service campaigns or replace faulty components.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety system grievances primarily involve seat belt retraction and locking performance, which should be verified across all seating positions.

**Seat Belt Retractor and Locking Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that seat belts may fail to lock properly during braking or sluggishly retract, occasionally getting caught in the vehicle doors.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies for smooth operation and proper locking behavior, replacing faulty mechanisms as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V060000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay can result in an engine compartment fire with the ignition on or off, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the relay, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V654000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V799000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the high beams to activate when the turn signal is used or activate the turn signal when the high beams are used. In addition, the turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the steering column control module (SCCM) software, and inspect and replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power or a loss of park function increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the valve body and control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ram 3500 remains a formidable heavy-duty choice for buyers needing immense towing capability and powerful engine choices. While most powertrain configurations prove durable, used-car shoppers should thoroughly test electrical functions—particularly exterior lighting and turn signals—and verify the proper operation of safety restraints and latches during a test drive.
