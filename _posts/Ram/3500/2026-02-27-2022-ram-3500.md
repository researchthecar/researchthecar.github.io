# 2022 Ram 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ram 3500 is a heavy-duty powerhouse designed for demanding towing and hauling tasks, featuring robust Cummins diesel and heavy gas engine options. While its capability is unmatched on the job site, used car buyers should pay close attention to potential powertrain quirks, specifically concerning heavy-duty automatic transmissions and associated mechanical components. Reviewing service records and verifying that all relevant campaigns have been performed is essential.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8 Gas Engine

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 delivers approximately 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, providing strong baseline performance for heavy-duty gas applications.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel (Standard & High Output)

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six diesel engine provides immense torque—ranging from 850 up to 1,075 lb-ft in High Output form—making it the preferred choice for heavy hauling, though emissions equipment and complex fuel systems require diligent maintenance.

**Engine Ticking and Knocking**
- **Symptoms:** Abnormal ticking or knocking noises coming from the engine bay that grow louder upon acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the valvetrain and fuel system components to diagnose the source of abnormal mechanical noise.

## Transmission

### 68RFE 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 68RFE six-speed automatic is paired with standard-output configurations and offers dependable service under moderate towing loads when properly maintained.

### Aisin AS69RC 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

The heavy-duty Aisin six-speed automatic transmission is engineered to handle the massive torque output of the High Output Cummins diesel, though some owners have reported unexpected mechanical disengagements and seal leaks.

**Sudden Loss of Drive / Clutch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected disengagement of gears, loss of forward motive power while driving, or failure of internal snap rings leading to a sudden inability to shift into gear.
- **Solution:** Requires professional teardown and inspection by a transmission specialist to replace damaged internal components, clutches, or seals.

**Transfer Case Seal Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Persistent fluid leaks occurring between the transmission and the transfer case.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty output shaft seal and inspect mating surfaces for excessive wear or misalignment.

## Brakes

### Trailer Brake Controller

The braking system provides substantial stopping power for heavy payloads, but electrical glitches in the integrated trailer brake control can cause intermittent warnings.

**Intermittent Trailer Brake Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard displays warnings that the trailer brake controller is not connected, even when properly plugged in and functioning.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring harnesses, trailer plugs, and integrated brake control modules for loose connections or intermittent electrical faults.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Steering components must endure heavy front-end axle weights, making periodic inspection of linkages and power assist systems vital for safety.

**Steering Stiffness and Linkage Disconnect**
- **Symptoms:** Increased steering effort required to maneuver the vehicle, sudden jerking, or temporary loss of steering responsiveness during low-speed turns.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering linkage, electrical power steering motor, and associated connections for mechanical failure or fault codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V798000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay may cause a vehicle fire with the ignition on or off. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if needed, replace the Solid State Intake Heater Grid Relay ("Relay"); if FCA US Recall ID Y08 (NHTSA Recall ID 21V-163) is open, then insulating material will be applied to the Relay after inspection. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V925000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The side air bag inflatable curtain (SABIC) assemblies may have been manufactured with the incorrect amount of compressed gas, which can result in an improper air bag deployment. An air bag that deploys improperly can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the SABIC assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V140000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." An ESC malfunction indicator light that fails to illuminate during an electronic stability control problem can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ABS control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V407000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V642000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An internal loss of communication can result in a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ECM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V835000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Leaking transmission fluid may contact an ignition source within the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the transmission dipstick and transmission vent assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V904000: Structure:Body:Tailgate:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The tailgate may not latch properly due to misaligned tailgate strikers, which could result in the tailgate opening while driving. An unintended tailgate opening while driving may result in a loss of unsecured cargo, possibly creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the tailgate striker alignment to the box latch and adjust, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V060000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay can result in an engine compartment fire with the ignition on or off, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the relay, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ram 3500 is a heavily capable workhorse, but potential buyers should thoroughly inspect the powertrain and transmission operation prior to purchase. Ensure all relevant manufacturer recalls have been completed by checking the VIN, and test-drive the vehicle under load if possible to verify smooth shifting and responsive steering.
