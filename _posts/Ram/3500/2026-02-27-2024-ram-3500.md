# 2024 Ram 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Ram 3500 is a heavy-duty powerhouse designed for demanding towing and hauling tasks, offering immense capability and rugged styling. While it provides strong performance and comfort for a commercial-grade truck, used buyers should carefully inspect early-ownership complaints. Potential areas of concern include heavy-duty braking performance under load, HVAC electrical components, and suspension tracking.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8 Gas Engine

The standard 6.4-liter HEMI V8 gasoline engine produces around 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. It offers capable performance for gas-preferring buyers, though heavy haulers typically lean toward the diesel option.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel (Standard Output)

The standard-output 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six diesel delivers impressive low-end torque—around 370 hp and 850 lb-ft—paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It is built for heavy towing and long-term durability in commercial applications.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel (High Output)

The high-output Cummins diesel churns out up to 420 hp and a massive 1,075 lb-ft of torque, paired with an Aisin heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission. It provides maximum towing capability for heavy fifth-wheel and gooseneck trailers.

**Stalling and Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Accelerator control sensor warning lights illuminate on the dash, followed by repeated engine stalling during operation.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a certified service center to diagnose faulty sensor components and update or replace as necessary.

### ZF 8-Speed Automatic (6.4L Gas)

The 8-speed ZF automatic transmission provides smooth shifting and optimal gear selection for the gas V8 engine, balancing performance and efficiency.

## Transmission

### 68RFE 6-Speed Automatic (Standard Diesel)

The 68RFE 6-speed automatic handles standard-output diesel duties with well-spaced gear ratios designed for heavy towing workloads.

### Aisin AS69RC Heavy-Duty 6-Speed Automatic (High Output Diesel)

The Aisin heavy-duty 6-speed automatic is engineered to withstand the extreme torque output of the high-output Cummins diesel engine under maximum load.

**Unintended Movement or Parking Pawl Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle makes a popping noise when parked on a slope, shifts out of park into reverse, and fails to hold position.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission linkage and parking pawl inspected by a certified dealer immediately.

## Suspension

### Suspension and Alignment

Heavy-duty solid front axle and leaf-spring rear suspensions provide high payload capacity, though ride harshness and alignment stability can vary under heavy loads.

**Rear Bounce and Rapid Front Alignment Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rear end bounces into oncoming lanes when towing over bumps, and front end goes out of alignment frequently.
- **Solution:** Check shock absorbers for leaks and replace worn dampeners in pairs; perform routine front-end alignment checks.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Trailer Brakes

The heavy-duty braking system is designed to stop fully loaded commercial setups, but owners have reported soft pedal feedback and integrated trailer brake communication warnings.

**Soft Pedal and Reduced Braking Efficiency**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal travels halfway down before engaging, offering a soft feel and reduced stopping power when towing heavy loads.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines, bleed the system, and have the master cylinder or ABS module tested by a dealer.

## Steering

### Steering Column Clockspring

Safety and steering controls rely on integrated column wiring harnesses and sensors to maintain driver assistance features.

**Steering Wheel Controls Inoperable**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel buttons fail to operate, often linked to an internal clockspring electrical failure.
- **Solution:** Replace the steering wheel clockspring assembly to restore electrical continuity to horn, audio, and cruise controls.

## Interior

### HVAC Heater

Cabin electrical and auxiliary climate control systems manage extensive cabin electronics and high-draw heating elements.

**PTC Heater Short Circuit and Odor**
- **Symptoms:** PTC heater short circuits, overheats, melts surrounding plastic HVAC components, and fills the cabin with toxic fumes.
- **Solution:** Inspect the heating unit and wiring harness; replace damaged HVAC housings and shorted electrical components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V799000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the high beams to activate when the turn signal is used or activate the turn signal when the high beams are used. In addition, the turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the steering column control module (SCCM) software, and inspect and replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Ram 3500 delivers immense capability for buyers needing serious towing and payload performance. While powertrain options are generally robust, used-car shoppers should thoroughly test the braking response under load, inspect the front suspension alignment, and verify proper electrical and HVAC operation during a pre-purchase inspection.
