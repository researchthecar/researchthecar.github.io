# 2016 Ram 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ram 3500 is a heavy-duty workhorse capable of handling massive towing and hauling jobs when properly equipped. While its heavy-duty powertrains provide exceptional capability, prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance records and previous recall histories. Owner feedback highlights certain recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that require thorough inspection prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8 Gasoline Engine

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 gasoline engine produces approximately 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, offering strong gas-powered hauling capability. It is a reliable pushrod powerplant suited for commercial or heavy towing applications.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six turbodiesel is renowned for massive torque output, available in multiple output ratings up to 900 lb-ft when paired with the Aisin automatic transmission. It serves as the primary choice for heavy gooseneck and fifth-wheel towing.

**Fuel System and Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling, failure to start, or fuel pump failure.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel system and replace failing fuel pumps or contaminated injectors as necessary.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent throttle delay or sudden loss of engine power while driving in traffic.
- **Solution:** Inspect throttle body connections, software calibrations, and fuel delivery components.

## Transmission

### 68RFE 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 68RFE six-speed automatic is the standard transmission paired with the gasoline engine and standard-output diesel, designed to balance towing performance with highway efficiency.

### Aisin AS69RC Heavy-Duty 6-Speed Automatic

The heavy-duty Aisin six-speed automatic transmission is engineered specifically to handle the high-torque output of the high-output Cummins diesel engine in commercial and heavy towing applications.

### G56 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The G56 six-speed manual transmission offers traditional clutch-pedal control for drivers preferring a stick shift with the diesel engine, though it is less common in later model years.

### Shifter and Park Mechanism

Drivetrain components on heavy-duty trucks experience high mechanical loads, making regular inspection of linkages and shift controls important.

**Gear Shifter Fracture or Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** The gear shifter handle may fracture or detach while attempting to shift out of park or change gears.
- **Solution:** Inspect the shifter assembly and replace damaged linkage handles or internal selector components.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Hubs

Suspension and wheel assemblies must support heavy payload capacities, requiring diligent attention to hardware torque and tire health.

**Wheel Separation and Tire Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Severe vehicle vibration, wobbling, or in severe cases, wheel separation or tire tread separation.
- **Solution:** Check wheel lug torque specifications regularly, inspect tire condition, and verify proper suspension maintenance.

## Steering

### Steering Linkage and Assist

Heavy-duty steering components endure significant stress, particularly when vehicles are used for frequent heavy towing or outfitted with oversized tires.

**Steering Assist and Rack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of steering assist, difficulty maneuvering, or excessive play and fracture in steering components.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering linkages, drag links, and steering racks for wear, and ensure all relevant steering recalls have been properly performed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V021000: Steering:Linkages:Drag:Link:Connection](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A drag link separation can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will check the steering linkage jam nut tightness, replacing the drag link assembly or welding the nut to the adjuster sleeve, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18E096000: Steering:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The weakened connection may result in a fractured steering drag link, causing a loss of steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Streetcar will notify owners and dealers will inspect and install a new steering knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V347000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Tailgate:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate opens while the vehicle is being driven, cargo may fall out the vehicle and create a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the tailgate latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V486000: Structure:Body:Tailgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate latch releases and the tailgate opens while driving, cargo may fall out, creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V632000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain:Inflator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A separated inflator end cap or inflator rupture can allow compressed gas to escape, resulting in the end cap or other components being propelled into the vehicle, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace as necessary, the SABIC assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V398000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect information in the Service and Owner's Manuals may cause the flanged lug nuts to be over-tightened, which could result in a broken wheel stud and possible wheel separation. Wheel separation can cause a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the torque specification in the owner's manual and other published service documents. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=3500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, inspect the BTSI, and, as necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ram 3500 remains a formidable heavy-duty truck capable of tackling demanding workloads. Buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection checking the steering linkages, fuel system components, and ensuring all safety recalls have been properly completed by a certified dealer.
