**Introduction:**

The 2012 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its rugged capabilities and impressive towing capacity. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engines:**
    * 5.7L Hemi V8: 390 hp, 407 lb-ft of torque
    * 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6: 350 hp, 650 lb-ft of torque
* **Transmissions:**
    * 6-speed manual
    * 6-speed automatic
    * 4-speed automatic (diesel only)

Despite its impressive performance, the 2012 Ram 2500 has experienced several issues that have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**5.7L Hemi V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: NHTSA 14V-601

**6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Grinding Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild transmission or replace valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, excessive tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: NHTSA 16V-154

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunction, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Dimming:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flickering, dimming
    * Solution: Replace headlight relays
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal parts underneath the vehicle
    * Solution: Rust treatment, undercoating
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cracked Tailgate Handle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or cracked tailgate handle
    * Solution: Replace tailgate handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower not working, temperature not regulating
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Crack:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Crack in the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Ram 2500 has had a range of reported issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Overall, it is important for potential buyers to be aware of the potential problems associated with this vehicle and to factor them into their decision-making process.
