# 2022 Ram 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty pickup designed for serious towing and hauling, offering substantial capability through powerful gas and diesel engine options. While it provides immense utility, prospective buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain electronics, steering components, and trailer brake functionality.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 is a naturally aspirated gasoline engine producing around 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, offering strong conventional capability for heavy-duty work.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden losses of motive power while driving or towing, sometimes accompanied by lifter failure or electrical warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician to diagnose valvetrain or electrical faults.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six turbo diesel delivers exceptional low-end torque (up to 850 lb-ft) and heavy towing capacity, making it a popular choice for high-demand hauling.

**Diesel Emissions and Fuel System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine lights, diesel particulate filter restriction codes, and fuel pump contamination concerns have been noted by owners.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel system for particulate contamination and scan the emissions control modules for diagnostic trouble codes.

## Transmission

### 68FE 6-Speed Automatic

Paired with the Cummins diesel, this heavy-duty 6-speed automatic is built to manage massive torque loads under demanding towing conditions.

### ZF 8HP75 8-Speed Automatic

Matched to the 6.4L gas V8, this 8-speed automatic provides smooth power delivery and optimized gear spacing for both highway driving and towing.

**Limp Mode and Shifting Irregularities**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may intermittently enter limp mode or display unusual RPM fluctuations and mechanical sounds during deceleration.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid levels and have the powertrain control module scanned for diagnostic fault codes.

## Suspension

### Suspension and Tires

The heavy-duty suspension is engineered to support substantial payload and towing weights while maintaining ride composure.

**Front-End Vibration and Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported front-end vibrations or handling instability at highway speeds, alongside premature sidewall cracking on original equipment tires.
- **Solution:** Inspect front-end alignment, steering dampers, and tire sidewalls for structural defects.

## Brakes

### Brake and Trailer Control System

The braking system integrates heavy-duty discs and specialized trailer brake controls designed to safely halt large loads.

**Trailer Brake and Stability Control Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience warning messages such as 'Trailer Brake System', 'Service Electronic Stability Control', or low voltage output from the trailer wiring pigtail.
- **Solution:** Inspect trailer wiring harnesses, connectors, and brake control modules for electrical resistance or faults.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Clock Spring

The steering system utilizes a traditional heavy-duty linkage setup paired with an electronic control interface in the steering column.

**Clock Spring Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping noises when turning the steering wheel accompanied by inoperative steering wheel controls, heated steering wheel failures, or airbag warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replace the steering column clock spring assembly.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment

The vehicle relies heavily on integrated electrical architectures to control cabin technology, driver aids, and powertrain functions.

**Infotainment and Warning Light Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Navigation or center display screens going blank, alongside random warning lights illuminating on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates or check central fuse boxes and wiring connections.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V798000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay may cause a vehicle fire with the ignition on or off. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if needed, replace the Solid State Intake Heater Grid Relay ("Relay"); if FCA US Recall ID Y08 (NHTSA Recall ID 21V-163) is open, then insulating material will be applied to the Relay after inspection. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V925000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The side air bag inflatable curtain (SABIC) assemblies may have been manufactured with the incorrect amount of compressed gas, which can result in an improper air bag deployment. An air bag that deploys improperly can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the SABIC assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V140000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." An ESC malfunction indicator light that fails to illuminate during an electronic stability control problem can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ABS control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V407000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V642000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An internal loss of communication can result in a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ECM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V835000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Leaking transmission fluid may contact an ignition source within the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the transmission dipstick and transmission vent assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V904000: Structure:Body:Tailgate:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The tailgate may not latch properly due to misaligned tailgate strikers, which could result in the tailgate opening while driving. An unintended tailgate opening while driving may result in a loss of unsecured cargo, possibly creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the tailgate striker alignment to the box latch and adjust, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V060000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay can result in an engine compartment fire with the ignition on or off, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the relay, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ram 2500 is a highly capable heavy-duty truck equipped to tackle demanding work and towing tasks. While most owners enjoy robust performance from its gas and diesel powertrains, used car shoppers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for electrical gremlins, steering column clock spring operation, and trailer brake functionality before purchasing.
