# 2019 Ram 2500

### Overview
The 2019 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its robust build and towing capabilities. As a workhorse vehicle, its reliability is a key concern for owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, examining common problem areas across its powertrain, chassis, and electrical systems. While generally well-regarded, certain components and engine/transmission pairings have shown specific patterns of concern that potential buyers or current owners should be aware of. The data presented aims to provide a clear, concise overview of potential reliability challenges.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel
**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per injector

**EGR System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, increased soot, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (68RFE)
**Torque Converter Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Vibration or shuddering during acceleration, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ram 2500 generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly with its 6.4L HEMI V8 engine and manual transmission. The 6.7L Cummins diesel, while powerful, has seen some reports of fuel injector and EGR system issues, which can be costly to repair. The 6-speed automatic transmission may experience torque converter shudder. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are also noted but are not typically catastrophic. Overall, the Ram 2500 is a capable truck, but potential buyers should be mindful of the specific powertrain configurations and their associated potential issues, especially concerning the diesel engine and automatic transmission.
