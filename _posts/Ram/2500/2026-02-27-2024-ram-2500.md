# 2024 Ram 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2024 Ram 2500 means exploring a heavy-duty truck designed for heavy towing and demanding workloads. Buyers should weigh the capabilities of its powerhouse engine options against typical heavy-duty ownership considerations. Reviewing common owner feedback helps ensure you select a configuration that best matches your hauling needs.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4L HEMI V8 gasoline engine produces around 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong standard performance for daily work. It offers a traditional gas powertrain experience for buyers who prefer to avoid diesel maintenance requirements.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel inline-six delivers massive low-end torque—often ranging from 370 hp / 850 lb-ft up to high-output variants reaching 420 hp / 1,075 lb-ft depending on the model year and transmission pairing. It is built for heavy towing and long-term durability under heavy loads.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP75-LCV)

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with the 6.4L HEMI V8 gas engine, providing smooth gear selection and optimized power delivery. It helps maintain efficient engine speeds during both city driving and towing duties.

### 6-Speed Automatic (68RFE)

The 6-speed automatic transmission is commonly paired with standard-output configurations of the 6.7L Cummins diesel engine. It is engineered to handle heavy towing applications with robust internal gearing.

### Aisin 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic

The heavy-duty Aisin 6-speed automatic is typically paired with high-output Cummins diesel variants for maximum towing capacity. It provides commercial-grade durability designed to withstand extreme workloads.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V654000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V799000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the high beams to activate when the turn signal is used or activate the turn signal when the high beams are used. In addition, the turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the steering column control module (SCCM) software, and inspect and replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V288000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Loss of vehicle control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the powertrain control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V327000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Contamination in the central vision processing module or improper programming may cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the central vision processing module software or replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Ram 2500 is a highly capable heavy-duty truck built for serious towing and hauling tasks. Prospective buyers should inspect the condition of their chosen gas or diesel powertrain and verify service history before purchasing.
