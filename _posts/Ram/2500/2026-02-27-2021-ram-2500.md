# 2021 Ram 2500

### Overview
The 2021 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its robust build and capability. Reliability for this model year is generally strong, with owners reporting satisfaction with its core mechanical components. However, like any vehicle, specific issues can arise, particularly concerning the advanced diesel exhaust systems and certain electronic features. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the various engine and transmission configurations available. Understanding these potential points of concern can help owners prepare for and address any necessary repairs, ensuring the longevity and performance of their Ram 2500.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light illumination, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, exhaust smell.
- **Solution:** Forced DPF regeneration, cleaning or replacement of the DPF, addressing underlying causes like faulty sensors or incomplete combustion.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000

**DEF (Diesel Exhaust Fluid) System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light, DEF system warning messages, reduced engine power, potential inability to start.
- **Solution:** Inspection and cleaning of DEF injector and lines, replacement of DEF pump or sensors, software updates.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ram 2500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly with its gasoline HEMI V8 engine and standard transmissions. The Cummins diesel, while powerful, can present issues related to its emissions control systems (DPF and DEF), which are common across modern diesel trucks and require diligent maintenance. The optional Auto-Level Rear Air Suspension is a convenience feature that may require attention for air leaks. Electrical gremlins, primarily concerning the Uconnect infotainment system, are also noted but often resolvable with software updates. Overall, the Ram 2500 is a dependable heavy-duty truck, with most potential issues being manageable and specific to certain configurations or advanced systems.
