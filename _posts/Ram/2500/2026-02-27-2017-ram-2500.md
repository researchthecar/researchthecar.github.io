# 2017 Ram 2500

### Overview
The 2017 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its robust towing and hauling capabilities. Reliability for this model year is generally strong, particularly with its powerful diesel engine options. Owners often praise its durability and performance in demanding work environments. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from mechanics and owners. This report focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for the 6.4L HEMI V8 and the 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engines, as well as their associated transmissions.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel
**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per injector

**EGR System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, increased soot, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of EGR valve and cooler.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (68RFE)
**Torque Converter Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Vibration or shuddering during acceleration, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Fluid flush and filter replacement, or torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ram 2500 demonstrates solid reliability, especially for a heavy-duty truck. The 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine, while generally robust, can experience issues with fuel injectors and the EGR system, which are common for modern diesel powertrains. The 68RFE automatic transmission may occasionally exhibit torque converter shudder. Other systems like suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free of widespread problems. Electrical issues are infrequent, with the trailer brake controller being a notable, though not universal, concern. Overall, the Ram 2500 is a dependable choice for those needing significant hauling and towing power, with most potential issues being manageable and repairable.
