# 2017 Ram 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its robust towing capability and comfortable ride, available with formidable gasoline and diesel engine choices. However, prospective buyers should review specific recurring system concerns documented by owners, particularly related to the anti-lock braking and electronic stability control systems.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8 Gasoline Engine

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 produces around 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, providing strong conventional towing power for everyday heavy-duty needs.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six turbo diesel offers high torque output designed for heavy towing, though buyers should watch for emissions and turbo actuator complications.

**Turbocharger Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The variable geometry turbocharger actuator can stick or fail, leading to heat degradation of the turbo fan and a potential loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty variable geometry turbocharger actuator and inspect the turbocharger assembly for heat damage.
- **Cost:** $4680.00

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission paired with select powertrains, delivering dependable gear ratios built for commercial-grade workloads.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

An available 6-speed manual transmission gives enthusiast drivers direct control over gear selection when paired with compatible diesel models.

### 68RFE 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The standard automatic transmission option for high-output diesel variants, balancing shift performance under heavy loads with general durability.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Control Unit and ABS

The braking system provides substantial stopping power for a heavy-duty platform, though anti-lock module stability concerns remain a primary area of owner complaint activity.

**Brake Hydraulic Control Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** ABS, traction control, and electronic stability warning lights illuminate, potentially leading to intermittent brake system failure or wheels locking up unexpectedly during sudden stops.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty hydraulic control unit (HCU) and anti-lock braking system module.
- **Cost:** $1500

## Steering

### Steering Linkage

Steering components handle high loads typical of heavy-duty trucks, but historical linkage redesigns require careful physical inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Steering Linkage and Alignment Limitations**
- **Symptoms:** Play or looseness in the steering components can affect vehicle tracking, and previous recall remedies involving welded jam nuts may complicate future front-end alignments.
- **Solution:** Inspect the drag link assembly and steering linkage integrity; replace worn components if adjustments are restricted.

## Electrical

### Electronic Braking and Stability System

Electrical architecture supports advanced vehicle systems and towing features, but integrated brake-control modules can trigger cascading feature dropouts when faults occur.

**Service Electronic Braking Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Instrument cluster displays a service electronic braking system warning message, which can concurrently disable convenient features like cruise control and remote start.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic stability control module and replace malfunctioning wheel speed sensors or control units.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V021000: Steering:Linkages:Drag:Link:Connection](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A drag link separation can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will check the steering linkage jam nut tightness, replacing the drag link assembly or welding the nut to the adjuster sleeve, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18E096000: Steering:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The weakened connection may result in a fractured steering drag link, causing a loss of steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Streetcar will notify owners and dealers will inspect and install a new steering knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V347000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Tailgate:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate opens while the vehicle is being driven, cargo may fall out the vehicle and create a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the tailgate latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V486000: Structure:Body:Tailgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate latch releases and the tailgate opens while driving, cargo may fall out, creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, inspect the BTSI, and, as necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V100000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.

**[Recall 17V499000: Wheels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the wheel fractures, a loss of vehicle control can occur, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** AEV will notify owners, and dealers will replace the wheels with wheels of similar value, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ram 2500 remains a capable choice for heavy-duty truck buyers seeking strong towing performance, but shoppers must verify the health of the braking system, turbocharger actuator, and steering linkage. Ensure all safety recalls have been properly addressed by an authorized dealership before purchase.
