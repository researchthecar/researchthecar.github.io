# 2018 Ram 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Ram 2500, buyers will find a heavy-duty truck known for serious towing capability and rugged utility. However, this model year carries notable owner-reported concerns, particularly regarding hydraulic brake control systems, steering wear, and interior electronics. Prospective owners should carefully review service histories and check for open safety campaigns before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8 Gasoline Engine

The 6.4L HEMI V8 gasoline engine produces around 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, offering strong payload and acceleration capabilities for heavy workloads. It provides a robust alternative for buyers who do not need heavy diesel towing capacity.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel Inline-6

The 6.7L Cummins inline-6 turbo diesel delivers high torque output—up to 370 hp and 800 lb-ft—making it a preferred choice for heavy-duty towing and hauling. It generally offers long-term durability when properly maintained, though emissions equipment requires attentive care.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission (68RFE)

The 68RFE 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with various gas and diesel setups, handling heavy towing duties. While generally robust, higher-mileage units can suffer from slipping or internal wear.

**Transmission Slippage and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience transmission slipping, harsh clunking noises while shifting, or complete mechanical failure, sometimes leading to costly replacements.
- **Solution:** Repair or rebuild internal components, or replace the transmission assembly.
- **Cost:** $10,000

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The heavy-duty 6-speed manual transmission gives drivers direct control over power delivery and towing management. It is less common in modern fleets but valued by enthusiasts for simplicity.

### Aisin 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The heavy-duty Aisin commercial-grade automatic transmission is available on high-output diesel models designed for maximum towing capacity. It provides rugged durability for severe commercial applications.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake Control Unit

The braking system provides stopping power for heavy loads, but numerous complaints highlight hydraulic control unit and ABS module vulnerabilities.

**ABS and Traction Control Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated ABS, electronic stability control, and traction control warning lights accompanied by the deactivation of cruise control and occasional locking of tires during heavy braking.
- **Solution:** Have the hydraulic control unit (HCU) inspected and repaired or replaced under manufacturer guidance.

## Steering

### Steering Linkage and Components

The steering system on heavy-duty live-axle trucks undergoes significant stress, requiring periodic inspection of linkage components to maintain proper alignment and handling.

**Steering Play and Alignment Wobble**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive play in the steering wheel, persistent pulling to one side, or looseness commonly referred to as front-end wobble.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace worn ball joints, drag links, steering stabilizers, or bushings.

## Electrical

### Uconnect Infotainment Touchscreen

Electrical accessories provide cabin connectivity and control, though the infotainment touchscreen is prone to visual degradation and internal failure over time.

**Screen Delamination and Ghost Touching**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen interface begins bubbling, delaminating, or registering phantom touch commands that randomly alter climate control, radio, and navigation settings.
- **Solution:** Replace the defective infotainment display unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18E096000: Steering:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The weakened connection may result in a fractured steering drag link, causing a loss of steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Streetcar will notify owners and dealers will inspect and install a new steering knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V347000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Tailgate:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate opens while the vehicle is being driven, cargo may fall out the vehicle and create a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the tailgate latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V398000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." A loss of image in the rearview camera while backing up can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rearview mirror, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V021000: Steering:Linkages:Drag:Link:Connection](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A drag link separation can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will check the steering linkage jam nut tightness, replacing the drag link assembly or welding the nut to the adjuster sleeve, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V824000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Chrysler (FCA US LLC) is recalling various Dodge, Chrysler, and RAM vehicles equipped with Kidde Plastic-Handle or Push Button 'Pindicator' Fire Extinguishers. These extinguishers may become clogged, preventing the extinguisher from discharging as expected or requiring excessive force to activate the extinguisher.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 18V100000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners and dealers will update the Body Control Module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V896000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A loss of anti-lock brakes, electronic stability control, or traction control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake HCU, or replace the HCU motor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ram 2500 is a capable workhorse with strong engine and towing options, but buyers should approach with informed caution. Potential owners must verify that hydraulic brake recalls and steering components have been thoroughly inspected and addressed. Checking the operation of the infotainment screen and transmission shifting behavior during a test drive will help avoid unexpected repair expenses.
