# 2016 Ram 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty pickup known for its robust towing capacity and comfortable ride, but used-car buyers should carefully evaluate potential mechanical and electrical hiccups. While it offers impressive capability, owner complaints highlight specific problem areas ranging from trailer brake module faults to steering linkage wear and powertrain glitches. Being aware of these common trouble spots will help you inspect a prospective purchase more thoroughly.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7L Cummins inline-six turbo diesel produces substantial torque for heavy towing and is renowned for longevity, though auxiliary emissions and turbo controls require careful maintenance.

**Grid Heater Nut Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The grid heater nut or bolt can detach and fall into the engine cylinders, causing catastrophic internal damage and total engine failure while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the intake horn and grid heater setup, and consider aftermarket relocation kits to prevent hardware ingestion.

**VGT Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Variable Geometry Turbocharger (VGT) actuator failure can lead to reduced engine performance, warning lights, and expensive repair costs.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty VGT actuator assembly.
- **Cost:** $2170

### 5.7L Hemi V8

The 5.7L Hemi V8 offers strong gasoline horsepower and smooth operation, making it a capable choice for lighter duty cycles despite lower torque outputs than the diesel.

### Infotainment and Engine Controls

Electrical complaints center around infotainment screen reliability and occasional sensor-related driveability quirks.

**Uconnect Radio and Stalling Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The Uconnect 8.4 touchscreen radio can experience daily freezing or screen failures, and intermittent engine stalling may occur while slowing down.
- **Solution:** Update or replace the infotainment unit, and inspect engine sensors and throttle bodies if stalling persists.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The heavy-duty 6-speed manual transmission gives drivers direct control over gear selection, though clutch longevity heavily depends on towing habits and payload weight.

**Clutch and Shifter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears or premature clutch wear can leave the vehicle unable to move in traffic.
- **Solution:** Inspect the clutch assembly and linkage for wear, replacing components as necessary.

### 6-Speed Automatic (68RFE)

The 68RFE 6-speed automatic is paired with the diesel engine to handle heavy loads, but regular fluid changes are vital to prevent premature wear.

### 6-Speed Automatic (AS69RC)

The heavy-duty Aisin 6-speed automatic is designed for maximum towing capacity and commercial-grade durability.

### 6-Speed Automatic (66RFE)

The 66RFE automatic transmission is matched to gasoline engine options, providing smooth power delivery for daily driving and light utility use.

## Brakes

### Trailer Brake and ABS Module

Braking components are critical on heavy-duty trucks, and owners report occasional electronic brake controller and ABS module faults.

**Integrated Trailer Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The Integrated Trailer Brake Module (ITBM) may fail to output correct braking voltage to connected trailers, or the ABS warning light may illuminate due to a failing control module.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the brake controller and ABS module codes, then replace faulty electrical modules or sensors.

## Steering

### Steering Linkage and Power Steering

Front-end steering geometry on solid-axle trucks requires periodic inspection for wear and play to maintain precise directional control.

**Drag Link and Wandering Steering**
- **Symptoms:** Worn drag links can prevent proper front-end alignment, cause the vehicle to wander, or result in loose steering response.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering linkages for play or loose welds, and replace worn drag links or low-pressure power steering hoses.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V021000: Steering:Linkages:Drag:Link:Connection](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A drag link separation can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will check the steering linkage jam nut tightness, replacing the drag link assembly or welding the nut to the adjuster sleeve, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18E096000: Steering:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The weakened connection may result in a fractured steering drag link, causing a loss of steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Streetcar will notify owners and dealers will inspect and install a new steering knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V347000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Tailgate:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate opens while the vehicle is being driven, cargo may fall out the vehicle and create a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the tailgate latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V486000: Structure:Body:Tailgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate latch releases and the tailgate opens while driving, cargo may fall out, creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V632000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain:Inflator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A separated inflator end cap or inflator rupture can allow compressed gas to escape, resulting in the end cap or other components being propelled into the vehicle, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace as necessary, the SABIC assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, inspect the BTSI, and, as necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V499000: Wheels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the wheel fractures, a loss of vehicle control can occur, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** AEV will notify owners, and dealers will replace the wheels with wheels of similar value, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ram 2500 is a powerful and capable workhorse, but buyers should approach with a thorough inspection checklist. Pay close attention to front-end steering components, trailer brake controller functionality, and diesel engine service history. Verifying that all recall and maintenance items have been addressed will significantly reduce the risk of unexpected repair bills.
