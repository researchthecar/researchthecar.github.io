# 2023 Ram 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ram 2500 is a heavy-duty powerhouse designed for serious towing and hauling, offering robust capability alongside a remarkably refined interior. When shopping for a used heavy-duty truck, evaluating the powertrain options and monitoring owner-reported issues can help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4L HEMI V8 gasoline engine delivers approximately 410 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque, providing strong standard performance for heavy-duty tasks. It offers a simpler maintenance profile compared to diesel alternatives, making it a popular choice for general commercial and personal use.

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel inline-six produces up to 370 horsepower and an impressive 850 lb-ft of torque, tailored for maximum towing capability and heavy workloads. Known for long-term durability when properly maintained, it remains a staple for heavy-duty truck buyers.

### 6.7L Cummins High Output Turbo Diesel I6

The High Output variant of the 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel boosts output to 420 horsepower and a massive 1,075 lb-ft of torque for maximum-capacity towing. Paired with specialized heavy-duty transmissions, it is built to handle the most demanding commercial applications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (8HP75)

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with the 6.4L HEMI V8, offering smooth and responsive gear selection to optimize power delivery and fuel efficiency. It is well-regarded for its wide gear spread and refined shifting characteristics under various loads.

### 6-Speed Automatic (68RFE)

The 6-speed automatic transmission is utilized in standard-output Cummins diesel models, engineered to handle heavy towing demands. Regular fluid maintenance is essential for maximizing the operational lifespan of this heavy-duty unit.

### Aisin 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic

The Aisin heavy-duty automatic transmission is coupled with the High Output Cummins diesel engine to manage extreme torque loads. It offers robust commercial-grade durability for buyers who regularly push their trucks to maximum capacity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V835000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Leaking transmission fluid may contact an ignition source within the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the transmission dipstick and transmission vent assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V060000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay can result in an engine compartment fire with the ignition on or off, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the relay, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V059000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display while in reverse decreases the driver's visibility of the rear view, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the TRSCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V654000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V799000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the high beams to activate when the turn signal is used or activate the turn signal when the high beams are used. In addition, the turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the steering column control module (SCCM) software, and inspect and replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power or a loss of park function increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the valve body and control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V288000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Loss of vehicle control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the powertrain control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ram 2500 is a formidable heavy-duty truck backed by a strong lineup of gasoline and diesel powertrains. Prospective buyers should carefully inspect the service history and verify that the chosen engine and transmission match their specific towing and hauling requirements.
