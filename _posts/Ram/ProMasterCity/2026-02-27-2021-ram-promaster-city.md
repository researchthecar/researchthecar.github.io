# 2021 Ram ProMaster City Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Ram ProMaster City, buyers should carefully review potential powertrain and mechanical concerns. While owner reports provide valuable insights into real-world performance, it is important to remember that these complaints reflect individual experiences rather than guaranteed defects. A thorough pre-purchase inspection can help uncover hidden issues before finalizing your purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Tigershark 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter Tigershark four-cylinder engine produces approximately 178 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with a front-wheel-drive system. Buyers should inspect the engine bay thoroughly for any signs of oil leaks or coolant issues during a test drive.

**Cracked Cylinder Head**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported cracked cylinder heads around 80,000 miles, which can necessitate a full engine replacement.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection and, if cracked, full engine replacement.

**Oil Filter Mounting Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leakage around the oil filter due to an uneven mounting surface.
- **Solution:** Have the mounting surface and affected components inspected and replaced as needed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels, providing highway efficiency for delivery duties. While no specific recurring complaint clusters dominate this unit, routine fluid checks are recommended.

## Exterior

### Roof Latch Equipment

Steering and general equipment function normally, though accessory hardware should be checked.

**Unexpected Latch Opening**
- **Symptoms:** Rooftop accessories or tent latches have opened unexpectedly while parked during wind gusts or while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect and secure all aftermarket or factory roof latches and attachment hardware.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V699000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt retractors as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V147000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module may have internal damage, which could result in a loss of braking performance or autonomous braking functions. The loss of braking performance or autonomous braking functions can increase the risk of a vehicle crash without prior warning.
- **What to do:** FCA US, LLC will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V728000: Exterior Lighting:Turn Signal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** If one or both turn signal lights fail, the flashing rate may not change to warn the driver of the failure. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may be unaware that the turn signals are not signaling their intentions.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Body Controller Module (BCM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Ram ProMaster City offers practical utility for commercial needs, but prospective buyers should remain vigilant regarding potential engine durability concerns such as cylinder head wear and oil leaks. Always secure a professional pre-purchase inspection and verify service histories to minimize unexpected repair costs.
