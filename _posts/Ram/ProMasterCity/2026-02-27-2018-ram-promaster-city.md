# 2018 Ram ProMaster City Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Ram ProMaster City, buyers will find a utilitarian compact van designed primarily for commercial delivery and cargo hauling. Equipped with a standard four-cylinder engine and a front-wheel-drive automatic transmission setup, it offers good maneuverability and fuel economy for city routes. Prospective buyers should review maintenance records carefully, as owner reports suggest keeping a close eye on fluid levels and general mechanical health during pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Tigershark MultiAir 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter Tigershark four-cylinder engine produces approximately 178 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for urban delivery duties. While generally reliable, owners should monitor oil consumption regularly to prevent unexpected mechanical issues.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may shut off unexpectedly while driving without displaying prior warning lights or low oil indicators.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the underlying cause of the stall, check engine fluid levels frequently, and inspect electrical or fuel delivery systems.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This nine-speed automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels, aiming to optimize both highway efficiency and low-end acceleration. Buyers should test drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure smooth shifting across all gears.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V129000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." If the tire placard indicates an incorrect seating capacity, the operator may overload the vehicle, which can cause tire failure, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will install a corrected tire placard, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V149000: Steering:Rack And Pinion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A hydraulic steering fluid leak may require additional sudden steering effort, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the steering gear housing, replacing any affected rack-and-pinion assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ram ProMaster City is a practical choice for small business owners and couriers needing versatile cargo space. While overall mechanical complaints remain limited in public data, buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection—paying special attention to fluid levels and powertrain operation—to ensure long-term dependability.
