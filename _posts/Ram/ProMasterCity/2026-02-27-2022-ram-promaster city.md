### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ram ProMaster City demonstrates a generally strong reliability record, particularly in its powertrain components. The 2.4L Tigershark engine and 9-speed automatic transmission have not shown widespread issues. The chassis and suspension are also robust for its intended commercial use. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically intermittent infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronics. Exterior and interior components are typically durable for commercial applications. Overall, the ProMaster City is a dependable choice for businesses seeking a cost-effective and functional urban commercial vehicle, with potential electrical gremlins being the most likely, though not guaranteed, technical hiccup.
