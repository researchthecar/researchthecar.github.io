# 2016 Ram ProMaster City Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ram ProMaster City is a popular choice for small business owners and delivery fleets seeking a compact cargo or passenger van with impressive utility. However, used car buyers should approach this model with awareness of its potential weak spots, particularly concerning electrical issues, frequent headlight bulb failures, and reports of major powertrain or transmission concerns. Thoroughly inspecting the service history and checking the functionality of all electronics and the transmission is strongly advised before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine produces around 280 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and towing capacity for larger payloads. It is generally regarded as durable, though auxiliary electrical and cooling components require regular monitoring.

### 2.4L Tigershark 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter Tigershark four-cylinder engine generates approximately 178 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with a front-wheel-drive automatic transmission. While it provides nimble fuel economy for urban delivery routes, buyers should inspect for timing and stalling concerns reported in owner feedback.

**Engine Stalling and Shutoff**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly shuts off while driving at highway speeds and fails to restart immediately.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic diagnose the electrical system, fuel delivery, and powertrain control module for fault codes.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The nine-speed automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels, offering efficient highway cruising. However, it is a frequent point of owner complaints regarding rough shifting and premature failure.

**Transmission Failure and Hard Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden transmission failure, severe jerking or jolting when shifting gears, or inability to shift properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition, check for pending software updates, or replace the transmission unit if catastrophic mechanical failure has occurred.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake

The parking brake mechanism is susceptible to moisture freezing inside the lines during cold weather conditions.

**Sticking Parking Brake in Freezing Temperatures**
- **Symptoms:** Parking brake fails to release when temperatures drop below freezing due to moisture trapped inside the cable housing.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace parking brake cables and ensure proper sealing against moisture infiltration.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems can experience recurring electrical resistance or bulb longevity issues, causing frequent replacements.

**Frequent Headlight Bulb Burnout**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight bulbs burn out repeatedly within short intervals, or turn signals become inoperable.
- **Solution:** Inspect the wiring harness, headlight housing connectors, and voltage regulators for underlying faults causing premature bulb failure.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V824000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Chrysler (FCA US LLC) is recalling various Dodge, Chrysler, and RAM vehicles equipped with Kidde Plastic-Handle or Push Button 'Pindicator' Fire Extinguishers. These extinguishers may become clogged, preventing the extinguisher from discharging as expected or requiring excessive force to activate the extinguisher.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V601000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." The incorrect tire placard can cause the operator to exceed the vehicle's weight capacity, or inflate the tires to an incorrect pressure, affecting vehicle handling, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the incorrect tire placard with a corrected one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ram ProMaster City offers practical cargo solutions and maneuverability, but used buyers should exercise caution due to notable complaints regarding transmission durability and electrical issues. A pre-purchase inspection focusing heavily on transmission shift quality, wiring health, and service records is essential to avoid costly surprise repairs.
