# 2019 Ram ProMaster City Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Ram ProMaster City, buyers will find a utilitarian compact van designed primarily for delivery and commercial hauling needs. While powertrain details are straightforward, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback regarding dashboard displays, steering responsiveness, and structural elements like the spare tire mount. Careful inspection during a test drive can help identify any lingering electrical or mechanical quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Tigershark MultiAir 4-Cylinder

The 2.4L Tigershark 4-cylinder engine produces around 178 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for city deliveries. It is a common powerplant for this model, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This front-wheel-drive van utilizes a 9-speed automatic transmission designed to balance highway efficiency with low-end gearing. Routine fluid checks are recommended to ensure smooth long-term operation.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

Steering feedback indicates occasional mechanical chattering during turns, warranting a close check of the front end.

**Steering Wheel Chattering and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced unusual noises and a chattering sensation in the steering wheel while making turns at low speeds or on declines.
- **Solution:** Have the steering rack, column, and suspension components inspected for wear or binding.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster & Dash

Electrical complaints center around intermittent failures of dashboard instrumentation, which can reduce driver awareness of vehicle speed.

**Dashboard and Speedometer Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the entire dashboard goes completely black or the speedometer stops working.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check fuses, wiring harnesses, or instrument cluster modules for replacement.

## Exterior

### Spare Tire Carrier

Body and structure notes highlight minor accessibility snags with exterior equipment, such as the spare tire carrier.

**Stuck Spare Tire Mount**
- **Symptoms:** Following a flat tire, some owners discovered that the spare tire could not be released or removed from its mounting location.
- **Solution:** Inspect the spare tire winch mechanism and lubricate or replace corroded release cables as necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety system reports include isolated claims of air bags failing to deploy in accidents, though these represent owner-submitted experiences rather than proven systemic faults.

**Frontal Air Bag Non-Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported a failure of the frontal air bags to deploy during a moderate-to-high-speed front-impact collision.
- **Solution:** Ensure the supplemental restraint system (SRS) module and sensors are professionally inspected if any warning lights are illuminated.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V728000: Exterior Lighting:Turn Signal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If one or both turn signal lights fail, the flashing rate may not change to warn the driver of the failure. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may be unaware that the turn signals are not signaling their intentions.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Body Controller Module (BCM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Ram ProMaster City offers practical cargo solutions for daily work, but used buyers should remain vigilant during pre-purchase inspections. Pay close attention to electrical functions like the dashboard and speedometer, test the steering responsiveness through tight turns, and verify that the spare tire mechanism operates freely. Maintaining a healthy skepticism regarding owner-reported complaints will help ensure a reliable commercial vehicle purchase.
