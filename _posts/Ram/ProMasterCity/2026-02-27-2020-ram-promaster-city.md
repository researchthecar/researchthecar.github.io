# 2020 Ram ProMaster City Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Ram ProMaster City is a compact commercial van designed for small businesses and urban deliveries, sharing its platform and mechanicals with global Fiat designs. It offers nimble handling and high cargo capacity, making it a popular choice for fleet operators. While owner feedback in early reports points to mostly standard utility wear, buyers should carefully inspect functional cargo features and overall mechanical condition before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Tigershark MultiAir 4-Cylinder

The 2.4L Tigershark 4-cylinder engine produces 178 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque. It is paired exclusively with front-wheel drive and serves as the sole power plant for the ProMaster City lineup.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through a 9-speed automatic transmission driving the front wheels. Buyers should test-drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure smooth shifting across all gears.

## Exterior

### Spare Tire Mounting

Exterior components are generally built for commercial durability, though minor latch and mounting mechanism issues can occur.

**Stuck Spare Tire Mechanism**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience difficulty releasing or removing the spare tire from its undercarriage mounting area when attempting to change a flat tire.
- **Solution:** Inspect and lubricate the spare tire winch mechanism periodically to prevent binding or corrosion.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V147000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module may have internal damage, which could result in a loss of braking performance or autonomous braking functions. The loss of braking performance or autonomous braking functions can increase the risk of a vehicle crash without prior warning.
- **What to do:** FCA US, LLC will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V728000: Exterior Lighting:Turn Signal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=ProMaster+City&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If one or both turn signal lights fail, the flashing rate may not change to warn the driver of the failure. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may be unaware that the turn signals are not signaling their intentions.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Body Controller Module (BCM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Ram ProMaster City provides practical urban utility and solid cargo space for commercial buyers. While available owner data indicates minimal major mechanical complaints, thorough pre-purchase inspections of utility features and routine maintenance history remain essential.
