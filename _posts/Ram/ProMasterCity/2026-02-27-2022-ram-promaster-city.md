# 2022 Ram ProMaster City Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ram ProMaster City is a compact commercial van powered by a single standard engine paired with a front-wheel-drive automatic transmission setup, making it a popular choice for urban delivery and trades. While overall complaint volume remains relatively low, buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain performance and electrical integrity during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Tigershark MultiAir 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter Tigershark four-cylinder engine produces 178 horsepower and 171 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for light commercial hauling. It is generally a straightforward powerplant, though routine monitoring of fluid levels and electrical systems is recommended for used buyers.

**Electrical Burning Odor**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unusual burning smells resembling electrical components after normal vehicle operation.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine bay wiring harness and electrical accessories for signs of overheating or shorts.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This nine-speed automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels and aids in highway fuel efficiency. Buyers should test-drive the vehicle to ensure smooth gear engagement and verify that no unexpected RPM surges occur during low-speed maneuvering.

**RPM Surge During Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** The engine RPM may unexpectedly spike to around 4,000 when shifting into drive or reverse, occasionally while the brake pedal is depressed.
- **Solution:** Check for available powertrain control module updates or have the transmission control system inspected by a specialist.

The 2022 Ram ProMaster City offers functional utility for business owners, but prospective buyers should conduct a thorough test drive to check for transmission shifting anomalies and inspect the engine bay for any electrical concerns.
