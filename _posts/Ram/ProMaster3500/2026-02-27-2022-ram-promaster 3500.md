### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ram ProMaster 3500 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine and the 6-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. Minor electrical issues, such as infotainment system glitches and occasional battery drain, are the most commonly reported concerns, though these do not appear to be systemic. Exterior and interior components are holding up well under typical commercial use. Overall, the ProMaster 3500 is a dependable work van with a solid reliability record, making it a sound investment for businesses prioritizing uptime and reduced unexpected repair costs.
