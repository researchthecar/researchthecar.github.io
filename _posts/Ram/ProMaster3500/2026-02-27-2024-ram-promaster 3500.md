### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Ram ProMaster 3500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.6L Pentastar V6 and 6-speed automatic transmission are well-established and generally perform without significant issues. Areas that may require attention over time include the electrical system, with some users reporting infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues. Exterior components like door mechanisms can experience stiffness due to heavy use. Interior elements, such as seating and the HVAC system, may show wear or require maintenance like filter replacements. Overall, the ProMaster 3500 is a dependable workhorse, with most reported issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures.
