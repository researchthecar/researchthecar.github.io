## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces approximately 276 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque, paired with front-wheel drive. It is a widely used powerplant designed to balance everyday commercial capability with standard gasoline fueling.

### Cooling Fan and Battery System

Electrical concerns on newer commercial vans typically involve accessory controls and battery management systems.

**Engine Cooling Fan Drain**
- **Symptoms:** The engine cooling fan may continue running indefinitely after the vehicle is turned off, draining the battery and causing a no-start condition the following morning.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fan control relay or module and replace faulty electrical components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 9-speed automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels, offering optimized gear ratios for highway cruising and city delivery work. It generally operates smoothly when properly maintained.
