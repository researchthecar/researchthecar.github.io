### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display may intermittently freeze or become unresponsive to touch inputs.
- **Solution:** Software update or infotainment system reset. In some cases, replacement of the unit may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, leading to starting issues. May be related to parasitic draw from certain modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the parasitic draw, which could involve checking modules, wiring, or aftermarket accessories. Battery replacement may be needed if damaged.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ram ProMaster 3500 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly with its 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission, which show no widespread issues. The chassis and suspension components are also generally robust for commercial duty. The primary areas requiring attention are within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit experiencing occasional freezing and potential intermittent battery drain issues. These electrical concerns are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex systems. Overall, the ProMaster 3500 is a dependable work vehicle, with most reported issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic powertrain failures.
