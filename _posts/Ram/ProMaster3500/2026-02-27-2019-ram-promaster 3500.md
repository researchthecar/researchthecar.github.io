### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ram ProMaster 3500 generally exhibits good reliability, with its core powertrain components (3.6L Pentastar V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The suspension and interior also appear to be robust for commercial use. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the electrical system, specifically intermittent power loss and battery drain, which can be attributed to wiring or parasitic draws. Exterior issues are minor, with occasional door latch malfunctions reported. Overall, the ProMaster 3500 is a dependable work vehicle, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of fundamental design flaws.
