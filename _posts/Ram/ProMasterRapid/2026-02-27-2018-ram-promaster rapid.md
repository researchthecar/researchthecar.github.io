### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Ram ProMaster Rapid demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain (3.6L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission) and suspension, with no widespread critical failures reported. The primary areas of concern tend to be electrical, specifically battery drain issues and occasional infotainment system glitches, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles. Exterior-wise, the sliding door mechanism can sometimes require attention. Overall, for a commercial vehicle, the ProMaster Rapid offers a dependable platform with manageable potential issues, making it a practical choice for businesses prioritizing functionality and uptime.
