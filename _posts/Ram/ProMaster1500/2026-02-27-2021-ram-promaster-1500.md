# 2021 Ram ProMaster 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ram ProMaster 1500 is a full-size cargo van popular with commercial fleets and camper-van converters. While it offers generous interior volume and front-wheel-drive maneuverability, buyers exploring the used market should review available owner feedback regarding equipment and auxiliary systems, noting that NHTSA complaints reflect individual owner reports rather than proven widespread defects.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces approximately 280 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing standard motive power for the lineup. This engine is a familiar and widely utilized powerplant across various Chrysler and Ram applications, offering predictable daily performance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the V6 engine to deliver power to the front wheels. It is tuned for commercial duty cycles, balancing low-end acceleration with highway cruising capability.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera

Electrical accessories and driver-assistance displays generally function well, though auxiliary systems warrant standard pre-purchase checks.

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rear backup camera image displays the ground instead of the area directly behind the vehicle, and parking grid lines may disappear.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera unit, wiring harness, and display settings, replacing faulty camera hardware if necessary.

## Exterior

### Exterior Equipment

Exterior body panels and factory fixtures hold up reasonably to commercial use, but aftermarket additions require careful secondary inspection.

**Aftermarket Solar Panel Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Roof-mounted aftermarket equipment or solar panels may fracture or detach unexpectedly while the vehicle is in motion.
- **Solution:** Inspect all aftermarket roof installations, mounting brackets, and hardware to ensure secure fastening.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2021 Ram ProMaster 1500, verify the condition of any aftermarket upfits, such as solar arrays or camper conversions, and test all electrical features including the backup camera. Conduct a thorough personal inspection to ensure all equipment is securely fastened and fully operational.
