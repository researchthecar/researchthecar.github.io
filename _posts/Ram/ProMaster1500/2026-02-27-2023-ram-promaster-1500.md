# 2023 Ram ProMaster 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Ram ProMaster 1500, buyers will find a capable commercial cargo van designed for heavy-duty work and custom upfits. Because this model year is relatively new to the pre-owned market, available owner feedback is still developing. Reviewing standard powertrain specifications and remaining factory warranties can help guide your purchase decision.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Pentastar

This 3.6-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces approximately 276 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque, providing steady power for daily hauling. It is a widely used and generally dependable powerplant across the automaker's commercial lineup.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission routing power to the front wheels, this setup is tuned to optimize highway efficiency and low-end city responsiveness. Buyers should ensure smooth shifting during a test drive.

## Exterior

### A-Pillar and Mirrors

Exterior panels and structures are built for commercial durability, though specific blind-spot configurations warrant careful evaluation during a test drive.

**A-Pillar Blind Spot**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may find that the driver's side A-pillar trim partially obstructs the line of sight toward the side view mirror, creating potential visibility concerns.
- **Solution:** Adjust seating position and mirrors carefully to minimize blind spots, or utilize auxiliary wide-angle stick-on mirrors.
