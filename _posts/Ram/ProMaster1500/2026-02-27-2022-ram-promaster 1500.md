## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or connectivity issues with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, especially when the vehicle is parked for extended periods or with aftermarket accessories installed.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, checking battery health, or potential replacement of battery or related modules.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Sliding Door Mechanism Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Sticking, difficulty opening/closing, or rattling noises from the sliding side door.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ram ProMaster 1500 demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain (3.6L Pentastar V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission) and suspension systems, where no significant widespread issues have been reported. The primary areas of concern are minor electrical glitches, such as infotainment system unresponsiveness and occasional battery drain, and some potential issues with the sliding door mechanism. These are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's utility as a commercial work van. With proper maintenance and attention to these specific components, the ProMaster 1500 should provide dependable service for its intended purpose.
