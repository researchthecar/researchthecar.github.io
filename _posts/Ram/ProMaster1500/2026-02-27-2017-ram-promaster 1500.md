### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ram ProMaster 1500 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core powertrain (3.6L Pentastar V6 and 62TE automatic transmission) being a strong point. The suspension and exterior components generally hold up well under typical commercial use. The primary areas that may require attention are certain electrical components, specifically the Body Control Module and the infotainment system, which can exhibit intermittent issues or unresponsiveness. While these are not universal problems, they are the most frequently cited technical concerns. Proactive diagnosis and potential software updates or component replacements for these electrical systems are recommended to maintain optimal functionality and prevent minor annoyances from escalating.
