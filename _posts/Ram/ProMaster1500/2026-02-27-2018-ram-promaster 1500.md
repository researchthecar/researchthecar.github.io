### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained battery drain leading to starting issues, potentially related to parasitic draw from certain modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Ram ProMaster 1500 demonstrates solid mechanical reliability, with its V6 engine and automatic transmission being largely trouble-free. The primary areas of concern are concentrated within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit and potential battery drain issues. Exterior and interior components, along with the suspension, appear to be robust. For owners, staying vigilant about electrical system diagnostics and ensuring software updates for the infotainment are applied can mitigate most common problems. Overall, it's a dependable work van with a few electrical quirks that are manageable.
