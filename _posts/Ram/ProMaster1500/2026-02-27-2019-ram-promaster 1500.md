# 2019 Ram ProMaster 1500

The 2019 Ram ProMaster 1500 is a popular choice for commercial use, known for its robust cargo capacity and versatile configurations. ## Electrical

### Body Control Module
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Various warning lights may illuminate, power locks or windows may malfunction, or infotainment system may reset unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the Body Control Module (BCM).
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine, or complete loss of electrical power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $800


## Exterior

### Sliding Door Mechanism
**Sticking or Difficult Operation**
- **Symptoms:** Sliding door may be difficult to open or close, may stick, or may not latch properly.
- **Solution:** Lubrication of the tracks and rollers, adjustment of the door, or replacement of worn components.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


### Rear Door Hinges
**Corrosion and Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Rear doors may be stiff to open or close, squeaking noises from hinges, or visible rust on hinge components.
- **Solution:** Cleaning and lubrication of hinges, or replacement if severely corroded.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### HVAC Blower Motor
**Failure to Operate**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or air only coming from certain vents regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


### Infotainment System
**Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes, or reboots randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update or potential replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ram ProMaster 1500 demonstrates good overall reliability, especially concerning its powertrain. The 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are generally robust and do not present widespread issues. The suspension system also holds up well under typical commercial use. However, potential owners should be aware of some electrical components, such as the Body Control Module and alternator, which have been reported to fail prematurely. Exterior issues are primarily related to the sliding door mechanism and rear door hinges, often requiring maintenance or minor repairs. Interior concerns are mainly focused on the HVAC blower motor and the infotainment system's responsiveness. With proactive maintenance and attention to these specific areas, the ProMaster 1500 can remain a dependable work vehicle.
