# 2018 Ram 5500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Ram 5500 is a heavy-duty commercial-grade chassis cab designed for demanding work environments, fleet use, and heavy towing. While built to handle rigorous tasks, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues and active service campaigns concerning the braking system, steering, and drivetrain components before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six turbo diesel produces substantial torque, making it the primary engine choice for heavy hauling and commercial upfits in the Ram 5500. It is generally regarded as a robust and durable commercial powerplant when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### Aisin 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

Paired with the Cummins diesel, the Aisin 6-speed automatic transmission is engineered specifically for heavy-duty commercial duty cycles and high gross combined weight ratings. It offers dependable shifting performance under heavy loads.

### Transfer Case and Front Driveshaft

Four-wheel-drive components must endure significant torque loads under commercial applications, requiring careful inspection for drivetrain wear.

**Transfer Case and Driveshaft Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loud mechanical noises, sudden vehicle deceleration or bucking, and potential physical damage to the cabin floorboard from fractured driveshafts.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transfer case, universal joints, and front driveshaft for excessive play, vibration, or structural damage.

## Suspension

### Wheel Hubs and Studs

Suspension and wheel components endure high weight stresses, making regular inspection of wheel hardware critical for safe operation.

**Rear Wheel Stud Fractures**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden wheel stud breakage while driving at highway speeds, potentially leading to wheel instability without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheel studs for wear or fatigue and replace damaged hubs or studs promptly.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Control Unit & ABS Pump

The braking system relies on heavy-duty components designed to manage commercial loads, though hydraulic control unit failures have been reported by owners.

**Anti-Lock Brake and Hydraulic Control Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated ABS, traction control, and brake warning lights, accompanied by a loss of anti-lock braking function, stability control, and cruise control.
- **Solution:** Have the hydraulic control unit and ABS pump inspected and replaced if affected by manufacturer recall or component failure.

## Steering

### Steering Shaft

The steering linkage handles heavy front-end loads typical of commercial utility trucks and requires thorough pre-purchase checks.

**Steering Shaft Complete Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of steering control while maneuvering or driving out of driveways.
- **Solution:** Inspect the intermediate steering shaft and linkage for wear or binding, and replace components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Uconnect Infotainment System

Cabin electronics generally function well, but infotainment display degradation can occur over time.

**Screen Delamination and 'Ghost Touch'**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen registers uncommanded inputs, randomly turning the radio on and off or initiating automated emergency calls.
- **Solution:** Replace the delaminated touchscreen or head unit assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V359000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Unruh Fire, Inc. (Unruh Fire) is recalling one 2018 Dodge 5500-based fire truck equipped with Whelen brake lights.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 18V277000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the ferrules corrode, the brake fluid may leak and cause a loss of braking, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the brake hoses and replace them if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V255000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the brake lights incorrectly illuminate, it can confuse other drivers, possibly increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Blanchat has notified the owner, and will replace the brake lights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V398000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect information in the Service and Owner's Manuals may cause the flanged lug nuts to be over-tightened, which could result in a broken wheel stud and possible wheel separation. Wheel separation can cause a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the torque specification in the owner's manual and other published service documents. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V100000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners and dealers will update the Body Control Module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V824000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Chrysler (FCA US LLC) is recalling various Dodge, Chrysler, and RAM vehicles equipped with Kidde Plastic-Handle or Push Button 'Pindicator' Fire Extinguishers. These extinguishers may become clogged, preventing the extinguisher from discharging as expected or requiring excessive force to activate the extinguisher.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V896000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A loss of anti-lock brakes, electronic stability control, or traction control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake HCU, or replace the HCU motor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ram 5500 is a formidable work truck capable of heavy commercial deployment, but used buyers should proceed with diligence. Review vehicle history records to confirm whether open safety recalls—particularly those involving hydraulic brakes and wheel components—have been completed, and thoroughly inspect the steering, suspension, and four-wheel-drive systems before finalizing a purchase.
