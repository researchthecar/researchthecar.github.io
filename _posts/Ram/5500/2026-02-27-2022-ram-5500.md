# 2022 Ram 5500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ram 5500 is a heavy-duty chassis cab built for demanding commercial and towing applications. While it offers immense capability, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate owner-reported complaints regarding powertrain and steering components before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins High Output Turbo Diesel I6

This heavy-duty inline-six diesel engine produces robust torque—up to 800 lb-ft or more depending on configuration—designed for heavy towing and commercial duty. It is generally regarded as a long-lasting commercial workhorse, though buyers should monitor for any reported auxiliary electrical or emissions concerns.

## Transmission

### Aisin 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

This heavy-duty six-speed automatic transmission is paired with the high-output diesel engine to handle extreme gross vehicle weight ratings. Early component failures, notably involving internal snap rings, have been reported by some owners and require professional service.

**K1/K2 Clutch Snap Ring Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden loss of motive power, failure to shift into forward gears, or the transmission slipping into reverse only, often accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes like P0731.
- **Solution:** Requires dealership inspection and repair or replacement of the affected clutch snap rings and internal transmission components under warranty.

## Suspension

### Wheel Hubs and Fasteners

Suspension and wheel mounting components on heavy-duty commercial trucks require vigilant maintenance to prevent dangerous roadside failures.

**Wheel Stud Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Wheel studs shear off while driving, potentially causing dual wheels to detach from the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect all wheel studs and lug nuts for signs of stretching or fatigue, and replace damaged hubs or studs promptly.

## Steering

### Steering Linkage and Gear

The steering system on commercial chassis cabs endures heavy loads, making regular linkage inspections essential for safe operation.

**Center Link Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden loud snapping sound accompanied by a complete loss of steering control while maneuvering or driving at low to moderate speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the steering linkage inspected for stress or fractures and replace the center link assembly immediately.

## Interior

### Grid Heater Relay

Electrical accessories and heavy-duty charging systems require periodic verification to ensure safe commercial operation.

**Grid Heater Electrical Short**
- **Symptoms:** Risk of an electrical relay failure in the intake grid heater circuit that may cause localized overheating or fire risks.
- **Solution:** Check with a Ram dealer to see if permanent recall remedies or updated relays have been installed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V798000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay may cause a vehicle fire with the ignition on or off. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if needed, replace the Solid State Intake Heater Grid Relay ("Relay"); if FCA US Recall ID Y08 (NHTSA Recall ID 21V-163) is open, then insulating material will be applied to the Relay after inspection. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V642000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An internal loss of communication can result in a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ECM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V060000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay can result in an engine compartment fire with the ignition on or off, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the relay, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V765000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the driver's side air bag not to deploy as intended. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A driver's side air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering column control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ram 5500 provides exceptional capability for heavy commercial applications, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Particular attention should be paid to verifying transmission health—specifically checking for any history of internal snap ring issues—as well as inspecting the steering linkage and wheel hardware for premature wear or stress.
