### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ram 5500 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly within its core powertrain and chassis components, which are designed for heavy-duty service. The 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine and the Aisin AS69RC transmission are robust and generally free from significant widespread issues. Suspension systems are also typically durable. Minor electrical gremlins, such as intermittent accessory power, have been noted but are not pervasive. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well under commercial use. Overall, the Ram 5500 is a dependable choice for commercial fleets and demanding work applications, with most potential issues being isolated rather than systemic.
