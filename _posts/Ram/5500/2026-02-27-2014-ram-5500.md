This analysis focuses on documented technical failures beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the various engine and transmission configurations available.

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Ram 5500 demonstrates solid reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain components. The 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine and its associated transmissions (both automatic and manual) are known for their durability and longevity when properly maintained. While electrical systems can occasionally present minor, intermittent issues, these are typically manageable and not indicative of systemic failures. Suspension, exterior, and interior components generally hold up well under typical heavy-duty use. Overall, the 2014 Ram 5500 is a dependable choice for demanding work applications, with few widespread technical concerns reported.
