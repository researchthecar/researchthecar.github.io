### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Ram 5500 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 6.7L Cummins diesel engine and its associated transmissions are known for their durability under heavy use. Beyond routine maintenance, the most commonly reported technical issues tend to be electrical in nature, such as intermittent faults in wiring harnesses or eventual alternator failure. Interior components like the HVAC blower motor can also be a point of concern. Overall, the Ram 5500 is a dependable workhorse, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
