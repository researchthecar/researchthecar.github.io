### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ram 5500 demonstrates a high level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine is a well-regarded unit known for its durability and longevity when properly maintained. Both the AISIN AS69RC automatic and G56 manual transmissions are robust and suited for the demanding nature of heavy-duty work. Issues within the suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems are not widely reported, suggesting good build quality and component selection for this commercial vehicle. Overall, the 2019 Ram 5500 is a dependable choice for commercial applications, with minimal widespread reliability concerns.
