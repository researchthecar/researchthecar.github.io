# 2016 Ram 5500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used heavy-duty commercial truck like the 2016 Ram 5500, evaluating past owner complaints and mechanical reliability is essential for fleet longevity. While commercial chassis cabs are built for heavy workloads and severe-duty applications, they are not immune to isolated mechanical glitches and critical wheel or drivetrain concerns reported to safety authorities.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel

The 6.7-liter inline-six Cummins turbo diesel engine delivers high torque outputs tailored for heavy towing and commercial upfits. It is widely praised for commercial durability, though complex emissions equipment requires attentive maintenance.

**Unintended Deceleration and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lose power and decelerate while driving at moderate speeds, accompanied by an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic scan for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect the fuel delivery and electronic control systems.

## Transmission

### Aisin 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with the high-output diesel engine in heavy commercial setups, the Aisin 6-speed automatic transmission is engineered for heavy payloads and demanding commercial duty cycles.

**Drivetrain Vibration and Mechanical Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice abnormal noises, sudden vibrations, or a loud bang from underneath the vehicle, potentially resulting in fluid leaks and complete loss of propulsion.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transmission and transfer case assembly for structural damage or fluid loss; professional drivetrain rebuild or replacement may be required.

## Suspension

### Wheel Studs and Lug Nuts

The wheel assembly and hub components on dual-rear-wheel commercial trucks endure immense loads, requiring careful inspection of lug hardware and fasteners.

**Wheel Separation and Swollen Lug Nuts**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may exhibit violent shaking or vibrations, and in severe cases, wheel studs can shear off, leading to dual rear or single wheels detaching from the axle while in motion.
- **Solution:** Inspect all wheel studs and lug nuts for wear, cracking, or thread damage, and ensure proper torque specifications are strictly maintained.

## Steering

### Power Steering Booster Hose

Hydraulic steering and brake assist components manage heavy commercial loads, though high-pressure hose liners can occasionally degrade under severe operational stress.

**Booster Hose Liner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The interior liner of the high-pressure booster hose can break apart and jam into the brake booster pressure port, resulting in a sudden loss of both power steering and power assist braking.
- **Solution:** Replace the high-pressure power steering and brake booster hose assembly and flush the fluid system.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V398000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect information in the Service and Owner's Manuals may cause the flanged lug nuts to be over-tightened, which could result in a broken wheel stud and possible wheel separation. Wheel separation can cause a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the torque specification in the owner's manual and other published service documents. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V824000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Chrysler (FCA US LLC) is recalling various Dodge, Chrysler, and RAM vehicles equipped with Kidde Plastic-Handle or Push Button 'Pindicator' Fire Extinguishers. These extinguishers may become clogged, preventing the extinguisher from discharging as expected or requiring excessive force to activate the extinguisher.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, inspect the BTSI, and, as necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V562000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The leaking water pump can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the water pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V495000: Power Train:Transfer Case (4-Wheel Drive)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The transfer case may have been manufactured with a misshapen main output shaft, creating voids that may cause a shaft fracture. If the main output shaft fractures, the vehicle may lose motive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the transfer case, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V592000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, the Engine Control Module (ECM) may short circuit, causing the engine to stall without warning. An engine that stalls without warning increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ECM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ram 5500 is a heavy-duty commercial workhorse capable of managing demanding towing and payload requirements. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the chassis for critical safety-related concerns, particularly regarding wheel fastener integrity and potential drivetrain or steering hydraulic failures. Verifying past service history and ensuring all relevant safety campaigns have been completed is strongly recommended.
