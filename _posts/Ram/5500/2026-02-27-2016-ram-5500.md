### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ram 5500 demonstrates a strong reliability record, particularly concerning its core powertrain components like the 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine and its available transmissions (AISIN AS69RC and G56 manual). These heavy-duty systems are engineered for durability and typically perform well under demanding conditions. Minor electrical gremlins, such as intermittent warning lights, are the most commonly cited non-wear-and-tear issues, generally requiring straightforward diagnostic and repair procedures. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free of widespread reliability concerns. Overall, the 2016 Ram 5500 is a dependable choice for commercial use, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
