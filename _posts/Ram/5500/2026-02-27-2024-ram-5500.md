### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Ram 5500 demonstrates strong initial reliability, particularly in its core powertrain and chassis components. The 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine and its associated transmissions (AISIN AS69RC Automatic and G56 6-Speed Manual) are well-regarded for durability in heavy-duty applications and have not shown widespread issues. Suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems are also performing as expected for a vehicle of this class. As with any heavy-duty truck, consistent maintenance and adherence to operational limits are key to long-term reliability. Early data suggests the Ram 5500 is a dependable choice for demanding commercial use.
