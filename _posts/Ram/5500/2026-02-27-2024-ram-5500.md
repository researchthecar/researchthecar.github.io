# 2024 Ram 5500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Ram 5500 is a heavy-duty chassis cab designed for demanding commercial applications, featuring robust powertrain options built for heavy towing and hauling. Because it is a recent model year, owner-reported complaint data is sparse, but prospective buyers should still evaluate standard commercial wear items and powertrain performance during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six turbo diesel produces high torque outputs tailored for heavy-duty commercial towing. It is a proven heavy-duty commercial platform known for long-term durability when properly maintained.

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4-liter HEMI gasoline V8 offers strong horsepower and reliable performance for commercial upfits requiring gasoline power. It provides a responsive alternative to diesel for fleets prioritizing simpler maintenance.

## Transmission

### Aisin 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic

The Aisin six-speed automatic transmission is paired with the Cummins diesel to handle extreme towing capacities. It is engineered specifically for commercial duty cycles and heavy loads.

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic

The ZF-sourced eight-speed automatic transmission is paired with the 6.4L HEMI V8. It delivers smooth shifting and optimizes power delivery across diverse driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V799000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the high beams to activate when the turn signal is used or activate the turn signal when the high beams are used. In addition, the turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the steering column control module (SCCM) software, and inspect and replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=5500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Ram 5500 remains a capable choice for commercial buyers requiring heavy towing and specialized upfits. With limited complaint history available for this model year, buyers should ensure routine inspections of the powertrain and commercial-grade components before purchase.
