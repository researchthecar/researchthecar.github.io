# 2024 Ram 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Ram 1500 continues to be a top contender in the full-size pickup market, offering a refined ride, upscale interior, and capable powertrain choices ranging from standard V6 power to robust V8 and high-output supercharged options. As a relatively recent model year, owner reports are sparse, reflecting a vehicle that is generally still under factory warranty. Prospective buyers should review any remaining warranty coverage and inspect the vehicle thoroughly during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6 with eTorque

This 3.6-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces approximately 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, assisted by a mild-hybrid eTorque system for smooth stop-start capability and low-end responsiveness. It serves as the standard entry-level powerplant, balancing everyday usability with respectable fuel efficiency.

### 5.7L HEMI V8 with eTorque

The legendary 5.7-liter HEMI V8 generates 395 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, also paired with the eTorque mild-hybrid system. It offers strong towing capacity and traditional V8 performance, though it has been gradually phased out in favor of newer inline-six options.

### 3.0L Hurricane Twin-Turbo Inline-6

Introduced as a high-performance and efficient alternative, this 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six delivers robust output, typically rated around 420 horsepower in standard output or up to 540 horsepower in high-output tune. It provides modern turbocharged responsiveness and strong hauling capabilities.

## Transmission

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This widely used 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across the Ram 1500 lineup, delivering crisp, seamless shifts and helping optimize both fuel economy and power delivery. It is generally regarded as a reliable and responsive unit in modern Stellantis trucks.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V653000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Driving with a disabled electronic stability control system can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ABS control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V729000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective devices, and Associated Equipment." Turn signals that do not function properly may fail to indicate the driver's intention to change vehicle direction.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V298000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's side air bag may not be connected properly, which can cause the air bag not to deploy as intended. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A driver's side air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V327000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Contamination in the central vision processing module or improper programming may cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the central vision processing module software or replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Ram 1500 stands out as a comfortable, capable, and technologically advanced choice in the used full-size truck market. Because most examples on the secondary market are still relatively new and potentially covered by remaining factory warranties, major mechanical complaints remain minimal. Buyers should prioritize vehicle history checks, verify remaining warranty transferability, and test all electronic features before finalizing a purchase.
