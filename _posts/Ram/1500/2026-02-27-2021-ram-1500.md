# 2021 Ram 1500

### Overview
The 2021 Ram 1500 is a highly capable and popular full-size pickup truck known for its robust build and comfortable ride. Reliability data for this model year generally indicates a strong performance, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where potential issues have been noted. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and common wear-and-tear items. We will examine the performance of its various engine and transmission options, as well as other key systems such as suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components.

**Air Suspension Leaks**

- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ram 1500 demonstrates good overall reliability across its powertrain options. The Pentastar V6, HEMI V8, and EcoDiesel V6 engines, along with the 8-speed automatic transmission, are generally robust with no widespread critical failures reported. The primary areas of concern are the optional Active-Level Four-Corner Air Suspension, which can develop leaks, and the Uconnect infotainment system, which may experience occasional glitches requiring software updates or resets. Premature battery failure and tailgate latch issues are also noted but appear to be less common. For owners opting for the air suspension, regular checks for leaks are advisable. Overall, the 2021 Ram 1500 is a dependable truck, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
