# 2017 Ram 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Ram 1500, buyers will find a comfortable and capable half-ton truck that remains a popular choice on the secondhand market. Known for its smooth ride and strong engine choices, it offers great utility for daily driving and towing. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for common electrical, steering, and anti-lock brake system concerns reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L EcoDiesel V6

A turbocharged diesel V6 engine producing approximately 240 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque, offering excellent fuel economy and towing torque, though requiring diligent maintenance.

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

A reliable gasoline V6 engine making 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, paired with smooth automatic shifting for daily commuting efficiency.

### 5.7L HEMI V8

A powerful legendary V8 engine putting out 395 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust towing capacity alongside familiar HEMI performance.

- **Symptoms:** Exhaust gas recirculation components fail, potentially causing operational issues or leaks.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace failed EGR cooler components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

A responsive and widely used multi-speed automatic transmission that optimizes power delivery and fuel efficiency across all engine options.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

Optional four-corner air suspension provides adjustable ride height and smooth leveling but can develop leaks over time.

**Air Suspension Pressure Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Rear air suspension suddenly drops to the lowest setting or loses pressure during cold weather conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect air lines, compressors, and airbags for leaks, and replace defective suspension components.

## Brakes

### Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) Module

Braking system components generally offer solid stopping power, but electronic module failures require careful attention.

**ABS Module Failure and Code C2200**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of ABS, traction control, and electronic stability control warning lights, extended stopping distances, and disabled cruise control.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning ABS control module or affected hardware components.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering System

The power assist configuration can experience intermittent electronic faults affecting steering effort.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering functionality accompanied by a 'Service Power Steering' warning message or seized steering feel.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic power steering rack and replace faulty steering gear components or wiring harnesses.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V475000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** FCA US will notify owners, and dealers will update the powertrain control module software to maintain vehicle propulsion by reading the camshaft position signal in the event that the crankshaft position signal is lost, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V347000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Tailgate:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate opens while the vehicle is being driven, cargo may fall out the vehicle and create a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the tailgate latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V486000: Structure:Body:Tailgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate latch releases and the tailgate opens while driving, cargo may fall out, creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V632000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain:Inflator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A separated inflator end cap or inflator rupture can allow compressed gas to escape, resulting in the end cap or other components being propelled into the vehicle, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace as necessary, the SABIC assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V757000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Combustion in the intake manifold can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the EGR cooler, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, inspect the BTSI, and, as necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V434000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 301, "Fuel System Integrity." In the event of a rollover crash, leaking fuel in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel tanks, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ram 1500 is a compelling choice for used truck buyers seeking comfort, utility, and diverse powertrain options. While it delivers strong performance, shoppers should carefully check for potential issues with the electronic power steering, ABS module warning lights, and optional air suspension system. A pre-purchase inspection focusing on these electronics and mechanical components will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
