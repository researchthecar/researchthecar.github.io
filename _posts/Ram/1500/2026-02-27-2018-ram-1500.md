# 2018 Ram 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used full-size truck, understanding common owner complaints can help you spot potential trouble spots before making a purchase. The Ram 1500 offers strong capability and a comfortable ride, but buyers should review specific mechanical and electrical areas—such as steering assistance, fuel delivery, and infotainment durability—to ensure long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Pentastar

The 3.6L V6 Pentastar engine produces around 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced mix of efficiency and everyday capability for light-duty tasks. It is generally regarded as a durable option when properly maintained.

### 5.7L V8 HEMI

The 5.7L V8 HEMI delivers roughly 395 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, providing robust towing power and strong acceleration. While popular for its performance, buyers should monitor for routine maintenance needs typical of larger displacement V8s.

### 3.0L V6 EcoDiesel

The 3.0L V6 EcoDiesel generates about 240 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing low-end pulling power and fuel economy. Buyers should thoroughly inspect diesel variants for proper maintenance records and previous powertrain repairs.

**Motive Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The truck may experience sudden power loss or enter a limp mode during acceleration or highway driving, accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes, inspect internal engine or fuel management components, and repair or replace failing parts as necessary.

### Fuel System

Delivers fuel from the tank to the engine under proper pressure to ensure smooth operation across all driving conditions.

**Fuel Pump and Tank Filling Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty fueling the vehicle due to the fuel tank rollover valve malfunctioning or fuel pump delivery failures causing engine stalls.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump module or fuel tank assembly as required.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with available engines to optimize fuel economy and power delivery. It generally exhibits dependable performance when subjected to routine fluid changes.

## Brakes

### Anti-Lock Brake System Module

Controls hydraulic pressure and wheel speed sensors to maintain stability and prevent lockups during emergency braking.

**ABS Control Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated traction control and ABS warning lights, brake lockup during hard stops, or unresponsive anti-lock functionality.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty ABS control module or hydraulic control unit.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering Rack

Manages wheel articulation via an electronic assist system to ensure responsive handling at various speeds.

**Steering Rack and Power Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Stiffness in the steering wheel, intermittent or complete loss of electronic power steering assist, and difficulty controlling the vehicle around turns.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic steering assembly and replace the power steering rack or associated control modules if failure is confirmed.

## Electrical

### Uconnect Infotainment and Display

Powers cabin electronics, navigation, audio controls, and the central display interface.

**Screen Delamination and Rebooting**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen displays bubbling or delamination, freezes, reboots independently, or causes erratic backup camera and hands-free calling behavior.
- **Solution:** Replace the touch screen display unit or update/replace the infotainment head unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V475000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** FCA US will notify owners, and dealers will update the powertrain control module software to maintain vehicle propulsion by reading the camshaft position signal in the event that the crankshaft position signal is lost, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V347000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Tailgate:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the tailgate opens while the vehicle is being driven, cargo may fall out the vehicle and create a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will repair the tailgate latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V398000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." A loss of image in the rearview camera while backing up can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rearview mirror, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V280000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the incorrect park lock rod is installed, the transmission may not shift into 'PARK' and keep the vehicle from moving, increasing the risk of unintended vehicle movement and the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will install the correct park lock rod, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V632000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain:Inflator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A separated inflator end cap or inflator rupture can allow compressed gas to escape, resulting in the end cap or other components being propelled into the vehicle, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace as necessary, the SABIC assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V324000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fractured rear driveshaft could detach from the vehicle and become a road hazard, or it may result in a loss of drive power, both of which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** FCA US will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V757000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Combustion in the intake manifold can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the EGR cooler, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When evaluating a used model, conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection can save you from unexpected repair bills. Pay close attention to steering response, verify that the infotainment system operates without screen delamination, and check for any history of fuel delivery or ABS warning lights. Choosing a well-maintained vehicle with documented service history offers the best path to reliable ownership.
