# 2022 Ram 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ram 1500 delivers a smooth ride, a high-end interior, and strong towing capabilities, making it a popular choice in the full-size pickup market. However, used car buyers should approach with caution due to notable owner complaints regarding intermittent engine stalling, electrical glitches, and steering assist failures. While many owners enjoy trouble-free operation, verifying maintenance history and checking for unresolved powertrain or electronic concerns is essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 eTorque

The 3.6L Pentastar V6 with eTorque mild-hybrid technology produces 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, offering solid efficiency and smooth power delivery for daily driving.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may intermittently stall while driving or when pressing the accelerator, accompanied by loss of motive power and illumination of warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing by a qualified technician to check the 48V eTorque system, fuel delivery, and software calibrations.

### 5.7L V8 HEMI

The legendary 5.7L HEMI V8 pushes out 395 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, providing robust towing capacity and authoritative acceleration for demanding hauling needs.

### 3.0L V6 EcoDiesel

The 3.0L EcoDiesel V6 delivers 260 horsepower and an impressive 480 lb-ft of torque, maximizing low-end pulling power and highway fuel economy.

### 6.2L Supercharged V8 HEMI

Exclusive to high-performance trims, the supercharged 6.2L HEMI V8 unleashes 702 horsepower for extreme off-road and street performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The TorqueFlite 8-speed automatic transmission pairs seamlessly with available engines to deliver crisp, timely gear changes and optimized fuel economy across all driving conditions.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Braking performance is generally strong, though complex electronic integration can occasionally trigger stability and hydraulic warning lights.

**Electronic Stability and Brake Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Electronic stability control warning lights illuminate, accompanied by reduced braking effectiveness or perceived brake failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines, electronic stability control modules, and wheel speed sensors for faults.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering Rack

Steering components generally provide responsive handling, but premature electronic steering rack failures have been reported by some owners.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power steering assist may fail abruptly while driving, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Replace the electronic power steering rack and pinion assembly.
- **Cost:** $4,000

## Electrical

### Uconnect Infotainment System

The electrical architecture supports advanced cabin tech, though software instability and screen glitches can occasionally disrupt functionality.

**Infotainment Screen Freezing and Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display may completely black out, freeze, or enter unexpected reboot cycles during operation, disabling the backup camera.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates or replace the infotainment control module if hardware failure is detected.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V871000: Visibility:Defroster/Defogger/Hvac System:Windshield:Electrical Heating Element](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 103, "Windshield Defrosting and Defogging Systems." An inoperable defrosting and defogging system can reduce visibility for drivers in certain conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V407000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V766000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt with pretensioner failure may not properly restrain an occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front seat belt retractors. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V767000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP and inspect and replace additional fuel system components, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V904000: Structure:Body:Tailgate:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The tailgate may not latch properly due to misaligned tailgate strikers, which could result in the tailgate opening while driving. An unintended tailgate opening while driving may result in a loss of unsecured cargo, possibly creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the tailgate striker alignment to the box latch and adjust, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V059000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display while in reverse decreases the driver's visibility of the rear view, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the TRSCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V263000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP and replace additional fuel system components, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V264000: Air Bags:Sensor:Side Impact](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The B-pillar impact sensors may be missing, which can result in delayed air bag deployment or non-deployment. Air bags that do not deploy as intended increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, install the B-pillar impact sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ram 1500 is a highly capable and comfortable full-size truck, but prospective buyers should inspect it carefully. Pay close attention to potential engine stalling issues, electronic steering assist reliability, and infotainment stability during a pre-purchase test drive. Ensuring that all relevant software updates have been applied can help mitigate some of the most commonly reported electronic and powertrain glitches.
