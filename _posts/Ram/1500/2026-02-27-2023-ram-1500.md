# 2023 Ram 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ram 1500 continues to offer a refined ride, upscale interior options, and robust capability for truck buyers. When shopping for a used model, understanding the specific engine and transmission configurations available can help ensure you select the right powertrain for your towing and daily driving needs.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6 with eTorque

This 3.6-liter naturally aspirated V6 produces 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, aided by a mild-hybrid eTorque system for smooth stop-start operation. It provides balanced efficiency and everyday drivability for light-duty tasks.

### 5.7L HEMI V8 with eTorque

The legendary 5.7-liter HEMI V8 generates 395 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, also featuring the eTorque mild-hybrid system. It offers strong towing performance and robust acceleration across various trims.

### 3.0L EcoDiesel V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged diesel V6 delivers 260 horsepower and a stout 480 lb-ft of torque. It is favored by buyers seeking high torque for towing combined with excellent highway fuel economy.

### 6.2L Supercharged HEMI V8 (TRX)

Exclusive to the high-performance TRX trim, this supercharged 6.2-liter V8 pumps out an immense 702 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque. It is built for high-speed off-roading and aggressive acceleration at the expense of fuel economy.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

A responsive 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across all powertrain options in the Ram 1500 lineup. It delivers smooth, well-timed shifts to optimize both performance and fuel efficiency.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V059000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display while in reverse decreases the driver's visibility of the rear view, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the TRSCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V263000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP and replace additional fuel system components, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V654000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V799000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the high beams to activate when the turn signal is used or activate the turn signal when the high beams are used. In addition, the turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the steering column control module (SCCM) software, and inspect and replace the module, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power or a loss of park function increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the valve body and control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V653000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Driving with a disabled electronic stability control system can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ABS control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V729000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=1500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The turn signal self-canceling feature may not function properly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective devices, and Associated Equipment." Turn signals that do not function properly may fail to indicate the driver's intention to change vehicle direction.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
