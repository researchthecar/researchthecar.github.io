**Introduction:**

The 2012 Ram 1500 is a full-size pickup truck offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. It has a spacious cabin, a powerful engine, and a sturdy suspension. However, it also has a few common problems that can affect its reliability and performance.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**
* **Intake manifold failure:** The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 15V188000, 16V020000
* **Excessive oil consumption:** Some engines may consume excessive oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings or overhaul the engine.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**
* **Water pump failure:** The water pump can fail prematurely, leading to engine overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the water pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic (4x2)**
* **Shifting problems:** The transmission may experience rough shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic (4x4)**
* **Transfer case failure:** The transfer case can fail, causing loss of power to the wheels.
    * Solution: Replace the transfer case.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear leaf spring failure:** The rear leaf springs can break, leading to a sagging rear end and unstable handling.
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or charging system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, preventing the battery from charging and causing electrical system problems.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Bedliner peeling:** The bedliner may peel or bubble, exposing the metal underneath to corrosion.
    * Solution: Replace the bedliner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC system failure:** The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system may fail, causing discomfort for occupants.
    * Solution: Repair or replace HVAC components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power window failure:** The power windows may fail to operate or become trapped in the open or closed position.
    * Solution: Replace the window motors or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Ram 1500 has several common issues that can affect its reliability and performance. Engine problems, such as intake manifold failure and excessive oil consumption, are particularly concerning. The transmission and suspension can also experience problems, leading to costly repairs. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and part involved.
