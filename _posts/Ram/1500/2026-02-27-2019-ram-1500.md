# 2019 Ram 1500

### Overview
The 2019 Ram 1500, a significant redesign from the previous generation, introduced a new platform and updated powertrains. Generally, this model year has demonstrated good reliability, particularly with its updated V8 engines and the new 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners have reported satisfaction with its robust build and comfortable ride. However, as with any new generation, some minor issues have surfaced, primarily related to the advanced technology features and initial production quirks. Addressing these early can ensure long-term dependability for this popular full-size pickup truck.

### Air Suspension (Optional)
**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag, suspension warning lights illuminated, loss of adjustable ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slowly deflating suspension, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair or replacement of leaking air springs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 per spring


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Uconnect Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500 (depending on repair)


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after sitting for a short period, warning lights appearing.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw, repair or replacement of faulty module or component causing drain.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ram 1500 is a strong contender in the full-size truck segment, offering a blend of capability and comfort. Its core mechanical components, including the Pentastar V6 and HEMI V8 engines paired with the 8-speed automatic transmission, have proven to be reliable. The primary areas where owners have encountered issues are with the optional air suspension system, some electrical gremlins within the Uconnect infotainment system, and occasional battery drain. While these issues can be frustrating and costly to repair, they do not appear to be systemic across the entire model range. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are recommended for optimal long-term ownership.
