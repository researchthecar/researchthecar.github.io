# 2015 Ram 700

### Overview
The 2015 Ram 700, a compact pickup truck primarily sold in Latin America, is known for its utilitarian design and affordability. Reliability data for this model year indicates a generally robust mechanical platform, particularly concerning its powertrain. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners. ## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional failure of dashboard lights, infotainment system, or power windows.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty wiring harness connections or ground points. Replacement of affected modules if necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Rusting**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion appearing on lower body panels, wheel arches, and tailgate, especially in areas with harsh weather or road salt.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and repair, repainting affected areas. Application of protective coatings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+


## Interior

### Interior Trim and Upholstery
**Wear and Tear on Upholstery**
- **Symptoms:** Premature wear or tearing of seat fabric or vinyl, particularly on driver's seat bolsters.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of upholstery. Use of seat covers for protection.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Ram 700 generally presents a reliable profile, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission showing few significant reported issues. The 1.6L E.torQ engine and its associated manual and automatic transmissions are considered robust for this model. Areas that have seen some owner-reported concerns include the electrical system, with intermittent power issues being the most notable, and the exterior, where rust can be a concern in certain climates. The interior upholstery may also show signs of wear over time. Overall, the Ram 700 is a dependable workhorse, but owners should be mindful of potential electrical gremlins and take preventative measures against rust.
