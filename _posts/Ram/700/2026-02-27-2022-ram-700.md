### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ram 700 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability for its segment, particularly with its core powertrain components. The 1.3L Firefly engine is generally robust, and the manual transmission option is largely trouble-free. The CVT, while offering convenience, shows some potential for minor issues like hesitation, which are often resolvable with maintenance or software updates. Electrical components, especially the infotainment system, can occasionally exhibit minor glitches. Overall, the Ram 700 is a practical and dependable choice for basic transportation and light-duty work, with most reported issues being minor and manageable.
