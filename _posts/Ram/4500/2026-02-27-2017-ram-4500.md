# 2017 Ram 4500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ram 4500 is a heavy-duty chassis cab truck designed for demanding commercial applications, typically powered by a robust Cummins turbo-diesel engine paired with a heavy-duty automatic transmission. When shopping for a used commercial vehicle of this caliber, buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain health, suspension stability, and service records. While NHTSA owner reports highlight a few distinct mechanical and operational complaints, a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains the best way to verify condition.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Inline-6 Turbo Diesel

The 6.7L Cummins inline-6 turbo diesel engine is known for producing substantial torque, making it a popular choice for heavy towing and commercial duty. It offers strong durability under heavy workloads when properly maintained, though buyers should verify regular fluid and filter changes.

## Transmission

### Aisin 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic

The Aisin 6-speed heavy-duty automatic transmission is engineered to handle high torque output in commercial towing applications. Proper fluid maintenance is vital to ensure long-term reliability in fleet or heavy-use environments.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may unexpectedly switch into neutral while driving at various speeds, causing the vehicle to decelerate and illuminating the service engine indicator.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician is recommended to check for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect internal transmission controls or software calibration.

## Steering

### Front Drivetrain and Steering Linkage

Drivetrain components on heavy-duty chassis cabs endure significant stress, requiring routine inspection of shafts, joints, and front-end geometry.

**Front End Vibration During Towing**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an unexpected vibration originating from the front of the vehicle while towing at highway speeds, often without any warning lights illuminating.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components, front suspension joints, wheel bearings, and driveline balance to identify the source of the vibration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V398000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect information in the Service and Owner's Manuals may cause the flanged lug nuts to be over-tightened, which could result in a broken wheel stud and possible wheel separation. Wheel separation can cause a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the torque specification in the owner's manual and other published service documents. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, inspect the BTSI, and, as necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V100000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Pushing the brake pedal for prolonged periods when the vehicle is running and in PARK may cause the Brake Transmission Shift Interlock (BTSI) pin to stick in the open position. Being able to shift the transmission without pushing the brake pedal and/or without a key in the ignition can increase the risk of an unintended vehicle rollaway that may result in.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners and dealers will update the Body Control Module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V824000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Chrysler (FCA US LLC) is recalling various Dodge, Chrysler, and RAM vehicles equipped with Kidde Plastic-Handle or Push Button 'Pindicator' Fire Extinguishers. These extinguishers may become clogged, preventing the extinguisher from discharging as expected or requiring excessive force to activate the extinguisher.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V562000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The leaking water pump can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the water pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V896000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A loss of anti-lock brakes, electronic stability control, or traction control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake HCU, or replace the HCU motor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ram 4500 is a capable commercial workhorse, but its heavy-duty nature means prior use and maintenance history vary wildly. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive the vehicle—paying close attention to transmission shift behavior and front-end stability under load—and obtain a professional pre-purchase inspection before finalizing a purchase.
