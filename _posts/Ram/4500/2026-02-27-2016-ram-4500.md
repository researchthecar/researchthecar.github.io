### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Random warning lights, non-functional accessories, or communication errors.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illumination, dimming headlights, or failure to start.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ram 4500 generally exhibits strong reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain components like the 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine and its available transmissions. These are built for heavy-duty applications and typically perform well under demanding conditions. The most frequently cited technical issues tend to be in the electrical system, with some reports of Body Control Module (BCM) glitches and occasional alternator failures. Exterior and interior components are largely free of widespread problems, though minor issues like headlight condensation can occur. Overall, the 2016 Ram 4500 is a dependable workhorse, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
