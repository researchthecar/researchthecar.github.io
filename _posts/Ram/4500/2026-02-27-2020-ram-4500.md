# 2020 Ram 4500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Ram 4500 is a heavy-duty commercial chassis cab built for demanding towing and hauling applications. While owner reports remain relatively sparse, prospective buyers should review available complaint data regarding fuel system performance and accessory electronics when evaluating used models.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7-liter Cummins Turbo Diesel inline-six delivers high torque outputs tailored for heavy commercial hauling. It is a proven heavy-duty powerplant, though buyers should verify fuel system health.

**CP4 Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The truck may stall unexpectedly while driving under load, blocking traffic, and fail to restart.
- **Solution:** Requires inspection and replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump and associated fuel system components by a qualified technician.

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 gasoline engine produces strong horsepower for medium-duty commercial applications. It offers a capable alternative to diesel power for fleets prioritizing lower upfront costs.

## Transmission

### Aisin 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The heavy-duty Aisin 6-speed automatic is paired with the high-output diesel engine to manage massive towing capacities. It is engineered for commercial durability under strenuous working conditions.

### ZF 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is utilized alongside the gasoline engine configuration. It provides smooth shifting and optimized gear selection for everyday utility and commercial tasks.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wiper Motor

Accessory and body electronics generally function well, though isolated switch or motor issues can impact driver visibility during inclement weather.

**Wiper Failure During Rain**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wipers may fail to activate or respond when needed during heavy rainstorms, severely reducing visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect the wiper motor, switch, and wiring harness, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V439000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The engine calibration software may not warn about low oil volume. Low oil can damage the engine, causing an oil leak or a punctured engine block, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine calibration software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V035000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Reduced visibility increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the wiper nuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V023000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A worn rear brake caliper flexible line may cause brake fluid leakage which may result in a partial or complete loss of the rear brakes, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Excellance will notify owners, and dealers will install a kit to remedy any damage and to prevent any future damage, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V880000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP, update the Powertrain Control Module (PCM) software, and inspect and replace additional fuel system components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V398000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect information in the Service and Owner's Manuals may cause the flanged lug nuts to be over-tightened, which could result in a broken wheel stud and possible wheel separation. Wheel separation can cause a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the torque specification in the owner's manual and other published service documents. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V511000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** This can result in engine damage and connecting rod failure, which could potentially puncture the engine block. A damaged engine block may leak oil, which can contact hot engine or exhaust components, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will flash the engine calibration software to enhance the engine warm up protection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V016000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A worn rear brake caliper flexible line may cause brake fluid leakage, which may result in a partial or complete loss of the rear brakes, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Custom Truck And Body Works will notify owners, and dealers will repair the affected suspension systems, and rear brake caliper flexible line, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20E031000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these parts fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 105, "Hydraulic and Electric Brake Systems." A worn rear brake caliper flexible line may cause brake fluid leakage which may result in a partial or complete loss of the rear brakes, increasing the risk of a crash.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Ram 4500 is a robust work truck designed for heavy commercial service, but used buyers should carefully inspect the fuel system and electrical accessories. Ensure all open safety campaigns are addressed by a certified dealer before finalizing a purchase.
