# 2021 Ram 4500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ram 4500 is a heavy-duty chassis cab truck built for demanding commercial and towing applications. Buyers considering a used model should evaluate its commercial-grade capabilities alongside its powertrain options, keeping in mind that heavy-duty trucks often experience rigorous work environments.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I4

The 6.7-liter Cummins turbo diesel engine provides high torque output designed for heavy hauling and towing duties. This inline-six diesel is widely recognized for its robust commercial durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission is engineered to handle high gross combined weight ratings in commercial applications. It offers stable gear selection under heavy loads, though proper fluid maintenance is critical for longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V798000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay may cause a vehicle fire with the ignition on or off. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if needed, replace the Solid State Intake Heater Grid Relay ("Relay"); if FCA US Recall ID Y08 (NHTSA Recall ID 21V-163) is open, then insulating material will be applied to the Relay after inspection. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V398000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect information in the Service and Owner's Manuals may cause the flanged lug nuts to be over-tightened, which could result in a broken wheel stud and possible wheel separation. Wheel separation can cause a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the torque specification in the owner's manual and other published service documents. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V163000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short may occur in the Solid State Intake Heater Grid Relay, which could result in a vehicle fire. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if needed, replace the Solid State Intake Heater Grid Relay ("Relay"); if FCA US Recall ID Y08 (NHTSA Recall ID 21V-163) is open, then insulating material will be applied to the Relay after inspection. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V060000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay can result in an engine compartment fire with the ignition on or off, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the relay, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V765000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the driver's side air bag not to deploy as intended. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A driver's side air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering column control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2021 Ram 4500, buyers should thoroughly inspect the service history, particularly if the vehicle was subjected to heavy commercial towing or payload demands. A pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic specializing in heavy-duty commercial trucks is strongly recommended.
