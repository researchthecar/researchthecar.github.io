### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ram 4500 demonstrates solid reliability, especially with its robust 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel engine and AISIN AS69RC transmission, which are workhorses in the commercial vehicle segment. Most reported issues are minor and related to electrical components like the infotainment system or trailer harness connections, often fixable with software updates or simple repairs. Exterior and interior components are generally durable, with occasional reports of condensation in light housings or HVAC blower motor issues. Overall, the Ram 4500 is a dependable choice for heavy-duty tasks, with potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic powertrain weaknesses.
