# 2022 Ram 4500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Ram 4500, buyers are looking at a heavy-duty commercial-grade chassis cab designed primarily for demanding work applications. Because these trucks often see heavy towing and fleet use, understanding their powertrain configuration and potential mechanical hiccups is essential for making a sound investment. While owner-reported complaints are relatively limited, issues affecting heavier drivetrain components warrant careful inspection before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 6.7L Cummins Turbo Diesel I6

The 6.7-liter Cummins inline-six turbodiesel is available in standard and high-output versions, generating substantial torque designed for heavy towing and payload demands. This powerhouse is paired with heavy-duty commercial transmissions and is generally valued for its rugged durability in commercial applications.

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4-liter HEMI gasoline V8 produces robust horsepower and strong low-end torque suitable for vocational and towing duty cycles. It serves as a capable alternative to the diesel engine for buyers who prefer traditional gasoline fueling in commercial settings.

## Transmission

### Aisin 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The heavy-duty Aisin 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with the high-output diesel engine to handle extreme towing capacities. While built for commercial durability, owners have occasionally reported significant internal gear failures.

**Loss of Forward and Reverse Gears**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle suddenly fails to engage reverse or forward drive gears, sometimes accompanied by loud mechanical clunks when attempting to shift.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis by a commercial truck specialist, which may involve rebuilding or replacing internal transmission components or the entire unit.

**Total Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden catastrophic transmission breakdowns requiring complete replacement of the unit while under operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition and maintenance history closely, and replace the transmission assembly if internal mechanical failure occurs.

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed TorqueFlite automatic transmission provides smooth shifting and optimized gear ratios for gasoline-powered models. It offers responsive power delivery across various driving and load conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V798000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay may cause a vehicle fire with the ignition on or off. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if needed, replace the Solid State Intake Heater Grid Relay ("Relay"); if FCA US Recall ID Y08 (NHTSA Recall ID 21V-163) is open, then insulating material will be applied to the Relay after inspection. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V642000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An internal loss of communication can result in a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ECM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V060000: Electrical System:Wiring:Relays/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short in the relay can result in an engine compartment fire with the ignition on or off, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the relay, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V765000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ram&model=4500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may cause the driver's side air bag not to deploy as intended. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A driver's side air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering column control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ram 4500 is a powerful commercial-grade platform, but used-car buyers must thoroughly inspect the powertrain before purchasing. Given potential reports of heavy-duty transmission failure, verifying service records, checking for smooth engagement in all gears, and confirming whether remaining powertrain warranties apply will help mitigate unexpected repair expenses.
