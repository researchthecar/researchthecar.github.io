# 2021 Ram 4000

### Overview
The 2021 Ram 4000 is a heavy-duty truck designed for commercial and vocational applications, emphasizing robustness and capability. Reliability data for this model year indicates a generally solid performance, particularly in its core powertrain components. While specific issues are not widespread, some owners have reported isolated incidents that, while not indicative of a systemic problem, are worth noting for potential buyers. The truck's focus on durability means that major component failures are uncommon, and most reported concerns are manageable. Maintenance and proper usage are key to maximizing the longevity of this workhorse vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ram 4000 demonstrates strong reliability, especially concerning its powertrain. The 6.7L Cummins diesel engine and the Aisin automatic transmission are known for their durability and are generally free from significant issues. Suspension components are robust, designed to handle heavy loads. Minor concerns are primarily isolated electrical glitches, such as intermittent warning lights, and occasional infotainment system unresponsiveness, both of which are typically resolved with software updates or simple diagnostics. Overall, the Ram 4000 is a dependable choice for demanding applications, with a low incidence of major mechanical failures.
