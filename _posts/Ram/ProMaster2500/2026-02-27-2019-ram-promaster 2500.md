# 2019 Ram ProMaster 2500

### Overview
The 2019 Ram ProMaster 2500 is a popular choice for commercial use, valued for its cargo capacity and maneuverability. Reliability data for this model year indicates a generally robust performance, particularly concerning its powertrain. While most owners report satisfactory experiences, some common areas for attention have emerged, primarily related to specific powertrain components and certain electrical systems. Addressing these potential issues proactively can ensure the longevity and operational efficiency of the ProMaster 2500, making it a dependable workhorse for various business needs. The focus of this analysis is on documented technical failures rather than routine maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Ram ProMaster 2500 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core powertrain components (3.6L Pentastar V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The suspension system is also generally robust. Areas that have seen some reports of concern include electrical gremlins potentially linked to the Body Control Module and infotainment system glitches. Exterior issues like a sticking sliding door are not uncommon for vans of this type but are typically addressable with maintenance. Interior components like the HVAC blower motor can occasionally fail. For commercial users, proactive checks of electrical systems and regular lubrication of door mechanisms are recommended to maintain optimal performance.
