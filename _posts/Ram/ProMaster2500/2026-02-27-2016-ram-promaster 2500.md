## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent battery drain leading to starting issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty module or accessory causing drain, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing or unresponsiveness of the Uconnect system.
- **Solution:** Software update or reset of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ram ProMaster 2500 demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core powertrain components (engine and transmission) showing minimal reported issues. The most common concerns tend to be in the electrical system, specifically battery drain and occasional infotainment glitches, which are generally manageable with software updates or component diagnosis. For commercial users, addressing these potential electrical and door issues proactively can ensure continued dependable service from the ProMaster 2500.
