### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Ram ProMaster 2500 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are well-regarded for their durability. Minor electrical issues, such as infotainment glitches and occasional battery drain, are the most frequently reported concerns, generally manageable with software updates or targeted repairs. The sliding door mechanism is another area where minor adjustments or component replacements might be needed over time. Overall, the ProMaster 2500 is a dependable work vehicle with a low incidence of major mechanical failures, making it a sound investment for commercial operations.
