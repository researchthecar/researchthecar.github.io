The 2020 Ram ProMaster 2500 is a popular choice for commercial use, valued for its cargo capacity and maneuverability. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern and their associated repair costs to aid in long-term ownership planning.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Ram ProMaster 2500 demonstrates good overall reliability for its class. The powertrain (3.6L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission) is generally trouble-free. Suspension components are also robust. The most common areas for potential concern lie within the electrical system, specifically with the body control module and infotainment unit, and some minor issues with exterior door latches and interior HVAC blower motors. These issues are typically repairable with moderate costs. With proactive maintenance and attention to these specific components, the ProMaster 2500 should provide dependable service for commercial applications.
