# 2021 Ram ProMaster 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ram ProMaster 2500 is a popular full-size commercial van designed for cargo hauling and fleet utility. While it offers immense interior space and front-wheel-drive maneuverability, used-car buyers should review available owner feedback regarding powertrain response and auxiliary equipment. Being a heavily utilized commercial vehicle, inspecting prior maintenance history and mechanical wear is essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Pentastar

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine produces approximately 280 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing standard gasoline power for daily commercial duties. It is a widely used powerplant known for generally dependable operation when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

This 9-speed automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels, offering optimized gear ratios for city delivery cycles and highway cruising. Buyers should thoroughly test-drive the transmission across various inclines to verify smooth shifting and engagement.

**Powertrain Responsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may momentarily fail to respond to accelerator input while driving uphill at low speeds, causing it to roll backward.
- **Solution:** Have the powertrain control module and transmission software inspected by a qualified technician for updates or diagnostic trouble codes.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system is vital for heavy cargo vans, and any hydraulic anomalies require immediate professional inspection.

**Brake Pedal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may suddenly travel all the way to the floor without returning, resulting in a complete loss of braking capability.
- **Solution:** Inspect the master cylinder, brake lines, and hydraulic fluid for leaks or failure, and replace faulty components immediately.

## Electrical

### Side Mirrors

Exterior mirrors on commercial vans endure frequent adjustments and wind resistance, making electrical integrity important.

**Unintended Mirror Folding**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger-side power mirror may unexpectedly fold inward while driving at highway speeds without driver input.
- **Solution:** Inspect the mirror assembly, wiring harness, and control switch for internal electrical faults or mechanical binding.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Ram ProMaster 2500 remains a capable workhorse for commercial applications, though used buyers should carefully evaluate brake system operation and transmission responsiveness during a test drive. NHTSA owner complaints highlight isolated concerns regarding low-speed hill behavior and unexpected mirror movement. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help uncover potential wear before finalizing a purchase.
