**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ram ProMaster 2500 demonstrates solid mechanical reliability, with its core powertrain (3.6L Pentastar V6 and 6-speed automatic transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The chassis and exterior components are also generally robust. However, potential owners should be aware of some reported electrical concerns, primarily related to the Body Control Module and infotainment system software glitches, which can manifest as intermittent problems or system unresponsiveness. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and require professional diagnosis and repair. Overall, the ProMaster 2500 is a dependable work van, with most issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
