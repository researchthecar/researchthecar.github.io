# 2023 Ram ProMaster 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Ram ProMaster 2500, buyers will find a capable full-size commercial van designed primarily for cargo utility and conversion flexibility. While owner-reported complaints are sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history records and inspect critical systems during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 24V VVT Engine

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine produces approximately 276 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque, delivering power to the front wheels. This powerplant is widely used across the manufacturer's lineup and generally offers reliable daily operation.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission designed to optimize highway efficiency and city power delivery. Routine fluid maintenance is recommended to ensure smooth long-term operation under heavy commercial loads.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Parking Brake

Owner reports indicate occasional anomalies with brake pedal firmness and electronic parking brake behavior.

**Random Parking Brake Engagement and Soft Pedal**
- **Symptoms:** The parking brake may randomly engage at low speeds, and the brake pedal can occasionally require pumping to achieve a solid feel.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the electronic parking brake actuator, brake lines, and master cylinder for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting System

electrical issues such as intermittent false dashboard warnings can occasionally affect auxiliary lighting systems.

**Intermittent False Lighting Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The instrument cluster may intermittently display false warnings indicating that turn signal lights or daytime running lights are out.
- **Solution:** Inspect body control module software, bulb connections, and wiring harnesses for intermittent faults.
