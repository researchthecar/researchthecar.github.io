**Introduction:**

The 2021 Tesla Model Y is a fully electric midsize SUV known for its sleek design, advanced technology, and high efficiency. It is available with a range of battery sizes and performance options.

**Electric Motor**

No Issues Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

**Noisy Struts**
Description/Symptoms: Unusual noises, such as clunks or rattles, from the front suspension when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replacing the affected strut assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V594000

**Electrical**

**Battery**

**Battery Pack Overheating**
Description/Symptoms: The battery pack may overheat during fast charging or under high load, leading to reduced performance or even a fire.
Solution: Software update to optimize battery management system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: None
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V510000

**High-Voltage Wiring Harness**

**Wiring Harness Damage**
Description/Symptoms: The wiring harness may become damaged, causing electrical issues such as flickering lights, power loss, or battery drain.
Solution: Inspecting and repairing or replacing the affected wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V514000

**Exterior**

**Glass Roof**

**Cracked or Shattered Glass**
Description/Symptoms: The panoramic glass roof may crack or shatter spontaneously, posing a safety hazard.
Solution: Replacing the entire glass roof panel.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V532000

**Interior**

**Center Console**

**Misaligned or Loose Center Console**
Description/Symptoms: The center console may be misaligned or loose, rattling or making noises.
Solution: Adjusting or replacing the console assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Tesla Model Y offers impressive features and performance, it has experienced several notable issues that require attention. The suspension issues can affect ride quality and safety, while the electrical problems can potentially lead to serious consequences. The exterior and interior issues, though less severe, can impact the overall ownership experience. It is recommended that owners stay informed about recalls and seek professional advice if they encounter any of these issues.
