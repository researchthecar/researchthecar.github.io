**Introduction to the 2023 Tesla Model 3**

The 2023 Tesla Model 3 is an electric vehicle that comes in two configurations: Long Range and Performance. The Long Range model has a single electric motor that produces 353 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque, while the Performance model has dual electric motors that produce a combined 513 horsepower and 471 lb-ft of torque. Both models have a 100-kWh battery that provides an estimated range of 353 miles for the Long Range model and 315 miles for the Performance model.

The Tesla Model 3 is known for its impressive performance, advanced technology, and sleek design. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

## **Electric Motor**

**No Issues Found**

## **Suspension**

**Squeaky Front Suspension**
Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises coming from the front suspension when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replacing the upper control arm bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension Noise**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises coming from the rear suspension when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replacing the rear lower control arms.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain when the vehicle is parked or not in use.
Solution: Varies depending on the cause of the drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies
Recalls: None Found

**12-Volt Battery Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Failure of the 12-volt battery, which can cause the vehicle to lose power and become inoperable.
Solution: Replacing the 12-volt battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

**Panel Gaps**
Description/Symptoms: Uneven spaces between the body panels, especially around the hood, trunk, and doors.
Solution: Adjusting or replacing the affected body panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies
Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

**Center Console Creaking**
Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling noises coming from the center console when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
Solution: Tightening or replacing the center console attachment points.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies
Recalls: None Found

**Door Panel Buzzing**
Description/Symptoms: Buzzing or rattling noises coming from the door panels when driving at certain speeds or frequencies.
Solution: Tightening or replacing the door panel clips.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies
Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

Overall, the 2023 Tesla Model 3 is a well-regarded electric vehicle with impressive performance and technology. However, it is not without its issues. The suspension and electrical systems seem to be the most common areas of concern, with battery drain, 12-volt battery failure, and suspension noises being the most reported problems. The severity of the issues varies, but the cost to fix can be significant in some cases.
