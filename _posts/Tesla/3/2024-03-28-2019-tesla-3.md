**Introduction:**

The 2019 Tesla Model 3 is an electric vehicle with a single electric motor and single-speed transmission. The electric motor produces 351 horsepower and 348 lb-ft of torque. The Model 3 is known for its impressive acceleration, high-tech features, and spacious interior. However, some owners have reported encountering various issues with the vehicle.

**Suspension**

**Squeaky Suspension**
* **Description/Symptoms:** A squeaking or groaning noise coming from the suspension, particularly over bumps or uneven surfaces.
* **Solution:** Replace the worn-out suspension components, such as the sway bar links or bushings.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
* **Description/Symptoms:** The battery discharges excessively, even when the vehicle is parked and turned off.
* **Solution:** Replace the faulty battery cells or update the software.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $5,000+

**Display Malfunction**
* **Description/Symptoms:** The touchscreen display becomes unresponsive, glitchy, or displays error messages.
* **Solution:** Update the software or replace the display assembly.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

**Panel Gap Issues**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Inconsistent gaps between body panels, resulting in uneven or misaligned seams.
* **Solution:** Realign or replace the affected body panels.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
**Recalls:** NHTSA Recall Number: 19V414

**Headlight Condensation**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies, causing fogging or water accumulation.
* **Solution:** Replace the headlights or seal them using specialized sealant.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $600+

**Interior**

**Noisy Sunroof**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Rattling or squeaking noises coming from the sunroof when open or closed.
* **Solution:** Adjust the sunroof alignment or replace the sunroof components as needed.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**Loose Door Handles**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Door handles become loose or difficult to operate, posing a safety hazard.
* **Solution:** Replace the faulty door handle assembly.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $250+


**Summary:**

The majority of issues reported with the 2019 Tesla Model 3 relate to electrical and exterior components. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor annoyances and others requiring significant repairs. The estimated cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars to over $5,000, depending on the issue and the extent of the damage. Overall, the Model 3 has received positive feedback for its performance and features, but owners should be aware of the potential issues associated with the vehicle.
