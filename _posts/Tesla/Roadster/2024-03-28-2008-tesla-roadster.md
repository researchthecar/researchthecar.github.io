**Introduction:**

The 2008 Tesla Roadster was the first production electric sports car. It was powered by a 248-hp electric motor and a 53 kWh lithium-ion battery pack. The Roadster was praised for its acceleration, handling, and innovative technology. However, it was also criticized for its short range and high price.

**Electric Motor**

* **Issue:** Battery degradation
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Reduced range, decreased performance
  * **Solution:** Battery replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $10,000 - $15,000+
  * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V899000

**Transmission**

* **Issue:** Gearbox failure
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
  * **Solution:** Gearbox replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Issue:** Front suspension failure
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Clunking noises, poor handling
  * **Solution:** Suspension replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Issue:** Charging port failure
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty charging, intermittent charging
  * **Solution:** Charging port replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
  * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Issue:** Inverter failure
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of power, limp mode
  * **Solution:** Inverter replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $7,000+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Issue:** Door handle failure
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty opening doors
  * **Solution:** Door handle replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Issue:** Dashboard electronics failure
  * **Description/Symptoms:** Malfunctioning gauges, touchscreen issues
  * **Solution:** Dashboard electronics replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2008 Tesla Roadster is a pioneering electric sports car with a reputation for performance and innovation. However, it has also been plagued by a number of issues, particularly related to its electric motor, gearbox, and electrical system. These issues can be expensive to fix, ranging from $500 to over $15,000. While the Roadster is a groundbreaking vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of its potential reliability concerns.
