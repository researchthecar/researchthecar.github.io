**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**
* **Squeaky Control Arms:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A squeaking or creaking sound coming from the front suspension when driving over bumps or rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace the control arms with updated ones.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Worn Ball Joints:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or worn ball joints can cause clunking or rattling noises when driving over rough surfaces.
    * Solution: Replace the ball joints with new ones.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension**
* **Leaking Shock Absorbers:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking shock absorbers can cause a loss of suspension damping and result in a rougher ride.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking shock absorbers with new ones.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per shock absorber
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery**
* **Battery Degradation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery capacity can degrade over time, reducing the vehicle's range and performance.
    * Solution: Replace the battery pack with a new one.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $20,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Charging Port Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the charging port to charge the battery properly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty charging port with a new one.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A creaking or rattling sound coming from the dashboard when driving over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard components that are causing the noise.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Climate Control:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the climate control system to regulate temperature or airflow properly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty climate control module or other components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles that are difficult to open or close due to broken components or wiring.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty door handles or wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Tesla Roadster is a highly desirable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The suspension can develop squeaks and rattles, the battery can degrade over time, and the interior can have its share of creaks and faults. The severity of these issues can vary depending on the mileage and maintenance history of individual vehicles. The cost to fix these issues can range from minor to significant, with battery replacement being the most expensive.
