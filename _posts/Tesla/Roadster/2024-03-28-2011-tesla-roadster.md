**Introduction:**
The 2011 Tesla Roadster is a fully electric sports car with a single electric motor and a single-speed transmission. It has a 288-horsepower electric motor and 295 lb-ft of torque. It's praised for its acceleration, handling, and technological features, but it's also criticized for its limited range and high price.
