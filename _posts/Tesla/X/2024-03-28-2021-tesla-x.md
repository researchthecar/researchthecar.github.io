## 2021 Tesla Model X

The 2021 Tesla Model X is a fully electric SUV that is available in two trims: Long Range and Plaid. The Long Range trim has a dual-motor all-wheel drive system that produces 670 horsepower and 659 lb-ft of torque. The Plaid trim has a tri-motor all-wheel drive system that produces 1,020 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque. Both trims have a top speed of 163 mph and a 0-60 mph acceleration time of 3.8 seconds (Long Range) and 2.5 seconds (Plaid).

People like the Tesla Model X for its spacious interior, futuristic design, and high-tech features. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's reliability.

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor fails, causing the vehicle to sag or ride low.
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Suspension Control Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension control module fails, causing the vehicle to sag or ride low.
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Suspension Linkages Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension linkages corrode, causing the vehicle to sag or ride low.
    * Solution: Replace the corroded air suspension linkages.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Pack Overheating**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery pack overheats, causing the vehicle to lose power or shut down.
    * Solution: Replace the battery pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20,000 - $30,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V795000
* **Charging System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The charging system malfunctions, causing the vehicle to not charge or charge slowly.
    * Solution: Replace the charging system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical harness corrodes, causing electrical issues throughout the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the corroded electrical harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handle malfunctions, causing the door to be difficult or impossible to open.
    * Solution: Replace the door handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof leaks water into the interior of the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or adjust the sunroof.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Screen Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The center console screen malfunctions, causing it to go blank or display distorted images.
    * Solution: Replace the center console screen.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V990000
