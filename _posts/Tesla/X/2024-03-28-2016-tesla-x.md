**Electrical**

* **12V Battery Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The 12V battery, which powers the car's accessories, may fail prematurely, resulting in a loss of power to the infotainment system, lights, and other electrical components.
    * Solution: Replace the 12V battery with a new one.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Handle Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handles on the Model X may malfunction, making it difficult to open the doors.
    * Solution: Replace the door handles with new ones.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 20V-071

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Intermittent Operation**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may open and close intermittently, or may not open or close at all.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof motor or mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Tesla Model X is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has a few issues that owners should be aware of. The most common issues are electrical problems, such as a failing 12V battery or malfunctioning door handles. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are not generally severe enough to cause major problems.
