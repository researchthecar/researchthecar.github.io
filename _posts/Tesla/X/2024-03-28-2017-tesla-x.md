**Introduction**

The 2017 Tesla Model X is an all-electric SUV that offers impressive performance and a luxurious interior. It is powered by a dual-motor electric powertrain that produces 691 horsepower and 713 lb-ft of torque. The Model X is available in three different versions: the standard Model X, the Model X P90D, and the Model X P100D.

**Electric Motor**

**No Issues Found**

**Exterior**

**Rear Liftgate**

* Issue: Liftgate malfunctions, such as failing to open or close properly
* Description/Symptoms: The liftgate may not respond to commands, open or close slowly, or make unusual noises.
* Solution: Replace or repair the liftgate motor or control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Windshield Wipers**

* Issue: Wipers malfunction, such as skipping, chattering, or not working at all
* Description/Symptoms: Poor visibility during rain or snowfall due to ineffective wiper performance.
* Solution: Replace or repair the wiper motor, wiper blades, or control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Center Console**

* Issue: Touchscreen malfunctions, such as freezing, lagging, or displaying errors
* Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete failure of the touchscreen, affecting navigation, entertainment, and other features.
* Solution: Update software, replace touchscreen, or repair control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Sunroof**

* Issue: Sunroof malfunctions, such as failing to open or close properly
* Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may not respond to commands, open or close slowly, or make unusual noises.
* Solution: Replace or repair the sunroof motor or control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery**

* Issue: Battery malfunctions, such as reduced range or charging issues
* Description/Symptoms: Decreased driving range, difficulty charging, or battery warning messages.
* Solution: Diagnose and repair battery cells or replace the battery.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Charging Equipment**

* Issue: Home charging malfunctions, such as connectivity issues or charging errors
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty connecting to home charging stations or slow charging rates.
* Solution: Inspect and repair charging cable, wall connector, or other equipment.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension**

* Issue: Air suspension malfunctions, such as uneven ride height or suspension failure
* Description/Symptoms: Vehicle leaning to one side, poor handling, or suspension warning messages.
* Solution: Replace or repair air compressor, air bags, or control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Tesla Model X is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some common issues. The most notable issues are malfunctions with the rear liftgate, touchscreen, and electric system (including the battery). These issues can be costly to repair, with some estimates exceeding $10,000. However, most of the recalls and issues have been addressed by Tesla through software updates or repair campaigns.
