# 2022 Tesla S

### Overview
The 2022 Tesla Model S represents a significant leap in electric vehicle technology, offering impressive performance, range, and a minimalist interior. As a high-performance EV, its reliability is generally strong, with most reported issues stemming from the complex electronic systems and software rather than traditional mechanical components. Owners often praise the driving experience and advanced features. However, like many cutting-edge vehicles, occasional software glitches and minor build quality concerns have been noted. The focus of reliability for the Model S shifts from internal combustion engine wear to the longevity and performance of its battery, electric motors, and integrated digital systems. Overall, the 2022 Model S is considered a reliable option within the premium EV segment, with a strong emphasis on continuous software updates to address and improve functionality.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks/Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one side, uneven ride height, error messages on the display.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, navigation issues, audio problems, system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential hardware replacement if persistent.
- **Cost:** $0 - $1500


### 12V Battery Failure
**Premature 12V Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may not start, electrical systems malfunctioning, error messages.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Paint
**Panel Gaps and Fitment**
- **Symptoms:** Uneven gaps between body panels, inconsistent paint application.
- **Solution:** Adjustment of panels by a Tesla service center or authorized body shop.
- **Cost:** $0 - $500


### Door Handles
**Flush Door Handle Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Door handles not presenting or retracting properly, difficulty opening doors.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the door handle mechanism.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Interior

### Build Quality
**Interior Trim Rattles/Loose Parts**
- **Symptoms:** Audible rattles from dashboard, door panels, or center console; loose trim pieces.
- **Solution:** Tightening of fasteners, application of sound-deadening material, or replacement of trim components.
- **Cost:** $100 - $600


### HVAC System
**HVAC Odors/Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Musty or unpleasant odors from vents, inconsistent heating or cooling.
- **Solution:** Cabin air filter replacement, AC system cleaning, or repair of HVAC components.
- **Cost:** $150 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Tesla Model S demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core electric powertrain components. Major mechanical failures are rare, aligning with the general trend of EVs. The most common reported issues tend to be software-related, affecting the advanced infotainment system, and occasional minor build quality concerns in the interior or exterior fitment. The adaptive air suspension is a potential area for concern, with some reports of leaks or malfunctions. However, Tesla's over-the-air updates often address software bugs, and the company's service network is continuously evolving. For potential buyers, it's advisable to be aware of these specific areas, but overall, the Model S offers a reliable and technologically advanced driving experience.
