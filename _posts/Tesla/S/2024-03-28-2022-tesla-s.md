**2022 Tesla Model S**

The 2022 Tesla Model S is an electric luxury sedan that offers impressive performance and a spacious interior. It comes with two electric motor options: the Long Range and the Plaid. The Long Range produces 670 horsepower and 520 lb-ft of torque, while the Plaid produces an astonishing 1,020 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque. Both models have a single-speed transmission.

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to lower unexpectedly. This can lead to loss of stability and control.
    * Solution: Replace the failed air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Coil Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: The coil springs in the suspension system may break, causing the vehicle to ride lower on one or more corners. This can affect handling and stability.
    * Solution: Replace the broken coil springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Pack Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery pack may experience a faulty charge, reduced range, or complete failure.
    * Solution: Replace the battery pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $15,000 - $25,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 23V078
* **Inverter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The inverter, which converts DC power from the battery to AC power for the motors, may fail. This can cause the vehicle to lose power or operate erratically.
    * Solution: Replace the failed inverter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Actuator Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door lock actuators may fail, causing the doors to lock or unlock unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Replace the failed door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panel Gaps**
    * Description/Symptoms: The body panels may not be aligned properly, resulting in uneven gaps.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the affected panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Trunk Lid Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: The underside of the trunk lid may develop rust.
    * Solution: Replace the rusted trunk lid or apply anti-rust treatments.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Screen Freezing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The touchscreen infotainment system may freeze or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Restart the infotainment system or update the software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: None
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headliner Sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: The headliner fabric may sag or become detached from the roof.
    * Solution: Reattach or replace the headliner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Tesla Model S is a high-performance electric sedan with a spacious interior and impressive range. However, it has been reported to experience some issues, including suspension failures, electrical malfunctions, body panel misalignment, and interior issues. While these issues can be costly to fix, the severity varies depending on the specific problem encountered.
