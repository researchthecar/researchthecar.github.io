**Introduction**

The 2016 Tesla Model S is an electric vehicle that delivers impressive performance and a luxurious interior. It's powered by a single electric motor and offers a single-speed transmission. The Model S has a spacious cabin, a large touchscreen infotainment system, and a sleek exterior.

Some of the things people like about the Tesla Model S include its powerful acceleration, long driving range, and advanced technology. However, some people have experienced issues with the vehicle, including problems with the electrical system, suspension, and exterior.

**Electrical**

**Battery**

**Battery Degradation**
Description/Symptoms: Premature loss of battery capacity, reduced driving range, or difficulty charging.
Solution: Replacement of battery cells or entire battery pack.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $20,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Charging System**

**Charging Port Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty connecting the charging cable, slow charging, or intermittent charging.
Solution: Replacement of the charging port assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Air Conditioning**

**Compressor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Inoperative air conditioning, lack of cold air, or loud noises from the compressor.
Solution: Replacement of the air conditioning compressor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Body Panels**

**Panel Gaps**
Description/Symptoms: Uneven or excessive gaps between body panels, affecting aerodynamics and aesthetics.
Solution: Adjustment or replacement of body panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Door Handles**

**Handle Sticking**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing doors due to sticking handles.
Solution: Repair or replacement of door handles.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Center Console**

**Screen Flickering**
Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or persistent flickering of the touchscreen infotainment display.
Solution: Replacement of the touchscreen assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Sunroof**

**Leaking Roof**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof during heavy rain or car washes.
Solution: Replacement or repair of sunroof assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Tesla Model S is a well-regarded electric vehicle with impressive performance and technology. However, some owners have encountered issues related to the electrical system, suspension, and exterior. Electrical problems can be particularly costly to repair, while issues with the suspension and exterior may affect the vehicle's ride quality and appearance. It's important to consider these potential issues when making a decision about whether or not to purchase this vehicle.
