**Introduction**

The 2006 Saturn Vue is a compact SUV available with various engine and transmission options. The standard 2.2-liter Ecotec inline-four engine generates 143 horsepower and 152 lb-ft of torque. Other engine choices include a 3.5-liter V6 with 250 horsepower and 242 lb-ft of torque and a 3.6-liter V6 with 252 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options consist of a five-speed manual, a four-speed automatic, and a six-speed automatic. The Saturn Vue offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and decent fuel economy. However, it has received criticism for its lackluster performance and outdated cabin design.

**Engine**

**2.2-liter Ecotec Inline-Four**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idling, poor performance
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**3.5-liter V6**

**Head Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust, rough idling
Solution: Replace head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**3.6-liter V6**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

**Shifting Difficulty**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
Solution: Replace or repair clutch or transmission components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic**

**Slipping Gears**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping out of gear, delayed or erratic shifts
Solution: Replace or repair transmission components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Front Struts**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, poor handling, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace front struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Shock Absorbers**
Description/Symptoms: Bouncing rear end, unstable handling
Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery**
Description/Symptoms: Electrical system failure, difficulty starting, dimming headlights
Solution: Replace battery
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Alternator**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system failure, battery not charging
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handles**
Description/Symptoms: Door handles breaking off or becoming loose
Solution: Replace door handles
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from dashboard
Solution: Add padding or insulation to dashboard
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Power Window Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Power windows not functioning
Solution: Replace window motor or switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2006 Saturn Vue has several potential issues, with the most common being engine and transmission problems. Intake manifold gasket failure and head gasket failure are known issues with the 2.2-liter Ecotec inline-four and 3.5-liter V6 engines, respectively. Transmission problems include shifting difficulty with the five-speed manual and slipping gears with the four-speed automatic. The suspension system may also experience issues with the front struts and rear shock absorbers. Electrical problems can occur with the battery and alternator, while exterior issues are limited to broken door handles. The interior has reported issues with dashboard rattle and power window failure. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and others potentially causing significant inconvenience or expense.
