**Introduction:**

The 2002 Saturn Vue is a compact SUV known for its stylish design and versatility. It is powered by a 2.2L Ecotec engine, producing 143 horsepower and 152 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The Vue is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and fuel efficiency. However, it has various issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**2.2L Ecotec**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Leaking gasket causing coolant leaks, overheating, and reduced engine performance. Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Head Gasket Failure:** Premature failure causing coolant and oil leaks, overheating, and reduced power. Solution: Replace head gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Loose chain causing rattling noises, engine misfires, and reduced power. Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:** Faulty sensor interrupting ignition timing, causing engine stalling and no-start conditions. Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Difficulty Shifting Gears:** Worn or damaged synchros causing difficulty engaging gears. Solution: Replace synchros and related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping and/or Hard Shifting:** Worn clutch packs and/or faulty solenoids causing stuttering, slipping, or rough shifting. Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:** Worn rear springs or shocks causing excessive sagging and reduced ride height. Solution: Replace rear springs or shocks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Front Strut Mount Failure:** Worn strut mounts causing rattling noises, reduced ride comfort, and premature tire wear. Solution: Replace strut mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Premature alternator failure causing battery discharge and electrical system malfunctions. Solution: Replace alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Starter Motor Failure:** Worn starter drive or faulty solenoid causing difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution: Replace starter motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Moisture:** Leaking headlight housings causing moisture build-up and reduced light output. Solution: Replace headlight housings or reseal existing ones. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Wiper Motor Failure:** Worn wiper motor causing intermittent or no wiper operation. Solution: Replace wiper motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:** Premature cracking of dashboard due to excessive heat or UV exposure. Solution: Replace dashboard or repair with aftermarket cover. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Faulty blend door actuator or HVAC module causing irregular or inoperable climate control. Solution: Replace blend door actuator or HVAC module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary:**

The 2002 Saturn Vue has a mix of common and severe issues to look out for. The most costly issues are related to the engine, such as intake manifold gasket failure and head gasket failure. Transmission issues can also be expensive to repair, especially rebuilding or replacing the transmission. Electrical and suspension issues, while less severe, can still be frustrating and costly. Potential buyers should thoroughly inspect potential vehicles and budget for potential repairs.
