**Introduction**
The Saturn Sky is a roadster produced from 2006 to 2010. It was available with a 2.4L Ecotec LE5 I4 engine producing 177 horsepower and 166 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission. The Red Line performance model featured a 2.0L Ecotec LNF I4 engine producing 260 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. Known for its sporty handling and stylish design, the Sky has some common issues that potential owners should be aware of.

**Engine**
**2.4L Ecotec LE5 I4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Camshaft Actuator Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, rough idle, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace camshaft actuator solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: 09V445000

**2.0L Ecotec LNF I4 (Red Line)**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, reduced engine performance, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**5-speed Manual**

* **Shift Linkage Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace shift linkage bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Automatic (2.4L Ecotec)**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-speed Manual (2.0L Ecotec)**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, spongy clutch pedal
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Strut Mount Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noises from suspension, reduced ride comfort
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Trailing Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or clunking noise from rear suspension, excessive tire wear
    * Solution: Replace trailing arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies frequently, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Identify and repair parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on source of drain
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimmed headlights, electrical system issues
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Convertible Top Weatherstripping Deterioration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks, excessive wind noise
    * Solution: Replace weatherstripping
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Sun Visor Hinge Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sun visor falls down when lifted
    * Solution: Replace sun visor hinge
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The Saturn Sky offers an engaging driving experience, but it has some common issues that potential owners should consider. Engine and transmission issues can be costly to repair, while suspension and electrical problems can affect ride comfort and safety. Some interior issues, such as the sun visor hinge failure, are minor but can be annoying. Overall, the Sky's issues are not overly severe and can be manageable with proper maintenance and repairs.
