**Introduction:**

The 2009 Saturn Sky is a sporty roadster that quickly gained popularity for its sleek design and aggressive performance. Offered with a range of engine and transmission options, the Sky appealed to enthusiasts seeking an engaging driving experience. The 2.4L Ecotec engine with 177 horsepower and 166 lb-ft of torque was paired with either a 5-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission, while the more powerful 2.0L turbocharged Ecotec engine boasted 260 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 6-speed manual transmission. Despite its appeal, the Saturn Sky was plagued by several issues that dampened its reputation.

**Engine**

**2.4L Ecotec**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil and coolant leaks, rough idling, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Intake manifold gasket replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Ecotec Turbo**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, exhaust smoke, loud noises from turbocharger
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Oil Pressure Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: False readings, oil pressure warning light illumination
    * Solution: Oil pressure sensor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping or grabbing clutch
    * Solution: Clutch replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed engagement, rough shifting, loss of power
    * Solution: Transmission repairs or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission stuck in gear, erratic shifting
    * Solution: Solenoid replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncing or harsh ride, poor handling
    * Solution: Shock absorber replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per shock
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Control Arm Bushings Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises when steering or driving, imprecise handling
    * Solution: Control arm bushing replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per bushing
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery loses charge quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Ignition Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Ignition switch replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Seal Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin during rain
    * Solution: Seal replacement or repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors fail to lock or unlock properly, power door locks inoperative
    * Solution: Door lock actuator or switch replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaks and Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises emanating from the dashboard
    * Solution: Dashboard disassembly and parts inspection
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Center Console Cup Holder Broke:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cup holder breaks or becomes loose
    * Solution: Center console cup holder replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Saturn Sky's reputation was marred by a number of issues, including mechanical problems with the turbocharged engine, transmission, and suspension. Electrical issues such as battery drain and ignition switch failure also plagued the vehicle. Exterior and interior problems were relatively minor and could often be fixed at a reasonable cost. While the Sky offered a fun and engaging driving experience, its reliability issues ultimately undermined its appeal.
