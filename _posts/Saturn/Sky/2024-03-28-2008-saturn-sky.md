**Introduction:**

The 2008 Saturn Sky is a roadster that offers a sporty and stylish driving experience. It is powered by a 2.4-liter engine that produces 177 horsepower and 166 lb-ft of torque. The Sky is available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission. While the Sky has been praised for its handling and performance, it has also faced several issues since its release.

**Engine**

**2.4-liter Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** Oil leak from the rear of the engine, causing reduced oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent oil additions or top-offs.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair underlying cause (e.g., worn piston rings, valve seals).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Failure of the timing chain tensioner, leading to timing misalignment and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and inspect timing components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $900 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: 10V423

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shifter Fork Wear:** Worn or damaged shifter forks, causing difficulty shifting or grinding noises.
    * Solution: Replace shifter fork(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:** Slipping or loss of power during acceleration, caused by worn clutches or torque converter issues.
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:** Premature wear or failure of the lower control arm bushings, leading to excessive play and alignment issues.
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Mount Weakness:** Weakness or failure of the rear shock mounts, resulting in rattling noises or alignment issues.
    * Solution: Replace shock mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Premature failure of the alternator, leading to insufficient electrical power or battery draining.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Wiring Harness Issues:** Faulty electrical connections or wiring harness problems, causing various electrical malfunctions.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace affected wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Motor Failure:** Failure of the convertible top motor, preventing the top from opening or closing.
    * Solution: Replace convertible top motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlamp Moisture:** Moisture buildup inside the headlamps, causing fogging or condensation.
    * Solution: Replace headlamp assembly or apply sealant.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:** Cracking or damage to the dashboard surface due to sun exposure or material defects.
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or cover with a dash mat.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Radio Malfunction:** Intermittent or complete failure of the radio system, requiring replacement or repairs.
    * Solution: Replace or repair radio components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2008 Saturn Sky, while offering a fun and sporty ride, has been plagued by a number of issues that can affect its reliability and enjoyment. Engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical malfunctions are some of the most prevalent concerns. Some of these issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be costly and require extensive repairs. It is important for potential buyers and owners to be aware of these potential issues and factor in the potential expenses associated with maintaining the vehicle.
