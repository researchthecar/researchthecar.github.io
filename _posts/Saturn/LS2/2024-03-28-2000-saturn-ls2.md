**Introduction**

The 2000 Saturn LS2 is a mid-size sedan that was offered with a 2.2L inline-four engine and a 3.0L V6 engine. Both engines were paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The LS2 was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordability. However, it was criticized for its lackluster performance and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.2L Inline-Four Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: 01V052000

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6 Engine**

* **Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from valve covers
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises from suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sway Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises from suspension, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead frequently
    * Solution: Identify source of electrical drain and repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Shorts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, malfunctioning electrical components
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty wires and components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior (excluding leather seat cracking or wear)**

* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning A/C or heater, inconsistent temperature control
    * Solution: Replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working, stuck in down position
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior (excluding paint chips or fading)**

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Mirror Adjustment Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Side mirrors not adjusting properly
    * Solution: Replace mirror adjustment motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2000 Saturn LS2 has a range of potential issues, with some being more severe and costly than others. Engine issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure and timing chain tensioner failure, can be particularly expensive to repair. Transmission problems, such as transmission slipping and shift solenoid failure, are also common. Other issues, such as electrical shorts and interior climate control failure, are less severe but can still be annoying and expensive to fix. Overall, the Saturn LS2 has a relatively high frequency of issues, and owners should be prepared for potential repair costs when considering this vehicle.
