**Introduction:**

The 2001 Saturn SC2 is a sporty coupe that offers a unique blend of performance and affordability. It is powered by a 2.2-liter Ecotec inline-four engine, which produces 150 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque. The SC2 comes standard with a five-speed manual transmission, while a four-speed automatic transmission is available as an option. The car handles well and has a comfortable interior, making it a good choice for drivers who want a fun and practical vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.2-liter Ecotec Inline-Four Engine**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, rough idling, overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, white smoke from exhaust, overheating
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear synchronization issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Solenoid valve failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, delayed shifts
    * Solution: Replace solenoid valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount bearing failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise from suspension, vibration while driving
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical problems
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Window switch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty operating windows
    * Solution: Replace window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2001 Saturn SC2 is a reliable and affordable vehicle with a few potential issues to be aware of. The engine and transmission problems are the most severe and can be expensive to fix. However, the other issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable price. Overall, the SC2 is a good choice for drivers who are looking for a fun and practical car.
