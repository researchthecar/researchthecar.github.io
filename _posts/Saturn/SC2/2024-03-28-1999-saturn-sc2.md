## 1999 Saturn SC2

The 1999 Saturn SC2 is a sporty coupe known for its comfortable interior and fuel-efficient engines. It was available with two engine options: a 1.9L I4 and a 2.2L I4, both paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. Despite its popularity, the SC2 has experienced various issues over the years.

### Engine

**1.9L I4**
* **Leaking valve cover gasket:** Oil leaks from the valve cover onto the engine. Replacing the gasket typically resolves the issue. **$150 - $300+**
* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Coolant leaks from the intake manifold gasket. Replacing the gasket is necessary. **$300 - $500+**
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner fails, causing a rattling noise and potential engine damage. Replacing the tensioner assembly is required. **$500 - $1,000+**
**2.2L I4**
* **No issues found**

### Transmission

**5-speed manual**
* **Grinding gears:** Difficulty shifting gears, especially when cold. Replacing the transmission fluid and adjusting the clutch cable may resolve the issue. **$100 - $250+**
* **Leaking axle seals:** Axle seals fail, causing transmission fluid to leak. Replacing the seals is necessary. **$200 - $400+**

### Suspension

* **Strut mounts worn:** Excessive noise and vibration when driving over bumps. Replacing the strut mounts typically resolves the issue. **$250 - $500+**
* **Rear suspension bushings deteriorated:** Loose or damaged rear suspension bushings cause a clunking noise and affect handling. Replacing the bushings is necessary. **$300 - $600+**

### Electrical

* **Electrical system failure:** Various electrical components may fail, including the alternator, starter, or battery. Replacing the affected component is typically necessary. **$150 - $1,000+**
* **Cruise control failure:** The cruise control system malfunctions. Replacing the cruise control module or related components may be required. **$200 - $500+**

### Exterior

* **Sunroof seal failure:** The sunroof seal deteriorates, causing water leaks. Replacing the sunroof seal is necessary. **$150 - $300+**
* **Door lock actuator failure:** The door lock actuators fail, preventing the doors from locking or unlocking. Replacing the actuators is necessary. **$200 - $400+**

### Interior

* **Plastic interior trim pieces breaking:** Various interior trim pieces, such as the dashboard or door panels, may break or become loose. Replacing the affected pieces is typically necessary. **$100 - $250+**
* **Instrument cluster failure:** The instrument cluster may malfunction, displaying inaccurate information. Replacing the instrument cluster is necessary. **$300 - $500+**

## Summary

Overall, the 1999 Saturn SC2 has experienced various issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. The severity and cost of repairs vary depending on the issue and the affected parts. Some issues, such as the intake manifold gasket failure or timing chain tensioner failure, can be costly to address. It's recommended to have regular maintenance and address issues promptly to ensure the vehicle's reliability and longevity.
