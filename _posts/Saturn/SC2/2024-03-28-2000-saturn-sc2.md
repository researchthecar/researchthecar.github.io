**Introduction:**

The 2000 Saturn SC2 was a sporty compact coupe powered by a 124 horsepower 2.2L inline-4 engine. It was available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The SC2 was praised for its responsive handling, spacious interior, and affordability, but it also had some notable issues.

**Engine**

**2.2L Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idling, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, oil leaks, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace the head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, especially at startup
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise, clunking, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace the front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Ignition Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace the ignition control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Moisture:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture build-up inside the tail lights
    * Solution: Replace the tail light assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Radio Display Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Non-functional display on the radio
    * Solution: Replace the radio display
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty operating power windows
    * Solution: Replace the window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2000 Saturn SC2 had several common issues, primarily related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The intake manifold gasket failure, head gasket failure, and timing chain noise in the engine were significant concerns, while the transmission slipping in the 4-speed automatic could result in costly repairs. Other issues, such as the suspension strut failure, electrical problems, and interior component failures, were generally less severe and less expensive to fix.
