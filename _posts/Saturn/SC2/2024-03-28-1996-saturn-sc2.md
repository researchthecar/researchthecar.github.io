The 1996 Saturn SC2 is a compact coupe powered by a 1.9L DOHC inline-4 engine paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. It has a horsepower of 124 hp and torque of 130 lb-ft. Drivers praise the SC2 for its sporty handling, affordability, and fuel efficiency, while criticizing its lack of amenities and cramped interior.

**Engine**

**1.9L DOHC Inline-4**

* **Leaking Oil Pan Gasket:** Oil leaks from the oil pan gasket, causing a burning oil smell and reduced oil levels. Replacement of the gasket is required. $200 - $400+
* **Timing Belt Failure:** The timing belt can fail prematurely, leading to catastrophic engine damage if not replaced on schedule. Regular replacement of the timing belt and associated components is crucial. $600 - $1000+
* **Coolant Leak:** The coolant system may leak, causing overheating and engine damage. The radiator, hoses, and water pump are common failure points. $200 - $800+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Wear:** The clutch disc and pressure plate can wear out, causing difficulty shifting gears or slipping. Replacement of the clutch components is necessary. $600 - $1200+
* **Input Shaft Bearing Failure:** The input shaft bearing can fail, resulting in grinding noises, difficulty shifting, and eventual transmission damage. Replacement of the bearing is required. $800 - $1500+

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter may fail, leading to loss of power and transmission damage. Replacement of the torque converter is necessary. $1000 - $2000+
* **Valve Body Malfunction:** The valve body can malfunction, causing erratic shifting, slipping, or no engagement. Repair or replacement of the valve body is required. $800 - $1500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:** The front struts can fail, causing excessive bouncing, poor handling, and tire wear. Replacement of the struts is required. $400 - $800+
* **Ball Joint Wear:** The ball joints can wear out, leading to loose steering and impaired handling. Replacement of the ball joints is necessary. $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The electrical system may experience battery drain, leaving the car unable to start. Alternator, starter, or battery issues are common causes. $200 - $600+
* **Headlight Switch Failure:** The headlight switch may fail, causing flickering or intermittent headlights. Replacement of the switch is required. $100 - $200+

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Breakage:** The door handles can break, making it difficult to enter or exit the vehicle. Replacement of the door handles is necessary. $100 - $200+
* **Taillight Lens Crazing:** The taillight lenses can develop crazing, allowing moisture to enter the housing and affecting visibility. Replacement of the taillights is required. $150 - $300+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:** The dashboard can crack and deteriorate over time, especially in hot climates. Repair or replacement of the dashboard is required. $200 - $600+
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:** The HVAC blower motor can fail, resulting in lack of air conditioning or heating. Replacement of the blower motor is required. $200 - $400+

**Summary**

The 1996 Saturn SC2 has several potential issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and suspension components. The cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the severity and location of the issue. Regular maintenance and timely repairs are crucial to ensure the reliability and longevity of the vehicle.
