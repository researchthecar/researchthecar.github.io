**Introduction:**

The 2000 Saturn LW2 is a compact sedan that was available with a 1.9-liter 124-hp engine and a 5-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission. It was praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and fuel efficiency. However, it was criticized for its lack of performance and handling.

**Engine**

* **1.9L Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, rough idle, engine overheating. Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500. Recalls: None Found
    * **EGR Valve Issues:** Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, reduced power, illuminated check engine light. Solution: Replace EGR valve. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300. Recalls: None Found
    * **Head Gasket Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust. Solution: Replace head gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000. Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, soft brake pedal. Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder. Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400. Recalls: None Found
    * **Shift Linkage Bushing Wear:** Description/Symptoms: Loose or sloppy gear shifter, difficulty shifting. Solution: Replace shift linkage bushings. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200. Recalls: None Found

* **5-Speed Automatic**
    * **Solenoid Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or not shifting properly, illuminated check engine light. Solution: Replace solenoid. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400. Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Fluid Filter Clogging:** Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, delayed engagement. Solution: Flush transmission fluid and replace filter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250. Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Sway Bar Links:** Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises from front of vehicle, poor handling. Solution: Replace sway bar links. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per link. Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Mounts:** Description/Symptoms: Thumping or rattling noises from rear of vehicle, poor ride quality. Solution: Replace shock absorber mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per mount. Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery drain, illuminated charging system light. Solution: Replace alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500. Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Motor Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Engine will not crank over, clicking noises from starter. Solution: Replace starter motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400. Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Lens Haze:** Description/Symptoms: Faded or discolored headlight lenses, reduced light output. Solution: Buff and restore headlight lenses. Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150. Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Regulator Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Windows not opening or closing properly, noise from door. Solution: Replace power window regulator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300 per window. Recalls: None Found
* **Heater Core Failure:** Description/Symptoms: No heat from heater, coolant leaks inside vehicle. Solution: Replace heater core. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700. Recalls: None Found
* **Dash Panel Crack:** Description/Symptoms: Crack in dashboard panel, often near passenger side airbag. Solution: Replace dash panel. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000. Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2000 Saturn LW2 has a few common issues that can be expensive to repair, such as head gasket failure, alternator failure, and heater core failure. However, many of the issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
