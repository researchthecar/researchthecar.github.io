**Introduction:**

The 2009 Saturn Astra is a compact hatchback that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The base 2.4-liter Ecotec engine produces 173 horsepower and 164 lb-ft of torque. A 2.0-liter turbocharged engine with 260 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque is available for performance-oriented models. Transmission choices include a 5-speed manual and a 6-speed automatic transmission. Owners appreciate the Astra's sporty handling, comfortable interior, and fuel efficiency. However, some issues have been reported with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.4-liter Ecotec Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks from the intake manifold gasket, causing overheating or performance issues.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner loosens, causing a rattling noise or engine stalling.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Engine**

* **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate sticks open, causing a loss of boost pressure and reduced performance.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger wastegate assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The camshaft position sensor malfunctions, causing the engine to run erratically or stall.
    * Solution: Replace the camshaft position sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Synchronizer Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially when the transmission is cold.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or harsh shifting, delayed shifting, or the transmission slipping into neutral.
    * Solution: Replace the shift solenoid pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping while shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noise when turning, irregular tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the lower control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, poor handling, leaking shock absorbers.
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even after a short period of time.
    * Solution: Check for parasitic electrical drains, such as faulty lights or modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting the car, or a warning light on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks become difficult to open or close, or may randomly unlock.
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers stop working or move erratically.
    * Solution: Replace the windshield wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cruise Control Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The cruise control switch fails to engage or disengage the cruise control system.
    * Solution: Replace the cruise control switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Heater Core Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sweet-smelling coolant odor inside the cabin, fogged-up windows, or a leaking heater core.
    * Solution: Replace the heater core.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Saturn Astra is a generally reliable vehicle, but certain issues have been reported, including engine and transmission problems, suspension failures, and electrical issues. Some of these issues, such as the timing chain tensioner failure or torque converter clutch failure, can be costly to repair. Overall, the severity of the issues and the associated repair costs vary depending on the specific problem and the vehicle's mileage and maintenance history.
