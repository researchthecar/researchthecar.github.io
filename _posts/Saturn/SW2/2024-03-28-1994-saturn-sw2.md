The Saturn SW2 is a sporty station wagon produced by Saturn from 1991 to 1999. It was available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* 1.9L DOHC I4 engine with a 5-speed manual transmission
* 1.9L DOHC I4 engine with a 4-speed automatic transmission
* 2.2L DOHC I4 engine with a 5-speed manual transmission
* 2.2L DOHC I4 engine with a 4-speed automatic transmission

The SW2 was praised for its handling and practicality, but it was also criticized for its lack of power and cramped interior.

**Engine**

* **1.9L DOHC I4 engine**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.2L DOHC I4 engine**
    * **Head gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed manual transmission**
    * **Synchronizer failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace synchronizers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Transmission fluid leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaks, slipping gears
        * Solution: Replace transmission fluid seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension**
    * **Strut failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noise, vibration, poor handling
        * Solution: Replace struts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension**
    * **Sway bar links failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noise, vibration, poor handling
        * Solution: Replace sway bar links
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Plastic trim pieces fading**
    * Description/Symptoms: Plastic trim pieces begin to fade and discolor over time.
    * Solution: Replace plastic trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power window failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows become inoperable
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heater or air conditioner stops working
    * Solution: Replace HVAC system components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The Saturn SW2 is a reliable and practical vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems include engine gasket failures, transmission fluid leaks, and suspension issues. These issues can be costly to repair, but they are not typically major problems. Overall, the Saturn SW2 is a good value for the money, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
