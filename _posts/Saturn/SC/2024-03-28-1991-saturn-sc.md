## Introduction

The 1991 Saturn SC is a compact sports car that was produced by Saturn Corporation. It was available with two engine options: a 1.9L SOHC inline-four and a 2.0L DOHC inline-four. Both engines were paired with a 5-speed manual transmission.

People who like the Saturn SC appreciate its sporty handling, responsive steering, and comfortable interior. However, some common issues have been reported with the vehicle over the years.

## Engine

### 1.9L SOHC Inline-Four

**Head Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Valve Guide Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, misfires, increased oil consumption
Solution: Replace intake valve guides
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

### 2.0L DOHC Inline-Four

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises from engine, loss of power
Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual

**Shifting Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
Solution: Adjust or replace shifter linkage, replace transmission fluid
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

**Front Lower Ball Joint Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from suspension, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace lower ball joints
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Shock Absorber Mounts Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Bouncy or unstable ride, excessive body roll
Solution: Replace shock absorber mounts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery discharge, electrical system malfunctions
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Ignition Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, engine stalls intermittently
Solution: Replace ignition switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

**Door Handle Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Handle breaks or becomes loose, difficulty opening doors
Solution: Replace door handle
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

## Interior

**Dashboard Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Cracks develop on dashboard surface, especially in hot climates
Solution: Replace dashboard
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Power Window Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Window switches malfunction, windows fail to operate
Solution: Replace power window motors or switches
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

## Summary

Overall, the 1991 Saturn SC is a reliable vehicle with some minor issues that can be relatively inexpensive to fix. The most common problems are related to the engine, suspension, and electrical system. However, the severity and cost of these issues are typically not major concerns for most owners.
