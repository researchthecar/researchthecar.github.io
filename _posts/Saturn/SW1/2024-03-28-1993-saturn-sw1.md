**Introduction:**

The 1993 Saturn SW1 is a compact station wagon known for its innovative design, spacious interior, and fuel efficiency. It was offered with a choice of two engines and two transmissions:

* 1.9L SOHC 4-cylinder, 100 hp and 113 lb-ft of torque
* 1.9L DOHC 4-cylinder, 124 hp and 122 lb-ft of torque

**Engine:**

**1.9L SOHC 4-cylinder**

* **Oil leaks:** From the camshaft covers, oil pan, or rear main seal. Symptoms include visible oil spots under the vehicle and a burning oil smell.
* Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400

**1.9L DOHC 4-cylinder**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Symptoms include coolant or oil leaks, rough idle, and reduced engine power.
* Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350

**Transmission:**

**5-speed Manual**

* **Shifter cable breakage:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears or the shifter becoming loose.
* Solution: Replace the shifter cable.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Suspension:**

* **Strut mount failure:** Symptoms include clunking noises when driving over bumps and reduced stability.
* Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator failure:** Symptoms include dim headlights, difficulty starting the engine, and a warning light on the dashboard.
* Solution: Replace the alternator.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $450

**Exterior:**

* **Door handle breakage:** Symptoms include difficulty opening or closing the doors.
* Solution: Replace the door handles.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard cracking:** Symptoms include visible cracks on the dashboard surface.
* Solution: Replace the dashboard or use a dash cover.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* Recalls: #97V054000

**Summary:**

The 1993 Saturn SW1 generally has minor issues that are relatively inexpensive to fix. However, the potential for engine oil leaks, intake manifold gasket failure, and alternator problems can be more costly.
