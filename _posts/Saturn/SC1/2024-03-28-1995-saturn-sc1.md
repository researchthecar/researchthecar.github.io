**Introduction:**

The 1995 Saturn SC1 is a compact coupe known for its fuel efficiency and affordability. It came with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine:**
    * 1.9L SOHC I4 (105 hp, 130 lb-ft of torque)
    * 2.2L DOHC I4 (124 hp, 145 lb-ft of torque)
* **Transmission:**
    * 5-speed manual
    * 4-speed automatic

Despite its strengths, the Saturn SC1 has been plagued by numerous issues over the years.

**Engine**

**1.9L SOHC I4**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, and rough idling
Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**2.2L DOHC I4**

**Head Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, and reduced engine power
Solution: Replace the head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

**Clutch Master Cylinder Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, clutch pedal goes to the floor
Solution: Replace the clutch master cylinder
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Delayed or no gear engagement, slipping between gears
Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noises, loose steering
Solution: Replace the bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, dashboard warning lights
Solution: Replace the alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly
Solution: Replace the door lock actuator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: #07V471000 (Front Door Lock Actuator)

**Interior**

**Dash Cluster Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working, warning lights not illuminating
Solution: Replace the dash cluster
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: #01V079000 (Instrument Panel Cluster)

**Summary:**

The 1995 Saturn SC1 is a problematic vehicle with numerous issues affecting various components. Engine problems are particularly severe and expensive to fix. Transmission issues are also common and can be costly to resolve. Suspension and electrical issues are less severe but still require attention. The interior and exterior have some problems, but these are typically less costly to fix. Overall, the Saturn SC1 is not a reliable choice and can be a costly vehicle to maintain.
