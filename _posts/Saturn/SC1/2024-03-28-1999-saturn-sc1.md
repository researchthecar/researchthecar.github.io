**Introduction**

The 1999 Saturn SC1 is a compact coupe known for its fuel efficiency and affordability. It features a 1.9-liter SOHC 4-cylinder engine mated to a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 124 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque. While praised for its smooth ride and spacious interior, the SC1 has faced criticism for its lack of power and dated technology.

**Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leak, rough idle, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Manual Transmission:**
    * **Clutch Slave Cylinder Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises, clutch pedal feels spongy
        * Solution: Replace clutch slave cylinder
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or refusing to shift gears, illuminated check engine light
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, poor handling, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, flickering dashboard lights, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors may not lock or unlock electronically, clicking noise
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows may not roll up or down, humming noise
    * Solution: Replace power window motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 1999 Saturn SC1 has a moderate reliability record. While minor issues such as electrical failures and door lock malfunctions can be relatively inexpensive to fix, more serious problems like engine or transmission failures can be costly. This vehicle is not recommended for drivers who prioritize performance or reliability in their vehicles.
