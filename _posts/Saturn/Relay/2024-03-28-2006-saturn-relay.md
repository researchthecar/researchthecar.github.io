**Introduction:**
The 2006 Saturn Relay is a midsize minivan offered with two engine options: a 3.5L V6 and a 3.9L V6. The 3.5L V6 produces 200 horsepower and 220 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.9L V6 produces 240 horsepower and 245 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Relay received praise for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordable price. However, it was criticized for its lack of features, subpar fuel economy, and low resale value.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: A leaking intake manifold gasket can cause coolant and oil leaks, engine overheating, and rough idle.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 07V412000

**3.9L V6**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Solenoid Failure**
Description/Symptoms: A malfunctioning shift solenoid can cause delayed or rough shifting, and in some cases, the transmission may not engage into gear.
Solution: Replace the shift solenoid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Lower Control Arm Bushings Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged front lower control arm bushings can cause excessive noise, vibration, and clunking sounds while driving or turning.
Solution: Replace the front lower control arm bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Electrical Wiring Harness Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Faulty wiring can cause a variety of electrical issues, including flickering lights, non-functional gauges, and intermittent starting problems.
Solution: Repair or replace the damaged wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rust on Tailgate**
Description/Symptoms: Rust can develop on the tailgate, especially around the license plate area and hinges.
Solution: Repair or replace the rusted tailgate.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Center Console Latch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: A faulty latch on the center console can cause it to open while driving, resulting in loose items falling out.
Solution: Replace the center console latch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2006 Saturn Relay has several potential issues that range from minor annoyances to more serious problems. The estimated cost to fix these issues varies, but overall, they are relatively affordable to repair. However, the severity and cost of the electrical and transmission issues should be considered when purchasing or owning this vehicle.
