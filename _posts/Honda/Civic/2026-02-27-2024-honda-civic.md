# 2024 Honda Civic Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Honda Civic continues to be a strong contender in the compact car segment, offering a balance of efficiency, modern technology, and refined driving dynamics. As a relatively new model year, the vehicle's long-term reliability picture is still emerging, though early ownership data shows standard stability across its core mechanical systems. Used car buyers looking at this model year can generally expect modern safety features and well-rounded powertrain options, but a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 158 horsepower and 138 lb-ft of torque. It provides dependable everyday performance for base trims with straightforward maintenance requirements.

### 1.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates around 180 horsepower and 177 lb-ft of torque, offering stronger acceleration and passing power. It is widely used across higher trim levels while maintaining competitive fuel economy.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

This continuously variable transmission is paired with front-wheel drive across most Civic configurations to optimize fuel efficiency. It delivers smooth power delivery under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V704000: Steering:Rack And Pinion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Civic&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A damaged tire can fail and increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the electric power steering rack, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V744000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Civic&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Difficulty steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the worm gear spring and redistribute or add grease as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V859000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Civic&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat cushion frame may not have been tightened properly, which can result in an unsecured seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 207, "Seating Systems." An unsecured driver's seat may not adequately restrain the driver during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's seat cushion frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Honda Civic remains a sensible and dependable choice for used car shoppers seeking a modern compact vehicle. Because this model year is still relatively fresh on the secondary market, keeping an eye out for any emerging service bulletins or specific component quirks during a test drive is wise. Overall, its solid engineering and efficient powertrain choices make it a low-stress daily driver.
