# 2024 Honda Civic Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Honda Civic delivers efficient performance and modern technology, making it a popular choice for commuters and small families. However, prospective used car buyers should carefully review potential owner complaints regarding steering component wear, advanced safety system operation, and occasional powertrain troubles.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Naturally Aspirated I4

This base 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 158 horsepower, offering reliable and fuel-efficient daily performance when properly maintained.

**Engine Stall and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle shakes violently while braking to a stop and unexpectedly stalls.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose the fuel delivery, ignition, and sensor systems.

**Clutch and Oil Seal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Clutch assembly failure accompanied by crankshaft oil seal leaks causing oil loss.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed clutch kit and install a new crankshaft oil seal.

### 1.5L Turbocharged I4

The available 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine pushes output to around 180 horsepower, delivering livelier acceleration while maintaining strong overall efficiency.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired primarily with mainstream trims, this continuously variable transmission prioritizes smooth operation and fuel savings, though rare premature failures have been reported.

**Transmission Breakdown**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of motive power and vehicle immobilization while driving at low mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transmission assembly and replace the unit if internal mechanical failure is confirmed.

### 6-Speed Manual

Available on select sportier trims, the 6-speed manual transmission offers an engaging driving experience for enthusiasts.

**Loss of Drive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission unexpectedly gives out while driving up grades, causing the vehicle to roll backward.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair or replace internal transmission components as necessary.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

The electronic power steering system generally provides responsive handling, but steering rack malfunctions have been a notable point of owner concern.

**Steering Rack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sticky or stiff steering feel, abnormal noises when turning, or complete steering rack binding requiring replacement.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer inspect the steering assembly and replace the steering rack if binding or failure is found.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Honda Sensing safety suites generally enhance driver awareness, though sporadic sensor calibration issues can lead to false braking events.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly and erroneously applies hard braking while using cruise control or during normal highway driving.
- **Solution:** Have the forward collision sensors and adaptive cruise control modules recalibrated or checked for faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V704000: Steering:Rack And Pinion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Civic&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A damaged tire can fail and increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the electric power steering rack, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V744000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Civic&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Difficulty steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the worm gear spring and redistribute or add grease as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V859000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Civic&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat cushion frame may not have been tightened properly, which can result in an unsecured seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 207, "Seating Systems." An unsecured driver's seat may not adequately restrain the driver during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's seat cushion frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Honda Civic remains an appealing compact car choice known for efficiency and everyday usability. Buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection paying close attention to steering feel, safety sensor calibration, and powertrain operation to ensure trouble-free ownership.
