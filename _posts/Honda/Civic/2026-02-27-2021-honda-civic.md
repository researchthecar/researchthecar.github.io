# 2021 Honda Civic

### Overview
The 2021 Honda Civic is a highly regarded compact car known for its blend of practicality, fuel efficiency, and engaging driving dynamics. Across its various trims and powertrain options, it generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of the Honda brand. Owners typically report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected mechanical or electrical problems. The vehicle's design prioritizes durability, making it a dependable choice for daily commuting and longer journeys. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 2021 Civic stands out for its robust engineering and consistent performance, contributing to its strong resale value and owner satisfaction.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Honda Civic demonstrates excellent reliability across its powertrain and component systems. The most notable, albeit infrequent, issue is potential oil dilution in the 1.5L turbo engine under specific extreme conditions, which is manageable with proper maintenance. Other reported issues are minor and often resolved with software updates or standard maintenance procedures. Overall, the 2021 Civic is a dependable vehicle with a low probability of significant mechanical or electrical failures, making it a strong contender in the compact car segment for those prioritizing reliability and low ownership costs.
