# 2002 Honda S2000

### Overview
The 2002 Honda S2000 is a high-performance roadster celebrated for its exhilarating driving dynamics and rev-happy engine. As a second-year model, it benefited from some refinements over the initial launch. Reliability is generally a strong suit for Honda, and the S2000 is no exception, particularly in its core mechanical components. However, like any enthusiast vehicle, specific areas can experience issues, often related to its performance-oriented nature and age. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L F20C
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level drops between changes, blue smoke from exhaust under acceleration.
- **Solution:** Engine rebuild or replacement, ensuring proper piston ring seating and valve stem seals.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $6,000

**VTEC Solenoid Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine Light, loss of power at higher RPMs, rough idle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the VTEC solenoid unit.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual (AP1)
**Synchronizer Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting into certain gears, grinding noises during shifts, especially 2nd and 3rd gear.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild with replacement of worn synchronizers and related components.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Clutch Master Cylinder Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Soft or spongy clutch pedal, difficulty disengaging clutch, fluid leaks near the pedal assembly.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch master cylinder.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Suspension

### Standard Independent Suspension
**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Gauge Cluster Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or non-functional gauges (speedometer, tachometer), flickering lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster or related wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Exterior

### Soft Top
**Tearing and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible tears in the fabric, water ingress into the cabin, difficulty operating the mechanism.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the soft top fabric or the entire soft top assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Honda S2000 is a remarkably reliable sports car, especially considering its performance focus. The F20C engine, while prone to oil consumption if not maintained or driven hard, is generally robust. Transmission issues, primarily synchronizer wear, are common on higher-mileage examples but are repairable. The soft top is a known wear item that may require attention. Electrical gremlins like gauge cluster problems can occur but are not universal. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing specific known issues, the 2002 S2000 offers a rewarding and dependable ownership experience for an enthusiast.
