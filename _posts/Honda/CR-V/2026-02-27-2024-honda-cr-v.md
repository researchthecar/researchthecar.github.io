# 2024 Honda CR-V Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Honda CR-V continues to be a top contender in the compact SUV segment, offering a spacious interior, refined ride quality, and a choice of efficient gasoline or hybrid powertrains. However, used car buyers should approach this model year with awareness of several prominent owner complaints and active recall campaigns. Specifically, steering gear concerns and advanced driver-assistance system glitches have drawn attention from drivers.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This direct-injected turbocharged engine produces approximately 190 horsepower and is paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It delivers peppy daily performance and strong fuel economy, though owners should monitor routine maintenance closely.

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Hybrid

Featuring a 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle engine paired with a dual-motor hybrid system, this powertrain generates 204 total system horsepower. It provides smooth acceleration and exceptional fuel efficiency for city-heavy driving.

**Coolant Intrusion / Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden loss of engine power accompanied by dashboard warnings such as emissions system alerts and engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection and potential cylinder head gasket replacement or engine repairs.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The conventional gasoline trims utilize a CVT that smoothly modulates ratios for optimal fuel efficiency under varied driving loads.

### E-CVT Hybrid Transmission

The hybrid variants use an electronic continuously variable transmission designed to seamlessly blend gasoline and electric motor power.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

Issues with electric power steering assist and mechanical binding have been reported by multiple owners, pointing toward potential steering rack durability concerns.

**Sticky Steering / Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel catches, notches, or feels stiff while driving, requiring excessive corrective force, often accompanied by creaks or clunks.
- **Solution:** Have a certified dealer inspect the steering gearbox and perform any necessary component replacements under applicable recalls or technical service guidelines.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior glass components, including windshields and sunroofs, are susceptible to stress fractures or unexpected shattering based on owner reports.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof or side windows shatter unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds accompanied by a loud popping sound.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for structural stress or alignment issues.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance overall protection but can occasionally generate false positives or sensor errors.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's collision mitigation system triggers unprompted braking events while driving in normal traffic conditions.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates related to the Honda Sensing suite at an authorized dealership.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V508000: Tires:Markings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=CR-V&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 139, "New Pneumatic Radial Tires for Light Vehicles." An owner whose TIN information is missing may not recognize that their vehicle is involved in a safety recall and continue to drive on recalled tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the tires as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V744000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=CR-V&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Difficulty steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the worm gear spring and redistribute or add grease as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Honda CR-V remains a highly practical and comfortable compact SUV choice, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the steering feel to check for any signs of the reported 'sticky steering' defect, and verify that all safety recalls have been addressed by a certified dealer. Reviewing maintenance records for hybrid variants is also recommended to ensure long-term mechanical peace of mind.
