# 2022 Honda CR-V Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Honda CR-V remains a popular compact SUV known for its versatile cargo room, comfortable ride, and fuel-efficient powertrain options. However, used-car buyers should pay close attention to several recurring owner-reported complaints, most notably a prevalent steering concern and occasional automated safety system glitches.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

A 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 190 horsepower, offering standard peppy performance and good fuel economy for daily commuting.

**Head Gasket Failure & Misfires**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine lights, engine sputtering, loss of power, or engine misfires.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis, replacement of spark plugs or ignition coils, or head gasket replacement in severe instances.

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Hybrid

A 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle four-cylinder paired with dual electric motors, generating 212 total horsepower and delivering strong, smooth acceleration with exceptional fuel economy.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A smooth-shifting continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, designed to optimize fuel efficiency during standard driving conditions.

### E-CVT Hybrid Transmission

An electronic continuously variable transmission tailored for the hybrid model, seamlessly blending gas and electric power delivery.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

Owners report notable issues involving sticky steering sensations and premature steering gear wear, prompting high attention from drivers.

**Sticky Steering and Rack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel catches, sticks, or hesitates during minor turns or at highway speeds, occasionally requiring extra driver effort to recenter.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealership inspect the steering gear box; a complete replacement of the steering rack may be necessary.

## Electrical

### Body Control Module and Battery

Electrical system concerns center around unexpected battery drain and control module glitches that require diagnostic troubleshooting.

**Battery Drain and Electrical Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle experiences unexpected dead batteries or warning lights linked to electrical component failures.
- **Solution:** Inspect the body control module (BCM) and replace faulty electrical modules or dead batteries as needed.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features generally enhance protection, though phantom braking events can temporarily startle drivers on open roads.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's automated collision avoidance system triggers sudden, unwanted braking events during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Check for system software updates at a certified service center and ensure sensor lenses remain clean and unobstructed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V524000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=CR-V&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An incorrectly sized tire may cause premature tread wear, or a tire blow out, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new corrected label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=CR-V&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=CR-V&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Honda CR-V is a well-rounded and practical compact SUV, but prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for steering responsiveness and electronic system health. Specifically, test drive the car at various speeds to check for any 'sticky steering' tendencies, and verify that all safety and electrical modules are operating without fault.
