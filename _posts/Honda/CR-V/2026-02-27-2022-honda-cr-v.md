# 2022 Honda CR-V

### Overview
The 2022 Honda CR-V is a popular compact SUV known for its practicality, fuel efficiency, and comfortable ride. Honda's reputation for reliability generally extends to this model year. The CR-V offers a choice between a naturally aspirated engine and a turbocharged option, both paired with a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). This generation of the CR-V has seen generally positive feedback regarding its mechanical and electrical systems. Owners typically report satisfaction with the vehicle's performance and durability, with few widespread or recurring issues surfacing in its first year on the market. Routine maintenance is standard for this class of vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Honda CR-V demonstrates strong reliability, consistent with Honda's brand reputation. Major mechanical issues are rare for both the 1.5L turbo and the 2.0L hybrid powertrains, and the CVT transmission has proven robust. Electrical systems are generally sound, with only minor, isolated reports of infotainment glitches that are typically resolved with software updates. Exterior and interior components are also holding up well. Overall, the 2022 CR-V is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a practical and reliable compact SUV with minimal expected long-term repair concerns beyond standard maintenance.
