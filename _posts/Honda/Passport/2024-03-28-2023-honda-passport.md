**Introduction to the 2023 Honda Passport**

The 2023 Honda Passport is a midsize SUV that offers a blend of ruggedness, space, and features. It is available with a single engine option, a 3.5-liter V6 that produces 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a standard 9-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the Passport often cite its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and reliability. However, some common complaints include a lack of interior storage space, a slow infotainment system, and a jouncy ride on rough roads.

**Engine**

* **3.5-liter V6**

    * **Knocking or ticking noise from the engine**
        * Description/Symptoms: A knocking or ticking noise coming from the engine, especially when the engine is cold.
        * Solution: Replacing the faulty valve lifters.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-speed automatic transmission**

    * **Hesitations or shuddering during shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or shudder when shifting, particularly when upshifting or downshifting.
        * Solution: Updating the transmission software or in some cases, replacing the transmission solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle or vibration**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling or vibrating noise coming from the dashboard, particularly when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing loose components in the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found


* **Cracking or peeling trim pieces**
    * Description/Symptoms: The trim pieces on the dashboard, doors, or center console may crack or peel, especially in areas exposed to sunlight.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2023 Honda Passport is a reliable and spacious SUV with a few minor issues. The engine and transmission issues can be costly to fix, but they are relatively rare. The interior issues are more common but are typically less expensive to address.
