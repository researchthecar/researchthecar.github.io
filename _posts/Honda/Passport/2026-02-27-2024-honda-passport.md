# 2024 Honda Passport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Honda Passport, buyers generally find a capable, spacious midsize SUV powered by a dependable V6 engine. However, early owner reports highlight a few recurring mechanical and electrical quirks that prospective buyers should inspect closely. Most notably, front brake rotor warping and start-stop system glitches have surfaced among early owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2024 Honda Passport comes standard with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing around 280 horsepower paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. This powertrain provides robust acceleration and confident towing capability for daily driving. Overall, the mechanical core of the engine is generally solid, though minor electrical integration issues like the auto stop-start feature have occasionally been reported by owners.

**Auto Idle Stop Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may shut off via the auto stop-start feature at a red light or intersection and fail to restart, locking up electrical features and preventing the car from shifting into neutral.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's starting and charging systems, as well as software modules, diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission coupled to an intelligent all-wheel-drive system, the Passport delivers smooth power delivery. This transmission option is widely used across Honda's lineup and generally exhibits good long-term reliability.

## Brakes

### Front Brake Rotors

Multiple owner reports indicate recurring issues with front brake rotors warping prematurely, leading to brake pedal vibration and steering wheel shake.

**Premature Rotor Warping**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience noticeable pulsing, vibration, or shaking in the front end and steering wheel when applying the brakes at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Have the brake rotors resurfaced or replaced under warranty if applicable, and inspect wheel hubs and calipers for proper operation.

## Electrical

### Turn Signal Audio

Cabin electronics generally function well, though isolated complaints point to minor glitches such as missing turn signal audio cues.

**Turn Signal Chime Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The visual dashboard indicators and exterior lights function normally, but the audible clicking or chime sound for turn signals and emergency flashers fails to operate.
- **Solution:** Inspect body control modules and instrument cluster settings, or update software as required by dealer service bulletins.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior trim and panels are standard for the class, but rare instances of spontaneous sunroof glass breakage have been documented.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass unexpectedly explodes or shatters inward while the vehicle is parked or driven, scattering glass across the cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the frame for any manufacturing stress points or foreign impacts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V900000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly:Filler Pipe And Cap](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Passport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the fuel filler neck tube and pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Honda Passport is a comfortable, spacious midsize crossover that appeals to families and adventurers alike. While its V6 engine and transmission pairing is fundamentally robust, used-car buyers should thoroughly test the brakes for any steering wheel shimmy or pulsing caused by rotor wear. Additionally, checking electrical features and the sunroof during a pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a worry-free ownership experience.
