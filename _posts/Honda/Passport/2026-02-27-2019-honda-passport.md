# 2019 Honda Passport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Honda Passport offers a spacious cabin, a comfortable ride, and practical everyday utility for families and road-trip enthusiasts alike. However, prospective buyers should review common owner complaints regarding electronic issues, driver-assist system hiccups, and powertrain reports before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine generating around 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, this model provides strong acceleration and confident passing power. While generally robust, some owners have noted electrical and fuel-related stalling quirks.

**Engine Stalling and Auto Stop-Start Issues**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may stall or fail to restart properly after shutting off at a red light or stop sign under the auto start-stop feature.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing of the starting system, battery, and fuel delivery components is recommended.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine no-starts or sudden power loss during operation due to fuel delivery failures.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump assembly.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive or torque-vectoring all-wheel drive, it delivers smooth cruising capability. A portion of drivers have reported rough engagement and rare premature transmission failures.

**Unintended Neutral Shift and Shifting Delays**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift into neutral or exhibit hard shifting and gear selection stickiness.
- **Solution:** Check for transmission software updates or inspect internal mechanical components if severe slipping occurs.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Cluster

**Infotainment Reboot and Screen Blanking**
- **Symptoms:** The center touchscreen or backup camera display may freeze, emit crackling noises, or go completely black while driving.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reset or update software at a dealership; persistent issues may require head unit replacement.

## Safety

### Honda Sensing Forward Collision System

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision mitigation braking system may falsely detect obstacles on clear roads, causing the vehicle to brake abruptly.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas around the windshield and bumpers, and have dealer calibration checked if false activations persist.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Honda Passport is a capable and spacious midsize crossover that appeals to road-trippers and daily commuters alike. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's service history, verify proper transmission operation, and test all electronics and driver-assistance features during a pre-purchase test drive to avoid unexpected repair bills.
