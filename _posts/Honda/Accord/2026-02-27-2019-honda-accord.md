# 2019 Honda Accord

### Overview
The 2019 Honda Accord is generally regarded as a highly reliable vehicle, building on Honda's long-standing reputation for durability and quality. Across its various trims and powertrain options, the Accord demonstrates strong performance and a low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems. Owners typically report satisfaction with its fuel efficiency, comfortable ride, and advanced technology features. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 2019 Accord stands out for its robust engineering and consistent performance, making it a solid choice for those seeking a dependable mid-size sedan. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its longevity.

**Oil Dilution**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel smell in engine oil, potential for increased oil level.
- **Solution:** Software update from Honda to optimize engine temperature and fuel mixture. In some cases, oil changes may be recommended more frequently.
- **Cost:** $0 - $150 (for potential oil change)

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Honda Accord is a highly reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The most commonly cited issue, oil dilution in the 1.5L turbo engine, is generally manageable with software updates and proper maintenance. Other powertrain options and major components like the transmission, suspension, and electrical systems show excellent reliability with no widespread problems reported. Minor infotainment glitches can occur but are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the 2019 Accord is a dependable choice, offering peace of mind for its owners.
