# 2022 Honda Accord Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Honda Accord is widely recognized for its refined driving dynamics, spacious cabin, and efficient powertrain lineup. However, owner reports submitted to federal safety regulators highlight a few recurring trouble spots that prospective buyers should keep in mind. The most notable concerns involve infotainment glitches, unexpected automated braking events, and occasional engine-related issues on turbocharged models.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged I4

The base 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 192 horsepower, offering a solid blend of daily performance and fuel efficiency. While generally dependable, this powerplant has drawn owner complaints regarding head gasket leaks and cooling system issues at higher mileages.

**Head Gasket Failure and Coolant Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Engine loses power, misfires, overheats, or triggers dashboard warning lights due to coolant leaking into the cylinders.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis, replacement of the head gasket, and inspection of the cylinder head for warping or damage.

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The available 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 generates 272 horsepower and provides brisk acceleration paired with refined power delivery. It is a robust option for enthusiasts seeking stronger performance.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)

Paired with the 1.5L engine, the Continuously Variable Transmission delivers smooth acceleration and maximizes fuel economy for everyday commuting.

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the 2.0L engine, the 10-speed automatic transmission offers quick gear changes and helps keep the engine in its optimal power band.

**Transmission Failure and Shifting Struggles**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden transmission failure, rough shifting, or hesitation after component replacement or service.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection, proper line connection, and potential transmission repair or replacement.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

The interior technology suite provides smartphone integration and digital controls, but owners frequently report software instability and touchscreen ghost touches.

**Ghost Touching and Screen Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen registers phantom inputs, randomly cycles through audio sources, freezes, or disrupts navigation functions while driving.
- **Solution:** May require a software update, system reset, or replacement of the infotainment display assembly.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Honda Sensing safety technologies add an extra layer of security, though occasional false positive alerts and unexpected braking events have been noted by drivers.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly applies the brakes without warning or obstacle presence, increasing the risk of a rear-end collision.
- **Solution:** Have the collision avoidance sensors and software calibrated or inspected by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V430000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Unintended vehicle movement or an unexpected increase in brake pedal travel can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the VSA modulator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V064000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, failing to suppress the air bag as intended. An air bag that deploys unintentionally during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Honda Accord remains a compelling choice in the midsize sedan market, combining sharp handling, passenger comfort, and strong fuel economy. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive the vehicle, verify the health of the infotainment touchscreen, and check maintenance records for any signs of cooling system or engine performance issues. Pre-purchase inspections are strongly recommended to ensure all mechanical and driver-assist systems operate as intended.
