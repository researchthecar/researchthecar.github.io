# 2024 Honda Accord Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Honda Accord continues the legacy of a dependable midsize sedan, offering modern tech and efficient powertrain choices. However, used car buyers should note that early-production owner complaints highlight specific recurring areas of concern, particularly related to steering components and electronic driver assistance systems.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged I4

A 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-four engine producing 192 horsepower, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It offers responsive everyday performance and solid fuel economy for daily commuting.

### 2.0L Hybrid Dual-Motor

A 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle four-cylinder engine combined with an electric motor system generating 204 total horsepower, paired with an eCVT. This hybrid setup provides smooth acceleration and exceptional fuel efficiency.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)

A continuously variable automatic transmission designed to optimize fuel efficiency across the standard 1.5L engine lineup. It delivers smooth, seamless power delivery under normal driving conditions.

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (eCVT)

A specialized electronic continuously variable transmission paired with the hybrid powertrain. It manages power blending between the gas engine and electric drive motors for seamless efficiency.

## Brakes

### Brake System

Electronic brake warning glitches and sensor anomalies have been reported, though physical hardware failures are less common.

**Brake System Warning Lights and Failure Messages**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard displays sudden warnings such as 'Brake System Failure' or 'Electric Parking Brake Failure' alongside multiple indicator lights, sometimes accompanied by a heavy or unresponsive brake pedal feel.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a dealership or specialist is required to check for fault codes or software glitches. Technicians may need to update software modules or reset electronic parking brake sensors.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

Steering complaints represent a notable pattern for this model year, with several owners reporting premature mechanical wear and sticky handling sensations.

**Steering Rack Failure and Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a sticky steering feel, resistance during minor adjustments on the highway, mechanical clunking noises on turns, or complete loss of steering assist. Owners frequently note that steering components have required multiple replacements.
- **Solution:** Have the steering rack and gearbox inspected by a qualified technician. Defective steering racks typically require complete assembly replacement under warranty.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Complex electronic architectures govern modern vehicle systems, and intermittent software alerts remain an area to monitor during a pre-purchase test drive.

**Dashboard Warning Lights and Electrical Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard instrument clusters can randomly illuminate with multiple error messages requiring immediate dealer attention, occasionally accompanied by intermittent infotainment or connectivity failures.
- **Solution:** Perform system updates or factory resets on the infotainment and body control modules as recommended by authorized service procedures.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance occupant protection, though sensitive driver-assist programming can occasionally cause false activation events.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision mitigation braking system or forward collision avoidance software may occasionally trigger unprompted vehicle deceleration or sudden braking during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Check for available manufacturer software updates or calibration services for the front-facing radar and camera sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V782000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 208, "Occupant Crash Protection," 209, "Seat Belt Assembles," and 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A seat belt pretensioner missing a rivet will not properly restrain the occupant, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt pretensioner assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V763000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-pressure fuel pump as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V859000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat cushion frame may not have been tightened properly, which can result in an unsecured seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 207, "Seating Systems." An unsecured driver's seat may not adequately restrain the driver during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's seat cushion frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Honda Accord remains an appealing midsize sedan choice, praised for its spacious cabin, refined ride quality, and excellent fuel economy options. Prospective buyers should thoroughly evaluate the steering feel and check service histories for any history of electronic warning lights or steering rack repairs during a pre-purchase inspection.
