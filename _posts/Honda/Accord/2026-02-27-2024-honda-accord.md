# 2024 Honda Accord Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Honda Accord represents a modern evolution of the brand's flagship midsize sedan, offering a sleek fastback design, updated technology, and efficient powertrain choices including both turbocharged four-cylinder and hybrid systems. As a relatively new model year, the vehicle generally benefits from modern engineering and advanced safety suites, though early owner reports give prospective buyers a glimpse into initial ownership experiences. While major mechanical failures are rare for this vintage, reviewing specific powertrain options and monitoring minor accessory or electrical reports can help ensure a confident used-car purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces roughly 192 horsepower and 192 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive daily power. It is generally regarded as a smooth and efficient powerplant for standard trims, though buyers should watch for standard turbo-engine maintenance habits.

### 2.0L Hybrid Inline-4

This 2.0-liter Atkinson-cycle hybrid powertrain pairs with dual electric motors to deliver a combined 204 horsepower, maximizing fuel efficiency. It stands out as a refined and highly efficient option for commuters and long-distance drivers alike.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired exclusively with the 1.5L turbocharged engine, this CVT features step-shift programming to mimic a traditional geared automatic. It delivers seamless power delivery, though proper fluid maintenance remains key to longevity.

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (e-CVT)

Integrated directly with the hybrid system, this e-CVT manages power split between the gasoline engine and electric motors. It operates smoothly and efficiently under normal driving conditions without conventional belts or pulleys.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V782000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 208, "Occupant Crash Protection," 209, "Seat Belt Assembles," and 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A seat belt pretensioner missing a rivet will not properly restrain the occupant, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt pretensioner assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V763000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-pressure fuel pump as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V859000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Accord&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat cushion frame may not have been tightened properly, which can result in an unsecured seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 207, "Seating Systems." An unsecured driver's seat may not adequately restrain the driver during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's seat cushion frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2024 Honda Accord stands out as a dependable, highly efficient midsize sedan option on the used market, blending refined powertrains with advanced daily usability. Because it is a recent model year, serious mechanical grievances are scarce, making standard pre-purchase inspections and verification of remaining factory warranty coverage the best safeguards for buyers.
