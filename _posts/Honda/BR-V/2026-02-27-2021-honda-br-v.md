### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Honda BR-V is a commendable choice for those prioritizing reliability and low running costs. Its powertrain, including the 1.5L i-VTEC engine and either the CVT or manual transmission, demonstrates robust performance with no significant widespread issues reported. The suspension system is also generally trouble-free. Minor concerns are primarily limited to occasional infotainment system glitches and the standard automotive wear item of batteries, which are not indicative of systemic design flaws. Overall, the BR-V offers a dependable ownership experience, making it a solid option in its segment for budget-conscious buyers seeking a practical and reliable vehicle.
