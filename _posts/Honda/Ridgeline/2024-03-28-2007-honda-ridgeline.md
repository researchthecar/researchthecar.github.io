**Introduction:**

The 2007 Honda Ridgeline is a mid-size pickup truck with a unibody construction and all-wheel drive. It is available with a single engine and transmission option:

* 3.5L V6 engine producing 255 horsepower and 252 lb-ft of torque
* 5-speed automatic transmission

The Ridgeline is known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and innovative features such as an in-bed trunk. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of off-road capability and high price.

## **Engine**

**3.5L V6 Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, engine misfiring, rough idling, decreased performance
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or slips when shifting gears, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Replace the transmission solenoid or torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front end, uneven tire wear, decreased handling
    * Solution: Replace the front suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning light illuminated
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for an extended period
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix the source of the battery drain (e.g., faulty parasitic draw)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Depends on the cause of the issue

## **Exterior**

* **Tailgate Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust or corrosion on the tailgate
    * Solution: Replace or repair the tailgate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Annoying noises coming from the dashboard
    * Solution: Locate and address the source of the noise (e.g., tighten loose panels, replace worn clips)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the issue (e.g., replace faulty sensors, actuators)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

Overall, the 2007 Honda Ridgeline has a relatively low number of significant issues reported. The most common problems include fuel injector failure, transmission slippage, and suspension strut failure. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are generally not severe and can be resolved through proper maintenance and repairs.
