**Introduction:**

The 2006 Honda Ridgeline is a midsize pickup truck offered with a 3.5-liter V6 engine and a 5-speed automatic transmission. It is praised for its spacious cabin, innovative features, and good fuel economy. However, there are some reported issues that owners should be aware of.

### **Engine**

**3.5-Liter V6**

* **VTC Solenoid Failure:** The Variable Timing Control (VTC) solenoid can fail, causing reduced engine power and a check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace the VTC solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Knock Sensor Harness Issue:** The knock sensor harness can become damaged or loose, leading to false knock detection and reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the knock sensor harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Flare:** Some vehicles may experience shift flare, which is a delay or hesitation during gear changes.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the shift solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sag:** The rear suspension may sag over time, especially when carrying heavy loads.
    * Solution: Replace or reinforce the rear leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some vehicles may experience a battery drain due to a faulty wiring harness.
    * Solution: Identify and repair the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, screen freezing, or software errors.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

* **Bed Rust:** The bed can develop rust, especially in areas exposed to moisture and road salt.
    * Solution: Regularly inspect and protect the bed with a bed liner or rust inhibitor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of damage
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty component, such as the actuator motor or temperature sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Cushion Deformation:** The front seat cushions may deform over time, reducing comfort.
    * Solution: Replace the seat cushions or use additional lumbar support.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

Overall, the 2006 Honda Ridgeline is a reliable and capable pickup truck. However, there are some potential issues that owners should be aware of, particularly related to the engine, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but they do not typically result in major problems or excessive repair bills.
