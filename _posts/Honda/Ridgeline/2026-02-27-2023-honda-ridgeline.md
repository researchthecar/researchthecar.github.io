# 2023 Honda Ridgeline

### Overview
The 2023 Honda Ridgeline is a mid-size pickup truck known for its comfortable ride, car-like handling, and versatile in-bed trunk. It is built on a unibody platform, sharing its underpinnings with Honda's other SUVs. This design contributes to its refined driving experience and fuel efficiency compared to traditional body-on-frame trucks. Reliability data for the 2023 model year generally reflects Honda's reputation for building dependable vehicles. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Ridgeline typically performs well in terms of mechanical and electrical systems. Owners often praise its practicality for daily use and light-duty hauling, with fewer reported major mechanical concerns than many competitors in its class.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Honda Ridgeline demonstrates strong reliability, consistent with Honda's reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that can usually be resolved with software updates, and a possibility of premature 12V battery failure, which is a common concern across many vehicle makes and models. Overall, the Ridgeline is a dependable choice for a mid-size pickup truck, with a low probability of major mechanical failures.
