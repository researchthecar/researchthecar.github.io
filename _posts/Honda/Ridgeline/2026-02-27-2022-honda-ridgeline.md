# 2022 Honda Ridgeline Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Honda Ridgeline is a comfortable and versatile unibody midsize pickup truck known for its car-like ride and practical trunk-in-bed design. While generally praised for daily usability and standard all-wheel drive, prospective buyers should review a few reported concerns regarding electrical features, driver-assistance aids, and powertrain behavior.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

The Ridgeline is powered by a 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine producing approximately 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth acceleration and dependable everyday performance.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling, sudden loss of motive power while driving, and failure to restart promptly.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis of fuel, electrical, or powertrain control systems at a certified repair center.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

Equipped with a 9-speed automatic transmission paired exclusively with standard all-wheel drive, offering refined gear changes for highway and city driving.

**Delayed or Hard Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have experienced delayed engagement when shifting into gear from a stop, followed by a harsh lurch forward.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid levels, update powertrain control software, or consult a dealer for mechanical inspection.

## Steering

### Steering and Braking Integration

**Steering Wheel Shake During Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported aggressive steering wheel shaking and slight vehicle pulling when slowing down from highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake rotors, pads, and steering components for wear or alignment issues.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

**Automatic High Beam Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic high beam feature may fail to dim appropriately when approaching oncoming traffic, potentially blinding other drivers.
- **Solution:** Disable automatic high beams and use manual headlight controls if erratic behavior persists.

## Safety

### Driver Assistance and Cameras

**Backup Camera and Sensor Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have noted intermittent blank screens on the backup camera or delayed feed activation when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses and connections; software updates may be required.

**Forward Collision Warning False Positives**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance system can trigger false warnings and steering wheel vibrations when no actual hazard is present.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas or have sensitivity settings adjusted by a dealership.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V458000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Master Cylinder](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Brake master cylinder separation can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the brake booster assembly as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V321000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that does not display an image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the RVC tailgate wire harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V365000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Failure of rear suspension components, such as the rear control arm, can cause a loss of vehicle handling and control, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear subframe and install a rear subframe reinforcement kit and, as necessary, repair or replace the rear subframe components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Honda Ridgeline is a practical and comfortable choice for buyers seeking truck utility with SUV-like driving dynamics. While mechanical issues are relatively uncommon, used-car shoppers should thoroughly test drive the vehicle to check for smooth transmission engagement, proper functioning of driver-assistance electronics, and steady braking performance.
