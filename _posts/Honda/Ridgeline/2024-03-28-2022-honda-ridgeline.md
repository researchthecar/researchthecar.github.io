**Introduction:**

The 2022 Honda Ridgeline is a versatile mid-size pickup truck that offers a comfortable ride and ample cargo space. It's powered by a V6 engine mated to a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Ridgeline is praised for its refined handling, spacious interior, and innovative features like its in-bed trunk. However, it has received some criticism for its limited towing capacity and fuel economy.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating, rough idle. Requires replacement of cylinder head gasket. **$2,000 - $4,000+**
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Engine rattling, reduced power, Check Engine Light. Requires replacement of timing chain tensioner. **$1,000 - $2,000**

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitation or Jerking:** Difficulty changing gears, delayed response to acceleration. Software update or transmission replacement. **$0 - $5,000+**
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Loss of transmission fluid, slipping gears, Check Transmission Light. Repair or replacement of leaking seals or lines. **$500 - $1,500**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Springs Squeaking:** Noisy suspension over bumps or uneven surfaces. Lubrication or replacement of leaf springs. **$200 - $800**
* **Front Stabilizer Bar Bushings Premature Wear:** Clunking or rattling noises while driving over bumps. Replacement of stabilizer bar bushings. **$200 - $400**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery dies prematurely. Requires diagnosis and repair of electrical system. **$100 - $500**
* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture buildup inside headlight assembly. Replacement of headlights. **$500 - $1,000**

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:** Tailgate not opening or closing properly. Replacement of tailgate latch assembly. **$300 - $600**
* **Fuel Door Switch Failure:** Fuel door not opening with key or remote. Replacement of fuel door switch. **$200 - $400**

**Interior**

* **Cup Holder Breakage:** Cup holders cracking or breaking. Replacement of cup holders. **$50 - $150**
* **Dashboard Creaking or Rattling:** Noises coming from the dashboard. Tightening or replacement of dashboard components. **$200 - $400**
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Air conditioning or heating not working or blowing at the wrong temperature. Repair or replacement of climate control unit. **$500 - $1,500**

**Summary:**

The 2022 Honda Ridgeline is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some common issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to significant repairs. The cost to fix these problems can vary depending on the specific part and labor costs in different regions.
