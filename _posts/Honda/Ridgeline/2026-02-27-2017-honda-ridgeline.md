# 2017 Honda Ridgeline Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Honda Ridgeline is a unibody midsize pickup known for its smooth, car-like ride, versatile dual-action tailgate, and practical in-bed trunk. While it offers superior everyday comfort and clever utility compared to traditional body-on-frame trucks, used car buyers should inspect powertrain history and electronics carefully. Owner complaints highlight potential torque converter issues, electrical issues, and occasional driver-assist anomalies.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 producing approximately 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, providing strong and smooth acceleration. It is generally robust, though owners have reported occasional fuel system and electrical sensor glitches.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights and engine performance issues related to failing injectors.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes and replace malfunctioning fuel injectors.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling, loss of motive power, or illumination of electrical warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel delivery, electronic control modules, and sensors; replace faulty components as needed.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features a 6-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. While capable, this transmission has been a focal point for reliability concerns regarding torque converter shudder and rough shifting.

**Torque Converter Failure and Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission judder, rough shifting, pulsing, or sudden locking up while driving.
- **Solution:** Flush transmission fluid or replace the torque converter and affected clutch components.

**Transmission Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, delayed engagement, and transmission warning codes.
- **Solution:** Inspect valve body, solenoids, and internal clutches; repair or replace transmission if internal wear is severe.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Cabin electronics and wiring harnesses can experience intermittent faults, causing warning lights or battery drain.

**Battery Drain and Dashboard Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Random car alarm activation, flickering dashboard lights, clicking noises, and drained batteries.
- **Solution:** Test battery health, check for parasitic draws, and update or replace faulty body control modules.

## Safety

### Forward Collision and Backup Systems

Advanced safety and driver-assistance features generally improve safety, but sensor and wiring glitches can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Collision mitigation braking system activates unexpectedly, causing sudden deceleration or lock-up.
- **Solution:** Calibrate collision sensors and check for software updates.

**Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Backup camera goes black or fails intermittently.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace frayed wiring harnesses where they span the tailgate and body.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V053000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A crack in the fuel pump feed port can allow pressurized fuel to leak out, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump, if necessary, and install a fuel pump cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V888000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Corrosion of the wiring harness can cause increased electrical resistance, unexpectedly activating the vehicle stability assist (VSA) system, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rear wiring harness and related components, replacing them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20E068000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the tonneau cover is not properly secured in the open or closed position, wind resistance may cause the cover to flip and buckle. A separated panel may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify all registered owners of 2017-2020 Honda Ridgelines, and dealers will install tethers onto any Honda Genuine Accessory Tonneau Cover to prevent panel separation and apply warning labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V932000: Structure:Body:Hood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The hood latch striker may become damaged and separate from the hood, which can result in the hood opening while driving. A hood that opens while driving can obstruct the driver's view and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will either repair the hood latch striker, or replace the hood if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V867000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative rearview camera can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera wiring harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V751000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A damaged engine may run improperly or stall while driving, increasing the risk of a fire, crash, or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair, or replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Ridgeline&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Honda Ridgeline is an attractive option for buyers seeking a comfortable, highly functional midsize truck for everyday tasks and road trips. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly test-drive the vehicle to check for transmission shudder or torque converter hesitation, and verify that all electrical components and safety assists operate correctly.
