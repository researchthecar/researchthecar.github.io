**2023 Honda Ridgeline**

The 2023 Honda Ridgeline is a midsize pickup truck available with a single powertrain consisting of a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired to a 9-speed automatic transmission. The V6 engine produces 280 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque.

The Ridgeline is known for its spacious and versatile interior, well-balanced handling, and impressive fuel efficiency for a pickup truck. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

### Engine

**3.5L V6**

* **Oil Consumption**: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups. Symptoms include low oil level warnings and increased exhaust smoke.
Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine rebuild.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**: Rough shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears. Symptoms may include sudden jolts or loss of acceleration.
Solution: Transmission software updates, transmission fluid changes, or transmission replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Leaf Spring Breakage**: Breakage of rear leaf springs, causing a sudden drop in ride height. Symptoms include a sagging rear end and abnormal handling.
Solution: Leaf spring replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain**: Excessive battery drain, resulting in dead batteries or difficulty starting the vehicle. Symptoms may include rapid battery discharge or electrical system malfunctions.
Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical components, such as alternators, starters, or parasitic drains.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Wheel Bearing Failure**: Premature failure of wheel bearings, causing noise, vibration, and potential safety hazards. Symptoms include grinding noises or excessive play in the wheels.
Solution: Replacement of wheel bearings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600 per wheel
Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Climate Control Malfunction**: Inoperative or malfunctioning climate control system, such as air conditioning or heating failures. Symptoms may include lack of airflow, temperature fluctuations, or system errors.
Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control components, such as AC compressor, heater core, or control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Summary

Overall, the 2023 Honda Ridgeline has received generally positive reviews for its comfort, practicality, and fuel efficiency. However, some issues, such as engine oil consumption and transmission problems, may impact its reliability and ownership experience. Repair costs for these issues can vary depending on the severity of the problem and the parts involved.
