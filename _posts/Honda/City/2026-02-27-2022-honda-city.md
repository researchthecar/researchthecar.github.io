## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen may become unresponsive, applications may crash, or the system may restart unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update, system reset, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500 (depending on solution)


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery depletion, difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from accessories or faulty modules, followed by repair or replacement of the faulty component.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Honda City demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential intermittent battery drain issues, which are generally manageable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely robust. Overall, the 2022 City is a dependable choice, with most potential issues being minor and repairable, contributing to a positive ownership experience.
