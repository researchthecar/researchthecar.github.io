# 2020 Honda Clarity

### Overview
The 2020 Honda Clarity is a unique offering in the automotive market, primarily known for its electrified powertrains. It was available in Plug-in Hybrid (PHEV) and Electric (EV) variants, with the Fuel Cell (FCV) model also part of the lineup. This analysis focuses on the reliability of its core components, excluding standard maintenance items. The Clarity generally benefits from Honda's reputation for building durable vehicles, but like any complex system, specific areas can experience issues. This report details common concerns and their potential resolutions, providing a technical overview for owners and prospective buyers.

## Electrical

### High-Voltage Battery Pack
**Reduced Capacity/Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Decreased electric-only range, slower charging times, battery warning lights.
- **Solution:** Battery diagnostics and potential replacement under warranty or out-of-pocket.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $10,000+


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunctioning, difficulty starting, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen becomes unresponsive, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $50 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Honda Clarity, particularly its PHEV and EV variants, demonstrates strong reliability in its core powertrain and chassis components, aligning with Honda's reputation. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the complex electrified systems. The high-voltage battery pack can experience degradation over time, impacting range, and the fuel cell stack in the FCV model is a significant potential expense if issues arise. Electrical gremlins, such as 12V battery failures or infotainment glitches, are also noted. However, for most owners, the Clarity offers a dependable and efficient driving experience with no widespread critical failures reported for its conventional mechanical parts.
