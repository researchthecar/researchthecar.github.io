# 2022 Honda Pilot

### Overview
The 2022 Honda Pilot is a popular three-row SUV known for its practicality and family-friendly features. Reliability is generally a strong suit for Honda, and the Pilot typically follows this trend. For the 2022 model year, it offers a robust V6 engine paired with a smooth transmission, aiming to provide a comfortable and dependable driving experience. While most owners report satisfaction, like any vehicle, there can be isolated incidents or specific component concerns that arise. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for the 2022 Pilot.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Honda Pilot generally exhibits strong reliability, aligning with Honda's reputation. The powertrain, consisting of the 3.5L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission, is robust and has not shown widespread issues. Suspension components are also holding up well. The most commonly reported, though not widespread, concerns tend to be in the electrical system, specifically with the infotainment unit experiencing occasional glitches and a few reports of premature battery failure. Minor issues with the climate control's AC performance in very hot conditions have also been noted. Overall, the 2022 Pilot is a dependable choice for families, with most potential issues being minor and addressable through standard maintenance or software updates.
