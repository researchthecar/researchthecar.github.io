# 2020 Honda Insight Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Honda Insight offers impressive hybrid efficiency and a refined driving experience, making it an appealing choice for used-car buyers looking to save on fuel. While general owner satisfaction is high, used car shoppers should review specific owner-reported complaints regarding driver-assistance quirks and occasional electrical or powertrain warnings. Keeping a close eye on maintenance records and verifying that all safety recalls have been addressed will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Inline-4 Hybrid

The 1.5-liter Atkinson-cycle inline-4 hybrid engine pairs with an electric motor to produce a combined 151 horsepower. It is known for strong fuel economy and smooth power delivery, though proper maintenance of hybrid components is essential.

**Fuel System Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may experience unexpected engine stalling while driving or coming to a stop.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the fuel system and check for applicable manufacturer recalls or software updates.

## Transmission

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (e-CVT)

The electronic continuously variable transmission routes power seamlessly to the front wheels. It contributes significantly to the Insight's high fuel efficiency, though occasional sensor or software alerts have been reported by owners.

**Transmission Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard illumination warning of a transmission problem, prompting a visit to the dealership.
- **Solution:** Scan the vehicle computer for diagnostic trouble codes and have the hybrid transaxle inspected by a professional.

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Automatic Braking

Components related to automated emergency braking and hydraulic service brakes.

**Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automated braking system activates unexpectedly while driving on the highway or approaching clear intersections without obstacles.
- **Solution:** Ensure camera and radar sensors are clean and properly calibrated; consult a dealer for potential software updates.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Steering assembly providing electronic power assistance for maneuverability.

**Notchy or Sticky Steering**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent notchy or sticky feeling while trying to steer straight at cruising speeds, with uneven left-to-right resistance.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering rack and steering column components for wear or binding.

## Electrical

### Charging and Power System

Electrical architecture managing vehicle accessories, charging systems, and hybrid electronics.

**Battery Draining**
- **Symptoms:** The 12V battery repeatedly dies, potentially leaving the driver stranded in cold weather.
- **Solution:** Test the 12V battery and have the DC/DC converter inspected for potential electrical draws or component defects.

## Exterior

### Hood and Underbody Shields

Exterior panels and protective underbody shielding.

**Hood Vibration and Splash Plate Loosening**
- **Symptoms:** The hood visibly vibrates at highway speeds or in windy conditions, and underbody splash shields may come loose.
- **Solution:** Adjust or replace hood latches and secure or replace loose underbody fastener hardware.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V798000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will update the power converter unit (PCU) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V771000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause intermittent or continuous disruptions in communication between the Body Control Module (BCM) and other components. Various system malfunctions such as inoperative windshield wipers, defroster, rearview camera, or exterior lighting can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will update the BCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V215000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Honda Insight is a smart pick for commuters seeking stellar fuel economy and a comfortable ride. While owners have reported isolated issues regarding forward collision sensor sensitivity, 12V battery drain, and minor trim or shield wear, these can typically be managed with pre-purchase inspections and prompt recall service. Buyers should test drive the vehicle to verify smooth operation of the hybrid powertrain, brakes, and steering before finalizing a purchase.
