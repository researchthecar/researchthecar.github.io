# 2019 Honda Insight Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Honda Insight is a well-regarded hybrid compact sedan that balances impressive fuel economy with comfortable driving dynamics. While it generally offers solid reliability, prospective buyers should review a few known owner-reported issues involving the fuel system, advanced safety suites, and climate control components.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

The 2019 Honda Insight features a 1.5-liter Atkinson-cycle 4-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 151 horsepower. This hybrid powertrain is generally efficient and robust, though early model years experienced notable owner complaints regarding fuel pump failures.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine stalling, unexpected loss of power while driving, or failure to restart.
- **Solution:** Replace the defective fuel pump assembly, often covered under applicable manufacturer recalls or campaigns.

## Transmission

### E-CVT Transmission

The Insight utilizes an electronic continuously variable transmission (E-CVT) that seamlessly blends power from the gasoline engine and electric motor. It is mechanically straightforward compared to traditional multi-speed gearboxes and maintains a good track record for durability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The regenerative braking system blends seamlessly with conventional friction brakes, but rare complaints highlight warning light or sensor response failures.

**Brake System Warning and Response Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Sensors fail to illuminate warning lights promptly or driver reports extended stopping distances during automated emergency braking events.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and electronic control modules for proper operation.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Backup Camera

Electrical complaints primarily center on intermittent glitches within the touchscreen infotainment system and rear-view camera.

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display freezes, shows rainbow colors, or the backup camera fails to activate when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reset, update software, or replace faulty display hardware if persistent.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Safety equipment includes Honda Sensing as standard, though sensors can occasionally misinterpret road conditions.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking or Collision Mitigation Braking System (CMBS) suddenly engages without any obstacle present, causing jarring slowdowns.
- **Solution:** Have the radar sensor and front-facing camera inspected and recalibrated by a qualified dealership technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V771000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause intermittent or continuous disruptions in communication between the Body Control Module (BCM) and other components. Various system malfunctions such as inoperative windshield wipers, defroster, rearview camera, or exterior lighting can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will update the BCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V215000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V629000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In certain scenarios, the back-up camera center display may not function properly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." If the rearview camera display does not show what is behind the vehicle, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the display audio unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V664000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) control unit may have a manufacturing error, possibly resulting in the air bags or seatbelt pretensioners not deploying in the event of a crash. In the event of a crash, if the air bags or seatbelt pretensioners do not function as intended, there would be an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the SRS unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V158000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A manufacturing issue with the front seat belts may cause the seat belt buckle channel to interfere with the release button, preventing the seat belt buckle from latching. An unlatched seat belt cannot properly restrain the seat occupant during a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver and front passenger seat belt buckle release buttons or the buckle assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Honda Insight is an appealing choice for used-car buyers seeking high fuel efficiency and modern tech features. While most mechanical systems prove durable, shoppers should verify that fuel pump recall services have been performed and test the safety sensors and infotainment display thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection.
