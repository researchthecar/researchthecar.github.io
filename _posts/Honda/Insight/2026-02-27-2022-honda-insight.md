# 2022 Honda Insight Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Honda Insight is generally recognized as a highly efficient and practical hybrid sedan, offering sleek styling and impressive fuel economy for daily commuters. However, used car buyers should pay attention to several emerging owner-reported complaints concerning electronics, advanced driver-assistance systems, and occasional powertrain hiccups. While it offers a refined ride, reviewing these specific areas during a pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid

The 2022 Honda Insight features a 1.5-liter Atkinson-cycle 4-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 151 horsepower. This hybrid powertrain is generally celebrated for its stellar fuel efficiency, though some owners have experienced isolated propulsion and shaking concerns.

**Power Loss and Shaking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine shaking, loud noises resembling a helicopter, and sudden deceleration or loss of power while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the hybrid propulsion system, fuel injectors, and diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint component failure.

## Transmission

### E-CVT Transmission

The Insight utilizes an Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (E-CVT) that seamlessly manages power delivery between the gas engine and electric motor. It operates smoothly during daily driving and currently shows very few widespread mechanical complaints.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Body Control Module

Electrical complaints primarily center around intermittent touchscreen glitches and occasional module or battery performance issues.

**Infotainment Ghost Taps and Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen interface may experience phantom inputs, randomly selecting menu items, changing audio sources, or freezing entirely.
- **Solution:** Software updates performed by a dealership or a hard system reset can sometimes resolve touchscreen calibration and glitch issues.

**Body Control Module and Starting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Failures in the Body Control Module (BCM) can lead to intermittent electrical issues and difficulties starting the vehicle, particularly in colder weather.
- **Solution:** Testing and replacing a faulty BCM or addressing weak 12V battery health can restore normal electrical functionality.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

While advanced safety features generally provide peace of mind, sensor calibration issues can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking maneuvers.

**Inadvertent Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision mitigation braking system may falsely detect obstacles and unexpectedly slam on the brakes while driving.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians recalibrate the camera and radar sensors associated with the Honda Sensing safety suite.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V900000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the second-row center seat belt assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Insight&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Honda Insight remains a smart, highly efficient choice for used car buyers seeking low fuel costs and comfortable daily transportation. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test the infotainment system, verify proper operation of the driver-assistance safety features, and ensure the 12V and hybrid electrical systems are functioning normally before finalizing a purchase.
