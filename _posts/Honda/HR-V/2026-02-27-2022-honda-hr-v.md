# 2022 Honda HR-V Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Honda HR-V, buyers generally find a practical and efficient subcompact crossover designed for daily commuting and urban driving. While Honda has built a strong reputation for long-term dependability, owner reports submitted to safety regulators highlight a few areas that require attention during a pre-purchase inspection. Paying close attention to steering feel, transmission operation, and advanced driver assistance systems can help ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces around 158 horsepower and provides adequate power for daily commuting with generally reliable mechanical performance.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lose power or shut off entirely while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electrical system, fuel delivery, and engine control modules for fault codes.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission (CVT) routes power smoothly to the front wheels or available all-wheel-drive system, emphasizing fuel efficiency over sporty engagement.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle revs excessively without accelerating, or experiences a sudden loss of drive while on the highway.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition and consult a specialist for potential CVT replacement or internal component repair.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering

**Steering Wheel Binding and Seizing**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic power steering system may cause the steering wheel to bind, lock up, or become extremely difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering rack and electronic power steering components for mechanical wear or sensor failure.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision mitigation or adaptive cruise control systems may trigger unexpected sudden braking events without obstacles present.
- **Solution:** Check camera and sensor calibration, and ensure windshield mounted driver-assist modules are clean and unobstructed.

### Air Bag System

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the SRS dashboard warning light indicating a fault within the supplemental restraint system.
- **Solution:** Scan the airbag control module for diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint faulty sensors or wiring harnesses.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V046000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the engine is started with a key, due to a design error in the audio display power circuit. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the display audio unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V064000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, failing to suppress the air bag as intended. An air bag that deploys unintentionally during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V384000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2022)**

## Summary Verdict

The Honda HR-V offers versatile packaging and efficient performance, making it an appealing option in the used crossover market. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive the vehicle, paying close attention to steering smoothness, transmission responsiveness, and the functionality of all driver-assistance technologies. Arranging a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an independent mechanic is strongly recommended to catch any underlying mechanical or electronic issues.
