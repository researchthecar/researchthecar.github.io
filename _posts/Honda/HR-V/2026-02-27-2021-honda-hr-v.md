# 2021 Honda HR-V Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Honda HR-V offers buyers a versatile subcompact crossover experience with clever interior packaging and decent fuel economy. However, prospective used-car buyers should review owner complaints regarding several recurring mechanical and electrical concerns, particularly pertaining to the instrument cluster display, steering feel, and continuously variable transmission operation.

---

## Engine

### 1.8L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 1.8-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing 141 horsepower and 127 lb-ft of torque. This conventional powerplant is generally durable and straightforward, though performance is modest.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped with a continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive or optional Real Time AWD. Used buyers should test the transmission thoroughly for smooth engagement and any signs of slipping.

**Transmission Failure and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected loss of vehicle propulsion while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by sudden shifting into neutral or internal mechanical failure indicated by metal shavings.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection, and in severe cases, a complete transmission rebuild or replacement.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Mechanism

Steering complaints indicate potential binding or feedback issues that warrant a close inspection during a test drive.

**Sticky Steering and Clunking Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a mechanical clunking sound when turning, accompanied by a steering wheel that feels sticky, locks briefly, or fails to return smoothly to the center position.
- **Solution:** Inspection of the steering rack and electric power steering components may require part lubrication, adjustment, or replacement.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster Display

The electrical system generally operates well, but recurring reports highlight instrument cluster reliability issues.

**Intermittent Screen Failure and Scrambled Data**
- **Symptoms:** The partial LCD screen and gauge cluster sections displaying fuel level, odometer, and trip information flicker, go completely blank, or display scrambled characters, notably during cold weather.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealership inspect the wiring connections and replace faulty display modules if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V046000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the engine is started with a key, due to a design error in the audio display power circuit. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the display audio unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V064000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, failing to suppress the air bag as intended. An air bag that deploys unintentionally during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Honda HR-V remains a practical choice for daily commuting thanks to its flexible interior cargo space and efficient performance. Nevertheless, used vehicle shoppers should verify proper functioning of the instrument cluster, test the steering system for any signs of binding or sticking, and ensure the transmission shifts properly without hesitation.
