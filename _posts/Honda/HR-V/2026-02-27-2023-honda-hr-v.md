# 2023 Honda HR-V Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Honda HR-V brings a complete redesign, offering a larger footprint, upgraded interior comfort, and a smoother ride compared to previous generations. However, early production models have drawn notable owner complaints concerning spontaneous rear window shattering and steering assist issues. When shopping for a used 2023 HR-V, buyers should carefully inspect these specific trouble spots and verify whether any prior repairs have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

Equipped with a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing 158 horsepower and 138 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate everyday power for city and highway driving. This powerplant has generally demonstrated dependable mechanical operation with minimal major drivetrain complaints reported by owners.

## Transmission

### CVT Automatic Transmission

Features a continuously variable transmission (CVT) paired with front-wheel drive or available all-wheel drive, tuned for smooth acceleration and optimal fuel efficiency. The transmission has maintained a solid reliability record without widespread reports of premature failure or mechanical defects.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

The electric power steering system has generated concerns regarding sticky steering sensations and temporary loss of smooth assist.

**Steering Assist Failure and Sticky Steering**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel becomes difficult to turn, develops a sticky or notched feeling around the center position, or fails to return smoothly after a turn.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering rack and control module; dealerships may need to service or replace internal components under warranty.

## Exterior

### Rear Windshield / Glass

Exterior complaints heavily concentrate on unexpected rear glass breakage, often linked to thermal stress or rear defroster usage.

**Spontaneous Rear Window Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear windshield or hatch glass unexpectedly shatters or explodes, often while driving, parked, or shortly after engaging the rear window defroster.
- **Solution:** Have the rear glass assembly replaced by a qualified technician, ensuring defroster grid connections and glass tempering meet manufacturer specifications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V782000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 208, "Occupant Crash Protection," 209, "Seat Belt Assembles," and 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A seat belt pretensioner missing a rivet will not properly restrain the occupant, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt pretensioner assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V744000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Difficulty steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the worm gear spring and redistribute or add grease as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Honda HR-V is a comfortable and practical compact crossover with a solid powertrain, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the condition of the rear hatch glass and test drive the vehicle to ensure the steering returns smoothly from turns without binding or catching. Securing a vehicle with remaining factory warranty coverage can provide valuable peace of mind against early component failures.
