# 2024 Honda HR-V Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Honda HR-V offers buyers a modern subcompact crossover experience with refreshed styling and updated technology. As a relatively new vehicle on the used market, it generally provides dependable daily transportation, though owners have reported a handful of electrical, driver-assistance, and minor body concerns that prospective buyers should note.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

Equipped with a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing around 158 horsepower and 138 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commutes. This conventional powerplant is generally straightforward and lacks the complexity of turbocharged options, making it a reliable choice for used buyers.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Features a continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive or optional all-wheel drive, designed to optimize fuel efficiency. While CVT units require consistent fluid maintenance, they deliver smooth acceleration under normal driving conditions without notable widespread mechanical failures.

## Steering

### Steering System

The electric power steering setup is generally responsive, though isolated reports highlight intermittent assist dropouts or sticky steering feel.

**Steering Assist Failure and Stickiness**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden lane-keep assist failures or sticky steering sensations, particularly noticeable when turning left.
- **Solution:** Inspect electric power steering modules and steering linkage components for binding or software errors.

## Exterior

### Door Locks and Windows

Electrical accessories generally operate as intended, though a few owners have experienced quirks with door locking logic and window regulators.

**Door Lock Actuator and Power Window Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Key fob unlock buttons may unlock all doors simultaneously despite settings, or power windows may spontaneously reverse or act erratically.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer reprogram body control modules or inspect faulty door lock actuators and window switches.

## Safety

### Collision Mitigation and Braking

Advanced safety features enhance occupant protection, but a small number of drivers have reported false-positive collision warnings or unexpected automated braking events.

**Unexpected Forward Collision Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision mitigation braking system may independently activate and apply hard braking while driving with no obstructions ahead.
- **Solution:** Check for sensor calibration updates or take the vehicle to a dealer to inspect radar and camera alignments.

**Service Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report instances where the vehicle continued rolling or failed to stop adequately when parking or navigating low-speed situations.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and hydraulic systems immediately if braking performance feels inconsistent.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V782000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 208, "Occupant Crash Protection," 209, "Seat Belt Assembles," and 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A seat belt pretensioner missing a rivet will not properly restrain the occupant, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt pretensioner assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V744000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Difficulty steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the worm gear spring and redistribute or add grease as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V859000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat cushion frame may not have been tightened properly, which can result in an unsecured seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 207, "Seating Systems." An unsecured driver's seat may not adequately restrain the driver during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's seat cushion frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V054000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat cushion frame may not have been tightened properly, which can result in a loose seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 207, "Seating Systems." A loose driver's seat may not adequately restrain the driver during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's seat cushion frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Honda HR-V remains a practical and efficient subcompact crossover choice for used-car shoppers. While most mechanical and powertrain components prove dependable, prospective buyers should thoroughly test the electronic safety aids, steering feel, and power accessories during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything operates correctly.
