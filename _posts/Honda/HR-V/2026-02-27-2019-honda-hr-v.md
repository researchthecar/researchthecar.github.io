# 2019 Honda HR-V Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Honda HR-V offers a practical and versatile subcompact crossover package with clever interior packaging and decent fuel economy. However, prospective buyers should carefully review potential trouble areas, notably concerning electrical instrument clusters and CVT transmission durability as vehicles approach higher mileage brackets.

---

## Engine

### 1.8L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 1.8-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing 141 horsepower and 127 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting with standard front-wheel drive or available Real Time AWD.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped with a continuously variable automatic transmission paired to the 1.8L engine. Owners have reported intermittent durability concerns and premature mechanical failures at higher mileages.

**CVT Transmission Failure and Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of motive power, crunching or clunking noises while accelerating from a stop, or complete mechanical transmission failure.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis and potentially a full CVT transmission replacement.

## Electrical

### Digital Instrument Cluster and Gauges

Electrical complaints center heavily on instrument cluster screen flickering and intermittent failures during cold weather conditions.

**Dashboard Display and Fuel Gauge Blanking**
- **Symptoms:** Digital instrument cluster flickers, goes completely blank, or fails to display vital information such as the speedometer, odometer, and fuel gauge, particularly during cold weather.
- **Solution:** Inspect electrical connections or replace the instrument cluster assembly as needed.

## Exterior

### Paint and Body Structure

Exterior body components generally hold up well, though isolated complaints highlight premature paint peeling and minor trim degradation.

**Premature Paint Peeling and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Paint begins peeling or chipping away in large chunks on the roof, doors, or trim areas, leading to underlying rust when exposed to moisture.
- **Solution:** Repaint affected body panels or repair corrosion damage.

## Safety

### Honda Sensing Forward Collision System

The Honda Sensing suite provides helpful safety aids, though owners occasionally experience unexpected sensor error warnings and system deactivation.

**Collision Avoidance and Sensor Error Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden illumination of warning lights for lane keeping assist, adaptive cruise control, and forward collision warnings without prior vehicle impacts.
- **Solution:** Calibrate or replace faulty camera and radar sensor modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V215000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V777000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." A reduction of braking performance increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Owners will be notified, and Honda or Acura dealers will bleed the brakes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V046000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the engine is started with a key, due to a design error in the audio display power circuit. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the display audio unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2019)**

- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=HR-V&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used model, inspect the vehicle's service history closely, particularly verifying CVT fluid maintenance and confirming that all dashboard displays and Honda Sensing features operate properly. Pay close attention to cold-weather starts to catch potential instrument cluster glitches before purchasing.
