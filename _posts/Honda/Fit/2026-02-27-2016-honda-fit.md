# 2016 Honda Fit Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Honda Fit, buyers will find a subcompact hatchback renowned for its clever interior packaging, remarkable cargo versatility, and respectable fuel efficiency. Powered by a zippy 1.5-liter four-cylinder engine, it handles daily commuting and tight city parking with ease. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical issues, dashboard display failures, and occasional powertrain hiccups before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

Equipped with a 1.5-liter direct-injection four-cylinder engine generating approximately 130 horsepower, this powertrain provides lively performance for a subcompact car. While generally efficient and dependable, it requires vigilance regarding fuel system components.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation or stumbling during acceleration, illumination of the check engine warning light, and potential secondary damage to catalytic converters.
- **Solution:** Replace failing fuel injectors and associated spark plugs or sensors as needed.

**Engine Misfire and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Engine running rough, loss of power under highway speeds, and unexpected vehicle stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnose cylinder misfires, inspect ignition coils, and service the fuel system components.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual / Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is sent to the front wheels via either a 6-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Both options offer great efficiency, though manual models can occasionally exhibit clutch-area noises.

**Clutch and Gearbox Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Audible ticking or clicking noises when releasing the clutch pedal, particularly noticeable when shifting into first gear on an incline.
- **Solution:** Inspect clutch components, linkage, and transmission fluid levels for premature wear.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Dashboard Display

The electrical system oversees cabin accessories, gauges, and power control modules. Owner reports highlight recurring dashboard screen blanking and climate-sensitive display failures.

**Dashboard Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The digital fuel gauge, odometer, and cluster panel go completely blank, flicker, or show unreadable gibberish especially during cold weather startups.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty instrument cluster panels or address loose circuit board solder connections.
- **Cost:** $1200

## Safety

### Airbags and Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs as expected in routine driving, though various owners have logged complaints regarding intermittent warning lights and stubborn seat belt mechanisms.

**Airbag Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Persistent illumination of the airbag system warning indicators for both driver and passenger side inflators.
- **Solution:** Diagnose airbag sensor faults and replace defective inflator components.

**Seat Belt Retraction Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rear seat belts or buckles becoming twisted, difficult to latch, or failing to retract smoothly.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace binding buckles or retractable mechanisms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V697000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Fit&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, the rear grab handle brackets were not manufactured properly, and as a result, the brackets may puncture the side curtain air bags upon their deployment. If the side curtain air bags are punctured upon deployment, the outboard seat occupants may be at a higher risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear grab handle bracket, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Honda Fit remains an appealing choice for buyers seeking exceptional interior space and fuel economy in a small package. While mechanical layouts are generally robust, used-car shoppers should thoroughly test all electronics, check the dashboard display functions for cold-weather quirks, and listen for any hesitations or warning lights during a test drive to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
