# 2019 Honda Fit Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Honda Fit remains a popular choice for used-car buyers seeking exceptional cargo versatility, impressive fuel economy, and nimble city driving dynamics. While its overall reputation for longevity is strong, prospective owners should pay close attention to potential issues involving dashboard display glitches, fuel system components, and recurring paint concerns on certain exterior finishes.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 1.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing around 130 horsepower, this powerplant offers peppy performance and strong fuel efficiency. It is generally regarded as reliable, though some owners have reported isolated concerns regarding fuel injectors and misfires.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) / 6-Speed Manual

Equipped with either a smooth-shifting 6-speed manual transmission or a responsive Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT), the powertrain delivers power effectively to the front wheels. Both options generally hold up well with routine fluid maintenance.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Dashboard Display

electrical issues are mostly limited to occasional screen and instrument cluster display failures that make tracking fuel and mileage difficult.

**Fuel Gauge and Odometer Screen Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The digital screen displaying the fuel level, odometer, and trip information goes completely blank, flickers, or displays garbled characters, particularly in colder weather.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing of the instrument cluster or replacement of the display unit.

## Exterior

### Paint and Body Structure

Body panels are generally solid, but specific paint finishes—notably white—have exhibited premature peeling and flaking issues.

**Paint Peeling and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** White exterior paint begins to peel off in large sheets, particularly on the roof and hood, exposing bare metal and leading to surface rust or corrosion.
- **Solution:** Professional repainting or body shop intervention may be necessary to halt corrosion.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Fit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V215000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Fit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V046000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Fit&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the engine is started with a key, due to a design error in the audio display power circuit. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the display audio unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Honda&model=Fit&modelYear=2019)**

- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Honda Fit is a practical, fuel-efficient subcompact hatchback that makes an excellent daily commuter or city runabout. When shopping for a used model, thoroughly inspect the instrument cluster to ensure all digital gauges and the odometer are functioning correctly, and examine the exterior paint closely for any signs of peeling or premature wear.
