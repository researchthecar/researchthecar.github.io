**Introduction:**

The 1998 Honda EV Plus was an electric vehicle that offered a unique driving experience. It was powered by a single electric motor and had a range of up to 80 miles on a single charge. The EV Plus was praised for its smooth ride, quiet operation, and zero emissions. However, it also had some notable issues that hindered its popularity.

**Electric Motor**

* **Range Loss:** The range of the EV Plus could be significantly reduced in cold weather or under heavy load.
* **Solution:** Driving more conservatively and charging the battery more frequently.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** None

**Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Pack Failure:** The battery pack in the EV Plus could fail prematurely, resulting in a loss of power.
* **Solution:** Replacing the battery pack.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Recall:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 06V198000

* **Electrical System Malfunctions:** Various electrical components, such as the headlights, taillights, and gauges, could malfunction due to faulty wiring or connectors.
* **Solution:** Replacing or repairing the faulty components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Rust:** The body panels of the EV Plus were prone to rust, especially in areas exposed to moisture and road salt.
* **Solution:** Replacing the rusted panels or applying rust-resistant treatments.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:** The dashboard of the EV Plus could crack over time, especially in hot climates.
* **Solution:** Replacing the dashboard.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

* **Upholstery Fading:** The upholstery in the EV Plus could fade or discolor over time due to sun exposure.
* **Solution:** Cleaning and protecting the upholstery with UV-resistant treatments.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

The 1998 Honda EV Plus had several issues, primarily affecting its electrical system and exterior. The cost of repairs could be substantial, with the battery pack replacement being the most expensive issue. Overall, these issues hindered the vehicle's long-term reliability and practicality, contributing to its limited production and popularity.
