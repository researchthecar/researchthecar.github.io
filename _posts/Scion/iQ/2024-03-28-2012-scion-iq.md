**Introduction:**

The 2012 Scion iQ is a subcompact hatchback that offers nimble handling and impressive fuel economy. It is powered by a 1.3-liter inline-four engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The iQ is known for its spacious interior and comfortable ride quality, but it also has some drawbacks, including limited rear legroom and a noisy cabin.

**Engine**

**1.3L Inline-Four**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Description/Symptoms: Owners may experience excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage. Solution: Replace the piston rings and valve stem seals. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Hesitation and Slippage:** Description/Symptoms: The CVT may hesitate or slip during acceleration, causing jerking or loss of power. Solution: Update the transmission control module (TCM) software. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ Recalls: None Found

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery may drain prematurely, leaving the car without power. Solution: Replace the defective battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Faulty Door Locks:** Description/Symptoms: The door locks may malfunction, preventing doors from opening or closing properly. Solution: Replace the actuator or door lock assembly. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cracking Dashboard:** Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may crack or warp, especially in hot climates. Solution: Replace the dashboard. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+ Recalls: None Found
* **Peeling Steering Wheel:** Description/Symptoms: The steering wheel may peel or become damaged. Solution: Replace the steering wheel. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Scion iQ has some reliability concerns, particularly with its engine and electronics. The excessive oil consumption issue is a major concern and can lead to costly repairs. Other issues, such as the transmission hesitation and electrical problems, are less severe but still inconvenient. The iQ's interior also has some durability issues with the dashboard and steering wheel. Overall, the vehicle's reliability issues are moderate in severity and can vary in cost from minor to major.
