### Overview
The 2016 Scion iM, a compact hatchback known for its value and practicality, generally exhibits good reliability. As a Toyota product, it benefits from the brand's reputation for durable engineering. Common ownership experiences highlight a robust powertrain and a well-built interior, contributing to a positive ownership experience for many. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the iM tends to avoid widespread, costly problems, making it a sensible choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking dependable transportation. Owners often praise its fuel efficiency and comfortable ride for its class.
