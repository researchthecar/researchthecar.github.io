**Introduction:**

The 2016 Scion iA is a subcompact sedan that was offered with a single engine and transmission option. The 1.5L inline-4 engine produced 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque, which was paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The iA was praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and spacious interior, but it was criticized for its lack of performance and some interior shortcomings.

    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Scion iA is a generally reliable and affordable vehicle. However, there have been some reported issues with the climate control system and power windows. These issues are not widespread and can be resolved with relatively modest repair costs. Overall, the iA offers a good value for the money, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
