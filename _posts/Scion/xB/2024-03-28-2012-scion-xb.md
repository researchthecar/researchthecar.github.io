**Introduction**

The 2012 Scion xB is a compact hatchback that offers a spacious interior and versatile cargo area. It comes standard with a 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 158 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque. A five-speed manual transmission is standard, while a four-speed automatic transmission is available as an option. The xB is also available with a 1.5-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque, but this engine is only available with the manual transmission.

**Engine**

**1.8-Liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, rattling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **VVT Gear Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace VVT gear
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.5-Liter Four-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Slave Cylinder Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, clutch pedal pulsation
    * Solution: Replace clutch slave cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fifth Gear Grind**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding noise when shifting into fifth gear
    * Solution: Replace synchronizer rings or transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission pan gasket or seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, loss of gears
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mounts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace front strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorbers**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains overnight
    * Solution: Replace battery or check for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical problems
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuators**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty unlocking or locking doors
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per door
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tail Light Assembly**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside tail light assembly
    * Solution: Replace tail light assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or vibration from dashboard
    * Solution: Install sound dampening material
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not working properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Scion xB has a number of common issues that can be costly to repair. These include engine problems, such as intake manifold gasket failure and timing chain tensioner failure, as well as transmission issues, such as clutch slave cylinder failure and fifth gear grind. Suspension problems, electrical issues, and interior problems are also relatively common. While the xB is a generally reliable vehicle, it is important to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing one.
