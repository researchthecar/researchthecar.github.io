**Introduction**

The 2010 Scion xB is a subcompact hatchback known for its boxy design and spacious interior. It was offered with a single engine and transmission option: a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 158 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque. Despite its popularity, the xB has encountered several issues over the years, ranging from minor inconveniences to more serious mechanical problems.

**Engine**

* **2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine**

>**Oil Consumption**
>Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
>Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000+
>Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**

>**Gear Grinding**
>Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, accompanied by grinding noises.
>Solution: Transmission repair or replacement
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
>Recalls: None Found

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

>**Shifting Hesitation**
>Description/Symptoms: Transmission delays or hesitates when shifting gears.
>Solution: Transmission fluid flush, valve body replacement, or transmission rebuild
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
>Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
>Description/Symptoms: Battery drains rapidly, even when the car is turned off.
>Solution: Identify and repair faulty electrical components (e.g., alternator, battery terminals)
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
>Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Failure**
>Description/Symptoms: Headlights malfunction or dim significantly.
>Solution: Replace headlight bulbs or wiring harness
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
>Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Breakage**
>Description/Symptoms: Exterior door handles break or become difficult to operate.
>Solution: Replace door handles
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
>Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Cushion Tear**
>Description/Symptoms: Seat cushions tear or rip at the seams.
>Solution: Seat cushion repair or replacement
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
>Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Cracking**
>Description/Symptoms: Dashboard develops cracks or splits.
>Solution: Dashboard replacement or repair
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
>Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2010 Scion xB has several notable issues that can range from minor annoyances to more expensive repairs. The most serious issues include oil consumption and transmission problems, which can require costly repairs or replacements. However, many of the other issues can be addressed with relatively inexpensive fixes. Despite these potential problems, the xB remains a popular choice for drivers seeking a spacious and affordable hatchback.
