# 2012 Scion xB

### Overview
The 2012 Scion xB is generally considered a reliable vehicle, known for its practicality and distinctive styling. As a Toyota product, it benefits from a reputation for durability and straightforward engineering. Owners often praise its fuel efficiency and spacious interior for its size. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the xB typically avoids major mechanical or electrical problems, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking dependable transportation. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring its longevity, but significant, widespread defects are uncommon.
