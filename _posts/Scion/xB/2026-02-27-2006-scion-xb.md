# 2006 Scion xB

### Overview
The 2006 Scion xB, known for its distinctive boxy design and practicality, generally offers a reliable ownership experience. As a Toyota product, it benefits from a reputation for durability and straightforward engineering. Common areas to inspect, as with any used vehicle, include routine maintenance items and age-related wear, but major systemic failures are not prevalent.

## Electrical

### Standard System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, or complete loss of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Power Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Window not operating with the switch.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500 per window


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Fading Paint/Clear Coat**
- **Symptoms:** Discoloration, chalkiness, or peeling of the paint, particularly on horizontal surfaces exposed to sun.
- **Solution:** Repainting or touch-up work.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+

**Headlight Lens Hazing**
- **Symptoms:** Cloudy or yellowed headlight lenses reducing light output.
- **Solution:** Headlight restoration kit or replacement of headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $50 - $200 for restoration, $300 - $600 for replacement


## Interior

### Upholstery and Trim
**Worn Seat Fabric**
- **Symptoms:** Tears, thinning, or excessive wear on seat upholstery, especially on the driver's side.
- **Solution:** Seat cover installation or professional upholstery repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $50 - $200 for covers, $300 - $800+ for repair/replacement

**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or intermittent airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 Scion xB is generally considered a reliable vehicle, aligning with Toyota's reputation for durability. The powertrain (engine and transmission) is robust and typically free from major widespread issues. The most common concerns tend to be age-related, such as potential alternator failure, power window motor issues, and cosmetic wear on the exterior paint and interior upholstery. HVAC blower motor failure is also a possibility. With regular maintenance and attention to these potential weak points, the 2006 xB can continue to be a dependable and cost-effective mode of transportation.
