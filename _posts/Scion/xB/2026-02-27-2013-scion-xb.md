# 2013 Scion xB

### Overview
The 2013 Scion xB is generally considered a reliable vehicle, known for its distinctive "boxy" design and practical interior space. As a Toyota product, it benefits from a reputation for durability and straightforward engineering. Owners often praise its fuel efficiency and low running costs. While no vehicle is entirely free from potential issues, the xB typically avoids major mechanical or electrical problems, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking dependable transportation. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its longevity, but significant widespread defects are uncommon for this model year.
