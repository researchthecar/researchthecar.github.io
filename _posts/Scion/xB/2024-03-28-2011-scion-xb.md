**Introduction**

The 2011 Scion xB is a compact, boxy hatchback known for its spacious interior and quirky styling. It is available with a 2.4-liter 4-cylinder engine paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission.

People generally like the xB for its roomy interior, versatile cargo space, and fuel efficiency. However, some dislike its polarizing design and lack of power.

**Engine**

* **2.4-liter 4-cylinder**

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Manual**

**No Issues Found**

* **4-speed Automatic**

**Transmission Fluid Leakage**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the transmission pan or cooler lines, causing a reduction in fluid levels.
Solution: Replace or repair leaking fluid pan or cooler lines.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

**Strut Bushing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the strut bushings, leading to clunking noises, vibrations, and poor handling.
Solution: Replace faulty strut bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Electrical Wiring Harness Rubbing**
Description/Symptoms: Electrical wiring harness rubbing against metal components, causing shorts or malfunctions.
Solution: Inspect and reposition or wrap wiring harness away from metal surfaces.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Battery Discharge**
Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely, even when the vehicle is turned off.
Solution: Test and replace faulty battery or electrical component causing parasitic draw.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Clock Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Digital clock on the dashboard not displaying correctly or going blank.
Solution: Replace faulty clock module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Scion xB has a few notable issues, primarily with its 4-speed automatic transmission and suspension. While the transmission fluid leakage issue can be costly to repair, other issues are generally minor and affordable to fix. Overall, the xB remains a reliable and practical compact hatchback, with its unique styling and spacious interior outweighing its occasional mechanical issues.
