**Introduction:**

The 2006 Scion xB is a compact hatchback known for its boxy design and spacious interior. It was offered with either a 1.5-liter 4-cylinder engine or a 2.4-liter 4-cylinder engine, paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The xB is praised for its practicality, fuel efficiency, and affordable price tag, but it has some notable drawbacks, including its poor crash test ratings and lack of features.

**Engine**

**1.5L 4-Cylinder**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating engine, misfiring
* Solution: Replace head gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or ticking noise, reduced engine power
* Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**2.4L 4-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Clutch pedal feels spongy or doesn't engage properly
* Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
* Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears
* Solution: Replace transmission fluid, repair or replace transmission
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Bearing Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Clunking or grinding noise when turning, reduced steering stability
* Solution: Replace front strut bearings
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noise over bumps, reduced stability
* Solution: Replace rear sway bar links
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system problems
* Solution: Replace alternator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Motor Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine won't crank or cranks slowly
* Solution: Replace starter motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Power door locks not working
* Solution: Replace door lock actuator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Wiper Motor Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Rear wiper not working
* Solution: Replace rear wiper motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
* Description/Symptoms: Cracking or warping of the dashboard
* Solution: No known fix, usually requires dashboard replacement
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

* **Center Console Lid Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Center console lid not closing properly, rattling
* Solution: Replace center console lid latch
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2006 Scion xB has a number of reported issues, particularly with the engine (head gasket and timing chain tensioner failure), transmission (shifting problems), and electrical (alternator and starter motor failure). Some of these issues can be costly to repair, but the overall severity of the problems received by owners can vary depending on the specific model and maintenance history of the vehicle.
