# 2016 Scion tC

### Overview
The 2016 Scion tC is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly for its segment. It was designed with a focus on affordability and practicality, which often translates to fewer complex systems prone to failure. The powertrain, a naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine paired with either a manual or automatic transmission, is a well-established unit known for its durability. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the tC typically avoids widespread, costly problems. Owners generally report satisfaction with its day-to-day operation and long-term dependability, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking a dependable compact coupe. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its reliability.
