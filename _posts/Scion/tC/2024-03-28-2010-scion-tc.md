**Introduction:**
The 2010 Scion tC is a sporty compact car with a stylish exterior and a comfortable interior. It offers a 2.4-liter inline-4 engine with 161 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The tC is known for its responsive handling, but it has received some complaints about its reliability.

**Engine:**

* **2.4-liter Inline-4**
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Premature failure of the valve cover gasket can cause an oil leak.
      * **Symptoms:** Oil leaking from the top of the engine.
      * **Solution:** Replace the valve cover gasket.
      * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
      * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause a rattling noise at startup or during acceleration.
      * **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine.
      * **Solution:** Replace the timing chain tensioner.
      * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800
      * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission:**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Difficulty Shifting Gears:** A worn-out clutch can cause difficulty shifting gears.
      * **Symptoms:** Difficulty engaging or disengaging gears.
      * **Solution:** Replace the clutch.
      * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000
      * **Recalls:** None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping:** A worn-out transmission may cause slipping or shuddering during gear changes.
      * **Symptoms:** Loss of power or delayed acceleration.
      * **Solution:** Rebuild or replace the transmission.
      * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,500
      * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior:**
* **Dashboard Warning Light Bulbing Out:** The dashboard warning lights may bulb out prematurely.
  * **Symptoms:** One or more warning lights not illuminating.
  * **Solution:** Replace the bulbs.
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $150

**Summary:**
The 2010 Scion tC has some potential reliability issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. Valve cover gasket leaks and timing chain tensioner failures have been reported. However, the interior and exterior of the vehicle seem to be well-made. The severity and cost of the issues may vary depending on the specific problem and the condition of the vehicle.
