**Introduction**

The 2013 Scion xD is a compact hatchback known for its affordability, reliability, and spacious interior. It's powered by a 1.8-liter 4-cylinder engine with 128 horsepower and 125 lb-ft of torque. The xD is offered with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. While the xD has been praised for its value and practicality, it has also faced some criticism due to its lack of performance and unrefined interior.

**Engine**

* **1.8L 4-Cylinder**
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak originating from the area around the valve cover
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak and rough idling
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $450
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Pedal Squeak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise when depressing the clutch pedal
        * Solution: Lubricate clutch release bearing
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, loss of power, and delayed gear engagement
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises when driving over bumps, excessive tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front lower control arms
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Continuous battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical component causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control not engaging or maintaining speed
    * Solution: Replace cruise control switch or module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks developing on the rear bumper, especially near the license plate area
    * Solution: Replace rear bumper
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations and rattling noises coming from the dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten loose fasteners, apply foam insulation
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Steering Wheel Buttons Malfunctioning**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel buttons not responding or working intermittently
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel buttons
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Scion xD generally faces minor issues that are relatively inexpensive to repair. The most common problems include engine gasket leaks, transmission slipping, and suspension bushing wear. However, some electrical issues, such as battery drain and cruise control failure, can be more costly to address. Overall, the xD offers a reliable and economical driving experience, with most major problems being addressed by recalls.
