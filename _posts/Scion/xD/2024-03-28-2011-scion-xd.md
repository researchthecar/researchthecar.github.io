**Introduction:**

The 2011 Scion xD is a compact hatchback offered with either a 1.8L 4-cylinder engine and 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission, or a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine and 5-speed automatic transmission. With 128 horsepower and 125 lb-ft of torque in the 1.8L engine and 158 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque in the 2.4L engine, the Scion xD offers a balance of fuel efficiency and moderate performance. While praised for its spacious interior and affordable price, the vehicle has faced some common issues over the years.

**Engine**

**1.8L 4-cylinder**

- **Leaking Valve Cover Gasket:** Oil leaks originating from the top of the engine, possibly causing a burning oil smell and engine smoke.
- **Piston Ring Failure:** Excessive oil consumption, decreased engine power, and blue smoke from the exhaust.
- **Timing Chain Guide Failure:** Engine rattles or knocking noises, potentially leading to a catastrophic engine failure.

**2.4L 4-cylinder**

- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

- **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:** Difficulty shifting gears or clutch pedal sinking to the floor.
- **Transmission Mount Failure:** Vibrations or noises when shifting or under load.

**4-speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Slippage:** Difficulty moving forward or backward, or transmission hesitation at higher speeds.
- **Valve Body Malfunction:** Rough shifting, delayed shifting, or transmission failure.
- **Torque Converter Failure:** Shudders or vibrations while accelerating or cruising.

**Suspension**

- **Loose Front Sway Bar End Links:** Knocking or rattling noises when turning or driving over bumps.
- **Premature Rear Suspension Strut Failure:** Leaking or damaged struts, affecting handling and ride quality.

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:** Dimming headlights, electrical system issues, or difficulty starting.
- **Battery Drain:** Battery discharges quickly or fails to hold a charge.
- **Clock Spring Malfunction:** Airbag warning light illumination, cruise control issues, or horn malfunction.

**Exterior**

- **Clear Coat Peeling:** Yellowing, peeling, or blistering of the clear coat paint finish.
- **Rear Spoiler Vibration:** Noises or vibrations from the rear spoiler at highway speeds.

**Interior**

- **Dash Panel Gaps:** Misaligned or rattling dash panels.
- **Center Console Armrest Breakage:** Cracking or breaking of the center console armrest plastic.
- **Door Panel Trim Cracking:** Cracking or fading of the door panel trim materials.

**Summary**

While the 2011 Scion xD offers a practical and affordable option, it has faced several issues that can be costly to repair. Engine problems, particularly with the 1.8L engine, can be severe and require major repairs. Transmission issues across both manual and automatic transmissions are also common, affecting drivability and performance. Suspension issues can affect handling and ride quality, while electrical problems can result in various system malfunctions. Exterior and interior issues, though not as severe, can contribute to the overall reliability and satisfaction of owning the vehicle.
