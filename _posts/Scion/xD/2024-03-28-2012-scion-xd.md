**Introduction:**

The 2012 Scion xD is a compact hatchback that offers a spacious interior and versatile cargo area. It comes with a 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine mated to either a five-speed manual or a four-speed automatic transmission. The xD received praise for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and practicality, but some complaints were also raised about its handling and lack of features.

**Engine**

**1.8L Four-Cylinder**

* **Engine Overheating**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine temperature gauge rising above normal, loss of engine power, coolant leaks
    * Solution: Replace faulty thermostat, repair or replace leaking hoses, flush cooling system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine coolant, rough idle, poor engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Pedal Stuttering**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clutch pedal vibrates or stutters when engaging
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder or slave cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage gears smoothly, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Replace or repair transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the rear suspension when driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace worn sway bar bushings or end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly when parked, electrical systems fail
    * Solution: Replace faulty battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks fail to operate, doors remain unlocked or locked
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling dashboard components
    * Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard screws, clips, or bolts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Scion xD has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. Some owners have reported minor issues related to the engine, transmission, and suspension, while others have encountered more significant problems that can be costly to repair. However, the severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
