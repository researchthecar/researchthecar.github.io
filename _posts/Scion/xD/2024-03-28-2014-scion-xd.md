The 2014 Scion xD is a subcompact hatchback that offers a 1.8L inline-4 engine paired with either a 6-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic transmission. It is known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**
**1.8L**

**Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**6-Speed Manual**

**Clutch Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Premature clutch wear, resulting in slipping or difficulty shifting gears.
Solution: Replace clutch assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Solenoid Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting problems, including delayed or harsh shifts, or getting stuck in gear.
Solution: Replace shift solenoids.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Alternator Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning alternator, leading to battery drain or electrical system problems.
Solution: Replace alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Roof Rack Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Roof rack crossbars detaching or breaking.
Solution: Replace roof rack crossbars or entire roof rack.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Cracking or warping of the dashboard material.
Solution: Replace dashboard.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Seat Fabric Tearing**
Description/Symptoms: Tearing or fraying of the seat fabric.
Solution: Repair or replace seat upholstery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Scion xD has had some reported issues, most notably with the engine and transmission. The oil consumption issue can be severe and costly to fix, while the transmission problems can lead to shifting issues or breakdowns. The electrical and exterior issues are less common but can still be inconvenient and expensive. Overall, the 2014 Scion xD is a budget-friendly vehicle, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
