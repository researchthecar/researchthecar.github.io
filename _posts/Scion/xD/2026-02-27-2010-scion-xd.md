### Summary Verdict
The 2010 Scion xD is a reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The powertrain, including the 1.8L engine and available transmissions, generally experiences very few issues. Suspension and exterior components are also robust. The most commonly cited, though not widespread, concerns relate to minor electrical gremlins such as intermittent power window operation or occasional battery drain, which are typically manageable and relatively inexpensive to fix. Overall, the xD offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal major mechanical or electrical problems.
