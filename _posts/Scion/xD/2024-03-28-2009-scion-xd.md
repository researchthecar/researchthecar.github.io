**Introduction**

The 2009 Scion xD is a subcompact hatchback known for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and affordable price. It comes with a single engine option, a 1.8-liter inline-4, and two transmission options: a 5-speed manual transmission and a 4-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 128 horsepower and 125 lb-ft of torque. The xD has received positive reviews for its practicality and value but has been criticized for its lackluster performance and handling.

**Faulty Window Regulators**
Description/Symptoms: The window regulators fail, causing the windows to become inoperable.

**Summary**

Overall, the 2009 Scion xD has relatively few reported issues, which is consistent with its reputation for reliability and value. The most significant issue is the cracked dashboard, which can be costly to repair. However, the xD's lackluster performance and handling may be a deterrent for some drivers.
