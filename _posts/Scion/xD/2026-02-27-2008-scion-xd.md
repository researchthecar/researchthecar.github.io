# 2008 Scion xD

### Overview
The 2008 Scion xD, a subcompact hatchback, was designed to offer a blend of practicality, fuel efficiency, and a touch of youthful style. As a Toyota product, it generally inherited a reputation for reliability, making it a popular choice for budget-conscious buyers. The xD typically came equipped with a single engine and transmission option, simplifying its mechanical makeup. While generally robust, like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that owners might encounter, providing insights into potential repair needs and associated costs.

## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Power Window Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows may operate slowly, intermittently, or stop working altogether.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Keyless Entry Fob Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Remote keyless entry system may become unresponsive or fail to lock/unlock doors.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming the key fob or replacing the fob battery. In some cases, the receiver module may need replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Exterior

### Standard Body and Trim
**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture or fogging may appear inside the headlight housings.
- **Solution:** Ensure seals are intact. Sometimes, removing the bulb and allowing it to dry with a desiccant pack can help. Replacement of the headlight assembly may be necessary if seals are compromised.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400 (per assembly)


## Interior

### Standard Interior Components
**Airbag Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light may illuminate on the dashboard, indicating a potential issue with the airbag system.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the fault, which could range from a faulty sensor, wiring issue, or clock spring. Repair or replacement of the faulty component.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Scion xD is generally considered a reliable vehicle, consistent with Toyota's reputation. The powertrain, including the 1.8L engine and available transmissions, shows no significant widespread issues. Most reported problems are minor and related to specific electrical components like power window regulators and keyless entry fobs, or occasional exterior issues such as headlight condensation. Interior electrical issues, like the airbag warning light, can occur but are not endemic. Overall, the xD offers a dependable ownership experience with manageable repair costs for the few common issues that may arise.
