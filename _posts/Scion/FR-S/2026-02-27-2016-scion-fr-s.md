# 2016 Scion FR-S

### Overview
The 2016 Scion FR-S, a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe, is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly for its target audience of driving enthusiasts. Its focus on a lightweight chassis and a naturally aspirated engine contributes to a straightforward mechanical design. While it doesn't boast the luxury features of some competitors, its interior and exterior components are typically robust. The primary appeal lies in its engaging driving dynamics, and for the most part, owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected mechanical failures. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its performance and longevity.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Scion FR-S demonstrates strong overall reliability, with no significant widespread mechanical or electrical issues commonly reported by owners. Its focused design, centered around a robust 2.0L Boxer engine and dependable transmission options (both manual and automatic), contributes to its positive reputation. Owners generally find the suspension, electrical systems, and interior/exterior components to be durable. As with any performance-oriented vehicle, consistent maintenance is recommended to ensure optimal performance and longevity, but major component failures are uncommon for this model year.
