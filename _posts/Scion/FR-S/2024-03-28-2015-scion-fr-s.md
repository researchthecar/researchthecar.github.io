**Introduction:**

The 2015 Scion FR-S is a sporty coupe known for its handling, performance, and affordability. It comes with a rear-wheel-drive layout and is powered by a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated boxer engine. The engine produces 200 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque. The FR-S is offered with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the FR-S appreciate its nimble handling, responsive steering, and engaging driving experience. However, some common complaints include its limited interior space, firm ride, and lack of low-end torque.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-Cylinder Boxer**

**Valve Spring Failure**
Description/Symptoms: At high RPMs, the valve springs can break, leading to engine damage. Symptoms include loss of power, rough idling, and misfiring.
Solution: Replace the affected valve springs and associated components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Grinding Noises**
Description/Symptoms: When shifting gears, the transmission can make grinding or crunching noises. This issue is often caused by worn or damaged synchronizers.
Solution: Replace the faulty synchronizers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Slipping Gears**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting between gears, causing a loss of power or a delayed response. This issue can be caused by worn clutches or solenoids.
Solution: Repair or replace the affected components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery drains excessively, even when the vehicle is parked. This issue can be caused by a faulty alternator or a parasitic drain.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical fault causing the drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Dashboard Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Over time, the dashboard can develop cracks, particularly around the edges. This issue is often caused by exposure to sunlight and temperature fluctuations.
Solution: Replace the affected part of the dashboard.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Scion FR-S is a fun-to-drive coupe with a few potential issues to consider. The engine valve spring failure and transmission grinding noises are the most serious concerns, potentially requiring expensive repairs. The other reported issues are less severe and should not have a significant impact on the overall reliability of the vehicle. As with any used car, it is recommended to have a thorough inspection before purchasing.
