**Introduction:**

The 2016 Volkswagen Crafter Van is a spacious and versatile cargo van offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. Its 2.0L TDI engine provides ample power and torque, while the available 4MOTION all-wheel-drive system gives it enhanced traction in challenging conditions. Positives of the Crafter Van include its comfortable cabin, impressive payload and towing capacities, and solid build quality. However, the vehicle has also been subject to some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L TDI Engine**
    * **Failed Injector Seals**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine oil leaks, rough idling, and decreased performance.
        * Solution: Replace injector seals and reprogram the engine control unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, decreased fuel economy, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Clean the intake manifold and EGR valve.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Transmission Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, difficulty shifting gears, and grinding noises.
        * Solution: Replace transmission seals or gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in shifting, jerky shifts, and slipping gears.
        * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Arms**
    * **Premature Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged suspension arms, misalignment, and uneven tire wear.
        * Solution: Replace suspension arms.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Issues**
    * **Premature Battery Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, starting problems, and electrical malfunctions.
        * Solution: Replace battery and inspect electrical system.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Gaps**
    * **Uneven Body Panel Alignment**
        * Description/Symptoms: Misaligned or loose body panels, gaps between panels.
        * Solution: Adjust body panels or replace damaged panels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks**
    * **Creaking or Rattling Noises**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noises emanating from the dashboard while driving over uneven surfaces.
        * Solution: Apply foam or felt gaskets to reduce vibration.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Cushioning**
    * **Insufficient Seat Support**
        * Description/Symptoms: Uncomfortable seats, lack of lumbar support, and fatigue on long drives.
        * Solution: Replace seat cushions or add aftermarket lumbar support.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Volkswagen Crafter Van has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While it offers several positive attributes, it has also been plagued by a variety of issues ranging from minor inconveniences to costly repairs. The most common issues affect the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and its extent.
