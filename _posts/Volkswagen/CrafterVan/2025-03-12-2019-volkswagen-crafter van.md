The 2019 Volkswagen Crafter is a popular choice for businesses and individuals seeking a versatile and capable van. It typically comes with a range of diesel engine options. One common engine is the 2.0L TDI diesel engine, which produces around 140 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual and an 8-speed automatic. Owners often praise the Crafter for its spacious cargo area, comfortable ride, and fuel efficiency. However, some common complaints include electrical issues, problems with the AdBlue system, and occasional concerns with the diesel particulate filter (DPF).

**Engine**

**2.0L TDI Diesel**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups between oil changes. Visible blue smoke from the exhaust may also be present.
    *   Solution: The fix varies depending on the cause. It may require replacing valve stem seals, piston rings, or, in severe cases, the entire engine.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: DPF blockage can cause reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption, and the DPF warning light illuminating on the dashboard. Short trips and infrequent regeneration cycles exacerbate the issue.
    *   Solution: Forced DPF regeneration by a mechanic, DPF cleaning, or DPF replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **AdBlue System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Faulty AdBlue (diesel exhaust fluid) sensors, pumps, or injectors can trigger warning lights and prevent the engine from starting.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty component (sensor, pump, or injector).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

*   **Clutch Slippage**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clutch slippage, especially under heavy loads or during acceleration, can be a problem. This can present as increased engine RPM without corresponding increase in speed.
    *   Solution: Clutch replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Rough Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report rough or jerky shifting, particularly between certain gears. This may be more pronounced when the transmission is cold.
    *   Solution: Transmission fluid flush, valve body replacement, or transmission rebuild/replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Central Locking System Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete failure of the central locking system, preventing doors from locking or unlocking remotely.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty door lock actuator, wiring harness, or central control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Infotainment System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing, crashing, or unresponsive touchscreen. Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    *   Solution: Software update, replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: ABS, ESP, or other warning lights illuminating due to faulty sensors.
    *   Solution: Identify and replace the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Premature Bushing Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Worn suspension bushings can cause clunking noises, excessive body roll, and uneven tire wear.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the worn bushings.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the sliding door. Excessive noise or binding during operation.
    * Solution: Adjust the door alignment, lubricate the rollers and tracks, or replace worn rollers or latches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling and Squeaking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Various rattles and squeaks emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or cargo area, especially when driving on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Tighten loose fasteners, apply felt tape to contact points, or replace damaged trim pieces.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

In summary, the 2019 Volkswagen Crafter can experience a range of issues, with electrical problems and engine-related concerns (particularly those stemming from the AdBlue system and DPF) being the most frequently reported. The cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the specific issue, ranging from minor fixes costing a few hundred dollars to major repairs potentially exceeding several thousand. While some problems are relatively minor annoyances, others can impact the vehicle's reliability and require prompt attention.
