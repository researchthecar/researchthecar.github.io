**Introduction to the 2008 Volkswagen Crafter Van**

The 2008 Volkswagen Crafter Van is a large commercial vehicle that was available with a wide range of engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 2.5-liter inline-five diesel engine with 109 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options included a 2.0-liter inline-four diesel engine with 136 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0-liter V6 diesel engine with 163 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options included a five-speed manual, a six-speed manual, and a six-speed automatic.

People who like the Crafter Van appreciate its spacious cargo area, powerful engines, and comfortable ride. Some drivers have complained about the Crafter Van's high fuel consumption and relatively expensive maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Inline-Five Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

* **EGR Valve Failure:** The EGR valve can fail, causing the engine to run rough and stall. Solution: Replace the EGR valve. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors can fail, causing the engine to misfire and run poorly. Solution: Replace the fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**2.0-liter Inline-Four Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Pump Failure:** The fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to stall. Solution: Replace the fuel pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**3.0-liter V6 Diesel Engine**

* **Timing Chain Failure:** The timing chain can fail, causing catastrophic engine damage. Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
* **DPF Filter Clogging:** The DPF filter can become clogged, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Clean or replace the DPF filter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gearbox Failure:** The gearbox can fail, causing the transmission to slip or become stuck in one gear. Solution: Replace the gearbox. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure:** The clutch can fail, making it difficult to shift gears. Solution: Replace the clutch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Control Unit Failure:** The transmission control unit can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not at all. Solution: Replace the transmission control unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Shock Absorber Failure:** The shock absorbers can fail, causing the ride to become harsh and unstable. Solution: Replace the shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

* **Spring Breakage:** The springs can break, causing the van to sag and become difficult to control. Solution: Replace the springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator can fail, causing the battery to discharge and the electrical system to fail. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

* **Starter Failure:** The starter can fail, preventing the engine from starting. Solution: Replace the starter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Exterior**

* **Rust:** The body panels can rust, especially in areas that are exposed to salt or moisture. Solution: Repair or replace the rusted panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:** The dashboard can crack, especially in areas that are exposed to sunlight. Solution: Replace the dashboard. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

* **Door Lock Failure:** The door locks can fail, making it difficult to open or close the doors. Solution: Replace the door locks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2008 Volkswagen Crafter Van is a spacious and powerful commercial vehicle, but it is not without its problems. Some of the most common issues include turbocharger failure, EGR valve failure, and timing chain failure. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to factor in the cost of potential repairs when considering purchasing a Crafter Van.
