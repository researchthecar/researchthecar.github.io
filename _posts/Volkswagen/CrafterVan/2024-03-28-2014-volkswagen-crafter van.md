**Introduction:**
The 2014 Volkswagen Crafter Van is a versatile and capable full-size commercial van that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It boasts a spacious cargo area, a comfortable cabin, and a robust build quality. However, like any vehicle, it has also been subject to its fair share of issues. Here's a comprehensive list of specific problems reported by owners.

**Engine:**

- **2.0L TDI (102 hp):**
  - **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, resulting in a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger and associated parts.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
  - **Fuel injector issues:** The fuel injectors may become clogged or malfunction, leading to engine misfires and reduced performance.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the affected fuel injectors.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500 per injector

- **2.0L TDI (140 hp):**
  - **Emissions system issues:** The emissions control system, including the EGR valve, can experience problems, resulting in increased emissions and potential engine damage.
    - **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve and related components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
  - **Timing belt failure:** The timing belt can fail prematurely, leading to catastrophic engine damage.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the timing belt and associated components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+

- **2.5L TDI (163 hp):**
  - **Cylinder head gasket failure:** The cylinder head gasket can fail, resulting in coolant leaks and engine overheating.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the cylinder head gasket.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,000+
  - **Transmission control module (TCM) failure:** The TCM may fail, leading to transmission problems and potential vehicle immobility.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200+

**Transmission:**

- **6-Speed Manual:**
  - **Clutch wear:** The clutch may wear prematurely, resulting in slipping or difficulty shifting gears.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch and associated components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

- **Automatic (6-Speed or 8-Speed):**
  - **Torque converter shudder:** The torque converter may experience vibration or shuddering, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter and associated components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
  - **Gear slipping:** The transmission may slip gears, resulting in a loss of power or difficulty accelerating.
    - **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission or affected components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $5,000+

**Suspension:**

- **Rear leaf springs:** The rear leaf springs can weaken or break, affecting the vehicle's handling and stability.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the affected leaf springs.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600 per spring

**Electrical:**

- **Starter failure:** The starter motor may fail to engage, preventing the engine from starting.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the starter motor.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+

**Exterior:**

- **Rear door latch issues:** The rear door latches may fail to engage or open properly.
    - **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the door latches.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500 per latch
- **Tail light condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the tail lights, affecting their visibility.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the affected tail lights.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200 per tail light

**Interior:**

- **Dashboard rattle:** The dashboard may rattle or vibrate excessively, creating an annoying noise.
    - **Solution:** Tightening or adjustment of the dashboard components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+

**Summary:**
The 2014 Volkswagen Crafter Van is a reliable workhorse but is not immune to certain issues. The severity and cost of these problems vary depending on the component affected. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more serious and require significant repairs. It's essential for owners to address these issues promptly to maintain the vehicle's performance and safety.
