**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volkswagen Crafter Van is a rugged and versatile workhorse designed to meet the demands of commercial use. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged diesel engine that produces 161 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or an optional 8-speed automatic transmission. The Crafter Van is praised for its spacious cargo area, comfortable cabin, and strong performance. However, it has also faced some issues, some of which have led to recalls.

### **Engine**

#### **2.0-Liter Turbocharged Diesel**

- **Fuel Injector Failure:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Leaking fuel injectors can cause rough idling, loss of power, and increased emissions.
 - Solution: Replacing the faulty fuel injectors.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 - Recalls: N/A

- **EGR Valve Problems:**
 - Description/Symptoms: A faulty EGR valve can lead to increased emissions, poor engine performance, and reduced fuel efficiency.
 - Solution: Cleaning or replacing the EGR valve.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
 - Recalls: N/A

### **Transmission**

#### **6-Speed Manual**

- **Clutch Issues:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Worn clutch plates can lead to slipping, difficulty shifting, and loss of power.
 - Solution: Replacing the clutch assembly.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
 - Recalls: N/A

#### **8-Speed Automatic**

- **Valve Body Problems:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Faulty valve bodies can cause delayed or improper gear shifts, as well as transmission slipping.
 - Solution: Replacing the valve body.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
 - Recalls: N/A

### **Suspension**

- **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Broken leaf springs can lead to a sagging rear end, reduced handling, and increased tire wear.
 - Solution: Replacing the faulty leaf springs.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
 - Recalls: N/A

- **Shock Absorber Failure:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Worn shock absorbers can cause poor ride quality, increased noise, and reduced stability.
 - Solution: Replacing the faulty shock absorbers.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
 - Recalls: N/A

### **Electrical**

- **Electrical Harness Issues:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Faulty wiring harnesses can lead to various electrical problems, such as malfunctioning lights, gauges, and accessories.
 - Solution: Repairing or replacing the faulty harness.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
 - Recalls: N/A

- **Battery Drain:**
 - Description/Symptoms: A parasitic battery drain can discharge the battery even when the vehicle is not in use.
 - Solution: Identifying and addressing the source of the drain.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
 - Recalls: N/A

### **Exterior**

- **Side Mirror Issues:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Power side mirrors may malfunction, resulting in poor visibility or broken glass.
 - Solution: Replacing the faulty side mirror assembly.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
 - Recalls: N/A

- **Rear Door Lock Problems:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Faulty rear door locks may prevent the doors from opening or closing properly.
 - Solution: Replacing the rear door lock assembly.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
 - Recalls: N/A

### **Interior**

- **Dashboard Panel Detachment:**
 - Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel may detach from the vehicle, creating a safety hazard.
 - Solution: Replacing the dashboard panel.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 - Recalls: VW21V-530
- **Steering Wheel Issues:**
 - Description/Symptoms: The steering wheel may vibrate excessively or become loose.
 - Solution: Replacing the faulty steering wheel or components.
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
 - Recalls: VW20V-725

**Summary:**

The 2017 Volkswagen Crafter Van has been impacted by several issues, ranging from minor inconveniences to more serious mechanical problems. The most common issues relate to the engine, transmission, and suspension. While some issues are relatively inexpensive to repair, others can be quite costly. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and take preventive measures to minimize the risk of breakdowns.
