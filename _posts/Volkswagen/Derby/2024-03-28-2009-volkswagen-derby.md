**Introduction:**

The 2009 Volkswagen Derby is a compact sedan known for its fuel efficiency and spacious interior. It came with two engine options: a 1.2-liter 3-cylinder and a 1.4-liter 4-cylinder, both mated to a 5-speed manual or optional 6-speed automatic transmission. The 1.2-liter engine produces 74 horsepower and 73 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.4-liter engine generates 84 horsepower and 93 lb-ft of torque. Drivers appreciate the car's low operating costs, but some have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**

**1.2-Liter 3-Cylinder**

* **Timing Chain Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise from the engine when starting, idling, or accelerating.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, difficulty starting, and rough idling.
    * Solution: Remove and clean the intake valves manually.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800

**1.4-Liter 4-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Delays:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update the transmission control module (TCM) software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Axle Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A clunking or squealing noise when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the worn rear axle bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges quickly, even when the car is parked.
    * Solution: Locate and fix the source of the electrical draw (e.g., parasitic load from aftermarket accessories).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, flickering gauges, and difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlights.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+

**Interior**

* **Window Regulator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows may fail to operate or make excessive noise.
    * Solution: Replace the window regulator motor or mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $450

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2009 Volkswagen Derby has received mixed reviews. While it offers good fuel economy and a spacious interior, some owners have reported issues with timing chain rattle, transmission shift delays, and electrical problems. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost of repairs can range from minor to expensive. Potential buyers should consider carefully the potential maintenance costs before purchasing this vehicle.
