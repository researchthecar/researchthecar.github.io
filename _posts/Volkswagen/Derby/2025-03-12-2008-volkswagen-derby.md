The 2008 Volkswagen Derby, primarily sold in Mexico, is a subcompact sedan known for its fuel efficiency and affordability. The 2008 model year Derby was sold with a 1.8L inline 4-cylinder engine rated at 100 horsepower and 125 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. Owners often appreciate the Derby for its practicality and low running costs, but common complaints revolve around build quality and basic features.

**Engine**

**1.8L Inline 4-Cylinder**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks may occur from the valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket, or rear main seal. Signs include oil spots under the car, a burning oil smell, and low oil levels.
    *   Solution: Replacing the leaking gasket or seal.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $250+ depending on the source of the leak.

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks can develop from hoses, the water pump, or the radiator. Symptoms include a sweet smell, low coolant levels, and overheating.
    *   Solution: Replacing the leaking hose, water pump, or radiator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+ depending on the part.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

*   **Shifting Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting into gear, grinding noises when shifting, or the transmission popping out of gear. Could be caused by worn synchros or a damaged shift linkage.
    *   Solution: Replacing the synchros, repairing or replacing the shift linkage, or a complete transmission rebuild/replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1500+ depending on the severity.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Worn Suspension Components**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, bouncy ride, uneven tire wear, and poor handling. Can be caused by worn shocks/struts, ball joints, tie rod ends, or bushings.
    *   Solution: Replacing the worn shocks/struts, ball joints, tie rod ends, or bushings.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $600+ depending on the components being replaced.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Faulty Window Regulator**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Window not going up or down
    *   Solution: Replacing the window regulator
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Faulty Wiper Motor**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Windshield wipers do not work
    *   Solution: Replacing the wiper motor
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50-$200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on body panels, especially around wheel wells, door edges, and undercarriage.
    *   Solution: Repairing or replacing rusted panels, applying rust inhibitors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+ depending on severity and location.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Faulty Door Handle**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The door handle breaks, preventing the door from opening
    *   Solution: Replacing the door handle
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2008 Volkswagen Derby, while appreciated for its fuel efficiency, presents some common issues that potential buyers should be aware of. Engine and transmission issues, while not universally prevalent, can lead to moderate repair costs. Suspension and electrical problems, along with potential rust concerns, further contribute to the maintenance considerations for this vehicle. Overall, owning a 2008 Derby might involve relatively affordable repairs, but careful inspection and maintenance are essential.
