**Introduction:**

The 1999 Volkswagen Derby is a compact sedan that offered a balance of practicality and affordability. It came with a choice of either a 1.4L or 1.6L engine and a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Derby was known for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and lively handling. However, it also had some reliability issues that potential owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**1.4L Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, rough idling, stalls
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket and bolts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Belt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screeching or whining noise from engine, loss of power, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace timing belt and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.6L Engine**

* **Oil Leaks from Cylinder Head Cover Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from top of engine, burning oil smell
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Coolant Thermostat Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, temperature gauge fluctuations
    * Solution: Replace coolant thermostat
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace clutch and pressure plate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping
    * Solution: Replace transmission solenoid pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Lock-up Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering during acceleration, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Struts/Shocks Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, reduced handling, noise over bumps
    * Solution: Replace suspension struts/shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400 per side
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Springs Sag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sits lower in the rear, reduced load capacity
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system not charging, vehicle stalls
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Clear Coat Peel:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sun damage, paint fading or chipping
    * Solution: None, cosmetic issue only
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard surface
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Retractor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt does not fully retract or lock
    * Solution: Replace seat belt retractor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300 per seat
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1999 Volkswagen Derby has several reliability issues that potential owners should be aware of. The most severe and costly issues include engine timing belt failure, transmission torque converter lock-up clutch failure, and alternator failure. However, many of the other issues are relatively inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Derby is a practical and affordable compact sedan, but it is important to factor in the potential repair costs before making a purchase decision.
