**2021 Volkswagen Robust**

The 2021 Volkswagen Robust is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable and spacious interior, a sturdy and reliable engine, and a variety of advanced safety and technology features. It comes standard with a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 1.4L turbocharged inline-4 engine with 158 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed manual or an 8-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.6L V6 engine with 276 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Robust has a roomy interior with plenty of legroom and headroom for both front and rear passengers. It also comes with a variety of infotainment and connectivity features, including a touchscreen display, Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility, and a navigation system.

However, there have been some issues reported by owners with the 2021 Volkswagen Robust.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause reduced engine performance, rough idling, and misfires.
        * Solution: Cleaning or replacing the intake valves and decarbonizing the engine.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        *Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail due to a variety of issues, such as oil leaks, bearing damage, or wastegate problems. This can lead to a loss of power, reduced fuel efficiency, and increased exhaust smoke.
        * Solution: Replacing the turbocharger.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        *Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifts, or may refuse to shift gears altogether. This can be caused by a variety of issues, such as faulty sensors, solenoids, or clutches.
        * Solution: Replacing the faulty components or reprogramming the transmission control unit.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        *Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The headlights may fail to turn on or may flicker intermittently. This can be caused by a variety of issues, such as faulty bulbs, wiring problems, or a malfunctioning headlight control module.
        * Solution: Replacing the faulty components.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        *Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use. This can be caused by a variety of issues, such as faulty electrical components, parasitic drains, or a weak battery.
        * Solution: Diagnosing the source of the drain and repairing or replacing the faulty components.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        *Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rust may develop on the body panels, especially in areas that are exposed to moisture and salt.
        * Solution: Repairing or replacing the affected body panels.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        *Recalls: None Found
* **Paint Defects**
        * Description/Symptoms: The paint may peel, chip, or fade prematurely.
        * Solution: Repairing or repainting the affected areas.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        *Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
        * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, such as freezing, crashing, or displaying error messages.
        * Solution: Updating the software or replacing the infotainment unit.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        *Recalls: None Found
* **Leather Seat Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: The leather seats may crack or tear prematurely.
        * Solution: Replacing the affected seat covers.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        *Recalls: None Found
* **Squeaky Interior**
        * Description/Symptoms: The interior may develop squeaks or rattles, especially over rough roads.
        * Solution: Identifying and устранениеing the source of the noise.
        *Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        *Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Volkswagen Robust is a reliable and capable SUV, but it has had some reported issues with its engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, and some may be more costly to repair than others. It is important to weigh the potential costs and risks when considering purchasing or owning a 2021 Volkswagen Robust.
