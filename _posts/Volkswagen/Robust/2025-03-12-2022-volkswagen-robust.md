The 2022 Volkswagen Taos Robust is a compact SUV known for its fuel efficiency and spacious interior for its class. It generally receives positive reviews for its comfortable ride and user-friendly technology. Some common criticisms include the sometimes sluggish acceleration and the slightly uninspired interior design. The 2022 Taos comes standard with a 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 158 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It is available with either an 8-speed automatic transmission (front-wheel drive models) or a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (all-wheel drive models).

**Engine**

**1.5L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the engine, loss of power, check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil level warning light, needing to add oil frequently between oil changes.
    * Solution: Depending on the severity, possible solutions range from replacing the PCV valve to piston ring replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Coolant Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak near the water pump housing, needing to top off coolant often, possible overheating.
    * Solution: Replace water pump and/or related coolant hoses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (Front-Wheel Drive Models)**

* **Rough Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh or jerky shifts, especially between 1st and 2nd gear.
    * Solution: Transmission software update or transmission fluid change. In some cases, valve body replacement may be required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (All-Wheel Drive Models)**

* **Hesitation/Jerking at Low Speeds**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or jerky movement when accelerating from a stop or at low speeds, particularly in stop-and-go traffic.
    * Solution: Software update, clutch replacement, or in severe cases, complete transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes, becomes unresponsive, or crashes frequently.
    * Solution: Software update, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sensor Malfunctions (e.g., Parking Sensors, Blind Spot Monitoring)**
    * Description/Symptoms: Parking sensors provide false readings or fail to activate. Blind spot monitoring system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per sensor
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof fails to open or close properly. Excessive wind noise when closed.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the sunroof motor, tracks, or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Rattling Noises**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises from the dashboard or door panels, especially when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Identifying the source of the rattle and applying foam or felt padding to secure loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Volkswagen Taos Robust presents a mix of potential issues. Engine problems, particularly turbocharger failures and oil consumption, can be costly to address. Transmission issues, particularly with the dual-clutch transmission in AWD models, are also a concern. While some issues are minor and easily addressed, others can be more significant, potentially leading to substantial repair bills. Overall, be aware of these potential problems when considering a used 2022 Volkswagen Taos Robust and perform a thorough inspection or pre-purchase inspection.
