**Introduction:**

The 2022 Volkswagen Robust, known for its rugged exterior and versatile interior, is equipped with various engine and transmission options. The base model features a 2.0L inline-4 engine with 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. A more powerful 2.5L inline-5 engine generating 230 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque is available, along with an optional 9-speed automatic transmission. While the Robust is praised for its practicality and off-road capabilities, certain issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4 Engine**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine burns an excessive amount of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: The engine may need to be repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, resulting in reduced engine performance and increased exhaust emissions.
    * Solution: The turbocharger needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
    
**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, causing jerking or hesitation.
    * Solution: The transmission fluid may need to be changed or the transmission may need to be repaired/replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, especially during cold weather.
    * Solution: The battery may need to be replaced or the electrical system needs to be inspected for parasitic drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
    
* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, such as freezing or lagging.
    * Solution: The infotainment system software may need to be updated or the system may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle or squeak over rough surfaces.
    * Solution: The dashboard may need to be tightened or cushioned.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, leading to ineffective heating/cooling.
    * Solution: The climate control components may need to be inspected or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the issues reported for the 2022 Volkswagen Robust are not overly severe and can be expected with any vehicle. The engine and transmission problems are the most concerning, as they can lead to potential breakdowns and expensive repairs. The other issues, such as electrical glitches and interior rattles, are more common and relatively inexpensive to fix.
