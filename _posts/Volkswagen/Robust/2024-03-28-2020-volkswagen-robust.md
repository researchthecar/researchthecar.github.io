**Introduction**

The 2020 Volkswagen Robust is a midsize SUV available with a choice of two gasoline engines and two transmission options. The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 230 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.6-liter V6 engine produces 276 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque. The 2.0-liter engine comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission, while the 3.6-liter engine comes standard with a six-speed automatic transmission. All-wheel drive is available on both models.

The Robust is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and excellent fuel economy. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of off-road capability and its high price.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Premature Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or knocking, lack of power, and potential catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain and components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed or Rough Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, delayed engagement, or difficulty shifting gears.
    * Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid dripping from the transmission, low fluid level, and potential transmission damage.
    * Solution: Replacing the leaking component or the entire transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noises from the front suspension, uneven tire wear, and impaired handling.
    * Solution: Replacing the strut bearing assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor handling, loss of traction, and potential damage to other suspension components.
    * Solution: Replacing the shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing or unresponsive, audio system malfunction, or navigation issues.
    * Solution: Updating the software or replacing the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof failing to open or close properly, or getting stuck.
    * Solution: Replacing the sunroof motor or other faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V458000

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: HVAC system not blowing cold air, temperature fluctuations, or other issues.
    * Solution: Replacing the climate control module or other faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Bolster Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or cracking of the seat bolster material, especially in the driver's seat.
    * Solution: Replacing the seat bolster or the entire seat.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Volkswagen Robust is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. Most of the issues are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension, and they can be expensive to fix. The severity of these issues can range from minor annoyances to potential safety hazards. Overall, the Robust is a good choice for a spacious and comfortable SUV, but potential buyers should carefully consider the potential costs of repairs before making a decision.
