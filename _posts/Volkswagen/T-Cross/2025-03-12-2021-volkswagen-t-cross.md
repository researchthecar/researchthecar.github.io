The 2021 Volkswagen T-Cross is a subcompact crossover SUV known for its stylish design, fuel efficiency, and nimble handling. Engine options for the 2021 model year include a 1.0-liter TSI three-cylinder engine producing around 95 horsepower and 128 lb-ft of torque, a more powerful 1.0-liter TSI with around 110 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque, and a 1.5-liter TSI four-cylinder engine delivering about 150 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. Transmission choices include a 5-speed manual, a 6-speed manual, and a 7-speed DSG dual-clutch automatic. Owners generally appreciate the T-Cross's comfortable ride, well-designed interior, and practicality for city driving. However, some criticisms include limited rear passenger space and the performance of the base engine being somewhat underpowered.

**Engine**

**1.0L TSI (95 HP/110 HP)**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the engine, loss of power, check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring engine, rough idle, check engine light illuminated.
    * Solution: Replacement of the faulty ignition coil(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.5L TSI (150 HP)**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil level warning, noticeable oil consumption requiring frequent topping up.
    * Solution: In some cases, replacement of piston rings or engine block may be required, while other cases can be fixed with updated PCV valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed DSG (Dual-Clutch Automatic)**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerky shifting, delayed engagement, inability to select gears, warning lights on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replacement or reprogramming of the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Clutch Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation during acceleration, RPM flare without corresponding speed increase, rough shifting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the clutch pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: System freezes, unresponsive touchscreen, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation problems.
    * Solution: Software updates, replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software update) - $1000+ (Replacement)
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sensor Failures (e.g., Parking Sensors, ABS Sensors)**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warning lights on the dashboard, malfunctioning driver-assistance systems.
    * Solution: Replacement of the faulty sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ per sensor
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various rattles and squeaks emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or seats, especially when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Tightening of loose components, application of sound-dampening materials.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+ (depending on severity and source)
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Air Conditioning Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent air conditioning performance, weak airflow, strange noises from the AC compressor.
    * Solution: Recharge the AC system, replace compressor, or any other parts that could be the cause of the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Volkswagen T-Cross experiences a range of issues, from relatively minor electrical glitches and interior noises to more significant engine and transmission problems. Engine issues, particularly turbocharger failure and oil consumption, can be expensive to repair. DSG transmission problems, such as mechatronic unit failure, are also costly concerns. While the T-Cross offers a stylish and practical package, potential buyers should be aware of these common issues and consider a thorough inspection before purchasing. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor annoyances and others requiring substantial investment for repairs.
