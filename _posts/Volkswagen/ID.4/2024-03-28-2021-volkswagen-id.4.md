**Introduction:**

The 2021 Volkswagen ID.4 is an all-electric compact SUV that offers a modern and spacious interior. It comes equipped with a single electric motor paired with a single-speed transmission. The electric motor produces 201 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. The ID.4 is praised for its impressive range, comfortable ride, and advanced technology. However, it does have some notable issues that potential owners should be aware of.

**Suspension**

* **Noisy Suspension**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise or clunking sounds coming from the suspension, especially over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replacing suspension components such as struts, shocks, or control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and replacing faulty electrical components or software updates.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Software Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues with the infotainment system, dashboard display, or other electronic features.
    * Solution: Software updates or reflashes.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Wind Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise entering the cabin, especially at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Replacing or adjusting weatherstripping or sealing around doors and windows.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, either manually or with the remote key.
    * Solution: Replacing door lock actuators or latch mechanisms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Loose Dashboard Panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard panels or trim pieces rattle or vibrate, causing annoying noises.
    * Solution: Securing loose components or replacing faulty panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Adjustment Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power or manual seat adjustments become inoperable or erratic.
    * Solution: Replacing faulty seat motors or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Volkswagen ID.4 is an impressive electric SUV, it does have some reported issues that potential owners should consider. The suspension noise, electrical glitches, and interior issues are among the most common problems. While the severity and cost of these issues vary, they can impact the overall ownership experience. It is recommended that prospective buyers thoroughly research these issues before making a purchase decision.
