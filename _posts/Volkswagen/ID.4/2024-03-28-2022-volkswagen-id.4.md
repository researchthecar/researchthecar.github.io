# Volkswagen ID.4 (2022) Complaints and Issues

The 2022 Volkswagen ID.4 is an electric SUV with an EPA-estimated range of up to 275 miles on a single charge. It boasts a sleek exterior, spacious interior, and advanced technology features. However, it has also drawn some criticism from owners. Here's a comprehensive list of the reported issues:

**Electric Motor**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Spring Breakage**
Description/Symptoms: The rear coil springs may break, causing the vehicle to sit lower in the rear and potentially affecting handling.
Solution: Replace the faulty springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 22V-358

**Electrical**

* **12-Volt Battery Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The 12-volt battery may fail prematurely, causing electrical problems such as difficulty starting the vehicle or malfunctioning accessories.
Solution: Replace the faulty battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

* **Headlight Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The headlights may flicker or fail completely.
Solution: Replace the affected headlight assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: The windshield may crack spontaneously or due to minor impacts.
Solution: Replace the windshield.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

* **Side Mirror Damage**
Description/Symptoms: The side mirrors may be easily damaged by impacts or even gentle nudges.
Solution: Replace the damaged mirror.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattling**
Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof may rattle or leak when driving.
Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof mechanism.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

* **Dashboard Squeaks**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may creak or squeak when driving over uneven surfaces.
Solution: Apply sound dampening material or tighten loose components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary**

While the 2022 Volkswagen ID.4 offers a comfortable and technology-rich driving experience, it has encountered some issues. Most of the reported problems are related to electrical glitches, exterior damage, and interior noises. The severity of the issues varies, with some requiring costly repairs. However, it is important to note that many owners have praised the ID.4 for its performance and overall value.
