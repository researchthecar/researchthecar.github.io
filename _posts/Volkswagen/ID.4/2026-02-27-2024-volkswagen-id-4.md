# 2024 Volkswagen ID.4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Volkswagen ID.4 is a modern all-electric crossover offering smooth driving dynamics, a spacious cabin, and competitive battery range options. However, early owner reports submitted to safety regulators highlight several recurring trouble spots—most notably concerning electrical glitches, software freezes, and exterior door handle malfunctions. Prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and ensure all relevant manufacturer campaigns have been performed before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### Single-Motor Electric Powertrain

Rear-wheel-drive configurations feature a permanent magnet synchronous motor producing approximately 201 to 282 horsepower depending on the battery pack. This rear-mounted electric drive unit delivers seamless acceleration with minimal mechanical complexity, though owners occasionally report high-voltage battery or propulsion warnings.

### Dual-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

All-wheel-drive models add an asynchronous front motor to boost total output to around 335 horsepower. This setup provides confident traction in inclement weather while maintaining strong overall reliability in everyday driving conditions.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Equipped with a single-speed automatic reduction gearbox that delivers seamless power delivery without traditional gear shifts. This direct-drive system is mechanically simple and generally trouble-free.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

The braking setup combines regenerative deceleration with traditional hydraulic friction brakes to safely scrub speed.

**Brake Pedal Travel and Pressure Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the brake pedal traveled unusually far toward the floorboard with reduced immediate stopping pressure.
- **Solution:** Have the brake hydraulic system inspected immediately for pressure leaks or bleeding issues by an authorized technician.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display System

Electrical architecture governs the cabin screens, driver assistance modules, and charging systems, occasionally prone to software bugs and temporary freezing.

**Infotainment Reboot and Screen Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment display and driver instrument cluster may suddenly freeze, go blank, or reboot while driving, temporarily restricting access to vehicle speed and navigation.
- **Solution:** Allow the vehicle system to power down and reset, or visit a dealership for software updates if glitches persist.

## Exterior

### Door Handles and Locking Mechanisms

Exterior hardware relies heavily on electronic touch sensors and power actuators, which have proved sensitive to moisture and hardware wear.

**Door Lock Actuator and Handle Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Doors may fail to unlock properly, automatically re-lock immediately upon opening, or exhibit dead spots in electronic handle touch sensors.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door handle assemblies or updated latch actuators through authorized service channels.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V750000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The interior sunshade for the panoramic glass roof may be insufficiently fire retardant. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 302, "Flammability of Interior Materials." In a fire, fabric that is not fire retardant may further fuel a fire, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply flame retardant to the sunshade fabric, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V643000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An improperly secured air bag inflator may not deploy as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V651000: Structure:Body:Door:Handle:Exterior](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The door handles may allow water to enter the circuit board assembly, which can cause the doors to open unexpectedly. A vehicle door that opens unexpectedly while driving increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen dealers will inspect and replace the door handles as necessary, and update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V125000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the OCDC, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V434000: Service Brakes](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Certain bolts on the chassis, brake system and/or suspension (such as bolts for certain cross member, engine mount, control arm, subframe, rear brake carrier, etc.) of affected vehicles may not have been correctly tightened during the manufacturing process. A loss of vehicle control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the bolts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V836000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The high voltage (HV) battery cell modules may contain misaligned electrodes that can result in a battery fire. A vehicle fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high voltage battery cell modules as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V028000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The high voltage (HV) battery cell modules may contain misaligned electrodes that can result in a battery fire. A vehicle fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high voltage battery cell modules as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V030000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A high-voltage battery that overheats increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the high-voltage battery software, and replace the high-voltage battery, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Volkswagen ID.4 is an appealing electric crossover for daily commuting, but it requires careful vetting regarding electrical software stability and past warranty repairs. Used car shoppers should verify that all open campaigns have been completed by a dealer and thoroughly test drive the vehicle to check infotainment responsiveness and brake feel.
