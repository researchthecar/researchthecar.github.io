The 2023 Volkswagen ID.4 is an all-electric compact SUV that offers a blend of practicality, comfort, and modern technology. It comes in both rear-wheel drive (RWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD) configurations. The RWD models produce around 201 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. The AWD models boost that to around 295 horsepower and 339 lb-ft of torque. Some owners appreciate the ID.4 for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and ample cargo space, along with the benefits of electric vehicle ownership such as lower running costs and environmental friendliness. Common complaints revolve around the infotainment system's responsiveness and complexity, as well as some concerns about software glitches and charging inconsistencies.

**Electrical System**

*   **Infotainment System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: System freezes, slow response times, random reboots, and unresponsive touch screen. Wireless Apple CarPlay/Android Auto connectivity problems are common.
    *   Solution: Software updates may fix some issues. In other cases, the infotainment unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Charging Port Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure to charge. Charging port not releasing the cable when complete or after interrupting charging. Error messages related to charging system.
    * Solution: Could need a software update, or replacement of the charging port.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix:
    * Recalls: None Found

*   **12V Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Premature drain of the 12V battery, leading to the vehicle being unable to start. This can be caused by software glitches or excessive drain from the vehicle's systems when parked.
    *   Solution: Software update to manage battery drain, or replacement of the 12V battery.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:

**Exterior**

*   **Rear Light Bar Cracking/Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing in the rear light bar that spans the tailgate. Light emitting diode (LED) failure with portions of the light bar not illuminating.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the rear light bar assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or rear cargo area while driving.
    *   Solution: Identifying and securing the source of the rattle with sound-deadening material or replacement of loose trim pieces.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix:
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Volkswagen ID.4 experiences a few notable issues. The electrical issues related to the infotainment system and charging port are the most frequently reported, often requiring software updates or component replacement. While some issues are minor annoyances, others, such as motor failure, can be more severe.
