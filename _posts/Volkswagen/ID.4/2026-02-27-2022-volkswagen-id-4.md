# 2022 Volkswagen ID.4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volkswagen ID.4 brings all-electric driving to the compact crossover market with a comfortable ride, spacious cabin, and quiet operation. However, prospective buyers should look closely at its reliability profile, as early model software glitches, electrical issues, and specific mechanical concerns have surfaced in owner reports. While many owners enjoy daily commuting, navigating the vehicle's tech suite and checking for completed software and hardware updates is recommended.

---

## Engine

### Single-Motor Permanent Magnet Synchronous (Rear-Wheel Drive)

This rear-wheel-drive powertrain option utilizes a single permanent magnet synchronous motor producing approximately 201 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. It delivers smooth, linear acceleration suited for daily commuting, though early software updates are vital for optimal system stability.

### Dual-Motor Permanent Magnet Synchronous / Asynchronous (All-Wheel Drive)

The dual-motor setup adds a front asynchronous motor to boost total output to approximately 295 horsepower while providing all-wheel-drive capability. It offers brisk acceleration and enhanced traction in adverse weather, though complex software integrations require careful attention.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Electric vehicles utilize a single-speed direct-drive transmission that delivers seamless power delivery without traditional gear shifts. This system is mechanically simple and generally robust, though occasional electronic shift-by-wire communications require software calibration.

## Electrical

### Electric Drive Control System

The electric drive control architecture manages power delivery, but software communication faults can occasionally force the vehicle into neutral.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle displays error messages and unexpectedly shifts into neutral while driving, refusing to re-engage drive or regenerative braking modes.
- **Solution:** Perform manufacturer software updates and inspect the electric drive control module.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuator

Electrical components govern key convenience features, though owners have reported recurring door lock and infotainment anomalies.

**Door Lock Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors may fail to lock or unlock properly, or door locks may behave erratically with the central console and key fob.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door lock actuators or update relevant body control modules.

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems generally perform well, though isolated thermal and charging events have caused daytime running light dimming.

**Headlight and Daytime Running Light Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Daytime running lights or headlights may remain dim or fail entirely after high-output charging sessions.
- **Solution:** Dealership technicians may need to replace affected lighting assemblies or modules.

## Safety

### Forward Collision / Driver Assistance

Advanced safety systems provide helpful driver aids, though sensor miscommunications can trigger false braking events.

**Automated Braking and Forward Collision Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected automated brake application or cruise control system faults during highway driving.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect sensor calibrations and apply the latest driver assistance software patches.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V764000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera with a delayed or deactivated image reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle's software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V823000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V312000: Structure:Body:Door:Handle:Exterior](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles may have been manufactured with door handles that allow water to enter the circuit board assembly, which can cause the doors to open unexpectedly when the vehicle is subject to high lateral forces at speeds below 9 mph/15 kmh. A vehicle door that opens unexpectedly while the vehicle is in motion increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle software, and inspect the door handles as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V436000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera with a delayed or deactivated image may reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V344000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 101, "Control and Displays" and 111, "Rear Visibility." Displays that do not show critical information, such as vehicle speed or the rearview camera image, increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V651000: Structure:Body:Door:Handle:Exterior](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The door handles may allow water to enter the circuit board assembly, which can cause the doors to open unexpectedly. A vehicle door that opens unexpectedly while driving increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen dealers will inspect and replace the door handles as necessary, and update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V120000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=ID.4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The instrument panel may not properly display the transmission gear lever indicator. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 102, "Transmission Shift Lever Sequence, Starter Interlock, and Transmission Braking Effect." Failure to display the gear position may result in a vehicle rollaway.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the brake control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volkswagen ID.4 is a compelling option for buyers transitioning to electric mobility, offering a smooth ride and versatile cabin space. However, prospective owners should verify that all software recalls and updates have been completed to mitigate reported electrical glitches, infotainment reboots, and drive-control quirks. A thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and door hardware will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
