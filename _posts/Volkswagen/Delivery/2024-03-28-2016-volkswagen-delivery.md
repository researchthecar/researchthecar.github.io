**Introduction**

The 2016 Volkswagen Delivery is a versatile cargo van designed for commercial use. It offers a spacious cargo area, various engine and transmission options, and a range of features to enhance productivity. While praised for its performance and utility, the Delivery has also encountered a few issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**2.0L TDI 4-cylinder Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Premature failure of the fuel injectors can lead to engine power loss, rough idle, and increased emissions.
  * Solution: Replacement of affected injectors.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:** Worn or faulty clutches can cause gear slipping, difficulty shifting, or complete clutch failure.
  * Solution: Replacement of the clutch assembly.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V606000

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:** Heavy loads or rough road conditions can cause the leaf springs to crack or break, affecting handling and stability.
  * Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
* **Shock Absorber Failure:** Worn or damaged shock absorbers can lead to a bumpy ride, reduced handling, and increased wear on other suspension components.
  * Solution: Replacement of the shock absorbers.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Electrical components drawing excess current can lead to a drained battery.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the electrical component causing the discharge.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue.

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:** Exposure to road salt and moisture can cause rust formation on the metal parts of the undercarriage.
  * Solution: Rust prevention measures, such as applying a rust inhibitor or regular washing and drying.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of rust.
* **Tailgate Lock Failure:** Faulty tailgate locks can prevent the tailgate from opening or locking properly.
  * Solution: Replacement of the tailgate lock mechanism.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+

**Interior**

* **Trim Panel Rattle:** Loose or poorly fitted trim panels can rattle or vibrate during driving.
  * Solution: Retightening or replacement of the affected trim panels.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Summary**

The 2016 Volkswagen Delivery is a capable cargo van with a few reported issues. The most significant issues are related to the fuel injectors and clutch, which can lead to costly repairs. Other issues are generally minor and can be addressed through routine maintenance or repairs. Overall, the Delivery provides a reliable and efficient solution for commercial needs, but owners should be aware of potential issues to ensure proper maintenance and avoid unexpected expenses.
