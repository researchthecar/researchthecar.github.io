## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start after sitting, dim lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find parasitic draw, replacement of faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming headlights, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Volkswagen Delivery demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly with its manual transmission options and core mechanical components. The diesel engines are generally robust. The primary areas to monitor are the DSG automatic transmission's mechatronic unit, which can be a significant repair cost, and potential electrical issues like battery drain or alternator failure, common in many commercial vehicles. Exterior and interior components are typically durable for their intended use. Proactive checks of the electrical system and careful maintenance of the DSG transmission can ensure continued dependable service from this commercial vehicle.
