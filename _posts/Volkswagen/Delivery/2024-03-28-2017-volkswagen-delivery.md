**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volkswagen Delivery is a versatile and efficient commercial van that offers a spacious cargo area and a variety of engine and transmission options. The vehicle is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 208 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Delivery is well-liked for its comfortable interior, ample storage space, and fuel efficiency, but it has received some criticism for its relatively high price and occasional reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Alternator**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical problems, such as dim headlights or battery drain
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Malfunctioning Sensors**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate readings from sensors, such as the tire pressure monitoring system or the coolant temperature sensor
    * Solution: Replace the faulty sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Cracking Headlight Lenses**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or clouding in the headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replace the headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Tailgate Latch**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate not closing properly or opening unexpectedly
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Loose Dashboard Trim**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard trim pieces rattling or falling off
    * Solution: Reattach or replace the trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Malfunctioning Infotainment System**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing or not responding, audio system not working
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Volkswagen Delivery is a capable and practical commercial van, but it has experienced a few reliability issues, particularly with electrical components and exterior parts. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix ranges from minor to moderate. It is important to note that these issues may not affect all Delivery models, and many owners have reported positive experiences with the vehicle.
