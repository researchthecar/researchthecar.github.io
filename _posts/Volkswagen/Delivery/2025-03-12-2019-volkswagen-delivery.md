The 2019 Volkswagen Delivery is a light commercial vehicle primarily sold in South America. It is known for its maneuverability in urban environments and its cargo capacity. While detailed reliability data specifically for the 2019 model year is limited in publicly accessible databases like NHTSA in the US, understanding potential problem areas based on general trends and available information for similar VW commercial vehicles can be helpful. Since the Delivery is predominantly sold in South America, detailed repair costs and widespread problem reports are harder to come by in comparison to vehicles sold in North America or Europe. The common complaint against the vehicle is the low build quality and lack of modern features that can be found in commercial vehicles sold in North America.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery failure, difficulty starting the vehicle, especially in cold weather. This may be due to the electrical system's demands or a battery not ideally suited for the vehicle's usage.
    * Solution: Replace the battery with a higher-quality or heavy-duty battery. Ensure proper charging system function and address any parasitic draws.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sensor Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Issues with various sensors (e.g., ABS sensors, oxygen sensors) leading to warning lights on the dashboard and potential drivability problems.
    * Solution: Diagnose the specific sensor causing the issue and replace it.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+ depending on the sensor.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Latch Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulties opening or closing doors, sticking latches, or the need to slam doors to ensure they are properly secured.
    * Solution: Lubricate the latches, adjust the striker plates, or replace faulty latch mechanisms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Component Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or breaking of plastic dashboard components.
    * Solution: Replacement of the broken or cracked plastic components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While detailed information about specific issues with the 2019 Volkswagen Delivery is limited, the potential problems listed above offer some insight into potential concerns. Given the intended use as a commercial vehicle, regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are crucial to mitigating potential issues and minimizing downtime. Overall, the cost of repairs seems to be moderate based on the issues listed.
