**Introduction:**

The 2016 Volkswagen Crafter is a full-size van available with a choice of 2.0L and 3.6L engines, and 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic transmissions. It offers a spacious cargo area, comfortable seating, and advanced safety features. However, owners have reported some issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.0L TDI**
    * **Injector Failure:** Premature failure of fuel injectors, leading to engine misfires and reduced power.
    * Solution: Replace faulty injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **3.6L VR6**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail prematurely, resulting in engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Synchronizer Wear:** Worn synchronizers can make gear shifting difficult, especially with higher gears.
    * Solution: Replace worn synchronizers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** The mechatronic unit, which controls shifting, can fail due to software problems or overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Mount Failure:** The front suspension strut mounts can fail, leading to abnormal suspension noises and reduced handling.
* Solution: Replace the failed strut mounts.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Electrical components, such as the starter motor, can drain the battery prematurely.
* Solution: Inspect and repair faulty electrical components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Washer Pump Failure:** The windshield washer pump can fail, resulting in intermittent or no windshield washer fluid dispensing.
* Solution: Replace the faulty pump.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system can malfunction, leading to inadequate heating or cooling.
* Solution: Repair or replace faulty control modules or sensors.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Volkswagen Crafter has been reported to have several issues, including engine injector failures, timing chain tensioner failures, transmission problems, suspension strut mount failures, and electrical problems. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor and others potentially leading to major repairs. As with any vehicle, it's important to address these issues promptly to ensure the reliability and safety of the vehicle.
