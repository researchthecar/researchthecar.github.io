**Introduction**

The 2004 Volkswagen Pointer Truck is a compact pickup truck that was offered with a single engine and transmission option. The 1.8L 4-cylinder engine produces 100 horsepower and 128 lb-ft of torque. The only available transmission option was a 5-speed manual. The Pointer Truck was praised for its fuel efficiency and affordability, but it was criticized for its lack of power and uncomfortable seats.

**Engine**

* **1.8L 4-cylinder**

    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket.
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Belt Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Timing belt fails, causing engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace timing belt.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Manual**

    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joints Wear Out**
    * Description/Symptoms: Front ball joints wear out, causing clunking noises and steering problems.
    * Solution: Replace ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Sag**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear leaf springs sag, causing the vehicle to ride low and handle poorly.
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Alternator fails, causing battery to drain and vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Starter solenoid fails, causing vehicle to not start.
    * Solution: Replace starter solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Cylinders Stick**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door lock cylinders stick, making it difficult to lock or unlock the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace door lock cylinders.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per door
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Bumper Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear bumper rusts and corrodes.
    * Solution: Replace or repair rear bumper.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard cracks over time.
    * Solution: Replace or repair dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioning Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning system fails to blow cold air or experiences other problems.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair specific issue, which can vary widely in cost.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 Volkswagen Pointer Truck has a number of potential issues, including engine valve cover gasket leaks, timing belt failures, and suspension problems. These issues can range in severity and cost to fix. While the Pointer Truck is a relatively affordable vehicle, it is important to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing one.
