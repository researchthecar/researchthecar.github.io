The 2017 Volkswagen CC is a stylish four-door coupe offering a blend of luxury and sportiness. It comes with a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 200 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a six-speed manual or a six-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise its sleek design and comfortable ride, but some dislike its limited rear headroom due to the sloping roofline. Common complaints range from minor electrical glitches to concerns about long-term reliability.

**Engine**

**2.0L TSI (Turbocharged Stratified Injection) Engine**

*   **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise upon startup, especially in cold weather. Can lead to catastrophic engine damage if the chain slips or breaks.
    *   Solution: Replace the timing chain, tensioner, guides, and possibly other related components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Leaks (Valve Cover, Timing Cover, Rear Main Seal)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks on the engine, burning oil smell, low oil level.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaking gasket or seal.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ depending on the location and severity of the leak.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling or whining noise from the turbocharger, check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission (09G/TF-61SN)**

*   **Valve Body Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, slipping between gears, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights.
    *   Solution: Replace or rebuild the valve body.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Torque Converter Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration at low speeds, stalling, poor acceleration.
    *   Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Strut Mount Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension, especially when going over bumps.
    *   Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Wandering steering, uneven tire wear, clunking noise when turning or braking.
    *   Solution: Replace the control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Power Window Regulator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Window not going up or down, slow or jerky movement, clicking noise.
    *   Solution: Replace the power window regulator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Central Locking System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking, alarm system malfunctions.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and repair wiring issues, replace faulty door lock actuators or the central control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Car not starting after being parked for a short period, requiring frequent jump starts.
    * Solution: Identify parasitic drain (aftermarket accessories, faulty modules), repair wiring, replace faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+ depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sunroof Issues (Sticking, Leaking)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof getting stuck open or closed, water leaks into the cabin.
    *   Solution: Clean and lubricate the sunroof tracks, replace damaged seals, repair or replace the sunroof motor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Navigation/Infotainment System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: System freezing, crashing, unresponsive touch screen, navigation errors.
    *   Solution: Software update, replacement of the head unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Volkswagen CC has a mix of potential issues, ranging from minor electrical glitches to more concerning engine and transmission problems. The timing chain tensioner issue in the 2.0L TSI engine is a major concern due to the potential for catastrophic engine damage, and the transmission valve body problems can lead to costly repairs. While the CC offers a stylish design and comfortable ride, potential buyers should be aware of these common issues and budget accordingly for potential repairs. The severity of these issues range from minor inconveniences to costly repairs.
