## Introduction to the 2014 Volkswagen Combi

The 2014 Volkswagen Combi is a spacious and versatile minivan that offers seating for up to seven passengers. It is available with a choice of two gasoline engines: a 2.0-liter inline-four with 136 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque, or a 3.6-liter V6 with 283 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The Combi has a starting price of $25,995.

Owners of the 2014 Volkswagen Combi appreciate its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and electrical system.

## Engine

### 2.0L Inline-Four

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner fails, causing the timing chain to slip and the engine to lose power.
Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Valve Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The intake valves fail, causing the engine to misfire and run rough.
Solution: Replace the intake valves.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2014 Volkswagen Combi is a reliable vehicle with a spacious interior and a comfortable ride. However, it has some potential issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. Overall, the Combi is a good choice for families and other buyers who need a spacious and versatile vehicle.
