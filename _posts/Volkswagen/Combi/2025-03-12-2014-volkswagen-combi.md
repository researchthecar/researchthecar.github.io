The 2014 Volkswagen Combi, also known as the Volkswagen Transporter or Caravelle in some markets, is a versatile van known for its spaciousness and practicality. While engine options vary depending on the region, a common choice is the 2.0-liter TDI diesel engine. Power output for this engine typically ranges around 140 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque. Owners often appreciate the Combi for its reliability, comfortable ride, and ample cargo or passenger capacity. Common dislikes include the basic interior features and potential maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**2.0L TDI Diesel**

*   **EGR Cooler Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) cooler failure can lead to coolant loss, white smoke from the exhaust, and potential engine overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace the EGR cooler.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Fuel Injector Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, misfires, decreased fuel economy, and black smoke from the exhaust could indicate failing or clogged fuel injectors.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty fuel injectors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1200+ (depending on the number of injectors needing replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**DSG (Direct Shift Gearbox) Automatic**

*   **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Mechatronic unit, which controls the DSG transmission, can fail, leading to erratic shifting, hesitation, or complete loss of gear engagement.
    *   Solution: Replace or rebuild the Mechatronic unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Clutch Wear/Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Slipping during acceleration, jerky shifts, or difficulty engaging gears can indicate worn or failing clutches in the DSG transmission.
    *   Solution: Replace the clutch pack.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing, poor handling, and noise from the rear suspension, indicating worn or leaking shock absorbers.
    *   Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Central Locking System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Failure of the central locking system, preventing doors from locking or unlocking remotely.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the central locking control module or door actuators.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Premature battery drain, leading to starting problems. This can be caused by parasitic draws from various electrical components.
    *   Solution: Identify and repair the source of the parasitic draw.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ (depending on the complexity of the issue)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulties opening or closing the sliding door, often caused by worn rollers, damaged tracks, or malfunctioning latch mechanisms.
    *   Solution: Repair or replace the affected components of the sliding door mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Cracking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the dashboard, especially in areas exposed to sunlight.
    *   Solution: Repair or replace the dashboard.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Volkswagen Combi, while generally reliable, has some common issues that owners may encounter. The most serious issues tend to involve the DSG transmission and the diesel engine's EGR cooler, which can be costly to repair. Electrical issues and suspension problems are also common, but usually less expensive to address. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues and budget accordingly.
