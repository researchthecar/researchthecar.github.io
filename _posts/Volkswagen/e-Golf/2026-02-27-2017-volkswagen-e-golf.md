# 2017 Volkswagen e-Golf Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Volkswagen e-Golf, buyers will find an all-electric hatchback that offers refined driving dynamics and everyday practicality. While overall owner feedback is limited, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and monitor vehicle electronics during a test drive to ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 134-hp Permanent Magnet Synchronous AC Electric Motor

This electric motor produces 134 horsepower and 214 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for urban commuting. It generally provides trouble-free operation as part of Volkswagen's EV propulsion system.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

A single-speed direct-drive transmission manages power delivery to the front wheels smoothly and efficiently. This unit lacks traditional gears, resulting in minimal mechanical wear and solid long-term dependability.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Vehicle Computer System

Owner reports indicate potential software or module glitches that can temporarily disable convenience and safety features.

**Infotainment and Sensor System Crashes**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle computer system may spontaneously crash every few minutes, causing proximity sensors, the rear backup camera, and drive mode selection to stop working.
- **Solution:** Requires a hard system reboot or diagnostic evaluation by a qualified technician to address persistent software instability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V321000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=e-Golf&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Vehicles that do not meet all regulatory requirements may have an increased risk of injury, crash, or fire.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Volkswagen e-Golf is an appealing choice for electric vehicle shoppers seeking a comfortable and well-built hatchback. Buyers should thoroughly test all electronics, infotainment functions, and driver assist sensors during pre-purchase inspections to catch any intermittent software glitches.
