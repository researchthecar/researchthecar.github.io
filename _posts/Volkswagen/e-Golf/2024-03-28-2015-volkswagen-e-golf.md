**Introduction to the 2015 Volkswagen e-Golf**

The 2015 Volkswagen e-Golf is an all-electric hatchback that delivers zero-emission driving. It is powered by a single electric motor that produces 115 horsepower and 199 lb-ft of torque, and it has a driving range of up to 83 miles on a fully charged battery. The e-Golf is known for its smooth and refined driving experience, as well as its spacious interior and user-friendly technology. However, it has also faced some issues that have been reported by owners.

**Electric Motor**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid depletion of battery charge
    * Solution: Battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 23V856000

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Strut replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Short**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, such as flickering lights or instrument panel issues
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V736000

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Trim Detachment**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trim pieces around the rear bumper detaching
    * Solution: Reattachment of trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Interior Trim Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noises coming from interior trim pieces
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Volkswagen e-Golf is a reliable electric vehicle with a comfortable interior and user-friendly technology. However, it has experienced some common issues that include battery drain, electrical harness shorts, front strut failure, and interior trim noise. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to significant. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to have their vehicle inspected and serviced regularly to address any issues promptly.
