# 2016 Volkswagen e-Golf Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Volkswagen e-Golf offers an efficient and refined all-electric driving experience, making it an appealing choice for used-car buyers seeking a budget-friendly EV for daily commuting. Because complaint data is relatively light, shoppers can generally focus on standard used-car inspection items, though evaluating the health of the high-voltage battery remains crucial.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous AC Electric Motor (115 hp / 199 lb-ft)

The e-Golf utilizes a 115-horsepower permanent magnet synchronous AC electric motor paired with a 24.2 kWh lithium-ion battery pack. This powertrain provides brisk low-end torque for city driving and has generally proven dependable, though driving range is modest by modern standards.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Power is delivered to the front wheels via a single-speed direct drive automatic transmission. The lack of traditional gears simplifies the drivetrain and minimizes mechanical wear, resulting in smooth and maintenance-free operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V329000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=e-Golf&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Modifications made while the vehicles were in an internal evaluation period may cause the affected vehicles to not comply with all of the applicable regulatory requirements. If the vehicles do not meet all regulatory requirements, there could be an increased risk of a crash, fire, or injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will repair the vehicles to make them fully compliant or Volkswagen will repurchase them if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V138000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=e-Golf&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Oversensitive diagnostics for the high-voltage battery management system may falsely detect an electrical surge resulting in the vehicle's electric drive motor shutting down unexpectedly. An unexpected vehicle shutdown can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen has notified owners, and dealers will update the battery management software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V955000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=e-Golf&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Additionally, certain 2017 Audi Q7, A4 Sedan, A4 Allroad, and 2018 Audi Q5 vehicles are being recalled because the seat belt pretensioners may not activate properly. In the event of a crash, if the air bags and/or the seat belt pretensioners do not inflate or function properly, the vehicle occupants have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen and Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected air bags and seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V464000: Latches/Locks/Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=e-Golf&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Vibrations, such as those created by opening and closing a vehicle door, may cause the rear child door locks to disengage, allowing a child to open a rear door while inside the vehicle. If an unrestrained child unexpectedly opens a door, they may fall from the vehicle increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and if necessary, replace the child lock(s), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V543000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=e-Golf&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During air bag deployment, the front passenger air bag may explode or deploy improperly. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Volkswagen e-Golf stands out as a comfortable and compliant electric hatchback with very few reported recurring mechanical issues in standard owner data. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the remaining battery capacity and charging functionality before purchase, but otherwise, this EV represents a sensible and low-maintenance used option.
