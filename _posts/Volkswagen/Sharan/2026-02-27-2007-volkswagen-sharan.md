# 2007 Volkswagen Sharan

### Overview
The 2007 Volkswagen Sharan, a family-oriented MPV, generally presents a mixed bag of reliability. While its practicality and spacious interior are strong suits, certain mechanical and electrical components have shown a propensity for issues. Owners often report on the durability of the core powertrain, but specific engine variants and associated systems, particularly emissions control, can be a source of concern. Transmission reliability is generally considered average for its age, with some manual gearbox issues noted. Electrical gremlins and interior trim wear are also common themes in owner feedback, though these are often not critical failures. Suspension components are typically robust, but age-related wear is expected.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TDI (136 PS)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** EGR valve cleaning or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 2.0L TDI (140 PS)
**Dual Mass Flywheel (DMF) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Clutch judder, rattling noise on startup/shutdown, difficulty changing gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of DMF and clutch kit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Engine misfires, rough running, increased fuel consumption, starting difficulties.
- **Solution:** Injector testing, cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per injector

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, stiff pedal.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### 6-Speed Manual
**Gear Selector Cable Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty selecting gears, loose gear lever.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gear selector cables.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### 6-Speed Automatic (Tiptronic)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, harsh shifting, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair of leaks, fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, visible leaks from shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of shock absorbers (often in pairs).
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not working, window stuck in position.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Central Locking Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking/unlocking consistently, individual door lock failures.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of actuators or control modules.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Dashboard Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of various warning lights without apparent cause, intermittent faults.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan to identify faulty sensors or modules, repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500

## Interior

### Trim and Upholstery
**Wear on Seat Bolsters**
- **Symptoms:** Fraying or tearing of fabric/leather on the sides of seats, especially the driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat covers.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
- **Symptoms:** Annoying noises from the dashboard area, particularly over uneven surfaces.
- **Solution:** Identification and insulation of loose trim pieces.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor or motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Volkswagen Sharan is a practical family vehicle, but potential buyers should be aware of common issues, particularly with the TDI diesel engines, including turbocharger and EGR problems, and the dual-mass flywheel on certain variants. Transmission reliability is average, with clutch wear being a common concern on manuals. Electrical gremlins like window regulators and central locking can be frustrating. Interior trim wear and dashboard rattles are typical for its age. While suspension components are generally robust, worn bushings and shock absorbers are expected maintenance items. Overall, it offers good space and utility, but requires diligent maintenance and awareness of potential repair costs, especially concerning the powertrain.
