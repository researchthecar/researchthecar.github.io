# 2022 Volkswagen Passat Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Volkswagen Passat, buyers will find a comfortable, spacious midsize sedan that marks the final year of the Passat's storied run in the American market. Powered exclusively by a turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a traditional automatic transmission, it offers a refined daily driving experience. However, owner feedback collected through federal safety databases points to a few recurring areas of concern, particularly regarding excessive oil consumption in the engine and premature wear in the braking system, making a thorough pre-purchase inspection a smart step for prospective owners.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2022 Passat features a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine producing 174 horsepower and 206 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily commuting, though buyers should watch closely for oil consumption habits during test drives.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report having to frequently add substantial amounts of oil between routine maintenance intervals, sometimes noting smoke or related turbo concerns.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly between changes and consult a qualified technician or dealer for diagnostics if consumption rates exceed normal manufacturer thresholds.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive 6-speed automatic transmission driving the front wheels, this setup delivers smooth gear changes suited for highway cruising. Overall mechanical durability is generally sound, with very few powertrain complaints reported.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system components require careful monitoring, as multiple owners have experienced accelerated wear on rear brake pads and components.

**Premature Rear Brake Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rear brake pads wear out rapidly at surprisingly low mileage, sometimes leading to sticking calipers, uneven pad wear, and metal-on-metal grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pad thickness and caliper slide pins during routine service, and replace worn pads and rotors as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical System and Door Locks

Cabin electronics generally function well, but minor software glitches and door lock anomalies have been reported by some owners.

**Random Door Unlocking and Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** All four vehicle doors may automatically unlock while parked, accompanied by occasional infotainment screen blackouts or audio static.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or technician check body control module software and related wiring harnesses for intermittent faults.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Safety and driver-assistance features enhance modern protection, though sensor calibration and sensitivity can occasionally frustrate drivers.

**Aggressive Lane Assist and Forward Collision Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The lane-keeping assist system can occasionally react too aggressively to road tar or tire marks, and some drivers report unexpected behavior from the front automatic braking assist.
- **Solution:** Adjust driver assistance settings through the infotainment menu where permitted, or have dealership technicians recalibrate sensors if system behavior is erratic.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volkswagen Passat is a spacious and comfortable choice for used car buyers seeking a traditional midsize sedan. While it offers a pleasant ride and straightforward powertrain options, prospective buyers should prioritize a pre-purchase inspection that specifically checks oil consumption history, rear brake pad life, and electronic functionality to ensure long-term satisfaction.
