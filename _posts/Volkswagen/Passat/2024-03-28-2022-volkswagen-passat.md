**Introduction:**

The 2022 Volkswagen Passat is a mid-size sedan offered with three engine options: a 174-horsepower 1.8L turbocharged four-cylinder, a 220-horsepower 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder, and a 150-horsepower 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder diesel. Transmission options include a six-speed automatic and a seven-speed DSG dual-clutch automatic.

Owners appreciate the Passat's spacious interior, comfortable ride, and efficient engines. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.8L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

- **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased emissions.
  - Solution: Walnut shell blasting to remove carbon buildup.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially on cold starts.
  - Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
  - Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

- **Turbocharger Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, blue smoke from the exhaust, and oil leaks.
  - Solution: Replacement of turbocharger.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Diesel**

- **Diesel Particulate Filter Clogging:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption, and a warning light on the dashboard.
  - Solution: Replacement of diesel particulate filter.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the transmission housing, causing slipping or shifting issues.
  - Solution: Repair or replacement of transmission seals or gasket.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed DSG Dual-Clutch Automatic**

- **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, loss of gears, and a flashing "DSG" warning light on the dashboard.
  - Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Struts Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, excessive bouncing, and difficulty steering.
  - Solution: Replacement of front struts.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Electrical Harness Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, such as flickering lights, non-functioning gauges, or starting problems.
  - Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of affected wiring.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Wiper blades not moving or intermittent failure.
  - Solution: Replacement of windshield wiper motor.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Infotainment System Glitches:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Freezing, crashing, or connectivity issues with the infotainment system.
  - Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment unit.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Volkswagen Passat has received mixed reviews from owners. While it offers a comfortable ride and efficient engines, it has experienced some reliability issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The estimated cost to fix these issues can be significant, so it's important to consider these potential costs when buying or owning this vehicle.
