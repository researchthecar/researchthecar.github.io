# 2017 Volkswagen Touareg Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Volkswagen Touareg, understanding its mechanical background helps set realistic ownership expectations. While owner-reported complaints highlight a few specific areas of concern, careful inspection during a test drive can help prospective buyers identify potential issues early.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 FSI

The 3.6-liter naturally aspirated V6 FSI engine delivers smooth performance and respectable towing capability. Routine fluid monitoring and preventative maintenance are essential for long-term reliability.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Oil splatter present on the vehicle body or smoke exiting from the exhaust due to oil leaking past pistons into the combustion chamber.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly and consult a qualified mechanic for internal engine diagnostics.

**Exhaust Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable exhaust smell inside the cabin caused by a crack in the exhaust line near the catalytic converter.
- **Solution:** Inspect the exhaust manifold and piping for cracks, and replace damaged exhaust components.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the all-wheel-drive system to provide smooth and efficient gear shifts. Owners generally report stable operation, provided regular fluid services are maintained.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Wheel

Steering components generally hold up well, though vibration issues should be thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Steering Wheel Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Visible shaking or vibration felt through the steering wheel while driving.
- **Solution:** Check wheel balancing, alignment, and suspension components for wear.

## Electrical

### Adaptive Cruise Control

Advanced safety features enhance driving convenience, but electronic sensors require careful calibration and clean mounting surfaces to function reliably.

**ACC System Disengagement**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle intermittently displays an error message stating ACC is not available and completely disengages cruise control functions.
- **Solution:** Have vehicle sensors and software modules scanned for diagnostic trouble codes by a specialist.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V321000: Unknown Or Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Touareg&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Vehicles that do not meet all regulatory requirements may have an increased risk of injury, crash, or fire.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The Volkswagen Touareg offers a refined driving experience and robust powertrain options, but prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Checking for oil consumption, exhaust integrity, and electronic sensor performance will help ensure a more confident ownership experience.
