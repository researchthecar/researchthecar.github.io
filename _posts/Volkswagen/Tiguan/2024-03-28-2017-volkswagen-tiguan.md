**Introduction**

The 2017 Volkswagen Tiguan is a compact SUV that offers comfortable seating for up to seven passengers. It's powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. The Tiguan is available with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, and comes standard with a six-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers like the Tiguan's roomy interior, comfortable ride, and user-friendly features. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Carbon buildup on intake valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud rattling or ticking sound from engine, loss of engine power
    * Solution: Replacing timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Software updates, transmission repairs, or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear suspension bushing wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from rear suspension, reduced handling stability
    * Solution: Replacing worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery becomes drained after prolonged periods of inactivity
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing electrical system faults, replacing battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment system issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, slow response, software glitches
    * Solution: Software updates, system resets, or replacement of infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from sunroof area while driving over bumps or uneven surfaces
    * Solution: Adjusting or replacing sunroof components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Heated seat switch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heated seat switch malfunctions, preventing seats from heating
    * Solution: Replacing heated seat switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from dashboard area while driving
    * Solution: Tightening loose components, installing sound deadening materials
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Volkswagen Tiguan has been reported to experience a range of issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, suspension issues, electrical issues, and interior issues. Some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more severe and costly. Overall, the Tiguan has received mixed reviews from owners, with some praising its comfort and features while others have encountered significant problems.
