**Introduction to the 2023 Volkswagen Tiguan**

The 2023 Volkswagen Tiguan is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable and spacious cabin, along with plenty of cargo space. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder that produces 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Tiguan is praised for its comfortable driving dynamics, roomy interior, and user-friendly technology, while some find it to be underpowered and lacking in premium interior materials.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

**Transmission**

* **Eight-Speed Automatic**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: Users experience random freezing or crashing of the infotainment system, resulting in loss of audio, navigation, and other functions.
  * Solution: Software updates and, in some cases, replacement of the infotainment control module.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Volkswagen Tiguan has a limited number of reported issues, most of which are related to the infotainment system. The severity of these issues varies, with some users experiencing minor annoyances while others require costly repairs. Overall, the Tiguan is a reliable and practical compact SUV that offers a good balance of comfort, space, and features.
