# 2022 Volkswagen Tiguan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volkswagen Tiguan presents a stylish and practical option for compact SUV buyers, featuring a comfortable cabin and composed handling. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints concerning steering assist malfunctions, engine stalling, and electrical issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate pep for daily commuting. While generally refined, owners have reported concerns regarding intermittent stalling and oil consumption.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lose motive power or stall while driving, accompanied by warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing by a qualified technician to identify faulty fuel components, sensors, or software glitches.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** The oil warning light illuminates on the dashboard, and drivers notice the engine is consuming an unusually high amount of engine oil.
- **Solution:** Have the engine inspected for leaks or internal wear, and maintain regular oil level checks.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the front wheels or the 4Motion all-wheel-drive system. Some drivers experience noticeable throttle lag or hesitation from a standstill.

**Throttle Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle intermittently hesitates or experiences lag when pressing the accelerator pedal from a dead stop before suddenly lurching forward.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealer for potential transmission control module software updates that may improve shift responsiveness.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Sensors and Travel Assist

The steering system incorporates advanced driver-assistance sensors, though capacitive sensor glitches in the steering wheel frequently trigger persistent warning chimes.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Repeated warnings for 'Travel Assist Not Available' pop up on the dashboard accompanied by loud, repetitive beeps or chimes.
- **Solution:** Replacing the steering wheel assembly to resolve capacitive touch sensor failures within the wheel ring.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

The electrical architecture supports an advanced touchscreen infotainment system and various cabin electronics, with occasional screen freezing and backup camera dropouts noted by owners.

**Infotainment Screen Freezing and Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment display freezes, turns completely black, or reboots randomly during operation, causing the backup camera feed to fail.
- **Solution:** Perform software resets or updates, or have faulty display hardware replaced under warranty if applicable.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volkswagen Tiguan is a comfortable and versatile compact SUV, but used car shoppers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to steering assist error messages, infotainment stability, and engine performance during a test drive to ensure prior fixes have been addressed.
