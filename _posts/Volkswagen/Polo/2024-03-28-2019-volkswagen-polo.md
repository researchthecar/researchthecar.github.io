**Introduction**

The 2019 Volkswagen Polo is a subcompact hatchback available in a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 1.0-liter three-cylinder, a 1.5-liter four-cylinder, and a 2.0-liter four-cylinder. All engines are paired with a five-speed manual transmission, while the 1.5-liter and 2.0-liter engines are also available with a six-speed automatic transmission. The Polo has received praise for its fun-to-drive nature, stylish design, and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**1.0-liter Three-Cylinder**

* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip and potentially damage the engine.
    * **Symptoms:** Engine knocking, loss of power, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**1.5-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, leading to a loss of boost pressure and power.
    * **Symptoms:** Loss of power, smoke from the engine, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $2,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding gears:** The transmission may grind gears, especially when shifting from second to third gear.
    * **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting, difficulty shifting
    * **Solution:** Replace the synchronizer rings in the transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure:** The strut mounts can fail, causing a knocking or rattling noise from the suspension.
    * **Symptoms:** Knocking or rattling noise when driving over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * **Solution:** Replace the strut mounts.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400 per strut mount
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical harness issues:** The electrical harness can develop problems, leading to various electrical issues.
    * **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical problems, such as power window failure, flickering lights, or dashboard malfunctions
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace the affected part of the electrical harness.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the location and severity of the issue
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate control issues:** The climate control system may experience problems, such as intermittent operation or incorrect temperature settings.
    * **Symptoms:** Difficulty regulating temperature, uneven airflow, hissing or clicking noises
    * **Solution:** Diagnose and repair the faulty component in the climate control system.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Volkswagen Polo is generally a reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported issues with specific components. The most common issues reported are timing chain tensioner failure (1.0-liter engine), turbocharger failure (1.5-liter engine), and strut mount failure. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most can be resolved for a reasonable price.
