## 2020 Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport

The 2020 Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport is a midsize SUV that seats up to seven passengers. It is available with two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 and a 3.6L V6. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Atlas Cross Sport is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features. However, it has also been plagued by several issues.

### Engine

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

- **Excessive Oil Consumption**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Some owners have reported that their Atlas Cross Sport consumes excessive oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
  - **Solution:** Volkswagen has issued a software update to address this issue.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Covered under warranty
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**3.6L V6**

- **No Issues Found**

### Transmission

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

- **Shifting Issues**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Some owners have reported that their Atlas Cross Sport experiences rough or delayed shifting, especially when accelerating from a stop.
  - **Solution:** Volkswagen has issued several software updates to address this issue.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Covered under warranty
  - **Recalls:** None Found

### Suspension

- **No Issues Found**

### Electrical

- **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Some owners have reported that their Atlas Cross Sport's infotainment system experiences frequent crashes, freezes, and glitches.
  - **Solution:** Volkswagen has issued several software updates to address this issue.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Covered under warranty
  - **Recalls:** None Found

- **Electrical System Issues**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Some owners have reported various electrical system issues, such as flickering lights, faulty sensors, and battery drain.
  - **Solution:** Volkswagen has issued several software updates and recalls to address these issues.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the specific issue
  - **Recalls:** 20V728, 21V245

### Exterior

- **No Issues Found**

### Interior

- **HVAC Control Malfunctions**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Some owners have reported that their Atlas Cross Sport's HVAC controls malfunction, causing irregular temperature regulation or fan operation.
  - **Solution:** Volkswagen has issued several software updates to address this issue.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Covered under warranty
  - **Recalls:** None Found

### Summary

While the 2020 Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport is a popular SUV with many positive attributes, it has been plagued by several issues. The most common problems involve the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior components. The severity of these issues varies, but many can be fixed under warranty. However, some owners have reported excessive oil consumption and electrical system problems that can be costly to repair. Overall, the Atlas Cross Sport has a mixed reliability record, with some owners experiencing significant problems while others have reported few.
