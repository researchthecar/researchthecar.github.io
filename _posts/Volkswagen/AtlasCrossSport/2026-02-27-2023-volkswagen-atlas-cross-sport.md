# 2023 Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport offers striking styling and a spacious, tech-forward cabin, making it an appealing midsize SUV option for families. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical glitches, unexpected engine stalling, and braking system noises. While many modern features impress, evaluating these specific reliability reports can help you make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate daily acceleration. Owners have reported occasional stalling incidents and oil leaks related to components like the PCV valve.

### 3.6L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 generates 276 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth power delivery and a higher towing capacity. It remains a popular choice for buyers seeking traditional V6 performance, though intermittent driveability complaints warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lose power and cut off while driving or stopping at traffic lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the fuel delivery, electrical sensors, or powertrain control module for fault codes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations to deliver smooth shifting and optimized highway efficiency. It generally operates reliably, though prospective buyers should still test-shift through all gears during a test drive.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides stopping power suited for a midsize SUV, though some owners note noisy operation and premature wear characteristics.

**Brake Noise and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience loud grinding noises, premature pad wear, and steering wheel vibration when applying the brakes.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for excessive wear or glazing, and replace components with quality aftermarket or updated OEM parts if necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment System

Electrical architecture governs numerous convenience and safety features, with sporadic owner complaints highlighting software and accessory control bugs.

**Erratic Electrical and Navigation Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle windows may roll down spontaneously, power tailgates open unexpectedly, or the navigation system may mislocate the vehicle by miles.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a dealership or service center to resolve module communication errors.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The cabin features modern amenities and expansive glass options, though isolated incidents of glass failure have been reported by owners.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof may shatter or explode unexpectedly while driving on highways.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress fractures and replace the assembly if damage or structural failure is found.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V152000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Atlas+Cross+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The door wiring harness electrical contacts may corrode, disrupting the electrical connection and delaying the deployment of the driver or passenger front side air bag during a side impact crash. A delayed air bag deployment can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure and, as necessary, replace any the front door wiring harnesses showing a fault code, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V718000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Atlas+Cross+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak may increase the distance required to stop, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the affected brake lines, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V753000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Atlas+Cross+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The engine connecting rod bearings may become damaged, which can result in engine failure. A damaged engine can result in engine failure and a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace an affected engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V807000: Suspension:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Atlas+Cross+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The driver-side rear trailing arm bolts may not have been tightened properly, allowing them to loosen and fall out. Unsecured bolts may cause the trailing arm to fracture, resulting in a sudden loss of vehicle control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver side rear trailing arm bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V464000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Atlas+Cross+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the PODS sensor mat and wiring harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V434000: Service Brakes](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Atlas+Cross+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Certain bolts on the chassis, brake system and/or suspension (such as bolts for certain cross member, engine mount, control arm, subframe, rear brake carrier, etc.) of affected vehicles may not have been correctly tightened during the manufacturing process. A loss of vehicle control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the bolts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport is a comfortable, stylish cruiser with generous passenger space and modern tech. While most vehicles operate without major incident, used-car buyers should perform a detailed pre-purchase inspection checking the brakes, electrical accessories, and powertrain functionality to ensure peace of mind.
