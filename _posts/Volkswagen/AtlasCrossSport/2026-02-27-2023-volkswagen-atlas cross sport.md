### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Volkswagen Atlas Cross Sport presents a generally reliable profile for its model year. Both the 2.0L turbocharged engine and the 3.6L V6, along with the 8-speed automatic transmission, have not shown significant widespread mechanical issues. The suspension and exterior components are also holding up well. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that can usually be resolved with software updates, and rare instances of battery drain requiring diagnosis. Overall, for a modern vehicle, the Atlas Cross Sport offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential issues being minor and related to electronics rather than core mechanical components.
