**Introduction to the 2022 Volkswagen Virtus**

The 2022 Volkswagen Virtus is a subcompact sedan available with two engine options: a 1.0-liter three-cylinder engine and a 1.5-liter four-cylinder engine. Both engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The 1.0-liter engine produces 114 horsepower and 148 pound-feet of torque, while the 1.5-liter engine produces 148 horsepower and 184 pound-feet of torque. The Virtus is known for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and responsive handling. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **1.0-Liter Three-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, increased fuel consumption
        * Solution: Remove and clean intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **1.5-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed shifts, slipping gears
        * Solution: Replace transmission fluid, adjust transmission linkage, or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, vibration in steering wheel
        * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Discharge Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting engine
        * Solution: Replace battery or alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**
    * **Failed Diode**
        * Description/Symptoms: Charging issues, battery not charging
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**
    * **Headlight Fogging**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlights
        * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
    * **Rattling Dashboard**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard making rattling noises over rough roads
        * Solution: Add soundproofing material to dashboard
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console**
    * **Loose Center Console**
        * Description/Symptoms: Center console shaking or wobbling
        * Solution: Tighten center console bolts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Volkswagen Virtus has received mostly positive reviews. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle, including the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues vary greatly, but some can be costly to repair. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
