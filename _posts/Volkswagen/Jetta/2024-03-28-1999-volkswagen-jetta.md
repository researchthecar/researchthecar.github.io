**Introduction**

The 1999 Volkswagen Jetta is a compact sedan offered with two engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 2.0L inline-4 producing 115 horsepower and 122 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The optional engine is a 2.8L VR6 producing 174 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The Jetta is praised for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and fuel efficiency. However, it has received criticism for its lack of power from the base engine and some reliability issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, rough idle, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cam Position Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, rough idle, stalling
    * Solution: Replace cam position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cracked Engine Block**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Engine replacement may be necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.8L VR6**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on startup, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Runner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runners
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Synchronizer Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding noise when shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace synchronizer rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage gears, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Overheating**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, burning smell
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: 04V417000

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**

    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**

    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to lock or unlock doors
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Window Regulator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to roll up or down windows

    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard surface
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

    * Solution: Replace seat belt retractor

**Summary**

The 1999 Volkswagen Jetta has a range of issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The estimated cost to repair these issues can be significant, ranging from hundreds to thousands of dollars. While it is generally a reliable car, potential buyers should be aware of the potential expenses associated with owning and maintaining one.
