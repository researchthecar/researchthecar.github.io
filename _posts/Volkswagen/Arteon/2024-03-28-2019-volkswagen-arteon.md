**Introduction:**
The 2019 Volkswagen Arteon is a mid-size sedan that combines performance and luxury. It offers two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 with 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.6L V6 with 276 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Both engines come standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission with Tiptronic shifting, with front-wheel drive or 4Motion all-wheel drive optional. Drivers appreciate the Arteon's stylish design, spacious interior, and responsive handling. However, some owners have reported issues with specific components.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Fuel Pump Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The fuel pump can fail, leading to loss of power, stalling, and difficulty starting.
Solution: Replace fuel pump.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may exhibit rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears, or hesitation.
Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission components as necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000-4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:**
Description/Symptoms: A knocking or clunking sound can be heard from the rear suspension, especially over bumps or uneven road surfaces.
Solution: Replace or repair suspension components such as bushings, links, or struts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, leading to difficulty starting or electrical system failures.
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, including alternator, battery, or wiring issues.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - 1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin from around the sunroof gasket.
Solution: Replace or repair sunroof gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - 1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Front Grille Misalignment:**
Description/Symptoms: The front grille may not be properly aligned, resulting in gaps or uneven appearance.
Solution: Adjust or replace grille assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - 1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience software glitches, freezes, or connectivity issues.
Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - 1,500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Power Seat Malfunctions:**
Description/Symptoms: The power seat may fail to adjust or operate properly.
Solution: Repair or replace power seat motor or control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - 1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Volkswagen Arteon offers a blend of performance and luxury, but it has its share of reported issues. While some problems, such as sunroof leaks or exterior misalignment, may be relatively minor, concerns with the engine, transmission, or infotainment system can be more costly to repair. The severity and cost of the issues vary, and it's important for potential buyers to research and consider these potential risks before making a purchase.
