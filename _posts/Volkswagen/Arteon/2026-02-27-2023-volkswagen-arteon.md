# 2023 Volkswagen Arteon Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2023 Volkswagen Arteon means considering a sleek, premium fastback that blends upscale styling with everyday practicality. While overall powertrain configurations are generally well-regarded, prospective buyers should review available owner-reported feedback concerning specific vehicle components before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It delivers lively acceleration and balanced efficiency, making it the standard powerhouse for this model year.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides quick, seamless gear shifts paired with all-wheel drive. It offers responsive performance for daily driving when properly maintained.

## Suspension

### Factory Tires and Wheels

Owner feedback highlights isolated durability concerns regarding original equipment tires and wheels under normal road conditions.

**Factory Low-Profile Tire Vulnerability**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden tire pressure loss, sidewall blowouts on minor bumps, and tread separation on low-profile factory tires at relatively low mileages.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire condition and tread depth thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection, and consider replacing vulnerable low-profile tires with durable alternatives if necessary.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V082000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volkswagen&model=Arteon&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the camera control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Volkswagen Arteon presents an attractive option for used car buyers seeking distinctive styling and strong performance. Buyers should perform a careful inspection of the tires and wheels, as owner reports highlight potential vulnerability with low-profile factory setups.
