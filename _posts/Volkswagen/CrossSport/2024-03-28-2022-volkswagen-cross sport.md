**Introduction:**

The 2022 Volkswagen Cross Sport is a compact SUV that offers a spacious interior, advanced technology, and available all-wheel drive. It is powered by a standard 2.0L inline-4 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, delivering 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. Optional engine choices include a 2.5L inline-4 with 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.6L V6 with 276 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque. All trims are equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Owners praise the Cross Sport for its comfortable ride, spacious cabin, and sharp handling. However, some have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**

*Rear Wheel Drive 2.0L Inline-4 Engine/8-Speed Automatic Transmission*
* **Engine Overheating:**

- Description/Symptoms: Engine runs hot, leading to possible damage or failure.
- Solution: Replacing the thermostat and water pump is necessary.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
- Recalls: None Found

*All-Wheel Drive 2.5L Inline-4 Engine/8-Speed Automatic Transmission*

- **No Issues Found**

*All-Wheel Drive 3.6L V6 Engine/8-Speed Automatic Transmission*

- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**

- Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed response, or sudden loss of power.
- Solution: Replacing or repairing the transmission may be necessary.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
- Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Squeaking:**

- Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the front suspension during low-speed maneuvers.
- Solution: Lubricating or replacing worn bushings or springs may resolve the issue.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
- Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Radio Malfunction:**

- Description/Symptoms: Radio screen not responding, distorted audio, or complete system failure.
- Solution: Software updates or replacing the radio unit may be required.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
- Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Door Handle Rust:**

- Description/Symptoms: Exterior door handles corroding and becoming difficult to operate.
- Solution: Replace affected door handles.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
- Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Rattle:**

- Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard area during driving.
- Solution: Tightening loose panels or components.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
- Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Volkswagen Cross Sport has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While some owners have reported minimal issues, others have encountered problems with specific components such as the transmission and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively minor and can be resolved through repairs or replacements. However, it's important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
