The 2007 Volkswagen SportVan, also known as the Golf Plus in some markets, is a compact MPV (Multi-Purpose Vehicle) that offers increased headroom and cargo space compared to the standard Golf. Engine options for this model year included a range of gasoline and diesel engines. This response will focus on potential issues regardless of the specific engine or transmission. While specific horsepower and torque figures vary depending on the engine chosen, they are typically in the range of 80-150 horsepower. Owners generally appreciate the SportVan for its practicality, comfortable ride, and fuel efficiency. Common complaints often revolve around reliability issues and the cost of repairs.

**Transmission**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Axle Bushings Worn/Cracked**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noise from the rear suspension, especially when going over bumps. Vehicle may feel unstable in corners.
    *   Solution: Replace the worn or cracked rear axle bushings.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Power Window Regulator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Window fails to move up or down, makes a grinding noise, or moves erratically.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty power window regulator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $450+
        Recalls: None Found

*   **Central Locking System Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Doors fail to lock or unlock with the remote or the key, or the alarm system may activate intermittently.
    *   Solution: Diagnose the issue by checking the wiring harness, door lock actuators, and the central locking module. Repair or replace the faulty components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+ (depending on the cause)
        Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rear Hatch Struts Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The rear hatch/trunk lid fails to stay open and falls down.
    *   Solution: Replace the worn-out rear hatch struts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Airbag Warning Light Illumination**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The airbag warning light remains on, indicating a potential issue with the airbag system.
    *   Solution: Scan the airbag system for fault codes to identify the issue. Common causes include faulty seat belt pretensioners, clock spring issues, or airbag module malfunction. Repair or replace the affected components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ (depending on the component)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Volkswagen SportVan, while appreciated for its practicality, can suffer from several common issues. Suspension components such as rear axle bushings can wear out, leading to clunking noises. Electrical problems, including power window regulator failure and central locking system malfunctions, can also occur. Exterior issues like rear hatch strut failure are also common. Interior issues include airbag warning light illumination. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to potentially serious safety concerns, and the cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the specific problem.
