**Introduction:**

The 2019 Volkswagen Constellation is a heavy-duty truck available with various engine and transmission options. It offers impressive performance with its powerful diesel engines and is known for its durability and reliability. However, like most vehicles, it has some reported issues that potential owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**12.0L Cummins ISX12**
- **Oil Leak from Oil Pan Gasket:** A faulty oil pan gasket can cause oil to leak onto the ground, potentially leading to engine damage if left unchecked. **Solution:** Replacing the oil pan gasket. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+. **Recalls:** None Found
- **Turbocharger Failure:** In some cases, the turbocharger may fail due to bearing issues or other internal problems, resulting in reduced engine power and performance. **Solution:** Replacing the turbocharger. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+. **Recalls:** None Found

**15.0L Cummins ISX15**
- **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Issues:** Problems with the EGR valve can lead to reduced engine performance, increased emissions, and rough idling. **Solution:** Cleaning or replacing the EGR valve. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+. **Recalls:** None Found
- **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Problems:** The DPF may experience issues with regeneration, resulting in reduced engine power or potential engine damage if untreated. **Solution:** Forced DPF regeneration or replacing the filter. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+. **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Eaton Fuller Automated Manual Transmission**
- **Shuddering or Jerking:** Some vehicles may exhibit shuddering or jerking during gear changes, potentially indicating a problem with the transmission control module (TCM) or other components. **Solution:** Reprogramming the TCM or replacing transmission parts as needed. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+. **Recalls:** None Found
- **Premature Clutch Wear:** In extreme cases, premature clutch wear can occur due to transmission issues or improper driving habits. **Solution:** Replacing the clutch assembly. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+. **Recalls:** None Found

**12-Speed Eaton Fuller Automated Manual Transmission**
- **Shifting Problems:** Some vehicles may encounter difficulty shifting gears or may get stuck in a specific gear. **Solution:** Diagnosing and repairing the transmission issue, which could involve replacing worn parts or reprogramming the TCM. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+. **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

- **Rear Axle Seal Failure:** Some Constellations may experience failure of the rear axle seals, leading to oil leaks that can affect braking performance. **Solution:** Replacing the axle seals. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+. **Recalls:** None Found
- **Air Suspension Compressor Noise:** The air suspension compressor may produce excessive noise during operation. **Solution:** Replacing the compressor or repairing any leaks in the system. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+. **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

- **Faulty Battery Cables:** In some cases, the battery cables may become loose or corroded, leading to electrical issues such as starting problems or power loss. **Solution:** Inspecting and replacing the battery cables as needed. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+. **Recalls:** None Found
- **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail, leading to loss of power and charging issues. **Solution:** Replacing the alternator. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+. **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rust on Frame:** Some Constellations may experience rust formation on the frame, particularly in areas exposed to harsh conditions. **Solution:** Repairing or replacing the affected frame sections. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $5,000+. **Recalls:** None Found
- **Headlamp Assembly Issues:** In some vehicles, the headlamps may become misaligned or experience condensation issues. **Solution:** Adjusting or replacing the headlamp assemblies. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+. **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Panel Cracking:** The dashboard panel may develop cracks over time, particularly in vehicles exposed to extreme heat or UV radiation. **Solution:** Replacing the dashboard panel. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+. **Recalls:** None Found
- **Seat Belt Tensioner Malfunctions:** The seat belt tensioners may not engage properly in certain situations, compromising occupant safety. **Solution:** Reprogramming the seat belt control module or replacing the tensioners. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+. **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Volkswagen Constellation is known for its durability and reliability, it does have some reported issues that owners should be aware of before purchasing. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some, such as engine leaks or transmission problems, can be costly to repair. It's important for potential owners to consider these issues and weigh them against the vehicle's overall capabilities and reputation before making a decision.
