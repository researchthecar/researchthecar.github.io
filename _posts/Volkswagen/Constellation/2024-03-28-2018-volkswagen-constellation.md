**Introduction:**

The 2018 Volkswagen Constellation is a medium-duty truck available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The 18.3-liter Cummins ISX15 engine produces 450 horsepower and 1,450 lb-ft of torque, while a 12.8-liter Cummins ISL9 engine is available with ratings of 360 horsepower and 1,050 lb-ft of torque or 380 horsepower and 1,150 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 10-speed automatic transmission, a 13-speed manual transmission, and a 12-speed automated manual transmission.

Owners have praised the Constellation for its powerful engines, comfortable ride, and spacious cabin. However, some issues have been reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**18.3-liter Cummins ISX15**

* **Fuel injector failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **EGR valve failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine hesitation, decreased acceleration, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**12.8-liter Cummins ISL9**

* **Turbocharger failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, whistling sound from engine
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-speed automatic transmission:**

* **Shifting issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, delayed shifts
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module or transmission fluid filter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**13-speed manual transmission:**

* **Clutch failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, slipping gears, grinding noise
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Leaf spring breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or banging noise, sagging suspension
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting vehicle, dead battery
    * Solution: Test and replace faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Cracked headlights:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracked or broken headlight lens
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noise from dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten loose bolts or replace dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate control issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air conditioning or heating system
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control unit or AC compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Volkswagen Constellation has some reported issues, but overall, it is a reliable and capable medium-duty truck. The engine and transmission issues, while expensive to fix, are not widespread. The other issues tend to be minor and inexpensive to resolve.
