**Electrical**

*   **Malfunctioning Instrument Cluster**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The instrument cluster may intermittently fail to display information, or display incorrect information such as speed, RPM, or fuel level.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the instrument cluster may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Failures (ABS, Wheel Speed)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: ABS or wheel speed sensors may fail, leading to warning lights and potentially affecting the vehicle's braking and stability control systems.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Poor quality headlight lens**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The headlight lens is low quality causing it to crack early and can possibly fog over.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the headlight lens.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Faulty HVAC controls**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Inability to control the temperature in the car. The temperature controls may be either stuck on full cold or full hot.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the HVAC control panel.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Volkswagen Constellation, while generally a robust workhorse, can experience electrical issues like instrument cluster malfunctions and sensor failures, potentially impacting safety systems. Interior HVAC control faults can affect comfort. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconvenience to potential safety concerns. Repair costs can vary significantly depending on the specific problem and the availability of parts, but can range from a few hundred dollars to over a thousand for some repairs.
