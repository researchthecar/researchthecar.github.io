The 2016 Volkswagen Gol is a subcompact car primarily sold in South America. The Gol offered several engine options, including a 1.0L 3-cylinder, a 1.6L 8V, and a 1.6L 16V inline-4 gasoline engine. The 1.6L 8V engine produces around 101 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque. The Gol is often praised for its affordability and fuel efficiency. Downsides often cited include a basic interior and limited features.

**Engine**

**1.6L 8V Inline-4**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners report higher-than-expected oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups. Symptoms include low oil level warnings and blue smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Initially, check for leaks. If no leaks are found, the issue may be worn piston rings or valve stem seals. Replacement of these components is required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rough Idling/Misfires**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, especially when cold, and occasional misfires. Check engine light may illuminate with codes related to misfires.
    * Solution: This is typically caused by faulty ignition coils or spark plugs. Replace the affected ignition coils and spark plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.6L 16V Inline-4**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty Shifting into Gear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported difficulty shifting into certain gears, particularly 1st and 2nd. This can be more pronounced when the transmission is cold.
    * Solution: Check the clutch cable and linkage for proper adjustment. If the problem persists, the clutch disc or synchros may be worn and need replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Clutch Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch may slip under acceleration, especially in higher gears. This indicates a worn clutch disc.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch disc, pressure plate, and throwout bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Worn Shock Absorbers**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the shock absorbers, leading to a bouncy ride and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the worn shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Noisy Suspension**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noises coming from the suspension, particularly when going over bumps. Often linked to worn sway bar links or bushings.
    * Solution: Replace the worn sway bar links and/or bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Central Locking System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Issues with the central locking system, where doors may not lock or unlock properly with the key fob or interior switch.
    * Solution: Check the door lock actuators and wiring harness for damage. Replace faulty actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 per door
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows failing to operate or operating erratically.
    * Solution: Check the power window motor and switch. Replace faulty motors or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 per window
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the dashboard, particularly in areas exposed to direct sunlight.
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard, or use of a dashboard cover to prevent further cracking.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500 for replacement
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Volkswagen Gol has some common issues, with engine oil consumption and transmission shifting problems being the most concerning and potentially expensive to fix. Suspension and electrical issues also appear, but are generally less severe. Dashboard cracking, while mostly cosmetic, can detract from the interior appearance. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and have the vehicle inspected before purchasing.
