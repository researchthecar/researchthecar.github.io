### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may fail to start after sitting for extended periods; battery may discharge prematurely.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from accessories or faulty modules. Battery replacement if necessary.

The 2022 Volkswagen Nivus demonstrates good overall reliability, with its powertrain and core mechanical components showing minimal reported issues.
