The 2014 Volkswagen Saveiro is a compact pickup truck available with a 1.6-liter gasoline engine or a 2.0-liter diesel engine. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. The Saveiro is known for its versatility, affordability, and fuel efficiency. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of problems.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Gasoline Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Cracking:** A faulty intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
  * Replacement of the intake manifold is required.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, leading to a loss of engine power and potential engine damage.
  * Replacement of the timing chain tensioner is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
  * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Faulty fuel injectors can cause poor engine performance, reduced fuel efficiency, and increased emissions.
  * Replacement of the fuel injectors is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per injector
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure:** The clutch can wear out prematurely, leading to difficulty shifting gears or a loss of power.
  * Replacement of the clutch kit is required.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorber Wear:** The front shock absorbers can wear out prematurely, causing a rough ride and poor handling.
  * Replacement of the front shock absorbers is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per shock
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:** The rear leaf springs can break, causing the vehicle to sag and affect handling.
  * Replacement of the leaf springs is required.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** A faulty alternator or parasitic draw can drain the battery prematurely.
  * Diagnosis and repair of the electrical issue is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Failure:** Premature failure of the headlights can reduce visibility at night.
  * Replacement of the headlight bulbs or assembly is required.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 per headlight
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Bed Liner Cracking:** The plastic bed liner can crack or tear, reducing its durability and ability to protect the bed.
  * Replacement of the bed liner is recommended.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure:** The tailgate hinges can become loose or fail, causing the tailgate to open unexpectedly.
  * Adjustment or replacement of the tailgate hinges is required.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** A loose or rattling dashboard can create annoying noises while driving.
  * Identification and tightening of loose components is necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Panel Trim Loose:** The door panel trim can become loose or detached, affecting its appearance and functionality.
  * Reattachment or replacement of the door panel trim is required.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Volkswagen Saveiro is a versatile and fuel-efficient pickup truck, but it is not without its issues. While some problems are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more serious and costly. Overall, the severity of the issues and the frequency with which they occur are typical for a vehicle in this class.
