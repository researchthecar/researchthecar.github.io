The 2022 Volkswagen Saveiro is a subcompact utility pickup truck popular in South America. It is typically equipped with a 1.6-liter inline-4 engine producing around 101-110 horsepower and 111-114 lb-ft of torque, depending on the fuel type and market. A 5-speed manual transmission is the standard option. Owners generally appreciate the Saveiro for its versatility, fuel efficiency, and compact size, making it suitable for urban environments and light-duty work. Common dislikes include limited rear passenger space, basic interior features, and engine power.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups between oil changes. Symptoms include low oil level warnings, blue smoke from the exhaust (indicating oil burning), and a noticeable decrease in oil level on the dipstick.
    *   Solution: In some cases, replacing the piston rings or valve stem seals has been suggested as a potential fix.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

*   **Difficult Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging certain gears, especially first and second, has been reported. This may be more pronounced when the transmission is cold. Some users experience a grinding noise when shifting.
    *   Solution: The recommended fix may be replacing the clutch or clutch cable.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Premature Bushing Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported premature wear of suspension bushings, particularly in the front. Symptoms include clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, and uneven tire wear.
    *   Solution: Replacing the worn bushings is the typical solution. Sometimes the entire control arm is replaced if the bushings are not available separately or are too difficult to press in/out.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can freeze or crash intermittently, leading to a loss of navigation, audio, and other functions.
    *   Solution: Performing a system reset or updating the software to the latest version can sometimes resolve the issue. In some cases, the head unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $500+ (Head Unit Replacement)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Poor Quality Bed Liner (if equipped)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some models are equipped with a bed liner that can be prone to cracking or peeling, especially under heavy use or exposure to sunlight.
    *   Solution: Replacing the bed liner with a more durable aftermarket option or spraying in a bed liner.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported various rattling noises emanating from the dashboard, door panels, and rear of the cab, particularly on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Identifying the source of the rattle and tightening loose panels or applying sound-deadening material.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies greatly, $50 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Volkswagen Saveiro has some common issues that owners encounter. Most of the issues are inexpensive to fix, but some issues such as engine issues can be quite expensive to repair.
