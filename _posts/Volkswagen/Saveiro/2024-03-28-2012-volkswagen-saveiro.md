**Introduction:**

The 2012 Volkswagen Saveiro is a compact pickup truck that combines practicality with a rugged exterior. It's available with a choice of two engine options: a 1.6-liter gasoline engine and a 1.8-liter gasoline engine. The 1.6-liter engine produces 101 horsepower and 153 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.8-liter engine generates 122 horsepower and 159 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a 5-speed manual transmission.

Owners appreciate the Saveiro's versatility, affordable price tag, and reliable performance. However, like any vehicle, it has its fair share of issues.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Gasoline Engine**

* **Oil Leaks:** Leaks can occur from the oil pan, oil filter housing, and valve cover gasket. Symptoms include oil spots under the vehicle and low oil levels. The solution is to replace the faulty gasket or seal. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $500+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, leading to a rattling noise from the engine. The engine may also lose power or stall. The solution is to replace the timing chain tensioner. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+

**1.8-liter Gasoline Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing a vacuum leak. Symptoms include rough idling, stalling, and loss of power. The solution is to replace the intake manifold gasket. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $400+
* **Water Pump Failure:** The water pump can fail, leading to overheating. Symptoms include a high-temperature gauge reading, steam from the engine, and coolant leaks. The solution is to replace the water pump. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $600+

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Problems:** The clutch can wear out or fail, resulting in difficulty shifting gears or slipping. The solution is to replace the clutch assembly. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Shift Linkage Issues:** The shift linkage can become loose or damaged, causing difficulty shifting gears. The solution is to repair or replace the shift linkage. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $300+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely due to a faulty alternator or parasitic draw. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle and dim headlights. The solution is to replace the alternator or trace and repair the parasitic draw. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $500+
* **Electrical Shorts:** Electrical shorts can occur in various parts of the wiring harness, leading to electrical malfunctions such as flickering lights, non-functional gauges, or blown fuses. The solution is to locate and repair the electrical short. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:** The door handles can break or become loose, making it difficult to open the doors. The solution is to replace the door handle assembly. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $300+
* **Rust:** Rust can occur on the body panels, particularly around the wheel wells and undercarriage. The solution is to repair the rust and apply rust protection coating. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** The dashboard can rattle due to loose screws or components. The solution is to tighten the screws or replace the rattling components. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $300+
* **Air Conditioning Issues:** The air conditioning system may not blow cold air due to a refrigerant leak, faulty compressor, or clogged condenser. The solution is to repair or replace the faulty components. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $1,000+
* **Seat Belt Buckle Malfunction:** The seat belt buckle may not engage or release properly. The solution is to replace the seat belt buckle assembly. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $200+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Volkswagen Saveiro is a reliable and practical pickup truck. However, it is not without its issues. Most of the reported problems are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. However, some engine and electrical issues can be more severe and costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing a Saveiro.
