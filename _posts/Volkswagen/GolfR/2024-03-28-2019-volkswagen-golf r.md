**Introduction**

The 2019 Volkswagen Golf R is a high-performance hatchback that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The Golf R comes standard with a six-speed manual transmission, and a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is available as an option.

People who like the Golf R praise its powerful engine, sharp handling, and spacious interior. However, some people find the ride to be too firm and the fuel economy to be disappointing.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Hesitation or stalling during acceleration**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may hesitate or stall during acceleration, especially when the vehicle is cold.
    * Solution: Update the engine control software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Excessive oil consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume excessive oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail, causing the engine to lose power and stall.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding noise when shifting gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may make a grinding noise when shifting gears, especially when the vehicle is cold.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch plate and/or pressure plate.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Hesitation or shuddering during shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or shudder during shifting, especially when the vehicle is cold.
    * Solution: Update the transmission control software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear axle vibration**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear axle may vibrate at certain speeds.
    * Solution: Replace the rear axle bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the battery and/or update the electrical control software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The headlights may fail to turn on or may flicker.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs and/or update the electrical control software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Minor corrosion on rear hatch**
    * Description/Symptoms: Minor corrosion may appear on the rear hatch.
    * Solution: Replace the rear hatch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle at certain speeds or over rough roads.
    * Solution: Apply foam tape or other sound-deadening material to the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak water into the interior.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Volkswagen Golf R is a high-performance hatchback with a number of potential issues. The most common issues are related to the engine, such as hesitation during acceleration, excessive oil consumption, and timing chain failure. Other issues include grinding noise when shifting gears (manual transmission), hesitation or shuddering during shifting (automatic transmission), dashboard rattles, sunroof leaks, and minor corrosion on the rear hatch.

While some of these issues can be minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Therefore, it is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a 2019 Volkswagen Golf R.
