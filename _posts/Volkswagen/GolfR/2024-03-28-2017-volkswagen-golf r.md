**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volkswagen Golf R is a performance-oriented variant of the Volkswagen Golf hatchback. It features a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with a 6-speed manual or 7-speed dual-clutch DSG automatic transmission. The Golf R produces 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It is known for its sharp handling, quick acceleration, and comfortable interior. However, it has received some criticism for its stiff ride and high price.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Walnut blasting to remove carbon buildup
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**7-Speed DSG Automatic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or hesitation during gear changes, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix cause of drain (e.g., parasitic draw)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on cause
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, lagging, or displaying errors
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof making noise when opened or closed
    * Solution: Adjust sunroof alignment or replace sunroof assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control not blowing air or malfunctioning
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair issue (e.g., blend door, blower motor)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on issue
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Squeaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises from rear seat area
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair source of squeaking (e.g., seat frame, seat belt)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Volkswagen Golf R has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While it offers impressive performance and handling, it has been plagued by some significant issues, particularly with the DSG transmission and engine carbon buildup. The cost to fix these issues can be high, making the Golf R a potentially expensive car to own and maintain.
