**Introduction:**

The 2013 Volkswagen Routan is a minivan produced from 2008 to 2014. It shares its platform with the Chrysler Town & Country, Dodge Grand Caravan, and the Volkswagen Caravelle. The Routan is powered by a 3.6L V6 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The V6 engine delivers 283 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The Routan has a comfortable and spacious interior, a variety of features, and good fuel economy. However, it is not as sporty as some of its competitors.

**Engine**

* **3.6L V6**

    * **Engine Misfire**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
        * Solution: Replace spark plugs and/or ignition coils
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and related components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, delayed acceleration
        * Solution: Repair or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminated, flickering headlights, difficulty starting the vehicle
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Heating or air conditioning not working properly
        * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Volkswagen Routan has some potential issues with its engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior climate control. The engine misfire and timing chain tensioner failure are the most serious issues, as they can lead to major engine damage. The transmission slipping and battery drain issues are also significant, as they can affect the vehicle's drivability and reliability. The climate control malfunction is less of a concern, but it can still be frustrating when the heating or air conditioning is not working properly. Overall, the severity and cost of the potential issues with the Routan should be considered before purchasing this vehicle.
