**Introduction:**

The 2009 Volkswagen Routan is a family-friendly minivan known for its spacious cabin, versatile seating arrangements, and comfortable ride. It offered a 3.8L V6 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6 (251 hp / 262 lb-ft)**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idling, and engine performance issues.
    * Solution: Replacement of intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations when the transmission shifts from 1st to 2nd gear.
    * Solution: Replacement of torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noises when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely.
    * Solution: Identify and fix electrical components causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Lights Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buildup of condensation inside the fog lights.
    * Solution: Replacement of fog light assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sliding Door Track Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing sliding doors due to corrosion on the tracks.
    * Solution: Lubrication or replacement of sliding door tracks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Volkswagen Routan has several issues ranging from moderate to severe in terms of cost and impact on performance. The most common issues include the engine intake manifold gasket failure, transmission torque converter shudder, and suspension control arm bushings. Overall, the cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the severity of the issue and the chosen repair facility.
