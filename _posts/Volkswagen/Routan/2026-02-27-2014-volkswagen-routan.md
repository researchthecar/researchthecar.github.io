# 2014 Volkswagen Routan

### Overview
The 2014 Volkswagen Routan, a rebadged Chrysler Town & Country, shares many of its mechanical components with its Dodge counterpart. While generally considered a decent family hauler, certain areas have shown a propensity for issues. Owners have reported a mixed bag of reliability, with some experiencing significant problems, particularly with the powertrain and electrical systems. Regular maintenance is crucial for this vehicle, but specific components may require attention beyond routine service. This analysis focuses on documented common failures and their associated costs, excluding standard wear and tear.

---


## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6
**Intake Manifold Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, rough idle, potential overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks around the valve covers, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500


## Transmission

### 62TE 6-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, potential internal repairs or transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000+

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, 'limp mode', check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sway bar links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Premature Strut/Shock Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, visible leaks from struts/shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of struts and/or shocks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent power windows, door locks, interior lights, or other accessory issues.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Power Sliding Doors
**Malfunctioning Power Sliding Doors**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not opening/closing properly, slow operation, error messages.
- **Solution:** Adjustment, lubrication, or replacement of motors, cables, or sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Volkswagen Routan presents a mixed reliability profile. The 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine can experience intake manifold leaks and valve cover gasket oil leaks. The 62TE automatic transmission is a common source of concern, with reports of harsh shifting, slipping, and TCM failures. Suspension components like sway bar links and struts may require replacement sooner than expected. Electrical issues, particularly with the Body Control Module and power sliding doors, are also noted. While the exterior and interior are generally robust, potential buyers should be aware of these common powertrain and electrical concerns and budget accordingly for potential repairs beyond standard maintenance.
