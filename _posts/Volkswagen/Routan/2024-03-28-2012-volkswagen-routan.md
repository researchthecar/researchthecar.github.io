**Introduction:**

The 2012 Volkswagen Routan is a minivan that was offered with two engine options: a 3.6-liter V6 and a 4.0-liter V6. Both engines were paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The Routan was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and strong V6 engines. However, it was criticized for its bland styling, high fuel consumption, and lack of some features found in competitors.

**Engine**

**3.6-liter V6**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain can fail, causing catastrophic engine damage. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, decreased performance, and illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: The timing chain and related components must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: 12V502

**4.0-liter V6**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, reducing engine performance and causing rough idling.
    * Solution: The intake valves must be cleaned or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The shift solenoids can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not at all. Symptoms include harsh shifting, slipping, and illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: The shift solenoids must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The strut mounts can fail, causing a clunking noise over bumps and reduced handling.
    * Solution: The strut mounts must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator can fail, causing the battery to drain and the vehicle to stall. Symptoms include difficulty starting, flickering lights, and a battery warning light on the dashboard.
    * Solution: The alternator must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cover Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear bumper cover can crack around the license plate area.
    * Solution: The bumper cover must be replaced or repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Interior**

* **Front Headliner Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front headliner can sag and detach from the roof of the vehicle.
    * Solution: The headliner must be reattached or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary**

The 2012 Volkswagen Routan has been plagued by several issues, including timing chain failure, intake valve deposits, and electrical problems. These issues can be costly to repair and can significantly impact the reliability of the vehicle. While the Routan offers a spacious interior and comfortable ride, potential buyers should be aware of the potential problems associated with this vehicle before making a purchasing decision.
