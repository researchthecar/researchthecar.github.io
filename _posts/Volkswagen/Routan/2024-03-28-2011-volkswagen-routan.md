**2011 Volkswagen Routan**

The 2011 Volkswagen Routan is a rebadged version of the Chrysler Town & Country. It is powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 283 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a six-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Routan for its spacious interior, comfortable seating, and powerful engine. However, some people have complained about its high fuel consumption and lack of features.

**Engine**

* **3.6L V6**
    * **Intake manifold runner control issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the engine to hesitate or stall at low speeds.
        * Solution: The intake manifold runner control valve or solenoid needs to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the engine to make a rattling noise and lose power.
        * Solution: The timing chain tensioner needs to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the transmission to hesitate or slip when shifting gears.
        * Solution: The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front struts leaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the front of the vehicle to sag and the ride to be rough.
    * Solution: The front struts need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Power window switches failing**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the power windows to stop working.
    * Solution: The power window switches need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the battery to drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not running.
    * Solution: The battery may need to be replaced, or an electrical draw may need to be diagnosed and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate latch failing**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the tailgate to open unexpectedly.
    * Solution: The tailgate latch needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the dashboard to crack and break.
    * Solution: The dashboard may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Touchscreen unresponsive**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue can cause the touchscreen to become unresponsive or difficult to use.
    * Solution: The touchscreen may need to be recalibrated or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Volkswagen Routan is a reliable vehicle, but it does have some common issues. The most serious issues include the intake manifold runner control issues, timing chain tensioner failure, and transmission slipping. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to factor them into the cost of ownership.
