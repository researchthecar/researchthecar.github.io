**Introduction**

The 2017 Volkswagen Caddy is a compact van that offers a spacious and practical interior. It is available with two engine options: a 1.4-liter turbocharged gasoline engine and a 2.0-liter turbocharged diesel engine. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

People who like the Volkswagen Caddy praise its roomy interior, comfortable seats, and versatile cargo space. However, some people have complained about its lack of power, especially with the gasoline engine.

**Engine**

* **1.4-liter Turbocharged Gasoline Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0-liter Turbocharged Diesel Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injector(s).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per injector
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Control Unit (TCU) Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or jerking, loss of gears.
        * Solution: Replace the TCU.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, excessive noise over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the front shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per shock absorber
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, dim headlights, electrical system failure.
    * Solution: Replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate won't open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Screen Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Black screen, unresponsive touchscreen, software glitches.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the screen.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 Volkswagen Caddy is a reliable and practical vehicle. However, there are some potential issues to be aware of, especially with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they should be considered when researching and purchasing this vehicle.
