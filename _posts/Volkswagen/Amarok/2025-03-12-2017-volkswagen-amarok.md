The 2017 Volkswagen Amarok is a popular pickup truck known for its car-like handling, comfortable interior, and robust build quality. While it generally receives positive reviews, some common issues have been reported by owners. Engine options for the 2017 model year primarily include a range of 2.0-liter TDI diesel engines with varying power outputs and a 3.0-liter TDI V6 diesel. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual and an 8-speed automatic. Owners often praise the Amarok for its refined driving experience and practical capabilities, but some common complaints include issues with the EGR cooler, turbocharger, and occasional electrical glitches.

**Engine**

**2.0L TDI Diesel**

*   **EGR Cooler Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The EGR cooler can crack or leak, leading to coolant loss, white smoke from the exhaust, and potential engine overheating. A check engine light may illuminate.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the EGR cooler.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noises from the engine, reduced power, and the engine entering limp mode. This can be caused by oil starvation, over-boosting or general wear and tear.
    *   Solution: Replacement or rebuild of the turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.0L TDI V6 Diesel**

*   **Intake Manifold Swirl Flap Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The swirl flaps within the intake manifold can become damaged or detached, leading to reduced engine performance, rough idling, and a check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing harsh or jerky shifting, particularly between lower gears.
    *   Solution: A software update to the transmission control module (TCM) might resolve the issue. In more severe cases, valve body replacement or complete transmission rebuild may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 (Software Update), $1500 - $4000+ (Valve Body/Rebuild)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

*   **Central Locking System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Central locking system failing to lock or unlock doors consistently.
    *   Solution: Diagnosing and repairing or replacing faulty door lock actuators or the central control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Glow Plug Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idling, and a check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty glow plug(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Tailgate Handle Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening the tailgate due to a sticking or broken handle mechanism.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the tailgate handle assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Infotainment System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, reboot unexpectedly, or experience issues with Bluetooth connectivity.
    *   Solution: Software updates can sometimes resolve these issues. In more severe cases, replacement of the infotainment unit may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $1000+ (Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2017 Volkswagen Amarok presents a mixed bag of potential issues. Engine-related problems, particularly with the EGR cooler, turbocharger, and intake manifold, can be costly to repair. Transmission issues, while potentially manageable with software updates, could escalate to more significant expenses. Electrical and exterior problems are generally less severe but still worth noting. Overall, a thorough inspection and maintenance record review are crucial before purchasing a used 2017 Amarok.
