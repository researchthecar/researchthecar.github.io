**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volkswagen Amarok is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a variety of engine and transmission options. It is known for its rugged off-road capabilities, spacious cabin, and impressive towing and payload capacities. The Amarok is powered by either a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine or a 3.0L turbocharged V6 diesel engine, both of which are mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine:**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Diesel**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Turbocharged V6 Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and loud noises from the engine.
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips out of gear, causing sudden loss of power and potential vehicle damage.
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or cracked leaf springs, resulting in a rough ride and poor handling.
    * Solution: Leaf spring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per leaf spring
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Battery replacement and repair of electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Clearcoat Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clearcoat on the exterior paint peels off, exposing the underlying paint.
    * Solution: Repainting the affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per panel
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Heater Fan Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heater fan stops working, resulting in poor heating and ventilation.
    * Solution: Heater fan replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cracked Dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks develop on the dashboard, especially in warm climates.
    * Solution: Dashboard replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Volkswagen Amarok is a capable and versatile pickup truck, but it is not without its issues. The most severe and expensive issues include transmission slipping, turbocharger failure, and clearcoat peeling. However, many of the interior and electrical issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Amarok is a well-built vehicle with a solid reputation for reliability, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing.
