# 2020 Bugatti Divo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Bugatti Divo is an ultra-exclusive, track-focused hypercar produced in extremely limited numbers. Because of its exotic nature, production volumes, and specialized hand-built engineering, broad long-term reliability trends and owner complaint data are virtually non-existent in public registries. Prospective buyers should rely heavily on dedicated exotic car specialists and official manufacturer maintenance protocols rather than conventional used-car statistics.

---

## Engine

### 8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16

The Divo features an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing approximately 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque. It delivers astonishing straight-line performance and intense track capability, though routine maintenance requires specialized factory service.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed through a heavy-duty 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission designed to handle the immense torque output of the W16 engine. It offers rapid gear shifts suited for high-speed track driving and demanding performance conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V732000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Divo&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** After restarting the car, the Drive Mode remains in Handling mode affecting the ESC performance, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bugatti will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the software of the affected control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V731000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Divo&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken driveshaft could result in parts detaching and becoming a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bugatti will notify owners, and dealers will replace rear left-hand driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Bugatti Divo is a rare collector's hypercar where traditional used-car reliability metrics do not apply. Maintenance, servicing, and inspection should be handled strictly through authorized Bugatti channels by certified technicians.
