# 2021 Bugatti Divo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Bugatti Divo is an ultra-exclusive, track-focused hypercar produced in extremely limited numbers. Because of its bespoke nature and low production volume, wide-scale reliability data and consumer complaint histories are virtually nonexistent. Prospective buyers should anticipate extraordinary maintenance overhead, requiring specialized factory servicing and highly specialized handling.

---

## Engine

### 8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16

The Divo features an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine producing approximately 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque. It delivers immense performance with exotic engineering, though long-term durability heavily depends on meticulous adherence to factory maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed through a heavy-duty 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission designed to handle massive torque loads. The unit offers lightning-fast shifts suited for track use, but clutch service and replacement costs are exceptionally high.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V732000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Divo&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** After restarting the car, the Drive Mode remains in Handling mode affecting the ESC performance, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bugatti will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the software of the affected control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Bugatti Divo is a collector-grade hypercar where traditional used-car reliability metrics do not apply. Maintenance must be performed exclusively by factory-certified technicians, and operating costs scale proportionately with its multi-million dollar valuation. Buyers should verify complete service history and professional pre-purchase inspections before acquisition.
