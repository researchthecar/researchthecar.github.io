**Introduction:**

The 2020 Bugatti Divo is a limited-edition hypercar limited to just 40 units. It is based on the Bugatti Chiron, but features a more aerodynamic design and a number of performance upgrades. The Divo is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine that produces 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission.

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system may occasionally freeze**
  * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze and become unresponsive.
  * Solution: Restart the infotainment system by pressing and holding the power button for 10 seconds.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0-$100
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate control system may not blow cold air**
  * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not blow cold air, even when set to the coldest setting.
  * Solution: Have the climate control system inspected by a qualified mechanic. The issue may be caused by a faulty compressor, condenser, or evaporator.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Bugatti Divo seems to be a very reliable vehicle with only a few minor issues. The most common issue is with the infotainment system freezing, which can be easily fixed by restarting the system. The climate control system may also not blow cold air, but this issue is more expensive to fix.
