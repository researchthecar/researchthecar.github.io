# 2020 Bugatti Chiron Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Bugatti Chiron is an ultra-high-performance hypercar built for elite speed and engineering excellence. As a low-volume, hand-built exotic vehicle, shopping for a used Chiron requires careful inspection by certified specialists rather than standard used-car checks. Because historical ownership is typically meticulous and mileage is usually very low, mechanical wear is rarely typical of ordinary passenger vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16

The Chiron features an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine generating approximately 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque. It is an exceptionally complex engineering masterpiece requiring specialized maintenance and servicing by authorized Bugatti technicians.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed through a specially developed 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission designed to handle immense torque outputs. The unit provides rapid gear shifts suited for extreme performance driving with high reliability when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V732000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Chiron&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** After restarting the car, the Drive Mode remains in Handling mode affecting the ESC performance, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bugatti will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the software of the affected control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V731000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Chiron&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken driveshaft could result in parts detaching and becoming a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bugatti will notify owners, and dealers will replace rear left-hand driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V096000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Chiron&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The audible chime may not activate when the vehicle starts, and the driver has not buckled their seat belt. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." The driver may be unaware that their seat belt is not fastened, increasing the risk of injury during.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Bugatti Chiron represents the pinnacle of automotive engineering, and its mechanical systems reflect extreme bespoke craftsmanship. While formal owner complaint data is virtually nonexistent due to its ultra-rare production numbers, buyers must factor in the extraordinarily high cost of routine maintenance, specialized servicing, and factory inspections. Always secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an authorized Bugatti service partner before finalizing a purchase.
