# 2021 Bugatti Chiron Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Bugatti Chiron represents the pinnacle of hypercar engineering, combining extreme performance with ultra-luxury craftsmanship. For prospective buyers exploring the pre-owned market, understanding the powertrain options and overall ownership context helps navigate this rare, high-end vehicle class.

---

## Engine

### 8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16

The Chiron features a massive 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine generating approximately 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque. It delivers astonishing acceleration and high-speed stability, though maintenance requires specialized servicing by certified technicians.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is managed by a rapid-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission designed to handle immense torque output while maintaining smooth shifts under aggressive driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V096000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Chiron&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The audible chime may not activate when the vehicle starts, and the driver has not buckled their seat belt. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." The driver may be unaware that their seat belt is not fastened, increasing the risk of injury during.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Bugatti Chiron is an engineering marvel with no widespread recurring complaint patterns reported in available owner data. Buyers should prioritize thorough professional pre-purchase inspections and verify specialized maintenance histories before finalizing a purchase.
