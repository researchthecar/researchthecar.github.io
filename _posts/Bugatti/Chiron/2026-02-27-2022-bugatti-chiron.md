# 2022 Bugatti Chiron Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Evaluating a hypercar like the Bugatti Chiron involves looking past conventional daily-driver metrics, focusing instead on ultra-exclusive engineering, hand-built craftsmanship, and specialized maintenance requirements. While standard owner complaint datasets often have minimal entries for ultra-low-volume exotic vehicles, prospective buyers must account for the extreme financial commitment required for upkeep, specialized servicing, and component replacements.

---

## Engine

### 8.0L Quad-Turbocharged W16

The 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine produces approximately 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque in standard configurations. This masterpiece of modern engineering delivers staggering performance but requires factory-trained technicians and specialized equipment for any major service.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is specifically engineered to handle the massive torque output of the W16 engine while providing rapid gear changes. It requires meticulous maintenance and adherence to service intervals to ensure reliable operation under extreme loads.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V096000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bugatti&model=Chiron&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The audible chime may not activate when the vehicle starts, and the driver has not buckled their seat belt. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." The driver may be unaware that their seat belt is not fastened, increasing the risk of injury during.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Bugatti Chiron is an engineering marvel designed for elite performance rather than conventional commuting. Buyers in the pre-owned market should prioritize comprehensive service history records, verification of factory maintenance compliance, and inspection by authorized Bugatti specialists before purchase.
