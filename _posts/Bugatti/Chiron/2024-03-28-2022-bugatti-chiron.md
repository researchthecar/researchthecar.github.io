**2022 Bugatti Chiron**

The 2022 Bugatti Chiron is a mid-engine sports car manufactured by Bugatti Automobiles S.A.S.. The Chiron is powered by an 8.0-liter quad-turbocharged W16 engine that produces 1,500 horsepower and 1,180 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The Chiron can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 2.4 seconds and has a top speed of 261 mph.

The Chiron is a very rare and expensive car, with only 500 units being produced. The base price of the Chiron is $2.998 million.

**Engine**

* **8.0-liter Quad-Turbocharged W16 Engine**

**Issues**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbochargers can fail, causing a loss of power and performance.
    * Solution: Replace the turbochargers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20,000 - $40,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The oil pump can fail, causing a loss of oil pressure and lubrication of the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

**Issues**

* **Clutch Pack Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch packs can fail, causing the transmission to slip or lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch packs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $20,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Control Unit (TCU) Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The TCU can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not at all.
    * Solution: Replace the TCU.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
