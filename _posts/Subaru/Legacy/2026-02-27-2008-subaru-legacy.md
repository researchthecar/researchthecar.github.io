# 2008 Subaru Legacy

### Overview
The 2008 Subaru Legacy, known for its Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive system and boxer engines, generally offers a solid ownership experience. While it excels in handling and all-weather capability, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding reliability. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for the 2.5L and 3.0L engine options, as well as the automatic and manual transmissions. Understanding these potential weak points can help owners budget for and address issues proactively, ensuring the continued performance and longevity of their Legacy.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L Boxer (Naturally Aspirated)
**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust, overheating, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gaskets and often associated components like head bolts.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil drips, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### 3.0L Boxer (H6)
**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Similar to 2.5L: coolant leaks, white smoke, overheating, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gaskets and associated hardware.
- **Cost:** $1800 - $3500

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil drips, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets.
- **Cost:** $350 - $900


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 5-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into gear.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or internal transmission repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sway bar end links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Worn Struts/Shocks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, excessive body roll, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of struts and/or shocks.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, door locks, interior lights, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels
**Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion on wheel arches, rocker panels, and around windows, especially in rust-belt regions.
- **Solution:** Rust repair and repainting, or replacement of affected panels.
- **Cost:** $300 - $2000+


### Headlights
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced light output, poor visibility at night.
- **Solution:** Headlight restoration kit or replacement of headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor and/or resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Seat Fabric
**Wear and Tear**
- **Symptoms:** Fraying or thinning of fabric on driver's seat bolsters.
- **Solution:** Seat cover installation or professional upholstery repair.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Subaru Legacy is a capable vehicle, but potential buyers and current owners should be aware of common issues, particularly head gasket failures on both engine variants and oil leaks. Transmission issues, while not universal, can also arise. Suspension components like sway bar links and struts are typical wear items that may require attention. Electrical problems, especially related to the BCM, and exterior rust in certain climates are also points to consider. Proactive maintenance and inspection for these specific areas can help mitigate unexpected repair costs and ensure a reliable ownership experience.
