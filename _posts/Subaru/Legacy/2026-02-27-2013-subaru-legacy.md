# 2013 Subaru Legacy

### Overview
The 2013 Subaru Legacy is generally considered a reliable vehicle, known for its standard all-wheel-drive system and comfortable ride. It offers a good balance of practicality and driving engagement, making it a solid choice for various drivers. While most owners report a positive experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from a reliability standpoint. This analysis focuses on documented issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for the 2013 model year across its different configurations.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L Boxer (Naturally Aspirated)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil burning between changes, potentially leading to low oil levels.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly. In some cases, piston ring replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Head Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gaskets and related components.
- **Cost:** $1,800 - $3,500


### 2.5L Boxer Turbocharged (2.5GT)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, excessive smoke, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Subaru Legacy presents a generally reliable profile, particularly with its standard all-wheel-drive system. The most frequently cited concerns revolve around the naturally aspirated and turbocharged 2.5L Boxer engines, specifically oil consumption and potential head gasket issues. The CVT transmission has also seen some reports of hesitation or jerking, though often resolvable with maintenance. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are present but not overwhelmingly common. The 3.6L engine and manual/automatic transmissions are notably robust. Overall, with diligent maintenance and awareness of potential engine oil consumption, the 2013 Legacy remains a dependable choice.
