**Introduction:**

The 2005 Subaru Legacy is a midsize sedan known for its all-wheel drive capability and spacious interior. It offers two engine options: a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated boxer engine producing 165 horsepower and 166 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.5-liter turbocharged boxer engine producing 243 horsepower and 241 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed manual transmission.

Owners generally praise the Legacy's handling, comfort, and reliability, but criticize its lackluster acceleration and fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.5-Liter Naturally Aspirated Boxer Engine**

**Oil Consumption:**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, resulting in blue smoke from the exhaust and low oil levels.
Solution: Piston ring replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**2.5-Liter Turbocharged Boxer Engine**

**Turbocharger Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, and a whining or whistling noise from the turbocharger.
Solution: Turbocharger replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, slipping gears, or refusing to downshift.
Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Knock:**
Description/Symptoms: Knocking sound from the front suspension while driving over bumps.
Solution: Replacing the front lower control arms.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, flickering dashboard, and difficulty starting the car.
Solution: Alternator replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Bulb Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Prematurely burnt-out headlight bulbs.
Solution: Replacing the headlight bulbs with higher-quality ones.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Sunroof Shattering:**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof spontaneously shattering while driving or parked.
Solution: Replacement sunroof glass.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2005 Subaru Legacy has some noteworthy issues, but they are generally not widespread and can be addressed with proper maintenance and repairs. The cost of repairs can be moderate to expensive, depending on the severity of the issue. Overall, the Legacy remains a solid choice for those seeking a reliable and comfortable all-wheel drive sedan.
