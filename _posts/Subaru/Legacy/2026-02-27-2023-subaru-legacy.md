# 2023 Subaru Legacy Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Subaru Legacy brings standard all-wheel drive, spacious accommodations, and updated technology to the midsize sedan class. While overall mechanical stability remains consistent with Subaru's reputation, early owner reports highlight a few electronic quirks and visibility concerns that prospective buyers should keep in mind during an inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The base 2.5-liter naturally aspirated flat-four engine produces 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. It is known for balanced efficiency and reliable everyday performance when properly maintained.

### 2.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The turbocharged 2.4-liter flat-four engine delivers a more spirited 260 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque for sportier trims. This engine offers brisk acceleration while maintaining Subaru's signature boxer layout, though routine maintenance is essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Matched to both engine choices, the Lineartronic CVT features simulated gear steps to mimic a traditional automatic while maximizing fuel economy. It pairs smoothly with the standard all-wheel-drive system under normal driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Panel

Electronic displays and infotainment functions are heavily integrated into the cabin, occasionally prompting owner reports concerning startup failures.

**Center Console and Instrument Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Upon cold starts, the main center console display and electronic instrument panels may fail to power on, knocking out the infotainment, climate controls, backup camera, and driver instrumentation.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's software updated or diagnostic codes scanned by a dealer or qualified technician, as a system reboot or module replacement may be required.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Factory glass durability is a common point of discussion, particularly given the integrated camera housings mounted near the rearview mirror.

**Premature Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden stress cracks or chips developing near the lower edge of the windshield or wiper area, sometimes expanding quickly across the glass.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield thoroughly for existing chips or stress fractures before purchase, and replace the glass with OEM-spec components if necessary to ensure proper camera calibration for safety systems.

## Safety

### EyeSight Driver Assist

Subaru's EyeSight suite manages safety features like pre-collision braking and adaptive cruise control, though sensor sensitivity can occasionally trigger false warnings.

**Spurious Forward Collision Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance warning system may independently activate and display an "Obstacle Detected" message without any physical obstructions present in front of the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Ensure the windshield camera sensors are clean and free of debris, and consult a dealer to check for system calibrations or software updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V647000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Legacy&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Separation of the front end of the driveshaft increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will clean the bolt mounting surfaces of the center support brace and install new bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V755000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Park/Neutral Start Interlock Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Legacy&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative inhibitor switch may prevent the reverse lights from illuminating and the rearview camera image from displaying, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the inhibitor switch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Subaru Legacy is a practical, capable sedan offering standard all-wheel drive and strong safety ratings. While owner complaints point to occasional electronic display glitches and sensitive driver-assistance warnings, these issues are manageable. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the infotainment system functionality, test all driver aids, and inspect the windshield for hidden cracks prior to purchase.
