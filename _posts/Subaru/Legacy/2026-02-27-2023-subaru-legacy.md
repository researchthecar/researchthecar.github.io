# 2023 Subaru Legacy

### Overview
The 2023 Subaru Legacy presents a generally strong reliability profile, building on Subaru's reputation for durable vehicles. It offers a comfortable ride, standard all-wheel drive, and a choice of two capable Boxer engines. While most owners report a positive experience with minimal unexpected issues, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen occasional reports. This analysis focuses on documented technical concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers. The vehicle's design prioritizes longevity, but understanding common, albeit infrequent, issues can aid in proactive maintenance and informed decision-making.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Subaru Legacy demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, with the majority of its systems performing without significant reported issues. The standard 2.5L engine and CVT transmission are particularly robust. The optional 2.4L turbocharged engine has seen minor reports of oil consumption in some early units, but this is not a widespread concern and is generally manageable. The infotainment system occasionally exhibits minor glitches, typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the Legacy is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a practical and capable sedan with standard all-wheel drive, offering a solid ownership experience with minimal expected technical headaches beyond routine maintenance.
