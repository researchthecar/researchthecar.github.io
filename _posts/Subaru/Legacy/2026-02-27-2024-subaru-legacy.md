# 2024 Subaru Legacy

The 2024 Subaru Legacy continues Subaru's tradition of offering a reliable and practical sedan, particularly appealing to those seeking standard all-wheel drive and a comfortable ride. ### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Subaru Legacy demonstrates strong overall reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engines and suspension generally performing without significant widespread issues. The primary areas of concern, based on early reports, tend to be within the infotainment system, specifically screen unresponsiveness, and occasional battery drain issues. The CVT transmission, while generally robust, has seen some isolated reports of hesitation. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can impact the ownership experience. Addressing these electrical and transmission nuances with timely software updates or maintenance can help ensure a smooth and dependable ownership journey for most drivers.
