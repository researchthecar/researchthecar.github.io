# 2024 Subaru Legacy Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Subaru Legacy continues to offer a comfortable ride, standard all-wheel drive, and an updated interior. However, early owner reports highlight a few recurring issues—particularly concerning infotainment stability, driver-assistance system glitches, and interior electronics—that prospective buyers should keep in mind.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

A naturally aspirated 2.5-liter horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine producing around 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque. It serves as the standard powerplant for base models, offering balanced efficiency and daily drivability.

### 2.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

A turbocharged 2.4-liter boxer engine pushing out approximately 260 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, equipped on higher trims like the Sport and Touring XT. It provides strong acceleration but requires careful attention to maintenance and monitoring.

**Engine Failure and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature engine failure requiring complete replacement, alongside warning lights for related vehicle systems.
- **Solution:** Consult a certified dealer or mechanic for diagnostic testing and potential warranty claim evaluation.
- **Cost:** Over $6,000

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A continuously variable transmission paired with Subaru's symmetrical all-wheel-drive system across all trims. It is designed for optimal fuel economy and smooth power delivery during everyday commuting.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

Manages the vehicle's central touchscreen, media playback, and smartphone integration features.

**Infotainment Freezing and CarPlay Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display may freeze, display loading error messages, randomly mute audio calls, or require system reboots while driving.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a local dealership or perform a system reset.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Seat Sensor

Oversees supplemental restraint systems and occupant detection sensors for front-seat passengers.

**Passenger Airbag Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger airbag warning light may illuminate and fail to turn on when an adult occupant weighing around 100 pounds or less sits in the front passenger seat.
- **Solution:** Have the occupant weight sensor calibrated or inspected by an authorized service center.

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Uses cameras and radar sensors to power automated emergency braking, lane keep assist, and adaptive cruise control.

**EyeSight False Activation and Safety Feature Drops**
- **Symptoms:** The emergency braking system may falsely trigger in response to stopped traffic, accompanied by temporary dropouts of active safety features.
- **Solution:** Ensure camera and sensor glass are clean; visit a dealer if sensor calibration or software updates are needed.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Subaru Legacy is a capable midsize sedan with strong safety credentials, but used-car buyers should thoroughly check the infotainment electronics and advanced driver-assistance systems during a test drive. Verifying service records and confirming that any software patches or sensor calibrations have been performed can help ensure a smoother ownership experience.
