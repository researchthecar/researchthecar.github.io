**Vehicle Introduction**

The 2023 Subaru Legacy is a midsize sedan that offers a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and excellent safety features. It is available with two engine options: a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated engine and a 2.4-liter turbocharged engine. Both engines are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

People like the Subaru Legacy for its roomy interior, comfortable ride, and excellent safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with the infotainment system, the CVT transmission, and the headlights.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Naturally Aspirated Engine**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Reports indicate that some engines consume excessive amounts of oil, potentially leading to engine damage.
* Description/Symptoms: Drivers may notice a drop in oil levels, blue or white smoke from the exhaust, and a knocking or rattling sound from the engine.
* Solution: Replacing worn or damaged piston rings, valve seals, or the entire engine may resolve this issue.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**2.4-liter Turbocharged Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Shuddering or Hesitation:** Owners report experiencing shuddering or hesitation during acceleration or gear changes.
* Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may vibrate, jerk, or feel sluggish while driving.
* Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control module or replacing the CVT fluid may resolve these issues.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Lag:** Several owners have reported experiencing lag or slow response times when using the infotainment system.
* Description/Symptoms: The touchscreen may freeze, apps may take time to load, or the system may reboot unexpectedly.
* Solution: Software updates or replacing the head unit may address these issues.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Some owners have noticed condensation forming inside the headlight assemblies.
* Description/Symptoms: Water droplets may appear inside the headlights after heavy rain or washing the car.
* Solution: Inspecting and resealing the headlight assemblies may prevent further condensation.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Occasional rattling noises have been reported from the dashboard or center console.
* Description/Symptoms: Loose panels or components may cause vibrations or rattles while driving.
* Solution: Identifying and tightening the affected components can eliminate the noises.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The Subaru Legacy is generally a reliable and well-rounded vehicle. However, some owners have reported issues with the infotainment system, the CVT transmission, and the headlights. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to substantial. Overall, the Legacy remains a good choice for drivers seeking a comfortable and safe family sedan.
