**Introduction:**
The 1994 Subaru Legacy is a mid-size sedan or station wagon renowned for its all-wheel drive system, reliability, and spacious interior. It comes equipped with a 2.2-liter naturally aspirated flat-four engine producing 135 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. The Legacy is available with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine:**
* **2.2L Non-Turbo**
    * **Head Gasket Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking externally, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gaskets and associated bolts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Belt Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls without warning, no compression
        * Solution: Replace timing belt, tensioner, and water pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**
* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Synchro Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace worn synchros
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power during acceleration, erratic shifting
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**
* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**
* **Alternator Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**
* **Rust on Rear Quarter Panels:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal around rear wheels
        * Solution: Repair or replace affected panels
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**
* **Cracked Dashboard:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible cracks developing on dashboard surface
        * Solution: Replace or repair dashboard
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 1994 Subaru Legacy has a reputation for reliability, it is not immune to certain issues. The most common and costly problems include head gasket failure, timing belt failure, alternator failure, and rust on the rear quarter panels. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and others potentially leading to major engine or transmission problems.
