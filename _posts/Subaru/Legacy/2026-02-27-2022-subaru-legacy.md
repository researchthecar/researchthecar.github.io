# 2022 Subaru Legacy Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Subaru Legacy is a comfortable, all-wheel-drive midsize sedan that offers strong safety ratings and a smooth ride. However, prospective buyers should review a few recurring owner-reported complaints regarding glass durability, driver-assist electronics, and telematics systems before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine produces 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate daily power with solid fuel economy and generally dependable long-term mechanical reliability.

### 2.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 2.4-liter turbocharged horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine generates 260 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, offering much quicker acceleration and passing power. This powerplant is similarly robust, though buyers should ensure regular maintenance intervals have been followed.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped across all trims paired with standard symmetrical all-wheel drive, this continuously variable transmission prioritizes fuel efficiency and smooth power delivery. While general mechanical durability is acceptable, routine fluid inspections are recommended for higher-mileage vehicles.

## Electrical

### DCM / Telematics Unit

Electrical system reports highlight occasional glitches in cabin technology and telematics equipment.

**Starlink and Remote App Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Remote app controls stop functioning and in-car Starlink emergency or support call buttons become completely unresponsive.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership inspect the data communication module (DCM) and replace or reprogram the unit if internal failures are confirmed.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Exterior complaints center primarily around susceptibility to windshield cracking.

**Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that the windshield develops long, unexpected cracks from minor impacts or temperature shifts, sometimes spreading rapidly across the glass.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield with a certified replacement, ensuring proper calibration if equipped with camera-based safety systems.

## Safety

### EyeSight Driver Assist

Safety technology generally enhances protection but can occasionally experience sensitivity issues with automated braking features.

**Spurious Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's collision avoidance system may occasionally trigger sudden, unexpected emergency braking in heavy traffic without an actual hazard present.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera calibration, clear any obstructions around the sensors, and check for software updates at a qualified service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V755000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Park/Neutral Start Interlock Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Legacy&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative inhibitor switch may prevent the reverse lights from illuminating and the rearview camera image from displaying, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the inhibitor switch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V227000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Legacy&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A capacitor in the sensors for the Occupant Detection System (ODS) may crack and short circuit, which can prevent the front passenger air bag from deploying in a crash. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ODS sensors on the front passenger seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Subaru Legacy is a practical and safe used sedan choice, highlighted by standard all-wheel drive and solid engine options. Used car shoppers should carefully inspect the condition of the windshield for any undocumented stress cracks and verify that all infotainment, telematics, and driver-assist features operate correctly during a test drive.
