**Introduction:**

The 2009 Subaru Tribeca is a midsize SUV offered with a 3.6L H6 engine and a 5-speed automatic transmission. The Tribeca is known for its spacious interior, all-wheel drive capability, and comfortable ride. However, it has received criticism for its thirsty fuel economy and dated design.

**Engine**

* **3.6L H6**

    * **Head gasket failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leakage of coolant, oil, or both into the combustion chamber, causing overheating, misfires, and engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace head gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking noise from the engine, loss of power, and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed automatic**

    * **Valve body failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or erratic shifting, loss of gears, and check engine light illumination.
        * Solution: Replace valve body or transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front strut failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking struts, excessive vibration, and decreased ride comfort.
    * Solution: Replace front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunction, and battery discharge.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seals or weatherstripping.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard warping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard deforming or bowing due to heat or UV exposure.
    * Solution: Replace dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Subaru Tribeca faces several potential issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and interior. While some of these issues can be severe and costly to repair, others are relatively minor. However, potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
