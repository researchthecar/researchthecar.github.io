**Introduction to the 2022 Subaru Ascent**

The 2022 Subaru Ascent is a three-row SUV known for its spacious interior, standard all-wheel drive, and reliable performance. It offers multiple engine and transmission options, including a 2.4-liter turbocharged boxer engine with 260 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

2.4-liter Turbocharged Boxer Engine (260 hp, 277 lb-ft of torque)

* **Oil Leak from Valve Cover**
    Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, causing an oily mess in the engine compartment.
    Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive exhaust smoke, and strange noises from the turbocharger.
    Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

* **Transmission Hesitation or Stuttering**
    Description/Symptoms: Jerking or hesitation when shifting gears.
    Solution: Update the transmission software.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty, otherwise around $200 - $500+
    Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Noises, vibrations, or difficulty handling.
    Solution: Replace the struts.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    Recalls: None Found

* **Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noises over bumps.
    Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    Description/Symptoms: Battery dies unexpectedly, even after a short period of time.
    Solution: Investigate sources of electrical draw and resolve issues.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the problem
    Recalls: None Found

* **Radio Malfunction**
    Description/Symptoms: Radio screen freezes, glitches, or goes black.
    Solution: Replace the radio unit.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking**
    Description/Symptoms: Windshield develops cracks or chips easily.
    Solution: Replace the windshield.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Leaks**
    Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin from the sunroof.
    Solution: Inspect and reseal the sunroof.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Rear Seat Headrest Obstruction**
    Description/Symptoms: Rear seat headrests block the rearview visibility.
    Solution: Lower or remove the headrests when not in use.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
    Recalls: None Found

* **Dash Panel Rattle**
    Description/Symptoms: Noises or rattles coming from the dashboard area.
    Solution: Secure or replace loose components.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Subaru Ascent is a reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues with the engine, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of the issues vary, but they should be taken into consideration when purchasing or owning this SUV.
