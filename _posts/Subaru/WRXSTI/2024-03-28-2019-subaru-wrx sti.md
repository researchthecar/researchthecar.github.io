## 2019 Subaru WRX STI

The 2019 Subaru WRX STI is a high-performance compact sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It comes with a standard 2.5-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder boxer engine that produces 310 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission.

People who like the WRX STI praise its powerful engine, sharp handling, and sporty interior. However, they often criticize its stiff ride, limited rear seat space, and poor fuel economy.

### Engine

**2.5-liter Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Boxer Engine**

**Engine Knock**
Description/Symptoms: Knocking or tapping sounds from the engine, especially under load.
Solution: Replace engine block
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V497000

**Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
Solution: Replace piston rings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V497000

### Transmission

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Grinding Gears**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, accompanied by grinding noises.
Solution: Replace transmission fluid or repair/replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**No Issues Found**

### Electrical

**Electrical System**
Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, such as flickering lights, power window failures, or engine control issues.
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

**Headlight Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Headlights flickering or failing to illuminate properly.
Solution: Replace headlight bulbs or repair electrical wiring
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V513000

### Interior

**Infotainment System Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing, connectivity problems, or software glitches.
Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Subaru WRX STI has some significant issues that can be costly to repair, namely engine knocking and oil consumption. These issues may affect the vehicle's reliability and overall driving experience. However, it's worth noting that these issues are not universal, and many WRX STI owners have not experienced any major problems.
