**Introduction:**

The 2014 Subaru WRX STI is a high-performance sports car that delivers thrilling driving experience with its potent turbocharged engine and advanced performance features. It is powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged boxer-4 engine paired with a 6-speed manual transmission, generating 305 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque. The WRX STI is praised for its impressive acceleration, sharp handling, spacious cabin, and a host of standard features. However, like any vehicle, it has faced some specific issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **2.5-liter Turbocharged Boxer-4 Engine**

 **Excessive Oil Consumption**
* Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent oil top-ups. The issue can manifest as blue smoke from the exhaust or oil leaks.
* Solution: Replacing piston rings and valve seals has been reported as a possible solution.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed Manual Transmission**

 **Grinding Noises**
* Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported grinding noises when shifting gears, especially during cold starts. The issue can worsen over time and lead to difficulty shifting.
* Solution: Replacing the transmission synchronizers has been reported as a potential fix.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

 **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

 **Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced premature battery drain, resulting in a dead battery or difficulty starting the vehicle. The issue can be caused by various factors, including a faulty alternator or parasitic electrical draws.
* Solution: Troubleshooting and replacing the faulty components, such as the alternator, battery, or parasitic devices, is necessary.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

 **Windshield Cracking**
* Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported cracks developing on the windshield, especially on the passenger side. The cracks can appear spontaneously or after small impacts.
* Solution: Replacing the windshield is the only solution.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

 **Sunroof Rattling**
* Description/Symptoms: Some owners have encountered a rattling noise coming from the sunroof, particularly when driving on uneven roads or at high speeds.
* Solution: Adjusting or tightening the sunroof assembly and lubricating moving parts can resolve the issue.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2014 Subaru WRX STI offers a thrilling driving experience but has faced specific issues related to its engine, transmission, electrical system, and exterior components. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost of repairs can be significant in some cases. While these issues may not be widespread, they should be considered when researching and purchasing this vehicle. It is important to note that many WRX STI owners enjoy their vehicles without encountering any major problems.
