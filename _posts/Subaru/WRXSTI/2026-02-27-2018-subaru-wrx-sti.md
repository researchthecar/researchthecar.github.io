# 2018 Subaru WRX STI Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2018 Subaru WRX STI means looking at a performance-focused sport compact with a dedicated enthusiast following. While owner-reported complaints for this model year are relatively sparse, buyers should always thoroughly inspect any performance car for signs of modifications, hard driving, and proper maintenance history. NHTSA complaints represent owner experiences and should be verified during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged Boxer 4-Cylinder (EJ25)

This 2.5-liter turbocharged horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine produces approximately 305 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling performance, though buyers should watch for signs of previous modifications and ensure consistent oil maintenance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The robust 6-speed manual gearbox is paired exclusively with the STI's driver-controlled center differential system. It offers direct, engaging shifts and generally holds up well to spirited driving when properly maintained.

## Exterior

### Headlight Housing

Electrical systems on the WRX STI are generally reliable, though owners should check all interior and exterior electronics during a test drive.

**Headlight Melting**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that a section of the headlight housing appeared to be melting from the inside.
- **Solution:** Inspect the headlight assemblies for any signs of thermal distortion or bulb-related damage, and replace the affected housing if necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag Deployment

Safety equipment on the WRX STI includes a comprehensive suite of airbags and structural crumple zones designed to protect occupants in a collision.

**Collision Air Bag Response**
- **Symptoms:** An owner noted a side-impact collision where occupants sustained injuries after being struck by another vehicle, noting the specifics of the crash dynamics.
- **Solution:** Ensure that the vehicle has a clean history without undisclosed frame damage or improper structural repairs from prior accidents.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Subaru WRX STI is an exciting performance machine, but its used-car viability heavily depends on how the previous owner drove and maintained it. Prioritize models with documented service records and no modifications, and always invest in a professional pre-purchase inspection by a Subaru specialist.
