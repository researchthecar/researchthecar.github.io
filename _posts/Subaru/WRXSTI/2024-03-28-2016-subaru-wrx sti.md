**2016 Subaru WRX STI**

The 2016 Subaru WRX STI is a high-performance all-wheel-drive sedan. It is powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged boxer-4 engine that produces 305 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. The WRX STI is known for its agile handling, strong acceleration, and aggressive styling. However, it is also known to have some reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.5-liter turbocharged boxer-4 engine**
    * **Valve Spring Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Broken valve springs can cause a loss of power, misfires, and knocking sounds from the engine.
        * Solution: Replacing the valve springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Rod Knock**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rod knock is a knocking sound that occurs when the connecting rods are damaged. It can be caused by excessive wear or overheating.
        * Solution: Replacing the crankshaft and connecting rods
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed manual transmission**
    * **Transmission Noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: A grinding or whining noise may be heard when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replacing the transmission gears
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail to charge the battery, causing the vehicle to stall.
        * Solution: Replacing the alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, causing the vehicle to be difficult to start.
        * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the electrical system
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Clearcoat Peeling**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clearcoat on the paint may peel or bubble.
        * Solution: Repainting the affected areas
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not work properly, causing the vehicle to be too hot or too cold.
        * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the climate control system
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Panel Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The instrument panel may malfunction, causing the gauges to be inaccurate or inoperable.
        * Solution: Replacing the instrument cluster
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Subaru WRX STI is a high-performance sedan that is known for its agile handling, strong acceleration, and aggressive styling. However, it is also known to have some reliability issues. The most common issues are with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem.
