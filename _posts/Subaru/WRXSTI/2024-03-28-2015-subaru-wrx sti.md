## 2015 Subaru WRX STI

The 2015 Subaru WRX STI is a high-performance sports sedan known for its powerful engine, precise handling, and all-wheel drive capabilities. It is powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-four engine that produces 305 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 6-speed manual transmission. The WRX STI is also available with a 5-speed automatic transmission.

Owners praise the WRX STI for its exhilarating driving experience, ample power, and sharp steering. However, it has been subject to several reported issues, detailed below:

**Engine**

* **2.5-liter Turbocharged Flat-Four**

  * **Premature Piston Ring Wear**
    Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, reduced engine power, and potential engine damage.
    Solution: Piston ring replacement and engine rebuild.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    Recalls: #16V661000

* **Intake Manifold Air Flap Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, stalling, and check engine light illumination.
    Solution: Intake manifold replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    Recalls: #19V130000

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**

  * **Gear Grinding and Difficulty Shifting**
    Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, grinding noises during shifts, and potential transmission damage.
    Solution: Transmission repair or replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Differential Breakage**
    Description/Symptoms: Sudden loss of power, grinding noises from the rear axle, and potential damage to the differential.
    Solution: Rear differential replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    Recalls: #18V049000, #19V130000

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning Headlight Switch**
    Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete loss of headlight functionality.
    Solution: Headlight switch replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350
    Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Antenna Noise**
    Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or buzzing noises emanating from the roof antenna.
    Solution: Antenna lubrication or replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cracked Dash Panel**
    Description/Symptoms: Splitting or cracking of the dashboard panel above the infotainment screen.
    Solution: Dashboard panel replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    Recalls: None Found

**Other**

* **Faulty Fuel Pressure Sensor**
    Description/Symptoms: Erratic fuel gauge readings, reduced engine power, and check engine light illumination.
    Solution: Fuel pressure sensor replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    Recalls: #17V419000

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Subaru WRX STI is a capable and enjoyable performance car, but it has experienced several issues that can impact the driving experience and reliability. The engine and transmission issues can be particularly costly to repair, so it is important to factor these potential expenses into the decision-making process.
