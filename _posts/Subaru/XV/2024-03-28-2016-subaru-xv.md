**2016 Subaru XV**

The 2016 Subaru XV is a compact crossover SUV that offers a combination of all-wheel drive, impressive fuel economy and a well-equipped interior. It is powered by a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 150 horsepower and 144 lb-ft of torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT) or a 5-speed manual transmission. The XV received praise for its spacious interior, comfortable ride and standard all-wheel drive, but some users have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Valve Body Solenoid Failure:** The valve body solenoid can malfunction, causing harsh shifting, slipping, or a loss of power. Requires replacement of the valve body. $500 - $1,000+
* **Head Gasket Leak:** The head gasket may develop a leak, resulting in coolant consumption and potential overheating. Engine disassembly and head gasket replacement is necessary. $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Timing Belt Replacement:** The timing belt should be replaced every 105,000 miles to prevent potential engine damage. Timing belt replacement kit and installation. $1,000 - $1,500+

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Transmission Slipping:** The CVT may experience slipping or shuddering, accompanied by noise and reduced performance. May require CVT replacement or reprogramming. $2,000 - $5,000+
* **Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Solenoid Failure:** The VVT solenoid may malfunction, causing power loss or hesitation during acceleration. Solenoid replacement is required. $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:** Some users have reported excessive noise coming from the rear suspension, particularly when driving over rough roads. Replacement of suspension components may be necessary. $500 - $1,500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may experience excessive discharge, causing difficulty in starting the vehicle. Battery replacement or alternator testing may be required. $200 - $500+
* **Headlight Malfunction:** The headlights may experience intermittent operation or failure due to electrical issues. Headlight bulb or wiring repair may be required. $100 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:** The wiper motor may malfunction, causing the wipers to stop working or operate intermittently. Motor replacement is required. $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Center Console Panel Gaps:** Some users have reported excessive gaps between the center console panel and the surrounding areas. Panel adjustment or replacement may be necessary. $100 - $200+

**Summary**

The 2016 Subaru XV offers a balanced combination of features and off-road capability, but some users have encountered issues with certain components. The engine and transmission issues, if left unresolved, can lead to significant repairs. The suspension and electrical issues can impact ride quality and functionality, while the appearance-related issues may affect the overall aesthetic. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and consider the estimated repair costs.
