**Introduction to the 1991 Subaru XT**

The 1991 Subaru XT is a compact coup√© designed with a focus on performance and style. It offered a 2.5-liter SOHC EJ25 engine with 165 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. Drivers enjoyed the XT's unique looks, spacious interior, and responsive handling, while some found the engine lacking in power and fuel efficiency.

**Engine**

* **2.5L SOHC EJ25**
    * **Valve Seat Inserts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn valve seat inserts can cause rough idling, loss of power, increased oil consumption, and misfires.
        * Solution: Replace the worn valve seat inserts and re-machine the cylinder heads.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A blown head gasket can lead to coolant leaks, overheating, and engine misfiring.
        * Solution: Replace the head gasket and have the cylinder heads inspected for damage.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Internal Gearbox Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A worn gearbox can result in rough shifting, slipping gears, and loss of power.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the gearbox assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Upper Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Deteriorated bushings can lead to excessive tire wear, impaired handling, and noise.
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Sway Bar Links**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn sway bar links can cause rattling noises, reduced handling, and tire cupping.
    * Solution: Replace the worn sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical problems can lead to intermittent or failed headlights.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty headlight switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuators**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning door lock actuators can result in doors not locking or unlocking properly.
    * Solution: Replace the failed door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Fade**
    * Description/Symptoms: Prolonged exposure to sunlight can cause the dashboard to fade and crack.
    * Solution: Install a dashboard cover or have the dashboard refinished.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical problems or failed components can lead to the climate control system not functioning properly.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical or mechanical fault.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1991 Subaru XT offers a unique and stylish driving experience with its performance-oriented engine and responsive handling. However, it has several notable issues that can be expensive to repair, such as valve seat inserts, head gasket failure, and gearbox failure. While some issues may be minor and affordable to fix, the more severe issues could significantly impact the vehicle's reliability and resale value.
