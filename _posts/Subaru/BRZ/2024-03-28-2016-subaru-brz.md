**Introduction:**

The 2016 Subaru BRZ is a sports car known for its handling, affordability, and classic rear-wheel-drive layout. It features a 2.0-liter boxer engine paired with a 6-speed manual or automatic transmission. While the BRZ is praised for its engaging driving experience, it has had its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter Boxer Engine (6-speed Manual Transmission)**
    * **Valve Spring Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, misfires, check engine light
        * Solution: Replacement of affected valve springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: 16V-582
    * **Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Diagnosis and potential replacement of piston rings or engine components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **2.0-liter Boxer Engine (6-speed Automatic Transmission)**
    * **Torque Converter Vibration:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibrations during acceleration
        * Solution: Replacement of torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Grinding or Difficulty Shifting:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of damaged synchronizers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Delayed or Abrupt Shifting:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically or with hesitation
        * Solution: Transmission software update or replacement of components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Struts:**
    * **Lowering Spring Compatibility Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Improper fitment or premature failure of lowering springs
        * Solution: Use of compatible springs or custom adjustments
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Subframe:**
    * **Cracking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noise or vibration from the rear of the vehicle
        * Solution: Reinforcement or replacement of subframe
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **HID Headlights:**
    * **Moisture Buildup:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Condensation inside headlight assemblies
        * Solution: Replacement or resealing of affected lights
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Spoiler:**
    * **Bubbling or Peeling Paint:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Paint defects on the rear spoiler
        * Solution: Refinishing or replacement of spoiler
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard:**
    * **Warped or Distorted:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Irregularities or bending of dashboard material
        * Solution: Replacement of affected dashboard component
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Subaru BRZ's reported issues include engine problems, transmission issues, and suspension concerns. While some issues may be more isolated or less frequent, others can be more serious and affect overall performance and reliability. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue and necessary parts replacement.
