# 2020 Subaru BRZ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Subaru BRZ offers engaging rear-wheel-drive handling and a lightweight sports car setup for driving enthusiasts. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should review standard inspection points, particularly concerning front suspension noises and glass durability, while enjoying the vehicle's dynamic character.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Naturally Aspirated Boxer 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine produces approximately 205 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque when paired with the manual transmission. It provides a low center of gravity and generally reliable operation for daily driving and spirited runs.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers direct shift feedback and appeals directly to enthusiast drivers. It is generally robust when maintained properly.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The 6-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear changes and convenient commuting capability. It maintains dependable performance under normal driving conditions.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension Struts

Owner reports indicate potential front suspension noise issues at low speeds that may require dealership inspection.

**Front Wheel Area Popping Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported a popping sound coming from the front wheel areas during low-speed maneuvers or when turning to park.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension components, strut mounts, and control arms thoroughly to isolate the source of the noise.

## Exterior

### Windshield

Glass durability complaints suggest that small chips can quickly turn into major cracks.

**Windshield Stress Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Minor initial impacts from small stones can rapidly develop into large spiderweb cracks across the windshield glass.
- **Solution:** Repair small chips immediately or replace the windshield once extensive cracking occurs.

The 2020 Subaru BRZ remains an exciting pre-owned choice for sports car enthusiasts, featuring balanced handling and reliable powertrain options. Buyers should perform a careful inspection of the front suspension and check for any existing windshield damage before finalizing a purchase.
