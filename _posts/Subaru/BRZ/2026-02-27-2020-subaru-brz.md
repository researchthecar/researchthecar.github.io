# 2020 Subaru BRZ

### Overview
The 2020 Subaru BRZ, a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe, is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly praised for its engaging driving dynamics and balanced chassis. It offers a focused driving experience for enthusiasts. While it doesn't boast the highest horsepower figures in its class, its performance is well-suited to its lightweight and agile platform. Reliability data suggests that most owners experience a smooth ownership period with minimal unexpected mechanical issues. Common areas of attention, as with many performance-oriented vehicles, tend to be related to the drivetrain and suspension components under spirited driving conditions. However, widespread critical failures are not a common theme for this model year.
