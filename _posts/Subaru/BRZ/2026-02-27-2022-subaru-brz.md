# 2022 Subaru BRZ

### Overview
The 2022 Subaru BRZ, a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe co-developed with Toyota, aims to deliver an engaging driving experience with its naturally aspirated boxer engine and agile handling. This generation marks a significant update, focusing on improved performance and refinement. Reliability data for the initial production year suggests a generally robust platform, with most reported issues being minor or related to early production quirks rather than systemic failures. Owners generally praise its driving dynamics and value, with reliability being a strong point for many.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Subaru BRZ demonstrates strong initial reliability, with no significant systemic issues reported across its powertrain or major mechanical components. The 2.4L boxer engine and both transmission options (manual and automatic) are performing well. Minor electrical quirks, primarily related to the infotainment system, have been noted but are generally resolved with software updates. The chassis, suspension, exterior, and interior components are holding up as expected for a new model. Overall, the BRZ is a reliable choice for enthusiasts seeking a fun-to-drive sports car with a solid mechanical foundation.
