**Introduction**

The 2022 Subaru BRZ is a sports car that boasts a 2.4L naturally aspirated boxer engine producing 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a standard 6-speed manual transmission or an optional 6-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters. The BRZ is praised for its handling, agility, and affordability, but some owners have encountered various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.4L Boxer Engine (Manual Transmission)**
    * **Premature Valve Spring Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rough idling, engine knocking, and illuminated Check Engine Light.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty valve springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.4L Boxer Engine (Automatic Transmission)**
    * **CVT Transmission Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Update the transmission control module software or replace the CVT transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Difficult Gear Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or resistance when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Lubricate or replace the shifter linkage.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leak from the transmission pan or other seals.
        * Solution: Replace the leaky seals or pan.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or diagnose and fix any electrical draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Wind Noise from Side Mirrors**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise at higher speeds.
    * Solution: Replace the side mirrors with updated versions or install wind deflectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cup Holder Design Flaw**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cup holders are too small or not deep enough to securely hold drinks.
    * Solution: Purchase aftermarket cup holders or find creative ways to secure drinks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Subaru BRZ has faced several issues, primarily related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of the problems varies, with some requiring costly repairs. However, the BRZ remains a popular choice among enthusiasts due to its overall performance and value for money.
