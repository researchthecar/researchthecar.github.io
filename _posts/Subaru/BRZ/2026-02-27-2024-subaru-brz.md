# 2024 Subaru BRZ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Subaru BRZ continues to deliver lightweight, rear-wheel-drive sports car performance with its responsive flat-four engine. While overall owner feedback is limited, buyers should remain aware of early reported concerns regarding engine bay clearance and equipment variations.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Naturally Aspirated Boxer 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter naturally aspirated boxer four-cylinder engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and a low center of gravity, though buyers should verify engine bay routing during inspections.

**Fuel Line Clearance and Rubbing**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that factory fuel lines on the driver's side can make contact with the underside of the hood, potentially leading to wear or severe fire hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect the engine bay for proper fuel line clearance and routing. Dealerships or owners may need to secure or adjust lines to prevent rubbing.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission offers short, direct throws favored by driving enthusiasts. It pairs well with the flat-four engine for engaged rear-wheel-drive performance.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The 6-speed automatic transmission provides smooth daily driving capability while retaining manual shift controls via steering wheel paddles.
