**Introduction**

The 2019 Subaru BRZ is a sporty coupe powered by a naturally aspirated 2.0L 4-cylinder boxer engine. It offers a 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission. The BRZ boasts impressive handling and performance, making it a favorite among driving enthusiasts. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

- **2.0L Boxer Engine**
    - **Piston Ring Failure (Manual Transmission Only):** Premature wear of the piston rings can lead to excessive oil consumption, smoking from the exhaust, and reduced engine performance.
        - Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

- **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    - **Grinding Gears (Reverse Gear):** Difficulty engaging the reverse gear, accompanied by a grinding noise.
        - Solution: Transmission replacement or repair
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        - Recalls: None Found
- **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    - **Shift Delay or Hesitation:** Delayed gear shifts or hesitation during acceleration.
        - Solution: Transmission software update
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (under warranty)
        - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Suspension**
    - **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** Failure of the strut mount bearing can cause rattling noises, vibration, and reduced handling.
        - Solution: Replacement of strut mounts
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Infotainment System**
    - **Glitches or Malfunctions:** Occasional software glitches or malfunctions can affect the infotainment system's functionality.
        - Solution: Software updates or system reset
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (under warranty)
        - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Headlights**
    - **Condensation or Fogging:** Moisture accumulation inside the headlights, causing reduced visibility.
        - Solution: Headlight replacement or moisture prevention measures
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Trim**
    - **Rattling or Squeaking:** Loose or poorly fitted dashboard trim pieces can produce annoying rattling or squeaking noises.
        - Solution: Inspection and tightening of loose trim components
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (under warranty)
        - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Subaru BRZ has a mixed track record regarding reliability. While it offers impressive driving dynamics, it has a history of some serious mechanical issues, particularly with the manual transmission and piston ring failure. The cost of repairs can be significant, making these issues a potential concern for potential buyers. The electrical, exterior, and interior issues are mostly minor and can be resolved through software updates or minor repairs.
