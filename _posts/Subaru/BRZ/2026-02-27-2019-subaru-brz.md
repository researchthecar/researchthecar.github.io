# 2019 Subaru BRZ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Subaru BRZ means looking at a lightweight, rear-wheel-drive sports car built for handling precision. While owner feedback is generally light, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and keep an eye out for minor random drivability quirks reported by some drivers.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Boxer 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-four boxer engine produces approximately 205 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque when paired with the manual transmission, or 200 horsepower with the automatic. It provides sharp throttle response and a low center of gravity, though buyers should verify smooth operation during test drives.

**Random Engine Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience random engine hiccups or hesitation once the engine reaches normal operating temperatures, without any warning lights illuminating on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the ignition system, fuel delivery, and engine sensors to diagnose the root cause of the random stutter.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The standard 6-speed manual transmission features short, mechanical throws designed for enthusiast driving and pairs directly with the boxer engine.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The optional 6-speed automatic transmission offers steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters for drivers seeking easier daily commuting without sacrificing manual control.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V587000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=BRZ&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the low-pressure fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Subaru BRZ is an engaging and generally reliable sports car option for used buyers, provided the vehicle has been well-maintained and inspected thoroughly. Prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and a detailed test drive to check for smooth engine performance.
