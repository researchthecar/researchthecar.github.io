# 2017 Subaru BRZ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2017 Subaru BRZ means looking at a lightweight, rear-wheel-drive sports car built for handling and driver engagement. While owner-reported complaints are sparse, prospective buyers should review available mechanical options and monitor for sporadic electrical or powertrain feedback.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Horizontally-Opposed 4-Cylinder (FA20)

The 2017 Subaru BRZ features a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated boxer four-cylinder engine generating approximately 205 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque when paired with the manual transmission. It provides sharp throttle response and a low center of gravity, though owners should remain mindful of early-production valve spring recalls and isolated mechanical reports.

**Valve Spring and Stalling Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalling accompanied by a check engine light and sweet-smelling white smoke.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the valve springs and fueling components; faulty components may require professional replacement.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The standard 6-speed manual transmission offers short, direct throws tailored for enthusiast driving. While generally robust, individual units may exhibit synchronizer wear or shift resistance over time.

**Gear Shifting Grinding and Resistance**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience loud grinding noises or difficulty shifting into specific gears while operating the manual shifter.
- **Solution:** Inspect the clutch assembly, transmission fluid condition, and synchronizers for excessive wear, replacing damaged internal components as necessary.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The optional 6-speed automatic transmission includes steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters for manual gear selection. It delivers smooth daily commuting while maintaining decent fuel economy.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Bluetooth System

The factory infotainment system handles standard media and smartphone connectivity, though early software versions are occasionally prone to minor pairing glitches.

**Intermittent Bluetooth Audio Dropouts**
- **Symptoms:** The stock head unit audio may occasionally cut out during Bluetooth playback before reconnecting on its own.
- **Solution:** Check for available software updates for the head unit or consider aftermarket receiver options if connectivity instability persists.

## Safety

### Air Bag and SRS Warning System

The occupant detection and airbag safety systems are critical for protection, but sporadic sensor warnings require prompt professional diagnosis to ensure proper deployment readiness.

**Intermittent SRS Warning Light and Occupant Sensor Faults**
- **Symptoms:** A yellow caution light and check SRS warning message may illuminate intermittently on the instrument cluster, and passenger airbag status lights may fluctuate.
- **Solution:** Have the Supplemental Restraint System scanned for diagnostic trouble codes to identify faulty seat sensors, wiring harnesses, or module issues.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Subaru BRZ is a rewarding driver's car with a relatively clean initial owner complaint history. Used car shoppers should carefully inspect the manual transmission shifting feel, verify proper operation of the SRS safety systems, and ensure all relevant recall service campaigns have been completed.
