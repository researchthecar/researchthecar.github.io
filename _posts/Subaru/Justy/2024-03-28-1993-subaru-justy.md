**Introduction:**
The 1993 Subaru Justy is a subcompact car available with either a 1.2-liter or 1.3-liter engine and either a 4-speed automatic or 5-speed manual transmission. It is praised for its fuel efficiency, maneuverability, and affordability. However, it is also known for its limited power and interior space.

**Engine**

**1.2-liter Engine**

* **Leaking valve cover gasket:** Oil may seep from the gasket, causing a burning oil smell or low oil levels. Replace the gasket. $100 - $200+

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Coolant or oil may leak from the gasket, leading to engine overheating or reduced power. Replace the gasket. $200 - $300+

**1.3-liter Engine**

* **Head gasket failure:** Coolant and oil may mix, causing white smoke from the exhaust or overheating. Replace the head gasket. $800 - $1200+

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping:** Transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes. Rebuild or replace the transmission. $1200 - $2500+

* **Leaking transmission fluid:** Fluid may leak from the transmission, causing low fluid levels and transmission damage. Replace the seals or gaskets. $200 - $500+

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty shifting:** May experience difficulty shifting gears or grinding noises. Replace worn or damaged components. $200 - $600+

**Suspension**

* **Worn struts:** May cause a rough ride, reduced handling, and uneven tire wear. Replace the struts. $300 - $600+

* **Broken springs:** May cause the car to sag or ride unevenly. Replace the springs. $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Failed starter motor:** May experience difficulty starting the car. Replace the starter motor. $300 - $500+

* **Alternator problems:** May cause dim lights, battery drain, or charging issues. Replace the alternator. $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Rusty wheel wells:** May develop rust due to exposure to moisture and road salt. Repair or replace the affected areas. $500 - $1500+

**Interior**

* **Cracked dashboard:** May develop cracks due to sun exposure or temperature fluctuations. Replace the dashboard. $200 - $500+

* **Worn seat upholstery:** May become faded, torn, or worn over time. Replace the seat covers or upholstery. $300 - $800+

**Summary:**

The 1993 Subaru Justy can be a reliable and affordable car, but it is known to have some common issues. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing a Justy and factor in the potential costs of repairs.
