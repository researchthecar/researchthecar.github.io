**Introduction**
The 1990 Subaru Justy is a subcompact hatchback that was only available in a front-wheel-drive layout. It came with a single naturally aspirated engine option paired with a four-speed automatic or five-speed manual transmission. The Justy was praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability, but it was also criticized for its lack of power and cramped interior.

**Engine**
* **1.2L Flat-Four**
    * **Blown Head Gasket**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking externally or internally, overheating engine, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idle, vacuum leak noises
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **Four-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slips or Hesitates**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission gears slipping or hesitating to engage, delayed acceleration
        * Solution: Overhaul transmission with new clutch packs and seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Five-Speed Manual**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**
* **Front Suspension**
    * **Strut Mounts Worn**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noise and vibration over bumps, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace strut mounts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per side
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Leaf Springs Sagging**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride height, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace leaf springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800 per side
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, warning light on dashboard
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Motor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks but doesn't start
        * Solution: Replace starter motor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Rust Prone**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on body panels, especially around wheel arches and rear hatch
        * Solution: Rust repair or prevention measures
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Dashboard Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracks forming on dashboard surface
        * Solution: Dashboard cover or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Creaking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling noises from center console
        * Solution: Tighten or lubricate mounting points
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Minimal
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 1990 Subaru Justy has several common issues, including engine head gasket failure and electrical problems. The suspension is prone to wear and tear, and the exterior is susceptible to rust. The interior has some issues, but they are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the severity and cost of the Justy's issues are moderate, but potential buyers should be aware of them before purchasing.
