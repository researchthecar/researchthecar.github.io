# 2022 Subaru Forester Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Subaru Forester continues to be a popular choice for compact SUV shoppers seeking standard all-wheel drive, practical cargo room, and strong safety ratings. However, owner complaints filed with federal regulators highlight several recurring areas of concern worth noting during a pre-purchase inspection. Prospective buyers should pay close attention to advanced driver-assistance system behavior, electrical battery performance, and exterior glass durability.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

Equipped with a 2.5-liter horizontally opposed (boxer) four-cylinder engine producing 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting and balanced efficiency. The engine itself is generally robust, though peripheral electronic components can occasionally trigger warning lights.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Features a Lineartronic CVT paired with standard symmetrical all-wheel drive, delivering smooth power delivery and competitive fuel economy for the compact SUV segment. Routine fluid maintenance is critical to ensuring long-term durability.

## Suspension

### Suspension Struts

Suspension components generally handle rough roads well, though suspension noise and wear have been flagged in isolated cases.

**Premature Strut Wear and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Front suspension produces clunking noises over bumps or experiences premature wear on suspension struts.
- **Solution:** Inspect struts, bushings, and mounts during a test drive; replace worn suspension components as needed.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical complaints primarily center around battery longevity and occasional infotainment glitches.

**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start unexpectedly or completely loses electrical power due to a deeply discharged battery.
- **Solution:** Inspect battery health and charging system draw; replace with a robust aftermarket or upgraded battery if necessary.

## Exterior

### Visibility and Glass

Body visibility and glass components can be prone to unexpected stress fractures reported by some owners.

**Spontaneous Windshield or Rear Glass Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields develop sudden stress cracks or rear glass shatters unexpectedly without direct impact from road debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect glass thoroughly prior to purchase and ensure any replacements utilize OEM glass and proper installation techniques.

### Exterior Lighting

Lighting systems provide strong illumination but specific headlight optic designs have generated visibility complaints.

**Headlight Projection Dark Spots**
- **Symptoms:** LED headlight beams project noticeable dark spots or shadows that move across the road surface during steering.
- **Solution:** Consult dealership technical service guidance or headlight adjustment procedures if available.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety systems enhance driver protection but can occasionally exhibit calibration or sensor sensitivity quirks.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The EyeSight system or automatic emergency braking may suddenly engage without an apparent obstacle or vehicle present in front of the car.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer check for EyeSight software updates, camera calibration issues, or sensor obstructions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V755000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Park/Neutral Start Interlock Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Forester&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative inhibitor switch may prevent the reverse lights from illuminating and the rearview camera image from displaying, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the inhibitor switch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Subaru Forester remains a sensible choice for used car buyers seeking versatility, comfort, and standard all-wheel drive. While federal owner complaints point to occasional issues with battery draw, unexpected driver-assist braking, and sensitive glass, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help verify that these systems are functioning properly. Buyers should prioritize models with complete service histories and thoroughly test all electronic features before finalizing their purchase.
