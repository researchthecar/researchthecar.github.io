**Introduction**

The 2016 Subaru Forester is a compact SUV that offers a spacious interior, standard all-wheel drive, and a variety of engine and transmission options. It is available with a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated engine with 170 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque, or a turbocharged 2.0-liter engine with 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed manual, a CVT (continuously variable transmission), and a five-speed automatic.

Owners of the 2016 Subaru Forester praise its reliability, safety, and fuel efficiency. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and exterior.

**Engine**

* **2.5-liter naturally aspirated engine**
    * **Oil consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and/or engine block.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0-liter turbocharged engine**
    * **Turbocharger failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, and/or unusual noises from the turbocharger.
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT (continuously variable transmission)**
    * **Hesitation or shuddering**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or shudders during acceleration or shifting.
        * Solution: Update transmission software or replace valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front struts**
    * **Leaking fluid**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of steering response, uneven tire wear, and/or fluid leaking from the front struts.
        * Solution: Replace front struts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Premature failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, requiring frequent replacement.
        * Solution: Replace battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof**
    * **Leaking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof.
        * Solution: Replace sunroof seal or drain tubes.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center console**
    * **Rattling**
        * Description/Symptoms: Center console rattles at certain speeds or over bumps.
        * Solution: Tighten or replace loose screws or components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Subaru Forester is a reliable and well-received SUV. However, it has been reported to have some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and exterior. While some of these issues can be minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
