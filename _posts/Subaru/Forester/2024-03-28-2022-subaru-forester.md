**Introduction:**

The 2022 Subaru Forester is a compact SUV known for its all-wheel drive, spacious interior, and safety features. It offers two engine options: a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated boxer engine and a 2.5-liter turbocharged boxer engine. Both engines are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

People appreciate the Forester's ruggedness, practicality, and fuel efficiency. However, some common complaints include a lack of power from the base engine, a noisy cabin, and limited rear legroom.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Naturally Aspirated Boxer Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings or engine rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-liter Turbocharged Boxer Engine**

* **No Major Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:** Some owners have experienced hesitation or jerking during acceleration.
    * Solution: Software update or CVT replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Noise:** Some owners have reported a clunking or rattling noise from the strut mounts.
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have experienced excessive battery drain, leading to the vehicle not starting.
    * Solution: Replace battery or identify and fix the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Keyless Entry Issues:** Some owners have faced difficulties with the keyless entry system, such as the remote not unlocking the vehicle or the vehicle not recognizing the key.
    * Solution: Replace or reprogram key fob or receiver module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking:** Some owners have experienced spontaneous windshield cracking, particularly in hot climates.
    * Solution: Replace windshield.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:** Some owners have reported issues with the climate control system, such as inconsistent temperature or malfunctioning controls.
    * Solution: Replace faulty components or reprogram the system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Lag:** Some owners have experienced lag and slow response from the infotainment system.
    * Solution: Software update or system reset.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2022 Subaru Forester is generally a reliable vehicle, there have been some reported issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components. The severity of these issues can vary, with some being minor annoyances and others more significant. The cost of repairs can also vary widely depending on the issue and the specific parts that need to be replaced.

Overall, the 2022 Subaru Forester remains a popular choice for consumers who value practicality, safety, and all-wheel drive capabilities. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that could arise with this vehicle.
