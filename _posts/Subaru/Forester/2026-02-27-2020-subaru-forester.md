# 2020 Subaru Forester Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Subaru Forester is a popular compact SUV known for its spacious cabin, standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive, and strong safety scores. However, used car buyers should carefully review recurring owner complaints regarding spontaneous windshield cracking, electrical battery drains, and thermo control valve failures that can disable key safety systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated boxer 4-cylinder engine producing 182 horsepower, the Forester offers steady acceleration and practical efficiency. While generally robust, buyers should watch for cooling system sensor and valve concerns.

**Thermo Control Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of warning lights such as Check Engine and EyeSight Off, potentially leading to overheating or disabled driver-assist features.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty thermo control valve assembly at an authorized service center.
- **Cost:** $1,500

**Engine Stalling at Auto-Stop**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle violently shakes and stalls out when attempting to accelerate from an Auto-Stop event at intersections or traffic lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect electrical connections, battery health, and powertrain control modules for proper idle management.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped with a Lineartronic CVT paired with standard all-wheel drive, this transmission delivers smooth power delivery and competitive fuel economy. No major recurring mechanical failure clusters were highlighted in owner complaint records.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Electrical complaints center primarily around parasitic battery drains and infotainment glitches, which can leave the vehicle unable to start if left parked for extended periods.

**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start unexpectedly due to a dead battery, requiring frequent battery replacements or jumps.
- **Solution:** Check for parasitic electrical draws and test alternator and battery health.

## Exterior

### Visibility and Windshield

Exterior complaints show a notable pattern of stress-related windshield cracks that can impair driver visibility and require expensive replacements.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Cracks suddenly appear across the windshield, often originating near the edges or pillars without clear evidence of impact from stones.
- **Solution:** Replace the windshield with a certified replacement glass compatible with EyeSight camera calibration.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Subaru Forester remains an appealing choice for daily commuting and outdoor adventures thanks to its excellent utility and standard all-wheel drive. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect service records for prior thermo control valve replacements, check battery health, and examine the windshield for any stress fractures.
