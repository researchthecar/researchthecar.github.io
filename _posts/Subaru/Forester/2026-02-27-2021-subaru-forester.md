# 2021 Subaru Forester Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Subaru Forester offers practical utility, standard all-wheel drive, and strong safety scores, making it a popular choice in the compact SUV segment. However, used car buyers should carefully review potential trouble areas reported by owners. Recurring owner complaints highlight issues with automatic stop-start system failures, electrical battery drains, and front windshield vulnerability.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated boxer four-cylinder engine producing 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting with standard symmetrical all-wheel drive. While generally robust mechanically, the engine's integration with the automatic start-stop system has drawn owner complaints regarding hesitation and stalling.

**Idle Stop/Start Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** When the automatic stop-start system shuts the engine off at a traffic stop, the vehicle intermittently fails to restart when the driver presses the accelerator.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnosis of the starting system or transmission control module, and may involve a software update or battery replacement.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic CVT Automatic

Pairs with the 2.5L engine using a Lineartronic continuously variable transmission (CVT) featuring active torque split all-wheel drive. Offers smooth power delivery and competitive fuel economy, though buyers should verify proper fluid maintenance history.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Battery

Electrical complaints primarily center around unexpected battery drain issues and telematics system warnings, which can leave drivers stranded if left unaddressed.

**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's battery repeatedly loses charge, resulting in a dead vehicle requiring frequent jump starts or towing.
- **Solution:** Check for parasitic draws, particularly from telematics systems like Subaru Starlink, and replace with a battery meeting proper reserve capacity specifications.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Body panels and exterior trim hold up well, but the factory windshield glass has been a frequent point of frustration for owners reporting low crack resistance.

**Frequent or Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields develop long cracks from minor impacts or spontaneously across the lower passenger side without an apparent preceding event.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield through a professional glass shop, ensuring proper recalibration for vehicles equipped with EyeSight driver assist cameras.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Subaru Forester remains a functional and comfortable used compact SUV pick, thanks to its spacious cabin and standard all-wheel drive. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the service history for any past electrical drains, ensure the stop-start system operates smoothly without stalling, and carefully inspect the windshield for existing chips or stress cracks.
