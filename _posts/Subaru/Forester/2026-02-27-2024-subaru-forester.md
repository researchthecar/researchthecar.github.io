# 2024 Subaru Forester Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Subaru Forester continues the brand's tradition of offering standard all-wheel drive, generous visibility, and practical utility. However, owner reports highlight several recurring areas of concern for used car buyers to evaluate carefully, particularly involving advanced driver assistance systems, exterior glass integrity, and occasional powertrain or electrical anomalies.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.5-liter horizontally opposed (boxer) 4-cylinder engine produces 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily commuting and good fuel efficiency, though owners should routinely inspect for minor fluid leaks.

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained oil spots or visible oil residue discovered on the side of the engine block during routine maintenance.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the leak source and repair or replace the affected gasket under factory warranty if applicable.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) is paired standard with all-wheel drive across all trims, delivering smooth power delivery and maximizing fuel economy.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior panels and mirrors generally hold up well, though windshield durability remains a common discussion point.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Cracks developing across the windshield unexpectedly without any discernible impact from road debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield with OEM glass calibrated properly for the EyeSight camera system.

## Safety

### EyeSight Driver Assistance

Advanced safety features offer great peace of mind, but some owners report unexpected deactivations and warning lights.

**EyeSight System Deactivation and False Alerts**
- **Symptoms:** The EyeSight system abruptly shuts down, disabling lane assist, collision avoidance, and adaptive cruise control, or issues unexpected warnings.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians check sensor alignment, clear diagnostic trouble codes, and update camera software if necessary.

**Air Bag Warning Indicator**
- **Symptoms:** Air bag warning lights illuminate on the dashboard or passenger seat occupancy sensors trigger unexpected alerts.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat weight sensors and wiring harnesses for loose connections or faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V723000: Engine And Engine Cooling](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Forester&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Leaking coolant can contact a hot exhaust pipe, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V754000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Forester&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the left and right front driveshafts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2024 Subaru Forester, prospective buyers should verify that all factory warranty coverages are transferable and carefully inspect the windshield and advanced driver assistance systems during a test drive. While most owners enjoy a capable and safe crossover, keeping an eye out for reported sensor quirks and glass durability will help ensure a satisfying purchase.
