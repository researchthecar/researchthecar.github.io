**Introduction:**

The 1994 Subaru Loyale is a compact all-wheel-drive wagon. It is powered by a 2.2-liter flat-four engine that produces 130 horsepower and 161 lb-ft of torque. The Loyale is available with a five-speed manual transmission or a four-speed automatic transmission. It is praised for its all-wheel-drive capability and ruggedness, but some owners have reported issues with its engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

**2.2-liter flat-four engine**

* **Head gasket failure:** The head gasket can fail, causing coolant and oil to leak. Symptoms include overheating, white smoke from the exhaust, and a loss of coolant. The solution is to replace the head gasket. Estimated cost: $1,000-$2,000+. Recalls: None Found
* **Valve cover gasket leak:** The valve cover gasket can leak, causing oil to leak onto the engine. Symptoms include a burning oil smell and oil stains on the engine. The solution is to replace the valve cover gasket. Estimated cost: $100-$200+. Recalls: None Found
* **Timing belt failure:** The timing belt can fail, causing the engine to lose power or stall. If the timing belt fails while the engine is running, it can cause catastrophic engine damage. The solution is to replace the timing belt and possibly other components, such as the water pump and belts. Estimated cost: $500-$1,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-speed manual transmission**

* **Grinding gears:** The gears can grind when shifting, especially when the transmission is cold. The solution is to adjust the linkage or replace the transmission. Estimated cost: $200-$1,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Four-speed automatic transmission**

* **Slipping gears:** The transmission can slip gears, causing a loss of power or the vehicle to jerk. The solution is to replace the transmission. Estimated cost: $1,500-$2,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure:** The front struts can fail, causing the car to lean to one side and the ride to become rough. The solution is to replace the struts. Estimated cost: $500-$1,000+. Recalls: None Found
* **Rear springs failure:** The rear springs can fail, causing the rear of the car to sag and the ride to become rough. The solution is to replace the springs. Estimated cost: $200-$500+. Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, causing the battery to die and the car to lose electrical power. The solution is to replace the alternator. Estimated cost: $500-$1,000+. Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure:** The starter can fail, causing the car to not start. The solution is to replace the starter. Estimated cost: $200-$500+. Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door handles:** The door handles can break, making it difficult to open the doors. The solution is to replace the door handles. Estimated cost: $100-$200+. Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate rust:** The tailgate can rust, causing it to become difficult to open or close. The solution is to replace the tailgate. Estimated cost: $500-$1,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat belt retractor failure:** The seat belt retractor can fail, causing the seat belt to not extend or lock properly. The solution is to replace the seat belt retractor. Estimated cost: $200-$400+. Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard cracking:** The dashboard can crack, especially in hot climates. The solution is to replace the dashboard. Estimated cost: $500-$1,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1994 Subaru Loyale is a reliable vehicle overall, but it can have some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components. The severity and cost of the issues can vary depending on the component failure and the specific part affected.
