**Introduction:**

The 1993 Subaru Loyale was a compact SUV offered with two engine and three transmission options. The base engine was a 1.8L naturally aspirated flat-four that produced 90 horsepower and 107 lb-ft of torque. The optional engine was a 2.2L turbocharged flat-four that produced 118 horsepower and 143 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual, 4-speed automatic, and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Loyale was known for its ruggedness, reliability, and off-road capability, but it also had some common issues.

**Engine**

**1.8L Naturally Aspirated Flat-Four**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blowing white smoke from the exhaust, overheating, loss of coolant
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power, vacuum leaks
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.2L Turbocharged Flat-Four**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blue or black smoke from the exhaust, loss of power, whining noise
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Synchros Wearing Out:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into 2nd or 3rd
    * Solution: Replace worn synchros
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips gears, hesitation when accelerating
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **Belt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission jerking or slipping, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace CVT belt and pulleys
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts Wearing Out:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise over bumps, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, dead battery, warning light on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible rust spots on fenders, hood, or roof
    * Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard, especially around the vents
    * Solution: Repair or replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary:**

The 1993 Subaru Loyale is a reliable and rugged SUV, but it has some common issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and body. The severity of the issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and expensive. Overall, the 1993 Subaru Loyale is a good choice for those looking for a reliable and capable off-road vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues and to budget for repairs.
