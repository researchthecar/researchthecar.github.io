## 2000 Subaru Impreza

The 2000 Subaru Impreza is a compact car that was available in sedan and wagon body styles. It came with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

- 2.2-liter naturally aspirated flat-four engine with 142 horsepower and 157 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission
- 2.5-liter naturally aspirated flat-four engine with 165 horsepower and 166 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission
- 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-four engine with 227 horsepower and 226 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission

The Impreza was praised for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and affordable price. However, it was also criticized for its lack of power, especially in the base 2.2-liter engine.

### Engine
**2.2-liter naturally aspirated flat-four**

**Head gasket failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the engine, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
Solution: Replace head gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Timing belt failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, no power, rattling noise from the engine
Solution: Replace timing belt
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**2.5-liter naturally aspirated flat-four**

**No Issues Found**

**2.5-liter turbocharged flat-four**

**Turbocharger failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noise from the engine, blue smoke from the exhaust
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission
**5-speed manual**

**No Issues Found**

**4-speed automatic**

**Transmission slippage**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, overheating transmission fluid
Solution: Replace transmission or rebuild
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Interior
**Dashboard rattles**
Description/Symptoms: Rattle from the dashboard at certain speeds or road conditions
Solution: Locate and fix source of rattle (tighten screws, replace clips, etc.)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2000 Subaru Impreza is a reliable and affordable compact car. However, it is not without its issues. The head gasket failure and timing belt failure are the most common engine issues, while the transmission slippage is the most common transmission issue. The interior is also prone to dashboard rattles. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues are moderate, but they should be considered before purchasing a used Impreza.
