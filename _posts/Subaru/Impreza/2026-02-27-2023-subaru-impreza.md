# 2023 Subaru Impreza Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Subaru Impreza offers dependable everyday transportation backed by standard symmetrical all-wheel drive, though buyers should review early owner-reported feedback regarding electronics and acceleration behavior.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L H4 Direct-Injected Boxer Engine

This 2.0-liter naturally aspirated horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine produces approximately 152 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. It provides efficient daily performance and balanced reliability for standard driving conditions.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The Lineartronic CVT features smooth power delivery and pairs across trims with standard all-wheel drive. Regular fluid maintenance helps preserve long-term transmission longevity.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

Owner complaints highlight sporadic connectivity quirks within the central infotainment interface.

**Infotainment Connection Instability**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report frequent difficulty establishing or maintaining reliable connections with smartphone integration platforms like Android Auto and Apple CarPlay.
- **Solution:** Software updates performed by a dealership or restarting mobile device connections can occasionally restore stable pairing.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V292000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights:Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Impreza&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle that shifts out of the park position unexpectedly can allow the vehicle to rollaway. Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the gap between the brake light switch and stopper, adjusting the switch as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V647000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Impreza&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Separation of the front end of the driveshaft increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will clean the bolt mounting surfaces of the center support brace and install new bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2023 Subaru Impreza, test-drive the vehicle to verify smooth acceleration response and inspect the infotainment system's smartphone connectivity.
