**Introduction:**

The 2016 Subaru WRX is a performance-oriented sedan available with a 2.0L turbocharged boxer engine and a 6-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). With 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, it offers a thrilling driving experience. Praised for its handling, acceleration, and all-wheel-drive capability, the WRX is also criticized for its firm ride, cramped interior, and lack of certain features.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Boxer Engine**

* **Cylinder Head Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the cylinder heads can cause coolant and oil leaks, leading to performance issues and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Cylinder head replacement or repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500-$5,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V601000

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shift Linkage Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or worn shift linkages can make it difficult to shift gears or lead to gear pops.
    * Solution: Shift linkage adjustment or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500+

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Noise and Vibration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Unusual whining or buzzing noises, as well as vibrations, may occur during acceleration or cruising.
    * Solution: CVT fluid change or replacement, potential solenoid or torque converter issues
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$2,000+

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Fuel Pump:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A defective fuel pump can lead to engine sputtering, power loss, or stalling.
    * Solution: Fuel pump replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 18V800000

* **Headlight Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both headlights may become inoperative due to a faulty relay or wiring issue.
    * Solution: Relay or wiring harness replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500+

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent freezing, slow response, or other software issues may occur.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$600+
* **Rattling Interior Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose interior panels or trim pieces can produce annoying rattles.
    * Solution: Panel tightening or insulation
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300+

**Summary:**

The 2016 Subaru WRX is a performance-oriented sedan with known issues primarily in engine, transmission, and electrical components. While some issues, such as headlight malfunctions and rattling interior panels, are relatively minor, others like cylinder head cracking and CVT problems can be more severe and expensive to fix.
