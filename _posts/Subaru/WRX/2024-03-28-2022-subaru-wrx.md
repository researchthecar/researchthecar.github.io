**Introduction to the 2022 Subaru WRX**

The 2022 Subaru WRX is a performance-oriented compact sedan that combines sporty handling with all-wheel drive capability. It is available with one engine and two transmission options:

* 2.4L Boxer Engine: 271 hp, 258 lb-ft of torque
    * 6-speed manual transmission
    * CVT automatic transmission

**Engine**

**2.4L Boxer Engine with 6-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Valve Body Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to shift gears smoothly; grinding or slipping may occur.
    * Solution: Replacement of valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Clutch Slave Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears; leaking fluid from the clutch line.
    * Solution: Replacement of clutch slave cylinder.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4L Boxer Engine with CVT Automatic Transmission**

* **CVT Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering or slipping; loss of power; hesitations during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replacement of CVT chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Transmission Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or noises when shifting gears; difficulty engaging gears.
    * Solution: Replacement of transmission mount.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT Automatic Transmission**

* **Programming Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitations or delays when shifting gears; rough shifting.
    * Solution: Software update.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free for recall service
    * Recalls: WUH-131 (2022)

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise from the front suspension; reduced stability when cornering.
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely; difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Battery testing and replacement if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or splits in the rear bumper, particularly around the license plate area.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear bumper.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, touch sensitivity issues, Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free for recall service
    * Recalls: WUH-130 (2022)
* **Steering Wheel Trim Detachment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel trim coming loose or detaching.
    * Solution: Reattachment of trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Subaru WRX is a capable performance car with a few notable issues. Some of the engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, but most of the other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the WRX remains a popular choice for enthusiasts who prioritize handling and all-wheel drive capability.
