# 2012 Subaru WRX

### Overview
The 2012 Subaru WRX, a performance-oriented compact car, is known for its spirited driving dynamics and all-wheel-drive capability. While generally considered a robust vehicle, like any performance car, it can experience specific issues related to its turbocharged engine and performance-oriented drivetrain. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. Understanding these common issues can help in proactive maintenance and informed decision-making regarding repairs.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged EJ255
**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust, engine overheating, loss of coolant, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gaskets, often with upgraded multi-layer steel gaskets.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, excessive smoke, oil leaks around the turbo.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Piston Ring Land Failure (Ringland Failure)**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust, loss of compression, knocking sounds.
- **Solution:** Engine rebuild with forged pistons or replacement engine.
- **Cost:** $3000 - $7000+


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell, shuddering on engagement.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Synchronizer Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting, difficulty engaging gears, especially in lower gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement of worn synchronizers.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell, shuddering on engagement.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $900 - $1600

**Input Shaft Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Whirring or grinding noise that changes with engine RPM, especially when clutch is disengaged.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1800 - $3500


## Suspension

### Standard Performance Suspension
**Worn Sway Bar Links/Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sway bar links and/or bushings.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Strut Mount Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noises when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of strut mounts.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement, grinding noise from the door.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Subaru WRX, while offering an engaging driving experience, presents several potential reliability concerns primarily centered around its high-performance turbocharged engine and drivetrain. Common issues include head gasket failures, turbocharger problems, and piston ringland issues, which can be costly to repair. The manual transmissions, particularly the 5-speed, can experience clutch wear and synchronizer issues. Suspension components like sway bar links and strut mounts are also noted for wear. Electrical issues such as alternator and window regulator failures are also documented. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor in the possibility of higher maintenance and repair costs compared to less performance-oriented vehicles. Thorough pre-purchase inspections are highly recommended.
