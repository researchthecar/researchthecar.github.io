**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2019 Subaru WRX is a compact sports sedan known for its turbocharged performance, all-wheel drive, and rally-inspired design. It is offered with a 2.0-liter turbocharged boxer engine, paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a CVT automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Boxer Engine**

**Valve Spring Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and failure of valve springs, leading to engine misfires, rough idling, and reduced performance.
Solution: Replacement of valve springs and related components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Piston Ring Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged piston rings can cause excessive oil consumption and blue smoke from the exhaust.
Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Clutch Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the clutch, resulting in difficulty shifting gears or slipping.
Solution: Clutch replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

**CVT Automatic Transmission**

**Belt Wear:**
Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the transmission belt, leading to shuddering, slipping, or loss of power.
Solution: Replacement of transmission belt and related components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Strut Bearing Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged strut bearings can cause abnormal noises, vibration, or reduced handling.
Solution: Replacement of strut bearings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Premature failure or weak batteries, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle or electrical malfunctions.
Solution: Battery replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rust Prone Subframe:**
Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust on the subframe, which can weaken the structural integrity of the vehicle.
Solution: Subframe replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Steering Wheel Leather Peeling:**
Description/Symptoms: Peeling or cracking of the leather on the steering wheel.
Solution: Steering wheel replacement or leather repair
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Subaru WRX is a capable and fun-to-drive car, but it is not without its issues. The engine and transmission problems can be severe and expensive to fix. The suspension, electrical, and exterior issues are less common but can still be costly to resolve. Overall, potential owners should be aware of these issues before purchasing a WRX.
