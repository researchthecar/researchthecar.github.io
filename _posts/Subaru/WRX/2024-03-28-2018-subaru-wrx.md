**2018 Subaru WRX**

The 2018 Subaru WRX is a high-performance sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It comes with a 2.0-liter turbocharged boxer engine that produces 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The WRX is available with a 6-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It has a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and impressive handling. However, there have been some complaints about reliability.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Boxer Engine**
    * **Premature ringland failure:** The piston rings can fail prematurely, causing excessive oil consumption and engine damage.
    * **Symptoms:** Blue smoke from the exhaust, low oil levels, rough idle, and loss of power.
    * **Solution:** Replace the piston rings and possibly the pistons.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $4,000+
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number 19V693

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Grinding gears:** The transmission can grind gears when shifting, especially when cold.
    * **Symptoms:** Difficult or impossible to shift gears, grinding noise when shifting.
    * **Solution:** Replace the synchronizers.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **CVT Transmission**
    * **Hesitation and lack of response:** The transmission can hesitate or fail to respond when shifting, causing a loss of power and acceleration.
    * **Symptoms:** Delay in acceleration, hesitation when shifting, and loss of power.
    * **Solution:** Replace the transmission control module or update the software.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number 18V909

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, even when the car is not in use.
* **Symptoms:** Dead battery, difficulty starting the car, and electrical system malfunctions.
* **Solution:** Replace the battery or investigate parasitic drain.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Wind noise:** Excessive wind noise can enter the cabin at highway speeds.
* **Symptoms:** Loud wind noise from around the windows and doors.
* **Solution:** Install wind deflectors or sound dampening material.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Subaru WRX is a great vehicle that offers a lot of performance and value. However, there are some reliability issues that should be considered before purchasing. The engine ringland failure can be a major problem, and the transmission issues can also be costly to fix. Wind noise is also an issue for some owners. Overall, the severity of these issues varies, and the cost of repairs can be high, which should be taken into consideration.
