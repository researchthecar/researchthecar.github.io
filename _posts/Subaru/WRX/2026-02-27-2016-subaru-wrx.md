# 2016 Subaru WRX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Subaru WRX brings rally-inspired performance and standard all-wheel drive to the compact sport sedan market. While enthusiasts appreciate its handling and turbocharged power, used buyers should carefully inspect powertrain and electrical systems. Owner reports indicate potential areas of concern regarding clutch durability, engine operation, and electrical battery draw.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Boxer 4-Cylinder (FA20F)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged direct-injection boxer four-cylinder engine produces 268 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It delivers punchy acceleration and standard Symmetrical All-Wheel Drive, though its complex turbo system and maintenance history require close scrutiny by used buyers.

**Engine Stalling and Oil Pressure Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loud rattle noises from the engine, inconsistent oil pressure, and potential engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Inspect oil pickup tubes for obstructions such as excess sealant, and diagnose underlying oil pressure faults.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The standard 6-speed manual transmission pairs directly with the 2.0L turbo engine. It offers engaging gear changes, but owners have reported premature clutch wear and mechanical shift linkages binding.

**Clutch Failure and Shifting Difficulties**
- **Symptoms:** Stiff or unusable clutch pedal, inability to shift into specific gears (such as 1st or reverse), or premature clutch component failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the clutch assembly, hydraulic master and slave cylinders, and replace worn friction discs or pressure plates as necessary.

### Sport Lineartronic CVT

The optional Sport Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission features manual shift modes via paddle shifters. It provides smoother daily commuting operation but is less common among enthusiast buyers.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Battery

Electrical complaints center primarily around parasitic battery drains and accessory integration.

**Recurring Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle battery drains repeatedly, leaving the car inoperable after sitting for short periods.
- **Solution:** Perform a parasitic draw test to identify faulty modules or accessories keeping the system awake, and replace the battery if damaged.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V366000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Crash Imminent Braking](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=WRX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the automatic pre-collision braking system does not function as intended, the vehicle will not react to an obstacle in its path, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Subaru will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the driver assist system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V162000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=WRX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Subaru will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the turbocharger air intake duct, and replace it as necessary, free of charge, depending on the lot number found on the duct. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V522000: Exterior Lighting:Back Up Lights:Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=WRX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative backup lights and an inoperative rearview camera can reduce vehicle visibility and driver awareness, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the backup light switch, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2016 Subaru WRX, a pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician is essential. Pay close attention to the service history of the manual transmission clutch and engine oil system. Finding a well-maintained, unmodified example significantly improves your chances of reliable ownership.
