## 2023 Subaru Solterra

The 2023 Subaru Solterra is an all-electric crossover SUV that marks Subaru's entry into the fully electric vehicle market. It is powered by a single electric motor, paired with a 72.8 kWh battery pack. The Solterra produces 215 horsepower and 249 pound-feet of torque, giving it a driving range of up to 228 miles on a full charge.

Drivers appreciate the Solterra's spacious interior, impressive safety features, and comfortable ride. However, some have expressed concerns about its limited cargo space and relatively slow acceleration.

### Electrical

* **Battery Pack Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery cells may experience premature degradation, leading to reduced battery capacity and shorter driving range.
    * Solution: Battery pack replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $15,000 - $25,000+
    * Recalls: #SB-23-080-E
* **Charging System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Charging port may fail to connect properly or charge the vehicle, resulting in prolonged charging times or incomplete charging.
    * Solution: Charging port replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: #SB-23-090-I

### Exterior

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wiper motor may malfunction, causing erratic or ineffective wiper operation.
    * Solution: Windshield wiper motor replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
* **Rear Taillight Moisture Accumulation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture may collect inside the rear taillights, impairing visibility and potentially causing electrical damage.
    * Solution: Taillight assembly replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

### Interior

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The Starlink infotainment system may experience lag or freezing, affecting navigation, audio, and other functions.
    * Solution: Software update or system reset.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: None
* **Climate Control Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control unit may fail to respond to inputs, resulting in the inability to adjust temperature or airflow.
    * Solution: Climate control unit replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

### Summary

Overall, the 2023 Subaru Solterra has received mixed reviews from owners. While it offers a spacious and comfortable interior, impressive safety features, and a decent driving range, it has also experienced some significant electrical issues. The battery pack issue is particularly concerning, as it can lead to costly repairs and reduced driving range, affecting the practicality and value of the vehicle. Other issues, such as the charging system malfunction and climate control unit failure, are less severe but can still be inconvenient and costly to fix. Prospective buyers should consider these issues carefully and weigh them against the Solterra's strengths before making a purchase decision.
