# 2024 Subaru Solterra Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Subaru Solterra marks the brand's all-electric entry into the compact SUV market, sharing its platform and dual-motor architecture with the Toyota bZ4X. As a relatively new EV model, prospective used buyers should weigh its all-wheel-drive capability and modern tech against early owner-reported quirks involving software warnings, accessory battery reliability, and driver-assistance sensitivities. While many drivers appreciate its packaging and standard capability, evaluating maintenance records and ensuring all software updates have been applied is wise.

---

## Engine

### Dual AC Synchronous Electric Motors with 72.8 kWh Battery

The Solterra utilizes dual AC synchronous electric motors producing around 215 horsepower and 249 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 72.8 kWh lithium-ion battery pack and standard symmetrical all-wheel drive. Powertrain responsiveness is generally smooth, though cold-weather range management and rare power reduction reports have been noted by early owners.

**Progressive Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences unexpected reductions in motor power or range while driving in cold weather conditions.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnostic codes checked by a certified dealer or service center to inspect high-voltage battery health and inverter software.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

A single-speed direct-drive automatic transmission distributes torque evenly to all four wheels on all trims. The direct-drive unit operates quietly and reliably without traditional gear shifting complexity.

## Electrical

### 12-Volt Accessory Battery and EV Control Systems

Electrical architecture governs the high-voltage drivetrain as well as standard 12-volt accessory functions. Early reports suggest occasional 12-volt battery drainage and auxiliary module alerts that require dealer diagnostics.

**12V Battery Failure and Malfunction Alerts**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden failure of the 12-volt battery leaving the vehicle stranded, accompanied by intermittent 'EV System Malfunction' or 'Drive Start Control Malfunction' dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Test the 12-volt accessory battery condition and update any associated vehicle control modules to the latest factory software versions.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance and Facial Recognition

Subaru's EyeSight-based safety suite provides comprehensive active safety features, though some operators report calibration sensitivities in driver monitoring and collision avoidance systems.

**Overly Sensitive Driver Monitoring and ADAS**
- **Symptoms:** Facial recognition frequently triggers false alerts while checking mirrors, and lane departure or collision avoidance systems can feel overly aggressive or intrusive.
- **Solution:** Adjust driver assistance sensitivity settings through the infotainment menu or consult a dealer for system calibration updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V577000: Visibility:Defroster/Defogger/Hvac System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Solterra&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** During certain HVAC system faults, such as electrical compressor failure, the defroster and defogger system may become inoperative. An inoperative defroster and defogger system can decrease the driver's visibility under certain driving conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the HVAC control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Solterra&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Subaru Solterra offers EV buyers standard all-wheel drive and a comfortable cabin, but its early production status demands careful vetting. Used shoppers should prioritize examples with verified service histories, checking that all battery management and electrical software campaigns have been properly completed by a dealer.
