# 2024 Subaru Crosstrek Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Subaru Crosstrek introduces a redesigned generation built on a stiffer platform, offering standard symmetrical all-wheel drive and increased ground clearance. While owners appreciate its practicality and rugged capability, early production models have accumulated reports concerning driver assistance quirks, infotainment distractions, and physical trim or lighting concerns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Horizontally Opposed 4-Cylinder (152 hp)

This naturally aspirated 2.0-liter boxer engine produces 152 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard power plant for Base and Premium trims. It provides reliable daily efficiency for most commuters, though acceleration can feel unhurried when passing at highway speeds.

### 2.5L Horizontally Opposed 4-Cylinder (182 hp)

Displacing 2.5 liters, this optional boxer engine generates 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque for Sport, Limited, and Wilderness trims. It offers noticeably punchier acceleration over the base engine while maintaining Subaru's signature low center of gravity.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A continuously variable transmission is paired standard with all engine options across the Crosstrek lineup, featuring active torque split all-wheel drive. It delivers smooth power delivery and optimized fuel economy, though buyers seeking traditional stepped gear shifts may find its operation droning under heavy throttle.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

Electric power steering provides light effort at low speeds, but select owners have reported unusual binding sensations during extended trips.

**Sticky or Binding Center Feel**
- **Symptoms:** After prolonged driving or at normal operating temperatures, the steering wheel can develop a slight hitch or 'sticky' spot on center, requiring extra effort and causing minor over-corrections.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer inspect the steering rack and electric power steering motor under warranty for binding components.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

Cabin electronics generally function well, though touchscreen software prompts can occasionally cause driver distraction.

**Intrusive Pop-Up Advertisements**
- **Symptoms:** Over-the-air promotional messages for services like SiriusXM suddenly cover the center touchscreen console while driving, forcing drivers to look away and manually dismiss them.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a dealership, though factory system interface behavior often requires manual dismissal.

## Exterior

### Side Mirrors

Exterior panels and glass are generally durable, though mirror stability at high velocities has drawn owner complaints.

**Driver Side Mirror Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** The exterior driver-side rearview mirror vibrates noticeably at highway speeds or on rough pavement, causing a distorted reflection.
- **Solution:** Dealer inspection may result in mirror glass or housing replacement if structural play is identified.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The EyeSight safety system occasionally triggers sudden phantom braking events or forward collision warnings when roads are clear or surface markings are unusual.
- **Solution:** Ensure the windshield camera glass is clean and have the EyeSight system recalibrated by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V551000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Crosstrek&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to insufficient clearance, the instrument panel harness may contact the steering beam bracket, damaging the wire insulation and causing a short circuit. A short circuit may cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the harness and install an additional clamp on the steering beam bracket. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V754000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Crosstrek&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the left and right front driveshafts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Subaru Crosstrek is a capable, highly praised compact crossover that benefits from a thorough redesign. While mechanical reliability for its boxer engines and standard all-wheel drive remains solid, prospective buyers should inspect electronics for software behavior, verify steering feel during a test drive, and ensure driver-assistance features are properly calibrated.
