**2022 Subaru Crosstrek**

The 2022 Subaru Crosstrek is a compact SUV offered with two engine options: a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine with 152 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine with 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Crosstrek is known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and standard all-wheel drive. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter 4-cylinder**
    * **Oil consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption. Symptoms include low oil levels on the dipstick and blue smoke coming from the exhaust. The solution is to replace the piston rings. Estimated cost to fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Head gasket failure:** There have been reports of head gasket failure, leading to coolant leaks and engine overheating. Symptoms include white smoke coming from the exhaust and coolant loss. The solution is to replace the head gasket. Estimated cost to fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
* **2.5-liter 4-cylinder**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **CVT**
    * **Hesitation and jerking:** Some owners have reported hesitation and jerking during acceleration. Symptoms include a delay in shifting and a rough, uneven ride. The solution is to update the transmission software. Estimated cost to fix: $0 - $200+
    * **Slipping:** There have been some reports of the transmission slipping while shifting. Symptoms include a loss of power and a high-pitched whining noise. The solution is to replace the transmission. Estimated cost to fix: $3,000 - $4,000+
* **6-speed manual**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front struts:** Some owners have reported premature failure of the front struts. Symptoms include a rough, bouncy ride and uneven tire wear. The solution is to replace the front struts. Estimated cost to fix: $400 - $600+
* **Rear shocks:** There have been some reports of the rear shocks failing prematurely. Symptoms include a soft, spongy ride and reduced handling. The solution is to replace the rear shocks. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $300+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some owners have reported excessive battery drain. Symptoms include a dead battery after a few days of non-use. The solution is to identify the source of the drain and repair it. Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment system:** There have been some reports of issues with the infotainment system, including freezing, crashing, and connectivity problems. The solution is to update the software or replace the infotainment unit. Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 23V001000

**Exterior**

* **Windshield cracking:** Some owners have reported windshield cracking. Symptoms include a small crack or several small cracks appearing in the windshield. The solution is to replace the windshield. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $500+
* **Body panel fit issues:** There have been some reports of body panel fit issues, such as gaps between panels and misaligned doors. The solution is to adjust the panels and doors. Estimated cost to fix: $0 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Steering wheel noise:** Some owners have reported a rattling or squeaking noise coming from the steering wheel. The solution is to lubricate the steering column. Estimated cost to fix: $0 - $100+
* **Plastic trim rattles:** Some owners have reported rattles coming from the plastic trim pieces in the interior. The solution is to tighten or replace the trim pieces. Estimated cost to fix: $0 - $200+
* **Carpet staining:** Some owners have reported that the carpet stains easily. The solution is to use a carpet protectant or clean the carpets regularly. Estimated cost to fix: $0 - $50+

**Summary**

The 2022 Subaru Crosstrek has received generally positive reviews, but there have been some reported issues. The most common issues include oil consumption, transmission hesitation, and suspension problems. While these issues can be frustrating, they are not widespread and most owners are satisfied with their Crosstrek. The estimated cost of repairs varies depending on the severity of the problem, but most issues can be fixed for a reasonable price.
