# 2022 Subaru Crosstrek Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Subaru Crosstrek blends hatchback practicality with rugged styling and standard all-wheel drive, making it a popular choice for commuters and weekend adventurers alike. While it generally offers dependable day-to-day transport, used car buyers should pay close attention to a few recurring owner-reported pain points, particularly regarding windshield durability and advanced driver-assistance system behavior.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

This 2.0-liter horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine produces approximately 152 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard power plant for base and premium trims. It provides adequate efficiency for daily driving, though acceleration can feel modest when passing at highway speeds.

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

Available on higher trims like the Sport and Limited, this larger 2.5-liter Boxer engine generates 182 horsepower and 176 lb-ft of torque. It delivers noticeably punchier acceleration, improving overall drivability compared to the base engine.

**Unexpected Acceleration and Braking Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported frightening incidents where the vehicle unexpectedly surged or accelerated while the foot was applied to the brake pedal, or experienced sudden hesitation.
- **Solution:** Have the throttle body, brake light switches, and electronic control modules inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired with standard symmetrical all-wheel drive across the lineup, this continuously variable transmission prioritizes fuel economy with smooth, gearless power delivery. While generally reliable under regular maintenance, buyers should listen for unusual whines or delayed engagement during test drives.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

Available exclusively on lower trims, this traditional manual transmission gives enthusiasts more direct control over gear selection. It features a positive shift action but is less common in the pre-owned market compared to its CVT counterpart.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

The exterior styling is rugged and well-liked, though the windshield glass has proven to be a notable weak spot for many owners.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners frequently report that the front windshield develops long cracks suddenly, often triggered by minor impacts or seemingly under normal ambient stress without prior visible chips.
- **Solution:** Replace the windshield with high-quality OEM or certified aftermarket glass, ensuring proper calibration of the EyeSight camera system if equipped.

## Safety

### EyeSight Driver Assist and Braking

Subaru's suite of active safety features provides valuable peace of mind, though sensor calibration and occasional false alerts remain minor points of frustration.

**Phantom Braking and Collision Avoidance Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may intermittently apply the brakes unexpectedly when no obstacles are present in the road, or fail to provide adequate collision warnings during heavy traffic.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates to the EyeSight module and ensure the upper windshield camera viewing area is completely clean and unobstructed.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Subaru Crosstrek is a well-rounded and capable crossover that offers great utility for everyday driving and light off-pavement excursions. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the service history, inspect the windshield for stress cracks or chips, and ensure all electronic driver-assistance systems function without erratic behavior during a test drive.
