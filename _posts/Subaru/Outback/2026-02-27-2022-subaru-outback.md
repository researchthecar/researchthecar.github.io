# 2022 Subaru Outback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Subaru Outback offers a blend of wagon practicality, standard all-wheel drive, and generous ground clearance, making it a popular choice for families and outdoor enthusiasts. However, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints regarding specific trouble spots, particularly related to exterior visibility and electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

A naturally aspirated 2.5-liter flat-four engine producing approximately 182 horsepower. It provides adequate daily power and efficiency, though some owners report concerns tied to the automatic start-stop system.

### 2.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

A turbocharged 2.4-liter flat-four engine delivering around 260 horsepower. It offers substantially stronger acceleration and towing capability for highway driving and heavier loads.

## Transmission

### Lineartronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A continuously variable transmission paired with standard symmetrical all-wheel drive across all trims. It maximizes fuel efficiency for daily commutes, though proper maintenance is essential for long-term durability.

## Brakes

### Brake Rotors and Pads

Brake components can experience premature wear or rotor warping, leading to vibrations during stopping.

**Premature Rotor Warping**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel and brake pedal shake uncontrollably when slowing down from highway speeds or descending hills.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for excessive wear or warping, and replace with quality aftermarket or updated OEM components if necessary.

## Electrical

### Battery and Infotainment

Electrical system complaints center around battery drains, intermittent infotainment screen blackouts, and occasional start-stop related engine stalls.

**Parasitic Battery Drain and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle battery loses charge unexpectedly, or the engine stalls out during low-speed or start-stop traffic conditions.
- **Solution:** Test battery health, check for parasitic electrical draws, and ensure all vehicle software modules are updated by a dealer.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior glass and visibility components are prone to unexpected cracking and weather-stripping wear.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers frequently report long cracks developing across the windshield seemingly without any prior rock impact or external damage.
- **Solution:** Have the windshield inspected and replaced by an experienced auto glass technician, ensuring proper calibration of any attached safety camera systems.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V473000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Outback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loose electrical connection to the ECU may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine wiring harness and ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V712000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Outback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 212, "Windshield Mounting." An insufficiently bonded windshield can detach during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V755000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Park/Neutral Start Interlock Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Outback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative inhibitor switch may prevent the reverse lights from illuminating and the rearview camera image from displaying, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the inhibitor switch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V227000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Subaru&model=Outback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A capacitor in the sensors for the Occupant Detection System (ODS) may crack and short circuit, which can prevent the front passenger air bag from deploying in a crash. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ODS sensors on the front passenger seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Subaru Outback is a versatile and capable crossover, but used-car buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly for common trouble spots. Pay close attention to the condition of the windshield, test all electrical features and infotainment functions, and verify that the brakes and battery have been well maintained.
