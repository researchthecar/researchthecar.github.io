## 2022 Subaru Outback

The 2022 Subaru Outback is a popular midsize SUV known for its ruggedness, spacious interior, and standard all-wheel drive. It is available with two engine options: a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine and a 2.4-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine. Both engines are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

Owners appreciate the Outback's versatile design, excellent fuel economy, and comfortable ride. However, some have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter 4-cylinder Engine**

* **Head gasket failure:** Oil and coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust. Solution: Head gasket replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000
* **Piston ring failure:** Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust. Solution: Piston ring replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000

**2.4-liter Turbocharged 4-cylinder Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** Loss of power, excessive oil consumption. Solution: Turbocharger replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $4000

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Premature wear:** Rough shifting, shuddering, slipping. Solution: CVT replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $5000 - $8000
* **Software issues:** Hesitation, delayed response, shuddering. Solution: Software update. Estimated Cost to Fix: Free

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure:** Abnormal noises, reduced handling, poor ride quality. Solution: Strut replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Battery discharge, difficulty starting. Solution: Battery replacement or electrical system diagnosis. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* **Alternator failure:** Dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions. Solution: Alternator replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000

**Exterior**

* **Windshield wiper motor failure:** Wipers inoperative or intermittent. Solution: Wiper motor replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system glitches:** Freezing, unresponsive touchscreen. Solution: Software update. Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
* **Climate control issues:** Malfunctioning fans, erratic temperature control. Solution: Climate control module replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
* **Seat heater failure:** Non-functioning seat heaters. Solution: Seat heater element replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Subaru Outback has received generally positive reviews. However, owners have reported a range of issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical glitches. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being relatively minor and others requiring significant repairs.
