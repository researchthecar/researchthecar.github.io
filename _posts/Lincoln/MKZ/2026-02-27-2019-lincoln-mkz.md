# 2019 Lincoln MKZ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2019 Lincoln MKZ, understanding its reliability profile is key to making a smart purchase. While this luxury sedan offers a comfortable ride and upscale styling, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues involving the powertrain and shift mechanisms before finalizing a deal.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 245 horsepower, providing a balance of efficiency and everyday performance. Buyers should carefully check service records for signs of coolant leaks.

**Coolant Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience engine misfires, rough acceleration, and unexpected engine shut-off while driving due to coolant leaking into the cylinders.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis; severe cases may necessitate engine block replacement.

**Oil Pan Seepage**
- **Symptoms:** Seepage or minor leaking around the perimeter of the oil pan, potentially developing into a steady leak over time.
- **Solution:** Inspect the oil pan seal during routine maintenance and replace gaskets or re-seal as needed.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

A potent 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 offering up to 400 horsepower for high-performance driving dynamics and smooth power delivery.

### 2.0L I4 Hybrid

A fuel-efficient hybrid powertrain pairing a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine with an electric motor for maximized city fuel economy.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with select gas engines, offering dependable and smooth gear shifts for daily commuting.

### CVT (Hybrid)

An electronic continuously variable transmission designed specifically for hybrid variants to optimize fuel efficiency and seamless power transfer.

### Shifter Mechanism

Cabin controls and gear selection components generally offer a refined feel, though owners have reported linkage durability concerns.

**Broken Shifter Cable Bushing**
- **Symptoms:** The shifter cable bushing may break, leaving the vehicle stuck in Park or preventing it from shifting out of gear properly.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged shifter cable bushing or assembly with updated components.

## Steering

### Steering Column and System

The electronic power steering setup provides light maneuverability at low speeds, but isolated complaints indicate potential column feedback and stiffness issues.

**Steering Stiffness and Wobble**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may occasionally experience a stiff steering wheel, unexpected resistance, wobbling while reversing, or a loose steering feel.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic power steering system, column connections, and sensors inspected by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V442000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKZ&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to display a blank image, or the image may remain on the display after the backing event has ended. Both of these situations can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lincoln MKZ delivers a quiet cabin, generous features, and appealing engine options, but buyers should exercise caution. Pay close attention to potential engine coolant leaks and shifter linkage issues during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid costly surprises.
