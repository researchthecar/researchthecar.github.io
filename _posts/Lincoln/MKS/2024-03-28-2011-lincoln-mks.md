**Introduction:**

The 2011 Lincoln MKS is a luxury sedan offered with two engine and transmission options: a 3.5L V6 paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.7L V6 paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable ride, a spacious cabin, and impressive performance. However, the MKS has faced criticism for its high price tag and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake Valve Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, rough idle
    * Solution: Replacement of intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: 13V348000

**3.7L V6**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replacement of fuel injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, delayed shifts, no movement
    * Solution: Overhaul or replacement of transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Clunking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension
    * Solution: Replacement of sway bar links or bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sits lower in the rear, poor handling
    * Solution: Replacement of rear coil springs or shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains even when the vehicle is parked
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical fault
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight assembly
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Panel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen display becomes unresponsive or glitchy
    * Solution: Replacement of center console panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2011 Lincoln MKS has some common issues across its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more costly and time-consuming. It is important to note that not all vehicles experience these issues, and the frequency and severity of the problems can depend on factors such as driving conditions, maintenance history, and previous repairs.
