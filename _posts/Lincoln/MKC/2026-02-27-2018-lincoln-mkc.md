# 2018 Lincoln MKC Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury compact SUV, understanding the mechanical background of your prospective vehicle is key to a satisfying ownership experience. This vehicle offers a comfortable ride and upscale cabin appointments, but prospective buyers should carefully review potential powertrain and component concerns reported by current owners before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L EcoBoost Turbocharged Inline-4

A small-displacement turbocharged four-cylinder engine designed for efficiency, generating around 180 horsepower. Buyers prioritizing fuel economy will find this option adequate, though service history should be checked.

### 2.0L EcoBoost Turbocharged Inline-4

A punchy turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing roughly 245 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for highway driving. It is the more common powerplant choice but requires close attention to cooling system health.

**Coolant Intrusion and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine lights, engine misfires, smoke from the tailpipe, and coolant loss into the engine cylinders.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis, which may involve engine teardown, gasket replacement, or complete engine replacement depending on the severity of internal damage.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic Transmission

A conventional six-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive systems. It generally offers smooth shifts and straightforward reliability compared to more complex modern multi-speed gearboxes.

## Suspension

### Wheel Lug Nuts

The wheel assembly and hardware are generally robust, though peripheral components like factory lug nuts can cause frustrating delays during tire rotations.

**Swollen Lug Nuts**
- **Symptoms:** Factory lug nuts become deformed or swollen over time, preventing standard tire irons or sockets from fitting properly during routine tire maintenance.
- **Solution:** Replace the factory two-piece lug nuts with durable, solid aftermarket replacements.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V104000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKC&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak may lengthen the distance needed to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the brake hoses, replacing any that are incorrect, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V378000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKC&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The battery monitor sensor may short-circuit and overheat, causing a fire in the engine compartment while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will add an in-line fuse to the battery monitor sensor power circuit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V572000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKC&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that displays an inverted, distorted, or blank image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V685000: Equipment:Electrical:Engine Block Heater](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKC&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The engine block heater may crack and develop a coolant leak, causing it to short circuit when the block heater is plugged in. An electrical short circuit can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the block heater, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, this luxury compact SUV delivers a refined driving dynamic and a quiet interior, making it an attractive daily driver on the used market. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the service records for any history of cooling system issues or engine coolant intrusion before finalizing their purchase.
