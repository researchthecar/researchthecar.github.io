# 2019 Lincoln Continental Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lincoln Continental represents the final chapters of this iconic luxury sedan's modern run, offering sophisticated styling, a plush ride, and a well-appointed cabin. While it delivers traditional American luxury and a quiet highway experience, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints regarding door latches, electrical warnings, and steering system alerts.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 305 horsepower, paired with front-wheel drive. It offers adequate acceleration and respectable efficiency for daily driving.

### 3.7L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 engine delivers around 305 horsepower with smooth power delivery. It provides traditional sedan performance paired with standard front-wheel drive or available all-wheel drive.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The high-output 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 generates up to 400 horsepower and comes standard with all-wheel drive. This option transforms the Continental into a swift, effortless cruiser, though it requires diligent maintenance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 6-speed automatic transmission handles gear changes smoothly across standard configurations. Regular fluid service helps maintain shift quality over high mileage.

### SelectShift 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, this version of the 6-speed automatic offers manual gear selection. It generally operates reliably with routine upkeep.

## Steering

### Adaptive Steering

The adaptive steering system can occasionally trigger fault codes and unusual steering behavior, requiring professional diagnosis.

**Adaptive Steering Failure Message**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice an 'Adaptive Steering Fault' warning message accompanied by unintended steering wheel angle shifts or heavy steering resistance.
- **Solution:** Have the adaptive steering module and related sensors inspected by a qualified technician; components may require replacement.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

electrical issues tied to door sensors and body control modules can trigger false alarms and warning messages.

**Door Ajar and Warning Message Alerts**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience recurring 'Door Fault Service Required' dashboard alerts or unexpected car alarm activation.
- **Solution:** Diagnose body control modules and repair faulty door sensor switches or latch wiring harnesses.

## Exterior

### Door Latch

Door latch mechanisms can experience mechanical binding or failure, posing potential convenience and safety concerns.

**Faulty Door Latch Mechanism**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report doors failing to latch properly, opening unexpectedly while driving, or refusing to close and stay shut.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the affected door latch assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V077000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Continental&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The door latch pawl motors may not function properly, possibly resulting in a door latch not fully engaging. A door latch that is not fully engaged may result in a door opening while driving, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the latch assemblies on all four doors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V644000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Continental&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Lack of a clear rearview camera image can reduce the driver's visibility of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V442000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Continental&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to display a blank image, or the image may remain on the display after the backing event has ended. Both of these situations can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lincoln Continental provides a quiet ride, spacious interior, and confident power options. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's door latches, electrical sensors, and steering systems during a pre-purchase evaluation to ensure all features operate correctly.
