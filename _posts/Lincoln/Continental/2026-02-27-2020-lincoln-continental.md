# 2020 Lincoln Continental Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lincoln Continental represents the final year of production for this luxury nameplate, offering a spacious cabin and serene ride quality. While overall complaint volume remains relatively low for this model year, prospective buyers should review specific owner reports regarding body hardware, visibility aids, and minor accessory behavior during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 engine produces approximately 305 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth, reliable power delivery for daily driving contexts.

### 2.7L EcoBoost V6

The twin-turbocharged 2.7-liter EcoBoost V6 generates roughly 335 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque. This engine pairs strong low-end acceleration with refined highway cruising capabilities.

### 3.0L EcoBoost V6

The high-output twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 pushes out around 400 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque exclusively with all-wheel drive. It offers effortless passing power, though buyers should ensure diligent maintenance of the turbocharging systems.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

A traditional 6-speed automatic transmission manages power delivery smoothly for base powertrains. It is generally regarded as a durable unit when standard fluid changes are performed.

### 8-Speed SelectShift Automatic

An 8-speed automatic transmission handles higher-output engines, providing responsive gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. Regular inspections help ensure long-term mechanical composure.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Visibility

Advanced safety systems offer high utility, but electronic vision aids should be verified for clear operation during pre-purchase evaluations.

**Foggy or Impaired Rearview Camera**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display may become foggy or obscure visibility when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Check camera lens clarity and electrical connections; replace the camera unit if internal degradation or failure is found.

### Power Trunk Lid

Electrical accessories function reliably overall, but power-operated convenience features should be checked for consistent sensor operation.

**Unexpected Trunk Closure**
- **Symptoms:** The power trunk lid may independently close unexpectedly while being loaded or accessed.
- **Solution:** Inspect power trunk struts, sensors, and control modules for proper calibration or mechanical failure.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Locks

Cabin hardware generally holds up well, though door latch mechanisms warrant functional testing during an inspection.

**Door Latch and Operation Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where front or rear doors fail to open properly either from the inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latch mechanisms and actuators; replace faulty door latch assemblies as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V644000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Continental&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Lack of a clear rearview camera image can reduce the driver's visibility of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V442000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Continental&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to display a blank image, or the image may remain on the display after the backing event has ended. Both of these situations can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lincoln Continental is a comfortable and well-appointed luxury sedan, though used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect power accessories, door latches, and driver assistance electronics. Conducting a comprehensive test drive and verifying all convenience features will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
