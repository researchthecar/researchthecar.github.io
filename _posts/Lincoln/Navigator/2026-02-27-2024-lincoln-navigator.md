# 2024 Lincoln Navigator Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lincoln Navigator stands as a commanding luxury full-size SUV, offering immense passenger space, premium interior appointments, and robust towing capabilities. However, early owner reports submitted to safety regulators highlight several recurring areas of concern, particularly regarding highway vibrations and unexpected powertrain behavior. Buyers evaluating a used model should pay close attention during a thorough test drive to spot potential mechanical or electronic quirks.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

Powered by a 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 producing around 440 horsepower and 510 lb-ft of torque, this engine delivers brisk acceleration and strong towing performance. Overall reliability for the powerplant itself is generally solid, though supporting electronic systems warrant careful inspection.

**Unexpected Lurching and Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lurch forward while parked or maneuvering, potentially culminating in a sudden engine shutdown.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the powertrain control modules, throttle body assembly, and related electronic sensors for fault codes.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission distributes power smoothly across the available rear-wheel and four-wheel-drive configurations. While gear changes are generally refined, occasional electronic shifting anomalies have been reported by owners.

**Spontaneous Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** While driving at highway speeds, the transmission may independently shift into neutral despite the drive gear being selected.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or qualified specialist to check for transmission software updates, control module faults, or internal linkage concerns.

### Suspension and Drivetrain Drivability

The suspension system is designed to provide a cloud-like luxury ride, but multiple owner reports point to stubborn high-speed vibration challenges that can be difficult to fully resolve.

**Persistent Highway Vibrations**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants may feel an unusual vibration, shimmy, or rough ride in the steering wheel and seats, particularly at speeds above 45 to 70 mph.
- **Solution:** Perform a thorough road-force balance on all tires and inspect the wheels, driveshaft, and transfer case components for balance or alignment issues.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Service Brakes

Braking hardware provides strong stopping power for this heavy SUV, though electronic safety intervention systems can occasionally trigger unintended engagement.

**Abrupt Braking and Warning Messages**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly brake abruptly in normal driving conditions accompanied by a 'Check Brake System' warning message.
- **Solution:** Have the brake control modules, sensors, and hydraulic actuators diagnosed by a professional technician for stored trouble codes.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Cameras

Advanced electrical architectures control a wide array of convenience and safety features, though occasional sensor misbehavior can affect exterior lighting and driver aids.

**Erratic Automatic High Beams**
- **Symptoms:** Automatic high beams may abruptly dim to illuminate only a very short distance ahead, failing to automatically switch back to brights.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield-mounted camera sensor and verify that the lighting control software is up to date.

### Glass and Trim

Exterior body panels and glass are generally robustly constructed, but isolated incidents of thermal or structural glass stress have been documented.

**Spontaneous Quarter Panel Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Rear quarter panel glass may suddenly shatter or break without apparent impact while the vehicle is stopped or parked.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the window frame mounting points for excessive structural stress or manufacturing flaws.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V851000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Navigator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rapid oil leak can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. In addition, an oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the engine cup plug alignment, and replace the plug if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V852000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Navigator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirement Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without a label that shows the proper restraint usage guidelines, the occupants may have an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V198000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Navigator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rapid oil leak can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. In addition, an oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the engine cup plug alignment, and replace the plug if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V314000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Navigator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of brake function increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front brake line and replace the brake line or air cleaner outlet pipe, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V315000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Navigator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera image to delay, freeze, or not display when the vehicle is in reverse. A frozen or missing rearview camera image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V104000: Electrical System: Integrated Trailer Brake Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Navigator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, " Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Trailer lights or brakes that do not function can reduce the driver’s ability to control the trailer and decrease the trailers visibility to other road users, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V165000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Navigator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Loss of the rearview camera image and certain driver assistance features, can reduce the driver’s ability to detect hazards, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Lincoln Navigator offers an exceptional blend of luxury, space, and capability for prospective used-car buyers. However, because early owner feedback highlights specific concerns regarding highway vibrations, electronic shifting anomalies, and erratic driver assistance behaviors, a comprehensive test drive at varying speeds is strongly advised. Buyers should verify that all software updates have been applied and secure a pre-purchase inspection to ensure long-term peace of mind.
