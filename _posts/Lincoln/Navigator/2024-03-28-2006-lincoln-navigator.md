**Introduction:**

The 2006 Lincoln Navigator is a full-size luxury SUV known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine options. It is available with three engine options: a 5.4L V8, a 6.8L V10, and a 4.6L V8, and a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 5.4L V8 produces 300 horsepower and 365 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.8L V10 produces 310 horsepower and 425 lb-ft of torque. The 4.6L V8 is less common and produces 239 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque.

The Navigator is well-received for its luxurious features, but some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**5.4L V8**

* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idle, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs and ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leak, reduced engine performance, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.8L V10**

* **Cam Phaser Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, reduced engine performance, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace cam phasers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.6L V8**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Problems:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, transmission shuddering
        * Solution: Replace transmission solenoids or rebuild transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque Converter Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, transmission overheating, shuddering
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven ride height
    * Solution: Replace air bags or compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling sounds from suspension, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty components (e.g., alternator, starter)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Shorts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, flickering lights, electrical components not working
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate not opening or closing properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 15V294000

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the interior through the sunroof
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seals or adjust sunroof alignment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing cold air, heater not working
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2006 Lincoln Navigator is a reliable vehicle overall, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners include engine and transmission issues. While some of the repairs can be costly, there are also several minor issues that are relatively inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Navigator offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience, but it is essential to be aware of the potential problems before making a purchase.
