# 2024 Lincoln Corsair Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lincoln Corsair is a luxury compact SUV that offers a refined cabin and modern powertrain choices. While overall owner feedback is limited, buyers should review known electrical complaints and active campaign notices closely during their shopping process.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 250 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque, providing standard front-wheel drive or optional all-wheel drive. It offers smooth everyday acceleration, though a few owners have flagged battery and charging interface interactions during operation.

### 2.5L Plug-In Hybrid (Grand Touring)

This plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with an electric motor to deliver a combined 266 horsepower with standard all-wheel drive. It provides efficient electric-only commuting range, though owners should verify hybrid software system statuses.

**EV Mode Software Override**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's software actively overrides Pure EV mode, automatically turning on the gas engine despite manufacturer interim instructions.
- **Solution:** Consult a certified dealer for software updates and follow ongoing manufacturer guidance regarding recall instructions.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This conventional 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with the 2.0L engine, offering smooth and responsive gear shifts. It generally operates reliably with no widespread complaint patterns reported by owners.

### CVT Automatic Transmission (Hybrid)

This electronic continuously variable transmission works alongside the plug-in hybrid system to optimize efficiency and power delivery. It offers seamless power blending without notable mechanical reliability complaints.

## Electrical

### Battery Saver and Charging System

Electrical complaints center primarily around battery drain issues and software notifications related to power management.

**Battery Saver Mode and Power Reduction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle frequently enters battery saver mode after sitting, reducing sensor effectiveness, disabling online features, and halting auto start-stop functions.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's battery health, charging system, and software versions inspected by a dealership.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior structural glass components have experienced isolated reports of spontaneous failure.

**Unexpected Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass suddenly explodes or shatters while driving at highway speeds without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass assembly for stress fractures and consult a dealer regarding potential glass replacement coverage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V852000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirement Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without a label that shows the proper restraint usage guidelines, the occupants may have an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V954000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Management System/Energy Control Module (Bms/Becm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A manufacturing defect in one or more of the high voltage battery cells may result in an internal short circuit and battery failure. Battery failure can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will perform a battery energy control module (BECM) software update, and replace the high voltage battery pack, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V315000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera image to delay, freeze, or not display when the vehicle is in reverse. A frozen or missing rearview camera image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V688000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Loss of rear lighting functions can reduce vehicle visibility and fail to alert other drivers that the vehicle is braking, turning, or reversing, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rear combination light assemblies, as necessary, free charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Lincoln Corsair delivers a comfortable luxury experience with sophisticated powertrain options. Prospective buyers should carefully verify vehicle history reports, check for open electrical recalls, and inspect the battery and infotainment systems prior to purchase.
