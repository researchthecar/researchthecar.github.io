# 2022 Lincoln Corsair Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lincoln Corsair offers a refined luxury compact SUV experience with comfortable seating, modern tech, and efficient powertrain choices. However, prospective buyers should review owner complaints regarding sporadic braking anomalies, electrical system glitches, and panoramic sunroof trim or glass durability. Ensuring that all manufacturer safety campaigns are completed is essential when evaluating a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This standard 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 250 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk everyday performance. It pairs well with the vehicle's standard layout and provides a balanced blend of power and efficiency for daily commuting.

### 2.3L Turbocharged I4

The available 2.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine bumps output to around 295 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque for punchier acceleration. It delivers strong performance for highway passing, though buyers should monitor for occasional powertrain electronic warnings.

**Powertrain Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that after coming to a stop, attempting to accelerate triggers a check engine light and a powertrain malfunction or reduced power message, requiring a vehicle restart.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnostics scanned by a qualified service center or dealership to check for stored trouble codes and apply any relevant software updates.

### 2.5L Plug-In Hybrid (Grand Touring)

The plug-in hybrid powertrain combines a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with electric motors to deliver an EPA-estimated electric-only driving range alongside strong overall efficiency. It provides smooth low-end torque but requires careful attention to high-voltage battery recalls.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly across front-wheel or all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally operates unobtrusively during everyday driving conditions.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (PHEV)

The eCVT paired with the plug-in hybrid powertrain seamlessly blends gas and electric operation for efficient city and highway commuting. Routine inspection of hybrid drivetrain components is recommended.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides firm stopping power under normal conditions, but isolated reports of low-speed pedal feel anomalies warrant a thorough pre-purchase mechanical inspection.

**Intermittent Low-Speed Brake Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced rare instances where the brake pedal feels unusual and unresponsive at very slow parking-lot speeds, requiring the vehicle to be shifted into park to stop.
- **Solution:** Have the braking system inspected immediately by a professional technician to check brake fluid, master cylinder operation, and electronic assist modules.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical architecture handles modern driver assists and cabin amenities, though occasional lighting and harness issues have been noted by owners.

**Rear Taillight Inoperability**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may discover that the rear exterior taillights become inoperable unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear lighting assemblies, wiring harnesses, and related fuses or control modules for faults.

## Interior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The exterior glass and trim require careful inspection, particularly around the panoramic roof assembly where shade retraction and trim retention issues have been reported.

**Sunshade Malfunction and Trim Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof shade may hang down, fail to roll up properly, or obstruct the rear-view mirror view due to loose top trim pieces.
- **Solution:** Have the sunroof mechanism and interior shade assembly adjusted or secured by a dealer or interior specialist.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V149000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high voltage battery, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V484000: Engine And Engine Cooling](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** In the event of an engine failure, engine oil and fuel vapor may be released into the engine compartment and accumulate near ignition sources such as hot engine or exhaust components, possibly resulting in an engine compartment fire. An engine compartment fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will modify the under engine shield and active grille shutter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V953000: Seat Belts:Front:Warning Light/Devices](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible warning chime that does not properly alert of an unbelted seat belt can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the audio control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V022000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of the rearview camera image can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the image processing module (IPMB) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V342000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of the rearview camera image can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the 360-degree camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V380000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** In the event of an engine failure, engine oil and fuel vapor may be released into the engine compartment and accumulate near ignition sources such as hot engine or exhaust components, possibly resulting in an engine compartment fire. An engine compartment fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine long-block in 2023 Escape and Corsair vehicles. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V330000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The Hybrid Powertrain Control Module (HPCM) software may cause the vehicle to shift into neutral unexpectedly, causing a loss of drive power. A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the HPCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V852000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=Corsair&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirement Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without a label that shows the proper restraint usage guidelines, the occupants may have an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lincoln Corsair is a well-appointed luxury crossover that appeals to used car shoppers seeking comfort and advanced features. While mechanical layout options are generally robust, buyers should verify that all open safety campaigns—especially those involving electrical systems and high-voltage components—have been addressed. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes, electrical accessories, and panoramic roof assembly will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
