# 2018 Lincoln MKT

### Overview
The 2018 Lincoln MKT, a full-size luxury crossover, generally presents a solid reliability profile, particularly for its powertrain. Owners often praise its comfortable ride and spacious interior, making it a capable family vehicle. While most systems perform well, some specific components have seen occasional reports of issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. The MKT's robust construction and advanced features contribute to its overall dependability, though vigilance with certain electrical and powertrain elements is advised.

### 2.7L EcoBoost V6
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic
**Hesitation or Shuddering**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking or rough shifting, especially at lower speeds or during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, potential software update or internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**AC/Heater Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent temperature, weak airflow, unusual noises from vents.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of blower motor, blend door actuators, or refrigerant recharge.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Lincoln MKT offers a generally reliable ownership experience, with its core powertrain components demonstrating good durability. The 3.7L V6 engine is particularly noted for its lack of widespread issues. However, owners should be aware of potential concerns with the 2.7L EcoBoost engine's coolant system and occasional transmission hesitation. Electrical gremlins, especially with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also areas to monitor. While the suspension, exterior, and interior systems are largely dependable, proactive maintenance and addressing minor electrical or climate control issues promptly can ensure continued satisfaction with this luxury SUV.
