# 2018 Lincoln MKT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lincoln MKT is a spacious, luxury three-row crossover sharing its platform with the Ford Flex, offering ample room and a comfortable highway ride. Used car buyers looking at this model should weigh its luxury features and powerful available engines against a handful of reported owner concerns involving body trim pieces and electrical accessories. As with any pre-owned luxury vehicle, a thorough personal inspection and test drive are recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 EcoBoost

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 produces around 365 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for highway driving. It is generally a strong performer, though higher-mileage examples may require routine maintenance on ignition and fuel components.

**Ignition and Fuel System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle hesitation, rough idling, potential stalling, and a flashing check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnose trouble codes, replace faulty spark plugs, ignition coils, or fuel injectors as necessary.

### 3.7L V6 Ti-VCT

The naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 generates approximately 303 horsepower and 278 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth power for everyday driving. This non-turbo option provides a simpler powertrain setup for buyers prioritizing straightforward reliability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

This conventional 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth shifts and pairs with both front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. Routine fluid changes help maintain long-term durability in used models.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Connectivity

Electrical accessories provide cabin convenience but can occasionally exhibit software glitches or connectivity drops reported by owners.

**Bluetooth and Navigation Disconnections**
- **Symptoms:** The navigation or Bluetooth system drops connections intermittently during trips.
- **Solution:** Update the SYNC software to the latest version or perform a system reset; inspect module connections if problems persist.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Pillar Trim

Exterior body trim pieces require periodic checks to ensure adhesive or retaining clips remain securely fastened.

**Detaching Exterior Trim**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior windshield trim or side pillar panels loosen and detach while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect exterior trim attachment clips and replace or resecure loose molding components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V072000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fractured rear toe link will cause a sudden change in vehicle handling and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear suspension toe links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V827000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Door trim that detaches while driving can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the B-pillar trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lincoln MKT offers a comfortable ride and generous interior space, making it an appealing option for families seeking a large crossover. While powertrain options are generally robust, buyers should inspect the condition of exterior body trim and test all electronic features during a pre-purchase examination.
