## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Sync System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, or failure to recognize connected devices.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential replacement of the module.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800


### Power Liftgate
**Liftgate Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Failure to open or close, unexpected stopping, or error messages.
- **Solution:** Inspection and adjustment of sensors, motor, or control module.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Blend Door Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or direct airflow to specific vents (e.g., only hot air or only cold air).
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty blend door actuator(s).
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


### Seat Adjustments
**Power Seat Motor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats may become slow, inoperable, or make grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the power seat motor or switch.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lincoln MKT generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly concerning its powertrain options (3.7L V6 and 3.5L EcoBoost V6) and the 6-speed automatic transmission, which show no widespread issues. The suspension system is also robust. However, potential owners should be aware of some common electrical and interior concerns. The infotainment system's Sync can experience glitches, and the power liftgate may develop malfunctions. Internally, issues with climate control blend door actuators and power seat motors have been reported. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and costly to repair. Overall, the MKT offers a comfortable and spacious experience, with most reliability concerns being manageable.
