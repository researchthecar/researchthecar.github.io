# 2017 Lincoln MKT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lincoln MKT is a full-size luxury crossover offering spacious three-row seating and robust available turbocharged performance. For used-car buyers, evaluating a model involves looking past standard luxury features to check powertrain health and address any lingering owner-reported concerns. While formal complaint volume remains relatively modest, prospective owners should carefully inspect the vehicle's mechanical and structural integrity before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

The 3.7-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces approximately 303 horsepower and 278 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth daily driving capability. This conventional engine option is generally straightforward to maintain compared to its turbocharged counterpart, making it a reliable choice for long-term ownership.

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged EcoBoost V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 delivers a robust 365 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque paired with standard all-wheel drive. While it offers strong acceleration and towing performance, buyers should watch for signs of turbocharger or oil consumption wear.

**Excessive Smoke from Exhaust**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an excessive amount of smoke coming from the exhaust pipe after the vehicle has been idling for several minutes, often without any accompanying warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the turbochargers, valve seals, and PCV system to diagnose the source of oil burning or combustion breakdown.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. Routine fluid changes are recommended to ensure longevity and crisp shifting behavior over higher mileage.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electronic power-assisted steering system provides light effort at low speeds and a heavier feel on the highway, though sporadic steering anomalies should be thoroughly checked.

**Unpredictable Steering Pull**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may begin to steer smoothly during lane changes at highway speeds, only to suddenly pull or shift direction more abruptly than intended.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering rack, alignment angles, and electronic power-assist components for faults or calibration issues.

## Safety

### Vehicle Structure

Body panels and structural assemblies generally hold up well against normal weathering, though owners should verify completion of any open safety campaigns.

**Recall-Related Structural Notification**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may receive safety recall notices regarding structural components while replacement parts are in transit or pending availability at local dealerships.
- **Solution:** Contact a local certified dealership with your VIN to check the status of open structural campaigns and schedule repairs once parts arrive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V072000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fractured rear toe link will cause a sudden change in vehicle handling and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear suspension toe links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V925000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak, in the presence of an ignition source, increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and if necessary, replace the turbocharger oil supply tubes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V827000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Door trim that detaches while driving can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the B-pillar trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lincoln MKT blends luxury comfort with capable hauling performance, but used-car shoppers should approach with standard due diligence. Thoroughly test drive both the naturally aspirated and EcoBoost powertrain options, check for signs of oil consumption or smoke during idle, and verify that all safety recalls have been properly addressed by a certified dealer.
