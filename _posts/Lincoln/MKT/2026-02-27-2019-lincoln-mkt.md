# 2019 Lincoln MKT

### Overview
The 2019 Lincoln MKT, a full-size luxury crossover, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting Lincoln's focus on comfort and refinement. While not immune to all potential issues, common problems tend to be relatively minor and manageable. Owners often praise its spacious interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine options. However, like many vehicles in its class and age, some specific components may experience wear or require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-routine issues that have been reported by owners and repair professionals, excluding standard maintenance and expected wear and tear.

---


## Engine

### 2.3L EcoBoost I4
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 3.5L EcoBoost V6
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of turbocharger assembly or related plumbing.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic (FWD/AWD)
**Hesitation or Shifting Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky shifts, delayed engagement, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or potential internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, vague steering feel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Sync System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, freezing or rebooting.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Battery testing and replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Lincoln MKT demonstrates a generally good reliability profile, with most reported issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws. The EcoBoost engines, while powerful, can occasionally present with turbocharger or coolant leak concerns, and the transmission may exhibit occasional shifting anomalies. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system, are not uncommon. However, these issues are often repairable without excessive cost. For prospective buyers, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is always recommended, focusing on the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. Overall, the MKT offers a comfortable and capable luxury SUV experience with a reliability that, while not perfect, is competitive for its segment.
