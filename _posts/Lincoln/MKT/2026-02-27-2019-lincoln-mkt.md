# 2019 Lincoln MKT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Lincoln MKT, buyers will find a spacious and distinctive luxury crossover built for comfortable family road trips. While powertrain options offer robust performance, potential buyers should review specific owner-reported feedback regarding visibility systems and rear components before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged EcoBoost V6 delivers approximately 365 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration and standard all-wheel drive. This engine is generally robust, though routine maintenance of the turbocharging system and cooling components is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

Equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission paired with paddle shifters, this drivetrain offers smooth gear changes suited for highway cruising. Regular fluid changes help maintain smooth shifting performance over high mileage.

## Suspension

### Rear Suspension

The rear suspension setup provides a comfortable ride quality, though critical link components should be inspected for wear or structural failure.

**Rear Toe-Link Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A loud noise from the rear of the vehicle while driving, followed by sudden swerving or difficulty maintaining directional control.
- **Solution:** Have the rear suspension components inspected by a qualified technician and replace damaged toe-links immediately.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera

Owner reports indicate occasional issues with rearview camera clarity and functionality.

**Intermittent or Fuzzy Display**
- **Symptoms:** The backup camera display may become blurry, intermittent, or completely unavailable with an error message.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera wiring harness, connections, and camera module, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V548000: Electrical System:Alternator/Generator/Regulator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The power supply cables at the starter and the alternator may not have been properly secured, possibly resulting in an electrical arc. An electrical arc can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the alternator and starter motor power supply cables to verify that they are properly secured, correcting them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V951000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that displays a blank or distorted image can reduce the drivers view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V695000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera that fails to properly display an image can reduce the driver’s view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V827000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Door trim that detaches while driving can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the B-pillar trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lincoln MKT offers a comfortable ride and strong twin-turbo performance, making it an appealing option for buyers seeking a large crossover. Prospective owners should carefully inspect the rear suspension components and verify that backup camera systems function properly during a pre-purchase test drive.
