# 2016 Lincoln MKT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Lincoln MKT, buyers will find a spacious and comfortable luxury crossover with ample power. While owner reports to federal regulators highlight a few notable concerns—particularly regarding glass components and steering functionality—the vehicle remains a capable cruiser. Prospective owners should carefully inspect areas prone to wear and verify vehicle history before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.7L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 engine produces roughly 303 horsepower and provides smooth, dependable power for daily driving. It generally serves as a solid and reliable baseline powertrain option for the MKT.

**Engine Running Lean**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminates repeatedly with diagnostic codes indicating a lean air-fuel mixture, while the vehicle otherwise continues to drive normally without smoke or performance loss.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel delivery system, vacuum lines, and mass airflow sensor to identify and resolve any unmetered air entering the engine.

### 3.5L EcoBoost V6

The twin-turbocharged 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6 generates approximately 365 horsepower, offering brisk acceleration and standard all-wheel drive. Buyers should ensure regular oil maintenance was performed to promote long-term turbocharger health.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SelectShift Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to either the front wheels or the intelligent all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid changes are recommended to maintain shift quality over high mileage.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Issues related to the electric power steering system can occasionally arise, requiring driver attention if assist functionality is lost.

**Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The electric power assist can suddenly fail while driving, requiring heavy manual effort to steer the vehicle accompanied by a warning message on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering module and wiring harnesses, and replace the steering gear assembly if internal electrical faults are found.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Owner feedback highlights rare instances of spontaneous glass breakage involving the panoramic roof panels and rear windows.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic moonroof or rear glass can suddenly fracture or shatter into pieces while driving or parked, often accompanied by a loud popping noise.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame alignment and weatherstripping to ensure no undue stress is placed on the replacement glass.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V925000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak, in the presence of an ignition source, increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and if necessary, replace the turbocharger oil supply tubes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V072000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fractured rear toe link will cause a sudden change in vehicle handling and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ford will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear suspension toe links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V827000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lincoln&model=MKT&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Door trim that detaches while driving can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the B-pillar trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lincoln MKT offers a compelling blend of utility, comfort, and performance for used vehicle buyers. While owners have submitted complaints regarding electric power steering failures and rare instances of shattering panoramic sunroof glass, standard powertrain options are generally robust. Buyers are advised to thoroughly inspect the glass panels and steering operation during a pre-purchase test drive.
