**Introduction:**

The 1991 Rolls-Royce Silver Spur is a luxurious sedan powered by a 6.75L V8 engine and paired with a 3-speed automatic transmission. It boasts an impressive 278 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque. While the Silver Spur is renowned for its opulence and comfort, it also has some reported issues that owners should be aware of.

---

**Engine:**

**6.75L V8**

* **Head gasket failure:** The head gasket can fail, leading to coolant leaks and overheating. Symptoms include white smoke from the exhaust, low coolant levels, and a loss of power. The issue is typically resolved by replacing the head gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Intake manifold gasket leak:** The intake manifold gasket can develop a leak, causing vacuum leaks and a rough idle. The solution involves replacing the gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

---

**Transmission:**

**3-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission may slip, resulting in difficulty shifting gears or a loss of power. The issue can be addressed by rebuilding or replacing the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+

---

**Suspension:**

* **Air suspension failure:** The air suspension system can fail, causing the vehicle to ride low and potentially hit speed bumps or obstacles. The issue is usually resolved by replacing the air suspension components or converting to a coil spring suspension. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

---

**Electrical:**

* **Electrical system failure:** The electrical system can malfunction, causing issues with the lights, ignition, or other electronic components. The problem can be traced to a faulty alternator, battery, or wiring harness. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Radio failure:** The factory radio can experience various issues, such as display problems, no sound output, or CD player malfunctions. The radio typically needs to be replaced or repaired. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

---

**Exterior:**

* **Rust:** The Silver Spur can develop rust on the body panels, especially around wheel arches and door seams. The rust can be treated and prevented with rustproofing measures. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of rust

---

**Interior:**

* **Wood trim cracking:** The wood trim on the dashboard and door panels can crack or delaminate over time. The trim can be refinished or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Power window failure:** The power window motors or switches can fail, resulting in inoperable windows. The issue is usually resolved by replacing the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+

---

**Summary:**

While the 1991 Rolls-Royce Silver Spur offers a remarkable driving experience, it has some potential issues that owners need to be aware of. The engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system can experience problems, requiring costly repairs. The interior and exterior also have some potential issues, but they are generally less severe. Overall, the Silver Spur requires careful maintenance and can be expensive to repair, but it remains a highly desirable and luxurious vehicle.
