## Suspension

### Hydropneumatic Self-Levelling
**Hydraulic Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, loss of suspension responsiveness.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, hoses, or hydraulic pump. System flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500

**Accumulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, bouncing, loss of self-levelling capability.
- **Solution:** Replacement of hydraulic accumulators.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Rolls-Royce Park Ward, while a masterpiece of engineering, demands a high level of specialized care.
