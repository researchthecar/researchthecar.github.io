# 2022 Rolls-Royce Cullinan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Rolls-Royce Cullinan represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, combining effortless power with an exceptionally plush ride. For prospective used-car buyers looking at this high-end SUV, understanding its foundational engineering and powertrain options helps clarify what to expect from ownership at this exclusive tier.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

This potent 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces roughly 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and quiet acceleration. It is built to high standards of refinement, providing effortless power delivery suitable for an ultra-luxury vehicle.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission works seamlessly with the V12 engine, utilizing GPS data to pre-select gears for upcoming corners. It offers nearly imperceptible shifts and robust long-term reliability when properly maintained.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Rolls-Royce Cullinan remains an engineering masterpiece focused on supreme comfort and stately performance. Buyers should ensure thorough professional inspections and verified service history, as maintenance and upkeep at this tier require significant financial commitment.
