The 2021 Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a luxury SUV that offers a combination of opulence and off-road capability. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.75-liter V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options are not available for the 2021 Cullinan.

People like the Cullinan for its spacious and luxurious interior, its powerful engine, and its all-wheel-drive system. However, some people have reported issues with the Cullinan, including:

**Engine**
* **Twin-Turbocharged 6.75-liter V12 Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Symptoms include reduced engine power, excessive smoke, and oil leaks. Solution: Replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * **Oil Leaks:** Symptoms include oil leaks from the engine, transmission, or differential. Solution: Repair or replace the leaking component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Cooling System Problems:** Symptoms include overheating, coolant leaks, and a loss of coolant. Solution: Repair or replace the cooling system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

**Transmission**

* **Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Slipping:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, delayed engagement, and a loss of power. Solution: Repair or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension System Malfunction:** Symptoms include a loss of ride height, uneven ride, and a rough ride. Solution: Replace the air suspension components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Rear Axle Bearing Failure:** Symptoms include a whining or grinding noise from the rear axle, and excessive play in the rear wheels. Solution: Replace the rear axle bearing. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Symptoms include a dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle, and flickering lights. Solution: Replace the battery or repair the electrical system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
* **Infotainment System Malfunction:** Symptoms include a frozen screen, unresponsive buttons, and a loss of sound. Solution: Reset the infotainment system or replace the system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**
* **Body Panel Gaps:** Symptoms include uneven gaps between the body panels. Solution: Adjust or replace the body panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Wheel Bearing Failure:** Symptoms include a grinding or whining noise from the wheels, and excessive play in the wheels. Solution: Replace the wheel bearing. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Interior**
* **Wood Trim Cracking:** Symptoms include cracks or splits in the wood trim. Solution: Replace the wood trim. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**
The 2021 Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a very expensive vehicle, and as such, the cost of repairs can be high. However, the issues that have been reported are not widespread, and most owners are very satisfied with their vehicles.
