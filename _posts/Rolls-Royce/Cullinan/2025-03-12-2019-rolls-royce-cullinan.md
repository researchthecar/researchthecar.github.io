The 2019 Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a luxury SUV known for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine. It is powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Cullinan is praised for its luxurious features, comfortable ride, and commanding presence. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's complex electronics, air suspension, and occasional engine-related problems.

**Engine**

**6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

* **Engine Misfires**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported engine misfires, often accompanied by a check engine light. The misfires can cause rough idling, reduced power, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Diagnosis with an OBD-II scanner to identify the affected cylinder(s) is needed. Replacement of faulty ignition coils or spark plugs is the typical fix. In some cases, fuel injector issues can also cause misfires.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Oil Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from various engine seals and gaskets have been reported. Symptoms can include oil spots under the vehicle, a burning oil smell, and low oil levels.
    * Solution: Identify the source of the leak (valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, rear main seal, etc.). Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced rough or delayed shifting, especially during cold starts or under heavy acceleration. Hesitation when accelerating from a stop has also been reported.
    * Solution: A transmission fluid flush and filter replacement may improve shifting performance. In some cases, software updates or valve body replacement may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The Cullinan's air suspension can experience issues such as sagging, uneven ride height, or complete failure. Warning lights on the dashboard may indicate a problem.
    * Solution: Diagnosis of the air suspension system to identify faulty components, such as air struts, air compressor, or ride height sensors. Replacement of the defective parts and recalibration of the system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can experience glitches such as freezing, crashing, or failure to respond to inputs. Connectivity issues with smartphones have also been reported.
    * Solution: Software updates can often resolve infotainment system glitches. In some cases, replacement of the infotainment unit may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $3,000+ (unit replacement)
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sensor Failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various sensors throughout the vehicle (e.g., parking sensors, blind spot monitoring sensors) can fail, leading to warning lights and system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Diagnosis with a diagnostic scan tool to identify the faulty sensor(s). Replacement of the defective sensor(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Trim Pieces Loosening**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that some trim pieces, particularly in the headliner or door panels, can become loose or detached over time.
    * Solution: Reattaching the trim pieces with appropriate adhesives or clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Cullinan, while being a pinnacle of luxury, can be prone to several issues, primarily related to its complex systems. The air suspension and electrical systems appear to be the most problematic areas, with potential for costly repairs. While the engine and transmission issues are less common, they can still result in significant expenses. Overall, prospective buyers should be aware of the potential for these issues and factor in the costs of maintenance and repair when considering this vehicle.
