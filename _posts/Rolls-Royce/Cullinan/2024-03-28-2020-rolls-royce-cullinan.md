**Introduction to the 2020 Rolls-Royce Cullinan**

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a luxury SUV that combines opulence with off-road capabilities. It is powered by a 6.75-liter V12 engine that generates 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The Cullinan comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

Owners praise the Cullinan for its exceptional comfort, luxurious interior, and impressive off-road performance. However, some have reported issues with various components, such as the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system.

**Engine**

**6.75L V12 Engine**

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
Solution: Replacing valve stem seals or piston rings is necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Delayed or Rough Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: Occasional delayed or rough gear shifts have been reported, particularly when accelerating or decelerating.
Solution: Updating transmission software or replacing transmission fluid may resolve the issue.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $1,000+
Recalls: #20V-561

**Suspension**

**Electronic Air Suspension Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning electronic air suspension modules can cause an uneven ride height or suspension failure.
Solution: Replacing the faulty air suspension modules is necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Discharge**
Description/Symptoms: Parasitic battery drain can occur, leading to a dead battery over time.
Solution: Identifying and isolating the cause of the parasitic drain is required.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: #20V-515

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Occasional failures of the climate control system, resulting in improper temperature distribution.
Solution: Replacing or repairing faulty sensors or control modules may be necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a luxurious and capable SUV that has received positive feedback from owners. However, some issues have been reported with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. These issues range in severity from minor annoyances to more significant repairs, with estimated costs varying depending on the specific problem.
