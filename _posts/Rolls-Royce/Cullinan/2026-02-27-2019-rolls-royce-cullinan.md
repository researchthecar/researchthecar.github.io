# 2019 Rolls-Royce Cullinan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Cullinan represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, combining effortless power with an exceptionally plush ride. As an ultra-exclusive hand-built vehicle, prospective buyers should anticipate unique ownership dynamics, where maintenance and specialized servicing require significant investment despite low volume owner reports.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering seamless and near-silent acceleration. This powerhouse is generally robust, though routine maintenance demands meticulous adherence to manufacturer intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission features satellite-aided shifting to anticipate road topography ahead. It provides ultra-smooth gear changes suited to the vehicle's luxurious character with minimal reported mechanical complications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V612000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Cullinan&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The brake lights may not be bright enough. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Without a sufficient level of brightness from the brake lights, the vehicle may have reduced visibility to other drivers when braking.
- **What to do:** Rolls-Royce Motor Cars will notify owners, and Rolls-Royce Motor Car dealers will update the brake light software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Cullinan&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Cullinan offers extraordinary craftsmanship and unmatched road presence.
