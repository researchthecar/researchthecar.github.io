# 2021 Rolls-Royce Cullinan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Cullinan represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury sport utility vehicles, pairing peerless craftsmanship with a commanding presence. Because used luxury vehicles of this caliber are typically maintained meticulously by their owners, prospective buyers benefit from low overall complaint volume in public reporting channels. However, maintaining an ultra-exotic vehicle requires specialized care and substantial financial preparation for routine servicing or unexpected mechanical attention.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

This 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and whisper-quiet acceleration. It is widely regarded for its smooth power delivery, though proper maintenance is critical to avoid costly upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an advanced 8-speed automatic transmission paired with all-wheel drive, the Cullinan utilizes satellite-aided gear selection to anticipate road geometry. The drivetrain prioritizes maximum refinement and seamless shifting under all driving conditions.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Cullinan shows virtually no widespread mechanical complaint trends in public owner databases, reflecting its elite engineering and limited production volume. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an authorized specialist, verify service history, and ensure they are prepared for the exceptionally high costs of ownership and maintenance associated with ultra-luxury vehicles.
