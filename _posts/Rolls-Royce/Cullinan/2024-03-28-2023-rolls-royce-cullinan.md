**Introduction to the 2023 Rolls-Royce Cullinan**

The Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a luxury SUV that combines performance with opulence. It is powered by a 6.75-liter V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, and comes paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Cullinan is praised for its exceptional comfort, powerful engine, and luxurious interior, but some owners have reported issues with certain aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**6.75-liter V12**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Fuel injectors may fail, causing rough idling, poor fuel economy, and a lack of power. Replace the faulty injectors. $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**8-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting, causing a loss of power and acceleration. Replace or repair the transmission. $3,000 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction:** The air suspension system may malfunction, causing the vehicle to sag or bounce excessively. Replace the air suspension struts or compressor. $2,000 - $4,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** A faulty battery or alternator may drain the battery, preventing the vehicle from starting. Replace the battery or alternator. $500 - $1,500+
* **Electrical Glitches:** Occasional electrical glitches may occur, causing intermittent issues with navigation, climate control, or other electronic systems. Software updates or component replacements are typically needed. $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:** Door handles may malfunction, making it difficult to open or close the doors. Replace the door handle assembly. $500 - $1,000+
* **Sunroof Leaks:** The sunroof may leak during heavy rain or car washes. Replace the sunroof seals or adjust the sunroof alignment. $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system may occasionally malfunction, causing the interior to become too hot or cold. Software updates or component replacements may be needed. $500 - $1,200+
* **Infotainment Display Pixelation:** The infotainment display may experience pixelation or flickering. Software updates or display replacement may be necessary. $1,000 - $2,000+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2023 Rolls-Royce Cullinan is a highly luxurious and capable SUV, but it has its fair share of issues. While some of the issues are minor, others can be more costly and affect the overall reliability of the vehicle. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
