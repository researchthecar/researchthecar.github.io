# 2020 Rolls-Royce Cullinan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Cullinan represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury sport utility vehicles, combining effortless power with an impeccably quiet ride. For prospective buyers exploring the pre-owned market, understanding its mechanical makeup and overall dependability is essential for a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and effortless acceleration. This engine is designed for supreme refinement and longevity, though routine maintenance costs remain exceptionally high.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the all-wheel-drive system with seamless, almost imperceptible gear shifts. It complements the V12 powerplant perfectly and generally proves highly reliable when properly maintained.
