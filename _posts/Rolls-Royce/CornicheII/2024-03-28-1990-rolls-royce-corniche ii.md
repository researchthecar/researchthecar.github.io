**Introduction:**

The 1990 Rolls-Royce Corniche II is a luxury convertible that was powered by a 6.75-liter V8 engine mated to a 3-speed automatic transmission. It produced 220 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. The Corniche II was praised for its comfort, style, and performance, but it was also known for its hefty price tag.

**Engine**

**6.75-liter V8**

* **Blown head gaskets:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil stains under the vehicle, low oil pressure
    * Solution: Replace gaskets and seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**3-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Worn shock absorbers:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per axle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical shorts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Flickering lights, malfunctioning electronics
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty wiring and components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin during rain
    * Solution: Replace or repair seals and gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty climate control:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning AC or heating system
    * Solution: Repair or replace AC compressor, heater core, or other components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1990 Rolls-Royce Corniche II is a classic luxury convertible that is known for its comfort and performance, but it can also be prone to certain issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can be high, so potential owners should be prepared for maintenance and repair expenses.
