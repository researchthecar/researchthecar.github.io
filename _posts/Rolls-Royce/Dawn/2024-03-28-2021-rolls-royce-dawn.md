**Introduction:**

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Dawn is a luxury convertible that offers the ultimate in opulence and performance. It is powered by a 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Dawn is also available with an optional Black Badge package that boosts the engine output to 593 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque.

Owners of the 2021 Rolls-Royce Dawn generally praise its sumptuous interior, powerful engine, and impressive build quality. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12**
    * **Intake valve sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation and rough running at low speeds, increased emissions
        * Solution: Replace intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic**
    * **Delayed shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission takes longer than usual to shift gears or shifts erratically
        * Solution: Update transmission software
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension**
    * **Air leaks in suspension struts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uneven ride height, decreased handling performance
        * Solution: Replace suspension struts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Premature battery failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, reduced battery life
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlights**
    * **Condensation inside headlight assemblies**
        * Description/Symptoms: Foggy or cloudy appearance inside headlights
        * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof mechanism**
    * **Convertible top retraction issue**
        * Description/Symptoms: Convertible top fails to retract or retract smoothly
        * Solution: Lubricate or replace convertible top mechanism components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control**
    * **AC compressor failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: AC system fails to cool or blows warm air
        * Solution: Replace AC compressor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary:**

While the 2021 Rolls-Royce Dawn offers exceptional luxury and performance, it is not without its issues. Some of the reported problems, such as engine intake valve sticking and transmission shifting delays, can be significant. However, most issues are relatively minor and can be resolved within a reasonable cost range.
