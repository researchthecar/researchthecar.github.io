# 2017 Rolls-Royce Dawn Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Rolls-Royce Dawn is an ultra-luxury convertible designed to offer supreme comfort, effortless performance, and hand-crafted opulence. While vehicles in this echelon are meticulously engineered and typically maintained with great care by their owners, prospective used-car buyers should approach with an understanding that ownership costs and specialized maintenance will be exceptionally high.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 563 horsepower and 575 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and silent acceleration. Overall, this powertrain is robustly built, though routine servicing requires specialist attention and premium fluids.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission utilizes GPS data to anticipate gear shifts based on the road ahead, ensuring seamless power delivery. It is widely regarded as highly reliable when subjected to regular maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V115000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Dawn&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat-mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Dawn&modelYear=2017)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Rolls-Royce Dawn offers an extraordinary motoring experience defined by peerless luxury and a powerful V12 engine. Because official owner complaints are virtually non-existent for this model year, buyers can focus primarily on verifying a comprehensive service history and conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to ensure flawless operation.
