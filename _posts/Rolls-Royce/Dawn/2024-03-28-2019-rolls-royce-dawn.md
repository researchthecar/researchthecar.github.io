**Introduction:**

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Dawn is a luxurious convertible that combines classic styling with modern technology. It is powered by a 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Dawn has received praise for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and impressive performance. However, it also has a few reported issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced power, illuminated check engine light
* Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Shifting Delays**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears, especially at low speeds
* Solution: Reprogram transmission control module or replace transmission fluid
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500

**Suspension**

**Adaptive Air Suspension**

**Air Compressor Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Suspension becomes overly soft or stiff, vehicle may not be able to maintain proper ride height
* Solution: Replace air compressor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Electrical**

**Battery Discharge**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, vehicle may not start after sitting for a period of time
* Solution: Test battery and alternator, replace faulty components
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Infotainment System**

**Touchscreen Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen becomes unresponsive or displays distorted images
* Solution: Update system software or replace touchscreen display
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

**Convertible Top Motor Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Convertible top fails to open or close, motor may make a grinding noise
* Solution: Replace convertible top motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Interior**

**Wood Trim Separation**
* Description/Symptoms: Wood trim panels on the dashboard or door panels may become loose or separate
* Solution: Replace affected trim panels
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Rolls-Royce Dawn is a highly desirable vehicle that offers exceptional performance and luxury. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners involve the fuel injectors, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. While the cost of repairs can be high, the severity of the issues is relatively minor and they should not significantly impact the overall enjoyment of this luxurious convertible.
