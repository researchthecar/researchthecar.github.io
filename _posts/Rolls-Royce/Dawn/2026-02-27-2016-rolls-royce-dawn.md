### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Rolls-Royce Dawn demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, consistent with its luxury positioning. The powertrain, suspension, and core structural components are robust and rarely present significant issues. The most commonly cited, albeit minor, concerns tend to be related to the sophisticated electrical systems, particularly the infotainment interface, and the complex power-operated convertible roof and door mechanisms. These issues are typically resolved with software updates or minor adjustments. Overall, the Dawn is a well-engineered vehicle with a strong track record for reliability, provided it receives appropriate care and maintenance.
