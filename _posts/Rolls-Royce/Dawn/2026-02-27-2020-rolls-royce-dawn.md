## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning light on dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant running of compressor, warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, occasional system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software update or system reset. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500 (for software/diagnostics), $1500+ (for module replacement)


### Power Closing Doors/Trunk
**Mechanism Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Doors/trunk not closing fully or automatically, unusual noises during operation.
- **Solution:** Lubrication, adjustment, or replacement of the actuator/motor.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Rolls-Royce Dawn demonstrates a high level of reliability, as expected from a vehicle of its caliber. The powertrain (engine and transmission) is exceptionally robust with no widespread issues reported. Potential concerns are primarily concentrated in the complex suspension and electrical systems, particularly the air suspension components and the infotainment system's software. The power-operated convertible roof and interior/exterior finishes are generally problem-free. While repairs can be costly due to the specialized nature and premium components, proactive maintenance and addressing minor electrical glitches promptly can ensure the continued flawless operation of this ultra-luxury convertible.
