**Introduction to the 2020 Rolls-Royce Dawn**

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Dawn is a luxurious convertible that combines unparalleled comfort and performance. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 605 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Dawn is available with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive.

Owners appreciate the Dawn's opulent interior, which features supple leather upholstery, a panoramic glass roof, and a state-of-the-art infotainment system. However, they have also reported some issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **Twin-Turbocharged 6.6-Liter V12**
    * **Oil Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the oil pan gasket or the oil cooler lines
        * Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or lines
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * **Misfiring**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power, and increased fuel consumption
        * Solution: Replace the faulty spark plugs or ignition coils
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 20V426000

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears
        * Solution: Reprogram or replace the transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Clunking Noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: A clunking noise may be heard when the transmission shifts into gear
        * Solution: Replace the faulty valve body
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze or become unresponsive
        * Solution: Update or replace the infotainment software
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
    * **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely
        * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or alternator

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Glass Roof Seal Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Water may leak into the interior through the panoramic glass roof
        * Solution: Replace the defective seal
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Dawn is a luxurious and powerful convertible. However, it has been reported to have some issues, including oil leaks, misfiring, transmission hesitations, infotainment system malfunctions, and battery drains. These issues can be expensive to fix, but Rolls-Royce typically covers them under warranty.
