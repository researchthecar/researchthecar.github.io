# 2021 Rolls-Royce Phantom Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Phantom represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, powered by a majestic V12 engine and a silk-smooth automatic transmission designed for effortless cruising. Because this flagship sedan is produced in very limited numbers and caters to an exclusive clientele, real-world owner feedback and complaint data are exceptionally scarce. For prospective used-car buyers, this means mechanical and electrical histories are rarely documented in public databases, requiring a thorough professional pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist before finalizing any transaction.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

This 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering near-silent and immense acceleration. It serves as the standard and sole powerplant for the Phantom, known for refined operation but requiring meticulous maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission utilizes satellite-aided technology to pre-select gears based on upcoming corners and terrain. It pairs seamlessly with the V12 engine to provide imperceptible gear shifts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V855000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The Central Information Display may not raise to the correct position, resulting in a rearview camera image that is partially blocked from view. A partially blocked rearview camera image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V554000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During a crash, a seat belt retractor that does not function as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the seat belt retractors (including the seat belts) and, if necessary, replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Phantom is an engineering marvel defined by bespoke luxury, an exceptionally powerful V12 engine, and a pillowy ride quality. Due to the complete absence of common complaint trends or recurring mechanical failures in public registries, traditional reliability metrics are less relevant than individual service history. Buyers must prioritize a comprehensive inspection by a certified Rolls-Royce technician to verify that all complex electronics, pneumatic suspensions, and powertrain systems operate flawlessly.
