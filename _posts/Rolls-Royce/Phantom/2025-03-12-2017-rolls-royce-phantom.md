The 2017 Rolls-Royce Phantom is the eighth generation of this ultra-luxury sedan, representing the pinnacle of automotive opulence. It comes equipped with a powerful 6.75-liter twin-turbo V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. Power is delivered through an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners typically praise the Phantom for its unparalleled ride quality, exquisite craftsmanship, and near-silent cabin. Criticisms often center around its sheer size, making it challenging to maneuver in tight spaces, and its considerable cost of ownership, including maintenance and repairs.

**Engine**

**6.75L Twin-Turbo V12**
*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks can develop from various engine seals and gaskets, leading to oil consumption and potential engine damage. Visible oil puddles under the car, a burning oil smell, and low oil level warnings are common symptoms.
    *   Solution: Replacing the faulty seals and gaskets. Requires specialized tools and expertise.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Turbocharger Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Potential issues with the turbochargers, including boost leaks, failure to build boost, and unusual noises. Can result in reduced engine power and performance.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis to determine the source of the issue, potentially requiring turbocharger replacement or repair.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $10,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Rough Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported occasional rough or delayed shifts, particularly when the transmission is cold or under heavy load.
    *   Solution: Requires diagnostic testing to determine the cause of the issue. Could potentially need a new valve body or solenoid replacement, software updates, or a full transmission replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $10,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Phantom's air suspension is known for its smooth ride. However, components such as air struts, compressors, and sensors can fail, causing the car to sag, ride unevenly, or trigger warning lights.
    *   Solution: Replacing the faulty air suspension components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $8,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Glitches, freezes, and unresponsive touchscreen controls. Navigation system errors.
    *   Solution: Software updates are often the first step. If the problem persists, component replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain can lead to starting problems, particularly if the car is not driven regularly.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis to identify the source of the parasitic drain. Could be a faulty module, sensor, or wiring issue.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Automatic Door Closing System Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The automatic door closing system can malfunction, causing doors to fail to close properly or become stuck.
    *   Solution: Requires diagnosis and repair or replacement of the affected door closing mechanisms.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Power Window Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows fail to operate correctly, either becoming slow, stuck, or inoperable.
    * Solution: Replacing the motor or regulator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Rolls-Royce Phantom, while a marvel of engineering and luxury, can be susceptible to some significant issues, particularly regarding the engine, suspension, and electrical systems. The cost of repairs can be substantial due to the complexity of the vehicle and the specialized parts and labor required. Addressing these potential problems is essential for maintaining the Phantom's performance, reliability, and overall value.
