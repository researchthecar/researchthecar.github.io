# 2022 Rolls-Royce Phantom Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Rolls-Royce Phantom represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering handcrafted elegance and an effortless driving experience. As an ultra-low-volume flagship vehicle, historical reliability data and owner complaints are extremely sparse, reflecting both its elite status and the meticulous maintenance habits of its owners.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and silent power. This sophisticated powerplant is built for effortless acceleration, though buyers should expect exceptionally high maintenance and servicing costs typical of ultra-luxury engineering.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an advanced 8-speed automatic transmission featuring satellite-aided gear shifting to anticipate road contours. It delivers seamless power delivery to the rear wheels, prioritizing ultimate ride comfort.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V855000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The Central Information Display may not raise to the correct position, resulting in a rearview camera image that is partially blocked from view. A partially blocked rearview camera image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Rolls-Royce Phantom is an engineering masterpiece built with elite craftsmanship, and it currently lacks widespread recurring complaint trends. Prospective buyers should always ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a specialized marque expert, keeping in mind that ownership costs, parts, and specialized labor will be extraordinarily high.
