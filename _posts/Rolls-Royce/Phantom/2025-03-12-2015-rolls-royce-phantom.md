The 2015 Rolls-Royce Phantom is a pinnacle of luxury and craftsmanship, powered by a 6.75-liter V12 engine producing 453 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the Phantom for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and unmistakable presence. Common dislikes revolve around its immense size, fuel consumption, and high maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**6.75L V12**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks may occur from various engine seals and gaskets, leading to a noticeable drop in oil level and potential oil spots under the vehicle.
    *   Solution: Replacing the affected seals and gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+ (depending on the location of the leak and complexity of the repair)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the valve cover area, causing burning smell and potential oil spots.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the valve cover gasket.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough or jerky gear changes, particularly during acceleration or deceleration.
    *   Solution: Transmission fluid flush and software update, or valve body replacement in severe cases.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $3000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor may fail, leading to a sagging or uneven ride height. Error messages might appear on the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $4000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Air Strut Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Air leaks from the air struts, causing the vehicle to sit unevenly or drop overnight. Hissing sound may be audible near the affected strut.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaking air strut(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $5000+ (per strut)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Malfunctioning**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, lag, or exhibit erratic behavior.
    *   Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Premature battery drain, causing difficulty starting the vehicle or requiring frequent jump starts.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair of parasitic drain, or replacement of the battery.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Self-Closing Door Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The self-closing door mechanism may malfunction, causing the doors to not fully close or latch properly.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the door latch mechanism or associated sensors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+ (per door)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Summary**

The 2015 Rolls-Royce Phantom, while offering unparalleled luxury, can experience issues typical of high-end vehicles. Suspension and electrical problems are the most commonly reported issues, with potential for high repair costs.
