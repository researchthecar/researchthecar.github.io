# 2023 Rolls-Royce Phantom Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Rolls-Royce Phantom represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, combining an ultra-smooth ride with immense handcrafted prestige. For prospective used-car buyers, ultra-luxury flagships demand careful inspection given their immense complexity and bespoke technology. While formal owner complaint volume remains exceptionally low for this model year, buyers should always verify service history and electrical systems before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 6.75-Liter Twin-Turbocharged V12

This 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless, silent acceleration. It is an exceptionally smooth and robust powerplant tailored for supreme refinement.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission utilizes GPS data to pre-emptively select the correct gear for upcoming corners and road contours. It offers seamless gear changes that complement the effortless power delivery of the V12 engine.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V721000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The headlights may not have received a metal coating application, which can result in the low beams not adequately illuminating road signs. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Devices." Improperly illuminated road signs may be difficult.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the headlights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Rolls-Royce Phantom is an engineering marvel designed to offer unmatched comfort and prestige. Because specific owner complaints are virtually non-existent for this model year, your primary focus as a used buyer should be verifying a meticulous dealer service history and ensuring all complex electronics operate perfectly.
