# 2019 Rolls-Royce Phantom Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a ultra-luxury used vehicle like the 2019 Rolls-Royce Phantom, buyers step into an exclusive tier of automotive engineering. Built to deliver effortless performance and supreme comfort, it relies on a potent V12 powerplant and sophisticated transmission engineering. Because vehicles in this class see varied ownership histories and complex electronic architectures, proper due diligence and a specialized pre-purchase inspection remain essential before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless, silent acceleration. It is exceptionally smooth and robust, though buyers should ensure consistent maintenance history given the complexity of a modern ultra-luxury twelve-cylinder engine.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Satellite-Aided Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission utilizes GPS data to anticipate gear shifts based on the road ahead, ensuring seamless power delivery to the rear wheels. It is well-matched to the V12's torque output, offering refined and imperceptible shifts suited to the car's luxury character.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V855000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The Central Information Display may not raise to the correct position, resulting in a rearview camera image that is partially blocked from view. A partially blocked rearview camera image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Phantom represents the pinnacle of handcrafted automotive luxury, pairing a potent V12 engine with an advanced multi-speed automatic transmission. While major mechanical troubles are rare, the vehicle's immense complexity means any service or maintenance can be exceptionally costly. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection performed by an authorized specialist or master technician.
