# 2018 Rolls-Royce Phantom Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Rolls-Royce Phantom represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering an uncompromising blend of hand-crafted elegance and effortless performance. As an ultra-low-volume flagship vehicle, comprehensive long-term reliability data can be scarce compared to mass-market luxury cars. Prospective buyers should approach pre-owned examples with careful inspection routines, keeping in mind that ultra-luxury maintenance and specialized repairs demand significant financial preparation.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and silent power. This sophisticated powerplant is generally robust, though routine servicing requires specialist care and premium maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission pairs seamlessly with the V12 engine, utilizing satellite-aided transmission mapping to pre-select gears based on upcoming topography. It offers seamless power delivery with a strong reputation for smooth operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Rolls-Royce Phantom is an extraordinary machine built to uncompromising standards, and available owner feedback indicates a generally quiet reliability profile for its core powertrain. Because formal defect reports are extremely rare for this exclusive model, buyers must prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an authorized specialist. Ownership involves exceptional operating costs, making a meticulous service history essential before purchase.
