# 2020 Rolls-Royce Phantom Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Phantom represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering peerless comfort, hand-crafted interior appointments, and effortless performance. As an ultra-low-volume flagship vehicle, evaluating its reliability involves understanding its highly complex bespoke electronics, advanced chassis systems, and robust V12 powertrain. While formal owner complaints and widespread mechanical failures are exceptionally rare for this tier of vehicle, prospective buyers should still approach shopping with an awareness of the intensive maintenance and specialized servicing required to keep a modern Rolls-Royce in top condition.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 2020 Phantom is powered by a smooth 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine generating approximately 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. It delivers seamless, silent acceleration suited to a luxury flagship and generally proves dependable when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed to the rear wheels via a sophisticated ZF 8-speed automatic transmission featuring satellite-aided gear shifting that anticipates terrain. It operates invisibly in the background and is known for refined, polished shifting behavior.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V855000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The Central Information Display may not raise to the correct position, resulting in a rearview camera image that is partially blocked from view. A partially blocked rearview camera image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Phantom is an engineering marvel designed for supreme comfort and prestige, lacking the common recurring mechanical complaints found in mainstream vehicles. Buyers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections by certified specialists, verify service history, and budget significantly for routine upkeep, insurance, and specialized care.
