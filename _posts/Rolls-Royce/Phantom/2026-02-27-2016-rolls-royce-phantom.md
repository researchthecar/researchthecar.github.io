# 2016 Rolls-Royce Phantom Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Rolls-Royce Phantom represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering handcrafted elegance and effortless performance. For prospective used-car buyers, ultra-luxury vehicles of this caliber require meticulous inspection and an appreciation for complex automotive engineering. While complaint volumes for this specific model year remain exceptionally low, maintaining such an exclusive machine demands specialized servicing and significant financial preparedness.

## Engine

### 6.75-liter V12 Engine

Equipped with a naturally aspirated 6.75-liter V12 engine producing approximately 453 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. This smooth powerplant delivers effortless acceleration suited for an ultra-luxury flagship, though regular professional maintenance is essential to ensure long-term mechanical health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with a rear-wheel-drive configuration. It provides seamless gear shifts that prioritize absolute ride comfort and effortless power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V787000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Phantom&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have left and right side curtain air bags that may not have been manufactured properly. As a result of the improper manufacturing, the air bags may not position themselves as intended during deployment, increasing the risk of occupant injury in the event of a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Rolls-Royce will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the side curtain air bags, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
