**Introduction:**

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Phantom is an ultraluxurious sedan that combines opulent craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology. It is powered by a 6.75-liter V12 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Phantom delivers 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. Drivers and critics alike praise its serene ride quality, sumptuous interior, and advanced features. However, as with any complex vehicle, some owners have reported specific issues.

**Engine**

* **V12 Engine**
    * **Oil Leaks:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gradual loss of engine oil, visible leaks from seals or gaskets.
        * Solution: Replace leaking gaskets or seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noises from under the hood, check engine light may illuminate.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking During Shifts:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission feels hesitant or jerky during gear changes.
        * Solution: Update transmission software, replace valve body or transmission solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+
    * **Transmission Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Complete loss of power, grinding noises, vehicle may become immobile.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5000 - $10000+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle may lean to one side, rough ride quality, compressor may run excessively.
    * Solution: Replace faulty air bags, compressor, or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3500+
* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing, poor handling, vehicle may feel unstable.
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power to various components, check engine light may illuminate.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty electrical components, replace battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2500+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, apps crash, audio system issues.
    * Solution: Update software, replace infotainment module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2500+

**Recalls:**

* NHTSA Recall Number: 21V922000
    * Issue: Electrical short circuit in front passenger seat fuse box can cause fire.
* NHTSA Recall Number: 20V857000
    * Issue: Fuel leak from fuel tank can cause fire.

**Summary:**

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Phantom is generally a reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The severity of the issues varies from minor to major, and the cost to repair can be significant. However, it's important to note that these issues are not widespread and most Phantom owners enjoy a trouble-free ownership experience.
