The 2023 Rolls-Royce Phantom is the epitome of luxury and craftsmanship, powered by a formidable 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. A "Black Badge" version is also available, which boosts the engine output to 592 horsepower. Owners praise the Phantom for its unparalleled ride quality, opulent interior, and bespoke customization options. However, some criticisms include its considerable size, weight, and high price point.
**Engine**

**6.75L Twin-Turbo V12**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from various engine seals, resulting in a burning smell and potential low oil levels. Oil spots may be visible under the vehicle.
    *   Solution: Replace the affected seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+ depending on the seal's location and labor involved.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Jerking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The transmission exhibits harsh or jerky shifts, especially during low-speed maneuvers or when shifting between gears.
    *   Solution: Software update to the transmission control module (TCM). If the problem persists, valve body replacement or transmission replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 for software update; $2,000 - $8,000+ for valve body/transmission replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system fails to maintain the proper ride height, leading to a sagging vehicle. Error messages related to the suspension system appear on the dashboard. Loud noises may be heard from the compressor.
    * Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes or crashes intermittently, causing loss of navigation, audio, and other system functions. The screen may become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Software update. If the problem persists, replacement of the infotainment unit may be required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500 for software update; $3,000 - $8,000+ for unit replacement.
    * Recalls: None Found
*   **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains rapidly, especially when the car is not in use for extended periods. This may result in difficulty starting the engine.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of parasitic drain. Possible replacement of the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ depending on the source of the drain and if the battery needs to be replaced.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Automatic Door Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The automatic doors fail to open or close properly. The door mechanism might be noisy or unresponsive.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of the door actuators and sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+ per door depending on the extent of the damage.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Navigation System Errors**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system shows incorrect location, has trouble determining position, or loses GPS signal.
    * Solution: Update navigation software, replace GPS antenna.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
*   **Power Seat Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power seats do not adjust correctly or fail to move.
    * Solution: Replacement of the seat motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+ per seat.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Rolls-Royce Phantom, while offering unparalleled luxury and performance, may experience issues, particularly related to the engine, transmission, air suspension, electrical systems, and automatic doors. The severity of these issues ranges from minor software glitches to major mechanical failures. Repair costs can be substantial, reflecting the high cost of parts and specialized labor associated with the vehicle.
