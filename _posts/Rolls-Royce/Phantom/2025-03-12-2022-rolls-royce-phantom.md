The 2022 Rolls-Royce Phantom is the pinnacle of luxury automobiles, renowned for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and powerful presence. The Phantom is powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Some owners appreciate the Phantom's unparalleled comfort, bespoke customization options, and prestige, while others find its immense size and high price tag to be drawbacks.

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Rebooting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system, responsible for navigation, audio, and vehicle settings, occasionally freezes or spontaneously reboots. This can occur intermittently and may disrupt the driving experience.
    *   Solution: Software update. If the problem persists, replacing the infotainment system may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $5,000+ (Infotainment System Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Driver Assist Features Malfunctioning**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Issues with the driver assist systems, such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, and park assist. These features may intermittently fail to activate or provide inaccurate alerts.
    *   Solution: Recalibration of the sensors or replacement of the faulty sensors/modules.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sticking Center Console Controls**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Occasional issue with some center console controls sticking.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the affected switches/controls.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Malfunctioning Power Window Regulator**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The power windows may become slow or fail to open or close completely due to a malfunctioning regulator.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the affected window regulator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Automatic Door Closure Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The doors have issues with either not closing when the button is pressed, or closing improperly.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of the door closure system, replacement of the door lock actuator, or recalibration of the system.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Engine**

**6.75-liter Twin-Turbocharged V12**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from various engine seals and gaskets. This can manifest as visible oil drips under the car or a burning smell.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaking gaskets and seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$5,000+ depending on severity of the leak
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Rough or Delayed Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or hesitant gear changes, particularly when accelerating or decelerating.
    *   Solution: Transmission software update or replacement of faulty solenoids. In severe cases, a complete transmission rebuild or replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $10,000+ (Transmission Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Rolls-Royce Phantom, while an exceptional vehicle, is not immune to issues. Electrical problems with the infotainment system and driver-assist features seem to be common issues. Interior complaints mostly come in the form of malfunctioning controls and window regulators, while exterior issues seem to be centered around the automatic door closures. Engine oil leaks and transmission shifting problems can occur, albeit with varying degrees of severity. The cost of repairs can range from relatively minor software updates to significant expenses for component replacements, reflecting the high cost of parts and labor associated with this luxury vehicle. Potential buyers should be aware of these common issues and ensure a thorough inspection before purchase.
