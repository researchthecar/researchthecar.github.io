**Introduction:**

The 2023 Rolls-Royce Phantom is the epitome of luxury and opulence. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.75L V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Phantom is praised for its smooth ride, spacious cabin, and attention to detail. However, it is also criticized for its high price tag and lack of modern technology.

**Exterior**

* **Chrome Trim Peeling:** Affects early 2023 models. The chrome trim on the grille, side vents, and exhaust tips may peel or discolor over time.
  * **Solution:** Replace affected trim with updated version.
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Some vehicles may experience intermittent or complete failure of the climate control system.
  * **Solution:** Replace faulty climate control module.
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Door Lock Failure:** The door locks on some vehicles may fail to operate properly, leading to difficulty opening or closing the doors.
  * **Solution:** Replace faulty door lock actuators.
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system may occasionally experience glitches, such as freezing, rebooting, or displaying error messages.
  * **Solution:** Update software or replace faulty components.
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $0 - $500+

**Recalls:**

* NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-608: Loss of Power Steering Assist
* NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V-520: Door Lock Failure

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2023 Rolls-Royce Phantom is a well-crafted vehicle with few reported issues. The most common problems affect the exterior chrome trim and the interior climate control and door lock systems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they are generally not considered major concerns.
