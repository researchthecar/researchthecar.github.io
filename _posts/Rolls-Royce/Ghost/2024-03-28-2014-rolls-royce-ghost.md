**Introduction:**

The 2014 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxurious sedan known for its exceptional comfort and sophisticated design. It is available with a single engine option: a 6.6-liter V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 575 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**6.6L V12 Engine**

**Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
Description/Symptoms: The intake valves can become clogged with carbon deposits over time, causing reduced performance and fuel efficiency. The symptoms may include decreased acceleration, rough idling, or hesitation.
Solution: The intake valves need to be cleaned or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Delays**
Description/Symptoms: Shifting between gears may be delayed or occur at the wrong time. This can lead to reduced performance and a rough driving experience.
Solution: The transmission control module (TCM) may need to be updated or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

**Front Air Suspension Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The front air suspension may fail, causing the vehicle to ride low and uneven. The symptoms may include reduced handling and stability.
Solution: The air suspension struts may need to be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Rear Suspension Bushing Wear**
Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension bushings may wear out over time, causing noise, vibration, and reduced handling.
Solution: The suspension bushings need to be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start. The symptoms may include dim headlights, slow cranking, or a dead battery.
Solution: The battery may need to be replaced or the electrical system may need to be diagnosed.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**Audio System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The audio system may malfunction, causing distorted sound, intermittent operation, or a complete failure.
Solution: The audio system may need to be repaired or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

**Chrome Trim Corrosion**
Description/Symptoms: The chrome trim on the vehicle may corrode over time, causing a dull or rough finish.
Solution: The chrome trim needs to be cleaned or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Condensation may form inside the headlights, causing fogging or reduced visibility.
Solution: The headlight lens may need to be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

**Wood Trim Panel Warping**
Description/Symptoms: The wood trim panels on the interior may warp or crack over time, causing an uneven or distorted appearance.
Solution: The wood trim panels may need to be refinished or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

**Infotainment System Glitch**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, causing slow performance, screen freezing, or random restarts.
Solution: The infotainment system may need a software update or may need to be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+

**Summary:**

The 2014 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan with a number of potential issues. The most common problems include intake valve carbon buildup, shifting delays, suspension failures, electrical problems, and interior issues. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicles regularly serviced to prevent or address them及时.
