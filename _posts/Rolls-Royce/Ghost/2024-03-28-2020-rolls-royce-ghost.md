**2020 Rolls-Royce Ghost**

The 2020 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan that comes with a 6.75-liter V12 engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. It produces 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost is known for its luxurious features, including a spacious cabin, soft leather seats, and advanced technology.

**Engine**

* **6.75-liter V12**

    * **Oil Leak:** Oil leaks can occur around the oil filter housing or valve covers. Symptoms include a burning oil smell, oil spots on the driveway, or low oil levels. Replacing the gaskets or seals typically fixes the problem. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
    * **Engine Misfiring:** Engine misfiring may be caused by faulty spark plugs or ignition coils. Symptoms include a rough idle, loss of power, and increased fuel consumption. Replacing the affected parts resolves the issue. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Coolant Leak:** Coolant leaks can occur from the radiator, water pump, or hoses. Symptoms include overheating, low coolant levels, and a sweet odor. Replacing the leaking components resolves the problem. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+

**Transmission**

* **Eight-speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:** Transmission fluid leaks can occur from the transmission pan or lines. Symptoms include low transmission fluid levels, slipping gears, or delayed shifting. Replacing the leaking parts typically fixes the problem. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600+
    * **Transmission Slipping:** Transmission slipping occurs when the transmission loses grip and fails to transfer torque properly. Symptoms include delayed shifting, loss of power, or shuddering. Repairing the transmission with new clutches or solenoids typically resolves the problem. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension**

    * **Air Bag Leak:** Air bag leaks can cause the suspension to sag or become uneven. Symptoms include a bumpy ride, difficulty maintaining ride height, or warning lights. Replacing the leaking air bags resolves the problem. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
    * **Compressor Failure:** The air compressor may fail, causing the suspension to become inoperable. Symptoms include the suspension not being able to raise or lower, or a warning light. Replacing the compressor fixes the problem. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** A parasitic drain on the battery may cause it to discharge prematurely. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, dim lights, or a dead battery. Diagnosing the cause of the drain is essential, as it could be caused by various components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the cause
* **Electrical Malfunctions:** Electrical malfunctions can occur due to faulty wiring, modules, or sensors. Symptoms can vary widely and may include warning lights, inoperative components, or system failures. Troubleshooting and repair are necessary to determine the exact cause and fix. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the fault
* **Heater/AC Malfunction:** The heater or air conditioner may malfunction due to issues with the compressor, blower motor, or control module. Symptoms include lack of heat or cooling, strange noises, or warning lights. Repair involves diagnosing and replacing the faulty components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Ambient Lighting Malfunction:** The ambient lighting system may malfunction due to faulty LEDs or control module. Symptoms include dim or flickering lights, uneven illumination, or warning lights. Repair involves replacing the affected components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600+
* **Infotainment System Glitch:** The infotainment system may experience glitches such as freezing, slow performance, or connectivity issues. Updating the software or replacing the head unit typically resolves the problem. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $1,000+
* **Panoramic Roof Issues:** The panoramic roof may develop leaks or rattling noises. Symptoms include water leaking into the cabin or excessive noise when the roof is opened or closed. Repair involves resealing or replacing the roof components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2020 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a reliable and luxurious vehicle. However, it does have some potential issues that owners should be aware of, such as oil leaks, engine misfiring, transmission fluid leaks, and suspension problems. These issues are generally not severe and can be resolved with repairs at an estimated cost of a few hundred to a few thousand dollars.
