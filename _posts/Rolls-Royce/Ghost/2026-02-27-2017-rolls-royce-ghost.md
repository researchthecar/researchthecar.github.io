# 2017 Rolls-Royce Ghost Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Rolls-Royce Ghost represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering effortless performance and hand-crafted opulence. When shopping for a used model in this exclusive tier, buyers should anticipate exceptional engineering paired with uniquely complex luxury systems. While formal owner-reported complaint data is sparse for this specific model year, maintenance and pre-purchase inspections remain paramount given the high cost of specialized service and bespoke parts.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 575 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and silent acceleration. This ultra-refined powerplant is generally robust, though proper maintenance and timely fluid changes are critical for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission features satellite-aided gear shifting, which utilizes GPS data to anticipate terrain and pre-select the ideal gear. It offers seamless power delivery suited to the car's luxurious character and generally operates without common mechanical faults.
