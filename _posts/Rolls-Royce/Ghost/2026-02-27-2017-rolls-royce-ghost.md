# 2017 Rolls-Royce Ghost

### Overview
The 2017 Rolls-Royce Ghost, a pinnacle of luxury and engineering, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium build quality and meticulous manufacturing. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Ghost's complexity means that when problems do arise, they can be significant and costly to repair. Owners typically report a high degree of satisfaction with the vehicle's performance and durability. However, as with any high-end automobile, proactive maintenance and addressing minor concerns promptly are crucial to preserving its long-term reliability and value. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, highlighting areas that may require attention.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or full system reset. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000


### Door Control Modules
**Phantom Door Openings**
- **Symptoms:** Doors unlocking or opening on their own, dashboard warnings indicating an open door when it is closed.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of faulty door control modules or associated wiring.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000 per door


## Exterior

### Coach Doors (Suicide Doors)
**Hinge Mechanism Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Stiff operation, difficulty in opening or closing, unusual noises from hinges.
- **Solution:** Lubrication, adjustment, or replacement of worn hinge components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### LED Lighting
**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture or fogging inside headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Inspection and sealing of headlight units, or replacement if severe damage occurs.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


## Interior

### Starlight Headliner
**Fiber Optic Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Individual 'stars' or sections of the headliner failing to illuminate.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the affected fiber optic strands or associated wiring.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000


### Leather Upholstery
**Excessive Creasing/Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Premature creasing or wear on high-contact areas of the leather.
- **Solution:** Professional cleaning, conditioning, and potential repair or replacement of leather panels.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Rolls-Royce Ghost demonstrates a high level of overall reliability, characteristic of its luxury segment. The powertrain (6.6L V12 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission) is exceptionally robust with no widespread issues reported. Areas that have seen some documented concerns include the adaptive air suspension, where leaks or compressor failures can occur, and certain electrical components like the infotainment system and door modules, which may experience software glitches or module malfunctions. The intricate interior features, such as the Starlight Headliner, can also be a source of repair needs. While these issues are not universal, they represent potential costs for owners. Regular servicing and prompt attention to any warning signs are paramount for maintaining the Ghost's legendary refinement and dependability.
