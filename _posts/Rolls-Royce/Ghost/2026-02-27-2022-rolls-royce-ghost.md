# 2022 Rolls-Royce Ghost Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a pre-owned 2022 Rolls-Royce Ghost means entering an ultra-luxury tier where mechanical refinement is exceptionally high. Because this model year sees very few widespread owner complaints logged with NHTSA, it generally presents a clean initial outlook for prospective buyers. However, given the extreme complexity of its hand-built electronics, air suspension, and twin-turbocharged V12 powerplant, thorough independent pre-purchase inspections remain an absolute necessity before finalizing any transaction.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces substantial horsepower and effortless low-end torque. It delivers exceptionally smooth and quiet power delivery suited to the vehicle's ultra-luxury character.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission utilizes GPS data to anticipate gear shifts based on the road ahead. It offers seamless power delivery to the rear wheels with minimal shift shock.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V645000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Ghost&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The single-layer tempered glass protecting the instrument cluster may break in a crash. Broken glass can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective coating on the instrument cluster's single-layer tempered glass, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a peerless ultra-luxury sedan defined by supreme engineering and craftsmanship. With virtually no recurring complaint trends reported for this model year, it stands as a solid candidate for buyers in the secondhand market. Always verify maintenance history and secure a specialized PPI to ensure total peace of mind.
