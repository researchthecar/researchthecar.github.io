The 2013 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a pinnacle of luxury, offering a smooth ride, opulent interior, and commanding presence. The 2013 Ghost is equipped with a 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 575 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the car's effortless power, luxurious interior appointments, and prestige, but may find issues related to the air suspension system, complex electronics, and high maintenance costs to be potential drawbacks.

**Engine**

**6.6L Twin-Turbo V12**

*   **Oil Leaks from Valve Cover Gaskets**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover gaskets, leading to oil drips on the engine and a burning smell.
    *   Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from various hoses and connections, leading to overheating and low coolant levels.
    *   Solution: Replace leaking hoses and connections.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Rough Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or jerky shifting between gears, particularly at lower speeds. May also experience delayed engagement.
    *   Solution: Perform a transmission fluid flush and potentially replace the valve body.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $2,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor fails, leading to a loss of ride height, a sagging vehicle, and a "Suspension Fault" warning light. The car may ride harshly.
    *   Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Air Strut Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Air leaks from the air struts, causing the vehicle to sit unevenly or lower than normal. A hissing sound may be audible.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaking air strut(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+ per strut
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, reboot unexpectedly, or experience glitches with the display screen or navigation.
    *   Solution: Software updates may resolve the issue, but sometimes the entire infotainment unit needs to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, especially if the car is not driven regularly. This can lead to difficulty starting the car.
    *   Solution: Check for parasitic draws, replace the battery, or inspect the alternator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Parking sensors, Blind Spot Monitoring (BSM), or Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) system malfunctions, displaying error messages or failing to operate correctly.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty sensors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+ per sensor
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may become stuck, fail to open or close properly, or leak.
    *   Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof motor or mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Rolls-Royce Ghost offers unparalleled luxury, but potential buyers should be aware of common issues that can arise. The most frequent problems revolve around the air suspension system, electrical glitches, and engine oil leaks. While some issues can be resolved with relatively inexpensive repairs, others, such as air suspension compressor failure or infotainment system replacement, can be costly. Pre-purchase inspections by qualified Rolls-Royce technicians are highly recommended to assess the overall condition of the vehicle and identify any potential issues.
