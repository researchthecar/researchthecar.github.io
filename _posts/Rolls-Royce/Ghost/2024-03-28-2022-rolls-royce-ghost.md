**Introduction**

The 2022 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a flagship luxury sedan known for its opulent design, refined craftsmanship, and advanced technology. It is powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, delivering 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost is praised for its smooth ride, responsive handling, and exquisite interior, but it also faces some reported issues.
