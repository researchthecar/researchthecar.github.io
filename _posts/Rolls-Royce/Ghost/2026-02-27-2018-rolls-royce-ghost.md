# 2018 Rolls-Royce Ghost Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a pre-owned ultra-luxury sedan like the 2018 Rolls-Royce Ghost means stepping into a world of peerless craftsmanship and formidable engineering. Built around a robust twin-turbo V12 powerplant and a smooth automatic transmission, this flagship offers an exceptionally refined driving experience. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, prospective buyers should always perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection given the specialized nature and complex systems of hand-built motor cars.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces roughly 563 horsepower and 575 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and silent acceleration. It is generally robust, though maintenance and servicing demands are exceptionally high given its intricate packaging.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed ZF automatic transmission featuring satellite-aided gear shifting, this drivetrain anticipates terrain changes for seamless power delivery. It is known for smooth operation and long-term mechanical dependability under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V097000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Ghost&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The head air bags in these vehicles may have been damaged during vehicle assembly, possibly causing the head air bags to not fully inflate upon deployment. If the head air bag does not inflate properly in a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Rolls-Royce dealers will inspect and replace the head air bags, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Rolls-Royce Ghost remains an engineering marvel that prioritizes unmatched comfort, status, and effortless V12 performance. Because official owner complaints for this model year are virtually non-existent, it presents as a mechanically sound proposition for its class. However, prospective buyers must budget carefully for specialized maintenance, insurance, and comprehensive inspections by factory-trained technicians to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
