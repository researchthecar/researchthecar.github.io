**Introduction:**

The 2017 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan known for its opulent interior, refined performance, and cutting-edge technology. It's powered by a 6.6-liter V12 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Despite its premium status, the Ghost has experienced some reliability issues in recent years.

**Engine**

**6.6L V12**

* **Timing chain tensioner failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn tensioner causes a rattling noise and reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake valve carbon buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Deposits form on intake valves, leading to engine misfires and reduced power.
    * Solution: Remove intake valves and clean carbon buildup.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting hesitation or rough shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or shifts roughly, especially at low speeds.
    * Solution: Update transmission control module software or replace valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission fluid leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leak from transmission pan or seals, resulting in low fluid levels.
    * Solution: Replace leaking components and refill transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension compressor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Compressor fails to maintain proper ride height, leading to uneven suspension or a sagging vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace air suspension compressor and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Control arm bushing wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn bushings cause noise and vibration over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Identify and repair faulty electrical components or replace battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment system glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random glitches or freezing of the infotainment system.
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks fail to operate or become stuck.
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per door actuator
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Wood trim cracking or peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Interior wood trim may crack or peel over time.
    * Solution: Replace or refinish affected wood trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof makes a rattling noise while driving.
    * Solution: Adjust sunroof alignment or replace sunroof seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2017 Rolls-Royce Ghost offers exceptional luxury and performance, it has experienced some reliability concerns in certain areas. The engine and transmission may have issues with timing chain tensioners and shifting hesitation. The suspension and electrical systems have reported problems with air suspension compressors and battery drain. Some interior and exterior components, such as door lock actuators and wood trim, may also require attention. The severity of these issues and their associated repair costs can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider regular maintenance and repairs to keep their Ghost in optimal condition.
