The 2021 Rolls-Royce Ghost represents the pinnacle of luxury sedans, boasting a smooth ride, opulent interior, and undeniable presence. It is equipped with a twin-turbocharged 6.75-liter V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Some owners praise the car's effortless power, serene cabin, and customizable features. Common dislikes often revolve around the large size making it difficult to maneuver in tight spaces and the high price of entry and maintenance.

**Engine**

**6.75L Twin-Turbo V12**
*   **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, check engine light illumination.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil spots under the car, burning oil smell, low oil level.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the valve cover gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Software Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifting, transmission getting stuck in a gear.
    *   Solution: Software update or replacement of affected TCU parts if damage is present.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+ depending on severity.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sitting unevenly, ride height adjustments not functioning, error messages on the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Air Strut Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Hissing sound from suspension, vehicle sinking overnight, poor ride quality.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaking air strut(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+ per strut
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: System unresponsive, screen freezing, loss of functionality.
    *   Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment system.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+ depending on severity.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Parking Sensors, Blind Spot Monitoring)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: False alerts, system not functioning properly, error messages.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty sensor(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per sensor
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Automatic Door Closing Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Doors failing to close automatically, requiring manual closing.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the door closing mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+ per door
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof failing to open or close properly, getting stuck.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the sunroof motor or mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Rolls-Royce Ghost, while an exceptional vehicle, can be susceptible to a range of issues, mainly relating to the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. Repairs can be costly, given the specialized components and labor involved. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and consider a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified Rolls-Royce technician.
