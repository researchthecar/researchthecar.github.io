**Introduction**

The 2013 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxurious sedan known for its opulence and performance. It is powered by a 6.6L V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 575 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and offers a smooth and comfortable ride. It features a spacious interior with high-quality materials and advanced technology. Owners appreciate the Ghost's elegant design, refined handling, and impressive power, while some complain about its high price and fuel consumption.

**Engine**

**6.6L V12**

**Fuel Pump Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine stalls or hesitates during acceleration, difficulty starting the vehicle.
* **Solution:** Replace fuel pump.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Rough shifting, loss of power during acceleration.
* **Solution:** Replace or repair transmission.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension Leak:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Uneven ride height, excessive bouncing or sagging.
* **Solution:** Repair or replace air suspension components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery frequently needs charging or dies unexpectedly.
* **Solution:** Replace battery or diagnose and repair electrical system.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Malfunction:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Door handle fails to open or close properly.
* **Solution:** Replace door handle assembly.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Climate control system not functioning properly, inconsistent temperatures.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair or replace climate control components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Infotainment System Malfunction:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, audio or navigation system not working.
* **Solution:** Update software or replace infotainment system.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Rolls-Royce Ghost generally receives positive reviews for its performance and luxury. However, the vehicle is prone to some issues, such as fuel pump failure, transmission slipping, and air suspension leaks. The cost to repair these issues can be significant, ranging from several hundred to several thousand dollars. While the Ghost offers an exceptional driving experience, buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor the associated costs into their decision.
