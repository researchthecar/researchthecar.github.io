**2023 Rolls-Royce Ghost**

The 2023 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxurious sedan powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost is available with a single 8-speed automatic transmission.

The Ghost has been praised for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported experiencing issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

**Valve Stem Seal Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
* Solution: Replace valve stem seals
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Solenoid Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: Delayed or hard shifting, slipping gears
* Solution: Replace shift solenoid
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Air Suspension**
* **Air Spring Leak**
* Description/Symptoms: Sagging front suspension, uneven ride height
* Solution: Replace air spring
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
* Recalls: None Found

**Rear Air Suspension**
* **Air Compressor Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Inoperative air suspension, rear of the vehicle sits low
* Solution: Replace air compressor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Discharge**
* Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains prematurely
* Solution: Replace battery or check for parasitic drain
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Corrosion**
* Description/Symptoms: Door handles become discolored or corroded
* Solution: Replace door handles
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Trim Panel Cracking**
* Description/Symptoms: Dashboard trim panel cracks or becomes loose
* Solution: Replace dashboard trim panel
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxurious sedan with a comfortable ride and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported experiencing issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from $500 to $3,000 or more. While some of these issues may be relatively minor, others could lead to more expensive repairs.
