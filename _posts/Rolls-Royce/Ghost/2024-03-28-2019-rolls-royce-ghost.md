**Introduction**

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a full-size luxury sedan powered by a 6.6-liter V12 engine producing 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It offers a combination of opulence, performance, and refinement. Despite its high price point, the Ghost is praised for its plush interior, smooth driving experience, and attention to detail. However, it does have some reported issues.

**Engine**

**6.6L V12**

- **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported unusually high oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
- **Fuel Injector Sensor Failure:** Fuel injector sensors may malfunction, causing improper fuel delivery and engine performance issues.
- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner may fail, leading to a rattling noise in the engine and a possible jump in timing.
- **Engine Control Module (ECM) Failure:** The ECM may experience malfunctions, resulting in engine performance issues, misfires, and in some cases, complete engine failure.

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

- **Hesitation or Delay in Shifting:** Occasionally, the transmission may exhibit hesitation or delay when shifting gears, particularly between low gears.

**Suspension**

- **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:** The air suspension compressor may fail, affecting the ride height and handling of the vehicle.
- **Strut Mount Wear:** The strut mounts may wear out prematurely, causing knocking or clunking noises during suspension movement.

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:** Some vehicles have experienced premature battery drain, resulting in frequent jump-starts or battery replacements.
- **Infotainment System Malfunction:** The infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, such as freezing or unresponsive screens.

**Exterior**

**Headlight Moisture Condensation:** Headlights may accumulate condensation inside the lens, affecting visibility at night.

**Interior**

- **Rearview Camera Failure:** The rearview camera may malfunction, displaying a black screen or distorted images.
- **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system may not function properly, resulting in inconsistent cooling or heating.
- **Sunroof Motor Failure:** The sunroof motor may fail, causing the sunroof to become stuck open or closed.

**Summary**

While the 2019 Rolls-Royce Ghost offers a luxurious and refined driving experience, it is not immune to mechanical issues. The reported problems range in severity and cost, from minor inconveniences to potentially expensive repairs. Potential buyers should consider these potential issues when making a purchase decision.
