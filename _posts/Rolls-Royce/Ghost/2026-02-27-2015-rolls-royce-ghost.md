# 2015 Rolls-Royce Ghost

### Overview
The 2015 Rolls-Royce Ghost, a pinnacle of luxury and engineering, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium build quality and meticulous manufacturing. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Ghost tends to avoid widespread, common problems. Owners typically report satisfaction with its performance and durability. However, as with any high-end automobile, potential repairs can be complex and costly, emphasizing the importance of diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any anomalies. This data focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000


### Door Handle Egress System
**Malfunctioning Egress System**
- **Symptoms:** Door handles not presenting or retracting correctly, difficulty opening doors.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the electric motor and mechanism within the door.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200 per door

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Rolls-Royce Ghost demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with its luxury segment positioning. The powertrain (engine and transmission) is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the adaptive air suspension, specifically potential air spring leaks, and certain electrical components like the infotainment system and the unique door handle egress mechanism, which can experience software glitches or mechanical faults. Interior and exterior components are generally free from major defects. While repair costs can be substantial due to the vehicle's complexity and premium nature, the overall reliability is commendable, suggesting that with proper care, the Ghost remains a dependable luxury vehicle.
