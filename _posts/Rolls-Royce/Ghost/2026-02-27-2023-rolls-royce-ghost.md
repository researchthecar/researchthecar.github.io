# 2023 Rolls-Royce Ghost Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Rolls-Royce Ghost represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering effortless performance and sublime craftsmanship. As a low-volume exotic vehicle, comprehensive owner-reported complaint data is exceptionally scarce, meaning used-car buyers must rely heavily on traditional pre-purchase inspections and meticulous service records rather than widespread trend analysis.

---

## Engine

### 6.75-Liter Twin-Turbocharged V12

This 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and silent acceleration. It is an intricate, hand-built powerplant requiring specialized maintenance, though widespread mechanical reliability concerns remain minimal in early owner reports.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission utilizes GPS data to pre-select gears for upcoming corners, ensuring seamless power delivery to the all-wheel-drive system. It is renowned for its refinement and durability, with no notable recurring issues reported by owners.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V645000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Ghost&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The single-layer tempered glass protecting the instrument cluster may break in a crash. Broken glass can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective coating on the instrument cluster's single-layer tempered glass, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

Purchasing a pre-owned 2023 Rolls-Royce Ghost is an exclusive undertaking that demands rigorous scrutiny of maintenance history and a professional pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist. While verified owner complaint data is virtually nonexistent for this model year, prospective buyers should always budget for the exceptionally high costs associated with ultra-luxury vehicle ownership.
