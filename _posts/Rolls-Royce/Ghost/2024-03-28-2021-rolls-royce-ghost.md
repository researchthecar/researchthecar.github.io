**Introduction:**
The 2021 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience with ample power and opulent features. It is powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbo V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and offers a smooth ride with precise handling. Despite its luxurious design, the Ghost has received some reports of specific issues affecting its various components.

**Engine**
* **6.75-liter twin-turbo V12**

* **Engine Misfire**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may experience erratic behavior, rough idling, and power loss.
    * Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils or spark plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil may seep from the engine gaskets or seals, causing visible leaks or burning smells.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the affected gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **8-speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears, or it may fail to engage gears properly.
    * Solution: Reconfigure the transmission control module or replace faulty sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, display errors, or fail to respond to inputs.
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**
* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks may malfunction, preventing the doors from locking or unlocking properly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Wind Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise may enter the cabin, especially at higher speeds.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace door seals as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Interior**
* **Trims Squeaks and Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some interior trims may develop squeaks or rattles over time.
    * Solution: Identify and tighten or replace loose fittings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in inadequate heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty sensors, actuators, or other components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**Summary:**
The 2021 Rolls-Royce Ghost offers a luxurious driving experience, but it has been reported to have a few specific issues affecting its engine, transmission, electrical system, exterior, and interior. While some issues may be minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider their impact before purchasing or owning this vehicle.
