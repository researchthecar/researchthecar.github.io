## 2015 Rolls-Royce Ghost

The 2015 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan known for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine. It is powered by a 6.6L V12 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 575 lb-ft of torque. The Ghost is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the Ghost appreciate its luxurious features, such as its spacious cabin, high-quality materials, and advanced technology. They also enjoy its smooth and quiet ride. However, some people dislike the Ghost's high price tag and its lack of agility compared to smaller sedans.

### Engine

- **6.6L V12**
  - **Oil Leaks From Valve Cover Gaskets:** Leaks can develop around the valve cover gaskets, causing oil to drip onto the engine. The gaskets need to be replaced to fix the leaks. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+**
  - **Water Pump Failure:** The water pump can fail prematurely, leading to overheating and potential engine damage. The water pump needs to be replaced to resolve the issue. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+**

### Transmission

- **8-Speed Automatic**
  - **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Leaks can occur from the transmission fluid lines, pan gasket, or torque converter seal. The leaking fluid needs to be replaced, and the faulty component repaired or replaced. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+**
  - **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears. This could be caused by a faulty transmission control module, solenoid, or clutch packs. The affected component(s) need to be repaired or replaced. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+**

### Suspension

- **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:** The air suspension compressor can fail, resulting in a loss of ride height and a rough ride. The compressor needs to be replaced to restore proper suspension function. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+**
- **Air Suspension Leak:** Leaks can develop in the air suspension lines or struts, causing the vehicle to sag on one or more corners. The leak needs to be located and repaired or replaced. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+**

### Electrical

- **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start. This could be caused by a faulty alternator, parasitic draw, or weak battery. The affected component(s) need to be diagnosed and repaired or replaced. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+**
- **Electrical System Malfunctions:** Various electrical system components can malfunction, such as the audio system, climate control, or lighting. The faulty component(s) need to be diagnosed and repaired or replaced. **Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component**

### Exterior

- **Door Lock Actuator Failure:** The door lock actuators can fail, preventing the doors from locking or unlocking. The faulty actuator(s) need to be replaced to restore proper door operation. **Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+**

### Interior

- **Infotainment System Bugs:** The infotainment system can experience bugs or glitches, such as freezing or crashing. Software updates or component replacements may be necessary to resolve the issues. **Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue**

### Summary

Overall, the 2015 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a well-built and luxurious sedan. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the most common problems reported by owners include oil leaks, water pump failures, transmission issues, and suspension problems. These issues can be costly to repair, especially if they require major component replacements. It is important to factor in the potential cost of ownership when considering a used 2015 Rolls-Royce Ghost.
