**Introduction:**
The 2012 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxury sedan with an elegant design and refined performance. It features a powerful 6.6L V12 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Ghost offers a smooth and luxurious ride, with ample interior space and advanced technology features. While it is highly regarded for its opulence and performance, there have been some reported issues associated with the vehicle.

**Engine**
* **6.6L V12**
    * **Oil Leaks:** Leaks may occur from the valve covers, oil pan, or other gaskets. Symptoms include a burning oil smell, reduced oil levels, and engine performance issues. Fix: Replace the faulty gaskets or seals. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Timing Chain Noise:** The timing chain may stretch or become loose over time, causing a rattling or knocking sound during engine operation. Fix: Replace the timing chain and components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Fuel injectors can fail, causing rough engine idle, poor fuel efficiency, or difficulty starting. Fix: Replace the faulty fuel injector(s). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**
* **8-speed Automatic**
    * **Slipping Gears:** The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears, causing a loss of power or rough shifting. Fix: Recondition or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
    * **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Leaks may occur from the transmission pan, seals, or lines. Symptoms include low transmission fluid levels, slipping gears, or delayed shifting. Fix: Replace the faulty seals or gaskets. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Suspension**
* **Air Suspension Failure:** The air suspension system may fail due to a leak in the air compressor or lines, or a faulty air suspension strut. Symptoms include a sagging ride height or uneven tire wear. Fix: Replace the faulty component(s). Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** Vehicles may experience excessive battery drain due to a faulty electrical component or parasitic draw. Symptoms include a dead battery or difficulty starting the vehicle. Fix: Diagnose and replace the faulty component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Electrical Wiring Harness Issues:** Electrical wiring harness issues can cause various electrical problems, such as malfunctioning lights, gauges, or accessories. Fix: Repair or replace the faulty wiring harness. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**
* **Chrome Trim Corrosion:** The chrome trim on the exterior of the vehicle may corrode or discolor over time. Fix: Replace or refinish the affected trim. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**
* **Satellite Navigation Malfunction:** The satellite navigation system may malfunction or lose connectivity due to a faulty antenna or software issue. Fix: Replace the antenna or update the software. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing issues such as insufficient cooling or heating. Fix: Diagnose and repair the faulty component(s). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Rear Entertainment System Malfunction:** The rear entertainment system may not function properly or experience connection issues. Fix: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty component(s). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary:**
While the 2012 Rolls-Royce Ghost is a luxurious and impressive vehicle, it has experienced several issues reported by owners. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor and others more significant. The cost to repair can be substantial, especially for major issues such as engine or transmission problems. It is important for prospective buyers to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a Ghost to ensure they can budget for any necessary repairs.
