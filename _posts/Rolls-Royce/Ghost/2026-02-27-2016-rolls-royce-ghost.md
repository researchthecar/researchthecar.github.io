# 2016 Rolls-Royce Ghost

### Overview
The 2016 Rolls-Royce Ghost represents a pinnacle of automotive luxury and engineering, aiming for exceptional reliability alongside its opulent features. While generally well-regarded for its robust build and sophisticated systems, like any complex vehicle, certain areas may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear. The Ghost is equipped with a powerful V12 engine and an advanced automatic transmission, both designed for smooth, effortless performance. Its advanced suspension and intricate electrical and interior systems contribute to the overall driving experience, but also present potential areas for technical scrutiny.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800


### Door Handle Actuators
**Malfunctioning Actuators**
- **Symptoms:** Power door handles not extending or retracting properly, doors not opening electronically.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty door handle actuator module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700 per door

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Rolls-Royce Ghost generally exhibits strong reliability, befitting its luxury status. The core powertrain, a 6.6L twin-turbo V12 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. Potential concerns tend to arise in more complex systems. The adaptive air suspension, while providing a sublime ride, can be susceptible to air spring leaks or compressor failures over time, with repair costs reflecting the specialized nature of the components. Electrical gremlins, particularly within the infotainment system and door handle actuators, are occasionally noted, often requiring software updates or component replacement. Interior climate control actuators can also present minor issues. Overall, the Ghost is a reliable vehicle, but owners should be prepared for potentially high repair costs associated with its advanced technology and luxury appointments.
