**Introduction:**

The 1995 Rolls-Royce Silver Dawn was a luxurious sedan powered by a 6.75-liter V8 engine with 302 horsepower and 415 lb-ft of torque. It came standard with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Owners praised its smooth ride, opulent interior, and timeless design, though some found its performance lacking and its fuel economy disappointing.
