# 2024 Rolls-Royce Spectre Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Rolls-Royce Spectre marks the ultra-luxury marque's bold entry into the all-electric vehicle segment. Combining effortless electric propulsion with legendary British craftsmanship, it delivers a silent, ultra-smooth ride. As an early-production luxury EV, prospective buyers should focus heavily on verifying proper software function and high-voltage battery health during pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### Dual-Motor Electric Powertrain

Equipped with dual excitation synchronous electric motors producing approximately 577 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. This setup delivers effortless, nearly silent acceleration befitting a flagship ultra-luxury grand tourer with exceptional long-term reliability expectations.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic

Utilizes a specialized single-speed reduction gear transmission designed to seamlessly transfer high torque directly to the wheels. The simplified drivetrain layout eliminates traditional gear-shifting friction, offering supreme refinement and low maintenance requirements.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V039000: Electrical System:Propulsion System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Spectre&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The ground connection cable between the front electric motor and the vehicle chassis may contain adhesive residue which can increase electrical resistance and cause an insufficient ground. An insufficient electrical ground can increase the risk of electric shock during service work, and also increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ground cable connection and remove any residue as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V104000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Spectre&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V697000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Spectre&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** In addition, the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Dynamic Stability Control (DSC) system may not function. Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V422000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Electric:Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Rolls-Royce&model=Spectre&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Rolls-Royce Spectre offers a serene, exceptionally powerful driving experience backed by advanced electric architecture. Because early-model luxury EVs rely heavily on complex software systems, buyers should ensure all dealer updates have been applied and comprehensive pre-purchase diagnostics are performed.
