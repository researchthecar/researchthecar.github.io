# 2017 Rolls-Royce Wraith

### Overview
The 2017 Rolls-Royce Wraith represents the pinnacle of automotive luxury and engineering, and as such, its reliability is generally very high. Built with meticulous attention to detail and using premium materials, the Wraith is designed for longevity and a refined ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Wraith's complex systems and robust construction contribute to a strong reliability record. Owners typically report satisfaction with its performance and build quality, with most concerns revolving around the sophisticated technology integrated into the vehicle rather than fundamental mechanical flaws. Regular maintenance by qualified technicians is crucial to preserving its exceptional condition.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Rolls-Royce Wraith demonstrates excellent reliability, consistent with its luxury positioning. The core mechanical components, including the powerful V12 engine and smooth 8-speed automatic transmission, are exceptionally robust. The adaptive air suspension also performs reliably. The most common, albeit infrequent, issues tend to be related to the sophisticated electrical systems, such as minor infotainment glitches or occasional hiccups with the power-operated doors. These are generally resolved with software updates or minor adjustments. Overall, the Wraith is a well-built vehicle with a strong track record for dependability, provided it receives diligent maintenance from specialized technicians.
