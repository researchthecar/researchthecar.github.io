**Introduction to the 2019 Rolls-Royce Wraith**

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Wraith is a luxurious grand tourer that combines opulent style with impressive performance. It is powered by a 6.6-liter twin-turbo V12 engine that produces 624 horsepower and 605 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Wraith offers a refined and comfortable driving experience, and its spacious cabin is adorned with premium materials.

    * Issue: Premature battery failure
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle may experience electrical problems or difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness**

**Summary**

The 2019 Rolls-Royce Wraith is a high-quality vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners are related to the suspension, electrical system, and exterior components. While these issues can be costly to repair, they do not appear to be widespread. Overall, the Wraith remains a highly desirable luxury vehicle for those who value refinement, performance, and exclusivity.
