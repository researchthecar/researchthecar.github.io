# 2018 Rolls-Royce Wraith

### Overview
The 2018 Rolls-Royce Wraith represents the pinnacle of automotive luxury and engineering, and as such, its reliability is generally very high. Built with meticulous attention to detail and premium materials, the Wraith is designed for longevity and a refined ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the Wraith's complex systems and robust construction contribute to a strong reliability record. Owners typically report satisfaction with the vehicle's performance and build quality, with most concerns revolving around the sophisticated technology integrated into the cabin and the powerful drivetrain. Regular maintenance by qualified technicians is crucial for preserving its exceptional performance and preventing minor issues from escalating.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


### Power Closing Doors/Trunk
**Malfunctioning Actuators**
- **Symptoms:** Doors or trunk not closing or opening automatically, unusual noises during operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the door or trunk actuator motor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800

**Compressor Failure**

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Rolls-Royce Wraith demonstrates excellent reliability, characteristic of ultra-luxury vehicles. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are the sophisticated electrical systems, particularly the infotainment, and the complex air suspension. Issues with power-operated doors and climate control components, while not common, can occur. The core powertrain, the 6.6L V12 engine and its associated transmission, are exceptionally robust and rarely present problems. Owners should prioritize regular, specialized maintenance to ensure the continued flawless operation of this magnificent automobile. Overall, the Wraith offers a highly dependable ownership experience for those seeking unparalleled luxury and performance.
