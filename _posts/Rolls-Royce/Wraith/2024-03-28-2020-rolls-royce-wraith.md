**Introduction:**
The 2020 Rolls-Royce Wraith is a handcrafted, ultra-luxury coupe that exudes power and opulence. It features a potent 6.6-liter, twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 624 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. The Wraith offers a seamless driving experience with an eight-speed automatic transmission. Despite its allure, the Wraith has encountered some specific issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**
- **6.6-liter V12 Engine**
  - **Oil Leak:** Description/Symptoms: Occurrence of oil leaking from the engine gasket. Solution: Replace the affected gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - **Intake Valve Deposits:** Description/Symptoms: Buildup of deposits on intake valves, causing reduced engine performance and fuel efficiency. Solution: Clean or replace affected valves. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
- **Transmission**
  - **Automatic Transmission:**
    - **Shifting Issues:** Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or transmission slipping. Solution: Replace or repair affected transmission components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    - **Overheating:** Description/Symptoms: Transmission overheating during operation. Solution: Repair or replace transmission cooler or related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Suspension**
- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
- **Battery Drain:** Description/Symptoms: Premature battery discharge, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace affected electrical components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**
- **Door Lock Malfunction:** Description/Symptoms: Inability to lock or unlock doors using remote or manual controls. Solution: Replace or repair door lock actuators. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**
- **Infotainment System Freezing:** Description/Symptoms: Intermittent freezing or malfunctioning of the infotainment system. Solution: Update or replace the affected software or components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
- **Trim Panel Squeaks:** Description/Symptoms: Noises or vibrations originating from interior trim panels. Solution: Adjust or replace affected trim panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary:**
While the Rolls-Royce Wraith is a remarkable vehicle, it is not immune to issues. Owners should be aware of potential engine oil leaks, intake valve deposits, transmission shifting problems, battery drain, door lock malfunctions, infotainment system freezing, and interior trim panel squeaks. These issues can vary in severity and cost to fix, but it is crucial to have them addressed promptly to maintain the vehicle's performance and longevity.
