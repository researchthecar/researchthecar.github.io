# 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith stands as an ultra-luxury grand tourer combining effortless V12 performance with handcrafted opulence. Prospective buyers investigating this tier of hand-built engineering should carefully review available owner feedback regarding visibility and electronic accessories, keeping in mind that routine maintenance and specialized repairs demand a substantial financial commitment.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

This twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 624 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and effortless acceleration. It is generally robust when maintained according to strict manufacturer schedules, though complex plumbing and high-end components require meticulous upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a satellite-aided automatic transmission that anticipates shifts using GPS data, this drivetrain offers seamless gear changes suited to a luxury grand tourer. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions if fluid services are kept up to date.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera System

Exterior lighting and camera systems incorporate advanced technology that enhances daily usability, though occasional electronic glitches have been reported by owners.

**Intermittent Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The backup camera display fails to activate when shifting into reverse, limiting rear visibility.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the camera wiring harness, software modules, and display interface to identify faulty connections or failing components.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith offers unmatched luxury, striking presence, and formidable performance, but prospective buyers must be prepared for the high costs associated with maintaining an ultra-luxury vehicle. While major mechanical complaints are sparse, checking all electronic features—such as driver-assistance cameras—during a pre-purchase inspection is highly recommended.
