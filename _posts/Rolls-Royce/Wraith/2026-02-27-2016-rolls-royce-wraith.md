# 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith

### Overview
The 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith represents the pinnacle of automotive luxury and engineering, aiming for exceptional reliability alongside its opulent features. While specific widespread mechanical failures are rare due to the bespoke nature of its construction and rigorous testing, like any complex vehicle, certain areas can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners of this exclusive grand tourer. The Wraith is built with a focus on durability and refinement, utilizing proven components from the BMW Group, which generally contributes to a high level of dependability.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800


### Door Handle Actuators
**Malfunctioning Actuators**
- **Symptoms:** Power door handles not extending or retracting properly, difficulty opening doors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected door handle actuator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700 per handle

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, consistent with its luxury segment positioning. The powertrain, comprising the 6.6L V12 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. Potential concerns are primarily concentrated in the sophisticated air suspension system, where leaks or compressor failures can occur, and in certain electrical components like infotainment glitches or door handle actuators. The interior, particularly the iconic Starlight Headliner, can also present minor issues with fiber optics. Overall, while not immune to the complexities of advanced automotive technology, the Wraith offers a dependable ownership experience when properly maintained, with most issues being repairable at a premium cost reflective of the vehicle's exclusivity.
