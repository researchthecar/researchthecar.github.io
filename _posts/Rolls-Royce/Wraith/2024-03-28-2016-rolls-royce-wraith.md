**Introduction:**

The 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith is a luxurious grand tourer powered by a 6.6L twin-turbocharged V12 engine. It offers a blend of power, elegance, and craftsmanship. However, like any vehicle, it is not immune to issues.

**Engine**

* **6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Premature turbocharger failure leading to loss of power, excessive exhaust smoke, and engine noise.
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: RR-2018-021
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Malfunction:** Faulty timing chain tensioner can cause rattling noises, engine misfires, and reduced performance.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **ZF 8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Delay or Hesitation:** Delayed or hesitant gear shifts, especially when shifting from low to higher gears.
        * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
        * Recalls: RR-2019-012

**Suspension**

* **Front Air Suspension Failure:** Malfunctioning air suspension system can lead to uneven ride height, excessive body roll, and discomfort.
        * Solution: Replace air suspension components or control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: RR-2016-015

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:** Intermittent or persistent issues with the infotainment system, including touchscreen unresponsive, system freezes, and audio problems.
        * Solution: Software update, firmware update, or replacement of infotainment components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: RR-2017-009

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies, causing poor visibility and reduced headlight effectiveness.
        * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Issues:** Loose or rattling center console trim pieces, creating an unsightly appearance and distracting noise.
        * Solution: Reinstall or replace trim pieces.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary:**

While the 2016 Rolls-Royce Wraith is an exceptional vehicle, it is not without its issues. The potential problems with the turbocharger, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior can be concerning, especially considering the vehicle's premium price tag. Some issues may be covered under warranty, but others can incur significant repair costs. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary, and interested buyers should carefully consider potential risks before purchasing.
