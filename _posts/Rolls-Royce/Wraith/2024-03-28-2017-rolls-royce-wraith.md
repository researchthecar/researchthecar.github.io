**Introduction:**

The 2017 Rolls-Royce Wraith is a luxurious grand tourer powered by a 6.6L twin-turbocharged V12 engine that produces 624 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Wraith is known for its opulent interior, elegant exterior, and impressive performance. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Deposits build up on the intake valves, causing reduced engine performance, rough idling, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shift Hesitation:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates to shift gears, resulting in delayed acceleration or jerky shifts.
        * Solution: Software update or transmission rebuild.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor fails, causing the vehicle to lose height and become difficult to drive.
    * Solution: Replacing the air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system experiences glitches, freezes, or displays errors.
    * Solution: Software updates or component replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Trim Panel Detachment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door trim panel may detach from the door, resulting in rattling or loose trim.
    * Solution: Reattaching or replacing the door trim panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system malfunctions, resulting in inconsistent temperature control or inadequate airflow.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing faulty components, such as sensors or actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2017 Rolls-Royce Wraith is a remarkable vehicle, it is not immune to issues. The most common problems reported by owners include intake valve deposits, transmission shift hesitations, air suspension compressor failure, infotainment system malfunctions, door trim panel detachment, and climate control system failures. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they should be considered before purchasing or servicing this vehicle.
