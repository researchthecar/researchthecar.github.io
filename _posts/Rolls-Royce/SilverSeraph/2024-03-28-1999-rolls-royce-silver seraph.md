**Introduction**

The 1999 Rolls-Royce Silver Seraph is a luxurious sedan that was powered by a BMW-sourced 5.4-liter V12 engine. Paired with the V12 engine was a 5-speed automatic transmission. The Silver Seraph was praised for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine. However, it was also criticized for its high price and lack of modern features.

**Engine**

**5.4-liter V12**

* **Intake camshaft position sensor failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls or hesitates during acceleration. Check Engine Light illuminates.
    * Solution: Replace the intake camshaft position sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Oil leaks from valve cover gaskets**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve covers, causing a burning smell and reduced oil levels.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* **Coolant leaks from water pump**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the water pump, causing overheating and reduced cooling system efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace the water pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+

**Transmission**

**5-speed automatic**

* **Transmission slipping or shuddering**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission does not engage properly, causing slipping or shuddering during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension compressor failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air suspension does not inflate or deflate properly, causing the vehicle to ride low or unevenly.
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Inspect for parasitic draw, replace battery if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin from the sunroof area.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Wood trim cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wood trim on the dashboard, center console, or doors cracks or splits.
    * Solution: Replace the wood trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 1999 Rolls-Royce Silver Seraph is a luxurious and powerful sedan, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems include engine oil leaks, transmission issues, suspension failures, and electrical problems. The cost of repairs can be significant, but the vehicle's overall reliability is still considered to be good for a luxury car of its age.
