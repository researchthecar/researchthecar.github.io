# 2021 McLaren 600LT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 McLaren 600LT is a high-performance track-focused supercar engineered to deliver blistering speed and precision handling. Because low-volume exotic vehicles experience unique ownership conditions and complex engineering, buyers should carefully evaluate maintenance history and professional inspections before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 592 horsepower and 457 lb-ft of torque. It provides explosive acceleration and power delivery, though proper maintenance and cooling management are vital for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddles. Routine fluid changes and software health checks are recommended to maintain shift quality.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V216000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=600LT&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A rear wheel locking condition may cause the driver to lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the brake line connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 McLaren 600LT remains an exhilarating exotic choice for driving enthusiasts. Prospective buyers are strongly advised to secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an authorized specialist to ensure optimal mechanical health.
