# 2019 McLaren 600LT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 McLaren 600LT is a track-focused supercar delivering blistering performance through its carbon-fiber tub and twin-turbocharged V8 engine. For prospective buyers exploring the used exotic market, understanding its powertrain setup and maintaining realistic expectations regarding maintenance and owner-reported nuances are essential before taking the plunge.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 592 horsepower and 457 lb-ft of torque, offering immense power delivery. Overall, this hand-built powerplant is robust when properly maintained, though high-performance exotic ownership demands rigorous adherence to service intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes suited for both aggressive track driving and spirited road use. It operates reliably under normal conditions, provided software calibrations and clutch wear parameters are periodically monitored.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V561000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=600LT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Brake failure in one corner can cause the vehicle to pull to one side during braking, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the banjo bolt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V484000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Radiator Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=600LT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The joint between the temperature sensor housing and the hose from the outboard radiator may not be sealed properly, which can result in coolant leaking into the engine compartment. A coolant leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as hot engine components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the coolant hose, temperature sensor housing, and constant pressure clamp, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2019 McLaren 600LT, prioritize vehicles with comprehensive service histories and documented professional inspections. While owner-reported complaints are sparse, exotic car ownership always carries the potential for specialized maintenance costs and unique mechanical quirks.
