# 2024 McLaren Artura Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 McLaren Artura represents an advanced high-performance hybrid supercar tier, blending a twin-turbocharged V6 with an electric motor. As a sophisticated exotic vehicle, prospective used-car buyers should approach ownership with an understanding of complex powertrain integration and specialized maintenance requirements. Reviewing owner feedback and structural configurations helps highlight what to expect when shopping for a pre-owned model.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6 Hybrid

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged 120-degree V6 engine pairs with an electric motor to produce a combined 690 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. It offers blistering performance and cutting-edge hybrid technology, though buyers should expect specialized servicing by certified technicians.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes and features an integrated electric reverse gear to save weight. It is engineered to handle high torque outputs while maintaining responsive everyday drivability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V796000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=Artura&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the low-pressure fuel pipe assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V216000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=Artura&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rear wheel locking condition may cause the driver to lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the brake line connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
