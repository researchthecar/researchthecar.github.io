# 2023 McLaren Artura Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 McLaren Artura represents an advanced high-performance hybrid supercar era for the brand, pairing a twin-turbo V6 with an electric motor. While early ownership experiences are generally thrilling, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate complex hybrid powertrain electronics and electrical systems. As a low-volume exotic vehicle, reviewing service history and ensuring remaining warranty coverage is strongly advised.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6 Hybrid

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine is paired with an electric axial-flux motor, producing a combined 671 horsepower and 531 lb-ft of torque. It delivers blistering performance but introduces complex high-voltage electronics that require specialized dealer maintenance.

**Hybrid Propulsion System Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden 'Hybrid System failure, call McLaren Service Center' warning message displayed on the instrument panel while driving at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic evaluation by an authorized McLaren service center to inspect hybrid components, clear error codes, or update software.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

An 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and features an electronic reverse gear to save weight. It is generally robust, though proper maintenance and software calibrations are essential for optimal drivability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V908000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=Artura&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as hot engine components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pipes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V796000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=Artura&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the low-pressure fuel pipe assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V216000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=Artura&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A rear wheel locking condition may cause the driver to lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the brake line connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 McLaren Artura is an engineering marvel that blends ferocious supercar performance with innovative hybrid technology. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with verified maintenance records, complete software updates, and active warranty protection to manage potential electronic or hybrid system complexities.
