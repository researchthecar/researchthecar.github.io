# 2021 McLaren 765LT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 McLaren 765LT is an extreme, track-focused supercar that delivers breathtaking performance alongside the specialized ownership experience typical of low-volume exotic vehicles. Prospective buyers should approach this high-performance machine with a clear understanding of specialized maintenance requirements and the complexities associated with modern carbon-fiber supercars.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces an estimated 755 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering explosive acceleration. This hand-built powerplant requires meticulous adherence to service intervals and high-octane fuel to maintain peak health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission features optimized gear ratios and lightning-fast shifts tailored for track use. It offers seamless power delivery under hard acceleration while requiring proper warm-up procedures to ensure long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V901000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=765LT&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A rear window that detaches from the vehicle can create a road hazard for other drivers, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install bespoke fasteners into each corner of the polycarbonate panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 McLaren 765LT is an engineering marvel designed for uncompromising track capability and exotic thrills. Buyers exploring the used market should prioritize thorough professional pre-purchase inspections and verify comprehensive service history to ensure trouble-free ownership.
