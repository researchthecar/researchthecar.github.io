# 2021 McLaren 765LT

### Overview
The 2021 McLaren 765LT is a track-focused supercar renowned for its extreme performance and lightweight construction. As a limited-production model, comprehensive long-term reliability data is less extensive than for mass-produced vehicles. However, based on available owner feedback and expert reviews, the 765LT generally exhibits a high level of engineering integrity, particularly concerning its powertrain. The focus on performance means that while robust, components are pushed to their limits, and maintenance is critical. Owners should expect specialized servicing due to the exotic nature of the vehicle. This analysis focuses on documented potential issues beyond standard maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 McLaren 765LT is a highly specialized supercar where reliability is generally strong, especially concerning its core powertrain components. The 4.0L twin-turbo V8 and the 7-speed dual-clutch transmission are robust and engineered for extreme performance. Most reported issues are minor and related to the infotainment system's software, which can typically be resolved with updates. The exotic materials and track-focused design mean that while major mechanical failures are rare, specialized maintenance and careful operation are paramount. Owners should budget for high-end servicing, but widespread systemic reliability concerns are not prevalent for this exclusive model.
