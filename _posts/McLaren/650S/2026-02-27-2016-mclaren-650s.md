# 2016 McLaren 650S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 McLaren 650S delivers breathtaking supercar performance paired with advanced engineering, making it an exhilarating option for used-car buyers looking for track-ready capability. As an exotic British supercar, it demands specialized maintenance and close attention to service history rather than traditional commuter-car expectations.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 641 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque, providing explosive acceleration. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though proactive monitoring of cooling and turbo plumbing is recommended for used buyers.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddles. It offers crisp shifting behavior suited for high-performance driving, but prospective buyers should inspect clutch engagement quality during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V141000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=650S&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are equipped with air bag inflators assembled as part of the passenger frontal air bag modules, used as original equipment or replacement equipment (such as after a vehicle crash necessitating replacement of the original air bags), that may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to high absolute humidity.
- **What to do:** McLaren will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 McLaren 650S is a formidable exotic supercar that offers incredible performance for its price bracket on the used market. Buyers should prioritize thorough pre-purchase inspections by a McLaren-certified specialist and verify comprehensive service history to ensure long-term ownership satisfaction.
