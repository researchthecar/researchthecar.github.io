# 2020 McLaren GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 McLaren GT combines exotic super-car performance with long-distance grand touring comfort, powered by a mid-mounted twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As with most low-volume exotic vehicles, prospective buyers should review maintenance history thoroughly and budget for specialized servicing.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 612 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating acceleration while serving as the core performance heart of the grand tourer.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides rapid gear shifts and seamless power delivery to the rear wheels. It handles high torque loads well under spirited driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V561000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Brake failure in one corner can cause the vehicle to pull to one side during braking, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the banjo bolt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V231000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** McLaren will notify owners, and dealers will remove the NVH foam pad from the vehicle and inspect the fuel tank. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V103000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag and the occupant restraint controller may be improperly connected, which can result in a deactivated driver's air bag that will not deploy in a crash. A driver's air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the driver's air bag connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V661000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hood:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The manual release cable of the hood latch may have excessive tension that can cause the latch to open, resulting in the hood opening unexpectedly. A hood that opens unexpectedly while driving can obstruct the driver's view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** McLaren will replace the hood latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V216000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A rear wheel locking condition may cause the driver to lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the brake line connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 McLaren GT offers breathtaking performance and distinctive styling for buyers seeking an exotic grand tourer. Because official complaint data for this low-volume model is sparse, buyers are strongly encouraged to secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an authorized or specialized technician before finalizing a purchase.
