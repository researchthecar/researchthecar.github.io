# 2021 McLaren GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 McLaren GT offers exotic performance and grand touring comfort, powered by a mid-rear mounted twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As with many hand-built low-volume supercars, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect service records and verify all vehicle systems are operating properly before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 612 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating acceleration and power delivery suited for grand touring, though proper maintenance and warm-up cycles are essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes with manual control via paddle shifters. It operates smoothly in comfort mode while offering crisp shifts under aggressive driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V103000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag and the occupant restraint controller may be improperly connected, which can result in a deactivated driver's air bag that will not deploy in a crash. A driver's air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the driver's air bag connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V661000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hood:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The manual release cable of the hood latch may have excessive tension that can cause the latch to open, resulting in the hood opening unexpectedly. A hood that opens unexpectedly while driving can obstruct the driver's view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** McLaren will replace the hood latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V216000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A rear wheel locking condition may cause the driver to lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the brake line connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
