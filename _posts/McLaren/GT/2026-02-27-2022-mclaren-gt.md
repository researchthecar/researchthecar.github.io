# 2022 McLaren GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 McLaren GT delivers exotic supercar performance with grand touring comfort, featuring a carbon-fiber monocoque and a mid-mounted twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As with many ultra-low-volume exotic vehicles, prospective buyers should review service histories carefully and verify the condition of all complex electronic and powertrain systems.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 produces approximately 612 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque, providing blistering acceleration and effortless highway cruising. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though exotic maintenance costs and warm-up requirements apply.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via paddle shifters with smooth operation in automatic mode. Regular fluid changes and software health checks are vital for long-term reliability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V661000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hood:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The manual release cable of the hood latch may have excessive tension that can cause the latch to open, resulting in the hood opening unexpectedly. A hood that opens unexpectedly while driving can obstruct the driver's view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** McLaren will replace the hood latch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V216000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A rear wheel locking condition may cause the driver to lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the brake line connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 McLaren GT remains a compelling choice for buyers seeking daily-drivable exotic performance. Because owner complaint data and independent service records for ultra-luxury models can be sparse, thorough pre-purchase inspections by a certified specialist are strongly advised before finalizing any purchase.
