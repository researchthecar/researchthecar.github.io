## 2019 McLaren 720S

The 2019 McLaren 720S is a high-performance mid-engine sports car powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine. It is available with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The 720S produces 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, and it can accelerate from 0-60 mph in 2.8 seconds. The 720S has received praise for its stunning design, blistering performance, and advanced technology. However, like any vehicle, it has had its share of issues.

### Engine

**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger Lag:** Turbocharger lag can cause hesitation or a lack of power when accelerating from a low RPM.
    * Solution: Reprogramming the engine's software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Valve Timing Issues:** Valve timing issues can lead to rough idling, misfires, and decreased performance.
    * Solution: Replacing the valve timing solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Oil Leaks:** Oil leaks can occur from various gaskets or seals in the engine.
    * Solution: Replacing the leaking gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recall: N/A

### Transmission

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Transmission Shudder:** Transmission shudder can be felt as a vibration or hesitation when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replacing the clutch pack or transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Gear Slippage:** Gear slippage can cause the transmission to slip out of gear, leading to a loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacing the transmission or specific gears.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recall: N/A

### Suspension

* **No Issues Found**

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains can occur due to a faulty battery or alternator.
    * Solution: Replacing the battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Infotainment System Issues:** Infotainment system issues can include glitches, slow performance, or connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recall: N/A

### Exterior

* **Door Alignment Issues:** Door alignment issues can lead to uneven gaps or difficulty closing the doors.
    * Solution: Adjusting the door hinges or replacing the door alignment pins.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Paint Defects:** Some owners have reported minor paint defects, such as chipping or fading.
    * Solution: Touch-up painting or repainting the affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

### Interior

* **Carbon Fiber Trim Cracking:** Some owners have reported cracking in the carbon fiber trim pieces.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

### Summary

Overall, the 2019 McLaren 720S is a high-performance vehicle with impressive capabilities. However, like any car, it has experienced some issues. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more serious and costly. Potential owners should be aware of these issues before purchasing a 720S and factor in potential repair costs into their ownership budget.
