# 2017 McLaren 720S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 McLaren 720S is a high-performance supercar known for its striking design and blistering speed. As with many exotic vehicles, used-car buyers should approach ownership with a clear understanding of potential maintenance costs and specialized service requirements.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 generating approximately 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. This engine delivers extreme performance, though maintenance requires specialized technician care and adherence to strict service intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Features a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission that offers lightning-fast shifts. Routine fluid changes and careful clutch management are essential for long-term operational smoothness.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V231000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=720S&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** McLaren will notify owners, and dealers will remove the NVH foam pad from the vehicle and inspect the fuel tank. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 McLaren 720S offers extraordinary exotic performance for buyers seeking top-tier supercar dynamics. Prospective owners should verify comprehensive service histories and budget for specialized maintenance to ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
