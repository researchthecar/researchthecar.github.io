### Summary Verdict
The 2021 McLaren 720S demonstrates strong mechanical reliability, with its core engine and transmission systems largely free from widespread reported issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter technical challenges are within the complex electrical systems, specifically the infotainment unit and various sensors, which can occasionally exhibit glitches or malfunctions. These are not uncommon in vehicles with such advanced technology. The exterior and interior components, as well as the advanced suspension, have shown good durability. Overall, the 720S offers a high level of reliability for a supercar, with most potential issues being related to sophisticated electronics rather than fundamental mechanical weaknesses.
