# 2020 McLaren 720S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 McLaren 720S is an exotic supercar offering blistering performance and striking design, powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. While buyers in this segment expect uncompromising engineering, exotic supercars can still present complex ownership considerations. When shopping for a used model, a thorough independent inspection by a marque specialist is essential to verify mechanical health.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (710 hp)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, delivering extreme acceleration. This high-output powertrain requires meticulous maintenance and premium fuel to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes suited for track and street driving. Buyers should verify smooth operation across all drive modes during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V561000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=720S&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Brake failure in one corner can cause the vehicle to pull to one side during braking, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the banjo bolt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V231000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=720S&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** McLaren will notify owners, and dealers will remove the NVH foam pad from the vehicle and inspect the fuel tank. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 McLaren 720S remains a pinnacle of modern supercar performance, combining everyday usability with fierce track capabilities. Because no major complaint patterns or common failure trends dominate the available owner data, prospective buyers should focus heavily on verifying service history, inspecting for leaks, and confirming professional maintenance upkeep.
