# 2019 McLaren 720S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Evaluating a used exotic supercar like the 2019 McLaren 720S requires careful attention to potential mechanical and hydraulic quirks. While these high-performance machines offer staggering speed and engineering brilliance, owners have noted specific areas—such as the suspension system—that require vigilance. Prospective buyers should prioritize thorough pre-purchase inspections by certified specialists to ensure all components are performing as expected.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2019 McLaren 720S is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing roughly 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It delivers blistering performance and generally robust operation, though routine maintenance costs remain exceptionally high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Power is routed to the rear wheels via a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission known for lightning-fast gear changes. The gearbox pairs well with the high-output V8, though proper fluid changes and software health are vital for long-term reliability.

## Suspension

### Hydropneumatic Suspension Accumulators

The sophisticated hydraulic suspension system provides exceptional handling balance, though early models may experience accumulator wear.

**Suspension Accumulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported issues with the suspension accumulators on early production models, which can impact ride quality and vehicle stability.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected components with updated, revised factory parts designed to address the underlying failure.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V561000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=720S&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Brake failure in one corner can cause the vehicle to pull to one side during braking, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the banjo bolt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V231000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=720S&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** McLaren will notify owners, and dealers will remove the NVH foam pad from the vehicle and inspect the fuel tank. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 McLaren 720S is an engineering masterpiece that delivers breathtaking performance for enthusiasts. While overall complaint volume is low, prospective buyers must keep a close eye on known trouble spots like the suspension accumulators. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a qualified technician is essential before finalizing any purchase.
