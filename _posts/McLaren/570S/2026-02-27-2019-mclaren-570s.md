# 2019 McLaren 570S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 McLaren 570S, buyers are looking at a high-performance exotic sports car that delivers breathtaking acceleration and sharp handling. While low-volume exotic vehicles often have limited complaint data available, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate structural and mechanical integrity prior to purchase. Independent pre-purchase inspections by a qualified McLaren specialist remain essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 570S is powered by a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing approximately 562 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers ferocious straight-line performance, though maintaining a high-strung exotic powertrain requires diligent adherence to service intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Power is routed to the rear wheels via a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission with paddle shifters. The gearbox delivers lightning-fast shifts suited for track or street driving, but buyers should check for smooth engagement during low-speed maneuvering.

## Exterior

### Dihedral Door Hinges

The signature dihedral doors rely on robust hinges to support their upward opening motion, making routine physical inspection of the pivot hardware crucial.

**Door Hinge Fractures**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported observing numerous fractures on the hinges supporting the dihedral doors, which raises safety concerns about door support.
- **Solution:** Inspect door hinges closely for structural cracking or fatigue and replace damaged assemblies as needed.
