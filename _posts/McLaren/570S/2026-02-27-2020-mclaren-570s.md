# 2020 McLaren 570S Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 McLaren 570S is a high-performance exotic sports car that delivers breathtaking acceleration and precision handling. For prospective used-car buyers, owning an exotic vehicle of this caliber requires careful budgeting for specialized maintenance, inspections, and insurance.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 562 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers ferocious power delivery, though proper routine servicing and monitoring of thermal management systems are vital for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. Buyers should verify smooth operation during low-speed maneuvering and ensure fluid changes have been performed according to schedule.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V561000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=McLaren&model=570S&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Brake failure in one corner can cause the vehicle to pull to one side during braking, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the banjo bolt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 McLaren 570S is an exhilarating exotic sports car that commands attention on the road and track. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a certified McLaren specialist to verify the condition of the powertrain and electronics before purchasing.
