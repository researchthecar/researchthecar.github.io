# 2021 Porsche 718 Spyder Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Porsche 718 Spyder stands out as a high-performance roadster delivering pure driving dynamics and a naturally aspirated thrill. Because it caters to enthusiasts, buyers should evaluate powertrain specifications and overall condition carefully during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Naturally Aspirated Boxer-6

The 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine produces 414 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque, offering high-revving performance. It is generally robust and rewards diligent maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The standard 6-speed manual transmission features a mechanical shift action tailored for enthusiast driving. It offers strong reliability when the clutch and linkage are properly maintained.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V157000: Suspension](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=718+Spyder&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Certain suspension components may not have been tightened correctly and can loosen. Loose suspension components may detach, causing a sudden loss of vehicle stability and control, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the affected suspension nuts and bolts. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V200000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=718+Spyder&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loose connection while driving may cause engine damage, a stall, or an oil leak, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will replace the connecting rods or, as necessary, the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Porsche 718 Spyder is a compelling choice for driving enthusiasts seeking analog engagement paired with modern capability. Prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection and verify service records to ensure a worry-free ownership experience.
