**Introduction:**

The 2022 Porsche 718 Spyder is a high-performance sports car with a mid-mounted engine. It is powered by a 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-six engine producing 414 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a six-speed manual transmission. The 718 Spyder has a sleek and aerodynamic design, and it is praised by critics for its handling and performance. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

**4.0-Liter Flat-Six Engine**

**Valve Spring Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The valve springs can fail prematurely, causing the engine to lose power and stall.
Solution: The valve springs must be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Shift Linkage Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The shift linkage can fail, making it difficult to shift gears.
Solution: The shift linkage must be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Clutch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The clutch can fail, causing the vehicle to slip out of gear.
Solution: The clutch must be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Adaptive Suspension**
Description/Symptoms: The adaptive suspension can malfunction, causing the vehicle to ride too stiffly or too softly.
Solution: The adaptive suspension control module must be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
Solution: The cause of the battery drain must be identified and fixed.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can malfunction, causing the screen to freeze or become unresponsive.
Solution: The infotainment system must be reprogrammed or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

The 2022 Porsche 718 Spyder is a high-performance sports car that has some reported issues. These issues range in severity and cost to fix.
