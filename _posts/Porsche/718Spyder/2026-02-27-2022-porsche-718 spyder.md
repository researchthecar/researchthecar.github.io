### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Porsche 718 Spyder demonstrates excellent technical reliability, consistent with Porsche's reputation for building durable performance vehicles. Major mechanical components such as the engine and transmission, available in both manual and PDK configurations, show no significant widespread issues. The chassis and suspension systems are also robust. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system's software, are the most commonly cited, though these are typically resolved with updates. Exterior and interior components are generally well-built and hold up well. Overall, the 718 Spyder is a highly reliable sports car, with owners enjoying a dependable driving experience.
