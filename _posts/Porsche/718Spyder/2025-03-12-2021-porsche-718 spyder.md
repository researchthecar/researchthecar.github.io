The 2021 Porsche 718 Spyder is a high-performance roadster celebrated for its engaging driving dynamics, powerful engine, and striking design. Powered by a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine producing 414 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque, the Spyder offers a thrilling experience. It comes exclusively with a 6-speed manual transmission. Owners frequently praise the car's precise handling, responsive throttle, and the visceral sound of the engine. However, some criticisms include the complexity of the manually operated soft top and the limited storage space.

**Engine**

**4.0L Flat-Six**

*   **Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, requiring more frequent oil top-offs.
    *   Solution: Monitoring oil levels frequently and addressing any leaks promptly can help mitigate this issue. In more severe cases, piston ring replacement might be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

*   **Difficulty Shifting into First Gear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have noted difficulty engaging first gear, especially when the transmission is cold. This can manifest as a notchy or resistant feeling when attempting to shift.
    *   Solution: A transmission fluid change can sometimes alleviate the issue. In more persistent cases, adjustment or replacement of the shift linkage or synchros may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Rebooting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Porsche Communication Management (PCM) system can occasionally freeze or spontaneously reboot. This can disrupt navigation, audio, and other system functions.
    *   Solution: Software updates from Porsche are often released to address these issues. In some cases, a full system reset or replacement of the PCM unit might be needed.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $2,000+ (PCM replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Soft Top Latch Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported issues with the manual soft top latches, specifically difficulty securing the top properly or latches that feel loose or misaligned.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of the soft top latch mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Leather Delamination on Dashboard**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warping or delamination of the leather on the dashboard, particularly in hot climates or vehicles exposed to direct sunlight, can cause the leather to peel away from the underlying material.
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard or professional leather repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Porsche 718 Spyder, while highly regarded for its performance and driving experience, can experience some issues. The most notable complaints include potential engine oil consumption and shifting issues with the manual transmission. Owners have also reported electrical glitches with the infotainment system and minor exterior and interior issues such as soft top latches and leather delamination. The severity and cost of these potential issues range from minor inconveniences to more substantial repairs, so prospective buyers should be aware and consider these factors.
