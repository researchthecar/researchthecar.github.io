**Introduction**

The 2021 Porsche Taycan is an all-electric luxury sedan that offers impressive performance, advanced technology, and a sleek design. It is powered by a dual-motor electric powertrain that produces 616 horsepower and 774 lb-ft of torque in the base model and up to 750 horsepower and 1,055 lb-ft of torque in the top-of-the-line Turbo S variant. The Taycan is available with a single-speed automatic transmission and comes standard with all-wheel drive. Owners appreciate the Taycan's thrilling acceleration, responsive handling, and luxurious interior. However, it is not without its issues.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Cooling Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant may leak from the battery cooling system, leading to overheating and battery damage.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking component(s) in the battery cooling system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience crashes, freezes, or display errors.
    * Solution: Update the infotainment system software or replace the affected module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Wind Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise may occur when the sunroof is open or slightly tilted.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof weatherstripping.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Handle Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handles may stick or become loose, making it difficult to open or close the doors.
    * Solution: Replace the affected door handle(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat adjustment controls may malfunction, preventing the driver from adjusting the seat position.
    * Solution: Replace the affected seat control module or switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may experience issues such as temperature fluctuations, fan noise, or refrigerant leaks.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the affected component(s) in the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2021 Porsche Taycan offers excellent performance and luxury, it has experienced some issues, particularly with its electrical system and exterior components. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the repair required. Owners should be aware of potential issues and address them promptly to ensure optimal performance and safety.
