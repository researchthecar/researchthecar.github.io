# 2023 Porsche 911 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Porsche 911 represents the pinnacle of modern sports car engineering, combining iconic styling with staggering performance and everyday usability. As a relatively new luxury performance vehicle on the used market, it generally commands strong confidence from buyers looking for exceptional driving dynamics and high build quality. Because owner reports are sparse, prospective buyers should focus on routine pre-purchase inspections and verify service history to ensure proper maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Boxer-6 (Base/S/GTS)

This twin-turbocharged flat-six engine produces anywhere from 379 to 473 horsepower depending on the trim level. It delivers robust power delivery across the rev range with strong reliability in modern applications.

### 4.0L Naturally Aspirated Boxer-6 (GT3/GT3 RS)

A high-revving naturally aspirated flat-six powerplant generating over 500 horsepower, built for track-focused performance. It requires meticulous maintenance and rewards drivers with an unmatched mechanical soundtrack.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed PDK Dual-Clutch Automatic

This lightning-fast dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes and exceptional track performance. It is widely regarded as one of the best automated gearboxes on the market.

### 7-Speed Manual

Offered on select trims for enthusiasts desiring a traditional three-pedal driving experience. It provides engaging shift mechanics coupled with grand touring highway gearing.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V897000: Structure:Interior Panels:Dashboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=911&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash with passenger air bag deployment, the dashboard console may break, causing the air bag to deploy improperly. An improper air bag deployment increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the dashboard, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V155000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=911&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front windshield and rear window may not be properly secured and can detach. During air bag deployment, an unsecured windshield may not support the front air bags as intended, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the windshield and rear window as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V809000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=911&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Wheel detachment can result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will check the center lock wheel fastening system and replace any incorrectly manufactured parts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V078000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=911&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The bolts for the rear seat belt buckles may not be tightened properly. A loose seat belt buckle may not properly restrain an occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the rear seat belt buckle bolt, and reposition the aluminum butyl soundproofing mat as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V896000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=911&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the driver assistance software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
