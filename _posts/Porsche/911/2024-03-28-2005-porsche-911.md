**Introduction:**

The 2005 Porsche 911 is a high-performance sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience. It comes equipped with a powerful 3.6L flat-six engine, producing 325 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The 911 is available with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. While the 911 is renowned for its impressive performance and handling, it has also been plagued by various issues over the years.

**Engine**

**3.6L Flat-Six (Manual Transmission)**

* **Bore Scoring:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wear and scoring on the cylinder walls, leading to reduced engine power and increased oil consumption.
    * Solution: Cylinder block replacement is required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
    * Recalls: PS67
* **Crankshaft Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the crankshaft, resulting in catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine replacement is required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20,000 - $30,000+
    * Recalls: PS98

**3.6L Flat-Six (Automatic Transmission)**

* **IMS Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the intermediate shaft bearing, leading to engine noise, oil leaks, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: IMS bearing replacement or engine rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Throttle Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction of the electronic throttle actuator, causing sudden loss of engine power and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Throttle actuator replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: PS96

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Slave Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the clutch slave cylinder, resulting in difficulty engaging or disengaging the clutch.
    * Solution: Clutch slave cylinder replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Lockup Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction of the torque converter lockup clutch, leading to reduced power transfer and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Torque converter replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Squeaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise coming from the rear suspension when driving over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear suspension bushing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the alternator, leading to battery drain and potential electrical issues.
    * Solution: Alternator replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: PS90
* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the headlight switch, resulting in intermittent or non-functioning headlights.
    * Solution: Headlight switch replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: PS91

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the vehicle through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replacement of sunroof seal or drainage tubes.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Control Buttons Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or non-functioning steering wheel control buttons.
    * Solution: Replacement of steering wheel control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2005 Porsche 911 is a desirable sports car but has been plagued by a variety of issues over the years. Some of these issues, such as engine bore scoring and crankshaft failure, are severe and can be extremely costly to repair. Other issues, such as suspension squeaking and electrical problems, are less severe but can still be frustrating for owners. While the 911 offers an exhilarating driving experience, potential buyers should be aware of the potential problems and factor in the cost of repairs when considering this vehicle.
