# 2019 Porsche 911 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Porsche 911 represents the pinnacle of everyday sports car performance, blending iconic styling with sophisticated engineering. As a used vehicle purchase, buyers can generally expect high build quality and engaging driving dynamics, though prospective owners should always thoroughly inspect service records and verify proper maintenance history.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Boxer-6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six engine produces approximately 370 to 450 horsepower depending on the specific Carrera variant. It offers strong low-end torque and impressive everyday drivability, though proper cooling and turbo maintenance are essential.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch (PDK) Transmission

The 7-speed PDK dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes and smooth automatic operation. It is widely regarded as one of the best automated transmissions on the market, requiring routine fluid changes to maintain longevity.

### 7-Speed Manual Transmission

The 7-speed manual transmission caters to purists seeking direct mechanical engagement. It features precise shift throws and well-spaced gear ratios, though drivers must adapt to the unique gated layout for seventh gear.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V201000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=911&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loose connection may fail, causing driving instability and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will rework and tighten the screw connection on the rear axle upper control arm, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Porsche 911 is a remarkably robust and well-engineered luxury sports car. While normal pre-purchase inspections are always recommended for high-performance vehicles, it stands out as a dependable choice for enthusiasts seeking both performance and usability.
