**2022 Porsche 911**

The 2022 Porsche 911 is a high-performance sports car available with various engine and transmission options. It offers exceptional handling, blistering acceleration, and luxurious amenities. However, like any vehicle, it has some reported issues that owners and potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

**3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Flat-Six**
- **Faulty Fuel Injector Seals:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Leaking fuel can cause rough idle, poor performance, and increased fuel consumption.
  - Solution: Replace the fuel injector seals.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
  - Recalls: 22V-751

**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged Flat-Six**
- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Manual**
- **Difficult Shifting:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Gears may be difficult to engage or shift smoothly.
  - Solution: May require adjustment or replacement of the shift linkage or transmission components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed PDK Dual-Clutch**
- **Intermittent Gear Slippage:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip gears, resulting in power loss and unreliable performance.
  - Solution: May require software updates or replacement of the transmission control module.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Squeaky Control Arms:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Worn control arm bushings can cause squeaking noises over bumps or during cornering.
  - Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Malfunctioning PCM (Porsche Communication Management System):**
  - Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, display errors, or become unresponsive.
  - Solution: Replace or update the PCM software.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: 22V-843

**Exterior**

- **Condensation in Headlights:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Water droplets may form inside the headlight assemblies due to a faulty seal.
  - Solution: Replace the headlight assemblies.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Faulty Seat Recliner:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The seat recliner may fail to operate properly, preventing the seat from being adjusted.
  - Solution: Replace the seat recliner mechanism.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Porsche 911 is a remarkable sports car but is not without its issues. Some problems, such as faulty fuel injector seals on the 3.0L engine, can be significant and require costly repairs. Other issues, such as squeaky control arms or seat recliner malfunctions, are less severe but can still be bothersome. While these issues affect a relatively small number of vehicles, it is essential for potential buyers to be aware of them before making a purchase decision.
