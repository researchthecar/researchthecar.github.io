**1996 Porsche 911**

The 1996 Porsche 911 is a high-performance sports car that combines exceptional handling, acceleration, and braking. It is powered by a rear-mounted, air-cooled flat-six engine and is available in a variety of configurations, including the Carrera 2, Carrera 4, Carrera S, and Turbo. The 911 is known for its precision engineering, but it also has some common issues.

**Engine**

* **Carrera 2/4/S:**
    * **VarioCam Plus timing chain tensioners:** Can fail, leading to chain slack and potential engine damage. Replace tensioners and timing chain. $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Intermediate shaft bearing (IMS):** Can wear out prematurely, causing oil leaks and engine noise. Replace IMS bearing. $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Turbo:**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** Can fail due to excessive heat or lubrication issues. Replace turbocharger. $3,000 - $5,000+
    * **Coolant system leaks:** Can occur due to faulty hoses or gaskets. Replace leaking components. $200 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

* **Manual Transmission:**
    * **Synchros:** Can wear out over time, causing difficulty shifting gears. Replace synchros. $800 - $2,000+
* **Tiptronic Transmission:**
    * **Valve body failure:** Can cause erratic shifting or transmission failure. Replace valve body. $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Rear suspension trailing arms:** Can crack or break under heavy loads. Replace trailing arms. $600 - $1,000+ per arm
* **Front struts:** Can wear out over time, leading to reduced handling and stability. Replace struts. $400 - $800+ per strut

**Electrical**

* **Ignition coils:** Can fail, causing misfires and poor performance. Replace ignition coils. $200 - $400+ per coil
* **Alternator:** Can fail, leading to electrical system problems and battery drain. Replace alternator. $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Door handles:** Can break or become loose. Replace door handles. $200 - $400+ per handle

**Interior**

* **Air conditioning condenser:** Can leak or fail. Replace condenser. $500 - $1,000+
* **Sunroof motor:** Can fail, preventing sunroof from opening or closing. Replace sunroof motor. $400 - $800+

**Summary**

The 1996 Porsche 911 is a reliable vehicle, but it is not without its problems. Some of the common issues can be costly to fix, but most are relatively minor and can be addressed with regular maintenance and repairs. Overall, the 911 is a well-built sports car that provides its owners with years of driving enjoyment.
