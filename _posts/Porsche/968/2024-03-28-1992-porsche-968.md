**Introduction**

The 1992 Porsche 968 is a sports car that was produced from 1992 to 1995. It was the successor to the Porsche 944 and was powered by a naturally aspirated 3.0-liter inline-four engine. The 968 was available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **3.0L M44/41 Inline-Four**
    * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Belt Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Catastrophic engine failure
        * Solution: Replace timing belt and tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption
        * Solution: Replace piston rings or valve stem seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Synchronizer Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace synchronizers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Valve Body Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hard shifts
        * Solution: Replace valve body
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Strut Mount Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises from the front of the car
        * Solution: Replace strut mounts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system failure
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies after sitting for a period of time
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix the source of the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system not working properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix the specific component that is failing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1992 Porsche 968 is a reliable and enjoyable sports car. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension. While the cost of repairs can be high, most issues can be resolved with a reasonable investment.
