**Introduction to the 2016 Porsche Cayman**

The 2016 Porsche Cayman is a mid-engine sports car powered by a 2.7-liter flat-six engine that produces 275 horsepower and 213 lb-ft of torque. The Cayman is available with either a six-speed manual or seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

The Cayman is praised for its sharp handling, powerful engine, and luxurious interior. However, it is also criticized for its lack of practicality and high price.

**Engine**

**2.7-liter Flat-Six**

* **Cylinder Head Leak**
  * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the cylinder head, leading to overheating and coolant loss.
  * Solution: Replace the cylinder head gaskets.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Gearshift Linkage Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second and third gear.
  * Solution: Replace the gearshift linkage.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
  * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Clutch Pack Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering during gear changes, especially at low speeds.
  * Solution: Replace the clutch pack.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise and vibration when driving over bumps.
  * Solution: Replace the front lower control arm bushings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the car.
  * Solution: Check for parasitic draw and replace the battery if necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Misalignment**
  * Description/Symptoms: Trunk lid does not close properly or sits unevenly.
  * Solution: Adjust the trunk hinges or replace the trunk lid if damaged.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Panel Squeak**
  * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noise coming from the center console.
  * Solution: Tighten or replace the trim panel.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Porsche Cayman is a well-engineered and reliable sports car. However, like all vehicles, it has its share of issues. The most common problems include cylinder head leaks, gearshift linkage failure, clutch pack failure, front lower control arm bushing wear, battery discharge, trunk lid misalignment, and center console trim panel squeak.

The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. However, some issues, such as cylinder head leaks and clutch pack failure, can be more serious and costly to repair.
