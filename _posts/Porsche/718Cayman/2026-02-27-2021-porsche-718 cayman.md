# 2021 Porsche 718 Cayman

### Overview
The 2021 Porsche 718 Cayman is a highly regarded sports car known for its exceptional driving dynamics and build quality. As a premium performance vehicle, it generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Porsche's engineering prowess. While individual owner experiences can vary, widespread mechanical or electrical failures are uncommon for this model year. Owners typically report satisfaction with the vehicle's performance and durability, with most maintenance revolving around scheduled servicing rather than unexpected repairs. The focus for this data is on documented, non-wear-and-tear related issues that have been reported by a notable number of owners or through automotive reliability surveys.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Porsche 718 Cayman demonstrates excellent reliability, consistent with Porsche's reputation for building high-performance vehicles that are also durable. Major mechanical failures are rare across all engine and transmission options. The most commonly cited, albeit minor, issues relate to the infotainment system experiencing occasional software glitches, which are typically resolved with updates. Other categories such as suspension, exterior, and interior components show a high degree of robustness. Owners can expect a dependable sports car experience, with maintenance primarily focused on routine servicing and the inherent costs associated with premium performance vehicles.
