# 2023 Porsche 718 Cayman Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Porsche 718 Cayman offers world-class handling and engaging powertrain options, making it a compelling choice for driving enthusiasts. While generally built to high manufacturing standards, prospective buyers should review available owner reports and inspect prospective vehicles thoroughly.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Flat-4

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged flat-4 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and delivers strong responsive performance. While generally robust, isolated owner complaints have noted severe internal mechanical failures.

**Internal Engine Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owner reports indicate engine misfires followed by the discovery of disintegrated cylinder wall linings and metal shavings in the engine oil.
- **Solution:** Requires engine replacement as diagnosed by authorized service centers.

### 2.5L Turbocharged Flat-4

The 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-4 engine powers S models, generating roughly 350 horsepower for heightened acceleration. It combines spirited output with solid overall engineering.

### 4.0L Naturally Aspirated Flat-6

The 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-6 engine powers GTS and GT4 variants, offering up to 394 or 418 horsepower and an exceptional exhaust note. This traditional boxer powerplant is prized by enthusiasts for its linear power delivery.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission provides crisp mechanical gear changes for purists. It is generally durable when operated properly.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch (PDK) Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed Porsche Doppelkupplung (PDK) automatic transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes and smooth power delivery in daily driving. It is widely regarded as one of the best automated gearboxes on the market.
