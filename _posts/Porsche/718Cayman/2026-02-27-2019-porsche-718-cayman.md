# 2019 Porsche 718 Cayman Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Porsche 718 Cayman is an exceptional mid-engine sports car praised for its precise handling, sharp driving dynamics, and strong performance capabilities. For used car buyers looking into this model year, reviewing foundational powertrain configurations and potential ownership quirks helps ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Flat-4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged horizontally opposed four-cylinder engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It offers responsive everyday performance and solid overall mechanical reliability when properly maintained.

### 2.5L Turbocharged Flat-4 (S)

The upgraded 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-four engine found in the S trim pushes output to roughly 350 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating acceleration while maintaining the balanced driving character expected of the platform.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission provides crisp, mechanical gear engagement for driving purists. It is generally robust, though clutch wear depends heavily on previous driving habits.

### 7-Speed PDK Dual-Clutch Automatic

Porsche's 7-speed Doppelkupplung (PDK) dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast and seamless gear changes. It is highly regarded for performance and durability, provided fluid service intervals are strictly followed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V113000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=718+Cayman&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, a crossmember may detach from the luggage compartment bracket, possibly causing a fuel leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will secure the crossmember with additional rivets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Porsche 718 Cayman stands out as a highly dependable and exceptionally engineered performance machine. Prospective buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection focusing on service history, general wear items, and overall mechanical condition to enjoy a rewarding ownership experience.
