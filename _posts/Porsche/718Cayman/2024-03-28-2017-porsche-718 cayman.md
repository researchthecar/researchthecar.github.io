**Introduction:**

The 2017 Porsche 718 Cayman is a mid-engine sports car that debuted as a replacement for the Boxster and Cayman models. It is available with two turbocharged flat-four engine options and a six-speed manual or seven-speed PDK dual-clutch transmission. The 2.0-liter engine produces 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.5-liter engine generates 350 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque. The 718 Cayman is praised for its sharp handling, powerful engines, and stylish design. However, it also has some drawbacks, including a cramped interior and a high price tag.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Flat-Four**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:** Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause reduced engine performance and misfires.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Turbocharger Failure:** Premature failure of the turbocharger can lead to reduced power and excessive smoke.
    * Solution: Replacing the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**2.5-Liter Turbocharged Flat-Four**

* **Rod Bearing Failure:** A failure in the rod bearings can cause catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000+

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Gearshift Linkage Wear:** Worn gear shift linkage can result in difficulty shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replacing the gear shift linkage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Seven-Speed PDK**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** This component controls the transmission's shifting and can fail over time.
    * Solution: Replacing the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000
* **Clutch Slippage:** The clutch may slip, causing loss of power and acceleration.
    * Solution: Replacing the clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:** Malfunctioning rear shock absorbers can compromise ride quality and handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** A parasitic electrical draw can drain the battery prematurely.
    * Solution: Identifying and eliminating the source of the parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Ignition Coil Failure:** Faulty ignition coils can cause misfires and engine performance issues.
    * Solution: Replacing the ignition coils.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture can accumulate inside the headlight housings.
    * Solution: Sealing the headlight housings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:** The infotainment system may freeze or experience other glitches.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary:**

The 2017 Porsche 718 Cayman is a capable sports car with some potential issues in its engine, transmission, and other components. The severity and cost of these issues vary, and some may only affect certain models or configurations. While the vehicle's sharp handling and powerful engines make it a desirable choice, potential owners should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
