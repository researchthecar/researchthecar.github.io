## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks/Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'Air suspension system fault' warning light, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking air struts/lines, replace faulty air compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, system freezing, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, battery warning light, dead battery after short periods of inactivity.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw from modules, replace faulty battery, update control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Porsche Panamera demonstrates a high level of reliability, with most major components like the engine and transmission showing no widespread issues. The primary areas that may require attention are the adaptive air suspension, which can develop leaks or compressor faults, and occasional electrical gremlins such as infotainment system glitches or intermittent battery drain. These issues, while not universal, are the most commonly reported technical concerns beyond standard maintenance. Overall, the Panamera is a robust vehicle, but owners should be prepared for potential costs associated with its advanced suspension and electrical systems.
