**Introduction:**

The 2022 Porsche Panamera is a luxurious and high-performance sedan available with various engine and transmission options. Its 3.0L V6 engine generates 325 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque, while the 4.0L V8 offers 443 horsepower and 405 lb-ft of torque. The 4.0L V8 with E-Hybrid produces 552 horsepower and 552 lb-ft of torque, and the Turbo S E-Hybrid delivers an astonishing 689 horsepower and 642 lb-ft of torque. The Panamera is renowned for its exceptional handling, sophisticated interior, and advanced technology features. However, like any vehicle, it has encountered some issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to oil level warnings and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $20,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, check engine light illumination, decreased fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replacement of fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.0L V8**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, whining or whistling noises.
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks, decreased coolant levels.
    * Solution: Replacement of water pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed PDK**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, grinding noises.
    * Solution: Replacement of transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to shift gears, transmission warning messages.
    * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, rough ride quality.
    * Solution: Replacement of air springs or compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing, poor handling, uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacement of shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replacement of battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing, software glitches, connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Software updates, reprogramming, or replacement of infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water entering the cabin through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of sunroof seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to control temperature, excessive noise from vents.
    * Solution: Repairs or replacement of climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Shortages:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning electrical components, dimming of lights.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Porsche Panamera is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some issues that can be costly to repair. Common concerns include oil consumption, turbocharger failure, and transmission slippage. Some electrical and interior issues are also reported. Owners may want to consider extended warranties or maintenance plans to cover potential repairs. It's important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and the severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific situation.
