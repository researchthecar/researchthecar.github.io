# 2022 Porsche Panamera Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2022 Porsche Panamera offers an exquisite blend of luxury, grand touring comfort, and sports-car performance. As a high-end luxury vehicle, it is packed with advanced engineering and sophisticated electronics. While major mechanical failures are rarely reported at this stage, buyers should still carefully inspect individual driver comfort and cabin features during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

This 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 serves as the baseline engine for the Panamera, producing around 325 horsepower. It delivers smooth, effortless acceleration and remains a sophisticated, dependable core for everyday driving.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 powers higher-tier trims with substantially higher output, offering thrilling performance. Proper adherence to maintenance schedules is essential to keep these complex high-performance powerplants running smoothly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch (PDK)

The 8-speed Porsche Doppelkupplung (PDK) automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and refined cruising behavior. It is widely praised for its responsiveness and durability when properly maintained.

## Interior

### Seat Frames and Floorboard

Cabin appointments are exceptionally crafted, though isolating isolated harmonic vibrations can occasionally require diagnostic attention from a specialist.

**Abnormal Seat Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers or passengers may notice an abnormal vibration originating from the seat frame and floorboard, typically emerging around 45 mph and peaking between 55-65 mph, or sometimes noticeable while idling in Park.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the seat frame mountings, floor pan torque specs, and driveline components for unusual harmonic vibrations.
