# 2023 Porsche 718 Boxster Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Porsche 718 Boxster continues to offer exceptional mid-engine performance, precision handling, and a driver-focused cockpit. As a modern sports car, it maintains high build quality standards, though prospective buyers should always evaluate mechanical condition and service history thoroughly.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Flat-4 Engine

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged flat-4 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It provides strong baseline performance and daily drivability for the 718 lineup.

### 2.5L Turbocharged Flat-4 Engine

The S-trim 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-4 engine generates around 350 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque. It offers enhanced acceleration while maintaining Porsche's signature mid-engine balance.

### 4.0L Naturally Aspirated Flat-6 Engine

Found in GTS and higher variants, the 4.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-6 engine delivers up to 394 horsepower with a spine-tingling exhaust note. It is prized by enthusiasts for its linear power delivery and lack of forced induction lag.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers short, precise throws and direct driver engagement. It is a favorite among purists looking for total control over gear selection.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch (PDK) Transmission

The 7-speed Porsche Doppelkupplung (PDK) automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery. It enhances both track performance and relaxed cruising.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides exceptional stopping power suited for both street and track use, though routine wear inspections are recommended.

**Brake System Inspection Note**
- **Symptoms:** Owner reports note general driving circumstances without immediate mechanical failure, but proper routine checks are essential.
- **Solution:** Perform a comprehensive inspection of brake pads, rotors, and fluid levels during routine maintenance.
