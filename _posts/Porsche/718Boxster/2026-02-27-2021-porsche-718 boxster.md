### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Porsche 718 Boxster demonstrates excellent mechanical reliability across its engine and transmission options. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and rare instances of battery drain, which are generally resolvable with software updates or diagnostics. The chassis, suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from significant reported problems. Overall, the 718 Boxster is a dependable sports car, with its reliability rating being a strong point, provided routine maintenance is performed and any minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
