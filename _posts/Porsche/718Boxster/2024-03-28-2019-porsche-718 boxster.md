**Introduction**

The 2019 Porsche 718 Boxster is a mid-engine sports car that is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter flat-four engine or a turbocharged 2.5-liter flat-four engine. The 2.0-liter engine produces 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.5-liter engine produces 350 horsepower and 305 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission (PDK).

The 718 Boxster is praised for its sharp handling, responsive steering, and powerful engines. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter**

* **Oil leaks from the camshaft adjuster:** This issue can cause the engine to lose oil and potentially damage the engine. The solution is to replace the camshaft adjuster.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**2.5-liter**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Six-speed manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Seven-speed PDK**

* **Clutches wearing out prematurely:** This issue can cause the transmission to slip or shudder when shifting gears. The solution is to replace the clutches.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension bushings wearing out prematurely:** This issue can cause the suspension to become noisy and the vehicle to handle poorly. The solution is to replace the front suspension bushings.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **PCM (Porsche Communication Management) system freezing:** This issue can cause the infotainment system to become unresponsive or to reboot itself. The solution is to update the PCM software.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $0 - $500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights fogging up:** This issue can occur due to condensation building up inside the headlights. The solution is to seal the headlights or replace them.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Center console trim peeling:** This issue can occur due to exposure to sunlight. The solution is to replace the center console trim.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Porsche 718 Boxster is a reliable sports car that offers impressive performance. However, there are some potential issues that owners should be aware of, including oil leaks, transmission problems, and suspension issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem.
