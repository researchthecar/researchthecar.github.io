# 2019 Porsche 718 Boxster Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Porsche 718 Boxster delivers world-class mid-engine handling, punchy turbocharged power, and renowned German engineering. While owner-reported complaints are sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should still carefully inspect the vehicle's powertrain health, electrical systems, and convertible top mechanisms prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Boxer 4-Cylinder

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged flat-four engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It offers quick throttle response and balanced performance, making it a robust and daily-drivable powerplant for the 718 lineup.

### 2.5L Turbocharged Boxer 4-Cylinder

Found in the S models, this 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-four generates roughly 350 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque. It provides enhanced straight-line acceleration while maintaining solid overall mechanical durability under normal driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual gearbox features short, precise throws and direct engagement. It is generally favored by enthusiasts for its mechanical purity and long-term dependability.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch (PDK) Automatic Transmission

Porsche's 7-speed Doppelkupplung (PDK) automatic delivers lightning-fast gear changes and smooth power delivery. Regular fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term reliability of the clutch packs and mechatronic unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V113000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=718+Boxster&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, a crossmember may detach from the luggage compartment bracket, possibly causing a fuel leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will secure the crossmember with additional rivets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Porsche 718 Boxster is an exceptional driver's car backed by high build quality and limited widespread owner complaints. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified marque specialist to verify overall mechanical condition and confirm proper electronic and powertrain operation.
