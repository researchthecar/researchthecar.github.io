**The 2023 Porsche 718 Boxster**

The 2023 Porsche 718 Boxster is a mid-engine sports car that's known for its sharp handling and style. It's offered with two engine options: a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder and a turbocharged 2.5-liter four-cylinder. Both engines are paired with a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The 2.0-liter engine produces 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.5-liter engine produces 350 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque.

People who like the 718 Boxster praise its handling, performance, and style. However, some people have complained about its small trunk and lack of features.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder**
    * **Intake valve coking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves can lead to reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption, and rough idling.
        * Solution: The intake valves need to be cleaned.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail, leading to a loss of power.
        * Solution: The turbocharger needs to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Six-speed manual transmission**
    * **Difficult shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may be difficult to shift into or out of gear.
        * Solution: The transmission may need to be adjusted or repaired.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission**
    * **Clutch failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clutch can fail, leading to a loss of power.
        * Solution: The clutch needs to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

If you're considering buying a 718 Boxster, be sure to factor in the potential cost of these repairs.
