### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Porsche 718 Boxster demonstrates strong overall reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing very few reported issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain. These issues are generally manageable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. The exterior and interior components are also robust. Owners can expect a dependable sports car experience, with potential repair costs largely related to these specific electrical gremlins rather than fundamental mechanical weaknesses.
