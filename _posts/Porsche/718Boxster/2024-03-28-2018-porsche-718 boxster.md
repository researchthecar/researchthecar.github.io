**Introduction:**

The 2018 Porsche 718 Boxster is a mid-engined roadster that comes in two engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged flat-four and a 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-four. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (PDK). The 718 Boxster is praised for its handling, performance, and stylish design, but some owners have reported a number of issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.0-Liter Turbocharged Flat-Four Engine**
    * **Piston Ring Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, loss of power
        * Solution: Engine rebuild
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Cracked Cylinder Heads**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
        * Solution: Cylinder head replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.5-Liter Turbocharged Flat-Four Engine**
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises from the engine, loss of power, engine stalling
        * Solution: Timing chain replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Shifter Linkage Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Shifter linkage repair or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission (PDK)**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, or refusing to shift
        * Solution: Mechatronic unit replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Battery replacement or charging system diagnosis
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, navigation issues, Bluetooth connectivity problems
    * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Top doesn't open or close properly, water leaks
    * Solution: Top motor or mechanism repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning doesn't work properly, heating system malfunctions
    * Solution: Climate control module or compressor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Sensor Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Airbag warning light comes on, seat belt doesn't engage properly
    * Solution: Seat sensor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the Porsche 718 Boxster is a highly desirable sports car, it has been plagued by a number of issues that can be expensive to fix. Engine problems, such as piston ring failure and cracked cylinder heads, are among the most serious and costly. Transmission issues, such as shifter linkage failure and mechatronic unit failure, can also be a major inconvenience. Electrical problems, such as battery discharge and infotainment system malfunctions, are relatively common. Overall, the 718 Boxster has a significant number of potential issues that buyers should be aware of before purchasing the vehicle.
