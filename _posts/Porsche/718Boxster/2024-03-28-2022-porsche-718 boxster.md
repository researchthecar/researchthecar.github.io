## 2022 Porsche 718 Boxster

The 2022 Porsche 718 Boxster is a mid-engine sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience. It comes with a 2.0-liter turbocharged flat-four engine that produces 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The 718 Boxster can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 4.7 seconds and has a top track speed of 170 mph.

The 718 Boxster is praised for its precise handling, responsive steering, and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Flat-Four Engine**

* **Valve Train Problems**
   * Description/Symptoms: Excessive valve train noise, engine misfires, reduced engine power
   * Solution: Replace the faulty valve components
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Piston Ring Failure**
   * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, loss of engine power
   * Solution: Replace the faulty piston rings
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear Grinding**
   * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting gears
   * Solution: Replace the faulty transmission components
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping**
   * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping when shifting gears, loss of power
   * Solution: Replace the faulty transmission components
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Squeaks and Rattles**
   * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the front suspension when driving over bumps or rough roads
   * Solution: Replace the faulty suspension components
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
   * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
   * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or electrical components
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Issues**
   * Description/Symptoms: Convertible top leaks, difficulty opening or closing
   * Solution: Replace or repair the faulty convertible top components
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
   * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezes, crashes, or has connectivity issues
   * Solution: Update the software or replace the faulty infotainment system
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Porsche 718 Boxster is a high-performance sports car that offers an exhilarating driving experience. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to repair them can be significant. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a 718 Boxster.
