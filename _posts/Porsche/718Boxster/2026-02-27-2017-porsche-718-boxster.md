# 2017 Porsche 718 Boxster Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2017 Porsche 718 Boxster, buyers can expect thrilling mid-engine performance and sports-car handling. While owner-reported complaints are sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect specific exterior, suspension, and visibility components during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Flat-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged flat-four engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, serving as the responsive base powerplant for the 718 lineup. Overall, it delivers strong performance with generally solid mechanical reliability for everyday sports car driving.

### 2.5L Turbocharged Flat-4 (S model)

Featuring variable turbine geometry, this 2.5-liter turbocharged flat-four pumps out around 350 horsepower and 309 lb-ft of torque in the S variant. It provides exhilarating acceleration while maintaining the balanced mid-engine dynamics typical of the brand.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers short, precise throws and direct mechanical engagement for enthusiast drivers. It is generally robust, provided the clutch has not been abused by previous owners.

### 7-Speed PDK Dual-Clutch Transmission

Porsche's 7-speed Doppelkupplung (PDK) automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes and smooth power delivery. Regular fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term operational durability.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Alignment

Suspension components are generally well-engineered, though owners should monitor tire wear patterns closely.

**Rear Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rear tires may experience premature baldness or accelerated wear despite proper alignment and normal driving habits.
- **Solution:** Verify suspension geometry, check tire pressure regularly, and replace tires as necessary.

## Exterior

### Visibility and Wipers

Visibility equipment generally performs well, but routine checks of wiper blades and lighting housings are advised.

**Wiper Contact and Visibility**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wipers may exhibit poor contact with the glass, resulting in reduced visibility during rainy driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiper blades for wear and replace them with quality inserts to restore proper windshield contact.

### Mirrors and Lighting Housings

Exterior body fittings are tightly assembled, though minor seal and trim issues can occasionally arise.

**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight lenses may develop foggy condensation on the interior surfaces during humid weather or after washing.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seal integrity and consult a specialist if moisture accumulation persists.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

The cabin features high-grade materials and solid construction, with only isolated reports of minor trim or fastener noise.

**Seat Belt Retractor Rattling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a loose seat belt retractor mechanism generating rattling noises while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the seat belt mounting hardware and retractor assembly for proper torque.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V113000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=718+Boxster&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, a crossmember may detach from the luggage compartment bracket, possibly causing a fuel leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will secure the crossmember with additional rivets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V054000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=718+Boxster&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will install improved fastening screws, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V946000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=718+Boxster&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The seat mounted side air bag inflator initiator may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the side air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2017 Porsche 718 Boxster is a compelling pre-owned luxury sports car backed by strong powertrain options and minimal recurring owner complaints.
