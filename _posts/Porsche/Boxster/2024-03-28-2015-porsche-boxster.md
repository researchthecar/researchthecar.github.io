**Introduction to the 2015 Porsche Boxster**

The 2015 Porsche Boxster is a compact, mid-engine sports car offered with three engine choices: a 2.7-liter flat-six producing 265 horsepower, a 3.4-liter flat-six generating 315 horsepower, and a 3.8-liter flat-six boasting 375 horsepower. Transmission options include a six-speed manual, a seven-speed PDK dual-clutch transmission, and an eight-speed Tiptronic S automatic.

Owners of the Boxster generally praise its sharp handling, responsive steering, and thrilling performance. However, some have reported certain issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

* **2.7-Liter Flat-Six**
    * **Intake Valve Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, rough idling, reduced engine performance
        * Solution: Replacement of intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.4-Liter Flat-Six**
    * **Water Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating engine
        * Solution: Replacement of water pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, check engine light
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.8-Liter Flat-Six**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual**
    * **Shift Linkage Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting into gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of shift linkage
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seven-Speed PDK**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, loss of power
        * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA 19V-334
* **Eight-Speed Tiptronic S**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, burnt smell
        * Solution: Replacement of transmission fluid cooler lines
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or knocking noises from rear suspension, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replacement of lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Suspension Alignment Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven tire wear, poor handling
    * Solution: Alignment correction
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty battery or charging system components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from HVAC system
    * Solution: Replacement of blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water or condensation inside headlight assembly
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight assembly or resealing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Handle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle becomes loose or falls off
    * Solution: Replacement of door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Frozen screen, connectivity issues, navigation errors
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks do not work properly, doors do not open or close
    * Solution: Replacement of door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2015 Porsche Boxster is a highly regarded sports car, it has experienced some issues that owners should be aware of. Some of the more common problems include engine intake valve and timing chain tensioner issues, PDK transmission mechatronic unit failure, and suspension lower control arm bushing failure. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Regular maintenance and prompt repairs can help prevent or minimize these issues and ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
