# 2017 Porsche Cayenne Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury performance SUV, understanding common owner complaints can help you evaluate a prospective purchase. While luxury models offer exceptional driving dynamics and prestige, they also feature complex electronics, specialized powertrains, and premium interior trims that require attentive upkeep. Reviewing owner feedback provides a helpful baseline for what to watch out for during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Supercharged

This supercharged V6 engine delivers smooth, responsive power for daily driving and highway cruising. Known for respectable output and decent longevity when properly maintained, it forms the baseline of the Cayenne lineup.

### 3.6L V6 Twin-Turbo

This twin-turbocharged V6 offers robust horsepower and strong mid-range torque for a more spirited driving experience. Buyers should verify regular oil change intervals and service history to ensure long-term reliability.

### 4.8L V8 Twin-Turbo

This high-output twin-turbo V8 provides formidable performance and rapid acceleration at the expense of higher fuel consumption. Maintenance costs can be substantial, making a detailed mechanical inspection essential prior to purchase.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic S Automatic

This conventional 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear changes and efficient highway cruising. It is generally robust, though timely fluid changes are recommended to maintain shift quality over high mileage.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical and lighting systems are generally advanced, though occasional weather-related sealing issues can affect visibility components.

**Headlight Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported moisture and condensation building up inside headlight housings during rain events or temperature drops, which can impair night driving visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seals for wear and have damaged or leaking housing assemblies replaced or resealed by a qualified technician.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim and Airbags

Safety equipment and structural trim generally hold up well, though cabin materials exposed to direct sunlight can experience cosmetic and fitment degradation.

**Dashboard Leather Shrinkage**
- **Symptoms:** The leather trim panel on top of the dashboard near the passenger airbag can shrink, peel, or pull away from the underlying structure.
- **Solution:** Have an upholstery specialist or dealership evaluate the dashboard panel; re-adhering or replacing the affected leather trim may be necessary to ensure proper airbag deployment pathways.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V844000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=Cayenne&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The ski bag fastening strap may have been sewn with incorrect thread, possibly resulting in the strap seams tearing and the ski bag being unsecured in the event of a crash. If the ski bag detaches during a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Porsche will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ski bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V656000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=Cayenne&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Improperly adjusted headlights may not illuminate the road properly, or cause a glare to oncoming drivers, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the headlights for sealing caps and install missing caps as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The vehicle offers a compelling blend of luxury, utility, and sporting pedigree, but prospective buyers should approach used examples with a thorough inspection mindset. Pay close attention to exterior lighting seals and interior dashboard trim condition. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a marque specialist will help uncover any latent issues before finalizing your purchase.
