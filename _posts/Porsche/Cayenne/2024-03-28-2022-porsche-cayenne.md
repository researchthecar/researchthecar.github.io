**Introduction**

The 2022 Porsche Cayenne is a high-performance luxury SUV that offers a choice of three engine options and two transmission options. The base engine is a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 with 340 horsepower and 331 pound-feet of torque, which is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Cayenne S gets a 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 with 434 horsepower and 405 pound-feet of torque, also paired with an eight-speed automatic. The range-topping Cayenne Turbo GT features a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 with 631 horsepower and 626 pound-feet of torque, which is mated to an eight-speed dual-clutch transmission.

Drivers love the Cayenne's athletic handling, powerful engines, and luxurious interior. However, some have reported issues with its reliability and high maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter turbocharged V6**

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure (Pre-2020 models)**
     - Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to jump or break, resulting in catastrophic engine damage. Symptoms include engine noise, reduced power, or an illuminated check engine light.
     - Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and tensioner is required.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $8,000+
     - Recalls: Unknown

**2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6**

- **Fuel Injector Issues**
     - Description/Symptoms: Fuel injectors can become clogged or fail, causing engine misfires, rough idling, or reduced power.
     - Solution: Replacement of the affected fuel injectors.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
     - Recalls: None Found

**4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8**

- **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure**
     - Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate can fail, causing reduced engine power and excessive exhaust smoke.
     - Solution: Replacement of the affected turbocharger.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
     - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-speed automatic**

- **Transmission Control Module Failure**
     - Description/Symptoms: The transmission control module (TCM) can fail, resulting in erratic shifting, loss of drive, or an illuminated check engine light.
     - Solution: Replacement of the TCM.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
     - Recalls: None Found

**Eight-speed dual-clutch**

- **Clutch Pack Wear**
     - Description/Symptoms: The clutch packs in the transmission can wear out prematurely, causing slippage, shuddering, or difficulty shifting gears.
     - Solution: Replacement of the clutch packs.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
     - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Air Suspension Leaks**
     - Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system can develop leaks, causing the vehicle to lose ride height or become unstable.
     - Solution: Repair of the air suspension components, including compressors, lines, or air struts.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
     - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Discharge**
     - Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience battery discharge, leaving it unable to start.
     - Solution: Inspection of the electrical system to identify the source of the discharge, such as parasitic drains.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
     - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Sunroof Panel Seal Failure**
     - Description/Symptoms: The sunroof panel seal can fail, allowing water to leak into the cabin.
     - Solution: Replacement of the sunroof panel seal.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
     - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Center Console Trim Peeling**
     - Description/Symptoms: The trim on the center console can peel or detach.
     - Solution: Replacement of the affected trim pieces.
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
     - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Porsche Cayenne has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While it offers impressive performance and luxurious appointments, it has been plagued by some significant issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can be substantial, especially for major component failures. Buyers should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a Cayenne and factor in the potential expenses associated with ownership.
