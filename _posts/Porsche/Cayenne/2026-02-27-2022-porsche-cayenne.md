# 2022 Porsche Cayenne Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Porsche Cayenne delivers exhilarating performance, sharp handling, and a luxurious cabin, making it a highly desirable option on the used luxury SUV market. While generally well-engineered, buyers should review service history and look out for isolated owner reports concerning electrical glitches and air suspension components.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

The 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 engine produces approximately 434 horsepower and 405 lb-ft of torque, powering variants like the Cayenne S with responsive acceleration. It is generally robust, though turbocharged components require diligent maintenance.

### 3.0L Turbo V6

The base 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 generates around 335 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, offering a refined and capable everyday driving experience. This powerplant is known for balanced performance and dependability when properly serviced.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The high-performance 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 churns out substantial power for GTS and Turbo trims, delivering blistering acceleration and robust capability. Buyers should anticipate higher routine maintenance and fuel costs for this sophisticated motor.

### 3.0L V6 Plug-In Hybrid

The E-Hybrid powertrain pairs a 3.0-liter V6 with an electric motor to deliver a combined output of around 455 horsepower. It provides efficient electric-only city driving alongside strong traditional performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic S Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic S automatic transmission provides smooth and quick gear changes across all trims and drivetrain configurations. It is a durable unit that pairs well with the Cayenne's diverse range of powertrain options.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension Struts

The available air suspension system provides exceptional ride comfort and adjustable height, though complex electronic dampers and air springs can occasionally experience component faults.

**Air Spring Strut Failure**
- **Symptoms:** An air suspension failure can occur while driving at highway speeds, potentially causing uneven ride height or handling instability.
- **Solution:** Inspect the air struts for leaks and replace the failed component at an authorized service center.
- **Cost:** $4,000

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Infotainment

Advanced electronics govern most of the Cayenne's cabin functions, making software reliability and sensor connections crucial for seamless daily operation.

**Intermittent Display and Camera Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may intermittently experience warning messages stating mileage cannot be recorded alongside an inoperable rearview camera.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose software modules and inspect related electrical connections or camera sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V040000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=Cayenne&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-271 (AMA9) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V896000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Porsche&model=Cayenne&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the driver assistance software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Porsche Cayenne is a thrilling and luxurious luxury SUV, but prospective buyers should inspect electronic accessories and suspension systems closely before purchasing. While owner-reported issues tend to be isolated, verifying dealer service records and obtaining a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
