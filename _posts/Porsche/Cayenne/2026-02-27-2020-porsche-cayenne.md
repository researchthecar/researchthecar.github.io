### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Porsche Cayenne demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its powertrain components. The V6 and V8 engines, along with the Tiptronic S transmission, are robust and generally free from significant issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are related to the adaptive air suspension, which can develop leaks over time, and occasional electrical gremlins within the infotainment system or intermittent battery drain. These issues, while not pervasive, are worth noting. Overall, the Cayenne offers a dependable luxury SUV experience, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential suspension or electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
