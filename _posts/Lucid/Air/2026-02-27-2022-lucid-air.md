# 2022 Lucid Air Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lucid Air is an impressive luxury electric sedan offering groundbreaking range, performance, and cutting-edge technology. However, as an early production year for a brand-new automaker, buyers should approach the used market with a healthy dose of caution. Early owner reports highlight significant software glitches, complex electronic sensor misfires, and unique supply chain hurdles regarding specialized hardware like high-load tires.

---

## Engine

### Dual-Motor AWD Electric Powertrain

The dual-motor all-wheel-drive configuration delivers immense power ranging from roughly 620 to over 1,000 horsepower depending on the trim level. It provides rapid acceleration and strong efficiency, though early production units have experienced software-related propulsion glitches.

### Single-Motor RWD Electric Powertrain

The single-motor rear-wheel-drive setup serves as the entry-level propulsion option, offering balanced power and optimized cruising range. While mechanically straightforward, it relies heavily on the vehicle's overarching electronic and software architecture.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

The single-speed direct-drive transmission delivers seamless, gearless power delivery across all trim levels and drivetrains. It is mechanically simple and generally robust, though electronic logic controls can occasionally produce unexpected directional engagement complaints.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Specialized Tires

The air suspension and chassis provide a refined ride quality, but the factory wheel and tire packages demand careful attention due to unique load specifications and limited aftermarket availability.

**Premature Tire Failure and Replacement Shortages**
- **Symptoms:** The specialized high-load rating tires (such as 21-inch HLC variants) have proven susceptible to sidewall bubbling, large gashes from minor road bumps, and blowouts, compounded by limited replacement availability.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged tires with manufacturer-approved high-load alternatives when available, and carefully inspect tire sidewalls during routine maintenance.

## Steering

### Power Adjustable Seats

The cabin features luxurious materials and advanced digital interfaces, though early electronics governing interior conveniences can occasionally experience unresponsive touchscreen controls or erratic component behavior.

**Unsolicited Seat Movement**
- **Symptoms:** Front seats have spontaneously adjusted forward or tilted aggressively without driver input, occasionally pinning passengers against the dashboard or steering wheel while the touchscreen becomes unresponsive.
- **Solution:** Use the manual override buttons located on the side of the seat to reposition it, and consult a dealer to check for seat control module software faults.

## Electrical

### Software and Drive System Electronics

The vehicle's heavy reliance on complex electronic architecture and frequent over-the-air updates has led to various reported software bugs, intermittent accessory failures, and powertrain management anomalies.

**Sudden Loss of Motive Power and System Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported dashboard warnings such as 'System Failure' or 'Drive System Fault' resulting in a sudden, unexpected loss of power while driving at highway speeds or when reversing out of a driveway.
- **Solution:** Requires dealership diagnostic evaluation, potential inverter or control module updates, and occasionally complete system reboots or hardware replacements.

**Spontaneous Acceleration and Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced erratic vehicle behavior, including unexpected sudden acceleration from a stop or severe, unprompted emergency braking events triggered by misjudged traffic or obstacles.
- **Solution:** Software updates deployed by the manufacturer aim to refine driver-assistance calibrations, though persistent sensor errors may require service inspection.

**[Recall 22V351000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lucid&model=Air&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The display wiring harness may not be secured properly, which can disable the display screens. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 101, "Control and Displays." Disabled display screens will not show critical information, such as the speedometer, gear selection indicators, and warning.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and secure the wiring harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V727000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lucid&model=Air&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached tow eye bracket can separate the vehicle from the tow truck, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lucid Service Centers will inspect the subframe and add rivets to the tow eye bracket as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V110000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Fuses, Relays, Contacts, And Shunts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lucid&model=Air&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The electrically activated contact switches that transfer energy to the drive motors, may unexpectedly open, cutting off power to the electric motors. Power loss will shut down electric motors, causing a loss of drive power and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lucid Service Centers will update the vehicle software and replace the contactor switches, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V520000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lucid&model=Air&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display while in reverse decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V521000: Visibility/Wiper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lucid&model=Air&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 104, "Windshield Wiping and Washing Systems." Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lucid Service Centers will inspect, tighten the wiper arm nuts, and replace damaged parts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V523000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lucid&model=Air&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Software versions 2.0.64 and older may not respond properly to an inverter power module failure, which can result in a loss of drive power without warning. A loss of drive power without warning increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V708000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly:Seat Heater/Cooler](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lucid&model=Air&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Under certain conditions, temperature monitoring for the rear seat heaters could become inoperative and result in the rear seats overheating. Overheated rear seats can increase the risk of a burn injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lucid Air is a marvel of electric vehicle engineering, but its status as a first-model-year luxury car means buyers should expect some growing pains. Prospective owners should verify that all software updates have been successfully applied and thoroughly inspect the condition and availability of the specialized tires. Prioritizing vehicles with documented service history and remaining warranty coverage can help mitigate early-production electronic risks.
