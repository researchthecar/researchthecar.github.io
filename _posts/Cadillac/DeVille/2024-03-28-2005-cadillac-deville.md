**Introduction:**

The 2005 Cadillac DeVille was a full-size luxury sedan offered with two engine and transmission combinations. The base engine was a 4.6L Northstar V8 with a 4-speed automatic transmission, while the performance-oriented DTS model came with a 5.1L Northstar V8 and a 5-speed automatic transmission. Despite its premium features and opulent interior, the DeVille had several common issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**4.6L Northstar V8**

- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, rough idle
  - Solution: Replace the intake manifold gaskets
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**5.1L Northstar V8**

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise during startup, reduced engine power
  - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

- **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, transmission slipping
  - Solution: Replace the shift solenoid
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Fluid Pan Leak:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears
  - Solution: Replace the transmission fluid pan
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Strut Mount Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise over bumps, reduced handling
  - Solution: Replace the strut mounts
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Headlamp Switch Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working intermittently or sporadically
  - Solution: Replace the headlamp switch
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Battery Draining Issue:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, parasitic draw
  - Solution: Check and repair the source of the parasitic draw (e.g., alternator, lights)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the draw
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Door Lock Mechanism Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly
  - Solution: Replace the door lock actuator
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Center Console Lid Malfunction:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Lid does not open or close properly
  - Solution: Repair or replace the center console lid hinge
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

- **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: HVAC system not blowing air, noisy fan
  - Solution: Replace the blower motor
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2005 Cadillac DeVille had a range of issues that could affect various components of the vehicle. The most significant engine and transmission issues were the timing chain tensioner failure and shift solenoid failure, respectively. Suspension problems and electrical issues were also common, though they were generally less severe. Interior and exterior issues were mostly minor in nature but could still be problematic for owners. Overall, the 2005 Cadillac DeVille was a comfortable and luxurious sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of the possible issues listed above.
