**2000 Cadillac DeVille**

The 2000 Cadillac DeVille is a full-size luxury sedan that offers a comfortable ride and a spacious cabin. It comes standard with a 3.6L V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission, but a 4.6L V8 engine and a 5-speed automatic transmission are optional. The DeVille is known for its smooth handling, elegant styling, and luxurious features, but it has also been plagued by some reliability issues.

**Engine**
**3.6L V6**

**Northstar V8 Intake Manifold Bolt Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Intake manifold bolts can fail, causing a loss of coolant and engine overheating.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold bolts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: NHTSA 00V135000

**Transmission**
**4-Speed Automatic**

**Delayed or No Engagement**
Description/Symptoms: Failure of the TCC solenoid or torque converter clutch can lead to delayed or no gear engagement.
Solution: Replace the TCC solenoid or torque converter clutch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
Solution: Repair the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Strut Mount Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The strut mounts can fail, causing the wheels to lose alignment and the vehicle to pull to one side.
Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Headlight Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The headlights may fail to turn on or produce dim lighting.
Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rear Spoiler Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: The plastic rear spoiler can crack during extreme weather conditions.
Solution: Replace the rear spoiler.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Radio Reception Issues**
Description/Symptoms: AM/FM radio reception can be weak or distorted.
Solution: Replace the antenna mast.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2000 Cadillac DeVille offers a luxurious driving experience, it has also been plagued by a number of reliability issues that can be expensive to fix. The most common problems include engine intake manifold bolt failure, transmission slipping, and strut mount failure. If you are considering purchasing a 2000 Cadillac DeVille, be sure to budget for potential repairs.
