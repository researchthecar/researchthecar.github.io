# 2021 Cadillac XT5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Cadillac XT5, buyers will find a stylish and comfortable luxury crossover with modern technology and smooth road manners. While generally well-received, owner-reported complaints highlight a few areas worth monitoring during a pre-purchase inspection, particularly regarding electrical glitches and occasional powertrain or engine accessory concerns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering solid efficiency for daily commuting. It provides responsive low-end power, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history during a pre-purchase inspection.

### 3.6L V6

The 3.6L V6 engine delivers 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration and confident passing power. It is generally robust, though isolated owner reports note potential head gasket leaks and unusual acceleration behavior.

**Head Gasket Oil Leak**
- **Symptoms:** An oil leak discovered during routine maintenance, potentially occurring without an immediate dashboard warning light.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect the engine bay for leaks and replace the head gasket if failure is confirmed.

**Acceleration and Surging Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual engine revving sounds while accelerating or a sensation that the engine is pushing against the brakes when stopping.
- **Solution:** Inspect powertrain control modules, throttle body operation, and braking systems to ensure proper synchronization.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options to deliver smooth gear shifts and optimized fuel economy across highway and city driving. It operates reliably under normal conditions, but a thorough test drive is recommended to check for smooth engagement.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Audio System

Electrical and infotainment glitches can occasionally occur, impacting screens and audio chimes.

**Infotainment Screen Blackout and Audio Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment display goes black while driving, or the vehicle starts with no audio, turn signal, or seatbelt warning chimes.
- **Solution:** Restart the vehicle or have the dealer check for software updates and module resets to restore proper function.

## Safety

### Airbags and Restraints

Safety systems generally protect occupants well, but sensor and airbag warning lights require prompt diagnostic attention.

**Safety Restraint Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag or safety restraint warning light illuminates on the instrument cluster and persists after initial reset attempts.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a qualified service center to diagnose sensor faults and replace faulty airbag components if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V422000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Improperly attached and seated fuel supply lines may loosen or separate, causing a fuel leak and increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace fuel supply lines as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V709000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative rearview camera can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the coaxial cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Cadillac XT5 offers a comfortable ride and upscale cabin amenities that appeal to used luxury crossover buyers. While owner complaints point to occasional electrical anomalies and isolated engine concerns, a careful pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electronics and powertrain can help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
