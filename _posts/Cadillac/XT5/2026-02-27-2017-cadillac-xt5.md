# 2017 Cadillac XT5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Cadillac XT5 offers a stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and plenty of modern features, making it a popular choice for used luxury crossover buyers. However, prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle carefully, as owner reports highlight recurring mechanical and electrical grievances. Familiarizing yourself with these common trouble spots can help you navigate your purchase and budget for potential repairs down the road.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing around 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, this model provides responsive performance for everyday driving. While generally capable, owners frequently report cooling system issues centered around the thermostat.

**Thermostat Failure & Steering Assist Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light, P0128 fault code, and a warning message stating 'Steering assist is reduced, drive with care.'
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty thermostat and clear associated diagnostic codes.
- **Cost:** $700

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive, this setup delivers smooth cruising when operating properly. However, some owners have reported premature wear, fluid leaks, and hesitation issues.

**Transmission Shudder and Shifting Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh gear engagement, transmission kicking, juddering or shuddering during acceleration, and service transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid levels, update control modules, or perform internal transmission repairs/replacement if component wear is severe.

## Brakes

### Brake Lines and Parking Brake

The braking system provides firm stopping power under normal conditions, though isolated reports indicate potential durability concerns with brake lines and parking brake engagement.

**Brake Line Fracture & Parking Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brake fluid leaks, fractured rear brake lines, or failure of the electronic parking brake to hold the vehicle securely on inclines.
- **Solution:** Replace fractured brake lines and service or replace the electronic parking brake actuator.

## Electrical

### Electrical and HVAC Controls

electrical issues can impact accessory systems, climate control modules, and auxiliary electronics, requiring thorough pre-purchase checks.

**Loss of HVAC Control**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent failure of climate control functions, affecting window defrosting and compromising visibility in rainy weather.
- **Solution:** Reprogram or replace the HVAC control module according to updated service procedures.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim hold up reasonably well, but the optional panoramic sunroof has been subject to rare instances of spontaneous glass fracture.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud explosion-like sound followed by the panoramic glass roof shattering unexpectedly during normal highway driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the panoramic glass assembly and clean glass debris from the interior cabin.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V802000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If there is a brake fluid leak, braking performance may be reduced, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front brake calipers, replacing the brake caliper assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Cadillac XT5 offers luxury appeal and strong performance, but used-car shoppers should approach with caution. Be sure to thoroughly test drive the vehicle to check for transmission hesitation, verify that cooling and heating systems function properly, and inspect for any history of electrical or sunroof issues before finalizing your purchase.
