# 2017 Cadillac XT5

### Overview
The 2017 Cadillac XT5 is a luxury crossover SUV that generally offers a comfortable ride and a well-appointed interior. As with any vehicle, there are specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear. We will examine the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, exterior, and interior components to provide a comprehensive reliability overview for this model year.

---


## Engine

### 3.6L V6
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell, low coolant level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (8L45/8L90)
**Hesitation/Shuddering**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky acceleration, rough shifting, shuddering during gear changes, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and refill, software update, or internal transmission repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light, burning smell, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Transmission cooler flush or replacement, internal transmission inspection and repair.
- **Cost:** $600 - $2000

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes or reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update, system reset, or replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**AC/Heater Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent temperature, weak airflow, unusual noises from vents.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of blower motor, blend door actuators, or AC compressor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


### Seat Actuators
**Power Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats fail to move forward, backward, or adjust height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat motor or control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Cadillac XT5 presents a mixed reliability profile. The 3.6L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are the primary areas of concern, with reported issues including oil and coolant leaks, as well as transmission shuddering and overheating. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, also require attention. While the suspension and exterior components appear to be relatively robust, interior issues like climate control malfunctions and power seat problems have been noted. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor in potential repair costs for these specific components.
