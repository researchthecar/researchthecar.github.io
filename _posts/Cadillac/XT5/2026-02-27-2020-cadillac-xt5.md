**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, vehicle not starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory), repair or replace faulty component.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

The 2020 Cadillac XT5 generally exhibits good reliability, with its powertrain and chassis components proving robust.
