# 2022 Cadillac XT5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Cadillac XT5 brings luxury style and versatile utility to the midsize crossover segment. As with any modern luxury vehicle equipped with advanced electronics and powertrain management systems, owners have noted a few recurring quirks and mechanical concerns. Prospective buyers should review these reported owner experiences to ensure a thorough inspection before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient power delivery for daily driving. It provides a balanced entry point for the XT5 lineup with generally steady operation.

### 3.6L V6

The available 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for highway passing and towing. Some owners have reported minor powertrain shudders tied to its active fuel management system.

**Engine Shudder Under Load**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle shudders during driving or slight acceleration, potentially linked to cylinder deactivation transitions.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the engine management and fuel/propulsion control systems to diagnose potential vibration sources.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The nine-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. While generally smooth, some drivers have reported intermittent shifting hesitation or mechanical noises.

**Transmission Jerking and Clunking**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission jerks when starting to move or exhibits excessive play and clunking sounds while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission mounts, fluid condition, and control modules for wear or software updates.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Drive Mode Controls

The electrical system manages advanced cabin technology, safety features, and customizable drive modes. Occasional glitches have been reported by owners regarding screen resets and mode selection.

**Dashboard Resets and Unintended Mode Switching**
- **Symptoms:** The digital dashboard cuts off and resets while driving, and drive modes may change unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Check for applicable software updates and inspect electrical connections or control modules.

### Back-Over Prevention Camera

Driver assistance features enhance situational awareness, though occasional visibility and sensor complaints have been documented by vehicle owners.

**Faint Camera Guidelines**
- **Symptoms:** Backup camera guide lines appear very faint and are difficult to see while reversing.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera lens, display settings, and rearview camera hardware for proper calibration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V427000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The left-rear suspension toe link may have been tightened improperly, which could result in a loose or separated toe link. A loose toe link can separate and cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the left-rear suspension and replace the fastener if it is loose or missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V446000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The fuel tank rollover valve may not provide a proper seal in the event of a rollover crash. A fuel tank rollover valve that does not properly seal can result in a fuel leak during a rollover crash, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the fuel tank and replace the fuel tank shell if the rollover valve is found to be dislodged or missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V172000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the sun gears, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Cadillac XT5 offers a comfortable ride and upscale cabin amenities, making it an attractive used luxury choice. Buyers should conduct a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on transmission shift quality, electrical electronics, and powertrain operation to ensure optimal condition.
