# 2024 Cadillac XT5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Cadillac XT5 balances luxury appeal with everyday utility, but prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding powertrain performance. While NHTSA complaints reflect individual experiences rather than guaranteed factory defects, early reports indicate some attention is needed around engine responsiveness and transmission reliability. Checking service histories and conducting thorough test drives are recommended steps before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This base engine produces roughly 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering standard power for daily driving. It provides reasonable efficiency, though buyers should monitor for any early hesitation or idle vibrations reported in owner feedback.

### 3.6L V6

The optional 3.6-liter V6 delivers 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque for robust acceleration. While generally smooth, some early-production owner reports highlight intermittent power loss and responsiveness concerns.

**Intermittent Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected hesitation, loss of motive power at highway speeds, and abnormal engine vibrations accompanied by a check engine light.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis by a certified dealer to inspect internal engine components, clutches, seals, or software calibrations.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with both engine options, the 9-speed automatic transmission handles gear selection across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive trims. Some early complaints cite shifting irregularities and mechanical failures requiring dealer intervention.

**Erratic Shifting and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced delayed shifting in lower gears, lurching, bucking, sudden loud racing noises, and total loss of forward drive requiring towing.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission assembly, ring gear, and internal hydraulic components for mechanical failure.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Covers exterior body panels, glass elements, and related trim components.

**Sunroof Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced a loud popping sound followed by sudden shattering or cracking of the rear panoramic sunroof glass during normal highway driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for stress cracks or impact damage and replace the shattered glass panel.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Monitors supplemental restraint systems and occupant protection mechanisms across the cabin.

**Inadvertent Air Bag Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** The driver-side seat air bag has been reported to deploy unexpectedly without prior warning or active collision indicators.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to an authorized dealer to inspect the supplemental restraint system sensors and wiring harness.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V681000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A windshield that is not properly bonded may detach during a crash and increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the windshield and apply primer and urethane sealant. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V311000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver's front air bag cushion may tear during deployment, resulting in the air bag not deploying as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's front air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V459000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The instrument panel may not illuminate when the hazard warning lights, high beams, or turn signals are activated. Equipment." Without the illuminated reminder, the driver could fail to turn off the lights when no longer needed.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Cadillac XT5 offers refined luxury and capable performance options, but prospective used-car buyers should pay close attention to powertrain behavior during a test drive. While isolated complaints involving transmission shifting irregularities and unexpected electrical or glass issues have surfaced, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection can help ensure any underlying concerns are identified.
