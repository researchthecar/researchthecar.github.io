**Introduction:**

The 2022 Cadillac CT4 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience with its powerful engine options and sharp handling. It comes with a standard 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine or an optional 3.6-liter naturally aspirated V6. Both engines are mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. The CT4 has been praised for its impressive performance, stylish design, and luxurious interior. However, some owners have reported some issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Faulty Turbocharger:**
    * **Description:** The turbocharger may fail prematurely, causing reduced engine power and increased oil consumption.
    * **Symptoms:** Loss of power, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
    * **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Timing Chain Noise:**
    * **Description:** The timing chain may become noisy, indicating a potential problem.
    * **Symptoms:** Rattle or ticking noise from the engine.
    * **Solution:** Replace the timing chain.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**3.6L Naturally Aspirated V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * **Description:** The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifts, causing hesitation and decreased performance.
    * **Symptoms:** Hard shifts, slipping gears, check engine light.
    * **Solution:** Update the transmission software or replace the transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure:**
    * **Description:** The front struts may fail prematurely, causing a loss of control and decreased ride quality.
    * **Symptoms:** Vehicle pulls to one side, uneven tire wear, clunking noise.
    * **Solution:** Replace the struts.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Battery Sensor:**
    * **Description:** The battery sensor may fail, causing the vehicle to not charge properly or not start at all.
    * **Symptoms:** Battery warning light, difficulty starting, decreased battery life.
    * **Solution:** Replace the battery sensor.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $150 - $300+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Headlight Malfunction:**
    * **Description:** The headlights may malfunction, resulting in decreased visibility and safety concerns.
    * **Symptoms:** Dim or flickering headlights, cut-out headlights.
    * **Solution:** Replace the headlights.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Paint Peeling:**
    * **Description:** The paint on the exterior may peel prematurely, affecting the appearance of the vehicle.
    * **Symptoms:** Loss of paint, exposed metal or primer.
    * **Solution:** Repaint the affected area.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Loose Trim Pieces:**
    * **Description:** Exterior trim pieces, such as molding or badges, may become loose or fall off.
    * **Symptoms:** Missing trim pieces, rattling or loose parts.
    * **Solution:** Reattach or replace the trim pieces.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * **Description:** The infotainment system may experience freezes, lags, or other performance issues.
    * **Symptoms:** Slow response, frozen screens, connectivity problems.
    * **Solution:** Reset or update the infotainment system.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $0 (if software update) - $500+ (if hardware issue)
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Cracked Dashboard:**
    * **Description:** The dashboard may develop cracks or defects prematurely.
    * **Symptoms:** Visible cracks or blemishes on the dashboard.
    * **Solution:** Replace the dashboard.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Cadillac CT4 is a capable and enjoyable vehicle. However, some owners have encountered a range of issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical malfunctions. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to more substantial. It's important to consider these potential issues when evaluating the CT4 and to make an informed decision before purchasing.
