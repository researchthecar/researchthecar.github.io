**Introduction:**

The 2021 Cadillac CT4 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a sporty driving experience. It is available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder with 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.6-liter V6 with 335 horsepower and 284 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an eight-speed automatic and a six-speed manual.

Overall, the CT4 is praised for its sharp handling, luxurious interior, and potent engine options. However, it has not been immune to certain issues that have affected owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

- **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** A faulty valve cover gasket can lead to oil leaks, engine noise, and potential engine damage.
  - Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
  - Recalls: None Found

- **PCV Valve Failure:** The positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve can fail, causing increased oil consumption, rough idling, and poor engine performance.
  - Solution: Replace the PCV valve.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

- **Timing Chain Noise:** Some early models experienced timing chain noise due to a faulty tensioner.
  - Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

- **Jerky Shifting:** Occasional jerky or delayed shifts can be experienced under certain driving conditions.
  - Solution: Update the transmission software.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
  - Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Manual**

- **Hard Shifting:** Owners have reported difficulties shifting gears, particularly into the second and sixth gears.
  - Solution: Replace the faulty transmission.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

- **Battery Discharge:** Some vehicles experienced premature battery discharge due to a faulty alternator or parasitic drain.
  - Solution: Replace the defective alternator or identify and fix the drain.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

- **Infotainment System Glitches:** Occasional glitches with the infotainment system, including freezing, unresponsive screens, and connectivity issues.
  - Solution: Update the software or replace the unit.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Rear Seat Noise:** Excessive noise from the rear seat area has been noticed by some owners.
  - Solution: Inspect and tighten or replace loose components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Cadillac CT4 is a capable vehicle with impressive performance and comfort. However, it has not been completely free of issues, including potential engine and transmission problems. While some issues are relatively minor and cost-effective to fix, others can be more severe and expensive to repair. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
