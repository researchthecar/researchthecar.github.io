# 2024 Cadillac CT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Cadillac CT4 delivers sharp handling and athletic styling, appealing to shoppers looking for a compact luxury sport sedan. While generally modern and well-equipped, owners have reported a few isolated mechanical and body concerns that prospective buyers should keep on their radar during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive daily performance. It is generally reliable, though owners should monitor for early-mileage fluid leaks.

**Camshaft Carrier Oil Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks developing from the camshaft carrier reseal area, often discovered during routine maintenance.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a qualified service center to have the camshaft carrier properly resealed.

**Abnormal Oil Burning Odor**
- **Symptoms:** An abnormal burning oil odor detected around the exterior of the vehicle while parking, without any corresponding dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a technician inspect the engine bay for minor fluid leaks or gasket seepage.

### 2.7L Turbocharged Inline-4

The available 2.7-liter turbocharged engine pushes output to around 310 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque in standard form, offering stronger acceleration for enthusiast drivers. It pairs with an upgraded automatic transmission for smooth power delivery.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6

Reserved for high-performance variants like the CT4-V, the 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 generates up to 325 horsepower or more depending on the specific tune. It provides robust acceleration and high-speed capability for spirited driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the base 2.0L engine to deliver smooth, efficient shifts in daily driving. It is generally dependable when regular fluid maintenance intervals are observed.

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission manages power for higher-output engine options, offering quick gear changes and optimized cruising efficiency. Overall reliability has been steady in contemporary GM applications.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission is available on select enthusiast-focused trims for a more connected driving experience. While engaging, some owners have reported mechanical shifting friction.

**Gear Engagement Difficulty**
- **Symptoms:** Shifting from 1st to 2nd gear can occasionally feel difficult, resulting in partial gear engagement, grinding noises, or popping out of gear when releasing the clutch.
- **Solution:** Inspect the clutch linkage, shift linkage, and transmission synchronizers for wear or misalignment.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Exterior panels and glass are generally well-assembled, though occasional optical imperfections in factory glass have been noted by early owners.

**Factory Windshield Distortion**
- **Symptoms:** Optical distortions or ripples present within the glass, affecting outward visibility.
- **Solution:** Have the windshield inspected by a dealership or glass specialist for possible replacement under warranty.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Cadillac CT4 is an engaging luxury sport sedan with solid powertrain choices, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough inspection. Check the engine bay for any signs of oil seepage and ensure the transmission—especially manual variants—shifts smoothly during a test drive.
