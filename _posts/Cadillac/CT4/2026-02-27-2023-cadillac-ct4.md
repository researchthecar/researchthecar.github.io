# 2023 Cadillac CT4

### Overview
The 2023 Cadillac CT4, a compact luxury sedan, aims to blend sporty performance with upscale comfort. It is offered with a choice of turbocharged four-cylinder engines and a rear-wheel-drive biased platform, with all-wheel drive available. While generally well-received for its driving dynamics and premium interior, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that warrant attention from a reliability standpoint. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Cadillac CT4 generally presents a strong reliability profile, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The primary area of concern noted is the infotainment system, which can occasionally experience software glitches requiring resets or updates. Other categories such as suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from reported widespread technical faults. Owners should be aware of potential infotainment quirks, but overall, the CT4 appears to be a dependable luxury sedan for its model year, provided regular maintenance is performed.
