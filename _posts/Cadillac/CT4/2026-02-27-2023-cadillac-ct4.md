# 2023 Cadillac CT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Cadillac CT4 is a nimble and engaging compact luxury sport sedan that brings sharp handling and distinctive styling to the segment. For prospective used-car buyers, it offers modern tech and a driver-focused cabin, but early owner feedback highlights a few noteworthy powertrain and electrical concerns to keep on your radar.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged engine produces 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced mix of efficiency and everyday pep. It generally serves as a dependable daily driver powerplant, though isolated owner reports mention severe internal failures.

### 2.7L Turbocharged Inline-4

The larger 2.7-liter turbocharged engine boosts output to 310 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque (higher in the V-Series), delivering significantly punchier acceleration. It provides strong performance credentials for enthusiasts, while maintaining standard luxury car composure.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

Exclusive to the high-performance CT4-V Blackwing, this twin-turbo V6 pushes out a robust 472 horsepower for track-ready capabilities. It is a thrilling, highly specialized powerhouse built for enthusiast drivers seeking maximum performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired primarily with the 2.0-liter engine, this 8-speed automatic aims to balance smooth cruising with responsive gear changes. Some owners have reported low-speed shift hesitation.

**Transmission Hesitation and Engine Racing**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden engine revving and transmission hesitation during low-speed maneuvers, such as shifting into drive and accelerating out of a driveway.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the transmission control module and powertrain software for potential calibration updates.

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

Found behind the 2.7-liter engine, this 10-speed automatic provides a wide gear ratio spread to optimize both fuel economy and power delivery. It generally offers smooth operation during highway cruising.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

Available exclusively on the CT4-V Blackwing, this 6-speed manual gives purists direct control over the twin-turbo V6's power delivery. It is a robust, enthusiast-oriented gearbox revered for its mechanical engagement.

### Electrical and Powertrain Management

The electrical architecture governs critical safety and powertrain systems, though rare owner complaints point to sudden system failures.

**Unexpected Engine Shutdown and Dashboard Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall or shut down completely while driving, accompanied by multiple dashboard warning lights and a sudden loss of responsiveness.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation at a certified dealership to identify faulty electrical modules or engine management components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V903000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT4&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The daytime running lights (DRLs) may not deactivate when the headlights are on. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." DRLs that do not deactivate as intended may result in a glare, reducing visibility, and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Cadillac CT4 presents an appealing option for buyers seeking sharp driving dynamics and distinct American luxury. While most examples run smoothly, used-car shoppers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history records and perform a comprehensive test drive to check for transmission hesitation or electrical anomalies.
