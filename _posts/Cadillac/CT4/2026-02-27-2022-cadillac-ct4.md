# 2022 Cadillac CT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Cadillac CT4 is a compact luxury sport sedan known for its sharp handling, aggressive styling, and engaging powertrain options. While it offers an impressive driving experience, used car buyers should review owner-reported electrical and braking system behavior to ensure proper vehicle operation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides a balanced blend of efficiency and daily drivability for entry-level trims.

### 2.7L Turbocharged I4

The available 2.7-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates up to 310 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust mid-range power and energetic performance across upscale trims.

### 3.6L Twin-Turbocharged V6

Exclusive to the high-performance CT4-V Blackwing, this 3.6-liter twin-turbo V6 pushes out 472 horsepower and 445 lb-ft of torque. It is a track-capable powerhouse paired with specialized performance hardware.

**Electrical and Brake Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** While driving at highway or street speeds, the dashboard may suddenly illuminate with warning lights, accompanied by a loss of power steering, brake assist pressure, and entry into limp mode.
- **Solution:** Restarting the vehicle may temporarily clear the faults, but owners should have diagnostic trouble codes scanned by a qualified technician to identify underlying electronic or sensor failures.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the base 2.0L engine to deliver smooth and efficient gear shifts in rear-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations.

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission works alongside the 2.7L engine to offer responsive shifting and optimized highway cruising capability.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

Available exclusively on the CT4-V Blackwing, this 6-speed manual transmission provides a direct, highly engaging shifting experience for enthusiast drivers.

## Safety

### Airbags

The CT4 features a comprehensive suite of advanced airbags designed to protect occupants, though rare incident reports highlight concerns regarding proper deployment during complex crashes.

**Airbag Non-Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** In significant multi-impact collisions, supplemental restraint system airbags may fail to deploy as expected upon impact.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's airbag control module and impact sensors thoroughly inspected by a certified repair facility following any collision.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V611000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The roof rail side-curtain air bags may not have been installed correctly, which could result in an improper air bag deployment during a crash. Air bags that do not deploy properly can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect both left and right-side roof rail air bags and reinstall the air bags, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V903000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The daytime running lights (DRLs) may not deactivate when the headlights are on. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." DRLs that do not deactivate as intended may result in a glare, reducing visibility, and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Cadillac CT4 delivers engaging performance and distinct luxury appeal, particularly in its high-output trims. Prospective buyers should carefully inspect vehicle electronics and braking systems during a test drive to ensure no intermittent dashboard warnings or assist failures are present.
