# 2023 Cadillac Escalade ESV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Cadillac Escalade ESV offers luxury and massive hauling capacity, but buyers should review potential powertrain and electrical concerns. Owner reports indicate occasional transmission and engine issues that warrant careful pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L V8 Engine

The standard 6.2-liter V8 engine produces 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for this large SUV, though some owners report unexpected stalling incidents.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly lose motive power on the highway or experience unexpected engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the powertrain control module and fuel delivery system for underlying faults.

### 3.0L Duramax Turbo-Diesel I6

The available 3.0-liter turbo-diesel inline-six produces 277 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, offering enhanced fuel economy and strong low-end torque for towing.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission is paired with both engine options and manages power delivery across rear-wheel and four-wheel-drive configurations, though early valve body wear has been noted by some drivers.

**Valve Body and Shifting Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Unintended neutral shifts, harsh jerking when shifting into reverse, and an inability to accelerate or shift gears properly.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the transmission valve body; replacement or internal component repair may be necessary.

## Suspension

### Suspension and Tires

The adaptive suspension delivers a plush ride quality typical of flagship luxury SUVs, but proper maintenance and periodic wheel alignments are essential to preserve tire life.

**Premature Tire Wear and Alignment Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rapid tread wear on factory tires accompanied by alignment damage requiring premature replacement.
- **Solution:** Perform a professional wheel alignment check and inspect suspension components for proper geometry.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Camera System

The advanced electronics and digital display arrays provide excellent visibility and control, but software glitches can temporarily affect auxiliary camera features.

**Missing Backup Camera Gridlines**
- **Symptoms:** Backup and forward camera gridlines become unavailable, appearing grayed out with a constant hourglass icon.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a dealership, as infotainment bugs frequently require a system reflash.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V903000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The daytime running lights (DRLs) may not deactivate when the headlights are on. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." DRLs that do not deactivate as intended may result in a glare, reducing visibility, and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V642000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The rear suspension outer control arm bolts may not have been properly heat-treated, which can cause the bolts to break. A broken suspension bolt can result in misalignment of the rear wheel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace both rear suspension outer control arm bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V674000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Brake Fluid Low Warning: Lamp](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Vehicle Safety Standard number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." Without a warning light, the vehicle may be driven with low brake fluid, which can reduce braking performance and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V274000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the engine, and as necessary, repair or replace the engine. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2023 Cadillac Escalade ESV, prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the transmission operation and electrical systems. While it remains a supremely comfortable and capable luxury cruiser, verifying that all software updates and mechanical recalls have been completed will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
