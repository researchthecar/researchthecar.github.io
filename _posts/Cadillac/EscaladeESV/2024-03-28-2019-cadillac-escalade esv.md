## 2019 Cadillac Escalade ESV

The 2019 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV with seating for up to eight passengers. It offers a powerful V8 engine and a smooth-shifting transmission, along with a spacious and well-equipped interior. However, the vehicle has received complaints regarding certain components.

### Engine
**6.2L V8**
- **Excessive Oil Consumption**: The engine may consume excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage. Requires engine rebuild or replacement. **$3,500 - $9,000+**
- **Timing Chain Failure**: The timing chain may prematurely fail, causing engine damage. Requires timing chain replacement. **$1,500 - $3,000**
- **Engine Misfire**: The engine may experience misfires due to faulty spark plugs or ignition coils. Requires spark plug or ignition coil replacement. **$100 - $500**

### Transmission
**10-Speed Automatic**
- **Shifting Delays**: The transmission may exhibit delayed shifts or harsh engagements. Requires transmission software update or replacement. **$150 - $1,000**
- **Driveline Vibrations**: The driveline may vibrate excessively during acceleration or deceleration. Requires driveshaft replacement. **$500 - $1,500**

### Suspension
- **Front Suspension Clunking**: A clunking noise may be present in the front suspension when driving over uneven roads. Requires replacement of ball joints or struts. **$500 - $1,000**
- **Rear Suspension Sagging**: The rear suspension may sag over time, affecting handling and ride quality. Requires replacement of springs or shocks. **$400 - $800**

### Electrical
- **Battery Drain**: The battery may drain prematurely due to a parasitic drain. Requires identification and repair of faulty electrical components. **$200 - $600**
- **Electrical Short**: An electrical short may occur, causing loss of power or malfunctioning of electrical components. Requires diagnosis and repair of faulty wiring or components. **Varies depending on issue**

### Exterior
- **Rear Door Alignment**: The rear doors may not align properly, resulting in gaps or difficulty closing. Requires door panel adjustment or replacement. **$200 - $500**

### Interior
- **Sunroof Leaks**: The sunroof may leak during heavy rain, causing water to enter the cabin. Requires sunroof seal or drain tube replacement. **$300 - $1,000**
- **Climate Control Issues**: The climate control system may malfunction, leading to improper heating or cooling. Requires replacement of sensors or control module. **$200 - $600**
- **Rear Seatbelt Recall**: Recall #20V334000: Rear seatbelts may not lock in place during a crash. Requires replacement of rear seatbelts. **None**

### Summary

The 2019 Cadillac Escalade ESV has received complaints regarding certain components, particularly its engine and transmission. These issues range in severity and cost, with some being relatively minor and others potentially more expensive.
