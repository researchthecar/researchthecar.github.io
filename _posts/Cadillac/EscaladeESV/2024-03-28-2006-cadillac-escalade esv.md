**Introduction:**

The 2006 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV known for its spacious interior, powerful V8 engines, and advanced features. Its engine lineup includes two options: a 6.0L V8 with 345 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.2L V8 with 403 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque. Both are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Drivers appreciate its comfortable ride, towing capabilities, and luxurious amenities but may find its fuel economy and handling lacking.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Symptoms include coolant leaks, overheating, and reduced engine power. Solution involves replacing the gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Oil Pressure Sensor Failure:** Symptoms include inaccurate oil pressure readings or warning lights. Solution involves replacing the sensor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**6.2L V8**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:** Symptoms include shuddering or vibration during acceleration or gear changes. Solution involves replacing the torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:** Symptoms include transmission slipping, erratic shifting, or warning lights. Solution involves replacing the TCM. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushings Wear:** Symptoms include clunking or rattling noises during suspension movement. Solution involves replacing the bushings. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300 per side
* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:** Symptoms include uneven ride height, reduced ground clearance, or warning lights. Solution involves replacing the compressor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include battery discharge, dimming lights, or warning lights. Solution involves replacing the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
* **Starter Relay Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle or no-crank conditions. Solution involves replacing the relay. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Exterior**

* **Rear Hatch Strut Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty lifting or supporting the rear hatch. Solution involves replacing the struts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300 per pair

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Fade/Peeling:** Symptoms include discoloration or peeling of the dashboard material. Solution involves replacing the dashboard or refinishing it. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* **Climate Control Actuator Failure:** Symptoms include problems with temperature control, fan speed, or air distribution. Solution involves replacing the actuator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary:**

The 2006 Cadillac Escalade ESV generally has moderate reliability, with average repair costs. The most common issues include suspension wear, alternator failure, and torque converter shudder. While some repairs can be expensive, such as the dashboard replacement or transmission torque converter, others are relatively affordable. Overall, the vehicle's performance, comfort, and features outweigh its potential drawbacks.
