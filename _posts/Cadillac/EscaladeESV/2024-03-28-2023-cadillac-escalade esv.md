**Introduction**

The 2023 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV that offers a spacious cabin, powerful engines, and advanced technology features. It is available with two engine options: a 6.2L V8 and a 3.0L Duramax diesel V6. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the Escalade ESV praise its impressive performance, comfortable interior, and luxurious amenities. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**6.2L V8**

* **Intake manifold leaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cylinder deactivation system failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, rough idle, reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace cylinder deactivation solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Duramax Diesel V6**

* **EGR valve failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, shuddering
    * Solution: Replace transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Torque converter failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, reduced ride height
    * Solution: Replace air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting vehicle, dead battery
    * Solution: Identify and resolve electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment system malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen unresponsive, Bluetooth connectivity issues
    * Solution: Update software or replace unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water droplets inside headlight assembly
    * Solution: Replace or repair headlight
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power liftgate failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Liftgate fails to open or close
    * Solution: Replace liftgate components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic sunroof rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from sunroof
    * Solution: Adjust sunroof alignment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Cadillac Escalade ESV has received positive reviews for its performance, comfort, and features. However, some owners have reported experiencing issues such as engine leaks, transmission problems, suspension failures, and electrical malfunctions. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can be significant.
