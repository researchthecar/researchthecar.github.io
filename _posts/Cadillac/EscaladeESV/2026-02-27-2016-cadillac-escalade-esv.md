# 2016 Cadillac Escalade ESV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Cadillac Escalade ESV delivers commanding luxury, strong towing capacity, and ample interior space. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding powertrain performance, electrical glitches, and suspension noises.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L V8 Engine

Equipped with a 6.2L V8 engine producing around 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and towing capability. While generally powerful, owners have reported occasional stalling and electrical charging integration issues.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may suddenly stall while driving or lose motive power, accompanied by low battery warnings or burning odors.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the charging and ignition systems, and inspect throttle bodies or crankshaft sensors.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel or four-wheel drive. Some owners report rough engagement, hesitation, and gear-hunting characteristics.

**Transmission Slipping, Hard Shifting, and Surging**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences harsh shifting, lunging when slowing down or accelerating, or pulsing vibrations under normal throttle.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition, update transmission control modules, or inspect internal clutches.

## Suspension

### Magnetic Ride Control Suspension

The adaptive suspension system is designed to provide a smooth ride, though mechanical wear can lead to noise and vibration complaints.

**Abnormal Suspension Noises and Vibrations**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual noises coming from underneath the vehicle accompanied by abnormal vibrations while driving at moderate speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect magnetic ride struts, bushings, and suspension mounting hardware for wear or fluid leaks.

## Steering

### Airbags and Steering Integration

Standard safety equipment includes a comprehensive airbag suite and electronic stability aids, though sensor faults can trigger dashboard warnings.

**Unexpected Airbag Warning or Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag warning lights illuminating or, in rare cases, side airbags deploying without impact.
- **Solution:** Scan SRS modules for fault codes and inspect wiring harnesses for damage or corrosion.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Starter System

Complex onboard electronics govern comfort and utility features, occasionally resulting in minor software glitches or accessory power failures.

**Intermittent Screen Failure and Starter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screens going blank intermittently, jammed DVD players, or starter motors failing unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Update infotainment software modules or replace faulty starters and relays.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V437000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** While being previously remedied for recall 16V-651, the affected vehicles may not have received the complete software update necessary to remedy the recall condition. Without the update, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V256000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Inadequate welds may allow the control arm to separate from the bushing, compromising steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the left and right front upper control arms and realign the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a striking and capable full-size luxury SUV, but used car buyers should perform a thorough mechanical and electronic inspection. Pay close attention to transmission shift quality, engine running stability, and suspension condition before finalizing a purchase.
