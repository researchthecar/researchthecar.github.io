**2014 Cadillac Escalade ESV**

The 2014 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV offered with the following engine and transmission options:

* 6.2L V8 with 6-speed automatic transmission (420 hp, 460 lb-ft torque)
* 6.2L V8 with 8-speed automatic transmission (420 hp, 460 lb-ft torque)

**Engine**

* **6.2L V8 with 6-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Leaking valve cover gaskets**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from valve cover area, visible under the hood.
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6.2L V8 with 8-speed automatic transmission**
    * No Issues Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping gears, delayed shifts, or shuddering.
        * Solution: Replace transmission solenoids or rebuild transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **8-speed automatic transmission**
    * No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension**
    * **Strut mount bearing failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises while driving, uneven tire wear.
        * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery issues**
    * **Battery drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
        * Solution: Check for parasitic drain from aftermarket accessories or faulty wiring. Replace battery if necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Airbag system**
    * **Airbag control module failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Airbag warning light illuminated, airbags may not deploy in an accident.
        * Solution: Replace airbag control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 16V812

**Exterior**

* **Door handles**
    * **Door handle breakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Door handles breaking off or becoming loose.
        * Solution: Replace door handles.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system**
    * **Screen flickering**
        * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment screen flickering or freezing intermittently.
        * Solution: Update software or replace screen.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Cadillac Escalade ESV has some reported issues, including oil leaks, transmission problems, and suspension noise. While most issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, some electrical and suspension issues can be more expensive. Overall, the vehicle offers a luxurious driving experience but may require occasional repairs and maintenance.
