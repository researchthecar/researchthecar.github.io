**2021 Cadillac Escalade ESV**

The 2021 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV manufactured by Cadillac. It is available with several engine and transmission options, including:

- 6.2L V8 engine with 10-speed automatic transmission (420 horsepower, 460 lb-ft torque)

- 3.0L Duramax turbo-diesel engine with 10-speed automatic transmission (277 horsepower, 460 lb-ft torque)

- 3.0L I6 turbocharged engine with 10-speed automatic transmission (403 horsepower, 417 lb-ft torque)

- 3.0L I6 turbocharged engine with 10-speed automatic transmission and eAssist (425 horsepower, 469 lb-ft torque)

**Engine**

**6.2L V8**

- **Fuel injector failure:** Loss of power, rough idling, check engine light. **Solution:** Replace fuel injectors. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+

- **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Engine oil leaks, coolant loss, rough idle. **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

- **Active fuel management system failure:** Engine hesitation, reduced fuel economy. **Solution:** Replace camshaft or crankshaft sensor. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**3.0L Duramax**

- **Turbocharger failure:** Loss of power, reduced fuel efficiency, increased exhaust smoke. **Solution:** Replace turbocharger. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+

- **EGR cooler failure:** Engine overheating, loss of power. **Solution:** Replace EGR cooler. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+

- **Diesel particulate filter (DPF) problems:** Reduced power, check engine light. **Solution:** Clean or replace DPF. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+

**3.0L I6**

- **Turbocharger wastegate failure:** Loss of boost, reduced power. **Solution:** Replace wastegate assembly. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

- **Fuel pump failure:** Engine stalls, loss of power. **Solution:** Replace fuel pump. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200+

- **Timing chain failure:** Engine rattling, reduced power. **Solution:** Replace timing chain and components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+

**Transmission**

**10-speed automatic**

- **Shuddering or vibration:** During gear shifts or acceleration. **Solution:** Replacement of transmission. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000+

- **Slipping gears:** Loss of power, difficulty accelerating. **Solution:** Repair or replacement of transmission. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+

- **Leaking fluid:** Drips or puddles of fluid under vehicle. **Solution:** Replace transmission pan gasket or other components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

- **Strut or shock failure:** Noise, vibration, loss of ride comfort. **Solution:** Replace struts or shocks. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+ per axle

- **Lower control arm bushing failure:** Clunking noise, reduced handling. **Solution:** Replace lower control arm bushings. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+ per side

- **Air suspension compressor failure:** Loss of air suspension functionality, rough ride. **Solution:** Replace air suspension compressor. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+

**Electrical**

- **Battery drain:** Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting vehicle. **Solution:** Check for parasitic drain and repair components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+

- **Alternator failure:** Dim lights, electrical problems. **Solution:** Replace alternator. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

- **Infotainment system glitches:** Touchscreen freezing, audio issues. **Solution:** Software updates or module replacement. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

- **Sunroof drain tube blockage:** Water accumulation, leaks. **Solution:** Clean or replace drain tubes. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

- **Rear hatch lock failure:** Difficulty opening or closing hatch. **Solution:** Replace hatch lock actuator. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

- **Headlamp condensation:** Moisture inside headlamp assembly. **Solution:** Replace or repair headlamp housing. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

- **Climate control system failure:** Malfunctioning temperature control, fan issues. **Solution:** Repair or replace climate control unit. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

- **Seat adjustment failure:** Difficulty adjusting seat positions. **Solution:** Repair or replace seat motor or control module. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

- **Instrument cluster display issues:** Pixelation, flickering, or complete failure. **Solution:** Replace instrument cluster display. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+

**Summary**

The 2021 Cadillac Escalade ESV has received mixed reviews from owners and critics. Some common complaints include engine and transmission issues, suspension problems, and electrical glitches. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the engine/transmission option. While some issues can be minor and easily resolved, others can be more serious and expensive to repair. Before purchasing a 2021 Cadillac Escalade ESV, it is important to do thorough research and consider the potential risks and costs associated with these reported issues.
