**Introduction:**

The 2016 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV. It is available with either a 6.2-liter V8 engine with 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque or a 3.6-liter V6 engine with 305 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Escalade ESV for its spacious cabin, luxurious amenities, and powerful engine options. However, some people have reported problems with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems.

**Engine**

**6.2-liter V8**

* **Valve train noise:** A faulty valve train can cause a ticking or rattling noise.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty valve train components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** A faulty intake manifold gasket can cause a vacuum leak, which can lead to poor engine performance.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty intake manifold gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 16V607000

* **Oil cooler failure:** A faulty oil cooler can cause the engine to overheat.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty oil cooler.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**3.6-liter V6**

* **Timing chain failure:** A faulty timing chain can cause the engine to lose power or stall.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty timing chain and related components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V444000

* **Oil consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption.
    * **Solution:** Replace the piston rings or other internal engine components as needed.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-speed automatic**

* **Shifting problems:** Some owners have reported problems with the transmission shifting smoothly.
    * **Solution:** Update the transmission software or replace the transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 16V637000

* **Transmission fluid leaks:** Some owners have reported leaks from the transmission.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty seals or gaskets.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise:** Some owners have reported a clunking noise from the front suspension.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty suspension components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Rear suspension sag:** Some owners have reported that the rear suspension sags over time.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty springs or shocks.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some owners have reported that the battery drains quickly.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty battery or alternator.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Electrical system failure:** Some owners have reported that the electrical system fails without warning.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty electrical components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** Some owners have reported that the headlights fill with condensation.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty headlights.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V562000

* **Tailgate latch failure:** Some owners have reported that the tailgate latch fails.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty latch.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system problems:** Some owners have reported problems with the infotainment system, such as freezing or crashing.
    * **Solution:** Update the software or replace the infotainment system.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Climate control problems:** Some owners have reported problems with the climate control system, such as not blowing hot or cold air.
    * **Solution:** Replace the faulty climate control components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a spacious and luxurious SUV, but it has some potential issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, and interior. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem. Some issues, such as headlight condensation, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. However, other issues, such as a faulty timing chain, can be more severe and expensive to fix.
