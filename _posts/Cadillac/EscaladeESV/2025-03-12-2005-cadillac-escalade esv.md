The 2005 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV known for its spaciousness, powerful engine, and upscale features. Available engines included a 5.3L V8 producing 290 hp and 325 lb-ft of torque and a 6.0L V8 producing 345 hp and 380 lb-ft of torque. All engines were paired with a 4L60-E 4-speed automatic transmission. Owners generally liked the Escalade ESV for its roomy interior, comfortable ride, and towing capability. Common complaints included poor fuel economy, high maintenance costs, and some reliability issues.

**Engine**

**5.3L V8**

*   **Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-offs. Blue smoke may be visible from the exhaust.
    *   Solution: Depending on the cause, possible solutions include replacing valve stem seals, piston rings, or the entire engine.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$5000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**6.0L V8**

*   **Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-offs. Blue smoke may be visible from the exhaust.
    *   Solution: Depending on the cause, possible solutions include replacing valve stem seals, piston rings, or the entire engine.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$5000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4L60-E 4-Speed Automatic**

*   **Slipping or Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips between gears, shifts harshly, or fails to shift properly.
    *   Solution: Rebuilding or replacing the transmission.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor fails, leading to a sagging rear end, a rough ride, and "Service Ride Control" warnings on the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replacing the air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Air Suspension Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Leaks in the air lines or air shocks can cause the suspension to deflate, resulting in a bumpy ride and potential damage to other suspension components.
    *   Solution: Repairing or replacing the leaking components (air lines, air shocks).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Instrument Panel Cluster Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Gauges stop working, become erratic, or the entire cluster goes dark.
    *   Solution: Replacing or repairing the instrument panel cluster.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Power Window Regulator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Power windows fail to operate, or operate slowly and erratically.
    *   Solution: Replacing the power window regulator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust on Lower Body Panels**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rust developing on the lower body panels, especially around the wheel wells and rocker panels.
    *   Solution: Rust repair, panel replacement, and repainting.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2000+ (depending on severity)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Delamination of Rear View Mirror**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The rearview mirror's reflective surface becomes delaminated or cloudy, impairing visibility.
    *   Solution: Replacing the rearview mirror.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Seat Heater Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The seat heaters stop working.
    *   Solution: Replacing the heating element or control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2005 Cadillac Escalade ESV suffers from a mix of age-related issues and inherent design flaws. Engine issues like oil consumption are concerning and can be costly to repair. Suspension problems, particularly with the air suspension, are also common and can impact ride quality. Electrical glitches, though generally less expensive, can be frustrating. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor in maintenance costs when considering purchasing this vehicle.
