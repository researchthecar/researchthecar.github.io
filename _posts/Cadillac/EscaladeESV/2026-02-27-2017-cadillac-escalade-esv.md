# 2017 Cadillac Escalade ESV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Cadillac Escalade ESV offers imposing styling, luxurious features, and robust utility, making it a popular choice for buyers seeking a full-size luxury SUV. However, used car shoppers should approach this model year with care, as owner complaints highlight several significant mechanical and electrical concerns. Potential buyers should thoroughly inspect the powertrain, brakes, and electronics before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L V8 Engine

Powered by a 6.2-liter V8 engine producing 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, this powerhouse provides swift acceleration and strong towing capability. While generally robust, buyers should watch out for issues related to the Active Fuel Management (AFM) system.

**AFM Lifter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine misfires, shaking, loud ticking noises, and stuttering or severe shaking while driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed Active Fuel Management (AFM) lifters and inspect the camshaft for associated damage.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive, it delivers smooth cruising when operating correctly. Some owners have reported harsh shifting behaviors that require professional inspection.

**Rough Shifting and Lurching**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises when shifting gears, severe lurching forward when moving into gear, or delayed response.
- **Solution:** Diagnose transmission health, update control modules, or service/replace internal components if mechanical wear is present.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system is designed to handle this heavy SUV, but proper maintenance is critical given reports of premature wear and assist warnings.

**Brake System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Hard brake pedal, difficult stopping at low speeds, service brake assist warnings, or severe brake component wear.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, calipers, and vacuum assist components for premature wear or failure.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering feel is generally light and manageable for a large vehicle, though electronic power steering faults can occasionally occur.

**Electric Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering while driving, making the steering wheel extremely stiff and hard to turn.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering (EPS) module, wiring, and steering rack assembly.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Accessories

Cabin electronics offer advanced connectivity and convenience features, but older CUE infotainment interfaces can experience software and touch responsiveness bugs.

**CUE Screen Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes inoperable, randomly scrolls through screens, or fails to display backup camera views.
- **Solution:** Reset or replace the touchscreen display module as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Cadillac Escalade ESV provides unmatched road presence, spaciousness, and luxury, but prospective buyers should perform a rigorous pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to engine idle quality for signs of lifter tick, test drive the transmission for crisp and smooth gear changes, and ensure all electronics function properly.
