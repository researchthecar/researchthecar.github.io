# 2024 Cadillac Escalade ESV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury full-size SUV, the Cadillac Escalade ESV stands out for its imposing presence, immense interior space, and powerful powertrain lineup. As with any modern, highly computerized flagship vehicle, prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback regarding engine operation and auxiliary systems to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L L87 V8

The 6.2-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for this heavy SUV. It is widely utilized across GM's full-size truck and SUV platforms, though buyers should monitor for occasional reports of internal mechanical noises.

**Abnormal Engine Noise and Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported abnormal ticking sounds upon starting the vehicle, followed in some instances by sudden engine lockups or stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a certified dealership or mechanic to diagnose internal mechanical wear or check for related recall service histories.

### 3.0L LM2 Duramax Turbo-Diesel I6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six diesel engine delivers 277 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, emphasizing strong low-end pulling power and high fuel efficiency. It pairs well with long-distance driving and heavy towing needs.

### Master Brake Cylinder

Components related to stopping power generally perform reliably, but occasional hydraulic component faults can require professional service.

**Brake System Failure Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience a dashboard warning message indicating a brake system failure due to hydraulic pressure or component malfunction.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a qualified service center for diagnostic testing and replacement of the defective master cylinder if necessary.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Hydra-Matic Automatic

This 10-speed automatic transmission is standard across all powertrain configurations, designed to optimize both highway fuel economy and power delivery. Overall, it provides smooth shifting dynamics under normal operating conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V674000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Brake Fluid Low Warning: Lamp](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Vehicle Safety Standard number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." Without a warning light, the vehicle may be driven with low brake fluid, which can reduce braking performance and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V274000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=Escalade+ESV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the engine, and as necessary, repair or replace the engine. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Cadillac Escalade ESV offers impressive capability and luxury, making it a dominant choice in the large SUV segment. Used car buyers should carefully review service records, verify that all applicable recall campaigns have been completed, and perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to catch potential engine or mechanical concerns early.
