**Introduction**
The 2009 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV known for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and impressive towing capabilities. It is powered by a 6.2L V8 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Escalade ESV also offers a 6.6L Duramax V8 diesel engine as an option.

**Engine**
* 6.2L V8

**Intake Valve Seat Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Intake valve seats can become loose and cause a variety of symptoms, including engine misfires, decreased power, and excessive oil consumption.
Solution: Replace the affected intake valve(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* 6-speed Automatic

**Shift Solenoid Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Shift solenoids can fail, leading to delayed or erratic shifting, as well as loss of power.
Solution: Replace the faulty shift solenoid(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700

**Suspension**
**Front Suspension**

**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The strut mount bearings can wear out over time, causing noises such as squeaks or clunks when driving over bumps.
Solution: Replace the affected strut mount bearings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Electrical**
**Body Control Module Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The body control module (BCM) can malfunction, leading to a variety of electrical issues, including problems with the lights, gauges, or climate control.
Solution: Replace the faulty BCM.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
**Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: The headlight assemblies can develop condensation inside, which can affect visibility.
Solution: Replace affected headlight(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
**Dashboard Panel Fitment Issues**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panels may not fit together properly, causing gaps or unevenness.
Solution: Realign or replace the affected panel(s).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Infotainment System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can experience issues such as screen freezing, audio glitches, or unresponsive buttons.
Solution: Update software or replace the system components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**
The 2009 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a capable and luxurious SUV, but it has some issues that can affect its performance and reliability. The severity of the issues varies, but some, such as the intake valve seat failure, can be costly to repair. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing an Escalade ESV.
