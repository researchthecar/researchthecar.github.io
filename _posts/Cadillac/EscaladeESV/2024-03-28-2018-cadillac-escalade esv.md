The 2018 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV that offers a commanding presence on the road. It is powered by a 6.2L V8 engine with 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Escalade ESV also comes standard with a variety of features, including a spacious cabin, a large touchscreen infotainment system, and a suite of advanced safety features.

Despite its luxurious appointments, the 2018 Cadillac Escalade ESV has been plagued by a number of issues.

**Engine**

* **6.2L V8 Engine**
    * **Intake valve deposits**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause a decrease in engine power, fuel economy, and performance.
        * Solution: Replace the intake valves and clean the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause a catastrophic engine failure.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain and other affected components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause the transmission to shift hard or erratically.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause the battery to discharge quickly, leaving the vehicle stranded.
        * Solution: Check for faulty electrical components and replace as needed.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment system problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause the infotainment system to crash, freeze, or display error messages.
        * Solution: Update the infotainment system software or replace the head unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause the headlights to fog up, reducing visibility.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate latch failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause the tailgate to open unexpectedly.
        * Solution: Replace the tailgate latch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause the HVAC system to malfunction, leading to discomfort for passengers.
        * Solution: Replace the climate control module or other affected components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear seat entertainment system problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Can cause the rear seat entertainment system to freeze, crash, or display error messages.
        * Solution: Update the software or replace the rear seat entertainment system.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a luxurious and capable SUV, but it has been plagued by a number of issues. The severity of these issues varies, but some of them can be quite costly to fix. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing an Escalade ESV.
