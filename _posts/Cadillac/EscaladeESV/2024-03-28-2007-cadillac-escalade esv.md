The 2007 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a full-size luxury SUV that offers a powerful engine and a spacious interior. It is available with a 6.2L V8 engine that produces 403 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 6.6L Duramax diesel V8 engine paired with an Allison 1000 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 6.2L supercharged V8 engine paired with a Hydra-Matic 6L80 6-speed automatic transmission. The Escalade ESV has a comfortable ride, a well-equipped cabin, and strong towing and hauling capabilities. However, it is also known for its high fuel consumption and some reliability issues.

**Engine**

**6.2L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: This issue affects engines produced before June 2006. The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing coolant and oil leaks. Symptoms include overheating, coolant loss, and oil leaks.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 07V209000, 07V220000

**6.6L Duramax Diesel V8**

* **Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors can fail, causing rough idling, loss of power, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 14V121000

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail, causing loss of power and increased exhaust smoke.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The torque converter can fail, causing shuddering, slipping, and loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system can fail, causing the vehicle to sag or ride rough.
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Axle Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear axle bearings can fail, causing noise and vibrations.
    * Solution: Replace the rear axle bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely due to a parasitic draw.
    * Solution: Identify and fix the source of the parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator can fail, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to stall.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture can accumulate inside the headlight housings.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight housings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Taillight Lens Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The taillight lenses can crack or break easily.
    * Solution: Replace the taillight lenses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the heater or air conditioner to not work properly.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power window motors or switches can fail, causing the windows to not operate properly.
    * Solution: Replace the failed components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Cadillac Escalade ESV is a capable and comfortable SUV, but it has some notable reliability issues. The engine and transmission can experience serious problems that can be expensive to fix. The suspension and electrical systems can also have issues, and the interior can suffer from various malfunctions. Overall, the Escalade ESV is a vehicle that requires regular maintenance and potential repairs, which should be considered when purchasing and owning one.
