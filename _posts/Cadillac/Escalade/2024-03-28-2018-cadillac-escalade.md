**Introduction:**

The 2018 Cadillac Escalade is a full-size luxury SUV available with two engine options: a 6.2L V8 and a 4.2L twin-turbocharged V8. Both engines mate to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Escalade offers a spacious cabin, impressive towing capacity, and a range of advanced features. However, owners have reported several issues related to various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**6.2L V8 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Bolt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bolts securing the intake manifold can loosen or break, causing vacuum leaks and potential engine damage. Symptoms include rough idling, loss of power, and engine stalling.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold bolts and inspect for any damage to the manifold or engine.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V631000

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may slip, hesitate, or fail to shift gears smoothly.
    * Solution: Re-program the transmission control module or replace transmission components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle may sag due to worn or damaged rear suspension components.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace failed or worn suspension components, such as springs, shocks, or struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery may discharge prematurely due to a parasitic drain.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the component causing the drain, such as faulty modules or wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Weatherstripping Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Weatherstripping around windows and doors may deteriorate or tear, allowing water and air leaks.
    * Solution: Replace the affected weatherstripping.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Instrument cluster may malfunction, displaying inaccurate or no information.
    * Solution: Replace the instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Cadillac Escalade has experienced several issues, including engine intake manifold bolt failure, transmission shifting problems, suspension sag, electrical battery drains, exterior weatherstripping issues, and interior instrument cluster malfunctions. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly to repair. Overall, it is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
