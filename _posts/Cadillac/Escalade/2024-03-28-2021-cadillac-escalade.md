**Introduction:**

The 2021 Cadillac Escalade is a full-size luxury SUV that offers a powerful engine and a spacious interior. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 6.2-liter naturally aspirated V8 and a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six. The V8 engine produces 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, while the inline-six produces 403 horsepower and 416 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission.

The Escalade is known for its comfortable ride and luxurious interior. However, it has received some criticism for its poor fuel economy and high price.

### **Engine**

**6.2-Liter Naturally Aspirated V8**

* **Lifter Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Tapping noise from the engine, loss of power
  * Solution: Replace the lifters
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3000 - $5000
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V191000
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, rough idle
  * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500
  * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-Liter Turbocharged Inline-Six**

* **No Issues Found**

### **Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems**
  * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping during shifts
  * Solution: Update the transmission software
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Suspension system does not function properly, vehicle sags
  * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V196000

### **Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains overnight
  * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty components (such as alternator, starter, or battery)
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000
  * Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

* **Power Running Boards Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: Running boards do not extend or retract properly
  * Solution: Replace the power running board motor or module
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000
  * Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**

* **Infotainment System Display Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Display screen goes blank or becomes unresponsive
  * Solution: Replace the infotainment system display
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Heater Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Seat heater does not produce enough heat or does not work
  * Solution: Replace the seat heater element
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
  * Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2021 Cadillac Escalade is a powerful and luxurious SUV that has received some criticism for its fuel economy and high price. While there are some issues that have been reported, they are generally not major and can be fixed relatively inexpensively. The severity of the issues is low to moderate, and the cost to fix them ranges from $0 to $5000.
