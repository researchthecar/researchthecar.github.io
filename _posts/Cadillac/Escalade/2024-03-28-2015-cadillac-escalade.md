**2015 Cadillac Escalade**

The 2015 Cadillac Escalade is a full-size luxury SUV that offers a spacious cabin, powerful engines, and advanced features. It is available with three engine options: a 6.2L V8, a 3.6L V6, and a 6.0L V8. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic and an 8-speed automatic.

Despite its overall desirability, the 2015 Escalade has received complaints regarding various issues across its major components.

**Engine**

* **6.2L V8**
    * **Intake Manifold Valve Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning intake manifold valves can lead to engine stalling, rough idling, and reduced power.
        * Solution: Replacing the intake manifold valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.6L V6**
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A faulty timing chain can cause engine damage, including skipped timing, lost power, and engine noises.
        * Solution: Replacing the timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: TSB #PIP5523A
* **6.0L V8**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slippage**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes, causing rough shifting and power loss.
        * Solution: Replacing or rebuilding the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Software Glitches**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission control module (TCM) may experience software glitches, resulting in incorrect gear selection or shifting problems.
        * Solution: Updating or reprogramming the TCM software.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A failing air suspension compressor can lead to a loss of ride height, causing the vehicle to sag and handle poorly.
    * Solution: Replacing the air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience freezes, crashes, or connectivity issues.
    * Solution: Updating or replacing the infotainment module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Wiring Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty wiring harnesses can cause a variety of electrical problems, including power loss, flickering lights, or malfunctioning components.
    * Solution: Inspecting and repairing or replacing the faulty wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate latch may fail to engage, allowing the tailgate to open unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Replacing the tailgate latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty sunroof seal can allow water to leak into the cabin, causing water damage to the interior.
    * Solution: Replacing the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2015 Cadillac Escalade is a capable and luxurious SUV, it has experienced some issues across its major components. Engine problems, notably with the 6.2L V8 and 3.6L V6, can be severe and expensive to repair. Transmission slippage and electrical glitches are also common complaints. However, issues with the interior and exterior are generally less severe and less costly to fix.
