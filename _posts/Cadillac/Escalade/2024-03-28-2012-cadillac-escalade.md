**Introduction:**

The 2012 Cadillac Escalade is a full-size luxury SUV that offers a powerful V8 engine and a spacious, well-appointed interior. It is available with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, and comes equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Escalade is known for its impressive towing capacity, comfortable ride, and high-tech features. However, like any vehicle, it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**6.2L V8 (403 hp / 417 lb-ft torque)**

* **Cam Phaser Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or knocking noises, reduced power, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replacement of cam phasers and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replacement of intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, delayed shifts
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, possible transmission rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, battery discharges quickly
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, replacement of battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows do not operate, switch malfunctions
    * Solution: Replacement of window motor or switch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks do not operate, key fob may not work
    * Solution: Replacement of door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not working properly, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattle or Vibration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise or vibration coming from dashboard area
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Cadillac Escalade is a reliable and capable vehicle, but it does have some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most common problems include engine cam phaser failure, intake manifold gasket leaks, and electrical issues. While some of these issues can be costly to repair, they are generally not major and can be addressed with regular maintenance and repairs.
