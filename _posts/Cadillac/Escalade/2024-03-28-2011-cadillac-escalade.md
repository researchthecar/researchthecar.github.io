The 2011 Cadillac Escalade is a luxury SUV that offers a powerful performance and a comfortable interior. It is available with two engine options: a 6.2-liter V8 that produces 403 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.8-liter V8 that produces 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque. The 6.2-liter V8 comes paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, while the 6.8-liter V8 comes paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the 2011 Cadillac Escalade praise its powerful engine, comfortable interior, and stylish exterior. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

**Engine**

**6.2-liter V8**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and gears
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.8-liter V8**

* **No Known Engine Issues**

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips gears, loss of power, difficulty shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Control Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission does not shift properly, loss of power, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, difficulty steering
    * Solution: Replace air suspension struts or compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate will not open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Software Bugs**
    * Description/Symptoms: System may freeze, crash, or give incorrect directions
    * Solution: Update software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: None
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Panel Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Controls do not respond, temperature settings are not maintained
    * Solution: Replace climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Cadillac Escalade is a powerful and luxurious SUV, but it has been reported to have some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Overall, the 2011 Cadillac Escalade is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues that it may have.
