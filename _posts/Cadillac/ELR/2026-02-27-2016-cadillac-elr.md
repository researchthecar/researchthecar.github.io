# 2016 Cadillac ELR Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Cadillac ELR, buyers get a striking luxury coupe built on advanced extended-range electric vehicle architecture. While powertrains of this vintage generally prove durable, prospective owners should review potential owner-reported safety concerns before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Ecotec 4-Cylinder Range Extender

This 1.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine acts primarily as a generator to supply electrical power to the drive system. It is generally known for solid mechanical durability in daily driving conditions.

## Transmission

### Voltec Electric Drive Unit

The multi-mode electronic drive unit seamlessly blends electric motor power and generator output to drive the front wheels. It delivers smooth, responsive acceleration and requires minimal routine maintenance.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Owner reports indicate occasional warnings related to the supplemental restraint system and occupant detection.

**Passenger Presence Module and Sensor Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an unexpected dashboard warning message or an illuminated red airbag light on the instrument cluster indicating an air bag system fault.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the trouble codes, such as communication loss with the passenger presence module, and replace faulty sensors if parts are available.
