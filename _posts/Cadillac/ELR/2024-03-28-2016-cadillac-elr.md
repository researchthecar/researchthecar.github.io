**Overview:**

The 2016 Cadillac ELR is a plug-in hybrid luxury coupe that debuted with a single powertrain option. It boasted a 1.4L turbocharged 4-cylinder gasoline engine paired to a 1.9 kWh lithium-ion battery pack and a single electric motor, generating a combined output of 207 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The ELR was praised for its sleek design, luxurious interior, and eco-friendly credentials. However, it faced criticism for its limited battery range and high price.

**Engine**

- **1.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

  - **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption leading to low oil levels and engine damage.
    - **Solution:** Engine rebuild or replacement.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

- **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

  - **Lag or Hesitation:** Delayed response or shuddering during gear changes.
    - **Solution:** Transmission software update or replacement.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

- **Battery:** Premature battery degradation leading to reduced range and power loss.
    - **Solution:** Battery replacement.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $8,000
    - **Recalls:** NHTSA Recall #16V491000 (Battery Powertrain Control Module)

- **Infotainment System:** Intermittent freezing, crashing, or blank screen.
    - **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

- **Windshield:** Prone to cracking or chipping.
    - **Solution:** Windshield replacement.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800

**Interior**

- **Sunroof:** Leaking or rattling.
    - **Solution:** Seal replacement or sunroof adjustment.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000

**Summary**

The 2016 Cadillac ELR has had a limited number of issues reported, with the most common issues being battery degradation and infotainment system glitches. The severity of these issues can vary, with battery replacement being the most expensive repair. Overall, the ELR appears to be a reliable and well-built vehicle, but owners should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
