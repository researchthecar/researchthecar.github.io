The 1996 Cadillac Eldorado is a luxurious coupe that offers a comfortable and refined ride. It is powered by a robust 4.6L Northstar V8 engine that produces 300 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque. The Eldorado comes standard with a four-speed automatic transmission. It is praised for its spacious interior, smooth handling, and powerful engine. However, it has received some criticism for its high fuel consumption and lack of modern safety features.

**Engine**

* **4.6L Northstar V8**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, rough idle
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Northstar Engine Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Complete engine failure, often due to timing chain failure
        * Solution: Engine replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears, loss of power
        * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uneven ride height, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension springs or air bags
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies after a short period of time
    * Solution: Check for parasitic draw and replace faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, flickering electrical components
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks or becomes loose
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Gauge Cluster Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges fail to operate properly, distorted or missing displays
    * Solution: Replace gauge cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows fail to operate or become stuck
    * Solution: Replace window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **A/C Condenser Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of cooling, warm air blowing from vents
    * Solution: Replace A/C condenser
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1996 Cadillac Eldorado is a reliable and comfortable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. Some of the most common problems include intake manifold gasket failure, Northstar engine failure, transmission slipping, rear suspension sagging, battery drain, alternator failure, door handle failure, dashboard gauge cluster failure, power window failure, and A/C condenser failure. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be quite expensive to repair.
