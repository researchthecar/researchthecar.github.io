**Introduction**

The 2001 Cadillac Eldorado is a full-size luxury coupe powered by a 4.6L Northstar V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. With 300 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, the Eldorado offers a comfortable and powerful driving experience. However, it has also encountered some specific issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**4.6L Northstar V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, misfires, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, slipping, no engagement
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaks from transmission pan or seals
    * Solution: Replace leaking seals or gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, vibration, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when parked
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair source of parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Roof Panel Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin through sunroof or around pillars
    * Solution: Replace or reseal roof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative HVAC system, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700
* **Power Seat Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat adjustments become inoperative
    * Solution: Replace seat motor or seat module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000

**Other**

* **Brakes**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature brake wear, reduced stopping power
    * Solution: Replace brake pads and/or rotors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary**

The 2001 Cadillac Eldorado has met with mixed reviews. While it offers a powerful engine and comfortable interior, it has also been plagued by several issues. The most severe and expensive problems include intake manifold gasket failure, head gasket failure, and transmission fluid leaks. However, many of the other issues are relatively minor and can be fixed at a reasonable cost.
