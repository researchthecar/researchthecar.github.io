# 2017 Cadillac CT6

### Overview
The 2017 Cadillac CT6 is a full-size luxury sedan that aimed to compete with established German rivals. It offered a sophisticated driving experience, a comfortable and quiet cabin, and a range of potent powertrains.  While generally well-received for its design and technology, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2017 model year across its various configurations. Understanding these specific components can help potential buyers and current owners anticipate and address potential maintenance needs.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### 3.6L V6
**Timing Chain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (8L45/8L90)
**Harsh Shifting / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, shuddering.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and fill, software update, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** N/A
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** N/A


### Active Rear Steering
**Actuator Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel vibration, warning lights related to steering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear steering actuator.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**System Freezes / Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, system reboots, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after sitting, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700


## Exterior

### Lighting Systems
**LED Headlight Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming or flickering headlights, complete failure of one or both units.
- **Solution:** Replacement of LED headlight modules.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,500 per unit


## Interior

### Seat Adjustments
**Power Seat Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving, unusual noises from seat mechanism.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat adjustment motors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per seat

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Cadillac CT6 presents a generally solid reliability record, particularly for its powertrain options. The most frequently cited issues tend to revolve around the infotainment system's software glitches and, in some cases, the longevity of LED headlights. While the V6 engines are robust, the turbocharged four-cylinder has seen some reports of turbocharger and coolant system issues. Transmission problems, though not widespread, can manifest as harsh shifting. Owners should be mindful of potential electrical gremlins like battery drain and ensure regular maintenance of the transmission fluid. Overall, the CT6 offers a luxurious experience with manageable potential repair costs if common issues are addressed proactively.
