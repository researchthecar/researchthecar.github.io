# 2019 Cadillac CT6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Cadillac CT6, prospective buyers will find a sophisticated luxury sedan offering advanced technology and smooth ride quality. However, reviewing owner experiences reveals important areas to inspect, particularly regarding drivetrain operation and electronic safety systems. Understanding these potential trouble spots helps ensure you select a well-maintained model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, serving as the base powerplant for efficiency-minded buyers. It provides adequate daily acceleration, though overall reliability depends heavily on consistent oil maintenance.

### 3.6L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers roughly 335 horsepower and 284 lb-ft of torque for a balanced blend of responsiveness and refinement. It is a generally dependable option within GM's engine lineup when serviced regularly.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The high-performance 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 pumps out an impressive 404 horsepower (or up to 550 hp in the V-Sport), offering brisk acceleration and effortless passing power. Buyers should verify complete service history, as complex forced-induction powertrains require diligent upkeep.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission pairs with the vehicle's engine options to optimize fuel economy and deliver seamless gear changes across various driving conditions. While generally smooth, owners should test-drive thoroughly to check for any harsh engagement or shifting anomalies.

**Hard Shifting and Wheel Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected hard shifting behaviors while underway, alongside rare instances of wheels locking up and traction control warnings illuminating.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission control module, software calibrations, and internal components for signs of mechanical wear or fluid pressure loss.

### Side View Mirrors

Exterior mirrors feature power-folding capabilities that add convenience in tight spaces, though minor electrical glitches can occasionally affect their operation.

**Mirrors Remain in Closed Position**
- **Symptoms:** Side view mirrors occasionally remain folded inward after the vehicle is put into gear and driven off, limiting side visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect power mirror folding motors, switches, and software logic to ensure proper deployment upon startup.

## Brakes

### ABS and Electronic Stability Systems

Advanced safety and stability features provide peace of mind but rely heavily on complex sensors that can occasionally trigger dashboard warnings.

**Stability and ABS Error Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden error messages related to the anti-lock braking system, StabiliTrak, and cruise control functions at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Check for stored diagnostic trouble codes and ensure wheel speed sensors and software modules are fully updated and functioning correctly.

### Service Brakes and Brake Booster

The braking system delivers robust stopping performance for a large luxury sedan, though mechanical component failures like brake boosters require immediate attention.

**Brake Booster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The brake system may fail to engage properly, resulting in a hard brake pedal or significantly reduced stopping power while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake booster assembly and vacuum or electronic assist systems, replacing faulty components immediately.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V889000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Abs Warning Light](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Due to an Electronic Brake Control Module (EBCM) software error, the vehicle's Electronic Stability Control (ESC) and Antilock Brake System (ABS) may become disabled. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 126, "Electronic Stability Control" and 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems.".
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the EBCM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V117000: Exterior Lighting:Turn Signal:Switch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An erroneous active turn-signal may mislead pedestrians and other drivers, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM has notified owners, and dealers will replace the turn-signal switch. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V673000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Certain second-row or third-row rear seatbelts retractor assemblies may not automatically lock when the seatbelt is fully pulled out of the retractor, possibly preventing a child seat from being properly secured. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rear seatbelt retractors and replace them if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V148000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The transmission may become damaged internally and cause the wheels to lock-up. Wheel lock-up while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install transmission control module monitoring software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Cadillac CT6 remains an appealing choice for used luxury car buyers seeking comfort, prestige, and advanced features. While owners have noted concerns regarding transmission shift quality, brake components, and electronic stability warnings, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help uncover potential issues. Prioritizing models with complete service records will provide greater confidence on the road.
