# 2016 Cadillac CT6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Cadillac CT6 is a flagship luxury sedan known for its spacious interior and advanced technology. As a used purchase, buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding powertrain drivability and safety systems to ensure a thorough inspection before buying.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 265 horsepower and provides efficient power for the large sedan. Buyers should inspect the turbocharger and surrounding plumbing for signs of wear.

**Turbo Boost and Cylinder Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported turbo boost failure affecting individual cylinders, leading to recurring engine performance issues after initial repairs.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the turbocharger assembly, boost pressure, and cylinder health.

### 3.6L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 engine generates around 335 horsepower, offering balanced performance and smooth power delivery. It is generally a conventional choice for everyday luxury driving.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 puts out an impressive 404 horsepower, paired with standard all-wheel drive for brisk acceleration. Maintenance history is key when considering this high-output forced-induction option.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with various engine options across the lineup. While it generally delivers smooth cruising gears, some owners note rough shifts and drivability hesitations.

**Rough Shifting and Powertrain Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced harsh engagement when shifting into gear, sudden jerking motions, delayed acceleration from rolling stops, and fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transmission for fluid leaks, check software calibration, and have a specialist evaluate shift quality.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

The safety restraint systems are designed to protect occupants during severe impacts. Pre-purchase inspections should include a comprehensive scan of the airbag control modules.

**Failure to Deploy in Collisions**
- **Symptoms:** Owner complaints note instances where frontal or side impacts occurred and the supplemental restraint airbags failed to deploy.
- **Solution:** Verify that the airbag indicator lights function correctly on startup and ensure no unresolved safety recalls apply to the vehicle.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V437000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT6&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." If the child seat cannot be attached correctly to the anchorage bar, there is an increased the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, remove any excess adhesive, and apply anti-corrosion primer to the inboard child seat anchorage bars, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V687000: Seat Belts:Front:Webbing](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT6&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the seat belt webbing detaches from the seat anchor, the seat belt may not properly restrain the front seat passenger in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the existing front passenger seat belt webbing anchor bolt, or install a bolt if it is missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V759000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=CT6&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The excessive brightness may affect the vision of other drivers, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install right and left side in-line headlight jumper harnesses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Cadillac CT6 offers impressive luxury and performance, but prospective buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain behavior and safety components. A pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable mechanic is recommended to check for transmission hesitation, fluid leaks, and electronic system integrity.
