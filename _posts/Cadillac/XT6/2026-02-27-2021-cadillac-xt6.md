# 2021 Cadillac XT6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury midsize SUV, the Cadillac XT6 brings sharp styling and comfortable three-row seating to the table. While generally capable, prospective buyers should review owner-reported concerns ranging from intermittent camera technology glitches to occasional brake noise and sunroof durability. Keeping a close eye on service records and past recall completions can help ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient daily performance for lower-tier trims.

### 3.6L V6

The 3.6-liter naturally aspirated V6 pushes out around 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and towing capability well-suited for a three-row crossover.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

Paired with both engine choices, the 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, shifting performance across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback indicates sporadic noise and vibration concerns that warrant a physical inspection prior to purchase.

**Brake Vibration and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners experience premature front brake vibration accompanied by grinding, rubbing, or screeching noises during application.
- **Solution:** Inspection by a qualified mechanic is recommended to determine if pad or rotor service is required.

## Electrical

### Back-Up Camera

Owner reports highlight occasional visibility aid glitches that typically require software interventions or component inspections.

**Intermittent Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that the rearview or surround-view camera may intermittently display a blank screen or fail to activate when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Dealers may attempt software updates, reprogramming of the video processing module, or replacement of affected wiring harnesses.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior glass integrity reports occasionally note rare instances of spontaneous sunroof breakage during highway travel.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud pop is heard while driving, followed by the panoramic sunroof glass shattering or cracking unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the glass assembly is typically required.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V422000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Improperly attached and seated fuel supply lines may loosen or separate, causing a fuel leak and increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace fuel supply lines as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V709000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative rearview camera can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the coaxial cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The Cadillac XT6 offers compelling luxury features, but used car buyers should perform thorough pre-purchase inspections. Pay special attention to working electronics, camera systems, brake feel, and the condition of glass components to avoid unexpected post-purchase repairs.
