The 2022 Cadillac XT6 is a mid-size luxury SUV that offers a blend of style, comfort, and technology. Engine options include a 2.0L Turbocharged I4 producing 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.6L V6 generating 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the XT6 for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features. Common criticisms include the somewhat uninspired interior design compared to rivals and the occasionally sluggish performance of the base engine.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged I4**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, unusual noises from the engine (whining or whistling), check engine light illuminated. Turbocharger vanes may become damaged or bearings may fail.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Low oil level warning, needing to add oil frequently between oil changes, blue smoke from the exhaust. May be caused by piston ring issues or valve seal leakage.
    *   Solution: Depending on the cause, repairs may range from replacing valve seals to replacing piston rings. In severe cases, engine replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 (valve seals) - $5,000+ (engine replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    *   **Harsh Shifting**
        *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable harsh or jerky shifts between gears, especially at lower speeds. May be more pronounced when the transmission is cold.
        *   Solution: Software update (reprogramming the transmission control module - TCM) or transmission valve body replacement.
        *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150-$300 (Software Update), $800 - $1,500 (Valve Body Replacement)
        *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen freezes, becomes unresponsive, or reboots unexpectedly. Affects navigation, audio, and climate controls integrated into the system.
    *   Solution: Software update for the infotainment system. In more severe cases, replacement of the infotainment module may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update), $500 - $1,200+ (Module Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains quickly, especially when the car is not in use for extended periods. May lead to difficulty starting the vehicle or complete battery failure. Can be caused by parasitic drain from a faulty module or component.
    *   Solution: Identifying and replacing the faulty module or component causing the parasitic drain.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ (depending on the faulty component)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rear Liftgate Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The power liftgate fails to open or close properly. It may stop mid-cycle, make unusual noises, or not respond to the remote or button inputs.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the liftgate motor or strut assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof fails to open or close smoothly, gets stuck, or makes excessive noise. Can be due to a faulty motor, damaged tracks, or a broken seal.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating the tracks, replacing the sunroof motor, or repairing/replacing the tracks.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 (lubrication) - $800+ (motor or track replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Seat Heater/Cooler Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The seat heaters or coolers stop working on one or more seats. May be caused by a faulty heating element, a defective control module, or a wiring issue.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the heating element, control module, or repair of the wiring harness.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ (depending on the component)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:** The 2022 Cadillac XT6 can suffer from various issues, ranging from minor infotainment glitches to more significant engine and transmission problems. Excessive oil consumption and turbocharger failure on the engines can lead to costly repairs. Electrical problems and sunroof malfunctions can also occur. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and carefully inspect the vehicle's service history and condition before purchasing. While some issues are minor and easily resolved, others could result in substantial repair bills.
