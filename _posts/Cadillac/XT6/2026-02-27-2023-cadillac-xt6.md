# 2023 Cadillac XT6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Cadillac XT6 offers three-row luxury, comfortable cruising, and a generous suite of features for families. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding electrical glitches, safety tech performance, and powertrain options. Because NHTSA reports reflect individual owner experiences rather than confirmed universal flaws, checking maintenance records and conducting a thorough test drive remain essential steps.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate efficiency for daily commuting. It offers a balanced entry point for buyers prioritizing fuel economy over heavy towing.

### 3.6L V6

The available 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque for stronger acceleration and passing power. It is a popular choice for buyers looking for more traditional midsize luxury SUV performance.

**Fuel System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience a loss of motive power while driving, display diagnostic trouble codes like P2635, and fail to restart due to fuel pump issues.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel pump and fuel pump control module, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The nine-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options to deliver smooth gear shifts across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. Overall, it operates reliably when properly maintained.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering feel is generally praised for its comfort and responsiveness, making long trips manageable.

**Steering Wheel Binding**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may momentarily stiffen or lock up when executing right turns at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the steering column, electronic power steering motor, and associated sensors inspected by a certified technician.

## Electrical

### Power Liftgate

Electrical accessories and convenience features generally function well, though occasional glitch reports warrant a careful check of all power systems.

**Spontaneous Liftgate Operation**
- **Symptoms:** The rear liftgate may open unexpectedly while the vehicle is shifted into park or while parked overnight, draining the battery.
- **Solution:** Inspect the liftgate switch and body control module for faults, and replace defective switch assemblies or sensors.

### Backup Camera and Sensors

Advanced safety systems provide helpful driver assistance, but prospective buyers should test backup cameras and active safety features in varying lighting conditions.

**Dim Rear Display and Sensor Inactivity**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display dims significantly at night or in low-light environments, making obstacles difficult to see, and backup alarms may fail to sound.
- **Solution:** Verify camera settings and software calibration with a dealer or technician, though some owners report the behavior is considered standard by the manufacturer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V708000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Illegible tire size information may result in the wrong tires being installed, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the vehicle certification label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V172000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the sun gears, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Cadillac XT6 is a comfortable and well-appointed luxury crossover, but used-car buyers should perform targeted checks during a pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the operation of the backup camera in low-light settings, ensure the steering feels fluid through various maneuvers, and verify that the powertrain runs smoothly without warning lights. Shopping for a model with remaining factory warranty coverage can provide additional peace of mind.
