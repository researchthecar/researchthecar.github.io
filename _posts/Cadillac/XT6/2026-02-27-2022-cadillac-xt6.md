# 2022 Cadillac XT6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Cadillac XT6 offers three-row family luxury with a comfortable ride and modern styling, but prospective used-car buyers should review owner-reported mechanical and electrical experiences. While many drivers enjoy its everyday utility, owner complaints submitted to safety agencies highlight occasional powertrain, electrical, and sensor concerns that warrant a careful pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, serving as the base power plant for lower trims. It provides reasonable efficiency for daily driving, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history during a pre-purchase inspection.

### 3.6L V6

The 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, offering stronger acceleration and passing power well-suited for a midsize luxury SUV. While generally robust, isolated owner reports note potential fuel delivery and stalling issues requiring professional diagnosis.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden engine stalling while maneuvering at low speeds or driving, sometimes accompanied by a flashing check engine light or fuel management system codes.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel system and replace the fuel pump module or associated components if diagnostic trouble codes indicate a failure.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The nine-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally delivers smooth gear shifts, though prospective buyers should test-drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure responsive operation.

## Brakes

### Driver Assistance and Mirrors

Advanced safety features enhance peace of mind, but buyers should test all active safety systems and exterior mirrors during a test drive.

**Automatic Emergency Braking and Mirror Deployment Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Brake assist may occasionally fail to engage when expected during sudden traffic events, and side-view mirrors may occasionally fail to unfold automatically upon startup.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer inspect the forward collision radar/camera calibration and verify power-folding mirror switch functionality.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Sensor System

Owner complaints point to occasional electrical glitches and sensor faults that can impact vehicle drivability.

**Sudden Power Loss and Dashboard Light Flashing**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard component lights may flash on and off erratically, accompanied by a sudden loss of vehicle power while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the thermostat sensor, wiring harnesses, and battery charging system, and replace faulty sensors or modules as diagnosed.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim

Interior fit and finish are generally upscale, though temperature-related cosmetic warping has been noted by some owners.

**Dashboard Panel Buckling**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard panel materials may warp or buckle when exposed to high ambient temperatures and intense sunlight.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or interior specialist to determine if warped trim panels can be adjusted or replaced under warranty.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V392000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." Without a warning label, the brake fluid may be unintentionally contaminated by debris, which can affect brake performance and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the brake fluid reservoir cap and replace it, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V427000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The left-rear suspension toe link may have been tightened improperly, which could result in a loose or separated toe link. A loose toe link can separate and cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the left-rear suspension and replace the fastener if it is loose or missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V446000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The fuel tank rollover valve may not provide a proper seal in the event of a rollover crash. A fuel tank rollover valve that does not properly seal can result in a fuel leak during a rollover crash, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the fuel tank and replace the fuel tank shell if the rollover valve is found to be dislodged or missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Cadillac XT6 is a comfortable and well-appointed luxury crossover, but used-car shoppers should approach with a healthy dose of due diligence. Reviewing service records and conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the fuel system, electrical components, and advanced safety features will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
