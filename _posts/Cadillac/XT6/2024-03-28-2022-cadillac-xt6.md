**Introduction**

The 2022 Cadillac XT6 is a three-row luxury SUV that offers a spacious cabin, comfortable ride, and a powerful engine lineup. It comes standard with a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission. A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque is also available, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The XT6 has received praise for its smooth ride, luxurious cabin, and ample cargo space. However, it has also been criticized for its high price, lack of driver-assistance features, and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Runner Control Valve Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold runner control valve may fail, causing a loss of engine power and poor fuel economy.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold runner control valve.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail, causing a catastrophic engine failure.
Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Nine-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitation and Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or slip during gear changes.
Solution: Replace or reprogram the transmission control module (TCM).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging**
Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension may sag, causing the vehicle to lean to one side or the other.
Solution: Replace the rear suspension springs or shock absorbers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the cause of the battery drain, such as a faulty alternator or parasitic draw.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The headlights may fail to turn on or may flicker intermittently.
Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs or ballast.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak water into the cabin.
Solution: Replace the sunroof seal.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, lag, or display error messages.
Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the head unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Cadillac XT6 has several notable issues, including engine problems such as intake manifold runner control valve failure and timing chain failure, transmission problems such as shift hesitation and slipping, and electrical problems such as battery drain and headlight failure. These issues can be costly to repair, with some costing thousands of dollars. Overall, the severity and cost of the XT6's issues may deter some potential buyers.
