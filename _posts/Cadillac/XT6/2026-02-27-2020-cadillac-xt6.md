# 2020 Cadillac XT6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Cadillac XT6 offers three-row luxury, comfortable road manners, and generous feature content, making it a tempting choice for family buyers on the used market. However, early production models like the 2020 version carry reports of electrical issues, intermittent camera failures, and powertrain quirks that prospective buyers should inspect closely before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Inline-4

A 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, serving as the base powerplant for lower trims. It offers punchy low-end responsiveness for daily commuting, though long-term reliability for auxiliary fuel system components warrants standard pre-purchase checks.

### 3.6L V6

A naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 engine generating 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth acceleration suited for a midsize luxury crossover. While generally robust, owners have reported thermostat cycling issues that can trigger check engine lights.

**Thermostat Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminates and the engine coolant thermostat may open at a lower temperature than intended, impacting warm-up and efficiency.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty thermostat assembly with an updated OEM part.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive across the model lineup. While it generally delivers smooth highway cruising, some owners experience cold-weather whining or low-speed shift hesitation.

**Shifting Stutter and Whining**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an intermittent high-pitched whining noise when cold, alongside low-RPM transmission shutter or rough gear engagement.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect transmission fluid condition, update powertrain control modules, or evaluate internal clutch packs if symptoms persist.

## Steering

### Steering Assist System

Electronic power steering system designed to provide light, effortless maneuverability at low speeds and heavier weight on the highway. A subset of owners have experienced intermittent assist reduction warnings.

**Reduced Steering Assist Warning**
- **Symptoms:** A warning message stating 'Steering Assist is Reduced Drive with Care' appears on the dashboard, occasionally disabling power steering assist and climate control functions.
- **Solution:** Have the steering control module and associated wiring diagnosed by a dealership or qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Body Control Module and Cameras

Manages cabin accessories, displays, and exterior lighting systems. Electrical complaints on early models frequently focus on troublesome body control modules and intermittent surround-vision camera dropouts.

**Backup Camera and BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may display a black screen with a warning triangle when shifting into reverse, or turn signals may fail due to body control module glitches.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty body control modules or update camera software modules as recommended by service bulletins.

## Safety

### Forward Collision and Braking

Driver assistance features designed to enhance accident avoidance. Some drivers report over-sensitive automatic emergency braking interventions in specific low-speed or sloped conditions.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience sudden, unexpected strong braking while descending slopes or navigating reversing maneuvers without an actual obstacle present.
- **Solution:** Check for available sensor calibrations, software updates, or radar alignment adjustments at a service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V639000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Insufficient fuel supply can result in an engine stall without warning, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump module. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V446000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain:Inflator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The diffuser component of the Roof-Rail Air Bag (RRAB) inflator may not have been properly crimped to the inflator and could separate from the inflator during air bag deployment. If the diffuser separates from the inflator during deployment, RRAB performance may be degraded, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the suspect RRAB modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V668000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Missing bolts on the start-stop accumulator endcap could result in a transmission oil leak and may progress to a loss of propulsion, increasing the risk of a crash. A transmission fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the start-stop transmission accumulator and replace it if any bolts are missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V709000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An inoperative rearview camera can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the coaxial cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Cadillac XT6 is a spacious and comfortable option for families seeking three rows of luxury, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough mechanical and electronic inspection. Pay close attention to the operation of the 9-speed transmission, infotainment and backup cameras, and steering assist electronics during a test drive to ensure no unresolved issues exist.
