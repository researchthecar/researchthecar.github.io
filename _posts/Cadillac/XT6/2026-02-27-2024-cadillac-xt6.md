# 2024 Cadillac XT6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Cadillac XT6, buyers will find a stylish and spacious three-row luxury crossover equipped with modern technology. However, early owner reports submitted to safety regulators highlight several recurring areas of concern, particularly regarding transmission performance, electrical stability, and driver-assistance sensors. Reviewing these owner experiences can help prospective buyers perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient daily performance for the luxury crossover. Overall, this powerplant operates smoothly, though owners should always verify proper maintenance records during a used car purchase.

### 3.6L V6

The available 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for highway driving and towing. It is a familiar, established option in the GM lineup, though buyers should test drive to ensure responsive operation.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine choices to manage power delivery across front-wheel or all-wheel drive layouts. While generally smooth, some owners have experienced shifting abnormalities and mechanical concerns requiring professional diagnosis.

**Transmission Slipping, Shuddering, and Reverse Engagement Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported transmission shuddering, hesitation, harsh shift jerks between gears, and sudden failures to engage reverse gear when attempting to back up.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission assembly, fluid condition, and internal components for wear or mechanical failure.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Cluster

The electrical architecture supports advanced cabin displays, driver aids, and convenience features. Screen blanking and dashboard malfunctions are among the reported electrical complaints.

**Blank Dashboard Screens and Display Freezes**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument panel, infotainment screen, and radio may suddenly go blank or freeze during operation, temporarily disabling display readouts and connected features.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to check for software updates or faulty control modules that may require resetting or replacement.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Parking Sensors

Advanced safety systems are designed to protect occupants, but sensitive or erratic sensor behavior has led to driver complaints regarding unexpected braking inputs.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience sudden, hard braking events or stop abruptly while reversing out of driveways or parking spots due to erratic sensor activation.
- **Solution:** Have the backup camera, collision sensors, and associated software calibrated or inspected by a certified service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V681000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A windshield that is not properly bonded may detach during a crash and increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the windshield and apply primer and urethane sealant. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V459000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The instrument panel may not illuminate when the hazard warning lights, high beams, or turn signals are activated. Equipment." Without the illuminated reminder, the driver could fail to turn off the lights when no longer needed.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Cadillac XT6 offers luxury comfort and strong engine options, but used car buyers should exercise diligence. Pay close attention during test drives to verify smooth transmission shifting, proper reverse engagement, and stable operation of all dashboard screens and safety sensors. A pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic is strongly recommended.
