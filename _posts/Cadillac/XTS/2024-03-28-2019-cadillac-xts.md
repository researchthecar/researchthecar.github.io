**Introduction:**

The 2019 Cadillac XTS is a full-size luxury sedan that offers a powerful V6 engine and a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. It has a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and a number of advanced features. However, some owners have reported issues with the XTS, including engine, transmission, and electrical problems.

**Engine**

* **3.6L V6**
    * **Timing chain failure:** The timing chain can stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or stall. This can be a serious and expensive repair.
        * **Symptoms:** Engine rattling, loss of power, stalling
        * **Solution:** Replace the timing chain and related components
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **2.0L Turbocharged I4**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, causing the engine to lose power or stall. This can be a serious and expensive repair.
        * **Symptoms:** Loss of power, stalling, blue smoke from the exhaust
        * **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission shudder:** The transmission may shudder or jerk when shifting gears. This can be a nuisance and can lead to transmission damage.
        * **Symptoms:** Shuddering or jerking when shifting gears
        * **Solution:** Replace the transmission fluid and filter
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise:** The front suspension can make a clunking or rattling noise when driving over bumps. This can be a nuisance and can lead to suspension damage.
        * **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension
        * **Solution:** Replace the sway bar links or bushings
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery problems:** The battery can fail prematurely, leaving the driver stranded. This can be a nuisance and can lead to other electrical problems.
        * **Symptoms:** Battery failure, difficulty starting the car, electrical system problems
        * **Solution:** Replace the battery
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** The headlights can develop condensation, which can reduce visibility and lead to headlight failure. This can be a nuisance and can lead to expensive repairs.
        * **Symptoms:** Condensation inside the headlights
        * **Solution:** Replace the headlights
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system problems:** The infotainment system can experience a variety of problems, including freezing, crashing, and connectivity issues. This can be a nuisance and can make it difficult to use the car's features.
        * **Symptoms:** Freezing, crashing, connectivity issues
        * **Solution:** Update the software, replace the infotainment system
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Cadillac XTS is a comfortable and powerful luxury sedan, but it has a number of potential issues. The most serious issues include timing chain failure, turbocharger failure, and transmission shudder. These issues can be expensive to repair, so it is important to be aware of them when considering purchasing an XTS.
