**Introduction:**

The 1991 Cadillac Allante was a luxury roadster available with a single engine and transmission option. The 4.5L V8 produced 200 hp and 270 lb-ft of torque and was paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Allante was praised for its comfortable and stylish interior and smooth ride, but was criticized for its reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **4.5L V8**

    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, poor engine performance
    Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Slipping**
    Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging**
    Description/Symptoms: Uneven tire wear, poor handling
    Solution: Replace rear coil springs
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions
    Solution: Replace alternator
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Inability to operate power windows
    Solution: Replace power window motors or switches
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Bulbs Burning Out Frequently**
    Description/Symptoms: Frequent need to replace headlight bulbs
    Solution: Upgrade to higher quality headlight bulbs
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    Description/Symptoms: Cracks forming on the dashboard
    Solution: Replace or repair dashboard
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1991 Cadillac Allante is a stylish and comfortable roadster with significant reliability issues. The most common problems include intake manifold gasket failure, transmission slipping, and electrical system malfunctions. These issues can be costly to fix, making the Allante a risky investment for some buyers.
