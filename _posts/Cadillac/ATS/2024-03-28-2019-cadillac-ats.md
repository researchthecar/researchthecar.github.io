**Introduction:**

The 2019 Cadillac ATS is a compact luxury sedan known for its sharp styling and agile handling. It is available with a range of turbocharged gasoline engines, paired with either a 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic transmission. The ATS is praised for its sporty performance, responsive steering, and comfortable interior. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

    * **Intake Valve Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Intake valves can become carbonized, leading to reduced engine performance, rough idle, and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent oil top-ups or resulting in engine failure.
        * Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine rebuild.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

    * **Ignition Coil Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Ignition coils can fail, causing misfires, reduced engine power, or engine stalling.
        * Solution: Replacing faulty ignition coils.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Fuel Injector Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clogged or faulty fuel injectors can lead to rough idle, poor acceleration, or engine stalling.
        * Solution: Replacing fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **3.6L V6**

    * **Cam Phaser Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of cam phasers, which control valve timing, can lead to reduced engine power, rough idle, or engine stalling.
        * Solution: Replacing cam phasers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Stretched or broken timing chains can cause engine damage or catastrophic failure.
        * Solution: Replacing timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**

    * **Shifter Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loose or imprecise shifting, difficulty shifting into gears.
        * Solution: Replacing or adjusting shifter components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **8-Speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hesitation, or delayed engagement of gears.
        * Solution: Replacing transmission solenoid or valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuttering during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Replacing torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Solution: Identifying and replacing faulty components or modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Replacing headlight seals or assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Lid Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Center console lid sticking or difficult to open.
    * Solution: Replacing or adjusting console lid components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow or unresponsive infotainment system, navigation issues, or Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Updating software or replacing system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Cadillac ATS has a somewhat mixed reliability record. While it offers impressive performance and handling, it is prone to certain engine, transmission, and electrical issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of repairs required. Owners should be aware of potential issues and consult with a trusted mechanic or dealership for timely maintenance and repairs.
