# 2017 Cadillac ATS Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Cadillac ATS offers sharp handling and energetic performance, but prospective buyers should carefully weigh known mechanical and electronic quirks. While its engine lineup delivers solid power, specific complaints regarding transmission shudder and electronics require close inspection during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 272 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering a lively and responsive driving experience. Regular maintenance and timely oil changes are vital to ensure long-term turbocharger health.

### 3.6L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 engine pushes out approximately 335 horsepower and 285 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth, linear power delivery. It is generally robust, making it a solid choice for buyers seeking traditional V6 performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the vehicle's powertrain options to offer quick gear changes, though some units have reported shifting anomalies and premature wear.

**Transmission Shudder and Harsh Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a noticeable shudder or vibration at high or low speeds, along with hesitation or rough lurching when the transmission shifts gears.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified mechanic; may require fluid flushes, component updates, or transmission service.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A 6-speed manual transmission was available on select turbo models, offering an engaging, hands-on driving dynamic for enthusiasts who prefer shifting their own gears.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is normally strong and confident, though sporadic pedal stiffness and shudder have been noted in owner complaints.

**Stiff Pedal and Reduced Braking Response**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal can occasionally feel exceptionally stiff or hard to depress, leading to extended stopping distances and shaking during application.
- **Solution:** Have the brake booster, fluid, and lines thoroughly inspected by a professional technician.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electronic power steering setup provides agile handling, but intermittent assist failures have been flagged by some owners.

**Intermittent Loss of Power Steering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may receive a dashboard warning message followed by a sudden loss of power steering assistance, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering components and address any active recall notices or electrical faults at a certified service center.

## Electrical

### CUE Infotainment System

The interior technology suite offers modern connectivity features, but the CUE infotainment display is prone to screen wear and electronic unresponsiveness over time.

**Touchscreen Failure and Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** The Cadillac User Experience (CUE) touchscreen may freeze, become unresponsive, develop webbed cracks, or fail completely, resulting in a loss of navigation and backup camera visibility.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty infotainment display unit or screen assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V086000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=ATS&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Loss of power steering assist would require a higher steering effort, especially at lower speeds, which may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the power steering gear assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V175000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=ATS&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A loss of power steering assist can require greater steering effort, especially at low speeds, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the power steering gear assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Cadillac ATS remains an engaging sport sedan with responsive handling and potent engine choices. Buyers should prioritize a thorough test drive to check for transmission shudder, verify that the infotainment and power steering systems function properly, and confirm that all relevant safety recalls have been completed.
