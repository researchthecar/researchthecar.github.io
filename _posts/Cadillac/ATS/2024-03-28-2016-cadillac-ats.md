**Introduction:**

The 2016 Cadillac ATS is a luxury compact sedan known for its sharp handling, stylish design, and high-quality interior. It comes with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder, a 2.5L 4-cylinder, and a 3.6L V6. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, 8-speed automatic, and 6-speed automatic.

Owners praise the ATS for its performance, luxury appointments, and sharp handling. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine power, rough idle, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, loss of power, engine failure
    * Solution: Replace timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L 4-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**3.6L V6**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from valve cover
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Runner Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine power, hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runner valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration during acceleration or deceleration
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 16V377000

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Audio System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No audio output, touchscreen unresponsive
    * Solution: Update software or replace audio system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Key Fob Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Key fob not starting vehicle, intermittent unlocking/locking
    * Solution: Replace key fob or have it reprogrammed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Backup Camera Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No image displayed on rearview camera
    * Solution: Replace rear backup camera
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, wind noise
    * Solution: Repair or replace sunroof motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working, warning lights illuminated
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2016 Cadillac ATS is a desirable vehicle, there have been several reported issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. Engine issues, such as intake manifold failure and timing chain failure, can be costly to repair. Transmission problems, like torque converter shudder, can also be expensive. Suspension issues, such as strut failure, can affect ride quality and handling. Electrical and interior problems, while less severe, can still be inconvenient and costly to fix. Overall, the 2016 Cadillac ATS may require more maintenance and repairs than some other vehicles in its class.
