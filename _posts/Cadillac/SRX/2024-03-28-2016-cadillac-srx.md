**Introduction**

The 2016 Cadillac SRX is a mid-size luxury SUV that offers a comfortable and refined ride. It comes with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a V6, a turbocharged V6, and a hybrid. The SRX has a spacious interior and a well-equipped cabin. However, it has been plagued by a number of issues that have affected its reliability and owner satisfaction.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

* **Intake manifold failure:** The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of engine power and a decrease in fuel economy. The solution is to replace the intake manifold. The estimated cost to fix is $1,000-$1,500+.
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip and damage the engine. The solution is to replace the timing chain tensioner and the timing chain. The estimated cost to fix is $2,000-$2,500+.

**2.8L Turbocharged V6**

* **Valve cover gasket leak:** The valve cover gasket can leak oil, which can cause a loss of oil and a decrease in engine performance. The solution is to replace the valve cover gasket. The estimated cost to fix is $500-$700+.

**Transmission**

**6-speed automatic transmission**

* **Shift flare:** The transmission can shift abruptly between gears, causing a shudder or jerk. The solution is to reprogram the transmission control module. The estimated cost to fix is $100-$200+.

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure:** The strut mount can fail, causing a clunking noise and a decrease in ride quality. The solution is to replace the strut mount. The estimated cost to fix is $200-$300+.
* **Sway bar end link failure:** The sway bar end link can fail, causing a rattling noise and a decrease in handling. The solution is to replace the sway bar end link. The estimated cost to fix is $100-$200+.

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start. The solution is to replace the battery. The estimated cost to fix is $100-$200+.
* **Headlight failure:** The headlights can fail prematurely, reducing visibility at night. The solution is to replace the headlights. The estimated cost to fix is $200-$300+.

**Exterior**

* **Roof rack crossbar failure:** The roof rack crossbars can fail, causing them to detach from the vehicle. The solution is to replace the roof rack crossbars. The estimated cost to fix is $200-$300+.

**Interior**

* **Climate control system failure:** The climate control system can fail, causing the vehicle to overheat or overcool. The solution is to replace the climate control system. The estimated cost to fix is $500-$1,000+.
* **Infotainment system failure:** The infotainment system can fail, causing the vehicle to lose audio, navigation, and climate control functionality. The solution is to replace the infotainment system. The estimated cost to fix is $500-$1,000+.

**Summary**

The 2016 Cadillac SRX has been plagued by a number of issues that have affected its reliability and owner satisfaction. The most serious issues include the intake manifold failure, the timing chain tensioner failure, and the climate control system failure. These issues can be expensive to fix and can leave the vehicle inoperable.
