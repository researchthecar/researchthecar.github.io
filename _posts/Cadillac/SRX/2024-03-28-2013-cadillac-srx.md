**Introduction**
The 2013 Cadillac SRX is a mid-size luxury SUV. It offers a single engine option, a 3.6L V6 producing 308 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic transmission and an 8-speed automatic transmission. The SRX is known for its stylish exterior, spacious cabin, and powerful engine. However, it has also received criticism for its fuel economy and interior quality.

**Engine**
* **3.6L V6**
  **Timing Chain Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or stall.
  Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioners.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
  **No Issues Found**
* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
  **Shifting Problems**
  Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift roughly or hesitate to shift gears.
  Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission solenoid pack.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front Suspension Struts**
  **Leaking**
  Description/Symptoms: The front suspension struts may leak fluid, causing the suspension to become unstable.
  Solution: Replace the front suspension struts.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery**
  **Premature Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: The battery may fail prematurely, leaving the vehicle stranded.
  Solution: Replace the battery.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**
  **Overcharging**
  Description/Symptoms: The alternator may overcharge the battery, causing the battery to explode or fail.
  Solution: Replace the alternator.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Rear Tail Light Assembly**
  **Moisture Intrusion**
  Description/Symptoms: Moisture may enter the rear tail light assembly, causing the lights to malfunction or fail.
  Solution: Replace the rear tail light assembly.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  Recalls: None Found
* **Windshield Wiper Cowl**
  **Cracking**
  Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper cowl may crack or break, allowing water to leak into the cabin.
  Solution: Replace the windshield wiper cowl.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Sunroof**
  **Leaks**
  Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak water into the cabin.
  Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or adjust the sunroof alignment.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  Recalls: None Found
* **Door Locks**
  **Malfunctioning**
  Description/Symptoms: The door locks may malfunction, preventing the doors from being opened or closed.
  Solution: Replace the door lock actuators.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster**
  **Fading**
  Description/Symptoms: The instrument cluster may fade or become difficult to read.
  Solution: Replace the instrument cluster.
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2013 Cadillac SRX has received positive reviews for its stylish exterior, spacious cabin, and powerful engine. However, it has also been criticized for its fuel economy and interior quality. Some owners have reported issues with the timing chain, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues vary widely, but some can be quite expensive to repair. As with any used vehicle, it's important to have it inspected by a qualified mechanic before purchase to assess its condition and identify any potential issues.
