**Introduction:**

The 2011 Cadillac SRX is a mid-size luxury crossover SUV that offers a combination of comfort, performance, and technology. It comes standard with a 3.0L V6 engine and a 6-speed automatic transmission, producing 268 horsepower and 223 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 2.8L V6 with 258 horsepower and a 3.6L V6 with 308 horsepower, both paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine burns excessive amounts of oil, leading to decreased oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine replacement or major rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $10,000+
* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the timing chain can lead to engine damage and costly repairs.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000

**2.8L V6**

* No Issues Found

**3.6L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leak in the intake manifold can cause rough idling, loss of power, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Harsh Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts roughly, causing uncomfortable and potentially dangerous driving conditions.
    * Solution: Service or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the front strut mounts can lead to noisy suspension, excessive tire wear, and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Electrical**

* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, stalls, or has difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace the ignition coils.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Identify and repair the electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof weatherstripping or make adjustments.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to regulate temperature or airflow.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty component in the HVAC system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* **Dash Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard components rattle when driving over rough roads.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace loose interior components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Summary:**

The 2011 Cadillac SRX is a comfortable and well-equipped luxury SUV, but it has some reliability concerns. The engine issues, particularly the excessive oil consumption and timing chain failure, are the most severe and expensive to fix. The transmission problems and electrical issues can also be costly to address. However, the interior and suspension issues are generally not as major or expensive to resolve. Overall, the 2011 Cadillac SRX requires careful consideration before purchasing, and prospective buyers should be aware of the potential mechanical issues.
