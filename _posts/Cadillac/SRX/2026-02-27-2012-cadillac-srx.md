### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Cadillac SRX demonstrates good mechanical reliability, with its V6 engines and automatic transmission generally performing without significant widespread issues. The primary areas of concern tend to be within the electrical system, specifically related to the infotainment unit and the power liftgate. These issues, while not universal, have been reported by a notable number of owners and can incur moderate repair costs. Owners should be aware of these potential electrical gremlins. Overall, the SRX offers a solid ownership experience if these specific electrical components are functioning correctly or are addressed promptly.
