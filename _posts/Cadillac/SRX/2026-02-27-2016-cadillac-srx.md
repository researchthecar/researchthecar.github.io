# 2016 Cadillac SRX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Cadillac SRX offers a comfortable ride, distinct styling, and a well-appointed cabin, making it an appealing option for luxury crossover shoppers. However, prospective buyers should review key owner complaints regarding lighting performance, suspension campaigns, and infotainment glitches to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 308 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque, providing confident acceleration for daily driving and highway passing. Generally considered robust, though routine maintenance is essential to prevent performance hiccups.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It delivers smooth shifts under normal conditions, though owners should inspect fluid health during a pre-purchase evaluation.

## Suspension

### Suspension and Control Arms

Suspension components generally hold up well, but rear suspension structural concerns and recall campaigns require careful verification before purchase.

**Suspension Control Arm Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Rear suspension issues, vehicle handling instability, or notification regarding NHTSA suspension safety campaigns.
- **Solution:** Verify that all relevant suspension recall repairs and alignment checks have been properly performed by a certified dealer.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

The exterior lighting system exhibits significant owner complaints centering around premature dimming of the low beams, often attributed to heat degradation within the reflector housings.

**Dim Low Beam Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Low beam headlights become extremely dim, making it difficult to see the road clearly at night and forcing drivers to rely on high beams.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlamp assemblies for reflector damage or condensation; replacing the complete headlamp assembly may be required.
- **Cost:** $3,000

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Standard safety equipment functions reliably overall, though isolated complaints point to mechanical binding in passenger seat belt retractors.

**Passenger Seat Belt Jamming**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger-side seat belt fails to extend properly, preventing occupants from buckling up securely.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt retractor mechanism and replace faulty seat belt assemblies as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V473000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=SRX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The rear suspension toe link adjuster may not have been tightened properly, which could result in a loose toe link. A loose toe link can separate and cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the adjustable toe link with a non-adjustable one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V329000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=SRX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The front-driver air bag inflator may rupture during deployment. An inflator rupture may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-driver air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used Cadillac SRX, prioritize models with documented service history and verified completion of safety recalls. Pay close attention to the headlight output during a nighttime test drive and ensure all electronic features operate properly before finalizing your purchase.
