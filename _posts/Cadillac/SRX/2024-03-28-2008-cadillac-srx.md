**Introduction**
The 2008 Cadillac SRX is a midsize luxury crossover SUV that debuted in 2003. It was significantly redesigned for the 2008 model year, offering a choice between a 3.6L V6 or a 4.6L V8 engine. Both engines were paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The SRX was praised for its stylish design, spacious interior, and comfortable ride, but it also faced some criticism for its less-than-stellar fuel economy.

**Engine**
* **3.6L V6**
 - **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
   - Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating engine, poor engine performance
   - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   - Recalls: None Found
 - **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, check engine light
   - Solution: Replace camshaft position sensor
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
   - Recalls: None Found
* **4.6L V8**
 - **Timing Chain Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, loss of power, check engine light
   - Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
 - **Slipping Gears**
   - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping out of gear
   - Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
   - Recalls: None Found
 - **Torque Converter Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Transmission overheating, shuddering, slipping gears
   - Solution: Replace torque converter
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
 - **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear**
   - Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noise from suspension, uneven tire wear
   - Solution: Replace worn bushings
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
   - Recalls: None Found
 - **Rear Sway Bar Links Worn**
   - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noise from rear suspension, excessive body roll
   - Solution: Replace sway bar links
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
 - **Headlight Switch Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, turn signals not working
   - Solution: Replace headlight switch
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
   - Recalls: None Found
 - **Power Window Motor Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working or making grinding noise
   - Solution: Replace power window motor
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
 - **Tailgate Latch Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Tailgate not opening or closing properly
   - Solution: Replace tailgate latch
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
 - **Climate Control Module Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not working, fan not blowing air
   - Solution: Replace climate control module
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   - Recalls: None Found
 - **Radio Display Screen Failure**
   - Description/Symptoms: Radio display screen not working, dim or distorted display
   - Solution: Replace radio display screen
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2008 Cadillac SRX has several common issues that owners should be aware of. While most of the problems are relatively minor and inexpensive to repair, some, such as the timing chain failure and transmission slipping, can be costly to fix. Overall, the SRX is a reliable and well-built vehicle that delivers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience.
