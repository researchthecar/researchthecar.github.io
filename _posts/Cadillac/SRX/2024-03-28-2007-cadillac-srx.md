**Introduction:**
The 2007 Cadillac SRX is a luxury SUV that offers a comfortable ride and a powerful engine. The SRX is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.6L V6 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 3.6L V6 engine produces 255 horsepower and 254 lb-ft of torque. The SRX is praised for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and smooth ride. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components of the vehicle.

**Engine**
* **3.6L V6**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing coolant and oil leaks. Symptoms include overheating, loss of coolant, and decreased engine performance.
        * Solution: The intake manifold gasket must be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing a rattling noise from the engine. If the tensioner fails completely, the timing chain can skip or break, resulting in catastrophic engine failure.
        * Solution: The timing chain tensioner must be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears. In some cases, the transmission may refuse to shift into certain gears.
        * Solution: The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front suspension strut mounts**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front suspension strut mounts can wear out, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: The strut mounts must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension bushings can crack or tear, causing a squealing noise when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: The rear suspension bushings must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Electrical gremlins**
    * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported experiencing electrical issues such as malfunctioning instrument panel gauges, flickering headlights, and dead batteries.
    * Solution: The electrical system may need to be inspected and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Sunroof leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak during heavy rain or car washes.
    * Solution: The sunroof seals must be inspected and repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Climate control issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not blow cold air or heat properly.
    * Solution: The climate control system may need to be repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2007 Cadillac SRX is a reliable vehicle overall, but it has been known to experience certain issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicles regularly inspected and maintained to avoid major problems.
