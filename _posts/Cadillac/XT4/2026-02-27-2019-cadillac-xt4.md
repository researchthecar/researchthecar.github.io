# 2019 Cadillac XT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2019 Cadillac XT4, buyers will find a stylish compact luxury SUV powered by a punchy turbocharged engine paired with a modern automatic transmission. While it offers nimble handling and a comfortable cabin, used car shoppers should review owner feedback concerning powertrain performance and specific accessory wear. Paying close attention to mechanical service records and thoroughly testing all vehicle functions during a pre-purchase inspection can help uncover potential upkeep items before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive daily acceleration. Buyers should routinely inspect the upper engine bay for signs of oil seepage around the sealing surfaces.

**Camshaft Carrier Oil Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported discovering oil leaks during routine inspections, stemming from a faulty seal on the camshaft carrier.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the upper engine seals and reseal or replace the faulty camshaft carrier gasket as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. Prospective buyers should carefully evaluate shift quality during a test drive to ensure proper operation.

**Transmission Shudder and Coolant Contamination**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience bucking or shuddering during acceleration, accompanied by tachometer RPM fluctuations at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transmission cooling system for internal leaks and repair or replace the transmission assembly if coolant contamination has caused internal component failure.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Ensures vehicle deceleration through anti-lock disc brakes and hydraulic pressure lines.

**Brake Pedal Travel Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may suddenly travel all the way to the floor without generating adequate stopping force, creating a severe safety hazard.
- **Solution:** Have the master cylinder, brake lines, and hydraulic fluid pressure inspected immediately by a professional technician.

## Steering

### Suspension and Steering Components

Provides ride comfort and handling stability via independent front and rear suspension layouts.

**Front-End Squeaking Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an annoying squeaking sound coming from the front suspension when navigating over speed bumps or making tight turns.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or replace worn suspension bushings and check related pivot points for excessive friction or wear.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Manages exterior illumination features, including headlights and signature daytime running lights.

**Daytime Running Light (DRL) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The daytime running lights or turn signal assemblies may appear dim, flicker, or stop working entirely due to excessive heat buildup near the circuit board.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed running light modules and ensure proper thermal management or updated components are installed to prevent recurring overheating.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V668000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Missing bolts on the start-stop accumulator endcap could result in a transmission oil leak and may progress to a loss of propulsion, increasing the risk of a crash. A transmission fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the start-stop transmission accumulator and replace it if any bolts are missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V649000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly secured seat belt retractor may not function properly in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the rear seat belt retractors, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V868000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the rearview camera image to display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the VPM software or replace the VPM, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Cadillac XT4 presents a mix of modern luxury features and compact utility, but buyers should approach with a healthy degree of mindfulness. While powertrain options provide engaging performance, potential issues involving upper engine oil leaks, transmission integrity, and lighting durability warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Verifying service history and confirming all electronic accessories operate correctly will significantly reduce the risk of unexpected repair bills.
