**Introduction:**

The 2023 Cadillac XT4 is a compact luxury crossover SUV that offers a combination of style, performance, and comfort. It features a 2.0L turbocharged engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The XT4 is praised for its spacious interior, responsive handling, and impressive technology features. However, certain issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Engine:**
    * **Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Engine replacement or overhaul.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or grinding noises, decreased performance, and potential catastrophic engine failure.
        * Solution: Timing chain and tensioner replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shift Hesitation and Jerking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifting, causing uncomfortable driving and potential transmission damage.
        * Solution: Software updates or transmission replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Rearview Camera Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera display goes blank or shows distorted images, impairing visibility.
        * Solution: Camera replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $750
        * Recalls: 22V621

**Exterior**

* **Window Trim Peeling:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Black trim around the windows peels or flakes off, affecting the vehicle's aesthetics.
        * Solution: Trim replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Noise and Leaks:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise or water leakage from the panoramic sunroof.
        * Solution: Sunroof adjustment or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Loose Door Panels:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Door panels rattle or come loose, causing a distracting noise.
        * Solution: Panel tightening or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Cadillac XT4 has received generally positive reviews, but some issues have been reported. The most significant concerns are related to engine oil consumption and timing chain tensioner failure, which can lead to costly repairs. Other issues, such as rearview camera failure and window trim peeling, are less severe but can still be frustrating for owners. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues vary, and it is recommended to consult with a qualified mechanic for accurate diagnosis and repair estimates.
