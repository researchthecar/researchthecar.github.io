The 2022 Cadillac XT4 is a compact luxury SUV that offers a blend of style, comfort, and technology. The standard and most common engine is a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 producing 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. All XT4 models come standard with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Some of the things people appreciate about the XT4 include its comfortable ride, user-friendly infotainment system, and attractive interior. Common complaints often revolve around the engine's fuel economy not matching expectations, a smaller cargo area compared to some competitors, and some feeling the interior materials aren't quite up to par for the luxury segment.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

*   **Engine Misfires**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, check engine light illuminated. Often caused by faulty ignition coils or spark plugs.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils and/or spark plugs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Low oil level warning light, needing to add oil frequently between oil changes, blue smoke from the exhaust in extreme cases.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis can involve checking for leaks, performing an oil consumption test, and potentially replacing valve stem seals or piston rings in more severe cases.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 for leak fixes, $1000 - $5000+ for more extensive internal repairs.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Turbocharger Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noises from the engine, decreased power, check engine light illuminated. Can be caused by a failing turbocharger.
    *   Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable clunking or jerking during gear changes, hesitation when accelerating, especially during downshifts.
    *   Solution: Software updates to the transmission control module (TCM) or replacement of valve body.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 for software update, $800 - $2000+ for valve body replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Transmission Slipping**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine revving high without a corresponding increase in vehicle speed. The transmission feels like it's struggling to engage.
    *   Solution: Possible causes include low transmission fluid, worn clutch packs, or valve body issues. May require a transmission rebuild or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 for fluid change and inspection, $3000 - $7000+ for rebuild or replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Premature Bushing Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises from the suspension, especially when going over bumps. Loose or vague steering feel.
    *   Solution: Replace worn bushings.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ per corner, depending on which bushings need replacement and labor rates.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen freezes, becomes unresponsive, or restarts unexpectedly.
    *   Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 for software update (if covered under warranty), $500 - $1500+ for unit replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, especially when the vehicle is parked for extended periods. Difficulty starting the car.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and repair parasitic drains, replace the battery if it's faulty, or update the vehicle's software.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 for diagnosis, $150 - $400 for battery replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin, especially after rain.
    * Solution: Clean sunroof drains and seals. If it's a damaged seal, it may require replacing the entire seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200 for cleaning, $200 - $600+ for seal replacement
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Seat Heater Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working, only partially working, or overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty seat heater element or control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Cadillac XT4, while offering a luxurious experience, does have some common issues reported by owners. Engine and transmission problems, such as misfires, oil consumption, harsh shifting, and slipping, can range from relatively inexpensive fixes like spark plug replacements to more costly repairs involving turbochargers or transmission overhauls. Electrical problems with the infotainment system and battery drain can also be frustrating. While not all owners will experience these issues, it is important to be aware of them when considering a used 2022 Cadillac XT4. The severity and cost of potential repairs can vary widely, so a thorough inspection by a qualified mechanic is recommended before purchasing.
