**Introduction**

The 2022 Cadillac XT4 is a compact luxury SUV known for its sleek design, comfortable interior, and advanced technology features. It's available with two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 and a 3.6L V6. Both engines are paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 produces 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.6L V6 produces 310 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque. The XT4 has received praise for its smooth ride, spacious cabin, and intuitive infotainment system. However, some users have reported specific issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Oil Leak from Engine Pan Gasket**
  * Description/Symptoms: Leak from the gasket between the engine pan and the engine block, resulting in oil loss.
  * Solution: Replace the engine pan gasket.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Jerking or Hesitation During Gear Shifts**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shudders or hesitates when shifting gears.
  * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission solenoids.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Squeaking or Clunking Noises from Suspension**
  * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the suspension, especially when driving over uneven surfaces.
  * Solution: Replace or lubricate suspension components (e.g., control arms, bushings).
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Crashing or Freezing**
  * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment display doesn't respond or freezes, losing access to features like navigation or audio.
  * Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment module.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Not Opening Properly**
  * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate doesn't open or close smoothly, or fails to open completely.
  * Solution: Lubricate or replace tailgate components (e.g., hinges, latch).
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Panel Separating from Tracks**
  * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof panel becomes loose or rattles, potentially detaching from its tracks.
  * Solution: Realign or replace sunroof panel.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2022 Cadillac XT4 is a well-regarded vehicle, it has had some reported issues with specific components. Engine oil leaks and transmission shifting problems are among the most common. The cost to fix these issues can vary depending on the severity of the issue and the specific parts that need to be replaced. It's important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to schedule regular maintenance and repairs as recommended by the manufacturer.
