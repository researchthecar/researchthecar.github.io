# 2022 Cadillac XT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2022 Cadillac XT4 means looking at a stylish and comfortable compact luxury crossover equipped with modern technology. While overall ownership feedback remains relatively quiet, buyers should review standard wear items and powertrain options carefully during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. It features modern direct-injection technology and generally operates reliably when routine maintenance schedules are followed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, offering smooth gear transitions on the highway. Buyers should test-drive the vehicle across various speeds to ensure crisp shifting behavior.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Owner reports indicate occasional brake noise during reverse maneuvers, though mechanical wear components generally perform as expected.

**Morning Brake Squeaking**
- **Symptoms:** The front brakes produce a squeaking noise when backing out of a garage or driveway during initial morning startups, though the noise typically disappears for the rest of the drive cycle.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for normal wear and ensure anti-rattle clips or pad guides are properly lubricated, though dealers may note pads have adequate thickness.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V359000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly welded seat frame may not adequately restrain the driver in a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the driver's seat and replace the cushion frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Cadillac XT4 presents a refined driving experience with minimal widespread mechanical complaints in early owner data. Prospective buyers should perform a standard test drive and check for minor cosmetic or acoustic annoyances like cold-start brake noises during an inspection.
