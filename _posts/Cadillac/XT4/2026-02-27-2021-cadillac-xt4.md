# 2021 Cadillac XT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Cadillac XT4, prospective buyers will find a stylish compact luxury crossover powered by a modern turbocharged engine. While many owners enjoy its comfort and features, owner complaints have surfaced regarding isolated electrical and fuel system concerns. Reviewing maintenance records and verifying that applicable powertrain updates have been completed can help ensure a more confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive daily power. While generally robust, buyers should monitor for sporadic fuel control module issues reported by some owners.

**Fuel Pump Control Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalling in traffic or crank without starting due to a failing fuel pump power control module.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the fuel system codes (such as P203F) and replace the faulty fuel pump control module.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth shifting across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. No widespread recurring mechanical failure patterns are currently highlighted in owner complaints.

## Electrical

### Audio and Chime System

Cabin electronics manage infotainment and safety chimes, though occasional glitches have been reported by owners.

**Intermittent Audio and Warning Chime Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Upon starting the vehicle, the audio system, turn signal clicks, and critical seatbelt warning chimes may produce no sound.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a dealership or specialist to check for software updates or faulty infotainment modules controlling cabin alerts.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting assemblies provide visibility but require inspection for premature component wear.

**Daytime Running Light Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Daytime running lights (DRL) may fail and show signs of burning or melting, potentially causing a vehicle to fail state inspections.
- **Solution:** Inspect the lighting assembly and replace the affected DRL unit or wiring harness components.

### Sunroof

Glass components like the sunroof add cabin light but can occasionally experience stress fractures.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof may shatter spontaneously while driving, often accompanied by a loud bang with no external impact evident.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the sunroof frame for alignment issues or stress points.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety restraint systems maintain occupant protection, though sensor or wiring faults can trigger warning lights.

**Side Airbag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The safety restraint warning light illuminates on the dashboard, indicating a fault within the supplemental restraint system.
- **Solution:** Have the dealership inspect the airbag wiring and replace faulty side airbag components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V649000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Cadillac&model=XT4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An improperly secured seat belt retractor may not function properly in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the rear seat belt retractors, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Cadillac XT4 offers engaging luxury features and everyday drivability, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to electrical functions, infotainment operation, and any history of engine stalling related to fuel control modules. Verifying service history and ensuring open campaigns or recalls are addressed will promote a more dependable ownership experience.
