# 2024 Cadillac XT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Cadillac XT4 is a compact luxury crossover offering sharp styling and a comfortable cabin. However, used car buyers should carefully review owner-reported issues involving unexpected forward collision braking and fuel system components. While many modern tech features enhance daily driving, electronic and powertrain quirks require thorough pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. It serves as the standard powerplant for the lineup, though buyers should watch out for reported fuel control module glitches.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Pump Module Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly lose power or stall while driving in traffic, occasionally accompanied by starting difficulties and unexpected shifts into neutral.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel pump driver control module and fuel system components; faulty modules must be replaced.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear changes across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. Overall mechanical durability is generally sound, though occasional shifting hesitation has been noted by some owners.

## Steering

### Power Steering Assembly

Electronic power steering offers light maneuverability, though rare startup faults should be verified during a test drive.

**Intermittent Steering Stiffness**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may occasionally experience a completely locked or heavily resistant steering wheel upon vehicle startup, requiring a car restart to resolve.
- **Solution:** Scan diagnostic trouble codes for electronic power steering modules and update software or replace failing steering sensors.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Display

Cabin electronics provide modern connectivity, but occasional screen blackouts and camera glitches have been reported.

**Backup Camera Failure and Instrument Panel Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera may fail to function properly in low-light conditions, or the instrument panel may temporarily lose power following warning messages.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring connections, software versions, and instrument cluster modules; replace failing components as needed.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety systems are standard, but sensitive sensor tuning can lead to false activations.

**Unexpected Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system may unexpectedly engage while driving or pulling away from a stop, creating a risk of rear-end collisions.
- **Solution:** Check for available software updates or sensor recalibrations at a certified dealership to ensure proper collision system calibration.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Cadillac XT4 offers appealing luxury features and comfortable road manners, but used car buyers should perform a rigorous test drive and inspection. Pay close attention to electrical systems, driver assistance sensors, and engine starting behavior to ensure trouble-free ownership.
