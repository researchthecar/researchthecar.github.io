# 2023 Cadillac XT4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Cadillac XT4, buyers will find a stylish compact luxury crossover powered by a modern turbocharged engine paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission. While owner feedback highlights some concerns regarding unexpected electronic behavior and powertrain operation, reviewing specific owner-reported experiences can help you evaluate a prospective vehicle before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate daily power for the luxury crossover. It is generally regarded as a competent powerplant, though buyers should watch for occasional reports of fuel module or electrical power interruptions.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Hydra-Matic Automatic Transmission

The nine-speed automatic transmission routes power through front-wheel drive or available all-wheel drive. While designed for efficiency, some drivers experience hesitation or shifting quirks in lower or higher gears.

**Transmission Shifting and Engagement Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that the transmission may fail to automatically shift into ninth gear or can feel jerky and unrefined during low-speed deceleration in gears one through three.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealer or qualified technician for software updates or diagnostic evaluations, noting that some shifting behaviors may be characterized by manufacturers as normal operational parameters.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment System

The vehicle's electrical architecture controls multiple comfort and safety conveniences, with occasional owner complaints centered on infotainment and accessory glitches.

**Infotainment and Turn Signal Audio Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced sudden loss of sound from the infotainment system, coupled with non-functional turn signal indicator clicks and auto-stop features.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the infotainment module and software configuration, as hard resets or software reflashes may be required.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior glass and body panels generally hold up well, though isolated structural glass incidents have been documented.

**Unexpected Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the rear portion of the panoramic sunroof shatters spontaneously while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the glass assembly completely replaced and inspect the frame for any structural stress points or defects.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety suites are designed to protect occupants, though sensor calibration sensitivities can occasionally lead to unexpected braking inputs reported by owners.

**Phantom Braking Events**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note instances where the automatic emergency braking system engages unexpectedly without an immediate obstacle or collision risk present.
- **Solution:** Inspect sensor alignments and check for any available manufacturer software calibrations for the collision avoidance system.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Cadillac XT4 offers a compelling blend of luxury features and efficient performance, but prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical functions, transmission shift quality, and advanced safety systems during a test drive. Checking service records and ensuring all software updates have been performed can mitigate potential ownership friction.
