# 2007 Cadillac BLS

### Overview
The 2007 Cadillac BLS, a model primarily sold in European markets, shares its platform with the Saab 9-3. As such, its reliability data often reflects components and engineering common to that era of GM-Europe vehicles. While generally considered a competent vehicle, certain areas have shown a propensity for specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of potential reliability concerns for owners and prospective buyers. The BLS offered a range of diesel engines and automatic transmissions, each with its own set of potential challenges.

---


## Engine

### 1.9L JTDm Diesel (Z19DTJ/Z19DTH)
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, engine running rough.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, poor performance, increased emissions.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 2.8L V6 Turbo (Z28NET)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of the turbocharger.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (AF23/AF33)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1800

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission warning light, failure to shift gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission solenoid pack.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Body Control Module (BCM) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, warning lights, issues with power windows, locks, or lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS/ESP)**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated ABS or ESP warning lights, faulty readings from wheel speed sensors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400 per sensor

## Interior

### Cabin Electronics and Trim
**Climate Control Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or airflow direction, unusual noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty HVAC actuators.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or potential head unit repair/replacement.

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Cadillac BLS, while offering a comfortable and stylish package, exhibits some common issues associated with its GM-Europe platform. The diesel engines, particularly the 1.9L variants, are prone to DPF and EGR system problems, which can be costly to repair. Automatic transmissions, especially the 5-speed, may develop issues with the torque converter or solenoid pack. Electrical gremlins, often related to the BCM or various sensors, can also surface. While the suspension, exterior, and interior are generally robust, owners should be aware of potential climate control and infotainment system quirks. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for mitigating these potential reliability concerns.
