**Introduction:**

The 2007 Cadillac Escalade EXT is a full-size luxury pickup truck that combines rugged capability with premium amenities. It features a powerful 6.0L V8 engine, which produces 345 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, and a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Escalade EXT has a spacious cabin with seating for up to six, and it offers a range of features, including a DVD entertainment system, navigation, and a sunroof. Despite its strengths, the Escalade EXT has been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the intake manifold gaskets, leading to overheating and loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacing the intake manifold gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: 16V899000, 19V045000
* **Piston Ring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption and blue or white smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Overboring the engine cylinders and installing new pistons and rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or clicking noises from the engine, especially at startup.
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain tensioner, timing chain, and sprockets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: 14V040000

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips, hesitates, or refuses to shift.
    * Solution: Replacing the shift solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Upper Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noises from the front suspension, especially over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacing the upper control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear of the vehicle sits lower than the front, causing uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacing the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replacing the defective battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical components not working, or difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replacing the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: 05V329000

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate does not open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replacing the tailgate latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Seat Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power seats do not move or move erratically.
    * Solution: Replacing the power seat motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appear on the dashboard, especially on the passenger side.
    * Solution: Replacing the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Cadillac Escalade EXT has several reported issues, ranging from engine and transmission problems to electrical and interior concerns. While it offers a powerful engine and a luxurious cabin, the vehicle's reliability may be a concern for some potential buyers. The severity and cost of the issues vary, with some being relatively minor and others potentially expensive to fix.
