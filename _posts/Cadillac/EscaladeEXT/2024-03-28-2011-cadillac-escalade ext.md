**Introduction**

The 2011 Cadillac Escalade EXT is a full-size luxury pickup truck with impressive performance and a luxurious interior. It comes equipped with a powerful 6.2L V8 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, delivering 403 horsepower and 417 lb-ft of torque.

Owners appreciate the Escalade EXT's spacious cabin, comfortable seating, and advanced technology features. However, it has also received criticism for its poor fuel economy and limited towing capacity compared to other heavy-duty trucks.

**Engine**

* **6.2L V8**

  * **Premature Head Bolt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cylinder head bolts can stretch or break, causing coolant leaks, engine overheating, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of affected cylinder head bolts and potentially the cylinder heads.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the intake manifold gaskets can cause a vacuum loss, resulting in rough idling, reduced engine performance, and check engine light activation.
    * Solution: Replacement of failed intake manifold gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

  * **Clunking Noises and Hard Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may exhibit clunking noises or hesitate and jolt during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty transmission components such as solenoids, torque converter, or transmission gears.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 13V043000
  * **Fluid Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks can cause reduced fluid levels, leading to slipping gears, rough shifting, and premature transmission failure.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of leaking seals or gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Worn lower control arm bushings can result in excessive play, causing clunking noises, reduced handling stability, and uneven tire wear.
  * Solution: Replacement of worn bushings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Overheating:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Overheating of electrical components can cause various electrical failures, including instrument cluster malfunctions, power window issues, or battery drain.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components or wiring harness.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Depends on the nature of the issue, but can range from $500 to $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially when the vehicle is parked.
  * Solution: Identification and disconnection of parasitic battery drains.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Liftgate Corrosion:**
  * Description/Symptoms: The rear liftgate can develop corrosion around the license plate area, causing rust and compromising the liftgate's structural integrity.
  * Solution: Repair or replacement of the affected liftgate.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Heated and Ventilated Seat Malfunctions:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Heated and ventilated seats may fail to function properly, causing discomfort for occupants.
  * Solution: Replacement of faulty heating or cooling elements, or repair of electrical connections.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Cadillac Escalade EXT offers impressive performance and luxury, but it comes with a few notable issues. The premature head bolt failure on the 6.2L V8 engine can be a significant concern, potentially leading to expensive repairs. The transmission issues with clunking and hard shifting can also be frustrating for owners. While the electrical and exterior issues are less severe, they can still cause inconvenience and detract from the overall ownership experience. The severity of the issues and potential repair costs should be considered before purchasing an Escalade EXT.
