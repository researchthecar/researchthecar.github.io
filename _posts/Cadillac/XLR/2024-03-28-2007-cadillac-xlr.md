**Introduction:**

The 2007 Cadillac XLR is a luxury roadster powered by a 4.6-liter Northstar V8 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The XLR was praised for its sleek styling, powerful engine, and comfortable interior but criticized for its high price and lack of rear seats.

**Engine**

* **Northstar 4.6L V8**

**Transmission**

* **6-speed Automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely.
    * Solution: Replace battery and check for electrical leaks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200+
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Alternator fails to charge the battery.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$700
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Starter fails to engage the engine.
    * Solution: Replace starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400
* **Ignition Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Ignition switch fails to turn on the engine.
    * Solution: Replace ignition switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$300

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Lens Fogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlight lenses become foggy or cloudy.
    * Solution: Replace headlight lenses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400

**Interior**

* **Radio Display Pixelation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Pixels on the radio display become distorted or fade.
    * Solution: Replace radio unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800
* **Dashboard Trim Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard trim pieces rattle or squeak when driving.
    * Solution: Secure or replace loose trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200

**Summary:**

While the 2007 Cadillac XLR is a stylish and powerful vehicle, it suffers from several electrical and interior issues. The cost of repairs for these issues can vary significantly, but many can be resolved for under $500.
