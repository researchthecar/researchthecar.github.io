**Introduction:**

The 2023 Cadillac CT5 is a mid-size luxury sedan praised for its stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and impressive performance. Engine options include a turbocharged 2.0L inline-4, a turbocharged 3.0L V6, and a 4.2L Blackwing V8. Transmission options include a 10-speed automatic and a 6-speed manual (only available with the Blackwing V8).

**Known Issues:**

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
    * **Intake Valve Deposit Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on intake valves reduces engine performance, causing rough idling, hesitation, and decreased fuel economy.
        * Solution: Intake valve cleaning or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the timing chain tensioner can lead to a loose timing chain, causing engine noise, decreased performance, and potential catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * **Fuel Injectors Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sticking fuel injectors can cause rough idling, difficulty starting, and poor fuel economy.
        * Solution: Replacement of fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* **3.0L Turbocharged V6**
    * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the high-pressure fuel pump can cause starting issues, loss of power, and check engine light illumination.
        * Solution: Replacement of high-pressure fuel pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **4.2L Blackwing V8**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **10-Speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering when shifting gears, particularly at low speeds.
        * Solution: Replacement of torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking or worn shock absorbers result in poor handling and reduced ride comfort.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Rearview Camera Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera becomes blurry, distorted, or inoperable.
    * Solution: Replacement of rearview camera
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains rapidly even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Troubleshooting and repair of electrical system, often involving replacement of faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Wheel Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy or grinding sounds while driving, particularly when turning.
    * Solution: Replacement of wheel bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+

**Interior**

* **Instrument Panel Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or permanent failures of the instrument panel, including display blackouts or inaccurate readings.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of instrument panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, resulting in difficulty maintaining desired temperature or excessive noise.
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Summary:**

While the 2023 Cadillac CT5 is generally a reliable vehicle, it has experienced some issues that can be costly to repair. Owners have reported problems with engine valve deposits, timing chain tensioner failures, and high-pressure fuel pump failures. Transmission issues, such as torque converter shudder, have also been reported. The suspension has experienced rear shock absorber failures, while the electrical system has seen rearview camera failures and battery drain issues. Interior problems include instrument panel glitches and climate control issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem and required repairs.
