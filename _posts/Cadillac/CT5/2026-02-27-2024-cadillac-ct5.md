# 2024 Cadillac CT5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Cadillac CT5, buyers will find a modern luxury sedan offering a blend of sharp styling and engaging dynamics. While early owner reports to safety and regulatory bodies are relatively sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect specific areas like body weatherstripping and supplemental restraint systems during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard power plant for base models. It offers responsive daily drivability and respectable fuel economy for a luxury midsize sedan.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 delivers an upgraded output of roughly 335 horsepower (or higher in V-Series trims), providing robust acceleration and passing power. It is a sophisticated engine option that requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 10-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options across various rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally delivers smooth gear changes and helps optimize highway efficiency.

## Exterior

### Door and Trunk Moldings

Exterior body seals and moldings protect the cabin and interior frame areas from moisture ingress.

**Water Intrusion in Door Frames**
- **Symptoms:** Water accumulates inside the door and trunk frames after heavy rain or washing the vehicle, which owners fear may lead to long-term rust.
- **Solution:** Inspect weatherstripping and door seals for proper alignment and seal integrity, replacing any compromised rubber components.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

The supplemental restraint system is designed to protect occupants during severe impacts.

**Failure to Deploy**
- **Symptoms:** Air bags failed to deploy during a severe collision, raising concerns about supplemental restraint system functionality.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the vehicle's diagnostic trouble codes and sensor connections if any restraint warnings appear.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Cadillac CT5 remains a compelling luxury sedan option, backed by refined powertrain choices. Because owner feedback indicates occasional isolated concerns regarding body sealing and safety equipment, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a careful review of vehicle history are strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
