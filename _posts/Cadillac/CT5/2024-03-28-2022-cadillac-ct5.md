**Introduction**
The 2022 Cadillac CT5 is a midsize luxury sedan powered by a choice of two turbocharged engines: a 2.0-liter with 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque or a 3.0-liter with 335 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a 10-speed automatic transmission. The CT5 is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and sharp handling, but some owners have reported various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Engine**
* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  * Solution: Engine replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000+
* **Turbocharger Failure:** Premature failure of the turbocharger, resulting in reduced performance or engine stalling.
  * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V802

**3.0-Liter Engine**
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Weak tensioner can allow the timing chain to slip, causing engine damage.
  * Solution: Replacement of timing chain and tensioner
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V764

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Shifting Hesitation:** Transmission delays or hesitates when shifting gears.
  * Solution: Transmission software update or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Transmission Slipping:** Transmission slipping between gears, causing loss of power or sudden acceleration.
  * Solution: Transmission replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V795

**Suspension**
* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:** Premature degradation of lower control arm bushings, resulting in increased noise and vibration.
  * Solution: Replacement of bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** Premature battery discharge due to electrical faults.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* **Infotainment System Issues:** Malfunctioning infotainment system with various problems, including freezing, crashing, and connectivity issues.
  * Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment unit
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Exterior**
* **Roof Antenna Malfunction:** Broken or jammed roof antenna affecting radio reception.
  * Solution: Antenna replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**
* **Windshield Defroster Malfunction:** Windshield defroster may not work effectively, causing reduced visibility in cold weather.
  * Solution: Defroster unit replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
* **Headliner Sagging:** Headliner material may sag or detach from the ceiling.
  * Solution: Replacement of headliner
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000

**Summary**
Overall, the 2022 Cadillac CT5 has received positive reviews, but some owners have encountered various issues with their vehicles. The severity and cost of the reported issues range from minor annoyances to significant repairs. It is important to note that not all CT5 owners will experience these problems, and the reliability of individual vehicles may vary.
