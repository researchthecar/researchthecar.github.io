**Introduction:**

The 1994 Cadillac Fleetwood was a full-size luxury sedan offered with two engine and two transmission options. The standard engine was a 5.7L V8 producing 260 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, paired with a four-speed automatic transmission. An optional 4.9L V8 produced 200 horsepower and 290 lb-ft of torque, paired with a three-speed automatic transmission.

Owners appreciate the Fleetwood's spacious interior, comfortable ride, and classic styling. However, it has received criticism for its lack of modern features and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idle, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.9L V8**

* **Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the bottom of the engine
    * Solution: Replace oil pan gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Four-Speed Automatic**

* **Overdrive Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No power in overdrive gear, slipping, harsh shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Three-Speed Automatic**

* **TCC Lock-Up Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration at highway speeds
    * Solution: Replace torque converter clutch solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts Leaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shocks Leaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, body roll in turns
    * Solution: Replace rear shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, battery warning light illuminated
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: 94V-015, 94V-016
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Weatherstripping Deterioration**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks, wind noise, drafts
    * Solution: Replace weatherstripping
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to operate power windows
    * Solution: Replace power window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1994 Cadillac Fleetwood has some common issues, including intake manifold gasket failure, head gasket failure, and transmission problems. These issues can range in severity and cost to fix, but most are relatively minor. Overall, the Fleetwood is a reliable and comfortable vehicle with a few known weaknesses.
