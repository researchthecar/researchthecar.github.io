**Introduction:**

The 1990 Cadillac Fleetwood was a full-size luxury sedan offered with two engine and transmission options:

* 5.7L V8 with 4L60 4-speed automatic transmission (200 hp, 300 lb-ft torque)
* 5.0L V8 with 4L60 4-speed automatic transmission (185 hp, 285 lb-ft torque)

Known for its spacious cabin, smooth ride, and stately appearance, the Fleetwood also had its share of issues.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, reduced engine performance
* **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**5.0L V8**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**4L60 4-speed Automatic Transmission**

**Delayed or Harsh Shifting:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission hesitates or shifts hard, especially during gear changes 1-2 or 2-3
* **Solution:** Flushing transmission fluid and filter, replacing solenoids or valve body
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension Leakage:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Sagging or uneven ride height, dashboard warning lights
* **Solution:** Replace faulty air springs, compressor, or air lines
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Dimming lights, electrical system failures, difficulty starting
* **Solution:** Replace alternator
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Lock Actuator Malfunction:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Doors do not lock or unlock properly, dashboard warning lights
* **Solution:** Replace door lock actuator
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** AC or heater not working properly, uneven temperature distribution
* **Solution:** Replace blend door actuator, blower motor, or temperature sensor
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 1990 Cadillac Fleetwood had a range of issues, including engine gasket failures, transmission shifting problems, and air suspension leaks. While some issues were relatively inexpensive to fix, others could be costly. Overall, the Fleetwood's aging design and lack of technological advancements contributed to its reliability issues.
