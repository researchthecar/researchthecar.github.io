**Introduction:**

The 2018 Cadillac CTS is a luxury midsize sedan available in various configurations. The standard engine is a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four producing 268 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Alternatively, it can be equipped with a 3.6L V6 engine producing 335 horsepower and 285 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic or a 10-speed automatic, depending on engine choice. The CTS is praised for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Intake Manifold Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leak, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitations:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delay or jerkiness when shifting gears
    * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid change
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing or unresponsive, navigation errors
    * Solution: Software update, system reset
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 21V106000

* **Headlight Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both headlights malfunctioning or flickering
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, uneven tire wear, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace front suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the tail light assembly
    * Solution: Replace tail light assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Mechanism Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace sunroof motor or tracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2018 Cadillac CTS has received positive reviews but has had some reported issues with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to repair can range from minor to significant. It's important to note that these issues are not普遍, and many CTS owners have reported having a trouble-free experience with their vehicles. However, it's always wise to research potential problems before making a purchasing decision.
