## 2011 Cadillac STS

The 2011 Cadillac STS is a midsize luxury sedan that offers a comfortable ride and a well-equipped interior. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 3.6-liter V6 producing 304 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, or a 4.6-liter V8 producing 320 horsepower and 315 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The STS is also available with a choice of rear- or all-wheel drive.

Owners of the 2011 Cadillac STS generally praise its comfortable ride, well-equipped interior, and powerful engines. However, some owners have reported experiencing issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

### Engine

**3.6L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold gasket may fail, causing a loss of coolant and a decrease in engine performance.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Timing chain failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail, causing the engine to stall or run erratically.
Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**4.6L V8**

* **Valve cover gasket leak**
Description/Symptoms: The valve cover gaskets may leak, causing a loss of oil and a decrease in engine performance.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter, or rebuild the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Torque converter failure**
Description/Symptoms: The torque converter may fail, causing a loss of power and a decrease in fuel economy.
Solution: Replace the torque converter.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Front control arm bushings worn**
Description/Symptoms: The front control arm bushings may wear out, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps.
Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Rear shock absorbers leaking**
Description/Symptoms: The rear shock absorbers may leak, causing a decrease in ride quality.
Solution: Replace the shock absorbers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Battery drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to not start.
Solution: Identify and repair the source of the battery drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator failure**
Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, causing the vehicle to lose power while driving.
Solution: Replace the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Headlight condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Condensation may form inside the headlights, causing a decrease in visibility.
Solution: Replace the headlights or reseal them.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Chrome trim peeling**
Description/Symptoms: The chrome trim on the exterior of the vehicle may peel or discolor.
Solution: Replace the affected trim pieces.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Sunroof leaks**
Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak, causing water to enter the cabin of the vehicle.
Solution: Replace the sunroof seals or adjust the sunroof.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard rattles**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle or squeak when driving over rough roads.
Solution: Identify and eliminate the source of the rattle.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

## Summary

Overall, the 2011 Cadillac STS is a reliable vehicle with a comfortable ride and a well-equipped interior. However, some owners have reported experiencing issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem. It is important to note that not all 2011 Cadillac STS models will experience these issues, and some may experience different issues altogether.
