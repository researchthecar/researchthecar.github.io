**Introduction:**

The 2009 Cadillac DTS is a full-size luxury sedan powered by a standard 3.6L V6 engine paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. It offers a smooth ride, spacious interior, and a refined driving experience. However, like many vehicles, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

- **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust
  - Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, check engine light, loss of power
  - Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

- **Shifting Problems:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears
  - Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Knocking, rattling noise when driving over bumps, uneven tire wear
  - Solution: Replace strut assembly
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Rear Suspension Coil Spring Breakage:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear, reduced handling
  - Solution: Replace coil springs
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Terminal Corrosion:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, electrical system malfunctions
  - Solution: Clean or replace battery terminals
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Power Window Switch Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Inoperable power windows
  - Solution: Replace power window switch
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Headlight Assembly Condensation:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Water or condensation inside headlight assembly
  - Solution: Inspect and seal the housing
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Climate Control System Malfunction:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or insufficient heating or cooling
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace components as needed
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Navigation System Freeze:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Navigation system fails to load or becomes unresponsive
  - Solution: Update software or replace navigation system
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2009 Cadillac DTS is generally reliable, but it has some common issues that can be costly to repair, especially engine and transmission problems. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor annoyances while others can lead to major breakdowns. It's important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and consider maintenance costs when evaluating this vehicle.
