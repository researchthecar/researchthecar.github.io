# 2007 Cadillac DTS

### Overview
The 2007 Cadillac DTS, a full-size luxury sedan, aimed to provide a comfortable and refined driving experience. While generally considered a solid vehicle for its era, like any car, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical problems that go beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. Understanding these potential weak points can help in proactive maintenance and informed repair decisions.

---


## Engine

### 4.6L Northstar V8
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of intake manifold gaskets, coolant elbows, or head gaskets depending on the source of the leak.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, or crankshaft seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900


## Transmission

### 4-Speed Automatic (4T80-E)
**Shifting Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, solenoid replacement, or complete transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Air Suspension Issues (if equipped)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, uneven ride height, compressor failure, air leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of air springs, compressor, or air lines.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement, grinding noises from the door.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator and motor assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsiveness, audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**No widespread issues reported.**

## Interior

### Interior Components
**Heated/Cooled Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Seats not heating or cooling, intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat heating/cooling elements or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Cadillac DTS offers a comfortable ride and a powerful engine, but potential buyers should be aware of common issues. The Northstar V8, while potent, can be prone to coolant and oil leaks, requiring attention. Transmission problems, particularly with shifting, are also a concern that may necessitate costly repairs. For models equipped with air suspension, failures in this system are not uncommon. Electrical gremlins, such as window regulator failures and infotainment glitches, can also arise. While the exterior is generally robust, interior components like heated/cooled seats can develop faults. Overall, the DTS can be a reliable luxury car if well-maintained, but vigilance regarding these specific potential issues is advised.
