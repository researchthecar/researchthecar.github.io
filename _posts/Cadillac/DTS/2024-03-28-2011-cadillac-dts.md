**Introduction:**

The 2011 Cadillac DTS is a full-size luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and well-equipped interior along with a powerful engine. It is powered by a standard 3.6L V6 engine producing 291 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque. The only available transmission is a 6-speed automatic.

While the DTS receives favorable reviews for its smooth ride and spacious cabin, it is criticized for its lack of updated technology features and uninspiring driving dynamics.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

* **Engine Stalling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine stalls unexpectedly, often while idling or at low speeds.
    * Solution: Replace the throttle body assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Oil Consumption Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings and/or valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or slips when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter. In severe cases, the transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3,000 (rebuilding/replacement)
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts Failing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The strut mounts deteriorate and fail, causing knocking noises and a rough ride.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Sway Bar Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sway bar links break, resulting in increased body roll and knocking sounds.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, causing the car to not start.
    * Solution: Replace the battery and investigate any parasitic electrical draws.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails to regulate voltage, causing electrical system problems.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Washer Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield washer fluid leaks from the reservoir or hoses.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handles become loose or break, making it difficult to open the doors.
    * Solution: Replace the door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power window switches fail or the window motors become inoperative.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty switches or window motors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system malfunctions, leading to inconsistent temperature or airflow.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace the malfunctioning components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2011 Cadillac DTS suffers from various issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical components. The engine can experience stalling and oil consumption problems, while the transmission may exhibit shifting issues. The suspension can develop worn strut mounts and sway bar links, leading to a rough ride and knocking noises. Electrical problems such as battery drain and alternator failure are also not uncommon. While the interior is generally reliable, some users report power window failures and climate control issues. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to major repairs with potentially high costs.
