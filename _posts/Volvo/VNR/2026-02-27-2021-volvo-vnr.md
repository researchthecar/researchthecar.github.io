# 2021 Volvo VNR Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2021 Volvo VNR means looking at a modern regional-haul truck designed for efficiency and maneuverability. Because used heavy-duty commercial vehicles often feature varied aftermarket modifications and high-idle service histories, buyers should carefully inspect each unit's maintenance records, cab equipment, and overall mechanical condition before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 11-liter Diesel

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-six diesel engine typically offering ratings between 325 and 425 horsepower, optimized for regional and vocational applications. It provides a lighter-weight alternative to larger displacement engines while maintaining solid commercial-grade durability.

### Volvo D13 13-liter Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-six diesel engine generating horsepower ratings ranging from 400 to 500 hp and robust torque for heavy regional hauling. It is a widely utilized powerplant known for strong performance and integrated powertrain compatibility.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is an advanced 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize fuel efficiency and simplify driver workload in regional traffic. It is generally regarded as a reliable and smooth-shifting system when properly maintained.

## Safety

### Sun Visor

Cab exterior accessories and visibility components should be checked for proper OEM fitment, especially if previous owners installed replacement parts.

**Aftermarket Sun Visor Modification**
- **Symptoms:** An off-brand replacement sun visor was installed that is substantially smaller and mounted incorrectly, reducing morning and evening sun protection.
- **Solution:** Replace the incorrect aftermarket sun visor with an OEM-spec sun visor installed in the correct orientation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V241000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The loose fasteners may result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will tighten the steering gear fasteners to specification, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V782000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** If the park brakes apply unexpectedly, the driver may lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air brake line for proper engagement of the fitting in the pass-through plate, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V417000: Wheels:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A separated hubcap can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hubcap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V536000: Service Brakes, Air](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 121, "Air Brake Systems." A dislodged brake pedal would be inoperable, increasing a risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the pedal plates if required, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V535000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The king pin nut may not have been installed or properly tightened during axle assembly. If the king pin is missing or not tightened properly, the knuckle may separate from the axle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicle to make sure that the king pin nut is installed and that it properly tightened, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V384000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." If the rear axles are overloaded, over time the wheel hubs may fail prematurely, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V255000: Equipment Adaptive/Mobility:Vehicle Controls:Braking](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Overly aggressive braking can result in the rear axle skidding, the brakes locking up, and/or a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure wiring and remove radar antennas and brackets from the vehicle. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V280000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Thermal Management:Fluid/Coolant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The coolant line in the high voltage battery pack may not be fully seated and locked, which can result in the loss of coolant and an electrical short circuit. A battery short circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Volvo VNR is a capable regional tractor equipped with proven proprietary powerplants and automated transmissions. Used truck buyers should verify that all cab components and aftermarket modifications meet standard operating expectations, and conduct a comprehensive mechanical inspection.
