# 2019 Volvo VNR Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Volvo VNR is a regional-haul commercial tractor designed for maneuverability, driver comfort, and efficiency in tight urban environments. Used-car buyers evaluating this heavy-duty truck should carefully inspect its powertrain configurations and auxiliary systems, as commercial vehicles often endure heavy duty cycles. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection are vital steps when shopping for a pre-owned VNR.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 10.8L Turbo-Diesel

The Volvo D11 is an 10.8-liter inline-six diesel engine typically rated between 325 and 425 horsepower, optimized for regional distribution and vocational applications. It offers a balance of fuel efficiency and strong low-end torque, making it a reliable workhorse when properly maintained.

### Volvo D13 12.8L Turbo-Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 12.8-liter inline-six diesel engine producing from 405 to 500 horsepower, designed for heavy regional hauling and maximum payload capacity. It is a widely utilized commercial powerplant known for durability, though routine emissions and fuel system upkeep are essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission engineered to maximize fuel economy and reduce driver fatigue in heavy commercial service. It is generally regarded as one of the smoother automated transmissions in the class, provided its clutch actuators and control modules receive regular service.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V510000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The deformation of the air tank strap t-bolts may cause the t-bolt to disconnect from the strap allowing the tank to drop to the ground and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the air tank strap, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V509000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuse blows the transmission may not shift, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuse, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V542000: Trailer Hitches:Fifth Wheel Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the trailer separates from the tractor, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fifth wheel slider bracket, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V384000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." If the rear axles are overloaded, over time the wheel hubs may fail prematurely, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Volvo VNR offers a capable lineup of heavy-duty diesel engines and automated manual transmissions suited for demanding commercial operations. Because reliability in commercial trucks heavily depends on prior operating conditions and maintenance schedules, buyers should prioritize comprehensive fleet service histories and professional inspections before purchasing.
