# 2022 Volvo VNR Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo VNR is a regional-haul commercial tractor designed for maneuverability and efficiency. When shopping for a used model, evaluating the heavy-duty diesel engines and automated manual transmissions is essential for long-term fleet or owner-operator reliability.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 11L Diesel

The 11-liter Volvo D11 inline-six diesel engine produces between 325 and 425 horsepower, offering a lighter-weight option suited for regional distribution and regional haul applications.

### Volvo D13 13L Diesel

The larger 13-liter Volvo D13 inline-six diesel engine delivers ratings from 405 to 500 horsepower, providing robust pulling power for heavier regional loads while maintaining strong fuel economy.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission engineered to optimize shifting points for fuel efficiency and driver comfort in regional and vocational duty cycles.

### Volvo I-Shift with Crawler Gears 13/14-Speed

This specialized variant of the I-Shift adds one or two ultra-low crawler gears to provide exceptional startability and slow-speed control for heavy loads or demanding terrain.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V417000: Wheels:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A separated hubcap can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hubcap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V255000: Equipment Adaptive/Mobility:Vehicle Controls:Braking](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Overly aggressive braking can result in the rear axle skidding, the brakes locking up, and/or a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure wiring and remove radar antennas and brackets from the vehicle. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V280000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Thermal Management:Fluid/Coolant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The coolant line in the high voltage battery pack may not be fully seated and locked, which can result in the loss of coolant and an electrical short circuit. A battery short circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volvo VNR is a capable regional commercial truck equipped with modern powertrain options. Used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the specific engine and I-Shift transmission configuration, along with standard service histories, to ensure it meets their operational requirements.
