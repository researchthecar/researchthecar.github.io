# 2024 Volvo VNR Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Volvo VNR is a modern regional-haul commercial tractor designed for efficiency, driver comfort, and versatility. As a relatively new model year on the used market, buyer focus typically centers on confirming proper integration of its heavy-duty powertrain and electronic systems. While commercial vehicles are subject to rigorous operational demands, examining available powertrain specifications helps prospective buyers select the ideal configuration for their fleet or owner-operator needs.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine typically rated between 325 and 425 horsepower, optimized for regional distribution and lighter weight applications. It provides a balanced combination of fuel efficiency and responsive power for urban and highway transit.

### Volvo D13

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine delivering horsepower ratings from 405 to 500 hp, tailored for heavier regional-haul duties. It is a widely utilized commercial powerplant known for strong torque output and dependable long-term performance.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize shifting points for maximum fuel economy and reduced driver fatigue. It is the primary transmission offering across VNR configurations, valued for smooth operation and robust commercial durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V173000: Wheels:Hubcap/Cover](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached hub cover can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hub covers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V280000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Thermal Management:Fluid/Coolant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The coolant line in the high voltage battery pack may not be fully seated and locked, which can result in the loss of coolant and an electrical short circuit. A battery short circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V689000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software programming error may cause the electronic stability control (ESC) to be inoperative. Driving with an inoperative electronic stability control system can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V690000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the castle nuts and install new cotter pins, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V121000: Steering:Gear Box:Shaft Sector](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNR&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fractured steering gear shaft can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the serial numbers, and replace the steering gears, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
