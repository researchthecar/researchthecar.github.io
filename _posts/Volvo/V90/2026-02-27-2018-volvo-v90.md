# 2018 Volvo V90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Volvo V90 is an elegant and spacious luxury wagon offering sophisticated styling and refined driving dynamics. Used-car shoppers looking at this model will find a vehicle equipped with advanced safety technology and premium interior appointments, though its complex powertrain and electronics require careful evaluation during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### T5 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder T5 engine produces approximately 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing front-wheel drive models with responsive daily power and respectable fuel efficiency. It is generally a smooth powerplant, though regular maintenance is essential for long-term health.

### T6 2.0L Supercharged & Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter dual-charged T6 engine combines a supercharger and turbocharger to deliver around 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque paired with all-wheel drive. While it offers brisk acceleration, its complex forced-induction system means buyers should verify complete service history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and prompt gear changes across all driving modes. It is well-matched to both engine configurations, though proper fluid maintenance is critical for sustained reliability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V800000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V90&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the GPS location is not provided to emergency personnel it can inhibit emergency response in the event of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will correct the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Volvo V90 stands out as a stylish, comfortable, and well-appointed luxury wagon with strong engine options. While formal complaint data is minimal for this model year, prospective buyers should still perform a thorough inspection of the vehicle's mechanical and electrical systems before purchasing.
