**Introduction:**

The 2021 Volvo V90 is a sleek and sophisticated wagon that offers a luxurious driving experience. It is available with several engine and transmission options, including a plug-in hybrid powertrain. Drivers appreciate the V90's comfortable interior, advanced safety features, and stylish design. However, the vehicle is not without its issues.

**Engine**

**T4 (2.0L Inline-4)**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Oil leaks and reduced engine power. Replacement of the turbocharger is necessary. $1,500 - $2,500+
* **ECU Malfunction:** Check engine light, engine stalling. ECU reprogramming or replacement. $500 - $1,000+

**T5 (2.0L Inline-4)**

* **Head Gasket Failure:** Coolant leaks and engine overheating. Head gasket replacement. $1,200 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** Hard or delayed shifting, hesitation. Software updates or transmission control module replacement. $500 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:** Leaking shocks, uneven tire wear. Strut replacement. $600 - $1,000+ per strut
* **Control Arm Bushing Failure:** Wobbling or excessive vibrations. Control arm bushing replacement. $200 - $400+ per arm

**Electrical**

* **Starter Motor Failure:** Engine not starting. Starter motor replacement. $500 - $1,000+
* **Battery Discharge:** Battery drains prematurely. Battery replacement or repair of electrical components. $200 - $600+
* **ECU Glitches:** Random electrical malfunctions, engine performance issues. ECU reprogramming or replacement. $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Clips:** Clips break or snap, causing the bumper to sag. Bumper clip replacement. $100 - $200+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Intermittent cooling or heating, strange noises. HVAC system diagnosis and repair. $200 - $600+
* **Infotainment System Lag:** Slow responsiveness, freezing. Software updates or infotainment system replacement. $500 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The 2021 Volvo V90 has several known issues, ranging from engine and transmission problems to electrical and suspension glitches. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix while others can be more costly and debilitating. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
