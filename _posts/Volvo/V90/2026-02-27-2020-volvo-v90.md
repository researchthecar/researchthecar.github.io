# 2020 Volvo V90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Volvo V90 delivers a sophisticated blend of Scandinavian luxury, comfort, and distinctive wagon styling. As buyers look at this model on the used market, understanding its powertrain options and historical owner feedback helps ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### T5 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The T5 features a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with front-wheel drive. It offers smooth power delivery and respectable efficiency for daily driving.

### T6 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged I4

The T6 utilizes a 2.0-liter engine that is both turbocharged and supercharged, generating roughly 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque with all-wheel drive. This setup provides robust acceleration across the power band, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly across various driving conditions and helps optimize fuel economy. Routine fluid checks and proper care are recommended to maintain long-term shifting refinement.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V144000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Automatic Emergency Braking](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V90&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software/hardware incompatibility, the Automatic Emergency Brake (AEB) system may not detect obstacles and engage as intended. If the automatic emergency braking system does not engage as intended, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will update the Active Safety Domain Master (ASDM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V414000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V90&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the low-pressure fuel pump can cause a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Volvo V90 is an appealing option for used car buyers seeking premium wagon comfort and advanced safety features. Thoroughly inspecting the service records and conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help secure a reliable ownership experience.
