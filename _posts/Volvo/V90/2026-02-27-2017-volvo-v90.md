# 2017 Volvo V90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Volvo V90 combines striking Scandinavian design, luxurious interior accommodations, and advanced safety features into an elegant wagon package. As a used vehicle purchase, buyers should weigh its sophisticated engineering against the realities of maintaining a complex, turbocharged and supercharged luxury European car. While official owner complaint volume is relatively modest, prospective owners should carefully inspect powertrain health and electrical accessories during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter dual-charged engine produces roughly 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, utilizing both a supercharger for low-end response and a turbocharger for high-end power. It provides brisk acceleration for the V90, though its complex forced-induction plumbing requires diligent maintenance and premium fuel.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed Geartronic automatic transmission routes power smoothly across all available driving modes. It generally delivers refined shifts, though regular fluid checks are recommended to ensure long-term durability in high-mileage examples.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V800000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V90&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the GPS location is not provided to emergency personnel it can inhibit emergency response in the event of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will correct the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Volvo V90 remains a stylish and comfortable luxury wagon choice for used-car shoppers who prioritize safety, cargo versatility, and distinct styling. Because comprehensive complaint data is sparse for this specific model year, buyers are strongly encouraged to obtain a detailed service history and perform a thorough mechanical inspection before finalizing a purchase.
