**Introduction to the 2019 Volvo Access**

The 2019 Volvo Access is a compact luxury crossover SUV available with a single engine and transmission option: a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, delivering a spirited performance and capable handling. While praised for its stylish design, luxurious interior, and advanced safety features, some owners have reported a few issues with the vehicle.
