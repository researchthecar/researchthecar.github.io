**Introduction**
The 2023 Volvo XC60 is a luxury compact crossover SUV that offers a compelling blend of style, performance, and safety. It is available with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 247 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers appreciate the XC60's spacious cabin, comfortable seats, and advanced safety features. However, there have been some issues reported with the vehicle, detailed below.

**Engine**
2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder

**Camshaft Actuator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Sudden loss of engine power, rough idling, check engine light.
Solution: Replacement of faulty camshaft actuator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
Eight-Speed Automatic

**Hesitation and Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: Intermittent hesitation or jerking during gear shifts.
Solution: Software updates or transmission repair.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery draining rapidly, failure to start.
Solution: Inspection and replacement of faulty components, such as the alternator or battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
**Sunroof Panel Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof failing to open or close properly, wind noise.
Solution: Repair or replacement of sunroof panel.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
**Instrument Cluster Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning instrument cluster, gauges displaying incorrect information.
Solution: Replacement of instrument cluster.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2023 Volvo XC60 is a well-regarded vehicle, but a few issues have been reported. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and others potentially more costly. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and consult with their dealer if they experience any of the symptoms described above.
