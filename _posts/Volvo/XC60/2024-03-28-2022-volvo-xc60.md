**Introduction**

The 2022 Volvo XC60 is a luxury compact SUV that offers a combination of style, performance, and safety. It is available with various engine and transmission options, including a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four, a turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter inline-four, and a plug-in hybrid powertrain. The XC60 is praised for its comfortable interior, high-tech features, and excellent safety ratings. However, it has also faced some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**T5** (Turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four, 250 horsepower, 258 lb-ft torque)

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**T6** (Turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter inline-four, 316 horsepower, 295 lb-ft torque)

* **Engine Knocking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Metallic rattling or knocking sound from the engine
    * Solution: Repair or replace the engine internals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, hesitation or slipping while accelerating
    * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling sounds from the rear suspension
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace the suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Flickering lights, loss of power accessories, battery problems
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from the roof rack during driving
    * Solution: Adjust or tighten the roof rack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (DIY)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or drain tube
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Volvo XC60 has faced some issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. However, instances of engine knocking and transmission slipping can be more costly and time-consuming to repair. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and address them promptly to avoid further problems.
