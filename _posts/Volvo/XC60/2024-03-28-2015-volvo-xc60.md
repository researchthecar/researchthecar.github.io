**Introduction:**

The 2015 Volvo XC60 is a mid-size luxury SUV that offers a combination of style, comfort, and performance. It is available with various engine and transmission options, including:

* 2.5L inline-5 turbocharged (T5): 250 horsepower, 266 lb-ft torque
* 2.0L inline-4 turbocharged and supercharged (T6): 302 horsepower, 295 lb-ft torque
* 2.0L inline-4 diesel turbocharged (D4): 181 horsepower, 295 lb-ft torque
* 8-speed automatic transmission (all models)

While the XC60 has received positive reviews for its interior quality, safety ratings, and handling, there have been some reported issues associated with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.5L inline-5 turbocharged (T5)**

* **Crankshaft seal failure:** Leaks oil from the rear of the engine. Repair involves replacing the crankshaft seal and any damaged components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Causes a rattling noise from the engine and can lead to catastrophic engine failure. Repair involves replacing the timing chain tensioner and any damaged components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,500+
* **Coil pack failure:** Causes misfires and engine performance issues. Repair involves replacing the faulty coil pack(s). **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**2.0L inline-4 turbocharged and supercharged (T6)**

* **Intake manifold failure:** Results in vacuum leaks and reduced engine performance. Repair involves replacing the intake manifold. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Turbocharger failure:** Causes loss of power and a whistling noise. Repair involves replacing the turbocharger and any damaged components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Engine knock:** Caused by excessive wear and tear on the engine components. Repair involves rebuilding or replacing the engine. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $10,000+

**2.0L inline-4 diesel turbocharged (D4)**

* **Diesel particulate filter (DPF) clogging:** Restricts exhaust flow and causes engine performance issues. Repair involves cleaning or replacing the DPF. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **EGR valve failure:** Causes reduced engine performance and increased emissions. Repair involves replacing the EGR valve. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Turbocharger wastegate failure:** Results in reduced boost pressure and engine performance issues. Repair involves replacing the turbocharger wastegate. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic transmission:**

* **Transmission fluid leak:** Causes shuddering, delayed shifts, or gear slippage. Repair involves fixing the leak and replacing the transmission fluid. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Mechatronic unit failure:** Results in transmission failure and inability to shift gears. Repair involves replacing the mechatronic unit. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Software issues:** Causes erratic shifting, delayed responses, or transmission lock-up. Repair involves updating the transmission's software. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Front lower control arm bushings:** Wear and tear can cause knocking or clunking noises from the suspension. Repair involves replacing the bushings. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Rear stabilizer bar links:** Can become loose or broken, causing rattling or clunking noises. Repair involves replacing the stabilizer bar links. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The vehicle's battery can drain quickly when parked. Repair involves diagnosing and fixing the electrical drain. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
* **Infotainment system failure:** The infotainment system can freeze, lose functionality, or stop working altogether. Repair involves resetting or updating the system or replacing components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
* **Adaptive cruise control issues:** The adaptive cruise control system may not function properly, including erratic braking or acceleration. Repair involves recalibrating or replacing components of the system. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Windshield cracking:** The windshield can crack easily from minor impacts. Repair involves replacing the windshield. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Door handle failure:** The exterior door handles can become loose or break. Repair involves replacing the door handle(s). **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Climate control issues:** The climate control system may not blow cold air or regulate temperature properly. Repair involves diagnosing and fixing the underlying cause. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Navigation system errors:** The navigation system may display incorrect information or lose functionality. Repair involves updating the software or replacing components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Seat belt warning sensor malfunction:** The seat belt warning sensor may falsely activate or fail to activate when the seat belt is in use. Repair involves diagnosing and fixing the electrical issue. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**Other**

* **Brake issues:** Premature brake wear or squealing noises. Repair involves inspecting and replacing brake pads or rotors as needed. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600+
* **Sunroof leaks:** The sunroof can leak water into the cabin. Repair involves inspecting and resealing the sunroof. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Fuel injector failure:** Can cause engine misfires, loss of power, and increased emissions. Repair involves cleaning or replacing the fuel injector(s). **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+

**Summary**

The 2015 Volvo XC60 has received high praise for its interior quality, safety ratings, and performance. However, there have been some reported issues that range in severity and cost to fix. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to repair, others can be major and costly, such as engine and transmission failures. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in the potential costs associated with repairs.
