# 2021 Volvo XC60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Volvo XC60 brings Scandinavian luxury, distinctive styling, and advanced safety features to the compact luxury SUV segment. While it offers a comfortable ride and refined powertrain options, used-car buyers should thoroughly review vehicle history and conduct a pre-purchase inspection to ensure all systems operate as intended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (T5)

A turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine producing around 250 horsepower, offering balanced daily performance and efficiency. This option provides steady reliability for routine commuting.

### 2.0L Supercharged and Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (T6)

A dual-charged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine generating approximately 316 horsepower for brisk acceleration. Regular maintenance is key to keeping the complex induction system running smoothly.

### 2.0L Supercharged and Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid (T8 Recharge)

A high-output plug-in hybrid powertrain combining forced induction with an electric motor to deliver around 400 total horsepower. It offers swift performance alongside electric-only driving capabilities.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An eight-speed automatic transmission paired across the XC60 lineup, delivering smooth and responsive gear shifts. Routine fluid checks help maintain long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V682000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V716000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Separation of the rotor and shaft may cause a reduction in braking assistance and a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Electronic Stability Control (ESC), increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the hydraulic unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V001000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The Supplementary Restraint System (SRS) control unit may not have been properly attached to the car body and may detach during a crash. A detached SRS control unit can decrease the performance of occupant protection systems such as air bags, seat belts, and high voltage battery disconnect, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Car will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, as necessary, tighten the SRS control unit mounting screws, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V001000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A screw inside the gearbox housing can cause the steering wheel to lock up and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
