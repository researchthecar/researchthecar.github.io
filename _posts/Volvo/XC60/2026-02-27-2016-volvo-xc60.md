## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or blank screens on the central display.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, especially when the vehicle is parked for extended periods.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, checking for faulty modules or aftermarket accessories, battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Volvo XC60 generally presents a strong reliability record, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. The most frequently cited technical concerns tend to be related to the vehicle's electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit experiencing occasional glitches and a potential for battery drain issues. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and require attention. Overall, the XC60 remains a solid choice for those prioritizing safety and comfort, with most issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
