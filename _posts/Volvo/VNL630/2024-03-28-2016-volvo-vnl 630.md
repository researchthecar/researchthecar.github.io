**Introduction:**

The 2016 Volvo VNL 630 is a heavy-duty truck known for its safety features, comfortable cab, and fuel efficiency. It is powered by a Volvo D13 engine rated at 455 horsepower and 1,850 lb-ft of torque and paired with an I-Shift 12-speed automated manual transmission.

**Engine:**

**Volvo D13**

* **EGR Cooler Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking or clogged EGR cooler, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace EGR cooler
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased boost pressure, oil leaks
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, increased emissions
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per injector
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission:**

**I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficult or delayed shifting, gear grinding
    * Solution: Update software, inspect and repair or replace transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V314000
* **Clutch Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering clutch, increased clutch noise
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension:**

* **Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or cracked leaf springs, decreased ride comfort
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting the engine
    * Solution: Inspect and replace failing electrical components, software updates
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of drain
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior:**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlights
    * Solution: Replace or reseal headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per headlight
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V993000

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Lights Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Flickering or dim dashboard lights
    * Solution: Replace faulty dashboard lights or control modules
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Cruise Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative cruise control system
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace electronic components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 20V044000

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Volvo VNL 630 is a reliable and capable heavy-duty truck. However, it has some common issues that can be expensive to fix, particularly those related to the engine and transmission. Regular maintenance and proactive repairs can help minimize these problems. The cost of the issues can vary depending on the severity and the location of the repairs.
