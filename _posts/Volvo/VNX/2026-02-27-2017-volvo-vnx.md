# 2017 Volvo VNX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Volvo VNX is a heavy-duty tractor designed for extreme vocational and heavy-haul applications, typically paired with high-output diesel powerplants and heavy-duty automated or manual transmissions. Because heavy-duty commercial trucks like the VNX operate in demanding environments, evaluating the condition of the specific powertrain configuration and ancillary systems is vital for prospective buyers. While commercial vehicle complaints often center on complex electrical integration and heavy-duty drivetrain components, a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains the best tool to verify remaining service life.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D16 16.1L Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D16 is a 16.1-liter inline-six turbocharged diesel engine, producing upwards of 500 to 600 horsepower and massive torque designed for heavy-haul applications. It offers immense pulling power for heavy vocational work, though buyers should closely inspect maintenance and emissions equipment histories.

### Cummins ISX15 15.0L Turbo Diesel

The Cummins ISX15 is a 15.0-liter heavy-duty diesel engine known for widespread parts availability and strong torque ratings suited for severe service. Regular service records and EGR system health are key factors when evaluating a used model with this powerplant.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel efficiency and heavy-load shifting performance. Buyers should verify smooth clutch engagement and check for proper actuator operation during a test drive.

### Eaton Fuller 18-Speed Manual

The Eaton Fuller 18-speed manual transmission provides maximum driver control and durability in extreme heavy-haul operations. Inspecting the clutch life and synchronizers is recommended for used vehicles.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V928000: Service Brakes, Air](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the brake system becomes contaminated, braking performance could be affected, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the vehicle control module and replace the air dryer filter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V097000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Also, the bolt connecting the upper steering shaft to the lower steering shaft may not have been properly tightened. Separation of the steering shaft will result in complete loss of steering which may lead to a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Volvo VNX is a robust heavy-duty platform built for demanding commercial hauling tasks. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive mechanical and electronic inspection focusing on the specific engine and transmission configuration, along with the chassis and braking systems, to ensure dependable operation.
