# 2004 Volvo S40

### Overview
The 2004 Volvo S40, a compact executive car, offered a blend of Scandinavian design, safety, and practicality. While generally considered a reliable vehicle for its era, like any car, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners and mechanics. This analysis focuses on common technical issues reported, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear. The S40 was available with various engine and transmission configurations, each with its own set of potential concerns. Understanding these can help prospective buyers and current owners address potential problems proactively.

---


## Engine

### 1.9L Turbocharged I4 (B4204T2)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, misfires, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per coil


### 2.4L Naturally Aspirated I5 (B5244S2)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine block, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gasket.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 2.4L Turbocharged I5 (B5244T3)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**PCV System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks, rough idle, engine sludge.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of PCV system components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual (M56)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


### 5-Speed Automatic (AW55-50SN)
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or transmission rebuild/replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 (fluid/filter), $2,000 - $4,000 (rebuild/replacement)

**Solenoid Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, transmission fault codes, erratic shifting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty transmission solenoids.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Worn Struts/Shocks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, excessive body roll, reduced handling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front or rear struts/shocks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement, grinding noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400 per window

**CEM Module Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, warning lights, non-functional accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the Central Electronic Module (CEM).
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, damp carpets, potential mold.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes.
- **Cost:** $100 - $200


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Volvo S40 is a reasonably reliable car, particularly praised for its safety features. Common issues tend to revolve around the turbocharged engines, automatic transmission performance, and some electrical components like window regulators and the CEM module. Suspension wear, especially bushings and struts, is also noted. While not immune to problems, most issues are repairable and not prohibitively expensive if addressed promptly. Regular maintenance, especially for the transmission and engine, is crucial for longevity. Overall, it represents a solid choice for those seeking a safe and stylish compact car, provided potential buyers are aware of these common areas of concern.
