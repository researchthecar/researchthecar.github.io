## 2020 Volvo B8R

The 2020 Volvo B8R is a heavy-duty bus powered by a 7.7-liter D8K engine that produces 320 horsepower and 1,200 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 6-speed automatic transmission, but a 9-speed automatic transmission is available as an option. The B8R is known for its fuel efficiency, reliability, and comfortable ride. However, it has also been plagued by several issues, which are detailed below.

### Engine

**D8K 7.7L**

**Oil Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks can occur from various parts of the engine, including the valve cover, oil pan, and oil filter housing. This can lead to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
Solution: Repair or replace the leaking part.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**6-Speed Automatic**
**Delayed Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears. This can cause sluggish acceleration and poor fuel economy.
Solution: Update the transmission control module software or replace the transmission solenoid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic**
**Harsh Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift gears harshly, causing a noticeable jerk or bump. This can occur during acceleration or deceleration.
Solution: Update the transmission control module software or replace the transmission fluid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

**No Issues Found**

### Electrical

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery may drain overnight, even when parked and turned off. This can make it difficult to start the vehicle in the morning.
Solution: Replace the battery or verify the alternator is charging properly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

**No Issues Found**

### Interior

**Dashboard Rattling**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle or make noise while driving. This can be caused by loose screws or panels.
Solution: Tighten the loose screws or replace the rattling panel.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2020 Volvo B8R has received mixed reviews from owners. While some praise its fuel efficiency and reliability, others have encountered issues with oil leaks, transmission problems, battery drain, and dashboard rattling. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some repairs can be expensive. Potential buyers should research these issues and consider their impact on their decision.
