**Introduction**

The 2019 Volvo B8R is a heavy-duty bus manufactured by Volvo Buses. It is powered by a Volvo D8 engine and comes with a ZF EcoLife 6-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 320 horsepower and 1,200 lb-ft of torque. The B8R is known for its reliability, safety, and fuel efficiency. However, it has been subject to a few reported issues.

**Engine**

* **D8**

* **Leaking Fuel Injector Seals**
  - Description/Symptoms: Fuel leaking from the injector seals, causing a strong fuel odor and possible engine misfires.
  - Solution: Replace the injector seals.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **ZF EcoLife 6-speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed shifts, or slipping gears.
  - Solution: Update the transmission control module or replace faulty solenoids.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Battery**
  - Description/Symptoms: Premature battery failure, causing difficulty starting the vehicle or electrical malfunctions.
  - Solution: Replace the battery.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage**
  - Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust buildup on the metal components underneath the vehicle.
  - Solution: Inspect and repair affected areas, apply anti-rust coating.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty HVAC Blower Motor**
  - Description/Symptoms: Blower motor fails to circulate air, resulting in poor ventilation and fogging windows.
  - Solution: Replace the blower motor.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Rattle**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise coming from the dashboard, especially on rough roads.
  - Solution: Inspect and tighten loose components or replace faulty dashboard trim.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Volvo B8R is a reliable and efficient bus. However, it has been subject to some issues with the transmission, fuel system, and interior components. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the repair required.
