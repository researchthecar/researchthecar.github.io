## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, audible hissing from suspension components, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per spring

**Ride Height Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height, suspension warning lights, rougher than usual ride.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of the faulty ride height sensor or its linkage.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350
