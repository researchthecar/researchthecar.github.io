**2021 Volvo B8R**

The 2021 Volvo B8R is a heavy-duty coach chassis powered by a 7.7-liter inline-6 diesel engine that produces 280 horsepower and 850 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission. The B8R is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and fuel efficiency. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and lack of modern safety features.
