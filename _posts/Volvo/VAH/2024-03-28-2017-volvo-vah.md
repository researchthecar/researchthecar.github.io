**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volvo VAH is a mid-size luxury wagon powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It features an eight-speed automatic transmission. Praised for its elegant design, advanced safety features, and comfortable interior, some owners have encountered various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and check engine light.
  * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V077000

* **Oil Leak:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the engine, noticeable as a puddle under the vehicle.
  * Solution: Inspection and repair of the leaking component.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, slow shifting, and loss of power.
  * Solution: Replacement of the transmission.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Noises over bumps, steering instability, and uneven tire wear.
  * Solution: Replacement of the front struts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Malfunction:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, inability to switch between low and high beams.
  * Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of the headlight assembly.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, sluggish electrical performance, and dead battery.
  * Solution: Inspection and testing of the electrical system to locate and repair the cause of the drain.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Paint Peeling:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Peeling of the paint on the rear bumper, exposing the underlying plastic.
  * Solution: Refinishing or replacement of the rear bumper.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Vibrating noise coming from the sunroof when closed.
  * Solution: Inspection and tightening or replacement of the sunroof assembly.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Volvo VAH has shown some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. While some issues are minor and inexpensive to repair, others can be significant and costly. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and seek timely repairs to ensure the safety and performance of their vehicle.
