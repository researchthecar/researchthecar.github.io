# 2024 Volvo VAH Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Volvo VAH is a specialized urban hauler designed for auto transport and vocational applications, leveraging Volvo's commercial truck engineering expertise. For prospective buyers considering a used commercial chassis, evaluating the core powertrain configurations and any reported operational quirks is essential for maximizing fleet uptime and operational efficiency.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine typically producing between 325 and 425 horsepower, optimized for weight savings and fuel efficiency in regional and vocational distribution. It provides a reliable power foundation for urban transport applications when maintained according to commercial service intervals.

### Volvo D13

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine offering ratings from 400 to 500 horsepower, built for heavier hauling duties and demanding vocational cycles. Known for robust torque delivery, it is a workhorse engine favored for heavy-duty commercial operations.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize fuel economy and smooth out shift points in heavy-duty applications. It is widely regarded for its durability and intelligent gear selection in commercial service.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V441000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear fasteners may not be properly tightened. Loose steering gear fasteners may result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the steering gear fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V790000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Electrical noise and low signal to the power line carrier may cause the ECU to incorrectly process commands or stop working. Safety systems that depend on the ECU (Automatic Traction Control, ABS, Electronic Stability Control, Active Cruise Control, and Collison Mitigation System) may have diminished or lost functionality, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V753000: Seat Belts:Front:Anchorage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A seat belt anchor that fails will not properly restrain an occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new weld nut and washer, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
