# 2022 Volvo VAH Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo VAH is a specialized urban haul tractor designed for maneuverability and efficiency in demanding delivery environments. As a commercial medium-duty platform, prospective buyers should evaluate both the heavy-duty powertrain options and standard wear items. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure long-term commercial reliability.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 10.8L Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D11 is an 10.8-liter inline-six turbocharged diesel engine typically producing between 325 and 425 horsepower. It is engineered for fuel efficiency and strong low-end torque in regional distribution applications.

### Volvo D13 12.8L Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 12.8-liter inline-six turbocharged diesel engine offering horsepower ratings ranging from 400 to 500 hp. It provides robust pulling power for heavier regional transport duties.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize fuel economy and seamless shifting. Regular fluid maintenance is essential for long-term commercial durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V417000: Wheels:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A separated hubcap can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hubcap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V790000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Electrical noise and low signal to the power line carrier may cause the ECU to incorrectly process commands or stop working. Safety systems that depend on the ECU (Automatic Traction Control, ABS, Electronic Stability Control, Active Cruise Control, and Collison Mitigation System) may have diminished or lost functionality, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V753000: Seat Belts:Front:Anchorage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." A seat belt anchor that fails will not properly restrain an occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new weld nut and washer, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2022 Volvo VAH offers capable regional hauling configurations backed by proven proprietary powertrain options. Buyers should prioritize comprehensive service history checks and inspect critical commercial components before finalizing a purchase.
