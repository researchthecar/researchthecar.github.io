## 2022 Volvo VAH

The 2022 Volvo VAH is a midsize luxury SUV that offers a comfortable ride, advanced safety features, and a spacious cabin. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The VAH is available with either a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission.

Some pros of the 2022 Volvo VAH include its comfortable interior, impressive safety features, and powerful engine. However, some cons include its high price tag, lack of off-road capability, and some reported issues with its transmission.

### Engine

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
Solution: Replace the transmission valve body.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
Description/Symptoms: The intake valves can become clogged with carbon deposits, which can lead to reduced engine power and fuel efficiency.
Solution: Clean the intake valves.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**6-Speed Manual**

**Clutch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The clutch may slip or become difficult to engage, making it difficult to shift gears.
Solution: Replace the clutch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found
