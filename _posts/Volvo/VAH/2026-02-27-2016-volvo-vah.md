# 2016 Volvo VAH Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Volvo VAH is a specialized auto hauler commercial vehicle designed for efficient transport of passenger vehicles. When evaluating a used commercial truck of this caliber, buyers should carefully review both the powertrain options and any reported commercial service issues. Because this model operates under heavy demands, inspecting engine performance, transmission shifting behavior, and overall mechanical condition is essential before purchase.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 11L Diesel

The 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine offers strong low-end torque optimized for regional hauling and commercial weight requirements. It is designed for durability in fleet and vocational applications, though routine maintenance of emissions and fuel systems is critical for long-term reliability.

### Volvo D13 13L Diesel

The larger 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine provides increased horsepower and torque for heavier transport duties. Buyers evaluating this option should look for comprehensive service history and monitor fluid levels and emissions components regularly.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is an automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel efficiency and simplify heavy-duty shifting. Prospective buyers should test shift smoothness across all gears and verify clutch actuator health.

### Eaton Fuller Manual Transmission

Conventional manual transmission options provide direct mechanical control favored by some fleet operators. Buyers should check for clutch wear, synchro health, and clean gear engagement during a test drive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V630000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Headlights that provide insufficient illumination increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the headlights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V523000: Steering:Linkages:Drag:Link:Connection](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VAH&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Corrosion will accelerate wear of the ball, possibly resulting in the ball joint separating from the socket, causing loss of steering and an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the drag link with one that has an improved geometry, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Volvo VAH offers robust commercial powertrain choices suited for dedicated vehicle transport operations. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough mechanical inspection, verify maintenance logs, and test all major drivetrain functions to ensure dependable performance on the road.
