The 2018 Volvo VNL 860 is a popular long-haul semi-truck known for its spacious cab and comfortable ride. Engine options include the Volvo D11, D13, and Cummins X15. The Volvo D13 engine produces between 405 and 500 horsepower and up to 1850 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include Volvo I-Shift automated manual transmissions. Drivers often praise the VNL 860 for its fuel efficiency and driver comfort, but some report concerns about electronic issues and the cost of parts and repairs.

**Engine**

**Volvo D13**

*   **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Frequent DPF regeneration cycles, reduced engine power, DPF clogging, and potential engine shutdown.
    *   Solution: DPF cleaning or replacement, addressing underlying issues causing excessive soot buildup (e.g., faulty injectors, EGR system problems).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **EGR Valve Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, decreased fuel economy, black smoke from the exhaust, and engine fault codes.
    *   Solution: EGR valve replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Cummins X15**

*   **Injector Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough running, misfires, poor fuel economy, black smoke, and potential engine damage.
    *   Solution: Injector replacement and proper calibration.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $4,000+ (depending on the number of injectors)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Turbocharger Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, unusual noises from the turbocharger, and potential engine damage.
    *   Solution: Turbocharger repair or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Volvo I-Shift**

*   **Shifting Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed shifting, failure to shift, and error codes.
    *   Solution: Software updates, clutch adjustments, solenoid replacements, or valve body repair/replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Clutch Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, and unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Clutch replacement and potential flywheel resurfacing.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, uneven ride height, and air compressor running constantly.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of leaking air bags, lines, or valves.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Shock Absorber Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, poor handling, and excessive wear on tires.
    *   Solution: Shock absorber replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Sensor Failures**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Various engine and transmission problems, ABS issues, and instrument panel warnings. Specific sensors include the NOx sensor and various temperature sensors.
    *   Solution: Sensor replacement and wiring harness inspection.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Wiring Harness Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, short circuits, and difficulty diagnosing problems.
    *   Solution: Wiring harness repair or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Headlight Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights or fogged headlights
    *   Solution: Headlight assembly replacement
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **HVAC System Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent heating or cooling, blower motor failure, and refrigerant leaks.
    *   Solution: HVAC component repair or replacement (e.g., compressor, condenser, evaporator, blower motor).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Infotainment System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing screen, slow response times, and Bluetooth connectivity issues.
    *   Solution: Software updates or infotainment system replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Volvo VNL 860 can be a reliable truck, but owners should be aware of potential issues, particularly with the DPF system on the Volvo D13 engine, turbo and injector problems on the Cummins X15, as well as potential electrical and transmission problems. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to major repairs, with costs varying accordingly. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs can help prevent more significant problems down the road. The cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars for sensor replacements to several thousand dollars for engine or transmission overhauls.
