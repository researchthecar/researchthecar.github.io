**Introduction:**

The 2020 Volvo VNL 860 is a heavy-duty truck powered by a Cummins X15 engine. It is available with either a 13-speed manual transmission or an I-Shift automated manual transmission. The X15 engine produces 400-605 horsepower and 1,550-2,050 lb-ft of torque. The VNL 860 is known for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and durability. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

**Cummins X15**

* **EGR Cooler Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) cooler, leading to decreased engine performance and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replacement of the EGR cooler.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sticking fuel injectors can cause rough idling, poor fuel economy, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of the affected fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed response from the turbocharger, resulting in poor acceleration and reduced power.
    * Solution: Inspection and/or replacement of the turbocharger or its components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**13-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the clutch, causing difficulty shifting gears or loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacement of the clutch and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**I-Shift Automated Manual**

* **Shift Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction of the shift control module, leading to erratic shifting or inability to shift gears.
    * Solution: Replacement of the shift control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the transmission case or oil pan, causing low oil levels and potential damage to the transmission.
    * Solution: Repair of the leak and replacement of lost oil.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or cracked leaf springs, resulting in reduced suspension support and potential handling issues.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per leaf spring
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge when the vehicle is parked, caused by parasitic electrical drain.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the source of the drain, such as faulty wiring or electronic components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Accumulation of moisture inside the headlight housing, affecting visibility.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected headlight unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature cracking of the plastic dashboard panel, affecting aesthetics and durability.
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Radio Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or intermittent functioning of the audio system.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the radio unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Volvo VNL 860 is a reliable and capable heavy-duty truck. However, like all vehicles, it has some potential issues to watch out for. The severity and cost of these issues vary widely, ranging from minor annoyances to more significant repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
