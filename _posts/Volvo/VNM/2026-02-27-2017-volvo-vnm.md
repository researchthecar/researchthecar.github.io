# 2017 Volvo VNM Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Volvo VNM is a versatile medium-duty commercial tractor built for regional haul and utility applications. Used-car buyers looking at this model will find a vehicle designed for durability and driver comfort, though commercial trucks of this class require careful inspection of their powertrain and auxiliary systems prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine offering strong torque and efficiency for regional distribution. It is generally regarded as a reliable commercial powerplant when subjected to regular maintenance schedules.

### Volvo D13

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine providing higher horsepower and torque ratings suited for heavier regional workloads. Buyers should verify proper emissions and aftertreatment upkeep.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize fuel economy and ease driver fatigue. It offers smooth shifting characteristics, though prospective buyers should test operation across all gears.

### Eaton Fuller 10-Speed Manual

The Eaton Fuller 10-speed manual transmission is a traditional mechanical gearbox known for straightforward durability and serviceability. It provides robust commercial reliability for fleets preferring manual control.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V928000: Service Brakes, Air](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNM&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the brake system becomes contaminated, braking performance could be affected, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the vehicle control module and replace the air dryer filter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V927000: Suspension:Front:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNM&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Because of this, the driver may lose control of the vehicle or parts may be dropped on the road, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the hub assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V097000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNM&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Also, the bolt connecting the upper steering shaft to the lower steering shaft may not have been properly tightened. Separation of the steering shaft will result in complete loss of steering which may lead to a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2017 Volvo VNM, thoroughly evaluate the condition of the engine and transmission options, along with service records. While specific owner complaint data is limited, commercial trucks demand rigorous pre-purchase inspections to ensure long-term operational readiness.
