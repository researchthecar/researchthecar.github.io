The 2008 Volvo VNM is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for regional and long-haul trucking. Engine options for this year included the Volvo D13 and Cummins ISX series. The Volvo D13 produced approximately 400-500 horsepower and 1450-1850 lb-ft of torque depending on the specific tuning. Transmission options included manual and automated manual transmissions, typically the Volvo I-Shift or Eaton Fuller models. Owners often praise the VNM for its comfortable cab, fuel efficiency, and reliability, while common complaints involve the complexity of the engine electronics, the cost of parts, and the occasional issues associated with the automated manual transmissions.

**Engine**

**Volvo D13**
* **EGR Cooler Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant loss, white smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR cooler.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough running engine, decreased fuel economy, black smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace faulty injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**Cummins ISX**
* **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, check engine light, excessive black smoke.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive oil consumption, unusual noises from the turbocharger.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Volvo I-Shift**
* **Shifting Problems/Erratic Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, skipping gears, harsh shifting, transmission warning lights.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty solenoids, sensors, or the transmission control module (TCM). Calibration of the transmission may also be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, unusual noises during clutch engagement.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Eaton Fuller Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Shift Actuator Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, error codes related to the shift actuators, inability to select certain gears.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the shift actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Truck sitting unevenly, hissing sounds from the air suspension components, air suspension warning lights.
    * Solution: Locate and repair or replace leaking air bags, air lines, or leveling valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ per air bag
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Worn King Pins**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose steering, wandering, uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the king pins and associated bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+ per axle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Sensor Failures (Various)**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine lights, ABS warning lights, transmission warning lights, performance issues.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty sensors (wheel speed sensors, engine sensors, etc.)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ per sensor
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Wiring Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, short circuits, corrosion.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace damaged sections of the wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or non-functioning headlights.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight bulb or assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Mirror Damage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracked or broken mirrors
    * Solution: Replace the mirror assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ per mirror
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inadequate heating or cooling, blower motor malfunctions, strange noises.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components (blower motor, compressor, etc.)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Gauge Cluster Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate readings, flickering lights, complete failure of the instrument cluster.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2008 Volvo VNM can experience a range of issues, from engine and transmission problems to suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior concerns. Engine issues can range from EGR cooler failures to turbocharger problems, while transmission issues include erratic shifting and clutch failures. Suspension problems like air leaks and worn king pins are common. Electrical problems and sensor failures can be costly. While some issues are minor, others can be quite expensive to repair, making careful inspection and maintenance crucial for owners.
