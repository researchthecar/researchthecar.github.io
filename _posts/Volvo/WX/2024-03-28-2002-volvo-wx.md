**Introduction**: The 2002 Volvo WX is a midsize luxury sedan offered with a 2.5L 5-cylinder engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It produces 197 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle is praised for its refined interior, comfortable ride, and safety features. However, it has received criticism for its uninspiring performance and lackluster fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.5L 5-cylinder Engine**
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, rough idle, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Belt Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing or grinding noises from the engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing belt tensioner and belt
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or delayed shifting, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises from the front or rear of the vehicle, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Control Arm Bushing Deterioration**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing or creaking noises from the suspension, premature tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, sluggish electrical accessories, charging system warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start after sitting for extended periods, electrical accessories draining battery
    * Solution: Identify and fix source of power drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies or seal leaks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative climate control functions, such as heating, cooling, or ventilation
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or sensors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Crack**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible cracks forming on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Volvo WX is a generally reliable vehicle, but it does have some potential issues to be aware of. The most common issues are related to the engine, such as intake manifold gasket failure and timing belt tensioner failure. Other issues, such as suspension and electrical problems, are less severe and can be addressed with routine maintenance. Overall, the cost to fix these issues can range from minor to moderate, depending on the severity of the problem.
