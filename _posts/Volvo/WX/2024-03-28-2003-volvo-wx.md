**Introduction**

The 2003 Volvo WX was a compact luxury sedan, known for its stylish design and comfortable interior. It came with two engine and transmission options: a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated I5 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission; and a turbocharged 2.5L I5 engine paired with a 6-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.5L Naturally Aspirated I5 Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Turbocharged 2.5L I5 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gaskets**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant or oil, rough idle, poor acceleration
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Fluid Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission seals and gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises when driving over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Shock Absorbers**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncing or swaying when driving over rough roads, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine starting difficulties, weak or dead battery
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Bulbs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or burned-out headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Buttons**
    * Description/Symptoms: Non-functional or sticky center console buttons
    * Solution: Replace center console buttons
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clogged or leaky sunroof drain plugs, leading to water leaks
    * Solution: Clean or replace sunroof drain plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2003 Volvo WX has a few common issues to watch out for. The most serious and expensive issue is the intake manifold gasket failure on the turbocharged engine. Other issues, such as suspension and electrical problems, are generally more minor and less costly to repair.
