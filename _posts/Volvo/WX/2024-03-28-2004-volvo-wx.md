**2004 Volvo WX**

The 2004 Volvo WX was a mid-size luxury sedan that offered a combination of performance and comfort. It featured a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and a potent V8 engine. However, the vehicle has received mixed reviews over the years, with some owners reporting various issues.

**Engine**

* **V8 Engine**
    * **Intake Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: 04V252000
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noises on startup, reduced engine power
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations during acceleration
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Ball Joints**
    * **Wear and Tear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling noises while turning or over bumps
        * Solution: Replace ball joints
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle doesn't start, reduced battery life
    * Solution: Identify and correct electrical connection issues
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage**
    * **Corrosion**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible rust on metal parts under the vehicle
        * Solution: Treat and repair rust
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Plastic trim cracking or breaking
    * Solution: Replace center console trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Lights Malfunctioning**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warning lights or gauges not working properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace defective light bulbs or sensors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 Volvo WX has received mixed reviews from owners, with some reporting significant issues, while others have enjoyed a reliable experience. Common problems include engine valve issues, transmission shudder, suspension wear, and electrical problems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, so it is important to consider the specific condition of a used WX before making a purchase decision.
