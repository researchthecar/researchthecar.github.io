**Introduction**

The 2019 Volvo VNL 740 is a heavy-duty truck available with a variety of engine and transmission options. Other engine options include a 13-liter inline-6 turbocharged diesel with 500 horsepower and 1,850 lb-ft of torque, and a 16-liter inline-6 turbocharged diesel with 605 horsepower and 2,250 lb-ft of torque. The VNL 740 is praised for its fuel efficiency, comfortable interior, and advanced safety features. However, it has also been plagued by several notable issues.
