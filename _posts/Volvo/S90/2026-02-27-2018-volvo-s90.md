# 2018 Volvo S90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Volvo S90 offers an elegant Scandinavian design, a spacious interior, and a choice of sophisticated turbocharged and supercharged powertrains. While owner complaints for this model year are relatively limited, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and monitor for minor electrical or component concerns commonly reported in luxury sedans of this era.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (T5)

The T5 engine utilizes a single turbocharger to deliver approximately 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides efficient power for daily driving while maintaining balanced reliability for everyday use.

### 2.0L Supercharged and Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (T6)

The T6 powerplant combines a supercharger and a turbocharger to generate roughly 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Buyers should ensure regular maintenance to support the complex forced-induction system.

### 2.0L Supercharged, Turbocharged, and Electric Hybrid (T8)

The T8 plug-in hybrid pairs the T6 twin-charged engine with an electric motor to produce around 400 total system horsepower. This option delivers brisk performance and electric-only range, though long-term hybrid component care should be considered.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Geartronic Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission pairs across the S90 lineup to deliver smooth gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. Routine fluid checks help maintain long-term shifting refinement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V220000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The driver and passenger front seat rail rear flange nuts may not have been installed during assembly. If the nuts are missing, in the event of a crash, the front seat structure and position may be affected, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front seat rails for missing flange nuts, installing them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V800000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the GPS location is not provided to emergency personnel it can inhibit emergency response in the event of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will correct the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Volvo S90 is a stylish and comfortable luxury sedan with robust powertrain choices and advanced features. Because owner complaint data for this model year is minimal, shoppers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, fluid levels, and overall vehicle maintenance history to ensure a dependable ownership experience.
