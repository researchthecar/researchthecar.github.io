**2020 Volvo S90**

The 2020 Volvo S90 is a luxury sedan known for its stylish design, advanced safety features, and comfortable interior. Engine options include a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder with 250 horsepower (T5) or 316 horsepower (T6), both mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**T5**

* **Premature Timing Belt Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing belt may fail prematurely, causing catastrophic engine damage. Symptoms include a loss of power, unusual noises, and a check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing belt and potentially other engine components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**T6**

* **Turbocharger Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger solenoid may fail, resulting in reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, and a check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may develop a leak, resulting in fluid loss, slipping gears, and difficulty shifting.
    * Solution: Repairing the leak, replacing seals or gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front control arm bushings may wear prematurely, causing clunking or rattling noises over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replacement of the control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Climate Control Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control module may malfunction, resulting in erratic temperature control, fan noise, or no AC/heat.
    * Solution: Replacement of the climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic Roof Creaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The panoramic roof may develop a creaking or rattling noise when driving over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Lubrication of the roof rails and adjustment of the roof panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sun Visor Hinge Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sun visor hinges may break, causing the visors to fall or become loose.
    * Solution: Replacement of the sun visor assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2020 Volvo S90 has had some reported issues, but the severity of the problems varies. While some issues, such as turbocharger solenoid failure, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, like premature timing belt failure, can be catastrophic and costly. It is important to note that these issues may not be common to all S90 models and many owners have reported a positive experience with the vehicle.
