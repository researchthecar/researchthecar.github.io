# 2023 Volvo S90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Volvo S90 delivers a refined luxury sedan experience with advanced powertrain technology and upscale styling. Prospective buyers evaluating used models should review standard inspection points, as owner reports offer helpful context on real-world ownership.

---

## Engine

### B48/B5204 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers efficient power suited for daily driving. It provides a balanced combination of performance and refinement for the luxury sedan segment.

### B48/B5204 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged Plug-in Hybrid

This plug-in hybrid powertrain combines a 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder engine with an electric motor for enhanced output and electric-only driving range. It offers strong performance alongside improved fuel economy.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifts across all driving conditions. It pairs effectively with both standard and hybrid powertrain configurations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V908000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.
