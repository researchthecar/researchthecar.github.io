# 2023 Volvo S90

### Overview
The 2023 Volvo S90 is a luxury sedan that prioritizes safety, comfort, and sophisticated Scandinavian design. It offers a refined driving experience with a focus on advanced technology and premium materials. Reliability for the 2023 model year, based on available data, indicates a generally strong performance, particularly in its powertrain and core systems. Volvo's commitment to quality is evident, though like any vehicle, specific components can occasionally experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Volvo S90 demonstrates a strong reliability profile, aligning with Volvo's reputation for building durable and safe vehicles. Powertrain components, including the various 2.0L engine options and the 8-speed automatic transmission, show minimal reported issues, indicating robust engineering. The chassis and suspension systems are also generally trouble-free. Electrical systems, particularly the infotainment, may occasionally encounter minor software glitches that are typically resolved with updates. The hybrid variant's battery cooling system is a point to monitor, though not a widespread concern. Overall, the S90 is a reliable luxury sedan with a focus on long-term ownership satisfaction, provided standard maintenance is adhered to.
