# 2022 Volvo S90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo S90 delivers sophisticated luxury, advanced safety features, and a sleek Scandinavian design. For used-car buyers looking at this model year, understanding how the available powertrain options and overall build quality hold up is essential for making a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### B48/B50 2.0L Turbocharged/Supercharged 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder engine utilizes forced induction to produce strong output figures while maintaining efficiency. Buyers should keep up with routine maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

### B48 2.0L Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid

The plug-in hybrid variant pairs a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine with an electric motor for enhanced performance and efficiency. This setup offers an appealing blend of electric driving range and traditional fueling convenience.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission handles gear shifting duties smoothly across all trims and drivetrains. It generally operates reliably when serviced according to factory schedules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V908000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. The 2022 Volvo S90 remains a comfortable and technologically advanced luxury sedan option on the used market. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify that all mechanical and electrical systems operate correctly.
