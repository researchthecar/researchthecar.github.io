# 2022 Volvo S90

### Overview
The 2022 Volvo S90 is a luxury sedan that generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Volvo's commitment to quality and safety. Owners typically report a positive ownership experience with fewer unexpected mechanical issues compared to some competitors. The vehicle's advanced technology and premium materials contribute to its overall appeal. While no vehicle is entirely immune to problems, the S90 demonstrates a solid track record for its model year, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific feature usage rather than fundamental design flaws. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Volvo S90 demonstrates a high level of reliability, with most powertrain and chassis components performing as expected. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some cases, premature 12V battery drain. The plug-in hybrid variant has a specific concern related to its battery cooling system. Optional air suspension can also present issues with leaks. Overall, the S90 offers a dependable luxury sedan experience, with most potential problems being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures across the model range.
