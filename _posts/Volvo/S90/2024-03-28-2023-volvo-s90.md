**Introduction**
The 2023 Volvo S90 is a luxury sedan that offers a refined and sophisticated driving experience. It comes with a variety of engine options, including a turbocharged inline-four and a supercharged and turbocharged inline-four. The S90 is also available with a choice of rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive.

People who like the Volvo S90 praise its comfortable ride, luxurious interior, and advanced safety features. However, some drivers have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and suspension.

**Engine**
**Turbocharged Inline-Four**
* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Over time, carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, causing reduced engine performance and fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Remove the intake manifold and clean the valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail due to a variety of reasons, such as oil starvation or bearing failure. This can lead to a loss of power and increased exhaust smoke.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Supercharged and Turbocharged Inline-Four**
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to jump or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and inspect the timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**8-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may develop a fluid leak due to a faulty seal or gasket. This can lead to low fluid levels and transmission damage.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty seal or gasket and refill the transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front Suspension Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front suspension strut mount bearing can fail, causing a clunking or rattling noise when the suspension is compressed.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Electrical System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system can malfunction due to a variety of reasons, such as a faulty battery or alternator. This can lead to a variety of electrical problems, such as difficulty starting the engine or power outages.
    * Solution: Diagnose the electrical system and replace any faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water vapor can accumulate inside the headlights, causing condensation. This can impair visibility at night.
    * Solution: Replace the headlights or use a desiccant packet to absorb moisture.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system can malfunction due to a variety of reasons, such as a faulty sensor or actuator. This can lead to difficulty controlling the temperature or airflow in the cabin.
    * Solution: Diagnose the climate control system and replace any faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Belt Retractor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear seat belt retractor can fail, causing the seat belt to become stuck or unable to retract properly.
    * Solution: Replace the rear seat belt retractor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Volvo S90 is a well-regarded luxury sedan, but it does have some potential issues to be aware of. Some of the more serious issues, such as engine and transmission problems, can be expensive to fix. However, many of the other issues are relatively minor and can be resolved at a reasonable cost. Overall, the S90 is a solid choice for drivers who are looking for a comfortable and reliable sedan.
