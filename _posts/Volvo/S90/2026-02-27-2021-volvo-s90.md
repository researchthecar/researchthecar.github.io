# 2021 Volvo S90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Volvo S90 delivers sophisticated Scandinavian design, a spacious cabin, and advanced powertrain technology to the luxury sedan market. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating its powertrain options and monitoring owner-reported feedback helps ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (T5)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 250 horsepower and provides a smooth balance of daily efficiency and power. It serves as the standard entry powerplant for front-wheel-drive models.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Inline-4 (T6)

Combining both a turbocharger and a supercharger, this 2.0-liter engine generates around 316 horsepower with standard all-wheel drive. It offers brisk acceleration, though buyers should ensure proper maintenance records are present.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Inline-4 Plug-In Hybrid (T8)

The T8 plug-in hybrid pairs forced-induction four-cylinder performance with an electric motor to deliver around 400 total horsepower. It offers enhanced performance and efficiency, though its complex hybrid systems require careful inspection.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed Geartronic automatic transmission is paired across the S90 lineup, offering smooth and responsive shifts. Overall reliability has been generally solid, though routine fluid checks are recommended during pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V716000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Separation of the rotor and shaft may cause a reduction in braking assistance and a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Electronic Stability Control (ESC), increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the hydraulic unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Volvo S90 is a stylish and well-appointed luxury cruiser with sophisticated powertrain choices. Used-car buyers should prioritize models with thorough service history records and conduct a comprehensive mechanical inspection before finalizing a purchase.
