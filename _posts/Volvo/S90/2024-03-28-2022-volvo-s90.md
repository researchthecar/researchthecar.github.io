**Introduction**

The 2022 Volvo S90 is a luxurious sedan that offers a blend of comfort, performance, and technology. It comes equipped with a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 295 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. Notable features of the S90 include its spacious cabin, intuitive infotainment system, and advanced safety features. However, like any vehicle, the S90 has experienced some issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Intake manifold runner control issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning intake manifold runner control can cause hesitation, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replacing the intake manifold runner control actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Gear shifting problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or delayed engagement.
    * Solution: Updating or replacing the transmission control module (TCM).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear suspension noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Abnormal noises from the rear suspension, particularly on uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Inspecting and replacing worn or damaged rear suspension components, such as bushings, struts, or springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment system freezing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may occasionally freeze or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Restarting the infotainment system or updating the software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: None (if covered under warranty)
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 22E021000

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle or warning messages.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the underlying issue, such as a faulty alternator or parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per assembly
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard creaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises coming from the dashboard area.
    * Solution: Tightening loose trim pieces or applying sound-absorbing materials.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Volvo S90 has some known issues, but most are relatively minor and can be addressed with regular maintenance and repairs. The overall reliability of the vehicle is still considered good. However, some issues, such as transmission problems or battery drain, can be more severe and expensive to fix.
