The 2022 Volvo S90 is a luxury sedan known for its stylish design, comfortable ride, and focus on safety. Available engine options for 2022 included the B5 mild-hybrid and the T8 Recharge plug-in hybrid. The B5 powertrain, a 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder engine paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system, produces 295 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. Reviewers often praise the S90 for its elegant interior, comfortable seating, and advanced safety features. Common complaints include the complexity of the infotainment system and occasional concerns about long-term reliability.

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The Sensus infotainment system, which controls navigation, audio, climate, and other vehicle functions, may freeze, crash, or become unresponsive. This can occur randomly or during specific operations such as using Apple CarPlay or Android Auto.
    * Solution: Volvo has released software updates to address these issues. A software update performed by the dealer or over-the-air (OTA) updates may resolve the problem. In some cases, a hard reset of the system or replacement of the central control module may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Software updates are typically free. Replacing the central control module can cost $500 - $1500+.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **12V Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Prematurely discharged 12V battery that can result in failure to start or electrical malfunctions with various systems.
    * Solution: Have the battery tested. If the battery is still good, determine if there is an electrical component that is not shutting down when the vehicle is off and draining the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof/Moonroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling, failing to open or close, or leaking.
    *   Solution: Inspect the sunroof assembly for damaged components and lubricate the tracks. If the motor is faulty, it will need to be replaced. Inspect the seals for any leaks.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Volvo S90 generally experiences a few notable issues. The most prominent concerns are with the electrical system, particularly the infotainment system's responsiveness. Other concerns that exist are sunroof malfunctions.
