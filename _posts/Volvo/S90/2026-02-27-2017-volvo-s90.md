# 2017 Volvo S90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Volvo S90 brought striking Scandinavian design and an array of advanced safety and luxury features to the midsize luxury sedan market. As an entry in Volvo's modern lineup, buyers should carefully evaluate both powertrain choices and reported owner complaints regarding electrical and auxiliary systems when shopping in the used marketplace.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing standard power for base trims. Owners generally find it smooth for daily driving, though proper maintenance is essential for longevity.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged 4-Cylinder

This dual-charged 2.0-liter engine utilizes both a supercharger and turbocharger to generate roughly 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque for higher-tier trims. While it offers brisk acceleration, its complex forced-induction system warrants a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It delivers prompt, smooth gear shifts designed to optimize both fuel efficiency and power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V800000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the GPS location is not provided to emergency personnel it can inhibit emergency response in the event of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will correct the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V690000: Equipment:Appliance:Air Conditioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S90&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Significant water drainage into the passenger compartment may adversely affect air bag deployment in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of occupant injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air conditioning drain hose for proper installation, replacing the hose as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Volvo S90 offers an upscale cabin and robust safety credentials, making it an appealing used luxury choice. Buyers should ensure all electronic features operate correctly and prioritize vehicles with documented maintenance histories.
