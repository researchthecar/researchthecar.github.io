The 2006 Volvo XC90 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its safety features, comfortable ride, and spacious interior. Engine options included a 2.5L turbocharged inline-5, a 3.2L inline-6, and a 4.4L V8. The 2.5T produced 208 hp and 236 lb-ft of torque. The inline-5 and inline-6 engines were paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission, while the V8 came with a 6-speed automatic. Owners often appreciate the vehicle's practicality and family-friendly design, but common complaints include higher-than-average maintenance costs and some reliability concerns.

**Engine**

**2.5L Turbocharged Inline-5**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the turbocharger, loss of power, check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **PCV System Clogging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, oil leaks, whistling noise from the engine, check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replace the PCV valve and associated components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.2L Inline-6**

*   **Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, stalling, loss of power, check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replace the VVT solenoid or actuator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Thermostat Housing Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace the thermostat housing.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**4.4L V8**

*   **Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks, burning smell.
    *   Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Alternator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Battery light illumination, dimming headlights, vehicle not starting.
    *   Solution: Replace the alternator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Aisin Warner 5-Speed Automatic (Inline-5 & Inline-6)**

*   **Valve Body Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, slipping, delayed engagement.
    *   Solution: Replace or rebuild the valve body.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Aisin Warner 6-Speed Automatic (V8)**

*   **Torque Converter Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering during acceleration, slipping.
    *   Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the front suspension, especially when turning.
    *   Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Rear Suspension Airbags (if equipped) Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging in the rear, air compressor running constantly.
    *   Solution: Replace the air springs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Driver Information Module (DIM) Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete failure of the instrument cluster, flickering lights, warning messages.
    *   Solution: Repair or replace the DIM.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Central Electronic Module (CEM) Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Various electrical malfunctions, including issues with the windows, locks, and lights.
    *   Solution: Repair or replace the CEM.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sunroof Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing.
    *   Solution: Replace the sunroof motor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Windshield Wiper Linkage Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Windshield wipers not working properly.
    *   Solution: Replace the wiper linkage.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Climate Control Blend Door Actuator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control, clicking noise from behind the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replace the blend door actuator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Seat Heater Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working.
    *   Solution: Replace the heating element or switch.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2006 Volvo XC90, while offering a comfortable and safe driving experience, is prone to various issues, particularly in the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand, depending on the severity and component affected. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into the overall cost of ownership. The severity of the issues varies. Some issues like thermostat housing leaks are minor and easy to fix. Other issues like turbo failure or transmission issues can be costly to fix.
