# 2016 Volvo XC90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Volvo XC90 marked the dramatic reinvention of the brand's flagship luxury SUV, featuring striking Scandinavian design and advanced technology. While its modern styling and comprehensive safety features continue to attract used-car buyers, early model-year vehicles can present unique mechanical and electronic complexities. Prospective owners should review vehicle history thoroughly and verify that all software updates and previous mechanical repairs have been properly handled.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged 4-Cylinder (T6)

The T6 engine is a 2.0-liter dual-charged four-cylinder generating approximately 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and solid efficiency, though its complex forced-induction system requires diligent maintenance.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (T5)

The T5 engine is a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder producing around 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides a balanced blend of everyday performance and efficiency for standard driving needs.

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged 4-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid (T8)

The T8 Twin Engine pairs a 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder with an electric motor to deliver around 400 total system horsepower. It provides strong electrified performance, though its advanced hybrid architecture demands attentive care.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across all major engine trims and features all-wheel drive or front-wheel drive configurations. It delivers smooth shifts and helps optimize highway fuel economy, though routine fluid checks are recommended.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V308000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Hoses/Lines/Piping/Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Exposure to heat and humidity may cause the engine coolant bleeder hose to crack, resulting in a coolant leak. If the coolant leak goes unnoticed, coolant may accumulate in the catalytic converter heat shield insulation and catch fire when the vehicle is stationary, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo Cars will notify owners, and dealers will replace the engine side coolant bleeder hose, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V273000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Liquid or condensation on the rear seat control display may cause the front passenger seat to move back as far as possible. The rear passenger may become trapped by the front seat without the ability to cancel the seat's movement, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo Car will notify owners, and dealers will update the rear seat control display software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V690000: Equipment:Appliance:Air Conditioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Significant water drainage into the passenger compartment may adversely affect air bag deployment in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of occupant injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air conditioning drain hose for proper installation, replacing the hose as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V497000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The third row seating side curtain air bags may not fully inflate. Underinflated air bags may not fully protect third row occupants, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will modify the D-pillar interior panels to allow full inflation of the third row airbags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the routing of the SIPS wiring, correcting the routing and installing a protective sleeve as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the second-row seat belt buckle anchorage bolts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Volvo XC90 offers exceptional comfort, high-end styling, and advanced safety equipment that make it stand out on the used market. However, because it was the inaugural year for a completely redesigned generation, buyers should approach with careful inspection. Ensuring a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verifying service history will go a long way toward securing a reliable ownership experience.
