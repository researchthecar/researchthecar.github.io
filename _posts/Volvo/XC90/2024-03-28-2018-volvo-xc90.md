**2018 Volvo XC90**

The 2018 Volvo XC90 is a luxury SUV that combines style, technology, and safety. It is offered with a choice of three engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder with 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, a 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder with 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder with an electric motor that produces 400 horsepower and 472 lb-ft of torque. The XC90 is also available with a choice of two transmission options: an eight-speed automatic transmission and an eight-speed automatic transmission with all-wheel drive.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Oil Leaks:** Oil may leak from the turbocharger oil lines. Symptoms include a burning smell, blue smoke from the exhaust, and low oil levels. The solution is to replace the oil lines. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail prematurely. Symptoms include a loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, and a whistling sound. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500

**2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Four-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Four-Cylinder with Electric Motor**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may shift harshly or slip. Symptoms include difficulty engaging gears, a shuddering sensation, and delayed gear changes. The solution is to replace the transmission fluid and filter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Transmission Failure:** The transmission may fail prematurely. Symptoms include a loss of power, a grinding noise, and a burnt smell. The solution is to replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission with All-Wheel Drive**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** The strut mounts may fail prematurely. Symptoms include a knocking sound, a vibration, and a loss of handling. The solution is to replace the strut mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Rear Suspension Noise:** A clicking or squeaking noise may come from the rear suspension. Symptoms are most noticeable when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces. The solution is to lubricate the suspension components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle and reduced electrical power. The solution is to replace the battery or identify and repair the source of the drain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system may experience glitches or freezes. Symptoms include distorted audio, a blank screen, or unresponsive controls. The solution is to update the software or replace the infotainment unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights. Symptoms include fogging or water droplets inside the lens. The solution is to replace the headlights. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system may malfunction. Symptoms include uneven temperatures, insufficient airflow, or a lack of heating or cooling. The solution is to repair or replace the climate control components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Sunroof Leaks:** Water may leak through the sunroof. Symptoms include water stains on the headliner or floor. The solution is to seal the sunroof. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary**

The 2018 Volvo XC90 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has had some reported issues. The most common problems are with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. If you are considering purchasing a 2018 Volvo XC90, it is important to be aware of these potential issues and factor them into your decision.
