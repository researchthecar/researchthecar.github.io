**Introduction:**

The 2019 Volvo XC90 is a midsize luxury SUV that boasts an upscale cabin, advanced safety features, and potent engine options. It is available with three engine choices: a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder, a turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder, and a plug-in hybrid powertrain that combines a 2.0-liter supercharged and turbocharged four-cylinder engine with an electric motor. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic for all three powertrains and an all-wheel drive system. While the XC90 has been praised for its comfort, safety, and performance, it has also faced some issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder (T5)**
* **Engine Stalling:** The engine may suddenly stall while driving, leading to a loss of power.
* Description/Symptoms: The engine stops unexpectedly and cannot be restarted.
* Solution: Reprogramming the engine control unit (ECU) with updated software addresses this issue.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Four-Cylinder (T6)**
* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger assembly can fail prematurely, resulting in reduced engine power.
* Description/Symptoms: Loss of acceleration, decreased power output, and potentially audible rattling or whistling noises.
* Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Plug-In Hybrid (T8)**
* **Battery Malfunction:** The high-voltage battery may experience overheating or other issues that affect its performance.
* Description/Symptoms: Limited electric range, reduced acceleration, and potential battery warning messages on the dashboard.
* Solution: Replacement of the battery pack or individual battery modules.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting Delays:** Occasional delays or hesitations may occur during gear changes.
* Description/Symptoms: Slow or rough gear transitions, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
* Solution: Software updates or transmission fluid flushes can resolve this issue.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:** The air suspension compressor can fail, resulting in loss of ride height control.
* Description/Symptoms: Lowered ride height, uneven vehicle stance, and potential suspension warning messages.
* Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Excessive battery drain can occur when the vehicle is left parked for extended periods.
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle or a dead battery.
* Solution: Identification and repair of electrical system faults or replacement of the battery.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture can accumulate inside the headlight housings, impairing visibility.
* Description/Symptoms: Fogging or condensation inside the headlights.
* Solution: Replacement or resealing of faulty headlight assemblies.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in inconsistent or ineffective temperature regulation.
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty controlling cabin temperature, excessive noise from the system, or air leaks.
* Solution: Repair or replacement of faulty climate control components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Volvo XC90 has faced some issues, particularly with certain engine and transmission components. While the severity of the issues varies, some of them can be costly to repair, potentially impacting the overall ownership experience. However, it's important to note that the XC90 also offers numerous advantages, including its safety features, comfort, and performance, making it a compelling choice for those who prioritize these attributes.
