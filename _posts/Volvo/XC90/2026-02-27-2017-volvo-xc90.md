# 2017 Volvo XC90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Volvo XC90 offers a sophisticated interior, advanced safety technology, and distinct powertrain choices that make it a compelling luxury midsize SUV on the used market. While it provides a refined driving experience, used-car buyers should review vehicle history thoroughly and inspect the complex electronics and drivetrain components prior to purchase, as owner-reported complaints highlight occasional mechanical and electrical snags.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (T5)

The T5 features a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing approximately 250 horsepower. It provides efficient performance for daily driving, though buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals.

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged 4-Cylinder (T6)

The T6 utilizes a 2.0-liter engine with both a turbocharger and a supercharger to generate roughly 316 horsepower. It delivers brisk acceleration, but its complex forced-induction system warrants a careful mechanical inspection.

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged 4-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid (T8)

The T8 plug-in hybrid pairs the T6's forced-induction engine with an electric motor to output around 400 total system horsepower. It offers enhanced efficiency and performance, though prospective buyers should check the health of the hybrid battery system.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across the vehicle's engine lineup and all-wheel-drive systems. It offers smooth gear changes, but proper fluid maintenance is essential for long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V273000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Liquid or condensation on the rear seat control display may cause the front passenger seat to move back as far as possible. The rear passenger may become trapped by the front seat without the ability to cancel the seat's movement, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo Car will notify owners, and dealers will update the rear seat control display software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V800000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the GPS location is not provided to emergency personnel it can inhibit emergency response in the event of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will correct the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V690000: Equipment:Appliance:Air Conditioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Significant water drainage into the passenger compartment may adversely affect air bag deployment in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of occupant injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air conditioning drain hose for proper installation, replacing the hose as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V370000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The Micro Gas Generator (MGG), a subcomponent part used within the right hand side seat belt retractor for the third row seats, may detach after the seatbelt retractor pretensioner is deployed. Although, the seat belt will operate as intended in the event of a crash, if the MGG detaches, it may be a projectile within the vehicle cabin, increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the third row passenger's side seatbelt retractor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Volvo XC90 remains a stylish and comfortable choice for families seeking a luxury three-row SUV. Because owner reports indicate potential complexity in its advanced electrical and powertrain systems, buyers are encouraged to secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify a consistent service history to ensure trouble-free ownership.
