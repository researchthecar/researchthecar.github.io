**Introduction:**

The 2016 Volvo XC90 is a luxury SUV powered by a turbocharged and supercharged four-cylinder engine mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. It boasts 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The XC90 is praised for its stylish exterior, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, it also has several reported issues.

**Engine**

**T6 Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, whistling or whining noises from the turbocharger.
   * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Supercharger Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, decreased fuel efficiency, unusual noises from the engine.
   * Solution: Replace the supercharger.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, loss of power, Check Engine light illumination.
   * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Harsh Shifting:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Hard or jerky gear changes, slipping or delayed shifting.
   * Solution: Software update or transmission repair/replacement.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises when turning or driving over bumps, reduced handling and stability.
   * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Control Arm Bushing Deterioration:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Squealing or creaking noises, vibration in the steering wheel, uneven tire wear.
   * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, slow engine cranking, flickering lights.
   * Solution: Software update, module replacement, or battery replacement.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Bluetooth connectivity issues, frozen screen, navigation errors.
   * Solution: Software update or infotainment unit replacement.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof won't open or close, grinding or clicking noises.
   * Solution: Replace the sunroof motor.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Latch Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate won't open or latch properly, warning messages on the instrument cluster.
   * Solution: Replace the tailgate latch.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Heater Core Leakage:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Steamy or musty odor inside the vehicle, coolant loss.
   * Solution: Replace the heater core.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from the vents, excessive noise from the blower motor.
   * Solution: Replace the blower motor.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Volvo XC90 has a wide range of reported issues across various components. The severity of these issues varies from minor inconveniences to more significant and expensive repairs. Overall, owners should be aware of potential engine and transmission problems, as well as suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior concerns.
