# 2022 Volvo XC90

### Overview
The 2022 Volvo XC90 is a luxury three-row SUV known for its safety, comfort, and sophisticated Scandinavian design. It offers a range of powertrains, including mild-hybrid and plug-in hybrid options, emphasizing fuel efficiency and performance. Reliability data for the 2022 model year suggests a generally strong performance, with most reported issues being minor or related to specific component failures rather than widespread systemic problems. Owners generally appreciate the vehicle's build quality and advanced technology, though like any complex vehicle, occasional electronic glitches or minor mechanical concerns can arise. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Volvo XC90 demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly with its mild-hybrid powertrains. The primary areas of concern are related to the plug-in hybrid system's battery cooling and potential issues with the optional air suspension, which are not universal to all models. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and occasional 12V battery failures, are also noted but are not considered widespread. For owners of the T8 Recharge model, monitoring the battery cooling system is advisable. Otherwise, the XC90 offers a dependable luxury SUV experience with a focus on safety and comfort.
