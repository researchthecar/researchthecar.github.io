# 2022 Volvo XC90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo XC90 blends Scandinavian luxury, advanced safety features, and flexible seating into a stylish midsize luxury SUV. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues involving complex electronics, turbocharger performance, and occasional fluid or fuel concerns. While many drivers appreciate its refined ride and striking design, careful pre-purchase inspection is advised.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged/Supercharged I4

The standard gasoline powertrain utilizes a 2.0-liter turbocharged and sometimes supercharged four-cylinder engine, delivering robust power for highway cruising. It provides competitive performance but requires attentive maintenance to ensure long-term reliability of its forced-induction systems.

**Turbocharger and Oil Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced turbocharger malfunctions requiring costly repairs, sometimes accompanied by unexpected oil consumption or smoke from under the hood.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic inspection by a qualified mechanic; may involve replacing turbo components or addressing oil leaks.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid T8

The T8 Recharge plug-in hybrid pairs a 2.0-liter turbocharged engine with an electric motor to offer strong combined horsepower and electric-only driving range. Buyers praise its efficiency and brisk acceleration, though complex hybrid electronics warrant a thorough check.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

An 8-speed automatic transmission comes paired with standard powertrains, delivering smooth gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. Routine fluid checks help maintain shift quality over the vehicle's lifespan.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Digital Display

Controls for navigation, climate, and vehicle settings rely heavily on digital interfaces, which can occasionally experience software-related glitches.

**Screen Freezing and Blackouts**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment display and digital gauge cluster can intermittently freeze, glitch, or go completely black, disabling climate controls and backup cameras.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets performed by a dealership can sometimes resolve display glitches, though hardware replacement may be necessary for persistent failures.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Advanced safety systems provide comprehensive occupant protection but depend on sensitive sensors that can trigger service warnings.

**SRS Warning Messages**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may encounter urgent dashboard warnings regarding the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS), requiring multiple dealership visits for software patches or sensor checks.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a certified technician is required to identify faulty sensors, wiring connections, or control modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V001000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A screw inside the gearbox housing can cause the steering wheel to lock up and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V827000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The second-row seat belt buckle and lower belt anchor bolts may not be tightened properly. An improperly tightened seat belt buckle may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing their risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the second-row seat belt buckle anchorage bolts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volvo XC90 offers strong safety ratings, comfortable seating, and premium styling, making it an attractive used luxury SUV choice. Buyers should verify that all software recalls have been completed and carefully inspect the infotainment electronics, turbocharger health, and auxiliary systems during a pre-purchase test drive.
