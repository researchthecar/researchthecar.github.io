# 2022 Volvo XC90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo XC90 represents a stylish and tech-forward option in the luxury midsize SUV segment, offering a sophisticated cabin and advanced safety features. For used-car buyers investigating this model year, reviewing powertrain options and owner-reported feedback helps ensure a confident purchase. While the vehicle offers strong performance and comfort, prospective owners should inspect the vehicle thoroughly for any electronic or mechanical anomalies reported by early adopters.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B5)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 247 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, utilizing mild-hybrid technology to balance efficiency and daily drivability. It serves as the standard powerplant for base trims, generally offering smooth operation for everyday commuting.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Inline-4 (B6)

Featuring both a turbocharger and a supercharger, this 2.0-liter engine generates approximately 295 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. It provides robust acceleration across the power band, though buyers should verify complete service records to ensure proper maintenance of the complex induction system.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid (T8 Recharge)

Pairing a turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter engine with an electric motor, the T8 Recharge delivers a combined output of around 455 horsepower. This plug-in hybrid setup offers strong performance and electric-only driving range, representing the high-performance option in the lineup.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission handles gear shifting across all trims, pairing with front-wheel or all-wheel-drive configurations. It is designed to deliver smooth gear changes and optimized highway efficiency, matching well with the diverse engine options.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V001000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A screw inside the gearbox housing can cause the steering wheel to lock up and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V827000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The second-row seat belt buckle and lower belt anchor bolts may not be tightened properly. An improperly tightened seat belt buckle may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing their risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the second-row seat belt buckle anchorage bolts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2022 Volvo XC90 remains an appealing choice for shoppers seeking a luxurious and well-equipped three-row SUV.
