# 2021 Volvo XC90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Volvo XC90 continues the brand's tradition of offering a luxurious, safety-focused midsize SUV with distinct Scandinavian styling and refined powertrain options. For used car buyers, the 2021 model year presents a sophisticated driving experience with advanced technology features, though prospective owners should review vehicle history and maintenance records carefully.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 (T5)

The T5 features a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 250 horsepower. It provides balanced performance and efficiency for daily driving.

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged I4 (T6)

The T6 utilizes both a turbocharger and a supercharger on its 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine to generate roughly 316 horsepower. This setup delivers strong acceleration across the power band.

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged Plug-In Hybrid (T8 Recharge)

The T8 Recharge pairs the turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter engine with electric drive components to produce a combined output of around 400 horsepower. It offers electric-only driving range alongside robust overall performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission comes paired across the XC90 lineup, delivering smooth and efficient gear shifts suited to front-wheel or all-wheel-drive configurations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V682000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V716000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Separation of the rotor and shaft may cause a reduction in braking assistance and a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Electronic Stability Control (ESC), increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the hydraulic unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V001000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The Supplementary Restraint System (SRS) control unit may not have been properly attached to the car body and may detach during a crash. A detached SRS control unit can decrease the performance of occupant protection systems such as air bags, seat belts, and high voltage battery disconnect, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Car will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, as necessary, tighten the SRS control unit mounting screws, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V001000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A screw inside the gearbox housing can cause the steering wheel to lock up and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V827000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The second-row seat belt buckle and lower belt anchor bolts may not be tightened properly. An improperly tightened seat belt buckle may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing their risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the second-row seat belt buckle anchorage bolts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Volvo XC90 offers a compelling blend of luxury, safety, and versatile powertrain choices for used car shoppers. While standard options and overall refinement make it an attractive midsize SUV choice, buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is completed.
