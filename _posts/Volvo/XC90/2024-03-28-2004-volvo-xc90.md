**Introduction to the 2004 Volvo XC90**

The 2004 Volvo XC90 is a mid-size luxury SUV that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and a high level of safety features. It was available with two engine options: a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-5 and a 4.4-liter V8, each paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Drivers praise its exceptional crash test ratings, refined interior, and versatile seating configuration. However, some have criticized its sluggish acceleration, poor fuel economy, and lack of certain modern amenities.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Turbocharged Inline-5**

* **Intake Manifold Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the composite intake manifold, causing coolant and oil leaks, engine overheating, and reduced power.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the turbocharger, causing loss of boost, reduced power, and increased exhaust smoke.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $2,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.4-liter V8**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, delayed engagements, and rough shifting.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the torque converter, causing shuddering when shifting, loss of power, and overheating.
    * Solution: Replacement of torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the air suspension compressor, causing uneven ride height, reduced handling, and increased wear on suspension components.
    * Solution: Replacement of compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Deterioration of rear suspension bushings, causing clunking noises, uneven tire wear, and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electronic Throttle Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the electronic throttle module, causing hesitation and loss of power while driving.
    * Solution: Replacement of throttle module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery discharge, causing difficulty starting the vehicle or premature battery failure.
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of electrical system components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Lens Hazing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Haziness of headlight lenses, reducing visibility at night.
    * Solution: Polishing or replacement of headlight lenses
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of climate control unit, resulting in lack of heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Replacement of climate control unit or associated components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the dashboard material, affecting aesthetics.
    * Solution: Replacement of dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 Volvo XC90 offers a luxurious and spacious experience, but it is not without its potential issues. The most common problems include intake manifold cracking, turbocharger failure, transmission slippage, air suspension compressor failure, and electronic throttle module failure. While the severity of these issues can vary, they can all result in significant repair costs. Overall, the XC90 is a dependable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchasing decision.
