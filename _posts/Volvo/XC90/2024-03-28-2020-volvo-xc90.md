**Introduction:**

The 2020 Volvo XC90 is a luxurious and spacious SUV that offers a refined driving experience. The base engine is a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder producing 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. A choice of turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder and a supercharged and turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engines are also available, generating 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, and 400 horsepower and 472 lb-ft of torque, respectively. All engine options come paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels.

Owners appreciate the XC90's comfortable ride, elegant interior, and advanced safety features. However, some have encountered issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine:**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, reduced engine power, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

**Front and Rear Suspension:**

* **Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, uneven tire wear, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Electrical Harness Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent power loss, starting issues, electrical component malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin from the sunroof
    * Solution: Replace or repair sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Tailgate Hinge Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate not opening or closing properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate hinge
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Heated Steering Wheel Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heated steering wheel not working
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2020 Volvo XC90 is a solid SUV with some potential issues to be aware of. While the engine and transmission issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, the suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems can be more costly and time-consuming to resolve. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues should be considered when making a purchasing decision.
