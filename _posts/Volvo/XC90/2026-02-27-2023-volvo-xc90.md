# 2023 Volvo XC90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Volvo XC90 represents a stylish, feature-rich luxury midsize SUV, but prospective used-car buyers should review potential trouble spots before purchasing. While it delivers strong comfort and high-end tech, owner feedback highlights recurring issues tied to software glitches, infotainment behavior, and powertrain performance quirks. Careful inspection of maintenance history and software update records is advised.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine provides balanced power for daily driving. It delivers competitive efficiency for a three-row luxury SUV, though select owners report occasional electronic performance throttling.

**Reduced Engine Performance / Turtle Mode**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a sudden loss of engine power accompanied by a check engine light and reduced speed limitations known as turtle mode.
- **Solution:** Dealer diagnosis, system software updates, or component resetting.

### Plug-In Hybrid Powertrain

The plug-in hybrid option combines forced induction with electric drive motors for strong combined horsepower and electric-only driving capability. It offers robust performance but adds complexity to the vehicle's electrical architecture.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The smooth 8-speed automatic transmission routes power reliably to front- or all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally operates seamlessly under normal driving conditions without systemic mechanical failures.

## Suspension

### Wheels

The suspension provides a compliant, luxury-oriented ride quality across varying road conditions, but low-profile wheel configurations merit careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Premature Wheel Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Alloy wheels develop structural cracks without any known impact from potholes or collisions.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheel rims thoroughly and replace damaged wheels as necessary.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components offer strong stopping power, though select owners note electronic brake integration glitches linked to remote software updates.

**Brake Pedal Stiffness and Failure Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual firmness or tension in the brake pedal upon shifting into reverse, or warning messages indicating brake failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake software calibration and update modules per manufacturer service guidelines.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Electrical System

The vehicle's heavy reliance on complex digital architecture can lead to occasional software-driven anomalies affecting displays and auxiliary systems.

**Infotainment Reboots and Screen Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment display unexpectedly shuts down and auto-restarts repeatedly while driving, temporarily disabling connected functions.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates or reset the central display module at an authorized service center.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling while operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electrical and fuel management systems inspected by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V012000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A diagnostic error in the Brake Control Module 2 (BCM2) may cause a loss of Antilock Brake System (ABS), Electronic Stability Control (ESC), and traction control. A reduction in brake support functions can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V188000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The false warning message could prevent the detection of an actual e-call system failure, which could delay an emergency response during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the telematics and connectivity antenna module (TCAM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V827000: Seat Belts:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The second-row seat belt buckle and lower belt anchor bolts may not be tightened properly. An improperly tightened seat belt buckle may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing their risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the second-row seat belt buckle anchorage bolts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V908000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC90&modelYear=2023)**

A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Volvo XC90 offers upscale luxury, safety, and flexible powertrain choices, making it an appealing used purchase. Buyers should thoroughly verify that all software updates have been successfully applied and test all infotainment, brake, and electronic systems during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid lingering software-related inconveniences.
