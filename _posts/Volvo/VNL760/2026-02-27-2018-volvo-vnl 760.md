### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Volvo VNL 760 demonstrates a high level of reliability across its major systems, including engines, transmissions, and suspension. The primary area where minor issues have been noted is within the electrical system, specifically concerning intermittent wiring harness connections, which can lead to various operational glitches. However, these are not indicative of systemic failure and are generally repairable. The exterior and interior components are robust, with no widespread durability concerns reported. Overall, the VNL 760 is a dependable choice for long-haul trucking, provided regular maintenance is performed and any electrical anomalies are addressed promptly.
