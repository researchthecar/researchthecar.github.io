**Introduction to the 2019 Volvo VNL 760**

The 2019 Volvo VNL 760 is a heavy-duty truck offered in various engine and transmission configurations. The standard engine is a 13-liter inline-6 engine with 455 horsepower and 1,850 lb-ft of torque. Optional engine choices include a 13-liter inline-6 engine with 500 horsepower and 1,850 lb-ft of torque, a 16-liter inline-6 engine with 600 horsepower and 2,050 lb-ft of torque, and a 16-liter inline-6 engine with 700 horsepower and 2,300 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 10-speed Eaton Fuller manual transmission, a 13-speed Eaton Fuller automated manual transmission, and a 12-speed Volvo I-Shift automated manual transmission.

Overall, the Volvo VNL 760 is praised for its fuel efficiency, ride quality, and comfortable interior. However, it has also faced some issues with various components.

**Engine**

**13-Liter Inline-6 Engine (455 HP)**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, whistling noise from the engine compartment
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, reduced fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Replace the faulty injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**13-Liter Inline-6 Engine (500 HP)**

* **No Issues Found**

**16-Liter Inline-6 Engine (600 HP)**

* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**16-Liter Inline-6 Engine (700 HP)**

* **Crankshaft Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Metallic knocking sound from the engine, loss of oil pressure
    * Solution: Replace the crankshaft bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Eaton Fuller Manual Transmission**

* **Synchronizer Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise during shifting
    * Solution: Replace the worn synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**13-Speed Eaton Fuller Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete loss of shifting, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**12-Speed Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud bang from the front of the vehicle, reduced ride quality
    * Solution: Replace the broken leaf spring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Air Suspension Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven ride height
    * Solution: Repair or replace the leaking component
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start after sitting for an extended period
    * Solution: Diagnose and resolve electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, flickering electrical components
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Frame:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Surface rust or perforation of the frame
    * Solution: Treat and prevent rust
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges or LCD display not working properly
    * Solution: Replace the instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No airflow or insufficient cooling or heating
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the HVAC system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Volvo VNL 760 is generally a reliable truck, it has faced some issues with various components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The severity of these issues varies from minor inconveniences to major repairs, with the estimated cost to fix ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. It's important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and have them addressed promptly to avoid further damage or inconvenience.
