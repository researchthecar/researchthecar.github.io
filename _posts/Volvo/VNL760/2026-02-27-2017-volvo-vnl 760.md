### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Volvo VNL 760 demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, consistent with Volvo's reputation in the heavy-duty truck market. Major powertrain components (engines and transmissions) show no widespread reported issues, indicating robust engineering and manufacturing. Areas that may require attention over time include the rear air suspension, particularly for air leaks, and potential electrical issues related to wiring harness integrity. The HVAC system's compressor is a component that may eventually require replacement. Overall, the VNL 760 is a dependable choice for long-haul operations, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
