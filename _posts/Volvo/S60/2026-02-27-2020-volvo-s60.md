# 2020 Volvo S60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Volvo S60 delivers European luxury, striking styling, and a refined driving dynamic. As a used vehicle purchase, it generally presents a comfortable ownership experience, though buyers should familiarize themselves with its powertrain choices and monitor minor electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (T5)

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It offers a balanced blend of everyday efficiency and responsive power for daily driving.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Inline-4 (T6)

This dual-charged 2.0-liter engine generates approximately 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration across the rev range but introduces added mechanical complexity.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Plug-In Hybrid Inline-4 (T8)

This high-output plug-in hybrid powertrain combines forced induction with an electric motor to deliver around 400 horsepower. It offers strong performance and electric-only capability with increased system sophistication.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with all powertrain configurations to deliver smooth, prompt gear shifts. It generally operates reliably when routine fluid maintenance is observed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V144000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Automatic Emergency Braking](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software/hardware incompatibility, the Automatic Emergency Brake (AEB) system may not detect obstacles and engage as intended. If the automatic emergency braking system does not engage as intended, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will update the Active Safety Domain Master (ASDM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V414000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the low-pressure fuel pump can cause a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V780000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may not be secured properly, which can cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that does not deploy properly in a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the bolts securing the passenger air bag, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Volvo S60 is a stylish and sophisticated choice for used luxury sedan buyers. Prospective owners should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and powertrain operation to ensure a trouble-free experience.
