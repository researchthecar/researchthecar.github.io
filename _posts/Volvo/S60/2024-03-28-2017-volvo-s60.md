The 2017 Volvo S60 is a luxury sedan that offers a blend of style, performance, and safety. It is powered by a range of engine options, including the T5, T6, and T8, and comes with either a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission. The S60 has received praise for its comfortable interior, responsive handling, and advanced safety features. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**
* **T5 Drive-E**
    * **Intake manifold cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks around the intake manifold, rough idling, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **T6**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or grinding noise from the engine, reduced engine power, loss of synchronization between the camshaft and crankshaft
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **T8**
    * **Hybrid battery degradation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced battery capacity, decreased fuel efficiency, loss of electric power
        * Solution: Replace hybrid battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **Six-speed manual**
    * **Synchronizer wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises when shifting
        * Solution: Replace synchronizers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Eight-speed automatic**
    * **Transmission control module (TCM) failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, loss of gears, limp mode
        * Solution: Replace TCM
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front suspension struts**
    * **Premature wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, vibration, and harsh ride, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace struts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per strut
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension springs**
    * **Sagging**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride height, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per spring
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Alternator**
    * **Premature failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system problems, battery not charging properly
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery**
    * **Premature discharge**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, reduced electrical performance
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Headlights**
    * **Condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Foggy or moist headlights
        * Solution: Replace headlight housing
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per headlight
        * Recalls: NHTSA 19V-137

**Interior**
* **Climate control panel**
    * **Buttons not working**
        * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on the climate control panel are unresponsive
        * Solution: Replace climate control panel
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument cluster**
    * **Display malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gauges or information display not functioning properly
        * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2017 Volvo S60 is a capable luxury sedan with some reported issues. While some issues, such as interior component failures, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as engine and transmission problems, can be more severe and costly. It is important to weigh these potential issues against the vehicle's strengths and weaknesses before making a purchase decision.
