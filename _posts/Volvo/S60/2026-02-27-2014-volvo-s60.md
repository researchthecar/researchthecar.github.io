# 2014 Volvo S60

### Overview
The 2014 Volvo S60, known for its blend of Scandinavian design, safety, and performance, generally presents a solid reliability record. Volvo's commitment to robust engineering is evident across its powertrain and chassis. While specific issues are not widespread, like any vehicle, certain components may experience problems over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers of the 2014 S60, covering its various engine and transmission configurations.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L GTDi Turbocharged I4 (T5)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### 3.0L Turbocharged I6 (T6)
**PCV System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks, rough idle, engine misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) system components.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (AW TF-80SC)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, hesitation when shifting into drive or reverse.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, software update, or internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Volvo S60 is a reliable vehicle, particularly praised for its safety features and comfortable ride. While the powertrains are generally robust, owners of the turbocharged engines (both 4-cylinder and 6-cylinder) should be aware of potential issues with the turbocharger, PCV system, and timing chain, especially as mileage increases. Transmission issues, though not rampant, can manifest as harsh shifting. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are also noted. Overall, with diligent maintenance and attention to these specific potential problem areas, the 2014 S60 should provide dependable service.
