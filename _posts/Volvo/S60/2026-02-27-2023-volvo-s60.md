# 2023 Volvo S60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Volvo S60 delivers a sophisticated European sport sedan experience with sleek styling, a luxurious cabin, and electrified powertrain options. For used car shoppers, evaluating this model involves looking closely at how its advanced turbocharged engines and complex electrical architectures hold up over time. While modern Volvos offer tremendous appeal and safety credentials, checking service histories for any early software or electrical glitches remains a smart strategy.

---

## Engine

### B4 Mild Hybrid 2.0L Turbocharged I4

Features a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a mild-hybrid system, producing approximately 247 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It delivers smooth everyday power and improved efficiency for front-wheel-drive trims, representing a solid daily driver option.

### B5 Mild Hybrid 2.0L Turbocharged I4

Utilizes a stronger tune of the 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder mild-hybrid powertrain, pushing out around 295 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque with all-wheel drive. This setup offers brisk acceleration while maintaining composed road manners for enthusiastic drivers.

### T8 Recharge Plug-In Hybrid 2.0L Turbo/Supercharged I4

Combines a turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter engine with an electric motor to generate a robust 455 horsepower and 523 lb-ft of torque. It provides thrilling performance and all-electric driving capability, though buyers should verify the health of the hybrid battery system.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Geartronic Automatic Transmission

An eight-speed automatic transmission paired across the S60 lineup, delivering smooth shifts and well-optimized gear ratios. It generally proves dependable, though prospective buyers should test-shift through all gears to ensure seamless operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V012000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A diagnostic error in the Brake Control Module 2 (BCM2) may cause a loss of Antilock Brake System (ABS), Electronic Stability Control (ESC), and traction control. A reduction in brake support functions can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V188000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The false warning message could prevent the detection of an actual e-call system failure, which could delay an emergency response during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the telematics and connectivity antenna module (TCAM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V780000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may not be secured properly, which can cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that does not deploy properly in a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the bolts securing the passenger air bag, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V908000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2023)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Volvo S60 remains a compelling choice in the luxury sport sedan segment, combining striking Scandinavian design with strong performance and efficiency options. Because owner-reported data for this model year is still maturing, buyers should prioritize well-maintained examples that come with comprehensive service records and a remaining portion of the factory warranty.
