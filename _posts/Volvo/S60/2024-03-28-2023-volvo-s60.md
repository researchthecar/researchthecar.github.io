**Introduction to 2023 Volvo S60**

The 2023 Volvo S60 is a luxury sedan renowned for its sleek design, advanced safety features, and refined cabin. Available engine options include a 250-horsepower 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four and a 400-horsepower 2.0-liter supercharged and turbocharged inline-four. Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. Owners generally praise the S60's handling, comfort, and premium interior, while some find its infotainment system slightly complex.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

    * **Check Engine Light Illumination**
        * Description/Symptoms: The check engine light illuminates, accompanied by a rough idle, reduced power, and poor fuel economy.
        * Solution: The issue is typically caused by a faulty oxygen sensor. Replacing the sensor resolves the problem.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Hesitation During Gear Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or slips when shifting gears, especially at low speeds.
        * Solution: The issue can be resolved with a software update or transmission fluid flush. In severe cases, the transmission may need to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains excessively, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: The issue is often caused by a faulty door lock actuator or trunk latch switch. Replacing the affected components resolves the problem.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system malfunctions, resulting in inconsistent temperature or fan speeds.
        * Solution: The issue can be fixed by updating the software or replacing the climate control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Volvo S60 has a relatively low number of reported issues, with most being centered around the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of the issues varies from minor inconveniences to more costly repairs, but the overall cost to fix is generally within a reasonable range. Owners should be aware of the potential issues and consider preventive maintenance to minimize the risk of costly repairs.
