**Introduction:**

The 2019 Volvo S60 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience and an array of advanced features. It is available in three engine and transmission configurations: a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 with an 8-speed automatic transmission, a 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged inline-4 with an 8-speed automatic transmission, and a 4-cylinder plug-in hybrid with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The S60 is praised for its comfortable interior, fuel efficiency, and safety features, while drawbacks include its limited rear seat space and relatively high price point.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the oil pan gasket, visible underneath the car.
Solution: Replace oil pan gasket and tighten bolts to specification.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged and Supercharged Inline-4**

* **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, or reduced power.
Solution: Replace high-pressure fuel pump.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Delays and Hesitations**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears.
Solution: Reprogram transmission control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noises from the front end while driving over bumps, reduced ride quality.
Solution: Replace front strut mounts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Infotainment screen freezes, glitches, or becomes unresponsive.
Solution: Update infotainment software or replace infotainment module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the car is off.
Solution: Inspect and repair electrical system components, replace battery if necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Arm Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Windshield wiper arms become loose or stop working.
Solution: Replace windshield wiper arms.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system malfunctioning, cabin temperature not reaching desired level.
Solution: Diagnose and repair HVAC system, replace components as necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Volvo S60 has some minor issues that may affect some vehicles. While major engine or transmission failures are uncommon, electrical issues and suspension problems have been reported. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively affordable to repair. It is important to note that not all S60 models will experience these issues, and regular maintenance can help prevent certain problems from occurring.
