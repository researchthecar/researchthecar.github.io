# 2024 Volvo S60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Volvo S60 delivers Scandinavian luxury, sophisticated styling, and a choice of efficient electrified powertrains. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern engineering and advanced safety technology. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, prospective buyers should always evaluate vehicle condition and maintenance history during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (B5)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 247 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, featuring mild-hybrid assistance. It offers a smooth power delivery and balanced efficiency for daily driving.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Plug-In Hybrid (T8 Recharge)

Pairing a turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter engine with an electric motor, the T8 Recharge generates a robust 455 horsepower. It delivers brisk acceleration alongside electric-only driving capability for short commutes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the front wheels or all four wheels depending on the configuration. It is designed to optimize fuel economy while maintaining responsive gear changes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V780000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may not be secured properly, which can cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that does not deploy properly in a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the bolts securing the passenger air bag, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.
