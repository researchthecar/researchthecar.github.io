# 2017 Volvo S60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Volvo S60 offers a refined European luxury sedan experience with strong safety credentials and comfortable driving dynamics. While generally well-regarded, prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback concerning specific mechanical and interior components to make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers competitive horsepower and torque for daily commuting. Routine maintenance is key to long-term reliability for these forced-induction powerplants.

**Unexpected Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light intermittently illuminates, and the vehicle may suddenly shut down while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic perform a diagnostic scan to identify fault codes related to fuel delivery, sensors, or ignition systems.

### 2.0L Supercharged and Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This dual-charged 2.0-liter engine combines a supercharger and turbocharger to provide robust power delivery across the rev range. Buyers should ensure complete service records are available when considering higher-mileage models.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with various drivetrain options to deliver smooth gear shifts and optimized fuel economy. Regular fluid inspections help maintain shifting quality over time.

## Exterior

### Door Latches

Interior components generally hold up well, though owner reports highlight occasional concerns regarding door latch reliability.

**Unexpected Door Opening or Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors may fail to latch securely when closed or can unexpectedly unlatch while the vehicle is in motion.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latch mechanisms and actuators for wear, and consult a mechanic or dealer to address faulty hardware.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V378000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The flexible steel cable that connects the seat belt to the front outboard seating positions may fatigue over time, causing the seat belt to not secure the occupant in the event of a crash. An unsecured occupant has an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat belt anchor cable for both front seats. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V849000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A door that is not securely latched could open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of injury to a vehicle occupant.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace all four door latches, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Volvo S60 combines compelling styling and safety features, but used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect service histories and verify proper operation of critical components like door latches and powertrain electronics during a pre-purchase test drive.
