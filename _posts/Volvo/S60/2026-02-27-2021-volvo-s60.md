# 2021 Volvo S60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Volvo S60, buyers will find a stylish and technologically advanced luxury sport sedan that generally provides a comfortable and secure ride. While owner experiences are mostly positive, reviewing specific areas such as the braking system, electrical accessories, and interior climate control can help you spot potential maintenance items before completing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine balances efficient daily driving with responsive power delivery. It is a common powerplant for this model year and typically offers reliable performance when properly maintained.

### High Voltage Coolant Heater

Electrical accessories and climate controls add modern convenience but require thorough testing to ensure all sensors and auxiliary heaters function correctly.

**Heater Failure and Defrost Issues**
- **Symptoms:** The high voltage coolant heater may fail to operate when running solely on battery power, preventing the climate control system from warming up and clearing the windshield.
- **Solution:** Test the vehicle's heating and defrost functions during battery-only operation, and replace the high voltage coolant heater assembly if faulty.

## Transmission

### Plug-In Hybrid Powertrain

The plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs a turbocharged engine with electric drive components to deliver enhanced performance and efficiency. Buyers should verify proper electrical and hybrid system operation during a test drive.

**Sudden Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the engine and vehicle mechanisms can suddenly shut off while driving, resulting in a complete loss of steering assist and braking functionality.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's powertrain control modules and electrical architecture inspected by a qualified professional to diagnose potential software or hardware failures.

### 8-Speed Automatic

The eight-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts suited to both city commuting and highway cruising. Regular fluid checks will help maintain its long-term reliability.

## Brakes

### Front Brakes and Rotors

The braking system provides strong stopping power, though front brake components should be closely evaluated for premature wear during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Premature Rotor Warping**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a severe vibration in the steering wheel and a pulsing sensation in the brake pedal during deceleration, often pointing to warped front rotors.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front brake rotors and pads for excessive wear or warping, and replace them with quality components if vibration persists.

## Interior

### Sunroof Drainage and Air Conditioning

The cabin is designed for comfort and luxury, but buyers should inspect the interior footwells for dampness and verify that climate control systems operate without leaks.

**Sunroof Leak and Evaporator Core Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Water may enter the cabin due to clogged sunroof drainage hoses, pooling under the front seats, while separate air conditioning issues can cause refrigerant leaks into the interior.
- **Solution:** Clear blocked sunroof drain tubes and inspect the evaporator core for refrigerant leaks, repairing or replacing affected seals and components as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V682000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V780000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may not be secured properly, which can cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that does not deploy properly in a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the bolts securing the passenger air bag, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Volvo S60 is an appealing luxury sedan offering refined performance, safety features, and upscale styling. While most examples provide a dependable driving experience, prospective buyers should carefully inspect the front brake rotors, test all hybrid powertrain and climate control functions, and check the cabin for any signs of water intrusion during a pre-purchase inspection.
