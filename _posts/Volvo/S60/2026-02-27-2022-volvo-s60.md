# 2022 Volvo S60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo S60 delivers Scandinavian luxury, sophisticated styling, and an array of advanced powertrain choices. For used car buyers, understanding how these engines and transmissions perform is key to finding a reliable daily driver. While overall owner feedback is balanced, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options carefully.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B4 / B5)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine serves as the core mild-hybrid powerplant, producing roughly 247 horsepower in B5 form. It offers smooth power delivery and competitive efficiency for daily driving.

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged Plug-in Hybrid (T8 Recharge)

Combining a turbo- and supercharged four-cylinder with an electric motor, the T8 Recharge puts out a robust 455 total system horsepower. It provides thrilling acceleration alongside electric-only driving range.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed Geartronic automatic transmission is paired across the S60 lineup, offering smooth gear shifts and refined highway cruising capability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V682000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V780000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may not be secured properly, which can cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that does not deploy properly in a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the bolts securing the passenger air bag, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=S60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
