# 2015 Volvo S60

### Overview
The 2015 Volvo S60 is a compact luxury sedan known for its safety features and comfortable ride. Reliability data for this model year generally indicates a solid performance, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for the various engine and transmission configurations offered. Owners can use this information to anticipate and address potential repairs, ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of their S60.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged (T5)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**PCV System Clog**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, engine misfires.
- **Solution:** PCV system cleaning or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 2.5L Turbocharged (T5)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light, misfires, rough idling, poor acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 3.0L Turbocharged (T6)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (AW TF-80SC)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable clunks or jolts when shifting gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or transmission repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $300 - $2,500

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dimming lights, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or infotainment unit repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Volvo S60 demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly for its luxury segment. The most commonly reported technical issues tend to be related to the turbocharged engines, specifically the 2.0L and 3.0L variants, with turbocharger and coolant leak concerns appearing. The 6-speed automatic transmission has seen some reports of harsh shifting. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain and infotainment glitches, are also noted. However, many components, including the suspension, exterior, and interior, are generally robust. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to early symptoms can significantly mitigate potential repair costs and ensure a dependable ownership experience.
