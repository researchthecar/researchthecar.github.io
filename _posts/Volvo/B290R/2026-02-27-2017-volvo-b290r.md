## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, loss of ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air springs or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 per corner

**ECAS Control Module Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height, warning lights on the dashboard, inability to adjust suspension.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the ECAS control module.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Electrical

### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion in Connectors**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning lights or accessories, dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Cleaning and sealing of corroded connectors, or replacement of affected harness sections.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900
