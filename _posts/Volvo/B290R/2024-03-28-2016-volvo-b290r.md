**Introduction to the 2016 Volvo B290R**

The 2016 Volvo B290R is a mid-size luxury SUV available with a 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. People like the B290R for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have experienced issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Symptoms include loss of power, excessive smoke, and decreased fuel economy. Solution: Replace turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200+ Recalls: None Found.
* **Intake Manifold Leak:** Symptoms include rough idle, poor acceleration, and increased emissions. Solution: Replace intake manifold. Estimated Cost to Fix: $600+. Recalls: None Found.

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shuddering or Hesitation During Shifts:** Symptoms include a vibration or hesitation when the transmission shifts. Solution: Replace or reprogram transmission control unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000. Recalls: None Found.

**Suspension**

* **Excessive Road Noise:** Symptoms include a loud humming or rumbling noise while driving. Solution: Replace or adjust suspension components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+. Recalls: None Found.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle or the battery not holding a charge. Solution: Replace battery or diagnose and fix electrical system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200+. Recalls: None Found.
* **Electrical System Malfunction:** Symptoms may include flickering lights, dashboard warnings, or power loss. Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system issue. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300+. Recalls: None Found.

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Symptoms include inconsistent temperature control or failure of the system to operate. Solution: Replace climate control unit or diagnose and repair electrical system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+. Recalls: None Found.

**Summary**

The 2016 Volvo B290R has been reported to have potential issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to more serious problems that could affect the safety or reliability of the vehicle. The estimated cost to fix these issues can vary significantly depending on the specific problem and the extent of the repairs required.
