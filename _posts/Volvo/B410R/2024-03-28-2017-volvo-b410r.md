**Introduction**

The 2017 Volvo B410R is a heavy-duty coach bus manufactured by Volvo Buses. It is powered by a D11K engine and comes standard with an I-Shift 12-speed automated manual transmission. The D11K engine produces 410 horsepower and 1,650 lb-ft of torque. The B410R is known for its reliability, comfort, and fuel efficiency. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of common issues.

**Engine**

* **D11K**

**EGR Cooler Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, loss of power, increased emissions
Solution: Replace EGR cooler
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission**

**Shifting Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
Solution: Replace shift actuator or transmission control module (TCM)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Clutch Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
Solution: Replace clutch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**

**Strut Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noises while turning or braking, loss of stability
Solution: Replace struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension**

**Airbag Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Suspension sagging, uneven ride height
Solution: Replace airbags
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, loss of power
Solution: Replace battery
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery draining
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlights
Solution: Replace headlights
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Mirrors Shaking**
Description/Symptoms: Mirrors vibrating while driving
Solution: Adjust or replace mirrors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracks**
Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard panel
Solution: Replace dashboard panel
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: AC or heat not working properly
Solution: Replace or repair climate control system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Volvo B410R is a generally reliable coach bus, but it does have some common issues. The most severe issues relate to engine and transmission failures, which can be expensive to fix. Electrical and suspension issues are also relatively common. Interior issues are mostly minor, such as dashboard panel cracks and climate control malfunctions. Overall, the B410R is a good choice for long-distance travel, but potential buyers should be aware of its potential issues.
