**Introduction:**

The 2018 Volvo S60 Cross Country is a compact luxury crossover that combines rugged off-road capabilities with refined urban styling. It features a turbocharged 2.0L inline-four engine paired with an 8-speed Geartronic automatic transmission. The S60 Cross Country is known for its comfortable interior, advanced safety features, and sporty driving dynamics. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-Four**

* **Engine Oil Consumption:** **High oil consumption**, leading to the need for frequent oil top-ups or even engine damage.
* **Solution:** Piston ring replacement or engine rebuild.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $5,000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign ID 22V242

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Geartronic Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:** **Rough or delayed shifting, slipping gears, or shuddering**.
* **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush, software update, or transmission replacement.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** **Excessive battery drain**, leading to dead batteries.
* **Solution:** Battery replacement, alternator check, or software update.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:** **Wiper blades stop working or operate intermittently**.
* **Solution:** Wiper motor replacement.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:** **Freezing, lagging, or unresponsive touchscreens**.
* **Solution:** Firmware update, software reset, or hardware replacement.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Volvo S60 Cross Country has a few notable issues, including engine oil consumption, transmission shifting problems, and electrical malfunctions. The severity of these issues can vary, but some can be costly to repair. However, it's important to note that these issues are not universal, and many S60 Cross Country owners have not experienced any major problems.
