### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Volvo S60 Cross Country is a generally reliable vehicle with a strong mechanical foundation. The powertrain, including both engine and transmission options, shows minimal widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are within the electrical system, specifically related to the infotainment unit and potential battery drain issues, which can be costly to diagnose and repair. Exterior and interior components are typically robust. Overall, the S60 Cross Country offers a dependable ownership experience, provided potential electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
