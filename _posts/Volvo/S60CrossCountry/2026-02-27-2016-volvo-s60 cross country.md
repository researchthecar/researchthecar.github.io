# 2016 Volvo S60 Cross Country

### Overview
The 2016 Volvo S60 Cross Country is a unique offering, blending the practicality of an all-wheel-drive wagon with the ruggedness of an SUV. Built on the S60 sedan platform, it features increased ground clearance and protective cladding. Reliability for this model year is generally considered good, reflecting Volvo's reputation for safety and durability. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L T5 Turbocharged I4 (Gasoline)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### 2.5L T5 Turbocharged I5 (Gasoline)
**PCV System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, engine misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) system components.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light, rough running, misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per coil


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Geartronic Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Shifting problems, slipping gears, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, lines, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting patterns, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Software update or recalibration by a dealer.
- **Cost:** $100 - $200

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Volvo S60 Cross Country demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its chassis and core structural components. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the turbocharged engines, specifically potential turbocharger issues and coolant leaks, and the automatic transmission, where fluid leaks or software glitches can occur. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system quirks and battery drain, are also noted but are not typically widespread. Overall, with proactive maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly, the S60 Cross Country should provide a dependable and comfortable ownership experience, especially given its unique all-wheel-drive capabilities and elevated stance.
