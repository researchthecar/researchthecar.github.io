**Introduction to the 2017 Volvo S60 Cross Country**

The Volvo S60 Cross Country is a premium compact luxury sedan with added off-road capabilities. It features a 2.0L 4-cylinder turbocharged engine that produces 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The S60 Cross Country has received praise for its smooth ride, luxurious interior, and advanced safety features. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and relatively small cargo space.

**Engine**

* **2.0L 4-cylinder Turbocharged**

    * **Engine Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, low oil levels on dipstick, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace piston rings or rebuild engine
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise from engine, loss of power, engine stalls
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

    * **Shift Solenoid Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, slipping gears, transmission lurches
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoids or transmission control module (TCM)
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the car is parked
    * Solution: Replace faulty component (e.g., alternator, starter, battery)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water or condensation inside headlight assembly
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Buttons Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel buttons (e.g., volume, cruise control) don't work properly
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system doesn't work properly, AC or heater doesn't function
    * Solution: Reset climate control system, replace AC compressor or heater core as needed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Volvo S60 Cross Country has had several reported issues, including engine oil consumption, transmission shift solenoid issues, battery drain, headlight condensation, and interior component malfunctions. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to major repairs. The estimated cost to fix these issues can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. It's important to note that not all S60 Cross Country vehicles will experience these issues, but it's worth being aware of them before purchasing or owning one.
