## 2017 Volvo 9700 Grand

The 2017 Volvo 9700 Grand is a luxury coach bus manufactured by Volvo Buses. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 13-liter Volvo D13 engine with 460 horsepower and 2,250 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 12-speed I-Shift automated manual transmission. The 9700 Grand offers a comfortable and spacious interior, with seating for up to 55 passengers. It is also equipped with a variety of safety features, including electronic stability control, lane departure warning, and adaptive cruise control.

Drivers and passengers alike appreciate the 9700 Grand's comfortable ride and quiet interior. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle, particularly with the engine and transmission.

**Engine**

* **13-liter Volvo D13 Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of power and acceleration. Symptoms include black smoke coming from the exhaust, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated cost to fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * **EGR Valve Failure:** The EGR valve can fail, causing the engine to run rough and emit excessive emissions. Symptoms include a decrease in engine performance, increased fuel consumption, and emissions. The solution is to replace the EGR valve. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:** The high-pressure fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to stall or refuse to start. Symptoms include a lack of power, rough idling, and difficulty starting the engine. The solution is to replace the high-pressure fuel pump. Estimated cost to fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

* **12-speed I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, causing a rough ride and decreased performance. Symptoms include delayed gear changes, slipping gears, and grinding noises. The solution is to reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission. Estimated cost to fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** The battery can discharge prematurely, causing the vehicle to lose power. Symptoms include a flickering dashboard, difficulty starting the engine, and loss of electrical accessories. The solution is to replace the battery or identify and repair any electrical drains. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $300+
* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator can fail, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to lose power. Symptoms include dimming headlights, flickering dashboard lights, and difficulty starting the engine. The solution is to replace the alternator. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** The headlights may experience condensation buildup, which can block the light output and reduce visibility. The solution is to replace the headlight assemblies. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Malfunction:** The HVAC system may malfunction, causing insufficient heating or cooling. Symptoms include a lack of airflow, uneven temperature distribution, and excessive noise. The solution is to diagnose and repair the HVAC system, which may involve replacing faulty components. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Seatbelt Pretensioner Failure:** The seatbelt pretensioners may fail to deploy in the event of a collision, increasing the risk of injury to occupants. The solution is to replace the seatbelt pretensioners. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $600+ per seatbelt

**Summary**

The 2017 Volvo 9700 Grand is a comfortable and reliable coach bus, but it has experienced some issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. While some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important for owners and operators of the 9700 Grand to be aware of these potential issues and to address them promptly to ensure the safety and reliability of the vehicle.
