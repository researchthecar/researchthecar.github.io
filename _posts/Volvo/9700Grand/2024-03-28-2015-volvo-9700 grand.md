**Introduction:**

The 2015 Volvo 9700 Grand is a luxury coach bus powered by a D13C engine paired with a 12-speed I-Shift transmission. Known for its spacious and comfortable interior, it offers a smooth ride and ample amenities. However, owners have encountered various issues with the vehicle, ranging from minor annoyances to more serious problems.

**Engine**

* **D13C Engine:**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Symptoms include rough idling, loss of power, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V696000
* **Turbocharger Boost Control Valve Malfunction:** Symptoms include reduced engine performance, increased exhaust smoke, and fault codes related to the boost control system.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the boost control valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **I-Shift 12-speed Transmission:**
    * **Gearbox Synchronization Issues:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises, and transmission fault codes.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the gearbox synchronizers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leaks:** Symptoms include sagging suspension, uneven vehicle height, and air compressor noises.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the air suspension components, such as air bags, lines, or valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock Absorber Failure:** Symptoms include excessive body roll, bouncing, and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per shock absorber
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include electrical system malfunctions, dim headlights, and a warning light on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge:** Symptoms include inability to start the vehicle, electrical system problems, and dead batteries.
    * Solution: Test and replace the battery and/or investigate electrical system for any parasitic draws or leaks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+ for a battery replacement
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wipers Malfunction:** Symptoms include intermittent wiper operation, slow wiper speeds, or wipers that fail to operate.
    * Solution: Replace the wiper motor or wiper transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Bulbs Burning Out Prematurely:** Symptoms include dim or flickering headlights that require frequent replacement.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs with higher quality bulbs or investigate the electrical system for any issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior (excluding leather wear)**

* **Dashboard Switch Failures:** Symptoms include non-functional switches for controls such as lights, climate, or audio.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per switch
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control System Malfunctions:** Symptoms include ineffective temperature control, fan noise, or faulty sensors.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system components, such as the compressor, fan, or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Retractors Malfunctioning:** Symptoms include seat belts that fail to retract properly or become jammed.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the seat belt retractors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per seat belt
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Volvo 9700 Grand has encountered various issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and suspension leaks. While some issues are relatively minor, others can be more serious and expensive to fix. It's important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to address them promptly to ensure the safety and reliability of their vehicle.
