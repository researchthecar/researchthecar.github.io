**Introduction**

The 2017 Volvo VNR 300 is a heavy-duty truck that has a 12.8-liter diesel engine and a standard 12-speed automated manual transmission. It has 425 horsepower and 1,550 lb-ft of torque. The VNR 300 comes standard with a variety of features, including lane departure warning, automatic emergency braking, and adaptive cruise control.

Owners have praised the VNR 300 for its fuel efficiency, reliability, and spacious cab. However, some have reported issues with the engine, transmission, and exterior of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**12.8-liter Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of power and increased exhaust smoke. The turbocharger must be replaced, which costs $1,500 - $2,500+.
* **Exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system failure:** The EGR system can fail, causing increased emissions and reduced engine performance. The EGR valve must be replaced, which costs $500 - $1,000+.
* **Fuel pump failure:** The fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to stall. The fuel pump must be replaced, which costs $1,000 - $1,500+.

**Transmission**

**12-speed Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch failure:** The clutch can wear out prematurely, causing slipping and difficulty shifting gears. The clutch must be replaced, which costs $1,500 - $2,500+.
* **Transmission control module (TCM) failure:** The TCM can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not at all. The TCM must be replaced, which costs $1,000 - $1,500+.
* **Driveline failure:** The driveline can fail, causing a loss of power to the wheels. The driveline must be replaced, which costs $2,000 - $3,000+.

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

* **Leaf spring failure:** The leaf springs can break, causing the front of the truck to sag. The leaf springs must be replaced, which costs $500 - $1,000+.
* **Shock absorber failure:** The shock absorbers can fail, causing a rough ride and reduced handling. The shock absorbers must be replaced, which costs $200 - $400+.

**Rear Suspension**

* **Air bag failure:** The air bags can fail, causing the rear of the truck to sag. The air bags must be replaced, which costs $500 - $1,000+.
* **Suspension control module (SCM) failure:** The SCM can fail, causing the suspension to malfunction. The SCM must be replaced, which costs $1,000 - $1,500+.

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure:** The battery can fail, preventing the truck from starting. The battery must be replaced, which costs $200 - $400+.
* **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, preventing the battery from charging. The alternator must be replaced, which costs $500 - $1,000+.
* **Wiring harness failure:** The wiring harness can fail, causing electrical problems throughout the truck. The wiring harness must be repaired or replaced, which costs $500 - $1,500+.

**Exterior**

* **Headlight failure:** The headlights can fail, reducing visibility at night. The headlights must be replaced, which costs $200 - $400+.
* **Tail light failure:** The tail lights can fail, reducing visibility at night. The tail lights must be replaced, which costs $100 - $200+.
* **Mirror failure:** The mirrors can fail, reducing visibility around the truck. The mirrors must be replaced, which costs $200 - $400+.

**Interior**

* **Dashboard failure:** The dashboard can fail, causing the gauges and controls to malfunction. The dashboard must be replaced, which costs $1,000 - $1,500+.
* **HVAC system failure:** The HVAC system can fail, causing the truck to overheat or become too cold. The HVAC system must be repaired or replaced, which costs $500 - $1,000+.
* **Seat failure:** The seat can fail, causing discomfort and reduced visibility. The seat must be replaced, which costs $500 - $1,000+.

**Summary**

The 2017 Volvo VNR 300 is a reliable and fuel-efficient truck, but it has some potential issues with the engine, transmission, and exterior. The severity of these issues can range from minor to major, and the cost to fix them can be significant.

Overall, the VNR 300 is a good choice for businesses that need a reliable and fuel-efficient truck. However, potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues that the truck can have before making a purchase decision.
