## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, loss of ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per spring

**Compressor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Failure to maintain ride height, 'check suspension' warning light, compressor running constantly or not at all.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic behavior of lights, wipers, door locks, or dashboard indicators. Can be difficult to diagnose due to intermittent nature.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500


### Wiring Harness Issues
**Corrosion or Abrasion**
- **Symptoms:** Chafed wires leading to short circuits, intermittent power loss to components, or warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection, repair, or replacement of affected sections of the wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Volvo B8RLE demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components like the D8K engine and I-Shift transmission, for which no widespread issues are reported.
