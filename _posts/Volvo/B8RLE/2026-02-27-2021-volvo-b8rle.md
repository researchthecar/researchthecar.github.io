## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, audible hissing from suspension components, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per corner

**Ride Height Control Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height, vehicle too high or too low, warning lights related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the ride height control valve.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Random warning lights, non-functional accessories (lights, wipers), communication errors with other modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Wiring Harness Issues
**Corrosion or Abrasion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent power loss to components, short circuits, corrosion on connectors.
- **Solution:** Inspection, repair, or replacement of damaged sections of the wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Volvo B8RLE chassis generally exhibits a high level of reliability, consistent with Volvo's reputation in the commercial vehicle sector. The D8K engine and I-Shift transmission are well-regarded for their durability and performance.
