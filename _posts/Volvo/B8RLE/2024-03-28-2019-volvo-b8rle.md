**Introduction**

The 2019 Volvo B8RLE is a heavy-duty bus manufactured by Volvo Buses. It is powered by a Volvo D8K engine and an Allison B500R automatic transmission. The bus has a seating capacity of up to 65 passengers and is available in a variety of configurations. Some of the features that people like about the Volvo B8RLE include its comfortable ride, fuel efficiency, and safety features. Some of the dislikes include its high price and limited engine options.

**Engine**

**Volvo D8K 320**

- **Engine Overheating:** The D8K 320 engine can overheat due to a faulty thermostat or radiator. Symptoms include high engine temperatures and white smoke from the exhaust. The solution is to replace the faulty component. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $500 - $1500.
- **Turbocharger Failure:** The D8K 320 engine can experience turbocharger failure due to a faulty wastegate or oil seal. Symptoms include loss of power and black smoke from the exhaust. The solution is to replace the faulty turbocharger. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $2000 - $3000.
- **Fuel Injector Failure:** The D8K 320 engine can experience fuel injector failure due to a faulty solenoid or nozzle. Symptoms include poor engine performance and black smoke from the exhaust. The solution is to replace the faulty fuel injectors. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $500 - $1000 per injector.

**Transmission**

**Allison B500R Automatic**

- **Transmission Slipping:** The B500R transmission can slip due to worn clutches or a faulty valve body. Symptoms include loss of power and erratic shifting. The solution is to repair or replace the faulty components. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $1500 - $2500.
- **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** The B500R transmission can leak fluid due to a faulty seal or gasket. Symptoms include low transmission fluid levels and a burnt smell. The solution is to replace the faulty seal or gasket. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200 - $500.
- **Shift Solenoid Failure:** The B500R transmission can experience shift solenoid failure due to a faulty coil or electrical connection. Symptoms include hard shifting and erratic transmission behavior. The solution is to replace the faulty shift solenoid. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200 - $500.

**Suspension**

- **Leaf Spring Breakage:** The leaf springs can break due to excessive weight or road damage. Symptoms include a sagging suspension and a rough ride. The solution is to replace the broken leaf springs. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $500 - $1000 per leaf spring.
- **Shock Absorber Failure:** The shock absorbers can fail due to worn seals or a faulty piston. Symptoms include a bouncy ride and poor handling. The solution is to replace the faulty shock absorbers. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200 - $500 per shock absorber.
- **Air Bag Leaks:** The air bags can leak due to a faulty seal or a puncture. Symptoms include a sagging suspension and a loss of ride height. The solution is to replace the faulty air bag. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $500 - $1000 per air bag.

**Electrical**

- **Battery Discharge:** The battery can discharge due to a faulty alternator or a parasitic drain. Symptoms include a dead battery and difficulty starting the vehicle. The solution is to replace the faulty alternator or fix the parasitic drain. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $500 - $1500.
- **Wiring Harness Damage:** The wiring harness can be damaged due to road debris or rodents. Symptoms include electrical problems such as flickering lights or inoperative components. The solution is to repair or replace the damaged wiring harness. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200 - $1000.
- **Computer Module Failure:** The computer module can fail due to a faulty circuit board or a software glitch. Symptoms include various electrical problems and engine performance issues. The solution is to replace the faulty computer module. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $1000 - $2000.

**Exterior**

- **Headlight Bulbs Burning Out:** The headlight bulbs can burn out prematurely due to a faulty bulb or a problem with the electrical system. Symptoms include dim or non-functioning headlights. The solution is to replace the faulty headlight bulbs. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $50 - $100 per bulb.
- **Taillight Lenses Cracking:** The taillight lenses can crack due to exposure to sunlight or road debris. Symptoms include cracked or broken taillights. The solution is to replace the cracked or broken taillight lenses. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $100 - $200 per lens.
- **Body Panel Rust:** The body panels can rust due to exposure to moisture and salt. Symptoms include rust spots or holes in the body panels. The solution is to repair or replace the rusted body panels. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $500 - $2000 per panel.

**Interior**

- **Seat Track Failure:** The seat tracks can fail due to worn gears or a faulty motor. Symptoms include difficulty adjusting the seat or the seat moving unexpectedly. The solution is to repair or replace the faulty seat tracks. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200 - $500 per seat.
- **HVAC System Malfunction:** The HVAC system can malfunction due to a faulty compressor or a clogged condenser. Symptoms include poor heating or cooling performance. The solution is to repair or replace the faulty components. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $500 - $1500.
- **Door Handle Breakage:** The door handles can break due to excessive force or wear. Symptoms include difficulty opening or closing the doors. The solution is to replace the broken door handles. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $100 - $200 per handle.
