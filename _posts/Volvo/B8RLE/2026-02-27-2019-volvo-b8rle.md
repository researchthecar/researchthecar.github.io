## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per spring

**Ride Height Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height, dashboard warning lights related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensor or recalibration.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Random malfunctions of lights, wipers, door locks, or dashboard indicators.
- **Solution:** Software diagnostics, module reset, or replacement of BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion or Abrasion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent power loss to components, short circuits, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection, repair of damaged sections, or replacement of harness.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200
