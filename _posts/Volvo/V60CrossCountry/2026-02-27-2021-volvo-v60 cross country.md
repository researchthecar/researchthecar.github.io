# 2021 Volvo V60 Cross Country

### Overview
The 2021 Volvo V60 Cross Country is a premium wagon that blends Scandinavian design with robust capability. It is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, benefiting from Volvo's commitment to safety and quality engineering. The V60 Cross Country typically comes with a turbocharged four-cylinder engine, paired with an all-wheel-drive system and an automatic transmission, designed to handle various road conditions. Owners often praise its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and advanced technology features. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the V60 Cross Country demonstrates a strong track record for dependability in its class, with most reported concerns being minor or related to typical usage rather than widespread manufacturing defects.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Volvo V60 Cross Country is a solid choice for those seeking a blend of luxury, practicality, and all-weather capability. Reliability is generally good, with the powertrain and chassis demonstrating robust performance. The most commonly cited issues, though infrequent, tend to be related to the infotainment system's software and, in some cases, premature battery failure. These are not typically indicative of systemic design flaws but rather common electronic or component wear points. Overall, the V60 Cross Country offers a dependable ownership experience, especially when routine maintenance is followed.
