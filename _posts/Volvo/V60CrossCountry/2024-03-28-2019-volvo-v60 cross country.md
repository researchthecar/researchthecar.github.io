**Introduction:**

The 2019 Volvo V60 Cross Country is a midsize luxury station wagon that combines versatile cargo space with off-road capability. It offers a choice of two engine options: a 250-horsepower 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four and a 400-horsepower 2.0-liter turbocharged and supercharged inline-four. Transmission options include an eight-speed automatic for the base engine and an eight-speed Geartronic automatic for the performance engine. Owners praise the V60 Cross Country for its refined interior, spacious cargo area, and impressive safety features. However, it has received some criticism for its firm ride quality and relatively high price.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or warping of the intake manifold can cause vacuum leaks, leading to rough idling, reduced power, and poor fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the turbocharger can cause a loss of boost pressure, resulting in reduced power and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Inline-Four**

* **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction of the high-pressure fuel pump can cause difficulty starting, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may exhibit rough shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears.
    * Solution: Software updates, replacement of the valve body or transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Eight-Speed Geartronic Automatic**

* No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Axle Differential Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear axle differential may fail, causing noises, vibrations, and reduced handling stability.
    * Solution: Replacement of the rear axle differential.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty electrical harnesses can lead to intermittent electrical problems, including malfunctions of lights, gauges, and other components.
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of the affected electrical harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight assemblies can impair visibility.
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, reboot unexpectedly, or display incorrect information.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Insufficient cooling or heating, blower malfunctions, or abnormal noises from the climate control system.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of affected components, such as the compressor, blower motor, or climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Volvo V60 Cross Country has a number of reported issues, with the most severe being engine and transmission failures. These issues can be costly to repair and may impact the vehicle's reliability. However, it's important to note that not all V60 Cross Country vehicles experience these problems, and many owners are satisfied with their vehicles.
