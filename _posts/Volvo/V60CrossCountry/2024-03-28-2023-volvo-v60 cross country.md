The 2023 Volvo V60 Cross Country is a versatile luxury wagon that delivers a blend of style, performance, and practicality. It offers a choice of powertrains, including a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 (T5) producing 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, and a plug-in hybrid (T8) with a combined output of 455 horsepower and 523 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **T5 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rattling noises from the engine, illuminated check engine light.
        * Solution: Replacing the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $900 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, hesitation during acceleration, illuminated check engine light.
        * Solution: Cleaning the intake valves and decarbonizing the engine.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **T8 Plug-in Hybrid**
    * **Hybrid Battery Degradation:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced electric range, diminished engine power, battery warning lights.
        * Solution: Replacing the hybrid battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 23V864000

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, loss of gears, illuminated check engine light.
        * Solution: Replacing the TCM.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Wiring Harness Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical faults, flickering lights, loss of power.
    * Solution: Inspecting and replacing the corroded wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment Screen Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow response time, freezing, screen glitching.
    * Solution: Updating the infotainment software or replacing the screen.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2023 Volvo V60 Cross Country offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury, it is not without its fair share of issues. Engine problems, particularly with the T5 engine, can be costly to repair, and hybrid battery degradation can be a major concern for T8 owners, potentially requiring a significant investment. Electrical wiring harness corrosion can lead to various electrical faults, while the infotainment system may experience lag or freezing. Overall, the severity of these issues varies, and the estimated cost to fix ranges from a few hundred dollars to upwards of $10,000.
