# 2022 Volvo V60 Cross Country

### Overview
The 2022 Volvo V60 Cross Country is a premium wagon that blends Scandinavian design with robust capability. It is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, benefiting from Volvo's commitment to safety and quality engineering. The V60 Cross Country typically comes with a turbocharged four-cylinder engine, paired with an all-wheel-drive system and an automatic transmission, designed to offer a comfortable and capable driving experience. Owners often praise its refined interior, advanced technology, and practical cargo space, making it a strong contender in its segment. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the V60 Cross Country demonstrates a solid track record for dependability, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific component failures rather than widespread systemic problems.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Volvo V60 Cross Country presents itself as a reliable option within the premium wagon segment. Its powertrain, suspension, and core structural components have shown good durability, with no significant widespread issues reported. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in rarer cases, premature battery failure. These are generally manageable with software updates or component replacement. Overall, the V60 Cross Country offers a dependable ownership experience, aligning with Volvo's reputation for building safe and enduring vehicles.
