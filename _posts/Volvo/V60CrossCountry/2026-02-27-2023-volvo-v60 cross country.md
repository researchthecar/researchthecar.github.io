# 2023 Volvo V60 Cross Country

### Overview
The 2023 Volvo V60 Cross Country is a premium wagon that blends Scandinavian design with robust capability. It is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, benefiting from Volvo's commitment to safety and quality engineering. The V60 Cross Country typically features a turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an all-wheel-drive system, offering a comfortable and refined driving experience suitable for both daily commutes and light off-road adventures. Owners often praise its spacious interior, advanced technology, and comfortable ride. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the V60 Cross Country has demonstrated a solid track record for dependability, with most reported concerns being minor or related to typical maintenance rather than widespread design flaws.
