**Introduction**

The 1998 Volvo VN is a heavy-duty truck known for its durability, fuel efficiency, and comfort. It comes equipped with a Cummins ISX engine and a 10-speed Eaton Fuller transmission. This combination provides 475 horsepower and 1,650 lb-ft of torque. Owners appreciate the truck's reliability, spacious cabin, and strong performance, but they also report some common issues.

**Engine**

**Cummins ISX**

**Cracked EGR Cooler**
Description/Symptoms: Exhaust gas leaks, reduced engine performance, increased emissions
Solution: Replace EGR cooler
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust, loud whistling sound
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eaton Fuller 10-speed**

**Difficulty Shifting Gears**
Description/Symptoms: Gears grind or refuse to engage
Solution: Adjust or replace shift linkage
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from transmission pan or seals
Solution: Replace seals or gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Rear Axle Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Abnormal tire wear, wobbling or vibration
Solution: Replace axle or bearings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering headlights, electrical system malfunctions
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Starter Motor Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Engine fails to start or cranks slowly
Solution: Replace starter motor or solenoid
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rust on Frame and Body**
Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust on metal components
Solution: Clean and repaint affected areas
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard while driving
Solution: Tighten or replace loose components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Seat Adjustments Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Seat does not adjust properly or becomes stuck
Solution: Repair or replace seat controls
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1998 Volvo VN is a reliable and powerful truck, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners include cracked EGR coolers, turbocharger failures, difficulty shifting gears, suspension wear, alternator failures, and interior squeaks and rattles. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can be addressed with proper maintenance and repairs.
