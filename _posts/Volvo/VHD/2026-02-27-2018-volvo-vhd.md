# 2018 Volvo VHD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Volvo VHD is a heavy-duty vocational truck designed for demanding work environments such as construction and municipal service. Buyers considering a used model should evaluate both its powertrain capability and the overall condition of its chassis and auxiliary systems. While heavy-duty commercial trucks generally experience rigorous use, inspecting individual maintenance histories remains a critical step for prospective purchasers.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 10.8L Turbo-Diesel Inline-6

The Volvo D11 is an 10.8-liter inline-6 diesel engine offering versatile horsepower and torque ratings suited for regional and vocational applications. It provides a balanced combination of efficiency and heavy-duty performance for commercial operators.

### Volvo D13 12.8L Turbo-Diesel Inline-6

The Volvo D13 is a 12.8-liter inline-6 diesel engine delivering high torque outputs designed for heavy vocational hauling. It is a widely utilized powerplant in the heavy-duty commercial truck market known for robust pull.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is an automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel efficiency and simplify heavy-duty shifting operations. It is widely paired with Volvo diesel engines in vocational configurations.

### Allison Vocational Automatic Transmission

The Allison automatic transmission provides torque converter reliability and smooth power delivery for severe vocational stop-and-go duty cycles. It is a preferred option for operators requiring frequent heavy-load maneuvering.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V509000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuse blows the transmission may not shift, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuse, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V766000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may operate the vehicle with the snow plow headlights on but the marker and tail lamps off, reducing the vehicle's visibility and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will update the software related to the plow lights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Volvo VHD is a robust commercial vehicle platform built for demanding vocational tasks. Prospective buyers should perform thorough mechanical inspections of the engine, transmission, and chassis components, keeping in mind that commercial usage history varies widely.
