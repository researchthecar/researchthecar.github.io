# 2021 Volvo VHD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Volvo VHD is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for demanding vocational applications like construction and municipal service. Buyers shopping for a pre-owned commercial chassis should evaluate powertrain health and operational history carefully, as vocational trucks often endure demanding duty cycles.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 10.8L Turbo-Diesel

The Volvo D11 is an 10.8-liter inline-six diesel engine producing up to 425 horsepower and 1,550 lb-ft of torque, tailored for lighter vocational applications. It offers a balance of vocational capability and fuel efficiency for regional haulers.

### Volvo D13 12.8L Turbo-Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 12.8-liter inline-six diesel engine delivering up to 500 horsepower and 1,850 lb-ft of torque for heavy-duty vocational demands. It is a robust power plant built for high-payload operations, though regular maintenance of emissions equipment is critical.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize shifting points and improve fuel economy in vocational settings. It features heavy-duty gearing suited for frequent stop-start job site conditions.

### Allison 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The Allison 6-speed traditional automatic transmission provides seamless torque multiplication and exceptional durability for severe vocational applications. It is frequently chosen by fleets requiring maximum reliability in heavy utility work.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V782000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** If the park brakes apply unexpectedly, the driver may lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air brake line for proper engagement of the fitting in the pass-through plate, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V417000: Wheels:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A separated hubcap can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hubcap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V535000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The king pin nut may not have been installed or properly tightened during axle assembly. If the king pin is missing or not tightened properly, the knuckle may separate from the axle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicle to make sure that the king pin nut is installed and that it properly tightened, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V536000: Service Brakes, Air](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 121, "Air Brake Systems." A dislodged brake pedal would be inoperable, increasing a risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the pedal plates if required, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V769000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 207, "Seating Systems" and 210, "Seatbelt Assembly Anchorage." The attachment points may fail, allowing the bench seat to shift and increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the weld nut with two nuts and a washer, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V790000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Electrical noise and low signal to the power line carrier may cause the ECU to incorrectly process commands or stop working. Safety systems that depend on the ECU (Automatic Traction Control, ABS, Electronic Stability Control, Active Cruise Control, and Collison Mitigation System) may have diminished or lost functionality, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a pre-owned 2021 Volvo VHD, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive mechanical inspection focusing on the engine, emissions equipment, and transmission performance. Reviewing the service history will help ensure the vocational chassis has been properly maintained.
