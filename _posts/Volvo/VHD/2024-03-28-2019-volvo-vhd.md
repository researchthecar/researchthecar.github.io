**Introduction:**

The 2019 Volvo VHD is a heavy-duty truck available with a range of engine and transmission options. It boasts a spacious cabin and advanced safety features. However, it has received some criticism for its infotainment system and fuel economy.

**Engine**

* **D11 Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **EGR Valve Malfunction:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, black smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* **D13 Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idling, poor fuel economy
        * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Transmission**

* **I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping or shuddering of clutch
        * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
    * **Software Bugs:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission does not shift smoothly, unexpected gear changes
        * Solution: Software updates
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise when driving over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery or repair electrical fault
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery warning light, charging system failure
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water droplets inside the headlight lenses
    * Solution: Seal headlight lenses
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Mirror Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Mirrors do not adjust electrically
    * Solution: Replace mirror motors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heater or AC not working properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
* **Instrument Cluster Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working, warning lights stuck on
    * Solution: Repair or replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Summary:**

The 2019 Volvo VHD has received mixed reviews. While it offers a spacious and comfortable cabin, there have been some concerns about its fuel economy and infotainment system. The engine and transmission issues reported are relatively infrequent but can be costly to repair. Overall, the severity of the issues and their impact on the vehicle's performance vary, but it is recommended to consider potential repair costs when considering purchasing a VHD.
