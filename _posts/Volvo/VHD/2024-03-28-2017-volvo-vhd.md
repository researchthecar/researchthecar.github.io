**Introduction**

The 2017 Volvo VHD is a heavy-duty truck that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The standard engine is a 13-liter D13 inline-6 diesel engine that produces 425 horsepower and 1,250 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 16-liter D16 inline-6 diesel engine (500 horsepower and 1,850 lb-ft of torque) and a 15-liter Cummins ISX15 inline-6 diesel engine (400 horsepower and 1,150 lb-ft of torque). The VHD is available with a 6-speed manual transmission, a 10-speed automated manual transmission, or a 12-speed automated manual transmission.

Owners of the VHD appreciate its powerful engines, comfortable cabin, and advanced safety features. However, some have reported issues with certain components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**D13 Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail prematurely, resulting in a loss of power and increased exhaust smoke.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **EGR system problems:** The exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) system can experience issues, leading to poor engine performance and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Clean or replace the EGR valve and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**D16 Engine**

* **Timing gear failure:** The timing gear can fail catastrophically, resulting in catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing gear.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V263000

**ISX15 Engine**

* **Injector problems:** The fuel injectors can fail, causing rough idling, loss of power, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per injector
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shifting problems:** The transmission may have problems shifting gears, making it difficult or impossible to drive.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**10-Speed Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch wear:** The clutch can wear out prematurely, causing the transmission to slip or engage improperly.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Shifting delays:** The transmission may experience delays when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Leaf spring failure:** The leaf springs can fail prematurely, resulting in a loss of load-bearing capacity.
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per spring
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, making it difficult or impossible to start the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, preventing the vehicle from charging its battery.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Hood latch failure:** The hood latch can fail, allowing the hood to open unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Replace the hood latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **HVAC blower motor failure:** The HVAC blower motor can fail, leaving the vehicle without heat or air conditioning.
    * Solution: Replace the blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2017 Volvo VHD is a capable heavy-duty truck, but it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior systems. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. Overall, the VHD is a reliable truck, but owners should be aware of the potential problems they may encounter.
