# 2016 Volvo VHD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Navigating the used commercial vehicle market requires a careful look at heavy-duty workhorses like the 2016 Volvo VHD. Designed for vocational and severe-service applications, this model integrates robust powertrain configurations built to handle demanding job sites. Prospective buyers should review vehicle maintenance logs and operational history closely to ensure long-term dependability in demanding commercial environments.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine offering strong torque for vocational duties. It balances power delivery with fuel efficiency, making it a reliable choice for regional and severe-service operations when properly maintained.

### Volvo D13

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine delivering high horsepower and robust low-end torque for heavy vocational tasks. It is widely utilized in heavy-duty fleets, though routine maintenance of emissions and fuel systems is critical for longevity.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is an automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel economy and shift performance in vocational applications. It provides smooth operation and durability under heavy loads when serviced according to manufacturer intervals.

### Manual Transmission Options

Heavy-duty manual transmission options offer direct mechanical engagement and robust gear sets for severe vocational environments. They provide traditional reliability for operators preferring manual shift control.

### Automatic Transmission Options

Available automatic transmission options deliver seamless power delivery and reduce driver fatigue in stop-and-go vocational settings. These units require regular fluid maintenance to ensure dependable commercial operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V766000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may operate the vehicle with the snow plow headlights on but the marker and tail lamps off, reducing the vehicle's visibility and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will update the software related to the plow lights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V258000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." If the GAWR information is incorrect, the operator may overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 16V735000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Upper Ball Joint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If water and contaminants enter the ball joint, corrosion can occur and lead to accelerated wear, which over time can result in failure of the ball joint, possibly resulting in complete loss of steering, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the drag links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V630000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Headlights that provide insufficient illumination increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the headlights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
