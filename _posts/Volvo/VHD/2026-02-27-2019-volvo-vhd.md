# 2019 Volvo VHD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Volvo VHD, buyers are looking at a heavy-duty vocational truck designed for demanding work environments like construction and refuse hauling. Because owner-submitted complaint data for this commercial model is relatively scarce in standard consumer registries, prospective purchasers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection focusing on heavy-duty powertrain components, PTO systems, and air brake setups.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 Diesel Engine

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine typically producing between 355 and 425 horsepower. It offers a balanced mix of performance and fuel efficiency tailored for vocational applications, generally proving durable when properly maintained.

### Volvo D13 Diesel Engine

The Volvo D13 is a larger 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine generating up to 500 horsepower and heavy torque outputs. It is built for demanding heavy-haul operations, though buyers should verify emission system health and service history.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is an automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel economy and smooth shifting in vocational duties. It is generally well-regarded for commercial reliability, provided the clutch actuation and software have been regularly serviced.

### Allison Vocational Automatic Transmission

The Allison fully automatic transmission options provide robust torque converter performance well-suited for stop-and-go vocational tasks and heavy PTO operation. They are known for long-term durability in severe service environments.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V509000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuse blows the transmission may not shift, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuse, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V510000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The deformation of the air tank strap t-bolts may cause the t-bolt to disconnect from the strap allowing the tank to drop to the ground and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the air tank strap, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V766000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may operate the vehicle with the snow plow headlights on but the marker and tail lamps off, reducing the vehicle's visibility and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will update the software related to the plow lights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V811000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 119, "New Pneumatic Tires - Other than Passenger Cars." Rapid tire air loss can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V769000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 207, "Seating Systems" and 210, "Seatbelt Assembly Anchorage." The attachment points may fail, allowing the bench seat to shift and increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the weld nut with two nuts and a washer, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Volvo VHD is a robust commercial vehicle platform built to handle tough vocational demands. While public complaint databases show minimal widespread trend issues for this model year, used-car buyers must conduct a rigorous physical and diagnostic inspection of the engine, transmission, and heavy-duty chassis equipment before purchasing.
