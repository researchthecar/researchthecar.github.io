### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Volvo VHD demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components across all engine and transmission options. The heavy-duty design generally holds up well under demanding vocational use. Potential areas for attention include the suspension system, specifically air leaks in the air suspension components, and electrical system issues such as wiring harness wear or control module software glitches. Interior components like the HVAC system and infotainment displays can occasionally experience failures. Overall, the VHD is a robust truck with a good reliability record, with most reported issues being manageable and repairable, reflecting its purpose-built nature for tough jobs.
