# 2024 Volvo VHD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Volvo VHD is a heavy-duty vocational truck designed for demanding work environments such as construction and municipal service. Buyers considering a used model should evaluate its commercial powertrain options and vocational build to ensure it fits their operational needs. Because vocational trucks operate under heavy loads and severe conditions, inspecting the powertrain and auxiliary systems is essential.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 Turbocharged Diesel Engine

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine offering reliable power and efficiency for medium-to-heavy vocational applications. It balances strong low-end torque with manageable operating costs for fleet and owner-operator buyers.

### Volvo D13 Turbocharged Diesel Engine

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine providing high horsepower and robust torque output for heavy-duty vocational hauling. It is a workhorse powertrain option designed for demanding job sites and heavy loads.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is an advanced automated manual transmission designed to optimize fuel efficiency and simplify heavy-duty shifting. It delivers smooth performance across vocational applications when properly maintained.

### Allison Vocational Automatic Transmission

The Allison automatic transmission provides dependable torque conversion and seamless gear engagement in stop-and-go vocational settings. It is a preferred choice for severe-duty operations requiring frequent shifting.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V173000: Wheels:Hubcap/Cover](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached hub cover can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hub covers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V403000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A missing circuit will prevent the side air bag from deploying during a crash. An air bag that does not deploy, increases the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will connect a jumper harness between the seat and main cab harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V441000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear fasteners may not be properly tightened. Loose steering gear fasteners may result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the steering gear fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V690000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the castle nuts and install new cotter pins, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V769000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 207, "Seating Systems" and 210, "Seatbelt Assembly Anchorage." The attachment points may fail, allowing the bench seat to shift and increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the weld nut with two nuts and a washer, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V816000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove and replace the label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V790000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Electrical noise and low signal to the power line carrier may cause the ECU to incorrectly process commands or stop working. Safety systems that depend on the ECU (Automatic Traction Control, ABS, Electronic Stability Control, Active Cruise Control, and Collison Mitigation System) may have diminished or lost functionality, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2024 Volvo VHD is built for rigorous commercial use, offering capable heavy-duty engine and transmission configurations suited for demanding jobs.
