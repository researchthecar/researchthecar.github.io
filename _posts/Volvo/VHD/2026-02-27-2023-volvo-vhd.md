# 2023 Volvo VHD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Volvo VHD is a heavy-duty vocational truck engineered for demanding work environments like construction and dump applications. Designed to handle severe service duty, it pairs robust chassis options with advanced powertrain configurations tailored for commercial fleets. Prospective buyers evaluating a used 2023 model should inspect the vehicle's maintenance history, service records, and operational duty cycle to ensure it meets their specific vocational needs.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 11-Liter Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-six diesel engine producing horsepower and torque configurations optimized for regional and vocational applications. It provides a balanced combination of fuel efficiency and heavy-duty strength for vocational fleets.

### Volvo D13 13-Liter Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-six diesel engine delivering high horsepower and robust low-end torque for demanding heavy-duty hauling. It is a widely utilized powerplant in vocational segments known for handling rigorous work demands.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is an automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel efficiency and seamless shifting under heavy loads. It is a staple in modern commercial fleets, offering specialized vocational software options.

### Allison Vocational Automatic Transmission

The Allison automatic transmission provides robust torque multiplication and reliable engagement for severe-service vocational applications. It is frequently chosen by operators requiring frequent start-stop capability and heavy PTO demands.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V403000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A missing circuit will prevent the side air bag from deploying during a crash. An air bag that does not deploy, increases the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will connect a jumper harness between the seat and main cab harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V441000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear fasteners may not be properly tightened. Loose steering gear fasteners may result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the steering gear fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V769000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 207, "Seating Systems" and 210, "Seatbelt Assembly Anchorage." The attachment points may fail, allowing the bench seat to shift and increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the weld nut with two nuts and a washer, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V790000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VHD&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Electrical noise and low signal to the power line carrier may cause the ECU to incorrectly process commands or stop working. Safety systems that depend on the ECU (Automatic Traction Control, ABS, Electronic Stability Control, Active Cruise Control, and Collison Mitigation System) may have diminished or lost functionality, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
