**Introduction**

The 2018 Volvo 7300 BRT is a heavy-duty bus designed for public transportation. It is offered with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 12.8-liter diesel engine with 420 horsepower and 1,650 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features. However, it has also been plagued by numerous issues that have affected its reliability and performance.

**Engine**

**12.8-liter Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel injector failure:** The fuel injectors can fail, causing the engine to lose power or stall. The issue is often accompanied by a warning light on the dashboard. Solution: Replace the failed fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, reducing engine power and fuel efficiency. The issue is often accompanied by a loss of boost pressure. Solution: Replace the failed turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **EGR system failure:** The EGR system can fail, causing the engine to run rough and produce excessive emissions. The issue is often accompanied by a warning light on the dashboard. Solution: Replace the failed EGR valve or other components of the system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Transmission**

**Allison H 5000 Series Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting problems:** The transmission may experience shifting problems, such as slipping gears or refusing to shift. The issue can be caused by a variety of factors, including faulty solenoids or a worn-out clutch pack. Solution: Repair or replace the failed components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
* **Leaking fluid:** The transmission may leak fluid, which can lead to reduced performance and eventual failure. The leak may be caused by a damaged seal or gasket. Solution: Replace the failed seal or gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air bag leaks:** The air bags that support the vehicle can develop leaks, causing the bus to sag or tilt. The issue is often accompanied by a warning light on the dashboard. Solution: Replace the failed air bag(s). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Leaf spring breakage:** The leaf springs that support the rear axle can break, causing the vehicle to lean to one side. The issue is often accompanied by a loud noise when driving over bumps. Solution: Replace the failed leaf spring(s). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Electrical shorts:** The electrical system can experience shorts, causing blown fuses or damage to various components. The issue can be caused by faulty wiring or loose connections. Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the short. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Cracked windshield:** The windshield can crack or shatter, impairing visibility and posing a safety hazard. Solution: Replace the windshield. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Floor panel damage:** The floor panels can become damaged from heavy foot traffic or improper cleaning methods. Solution: Repair or replace the damaged floor panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

The 2018 Volvo 7300 BRT is a heavy-duty bus with a variety of engine and transmission options. However, it has been plagued by numerous issues that affect its reliability and performance.
