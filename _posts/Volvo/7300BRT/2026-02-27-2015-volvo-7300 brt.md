### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Volvo 7300 BRT demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain and suspension systems, with no widespread issues reported for its primary engine and transmission options. The most common technical concerns tend to be in the electrical system, specifically related to the Body Control Module and wiring harness integrity, which can manifest as intermittent faults. Exterior components like door actuators and interior systems such as HVAC can also experience failures, though these are typically manageable. Proactive maintenance and regular inspections, especially for electrical connections and HVAC components, are recommended to ensure continued optimal performance and minimize downtime.
