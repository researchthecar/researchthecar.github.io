## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle leaning to one side, uneven ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 per corner

**Ride Height Control Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height, vehicle too high or too low, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the ride height control valve.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


## Electrical

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Volvo 7300 BRT demonstrates strong reliability, consistent with Volvo's commercial vehicle standards. Major powertrain components (engine and transmission) show no widespread issues, indicating robust engineering. Overall, the 7300 BRT is a dependable platform, with most reported issues being manageable and repairable, rather than indicative of systemic design flaws. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring continued high performance.
