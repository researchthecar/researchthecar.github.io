**2022 Volvo 7300 BRT**

The Volvo 7300 BRT is a heavy-duty bus designed for public transportation. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* Engine: D8K 320 hp/1,200 lb-ft torque, D11K 380 hp/1,400 lb-ft torque, D13K 420 hp/1,600 lb-ft torque
* Transmission: ZF EcoShift 6-speed automatic, Volvo I-Shift 12-speed automated manual

People who like the Volvo 7300 BRT appreciate its fuel efficiency, reliability, and comfortable ride. Some people have reported minor issues with the vehicle, which are detailed below.

**Engine**

**D8K Engine**

* **EGR Valve Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The EGR valve can become clogged with soot, leading to reduced engine power and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Clean or replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail, resulting in a loss of power and increased smoke emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**D11K Engine**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The head gasket can fail, causing coolant and oil to leak. This can lead to overheating and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the head gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**D13K Engine**

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors can fail, resulting in poor fuel economy, rough idling, and reduced power.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**ZF EcoShift 6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The shift solenoids can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all.
    * Solution: Replace the shift solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch can wear out prematurely, resulting in slipping and difficulty shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The leaf springs can break, causing the bus to sag and affect ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can fail, preventing the bus from starting.
    * Solution: Replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator can fail, preventing the vehicle's electrical system from charging.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation can build up inside the headlights, impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Replace the headlights or reseal them.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The undercarriage can develop rust, leading to structural damage over time.
    * Solution: Clean and treat rust with rust-resistant coatings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the bus to be too hot or too cold.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Fabric Tearing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat fabric can tear, especially in high-traffic areas.
    * Solution: Replace the seat fabric.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Volvo 7300 BRT has received positive reviews overall, but some minor issues have been reported. The most common issues reported include EGR valve clogging, turbocharger failure, and shift solenoid failure. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most can be resolved for under $1,000.
