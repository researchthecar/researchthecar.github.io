**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volvo 7300 BRT is a high-capacity, heavy-duty transit bus. It is available with two engine options: a diesel engine with 320 horsepower and 1,200 lb-ft of torque, and a hybrid diesel-electric engine with 320 horsepower and 1,200 lb-ft of torque. Both engine options are paired with an Allison B500R automatic transmission. The 7300 BRT is known for its fuel efficiency, reliability, and passenger comfort. However, there are a few issues that have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail prematurely, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
    * **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $5,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Hybrid Diesel-Electric Engine**

* **Battery pack overheating:** The battery pack can overheat, causing the vehicle to enter limp mode or shut down.
    * **Solution:** Replace the battery pack.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $10,000 - $15,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**Allison B500R Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission can slip, causing a loss of power and acceleration.
    * **Solution:** Replace the transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $10,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure:** The air suspension can fail, causing the vehicle to sit lower and ride rougher.
    * **Solution:** Replace the air suspension system.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $5,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical system failure:** The electrical system can fail, causing a loss of power and lighting.
    * **Solution:** Diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Seat upholstery tearing:** The seat upholstery can tear prematurely.
    * **Solution:** Replace the seat upholstery.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Interior trim rattling:** The interior trim can rattle, causing a noise annoyance.
    * **Solution:** Replace or tighten the interior trim.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Volvo 7300 BRT is a generally reliable and fuel-efficient vehicle. However, it does have some issues that can be costly to fix. The most severe issue is the battery pack overheating on the hybrid diesel-electric engine, which can result in a loss of power or a complete shutdown. Other issues, such as transmission slipping and air suspension failure, can also be significant. While the interior issues are not as severe, they can be annoying and detract from the overall passenger experience. Overall, the 7300 BRT is a solid vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing or leasing one.
