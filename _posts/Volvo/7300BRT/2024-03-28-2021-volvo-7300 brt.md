## 2021 Volvo 7300 BRT

The 2021 Volvo 7300 BRT is a heavy-duty bus designed for public transport. It is available with various engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine:**
    * D8K diesel engine (320 hp, 1200 Nm)
    * D11K diesel engine (380 hp, 1600 Nm)
* **Transmission:**
    * Volvo I-Shift 12-speed automated manual transmission

People appreciate the 7300 BRT for its fuel efficiency, reliability, and spacious interior. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

### **Engine**

**D8K Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description: The fuel injectors can fail, causing a loss of power and difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injector(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**D11K Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description: The turbocharger can fail, resulting in reduced power and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description: The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, causing jerking or slipping.
    * Solution: Reconfigure the transmission software or replace the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description: The rear leaf springs can break, causing the vehicle to sag and handle poorly.
    * Solution: Replace the broken leaf spring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description: The battery can drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Identify and repair the electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

* **Headlight Malfunction:**
    * Description: The headlights may not function properly or may dim prematurely.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty headlight bulbs or the headlight control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the temperature inside the vehicle to be uncomfortable.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seatbelt Retractor Malfunction:**
    * Description: The seatbelt retractor may not properly retract the seatbelt after use.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty seatbelt retractor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description: The door locks may not operate correctly, making it difficult to enter or exit the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2021 Volvo 7300 BRT is generally a reliable vehicle; however, it has been reported to have some issues in different areas. Most issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. However, some problems, such as turbocharger failure, can be more severe and costly to repair. Overall, the 7300 BRT is a solid choice for heavy-duty public transportation, but potential buyers should be aware of its potential issues.
