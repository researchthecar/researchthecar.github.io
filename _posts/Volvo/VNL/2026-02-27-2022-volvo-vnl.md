# 2022 Volvo VNL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo VNL is a prominent heavy-duty commercial truck designed for long-haul efficiency and driver comfort. When shopping for a used commercial vehicle of this caliber, understanding powertrain configurations and monitoring early owner-reported drivability concerns can help fleet buyers and independent operators make informed purchasing decisions.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 10.8L Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D11 is an 10.8-liter inline-six diesel engine producing around 325 to 425 horsepower, optimized for regional and vocational applications. It offers strong low-end torque while maintaining favorable fuel economy for lighter long-haul configurations.

### Volvo D13 12.8L Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 12.8-liter inline-six diesel engine ranging from 400 to 500 horsepower, serving as the flagship powerplant for heavy long-haul trucking. It delivers robust performance and pulling power, though buyers should closely inspect cooling and emission systems.

**Loss of Motive Power and Coolant Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected losses of motive power where the vehicle struggles to accelerate past low speeds, accompanied by a low coolant warning light.
- **Solution:** Have a certified commercial technician inspect the cooling system, check for leaks or head gasket integrity, and diagnose powertrain control module fault codes.

### Volvo D15 14.9L Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D15 is a heavy-duty 14.9-liter inline-six diesel engine engineered for heavy haul and demanding over-the-road routes, typically rated up to 500 or 600 horsepower. It provides maximum muscle for extreme weights, though it is less common in standard fleet configurations.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is an advanced 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to maximize fuel efficiency and ease driver fatigue in long-haul operations. It is widely praised for smooth shifting, though proper software calibration and clutch maintenance are vital for longevity.

### Allison 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The Allison 6-speed automatic transmission utilizes a traditional torque converter setup favored in vocational and severe-duty configurations. It offers rugged durability and dependable engagement under heavy stop-and-start workloads.

## Suspension

### Air Tank and Chassis Components

Electrical and pneumatic accessory systems require routine maintenance to ensure auxiliary components and chassis connections remain secure.

**Loose Air Tank Flaring**
- **Symptoms:** Operators may notice loose air tank fittings or flaring during routine pre-trip inspections, which can lead to air pressure loss in the pneumatic system.
- **Solution:** Inspect all pneumatic lines and tank fittings regularly, and tighten or replace compromised flaring connections as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V098000: Wheels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Under certain circumstances, these wheels may not be able to support the load, which can cause the wheels to bend or break while in use. Bent or broken wheels can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the wheels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V781000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The electronic control unit software may allow the transmission to shift into neutral unexpectedly. An unexpected shift to neutral can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the electronic control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V417000: Wheels:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A separated hubcap can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hubcap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V065000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached flywheel can cause loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, driveline pieces may be ejected from the engine compartment, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V896000: Service Brakes, Air:Supply:Reservoir/Tank](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Broken fasteners may allow the air tank to detach from the vehicle, becoming a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V403000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A missing circuit will prevent the side air bag from deploying during a crash. An air bag that does not deploy, increases the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will connect a jumper harness between the seat and main cab harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volvo VNL remains a capable option for commercial buyers seeking advanced aerodynamics and reliable powertrain choices like the D13 and I-Shift. Prospective buyers should perform thorough pre-purchase inspections focusing on cooling system integrity, pneumatic connections, and service history.
