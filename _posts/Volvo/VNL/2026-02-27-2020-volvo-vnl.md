# 2020 Volvo VNL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Volvo VNL is a prominent Class 8 long-haul semi-truck designed for efficiency, driver comfort, and versatility on the highway. As a modern commercial vehicle, it relies heavily on integrated proprietary powertrains and advanced electronic architecture. While fleet operators generally appreciate its aerodynamic profile and driver-focused cab environment, prospective buyers of used models should carefully evaluate the condition of its heavy-duty diesel engines, automated manual transmissions, and complex electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine typically rated between 325 and 425 horsepower, designed for regional haul and lighter distribution applications. It offers solid fuel economy and a lighter weight profile compared to larger displacement options, though proper maintenance of emissions equipment is essential.

### Volvo D13

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine delivering horsepower ratings from 400 to 500 hp, serving as the flagship powerplant for long-haul applications. It is widely utilized for its strong torque output at lower RPMs, though buyers should verify service history regarding fuel systems and aftertreatment components.

### Cummins X15

The Cummins X15 is a 15-liter heavy-duty diesel engine available in various power ratings up to 500+ horsepower, tailored for heavy payload and demanding long-haul routes. It is known for robust parts availability and service support across North America, making it a popular alternative for fleet buyers.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel efficiency and smooth shifting for proprietary Volvo engines. It is a dominant choice in the market, though proper clutch calibration and software maintenance are important for longevity.

### Eaton Fuller Automated/Manual Transmissions

Eaton Fuller transmissions—ranging from manual options to automated Endurant systems—are frequently paired with Cummins powerplants in the VNL lineup. These gearboxes are well-regarded for durability and straightforward servicing in heavy-duty commercial operations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V510000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The deformation of the air tank strap t-bolts may cause the t-bolt to disconnect from the strap allowing the tank to drop to the ground and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the air tank strap, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V509000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If the fuse blows the transmission may not shift, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuse, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V241000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The loose fasteners may result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will tighten the steering gear fasteners to specification, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V613000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If a window detaches from the vehicle, it may strike another vehicle or pedestrian, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks has notified owners, and dealers will replace the window hinge, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V208000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If a window detaches from the vehicle, it may strike another vehicle or pedestrian, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks has notified owners, and dealers will replace the window hinge, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V756000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A loose steering gear can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and re-torque the steering gear to a higher value. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V536000: Service Brakes, Air](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 121, "Air Brake Systems." A dislodged brake pedal would be inoperable, increasing a risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the pedal plates if required, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V384000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." If the rear axles are overloaded, over time the wheel hubs may fail prematurely, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2020 Volvo VNL, thorough pre-purchase inspections focusing on the specific engine and transmission configuration are critical. Because commercial trucks endure rigorous operational demands, verifying maintenance records, aftertreatment health, and powertrain software updates will help ensure reliable long-term performance on the highway.
