# 2024 Volvo VNL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Volvo VNL is a prominent long-haul heavy-duty tractor designed for efficiency, driver comfort, and advanced over-the-road performance. Because this model year is relatively new, official owner complaint data and long-term reliability histories are still developing. Prospective buyers should carefully review any remaining factory warranty coverage and perform thorough pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D13 12.8L Turbo Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 12.8-liter inline-six turbo diesel engine typically outputting between 405 and 500 horsepower and up to 1,850 lb-ft of torque. It is widely utilized in heavy-duty commercial applications for its fuel efficiency and robust performance under heavy loads.

### Cummins X15 15.0L Turbo Diesel

The Cummins X15 is a 15-liter heavy-duty diesel engine offering horsepower ratings ranging from 400 to 600 hp and massive torque output for demanding line-haul operations. It is known for high durability and broad service network support across North America.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize shift points seamlessly for maximum fuel economy. It is a dominant choice in modern heavy-duty fleets due to its smooth operation and reliability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V173000: Wheels:Hubcap/Cover](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached hub cover can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hub covers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V403000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A missing circuit will prevent the side air bag from deploying during a crash. An air bag that does not deploy, increases the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will connect a jumper harness between the seat and main cab harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V511000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V689000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software programming error may cause the electronic stability control (ESC) to be inoperative. Driving with an inoperative electronic stability control system can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V690000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the castle nuts and install new cotter pins, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V886000: Steering:Linkages:Knuckle:Spindle:Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The bearing journals may not be fully seated in the steering knuckle assembly, preventing the proper installation of the wheel end hub assembly. An improperly installed wheel end hub assembly can leak oil, causing the axle spindles to loosen or fracture and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Hendrickson will inspect and replace the steering knuckle assembly as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V121000: Steering:Gear Box:Shaft Sector](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fractured steering gear shaft can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the serial numbers, and replace the steering gears, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
