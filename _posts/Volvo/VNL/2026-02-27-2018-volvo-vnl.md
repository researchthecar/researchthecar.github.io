# 2018 Volvo VNL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2018 Volvo VNL heavy-duty commercial truck means evaluating robust long-haul platforms that occasionally draw owner reports regarding powertrain responsiveness and steering components. While owner complaints reflect localized mechanical frustrations, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect service histories, particularly concerning fuel delivery and driveline components.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 10.8L Diesel Engine

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-six diesel engine typically rated between 325 and 425 horsepower, designed for regional haul efficiency. It offers a balance of weight savings and pulling power, though buyers should verify proper maintenance of its fuel delivery systems.

### Volvo D13 12.8L Diesel Engine

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-six diesel engine delivering robust horsepower ratings up to 500 hp for heavy long-haul applications. It is a widespread fleet option known for strong torque output, though sporadic owner reports highlight fuel pump and operational smoothness concerns.

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may unexpectedly shut down while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the high-pressure fuel system and pump inspected by a qualified technician, and replace faulty components as necessary.

**Engine Shaking and Rough Operation**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may run rough and exhibit noticeable shaking during operation.
- **Solution:** Perform a comprehensive diagnostic scan of the fuel injectors, ignition/fuel delivery sensors, and engine mounts.

### Cummins X15 15.0L Diesel Engine

The Cummins X15 is a heavy-duty 15-liter diesel engine offering high torque outputs up to 2,050 lb-ft for demanding over-the-road hauls. It is appreciated for massive pulling capability and broad parts availability, making it a favorite among heavy-load operators.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission engineered to optimize fuel economy and seamless shifting in long-haul operations. While widely praised for its efficiency, some owner reports note occasional throttle response delays and clutch engagement issues.

**Powertrain Hesitation and Lunging**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may lunge forward unpredictably or fail to properly respond when the accelerator pedal is depressed.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transmission control module, clutch actuation systems, and software calibration for updates or wear.

### Eaton Fuller 10/13/18-Speed Manual Transmission

Eaton Fuller manual transmissions provide traditional driver-controlled gear selection favored by operators wanting maximum mechanical directness. These units are generally durable, provided the clutch and synchronizers have been maintained properly.

## Suspension

### Driveshaft and Driveline Support

The driveline components transfer power efficiently from the transmission to the drive axles. Proper balancing and universal joint maintenance are essential to prevent high-speed vibrations.

**Driveline Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Violent vehicle vibrations occurring at highway speeds between 57 and 65 mph.
- **Solution:** Inspect the driveshaft, universal joints, carrier bearings, and balancing weights; replace components if imbalance persists.

## Steering

### Steering Linkage and Bushings

The steering system manages front axle tracking and directional stability under heavy commercial loads. Routine checks of linkages and alignment help prevent premature tire wear.

**Steering Vibration and Premature Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Uneven front tire wear and noticeable front-end vibrations while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components, front end alignment, and plastic bushings for premature wear and replace affected parts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V668000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The engine may also stall without warning, resulting in the vehicle's inability to restart, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Cummins will notify owners, and dealers will replace the single screen filter banjo bolt will be replaced with a dual screen filter banjo bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V509000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuse blows the transmission may not shift, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuse, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V208000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If a window detaches from the vehicle, it may strike another vehicle or pedestrian, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks has notified owners, and dealers will replace the window hinge, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V032000: Power Train:Clutch Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Unintended vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo has notified owners and dealers will update the Transmission Control Module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V384000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." If the rear axles are overloaded, over time the wheel hubs may fail prematurely, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Volvo VNL is a capable workhorse equipped with dependable engine and transmission choices well-suited for commercial fleet duties. Used-car buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on fuel delivery systems, driveline vibrations, and front steering geometry to ensure long-term reliability on the road.
