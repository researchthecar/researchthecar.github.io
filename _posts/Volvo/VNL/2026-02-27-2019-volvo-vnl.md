# 2019 Volvo VNL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Volvo VNL heavy-duty commercial truck means evaluating robust heavy-duty equipment built for long-haul transport. While these trucks are engineered for high mileage and endurance, prospective buyers should review available owner reports regarding powertrain behavior, suspension alignment, and steering components to ensure optimal road-readiness.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11 10.8L Diesel

The Volvo D11 is an 10.8-liter inline-six diesel engine typically rated between 325 and 425 horsepower, designed for regional haul efficiency. Owners generally find it dependable, though intermittent sensor and check engine warnings have been reported.

### Volvo D13 12.8L Diesel

The Volvo D13 is a 12.8-liter inline-six diesel engine offering horsepower ratings from 400 to 500 hp, serving as a primary powerhouse for long-haul operations. It provides strong torque delivery, though auxiliary systems require regular monitoring.

**Intermittent Check Engine Light**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine warning light illuminates intermittently during operation, sometimes accompanied by secondary thermal events such as rear axle smoking.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician perform a full diagnostic scan to identify fault codes, inspect emissions and sensor systems, and check axle and differential temperatures.

### Cummins X15 15.0L Diesel

The optional Cummins X15 is a heavy-duty 15-liter diesel engine delivering up to 500-600 horsepower for demanding heavy-haul applications. It is a widely serviced commercial powerplant known for substantial torque output.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission designed to optimize fuel economy and smooth out heavy-duty shifting. Routine software maintenance keeps this sophisticated unit operating reliably.

**Unexpected Neutral Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may occasionally shift into neutral on its own while coasting downhill or when lifting off the accelerator pedal.
- **Solution:** Update the transmission control module (TCM) software or inspect the clutch actuator and electronic shift components for faults.

### Eaton Fuller 10/13/18-Speed Manual

Traditional Eaton Fuller manual transmissions offer durable, driver-controlled mechanical gear selection for vocational and long-haul fleets. They are valued for mechanical simplicity and straightforward maintenance.

## Steering

### Steering Alignment and U-Bolts

The steering system relies on secure front axle fastening components to maintain directional stability. Proper fastener torque is critical for preventing wheel alignment drift during heavy use.

**Steering Pull and Wheel Orientation Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may pull noticeably to one side, requiring the driver to hold the steering wheel at an offset angle after driving over bumps.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front axle U-bolts and leaf springs for proper torque retention, and check the front-end alignment.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V668000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The engine may also stall without warning, resulting in the vehicle's inability to restart, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Cummins will notify owners, and dealers will replace the single screen filter banjo bolt will be replaced with a dual screen filter banjo bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V510000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The deformation of the air tank strap t-bolts may cause the t-bolt to disconnect from the strap allowing the tank to drop to the ground and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the air tank strap, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V509000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuse blows the transmission may not shift, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuse, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V208000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If a window detaches from the vehicle, it may strike another vehicle or pedestrian, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks has notified owners, and dealers will replace the window hinge, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V542000: Trailer Hitches:Fifth Wheel Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the trailer separates from the tractor, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fifth wheel slider bracket, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V032000: Power Train:Clutch Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Unintended vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo has notified owners and dealers will update the Transmission Control Module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V811000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 119, "New Pneumatic Tires - Other than Passenger Cars." Rapid tire air loss can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 20V384000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." If the rear axles are overloaded, over time the wheel hubs may fail prematurely, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Volvo VNL remains a capable option for commercial buyers, provided thorough pre-purchase inspections are conducted. Shoppers should pay close attention to front axle suspension hardware, steering alignment stability, and transmission shift behavior during a test drive to ensure long-term reliability.
