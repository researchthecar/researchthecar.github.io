# 2021 Volvo VNL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Volvo VNL is a prominent class 8 long-haul semi-truck designed for highway efficiency, driver comfort, and advanced safety features. As a heavy-duty commercial vehicle, prospective buyers should evaluate its powertrain capabilities and monitor owner-reported complaints regarding electrical systems and exterior components to ensure optimal fleet uptime.

---

## Engine

### Volvo D11

The Volvo D11 is an 11-liter inline-6 diesel engine offering strong fuel efficiency for regional haul applications, backed by dependable medium-duty performance.

### Volvo D13

The Volvo D13 is a 13-liter inline-6 diesel engine delivering up to 500 horsepower, serving as the flagship long-haul powerplant known for high torque and reliability.

### Cummins X15

The Cummins X15 is a 15-liter heavy-duty diesel engine providing up to 605 horsepower, widely favored in the industry for heavy-haul strength and robust service network support.

## Transmission

### Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual

The Volvo I-Shift is a 12-speed automated manual transmission engineered for seamless gear shifts and maximum fuel economy in long-haul operations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V241000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The loose fasteners may result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will tighten the steering gear fasteners to specification, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V782000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** If the park brakes apply unexpectedly, the driver may lose control of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a vehicle crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air brake line for proper engagement of the fitting in the pass-through plate, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V417000: Wheels:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A separated hubcap can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove the hubcap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V536000: Service Brakes, Air](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 121, "Air Brake Systems." A dislodged brake pedal would be inoperable, increasing a risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the pedal plates if required, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V535000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The king pin nut may not have been installed or properly tightened during axle assembly. If the king pin is missing or not tightened properly, the knuckle may separate from the axle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Trucks will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicle to make sure that the king pin nut is installed and that it properly tightened, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V858000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A machining defect may cause pressure to build in the power steering pump, breaking the shaft seal and resulting in a loss of power steering assist. A loss of power steering assist increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the power steering pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V384000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=VNL&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." If the rear axles are overloaded, over time the wheel hubs may fail prematurely, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2021 Volvo VNL, inspect the heavy-duty engine and I-Shift transmission options thoroughly. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a pre-purchase inspection will help secure a dependable long-haul asset.
