## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, loss of ride height, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air springs or associated air lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 per corner

**Compressor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Failure to maintain ride height, constant running of compressor, warning lights on dashboard.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Volvo 9700 is generally considered a reliable coach, with its powertrain and core systems demonstrating good durability. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the air suspension system, specifically air spring leaks and compressor malfunctions, which are not uncommon for vehicles of this type with extensive mileage. Electrical and interior components, such as the HVAC blower motor, can also experience failures. However, these are typically manageable repairs. Overall, with diligent maintenance, the 2018 Volvo 9700 offers a dependable platform for passenger transport.
