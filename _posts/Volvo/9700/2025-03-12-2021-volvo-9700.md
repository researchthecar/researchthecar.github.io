The 2021 Volvo 9700 is a premium coach bus designed for long-distance travel and charter services. This model is known for its comfort, safety features, and fuel efficiency. Available with Volvo's D11 or D13 engine options paired with Volvo's I-Shift automated manual transmission, the 9700 delivers a smooth and powerful ride. Drivers often appreciate its handling and the comprehensive safety systems, while passengers enjoy the spacious interior and comfortable seating. Common complaints often relate to the complexity of the onboard electronics and occasional issues with specific components in high-mileage use.

**Engine**

**D13 Engine**
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive black smoke, unusual whistling noise from the engine compartment. Often related to bearing failure within the turbocharger itself.
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement. Ensure proper lubrication lines are inspected and clear of blockage during installation of the new turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, poor fuel economy, check engine light. May also experience increased emissions.
    * Solution: EGR valve replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**D11 Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough running engine, decreased fuel efficiency, check engine light with codes relating to fuel injector performance.
    * Solution: Diagnosis to identify the faulty injector(s), followed by replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Volvo I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Shift Hesitation and Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation between gear changes, particularly noticeable during upshifts. Jerky shifts, especially in lower gears.
    * Solution: Software update for the transmission control module (TCM). If the problem persists, inspection and possible replacement of the clutch actuator or the shift actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200+ for software update, $2,000 - $4,000+ for actuator replacement
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine RPMs increase without a corresponding increase in vehicle speed, indicating clutch slippage.
    * Solution: Clutch adjustment or replacement. Inspection of the clutch actuator is also recommended.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle leaning to one side, inability to maintain proper ride height, slow or non-existent inflation of air springs, fault codes related to the air suspension system.
    * Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor. Check for leaks in the air lines and air springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Multiplexing System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent failures of various electrical components, such as lighting, climate control, or entertainment systems. Erratic behavior or error messages on the driver display.
    * Solution: Software updates to the vehicle's central control unit. In some cases, replacement of a faulty control module may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200+ for software updates, $1,000 - $3,000+ for module replacement
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining overnight or after a short period of inactivity. Difficulty starting the engine.
    * Solution: Diagnosis to identify the parasitic draw. Common causes include faulty relays, improperly installed aftermarket accessories, or a failing alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Automatic Door Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors failing to open or close properly, jamming, or making unusual noises. Malfunctions can stem from wear in the door mechanism or issues with the electric motors.
    * Solution: Lubrication of the door mechanism, adjustment of the door alignment, or replacement of faulty electric motors or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+ depending on the severity

* **Lighting System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights, taillights, or signal lights malfunctioning or failing prematurely. This can include issues with the light bulbs, wiring, or control modules.
    * Solution: Replacement of light bulbs, repair of wiring, or replacement of faulty control modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control, weak airflow, or unusual noises from the HVAC system. Problems can arise from refrigerant leaks, faulty compressors, or issues with the blower motor.
    * Solution: Diagnosis of the HVAC system, repair of refrigerant leaks, replacement of faulty compressors, or replacement of the blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+ depending on the issue

* **Infotainment System Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezing, crashing, or malfunctioning. Problems can range from software glitches to hardware failures.
    * Solution: Software updates, system resets, or replacement of faulty hardware components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2021 Volvo 9700, while a premium coach bus, has experienced a range of issues. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor electrical glitches or software problems, while others, such as engine and transmission failures, are more significant and costly to repair. Based on user reports, owners should be prepared for potential expenses ranging from a few hundred dollars for minor fixes to several thousand for major component replacements. Regular maintenance and proactive monitoring of the vehicle's systems can help mitigate these risks.
