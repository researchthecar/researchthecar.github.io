**Introduction**

The 2017 Volvo 9700 is a luxury coach bus designed for long-distance travel. It comes standard with a 13-liter D13C diesel engine producing 460 horsepower and 2,300 lb-ft of torque. A 12.8-liter D13K diesel engine producing 500 horsepower and 2,500 lb-ft of torque is also available. Both engines are paired with an I-Shift automated manual transmission.

The 9700 is praised for its comfortable ride, quiet cabin, and advanced safety features. However, it has also faced some issues that have affected its reliability.

**Engine**

**D13C Diesel Engine**

* **Engine oil leakage:** Oil leaks have been reported from the engine oil pan gasket.
* **Solution:** Replace the oil pan gasket.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
* **Recalls:** None Found

**D13K Diesel Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**I-Shift Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Shifting problems:** Some vehicles have experienced difficulty shifting gears, particularly when cold or under heavy loads.
* **Solution:** Update transmission software and/or replace transmission control module.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension leaks:** Air leaks from the air suspension system have been reported, which can lead to suspension failure.
* **Solution:** Identify and repair air leaks.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery discharge:** Some vehicles have experienced battery discharge issues, which can be caused by a faulty alternator or parasitic drain.
* **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty alternator or diagnose and fix parasitic drain.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield wiper arm failure:** Wiper arms have been known to fail prematurely.
* **Solution:** Replace wiper arms.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control issues:** Some vehicles have experienced problems with the climate control system, including failure to maintain desired temperature or excessive noise.
* **Solution:** Inspect and repair or replace climate control components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Volvo 9700 is a capable and luxurious coach bus, but it has faced some issues that affect its reliability. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to significant. It is important to note that not all vehicles experience all of these issues, but potential buyers should be aware of them before making a purchase decision.
