### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows may become slow, inoperative, or get stuck.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Sensor Malfunctions (e.g., ABS, Speed Sensor)**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights on the dashboard (ABS, Traction Control, Check Engine), potential impact on braking or speedometer accuracy.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Volvo C30 generally presents a good reliability record, with most mechanical components performing as expected. The primary areas of concern tend to be electrical, specifically related to window regulators and various sensors that can trigger warning lights. These issues are not typically catastrophic but require attention and can incur moderate repair costs. Engine and transmission options, across both petrol and diesel variants, are largely robust. Suspension and interior components also show good durability. Overall, the C30 is a solid choice for those seeking a stylish and practical compact car, provided potential electrical gremlins are addressed promptly.
