**Introduction:**

The 2009 Volvo C30 is a sporty two-door compact hatchback that combines style with functionality. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.5-liter inline-five engine paired with a six-speed manual or five-speed automatic transmission, and a 2.0-liter inline-five turbocharged engine mated to a six-speed manual or six-speed automatic transmission. Despite its sleek design and comfortable interior, the C30 has experienced some reported issues.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Inline-Five**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Restricted airflow, decreased engine performance, misfires.
    * Solution: Valve cleaning.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Inline-Five Turbocharged**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, smoke from the engine bay, whistling sounds.
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, particularly when cold.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid change, clutch replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Five-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or erratic gear changes, loss of power.
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mount.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering lights, difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Alternator replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking electronically.
    * Solution: Actuator replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or noise coming from the dashboard.
    * Solution: Reinforcement or replacement of dashboard components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Volvo C30 has experienced some issues, with the most common being transmission slippage, intake valve carbon buildup, and turbocharger failure. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some repairs being relatively minor while others can be quite expensive.
