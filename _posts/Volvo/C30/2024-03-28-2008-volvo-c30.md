**Introduction:**

The 2008 Volvo C30 is a compact luxury hatchback. It is available with two engine options: a 2.4L naturally aspirated inline-five and a 2.5L turbocharged inline-five. Both engines are paired with a five-speed manual transmission or a five-speed automatic transmission. The C30 is known for its stylish design, sporty handling, and comfortable interior. However, it has been reported to have several issues.

**Engine**

**2.4L Naturally Aspirated Inline-Five**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the intake manifold gasket, causing overheating and rough running.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L Turbocharged Inline-Five**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, blue or black smoke from the exhaust, and excessive oil consumption.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks from the transmission case or the axle seals, causing slipping and shifting issues.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking seals and refill the transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Five-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **TCM Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission control module (TCM) fails, causing shifting problems, limp mode, or complete transmission failure.
    * Solution: Replace the TCM.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged strut mounts cause excessive noise and vibration when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Identify and fix the source of the battery drain, such as a faulty alternator or a parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both fog lights fail to turn on or produce insufficient light.
    * Solution: Replace the fog light assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or more power windows fail to operate or become noisy.
    * Solution: Replace the power window motor or switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2008 Volvo C30 has a number of reported issues, including engine failures, transmission problems, and electrical issues. The severity of these issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. If you are considering purchasing a C30, it is important to be aware of these potential problems and factor them into your decision.
