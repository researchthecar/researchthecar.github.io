**2007 Volvo C30**

The 2007 Volvo C30 is a compact luxury hatchback that was praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and sporty handling. It was available with several engine and transmission options, including:

* 2.4L naturally aspirated inline-5 (140 hp, 166 lb-ft)
* T5 2.5L turbocharged inline-5 (227 hp, 236 lb-ft)
* T5 2.5L turbocharged inline-5 (263 hp, 250 lb-ft)
* D5 2.4L turbodiesel inline-5 (185 hp, 295 lb-ft)

**Engine**

**2.4L naturally aspirated inline-5**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace defective piston rings or cylinder head.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**T5 2.5L turbocharged inline-5 (227 hp)**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Turbocharger failure can cause loss of power, smoke, and oil leaks.
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**T5 2.5L turbocharged inline-5 (263 hp)**

* **No Issues Found**

**D5 2.4L turbodiesel inline-5**

* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues:** Clogged DPF can cause reduced performance, increased fuel consumption, and engine warning lights.
    * Solution: Clean or replace DPF.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**5-speed Geartronic automatic transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:** Transmission slipping out of gear, especially under load or acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace worn-out clutches or transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-speed manual transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**6-speed Geartronic automatic transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Lower Control Arm Bushings:** Worn-out bushings can cause clunking, rattling, and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Upper Mount Bushings:** Worn-out bushings can cause noise and vibration from the rear suspension.
    * Solution: Replace upper mount bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Alternator failure can cause loss of charging, battery drain, and electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Central Locking System Malfunction:** Central locking system may fail to lock or unlock doors, or may lock doors unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Repair or replace door lock actuators or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:** Headlight switch may fail to turn on headlights or may turn them on inadvertently.
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Keyless Entry Fob Malfunction:** Keyless entry fob may fail to unlock or start the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace fob or reprogram fob.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling:** Dashboard may rattle or buzz, especially over rough roads or at high speeds.
    * Solution: Apply soundproofing material or replace defective dashboard components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Climate control system may fail to adjust temperature or may not blow air.
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control actuators or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2007 Volvo C30 has some common issues that can impact its reliability and performance. These issues range from moderate to severe in severity, and can be expensive to repair. Potential owners should be aware of these issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
