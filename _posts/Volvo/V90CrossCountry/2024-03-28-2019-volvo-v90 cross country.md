**Introduction**
The 2019 Volvo V90 Cross Country is a luxury station wagon available with two engine and transmission options:

- 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4, 8-speed automatic transmission (B4) - 250 hp, 258 lb-ft torque
- 2.0L Supercharged and Turbocharged Inline-4, 8-speed automatic transmission (B5) - 316 hp, 295 lb-ft torque

The V90 Cross Country is praised for its elegant design, comfortable interior, and impressive safety features. However, it has received some criticism for its high price and occasional reliability issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B4)**

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace the failed fuel injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive noise, increased oil consumption
    * Solution: Repair or replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Supercharged and Turbocharged Inline-4 (B5)**

* **Water Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks
    * Solution: Replace the water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Steering Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty steering, erratic power steering behavior
    * Solution: Replace the power steering pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifts, delayed shifts, slipping gears
    * Solution: Update software, repair or replace transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Squeak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise from the rear suspension
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace the affected suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly when parked
    * Solution: Identify and address the source of the battery drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, malfunctioning components
    * Solution: Repair or replace the affected electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle does not open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace the door handle(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Bugs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues, freezing, unresponsive touch screen
    * Solution: Update the system software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free of charge
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, reduced AC efficiency
    * Solution: Repair or replace the affected components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Volvo V90 Cross Country offers a comfortable ride, elegant design, and advanced safety features, it has been plagued by several issues affecting the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to major breakdowns, with estimated repair costs varying significantly. It's important to note that these issues may not affect all V90 Cross Country models, and individual vehicles may have different experiences.
