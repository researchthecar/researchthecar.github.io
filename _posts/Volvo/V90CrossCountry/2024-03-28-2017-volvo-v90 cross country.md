**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volvo V90 Cross Country is a luxury wagon that offers a spacious interior, advanced safety features, and a potent engine. Engine options include a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder and a 2.0L turbocharged and supercharged 4-cylinder, both paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Despite its overall reliability, the V90 Cross Country has encountered a number of specific issues.

**Engine**
* **2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, engine noise, check engine light
        * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Belt Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, loss of power, catastrophic engine failure
        * Solution: Timing belt replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged 4-Cylinder**
    * **Supercharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, abnormal engine noise
        * Solution: Supercharger replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Fluid puddles under the vehicle, transmission slipping or hesitation
        * Solution: Repair or replace leaking seals or gaskets
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front Suspension:**
    * **Lower Control Arm Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, misalignment
        * Solution: Lower control arm replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight housing
    * Solution: Headlight replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V808

**Interior**
* **Infotainment System Glitch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes, system crashes
    * Solution: Software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if under warranty
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Volvo V90 Cross Country has experienced several issues, including turbocharger failures, transmission fluid leaks, and suspension problems. While these issues are not widespread and may not affect all models, they can be costly to repair. Overall, the V90 Cross Country remains a reliable vehicle with a comfortable interior and good performance. However, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing.
