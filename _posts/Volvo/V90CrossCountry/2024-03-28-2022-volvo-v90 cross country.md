**Introduction:**

The 2022 Volvo V90 Cross Country is a stylish and versatile luxury station wagon that combines rugged off-road capabilities with premium features. It is powered by a standard 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The V90 CC also offers an optional 2.0-liter plug-in hybrid powertrain that combines an electric motor with the gasoline engine for a combined output of 455 horsepower and 523 lb-ft of torque.

Some of the highly praised features of the V90 Cross Country include its spacious and well-appointed interior, comfortable ride quality, and advanced safety technologies. However, some owners have reported certain issues with the vehicle.

### **Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine**
   **Fuel Injector Failure:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, loss of power
      - Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Coolant Leak:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Visible coolant dripping from engine compartment, overheating
      - Solution: Repair or replace faulty coolant hoses or gaskets
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Turbocharger Lag:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delayed response when accelerating
      - Solution: Software update or turbocharger replacement
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,500+

### **Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
   **Shifting Delays:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Jerky or delayed shifting
      - Solution: Software update or transmission overhau
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

### **Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sags on one or more corners, reduced ride height
      - Solution: Replace faulty compressor
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise over bumps
      - Solution: Replace faulty strut mount bearings
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

### **Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting
      - Solution: Diagnose and repair parasitic drain
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Infotainment System Glitch:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Screen unresponsive, random reboots, connectivity issues
      - Solution: Software update or infotainment system replacement
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

### **Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight units
      - Solution: Replacement of affected headlights
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Plastic Trim Discoloration:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Exterior plastic trim fades or stains
      - Solution: Replace affected trim pieces
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

### **Interior**

* **Panoramic Moonroof Creaking:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Loud creaking noise when moonroof is open or closed
      - Solution: Lubrication or replacement of moonroof components
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Rear Seat Latch Failure:**
      - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging or disengaging rear seat latches
      - Solution: Replace faulty latches
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+

### **Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Volvo V90 Cross Country is a reliable and well-equipped vehicle. However, some owners have encountered issues related to the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. While some of these issues may be minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is advisable for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
