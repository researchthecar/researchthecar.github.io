**Introduction of the 2022 Volvo C40 Recharge**

The 2022 Volvo C40 Recharge is a fully electric compact luxury crossover SUV. It is powered by a single electric motor producing 402 horsepower and 486 lb-ft of torque. The C40 Recharge comes standard with a panoramic sunroof, a 12.3-inch touchscreen infotainment system, and a premium audio system.

**Electrical**

* **Software Glitches**
  * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, such as freezing, crashing, or slow performance.
  * Solution: Software updates can often resolve these issues.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0-$200+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported excessive battery drain when the vehicle is parked.
  * Solution: Volvo has released a software update to address this issue.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper motor may fail, causing the wipers to stop working.
  * Solution: Replacement of the wiper motor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400-$600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Rear Seat Squeaks**
  * Description/Symptoms: The rear seats may develop squeaks or rattles over time.
  * Solution: Lubricating the seat tracks and fasteners.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Volvo C40 Recharge is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some issues, particularly with software glitches and battery drain. These issues are often resolved with software updates. However, the windshield wiper motor failure and rear seat squeaks can be more costly to fix. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues are relatively minor.
