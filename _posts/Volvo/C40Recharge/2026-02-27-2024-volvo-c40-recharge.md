# 2024 Volvo C40 Recharge Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Volvo C40 Recharge is a stylish, all-electric crossover coupe offering robust performance and premium Scandinavian design. While generally well-regarded for its modern technology and electric capabilities, used-car buyers should review owner-reported feedback regarding advanced driver assistance features.

---

## Engine

### Single Motor Extended Range Electric Motor

A rear-mounted permanent magnet electric motor producing approximately 248 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. This single-motor configuration delivers efficient everyday power and solid driving range, representing a reliable and straightforward powertrain choice.

### Twin Motor Electric Powertrain

A dual-motor all-wheel-drive setup producing around 402 horsepower and 486 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and enhanced traction in adverse weather conditions with typical electric vehicle simplicity and durability.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

A fixed-ratio single-speed direct-drive transmission paired with both electric motor configurations. Because it lacks traditional gear shifting, it offers smooth, continuous power delivery and minimal mechanical complexity for long-term ownership.

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Automatic Braking

Reports indicate isolated incidents involving advanced collision avoidance and automated braking features acting unexpectedly.

**Unintended Brake Engagement or Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the automatic rear braking system engaged unexpectedly while backing up slowly, or where the vehicle experienced an unintended lunge forward during braking maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's sensor calibration and driver assistance software inspected by an authorized service center to ensure proper operation.
