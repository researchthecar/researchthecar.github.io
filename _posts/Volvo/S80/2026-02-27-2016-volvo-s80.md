### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Volvo S80 is a solid choice for those seeking a comfortable and safe luxury sedan with a generally good reliability record. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, owners should be aware of potential minor electrical issues, particularly with the infotainment system and the possibility of parasitic battery drain. These are not endemic to the model but can occur in complex vehicles. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring longevity. Overall, the S80 offers a dependable ownership experience, especially when compared to some of its more complex European rivals.
