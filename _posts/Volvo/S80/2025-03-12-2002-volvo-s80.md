The 2002 Volvo S80 is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its comfort and safety features. Engine options for this year include a 2.9L inline-6, a 2.9L twin-turbo inline-6, and a 2.4L light-pressure turbo inline-5. The 2.9L inline-6 engine produces 200 hp and 207 lb-ft of torque. What people like about the S80 is its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and reputation for safety. Common dislikes include its reliability issues, particularly with the electronic throttle module (ETM), and some of the engine management systems.

**Engine**

**2.9L Inline-6**

*   **Electronic Throttle Module (ETM) Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Erratic idle, reduced engine power, stalling, and a warning light on the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the ETM. Sometimes, the unit can be rebuilt.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coil Pack Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough running, and a check engine light with codes related to cylinder misfires.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty coil pack(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ (per coil pack, including labor)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**2.9L Twin-Turbo Inline-6**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noise from the engine, and potentially excessive oil consumption.
    *   Solution: Turbocharger replacement or rebuild.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Electronic Throttle Module (ETM) Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Erratic idle, reduced engine power, stalling, and a warning light on the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the ETM. Sometimes, the unit can be rebuilt.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**2.4L Light-Pressure Turbo Inline-5**

*   **Electronic Throttle Module (ETM) Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Erratic idle, reduced engine power, stalling, and a warning light on the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the ETM. Sometimes, the unit can be rebuilt.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Aisin Warner AW50-42LE 4-Speed Automatic**

*   **Slipping or Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping between gears, harsh or delayed shifting, and a possible check engine light.
    *   Solution: Fluid flush and filter replacement, or valve body replacement, or complete transmission rebuild/replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 (fluid flush) - $3500+ (rebuild/replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the front suspension, especially over bumps, and poor handling.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the lower control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per side
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Strut Mount Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or groaning noises from the suspension, especially when turning the steering wheel or going over bumps.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the strut mounts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+ per pair
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **ABS Module Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: ABS light on the dashboard, loss of ABS function, and potentially other related warning lights.
    *   Solution: ABS module repair or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Climate Control Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing cold air, heater not blowing hot air, or erratic temperature control. Could be a failing blend door motor.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of the climate control system and repair or replacement of the faulty components (e.g., blend door motor, compressor, etc.).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+ (depending on the issue)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Headlight Lens Clouding/Yellowing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reduced headlight brightness and visibility due to oxidation of the plastic lens.
    *   Solution: Headlight lens restoration or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+ (restoration) or $200 - $500+ (replacement) per headlight
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof getting stuck, making noise, or failing to open or close properly.
    *   Solution: Inspection of the sunroof mechanism, cleaning and lubrication, or repair/replacement of faulty parts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Volvo S80 is a comfortable car, but owners should be aware of potential issues. The most common problems revolve around the electronic throttle module (ETM), transmission, and suspension components. The ETM issues can cause significant drivability problems. Repair costs can range from a few hundred dollars for minor fixes to several thousand for major component replacements. The ETM and ABS module failures are the most severe and most common issues.
