**Introduction:**

The 2008 Volvo S80 is a luxury sedan renowned for its safety features, refined interior, and responsive handling. It's available with various engine and transmission options, including a 3.2L inline-6 and a 4.4L V8, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Despite its overall reliability, the S80 has faced some specific issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.2L Inline-6**

* **Intake Manifold Runner Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, stalling
    * Solution: Replacement of intake manifold runner control actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**4.4L V8**

* **Timing Belt Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, reduced performance
    * Solution: Replacement of timing belt tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* **Air Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, whistling noise during acceleration
    * Solution: Replacement of air pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Valve Body Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed shifting, slipping gears, harsh shifting
    * Solution: Replacement or rebuild of valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
* **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No shifting, transmission fault codes
    * Solution: Replacement of transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise when turning or over bumps
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mount bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly
    * Solution: Replacement of battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Replacement of sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control, blower issues
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+

**Summary:**

The 2008 Volvo S80 has some reported issues, including engine component failures, transmission problems, and electrical glitches. While the severity of these issues varies, they can be costly to repair, especially for the transmission and climate control system. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider regular maintenance and repairs to mitigate any major expenses.
