# 2013 Volvo V60

### Overview
The 2013 Volvo V60, a compact luxury wagon, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, particularly known for its safety features and comfortable ride. It offers a range of powertrain options, including efficient turbocharged gasoline engines and a potent diesel variant in some markets. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, the V60 has specific areas that have seen more attention from repair shops. Common concerns tend to revolve around certain electronic components and, depending on the engine, emissions-related systems. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of the V60.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L GTDi Turbocharged I4 (T5)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection, repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**PCV System Clogging**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks, rough idle, engine misfires, smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) system.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


### 3.0L T6 Turbocharged I6
**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Thermostat)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking coolant hoses or thermostat housing.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


### 2.4L D5 Turbocharged Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, rough idling.
- **Solution:** DPF regeneration cycle, cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, poor performance, increased emissions, rough idle.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (Geartronic)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough shifting, warning lights, low transmission fluid level.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of seals, lines, or transmission pan; fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or replacement of head unit components.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,500

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Sensor Failures (ABS/Wheel Speed)**
- **Symptoms:** ABS warning light, traction control light, inaccurate speedometer.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ABS or wheel speed sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, damp carpets, potential mold growth.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Volvo V60 is a solid choice for those seeking a safe and comfortable compact wagon. While generally reliable, potential owners should be aware of specific issues that can arise, particularly concerning the turbocharger and PCV system on gasoline models, and DPF/EGR issues on diesel variants. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are also noted. Transmission issues are less common but can be costly if they occur. With diligent maintenance and attention to these potential problem areas, the V60 can provide many years of dependable service.
