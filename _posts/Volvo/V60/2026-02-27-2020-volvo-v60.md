# 2020 Volvo V60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Volvo V60 blends Scandinavian luxury, practical wagon utility, and refined turbocharged performance. For used-car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and monitoring potential owner-reported electrical or driver assistance quirks can help ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (T5)

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive models. It offers a balanced mix of efficiency and responsive daily power.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged 4-Cylinder (T6)

A dual-charged 2.0-liter four-cylinder generating approximately 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with all-wheel drive. This option delivers brisk acceleration, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history due to its complex forced-induction system.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Plug-in Hybrid (T8)

A high-output plug-in hybrid powertrain combining the dual-charged four-cylinder engine with an electric motor to produce around 415 horsepower. It provides strong performance and electric-only driving range, though complexity is higher.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed Geartronic automatic transmission paired across all trims and drivetrains. It generally delivers smooth gear shifts and appropriate gearing for efficiency and performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V144000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Automatic Emergency Braking](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software/hardware incompatibility, the Automatic Emergency Brake (AEB) system may not detect obstacles and engage as intended. If the automatic emergency braking system does not engage as intended, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will update the Active Safety Domain Master (ASDM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V414000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the low-pressure fuel pump can cause a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Volvo V60 is a stylish and capable luxury wagon with sophisticated powertrain choices. While overall mechanical complaints remain minimal, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical systems and advanced safety features during a test drive.
