# 2022 Volvo V60

### Overview
The 2022 Volvo V60 is a premium compact wagon known for its sophisticated design, advanced safety features, and comfortable interior. It offers a range of powertrain options, including efficient mild-hybrid systems. ### Infotainment System
**System Freezes/Lag**

**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, "12V battery low" warnings, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Testing and replacement of the 12V battery. Investigating parasitic draws from electrical systems.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Volvo V60 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The standard 2.0L turbocharged engines and the 8-speed automatic transmission have shown no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit experiencing occasional freezes or lag, and the 12V battery showing potential for premature drain. Plug-in hybrid models may encounter specific hybrid system warnings. Exterior and interior components are generally robust. With proactive maintenance and attention to potential electrical quirks, the V60 should provide a dependable ownership experience.
