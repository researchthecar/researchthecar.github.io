# 2022 Volvo V60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo V60 delivers a sophisticated blend of Scandinavian design, practical wagon utility, and refined turbocharged or plug-in hybrid powertrains. As a contemporary luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate both powertrain performance and overall electronics reliability when shopping on the used market.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B5)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates around 247 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth power delivery and competitive efficiency. It serves as a solid daily driver option for buyers seeking balanced performance.

### 2.0L Turbocharged and Supercharged Plug-In Hybrid (T8 Recharge)

Pairing a forced-induction four-cylinder engine with an electric motor, the T8 Recharge produces over 450 horsepower while offering electric-only driving range. It provides thrilling acceleration but requires thorough verification of charging hardware and battery health on used models.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Geartronic Automatic Transmission

This conventional 8-speed automatic transmission works across various trim levels to deliver smooth, prompt gear changes. It generally proves dependable when paired with regular fluid maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V682000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V179000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module when the battery is fully charged and the vehicle is parked. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the high-voltage battery module as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volvo V60 remains an appealing and stylish choice for used luxury wagon shoppers, featuring robust powertrain options and premium features. Buyers should ensure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is performed, with particular attention paid to the vehicle's advanced electrical and hybrid systems where applicable.
