# 2023 Volvo V60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Volvo V60 brings sleek Scandinavian styling, advanced safety features, and electrified powertrain options to the luxury wagon segment. As a relatively fresh model year with limited owner complaints, it generally presents a modern and sophisticated option for used car buyers seeking both utility and performance.

---

## Engine

### B4/B5 2.0L Turbocharged Mild-Hybrid 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter turbocharged mild-hybrid four-cylinder engine produces approximately 247 horsepower in B5 configurations, balancing everyday efficiency with smooth power delivery. It serves as a modern baseline powerplant for the V60 lineup.

### T8 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged Plug-In Hybrid

Combining forced induction with an electric motor, the T8 plug-in hybrid delivers a robust output of around 455 total horsepower alongside strong electric-only driving range. It offers brisk acceleration and advanced efficiency for buyers wanting dual-nature performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Geartronic Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed traditional automatic transmission pairs with all-wheel-drive variants to deliver seamless and efficient gear shifts. It matches well with both mild-hybrid and plug-in hybrid setups for refined daily driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V793000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may lose drive power as the high voltage battery is depleted, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V012000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A diagnostic error in the Brake Control Module 2 (BCM2) may cause a loss of Antilock Brake System (ABS), Electronic Stability Control (ESC), and traction control. A reduction in brake support functions can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V908000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2023)**

The 2023 Volvo V60 remains a compelling choice for luxury wagon enthusiasts, supported by sophisticated powertrains and advanced technology.
