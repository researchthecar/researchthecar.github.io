# 2016 Volvo V60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Volvo V60, buyers will find a stylish and practical wagon that delivers Scandinavian luxury alongside spirited driving dynamics. While it offers a refined ride and strong safety credentials, potential owners should review common owner-reported issues regarding engine oil consumption, electrical glitches, and occasional body trim concerns. Thoroughly checking maintenance records and verifying the vehicle's powertrain health will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine provides a strong balance of efficiency and power for daily driving. Buyers should inspect this engine closely for oil consumption habits and proper maintenance history.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Low oil level warnings appearing shortly after an oil change, sometimes linked to worn piston rings.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly between changes and consult a qualified mechanic or dealer to inspect piston rings and seals.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent engine stalling without prior warning, particularly when maneuvering at low speeds or pulling out of a driveway.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose the fuel delivery, ignition, and engine management systems for fault codes.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six (or V6 configuration depending on specific model year trims) delivers robust performance and smooth acceleration. It is generally reliable when maintained with timely fluid and filter changes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides quick and smooth gear changes that complement the vehicle's performance. Routine fluid checks ensure long-term operational health.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

The electrical architecture supports advanced comfort and convenience features, though occasional software glitches can affect center display functionality.

**Continuous Infotainment Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment screen continuously reboots and fails to load completely, rendering climate and audio controls temporarily inaccessible.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a dealership or service center, or inspect system fuses and power modules.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior panels and glass elements provide a sleek aesthetic, but rare instances of spontaneous sunroof shattering have been reported by owners.

**Sudden Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud popping sound resembling a gunshot followed by the sunroof glass suddenly cracking or shattering while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress fractures and replace the assembly with factory-approved components if damaged.

### Rearview Mirror

The cabin features premium materials and ergonomic seating, though minor interior trim or mirror adjustments may occasionally require attention.

**Mirror Glass Migration**
- **Symptoms:** The interior rearview mirror glass slowly drifts downward over time due to vehicle vibrations, requiring frequent manual readjustment.
- **Solution:** Replace the rearview mirror assembly if the internal adjustment mechanism fails to hold its position.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V798000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages" and number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." If the seat belt buckle separates from the bracket, the front seat passenger may not be adequately restrained in the event of a crash, increasing their risk of.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front passenger seat belt buckle stud, replacing the seat belt buckle as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V555000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the vehicle is unexpectedly unable to be started in the middle of traffic, such as when stopped at an intersection, there is an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the existing starter motor fuse with a higher amperage fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V378000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The flexible steel cable that connects the seat belt to the front outboard seating positions may fatigue over time, causing the seat belt to not secure the occupant in the event of a crash. An unsecured occupant has an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace the seat belt anchor cable for both front seats. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V849000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=V60&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A door that is not securely latched could open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of injury to a vehicle occupant.
- **What to do:** Volvo will notify owners, and dealers will replace all four door latches, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Volvo V60 remains a compelling choice for used car buyers seeking versatility, comfort, and safety. While most systems prove dependable, prospective owners should perform a detailed pre-purchase inspection focusing on engine oil levels, electrical responsiveness, and glass integrity. Verifying a complete service history will significantly minimize potential ownership surprises.
