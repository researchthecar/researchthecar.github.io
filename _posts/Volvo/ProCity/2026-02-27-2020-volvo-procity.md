### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Volvo ProCity demonstrates a high level of reliability, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. Major mechanical failures are uncommon, making it a dependable choice for commercial operations. The primary area requiring attention is the electrical system, specifically the Body Control Module, which can occasionally present intermittent faults. Fleet managers can expect consistent performance with routine maintenance, and proactive diagnosis of any electrical anomalies will further enhance the ProCity's already strong reliability record. Overall, it is a well-built vehicle designed for demanding urban duty cycles.
