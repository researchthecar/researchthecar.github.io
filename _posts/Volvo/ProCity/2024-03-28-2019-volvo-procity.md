**Introduction**

The 2019 Volvo ProCity is a compact crossover SUV featuring a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission, producing 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It is known for its sleek design, luxurious interior, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported certain issues with the vehicle, categorized as follows:

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Valve Sticking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, and reduced engine performance
  * Solution: Replace the intake manifold valve
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, misfiring, and possible stalling
  * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping during gear changes, transmission shudder
  * Solution: Replace the shift solenoid
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises when driving over bumps, excessive tire wear
  * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
  * Solution: Check for parasitic battery drain, replace the battery if necessary
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Dimmer Switch Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Headlights only operate on high beam, dimmer switch becomes stuck
  * Solution: Replace the dimmer switch
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging**
  * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the vehicle's cabin during heavy rain
  * Solution: Clean or replace the sunroof drain plugs
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Storage Bin Hinge Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Hinge breaks, causing the bin to fall out
  * Solution: Replace the center console storage bin
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Volvo ProCity offers impressive performance and features, potential issues related to the engine, transmission, and suspension can affect its overall reliability. The severity of these issues varies, with some requiring expensive repairs. It's crucial for owners to be aware of these potential problems and consult with a reputable mechanic if any symptoms arise.
