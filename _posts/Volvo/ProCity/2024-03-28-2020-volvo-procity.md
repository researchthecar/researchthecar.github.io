**Introduction**

The 2020 Volvo ProCity is a compact SUV that combines style, performance, and practicality. It offers a choice of two turbocharged engines mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The T4 model produces 187 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, while the T5 delivers 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The ProCity is well-regarded for its comfortable interior, advanced safety features, and responsive handling, but it has also faced criticism for its limited cargo space and some reliability issues.

**Engine**

**T4**

* **Fuel injector issues:** Symptoms include engine misfiring, rough idling, and poor fuel economy. Solution involves replacing the affected fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Symptoms include coolant leaks and engine overheating. Solution requires replacing the intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**T5**

* **Turbocharger failure:** Symptoms include decreased engine power, whistling noises, and blue smoke from the exhaust. Solution involves replacing the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

**8-speed automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting, shuddering, or hesitation during acceleration. Solution involves replacing or repairing the transmission components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount bearing failure:** Symptoms include clunking or knocking noises when going over bumps. Solution involves replacing the strut mount bearings. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Shock absorber failure:** Symptoms include a bouncy or unstable ride. Solution involves replacing the shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drainage:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle or dead battery. Solution involves diagnosing and repairing electrical system issues. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Alternator failure:** Symptoms include dim headlights, flickering dashboard lights, and difficulty charging the battery. Solution involves replacing the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuator failure:** Symptoms include difficulty locking or unlocking the doors. Solution involves replacing the door lock actuator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
* **Bumper cover damage:** Symptoms include cracks or dents in the bumper cover. Solution involves repairing or replacing the bumper cover. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system malfunction:** Symptoms include freezing, system crashes, or unresponsive touchscreens. Solution involves software updates or hardware replacements. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
* **Seat belt sensor malfunction:** Symptoms include incorrect seat belt warnings or airbag deployment issues. Solution involves diagnosing and repairing the seat belt system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Summary**

While the 2020 Volvo ProCity offers a comfortable and capable driving experience, it has been plagued by several reliability issues that can be costly to fix. Engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical failures are among the most common concerns reported by owners. These issues can range from minor inconveniences to major expenses, so it's important to be aware of them before purchasing a used or new ProCity.
