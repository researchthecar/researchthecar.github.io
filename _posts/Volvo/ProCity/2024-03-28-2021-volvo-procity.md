**Introduction:**

The 2021 Volvo ProCity is a versatile electric city car that offers impressive maneuverability, advanced technology, and a spacious interior. It is equipped with a single electric motor and a 40 kWh lithium-ion battery, delivering 168 horsepower and 206 lb-ft of torque. While it boasts several desirable features, the ProCity has faced some reported issues.

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Early reports suggest that some ProCities experience excessive battery drain while parked. Symptoms include a rapidly depleting battery even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Volvo has issued a software update to address this issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Covered under warranty
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:** Users have reported instances where the infotainment system freezes, becomes unresponsive, or displays errors.
    * Solution: A software reset or firmware update typically resolves the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if within warranty, otherwise $50 - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Failure:** Some ProCities have experienced premature failure of the windshield wipers. Symptoms include weak or inconsistent wiper performance.
    * Solution: Replacing the wiper motor or linkage assembly is usually necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Loose Dashboard Panel:** In certain models, the dashboard panel may become loose, causing rattling noises while driving.
    * Solution: Reattaching or replacing the loose panel resolves the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if within warranty, otherwise $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty Seatbelt Sensors:** The seatbelt sensors may occasionally malfunction, triggering false warnings or preventing the vehicle from starting.
    * Solution: Resetting the seatbelt tensioner or replacing the sensor assembly usually fixes the problem.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Volvo ProCity offers several desirable features, it has faced some reported issues related to battery drain, electrical malfunctions, and loose interior components. The severity of these issues varies, and some can be addressed under warranty. It is advisable for potential buyers to research these issues and address them with their dealer before purchasing the vehicle.
