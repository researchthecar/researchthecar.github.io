**Introduction:**

The 2017 Volvo VNL 300 is a heavy-duty semi-truck designed for long-haul transportation. It comes standard with a 13-liter Volvo D13 engine producing 475 horsepower and 1,650 lb-ft of torque. The VNL 300 offers two transmission options: an Eaton Fuller 13-speed manual transmission and a Volvo I-Shift 12-speed automated manual transmission.

Owners appreciate the VNL 300's comfortable interior, fuel efficiency, and reliable performance. However, some have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**D13 Engine**

* **EGR Cooler Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, reduced engine power, and elevated exhaust gas temperatures
    * Solution: Replace EGR cooler
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eaton Fuller 13-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shifting Difficulty:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Resistance or grinding when shifting gears
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace worn components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Volvo I-Shift 12-Speed Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Mechatronics Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fault codes, intermittent or complete loss of power
    * Solution: Replace mechatronics unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging, noise, and instability on the road
    * Solution: Replace broken leaf spring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dead batteries
    * Solution: Inspect and repair electrical system for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlights or inspect electrical connections
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Display Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, blank display, or intermittent malfunction
    * Solution: Update software or replace dashboard display
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not functioning properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace HVAC components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Volvo VNL 300 has a mixed reputation. While it is praised for its comfort and performance, some owners have experienced costly issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues can vary depending on the specific components affected and the frequency of use. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can help mitigate potential problems and extend the lifespan of the vehicle.
