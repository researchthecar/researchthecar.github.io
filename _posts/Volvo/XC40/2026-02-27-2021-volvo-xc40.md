# 2021 Volvo XC40 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Volvo XC40 is a stylish and comfortable luxury subcompact SUV that appeals to buyers looking for Scandinavian design and advanced safety features. As a used vehicle purchase, it generally offers a refined driving experience, though prospective buyers should evaluate both the powertrain options and any reported owner concerns before finalizing a deal.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (T4 / T5)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers competitive horsepower and torque outputs tailored to front-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It provides responsive daily performance, though regular maintenance is essential to ensure long-term mechanical reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission handles gear shifting smoothly across various trims and drivetrain layouts. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V109000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Management System/Energy Control Module (Bms/Becm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A disconnected high voltage system can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Car will notify owners, and dealers will install a software update, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V682000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V881000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loose or missing bolt may decrease the structural protection of the vehicle, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the cross-member bracket and install new bracket bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V908000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

Overall, the 2021 Volvo XC40 remains an attractive option in the luxury subcompact segment, balancing comfort, styling, and everyday usability. Used car buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of the powertrain and vehicle electronics before buying.
