# 2022 Volvo XC40 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo XC40 is a stylish and premium compact luxury SUV offering modern tech, comfortable seating, and turbocharged performance. While it generally delivers a refined driving experience, used-car buyers should pay close attention to potential electrical and software-related glitches, particularly concerning driver-assistance features and rearview systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers strong power and smooth acceleration suitable for daily commuting and highway driving. Overall mechanical reliability is generally solid, though occasional electronic or software-related powertrain hiccups have been reported by owners.

**Unexpected Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences complete electrical or motive power failure while idling, turning, or driving, requiring a tow.
- **Solution:** Dealer diagnosis and software updates or electrical system inspections may be required to resolve underlying faults.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The eight-speed automatic transmission provides smooth, responsive gear shifts that complement the turbocharged engine's power delivery. No major recurring mechanical defects have been widely reported for this gearbox.

## Suspension

### Front Strut Assemblies

The suspension system offers a well-balanced ride quality between comfort and agility. However, early wear on front strut components has been documented in rare instances.

**Strut Upper Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Audible noises or clunking sounds originating from the front end during steering and suspension movement.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed front strut upper bearings and affected struts.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electronic power steering system provides precise handling and comfortable maneuverability. Occasional software glitches have temporarily disrupted assistance for some drivers.

**Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance while driving, making the steering wheel significantly heavier to turn.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to verify software calibrations and inspect steering control modules.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Advanced safety systems provide strong protection and peace of mind, though backup camera software bugs have frustrated several owners.

**Backup Camera Unavailable**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera fails to activate when shifting into reverse, displaying a 'Camera temporarily unavailable' message.
- **Solution:** Apply factory software updates or visit a dealer for camera system recalibration.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior styling and glass panels are generally durable, though a small number of owners have experienced spontaneous sunroof glass fractures.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Large, linear cracks form across the panoramic sunroof glass panel without any direct external impact or debris strike.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel under warranty or insurance coverage.

## Safety

### Forward Collision

Active collision mitigation features enhance daily driving safety, but phantom braking events remain a recognized owner complaint.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system engages abruptly without any apparent obstacles or hazards present.
- **Solution:** Have the collision avoidance sensors and software inspected and recalibrated by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V109000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Management System/Energy Control Module (Bms/Becm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A disconnected high voltage system can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Car will notify owners, and dealers will install a software update, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volvo XC40 is a compelling choice for used compact luxury SUV shoppers due to its upscale cabin, refined driving dynamics, and strong safety suite. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the vehicle's service history for software updates addressing safety systems and backup cameras, and inspect the panoramic roof glass and front suspension during a pre-purchase test drive.
