# 2022 Volvo XC40 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Volvo XC40 brings distinctive Scandinavian styling, a comfortable cabin, and confident driving dynamics to the subcompact luxury SUV segment. As a used option, it offers modern tech and safety features, though prospective buyers should evaluate how well individual powertrain variants and standard electronics suit their daily driving needs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (B4 / T4)

The entry-level 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 184 to 187 horsepower, offering smooth power delivery for daily commuting. It serves as a balanced option for buyers prioritizing everyday efficiency.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Mild-Hybrid 4-Cylinder (B5 / T5)

The higher-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates roughly 247 to 248 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration and standard all-wheel-drive capability. This option suits drivers looking for more robust performance while maintaining typical luxury crossover drivability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed traditional automatic transmission manages power delivery across the XC40 lineup with smooth, prompt gear shifts. It pairs well with both engine configurations for highway cruising and city navigation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V109000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Management System/Energy Control Module (Bms/Becm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A disconnected high voltage system can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volvo Car will notify owners, and dealers will install a software update, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V282000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V908000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Volvo&model=XC40&modelYear=2022)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Volvo XC40 remains an attractive choice in the used luxury subcompact SUV market, thanks to its refined interior and strong safety pedigree. While powertrain options are generally dependable based on standard manufacturer engineering, thorough test driving and pre-purchase inspections are always recommended to verify the health of specific mechanical and electrical systems.
