## 2019 Volvo VAH 300

The 2019 Volvo VAH 300 is a compact SUV that offers a blend of style, practicality, and performance. It comes with two engine options, a turbocharged 2.0-liter T5 engine and a supercharged and turbocharged 2.0-liter T6 engine. The T5 engine produces 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, while the T6 engine delivers 316 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The VAH 300 has a spacious and well-appointed interior, along with a range of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter T5**

    * **Cracked pistons**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, decreased engine power, knocking sounds
        * Solution: Replace pistons and piston rings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake valve deposits**
        * Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, decreased fuel economy
        * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0-liter T6**

    * **Timing belt failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Catastrophic engine damage
        * Solution: Replace timing belt
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic**

    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in shifting, rough shifts
        * Solution: Reprogram transmission control module, replace transmission fluid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

    * Description/Symptoms: A crack in the windshield
    * Solution: Replace windshield

* **No Issues Found**

**Summary**

The 2019 Volvo VAH 300 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it does have some potential issues to be aware of. The most severe and expensive issue is the potential for timing belt failure on the 2.0-liter T6 engine. Other issues, such as cracked pistons and a faulty alternator, are less common but can still be costly to fix. Overall, the VAH 300 is a solid choice for those looking for a compact SUV with a premium feel and a range of advanced features.
