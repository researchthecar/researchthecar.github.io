# 2012 Toyota 4Runner

### Overview
The 2012 Toyota 4Runner is a robust mid-size SUV known for its off-road prowess and legendary Toyota reliability. This generation, particularly the 2012 model year, generally exhibits strong mechanical integrity. Owners often praise its durable powertrain and solid construction, making it a popular choice for those seeking a dependable vehicle for both daily driving and adventurous excursions. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 4Runner typically requires only routine maintenance, with major component failures being relatively uncommon. This data focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related concerns that have been reported by owners and technicians.

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Toyota 4Runner stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) demonstrating exceptional durability. The primary area where owners might encounter specific issues is with the optional adjustable air suspension system, which can develop leaks over time. Beyond this, the 4Runner is known for its robust build quality, making it a sound investment for those prioritizing longevity and minimal unexpected repairs. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its excellent track record.
