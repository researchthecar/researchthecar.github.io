**Introduction**

The 1993 Toyota 4Runner is a mid-size SUV available in both two-door and four-door configurations. It is powered by a variety of engines, including the 2.4L 22R-E inline-four, the 3.0L 3VZ-E V6, and the 3.0L 5VZ-FE V6. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual, a 4-speed automatic, and a 5-speed automatic. The 4Runner is known for its off-road capabilities, reliability, and spacious interior. However, it has also been known to experience a number of issues.

**Engine**

**2.4L 22R-E**

* **Blown head gasket**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust, overheating
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise from engine
    * Solution: Replace timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks of oil from engine
    * Solution: Replace leaking gaskets or seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L 3VZ-E**

* **Crankshaft position sensor failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, difficult starting, no start
    * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water pump failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating
    * Solution: Replace water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake manifold gasket leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, loss of coolant
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L 5VZ-FE**

* **Intake plenum gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leaks, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake plenum gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Throttle position sensor failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idle, lack of power
    * Solution: Replace throttle position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition coil failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace ignition coil
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding when shifting gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, noise when shifting
    * Solution: Replace worn gears or synchros
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Leaking transmission fluid**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks of transmission fluid under vehicle
    * Solution: Replace leaking seals or gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping gears**

    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **No reverse gear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle does not move in reverse
    * Solution: Replace reverse gear assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
