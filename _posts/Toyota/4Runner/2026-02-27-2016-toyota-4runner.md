# 2016 Toyota 4Runner Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Toyota 4Runner continues the nameplate's long-standing tradition of rugged body-on-frame capability and old-school mechanical durability. While its powertrain and mechanical assemblies generally hold up well over high mileage, used-car buyers should inspect specific trouble areas. Reviewing owner complaints reveals recurring issues concerning door lock actuators, exterior paint/clear coat degradation, and occasional suspension or electrical snags.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V6

The 4.0-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces 270 horsepower and 278 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as robust and dependable, forming the reliable core of the 4Runner's longevity.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic

The 5-speed ECT-i automatic transmission utilizes a traditional geared design paired with rear-wheel drive or selectable four-wheel-drive systems. Though older in design, it is generally dependable, with only isolated reports of shuddering.

## Suspension

### Suspension Struts and Springs

Suspension joints and optional hydraulic dampening systems may require component service or replacement around moderate mileage intervals.

**X-REAS System Leaks and Spring Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps or premature fluid leaking from front struts and rear shock absorbers equipped with the X-REAS sport enhancement system.
- **Solution:** Replace leaking shock absorbers or upgrade to standard aftermarket suspension components if the X-REAS system fails.

## Exterior

### Door Locks

Power accessory failures can affect convenience features like door locks over extended ownership.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door locks fail to secure certain doors (frequently passenger or driver sides) via keyless entry, sometimes failing to lock while still responding to unlock commands.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty door lock actuator assembly within the affected door.

### Paint and Clear Coat

Body paint integrity can degrade prematurely on select panels, particularly when exposed to prolonged sunlight.

**Premature Clear Coat Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Clear coat and paint begin to bubble, peel, or fail prematurely on upper horizontal surfaces such as the roof, hood, and tailgate.
- **Solution:** Professional repainting or clear coat refinishing of the affected body panels.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Toyota 4Runner remains an appealing choice for buyers seeking truck-based dependability and off-road capability. While mechanical major components are solidly built, prospective owners should thoroughly check for minor electrical inconveniences like failing door lock actuators and inspect exterior paint conditions for signs of clear coat peeling.
