# 2016 Toyota 4Runner

### Overview
The 2016 Toyota 4Runner is a body-on-frame SUV renowned for its ruggedness and off-road capability. It generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of Toyota vehicles. The powertrain, particularly the 4.0L V6 engine and its associated transmission, is a well-proven unit with a history of durability. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, there are a few areas that have seen occasional reports of issues. These tend to be minor and do not detract significantly from the overall dependable nature of the 4Runner. Maintenance remains crucial for longevity, but major component failures are uncommon.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Toyota 4Runner stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The robust 4.0L V6 and 5-speed automatic are known for their longevity and minimal issues. While minor electrical glitches with the infotainment system and occasional battery failures have been reported, these are not widespread and are generally manageable. The suspension, exterior, and interior components also demonstrate good durability. Overall, the 4Runner is a solid choice for those seeking a dependable SUV with excellent off-road prowess and a low likelihood of major mechanical problems.
