# 2021 Toyota 4Runner

### Overview
The 2021 Toyota 4Runner is a body-on-frame SUV renowned for its ruggedness and off-road capability. It continues to offer a proven, albeit aging, powertrain that prioritizes durability over cutting-edge fuel efficiency. Reliability is a strong suit for the 4Runner, with many owners reporting minimal issues outside of routine maintenance. The vehicle's robust construction and straightforward mechanicals contribute to its reputation for longevity. While some modern amenities might be lacking compared to competitors, the 4Runner's core engineering emphasizes dependability, making it a solid choice for those seeking a vehicle that can withstand demanding use and last for many years.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Toyota 4Runner stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly in its core mechanical components. The robust 4.0L V6 engine and the dependable automatic transmissions (both 5-speed and 6-speed) have a strong track record with minimal reported failures. Suspension systems, whether standard or TRD Pro specific, are generally durable. Electrical issues are infrequent, with the most common concern being minor infotainment system glitches that are often resolved with software updates. Exterior and interior components also exhibit good longevity, aligning with the 4Runner's reputation for toughness. Overall, the 2021 model continues this legacy, offering peace of mind for owners prioritizing long-term dependability and off-road resilience.
