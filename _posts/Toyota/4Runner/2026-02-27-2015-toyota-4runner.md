# 2015 Toyota 4Runner

### Overview
The 2015 Toyota 4Runner is a robust body-on-frame SUV renowned for its off-road capability and durability. It generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of Toyota's engineering. The powertrain, particularly the V6 engine and automatic transmission, is well-regarded for its longevity and ability to withstand demanding use. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that may require attention, providing insights into potential areas of concern and their associated repair costs.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Toyota 4Runner stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. The 4.0L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are known for their robustness and longevity, with no significant widespread issues reported. Suspension systems, whether standard or TRD-tuned, also hold up well under typical and off-road conditions. The primary area where minor issues have been noted is the electrical system, specifically with infotainment unit glitches, which are generally resolvable with software updates or, in rarer cases, replacement. Exterior and interior components are also generally durable. Overall, the 2015 4Runner is an excellent choice for those seeking a dependable SUV with strong resale value and minimal unexpected repair costs.
