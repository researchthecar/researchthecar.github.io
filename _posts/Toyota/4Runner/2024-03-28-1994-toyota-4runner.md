The 1994 Toyota 4Runner is a mid-size SUV that is known for its off-road capabilities. It is powered by a 3.0L V6 engine that produces 150 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque. The 4Runner is available with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers generally praise the 4Runner for its reliability, off-road performance, and versatility. However, there are some common issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:** This can cause coolant and oil leaks, as well as overheating. The solution is to replace the gasket. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000
    * **Timing belt failure:** This can cause catastrophic engine damage. The solution is to replace the timing belt and water pump at the recommended intervals. Estimated cost to fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * **Oil leaks:** These can be caused by a variety of issues, including a faulty oil pan gasket or a leaking valve cover. The solution is to replace the leaking component. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500+

**Transmission**

* **5-speed manual transmission**
    * **Clutch failure:** This can cause difficulty shifting gears or slipping out of gear. The solution is to replace the clutch. Estimated cost to fix: $800 - $1,200+
* **4-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Transmission slipping:** This can cause the transmission to lose power or shift erratically. The solution is to replace the transmission. Estimated cost to fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension:**
    * **Ball joint failure:** This can cause the front end of the vehicle to shake or wobble. The solution is to replace the ball joints. Estimated cost to fix: $400 - $600+
    * **Strut failure:** This can cause the front end of the vehicle to bounce or sag. The solution is to replace the struts. Estimated cost to fix: $600 - $1,000+
* **Rear suspension:**
    * **Leaf spring failure:** This can cause the rear end of the vehicle to sag or bounce. The solution is to replace the leaf springs. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $800+
    * **Shock absorber failure:** This can cause the rear end of the vehicle to bounce or bottom out. The solution is to replace the shock absorbers. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure:** This can cause the vehicle to not start or lose power. The solution is to replace the battery. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $200
* **Alternator failure:** This can cause the vehicle to lose power or the battery to not charge. The solution is to replace the alternator. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $800+
* **Starter failure:** This can cause the vehicle to not start. The solution is to replace the starter. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Rust:** This can occur on the body panels, frame, or undercarriage. The solution is to treat the rust with a rust inhibitor. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500+
* **Paint peeling:** This can occur on the body panels. The solution is to repaint the affected areas. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Dents:** These can occur from impact with objects. The solution is to have the dents repaired by a body shop. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking:** This can occur on the dashboard due to exposure to sunlight. The solution is to replace the dashboard. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $800+
* **Seat fabric tearing:** This can occur on the seats due to wear and tear. The solution is to repair or replace the seat fabric. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500+
* **HVAC system failure:** This can cause the heating or cooling system to not work properly. The solution is to repair or replace the affected components. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

Overall, the 1994 Toyota 4Runner is a reliable and capable vehicle. However, there are some common issues that owners should be aware of. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can be significant. It is important to have the vehicle inspected regularly by a qualified mechanic to identify and address any potential problems early on.
