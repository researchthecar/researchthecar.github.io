# 2017 Toyota 4Runner Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Toyota 4Runner remains a staple in the rugged, body-on-frame SUV market, praised for its durability, off-road capabilities, and strong resale value. For prospective used-car buyers, the vehicle generally maintains a reputation for mechanical resilience, though owners have reported a handful of isolated issues involving braking components, electrical wiring, and minor interior or body concerns.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V6

Equipped with a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter V6 producing approximately 270 horsepower and 278 lb-ft of torque, this proven powerplant is widely regarded as robust and long-lasting.

**Throttle Lag and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers occasionally experience a noticeable delay or dead pedal feeling when pressing the accelerator, along with rare instances of engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Consult a trusted mechanic or dealership to check for relevant technical service bulletins or software updates governing throttle response.

### Brake Master Cylinder and Pedal

Brake system complaints are uncommon relative to total sales, but any reports of soft pedals or master cylinder failure warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

**Soft Pedal or Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a soft brake pedal travel that sinks toward the floor, alongside rare failures of the brake master cylinder leading to reduced stopping power.
- **Solution:** Have the braking system, master cylinder, and brake fluid inspected immediately upon noticing any spongy pedal feel.

### Wiring Harness

Electrical components are generally reliable, though environmental factors like soy-based wiring materials can occasionally attract animal damage.

**Rodent Damage to Wiring**
- **Symptoms:** Main wiring harnesses and sensors may be targeted by rodents due to soy-based insulation, leading to electrical malfunctions and fault codes.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine bay wiring during routine maintenance and consider protective deterrents if parking in areas prone to rodents.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic

The traditional 5-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel drive or selectable four-wheel-drive systems, offering dependable, old-school mechanical simplicity despite trailing modern gear counts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V753000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=4Runner&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V831000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=4Runner&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An incorrect load information label can result in the operator overloading the vehicle and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V763000: Structure:Body:Hood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=4Runner&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the Billet Grille Overlay detaches while driving, it can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** SET will notify owners, and dealers will replace the securing U-nuts with upgraded hardware, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V295000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=4Runner&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A spare tire that is not inflated to the proper value may not perform as intended, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Gulf States will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the spare tires, adjusting their pressure as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V291000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=4Runner&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the roof rack fasteners loosen, the roof rack may detach from the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Gulf States will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, as necessary, tighten the roof rack fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Toyota 4Runner continues to be a solid choice for buyers seeking a tough, traditional SUV with enduring market value. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential concerns with brake master cylinders and throttle response, routine pre-purchase inspections can help uncover these issues before finalizing a purchase.
