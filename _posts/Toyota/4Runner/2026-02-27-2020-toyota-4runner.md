# 2020 Toyota 4Runner

### Overview
The 2020 Toyota 4Runner is a body-on-frame SUV renowned for its ruggedness and off-road capability. It generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of Toyota vehicles. The powertrain, while not the most modern, is well-proven and durable. Common areas of concern, if any, tend to be minor and related to specific components rather than widespread systemic failures. Owners generally report satisfaction with the vehicle's longevity and low cost of ownership, especially when considering its intended use for adventurous driving and off-road excursions. The 4Runner's robust construction contributes significantly to its positive reliability ratings.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Toyota 4Runner stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly for its segment. The powertrain is exceptionally durable, and most components are built to last. The few reported issues, such as minor infotainment glitches, are generally not critical and can often be resolved with software updates. Its robust construction and proven mechanicals make it a dependable choice for those seeking an off-road capable SUV with a long service life and minimal unexpected repair costs. The 4Runner's reputation for reliability is well-earned.
