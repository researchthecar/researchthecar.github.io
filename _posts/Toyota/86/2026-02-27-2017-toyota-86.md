# 2017 Toyota 86 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Toyota 86 is a lightweight, rear-wheel-drive sports coupe developed in partnership with Subaru, offering sharp handling and an engaging driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its mechanical layout and driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review available owner reports and inspect the vehicle thoroughly for any signs of prior wear or mechanical neglect.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Flat-4 Boxer Engine

This 2.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-four boxer engine produces approximately 205 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque when paired with the manual transmission. It provides a low center of gravity that enhances cornering agility, though proper maintenance history is essential for used buyers.

**Valve Spring Tension**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that low tension in the valve springs on cylinder two caused the vehicle to run improperly, requiring component replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine valve springs and replace affected components as needed based on diagnostic findings.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The conventional 6-speed manual transmission features short throws and a mechanical feel tailored for enthusiastic drivers. It generally offers solid reliability when standard fluid change intervals are maintained.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The 6-speed automatic transmission includes manual shift modes and steering-wheel paddle shifters for a more versatile driving character. It provides smooth daily commuting operation while retaining respectable responsiveness.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V295000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=86&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A spare tire that is not inflated to the proper value may not perform as intended, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Gulf States will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the spare tires, adjusting their pressure as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V920000: Electrical System:Ignition](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=86&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to conform to the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 114, "Theft Protection and Rollaway Prevention." If the key can be removed from the ignition when the transmission is not in the "Park" position, the vehicle could roll away increasing the risk of a crash and occupant or pedestrian injuries.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the ignition key/transmission interlock wiring connections, correcting them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Toyota 86 remains an appealing choice for driving enthusiasts seeking rear-wheel-drive balance and agile handling. Buyers should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the powertrain, suspension, and overall maintenance history to ensure long-term dependability.
