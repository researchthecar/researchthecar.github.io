# 2018 Toyota 86

### Overview
The 2018 Toyota 86, a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, particularly given its performance-oriented nature. It shares its platform with the Subaru BRZ, benefiting from Subaru's engineering prowess. The 86 is praised for its engaging driving dynamics, balanced chassis, and accessible performance. While it's not immune to the occasional minor issue, widespread critical failures are uncommon. Owners typically report satisfaction with its mechanical robustness, especially concerning the powertrain and chassis, which are designed to withstand spirited driving. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its long-term dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Toyota 86 demonstrates strong reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission showing minimal reported issues. The chassis and suspension are also robust. The most frequently cited minor concern relates to occasional infotainment system glitches, which are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the 86 is a dependable sports car for enthusiasts who perform routine maintenance. Its engineering, shared with Subaru, contributes to its solid reputation for durability in its class.
