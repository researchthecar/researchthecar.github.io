# 2019 Toyota 86

### Overview
The 2019 Toyota 86, a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, particularly given its performance-oriented nature. It shares its platform with the Subaru BRZ, benefiting from Subaru's engineering prowess. The 86 is praised for its engaging driving dynamics, balanced chassis, and accessible performance. While it's not immune to the occasional minor issue, widespread critical failures are uncommon. Owners typically report satisfaction with its mechanical robustness, especially concerning the powertrain and chassis, which are designed to withstand spirited driving. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its long-term dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Toyota 86 demonstrates strong overall reliability, with no significant widespread issues reported across its primary mechanical and electrical systems. Both the 2.0L naturally aspirated engine and its associated manual and automatic transmissions are considered robust. The chassis and suspension components are also generally dependable. Minor interior or exterior trim issues are rare and not indicative of systemic problems. Owners can expect a reliable sports car experience, provided routine maintenance is performed. The 86 is a solid choice for those seeking an engaging and dependable driver's car.
