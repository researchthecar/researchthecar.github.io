# 2019 Toyota 86 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Toyota 86 offers engaging, lightweight rear-wheel-drive handling packaged in an affordable sports coupe. While many owners enjoy its mechanical agility, used car buyers should carefully review service history and pre-purchase inspection reports to spot any underlying mechanical or electrical vulnerabilities.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Naturally Aspirated Boxer 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated flat-four engine produces approximately 205 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque (when paired with the manual transmission). It delivers nimble performance, though buyers should watch for rare reports of internal knocking or sudden power loss.

**Engine Failure and Tapping Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported hearing an unusual tapping sound coming from the engine compartment, followed by a sudden loss of motive power and engine failure while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine internals and replace damaged components or the engine assembly if catastrophic failure occurs.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual gearbox provides direct engagement for driving enthusiasts. It generally offers solid mechanical reliability when maintained properly.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The 6-speed automatic transmission features steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters for a sportier feel. It provides smooth daily driving manners and stable long-term durability.

## Interior

### Wiring and Dashboard Electronics

Electrical system concerns are infrequent, but cabin water leaks can create hazardous shorts behind the dashboard.

**Dashboard Water Leaks and Electrical Short**
- **Symptoms:** Water may leak from behind the dashboard onto the driver's feet, accompanied by flickering dash lights and potential electrical burning risks.
- **Solution:** Inspect cowl seals and windshield gaskets for leaks, and check behind the steering wheel and dash for damaged or shorted wiring harnesses.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

The safety equipment performs reliably overall, though dashboard warning lights require prompt diagnostic attention.

**SRS Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** The Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) warning light remains illuminated continuously on the dashboard upon starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the airbag control module and seat sensors using an OBD-II scanner to identify faulty wiring or sensor components requiring replacement.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V587000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=86&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the low-pressure fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Toyota 86 remains an exciting and dynamic choice for sports car buyers on a budget. Prospective owners should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the engine, electronics, and undercarriage condition to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
