**Introduction**

The 2022 Toyota Camry is a popular midsize sedan known for its reliability, comfort, and fuel efficiency. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including a 2.5-liter four-cylinder, a 3.5-liter V6, and a hybrid powertrain. The Camry received praise for its spacious interior, advanced safety features, and smooth ride. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Loud Valve Train Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine makes excessive ticking or tapping noises, especially when cold.
    * Solution: Replace noisy lifters or adjust valve clearances.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Engine Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings or other internal components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.5-liter V6**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine chain may become loose, causing rattling noises or reduced power.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission:**

* **Hesitation or Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking noises from the rear suspension.
    * Solution: Replace worn sway bar bushings or end links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, especially when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Identify and repair electrical components causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Scratches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windshield develops fine scratches or abrasions.
    * Solution: Replace or repair windshield.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating, ventilation, or air conditioning system does not function properly.
    * Solution: Replace faulty components such as blower motor, thermostat, or control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof makes a rattling noise when closed or open.
    * Solution: Adjust or lubricate sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Toyota Camry is a reliable vehicle with relatively few reported issues. However, some owners have experienced problems with the engine, transmission, suspension, and other components. The cost of repairs varies depending on the severity of the issue and the affected part. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a Camry.
