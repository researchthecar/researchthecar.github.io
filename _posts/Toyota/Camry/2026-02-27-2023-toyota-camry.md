# 2023 Toyota Camry Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Toyota Camry maintains the model's strong reputation for everyday dependability, fuel efficiency, and practical comfort. While the vast majority of owners experience trouble-free driving, a small number of driver reports submitted to federal safety regulators highlight isolated concerns across the electrical, powertrain, and braking systems. Prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, but overall, this model year remains a solid and dependable choice in the midsize sedan market.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

This naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 203 to 206 horsepower and offers smooth power delivery alongside excellent fuel economy for daily commuting. It is widely considered a durable and low-maintenance powerplant.

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The robust 3.5-liter V6 engine delivers around 301 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration and refined highway cruising performance. This engine has a long-standing track record of mechanical sturdiness when properly maintained.

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Powertrain

Pairing a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with an electric motor system, this hybrid setup maximizes fuel efficiency while delivering responsive low-end torque. It is a proven, highly reliable system in Toyota's lineup.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 8-speed automatic transmission handles power for non-hybrid models, delivering smooth shifts under normal driving conditions with occasional owner-reported quirks.

**Transmission Shudder and Rough Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unusual whining or whistling noises, acceleration shudder, and delayed or rough gear shifts during operation.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the transmission control module, fluid condition, and overall drivetrain operation.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (Hybrid)

The electronically controlled planetary continuously variable transmission manages power seamlessly in hybrid trims, prioritizing efficiency and smooth acceleration.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Provides reliable stopping power supported by an anti-lock braking system and electronic parking brake features.

**Brake Vibration and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience steering wheel vibration when braking from highway speeds, along with clicking sounds from the anti-lock brake system.
- **Solution:** Inspect front brake rotors for wear or warping, and have the brake pads and ABS components evaluated.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

Controls cabin audio, navigation, and vehicle settings through a central touchscreen interface.

**Screen Freezing and Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display and backup camera interface may intermittently freeze, go blank, or spontaneously reboot while driving.
- **Solution:** Check for dealership software updates or have the infotainment unit inspected if symptoms persist.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V432000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Camry&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A wheel that detaches from a moving vehicle can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the lug nuts, and replace any damaged components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V128000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Camry&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 202, "Head Restraints." A head restraint bracket that is insufficiently welded may not properly restrain an occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the 60% rear seat-back frame sub-assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Toyota Camry stands out as a dependable, fuel-efficient used car with an exceptional overall reliability record. While isolated owner complaints point to minor infotainment glitches and occasional transmission hesitation, these issues are not widespread enough to overshadow the vehicle's inherent strengths. Buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes and electronics to ensure everything operates smoothly.
