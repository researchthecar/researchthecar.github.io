# 2021 Toyota Camry Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Toyota Camry continues to hold its reputation as a dependable, highly efficient midsize sedan suited for daily commuting. While overall owner satisfaction remains high and mechanical trouble spots are relatively rare, used car buyers should pay attention to a few recurring owner complaints regarding the transmission, parking brake operation, and panoramic sunroof mechanisms.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder

A 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing around 203 horsepower, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. This standard engine provides a strong balance of everyday acceleration and excellent fuel economy for commuters.

### 3.5L V6

A smooth 3.5-liter V6 engine generating 301 horsepower, available on higher trims like the XLE and XSE. It offers spirited performance and robust power delivery with strong long-term durability.

### 2.5L Hybrid

A 2.5-liter four-cylinder hybrid powertrain coupled with an electronically controlled CVT. This setup prioritizes maximum fuel efficiency while delivering seamless power delivery for city and highway driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission used across standard 4-cylinder and V6 models. While generally robust, some owners have reported shifting hesitations or unusual mechanical noises.

**Transmission Hesitation and Whining Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report occasional hesitation or gear lag when shifting between 1st and 2nd gear, as well as a noticeable whining noise that increases in volume as vehicle speed rises above 20 mph.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission fluid levels, update control modules if applicable, or check internal components for abnormal wear.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Parking Brake

Braking systems generally perform reliably, though a subset of owners have reported unexpected movement while the electronic parking brake is set.

**Parking Brake Holding Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may roll or move while parked on an incline even when the parking brake has been engaged.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic parking brake actuator, cabling, and rear brake pads, and adjust or replace components as necessary.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior lighting and body panels are mostly durable, but equipped models with the panoramic sunroof have experienced mechanical sticking issues.

**Sunroof Jamming or Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof can become stuck in the vent or open position, sometimes accompanied by clicking or popping noises from the motor.
- **Solution:** Clear track debris, lubricate seals, or replace the sunroof motor and track assembly if mechanical binding has occurred.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Standard safety equipment is comprehensive, though buyers should verify that all active safety recalls have been successfully resolved.

**SRS Warning Lights and Recall Part Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning lights on the dashboard or notices regarding incomplete airbag safety recalls with delayed remedy parts.
- **Solution:** Check the vehicle identification number (VIN) against official recall databases and have authorized dealerships perform necessary campaign repairs once parts are available.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Camry&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Toyota Camry remains an exceptionally strong contender in the used midsize sedan market, offering comfortable ride quality, multiple dependable engine choices, and great fuel economy. While prospective buyers should thoroughly check for transmission smoothness, test the operation of the panoramic sunroof, and verify that all open safety recalls have been addressed, it stands out as a sensible and reliable purchase.
