# 2022 Toyota Camry Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Toyota Camry maintains a solid reputation for everyday usability, fuel efficiency, and dependable daily transportation. Prospective buyers should note that while mechanical components generally hold up well, owner reports highlight a few recurring electronic and mechanical glitches to watch out for during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 203 horsepower and delivers dependable performance with strong fuel economy for daily driving.

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The available 3.5-liter V6 engine offers a robust 301 horsepower, providing smooth, authoritative acceleration for drivers seeking more power.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with both the four-cylinder and V6 engines, providing smooth gear selection under normal operating conditions.

**Transmission Noise and Gear Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience loud gear grinding noises during transitions, hesitation upon acceleration, or humming sounds at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect transmission fluid condition and software calibration; some severe cases require component replacement.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides confident stopping power, but owners have reported isolated master cylinder and booster concerns.

**Brake Pedal Sinking and Booster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may slowly sink while held down under pressure, or the brake booster may fail, making it difficult to stop the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect the master cylinder and brake booster immediately for internal leaks or mechanical failure and replace faulty components.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

The electrical system powers advanced lighting and infotainment features, though sporadic lighting and screen concerns have been reported.

**Headlight Flashing and Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Low beam LED headlights may flicker or flash unexpectedly, particularly when high beams are engaged.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight control modules and wiring harnesses for proper operation and software updates.

### Door Latch Actuators

Interior convenience features generally function well, though door lock actuators are prone to premature wear.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver or passenger side doors fail to lock or unlock using either the key fob or interior door panel buttons.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuator assembly.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Camry&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Toyota Camry remains an appealing choice for used-car buyers looking for comfort and reliability. While most systems prove dependable, shoppers should thoroughly test the transmission for smooth shifting, verify that all power door locks and headlights function correctly, and ensure the braking system holds firm during a pre-purchase inspection.
