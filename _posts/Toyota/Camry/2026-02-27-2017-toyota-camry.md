# 2017 Toyota Camry Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Toyota Camry continues a long-standing reputation for dependable daily transportation, holding up well over years of ownership. While generally robust, used-car buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding certain electrical glitches, braking feedback, and powertrain quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder

The 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces around 178 horsepower, providing a balanced blend of everyday performance and strong fuel economy for daily commuters.

**Oil Consumption and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine or stability control lights accompanied by excessive oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Check oil levels regularly and consult a mechanic for diagnostics; persistent consumption may require major engine repairs.

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter V6 engine pushes roughly 268 horsepower, offering brisk acceleration and smooth power delivery while maintaining respectable long-term durability.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Engine unexpected stalling accompanied by illuminated check engine lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel system, pump, and electrical connections to identify failing components.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission shifts smoothly across all trims and pairs reliably with both engine choices for worry-free operation.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components generally perform adequately, though occasional owner reports note unusual pedal feel and clunking noises.

**Brake Pedal Travel and Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal traveling all the way to the floorboard, reduced stopping power, or abnormal grinding and clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, lines, and master cylinders thoroughly; replace worn or defective hydraulic components.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical accessories are generally dependable, but minor issues involving infotainment flickering or premature battery drain can occasionally occur.

**Battery Drain and Radio Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle failing to start due to a dead battery, or infotainment screens and dashboard lights flickering randomly.
- **Solution:** Test the battery and charging system for parasitic draws or failing alternators; update or repair infotainment units as needed.

## Exterior

### Climate Control and Door Actuators

Cabin features remain comfortable and user-friendly, though peripheral components like door lock actuators may wear out over time.

**Door Lock Actuator and Servo Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door locks failing to engage or climate control servo motors malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Replace failed door lock actuators or climate control servo motors.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Seat Belts

Safety restraint systems provide comprehensive occupant protection, though rare sensor lights and latch issues have been documented by owners.

**Airbag Warning and Seat Belt Latch Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning lights or difficulty securing seat belt buckles properly.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect seat belt assemblies and airbag sensor wiring harnesses for faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V503000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Camry&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An incorrect label may lead to unintentionally overloading the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 16V906000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Camry&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the air bag was installed with incorrect fasteners, the fasteners may become loose over time, affecting the air bag deployment and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fasteners, and if necessary, replace the instrument panel brace and body bracket and reattach the air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V295000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Camry&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A spare tire that is not inflated to the proper value may not perform as intended, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Gulf States will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the spare tires, adjusting their pressure as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The vehicle remains a top recommendation in the used midsize sedan market due to its stellar reputation for longevity, comfortable ride, and fuel efficiency. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes, electrical accessories, and fluid levels to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
