The 2016 Toyota Camry is a mid-size sedan that offers a choice of four-cylinder or V6 engines, paired with either a six-speed automatic or an eight-speed automatic transmission. The four-cylinder engine produces 181 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, while the V6 engine produces 268 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque. The Camry is known for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle, including problems with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system.

**Engine**

* **2.5L Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Premature Timing Chain Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may wear prematurely, causing the engine to rattle or lose power.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.5L V6 Engine**
    * **Valve Spring Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The valve springs may fail, causing the engine to misfire or run roughly.
        * Solution: Replace the valve springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Hesitation or Delay in Gear Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears, causing the vehicle to jerk or lose power.
        * Solution: Update the transmission control software or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 19V556000
* **Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears or may get stuck in a particular gear.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission control module or the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Premature Strut Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: The front struts may wear prematurely, causing the vehicle to ride rough or handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replace the front struts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Sudden Battery Discharge**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may suddenly discharge, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
        * Solution: Replace the battery or the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Lighting**
    * **Headlight Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The headlights may not turn on or may flicker.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs or the headlight assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body Panels**
    * **Clear Coat Peeling**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clear coat on the body panels may peel or flake off.
        * Solution: Repaint the affected body panels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
    * **Cracked Dashboard**
        * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may crack or warp.
        * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control**
    * **AC Compressor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The AC compressor may fail, causing the air conditioning system to not work.
        * Solution: Replace the AC compressor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Toyota Camry is a generally reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the year of the vehicle. Overall, the Camry is a good choice for those looking for a reliable and fuel-efficient sedan, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
