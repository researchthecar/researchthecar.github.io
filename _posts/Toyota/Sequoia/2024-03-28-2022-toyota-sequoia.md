**Introduction:**
The 2022 Toyota Sequoia is a full-size SUV with a 5.7L V8 engine, an 8-speed automatic transmission, and 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque. It's praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine:**
**5.7L V8**

* **Piston ring failure:** Excessive oil consumption, engine knocking, increased emissions
    * Replacement of piston rings and other engine components
    * $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**
**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems:** Difficulty changing gears, rough shifts, slipping gears
    * Software updates, transmission rebuild or replacement
    * $1,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**
* **Rear suspension sagging:** Premature wear of rear suspension components
    * Replacement of rear suspension components
    * $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**
* **Battery drain:** Premature battery discharge, electronic system failures
    * Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components
    * $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**
* **Body panel alignment issues:** Misaligned body panels, uneven gaps
    * Adjustment or replacement of body panels
    * $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**
* **Dashboard creaking:** Squeaking or rattling noises from the dashboard
    * Replacement or adjustment of dashboard components
    * $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Third row seat latch failure:** Inability to latch the third row seats into place
    * Replacement of seat latches
    * $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2022 Toyota Sequoia has received positive reviews for its comfort, performance, and reliability. However, some owners have encountered issues with engine piston rings, transmission shifting, suspension components, electrical systems, and certain interior features. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem and required repairs.
