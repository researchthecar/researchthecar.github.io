# 2021 Toyota Sequoia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Toyota Sequoia represents the final year of the model's second generation, offering a robust V8 engine and traditional SUV capability. As a used vehicle, it generally maintains a strong reputation for durability, though prospective buyers should review a handful of owner-reported concerns ranging from minor suspension noises to unusual power train behavior.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 Engine

This 5.7-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque, providing strong towing capacity and steady performance. It is widely regarded as a highly durable and dependable powerplant for this large SUV platform.

### Fuel Tank

Exterior body components and hardware are durable, though isolated structural leaks can occur.

**Fuel Tank Cracking and Leaking**
- **Symptoms:** The fuel tank may develop a crack leading to a fuel leak while the vehicle is parked, without any prior impact or accident.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel tank for physical cracks and replace the tank assembly if leakage is detected.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission works in tandem with the rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive systems to deliver power smoothly. It is generally robust and free from widespread mechanical defects.

### Gear Shift and Parking Beep

Electrical and shifting systems are normally reliable, but intermittent anomalies should be checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Inadvertent Movement Concern**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported an instance where a beeping noise sounded and the vehicle began reversing inadvertently while parked after the driver door was opened.
- **Solution:** Have the shift-by-wire or mechanical gear selector mechanisms and parking sensors inspected by a qualified technician.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension Shocks

Suspension components generally hold up well, though occasional noise issues can develop over time.

**Creaking and Grinding Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear creaking, grinding, or knocking noises from the driver-side shock when driving over mild bumps or road defects at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front shock assemblies and replace worn suspension components if physical wear or component failure is confirmed.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Sunroof panels require routine seal maintenance to ensure proper operation and prevent glass stress.

**Unexpected Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass sunroof panel may spontaneously shatter or explode with a loud bang while driving on the highway.
- **Solution:** Clear away broken glass fragments and replace the sunroof assembly, verifying proper frame alignment.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Toyota Sequoia is a solid, capable full-size SUV backed by a proven V8 powertrain and strong overall reliability ratings. While isolated owner reports highlight concerns such as unexpected sunroof glass breakage, suspension noises, or fuel tank leaks, these do not appear to indicate widespread systemic defects. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the suspension, exterior glass, and undercarriage to ensure a worry-free ownership experience.
