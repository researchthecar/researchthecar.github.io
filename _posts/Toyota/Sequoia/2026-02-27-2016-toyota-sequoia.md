# 2016 Toyota Sequoia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Toyota Sequoia is a large, full-size SUV known for its cavernous interior, robust capability, and rugged truck-based platform. When shopping for a used model, buyers generally find a dependable hauler with a thirsty V8 engine, though a few recurring mechanical and electrical quirks have been reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 3UR-FE

The 5.7L V8 engine produces 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque, offering strong towing performance. It is widely regarded as exceptionally durable and long-lasting when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the V8 engine across rear-wheel and four-wheel-drive configurations, generally exhibiting very strong reliability.

## Brakes

### Brake Rotors and Pads

Owner complaints frequently highlight premature wear and warping on factory brake rotors, often requiring early replacement or resurfacing.

**Premature Rotor Warping**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience violent shaking and vibration in the steering wheel and vehicle when applying the brakes at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the brake rotors resurfaced or replaced with heavy-duty aftermarket alternatives if warping recurs.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster

Electrical system concerns are minimal, though isolated visibility issues with dashboard gauges under bright sunlight have been noted by owners.

**Dashboard Display Dimming**
- **Symptoms:** The instrument panel and gauges become extremely dark and unreadable when driving in bright daylight conditions.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealer or automotive electrician to check for cluster illumination settings, sensor operation, or potential display component failure.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V311000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sequoia&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Lugnuts that crack and detach may cause the wheels to separate from the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** SET will notify owners, and dealers will install new replacement lugnuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V920000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sequoia&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak may cause a sudden loss of power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the power steering gear assembly as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Toyota Sequoia remains a solid choice for buyers seeking a spacious and durable family SUV. While owners have reported occasional issues with premature brake rotor wear and isolated electrical quirks, its overall mechanical foundation is tough and dependable. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the brake system during a test drive.
