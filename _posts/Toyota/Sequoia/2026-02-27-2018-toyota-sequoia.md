### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Toyota Sequoia demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain (5.7L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission) and chassis components, where no significant widespread issues are reported. The primary areas to monitor are the electrical system, specifically potential infotainment glitches and intermittent battery drain issues, which can range in cost to resolve. Exterior and interior components are generally robust. With proactive maintenance and attention to the minor electrical concerns, the 2018 Sequoia is expected to provide dependable service for many years.
