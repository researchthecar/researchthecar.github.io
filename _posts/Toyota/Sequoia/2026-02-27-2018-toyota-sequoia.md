# 2018 Toyota Sequoia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Toyota Sequoia continues the vehicle's reputation for rugged, old-school full-size SUV capability and long-term durability. Built on a truck-based platform, it offers generous interior space and a powerful V8 engine. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical anomalies, safety sensor glitches, and isolated mechanical wear to ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 (381 hp / 401 lb-ft)

The 5.7-liter V8 engine produces 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust towing capacity and reliable performance. This powertrain is generally considered a strong, long-lasting highlight of the Sequoia lineup.

**Fuel Pump Operation Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported extended cranking times, intermittent failure to start, or loss of power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel pump and verify whether recall or component replacement is necessary.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the V8 engine across various drivetrains. It has a solid reputation for durability with few widespread owner complaints.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Seals

Steering components are generally durable, though occasional fluid leaks from the rack and pinion assembly have been documented.

**Steering Rack Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Outer seals may show fluid accumulation and leakage during routine maintenance inspections.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering rack assembly and replace leaking seals or the complete rack if internal wear is present.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Visibility and exterior glass components are generally sturdy, though rare instances of spontaneous sunroof shattering have been reported.

**Unexpected Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass may fracture or explode outward spontaneously while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof assembly and inspect the frame alignment to relieve any tension.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Driver assistance and safety suite features enhance protection but can occasionally generate false alerts or require sensor recalibration.

**False Collision Warning and Braking Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking or lane departure systems may activate unexpectedly without obstacles or trigger persistent warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or specialist recalibrate the forward cameras, radar sensors, and inspect related wiring harnesses.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Toyota Sequoia is a dependable and capable family hauler backed by a robust V8 engine and solid mechanical construction. While most complaints center around minor electrical glitches, sensor sensitivities, or isolated fuel system concerns, buyers should perform a comprehensive inspection of the electronics, safety aids, and steering rack prior to purchase.
