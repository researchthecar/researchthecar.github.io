# 2017 Toyota Sequoia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Toyota Sequoia, buyers will find a robust full-size SUV built on a traditional truck-based platform. Overall, owner reports indicate a relatively dependable mechanical layout, though prospective purchasers should still review maintenance history and inspect key wear items carefully during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 3UR-FE

The 5.7-liter V8 engine produces 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque, paired with a heavy-duty setup designed for towing and longevity. It is generally praised for its durability, though isolated fuel system complaints have been documented by owners.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported engine shaking at higher speeds, accompanied by illuminated check engine lights, or instances where the lower fuel pump failed during cold weather or high-speed driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel delivery system and replace the faulty fuel pump assembly if pressure tests fail.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to either the rear wheels or the multi-mode four-wheel-drive system. It maintains a solid reputation for holding up well under normal driving and towing duties when fluid changes are kept up to date.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body fittings and panels are generally well-assembled, though rare glass-related incidents have been reported.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass panel on the sunroof may fracture or shatter unexpectedly while driving down the highway without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding track for alignment or stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V311000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sequoia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Lugnuts that crack and detach may cause the wheels to separate from the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** SET will notify owners, and dealers will install new replacement lugnuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V920000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sequoia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak may cause a sudden loss of power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the power steering gear assembly as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21E103000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sequoia&modelYear=2017)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Toyota Sequoia remains a durable choice for buyers seeking a spacious, traditional full-size SUV with strong V8 performance. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential issues like fuel pump failures or rare glass incidents, regular service records and a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help ensure you find a reliable example.
