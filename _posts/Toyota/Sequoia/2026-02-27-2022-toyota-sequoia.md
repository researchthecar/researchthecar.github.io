# 2022 Toyota Sequoia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Toyota Sequoia represents the final model year of the legendary second-generation full-size SUV platform before its complete redesign. Known for its rugged build and traditional V8 power, this model year generally maintains a strong reputation for durability, though buyers should still review maintenance history and open manufacturer service campaigns.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 Engine

This 5.7-liter V8 engine produces 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque, paired with a traditional six-speed automatic transmission. It offers robust towing capability and proven mechanical durability, making it a dependable choice for used SUV buyers.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional six-speed automatic transmission works in tandem with the V8 engine to deliver power to either the rear wheels or a four-wheel-drive system. It is widely regarded as a sturdy and reliable unit that requires standard fluid maintenance.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

Steering components are generally robust, though owners should verify that all applicable steering-related safety recalls have been successfully resolved.

**Recall Part Availability Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience delays in scheduling safety recall repairs through local dealerships due to parts availability.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized dealership to check the status of open recall parts and schedule the repair as soon as components become available.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V920000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sequoia&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak may cause a sudden loss of power steering assist, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the power steering gear assembly as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Toyota Sequoia is a dependable and capable full-size SUV backed by a proven V8 powertrain. While owner reports are minimal, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle history and ensure that any open safety campaigns have been addressed by a certified dealer.
