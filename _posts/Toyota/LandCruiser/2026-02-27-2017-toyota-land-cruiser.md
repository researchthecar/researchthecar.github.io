# 2017 Toyota Land Cruiser Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Toyota Land Cruiser is built on a legendary reputation for durability and rugged luxury, making it a highly sought-after full-size SUV on the used market. Powered by a robust V8 engine and equipped with a dependable automatic transmission, it handles tough off-road trails and highway cruising with equal competence. While overall owner satisfaction is exceptionally high, prospective buyers should review available complaint reports and recall notices before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8

The 5.7-liter V8 engine produces 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque, providing strong towing capability and effortless acceleration. It is widely regarded as an extremely durable and long-lasting powerplant requiring standard scheduled maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts and helps optimize highway fuel efficiency for the heavy four-wheel-drive platform. This gearbox generally exhibits strong reliability when maintained properly.

## Interior

### Driver Seat Memory

Cabin appointments are generally durable, though minor ergonomic quirks in comfort features have been reported by owners.

**Seat Memory Programming**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle allows fully reclined seating positions to be saved into the driver's memory preset controls.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution when programming memory seating positions to avoid accidentally storing unsafe or uncomfortable recline angles.

## Safety

### Air Bags and Seat Belts

Safety systems are comprehensive, but buyers should verify that all factory campaigns have been addressed to maintain optimal occupant protection.

**Airbag Sensor and Recall Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported passenger airbag sensors malfunctioning and turning the airbag off, alongside complaints regarding open recall remedy timelines.
- **Solution:** Check the vehicle identification number (VIN) with a dealership to ensure all open safety campaigns and airbag component recalls have been completed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V887000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Land+Cruiser&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, deactivated air bags can increase the risk of injury .
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will modify the front passenger seat belt assembly or replace it with an improved one, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Toyota Land Cruiser remains a solid, highly dependable choice for used luxury SUV buyers who value mechanical longevity and off-road capability. While isolated owner complaints highlight minor interior memory programming quirks and important safety recall considerations, the core mechanical components are exceptionally tough. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify recall completion.
