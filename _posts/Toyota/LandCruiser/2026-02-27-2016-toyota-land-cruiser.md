# 2016 Toyota Land Cruiser Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Toyota Land Cruiser is renowned for its exceptional durability, robust off-road capability, and luxury positioning. While owner feedback in federal databases is sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect various vehicle systems during a used car pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything operates correctly.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8

The 5.7-liter V8 engine produces 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque, offering strong towing and acceleration performance. It is generally regarded as a long-lasting, highly reliable powerplant for this heavy SUV platform.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the standard full-time four-wheel-drive system. It is robustly built to handle heavy-duty hauling and off-road excursions with proper fluid maintenance.

## Suspension

### Rear Wheels

Suspension components generally hold up well, though standard wear-and-tear inspections are recommended.

**Rear Wheel Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear unusual noises originating from the rear wheels while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the wheels, bearings, and suspension components for wear or loose hardware.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Driver Assistance

Advanced safety features and electronics generally provide good protection, but owners should verify all displays and driver aids function properly.

**Navigation Screen Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The navigation display screen goes dark, potentially disabling or limiting visibility of camera and driver assistance features.
- **Solution:** Inspect the infotainment system fuses, software wiring connections, or replace the display unit if faulty.

## Interior

### Cabin Air Filter Housing

Electrical and cabin systems are generally reliable, though owners should check for environmental intrusion issues during inspections.

**Rodent Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Holes in interior panels and dashboard areas caused by nesting rodents entering through the cabin air filter.
- **Solution:** Repair damaged interior trim panels and inspect the cabin air intake for protective screening to deter pests.
- **Cost:** $700

### Headrest Entertainment Screens

The cabin features high-end materials and standard rear-entertainment amenities, though specific configurations may require ergonomic adjustments.

**Headrest Monitor Placement**
- **Symptoms:** Rear-seat entertainment monitors mounted to the front seat headrests may pose a head or facial injury risk during sudden stops.
- **Solution:** Adjust seating positions or consult a dealership regarding the removal or repositioning of the screens if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V887000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Land+Cruiser&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, deactivated air bags can increase the risk of injury .
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will modify the front passenger seat belt assembly or replace it with an improved one, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V548000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Land+Cruiser&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An incorrect driver or front passenger air bag may not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected air bag assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Toyota Land Cruiser remains a dependable, highly capable luxury utility vehicle with an impressive powertrain. Prospective buyers should perform a standard comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, wheels, and interior accessories to ensure long-term satisfaction.
