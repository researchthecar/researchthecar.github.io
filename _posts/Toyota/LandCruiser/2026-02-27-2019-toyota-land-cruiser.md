# 2019 Toyota Land Cruiser Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Toyota Land Cruiser is renowned for its exceptional durability, robust off-road capability, and luxury craftsmanship. As a low-volume flagship SUV, it generally commands a strong reputation for dependability among used-car buyers, backed by few widespread owner complaints.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8

Equipped with a 5.7-liter V8 engine producing approximately 381 horsepower and 401 lb-ft of torque. This powerhouse is celebrated for its longevity and tough mechanical build, though fuel economy is predictably low.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with full-time four-wheel drive. It offers smooth gear shifts and robust durability tailored for both highway cruising and heavy-duty trail work.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Land+Cruiser&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V887000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Land+Cruiser&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, deactivated air bags can increase the risk of injury .
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will modify the front passenger seat belt assembly or replace it with an improved one, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V244000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Land+Cruiser&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The text on the load capacity label may become illegible. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." If the driver cannot read the label, they may unknowingly overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GST will notify owners, and dealers will provide corrected load carrying capacity modification labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Toyota Land Cruiser represents a remarkably solid choice for used SUV buyers prioritizing longevity and rugged performance. Because available owner complaint data is sparse, prospective buyers should still perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and cosmetic health.
