# 2024 Toyota Land Cruiser Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Toyota Land Cruiser marks a fresh chapter for the legendary nameplate, returning to its rugged, utilitarian roots with a turbocharged hybrid powertrain. As a newly introduced model year, prospective used-car buyers will find a high-tech off-road machine built on Toyota's TNGA-F global truck platform. Because it is so new to the market, long-term reliability trends are still unfolding, and early production units should be evaluated carefully for model-year quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Turbocharged Inline-4 Hybrid (i-Force MAX)

This 2.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder hybrid system generates 326 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque, combining traditional forced induction with electric motor assistance. It provides robust low-end power for off-roading and towing while aiming to improve efficiency over past V8 configurations.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with the i-Force MAX hybrid powertrain, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear selection across varied driving conditions and low-range off-road maneuvers.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V548000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Land+Cruiser&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." A vehicle with an incorrect maximum capacity weight value may be overloaded, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Toyota Land Cruiser brings a compelling blend of modern hybrid efficiency and traditional off-road capability to the used market. Since it represents the debut year of a completely redesigned generation, buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electronics and mechanical systems before purchasing. Overall, it remains an exciting and highly capable choice for adventure-seeking drivers.
