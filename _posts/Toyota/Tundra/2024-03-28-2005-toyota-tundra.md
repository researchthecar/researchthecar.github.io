The 2005 Toyota Tundra is a full-size pickup truck that is known for its reliability and durability. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 4.0L V6, a 4.7L V8, and a 5.7L V8. The Tundra is also available with a choice of rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** This issue can cause coolant or oil leaks, overheating, and decreased engine performance. The solution is to replace the intake manifold gasket. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000**

**4.7L V8**

* **Timing belt tensioner failure:** This issue can cause the timing belt to skip or break, which can lead to catastrophic engine damage. The solution is to replace the timing belt tensioner. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000**

**5.7L V8**

* **Valve cover gaskets leak:** This issue can cause oil leaks and decreased engine performance. The solution is to replace the valve cover gaskets. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500**
* **Spark plug failure:** This issue can cause misfires, decreased engine performance, and increased fuel consumption. The solution is to replace the spark plugs. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200**

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual**

* **No issues found**

**6-speed automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:** This issue can cause the transmission to slip out of gear, which can lead to decreased performance and safety concerns. The solution is to rebuild or replace the transmission. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000**

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension:** This issue can cause the front end of the Tundra to sag, which can lead to decreased handling and stability. The solution is to replace the front suspension components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000**
* **Rear suspension:** This issue can cause the rear end of the Tundra to sag, which can lead to decreased handling and stability. The solution is to replace the rear suspension components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** This issue can cause the battery to drain quickly, which can lead to a dead battery and a no-start condition. The solution is to identify and fix the source of the battery drain. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500**
* **Alternator failure:** This issue can cause the alternator to fail, which can lead to a dead battery and a no-start condition. The solution is to replace the alternator. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000**

**Exterior**

* **Headlight switch failure:** This issue can cause the headlights to fail, which can lead to decreased visibility and safety concerns. The solution is to replace the headlight switch. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200**
* **Taillight switch failure:** This issue can cause the taillights to fail, which can lead to decreased visibility and safety concerns. The solution is to replace the taillight switch. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200**

**Interior**

* **Climate control failure:** This issue can cause the climate control system to malfunction, which can lead to decreased comfort and safety. The solution is to replace the climate control module. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000**
* **Cruise control failure:** This issue can cause the cruise control system to malfunction, which can lead to decreased convenience and safety. The solution is to replace the cruise control module. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500**
* **Power window failure:** This issue can cause the power windows to fail, which can lead to decreased convenience and safety. The solution is to replace the power window motor or switch. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500**

**Summary**

The 2005 Toyota Tundra is a generally reliable and durable vehicle. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the most common issues include intake manifold gasket failure, timing belt tensioner failure, transmission slipping, and suspension problems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can be expensive to repair. It is important to be aware of these issues before purchasing a 2005 Toyota Tundra.
