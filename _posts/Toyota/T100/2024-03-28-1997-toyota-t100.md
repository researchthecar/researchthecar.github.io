**1997 Toyota T100**

The 1997 Toyota T100 is a mid-size pickup truck that was available with two engine options: a 3.4-liter V6 and a 4.7-liter V8. Both engines were paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The T100 was praised for its reliability, off-road capabilities, and spacious interior. However, it was also criticized for its lack of power and outdated design.

**Engine**

* **3.4L V6**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4.7L V8**
    * **Timing Belt Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine failure, rattling noise, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace timing belt and water pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slippage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace transmission fluid, rebuild or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, loose steering, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front suspension bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Leaf Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, electrical components malfunction
    * Solution: Test and replace battery, inspect charging system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, battery not charging, electrical components malfunction
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate won't open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Bed Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust on truck bed, particularly around the wheel wells
    * Solution: Clean and treat rust, repaint or replace bed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard, especially in areas exposed to sunlight
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Covers Tearing**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tearing in the fabric seat covers
    * Solution: Replace seat covers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1997 Toyota T100 has some common issues, but overall it is a reliable vehicle. The most severe issues are the timing belt failure and intake manifold gasket failure, which can lead to engine damage if not addressed promptly. Other issues, such as suspension bushing failure and electrical problems, are relatively minor and can be fixed at a reasonable cost.
