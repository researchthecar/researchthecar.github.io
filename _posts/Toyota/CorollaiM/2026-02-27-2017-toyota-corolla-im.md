# 2017 Toyota Corolla iM Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Toyota Corolla iM inherits Scion's practical hatchback formula, offering an efficient and straightforward daily driving experience for used car buyers. Because available data shows very few major recurring mechanical complaints for this model year, it remains a solid and dependable choice in the pre-owned compact hatchback market.

---

## Engine

### 1.8L 4-Cylinder (2ZR-FAE)

This 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 137 horsepower and 126 lb-ft of torque using Valvematic technology. It is generally regarded as highly reliable and low-maintenance for daily commuting.

## Transmission

### CVT Automatic

The continuously variable transmission is designed for optimal fuel efficiency during highway and city driving. When properly maintained, it offers smooth operation without notable widespread mechanical failure patterns.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional six-speed manual transmission provides a more engaging driving dynamic for enthusiasts. It utilizes a standard clutch setup and has a reputation for straightforward mechanical durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V295000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Corolla+iM&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A spare tire that is not inflated to the proper value may not perform as intended, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Gulf States will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the spare tires, adjusting their pressure as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Toyota Corolla iM stands out as a dependable and low-risk used vehicle option, backed by Toyota's reputation for longevity and a notable absence of widespread complaint trends. Prospective buyers should still perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify overall condition and maintenance history.
