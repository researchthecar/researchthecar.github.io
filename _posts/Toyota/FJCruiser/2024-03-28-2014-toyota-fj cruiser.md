**2014 Toyota FJ Cruiser**

The 2014 Toyota FJ Cruiser is a rugged SUV that combines off-road capability with retro styling. It is powered by a 4.0-liter V6 engine that produces 260 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque. The FJ Cruiser is available with either a 5-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual transmission. It also comes standard with four-wheel drive.
