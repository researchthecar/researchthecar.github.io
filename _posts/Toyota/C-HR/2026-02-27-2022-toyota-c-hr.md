### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Toyota C-HR demonstrates strong overall reliability, aligning with Toyota's established reputation. The powertrain (2.0L engine and CVT transmission) shows no significant widespread issues. Key areas to monitor include potential glitches with the infotainment system, which may require software updates or resets, and the possibility of premature 12V battery failure, though this is not a common complaint. Minor concerns like condensation in LED headlights and occasional HVAC blower motor issues have been reported but are not indicative of systemic problems. For the most part, the C-HR offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal unexpected repair costs beyond standard maintenance.
