### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Toyota C-HR generally exhibits strong reliability, aligning with Toyota's reputation. The powertrain (engine and transmission) and core chassis components show minimal reported issues. The primary areas of concern are within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit experiencing occasional glitches and a potential for premature 12V battery failure. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and incur repair costs. Overall, the C-HR is a dependable choice, with most potential issues being relatively minor and manageable.
