## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light illuminated, difficulty starting the engine, or complete electrical failure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Power Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, or unusual noises when attempting to use them.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected power window motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Toyota Echo is a highly reliable vehicle, with its powertrain (engine and transmission) and suspension systems demonstrating exceptional durability. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning the alternator and power window motors. These are not uncommon failures for vehicles of this age and type. Overall, the Echo is a sound choice for those seeking a dependable and economical car, with most potential repairs being relatively minor and affordable.
