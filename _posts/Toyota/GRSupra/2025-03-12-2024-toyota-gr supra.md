The 2024 Toyota GR Supra is a performance-focused sports car that blends Toyota reliability with BMW engineering. The GR Supra is available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder producing 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six producing 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the GR Supra for its sharp handling, powerful engine options, and distinctive styling. However, some criticisms include limited headroom, a small trunk, and reliance on BMW components for some systems.

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system, which is heavily based on BMW's iDrive, can occasionally freeze or crash, requiring a restart of the vehicle. This can affect navigation, audio, and other vehicle settings.
    *   Solution: Software updates from Toyota/BMW can often resolve these issues. In some cases, a complete replacement of the infotainment unit may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $1,000+ (Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Excessive Wind Noise**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported excessive wind noise, particularly around the door seals and windows, at higher speeds.
    *   Solution: Adjusting or replacing the door seals and window seals. Proper adjustment of window height.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Creaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Various interior panels may exhibit rattling or creaking noises, especially when driving on rough roads. Common areas include the dashboard, center console, and door panels.
    *   Solution: Locating the source of the noise and applying felt tape or other sound-deadening material to the affected areas. Retightening any loose fasteners.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2024 Toyota GR Supra appears to be a relatively reliable vehicle, though some issues have surfaced. The most commonly reported problems involve the infotainment system, wind noise, and interior rattles. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to more significant electronic malfunctions. The cost to address these problems can vary widely, from free software updates to potentially expensive component replacements. Overall, the GR Supra appears to be a good performing car, but be sure to address these issues during any pre-purchase inspection and keep up to date on software updates for the car.
