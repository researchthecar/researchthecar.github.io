**Introduction:**

The 2022 Toyota GR Supra is a two-seat sports car available with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, or a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six making 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. The 2.0-liter engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, while the 3.0-liter engine gets an 8-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual transmission.

Drivers appreciate the Supra's sharp handling, comfortable cabin, and evocative styling. Common complaints include a lack of storage space, a stiff ride, and its high price.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine**

* **Premature wear on piston rings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Turbocharger failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, loud noises from the turbocharger, oil leaks
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine**

* **Fuel injector issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Fuel injector replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Water pump failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks
    * Solution: Water pump replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Transmission**

**8-speed automatic transmission**

* **Shifting problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed shifts, slipping gears
    * Solution: Transmission fluid change, transmission solenoid replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Torque converter failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration during acceleration, slipping gears
    * Solution: Torque converter replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**6-speed manual transmission**

* **Grinding gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Synchronizer replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Clutch issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Clutch replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+

**Suspension**

* **Rear suspension noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the rear of the vehicle
    * Solution: Rear suspension bushings replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Electrical system diagnosis, battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

* **Infotainment system issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, software glitches
    * Solution: Infotainment system update, hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Body panel alignment issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misaligned body panels, uneven gaps
    * Solution: Body panel adjustment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Uncomfortable seats:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Thin cushioning, lack of support
    * Solution: Seat cushion replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

* **Squeaky dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard creaks or rattles
    * Solution: Dashboard panel adjustment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+

**Summary:**

The 2022 Toyota GR Supra has a number of potential issues, ranging from minor annoyances to more serious mechanical problems. The most common issues involve the engine, transmission, and infotainment system. The severity of these issues and the associated repair costs can vary significantly. However, overall, the Supra is a well-engineered vehicle that provides a thrilling driving experience.
