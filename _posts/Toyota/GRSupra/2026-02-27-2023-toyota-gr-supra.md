# 2023 Toyota GR Supra Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Toyota GR Supra delivers thrilling performance, sharp handling, and a choice of potent BMW-sourced powertrains. For used-car buyers, it represents a relatively young and modern sports car option that benefits from robust engineering, though prospective owners should still carefully inspect any pre-owned model for signs of aggressive driving or prior modifications.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering a lighter front-end feel. It provides a reliable and efficient entry point to the Supra lineup for everyday driving.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

The flagship 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine generates around 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is generally robust and well-regarded, though proper maintenance is vital given its high-performance output.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission is paired exclusively with the 3.0-liter engine, offering an engaging, traditional shifting experience for driving enthusiasts.

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The quick-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission is available across both engine options, delivering smooth gear changes and reliable daily drivability.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Toyota GR Supra stands out as a dependable and exciting sports car choice for used buyers. With no major recurring mechanical failure trends heavily reported, standard pre-purchase inspections and reviewing maintenance history remain the best steps to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
