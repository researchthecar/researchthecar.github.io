**Introduction**

The 2023 Toyota GR Supra is a sports car that is available with two engine options: a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four and a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six. The 2.0-liter engine produces 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.0-liter engine produces 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

People like the GR Supra for its stylish design, impressive performance, and sharp handling. However, some owners have reported a number of issues with the vehicle, including problems with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter inline-four**
    * **Blown head gasket**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
        * Solution: Replace the head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.0-liter inline-six**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Jerking or hesitation during shifts**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may jerk or hesitate during shifts, especially when shifting from first to second gear.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or install a battery tender
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may fail, causing the vehicle to lose power or experience other problems.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Squeaky interior parts**
    * Description/Symptoms: The interior of the vehicle may squeak or rattle, especially over rough roads.
    * Solution: Install sound-dampening material or identify and tighten loose parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Toyota GR Supra is a sports car with a number of positive qualities. However, some owners have reported issues with the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be costly to repair.
