**Introduction:**
The 2016 Toyota Venza is a midsize crossover SUV that offers a spacious interior, ample cargo capacity, and a comfortable ride. It is a popular choice for families and commuters due to its reliability, safety, and value for money. The Venza is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.7-liter four-cylinder engine mated to a six-speed automatic transmission or a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a six-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**
**2.7L Four-Cylinder Engine**
* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $6,000+
  * Recalls: N/A

**3.5L V6 Engine**
* **Timing Chain Wear:** Premature wear of the timing chain, causing engine noise and potential engine failure.
  * Solution: Replacement of timing chain and related components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**
**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Shifting Issues:** Hesitation or difficulty shifting gears, rough or delayed shifts.
  * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, potential transmission rebuild.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**
* **Front Strut Failure:** Premature failure of front struts, leading to reduced ride quality and handling.
  * Solution: Replacement of struts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**
* **Keyless Entry System Malfunction:** Faulty keyless entry system, preventing access to the vehicle or starting it.
  * Solution: Replacement of keyless entry system components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**
* **Rear Bumper Cover Cracking:** Cracking of the rear bumper cover, particularly at the corners.
  * Solution: Replacement of rear bumper cover.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**
* **Center Console Trim Peeling:** Peeling or cracking of the center console trim, exposing the underlying material.
  * Solution: Replacement of center console trim.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: N/A
* **Door Lock Knobs Sticking:** Difficulty or failure in operating door lock knobs, leading to difficulty locking or unlocking doors.
  * Solution: Replacement of door lock knobs.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
  * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2016 Toyota Venza has some potential issues, but they are generally not severe or widespread. The most significant issues involve engine oil consumption and transmission shifting problems, which can be costly to repair. However, the Venza remains a reliable and practical vehicle for many drivers.
