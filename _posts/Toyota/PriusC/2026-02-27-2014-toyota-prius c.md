# 2014 Toyota Prius C

### Overview
The 2014 Toyota Prius C is a subcompact hybrid hatchback known for its exceptional fuel efficiency and urban maneuverability. As a member of the renowned Toyota Prius family, it generally inherits a strong reputation for reliability. This model utilizes a 1.5-liter Atkinson-cycle four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, forming a robust hybrid synergy drive system. Owners typically praise its low running costs and dependable performance for daily commuting. While generally solid, like any vehicle, specific components can occasionally experience issues, though widespread problems are uncommon for this generation.

## Electrical

### Hybrid Battery System
**Reduced Hybrid Battery Capacity**
- **Symptoms:** Decreased fuel economy, reduced electric-only driving range, dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Hybrid battery pack replacement.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the gasoline engine, electrical system malfunctions (lights flickering, infotainment issues).
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Condensation Inside Headlight Housings**
- **Symptoms:** Visible moisture or fogging inside the headlight assemblies.

- **Cost:** $50 - $100 (for seals), $300 - $600 (for assembly)

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Toyota Prius C is a highly reliable vehicle, particularly for its segment. Its powertrain, combining a 1.5L engine with the hybrid system and ECVT, is exceptionally robust and rarely presents significant issues beyond the typical lifespan of hybrid batteries. The primary electrical concern revolves around the potential for hybrid battery degradation over time, which is a common characteristic of hybrid vehicles and can be costly to address. The 12V auxiliary battery can also require replacement. Exterior and interior components are generally durable, with minor issues like headlight condensation being infrequent. Overall, the Prius C offers excellent long-term value and dependability for budget-conscious and eco-friendly drivers.
