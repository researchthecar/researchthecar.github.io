**2021 Toyota Prius C**

The 2021 Toyota Prius C is a hybrid vehicle that combines efficient fuel consumption with a small and practical design. It features a 1.5-liter gasoline engine with an electric motor, offering a combined horsepower of 96. A continuously variable transmission (CVT) is the only transmission option.

People like the Prius C for its impressive fuel economy, affordability, and compact size. However, it has received complaints about its lack of power and acceleration, and some find the interior to be cramped.

**Engine Issues**

* **1.5-liter Gasoline Engine**
    * **Carbon Buildup in Intake Valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, rough idling
        * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Water Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks
        * Solution: Replace water pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission Issues**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty accelerating, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace transmission or transmission components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Whining Noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: High-pitched noise coming from the transmission
        * Solution: Replace transmission or transmission components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior Issues**

* **Steering Wheel Vibration**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration in the steering wheel
    * Solution: Inspect and replace steering components

**Summary**

The 2021 Toyota Prius C has some potential issues to consider, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem. Overall, the Prius C is a reliable and fuel-efficient vehicle, but it may not be suitable for drivers who require a lot of power or interior space.
