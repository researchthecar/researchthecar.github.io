**Introduction:**

The 2019 Toyota Prius C is a subcompact hybrid vehicle known for its fuel efficiency and affordability. It features a 1.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, delivering a combined 96 horsepower and 105 lb-ft of torque. The only transmission option is a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Owners appreciate its excellent fuel economy, reliability, and affordability. However, it has received criticism for its lackluster performance and cramped interior space.

* **Dashboard Vibration**

  * Solution: Toyota has released a fix involving replacing the dashboard panel inserts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not function properly, resulting in uneven cooling or heating.
  * Solution: The HVAC system may require servicing or replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Toyota Prius C has a limited number of reported issues, suggesting good reliability.
