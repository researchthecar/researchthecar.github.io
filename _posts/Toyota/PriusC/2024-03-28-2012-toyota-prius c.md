**2012 Toyota Prius C**

The 2012 Toyota Prius C is a hybrid vehicle with a 1.5L 4-cylinder engine and an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission (CVT). It has a combined horsepower of 99 hp and 103 lb-ft of torque. The Prius C is praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and reliability. However, it is criticized for its lack of power, uninspired driving experience, and cramped interior.

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the dashboard, particularly at higher speeds.
Solution: Replace or tighten the dashboard components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

* **Climate Control Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, including issues with temperature regulation, fan speed, or air flow direction.
Solution: Replace or repair the climate control module or associated components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2012 Toyota Prius C is a reliable and fuel-efficient vehicle with a few minor interior issues. The cost of repairs for these issues is generally low, making them relatively affordable to address.
