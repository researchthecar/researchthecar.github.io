# 2023 Toyota RAV4 Prime

### Overview
The 2023 Toyota RAV4 Prime is a highly regarded plug-in hybrid SUV, known for its blend of performance, fuel efficiency, and practicality. As a relatively new model year, widespread long-term reliability data is still accumulating. However, based on initial owner feedback and industry analysis, the RAV4 Prime demonstrates strong build quality and dependable performance, particularly in its powertrain. Toyota's established reputation for reliability extends to its hybrid systems, and the RAV4 Prime benefits from this legacy. Owners generally report a satisfying ownership experience with minimal unexpected issues, focusing on the vehicle's advanced technology and efficient operation.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Toyota RAV4 Prime exhibits excellent reliability, consistent with Toyota's strong reputation. The powertrain, including the 2.5L hybrid engine and eCVT, shows no significant widespread issues. Electrical components, particularly the hybrid battery, are robust. Minor infotainment glitches are the most commonly cited, but these are typically resolved with software updates. Exterior and interior components are also holding up well. Overall, the RAV4 Prime is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a fuel-efficient and practical SUV with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
