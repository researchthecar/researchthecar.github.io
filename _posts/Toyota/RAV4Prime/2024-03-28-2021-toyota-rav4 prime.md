**Introduction:**

The 2021 Toyota RAV4 Prime is a plug-in hybrid SUV that offers impressive fuel efficiency and a spacious cabin. It comes with a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor and an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission (eCVT). It generates 302 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Despite its excellent fuel economy, some owners have encountered various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to frequent oil level check intervals and potential engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replacement of piston rings or engine rebuild.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $6,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**eCVT Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears:** Transmission gears slip or fail to engage, causing drivability problems or power loss.
* **Solution:** Transmission replacement or repair.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Noise:** Excessive noise from the front struts, especially over rough surfaces.
* **Solution:** Replacement of struts or suspension components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** The 12-volt battery discharges prematurely, leading to starting issues or other electrical malfunctions.
* **Solution:** Battery replacement or charging system diagnosis.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number 21V693

* **Infotainment System Issues:** Malfunctions or glitches with the infotainment system, such as freezing, incorrect displays, or connectivity problems.
* **Solution:** Software updates or hardware replacement.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $0 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Seal Leakage:** Water leaks through the sunroof seal, potentially causing damage to the interior.
* **Solution:** Replacement of sunroof seal.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Headliner Noise:** Excessive noise from the headliner, especially over bumps or vibrations.
* **Solution:** Upholstery or insulation repair.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Toyota RAV4 Prime has encountered some issues that can be concerning. While most issues are relatively minor and affordable to fix, the engine and transmission problems can be more severe and expensive. Potential owners should be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
