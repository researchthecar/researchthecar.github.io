# 2022 Toyota RAV4 Prime Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Toyota RAV4 Prime is a plug-in hybrid compact SUV known for its impressive performance, electric-only driving range, and everyday practicality. While overall owner satisfaction tends to be high, used-car buyers should review potential electrical quirks and specific mechanical reports. As with any modern hybrid, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can offer peace of mind regarding the high-voltage systems and overall vehicle condition.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid Engine

The 2.5-liter four-cylinder Dynamic Force engine pairs with electric motors to produce a net 302 horsepower, offering brisk acceleration and standard all-wheel drive. This powertrain is generally considered robust and reliable, though occasional diagnostic checks are wise on higher-mileage used models.

**Idle Knock and Engine Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a slight knocking sound during cold idling and occasional minor jerking while driving, sometimes accompanied by a delayed check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose the fuel and ignition systems, check for stored diagnostic trouble codes, and ensure software updates are current.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (eCVT)

The planetary gear-type eCVT seamlessly blends electric motor output with gasoline engine power across all-wheel-drive trims. It is widely regarded as a durable, low-maintenance design requiring routine fluid inspections.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The regenerative braking system works in tandem with traditional hydraulic disc brakes to capture energy during deceleration. Proper brake fluid maintenance ensures consistent pedal feedback.

**Regenerative Braking and Pedal Feel Transition**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted temporary skidding sensations or perceived reductions in immediate stopping bite when hitting road bumps or manhole covers while braking.
- **Solution:** Ensure the anti-lock brake system (ABS) and hybrid regenerative braking modules have the latest manufacturer software calibrations applied.

## Electrical

### 12V Battery and Charging System

Electrical architecture supports advanced infotainment, driver assists, and plug-in charging capabilities. Buyers should verify proper operation of the charging port and test battery health.

**Premature 12V Battery Discharge**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced unexpected 12V battery drain, resulting in a no-start condition even after normal driving and standard garage storage.
- **Solution:** Test the 12V battery capacity, inspect for parasitic electrical draws, and replace the battery if cells have degraded.

**High-Voltage Charging Port Jam**
- **Symptoms:** The charging cable can occasionally become physically stuck inside the vehicle's high-voltage charging port.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to an authorized service center to safely release the locking mechanism and inspect the port assembly for internal damage.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior body panels and glass require standard upkeep, though prospective buyers should closely inspect the panoramic roof and power liftgate operation.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported instances of the panoramic sunroof or moonroof shattering unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds with a loud pop.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass and frame for prior stress or stone chips, and replace the glass assembly if compromised under comprehensive insurance.

**Power Liftgate Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The mechanized rear liftgate may fail to open or close completely, accompanied by a continuous warning beep.
- **Solution:** Inspect the liftgate struts, alignment sensors, and wiring harness, and reset or replace the liftgate control module as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V239000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=RAV4+Prime&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The Skid Control ECU software may not turn on the Vehicle Stability Control (VSC) system when the vehicle is restarted after the driver has disabled the VSC system. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Driving with a deactivated stability.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Skid Control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V519000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=RAV4+Prime&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat may have been assembled with interference between internal parts that may cause the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor to incorrectly detect the occupant. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Incorrect detection of an.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the front passenger seat, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V478000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=RAV4+Prime&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit can cause the DC-DC converter to overheat, increasing the risk of a vehicle fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the DC-DC converter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Toyota RAV4 Prime stands out as a versatile and efficient plug-in hybrid, combining strong performance with daily electric commuting capability. While most reported issues are isolated owner complaints regarding electrical accessories, minor drivetrain feedback, and glass durability, a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains essential. Buyers should verify the health of both the 12V and high-voltage charging systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
