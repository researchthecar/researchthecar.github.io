The 2023 Toyota RAV4 Prime is a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle (PHEV) that combines a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with an electric motor and a lithium-ion battery pack. This powertrain delivers a combined 302 horsepower and offers an EPA-estimated 42 miles of all-electric range. It uses an Electronically controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT). The RAV4 Prime is known for its impressive fuel economy, quick acceleration, and comfortable ride. Some owners appreciate its spacious interior and advanced safety features, while others dislike its higher price tag compared to the standard RAV4 and limited availability.

**Electric Motor**

**RAV4 Prime Electric Motor**

*   **High Voltage Coolant Leak**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported coolant leaks from the high-voltage cooling system, leading to potential overheating of the battery and reduced performance.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the affected coolant lines, or components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Battery**

**RAV4 Prime Battery**

*   **Reduced Electric Range**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that the range of the battery is less than that of the EPA estimated range.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the battery.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $8,000 - $15,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, crash, or become unresponsive. This can affect navigation, audio, and climate control functions.
    *   Solution: Software updates are often the first step. In some cases, the head unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1,500+ (head unit replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Wireless Charger Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The wireless phone charger may not consistently charge devices, or it may stop charging intermittently.
    *   Solution: Software update, or replacement of the charging pad.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $300+ (charger replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Backup Camera Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Backup camera image is distorted, delayed, or completely inoperable.
    *   Solution: Check connections, replace the camera, or update software.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Automatic Liftgate Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The automatic liftgate may fail to open or close properly, or it may become stuck in one position.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the liftgate motor or struts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **HVAC System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported issues with the HVAC system, such as inconsistent temperature control or failure to blow hot or cold air.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the affected components, such as the blower motor, compressor, or thermostat.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Toyota RAV4 Prime generally receives positive reviews, however, some owners have reported issues with the electrical and interior components of the vehicle. The high voltage coolant leaks can cause major damage to the high voltage system. Issues such as the infotainment system and liftgate can be very inconvenient for daily use. It is important to check the functionality of these systems during inspection before purchase.
