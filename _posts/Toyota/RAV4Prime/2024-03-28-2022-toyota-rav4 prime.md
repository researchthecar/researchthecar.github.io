**2022 Toyota RAV4 Prime**

The 2022 Toyota RAV4 Prime is a plug-in hybrid SUV with an impressive 42 miles of electric-only range. It is powered by a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a continuously variable automatic transmission (CVT). The RAV4 Prime has a combined horsepower of 302 and torque of 295 lb-ft. It is praised for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as a firm ride and limited cargo space when the rear seats are folded down.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Piston Ring Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $8,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Hesitation and Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, or hesitating during acceleration or shifting
Solution: Software update or transmission replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the rear suspension when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces
Solution: Replace rear suspension components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly even when the vehicle is not in use
Solution: Update software or replace battery
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Crossbars Corrosion**
Description/Symptoms: Crossbars rusting and peeling
Solution: Replace crossbars
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Freezing**
Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen becomes unresponsive or freezes
Solution: Software update or infotainment system replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Toyota RAV4 Prime is a reliable vehicle with some minor issues. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the severity of the issue and the location of the repair. Owners should be aware of the potential engine, transmission, and suspension issues and address them promptly to avoid further damage.
