**Introduction:**

The 2015 Toyota Avalon is a full-size sedan known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive fuel economy. It is available in three trim levels: XLE, XLE Plus, and Limited. The XLE comes with a 3.5L V6 engine (268 horsepower, 248 lb-ft of torque) and a 6-speed automatic transmission. The XLE Plus and Limited trims offer a hybrid powertrain, which combines a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine (156 horsepower, 156 lb-ft of torque) with an electric motor for a combined output of 200 horsepower. All trims come standard with front-wheel drive.

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

**Strut Bearing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front of the vehicle, especially over bumps or uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replacement of the strut bearing
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
Solution: Identification and repair of the source of the drain (e.g., faulty electrical component, parasitic draw)
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain
Recalls: None Found

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Electrical system issues, such as dim lights, difficulty starting, or flickering electronics.
Solution: Replacement of the alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Corrosion**
Description/Symptoms: Door handles become corroded and difficult to operate, especially in humid or salty environments.
Solution: Replacement of the door handles with ones that have better corrosion resistance
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Creaks and Rattles**
Description/Symptoms: Noises from the dashboard, such as creaking, rattling, or buzzing, particularly during driving over rough roads.
Solution: Inspection of the dashboard for loose or vibrating components and application of sound-dampening materials as needed
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Toyota Avalon has a good overall reliability record. However, it has some potential issues to be aware of, including a strut bearing failure in the front suspension, battery drain, alternator failure, door handle corrosion, and dashboard creaks and rattles. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively minor and affordable to fix.
