# 2016 Toyota Avalon Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Toyota Avalon continues the brand's tradition of offering a spacious, comfortable ride with a reputation for solid overall build quality. While owner complaints are relatively low given its age and popularity, used-car buyers should review specific owner-reported concerns regarding sunroof trim, momentary transmission hesitations, and exterior glass durability. Overall, it remains a strong choice for those seeking a dependable large sedan.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2016 Toyota Avalon features a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 268 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and robust performance. It is generally praised for long-term durability, though isolated electronic throttle response quirks have been noted by some owners.

**Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a brief hesitation or delay in acceleration when starting from a complete stop or at low speeds in city traffic.
- **Solution:** Dealers often check the powertrain control module; some owners find that adaptive learning updates help, though it is frequently noted as a normal characteristic of the electronic throttle mapping.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The standard 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels and matches well with the V6 engine. It is broadly reliable, with only occasional reports of erratic gear indication or shifting hesitation.

**Intermittent Shifting Delays**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally fail to upshift properly from a low gear, displaying a lower gear indicator until coming to a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection by a qualified technician is recommended to check for software glitches or internal transmission wear.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior glass and trim components are generally durable, though owners should inspect the sunroof and surrounding weatherstripping for proper adhesion and wear.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Rare instances where the sunroof glass has spontaneously shattered or exploded while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for prior impact damage and replace the glass panel if compromised.

**Weatherstripping and Trim Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Accordion-shaped rubber trim pieces and side weatherstripping around the sunroof can loosen and fall off over time.
- **Solution:** Re-secure or replace the weatherstripping clips and trim pieces to prevent wind noise and leaks.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Safety technology features like lane departure and collision avoidance generally perform well, but require careful attention during glass service to maintain proper sensor alignment.

**Windshield Calibration Challenges**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty recalibrating driver-assistance safety cameras after aftermarket windshield replacements due to part availability.
- **Solution:** Ensure that any windshield replacements utilize certified OEM glass and proper calibration procedures for safety sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V906000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Avalon&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the air bag was installed with incorrect fasteners, the fasteners may become loose over time, affecting the air bag deployment and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fasteners, and if necessary, replace the instrument panel brace and body bracket and reattach the air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V215000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Avalon&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As a result, the front passenger air bag and the front passenger knee air bag may not deploy as intended in certain crash scenarios. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Air bags that do not deploy as intended during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will recalibrate the OCS, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V024000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Avalon&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During certain crashes, the air bag electronic control unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly disabling the deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended may increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the ECU and install a noise filter between the air bag control module and its wire harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Toyota Avalon is a highly reliable and comfortable full-size sedan that delivers a great ownership experience. While minor complaints exist regarding sunroof trim, rare glass issues, and occasional throttle or shifting hesitation, these do not overshadow its strong mechanical foundation. Buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, glass, and powertrain responsiveness to ensure a worry-free purchase.
