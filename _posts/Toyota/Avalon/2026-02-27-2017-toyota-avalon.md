# 2017 Toyota Avalon Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Toyota Avalon, buyers generally find a spacious, comfortable, and well-regarded full-size sedan known for strong highway road manners and solid build quality. While overall owner satisfaction tends to be high, it is still wise to review common owner complaints regarding engine operation, auxiliary systems, and structural details before making a purchase. Keeping these potential trouble spots in mind can help you inspect prospective vehicles thoroughly and negotiate with confidence.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces roughly 268 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is generally regarded as a durable, low-maintenance powerplant capable of high mileage when properly cared for.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling incidents while operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the ignition, fuel delivery, and electronic control systems to diagnose the root cause.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The traditional 6-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the front wheels, providing seamless gear changes and long-term mechanical reliability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The conventional hydraulic braking setup provides reliable stopping power, though routine inspection of pads and rotors remains essential.

**Brake Assist Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported concerns regarding the activation or feel of the brake assist feature.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the brake booster, master cylinder, and related sensors for proper operation.

## Electrical

### Infotainment Navigation

Cabin electronics generally function well, though early smartphone pairing and app integration interfaces can occasionally display minor glitches.

**Navigation Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Smartphone-integrated navigation apps can freeze or become unresponsive when the connected phone enters sleep mode.
- **Solution:** Check smartphone display timeout settings or update the connected application software.

## Exterior

### Trunk Lid Torsion Springs

Exterior panels and hardware are generally robust, though occasional adjustments to decklid mechanisms may be necessary.

**Weak Trunk Springs**
- **Symptoms:** The trunk lid may fail to stay open securely and can drop unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the torsion springs or struts supporting the trunk lid.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V024000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Avalon&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** During certain crashes, the air bag electronic control unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly disabling the deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended may increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the ECU and install a noise filter between the air bag control module and its wire harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V295000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Avalon&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A spare tire that is not inflated to the proper value may not perform as intended, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Gulf States will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the spare tires, adjusting their pressure as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Toyota Avalon remains a compelling choice in the used full-size sedan segment, combining refined luxury with Toyota's traditional reputation for dependability. While isolated owner complaints exist regarding engine stalling, trunk springs, and auxiliary electronics, they do not detract from the vehicle's overall strong reliability profile. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify proper mechanical and electronic operation.
