# 2019 Toyota Prius AWD-e

### Overview
The 2019 Toyota Prius AWD-e, known for its exceptional fuel efficiency and hybrid technology, generally exhibits strong reliability. This model year introduced the all-wheel-drive option, adding versatility without significantly compromising the established dependability of the Prius line. Owners typically report satisfaction with its powertrain, comfortable ride, and user-friendly technology. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Prius AWD-e has a solid reputation for minimizing common automotive problems, making it a sound choice for those prioritizing long-term value and low running costs. Its robust engineering and Toyota's commitment to quality contribute to its favorable reliability standing.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Toyota Prius AWD-e is a highly reliable vehicle, consistently demonstrating Toyota's commitment to quality and durability. The hybrid powertrain and AWD-e system have proven robust with minimal reported issues. The most common non-wear-and-tear related concern is the potential for premature failure of the 12V auxiliary battery, a relatively inexpensive fix. Other major components across the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems (excluding the 12V battery), exterior, and interior show excellent reliability. Overall, the Prius AWD-e is a dependable choice with a strong track record.
