## 2020 Toyota Corolla

The 2020 Toyota Corolla is a compact sedan that offers a comfortable ride, good fuel efficiency, and a spacious interior. It's available with a choice of three engine and transmission options:

* **1.8L 4-cylinder engine with a 6-speed manual transmission:** 139 horsepower, 126 lb-ft of torque
* **1.8L 4-cylinder engine with a CVT:** 139 horsepower, 126 lb-ft of torque
* **2.0L 4-cylinder engine with a CVT:** 169 horsepower, 151 lb-ft of torque

The Corolla has been praised for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, bland styling, and noisy cabin.

### Summary
