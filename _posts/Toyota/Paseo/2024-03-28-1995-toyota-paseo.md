**Introduction:**

The 1995 Toyota Paseo is a compact sports coupe known for its affordability, handling, and fuel efficiency. It was powered by a 1.5-liter inline-four engine, which was offered with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The Paseo was praised for its nimble chassis and responsive steering, but was criticized for its underpowered engine and lack of interior space.

**Engine**

**1.5-liter inline-four**

**Blown head gasket**
Description/Symptoms: White smoke from exhaust, coolant leaks, overheating
Solution: Replace head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual**

**Synchronizer failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
Solution: Replace synchronizers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**4-speed automatic**

**Slipping gears**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips out of gear, hesitates to shift
Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front strut failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noisy ride, poor handling, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficult starting, dead battery
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $750+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rear bumper sags**
Description/Symptoms: Rear bumper sags down or becomes loose
Solution: Tighten or replace bumper mounts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Cracks appear on the dashboard
Solution: Replace or repair dashboard
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1995 Toyota Paseo is generally a reliable vehicle, but can experience some costly mechanical issues. The most significant issues include head gasket failure, synchronizer failure, and alternator failure. The suspension and electrical components may also require repairs over time. Overall, the cost of ownership for the Paseo is moderate, but it's important to factor in the potential expenses for major repairs.
