The 1994 Toyota Paseo is a compact sports coupe known for its affordability, sporty handling, and fuel efficiency. It came with two engine options: a 1.5-liter inline four-cylinder and a 1.6-liter inline four-cylinder. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic.

The car was praised for its nimble handling, responsive steering, and comfortable interior. However, it also had some drawbacks, such as a lack of power from the 1.5-liter engine, limited rear-seat legroom, and a somewhat cramped trunk.

## Engine
### 1.5L Inline-Four Cylinder
#### Engine Stalling
- **Description/Symptoms:** Engine randomly stalls while driving or idling.
- **Solution:** Replace the ignition coil or crankshaft position sensor.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
- **Recalls:** None Found

#### Oil Leaks
- **Description/Symptoms:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket or oil pan gasket.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty gasket.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+
- **Recalls:** None Found

#### No Issues Found

## Transmission
### 5-Speed Manual
- **Difficulty Shifting Gears**
- **Description/Symptoms:** Hard to shift gears, especially into first or second gear.
- **Solution:** Replace the clutch master cylinder or slave cylinder.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
- **Recalls:** None Found

### 4-Speed Automatic
- **Transmission Slipping**
- **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission slips out of gear or struggles to shift smoothly.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replace the transmission.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
- **Recalls:** None Found

## Electrical
- **Alternator Failure**
- **Description/Symptoms:** Battery dies quickly, dimming headlights, flickering electronics.
- **Solution:** Replace the alternator.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
- **Recalls:** None Found

- **Window Regulator Failure**
- **Description/Symptoms:** Power windows fail to operate or become stuck.
- **Solution:** Replace the window regulator motor or switch.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
- **Recalls:** None Found

## Interior
- **Dashboard Rattle**
- **Description/Symptoms:** Dashboard rattles or squeaks when driving.
- **Solution:** Tighten or replace loose dashboard components.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $50 - $200+
- **Recalls:** None Found

- **Seat Belt Buckle Failure**
- **Description/Symptoms:** Seat belt buckles fail to latch or release properly.
- **Solution:** Replace the seat belt buckle.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
- **Recalls:** None Found

## Summary
The 1994 Toyota Paseo has some minor issues that can affect its reliability, but overall, it is a well-built and affordable sports coupe. The most common issues are related to the ignition coil, transmission, and electrical components. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost of repairs can be moderate to expensive. However, with proper maintenance and timely repairs, the Paseo can provide many years of enjoyable driving.
