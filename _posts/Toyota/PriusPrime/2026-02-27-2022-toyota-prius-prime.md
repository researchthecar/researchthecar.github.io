# 2022 Toyota Prius Prime Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Toyota Prius Prime is a plug-in hybrid known for its impressive fuel efficiency and eco-friendly design. While overall owner satisfaction tends to be high, prospective buyers should review available feedback regarding electronics, vehicle speed control, and steering feel to ensure a well-rounded pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 1.8L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

Features a 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor system, delivering a combined 121 horsepower. This powertrain is generally robust and highly efficient, though isolated owner reports note speed control anomalies.

**Unintended Acceleration and Speed Control**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected sudden acceleration or temporary failure to accelerate as intended, accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electronic throttle control and hybrid propulsion software inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

Utilizes a planetary gear-type continuously variable transmission designed specifically for Toyota's hybrid synergy drive systems. It offers seamless power delivery with a strong track record for long-term durability.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Issues reported by owners concerning steering stability.

**Wandering or Loose Steering Feel**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may feel loose at highway speeds, causing it to pull or drift unexpectedly to the left or right.
- **Solution:** Inspect front-end suspension components, wheel alignment, and electronic steering calibration.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Concerns related to advanced driver assistance sensor behaviors.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision avoidance system may deploy the brakes unexpectedly when sensing moving machinery or rapid traffic cuts.
- **Solution:** Check sensor alignment and ensure safety software is up to date according to manufacturer specifications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V734000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Prius+Prime&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Toyota Prius Prime remains a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking exceptional fuel economy and plug-in versatility. While some owners have noted isolated concerns regarding adaptive speed control, steering tracking, and driver-assist system behaviors, these owner-reported complaints do not point to systemic mechanical failure. A thorough test drive and pre-purchase inspection should give you confidence in your purchase.
