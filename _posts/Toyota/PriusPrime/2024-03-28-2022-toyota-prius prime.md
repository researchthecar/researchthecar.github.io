**Introduction:**

The 2022 Toyota Prius Prime is a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle that combines a 1.8-liter gasoline engine with two electric motors for a total output of 121 horsepower and 105 pound-feet of torque. It offers an all-electric range of up to 25 miles, and a combined fuel economy of up to 133 MPGe. The Prius Prime is praised for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and advanced safety features, but it has also received criticism for its lack of driving excitement and low electric-only range.

**Engine**

* **1.8-liter Gasoline Engine**
    * **Delayed Acceleration:** Slow response to throttle input, especially at low speeds.
        * Solution: Throttle body cleaning or software update.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400
        * Recalls: 21V-624
    * **Misfiring:** Engine stumbling or hesitation during acceleration or idling.
        * Solution: Replace spark plugs or ignition coils.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Leakage:** Accumulation of oil around the engine valve cover or oil pan.
        * Solution: Replace gaskets or seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (eCVT)**
    * **Shifting Issues:** Abnormally rough or jerky shifts, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Software update or transmission fluid replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts:** Noise or clunking when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace worn struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorbers:** Reduced damping or excessive bounce.
    * Solution: Replace worn shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure:** Loss of power or reduced battery capacity.
    * Solution: Replace battery cells.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: 20V-441
* **Electrical System Malfunction:** Warning lights or error messages related to electrical components.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Antenna Issues:** Signal reception problems or antenna breakage.
    * Solution: Replace or repair antenna.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: 20V-626

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** Failure or abnormal operation of the heating or air conditioning system.
    * Solution: Replace or repair climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Problems:** Touchscreen glitches, connectivity issues, or system crashes.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: 22V-192

**Summary**

The 2022 Toyota Prius Prime is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some issues. The most common problems include engine misfiring, shifting issues, and electrical system malfunctions. These issues can range from minor inconveniences to costly repairs. However, overall, the Prius Prime remains a fuel-efficient and dependable choice for eco-conscious consumers.
