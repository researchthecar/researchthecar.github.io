# 2021 Toyota Prius Prime

### Overview
The 2021 Toyota Prius Prime, a plug-in hybrid electric vehicle, is generally recognized for its exceptional reliability and fuel efficiency. This model year continues Toyota's legacy of building durable and dependable vehicles, particularly within its hybrid powertrain systems. Owners typically report high satisfaction with the longevity and low maintenance costs associated with the Prius Prime. The integration of electric and gasoline power is seamless, contributing to a smooth driving experience and reduced environmental impact. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the 2021 Prius Prime demonstrates a strong track record, with most reported concerns being minor and easily addressed.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Toyota Prius Prime stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly in its core powertrain components. The hybrid system and electric transmission are exceptionally robust, with no significant widespread issues documented. The primary electrical concern noted is the potential for premature failure of the 12V auxiliary battery, a common item across many vehicle types. Minor software glitches in the infotainment system have been reported but are typically resolved with updates. Overall, the Prius Prime offers excellent long-term value and dependability, making it a sound choice for budget-conscious and environmentally aware drivers.
