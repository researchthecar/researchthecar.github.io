The 2024 Toyota Prius Prime is a plug-in hybrid vehicle known for its exceptional fuel efficiency, impressive electric driving range (estimated at 44 miles), and stylish design. It features a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 220 horsepower and an estimated 139 lb-ft of torque. While praised for its efficiency and modern features, some owners have reported certain issues. Common praises include its fuel economy, quiet cabin, and quick acceleration. Common gripes include the overly sensitive touch controls and a small back seat.

    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners report that the real-world EV range is less than the advertised 44 miles, especially in cold weather or with aggressive driving.
    * Solution: No specific fix is available beyond driving more conservatively, pre-conditioning the cabin while plugged in, and ensuring the battery is fully charged before each trip.

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Freezing/Lagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can occasionally freeze, lag, or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: A software update from the dealership may resolve the issue. In some cases, a full system reset may be required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update typically covered under warranty.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Wireless Charger Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The wireless phone charger intermittently fails to recognize and charge devices.
    * Solution: Ensure the phone is properly aligned. Software updates from the dealership have also been known to help.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Usually covered under warranty if a hardware replacement is necessary.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Hatch Alignment Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have noticed that the rear hatch is not perfectly aligned with the body panels, leading to uneven gaps.
    * Solution: Adjustment by a qualified body shop or dealer technician.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Typically covered under warranty, but may cost $100 - $300+ if not.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Display Glare**
    * Description/Symptoms: The large center display can create significant glare in direct sunlight, making it difficult to view.
    * Solution: Aftermarket anti-glare screen protectors can help mitigate this issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20 - $50
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Touch Sensitive Steering Wheel Controls**
  * Description/Symptoms: The touch-sensitive steering wheel controls can be overly sensitive, leading to accidental activation of functions.
  * Solution: Adjusting to the sensitivity or utilizing the voice command system. Some owners are seeking aftermarket button overlays.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10 - $30
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2024 Toyota Prius Prime, while generally reliable and efficient, has exhibited some minor issues. The most notable include infotainment glitches, inconsistent EV range, and potential rear hatch alignment concerns. The touch-sensitive steering wheel and center display glare can also cause frustration. Most of these issues are relatively minor and can be addressed through software updates or minor adjustments. The severity of these issues is generally low, and the potential cost to fix is usually reasonable, especially if covered under warranty.
