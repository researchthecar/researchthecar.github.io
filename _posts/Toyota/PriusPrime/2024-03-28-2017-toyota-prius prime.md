**Introduction**

The 2017 Toyota Prius Prime is an advanced plug-in hybrid vehicle that combines a 1.8-liter gasoline engine with an electric motor and battery pack to provide an impressive fuel efficiency of up to 133 MPGe. It features a spacious and practical interior, a comfortable ride, and a wide range of standard and available safety features. However, some owners have reported certain issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

* **1.8L 4-Cylinder Gas Engine**
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on the intake valves can lead to reduced engine performance, misfiring, and potentially even engine damage.
        * Solution: Cleaning the intake valves and removing the carbon buildup.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Hybrid Battery:**
    * **Battery Drain:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The hybrid battery may become depleted, leaving the vehicle without power.
        * Solution: Replacing the hybrid battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking During Acceleration:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The CVT may hesitate or jerk during acceleration, especially when going uphill.
        * Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the CVT fluid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The strut mount bearing may fail, causing a rattling or clunking noise over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacing the strut mount bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **12V Battery Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The 12V battery may prematurely fail, especially in cold weather.
    * Solution: Replacing the 12V battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture can accumulate inside the headlight lenses, affecting visibility at night.
    * Solution: Replacing the headlight assemblies or installing a ventilation kit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty Dashboard Display**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard display may flicker, become unresponsive, or go completely blank.
    * Solution: Updating the software or replacing the display unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2017 Toyota Prius Prime offers a fuel-efficient and eco-friendly driving experience, some owners have encountered issues related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While the severity of these issues can vary, the costs associated with repairs can be significant. It's important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before purchasing a used Prius Prime and to factor in the potential costs of repairs.
