**Introduction:**

The 2018 Toyota Prius Prime is a plug-in hybrid vehicle that offers the fuel efficiency of a fully electric vehicle with the convenience of a gasoline engine for extended range. It comes with a 1.8-liter four-cylinder gasoline engine and two electric motors, delivering a combined output of 121 horsepower and 105 lb-ft of torque. The Prius Prime is praised for its exceptional fuel economy, advanced safety features, and spacious interior, but some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **1.8-Liter Four-Cylinder Gasoline Engine**

    * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Burning excessive oil, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Head Gasket Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, and loss of engine power.
        * Solution: Head gasket replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)**

    * **Shuddering or Slipping:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission vibrations, hesitation during acceleration, and slipping gears.
        * Solution: Transmission fluid replacement or transmission rebuild.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clicking or rattling noises coming from the front suspension, especially over bumps.
    * Solution: Strut or shock absorber replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain when the vehicle is parked, resulting in a dead battery.
    * Solution: Electrical system diagnostic and repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Inverter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, overheating, and warning lights on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Inverter replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: SB-TC01-19

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wiper blades streaking or chattering, reduced visibility during rain.
    * Solution: Wiper blade replacement or windshield repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or vibrating dashboard components, creating annoying noises.
    * Solution: Panel adjustment or component replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Toyota Prius Prime has a good reputation for reliability, but it has experienced some issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. These issues can be costly to repair, but they are not widespread and most owners report satisfaction with their vehicles.
