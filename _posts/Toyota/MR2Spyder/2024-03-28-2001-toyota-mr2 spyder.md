**Introduction:** The 2001 Toyota MR2 Spyder is a mid-engine, rear-wheel-drive sports car powered by a 1.8L inline-four engine. It is known for its handling, performance, and affordability. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**
* **1ZZ-FE 1.8L Inline-Four**
    * **Oil Consumption Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacing piston rings and valve stem seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **C60 6-Speed Manual**
    * **5th and 6th Gear Pop-Out**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gears 5th and 6th may occasionally pop out of engagement during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Replacing or repairing the shift forks or detent springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Rear Suspension Bushing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the rear suspension bushings, leading to excessive noise, vibrations, and poor handling.
        * Solution: Replacing the worn bushings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Alternator fails to charge the battery, leading to electrical system issues.
        * Solution: Replacing the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Convertible Top Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Convertible top may malfunction, leak, or become torn.
        * Solution: Repairing or replacing the top.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Door Panel Clips Breakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Plastic clips that hold the door panels in place may break.
        * Solution: Replacing the clips.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10 - $50
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:** The 2001 Toyota MR2 Spyder is a capable sports car with some known issues. The most severe and expensive issues are related to the engine (oil consumption) and the convertible top (malfunctions and leaks). Other issues, such as suspension bushing failure and electrical problems, are relatively minor and less costly to fix. Overall, the MR2 Spyder is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
