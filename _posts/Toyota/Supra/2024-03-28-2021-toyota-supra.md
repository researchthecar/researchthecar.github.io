**Introduction:**

The 2021 Toyota Supra is a sports car praised for its exhilarating performance and sleek design. It is powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine, delivering 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. The Supra is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission.

**Suspension:**

* **Knocking Noise When Driving Over Bumps:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud knocking sound when driving over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Radio Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Radio display freezes or becomes unresponsive.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of faulty radio unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty Electrical Harness:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, such as flickering lights or power loss.
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of damaged harness component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior:**

* **Rattling Center Console:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose center console that rattles during acceleration or braking.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of console clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Misaligned Steering Wheel:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel not aligned with the wheels, causing uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Inspection and adjustment of steering rack components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Toyota Supra has a few minor issues that are not considered severe. The cost of repairs is generally affordable, ranging from $100 to $1,000+. The Supra has received positive reviews for its overall performance and handling, making these issues less of a concern.
