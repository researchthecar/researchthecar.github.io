# 2022 Toyota Supra Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Toyota Supra, buyers are looking at a high-performance sports car co-developed with BMW, offering sharp handling and potent powertrain choices. While overall owner complaint volume is very low for this model year, prospective buyers should still inspect exterior glass and body components carefully during a pre-purchase inspection. Knowing the specific engine and transmission options helps ensure you select the performance tier that best fits your driving style.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering a lighter front-end feel and strong efficiency. It serves as the entry-level powerplant for the Supra lineup and generally maintains a solid reputation for mechanical dependability.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine pumps out around 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque, delivering exhilarating acceleration and robust performance. It is a favored choice among enthusiasts, though proper maintenance and warm-up routines are recommended for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and delivers lightning-fast, smooth gear changes across both engine options. It is widely regarded as robust and responsive in daily driving and spirited track use alike.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission is paired exclusively with the 3.0-liter engine option, offering an engaging, traditional shifting experience for driving purists. It adds a high level of driver involvement to the Supra lineup.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Exterior glass and body panels are generally well-fitted, though isolated owner reports indicate susceptibility to stress cracks on the windshield.

**Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield glass may develop sudden cracks without any visible impact point or evidence of a rock strike.
- **Solution:** Have the windshield professionally inspected and replaced if structural cracks compromise driver visibility.
- **Cost:** $900

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V636000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Relay](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Supra&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter relay may corrode, causing the relay to overheat and short circuit. A short circuit in the starter relay may increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Supra&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Toyota Supra is an exciting and largely reliable sports car option for used buyers, backed by stout BMW-derived powertrains and Toyota's engineering oversight. Aside from checking for minor cosmetic or glass concerns during your test drive, focus on verifying the maintenance history and choosing between the energetic four-cylinder or the thrilling six-cylinder configurations.
