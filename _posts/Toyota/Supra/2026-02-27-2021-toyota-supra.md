# 2021 Toyota Supra

### Overview
The 2021 Toyota Supra, a joint venture with BMW, offers a compelling blend of performance and style. It is generally regarded as a reliable sports car, benefiting from proven BMW powertrain components. Owners often praise its sharp handling and exhilarating driving experience. While it shares its platform with the BMW Z4, the Supra maintains its distinct identity. Reliability data suggests a strong showing for the model year, with most reported issues being minor or isolated incidents rather than widespread defects. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear and tear.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Toyota Supra demonstrates strong technical reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain components which are sourced from BMW and have a proven track record. The inline-six and inline-four turbocharged engines, along with the 8-speed automatic transmission, show minimal reported issues. The most notable, albeit rare, concerns are minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system, typically resolvable with software updates. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. Overall, the 2021 Supra is a reliable sports car, with owners likely to experience a trouble-free ownership experience, provided routine maintenance is performed.
