**Introduction:**

The 2022 Toyota Supra is a sports car available with a 3.0-liter inline-6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, producing 382 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It is known for its handling, performance, and iconic design. However, like any vehicle, it has some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **3.0-Liter Inline-6**
    * **Fuel Pump Failure:** Loss of engine power during driving, check engine light illumination. Solution: Replace the fuel pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Turbocharger Boost Leak:** Reduced engine performance, whistling or hissing sound from the engine bay. Solution: Repair the boost leak. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **B58B30 Engine:**
    * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:** Engine misfiring, reduced power, engine stalling. Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

* **ZF 8-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shift Hesitations and Delays:** Transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears. Solution: Software update or transmission overhaul. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension:**
    * **Strut Mount Failure:** Clunking or knocking noises from the front suspension, reduced steering response. Solution: Replace the strut mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Rear Suspension:**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery draining quickly even with the vehicle turned off. Solution: Check for parasitic drains or replace the battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
* **Infotainment System:**
    * **Touchscreen Lag and Crashes:** Occasional touchscreen lag or crashing, requiring a reboot. Solution: Software update. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Misalignment:** Trunk lid may not align properly when closed, causing gaps or unevenness. Solution: Adjust the trunk lid hinges. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
* **Paint Peel:** Paint peeling or bubbling on certain areas of the vehicle, particularly around the rear bumper. Solution: Repaint the affected areas. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Air Conditioning Malfunction:** AC system may not blow cold air or have inconsistent performance. Solution: Check and repair or replace AC components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Steering Wheel Controls Issues:** Steering wheel controls may not respond or work intermittently. Solution: Replace the affected steering wheel controls. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

The 2022 Toyota Supra has some reported issues, but overall, it is a reliable and enjoyable sports car. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the authorized dealership performing the repairs. It is recommended to stay informed and up-to-date with any recalls or service bulletins issued for your vehicle to ensure optimal performance and longevity.
