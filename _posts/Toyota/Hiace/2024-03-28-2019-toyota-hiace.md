**Introduction:**

The 2019 Toyota Hiace is a versatile commercial van available with a range of engine and transmission options. It offers impressive cargo capacity and practical features, making it popular for various businesses and transportation needs. However, like most vehicles, the Hiace is not immune to certain issues.

**Engine**

* **Engine: 2.7L 4-cylinder Petrol**
    * **Valve Stem Oil Seal Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust during acceleration.
        * Solution: Replace valve stem seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Engine: 2.8L 4-cylinder Diesel**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from exhaust.
        * Solution: Replace or repair turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Transmission: 6-speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power at higher speeds.
        * Solution: Replace clutch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission: 5-speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuttering at low speeds.
        * Solution: Replace torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Strut Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, uneven tire wear.
        * Solution: Replace struts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800 per strut
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Leaf Spring Breakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear.
        * Solution: Replace leaf springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600 per leaf spring
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle won't start, dim headlights.
    * Solution: Replace battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **ECU Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine malfunction, dashboard warning lights.
    * Solution: Replace or repair ECU.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing doors, broken door handles.
    * Solution: Replace door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises from the dashboard.
    * Solution: Apply lubricant or add padding to reduce friction.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: DIY, typically under $50
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fan or AC/heat not functioning properly.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical or mechanical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Toyota Hiace is generally a reliable and capable vehicle, but like most vehicles, it has certain issues that can arise. The most common problems are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity and cost of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem, but most repairs can be performed within a reasonable budget. Overall, the Toyota Hiace remains a practical and efficient commercial van with a good reputation for durability.
