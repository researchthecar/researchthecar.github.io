**Introduction**

The 2022 Toyota Raize is a subcompact SUV that has become popular for its affordability and fuel efficiency. It is available with a 1.0-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Raize has received praise for its compact size, sporty handling, and comfortable interior. However, there have been some issues reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.0-Liter Turbocharged Three-Cylinder**

**Turbocharger Lag:**
* Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can experience lag when accelerating, resulting in a hesitation or lack of power.
* Solution: Software updates may be available to improve turbocharger response and reduce lag.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on software updates and labor costs.
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

**Slipping:**
* Description/Symptoms: The CVT may exhibit slipping, particularly when under load or accelerating. This can result in reduced acceleration and transmission noise.
* Solution: CVT repairs or replacement may be necessary.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**No Issues Found**

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience excessive battery drain when parked.
* Solution: Electrical system diagnostics and replacement of any faulty components may be required.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the specific issue and labor costs.

**Exterior**

**Wind Noise:**
* Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise may be noticeable around the windshield or side mirrors.
* Solution: Replacing weatherstripping or adjusting panels may reduce wind noise.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattle:**
* Description/Symptoms: Rattles or squeaks may occur in the dashboard area, particularly over rough roads.
* Solution: Securing loose components or applying insulation can eliminate rattles.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
* Recalls: None Found

**Center Console Discoloration:**
* Description/Symptoms: The center console trim may exhibit discoloration or fading over time.
* Solution: Replacement of the center console trim may be necessary.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* Recalls: None Found

**Other**

**No Issues Found**

**Summary**

Overall, while the 2022 Toyota Raize has received positive feedback, there have been some reported issues. The most significant issues appear to be related to the transmission, particularly CVT slipping. While the other issues are generally minor, they can still impact the overall ownership experience. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost of repairs can range from relatively affordable to moderate. It is important to note that these issues may not be present in all vehicles, and individual experiences may vary.
