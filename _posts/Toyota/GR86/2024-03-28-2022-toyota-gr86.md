**Introduction:**

The 2022 Toyota GR86 is a high-performance sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is available with a 2.4L naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine that produces 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, paired to either a 6-speed manual or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The GR86 has received praise for its exceptional handling, responsive engine, and affordable price point. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.4L 4-Cylinder**

* **Valve Spring Recall:** Valve springs may fracture, leading to engine failure.
  * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illumination, reduced engine power, knocking or rattling noises.
  * Solution: Replace affected valve springs.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Covered under warranty.
  * Recalls: NHTSA ID#22V-820

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:** Torque converter may fail prematurely, causing transmission slipping or shuddering.
  * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed gear changes, loss of power, check engine light illumination.
  * Solution: Replace torque converter.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue:** Battery may drain prematurely due to a faulty parasitic draw.
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, drained battery after extended periods of inactivity.
  * Solution: Identify and repair the parasitic draw.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, such as freezing or restarting.
  * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent touchscreen responsiveness, unexpected system shutdowns.
  * Solution: Software update or system replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Covered under warranty.
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Toyota GR86 is a capable sports car with a few minor issues. The engine valve spring recall is a serious concern that should be addressed immediately. The torque converter failure in the automatic transmission is a potential problem, but it is less severe than the engine issue. The other issues are generally minor and should not significantly impact the overall experience with the vehicle. However, it is important to be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
