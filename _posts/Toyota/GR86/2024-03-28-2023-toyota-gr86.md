**Introduction:**

The 2023 Toyota GR86 is a compact sports car known for its affordable price and engaging driving dynamics. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 2.4-liter boxer engine that produces 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed manual or an 8-speed automatic transmission. The GR86 is praised for its handling, steering feedback, and overall value, but some minor issues have been reported.

**Engine**

* **2.4L Boxer Engine**

  * **Knocking Noise During Cold Start:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A knocking noise is heard from the engine during cold starts, especially in cold weather.
    * Solution: The issue is attributed to piston slap, a common occurrence in high-performance boxer engines. No repairs are necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**

  * **Difficulty Shifting into First Gear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The shifter struggles to engage first gear smoothly, requiring excessive force or multiple attempts.
    * Solution: Inspect and lubricate the shifter mechanism. In some cases, replacing the shifter cable may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

  * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, especially when the vehicle is parked for extended periods.
    * Solution: Inspect the vehicle for parasitic power draw. Potential sources include faulty interior lights, aftermarket electronics, or a faulty alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Alignment Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The trunk lid may not align properly when closed, resulting in uneven gaps or rattling.
    * Solution: Adjust the trunk lid hinges or replace the trunk lid if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Loud Gearbox Whine:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A whining noise is audible from the gearbox area, particularly in lower gears.
    * Solution: The issue is attributed to a misalignment in the gearbox. Replacing the affected gears may resolve the noise.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Dashboard Cracks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks may appear on the dashboard, especially near the windshield.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard or repair the cracks using epoxy or other adhesive.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The Toyota GR86 faces some minor issues, but they are generally not severe and can be resolved with reasonable repair costs. The engine knocking noise is a common occurrence in boxer engines, while the transmission issues can be addressed through lubrication or cable replacement. The electrical battery drain requires investigation to identify the source, and exterior alignment issues can be rectified with hinge adjustments or part replacements. The interior gearbox whine and dashboard cracks, while annoying, are not major concerns and can be repaired or replaced at a reasonable cost.
