# 2023 Toyota GR86

### Overview
The 2023 Toyota GR86, a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe co-developed with Subaru, is generally praised for its engaging driving dynamics and accessible performance. Built on a shared platform with the Subaru BRZ, it utilizes a naturally aspirated boxer engine. Reliability data for the 2023 model year, while still emerging, indicates a strong performance, consistent with Toyota's reputation. Most reported issues are minor and infrequent, with the vehicle demonstrating good build quality and component durability. Owners generally report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected mechanical problems, focusing more on the car's intended performance aspects.
