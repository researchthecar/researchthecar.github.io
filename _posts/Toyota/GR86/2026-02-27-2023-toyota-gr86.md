# 2023 Toyota GR86 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Toyota GR86, buyers are looking at a nimble, lightweight sports car developed in partnership with Subaru. Powered by a naturally aspirated flat-four engine, it delivers sharp handling and engaging rear-wheel-drive dynamics. While overall owner satisfaction is high, early reports suggest keeping an eye on powertrain stability and minor electrical or body components.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Naturally Aspirated Flat-4 Engine (6-Speed Manual)

The 2023 Toyota GR86 comes standard with a 2.4-liter naturally aspirated boxer four-cylinder engine producing 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. Paired with the 6-speed manual transmission, this setup offers responsive acceleration, though used buyers should thoroughly review service and maintenance records.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden engine stalling, unexpected loss of power at highway speeds, or severe misfires accompanied by smoke and warning messages.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the ignition components, spark plugs, and engine management system for signs of internal failure or severe misfires.

### 2.4L Naturally Aspirated Flat-4 Engine (6-Speed Automatic)

The available 6-speed automatic transmission option pairs with the same 2.4-liter boxer engine, offering steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters for a more relaxed daily driving experience while retaining the car's sporty character.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission provides short, mechanical throws and a direct connection to the drivetrain, appealing to driving enthusiasts seeking maximum engagement.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission option features quick gear changes in manual mode and smooth shifting for city commutes, making it a versatile choice for daily drivers.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Accessories

Electrical complaints are generally minor, though auxiliary power ports and accessory lighting require routine checks during a pre-purchase inspection.

**License Plate Light Security**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have observed exterior license plate lights becoming loose or dangling from their mounting locations.
- **Solution:** Inspect housing clips and secure or replace damaged light assemblies as needed.

### Side Glass and Structure

Exterior body components are generally well-assembled, though isolated incidents involving glass integrity have been documented by owners.

**Unexpected Window Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Side door windows may occasionally crack or shatter completely into small pieces while driving without apparent impact from road debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected window glass and inspect the window regulator alignment and door frame tension.

## Safety

### Safety and Driver Assistance Systems

Advanced safety and driver assistance features add confidence on the road, but can occasionally trigger warning lights due to accessory electrical draws.

**False Safety System Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden dashboard alerts indicating SRS system failures, Reverse Automatic Braking (RAB) failure, or disabled EyeSight safety features.
- **Solution:** Check vehicle electrical loads and accessory power port usage, as aftermarket plug-in devices can occasionally interfere with sensitive circuit fuses.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Toyota GR86 is an exhilarating sports coupe that delivers exceptional driving dynamics for its price class. While most owners enjoy trouble-free motoring, potential buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the engine's health, service history, and electrical accessories. Paying close attention to these areas helps ensure a reliable and rewarding ownership experience.
