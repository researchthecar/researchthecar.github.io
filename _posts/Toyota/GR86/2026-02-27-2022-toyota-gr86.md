# 2022 Toyota GR86

### Overview
The 2022 Toyota GR86, a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe co-developed with Subaru, is generally praised for its engaging driving dynamics and affordable performance. As a relatively new model in its second generation, long-term reliability data is still accumulating. However, initial reports and the shared platform with the Subaru BRZ suggest a solid foundation. Owners generally report a positive experience, with the vehicle performing as expected for its class. The focus on driving pleasure means some components are designed for spirited use, but widespread premature failures are not a common concern among early adopters. Maintenance is typical for a performance-oriented vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Toyota GR86 demonstrates strong initial reliability, with no significant widespread mechanical issues reported for its engine or transmissions. Minor electrical glitches, particularly with the infotainment system, are the most commonly cited, though these are typically resolved with software updates. The chassis, suspension, exterior, and interior components are holding up well under normal use. As with any performance car, regular maintenance is crucial for optimal longevity. Overall, the GR86 is a dependable choice for enthusiasts seeking an engaging driving experience without major reliability concerns.
