# 2022 Toyota GR86 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Toyota GR86 delivers thrilling rear-wheel-drive performance and agile handling, making it a popular choice for sports car enthusiasts. While it offers an engaging driving experience, prospective buyers should review owner-reported concerns carefully during their pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Flat-4 Engine

The 2022 Toyota GR86 is powered by a naturally aspirated 2.4-liter flat-four engine producing approximately 228 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. This boxer engine provides sharp throttle response and robust power delivery, though used buyers should verify maintenance records and monitor oil levels closely.

**Engine Failure and Oil Starvation**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden engine failure while accelerating, sometimes accompanied by the oil pressure warning light illuminating or occurring without warning due to excessive oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Requires dealership inspection, which may lead to a short or long block engine replacement.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers short throws and a connected shift feel for enthusiast drivers. It is generally robust, though performance use warrants a thorough inspection of the clutch and synchros.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters provides convenient daily driving while maintaining sporty gear changes. Overall reliability appears steady, with no widespread recurring mechanical defects reported by owners.

## Exterior

### Turn Signal and Lighting System

Electrical reports highlight specific exterior lighting quirks, particularly related to rear turn signal operation under direct sunlight.

**Turn Signal Failure in Direct Sunlight**
- **Symptoms:** When direct sunlight shines on the rear of the vehicle, the rear turn signals may fail to function and the dashboard indicators will hyperflash.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or qualified technician to check for potential lighting module or sensor sensitivities.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V609000: Exterior Lighting:Turn Signal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=GR86&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative rear turn signals may not notify other drivers of a turning vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace both rear combination lamp assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2022 Toyota GR86, a comprehensive mechanical inspection and a careful review of service history are strongly advised. Paying close attention to engine performance and oil maintenance will help ensure a safer and more dependable ownership experience.
