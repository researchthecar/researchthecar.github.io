**Introduction**

The 2022 Toyota Corolla Cross is a compact crossover SUV offered with two engine and transmission options: a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine paired with a CVT (continuously variable transmission) and a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The 2.0-liter engine produces 169 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.5-liter engine produces 171 horsepower and 163 lb-ft of torque. The Corolla Cross is praised for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, it has also received criticism for its lackluster performance and uninspiring cabin design.

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaks:** The dashboard may develop creaks and rattles over time, especially on rough roads.
    * Solution: Applying foam padding or adhesive tape to the affected areas can help reduce noise.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, such as freezing or lagging.
    * Solution: Updating the software or resetting the system usually resolves the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if covered under warranty, otherwise $50 - $200
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing the AC or heater to not function properly.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty climate control module or actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Toyota Corolla Cross has relatively few reported issues. The interior seems to have some issues, but they are generally minor and inexpensive to fix. The engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and exterior components appear to be reliable.
