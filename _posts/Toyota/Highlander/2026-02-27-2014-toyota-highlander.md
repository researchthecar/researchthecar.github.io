# 2014 Toyota Highlander

### Overview
The 2014 Toyota Highlander is generally regarded as a reliable mid-size SUV, known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and practical features. It was offered with two primary powertrain options: a 2.7L 4-cylinder engine and a more powerful 3.5L V6 engine, both paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Toyota's reputation for durability extends to the Highlander, with most owners reporting satisfaction with its long-term performance. Common areas of concern, while not widespread, tend to be minor and related to specific components rather than systemic failures. Overall, the 2014 Highlander presents a solid choice for families seeking a dependable and well-rounded vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Toyota Highlander is a highly reliable vehicle, particularly for its powertrain components. While minor electrical issues like infotainment glitches and occasional battery drain have been noted, these are not pervasive and often addressable with software updates or component replacement. The majority of owners experience a trouble-free ownership experience, making it a sound investment for those seeking a dependable family SUV.
