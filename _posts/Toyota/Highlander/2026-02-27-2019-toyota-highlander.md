# 2019 Toyota Highlander

### Overview
The 2019 Toyota Highlander is generally regarded as a reliable mid-size SUV, building on Toyota's reputation for dependability. It offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and a good range of standard safety features. The vehicle was available with two primary powertrain options: a 2.5L four-cylinder engine (primarily for the hybrid model) and a more common 3.5L V6 engine. Both are known for their durability. Transmission options were primarily a continuously variable transmission (CVT) for the hybrid and an 8-speed automatic for the V6. Overall, the 2019 Highlander presents a solid choice for families seeking a practical and trustworthy vehicle with minimal widespread mechanical concerns.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Toyota Highlander stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its powertrain and core mechanical components demonstrating excellent durability. The most commonly reported issues are minor, such as occasional infotainment system glitches and, less frequently, premature battery failure. These are generally manageable and do not indicate systemic weaknesses. The SUV's robust construction and Toyota's engineering prowess contribute to a low likelihood of significant repair costs outside of routine maintenance. For buyers prioritizing long-term dependability and peace of mind, the 2019 Highlander remains a strong contender in the competitive mid-size SUV market.
