# 2009 Toyota Highlander

### Overview
The 2009 Toyota Highlander is generally regarded as a reliable mid-size SUV, known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and practical features. It was offered with two main powertrain options: a fuel-efficient 3.3L V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission, and a more powerful 3.5L V6 engine also with a 5-speed automatic. This generation of the Highlander typically performs well in terms of mechanical longevity and everyday usability. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the 2009 model year benefits from Toyota's reputation for building durable vehicles. Owners often praise its family-friendly nature and solid build quality, contributing to its positive standing in the used car market.
