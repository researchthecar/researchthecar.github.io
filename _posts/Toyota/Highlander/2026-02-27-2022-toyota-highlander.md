# 2022 Toyota Highlander Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Toyota Highlander offers a blend of family-friendly utility and solid overall construction, but prospective buyers should review certain recurring problem areas before purchasing. While Toyota enjoys a strong reputation for durability, owner reports highlight specific concerns regarding drivetrain components, safety system warnings, and minor body or interior attachments.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.4-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 265 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard power plant for recent models. It offers responsive acceleration and balanced efficiency for daily driving.

### 3.5L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 engine pushes out 295 horsepower and 263 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth power delivery and a robust towing capacity. It is widely regarded as a dependable and refined option.

### 2.5L Hybrid Inline-4

Pairing a 2.5-liter inline-4 engine with electric motors, this hybrid setup delivers 243 net horsepower with exceptional fuel economy. It provides reliable and efficient everyday commuting performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the gasoline engines to deliver smooth gear selection, though some owners have reported premature whining noises and mechanical failures.

**Transmission Whining Noise and Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice an abnormal whining noise during acceleration, erratic shifting behavior, or complete transmission failure around highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect the transmission assembly; replacement or major internal repairs may be required.

### Electronically Controlled CVT (Hybrid)

The continuously variable transmission designed for hybrid models manages power blending seamlessly between the gasoline engine and electric motors, generally maintaining a strong reliability record.

## Exterior

### Bumper and Fender Liners

Exterior plastic cladding and underbody shields are generally secure, but clips can degrade over time and lead to loose components.

**Lower Bumper and Fender Liner Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** The front lower bumper cover or fender liners loosen during highway driving, exposing underlying structural components or dragging against the tires.
- **Solution:** Secure or replace damaged fasteners, clips, or retainer tabs holding the plastic protective shields in place.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

The suite of electronic safety features provides comprehensive protection, though occasional sensor glitches can trigger multiple dashboard warnings.

**SRS and Pre-Collision System Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple warning lights illuminate simultaneously on the dashboard, including alerts for the pre-collision system, lane tracing assist, and airbag malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning at a dealership or specialized repair shop is necessary to reset or replace faulty sensors and control modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V310000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Highlander&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The load carrying capacity modification labels may not be permanent and can fade, becoming illegible. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An illegible label may allow the vehicle to be overloaded, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V720000: Structure:Body:Bumpers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Highlander&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached front bumper cover can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair or replace the upper and lower front bumper covers as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V419000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Highlander&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Tires with an insufficient load rating may be overloaded and fail, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GST will inspect and replace the tires as necessary, and install a new tire information label as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V452000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Highlander&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Tires with an insufficient load rating can be overloaded and fail, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** SET will inspect and replace the tires and information placard, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V128000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly:Recliner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Highlander&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat back that fails to lock may not properly restrain an occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the return springs in the seat back recliner assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Toyota Highlander remains a competent and versatile three-row crossover SUV, but potential buyers should thoroughly check the service history and test drive the vehicle to listen for any unusual drivetrain noises. While most examples offer dependable service, checking for unresolved safety sensor warnings and transmission performance is a smart precaution.
