# 2015 Toyota Highlander

### Overview
The 2015 Toyota Highlander is a mid-size SUV known for its practicality, comfortable ride, and family-friendly features. It was offered with a choice of two powertrains: a fuel-efficient 2.7L 4-cylinder engine or a more powerful 3.5L V6 engine, both paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Highlander generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of Toyota vehicles. Owners often praise its spacious interior, smooth handling, and good fuel economy for its class. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, some specific areas have seen occasional reports of issues that are worth noting for potential buyers.

**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not starting, battery dying prematurely.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw, replacement of faulty component (e.g., module, relay), or battery replacement.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Toyota Highlander is a highly reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The powertrain components, suspension, and exterior/interior elements generally perform without significant issues. The most commonly reported, though not widespread, concerns relate to the infotainment system experiencing occasional glitches and, less frequently, intermittent battery drain issues. These are typically manageable with software updates or diagnosis of specific electrical faults. Overall, the 2015 Highlander is a solid choice for those seeking a dependable family SUV with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
