**Introduction**

The 2014 Toyota Hilux is a mid-size pickup truck known for its reliability and capability. It is available with a choice of engines and transmissions, including a 2.8-liter diesel engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission or a 2.7-liter gasoline engine paired with a 5-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission.

Owners appreciate the Hilux for its rugged construction, spacious cabin, and comfortable ride. However, like any vehicle, the Hilux has some known issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

**2.8-liter Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Leaking fuel injectors can cause rough idling, misfiring, and decreased fuel efficiency. Solution: Replacement of the faulty fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **EGR Valve Issues:** Description/Symptoms: A faulty EGR valve can lead to reduced engine performance, increased emissions, and black smoke from the exhaust. Solution: Replacement of the EGR valve. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**2.7-liter Gasoline Engine**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Early model Hilux with the 2.7-liter engine may experience timing chain tensioner failure, resulting in a rattling noise and potential engine damage. Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and guide. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** Description/Symptoms: A leaking intake manifold gasket can cause a vacuum leak, leading to rough idling and reduced engine performance. Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leakage:** Description/Symptoms: A leak in the transmission case or seals can lead to fluid loss, resulting in transmission slipping and potential damage. Solution: Repair of the leak and replacement of the transmission fluid. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Harsh Shifting:** Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting can indicate transmission issues such as worn clutches or faulty solenoids. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the underlying issue. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficult Shifting:** Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears or jumping out of gear can be caused by worn synchros or linkage issues. Solution: Replacement of the faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:** Description/Symptoms: Overloaded Hilux models may experience leaf spring breakage, leading to a sagging rear end and reduced handling. Solution: Replacement of the broken leaf springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Shock Absorber Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged shock absorbers can cause poor handling, instability, and reduced braking performance. Solution: Replacement of the faulty shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Description/Symptoms: A faulty alternator can lead to battery drainage, dimming lights, and starting issues. Solution: Replacement of the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Battery Discharge:** Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain when the vehicle is parked can be caused by parasitic draw from accessories or electrical faults. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the underlying issue. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies can occur due to poor sealing or ventilation. Solution: Replacement of the affected headlights. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Tailgate Hinge Corrosion:** Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the tailgate hinges can cause difficulty opening or closing the tailgate. Solution: Replacement of the corroded hinges. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Interior**

* **Radio Malfunctions:** Description/Symptoms: Faulty head units or audio systems can exhibit issues such as no sound, distortion, or CD player problems. Solution: Replacement or repair of the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
* **Climate Control Problems:** Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control systems may result in inconsistent temperature regulation or inoperative functions. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the underlying issue. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+

**Summary**

The 2014 Toyota Hilux is generally a reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The severity and cost of the problems vary, ranging from minor inconveniences to more serious and expensive repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their decision-making process.
