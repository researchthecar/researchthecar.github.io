**Introduction:**
The 2015 Toyota Hilux is a reliable and capable mid-size pickup truck. It offers a choice of gasoline and diesel engines paired with either a manual or automatic transmission. The Hilux is known for its off-road prowess, spacious cabin, and strong safety features. However, like any vehicle, it does have some reported issues.

## **Engine:**

**2.4L Diesel**

- **Blown Turbocharger:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive black smoke, oil leaking from the turbocharger
    - Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Injector Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, poor fuel economy, excessive smoke
    - Solution: Replace the injectors
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**2.7L Gasoline**

- **No Issues Found**

## **Transmission:**

**5-Speed Manual**

- **Shifting Difficulty:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Hard to shift gears, especially into reverse
    - Solution: Replace the shift linkage
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

- **Slipping Gears:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips out of gear during acceleration
    - Solution: Replace the valve body
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension:**

- **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loud bang, loss of control, broken or sagging rear suspension
    - Solution: Replace the leaf springs
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    - Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 20V365000

## **Electrical:**

- **Alternator Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery light illuminates, loss of electrical power
    - Solution: Replace the alternator
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior:**

- **Tailgate Corrosion:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rust on the tailgate, especially around the hinges
    - Solution: Replace the tailgate
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Interior:**

- **Headliner Sagging:**
    - Description/Symptoms: The roof liner inside the cabin sags and may come loose
    - Solution: Re-attach or replace the headliner
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Dashboard Rattle:**
    - Description/Symptoms: The dashboard rattles or vibrates at certain speeds or road conditions
    - Solution: Tighten the dashboard and console bolts
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Summary:**

The 2015 Toyota Hilux is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has some reported issues. The most severe and expensive issues are turbocharger failure, injector failure, and leaf spring breakage. However, many of these issues are not widespread and can be addressed with proper maintenance and repairs.
