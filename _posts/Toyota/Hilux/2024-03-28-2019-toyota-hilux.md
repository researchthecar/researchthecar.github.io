**Introduction**

The 2019 Toyota Hilux is a mid-size pickup truck available in various engine and transmission configurations. The base model features a 2.4-liter gasoline engine and a 5-speed manual transmission, while higher trims offer a 2.8-liter turbo-diesel engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Hilux is praised for its ruggedness, reliability, and off-road capabilities, but it has also been subject to some reported issues.

**Engine**

**2.4-liter Gasoline Engine**
* **Leaking oil pan gasket:** Oil may leak from the area around the oil pan. Requires replacement of the gasket. ($100 - $250)
* **Faulty spark plugs:** Misfires and poor performance. Replace spark plugs. ($50 - $120+)
* **Carbon buildup on intake valves:** Reduced engine performance and fuel efficiency. Requires cleaning or replacing valves. ($500 - $1000+)

**2.8-liter Turbo-Diesel Engine**
* **Turbocharger failure:** Boost pressure loss, reduced power. Replace turbocharger. ($2000 - $4000+)
* **Injector problems:** Engine misfires, poor fuel economy. Replace injectors. ($800 - $1200+)
* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) system issues:** Engine performance problems, black smoke from exhaust. Clean or replace EGR valve. ($300 - $600+)

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**
* **Difficult shifting:** Gears may be hard to engage or shift smoothly. Requires gearbox adjustment or clutch replacement. ($500 - $1000+)
* **Clutch issues:** Slipping or chattering clutch. Replace clutch assembly. ($800 - $1200+)

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Transmission slips or jerks:** Hesitation or rough shifting. May require transmission fluid flush or replacement. ($200 - $500+)
* **Torque converter failure:** Loss of power, overheating. Replace torque converter. ($1500 - $2500+)
* **Valve body problems:** Transmission shifting issues. Replace valve body. ($800 - $1200+)

**Suspension**
* **Leaking shock absorbers:** Oil leaking from shock absorbers. Replace shock absorbers. ($400 - $800+)
* **Worn suspension bushings:** Noise, vibrations, and reduced handling. Replace bushings. ($150 - $300+)
* **Leaf spring breakage:** Snap or crack in leaf spring. Replace leaf spring. ($400 - $600+)

**Electrical**
* **Alternator problems:** Battery drain, dim lights. Replace alternator. ($500 - $800+)
* **Starter failure:** Engine won't start. Replace starter. ($300 - $500+)
* **Electrical shorts:** Fuses blown, lights not working. Repair electrical system. ($100 - $500+)

**Exterior**
* **Bedliner peeling or cracking:** UV damage or moisture penetration. Reapply or replace bedliner. ($300 - $600+)

**Interior**
* **Dashboard rattles:** Vibration or noise from dashboard. Requires dashboard disassembly and adjustment. ($100 - $300+)
* **Climate control issues:** Malfunctioning air conditioning or heating. Requires repair or replacement of climate control components. ($200 - $700+)

**Summary**

While the 2019 Toyota Hilux is generally a reliable and rugged vehicle, it has experienced some issues. Engine and transmission problems are the most common, with potential costs ranging from hundreds to thousands of dollars to repair. Suspension and electrical issues are also reported, though less frequently. Interior issues are relatively minor and may not require expensive repairs. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary, but it's important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing.
