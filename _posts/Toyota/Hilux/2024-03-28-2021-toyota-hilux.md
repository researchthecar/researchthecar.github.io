## 2021 Toyota Hilux

The 2021 Toyota Hilux is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its reliability, off-road capabilities, and towing capacity. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its issues.

The Hilux is available with a 2.8L turbo-diesel engine that produces 201 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, or a 2.4L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine that produces 221 horsepower and 343 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, a 6-speed automatic, and a 6-speed automatic with sequential shift mode.

## Engine

### 2.8L Turbo-Diesel

**Engine Overheating**
Description/Symptoms: The engine may overheat, causing the temperature gauge to rise and the engine to lose power.
Solution: Replace the thermostat or water pump.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual

**Gear Grinding**
Description/Symptoms: The gears may grind when shifting, especially when shifting into first or second gear.
Solution: Replace the synchronizers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### 6-Speed Automatic

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip between gears, causing a loss of power and acceleration.
Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter, or rebuild the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**No Transmission Issues Found**

## Suspension

**Rear Leaf Spring Sagging**
Description/Symptoms: The rear leaf springs may sag, causing the rear of the vehicle to sit lower and affect handling.
Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

## Interior

**Dashboard Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle over bumps or rough roads.
Solution: Tighten loose screws or apply sound dampening material.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Air Conditioning Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The air conditioning system may not blow cold air or may make noises.
Solution: Recharge the refrigerant, replace the compressor, or repair any leaks.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

## Overall Summary

While the 2021 Toyota Hilux is generally a reliable vehicle, it is not immune to issues. Some of the more severe and costly issues include engine overheating, turbocharger failure, transmission slipping, and suspension sagging. Other issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Hilux is a capable and dependable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of potential issues before purchasing one.
