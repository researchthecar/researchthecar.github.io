## 2022 Toyota Hilux

The 2022 Toyota Hilux is a mid-size pickup truck that is offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 2.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 150 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or a six-speed automatic transmission. The optional engine is a 2.8-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 201 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The Hilux is praised for its ruggedness, reliability, and off-road capabilities. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of interior refinement and advanced safety features.

### Engine

**2.4-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Low Oil Pressure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warning light illuminates, engine knocking, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace oil pump, inspect oil passages
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.8-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noise
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding Noises**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise during gear changes
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping when shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Leaf Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride, clunking noise
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, loss of control, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead frequently, electrical components malfunctioning
    * Solution: Replace battery, inspect charging system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dimming headlights, electrical components failing
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Tailgate Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on tailgate, paint peeling
    * Solution: Replace tailgate, repaint
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors will not lock or unlock, keyless entry not working
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Dashboard Squeaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattles and squeaks from dashboard area
    * Solution: Apply sound deadening material, tighten loose panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat Belt Retractor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belts do not retract properly
    * Solution: Replace seat belt retractors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2022 Toyota Hilux is a rugged and reliable pickup truck that has some significant issues to be aware of. The most common issues include engine problems, transmission issues, and suspension issues. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to factor in the cost of repairs when considering purchasing a Hilux. However, the Hilux also has many positive qualities, such as its off-road capabilities and high resale value.
