**Introduction:**

The 2017 Toyota Avanza is a versatile MPV with a spacious cabin and fuel-efficient engine options. It offers a 1.3L engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or 4-speed automatic transmission, and a 1.5L engine mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Avanza is praised for its practicality, affordability, and low maintenance costs. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

* **1.3L Engine**
    * **Valve Leaks:** Premature valve leaks can occur, leading to increased oil consumption and loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace affected valves and seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **1.5L Engine**
    * **Timing Chain Noise:** A rattling noise may be heard from the timing chain, especially during cold starts or acceleration.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission:**
    * **Gear Difficulty Engaging:** Difficulty engaging gears, particularly when shifting from neutral to 1st or 2nd gear.
        * Solution: Replace gearshift linkage or clutch master and slave cylinders.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Slipping Gears:** Transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting between gears.
        * Solution: Replace or repair transmission valve body or solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Plastic Trim Rattle:** Loose or ill-fitting plastic trim panels may rattle or squeak.
        * Solution: Tighten or replace affected panels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioning Inoperative:** Failure of the air conditioning system, resulting in lack of cooling or airflow.
        * Solution: Repair or replace failed components, such as the compressor, condenser, or expansion valve.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Toyota Avanza is generally a reliable vehicle, but some owners have encountered issues with the engine, transmission, and interior components. The severity of these issues varies, with some requiring significant repairs and others being relatively minor. The cost of repair can range from a few hundred dollars to over $2,000, depending on the issue and the extent of repairs needed. Overall, the Avanza remains a practical and affordable choice for those seeking a versatile MPV, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
