**Introduction to the 2022 Toyota Avanza**

The 2022 Toyota Avanza is a compact MPV manufactured by Toyota Motor Corporation. It is offered with a 1.3-liter 1NR-VE inline-4 gasoline engine with 97 horsepower and 121 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a five-speed manual transmission or a CVT automatic transmission.

It is praised for its spacious interior, affordable price, and fuel efficiency. However, it has received some criticism for its lack of modern safety features and somewhat dated appearance.

**Engine**

**1.3L 1NR-VE Inline-4**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine burns excessive oil, requiring frequent topping up.
    * Solution: Replace the defective piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner fails, leading to a loss of engine power and rough running.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty tensioner and timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Noises:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission makes grinding noises when shifting gears, especially into second and third.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty synchronizers within the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT Automatic**

* **Slipping or Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips or hesitates during acceleration or gear changes.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the CVT unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front struts fail, causing a rough ride and clunking noises.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per strut (both sides usually replaced)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or identify and fix the electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Door Handles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handles develop rust, despite the vehicle's age.
    * Solution: Replace the affected door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250 per handle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **A/C Fan Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The A/C fan stops working intermittently or blows weakly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty blower motor or resistor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2022 Toyota Avanza is a popular choice for those seeking a budget-friendly family vehicle, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that can arise. Some of the issues, such as excessive oil consumption and timing chain tensioner failure, can be severe and costly to repair. Other issues, such as grinding noises in the manual transmission or rust on the door handles, are less severe but can still be an annoyance. Overall, it is recommended to thoroughly research and consider these issues before purchasing an Avanza, and to schedule regular maintenance to help prevent or mitigate them.
