**Introduction:**

The 2014 Toyota Avanza is a compact MPV that seats up to seven passengers. It is available with two engine options: a 1.3L inline-four and a 1.5L inline-four. Both engines are mated to a five-speed manual transmission or a four-speed automatic transmission. The Avanza is praised for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and reliability. However, it has received some criticism for its dated design and lack of modern features.

**Engine**

**1.3L Inline-Four**
* No Issues Found

**1.5L Inline-Four**
* **Engine Overheating**
  * Description/Symptoms: The engine overheats without warning, causing the temperature gauge to rise rapidly.
  * Solution: Replace the faulty thermostat or water pump.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**
* No Issues Found

**Four-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting Problems**
  * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips or hesitates when shifting gears.
  * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter, or rebuild the transmission.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise**
  * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension makes a clunking or squeaking noise when going over bumps.
  * Solution: Replace the worn suspension bushings or struts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Draining**
  * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly even when the vehicle is not in use.
  * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or alternator.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
  * Description/Symptoms: Condensation forms inside the headlights after washing the vehicle or during humid weather.
  * Solution: Replace the headlights or drill small holes for ventilation.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
  * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard rattles or makes a buzzing sound when driving on rough roads.
  * Solution: Tighten or replace the loose trim pieces.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2014 Toyota Avanza is a reliable and practical vehicle with a spacious interior and good fuel economy. However, it does have a few common issues, such as engine overheating, transmission shifting problems, and suspension noise. Fortunately, these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix.
