# 2020 Toyota Yaris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Toyota Yaris is a subcompact car known for its efficiency and practicality, largely built on Mazda engineering for its final model year. While owner complaints are very sparse, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history records carefully. As with any used subcompact, a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains the best way to catch minor electrical or mechanical quirks before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L 4-Cylinder

The 1.5-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine produces approximately 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as a reliable, highly efficient powerplant suited for daily commuting.

**Starting and Battery Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported the engine laboring to start accompanied by a drained or dead battery.
- **Solution:** Have the battery, charging system, and starter tested, and replace the battery if it fails load testing.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels and avoids the common longevity concerns associated with some CVTs. It provides a refined driving dynamic for the subcompact class.

### 6-Speed Manual

The 6-speed manual transmission offers direct gear selection for drivers who prefer a traditional shifting experience. It is mechanically robust and pairs well with the efficient 4-cylinder engine.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features help protect occupants, though sensor calibration is critical.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected, abrupt braking events while navigating highway road work or when parking near other vehicles.
- **Solution:** Ensure driver assistance sensors are clean and check with a dealer for any applicable software updates or calibrations.

### Seat Belts

Standard restraint systems designed to secure occupants in the event of a collision.

**Seat Belt Buckle Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The front passenger seat belt may occasionally fail to securely click and fasten into the buckle receiver.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt assembly and buckle mechanism, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V617000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Yaris&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the low-pressure fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Toyota Yaris maintains a strong reputation for dependability backed by minimal owner complaints. Used car shoppers should verify that all open recalls have been completed by a certified dealer and perform a standard check of the electrical and safety systems during a test drive.
