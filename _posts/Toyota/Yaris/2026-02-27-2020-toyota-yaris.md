# 2020 Toyota Yaris

### Overview
The 2020 Toyota Yaris, particularly in its European and Australian configurations, is generally regarded as a reliable subcompact car. It benefits from Toyota's long-standing reputation for durability and low running costs. The model typically features efficient gasoline engines and a well-regarded automatic transmission. While individual component failures can occur in any vehicle, widespread systemic issues are uncommon for the 2020 Yaris. Owners generally report satisfaction with its dependability, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing longevity and minimal unexpected repair bills. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its reliability.
