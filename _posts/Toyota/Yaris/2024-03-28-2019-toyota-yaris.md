The 2019 Toyota Yaris is a subcompact car offered with a 1.5-liter inline-4 engine and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The engine produces 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque. The Yaris is known for its fuel efficiency, reliability, and affordability, but it has also been criticized for its lack of power and features.

**Engine**

* **1.5-liter inline-4**

    * **Excessive oil consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine burns excessive amounts of oil, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings or engine block.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

    * **Hesitation and shuddering**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or shudders during acceleration or shifting.
        * Solution: Update the transmission control module (TCM) software or replace the valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, preventing the vehicle from starting.
        * Solution: Replace the defective battery or alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield wiper motor failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers stop working properly.
        * Solution: Replace the wiper motor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Air conditioning malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The air conditioning system blows warm or no air.
        * Solution: Replace the compressor, condenser, or evaporator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Toyota Yaris is a reliable and fuel-efficient subcompact car, but it has some notable issues, including excessive oil consumption, transmission problems, battery drain, and windshield wiper motor failure. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can range from minor to major.
