# 2017 Toyota Yaris Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Toyota Yaris is generally recognized as a dependable and economical subcompact car, offering straightforward transportation for daily commuters. While owner-reported complaints are relatively limited, buyers should inspect the powertrain and electrical systems during a pre-purchase test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L 4-Cylinder

The 2017 Toyota Yaris is powered by a 1.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing approximately 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated engine is known for straightforward mechanical reliability and solid fuel economy in everyday driving.

### Engine and Starting Electrical

Electrical and starting controls manage vehicle ignition and power distribution.

**Intermittent Stalling and Starting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall after being shifted into gear or cycle on and off while starting or driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing of the electrical system, fuel delivery, and ignition components is recommended to locate intermittent faults.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 5-speed manual transmission offers direct gear control for drivers who prefer a hands-on shifting experience, though rare clutch component wear has been reported.

**Clutch and Gear Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a noticeable shudder sensation from a dead stop or when accelerating through higher gears at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the clutch assembly, flywheel, and gear linkage for premature wear or alignment issues.

### 4-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 4-speed automatic transmission provides steady, simple gear changes suited for city commuting, generally maintaining a dependable long-term service record.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power, though routine inspection of pads and rotors helps prevent premature wear.

**Front Rotor Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Front brake rotors may experience premature wear or warping under normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for thickness and surface condition, and replace them if excessive wear or vibration is detected.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Supplemental restraint systems rely on complex sensors and wiring to ensure proper deployment in a collision.

**Airbag Warning Codes**
- **Symptoms:** Diagnostic scanners may reveal airbag error codes, such as circuit open faults, even if no dashboard warning light is immediately visible.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the seat wiring harnesses and airbag sensor connections to resolve fault codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V319000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Yaris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The wire harnesses for the side air bag sensors located within the front doors may crack and corrode, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the front seat side air bags and the curtain shield air bags. In the event of a crash, front seat side air bags and curtain shield air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the wire harness assembly in the front doors, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V503000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Yaris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An incorrect label may lead to unintentionally overloading the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 17V295000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Yaris&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A spare tire that is not inflated to the proper value may not perform as intended, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Gulf States will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the spare tires, adjusting their pressure as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Toyota Yaris remains a sensible choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking efficient urban transportation. Prospective owners should perform a thorough test drive to check for manual transmission clutch smoothness, engine idle stability, and proper operation of all safety and electrical components.
