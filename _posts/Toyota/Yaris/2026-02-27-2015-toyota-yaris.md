### Overview
The 2015 Toyota Yaris is generally recognized for its strong reliability, a hallmark of Toyota's engineering. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for specific engine and transmission configurations.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Toyota Yaris stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its powertrain and core mechanical components demonstrating exceptional durability. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning intermittent power window operation and occasional infotainment system glitches. These are generally not critical failures and are often resolvable with relatively minor repairs. Overall, the Yaris offers excellent long-term value and peace of mind for owners, with a low probability of encountering significant or costly mechanical problems.
