# 2016 Toyota Mirai Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Toyota Mirai is a pioneering hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicle that offers a zero-emission driving experience. For prospective used-car buyers, it is important to consider both the innovative hydrogen powertrain and the unique ownership logistics involved with alternative fuel infrastructure.

---

## Engine

### Hydrogen Fuel Cell System

The 2016 Toyota Mirai utilizes a hydrogen fuel cell stack generating electricity to power an electric drive motor, producing approximately 151 horsepower. This cutting-edge propulsion system requires careful evaluation of local hydrogen fueling infrastructure and refueling reliability.

**Fuel Range and Infrastructure Limitations**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report very poor practical range per tank, often requiring frequent refuelings every few days or less than 150 miles per tank in real-world conditions.
- **Solution:** Plan routes carefully around functioning hydrogen refueling stations and monitor local hydrogen station availability.

**Unexpected Powertrain Shutdown and Towing**
- **Symptoms:** Owner reports indicate the vehicle may become unreliable when local hydrogen stations are out of service, occasionally requiring towing.
- **Solution:** Ensure access to backup transportation and verify reliable hydrogen supply lines in your local driving area before purchase.

**Loss of Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected hesitation or a sudden loss of acceleration while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel cell control modules and electronic throttle systems inspected by a certified Toyota technician.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

The Mirai uses a simple single-speed direct drive automatic transmission optimized for electric motor characteristics. It generally operates seamlessly with minimal mechanical complications.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Toyota Mirai is an intriguing alternative-fuel vehicle, but its viability as a used car depends heavily on local hydrogen station availability and fueling consistency. Buyers should thoroughly research regional refueling infrastructure and verify mechanical condition before committing to purchase.
