# 2022 Toyota Mirai

### Overview
The 2022 Toyota Mirai represents a significant step forward in hydrogen fuel cell technology, offering a zero-emission driving experience with impressive performance and range. As a relatively new technology in the mainstream automotive market, its long-term reliability is still being established. However, Toyota's extensive experience with hybrid systems and its reputation for building durable vehicles provide a strong foundation. Early data suggests a high level of integration and robust engineering, particularly concerning the fuel cell stack and hydrogen storage systems. While specific component failures are rare, the complexity of the powertrain necessitates specialized maintenance and diagnostics.

The 2022 Toyota Mirai demonstrates strong initial reliability, benefiting from Toyota's established engineering principles and the maturity of its fuel cell technology. The Mirai is a promising indicator of the future of hydrogen-powered transportation.
