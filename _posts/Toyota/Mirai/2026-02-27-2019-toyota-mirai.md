# 2019 Toyota Mirai Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Toyota Mirai is a hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicle (FCEV) offering zero-tailpipe-emissions driving. As an alternative-fuel vehicle, prospective used-car buyers should carefully weigh its niche powertrain technology against real-world ownership factors such as hydrogen refueling infrastructure availability. Owner reports submitted to federal safety regulators highlight occasional system warnings and rare operational glitches.

---

## Engine

### Hydrogen Fuel Cell System

The 2019 Toyota Mirai utilizes a hydrogen fuel cell stack paired with an electric motor, generating approximately 151 horsepower. This innovative powertrain delivers smooth, silent electric drive but requires specialized infrastructure that remains limited outside select regions.

**Fuel System and Refueling Infrastructure Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported extreme challenges with finding functioning hydrogen refueling stations and concerns regarding fuel pump reliability.
- **Solution:** Verify local hydrogen station availability and maintain regular inspections of the fuel supply and battery systems.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

The Mirai uses a straightforward single-speed direct drive automatic transmission routing power to the front wheels. This simplified direct-drive unit is generally robust and free of complex gear-shifting mechanical issues.

## Steering

### Vehicle Speed Control and Steering

**Power Steering and Acceleration Glitch**
- **Symptoms:** Rare reports indicate unexpected acceleration issues coupled with a sudden loss of power steering assist during braking or maneuvering.
- **Solution:** Inspect electronic control units, steering sensors, and throttle systems immediately if unusual handling or braking behavior occurs.

## Safety

### Air Bags

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected illumination of the supplemental restraint system or air bag warning light on the dashboard display.
- **Solution:** Have a certified dealership or technician inspect the occupant detection sensors, associated wiring harnesses, and control modules.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Toyota Mirai offers an advanced hydrogen fuel cell experience, but ownership requires careful consideration of regional hydrogen fueling station networks. While the mechanical powertrain is largely dependable, used-car shoppers should thoroughly inspect vehicle electronics, dashboard warning indicators, and local fuel availability before purchasing.
