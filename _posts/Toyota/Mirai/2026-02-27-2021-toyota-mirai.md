# 2021 Toyota Mirai

### Overview
The 2021 Toyota Mirai represents a significant advancement in hydrogen fuel cell technology, offering a zero-emission driving experience with impressive performance and range. As a second-generation model, it builds upon the foundation of its predecessor, incorporating a more refined design, enhanced fuel cell system, and improved driving dynamics. Toyota's commitment to hydrogen power is evident in the Mirai's sophisticated engineering, aiming to provide a practical and sustainable alternative to traditional internal combustion engine vehicles. Reliability data for this advanced powertrain is still emerging, but initial indications suggest a strong focus on durability and quality, characteristic of Toyota's reputation.
