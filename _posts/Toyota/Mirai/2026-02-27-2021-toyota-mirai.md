# 2021 Toyota Mirai Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Navigating the used market for this hydrogen fuel cell vehicle requires careful consideration of alternative fuel infrastructure and overall vehicle health. While traditional mechanical data is sparse, prospective buyers should thoroughly evaluate the vehicle's unique fuel system and ensure local hydrogen availability before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### Hydrogen Fuel Cell System

The Mirai utilizes a hydrogen fuel cell power system that generates electricity to drive an electric motor. It offers eco-friendly operation, but buyers should verify specialized maintenance support and hydrogen station availability.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive

Power is sent to the wheels via a single-speed direct-drive transmission paired with the electric motor. This setup is generally mechanically simple and low-maintenance compared to multi-speed automatic gearboxes.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V239000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Mirai&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The Skid Control ECU software may not turn on the Vehicle Stability Control (VSC) system when the vehicle is restarted after the driver has disabled the VSC system. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Driving with a deactivated stability.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Skid Control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

Overall, the Toyota Mirai is an unconventional alternative-fuel sedan that requires a dedicated local hydrogen refueling network. Buyers should perform a comprehensive inspection of the fuel system and confirm ongoing fuel availability before making a commitment.
