**Introduction**

The 2017 Toyota Mirai is a hydrogen-powered electric vehicle with an impressive range of up to 312 miles on a single tank of hydrogen. The Mirai is powered by a 153 horsepower electric motor mated to a unique 11.3 kWh lithium-ion battery pack. Despite its advanced powertrain, the Mirai has received mixed reviews from consumers. Some praise its spacious interior and comfortable ride, while others have encountered various issues that can be costly to fix.

**Engine**

* **Hydrogen Fuel Cell System**

  * **Issue:** Faulty hydrogen fuel cells
  * **Symptoms:** Loss of power, decreased fuel efficiency, engine warning lights
  * **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel cells
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $10,000+
  * **Recalls:** 19V-604

**Electrical**

* **12V Battery**

  * **Issue:** premature battery failure
  * **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions, warning lights
  * **Solution:** Battery replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Power Control Module**

  * **Issue:** Software glitches
  * **Symptoms:** Erratic power delivery, engine stalling, warning lights
  * **Solution:** Software updates, module replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
  * **Recalls:** 19V-603

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**

  * **Issue:** Condensation buildup
  * **Symptoms:** Foggy headlights, reduced visibility
  * **Solution:** Headlight lens seal replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System**

  * **Issue:** A/C compressor failure
  * **Symptoms:** Weak or no cooling, unusual noises
  * **Solution:** Compressor replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
  * **Recalls:** 19V-602

* **Center Console Trim**

  * **Issue:** Loose or rattling trim
  * **Symptoms:** Noises coming from the center console area
  * **Solution:** Trim alignment or replacement
  * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
  * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

While the 2017 Toyota Mirai offers an impressive range and environmentally friendly powertrain, it has experienced several issues related to its hydrogen fuel cell system, electrical components, and interior trim. These issues can be costly to fix and may compromise the vehicle's reliability. Consumers may want to consider other options or thoroughly research the potential problems before purchasing a Mirai.
