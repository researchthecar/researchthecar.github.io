**Introduction:**

The 2015 Toyota Sienna is a popular minivan that offers spacious seating, a comfortable ride, and a variety of features. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 266 horsepower and 245 lb-ft of torque. The Sienna is available with either an 8-speed automatic transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

Owners generally praise the Sienna for its reliability, safety, and practicality. However, some have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported that their Sienna consumes excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing a rattling noise and potentially catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission:**

* **Shift Hesitation:** Some owners have experienced hesitation or jerkiness when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission control module software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT):**

* **Belt Slippage:** The transmission belt can slip, causing loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace transmission belt and pulleys.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Axle Bushing Failure:** The rear axle bushings can wear out prematurely, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace rear axle bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported that their Sienna's battery drains quickly even when the car is not being used.
    * Solution: Check for electrical system parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** The headlights can collect condensation, which can affect visibility.
    * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Glitch:** The navigation system can freeze or display incorrect information.
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Toyota Sienna is a reliable and practical minivan. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
