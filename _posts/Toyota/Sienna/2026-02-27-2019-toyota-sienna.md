# 2019 Toyota Sienna Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Toyota Sienna, buyers generally find a dependable, spacious family hauler powered by a capable V6 engine. However, owner reports highlight a few mechanical and electrical areas to inspect closely before purchasing, particularly concerning transmission longevity and specific accessory failures.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces around 296 horsepower and 263 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for highway driving and hauling. Overall, this powertrain is regarded as durable, though routine checks of fuel system components are recommended.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported engine hesitation, lagging when the accelerator is pushed, or unexpected stalling due to fuel delivery issues.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel pump inspected and replaced if affected by known service campaigns or component failure.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (UA80)

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel drive or available all-wheel drive, offering smooth shifts and efficient highway cruising. Buyers should listen carefully during a test drive for any unusual whining or hesitation.

**Transmission Whining and Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note a distinct whining noise coming from the final gear area upon acceleration, sometimes progressing to complete transmission failure.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation; severe cases necessitate a full transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $9,000 to $10,000

## Exterior

### Sliding Door and Lighting Electronics

Electrical accessories provide convenience features like power sliding doors and advanced lighting, but body-control harnesses and exterior LED strips require functional testing prior to purchase.

**Sliding Door Electrical Harness and LED Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power sliding doors may fail to open completely, leaving internal electrical cables exposed, or daytime running LED strips may flicker and fail.
- **Solution:** Inspect door wiring harnesses for wear and replace malfunctioning LED light assemblies or door sensors.

## Safety

### Seat Belt

Safety features generally perform well in protecting occupants, but proper physical inspection of all seatbelt buckles and retractors is advised on a used model.

**Seat Belt Jamming**
- **Symptoms:** Second-row passenger seatbelts can unexpectedly jam and become completely unusable, leaving occupants unrestrained.
- **Solution:** Replace the jammed seatbelt assembly mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V012000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V318000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The incorrect air bag sensor readings could cause the affected air bags to unintentionally deploy or to not deploy when intended, increasing the risk of a crash and/or injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger side front door sub assembly with a new one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V532000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the steering shaft extension completely fractures, it can cause a loss of steering, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Braun has notified owners, and dealers will replace the steering shaft extensions, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V410000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The brake lines may have incorrect tube nut fittings installed, which may cause a pressure drop within the braking system. The reduction in the braking system pressure may cause a loss of braking power in the rear braking system, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Braun has notified owners, and dealers will install an adaptor to correct the issue, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V503000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An incorrect label may lead to unintentionally overloading the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 19V244000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The text on the load capacity label may become illegible. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." If the driver cannot read the label, they may unknowingly overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GST will notify owners, and dealers will provide corrected load carrying capacity modification labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Toyota Sienna remains a strong contender in the used minivan market thanks to its powerful V6 engine and versatile interior space. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the transmission for any whining noises and verify that all power sliding doors, seatbelts, and fuel system components operate without issue.
