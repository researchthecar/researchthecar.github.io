**Introduction:**

The 2022 Toyota Sienna is a popular minivan that offers a spacious interior, versatile seating options, and a powerful V6 engine. It is a popular choice for families and those who need a vehicle with ample cargo space. The Sienna is available with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, and it comes in a variety of trim levels.

**Engine:**

* 3.5L V6 (296 hp, 263 lb-ft)

**Transmission:**

* 8-speed automatic

**Suspension:**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

**No Issues Found**

**Exterior:**

**Rear Bumper Cover**
Description/Symptoms: The rear bumper cover may crack or break. This is caused by a design flaw that makes the bumper cover susceptible to damage from minor impacts.
Solution: Replace the rear bumper cover.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Center Console**
Description/Symptoms: The center console may rattle or squeak. This is caused by a loose or defective mounting point.
Solution: Tighten or replace the mounting point.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

* **Seatbelts**
Description/Symptoms: The seatbelts may not retract properly. This is a safety hazard that could cause the occupant to be ejected from the vehicle in the event of a crash.
Solution: Replace the seatbelts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: Safety Recall 22V-152 (Seatbelts may not Retract Properly)

**Summary:**

The 2022 Toyota Sienna is overall a good vehicle. However, there are a few issues that have been reported, including a cracked rear bumper cover, a rattling center console, and seatbelts that may not retract properly. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they are something to be aware of when considering purchasing a Sienna.
