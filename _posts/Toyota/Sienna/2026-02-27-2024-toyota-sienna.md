# 2024 Toyota Sienna Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Toyota Sienna continues its legacy as a practical and efficient family mover, relying exclusively on a hybrid powertrain to deliver impressive fuel economy. As a modern model year, it benefits from advanced safety suites and comfortable accommodations for up to eight passengers. Because it is still early in its ownership lifecycle, comprehensive long-term reliability data is still developing, though owner reports remain relatively sparse.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid

This 2.5-liter four-cylinder hybrid engine pairs with electric motors to produce a combined 245 horsepower. It offers exceptional fuel efficiency for a large minivan and generally demonstrates strong, reliable everyday performance.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The ECVT seamlessly manages power delivery between the gas engine and electric motors, routing power to either front-wheel drive or electronic all-wheel drive. It is designed for smooth operation and long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V877000: Equipment Adaptive/Mobility:Wheelchair Restraints/Securement:Latch/Anchor:](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The retractors may not lock, preventing the wheelchair from being properly secured. An unsecured wheelchair can move during transit, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Toyota Sienna presents a compelling option for used-car buyers looking for maximum fuel efficiency and modern family features in a minivan. Its hybrid powertrain is well-established within the Toyota lineup, offering peace of mind alongside low operating costs. As with any newer used vehicle, conducting a standard pre-purchase inspection is recommended to ensure everything is in proper order.
