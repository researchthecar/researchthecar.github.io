# 2023 Toyota Sienna

### Overview
The 2023 Toyota Sienna, a popular minivan, generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of the Toyota brand. It is equipped with a standard hybrid powertrain across all trims, combining a 2.5L four-cylinder engine with electric motors for efficient performance. This integrated system is designed for longevity and has a proven track record. The Sienna's construction and interior materials are typically robust, contributing to a positive ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the Sienna's design and engineering focus on minimizing common automotive problems, making it a dependable choice for families and long-distance travel.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Toyota Sienna stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, primarily due to its standard and well-established hybrid powertrain. Most major mechanical components, including the engine and transmission, show no significant widespread issues. Minor concerns are predominantly related to the infotainment system's software and, in some instances, the longevity of the 12V battery, which is a common point of failure across many modern vehicles. Overall, the Sienna offers a dependable and low-maintenance ownership experience, making it a strong contender in the minivan segment for those prioritizing reliability.
