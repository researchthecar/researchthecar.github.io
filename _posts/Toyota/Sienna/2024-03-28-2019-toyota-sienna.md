**2019 Toyota Sienna**

The 2019 Toyota Sienna is a minivan that offers a spacious cabin, a smooth ride, and a variety of standard features. It is available with a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 296 horsepower and 263 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Some of the things people like about the Sienna include its roomy interior, comfortable seats, and good fuel economy. However, some people have also complained about its bland styling, lack of higher-performance engine options, and high price.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6 Engine**
    * **Valve Train Noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: Tapping or clicking noise from the engine
        * Solution: Cam follower replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups
        * Solution: Piston ring replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears
        * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or transmission rebuild
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts**
    * **Strut Mounts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front end
        * Solution: Strut mount replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorbers**
    * **Premature Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking or damaged shock absorbers
        * Solution: Shock absorber replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Premature Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, requiring frequent replacement
        * Solution: Battery replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**
    * **Voltage Regulator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical problems, dim or flickering lights, battery not charging
        * Solution: Alternator replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield**
    * **Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sudden appearance of cracks on the windshield
        * Solution: Windshield replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Sliding Doors**
    * **Malfunctioning**
        * Description/Symptoms: Doors do not open or close properly, or make unusual noises
        * Solution: Door motor or track repair or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console**
    * **Plastic Trim**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling trim panels
        * Solution: Trim panel replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control**
    * **Cabin Filter**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced airflow from vents, musty or unpleasant odors
        * Solution: Cabin filter replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $30 - $50+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Toyota Sienna is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be expensive to repair. Before purchasing a Sienna, potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and consider the estimated repair costs.
