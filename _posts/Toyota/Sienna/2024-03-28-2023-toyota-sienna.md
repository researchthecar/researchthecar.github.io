**Introduction to the 2023 Toyota Sienna**

The 2023 Toyota Sienna is a hybrid minivan with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It is powered by a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine and two electric motors, producing a combined 245 horsepower. The Sienna is appreciated for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and fuel efficiency. However, it has received some criticism for its lack of power, CVT transmission, and occasionally underwhelming technology.

**Summary**

The 2023 Toyota Sienna is a reliable and practical minivan with a few minor issues. The CVT transmission's performance and occasional electrical glitches are the most common concerns reported by owners. While these issues are not severe and can be addressed with repairs, they may be worth considering when making a purchase decision.
