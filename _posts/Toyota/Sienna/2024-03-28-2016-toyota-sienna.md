**Introduction:**

The 2016 Toyota Sienna is an 8-passenger minivan that is known for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. It offers two engine options: a 2.7-liter 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Despite its positive reputation, the 2016 Sienna has been reported to have a few common issues.

**Engine**

**2.7-Liter 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and engine damage
    * Solution: Repair or replace pistons and piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V570000

**3.5-Liter V6 Engine**

* **Timing Chain Rattle:** Noisy rattle or ticking sound on cold engine start
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Shudder:** Rough or jerking sensation during gear changes
    * Solution: Replace or reprogram transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:** Fluid leak from the transmission pan or seals
    * Solution: Replace or reseal transmission pan gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:** Loose or broken sway bar links, causing clunking sounds and poor handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Automatic Headlights Failure:** Headlights fail to turn on or off automatically
    * Solution: Replace body control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:** Rapid battery discharge, leading to starting issues
    * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty electrical components (e.g., alternator)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cover Sagging:** Loosened or detached rear bumper cover
    * Solution: Reattach or replace bumper cover brackets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Sliding Door Malfunction:** Difficulty opening or closing the power sliding doors
    * Solution: Inspect and repair faulty door motor or sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Noise or shaking coming from the dashboard
    * Solution: Reposition or secure dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Toyota Sienna is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has a few known issues. The most severe and expensive issue is the excessive oil consumption in the 2.7-liter 4-cylinder engine, which can lead to engine damage. The cost of repairs for the common issues can vary widely, but most can be resolved for under $1,500.
