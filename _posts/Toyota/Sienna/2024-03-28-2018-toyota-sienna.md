**Introduction**

The 2018 Toyota Sienna is a popular minivan offered with two engine and transmission options:

* 3.5L V6 with 8-speed automatic transmission (296 hp, 263 lb-ft torque)
* 2.5L I-4 hybrid with CVT (189 hp, 173 lb-ft torque)

Owners appreciate the Sienna's spacious interior, comfortable ride, and excellent fuel economy. However, it has received criticism for its bland styling and lack of advanced safety features.

## **Engine**

### 3.5L V6 with 8-speed automatic transmission

**Oil Consumption**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replacement of piston rings and/or valve stem seals.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Timing Chain Noise**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Rattling or ticking noise during engine operation, particularly at low RPMs.
* **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain tensioner and/or timing chain.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### 2.5L I-4 Hybrid with CVT

**No Issues Found**

## **Transmission**

### 3.5L V6 with 8-speed automatic transmission

**Shifting Issues**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Rough or delayed shifting, slipping gears, or sudden transmission failure.
* **Solution:** Replacement or repair of transmission components, including solenoids, valve body, or torque converter.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### 2.5L I-4 Hybrid with CVT

**No Issues Found**

## **Suspension**

**Strut Bearing Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension, especially over bumps or uneven surfaces.
* **Solution:** Replacement of strut mount assembly.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

## **Electrical**

**HID Headlight Ballast Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Headlight flickering or failure, particularly in cold weather.
* **Solution:** Replacement of headlight ballast.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V-523

## **Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

## **Interior**

**Infotainment System Software Bugs**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Touchscreen freezing, navigation errors, or system crashes.
* **Solution:** Software update or replacement of infotainment module.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $0 - $500+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 19V-781

## **Summary**

The 2018 Toyota Sienna has some potential issues in its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. However, many of these issues are not widespread and can be relatively inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Sienna remains a reliable and practical vehicle for families and those who value space and comfort.
