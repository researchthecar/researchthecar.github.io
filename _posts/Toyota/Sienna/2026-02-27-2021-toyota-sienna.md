# 2021 Toyota Sienna

### Overview
The 2021 Toyota Sienna, a popular minivan, is generally recognized for its strong reliability, a hallmark of the Toyota brand. This model year introduced a hybrid powertrain as standard across all trims, aiming for improved fuel efficiency without compromising performance. Consumer feedback and professional reviews indicate a high level of satisfaction with its mechanical components and overall build quality. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Sienna demonstrates a robust design with a focus on long-term durability. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience with minimal unexpected repairs, contributing to its appeal as a family vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Toyota Sienna stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly given its standard hybrid powertrain. The vast majority of components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, and core interior/exterior elements, show no significant widespread issues. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is the infotainment system, which can occasionally experience software glitches requiring a reset or update. Overall, the Sienna is a dependable choice for families seeking a practical and trouble-free minivan with excellent fuel economy.
