# 2016 Toyota Sienna Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Toyota Sienna is a practical, spacious family minivan known for its comfortable ride and strong overall reputation. While it generally delivers dependable daily transportation, used-car buyers should review certain recurring issues reported by owners—particularly concerning sliding door functionality and front tire wear—to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 266 horsepower and 245 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for highway passing and hauling. This engine option is widely recognized for long-term durability and solid overall reliability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features a 6-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, delivering smooth and seamless gear shifts. The transmission has a solid track record for longevity with routine fluid maintenance.

## Exterior

### Sliding Door Latch

Issues related to power sliding doors and latch mechanisms can occasionally cause operational inconveniences or unexpected movement.

**Door Latch and Closure Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The sliding doors may fail to latch securely, or may open and close intermittently and unexpectedly while operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latches, sensors, and actuators, and perform any necessary manufacturer recall or latch mechanism replacements.

## Safety

### Seat Belt Attachment

Safety equipment generally performs reliably, though rare instances of seat belt anchor detachment have been reported by owners.

**Seat Belt Mounting Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** The seat belt attachment anchor may loosen or detach from the B-pillar unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt anchor bolts for proper torque, thread engagement, and secure attachment to the vehicle frame.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V410000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The brake lines may have incorrect tube nut fittings installed, which may cause a pressure drop within the braking system. The reduction in the braking system pressure may cause a loss of braking power in the rear braking system, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Braun has notified owners, and dealers will install an adaptor to correct the issue, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V553000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The contact may cause failure of the rear brake hoses, resulting in a loss of vehicle control and reduced braking ability, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Vantage will notify owners, and dealers will install a set of rear brake hoses that will provide the proper clearance, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V858000: Structure:Body:Door](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the power sliding door is unable to be opened when commanded, such as if the door is frozen shut, the door may subsequently open unexpectedly, possibly while the vehicle is moving. If door opens while the vehicle is moving, there would be an increased risk of injury to the vehicle occupants.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the junction block, rear sliding door wire harnesses, and install two sub wire harnesses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V197000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During a crash, this incorrect height adjuster may contact the B-pillar and tear or separate. Failure and detachment of seat belt height adjuster would result in the occupant not being properly restrained in a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front seat belt height adjusters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V876000: Equipment Adaptive/Mobility:Wheelchair Restraints/Securement:Latch/Anchor:](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Toyota&model=Sienna&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The retractors may not lock, preventing the wheelchair from being properly secured. An unsecured wheelchair can move during transit, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Toyota Sienna remains a compelling choice for families seeking space and reliability. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the operation of the power sliding doors and inspect the condition of tires and seat belt mounts during a pre-purchase inspection.
