**2016 Toyota RAV4**

The 2016 Toyota RAV4 is a compact SUV that is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with a six-speed automatic transmission, a 3.5-liter V6 engine with a six-speed automatic transmission, and a hybrid powertrain with an electric motor and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The RAV4 is known for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior, but it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **2.5-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

    * **Oil consumption**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive oil consumption, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage
        * **Solution:** Replace piston rings and valve stem seals
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

    * **Timing chain rattle**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Noisy rattling sound from the engine, especially at startup
        * **Solution:** Replace timing chain and tensioner
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

* **3.5-liter V6 Engine**

    * **Valve train noise**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Tapping or ticking sound from the engine
        * **Solution:** Replace valve lifters
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Hesitation or shuddering during shifting**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission hesitation or shuddering when shifting gears
        * **Solution:** Update transmission control software
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Free (under warranty)
        * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V504000

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

    * **Premature wear and failure**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission slipping or shuddering, loss of power
        * **Solution:** Replace transmission
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not being used
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty battery or alternator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Electrical problems with infotainment system**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Infotainment system malfunctioning, freezing, or crashing
    * **Solution:** Update software or replace infotainment system
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate control issues**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Air conditioning or heating system not working properly
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty climate control module or actuator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

    * **Description/Symptoms:** Power windows not working properly
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty window motor or switch
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2016 Toyota RAV4 is a reliable and fuel-efficient SUV, but it has been reported to have some issues with its engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. While the severity and cost of these issues vary, some of them can be costly to repair. It is important to research these potential problems before purchasing a used RAV4 and to consider the potential costs of repairs.
