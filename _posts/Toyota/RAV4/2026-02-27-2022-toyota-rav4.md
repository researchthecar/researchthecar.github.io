# 2022 Toyota RAV4

### Overview
The 2022 Toyota RAV4 is a highly popular compact SUV known for its blend of practicality, fuel efficiency, and Toyota's renowned reliability. This model year offers a range of powertrain options, including efficient gasoline engines and a hybrid variant, catering to diverse driver needs. Generally, the RAV4 maintains a strong reputation for dependability, with owners reporting minimal significant mechanical or electrical problems. Routine maintenance is typically straightforward and cost-effective, contributing to its low cost of ownership. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 2022 RAV4 demonstrates a robust build quality and well-engineered systems, making it a solid choice for those prioritizing long-term reliability and peace of mind.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Toyota RAV4 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle across its various powertrain configurations. Major mechanical failures are rare, and most reported issues are minor, such as occasional infotainment system glitches that are typically resolved with software updates. The gasoline and hybrid powertrains are robust, and the transmissions (8-speed automatic and eCVT) have proven durable. Suspension, electrical components (excluding minor infotainment quirks), exterior, and interior elements generally perform without significant widespread problems. Toyota's commitment to quality engineering is evident, making the 2022 RAV4 a dependable choice with a low likelihood of costly repairs outside of standard maintenance.
