**Introduction**

The 2017 Toyota Yaris R is a compact hatchback that offers an economical and sporty driving experience. It comes with a 1.5L 4-cylinder engine that produces 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a CVT automatic transmission. The Yaris R is known for its agile handling, fuel efficiency, and affordability. However, it has received some criticism for its limited rear seat space and lack of some features.

**Engine**

* **1.5L 4-Cylinder Engine**

    * **Oil Leak from Valve Cover Gasket**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the valve cover gasket, causing oil to drip onto the engine and ground.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracking in the intake manifold, causing loss of power and possible engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**

    * **Difficulty Shifting Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears or shifting smoothly.
        * Solution: Inspect and lubricate the clutch system. If necessary, replace the clutch or transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **CVT Automatic Transmission**

    * **Shuddering or Hesitation During Acceleration**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission shudders or hesitates while accelerating.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control unit or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Moisture**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlight lenses, impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly or use a moisture absorbent.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard rattles or makes noise while driving.
    * Solution: Inspect and tighten loose components or apply damping material.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Airbag Control Unit Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The airbag control unit malfunctions, preventing the airbags from deploying in a collision.
    * Solution: Replace the airbag control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V026000

**Summary**

The 2017 Toyota Yaris R has a mixed bag of issues ranging from minor to potentially severe. The engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, while the other issues are typically less expensive. Overall, the Yaris R is a reliable vehicle, but buyers should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing.
