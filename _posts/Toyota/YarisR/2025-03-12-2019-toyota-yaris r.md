The 2019 Toyota Yaris R is a subcompact sedan known for its fuel efficiency and affordability. It is a rebadged Mazda2. It comes standard with a 1.5-liter inline-4 engine producing 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque. It's only available with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the Yaris R for its nimble handling and user-friendly infotainment system, while some criticize its somewhat cramped rear seats and basic interior materials.

**Engine**

**1.5L Inline-4**
*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher-than-expected oil consumption, requiring them to add oil between scheduled oil changes.
    *   Solution: Monitoring oil levels and topping off as needed. A potential fix could be replacing the piston rings or even the entire engine in severe cases.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies greatly depending on the severity and chosen solution. Replacing piston rings could range from $1,500-$3,000+, while a new engine could cost $4,000+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported harsh or jerky shifting, especially when the transmission is cold.
    * Solution: Software update or transmission fluid change. In severe cases, valve body replacement or complete transmission overhaul might be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update is usually free, fluid change between $100 - $300, valve body replacement $800 - $1500+, transmission overhaul $2,500 - $4,500+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system occasionally freezes or crashes, requiring a restart.
    *   Solution: Software update from the dealership. In some cases, replacing the head unit is required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software updates are usually free. Replacing the head unit can cost $500+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

No Issues Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises coming from the dashboard or door panels, especially when driving on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Identifying the source of the rattle and tightening or replacing the affected components. Common areas include loose trim pieces or wiring harnesses.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300 depending on the complexity and source of the rattle.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Toyota Yaris R is generally a reliable vehicle. The most common issues reported are related to the engine and transmission. The reported engine issue is excessive oil consumption, and a harsh shifting issue has been reported. The issues are typically not catastrophic, though engine issues can be expensive to repair.
