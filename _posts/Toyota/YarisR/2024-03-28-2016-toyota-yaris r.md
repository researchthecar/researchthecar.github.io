**Introduction:**

The 2016 Toyota Yaris R is a sporty hatchback that offers a combination of performance and practicality. It is powered by a 1.5-liter inline-4 engine that produces 106 horsepower and 103 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 5-speed manual transmission or an optional continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Yaris R has been praised for its agile handling, fuel efficiency, and affordability. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, noisy cabin, and cramped interior.

**Climate control system malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in the inability to adjust the temperature or airflow.
Solution: Replace the climate control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Recalls:**

* **NHTSA Campaign Number:** 22V104
Description: A defect in the side curtain airbag inflator may cause the airbag to deploy with excessive force, posing a risk of injury to occupants.
Solution: Replace the side curtain airbag inflators.

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Toyota Yaris R has a good reliability record with few reported issues. The most common issue is with the climate control system, which can malfunction and require replacement. While the Yaris R may not be the most powerful or spacious vehicle in its class, it offers a reliable and fuel-efficient driving experience.
