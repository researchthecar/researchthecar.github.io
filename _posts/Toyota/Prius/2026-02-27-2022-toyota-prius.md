### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Toyota Prius demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with Toyota's reputation. The primary areas of concern are related to the longevity of the hybrid battery system and occasional issues with the 12V auxiliary battery, which are common in hybrid vehicles. Infotainment software glitches are also noted but are typically resolvable with updates. Engine, transmission, suspension, and exterior components show a high degree of reliability with no widespread issues reported. Owners should be aware of potential hybrid battery degradation over the vehicle's lifespan and ensure proper maintenance of the 12V battery.
