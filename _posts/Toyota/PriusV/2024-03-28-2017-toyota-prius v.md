**Introduction**

The 2017 Toyota Prius V is a hybrid vehicle available with a 1.8L 4-cylinder engine paired with a CVT transmission. It offers 134 horsepower and 105 lb-ft of torque. The Prius V is known for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and reliability. However, it has received some criticism for its lackluster acceleration and uninspired driving dynamics.

**Engine**

**1.8L 4-cylinder**

* **Oil Burning:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Issues:** EGR valve fails to open or close properly, resulting in reduced engine performance and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **CVT Failure:** Premature failure of the continuously variable transmission, leading to loss of power or complete transmission failure.
    * Solution: CVT replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **12-Volt Battery Failure:** Premature failure of the 12-volt auxiliary battery, leading to electrical system problems.
    * Solution: Replace the battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:** Occasional freezing, crashing, or glitches with the infotainment system.
    * Solution: Software update or system reset
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies based on the severity of the issue
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:** Door lock actuators fail to lock or unlock the doors.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary**

While the 2017 Toyota Prius V is generally a reliable vehicle, it has faced some issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues can vary widely depending on the specific problem and the necessary repairs. It is important to note that these issues may not affect all vehicles and that many owners have reported a positive experience with the Prius V.
