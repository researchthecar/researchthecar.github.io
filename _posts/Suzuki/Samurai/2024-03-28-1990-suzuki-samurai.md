**1990 Suzuki Samurai**

The 1990 Suzuki Samurai is a compact, off-road vehicle known for its ruggedness and affordability. It was available with two engine options: a 1.3L inline-four and a 1.6L inline-four, both paired with a 5-speed manual transmission.

Despite its popularity, the Samurai has been plagued by a number of issues over the years. These issues range from minor inconveniences to serious safety concerns.

**Engine**

**1.3L Inline-Four**

- **Cracked Exhaust Manifold:** Exhaust manifold cracks can lead to leaks and loss of power.
  - Solution: Replace exhaust manifold.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Timing chain tensioner failure can lead to skipped or broken timing chains, which can cause catastrophic engine damage.
  - Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
  - Recalls: None Found

**1.6L Inline-Four**

- **Connecting Rod Bearing Failure:** Connecting rod bearing failure is a serious issue that can lead to catastrophic engine damage. This issue is more commonly found in the earlier models.
  - Solution: Rebuild or replace engine.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

- **Grinding Gears:** Gears can grind when shifting, making it difficult to engage gears smoothly. This issue is more likely to happen with heavy use of 4x4.
  - Solution: Adjust or replace transmission components as needed.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Slipping Gears:** Gears can slip out of engagement while driving, leading to loss of power and potential safety hazards. This issue is more likely to happen with heavy use of 4x4.
  - Solution: Adjust or replace transmission components as needed.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Leaf Spring Failure:** Leaf springs can break or crack, affecting ride quality and handling.
  - Solution: Replace leaf springs.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Ball Joint Failure:** Ball joints can wear out or fail, leading to loss of steering control and potential accidents.
  - Solution: Replace ball joints.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:** Alternator failure can lead to a loss of electrical power, causing the vehicle to stall or lose important functions.
  - Solution: Replace alternator.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Battery Drain:** Batteries can drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start. This issue is likely due to incorrectly wired accessories or faulty electrical components.
  - Solution: Test electrical system and replace faulty components as needed.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rust:** The Samurai is prone to rust on the body panels, especially around the fenders and wheel wells.
  - Solution: Rust treatment and/or replacement of affected panels.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of rust
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Cracked Dashboard:** The dashboard can crack over time due to sun exposure or improper care.
  - Solution: Replace or repair dashboard.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Air Conditioning Issues:** Air conditioning systems can malfunction or become inefficient, leading to uncomfortable cabin temperatures.
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace air conditioning components as needed.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1990 Suzuki Samurai is a capable off-road vehicle, but it has been plagued by numerous issues over the years. While some of these issues are relatively minor, others can be serious and expensive to repair. The severity of the issues varies, but some, such as engine and transmission problems, can be significant. The cost of repairs can also vary widely, but many issues can be addressed for a reasonable price.
