**Introduction to the 2021 Suzuki Swift**

The 2021 Suzuki Swift is a subcompact hatchback offered with two engine and transmission options: a 1.2L engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission and a 1.4L engine mated to a CVT. With 82 horsepower and 79 lb-ft of torque, the 1.2L engine offers decent fuel efficiency, while the 1.4L engine provides more power with 91 horsepower and 96 lb-ft of torque. The Swift is praised for its sporty handling, fuel efficiency, and compact size, but it also has some drawbacks such as limited interior space and a lack of advanced safety features.
