**Introduction**
The 2014 Suzuki Swift is a subcompact hatchback that is offered in two engine and transmission configurations:

* 1.6-liter VVT four-cylinder engine with a five-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT) (92 horsepower, 101 lb-ft of torque)
* 1.6-liter VVT four-cylinder engine with a six-speed automatic transmission (120 horsepower, 112 lb-ft of torque)

The 2014 Suzuki Swift has received high marks for its fuel economy, fun-to-drive nature, and sporty handling. However, it has also been criticized for its cramped interior and lack of standard features.

**Engine**
**1.6-liter VVT four-cylinder engine (manual transmission)**
* **Engine misfires:** Description/Symptoms: The engine misfires randomly, causing loss of power and rough idling. Solution: Replace the ignition coils. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+. Recalls: None Found
* **Oil leaks:** Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the engine valve cover gasket. Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+. Recalls: None Found

**1.6-liter VVT four-cylinder engine (CVT transmission)**
* **Transmission slipping:** Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips between gears, causing loss of power and erratic shifting. Solution: Replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**Five-speed manual transmission**
* **Grinding gears:** Description/Symptoms: The transmission grinds when shifting into gears. Solution: Replace the synchronizers or shift forks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+. Recalls: None Found

**CVT transmission**
* **Transmission failure:** Description/Symptoms: The transmission fails completely, causing loss of power and a grinding noise. Solution: Replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front suspension noise:** Description/Symptoms: The front suspension makes a clunking or squeaking noise over bumps. Solution: Replace the front suspension struts or control arms. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Electrical system failure:** Description/Symptoms: The electrical system fails completely, causing loss of power and a dead battery. Solution: Replace the battery or alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**
* **Airbag deployment failure:** Description/Symptoms: The airbags fail to deploy in a crash. Solution: Replace the airbag control unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+. Recalls: None Found
* **Seat belt problems:** Description/Symptoms: The seat belts fail to latch properly. Solution: Replace the seat belt buckles. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+. Recalls: None Found

The 2014 Suzuki Swift has a number of potential issues, including engine misfires, transmission failures, and electrical problems. These issues can be costly to fix, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing this vehicle.
