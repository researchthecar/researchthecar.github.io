**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattling**
* Description/Symptoms: Dashboard rattles, especially over rough roads
* Solution: Tightening dashboard screws or replacing dashboard trim
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Door Lock Issue**
* Description/Symptoms: Door locks malfunctioning, doors not unlocking or locking with key fob
* Solution: Replacing door lock actuators
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2023 Suzuki Swift has a good reliability record with no major known issues. The few reported issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. The Swift continues to be a solid choice for those seeking a reliable and economical subcompact hatchback.
