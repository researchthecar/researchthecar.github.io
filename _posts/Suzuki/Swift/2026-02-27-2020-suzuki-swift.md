### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Suzuki Swift demonstrates strong reliability across its core mechanical components, including both engine and transmission options. The 1.2L K12C and 1.0L K10C engines, along with the manual and CVT transmissions, have not shown significant widespread failure trends. Suspension and exterior components are also generally robust. The most commonly cited, though not widespread, issue relates to minor glitches within the infotainment system, typically resolvable with software updates. Overall, the Swift is a dependable choice for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing low running costs and minimal unexpected repairs.
