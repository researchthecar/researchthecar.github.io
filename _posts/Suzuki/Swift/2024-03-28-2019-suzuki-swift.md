**Introduction:**

The 2019 Suzuki Swift is a compact hatchback offering a lively driving experience with its agile handling and fuel-efficient engines. It comes equipped with a 1.2L or 1.4L gasoline engine paired with either a 5-speed manual or CVT transmission. Despite its sporty nature, the Swift is lauded for its affordability, practicality, and stylish design. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle, detailed below:

## Engine

### 1.2L K12C Engine

- **Timing Chain Rattle:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine compartment during startup or acceleration, especially when the engine is cold.
  - Solution: Replacement of the timing chain or tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  - Recalls: None Found

### 1.4L K14C Engine

- **No Issues Found**

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

- **Shifting Difficulty:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, especially when shifting into second or reverse.
  - Solution: Adjusting or replacing the shift linkage or synchronizers.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### CVT Transmission

- **Whining Noise:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Persistent whining noise coming from the transmission while driving.
  - Solution: Replacement of the CVT fluid or torque converter.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

- **Front Strut Squeak:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
  - Solution: Replacement of the strut mounts or bearings.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

- **Battery Drain:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery draining while the vehicle is parked or even when driving.
  - Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, such as replacing faulty wiring or components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

- **Rear Spoiler Degradation:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The rear spoiler becomes brittle and cracks over time.
  - Solution: Replacement of the rear spoiler.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

## Interior

- **Steering Wheel Controls Malfunction:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The steering wheel controls, such as the audio and cruise control buttons, become unresponsive or work intermittently.
  - Solution: Replacement of the steering wheel buttons or control module.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2019 Suzuki Swift has been generally well-received, but it has experienced some issues that may affect its reliability and owner satisfaction. The engine and transmission issues can be costly to fix, while the suspension and electrical problems are typically less severe. The overall severity and cost of the issues vary, but it's important for potential owners to be aware of them when considering this vehicle.
