**Introduction**

The 1991 Suzuki Swift is a compact hatchback that was available with various engine and transmission options. It was known for its affordability, maneuverability, and fuel efficiency. Some owners praised its fun-to-drive nature, while others criticized its lack of interior space and power.

**Engine**

**1.0L G16A**

* **Head Gasket Failure:** Blown head gasket can leak coolant into the engine, causing overheating and loss of power. Replace the head gasket and resurfaces the cylinder head. $500 - $1,000+
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Leaking valve cover gasket can allow oil to seep into the engine compartment. Replace the gasket. $100 - $200

**1.3L G13B**

* **Timing Belt Failure:** A broken timing belt can cause catastrophic engine damage. Replace the timing belt and tensioner at the recommended intervals. $200 - $500
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** Leaking intake manifold gasket can cause vacuum leaks, leading to poor fuel economy and engine performance. Replace the gasket. $150 - $250

**1.6L G16B**

* **Pistons Ring Wear:** Worn piston rings can lead to excessive oil consumption and reduced compression. Replace the piston rings and hone the cylinders. $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:** A faulty crankshaft position sensor can cause the engine to stall or run erratically. Replace the sensor. $200 - $400

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Shift Linkage Wear:** Worn shift linkage can make shifting gears difficult. Replace the worn components. $150 - $300
* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:** A leaking clutch master cylinder can prevent the clutch from engaging properly. Replace the master cylinder and bleed the system. $300 - $500

**3-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:** Leaking transmission can lead to fluid loss and transmission failure. Repair the leak or replace the transmission. $500 - $2,000+
* **Shift Solenoid Failure:** Faulty shift solenoid can cause the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all. Replace the solenoid. $200 - $400

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** Worn strut mounts can cause knocking noises and poor handling. Replace the strut mounts. $200 - $400 per corner
* **Ball Joint Wear:** Worn ball joints can lead to loose steering and uneven tire wear. Replace the ball joints. $100 - $200 per side

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** A faulty alternator can cause electrical problems and battery discharge. Replace the alternator. $300 - $600
* **Starter Motor Failure:** A weak or faulty starter motor can prevent the engine from starting. Replace the starter motor. $200 - $400

**Exterior**

* **Rusty Rear Quarter Panels:** Rear quarter panels are prone to rust, especially in cold climates. Repair or replace the panels. $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracks:** The dashboard can crack over time, especially if exposed to sunlight. Replace the dashboard. $200 - $400
* **Airbag Deployment Failure:** The passenger airbag may not deploy during a collision. Fix the airbag system. Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID 91V-143
* **Seat Belt Lockup:** The front seat belts may lock up and become difficult to release. Lubricate or replace the seat belt mechanisms. Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID 91V-143

**Summary**

The 1991 Suzuki Swift is a reliable and affordable vehicle that is fun to drive. However, it does have some potential issues that owners should be aware of, including engine gasket failures, transmission leaks, and electrical problems. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem and the condition of the vehicle.
