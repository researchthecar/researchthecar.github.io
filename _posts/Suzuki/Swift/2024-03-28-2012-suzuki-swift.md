**Introduction:**

The 2012 Suzuki Swift is a compact hatchback praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and agility. It is available with multiple engine and transmission options, including the 1.4L gasoline engine with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**1.4L Gasoline Engine**

* **Accelerator Pedal Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine may stall or hesitate during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the accelerator pedal sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the intake manifold, causing overheating and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear Selector Rod Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or grinding noises during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace the gear selector rod bushing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically, slipping, or refusing to shift gears.
    * Solution: Replace the TCM.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts Leaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise and vibration when driving over uneven surfaces, reduced ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace the front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly when the car is parked.
    * Solution: Test and replace faulty electrical components, such as the alternator or starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400 per component
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Glass Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof glass cracks or shatters during high temperatures.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof glass.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Panel Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling sound from the center console trim panel.
    * Solution: Secure or replace the trim panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Suzuki Swift generally has minor issues that are relatively inexpensive to fix. Engine and suspension concerns are the most common, but they are not widespread and usually do not pose a significant safety hazard. Overall, the Swift is a reliable and affordable vehicle for city driving.
