## 2022 Suzuki Baleno

The 2022 Suzuki Baleno is a subcompact hatchback known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. It comes with a single engine and transmission option: a 1.4-liter K14B inline-four gasoline engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

## **Engine**
**1.4L K14B**

* **Valve Tappet Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from the engine, particularly during acceleration.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of valve tappets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**
**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitations/Jerks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, slipping gears, or abrupt shifting.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, filter replacement, or transmission overhaul.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**
* **Strut Bearing Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or grinding noise when turning or going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of strut bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Rear Spring Sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sits lower than the front, especially with a load.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

## **Electrical**
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead quickly, even with the vehicle off.
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of electrical system, identification and replacement of faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warning light illuminates, battery does not charge, or electrical systems malfunction.
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Fuse Box Overheating**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning smell, smoke, or melted fuse box.
    * Solution: Fuse box replacement, inspection of electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

## **Exterior**
* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture build-up inside the headlight lenses.
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight lenses with improved seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Antenna Base Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Antenna becomes loose, signal reception is poor.
    * Solution: Replacement of antenna base.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

## **Interior**
* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises from the dashboard, particularly when driving over rough roads.
    * Solution: Installation of sound dampening material, tightening of loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Seat Belt Retractor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt fails to retract properly.
    * Solution: Replacement of seat belt retractor.
    * Recalls: 17V-103

## **Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Suzuki Baleno has a few reported issues across various components, but they are generally considered to be minor in severity and relatively affordable to fix. The most common problems include engine valve tappet noise, transmission shift hesitations, and electrical issues like battery drain. While the Baleno has its drawbacks, it remains a reliable and fuel-efficient vehicle for its price range.
