**Introduction:**

The 1998 Suzuki X-90 is a quirky and distinctive compact SUV. It was available with a single engine and transmission option: a 1.6L V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Despite its unique styling and off-road capabilities, the X-90 has faced criticism for its cramped interior and lackluster performance.

### **Engine**

* **1.6L V6**

    * **Weak Acceleration:** Symptoms include sluggish acceleration and poor response from the engine.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the fuel injectors or spark plugs can improve acceleration.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

### **Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**

    * **Slipping Gears:** Symptoms include the transmission slipping out of gear or having difficulty shifting.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the transmission fluid or the transmission itself may be necessary.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

### **Suspension**

* **Rear Axle:** Symptoms include a clunking noise or vibration coming from the rear of the vehicle.
* **Solution:** Replacing the rear axle can fix the issue.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### **Electrical**

* **Battery:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle or electrical problems.
* **Solution:** Replacing the battery can resolve the issue.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### **Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper:** Symptoms include a loose or detached bumper.
* **Solution:** Reattaching or replacing the bumper is necessary.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### **Interior**

* **Climate Control:** Symptoms include malfunctioning heating or air conditioning.
* **Solution:** Replacing the climate control unit or diagnosing electrical issues may be needed.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### **Summary**

The 1998 Suzuki X-90 has a few known issues, primarily related to the transmission and rear axle. These repairs can be costly, but the X-90's unique styling and off-road capabilities may make it worth considering for some drivers. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues are moderate, but it's important to factor in the potential repair expenses when purchasing a used X-90.
