**Introduction:**

The 2012 Suzuki SX4 Crossover is a compact SUV available with two engine and transmission options:

* 2.0L 4-cylinder engine with a 6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission, producing 150 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque.
* 2.4L 4-cylinder engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission, producing 164 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque.

The SX4 Crossover is praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and spacious interior. However, some owners have reported issues with specific components.

**Engine**

**2.0L Engine**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, reduced performance, possible engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain and components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the valve cover area, visible oil residue.
    * Solution: Replacement of valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4L Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, lack of power, shuddering during acceleration.
    * Solution: Overhaul or replacement of transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noise from front of vehicle, especially over bumps. May lead to premature tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacement of front strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially when vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical fault, possible replacement of battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power door locks malfunction, may become stuck in locked or unlocked position.
    * Solution: Replacement of door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from HVAC blower, especially on high settings.
    * Solution: Replacement of blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Lid Hinge Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hinge on the center console lid breaks, causing the lid to fall or not stay open.
    * Solution: Replacement of center console lid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2012 Suzuki SX4 Crossover is generally a reliable vehicle, several issues can be costly to repair. The most severe issues include timing chain failure, transmission slipping, and front strut mount failure. It is important to address these issues promptly to avoid further damage or safety concerns.
