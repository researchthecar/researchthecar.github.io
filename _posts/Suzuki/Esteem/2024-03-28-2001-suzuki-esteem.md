**Introduction:**

The 2001 Suzuki Esteem is a subcompact sedan available with three engine options: a 1.3-liter 16-valve inline-four pushing out 85 horsepower, a 1.6-liter inline-four producing 97 horsepower, and a 2.0-liter inline-four generating 127 horsepower. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual, a 4-speed automatic, and a continuously variable transmission. Despite its relatively small size, the Esteem is praised for its fuel efficiency and maneuverability. However, it has been criticized for its cramped interior and lack of power with the base engine.
