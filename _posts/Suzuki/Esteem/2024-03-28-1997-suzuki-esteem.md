**Introduction:**

The 1997 Suzuki Esteem is a subcompact sedan and hatchback offered with a choice of three engines and two transmission options. The base engine is a 1.6-liter inline-four with 95 horsepower, while the more powerful engines are a 1.8-liter inline-four with 125 horsepower and a 1.6-liter V6 with 118 horsepower. The 1.6-liter inline-four engine is paired with a five-speed manual transmission, while the 1.8-liter inline-four and 1.6-liter V6 are available with either a five-speed manual or a four-speed automatic transmission.

The Esteem is known for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and reliability. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, cramped interior, and poor handling.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-Four**

* **Blown Head Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking externally or internally, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the intake manifold, rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.8L Inline-Four**

* **No Issues Found**

**1.6L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

* **Shifter Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loose shifter feel
    * Solution: Replace shifter bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping during gear changes, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace transmission or rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, vibration when driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar End Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking noise when turning or driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace sway bar end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely after being parked
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix source of battery drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warning light on dashboard, dimming headlights, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on body panels, especially around wheel wells and door sills
    * Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Lens Yellowing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlight lenses become yellowed and cloudy, reducing light output
    * Solution: Replace headlight lenses
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks forming on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1997 Suzuki Esteem is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension, with repairs ranging from minor (replacing bushings) to major (rebuilding the transmission). While interior and exterior issues are not as severe, they can still be annoying and costly to fix. Overall, the Esteem is a decent choice for budget-minded shoppers, but it is important to be aware of its potential problems before purchasing.
