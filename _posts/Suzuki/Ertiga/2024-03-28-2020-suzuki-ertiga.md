**Introduction**

The 2020 Suzuki Ertiga is a compact MPV that offers seating for up to seven passengers. It is powered by a 1.5-liter naturally aspirated gasoline engine that produces 103 horsepower and 138 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Ertiga for its spacious interior, affordable price, and good fuel economy. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, outdated design, and poor handling.

**Engine**

**1.5-liter naturally aspirated gasoline engine**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning smell and low oil levels. **Replace the valve cover gasket.** **$200-$400.**
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** Coolant leaks from the intake manifold gasket, causing overheating and coolant loss. **Replace the intake manifold gasket.** **$300-$500.**

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual transmission:**

* **Shift Linkage Issues:** The shift linkage becomes loose or damaged, making it difficult or impossible to shift gears. **Replace the shift linkage.** **$100-$200.**

**4-speed automatic transmission:**

* **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter fails, causing shuddering, slipping, and loss of power. **Replace the torque converter.** **$800-$1200.**
* **Electrical Connector Issues:** Electrical connectors in the transmission become loose or damaged, causing transmission control problems. **Repair or replace the electrical connectors.** **$100-$300.**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** The strut mounts fail, causing knocking or clunking noises and poor handling. **Replace the strut mounts.** **$200-$400.**
* **Sway Bar Link Failure:** The sway bar links fail, causing rattling noises and poor handling. **Replace the sway bar links.** **$100-$200.**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is not being used. **Test and replace the battery or identify and repair the source of the drain.** **$100-$300.**
* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator fails to charge the battery, causing electrical problems and eventually leading to a dead battery. **Replace the alternator.** **$300-$500.**

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:** The tailgate latch fails, making it difficult or impossible to open the tailgate. **Replace the tailgate latch.** **$100-$200.**
* **Rust:** The vehicle is prone to rust, especially in areas with high humidity or road salt. **Rust prevention or repair.** **Varies depending on the severity of the rust.**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:** The dashboard squeaks or rattles when driving over rough roads. **Identify and fix the source of the noise (e.g., tighten screws, apply padding).** **Varies depending on the source of the noise.**
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system malfunctions, causing the air conditioning or heater to not work properly. **Diagnose and repair the climate control system.** **$200-$500.**

**Summary**

The 2020 Suzuki Ertiga has some known issues, but they are generally not severe or expensive to fix. The most common issues are related to the engine, transmission, and electrical system. Overall, the Ertiga is a reliable and affordable vehicle that offers a lot of space and value for the money.
