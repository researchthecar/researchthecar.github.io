**Introduction**

The 2023 Suzuki Ertiga is a compact multi-purpose vehicle (MPV) available with a single engine and transmission option. It features a 1.5-liter gasoline engine paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The Ertiga is praised for its spacious interior, affordable price, and fuel efficiency. However, some owners have reported certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **1.5L Gasoline Engine**

    * **Engine Misfire**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine may experience misfiring, causing rough idling and decreased performance.
        * Solution: Replacing the spark plugs or ignition coils is typically necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks may occur from various engine components, such as the oil pan or valve cover gasket.
        * Solution: Replacing the affected gaskets or seals is required.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**

    * **Clutch Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clutch may slip or engage too abruptly, causing difficulty shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replacing the clutch assembly is usually the solution.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip between gears or fail to engage properly.
        * Solution: Overhauling or replacing the transmission is necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and replacing the faulty electrical component causing the drain (e.g., parasitic draw).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **HVAC Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system may produce excessive noise.
    * Solution: Replacing the blower motor or repairing the air vent louvers is often necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Loose Dashboard Components**
    * Description/Symptoms: Certain dashboard components, such as the glovebox or center console, may become loose or rattle.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing the affected components is typically required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2023 Suzuki Ertiga offers a spacious and affordable option for families, it does have some reported issues. The most common issues appear to be related to the engine and transmission, such as engine misfires, oil leaks, clutch issues, and transmission slipping. The estimated cost to fix these issues can range from minor expenses to more significant repairs. Additionally, some owners have reported issues with HVAC noise and loose dashboard components. Overall, the severity and cost of the reported issues vary, and not all vehicles may be affected.
