**Introduction:**

The 2022 Suzuki Ertiga is a seven-seat MPV powered by a 1.5-liter naturally aspirated gasoline engine paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. With 103 horsepower and 101 lb-ft of torque, the Ertiga offers a balance of fuel efficiency and practicality. While it is appreciated for its spacious cabin and versatile seating, some owners have reported specific issues with certain components.

**Engine**

* **1.5-liter Gasoline Engine**
    * **Faulty Fuel Pump:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, difficulty starting, reduced engine power
        * Solution: Replace the fuel pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Leaking Oil Pan Gasket:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks, visible oil stains under the vehicle
        * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Difficulty Shifting Gears:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Stiff or grinding when shifting
        * Solution: Adjust or replace the clutch or transmission linkage
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Delayed Gear Shifts:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping when shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter, or the transmission valve body
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Coil Springs:**
    * **Sagging:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive body roll, reduced load-carrying capacity
        * Solution: Replace the rear coil springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery discharge overnight
    * Solution: Identify and replace the source of power drain (e.g., parasitic electrical draw)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust or peeling on the door handles
    * Solution: Replace the affected door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or ineffective cooling/heating
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system (e.g., replace blower motor or refrigerant)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Suzuki Ertiga exhibits several issues, primarily related to the engine, transmission, and suspension components. While some issues, such as oil leaks and climate control malfunctions, are relatively common, others like delayed gear shifts and sagging rear coil springs occur less frequently. The severity of these issues varies, and repair costs can range from minor to major. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and seek prompt attention to address them if they arise.
