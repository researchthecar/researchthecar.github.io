**Introduction:**

The 2019 Suzuki Vitara is a subcompact SUV powered by a 1.6L gasoline engine. It is available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 118 horsepower and 115 lb-ft of torque. The Vitara is praised for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and off-road capabilities. However, it is criticized for its lack of standard safety features and uninspiring performance.

**Noisy Climate Control**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system makes a loud noise when it is turned on.
Solution: The climate control fan resistor needs to be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $150
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Suzuki Vitara has few reported issues, most of which are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. The most common issue seems to be the noisy climate control system. Overall, the Vitara appears to be a reliable and affordable SUV choice.
