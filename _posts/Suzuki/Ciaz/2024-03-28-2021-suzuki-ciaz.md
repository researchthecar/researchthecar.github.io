**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2021 Suzuki Ciaz is a compact sedan powered by a 1.4-liter K14B gasoline engine paired with either a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 91 horsepower and 96 lb-ft of torque. The Ciaz has been praised for its comfortable interior, fuel efficiency, and affordable price. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's drivability, technology, and exterior build quality.
