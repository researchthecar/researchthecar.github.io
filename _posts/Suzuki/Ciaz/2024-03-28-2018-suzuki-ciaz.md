**Introduction**

The 2018 Suzuki Ciaz is a compact sedan that offers a comfortable ride and spacious interior. It is available with a 1.4-liter gasoline engine or a 1.3-liter diesel engine, both paired with a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Ciaz is praised for its fuel efficiency, spaciousness, and affordability, but it has been criticized for its lack of features and power.

**Engine**

**1.4-liter Gasoline**

* **Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.3-liter Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Plate Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch plate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Red fluid leak under car, transmission slipping or jerking
    * Solution: Replace transmission pan gasket or fluid lines
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission not shifting properly, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncing or swaying, excessive noise over bumps
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per set
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Car not starting, dim headlights, electrical system failure
    * Solution: Replace battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Car not starting, clicking sound when trying to start
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks not locking or unlocking properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per door
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noise from dashboard
    * Solution: Find and dampen the source of the noise
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source
* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control not working properly, blower not functioning
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2018 Suzuki Ciaz has a few common issues, but overall, it is a reliable vehicle. The engine and transmission issues can be costly to repair, but the other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and budget accordingly.
