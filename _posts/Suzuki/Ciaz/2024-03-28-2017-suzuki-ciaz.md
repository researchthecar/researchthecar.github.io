**Introduction:** The Suzuki Ciaz is a compact sedan introduced in 2014. It is available with a 1.4-liter petrol engine and a 1.5-liter diesel engine. The diesel engine is available with a 6-speed manual transmission, while the petrol engine is available with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Ciaz has received praise for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and low maintenance costs. However, there have also been some reported issues with the vehicle.

### **Engine**
- **1.4-liter Petrol**
  - **Intake Manifold Issue**
    - Description/Symptoms: Faulty intake manifold can lead to reduced engine power and increased fuel consumption
    - Solution: Replace intake manifold
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    - Recalls: None Found
  - **Fuel Injector Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Clogged or malfunctioning fuel injectors can cause engine misfires and rough idling
    - Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    - Recalls: None Found
- **1.5-liter Diesel**
  - **Turbocharger Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Faulty turbocharger can lead to loss of power and decreased fuel efficiency
    - Solution: Replace turbocharger
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500
    - Recalls: None Found
  - **EGR Valve Issues**
    - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning EGR valve can lead to increased emissions and decreased engine performance

    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found
