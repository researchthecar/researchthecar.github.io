**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Testing and replacement of the battery. Investigating parasitic draw if the new battery also fails quickly.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Suzuki Ciaz is generally considered a reliable vehicle with a strong emphasis on value and fuel efficiency. Major mechanical failures are uncommon, and most reported issues tend to be minor, often related to the infotainment system or electrical components like the battery. The powertrain, consisting of the 1.5L K15B petrol engine and either a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission, demonstrates good durability. Suspension and exterior/interior components also appear to hold up well under normal usage. Owners should be mindful of potential infotainment glitches and ensure regular battery maintenance. Overall, the Ciaz offers a dependable ownership experience for its segment.
