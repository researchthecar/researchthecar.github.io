**Introduction:**

The 2022 Suzuki S-Cross is a compact SUV offering a blend of practicality and affordability. It is available with two engine options: a 1.4-liter turbocharged gasoline engine and a 1.5-liter naturally aspirated gasoline engine. Both engines come mated to either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The S-Cross has received praise for its spacious interior, comfortable ride quality, and fuel efficiency. However, some owners have reported certain issues with the vehicle.

## Engine 
### 1.4-liter Turbocharged Gasoline Engine

**Overheating:**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine temperature gauge rises rapidly, indicating overheating.
* Solution: Inspect and replace faulty thermostat or coolant sensor.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $120 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

### 1.5-liter Naturally Aspirated Gasoline Engine

**No Issues Found**

## Transmission 
### 6-speed Manual Transmission

**Shift Linkage Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially from 2nd to 3rd.
* Solution: Replace faulty shift linkage.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
* Recalls: None Found

### 6-speed Automatic Transmission

**Delay in Gear Changes:**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or takes longer than expected to shift gears.
* Solution: Update transmission software or replace malfunctioning shift solenoid.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

**Front Strut Noise:**
* Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking sound from the front of the vehicle, especially over bumps.
* Solution: Replace faulty strut assembly.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $350 - $550+ per strut
* Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, even when the vehicle is not being used.
* Solution: Test and replace faulty battery or alternator.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
* Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

**Tailgate Corrosion**
* Description/Symptoms: Rust or corrosion appears on the tailgate, leading to unsightly damage.
* Solution: Repair or replace the affected area, followed by protective coating.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

## Interior

**Rear Seatbelt Buckle Malfunction:**
* Description/Symptoms: Rear seatbelt buckles may stick or fail to latch securely.
* Solution: Replace faulty seatbelt buckle assembly.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
* Recalls: None Found

**Dashboard Creaks:**
* Description/Symptoms: Dashboard panels may develop creaking noises, especially when the vehicle is moving over uneven surfaces.
* Solution: Identify and lubricate the source of the noise, or replace the affected panel.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2022 Suzuki S-Cross has received mixed reviews from owners, with some reporting issues with certain components. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others may require more extensive repairs. It is important to consider these issues when evaluating the S-Cross for purchase or long-term ownership.
