**2014 Suzuki S-Cross**

The 2014 Suzuki S-Cross is a compact crossover SUV that is available with either a 1.6-liter gasoline engine or a 1.6-liter diesel engine. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 1.6-liter gasoline engine produces 120 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.6-liter diesel engine produces 120 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. The S-Cross has received praise for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and affordability, but it has also been criticized for its lack of power and its somewhat dated design.

**Engine**

**1.6-Liter Gasoline**
**Oil Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Engine oil may leak from the valve cover gasket or the oil pan gasket. This can cause a burning oil smell, blue smoke from the exhaust, and low oil levels.
Solution: Replace the leaking gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**1.6-Liter Diesel**
**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and increased exhaust smoke.
Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**
**Grinding Gears**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may grind gears when shifting, especially when shifting into second or third gear.
Solution: Replace the synchronizers in the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**
**Slipping Gears**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip gears, causing the vehicle to lose power or accelerate erratically.
Solution: Overhaul or replace the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**
**Strut Mounts**
Description/Symptoms: The strut mounts may wear out, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps.
Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator**
Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, causing the battery to not charge and the vehicle to lose power.
Solution: Replace the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Plastic Trim**
Description/Symptoms: The plastic trim on the exterior of the vehicle may fade or crack over time.
Solution: Replace the faded or cracked trim.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Headliner**
Description/Symptoms: The headliner may sag or detach from the ceiling.
Solution: Reattach or replace the headliner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Suzuki S-Cross has a few common issues, but most of them are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. The most serious issue is the turbocharger failure on the 1.6-liter diesel engine, which can be expensive to repair. Overall, the S-Cross is a reliable and affordable compact crossover SUV.
