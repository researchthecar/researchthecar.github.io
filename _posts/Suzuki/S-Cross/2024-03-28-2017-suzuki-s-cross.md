## 2017 Suzuki S-Cross

The 2017 Suzuki S-Cross is a compact crossover SUV that offers a combination of practicality and affordability. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated gasoline engine and a 1.6-liter turbocharged diesel engine. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual gearbox and a 6-speed automatic transmission. The S-Cross has received praise for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and fuel-efficient performance. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, dated interior design, and limited safety features.

**Interior**

* **Plastic Trim Pieces Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The plastic trim pieces on the dashboard and door panels may start to rattle over time, especially on rough roads.
    * Solution: The fix involves tightening the screws that hold the trim pieces in place.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Insufficient Front Seat Lumbar Support**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front seats lack adequate lumbar support, which can lead to back pain during long drives.
    * Solution: Installing an aftermarket lumbar support pillow or having the seats modified by an upholsterer can alleviate this issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary**

Overall, the 2017 Suzuki S-Cross is a reliable and practical vehicle with few reported issues. The most common problems are relatively minor and can be fixed at a reasonable cost. However, it is important to acknowledge that the lack of power and dated interior design may be drawbacks for some buyers.
