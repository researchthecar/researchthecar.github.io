### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Suzuki S-Cross demonstrates strong reliability across its core mechanical components, including the engine and transmission options. The 1.4L Boosterjet mild-hybrid powertrain is particularly well-regarded for its efficiency and dependability. While minor electrical glitches with the infotainment system and occasional paint chipping are noted, these are generally not critical failures and fall within expected parameters for the segment. The suspension and interior components have shown good resilience. Overall, the S-Cross is a sensible choice for those prioritizing a trouble-free ownership experience with minimal unexpected repair costs.
