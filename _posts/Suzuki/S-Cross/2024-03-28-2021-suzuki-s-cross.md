**Introduction:**

The 2021 Suzuki S-Cross is a compact crossover SUV known for its combination of practicality and affordability. Engine options include a 1.4L Boosterjet turbocharged engine and a 1.6L DDiS diesel engine. Transmission choices range from a 5-speed manual to a 6-speed automatic. Drivers appreciate its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and competitive fuel economy. However, some owners have reported concerns with certain aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.4L Boosterjet Turbocharged Engine**

* **Turbocharger Boost Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, unusual hissing sound under acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger or boost hoses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear Linkage Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises.
    * Solution: Repair or replace defective shift linkage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Known Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noises from the front suspension, especially over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace worn strut bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Audio System Faulty:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent audio issues, no sound from speakers, Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Reprogram audio module or replace faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Hatch Seal Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cargo area during heavy rain or car washes.
    * Solution: Replace or adjust the rear hatch seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty regulating cabin temperature, uneven airflow.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty climate control actuator or other components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Suzuki S-Cross offers a solid overall experience, certain issues can affect specific components. The most common concerns are related to the engine turbocharger, suspension, and electrical system. The severity and cost of repairs vary depending on the issue, but many can be resolved for under $1,000. Regular maintenance and professional inspections can help mitigate potential problems and extend the vehicle's lifespan.
