The 2020 Suzuki S-Cross is a compact crossover known for its practicality and fuel efficiency. A common engine option for this year is the 1.4L Boosterjet turbocharged four-cylinder engine, producing around 138 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque. While owners appreciate its spacious interior and affordability, some criticisms include its relatively basic interior design and somewhat unrefined driving experience compared to competitors. Other engine options may be available in different markets.

**Engine**

**1.4L Boosterjet**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than normal oil consumption, requiring more frequent oil top-ups between scheduled services. Symptoms include low oil level warning lights and potentially, blue smoke from the exhaust.
    *   Solution: Monitor oil levels frequently and address any leaks promptly. If consumption is excessive, a dealer inspection may be needed to determine the cause.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Could range from a few hundred dollars for minor repairs to several thousand if engine damage has occurred.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

No Issues Found

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Shock Absorber Noise**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported a clunking or knocking noise coming from the rear suspension, particularly when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers, bushings, or struts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ depending on the repair shop.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Lagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can sometimes freeze, lag, or become unresponsive, requiring a reset.
    *   Solution: Software updates may resolve the issue. In some cases, replacement of the head unit may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 for software update, $500 - $1000+ for head unit replacement.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

No Issues Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling Noises from Dashboard**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rattling noises emanating from the dashboard, particularly when driving on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Identifying and securing the source of the rattle, which may involve adding padding or tightening loose components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+ depending on the source and complexity of the rattle.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Suzuki S-Cross generally appears to be a reliable vehicle, though some owners have reported issues with oil consumption, suspension noises, and the infotainment system. The severity of these issues can vary, but repair costs can range from relatively minor to potentially significant depending on the problem and its extent.
