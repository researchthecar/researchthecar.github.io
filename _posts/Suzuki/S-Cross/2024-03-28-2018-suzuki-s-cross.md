## 2018 Suzuki S-Cross

The 2018 Suzuki S-Cross is a compact crossover SUV that comes with a standard 1.4L Boosterjet turbocharged gasoline engine producing 138 horsepower and 173 lb-ft of torque. It is available with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The AllGrip all-wheel-drive system is optional on the automatic-equipped models.

Despite its affordability, the S-Cross has received positive reviews for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and capable off-road performance. However, it has also been criticized for its uninspired exterior design and lackluster fuel economy.

### Engine
**1.4L Boosterjet Turbocharged Gasoline**

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
Solution: Suzuki has issued a technical service bulletin (TSB) to address this issue. The solution involves replacing the piston rings and oil pump.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission
**6-Speed Manual**

**No Issues Found**

**6-Speed Automatic**

**Hesitation and Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced hesitation and jerking during gear changes.
Solution: Suzuki has issued a TSB to address this issue. The solution involves updating the transmission control module (TCM) software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension
**No Issues Found**

**No Issues Found**

### Exterior
**No Issues Found**

### Interior
**Squeaky Visor**
Description/Symptoms: A squeaky noise can be heard from the visor when extended.
Solution: Replacing the visor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Radio Malfunctions**
Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported issues with the radio display, including freezing and unresponsive touchscreens.
Solution: Replacing the radio unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

### Summary
Overall, the 2018 Suzuki S-Cross appears to have a manageable number of common issues. The engine oil consumption issue can be significant, but it is covered under warranty by Suzuki. The transmission issue is less severe and also has a relatively low repair cost. The interior and electrical issues are generally minor and can be resolved without major expense.
