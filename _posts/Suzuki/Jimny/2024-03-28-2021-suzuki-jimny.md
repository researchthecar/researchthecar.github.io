The Suzuki Jimny, released in 2021, is a compact SUV known for its rugged design and off-road capabilities. It is powered by a 1.5-liter, four-cylinder engine, mated to either a five-speed manual transmission or a four-speed automatic transmission. While the Jimny has been praised for its reliability and value, there are a few known issues that have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **1.5-liter, four-cylinder**

* **Valve cover gasket leak:** The valve cover gasket may develop a leak, causing oil to seep out. 
Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Five-speed manual:**

* **Gear noise:** Some owners have reported a whining or grinding noise when shifting gears.
Solution: Replace affected gears
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Four-speed automatic:**

* **Shift hesitation:** The transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting gears. 
Solution: Update transmission control module software or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension:**

* **Strut mount noise:** The strut mounts may make a clunking or rattling noise over bumps. 
Solution: Replace strut mounts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical system:**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, especially if the vehicle is equipped with a lot of aftermarket accessories. 
Solution: Disconnect aftermarket accessories or replace battery
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield:**

* **Cracking:** The windshield may crack easily, especially in cold weather.
Solution: Replace windshield
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard:**

* **Rattling:** The dashboard may rattle over rough roads. 
Solution: Add sound dampening material or tighten loose components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Suzuki Jimny is a reliable and capable SUV, but it does have a few known issues. While the severity of these issues is generally minor, the cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific problem. Owners should be aware of these issues and consider the potential cost of repairs before purchasing a Jimny.
