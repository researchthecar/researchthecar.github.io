**Introduction:**

The 2017 Suzuki Ignis is a subcompact crossover SUV that has been praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. It is powered by a 1.2-liter DualJet engine that produces 82 horsepower and 79 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a 5-speed manual or CVT transmission. Despite its small size, the Ignis offers a comfortable and practical driving experience, but it also has some notable issues.

**Engine:**

**1.2L DualJet (MK6)**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing a rattling noise and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Difficulty Shifting into Gear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Drivers may experience difficulty shifting into certain gears, particularly reverse, due to a faulty transmission cable.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission cable.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **Hesitation or Jerking During Acceleration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The CVT transmission may hesitate or jerk during acceleration, especially at low speeds.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the CVT unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely due to a parasitic draw.
    * Solution: Identify and eliminate the source of the parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Tailgate Hinge Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate hinges may rust, causing the tailgate to sag or become difficult to open.
    * Solution: Replace the rusted hinges.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Loose Dashboard Trim:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard trim may become loose and rattle over uneven roads.
    * Solution: Reattach or replace the trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2017 Suzuki Ignis offers a good value proposition, it has some potential issues that can affect its reliability. The most significant issues are related to the engine timing chain tensioner and transmission hesitation. However, these issues are relatively uncommon and can be addressed with timely repairs.
