**Interior**

* **Vibrating Center Console**
  * Description/Symptoms: The center console vibrates excessively when the vehicle is in motion, particularly at low speeds.
  * Solution: Replacing the center console assembly or adjusting the mounting points.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400+
* **Faulty AC Compressor**
  * Description/Symptoms: The AC compressor fails to engage or struggles to maintain desired temperature levels.
  * Solution: Replacing the AC compressor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Suzuki Ignis has a relatively limited history of issues, with only a few reported concerns. The majority of problems involve minor interior components and can be resolved at a reasonable cost. The engine, transmission, and major mechanical components appear to be reliable.
