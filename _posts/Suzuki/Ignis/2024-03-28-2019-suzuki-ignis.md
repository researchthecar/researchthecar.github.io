**Introduction:**

The 2019 Suzuki Ignis is a subcompact crossover SUV that offers a blend of style, versatility, and fuel efficiency. It comes with a 1.2-liter, four-cylinder gasoline engine mated either to a five-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Ignis has received praise for its spacious cabin, user-friendly infotainment system, and maneuverability. However, owners have reported some issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.2-liter, Four-Cylinder Gasoline Engine**

* **Valve Body Assembly Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine power, rough idle, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Replace valve body assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT):**

* **Premature Belt Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gradual loss of acceleration, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace CVT belt and pulleys
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Ignition Switch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, no response from ignition
    * Solution: Replace ignition switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Defective Door Seal:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leakage into the cabin during heavy rain
    * Solution: Replace door seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Loose Center Console Trim:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or squeaking noise from the center console
    * Solution: Tighten or replace trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Suzuki Ignis is a reliable vehicle with a few minor issues reported by owners. The most significant issues include premature belt wear in the CVT and a faulty ignition switch. These problems can be relatively expensive to fix, but they are not widespread. The majority of owners are satisfied with the Ignis's performance, practicality, and fuel efficiency.
