**Introduction:**

The 2018 Suzuki Ignis is a subcompact crossover SUV available with two engine options: a 1.2-liter DualJet and a 1.2-liter SHVS (Smart Hybrid Vehicle by Suzuki). The DualJet engine is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a CVT, while the SHVS engine comes exclusively with a CVT. The Ignis has received praise for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and compact size, but it has also faced criticism for its lack of power and basic interior features.

**Engine**

**1.2-liter DualJet**

* **Faulty Fuel Injector:** Injector becomes clogged, leading to rough idle and poor acceleration. Solution: Replacement of fuel injector. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:** Buildup of carbon deposits on intake valves, causing reduced engine performance and efficiency. Solution: Cleaning or replacement of intake valves. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**1.2-liter SHVS**

* **Hybrid System Malfunction:** Issues with the hybrid system, such as the battery or electric motor, leading to decreased fuel efficiency and reduced power. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of hybrid system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Starter Motor Failure:** Starter motor may fail to engage the engine, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution: Replacement of starter motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Gear Linkage Problems:** Difficulty shifting gears or grinding noises during shifts. Solution: Adjustment or replacement of gear linkage components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Clutch Wear:** Clutch may wear prematurely, leading to slipping or difficulty engaging gears. Solution: Replacement of clutch disc and pressure plate. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**CVT**

* **Belt Wear:** CVT belt may wear excessively, causing slipping or transmission failure. Solution: Replacement of CVT belt and pulleys. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Valve Body Malfunction:** Issues with the CVT valve body, leading to erratic shifting or transmission failure. Solution: Repair or replacement of valve body. Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Noise:** Strut mounts may deteriorate, causing clunking or rattling noises over bumps. Solution: Replacement of strut mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:** Premature sagging of the rear suspension, resulting in reduced ride height and handling issues. Solution: Replacement of rear suspension components, such as springs or shocks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Battery may discharge prematurely, leading to starting difficulties. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, such as alternator or battery replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

* **Electrical Faults:** Minor electrical faults, such as malfunctioning switches or faulty wiring, may occur. Solution: Diagnosis and repair or replacement of electrical components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction:** Door locks may malfunction, leading to difficulty locking or unlocking doors. Solution: Repair or replacement of door lock actuators. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+

* **Headlight Condensation:** Headlights may develop condensation due to poor sealing. Solution: Resealing of headlights. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Interior**

* **Seat Fabric Pilling:** Seat fabric may pill excessively, resulting in a deteriorated appearance. Solution: Replacement of seat covers or entire seats. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Dashboard Rattle:** Dashboard components may rattle or vibrate excessively over rough roads. Solution: Adjustment or replacement of dashboard components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Summary:**

The 2018 Suzuki Ignis has a range of potential issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and suspension systems. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their decision when considering purchasing or owning this vehicle.
