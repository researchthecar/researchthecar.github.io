# 2019 Suzuki Ignis

### Overview
The 2019 Suzuki Ignis is a compact city car known for its fuel efficiency and maneuverability. Generally, it presents a solid reliability record for its segment, with owners reporting fewer significant mechanical or electrical faults compared to some competitors. The Ignis typically utilizes a small displacement petrol engine, which contributes to its low running costs and straightforward maintenance. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the Ignis tends to avoid widespread, costly problems, making it a sensible choice for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing dependability in urban environments. Its design prioritizes simplicity, which often translates to fewer complex systems that could potentially fail.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Suzuki Ignis demonstrates strong reliability, particularly with its petrol engine options. The manual transmission is generally trouble-free. The automated manual (AGS) transmission is the primary area where minor issues, such as jerky shifts, have been noted, though these are not typically severe or widespread. Other systems like suspension, electrical components, exterior, and interior have shown good durability. Overall, the Ignis is a dependable choice for its class, with a low likelihood of encountering significant mechanical failures.
