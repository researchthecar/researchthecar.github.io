### Overview
The 2021 Suzuki Ignis is a compact city car known for its fuel efficiency and maneuverability. Generally, it presents a solid reliability record, particularly for its segment. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining key areas like the powertrain, electrical systems, and structural components to provide a clear picture for potential owners and current users.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Suzuki Ignis demonstrates strong reliability, especially considering its budget-friendly nature. The petrol engine is generally trouble-free. The main area to be aware of is the AGS automated manual transmission, which can sometimes exhibit less refined shifting characteristics. Electrical issues are minor and often software-related. Overall, the Ignis is a dependable choice for urban commuting, offering a low risk of significant mechanical or electrical failures beyond standard wear and tear. Its simple construction contributes to its robust performance in typical ownership scenarios.
