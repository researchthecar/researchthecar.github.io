**Introduction:**

The 2009 Suzuki Swift+ is a subcompact hatchback available with a choice of gasoline engines and transmissions. It boasts agile handling, spacious interior, and impressive fuel efficiency. However, some owners have encountered various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**1.3L Gasoline Engine**

* **Leaking Oil Pan Gasket:** Oil leaks from the oil pan gasket due to deterioration.
  * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

**1.5L Gasoline Engine**

* **Timing Chain Rattle:** The timing chain may stretch and rattle over time, particularly on high-mileage vehicles.
  * Solution: Replace the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty Shifting:** Shifting into gears can be difficult, especially when cold.
  * Solution: Repair or replace the shift linkage or synchronizers.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Hesitation:** The transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears.
  * Solution: Flush the transmission fluid and replace the filter.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Noise:** The front strut mounts may wear and cause a knocking or clunking noise over bumps.
  * Solution: Replace the strut mounts and bearings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Power Window Motor:** Power window motors may fail, causing windows to become inoperable.
  * Solution: Replace the window motor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail, leading to a loss of charging power and vehicle stalling.
  * Solution: Replace the alternator.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Wheel Wells:** Rust may form on the wheel wells, particularly in areas with frequent rainfall.
  * Solution: Treat and prevent rust with anti-rust spray or coatings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Loose Dashboard:** The dashboard may become loose and rattle over rough roads.
  * Solution: Tighten or replace the dashboard mounting brackets.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty Door Lock Actuator:** The door lock actuator may fail, preventing doors from locking or unlocking properly.
  * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Suzuki Swift+ is prone to some specific issues, including engine oil leaks, timing chain rattle, and suspension noise. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others, such as transmission shifting problems or electrical failures, can be more costly. Overall, the severity and frequency of these issues vary, but it's important for owners to be aware of them and budget for potential repairs.
