**Introduction**

The 2008 Suzuki Swift+ is a subcompact hatchback powered by a 1.5-liter inline-four engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed manual transmission. The engine delivers 102 horsepower and 98 lb-ft of torque. Drivers appreciate its fuel efficiency, affordability, and maneuverability, while common criticisms include a lack of power and a cramped interior.

**Engine**

**1.5-Liter Inline-Four**

* **Damaged Piston Rings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak from the intake manifold
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission struggles to engage gears, causing hesitation or jerking
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Stiff Gear Shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gears are difficult to engage, especially when cold
    * Solution: Replace or repair shift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Premature Strut Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking sounds from the suspension, especially over bumps
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, dead battery
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the car is not running
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Rear Liftgate**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the interior or exterior of the rear liftgate
    * Solution: Rust repair or liftgate replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors fail to lock or unlock electronically
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per actuator
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2008 Suzuki Swift+ has some notable issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can be moderate to expensive, but the overall severity of the problems is not overly concerning. The car's fuel efficiency and affordability make it a decent choice for budget-minded drivers willing to deal with potential mechanical issues.
