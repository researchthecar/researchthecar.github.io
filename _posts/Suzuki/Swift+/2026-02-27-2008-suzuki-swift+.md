## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows may become slow, stop working, or make grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminates, dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Suzuki Swift+ demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 1.5L engine and its associated transmissions are generally robust and do not present common widespread failures. The primary areas of concern are within the electrical system, specifically with power window regulators and the alternator, which can require repair or replacement. While these are not catastrophic issues, they represent the most frequent non-routine repairs reported by owners. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from significant documented problems, contributing to the Swift+'s reputation as a dependable subcompact vehicle when basic maintenance is performed.
