## 2010 Suzuki Swift+

The 2010 Suzuki Swift+ is a subcompact hatchback that offers a balance of affordability, fuel efficiency, and practicality. It comes in various trim levels, with two engine and transmission options:

**Engine Options:**

* 1.2-liter K12B petrol engine (85 horsepower, 114 Nm)
* 1.4-liter K14B petrol engine (94 horsepower, 130 Nm)

**Transmission Options:**

* 5-speed manual transmission
* 4-speed automatic transmission
* Continuously variable transmission (CVT)

**Engine**

**1.2-liter K12B Petrol**
- **Oil Consumption Issue:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  - Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+
- **Timing Chain Noise:** Rattling or ticking sounds from the timing chain, indicating wear or stretching.
  - Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400+
- **Accelerator Stalling:** Loss of power while driving, accompanied by a check engine light.
  - Solution: Inspect and clean the throttle body.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200
  - Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 10V105000

**1.4-liter K14B Petrol**
- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**
- **Grinding Gears:** Difficulty shifting gears, accompanied by grinding noises.
  - Solution: Replace worn synchronizers or gears.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300+

**4-speed Automatic**
- **Slipping Transmission:** Transmission hesitates or slips when changing gears.
  - Solution: Replace worn transmission parts, such as clutches or solenoids.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800+

**CVT**
- **Shuddering or Jerking:** Car experiences vibrations or jerks while driving.
  - Solution: Replace or rebuild the CVT transmission.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500+

**Suspension**
- **Rear Suspension Noise:** Knocking or rattling sounds from the rear suspension during bumps and corners.
  - Solution: Replace worn suspension components, such as bushings or shocks.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150-$400

**Electrical**
- **Battery Drain:** Battery discharges quickly, even when the car is off.
  - Solution: Inspect for electrical leaks or faulty components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300

**Exterior**
- **Door Handle Failure:** Door handles become loose or difficult to operate.
  - Solution: Replace worn door handle mechanisms.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200

**Interior**
- **Dashboard Rattle:** Noises from the dashboard when driving on rough roads.
  - Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200

**Summary**

Overall, the 2010 Suzuki Swift+ has some potential issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. However, the severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem and its severity. Some issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others may require significant repairs.
