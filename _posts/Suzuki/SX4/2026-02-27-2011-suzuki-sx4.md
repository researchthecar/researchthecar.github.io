## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent battery drain, leading to starting issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw, repair or replace faulty component.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Power Window Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating correctly, slow movement, or failure to move.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor/regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $450 per window

### Summary Verdict
The 2011 Suzuki SX4 demonstrates solid overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 2.0L engine and available transmissions (CVT and manual) are generally robust and do not present widespread mechanical concerns. Suspension and exterior components also tend to hold up well. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning battery drain and power window actuator failures. These are not typically catastrophic but can be inconvenient and require professional diagnosis and repair. For prospective buyers, the SX4 represents a dependable choice in the subcompact segment, provided these minor electrical quirks are addressed if they arise.
