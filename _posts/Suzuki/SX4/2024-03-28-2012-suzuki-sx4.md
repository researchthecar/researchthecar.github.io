**Introduction:**

The 2012 Suzuki SX4 is a compact crossover SUV known for its fuel efficiency and versatile design. It comes with two engine options: a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder (144 hp and 140 lb-ft) paired with a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic transmission and a 2.4-liter 4-cylinder (165 hp and 162 lb-ft) mated to a 5-speed manual or a CVT.

Owners appreciate the SX4's affordability, spacious interior, and smooth ride, while some find fault with its uninspired exterior design and sluggish performance.

## **Engine**

**2.0-liter 4-cylinder**

* **Intake Valve Sticking:**
>Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, difficulty starting
>Solution: Replace intake valves and clean carbon buildup
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
>Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
>Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, reduced power
>Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
>Recalls: None Found

**2.4-liter 4-cylinder**

* **Spark Plug Misfires:**
>Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power
>Solution: Replace spark plugs
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
>Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitation or Shuddering:**
>Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed shifting, vibrations
>Solution: Transmission fluid flush or replacement
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
>Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **Premature Belt Wear:**
>Description/Symptoms: Slipping or grinding noises, reduced power
>Solution: Replace CVT belt and pulleys
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
>Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

## **Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
>Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after short periods of parking
>Solution: Inspect and repair faulty wiring or electrical components
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
>Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure:**
>Description/Symptoms: Windows not responding to switch controls
>Solution: Replace window motor or switch
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
>Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

* **Door Handle Breakage:**
>Description/Symptoms: Door handles cracking or breaking
>Solution: Replace door handles
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
>Recalls: None Found

* **Tail Light Assembly Moisture:**
>Description/Symptoms: Condensation or water buildup inside tail light housing
>Solution: Seal assembly or replace tail light
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
>Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
>Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard or console
>Solution: Secure loose trim panels or components
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
>Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
>Description/Symptoms: Blower not working or temperature not regulating
>Solution: Replace climate control module or repair wiring
>Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
>Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

The 2012 Suzuki SX4 has some notable issues, particularly with its engine and CVT transmission. While the costs of these repairs can be significant, they are generally not as severe or expensive as some other vehicles. Overall, the SX4 is a generally reliable car for its age and price range, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their decision-making.
