**Introduction:**

The 2005 Suzuki Reno is a compact SUV that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and standard all-wheel drive. It's available with a 1.6L inline-4 engine with 107 horsepower and 105 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, poor engine performance
* **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Rear Leaf Springs:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear
* **Solution:** Replace rear leaf springs
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery not charging, dimming headlights, electrical problems
* **Solution:** Replace alternator
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $600
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Battery Drain:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery goes dead quickly, especially after sitting for a while
* **Solution:** Replace battery or find and fix source of parasitic drain
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Rear Window Wiper Motor Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Rear window wiper doesn't work
* **Solution:** Replace rear window wiper motor
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Plastic Cracking:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Cracking on the dashboard
* **Solution:** Replace dashboard
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2005 Suzuki Reno has a few common issues to watch out for, including intake manifold gasket failure, alternator failure, and cracked dashboard plastic. Most of the issues are relatively inexpensive to fix, except for potential dashboard replacement. While the Reno is generally reliable, it's important to be aware of these potential problems to ensure a smooth ownership experience.
