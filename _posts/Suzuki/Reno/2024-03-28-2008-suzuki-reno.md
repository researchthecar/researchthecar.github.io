**Introduction:**

The 2008 Suzuki Reno is a compact SUV powered by a 2.4L inline-4 engine mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission. It produces 162 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque. The Reno is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordability. However, it falls short in terms of fuel economy and handling.

**Engine**

* **2.4L Inline-4**

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Starter Motor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, clicking or grinding noises during startup
Solution: Replace starter motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $350+
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly even when the vehicle is not in use
Solution: Replace or repair battery, check for parasitic drains (e.g. faulty wiring, electrical components)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $75 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cover Loose**
Description/Symptoms: Rear bumper cover separates from the body when pushed or bumped
Solution: Secure bumper cover with clips or bolts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate Lock Mechanism Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Tailgate will not open or close properly, key or button may not work
Solution: Replace tailgate lock mechanism
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Airbag Control Module Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Airbag warning light stays on, airbags may not deploy
Solution: Replace airbag control module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V394000

* **Headliner Sagging**
Description/Symptoms: Fabric on the roof lining of the cabin droops and sags
Solution: Replace headliner material
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Window buttons do not work, windows will not open or close
Solution: Replace power window switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2008 Suzuki Reno experiences a range of issues, including electrical problems like starter motor failure and battery drain, exterior issues such as loose rear bumper covers and tailgate lock failures, and interior problems like airbag control module failures, headliner sagging, and power window switch malfunctions. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and costly. Overall, these issues can detract from the vehicle's reliability and value.
