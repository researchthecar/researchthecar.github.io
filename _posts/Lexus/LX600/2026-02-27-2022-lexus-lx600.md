# 2022 Lexus LX600 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lexus LX600 represents the flagship of the brand's SUV lineup, combining rugged capability with high-end luxury. As a relatively new generation built on a robust platform, it serves as an appealing option for used-car buyers seeking refinement and durability. Prospective owners should review available models carefully, keeping in mind that early production years can occasionally surface minor initial quirks.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 409 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque. It provides strong acceleration and towing capability while replacing the older V8, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission is paired with standard four-wheel drive, delivering smooth shifts and optimized highway cruising efficiency. It is generally robust, though proper fluid service remains essential for longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V239000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX600&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The Skid Control ECU software may not turn on the Vehicle Stability Control (VSC) system when the vehicle is restarted after the driver has disabled the VSC system. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Driving with a deactivated stability.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Skid Control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus LX600 generally maintains the brand's strong reputation for build quality and luxury reliability. Used-car buyers should perform standard pre-purchase inspections and verify service records to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
