# 2024 Lexus LX600 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus LX 600 represents the pinnacle of luxury and rugged capability in the flagship full-size SUV segment. Built on a robust platform with advanced engineering, it offers impressive power and refined road manners. While major mechanical trouble spots are rare for this model year, prospective buyers should review available owner reports and inspect specific vehicle conditions during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 409 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque. It provides strong acceleration and towing capability while replacing the older V8 architecture.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and efficient gear shifts across various driving conditions. It pairs seamlessly with the standard four-wheel-drive system for refined power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V125000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Unexpected vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the transmission control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V767000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will perform an inspection on the #1 main bearing using vehicle inspection software and drive data, and if necessary, replace the engine. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V180000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Incorrect detection of an occupant may result in improper air bag deployment during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front passenger seat frame assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
