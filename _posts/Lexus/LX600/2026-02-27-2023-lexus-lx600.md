# 2023 Lexus LX600 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Lexus LX600 represents the flagship of the brand's luxury SUV lineup, combining rugged body-on-frame capability with high-end refinement. As a relatively new generation powered by a modern twin-turbocharged V6, prospective used buyers generally look toward strong initial build quality and luxurious appointments. While early production models carry standard factory warranties, monitoring any emerging powertrain or electronic quirks remains wise when shopping on the secondary market.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

This 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces approximately 409 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque, replacing the older V8 architecture. It provides robust towing capability and smooth power delivery, though used buyers should verify proper maintenance history given its complex forced-induction setup.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Electronic Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission is paired exclusively with a full-time four-wheel-drive system across all trims. It offers seamless gear changes designed to optimize both highway efficiency and low-speed off-road control.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V125000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Unexpected vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the transmission control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V381000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V767000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will perform an inspection on the #1 main bearing using vehicle inspection software and drive data, and if necessary, replace the engine. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V180000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Incorrect detection of an occupant may result in improper air bag deployment during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front passenger seat frame assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
