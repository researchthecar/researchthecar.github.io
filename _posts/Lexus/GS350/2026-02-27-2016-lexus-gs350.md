### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus GS350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. The suspension and chassis components also demonstrate strong durability. Minor electrical concerns, such as occasional infotainment glitches or intermittent battery drain, are the most commonly cited non-routine problems, but these are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall dependability. The exterior and interior components are also well-built, contributing to a consistently positive ownership experience. Overall, the 2016 GS350 is a solid choice for those seeking a dependable and luxurious vehicle.
