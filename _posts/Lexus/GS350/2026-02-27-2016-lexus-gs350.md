# 2016 Lexus GS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus GS350 is generally celebrated for its robust build quality, luxurious cabin, and engaging performance. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, prospective buyers should still inspect specific mechanical areas during a pre-purchase evaluation to ensure long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (311 hp / 280 lb-ft)

This 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with a smooth power delivery. It is widely regarded as a dependable powerplant for the luxury sedan lineup.

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported a loud chirping noise emanating from the engine bay during idling conditions.
- **Solution:** While dealerships may characterize this high-pressure fuel pump operating sound as normal, a thorough inspection can confirm whether component wear is present.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers refined gear shifts suited for both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It maintains a strong reputation for durability with proper fluid maintenance.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

Steering complaints are infrequent, though any steering stiffness should be checked immediately.

**Steering Wheel Binding at High Speeds**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that the steering wheel can occasionally feel stiff or as if it temporarily locks at highway speeds, requiring extra effort to adjust position.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the steering column, electronic power steering assist, and linkage components for binding or alignment faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus GS350 remains a solid choice in the used luxury car market, backed by strong powertrain reliability. Buyers should perform a comprehensive test drive to check for unusual engine bay noises and ensure responsive steering behavior.
