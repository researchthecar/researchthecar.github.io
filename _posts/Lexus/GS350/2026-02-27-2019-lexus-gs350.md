# 2019 Lexus GS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus GS350 is a luxury midsize sedan known for its refined cabin, comfortable ride, and strong brand reputation for long-term reliability. When shopping for a used model, understanding potential mechanical and steering traits helps ensure a confident purchase. While owner-reported complaints are relatively sparse, reviewing specific component feedback can guide your inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, utilizing both direct and port fuel injection. It is generally recognized as a robust and dependable powerplant for everyday luxury driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel drive models to deliver smooth power delivery and responsive shifting. It maintains a solid reputation for durability when routine fluid maintenance is observed.

## Steering

### Steering Rack

Owner reports indicate occasional concerns regarding steering feel, though the system generally performs well.

**Sticky Steering at Highway Speeds**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the steering rack can feel hesitant or 'sticky' during sweeping turns at highway speeds, requiring a slight jerk to correct.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the steering rack and electric power steering system for internal binding or mechanical resistance.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus GS350 remains a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking a blend of performance and luxury. While isolated owner complaints regarding steering feel have been noted, the vehicle's overall mechanical foundation is solid. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive the car at highway speeds to verify smooth steering operation before finalizing their purchase.
