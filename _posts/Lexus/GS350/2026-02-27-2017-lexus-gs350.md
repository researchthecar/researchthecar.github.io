# 2017 Lexus GS350

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus GS350 is generally regarded as a highly reliable luxury sedan, benefiting from Lexus's long-standing reputation for quality and durability. This model year typically features a robust V6 engine and a smooth-shifting automatic transmission, both of which have proven dependable in real-world conditions. Owners generally report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs. The focus on premium materials and meticulous assembly contributes to a well-built interior and exterior, further enhancing its appeal as a long-term vehicle. While no car is entirely immune to issues, the GS350 stands out for its consistent performance and low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus GS350 is a strong contender in the luxury sedan market, particularly for those prioritizing reliability. Its powertrain, consisting of the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust and rarely presents significant problems. Suspension and exterior components are also generally trouble-free. The most common, albeit infrequent, issues tend to be minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system or the standard wear-and-tear of a battery over time. Overall, the GS350 offers a dependable and luxurious ownership experience with a low probability of major mechanical failures, making it a wise choice for buyers seeking peace of mind.
