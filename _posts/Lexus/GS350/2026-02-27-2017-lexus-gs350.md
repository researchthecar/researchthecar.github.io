# 2017 Lexus GS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus GS 350 is a luxury midsize sedan known for its blend of comfort, refined handling, and traditional Lexus reliability. As a used vehicle option, it typically offers a robust ownership experience, though prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate individual vehicle condition, maintenance records, and any isolated owner reports.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, utilizing both direct and port injection. This engine is widely regarded as durable and dependable when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive, offering smooth and responsive gear shifts. It generally maintains a solid reputation for long-term mechanical reliability.

### 6-Speed Automatic

Features a 6-speed automatic transmission typically found on all-wheel-drive configurations. It is a well-established powertrain choice known for consistent operation.

## Exterior

### Interior Trim and Body

Safety components generally perform as expected, though isolated owner complaints highlight minor interior noises.

**Cabin Ticking Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may occasionally report a metal ticking noise originating from the cabin area while driving over rough roads, uneven pavement, or applying the brakes.
- **Solution:** Inspect interior trim, body panels, and related mounting points to locate and insulate the source of the noise.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Advanced restraint systems are standard, though individual incident reports remind buyers to verify full system functionality.

**Air Bag Deployment Concern**
- **Symptoms:** In rare owner-reported incidents involving certain collision angles, frontal air bags may fail to deploy as anticipated.
- **Solution:** Have the supplemental restraint system (SRS) thoroughly inspected by a qualified technician if any warning lights are illuminated or after any vehicle impact.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus GS 350 remains a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking a dependable luxury sedan with strong powertrain options. While isolated owner complaints exist regarding minor noises or rare system anomalies, the vehicle's overall mechanical baseline is solid. Buyers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections and verify regular maintenance history.
