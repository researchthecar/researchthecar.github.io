# 2018 Lexus GS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus GS350 represents the tail end of a well-regarded generation of midsize luxury sport sedans known for comfort, build quality, and strong naturally aspirated performance. For used car shoppers, looking at this model means weighing strong luxury credentials against limited complaint data. While owner feedback is sparse, inspecting individual vehicles thoroughly for prior wear or environmental exposure remains a sensible precaution.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

This 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, utilizing both port and direct injection. It is widely considered exceptionally durable and dependable for everyday luxury driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel-drive models to offer smooth gear changes and responsive highway cruising. It generally exhibits strong long-term mechanical reliability.

### 6-Speed Automatic

This traditional 6-speed automatic transmission is found on all-wheel-drive variants of the GS350. It provides reliable and smooth power delivery across varied weather conditions.

## Interior

### Cabin Environment

Interior components are generally crafted from high-grade materials, though environmental factors can occasionally cause localized moisture or odor concerns.

**Passenger Cabin Mold**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may occasionally discover unusual mold or moisture accumulation within the passenger cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect all weatherstripping, sunrooof drains, and the HVAC evaporator core for blockages or leaks, and thoroughly clean affected interior surfaces.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus GS350 is a solid choice for a used luxury sedan, backed by a robust powertrain lineup and high overall assembly standards. Although minor cabin environment complaints exist in owner reports, they do not point to a systemic manufacturing flaw. Buyers should conduct a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection to ensure the vehicle has been well-maintained and kept free of moisture intrusion.
