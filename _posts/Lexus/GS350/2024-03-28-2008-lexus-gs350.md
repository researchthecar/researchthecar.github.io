**2008 Lexus GS350**

The 2008 Lexus GS350 is a midsize luxury sedan with a reputation for reliability, performance, and comfort. It features a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing 303 horsepower and 274 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 2.5-liter V6 with a six-speed automatic transmission and a hybrid powertrain combining a 3.5-liter V6 with an electric motor and CVT.

**Engine**

* **3.5-liter V6**
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, hesitation, rough idling
        * Solution: Intake valve cleaning, decarbonization
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.5-liter V6**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, delayed engagement, overheating
        * Solution: Torque converter replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **CVT** (Hybrid Model)
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension:**
    * **Air Spring Leaks:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, reduced ride height
        * Solution: Air spring replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 per side
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Conventional Suspension:**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery:**
    * **Premature Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, electrical component malfunctions
        * Solution: Battery replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator:**
    * **Voltage Regulator Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Overcharging, undercharging
        * Solution: Alternator replacement or regulator repair
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights:**
    * **Condensation:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight housings
        * Solution: Headlight housing replacement or resealing
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Weatherstripping:**
    * **Cracking or Damage:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Wind noise, water leaks
        * Solution: Weatherstripping replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control:**
    * **Blower Motor Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: No airflow from vents
        * Solution: Blower motor replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System:**
    * **Display Screen Malfunction:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Blank screen, distorted display
        * Solution: Display screen replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2008 Lexus GS350 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some common issues, particularly with the engine (carbon buildup), transmission (torque converter failure), and suspension (air spring leaks). While these issues can be expensive to repair, they are not widespread and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall quality. Overall, the GS350 is a well-built and enjoyable luxury sedan that offers a balance of performance, comfort, and technology.
