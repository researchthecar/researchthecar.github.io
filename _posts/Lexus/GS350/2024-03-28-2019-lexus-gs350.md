**2019 Lexus GS350**

The 2019 Lexus GS350 is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its combination of performance, comfort, and technology. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The GS350 is praised for its agile handling, spacious and well-appointed interior, and advanced safety features. However, it has received some criticism for its firm ride quality and lack of distinctive styling.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**

    * **Piston Ring Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace piston rings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive engine noise, rattling sound
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

    * **Shifting Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed shifts, hard shifts
        * Solution: Update transmission software, replace transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely
        * Solution: Replace battery, check electrical system for parasitic drain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation Issue:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Condensation buildup inside headlights
        * Solution: Replace headlights with sealed units
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Assembly Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Center console buttons malfunctioning, loose or broken components
        * Solution: Replace center console assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Update Issue:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system not updating or malfunctioning
        * Solution: Update navigation software
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (if under warranty) or $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Lexus GS350 is generally a reliable and well-built vehicle. However, it has had some issues reported, primarily with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more costly and require major repairs. Overall, the GS350 is a solid choice for those seeking a luxury sedan, but it is important to be aware of potential issues before purchasing.
