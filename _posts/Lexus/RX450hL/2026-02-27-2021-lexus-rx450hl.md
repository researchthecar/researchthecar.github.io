# 2021 Lexus RX450hL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus RX 450hL combines three-row seating with Lexus's renowned hybrid efficiency and luxury appointments. As a relatively recent model year, owner feedback indicates a strong overall build quality with very few widespread mechanical complaints. Prospective used-car buyers can generally expect a refined, dependable driving experience, though checking individual maintenance histories remains a smart precaution.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid (308 hp / 247 lb-ft)

This powertrain pairs a 3.5-liter V6 gas engine with electric motors to generate a total system output of 308 horsepower. It provides smooth acceleration and exceptional fuel economy for a three-row luxury crossover with a strong reputation for long-term durability.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The hybrid system utilizes an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission paired with all-wheel drive. It operates seamlessly in daily driving and generally ranks as a low-maintenance, highly reliable unit.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus RX 450hL is a solid choice for used-car buyers seeking luxury, fuel efficiency, and three-row capability without major reliability headaches. With virtually no recurring mechanical complaints reported for this model year, it maintains the brand's high standards for dependable ownership. As always, a pre-purchase inspection is recommended to ensure everything is in top shape.
