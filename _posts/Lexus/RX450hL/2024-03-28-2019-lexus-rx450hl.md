**Introduction:**

The 2019 Lexus RX450hL is a luxury hybrid SUV that offers a comfortable and spacious interior with a fuel-efficient powertrain. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined output of 308 horsepower and 247 lb-ft of torque. Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, the RX450hL boasts EPA-estimated fuel economy ratings of 31 mpg city and 28 mpg highway.

The RX450hL has earned praise for its impressive interior space, refined driving dynamics, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.5-Liter V6**

  * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption has been reported by some owners. Symptoms include low oil levels, oil leaks, and decreased engine performance. The issue can be addressed by replacing the piston rings and valve seals, with an estimated cost of $2,000 - $4,000+.
  * **Timing Chain Noise:** A rattling or ticking sound emanating from the engine may indicate a faulty timing chain. Replacing the timing chain and related components can cost $1,500 - $2,500+.

**Summary**

Prospective buyers should consider the potential costs associated with ownership and weigh them against the vehicle's strengths before making a purchase decision.
