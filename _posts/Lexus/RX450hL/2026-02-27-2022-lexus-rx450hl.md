# 2022 Lexus RX450hL

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus RX450hL is a luxury mid-size SUV that builds upon Lexus's reputation for reliability and refinement. This model, specifically the long-wheelbase hybrid variant, offers a blend of V6 power, electric assistance, and the practicality of three rows of seating. It is engineered with a focus on smooth performance, quiet operation, and advanced safety features. Owners generally report a high level of satisfaction with its build quality and long-term durability, characteristic of the Lexus brand. The hybrid powertrain is designed for efficiency without significant compromise on power delivery, making it a compelling option for families seeking a premium and dependable vehicle.
