# 2020 Lexus RX450hL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lexus RX450hL combines luxury, hybrid efficiency, and three-row seating into a sophisticated package. As a used vehicle option, it benefits from Lexus's strong reputation for long-term build quality and mechanical durability.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

The 3.5-liter V6 hybrid powertrain produces a combined 308 horsepower, pairing smooth acceleration with excellent fuel economy for a midsize luxury SUV. This engine architecture is widely regarded as robust and dependable for high-mileage ownership.

## Transmission

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission manages power blending between the gasoline engine and electric motors seamlessly. It is generally recognized for exceptional longevity and smooth operation under normal driving conditions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus RX450hL stands out as a dependable choice in the used luxury hybrid market, backed by proven powertrain engineering. Prospective buyers should still conduct a standard pre-purchase inspection to ensure all hybrid systems and electronics function properly.
