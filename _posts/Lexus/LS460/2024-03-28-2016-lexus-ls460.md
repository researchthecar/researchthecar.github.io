**Introduction**

The 2016 Lexus LS460 is a luxury sedan that offers a combination of comfort, refinement, and performance. It comes with a powerful 4.6-liter V8 engine that produces 386 horsepower and 367 lb-ft of torque. The LS460 is available with rear-wheel drive (RWD) or all-wheel drive (AWD), and it comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The LS460 is known for its spacious interior, smooth ride, and excellent fuel economy. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **4.6-liter V8**
    * **Engine Ticking Noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: Ticking noise from the engine at idle or low RPMs
        * Solution: Replace the variable valve timing (VVT) system
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle consumes excessive amounts of oil
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings or valve stems
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates to shift gears or slips out of gear
        * Solution: Replace the transmission control module (TCM) or rebuild the transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly when the vehicle is parked
    * Solution: Replace the alternator or install a battery tender
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flicker or turn off unexpectedly
    * Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs or control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Paint Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Paint on the trunk lid peels or flakes off
    * Solution: Repaint the trunk lid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof rattles when opened or closed
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the sunroof bolts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Panel Squeaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Instrument panel squeaks or rattles when driving over uneven surfaces
    * Solution: Apply lubricant to the dashboard bracket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezes or becomes unresponsive
    * Solution: Update the navigation software or replace the navigation unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Lexus LS460 is a reliable and luxurious sedan. However, it has its share of issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can be significant, especially for major problems like engine oil consumption or transmission failure. It is important to factor in these potential costs when considering purchasing or owning an LS460.
