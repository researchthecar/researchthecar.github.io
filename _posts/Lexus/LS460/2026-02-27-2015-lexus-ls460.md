# 2015 Lexus LS460

### Overview
The 2015 Lexus LS460 is renowned for its exceptional build quality and long-term reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. This luxury sedan was engineered with a focus on durability and refinement, utilizing high-quality materials and robust mechanical components. Owners generally report a very positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs. The LS460's powertrain, in particular, is a strong point, offering smooth performance and impressive longevity when properly maintained. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the LS460 stands out for its consistent performance and low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems, making it a dependable choice in the luxury segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Lexus LS460 is a highly reliable luxury sedan with a strong track record. Major mechanical issues are rare, and most reported problems are minor and easily resolved. The powertrain is exceptionally durable, and the overall build quality contributes to a dependable ownership experience. While the adaptive suspension can occasionally develop leaks, this is not a common occurrence. Electrical glitches in the infotainment system are also infrequent. For buyers seeking a luxurious and dependable vehicle with minimal long-term repair concerns, the 2015 LS460 is an excellent choice.
