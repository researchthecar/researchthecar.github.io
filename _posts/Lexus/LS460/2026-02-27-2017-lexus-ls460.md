# 2017 Lexus LS460 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus LS460 represents the final year of the flagship sedan's fourth generation, offering an exceptionally smooth ride, high-end cabin materials, and a powerful V8 engine. As a luxury flagship, it is generally built to high standards of durability, though prospective buyers should still verify maintenance histories and keep an eye out for any isolated owner-reported component issues.

---

## Engine

### 4.6L V8

Powered by a 4.6-liter V8 engine producing around 386 horsepower, this powertrain delivers effortless acceleration and highway cruising. It is widely regarded as robust, though proper maintenance is essential for long-term health.

**Fuel Pump Concern**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have expressed concern that the vehicle may share part numbers with fuel pump assemblies included in earlier recall campaigns, potentially leading to stalling or failure.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the fuel system and check the vehicle identification number against manufacturer service campaigns to confirm whether any warranty extensions or repairs apply.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It offers smooth, refined shifting that complements the vehicle's luxury demeanor with few widespread reliability complaints.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus LS460 continues the brand's tradition of comfort and solid engineering, making it an appealing option for used luxury sedan buyers. While overall mechanical dependability is strong, shoppers should carefully review service records and verify that all safety campaigns or potential fuel system concerns have been properly addressed.
