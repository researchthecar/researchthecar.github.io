# 2017 Lexus LS460

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus LS460 is a luxury sedan renowned for its exceptional build quality and long-term reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. This model year benefits from Lexus's meticulous engineering and rigorous testing, resulting in a vehicle that generally offers a trouble-free ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the LS460 stands out for its robust powertrain and well-integrated systems. Owners typically report high satisfaction with its durability, comfort, and refined performance, making it a strong contender in the used luxury sedan market for those prioritizing dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus LS460 is a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its robust 4.6L V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are known for their durability and lack of common issues. While some rare instances of air suspension leaks on models equipped with AVS or minor infotainment glitches can occur, these are not widespread. The electrical, exterior, and interior components generally hold up very well, reflecting Lexus's commitment to quality. Overall, the LS460 offers an excellent ownership experience with minimal unexpected repair costs, making it a dependable choice for a used luxury vehicle.
