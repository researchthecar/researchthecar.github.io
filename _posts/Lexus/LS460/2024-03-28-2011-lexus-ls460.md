**2011 Lexus LS460**

The 2011 Lexus LS460 is a full-size luxury sedan known for its comfortable and spacious cabin, powerful engine, and advanced technologies. It is available with two engine options:

* 4.6-liter V8 (380 hp, 367 lb-ft torque) paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission
* 5.0-liter V8 (430 hp, 390 lb-ft torque) paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission

The LS460 is praised for its refined ride, luxurious interior, and impressive fuel efficiency for its size. However, there have been some reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

* **4.6-liter V8: Piston Ring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic: Torque Converter Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration during acceleration or gear changes
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter and transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: L13029

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension: Compressor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to maintain ride height, uneven vehicle stance
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Electrical**

* **Navigation System: Screen Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blank or unresponsive navigation screen
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the navigation system unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Chrome Trim: Peeling or Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Peeling or discolored chrome trim around the windows or grille
    * Solution: Replace the affected trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control: Malfunctioning Thermostat**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty maintaining desired cabin temperature
    * Solution: Replace the thermostat
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2011 Lexus LS460 is a reliable and well-regarded luxury sedan. However, some issues, such as piston ring failure and torque converter shudder, can be costly to repair. It is important to consider these potential issues when purchasing or servicing this vehicle.
