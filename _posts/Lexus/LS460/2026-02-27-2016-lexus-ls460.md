# 2016 Lexus LS460

### Overview
The 2016 Lexus LS460 is a luxury sedan renowned for its exceptional build quality and long-term reliability. As the flagship model, it benefits from Lexus's meticulous engineering and rigorous testing. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the LS460 generally exhibits a very strong track record, with owners reporting high satisfaction levels regarding its durability and performance. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, highlighting areas where owners might encounter specific problems and their potential resolutions. The LS460's robust powertrain and sophisticated systems contribute significantly to its reputation as a dependable luxury vehicle.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Variable Suspension (AVS)
**Air Suspension Leaks/Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'Air suspension system fault' warning light, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking air springs, replace faulty compressor, check air lines for damage.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, inaccurate GPS.
- **Solution:** Software update, reset infotainment system, potential replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after sitting for a few days, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw from modules (e.g., infotainment, climate control), replace faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Climate Control System
**Dual-Zone Climate Control Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent temperature control between driver and passenger sides, blower motor issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace blend door actuators, blower motor resistor, or climate control module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus LS460 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its 4.6L V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are exceptionally robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the optional Adaptive Variable Suspension, where leaks or compressor failures can occur, and certain electrical components, such as infotainment system glitches or intermittent battery drain. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well, though minor issues like headlight condensation can arise. Overall, the LS460 offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential problems being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
