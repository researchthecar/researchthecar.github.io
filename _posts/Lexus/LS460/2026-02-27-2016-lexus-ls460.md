# 2016 Lexus LS460 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus LS460 delivers a luxurious ride and strong V8 performance, representing a traditional flagship sedan experience. While owner feedback is sparse, prospective buyers should review available reliability reports and remain aware of potential electrical, steering, and powertrain concerns reported to federal authorities.

---

## Engine

### 4.6L V8 1UR-FSE

The 4.6-liter naturally aspirated V8 (1UR-FSE) generates roughly 386 horsepower and 367 lb-ft of torque, offering robust and smooth acceleration. It is generally regarded as durable, though owners should monitor for isolated fuel system or noise concerns.

**Fuel System Lag**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience acceleration hesitation or lag when shifting into second or third gear.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel pump and fuel delivery system for proper operation.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes suited for a luxury cruiser. It maintains a solid reputation for long-term durability when properly maintained.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering System

Issues related to the electronic power steering system require immediate professional diagnosis to ensure safe vehicle control.

**Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a complete loss of power steering assistance, making the steering wheel extremely heavy.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering module and wiring harness, and replace faulty components as diagnosed by a professional.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety system components should be thoroughly inspected during a pre-purchase evaluation to ensure proper occupant protection functionality.

**Air Bag Non-Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver and passenger airbags may fail to deploy during a frontal collision.
- **Solution:** Have the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) diagnostic codes checked and damaged impact sensors or control modules replaced.

The 2016 Lexus LS460 remains a comfortable and prestigious luxury sedan choice, though used-car buyers should perform a thorough mechanical inspection. Pay close attention to steering functionality, electronics, and overall vehicle history before finalizing a purchase.
