**2009 Lexus LS460**

The 2009 Lexus LS460 is a full-size luxury sedan powered by a 4.6-liter V8 engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. It produces 380 horsepower and 367 lb-ft of torque. The LS460 is praised for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and quiet cabin. However, it has faced various issues affecting different components.

**Engine**

**4.6L V8**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Oil consumption exceeding specifications, leading to reduced engine performance and potential damage.
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine overhaul.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Rattle:** Noisy timing chain, especially on cold starts, indicating premature wear.
    * Solution: Timing chain and components replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitation:** Transmission hesitates or jerks during gear changes, resulting in rough driving experience.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid change or valve body replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Cooler Line Leak:** The transmission cooler lines may develop leaks, causing fluid loss and potential transmission failure.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of leaking cooler lines.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:** Air suspension system may malfunction, leading to uneven ride height or lack of suspension adjustment.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of air struts or compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Battery may discharge prematurely, causing starting difficulties or component failure.
    * Solution: Battery replacement or electrical system diagnosis.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Issues:** Navigation system may experience software glitches, freezing, or inaccurate positioning.
    * Solution: Software update or component replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights, impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Headlight replacement or resealing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Trunk Lid Misalignment:** Trunk lid may not close or open properly, indicating misaligned hinges or latches.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of hinges or latches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Climate control system may not regulate temperature effectively, leading to inconsistent cabin temperature.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of temperature sensors or actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:** Power windows may fail to operate or become stuck in open or closed positions.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of window motors or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Lexus LS460 is known for its luxury and comfort but has encountered various issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior components. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potential safety hazards. Repair costs can be significant, but some issues may be covered under warranty.
