# 2004 Lexus ES330

### Overview
The 2004 Lexus ES330 is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its comfortable ride, quiet cabin, and strong reliability, building on Toyota's reputation for dependable vehicles. Powered by a smooth V6 engine, it offers a refined driving experience suitable for daily commuting and longer journeys. While generally well-regarded for its durability, like any vehicle, it can encounter specific issues as it ages. This report details common areas of concern, focusing on components that may require attention beyond routine maintenance, providing insights for owners and potential buyers.

- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or cam seal(s).

## Suspension

### Standard Independent Suspension
**Strut Mounts/Bearings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises over bumps, especially at low speeds; steering may feel rough.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn strut mounts and bearings.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling or clunking noise from the front or rear suspension when going over uneven surfaces.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn sway bar links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window getting stuck, unusual noises when operating windows.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on the highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Lexus ES330 is a highly reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The primary areas that may require attention are typical for a car of this age and mileage, such as potential oil leaks from valve covers or cam seals, and the recommended preventative replacement of the timing belt and associated components. Suspension components like strut mounts and sway bar links can also wear over time, leading to noise. Electrical issues are generally minor, with window regulators and HVAC resistors being the most commonly cited. Overall, the ES330 offers excellent long-term value and dependability, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
