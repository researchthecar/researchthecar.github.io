**Introduction:**
The 2023 Lexus ES250 is a luxurious sedan that offers a smooth and refined driving experience. It comes with a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 203 horsepower and 184 pound-feet of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and comes standard with front-wheel drive. Owners praise the ES250 for its comfortable interior, quiet cabin, and impressive fuel economy. However, there have been some issues reported with certain aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine:**
**2.5L Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:** Carbon deposits can accumulate on the intake valves over time, causing engine misfires and decreased performance.
  * Solution: Intake manifold cleaning or valve cleaning
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**
**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues:** Some owners have reported rough or delayed shifts, especially when accelerating or decelerating.
  * Solution: Transmission fluid change or reprogramming
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**
**Front Suspension:**

* **Strut Mount Noise:** Excessive noise may be heard from the front suspension, especially over rough roads.
  * Solution: Replacement of strut mounts
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:** Some owners have experienced glitches or freezes with the infotainment system, including issues with audio playback and navigation.
  * Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment unit
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Sensor Issues:** Faulty sensors, such as parking sensors or rain sensors, may provide inaccurate or inconsistent readings.
  * Solution: Sensor replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Rear Spoiler Vibration:** Some owners have reported excessive vibration or noise from the rear spoiler at certain speeds.
  * Solution: Spoiler tightening or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Climate Control Issues:** Inconsistent temperatures or malfunctioning climate control settings have been reported.
  * Solution: Climate control system diagnostics and repairs
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Creaks or Rattles:** Some owners have noticed creaks or rattles coming from the dashboard, particularly when driving over rough roads.
  * Solution: Dashboard disassembly and isolation of noise sources
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 2023 Lexus ES250 offers a refined and comfortable driving experience, it has had some reported issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but owners should be aware of potential problems before making a purchase decision.
