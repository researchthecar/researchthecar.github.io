# 2023 Lexus ES250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Lexus ES 250 is a luxury midsize sedan that pairs refined comfort with standard all-wheel drive. As a relatively new model-year vehicle in the used market, it benefits from Toyota's strong reputation for overall powertrain dependability and build quality. Prospective buyers evaluating this luxury sedan will find a quiet, well-appointed cabin alongside an efficient four-cylinder engine designed for everyday commuting rather than aggressive performance.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

This 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 203 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It prioritizes fuel efficiency and smooth daily power delivery, backed by a strong track record of longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with the all-wheel-drive system, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes to maximize efficiency and comfort. It generally operates trouble-free with standard fluid maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V124000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Due to incorrect programming of the parking-assist electronic control unit (ECU) software, the rearview camera image may not immediately display within a couple of seconds after the shifter is placed into reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
