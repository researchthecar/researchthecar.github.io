# 2021 Lexus ES250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus ES250 offers a refined luxury sedan experience backed by Toyota's reputation for strong overall dependability. Used car buyers looking at this model will generally find a quiet ride and robust mechanical build, though looking through owner reports highlights a few isolated electrical, steering, and safety concerns worth keeping in mind during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 203 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. Paired with all-wheel drive, this powerplant provides efficient performance and is widely regarded as a dependable choice for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the all-wheel-drive system. It generally exhibits solid long-term durability with no major widespread mechanical defects reported by owners.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Steering system complaints are rare, but isolated incidents of electronic assist failure have been documented.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a red steering wheel warning light accompanied by a sudden loss of power assist, making the steering wheel very heavy and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnostic codes scanned by a qualified technician to check for electronic power steering control module or sensor faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus ES250 is a solid choice in the used luxury sedan market, combining efficient performance with strong powertrain reliability. While isolated owner complaints touch on electronic steering issues and safety recall parts availability, the vehicle remains an overall dependable option for buyers prioritizing comfort and long-term peace of mind.
