# 2022 Lexus NX350h

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus NX350h, a popular luxury compact SUV, is generally recognized for its strong reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. This model year introduced a significant redesign, including updated powertrains and technology. Consumer feedback and initial reliability reports suggest a robust performance, with owners appreciating the refined driving experience, fuel efficiency of the hybrid system, and the premium interior. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the NX350h appears to have a solid foundation, benefiting from Lexus's extensive engineering and quality control processes. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of its long-term dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus NX350h demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with Lexus's reputation. The hybrid powertrain and eCVT transmission have shown no significant widespread issues. Minor concerns are primarily limited to occasional infotainment system glitches, which are often resolvable with software updates, and a rare instance of premature 12V battery failure. The suspension, exterior, and interior components are performing as expected with no notable widespread defects. Overall, the NX350h is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a luxurious and efficient compact SUV with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
