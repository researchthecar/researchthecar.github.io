# 2022 Lexus NX350h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lexus NX 350h offers a blend of luxury and impressive fuel economy, making it an appealing option for used car shoppers looking for a compact hybrid crossover. While it carries the brand's reputation for strong overall build quality, early model year vehicles can sometimes present unique quirks reported by owners. Buyers should take time to thoroughly inspect electronics, exterior mirrors, and body alignment during a test drive to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid (A25A-FXS)

This hybrid powertrain pairs a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with electric motors to produce a net 240 horsepower, paired with an electronic continuously variable transmission. It provides efficient daily driving dynamics and is generally considered reliable for everyday use.

## Transmission

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (eCVT)

An electronic continuously variable transmission distributes power smoothly to the all-wheel-drive system. It operates quietly and reliably under normal commuting conditions.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical complaints center on rare power distribution issues and software display pop-ups.

**Ignition and Power Lock Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the vehicle fails to respond to the start button and electronic locks fail to open, occasionally trapping occupants inside.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the 12V battery and body control module connections to ensure proper power distribution.

### Infotainment and Display

Infotainment system concerns are primarily software-related and require proper updates.

**Infotainment Freeze and Apple CarPlay Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience persistent screen takeovers or failure to connect to Apple CarPlay, requiring a system restart or ignition cycle.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates through a dealership or check for available over-the-air patches to resolve connectivity software bugs.

## Exterior

### Side Mirrors

Exterior concerns primarily involve mirror stability and occasional cold-weather door latch resistance.

**Excessive Mirror Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** The driver-side mirror shakes noticeably at certain speeds, causing a blurry reflection and reduced visibility at night.
- **Solution:** Inspect the mirror glass mounting assembly and have the dealership secure or replace loose mirror components.

## Interior

### Driver Seat

Interior comfort reports highlight rare adjustments and physical alignment quirks in the seating arrangement.

**Misaligned Seat Assembly**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that the front seat feels crooked or sits unevenly relative to the dashboard and steering wheel.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership inspect the seat track and mounting bolts to verify proper factory installation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V239000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX350h&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The Skid Control ECU software may not turn on the Vehicle Stability Control (VSC) system when the vehicle is restarted after the driver has disabled the VSC system. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Driving with a deactivated stability.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Skid Control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V238000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX350h&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Missing spot-welds between the body panels and the front shock absorber mounting areas may cause the front shock absorber to separate from the mounting area. A separated shock absorber can cause a loss of vehicle stability, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will add welds to body panels or replace the body panels where welds have failed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus NX 350h remains a strong contender in the luxury hybrid market, blending excellent fuel economy with refined comfort. While owner reports point to minor electrical glitches, software software quirks, and mirror vibration, these are manageable aspects rather than widespread mechanical failures. Prospective buyers should verify that all software updates have been applied and carefully inspect the vehicle's electronics and exterior trim prior to purchase.
