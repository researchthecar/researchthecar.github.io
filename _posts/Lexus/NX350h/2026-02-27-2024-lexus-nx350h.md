# 2024 Lexus NX350h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus NX350h brings hybrid efficiency and luxury to the compact crossover segment, pairing strong fuel economy with upscale features. While owner feedback highlights comfort and tech appeal, used car buyers should always evaluate any modern luxury hybrid for typical early-production electronic or powertrain nuances. Overall, the NX350h maintains a strong reputation for build quality backed by Lexus's dependable engineering.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

This 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine combines with electric motors to produce a net 240 horsepower, paired with an electronic continuously variable transmission. It provides smooth acceleration alongside exceptional fuel economy for daily commuting and long trips.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (e-CVT)

An electronic continuously variable transmission distributes power seamlessly between the hybrid system and the all-wheel-drive layout. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions with minimal mechanical complexity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V482000: Seats:Front Assembly:Head Restraint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The head restraints on the driver and front passenger seats may be inadvertently removed without pressing the lock release button. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) Number 202, "Head Restraints." A head restraint that is inadvertently removed and not reinstalled can increase the.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver and front passenger seat head rest assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V040000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A deactivated driver's air bag will not deploy as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the spiral cable assembly as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V162000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software or replace the rearview camera, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
