### Overview
The 2024 Lexus NX350h, a popular luxury compact SUV, is built upon Toyota's renowned reputation for reliability, particularly with its hybrid powertrain. Owners generally report a high level of satisfaction with its build quality and the smooth operation of its hybrid system. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the NX350h is expected to offer a dependable ownership experience, characteristic of the Lexus brand. ### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Lexus NX350h demonstrates strong reliability, aligning with Lexus's established reputation. The hybrid powertrain and eCVT transmission are particularly robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. The primary areas to monitor are potential minor glitches within the infotainment system, which are often resolvable with software updates, and the lifespan of the 12V battery, which can occasionally require replacement sooner than expected. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well, reflecting the premium build quality. Overall, the NX350h is a highly dependable choice for a luxury hybrid SUV, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
