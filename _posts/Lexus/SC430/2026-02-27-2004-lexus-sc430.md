# 2004 Lexus SC430

### Overview
The 2004 Lexus SC430 is a luxury grand tourer known for its refined driving experience and robust build quality. As a product of Lexus's reputation for reliability, the SC430 generally performs well, with owners reporting fewer significant mechanical or electrical problems compared to many contemporaries. The V8 engine and smooth automatic transmission are typically dependable, contributing to a positive ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the SC430's common problems tend to be minor and often related to age rather than inherent design flaws. This data focuses on documented, recurring issues beyond standard maintenance.

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Power Antenna Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Antenna fails to retract or extend, often accompanied by grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power antenna motor assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Window Regulator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows operate slowly, get stuck, or fail to move.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, or incorrect map data.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the navigation unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Exterior

### Retractable Hardtop Mechanism
**Top Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The hardtop may fail to open or close completely, or operate slowly. Leaks can occur if seals degrade.
- **Solution:** Lubrication of hinges and tracks, adjustment of sensors, or replacement of hydraulic components or seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture or fogging inside the headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Sealing the headlight housing or replacement of the assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Interior

### General Interior Components
**Leather Seat Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Cracking or tearing of leather on seats, especially driver's side.
- **Solution:** Professional cleaning, conditioning, or reupholstering.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700

**Climate Control Display Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Pixels missing or dimming on the climate control display.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the climate control module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Lexus SC430 stands out as a highly reliable luxury vehicle. Its core mechanical components, the 4.3L V8 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission, are exceptionally robust and rarely present significant issues beyond typical age-related wear. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are in the electrical system, particularly with the power antenna motor and window regulators, and the complex retractable hardtop mechanism, which can develop malfunctions over time. Minor interior wear on leather and occasional issues with the climate control display are also noted. Overall, the SC430 offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential problems being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
