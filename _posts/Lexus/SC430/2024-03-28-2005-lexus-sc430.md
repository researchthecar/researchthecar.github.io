**Introduction:**

The 2005 Lexus SC430 is a luxurious and sporty coupe offered with a single powertrain option: a 4.3-liter V8 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. With 288 horsepower and 291 pound-feet of torque, the SC430 provides ample performance. While it features impressive styling, a comfortable interior, and a smooth ride, it also faces certain issues that owners have reported.

**Engine:**

* **4.3L V8**

**Transmission:**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Cruise Control Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The adaptive cruise control may intermittently fail to engage or disengage, causing unintended acceleration or deceleration.
Solution: Reprogramming the cruise control module software
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Fog Light Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Condensation may accumulate inside the fog light housings, reducing visibility and possibly causing electrical damage.
Solution: Replacing the fog light housings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300 per housing
Recalls: 11V-103 (Fog Light Assembly Replacement)

**Interior:**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The automatic climate control system may not regulate temperature effectively, leading to uncomfortable cabin conditions.
Solution: Replacing the climate control actuator or related components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The touch screen display may become unresponsive, freeze, or malfunction, affecting navigation, audio, and other functions.
Solution: Updating software or replacing the infotainment module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2005 Lexus SC430 is a well-built and reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. Electrical problems and interior malfunctions seem to be the most common. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potential safety concerns. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue encountered.
