**2009 Lexus SC430**

The 2009 Lexus SC430 is a luxury grand tourer coupe offered with a single engine and transmission option: a 4.3-liter V8 paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It produces 288 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. The SC430 is praised for its smooth ride, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it has been criticized for its high fuel consumption and small trunk.

**Engine**

* **4.3L V8**

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Keyless Entry System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The keyless entry system may fail to unlock doors or start the engine.
    * Solution: Replace keyless entry module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely.
    * Solution: Replace faulty components, such as alternator or starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture may accumulate inside headlights.
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may freeze or display incorrect information.
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise may come from the dashboard.
    * Solution: Inspect and tighten loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power window may fail to operate properly.
    * Solution: Replace power window motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Lexus SC430 generally has a good reliability record. However, it is not without its issues. Owners have reported problems with electrical components, such as the keyless entry system and battery drain. Interior issues, such as navigation system malfunctions and dashboard rattling, have also been reported. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost to repair can range from minor to major.
