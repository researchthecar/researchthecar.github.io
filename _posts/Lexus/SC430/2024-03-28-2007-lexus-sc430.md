**Introduction**

The 2007 Lexus SC430 is a luxurious coupe that boasts a refined design, sporty performance, and a wealth of amenities. It is powered by a 4.3L V8 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission, delivering 288 horsepower and 312 lb-ft of torque. Praised for its smooth ride, responsive handling, and comfortable interior, some drivers have reported certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.3L V8 Engine**

**Oil Leak from Valve Cover Gaskets**
Description/Symptoms: Leaks oil from the valve cover gaskets, causing low oil levels and reduced performance.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Hesitation**
Description/Symptoms: Hesitates or delays when shifting gears.
Solution: Perform a transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or replace the transmission solenoids.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Control Arm Bushings**
Description/Symptoms: Worn bushings cause excessive play, leading to loose handling and reduced stability.
Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, including dim lights, loss of power, and difficulty starting.
Solution: Replace the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Dashboard Squeaks**
Description/Symptoms: Annoying squeaks and rattles from the dashboard, especially in hot or cold weather.
Solution: Identify and lubricate the source of the noise, or replace the affected dashboard components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2007 Lexus SC430 offers an impressive driving experience, some reported issues can be relatively expensive to fix. Engine oil leaks, transmission hesitation, and alternator failures are the most commonly reported problems. Suspension issues and interior squeaks are less frequent but can still be annoying. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues vary, and many vehicles may not experience any problems at all.
