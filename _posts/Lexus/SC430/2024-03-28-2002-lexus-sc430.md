### 2002 Lexus SC430 Overview

The 2002 Lexus SC430 is a luxury coupe that offers a sophisticated driving experience. It features a V8 4.3-liter engine that produces 290 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque. The SC430 comes standard with a five-speed automatic transmission. It offers a smooth and responsive ride, while its well-equipped interior provides a comfortable and luxurious environment.

While the SC430 is generally a reliable vehicle, there are a few common issues that owners may encounter.

**Engine**

* **Intake VVT System Failure**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Engine runs rough, lack of power, poor fuel economy
    * **Solution:** Replace VVT solenoid
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission Failure**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission slips, rough shifting, no engagement
    * **Solution:** Overhaul or replace transmission
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * **Description/ Symptoms:** Clunking or knocking noise from front suspension, poor handling
    * **Solution:** Replace bushing
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * **Solution:** Test and replace faulty components, such as alternator or starter
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Hood Struts Failure**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Hood does not stay open, can slam down unexpectedly
    * **Solution:** Replace hood struts
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Failure**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Screen goes black, system freezes or crashes
    * **Solution:** Update software, replace DVD unit
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2002 Lexus SC430 is a reliable vehicle with a few potential issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively minor and can be resolved for a reasonable price.
