# 2022 Lexus NX450h+

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus NX450h+ is a plug-in hybrid compact luxury SUV that aims to blend efficiency with Lexus's renowned reliability. As a first-year model for this specific powertrain and generation, early data suggests a strong start. The vehicle benefits from Lexus's extensive experience with hybrid technology and its commitment to quality control. Owners generally report a satisfying ownership experience, with the advanced hybrid system performing as expected. While any new model can have initial teething issues, the NX450h+ appears to be off to a solid start in terms of dependability, building on the NX's established reputation.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus NX450h+ demonstrates strong initial reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The plug-in hybrid powertrain and eCVT transmission have shown no significant widespread issues. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system's software, have been reported but are typically resolved with updates. The mechanical components, suspension, and interior/exterior elements are performing well. Overall, the NX450h+ is a dependable choice for a luxury plug-in hybrid SUV, with potential buyers able to expect a high level of quality and durability.
