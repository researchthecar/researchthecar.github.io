# 2019 Lexus LX570 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus LX 570 represents the pinnacle of traditional luxury SUV durability, building upon a long-standing reputation for rugged body-on-frame engineering and upscale comfort. While owner complaints for this model year are exceptionally sparse, buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify that all electronic conveniences and power accessories operate as intended.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 Engine

Equipped with a naturally aspirated 5.7-liter V8 producing 383 horsepower and 403 lb-ft of torque, this powerplant is renowned for exceptional mechanical longevity. It offers robust towing capability and dependable performance, though fuel economy is notably low by modern standards.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission routes power seamlessly to a full-time four-wheel-drive system. It generally exhibits high reliability and pairs well with the V8 engine for refined highway cruising and capable off-roading.

## Exterior

### Power Liftgate

Exterior body components and automated features are generally robust, though occasional sensor or switch calibration issues may arise.

**Unexpected Liftgate Closure**
- **Symptoms:** The power liftgate may occasionally close unexpectedly while loading or unloading cargo without detecting obstacles or sounding a warning chime.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the liftgate pinch sensors, electronic control modules, and actuator alignment to ensure proper obstruction detection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V887000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX570&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, deactivated air bags can increase the risk of injury .
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will modify the front passenger seat belt assembly or replace it with an improved one, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX570&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus LX 570 remains an exceptionally dependable choice in the luxury full-size SUV segment, backed by stout mechanical underpinnings and minimal owner-reported grievances. Buyers prioritizing long-term durability and luxury will find this vehicle to be a solid investment, provided standard pre-purchase checks are performed.
