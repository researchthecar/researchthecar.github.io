# 2016 Lexus LX570 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus LX570 represents a pinnacle of luxury and rugged body-on-frame capability, anchored by a legendary V8 powertrain. While owner reports to federal safety regulators are extremely sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should still review standard pre-purchase inspection items and remain aware of broader platform characteristics. As with any high-end luxury SUV, checking service records and verifying proper operation of all electronic and safety systems is essential.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 Engine

The 5.7-liter V8 engine produces 383 horsepower and 403 lb-ft of torque, offering robust towing capacity and smooth power delivery. This powerplant is widely regarded as exceptionally durable and long-lasting when subjected to routine maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the standard full-time four-wheel-drive system. It generally proves dependable, benefiting from conservative tuning that prioritizes longevity and seamless gear changes.

## Exterior

### LED Headlights

Electrical systems are generally robust, though modern LED lighting can occasionally accumulate snow in extreme winter environments due to lower thermal radiation.

**Snow Accumulation on Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** An owner experienced snow and ice buildup on the LED headlight housings during severe winter weather due to lower heat output compared to traditional halogen bulbs.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution and periodically clear accumulated snow from the headlight assemblies when driving in heavy winter storms.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Reports concerning supplemental restraint systems are rare, though owners should ensure all safety recalls and warning messages are thoroughly investigated.

**SRS Airbag Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the dashboard screen displays an SRS airbag system malfunction message, advising immediate dealer inspection.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a certified Lexus dealership to diagnose the underlying sensor or component fault.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V887000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX570&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, deactivated air bags can increase the risk of injury .
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will modify the front passenger seat belt assembly or replace it with an improved one, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus LX570 maintains an enviable reputation for build quality and mechanical durability, backed by very few owner complaints. Buyers inspecting a used model should focus on verifying a complete service history, checking for active safety warnings, and ensuring all vehicle electronics function properly.
