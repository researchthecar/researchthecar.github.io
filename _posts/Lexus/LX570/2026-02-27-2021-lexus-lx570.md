# 2021 Lexus LX570

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus LX 570 is a full-size luxury SUV renowned for its robust construction and legendary Toyota/Lexus reliability. Built on a truck-like body-on-frame chassis, it offers exceptional durability and off-road capability. The LX 570 is equipped with a powerful V8 engine and a smooth-shifting automatic transmission, designed for longevity and consistent performance. While generally very dependable, like any complex vehicle, some owners may encounter specific issues. This report details known technical reliability data, focusing on components beyond routine maintenance, to provide a comprehensive understanding of potential concerns.

## Suspension

### Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Stiff ride, increased body roll, potential warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking hydraulic lines and seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Active Height Control (AHC)
**Pump or Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unable to adjust ride height, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty AHC pump or actuator.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive or freezes intermittently.
- **Solution:** Software update or infotainment unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair parasitic draw from modules or accessories.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus LX 570 demonstrates excellent overall reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The powertrain, consisting of the 5.7L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust and rarely presents significant issues outside of normal maintenance. Potential areas of concern are primarily related to the advanced suspension systems (KDSS and AHC), which can develop hydraulic leaks or component failures over time, leading to repair costs. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches or intermittent battery drain, are also noted but are not pervasive. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well. With proactive maintenance and addressing any emerging suspension or electrical quirks promptly, the LX 570 is a highly dependable luxury SUV.
