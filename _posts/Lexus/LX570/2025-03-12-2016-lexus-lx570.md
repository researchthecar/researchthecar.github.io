The 2016 Lexus LX 570 is a full-size luxury SUV known for its opulent interior, smooth ride, and impressive off-road capabilities. It comes exclusively with a 5.7-liter V8 engine producing 383 horsepower and 403 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise its reliability, comfortable cabin, and impressive suite of standard features. Criticisms often include its dated infotainment system, relatively poor fuel economy, and cumbersome size for city driving.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**

*   **Oil Leaks from Timing Cover**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the front timing cover area, often detected by oil spots on the ground or a burning oil smell.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the timing cover gasket or resealing the timing cover.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, resulting in oil spots, burning smell, and potential smoke.
    *   Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Torque Converter Shudder**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration felt during light acceleration, often around 40-60 mph.
    *   Solution: Replacing the torque converter.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Delayed or Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable delays when shifting gears, or harsh and abrupt shifts.
    *   Solution: Transmission fluid flush and fill. If the problem is persistent, a more in-depth solution like replacing the transmission control module or valve body might be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ (fluid flush), $1,000 - $3,000+ (valve body/TCM)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The vehicle sits lower than normal, especially after being parked overnight. The air suspension system warning light illuminates. Compressor may be excessively noisy before failing completely.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Air Suspension Strut Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Similar symptoms to compressor failure, but may affect only one corner of the vehicle. Hissing sound may be heard near the affected strut.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaking air suspension strut.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+ per strut
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Navigation/Infotainment System Freezing or Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The navigation screen freezes, becomes unresponsive, or the entire infotainment system crashes and reboots frequently.
    *   Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 (software update), $1,000 - $3,000+ (replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Faulty Parking Sensors**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Parking sensors fail to detect objects, giving false alarms, or not activating at all.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty parking sensor(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per sensor
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Power Tailgate Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The power tailgate fails to open or close, or it opens/closes erratically.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the tailgate motor or struts, or repair of the wiring harness.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof gets stuck, makes grinding noises, or fails to close completely.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the sunroof motor, tracks, or seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Lexus LX 570 is generally a reliable vehicle, but owners may experience some issues, particularly with the air suspension, transmission, and electrical systems. Repairs can be expensive due to the vehicle's luxury status and complex components. While the engine itself is robust, oil leaks from gaskets can be a common issue. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor in maintenance costs when considering purchasing a used 2016 LX 570.
