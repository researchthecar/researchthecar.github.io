# 2017 Lexus LX570 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus LX 570 carries forward Lexus's legendary reputation for ruggedness, luxury, and build quality. Powered by a stout V8 engine and paired with a durable multi-speed automatic transmission, it is designed for long-term endurance both on and off the road. While owner reports are minimal, prospective buyers should always evaluate mechanical condition carefully.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 Engine

The 5.7-liter V8 engine produces approximately 383 horsepower and 403 lb-ft of torque, offering robust towing capacity and steady acceleration. It is broadly considered highly reliable, though isolated incidents of unexpected power loss have been reported by individual owners.

**Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the vehicle sputtered and lost power while driving uphill on an interstate.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check fuel delivery, ignition, and throttle systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear shifts and works in tandem with a full-time four-wheel-drive system. This transmission is generally robust and lacks widespread recurring complaint trends.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V887000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX570&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, deactivated air bags can increase the risk of injury .
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will modify the front passenger seat belt assembly or replace it with an improved one, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus LX 570 remains a formidable choice for buyers seeking a luxurious, highly capable full-size SUV with strong mechanical endurance. With very few owner-reported complaints on file, it continues to project strong reliability. A pre-purchase inspection by a trusted specialist is recommended to ensure optimal performance.
