# 2018 Lexus LX570 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus LX 570 delivers the rugged body-on-frame capability and luxurious appointment expected from a flagship full-size SUV. Prospective buyers looking at the pre-owned market will find a vehicle built with robust traditional engineering, though it pays to review owner-reported grievances regarding occasional component glitches and safety recalls before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 5.7L V8 (3UR-FE)

The 5.7-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces 383 horsepower and 403 lb-ft of torque, offering strong towing capacity and smooth power delivery. It is widely regarded as an extremely durable, long-lasting powerplant capable of high mileage when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission sends power to a full-time four-wheel-drive system, providing smooth gear changes suited for both highway cruising and off-road excursions. This transmission generally maintains a strong reputation for long-term mechanical dependability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake components provide strong stopping power for this heavy luxury SUV, though isolated sensitivity concerns have been reported by owners.

**Overly Sensitive Brake Response**
- **Symptoms:** Occasionally when just touching the brake pedal, the vehicle reacts abruptly as if the brakes had been slammed on.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the brake pedal sensor, master cylinder, and brake booster assembly for proper calibration.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior trim and body panels are generally well-assembled, though rare structural glass incidents have been noted in owner feedback.

**Unexpected Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass unexpectedly explodes or shatters outward while driving on the highway without external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for stress or alignment issues.

## Safety

### Airbags and Seat Belts

Safety equipment is comprehensive, featuring advanced airbag systems and driver-assistance technologies, though active recall campaigns require verification on used models.

**Recall Parts Delay and Notification Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners experienced wait times or delays in receiving remedy parts and scheduling safety recall repairs for airbag systems.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized Lexus dealership with your Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) to check if all open safety recall campaigns have been completed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V887000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX570&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, deactivated air bags can increase the risk of injury .
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will modify the front passenger seat belt assembly or replace it with an improved one, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LX570&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus LX 570 remains a durable and highly capable luxury SUV choice for used-car buyers who prioritize mechanical longevity and comfort. While isolated owner reports note sensitive braking behavior or rare exterior glass issues, verifying that all manufacturer safety recalls have been addressed will ensure a confident ownership experience.
