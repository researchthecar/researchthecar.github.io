# 2016 Lexus NX200t Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury compact crossover, exploring owner experiences can help highlight potential areas to inspect. The Lexus brand maintains a strong reputation for overall dependability, but individual vehicles can still develop mechanical concerns over time. Prospective buyers should carefully evaluate the vehicle's maintenance history, service records, and functional systems during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering punchy performance for daily driving. While generally durable, owners should watch for occasional reports of electronic or fuel-related hesitation.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden engine stalling, illumination of the check engine light, and unexpected entry into a low-power safe mode while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is necessary to identify failing sensors, fuel system components, or electronic controls.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the turbocharged engine to deliver smooth power delivery across various driving conditions. It is generally robust, though rare electronic or mechanical glitches have been noted by owners.

**Limp Mode Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly enter a restricted limp mode while traveling at highway speeds, limiting acceleration capability.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module scanned for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect fluid condition and transmission internals.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System and ABS Actuator

The braking system provides firm initial bite under normal circumstances, but complex electronic stability and ABS actuators warrant thorough checks during an inspection.

**Brake Pedal Softness and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced soft brake pedals requiring deep depression to achieve stopping power, reduced braking responsiveness, or unexpected brake locking.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hydraulic lines, brake fluid condition, and ABS actuator assembly for signs of internal failure or pressure loss.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior glass and body panels generally hold up well over time, though isolated incidents of spontaneous stress fractures in factory sunroof glass have been documented.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass can suddenly shatter while driving at highway speeds without any prior impact or collision.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the shattered glass panel and thorough cleaning of the surrounding track and interior cabin from glass debris.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V085000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag pressure sensors and/or the acceleration sensors may fail causing the side/curtain shield air bags and/or the front air bags to not deploy in the event of a crash. If the air bags fail to deploy in a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the sensors as necessary based on their serial numbers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

Overall, this vehicle offers a comfortable ride and luxury appointments backed by solid engineering roots. While owner-reported complaints highlight occasional concerns involving the braking system, engine electronics, and sunroof glass, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an independent mechanic can help identify potential issues before finalizing your purchase.
