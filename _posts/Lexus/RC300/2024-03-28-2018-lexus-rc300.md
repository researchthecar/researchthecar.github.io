**Introduction**

The 2018 Lexus RC300 is a luxury sports coupe that combines elegant styling and responsive handling. It features a standard 3.5-liter V6 engine that delivers 255 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)**

**Leaking valve cover gaskets**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from beneath the valve covers, causing smoke and foul odors.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets and associated hardware.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (A8LD)**

**Shifting issues**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or lurches during gear changes, especially in cold temperatures.
Solution: Update the transmission control software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: None Found
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Cracked dashboard**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard develops cracks over time, especially in areas exposed to sunlight.
Solution: Replace the dashboard.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**HVAC system failure**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system stops functioning, resulting in lack of heating or cooling.
Solution: Replace the HVAC control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Lexus RC300 is a reliable vehicle with relatively minor issues. The most severe issue is the cracked dashboard, which can be costly to fix. The other issues are generally less expensive to resolve, and the vehicle has no outstanding recalls. Overall, the RC300 is a solid choice for those seeking a stylish and dependable sports coupe.
