**Introduction**

The 2016 Lexus RC 300 is a luxury sports coupe that offers a blend of performance and refinement. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 255 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The RC 300 also offers a range of advanced safety and technology features.

While the RC 300 has received praise for its sporty handling and comfortable interior, some owners have reported certain issues with the vehicle. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific problems people have encountered:

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 Engine**

* **Oil Consumption Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Hesitation and Shuddering During Gear Shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or shudders when changing gears, especially at low speeds.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or identify and fix the underlying electrical drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks through the sunroof during heavy rain or when the vehicle is washed.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the sunroof gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) system fails to operate properly, leading to temperature control issues.
    * Solution: Replace or repair HVAC components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the Lexus RC 300 is a reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported specific issues related to the engine, transmission, electrical, exterior, and interior components. While most of these issues are relatively minor, some can be more severe and costly to fix. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
