# 2021 Lexus RC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus RC 300 continues the brand's tradition of luxury coupé styling paired with a refined, driver-focused cabin. Prospective used buyers will find that Lexus has built a generally dependable luxury sports coupé, backed by solid powertrain engineering. While owner reports to federal safety regulators are extremely sparse for this model year, reviewing its mechanical setup helps prospective buyers understand what to look for on the used market.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering rear-wheel drive trims. It provides a balance of everyday efficiency and boost-responsive performance, with a strong reputation for durability when properly maintained.

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5L V6 engine generates around 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque in all-wheel drive configurations, or higher outputs in other Lexus applications. It is widely regarded as a remarkably smooth and robust powerplant with an exceptional long-term reliability track record.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

Paired exclusively with rear-wheel drive models, this 8-speed automatic delivers crisp gear changes and smooth highway cruising. It is a mechanically proven unit that rarely exhibits widespread operational defects.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

Coupled with all-wheel drive variants, this 6-speed automatic prioritizes smooth engagement and all-weather traction. It is a traditional torque-converter unit known for holding up well over high mileage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus RC 300 stands out as a dependable and luxurious choice in the used coupé segment, benefiting from proven Toyota engineering. With minimal owner complaints reported and robust powertrain options, it offers strong peace of mind for prospective shoppers. As always, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a clean service history remain your best tools when buying used.
