# 2021 Lexus RC300

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus RC300 is a luxury sport coupe that generally upholds Lexus's reputation for reliability. It offers a blend of performance and comfort, with a focus on refined driving dynamics. While specific engine options vary by market (rear-wheel drive vs. all-wheel drive), the core engineering is robust. Owners typically report a positive ownership experience, with the vehicle demonstrating strong build quality and dependable mechanicals. Common areas of attention, as with most vehicles, tend to be minor and related to user experience rather than fundamental mechanical failures. ### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus RC300 demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. Major mechanical failures in the engine and transmission are exceptionally rare. The most commonly cited issues are minor electrical glitches, particularly with the infotainment system, which are often resolved with software updates. Battery issues, while not prevalent, can occur as with any vehicle. Overall, the RC300 is a dependable choice for a luxury sport coupe, with owners likely to experience a trouble-free ownership period focused on routine maintenance rather than significant repairs.
