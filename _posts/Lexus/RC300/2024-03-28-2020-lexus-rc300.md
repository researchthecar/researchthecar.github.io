**Introduction**

The 2020 Lexus RC300 is a luxury sports coupe featuring a 3.5-liter V6 engine with 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its premium nature, the RC300 has encountered several issues as reported by owners. These issues range from minor inconveniences to potential safety hazards.

**Engine**

* **V6 Engine: Rear Main Seal Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the rear of the engine near the transmission
    * Solution: Replacing the rear main seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission: Gear-Hunting and Delayed Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in shifting gears, particularly during low-speed maneuvers or acceleration
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission overhaul
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **12-Volt Battery: Premature Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery failure within a relatively short lifespan (less than 3 years)
    * Solution: Replacing the battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlights: Condensation Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight housings, leading to impaired visibility
    * Solution: Replacing the headlight assemblies or using anti-condensation measures
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid: Misalignment**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misalignment of the trunk lid, causing it to not close properly
    * Solution: Adjusting the trunk hinges or replacing misaligned parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System: Bluetooth Connectivity Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty connecting or maintaining a Bluetooth connection with smartphones
    * Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System: Inaccurate Directions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Incorrect directions or poor navigation accuracy
    * Solution: Updating the navigation software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2020 Lexus RC300 offers a luxurious driving experience, it has not been immune to various issues. Some of the problems, such as battery failure and Bluetooth connectivity issues, are more common. Others, like the rear main seal leak and headlight condensation, are more severe and expensive to fix. However, it's important to note that these issues may not affect all vehicles, and some owners have reported a trouble-free experience.
