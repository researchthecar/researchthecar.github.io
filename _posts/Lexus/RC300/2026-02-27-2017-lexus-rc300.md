# 2017 Lexus RC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Navigating the market for a used 2017 Lexus RC 300 means weighing luxury coupe styling against known powertrain options and owner-reported issues. While Lexus maintains a strong reputation for overall build quality, prospective buyers should review available engine and transmission specifications alongside limited owner complaints to ensure a reliable purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a balance of efficiency and responsiveness for the rear-wheel-drive RC 300. Overall, it is generally dependable, though buyers should verify the status of any fuel system repairs.

**Hard Starting or No-Start Condition**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the vehicle has difficulty starting or ultimately fails to start altogether, which some owners associate with fuel system faults.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check fuel delivery pressures and inspect the low-pressure fuel pump assembly.

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 delivers around 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with all-wheel drive. It is widely regarded as a robust and long-lasting powertrain option for buyers seeking smooth delivery.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel-drive configurations and offers smooth shifting characteristics with strong durability under normal operating conditions.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

This 6-speed automatic transmission handles all-wheel-drive models, providing reliable power delivery and generally solid mechanical longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus RC 300 offers a compelling blend of luxury, striking design, and dependable engineering. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the fuel system and verify maintenance history, keeping in mind that owner-reported complaints are relatively scarce for this model year.
