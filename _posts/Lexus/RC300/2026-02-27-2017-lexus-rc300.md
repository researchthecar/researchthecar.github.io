### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus RC300 stands out as a highly reliable coupe. Its powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions (8-speed for RWD and 6-speed for AWD), shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. Interior components generally hold up well. Overall, the RC300 offers a dependable ownership experience with a low probability of major mechanical failures, making it a strong contender in its segment for those prioritizing reliability.
