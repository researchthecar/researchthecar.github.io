**Introduction to the 2019 Lexus RC300**

The 2019 Lexus RC300 is a luxury sport coupe powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that generates 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive. The RC300 offers a refined interior, sharp handling, and a comfortable ride. However, it has been criticized for its lack of rear-seat space and its relatively high price tag.

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Lexus RC300 has received positive reviews for its performance, handling, and interior quality. However, it has a few minor issues, such as the infotainment system crashing and the climate control system malfunctioning. These issues have been addressed by Lexus with software updates and recalls, respectively. The cost to fix these issues is generally low, making the RC300 a reliable and enjoyable luxury sport coupe.
