# 2022 Lexus RC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lexus RC 300 continues the brand's tradition of offering a stylish, luxury sport coupe with a focus on refined cruising and solid build quality. Prospective used-car buyers looking at this model will find a vehicle largely praised for its interior comfort and mechanical composure, backed by Lexus's strong reputation for long-term dependability. Because major complaint trends are virtually non-existent for this model year in owner reporting, shoppers can approach the pre-owned market with confidence, provided standard pre-purchase inspections are met.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces roughly 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, typically powering rear-wheel-drive trims. It offers a balanced mix of efficiency and responsive daily power for the luxury coupe.

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 delivers approximately 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque in all-wheel-drive configurations. It is widely regarded as a smooth, durable powertrain option suited for confident highway cruising.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired exclusively with rear-wheel-drive models, offering smooth gear changes and manual paddle shifters. It generally provides dependable long-term operation under normal driving conditions.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

This traditional 6-speed automatic transmission works in tandem with the all-wheel-drive V6 setup. It is known for robust, straightforward operation and solid mechanical reliability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus RC 300 stands out as a highly dependable pre-owned luxury coupe choice. With zero significant recurring complaint patterns reported in current owner data, buyers can prioritize finding a well-maintained example with a clean history and verified service records.
