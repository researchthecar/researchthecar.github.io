# 2016 Lexus RC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Lexus RC 300, buyers can generally expect the high level of refinement and luxury associated with the brand. While the available data points are extremely limited, used car shoppers should always perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to check for hidden wear or prior damage.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth power delivery for this luxury coupe. It is generally regarded as a durable and robust powerplant when properly maintained.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers around 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering an efficient alternative for rear-wheel-drive variants. This engine balances responsive low-end torque with everyday reliability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The six-speed automatic transmission is paired with all-wheel-drive configurations to provide consistent gear selection and stable power distribution. It is typically a dependable unit requiring standard fluid changes.

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

The eight-speed sport direct-shift automatic transmission works alongside rear-wheel-drive layouts to deliver quick gear changes and highway efficiency. This unit is generally robust, though regular servicing helps ensure longevity.

## Electrical

### Vehicle Electrical System

General electrical and wiring complaints are rare, but catastrophic events remain an isolated possibility noted in owner feedback.

**Catastrophic Engine Compartment Fire**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the vehicle suddenly burst into flames while driving on the highway, consuming the entire car within minutes.
- **Solution:** Thoroughly inspect the engine bay for any signs of fluid leaks, frayed wiring, or prior fire damage during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus RC 300 maintains a strong reputation for luxury and mechanical durability, backed by solid powertrain options. Due to the limited nature of available complaint data, buyers should prioritize a comprehensive inspection by a qualified professional to verify overall vehicle safety and condition.
