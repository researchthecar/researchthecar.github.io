# 2020 Lexus RC300

### Overview
The 2020 Lexus RC300 is generally regarded as a reliable luxury coupe, benefiting from Lexus's reputation for quality engineering and robust build. Owners typically report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected mechanical or electrical problems. The vehicle's powertrain options, while offering different performance characteristics, have demonstrated good durability. Common areas of concern, if any, tend to be minor and related to specific features rather than systemic failures. Overall, the RC300 is a solid choice for those seeking a dependable and well-built performance-oriented vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Lexus RC300 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle. Major mechanical issues are exceptionally rare across its engine and transmission options. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the infotainment system, which can occasionally experience software glitches, and a rare instance of premature battery failure. These are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the overall excellent reliability record. For buyers seeking a stylish and dependable luxury coupe, the RC300 is a strong contender.
