# 2020 Lexus RC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2020 Lexus RC 300 means looking at a stylish luxury coupe known for Lexus's generally solid build quality and refined driving dynamics. While available data shows very few owner-reported complaints for this model year, prospective buyers should still carefully inspect individual vehicle histories, previous repairs, and mechanical wear during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering rear-wheel-drive configurations. It offers responsive everyday power and decent efficiency, though buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals.

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter V6 engine delivers around 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque in all-wheel-drive models, or up to 311 horsepower in the RC 300 F Sport RWD variant. It is widely praised for its smooth power delivery and dependable long-term reputation.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

Paired with rear-wheel-drive models, this 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and quick gear changes. It generally maintains a strong reputation for durability when properly serviced.

### 6-Speed Automatic

Utilized in all-wheel-drive models, this 6-speed automatic transmission delivers reliable power distribution across various road conditions. Routine fluid changes help ensure long-term mechanical health.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The suspension and wheel assembly generally ride comfortably, but buyers should verify tire dimensions and overall condition during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Incorrect Tire Sizing**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may be delivered or equipped with incorrect tire sizes, potentially impacting handling and service acceptance.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire sizes against manufacturer placard specifications and replace any mismatched or incorrect tires.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus RC 300 stands out as a dependable luxury coupe option with minimal reported owner complaints. As with any used luxury vehicle, conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection and reviewing the vehicle's maintenance history remain essential steps before buying.
