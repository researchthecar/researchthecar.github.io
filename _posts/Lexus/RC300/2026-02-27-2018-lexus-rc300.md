# 2018 Lexus RC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2018 Lexus RC 300 gives buyers a blend of luxury coupe styling and refined performance. While Lexus generally enjoys a strong reputation for dependability, reviewing owner reports and common mechanical areas helps ensure a sound purchase. As with any luxury vehicle, paying attention to specific maintenance history and minor service quirks will lead to a better ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering rear-wheel-drive configurations. It offers responsive daily driving dynamics paired with solid overall durability when properly maintained.

### 3.5L V6

This 3.5-liter V6 engine delivers roughly 260 horsepower in all-wheel-drive models or up to 311 horsepower in rear-wheel-drive F Sport trims, providing smooth power delivery. It is widely regarded as a robust and long-lasting powertrain option for luxury sport coupe buyers.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel-drive models to offer quick shifts and smooth highway cruising. Routine fluid checks ensure long-term mechanical health.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

This traditional 6-speed automatic transmission handles power for all-wheel-drive configurations, prioritizing smooth engagement and steady reliability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide strong stopping power, though specific noise concerns have been noted by some drivers.

**Brake Pad Squeaking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience an audible squeaking noise from the brake pads during normal operation, mimicking worn-out brakes despite adequate pad life.
- **Solution:** Dealers may address persistent noise issues by replacing the factory brake pads under specific service guidance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus RC 300 is a stylish and generally dependable luxury sport coupe backed by reliable engine and transmission choices. While minor owner-reported annoyances like brake pad noise can occur, they rarely reflect deep-seated mechanical failure. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough test drive and inspect service records to secure a quality vehicle.
