### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Lexus RC300 demonstrates strong overall reliability, with the powertrain and core mechanical components showing minimal reported issues. The most frequently cited concerns tend to be in the electrical system, specifically related to the infotainment unit experiencing occasional glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain issues. These electrical problems are generally manageable with software updates or diagnosis of parasitic draws. Beyond these minor electrical quirks, the RC300 is a dependable choice for a luxury coupe, aligning with Lexus's reputation for durability and owner satisfaction.
