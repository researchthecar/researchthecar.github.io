# 2019 Lexus RC300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus RC300 is a luxury coupe known for its sharp styling, comfortable cabin, and the brand's legendary build quality. While overall owner satisfaction and reliability are typically strong, prospective used car buyers should still inspect various vehicle systems carefully. Reviewing the available powertrain options and occasional owner-reported quirks helps ensure you find a dependable pre-owned luxury car.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering rear-wheel-drive models. It provides a balanced blend of daily efficiency and responsive acceleration, supported by solid long-term durability when properly maintained.

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 generates roughly 260 horsepower in all-wheel-drive configurations. It is celebrated for its exceptionally smooth power delivery and robust mechanical reliability, making it a favorite for buyers seeking traditional V6 performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

Paired with the rear-wheel-drive turbocharged engine, this 8-speed automatic delivers quick shifts and highway cruising efficiency. Routine fluid changes help maintain its smooth operation over high mileage.

### 6-Speed Automatic

Used in conjunction with the all-wheel-drive V6 configuration, this 6-speed automatic prioritizes steady engagement and durability. It offers reliable, proven performance suited to varied weather conditions.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body panels and glass components are generally well-assembled, though isolated incidents of glass failure have been reported by owners.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass may suddenly shatter while driving at highway speeds without any prior impact, debris, or warning noises.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass assembly for stress fractures and replace the damaged panel with a new factory glass unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus RC300 remains a compelling and generally dependable choice in the used luxury coupe segment. Powertrain options are robust and trouble-free in daily driving, while isolated complaints like sunroof glass issues highlight the importance of a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Buyers who carefully evaluate individual vehicle condition can expect a stylish and reliable ownership experience.
