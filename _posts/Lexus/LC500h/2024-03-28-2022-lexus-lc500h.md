**Introduction**

The 2022 Lexus LC500h is a performance coupe that combines luxury and sportiness. It is powered by a hybrid powertrain that consists of a 3.5-liter V6 engine and an electric motor, producing a combined output of 354 horsepower and 346 lb-ft of torque. The LC500h is available with a 10-speed automatic transmission, rear-wheel drive, and all-wheel drive. It is known for its sleek design, responsive handling, and refined interior.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6 Hybrid**
    * **Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace affected valves and adjust valve clearances
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing System Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace timing chain or tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **10-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifter Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, stuck in park
        * Solution: Replace or repair shifter assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting
        * Solution: Replace or repair faulty battery or alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, system unresponsive
        * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattles or wind noise from the sunroof
        * Solution: Adjust or lubricate sunroof components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Lexus LC500h has a few notable issues, particularly with its engine and electrical systems. The engine valve sticking and timing system failure can lead to costly repairs. Electrical issues, such as battery drain and infotainment system failures, can also be inconvenient and expensive to address. While the suspension, exterior, and interior issues are relatively minor, they can still affect the overall ownership experience. It is important to factor these potential issues into consideration when making a purchase decision.
