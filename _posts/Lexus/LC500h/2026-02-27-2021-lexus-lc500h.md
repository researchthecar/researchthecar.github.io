# 2021 Lexus LC500h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus LC 500h represents a striking blend of grand touring luxury and advanced hybrid efficiency, featuring flagship craftsmanship and a high-end cabin. While owner reports for this luxury hybrid coupe are very sparse, buyers should still evaluate powertrain options and general electronics carefully during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid Multi-Stage

The 3.5-liter V6 paired with Lexus's Multi-Stage Hybrid System generates a combined 354 horsepower, offering smooth acceleration and impressive fuel economy for a luxury grand tourer. This powertrain is generally robust, though early owner feedback highlights rare driveability stumbles.

**Engine Stumble or Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may stutter and nearly stall when accelerating from a low speed or pulling out of a stop.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the fuel and hybrid powertrain control modules for diagnostic codes or software updates.

## Transmission

### Multi-Stage CVT Automatic

The multi-stage hybrid transmission combines a traditional 4-speed automatic with a continuously variable transmission to mimic a 10-speed gearbox. It delivers refined cruising capability and strong reliability overall.
