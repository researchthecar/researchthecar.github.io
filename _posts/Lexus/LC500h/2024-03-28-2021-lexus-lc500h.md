**Introduction:**

The 2021 Lexus LC500h is a luxurious hybrid sports car that combines a powerful V6 engine with an electric motor to deliver impressive performance and fuel efficiency. It boasts 354 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque, providing a thrilling driving experience. Owners appreciate its sleek design, comfortable interior, and advanced technology features. However, like any vehicle, the LC500h has experienced some issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6 Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors can malfunction, causing engine misfiring, poor fuel economy, and difficulty starting.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
        * Recalls: 21V-511

**Transmission**

* **10-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shudder or vibrate during shifting, particularly when the vehicle is cold.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission fluid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to ride low or unevenly.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: 22V-236

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, especially if the vehicle is left parked for extended periods.
    * Solution: Identify and address the source of the parasitic battery drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Radio Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The radio may experience intermittent or complete failure.
    * Solution: Reset or replace the faulty radio unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights, causing condensation fogging.
    * Solution: Seal or replace the affected headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze or display errors.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in poor heating or cooling performance.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Lexus LC500h has experienced a range of issues that vary in severity and cost. The most significant concern is the fuel injector failure, which can lead to engine misfiring and potential engine damage. Other issues, such as the transmission shudder, suspension malfunction, and headlight condensation, can also be inconvenient and costly to repair. It's important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and address them promptly to ensure the safety and reliability of their vehicles.
