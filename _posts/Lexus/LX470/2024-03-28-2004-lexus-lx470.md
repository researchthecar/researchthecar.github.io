**Introduction:**

The 2004 Lexus LX470 is a full-size SUV known for its ruggedness, luxury, and reliability. It features a powerful 4.7-liter V8 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The LX470 offers a spacious interior with premium amenities and impressive off-road capabilities. However, despite its overall reliability, it has faced some issues over the years.

**Engine**

**4.7L V8**

* **Intake manifold gasket leak:** Symptoms include coolant leaks, overheating, and engine misfires. Fix involves replacing the intake manifold gaskets. $300 - $700+.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Oxygen sensor failure:** Symptoms include reduced fuel efficiency, engine performance issues, and potential damage to the catalytic converter. Fix involves replacing the faulty oxygen sensor. $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Water pump failure:** Symptoms include coolant leaks, overheating, and potentially catastrophic engine damage. Fix involves replacing the water pump. $400 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission solenoid issues:** Symptoms include shifting problems, slipping, and delayed engagement. Fix involves replacing the faulty solenoids. $300 - $1000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID 10110845, 10095983

* **Torque converter shudder:** Symptoms include vibration while accelerating. Fix involves replacing the torque converter. $1000 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front lower control arm bushings wear:** Symptoms include clunking noises and reduced handling. Fix involves replacing the worn bushings. $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear shock absorbers leaking:** Symptoms include decreased suspension performance, bouncy ride, and fluid leaks. Fix involves replacing the faulty shock absorbers. $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain issue:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights, and electrical system malfunctions. Fix involves identifying and repairing the source of the drain. $100 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator failure:** Symptoms include dim lights, battery charging issues, and potential electrical system failures. Fix involves replacing the alternator. $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Weatherstripping damage:** Symptoms include water leaks and excessive wind noise. Fix involves replacing the damaged weatherstripping. $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof drain blockage:** Symptoms include water leaks into the cabin. Fix involves cleaning or unclogging the sunroof drains. $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power window switch failure:** Symptoms include inoperable power windows. Fix involves replacing the faulty switch. $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Radio display screen issues:** Symptoms include flickering or malfunctioning display. Fix may involve software updates or replacing the display screen. $200 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2004 Lexus LX470 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has faced some common issues over the years, including engine leaks, transmission problems, suspension wear, and electrical malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but repairs can be costly, especially for more complex issues like transmission solenoids or alternator failure. It is important to maintain the vehicle regularly and address any issues promptly to minimize the likelihood of more significant problems.
