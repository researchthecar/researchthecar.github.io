# 2005 Lexus LX470

### Overview
The 2005 Lexus LX470, a luxury full-size SUV, is renowned for its robust build quality and exceptional durability, largely inherited from its Toyota Land Cruiser underpinnings. This generation is particularly praised for its reliable powertrain and well-engineered chassis, making it a popular choice for those seeking a dependable vehicle for both daily driving and off-road adventures. While generally very reliable, like any vehicle, it has a few specific areas that have seen more attention from owners over time. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 4.7L V8 (2UZ-FE)
**Timing Belt Replacement**
- **Symptoms:** While not a failure, this is a critical maintenance item. Neglecting it can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
- **Solution:** Scheduled replacement every 90,000 miles.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000

**Head Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, engine overheating, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gaskets and associated components.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $2500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers, Oil Pan)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil drips, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Suspension

### Adjustable Height Control (AHC) System
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, loss of adjustable height function, warning lights on the dash.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking hydraulic lines, accumulators, or pump.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust vehicle height, loud pump noise, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the AHC pump.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical gremlins, such as power window issues, door lock problems, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light, dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

## Interior

### Leather Upholstery
**Wear and Tear**
- **Symptoms:** Cracking or tearing of leather, particularly on driver's seat bolsters.

- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### Navigation System (if equipped)
**Outdated Maps/Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Inaccurate navigation, system freezing.

### Summary Verdict
The 2005 Lexus LX470 is a highly reliable vehicle, consistently praised for its durability and longevity. The 4.7L V8 engine is a workhorse, though routine maintenance like timing belt replacement is crucial. The AHC suspension system, while advanced, can be a source of repair costs if leaks or pump failures occur; however, many owners opt for conventional suspension upgrades. Electrical gremlins are infrequent but can sometimes be traced to the BCM. Overall, the LX470 offers excellent long-term value, with most potential issues being manageable or related to the advanced features of its era. Regular maintenance and addressing specific known issues proactively will ensure continued dependable service.
