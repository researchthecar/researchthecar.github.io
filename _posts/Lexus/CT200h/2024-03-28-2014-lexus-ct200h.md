**Introduction**

The 2014 Lexus CT200h is a compact hybrid luxury car that offers fuel efficiency and a comfortable cabin. It comes with a 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The CT200h has a combined horsepower of 134 hp and 151 lb-ft of torque. It is praised for its smooth ride, intuitive infotainment system, and high fuel efficiency. However, it is criticized for its limited cargo space and lack of power.

**Engine**

**1.8L Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause a loss of power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Remove the intake manifold and clean the valves manually.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty EGR valve can lead to engine stalling, reduced fuel efficiency, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT Pulley Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The CVT pulley can fail, causing the transmission to slip or shudder.
    * Solution: Replace the CVT pulley and belt.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn strut bushings can lead to excessive noise, vibration, and reduced stability.
    * Solution: Replace the worn strut bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **12V Battery Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The 12V battery can fail, leading to a loss of power and difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the 12V battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Clogs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clogged sunroof drains can cause water leaks into the cabin.
    * Solution: Clear the clogged sunroof drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system can malfunction, leading to inadequate heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Check and repair the climate control system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard can develop rattles or squeaks over time.
    * Solution: Remove and tighten any loose fasteners or apply sound-deadening materials.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Lexus CT200h is generally a reliable vehicle with few major issues. However, it is prone to some common problems, including intake valve carbon buildup, CVT pulley failure, and battery failure. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to significant. Overall, the CT200h offers a solid value for those seeking a fuel-efficient and comfortable compact car.
