**Introduction:**

The 2015 Lexus CT200h is a compact hybrid luxury hatchback powered by a 1.8L 4-cylinder gasoline engine paired with an electric motor. It delivers 134 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque. The CT200h is known for its fuel efficiency, refined interior, and quiet ride. However, it falls short in terms of performance and cargo capacity.

**Engine**

**1.8L 4-Cylinder Gasoline Engine**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket, visible on the engine as a wet or oily surface.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)**

* **Hesitation and Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or jerks during acceleration or gear changes.
Solution: Update the transmission control software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if under warranty
Recalls: SBDC-150198

**Suspension**

* **Rear Sway Bar End Links**
Description/Symptoms: Noises or clunking sounds from the rear suspension, especially over uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replace the sway bar end links.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Condensation or water droplets forming inside the headlights.
Solution: Replace the headlights.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Backup Camera Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The backup camera display freezes or shows a blank screen.
Solution: Replace the backup camera.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks**
Description/Symptoms: Squeaks or rattles coming from the dashboard, especially when driving over uneven surfaces.
Solution: Tighten or replace the dashboard panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

* **Center Console Trim Detachment**
Description/Symptoms: The trim on the center console becomes loose or detaching.
Solution: Replace or reattach the center console trim.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Scratches**
Description/Symptoms: Tiny scratches or streaks appear on the windshield after using the wipers.
Solution: Inspect and replace the wiper blades if necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $20 - $50
Recalls: None Found

* **Side Mirror Shake**
Description/Symptoms: The side mirrors shake or vibrate excessively at highway speeds.
Solution: Adjust the side mirrors or replace the mirror housings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Lexus CT200h has some reported issues, including valve cover gasket leaks, transmission hesitation, and interior noises. However, most of these issues are relatively minor and can be resolved at a moderate cost. Overall, the CT200h is a generally reliable and well-built vehicle that offers a balance of fuel efficiency, comfort, and luxury.
