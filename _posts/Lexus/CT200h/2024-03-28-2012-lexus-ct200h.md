**2012 Lexus CT200h**

The 2012 Lexus CT200h is a compact luxury hybrid hatchback. It is available with a single engine option: a 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, producing 134 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque. The CT200h is offered with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

Owners praise the CT200h for its fuel efficiency, comfortable ride, and luxurious features. However, it is criticized for its lack of power, poor acceleration, and cramped interior.

**Engine**
* **1.8L Four-Cylinder Hybrid Engine**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and engine damage
        * Solution: Replacing the piston rings and engine seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Tensioner fails, causing the timing chain to skip or break, resulting in engine damage
        * Solution: Replacing the timing chain and tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **CVT Transmission**
    * **Transmission Shudder:** Transmission experiences shuddering or vibrations during acceleration or deceleration
        * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control unit (TCU) or replacing the transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Windshield Washer System Malfunction:** Washer fluid leaks or fails to dispense properly
        * Solution: Replacing the washer fluid reservoir or pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **HVAC System Malfunction:** Air conditioning or heating system fails to function properly
        * Solution: Troubleshooting and repairing or replacing AC components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:** Power windows fail to operate or operate intermittently
        * Solution: Replacing the window motor or switch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Lexus CT200h is a fuel-efficient and luxurious hatchback, but it is not without its issues. Common problems include excessive oil consumption, timing chain tensioner failure, transmission shudder, electrical system failures, and HVAC system malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can be costly to repair. Overall, the CT200h is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing one.
