# 2007 Lexus IS250

### Overview
The 2007 Lexus IS250, a compact luxury sedan, is generally known for its strong reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. It offers a blend of sporty handling and refined comfort, powered by a capable V6 engine. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that have been reported by owners and technicians, providing insights into potential areas of concern and their typical resolutions.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L V6 (4GR-FSE)
**Carbon Buildup**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, reduced power, check engine light, misfires.
- **Solution:** Intake manifold cleaning, walnut blasting, or manual cleaning of intake valves.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Key Fob Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Key fob stops working unexpectedly, requires frequent battery replacement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of key fob battery, potential reprogramming if issues persist.
- **Cost:** $5 - $50

**Headlight Bulb Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not illuminating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of headlight bulbs.
- **Cost:** $50 - $150

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Lexus IS250 is a highly reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The primary areas that may require attention are engine-related, specifically carbon buildup on the intake valves and occasional oil leaks from valve cover gaskets. These are not uncommon for direct-injection engines of this era and are generally manageable with proper maintenance and timely repairs. Other systems like the transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components are typically robust and do not present widespread issues. Overall, the IS250 offers a dependable luxury car experience, provided routine maintenance is performed.
