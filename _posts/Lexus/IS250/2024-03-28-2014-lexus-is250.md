**Introduction**

The 2014 Lexus IS250 is a compact luxury sedan powered by a 2.5-liter V6 engine producing 204 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque. Paired with either a 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission, the IS250 offers a refined and sporty driving experience. It is praised for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.5L V6 Engine**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking engine oil, loss of engine power, engine overheating
Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Gear Grinding**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
Solution: Replace transmission gears
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Transmission Slippage**
Description/Symptoms: Delayed gear shifts, slipping out of gear
Solution: Replace transmission fluid, repair or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Strut Mounts**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, clunking sounds over bumps
Solution: Replace front suspension strut mounts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after a period of time
Solution: Inspect battery and charging system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside headlights
Solution: Replace headlights
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Center Console Trim Peeling**
Description/Symptoms: Peeling or chipping trim on center console
Solution: Replace center console trim
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Lexus IS250 has a number of potential issues that owners should be aware of. While most repairs are relatively minor and affordable, some problems such as transmission slippage and engine gasket failure can be costly to fix. It is important to consider these issues when making a purchase decision and to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic before purchasing.
