**Introduction:**

The 2012 Lexus IS250 is a compact luxury sedan praised for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and reliable performance. It comes standard with a 2.5-liter V6 engine producing 204 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 3.5-liter V6 with 306 horsepower and a six-speed manual transmission.

**Interior**

**Power Window Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The power window switch may fail, causing the windows to become inoperable.
Solution: Replace the faulty switch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
Recalls: None Found

**Climate Control Module Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control module may fail, causing the air conditioning, heating, and ventilation systems to malfunction.
Solution: Replace the faulty module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Lexus IS250 has a good track record of reliability. The few reported issues are relatively minor and affordable to fix. The interior power window switch and climate control module failures are the most notable issues, but they are not widespread.
