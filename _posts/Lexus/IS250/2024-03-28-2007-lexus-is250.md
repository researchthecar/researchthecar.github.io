## 2007 Lexus IS250

The 2007 Lexus IS250 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. It features a 2.5-liter V6 engine that produces 204 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission. The IS250 was lauded for its sharp handling, luxurious interior, and impressive fuel efficiency. However, it also garnered some criticism for its lack of rear legroom and limited cargo capacity.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter V6 with 6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Oil seeps from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning oil smell and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **VVT-i System Failure:** The Variable Valve Timing (VVT-i) system may malfunction, leading to reduced engine performance and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the VVT-i components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-liter V6 with 6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:** The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears, causing delayed acceleration and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding Gears:** Difficulty shifting into certain gears, accompanied by a grinding noise.
    * Solution: Replace the worn synchronizers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter may fail, leading to slipping, shuddering, and overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Ball Joint Failure:** The front lower ball joints can wear out over time, causing clunking noises and reduced steering response.
    * Solution: Replace the failed ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per ball joint
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Power Window Switch Failure:** The power window switches may become faulty, causing intermittent or complete window operation.
    * Solution: Replace the failed switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per switch
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Discharge:** A parasitic battery drain may occur, resulting in a dead battery when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the parasitic drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlight assemblies, causing condensation and impaired visibility.
    * Solution: Replace the defective headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per assembly
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Handle Malfunction:** The door handles may become loose or difficult to operate.
    * Solution: Replace the affected door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per handle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Radio Display Screen Pixelation:** The pixels on the radio display screen may become distorted or discolored.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty display screen.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing difficulty setting or maintaining the desired cabin temperature.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty component within the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

Overall, the 2007 Lexus IS250 has a relatively good track record of reliability. The most common issues include valve cover gasket leaks, VVT-i system failures, transmission slipping, and power window switch failure. While some issues, such as transmission rebuilds and torque converter replacements, can be expensive, most repairs are relatively affordable. The vehicle's main strengths lie in its comfortable and refined driving experience, while its weaknesses include limited rear legroom and cargo capacity.
