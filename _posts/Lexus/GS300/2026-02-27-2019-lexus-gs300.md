# 2019 Lexus GS300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Lexus GS300 means looking at a luxury sport sedan known for exceptional build quality and a quiet cabin. While owner reports for this model year are limited, prospective buyers should still inspect steering feel and standard mechanical components during a test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering the rear wheels. It offers a balanced blend of efficiency and daily drivability, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters and delivers smooth, responsive gear changes. It is generally robust and pairs well with the powertrain options for highway cruising.

## Steering

### Steering Rack

Owner feedback highlights rare instances of steering resistance at higher speeds, which should be checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Sticky Steering at Highway Speeds**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the steering wheel can feel stuck or resistant during sweeping turns at highway speeds, requiring a slight jerk to correct.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the steering rack and electronic power steering system for binding or mechanical resistance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS300&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
