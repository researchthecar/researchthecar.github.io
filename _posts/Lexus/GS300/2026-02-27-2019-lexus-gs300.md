### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Lexus GS300 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, demonstrates exceptional durability with no significant widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. These issues are typically manageable with software updates or component replacement and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall excellent reliability record. Owners can expect a dependable and low-maintenance ownership experience.
