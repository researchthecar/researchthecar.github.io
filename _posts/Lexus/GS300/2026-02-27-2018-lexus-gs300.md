### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Lexus GS300 demonstrates excellent mechanical reliability, with its 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The chassis and suspension components are also robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well, consistent with Lexus's reputation for quality. Overall, the 2018 GS300 is a highly reliable luxury sedan with a low probability of major mechanical problems, making it a sound choice for consumers prioritizing dependability.
