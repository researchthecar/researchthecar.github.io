# 2005 Lexus RX330

### Overview
The 2005 Lexus RX330 is a luxury crossover SUV known for its comfortable ride, refined interior, and strong reputation for reliability. It was offered with a capable 3.3L V6 engine paired with a smooth-shifting 5-speed automatic transmission. This generation of the RX is generally well-regarded for its durability, with owners often reporting few significant mechanical problems outside of routine maintenance. Common areas of concern, though not widespread, can include minor electrical glitches and occasional suspension component wear, typical for a vehicle of this age. Overall, the RX330 is considered a dependable choice in the used luxury SUV market.

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mounts/Bearings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises over bumps, especially at low speeds or when turning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn strut mounts and bearings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Squeaking or creaking noises from the front or rear suspension, vague steering feel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Power Locks/Windows**
- **Symptoms:** Power locks and windows may operate erratically or stop working altogether.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2005 Lexus RX330 stands out as a highly reliable luxury SUV. Its powertrain, consisting of the 3.3L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are related to suspension components like strut mounts and control arm bushings, which can develop wear over time leading to noises. Minor electrical concerns, such as intermittent issues with power accessories or occasional alternator failure, are also noted but not universally experienced. The exterior and interior generally hold up well. For its age, the RX330 offers a dependable ownership experience with manageable repair costs for the few common issues.
