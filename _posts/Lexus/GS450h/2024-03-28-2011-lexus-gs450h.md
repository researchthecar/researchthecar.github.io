**Introduction:**
The 2011 Lexus GS450h is a luxury hybrid sedan with a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an electric motor, producing 338 horsepower and 357 lb-ft of torque. It features a 10-speed automatic transmission and is praised for its smooth performance, fuel efficiency, and refined interior. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**
* **V6**
    * **Oil Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption leading to oil leaks, indicated by low oil levels and visible oil droplets underneath the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other affected seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Piston Ring Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Severe engine knocking, loss of power, and increased oil consumption.
        * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **10-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shift Shock:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Abrupt or delayed gear shifts, accompanied by jerking or clunking noises.
        * Solution: Software update or transmission fluid replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises when driving over uneven surfaces, accompanied by reduced steering precision.
    * Solution: Replacement of lower control arm bushings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Faulty Navigation System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: System failure, freezing, or incorrect route calculation.
    * Solution: Software update or navigation unit replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly when parked, leading to difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components, such as door switches or trunk latches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside headlight assemblies, impairing visibility at night.
    * Solution: Replacement of headlights or installation of aftermarket vents.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200 per side
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Sunroof Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or wind noise coming from the sunroof area when driving at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty temperature control, uneven airflow, or excessive noise from the air conditioning system.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty sensors, actuators, or blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2011 Lexus GS450h generally receives positive feedback for its performance and luxury. However, some owners have reported issues with various components, including engine oil leaks, transmission shift shock, and electrical problems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but they should be considered before purchasing a used GS450h.
