# 2016 Lexus GS450h

### Overview
The 2016 Lexus GS450h is a luxury hybrid sedan known for its blend of performance, comfort, and fuel efficiency. As part of Lexus's commitment to quality, the GS450h generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting the brand's reputation for durable powertrains and well-built interiors. This model year benefits from Lexus's extensive experience with hybrid technology, integrating the electric motor and gasoline engine seamlessly. Owners typically report a satisfying ownership experience with fewer unexpected repairs compared to many competitors in its class. The focus on premium materials and advanced engineering contributes to its long-term dependability, making it a solid choice for those seeking a reliable luxury hybrid.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus GS450h is a highly reliable luxury sedan, particularly noted for its robust hybrid powertrain and overall build quality. While most components function without significant issues, potential owners should be aware of the long-term cost associated with hybrid battery degradation, which is a common factor in most hybrid vehicles as they age. Infotainment system glitches are also a possibility, though typically resolvable with software updates. The engine, transmission, suspension, exterior, and interior generally hold up very well, contributing to a low cost of ownership in terms of unexpected repairs. Overall, the GS450h offers a dependable and premium driving experience.
