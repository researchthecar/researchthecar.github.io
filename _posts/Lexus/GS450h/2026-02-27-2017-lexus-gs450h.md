# 2017 Lexus GS450h

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus GS450h is a luxury hybrid sedan known for its blend of performance, comfort, and fuel efficiency. As a Lexus product, it generally upholds the brand's reputation for reliability. The GS450h utilizes a sophisticated hybrid powertrain, combining a V6 gasoline engine with electric motors. This advanced system, while complex, has proven to be robust. Owners typically report a high degree of satisfaction with the vehicle's long-term dependability, with most issues being minor or related to standard maintenance rather than inherent design flaws. The build quality and premium materials also contribute to a positive ownership experience, minimizing common interior and exterior wear.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus GS450h is a highly reliable luxury sedan, consistent with Lexus's reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 hybrid engine and ECVT transmission, demonstrates excellent durability. The primary area where owners might encounter costs is related to the hybrid battery system, which can degrade over its lifespan, though this is typical for hybrid vehicles. Minor electrical glitches with the infotainment system are also occasionally reported. Overall, the GS450h offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal concerns regarding its core mechanical and structural components.
