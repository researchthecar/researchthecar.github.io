# 2017 Lexus GS450h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus GS450h combines luxury, performance, and hybrid efficiency into an upscale midsize sedan. For used car shoppers, Lexus models generally command a strong reputation for overall dependability, though buyers should still carefully inspect individual vehicle condition and maintenance history.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

This powertrain features a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an electric motor system, generating around 338 total system horsepower. It delivers smooth, responsive acceleration alongside excellent fuel economy for a midsize luxury sedan, maintaining the high standards expected of Lexus hybrid engineering.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The continuously variable transmission is specifically tuned for hybrid applications, seamlessly blending power from the gas engine and electric drive. It offers smooth operation and robust long-term reliability under normal driving conditions.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Brake system complaints are rare for this model, but any reports of stopping concerns should be thoroughly evaluated.

**Brake System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported experiencing a sudden failure of the service brakes.
- **Solution:** Have the braking system inspected immediately by a qualified technician to diagnose any hydraulic or electronic faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus GS450h remains a compelling choice for used luxury sedan buyers who value advanced hybrid efficiency and strong brand reliability. While isolated owner complaints can occur with any pre-owned vehicle, a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help ensure the car is in top mechanical condition.
