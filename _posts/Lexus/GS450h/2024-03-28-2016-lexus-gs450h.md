**Introduction:**

The 2016 Lexus GS450h is a luxury hybrid sedan powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 338 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It offers a smooth and quiet ride, with praise for its refined interior and advanced technology features. However, there are some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **Coolant Leak at Water Pump:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant from the water pump, causing overheating or engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace water pump and gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT Transmission Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation, shuddering, or slipping during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,500 - $5,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Air Suspension Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of air pressure in rear air suspension, resulting in sagging or uneven ride height.
    * Solution: Replace air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: LED headlights flicker or turn off intermittently.
    * Solution: Replace affected headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Keyless Entry System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Keyless entry system not recognizing the key fob, preventing door unlocking and starting.
    * Solution: Replace key fob battery or reprogram keyless entry module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Fogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation or fogging inside the rear bumper, compromising visibility.
    * Solution: Replace bumper assembly or seal leaks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises or rattling vindo from the dashboard area.
    * Solution: Replace faulty dashboard clips or trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System Freezing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation screen freezes or becomes unresponsive.
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Lexus GS450h is a well-received vehicle, but it has some reported issues that range in severity and cost. Major issues such as transmission malfunctions or engine leaks can be expensive to repair, while minor issues like dashboard rattles or navigation system freezing are less costly to address. It's important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their consideration when making a purchase decision.
