**Introduction**

The 2019 Lexus NX300h is a compact luxury crossover SUV that offers a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and a hybrid powertrain that delivers impressive fuel economy. It is available with a 2.5-liter four-cylinder gasoline engine and an electric motor that produces a combined 194 horsepower and 152 lb-ft of torque. The NX300h comes standard with a continuously variable transmission (CVT) and front-wheel drive, while all-wheel drive is optional.

**Engine**

* **2.5-Liter Four-Cylinder**
    * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, misfiring, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * **Timing Chain Noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or grinding noise from engine
        * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, poor fuel economy, rough idle
        * Solution: Clean intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Electric Motor**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **CVT**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears, overheating
        * Solution: Replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from suspension, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery or electrical components that are draining the battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Window Trim Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Chrome window trim peeling or losing its shine
    * Solution: Replace window trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Freeze**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes or becomes unresponsive
    * Solution: Reset infotainment system or update software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $200+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Lexus NX300h is a reliable vehicle with only a few known issues. The engine and transmission issues can be expensive to fix, but they are relatively rare. The other issues are less severe and generally less expensive to repair.
