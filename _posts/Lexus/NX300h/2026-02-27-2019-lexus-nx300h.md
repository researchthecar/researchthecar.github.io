# 2019 Lexus NX300h

### Overview
The 2019 Lexus NX300h is a compact luxury hybrid SUV known for its strong reliability and comfortable ride. It combines a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with electric motors, offering a good balance of performance and fuel efficiency. Lexus's reputation for meticulous engineering and quality materials generally translates into a dependable ownership experience. Common owner feedback highlights its quiet cabin, user-friendly technology, and Lexus's renowned customer service. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the NX300h typically performs well in long-term reliability surveys, with owners reporting satisfaction with its powertrain and overall build quality. Potential concerns, though infrequent, tend to be minor and related to specific electronic components or accessory functions.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Lexus NX300h demonstrates excellent reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The hybrid powertrain and drivetrain components are exceptionally robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. Minor concerns are primarily isolated to the infotainment system, which may experience occasional glitches requiring software updates or resets, and a possibility of premature 12V battery failure, a common issue across many modern vehicles. Overall, the NX300h is a highly dependable choice for consumers seeking a luxury hybrid SUV with low running costs and minimal unexpected repairs. Its build quality and meticulous engineering contribute to a positive ownership experience.
