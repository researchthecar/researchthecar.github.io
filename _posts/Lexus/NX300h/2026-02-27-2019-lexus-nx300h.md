# 2019 Lexus NX300h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Lexus NX 300h, buyers generally expect the renowned reliability and upscale comfort associated with the brand. This hybrid luxury compact SUV combines efficiency with practical styling, making it an appealing daily driver. While owner reports to federal safety regulators highlight a few isolated areas of concern, the vehicle overall maintains a strong reputation for dependability.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

The 2.5-liter four-cylinder hybrid engine pairs with electric motors to produce a net 194 horsepower, delivering smooth and efficient performance. This powertrain is generally very robust and long-lasting, though sporadic owner complaints regarding fuel system components and power delivery do exist.

**Sudden Loss of Power and RPM Fluctuation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected drops in vehicle speed while driving or erratic engine RPM idling at stops, accompanied by warning dashboard lights.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic diagnose any stored diagnostic trouble codes (DTCs) and inspect fuel delivery components or throttle sensors.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The electronically controlled continuously variable transmission distributes power seamlessly to the front wheels or available all-wheel-drive system. It is widely considered durable and trouble-free with no widespread complaint patterns reported by owners.

## Exterior

### Powered Tailgate

Exterior convenience features like the powered liftgate generally operate reliably, though occasional mechanical or electrical glitches can require component servicing.

**Trunk Failure to Open Automatically**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that the powered rear liftgate occasionally fails to open via electronic switches, requiring manual force.
- **Solution:** Inspect the tailgate lift motors, wiring harnesses, and latch mechanism for binding or electrical faults, and replace faulty actuators if necessary.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Parking Sensors

Advanced safety systems provide helpful driver assistance, but environmental factors can temporarily interfere with sensor functionality.

**Sensor Faults in Heavy Weather**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience error messages during heavy rain or severe weather indicating that front active safety systems and parking sensors are blocked or disabled.
- **Solution:** Inspect and clean the front bumper sensor areas; if faults persist after weather clears, have the sensor alignment and software checked by a dealer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V028000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus NX 300h stands out as a dependable and fuel-efficient option in the luxury hybrid SUV segment. While prospective buyers should check for proper function of the powered liftgate and verify that advanced safety sensors operate without weather interference, the vehicle's powertrain and overall build quality inspire confidence. A thorough pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a worry-free ownership experience.
