# 2016 Lexus NX300h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus NX 300h delivers the expected luxury and efficiency associated with the brand, making it an appealing option for used car buyers seeking a compact hybrid crossover. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain performance and braking systems before purchasing. While Lexus models generally maintain strong reputation scores for long-term dependability, individual vehicle maintenance history remains critical.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 (NX 200t)

A 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing roughly 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It offers responsive acceleration for daily driving, though owners have occasionally reported stalling and oil leak issues.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden engine stalling, loss of acceleration, or enters a low-speed idle safe mode while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes, inspect fuel and ignition systems, and repair or replace failing engine management components.

**Rear Main Seal Oil Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Fresh engine oil spots discovered on the ground underneath the vehicle, indicating a seal failure.
- **Solution:** Replace the leaking rear main seal, which typically requires separating the engine and transmission.

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid (NX 300h)

A 2.5-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine paired with an electric hybrid system delivering a combined 194 horsepower. It prioritizes high fuel economy and smooth power delivery with generally solid reliability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (NX 200t)

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with the turbocharged engine, delivering smooth shifting behavior under normal operating conditions.

**Limp Mode and Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission unexpectedly enters 'limp mode' while driving on the highway, restricting vehicle speed and power.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module scanned for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect internal components or fluid condition.

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

An electronic continuously variable transmission paired with the hybrid drivetrain, designed for seamless power blending and optimal fuel efficiency.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

The braking system provides stopping power suited for a compact luxury crossover, but a small number of owners have reported concerning low-pedal and ABS actuator failures.

**Soft Pedal and Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal feels loose, spongy, or requires being pressed fully to the floor, resulting in significantly reduced stopping performance.
- **Solution:** Inspect the master cylinder, brake booster, and ABS actuator, and bleed or flush the brake fluid system.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

The exterior glass and panels are generally well-constructed, though isolated instances of spontaneous sunroof breakage have been documented.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof glass suddenly shatters while driving at highway speeds without any external impact or collision.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered sunroof glass assembly and clean up any broken glass debris from the interior headliner.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V085000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag pressure sensors and/or the acceleration sensors may fail causing the side/curtain shield air bags and/or the front air bags to not deploy in the event of a crash. If the air bags fail to deploy in a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the sensors as necessary based on their serial numbers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus NX 300h and its sibling powertrains provide a comfortable, fuel-efficient luxury experience, but prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle prior to purchase. Pay close attention to brake pedal responsiveness, check for any signs of engine oil leaks or stalling history, and verify that all service records are up to date to minimize unexpected repair costs.
