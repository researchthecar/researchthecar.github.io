# 2021 Lexus NX300h

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus NX300h is a hybrid luxury compact SUV known for its refined interior, comfortable ride, and excellent fuel efficiency. As part of Lexus's commitment to reliability, the NX300h generally performs very well, with owners reporting a high degree of satisfaction. The hybrid powertrain, combining a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with electric motors, is a well-established and robust system. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the NX300h stands out for its low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems, making it a strong contender for those seeking a dependable premium SUV. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its long-term performance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus NX300h demonstrates exceptional reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. Major mechanical and powertrain issues are exceedingly rare. The most commonly cited, albeit minor, concern relates to occasional infotainment system glitches, which are typically resolved with software updates. The hybrid system, including the battery, is robust and designed for longevity. Owners can expect a dependable ownership experience with minimal unexpected repair costs, provided regular maintenance is performed. This model is a solid choice for those prioritizing durability and low running costs in the luxury compact SUV segment.
