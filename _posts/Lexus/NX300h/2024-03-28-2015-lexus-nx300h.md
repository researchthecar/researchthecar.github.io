**Introduction to the 2015 Lexus NX300h**

The 2015 Lexus NX300h is a luxury compact crossover SUV that boasts a fuel-efficient hybrid powertrain. It is powered by a 2.5-liter four-cylinder gasoline engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined output of 194 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque. The NX300h is offered with two transmission options: a continuously variable transmission (CVT) or a six-speed automatic transmission.

Owners generally praise the NX300h for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, some issues have been reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Four-Cylinder Gasoline Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating engine, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-liter Four-Cylinder Hybrid Engine**

* No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Transmission Shuddering**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration or gear changes
    * Solution: Replace CVT fluid or transmission assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from rear suspension
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension bushings or sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flicker or turn off while driving
    * Solution: Replace headlight ballast or wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Seal Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin from sunroof
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system does not work properly
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Lexus NX300h is a reliable vehicle overall, but some issues have been reported, particularly with the intake manifold gasket, transmission shuddering, rear suspension noise, headlight malfunctions, sunroof seal leaks, and climate control problems. The cost to fix these issues can vary depending on the severity of the problem and the labor costs in your area.
