# 2017 Lexus NX300h

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus NX300h is a compact luxury hybrid SUV known for its strong reliability and comfortable ride. It combines a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine with electric motors, offering a good balance of performance and fuel efficiency. Owners generally report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs. The hybrid system is a particular highlight, demonstrating Lexus's long-standing expertise in this technology. Build quality is typically high, contributing to its reputation for durability. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the NX300h stands out for its consistent performance and low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems, making it a sound choice for those seeking a dependable luxury crossover.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus NX300h demonstrates excellent reliability across most major components. The hybrid powertrain is particularly robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system's responsiveness, are the most commonly cited, but these are typically resolved with software updates and do not represent major mechanical failures. The vehicle's build quality and meticulous engineering contribute to its strong reputation for dependability, making it a highly recommended option for buyers prioritizing long-term reliability and low ownership costs.
