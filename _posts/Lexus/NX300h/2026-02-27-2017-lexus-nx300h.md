# 2017 Lexus NX300h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Lexus NX 300h, buyers generally expect the refined build quality and durability associated with the brand. While owner reports to federal regulators highlight a few isolated areas of concern regarding electronics and equipment, the overall ownership experience tends to be quite dependable. Reviewing specific components before purchase remains a sensible precaution for any pre-owned luxury hybrid.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

This 2.5-liter four-cylinder hybrid engine pairs with an electric motor to produce a net 194 horsepower. It is generally known for strong efficiency and solid mechanical durability.

**Water Pump Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks from the water pump can compromise the engine cooling system and potentially lead to overheating or internal damage if ignored.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump for signs of coolant residue and replace the unit immediately if a leak is detected.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled CVT

An electronically controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) distributes power smoothly to the front wheels or available all-wheel-drive system, offering high reliability in daily driving.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering concerns are rare, but occasional electronic assist dropouts have been reported by owners.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may suddenly become extremely difficult to turn at low speeds, accompanied by a loss of power assist.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic power steering module and sensors diagnosed; restarting the vehicle may temporarily clear the fault, but faulty components may need replacement.

## Interior

### Seats

Cabin comforts are well-appointed, though localized electrical issues within climate-controlled seats have occasionally surfaced.

**Heated and Cooled Seat Burn**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced localized overheating or minor burning on the surface of the front passenger seat upholstery.
- **Solution:** Discontinue use of the affected seat heater and replace the internal heating element and upholstery cover.

## Safety

### Forward Collision and Parking Assist

Advanced driver-assistance technologies generally enhance safety, though sensor calibration issues can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking.

**Unintended Forward Braking or Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected activation of the emergency braking system at highway speeds or sudden acceleration issues while parking.
- **Solution:** Inspect radar and camera sensors for obstruction or misalignment, and check for software updates at a qualified service center.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus NX 300h offers an appealing blend of hybrid fuel economy and upscale comfort, making it a solid contender in the used luxury crossover market. While a few owners have experienced issues ranging from coolant leaks to electronic safety system glitches, these reports remain relatively isolated. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the cooling system, electronics, and interior features to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
