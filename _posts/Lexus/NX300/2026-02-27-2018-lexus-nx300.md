# 2018 Lexus NX300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus NX300 blends luxury compact SUV styling with everyday practicality, drawing on a reputation for solid overall build quality. While owner-reported complaints are relatively few, prospective buyers should review specific powertrain and electrical feedback before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering punchy acceleration for daily driving. It generally provides strong reliability, though owners have occasionally noted isolated fuel delivery and stalling issues.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel System Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving, sometimes accompanied by warning lights indicating reduced engine power or oil pressure.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check fuel pump operation, software calibrations, or related fault codes.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the turbocharged engine across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It is widely considered durable with minimal widespread mechanical complaints.

## Exterior

### Body and Glass

Exterior styling is sharp and distinctive, though buyers should inspect trim seals and glass panels during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A few owners have experienced spontaneous shattering of the glass sunroof without any visible impact or debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the sunroof frame for structural alignment issues.

## Safety

### Brakes and Collision Avoidance

Safety equipment generally performs well, though individual owner reports highlight rare anomalies with braking response.

**Brake Pedal Travel and Collision Avoidance Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Isolated reports describe unusual brake pedal softness during heavy traffic or unexpected vehicle acceleration.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake hydraulic system, master cylinder, and throttle linkages thoroughly.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX300&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus NX300 remains a comfortable and generally dependable luxury crossover choice. Buyers should perform a thorough test drive to check for proper engine operation, smooth transmission shifting, and sound mechanical integrity.
