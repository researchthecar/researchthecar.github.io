# 2019 Lexus NX300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury compact SUV, the Lexus NX300 generally offers a strong reputation for comfort and brand reliability. However, owner reports highlight a few specific areas worth inspecting closely before making a purchase, ranging from fuel system concerns to accelerated component wear.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive daily power. While generally robust, buyers should verify that fuel pump recall services have been properly completed.

**Fuel Pump Failure and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving, which can create safety risks in traffic.
- **Solution:** Check service history for completed fuel pump recalls or have the low-pressure fuel pump inspected and replaced if necessary.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth power delivery to either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. It maintains a solid reputation for long-term durability with standard fluid maintenance.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides confident stopping power, though some drivers experience shorter-than-average pad life depending on driving style.

**Accelerated Brake Pad Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced unusually rapid wear on the front brake pads, requiring replacements at relatively low mileage intervals.
- **Solution:** Inspect front brake pad thickness and rotor condition during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Exterior

### Mirrors and Glass

Exterior body panels and trim are generally well-assembled, though minor wind-buffeting or glass-related complaints have been documented by owners.

**Side Mirror Vibration and Sunroof Shutter**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted noticeable vibration from the driver-side mirror at highway speeds, alongside rare instances of spontaneous sunroof glass shattering.
- **Solution:** Inspect exterior mirrors during a test drive at highway speeds and carefully check the panoramic or standard sunroof glass for any pre-existing stress cracks.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX300&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The Lexus NX300 remains a comfortable and upscale choice for used luxury SUV buyers, backed by solid overall mechanical engineering. Prospective buyers should prioritize verifying that all manufacturer safety recalls—particularly concerning the fuel system—have been addressed, and ensure a thorough inspection of the brakes and exterior glass.
