# 2021 Lexus NX300

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus NX300 is a compact luxury SUV known for its refined interior, comfortable ride, and strong reputation for reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. It typically comes equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a six-speed automatic transmission, offering a balanced blend of performance and fuel efficiency. Owners generally praise its build quality and the advanced technology features integrated into the cabin. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the NX300 generally demonstrates a high level of dependability, with most reported concerns being minor and infrequent. ### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus NX300 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, aligning with Lexus's established reputation for durability. Major mechanical components like the engine and transmission show no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that can often be resolved with software updates, and in rare cases, premature battery failure. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well, with no common complaints regarding wear or defects. Overall, the NX300 is a dependable choice for luxury SUV buyers seeking a vehicle with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
