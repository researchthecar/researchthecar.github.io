**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2022 Lexus GX460 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its rugged off-road capabilities and spacious interior. It comes with a single engine option: a 4.6-liter V8 that generates 301 horsepower and 329 lb-ft of torque. The only transmission offered is a six-speed automatic transmission. Despite its age, the GX460 remains a popular choice among those seeking a blend of luxury and off-road prowess. However, it has been criticized for its high fuel consumption, lack of modern safety features, and dated interior design.

**Interior**
* **Instrument Cluster Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The instrument cluster may experience intermittent flickering or complete failure, rendering gauges and warning lights inoperable.
    * Solution: Replacement of the instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
* **Rear Seat Entertainment System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear seat entertainment system may malfunction, displaying error messages or freezing.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the entertainment system module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
* **Climate Control Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may emit excessive noise, such as a rattling or buzzing sound.
    * Solution: Lubrication or replacement of the climate control motor or fan.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Moonroof Drain Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: The moonroof drain lines may become clogged, causing water to leak into the interior headliner.
    * Solution: Cleaning of the drain lines or replacement of the moonroof cassette.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+

**Recalls:**

* NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V628000
    * Issue: Failure of the front seat belt pretensioner
* NHTSA Campaign Number: 23V065000
    * Issue: Software error in the skid control management system

**Summary:**

The 2022 Lexus GX460 faces a range of issues, mostly affecting its interior and technology features. While the instrument cluster malfunction and rear seat entertainment system failure can be costly to repair, the climate control noise and moonroof drain clogging are less severe and less expensive to fix. Overall, the GX460 remains a capable and luxurious SUV, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
