**Introduction:**

The 2023 Lexus GX460 is a midsize luxury SUV renowned for its rugged off-road capabilities and premium interior. It features a powerful V8 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, delivering 301 horsepower and 329 lb-ft of torque. Despite its durability, several issues have emerged with specific components of the GX460.

**Engine**

**4.6L V8 Engine**

* **Oil leaks:** Various gaskets and seals may develop leaks, causing oil loss and potential engine damage. Symptoms include low oil levels and visible oil stains.
    * Solution: Replace faulty gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Delayed shifting:** The transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears. Symptoms include rough shifts and poor acceleration.
    * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission module or replacing defective components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Worn rear shock absorbers:** Excessive play or noise from the rear suspension. Symptoms include increased body roll and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace worn shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Faulty alternator:** The alternator may fail to charge the battery properly. Symptoms include flickering lights, battery warning lights, and difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace faulty alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Loose tailgate handle:** The tailgate handle may become loose and difficult to operate. Symptoms include rattling or inability to open the tailgate.
    * Solution: Replace or tighten the tailgate handle assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: 930301

**Interior**

* **Squeaky sun visor:** The sun visor may make creaking or squeaking noises when adjusted.
    * Solution: Lubricate the visor hinges.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Lubrication can be done by the owner)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2023 Lexus GX460 offers many desirable features, it has experienced several issues with its engine, transmission, and other components. The severity of these issues varies, with some requiring costly repairs. Potential owners should be aware of these issues and consider the potential costs before making a purchase decision.
