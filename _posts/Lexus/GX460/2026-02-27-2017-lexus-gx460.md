# 2017 Lexus GX460 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Lexus GX460 is widely regarded as a rugged, highly durable luxury SUV known for its body-on-frame construction and traditional off-road capability. While its mechanical foundation is exceptionally stout compared to many modern crossovers, used-car buyers should still review owner-reported complaints regarding electronics, body paint, and forward collision systems. Keeping up with routine maintenance and thoroughly inspecting used models will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.6L V8 Engine

Powered by a 4.6-liter V8 engine producing roughly 301 horsepower and 329 lb-ft of torque, this powerplant offers robust performance and traditional truck-based durability. Overall, the engine is considered exceptionally reliable when properly maintained.

**Engine Stalling and Starting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the engine unexpectedly stalled while driving or failed to start, requiring a tow and battery replacement.
- **Solution:** Have the battery, charging system, and fuel components thoroughly tested by a qualified mechanic to ensure proper electrical voltage and fuel delivery.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission paired with a full-time four-wheel-drive system, this drivetrain setup is known for smooth operation and long-term durability with no major recurring complaint trends.

## Suspension

### Suspension Alignment and Vehicle Lean

The suspension balances highway comfort with heavy-duty off-road capability, though a small number of owners report minor cosmetic ride-height variances.

**Passenger Side Lean**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners notice that the vehicle appears to sit slightly lower on the passenger side compared to the driver's side.
- **Solution:** Inspect the coil springs, air suspension components (if equipped), and ride height sensors for wear or out-of-specification calibration.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display Screen

Electrical systems are generally dependable, though occasional glitches in the infotainment screen and sensor integration have been documented by owners.

**Screen Blanking and System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The navigation and infotainment screen may go completely black, temporarily disabling controls for climate systems, audio, and defrosters.
- **Solution:** Check software updates, fuses, and electrical connections at the display unit; replacement of the display module may be necessary in persistent failures.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior panels and trim hold up well over time, though isolated reports of spontaneous sunroof glass breakage warrant careful inspection.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass has been reported to spontaneously shatter while driving at highway speeds without any apparent external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the sunroof frame for alignment issues or stress concentrations.

### Structure and Paint

Body structure and corrosion resistance are generally high, though certain exterior paint finishes have exhibited premature peeling issues.

**Roof Paint Peeling and Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The factory paint on the roof of white vehicles has been reported to bubble, peel, and crack prematurely.
- **Solution:** Consult a professional auto body shop for repainting and surface sealing to prevent further clear coat or paint degradation.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance protection, but a few driver complaints highlight issues with overly sensitive or false-positive collision mitigation braking.

**Erroneous Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected activation of the automatic emergency braking system while traveling at normal speeds, causing the vehicle to brake abruptly.
- **Solution:** Have the radar and camera sensors inspected, recalibrated, or checked for obstructions by a trained technician.

## Summary Verdict

The Lexus GX460 stands out as a remarkably durable and capable luxury SUV for used vehicle shoppers. While minor complaints regarding paint integrity, sunroof glass, and driver-assistance sensors do exist, its robust V8 powertrain and solid frame construction mean it routinely outlasts many competitors when properly maintained. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, paint condition, and safety sensors to secure a trouble-free vehicle.
