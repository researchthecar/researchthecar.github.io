# 2017 Lexus GX460

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus GX460 is a body-on-frame SUV known for its robust construction and off-road capability. Built on a proven platform, it generally exhibits strong reliability, especially in its powertrain. Owners often praise its durability and the smooth operation of its V8 engine. While it's a capable vehicle, like any automotive product, it can experience specific issues. This report focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for the 2017 model year.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus GX460 demonstrates a high level of reliability, particularly with its core powertrain components. The 4.6L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are known for their durability and longevity, with no significant widespread issues reported. Areas that may require attention include the Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS), which can develop leaks, and occasional electrical gremlins within the infotainment system or parasitic battery drain. Interior and exterior components are generally robust, though minor issues like headlight condensation or climate control blend door actuator failures can occur. Overall, the GX460 remains a dependable choice for those seeking a capable and long-lasting luxury SUV.
