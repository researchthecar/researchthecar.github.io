# 2016 Lexus GX460 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus GX460 carries a strong reputation for rugged, body-on-frame durability and luxurious everyday comfort. While owner reports to NHTSA are generally sparse for this model year, used-car buyers should still review common pre-owned considerations including occasional electronic lock failures and isolated owner reports regarding braking and throttle response.

---

## Engine

### 4.6L V8 (1UR-FE)

The 4.6-liter V8 engine produces 301 horsepower and 329 lb-ft of torque, providing robust towing capability and smooth power delivery. It is generally regarded as exceptionally durable, though isolated owner complaints note rare stalling incidents.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalling while coasting or slowing down.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a qualified technician; monitor for any applicable manufacturer service campaigns.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The traditional 6-speed automatic transmission comes paired with full-time four-wheel drive. It offers smooth, predictable gear changes and generally maintains a strong track record for long-term mechanical reliability.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

The braking hardware is generally robust, though pre-owned buyers should thoroughly inspect the hydraulic system and brake pads during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Brake Pedal Response and Hold Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the brakes felt unresponsive at low speeds or released slightly after being held for an extended period at a stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake booster, master cylinder, and ABS actuator components for proper function and hydraulic pressure retention.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators

Interior electronics and convenience features are mostly trouble-free, with occasional reports of power door lock actuator wear.

**Electronic Door Lock Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Driver, passenger, or rear tailgate electronic door locks may fail to lock or unlock properly.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty door lock actuator assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V741000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GX460&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Depending on the vehicle model, dealers will replace the front driver inflator, front passenger airbag inflator, the airbag assembly, or the front passenger airbag sub-assembly and inflator. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus GX460 remains a highly dependable luxury SUV choice for used-car buyers seeking traditional truck-based capability. While isolated owner complaints highlight minor electrical lock wear and rare brake-related concerns, its overall mechanical foundation is exceptionally solid. Always perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection before finalizing your purchase.
