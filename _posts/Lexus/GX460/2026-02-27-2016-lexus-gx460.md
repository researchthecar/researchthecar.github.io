# 2016 Lexus GX460

### Overview
The 2016 Lexus GX460 is a body-on-frame SUV known for its robust construction and off-road capability. Built on a proven platform, it generally exhibits strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain. Owners often praise its durability and the smooth operation of its V8 engine. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the GX460 tends to avoid widespread, recurring problems that plague many other SUVs in its class. Attention to regular maintenance is key to preserving its long-term performance. The focus for this model year is on its established mechanicals, offering a dependable choice for those seeking a capable and luxurious utility vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus GX460 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly concerning its core powertrain. The 4.6L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are exceptionally durable, with no significant widespread issues reported. The primary area of concern, though not common, is the Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS), which can occasionally develop leaks in its actuator, impacting performance and requiring a moderately expensive repair. Electrical components, especially the infotainment system, may experience minor glitches, but these are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the GX460 offers excellent long-term reliability, making it a sound investment for those prioritizing durability and capability over cutting-edge technology.
