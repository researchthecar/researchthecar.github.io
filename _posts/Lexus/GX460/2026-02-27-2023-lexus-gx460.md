# 2023 Lexus GX460

### Overview
The 2023 Lexus GX 460 is a body-on-frame SUV known for its robust construction and off-road capability. It continues to offer a proven powertrain and a luxurious interior, aiming for long-term durability. While generally reliable, like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The GX 460's reputation for reliability is largely built on its well-established components and Lexus's commitment to quality control, though some specific systems may require attention over time.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Lexus GX 460 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly with its core powertrain. The 4.6L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are well-regarded for their durability. Potential areas for attention are primarily related to the advanced Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS), which can develop hydraulic leaks, and some electrical components, such as the infotainment system experiencing glitches or intermittent battery drain issues. These are not universal problems but are documented concerns that owners might encounter. For the most part, the GX 460 remains a dependable choice, with most issues being repairable and not indicative of fundamental design flaws.
