# 2022 Lexus GX460

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus GX460 is a body-on-frame SUV known for its robust construction and off-road capability. It largely carries over its proven powertrain and chassis from previous generations, which contributes to its generally strong reliability record. While not the most modern vehicle in its class, its established components tend to be dependable. Owners generally report satisfaction with its durability, with most issues being minor or related to typical usage rather than inherent design flaws. The GX460 prioritizes longevity and a comfortable, albeit somewhat dated, driving experience, making it a solid choice for those seeking a reliable luxury SUV.

**KDSS Actuator Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Potential for hydraulic fluid leaks from the KDSS actuator, leading to reduced on-road handling and off-road articulation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the KDSS actuator unit.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus GX460 demonstrates strong overall reliability, largely due to its mature and well-tested platform. The 4.6L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are known for their durability and have a minimal track record of significant issues. The primary area where owners might encounter specific problems is with the Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS), which can occasionally develop leaks in its actuator. Electrical components, particularly the infotainment system, may experience minor, infrequent glitches. However, these are generally not widespread and often resolvable with software updates. The interior and exterior components are typical of Lexus quality, showing good longevity. For buyers prioritizing dependability and a robust build, the GX460 is a commendable choice, with most potential repairs being manageable.
