# 2021 Lexus GX460

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus GX460 is a body-on-frame SUV known for its robust construction and off-road capability. Built on a proven platform, it generally exhibits strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain. Owners often praise its durability and the longevity of its mechanical components. While it may not offer the latest in automotive technology or fuel efficiency compared to its unibody competitors, its focus on ruggedness translates to fewer widespread electronic glitches or complex system failures. The GX460 is a vehicle designed for longevity and dependable service, especially when subjected to regular maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus GX460 demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with Lexus's reputation. The 4.6L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are well-regarded for their durability and lack of common failures. The primary area where owners might encounter issues is the Kinetic Dynamic Suspension System (KDSS), which can develop leaks over time, impacting ride quality. Electrical components, particularly the infotainment system, can occasionally exhibit minor glitches, but these are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the GX460 is a dependable choice for those prioritizing longevity and robust performance over cutting-edge technology.
