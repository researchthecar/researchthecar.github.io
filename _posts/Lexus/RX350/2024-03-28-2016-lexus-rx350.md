**Introduction**

The 2016 Lexus RX350 is a midsize luxury SUV that offers a refined driving experience and a spacious interior. It comes standard with a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 295 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The RX350 also offers an optional hybrid powertrain that combines a 3.5-liter V6 engine with two electric motors, providing a combined output of 308 horsepower.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume excessive oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and oil control rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation or Jerking During Gear Shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk during gear shifts, especially when accelerating or decelerating.
    * Solution: Update transmission software and/or replace valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may experience malfunctions, causing issues with various components such as lights, gauges, or the infotainment system.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V605 (Battery Terminal Corrosion)

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, leading to issues with temperature regulation or airflow.
    * Solution: Replace or repair faulty control module, blower motor, or other related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Lexus RX350 is a reliable and well-made vehicle. However, it has been reported with some issues, primarily related to excessive oil consumption in the 3.5-liter V6 engine and electrical system malfunctions. These issues can be expensive to fix, but overall the RX350 is still a good choice for those seeking a comfortable and luxurious SUV.
