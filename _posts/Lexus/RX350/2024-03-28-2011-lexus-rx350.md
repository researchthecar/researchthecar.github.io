**2011 Lexus RX350**

The 2011 Lexus RX350 is a midsize luxury SUV that is available with a 3.5L V6 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable ride, a spacious interior, and a variety of features. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Oil consumption:** Some owners have reported that their vehicles are consuming excessive amounts of oil. The issue can be caused by a variety of factors, including worn piston rings or valve guides.
        * **Solution:** The solution to this issue is to replace the affected parts.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Some owners have experienced timing chain tensioner failures, which can cause the timing chain to skip or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
        * **Solution:** The solution to this issue is to replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
        * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 14V054000

**Transmission**

* **6-speed automatic**
    * **Shifting problems:** Some owners have reported experiencing shifting problems, such as delayed shifting or harsh shifts. The issue can be caused by a variety of factors, including a faulty transmission control module or a worn torque converter.
        * **Solution:** The solution to this issue is to replace the affected parts.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
