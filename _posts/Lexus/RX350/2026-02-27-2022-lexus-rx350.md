# 2022 Lexus RX350

### Overview

Built on Toyota's proven engineering, the RX 350 generally offers a dependable ownership experience with fewer unexpected repairs compared to many competitors. While specific model years and powertrains can have unique points of attention, the 2022 model benefits from years of refinement. ### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus RX 350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury SUV. Its powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, demonstrates exceptional durability with no significant widespread issues reported. The chassis and suspension components are also robust. Minor electrical concerns, primarily related to the infotainment system's software and rare instances of premature battery failure, are the most notable areas for potential attention, though these are generally minor and manageable. Overall, the RX 350 offers a dependable and low-stress ownership experience, aligning with Lexus's strong reputation for quality and longevity.
