# 2024 Lexus RX350

### Overview
The 2024 Lexus RX350, a popular luxury mid-size SUV, generally upholds Lexus's reputation for reliability and build quality. This model year benefits from a refined platform and powertrain options designed for a balance of performance and efficiency. While specific long-term data for the 2024 model is still emerging, historical trends and early owner feedback suggest a strong showing in most critical areas.
