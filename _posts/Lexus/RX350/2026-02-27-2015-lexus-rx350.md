# 2015 Lexus RX350

### Overview
The 2015 Lexus RX 350 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its comfortable ride, upscale interior, and strong reputation for reliability. It typically comes equipped with a robust 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. This generation of the RX series generally benefits from Lexus's meticulous engineering and quality control, leading to fewer unexpected mechanical problems compared to many competitors. Owners often praise its quiet cabin and user-friendly features, contributing to a positive ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 2015 RX 350 stands out for its consistent performance and durability, making it a solid choice for those seeking a dependable luxury SUV.

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing or unresponsiveness of the touchscreen infotainment system.
- **Solution:** Software update or system reset. In rare cases, replacement of the head unit may be required.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery failure or unexplained battery drain, sometimes linked to parasitic draw from modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain, potentially involving parasitic draw testing and module inspection. Battery replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Lexus RX 350 is a highly reliable vehicle, with its powertrain (engine and transmission) and core mechanical systems showing exceptional durability. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues. These are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the overall excellent reliability record. Exterior and interior components are also robust, reflecting Lexus's commitment to quality. For potential buyers, focusing on a thorough pre-purchase inspection, especially for the electrical components, is advisable, but overall, the 2015 RX 350 is a dependable choice.
