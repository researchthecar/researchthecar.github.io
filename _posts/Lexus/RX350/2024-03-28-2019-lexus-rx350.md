**Introduction:**

The 2019 Lexus RX350 is a mid-size luxury SUV praised for its comfortable interior, refined handling, and impressive fuel economy. It comes standard with a 3.5L V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, producing 295 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. The RX350 also offers multiple engine and transmission options, including the RX350L with a 3.5L V6 and 10-speed automatic transmission, and the RX450h hybrid with a 3.5L V6 and CVT.

Despite its overall reliability, the 2019 Lexus RX350 has experienced some reported issues.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Oil Consumption Issue**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
Solution: Piston ring and valve guide replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V695000

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
Solution: Transmission control module update or solenoid replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Failure of the air suspension system, resulting in uneven ride height and reduced handling.
Solution: Replacement of air struts or compressor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Ignition Coil Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, and reduced performance.
Solution: Replacement of ignition coils.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Misalignment**
Description/Symptoms: Misaligned body panels, such as doors and trunk lid, resulting in uneven gaps and reduced aesthetic appeal.
Solution: Adjustment or replacement of body panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Failure of the climate control system, resulting in inconsistent temperatures or malfunctioning controls.
Solution: Replacement or repair of climate control components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System Glitch**
Description/Symptoms: Freezing, lag, or incorrect display of information on the navigation system.
Solution: Software update or system replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Lexus RX350 has experienced some issues, including engine oil consumption, transmission shifting problems, suspension malfunctions, electrical issues, and interior climate control and navigation system glitches. While some of these issues can be costly to repair, they are relatively infrequent and do not significantly detract from the overall reliability of the vehicle. The RX350 remains a popular choice for those seeking a comfortable and sophisticated SUV.
