**Introduction:**

The 2009 Lexus RX350 is a luxury SUV offered with a single engine and transmission option: a 3.5-liter V6 paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It produces 270 horsepower and 254 lb-ft of torque. Drivers appreciate the model's reliability, comfort, and spacious interior. However, some common issues have been reported.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake manifold runner control:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold runner control may fail, causing reduced engine power and fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold runner control.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water pump:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water pump failure can lead to a loss of coolant, overheating, and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the water pump and related gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping occurs when the transmission cannot engage properly, resulting in loss of power and difficulty accelerating.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front lower control arms:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged front lower control arms can cause clunking and banging noises, as well as uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacement of the front lower control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Ignition coils:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty ignition coils can cause misfires, rough idle, and reduced engine power.
    * Solution: Replacement of the ignition coils.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof drain tubes:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clogged sunroof drain tubes can cause water to leak into the cabin.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of the sunroof drain tubes.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control system:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control systems can result in insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the climate control actuator or other related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2009 Lexus RX350 is a reliable and comfortable SUV, it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The frequency and severity of these issues vary, and the cost to fix them can be significant in some cases. However, these issues do not typically affect the overall reliability or safety of the vehicle.
