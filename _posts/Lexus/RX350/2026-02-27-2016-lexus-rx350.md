# 2016 Lexus RX350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus RX350 is widely recognized for its plush interior, refined ride quality, and strong luxury pedigree. While it generally maintains the brand's reputation for high build quality, used-car buyers should review specific mechanical and electrical pain points reported by owners, including potential engine gasket concerns, braking assist issues, and transmission shifting behavior.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces roughly 295 horsepower and provides smooth, strong acceleration suitable for a luxury crossover. It is generally dependable, though buyers should verify the health of gaskets and cooling components during pre-purchase inspections.

**Timing Cover Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks around the engine timing cover, potentially leading to low oil levels and expensive repairs.
- **Solution:** Inspect the timing cover for leaks and replace the gasket seals if compromised.
- **Cost:** ~$7,000

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to front-wheel or all-wheel-drive configurations under normal conditions. Some drivers experience occasional gear-hunting or abrupt engagement from a stop.

**Erratic Shifting and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle jerks during acceleration, hesitates, or searches for gears when starting from a stop.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates or consult a dealership to evaluate transmission control modules.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Vacuum Pump

Braking components provide solid stopping power under normal conditions, but prospective owners should inspect the vacuum pump and hydraulic system for signs of pressure loss.

**Loss of Power Brake Assist**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning messages regarding low braking power or a hard brake pedal requiring extra effort to stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the vacuum pump if failure is diagnosed.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Electronics

The electrical architecture supports numerous luxury features and driver assists, though sporadic infotainment screen glitches have been reported by owners.

**Dashboard Screen Discoloration and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The central electronic display turns pink then black, temporarily removing access to climate and navigation controls.
- **Solution:** Perform system resets or have the display unit and software inspected by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Door Handles and Glass

Exterior styling and body panels are durable, but minor mechanical wear items like interior handles and sunroof glass warrant careful evaluation.

**Interior Door Handle Inoperable**
- **Symptoms:** Inside door handles become loose or break, preventing occupants from opening doors from within.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged interior door handle mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V085000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag pressure sensors and/or the acceleration sensors may fail causing the side/curtain shield air bags and/or the front air bags to not deploy in the event of a crash. If the air bags fail to deploy in a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the sensors as necessary based on their serial numbers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V053000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A defective inflator may increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the knee air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V198000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are equipped with an ABS actuator that may have a damaged O-ring which could result in improper brake fluid pressure control during ABS, Traction Control, or Stability Control activation. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 126, "Electronic Stability Control.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the brake actuator, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus RX350 remains a compelling choice for used-luxury crossover buyers, offering a quiet cabin, comfortable ride, and strong resale value. While most vehicles provide trouble-free service, shoppers should thoroughly check for potential oil leaks around the timing cover, test the braking assist system, and verify proper transmission operation during a test drive.
