### Overview
The 2016 Lexus RX350 is generally recognized for its strong reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers of the 2016 model year.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus RX350 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its powertrain and core mechanical components demonstrating exceptional durability. The most commonly cited issues, though not widespread, tend to be related to the infotainment system's software and occasional electrical gremlins like battery drain. These are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the overall excellent reliability record. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience with a low probability of major mechanical failures, making it a sound investment for those prioritizing longevity and peace of mind.
