**About the 2022 Lexus RX350**

The 2022 Lexus RX350 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its comfortable interior, high-tech features, and impressive safety ratings. The RX350 comes in various trim levels that cater to different preferences and budgets.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Engine Misfires (G0K Engine Recall)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Sporadic engine misfires, rough idling, and hesitation while accelerating.
  * Solution: Replace affected engine components, reprogram engine control module
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Covered under recall
  * Recalls: G0K, S2C
* **Catalytic Converter Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illumination, reduced engine power, rotten egg smell from exhaust
  * Solution: Replace failed catalytic converter
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* **Oil Leaks (2GR-FXS Engine)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks from engine gaskets or seals
  * Solution: Replace or repair leaking gaskets/seals
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $700+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation or Jerking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitating or jerking during gear changes, especially during low-speed maneuvers.
  * Solution: Update transmission control module software or replace failing components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
* **Transmission Slipping**
  * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage or maintain gears, delayed gear changes, or loss of power under acceleration.
  * Solution: Overhaul or replace affected transmission components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Excessive Rear Sway Bar Noise**
  * Description/Symptoms: Noisy popping or creaking sounds from the rear suspension when driving over uneven surfaces.
  * Solution: Replace defective rear sway bar links
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Adaptive Variable Suspension Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride comfort, abnormal vehicle handling, and error messages on the instrument panel.
  * Solution: Replace faulty suspension components or update software
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery discharging prematurely, even overnight when parked.
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system issues, such as faulty wiring or parasitic battery draw.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Infotainment System Malfunctioning**
  * Description/Symptoms: System freezing, black screen, or other operational issues.
  * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacements
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Exterior Trim Peeling**
  * Description/Symptoms: Decorative exterior trim pieces (e.g., chrome accents) peeling or flaking off.
  * Solution: Replace or refinish affected trim pieces
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle (Panoramic Sunroof Option)**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise or rattling from the panoramic sunroof when closed.
  * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
* **Ventilated Seat Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: Ventilated seats not providing sufficient or consistent airflow.
  * Solution: Replace or repair faulty seat fans or control module
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary**

The 2022 Lexus RX350 faces several issues that range in severity and cost. While some issues are covered under recall or can be resolved with relatively minor repairs, others, such as transmission problems or electrical system malfunctions, can be more expensive and time-consuming to fix. Overall, it's essential to consider these potential issues before purchasing or owning a 2022 Lexus RX350 and to perform regular maintenance to help prevent or mitigate them.
