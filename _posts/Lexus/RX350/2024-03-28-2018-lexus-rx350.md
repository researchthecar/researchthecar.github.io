## 2018 Lexus RX350

The 2018 Lexus RX350 is a popular midsize luxury SUV known for its reliability, comfort, and high-end features. It comes with a single engine and transmission option, a 3.5L V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It produces 295 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, offering a smooth and powerful driving experience. The vehicle has received positive feedback for its luxurious interior, spacious cabin, and advanced safety features, but some owners have reported various issues.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6 Engine**

    * **Oil Leak from Valve Cover Gasket:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, causing oil to drip onto the engine and potentially leading to engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Rough Shifting:** The transmission may shift roughly or hesitate during gear changes.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
        * Solution: Replace the battery or check for electrical system issues.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction:** The headlights may flicker or fail to turn on.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly or check for electrical issues.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:** Water may leak through the sunroof during heavy rain.
        * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Trim Panel Peeling:** The exterior trim panels may peel or become loose.
        * Solution: Replace or reattach the trim panels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system may experience occasional glitches or freezing.
        * Solution: Update the system software.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may not blow air or may not maintain the desired temperature.
        * Solution: Check for electrical issues or replace the climate control unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Lexus RX350 is a reliable and luxurious SUV, but it has experienced some issues. The most common issues include oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, rough shifting in the transmission, and electrical problems such as battery drain and headlight malfunctions. The severity of the issues is generally moderate, and the cost to fix them ranges from minor to moderate.
