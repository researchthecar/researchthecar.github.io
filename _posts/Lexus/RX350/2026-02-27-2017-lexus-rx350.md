# 2017 Lexus RX350

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus RX350 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its comfortable ride, upscale interior, and strong reputation for reliability. Built on Toyota's robust engineering principles, the RX350 generally offers a dependable ownership experience. This model year features a potent 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, providing a smooth and responsive powertrain. While generally well-regarded, like any vehicle, there can be isolated reports of specific component concerns. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of potential reliability concerns for prospective owners.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus RX350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury SUV. Its powertrain, consisting of the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment system glitches and intermittent battery drain issues. These are typically addressable through software updates or targeted repairs. Overall, the RX350 offers a dependable and low-maintenance ownership experience, making it a strong contender in its segment for those prioritizing reliability.
