# 2017 Lexus RX350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus RX 350 brings a strong reputation for luxury, comfort, and long-term durability to the midsize crossover market. Most owners praise its ride quality and overall refinement, but prospective buyers should still review specific complaint areas reported to safety authorities. Paying attention to known powertrain characteristics, brake system components, and occasional electrical or body accessory glitches can help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 295 horsepower and 268 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and responsive acceleration. It is widely considered a highly durable powerplant, though minor electronic and fuel-related complaints surface occasionally.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine hesitation or stalling while operating at highway speeds or in traffic.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery and electronic throttle control components; update engine control module software if applicable.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. While generally robust, some drivers experience notable shift delays or adaptive learning quirks at lower speeds.

**Shift Hesitation and Sputtering**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission hesitation, jerky throttle response, or tachometer fluctuations during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Check for transmission control module updates or adaptative learning resets at a certified service center.

## Brakes

### Brake Actuator and Booster

The braking system generally provides confident stopping power, but the hydraulic actuator assembly requires vigilance for unusual noises and pressure issues.

**Brake Actuator Noise and Pedal Feel**
- **Symptoms:** An abnormal squawk or bark noise accompanied by unusual brake pedal firmness or pressure loss during application.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hydraulic brake booster and actuator assembly; replace faulty components under applicable manufacturer support programs.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Seat Heaters

Cabin electronics generally function well, though screen blackouts and climate-controlled seat inconsistencies are documented in isolated owner complaints.

**Infotainment Blank Screen and Seat Heater Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment display freezing or failing to show anything other than backup camera feeds, or seat heaters becoming excessively hot or slow to warm.
- **Solution:** Reset or update infotainment firmware, and inspect seat heating elements and control switches.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body panels and trim hold up well over time, with the rare exception of spontaneous glass breakage on equipped sunroof assemblies.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass unexpectedly fractures or explodes while driving under normal road conditions without direct impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the sunroof frame alignment and gasket seals.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance protection but can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking activations due to environmental factors.

**Unexpected Braking or Sensor Fogging**
- **Symptoms:** Automatic emergency braking activating unexpectedly, or lane departure camera visibility issues due to internal windshield fogging.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas, inspect windshield mounting seals, and recalibrate advanced driver assistance systems if needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V012000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus RX 350 remains a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking luxury, comfort, and proven mechanical durability. While owners have submitted complaints regarding brake actuator noises, transmission shift quirks, and minor electronics or sunroof issues, the vehicle's overall track record is solid. A thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes, electronic accessories, and service history will help ensure you find a reliable and rewarding example.
