# 2018 Lexus RX350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus RX 350 continues the model's strong reputation for luxury, comfort, and general reliability. While it remains a popular and solid choice on the used market, owners have reported a handful of recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that prospective buyers should keep on their radar during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (295 hp)

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine generating approximately 295 horsepower, this powertrain offers smooth acceleration and refined performance. It generally exhibits high mechanical durability, though occasional fuel system and sensor issues have been documented.

**Fuel Pump Failure and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience engine stalling or rough running due to fuel pump sensor electrical shorts and assembly failure.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump assembly, suction tube set, and level sensors.

**Engine Misfire and Fuel Odor**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine lights illuminate, accompanied by cylinder misfires or a gasoline odor entering the vehicle cabin.
- **Solution:** Diagnose misfire codes, inspect O2/gasoline ratio levels, and repair or replace faulty fuel components or ignition coils.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels or available all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust and lacks widespread mechanical failure trends.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Actuator

Braking components provide strong stopping power, but owners should listen closely for abnormal squeaks or actuator noises during a test drive.

**Brake Squawk Noise and Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** An annoying 'squawk' or barking noise occurs when pressing the brake pedal, and faulty actuators can occasionally cause braking performance concerns.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake assembly and replace the brake actuator if internal component failure is confirmed.

## Steering

### Steering and Suspension Joints

Steering precision is well-regarded for a midsize luxury crossover, though front suspension wear items should be checked during routine maintenance.

**Leaking Ball Joints and Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Premature grease leaks from front ball joints or sway bar links, potentially leading to loose steering feel or suspension clunks.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension boots and replace worn ball joints or sway bar links as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical and HVAC Systems

Onboard electronics offer a high degree of luxury and convenience, but complex electrical accessories can occasionally experience component wear or sensor faults.

**HVAC Servo Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Climate control air distribution or temperature regulation fails due to a malfunctioning HVAC blend door servo motor.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed HVAC servo motor assembly.

## Exterior

### Body and Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim hold up well over time, though minor mechanical snags involving door latches and panoramic sunroof shades have been noted by owners.

**Interior Door Handle and Sunshade Jams**
- **Symptoms:** Interior door handles may become difficult to pull or fail to unlatch the door, and panoramic sunroof sunshades can bunch up when retracting.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace broken interior door handle mechanisms or binding sunshade track components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V012000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus RX 350 maintains a strong reputation for overall dependability and comfort, making it a compelling option in the used luxury SUV segment. Buyers should perform a thorough pre-inspection focusing on the brakes, fuel system components, and interior mechanical features like door handles and sunroof operation to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
