# 2009 Lexus RX350

### Overview
The 2009 Lexus RX350 is generally regarded as a reliable luxury crossover SUV. It benefits from Toyota's reputation for building durable vehicles, and the RX350 typically exhibits strong performance with fewer unexpected mechanical problems compared to many competitors in its class. Owners often praise its comfortable ride, quiet cabin, and upscale interior. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the RX350's common problems tend to be minor and manageable, contributing to its positive long-term ownership experience. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its continued dependability.

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Power Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Windows may become slow to operate or stop working altogether.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per window

**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or incorrect map data.
- **Solution:** Software update or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Lexus RX350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury SUV. Its powertrain, including the robust 3.5L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission, demonstrates excellent durability with no widespread reported issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically power window motors and occasional navigation system glitches, which are typically repairable. Interior components also hold up well. Overall, the RX350 offers a dependable ownership experience, making it a solid choice for those seeking a comfortable and reliable vehicle.
