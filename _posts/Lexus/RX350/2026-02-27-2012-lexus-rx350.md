# 2012 Lexus RX350

### Overview
The 2012 Lexus RX350 is a luxury mid-size SUV known for its comfortable ride, refined interior, and strong reputation for reliability. It typically came equipped with a robust 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. While generally a dependable vehicle, like any car, it can experience specific issues as it ages. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The RX350's build quality and Lexus's commitment to quality control contribute significantly to its overall positive reliability record.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FE)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Timing Cover)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or timing cover seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, whining noise from the front of the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump and potentially thermostat.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Touchscreen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Unresponsive touch screen, freezing, or erratic behavior.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find parasitic draw, often related to modules or accessories.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Lexus RX350 is a highly reliable luxury SUV. The primary areas that may require attention are potential oil leaks from the valve covers or timing cover and occasional water pump failures, which are not uncommon for vehicles of this age and mileage. Electrical issues, particularly with the infotainment system's touchscreen and intermittent battery drain, are also documented. However, these are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall strong dependability. The transmission and suspension systems are typically robust. With regular maintenance, the RX350 should continue to provide a comfortable and trouble-free ownership experience.
