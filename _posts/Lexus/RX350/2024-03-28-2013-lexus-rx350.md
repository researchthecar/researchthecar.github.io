**Introduction:**

The 2013 Lexus RX350 is a popular luxury SUV that offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. With 270 horsepower and 244 lb-ft of torque, the RX350 provides ample power for daily driving.

**Engine:**

**3.5L V6 VVT-i**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idle, hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Clean intake valves and/or injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaks from the transmission pan or seals
    * Solution: Replace leaking seals or pan
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Sway Bar Links:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises over bumps, reduced stability
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Navigation System Display Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation screen becomes unresponsive or blank
    * Solution: Replace or repair navigation system display
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V491000

**Exterior:**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace or reseal headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 13V111000

**Interior:**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the sunroof when it is open or closed
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof tracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Seat Entertainment System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: System does not turn on, screen is blank, or DVD player malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace or repair rear seat entertainment system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Lexus RX350 has some common issues, including intake valve carbon buildup, transmission fluid leaks, and sunroof rattles. Some electrical and interior components may also experience malfunctions. These issues can range in severity and cost of repair, but most can be addressed for a reasonable price. Overall, the RX350 remains a reliable and well-rounded luxury SUV.
