# 2020 Lexus RX350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lexus RX 350 continues the model's tradition of offering a luxurious cabin, a smooth ride, and strong brand reliability. As prospective buyers shop the used market, understanding the powertrain specifications and any reported owner concerns helps ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (295 hp, 267 lb-ft)

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 producing approximately 295 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. This engine option is generally well-regarded for its longevity and smooth power delivery in daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission available in front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. It provides refined gear shifts that complement the V6 engine's smooth power band.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus RX 350 is a solid choice for used car buyers seeking luxury and dependability. Thoroughly inspect any vehicle of interest and review its service history before finalizing your purchase.
