# 2014 Lexus RX350

### Overview
The 2014 Lexus RX350 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its comfortable ride, refined interior, and strong reputation for reliability. It typically comes equipped with a robust 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. This generation of the RX350 generally exhibits excellent build quality and durability, making it a popular choice for those seeking a dependable luxury vehicle. While most owners report a trouble-free experience, like any vehicle, there are a few areas that have seen occasional reports of issues, though these are not widespread.

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Lexus RX350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury SUV. Its core mechanical components, including the engine and transmission, are exceptionally robust and rarely present significant issues. The most commonly reported, though still infrequent, problems tend to be minor electrical glitches with the infotainment system or, in some cases, premature battery failure. The climate control system's blend door actuator is another component that has seen occasional reports of failure. Overall, the RX350 offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal major mechanical concerns, making it a solid choice for a pre-owned luxury SUV.
