# 2021 Lexus RX350

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus RX 350 is a mid-size luxury SUV renowned for its comfortable ride, upscale interior, and strong reputation for reliability. Built on Toyota's proven engineering, the RX 350 generally offers a dependable ownership experience with fewer unexpected repairs compared to many competitors in its class. This model year benefits from Lexus's meticulous attention to detail in manufacturing and quality control, contributing to its high owner satisfaction ratings. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the RX 350's design and componentry are typically robust, focusing on longevity and driver comfort. Owners can expect a refined driving experience with a focus on minimizing common automotive problems.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus RX 350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury SUV. Its powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, demonstrates exceptional durability with no significant widespread issues reported. The suspension and exterior components are also generally robust. Minor electrical concerns, such as occasional infotainment glitches or rare instances of premature battery failure, are the most commonly cited, though these are not pervasive. Overall, the RX 350 offers a dependable and low-stress ownership experience, aligning with Lexus's reputation for building vehicles that last.
