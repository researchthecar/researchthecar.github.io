# 2021 Lexus RX350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus RX350 continues the brand's long-standing tradition of comfort, luxury, and dependable daily motoring. As a used luxury midsize crossover, it appeals to buyers seeking a refined ride quality, a quiet cabin, and strong predicted longevity. While early ownership feedback highlights very few systemic mechanical flaws, prospective buyers should always evaluate individual service histories and test drive thoroughly to ensure all features operate as intended.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

This 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces approximately 295 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as a smooth, robust, and mechanically durable powerplant well-suited for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the front wheels or the available all-wheel-drive system. It generally delivers seamless gear changes with minimal reported mechanical complications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V043000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly:Filler Pipe And Cap](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The transport hook may not have been removed prior to sale. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 301, "Fuel System Integrity." During a rear-end crash, the transport hook can damage the fuel filler pipe, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Lexus will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, as necessary, remove the rear transport hook, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus RX350 remains a solid and reassuring choice in the used luxury crossover segment. With a proven powertrain and minimal widespread complaint trends, it offers strong peace of mind for prospective buyers. Conducting a standard pre-purchase inspection will help ensure the vehicle has been well-maintained by previous owners.
