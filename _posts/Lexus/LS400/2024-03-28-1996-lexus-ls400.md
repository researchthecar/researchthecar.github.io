**Introduction:**

The 1996 Lexus LS400 is a luxury sedan known for its exceptional comfort, performance, and reliability. It features a 4.0-liter V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission, delivering 290 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. The LS400 is praised for its smooth ride, spacious cabin, and advanced technology for its time. However, certain issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8**

* **Exhaust Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive noise from the engine bay, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Valve Body Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, slipping gears, transmission overheating
    * Solution: Replace valve body or repair malfunctioning components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushings Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing or clunking noises while turning or braking, excessive tire wear
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dashboard gauges not working properly
    * Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic drain and repair/replace failing components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the trunk
    * Solution: Replace trunk lid seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window not moving or moving slowly
    * Solution: Replace power window motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from vents
    * Solution: Replace HVAC blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 1996 Lexus LS400 is generally a reliable vehicle, certain issues can arise. Engine issues, such as exhaust manifold gasket failure, are relatively common and potentially costly to repair. Transmission problems, like valve body wear, can also impact driving performance. Other issues, such as electrical problems and interior component failures, are less severe and generally more affordable to fix. Overall, the cost of repairs will vary depending on the severity of the issue and the specific components involved.
