# 2015 Lexus LS600h

### Overview
The 2015 Lexus LS600h is a flagship luxury sedan renowned for its sophisticated hybrid powertrain and opulent interior. As a Lexus, it generally upholds a strong reputation for reliability, benefiting from Toyota's meticulous engineering and quality control. The LS600h combines a powerful V8 engine with an advanced electric motor system, offering a blend of performance and fuel efficiency. While the hybrid system adds complexity, Lexus has a proven track record with its hybrid technology. Owners typically experience a high level of satisfaction with the vehicle's build quality and long-term durability, though like any complex vehicle, specific components can occasionally require attention.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Variable Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light illuminated, audible hissing from suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Lexus LS600h is a highly reliable luxury sedan, particularly noted for its robust hybrid powertrain and overall build quality. While most systems perform exceptionally well with no significant reported issues, the adaptive variable air suspension is the most common area for potential concern, with occasional reports of air spring leaks or compressor failures. These are generally repairable with moderate cost. The electrical system, including the complex hybrid battery, has proven very durable. Overall, the LS600h offers a dependable ownership experience, aligning with Lexus's reputation for excellence in the luxury segment.
