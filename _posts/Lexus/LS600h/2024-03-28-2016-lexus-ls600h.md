**Introduction:**

The 2016 Lexus LS600h is a full-size luxury sedan known for its refined interior, quiet ride, and advanced technology. It comes with a 5.0-liter V8 engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 445 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. The LS600h is praised for its exceptional craftsmanship, spacious cabin, and smooth power delivery, but some owners report experiencing specific issues.

**Engine**

**5.0L V8 Hybrid**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, and reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replacing the intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Hybrid Battery Degradation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced battery capacity, impaired acceleration, and increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replacing the hybrid battery or individual battery modules
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **CVT Belt Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, jerking, or shuddering during acceleration
    * Solution: Replacing the CVT belt and pulleys
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Air Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Front end of the vehicle drooping, reduced ride height
    * Solution: Replacing the air suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, incorrect navigation information
    * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Ballast Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both headlights dim or flickering
    * Solution: Replacing the headlight ballast
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty unlocking or locking doors, door locks not responding
    * Solution: Replacing the door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Panel Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control settings not responding, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Replacing the climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Lexus LS600h presents a blend of luxury and performance. While some owners have experienced issues with its hybrid system, transmission, and suspension, the severity and cost of these problems vary. Potential buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle before purchase and consider the potential costs associated with these known issues.
