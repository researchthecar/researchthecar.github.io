# 2023 Lexus NX250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Lexus NX 250 represents a modern entry into the luxury compact SUV segment, bringing advanced technology, refined styling, and the brand's legendary build quality. As a relatively new model in the lineup, prospective used-car buyers can generally expect high levels of dependability and solid engineering. While owner reports to regulatory bodies are minimal, understanding its powertrain options and baseline characteristics helps ensure a confident purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Inline-4 Engine

This naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 203 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It prioritizes smooth power delivery and fuel efficiency, offering a dependable, straightforward mechanical baseline for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 8-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the four-cylinder engine, managing power delivery across front-wheel or all-wheel-drive configurations. It is generally regarded as a durable, refined unit requiring routine maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V480000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX250&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A deactivated driver's air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the spiral cable assembly, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
