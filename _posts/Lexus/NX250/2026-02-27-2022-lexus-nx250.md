# 2022 Lexus NX250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Lexus NX 250, buyers will find a stylish and technologically advanced compact luxury crossover that builds on Toyota's reputation for dependable engineering. While owner reports to federal safety regulators point out a few early-production quirks—ranging from infotainment glitches to physical fit issues—the vehicle generally maintains the high build quality expected from the brand. Prospective owners should review vehicle history records and inspect key systems during a test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 275 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for everyday driving. It is generally regarded as a robust and dependable powerplant for buyers seeking balanced performance.

### 2.5L Naturally Aspirated Inline-4

The 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine generates 203 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing smooth power delivery and fuel efficiency. It serves as a reliable, low-stress option for daily commuting.

**Fuel Door Alignment**
- **Symptoms:** The external fuel door may become visibly misaligned and fail to open properly when releasing the latch.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership or service technician inspect the latch mechanism and adjust or replace the mounting assembly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This conventional 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options to provide smooth, prompt gear shifts across various driving conditions. It is a traditional geared automatic known for longevity when properly maintained.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Software

Issues related to interior electronics, software updates, and accessory power management reported by vehicle owners.

**Infotainment Connectivity and Display Freezes**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen infotainment system may fail to connect to smartphones or Apple CarPlay, or display error screens that block vehicle information.
- **Solution:** Restart the infotainment display or power-cycle the vehicle; software updates performed at a dealership may help resolve recurring glitches.

## Exterior

### Side Mirrors

Exterior body components, glass, doors, and visibility aids.

**Excessive Mirror Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's side mirror may shake or vibrate excessively while driving, causing a blurry reflection and potential driver discomfort.
- **Solution:** Have the mirror assembly inspected by a technician to ensure the internal mounting bracket is securely fastened.

## Interior

### Driver Seat

Interior cabin amenities, seating comfort, and driver ergonomics.

**Crooked Seat Alignment**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's seat may feel misaligned or crooked relative to the dashboard and steering wheel, despite being securely bolted.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to verify seat track installation and check for warranty coverage on interior adjustments.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V238000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX250&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Missing spot-welds between the body panels and the front shock absorber mounting areas may cause the front shock absorber to separate from the mounting area. A separated shock absorber can cause a loss of vehicle stability, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will add welds to body panels or replace the body panels where welds have failed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V661000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX250&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." An electronic parking brake that cannot be engaged while in "park," may result in a vehicle rollaway, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the Skid Control ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus NX 250 is a refined luxury crossover that generally delivers strong reliability and comfort. While early owners have reported isolated concerns regarding infotainment responsiveness, mirror vibration, and minor body fitment, these issues do not point to widespread mechanical failures. Used car buyers should perform a thorough test drive to check electronics, mirrors, and seating comfort before finalizing a purchase.
