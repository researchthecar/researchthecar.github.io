# 2024 Lexus NX250

### Overview
The 2024 Lexus NX250, a compact luxury SUV, is built upon Toyota's renowned reputation for reliability. It typically offers a smooth ride, a well-appointed interior, and a suite of advanced safety features. The NX250 is powered by a naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, designed for efficiency and everyday drivability. While specific long-term data for the 2024 model year is still emerging, the underlying platform and powertrain components have a history of dependability within the Lexus and Toyota lineups. This model aims to provide a balanced blend of luxury, practicality, and peace of mind for its segment.
