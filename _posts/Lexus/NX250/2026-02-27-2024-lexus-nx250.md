# 2024 Lexus NX250 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus NX250 serves as the entry point into the brand's luxury compact crossover lineup, offering a refined ride and a reputation for solid build quality. Because it is a relatively recent model year, the volume of owner-reported complaints is minimal, suggesting that early-adopter buyers are largely experiencing trouble-free driving. As with any modern luxury vehicle equipped with complex electronics and direct-injection mechanics, prospective used-car buyers should still verify maintenance histories and thoroughly test-drive all onboard systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 203 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth, predictable power delivery for daily commuting and maintains a strong reputation for long-term mechanical durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

An eight-speed automatic transmission routes power to either the front wheels or all four wheels depending on the chosen drivetrain. It is generally praised for crisp gear selections and efficient highway cruising behavior.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V480000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX250&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A deactivated driver's air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the spiral cable assembly, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Lexus NX250 presents a compelling option in the used luxury crossover market, backed by strong powertrain credentials and a lack of widespread owner complaints. Used-car shoppers can approach this model with confidence, provided they ensure a standard pre-purchase inspection confirms proper mechanical and electronic health.
