# 2022 Lexus IS500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lexus IS 500 combines luxury performance with the brand's renowned reputation for reliability, anchored by a naturally aspirated V8 engine. As a relatively low-volume performance sedan, it attracts driving enthusiasts looking for traditional power without widespread mechanical plagues. Prospective used buyers should still carefully inspect individual vehicles for overall condition, maintenance history, and any signs of aggressive driving use.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

Equipped with a 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 producing around 472 horsepower and 395 lb-ft of torque. This robust powerplant delivers thrilling performance with strong long-term durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission paired exclusively with the rear-wheel-drive layout. It offers crisp, responsive shifts suited for spirited driving while maintaining smooth everyday cruising.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=IS&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus IS 500 stands out as a solid used luxury performance purchase, offering a blend of old-school V8 performance and modern reliability. Because widespread recurring mechanical complaints are minimal, buyers should focus on standard pre-purchase inspections, verifying service records, and checking for wear and tear from previous ownership.
