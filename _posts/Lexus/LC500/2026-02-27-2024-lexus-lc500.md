# 2024 Lexus LC500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus LC 500 stands as a striking flagbearer for naturally aspirated luxury performance, blending grand touring comfort with exotic styling. Because used-car buyers are primarily investing in its high-end mechanical pedigree, understanding its powertrain options and any early owner-reported feedback is essential. Overall, the LC 500 maintains a reputation for high build quality, though prospective buyers should still review service history carefully.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces approximately 471 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its linear power delivery and exotic soundtrack, serving as a robust and reliable core for the grand tourer.

### 3.5L V6 MultiStage Hybrid

The 3.5-liter V6 hybrid powertrain pairs with a multi-stage transmission to deliver a combined 354 horsepower. This setup offers refined efficiency for long-distance cruising without sacrificing Lexus's standard of long-term mechanical dependability.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Direct-Shift Automatic

Paired exclusively with the V8 engine, this 10-speed automatic provides rapid shifts and seamless highway cruising. It is generally robust, matching the high-revving character of the flagship coupe and convertible.

### Multi-Stage Hybrid Transmission

Coupled with the V6 hybrid system, this innovative transmission simulates gear changes to mimic a traditional stepped automatic. It offers smooth operation and dependable long-term performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V124000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to incorrect programming of the parking-assist electronic control unit (ECU) software, the rearview camera image may not immediately display within a couple of seconds after the shifter is placed into reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
