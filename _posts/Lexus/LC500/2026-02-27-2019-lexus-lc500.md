# 2019 Lexus LC500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus LC500 is a striking luxury grand tourer known for its exceptional build quality, striking design, and naturally aspirated V8 performance. While owner-reported complaints are sparse given its low production volume and high standard of engineering, buyers should still evaluate powertrain options and general electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 (2UR-GSE)

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces 471 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque, offering robust performance and high-revving character. It is generally very reliable, with no widespread recurring mechanical failures reported by owners.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Direct Shift Automatic

This 10-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts tailored for both relaxed cruising and spirited driving. It pairs well with the V8 engine and maintains a strong reputation for long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V544000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Deactivation of the vehicle stability control system or a loss of braking assist increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the brake booster pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V012000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus LC500 stands out as a remarkably solid and dependable luxury grand tourer with virtually no widespread mechanical flaws in current owner feedback. Prospective buyers should perform a standard comprehensive inspection of the electronics, tires, and mechanical systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
