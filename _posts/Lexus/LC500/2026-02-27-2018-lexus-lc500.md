# 2018 Lexus LC500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus LC500 is a flagship luxury grand tourer known for its striking design and exceptional build quality. As a high-end luxury vehicle, it combines performance and comfort, though prospective buyers should still review owner-reported feedback regarding minor body hardware concerns.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

Powered by a naturally aspirated 5.0-liter V8 engine producing around 471 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain is renowned for its robust performance, smooth delivery, and strong overall reliability.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Direct Shift Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive 10-speed automatic transmission featuring rear-wheel drive. It offers crisp gear shifts and generally displays high reliability in everyday driving conditions.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Actuators

Exterior body hardware is generally durable, though isolated owner complaints note potential water intrusion leading to internal door lock actuator corrosion.

**Door Latch Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that internal door handles fail to open the doors from either the inside or outside of the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty door latch actuator assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V012000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V107000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LC500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A cracked cover may leak fuel, which, in the presence of an ignition source, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus LC500 offers an impressive blend of exotic styling and grand touring comfort backed by solid mechanical engineering. While owners have reported occasional issues with door latches, the core powertrain remains exceptionally reliable. Buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection of all body hardware and electronics.
