# 2021 Lexus LC500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus LC500 is a flagship luxury grand tourer known for its striking design and exceptional craftsmanship. As a low-volume, high-end performance coupe and convertible, it generally maintains a strong reputation for overall build quality and mechanical refinement among used luxury car buyers.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 produces 471 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque, delivering thrilling performance and a signature exhaust note. It is generally regarded as a robust and mechanically stout powerplant with minimal widespread reliability complaints.

**Fuel/Propulsion System Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experienced a sudden shuttering sensation and nearly stalled when pulling away from a stationary position.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the fuel delivery system, throttle body, and engine control modules for transient faults or software updates.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

The 3.5-liter V6 multi-stage hybrid system pairs dual electric motors with a V6 engine to generate a combined 354 horsepower. This powertrain offers high-end efficiency and smooth power delivery suited for grand touring.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Direct Shift Automatic

The 10-speed automatic transmission features quick, paddle-shifted gear changes tailored for both relaxed cruising and aggressive driving. It is broadly considered dependable in daily use.

### Multi-Stage Hybrid Continuously Variable Transmission

The multi-stage hybrid transmission integrates a planetary gear set with a traditional automatic mechanism to simulate stepped ratios. It provides seamless acceleration transitions for the hybrid variant.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus LC500 stands out as an exceptionally well-built luxury grand tourer with very few reported complaints from owners. Prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and electronic health, but the vehicle remains a compelling and generally dependable choice in the used luxury segment.
