# 2008 Lexus RX400h

### Overview
The 2008 Lexus RX400h is a luxury hybrid SUV known for its blend of performance, fuel efficiency, and comfort. As a hybrid, it features a sophisticated powertrain combining a gasoline engine with electric motors. This generation of the RX series generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. Owners often praise its quiet ride, upscale interior, and advanced features for its time. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can experience issues over time, particularly as the vehicle ages. This analysis focuses on documented, non-routine reliability concerns.

---


## Engine

### 3.3L V6 Hybrid (2GR-FXE)
**Inverter Cooling Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating of the hybrid system, warning lights on the dashboard, reduced hybrid system performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the inverter cooling pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Hybrid Battery Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric-only driving range, decreased overall fuel economy, potential for hybrid system warning lights.
- **Solution:** Hybrid battery pack replacement (often a costly repair).
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $6,000

## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Key Fob Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Key fob stops working unexpectedly, requires frequent battery replacement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of key fob battery, potential reprogramming if issues persist.
- **Cost:** $10 - $50

**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, incorrect map data.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the navigation unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Lexus RX400h is a highly reliable luxury hybrid SUV. The powertrain, while complex, is generally robust, with the most common significant issues being related to the hybrid inverter cooling pump and eventual hybrid battery degradation, which are expected for a vehicle of this age and technology. Electrical components like key fobs and navigation systems can occasionally present minor glitches. Overall, the RX400h offers a dependable ownership experience with fewer widespread mechanical or electrical failures compared to many of its contemporaries. Regular maintenance and addressing any hybrid system warnings promptly are key to long-term reliability.
