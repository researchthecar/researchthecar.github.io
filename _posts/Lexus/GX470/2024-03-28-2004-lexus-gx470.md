**Introduction:**

The 2004 Lexus GX470 is a midsize luxury SUV known for its off-road capabilities and spacious interior. It comes with a single engine and transmission option:

* Engine: 4.7L V8
* Transmission: 5-speed automatic

**Engine**

* **4.7L V8**

**Issues:**

* **Water pump failure:** Leaks coolant and may lead to overheating.
    * Solution: Replace water pump and related parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Timing belt failure:** Can cause engine damage if not replaced on schedule.
    * Solution: Replace timing belt and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **5-speed automatic:**

**Issues:**

* **Shifting issues:** May experience slipping or hard shifts.
    * Solution: Flush transmission fluid and replace filter, or rebuild transmission if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

**Issues:**

* **Air suspension compressor failure:** Can cause suspension to fail, leading to a rough ride.
    * Solution: Replace air suspension compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Rear shocks leaking:** Can cause the rear end to sag and compromise handling.
    * Solution: Replace rear shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

**Issues:**

* **Electrical short:** Can cause various electrical issues, such as battery drain or component failure.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair short circuit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Radio display issues:** May experience fading or intermittent display.
    * Solution: Replace radio display.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

**Issues:**

* **Sunroof drain plugs clogging:** Can lead to water leakage into the interior.
    * Solution: Clear or replace drain plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

**Issues:**

* **Climate control issues:** May not regulate temperature effectively.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Navigation system glitches:** May experience errors or display issues.
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2004 Lexus GX470 is a reliable SUV with some potential issues. Engine and transmission issues can be severe and expensive, but most other issues are relatively minor and can be addressed at a reasonable cost.
