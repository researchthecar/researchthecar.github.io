**Introduction**

The 2014 Lexus IS F is a high-performance sedan powered by a naturally aspirated 5.0-liter V8 engine producing 416 horsepower and 371 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It is known for its impressive handling, powerful engine, and luxurious interior. However, like any vehicle, it has some reported issues worth noting.

**Engine**

**5.0-Liter V8**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups or even engine overhauls.
    * Description/Symptoms: Low engine oil levels, blue smoke from the exhaust, engine knocking
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Rough Shifting:** Some owners have experienced rough or delayed gear shifts, especially during high-performance driving.
    * Description/Symptoms: Sudden or jerky shifts, delayed downshifts
    * Solution: Software updates, solenoid replacement, or transmission overhaul
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported parasitic battery drains, leading to dead batteries.
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, flickering lights, dead battery
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing electrical faults, alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Issues:** Some owners have experienced problems with the headlights, including flickering or premature bulb failure.
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, blown bulbs
    * Solution: Bulb replacement, headlight assembly repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:** Some owners have reported the door handles becoming stiff or inoperable.
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening the doors
    * Solution: Replacement of the door handle assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Issues:** Some owners have encountered problems with the navigation system, including freezing or inaccurate directions.
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative navigation, incorrect routing
    * Solution: Software updates, GPS antenna replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** Some owners have experienced issues with the climate control system, such as inconsistent temperature regulation or fan issues.
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven cabin temperature, inoperative fan
    * Solution: Control module replacement, blower motor repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Lexus IS F is generally a reliable and well-built vehicle; however, it has some reported issues that owners should be aware of. The most significant concern is the excessive oil consumption, which can be expensive to repair. The transmission and electrical systems have also experienced some problems, but they are generally less severe and costly to fix. Overall, the potential issues with the IS F should be considered when making a purchase decision.
