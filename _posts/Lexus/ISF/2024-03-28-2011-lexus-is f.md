**Introduction**

The 2011 Lexus IS F is a high-performance luxury sports sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience with its powerful engine and sharp handling. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 5.0-liter V8 engine that produces 416 horsepower and 371 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The IS F is praised for its potent engine, responsive steering, and luxurious interior, but it is also criticized for its firm ride and limited rear-seat space.

**Engine**

* **5.0-liter V8**

    * **Injector Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Failure of fuel injectors to deliver proper fuel flow, leading to hesitation, misfiring, and engine stalling.
    Solution: Replace faulty injectors.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    Recalls: None Found
    
    * **Intake Manifold Leak**
    Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leak from a damaged intake manifold gasket, causing rough idling, decreased power, and increased fuel consumption.
    Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    Recalls: None Found
    
**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

    * **Shifting Problems**
    Description/Symptoms: Hesitation, slipping, or hard shifting during gear changes.
    Solution: Replace or repair faulty solenoids or transmission fluid flush.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Failure of the alternator to generate sufficient power, leading to electrical system issues, dimming headlights, and battery drain.
Solution: Replace alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Clear Coat Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced clarity, peeling, or discoloration of the protective clear coat finish, especially on hoods and roofs.
Solution: Repaint or touch up affected areas.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Trim Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the plastic trim material on the dashboard, usually around air vents or gauges.
Solution: Replace dashboard trim or touch up with paint.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Door Latch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning door latches that prevent doors from opening or closing properly.
Solution: Replace faulty latches.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Lexus IS F is a reliable vehicle, but it has some known issues that owners should be aware of, particularly with the engine and electrical systems. While some repairs can be costly, the overall reliability and driving experience of the IS F make it a desirable choice for enthusiasts.
