The 2020 Lexus IS300 is a luxury sports sedan known for its comfortable ride, stylish interior, and reputation for reliability. The base model IS300 comes equipped with a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission in the RWD configuration. A 3.5L V6 engine producing 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque is also available in the IS300 AWD trim, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The IS350 trim offers a more powerful 3.5L V6 producing 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque paired with the 8-speed automatic in RWD or the 6-speed automatic in AWD. Some owners appreciate the Lexus IS300's smooth ride and luxurious features, while others feel the engine is underpowered, especially in the base model, and the infotainment system feels dated.
