# 2018 Lexus IS300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus IS300 brings luxury, rear-wheel or all-wheel drive performance, and the brand's legendary build quality to the compact executive sedan market. While owner feedback is generally sparse, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and inspect key areas like the sunroof and powertrain responsiveness during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (241 hp / 258 lb-ft)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering rear-wheel-drive variants. It offers a balanced blend of efficiency and everyday pep, though owners should test throttle response thoroughly.

**Acceleration Lag or Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the engine hesitates or experiences a noticeable delay lasting a few seconds when attempting to accelerate from a stop or while moving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the throttle body, turbocharger operation, and engine management software for updates or faults.

### 3.5L V6 (260 hp / 236 lb-ft)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine delivers 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque in all-wheel-drive configurations. This naturally aspirated motor is known for smooth power delivery and durable operation.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel-drive models to offer responsive shifting and highway cruising efficiency.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

This traditional 6-speed automatic transmission is coupled with all-wheel-drive configurations, prioritizing smooth engagement and steady power transfer.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior glass and body panels are generally well-assembled, though isolated reports highlight issues with sunroof glass durability.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced instances where the sunroof glass suddenly exploded or shattered while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by a loud bang.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for proper alignment and stress points.

The 2018 Lexus IS300 is a compelling choice for used-car shoppers seeking a dependable and luxurious sport sedan. Powertrain options are robust, though buyers should watch for occasional reports of hesitation or sunroof glass issues during their pre-purchase inspection.
