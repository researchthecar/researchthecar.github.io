# 2023 Lexus IS300

### Overview
The 2023 Lexus IS300 is a compact luxury sedan known for its refined interior, engaging driving dynamics, and Lexus's reputation for reliability. It offers a choice between a potent turbocharged four-cylinder engine in rear-wheel drive or a naturally aspirated V6 in all-wheel drive, paired with a smooth automatic transmission. This model year generally continues the IS line's tradition of solid build quality and dependable performance. Owners can expect a premium experience with a focus on comfort and advanced technology, contributing to its appeal in the competitive luxury sedan segment. Lexus's commitment to quality control and rigorous testing is evident in the IS300's overall robust construction.

The 2023 Lexus IS300 demonstrates strong reliability across all major components, aligning with Lexus's established reputation for building durable vehicles.
