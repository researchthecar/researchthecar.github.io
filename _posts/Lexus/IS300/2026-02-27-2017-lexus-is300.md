### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus IS300 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, consistent with Lexus's reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions (8-speed for RWD, 6-speed for AWD), demonstrates exceptional durability with no widespread reported issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain issues. These are typically manageable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. Overall, the 2017 IS300 offers a dependable ownership experience with a low probability of significant mechanical failures.
