# 2017 Lexus IS300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Lexus IS300, buyers generally expect the hallmark luxury, refinement, and dependability associated with the brand. While the available owner reports are sparse, reviewing common areas like suspension and tire wear can help you ensure a smooth pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (241 hp)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering rear-wheel-drive configurations. It generally offers a refined driving experience, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

### 3.5L V6 (260 hp)

The 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 produces roughly 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with all-wheel drive. This motor is widely regarded for its smooth power delivery and durable mechanical architecture.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission handles power in rear-wheel-drive models, offering smooth and responsive gear shifts. Regular fluid checks during routine service will help maintain long-term reliability.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission is coupled with all-wheel-drive variants, delivering dependable operation under varied driving conditions. Routine fluid inspections are recommended to ensure lasting performance.

## Suspension

### Alignment and Tires

Suspension components generally provide a comfortable and composed ride, though owners should monitor tire wear patterns closely.

**Accelerated and Uneven Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature tire wear, inside edge wear, and occasional tire blowouts within short operating intervals.
- **Solution:** Perform frequent alignment checks and ensure suspension geometry is properly adjusted.
