# 2019 Lexus IS300

### Overview
The 2019 Lexus IS300 is generally regarded as a reliable luxury sedan, continuing Lexus's tradition of building durable vehicles. It offers a choice between a turbocharged four-cylinder engine (RWD) and a naturally aspirated V6 engine (AWD), each with its own transmission pairing. While the IS300 doesn't typically suffer from widespread, critical mechanical failures, like any vehicle, it can experience isolated issues. This report focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers. The focus is on specific components and their reported issues, along with estimated repair costs where applicable.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Lexus IS300 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with most major mechanical components showing no significant widespread issues. Owners can generally expect dependable performance from both the turbocharged four-cylinder and V6 engines, as well as their respective transmissions. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain issues. These are typically manageable with software updates or diagnosis of parasitic draws. Overall, the IS300 is a solid choice for those seeking a dependable luxury sedan with a strong track record for longevity and minimal unexpected repairs.
