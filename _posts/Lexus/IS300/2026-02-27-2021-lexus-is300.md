# 2021 Lexus IS300

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus IS300 is a compact luxury sedan known for its refined interior, comfortable ride, and Lexus's reputation for reliability. It offers a choice between a potent turbocharged four-cylinder engine or a smoother V6, paired with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. While generally well-regarded, like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This report details common technical reliability data for the 2021 IS300, focusing on documented problems beyond routine maintenance, to provide a comprehensive understanding of its long-term dependability across its various configurations.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus IS300 demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain components, including both the 2.0L turbo and 3.5L V6 engines and their respective automatic transmissions, show no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that can usually be resolved with software updates, and a few reports of premature battery failure, which is a common concern across many vehicle makes and models. These are typically manageable and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's excellent reliability record.
