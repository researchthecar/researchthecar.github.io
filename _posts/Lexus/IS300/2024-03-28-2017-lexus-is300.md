**Introduction:** The 2017 Lexus IS300 is a compact luxury sedan known for its sharp handling, refined interior, and reliable performance. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 255 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. The IS300 is available with either a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission.

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Solution: Replace the defective battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails to charge the battery, causing electrical problems.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, lag, or display error messages.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:** The 2017 Lexus IS300 is a generally reliable vehicle. The few issues reported are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. The electrical issues are the most common, but they can be resolved by updating the software or replacing the battery or alternator. Overall, the IS300 offers a high level of comfort and performance with minimal maintenance concerns.
