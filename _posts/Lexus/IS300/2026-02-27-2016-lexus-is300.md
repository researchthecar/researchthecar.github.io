# 2016 Lexus IS300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus IS300 brings the luxury, build quality, and comfort expected from the brand, paired with standard all-wheel drive. While generally regarded as a solid and dependable pre-owned luxury sedan choice, prospective buyers should still inspect various vehicle systems carefully during a test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2016 Lexus IS300 features a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 255 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. This smooth-running powerplant offers dependable performance and good longevity for everyday driving.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

This model is equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission paired with all-wheel drive. It delivers refined shifting characteristics and generally maintains strong reliability when properly maintained.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system generally performs well, though routine inspections are recommended to catch wear or environmental noise early.

**Cold Start Brake Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice unfamiliar noises coming from the braking system during cold morning starts when shifting into drive.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers for excessive wear, moisture accumulation, or cold-weather friction issues.

## Electrical

### Instrument Panel and Audio

Electrical complaints are infrequent, but owners should verify all cabin electronics, dashboard displays, and infotainment features function correctly.

**Electrical Short Warning and Audio Noise**
- **Symptoms:** The instrument panel may display electrical short warnings that cause lights to flash and emit loud noises from the radio system.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the electrical architecture and diagnostic trouble codes to locate and repair the short.

The 2016 Lexus IS300 is a comfortable and well-constructed luxury sedan with very few reported recurring mechanical flaws.
