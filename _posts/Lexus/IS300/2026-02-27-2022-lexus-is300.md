# 2022 Lexus IS300

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus IS300 is a compact luxury sedan known for its refined interior, engaging driving dynamics, and Lexus's reputation for reliability. This model year offers a choice between two distinct powertrains: a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four for rear-wheel-drive models and a naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 for all-wheel-drive variants. Both are paired with automatic transmissions. Overall, the IS300 generally upholds Lexus's commitment to quality, with owners reporting a high degree of satisfaction and minimal unexpected mechanical problems. The focus remains on delivering a premium experience with dependable performance, making it a strong contender in its segment for those prioritizing longevity and a smooth ownership journey.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus IS300 demonstrates strong reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. Both the 2.0L turbo and 3.5L V6 engines, along with their respective automatic transmissions, show no significant widespread mechanical issues. The chassis, electrical systems (barring minor infotainment glitches), exterior, and interior components are generally robust. Owners can expect a dependable luxury sedan with minimal unexpected repair costs outside of routine maintenance. The IS300 is a solid choice for those seeking a well-built and reliable vehicle in the compact executive segment.
