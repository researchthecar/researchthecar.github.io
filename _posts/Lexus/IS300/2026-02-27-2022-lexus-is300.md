# 2022 Lexus IS300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lexus IS300 carries forward Lexus's reputation for strong build quality and refined luxury. As buyers look at this compact luxury sport sedan on the used market, understanding its powertrain options and general owner-reported experiences can help guide a smart purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, powering rear-wheel-drive models. It offers responsive daily drivability and solid long-term reliability when properly maintained.

### 3.5L Naturally Aspirated V6

The 3.5-liter V6 engine delivers roughly 260 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with the all-wheel-drive system. This smooth powerplant is known for robust performance and dependable mechanical longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel-drive models, delivering smooth and quick gear changes. It generally maintains a strong track record for durability.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission comes standard on all-wheel-drive variants, providing reliable power distribution across varying road conditions. It is a proven, straightforward unit with minimal widespread concerns.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=IS&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus IS300 is a dependable choice for used luxury sedan buyers, backed by proven powertrain options and high manufacturing standards. Prospective owners should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and cosmetic condition.
