**Power Window Actuator Failure**

- **Solution:** Replacement of the window motor/regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per window

### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Lexus GS460 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its powertrain, suspension, and core structural components are exceptionally durable, with no widespread critical failures reported. The primary areas where minor issues might surface are within the electrical system, specifically related to the infotainment unit and power window actuators. These are generally not deal-breakers and are often repairable with moderate cost. Overall, the GS460 offers a dependable ownership experience, aligning with Lexus's reputation for quality and longevity, provided routine maintenance is performed.
