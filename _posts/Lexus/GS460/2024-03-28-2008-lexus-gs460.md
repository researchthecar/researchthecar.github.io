## 2008 Lexus GS460

The 2008 Lexus GS460 is a luxury sedan that offers a powerful V8 engine and a smooth ride. It comes standard with a 4.6-liter V8 engine that produces 342 horsepower and 339 lb-ft of torque. A six-speed automatic transmission is the only transmission option. The GS460 is also available with all-wheel drive.

People who like the GS460 praise its comfortable interior, powerful engine, and smooth ride. However, some people find the GS460 to be too expensive and not as sporty as some competitors.

### **Engine**

**4.6L V8**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing coolant and oil to leak. **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold gasket. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Timing chain rattle:** The timing chain can rattle, especially on startup. **Solution:** Replace the timing chain. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **VVT-i system failure:** The VVT-i system can fail, causing a loss of power and fuel economy. **Solution:** Replace the VVT-i system. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Knock sensors failure:** The knock sensors can fail causing the engine to run poorly and potentially cause damage to the engine. Solution: Replace the knock sensors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

### **Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission may slip, especially when shifting gears. **Solution:** Replace the transmission. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Shift solenoid failure:** The shift solenoid can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically. **Solution:** Replace the shift solenoid. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Torque converter shudder:** The torque converter can shudder, causing vibrations when accelerating. **Solution:** Replace the torque converter. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+

### **Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise:** The front suspension can make noise, especially when going over bumps. **Solution:** Replace the front suspension components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Rear suspension sagging:** The rear suspension can sag, causing the vehicle to ride lower in the rear. **Solution:** Replace the rear suspension components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

### **Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain quickly, especially when the vehicle is not in use. **Solution:** Test the battery and replace if necessary. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
* **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, causing the battery to not charge. **Solution:** Replace the alternator. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Starter failure:** The starter can fail, preventing the engine from starting. **Solution:** Replace the starter. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

### **Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** The headlights can get condensation inside them. **Solution:** Replace the headlights. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Tail light bulbs burning out:** The tail light bulbs can burn out prematurely. **Solution:** Replace the tail light bulbs. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $50 - $100+

### **Interior**

* **Climate control malfunction:** The climate control system can malfunction, causing the temperature to not be regulated properly. **Solution:** Replace the climate control unit. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Navigation system freezing:** The navigation system can freeze, causing it to become inoperable. **Solution:** Update the navigation system software. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
* **Audio system speakers blown:** The audio system speakers can blow out, causing distorted sound. **Solution:** Replace the audio system speakers. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

### **Summary**

The 2008 Lexus GS460 is a reliable and well-built vehicle. However, it is not without its problems. Some of the most common issues include intake manifold gasket failure, timing chain rattle, and transmission slipping. The severity of these issues can vary, but they can be expensive to fix. Overall, the GS460 is a good choice for those who want a luxury sedan that is comfortable and powerful. However, it is important to be aware of the potential problems that can occur.
