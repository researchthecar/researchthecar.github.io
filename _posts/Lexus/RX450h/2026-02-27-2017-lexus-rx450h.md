# 2017 Lexus RX450h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus RX 450h continues the brand's tradition of offering a plush ride and dependable hybrid ownership experience, though used-car buyers should review certain recurring component patterns. While general mechanical durability remains strong, owner reports indicate specific attention should be given to brake components and select interior and electrical accessories. Familiarizing yourself with these common trouble spots can help ensure a smoother vehicle selection process.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid (2GR-FXS)

The 3.5-liter V6 Atkinson-cycle engine paired with electric motors generates a net 308 horsepower, routing power through an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission. This powertrain option is widely regarded as robust and long-lasting with standard maintenance.

## Transmission

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (e-CVT)

The planetary-gear-based e-CVT integrates hybrid motor-generators seamlessly with the gasoline engine, providing smooth power delivery and high overall reliability for buyers.

## Brakes

### Brake Actuator and Booster Assembly

Reports highlight potential mechanical wear in the regenerative braking and hydraulic accumulator systems.

**Brake Actuator Noise and Pedal Feel**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an abnormal squawk or barking noise during braking accompanied by unusual brake pedal feedback.
- **Solution:** Have the brake booster and actuator assembly inspected and replaced if failing.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display Screen

Cabin electronics generally function well, though intermittent display failures have been noted by some owners.

**Main Display Screen Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment screen may go completely blank, potentially impacting backup camera visibility and secondary vehicle settings.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the multimedia unit and software modules; replace faulty display hardware if necessary.

## Interior

### Seat Heaters

Interior comfort amenities are generally durable, with occasional reports concerning seat heater regulation.

**Overheating or Slow Seat Warmers**
- **Symptoms:** Heated seats may take an excessive time to activate on lower settings or become uncomfortably hot during operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat heating elements and control modules, replacing faulty components as required.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus RX 450h is a comfortable and highly efficient luxury hybrid SUV that usually rewards buyers with dependable service. When evaluating a used model, ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is performed with particular emphasis on the hybrid braking actuator assembly and interior electronics.
