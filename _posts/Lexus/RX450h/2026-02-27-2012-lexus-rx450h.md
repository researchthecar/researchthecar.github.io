# 2012 Lexus RX450h

### Overview
The 2012 Lexus RX450h is a luxury hybrid SUV known for its comfortable ride, refined interior, and strong fuel efficiency. As a Lexus, it generally boasts high reliability ratings, reflecting Toyota's robust engineering and build quality. The hybrid powertrain, combining a 3.5-liter V6 engine with electric motors, is a well-proven system. Owners typically report satisfaction with the vehicle's long-term durability, with most issues being minor or related to age rather than inherent design flaws. Regular maintenance is key to preserving its excellent condition.

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Lexus RX450h is a highly reliable luxury SUV, particularly noted for its robust hybrid powertrain and overall build quality. While most components exhibit excellent durability, potential owners should be aware of the possibility of hybrid battery degradation over time, which can be a significant expense. Minor electrical issues with the infotainment system and occasional HVAC blower motor failures are also documented. Despite these points, the RX450h remains a strong contender for those seeking a dependable and comfortable hybrid vehicle with a proven track record.
