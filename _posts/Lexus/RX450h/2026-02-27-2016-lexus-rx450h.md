# 2016 Lexus RX450h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus RX 450h combines luxury and hybrid efficiency, but prospective used-car buyers should review common owner-reported complaints. While the powertrain is generally robust, specific issues regarding braking assistance, interior accessories, and minor structural components have surfaced in owner feedback.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid (2GR-FXS)

The 3.5-liter V6 hybrid engine pairs with electric motors to deliver approximately 308 net horsepower with strong fuel economy. It is generally long-lasting, though rare gasket leaks can be costly to address.

**Timing Cover Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks around the timing cover area, requiring expensive engine-out resealing.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front of the engine for oil seepage; repair requires removing the engine to reseal the timing cover.
- **Cost:** $7,000

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The hybrid planetary gear transmission manages power delivery smoothly between the gas engine and electric motors. It typically demonstrates high durability with minimal mechanical failures.

**Gear Hesitation and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may occasionally jerk or hesitate when accelerating from a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Check for available powertrain software updates or consult a dealership for diagnostic evaluation.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The regenerative braking system blends seamlessly with traditional friction brakes, though component wear or vacuum pump issues require prompt attention.

**Brake Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a sudden loss of power brake assist, a 'low braking power' warning message, or significantly increased stopping distances.
- **Solution:** Inspect the vacuum pump and braking hydraulic system for failures; replace faulty components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Electronics

Interior electronics provide comprehensive connectivity and convenience features, though occasional software glitches or screen failures have been reported.

**Dashboard Screen Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic display screen turns pink, goes completely black, and disables access to navigation and HVAC controls.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reset or replace the failing head unit/display assembly if the issue persists.

## Exterior

### Body Trim and Glass

Exterior panels and glass assemblies hold up well overall, but sporadic reports of spontaneous glass breakage warrant a careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof or moonroof glass shatters unexpectedly while driving without any impact from outside objects.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame alignment to relieve stress points.

## Interior

### Interior Trim and Latches

Cabin materials are generally luxurious and durable, though mechanical cables and interior handles can occasionally experience wear-related binding or breakage.

**Hood Latch and Cable Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The hood release mechanism fails to operate, preventing access to the engine compartment for routine maintenance.
- **Solution:** Replace the worn hood latch assembly and binding release cables.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V085000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX450h&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag pressure sensors and/or the acceleration sensors may fail causing the side/curtain shield air bags and/or the front air bags to not deploy in the event of a crash. If the air bags fail to deploy in a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the sensors as necessary based on their serial numbers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V053000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX450h&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A defective inflator may increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the knee air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus RX 450h remains a comfortable, highly efficient luxury hybrid SUV with a strong reputation for overall longevity. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the braking system, check for any oil seepage around the engine, and test all interior electronics and glass panels prior to purchase.
