### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus RX450h demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 hybrid engine and the ECVT transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally trouble-free. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the hybrid battery system, which can experience degradation over time, and occasional glitches with the infotainment system. These are not uncommon for vehicles of this age and technology, and repair costs, while significant for the battery, are within the expected range for luxury hybrid vehicles. Overall, the RX450h is a dependable choice.
