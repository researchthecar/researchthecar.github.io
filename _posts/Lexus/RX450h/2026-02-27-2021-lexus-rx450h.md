# 2021 Lexus RX450h

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus RX450h is a luxury mid-size SUV that builds upon Lexus's reputation for reliability and refinement. It combines a potent V6 engine with an advanced hybrid system, offering a blend of performance and fuel efficiency. Known for its comfortable ride, quiet cabin, and high-quality interior materials, the RX450h is designed for discerning buyers who prioritize a smooth and dependable ownership experience. This model year generally continues the trend of strong reliability, with owners reporting satisfaction with its powertrain and overall build quality. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the RX450h typically demonstrates fewer significant problems compared to many competitors in its segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus RX450h stands out as a highly reliable luxury hybrid SUV. Its powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 hybrid system and ECVT transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. The chassis and suspension components are also generally trouble-free. Electrical systems, particularly the hybrid battery, are typically dependable, though minor infotainment glitches can occur and are usually resolved with software updates. Exterior and interior components are built to Lexus's high standards, with no significant recurring problems noted. Overall, the RX450h offers excellent long-term reliability, making it a sound investment for those seeking a premium and dependable vehicle.
