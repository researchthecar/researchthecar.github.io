# 2021 Lexus RX450h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus RX 450h continues the brand's tradition of offering a luxurious, highly efficient hybrid midsize SUV experience. Combining a refined V6 engine with electric motor assistance, it provides smooth power delivery and stellar fuel economy for daily commuting and long road trips alike. For used car shoppers, this model generally enjoys a strong reputation for dependability, though prospective buyers should still carefully inspect the vehicle history and test drive all powertrain modes to ensure seamless operation.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid (308 hp / 247 lb-ft)

This 3.5-liter V6 paired with Lexus Hybrid Drive delivers a combined 308 horsepower through an electronic continuously variable transmission. It provides robust acceleration and excellent fuel efficiency while maintaining high standards of long-term mechanical reliability.

## Transmission

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (e-CVT)

The planetary-type electronic continuously variable transmission manages power blending between the gasoline engine and electric motors. It operates smoothly and reliably with very few reported mechanical complications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus RX 450h is a dependable and well-engineered luxury hybrid SUV that offers strong peace of mind for used car buyers. With a robust powertrain and an absence of widespread recurring mechanical complaints, it stands out as a solid long-term investment. Standard used-car precautions, such as verifying routine maintenance records and checking hybrid battery health, are still recommended prior to purchase.
