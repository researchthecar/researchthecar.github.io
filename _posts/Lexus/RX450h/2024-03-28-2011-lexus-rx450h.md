**Introduction:**

The 2011 Lexus RX450h is a mid-size luxury hybrid SUV known for its fuel efficiency, reliability, and comfortable ride. It features a 3.5-liter V6 gasoline engine and an electric motor that generate a combined 295 horsepower and 257 lb-ft of torque. The RX450h is available with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

* **3.5-Liter V6**

    * **Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine overhaul.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $6,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Coolant Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the water pump or radiator, leading to overheating and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Water pump or radiator replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **VVT-i System Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Variable Valve Timing (VVT-i) system malfunction, resulting in reduced engine performance and fuel efficiency.
        * Solution: VVT-i actuator or solenoid replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

    * **Hesitation or Shuddering:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or shuddering during acceleration or gear changes.
        * Solution: CVT software update or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: NHTSA ID Number: 17V-551
    * **Premature Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission failure before the expected lifespan, resulting in loss of power and drivability.
        * Solution: Transmission replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA ID Number: 11V-175

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining quickly when the vehicle is parked or not in use.
    * Solution: Parasitic draw diagnosis and repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* **SRS Airbag Light:**
    * Description/Symptoms: SRS airbag light illuminates on the instrument panel without an apparent reason.
    * Solution: Airbag control module reset or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezes, displays incorrect information, or fails to function properly.
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight housings.
    * Solution: Headlight replacement or resealing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, resulting in insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Climate control actuator or sensor replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or vibration from the dashboard while driving.
    * Solution: Dashboard disassembly and tightening.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
* **Bluetooth Connectivity Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty pairing or maintaining Bluetooth connections with mobile devices.
    * Solution: Software update or hardware repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500

**Summary:**

The 2011 Lexus RX450h has a few potential issues, mostly related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. However, the vehicle remains a generally reliable and fuel-efficient option within its class.
