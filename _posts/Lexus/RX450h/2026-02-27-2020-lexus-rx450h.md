# 2020 Lexus RX450h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lexus RX 450h continues the brand's tradition of offering a luxurious, highly efficient hybrid SUV experience. Built with a robust powertrain and a comfortable cabin, it appeals to buyers looking for long-term comfort and fuel savings. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, prospective buyers should review available systems carefully to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid (2RE/2GR-FXS)

This 3.5-liter V6 hybrid powertrain pairs with electric motors to generate a combined 308 horsepower, delivering smooth acceleration and impressive fuel economy for its class. It is widely regarded as exceptionally durable and reliable with routine maintenance.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The dedicated hybrid transmission seamlessly blends power from the gasoline engine and electric motors to drive the front wheels or electronic all-wheel-drive system. It generally exhibits high reliability and smooth operation in daily driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus RX 450h is a dependable and refined hybrid luxury SUV backed by a strong reputation for powertrain durability. With very few reported owner complaints, it stands out as a solid choice for used-car shoppers prioritizing fuel efficiency, comfort, and peace of mind.
