# 2024 Lexus TX350

### Overview
The 2024 Lexus TX350 represents a new entrant in the luxury three-row SUV segment, built on a platform shared with Toyota's Grand Highlander. As a brand-new model, comprehensive long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, Lexus's established reputation for quality and the use of proven powertrain components suggest a strong initial reliability outlook. Early reports and the vehicle's underlying architecture, which leverages existing Toyota/Lexus technologies, indicate a focus on durability and minimizing common issues. Owners can generally expect a refined and dependable ownership experience, consistent with Lexus's brand promise, though specific long-term performance of certain new features will become clearer with time.

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Lexus TX350, being a new model, exhibits a strong initial reliability profile, largely due to its shared platform and powertrain with proven Toyota/Lexus components. The 2.4L turbocharged engine and 8-speed automatic transmission have a solid track record. The primary area for potential minor concern, as with many new vehicles, lies in the infotainment system, where occasional software glitches have been noted, typically resolvable with updates. Other categories such as suspension, exterior, and interior components show no widespread issues. Overall, the TX350 is expected to offer a dependable ownership experience, aligning with Lexus's reputation for quality and durability.
