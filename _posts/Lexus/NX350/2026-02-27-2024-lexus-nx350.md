# 2024 Lexus NX350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus NX 350 brings modern luxury, sharp styling, and advanced technology to the compact luxury crossover segment. As a relatively new model year with sparse owner complaints, prospective buyers can generally approach the vehicle with confidence, backed by Lexus's strong reputation for long-term build quality and reliability.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.4-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine produces approximately 275 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It is generally regarded as a robust and refined powertrain option for this luxury crossover.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

Paired with standard all-wheel drive, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifts. It complements the turbocharged engine well and shows no widespread pattern of mechanical concern.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V480000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX350&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A deactivated driver's air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the spiral cable assembly, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Lexus NX 350 presents a very solid choice in the used luxury market, combining refined performance with high anticipated reliability. Because it is still early in its lifecycle and lacks significant recurring complaint trends, buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything is in order.
