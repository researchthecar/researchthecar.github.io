### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Lexus NX350 demonstrates strong reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The powertrain, including the 2.4L turbocharged engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that can usually be resolved with software updates, and a rare instance of premature battery drain. Overall, the NX350 is a dependable choice for luxury SUV buyers seeking a vehicle with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
