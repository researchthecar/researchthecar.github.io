# 2023 Lexus NX350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Lexus NX 350 represents a modern luxury compact SUV offering advanced technology and refined styling. When shopping for a used model, understanding the powertrain options and monitoring owner-reported experiences can help ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.4-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine produces approximately 275 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving while maintaining balanced reliability for buyers seeking modern turbocharged performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the standard all-wheel-drive system, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifting. It generally offers solid dependability under normal operating conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V480000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=NX350&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A deactivated driver's air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the spiral cable assembly, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Lexus NX 350 is a well-engineered luxury crossover with strong performance credentials. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough test drive and inspect vehicle service histories to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
