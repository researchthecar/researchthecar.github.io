# 2022 Lexus IS350

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sport sedan known for its refined driving experience and Lexus's reputation for reliability. This model year continues that tradition, offering a potent V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission. Owners generally report a high level of satisfaction with the vehicle's build quality and performance. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the IS350 has demonstrated a strong track record, with most reported concerns being minor or related to typical usage rather than inherent design flaws. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability data, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus IS350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its core mechanical components, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, show no significant widespread issues. The chassis and suspension systems are also robust. Minor electrical concerns, primarily related to occasional infotainment system glitches, are the most commonly cited, but these are typically resolved with software updates and do not represent a major reliability concern. Exterior and interior components are generally well-built and durable. Overall, the 2022 IS350 is a strong performer in terms of long-term dependability, aligning with Lexus's established reputation for quality.
