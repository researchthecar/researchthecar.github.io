### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus IS350 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, consistent with Lexus's brand promise. The powertrain, including the robust 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions (8-speed for RWD, 6-speed for AWD), demonstrates exceptional durability with no significant widespread mechanical failures reported. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally free from common issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and rare instances of battery drain, which are typically addressable through software updates or diagnostics. Overall, the IS350 offers a dependable luxury driving experience with minimal risk of major mechanical problems.
