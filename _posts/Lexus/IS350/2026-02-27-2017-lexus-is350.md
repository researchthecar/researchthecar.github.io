# 2017 Lexus IS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus IS350 continues the brand's tradition of offering a sporty, luxurious, and highly dependable compact sedan experience. Known for its solid build quality and comfortable cabin, it remains an appealing choice for used-car shoppers looking for rear-wheel or all-wheel-drive performance. While owner complaints are very minimal for this model year, buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and electronic health.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, utilizing both direct and port fuel injection. It is widely regarded as a robust and long-lasting powerplant with few widespread mechanical flaws.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel-drive models to offer smooth gear changes and responsive performance. Routine fluid maintenance is generally all that is required to keep this gearbox operating reliably.

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission is utilized on all-wheel-drive variants, delivering stable power distribution across slippery conditions. It is a proven, durable unit with a strong reputation for longevity.

## Steering

### Traction Control System

The stability and traction control systems generally operate reliably, though isolated electronic glitches have been reported by owners.

**Unexpected Traction Control Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The traction control warning light illuminates unexpectedly, and the driver may experience a brief sensation of lost vehicle control while maneuvering at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle stability control module, check wheel speed sensors, and inspect the steering angle sensor for proper calibration.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus IS350 stands out as a dependable choice in the luxury sport sedan market, backed by strong powertrain options and very few owner complaints. Prospective buyers should verify proper operation of all electronic safety systems during a test drive. Overall, it remains a sound investment for those seeking a balance of luxury and peace of mind.
