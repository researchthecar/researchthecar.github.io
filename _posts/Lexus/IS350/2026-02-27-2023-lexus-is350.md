# 2023 Lexus IS350

### Overview
The 2023 Lexus IS350 is a compact luxury sedan known for its blend of sporty performance and Lexus's renowned reliability. It typically comes equipped with a potent V6 engine and an automatic transmission, offering a refined driving experience. This model year continues Lexus's tradition of building durable vehicles with a focus on quality materials and meticulous engineering. Owners generally report high satisfaction with the IS350's performance and long-term dependability, with few widespread mechanical or electrical concerns surfacing in early ownership data. The vehicle's design prioritizes driver engagement while maintaining the comfort and quietness expected from the Lexus brand.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Lexus IS350 demonstrates excellent reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is robust and shows no significant widespread issues. Suspension and exterior components are also holding up well. Overall, the IS350 is a dependable choice for luxury sedan buyers seeking a blend of performance and long-term peace of mind, with minimal expected repair costs beyond routine maintenance.
