### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus IS350 demonstrates strong reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the infotainment system, which can occasionally experience software glitches requiring updates or resets, and the battery, which in some instances may require premature replacement. Overall, the IS350 is a dependable luxury sedan with a low probability of major mechanical failures, making it a solid choice for those seeking a reliable vehicle.
