# 2021 Lexus IS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus IS350 blends sharp handling, a refined interior, and a strong track record of brand reliability. While owner reports to regulatory bodies are sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should still inspect standard wear items and remain aware of isolated user grievances regarding exterior glass and auxiliary controls.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (311 hp / 280 lb-ft)

The 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 produces 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth power delivery and a strong reputation for long-term mechanical durability. This proven engine is generally considered a low-risk choice for used buyers.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel-drive configurations to provide quick shifts and refined cruising capability. It is a robust unit with few widespread mechanical complaints reported by owners.

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission is utilized in all-wheel-drive models, delivering steady power distribution across varying road conditions. It is widely regarded as a dependable and durable transmission option.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body and glass components are generally well-assembled, though isolated incidents involving glass breakage have been noted.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass may spontaneously shatter while driving on the highway without any external impact from rocks or debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass assembly for stress fractures and replace the shattered panel with a new OEM sunroof assembly.

## Safety

### Horn

Safety equipment generally performs well, though individual perception of auxiliary warning devices like the horn can vary.

**Low Horn Volume**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may find the factory horn insufficiently loud to alert larger vehicles or distracted drivers in emergency situations.
- **Solution:** Upgrade to an aftermarket dual-tone horn kit if higher decibel output is desired for alerting other motorists.

The 2021 Lexus IS350 is a dependable luxury sport sedan that benefits from time-tested powertrain options and strong overall build quality.
