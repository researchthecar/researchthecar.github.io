**Introduction:**

The 2021 Lexus IS350 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, producing 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The IS350 is praised for its sharp handling, refined interior, and advanced safety features. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its potential issues.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Abnormal Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption leading to low oil levels and increased oil pressure. Vehicles may exhibit blue smoke from the exhaust.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stems.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or sudden jerking during shifts, particularly from 1st to 2nd gear.
        * Solution: Software update or transmission overhaul.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace faulty battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control may not engage or maintain set speed properly.
    * Solution: Replace cruise control switch or module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise or rattling from the sunroof.
    * Solution: Adjust sunroof alignment or replace seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Paint Bubbling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Small bubbles or blisters forming beneath the paint surface.
    * Solution: Repair or repaint the affected area.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Occasional freezing or lagging of the infotainment screen, navigation errors, or audio distortion.
    * Solution: Software update or system reset.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Squeaky Door Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Annoying squeaking or rattling noises from door panels.
    * Solution: Apply lubrication or replace door panel clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Lexus IS350 is a mechanically sound vehicle with no major widespread issues. The minor issues that do exist are relatively inexpensive to fix and most can be resolved with software updates or simple repairs. While paint bubbling is a concern, it is not a prevalent problem. As with any vehicle, proper maintenance and timely repairs are crucial for maintaining its longevity and performance.
