**Introduction:**

The 2011 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sport sedan equipped with a powerful 3.5L V6 engine that delivers 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque. Paired with it is a six-speed automatic transmission. The IS350 is known for its refined driving experience, comfortable interior, and sporty handling. However, it has faced some issues over the years.

**Engine:**

**3.5L V6**

**Engine Knock:**
Description/Symptoms: A loud knocking sound coming from the engine, usually during acceleration or when under load.
Solution: Typically requires replacing the piston rings and connecting rods.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,500 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Coolant Leak:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, visible leak from the engine, or overheating.
Solution: Repair or replace the leaking component, which could be the radiator, water pump, or a hose.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slippage:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, delayed or jerky shifting, or whining noises from the transmission.
Solution: May require a transmission rebuild or replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

**Battery Discharge:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even with the car turned off.
Solution: Test for battery drain, check for any electrical components staying active, and replace the battery if necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior:**

**Infotainment System Glitch:**
Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezing, crashing, or displaying errors.
Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2011 Lexus IS350 generally receives positive reviews for its performance and luxury. However, it has faced some issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. These issues can range from relatively minor electrical glitches to more serious engine and transmission problems that can be costly to fix.
