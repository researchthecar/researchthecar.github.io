The 2022 Lexus IS350 is a compact luxury sedan. It is available with a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The IS350 is praised for its smooth ride, luxurious interior, and responsive handling. However, it is criticized for its lack of driver-assistance features and its cramped rear seats.

**Engine**
* **3.5-Liter V6 Engine**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage. Symptoms may include a burning oil smell, blue smoke from the exhaust, and a check engine light.
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings and valve seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail prematurely, resulting in engine damage. Symptoms may include a rattling or knocking noise from the engine, reduced engine power, and a check engine light.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shudder or hesitate during gear shifts. Symptoms may worsen over time.
        * Solution: Update the transmission control software or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start. Symptoms may include a dead battery, flickering lights, or a malfunctioning audio system.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the battery drain, such as a faulty alternator, parasitic draw, or battery issue.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Air Conditioning Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The air conditioning system may not work properly, resulting in insufficient cooling or heating. Symptoms may include weak airflow, strange noises, or a refrigerant leak.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty component, such as the compressor, condenser, or evaporator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary**
The 2022 Lexus IS350 has some notable issues that can be costly to repair. The excessive oil consumption and timing chain failure in the engine are the most severe and expensive issues. The transmission shudder can also be an expensive problem to solve. The other issues are less severe and generally less expensive to fix.
