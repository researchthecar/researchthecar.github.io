# 2019 Lexus IS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Lexus IS350 means looking at a well-regarded luxury sport sedan known for strong build quality and engaging performance. While owner complaints in public logs are minimal for this model year, buyers should still carefully inspect the vehicle during a pre-purchase test drive, paying particular attention to body glass and structural integrity.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (311 hp / 280 lb-ft)

This naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 produces 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive. It is widely considered a highly reliable powerplant with a robust mechanical track record.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery for rear-wheel-drive models smoothly and exhibits very few widespread mechanical complaints from owners.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with all-wheel-drive variants, offering proven durability and dependable long-term shifting behavior.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Owner reports highlight rare incidents involving exterior glass components.

**Sudden Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass may fracture unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds, dropping glass fragments into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for prior stress cracks, and replace the shattered glass panel if damaged.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus IS350 maintains a strong reputation for dependability, supported by very few reported owner complaints. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough visual inspection of all exterior glass and trim, but can generally approach this luxury sport sedan with confidence.
