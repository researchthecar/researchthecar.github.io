# 2019 Lexus IS350

### Overview
The 2019 Lexus IS350 is a compact luxury sedan known for its blend of sporty performance and Lexus's renowned reliability. Powered by a potent V6 engine and paired with a smooth automatic transmission, it offers an engaging driving experience. The IS350 generally upholds Lexus's reputation for quality construction and dependable operation. Owners typically report high satisfaction with its performance, comfort, and long-term durability. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the IS350 has demonstrated a strong track record, with most reported concerns being minor or related to typical usage rather than inherent design flaws. This model continues the Lexus tradition of building vehicles that are both enjoyable to drive and reliable over many years.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Lexus IS350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its powertrain, including the robust 3.5L V6 engine and the smooth automatic transmissions (8-speed for RWD, 6-speed for AWD), shows no significant widespread issues. The suspension and chassis components are also generally trouble-free. Minor electrical concerns, such as occasional infotainment glitches or premature battery failure, are the most commonly cited, though these are not pervasive. Exterior and interior components have proven durable. Overall, the IS350 offers excellent long-term reliability, making it a sound choice for buyers seeking a dependable and enjoyable premium vehicle.
