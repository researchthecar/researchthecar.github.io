**Introduction**

The 2016 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sports sedan that offers an engaging driving experience with a powerful V6 engine and responsive handling. The IS350 comes standard with a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its overall reliability, the 2016 Lexus IS350 has been reported to experience some issues in specific areas.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:** Carbon deposits can form on the intake valves, leading to decreased engine performance, rough idling, and reduced fuel economy.
    * Solution: Valve cleaning and decarbonization.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking:** Some drivers have reported intermittent hesitation or jerking during gear changes.
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission fluid replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** Premature failure of the strut mounts can cause excessive noise, vibration, and decreased handling.
* Solution: Replacement of strut mounts.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+ per corner
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** In certain cases, the vehicle may experience excessive battery drain, leading to dead batteries or difficulty starting.
* Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system components or software updates.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Sticking:** Some owners have reported sticking door handles, making it difficult to open the doors.
* Solution: Replacement of door handles or lubrication of mechanisms.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, leading to inconsistent temperatures or blower fan issues.
* Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control components or software updates.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2016 Lexus IS350 is generally a reliable vehicle, it has some reported issues in specific areas, particularly with the intake valve carbon buildup, strut mount failure, and electrical system. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to more serious mechanical problems. The estimated cost to fix these issues varies depending on the specific parts and labor required.
