# 2009 Lexus IS350

### Overview
The 2009 Lexus IS350 is generally regarded as a reliable luxury sport sedan, benefiting from Toyota's robust engineering and quality control. This analysis focuses on documented, non-routine problems that may arise, excluding standard maintenance and expected wear and tear. The IS350's powertrain and core systems are known for their durability, contributing to its reputation as a solid choice in the premium compact executive car segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Lexus IS350 stands out for its strong mechanical reliability, with its 3.5L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission being particularly robust and rarely presenting significant issues. Suspension and exterior components also generally hold up well. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues. These are not universal but are the most commonly reported non-routine problems. Overall, the IS350 offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential issues being relatively minor and manageable, reinforcing Lexus's reputation for quality.
