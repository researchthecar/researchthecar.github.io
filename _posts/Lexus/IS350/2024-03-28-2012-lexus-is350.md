**Introduction:**
The 2012 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sports sedan renowned for its exceptional performance, elegant design, and premium amenities. It is equipped with a powerful 3.5-liter V6 engine mated to a six-speed automatic transmission. With 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, the IS350 offers an exhilarating driving experience.

**Engine:**
**3.5-liter V6**

* **Valve cover gasket leak:** Oil leaks from the area where the valve cover meets the engine head. This can lead to decreased oil levels and engine damage if not addressed.
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket and clean oil from affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**
**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation during shifts:** Transmission hesitates or slips during gear changes, especially when accelerating.
    * Solution: Update transmission control module software and perform transmission fluid flush.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**
* **Strut mount bearing failure:** Worn or damaged strut mount bearings can cause clunking noises, vibration, and uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per corner
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**
* **Battery drain:** Vehicle battery drains quickly when parked, even with accessories turned off.
    * Solution: Diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain and replace faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**
* **Door handle sensor malfunction:** Door handles may not unlock or lock properly due to faulty sensor.
    * Solution: Replace door handle sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per door
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**
* **Center console rattle:** Center console makes a rattling noise due to loose components or shifting parts.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
Overall, the 2012 Lexus IS350 is a reliable and well-built vehicle. The issues listed above are relatively minor and do not significantly affect the overall performance or reliability of the vehicle. However, some issues, such as the valve cover gasket leak and transmission hesitation, can be costly to repair if not addressed promptly.
