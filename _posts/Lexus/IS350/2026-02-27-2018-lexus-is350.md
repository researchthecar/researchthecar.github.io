# 2018 Lexus IS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sport sedan known for its plush interior, sharp handling, and robust mechanical engineering. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers generally find a dependable luxury cruiser, though isolated owner reports point to potential cosmetic and convenience issues that are worth checking during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (311 hp)

This 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is widely regarded as a highly durable and dependable powerplant in the luxury sport sedan segment.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission features rear-wheel drive and paddle shifters for responsive gear changes. It generally maintains a strong reputation for long-term reliability with standard fluid maintenance.

### 6-Speed Automatic

This traditional 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with all-wheel drive configurations. It offers smooth, predictable shifting and solid overall durability.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators

Interior components are generally well-assembled, though occasional electrical actuator failures can affect door accessibility.

**Failing Door Actuators**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle door locks stop working or operate intermittently across multiple doors, preventing doors from opening properly.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the affected door latch assemblies and replace the failed power door lock actuators.

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior panels and trim hold up well over time, though isolated glass stress incidents have been reported by owners.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shatter**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass suddenly shatters or explodes while driving on the highway without prior impact or debris contact.
- **Solution:** Clear all broken glass shards from the vehicle interior and replace the complete sunroof glass assembly.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus IS350 is a strong contender in the used luxury sedan market, backed by strong powertrain reliability and high-end build quality. While rare owner complaints note isolated issues like failing door actuators or spontaneous sunroof glass breakage, these do not represent widespread systemic flaws. Buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify all power accessories and glass integrity.
