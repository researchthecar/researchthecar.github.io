# 2007 Lexus IS350

### Overview
The 2007 Lexus IS350 is generally regarded as a reliable luxury sport sedan, benefiting from Lexus's reputation for quality engineering and robust build. It features a potent V6 engine paired with a smooth automatic transmission, offering a compelling blend of performance and comfort. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that may arise, providing insights into potential repair areas and their associated costs to help prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FSE)
**Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, rough idle, decreased fuel efficiency, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Manual cleaning of intake valves, or in severe cases, walnut blasting.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, potential for oil spots under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

**Key Fob Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Key fob requires frequent battery replacement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of key fob battery, or potential issue with the fob itself.
- **Cost:** $5 - $20 (battery), $100+ (new fob)

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Lexus IS350 is a highly reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the direct-injection engine's tendency for carbon buildup on intake valves and potential minor oil leaks from valve cover gaskets. Electrical issues are infrequent, with the HVAC blower motor resistor being a common, albeit minor, repair. Overall, the IS350 offers excellent long-term reliability, making it a solid choice for those seeking a dependable luxury sport sedan. Regular maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly will ensure continued optimal performance.
