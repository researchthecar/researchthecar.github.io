**Introduction**

The 2013 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sports sedan that is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque. It is available with either a six-speed automatic transmission or a six-speed manual transmission. The IS350 is praised for its comfortable ride, luxurious interior, and powerful engine. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and relatively small cargo space.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**

* **Knocking or ticking noise at cold start**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faint knocking or ticking noise is heard after a cold start.
    * Solution: Replace the hydraulic valve lifters.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Check engine light due to P0430 code**
    * Description/Symptoms: The check engine light illuminates, and the code P0430 is stored.
    * Solution: Replace the catalytic converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $900 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rough idle or hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine idles roughly or hesitates when accelerating.
    * Solution: Clean the throttle body and replace the spark plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems or slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission shifts roughly or slips between gears.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter. If the problem persists, replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **No major issues found**
* **Recalls: None Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear suspension noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A clunking or rattling noise is heard from the rear suspension when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the rear sway bar end links or bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or check for electrical parasites.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof seal leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof seal.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation system freezing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system freezes or becomes unresponsive.
    * Solution: Update the navigation software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $200+ depending on dealership
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Lexus IS350 is a reliable vehicle with few major issues. However, some owners have reported problems with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can be high. Overall, the IS350 is a well-built vehicle that offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience.
