**Introduction**

The 2017 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sport sedan that delivers both performance and sophistication. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 engine, which produces 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The IS350 is available with either an 8-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission.

The IS350 has received positive reviews for its athletic handling, comfortable interior, and stylish design. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of rear-seat space and its relatively high price.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6 Engine**

**Issue:** **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, poor acceleration
Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves and intake manifold
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Issue:** **Hesitation or Jerking During Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips, delayed shifts, rough shifting
Solution: Flushing transmission fluid, updating transmission software
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Issue:** **Rear Suspension Sagging**
Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of rear suspension components, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replacing rear suspension components (such as springs, shocks, struts)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Issue:** **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Vehicle won't start, rapid battery discharge
Solution: Inspecting electrical system for parasitic drains, replacing battery
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Issue:** **Infotainment System Lag**
Description/Symptoms: Slow response or freezing of infotainment system
Solution: Updating infotainment software
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Lexus IS350 is a generally reliable luxury sedan, but it has experienced some issues related to engine performance, transmission issues, suspension problems, and interior electronics. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can be substantial. Overall, the IS350 is a well-built vehicle, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
