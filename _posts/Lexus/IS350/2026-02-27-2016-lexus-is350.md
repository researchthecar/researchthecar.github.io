# 2016 Lexus IS350

### Overview
The 2016 Lexus IS350 is generally recognized for its strong reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. This model year benefits from Lexus's commitment to quality engineering and rigorous testing. Owners typically report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs. The vehicle's powertrain, interior, and exterior components are designed for durability, contributing to its reputation as a dependable luxury sedan. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the IS350 from 2016 stands out for its consistent performance and low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems, making it a solid choice for those seeking a reliable premium compact executive car.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus IS350 is a highly reliable vehicle, consistently performing well across all major categories. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions (8-speed for RWD, 6-speed for AWD), shows no significant widespread issues. Suspension and exterior components are also robust. The primary areas where minor concerns might arise are the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that are typically resolved with software updates, and the possibility of premature battery failure, which is a common concern across many vehicle makes and models. Interior components are generally durable. Overall, the 2016 IS350 offers excellent long-term reliability with minimal expected repair costs beyond routine maintenance.
