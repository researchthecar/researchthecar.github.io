# 2016 Lexus IS350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Lexus IS350 brings you into contact with one of the more refined luxury sport sedans of its era, known for a strong mix of luxury and everyday usability. While owner reports to federal safety regulators remain sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect foundational areas like the suspension and advanced driver aids during a test drive. As with any used luxury vehicle, a thorough pre-purchase inspection helps verify that everything from the powertrain to the cabin electronics operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and robust overall reliability. This naturally aspirated powerplant is generally regarded as a long-lasting cornerstone of the Lexus lineup with proper routine maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel-drive models, delivering quick shifts and responsive power delivery. It maintains a solid reputation for durability when fluid changes are performed at recommended intervals.

### 6-Speed Automatic

This traditional 6-speed automatic transmission is utilized in all-wheel-drive variants to manage power distribution effectively across varied road conditions. It is broadly considered dependable and robust for everyday driving.

## Suspension

### Rear Suspension

The suspension system provides a balanced blend of athletic handling and ride comfort, though individual components should be checked for wear during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Rear Suspension Thumping Noise**
- **Symptoms:** A noticeable thump or noise may emanate from the rear passenger side when driving over speed bumps, potholes, or uneven road surfaces at moderate speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear struts, mounts, and bushings for premature wear or failure, and replace the affected components as necessary.

## Safety

### Pre-Collision System

Advanced safety features enhance driver awareness, though sensor calibration and system behavior should be verified during a test drive.

**Pre-Collision System Warning Beeps**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may emit warning beeps or alerts during sudden acceleration maneuvers or when approaching slower traffic.
- **Solution:** Have the pre-collision sensors and software calibration inspected by a qualified technician to ensure proper system sensitivity.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus IS350 generally provides a dependable ownership experience backed by strong powertrain options and high overall build quality. While isolated owner complaints point to minor suspension noises and sensitive collision warning behavior, these reports do not indicate widespread systemic defects. Buyers are encouraged to perform a comprehensive test drive and pre-purchase inspection to confirm the condition of the suspension and electronics before finalizing a purchase.
