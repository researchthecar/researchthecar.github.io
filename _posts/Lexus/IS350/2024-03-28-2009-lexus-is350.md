**Introduction**

The 2009 Lexus IS350 is a luxury sport sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience with its powerful V6 engine. It is renowned for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and sleek design. However, like all vehicles, the IS350 has faced some specific issues over the years.

**Engine**

**3.5L 2GR-FSE V6**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, hesitation under acceleration
    * Solution: Cleaning the intake valves by walnut blasting or manual removal of carbon deposits
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from the engine, especially during cold starts or low RPMs
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain, tensioners, and guides
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic (A650E)**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or delayed shifting
    * Solution: Replacing solenoids, valve body, or performing transmission rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise or vibration while driving over bumps, uneven handling
    * Solution: Replacing the lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Coil Spring Snap Ring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The coil spring pops out of its seat, causing the vehicle to ride lower on one side
    * Solution: Replacing the coil spring and snap ring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Smart Entry System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Key fob is not recognized or fails to unlock the doors
    * Solution: Replacing the smart entry system actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery is drained after being parked for a period of time
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing the source of the electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlights
    * Solution: Replacing the headlight assembly with a redesigned version
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door lock actuator fails to lock or unlock the doors
    * Solution: Replacing the door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per door
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, inconsistent temperature, or AC malfunction
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing the HVAC system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system display malfunction, loss of functionality
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the navigation system unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Lexus IS350 has a reputation for reliability and performance. However, it has experienced specific issues in the areas of engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior. While some of these issues are relatively minor and can be easily resolved, others can be more costly and require major repairs. Potential owners should be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
