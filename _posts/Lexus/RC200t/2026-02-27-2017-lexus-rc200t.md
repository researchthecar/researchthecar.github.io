# 2017 Lexus RC200t Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus RC 200t delivers luxury coupe styling and rear-wheel-drive dynamics powered by a turbocharged four-cylinder engine. While Lexus generally maintains a strong reputation for overall dependability, used-car buyers should review owner-reported feedback concerning various mechanical and electrical systems during pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It pairs rear-wheel drive with smooth daily power delivery, though owners should monitor for occasional fuel system odors.

**Fuel Odor in Cabin**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may occasionally notice fuel fumes inside the cabin while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel system lines, connections, and components for potential leaks.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power to the rear wheels with paddle shifters for manual control. While generally robust, some owners have reported erratic shifting behavior under specific driving loads.

**Hard Shifting and Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may shift harshly or slip, most noticeably when accelerating through turns, causing a bucking sensation.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition and have a dealer or specialist evaluate the control module and drivetrain software.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake components require routine inspection during standard maintenance intervals to prevent premature wear.

**Premature Pad and Rotor Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pads may wear down prematurely, leading to grinding noises as they contact the rotors.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pad thickness and rotor condition regularly, replacing components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus RC 200t combines striking coupe design with luxury features, but prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to transmission shift quality, brake wear, and any unusual engine or fuel odors noted during a test drive.
