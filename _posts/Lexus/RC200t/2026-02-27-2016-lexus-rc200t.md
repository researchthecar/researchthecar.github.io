# 2016 Lexus RC200t Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus RC 200t enters the used luxury coupe market with a reputation for solid overall build quality and engaging rear-wheel-drive dynamics. Buyers looking at this model will find a stylish coupe powered by a turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. While initial owner feedback and historical data suggest typical Lexus dependability, prospective shoppers should thoroughly inspect individual used models for minor mechanical and electronic quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a balance of efficiency and punchy acceleration for daily driving. It generally maintains strong long-term reliability provided routine oil maintenance schedules are strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The eight-speed automatic transmission features manual shift paddles and is paired exclusively with rear-wheel drive. It is known for smooth operation and responsive gear selection, with few widespread reliability concerns reported by owners.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Locks

Cabin electronics and physical hardware generally hold up well, though isolated power lock components can occasionally require attention.

**Driver Door Lock Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The driver side door may fail to lock or unlock using either the key fob or the interior master door lock button.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the door lock actuator assembly and replace the faulty actuator motor or latch mechanism as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus RC 200t remains an appealing option for used car buyers seeking luxury styling and everyday usability without excessive mechanical drama. While isolated issues such as power door lock failures can occasionally surface, the powertrain options are robust. As with any used luxury vehicle, obtaining a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and reviewing maintenance records will help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
