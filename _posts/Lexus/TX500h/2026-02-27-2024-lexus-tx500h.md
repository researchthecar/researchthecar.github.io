The 2024 Lexus TX500h represents a new entrant in the luxury three-row SUV segment, built on Toyota's GA-K platform. It aims to blend Lexus's renowned reliability with enhanced practicality and a focus on hybrid performance. Early data suggests a strong start for the TX500h, with Lexus's established reputation for quality and durability underpinning its design.
