# 2019 Lexus LS500h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus LS500h represents the pinnacle of hybrid luxury, combining sophisticated engineering with high-end comfort. While overall owner satisfaction and brand reputation for reliability are strong, prospective buyers should always evaluate powertrain health and potential mechanical issues thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbo V6 Hybrid (354 hp)

The 3.5-liter twin-turbo V6 hybrid system pairs gasoline power with electric motors to deliver approximately 354 horsepower and robust torque. This advanced setup generally offers smooth operation, though rare owner reports highlight unexpected mechanical behavior.

**Unexpected Deceleration and Engine Knocking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly decelerates from cruising speeds accompanied by an unusual knocking noise from the engine bay, with no warning lights illuminated.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation by a certified technician to inspect internal engine components and powertrain control modules.

## Transmission

### Multi-Stage Hybrid Automatic Transmission

The multi-stage hybrid transmission blends a planetary gear set with a traditional automatic gear arrangement to provide seamless power delivery. It is generally robust and engineered to handle the complex demands of the hybrid powertrain.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V275000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Incorrect detection of an occupant may result in improper air bag deployment during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the front passenger seat, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus LS500h is a technologically advanced luxury sedan that generally delivers the reliability expected of the brand. Used-car buyers should perform a detailed inspection of the hybrid powertrain and ensure a comprehensive test drive to check for any unusual noises or performance hesitations.
