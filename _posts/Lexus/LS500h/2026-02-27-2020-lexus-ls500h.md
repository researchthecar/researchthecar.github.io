# 2020 Lexus LS500h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lexus LS 500h represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining flagship comfort with advanced hybrid technology. As a high-end luxury sedan, it is generally built to high standards of craftsmanship and reliability. Prospective buyers looking at a pre-owned model should review powertrain options and check owner-reported experiences to ensure a smooth ownership journey.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid Multi-Stage

This 3.5-liter V6 hybrid powertrain pairs gasoline combustion with electric motors to produce approximately 354 total horsepower. It delivers seamless power delivery and strong fuel efficiency for a large luxury sedan, though prospective buyers should inspect hybrid component health.

## Transmission

### Multi-Stage Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The Multi-Stage ECVT integrates a traditional automatic gear-stepping mechanism with hybrid electric drives to mimic a multi-speed transmission. It generally offers refined operation and robust durability suited for daily luxury driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V275000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Incorrect detection of an occupant may result in improper air bag deployment during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the front passenger seat, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus LS 500h is a sophisticated luxury hybrid offering impressive refinement and efficiency. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the hybrid battery system and general electronics to ensure everything operates as intended.
