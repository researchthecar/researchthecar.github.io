### Introduction

The 2023 Lexus LS500h is a full-size luxury sedan that offers a hybrid powertrain. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 354 horsepower and 350 lb-ft of torque. The LS500h is available in either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, and it comes standard with a 10-speed automatic transmission.

The LS500h is praised for its luxurious cabin, smooth ride, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

### Engine

**3.5-Liter V6 Hybrid**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, reduced engine power
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Water pump failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, noise from engine
Solution: Replace water pump
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**10-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting issues**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, transmission slipping out of gear
Solution: Software update, transmission repair or replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **No Issues Found**

### Electrical

* **Battery drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for a period of time
Solution: Replace battery, check for parasitic drain
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment system issues**
Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, navigation system not working, audio system not working
Solution: Software update, infotainment system replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **No Issues Found**

### Interior

* **Door panel rattling**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from door panels
Solution: Adjust door panels, replace door panel clips
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

### Conclusion

The 2023 Lexus LS500h is a luxurious sedan that offers a smooth ride and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Overall, the LS500h is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues that can occur.
