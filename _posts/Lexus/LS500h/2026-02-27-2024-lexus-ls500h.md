# 2024 Lexus LS500h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus LS 500h represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining flagship comfort with an advanced hybrid powertrain designed for refined cruising and efficiency. As a high-end luxury sedan, buyers expect exceptional build quality, advanced technology, and dependable daily operation. Because this vehicle has very few recorded complaints in its early lifecycle, prospective used-car buyers can generally approach it with confidence, though a pre-purchase inspection remains a wise step for any complex modern luxury flagship.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

This 3.5-liter V6 hybrid engine pairs with electric motors to produce a combined output of around 354 horsepower, delivering smooth and effortless acceleration. It is generally regarded as highly reliable, benefiting from Toyota and Lexus's long-standing expertise in hybrid propulsion systems.

## Transmission

### Multi-Stage Continuously Variable Transmission

The multi-stage continuously variable transmission integrates a conventional 4-speed automatic gear set with the hybrid system to simulate traditional gear shifts and eliminate the rubber-band feel of standard CVTs. It operates smoothly across various driving conditions and maintains strong dependability records.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V595000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An instrument panel display that does not show critical information can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel software over-the-air (OTA) for non-PHEV vehicles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Lexus LS 500h stands out as a sophisticated and exceptionally dependable choice in the full-size luxury hybrid sedan segment. With a powertrain known for longevity and a distinct lack of widespread owner complaints, it offers peace of mind alongside upscale comfort. Used-car shoppers should still verify vehicle history and ensure all routine maintenance has been properly performed.
