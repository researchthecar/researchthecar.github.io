# 2023 Lexus RZ450e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Lexus RZ450e is Lexus's first dedicated battery-electric luxury crossover, offering refined styling and a quiet, comfortable cabin. As a relatively new EV entry on the market, prospective used buyers should weigh its advanced technology and smooth ride against emerging owner feedback and initial model-year quirks typical of a completely new platform.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motor Setup (308 hp)

Equipped with a dual-motor electric powertrain generating an estimated 308 horsepower, utilizing front and rear permanent magnet synchronous motors. This system delivers brisk acceleration and all-wheel-drive capability with very few mechanical moving parts compared to traditional internal combustion engines.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Features a single-speed direct-drive automatic transmission paired with the dual electric motors. This direct-drive unit provides seamless, shift-free power delivery and generally requires minimal maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V577000: Visibility:Defroster/Defogger/Hvac System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RZ&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** During certain HVAC system faults, such as electrical compressor failure, the defroster and defogger system may become inoperative. An inoperative defroster and defogger system can decrease the driver's visibility under certain driving conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the HVAC control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RZ&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Lexus RZ450e presents an appealing option for used car buyers seeking luxury electric mobility without major mechanical hurdles in its initial run. While early adopter feedback should be kept in mind, its core electric powertrain components are fundamentally simple and robust, offering a quiet and serene driving experience.
