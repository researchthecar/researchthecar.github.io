# 2024 Lexus RZ450e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus RZ450e represents Lexus's entry into the battery-electric luxury crossover segment, offering refined styling and a quiet, comfortable cabin. As a relatively new EV model built on a dedicated electric platform, used car buyers can generally expect high build quality and standard luxury features, though early-production electric powertrains and modern electronic suites warrant a thorough inspection.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motor Setup (308 hp)

The 2024 Lexus RZ450e is equipped with a dual electric motor setup producing approximately 308 horsepower, utilizing Direct4 all-wheel drive. This powertrain delivers brisk, smooth acceleration with the quiet operation expected of an electric luxury vehicle.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Power is delivered to all four wheels via a single-speed direct drive transmission. This system provides seamless power delivery without traditional gear shifts, contributing to a reliable and low-maintenance driving experience.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V577000: Visibility:Defroster/Defogger/Hvac System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RZ&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** During certain HVAC system faults, such as electrical compressor failure, the defroster and defogger system may become inoperative. An inoperative defroster and defogger system can decrease the driver's visibility under certain driving conditions, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the HVAC control ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RZ&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Lexus RZ450e is a sophisticated luxury electric crossover backed by strong brand reputation and advanced technology. While early owner reports are limited, prospective buyers should verify all electronic systems and software updates function correctly before purchasing.
