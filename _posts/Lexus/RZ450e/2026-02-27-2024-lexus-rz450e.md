# 2024 Lexus RZ450e

### Overview
The 2024 Lexus RZ450e represents Lexus's commitment to electrification, built on the e-TNGA platform shared with Toyota's bZ4X. As a relatively new entrant in the premium electric SUV segment, early reliability data is still emerging. However, drawing from Lexus's established reputation for quality and the foundational technology from Toyota, the RZ450e is anticipated to offer a high level of dependability. Initial reports and the vehicle's design suggest a focus on robust battery management and durable electric powertrains. Owners can expect a refined driving experience with the typical Lexus attention to detail in interior comfort and build quality. While long-term performance is yet to be fully documented, the RZ450e is positioned to be a reliable choice in the growing EV market.

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Lexus RZ450e demonstrates strong potential for reliability, building on Lexus's legacy and Toyota's EV platform. While the electric powertrain and chassis components show no significant widespread issues, early data indicates potential concerns with the charging port, infotainment system's software stability, and the longevity of the 12V auxiliary battery. These are not uncommon for early-model EVs and are generally addressable through software updates or component replacements. Overall, the RZ450e is expected to be a dependable electric SUV, with most reported issues being minor or related to the evolving nature of EV technology.
