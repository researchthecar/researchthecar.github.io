# 2016 Lexus RC350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus RC 350 delivers a blend of luxury and sporty performance, backed by Lexus's strong reputation for overall dependability. While overall owner feedback for this model year is very sparse, prospective buyers should still carefully inspect core mechanical systems during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (306 hp, 277 lb-ft)

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 producing 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, this engine offers smooth power delivery and robust long-term durability. It is generally considered a highly reliable powerplant requiring standard routine maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel-drive configurations and delivers responsive gear shifts. It has a solid reputation for longevity when properly maintained.

### 6-Speed Automatic

Found in all-wheel-drive models, this 6-speed automatic transmission offers smooth, predictable shifting characteristics. It stands out as a dependable mechanical option for everyday driving.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system generally provides confident stopping power, though individual owner reports suggest monitoring for premature wear or noise issues.

**Brake Noise and Reduced Performance**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience loud noises from the braking system and a noticeable reduction in braking performance, even following component replacements.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the entire braking assembly, including pads, rotors, and calipers, to ensure proper installation and component quality.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus RC 350 is a compelling choice for used luxury coupe buyers, combining strong powertrain options with a historically dependable brand record. Prospective owners should perform a thorough test drive and mechanical inspection, paying specific attention to brake operation and service history.
