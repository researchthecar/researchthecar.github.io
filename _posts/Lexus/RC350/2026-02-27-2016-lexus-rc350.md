### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus RC350 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, aligning with Lexus's reputation for durability. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions (8-speed for RWD, 6-speed for AWD), demonstrates excellent longevity with no significant widespread mechanical failures reported. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning occasional infotainment system glitches and intermittent battery drain. These are typically manageable with software updates or component diagnosis and replacement, and do not detract significantly from the overall excellent reliability of the RC350.
