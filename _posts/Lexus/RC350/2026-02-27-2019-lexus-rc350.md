# 2019 Lexus RC350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus RC 350 delivers a blend of luxury, striking coupe styling, and grand-touring comfort. While overall ownership satisfaction tends to be high thanks to Lexus's strong reputation for build quality, prospective used-car buyers should still review common owner feedback and inspect specific areas like exterior glass during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

This 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 produces approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as a robust and dependable powerplant with a strong reputation for long-term mechanical durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel-drive configurations to provide smooth and responsive shifts. It maintains a solid track record for reliability when properly maintained.

### 6-Speed Multi-Mode Automatic

This 6-speed automatic transmission is utilized in all-wheel-drive models, delivering power smoothly across varying road conditions. It is known to be a durable unit requiring standard fluid maintenance.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Glass

Owner complaints highlight rare instances of unexpected glass failure, which is worth verifying during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience the sunroof glass shattering completely while driving at highway speeds without any prior warning or visible impact from outside objects.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for alignment and stress points, and replace the shattered glass panel with a new OEM unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus RC 350 remains a solid choice for a luxury performance coupe, backed by stout powertrain options and strong baseline reliability. Used-car shoppers should perform a standard inspection of all body panels and glass, but overall, this model offers a reassuring ownership experience.
