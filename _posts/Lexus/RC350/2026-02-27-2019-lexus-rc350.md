### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Lexus RC350 demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally durable. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. These issues are typically addressable through software updates or component replacement and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall dependability. Owners should be aware of these potential electrical quirks but can expect a reliable ownership experience.
