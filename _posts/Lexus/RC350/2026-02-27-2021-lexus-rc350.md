### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus RC350 demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions (8-speed for RWD, 6-speed for AWD), shows no significant widespread issues. Suspension and exterior components are also generally robust. The most commonly cited, though not necessarily widespread, concerns revolve around minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system and, in some instances, premature battery failure. These issues are typically manageable with software updates or standard component replacement. Overall, the RC350 is a dependable choice in its class, with potential buyers advised to be aware of the minor electrical and battery considerations.
