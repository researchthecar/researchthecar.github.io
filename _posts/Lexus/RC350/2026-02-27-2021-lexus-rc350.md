# 2021 Lexus RC350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus RC 350 carries forward the brand's strong reputation for craftsmanship and durability, offering a blend of luxury and sporty performance. While used-car shoppers generally find this coupe to be a dependable daily driver, it is always wise to evaluate powertrain options and inspect individual vehicle condition thoroughly prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (311 hp / 280 lb-ft)

This naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 delivers smooth power and strong acceleration paired with traditional Lexus mechanical durability. It generally serves as a highly reliable option for buyers seeking straightforward performance without forced induction complexities.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides crisp shifts for rear-wheel-drive configurations and maintains a solid long-term reliability track record. Regular fluid maintenance helps ensure smooth operation over high mileage.

### 6-Speed Electronically Controlled Automatic Transmission

Paired with all-wheel-drive variants, this 6-speed automatic prioritizes smooth engagement and mechanical sturdiness. It is widely considered a dependable gearbox for varied weather conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus RC 350 remains a solid choice in the luxury sport coupe segment, combining robust powertrain options with a refined interior. Prospective buyers should conduct a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and cosmetic health.
