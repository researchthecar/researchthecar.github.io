**Introduction to the 2020 Lexus RC350**

The Lexus RC350 is a luxury sports coupe that offers a captivating blend of style, performance, and technology. It boasts a naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 engine that generates 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. Power is routed through an eight-speed automatic transmission, delivering a smooth and responsive driving experience.

While the RC350 has received praise for its sleek design, refined interior, and energetic handling, some owners have encountered certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.5-liter V6**

  * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Symptoms include rough idling, engine misfiring, and reduced power. Solution: Replacement of failed fuel injector(s). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * **Timing Belt Failure:** Symptoms include loss of engine power, rattling or grinding noises, and engine stalling. Solution: Replacement of timing belt and associated components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Oil Pan Leak:** Symptoms include oil drips under the vehicle. Solution: Replacement of oil pan gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

  * **Gear Shift Delay:** Symptoms include slow or hesitant gear shifts. Solution: Software update or transmission control module replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * **Torque Converter Shudder:** Symptoms include vibrations during acceleration. Solution: Replacement of torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:** Symptoms include rattling or clunking noises over rough roads. Solution: Replacement of worn suspension components (e.g., ball joints, bushings). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle or complete electrical failure. Solution: Replacement of battery and inspection for current draw. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Symptoms include moisture build-up inside headlight assemblies. Solution: Replacement of headlight assemblies. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Symptoms include annoying rattling noises from the dashboard. Solution: Removal and reinstallation of dashboard components or dashboard replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Symptoms include intermittent failure or improper operation of the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system. Solution: Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2020 Lexus RC350 is generally a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but it is not immune to certain issues. While some problems are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important to consider these potential issues when purchasing or owning an RC350.
