# 2018 Lexus RC350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus RC 350 delivers luxury coupe styling with strong overall build quality and everyday drivability. While owner feedback is generally sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect standard wear items like brakes during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

This 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, utilizing both direct and port injection. It is widely regarded as a robust and dependable powerplant for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

An 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive, offering smooth shifts and responsive gear selection. It maintains a strong reputation for long-term mechanical durability.

### 6-Speed Automatic

A 6-speed automatic transmission equipped on all-wheel-drive variants, delivering reliable power delivery across varying road conditions. It is known for solid construction and minimal widespread issues.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Owner reports indicate occasional noise concerns with factory brake components.

**Brake Pad Squeaking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the factory brake pads produce an annoying squeaking noise during normal operation, mimicking worn pads.
- **Solution:** Some dealerships address the noise by replacing the factory brake pads with updated components, though minor brake noise can sometimes be considered a normal characteristic.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus RC 350 is a dependable luxury sports coupe backed by solid powertrain options and high build standards. Buyers should perform a standard test drive and inspection to verify that minor wear items, such as the brakes, operate quietly and smoothly.
