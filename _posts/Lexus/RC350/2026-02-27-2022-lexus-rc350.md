While specific widespread mechanical failures are uncommon, like any vehicle, potential owners should be aware of minor electrical gremlins and the typical considerations for a performance-oriented vehicle. The RC350 aims to balance sporty dynamics with the comfort and dependability expected from the Lexus brand, making it a strong contender in its segment for those prioritizing long-term ownership satisfaction.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus RC350 demonstrates strong reliability, aligning with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and rare instances of battery drain, which are typically resolvable with software updates or professional diagnostics. Overall, the RC350 is a dependable choice for a luxury sports coupe, with a low probability of major mechanical failures.
