# 2022 Lexus RC350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lexus RC 350 delivers luxury coupe styling paired with Lexus's renowned build quality and a strong reputation for dependability. As a used luxury purchase, it typically offers a more trouble-free ownership experience than many of its German competitors, provided routine maintenance is kept up to date. Prospective buyers should still inspect the vehicle thoroughly, as even highly reliable luxury platforms can occasionally exhibit isolated owner-reported quirks.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, utilizing both port and direct injection. It is widely regarded as a robust and long-lasting powerplant with a strong track record for reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission features steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters and is paired with rear-wheel drive. It offers smooth, refined shifting and generally proves durable under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus RC 350 stands out as a solid choice in the used luxury coupe segment, offering a compelling blend of everyday drivability, upscale comfort, and dependable mechanics. While owner reports for this model year are minimal, standard used-car due diligence—such as reviewing maintenance records and conducting a pre-purchase inspection—remains essential before finalizing any deal.
