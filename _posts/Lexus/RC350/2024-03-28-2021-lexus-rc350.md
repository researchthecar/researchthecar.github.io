**Introduction**

The 2021 Lexus RC350 is a luxury coupe with a powerful V6 engine and a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable ride and a spacious cabin, but it has some critics for its lack of performance and fuel efficiency. Other engine and transmission options include a 3.5-liter V6 engine with 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. People like the RC350 for its stylish design, luxurious interior, and smooth ride, but they dislike its lack of performance and fuel efficiency.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**

* **Timing Chain Rattle:**
  - Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the engine at startup or acceleration.
  - Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning oil smell and smoke.
  - Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The transmission slipping out of gear or hesitating during shifts.
  - Solution: Replace the transmission solenoid or valve body.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: A clunking or rattling noise from the front or rear suspension, especially over bumps.
  - Solution: Replace the strut mount.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per corner
  - Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar Link Wear:**
  - Description/Symptoms: A rattling or clunking noise from the suspension, especially during turns.
  - Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per side
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Bulb Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: One or more headlight bulbs burning out prematurely.
  - Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per bulb
  - Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The battery draining excessively, even when the vehicle is turned off.
  - Solution: Replace the battery or check for parasitic drain.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Paint Peeling:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The paint on the exterior door handles peeling or chipping.
  - Solution: Touch up the paint or replace the door handle.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per handle
  - Recalls: None Found
* **Tail Light Condensation:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation inside the tail lights.
  - Solution: Replace the tail light housing.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per tail light
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The climate control system not blowing cold air or not adjusting the temperature properly.
  - Solution: Replace the climate control module or blend door actuator.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
  - Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Screen Freezing:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system screen freezing or becoming unresponsive.
  - Solution: Reset the infotainment system or replace the screen.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Lexus RC350 is a stylish and luxurious coupe, but it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most common issues are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension, and they can be expensive to repair. However, many of the issues are minor and can be resolved relatively easily. Overall, the RC350 is a reliable vehicle with a good reputation.
