**Introduction:**

The 2022 Lexus RC350 is a sports coupe powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The RC350 is praised for its sharp handling, luxurious interior, and stylish exterior. However, some owners have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**

* **V6 3.5L**
    * **Piston Ring Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust, loss of power
        * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * **Coolant Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Low coolant level, overheating, visible leak from engine
        * Solution: Radiator or hose replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * **Timing Chain Rattle:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy rattle or ticking sound from engine at startup or acceleration
        * Solution: Timing chain or tensioner replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears
        * Solution: Transmission fluid change or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500
    * **Torque Converter Shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shaking when accelerating
        * Solution: Torque converter replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noise from front suspension, loose or worn bushings
    * Solution: Control arm replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700 per side
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking or inoperative shock absorbers, poor ride quality
    * Solution: Shock absorber replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per side

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Battery or alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
* **Infotainment Screen Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blank screen, freezing, unresponsive touchscreen
    * Solution: Software update or screen replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Sunroof seal replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Vibration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration in steering wheel at high speeds
    * Solution: Wheel balancing or tire rotation
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150

**Summary:**

While the 2022 Lexus RC350 generally receives positive reviews, it is not without its issues. Some engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, but most other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the RC350 offers a balance of performance, luxury, and reliability, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
