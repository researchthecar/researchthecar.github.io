# 2020 Lexus RC350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lexus RC 350 delivers a blend of luxury and sporty performance, backed by Lexus's strong reputation for overall dependability. Prospective buyers looking at this luxury coupe should still evaluate vehicle history and standard maintenance records carefully during their pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (311 hp, 280 lb-ft)

This 3.5-liter V6 engine generates 311 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth power delivery and refined driving dynamics. It is widely regarded as a very reliable powertrain option for buyers seeking long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission comes paired with rear-wheel-drive configurations and offers crisp, responsive gear shifts. It generally exhibits solid reliability with minimal reported owner complaints.

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission is utilized on all-wheel-drive models, providing smooth power distribution across varying road conditions. It maintains a strong track record for mechanical dependability.

## Suspension

### Tires and Wheels

Suspension and wheel components are generally robust, though individual configuration errors can occur.

**Incorrect Tire Installation**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported taking a vehicle in for service only to discover incorrect tire sizes were installed, which complicated routine maintenance and servicing.
- **Solution:** Verify that the vehicle is fitted with the correct manufacturer-specified tire dimensions during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus RC 350 is a dependable luxury sports coupe choice, benefiting from proven powertrain engineering. Buyers should perform a thorough visual and mechanical inspection, paying close attention to tire specifications and vehicle history records.
