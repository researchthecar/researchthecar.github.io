**Introduction to the 2015 Lexus RC350**

The 2015 Lexus RC350 is a midsize luxury sports coupe loved by owners for its stylish design, potent naturally aspirated V6 engine, and agile handling. It is powered by a 3.5L 2GR-FKS V6 engine mated to an 8-speed Sport Direct Shift automatic transmission, producing 306 hp and 277 lb-ft of torque.

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Lexus RC350 has relatively few reported issues. The most common problems include carbon buildup on the valvetrain and power seat malfunctions. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are not widespread and should not significantly impact the overall reliability of the vehicle.
