### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus RC350 generally exhibits excellent reliability, aligning with Lexus's reputation for building durable vehicles. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and its associated automatic transmissions, is robust and rarely presents significant issues. While the chassis and exterior components are typically trouble-free, some owners have reported minor electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and occasional battery drain issues. These are not widespread, but potential buyers should be aware. Overall, the RC350 is a solid choice for those seeking a stylish and dependable luxury coupe.
