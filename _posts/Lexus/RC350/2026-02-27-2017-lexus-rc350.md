# 2017 Lexus RC350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus RC 350 combines luxury coupe styling with the brand's renowned build quality and engineering pedigree. For used car shoppers, it generally offers a dependable ownership experience backed by solid mechanical underpinnings. While owner feedback is exceptionally light for this model year, buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to catch any isolated mechanical or electrical quirks.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

This 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 produces roughly 306 horsepower and 277 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and responsive acceleration. It is widely regarded as a highly durable engine option with a strong track record for long-term reliability.

### Fuel Pump

On-board electrical and fuel delivery systems are generally dependable, though occasional component noise reports warrant inspection.

**Abnormal Fuel Pump Noise**
- **Symptoms:** An intermittent, abnormally loud buzzing or humming noise may emanate from the rear of the vehicle near the fuel tank area.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel pump assembly and replace the unit if internal mechanical wear or excessive noise is confirmed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic

Paired with rear-wheel drive models, this 8-speed automatic delivers quick, seamless gear changes to balance performance and efficiency. It is generally a robust unit with few reported systemic failure trends.

### 6-Speed Sequential Automatic

Utilized in all-wheel drive configurations, this 6-speed automatic prioritizes smooth power delivery across varying road conditions. It maintains traditional Lexus standards for mechanical longevity.

## Suspension

### Rear Shocks

The suspension balances sporty handling with a composed ride, though isolated damping component failures can occasionally occur.

**Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Both rear shock absorbers may fail simultaneously, resulting in a complete loss of rebound control and making the vehicle difficult to handle on uneven surfaces or during cornering.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the rear suspension dampers and replace both rear shock absorbers as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus RC 350 stands out as a reliable and stylish choice in the luxury sport coupe segment. Major mechanical issues are rare, though buyers should verify the condition of the suspension and listen for any unusual noises during a test drive. Overall, standard pre-purchase due diligence will help ensure a rewarding and trouble-free ownership experience.
