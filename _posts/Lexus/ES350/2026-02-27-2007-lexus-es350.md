# 2007 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2007 Lexus ES350 is generally regarded as a reliable luxury sedan, benefiting from Toyota's robust engineering and quality control. It typically offers a comfortable ride and a well-appointed interior. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that may arise, providing insights into potential repair areas and associated costs for proactive maintenance or troubleshooting.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FE)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil residue around the engine, burning oil smell, potential for oil drips under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Carbon Buildup (Intake Valves)**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, rough idle, potential for check engine light.
- **Solution:** Professional intake valve cleaning service.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Power Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inoperative power windows, slow window operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500 per window

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Lexus ES350 demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The 3.5L V6 engine is generally robust, though occasional oil leaks from valve cover gaskets and potential carbon buildup on intake valves are noted. The transmission and suspension systems are typically trouble-free. Electrical issues, such as power window motor failures and infotainment system glitches, are the most commonly reported non-routine problems. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well. With routine maintenance and attention to these specific potential issues, the ES350 should provide many years of dependable service.
