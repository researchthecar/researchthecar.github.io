**2019 Lexus ES350**

The 2019 Lexus ES350 is a midsize luxury sedan powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It delivers 302 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. The ES350 is praised for its smooth ride, comfortable cabin, and impressive fuel economy. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Suspension**

* **Premature Wear of Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clicking/popping sound from the front suspension, especially when going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Excessive Noise from Rear Trailing Arms**
    * Description/Symptoms: Thumping or banging noise from the rear suspension when driving over rough roads or when braking.
    * Solution: Lubrication and adjustment of the trailing arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning Air Conditioning Compressor**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inadequate cooling or intermittent AC operation.
    * Solution: Replacement of the compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Defective Headlight Wiring Harness**
    * Description/Symptoms: Flickering or dimmed headlights, or complete headlight failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the faulty wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Faulty Door Handle Chrome Trim**

    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Excessive Creaking Noise from Dashboard**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or popping sound coming from the dashboard, particularly in hot or cold conditions.
    * Solution: Application of a lubricant or soundproofing material to the affected area.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Lexus ES350 is a solid and reliable vehicle. However, some owners have encountered various issues, ranging from suspension noise to faulty interior components. The severity of the issues vary, and the cost of repairs can be significant. It is important to consider these potential issues when evaluating the vehicle for purchase or maintenance.
