## 2014 Lexus ES350 Comprehensive Vehicle Issues List

The 2014 Lexus ES350 is a luxurious sedan that seats five and is offered in various trim levels. It comes standard with a 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 268 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Other available engine options include a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine and a 3.5-liter V6 engine with hybrid technology. The ES350 is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, it has received some criticism for its uninspiring handling and lackluster fuel economy.

### Engine
**3.5-liter V6 Engine**

**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover area, causing a burning oil smell and potential engine damage.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Timing Belt Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing belt tensioner can fail, leading to a loss of engine power and potential catastrophic engine damage.
Solution: Replace the timing belt and tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission
**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

**Jerky Gear Shifts**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience jerky or rough gear shifts, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module (TCM) or replace the transmission fluid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

**No Issues Found**

### Electrical

**Radio Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The radio may experience various malfunctions, including loss of sound, distorted audio, or inoperability.
Solution: Replace the radio head unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
Solution: Identify and fix the source of the electrical drain (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic draw).
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies based on the cause
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

**No Issues Found**

### Interior

**Heated Seat Failure**
Description/Symptoms: One or both heated seats may fail to warm up properly.
Solution: Replace the heating element or control switch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Panoramic Moonroof Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: The panoramic moonroof may rattle or squeak while driving, especially over rough roads.
Solution: Install a wind deflector or adjust the moonroof alignment.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
Recalls: None Found

## Vehicle Summary

Overall, the 2014 Lexus ES350 is a reliable sedan with a comfortable and well-equipped interior. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the more common problems include valve cover gasket leaks, timing belt tensioner failures, and jerky gear shifts. The cost to fix these issues can range from minor to moderate.
