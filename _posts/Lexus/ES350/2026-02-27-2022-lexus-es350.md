# 2022 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus ES 350 is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its exceptional reliability and comfortable ride. Built on Toyota's global platform, it benefits from a robust engineering heritage. The ES 350 typically features a potent V6 engine paired with a smooth automatic transmission, offering a refined driving experience. Owners generally report high satisfaction with its build quality and long-term durability. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the ES 350 consistently ranks well in reliability surveys, indicating a well-built and dependable automobile with a focus on minimizing common automotive problems.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus ES 350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its powertrain, consisting of the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. The chassis and suspension components also demonstrate strong durability. Minor electrical concerns, primarily related to occasional infotainment system glitches, are the most commonly cited, but these are typically resolved with software updates. Exterior and interior components are generally free from significant defects. Overall, the ES 350 offers a dependable ownership experience with a low probability of major mechanical or electrical failures.
