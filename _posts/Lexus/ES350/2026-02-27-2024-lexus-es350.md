# 2024 Lexus ES350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus ES350 continues the model's tradition of offering a refined, quiet ride and a luxurious interior backed by strong Toyota reliability. As a relatively new model year, it benefits from mature powertrain engineering and modern safety tech, though used buyers should always verify vehicle history and ensure routine maintenance has been performed.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

This 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and robust acceleration. It is widely regarded as a highly durable and dependable powerplant with a strong track record of longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes paired with front-wheel drive. It is designed to optimize both fuel efficiency and highway cruising comfort while maintaining solid mechanical dependability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V124000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to incorrect programming of the parking-assist electronic control unit (ECU) software, the rearview camera image may not immediately display within a couple of seconds after the shifter is placed into reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
