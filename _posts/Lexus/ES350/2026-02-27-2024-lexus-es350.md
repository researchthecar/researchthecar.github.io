# 2024 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2024 Lexus ES 350 is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its exceptional reliability and refined driving experience. Built on Toyota's global platform, it inherits a legacy of robust engineering and meticulous quality control. The ES 350 typically features a potent V6 engine paired with a smooth automatic transmission, offering a blend of performance and efficiency. Its interior is crafted with premium materials, and the exterior boasts elegant styling. Lexus's commitment to customer satisfaction is reflected in its strong performance in various reliability surveys, making it a compelling choice for buyers prioritizing dependability and long-term value. ### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Lexus ES 350 demonstrates exceptional reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. Suspension and exterior components also appear to be very durable. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is the electrical system, specifically occasional, minor glitches within the infotainment system that are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the ES 350 is a highly dependable luxury sedan, making it an excellent choice for consumers prioritizing long-term ownership satisfaction and minimal unscheduled repairs.
