# 2021 Lexus ES350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus ES 350 continues the nameplate's reputation for luxury, comfort, and refined highway cruising. For prospective used-car buyers, it represents a generally dependable luxury sedan choice with relatively few widespread mechanical complaints logged in early ownership data. While overall owner satisfaction remains high, buyers should still review maintenance histories and check for any outstanding safety recalls during their shopping process.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

This model features a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 302 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth, responsive acceleration and is widely regarded as a very reliable powertrain option for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is sent to the front wheels through an 8-speed automatic transmission. It offers seamless gear changes designed to optimize both highway efficiency and responsive low-end power delivery.

## Steering

### Steering System

Steering complaints are rare, though isolated electronic assist failures have been reported by individual owners.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a warning light and a sudden loss of power assist, making the steering wheel notably difficult to turn in either direction.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician or dealership to diagnose electronic power steering module or sensor fault codes.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Air bag components are subject to manufacturer safety campaigns that require timely dealer verification.

**Air Bag Recall Part Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported receiving recall notices regarding air bag components while experiencing extended wait times for replacement parts to become available at dealerships.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized dealership with your Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) to check recall status and schedule repairs as soon as replacement parts are stocked.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus ES 350 stands out as a solid used luxury sedan option, backed by a proven V6 powertrain and high baseline build quality. While isolated owner reports note steering assist warnings and pending safety recall remedies, these issues do not detract from the vehicle's strong overall reputation for reliability. Used car shoppers should verify that all open safety campaigns have been completed prior to purchase.
