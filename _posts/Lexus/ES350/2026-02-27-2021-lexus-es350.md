# 2021 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus ES350 is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its comfortable ride, refined interior, and strong reputation for reliability. Built on Toyota's global architecture platform, it benefits from proven engineering and quality control. The ES350 typically comes with a potent V6 engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission, offering a smooth and responsive driving experience. Owners generally report high satisfaction with its durability and low incidence of significant mechanical problems, making it a solid choice for those seeking a dependable luxury vehicle. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus ES350 demonstrates excellent technical reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand promise. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. Suspension components also hold up well. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the infotainment system, which can occasionally experience software glitches, and a few reports of premature battery failure. These are generally minor and manageable issues, not indicative of systemic problems. Overall, the ES350 is a highly dependable vehicle with a low probability of significant mechanical or electrical failures.
