**Introduction:**

The 2020 Lexus ES350 is a premium midsize sedan powered by a 3.5L V6 engine that produces 302 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The ES350 is known for its smooth ride, luxurious interior, and impressive safety features. However, there have been several reported issues with this vehicle.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

**Engine Oil Leak:**
_Description/Symptoms:_ Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket or oil pan gasket. Symptoms may include visible oil stains under the vehicle or a burning oil smell.
_Solution:_ Replace the leaking gasket.
_Estimated Cost to Fix:_ $400 - $600+
_Recalls:_ None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Hesitation during Gear Shifts:**
_Description/Symptoms:_ The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears, especially at low speeds.
_Solution:_ Update the transmission control module software.
_Estimated Cost to Fix:_ $0 - $200
_Recalls:_ None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
_Description/Symptoms:_ The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is parked and turned off.
_Solution:_ Diagnose and repair the electrical system component causing the drain.
_Estimated Cost to Fix:_ $200 - $600+
_Recalls:_ None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation:**
_Description/Symptoms:_ Moisture or condensation may accumulate inside the headlight assembly.
_Solution:_ Replace the affected headlight.
_Estimated Cost to Fix:_ $500 - $1000+
_Recalls:_ None Found

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Glitches:**
_Description/Symptoms:_ The infotainment system may experience glitches, such as freezing or unresponsive touch controls.
_Solution:_ Update the infotainment system software or replace the unit if necessary.
_Estimated Cost to Fix:_ $0 - $1000+
_Recalls:_ None Found

**Summary:**

The 2020 Lexus ES350 has some reported issues, but they are generally considered minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. The most common issues are an engine oil leak, transmission hesitation, and headlight condensation. Overall, the ES350 is still a reliable and luxurious midsize sedan.
