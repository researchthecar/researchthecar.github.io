**2007 Lexus ES350**

The 2007 Lexus ES350 is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable ride, a spacious cabin, and various luxury features. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**
- **Oil Consumption**: Some owners have experienced excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  - **Solution**: The problem may require replacing the piston rings or valve stem seals, which could cost $2,000 - $4,000+ to fix.
  - **Recalls**: None Found
- **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**: Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause rough idling, decreased power, and reduced fuel economy.
  - **Solution**: Cleaning the intake valves is necessary to resolve the issue, costing around $500 - $1,000.
  - **Recalls**: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**
- **Transmission Slippage**: Some owners have reported instances of transmission slippage, resulting in difficulty shifting gears or loss of power.
  - **Solution**: Repairing or rebuilding the transmission may be necessary, potentially costing $2,500 - $5,000+.
  - **Recalls**: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Suspension Noise**: Noise from the front suspension, such as clunking or rattling, has been reported on some ES350s.
  - **Solution**: Replacing worn or damaged suspension components, such as control arms or struts, can resolve the issue, costing $500 - $1,500+.
  - **Recalls**: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Failure**: Some owners have encountered premature battery failure, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
  - **Solution**: Replacing the battery is a straightforward repair, typically costing $200 - $500.
  - **Recalls**: None Found
- **Electrical System Malfunctions**: Electrical system malfunctions, such as faulty wiring or component failures, have been reported in some ES350s.
  - **Solution**: Diagnosis and repairs vary depending on the specific issue, with costs varying widely.
  - **Recalls**: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Headlight Condensation**: Some owners have reported condensation forming inside the headlights after heavy rain or car washes.
  - **Solution**: Replacing the headlight assemblies is often necessary to resolve the issue, costing $500 - $1,000+ per assembly.
  - **Recalls**: None Found

**Interior**
- **Creaking Center Console**: Some owners have experienced creaking or rattling noises coming from the center console.
  - **Solution**: Tightening or replacing the console assembly may be necessary, costing $200 - $500.
  - **Recalls**: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Lexus ES350 is generally a reliable and luxurious vehicle, but it is not without its potential issues. While some reported problems are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as engine oil consumption and transmission slippage, can be more severe and costly to repair. Overall, it is essential to weigh the potential risks against the vehicle's positive attributes when considering a used ES350.
