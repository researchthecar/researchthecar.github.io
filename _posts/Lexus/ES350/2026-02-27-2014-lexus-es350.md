# 2014 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2014 Lexus ES 350 is generally recognized for its strong reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. This mid-size luxury sedan is built upon a robust platform, featuring a smooth and powerful V6 engine paired with a well-regarded automatic transmission. Owners typically experience a dependable driving experience with fewer unexpected repairs compared to many competitors in its class. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the ES 350's design and engineering prioritize longevity and owner satisfaction, making it a solid choice for those seeking a comfortable and reliable luxury sedan.

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Screen Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive, or display minor graphical anomalies.
- **Solution:** Software updates or a system reset may resolve the issue. In rare cases, component replacement might be necessary.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** The battery may drain prematurely, leading to starting issues. This can sometimes be linked to aftermarket accessories or specific modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the parasitic draw. May involve checking modules, fuses, or accessory installations.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Lexus ES 350 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle. Its powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. The chassis and suspension components also demonstrate strong durability. Minor electrical concerns, such as occasional infotainment glitches and rare instances of battery drain, are the most commonly cited, though these are generally manageable. Interior and exterior components are also well-built, with the primary interior concern being potential blend door actuator issues within the climate control system. Overall, the ES 350 offers a dependable ownership experience with a low probability of major mechanical failures.
