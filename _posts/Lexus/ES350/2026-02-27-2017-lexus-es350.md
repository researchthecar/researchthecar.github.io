# 2017 Lexus ES350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus ES350 continues the model's long-standing reputation for luxury, comfort, and exceptional mechanical refinement. As a used vehicle, it generally provides a serene ride quality and strong reliability, though prospective buyers should review a few owner-reported electrical, exterior, and safety system concerns before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FE / 2GR-FKS)

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 268 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and responsive acceleration. This powertrain is widely regarded as exceptionally durable and dependable for high-mileage ownership.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the front wheels with refined shift logic. It has a strong track record of longevity and requires standard fluid maintenance.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical accessories and cabin technology generally function well, though occasional software glitches or telematics disconnects have been noted by owners.

**Safety Connect and Feature Disconnection**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported issues related to the vehicle's Safety Connect telematics system disconnecting or losing functionality.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or qualified technician inspect the telematics module, software version, and cellular subscription status.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body panels and trim are generally robust, though rare instances of spontaneous sunroof glass failure have been documented.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the factory glass sunroof shattered or exploded suddenly while driving at highway speeds without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress cracks and replace the glass assembly with OEM components if damage occurs.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Advanced safety and driver-assistance features provide added peace of mind, though a small number of owners have reported sensor warnings or unexpected system behavior.

**Airbag Non-Deployment in Collisions**
- **Symptoms:** Owners reported that frontal or rear-end collisions occurred without subsequent deployment of the supplemental restraint airbags, accompanied by warning indicator lights.
- **Solution:** Have the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) diagnostic codes read by a certified repair facility to inspect sensors and control modules.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V415000: Steering:Linkages:Tie Rod Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES350&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the lock nut loosens, the tie rod may separate causing a loss of steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the lock nut and, if it is found loose, replace the lock nut, steering gear assembly, and tie rod assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus ES350 is a comfortable, well-built luxury sedan that offers strong powertrain reliability and a quiet cabin. While most owners experience trouble-free driving, used car shoppers should carefully inspect the vehicle history, check all electronic features, and ensure there are no signs of glass or structural issues before finalizing a purchase.
