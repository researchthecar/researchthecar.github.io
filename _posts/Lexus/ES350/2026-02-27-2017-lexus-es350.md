# 2017 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus ES 350 is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its comfortable ride, quiet cabin, and strong reputation for reliability. It typically comes equipped with a potent V6 engine and a smooth-shifting automatic transmission, offering a refined driving experience. This model year generally benefits from Lexus's commitment to quality engineering and rigorous testing, leading to a vehicle that often requires minimal unscheduled repairs. Owners frequently praise its long-term durability and low cost of ownership, especially when compared to other vehicles in its class. The ES 350 is a solid choice for those prioritizing comfort, quietness, and dependable transportation.
