# 2018 Lexus ES350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus ES350 is generally known for its high build quality and dependable luxury, but used-car buyers should still review owner-reported concerns. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, a small number of drivers have flagged isolated issues ranging from unexpected engine behavior to minor body glass stress. As with any pre-owned luxury sedan, a thorough inspection and test drive remain essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2018 Lexus ES350 is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 268 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its smooth power delivery and strong long-term durability.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the engine unexpectedly shuts off while driving at speed, followed by a delayed restart a few seconds later.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electronic throttle body, fuel delivery system, and diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint any underlying electrical or sensor faults.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive, this powertrain shifts smoothly and contributes to the car's refined highway cruising character with very few reported reliability concerns.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Standard braking components generally offer solid performance, though routine checks of pads and fluid are recommended.

**Brake System Behavior**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have noted unexpected vehicle movement or hesitation while stopped in traffic, though specific mechanical failures are rare.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and the anti-lock braking system modules during routine maintenance to ensure proper clamping force and pedal response.

## Exterior

### Windshield

Exterior body panels and glass are generally durable, though environmental stress and extreme temperature swings can occasionally cause glass damage.

**Windshield Stress Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** In extreme cabin and ambient temperature conditions, glass can develop sudden cracks or splits, particularly when rapid climate adjustments are made.
- **Solution:** Check the windshield for pre-existing chips or stress fractures, and have damaged glass replaced by a professional technician using OEM-spec materials.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES350&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus ES350 remains a dependable and luxurious choice for used-car shoppers, backed by a strong reputation for powertrain longevity. While isolated owner complaints highlight rare electronic or glass concerns, they do not point to widespread systemic defects. Buyers should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection to verify vehicle history and overall mechanical health.
