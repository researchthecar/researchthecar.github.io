# 2016 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2016 Lexus ES 350 is generally recognized for its strong reliability, a hallmark of the Lexus brand. This mid-size luxury sedan typically offers a smooth and comfortable driving experience, backed by robust engineering. Owners often praise its quiet cabin, refined ride quality, and the dependable performance of its powertrain. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the ES 350 from this model year tends to exhibit fewer significant problems compared to many competitors. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its longevity, but major mechanical or electrical failures are not commonly reported, contributing to its reputation as a solid choice for those seeking a dependable luxury vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus ES 350 stands out as a highly reliable luxury sedan. Its powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. The suspension system also generally performs without major problems. Minor concerns are primarily limited to the electrical system, such as occasional infotainment glitches or premature battery failure, and some interior wear on high-mileage units. Overall, the ES 350 offers excellent long-term dependability, making it a sound investment for buyers prioritizing reliability and comfort.
