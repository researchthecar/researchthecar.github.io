# 2016 Lexus ES350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus ES 350 is widely recognized for its plush ride, upscale cabin, and strong reputation for overall dependability. While major mechanical failures are relatively uncommon compared to other luxury sedans of its era, used car shoppers should review minor owner-reported complaints regarding body components and select electrical quirks before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 268 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, this model delivers smooth and dependable performance. The engine itself is fundamentally robust, though high-mileage examples may occasionally require standard accessory belt or water pump maintenance.

**Engine Accessory Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear an intermittent rattling noise coming from the front or side of the engine bay, often attributed to the water pump pulley.
- **Solution:** Have a mechanic inspect the accessory drive belts and pulleys, and replace the water pump or tensioner if bearing wear is detected.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a conventional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive, offering seamless gear shifts and a durable track record. Routine fluid checks are generally all that is needed to keep this unit running smoothly.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel and Column

The steering system provides a comfortable and isolated feel, though wheel vibrations at highway speeds occasionally require balancing or alignment corrections.

**High-Speed Steering Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** A noticeable vibration or shimmy is felt through the steering wheel when cruising at highway speeds above 65 mph, persisting even after tire balancing.
- **Solution:** Check wheel balance, alignment, and suspension components for uneven wear or minor imbalances.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior panels and structure are generally well-assembled, though owners have reported occasional isolated incidents involving sunroof glass breakage.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud pop resembling a gunshot can be heard while driving, followed by the sunroof glass suddenly shattering and scattering fragments into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass panel and inspect the frame alignment for any stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V198000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES350&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are equipped with an ABS actuator that may have a damaged O-ring which could result in improper brake fluid pressure control during ABS, Traction Control, or Stability Control activation. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 126, "Electronic Stability Control.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the brake actuator, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus ES 350 remains a solid, comfortable choice for used luxury sedan buyers thanks to its refined V6 powertrain and high comfort levels. Prospective owners should perform a thorough test drive to check for high-speed steering vibrations and inspect glass and accessory components during a pre-purchase inspection.
