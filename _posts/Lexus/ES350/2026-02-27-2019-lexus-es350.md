# 2019 Lexus ES350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus ES 350 delivers the plush ride, quiet cabin, and exceptional build quality that luxury sedan buyers expect. Underpinned by a strong reputation for dependability, it remains a compelling choice on the used market for those prioritizing comfort and long-term mechanical endurance.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 302 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and responsive acceleration. It is widely regarded as a highly durable and low-maintenance powerplant for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the front wheels with well-timed gear changes. It generally exhibits strong mechanical reliability with very few widespread owner complaints.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES350&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V012000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES350&modelYear=2019)**

The 2019 Lexus ES 350 maintains a stellar reputation for reliability with minimal widespread complaints across its major mechanical systems. Prospective used car buyers can approach this luxury sedan with confidence, provided standard pre-purchase inspections are completed.
