# 2019 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2019 Lexus ES 350 is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its exceptional reliability and comfortable ride. Built on Toyota's global platform, it shares many components with other highly-rated Toyota and Lexus models, contributing to its strong reputation. The ES 350 typically features a robust V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission, designed for longevity and minimal maintenance beyond routine service. Owners generally report a high degree of satisfaction with the vehicle's build quality and the absence of significant mechanical or electrical problems, making it a dependable choice in its segment. Its focus on refinement and durability translates into a low incidence of unexpected repairs.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Lexus ES 350 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally trouble-free. The most commonly cited, though still infrequent, issues relate to minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system, which are often resolved with software updates, and the standard wear-and-tear item of the battery, which can occasionally require replacement sooner than expected. Overall, the ES 350 offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal risk of major mechanical failures.
