# 2020 Lexus ES350

### Overview
The 2020 Lexus ES 350 is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its comfortable ride, refined interior, and strong reputation for reliability. Powered by a robust V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission, it generally offers a dependable ownership experience. Lexus's commitment to quality is evident in the ES 350's construction and components, leading to fewer unexpected issues compared to many competitors. While no vehicle is entirely immune to problems, the ES 350 typically requires only routine maintenance, making it a solid choice for those prioritizing long-term dependability and low ownership costs.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Lexus ES 350 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing exceptional durability. Electrical systems, particularly the infotainment, may occasionally exhibit minor software-related glitches, and like any car, the battery is a wear item that may need replacement within its expected lifespan. However, these are generally minor and infrequent issues. The exterior and interior components are also well-built, contributing to the ES 350's reputation as a dependable luxury sedan with low long-term ownership concerns.
