# 2020 Lexus ES350 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lexus ES350 continues the model's reputation for exceptional comfort, refined road manners, and strong build quality. Buyers shopping for a used model will generally find a luxurious and dependable sedan, though looking over owner reports reveals a few specific items to watch out for, ranging from minor electrical quirks to isolated powertrain and braking concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (2GR-FKS)

Equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 302 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, paired with front-wheel drive. This engine is generally robust and long-lasting, though rare gasket leaks can occur.

**Timing Chain Cover Oil Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil can begin leaking from the front timing chain cover area at moderate mileage.
- **Solution:** Requires cleaning the sealing surface and resealing the timing cover with fresh gasket sealant.

## Transmission

### Direct Shift 8-Speed Automatic

Uses an 8-speed automatic transmission delivering smooth power delivery to the front wheels. It is widely considered dependable, with few widespread mechanical complaints reported by owners.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake

Braking performance is normally secure, but owners have reported rare mechanical behavior from the parking brake mechanism.

**Parking Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic parking brake may jerk or fail to hold the vehicle securely on an incline.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic parking brake actuator and switch, and replace components if mechanical or electrical failure is found.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Electrical systems are generally modern and feature-rich, though isolated battery longevity issues have been noted by some drivers.

**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience frequent battery failures or require replacement sooner than expected.
- **Solution:** Test the charging system and battery draw; replace the battery and address any parasitic drains if present.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES350&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus ES350 remains a highly recommended used luxury sedan due to its renowned comfort, reliability, and strong V6 performance. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential battery wear, parking brake operation, and occasional front timing chain cover leaks, these issues do not appear to represent widespread systemic failure. Used car buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is performed, verifying all electronics and powertrain components are in top shape.
