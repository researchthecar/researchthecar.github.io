### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus GS F demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The core powertrain (5.0L V8 and 8-speed automatic transmission) is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. Suspension components also appear to be durable. Minor electrical concerns, primarily related to infotainment system glitches, are the most commonly cited, usually resolvable with software updates. Premature battery failure is a rare but possible issue. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well. For owners, proactive maintenance and addressing any minor electrical quirks promptly will ensure continued dependable performance from this enthusiast-focused sedan.
