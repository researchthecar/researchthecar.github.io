# 2017 Lexus GS F Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus GS F blends luxury executive sedan comfort with high-performance V8 capability, making it a compelling option for used-car buyers looking for rear-wheel-drive performance with Lexus-renowned build quality. Because this low-volume performance variant shares much of its robust engineering with the broader Lexus lineup, it generally avoids widespread mechanical grievances. Prospective buyers should still review maintenance histories closely, as high-performance driving components like brakes and tires wear significantly faster than standard luxury trims.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces 467 horsepower and 389 lb-ft of torque, offering strong linear power delivery and generally exceptional reliability. Routine maintenance and timely fluid changes are critical to keeping this high-revving performance powerplant running smoothly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features manual paddle shifters and aggressive shift logic tuned for the GS F's performance mission. It is generally durable under normal operating conditions, though buyers should verify smooth engagement across all modes during a test drive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V107000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS+F&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A cracked cover may leak fuel, which, in the presence of an ignition source, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus GS F stands out as a remarkably dependable choice in the high-performance luxury sedan segment, largely insulated from the systemic reliability concerns plaguing European rivals. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, buyers should prioritize a pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of consumables like brakes, suspension bushings, and tires. Finding a well-maintained, unmodified example is the best way to secure a rewarding and trouble-free ownership experience.
