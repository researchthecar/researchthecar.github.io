# 2016 Lexus GS F Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus GS F combines luxury sedan comfort with a high-performance V8 engine, offering a compelling option for used-car buyers looking for reliability paired with sportiness. While NHTSA complaints for this performance model are extremely rare, prospective buyers should always evaluate the mechanical condition of high-performance powertrains, brakes, and tires prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

Equipped with a naturally aspirated 5.0-liter V8 producing approximately 467 horsepower and 389 lb-ft of torque. This robust engine is generally regarded as exceptionally reliable for a high-performance application when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct Shift Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission with manual paddle shifters, driving the rear wheels. It delivers crisp gear changes suited for spirited driving while maintaining smooth everyday operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V107000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS+F&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A cracked cover may leak fuel, which, in the presence of an ignition source, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus GS F represents a remarkably solid choice in the used performance sedan market, backed by Lexus's reputation for long-term build quality. Because owner complaint data is virtually nonexistent for this model year, buyers can focus their pre-purchase inspections on routine wear items, fluid changes, and overall vehicle history.
