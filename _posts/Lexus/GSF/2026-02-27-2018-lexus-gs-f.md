# 2018 Lexus GS F Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus GS F delivers a high-performance luxury experience powered by a naturally aspirated V8 engine. As a low-volume, performance-oriented sedan from Lexus, it generally benefits from the brand's strong reputation for overall build quality and mechanical durability. Buyers looking at the used market will find that major drivetrain complaints are rare, making it a compelling option for enthusiasts seeking daily-drivable V8 performance.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces approximately 467 horsepower and 389 lb-ft of torque. It is known for its high-revving character and robust mechanical build, generally offering strong reliability for a performance sedan.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features manual shift modes and steering wheel paddle shifters, routing power to the rear wheels. It shifts crisply under performance driving conditions while maintaining smooth operation in everyday traffic.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V107000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS+F&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A cracked cover may leak fuel, which, in the presence of an ignition source, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus GS F stands out as a reliable and potent luxury sport sedan. While standard used-car inspection diligence is always recommended—especially for high-performance braking and suspension components—the vehicle boasts a solid foundation of engineering quality and low owner complaint rates.
