**Introduction:**

The 2020 Lexus GS F is a luxury performance sedan powered by a naturally aspirated 5.0-liter V8 engine producing 467 horsepower and 389 lb-ft of torque. The sole transmission option for the GS F is an 8-speed Sport Direct Shift automatic. The GS F is praised for its sharp handling, responsive engine, and premium interior, but it is criticized for its firm ride and lack of driver-assistance features.

**Engine**

* **5.0L V8**

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Sport Direct Shift Automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power windows may fail to operate, causing the windows to become stuck in an open or closed position.
    * Solution: Replacing the window motor or switch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Misalignment**
    * Description/Symptoms: The trunk lid may not align properly when closed, causing a gap between the lid and the body of the vehicle.
    * Solution: Adjusting the hinges or latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Creaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The center console may creak or rattle when driving over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replacing or adjusting the plastic trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Freeze**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may freeze or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Resetting the system or updating software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2020 Lexus GS F is a reliable and well-built vehicle with a few minor issues. The most common problems reported by owners include power window failure, trunk lid misalignment, center console creaking, and navigation system freeze. The severity of these issues is generally low, and the cost to fix them is reasonable. Overall, the GS F is a solid choice for performance-minded drivers looking for a luxury sedan.
