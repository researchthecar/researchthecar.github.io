# 2019 Lexus UX200 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus UX200 offers buyers a stylish and efficient entry point into luxury compact crossovers, sharing its core engineering with trusted Toyota platforms. While owner-reported complaints are sparse for this model year, buyers should always verify standard pre-owned mechanical conditions and service history before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Inline-4 Engine

The 2.0-liter Dynamic Force four-cylinder engine produces approximately 169 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque. It is generally praised for strong fuel efficiency and dependable everyday performance.

## Transmission

### Direct Shift-CVT Automatic Transmission

The Direct Shift continuously variable transmission features a physical first launch gear for a more responsive takeoff. It delivers smooth power delivery to the front wheels with a solid reputation for normal operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=UX200&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus UX200 demonstrates strong foundational reliability with minimal widespread powertrain or mechanical complaints reported by owners. Prospective buyers can approach this luxury crossover with confidence, provided a routine pre-purchase inspection confirms proper care and maintenance.
