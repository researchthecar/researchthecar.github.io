## 2020 Lexus UX200

The 2020 Lexus UX200 is a subcompact luxury crossover SUV that combines style, comfort, and efficiency. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine producing 169 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque paired with a Direct-Shift Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). Other engine options include a 2.0-liter four-cylinder hybrid powertrain with 181 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with 235 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The UX200 has received praise for its sophisticated design, plush interior, and advanced safety features, but it has also attracted some criticism for its limited rear seat space and average performance.

### Engine

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, poor performance, increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**Direct-Shift Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks during acceleration or shifting gears.
    * Solution: Software updates or CVT replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **No Issues Found**

### Electrical

* **12-Volt Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or diagnose electrical system for excessive parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Sunroof Wind Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise or whistling when the sunroof is open.
    * Solution: Install updated weatherstripping or adjust sunroof alignment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system not functioning properly, producing excessive or insufficient air flow.
    * Solution: Replace faulty actuators or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2020 Lexus UX200 is a well-regarded compact luxury SUV but has experienced some common issues. While the issues are not widespread, they can be costly to repair. Engine and transmission problems have been the most prevalent, with issues such as fuel injector failure and CVT hesitation. Other concerns include 12-volt battery discharge, sunroof wind noise, and climate control malfunctions. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem, but owners should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing or owning a UX200.
