**Introduction:**

The 2022 Lexus UX200 is a subcompact luxury crossover SUV featuring a 2.0-liter inline-four engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The UX200 offers a blend of refinement, fuel efficiency, and a spacious cabin. Despite its commendable qualities, some issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine:**

* **2.0-liter Inline-Four**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption is a common issue, particularly with higher-mileage vehicles. This can lead to reduced engine performance and potential damage. Solution involves replacing piston rings and valve seals. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * **Timing Chain Noise:** In some cases, the timing chain may make excessive noise during startup or acceleration. This is caused by premature wear or a faulty tensioner. Solution involves replacing the timing chain and tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 22T029 (Fuel Leaks), NHTSA Campaign Number: 23V033 (Faulty Starter Motor)

**Transmission:**

* **CVT**
    * **Hesitation and Jerking:** Sporadic hesitation or jerking during acceleration is an issue faced by some owners. This can be caused by worn transmission components or a faulty control module. Solution involves replacing the CVT or reprogramming the control module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $4000+
    * **Noises:** Certain CVT transmissions may develop humming or whining noises. This is typically indicative of worn bearings or gears within the transmission. Solution involves replacing the faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have experienced excessive battery drain when the vehicle is parked. This can be caused by a faulty electrical component or a parasitic drain. Solution involves diagnosing and replacing the faulty component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Occasional glitches or malfunctions with the infotainment system have been reported. These can range from frozen screens to connectivity issues. Solution involves updating the software or replacing system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+

**Exterior:**

* **Sunroof Rattling:** Some owners have reported sunroof rattling or whistling noises at certain speeds. This can be caused by loose mounting bolts or a misaligned sunroof panel. Solution involves tightening bolts or adjusting the sunroof panel. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Interior:**

* **Center Console Buzzing:** A buzzing or rattling noise from the center console area has been reported by some owners. This can be caused by loose trim panels or faulty assembly. Solution involves identifying and tightening loose components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
* **Steering Wheel Vibration:** In some cases, the steering wheel may vibrate excessively at certain speeds. This can be caused by a faulty steering rack or worn suspension components. Solution involves replacing the faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+

**Summary:**

The Lexus UX200, while a refined and fuel-efficient vehicle, has been noted for certain issues affecting its engine, transmission, and other components. The most significant concerns are excessive oil consumption and timing chain noise in the engine, as well as hesitation and jerking during acceleration with the CVT transmission. Other issues include battery drain, infotainment glitches, sunroof rattling, center console buzzing, and steering wheel vibration. The severity and cost of these issues vary, and it's important to consult with a qualified mechanic for diagnosis and repair estimates.
