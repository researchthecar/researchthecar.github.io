The 2022 Lexus UX 250h is a subcompact hybrid crossover known for its fuel efficiency, comfortable ride, and luxurious interior. The UX 250h features a 2.0-liter inline-4 engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 181 horsepower and is only available with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The UX 250h is appreciated for its stylish design and Lexus reliability but criticized for its limited cargo space and somewhat underwhelming acceleration.

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

*   **Hesitation/Jerking During Acceleration**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing hesitation or a slight jerking sensation during initial acceleration, especially from a stop or at low speeds. This can be more noticeable in stop-and-go traffic.
    *   Solution: This issue is sometimes related to the CVT's programming. A software update from Lexus may resolve the problem by recalibrating the transmission's shift points. Some owners have also found that adjusting their driving style, such as applying the accelerator more smoothly, can minimize the issue.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update: Typically covered under warranty. Out of warranty diagnosis and potential re-flash: $100 - $200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Rebooting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Lexus infotainment system, including the navigation and multimedia functions, may occasionally freeze or spontaneously reboot. This can interrupt audio playback, navigation guidance, and other features.
    *   Solution: A software update from Lexus can often resolve this issue. In some cases, a hardware malfunction may require replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update: Typically covered under warranty. Infotainment unit replacement: $1,000 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Backup Camera Failure/Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The backup camera may fail to display an image, display a distorted or blurry image, or experience intermittent functionality.
    *   Solution: The issue may be a faulty camera unit, a loose connection, or a software glitch. A dealer inspection can diagnose the problem. Repair may involve replacing the camera, tightening connections, or performing a software update.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Camera replacement: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rattling or squeaking noises emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or rear cargo area.
    *   Solution: Locating and isolating the source of the noise can be challenging. Applying felt tape or foam padding to contact points between interior panels can sometimes dampen or eliminate the noise.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source and complexity of the repair. $50 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Lexus UX 250h generally demonstrates good reliability. The most common issues reported pertain to the CVT hesitation, infotainment system glitches, and occasional interior noises. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to potentially impacting vehicle functionality. Repair costs can vary significantly, with software updates often covered under warranty, while hardware replacements can be more expensive.
