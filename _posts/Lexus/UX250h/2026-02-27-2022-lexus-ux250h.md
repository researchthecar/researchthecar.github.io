The 2022 Lexus UX250h is a compact luxury hybrid SUV known for its refined interior, comfortable ride, and excellent fuel efficiency. ### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus UX250h exhibits a high level of technical reliability, consistent with Lexus's reputation. The powertrain, including the 2.0L hybrid engine and eCVT transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior components, and interior systems are generally robust. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is the infotainment system, which can occasionally experience minor glitches requiring software updates or resets. Overall, the UX250h is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a fuel-efficient and reliable luxury compact SUV, with minimal reported technical faults that would detract from its ownership experience.
