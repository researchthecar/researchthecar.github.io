**Introduction**

The 2021 Lexus UX250h is a compact luxury crossover SUV that combines a 2.0-liter inline-four engine with an electric motor to produce a combined 181 horsepower and 151 pound-feet of torque. The UX250h comes standard with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery may drain quickly when the vehicle is parked and off.
    * Solution: Replace faulty battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system may freeze, crash, or display errors.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Crossbars Loosen**
    * Description/Symptoms: Roof rack crossbars may become loose and noisy.
    * Solution: Tighten crossbars or replace faulty mounting hardware.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system may not function properly, resulting in insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty climate control components, such as the HVAC module or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Noises**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof may produce excessive noise when opening or closing.
    * Solution: Lubricate sunroof tracks or replace faulty sunroof mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Lexus UX250h has relatively few reported issues, with the majority being minor electrical glitches and exterior annoyances. The severity of the issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potential safety concerns. The estimated costs to repair the issues vary depending on the extent of the problem and the parts required. Overall, the UX250h is a reliable and well-built vehicle with a high owner satisfaction rating.
