**Introduction**

The 2020 Lexus UX250h is a compact luxury SUV that offers a hybrid powertrain. It features a 2.0-liter inline-four engine paired with an electric motor for a combined output of 181 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque. The UX250h comes standard with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

People like the Lexus UX250h for its fuel efficiency, comfortable ride, and luxurious interior. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-Four Hybrid**

- **Knocking or ticking sounds**
  - Description/Symptoms: Knocking or ticking noises coming from the engine at idle or low RPMs
  - Solution: Repair or replacement of the VVT-i system
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

- **Jerking or shuddering**
  - Description/Symptoms: The transmission jerks or shudders during shifts or acceleration
  - Solution: Reprogramming of the transmission control unit (TCU)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

- **Battery drain**
  - Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains quickly when parked
  - Solution: Replacement of the battery
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
- **Check engine light**
  - Description/Symptoms: The check engine light illuminates due to an issue with the emissions control system
  - Solution: Repair or replacement of the emissions control system components
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Wind noise**
  - Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise around the windows and doors at highway speeds
  - Solution: Adjustment or replacement of the weatherstripping
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Climate control issues**
  - Description/Symptoms: The climate control system fails to maintain a consistent temperature or does not blow air properly
  - Solution: Repair or replacement of the climate control system components
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Squeaky seats**
  - Description/Symptoms: The seats squeak or rattle when sitting in them
  - Solution: Replacement of the seat tracks or seat cushions
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2020 Lexus UX250h is a comfortable and fuel-efficient luxury SUV. However, some owners have reported issues with the engine, transmission, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem.
