**Introduction**

The 2019 Lexus RX350L is a luxury midsize SUV with a spacious interior and capable performance. It is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing 295 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The RX350L also offers a hybrid variant with a 3.5-liter V6 engine and electric motors, producing a combined 308 horsepower.

Despite its positive attributes, the 2019 Lexus RX350L has several reported issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**
* **Carbon buildup on intake valves:** Causes reduced engine power, rough idle, and increased fuel consumption. **Solution:** Intake valve cleaning. **Cost:** $500 - $1,000+.
* **Fuel injector failure:** Results in engine misfiring, hesitation, and reduced fuel economy. **Solution:** Fuel injector replacement. **Cost:** $300 - $500+ per injector.
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Can lead to engine noise, reduced power, or even catastrophic engine failure. **Solution:** Timing chain tensioner replacement. **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000+.
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**
* **Hesitation and shuddering:** Transmission may hesitate or shudder during shifts, especially when under load. **Solution:** Transmission software update or replacement. **Cost:** $100 - $1,000+.
* **Premature wear of transmission components:** Can cause excessive noise, vibration, and transmission failure. **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement. **Cost:** $2,000 - $5,000+.
* **Leakage from transmission cooler lines:** May result in fluid leaks and transmission overheating. **Solution:** Cooler line replacement. **Cost:** $200 - $500+.

**Suspension**

* **Rear suspension noise:** Noises from the rear suspension, such as squeaks or rattles, when driving over uneven surfaces. **Solution:** Suspension component lubrication or replacement. **Cost:** $100 - $500+.
* **Front strut mount failure:** Can cause clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension. **Solution:** Strut mount replacement. **Cost:** $200 - $500+ per strut.

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is parked. **Solution:** Electrical system diagnosis and repair. **Cost:** $100 - $500+.
* **Electrical glitches:** Occasional electrical glitches, such as malfunctioning lights or gauges. **Solution:** Software update or component replacement. **Cost:** $100 - $1,000+.
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Wind noise from windows:** Excessive wind noise from the windows when driving at high speeds. **Solution:** Window adjustment or replacement. **Cost:** $100 - $500+ per window.
* **Rear hatch not closing properly:** Hatch may fail to close securely or require excessive force to open. **Solution:** Latch adjustment or component replacement. **Cost:** $100 - $500+.
* **Headlight condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights. **Solution:** Headlight replacement. **Cost:** $500 - $1,000+ per headlight.

**Interior**

* **Cracking dashboard:** Dashboard material may crack over time. **Solution:** Dashboard replacement. **Cost:** $500 - $1,000+.
* **HVAC system noise:** Noise from the HVAC system, such as clicking or rattling, during operation. **Solution:** HVAC component replacement or repair. **Cost:** $100 - $500+.
* **Sunroof rattle:** Noise from the sunroof when closed or open. **Solution:** Sunroof adjustment or component replacement. **Cost:** $100 - $500+.

**Summary**

The 2019 Lexus RX350L has several reported issues that may affect its reliability and ownership experience. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Potential owners should be aware of these issues and consider their severity and potential repair costs before making a purchasing decision.
