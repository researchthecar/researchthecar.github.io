# 2020 Lexus RX350L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lexus RX 350L extends the popular midsize luxury crossover platform to offer a third row of seating, combining family-friendly versatility with Lexus's renowned reputation for build quality and refinement. While overall owner satisfaction remains high and traditional mechanical trouble spots are rare, prospective used-car buyers should still evaluate vehicle condition thoroughly during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (295 hp / 268 lb-ft)

Equipped with a 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 producing 295 horsepower and 268 lb-ft of torque, paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. This engine is widely regarded as exceptionally durable and trouble-free under routine maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed automatic transmission designed to balance highway efficiency with smooth power delivery across all driving modes. It generally maintains a strong track record for longevity when serviced according to factory schedules.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX350L&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lexus RX 350L stands out as a dependable and luxurious choice in the used midsize SUV market, backed by a proven powertrain and strong overall build quality. Buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify proper operation and ensure all routine maintenance has been completed.
