# 2021 Lexus RX350L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus RX 350L is a luxury midsize crossover that extends the standard RX lineup to offer a third row of seating and additional cargo flexibility. Powered by a dependable V6 engine paired with a traditional automatic transmission, it carries the strong reputation for comfort and longevity expected of the Lexus brand. For used-car shoppers, the vehicle generally presents a low-stress ownership experience, though buyers should still carefully inspect individual listings for standard wear and tear.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

This 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 290 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and robust acceleration for the extended body. It is widely regarded as a highly durable and reliable powerplant with very few mechanical troubles.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to either the front wheels or all four wheels depending on the configuration. It shifts seamlessly and maintains a strong track record for long-term mechanical reliability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RX&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus RX 350L is an exceptionally solid choice for used-car buyers seeking luxury, everyday comfort, and peace of mind. With robust powertrain options and a strong baseline of reliability, it remains a smart investment. As always, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a detailed service history review are recommended before finalizing any purchase.
