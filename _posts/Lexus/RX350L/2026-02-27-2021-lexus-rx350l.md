# 2021 Lexus RX350L

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus RX350L is a three-row luxury SUV known for its comfortable ride, premium interior, and strong reputation for reliability. Built on the proven platform of the standard RX, the 'L' variant offers extended cargo space and seating for up to seven passengers.  Under the hood, it features a robust V6 engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, delivering smooth power delivery.  While generally well-regarded, like any vehicle, some owners have reported specific concerns. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus RX350L demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain (3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission) is exceptionally robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components generally hold up well. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced relate to the infotainment system, with occasional glitches and connectivity problems, and a few reports of premature battery failure. These issues are typically addressable through software updates or component replacement and do not detract significantly from the vehicle's overall dependable nature. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its long-term performance.
