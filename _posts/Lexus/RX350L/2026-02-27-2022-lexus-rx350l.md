The RX 350L typically comes with a robust V6 engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission, both of which have a history of durability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus RX 350L demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, consistent with Lexus's brand reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no significant widespread issues reported. Suspension components are also generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that are typically resolved with software updates, and a possibility of premature battery failure, though this is not a common occurrence. Exterior and interior components, including upholstery and paint, are built to a high standard and rarely present issues beyond normal wear. Overall, the RX 350L is a very dependable choice for a luxury three-row SUV.
