**Introduction:**

The 2021 Lexus RX350L is a luxury midsize SUV that offers a spacious interior and a refined driving experience. It features a standard 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 3.5-liter V6 hybrid with CVT and a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The RX350L is known for its comfortable ride, high-quality interior materials, and advanced safety features. However, it has also received some negative reviews regarding fuel economy and infotainment system functionality.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**

**Timing Chain Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine on startup or acceleration.
Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and/or timing chain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-speed Automatic**

**Shift Hesitation**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or delay when shifting gears.
Solution: Software update or replacement of the transmission control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Panoramic Sunroof Malfunctions**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, making unusual noises.
Solution: Replacement of the sunroof motor or sunroof control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Lexus RX350L has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While it has no major mechanical issues, it has experienced some minor problems with the engine timing chain, transmission shift hesitation, and panoramic sunroof malfunctions. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost of repairs can range from minor to significant. Overall, the RX350L offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience, but potential owners should be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase.
