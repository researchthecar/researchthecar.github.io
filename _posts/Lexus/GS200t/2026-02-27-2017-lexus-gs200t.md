# 2017 Lexus GS200t Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lexus GS200t offers a blend of luxury and performance, powered by a turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. While Lexus is renowned for overall build quality and dependability, prospective buyers should still evaluate individual vehicle condition, maintenance records, and specific feature functionality during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily commuting while maintaining competitive efficiency for a luxury sedan.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the rear wheels. It is generally regarded as durable when paired with routine fluid changes.

## Steering

### Lane Keep Assist

Driver assistance systems enhance security but may occasionally exhibit sensor limitations.

**Intermittent Lane Keeping Assistance Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the lane keeping assistance system may function adequately on straight highways, but can experience intermittent failures or tracking difficulties on curved roads.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the front camera sensor calibration, software version, and steering system electronics to ensure proper operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V222000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=GS&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus GS200t is a solid luxury sedan choice characterized by robust foundational engineering and comfortable driving dynamics. Buyers should carefully inspect advanced driver-assistance features like lane keeping systems during a pre-purchase test drive, and verify that all routine maintenance has been thoroughly completed.
