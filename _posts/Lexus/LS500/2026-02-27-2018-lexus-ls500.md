# 2018 Lexus LS500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lexus LS500 serves as the flagship luxury sedan for the brand, offering striking styling, high-end cabin amenities, and advanced technology. While Lexus maintains an exceptional reputation for overall long-term dependability, used-car buyers should review available owner feedback regarding isolated mechanical quirks and specific component recalls before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 416 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque, providing swift and effortless acceleration. This powertrain is generally considered robust and reliable, benefiting from standard Lexus engineering standards.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear transitions across rear-wheel-drive and all-wheel-drive configurations. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions with few widespread mechanical complaints.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake components provide strong stopping power suited for a heavy luxury sedan, though isolated noises have been reported by owners.

**Brake Pedal Knocking Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an abnormally loud knocking sound originating from underneath the vehicle near the suspension area when depressing the brake pedal.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the brake assembly, mounting hardware, and adjacent suspension components to isolate the source of the noise.

## Safety

### Wheels and Tires

The suspension system delivers a plush, compliant ride quality characteristic of a flagship luxury cruiser.

**Run-Flat Tire Availability and Recalls**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced difficulties with replacement part availability and backorders when attempting to service factory run-flat tires tied to safety campaigns.
- **Solution:** Verify that all applicable tire-related safety recalls have been completed by a certified dealership and inspect tire wear prior to purchase.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS500&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Lexus LS500 remains a sophisticated and comfortable pre-owned luxury choice, provided it has been thoroughly inspected. Prospective buyers should verify that all open recalls—particularly those involving factory tires—have been properly addressed and check for any suspension or braking noises during a test drive.
