### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus LS500 demonstrates strong technical reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The powertrain, including the 3.5L twin-turbo V6 and 10-speed automatic transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension and core exterior/interior components are robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature 12V battery failure. These are generally manageable and do not detract significantly from the overall excellent reliability expected from a Lexus flagship sedan. Regular maintenance and addressing software updates promptly are recommended for optimal performance.
