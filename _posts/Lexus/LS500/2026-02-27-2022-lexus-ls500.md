# 2022 Lexus LS500

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus LS500 represents the pinnacle of Lexus's luxury sedan offerings, combining a powerful twin-turbo V6 engine with a sophisticated drivetrain and a host of advanced technology. As expected from the Lexus brand, reliability is a key focus. This model year generally upholds Lexus's reputation for dependability, with owners reporting a high degree of satisfaction. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the LS500 demonstrates strong performance in most critical areas. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus LS500 demonstrates exceptional reliability, aligning with Lexus's long-standing reputation for building durable and dependable luxury vehicles. The powertrain, including the 3.5L twin-turbo V6 and the 10-speed automatic transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are robust. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is within the infotainment system, with occasional software glitches that are typically resolved through updates. Electrical systems, particularly ADAS, and interior components are generally problem-free. Overall, the LS500 is a highly reliable choice for luxury sedan buyers seeking peace of mind.
