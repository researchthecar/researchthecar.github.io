# 2024 Lexus LS500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus LS500 represents the pinnacle of Japanese luxury and craftsmanship, offering supreme comfort and advanced technology. As a relatively new model-year vehicle, it benefits from Lexus's strong overall brand reputation for quality, though buyers should still evaluate its complex modern powertrain and onboard electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

This 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces approximately 416 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and effortless acceleration. It serves as the primary powerplant for the LS500, balancing high-end performance with traditional Lexus engineering standards.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission features quick, seamless shifts and is paired with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It is designed to optimize fuel efficiency and maintain refined power delivery across all driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V595000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An instrument panel display that does not show critical information can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel software over-the-air (OTA) for non-PHEV vehicles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
