# 2019 Lexus LS500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus LS500 serves as the brand's flagship luxury sedan, offering advanced technology, a serene cabin, and a twin-turbocharged powertrain. While overall owner satisfaction and brand reputation for long-term dependability remain strong, used-car buyers should always evaluate individual vehicle history and maintenance records carefully.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces approximately 416 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift and smooth acceleration. It represents the standard powerplant for this generation, balancing luxury-tier performance with modern engineering complexity.

**Engine Knocking and Deceleration**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly decelerates from cruising speeds to lower velocities while emitting a knocking sound from the engine bay, with no prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic or dealership perform a complete diagnostic inspection of the engine internals and powertrain control modules.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission works seamlessly with the V6 engine to optimize fuel efficiency and highway cruising refinement. It is generally robust, though prospective buyers should test-shift through all gears during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V682000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly with an improved one, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V544000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Deactivation of the vehicle stability control system or a loss of braking assist increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the brake booster pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V012000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=LS500&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus LS500 is a sophisticated luxury sedan that provides a comfortable ride and upscale amenities. While isolated owner reports highlight potential mechanical concerns, thorough pre-purchase inspections and a clean service history remain the best tools for used-car buyers.
