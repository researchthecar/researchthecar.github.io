# 2021 Lexus ES300h

### Overview
The 2021 Lexus ES300h is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its exceptional reliability and comfort, building upon Lexus's long-standing reputation for quality. It primarily features a sophisticated hybrid powertrain, combining a gasoline engine with electric motors for impressive fuel efficiency and smooth performance. The ES300h is engineered with robust components and meticulous build quality, contributing to its strong showing in reliability ratings. Owners generally report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, making it a compelling choice for those seeking a dependable and refined vehicle. The focus on advanced technology and premium materials further enhances its appeal, though like any vehicle, specific components can occasionally experience issues.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lexus ES300h stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly due to its well-engineered hybrid powertrain and the brand's commitment to quality. Major mechanical issues are rare, and most reported concerns are minor, such as occasional infotainment system glitches that are typically resolved with software updates. The powertrain, transmission, suspension, and core interior/exterior components demonstrate exceptional durability. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience with minimal unexpected repair costs, reinforcing its position as a top-tier choice in the luxury sedan segment for those prioritizing long-term reliability and peace of mind.
