# 2021 Lexus ES300h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lexus ES 300h continues the brand's tradition of offering exceptional fuel efficiency, high-end comfort, and strong reliability. While owner complaints are very minimal for this model year, prospective buyers should review any applicable safety notifications and thoroughly inspect the vehicle during a test drive to ensure all electronic and steering systems operate smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid (A25A-FXS)

This 2.5-liter four-cylinder hybrid powertrain pairs with an electric motor to produce a net 215 horsepower while delivering exceptional fuel economy. It is widely regarded as a highly durable and dependable option for everyday commuting.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

The dedicated hybrid planetary continuously variable transmission routes power smoothly to the front wheels with minimal mechanical fuss. It is known for robust long-term reliability and seamless power delivery.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Steering reports are extremely scarce, though isolated electronic assist issues have been noted by individual owners.

**Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a power steering warning light and a sudden loss of power assist, making the steering wheel very difficult to turn in either direction.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified repair facility or dealer to diagnose potential electronic steering module or sensor faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V865000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A short circuit may develop in the Occupant Classification System (OCS) sensor, preventing the front passenger air bag from deploying. An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the sensor as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lexus ES 300h is an exceptionally dependable luxury hybrid choice for used-car buyers. With almost no recurring mechanical complaint patterns outside of isolated electronic anomalies, shoppers can approach this vehicle with high confidence after verifying that all routine safety campaigns have been addressed by a dealer.
