# 2019 Lexus ES300h Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lexus ES 300h continues the brand's tradition of offering exceptional fuel efficiency, high-end comfort, and notable hybrid reliability. Prospective used-car buyers looking at this luxury hybrid sedan will find a well-built vehicle that generally avoids major mechanical trouble spots. While NHTSA owner reports are minimal for this model year, checking service history and proper hybrid battery maintenance remains standard practice when shopping in the used luxury market.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid (A25A-FXS)

This 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine operates on the Atkinson cycle and pairs with an electric motor to produce a net 215 horsepower. Known for outstanding efficiency and longevity, it serves as a highly reliable powertrain choice for daily commuting.

## Transmission

### Electronic Continuously Variable Transmission (e-CVT)

The planetary-gear-based electronic continuously variable transmission distributes power seamlessly between the gasoline engine and electric motor. It is exceptionally durable and rarely experiences mechanical failures.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V544000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=ES300h&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Deactivation of the vehicle stability control system or a loss of braking assist increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the brake booster pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lexus ES 300h is a remarkably sound choice for used-car buyers seeking luxury combined with hybrid fuel savings. With a clean slate of reported major mechanical defects and a proven powertrain heritage, it stands out as a dependable daily driver. Buyers should still conduct a standard pre-purchase inspection and verify the condition of the hybrid components before finalizing a purchase.
