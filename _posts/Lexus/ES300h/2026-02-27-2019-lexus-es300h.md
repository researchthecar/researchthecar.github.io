# 2019 Lexus ES300h

### Overview
The 2019 Lexus ES300h is a luxury mid-size sedan known for its exceptional reliability and comfort, particularly due to its hybrid powertrain. This generation of the ES300h builds upon Lexus's reputation for building durable vehicles with a focus on sophisticated engineering and premium materials. Owners generally report a smooth and quiet driving experience, with the hybrid system contributing to excellent fuel efficiency without compromising performance. The vehicle is designed for long-term ownership, with many components engineered for longevity. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the ES300h typically exhibits a low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems, making it a strong contender in its segment for those prioritizing dependability.
