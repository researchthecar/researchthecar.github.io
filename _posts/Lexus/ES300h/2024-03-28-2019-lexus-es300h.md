**Introduction:**

The 2019 Lexus ES300h is a luxury hybrid sedan powered by a 2.5-liter inline-four engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 215 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque. The ES300h is offered exclusively with an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission (ECVT). It is praised for its comfortable ride, fuel efficiency, and luxurious interior, while some may find its handling to be on the softer side.

**Suspension:**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

**12-Volt Battery:**

**Issue: Premature Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery fails prematurely, causing starting issues or electrical malfunctions.
Solution: Replace battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Headlight Harness:**

**Issue: Overheating**
Description/Symptoms: Headlight harness overheats, causing headlights to dim or fail.
Solution: Replace headlight harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

**Sunroof:**

**Issue: Glass Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof glass cracks or shatters spontaneously.
Solution: Replace sunroof glass.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

**Instrument Cluster:**

**Issue: Dimming or Flickering**
Description/Symptoms: Instrument cluster dims or flickers intermittently.
Solution: Replace faulty instrument cluster.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System:**

**Issue: Freezing or Lagging**
Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezes or lags, affecting navigation, audio, and other functions.
Solution: Software update or replacement of faulty components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Lexus ES300h has a relatively low number of reported issues. The electrical problems, particularly with the battery and headlight harness, are the most concerning, as they can impact the vehicle's reliability and safety. The other issues are less severe and can be addressed with repairs or replacements.
