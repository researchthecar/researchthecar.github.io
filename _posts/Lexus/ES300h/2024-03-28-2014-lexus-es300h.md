**Introduction:**

The 2014 Lexus ES300h is a luxury hybrid sedan known for its comfortable ride, fuel efficiency, and advanced technology. It comes with a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder gasoline engine and an electric motor paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The ES300h has a combined output of 200 horsepower and 208 lb-ft of torque. It is appreciated for its spacious cabin, intuitive infotainment system, and impressive safety ratings. However, like any vehicle, the 2014 Lexus ES300h has its fair share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.5L Inline-4 Gas Engine**
    * **Oil Consumption:** 
         * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, causing low oil levels and potential engine damage.
         * Solution: Repair or replacement of piston rings, valve stems, or other affected components.
         * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
         * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**
    * **CVT Hesitation and Shuddering:** 
         * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent hesitation, slipping, or shuddering during acceleration and gear changes.
         * Solution: Replacement of CVT fluid, reprogramming of transmission control module, or potential transmission overhaul.
         * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
         * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Recall:**
         * Description/Symptoms: Battery pack defect can lead to sudden loss of power.
         * Solution: Battery pack replacement under recall.
         * Estimated Cost to Fix: None (covered under recall)
         * Recalls: 14V-334
* **Electrical System Interruptions:** 
         * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, such as flickering lights, power window malfunctions, or audio system failures.
         * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of affected electrical components or wiring.
         * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
         * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** 
         * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight housing, causing poor visibility at night.
         * Solution: Replacement of headlight assembly or installation of ventilation kit.
         * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
         * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:** 
         * Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof rattles or vibrates while driving, producing annoying noise.
         * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of sunroof seals or other affected components.
         * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
         * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Odors:** 
         * Description/Symptoms: Unpleasant odors emitted from the air conditioning or heating system.
         * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of air filters, evaporator core, or other affected components.
         * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
         * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2014 Lexus ES300h is generally a reliable vehicle, it has had some reported issues that owners should be aware of. The most notable issues include oil consumption in the 2.5L engine, CVT hesitation and shuddering, and battery pack issues. Electrical system interruptions, headlight condensation, sunroof rattles, and climate control odors are also worth considering. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the problem's extent and the vehicle's mileage and condition.
