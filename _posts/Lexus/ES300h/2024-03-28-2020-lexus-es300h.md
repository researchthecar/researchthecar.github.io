**Introduction**
The 2020 Lexus ES300h is a mid-size luxury sedan that offers a blend of comfort, efficiency, and technology. It comes with a 2.5-liter inline-four engine paired with an electric motor, delivering a combined output of 215 horsepower and 153 lb-ft of torque. The ES300h is offered in a single trim level, with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Interior**
- **Climate Control Issues**
- Description/Symptoms: Occasional malfunction of the climate control system, resulting in inconsistent cooling or heating.
- Solution: Reinitialization of the climate control module or replacement of faulty sensors.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
- Recalls: None Found
- **Infotainment System Issues**
- Description/Symptoms: Intermittent touchscreen responsiveness and Bluetooth connectivity problems.
- Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
- Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2020 Lexus ES300h has a good reliability record, with minimal issues reported by owners. However, some minor interior and infotainment problems have been noted. The severity of these issues is generally minor, and the cost to fix them is typically within a reasonable range.
