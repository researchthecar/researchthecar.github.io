# 2017 Lexus ES300h

### Overview
The 2017 Lexus ES300h is a luxury mid-size sedan known for its exceptional reliability and smooth driving experience, particularly due to its hybrid powertrain. This model year benefits from Lexus's long-standing reputation for quality engineering and meticulous manufacturing. The ES300h combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor to deliver impressive fuel efficiency without compromising on performance or comfort. Owners generally report a high level of satisfaction with its durability and low running costs. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the ES300h typically exhibits very few significant problems, making it a strong contender for those seeking a dependable and refined vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus ES300h stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, consistently earning praise for its robust engineering and minimal mechanical issues. The hybrid powertrain is particularly well-regarded for its longevity and efficiency. While minor electrical glitches with the infotainment system can occur, they are infrequent and generally easily resolved. Other major components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, and exterior/interior elements, show no widespread reliability concerns. This model is an excellent choice for buyers prioritizing dependability, comfort, and low long-term ownership costs, reinforcing Lexus's reputation for building durable luxury sedans.
