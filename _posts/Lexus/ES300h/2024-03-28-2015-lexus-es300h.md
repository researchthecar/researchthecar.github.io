**Introduction:**
The 2015 Lexus ES300h is a luxury hybrid sedan powered by a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined horsepower of 200 and torque of 156 lb-ft. The ES300h offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and excellent fuel economy. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

* **2.5L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption has been reported, requiring frequent oil top-ups.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT Transmission**
    * **Slipping Gears:** The transmission may slip gears, causing loss of power and acceleration.
    * Solution: Transmission repair or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension:**
    * **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** The strut mount bearings may fail, causing a clunking noise and reduced stability.
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery:**
    * **Premature Battery Failure:** The 12-volt battery may fail prematurely, preventing the vehicle from starting.
    * Solution: Replace battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: 2014-2015 Lexus ES300h (NHTSA ID: 15V387000)

* **Electrical System:**
    * **Electrical System Malfunction:** The electrical system may experience malfunctions, causing a variety of symptoms, such as flickering lights, power loss, or non-functioning electronics.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights:**
    * **Condensation in Headlights:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights, affecting visibility.
    * Solution: Replace headlight seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: 2014-2015 Lexus ES300h (NHTSA ID: 14V296000)

* **Trunk:**
    * **Trunk Lid Misalignment:** The trunk lid may not align properly, causing a rattle or difficulty closing.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace trunk lid components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard:**
    * **Creaking Dashboard:** The dashboard may creak or rattle, particularly during cold weather.
    * Solution: Apply sound-dampening material or replace dashboard components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2015 Lexus ES300h is a reliable and luxurious sedan, but it is not immune to issues. The most common problems reported include oil consumption, transmission slippage, and electrical system malfunctions. While some issues may be minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as engine and transmission repairs, can be costly. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues and consider them when making a purchasing decision.
