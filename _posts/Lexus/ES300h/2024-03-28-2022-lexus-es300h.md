The 2022 Lexus ES300h is a midsize luxury sedan powered by a 2.5-liter inline-4 engine paired with an electric motor for a combined output of 215 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque. It comes with an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

* **2.5L Inline-4 Engine**
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000-$7,000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn timing chain tensioner, resulting in rattling noises, reduced engine power, and potential catastrophic engine failure.
        * Solution: Timing chain tensioner replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800-$1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT**
    * **Transmission Shudder/Jerking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering or jerking during acceleration or deceleration, especially at low speeds.
        * Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid flush, or CVT replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sudden battery drain, resulting in difficulty starting or dead battery.
        * Solution: Identifying and fixing the underlying electrical issue causing the drain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezing, crashing, or displaying error messages.
        * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Cracked Headlights**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracked or broken headlight lenses, allowing moisture and debris to enter.
        * Solution: Headlight replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling**
        * Description/Symptoms: Annoying rattling noises from the dashboard area.
        * Solution: Locating and tightening loose parts or applying sound dampening materials
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, stuck in various positions.
        * Solution: Sunroof motor or track repairs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Lexus ES300h has a few potential issues, with varying severity and costs to fix. Issues such as oil consumption and timing chain tensioner failure can be major concerns. However, the vehicle's overall reliability is generally considered good.
