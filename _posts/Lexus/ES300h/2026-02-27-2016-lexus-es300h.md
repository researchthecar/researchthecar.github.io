# 2016 Lexus ES300h

### Overview
The 2016 Lexus ES 300h is a luxury mid-size sedan known for its exceptional reliability and comfort, particularly due to its hybrid powertrain. This model combines a gasoline engine with electric motors to offer impressive fuel efficiency without compromising performance. Lexus, a brand synonymous with quality, generally produces vehicles with robust engineering and meticulous build quality. The ES 300h, in particular, benefits from Toyota's proven hybrid technology, which has a long track record of durability. Owners typically report a smooth driving experience and low ownership costs, with the vehicle excelling in areas like powertrain longevity and interior refinement. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the ES 300h stands out for its consistent dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Lexus ES 300h is a highly reliable vehicle, consistently praised for its durable hybrid powertrain and overall build quality. The primary potential concern, as with most hybrid vehicles, is the long-term health of the hybrid battery, though these systems are generally very long-lasting. Minor electrical glitches with the infotainment system can occur but are typically resolved with software updates. Other major mechanical or structural issues are rare. For buyers seeking a dependable, comfortable, and fuel-efficient luxury sedan, the 2016 ES 300h remains an excellent choice with a strong reliability record.
