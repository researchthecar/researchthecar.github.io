**Introduction**

The 2017 Lexus ES300h is a luxury hybrid sedan powered by a 2.5-liter inline-four gasoline engine paired with an electric motor. The hybrid powertrain produces a combined 200 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque. The ES300h is known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and fuel efficiency. However, it has received some complaints about its handling and lack of performance.

**Engine**

**2.5L Inline-Four**

* **Faulty Oxygen Sensor:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty oxygen sensor leads to inaccurate fuel mixture and poor engine performance, including reduced fuel efficiency and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace oxygen sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature stretching or breaking of timing chain can lead to engine failure. Symptoms include rattling noises from the engine and reduced power.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **CVT Shuddering or Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission experiences shuddering or slipping during acceleration or gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace or repair CVT.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Struts can corrode prematurely, causing noises, vibration, and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery failure or poor battery health can lead to starting issues or reduced fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Water Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water can leak into the trunk through the taillight seals.
    * Solution: Replace taillight seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard may rattle or buzz during certain driving conditions.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 Lexus ES300h has received some complaints regarding engine issues, such as faulty oxygen sensors and timing chain problems. Transmission shuddering or slipping is also a reported issue. The vehicle may also experience suspension issues due to strut corrosion. Electrical issues include battery problems, and exterior issues include trunk water leakage. Interior issues are limited to dashboard rattling. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and others potentially costly to repair.
