# 2024 Lexus TX550h+ Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lexus TX 550h+ represents a modern luxury hybrid option for shoppers seeking three-row versatility and advanced electrified performance. As a newer addition to the luxury crossover market, it pairs upscale interior comfort with sophisticated plug-in hybrid engineering. Because this vehicle is still in its initial production year, used-car buyers should carefully review any early owner feedback and verify that all necessary manufacturer campaigns have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Plug-in Hybrid

This 3.5-liter V6 plug-in hybrid powertrain delivers robust combined horsepower and strong low-end torque through electric motor assistance. Overall reliability is expected to align with traditional Lexus standards, though early-production complex hybrid systems warrant a thorough inspection.

## Transmission

### Electronically Controlled Continuously Variable Transmission (ECVT)

An electronically controlled continuously variable transmission manages power delivery seamlessly across all driving modes. It is paired with an electronic all-wheel-drive system designed to offer smooth, responsive acceleration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V461000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=TX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver and passenger side curtain air bags may not unroll as intended and could partially deploy outside of an open window. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." An improper deployment of the side curtain air bag can reduce occupant protection during a.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver and passenger curtain shield air bag assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V595000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=TX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An instrument panel display that does not show critical information can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel software over-the-air (OTA) for non-PHEV vehicles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V744000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=TX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the rearview camera to freeze or display a blank screen when the vehicle is in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that fails to display an image can reduce the driver's view behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V162000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=TX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display when the vehicle is placed in reverse. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software or replace the rearview camera, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
