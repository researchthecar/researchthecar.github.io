# 2017 Lexus RC F Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Lexus RC F, buyers are generally looking at a high-performance luxury coupe known for its robust V8 power and upscale interior. Because overall complaint volume for this specific model year is extremely low, it tends to present a strong reliability profile compared to typical performance competitors. However, as with any sports coupe, thorough pre-purchase inspections remain essential to ensure proper mechanical and physical condition.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

The 2017 Lexus RC F features a naturally aspirated 5.0-liter V8 engine producing roughly 467 horsepower and 389 lb-ft of torque. This high-output powerplant is generally praised for its stout reliability and durability under spirited driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

Power is sent to the rear wheels via an eight-speed sport direct-shift automatic transmission equipped with paddle shifters. This unit is widely regarded as dependable, offering smooth daily cruising and crisp gear changes.

## Interior

### Accelerator Pedal Area

Cabin ergonomics are generally well-received, though individual drivers should test fit and pedal clearance during a test drive.

**Pedal Obstruction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers with larger shoes may find that the right side of their foot encounters physical obstructions in the accelerator pedal footwell area during rapid acceleration.
- **Solution:** Ensure proper foot placement and verify that floor mats or trim pieces are securely fitted and do not interfere with pedal travel.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V107000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC+F&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A cracked cover may leak fuel, which, in the presence of an ignition source, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lexus RC F combines aggressive styling with a bulletproof V8 powertrain, making it an appealing choice for used luxury sports coupe buyers. With minimal owner-reported complaints on file, it stands out as a relatively trouble-free option in its class. Prospective buyers should still perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical health and check for any ergonomic fit quirks.
