### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Lexus RC F demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The core mechanical components, including the powerful 5.0L V8 engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission, are exceptionally robust and rarely present significant issues. The chassis and suspension systems are also well-engineered for performance and durability. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery drain. These are generally manageable with software updates or battery replacement. Exterior and interior components are typically of high quality and do not present widespread problems. For owners seeking a reliable performance coupe, the 2018 RC F is a solid choice, with most potential issues being minor and addressable.
