### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Lexus RC F demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with the brand's reputation. The powerful 5.0L V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have few documented issues. Suspension components are also generally trouble-free. The most common areas for minor concern are the infotainment system, which can experience occasional software glitches, and the potential for premature battery failure, though this is not unique to the RC F. Interior trim can sometimes develop minor rattles. Overall, the RC F offers a dependable ownership experience for a performance coupe, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
