### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Lexus RC F demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 5.0L V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have few documented issues. Suspension and exterior components are also generally problem-free. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the infotainment system, which can experience occasional glitches requiring software updates, and the battery, which in some instances may require premature replacement. These are relatively minor issues in the context of a performance vehicle and do not detract significantly from the RC F's reputation for dependability. Regular maintenance and addressing any electrical quirks promptly will ensure a reliable ownership experience.
