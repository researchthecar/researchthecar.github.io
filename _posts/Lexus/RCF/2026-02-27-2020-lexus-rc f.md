### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Lexus RC F demonstrates excellent mechanical reliability, with its powerful 5.0L V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The chassis, suspension, and core interior/exterior components are also robust. The primary area for potential minor concern lies within the infotainment system, which may occasionally exhibit software glitches or connectivity problems, typically resolvable with updates. Overall, the RC F is a dependable performance coupe, with its reliability rating being a strong point, provided routine maintenance is performed and any minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
