# 2016 Lexus RC F Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lexus RC F is a high-performance luxury coupe known for its naturally aspirated V8 power and upscale interior. Prospective buyers evaluating a used model should review standard powertrain specifications alongside owner-reported feedback to ensure a well-maintained vehicle.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

The 5.0-liter V8 engine produces approximately 467 horsepower and 389 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong performance and traditional Lexus durability. Regular maintenance and attention to open safety recalls are recommended for used shoppers.

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump Recall**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience recall notices regarding the high-pressure fuel pumps, though parts availability can sometimes be delayed.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealership inspect and replace the high-pressure fuel pumps as part of the safety recall procedure.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features manual paddle shifters and routes power to the rear wheels. It generally offers smooth operation and robust reliability under normal driving conditions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V107000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC+F&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A cracked cover may leak fuel, which, in the presence of an ignition source, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Toyota will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lexus RC F combines robust V8 performance with luxury coupe styling, making it an appealing option for enthusiasts. Buyers should verify that all open recalls have been completed by a qualified dealer prior to purchase.
