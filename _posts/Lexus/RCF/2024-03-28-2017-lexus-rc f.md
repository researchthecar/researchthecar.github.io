**Introduction:**

The 2017 Lexus RC F is a high-performance coupe powered by a naturally aspirated 5.0L V8 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The RC F is known for its potent engine, sharp handling, and luxurious interior. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced some issues over the years.

**Engine**

**5.0L V8**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** The 5.0L V8 engine has been reported to consume excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
   * Solution: Replacing the piston rings and valve stem seals can fix the issue.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Some RC F owners have experienced fuel injector failures, resulting in rough idling, misfires, and reduced power.
   * Solution: Replacing the faulty fuel injectors and reprogramming the engine control unit usually resolves the problem.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:** Occasional issues with the 8-speed automatic transmission have been reported, including delayed shifts, harsh shifting, and gear hunting.
   * Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the transmission control module can often resolve these problems.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have encountered a parasitic battery drain issue that can leave the vehicle with a dead battery.
   * Solution: Inspecting the vehicle's electrical system and isolating the faulty component usually fixes the problem.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Issues:** The convertible models have experienced issues with the convertible top mechanism, including difficulty opening and closing, water leaks, and excessive noise.
   * Solution: Repairing or replacing the convertible top motor, cables, and seals can address these problems.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunctions:** The RC F's navigation system has occasionally malfunctioned, resulting in incorrect directions, frozen screens, and difficulty finding destinations.
   * Solution: Updating the navigation software or replacing the navigation unit can resolve the issues.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
   * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat Heater Failure:** Some RC F owners have reported failures of the seat heaters, leading to inoperative heating elements.
   * Solution: Replacing the seat heater elements or the seat heater control module can fix the issue.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Lexus RC F is an impressive sports coupe but has encountered some issues, primarily with the engine and transmission. Some of these issues can be expensive to fix, but overall, the severity and cost of the problems are not overwhelming. The majority of owners have positive experiences with their RC Fs, appreciating their performance, handling, and luxury features.
