**Introduction:**

The 2021 Lexus RC F is a high-performance coupe offered with a 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The RC F is known for its powerful engine, responsive handling, and luxurious interior. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **5.0-Liter V8 Engine**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
        * Symptoms: Burning smell, low oil level, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Valve Train Noise:**
        * Symptoms: Ticking or tapping sound from engine
        * Solution: Valve adjustment or lifter replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking During Shifts:**
        * Symptoms: Rough shifting, delayed response
        * Solution: Transmission software update or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
        * Symptoms: Dead battery, slow starting
        * Solution: Replace battery or check for parasitic drain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction:**
        * Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights
        * Solution: Replace headlight bulb or wiring harness
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Leaks:**
        * Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin
        * Solution: Replace convertible top seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
        * Symptoms: Inconsistent air temperature, fan not working
        * Solution: Replace climate control module or blower motor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Lag:**
        * Symptoms: Slow response, freezing or crashing
        * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the Lexus RC F is a capable and luxurious sports car, it has been reported to experience some issues. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor and easily fixed, while others can be more costly and time-consuming to resolve. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to address them promptly to ensure the continued reliability and performance of their vehicle.
