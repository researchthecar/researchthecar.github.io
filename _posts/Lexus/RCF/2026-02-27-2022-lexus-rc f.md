# 2022 Lexus RC F

### Overview
The 2022 Lexus RC F is a high-performance luxury coupe that generally upholds Lexus's reputation for reliability. While its powerful V8 engine and sophisticated drivetrain are engineered for spirited driving, owners can expect a robust and dependable experience. The RC F is built with quality materials and advanced engineering, contributing to its strong reliability ratings. However, as with any performance vehicle, specific components may be subject to more scrutiny than those in a standard sedan. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern and overall dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Lexus RC F demonstrates excellent mechanical reliability, particularly with its potent 5.0L V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, which are largely free from widespread issues. The suspension and exterior components also perform admirably. Minor electrical concerns, such as occasional infotainment glitches and the possibility of premature battery failure, are the most commonly cited areas for potential attention. Interior components generally hold up well. Overall, the RC F offers a dependable ownership experience for a performance-oriented luxury coupe, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
