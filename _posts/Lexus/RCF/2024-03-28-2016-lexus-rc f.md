## 2016 Lexus RC F

The 2016 Lexus RC F is a high-performance coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 5.0-liter V8 engine that produces 467 horsepower and 389 lb-ft of torque. The RC F is available with a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its impressive performance, the 2016 Lexus RC F has had its share of problems.

### Engine

**5.0-liter V8**

* **Intake manifold runners sticking:** The intake manifold runners can stick, causing a loss of power and fuel economy. The solution is to replace the intake manifold. **$1000-$1500+**
* **Valve cover gaskets leaking:** The valve cover gaskets can leak, causing oil to leak onto the engine. The solution is to replace the valve cover gaskets. **$500-$1000+**
* **Timing chain tensioner failing:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip and damage the engine. The solution is to replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain. **$2000-$3000+**

### Transmission

**6-speed manual**

* **Clutch slipping:** The clutch can slip, causing difficulty shifting gears and a loss of power. The solution is to replace the clutch. **$1000-$1500+**
* **Transmission grinding:** The transmission can grind when shifting gears, indicating a problem with the synchros. The solution is to rebuild or replace the transmission. **$2000-$3000+**

**8-speed automatic**

* **Torque converter shudder:** The torque converter can shudder when shifting gears, causing a vibration. The solution is to replace the torque converter. **$1500-$2000+**
* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission can slip, causing a loss of power and difficulty shifting gears. The solution is to rebuild or replace the transmission. **$2000-$3000+**

### Suspension

* **Front suspension clunking:** The front suspension can clunk when going over bumps, indicating a problem with the struts or bushings. The solution is to replace the struts or bushings. **$500-$1000+**
* **Rear suspension sagging:** The rear suspension can sag, causing the car to sit lower in the back. The solution is to replace the rear springs or shocks. **$500-$1000+**

### Electrical

* **Battery draining:** The battery can drain quickly, even when the car is not being used. The solution is to replace the battery or diagnose and fix any electrical problems that are draining the battery. **$200-$500+**
* **Electrical shorts:** Electrical shorts can occur, causing various electrical problems, such as flickering lights or inoperative components. The solution is to diagnose and fix the electrical short. **Cost varies**
* **ECU software glitches:** The ECU software can have glitches, causing various problems, such as engine performance issues or transmission problems. The solution is to update the ECU software. **No cost if under warranty**

### Exterior

* **Headlight condensation:** The headlights can develop condensation inside, indicating a problem with the headlight seal. The solution is to replace the headlight seal or the entire headlight assembly. **$500-$1000+**
* **Tail light moisture:** The tail lights can develop moisture inside, indicating a problem with the tail light seal. The solution is to replace the tail light seal or the entire tail light assembly. **$500-$1000+**

### Interior

* **Climate control malfunctioning:** The climate control system can malfunction, causing problems such as the AC not working or the heat not working. The solution is to diagnose and fix the problem with the climate control system. **Cost varies**
* **Infotainment system freezing:** The infotainment system can freeze, causing it to be unresponsive. The solution is to restart the infotainment system or update the software. **No cost if under warranty**
* **Power seat malfunctioning:** The power seat can malfunction, causing it to be difficult to adjust or move. The solution is to diagnose and fix the problem with the power seat. **Cost varies**

### Summary

The 2016 Lexus RC F is a high-performance coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. However, it has had its share of problems, including engine issues, transmission issues, suspension issues, electrical issues, exterior issues, and interior issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be quite expensive to fix.
