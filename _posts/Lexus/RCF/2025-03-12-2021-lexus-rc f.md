The 2021 Lexus RC F is a high-performance coupe known for its naturally aspirated V8 engine and sharp handling. It offers a blend of luxury and sporty driving dynamics, appealing to enthusiasts seeking a visceral driving experience. The RC F's sole engine option is a 5.0-liter V8 producing 472 horsepower and 395 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Many appreciate the RC F for its distinctive styling, powerful engine, and comfortable interior. However, some criticisms include its relatively high price point, limited rear seat space, and the fact that it's not as technologically advanced as some of its competitors.
