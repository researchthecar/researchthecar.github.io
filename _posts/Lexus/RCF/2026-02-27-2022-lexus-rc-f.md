# 2022 Lexus RC F Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lexus RC F represents a compelling choice for enthusiasts looking for naturally aspirated V8 performance paired with traditional Lexus build quality and everyday usability. As a low-volume luxury sports coupe, it avoids many of the common mechanical pitfalls plaguing turbo competitors, though buyers should still evaluate a used model through a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Engine

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces approximately 472 horsepower and 395 lb-ft of torque. It is known for robust mechanical durability and high-revving performance, making it a reliable heart for this luxury performance coupe.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Direct-Shift Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features manual paddle shifters and aggressive downshift blips for track-inspired driving. It generally offers smooth daily driving characteristics alongside quick shifts when pushed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V615000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lexus&model=RC+F&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." The vehicle can be overloaded without the weight capacity information, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the tire information label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lexus RC F stands out as a dependable and thrilling option in the luxury sports coupe segment. Thanks to its strong V8 powerplant and solid construction, it offers peace of mind that is rare in high-performance classes, provided routine maintenance is verified.
