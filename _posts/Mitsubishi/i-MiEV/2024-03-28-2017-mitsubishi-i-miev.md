**Introduction:**
The 2017 Mitsubishi i-MiEV is an all-electric subcompact hatchback. It features a single 66-kWh electric motor that produces 66 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. The i-MiEV offers a range of up to 102 miles on a single charge. The i-MiEV is known for its affordable price, compact size, and environmental friendliness but is criticized for its limited range and cramped interior.

**Electrical**
* **Battery**
    * **Battery Prematurely Draining**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly even when the vehicle is parked. The dash may display a warning indicating the battery is low.
        * Solution: Replace or recondition the battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system experiences random failures, causing components like the lights, wipers, or power windows to malfunction.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty electrical components, wiring, or the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper motor fails, causing the wipers to stop working properly.
    * Solution: Replace the wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system malfunctions, causing the heat or air conditioning to not function properly.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components in the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks fail to operate properly, preventing the doors from being locked or unlocked.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2017 Mitsubishi i-MiEV has a limited number of reported issues. The most common concerns are electrical problems, particularly battery issues, and electrical system failures. Other issues include windshield wiper motor failure, climate control malfunctions, and door lock malfunctions. The severity and cost of the repairs vary depending on the specific issue encountered. Overall, the i-MiEV is a reliable vehicle with affordable repair costs.
