# 2016 Mitsubishi i-MiEV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mitsubishi i-MiEV is a pioneering all-electric subcompact hatchback designed primarily for urban commuting. As one of the more affordable early EV options on the used market, it offers straightforward packaging and zero tailpipe emissions, though buyers should expect modest power and limited driving range compared to modern electric vehicles.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Electric Motor (66 hp)

The propulsion system features a permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing approximately 66 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, powered by a 16 kWh lithium-ion battery pack. It provides adequate pep for city speeds, though overall highway power and modern driving range are quite limited.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Direct Drive

Power is sent to the rear wheels via a single-speed fixed-gear direct-drive transmission. This setup is mechanically simple and generally reliable, lacking the complex multi-speed gear changes found in traditional internal combustion vehicles.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V804000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Vacuum](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=i-MiEV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Without the brake vacuum pump, the driver will experience a sudden loss of braking assist, lengthening the distance needed to stop the vehicle and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the brake vacuum pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V793000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=i-MiEV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may rupture due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator rupture may result in metal fragments striking the vehicle occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger side frontal air bag inflator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mitsubishi i-MiEV represents a niche choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking an inexpensive electric commuter car. While its mechanical simplicity keeps traditional powertrain complaints very low, shoppers should carefully verify remaining high-voltage battery health and overall driving range before purchasing.
