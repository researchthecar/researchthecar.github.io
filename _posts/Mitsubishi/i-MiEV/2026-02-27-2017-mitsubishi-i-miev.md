# 2017 Mitsubishi i-MiEV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Mitsubishi i-MiEV is a distinctive, ultra-compact electric vehicle tailored primarily for dense urban environments and short-distance commuting. Because it features an electric powertrain rather than a traditional internal combustion engine, its mechanical profile differs vastly from standard compact cars. Used car buyers looking at the i-MiEV will find a minimalist cabin and a simple propulsion layout, though battery health and overall electric system integrity remain critical areas for inspection prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (66 hp)

This permanent magnet synchronous electric motor delivers approximately 66 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. It provides straightforward power for city driving with very few moving parts, translating to minimal routine powertrain maintenance compared to gas-powered vehicles.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

The single-speed fixed gear transmission channels power directly from the electric motor to the rear wheels without traditional gear shifting. Its direct-drive design is mechanically simple and generally robust over the vehicle's lifespan.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V804000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Vacuum](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=i-MiEV&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Without the brake vacuum pump, the driver will experience a sudden loss of braking assist, lengthening the distance needed to stop the vehicle and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the brake vacuum pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V793000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=i-MiEV&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may rupture due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator rupture may result in metal fragments striking the vehicle occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger side frontal air bag inflator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Mitsubishi i-MiEV offers a simplistic, ultra-efficient approach to urban electric mobility. While its lack of a traditional engine and multi-speed transmission reduces typical mechanical upkeep, prospective buyers must carefully evaluate the remaining high-voltage battery capacity and overall electrical health. It serves best as an inexpensive, short-range secondary commuter vehicle.
