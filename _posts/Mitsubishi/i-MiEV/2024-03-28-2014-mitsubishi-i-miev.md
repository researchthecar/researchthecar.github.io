**Introduction**

The 2014 Mitsubishi i-MiEV is a fully electric subcompact hatchback. It features a single electric motor that produces 66 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. The i-MiEV is praised for its low operating costs, spacious interior, and peppy acceleration for a city car. However, it is criticized for its short driving range, lack of fast-charging capability, and rudimentary interior.

**Electric Motor**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Pack Degradation**
  * Description/Symptoms: Over time, the battery pack's capacity decreases, reducing the driving range.
  * Solution: Battery pack replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
* **Charging Port Issues**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or failure to charge the vehicle.
  * Solution: Charging port replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 15V369000
* **Electrical Harness Damage**
  * Description/Symptoms: Damage to electrical harnesses can cause various electrical malfunctions.
  * Solution: Harness repair or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Issues**
  * Description/Symptoms: Wiper blades fail to make proper contact with the windshield, leading to poor visibility.
  * Solution: Wiper blade replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20 - $50
* **Door Lock Malfunctions**
  * Description/Symptoms: Door locks fail to operate or become stuck, making it difficult to enter or exit the vehicle.
  * Solution: Door lock actuator replacement or repair
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracking**
  * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel develops cracks and discolors.
  * Solution: Dashboard panel replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Steering Wheel Trim Peeling**
  * Description/Symptoms: The leather covering on the steering wheel peels and wears prematurely.
  * Solution: Steering wheel trim replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or clunking noises coming from the front suspension while driving over bumps.
  * Solution: Strut replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Summary**

Overall, the 2014 Mitsubishi i-MiEV is a reliable and economical electric car. The most severe issues are related to battery degradation and electrical problems, which can be costly to fix. However, the majority of issues reported are minor and inexpensive to resolve.
