### Overview
The 2022 Mitsubishi Xpander, a popular compact MPV, generally exhibits a good reliability record, particularly in its core mechanical components. Owners often praise its practicality and fuel efficiency for its segment. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the Xpander tends to avoid widespread, severe problems. Common areas of attention, if any arise, are typically minor and related to specific component performance rather than systemic failures. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear and tear, to provide a clear picture for potential buyers and owners.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mitsubishi Xpander demonstrates strong reliability across its primary mechanical systems, including the 1.5L MIVEC engine and its available automatic and manual transmissions. Suspension components are also generally robust. Minor electrical quirks, primarily with the infotainment system, are the most commonly noted, but these are typically resolved with software updates and do not indicate systemic flaws. Exterior and interior components are largely free of widespread issues. Overall, the Xpander is a dependable choice for families seeking a practical and reliable vehicle in its class, with minimal expected technical headaches beyond standard maintenance.
