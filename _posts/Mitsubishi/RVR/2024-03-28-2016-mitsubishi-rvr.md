**2016 Mitsubishi RVR Overview**

The 2016 Mitsubishi RVR is a compact SUV offered with two engine and transmission options: a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine with a 6-speed CVT transmission (ES trim) and a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine with a 4-speed automatic transmission (SE trim). The 2.0L engine produces 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.4L engine produces 170 horsepower and 167 lb-ft of torque. Praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior, the RVR has also garnered criticism for its lackluster performance, poor sound insulation, and outdated infotainment system.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-cylinder**

* **Valve Body Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or erratic shifting, difficulty changing gears
    * Solution: Valve body replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Knock Sensor**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine knock, reduced performance, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Knock sensor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4L 4-cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-speed CVT**

* **CVT Fluid Contamination**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, shuddering, difficulty accelerating
    * Solution: CVT fluid flush or замена
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Overheating**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, overheating warning light illumination
    * Solution: Transmission cooler installation or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Sway Bar End Links**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises when turning or going over bumps
    * Solution: Replacement of end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions, battery not charging
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Battery replacement or repair of electrical system drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Loose Side Mirrors**
    * Description/Symptoms: Mirrors shaking or vibrating excessively
    * Solution: Mirror housing adjustment or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buzzing or rattling noises from the dashboard
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Trim Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Peeling or bubbling seat fabric
    * Solution: Seat trim replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600 per seat
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Mitsubishi RVR presents some common issues that can vary in severity and repair costs. While some issues, such as valve body failure or CVT fluid contamination, can be significant and expensive to fix, others like loose side mirrors or battery discharge are relatively minor and less costly. Overall, the RVR's reliability has been average for the model year, with some areas of concern but also areas of strength. It's important to research potential issues and factor repair costs into the decision-making process before purchasing or maintaining a 2016 Mitsubishi RVR.
