# 2022 Mitsubishi RVR

### Overview
The 2022 Mitsubishi RVR, also known as the Outlander Sport in some markets, is a subcompact crossover SUV that aims to provide an affordable and practical option for urban driving. It generally offers a straightforward ownership experience with a focus on value. Reliability data for the 2022 model year suggests a generally solid performance, with most owners reporting few significant mechanical or electrical problems. The RVR typically comes with a standard powertrain that has seen incremental updates over its lifespan, contributing to its dependable nature. While not a segment leader in terms of advanced technology or performance, its mechanical simplicity often translates to fewer potential points of failure, making it a sensible choice for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing consistent operation over cutting-edge features.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mitsubishi RVR demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components. Both the 2.0L and 2.4L engines, along with the CVT and 8-speed automatic transmissions, show no significant widespread issues. The suspension system is also robust. Minor electrical concerns, primarily related to the infotainment system's responsiveness or connectivity, are the most commonly cited, though these are typically resolved with software updates and are not indicative of major system failures. Exterior and interior components are generally durable. Overall, the RVR is a dependable choice for its segment, offering peace of mind for owners concerned about frequent repairs.
