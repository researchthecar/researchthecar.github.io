**2019 Mitsubishi RVR**

The 2019 Mitsubishi RVR is a subcompact crossover SUV that is powered by a 2.0-liter inline-4 engine that produces 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The RVR is available in both front-wheel drive (FWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD) configurations.

The RVR has received positive reviews for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and affordability. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes an excessive amount of oil, requiring frequent top-ups. The issue may be caused by worn piston rings or valve seals.
    * Solution: Replace the worn piston rings or valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail prematurely, causing the engine to lose power or stall. The issue may be caused by a defective chain or tensioner.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Hesitation and Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or jerks during gear changes. The issue may be caused by a faulty transmission control module (TCM) or torque converter.
    * Solution: Update the TCM software or replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips out of gear while driving. The issue may be caused by worn clutch plates or a faulty valve body.
    * Solution: Replace the worn clutch plates or valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front struts fail prematurely, causing the vehicle to bounce or handle poorly. The issue may be caused by a defective strut mount or bearing.
    * Solution: Replace the front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per strut
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sway bar links fail prematurely, causing the vehicle to roll excessively in corners. The issue may be caused by a defective link or bushing.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per link
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains excessively when the vehicle is parked. The issue may be caused by a parasitic draw from an electrical component.
    * Solution: Identify and fix the parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the draw
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails prematurely, resulting in a loss of electrical power. The issue may be caused by a faulty voltage regulator or diode.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Crossbars Rusting**
    * Description/Symptoms: The roof rack crossbars rust prematurely. The issue may be caused by a lack of protective coating.
    * Solution: Replace the crossbars or apply a protective coating.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per crossbar
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system malfunctions, resulting in inconsistent temperatures or airflow. The issue may be caused by a faulty actuator or sensor.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mitsubishi RVR has received mixed reviews from owners. While the vehicle offers good fuel efficiency and a spacious interior, it has also been plagued by various issues, including excessive oil consumption, timing chain failure, transmission problems, suspension failures, and electrical issues. The severity of the issues ranges from minor inconveniences to major breakdowns, and the cost to fix can be significant.
