**Introduction:**

The 2012 Mitsubishi RVR is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable and fuel-efficient option for drivers. It is powered by a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine that produces 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. The RVR is available with either a CVT or a 6-speed manual transmission. Drivers appreciate the RVR's spacious interior, good fuel economy, and affordable price point. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's reliability, particularly with the engine and transmission.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **Oil Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the engine's oil pan can cause oil to drip onto the ground.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause the timing chain to skip or break, resulting in catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT Transmission**

* **Shuddering or Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shudder or hesitate during acceleration or gear changes.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding or Difficulty Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may grind or become difficult to shift gears, especially into reverse.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission synchronizers or bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Battery:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may fail prematurely, causing the vehicle to experience starting problems or electrical issues.
    * Solution: Replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Wind Noise Around Windows:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise may be noticeable around the windows, particularly at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Adjust the window seals or install wind deflectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Trim Rattle or Buzzing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or misaligned trim pieces may rattle or buzz during driving.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seatbelt Warning Light Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seatbelt warning light may illuminate even when the seatbelt is fastened.
    * Solution: Replace the seatbelt buckle sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Mitsubishi RVR is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The engine and transmission have been known to experience some problems, particularly the oil leak and timing chain tensioner failure. However, these issues are relatively rare and can be fixed for a reasonable cost. The RVR is still a good option for drivers who prioritize fuel economy and affordability.
