**Introduction:**

The 2018 Mitsubishi RVR is a compact crossover SUV available with a 2.0L or 2.4L four-cylinder engine and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and spacious interior, the RVR also faces criticism for its lackluster performance and unimpressive handling.

**Engine**

**2.0L Engine**
* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Consuming more than 1 quart of oil per 1,000 miles.
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, misfiring, and stalling.
    * Solution: Timing chain replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4L Engine**
* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**
* **Shift Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or delay during acceleration.
    * Solution: CVT fluid replacement or software update.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Premature Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering, slipping, or not engaging.
    * Solution: CVT replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps.
    * Solution: Strut mount replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control not functioning.
    * Solution: Cruise control switch or module replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear wiper not functioning.
    * Solution: Rear wiper motor replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower motor not functioning.
    * Solution: Blower motor replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges or displays not functioning correctly.
    * Solution: Instrument cluster replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Mitsubishi RVR is a budget-friendly compact crossover that offers good fuel economy and a spacious interior. However, it is plagued by several reliability issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars for minor issues to thousands of dollars for major repairs. These issues can be a major inconvenience and financial burden for owners, tarnishing the overall experience of the vehicle.
