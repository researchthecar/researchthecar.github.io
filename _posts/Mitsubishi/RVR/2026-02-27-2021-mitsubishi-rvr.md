# 2021 Mitsubishi RVR

### Overview
The 2021 Mitsubishi RVR, also known as the Outlander Sport in some markets, is a subcompact crossover SUV that aims to provide an affordable and practical option for urban driving. It typically features a straightforward mechanical setup with a focus on value. Reliability for the 2021 model year generally aligns with expectations for its segment, with most owners reporting a positive ownership experience. However, as with any vehicle, specific components can occasionally present issues. This report details the technical reliability data for the 2021 Mitsubishi RVR, covering its primary mechanical and electrical systems, as well as its interior and exterior components, to provide a comprehensive overview for potential buyers and current owners.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display may freeze, become unresponsive, or require a reboot.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment unit. Factory reset if issues persist.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


### Battery
**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may drain faster than expected, leading to starting issues.
- **Solution:** Check for parasitic draws or faulty charging system components. Battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mitsubishi RVR demonstrates a generally reliable performance, particularly in its engine and suspension systems, which show no widespread reported issues. The Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) is the most notable area for potential concern, with some owners experiencing hesitation or jerking, often resolvable with maintenance or software updates, though more serious issues can lead to costly repairs. Electrical components, specifically the infotainment screen and battery, have seen isolated reports of glitches or premature drain, respectively. Exterior and interior components are largely free from significant reliability concerns. Overall, the RVR is a dependable choice for its segment, provided potential buyers are aware of the minor transmission and electrical quirks.
