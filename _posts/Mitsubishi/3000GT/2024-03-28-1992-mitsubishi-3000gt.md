**Introduction:**
The 1992 Mitsubishi 3000GT is a high-performance sports car powered by a twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 engine. It is available with a 4-speed automatic or 5-speed manual transmission. The engine produces 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. The 3000GT is praised for its impressive performance and sleek design, but it is known for its reliability issues.

**Engine**
**3.0-liter Twin-Turbocharged V6**
- None Found

**Transmission**
**4-Speed Automatic**
- **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter may fail, causing the transmission to slip or lose power.
  - Solution: Replace the torque converter.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
- **Solenoid Issues:** The transmission solenoids may become faulty, leading to shifting problems or transmission failure.
  - Solution: Replace the faulty solenoids.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**5-Speed Manual**
- **Synchronizer Ring Problems:** The synchronizer rings in the manual transmission may wear out prematurely, causing difficulty shifting gears.
  - Solution: Replace the synchronizer rings.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**
- **Shock Absorber Leaks:** The shock absorbers may develop leaks, leading to a rough ride and reduced handling.
  - Solution: Replace the shock absorbers.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+

**Electrical**
- **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail, causing electrical problems and battery drain.
  - Solution: Replace the alternator.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
- **Wiring Harness Issues:** The electrical wiring harness may become damaged or faulty, leading to a variety of electrical problems.
  - Solution: Repair or replace the wiring harness.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**
- **Clear Coat Failure:** The clear coat on the paint may fail, causing the paint to fade or peel.
  - Solution: Refinish the paint.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Interior**
- **Dashboard Plastic Cracks:** The plastic dashboard may crack or deform due to heat.
  - Solution: Replace the dashboard.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
- **Seat Adjuster Failure:** The seat adjuster may become damaged or fail, making it difficult to adjust the seat.
  - Solution: Replace the seat adjuster.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Summary:**
The 1992 Mitsubishi 3000GT is a high-performance sports car with a number of reliability issues. The most severe issues involve the transmission and suspension, which can be expensive to repair. Electrical problems and interior issues are also common. Overall, the 3000GT is not recommended for those seeking a reliable vehicle.
