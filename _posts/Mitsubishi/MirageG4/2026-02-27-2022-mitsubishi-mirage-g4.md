# 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 means looking at a budget-friendly subcompact sedan known primarily for its high fuel efficiency. While it offers straightforward daily commuting and an appealing price point, used car buyers should carefully inspect vehicle history and functionality during a test drive to ensure everything is operating smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

The 1.2-liter three-cylinder engine produces approximately 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing fuel economy over spirited performance. It provides basic, utilitarian motivation for daily commuting with a simple design.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The automatic CVT distributes power smoothly to the front wheels while maximizing fuel efficiency in city and highway driving. Buyers should listen for any unusual whining or hesitation during a test drive.

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

The available 5-speed manual transmission offers a more direct shifting experience for drivers who prefer a traditional three-pedal setup. It is generally robust, provided the clutch has been maintained properly.

## Brakes

### Front Brakes

Brake components should be thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure adequate pad and rotor life remain.

**Premature Brake Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Front brakes may wear out unexpectedly early, leading to reduced stopping power or scraping noises.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for premature wear and replace components as necessary.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Electric Power Steering

Issues related to the electric power steering and front-end mechanical noise have been reported by some owners.

**Steering Wandering and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear loud mechanical noises upon ignition or steering activation, alongside highway wandering that requires constant correction to stay centered.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the steering components, alignment, and electric power steering system for wear or malfunction.

## Electrical

### Dashboard Instrument Illumination

Electrical accessories and instrument lighting should be tested under various lighting conditions before purchasing.

**Difficult-to-Read Gauges**
- **Symptoms:** Instrument panel gauges can be hard to see in shadowed areas or during overcast days, even with maximum brightness settings.
- **Solution:** Verify instrument cluster illumination operation and check if software or hardware adjustments can improve visibility.

The 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is an economical choice for budget-conscious buyers, but a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is recommended. Pay close attention to steering behavior, brake wear, and electrical systems during your test drive to ensure a reliable ownership experience.
