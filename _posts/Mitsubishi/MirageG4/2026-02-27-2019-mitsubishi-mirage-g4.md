# 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage G4, buyers are typically looking for maximum fuel efficiency and budget-friendly daily transportation. While this subcompact sedan delivers on economy, owner reports submitted to the NHTSA point to a few recurring areas of concern regarding electrical stability, fuel system wiring, and supplemental restraint systems.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

The 2019 Mirage G4 comes equipped with a 1.2-liter 3-cylinder engine producing around 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with front-wheel drive and offers basic, ultra-efficient transportation, though owners have reported occasional unexpected stalling issues.

**Random Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle shuts off completely while driving at highway speeds without prior warning lights, and eventually fails to restart.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the ignition, fuel delivery, and engine control modules to diagnose the root electrical or fuel fault.

### Fuel Pump Wiring

Owner feedback highlights vulnerabilities in specific rear body and fuel pump wiring harnesses, which can degrade and cause operational failures.

**Degraded Rear Wiring and Fuel System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Wires near the rear fuel pump fray or degrade over time, leading to potential fuel system failure and fire hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear harness wiring near the fuel pump for fraying or damage, and repair or replace compromised circuits.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission (CVT) routes power to the front wheels, prioritizing fuel economy over spirited performance. While no major transmission mechanical failures dominate owner complaints for this model year, overall acceleration feel is leisurely.

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A 5-speed manual transmission is available on base trims, offering a more direct connection for drivers who prefer shifting their own gears. It generally provides simple, mechanical durability without complex automatic hydraulic controls.

## Safety

### Airbag System

The restraint systems are designed to protect occupants, but multiple owner reports note warning light illuminations and failures of airbags to deploy during crashes.

**Airbag Malfunction and Non-Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates persistently, or airbags fail to deploy during a physical impact.
- **Solution:** Have the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) diagnostic codes read by a professional to identify faulty sensors, modules, or clocksprings.

The 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is an economical commuter car, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect vehicle history and functionality. Pay close attention to electrical accessories, fuel pump wiring integrity, and dashboard warning lights during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure safe, reliable operation.
