**Introduction to the 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage G4**

The 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is a subcompact sedan renowned for its fuel efficiency and affordability. It is powered by a 1.2-liter three-cylinder engine that produces 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Mirage G4 has received positive reviews for its low operating costs, spacious interior for its size, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, it has also been criticized for its underpowered engine, uninspired handling, and basic interior materials.

**Engine**

* **1.2-liter Three-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Some vehicles may exhibit excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings or rebuild the engine.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: In rare cases, the timing chain may fail prematurely, causing catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain and associated components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**
    * **Hesitation/Jerking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The CVT may hesitate or jerk during acceleration or shifting, affecting performance and driveability.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the CVT unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: In extreme cases, the CVT may fail completely, rendering the vehicle inoperable.
        * Solution: Replace the CVT unit and associated components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, failing to adjust temperature or air flow properly.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control unit or associated sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 generally has a good reliability record, with few major issues reported. However, certain engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair. The most severe issue is timing chain failure, which can lead to catastrophic engine damage. Other issues, such as battery drain, alternator failure, and climate control malfunction, are less severe but still require attention and repair. Overall, the Mirage G4's reliability is average, with some potential issues that should be considered before purchasing.
