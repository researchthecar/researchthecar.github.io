# 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is an ultra-compact commuter car known primarily for its high fuel efficiency and budget-friendly price point. While it offers simple daily transportation, used car buyers should note that owner feedback highlights several early concerns regarding steering stability, braking frequency, and high-speed handling.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

The Mirage G4 is powered by a 1.2-liter naturally aspirated three-cylinder engine producing 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque. It is paired strictly for efficiency rather than performance, and powertrain complaints have been minimal regarding internal mechanical failures.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed through an electronically controlled continuously variable transmission (CVT) designed to maximize fuel economy. While some trims historically offered a 5-speed manual, the CVT is standard on most models, and owners have reported few mechanical breakdowns.

## Suspension

### Suspension and Handling

Highway stability can be unsettling due to light vehicle weight and suspension tracking.

**Vehicle Sway and Fishtailing at Highway Speeds**
- **Symptoms:** While driving at highway speeds around 65 MPH, the vehicle may sway left to right or fishtail, requiring the driver to slow down.
- **Solution:** Check tire pressures, suspension alignment, shock absorbers, and rear suspension geometry for wear or looseness.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking complaints focus on unexpectedly frequent pad replacements and soft pedal feel.

**Rapid Brake Pad Wear and Pedal Travel**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pads may wear out prematurely every few months, or the brake pedal may feel soft and sink excessively when applied.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and the master cylinder for premature wear or hydraulic pressure loss, and replace worn components as necessary.

## Steering

### Steering System

Owner reports indicate potential concerns with steering responsiveness and highway control.

**Steering Defect and Handling Instability**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the vehicle feels out of control, exceptionally slow to respond, or difficult to keep stable at higher speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the steering linkage, electronic power steering module, and front-end alignment inspected by a qualified technician.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 appeals to budget-conscious shoppers prioritizing fuel economy, but prospective buyers should inspect suspension alignment, steering feel, and brake wear closely during a test drive.
