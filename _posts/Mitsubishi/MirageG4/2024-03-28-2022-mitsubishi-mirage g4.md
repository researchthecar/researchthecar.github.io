**Introduction**

The 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is a subcompact sedan known for its outstanding fuel economy. It is equipped with a 1.2-liter three-cylinder engine, paired with either a five-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The engine delivers 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque. Despite its modest power, the Mirage G4 is praised for its low price point, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. However, it is also criticized for its lackluster performance, cramped interior, and poor sound insulation.

**Summary**

The 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 has a relatively low frequency of reported issues. The most common issues include CVT shuddering and loose body panels, which can be addressed at a reasonable cost. Overall, the Mirage G4 is a reliable and budget-friendly vehicle with limited major problems.
