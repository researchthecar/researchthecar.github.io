# 2017 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is an economical subcompact sedan designed primarily for budget-conscious commuters seeking high fuel efficiency. While it offers practical transportation, used car buyers should carefully inspect vehicle history and address potential electrical and safety concerns reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

The 1.2-liter three-cylinder engine produces 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing fuel economy over spirited performance. It is generally a straightforward and reliable powerplant, though some owners have reported intermittent power loss issues linked to electrical integration.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 5-speed manual transmission is standard on base trims, offering simple mechanical operation and low long-term maintenance costs for buyers who prefer shifting gears themselves.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The available continuously variable transmission optimizes fuel efficiency for daily driving. Prospective buyers should test drive CVT models thoroughly to ensure smooth operation without hesitation or abnormal noise.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback indicates rare instances of soft pedal feel that require careful inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Brake Pedal Softness**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal occasionally travels all the way to the floor during city driving before recovering after a quick pump.
- **Solution:** Bleed the brake system to remove air, and inspect the master cylinder and brake lines for leaks or wear.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Owner complaints highlight occasional unexpected electrical glitches, ranging from intermittent dashboard resets to window motor failures.

**Sudden Electrical Shutoff**
- **Symptoms:** All vehicle electronics, dashboard lights, and the radio shut off unexpectedly while driving, followed by a system reset after rough roads or potholes.
- **Solution:** Inspect battery terminals, main ground connections, and wiring harnesses for loose or damaged connections.

**Power Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows fail to operate, occasionally leaving windows stuck in the open position during inclement weather.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty window motor or window regulator assembly.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety system complaints primarily center around warning lights and owner concerns regarding proper deployment during severe impacts.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The supplemental restraint system warning light illuminates on the dashboard, indicating a potential fault in the airbag network.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the diagnostic trouble codes and inspect airbag sensors and wiring.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V330000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Mirage+G4&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Air bags that do not deploy increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA notified owners, and dealers will reprogram the SRS-ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is an affordable, high-mpg commuter car, but buyers should conduct a thorough inspection before purchasing. Pay close attention to electrical accessories, verify proper brake and airbag system operation, and test drive both the manual and CVT options to ensure mechanical health.
