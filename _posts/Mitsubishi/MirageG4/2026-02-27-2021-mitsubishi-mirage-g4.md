# 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is an economical subcompact sedan designed primarily for budget-conscious commuters seeking high fuel efficiency. While basic mechanical systems tend to be straightforward, used car buyers should inspect modern driver-assist features and electronic modules carefully to ensure all warning systems function properly.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 1.2-liter 3-cylinder engine producing 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing efficiency over acceleration. It is a simple, naturally aspirated design with generally dependable mechanical longevity.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 5-speed manual transmission is available on base trims, offering a simpler mechanical setup that avoids the complexities of continuously variable designs.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The automatic option is a continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive, designed to maximize fuel economy during daily urban commuting.

## Safety

### Driver Assistance Electronics

Electrical complaints center primarily around sensitive advanced driver-assistance sensors and associated dashboard warning indicators.

**Forward Collision Mitigation Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden activation of warning lights on the driver's console, accompanied by an inoperable forward collision mitigation or lane departure system.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer or qualified technician scan the vehicle computer modules for fault codes and recalibrate or replace faulty sensor units as necessary.
