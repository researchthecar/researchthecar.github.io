**Introduction**

The 2023 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is a subcompact sedan that comes equipped with a 1.2L 3-cylinder engine mated to a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It offers a budget-friendly option for those seeking fuel efficiency. Praised for its low price point and decent gas mileage, the Mirage G4 has received criticism for its lackluster performance and cramped interior.

**Engine**

1.2L 3-cylinder, 78 horsepower, 74 lb-ft of torque

**Transmission**

CVT
