**Introduction:**
The 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is a subcompact sedan powered by a 1.2-liter 3-cylinder engine producing 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque. It is offered with a 5-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Mirage G4 is known for its fuel efficiency, but it also has some notable drawbacks.

**Engine**
* **1.2-liter 3-cylinder Engine**
    * **Engine Stalling**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine may stall intermittently, especially when idling or decelerating.
        * Solution: Replace the throttle position sensor or clean the throttle body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings or valve stem seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Check Engine Light**
        * Description/Symptoms: The check engine light may illuminate due to various issues, such as a faulty oxygen sensor or fuel injector.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the underlying cause.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Transmission**
* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Grinding Noises**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may make grinding noises when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replace the damaged gears or synchronizers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **CVT Transmission**
    * **Shuddering or Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shudder or slip when accelerating or shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * **Overheating**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may overheat and go into limp mode.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid or cooler.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Suspension**
* **Loose or Rattling Suspension**
    * Description/Symptoms: The suspension may make rattling noises or feel loose when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the worn or damaged suspension components, such as struts, shocks, or control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the car to not start.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and repair any electrical components causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Electrical Shorts**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may experience intermittent shorts, causing fuses to blow or components to fail.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the shorted wires or components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**
* **Rearview Camera Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rearview camera may fail to display the image on the dashboard screen.
    * Solution: Replace the rearview camera.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers may fail to work or may operate erratically.
    * Solution: Replace the wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+

**Interior**
* **Dashboard Panel Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel may rattle or make creaking noises when driving.
    * Solution: Secure the loose panel or replace the damaged clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not work properly, leading to inadequate heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Replace the HVAC actuator or repair the electrical wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary:**
Overall, the 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 has some significant issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. These problems can be costly to repair, especially the transmission issues. While the Mirage G4 is known for its fuel efficiency, the reliability issues may outweigh the savings in gas costs.
