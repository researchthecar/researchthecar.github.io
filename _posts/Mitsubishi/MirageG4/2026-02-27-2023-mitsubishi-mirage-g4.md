# 2023 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is an ultra-budget subcompact sedan that prioritizes high fuel economy and an affordable price point over performance or refinement. When shopping for a used model, understanding the vehicle's standard powertrain options and minor owner-reported quirks can help you evaluate prospective purchases. While complaints are relatively limited, buyers should inspect specific mechanical and driver-assistance features during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L I3 3A92 Engine

The Mirage G4 comes equipped with a 1.2-liter naturally aspirated three-cylinder engine producing approximately 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque. Connected to either a 5-speed manual or a continuously variable transmission, this engine is designed strictly for basic commuting and maximum fuel efficiency rather than brisk acceleration.

**Valve Cover Oil Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported oil leaks developing around the back and left side of the valve cover at very low mileages.
- **Solution:** Inspect the valve cover gasket for seepage and replace the gasket or seal as necessary.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The automatic option is a continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive, tuned primarily to maximize fuel economy in city and highway driving. Buyers should listen for unusual whines or hesitation during acceleration to ensure healthy operation.

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 5-speed manual transmission is available on base trims, offering a more direct mechanical connection for budget-focused drivers. It generally proves dependable if the clutch has been maintained properly by previous owners.

## Interior

### Seats and Cabin Comfort

The interior emphasizes durable, low-cost materials to maintain an accessible purchase price, though ergonomics may not suit every driver.

**Seat Discomfort**
- **Symptoms:** Front seat occupants may experience back and hip discomfort on longer drives due to limited bolstering and cushioning.
- **Solution:** Consider using aftermarket seat cushions or lumbar support pillows to improve long-distance ergonomics.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Standard safety tech provides basic driver aids, though system sensitivity and real-world responsiveness can occasionally draw owner complaints.

**Automatic Emergency Braking Inconsistency**
- **Symptoms:** Some drivers report that the automatic braking system fails to engage properly or assist when approaching objects closely.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect sensor calibration and clear any related error codes in the driver-assistance module.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Mitsubishi Mirage G4 is a straightforward, budget-conscious choice for daily commuters who value fuel economy above all else. While initial owner complaints highlight isolated issues like early valve cover leaks or driver-assist quirks, the vehicle remains a simple machine. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the engine bay and verify that all electronic safety features operate correctly.
