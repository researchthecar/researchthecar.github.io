**Introduction:**
The 2019 Mitsubishi L200 is a mid-size pickup truck available with a 2.4L turbocharged diesel engine paired with either a 6-speed manual or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The L200 is praised for its ruggedness, off-road capabilities, and spacious interior, while some criticisms include a lack of modern features and a rough ride quality.

**Engine**
2.4L Turbocharged Diesel

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive smoke, loss of power, turbocharger noise
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Injector Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, lack of power
    * Solution: Injector replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500 per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
6-Speed Manual

* **Clutch Plate Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Clutch kit replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

6-Speed Automatic

* **Solenoid Valve Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, delayed shifts, harsh shifting
    * Solution: Solenoid valve replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, broken leaf springs
    * Solution: Leaf spring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per leaf spring
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shock Absorber Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor shock absorption, uneven ride
    * Solution: Shock absorber replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per shock absorber
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **ECU Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine performance issues, warning lights, electrical failures
    * Solution: ECU reflash or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining, low electrical system voltage
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken door handles, difficulty opening doors
    * Solution: Door handle replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per handle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Faults:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning gauges, blank screens, warning lights
    * Solution: Instrument cluster replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2019 Mitsubishi L200 generally has a good reliability record, but some issues can occur. Engine problems like turbocharger failures and injector failures can be costly to repair, while issues like leaf spring failures and ECU problems are less severe but still require attention. Overall, maintenance and repair costs for the L200 tend to be moderate compared to similar vehicles in its class.
