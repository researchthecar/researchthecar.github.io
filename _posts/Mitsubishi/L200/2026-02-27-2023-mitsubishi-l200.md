# 2023 Mitsubishi L200

### Overview
The 2023 Mitsubishi L200, also known as the Triton in some markets, is a robust and capable pickup truck designed for both work and leisure. It is generally recognized for its durability and straightforward engineering, which contributes to its overall reliability. Mitsubishi has a long-standing reputation for building tough vehicles, and the L200/Triton continues this legacy. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the L200 typically performs well in reliability surveys, especially concerning its core mechanical components. Owners often praise its off-road prowess and load-carrying capacity. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective buyers and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 2.4L 4D56 Turbo Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Glow Plug Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 2.4L 4N15 MIVEC Turbo Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced DPF regeneration or cleaning; replacement if severely clogged.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable delay when shifting gears or a rough transition between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or potential internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500


### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Standard Leaf Spring Rear
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start after sitting, dimming lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find parasitic draw, replacement of faulty component or battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Mitsubishi L200 demonstrates a solid reliability record, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. While specific issues like turbocharger problems or DPF regeneration can occur with the diesel engines, they are not universally reported and often stem from specific operating conditions or maintenance practices. Transmission issues are infrequent, and suspension components, while subject to wear, are generally robust. Electrical gremlins are minimal, and interior and exterior elements are typically durable. Overall, the L200 is a dependable choice for a pickup truck, with most potential issues being manageable and repairable within reasonable cost expectations for its class.
