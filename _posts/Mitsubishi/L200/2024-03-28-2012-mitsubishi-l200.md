**Introduction:**

The 2012 Mitsubishi L200 is a mid-size pickup truck with a reputation for durability and off-road capabilities. It is offered with a 2.5L turbodiesel engine that produces 178 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or a 5-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the L200 often praise its ruggedness, towing capacity, and affordability. However, they have also reported some common issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.5L Turbodiesel**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust
   * Solution: Replace intake manifold
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Belt Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rattling noises
   * Solution: Replace timing belt and water pump
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Noises When Shifting:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
   * Solution: Replace synchronizers or gears
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1500
   * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage gears properly
   * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $4000
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Sagging:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Rear end of vehicle sags
   * Solution: Replace leaf springs
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
   * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after prolonged periods of inactivity
   * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Rust:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Rust forms on the tailgate
   * Solution: Sand, prime, and paint tailgate
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Door locks fail to engage or disengage
   * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Mitsubishi L200 is a reliable pickup truck with some notable issues. While some problems like engine and transmission failures can be expensive to fix, many others are relatively minor and can be addressed for a reasonable cost. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
