## 2021 Mitsubishi L200

The 2021 Mitsubishi L200 comes standard with a 2.4L turbodiesel engine that produces 178 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. An automatic transmission is available as an option. People enjoy the L200 for its fuel efficiency and off-road capabilities, but some common complaints include a lack of interior space and a stiff ride.

### Engine
- 2.4L Diesel

**EGR Valve Failures**
Description/Symptoms: Engine power loss, decreased fuel efficiency, check engine light
Solution: Replace EGR valve
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission
- 6-Speed Manual
**Transmission Noise**
Description/Symptoms: Grinding or whining noises during shifting
Solution: Replace transmission gears
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
Recalls: None Found

- 6-Speed Automatic
**Shifting Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, slipping gears
Solution: Replace transmission fluid and filter, or transmission solenoid
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V620000

### Suspension

**Leaf Spring Failures**
Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, decreased ride height, rough ride
Solution: Replace leaf springs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per spring
Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, electrical system malfunctions
Solution: Replace battery or alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

**Rust on Frame**
Description/Symptoms: Appearance of rust on the vehicle frame
Solution: Apply rust-resistant coating, replace frame if necessary
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Interior

**Steering Wheel Buttons Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Buttons on steering wheel do not respond or function erratically
Solution: Replace steering wheel buttons or control module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

## Summary

While the 2021 Mitsubishi L200 is a capable and efficient vehicle, it is not without its issues. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, and suspension and electrical issues can compromise the vehicle's safety and reliability. Additionally, interior problems can make the vehicle less comfortable and convenient to drive. It's important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
