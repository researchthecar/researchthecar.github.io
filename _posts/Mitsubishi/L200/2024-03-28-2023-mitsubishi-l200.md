**Introduction:**

The 2023 Mitsubishi L200 is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a 2.4-liter turbocharged diesel engine mated to either a 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission. It is known for its ruggedness, off-road capabilities, and spacious interior. However, like any vehicle, it has had its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.4L Diesel**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, loss of power
        * Solution: Replacement of fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises
        * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Grinding Gears:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of gear selector components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Delays:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears
        * Solution: Transmission software update or replacement of transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken rear suspension, loss of ride height
    * Solution: Replacement of leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle failing to start after being parked
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic drain)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Moisture:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replacement of headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or improper temperature control
    * Solution: Replacement of climate control module or actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seatbelt Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: False seatbelt warnings, airbag deployment issues
    * Solution: Replacement of seatbelt sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Mitsubishi L200 is generally a reliable vehicle, but some specific issues have been reported. The most common issues involve engine components, such as fuel injector and turbocharger failures. Transmission issues and suspension failures have also been reported, while electrical and interior issues are less severe. The cost of repairs for these issues can range from affordable to expensive, depending on the severity of the problem. Overall, while the L200 is a capable off-roader, it is important to be aware of potential issues before making a purchase decision.
