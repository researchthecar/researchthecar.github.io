# 2012 Mitsubishi Outlander

### Overview
The 2012 Mitsubishi Outlander is a compact SUV that offered a range of engine and drivetrain options, catering to different needs for fuel efficiency and performance. Generally, the Outlander from this model year is considered a reasonably reliable vehicle, with many owners reporting positive experiences. However, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from mechanics and owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)
**CVT Fluid Leaks/Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation, slipping, whining noises, warning lights, reduced acceleration.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair leaks, flush and replace CVT fluid, potentially replace transmission.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500 (for fluid service/minor repairs), $3,000 - $7,000 (for replacement)

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Power Window Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, clicking noises.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty power window actuator motor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350 per window

**Keyless Entry System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Remote key fob not working, doors not locking/unlocking remotely.
- **Solution:** Reprogram key fob, replace key fob battery, or diagnose and repair receiver module.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Mitsubishi Outlander generally presents a good reliability record, especially with its 2.4L engine option. The primary area of concern tends to be the Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT), which can experience issues related to leaks or overheating if not properly maintained. Electrical components like power window actuators and keyless entry systems have also seen some isolated reports of failure. However, these are not typically widespread or catastrophic issues. For the most part, the suspension, exterior, and interior components have proven durable. Regular maintenance, particularly for the CVT fluid, is recommended to ensure longevity and prevent potential costly repairs.
