**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Outlander is a compact SUV available with three engine options: a 2.4L inline-4, a 3.0L V6, and a 2.2L turbocharged diesel inline-4. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic for the 2.4L and 3.0L engines and a 6-speed manual or 8-speed automatic for the diesel engine. The Outlander is praised for its spacious interior, advanced safety features, and comfortable ride. However, it has received some criticism for its dated design and lack of fuel efficiency.

**Engine**

**2.4L Inline-4**

* **Timing chain tensioner failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Unusual noise from the engine, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Oil consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6**

* **Piston ring failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil pressure, excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Engine overhaul or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $7,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.2L Turbocharged Diesel Inline-4**

* **Diesel particulate filter (DPF) clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of the DPF
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic (2.4L and 3.0L)**

* **Shift solenoid failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Replacement of the shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Automatic (2.2L Diesel)**

* **Valve body failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, loss of gears
    * Solution: Replacement of the valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the front end, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replacement of the strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after a short period of time
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the source of the electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, flickering dashboard lights
    * Solution: Replacement of the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight housings
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature, blower motor noise
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat heater failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heater does not work or overheats
    * Solution: Replacement of the seat heater element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Outlander has some potential issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The timing chain tensioner failure, piston ring failure, and transmission solenoid failure can be significant and costly to repair. However, overall, the severity and cost of these issues are not uncommon for vehicles in this class.
