**Introduction**

The 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander is a compact SUV that comes with two engine options: a 2.4-liter 4-cylinder and a 3.0-liter V6. Both engines are paired with a Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). The Outlander is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordable price point. However, it has received some criticism for its lackluster performance and subpar fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.4-liter 4-cylinder**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may burn excessive oil, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter V6**

No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Premature Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The CVT may fail prematurely, causing loss of power and transmission problems.
    * Solution: Replace the CVT.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A clunking or rattling noise may come from the front suspension when driving over bumps or rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace the front strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
    * Solution: Replace the battery and check for electrical system faults.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Rear Hatch**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear hatch may develop rust around the license plate area.
    * Solution: Replace the rear hatch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise may come from the dashboard, particularly when driving over rough roads.
    * Solution: Secure or replace loose dashboard components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander has a few reported issues, particularly in the engine and transmission categories. The potential for excessive oil consumption and premature CVT failure can be significant concerns. The other issues found are less critical and have relatively lower repair costs. Overall, it is advisable to research these potential issues before purchasing a 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander.
