# 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander offers a bold design and practical three-row seating, sharing its platform and mechanical foundations with the Nissan Rogue. While relatively fresh on the used market, buyers should review owner-reported concerns ranging from electronic glitches to occasional braking and powertrain behaviors.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 181 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting.

**Engine Idle and Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** RPM fluctuates erratically during idle, followed by a check engine light and coolant loss due to a failed head gasket.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing, ignition coil replacement if misfiring, and potentially head gasket replacement or engine service.

## Transmission

### CVT Automatic Transmission

The continuously variable transmission (CVT) is paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, prioritizing smooth power delivery and fuel efficiency.

**Unexpected Rollback on Inclines**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle rolls backward when stopped on slight hills while in drive, behaving unexpectedly for an automatic transmission.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and hill start assist systems inspected by a qualified technician.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is generally sound, though electronic brake-by-wire and ABS responsiveness have drawn isolated owner complaints.

**Brake Pedal Softness and Loss of Responsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal travels completely to the floor with little to no stopping response, particularly noted in demanding driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake-by-wire system, master cylinder, and brake fluid lines immediately.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Audio System

The cabin features modern digital displays and infotainment tech, though software bugs can occasionally disrupt audio and connectivity features.

**Random Volume Surges and Voice Activation Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Audio system suddenly blasts volume or voice activation engages spontaneously, potentially startling the driver.
- **Solution:** Check for infotainment software updates at a dealership.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Advanced safety features provide robust protection, but basic mechanical elements like seat belt retractors require operational verification during a used vehicle inspection.

**Driver Seat Belt Retraction Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver seat belt fails to retract properly, causing physical interference and potential damage to the vehicle door.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the seat belt assembly and retraction mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V369000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the A-IVI software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander, prioritize a thorough inspection of the brakes, electrical accessories, and powertrain operation. While many owners enjoy their vehicles without major hurdles, verifying service history and checking for unresolved electronic or mechanical complaints will help ensure a confident purchase.
