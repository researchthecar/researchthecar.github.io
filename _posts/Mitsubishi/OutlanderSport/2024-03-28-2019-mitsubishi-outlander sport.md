## 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport

The 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport is a compact crossover SUV that comes with one engine option and two transmission options. The 2.4-liter 4-cylinder engine produces 166 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque. The Outlander Sport is available with either a continuously variable transmission (CVT) or a 6-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Outlander Sport for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordable price. However, some people have complained about its lack of power, poor fuel economy, and dated infotainment system.

### Engine

- **2.4-liter 4-cylinder engine**

- **Excessive Oil Consumption**
  - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that their Outlander Sport consumes excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  - Solution: In some cases, a software update has been released to address this issue. In more severe cases, the engine may need to be replaced.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Timing Chain Noise**
  - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported hearing a rattling or ticking noise coming from the engine. This noise may be caused by a faulty timing chain.
  - Solution: Replacing the timing chain and related components is necessary to fix this issue.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
  - Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

- **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

- **Hesitation and Jerking**
  - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced hesitation and jerking during acceleration or shifting.
  - Solution: A software update may be available to address this issue. In some cases, the CVT may need to be replaced.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Interior

The 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport has some potential issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
