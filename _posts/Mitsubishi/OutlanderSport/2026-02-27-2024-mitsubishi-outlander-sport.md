# 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport, buyers will find a straightforward and budget-friendly compact crossover option. Reviewing real-world owner feedback helps highlight areas to watch out for during a test drive, ensuring you make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L MIVEC 4-Cylinder

The standard 2.0-liter MIVEC four-cylinder engine produces 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, offering basic daily commuting performance. It is generally regarded as a durable, straightforward powerplant, though acceleration is modest.

### 2.4L MIVEC 4-Cylinder

The available 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine generates 168 horsepower and 167 lb-ft of torque, providing slightly punchier acceleration. This engine option is dependable for routine driving needs.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The automatic continuously variable transmission pairs with both engine options and front-wheel or all-wheel drive. Buyers should pay close attention to smooth operation during test drives.

**Jerky Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may feel jerky or unpredictable during acceleration, particularly when navigating turns.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the powertrain control module and transmission operation to diagnose the hesitation or jerkiness.
