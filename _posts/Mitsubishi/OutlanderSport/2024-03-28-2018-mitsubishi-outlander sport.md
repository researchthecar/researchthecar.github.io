**Introduction to the 2018 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport**

The 2018 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport is a compact crossover SUV available with two engine and transmission options: a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine with a CVT transmission and a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 2.0L engine produces 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.4L engine produces 168 horsepower and 167 lb-ft of torque.

The Outlander Sport is praised for its spacious interior, affordability, and maneuverability. However, it receives criticism for its bland styling, poor fuel economy, and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **Knocking Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A distinct knocking sound emanating from the engine compartment.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake valve timing belt tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **Oil Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noticeable oil leakage from the engine area.
    * Solution: Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT Transmission**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifts, especially at low speeds.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting or staying in gear.
    * Solution: Replacement of the transmission filter and fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly, leading to difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Crossbars:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Crossbars on the roof rack loosen or fall off.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of the crossbar bolts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Storage Bin:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling storage bin in the center console.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of the bin hinges.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport has a few moderate to minor issues, mainly related to the engine and transmission. The severity and cost of these issues are generally not significant, making it a relatively reliable vehicle. However, it's important to be aware of these issues before purchasing or owning one.
