# 2016 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport offers budget-conscious shoppers an affordable compact SUV option with decent fuel economy and available all-wheel drive. However, used-car buyers should approach this model with caution due to frequent owner complaints involving powertrain hesitation, transmission shudder, parking brake failures, and recurring electrical issues. Thoroughly inspecting the vehicle's maintenance history and confirming all safety recalls have been addressed is strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 168 horsepower and 167 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. While the mechanical engine core is generally durable, supporting fuel and electronic management systems can occasionally exhibit performance hiccups.

**Engine Stalling and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden hesitation upon acceleration, sluggish response, or unexpected engine stalling while driving or at rest.
- **Solution:** Diagnose throttle body operation, inspect fuel delivery components, and check for relevant powertrain control module updates.

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The entry-level 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine generates 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing fuel efficiency over spirited acceleration. It is a straightforward power plant, though routine maintenance is essential to avoid long-term drivability concerns.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive systems, the continuously variable transmission is designed to optimize fuel economy. However, owner reports frequently cite hesitation, shuddering, and slipping symptoms that require careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Transmission Slipping and Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences severe transmission shudder during acceleration, delayed gear engagement, slipping, or sudden hesitation.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition and level, perform system recalibration, or seek professional transmission overhaul if internal component wear is present.

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

The available 5-speed manual transmission offers direct gear control for base models equipped with the 2.0L engine. It is mechanically simple and less prone to the complex drivability complaints associated with the automatic CVT option.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake

Braking components provide standard stopping power, but the parking brake mechanism has notable reliability complaints regarding tension loss and sticking.

**Parking Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The emergency brake lever loses tension, fails to engage properly, or sticks while the vehicle is in motion.
- **Solution:** Inspect parking brake cables, calipers, and adjustment mechanisms, and verify if manufacturer recall remedies apply to the vehicle.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical accessories function adequately for the most part, though owners frequently report recurring issues with exterior lighting components and fuses.

**Headlight Bulb Burnout and Assembly Melting**
- **Symptoms:** Frequent headlight bulb failure, premature burnout, or melting interior headlight assembly casings causing total loss of illumination.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged headlight assemblies, check wiring harnesses for heat damage, and install quality replacement bulbs.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety equipment is standard for the class, though sporadic electrical faults can trigger warning lights within the airbag restraint monitoring system.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The SRS airbag warning light illuminates on the dashboard and remains active without an apparent preceding collision.
- **Solution:** Scan the Supplemental Restraint System computer for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect seat belt tensioners or clockspring connections.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V741000: Parking Brake](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The rust prevention coating on the rear brake operating shafts could have been peeled off during the assembly process. As a result, water entering the rear brake caliper boot may cause the parking brake operating shafts to corrode and possibly bind.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear brake caliper assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V279000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A detached front control arm can result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front cross members. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V609000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Relay](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the engine stalls, there is an increased the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected relays, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V233000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The inside door latch mechanisms may not latch securely in high temperatures, allowing the doors to open while vehicle is in motion. If a door opens while the vehicle is being driven, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the door latches, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V103000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Loss of wiper function can reduce visibility and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rod of the wiper link and the wiper motor crank arm, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V867000: Structure:Body:Hatchback/Liftgate:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the outer tube corrodes, there may be a rapid loss of the gas used to provide lift assistance, causing the lift gate to fall suddenly, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will replace the lift gate supports, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V070000: Parking Brake](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the parking brake actuator shaft binds, the parking brake may not engage or disengage fully. If the parking brake does not engage properly, the vehicle may move unexpectedly if it is parked on a slope, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rear brake calipers and replace the parking brake boot and lever or the brake caliper body, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V563000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles are equipped with a constant velocity transmission (CVT) that, in certain driving conditions, may cause a delay in the vehicle accelerating. A hesitation during acceleration can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA has notified owners, and dealers will reprogram the CVT control unit with modified software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport presents a mixed reliability picture for used-car buyers. While its affordable pricing and utility are appealing, prospective owners should pay close attention during a test drive to evaluate CVT transmission smoothness and engine responsiveness. Verifying that all parking brake and electrical repairs have been properly handled by a qualified technician is essential before finalizing a purchase.
