**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport is a compact crossover SUV available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine producing 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine producing 168 horsepower and 167 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual transmission and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Outlander Sport is praised for its fuel efficiency, versatility, and spacious interior, while some owners have reported concerns with certain aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Piston Ring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, increased oil consumption, blue or white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle or ticking noise, decreased engine power
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Clutch replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Premature Belt Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering transmission, reduced acceleration
    * Solution: CVT belt and pulley replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy or clunking sounds from front suspension, poor handling
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mount bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Bushings Deterioration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear suspension squeaks or noise, reduced stability
    * Solution: Replacement of rear suspension bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunction, battery discharge
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Electrical system diagnosis and repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rail Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust on roof rails
    * Solution: Roof rail replacement or repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Fan Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: HVAC system not blowing air, excessive noise
    * Solution: HVAC fan motor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport has received mixed reviews from owners. While some have reported significant engine and transmission issues, others have experienced minimal problems. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
