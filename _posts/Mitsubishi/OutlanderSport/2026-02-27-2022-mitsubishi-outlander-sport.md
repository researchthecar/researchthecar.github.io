# 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport remains a budget-friendly option in the subcompact crossover segment, offering a familiar, utilitarian design. While overall volume from owner feedback is relatively low, early reports highlight a few recurring electronic and visibility concerns that prospective buyers should keep in mind during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The base 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 148 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily commuting, though it can feel strained during hard acceleration.

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

The larger 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine puts out 168 horsepower and 167 lb-ft of torque, offering slightly better passing response. It pairs with the vehicle's standard all-wheel-drive system on higher trims and maintains a straightforward mechanical layout.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission routes power to either the front wheels or an all-wheel-drive system depending on the trim level. It is tuned primarily for fuel efficiency in daily driving conditions.

**Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a noticeable pause or lack of responsiveness when attempting to accelerate into fast-moving traffic.
- **Solution:** Consult a qualified technician to check for powertrain control module updates or transmission fluid condition.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

The interior electronics manage the multimedia display, phone integration, and vehicle settings.

**Infotainment and Connectivity Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The navigational screen and touchscreen interface may freeze, fail to connect to smartphones, or stop working entirely while driving.
- **Solution:** Restart the vehicle or visit a dealership to check for software patches and system updates.

### Backup Camera

Exterior visibility systems assist the driver with parking and maneuvering in tight spaces.

**Dark or Blurry Backup Camera Display**
- **Symptoms:** The rear-view camera image appears extremely dark, blurry, or washed out during both daytime and nighttime conditions, making objects difficult to see.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera lens for physical obstructions or damage, and verify that display settings are functioning correctly; replacement of the camera unit may be necessary if internal failure occurs.

## Exterior

### Windshield

The front glass structure protects occupants and houses advanced driver-assistance hardware.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The front windshield may develop stress cracks originating from the edges without any visible impact from rocks or road debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield through a professional glass service, ensuring proper installation and calibration of any attached safety sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V563000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+Sport&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the CVT-ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport offers an accessible entry point into the crossover market, but buyers should thoroughly inspect functional areas like the infotainment software and rear-view camera visibility during a test drive. Paying close attention to these common owner-reported gripes can help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
