**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport is a compact SUV with a standard 2.4-liter, 166-horsepower engine and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It also offers a 2.5-liter, 181-horsepower engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Despite its affordability and compact size, the Outlander Sport has received some criticism for its lackluster interior and slightly cramped third row. However, its reliability and spacious cargo area have been praised.

### **Engine** ###

**2.4-liter Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement or overhauling the engine.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission** ###

**CVT Transmission**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks while shifting gears, causing a lack of smoothness.
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension** ###

* **No Issues Found**

### **Electrical** ###

* **Front Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both front power windows fail to operate or experience intermittent performance.
    * Solution: Replacing the master window switch or the window motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior** ###

* **Paint Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Paint peeling occurs prematurely, especially on the hood and front bumper.
    * Solution: Repainting the affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks fail to engage or unlock using the key or remote.
    * Solution: Replacing the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Interior** ###

* **Dashboard Creaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard rattles or creaks when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Applying sound dampening material to the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating, ventilation, or air conditioning system doesn't work properly.
    * Solution: Replacing faulty sensors, actuators, or the climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport is a reliable and affordable SUV, but it is not without its issues. Engine oil consumption, transmission hesitation, and front power window failure have been reported by owners. Other problems include paint peeling, door lock failure, dashboard creaks, and HVAC malfunctions. While some of these issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others may require more significant repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing an Outlander Sport.
