**Introduction:**

The 2020 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport is a compact SUV available with two engine options: a 2.0L 4-cylinder and a 2.4L 4-cylinder. Both engines are mated to a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Outlander Sport is praised for its affordability, spacious interior, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, it has received some criticism for its lackluster performance and dated exterior design.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-Cylinder**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of defective piston rings or valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the timing chain, leading to engine stalling or catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4L 4-Cylinder**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Hesitation/Jerking While Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or jerking during gear changes, particularly at low speeds.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of faulty transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Premature Wear and Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Accelerated wear and premature failure of internal transmission components, leading to loss of power or complete transmission failure.
    * Solution: Overhaul or replacement of the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Brake Light Switch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning brake light switch, causing brake lights to illuminate intermittently or not at all.
    * Solution: Replacement of the brake light switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Malfunctioning Infotainment System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete failure of the infotainment system, including the display, audio system, and navigation features.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Lens Fogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the fog light lenses, leading to reduced visibility and potential electrical problems.
    * Solution: Replacement of the fog light lenses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty HVAC System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning HVAC controls, leading to incorrect air temperature or airflow direction.
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty HVAC components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Conclusion:**

Overall, the 2020 Mitsubishi Outlander Sport has some potential reliability issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. These issues can be costly to repair and may impact the overall ownership experience. However, the Outlander Sport remains a good value for the price when compared to its competitors.
