# 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage offers budget-minded shoppers an ultra-affordable and exceptionally fuel-efficient subcompact option, available in both hatchback and sedan body styles. While basic transportation is its primary strong suit, buyers should keep expectations modest regarding interior refinement and overall performance. When shopping for a used Mirage, reviewing vehicle history reports and verifying that all standard equipment functions correctly remains important.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

A 1.2-liter naturally aspirated three-cylinder engine producing 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate fuel economy for city commuting, though overall acceleration is relaxed.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional five-speed manual transmission paired with the base model, offering straightforward shifting mechanics and reliable daily operation.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A continuously variable automatic transmission designed to maximize fuel efficiency across driving conditions. Buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth operation.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Service Brakes

Hydraulic service brakes responsible for stopping power.

**Brake Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected brake warning light illumination while driving at cruising speeds, requiring the vehicle to be pulled over and towed.
- **Solution:** Inspect the braking system hydraulics, fluid levels, and sensor connections immediately if a warning light appears.

## Electrical

### Radio System

In-vehicle entertainment and electrical accessory systems.

**Radio Powers On Automatically**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that the radio system turns on immediately upon starting the vehicle with the key, even if it was manually turned off prior to shutting the car down.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership or qualified technician inspect the infotainment software and audio system wiring for potential glitches.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mitsubishi Mirage is a straightforward, highly efficient commuter car with limited reported powertrain complaints in early owner data. Used car buyers should perform a thorough test drive, check for proper brake and electrical functionality, and ensure routine maintenance has been performed.
