**Introduction**

The 2023 Mitsubishi Mirage is a subcompact hatchback that offers impressive fuel economy and a low starting price. It is powered by a 1.2-liter three-cylinder engine paired to a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Mirage has received praise for its practicality, fuel efficiency, and affordability, but some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

* **1.2-liter Three-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of piston rings, oil pan, or other affected components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Noise:** Noisy timing chain, especially at cold start-up.
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain, tensioner, or related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**
    * **Jerking and Hesitation:** Transmission hesitates or jerks during acceleration or gear changes.
        * Solution: Fluid replacement, software update, or replacement of transmission components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Issues:** Flickering or intermittent headlight operation.
        * Solution: Replacement of headlight bulbs, wiring harness, or electrical connectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:** Rapid battery discharge, leading to starting problems.
        * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of parasitic drain, typically involving electrical components or sensors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Noises or vibrations coming from the dashboard.
        * Solution: Tightening of loose components or installation of sound-dampening materials.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Fabric Sagging:** Premature sagging or wear of seat fabric.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of seat upholstery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2023 Mitsubishi Mirage offers several advantages, it has also experienced some reported issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior components. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some requiring major repairs. Potential owners should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
