# 2017 Mitsubishi Mirage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Mitsubishi Mirage, buyers should keep in mind that owner feedback highlights several electrical, braking, and safety concerns. While it offers high fuel efficiency and an affordable entry point, prospective owners should thoroughly inspect vehicle systems and review maintenance records before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

The 2017 Mitsubishi Mirage comes equipped with a 1.2-liter 3-cylinder engine producing approximately 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque. It is designed primarily for maximum fuel efficiency in urban commuting rather than spirited acceleration.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 5-speed manual transmission is available on base trims, offering straightforward mechanical operation and basic commuter reliability.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission (CVT) is paired with the 3-cylinder engine on higher trims to optimize fuel economy, though buyers should test drive to ensure smooth operation.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback indicates occasional pedal travel anomalies that warrant a thorough mechanical inspection.

**Brake Pedal Softness**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced instances where the brake pedal travels unexpectedly toward the floor before recovering after a quick pump.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake master cylinder, check for air in the brake lines, and ensure brake fluid is flushed regularly.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical complaints center around intermittent power disruptions and accessory motor failures.

**Power Loss and Dashboard Reset**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that electronics, dashboard lights, and the radio suddenly shut off while driving, occasionally resulting in temporary power loss.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the battery terminals, main ground connections, and alternator output for loose wiring or component failure.

**Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power window motors can fail prematurely, occasionally leaving windows stuck in the open position.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty window motor and regulator assembly.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety system reports highlight driver concerns regarding warning lights and airbag performance during impacts.

**Air Bag Warning Light / Non-Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated air bag warning lights or, in severe collisions, air bags failing to deploy as expected.
- **Solution:** Have diagnostic trouble codes scanned by a professional to inspect seatbelt pre-tensioners, crash sensors, and the airbag control module.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V675000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Mirage&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Both conditions can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will secure the horizontal locking clips as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V686000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Mirage&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The internal acceleration sensor in the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) Electronic Control Unit (ECU) can misinterpret vibrations such as those from poor road conditions or a flat tire to be a sensor error thereby disabling the air bags from deploying in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as designed in a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the SRS-ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mitsubishi Mirage is a budget-friendly city car, but potential buyers should pay close attention to its electrical systems, brake responsiveness, and safety equipment. Conducting a pre-purchase inspection can help uncover hidden issues before finalizing a deal.
