**Introduction:**

The 2014 Mitsubishi Mirage is a compact, fuel-efficient hatchback powered by a 1.2-liter three-cylinder engine mated to either a 5-speed manual or Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). It offers economical transportation but has been criticized for its poor performance and unrefined interior.

**Engine:**

* **1.2-liter Three-Cylinder**

  * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning large amounts of oil, resulting in low oil levels, blue smoke from the exhaust, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buildup of carbon deposits on the intake valves, leading to rough idling, reduced power, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing the intake valves and cleaning the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **5-Speed Manual**

  * **Difficult Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or grinding when shifting gears, particularly when shifting into reverse.
    * Solution: Replacing the shift linkage or transmission mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

* **CVT**

  * **Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle hesitates or jerks when accelerating or changing gears.
    * Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the transmission valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly or fails to hold a charge.
    * Solution: Replacing the battery, alternator, or electrical components causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power door locks fail to operate or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Replacing the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Cracking Dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appear on the dashboard, particularly in hot climates.
    * Solution: Replacing the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty HVAC System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating, ventilation, or air conditioning system malfunctions, causing insufficient airflow or temperature control.
    * Solution: Replacing or repairing the HVAC blower motor, fan switch, or other components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2014 Mitsubishi Mirage has experienced several issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. While some repairs, such as difficult shifting, are relatively inexpensive, others, like excessive oil consumption, can be costly and require major engine work. The interior and electrical systems have also had their share of problems, leading to potential discomfort and inconvenience. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues vary, so it's important to consider when purchasing or maintaining this vehicle.
