# 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage

### Overview
The 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage is a subcompact car known for its affordability and fuel efficiency. As a budget-friendly option, its engineering prioritizes cost-effectiveness, which can sometimes translate to a less robust build compared to more premium vehicles. Reliability data for the Mirage generally indicates a mixed bag, with some areas performing well and others showing potential for concern, particularly as the vehicle ages or accumulates mileage. Owners often report satisfaction with its basic functionality and low running costs, but it's important to be aware of common problem areas to ensure long-term ownership satisfaction and to budget for potential repairs.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display may freeze, become unresponsive to touch inputs, or reboot unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update or a reset of the infotainment unit. Replacement of the unit is a last resort.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


### Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the engine, dimming headlights, or warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Battery testing and replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Exterior

### Paint and Body Panels
**Paint Peeling or Fading**
- **Symptoms:** Paint may show signs of peeling or fading, particularly on horizontal surfaces exposed to harsh sunlight.
- **Solution:** Professional repainting or touch-up work.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000+


### Headlights
**Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture or fogging inside the headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Sealing the headlight assembly or replacement of the unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Interior

### Seat Upholstery
**Premature Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Fabric or synthetic upholstery may show signs of premature wear, such as pilling or thinning.
- **Solution:** Seat covers or professional upholstery repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Lack of airflow from the vents, or intermittent operation of the fan.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mitsubishi Mirage offers a basic transportation solution with a focus on economy. While its powertrain is generally considered reliable for its class, potential owners should be aware of reported issues with the CVT transmission, particularly concerning hesitation and jerky operation. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and occasional battery issues, are also noted. Exterior concerns like paint durability and headlight condensation can arise. Interior wear on upholstery is not uncommon. Despite these points, the Mirage remains a viable option for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing low purchase and running costs over premium features and long-term durability in all aspects.
