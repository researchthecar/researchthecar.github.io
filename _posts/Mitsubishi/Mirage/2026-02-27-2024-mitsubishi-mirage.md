# 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage, buyers are looking at an economical subcompact known primarily for its high fuel efficiency and budget-friendly price point. While overall owner feedback is relatively light, prospective buyers should review available powertrain configurations and note sporadic owner reports concerning drivability and driver-assistance features.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

The 1.2-liter naturally aspirated 3-cylinder engine produces 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing fuel economy over spirited performance. It serves as the sole engine option for the Mirage lineup and generally offers straightforward, conventional mechanical reliability.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

The standard 5-speed manual transmission is available on base trims, offering a traditional shifting experience and simple mechanical design. It is generally robust, though less common on dealership lots.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission is paired with most trim levels to maximize fuel efficiency during daily commuting. Owners occasionally report unusual engagement sensations from a standstill.

**Powertrain Surging or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Occasionally when accelerating from a complete stop, the vehicle may exhibit unexpected surging or jumping behavior.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission control module and powertrain operation to diagnose potential drivability anomalies.

## Steering

### Front Suspension and Steering Geometry

The front suspension handles standard subcompact duties but can be susceptible to alignment-related wear if not properly monitored.

**Rapid Front Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Front tires may experience premature and excessive tread wear, drastically shortening tire lifespan and reducing available traction.
- **Solution:** Check front-end alignment, suspension components, and tire wear patterns regularly to correct any alignment or geometry issues.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Mitigation

Advanced safety features provide added driver confidence, though electronic sensor suites can occasionally trigger warning lights requiring professional diagnosis.

**Forward Collision Warning Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights for the forward collision mitigation system accompanied by concerns regarding the automatic braking functionality.
- **Solution:** Consult a certified dealership or service center to run diagnostic scans on the safety sensor modules and braking systems.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mitsubishi Mirage remains a straightforward, ultra-efficient option for budget-conscious drivers, but used car buyers should perform thorough pre-purchase inspections. Pay close attention to tire wear patterns, test drive the transmission thoroughly from a dead stop to check for smooth acceleration, and verify that all safety electronics function properly.
