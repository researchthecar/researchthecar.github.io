# 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage, buyers are typically looking for maximum fuel efficiency and budget-friendly daily transportation. While owner reports to NHTSA highlight a few mechanical and electrical concerns, keeping an eye on powertrain performance and routine inspections can help secure a reliable commuter.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-cylinder Engine

The 1.2-liter 3-cylinder engine produces 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing fuel economy over spirited performance. It generally offers simple mechanical reliability, though owners have reported occasional electrical and stalling concerns.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle shuts off unexpectedly while driving at random times, initially restarting before failing to start altogether.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel delivery, electrical wiring, and ignition system to diagnose the root cause of the power loss.

**Fuel Pump Wiring Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Wires near the rear fuel pump fray or degrade, potentially leading to fuel system failure or a fire hazard.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel pump wiring harness for fraying or damage, and repair or replace compromised circuits.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission routes power smoothly to the front wheels while maximizing efficiency. Routine fluid maintenance is key to ensuring long-term durability in commuter traffic.

### 5-speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 5-speed manual transmission gives drivers direct control over the 3-cylinder engine and features a simple mechanical design with few reported widespread issues.

## Suspension

### Front Strut Mounts

Front suspension components generally handle daily driving well, though early wear on mount assemblies has been noted in isolated owner reports.

**Early Strut Mount Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual noises coming from the front end and altered vehicle responsiveness or sluggish acceleration feel.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front suspension struts and mounts, and replace worn components to restore ride quality and handling.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

The safety restraint system is designed to protect occupants, but owners have submitted complaints regarding unexpected non-deployment during impacts.

**Non-Deployment in Collisions**
- **Symptoms:** Air bags fail to deploy during an impact despite significant collision forces and absence of warning lamps.
- **Solution:** Have the supplemental restraint system (SRS) and impact sensors professionally diagnosed and repaired following any collision or diagnostic trouble code.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mitsubishi Mirage is a straightforward, ultra-efficient subcompact that serves well as an economical commuter car. Used car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electrical system, fuel pump wiring, and front suspension to ensure a dependable ownership experience.
