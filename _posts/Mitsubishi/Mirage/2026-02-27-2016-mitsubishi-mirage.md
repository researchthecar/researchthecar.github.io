# 2016 Mitsubishi Mirage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mitsubishi Mirage is an ultra-compact, efficiency-focused hatchback powered by a small three-cylinder engine. While it serves as an economical commuter choice, used car buyers should carefully review service histories and check for outstanding manufacturer campaigns before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L 3-Cylinder Engine

The 1.2-liter three-cylinder engine produces 78 horsepower and 74 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing fuel economy over spirited performance. It is generally simple and straightforward, though owners should monitor routine maintenance indicators.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

The standard five-speed manual transmission offers a direct connection and simple mechanical design. It is typically durable when routine clutch and fluid maintenance is observed.

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The available continuously variable transmission maximizes fuel efficiency for urban driving. Buyers should test drive CVT models thoroughly to ensure smooth acceleration and prompt engagement.

## Interior

### Dashboard Indicators

Electrical complaints for this model year are minimal, though minor dashboard indicator glitches have been reported by individual owners.

**Maintenance Light Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The maintenance reminder light on the dashboard may illuminate unexpectedly or fail to reset properly after an oil change.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or service center reset the maintenance indicator system using a diagnostic tool.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V686000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Mirage&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The internal acceleration sensor in the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) Electronic Control Unit (ECU) can misinterpret vibrations such as those from poor road conditions or a flat tire to be a sensor error thereby disabling the air bags from deploying in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as designed in a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MMNA will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the SRS-ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

Overall, the 2016 Mitsubishi Mirage is a basic, highly efficient vehicle with very few reported owner complaints on record. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify that all safety recalls have been completed by a licensed dealer.
