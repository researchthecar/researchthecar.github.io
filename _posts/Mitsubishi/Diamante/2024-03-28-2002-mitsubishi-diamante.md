**Introduction:** The 2002 Mitsubishi Diamante is a mid-size sedan that offered a blend of luxury and performance. It was available with two engine options: a 3.0-liter V6 and a 3.5-liter V6, both paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. While praised for its comfortable interior and smooth ride, the Diamante had its share of issues.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the edges of the valve covers.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the intake manifold.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**3.5-liter V6**

* **Valve Timing Control Solenoid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, reduced power, and check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace the valve timing control solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500

* **Timing Belt Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screeching or whining noise, reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Replace the timing belt and tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gears slipping or not engaging properly.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking sound when turning or going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the front strut bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dimming electrical components.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

* **Starter Solenoid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, clicking sound.
    * Solution: Replace the starter solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not turning on or intermittent operation.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300

* **Door Lock Actuator Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400

**Interior**

* **Power Window Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working or intermittent.
    * Solution: Replace the power window switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250

* **Climate Control Fan Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heater not working or intermittent.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control fan motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary:** The 2002 Mitsubishi Diamante has some potential issues that can range in severity and cost. Engine and transmission issues can be costly to repair, while suspension and electrical issues tend to be less expensive. Regular maintenance and preventative repairs can help mitigate some of these problems and extend the lifespan of the vehicle.
