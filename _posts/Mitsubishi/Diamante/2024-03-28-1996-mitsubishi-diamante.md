**Introduction:**

The 1996 Mitsubishi Diamante is a mid-size luxury sedan powered by a 3.0-liter V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. It offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and decent performance for its class. However, like all vehicles, it has some known issues to be aware of.

**Engine:**

**3.0-Liter V6**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, overheating, and white smoke from the exhaust
   * Solution: Replace head gaskets
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leaks, rough idle, and decreased fuel economy
   * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed engagement, or slipping out of gear
   * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Noises while turning or braking, vibrations in the steering wheel
   * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Bumpy ride, loss of handling
   * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
   * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions
   * Solution: Replace alternator
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle after prolonged parking
   * Solution: Replace battery or identify and repair the source of the drain
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Door Panel Rust:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on the inside of the door panels
   * Solution: Replace door panels
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control, airflow issues
   * Solution: Replace climate control module or actuators
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Windows not operating properly
   * Solution: Replace window motor or switch
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1996 Mitsubishi Diamante offers a comfortable and reliable driving experience. However, it does have some potential problems to watch out for, particularly related to the engine, transmission, and electrical components. The severity and cost of these repairs can vary depending on the specific issue, but it's important to be aware of them to avoid unexpected expenses down the road.
