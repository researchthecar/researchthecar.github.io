**Introduction**

The 1993 Mitsubishi Diamante is a mid-size sedan that was available with two engine options: a 3.0-liter V6 and a 3.5-liter V6. Both engines were paired with a four-speed automatic transmission. The Diamante was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engines. However, it was also criticized for its lack of handling and its dated exterior styling.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the intake manifold area, rough idle, loss of power.
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
Recalls: None Found

**3.5-liter V6**

**Timing Belt Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, ticking or grinding noise from engine, loss of power.
Solution: Replace timing belt and related components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping between gears, delayed shifting, jerking.
Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

** Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery light on, flickering lights, difficulty starting.
Solution: Replace alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rust Prone**
Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on the body panels, especially around the wheel wells and rocker panels.
Solution: Repair and prevent further rusting.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of the rust.

**Interior**

**Climate Control Panel Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control buttons and knobs not functioning, display not working.
Solution: Replace climate control panel.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$ 200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1993 Mitsubishi Diamante has a few potential issues that buyers should be aware of. The most serious issues are with the engine, transmission, and alternator. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic before purchasing. However, the Diamante is a well-built car with a comfortable interior and powerful engines. With proper maintenance, it can provide years of reliable service.
