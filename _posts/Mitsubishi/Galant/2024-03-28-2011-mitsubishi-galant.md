**Introduction:**

The 2011 Mitsubishi Galant is a mid-size sedan that offers a combination of performance, comfort, and affordability. It comes equipped with a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine (160 hp, 162 lb-ft torque) paired to a 4-speed automatic transmission.

People appreciate the Galant's spacious interior, comfortable ride, and responsive handling. However, some have expressed concerns about its fuel efficiency and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**2.4L 4-Cylinder**

* **Intake Valve Sticking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, engine stalling
  * Solution: Replace intake valves
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
  * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation in acceleration, difficulty shifting gears
  * Solution: Replace transmission valve body
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, vibration, premature tire wear
  * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Power Window Switch**
  * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or non-working power windows
  * Solution: Replace power window switch
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Bumper Fade**
  * Description/Symptoms: Discoloration or fading of plastic bumper
  * Solution: Replace or repaint bumper
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Trim Rattle**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling trim on interior door panels
  * Solution: Reattach or replace trim clips
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2011 Mitsubishi Galant is a generally reliable vehicle. However, it has some common issues, including intake valve sticking, transmission slipping, and suspension wear. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix can range from minor to major repairs. It's important for owners to stay informed about potential problems and address them promptly to maintain the vehicle's safety and performance.
