## 2007 Mitsubishi Galant

The 2007 Mitsubishi Galant is a midsize sedan offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine produces 160 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.8-liter V6 engine produces 230 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are available with a four-speed automatic transmission or a five-speed manual transmission.

The Galant is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and affordable price. However, it is criticized for its poor fuel economy, lackluster performance, and outdated design.

### **Engine**

**2.4L Four-Cylinder**
- **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  - Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Timing Chain Failure:** The timing chain can stretch or break, causing engine damage.
  - Solution: Timing chain replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**3.8L V6**
- **Cam Phaser Failure:** The cam phaser, which adjusts the timing of the camshaft, can fail, affecting engine performance.
  - Solution: Cam phaser replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** The valve cover gasket can leak oil, causing a burning smell in the cabin and potential engine damage.
  - Solution: Valve cover gasket replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**Four-Speed Automatic**
- **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may experience difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or jerking.
  - Solution: Transmission fluid flush or rebuild.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Five-Speed Manual**
- **Clutch Failure:** The clutch can wear out prematurely, leading to difficulty shifting gears or a loss of power.
  - Solution: Clutch replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**

- **Strut Failure:** The struts can leak or wear out, affecting ride quality and handling.
  - Solution: Strut replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+ per axle
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
  - Solution: Electrical system inspection and repair.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

- **Sunroof Drainage Issues:** The sunroof drainage system can clog, leading to water leaks into the cabin.
  - Solution: Drainage system cleaning or repair.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**

- **Door Panel Noise:** The door panels can vibrate or rattle at certain speeds or road conditions.
  - Solution: Door panel adjustment or insulation.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2007 Mitsubishi Galant has a range of issues affecting the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The most severe issues are related to engine oil consumption and cam phaser failure. While the Galant offers a comfortable ride and spacious interior at an affordable price, its reliability issues may make it a less desirable option for some buyers.
