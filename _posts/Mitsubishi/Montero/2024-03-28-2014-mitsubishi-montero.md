**Introduction to the 2014 Mitsubishi Montero**

The 2014 Mitsubishi Montero is a mid-size SUV that offers rugged off-road capability and a spacious cabin. It is powered by a 3.8L V6 engine that produces 210 horsepower and 235 lb-ft of torque. The Montero is available with either a 5-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission.

Owners praise the Montero's off-road performance, reliability, and spacious interior. However, it is criticized for its dated design, lack of advanced safety features, and thirsty fuel economy.
