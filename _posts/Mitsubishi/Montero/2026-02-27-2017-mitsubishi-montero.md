# 2017 Mitsubishi Montero

### Overview
The 2017 Mitsubishi Montero, also known as the Pajero in many markets, is a body-on-frame SUV renowned for its off-road prowess and rugged construction. While generally considered a durable vehicle, like any automotive model, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining common problems reported by owners and mechanics. We will delve into the performance and potential weaknesses of its powertrain, suspension, electrical systems, and other key areas to provide a comprehensive reliability overview for the 2017 model year.

## Suspension

### Independent Front Suspension / Multi-link Rear
**Front Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises from the front end, especially over bumps; uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or failure to connect Bluetooth devices.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset; in some cases, unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800


## Exterior

### Body and Trim

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Mitsubishi Montero demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain, with its V6 engines and available transmissions generally performing without widespread issues. The vehicle's robust construction contributes to its overall durability. However, owners have occasionally reported minor concerns such as front ball joint wear, which can manifest as noise and affect tire wear, and intermittent glitches with the infotainment system. The rear hatch struts are another component that may require replacement over time. Despite these isolated issues, the Montero remains a capable and dependable SUV, especially for those prioritizing off-road capability and longevity, provided routine maintenance is performed and these specific potential issues are addressed promptly.
