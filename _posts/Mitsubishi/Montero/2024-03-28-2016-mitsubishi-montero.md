**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Montero is a mid-size SUV that offers a combination of off-road capability and everyday practicality. It is powered by a 3.8-liter V6 engine that produces 281 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The Montero is known for its spacious interior, rugged exterior, and advanced all-wheel-drive system. However, it has also received some criticism for its outdated design and lack of certain modern features.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**

* **Intake Valve Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replacement of intake valves and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Timing Belt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sudden engine failure or loss of power, accompanied by loud noises from the engine.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing belt, idlers, and tensioners.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to shift gears smoothly, delayed or harsh shifting, and loss of power while driving.
    * Solution: Replacement or rebuild of the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride quality, excessive bouncing, and uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights, and failure of electronic components.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, including battery replacement if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system warning lights, flickering headlights, and battery not charging properly.
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on the metal tailgate panel.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of tailgate panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy dashboard with rattling or squeaking noises, particularly during rough roads.
    * Solution: Identification and fixing of loose components or padding.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Montero has some common issues that owners have reported. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances (such as dashboard squeaks) to more serious problems (such as engine or transmission failures). The estimated cost to fix these issues can vary depending on the issue and the specific vehicle. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to have their vehicles regularly serviced and inspected to prevent or address them in a timely manner.
