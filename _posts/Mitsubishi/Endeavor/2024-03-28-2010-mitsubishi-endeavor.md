**Introduction:**

The 2010 Mitsubishi Endeavor is a mid-size SUV known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive towing capacity. It was offered with two engine options: a 3.8-liter V6 and a 4.7-liter V8. Both engines could be paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Despite its strengths, the Endeavor faced some common issues that owners reported.

**Engine**

**3.8-Liter V6**

* **Intake Camshaft Actuator Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description: The intake camshaft actuator solenoid fails, causing reduced engine power and rough idling.
    * Solution: Replace the intake camshaft actuator solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description: The timing chain tensioner fails, leading to excessive chain slack and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.7-Liter V8**

* **Cylinder Head Cracking:**
    * Description: The cylinder heads can crack, causing coolant leaks and engine overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the cracked cylinder heads.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description: The torque converter shudders when shifting or accelerating, resulting in a rough ride.
    * Solution: Flush and replace the transmission fluid and replace the torque converter if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Delay:**
    * Description: The transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update the transmission control module software or rebuild the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Failure:**
    * Description: The front control arm bushings deteriorate and cause clunking noises when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description: The rear shock absorbers leak or become damaged, reducing ride quality and stability.
    * Solution: Replace the shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description: The alternator fails, causing battery drain and electrical system issues.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Corrosion:**
    * Description: The battery terminals corrode, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Clean or replace the battery terminals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description: The sunroof seals deteriorate and allow water to leak into the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Handle Failure:**
    * Description: The tailgate handle breaks or becomes loose, making it difficult to open or close the tailgate.
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Crack:**
    * Description: The dashboard cracks or warps, especially in direct sunlight.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description: The HVAC blower motor fails, reducing or eliminating airflow through the climate control system.
    * Solution: Replace the blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2010 Mitsubishi Endeavor had various issues ranging from minor inconveniences to more serious and costly repairs, such as cylinder head cracking and timing chain tensioner failures. However, it also had redeeming qualities, such as its spacious interior and comfortable ride. The cost of repairs varied significantly depending on the severity of the issue.
