**Introduction:**
The 2018 Mitsubishi Montero Sport is a mid-size SUV powered by a 3.0-liter V6 engine producing 215 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. It comes paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Despite its rugged exterior, the Montero Sport has received mixed reviews due to its dated interior and lack of modern safety features.

- 3.0-liter V6

**Suspension Noise**
Description/Symptoms: A loud clunking or rattling noise coming from the suspension system, particularly when going over bumps or rough roads.
Solution: The issue can be due to worn or damaged suspension components, such as the shock absorbers, struts, or control arms. Replacing these components can resolve the noise.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains quickly, even after being parked for extended periods.
Solution: The problem could be caused by a faulty alternator, parasitic battery drain, or a malfunctioning electrical component. Diagnosing and replacing the defective part can solve the issue.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rear Hatch Seal**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cargo area or passenger cabin through a faulty rear hatch seal.
Solution: Replacing the worn or damaged hatch seal can prevent water intrusion.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system does not function properly, resulting in inconsistent temperature and airflow.
Solution: The issue can be traced back to a faulty blower motor, AC compressor, or control module. Replacing the defective component is necessary to restore proper climate control functionality.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 2018 Mitsubishi Montero Sport has its drawbacks, the reported issues are primarily related to suspension noise and battery drain. The cost to fix these problems can range from $200 to over $1,000, depending on the severity of the issue and the specific parts that need to be replaced.
