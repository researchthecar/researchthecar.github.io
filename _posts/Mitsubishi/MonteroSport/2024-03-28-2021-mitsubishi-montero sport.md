**Introduction:**

The 2021 Mitsubishi Montero Sport is a mid-size SUV that offers a combination of off-road capability and everyday practicality. It is powered by a 3.0-liter V6 gasoline engine that produces 220 horsepower and 209 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Montero Sport is available in both four-wheel drive and two-wheel drive configurations. It is praised for its rugged exterior styling, spacious interior, and reliable performance, but criticized for its outdated interior and lack of certain modern features.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
Symptoms: Excess oil consumption, white smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic:**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
Symptoms: Low transmission fluid levels, slipping gears, difficulty shifting
Solution: Replace seals or gaskets causing the leaks
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
Symptoms: Rough ride, knocking noises from the rear
Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
Solution: Check for parasitic draw and replace faulty components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Condensation:**
Symptoms: Fog inside fog light lenses
Solution: Replace or repair fog light lenses
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Air Conditioning Compressor Failure:**
Symptoms: Weak or no air conditioning
Solution: Replace air conditioning compressor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
Symptoms: Navigation system not working, freezing, or giving incorrect directions
Solution: Update software or replace navigation system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Mitsubishi Montero Sport has some notable issues to be aware of, particularly in its engine, transmission, electrical, and interior components. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to more expensive repairs. While the Montero Sport is a capable and reliable vehicle, potential buyers should consider the potential costs associated with these issues before making a purchase decision.
