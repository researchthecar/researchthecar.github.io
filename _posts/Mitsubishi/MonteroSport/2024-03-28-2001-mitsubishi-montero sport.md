**Introduction**

The 2001 Mitsubishi Montero Sport is a mid-size SUV known for its off-road capabilities. It offered two engine options: a 3.0L V6 and a 3.5L V6. The 3.0L V6 produces 173 horsepower and 198 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.5L V6 produces 200 horsepower and 228 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers appreciate the Montero Sport's rugged exterior, comfortable interior, and reliable performance, but some have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**
- **3.0L V6**
    - **Oil Leaks:** Leaks develop from the valve cover gaskets, causing oil to drip onto the exhaust manifold and create a burning smell.
        - Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    - **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** The intake manifold gasket deteriorates, allowing coolant to leak into the engine oil. This can lead to overheating and engine damage.
        - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - **EGR Valve Issues:** The EGR valve can become clogged or faulty, causing engine performance problems and increased emissions.
        - Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
- **3.5L V6**
    - **Timing Belt Failure:** The timing belt can break prematurely, causing catastrophic engine damage.
        - Solution: Replace timing belt and components.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - **Intake Valve Sticking:** Intake valves can become stuck due to carbon buildup, resulting in decreased engine power and fuel economy.
        - Solution: Clean or replace intake valves.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**
- **5-speed Manual**
    - **Clutch Assembly Wear:** The clutch assembly can wear out prematurely, causing difficulties when shifting or slipping.
        - Solution: Replace clutch assembly.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
- **4-speed Automatic**
    - **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Seals within the transmission can fail, leading to fluid leaks and transmission damage.
        - Solution: Replace leaking seals.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    - **Torque Converter Problems:** The torque converter can fail, causing shuddering or slipping when shifting.
        - Solution: Replace torque converter.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Suspension**
- **Coil Spring Sag:** The coil springs can sag over time, reducing ride height and affecting handling.
    - Solution: Replace coil springs.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
- **Ball Joint Wear:** Ball joints can wear out, causing clunking noises and decreased stability.
    - Solution: Replace ball joints.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+

**Electrical**
- **Alternator Failure:** The alternator can fail, leading to electrical system malfunctions and battery discharge.
    - Solution: Replace alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
- **Starter Motor Issues:** The starter motor can fail, preventing the engine from starting.
    - Solution: Replace starter motor.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+

**Exterior**
- **Rust Prone:** The vehicle is prone to rust, particularly on the frame and underbody.
    - Solution: Apply rust protection measures.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of damage.

**Interior**
- **Power Window Regulator Failure:** The power window regulators can fail, causing windows to become stuck or inoperable.
    - Solution: Replace power window regulators.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary**

The 2001 Mitsubishi Montero Sport is a reliable SUV with off-road capabilities, but it is not without its issues. Some of the most common problems reported include engine oil leaks, transmission fluid leaks, and suspension component wear. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can be significant. However, overall, the Montero Sport is a solid choice for those seeking a capable and durable SUV.
