## 2020 Mitsubishi Montero Sport

The 2020 Mitsubishi Montero Sport is a midsize SUV that offers a comfortable and spacious cabin, a powerful engine, and capable off-road performance. It comes standard with a 3.0-liter V6 engine that produces 181 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 2.4-liter turbocharged inline-4 with 179 horsepower and 315 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Montero Sport is known for its reliability, ruggedness, and value for money. However, like any vehicle, it has its fair share of issues.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter V6**

**Valve Body Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: The valve body, which controls the transmission of fluid to the clutch packs, can fail, causing the transmission to slip or shift erratically.
* Solution: Replace the valve body.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Torque Converter Shudder**
* Description/Symptoms: The torque converter, which connects the engine to the transmission, can develop a shudder or vibration during acceleration.
* Solution: Replace the torque converter.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
* Recalls: None found

**Suspension**

**Rear Suspension Sag**
* Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension can sag prematurely, causing the vehicle to sit lower in the rear.
* Solution: Replace the rear coil springs or air springs.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
* Solution: Test and replace the battery or alternator.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $600+
* Recalls: None found

**Exterior**

**Tailgate Latch Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: The tailgate latch can fail, causing the tailgate to open unexpectedly or not open at all.
* Solution: Replace the tailgate latch.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: The climate control system can malfunction, causing the cabin to be too hot or too cold.
* Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* Recalls: None found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2020 Mitsubishi Montero Sport is a reliable and capable SUV. However, it has some potential issues, particularly with the transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost of repairs can be significant. It's important to note that these issues are not universal and may not affect all vehicles.
