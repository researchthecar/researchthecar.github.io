# 2019 Mitsubishi Montero Sport

### Overview
The 2019 Mitsubishi Montero Sport, also known as the Pajero Sport or Nativa in some markets, is a body-on-frame SUV designed for off-road capability and ruggedness. It typically comes equipped with a robust diesel engine and a capable four-wheel-drive system. Reliability for this model generally leans towards the positive, with owners appreciating its durability in demanding conditions. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for different configurations.

---


## Engine

### 2.4L 4N15 MIVEC Turbo Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Regeneration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, potential engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Forced DPF regeneration via diagnostic tool, or professional cleaning/replacement of the DPF.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, black smoke from exhaust, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of the turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission (W8A42)
**Hesitation or Jerking During Shifts**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable delay or rough shifts between gears, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software update for the transmission control module (TCM), fluid flush and replacement, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mitsubishi Montero Sport demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain and chassis, aligning with its reputation as a durable off-road capable SUV. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the diesel engine's emissions system, specifically the Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF), which can require regeneration or cleaning, and potential turbocharger issues under heavy use. The automatic transmission may occasionally exhibit minor shifting anomalies. Electrical issues are generally minor, often related to the infotainment system. Overall, the Montero Sport is a dependable choice for those seeking a rugged vehicle, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
