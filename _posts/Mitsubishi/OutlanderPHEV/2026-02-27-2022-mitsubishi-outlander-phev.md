# 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV brings plug-in hybrid versatility to the compact SUV segment, combining an efficient gasoline engine with electric motor assistance. Buyers considering a used model should evaluate both its powertrain configurations and known owner feedback regarding visibility and electronics to ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid

This 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine operates alongside electric motors to deliver smooth everyday efficiency and adequate power for the compact SUV. It is generally regarded as a dependable core for the plug-in hybrid system.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

The dedicated plug-in hybrid transmission system manages power distribution between the gas engine and electric drive units without traditional gear shifting. It offers a seamless driving feel with minimal mechanical complexity.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera

Owner reports highlight occasional visibility concerns with the reversing camera system.

**Dim Rear Camera Display**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice that the rear camera lighting appears excessively dim while reversing.
- **Solution:** Have the camera module and electrical connections inspected by a dealership or technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V507000: Structure:Body:Hatchback/Liftgate:Support Device/Strut](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+PHEV&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The liftgate gas spring cylinders may corrode and lose pressure, which can result in a gas spring cylinder rupture or the liftgate falling unexpectedly. A gas spring cylinder rupture or a liftgate falling unexpectedly can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MMNA will replace the left and right liftgate gas springs, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV is a compelling choice for buyers seeking plug-in hybrid efficiency in an SUV package. While long-term powertrain reliability is generally solid, prospective owners should thoroughly inspect electrical features and driver-assistance cameras during a pre-purchase test drive.
