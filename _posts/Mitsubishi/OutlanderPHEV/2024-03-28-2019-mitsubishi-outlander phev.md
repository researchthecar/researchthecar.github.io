**Introduction:**

The 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV is a plug-in hybrid SUV that combines a 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine with an electric motor and battery pack. It offers an EPA-estimated 22 miles of pure electric range and can travel up to 380 miles on a full tank of gas. The Outlander PHEV is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features. However, it is criticized for its slow acceleration and poor fuel economy in non-hybrid mode.

**Engine**

**2.4L Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption has been reported by some owners, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * **Solution:** Replenishing oil levels or replacing piston rings.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT Transmission**

* **Hesitation During Acceleration:** Some owners have experienced hesitation or sluggishness during acceleration, especially at low speeds.
    * **Solution:** Software updates or transmission servicing.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** Premature failure of the front strut mounts has been reported, causing noise and vibration.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the strut mounts.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Some owners have reported rapid battery discharge, leading to the vehicle failing to start.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the battery or addressing other electrical issues.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Charging System Issues:** Malfunctions in the charging system, including the charger or charging port, have been reported.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the affected components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Separation:** The rear bumper may detach from the vehicle, especially in cold weather.
    * **Solution:** Refitting the bumper or replacing its brackets.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Malfunctions:** Malfunctioning seat heaters have been reported, resulting in excessive heat or failure to work.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the heated seat elements.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Climate Control Problems:** Issues with the climate control system, including fan speed irregularities and temperature fluctuations, have been reported.
    * **Solution:** Software updates or replacing components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV offers a spacious interior and advanced features, it has faced several issues that can impact its performance and reliability. Engine oil consumption and transmission hesitation are significant concerns. Electrical problems, such as battery discharge and charging system issues, can be costly to repair. Additionally, exterior and interior components have also experienced defects. The severity and cost of these issues should be considered when evaluating this vehicle.
