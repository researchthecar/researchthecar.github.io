# 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV

### Overview
The 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV is a plug-in hybrid SUV that aims to blend electric efficiency with the practicality of an SUV. It features a unique dual-motor all-wheel-drive system and a gasoline engine that acts primarily as a generator. This model year generally benefits from Mitsubishi's experience in PHEV technology, offering a solid, if not class-leading, ownership experience. Reliability is a key selling point for many hybrid vehicles, and the Outlander PHEV largely delivers on this front, with a focus on its powertrain and battery system. However, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that owners and technicians should be aware of to ensure long-term performance and satisfaction.

## Electrical

### Hybrid Battery System
**Reduced Range/Performance**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable decrease in electric-only driving range, slower acceleration, or warning lights related to the hybrid system.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician to check battery health, charging system, and control modules. Potential battery module replacement or software updates.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000+


### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment unit, or in some cases, replacement of the unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The hybrid battery system and infotainment unit are the most commonly cited areas for potential issues, though these are not universally reported and often addressable with software updates or professional diagnosis. Owners can expect a dependable vehicle with proper maintenance, with the primary concerns revolving around the longevity and performance of the advanced electrical systems. For most owners, the Outlander PHEV should provide a reliable and efficient driving experience.
