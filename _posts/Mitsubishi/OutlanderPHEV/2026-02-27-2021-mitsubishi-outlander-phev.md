# 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV offers plug-in hybrid efficiency and practical all-wheel-drive capability for shoppers seeking an eco-friendly crossover. While it provides a versatile driving experience, prospective used-car buyers should carefully review how the complex hybrid powertrain and associated electronics behave during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

This 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine operates on the Atkinson cycle, pairing with electric motors to produce an estimated total system output of around 221 horsepower. It serves primarily as a generator or direct drive source at highway speeds, generally proving durable if regular maintenance intervals are observed.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed-Gear Transaxle

The plug-in hybrid system utilizes a specialized single-speed transaxle paired with front and rear electric motors to distribute power smoothly without traditional gear shifts. This direct-drive setup is mechanically simpler than conventional multi-speed automatic transmissions, though buyers should verify smooth operation across hybrid and electric driving modes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V507000: Structure:Body:Hatchback/Liftgate:Support Device/Strut](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+PHEV&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The liftgate gas spring cylinders may corrode and lose pressure, which can result in a gas spring cylinder rupture or the liftgate falling unexpectedly. A gas spring cylinder rupture or a liftgate falling unexpectedly can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MMNA will replace the left and right liftgate gas springs, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2021 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV is an appealing option for daily commuting and efficient family driving, provided the hybrid system checks out.
