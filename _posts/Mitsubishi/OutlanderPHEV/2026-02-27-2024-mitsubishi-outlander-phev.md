# 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV offers plug-in hybrid efficiency paired with standard all-wheel drive, making it a compelling option for buyers seeking an eco-friendly three-row crossover. As a relatively fresh model in its generation, it benefits from modern tech and a refined chassis. However, used car shoppers should always carefully review vehicle history records and inspect the high-voltage battery health, charging equipment, and onboard electronics prior to purchase to ensure trouble-free ownership.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Inline-4 Plug-In Hybrid

This powertrain pairs a 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder gas engine with dual electric motors for a combined output of approximately 248 horsepower. It provides smooth, efficient power delivery for daily commuting and road trips alike.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

A specialized direct-drive system integrates seamlessly with the dual-motor plug-in hybrid architecture to distribute power efficiently to all four wheels. It delivers seamless acceleration without traditional gear shifts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V369000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mitsubishi&model=Outlander+PHEV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the A-IVI software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mitsubishi Outlander PHEV represents an appealing choice for used plug-in hybrid shoppers, offering strong versatility and an advanced electric-gas setup. While broad owner complaint data is sparse for this model year, buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electrical systems, charging components, and overall hybrid health to secure a reliable vehicle.
