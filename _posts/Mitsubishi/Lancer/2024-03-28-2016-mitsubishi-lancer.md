**Introduction:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Lancer is a compact sedan that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It comes with a standard 148-hp 2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine mated to a 5-speed manual or continuously variable transmission (CVT). A 168-hp 2.4-liter 4-cylinder engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission is also available. The Lancer is known for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and affordable price. However, it has also faced some issues that have affected the satisfaction of its owners.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter 4-Cylinder Engine with 5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Engine Stalling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may suddenly stall while driving, especially when at low speeds or during acceleration.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the throttle body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty timing chain tensioner can lead to excessive chain slack, causing the engine to make a rattling noise.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-Liter 4-Cylinder Engine with CVT**

* **CVT Transmission Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The CVT transmission may experience slipping, hesitation, or shuddering during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the CVT transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: 18V-531

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may leak fluid, causing a drop in fluid levels and affecting the transmission's operation.
    * Solution: Repair the leak, replace the transmission gasket or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or refusing to change gears.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the transmission valve body or solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the strut mounts can cause knocking or clunking noises from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear shock absorbers may become weak or damaged, leading to poor handling and reduced ride comfort.
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+ per pair
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain rapidly, especially after sitting overnight.
    * Solution: Identify and repair the electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail to charge the battery properly, leading to electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: 17V-667

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Fogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The headlights may become foggy or cloudy, reducing visibility.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per light
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may develop cracks or warping.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: 16V-309
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience software glitches, display errors, or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Update or reprogram the infotainment system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the repair
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Mitsubishi Lancer has faced several issues ranging from minor electrical glitches to more severe engine and transmission problems. The cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the issue and the severity of the damage. While the Lancer offers a pleasant driving experience, owners should be aware of the potential issues and factor in the potential cost of repairs before making a purchase decision.
