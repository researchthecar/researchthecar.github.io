**2013 Mitsubishi Lancer**

The 2013 Mitsubishi Lancer is a compact car that is available in sedan and hatchback body styles. It is offered with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine with a five-speed manual or CVT transmission, a 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine with a five-speed manual or CVT transmission, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with a five-speed manual or six-speed dual-clutch transmission.

The Lancer has received mixed reviews from critics. Some have praised its affordability, fuel efficiency, and sporty handling, while others have criticized its interior quality, cramped rear seat, and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine Issues:**

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder Engine (5-speed manual transmission)**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine vacuum leak, decreased engine performance, misfires
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, reduced engine power, misfiring
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder Engine (CVT transmission)**

* **CVT transmission failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears, shuddering
    * Solution: Replace the CVT transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: 16V571

**2.4-liter Four-Cylinder Engine (5-speed manual transmission)**

* **Crankshaft position sensor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, misfiring
    * Solution: Replace the crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: 15V651
* **Water pump failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, coolant leak
    * Solution: Replace the water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4-liter Four-Cylinder Engine (CVT transmission)**

* **CVT transmission failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears, shuddering
    * Solution: Replace the CVT transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: 16V571

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine (5-speed manual transmission)**

* **Turbocharger failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Clutch failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, difficulty shifting
    * Solution: Replace the clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine (6-speed dual-clutch transmission)**

* **Dual-clutch transmission failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gear slipping, shuddering, difficulty shifting
    * Solution: Replace the dual-clutch transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: 16V570

**Transmission Issues:**

* **Transmission mount failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive vibration, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace the transmission mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
