**Introduction:**

The 2023 Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross is a compact crossover SUV that offers a comfortable and stylish ride. It comes standard with a 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). All-wheel drive is available as an option. The Eclipse Cross has received praise for its spacious interior, user-friendly infotainment system, and affordable price point. However, it has also been criticized for its lackluster performance and uninspiring exterior design.

**Engine**

**1.5-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Turbocharged Oil Leaks:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive oil leakage around the turbocharger, causing a burning oil smell and potential engine damage.
    * **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $3,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Premature Belt Wear:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive wear on the CVT belt, leading to slipping, shuddering, and potential transmission failure.
    * **Solution:** Replace the CVT belt and pulleys.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found (excluding paint chips or fading)**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Inconsistent temperature control, fan noises, or failure to operate.
    * **Solution:** Diagnose and repair/replace the affected components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $800+
* **Seat Heater Shorts:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Overheating or malfunctions of the seat heaters, causing discomfort or potential burn hazards.
    * **Solution:** Replace the heating elements or control module.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross has a few notable issues that can impact its reliability and owner satisfaction. The turbocharger oil leaks and CVT belt wear can be expensive to repair, while the electrical glitches and interior malfunctions can be frustrating and distracting. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues should be considered when evaluating the Eclipse Cross for potential ownership.
