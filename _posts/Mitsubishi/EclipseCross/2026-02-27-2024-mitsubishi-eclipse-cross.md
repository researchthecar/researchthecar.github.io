# 2024 Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross, buyers will find a modern compact crossover backed by Mitsubishi's standard warranty coverage. While relatively new to the used market, early owner feedback highlights a few electrical and body-related concerns worth noting during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 152 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily commuting and efficient driving dynamics for the crossover.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) is paired with the standard all-wheel-drive system on the Eclipse Cross. It delivers smooth power delivery focused primarily on fuel economy.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Wiring

Issues related to interior electronics, wiring, and infotainment software.

**Infotainment Connectivity and Electrical Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported issues with smartphone connectivity, unresponsive emergency features, and in rare instances, severe electrical fires originating near the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the vehicle's wiring harness and software version to ensure all modules are functioning properly.

## Exterior

### Panoptic Sunroof

Exterior glass and visibility components, including panoramic roof options.

**Panoptic Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass panel on the panoramic sunroof may shatter or explode unexpectedly while driving or parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any pre-existing stress fractures and verify whether coverage applies under warranty.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mitsubishi Eclipse Cross offers a contemporary styling and standard all-wheel drive, but used buyers should remain vigilant. Reviewing vehicle history reports and thoroughly checking electrical systems and glass panels during a pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
