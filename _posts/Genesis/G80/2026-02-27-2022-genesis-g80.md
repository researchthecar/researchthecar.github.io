# 2022 Genesis G80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Genesis G80 delivers a striking luxury sedan experience with refined styling and advanced technology. As owners have driven these vehicles off the lot, a small number of electrical, powertrain, and driver-assistance quirks have surfaced in early feedback. Prospective buyers should carefully inspect tech features and test drive the transmission to ensure smooth operation.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

This base 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 300 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk daily acceleration and solid everyday efficiency for the luxury sedan.

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The available 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 generates approximately 375 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque. It offers effortless high-speed passing power and refined, quiet operation.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the rear wheels or all four wheels depending on configuration. It generally shifts imperceptibly, though rare software hiccups have been reported by owners.

**Delayed Up-Shift During Passing**
- **Symptoms:** When accelerating quickly to pass another vehicle, the transmission may downshift and become stuck in a lower gear rather than upshifting normally.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission software calibration and fluid levels to ensure proper gear shifting operation.

## Electrical

### Instrument Panel and Infotainment

Advanced electronics power the cabin displays and convenience features, though isolated software glitches can affect screens and security components.

**Digital Dashboard and Navigation Freezes**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument panel may occasionally fail to turn on or display speed and RPM, while the navigation system and driver seat modules act sporadically.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a dealership or inspect relevant control modules and wiring harnesses for intermittent faults.

**Random Vehicle Alarm Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The car alarm may trigger spontaneously multiple times without an apparent trigger, requiring the key fob or driving the vehicle to stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latches, hood switches, and the security system module for false trigger signals.

### Lane Change Assist

Active safety suites provide robust driver assistance, though sensor calibration sensitivity can occasionally trigger false warnings.

**Lane Change Assist Warning Messages**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden warning messages on the dashboard instructing them to check the lane change assist function.
- **Solution:** Inspect sensor alignment, clean camera lenses and bumper radar areas, and check for system software updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V094000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioners with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V630000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V282000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V229000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Genesis G80 is a compelling luxury option packed with high-end features and strong engine choices. While early owner reports highlight minor electronic glitches and occasional transmission quirks, these do not outweigh the vehicle's overall luxury appeal. Buyers should perform a thorough test drive verifying all electronics, driver aids, and transmission shift behaviors before purchasing.
