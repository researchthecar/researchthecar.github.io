### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Genesis G80 demonstrates strong initial reliability across its core mechanical components, including the 2.5L and 3.5L engines and the 8-speed automatic transmission. The chassis and suspension systems also appear robust. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning occasional infotainment glitches and intermittent operation of advanced driver-assistance features. These are often resolvable with software updates or recalibrations. Exterior and interior components have shown no significant widespread problems. Overall, the G80 presents as a reliable luxury vehicle with a focus on advanced technology, where most reported issues are minor and software-related.
