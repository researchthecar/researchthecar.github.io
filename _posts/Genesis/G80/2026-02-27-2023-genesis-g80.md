# 2023 Genesis G80

### Overview
The 2023 Genesis G80 is a luxury sedan that aims to blend sophisticated design with advanced technology and a comfortable driving experience. As a relatively new model in the premium segment, its initial reliability data suggests a strong performance, with owners generally reporting positive ownership experiences. Genesis, as a brand, has been focusing on building a reputation for quality and dependability, and the G80 appears to be a strong contender in this regard. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the G80 demonstrates a commendable level of build quality and component integration, particularly in its powertrain and core systems. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Genesis G80 demonstrates strong overall reliability, aligning with Genesis's commitment to quality. The powertrain, including both the 2.5L and 3.5L engines and the 8-speed automatic transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential sensor calibration needs for ADAS features. These are not indicative of systemic failures but rather typical of complex modern vehicle electronics. For potential owners, the G80 presents a compelling option with a high degree of expected dependability, with most reported issues being minor and manageable.
