# 2020 Genesis G80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Genesis G80 delivers a luxury midsize sedan experience with robust performance and a well-appointed cabin. As buyers look at the used market, understanding the powertrain options and any reported owner experiences helps guide a smart purchase. Reviewing known mechanical options and owner feedback ensures you select a reliable model that meets your driving needs.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6

This naturally aspirated 3.8-liter V6 engine produces around 311 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and dependable power delivery. It serves as the standard powerplant for the lineup and generally maintains a solid reputation for long-term durability.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbo V6

This twin-turbocharged 3.3-liter V6 generates approximately 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for Sport trims. It balances spirited performance with everyday drivability while requiring standard routine maintenance.

### 5.0L V8

The larger 5.0-liter V8 engine puts out roughly 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque for maximum performance in upper trims. It delivers classic eight-cylinder power and refinement, though fuel economy is lower compared to the V6 variants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

This traditional 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive across the lineup. It delivers smooth, seamless gear shifts suited to a luxury sedan.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V160000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module could malfunction and short circuit. An electrical short circuit inside the ABS module could cause an engine compartment fire while parked or driving.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2020 Genesis G80 stands out as a strong used luxury sedan option, offering a refined ride and multiple robust engine choices. Prospective buyers should still perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall vehicle condition before finalizing their purchase.
