# 2019 Genesis G80

The 2019 Genesis G80 is a luxury sedan that generally offers a strong reliability record, reflecting Genesis's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Built on a foundation of proven Hyundai/Kia components, the G80 typically avoids the widespread, costly issues that can plague some competitors. Owners often praise its comfortable ride, premium interior, and robust powertrain options. While no vehicle is entirely immune to problems, the G80's design and manufacturing focus on durability contribute to a positive ownership experience, with most reported issues being minor or related to typical usage rather than inherent design flaws. ### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Genesis G80 stands out as a reliable luxury sedan. Its powertrain, including the 3.8L V6 and 5.0L V8 engines paired with the 8-speed automatic transmission, demonstrates excellent durability with no significant widespread issues reported. The chassis and suspension components are also generally robust. Minor concerns are primarily concentrated in the electrical system, with occasional infotainment glitches and rare instances of battery drain requiring diagnosis. Interior trim can sometimes develop minor rattles, but these are typically easily resolved. Overall, the G80 offers a dependable ownership experience, making it a strong contender in its segment for those prioritizing reliability.
