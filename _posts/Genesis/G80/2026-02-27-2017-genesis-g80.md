# 2017 Genesis G80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Genesis G80 delivers a luxurious ride and strong value as a used executive sedan, but prospective buyers should inspect powertrain behavior and known mechanical areas carefully. While owner satisfaction tends to be high regarding comfort and features, various complaints logged with federal safety regulators highlight intermittent mechanical and electrical concerns that shoppers should keep in mind.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6

A 3.8-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine producing around 311 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and reliable daily performance.

### 5.0L V8

A powerful 5.0-liter V8 engine pushing roughly 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration for enthusiasts.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, generally smooth shifting but occasionally prone to rough operation reported by owners.

**Shifting Hesitation and Hard Shifts**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience intermittent hesitation, jerking, or loud thumping noises when changing gears.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealership inspect the transmission control module and fluid condition for potential software updates or component wear.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Mechanism

Provides responsive handling, though select owners have noted binding or failure to self-center during turns.

**Steering Wheel Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may stick or fail to return smoothly to center after completing a left turn, requiring manual correction.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and intermediate shaft assembly; replace worn steering components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Manages core vehicle electronics and starting protocols, with occasional owner reports of unexpected electrical failures.

**Starting Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may intermittently fail to crank or start after being parked, requiring a tow to a service center.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing of the starting system, battery, and electrical connections is required to isolate failing modules.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior glass and trim components generally hold up well, though isolated incidents of spontaneous glass breakage have been documented.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass moonroof or sunroof may suddenly shatter or explode while driving at highway speeds without any apparent impact from outside objects.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame alignment to ensure proper stress distribution.

### Door Latches

Cabin appointments offer luxury comfort, though door mechanism reliability warrants checking during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Door Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors may fail to unlatch or open from either the inside or outside, potentially trapping occupants.
- **Solution:** Replace malfunctioning door lock actuators or latch mechanisms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V160000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module could malfunction and short circuit. An electrical short circuit inside the ABS module could cause an engine compartment fire while parked or driving.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V107000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Solenoid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Water may enter the starter solenoid and cause an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a remedy relay kit in the engine junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Genesis G80 offers compelling luxury and strong performance options, but used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle before purchasing. Pay close attention during a test drive to transmission shift quality and steering return behavior, and ensure all relevant safety recalls have been addressed by a certified dealer.
