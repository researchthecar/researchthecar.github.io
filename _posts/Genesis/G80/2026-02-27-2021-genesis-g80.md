# 2021 Genesis G80

### Overview
The 2021 Genesis G80, a luxury mid-size sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Genesis's commitment to quality and advanced engineering. This model year introduced a new platform and updated powertrains, which have largely proven robust. Owners have reported a low incidence of significant mechanical or electrical problems, with most concerns falling within the expected range for a new vehicle generation. The G80's sophisticated technology and premium materials are also holding up well, contributing to a positive ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 2021 G80 stands out for its dependable performance and build quality, making it a solid choice in its segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Genesis G80 demonstrates excellent reliability across its powertrain, suspension, and core structural components. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the infotainment system, typically manifesting as software-related glitches that are often resolved with updates. The advanced driver-assistance systems and exterior/interior elements are performing as expected for a vehicle of this caliber. Overall, the G80 offers a dependable luxury experience with a low probability of significant mechanical or electrical failures, making it a highly reliable choice in the luxury sedan market.
