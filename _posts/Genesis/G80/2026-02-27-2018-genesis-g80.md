# 2018 Genesis G80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Genesis G80 delivers a luxury-focused driving experience with a well-appointed cabin and composed ride quality. However, prospective buyers should inspect specific recurring trouble spots reported by owners, including steering feel anomalies, door lock actuator failures, and intermittent climate control issues.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6

The 3.8L V6 engine produces around 311 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth acceleration for daily driving. It generally offers solid long-term mechanical reliability, though owners occasionally monitor basic fluid levels and cooling performance.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.3L twin-turbocharged V6 generates roughly 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, offering stronger low-end power for the sport-oriented trims. Maintenance requires attentive oil change intervals to keep the turbochargers running reliably over high mileage.

### 5.0L V8

The 5.0L V8 engine pushes out an impressive 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque for effortless highway passing. While robust, it demands higher fuel consumption and regular preventative maintenance to avoid minor accessory component wear.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The standard 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the rear wheels or HTRAC all-wheel-drive system. It shifts crisply under normal driving conditions and maintains a dependable service record when fluid changes are performed on schedule.

## Steering

### Steering Mechanism

Steering components generally provide precise control, but owners should watch for steering resistance or a failure to return to center during test drives.

**Steering Wheel Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel tends to stick or fail to return smoothly to the center position after completing a turn, requiring extra driver effort to straighten out.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and intermediate shaft assembly; replacement or lubrication may be necessary as outlined in manufacturer service bulletins.

## Electrical

### Climate Control System

Electrical accessories add convenience, but intermittent climate control failures can reduce cabin comfort during hot summer months.

**Air Conditioning Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The air conditioning blower stops producing cold air or the climate system shuts down entirely after extended periods of driving in hot weather.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the compressor, climate control module, or evaporator sensors and replace faulty electrical components as needed.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Locks

Cabin hardware holds up well overall, though rear door lock actuators are prone to mechanical or electrical failure over time.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rear doors fail to lock or unlock electronically, and occasionally become stuck shut so they cannot be opened from either the inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuator mechanism within the affected door assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V160000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module could malfunction and short circuit. An electrical short circuit inside the ABS module could cause an engine compartment fire while parked or driving.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V538000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Manifold/Header/Muffler/Tail Pipe](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Oil leaking onto the exhaust manifold increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the turbocharger oil supply pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V305000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the glass bonding strength is reduced over time, the windshield and rear window may detach while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front and rear glass, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V496000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, if a child or infant is in the seat, the front passenger air bag may deploy instead of being deactivated. In the event of a crash, if the front passenger air bag deploys instead of being deactivated when a child or infant are in the front passenger seat, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the OCS, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V107000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Solenoid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Water may enter the starter solenoid and cause an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a remedy relay kit in the engine junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G80&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Genesis G80 is an appealing pre-owned luxury sedan offering strong powertrain choices and a refined ride. Buyers should thoroughly test the air conditioning, check all door latches for proper locking operation, and pay close attention to steering return feel during a pre-purchase inspection.
