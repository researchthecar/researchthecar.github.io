# 2021 Genesis GV80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Genesis GV80 offers striking luxury, comfort, and performance, but prospective buyers should review recurring owner-reported complaints closely. While luxury features and styling impress, electrical issues, headlight condensation, and powertrain concerns have surfaced in owner feedback. Careful inspection during a test drive can help identify whether a specific pre-owned model exhibits these early-production quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged I4

This base 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-four produces around 300 horsepower, providing adequate daily acceleration for the luxury midsize SUV. It balances everyday efficiency with respectable pep, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

### 3.5L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 generates approximately 375 horsepower, offering smooth, robust acceleration suited for highway cruising. It represents a popular powertrain choice for buyers seeking strong performance.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly loses drive power without warning, while the engine continues to run and displays no dashboard error codes.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation of the fuel delivery system and sensors.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The standard 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes across all trim levels and drivetrain configurations. It generally operates reliably when fluid changes are maintained according to schedule.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Warning Lights

Advanced driver assistance and electrical architecture can occasionally trigger false warnings.

**Spurious Dashboard Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple warning lights illuminate simultaneously on the dashboard, ranging from collision sensor alerts to headlamp failure notifications.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician scan for diagnostic trouble codes and check module software integrity.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems on early models have shown susceptibility to moisture intrusion.

**Headlight Failure and Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture and condensation accumulate inside the headlamp housing, leading to short circuits, warning messages, and complete headlight or turn signal failure.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected headlight assembly and ensure proper weather sealing.
- **Cost:** $2,000+

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V796000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioner(s) may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner could project metal fragments into the vehicle, striking vehicle occupants and resulting in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V208000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The fuel tube connecting the high pressure fuel pump to the fuel rail may not seal properly, which could allow fuel to leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel tube, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V094000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioners with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V630000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V229000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Genesis GV80 is an appealing pre-owned luxury SUV, but shoppers should prioritize inspecting the electrical systems, exterior headlamp housings, and powertrain performance. Checking for accumulated moisture in headlights and ensuring all recall updates have been completed will help secure a more reliable ownership experience.
