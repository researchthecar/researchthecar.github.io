# 2022 Genesis GV80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Genesis GV80 brings striking luxury, cutting-edge technology, and impressive comfort to the midsize luxury SUV segment. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints concerning powertrain quirks, electrical glitches, and safety system anomalies to make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

The base 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily commuting while maintaining competitive efficiency for a luxury SUV.

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 generates around 375 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and confident highway passing power.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving, accompanied by hard starting, fuel warnings, or fuel leaks.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect fuel lines, O-rings, and the fuel pump assembly for leaks or pressure drops, replacing damaged components as needed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options and all-wheel-drive configurations to deliver refined power delivery during normal driving conditions.

## Interior

### Headlamp Assemblies and Modules

Exterior lighting and electrical modules generally operate reliably, though occasional reports highlight moisture sealing concerns.

**Moisture Intrusion and Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlamp assemblies collect condensation, causing daytime running lights and headlights to fail alongside multiple dashboard warning codes.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlamp seals and replace compromised headlamp assemblies and control modules under warranty or service guidelines.
- **Cost:** Variable depending on dealer diagnosis and warranty coverage.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Advanced safety features provide reassuring protection, though owners have noted occasional mechanical gripes with seat belt hardware.

**Seat Belt Retraction Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants may experience seat belts failing to retract smoothly or binding during normal use.
- **Solution:** Have the seat belt mechanism inspected by a dealership to ensure proper tensioner and retractor operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V094000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioners with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V630000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V282000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V229000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV80&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Genesis GV80 offers compelling luxury and strong performance, but used-car shoppers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history reports, verify that all software updates have been applied, and check for any signs of electrical or fuel system trouble before purchasing.
