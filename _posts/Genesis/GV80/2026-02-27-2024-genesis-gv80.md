### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Genesis GV80 demonstrates a commendable level of technical reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The available 2.5L Turbo and 3.5L Twin-Turbo V6 engines, paired with the 8-speed automatic transmission, have shown minimal reported issues. Suspension systems are also performing robustly. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and the need for sensor recalibration for ADAS features. These are not typically indicative of major failures but rather common in complex modern vehicle systems. Exterior and interior components are generally holding up well, with no widespread durability issues reported. Overall, the GV80 offers a reliable luxury SUV experience, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
