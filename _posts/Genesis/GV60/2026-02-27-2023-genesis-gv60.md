# 2023 Genesis GV60 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Genesis GV60 is a striking, tech-forward all-electric luxury crossover that shares its modern E-GMP platform with other acclaimed Hyundai and Kia EVs. While it offers rapid charging, luxury interior finishes, and impressive performance, used car buyers should pay close attention to reported electrical and charging powertrain issues, particularly regarding the Integrated Charging Control Unit (ICCU). Potential buyers should verify that all factory campaigns and software updates have been properly applied before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motor AWD (Standard / Advanced)

The standard and Advanced trims utilize a dual-motor all-wheel-drive electric powertrain generating roughly 314 horsepower, offering smooth and rapid acceleration. Overall system durability is generally praised, though early production units have experienced notable high-voltage charging and power delivery complaints.

**ICCU and Propulsion System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden loss of motive power, enters a limp mode accompanied by a popping sound, or refuses to charge.
- **Solution:** Dealer inspection, replacement of the Integrated Charging Control Unit (ICCU) and associated fuses, and updating vehicle software.

**Unexpected Speed Limitation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly decelerates while driving and restricts maximum speed to approximately 20 mph.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan by a certified dealer to check for powertrain management faults and apply relevant software patches.

### Dual Electric Motor AWD (Performance)

The range-topping Performance trim boosts output to roughly 429 horsepower (with temporary boost modes reaching 483 hp) for exhilarating acceleration. It features identical high-voltage architecture to the standard models, sharing the same platform mechanics.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Equipped with a fixed-ratio single-speed direct drive automatic transmission that delivers seamless, gearless power delivery to the wheels. This simplified EV drivetrain setup eliminates traditional multi-gear mechanical wear points and generally demonstrates solid mechanical reliability.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster Display

The cabin relies heavily on high-resolution digital screens and advanced driver-assistance features, which occasionally suffer from software-related initialization delays.

**Blank Instrument Cluster at Startup**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard instrument screen fails to power on upon vehicle startup, leaving the driver without a speedometer, warning indicators, or safety data for several minutes.
- **Solution:** Rebooting the infotainment system or performing software updates; persistent failures may require module replacement.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment includes a comprehensive suite of active driver aids and pre-collision systems designed to protect occupants.

**Pre-Active Seat Belt Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The seat belt pre-tensioner activates unexpectedly while driving, tightening the seat belt without cause.
- **Solution:** Dealer inspection of the seat belt pretensioner sensors and control modules for calibration or replacement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V094000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioners with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V300000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left and right rear driveshafts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V557000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 208, "Occupant Crash Protection" and 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." A seat belt that fails to fasten or extend properly may not adequately restrain an occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front passenger-side seat belt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V065000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rear inner driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V204000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The Integrated Charging Control Unit (ICCU) may become damaged and stop charging the 12-Volt battery, which can result in a loss of drive power. A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the ICCU and its fuse, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V868000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The Integrated Charging Control Unit (ICCU) may become damaged and stop charging the 12-volt battery, which can result in a loss of drive power. A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the ICCU and its fuse, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V474000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Controls and Displays." An instrument panel display that fails to show critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V624000: Structure:Exterior Trim](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV60&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Detached molding can create a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield molding, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Genesis GV60 is an appealing pre-owned luxury electric crossover that delivers stellar performance, comfort, and cutting-edge features. However, prospective buyers should exercise caution and ensure that all electrical and powertrain updates—specifically concerning the ICCU—have been fully addressed by a certified dealer. Securing a complete service history and verifying open recall statuses are essential steps before finalizing a purchase.
