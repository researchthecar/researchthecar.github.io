# 2024 Genesis GV70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Genesis GV70, buyers get a stylish and tech-forward luxury compact SUV. While the ownership experience is generally positive, owner reports submitted to safety authorities highlight a few intermittent mechanical and electrical concerns to keep in mind during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

The base 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk daily performance. It is paired with an all-wheel-drive system and offers a refined balance of power and efficiency for everyday driving.

**Unexpected Acceleration Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle suddenly lost power or the ability to accelerate properly while driving or entering a highway.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the powertrain control module, electronic throttle body, and associated sensors for fault codes.

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The available 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine steps up output to roughly 375 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque. It delivers effortless acceleration and smooth power delivery, though prospective buyers should verify all electronic powertrain diagnostics during an inspection.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters and an electronic gear selector. It delivers smooth, quick shifts that complement both engine options.

**Unintended Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported an issue where the electronic gear shifter independently moved from the Drive position into Neutral while traveling at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic shifter assembly, transmission control module, and software calibrations for potential updates or component failure.

### Drivetrain and Suspension Noise

The suspension and drivetrain are engineered to provide a composed ride, though buyers should road test the vehicle thoroughly at highway speeds to listen for unusual vibrations or wind noise.

**Highway Shaking and Wind/Mechanical Whirling Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted persistent vehicle vibrations between 40 and 75 mph, alongside loud whistling or whirling noises originating from underneath the vehicle at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Check wheel balancing, driveshaft components, and underbody aerodynamic shielding for loose fasteners or alignment issues.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Mirrors

Advanced electrical architectures govern everything from driver assistance systems to lighting and convenience features, making a thorough battery and module scan crucial during a used car purchase.

**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have observed noticeable moisture and water droplets accumulating inside the front headlight assemblies after rain or car washes.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seal integrity. Dealerships may evaluate housings under warranty if moisture pooling causes excessive internal fogging.

**Unprompted Sideview Mirror Folding**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger-side exterior mirror has been reported to unexpectedly fold inward while the vehicle is in motion, momentarily obstructing side visibility.
- **Solution:** Check the power mirror assembly and associated control modules for electrical faults or software glitches.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V205000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV70&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Water may leak into the transmission control harness, which can cause a short circuit and unexpectedly shift the transmission from Park to Neutral. An unexpected shift from Park to Neutral can cause a vehicle roll away and increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the transmission control harness and repair the connector, or replace the harness and internal wiring, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V229000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=GV70&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Genesis GV70 is a compelling luxury SUV packed with performance and high-end features, but prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and conduct thorough test drives. Pay close attention to highway ride smoothness, electronics operation, and powertrain responsiveness to ensure everything functions as intended.
