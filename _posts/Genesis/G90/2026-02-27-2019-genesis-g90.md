### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Genesis G90 demonstrates a strong overall reliability profile, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission, where no significant widespread issues have been reported. The luxury sedan excels in providing a dependable driving experience, aligning with Genesis's goal of establishing a reputation for quality. Minor concerns are primarily concentrated within the electrical system, specifically related to occasional infotainment glitches and potential intermittent battery drain, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronics. These issues are generally manageable with software updates or targeted component diagnosis. The exterior and interior elements have shown good durability. Overall, the G90 is a reliable choice for a luxury sedan, with most potential issues being minor and addressable.
