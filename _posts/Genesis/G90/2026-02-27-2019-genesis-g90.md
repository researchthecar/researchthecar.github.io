# 2019 Genesis G90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Genesis G90 is a flagship luxury sedan known for its supreme comfort, upscale cabin, and sophisticated ride quality. While overall owner satisfaction remains high, prospective buyers should review available complaint logs for isolated mechanical and electrical concerns. As with any complex modern luxury vehicle, a thorough pre-purchase inspection helps verify that all onboard systems and powertrain options are operating smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.3L twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and effortless acceleration. This powertrain option balances refined luxury performance with modern efficiency for everyday driving.

### 5.0L Naturally Aspirated V8

The 5.0L naturally aspirated V8 engine delivers a robust 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque for traditional power delivery. It offers strong, linear performance suited to a large luxury flagship sedan.

**Fuel System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected fuel pump and fuel injection failures affecting vehicle operation.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the fuel delivery system and replace any faulty fuel pumps or injectors.

**Burning Odor and Smoke**
- **Symptoms:** An abnormal burning odor and blue smoke originating from underneath the vehicle have been observed while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the engine bay and exhaust components for fluid leaks or burning oil, and repair as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes paired with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive configurations. It is generally robust and supports the vehicle's smooth cruising character.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Issues related to steering feel or extraneous noise should be inspected to ensure proper handling dynamics.

**Steering Wheel Clicking Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an abnormal clicking noise emanating from the steering wheel area while operating the vehicle at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a certified service center inspect the steering column and intermediate shaft for wear or loose components.

## Electrical

### Main Electrical System

The complex electronic architecture requires proper battery and alternator health to prevent unexpected disruptions.

**Intermittent Electrical Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden and unexpected electrical system failures while driving in traffic.
- **Solution:** Check battery connections, charging systems, and relevant wiring harnesses, and follow any manufacturer recall notices.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V107000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Solenoid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Water may enter the starter solenoid and cause an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a remedy relay kit in the engine junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Genesis G90 delivers impressive luxury and performance, though used-car buyers should remain vigilant regarding isolated electrical and engine reports. Verifying that all manufacturer recalls have been addressed and conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
