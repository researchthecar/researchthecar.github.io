# 2021 Genesis G90

### Overview
The 2021 Genesis G90 represents the pinnacle of Genesis's luxury sedan offerings, emphasizing refined comfort, advanced technology, and powerful performance. As a flagship model, it generally benefits from robust engineering and high-quality materials. Reliability data for the 2021 G90 indicates a strong performance, with owners reporting minimal significant mechanical or electrical issues outside of expected maintenance. The focus on premium components and rigorous testing contributes to its reputation for dependability. While no vehicle is entirely immune to problems, the G90 stands out for its overall build quality and the low incidence of widespread, costly defects, making it a solid choice for luxury sedan buyers seeking peace of mind.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Genesis G90 demonstrates excellent reliability, with its powertrain and core mechanical systems performing exceptionally well. The most commonly reported, albeit minor, issues tend to be related to the advanced electronic systems, such as occasional infotainment glitches or sensor recalibration needs for ADAS features. These are not indicative of systemic failures but rather typical of complex modern vehicle technology. Overall, the G90 offers a dependable luxury experience with a low probability of significant, costly repairs, making it a strong contender in its segment for buyers prioritizing reliability alongside luxury.
