# 2017 Genesis G90

### Overview
The 2017 Genesis G90, a flagship luxury sedan, was designed to compete with established premium brands, offering a blend of sophisticated styling, advanced technology, and powerful performance. As the first-generation G90, it aimed to set a high standard for Genesis's future offerings. Reliability is a key consideration for luxury vehicles, and the G90 generally performs well in this regard, with owners reporting a solid build quality and fewer significant mechanical issues compared to some competitors. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where minor concerns have been noted, primarily related to electronics and some less common component failures. Overall, the G90 presents a compelling option for those seeking a dependable luxury sedan.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Genesis G90 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanical systems. Owners generally experience a dependable and luxurious driving experience with minimal unexpected repairs. The most frequently cited areas for potential concern are within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit experiencing occasional glitches and a possibility of premature battery failure. These issues, while not widespread, are worth noting for potential buyers. The exterior and interior components, including upholstery and bodywork, have proven to be durable. For a first-generation luxury vehicle, the G90 offers a commendable reliability record, making it a solid choice in its segment.
