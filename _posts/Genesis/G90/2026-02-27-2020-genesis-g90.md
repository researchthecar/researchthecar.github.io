# 2020 Genesis G90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Genesis G90 is a flagship luxury sedan offering exceptional comfort, sophisticated styling, and robust performance. As a pre-owned purchase, it generally delivers a plush driving experience backed by high-end amenities. Buyers should still conduct a thorough inspection, keeping an eye out for minor reported glitches in auxiliary systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

This 3.3-liter twin-turbo V6 produces approximately 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and responsive acceleration. It serves as the standard powerplant for the G90, combining refined performance with commendable daily driveability.

### 5.0L Naturally Aspirated V8

The larger 5.0-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, delivering authoritative power and effortless highway cruising. It is a robust option known for its refined power delivery and overall mechanical durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The standard 8-speed automatic transmission features smooth, seamless gear shifts tailored for luxury touring and pairs with both rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally proves durable and dependable under normal driving conditions.

## Suspension

### Tires and Wheels

Suspension and wheel assemblies are designed for supreme ride comfort, though occasional tire-related vibrations have been reported by owners.

**Constant Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a persistent vibration or a sensation similar to driving over rumble strips at certain speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect tires for flat spots, balance wheels, and check alignment; replace worn or defective tires if necessary.

## Exterior

### Headlights

Lighting systems generally perform well, though isolated owner reports note minor visibility concerns.

**Headlight Beam Irregularities**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice unusual light distribution or perceived obstructions in the headlight beam pattern while driving at night.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the headlight assemblies and alignment to ensure proper illumination and functionality.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Genesis G90 remains a compelling choice for used luxury car buyers seeking comfort, advanced features, and strong powertrain choices. While isolated owner complaints exist regarding lighting and tire vibrations, the vehicle offers solid overall dependability. Prospective buyers should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection to verify all electronic and mechanical systems are in top condition.
