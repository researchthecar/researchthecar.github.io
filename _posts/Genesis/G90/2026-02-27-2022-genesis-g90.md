# 2022 Genesis G90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Genesis G90 represents the pinnacle of luxury and refinement for the brand, offering executive-level comfort and advanced technology. As a flagship sedan, it is typically equipped with robust powertrain choices designed for smooth, effortless cruising. While owner reports to safety authorities are relatively sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and verify that all electronic features operate as intended.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.3-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift and quiet acceleration. This engine is well-suited for everyday luxury driving, balancing executive performance with respectable refinement.

### 5.0L Naturally Aspirated V8

The 5.0-liter V8 engine generates roughly 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque for effortless power delivery. It provides a traditional, authoritative performance feel that complements the flagship sedan's grand touring character.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The smooth 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options and rear-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It shifts imperceptibly under normal driving conditions, enhancing the overall ride quality expected of a luxury flagship.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Main Display

Electrical architecture supports numerous advanced displays and driver-aid systems, though intermittent software glitches have been reported by some owners.

**Screen and Power Shutdown Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The main viewing screen goes blank, backup cameras fail to function, and the vehicle may occasionally fail to shut off properly.
- **Solution:** Perform a full system reset or have a dealership diagnose the software controller for updates.

### Power Seats

Cabin appointments are opulent and highly adjustable, though occasional power seat memory bugs can affect driver convenience.

**Seat Memory Function Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Saved seat memory positions may fail to automatically adjust when starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat control modules and reprogram memory settings through the infotainment interface.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety suites provide robust protection, but drivers should remain mindful of environmental limitations on camera and radar sensors.

**Pedestrian Detection Limitations in Darkness**
- **Symptoms:** Pedestrian avoidance systems may have operational limitations and reduced effectiveness in dark environments.
- **Solution:** Be aware of system parameters outlined in the owner's manual regarding sensor limitations in low-light conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V282000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Genesis G90 delivers an exceptionally comfortable and feature-rich driving experience, backed by capable powertrain options. While isolated owner complaints highlight minor electrical and interior accessory quirks, these do not indicate widespread systemic failure. Used-car shoppers should conduct a thorough inspection of all electronics, infotainment functions, and driver-assistance features prior to purchase.
