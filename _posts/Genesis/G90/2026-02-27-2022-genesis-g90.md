# 2022 Genesis G90

### Overview
The 2022 Genesis G90 represents the pinnacle of Genesis's luxury sedan offerings, aiming to compete with established German and Japanese flagships. It is equipped with a potent V8 engine and a sophisticated drivetrain, designed for smooth power delivery and a refined driving experience. As a luxury vehicle, the G90 prioritizes comfort, advanced technology, and premium materials. Reliability is a key consideration for vehicles in this segment, and Genesis has generally focused on building dependable powertrains and integrating advanced systems with a focus on long-term performance. ### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Genesis G90 demonstrates strong mechanical reliability, with its V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The suspension system also appears robust. However, like many modern luxury vehicles laden with technology, some minor electrical gremlins have been reported, primarily concerning the infotainment system's responsiveness and occasional calibration needs for advanced driver-assistance systems. These are generally manageable with software updates or professional recalibration. Exterior and interior components are holding up well, reflecting the premium build quality expected of a flagship sedan. Overall, the G90 offers a reliable ownership experience, with most potential issues being minor and related to its complex electronic systems rather than fundamental mechanical flaws.
