# 2018 Genesis G90

### Overview
The 2018 Genesis G90 represents a strong entry into the luxury sedan market, emphasizing comfort, advanced technology, and a refined driving experience. As a flagship model, it aims to compete with established German and Japanese luxury brands. Reliability is a key consideration for luxury vehicle buyers, and the G90 generally performs well in this regard. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 2018 G90 has demonstrated a commendable level of dependability, with most reported problems being minor or related to specific component failures rather than widespread systemic flaws. Owners generally appreciate the build quality and the sophisticated features offered.

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Genesis G90 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanical components. The 5.0L V8 and 3.3L Twin-Turbo V6 engines, along with the 8-speed automatic transmission, have proven to be robust. Suspension systems are also generally issue-free. The most commonly cited areas for potential concern, though not widespread, are minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system and, in some instances, premature battery failure. These issues are typically manageable with software updates or battery replacement. For a luxury vehicle, the G90 offers a dependable ownership experience with a focus on comfort and advanced features.
