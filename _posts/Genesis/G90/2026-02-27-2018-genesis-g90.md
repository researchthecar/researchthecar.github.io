# 2018 Genesis G90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Genesis G90 is a flagship luxury sedan offering refined comfort, robust performance, and an extensive suite of high-tech features. While generally praised for its plush ride and executive styling, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints concerning occasional electrical drain issues, transmission selector quirks, and initial throttle hesitation from a stop. Overall, the G90 remains a compelling pre-owned luxury value, though a thorough inspection of electrical systems and powertrain responsiveness is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.3-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces approximately 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and effortless acceleration. It offers a balanced blend of daily efficiency and strong passing power for this large luxury sedan.

**Throttle Hesitation from a Stop**
- **Symptoms:** When attempting to accelerate from a complete stop, the vehicle may hesitate or briefly delay power delivery before responding.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the throttle body, software calibrations, and accelerator pedal sensors for potential updates or faults.

### 5.0L Naturally Aspirated V8

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 delivers around 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque for effortless, traditional luxury performance. It provides refined power delivery with strong long-term reliability expectations when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission works seamlessly across all trims to provide refined, barely perceptible gear changes. It pairs well with both available engine options to optimize highway cruising efficiency and responsive low-end power.

**Gear Selector Engagement Difficulty**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic gear shifter may fail to select Reverse or Drive on the first attempt, requiring multiple tries to engage the correct gear.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic shifter assembly, control modules, and linkage components for proper operation or wear.

## Electrical

### Battery and Wiring Harness

The G90 relies heavily on advanced electronics and complex wiring networks to power its extensive luxury and safety features. Ensuring the battery and electrical connections are in top condition is vital for reliable operation.

**Unexpected Battery Discharge**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience complete electrical power loss or a dead battery, sometimes linked to moisture intrusion or corrosion in the wiring harness.
- **Solution:** Inspect the battery, charging system, and main wiring harnesses for signs of water corrosion or parasitic draws.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V538000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Manifold/Header/Muffler/Tail Pipe](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Oil leaking onto the exhaust manifold increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the turbocharger oil supply pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V305000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the glass bonding strength is reduced over time, the windshield and rear window may detach while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front and rear glass, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V107000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Solenoid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Water may enter the starter solenoid and cause an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a remedy relay kit in the engine junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Genesis G90 delivers top-tier luxury, impressive performance, and a serene cabin experience at a fraction of its original price. While owners have reported occasional electrical drainage concerns, throttle hesitation, and shifter quirks, these issues can be managed with a pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electrical system and powertrain responsiveness. Buyers who prioritize comfort and value will find the G90 to be a remarkably rewarding pre-owned flagship.
