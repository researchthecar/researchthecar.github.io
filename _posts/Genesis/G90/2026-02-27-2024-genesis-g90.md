# 2024 Genesis G90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Genesis G90 represents the pinnacle of the brand's luxury lineup, offering sophisticated styling and advanced technology. As a flagship luxury sedan, it is equipped with high-performance powertrain options designed for refined cruising. While owner reports to safety authorities are relatively sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should remain mindful of early production complexities and maintain awareness of official manufacturer notices.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers robust power, generating around 375 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth acceleration suited for executive transport, balancing responsive performance with modern luxury standards.

### 3.5L Twin-Turbo V6 with 48V Electric Supercharger

Featuring an advanced 48-volt electric supercharger, this upgraded V6 option increases output to roughly 409 horsepower and 405 lb-ft of torque. It enhances low-end throttle response and delivers effortless, turbine-like power delivery for the flagship sedan.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 8-speed automatic transmission routes power seamlessly to the standard all-wheel-drive system. It is engineered for refined, nearly imperceptible gear changes to complement the vehicle's serene ride quality.

## Exterior

### Power Distribution and Door Handles

The G90 relies heavily on complex electronic architectures and power-operated features that require thorough inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Total Electrical and Door Handle Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle displays braking system warnings, refuses to start, and electronic door handles fail, potentially trapping occupants inside.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by an authorized dealer to diagnose electrical faults and check for affected software or hardware components.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Advanced safety suites incorporate numerous cameras and radar sensors that demand precise calibration to function reliably.

**Driver Assist System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Forward collision avoidance and lane departure systems may exhibit intermittent operational warnings or persistent faults over extended periods.
- **Solution:** Consult a certified dealership to perform software recalibrations or sensor diagnostic tests.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V474000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Controls and Displays." An instrument panel display that fails to show critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V833000: Electrical System:Adas](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Sudden, unintended braking increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front bumper beam, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V218000: Seat Belts:Front:Anchorage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached seat belt anchor will not adequately restrain the seat occupant, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinforce or replace the seat belt anchors, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V229000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Genesis G90 delivers impressive luxury, high-tech features, and sophisticated powertrain choices. While overall owner complaints are minimal, isolated reports highlight potential electrical and advanced driver assistance system concerns. Used-car buyers should ensure all open safety recalls are addressed and verify that electronic systems operate correctly prior to purchase.
