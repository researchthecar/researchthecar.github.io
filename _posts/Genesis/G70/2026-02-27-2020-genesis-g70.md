## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive, or exhibit minor graphical glitches. Navigation system may occasionally lose signal.
- **Solution:** Software update or system reset. In rare cases, replacement of the head unit may be required.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1,500 (depending on software fix vs. hardware replacement)


### Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming headlights, or complete electrical system failure.
- **Solution:** Battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Genesis G70 demonstrates a high level of reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing very few reported issues. The primary areas that have seen occasional owner complaints are related to the electrical system, specifically infotainment glitches and, less commonly, premature battery failure. These issues are generally manageable with software updates or component replacement. Overall, the G70 is a dependable luxury sedan, offering a robust ownership experience with minimal risk of major mechanical problems.
