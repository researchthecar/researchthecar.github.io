# 2019 Genesis G70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Genesis G70 delivers dynamic performance and luxury appeal, making it an attractive option on the used market. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding fuel system components, electrical glitches, and certain powertrain quirks. Being aware of these areas will help ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 252 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced mix of efficiency and everyday performance. Routine maintenance is key to keeping this powerplant trouble-free.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The robust 3.3-liter twin-turbo V6 pushes out approximately 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, providing exhilarating acceleration. Buyers should verify that fuel pump safety recalls have been properly completed.

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden loss of drive power at highway speeds or unexpected stalling, sometimes accompanied by fault codes.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the high-Pressure fuel pump components under applicable recall or service campaigns.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Engine cuts out unexpectedly while idling, parking, or coming to a stop, making it difficult to restart immediately.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel pressure delivery and update powertrain control modules if necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with either rear-wheel or all-wheel drive, delivering smooth and responsive gear shifts. Overall, this unit has proven dependable when properly maintained.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission was offered exclusively on select 2.0T trims for enthusiasts wanting direct control. Prospective owners should carefully check clutch feel and engagement behavior during a test drive.

**Unintended Movement / Clutch Operation**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected vehicle movement or engagement behavior when starting the car while parked in neutral with the parking brake engaged.
- **Solution:** Inspect the clutch assembly, starter interlock switch, and associated pedal linkages.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Steering response is sharp and direct, though mechanical feedback in the column should be evaluated during low-speed maneuvers.

**Steering Column Popping**
- **Symptoms:** A noticeable popping sensation or noise is felt through the steering wheel when turning slightly at low speeds or while stationary.
- **Solution:** Inspect the intermediate steering shaft and lubricate or replace worn internal components.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Wiring

Electrical architecture handles advanced driver assists and cabin tech, though some owners report screen freezes and wiring harness concerns.

**Audio-Visual Screen Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** The navigation or infotainment screen goes blank, freezes entirely, or changes menus without driver input.
- **Solution:** Check for available software updates or replace the infotainment display unit if internal hardware fails.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim hold up well, but the panoramic sunroof has occasional reports of stress failure.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass panel on the sunroof abruptly explodes or shatters while driving at highway speeds without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment stress.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V161000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module could malfunction and short circuit. An electrical short circuit inside the ABS module could cause an engine compartment fire while parked or driving.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V196000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the trunk latch and replace the trunk latch base, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V107000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Solenoid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Water may enter the starter solenoid and cause an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a remedy relay kit in the engine junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V528000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update engine control module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Genesis G70 is a compelling sports sedan offering high-end features and strong performance at an appealing used price point. While most mechanical systems prove solid, buyers should ensure that high-pressure fuel pump recalls and electrical software updates have been fully addressed by a certified dealer. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help catch any lingering sunroof, steering, or infotainment issues before finalizing the purchase.
