# 2021 Genesis G70 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Genesis G70 delivers athletic performance and a refined cabin, making it a compelling sport sedan alternative to traditional luxury rivals. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding powertrain behavior and vehicle electronics.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This base turbocharged 4-cylinder engine produces around 252 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering a balance of efficiency and punchy acceleration for daily driving. It is generally robust, though owners should inspect service history for consistent maintenance.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The potent twin-turbo V6 pushes out 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, delivering thrilling straight-line performance. It provides smooth power delivery, but buyers should listen for any unusual noises during cold starts.

### 6-Speed Manual (2.0L Only)

A rare 6-speed manual transmission was offered exclusively on select rear-wheel-drive 2.0-liter models for enthusiasts. It offers an engaging shift feel, though clutch wear will depend heavily on the previous owner's driving habits.

### Engine Management & Idle Stop/Go

Electrical complaints are infrequent, but owners have occasionally noted issues tied to powertrain management and stop-start functionality.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported instances where the engine shuts off unexpectedly when coming to a stop or while operating, sometimes related to the Idle Stop and Go system.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the vehicle software calibrations, battery health, and idle stop-start sensors.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission features quick, responsive shifts and pairs smoothly with both engine options. Overall, it has a solid reputation for durability when properly serviced.

## Exterior

### Under-Hood Area

Exterior body fit and finish are generally high quality, though isolated major incidents have been noted by individual owners.

**Isolated Engine Compartment Fire Reports**
- **Symptoms:** Rare owner reports indicate smoke or fire originating from under the hood while the vehicle was parked or turned off.
- **Solution:** Ensure all relevant manufacturer recalls and technical bulletins have been addressed by a certified dealer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V161000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module could malfunction and short circuit. An electrical short circuit inside the ABS module could cause an engine compartment fire while parked or driving.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V569000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Insufficient fuel supply can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and repair the fuel pump assembly jet nozzle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V191000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V528000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Genesis&model=G70&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update engine control module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Genesis G70 is a stylish and capable luxury sport sedan that offers strong value compared to its German competitors. While most owners enjoy trouble-free driving, prospective buyers should thoroughly check the vehicle's history, verify that all safety recalls are completed, and test drive the car to ensure smooth powertrain operation.
