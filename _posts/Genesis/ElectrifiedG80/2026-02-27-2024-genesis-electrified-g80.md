# 2024 Genesis Electrified G80 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Genesis Electrified G80 represents a luxury electric sedan offering refined comfort, high-end appointments, and standard dual-motor all-wheel drive. As an upscale alternative to traditional executive cars, it brings strong performance and advanced EV tech to the market. For used car buyers, investigating early-production owner experiences can highlight initial quirks, though overall the vehicle remains a sophisticated choice.

---

## Engine

### Dual Motor Electric Powertrain

This dual-motor electric setup produces 365 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift acceleration paired with standard all-wheel drive. The electric drive system offers smooth, silent power delivery with minimal mechanical complexity compared to internal combustion engines.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive

A single-speed direct-drive transmission routes power seamlessly to all four wheels without traditional gear shifts. This setup is generally very reliable and maintenance-free, contributing to the serene driving experience.

## Electrical

### Charging System

Electrical and charging components manage onboard power delivery and high-voltage replenishment.

**Level 2 Charging Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported issues with Level 2 charging functionality, sometimes complicated by dealership service network limitations.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized Genesis EV service center for software updates or recall-related hardware inspections.

### Regenerative Braking System

The braking system integrates traditional hydraulic friction brakes with advanced regenerative capability.

**i-Pedal One-Pedal Driving Behavior**
- **Symptoms:** When utilizing the maximum regenerative braking setting, known as i-Pedal, the vehicle brings itself to a complete stop abruptly upon releasing the accelerator.
- **Solution:** Adjust driving habits to account for aggressive regeneration or modify the regenerative braking settings through the infotainment menu.

The 2024 Genesis Electrified G80 is a compelling luxury electric vehicle that combines impressive performance with a high-end interior.
