**Introduction:**

The 2007 Jeep Liberty is a compact SUV that offers off-road capabilities and a spacious interior. It is equipped with a 3.7L V6 engine mated to a 6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission, producing 210 horsepower and 235 lb-ft of torque. The Liberty is praised for its rugged exterior, comfortable seating, and strong towing capacity. However, it has received criticism for its subpar fuel economy and dated interior design.

**Engine**

**3.7L V6**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking, overheating, rough running
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine vacuum leak, performance loss
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak, engine smoke
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, loose steering
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical drain source
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim lights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Handle becomes loose or breaks off
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per door
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Hinge Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate becomes difficult to open or close
    * Solution: Replace tailgate hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Bubble:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard material warps and bubbles
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cracked Steering Wheel:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel develops cracks
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Jeep Liberty is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some issues, particularly with engine gaskets and electrical components. The severity of the issues ranges from minor inconveniences like door handle breakage to more serious problems like torque converter failure. Repair costs can be significant, but many of the issues are covered under warranty.
