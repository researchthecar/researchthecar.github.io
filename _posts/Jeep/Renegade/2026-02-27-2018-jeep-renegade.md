# 2018 Jeep Renegade Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Jeep Renegade offers a distinctive, boxy design and genuine off-road capability in a subcompact crossover package. However, prospective buyers should approach the used market with caution. Numerous owner complaints highlight recurring powertrain and electrical concerns, particularly related to engine stalling, fuel delivery issues, and infotainment screen failures. Careful pre-purchase inspections and a thorough check of service history are recommended.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

A 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 160 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, often paired with a manual transmission. It offers a spirited drive, though used buyers should monitor for any turbo or fluid consumption issues.

### 2.4L Tigershark 4-Cylinder

A naturally aspirated 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine putting out 180 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate daily power, but buyers should routinely check oil levels as some units experience high oil consumption.

### Engine Management and Fuel Delivery

Core fuel and engine management systems can develop unexpected stalling and fuel pump faults that require professional diagnosis.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling while driving at various speeds, often accompanied by hesitation, high RPMs, or loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis typically requires checking throttle body operation, software updates, fuel pump pressure, or replacing faulty sensors.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start or unexpectedly stalls out on the road, frequently requiring towing service.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning fuel pump assembly.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 6-speed manual gearbox available on select base trims, providing direct engagement for drivers who prefer shifting their own gears.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive or four-wheel drive variants. Used buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure smooth shifting across gears.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

While generally reliable, hydraulic brake components can occasionally exhibit booster or unexpected activation issues.

**Brake Pedal Firmness and Hissing**
- **Symptoms:** Depressing the brake pedal produces a hissing sound, and the pedal becomes exceptionally firm and difficult to push, reducing stopping power.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake booster and hydraulic lines for leaks or mechanical failure.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Touchscreen Display

The Uconnect infotainment system is prone to screen failure, random phantom inputs, and complete blackouts, which can affect backup camera visibility and climate controls.

**Screen Delamination and Glitching**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen radio delaminates, becomes completely unresponsive, goes blank, or randomly activates settings and changes radio stations while driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning head unit display assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V731000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Renegade&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A loss of fuel pressure can cause a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V524000: Electrical System:Wiring:Front Underhood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Renegade&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Jeep Renegade presents a mixed bag for used car buyers. While its charming styling and capability appeal to urban adventurers, potential owners must weigh these against frequent reports of engine stalling, fuel pump failures, and troublesome infotainment screen delamination. A rigorous pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electrical system and powertrain operation is strongly advised before finalizing any purchase.
