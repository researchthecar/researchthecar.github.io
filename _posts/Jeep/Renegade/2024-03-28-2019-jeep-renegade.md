**Introduction:**

The 2019 Jeep Renegade is a subcompact crossover SUV with a rugged design and a range of engine and transmission options. It offers 180 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque from its 2.4L Tigershark gasoline engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission, or 177 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque from its 2.0L MultiJet II diesel engine mated to a 6-speed manual or 9-speed automatic transmission. The Renegade is praised for its off-road capabilities, agile handling, and spacious interior, but some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.4L Tigershark Gasoline Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L MultiJet II Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lack of power, engine overheating, oil leaks
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Hesitation or Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission delays in shifting, rough or jerky gear changes
    * Solution: Update transmission software, replace valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration during acceleration, slipping or loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty Shifting Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard to engage or shift gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged gears, synchros
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or thumping noises from rear suspension, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting or loss of electrical functions
    * Solution: Check for electrical parasitic draw, replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, no headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs, check wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Lift Support Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate opens or closes slowly, sags when opened
    * Solution: Replace lift supports
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Roof Rack Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust or corrosion on roof rack bars or mounting points
    * Solution: Clean and protect with rust inhibitor, replace if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Lid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lid breaks or becomes loose, difficulty opening or closing
    * Solution: Replace center console lid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical components, replace AC compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Jeep Renegade has received positive feedback for its rugged capabilities and spacious interior, but some owners have encountered issues with certain components. The engine and transmission issues can lead to reduced performance and costly repairs, while the suspension issues may affect handling and ride quality. Electrical problems can cause battery discharge or lighting malfunctions, while interior issues such as a broken center console lid or climate control problems can disrupt comfort and convenience. The severity of these issues can vary, but some may warrant immediate attention and significant repair expenses.
