# 2022 Jeep Renegade Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Jeep Renegade brings distinctive styling and standard all-wheel drive to the subcompact SUV segment. While offering a nimble footprint and recognizable brand character, used-car buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain responsiveness, electrical glitches, and advanced safety sensor behavior.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 1.3-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces roughly 177 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate pep for daily commuting. Buyers should check closely for oil leaks or abnormal engine noises during a test drive.

**Rear Main Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks discovered during routine maintenance at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect the rear main seal area during a pre-purchase inspection and replace the seal if active leaking is confirmed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired exclusively with the standard all-wheel-drive system across all trims. Buyers should evaluate shifting smoothness and off-the-line responsiveness thoroughly during a test drive.

**Acceleration Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note a noticeable 2 to 4 second delay when attempting to accelerate from a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Check for available powertrain control module software updates at a qualified service center.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

The electric power steering system generally provides light, maneuverable handling, though sporadic tracking concerns have been reported.

**Intermittent Steering Jerk**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may intermittently jerk a few inches to the left or right while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a mechanic inspect the electronic power steering system, front suspension components, and wheel alignment.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Owner reports highlight intermittent electrical anomalies, ranging from erroneous seatbelt warnings to unexpected vehicle shutdowns.

**Random Warning Lights and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple dashboard warning lights illuminate unexpectedly, occasionally accompanied by engine stalling or unexpected parking brake engagement.
- **Solution:** Diagnose battery health, charging system output, and body control module fault codes.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features add convenience and security, but sensor sensitivity can lead to occasional fault messages.

**Inoperative Forward Collision Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Collision warning system lights illuminate or the system becomes inoperative without apparent obstruction.
- **Solution:** Inspect radar sensors and camera modules for calibration or wiring faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Renegade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V918000: Exterior Lighting:Back Up Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Renegade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The incorrect reverse lights may have reduced visibility, and not alert pedestrians and other drivers that the vehicle is backing up, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the bumper reverse light assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Jeep Renegade presents a fun, capable package for buyers seeking compact dimensions and standard all-wheel drive, but it requires careful vetting. Prospective owners should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the powertrain, electronics, and advanced driver assistance sensors to catch any recurring owner-reported glitches before finalizing a purchase.
