# 2022 Jeep Renegade Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Jeep Renegade, buyers will find a compact SUV that offers distinctive styling and standard all-wheel drive, though it is important to review known owner feedback regarding powertrain performance and electrical systems. Potential purchasers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle during a test drive to ensure smooth operation across all driving conditions.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged I4

The 1.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 177 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate pep for daily commutes. Buyers should keep a close eye on maintenance records and watch for any signs of fluid leaks or drivability concerns.

**Engine Stalling and Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving and mechanics discovering rear main oil leaks during routine service.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine seals and electrical connections for potential leaks or fault codes.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The nine-speed automatic transmission routes power to the standard four-wheel-drive system, helping balance highway efficiency with off-road readiness. Prospective owners should carefully test the transmission's engagement and shift quality from a standstill.

**Acceleration Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted a noticeable two-to-four-second delay when attempting to accelerate from a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Check for available manufacturer software updates or powertrain control module recalibrations at a certified dealership.

## Steering

### Steering System

The power-assisted steering system should deliver predictable feedback and tracking during routine test drives.

**Intermittent Steering Jerk**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally pull or jerk several inches to the left or right while driving at normal speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a professional inspect the steering linkage, wheel alignment, and electronic power steering software.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The vehicle's onboard electronics control everything from convenience features to vital safety systems, making a thorough electrical check essential before purchase.

**Random Warning Lights and Electrical Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience intermittent false dashboard warnings, malfunctioning seatbelt chimes, and unexpected activation of the electronic parking brake.
- **Solution:** Inspect the battery, charging system, and body control module connections for corrosion or software anomalies.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features enhance modern safety, but prospective buyers should verify that all warning sensors and collision avoidance systems are fully operational.

**Inoperative Collision Warning**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision warning system may display error messages or become inoperative without an apparent obstruction or cause.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas and have a dealer run diagnostics on the collision avoidance camera and radar modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Renegade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V918000: Exterior Lighting:Back Up Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Renegade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The incorrect reverse lights may have reduced visibility, and not alert pedestrians and other drivers that the vehicle is backing up, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the bumper reverse light assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Jeep Renegade presents a mixed reliability picture, highlighted by potential owner-reported concerns regarding engine stalling, acceleration lag, and electrical glitches. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify that all safety and powertrain software updates have been properly completed.
