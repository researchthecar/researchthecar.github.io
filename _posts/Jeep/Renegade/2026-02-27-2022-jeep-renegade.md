# 2022 Jeep Renegade

### Overview
The 2022 Jeep Renegade, a subcompact SUV, offers a blend of urban maneuverability and off-road capability. While generally considered a decent option in its segment, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear. We will examine the performance and potential problems associated with its powertrain options, suspension, electrical systems, and other key components to provide a comprehensive reliability picture for potential buyers and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged I4 (T4)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant level, overheating, visible coolant puddles under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the turbocharger assembly or related lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic (948TE)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough gear changes, hesitation during acceleration, noticeable jolts.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module, or internal transmission repairs if necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or seals, potential for slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, gaskets, or the transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes or restarts randomly.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery warning light illuminated.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw or replacement of the battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Exterior

### Body Panels & Seals
**Water Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Water ingress into the cabin, particularly around doors and sunroof.
- **Solution:** Adjustment or replacement of door seals, sunroof drains, or body seals.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Jeep Renegade presents a mixed reliability profile. The primary concerns revolve around the 1.3L Turbocharged engine, with reports of coolant leaks and turbocharger issues, and the 9-speed automatic transmission, which can exhibit harsh shifting and fluid leaks. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also noted. Exterior issues like water leaks are less common but have been reported. However, the suspension and interior trim generally appear to be reliable. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues and consider the cost of repairs when evaluating this model.
