The 2024 Jeep Renegade is a subcompact SUV known for its distinctive Jeep styling and off-road capability for its class. It comes standard with a 1.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 177 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission. While some appreciate its compact size and available 4x4 system, others find the Renegade's engine performance and fuel economy to be underwhelming. Common complaints include the small cargo area, and some concerns regarding reliability.

**Engine**

**1.3L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

*   **Engine Stalling**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported instances of the engine stalling, particularly at low speeds or when idling. This may or may not trigger a warning light.
    *   Solution: The stalling issue may be related to software calibration issues within the ECU. A reprogram or update of the ECU software will fix this issue.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring them to add oil more frequently than expected between scheduled oil changes. There may be no visible leaks.
    *   Solution: The issue may be related to wear and tear on the piston rings and/or cylinder walls. A solution would be to replace the piston rings and/or the short block of the engine.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $7,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported rough or harsh shifting, particularly between certain gears. There are reports of hesitation when accelerating or changing gears, and sometimes the transmission gets stuck in a gear.
    *   Solution: The issue may be related to software calibration issues within the transmission control module (TCM). A reprogram or update of the TCM software will fix this issue. Replacing the transmission may be required in extreme cases.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ for software update; $3,000 - $6,000+ for transmission replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Uconnect infotainment system may freeze, crash, or become unresponsive. This can affect navigation, audio, and other system features.
    *   Solution: A software update or replacement of the infotainment system may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+ for software update (if under warranty); $500 - $1500+ for replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Backup Camera Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported issues with the backup camera, including a distorted view, delayed image display, or the camera failing to activate altogether.
    * Solution: Replacing the backup camera.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Water Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report water leaks into the cabin, typically around the doors or sunroof (if equipped). This can lead to dampness and potential mold growth.
    *   Solution: Replacement of weather stripping and checking/resealing around the sunroof.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Creaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported rattling or creaking noises coming from the dashboard, door panels, or rear cargo area. This is more prevalent on bumpy roads.
    *   Solution: Tightening loose trim pieces or applying foam padding to reduce friction between parts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+

    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Poor Seat Comfort**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners find the seats uncomfortable, particularly on longer trips. This may be due to inadequate lumbar support or firm cushioning.
    *   Solution: Seat cushions can be added or upgraded by an aftermarket company. The seat frames are typically fine.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+

    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2024 Jeep Renegade has several common issues reported by owners. The most significant include engine stalling, excessive oil consumption, transmission issues, and electrical problems, specifically related to the infotainment system and backup camera. Interior rattles and water leaks can also be problematic. The cost of repairs can vary widely, from relatively inexpensive software updates to more substantial expenses for engine or transmission work. These issues can range from being mildly irritating to significantly impacting the vehicle's usability and reliability.
