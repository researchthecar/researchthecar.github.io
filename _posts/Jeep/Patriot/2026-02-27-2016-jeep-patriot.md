# 2016 Jeep Patriot

### Overview
The 2016 Jeep Patriot, while offering a budget-friendly entry into the SUV market, presents a mixed bag in terms of long-term reliability. It's known for its rugged, utilitarian design and capable off-road performance, especially in its Freedom Drive II configuration. However, this generation of the Patriot has seen its share of reported issues, particularly concerning its powertrain and some electrical components. Owners often praise its affordability and practicality for light-duty use, but potential buyers should be aware of common problems that may require attention over time. Regular maintenance is crucial to mitigate some of these concerns and ensure the vehicle's longevity.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L I4 DOHC 16V Dual VVT engine
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, gaskets, or oil pan.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Cooling System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, heater not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of radiator, thermostat, water pump, or hoses.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle hesitates during acceleration, jerky shifts, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and refill, transmission control module (TCM) update, or transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $3,500

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fluid smells burnt, vehicle loses power when hot.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid cooler flush or replacement, transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $700 - $3,000


### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission
**Shifting Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid service, solenoid replacement, or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Jeep Patriot is a budget-friendly option that can be a decent choice for those prioritizing affordability and basic utility. However, its reliability is a concern, particularly with the CVT transmission, which is prone to issues like hesitation and overheating. Engine oil leaks and cooling system problems are also noted. Electrical gremlins, especially related to the BCM and power windows, can add to repair costs. While the suspension and exterior are generally robust, potential buyers should be prepared for potential powertrain and electrical repairs. Thorough pre-purchase inspection and a history of diligent maintenance are highly recommended.
