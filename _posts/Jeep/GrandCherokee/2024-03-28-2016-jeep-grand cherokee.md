**2016 Jeep Grand Cherokee**

The 2016 Jeep Grand Cherokee is a mid-size SUV known for its ruggedness and off-road capabilities. It comes with various engine and transmission options, including a 3.6L Pentastar V6, a 5.7L Hemi V8, and an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Grand Cherokee has received praise for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and comfortable ride. However, it has also been reported to have several issues.

**Engine**

**3.6L Pentastar V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, rough idling, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.7L Hemi V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, misfires, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace fuel injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cam Phaser Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine ticking, rough idling, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace cam phaser(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration, shuddering sensation during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, poor handling, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Coil Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension collapse, uneven ride height
    * Solution: Replace coil springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for several hours
    * Solution: Identify and fix electrical component causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flicker, dim, or fail completely
    * Solution: Replace headlights or electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Taillight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water or moisture accumulates inside taillights
    * Solution: Replace taillights with updated versions
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Bluetooth Connectivity Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty connecting to Bluetooth devices, audio streaming issues
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate directions, system freezes or crashes
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Jeep Grand Cherokee has been reported to have various issues across different categories, though many of them are not particularly severe or expensive to fix. However, it is important to be aware of potential problems before purchasing or owning this vehicle.
