# 2024 Jeep Grand Cherokee Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Jeep Grand Cherokee offers a comfortable, tech-forward cabin and a range of powertrain choices, including traditional V6 power and plug-in hybrid options. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner reports regarding complex electrical glitches, hybrid system behaviors, and advanced safety feature anomalies. While many drivers enjoy its premium styling and capability, thorough pre-purchase inspections and verification of completed service updates are recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

A naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing standard motive power for the lineup.

### 2.0L Turbo 4-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid (4xe)

A turbocharged 2.0-liter plug-in hybrid powertrain generating a combined 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque, offering electric-only driving range alongside traditional fueling.

**Hybrid Powertrain Surge and Drivability**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may experience unexpected surging or hesitation when transitioning between electric mode and gasoline engine operation.
- **Solution:** Consult dealership for applicable powertrain software updates and system diagnostics.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission paired across various trims and drivetrains, designed to optimize fuel economy and smooth power delivery.

**Unintended Neutral Shift and Loss of Drive**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected shifts into neutral or momentary loss of motive power while operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have transmission control modules and software levels checked by a qualified technician.

## Suspension

### Suspension System

Supports ride comfort, handling stability, and road shock absorption.

**Component Wear and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice premature suspension component failure or persistent creaking noises from the front or rear ends during turns.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension bushings, joints, and struts for wear and replace faulty hardware.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Hydraulic and electronic braking components responsible for vehicle deceleration and stability control.

**Brake Bleeding Procedure Omissions**
- **Symptoms:** Improper servicing during standard maintenance can leave unpurged air in electronic brake booster or ABS modules.
- **Solution:** Ensure servicing facilities perform mandatory factory electronic scan bleeds when required.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Manages vehicle power distribution, starting systems, and accessory charging.

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle batteries may experience rapid discharge, resulting in a completely dead battery after a few days of non-use.
- **Solution:** Test battery health, check for parasitic draws, and perform software resets if necessary.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Relies on cameras and radar sensors to monitor traffic and provide automated emergency braking support.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Collision avoidance systems may activate unnecessarily during routine turns or intersections, causing abrupt slowing.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera and radar sensor alignment and check for sensor calibration updates.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2024 Jeep Grand Cherokee, buyers should pay close attention to the vehicle's electrical health, hybrid powertrain operation, and driver-assistance sensor behavior. Ensuring that all manufacturer software updates have been applied can prevent many reported electronic glitches. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified technician will help verify the condition of the powertrain, brakes, and suspension before finalizing your purchase.
