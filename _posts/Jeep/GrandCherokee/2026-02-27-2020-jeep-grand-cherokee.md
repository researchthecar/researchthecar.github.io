# 2020 Jeep Grand Cherokee Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Jeep Grand Cherokee, buyers will find a capable midsize SUV known for strong off-road performance and a comfortable cabin. However, looking closely at owner feedback reveals notable patterns of mechanical and electrical concerns that prospective owners should inspect carefully before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6

The standard 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces 295 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced mix of daily driveability and towing capability. While generally robust, some owners have reported top-end valve train wear and cooling concerns.

**Head Gasket and Engine Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine lights, rough idling, engine misfires, and potential coolant leaks.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection by a qualified mechanic; replacement of failing head gaskets, rocker arms, or lifters as required.

### 5.7L V8 HEMI

The optional 5.7-liter HEMI V8 delivers 360 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque, providing robust towing capacity and strong acceleration. It is a favored choice for buyers needing maximum utility, though fuel economy is lower.

### 6.4L V8 HEMI

Exclusive to the high-performance SRT trim, the 6.4-liter V8 pumps out 475 horsepower for rapid acceleration and athletic road handling. Maintenance and fuel costs reflect its performance orientation.

### 6.2L Supercharged V8

Powering the Trackhawk model, this supercharged V8 generates an immense 707 horsepower for supercar-rivaling performance. It is a rare, highly specialized option demanding careful pre-purchase inspection.

### Fuel

Delivers fuel reliably under normal conditions, though occasional pump failures can leave drivers stranded.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, sudden engine stalling while driving, or failure to crank after fueling.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump assembly and inspect the fuel system lines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission paired across most trims delivers smooth, responsive gear changes and optimizes fuel economy. Overall durability is generally good, though minor electronic shift control glitches can occur.

**Shifter or Park Defect**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning lights, transmission failing to register park properly, or unexpected shifting behavior.
- **Solution:** Inspect shifter linkage, update powertrain control modules, or service transmission components.

## Suspension

### Suspension and Ball Joints

Provides a comfortable ride quality both on-road and over rough terrain, though front-end components like ball joints can experience premature wear.

**Front Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, and loose handling feel.
- **Solution:** Replace worn front control arms and ball joint assemblies.

## Steering

### Power Steering

Steering is generally light and maneuverable for an SUV of its size, though intermittent electronic power assist failures have been documented by owners.

**Power Steering Loss and Pulling**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assist, illuminated warning icons on the dash, or the vehicle persistently pulling to one side.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering module, steering rack, and perform professional wheel alignments.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment

Cabin electronics provide modern connectivity features via the Uconnect system, though screen delamination remains a widely reported cosmetic and functional annoyance.

**Touchscreen Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Screen layers separating creating visual bubbles or cracks, unresponsive touch controls, and 'ghost touches' changing radio or climate settings independently.
- **Solution:** Replace the touchscreen infotainment display unit.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Equipped with standard modern passive safety features, though occupant restraint mechanisms require verification during a used vehicle inspection.

**Seat Belt Retractor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belts failing to retract smoothly, sticking, or displaying warning indicators on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty seat belt assembly retractors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V191000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lingering rearview image can distract the driver, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler has notified owners, and dealers will update the radio display software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V406000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel starvation may result in an unexpected loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP, and inspect and replace, if necessary, additional fuel system components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V411000: Engine And Engine Cooling](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the powertrain control module software to maintain vehicle propulsion, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Jeep Grand Cherokee offers impressive versatility, powerful engine options, and family-friendly comfort. However, buyers should test all electrical features, check the touchscreen for signs of delamination, and verify the health of the front suspension and steering components. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help ensure you find a reliable example.
