# 2022 Jeep Grand Cherokee Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Jeep Grand Cherokee offers buyers sophisticated styling, advanced technology, and multiple capable powertrain options. However, owner reports highlight a cluster of electronic and mechanical concerns, ranging from sporadic propulsion loss in hybrid variants to unexpected forward collision warnings and electrical system glitches. Prospective buyers should carefully review vehicle history records and ensure all pertinent factory software updates have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6

The standard 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine produces approximately 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing reliable everyday performance and towing capability for most drivers.

### 5.7L HEMI V8

The available 5.7-liter HEMI V8 engine generates roughly 357 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque, offering robust towing performance for buyers needing heavier utility capabilities.

### 2.0L Turbo Plug-In Hybrid (4xe)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged plug-in hybrid (4xe) powertrain delivers 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque with electric-only driving range, though early model years have generated owner complaints regarding hybrid propulsion and electrical software stability.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected loss of motive power or propulsion while driving, sometimes accompanied by hybrid system warnings or prompts to condition the battery.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic evaluation by a certified dealership, which may involve software updates or component inspections tied to known electrical and battery campaigns.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across various engine configurations to deliver smooth shifting and optimized fuel efficiency, though intermittent stalling or neutral shift events have been reported by some owners.

**Engine Stalling and Power Interruption**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may experience sudden engine stalling or unexpected drops into neutral while slowing down or maneuvering at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection of powertrain control modules and software reprogramming by a qualified service center.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment System

The interior technology suite controls vital cabin functions and entertainment, but software instability can lead to recurring electronic quirks.

**Infotainment and Instrument Cluster Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note screens freezing, Bluetooth disconnecting randomly, language settings changing spontaneously, and backup camera lines disappearing.
- **Solution:** Updating infotainment software firmware or performing system resets can resolve temporary display and connectivity bugs.

## Safety

### Forward Collision System

Advanced driver-assistance features provide added security on the road, though sensor sensitivity and software bugs can occasionally lead to false alerts or unintended system interventions.

**Unexpected Braking and Warning Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance system may trigger unexpected automatic braking events or illuminate warning lights intermittently.
- **Solution:** Have the sensor alignments, software calibrations, and related camera modules checked and updated by a dealership.

### Seats and Restraints

Cabin seating is generally designed for comfort and safety, though mechanical faults in adjustable lumbar supports and head restraints have required component repairs for some owners.

**Active Head Restraint and Lumbar Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Active head restraints may deploy unexpectedly, or power seat lumbar supports can over-inflate and deform the seatback, causing discomfort.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of damaged seat mechanisms or inflatable bladders by a service professional.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Jeep Grand Cherokee is a handsome and versatile SUV, but used car shoppers should perform due diligence. Pay close attention to hybrid battery system records, electrical software updates, and potential powertrain hesitation during a test drive. Verifying that all open recalls and technical service updates have been addressed will significantly improve the ownership experience.
