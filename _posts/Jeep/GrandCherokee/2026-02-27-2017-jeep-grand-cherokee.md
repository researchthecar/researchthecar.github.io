# 2017 Jeep Grand Cherokee Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Jeep Grand Cherokee brings robust capability and a comfortable interior to the midsize SUV segment, but used-car buyers should carefully review its reliability history. While many owners enjoy its versatile performance and powertrain offerings, common owner complaints center around engine mechanical issues, electrical glitches, and unexpected active headrest deployment. Taking the time to verify service records and test all features thoroughly can help you secure a reliable example.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

The standard 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces around 295 horsepower, offering a smooth blend of everyday drivability and efficiency. It is generally robust, though owners have reported occasional issues with stalling and head gasket leaks.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may unexpectedly stall while driving or experience rough idling and difficulty restarting.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the root cause—commonly linked to sensors, fuel delivery, or electrical faults—and replace faulty components.

**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine overheating, coolant loss, white smoke from the exhaust, or contaminated engine oil.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged head gasket and inspect the cylinder heads for warping.

### 5.7L V8 HEMI Engine

The optional 5.7-liter HEMI V8 pushes out 360 horsepower and strong towing capability, appealing to buyers needing extra muscle. Maintenance history is key, as fuel system and electrical concerns occasionally surface.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, engine sputtering, or sudden loss of power during operation.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump module or associated relay components.

### 3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel Engine

The eco-diesel V6 option provides high torque and strong highway fuel economy for long-distance drivers. It requires diligent maintenance to avoid costly emission system and turbo-related repairs.

### 6.4L V8 SRT Engine

The high-performance SRT V8 delivers 475 horsepower for sports-car-like acceleration in an SUV package. It demands premium fuel and diligent upkeep to keep its complex mechanical systems running properly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp shifts and optimizes both performance and fuel economy across trims. While generally durable, drivers should check for proper engagement and software updates during a test drive.

**Shifter or Park Defect**
- **Symptoms:** Electronic shifter fails to register park properly, or warning messages appear indicating a transmission fault.
- **Solution:** Update transmission control modules or replace faulty shifter assembly components as needed.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

electrical issues can affect various body systems, including exterior lighting and convenience features like power liftgates.

**Headlight Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights flicker, fail intermittently, or stop working entirely during night driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect bulbs, wiring harnesses, and lighting control modules for corrosion or failure.

## Interior

### Seat Headrests

Cabin materials are generally comfortable, but the active head restraint system has been a notable source of sudden, unexpected deployment complaints.

**Active Headrest Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Front seat headrests deploy forcefully without warning while driving on normal roads.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged active headrest assembly and check restraint control modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V699000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Combustion in the intake manifold can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** FCA US LLC will notify owners, and dealers will replace the EGR cooler, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V686000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Either scenario increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will confirm both tow hook bracket bolts are properly tightened, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17E061000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak may lengthen the distance needed to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** ATI will notify owners and will replace the supercharger brackets, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V406000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel starvation may result in an unexpected loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP, and inspect and replace, if necessary, additional fuel system components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V411000: Engine And Engine Cooling](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the powertrain control module software to maintain vehicle propulsion, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Jeep Grand Cherokee offers impressive versatility, strong engine choices, and a refined cabin, making it an attractive used SUV. However, buyers should perform a thorough inspection checking for potential engine electrical glitches, transmission shifter behavior, and headrest deployment history. Prioritizing models with documented maintenance records will significantly reduce the risk of unexpected repair bills.
