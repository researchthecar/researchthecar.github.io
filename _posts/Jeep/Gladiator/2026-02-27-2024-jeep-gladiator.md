# 2024 Jeep Gladiator Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Jeep Gladiator brings iconic truck styling and open-air capability to the midsize pickup market, backed by modern interior tech and robust off-road hardware. As a relatively new model year, owners have submitted various initial complaints centered around electrical glitches, steering concerns, and drivetrain components. Buyers should review service records carefully and ensure any open manufacturer safety campaigns have been properly addressed.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard powerplant for the Gladiator lineup. It offers proven mechanical reliability and steady power delivery for daily driving and towing alike.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission is paired with the V6 engine for a more engaging driving experience. Buyers should verify clutch operation and check for any prior drivetrain service history.

**Clutch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle enters limp mode and becomes undriveable, often occurring when engaging four-wheel drive at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Requires inspection and replacement of the clutch assembly by a qualified technician.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The available 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth gear changes and optimizes power delivery across diverse driving conditions. It is widely regarded as a refined and durable option for this platform.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components offer adequate stopping power for daily duties, though pedal feel anomalies should be checked prior to purchase.

**Stiff Brake Pedal**
- **Symptoms:** Upon starting the vehicle after sitting, the brake pedal can feel unusually hard and sit lower, requiring excessive initial pedal force.
- **Solution:** Have the brake booster and hydraulic system inspected by a professional technician.

## Steering

### Steering System

Steering components provide responsive handling on and off the road, though specific model campaigns require owner diligence.

**Heavy Steering Feel and Safety Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Steering effort may feel progressively heavier over time, with some owners noting parts availability delays for related campaigns.
- **Solution:** Consult a certified dealer to check for active steering recalls and replacement part availability.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment System

Modern electronics manage connectivity, safety features, and convenience systems throughout the cabin.

**Infotainment Freeze and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The central display screen may issue recurring warning pop-ups, rendering maps and the backup camera temporarily inaccessible, or lead to sudden electrical failure.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates at the dealership or replace faulty infotainment modules as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

- **Issue:** The instrument panel cluster may experience an internal short circuit and fail. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 101, "Controls and Displays." An instrument panel that fails to display critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, can increase the risk of a.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the instrument panel cluster, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V363000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Gladiator&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The electric hydraulic power steering pump wiring may overheat and cause a vehicle fire, even when the vehicle is parked with the ignition in the "Off" position. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the wiring and electric hydraulic power steering pump, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Jeep Gladiator remains a compelling choice for truck enthusiasts seeking open-air utility and genuine off-road capability. While mechanical powertrains are generally robust, used-car buyers should carefully inspect manual clutch operation, test all onboard electronics, and verify that all safety recalls have been fully resolved by a certified dealer.
