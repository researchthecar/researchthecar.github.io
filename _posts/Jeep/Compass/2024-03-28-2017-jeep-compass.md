**2017 Jeep Compass**

The 2017 Jeep Compass is a compact SUV offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. It features a 2.4-liter Tigershark I-4 engine as standard, producing 180 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 2.0-liter Tigershark I-4 engine and a 2.4-liter Tigershark MultiAir2 I-4 engine. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual transmission and a 9-speed automatic transmission.

The Jeep Compass has received positive reviews for its off-road capabilities, spacious interior, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, it has also been criticized for its lackluster performance, poor fuel economy, and uncomfortable seats.

**Engine**

**2.4-liter Tigershark I-4**

- **VVT Solenoid Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, reduced power
  - Solution: Replacement of VVT solenoid
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Tigershark I-4**

- **Timing Chain Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, engine failure
  - Solution: Replacement of timing chain and gears
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

- **Shift Solenoid Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, delayed shifting, inability to shift
  - Solution: Replacement of shift solenoid
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Strut Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Front-end clunking, premature tire wear, poor handling
  - Solution: Replacement of front struts
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for a few days
  - Solution: Diagnostics and repair/replacement of faulty electrical components
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rear Wiper Motor Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rear wiper not working intermittently or permanently
  - Solution: Replacement of rear wiper motor
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Sunroof Wind Noise**
  - Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise from the sunroof when it is closed
  - Solution: Replacement of sunroof seals or recalibration of sunroof mechanisms
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Loose Door Panel Clips**
  - Description/Symptoms: Door panels rattling or popping out of place
  - Solution: Replacement of door panel clips
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Jeep Compass has several common issues, with the engine and transmission being the most problematic areas. These issues can range from minor inconveniences to major repairs, costing anywhere from a few hundred to thousands of dollars. While the Compass offers desirable features such as off-road capabilities and a spacious interior, its reliability issues may be a concern for potential buyers.
