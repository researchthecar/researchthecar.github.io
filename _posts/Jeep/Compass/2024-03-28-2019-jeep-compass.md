**Introduction to the 2019 Jeep Compass**

The 2019 Jeep Compass is a compact SUV that offers a blend of rugged off-road capability and everyday practicality. It is available with a choice of two gasoline engines and two transmission options:

* **2.4L Tigershark I4** with 180 hp and 175 lb-ft of torque
* **2.0L Tigershark MultiAir II I4** with 260 hp and 295 lb-ft of torque
* **6-speed manual** transmission
* **9-speed automatic** transmission

Critics praise the Compass for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and numerous standard safety features. However, some have criticized its dated infotainment system and lackluster fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.4L Tigershark I4**

* **Intake Valve Deposits**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buildup of carbon deposits on the intake valves can cause reduced engine performance, rough idling, and knocking.
    * Solution: Valve cleaning
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause a rattling or ticking noise from the engine. In severe cases, it can lead to engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: N/A

**2.0L Tigershark MultiAir II I4**

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty fuel injector can cause the engine to run rough, misfire, or stall.
    * Solution: Replacement of fuel injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Hard Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into reverse or first gear.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of clutch or transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

**9-speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A worn-out strut mount bearing can cause a clunking or popping noise when turning or driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mount bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Rear Suspension Sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear of the vehicle may sag due to weak or damaged rear springs.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty alternator may cause the battery to not charge, leading to electrical problems or stalling.
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical issues causing the battery drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water may leak into the interior of the vehicle through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replacement or repair of sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not function properly, resulting in temperature fluctuations or malfunctioning fans.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Wind Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise can be heard while driving.
    * Solution: Adjustment of door seals or weatherstripping
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 Jeep Compass has received mixed reviews for its reliability. While it offers a comfortable ride and off-road capability, it has been plagued by several common issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Regular maintenance and addressing issues promptly can help minimize repair costs and extend the vehicle's lifespan.
