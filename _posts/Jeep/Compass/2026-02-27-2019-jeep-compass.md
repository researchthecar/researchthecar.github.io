# 2019 Jeep Compass Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Jeep Compass offers distinctive styling, a comfortable interior, and solid off-road capability for its class, making it an appealing compact SUV on the used market. However, prospective buyers should approach with care due to a significant cluster of owner complaints regarding engine stalling, electrical glitches, and powertrain hiccups. While many owners enjoy its everyday utility, checking service records and ensuring all software updates have been completed is strongly advised.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Tigershark MultiAir I4

The 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 180 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. Buyers should review service histories carefully, as this engine accounts for a notable volume of stalling and coolant-related complaints.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may unexpectedly stall or shut off completely while driving, often displaying warning messages such as 'Stop safely, vehicle will shut off soon' before losing motive power.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing is required; repairs often involve software updates, sensor replacements, or addressing underlying fuel and electrical system faults.

**Coolant Leaks and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected coolant leaks leading to high engine temperature warnings and potential overheating risks.
- **Solution:** Inspect coolant hoses, reservoirs, and the radiator for leaks or degradation, and replace damaged components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers a direct shifting experience, primarily paired with front-wheel-drive base trims. It is relatively uncommon and generally avoids major automated shifting complaints.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic is paired with specific front-wheel-drive configurations, delivering predictable gear changes for daily driving without widespread mechanical defect patterns.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is standard on all-wheel-drive models, designed to optimize highway fuel economy. Buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth shifting through all gears without hesitation or slipping.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may unexpectedly slip or shift into neutral while driving, accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes.
- **Solution:** Requires professional transmission diagnosis, which may involve software recalibration or valve body service.

## Brakes

### Brake and Parking Brake System

The braking system provides firm stopping power under normal conditions, though electronic integration issues can occasionally affect operation.

**Unexpected Brake Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners report that the auto start-stop feature or electrical glitches inappropriately engage the parking brake or cause unexpected braking behavior.
- **Solution:** Check battery health and have the electronic brake module and software inspected by a qualified technician.

## Electrical

### Uconnect Infotainment and Touchscreen

The interior electronics manage vital climate, audio, and safety features. Screen failures can be frustrating and temporarily disable the backup camera.

**Screen Freezing and Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The Uconnect infotainment display may freeze, turn black, display distorted lines, or cause the rearview backup camera to fail intermittently.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacing the touchscreen display unit typically resolves these glitches.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V208000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Reduced visibility increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will tighten the wiper nuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V523000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A reduction of braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the vehicle's brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Jeep Compass balances attractive styling and everyday practicality, but prospective buyers should proceed with a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to potential engine stalling history, electrical issues, and infotainment screen reliability. Verifying that all factory software updates have been applied can significantly improve the ownership experience.
