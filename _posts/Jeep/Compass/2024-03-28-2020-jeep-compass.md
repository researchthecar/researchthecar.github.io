**2020 Jeep Compass**

The 2020 Jeep Compass is a compact SUV that comes with two engine options: a 2.4L Tigershark MultiAir 2 engine that produces 180 horsepower and 175 lb.-ft. of torque, and a 1.3L Direct Injection Turbo engine that produces 177 horsepower and 200 lb.-ft. of torque. The 2.4L engine is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, while the 1.3L engine is paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission.

The Compass is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and off-road capabilities. However, it has also received some criticism for its lack of power and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.4L Tigershark MultiAir 2**

**Misfiring Engine**
Description/Symptoms: The engine misfires and hesitates when accelerating.
Solution: Replace the spark plugs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 20V550000

**1.3L Direct Injection Turbo**

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner fails, causing the timing chain to skip. This can lead to engine damage.
Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**TCM Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission control module (TCM) fails, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all.
Solution: Replace the TCM.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Front Struts**
Description/Symptoms: The front struts wear out prematurely, causing the vehicle to bounce and handle poorly.
Solution: Replace the front struts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is turned off.
Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and repair the electrical system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Infotainment System**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezes or crashes.

Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2020 Jeep Compass has some potential issues, including misfiring engines, timing chain tensioner failures, and faulty TCMs. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Overall, the Compass is a reliable vehicle, but it's important to be aware of the potential problems before making a purchase
