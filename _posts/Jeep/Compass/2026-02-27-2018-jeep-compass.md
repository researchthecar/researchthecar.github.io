# 2018 Jeep Compass Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Jeep Compass, prospective buyers will find a stylish compact SUV with a comfortable cabin and competent road manners. However, a review of owner complaints highlights several recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that warrant a careful pre-purchase inspection. Chief among these are reports involving engine stalling, intermittent electrical glitches, and suspension wear.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L Tigershark 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter Tigershark four-cylinder engine produces 180 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. While generally dependable mechanically, it is frequently associated in owner reports with electrical and stop-start system issues.

**Engine Stalling and Stop-Start System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall in traffic or fail to restart after engaging the automatic stop-start feature, often accompanied by dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis typically involves checking powertrain control software, inspecting wiring harnesses, or servicing the alternator and battery charging system.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The available 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with all-wheel-drive variants to deliver highway efficiency. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure smooth shifting across all gears.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission is found on select lower trim levels with front-wheel drive, offering a more direct engagement for drivers who prefer shifting gears themselves.

## Suspension

### Sway Bar Links and Control Arms

The suspension is tuned for a smooth ride, though certain components like sway bar links may experience premature wear resulting in noticeable chassis noise.

**Premature Suspension Clunking and Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear rattling, clunking, or shuddering noises when driving over bumps and rough road surfaces.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace worn sway bar links, bushings, or control arms as needed during routine maintenance.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Parking Brake

Braking components provide adequate stopping power under normal conditions, though electronic integration issues can occasionally trigger unintended parking brake engagement.

**Brake Vibration and Parking Brake Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience shaking or rattling when coming to a stop, or electronic parking brakes may inadvertently lock due to system errors.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake rotors and pads for warping, and check electronic module connections related to the automatic parking brake feature.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Display System

Cabin electronics control major climate, audio, and safety features, but owner reports indicate that software instability and display blackouts are relatively common.

**Touchscreen and Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display screen may go black, freeze, reboot continuously, or display a distorted backup camera image.
- **Solution:** Software updates, radio module resets, or full display unit replacement may be required to resolve persistent freezing.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V279000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the lower control arm separates, it can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the front lower control arms, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V740000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Controls and Displays." An instrument cluster that is too bright, and cannot be dimmed, can reduce the driver's ability to see at night, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update IPC software, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V524000: Electrical System:Wiring:Front Underhood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V523000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A reduction of braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the vehicle's brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V670000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the oil pump fails, the engine will stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the oil pump, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V732000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Compass&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating air bag deployment, unsecured inflator mounting nuts outside of the cushion assembly are potential projectiles that may strike the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger air bag module assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Jeep Compass offers attractive styling and practical everyday utility, but used car shoppers should approach with caution and verify vehicle history. Paying close attention to electrical system stability, engine performance during test drives, and suspension condition will help ensure a reliable purchase.
