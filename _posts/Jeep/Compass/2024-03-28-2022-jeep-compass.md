**Introduction:**

The 2022 Jeep Compass is a compact SUV available with three engine options: a 2.4L Tigershark inline-4 (180 hp, 175 lb-ft torque), a 1.3L turbocharged inline-4 (177 hp, 210 lb-ft torque), and a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 (237 hp, 258 lb-ft torque). Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, a 6-speed automatic, a 9-speed automatic, and a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission.

Owners appreciate the Compass's off-road capabilities, spacious interior, and modern infotainment system. However, they express concerns about reliability, transmission issues, and a lack of refinement.

**Engine**

**2.4L Tigershark Inline-4**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**1.3L Turbocharged Inline-4**

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: High oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
Solution: Piston ring replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
Recalls: P73

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noises
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
**Slipping Gears**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission hesitations
Solution: Transmission fluid flush, solenoid replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic**
**Shifting Delays**
Description/Symptoms: Slow or delayed gear shifts
Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid flush
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V605000

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
**Clutch Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during acceleration
Solution: Clutch replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**

**Interior**

**Faulty Infotainment System**
Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, audio issues
Solution: Software updates, hardware replacement

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Summary:**

The 2022 Jeep Compass has several notable issues, particularly with its 1.3L turbocharged engine and 6-speed automatic transmission. Engine issues can be severe and costly to repair, while transmission problems can cause significant drivability concerns. Electrical and interior issues are also present but are generally less severe and expensive to fix. Overall, the Compass's reliability concerns may overshadow its positive attributes for some potential buyers.
