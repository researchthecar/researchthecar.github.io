**Introduction**

The 2018 Jeep Compass is a compact SUV that offers a combination of ruggedness and urban styling. It comes standard with a 2.4L Tigershark I4 engine and a 6-speed automatic transmission. Other available powertrains include a 1.4L MultiAir Turbo I4 engine with a 6-speed manual or 6-speed dual-clutch transmission, and a 2.0L Tigershark I4 engine with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Popular features include its spacious interior, off-road capabilities, and advanced safety systems. However, owners have reported several issues with the vehicle, which are detailed below.

**Engine**

**2.4L Tigershark I4**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, loss of power, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Variable Valve Timing Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Cooler Filter Housing Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak, low oil pressure, engine damage
    * Solution: Replace filter housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.4L MultiAir Turbo I4**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or stutters when shifting gears
    * Solution: Reprogram transmission computer
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, or refusing to shift gears
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage gears properly
    * Solution: Replace solenoid pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clutch pedal feels soft or spongy, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shifter Linkage Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loose shifter
    * Solution: Replace linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **TCM Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Body Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, harsh shifts
    * Solution: Replace valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of damping, rough ride
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is off
    * Solution: Check for parasitic draw, repair as needed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flicker or fail to turn on
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows fail to operate
    * Solution: Replace switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Water Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water entering the cabin through the sunroof
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges flicker or fail to display information
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC, heat, or ventilation system fails to operate properly
    * Solution: Replace climate control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Release Lever Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lever fails to release rear seat backrest
    * Solution: Replace lever
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2018 Jeep Compass offers many desirable features, it has been plagued by several significant issues, particularly in the powertrain and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can result in major inconveniences and safety concerns for owners. It is important to consider these potential problems when weighing the pros and cons of this vehicle.
