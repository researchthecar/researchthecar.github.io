# 2023 Jeep Cherokee Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a modern used Jeep Cherokee requires a balanced look at its available powertrain configurations and reported owner experiences. While newer model years benefit from simplified lineup offerings, buyers should carefully review specific mechanical and electrical feedback to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 270 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. It provides a robust alternative for buyers prioritizing passing power and capability.

### 3.2L Pentastar V6

The 3.2-liter V6 engine generates about 271 horsepower and 239 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth power delivery across the rev range. It remains a popular choice for buyers seeking traditional naturally aspirated performance.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with standard all-wheel-drive configurations on later models, this 9-speed automatic transmission aims to balance highway efficiency with responsive gearing. Buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth shifting behavior.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake

Owner reports indicate rare instances of unexpected electronic parking brake activation during operation.

**Unintended Parking Brake Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may suddenly come to a complete stop while driving due to the parking brake engaging independently.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician to diagnose electronic parking brake sensor or software faults.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Isolated complaints highlight unexpected deployment of supplemental restraint systems.

**Erroneous Air Bag Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple air bags, including front passenger and driver units, may deploy unexpectedly during low-speed maneuvers like reversing.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or certified repair facility to inspect the restraint control module and sensor wiring.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V290000: Power Train](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Cherokee&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A failed PTU may result in a loss of drive power or vehicle rollaway, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Jeep Cherokee offers capable engine choices and versatile handling, but prospective buyers should remain vigilant during pre-purchase inspections. Checking for open recalls and verifying electronic system integrity will help ensure a reliable pre-owned purchase.
