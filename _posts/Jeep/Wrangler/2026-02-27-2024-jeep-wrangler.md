# 2024 Jeep Wrangler Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Jeep Wrangler continues its legendary off-road legacy with updated technology and styling. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating the powertrain options and overall build quality is essential to ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo I4

The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 270 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering a nimble and efficient drive. It provides a balanced blend of daily drivability and forced-induction power for trail capability.

### 3.6L V6

The traditional 3.6L Pentastar V6 engine delivers roughly 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, known for its smooth power delivery. It remains a popular, time-tested choice for buyers seeking naturally aspirated reliability.

### 6.4L V8 Rubicon 392

The robust 6.4L HEMI V8 engine churns out 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque for maximum performance. It is reserved for high-end trims, delivering exhilarating acceleration both on and off-road.

### 2.0L Plug-In Hybrid 4xe

The 4xe plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs a 2.0L turbocharged engine with electric motors to generate 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. It offers instantaneous low-end torque and electric-only driving range, though shoppers should carefully verify hybrid system health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, responsive shifting across various driving conditions and pairs well with all major engine options. It is widely praised for optimizing both highway fuel economy and low-range crawling.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission offers traditional engagement for driving enthusiasts choosing the V6 engine. Buyers should ensure proper clutch operation during test drives.
