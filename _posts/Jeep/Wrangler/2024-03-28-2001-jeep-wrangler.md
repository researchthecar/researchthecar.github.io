**2001 Jeep Wrangler**

The 2001 Jeep Wrangler is a rugged and versatile SUV known for its off-road capabilities. It comes with two engine and transmission options:

* **4.0L I-6 Engine**
    * Horsepower: 190 hp
    * Torque: 235 lb-ft
* **2.5L I-4 Engine**
    * Horsepower: 127 hp
    * Torque: 185 lb-ft
* **X5 5-speed Manual Transmission**
* **42RLE 4-speed Automatic Transmission**

Owners appreciate the Wrangler's ruggedness, off-road handling, and iconic design. However, it has also been known to have some common issues.

**Engine**

**4.0L I-6 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cylinder Head Crack**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Crack**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy exhaust, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L I-4 Engine**

* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or refusing to start
    * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Coolant Temperature Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate temperature gauge readings, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace coolant temperature sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**X5 5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, difficulty shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission input shaft seal, rear main seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Grinding Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, grinding noise when shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission synchronizers, slider gear
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**42RLE 4-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Lockup Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, shuddering, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Control Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, no shift, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaking Shock Absorbers**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of suspension damping, uneven wear on tires
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ball Joint Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Popping or clicking noise when turning, decreased steering control
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, dead battery, electrical system failure
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, clicking noise when attempting to start
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Paint Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faded or peeling paint on tailgate
    * Solution: Repaint tailgate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to operate power windows
    * Solution: Replace power window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: 05V435000
* **Interior Trim Panel Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or vibrating interior trim panels
    * Solution: Secure or replace trim panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary**

The 2001 Jeep Wrangler is a capable off-roader, but it does have some common issues that owners should be aware of. Engine and transmission problems are the most severe and expensive, while interior and electrical issues are generally minor and inexpensive to fix. Potential buyers should factor in the cost of repairs when considering a used Wrangler.
