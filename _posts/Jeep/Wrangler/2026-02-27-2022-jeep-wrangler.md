# 2022 Jeep Wrangler Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Jeep Wrangler offers legendary off-road capability and multiple iconic powertrain configurations, including a turbocharged four-cylinder, a robust V6, a plug-in hybrid (4xe), and a high-performance V8. However, used-car buyers should review vehicle history thoroughly, as owner reports to safety authorities highlight several recurring issues concerning sudden loss of motive power, electrical system glitches, and front-end handling instability commonly referred to as the death wobble.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

A turbocharged 4-cylinder engine producing around 270 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering lively acceleration and efficiency for daily driving and trail use.

### 3.6L V6

A naturally aspirated V6 putting out approximately 285 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing traditional Wrangler performance and available with a manual or automatic transmission.

### 2.0L Plug-in Hybrid (4xe)

A plug-in hybrid powertrain combining a turbocharged 2.0-liter engine with electric motors for a total output of 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque, though owners have reported high-voltage electrical and motive power concerns.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected vehicle hesitation, limited electric drive power, or complete shutdowns while driving at highway speeds or merging into traffic.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a dealership for relevant high-voltage battery recalls and software updates.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall or display warning lights related to fuel and electrical systems while in motion.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan required to check for fault codes in fuel delivery or powertrain control modules.

### 6.4L V8 (Rubicon 392)

A high-performance HEMI V8 delivering 470 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque for maximum acceleration and off-road power.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional six-speed manual gearbox paired exclusively with the 3.6L V6 engine for an engaged driving experience.

**Clutch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting, clutch pedal sticking, or premature clutch wear leading to roadside breakdowns.
- **Solution:** Inspect clutch components and replace worn mechanical parts or address associated safety recall campaigns.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

A smooth-shifting eight-speed automatic transmission available across most engine options, providing refined highway cruising and crawl ratios.

**Shift Engagement Delays**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission fails to immediately respond when shifting from park to reverse or drive, or hesitates upon acceleration.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid levels, update powertrain control modules, or inspect internal valve bodies.

## Steering

### Steering and Front Suspension

Solid front axle steering setup common to solid-axle 4x4 vehicles, which can exhibit steering play or severe vibrations if front-end components wear out or encounter road imperfections.

**Front-End Oscillation (Death Wobble)**
- **Symptoms:** Violent shaking or vibration of the steering wheel and front end after hitting road bumps at highway speeds, making the vehicle temporarily difficult to control.
- **Solution:** Inspect track bars, ball joints, steering dampers, and control arm bushings for wear or looseness.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Wiring

Electrical architecture managing vehicle displays, charging systems, and accessory controls, with some owner reports of intermittent wiring and cluster failures.

**Instrument Panel Blank Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard instrument cluster or infotainment screen goes completely blank while driving, removing access to critical driver information.
- **Solution:** Check battery connections, software firmware levels, and wiring harnesses for loose terminals.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior glass and visibility components designed for open-air driving, though the flat windshield angle makes it frequently susceptible to stone impacts.

**Windshield Cracks and Distortion**
- **Symptoms:** The upright windshield is highly prone to stone chips, sudden stress cracks, or optical distortions causing glare.
- **Solution:** Repair minor chips quickly or replace the windshield with quality aftermarket or OEM glass when cracked.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V326000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The side marker lights may not function due to an incompatible tail light assembly wiring harness. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Nonfunctional side marker lights may reduce vehicle visibility to other drivers, increasing.
- **What to do:** FCA will replace the tail light assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V768000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Excessive heat may cause the fuse to fail and result in a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the HV battery fuse, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V767000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP and inspect and replace additional fuel system components, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V865000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of communication may cause an engine shutdown. An engine shutdown can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the transmission control module software, Hybrid Control Processor and Auxiliary Hybrid Control Processor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V116000: Power Train:Clutch Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Overheated clutch components may increase the risk of a fire. Additionally, damage to other nearby components can result in debris falling onto the road or a loss of drive, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the clutch assembly and update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V191000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A stud on the frame assembly may puncture the fuel tank in a crash, which can result in a fuel leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, remove the frame stud and apply paint, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V263000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HPFP and replace additional fuel system components, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V303000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wrangler&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Excessive heat may cause the fuse to fail and result in a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the HV battery fuse, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Jeep Wrangler remains a top choice for off-road enthusiasts thanks to its unmatched capability and diverse powertrain lineup. However, prospective buyers should carefully inspect used models for potential issues involving front-end suspension wear, hybrid electrical system recalls, and transmission shift responsiveness. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verifying that all safety recalls have been completed can help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.
