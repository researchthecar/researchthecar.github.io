**Introduction:**

The 2022 Jeep Grand Cherokee L is a full-size SUV that offers both traditional gasoline engines and a plug-in hybrid option. The gasoline engine lineup includes a 3.6-liter V6 producing 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.7-liter V8 producing 357 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque. The plug-in hybrid model combines a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with an electric motor for a total output of 375 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic for the V6 and V8 engines and a 6-speed automatic for the plug-in hybrid model. The Grand Cherokee L received praise for its spacious interior, off-road capabilities, and impressive towing capacity. However, it also faced criticism for its high price and questionable reliability.

**Engine**

**3.6-liter V6**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Accumulation of carbon deposits on intake valves reduces engine efficiency, causing decreased fuel economy, power loss, and rough idling.
    * Solution: Intake valve cleaning, typically performed by a mechanic.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent oil top-ups.
    * Solution: Engine inspection to determine the source of the leak; potential repair or replacement of engine components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.7-liter V8**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**8-speed Automatic (V6/V8 Engines)**

* **Transmission Hesitation/Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or jerking during gear changes, particularly at low speeds.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid replacement, software updates, or transmission overhaul.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-speed Automatic (Plug-in Hybrid Model)**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sagging, causing uneven tire wear and poor handling.
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of worn suspension components, such as coils or shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical malfunctions, such as flickering headlights, inoperative dashboard displays, or difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical components, including wiring, fuses, and relays.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Exterior Trim Detachment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exterior trim pieces, such as side mirrors or roof rails, detaching from the vehicle.
    * Solution: Reinstallation of trim pieces using adhesive or bolts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Defects:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroofs leaking or malfunctioning, causing water damage or other issues.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of sunroof components, such as seals or motors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not functioning properly, leading to insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of climate control components, such as sensors or actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Jeep Grand Cherokee L has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While it offers impressive features and capabilities, it has also faced several issues, including engine valve deposits, oil consumption, transmission issues, suspension sagging, electrical malfunctions, exterior trim detachment, sunroof defects, and climate control issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be significant. Potential buyers should be aware of these concerns and carefully consider the vehicle's reliability before making a purchase decision.
