# 2022 Jeep Grand Cherokee L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Jeep Grand Cherokee L offers spacious three-row seating, upscale styling, and capable four-wheel-drive systems, making it a popular choice for families seeking adventure and utility. However, used car buyers should approach this model with caution due to a notable volume of owner complaints involving advanced driver-assistance systems, electrical glitches, and powertrain hiccups. While many owners enjoy the comfortable ride and refined interior, verifying vehicle history and ensuring all software updates have been completed is critical.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6

The standard 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate everyday performance and towing capability for most families.

**Engine Stalling and Knocking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a loud knocking noise coming from the engine bay at idle, alongside occasional rough running or stalling issues.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine mounts and replace them if worn or failed, as updated mounts are available to address knocking sounds.

### 5.7L V8

The available 5.7-liter HEMI V8 generates 357 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque, offering stronger towing capacity and effortless acceleration for heavier loads.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission generally delivers smooth and responsive gear changes across all trims.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lose propulsion or drop into neutral while driving, leading to sudden power loss on highways or in traffic.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning of the transmission control module and software updates may be required; consult a dealership for specific powertrain TSBs.

## Brakes

### Brake System

Stopping power is generally reliable, but warning light illuminations require prompt professional diagnosis.

**Brake Warning and Pedal Feel**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated brake warning lights on the dashboard and unusual brake pedal firmness or failure notifications.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and ABS wheel speed sensors for proper operation and fault codes.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

The steering system provides a tight turning circle and composed road feel, though intermittent noise and assist dropouts have been reported.

**Steering Assist Failure and Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note loud screeching sounds when turning the steering wheel left or right, accompanied by temporary losses of power steering assist.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering linkage, power steering components, and suspension joints for wear or binding.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Body Electronics

Electrical architecture handles extensive cabin technology and seat functions, but software stability and parasitic drains are recurring owner concerns.

**Screen Freezing and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen display may go completely blank, freeze while driving, or fail to show the backup camera view. Unexplained parasitic battery drain can also leave the vehicle dead overnight.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates for the Uconnect system or troubleshoot faulty electrical modules and circuit draws.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior panels and glass provide rugged styling, though susceptibility to stress cracks on factory windshields requires close inspection during purchase.

**Spontaneous Glass Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields develop stress cracks originating from the bottom seal without any impact from rocks or road debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the windshield, ensuring the lower glass seal and mounting frame are properly installed to relieve tension.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver-assistance features provide helpful active safety measures, though sensor calibration and software glitches can trigger erratic warnings.

**Unexpected Braking and Warning Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden unexpected emergency braking, spontaneous instrument cluster language changes, or simultaneous failure of multiple safety and collision warning lights.
- **Solution:** Check for pending software updates or recalibrate the forward-facing camera and radar sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V897000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee+L&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly secured connector for the front seat side air bags (SAB) may prevent the air bags from deploying. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V083000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee+L&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V051000: Suspension:Rear:Springs:Coil Springs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Grand+Cherokee+L&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Rear coil springs that detach from the vehicle while driving increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the rear coil spring assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Jeep Grand Cherokee L remains an appealing used three-row SUV thanks to its luxury-oriented cabin, strong engine choices, and excellent capability. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for electrical issues, infotainment glitches, and potential software-related safety system warnings. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verification of completed service bulletins are strongly recommended before buying.
