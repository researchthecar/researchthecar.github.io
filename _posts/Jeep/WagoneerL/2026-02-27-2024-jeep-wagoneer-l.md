# 2024 Jeep Wagoneer L Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Jeep Wagoneer L extends the brand's large SUV lineup with massive cargo capacity and upscale comfort. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating the available powertrain choices and monitoring owner-reported feedback is essential to making an informed purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Hurricane Twin-Turbo I6

This twin-turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 420 horsepower and 468 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration and towing capability. It serves as the standard powerplant for the Wagoneer L lineup.

### 3.0L Hurricane High-Output Twin-Turbo I6

Upgrading to the high-output version pushes output to roughly 510 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque for maximum performance. This option provides robust power delivery suitable for heavy hauling.

## Transmission

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with both engine configurations, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifting across rear-wheel and four-wheel-drive layouts.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V413000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Jeep&model=Wagoneer+L&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the transmission gear selection indicator not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 102, "Transmission shift lever sequence, starter interlock, and transmission braking effect." A missing gear selection indicator may lead to unintended vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the shifter module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Jeep Wagoneer L offers impressive space and potent powertrain options for buyers seeking a large family utility vehicle. Prospective owners should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's history and test all major electronic and mechanical systems before finalizing a purchase.
