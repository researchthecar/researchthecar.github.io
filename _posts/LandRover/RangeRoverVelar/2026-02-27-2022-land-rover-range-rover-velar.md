# 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar delivers striking modern design and luxury appointments, but used-car buyers should carefully review owner feedback regarding powertrain behavior. While NHTSA complaints are owner reports rather than definitive proof of widespread defects, prospective buyers should pay attention to recurring transmission quirks noted by drivers. Thoroughly inspecting the vehicle's service history and conducting a detailed test drive remain essential steps when shopping for this luxury SUV.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 247 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, offering balanced everyday performance for the Velar. Overall reliability is typical for modern luxury turbo engines, requiring diligent adherence to scheduled maintenance intervals.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV

Featuring mild-hybrid technology, this 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 generates around 335 to 395 horsepower depending on the tune, providing robust acceleration. Buyers should ensure complex electrical and auxiliary hybrid systems are fully inspected prior to purchase.

## Transmission

### ZF 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired standard with all-wheel drive across the Velar lineup, delivering smooth shifts under normal conditions. However, prospective buyers should verify proper transmission operation during a test drive due to owner reports of unexpected gear selection behavior.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the vehicle intermittently and unexpectedly shifts from Drive to Neutral while stopped or attempting to accelerate, leaving the car unresponsive until manually shifted into Park and back to Drive.
- **Solution:** Dealerships may apply software updates or reprogram transmission control modules to address specific shift behavior concerns.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V523000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Improperly restrained occupants have an increased risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V581000: Electrical System:Adas](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Missing audio safety features, such as seat belt reminders, turn signal sounds, and Advanced Driver Assistance System (ADAS) warnings, can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install the missing AAM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar is a compelling luxury utility vehicle that combines high-end styling with capable performance. Buyers should approach used models with attention to transmission operation and verify that any applicable powertrain software updates have been performed by a qualified service center.
