Alright, here's a breakdown of common issues reported for the 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar, organized as requested.

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar is a luxury compact SUV known for its sleek design, comfortable interior, and off-road capabilities. Available engine options include a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder (247 hp, 269 lb-ft of torque), a 3.0L turbocharged inline-six with mild hybrid assist (335 hp or 395 hp, 354 lb-ft or 405 lb-ft of torque respectively), and a 5.0L supercharged V8 (550 hp, 516 lb-ft of torque) (though the availability of the V8 may be limited depending on the model year and market). All engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the Velar for its stylish aesthetics and refined driving experience, but some criticisms include its complex infotainment system, reliability concerns, and relatively high price point for the features offered.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, requiring more frequent oil top-offs between scheduled services. Symptoms include low oil level warnings and the need to add oil every few thousand miles.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis by a qualified mechanic to determine the cause of the oil consumption (e.g., piston rings, valve seals, turbocharger). Repair options range from replacing valve seals to a complete engine rebuild.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six (MHEV)**

*   **Mild Hybrid System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Malfunctions within the mild hybrid system, potentially causing error messages, reduced performance, or starting difficulties.
    *   Solution: Diagnostic check and repair/replacement of faulty components within the MHEV system (e.g., battery pack, starter generator).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**5.0L Supercharged V8**

*   **Supercharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, unusual noises (whining, rattling) from the supercharger, and potential error codes.
    *   Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of the supercharger unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission (All Engine Options)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Jerky or abrupt gear changes, particularly during low-speed driving. Hesitation when accelerating or downshifting.
    *   Solution: Initial step is often a transmission software update. If the problem persists, valve body replacement or complete transmission replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 (Software Update) - $6,000+ (Transmission Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Malfunctions (If Equipped)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, failure to adjust ride height, error messages related to the air suspension system, compressor failure.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair/replacement of faulty components, such as air struts, air compressor, height sensors, or valve block.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Pivi Pro infotainment system may experience intermittent freezing, crashing, or unresponsiveness.
    *   Solution: Software updates are often released to address these issues. In some cases, a full system reset or hardware replacement may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $1,500+ (Hardware Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Sensor Issues (Parking Sensors, Blind Spot Monitoring)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: False alarms, intermittent failures, or complete malfunction of parking sensors or blind spot monitoring systems.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of the specific sensor(s) causing the problem, followed by replacement. Calibration of the system may also be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains quickly, especially when parked for extended periods, leading to starting problems.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis to identify the source of the parasitic drain (e.g., faulty module, improperly shut down system). Repair or replacement of the offending component.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Panoramic Roof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling, leaks, or failure to open/close properly.
    *   Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of seals, motors, or the entire panoramic roof assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Electrical Issues With Interior Lights**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Interior lights flickering, not turning on/off properly, or causing error messages.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair/replacement of faulty lighting components, such as LED modules, switches, or wiring harnesses.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Issues With Power Seats**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Seats not moving, only moving in one direction, or unusual noises from the motor.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair/replacement of motors, switches, wiring, or other components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar has a mix of potential issues ranging in severity and cost. Electrical and infotainment system glitches are relatively common and can be frustrating. Engine and transmission problems, while less frequent, can be more expensive to address. Air suspension issues, if equipped, also represent a potential source of significant repair costs. Overall, prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues and consider extended warranties or thorough pre-purchase inspections.
