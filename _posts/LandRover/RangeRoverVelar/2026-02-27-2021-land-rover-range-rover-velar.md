# 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Velar Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Velar, buyers will find a stylish luxury SUV with advanced technology and premium interior comforts. While most owners enjoy its refined ride and striking design, a small number of owner complaints highlight isolated issues ranging from electrical warnings to seat belt latch malfunctions. As you evaluate potential vehicles on the market, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a careful review of maintenance history are recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 247 horsepower, offering a balance of efficiency and daily drivability. Routine maintenance is crucial to ensure long-term reliability and performance.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV

This mild-hybrid inline-six engine delivers robust power around 335 or 395 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration and smooth highway performance. Buyers should ensure proper service history for the complex mild-hybrid electrical systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes across all powertrain options. It generally operates reliably, provided transmission fluid intervals are respected.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is strong and responsive, though routine friction-material wear is expected under normal driving conditions.

**Brake Pad Wear Warning and Pedal Response**
- **Symptoms:** Brake wear indicator messages illuminated while driving, accompanied by changes in brake pedal feel.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and fluid levels, replacing worn components as needed.

## Steering

### Steering and Stability Control

Power steering and handling components typically offer precise feedback, but electronic steering assist and stability systems warrant attention if unusual behavior occurs.

**Erratic Steering and Stability Control Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experienced unexpected erratic steering behavior at highway speeds or stability control intervention issues in wet conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components, wheel alignment, and stability control sensors for faults or software updates.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The vehicle relies heavily on complex onboard electronics and wiring networks that require professional diagnostic checks if warning signs appear.

**Intermittent Electrical Warnings and Smoke**
- **Symptoms:** An owner noted brief electrical warning alerts appearing before smoke and a fire occurred in the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Check the wiring harness, battery connections, and major electrical modules during a pre-purchase inspection for signs of wear or damage.

## Safety

### Seat Belt

Safety restraint systems generally perform as expected, though isolated latch failures have been documented.

**Seat Belt Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the seat belt buckle fails to catch or securely latch when inserted.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the seat belt receiver assembly and replace the latch mechanism if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V668000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Velar offers luxury appeal and strong performance options, but prospective buyers should remain vigilant regarding potential electrical, steering, and safety component complaints. Conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a specialist familiar with the brand will help uncover hidden issues and ensure a more confident purchasing decision.
