**Introduction:**

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its sleek design, capable off-road performance, and high-tech features. It is powered by a variety of engine options, including a 2.0L inline-4 turbocharged engine producing 247 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, a 3.0L V6 turbocharged engine producing 335 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.0L V8 supercharged engine producing 518 horsepower and 461 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic transmission and a 9-speed automatic transmission. Drivers appreciate the Velar's comfortable interior, advanced technology, and responsive handling, while some dislike its high price and limited rear seat space.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4 Turbocharged**

* No Issues Found

**3.0L V6 Turbocharged**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, strange noises from the engine, check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil spots under the vehicle, low oil levels.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the leaking components, such as gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.0L V8 Supercharged**

* **Supercharger Belt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing noises from the engine, loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace the supercharger belt.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or hesitating during gear changes.
    * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during acceleration, loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Delay:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission taking too long to shift gears.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle leaning to one side, bumpy ride.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the air suspension system components, such as air bags or compressors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery going dead prematurely.
    * Solution: Test and diagnose the electrical system to identify the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen not responding, freezing, or displaying errors.
    * Solution: Reset or update the infotainment system software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic Roof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the vehicle through the panoramic sunroof.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the panoramic roof.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Plastic Trim Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from plastic trim pieces in the interior.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the loose trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Creaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking noises coming from the dashboard.
    * Solution: Remove and reinstall the dashboard or apply sound-dampening material.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Velar has received mixed feedback from owners regarding its reliability. While some owners report no major issues, others have experienced problems with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific parts affected and the extent of the damage. It is recommended to research potential problems before purchasing and consider an extended warranty for peace of mind.
