# 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Velar Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a modern luxury SUV like the 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Velar brings striking design and advanced technology to the forefront. When evaluating a used model, prospective buyers should carefully review available powertrain configurations and monitor owner-reported feedback as real-world mileage accumulates. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains a vital step in ensuring long-term satisfaction with this luxury vehicle.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 247 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient daily performance. Buyers should ensure regular maintenance intervals have been strictly followed to support long-term mechanical reliability.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV

This 3.0-liter mild-hybrid turbocharged inline-six delivers robust power outputs ranging up to 395 horsepower, paired with smooth power delivery. It provides an energetic driving experience while incorporating modern electrified powertrain technology.

### Engine Control and Throttle System

Electrical and throttle management systems control core vehicle operations, though isolated owner reports indicate rare anomalies.

**Unexpected Engine Revving**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the engine unexpectedly surged or revved up while pulling into a parking space, leading to a low-speed collision.
- **Solution:** Have the throttle body, accelerator pedal sensor, and engine control module inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF eight-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear shifts and optimizes fuel efficiency across all driving conditions. It is standard across all trims and pairs smoothly with the standard all-wheel-drive system.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V450000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Filter/Screen](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the filter housing, oil filter, and o-rings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V678000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the NFSM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Velar&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.
