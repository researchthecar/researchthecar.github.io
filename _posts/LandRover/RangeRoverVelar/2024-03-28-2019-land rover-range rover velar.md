**Introduction:**

The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Velar is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its sleek design, sophisticated interior, and advanced technology. It is powered by a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0L Ingenium diesel engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission, a 2.0L Ingenium gasoline engine with an 8-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.0L V6 Supercharged gasoline engine with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners praise the Velar's comfortable ride, spacious cabin, and impressive off-road capabilities, but some have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.0L Ingenium Diesel**
No Issues Found

**2.0L Ingenium Gasoline**
- **Fuel Injector Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the engine, engine misfires, decreased fuel efficiency
  - Solution: Replace fuel injectors
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
  - Recalls: N/A

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rattling or whining noise from the engine, decreased engine performance, possible engine failure
  - Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500
  - Recalls: LR044962

**3.0L V6 Supercharged Gasoline**
- **Supercharger Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power under acceleration, reduced engine performance, whistling or whining noise from the engine
  - Solution: Replace supercharger
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000
  - Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
No Issues Found

**8-Speed Automatic**
- **Transmission Control Module Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed gear changes, loss of power
  - Solution: Replace transmission control module
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000
  - Recalls: LR047357

**Suspension**
- **Air Suspension Leak**
  - Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uneven ride height, dashboard warning light
  - Solution: Replace air suspension components (e.g., air bags, compressor)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000
  - Recalls: N/A

- **Rear Coil Spring Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing or shaking of the vehicle, uneven ride height
  - Solution: Replace rear coil springs
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
  - Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**
- **Battery Drain**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle after periods of inactivity, dashboard warning light
  - Solution: Replace battery, repair electrical system
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  - Recalls: N/A

- **Infotainment System Freeze**
  - Description/Symptoms: Infotainment display freezes or becomes unresponsive, loss of audio or navigation functions
  - Solution: Reset or update infotainment system, replace if necessary
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500
  - Recalls: LR045564

**Exterior**
- **Sunroof Leak**
  - Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
  - Solution: Replace sunroof seals, repair drainage system
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
  - Recalls: N/A

**Interior**
- **Climate Control Malfunction**
  - Description/Symptoms: Insufficient heating or cooling, fluctuating temperatures
  - Solution: Repair or replace climate control components (e.g., thermostat, blower motor)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500
  - Recalls: N/A

- **Panoramic Roof Shattering**
  - Description/Symptoms: Sudden shattering of the panoramic sunroof, potential injury to occupants
  - Solution: Replace panoramic sunroof glass
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000
  - Recalls: LR043833

**Summary:**

While the Land Rover Range Rover Velar offers a premium driving experience, it is not without its drawbacks. Several reported issues, including engine and transmission problems, suspension failures, and electrical glitches, can impact the vehicle's reliability and performance. Repair costs for these issues can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Therefore, potential buyers and owners should be aware of these issues and consider these factors when making a purchase decision.
