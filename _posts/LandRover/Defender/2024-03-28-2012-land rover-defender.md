**Introduction**

The 2012 Land Rover Defender is an iconic off-road SUV known for its ruggedness and go-anywhere capabilities. It is powered by a 2.4-liter diesel engine mated to a 6-speed manual transmission and produces 120 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque. Despite its legendary status, the Defender has been plagued by several notable issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.4-liter Diesel**

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, reduced power, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,500+
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whining or whistling sound
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **EGR Valve Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Emissions control problems, reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Pedal Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging or disengaging the clutch
    * Solution: Replace master or slave cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Gearshift Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Repair or replace gear selector mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
* **Input Shaft Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise or vibration during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace input shaft bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Suspension**

* **Coil Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or collapse of suspension, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replace coil springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* **Shock Absorber Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, excessive bouncing
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Rear Axle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise or vibration from rear axle, loss of power
    * Solution: Repair or replace rear axle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly after sitting for extended periods
    * Solution: Identify and fix parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Wiring Harness Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, intermittent problems
    * Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Surface corrosion on body panels
    * Solution: Treat or replace affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Lights Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various dashboard lights not working
    * Solution: Replace faulty bulbs or repair wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Seat Fabric Tearing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Damage to seat material
    * Solution: Repair or replace seat fabric
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+

**Summary**

The 2012 Land Rover Defender is a capable off-roader, but it comes with a long list of potential issues. Many of these problems are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension, and they can be costly to repair. Despite these issues, the Defender's ruggedness and off-road performance still attract buyers who are willing to tolerate its reliability shortcomings.
