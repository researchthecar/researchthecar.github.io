**Introduction:**

The 2016 Land Rover Defender is a robust and capable off-road SUV powered by a turbocharged 2.2L diesel engine producing 120 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. Owners appreciate its ruggedness, off-road capabilities, and timeless design, but express concerns about its limited power and fuel efficiency.

**Engine:**

**2.2L Turbocharged Diesel**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, turbo noises, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor starting, uneven idle, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace injector(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Transmission:**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, spongy clutch pedal
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Synchro Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding noises when shifting gears, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Replace synchro(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Suspension:**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Deterioration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Rear Axle Seal Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking axle fluid, noise from rear axle
    * Solution: Replace axle seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drain, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Find and repair source of power drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Body Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, engine control issues
    * Solution: Replace body control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Exterior:**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Repair or replace sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior:**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from vents
    * Solution: Replace blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
* **Cruise Control Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control not working
    * Solution: Replace cruise control switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Summary:**

The 2016 Land Rover Defender is a rugged and capable SUV with a host of off-road features. However, it has its share of issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. While not all issues are severe or expensive to fix, some can be costly and require professional repair. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
