# 2022 Land Rover Discovery

### Overview
The 2022 Land Rover Discovery, a premium full-size SUV, offers a blend of luxury, capability, and advanced technology. It is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanicals, benefiting from Land Rover's extensive engineering. However, like many complex luxury vehicles, it can experience specific issues, often related to its sophisticated electronic systems and optional advanced features. Owners should be aware of potential concerns in areas such as infotainment and certain engine variants, though widespread critical failures are not a dominant theme. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of this capable SUV.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one or more corners, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation errors, connectivity issues (Bluetooth, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto).
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or in rare cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500 (depending on software update vs. hardware replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, vehicle won't start, warning lights related to battery voltage.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or removal.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Land Rover Discovery presents a generally strong reliability profile, with its core powertrain and transmission systems demonstrating robustness. The primary areas of concern tend to be the sophisticated air suspension, which can develop leaks or compressor issues, and the complex electrical system, particularly the infotainment unit which may suffer from software glitches. Battery drain issues, while not endemic, can occur and require careful diagnosis. Exterior and interior components are typically well-built and do not present widespread problems. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are recommended to mitigate potential repair costs and ensure a positive ownership experience.
