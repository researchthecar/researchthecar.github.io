# 2021 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Land Rover Discovery delivers impressive luxury, versatile seating, and robust capability, but prospective buyers should approach with careful consideration of its complex electrical systems. While owner reports to safety regulators are relatively limited, severe electrical and hybrid-system faults can compromise drivability. Reviewing maintenance histories and verifying that all manufacturer campaigns have been completed is essential before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 (P300)

The P300 turbocharged 4-cylinder engine produces approximately 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering adequate pep for everyday driving. Regular maintenance and timely fluid changes are critical to ensure long-term dependability.

### 3.0L Turbocharged I6 Mild Hybrid (P360 MHEV)

The P360 mild-hybrid inline-six generates around 355 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, blending smooth power delivery with forced induction. Buyers should inspect the 48-volt mild-hybrid architecture carefully for any historical charging or battery warnings.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with standard all-wheel drive, providing quick, seamless shifts and refined highway cruising. Overall mechanical durability is solid when fluid intervals are properly maintained.

## Steering

### Steering and Traction Modules

Steering and traction control hardware are generally robust, though initial software module configurations require proper dealer setup.

**Incomplete Initial Module Configuration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may receive premature error messages on the instrument cluster indicating reduced traction or limited drive modes shortly after vehicle delivery.
- **Solution:** A dealership or specialist must reprogram and update the vehicle control modules to complete initial factory configurations.

## Electrical

### 12V and 48V Charging System

The sophisticated electrical and mild-hybrid systems provide modern luxury features but can occasionally trigger complex charging faults or unexpected shutdowns.

**MHEV DC-DC Converter and Battery Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden dashboard warnings such as 'Electrical system fault – Stop safely' followed by complete loss of motive power and vehicle shutdown while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the mild-hybrid system, DC-DC converter, and 12V battery health, and ensure all relevant manufacturer campaigns have been performed.

**Intermittent Starting and No-Start Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally fail to crank or start using the push-button ignition despite electrical accessories appearing fully powered.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning is required to isolate software glitches or failing battery modules in the vehicle's complex electrical architecture.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V457000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle that shuts down or loses exterior lighting while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ground stud, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V668000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V389000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The driver’s air bag clockspring connector may corrode, which can cause the air bag not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective lubricant gel to the driver’s air bag connector terminals, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V458000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Water may not be able to drain away from the rearview camera due to insufficient drain holes, which may result in damage to the rearview camera. A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will drill additional drain holes in the underside of the tailgate trim. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Land Rover Discovery offers high-end comfort, premium utility, and advanced powertrain choices, but its intricate electrical architecture demands caution from used-car shoppers. Prioritize vehicles with thorough service records, and confirm that all electrical and hybrid system updates have been successfully applied by a certified facility before finalizing your purchase.
