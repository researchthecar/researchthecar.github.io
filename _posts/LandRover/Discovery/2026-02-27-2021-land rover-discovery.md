# 2021 Land Rover Discovery

### Overview
The 2021 Land Rover Discovery, a premium full-size SUV, offers a blend of luxury, capability, and advanced technology. It is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanicals, benefiting from Land Rover's extensive engineering. However, like many complex luxury vehicles, it can be susceptible to certain electronic glitches and specific component failures that, while not universal, warrant attention. Owners should be aware of potential issues related to infotainment systems and some sensor malfunctions. Regular maintenance is crucial to ensure the longevity and optimal performance of this capable SUV, especially given its off-road prowess and sophisticated systems.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light illuminated, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (Pivi Pro)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors, occasional reboots.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or in rare cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500 (for software updates/resets), $1500+ (for head unit replacement)


### Sensors (Parking, Blind Spot)
**Intermittent Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, sensors not detecting objects, system warnings related to sensor obstruction or failure.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sensors, recalibration, or replacement of faulty sensor modules.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 per sensor

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Land Rover Discovery presents a generally strong reliability profile, with its core powertrain and transmission systems demonstrating robustness. The primary areas of concern tend to be within the sophisticated electronic systems, particularly the infotainment and various sensor arrays, which can experience software-related glitches or intermittent malfunctions. The air suspension system, while highly capable, is also a potential source of repair costs due to leaks or compressor issues. Owners should budget for potential electronic repairs and routine maintenance to ensure the long-term dependability of this luxurious and capable SUV.
