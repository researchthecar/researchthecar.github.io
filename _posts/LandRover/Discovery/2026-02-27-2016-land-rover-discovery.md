# 2016 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Land Rover Discovery, buyers should carefully evaluate both powertrain choices and common owner-reported vulnerabilities. While luxury features and capability are hallmarks of the brand, reviewing reported engine and transmission behavior helps ensure a more dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine provides efficient power delivery for daily driving. Buyers should monitor cooling system integrity closely as some owners have noted overheating concerns.

**Cooling System and Engine Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature water pump failure leading to coolant leaks, subsequent engine overheating, and eventual loss of cylinder compression.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system thoroughly and replace any leaking water pumps or compromised hoses immediately to prevent severe engine damage.

**Unexpected Engine Overheat and Electrical Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced sudden loud thumps from the engine compartment at idle, immediately followed by electrical system and engine overheat warnings.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle safely towed to a qualified mechanic to diagnose potential cooling, electrical, or mechanical failures.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine delivers strong horsepower and robust torque for enhanced towing and performance. Maintenance history is crucial for maintaining long-term reliability on this option.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with standard all-wheel drive configurations to optimize highway efficiency. Prospective buyers should test drive the vehicle across various speeds to ensure smooth, responsive shifting.

**Gear Shifting Hesitation and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted the vehicle feeling stuck in a higher gear between 30 and 50 mph, resulting in intermittent jerks while driving.
- **Solution:** Check for available manufacturer software updates or consult a transmission specialist to evaluate shift logic and fluid condition.

**Gear Selector Failure and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden warning lights accompanied by a non-operational gear dial selector while the vehicle is in operation.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic shifter module and transmission control unit inspected by a certified technician for fault codes.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Critical steering components require thorough professional inspection to ensure complete vehicle control and occupant safety.

**Steering Wheel Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported the steering wheel completely detaching from the steering column, resulting in a total loss of steering control while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column assembly and steering wheel retention hardware for any looseness or structural defects during a pre-purchase inspection.

The 2016 Land Rover Discovery offers distinct luxury and capability, but used-car buyers must proceed with caution.
