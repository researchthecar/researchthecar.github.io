# 2016 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Land Rover Discovery, prospective buyers should carefully weigh its upscale features and capability against reported reliability concerns. Owner feedback highlights several recurring problem areas across steering, brakes, and electrical systems that require close inspection prior to purchase. Maintaining a comprehensive service history is essential to managing ownership costs for this luxury SUV.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This turbocharged 4-cylinder engine provides efficient power delivery for daily driving, producing around 240 horsepower. Buyers should monitor cooling system integrity and look for professional inspections due to potential overheating risks.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth highway cruising performance when operating properly, though occasional shifting hesitation and electrical gear selector faults have been documented by owners.

**Gearbox Fault and Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may coast unexpectedly from a stop accompanied by a gearbox fault warning message on the dash.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and fluid inspected by a specialist, which may require software updates or component rebuilds.

## Brakes

### Brake Lines

Brake system components require proactive inspection for leaks and line corrosion to prevent dangerous loss of hydraulic pressure.

**Brake Line Rupture**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal feels spongy, low brake fluid pressure alarms trigger, and brake fluid sprays from a ruptured metal line leading to a loss of stopping power.
- **Solution:** Immediately replace damaged brake lines with upgraded or OEM components and thoroughly bleed the braking system.

## Steering

### Power Steering Assist Motor

Electronic power steering systems provide light and responsive maneuvering, but mounting hardware failure can lead to sudden loss of assist.

**Steering Motor Bolt Shear**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assist while driving, often accompanied by a metal crunching sound when turning the wheel.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering motor mounting bolts for shearing and replace the power steering assembly if structural failure has occurred.

## Exterior

### Door Latches

Interior convenience features and door mechanisms should be thoroughly tested during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure proper latching and locking.

**Door Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors fail to latch securely or may unexpectedly swing open while the vehicle is in motion without dash warnings.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty door latch assemblies and door lock actuators.

The Land Rover Discovery offers compelling luxury and capability, but used buyers should proceed with diligence. Prioritizing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on brake lines, steering components, and transmission shifting behavior can help avoid costly surprises and ensure a safer ownership experience.
