# 2022 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Land Rover Discovery offers a blend of luxury, advanced technology, and genuine off-road capability. However, used car buyers should approach with caution, as owner reports highlight significant electrical and powertrain glitches that can severely impact drivability.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P300)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate entry-level motivation for the Discovery, though buyers should ensure proper maintenance records.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV (P360)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six mild-hybrid engine generates roughly 355 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and robust performance. It represents a more common powertrain choice for this model year.

**Unexpected Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shut down while driving at higher speeds without prior warning messages.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle thoroughly diagnosed by a certified specialist to check for electrical faults, software glitches, or fuel delivery issues.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and smooth shifts across all driving conditions. It is generally robust, though integrated electronic controls can occasionally trigger related diagnostic alerts.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering functionality is electronically governed, meaning steering faults are often tied back to broader vehicle electrical anomalies.

**Sudden Loss of Power Steering**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of power steering assistance coinciding with dashboard blackouts and critical safety warnings while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect electrical harnesses, steering control modules, and software calibration to resolve intermittent power drops.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & DC/DC Converter

The Discovery relies heavily on complex digital architecture, which has been a primary source of owner frustrations and unexpected system failures.

**Cascading Electrical Faults and DC/DC Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of power steering, brake control, pitch-black dashboards, and cascading warning lights due to electrical or converter failures.
- **Solution:** Software updates and hardware replacements of the DC/DC converter or related modules by an authorized service center may be required.

## Exterior

### Windshield

Exterior glass integrity is generally solid, though isolated reports suggest susceptibility to unexplained stress fractures.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Cracks developing along the bottom of the driver side windshield and spreading across the glass without any apparent impact or stress origin.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield through a professional glass service, ensuring proper recalibration of any attached safety cameras.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V523000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Improperly restrained occupants have an increased risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V044000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine cam carrier and related components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V389000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The driver’s air bag clockspring connector may corrode, which can cause the air bag not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective lubricant gel to the driver’s air bag connector terminals, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V458000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Water may not be able to drain away from the rearview camera due to insufficient drain holes, which may result in damage to the rearview camera. A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will drill additional drain holes in the underside of the tailgate trim. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Land Rover Discovery delivers supreme comfort and luxury features, but prospective used buyers must carefully evaluate its reliability history. Due to prominent owner complaints regarding unexpected electrical shutdowns and cascading system failures, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician is strongly advised.
