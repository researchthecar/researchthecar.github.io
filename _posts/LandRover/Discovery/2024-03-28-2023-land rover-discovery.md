**Introduction:**

The 2023 Land Rover Discovery is a versatile SUV known for its off-road capabilities and luxurious interior. It is available with two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder and a 3.0L supercharged V6. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

The Discovery is praised for its impressive towing capacity, spacious cabin, and comfortable ride. However, it also has a few drawbacks, including a high price tag, limited fuel efficiency, and potential reliability concerns.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption has been reported by some owners. Symptoms include low oil levels and blue smoke from the exhaust. The issue can be resolved by replacing the piston rings and valve guides. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+ **Recalls:** N/A
* **Turbocharger Failure:** Premature failure of the turbocharger has been reported, leading to a loss of power. The solution is to replace the turbocharger and associated parts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+ **Recalls:** N/A

**3.0L Supercharged V6**

* **Supercharger Whine:** A whining noise coming from the supercharger has been experienced by some drivers. The issue is caused by a faulty supercharger clutch and can be resolved by replacing the clutch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+ **Recalls:** N/A
* **Water Pump Failure:** A faulty water pump can lead to coolant leaks and overheating. The solution is to replace the water pump and related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+ **Recalls:** N/A

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:** Hard or delayed shifting has been reported, which can be caused by faulty transmission solenoids or a failing torque converter. The solution involves replacing the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+ **Recalls:** N/A
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:** A leak from the transmission fluid pan has been observed in some vehicles. The issue can be resolved by replacing the pan gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ **Recalls:** N/A

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** A parasitic battery drain has been reported, which can lead to a dead battery. The issue can be caused by a faulty audio system or other electrical components. The solution is to identify and repair the source of the drain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+ **Recalls:** N/A
* **Infotainment System Problems:** Occasional glitches and malfunctions with the infotainment system have been experienced. The issue can be resolved by updating the software or replacing the system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ **Recalls:** N/A

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Issues:** Improperly secured roof racks have been reported to detach, which can pose a safety hazard. The solution is to tighten the bolts and ensure the rack is properly mounted. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ **Recalls:** N/A
* **Fog Light Condensation:** Moisture accumulation inside the fog lights has been observed, which can impair visibility. The issue can be resolved by replacing the fog lights with sealed units. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ **Recalls:** N/A

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Noise:** Wind noise and creaking sounds coming from the panoramic sunroof have been reported. The issue can be addressed by adjusting or lubricating the sunroof mechanism. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ **Recalls:** N/A
* **Seat Adjustment Problems:** Difficulties adjusting the power seats have been experienced, caused by faulty switches or motor failures. The solution is to replace the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ **Recalls:** N/A

**Summary**

The 2023 Land Rover Discovery is a capable and luxurious SUV, but it has experienced several issues that have affected engine, transmission, electrical, exterior, and interior components. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost of repairs can be substantial. Potential buyers should be aware of these concerns before making a purchase decision.
