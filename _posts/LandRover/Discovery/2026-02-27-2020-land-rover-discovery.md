# 2020 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Land Rover Discovery offers compelling luxury, versatile three-row seating, and robust capability. However, used car buyers should approach with caution due to recurring owner-reported complaints concerning complex electrical issues, water leaks around the cabin, and occasional powertrain anomalies. Thoroughly inspecting vehicle history and electronics functionality is strongly advised prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing approximately 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate entry-level motivation for the Discovery, though owners should monitor routine maintenance closely.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

A 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine rated at around 335 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque. This option delivers brisk acceleration and strong towing capacity, though historical complexity requires diligent upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

A smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission paired with standard four-wheel drive across all trims. It generally operates reliably, contributing to both highway efficiency and off-road capability when maintained properly.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Starting System

Monitors auxiliary and primary engine electronics that coordinate starting, charging, and onboard computer systems.

**Charging System and Starting Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected battery warning lights, charging system error messages, or random failures to start accompanied by blank instrument or infotainment screens.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic test the battery, alternator, and primary ground studs to ensure proper electrical continuity and charging output.

### Cabin Electrical and Windshield Sealing

Manages cabin lighting, infotainment displays, and weatherstripping integrity to keep interior electronics dry and functional.

**Water Intrusion and Electrical Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks through the top of the windshield or roof rails during rainstorms, dripping onto the center console or rearview mirror and triggering erratic electrical behavior, flashing lights, and drained batteries.
- **Solution:** Inspect windshield seals and roof trim for proper adhesion, and check the cabin wiring harnesses for moisture damage or corrosion.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Seals

Encompasses exterior glass installation, moldings, and weather protection barriers designed to seal passenger compartments from outside elements.

**Windshield Seal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior windshield seals deteriorate or fail prematurely, allowing water to infiltrate the upper cabin and pillar areas during wet weather.
- **Solution:** Have a glass specialist inspect the windshield bonding and replace degraded seals or moldings as outlined in relevant technical service communications.

## Safety

### Doors and Latches

Covers vehicle access points, latch security mechanisms, and automated safety sensors intended to keep occupants secure.

**Unexpected Door Unlatching**
- **Symptoms:** Rear doors or tailgates unlatch or open spontaneously while driving or parked, creating a safety hazard for passengers and cargo.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latches, electronic lock actuators, and software modules for proper engagement, replacing faulty latch assemblies if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V457000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle that shuts down or loses exterior lighting while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ground stud, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V668000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V053000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the seats may not properly secure the occupants, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the second row seat frame, tightening the bolts properly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V117000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may be unaware that the high beams are illuminated, reducing visibility for oncoming traffic, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V694000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** If the vehicle shuts down or loses exterior lighting while driving there could be an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ground stud, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V393000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Rear passenger doors that open while driving can increase the risk of a crash. An instrument cluster warning light that fails to alert the driver about unsecured doors can increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will disable the UniLatch Keyless Vehicle (KV) entry function, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Land Rover Discovery is a capable, premium SUV that demands careful evaluation before purchasing used. Buyers should prioritize checking for signs of water intrusion around the windshield and verifying that all electrical accessories and door latches operate seamlessly. Procuring a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a specialist familiar with the brand will help mitigate unexpected repair expenses.
