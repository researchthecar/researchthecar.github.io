# 2023 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Land Rover Discovery, buyers will find a capable luxury SUV offering strong engine choices and refined road manners. While generally well-equipped, used car shoppers should review owner-reported complaints carefully, particularly regarding electrical system components and potential parts delays.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers a balanced entry-level option for daily driving, though buyers should verify routine maintenance history.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Mild-Hybrid Inline-6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine features mild-hybrid technology to generate around 355 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain provides brisk acceleration and smooth power delivery suited for highway cruising.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF eight-speed automatic transmission is paired with standard all-wheel drive across the lineup. It delivers crisp, smooth shifts and generally maintains solid long-term mechanical reliability.

## Suspension

### Factory Tires

Suspension components generally hold up well, though factory-equipped tires may wear out faster than expected.

**Premature Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Factory tires may experience excessive tread wear down to the cords in under 15,000 miles due to vehicle weight.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire tread depth and replace with properly load-rated alternative tires if excessive wear is detected.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Locking Mechanism

Advanced safety and steering systems operate reliably for the most part, though isolated electronic lock complaints have been noted.

**Unexpected Steering Wheel Lock**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may lock unexpectedly after the vehicle has been parked for an extended period.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the steering column lock mechanism and software modules.

## Electrical

### DC/DC Converter

Owner reports indicate potential vulnerabilities in the electrical charging system, specifically concerning the DC/DC converter.

**DC/DC Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may completely shut down and lose power while driving, causing it to roll to a stop with potential long delays for replacement parts.
- **Solution:** Have the dealership inspect and replace the faulty DC/DC converter unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V523000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Improperly restrained occupants have an increased risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V044000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine cam carrier and related components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V389000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The driver’s air bag clockspring connector may corrode, which can cause the air bag not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective lubricant gel to the driver’s air bag connector terminals, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V458000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Water may not be able to drain away from the rearview camera due to insufficient drain holes, which may result in damage to the rearview camera. A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will drill additional drain holes in the underside of the tailgate trim. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Land Rover Discovery provides upscale comfort and capable performance, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to electrical charging system functionality and verify the condition of tires and steering controls before finalizing a purchase.
