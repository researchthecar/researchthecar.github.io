# 2019 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Land Rover Discovery offers exceptional capability, luxury, and versatility, but prospective buyers should approach with a careful eye toward historical reliability. Known for complex electronic architecture and luxurious features, this SUV can occasionally present frustrating ownership hurdles. Reviewing owner feedback and mechanical patterns helps ensure you find a well-maintained example that avoids chronic headaches.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 (Si4)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 296 horsepower, offering efficient power delivery for daily commuting. While generally adequate for the chassis, buyers should thoroughly inspect the forced-induction components and maintenance history.

**Motive Power Loss and Shaking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly loses motive power while driving accompanied by odd noises, engine knocking, and severe shaking.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation of the fuel delivery, ignition, or turbo systems by a qualified technician.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 (Si6)

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine puts out around 340 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration and robust towing capability. Regular cooling system and supercharger maintenance are essential to keep this powerplant running smoothly.

### 3.0L Turbo Diesel V6 (Td6)

The 3.0-liter turbo diesel V6 delivers robust low-end torque and impressive highway fuel economy, making it ideal for towing. Proper emissions system maintenance is critical for long-term reliability on diesel variants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission across all trims and drivetrains, offering smooth and rapid gear changes. Routine fluid changes help maintain long-term shifting refinement.

**Shifter and Park Engagement Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic gear shifter fails to release from a selected gear like reverse or park, requiring an engine restart to reset the system.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module scanned for diagnostic trouble codes and perform software updates if available.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The Discovery relies heavily on complex computerized modules that can occasionally trigger erratic electrical glitches and power drains.

**Battery and Door Access Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start or unlock electronically, occasionally causing total loss of function on the driver's side systems despite a charged battery.
- **Solution:** Inspect body control modules, key fob transceivers, and door wiring harnesses for corrosion or software glitches.

## Exterior

### Latches and Door Mechanisms

Cabin hardware and latches generally hold up well, though mechanical latch components can occasionally wear out prematurely.

**Door Latch Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Rear or side doors fail to latch or stay closed properly while driving, posing safety concerns for passengers.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty door latch assemblies and verify proper alignment of the striker plates.

### Windshield and Seals

Exterior weather sealing around glass panels requires periodic inspection to prevent moisture intrusion into sensitive cabin electronics.

**Windshield Water Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks through the top or sides of the windshield during heavy rain, dripping onto the dashboard and center console electronics.
- **Solution:** Inspect and reseal or replace the windshield perimeter adhesive and upper weatherstripping.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V040000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure can increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover has notified owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft pulley center retaining bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V117000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may be unaware that the high beams are illuminated, reducing visibility for oncoming traffic, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V694000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the vehicle shuts down or loses exterior lighting while driving there could be an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ground stud, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V393000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Rear passenger doors that open while driving can increase the risk of a crash. An instrument cluster warning light that fails to alert the driver about unsecured doors can increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will disable the UniLatch Keyless Vehicle (KV) entry function, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Land Rover Discovery remains a luxurious and highly capable choice for adventurous drivers, but prospective buyers should conduct a rigorous pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to electrical operation, transmission shifting behavior, and windshield seal integrity. Securing a comprehensive service history will greatly enhance your peace of mind.
