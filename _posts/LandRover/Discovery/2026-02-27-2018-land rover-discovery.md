# 2018 Land Rover Discovery

### Overview
The 2018 Land Rover Discovery, a capable and luxurious SUV, generally presents a solid reliability record, though like many complex vehicles, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners. Its robust construction and advanced features contribute to its appeal, but understanding potential weak points is crucial for prospective and current owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems, their symptoms, typical solutions, and estimated repair costs across its various powertrain configurations. The aim is to offer a clear picture of the Discovery's reliability for informed decision-making.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Ingenium Diesel (Td4/Sd4)
**EGR Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the EGR cooler unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection and potential rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**DPF (Diesel Particulate Filter) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration or DPF cleaning/replacement.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, 'Suspension Fault' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air lines.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not raising, constant compressor noise, 'Suspension Fault' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Touchscreen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen unresponsive, system reboots, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500 (depending on solution)


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies overnight, difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Land Rover Discovery offers a compelling blend of luxury and off-road capability. While generally reliable, potential owners should be aware of specific issues, particularly concerning the air suspension system (leaks, compressor failure) and certain diesel engine components (EGR cooler, DPF). Electrical gremlins, especially with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are also noted. The petrol V6 and the automatic transmission have proven to be quite robust. Addressing these potential issues proactively through diligent maintenance and prompt repairs can ensure a more positive ownership experience with this capable SUV.
