**Introduction**

The 1996 Land Rover Discovery is a mid-size SUV known for its rugged off-road capabilities and comfortable interior. It is powered by a 4.0L V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

The Discovery has been praised for its spacious interior, powerful V8 engine, and excellent off-road performance. However, it has also been criticized for its poor fuel economy, unreliable electrical system, and lack of modern amenities.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Oil Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil pressure, engine knocking
    * Solution: Replace oil pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission not engaging gears properly, sluggish acceleration
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Overheating**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, burning smell
    * Solution: Fix fluid leaks, flush and replace transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace suspension bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Axle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud whining noise, difficulty turning
    * Solution: Replace rear axle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for a period of time
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic draw, replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Wiring Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, shorts
    * Solution: Inspect and repair wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, underbody, and suspension components
    * Solution: Remove rust and apply protective coatings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heater not working properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Cracks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard surface
    * Solution: Cover cracks with dash mat or replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1996 Land Rover Discovery has some common issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the cost of parts and labor. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicles regularly serviced and inspected to avoid costly repairs.
