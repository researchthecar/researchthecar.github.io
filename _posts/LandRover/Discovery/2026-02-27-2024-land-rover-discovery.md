# 2024 Land Rover Discovery Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Land Rover Discovery, buyers should weigh its modern capability against reported owner experiences regarding electrical stability and structural sealing. While luxury SUVs of this caliber offer advanced powertrains and refined comfort, reviewing potential owner-reported trouble spots can help prospective buyers make a well-informed decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It provides a balanced blend of efficiency and daily drivability for the entry-level luxury tier.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Mild Hybrid

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 mild hybrid engine generates around 355 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust acceleration and smooth power delivery, though proper electronic maintenance remains crucial.

**Sudden Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected electrical faults causing the vehicle to completely shut down while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation by a certified dealership to inspect electrical harnesses and powertrain control modules.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with standard all-wheel drive across all trim levels to deliver seamless gear shifts. It is generally robust, though total vehicle reliability can occasionally be impacted by upstream electrical issues.

## Electrical

### Wiring and Electronics

Owner reports indicate that cabin water leaks can compromise sensitive onboard electronics, requiring thorough inspection.

**Water Intrusion and Electrical Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaking through the windshield can infiltrate electrical compartments, leading to severe component damage and inoperability.
- **Solution:** Inspect windshield seals for proper bonding and replace any water-damaged electrical modules or wiring harnesses.
- **Cost:** $10,000

## Exterior

### Windshield and Bonding

Body sealing and glass bonding integrity are critical areas to verify during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Windshield Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Front windshield bonding may delaminate, leading to water egress and compromised structural integrity.
- **Solution:** Have the windshield professionally removed, inspected, and properly re-bonded with factory-approved adhesives.

## Interior

### Seating and Cabin Components

Interior electrical accessories and seat mechanisms require careful evaluation to ensure cabin safety.

**Seat Wiring Thermal Event**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have observed melted plastic odors, smoke, and localized heat or flames originating beneath the driver seat shortly after vehicle purchase.
- **Solution:** Inspect power seat wiring harnesses, fuses, and heating elements for short circuits or chafing.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V450000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Filter/Screen](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the filter housing, oil filter, and o-rings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V389000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver’s air bag clockspring connector may corrode, which can cause the air bag not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective lubricant gel to the driver’s air bag connector terminals, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V458000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Water may not be able to drain away from the rearview camera due to insufficient drain holes, which may result in damage to the rearview camera. A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will drill additional drain holes in the underside of the tailgate trim. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Land Rover Discovery offers strong engine options and impressive luxury features, but used-car buyers should pay close attention to potential electrical and structural sealing concerns. Ensuring that windshield bonding has been inspected and that no prior water intrusion has affected onboard electronics will help secure a more reliable ownership experience.
