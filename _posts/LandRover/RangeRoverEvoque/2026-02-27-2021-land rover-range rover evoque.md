# 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque

### Overview
The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque, a compact luxury SUV, generally presents a solid reliability profile, though like many vehicles in its class, it's not entirely immune to specific technical concerns. Owners often praise its distinctive styling, refined interior, and capable all-wheel-drive system. However, potential buyers should be aware of certain areas that have seen more frequent reports of issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of the Evoque's reliability across its key systems for the 2021 model year.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Ingenium Turbocharged I4 (P250)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the turbocharger unit or related plumbing.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


### 2.0L Ingenium Turbocharged I4 MHEV (P300)
**Mild Hybrid System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights related to the MHEV system, reduced performance, issues with battery charging.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan and potential software updates or replacement of MHEV components (e.g., starter-generator, battery).
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000


## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic (9HP45/9HP75)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough shifting, delayed engagement, noticeable jerks during acceleration or deceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module, fluid flush and replacement, or internal transmission repairs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,500

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes, glitches in display.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset of the system, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,800


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Discharge**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery warning lights, electrical components not functioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, checking for faulty modules, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque offers a compelling blend of luxury and capability. While generally reliable, it's advisable to be aware of potential issues such as coolant leaks and turbocharger problems in the gasoline engines, and mild hybrid system glitches in the MHEV variants. Transmission can occasionally exhibit shifting anomalies. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also noted. The suspension, exterior, and interior components appear to be robust with no widespread reported defects. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are recommended to ensure long-term dependability.
