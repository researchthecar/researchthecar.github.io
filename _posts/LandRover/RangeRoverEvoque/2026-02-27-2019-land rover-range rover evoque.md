# 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque

### Overview
The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque, while offering a premium experience and capable off-road performance, presents a mixed bag in terms of long-term reliability. Owners have reported a number of issues, particularly concerning the powertrain and certain electronic systems. While the vehicle's design and features are appealing, potential buyers should be aware of common problems that may arise, impacting both performance and ownership costs. Regular maintenance is crucial, but some issues appear to be more systemic than typical wear and tear. This analysis focuses on documented, recurring problems beyond standard servicing.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Ingenium Turbocharged I4 (Petrol)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, engine overheating, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or potentially the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


### 2.0L Ingenium Turbocharged Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, rough idling, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Poor engine performance, increased emissions, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission
**Hesitation and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough gear changes, delayed acceleration, shuddering during shifts.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid under the vehicle, warning lights on the dashboard, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets, or transmission overhaul.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**System Freezing/Crashing**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, system reboots unexpectedly, loss of audio or navigation.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of battery, or faulty module.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Range Rover Evoque exhibits some common reliability concerns, particularly within its powertrain and electrical systems. Engine issues like coolant leaks and turbocharger problems, along with transmission hesitation and potential leaks, are frequently reported. The infotainment system can also be a source of frustration due to freezing or crashing. While the suspension, exterior, and interior generally hold up well, these other areas require attention. Prospective buyers should factor in potential repair costs for these known issues, especially if purchasing a used model. Regular and thorough maintenance is essential to mitigate some of these problems.
