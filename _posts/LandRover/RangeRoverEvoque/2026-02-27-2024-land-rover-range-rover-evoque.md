# 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque offers luxury styling and refined capability, but prospective buyers should review available vehicle history carefully. While early-ownership reliability reports are generally limited, owner communications highlight notable parts availability delays regarding safety campaigns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive acceleration for daily driving. It pairs standard turbo performance with modern direct-injection technology, requiring consistent maintenance to ensure long-term dependability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts and helps optimize highway fuel efficiency across all-wheel-drive trims. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure crisp shifting behavior under various driving conditions.

## Safety

### Airbags

Owner reports indicate frustration with prolonged wait times for safety recall component supplies.

**Recall Parts Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Owners receive safety recall notifications for airbag campaigns but experience extended delays where replacement parts and remedies are unavailable at local dealerships.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized dealership to check the status of open recall parts availability and request interim safety updates.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V678000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the NFSM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V454000: Air Bags:Passenger Side Frontal:Cushion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may tear during deployment. A torn air bag may not adequately protect an occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque remains a stylish luxury compact SUV choice, though buyers should verify that all open safety campaigns have been successfully resolved. Checking vehicle identification numbers for pending manufacturer recalls before purchase is strongly recommended.
