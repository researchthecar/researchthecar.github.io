# 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque, prospective buyers will find a stylish luxury compact SUV equipped with modern amenities and turbocharged performance. While owner-reported complaints are sparse for this model year, early-stage ownership reports highlight minor administrative and dealer communication challenges regarding safety recalls, alongside isolated mechanical concerns. As with any luxury vehicle, reviewing service histories and ensuring all recall updates have been completed is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 P250

The P250 features a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing approximately 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth daily acceleration and adequate power for the luxury compact SUV, though routine maintenance is essential to ensure long-term longevity.

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 P300

The P300 variant upgrades the 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine to deliver around 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Paired exclusively with all-wheel drive, it offers brisk performance for buyers seeking a sportier driving dynamic.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission comes standard across all trims, routing power efficiently to the standard all-wheel-drive system. It generally delivers smooth gear shifts, though buyers should test drive to ensure responsive operation.

## Safety

### Passenger Air Bag

Safety equipment generally performs as expected, though active safety recalls require careful verification before purchase.

**Air Bag Tear Risk**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have received safety recall notifications regarding a potential defect where the passenger air bag may tear upon deployment.
- **Solution:** Dealers are instructed to perform recall repairs and replace affected parts free of charge once components become available.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V016000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the touchscreen software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V454000: Air Bags:Passenger Side Frontal:Cushion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may tear during deployment. A torn air bag may not adequately protect an occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V645000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque remains a compelling choice for luxury compact SUV buyers due to its distinctive styling and refined powertrain options. Based on available owner feedback, mechanical complaints are minimal, though prospective buyers must verify that official safety recall repairs—particularly concerning air bag components—have been properly addressed by an authorized dealer.
