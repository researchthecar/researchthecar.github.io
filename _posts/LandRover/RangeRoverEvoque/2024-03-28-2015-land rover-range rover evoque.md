**Introduction:**

The 2015 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque is a compact luxury SUV known for its stylish design, impressive off-road capabilities, and comfortable interior. It offers two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder and a 3.0L supercharged V6. Transmission options include a six-speed manual, a nine-speed automatic, and a six-speed automatic.

Owners have generally praised the Evoque for its unique styling, premium interior, and versatility. Common criticisms include limited rear-seat space, a firm ride, and high fuel consumption.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, reduced power.
    * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves and injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust, whining noise.
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Supercharged V6**

* **Oil Leaks from Valve Cover Gaskets:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks around valve covers, visible as smoke near the engine.
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise from the engine, loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and guides.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: LR07972

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Nine-Speed Automatic**

* **Premature Gearbox Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips, difficulty shifting, grinding noises.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Premature Shock Absorber Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive body roll, poor ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per corner
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Front Suspension Knuckle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or grinding noise from the front suspension, uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace suspension knuckle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains overnight, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic drains, replace battery if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow response time, freezing, crashing.
    * Solution: Update software, reset infotainment system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks through the sunroof, causing interior water damage.
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seals, drain tubes.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Handle Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles break off, making it difficult to open doors.
    * Solution: Replace door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per handle
    * Recalls: LR07918

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the dashboard area.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard brackets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat Belt Sensor Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt warning light remains on even when seat belt is fastened.
    * Solution: Replace seat belt sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per sensor
    * Recalls: LR07991

**Summary**

The 2015 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque has a range of potential issues that can vary in severity and cost. Some of the more serious problems can be expensive to repair, such as transmission failure or timing chain issues. However, many of the reported issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable price.
