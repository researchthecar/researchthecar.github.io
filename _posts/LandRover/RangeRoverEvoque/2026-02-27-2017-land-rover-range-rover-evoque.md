# 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque blends striking exterior styling with upscale interior appointments and capable performance. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating its powertrain choices and remaining mindful of potential electrical or body accessory quirks is essential for a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving, though buyers should verify thorough service records given its complex turbo and fueling systems.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly through the standard all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid inspections are recommended to ensure longevity and crisp gear engagement.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V941000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a front passenger air bag inflator initiator that may fail to ignite during a crash, preventing the air bag from deploying. In the event of a crash of sufficient severity, the front passenger air bag may not deploy as required, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V889000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the fasteners were to fail causing the knuckle to separate, vehicle handling may be affected, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the hardware that connects the front lower control arm to the knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque remains a stylish and capable compact luxury SUV choice on the used market. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive maintenance histories and thoroughly inspect all electronics and mechanical systems prior to purchase.
