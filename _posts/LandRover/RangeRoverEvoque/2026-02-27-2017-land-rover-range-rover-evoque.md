# 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury compact SUV, buyers should weigh the distinctive styling and nimble dynamics of this vehicle against a history of complex owner complaints. While many examples provide a comfortable ride and premium cabin appointments, prospective buyers need to be mindful of recurring issues spanning the powertrain, electrical systems, and suspension components. Careful pre-purchase inspections and a thorough vehicle history check are strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine delivers brisk acceleration and competitive fuel efficiency for a luxury compact SUV, but it requires diligent maintenance. Buyers should closely monitor service records for oil change intervals and any history of timing or stalling issues.

**Electrical Warning Lights and Timing Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report various warning lights illuminating on the dashboard, occasionally linked to timing chain or sensor performance.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic scan for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect the timing chain components and electrical harnesses.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall while driving, posing severe safety risks in traffic.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel delivery system, throttle body, and powertrain control modules for faults.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth highway cruising and optimizes fuel economy across a wide gear range. Proper fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term shifting refinement and reliability.

### Electronics and Shifter Assembly

Electrical architecture supports numerous advanced cabin and convenience features, but intermittent glitches in modules and switchgear can occasionally arise.

**Rotary Shifter and Stalling Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic rotary gear selector may fail to rise from the console, leaving the driver stranded, or the vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalls.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic shifter module, battery health, and associated wiring harnesses for corrosion or software failures.

## Steering

### Steering Knuckles and Suspension

The suspension system offers a balanced blend of ride comfort and handling composure, though front steering components require careful verification against known service recalls.

**Steering Knuckle Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Cracks may appear on the front steering knuckles, potentially leading to compromised handling or component failure.
- **Solution:** Check for active manufacturer campaigns and have a certified technician inspect the front suspension and steering knuckles for structural fatigue.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment includes a comprehensive suite of airbags and active restraints designed to protect vehicle occupants.

**Passenger Seat Belt Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants may experience issues with front passenger seat belt locking mechanisms or related warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt retractors, buckles, and sensors for proper mechanical engagement and electrical continuity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V941000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a front passenger air bag inflator initiator that may fail to ignite during a crash, preventing the air bag from deploying. In the event of a crash of sufficient severity, the front passenger air bag may not deploy as required, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V889000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the fasteners were to fail causing the knuckle to separate, vehicle handling may be affected, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the hardware that connects the front lower control arm to the knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Purchasing a used model requires careful attention to historical maintenance and potential mechanical or electrical quirks. While the vehicle offers attractive styling and a premium driving experience, prospective owners should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a specialist to verify the condition of the engine, transmission, and suspension components before finalizing a deal.
