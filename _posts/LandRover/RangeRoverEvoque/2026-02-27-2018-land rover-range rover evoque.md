# 2018 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque

### Overview
The 2018 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque, while offering a premium experience and distinctive styling, presents a mixed reliability profile. Owners generally appreciate its on-road comfort and off-road capability for its class. However, like many luxury compact SUVs, it can be susceptible to certain technical issues, particularly concerning its powertrain and electrical systems. Maintenance costs can also be higher than average, reflecting its luxury branding and complex engineering. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Ingenium Turbocharged I4 (Petrol)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or potentially the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased fuel consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger unit and associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


### 2.0L Ingenium Turbocharged Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, illuminated DPF warning light, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration cycle, cleaning, or replacement of the DPF.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Poor engine performance, black smoke from exhaust, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) valve.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission (Z F)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable clunks or jolts when shifting gears, hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,800

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid under the vehicle, burning smell, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, gaskets, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Suspension

### Standard Suspension
**Worn Bushings and Ball Joints**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings and ball joints.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


### Adaptive Dynamics (Optional)
**Electronic Damper Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride quality, error messages related to suspension, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty electronic dampers.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Touchscreen Glitches / Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, not responding to touch inputs, random reboots.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies frequently, car struggles to start, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


### Sensor Failures
**Parking Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, sensors not detecting obstacles, error messages.
- **Solution:** Cleaning, recalibration, or replacement of faulty parking sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Range Rover Evoque is a stylish and capable compact luxury SUV. While its exterior and interior generally hold up well, potential owners should be aware of possible issues with the powertrain, particularly concerning coolant leaks and turbocharger problems in petrol engines, and DPF/EGR issues in diesel variants. The 9-speed automatic transmission can sometimes exhibit harsh shifting. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also noted. Proactive maintenance and addressing issues promptly are key to maintaining the Evoque's luxury experience and avoiding more significant repair bills.
