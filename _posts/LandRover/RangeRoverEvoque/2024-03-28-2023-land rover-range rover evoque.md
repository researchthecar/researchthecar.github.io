**2023 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque**

The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque is a compact luxury SUV known for its sleek design, premium interior, and off-road capabilities. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four with 246 hp and 269 lb-ft of torque
* 2.0L Mild Hybrid Inline-Four with 296 hp and 315 lb-ft of torque
* 3.0L Supercharged Inline-Six with 395 hp and 406 lb-ft of torque
* 8-speed Automatic Transmission

The Evoque has been praised for its stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Accumulation of carbon deposits on intake valves, causing rough idle, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Intake manifold cleaning or valve replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.0L Supercharged Inline-Six**
    * **Oil Leak from Oil Pressure Relief Valve**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the oil pressure relief valve, causing oil loss and potential damage to the engine.
        * Solution: Replacement of the oil pressure relief valve.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Delay**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delayed gear shifts, especially when accelerating or decelerating.
        * Solution: Software update or transmission repair.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Rear Brake Light Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the rear brake lights to illuminate, increasing the risk of rear-end collisions.
    * Solution: Replacement of the brake light assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: Recalls 23V-216 and 23V-401
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of faulty components or software updates.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Accumulation of moisture inside the headlight housings, impairing visibility and potentially damaging the headlights.
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty headlights or resealing of the housings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: Recall 23V-458

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the panoramic sunroof, especially when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of the sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, resulting in ineffective heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the HVAC system, potentially involving replacement of faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque has received positive reviews for its design and capabilities. However, some owners have experienced issues with components such as the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior features. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and consider them when researching and selecting a vehicle.
