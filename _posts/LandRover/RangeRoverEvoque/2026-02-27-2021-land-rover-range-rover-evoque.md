# 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque offers luxury buyers a stylish, compact footprint with upscale interior appointments and standard all-wheel drive. While it carries the traditional prestige of the brand, prospective buyers should approach the used market with careful inspection, balancing its modern design and impressive feature list against historical brand reliability tendencies.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P250)

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate daily power and refinement, though ongoing maintenance history is key to long-term ownership satisfaction.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Mild Hybrid (P300)

This higher-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder mild-hybrid powertrain generates around 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and enhanced efficiency, though the added electrical complexity requires careful verification during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

Paired across the lineup with standard all-wheel drive, this 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth highway cruising and responsive gearing. Buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure crisp, hesitation-free shifting across all gears.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V454000: Air Bags:Passenger Side Frontal:Cushion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may tear during deployment. A torn air bag may not adequately protect an occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque remains a striking and luxurious option for used vehicle shoppers prioritizing style and tech. Because formal owner complaint data is sparse for this model year, buyers are strongly encouraged to secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify complete service records before finalizing a purchase.
