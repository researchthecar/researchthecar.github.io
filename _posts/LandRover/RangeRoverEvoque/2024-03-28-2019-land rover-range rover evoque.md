**Introduction:**

The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque is a compact luxury SUV known for its sleek design and off-road capabilities. It comes with a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine producing 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Evoque is praised for its stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and advanced technology, but some owners have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine**

- **Intake Manifold Runner Control Valve Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning valve restricts airflow, causing rough idling, decreased acceleration, and increased emissions.
    - Solution: Replace intake manifold runner control valve.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Turbocharger Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil, whistling or grinding noises, reduced engine performance.
    - Solution: Replace turbocharger.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

- **Shift Hesitation or Jerking:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks during gear changes.
    - Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission control module.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Fluid Leaks:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks can result in slipping gears, grinding noises, and loss of power.
    - Solution: Repair or replace damaged seals or gaskets.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Suspension Bushing Wear:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of front suspension bushings leads to increased vibration, noise, and poor handling.
    - Solution: Replace worn bushings.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning rear shock absorbers cause a bumpy ride, reduced stability, and uneven tire wear.
    - Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery loses charge quickly, especially when the vehicle is parked overnight.
    - Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical drain, such as faulty modules or parasitic drain.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Infotainment System Issues:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes, Bluetooth connectivity problems, navigation errors.
    - Solution: Software updates or hardware replacements.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Plugged sunroof drains can lead to water leaks into the cabin.
    - Solution: Clean or replace clogged drain plugs.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Headlight Condensation:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Condensation builds up inside the headlights, affecting visibility.
    - Solution: Replace affected headlight assembly.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Rattle:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loose dashboard components create a rattling noise while driving.
    - Solution: Secure dashboard fasteners or replace affected components.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system not functioning properly.
    - Solution: Repair or replace faulty components, such as AC compressor, blower motor, or control module.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While some owners report minor issues that can be easily addressed, others have encountered more significant problems with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue and whether it is covered under warranty.
