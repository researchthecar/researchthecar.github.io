**2020 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque**

The 2020 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque is a compact luxury SUV that offers a comfortable and stylish interior, impressive off-road capabilities, and a range of powerful engine and transmission options. It comes with a choice of three engines: a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four with 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four with 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0L turbocharged diesel inline-four with 180 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 9-speed automatic and an 8-speed automatic.

The Evoque is known for its sleek design, luxurious interior, and impressive off-road capabilities. However, it has also been plagued by various issues, as outlined below:

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four (246 hp)**
    * **Intake Valve Deposits:** Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause misfiring, rough idling, and reduced power. Solution: Clean the intake valves. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Turbocharger can fail prematurely, causing a loss of power and increased emissions. Solution: Replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four (296 hp)**
    * **Timing Chain Failure:** Timing chain can stretch or break prematurely, causing catastrophic engine failure. Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000
* **2.0L Turbocharged Diesel Inline-Four**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Fuel injectors can clog or fail, causing rough idling, decreased fuel economy, and black smoke from the exhaust. Solution: Replace the fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:** DPF can become clogged with soot, reducing engine performance and causing the DPF warning light to illuminate. Solution: Clean or replace the DPF. Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shift Hesitation:** Transmission can hesitate or jerk during gear changes. Solution: Update the transmission control module (TCM) software. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * **Torque Converter Shudder:** Torque converter can shudder during acceleration or deceleration. Solution: Replace the torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:** Transmission can leak fluid from the pan or seals. Solution: Replace the leaking components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:** Front suspension can make clunking or rattling noises over bumps. Solution: Replace the front suspension struts or mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
* **Rear Suspension Sagging:** Rear suspension can sag prematurely, causing the vehicle to lean to one side. Solution: Replace the rear suspension springs or shocks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery can drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start. Solution: Check for a drain on the electrical system and repair as necessary. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Infotainment system can experience glitches, such as freezing or restarting. Solution: Update the infotainment system software. Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if under warranty, otherwise $100 - $200
* **Headlight Condensation:** Headlights can develop condensation on the inside. Solution: Replace the headlight assembly. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:** Windshield wiper motor can fail prematurely, causing the wipers to stop working. Solution: Replace the wiper motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
* **Sunroof Rattle:** Sunroof can rattle while driving. Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof seals. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Climate control system can malfunction, causing the vehicle to overheat or blow cold air. Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module or related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
* **Seat Adjustment Motor Failure:** Seat adjustment motor can fail, preventing the driver or passenger from adjusting the seat position. Solution: Replace the seat adjustment motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary**

The 2020 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque offers a luxurious and capable driving experience, but it has been plagued by a number of issues that can impact its reliability and cost of ownership. The most severe issues include engine timing chain failure, transmission torque converter shudder, and suspension noise. Electrical glitches and infotainment system problems are also common. Potential owners should be aware of these issues and factor in the cost of potential repairs when considering the Evoque.
