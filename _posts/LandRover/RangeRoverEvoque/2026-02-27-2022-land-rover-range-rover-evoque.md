# 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque blends luxury, distinct styling, and compact utility into a premium package. For prospective used-car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and typical ownership experiences is essential for a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P250)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving, though buyers should maintain regular service intervals to ensure long-term dependability.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Mild Hybrid (P300)

This higher-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder utilizes mild hybrid technology to generate around 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers enhanced performance for spirited driving while incorporating complex electrical assist systems.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with standard all-wheel drive to deliver smooth highway cruising and efficient power delivery. Routine fluid checks are recommended to maintain optimal shift quality over time.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V454000: Air Bags:Passenger Side Frontal:Cushion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may tear during deployment. A torn air bag may not adequately protect an occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque offers refined styling and capable performance for luxury compact SUV shoppers. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's service history and electronics prior to purchase.
