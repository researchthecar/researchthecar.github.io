**Introduction:**

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque is a compact luxury SUV known for its sporty handling, premium cabin, and rugged off-road capabilities. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 mild-hybrid with 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque and a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 plug-in hybrid with 300 horsepower and 318 lb-ft of torque.

Owners appreciate the Evoque's powerful engines, elegant interior, and advanced technology features. However, some have encountered various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (246 hp)**

* **Faulty Turbocharger:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive oil consumption, blue or white smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Delayed Gear Shifts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in gear changes, particularly during acceleration.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Worn Air Suspension:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking air suspension struts, reduced ride height, poor handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the air suspension struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+ per strut
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

**Electrical**

* **Inoperative Touchscreen:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Unresponsive or intermittent touchscreen display.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the touchscreen module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

* **Malfunctioning Brake Lights:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative brake lights, potentially leading to safety hazards.
    * Solution: Replacement of the brake light switch or brake light assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

**Exterior**

* **Loose Roof Rails:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling roof rails, potential for detachment.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of the roof rails.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

* **Premature Rusting of Exhaust Components:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature corrosion of exhaust system components, potentially affecting performance and longevity.
    * Solution: Replacement of rusted components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty Climate Control System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control unit, leading to inconsistent temperature and airflow.
    * Solution: Replacement of the climate control unit or software update.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

* **Broken Window Regulators:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to open or close windows, potential for safety hazards.
    * Solution: Replacement of the window regulators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ per window
    * Recalls: No Issues Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque has been praised for its performance, design, and technology. However, some owners have reported issues with engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components. While the severity and cost of these issues vary, they can range from minor inconveniences to more significant repair expenses. It is important to note that not all vehicles are affected by these issues, and individual experiences may differ based on factors such as driving habits and maintenance practices.
