**Introduction**
The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque is a luxury compact SUV that offers a combination of style, performance, and off-road capability. It comes with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0L Ingenium gasoline engine and a 9-speed automatic transmission. The gasoline engine produces 240 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque.

Despite its overall popularity, the 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque has experienced several issues that have been reported by owners. These issues range from minor annoyances to more serious problems that can affect the vehicle's performance or safety.

**Engine**
* **2.0L Ingenium Gasoline Engine**
  * **Intake Valve Deposits**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves can lead to rough idling, hesitation, and reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Professional cleaning of the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Timing Chain Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain can result in engine misfiring, loss of power, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **9-Speed Automatic Transmission**
  * **Hesitation and Shuddering**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, hesitation, or shuddering during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission repairs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged front strut mounts can cause excessive noise, vibration, and reduced handling stability.
    * Solution: Replacement of the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the rear air suspension compressor can lead to reduced ride height and uneven vehicle stance.
    * Solution: Replacement of the compressor and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete failure of the infotainment system, including navigation, audio, and climate control.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V436000
* **Electrical Gremlins**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random electrical issues, such as flickering lights, power window failures, or battery drain.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the affected electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Cracked Headlamps**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or damage to the headlamp lenses.
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlamp unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per headlamp
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust on Undercarriage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive corrosion on the undercarriage components, such as the exhaust system or suspension parts.
    * Solution: Rust removal and protective coating.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the panoramic sunroof.
    * Solution: Inspection and repairs to the sunroof mechanism and seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque has experienced various issues, ranging from minor electrical glitches to more serious engine and transmission problems. While some issues are relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be costly. Potential owners should be aware of these issues before purchasing a vehicle. It is advisable to research recalls and perform a thorough inspection before making a decision.
