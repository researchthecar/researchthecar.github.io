# 2020 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque blends upscale styling and luxury features into a compact SUV package. However, owner reports highlight several potential trouble spots that used-car buyers should inspect closely before purchasing, particularly concerning electrical stability and powertrain reliability.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine provides brisk acceleration suited for daily driving. While generally adequate for performance, buyers should monitor for unexpected stalling and fuel system integrity.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall while idling or driving in traffic, occasionally accompanied by a complete electrical shutdown.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check battery health, charging systems, or engine management software.

**Fuel System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported fuel odors, pooling fuel underneath the vehicle, and fuel being ejected from the exhaust system.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel lines, injectors, and exhaust components thoroughly, and replace any leaking components immediately.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth highway cruising power when operating properly. Prospective buyers should test shift responsiveness thoroughly across all gears during a test drive.

**Gearbox Fault and Lag**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience dashboard warnings for gearbox faults, delayed engagement, or hesitation when shifting into drive.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module scanned for diagnostic trouble codes and check fluid condition.

## Steering

### Front Suspension and Steering Linkage

Suspension components are subject to premature wear on rough surfaces, leading to unwanted noise and handling vibrations.

**Grinding Noises and Unstable Ride**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear grinding noises during low-speed turns and experience frontend vibration or instability over road bumps.
- **Solution:** Inspect control arms, bushings, and hydraulic components for wear; replace faulty components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment System

The vehicle relies heavily on complex digital architecture that can occasionally experience random software anomalies.

**Randomized Instrument Cluster and Feature Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Speedometers and odometers may randomly toggle between units of measurement, and terrain selection modes may change unprompted.
- **Solution:** Software updates performed by a dealership can sometimes resolve persistent digital communication glitches.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Roof Seals

Exterior weather stripping and seals require careful inspection, as leaks around the glass can introduce moisture into the cabin.

**Cabin Water Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Rainwater may leak through faulty windshield seals or roof junctions, leading to saturated headliners, damp carpets, and interior condensation.
- **Solution:** Reseal or replace compromised windshield and roof moldings to prevent moisture intrusion and subsequent electrical damage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V840000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating the deployment of side curtain air bags, the metal plate could detach from the headliner and strike vehicle occupants, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will apply self-adhesive fleece tape to the metal plate and the driver and passenger sides of the headliner, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V517000: Tires:Pressure Monitoring And Regulating Systems](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 138, "Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems." Drivers may unknowingly operate the vehicle with low tire pressure, negatively impacting vehicle handling and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V793000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** These seat belt assemblies may not function properly in the event of a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) numbers 208, "Occupant Crash Protection" and 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." Seat belts that do not function properly can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the right and/or left-side outer second-row seat belt assemblies and replace them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V794000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a fire, the seat material may burn quicker than allowed. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 302, "Flammability of Interior Materials." A seat cover that burns too quickly can increase the risk of injury in the event of a fire.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front seat covers, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V751000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the instrument cluster to randomly alternate the speedometer and odometer units between Miles Per Hour (MPH) and Kilometers Per Hour (KM/H) without driver input to the display unit. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Control and Displays.".
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V421000: Visibility:Rear Window Wiper/Washer](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 104, "Windshield Wiping and Washing Systems." The driver's reduced visibility can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuse for the front and rear washer systems, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V168000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The cushion cover on the left-side lower knee air bag may come loose, which could result in the cushion unfolding from the air bag module housing. In the event of a crash, an unfolded knee air bag may not offer the driver full protection, increasing the risk for injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the left-side lower knee air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20E017000: Structure](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Evoque&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 201, "Occupant Protection in Interior Impact." An improperly manufactured headliner may increase the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify vehicle owners, and dealers will replace the headliner, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Land Rover Range Rover Evoque offers luxury and distinctive styling, but potential buyers should approach with careful inspection. Pay close attention to electrical system stability, transmission responsiveness, and signs of cabin water leaks. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a specialist familiar with the brand is strongly recommended.
