The 2015 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxury SUV known for its off-road capability, refined interior, and distinctive styling. Engine options for the 2015 model year include a 3.0-liter supercharged V6 (340 hp, 332 lb-ft of torque), a 5.0-liter supercharged V8 (510 hp, 461 lb-ft of torque), and a 3.0-liter turbocharged diesel V6 (254 hp, 443 lb-ft of torque). All engines were paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners generally appreciate the Range Rover Sport's luxurious features, powerful engines, and off-road prowess. Common dislikes include high maintenance costs, reliability concerns, and complex electronics.

**Engine**

**3.0L Supercharged V6**

*   **Supercharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the supercharger, loss of power, and potential engine damage.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the supercharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant level decreasing, overheating, coolant puddles under the vehicle, and white smoke from the exhaust.
    *   Solution: Identify and repair the source of the leak, which may involve replacing hoses, water pump, or other cooling system components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**5.0L Supercharged V8**

*   **Timing Chain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise upon startup, especially when cold. Potential for engine damage if the chain stretches or breaks.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Supercharger Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noises, loss of power, potential engine damage.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the supercharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $7,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Turbocharged Diesel V6**

*   **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, warning lights on the dashboard, and potential for the DPF to become clogged.
    *   Solution: DPF regeneration, cleaning, or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (All Engines)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifts between gears, hesitation upon acceleration, and potential for transmission slipping.
    *   Solution: Transmission fluid flush, software update, valve body repair or replacement, or complete transmission replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $6,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging or not raising properly, "Suspension Fault" warning light, and compressor running constantly.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Air Strut Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, especially overnight, hissing sound from the suspension, and "Suspension Fault" warning light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaking air strut(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+ per strut
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing, unresponsive touchscreen, Bluetooth connectivity problems, and navigation malfunctions.
    *   Solution: Software update, replacement of the infotainment system.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Battery draining overnight or after a short period of inactivity, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the parasitic drain, which may involve replacing faulty modules or wiring.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Tailgate Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Tailgate failing to open or close properly, issues with the automatic opening/closing mechanism.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the tailgate motor, struts, or latch mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof failing to open or close smoothly, leaking, or making unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Repair or replacement of the sunroof motor, tracks, or seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Land Rover Range Rover Sport presents a mix of luxury and potential reliability challenges. Engine issues, particularly with the supercharged V6 and V8 models, and air suspension problems are common and can lead to significant repair bills. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system malfunctions and battery drain, can also be frustrating. While the diesel engine has its own set of potential issues, it can be a more reliable option. Potential buyers should factor in the high potential costs of ownership, and consider a pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic familiar with Land Rover vehicles. Overall, this is a luxury SUV that can be great as long as the proper maintenance is performed to prevent issues from occurring.
