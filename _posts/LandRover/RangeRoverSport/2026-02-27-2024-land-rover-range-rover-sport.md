# 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Sport delivers a striking combination of luxury, modern styling, and impressive performance. However, early owner reports submitted to safety regulators indicate that buyers should carefully evaluate potential electrical and powertrain glitches before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (P360 / P400)

This mild-hybrid 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 produces approximately 355 to 395 horsepower depending on the tune, providing brisk acceleration. While generally smooth and refined, early model year units have seen complaints regarding electrical integration and unexpected shutdowns.

**Sudden Power Loss and Electrical Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience a sudden and complete loss of electrical power while driving, leading to an unexpected shutdown in traffic.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnosis by a dealership, often related to DC/DC converter or charging system inspections and software updates.

**Engine Overheating and Fire Risk**
- **Symptoms:** An overheating warning message may appear on the dashboard, followed in severe cases by smoke or an engine fire.
- **Solution:** Immediate inspection of the cooling system, wiring harnesses, and fluid levels by a qualified technician.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This powerful twin-turbocharged V8 delivers robust high-end performance and effortless towing capability. It is paired with an advanced all-wheel-drive system, though buyers should watch for complex engine bay heat and electronic management issues common in modern high-output luxury vehicles.

### 3.0L Plug-In Hybrid (P550e)

Combining a 3.0-liter inline-6 with an electric motor and battery pack, the plug-in hybrid offers substantial total output and electric-only driving range. Buyers should verify proper charging functionality and ensure all hybrid-system software campaigns have been applied.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission shifts smoothly across all trims and pairs seamlessly with the standard all-wheel-drive system. Overall durability is generally high, though electronic module communication faults can occasionally trigger limp-mode or warning lights.

**Drivetrain and Traction Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden warning messages such as 'two-wheel drive only' accompanied by illuminated traction control lights while cruising at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Dealer diagnostic scan to check for transmission control module faults or software glitches.

## Electrical

### DC/DC Converter and Charging System

The complex electrical architecture controls nearly every vehicle function, with early reports pointing toward charging system and converter vulnerabilities that warrant a thorough pre-purchase scan.

**DC/DC Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle displays warning lights, flashers, and prompts to pull over immediately due to electrical power loss.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of affected DC/DC converter components through authorized service channels.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wipers

Exterior panels and glass are generally well-assembled, though proprietary wiper systems have drawn owner complaints regarding wet-weather visibility.

**Improper Fluid Distribution and Visibility Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Washer fluid and wiper design may fail to clear winter salt or heavy rain effectively from the driver's field of view.
- **Solution:** Aftermarket wiper blade replacements or dealer adjustment of the spray nozzles.

### Doors and Trim

Cabin luxury is top-tier, but minor fit-and-finish complaints regarding door seals and latch alignment have been noted by early owners.

**Driver Door Movement and Wind Noise**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's door may shift slightly over bumps and rough pavement, creating noticeable wind noise at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Adjustment of door strikes, latches, or weatherstripping by dealership technicians.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V872000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The body panels may not be bonded properly, reducing the structural strength of the vehicle. Reduced structural strength may not adequately protect passengers, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install two rivets and apply sealant, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V380000: Power Train:Clutch Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V450000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Filter/Screen](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the filter housing, oil filter, and o-rings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V678000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the NFSM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V097000: Structure:Exterior Trim](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The panoramic sunroof side finisher trim may not be properly secured and can detach. A detached panoramic sunroof side trim can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the panoramic sunroof side finisher trim and secure it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is an exceptionally luxurious and capable SUV, but used-car buyers should proceed with caution. Ensure that all electrical software updates and converter-related campaigns have been completed by checking service records prior to purchase.
