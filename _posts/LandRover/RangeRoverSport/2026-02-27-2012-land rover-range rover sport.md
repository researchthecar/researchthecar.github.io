# 2012 Land Rover Range Rover Sport

### Overview
The 2012 Land Rover Range Rover Sport, while offering a luxurious and capable off-road experience, presents a mixed bag in terms of long-term reliability. This model year is known for its robust powertrain options, particularly the V8 engines, which generally perform well. However, like many luxury SUVs of its era, it is susceptible to certain electrical gremlins and can incur significant repair costs, especially concerning its advanced air suspension system. Owners should be prepared for potential maintenance needs that are above average for the segment, focusing on proactive checks of common failure points to mitigate unexpected expenses and ensure continued performance.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Naturally Aspirated
**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, guides, and potentially sprockets.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,500

**Coolant Leaks (Water Pump/Thermostat Housing)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant leaks, overheating, low coolant level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of water pump and/or thermostat housing.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


### 5.0L V8 Supercharged
**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, guides, and potentially sprockets.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,500

**Supercharger Belt Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whining noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of supercharger belt and potentially idler pulleys.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (ZF 6HP28)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping gears, rough engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, 'suspension fault' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000 per corner (spring), $700 - $1,500 (compressor)

**Height Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Incorrect vehicle height, suspension warning lights, erratic ride height adjustments.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty height sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, navigation errors, audio system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500

**Battery Drain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery, electrical components not working, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**Keyless Entry/Start Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle not recognizing key fob, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or replacement of key fobs, or related modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No Major Issues
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Cabin Features
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, headliner staining.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sunroof drains.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Heated Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** One or both heated seats not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of heating elements or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Range Rover Sport is a capable luxury SUV, but potential buyers should be aware of its reliability profile. The V8 engines are generally strong, but timing chain issues can be costly. The air suspension system is a common source of expensive repairs due to leaks or component failures. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and keyless entry, are also noted. While the exterior and interior are generally well-built, issues like clogged sunroof drains can occur. Proactive maintenance and a budget for potential repairs are highly recommended for this model.
