# 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Sport delivers exceptional luxury, commanding performance, and legendary off-road capability, but prospective buyers should approach with a measure of caution. While its available supercharged engines and diesel options offer robust power, owner reports highlight potential mechanical and electrical vulnerabilities. Paying close attention to maintenance history and prior recall completions is essential for anyone considering a used model.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

A supercharged gasoline V6 producing roughly 340 to 380 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration and refined power delivery for daily driving.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience random engine stalling while driving, accompanied by electrical warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician to identify faulty sensors, fuel delivery issues, or software anomalies.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8

A high-output V8 engine generating up to 510 or 550 horsepower, offering effortless performance and substantial towing capacity at the expense of fuel economy.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 Diesel

A torque-heavy diesel powerplant delivering strong low-end pulling power and improved fuel range for long-distance driving.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected loss of motive power or catastrophic engine failure while traveling at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires thorough professional inspection of internal components and adherence to manufacturer maintenance guidelines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

A smooth-shifting ZF 8-speed automatic transmission paired across the lineup to optimize both performance and cruising efficiency.

**Shifter and Park Defects**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may display gear shifter warnings, fail to register when parked, or become stuck in park.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic shifter assembly and update or replace faulty control modules.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension

Advanced air suspension system designed to balance dynamic handling on-road with exceptional articulation off-road.

**Steering Rack and Knuckle Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual steering noises, cracking components, or vehicle pulling during maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Have the suspension inspected by a dealer to address any active safety recalls or component cracks.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Standard restraint systems incorporating pre-tensioners and adjustable anchors for occupant protection.

**Seat Belt Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Passenger or driver seat belts may fail to lock securely or register incorrectly.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace faulty buckle mechanisms or sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V350000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat belt Emergency Locking Retractor (ELR) may not lock as designed. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." If the driver brakes prior to a crash, the ELR will not lock at the correct g-force level, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the seat belt assembly, replacing it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V337000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Both scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will install a software update to correct the fuel level reading, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V942000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seatbelt pretensioners that may not function properly in the event of a crash. If the front seat seatbelt pretensioners do not function properly, the seat occupants may not be adequately restrained in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front seat belt assemblies, replacing them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V679000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A blank instrument cluster will not show important information like vehicle speed, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V219000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat belt emergency locking retractor may not lock as intended. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) numbers 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies" and 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During hard braking before a crash, the seat belt emergency locking retractor may not lock as.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V514000: Steering:Linkages:Knuckle:Spindle:Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Suspension arm detachment can cause a loss of vehicle handling and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front suspension knuckles and install a retaining bracket or replace the knuckle, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is an appealing option for buyers seeking luxury and capability, but it requires careful vetting. Prospective owners should verify that all open safety recalls have been addressed and secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a marque specialist to catch potential engine, transmission, or suspension concerns early.
