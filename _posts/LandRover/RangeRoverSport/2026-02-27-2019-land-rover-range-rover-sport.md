# 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Sport blends high-end luxury with rugged capability, offering buyers a striking presence and a diverse range of powerful engine options. While it delivers an engaging driving experience and premium cabin appointments, prospective buyers should approach the used market with careful attention to vehicle history and maintenance records. As with many complex luxury SUVs, addressing underlying mechanical or electronic issues can require specialized service and careful budgeting.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 Gas

This supercharged 3.0-liter V6 engine produces approximately 340 to 380 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving, though cooling and supercharger belt systems warrant periodic inspection.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 Diesel

The 3.0-liter turbo diesel generates around 254 horsepower and a robust 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers excellent low-end pulling power and highway fuel economy, making it a strong choice for long-distance cruising.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8 Gas

This high-performance supercharged V8 pumps out 518 to 575 horsepower depending on the tune. It delivers blistering performance and a signature exhaust note, but demands vigilant fluid maintenance and higher operating costs.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid

Paired with an electric motor, this plug-in hybrid powertrain delivers a combined 398 horsepower. It offers efficient short-distance electric driving alongside traditional gasoline backup, though complex hybrid electronics require careful oversight.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

This smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across all engine options and standard four-wheel drive. It is generally robust and responsive, delivering crisp gear changes under varied driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V040000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure can increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover has notified owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft pulley center retaining bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V117000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." The driver may be unaware that the high beams are illuminated, reducing visibility for oncoming traffic, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V044000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine cam carrier and related components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V023000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display while in reverse decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rearview camera and add a new seal or replace the camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V364000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rearview camera for damage. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.
