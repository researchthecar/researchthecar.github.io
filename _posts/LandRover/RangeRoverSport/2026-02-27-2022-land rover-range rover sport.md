# 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Sport

### Overview
The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Sport, while offering a luxurious and capable driving experience, presents a mixed reliability profile. Owners generally praise its on-road comfort and off-road prowess. However, like many high-end, technologically advanced vehicles, it is susceptible to certain electronic and powertrain-related issues that can impact long-term ownership satisfaction. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners. The vehicle's complexity means that while many systems perform admirably, specific components may require attention over time.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Ingenium I6 MHEV (P360)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the turbocharger unit or related plumbing.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


### 5.0L Supercharged V8 (P525)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chains, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,500

**Supercharger Belt Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whining noise from the supercharger area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger drive belt and potentially tensioner.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)
**Hesitation or Jerking Shifts**
- **Symptoms:** Rough gear changes, delayed engagement, or noticeable jolts during acceleration or deceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid and filter service, or potential internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air lines.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant compressor noise, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system glitches, or reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a premium SUV with sophisticated systems that can occasionally lead to specific technical challenges. Common areas of concern include potential coolant leaks and turbocharger issues with the Ingenium engines, and timing chain wear with the supercharged V8. The adaptive air suspension is prone to leaks and compressor failures. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also noted. While not indicative of universal failure, these issues suggest that owners should be prepared for potential repairs beyond standard maintenance, especially as the vehicle ages. Regular servicing by qualified technicians is crucial for mitigating these risks.
