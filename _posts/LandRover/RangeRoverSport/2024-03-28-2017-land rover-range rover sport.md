**Introduction to the 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Sport**

The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxury SUV that combines rugged off-road capabilities with sophisticated on-road handling. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.0L V6 diesel, a 3.0L V6 supercharged gasoline, a 5.0L V8 supercharged gasoline, and a 4.4L V8 turbocharged diesel. The Range Rover Sport has a spacious and well-appointed interior, but it is also expensive to purchase and maintain.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Diesel**
* **Fuel Pump Failure:** The fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to stall or lose power. The fuel pump must be replaced. $1000 - $1500+
* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail, resulting in reduced engine power and increased fuel consumption. The turbocharger must be replaced. $2000 - $3000+

**3.0L V6 Supercharged Gasoline**
* **Supercharger Failure:** The supercharger can fail, causing a loss of power and fuel economy. The supercharger must be replaced. $3000 - $4000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip teeth and damage the engine. The timing chain and tensioner must be replaced. $2500 - $3500+

**5.0L V8 Supercharged Gasoline**
* **Engine Oil Cooler Failure:** The engine oil cooler can leak, causing the engine to overheat. The engine oil cooler must be replaced. $1200 - $1800+
* **Intake Manifold Failure:** The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power and fuel economy. The intake manifold must be replaced. $1500 - $2000+

**4.4L V8 Turbocharged Diesel**
* **EGR Valve Failure:** The EGR valve can fail, causing the engine to run rough and produce excessive emissions. The EGR valve must be replaced. $800 - $1200+
* **Diesel Particulate Filter Failure:** The diesel particulate filter can become clogged, reducing engine power and fuel economy. The diesel particulate filter must be cleaned or replaced. $1000 - $1500+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Transmission Control Module Failure:** The transmission control module can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not at all. The transmission control module must be replaced. $1500 - $2000+
* **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter can fail, causing the transmission to shudder or slip. The torque converter must be replaced. $2000 - $3000+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:** The air suspension compressor can fail, causing the suspension to sag or become inoperable. The air suspension compressor must be replaced. $1200 - $1800+
* **Air Suspension Shock Absorber Failure:** The air suspension shock absorbers can fail, causing the suspension to become bouncy or unstable. The air suspension shock absorbers must be replaced. $1000 - $1500+ per shock

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle stranded. The cause of the battery drain must be diagnosed and repaired. $500 - $1500+
* **Electrical System Failure:** The electrical system can fail, causing a variety of problems, such as loss of power, lights, or instrumentation. The cause of the electrical system failure must be diagnosed and repaired. $500 - $2000+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:** The sunroof can leak water into the cabin. The sunroof seals must be replaced. $500 - $1000+
* **Door Lock Failure:** The door locks can fail, making it difficult to enter or exit the vehicle. The door locks must be repaired or replaced. $500 - $1200+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system can malfunction, causing the interior of the vehicle to become too hot or too cold. The climate control system must be diagnosed and repaired. $500 - $1500+
* **Infotainment System Failure:** The infotainment system can fail, causing the loss of audio, navigation, and other functions. The infotainment system must be replaced. $1000 - $2000+

**Summary**

The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxurious and capable SUV, but it is also expensive to purchase and maintain. Common issues include fuel pump failure, turbocharger failure, electrical system issues, and sunroof leaks. The cost to fix these issues can vary widely, from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
