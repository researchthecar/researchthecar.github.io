**Introduction:**
The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxurious SUV known for its on-road performance and off-road capabilities. It's powered by a range of petrol and diesel engines, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle's interior is highly praised for its comfort and technology features, while its exterior is rugged and stylish. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Supercharged** (355 hp, 365 lb-ft)
- **Timing Chain Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or ticking noise, reduced power, or check engine light.
  - Solution: Replace timing chain and related components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
  - Recalls: LR04228, LR04229, LR04231

**3.0L V6 Diesel** (254 hp, 443 lb-ft)
- **Fuel Injector Failures:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, poor fuel economy, loss of power.
  - Solution: Replace fuel injectors.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per injector

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**
- **Gearbox Control Unit Failures:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission not shifting smoothly, stuck in gear, or displaying error messages.
  - Solution: Replace gearbox control unit.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
  - Recalls: LR05925

**Suspension**

- **Front Air Suspension Malfunctions:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, loss of air pressure, or warning lights.
  - Solution: Replace air compressor or air suspension struts.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

- **Rear Air Suspension Malfunctions:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Similar to front air suspension malfunctions, but affects the rear axle.
  - Solution: Similar to front air suspension repairs.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, even when the vehicle is parked.
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, replace battery if necessary.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+

- **Electrical Component Failures:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Issues with various electrical components such as lights, windows, or infotainment system.
  - Solution: Replace or repair faulty electrical components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component

**Exterior**

- **Sunroof Drainage Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the vehicle through the sunroof.
  - Solution: Clean sunroof drains or replace sunroof assembly.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

- **Paint Defects:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Factory paint defects such as bubbling, peeling, or flaking.
  - Solution: Bodywork repairs or repainting.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+

**Interior**

- **Infotainment System Glitches:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing, navigation issues, or Bluetooth connectivity problems.
  - Solution: Software updates or hardware repairs.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

- **Trim and Panel Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling trim pieces, faded or damaged panels.
  - Solution: Replace or repair trim and panels.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Summary:**

The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Sport has several reported issues, ranging from engine and transmission problems to electrical and interior glitches. While some issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be major and costly. It's essential for potential owners to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
