# 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Sport, buyers will find a luxury SUV offering impressive performance and upscale features. However, prospective owners should review owner-reported reliability trends, particularly regarding powertrain cooling and electrical systems, to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV

This electrified 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 mild-hybrid engine delivers approximately 355 to 395 horsepower. It provides smooth power delivery for daily driving, though buyers should monitor cooling systems closely.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8

The high-performance 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine pumps out between 518 and 575 horsepower, offering brisk acceleration. Routine maintenance and vigilant fluid checks are key to keeping this powerful motor running reliably.

### Cooling System

Owner reports indicate occasional cooling system concerns involving fluid leaks and associated warning lights.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a sweet burning smell of engine coolant or low coolant warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Inspect hoses, connections, and related components, and replace any leaking parts or faulty components as needed.

### Fuel System

Fuel delivery components require careful inspection if any unusual gasoline odors are detected.

**Fuel Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A faint fuel odor may be detected inside or around the vehicle during routine operation or after refueling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect fuel lines and components, and replace damaged parts such as the fuel line connector.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission paired with standard four-wheel drive, this setup delivers seamless gear shifts. No widespread recurring mechanical failures are highlighted in owner complaints.

## Electrical

### 48V Mild Hybrid Electrical System

The 48V mild hybrid electrical system and DC-to-DC converter components are focal points for certain owner electrical complaints.

**Charging System Fault and DC-to-DC Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may display charging system warnings, lose acceleration power, enter limp mode, or experience sudden electrical shutdown.
- **Solution:** Have the 48V electrical architecture, DC-to-DC converter, and battery modules diagnosed and serviced by a certified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V668000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V586000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Failure to inflate tires to the correct pressure could lead to loss of vehicle stability and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will apply the correct labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V585000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 138, "Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems." Drivers may unknowingly operate the vehicle with low tire pressure, possibly causing tire tread separation, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V044000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine cam carrier and related components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V023000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display while in reverse decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rearview camera and add a new seal or replace the camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V364000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rearview camera for damage. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Sport delivers dynamic performance and luxury appeal, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the cooling system, fuel lines, and the 48V mild-hybrid electrical architecture to catch potential issues early.
