**2021 Land Rover Range Rover Sport**

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxury SUV that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It boasts a spacious and well-appointed interior, along with advanced technology features. However, some owners have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**

* **Gasoline 3.0L Inline-6**

  * **Intake valve deposits:** Leading to reduced power and fuel efficiency. Requires replacement of intake valves and cleaning of combustion chambers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Gasoline 5.0L V8 (Supercharged)**

  * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Causing rattling noises and potential engine damage. Requires replacement of timing chain tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 21V558, NHTSA ID: 20E006

* **Diesel 3.0L V6**

  * **EGR system failure:** Leading to engine performance issues and increased emissions. Requires replacement of EGR components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 21V559

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

  * **Shifting problems:** Difficulty shifting gears or intermittent shifting. Requires replacement or repair of transmission components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 21V221, NHTSA ID: 21V510

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension compressor failure:** Resulting in reduced ride height and handling issues. Requires replacement of air suspension compressor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Front lower control arms:** Premature wear leading to rattling noises and reduced stability. Requires replacement of control arms. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery discharge:** Excessive battery drain leading to starting problems. Requires replacement of battery or repair of electrical components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Infotainment screen issues:** Touchscreen unresponsiveness, glitches, or display problems. Requires replacement of infotainment unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 21V560

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** Moisture buildup inside headlights affecting visibility. Requires replacement of headlights. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Roof panel paint peeling:** Premature peeling of paint on roof panel. Requires repainting or replacement of roof panel. Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Sunroof noise:** Rattling or wind noise from sunroof during driving. Requires adjustment or replacement of sunroof components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Navigation system software glitches:** Inaccurate navigation information or system crashes. Requires software updates or replacement of navigation unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2021 Land Rover Range Rover Sport offers a luxurious driving experience, but it has been plagued by various issues that affect the engine, transmission, and other components. The severity of these issues can range from minor annoyances to major repairs. The cost of fixing these issues can be significant, potentially affecting the overall ownership experience.
