# 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Sport brings striking modern design, impressive luxury, and advanced technology to the luxury performance SUV segment. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability trends carefully, particularly concerning complex onboard electronics, powertrain power delivery glitches, and initial-year bugs. While many owners enjoy exceptional comfort and performance, researching maintenance history and remaining warranty coverage is strongly advised before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV (P360 / P400)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 mild-hybrid engine delivers smooth power ranging from roughly 355 to 395 horsepower depending on the tune, offering strong performance and daily drivability. Buyers should routinely monitor fluid levels and check for early signs of cooling system leaks.

**Coolant Loss and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Persistent loss of engine coolant from the cooling system, potentially causing hot engine components to contact leaked fluid.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling lines, hoses, and connections; repair or replace faulty components as necessary.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (P530)

The potent 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine pumps out approximately 523 horsepower, providing rapid acceleration and effortless towing capability. It is a complex high-performance powerplant that demands meticulous adherence to factory maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly across all trims and four-wheel-drive systems, offering crisp shifts and refined cruising. It operates reliably overall, with few systemic mechanical complaints reported by owners.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power appropriate for a heavy luxury performance SUV, though routine inspection is necessary due to rapid pad wear under aggressive driving.

**Premature Brake Wear and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the brake warning light at relatively low mileage, indicating worn brake pads or rotor service needs.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for wear; replace friction components as required.

## Steering

### Power Steering and Lane Assist

Steering performance is precise and responsive under normal conditions, but electronic steering assist features can occasionally experience diagnostic fault triggers.

**Steering Loss and Lane Assist Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden flashing of 'Lane assist not available' messages paired with heavy steering resistance or loss of power steering assistance.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan of steering control modules and software updates to resolve communication faults.

## Electrical

### Electrical System and DC Converter

Electrical architecture complaints are among the most notable owner-reported issues for this model year, frequently involving software glitches or charging component failures.

**Sudden Electrical Shutdowns and Fault Messages**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning messages such as 'Electrical fault detected' or 'Stop safely', followed by complete vehicle power loss, steering loss, and engine shutdown.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect the DC-DC converter, charging system, and software modules for faults or required updates.

**Smart Key Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** The keyless vehicle security system simultaneously deactivates smart keys, potentially locking keys inside the vehicle or rendering them completely unresponsive.
- **Solution:** Reprogram or replace key fobs and update the keyless vehicle module software at an authorized service center.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a masterclass in modern luxury, style, and capability, but its sophisticated technology suite requires careful vetting on the used market. Prospective buyers should prioritize vehicles with comprehensive service histories, verify that all software updates and recall campaigns have been completed, and strongly consider remaining factory warranty or an extended service plan to mitigate potential electronic repair costs.
