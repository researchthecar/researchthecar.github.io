## 2016 Land Rover Range Rover Sport

The 2016 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxury SUV that offers a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* 3.0-liter V6 diesel engine with 254 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque
* 5.0-liter V8 supercharged gasoline engine with 510 horsepower and 461 lb-ft of torque
* 3.0-liter V6 supercharged gasoline engine with 340 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque
* 3.0-liter V6 supercharged gasoline engine with 380 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque
* 8-speed automatic transmission

The Range Rover Sport is known for its luxurious interior, impressive off-road capabilities, and powerful engines. However, it has also been plagued by a number of issues that have affected its reliability and overall owner satisfaction.

**Engine**

* **3.0-liter V6 Diesel Engine**
   * **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure:** The EGR valve can become clogged with carbon deposits, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency. The valve must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   * **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail due to oil starvation or other issues, resulting in a loss of power and increased emissions. The turbocharger must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* **5.0-liter V8 Supercharged Gasoline Engine**
   * **Supercharger Failure:** The supercharger can fail due to oil starvation or other issues, resulting in a loss of power and increased emissions. The supercharger must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000+
   * **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors can become clogged or fail, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency. The fuel injectors must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **3.0-liter V6 Supercharged Gasoline Engine (340 horsepower version)**
   * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing a loss of power and potential engine damage. The timing chain and tensioner must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **3.0-liter V6 Supercharged Gasoline Engine (380 horsepower version)**
   * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic Transmission**
   * **Shifting Issues:** The transmission may experience shifting issues, such as slipping gears or refusing to shift. The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
   * **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** The transmission may develop leaks from the seals or gaskets. The leaks must be repaired or the transmission could fail. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   * **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter can fail, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency. The torque converter must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:** The air suspension system can fail due to leaks or other issues, resulting in a loss of ride height and reduced handling. The air suspension components must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* **Control Arm Bushing Failure:** The control arm bushings can wear out or fail, causing a loss of alignment and reduced handling. The bushings must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to a parasitic drain or other issues. The electrical system must be inspected and the drain source identified. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Electrical Component Failures:** Various electrical components, such as sensors, modules, or switches, can fail, causing a variety of issues, such as loss of power, reduced functionality, or warning lights. The failed component must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:** The door handles can fail to open or close properly due to broken cables or other issues. The door handle must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Sunroof Leaks:** The sunroof may develop leaks due to faulty seals or other issues. The sunroof seals must be replaced or repaired. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system may experience glitches or failures, such as loss of functionality, freezing, or navigation issues. The infotainment system may need to be updated or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system may experience issues, such as blower motor failure, temperature fluctuations, or AC compressor failure. The climate control components must be diagnosed and repaired or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**

The 2016 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a capable and luxurious SUV, but it has been plagued by a number of issues that have affected its reliability and overall owner satisfaction. Engine problems, transmission failures, and electrical issues are among the most commonly reported. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
