**Introduction**

The 2011 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxury SUV known for its off-road capability, refined interior, and powerful engine options. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* **3.0L V6 Diesel:** 240 hp, 442 lb-ft of torque
* **5.0L V8:** 375 hp, 375 lb-ft of torque
* **5.0L V8 Supercharged:** 510 hp, 461 lb-ft of torque
* **6-speed automatic transmission**
* **8-speed ZF automatic transmission**

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Injectors can become clogged or fail, leading to poor engine performance, reduced fuel economy, and increased emissions. Solution: Replace fuel injectors. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $1,500+. Recalls: NHTSA 20V-146
* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues:** The DPF can become clogged, leading to reduced engine power and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Replace DPF. Estimated Cost: $2,000 - $3,000+. Recalls: NHTSA 21V-297

**5.0L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:** The intake manifold can develop cracks or leaks, causing rough idling, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Replace intake manifold. Estimated Cost: $1,500 - $2,000+. Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, leading to a rattling noise on startup and potential engine damage. Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $1,500+. Recalls: None Found

**5.0L V8 Supercharged**

* **Supercharger Failure:** The supercharger can fail, leading to a loss of boost pressure, reduced engine performance, and increased fuel consumption. Solution: Replace supercharger. Estimated Cost: $5,000 - $10,000+. Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** Transmission may slip or fail to engage properly, causing difficulty shifting and loss of power. Solution: Replace transmission. Estimated Cost: $5,000 - $8,000+. Recalls: NHTSA 21V-133
* **Torque Converter Failure:** Torque converter can fail, causing shuddering, transmission slippage, and overheating. Solution: Replace torque converter. Estimated Cost: $2,000 - $3,000+. Recalls: None Found

**8-speed ZF Automatic**

* **Mechatronic Sleeve Failure:** Mechatronic sleeve can tear or fail, leading to transmission fluid leaks and transmission failure. Solution: Replace mechatronic sleeve. Estimated Cost: $1,500 - $2,000+. Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Body Failure:** Valve body can malfunction, causing shifting problems, hesitation, and transmission overheating. Solution: Replace valve body. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $1,500+. Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:** Compressor can fail, leading to a loss of air pressure and a lowered ride height. Solution: Replace air suspension compressor. Estimated Cost: $1,500 - $2,000+. Recalls: None Found
* **Air Suspension Strut Leaks:** Air suspension struts can develop leaks, causing a lowered ride height and reduced handling. Solution: Replace air suspension struts. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $1,500 per strut. Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Battery can discharge excessively, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain. Estimated Cost: $200 - $500. Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster Failure:** Instrument cluster can malfunction, leading to inaccurate readings or complete failure. Solution: Replace instrument cluster. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $1,500. Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cracking:** Rear bumper can crack or break, especially in cold weather. Solution: Replace rear bumper. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $1,500. Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Roof Headliner Sagging:** Roof headliner can sag or detach, impairing visibility and aesthetics. Solution: Reattach or replace headliner. Estimated Cost: $500 - $1,000. Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Mechanism Failure:** Sunroof mechanism can fail, leading to a stuck or leaky sunroof. Solution: Repair or replace sunroof mechanism. Estimated Cost: $1,000 - $2,000+. Recalls: NHTSA 15V-691

**Summary**

While the 2011 Land Rover Range Rover Sport offers a luxurious and capable driving experience, it is not without its drawbacks. Engine and transmission issues can be costly to repair, while suspension and electrical problems can also be significant. Interior and exterior issues are generally less severe but can still detract from the overall ownership experience.
