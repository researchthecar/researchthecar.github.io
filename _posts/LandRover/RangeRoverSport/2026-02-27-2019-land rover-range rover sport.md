# 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Sport

### Overview
The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a luxury SUV known for its off-road capability and refined interior. While generally well-regarded, like any complex vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, covering its various powertrain options and key systems. Understanding these potential problem areas can help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and potential repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 (Gasoline)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, overheating, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Timing Chain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


### 5.0L Supercharged V8 (Gasoline)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, overheating, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Timing Chain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


### 3.0L SDV6 Diesel
**DPF Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration or DPF cleaning/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**EGR Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)
**Hesitation/Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Slight delay or jolt when shifting gears, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software update or transmission fluid and filter service.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or seals, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, pan gasket, or transmission cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000


## Suspension

### Air Suspension System
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air lines.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant compressor running, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive or freezes, system reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software update or system reset. In some cases, head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies overnight or after a short period, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover Sport offers a compelling blend of luxury and capability. While its core mechanical components are generally robust, potential owners should be aware of specific issues that can arise, particularly concerning the air suspension system and certain engine variants (especially coolant leaks and timing chain concerns on V6/V8 gasoline engines, and DPF/EGR on diesel). Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are also noted. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the reliability and longevity of this premium SUV.
