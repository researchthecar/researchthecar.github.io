# 2016 Land Rover Range Rover Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Land Rover Range Rover Sport delivers exceptional luxury, dynamic handling, and robust capability, but prospective buyers should approach used models with care. Like many European luxury SUVs from this era, it requires meticulous maintenance records and can present high repair costs when electrical or mechanical issues arise. Owners have highlighted a mix of recurring minor annoyances and more serious safety-related concerns, making a thorough pre-purchase inspection essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

This supercharged gasoline V6 offers strong acceleration and smooth power delivery, though ongoing cooling system and timing component monitoring is recommended at higher mileages.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8

Providing substantial horsepower and torque, this V8 option turns the Range Rover Sport into a high-performance machine while demanding strict adherence to maintenance schedules.

### 3.0L V6 Turbo Diesel

This diesel powertrain prioritizes strong low-end torque and impressive fuel economy for long-distance cruising, though complex emissions equipment can occasionally require professional attention.

**Diesel Emissions and Exhaust System Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning indicators appear alongside reduced power warnings, drivability instability, and repeated DEF, SCR, or DPF system faults.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a qualified specialist to clean or replace affected exhaust and fluid components.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

This responsive multi-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes across all driving modes and pairs well with both gasoline and diesel engine options.

## Steering

### Steering and Front Suspension

Steering and front suspension components provide precise handling, but owners have reported occasional electronic warnings and mechanical wear issues.

**Suspension Faults and Steering Knuckle Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of suspension or electrical warning lights on the dashboard, accompanied by abnormal shaking or steering assist failure.
- **Solution:** Inspection of front steering knuckles and suspension components, replacing worn parts as necessary.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Locks

Interior cabin hardware is generally plush, though mechanical latch failures in passenger doors require prompt attention.

**Unexpected Door Opening**
- **Symptoms:** Doors fail to latch securely or swing open unexpectedly while the vehicle is in motion, sometimes triggering the automatic parking brake.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door latch mechanisms or keyless locking actuators.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Occupant safety systems provide comprehensive protection, though occasional sensor faults can trigger warning lights.

**Intermittent Airbag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning lamp illuminates intermittently on the instrument cluster, indicating a potential fault in the supplementary restraint system.
- **Solution:** Run diagnostic scans to locate faulty sensors or wiring harnesses and replace defective components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V390000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The unlatched door may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of injury or a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the software, and inspect the KV latches, replacing them if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V325000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles were previously recalled and repaired under Recall 19V-390, however the repair may not have been completed properly. An unlatched door may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of injury or a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the software, and inspect the KV latches, replacing them if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V350000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat belt Emergency Locking Retractor (ELR) may not lock as designed. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." If the driver brakes prior to a crash, the ELR will not lock at the correct g-force level, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the seat belt assembly, replacing it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V392000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The unlatched door may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect for any issues with the KV system, disabling the KV mechanisms if necessary, and update the KV Latch software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V387000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles were previously recalled and repaired under Recall 19V-392, however the repair may not have been completed properly. Unlatched doors may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect for any issues with the KV system, disabling the KV mechanisms if necessary, and update the KV Latch software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The passenger frontal air bag inflator initiator may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the passenger frontal air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V942000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seatbelt pretensioners that may not function properly in the event of a crash. If the front seat seatbelt pretensioners do not function properly, the seat occupants may not be adequately restrained in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front seat belt assemblies, replacing them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V941000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a front passenger air bag inflator initiator that may fail to ignite during a crash, preventing the air bag from deploying. In the event of a crash of sufficient severity, the front passenger air bag may not deploy as required, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Land Rover Range Rover Sport is a highly capable and luxurious SUV that offers impressive performance on and off the road. However, used buyers should budget for potentially higher maintenance expenses and carefully check for unresolved mechanical or electrical gremlins. A detailed inspection by a marque specialist prior to purchase is strongly advised to ensure long-term reliability.
