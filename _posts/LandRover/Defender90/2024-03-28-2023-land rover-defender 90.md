## 2023 Land Rover Defender 90
The 2023 Land Rover Defender 90 is a compact SUV that offers off-road capability and luxurious features. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine that produces 395 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

The Defender 90 is praised for its rugged design, powerful engine, and comfortable interior. However, it has also been criticized for its high price, limited cargo space, and infotainment system issues.

**Engine**

* **P300 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**
    * **Oil Leaks:** The engine may develop oil leaks from the oil pan gasket or valve cover gasket. Symptoms include oil spots under the vehicle or a burning oil smell. The solution is to replace the faulty gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * **Timing Chain Issues:** The timing chain may stretch or break, causing the engine to misfire or lose power. The solution is to replace the timing chain and related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **P400 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors may fail, causing the engine to run rough or misfire. The solution is to replace the faulty fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Turbocharger Wastegate Issues:** The turbocharger wastegate may fail, causing a loss of power or increased exhaust emissions. The solution is to replace the wastegate or the entire turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Issues:** The transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting gears. The solution may involve reprogramming the transmission control module, replacing the transmission fluid, or overhauling the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The vehicle's battery may discharge prematurely due to faulty wiring, parasitic draw, or a failing alternator. The solution is to diagnose and repair the underlying cause. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, freezing, or connectivity problems. The solution may involve updating the software, replacing the head unit, or resetting the system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing the heating or air conditioning to not work properly. The solution may involve replacing the climate control module, sensors, or actuators. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

## Summary
The 2023 Land Rover Defender 90 is a capable off-roader with a powerful engine and luxurious interior. However, it has been plagued by a number of issues, including engine leaks, timing chain problems, fuel injector failures, and electrical problems. Some of these issues can be costly to repair, but others can be addressed by software updates or minor repairs. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues with the Defender 90 vary depending on the specific problem.
