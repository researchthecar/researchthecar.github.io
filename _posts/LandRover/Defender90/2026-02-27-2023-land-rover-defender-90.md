# 2023 Land Rover Defender 90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Land Rover Defender 90 combines iconic styling with modern capability, but prospective buyers should approach with an awareness of reported electrical and structural issues. Owner feedback highlights several recurring concerns, particularly involving the vehicle's mild-hybrid charging system and exterior glass durability. While it delivers impressive performance on and off the road, monitoring potential electrical faults and windshield integrity is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P300)

The entry-level 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and solid everyday drivability, though owners should remain attentive to general maintenance and cooling system integrity.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV (P400)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 mild-hybrid engine delivers around 395 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque for robust performance. It features complex electrical architecture tied to the mild-hybrid system, which has been associated with notable charging converter failures in owner reports.

**DC/DC Converter Electrical Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery charging fault warnings appear on the dashboard, followed by a sudden loss of electrical power, shutting down vehicle systems and causing engine stall while driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed DC/DC converter unit and update software as required.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

Equipped across the lineup, the 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and responsive shifting in varied driving conditions. It generally maintains a solid reputation for mechanical reliability when properly serviced.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators

Electrical accessories and cabin convenience features generally operate as expected, though isolated actuator and wiring faults have been documented.

**Door Lock Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Doors become unresponsive to remote or manual locking commands, preventing proper entry or security.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the malfunctioning door lock actuator assembly.

### Windshield Glass

The upright exterior profile and flat glass design make the vehicle susceptible to stone chips and windshield stress fractures.

**Spontaneous or Fragile Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields develop long stress cracks across the glass unexpectedly or spread rapidly after minor stone impacts.
- **Solution:** Replace the windshield, ensuring proper professional installation to minimize residual stress points.

When shopping for a used 2023 Land Rover Defender 90, buyers should thoroughly inspect the electrical system and verify whether factory recalls or DC/DC converter updates have been completed. While the powertrain options offer strong performance, the upright windshield design and mild-hybrid electrical components warrant careful pre-purchase evaluation.
