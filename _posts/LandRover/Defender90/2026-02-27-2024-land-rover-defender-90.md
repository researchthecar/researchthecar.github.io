# 2024 Land Rover Defender 90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Land Rover Defender 90 delivers iconic styling and impressive capability, but early owner reports point to notable electrical and charging system concerns. Prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance records and verify that all software updates and related campaigns have been properly handled by a certified dealer.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P300)

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 296 horsepower, offering punchy everyday performance. Owners should monitor general vehicle health and ensure routine maintenance schedules are strictly followed.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV (P400)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 mild-hybrid engine delivers around 395 horsepower with smooth power delivery. This powertrain balances strong performance with modern electrified support, though complex supporting electronics require careful attention.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8 (P525 / P525 V8)

The 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine pumps out over 500 horsepower for high-performance capability. It provides robust acceleration and a commanding exhaust note, though premium maintenance and fuel costs are expected.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

A smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission comes paired across all powertrain options. It offers refined gear selection suited for both off-road crawling and highway cruising.

## Steering

### DC-DC Converter & Charging System

Owner complaints highlight vulnerabilities within the charging system and power management modules, which can lead to sudden electrical shutdowns.

**DC-DC Converter Failure and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden electrical fault warnings, total failure of the 12-volt battery system, loss of power steering, and unexpected shifting into neutral while in motion.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a dealership to determine if software updates or hardware replacements for the charging module are required.

## Electrical

### Infotainment & Display Screen

Cabin electronics can experience intermittent screen failures that affect driver visibility aids and convenience features.

**Black or Unresponsive Display Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display screen intermittently goes completely black or fails to boot up upon starting the vehicle, disabling the backup camera.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reboot or visit the dealer for software diagnostic updates.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Upright glass geometry makes the Defender prone to stone strikes and subsequent windshield damage.

**Frequent Windshield Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields develop cracks rapidly from minor stone impacts, raising visibility and safety concerns.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield thoroughly for existing chips or cracks before purchase, and consider glass coverage on your insurance policy.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Manual inspection of restraint anchor points is recommended to ensure occupant safety features are fully intact.

**Unsecured Seat Belt Assembly**
- **Symptoms:** Rear passenger seat belts may be improperly stitched or disconnected from the vehicle frame.
- **Solution:** Manually inspect all seat belt anchor points and webbing integrity during a pre-purchase walkthrough.

The 2024 Land Rover Defender 90 is a highly capable and luxurious choice, but used-car buyers should proceed with caution regarding its complex electrical architecture.
