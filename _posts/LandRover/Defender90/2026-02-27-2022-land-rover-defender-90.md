# 2022 Land Rover Defender 90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Land Rover Defender 90 delivers iconic styling, impressive off-road capability, and modern luxury. However, prospective used buyers should thoroughly inspect vehicle records for recurring electrical and body-trim issues. While owner complaints highlight several frustrating issues, understanding common trouble spots can help you navigate a purchase successfully.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P300)

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering adequate daily drivability and respectable efficiency for everyday commuting and light trail work.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Mild Hybrid (P400)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 features mild-hybrid technology, generating around 395 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. It provides swift acceleration and smooth power delivery, though its complex electrical architecture requires careful maintenance tracking.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8 (P525)

The range-topping 5.0-liter supercharged V8 pumps out 518 horsepower and 461 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally rapid performance and a throaty exhaust note at the expense of heavy fuel consumption.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped across all powertrain options, this ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is generally praised for crisp, smooth shifting and refined operation under various driving conditions.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

The sophisticated air suspension system delivers superb ride comfort and off-road clearance, but component wear and bulletin-related repairs require careful inspection.

**Suspension Component and Control Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Banging noises, warning lights, or unexpected system failure affecting upper control arms, bushings, or leveling.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension arms, bushings, and control modules according to updated manufacturer service bulletins.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Intermittent power steering assist failures have been reported, primarily linked to underlying electrical faults.

**Steering Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Power steering assist fails upon startup or while driving, accompanied by lane-assist error warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering control modules, software calibrations, and electrical connections.

## Electrical

### Charging System

Electrical architecture complaints frequently highlight DC-to-DC converter failures that compromise vehicle operation and safety systems.

**DC-to-DC Converter and Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Warning messages such as 'Stop Safely Electrical Fault Detected' appear, potentially leading to total electrical shutdown and discharged 12V batteries.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty DC-to-DC power converter and inspect charging system components.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting issues center primarily on premature burnout of LED daytime running elements, which can be costly to resolve out of warranty.

**Daytime Running Light (DRL) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The LED daytime running lights fail prematurely, reducing vehicle visibility.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected headlight assembly.

### Windshield and Visibility

The upright windshield design makes it vulnerable to stone chips, and numerous owners report stress-induced cracking under standard weather variations.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields develop stress cracks spontaneously without any noticeable impact from rocks or debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the windshield glass, often requiring recalibration of driver assistance cameras.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Land Rover Defender 90 is a highly capable and luxurious choice, but used-car buyers should proceed with caution. Prioritize models with complete service records, verify that electrical charging updates and recalls have been addressed, and ensure the vehicle is inspected by a qualified technician familiar with modern Land Rover platforms.
