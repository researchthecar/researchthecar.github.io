# 2024 Land Rover Defender 130 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Land Rover Defender 130 offers rugged capability, distinctive styling, and spacious three-row seating. However, owner reports submitted to federal safety authorities indicate a prominent trend of electrical system concerns that prospective buyers should carefully weigh before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Mild Hybrid

This turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-6 engine utilizes mild-hybrid technology to produce approximately 296 to 395 horsepower depending on the specific tune. It provides strong acceleration and towing capability, though owners should monitor the complex associated electrical systems.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8

The high-performance supercharged 5.0-liter V8 engine pushes out over 500 horsepower, delivering rapid acceleration and robust capability. While mechanically potent, this complex powertrain requires diligent maintenance and careful inspection of related support systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with standard all-wheel drive across the lineup, the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifting under normal driving conditions. Overall mechanical durability is generally solid, though electrical faults can occasionally impact shifting functionality.

## Electrical

### DC-DC Converter & Charging System

Owner complaints highlight recurring challenges involving charging modules and power distribution converters, which can lead to sudden loss of vehicle systems.

**DC-DC Converter Failure and 12V Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden electrical fault warnings, complete drainage of the 12V battery, and unexpected vehicle shutdowns while in motion.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician or dealership to replace faulty DC-DC converter hardware and software as needed.

### Infotainment and Display Screens

Dashboard and infotainment electronics can occasionally experience blank screen failures or loss of backup camera visibility.

**Intermittent Black Display Screens**
- **Symptoms:** The main instrument or infotainment display screen suddenly goes dark, temporarily disabling access to key vehicle controls and the backup camera.
- **Solution:** Perform software resets or visit a dealership to apply updated software patches designed to resolve display initialization errors.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

The upright stance of the Defender makes the front glass particularly vulnerable to rock chips and recurring cracks.

**Frequent Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields crack or shatter easily after minor stone impacts from normal road debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield for existing pitting or cracks prior to purchase, and consider comprehensive glass coverage on your insurance policy.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Physical safety restraints require careful pre-purchase verification to ensure proper factory assembly and secure attachment.

**Unsecured Rear Seat Belts**
- **Symptoms:** Rear passenger seat belts may be improperly stitched or disconnected from the vehicle frame.
- **Solution:** Inspect all seating positions to ensure seat belts are securely anchored and properly stitched.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Land Rover Defender 130 is an undeniably capable and luxurious adventure vehicle, but prospective buyers should approach used models with caution. Due to reported electrical system faults, charging converter issues, and screen malfunctions, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the vehicle's electrical health is strongly recommended.
