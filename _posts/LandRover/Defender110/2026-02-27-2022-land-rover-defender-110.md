# 2022 Land Rover Defender 110 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Land Rover Defender 110 is a highly capable and luxurious modern off-roader, but prospective buyers should approach with an awareness of potential electronic and trim durability concerns. While owners generally praise its striking design and road manners, collected owner feedback highlights several recurring trouble spots ranging from minor body trim detachments to complex electrical charging failures.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P300)

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing approximately 296 horsepower, serving as the entry-level powerplant for the Defender line. It provides adequate daily performance, though buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Mild Hybrid (P400)

A 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six featuring mild-hybrid technology, generating around 395 horsepower and robust torque. This powertrain balances strong acceleration with refined highway cruising.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8 (P525)

A high-performance 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine delivering approximately 518 horsepower for maximum towing and acceleration. It offers exhilarating performance at the expense of fuel economy.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An eight-speed automatic transmission paired standard across all engine options and trims. It delivers smooth, prompt gear changes suited to both rugged terrain and highway driving.

### Charging System

The charging system exhibits vulnerabilities surrounding the DC-to-DC converter, which can trigger severe electrical faults and unexpected vehicle shutdowns.

**DC-to-DC Converter and Electrical System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle displays 'Stop Safely Electrical Fault Detected' warnings, followed by low battery alerts, battery short-circuits, and sudden powertrain or system shutdowns.
- **Solution:** Replace the DC-to-DC converter and recharge or replace affected batteries.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

The air suspension and associated control components can experience premature wear and mechanical noise, requiring targeted component replacements.

**Suspension Component and Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or banging noises from the suspension accompanied by upper control arm, bushing, or link wear, eventually leading to system faults.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace worn upper arms, bushings, sway bars, and links according to service bulletins.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering assist failures can temporarily disable power steering support upon startup, impacting maneuverability.

**Steering Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Complete shutdown of power steering upon vehicle startup accompanied by lane assist warning messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnose electronic steering modules and software calibrations; replace faulty steering control components if necessary.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical lighting issues primarily involve premature failure of LED daytime running lights, which can be costly to replace out of warranty.

**Daytime Running Light (DRL) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** LED daytime running lights fail prematurely, leading to diminished front visibility and potential law enforcement stops.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty headlight or DRL assembly.
- **Cost:** $3,000 to $5,000

### Windshield

Windshield vulnerability is a frequent complaint among owners, with glass prone to cracking easily from minor impacts or temperature shifts.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields develop stress cracks or chips unexpectedly from minor impacts or standard temperature fluctuations without apparent heavy debris strikes.
- **Solution:** Replace the windshield glass.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Land Rover Defender 110 delivers exceptional capability and luxury, but prospective used buyers should carefully inspect electronic charging systems, lighting assemblies, and suspension components. Ensuring any pre-owned model has a documented service history and remaining warranty coverage can mitigate the financial impact of complex electrical repairs.
