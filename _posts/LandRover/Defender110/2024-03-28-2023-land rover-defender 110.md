**2023 Land Rover Defender 110**

The 2023 Land Rover Defender 110 is a rugged and capable off-road SUV that combines classic design elements with modern technology. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system that produces 395 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its rugged nature, the Defender 110 has received reports of several issues, ranging from minor annoyances to more serious concerns.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six with Mild Hybrid**
* **Oil Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil leakage from the rear main seal or oil pan gasket, resulting in oil loss and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected seal or gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the turbocharger, leading to reduced engine performance and possible overheating.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**
* **Delayed Gear Shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Electrical**
* **Faulty Electrical Wiring**

    * Solution: Inspection and repair of the affected wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+

**Exterior**
* **Body Panel Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust or corrosion on the body panels, particularly around the wheel wells or beneath the vehicle.
    * Solution: Body panel replacement or treatment with rust-resistant chemicals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
* **Exterior Trim Detachment**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exterior trim pieces, such as side skirts or bumper covers, detaching or becoming loose.
    * Solution: Reattachment or replacement of the affected trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Interior**
* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the panoramic sunroof, causing damage to the interior.
    * Solution: Replacement of the sunroof seals or reinstallation of the sunroof.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues with the infotainment system, including touch screen freezing or unresponsive buttons.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+

**Recalls**
* NHTSA Recall Number: 22V-737
    * Description: Potential for brake fluid leakage, affecting vehicles built between November 11, 2022 and January 6, 2023.
* NHTSA Recall Number: 22V-867
    * Description: Possible fuel leak from the fuel pump flange, affecting vehicles built between April 1, 2022 and July 15, 2022.

**Summary**

The 2023 Land Rover Defender 110 has received mixed reviews from owners. While its off-road capabilities and rugged design are praised, the vehicle has also been plagued by several issues, including oil leaks, turbocharger failures, and electrical problems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, and some may be covered under warranty. However, potential buyers should be aware of the reported problems before making a purchase decision.
