**Introduction:**

The 2022 Land Rover Defender 110 is a rugged SUV with a powerful engine and advanced off-road capabilities. It comes with a choice of two engines: a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six and a 5.0-liter supercharged V8. Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Defender 110 is known for its exceptional off-road performance, luxurious interior, and stylish design. However, owners have reported several issues with the vehicle, including some serious ones.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-Six**

* **Intake Valve Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intake valves become stuck open or closed, causing engine misfires, reduced power, and possibly catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of intake valves and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: LR07693

**5.0-liter Supercharged V8**

* **Water Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The water pump fails, causing overheating and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the water pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: LR07196

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive vibrations felt through the vehicle when accelerating or shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,500 - $5,000
    * Recalls: LR07692

**Suspension**

* **Rear Axle Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged rear axle bushings cause excessive noise, vibration, and handling issues.
    * Solution: Replacement of the rear axle bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Front Suspension Component Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various front suspension components, such as ball joints, control arms, and bushings, fail prematurely.
    * Solution: Replacement of the failed components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500 per component
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning instrument cluster, resulting in loss of critical vehicle information.
    * Solution: Replacement of the instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: LR07765

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system faults, such as faulty parasitic draws.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust Formation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature rust formation on various exterior components, including the body, frame, and suspension.
    * Solution: Rust prevention and treatment measures.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of rust damage.
    * Recalls: LR07766

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof seals.
    * Solution: Replacement of sunroof seals or reinstallation of the sunroof.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes, unresponsive buttons, and software glitches.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Land Rover Defender 110 has received mixed reviews from owners. While it is a capable off-road vehicle with a luxurious interior, it has faced several reliability issues, some of which can be costly to repair. Engine problems, transmission issues, and suspension failures are among the most serious concerns reported. Additionally, electrical gremlins, exterior rust formation, and interior malfunctions have been experienced by some owners. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchase decision.
