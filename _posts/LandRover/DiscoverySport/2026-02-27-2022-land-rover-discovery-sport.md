# 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport offers a stylish, versatile package for luxury compact SUV shoppers, but prospective buyers should approach with careful consideration regarding its long-term reliability. While owner-reported complaints are relatively limited, issues tend to center around unpredictable electronics and occasional mechanical quirks. Reviewing maintenance records and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help mitigate potential ownership surprises.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine typically produces around 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting and light adventuring. It balances respectable performance with luxury refinement, though routine upkeep and timely maintenance are crucial for longevity.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Managed by a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with standard four-wheel drive, this setup aims to optimize both highway fuel efficiency and low-speed traction. Drivers should test shift responsiveness thoroughly during a test drive to ensure smooth operation.

**Gear Engagement Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may occasionally experience instances where the vehicle fails to engage into 'drive' or 'reverse' after startup, leaving the car temporarily immobilized.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the transmission control module and linkage; software updates or hardware repairs may be required.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Parking Sensors

Advanced safety and driver-assistance features provide added convenience, but intermittent electrical glitches can temporarily disable parking aids and visibility cameras.

**Rear Camera Inaccessibility**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display may show a crossed-out red circle, indicating no access to the rear cameras, and proximity warning chimes may abruptly stop working.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, software modules, and connections for faults or moisture intrusion.

## Exterior

### Roof Molding Strips

Exterior trim pieces add a polished finish to the body, but owners have reported isolated incidents of loose components requiring proactive securing.

**Detaching Roof Trim**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior roof molding strips can loosen and unexpectedly detach while driving at highway speeds, creating a hazard for surrounding traffic.
- **Solution:** Inspect roof trim clips and secure or replace loose molding strips with fresh mounting fasteners.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V523000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Improperly restrained occupants have an increased risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport combines premium cabin appointments with capable utility, making it an appealing option on the used market. However, because minor electrical anomalies and isolated powertrain complaints have been noted by owners, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is strongly recommended. Prioritize models with documented service history to ensure maximum peace of mind.
