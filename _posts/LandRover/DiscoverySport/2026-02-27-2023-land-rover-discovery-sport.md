# 2023 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Land Rover Discovery Sport offers a stylish design and versatile cabin, but used-car buyers should review its overall reliability history carefully. While complaints remain relatively limited for this model year, early owner reports highlight a few mechanical and electrical areas to inspect closely before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate acceleration for daily driving. It serves as the standard powerplant for the Discovery Sport lineup, balancing efficiency with traditional Land Rover capability.

### Auto Start-Stop System

Electrical and powertrain integration components govern convenience features like auto start-stop, which can occasionally experience operational glitches.

**Engine Failure to Restart from Start-Stop**
- **Symptoms:** While stopped in traffic with the automatic start-stop feature engaged, the vehicle fails to respond when the accelerator pedal is pressed.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's starting and electrical systems inspected by a qualified technician to check for software glitches or battery management faults.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission comes paired with standard all-wheel drive across all trims. It aims to optimize fuel economy on the highway, though proper maintenance is essential for smooth operation over time.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Service Brakes

The hydraulic service brakes provide stopping power for the SUV, though owners have reported rare instances of component cracking and vibration.

**Brake Vibration and Component Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Depressing the brake pedal results in erratic vehicle shaking, often linked upon inspection to cracked brake components.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers for premature wear or cracking, and replace damaged hardware immediately.

## Exterior

### Roof Molding

Exterior body trim and moldings complete the vehicle's styling, though certain adhesive or clip attachments may require reinforcement.

**Detaching Roof Trim**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior roof moldings loosen or detach completely from the vehicle body with little to no external force applied.
- **Solution:** Re-secure or replace loose roof molding clips and trim pieces to prevent them from flying off while driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V523000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Improperly restrained occupants have an increased risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V016000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the touchscreen software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2023 Land Rover Discovery Sport, ensure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is performed to check the brakes, electrical systems, and exterior trim. Addressing these specific owner-reported quirks early can help secure a more dependable ownership experience.
