# 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport

### Overview
The 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport, a compact luxury SUV, aims to blend premium comfort with off-road capability. It offers a refined interior, advanced technology, and a choice of efficient powertrains. While generally well-received for its design and practicality, like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining common problem areas across its available configurations to provide a clear picture for potential owners and service professionals. We will delve into engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems.

## Engine

### 2.0L Ingenium Turbocharged I4 (P250)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, seals, or the water pump.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes, or reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, followed by repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, with most reported issues being relatively minor and addressable. The primary concerns revolve around potential coolant leaks in the P250 engine variant and occasional hesitations or jerks in the 9-speed automatic transmission. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system's responsiveness and intermittent battery drain, are also noted but not pervasive. Exterior and interior components, along with the suspension and the MHEV powertrain, appear to be robust. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and ensure regular maintenance, especially concerning the cooling system and transmission software.
