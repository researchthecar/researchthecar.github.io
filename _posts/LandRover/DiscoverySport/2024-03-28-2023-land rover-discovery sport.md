**Introduction**

The 2023 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a compact luxury SUV known for its off-road capabilities and sleek design. It comes with two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission, and a 2.0L turbocharged diesel engine mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Discovery Sport's handling and comfortable interior are highly praised, while its poor fuel economy and limited cargo space have been criticized.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Issue:** Excessive oil consumption
* **Description/Symptoms:** The engine burns through oil rapidly, requiring frequent top-ups.
* **Solution:** Replace piston rings
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

* **Issue:** Timing belt failure
* **Description/Symptoms:** The timing belt breaks prematurely, causing catastrophic engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replace the timing belt and tensioner
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Diesel Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Issue:** Transmission slipping
* **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission hesitates or slips between gears, causing a loss of power.
* **Solution:** Replace or rebuild the transmission
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $4,000 - $8,000+
* **Recalls:** 22V-798

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Issue:** Gearbox failure
* **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission fails to shift gears or makes grinding noises.
* **Solution:** Replace the gearbox
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $6,000 - $10,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Issue:** Front suspension clunking
* **Description/Symptoms:** A clunking noise is heard from the front suspension when driving over bumps.
* **Solution:** Replace lower control arms
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

* **Issue:** Rear suspension sagging
* **Description/Symptoms:** The rear of the vehicle sags due to worn-out rear springs.
* **Solution:** Replace rear springs
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Issue:** Battery drain
* **Description/Symptoms:** The battery drains rapidly, even when the vehicle is parked.
* **Solution:** Replace the alternator or battery
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

* **Issue:** Electrical gremlins
* **Description/Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunction intermittently, such as lighting, windows, and infotainment system.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair specific electrical components
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Issue:** Headlight condensation
* **Description/Symptoms:** Moisture accumulates inside the headlights.
* **Solution:** Replace headlight assembly
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

* **Issue:** Rust on body panels
* **Description/Symptoms:** Premature rust forms on body panels, especially around wheel wells and undercarriage.
* **Solution:** Repair or replace affected body panels
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $5,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Issue:** Infotainment system freezing
* **Description/Symptoms:** The infotainment system fails to respond or freezes during operation.
* **Solution:** Update software or replace infotainment unit
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

* **Issue:** Panoramic sunroof water leak
* **Description/Symptoms:** Water leaks into the interior through the panoramic sunroof.
* **Solution:** Replace or seal the sunroof gasket
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Land Rover Discovery Sport has a number of potential issues across various components. While certain issues like oil consumption and timing belt failure can be severe and costly, it's important to note that the frequency of these problems may vary depending on factors such as driving habits and maintenance practices. The occurrence of issues can also vary between individual vehicles, and some owners may not experience any major problems. It is recommended to research specific issues and their potential impact before purchasing this vehicle.
