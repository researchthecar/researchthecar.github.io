**Introduction:**

The 2016 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a compact luxury SUV offered with two engine and transmission options: a 2.0L turbocharged Ingenium diesel engine with a 9-speed automatic transmission and a 2.0L turbocharged Ingenium gasoline engine with a 6-speed manual or 9-speed automatic transmission. The diesel engine produces 180 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque, while the gasoline engine produces 240 horsepower and 251 lb-ft of torque. The Discovery Sport is known for its comfortable interior, spacious cargo area, and off-road capabilities, but it has also received criticism for its unreliable infotainment system and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Ingenium Diesel Engine**

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve restricts exhaust flow, leading to reduced performance, increased emissions, and potential engine damage.
- Solution: Replacement of the EGR valve.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
- Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Ingenium Gasoline Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
- Description/Symptoms: A faulty intake manifold gasket can cause a vacuum leak, resulting in engine performance issues, rough idling, and increased emissions.
- Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold gasket.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
- Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: A malfunctioning mechatronic unit can cause gears to slip, shift irregularly, or fail to engage, leading to transmission issues and loss of power.
- Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $4000 - $6000+
- Recalls: LR068628, LR069927

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Arm Ball Joint Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged front suspension arm ball joints can cause clunking or rattling noises, decreased handling, and premature tire wear.
- Solution: Replacement of the suspension arm ball joints.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
- Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged rear shock absorbers can lead to a rough ride, poor handling, and decreased braking performance.
- Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
- Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
- Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, unresponsive, or display errors, affecting navigation, audio, and other features.
- Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1200+
- Recalls: LR071890, LR073198, LR077054

* **Electrical Harness Failure:**
- Description/Symptoms: A damaged or faulty electrical harness can cause intermittent electrical issues, including power loss, lighting malfunctions, or other electrical problems.
- Solution: Repair or replacement of the damaged harness.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
- Recalls: LR072768

**Exterior**

* **Cracked Sunroof Glass:**
- Description/Symptoms: A weak point in the sunroof design can lead to cracking of the glass, potentially allowing water leaks and wind noise.
- Solution: Replacement of the sunroof glass.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $1800+
- Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty Door Handles:**
- Description/Symptoms: The door handles may break or become stuck, making it difficult to open the doors.
- Solution: Replacement of the door handles.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
- Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty Climate Control System:**
- Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, leading to uneven cooling or heating, condensation on the windows, or other issues.
- Solution: Repair or replacement of the climate control components.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
- Recalls: None Found

* **Loose Center Console:**
- Description/Symptoms: The center console may become loose or rattle, causing a distracting noise.
- Solution: Tightening or replacement of the console.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
- Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Land Rover Discovery Sport has experienced a range of issues, including engine and transmission problems, suspension malfunctions, electrical failures, and exterior and interior issues. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as mechatronic unit failure and infotainment system malfunctions, can be costly and time-consuming to repair. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
