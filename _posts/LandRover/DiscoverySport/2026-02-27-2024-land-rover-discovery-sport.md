# 2024 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Land Rover Discovery Sport offers a blend of luxury, compact practicality, and standard all-wheel-drive capability for shoppers seeking a premium small SUV. Because this model year is relatively new to the used market, long-term reliability trends are still emerging, and prospective buyers should review available vehicle history carefully. While ownership feedback remains limited, understanding the available powertrain options and typical vehicle systems will help you make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive acceleration for daily driving. It pairs standard forced induction with refined power delivery, though proper maintenance and timely oil changes are critical for long-term longevity.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission utilizes a wide gear spread to optimize highway efficiency and power delivery across various driving conditions. Buyers should ensure smooth gear transitions during a test drive, as proper software calibration is vital for optimal drivability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V163000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A body structure with reduced structural strength may not adequately protect passengers in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the right side, lower A-pillar bolt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V678000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the NFSM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2024 Land Rover Discovery Sport, prioritize models with a documented service history and remaining factory warranty coverage. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist is strongly recommended to verify mechanical health and proper electronic function.
