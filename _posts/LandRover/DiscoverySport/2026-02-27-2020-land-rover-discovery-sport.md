# 2020 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Land Rover Discovery Sport combines luxury styling and practical utility, but prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability trends carefully. While official data indicates a low volume of total complaints, early adopters have noted sporadic powertrain hesitations and electrical glitches that warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P250)

The base 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 246 horsepower and is paired with a smooth shift layout. It provides adequate power for daily commuting, though owners note occasional throttle response delays.

**Acceleration Hesitation and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the vehicle occasionally hesitates, stumbles, or stalls when attempting to accelerate from a stop, raising safety concerns in traffic.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician check for engine control module software updates or throttle body carbon buildup.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Mild Hybrid (P300)

The higher-output 2.0-liter turbocharged mild-hybrid powertrain pushes around 296 horsepower for more spirited performance. It integrates a 48-volt electrical system to assist with efficiency and responsiveness.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power through a standard all-wheel-drive system across all trims. It generally offers smooth highway cruising, though low-speed gear selection can occasionally feel busy.

## Steering

### Suspension and Steering Components

The multi-link suspension delivers a comfortable and composed ride quality on rough surfaces, though heavy vehicle weight can stress wear items.

**Premature Alignment and Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced sudden mechanical handling disruptions due to accelerated wear on bushings, bearings, and wheel alignment settings.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension joints, control arm bushings, and wheel alignment parameters regularly during routine maintenance.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Body Control Software

Cabin electronics manage luxury features and driver assists, but software bugs can cause intermittent accessory failures.

**Software and Accessory Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note various electronic disruptions affecting the infotainment screen, climate control systems, and associated convenience features.
- **Solution:** Update the vehicle's software modules to the latest manufacturer firmware version at a dealership or specialized service center.

### Blind Spot Monitoring

Advanced driver assistance features enhance safety on paper, though sensor reliability can occasionally require recalibration.

**Intermittent System Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** The blind spot monitoring system may randomly turn itself off during operation without active warning lights, increasing lane-change risks.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sensor modules and check for relevant software updates or diagnostic trouble codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V668000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V751000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A software error may cause the instrument cluster to randomly alternate the speedometer and odometer units between Miles Per Hour (MPH) and Kilometers Per Hour (KM/H) without driver input to the display unit. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Control and Displays.".
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V683000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short-circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Land Rover Discovery Sport offers a refined cabin and capable handling, but buyers should approach with attentive care. Prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronic software versions, suspension wear, and powertrain responsiveness to avoid unexpected repair bills.
