**2019 Land Rover Discovery Sport**

The 2019 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a compact luxury SUV that combines off-road capability with everyday practicality. It offers a choice of two engine options: a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder with 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque and a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder diesel with 180 horsepower and 318 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

People love the Discovery Sport for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive off-road capabilities. However, it has received some complaints about its reliability and build quality.

**Engine**

**Turbocharged 2.0-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Oil Leaks from Valve Cover Gasket**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning smell and oil spots under the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharged 2.0-liter Four-Cylinder Diesel**

* **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve, leading to reduced engine performance, black smoke from the exhaust, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Nine-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Jerking, slipping, or delayed shifting.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the front suspension bushings, causing clunking noises and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining rapidly or failing to hold charge.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or check for electrical system issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the interior through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: System freezing, crashing, or displaying errors.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a capable and luxurious vehicle, but it has received some reports of reliability issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and maintain their vehicles regularly to minimize the risk of costly repairs.
