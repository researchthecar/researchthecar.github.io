# 2021 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Land Rover Discovery Sport, buyers will find a stylish and capable compact luxury SUV that offers modern tech and standard all-wheel drive. While owner reports to NHTSA are relatively limited, prospective buyers should review available feedback carefully, as some drivers have noted critical powertrain and electrical concerns. Understanding both the available engine options and common owner-reported quirks will help ensure a more confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (P250)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces roughly 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It pairs exclusively with all-wheel drive, though buyers should watch for occasional ignition-related issues reported by some owners.

**Cylinder Misfires**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience sudden engine misfires and rough running conditions at moderate mileage.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the ignition coils, spark plugs, and fuel injectors to diagnose and replace failing components.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission comes standard across all trims, routing power smoothly through the all-wheel-drive system under normal operating conditions. While generally responsive, a small number of owners have flagged concerning shifting behavior and propulsion loss.

**Unexpected Shifting and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly drop out of gear while stopped at traffic lights or stall completely, requiring a restart.
- **Solution:** Update the transmission control module software or have the transmission inspected by a specialist for internal wear.

## Electrical

### Ignition and Starting System

Monitors vehicle power distribution and keyless ignition functions, though occasional software or switch failures can disrupt starting.

**Failure to Power On**
- **Symptoms:** The push-button ignition may fail to activate, leaving the vehicle completely unresponsive to the key.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the starter switch, key fob recognition system, or electrical harness, and replace faulty modules as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V668000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Land Rover Discovery Sport blends luxury appeal with versatile capability, but used-car buyers should proceed with a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to powertrain responsiveness and electrical accessories during a test drive to catch any hidden issues before finalizing the purchase.
