**Introduction**

The 2021 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a compact premium SUV that offers a refined and capable driving experience. It is offered with a choice of two turbocharged engines and four-wheel drive. The P250 engine produces 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, while the P300 engine produces 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Discovery Sport has a spacious and well-equipped interior, along with a host of advanced safety and driver assistance features. While it is generally a reliable vehicle, there are some common issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **P250 Engine**
    **Oil Leak**
    Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the rear main oil seal
    Solution: Replace the rear main oil seal
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    Recalls: N/A

* **P300 Engine**
    **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    **Shifting Issues**
    Description/Symptoms: Transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting gears
    Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission control unit
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    Recalls: NHTSA ID: 21V961000

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension**
    **Leaking Air Struts**
    Description/Symptoms: Air suspension may leak air, causing the vehicle to sag or ride unevenly
    Solution: Replace the air struts
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000 per strut
    Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System**
    **Battery Drain**
    Description/Symptoms: Battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is parked
    Solution: Identify and repair the electrical drain, often caused by a faulty module or accessory
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof**
    **Leaking Sunroof**
    Description/Symptoms: Sunroof may leak water into the cabin
    Solution: Replace the sunroof seals or adjust the sunroof alignment
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System**
    **Glitching Infotainment System**
    Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system may freeze, crash, or display errors
    Solution: Update the infotainment system software or replace the infotainment unit
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a capable and comfortable SUV. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners include oil leaks, transmission shifting issues, and electrical system problems. While some of these issues can be costly to fix, most are relatively minor and can be resolved with a software update or part replacement.
