# 2016 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Land Rover Discovery Sport, prospective buyers will find a stylish and capable compact luxury SUV that combines traditional British off-road pedigree with everyday family practicality. However, used car shoppers should approach this model with a careful inspection mindset, as owners have logged recurring complaints concerning serious mechanical, braking, and structural concerns that require thorough pre-purchase scrutiny.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces around 240 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration suitable for both city driving and highway cruising. While efficient for its size, buyers should watch for potential turbo-manifold and valvetrain issues as the vehicle ages.

**Camshaft Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The engine shuts down completely and loses all power while driving due to a loose camshaft actuator.
- **Solution:** Inspect and re-torque or replace the camshaft actuator components.

**Cracked Turbo-Manifold**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light illuminates and noxious exhaust fumes enter the passenger cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty cracked turbo-manifold.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power through an advanced all-wheel-drive system, aiming to optimize both fuel efficiency and off-road traction. Buyers should thoroughly test-shift this transmission through all gears to check for hesitation or fault messages.

**Gearbox Fault and Sudden Coasting**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly coasts from a stop or while driving accompanied by a gearbox fault warning message.
- **Solution:** Diagnose transmission control module and internal components; software updates or hardware repairs may be necessary.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides firm stopping power under normal conditions, but vulnerable brake lines require regular professional inspection.

**Brake Line Rupture**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal feels spongy, low brake fluid warnings activate, and brake lines rupture resulting in a complete loss of braking function.
- **Solution:** Inspect metal brake lines for corrosion or wear and replace ruptured lines immediately.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electric power steering system delivers light and responsive handling at low speeds, though unexpected motor mount failures can cause a total loss of power assist.

**Electric Power Steering Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of power steering assistance accompanied by a metal crunch sound when turning the steering wheel.
- **Solution:** Replace the sheared mounting bolts or the electric power steering assist assembly.

## Exterior

### Structure and Door Latches

Cabin hardware and structural components generally offer high-end comfort, but door latches and locking mechanisms should be rigorously checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Door Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors fail to latch properly and can unexpectedly fly open while the vehicle is in motion without warning dash lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door latch mechanisms and actuators.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V889000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the fasteners were to fail causing the knuckle to separate, vehicle handling may be affected, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the hardware that connects the front lower control arm to the knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V274000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, the license plate illumination lamp may fall out. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Without license plate illumination, the vehicle may be difficult to identify at night.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace both rear license plate lamp housings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Land Rover Discovery Sport offers an appealing blend of luxury, utility, and all-weather capability for used car buyers. However, because owners have reported critical mechanical concerns such as brake line failures, power steering bolt shearing, and powertrain issues, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist is strongly advised.
