# 2016 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Land Rover Discovery Sport combines premium British styling, versatile interior space, and robust off-road capability in a compact luxury package. However, prospective buyers should approach the used market with careful inspection, as owner reports point to potential areas of mechanical and electrical concern. Understanding the vehicle's powertrain options and common trouble spots can help ensure a more confident purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving, though proper maintenance and oil change history are critical for long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The ZF 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth highway cruising and optimizes fuel efficiency across a wide range of speeds. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure crisp shifting behavior without hesitation or harsh engagement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V889000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the fasteners were to fail causing the knuckle to separate, vehicle handling may be affected, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the hardware that connects the front lower control arm to the knuckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V274000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, the license plate illumination lamp may fall out. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Without license plate illumination, the vehicle may be difficult to identify at night.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace both rear license plate lamp housings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Land Rover Discovery Sport offers appealing luxury and capability, but used-car buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection. Verifying service records and checking all electrical and powertrain functions will help mitigate potential ownership risks.
