# 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport, buyers will find a stylish and capable compact luxury SUV. However, a review of owner feedback highlights potential areas of concern regarding powertrain behavior and electrical stability that prospective owners should inspect closely.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine provides balanced performance for everyday driving. Buyers should thoroughly verify engine health and service history during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Loss of Motive Power and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine hesitation, sudden loss of motive power, or complete stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a qualified technician is required to identify faulty ignition, fuel system, or sensor components.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The nine-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth cruising gears, though some owners have experienced electronic and mechanical shifting irregularities.

**Unexpected Shifting and Gearbox Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may occasionally shift unexpectedly into neutral while driving or display gearbox defect warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission control modules, software calibrations, and mechanical linkages for wear or failure.

### All-Wheel Drive System

The all-wheel-drive system provides confident traction in diverse weather conditions, but drivetrain components require attentive maintenance.

**Power Transfer Unit and Shifter Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted issues with the gear shifter failing to engage park properly or unusual noises and failures originating from the power transfer unit.
- **Solution:** Check the power transfer unit and gear shifter assembly for fluid leaks, mechanical wear, or electronic faults.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Sensor Systems

Onboard electronics manage a wide array of convenience and safety features, making thorough electrical testing essential before purchase.

**Intermittent Warning Lights and Sensor Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of unexpected warning lights, persistent check engine indicators, and false coolant level sensor warnings have been documented by owners.
- **Solution:** Scan the vehicle computer for stored diagnostic codes and inspect wiring harnesses and sensor modules.

## Exterior

### Body Trim and Panels

Exterior styling is sleek and modern, though buyers should verify that all trim attachments remain secure.

**Loose Roof Panel Trim**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior trim pieces, such as roof panel molding, may become loose or detach while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect exterior clips and weatherstripping to ensure all body trim is securely fastened.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V087000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport offers attractive luxury features and capable performance, but prospective buyers should approach with care. Conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the powertrain, drivetrain components, and electrical systems will help minimize unexpected repair surprises.
