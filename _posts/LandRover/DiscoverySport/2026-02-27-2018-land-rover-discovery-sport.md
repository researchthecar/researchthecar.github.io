# 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport combines premium styling and versatile capability in a compact luxury SUV package. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating the powertrain choices and monitoring known electrical or body accessory functions are key to a confident purchase. While luxury vehicles demand attentive maintenance, understanding common owner-reported grievances can help you spot potential trouble spots before bringing one home.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (Ingenium)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 237 horsepower and 251 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration suited for daily driving, though buyers should verify meticulous oil change history to ensure longevity.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly across all four wheels in standard all-wheel-drive configurations. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure crisp shifts and immediate engagement across all gears.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V087000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Discovery+Sport&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport, prioritize models with a documented service history and a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection. Checking all electrical accessories, infotainment functions, and powertrain responsiveness during a test drive will help ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
