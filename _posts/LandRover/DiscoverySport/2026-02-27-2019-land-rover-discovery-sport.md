# 2019 Land Rover Discovery Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Land Rover Discovery Sport, buyers will find a stylish and capable compact luxury SUV. However, owner reports submitted to safety agencies highlight certain powertrain, electrical, and structural quirks that potential buyers should evaluate carefully during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (Ingenium)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 Ingenium engine produces around 246 horsepower and provides brisk acceleration. Buyers should ensure proper maintenance records and thorough diagnostic testing, as complex engine electronics can occasionally trigger unexpected fault messages.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle unexpectedly shifts into neutral or displays unexpected gear selection messages while operating.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician check for powertrain control module software updates or transmission control issues.

**Loss of Power and Gearbox Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners experienced sudden 'gearbox fault' warnings accompanied by a total loss of engine power while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic scanning to determine whether the fault stems from electrical management, software glitches, or internal mechanical failure.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission routes power through an all-wheel-drive system. Smooth operation is vital, as internal electronic or mechanical faults can occasionally lead to expensive repairs.

**Sudden Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Operators have reported sudden dashboard warnings regarding gearbox failure, leading to an immediate shutdown or immobilization of the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition and consult a specialist for diagnostic codes if shifting irregularities occur.

## Electrical

### Charging and Infotainment Systems

electrical issues in the Discovery Sport can range from minor infotainment glitches to more severe charging system concerns.

**Charging System Stalls**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of charging system warning lights accompanied by unexpected vehicle stalling while in motion.
- **Solution:** Test the alternator, battery health, and electrical charging circuits thoroughly.

**Bluetooth Audio Interference**
- **Symptoms:** Phone callers intermittently hear a loud horn sound while the user is driving or navigating turns.
- **Solution:** Perform infotainment software updates at an authorized service center.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Mechanisms

Interior components are generally plush, though physical latch mechanisms require verification during a used car inspection.

**Unexpected Door Opening**
- **Symptoms:** The rear door latching mechanism has been reported to fail, causing the door to fly open while the vehicle is in motion without prior dashboard warning.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door latches or locking assemblies immediately.

### Structure and Body Welds

Exterior body panels and hatches should be checked for alignment issues and unusual noises during a test drive.

**Rear Hatch Body Weld Rattle**
- **Symptoms:** Rear hatch body welds can fail over time, creating an audible rattling noise from the back of the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Apply sound-deadening material or consult a professional body shop for structural weld reinforcement.

The Land Rover Discovery Sport offers compelling styling and utility, but prospective buyers must approach used models with diligence.
