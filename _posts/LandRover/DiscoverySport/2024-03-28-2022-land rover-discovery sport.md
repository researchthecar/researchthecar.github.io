**2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport**

The 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a compact luxury SUV that offers a combination of practicality and off-road capability. It is available with two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 with 246 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 with 296 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

The Discovery Sport is well-regarded for its comfortable interior, spacious cargo area, and capable off-road performance. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's reliability.

**Engine**
- **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (246 hp)**
    - **Turbocharger Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail prematurely, causing a loss of power and potential engine damage. Solution: Replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+. Recalls: None Found
    - **Timing Chain Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may stretch or break prematurely, causing the engine to lose power or stall. Solution: Replace the timing chain and associated components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000. Recalls: None Found
- **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (296 hp)**
    - **Fuel Pump Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The fuel pump may fail prematurely, causing the engine to stall or hesitate. Solution: Replace the fuel pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200. Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
- **8-Speed Automatic Transmission:** Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift harshly, slip, or shudder. Solution: Repair or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000. Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **Front Suspension Lower Control Arm Wear:** Description/Symptoms: The lower control arm bushings may wear prematurely, causing a clunking noise when turning or braking. Solution: Replace the lower control arms. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $750. Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
- **Electrical Faults:** Description/Symptoms: Various electrical faults may occur, including problems with the infotainment system, power windows, and exterior lights. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue. Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Rear Window Trim Panel Detachment:** Description/Symptoms: The trim panel around the rear window may detach, causing a water leak. Solution: Replace the trim panel. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400. Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
- **Panoramic Roof Leak:** Description/Symptoms: The panoramic roof may leak water into the cabin, especially during heavy rain. Solution: Apply sealant or replace the panoramic roof panel. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000. Recalls: None Found
- **Climate Control System Malfunction:** Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the interior to become too hot or too cold. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500. Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Land Rover Discovery Sport has a number of potential issues, with the most severe being engine and transmission failures. These issues can be costly to repair and may affect the vehicle's reliability. However, it is important to note that not all Discovery Sport owners will experience these problems.
