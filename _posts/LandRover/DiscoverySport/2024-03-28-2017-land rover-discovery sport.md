**Introduction:**

The 2017 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a compact luxury SUV that offers a blend of sophistication and off-road capability. Its engine options include a 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder, a 3.0-liter supercharged V6, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged diesel. The vehicle is available with a 6-speed manual, 8-speed automatic, or 9-speed automatic transmission, depending on the engine option. While the Discovery Sport has received praise for its stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and advanced technology, it has also faced criticism for its reliability issues.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged 4-cylinder**

* **Intake Manifold Valve Sticking:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Engine stalls or rough idle, reduced performance, increased fuel consumption
    * **Solution:** Replace intake manifold
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Engine noise (rattling), reduced performance, possible engine damage
    * **Solution:** Replace timing chain tensioner and timing belt
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, oil leak, whistling noise
    * **Solution:** Replace turbocharger
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $4,000+

**3.0-liter Supercharged V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Syncro Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise
    * **Solution:** Replace syncro
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,200 - $1,800+
* **Clutch Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears
    * **Solution:** Replace clutch
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+

**8-speed Automatic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission won't shift, shuddering or hesitation during shifting
    * **Solution:** Replace mechatronic unit
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $4,000 - $6,000+
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission slipping, vibration during acceleration, shuddering
    * **Solution:** Replace torque converter
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+

**9-speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Suspension won't adjust, loss of ride height, reduced handling
    * **Solution:** Replace air suspension components (air bags, compressor, lines)
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Strut or Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Bumpy ride, reduced handling, uneven tire wear
    * **Solution:** Replace struts or shocks
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Battery not charging, warning lights, dim headlights
    * **Solution:** Replace alternator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
* **Battery Draining:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Battery goes dead after sitting, rapid battery discharge
    * **Solution:** Diagnose and repair electrical drain (usually caused by parasitic draw)
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Water leaking into the cabin from the panoramic sunroof
    * **Solution:** Replace or repair sunroof seal
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Power Liftgate Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Power liftgate won't open or close properly
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace liftgate motor or sensors
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** AC or heater not working properly, temperature fluctuations
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace climate control module or blend door actuator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+
* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, software glitches, navigation issues
    * **Solution:** Update software, replace touchscreen or infotainment module
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+

**Summary:**

The 2017 Land Rover Discovery Sport has been met with mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While it offers a compelling combination of style, comfort, and off-road capability, it has also faced numerous issues across multiple components, including its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and exterior. Some of the reported issues can be severe and costly to repair, which may deter potential buyers or raise concerns for current owners. It's recommended to thoroughly research and consider these potential problems before making a purchase decision or owning one of these vehicles.
