**Introduction:**

The 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport is a compact luxury SUV that combines off-road capabilities with an upscale interior. It is powered by a range of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4, a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 diesel, and a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 hybrid. The Discovery Sport offers a comfortable and spacious interior, but it has been criticized for its limited cargo space and fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
- **Leaking oil pan:** Oil leaks from the oil pan can occur due to a faulty gasket or a damaged pan. Symptoms include low oil levels, oil stains under the car, and a burning oil smell. The solution is to replace the oil pan gasket or repair the damaged pan. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500+.
- **Faulty fuel injector:** A faulty fuel injector can cause a decrease in power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption. The solution is to replace the faulty fuel injector. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $600+.

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Diesel**
- **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail due to a lack of lubrication or a mechanical issue. Symptoms include a loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, and a whistling noise. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated cost to fix: $1,500 - $2,500+.

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic Transmission**
- **Shifting problems:** Some owners have reported experiencing shifting problems, such as delayed shifts, hard shifts, or slipping gears. The solution may involve updating the transmission software or replacing the transmission. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $2,000+.

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension:**
- **Strut mounts:** The strut mounts can wear out and cause a knocking or rattling noise when driving over bumps. The solution is to replace the strut mounts. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+.

**Electrical**

**Battery:**
- **Premature battery failure:** Some owners have reported experiencing premature battery failure. The solution is to replace the battery. Estimated cost to fix: $150 - $300+.

**Exterior**

**Body Panels:**
- **Dents or scratches:** Dents or scratches can occur due to road debris, collisions, or vandalism. The solution is to repair or replace the damaged body panels. Estimated cost to fix: Varies depending on the extent of the damage.

**Interior**

**Climate Control:**
- **Malfunctioning AC compressor:** The AC compressor can fail due to a refrigerant leak or a mechanical issue. Symptoms include a lack of cold air or warm air being blown. The solution is to repair or replace the AC compressor. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,500+.

**Dashboard:**
- **Faulty instrument cluster:** The instrument cluster may malfunction and display incorrect information. The solution is to replace the instrument cluster. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+.

**Summary:**

The 2018 Land Rover Discovery Sport has a variety of issues, including engine oil leaks, fuel injector problems, turbocharger failures, shifting problems, worn strut mounts, premature battery failure, and malfunctioning AC compressors and instrument clusters. The severity of the issues varies, and some can be quite costly to fix. Overall, the Discovery Sport has a number of issues that potential buyers should be aware of before purchasing.
