**2016 Land Rover LR4**

The 2016 Land Rover LR4 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its off-road capabilities and spacious interior. It comes with a 3.0L V6 engine producing 340 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The LR4 received positive reviews for its comfortable ride, powerful engine, and impressive off-road performance. However, it has also been criticized for its high price, lack of fuel efficiency, and some reliability concerns.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**

**Intake Manifold Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the intake manifold can lead to coolant leaks, loss of power, and engine overheating.
Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-speed Automatic**

**Shifting Hesitation**
Description/Symptoms: Delayed or hesitant gear shifting, particularly during acceleration or deceleration.
Solution: Transmission software update or replacement of the transmission control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air suspension system can cause uneven ride height, reduced handling, and potential tire wear.
Solution: Replacement of air suspension components, such as compressors, sensors, or air springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is parked and turned off.
Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty electrical components, such as the alternator, starter, or battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Sunroof Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof during heavy rain or while washing the vehicle.
Solution: Replacement of sunroof seals or gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Failure or malfunction of the climate control system, resulting in inadequate heating or cooling.
Solution: Repair or replacement of climate control components, such as the blower motor, control module, or sensors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Land Rover LR4 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has some known issues that can be costly to fix. The engine and suspension issues are the most severe and can lead to significant performance problems or safety concerns. The transmission and electrical issues are more common but typically less expensive to address. The exterior and interior issues are generally minor but can still be frustrating for owners. Overall, the LR4 is a capable off-roader with a comfortable and spacious interior, but its reliability concerns should be considered before purchasing.
