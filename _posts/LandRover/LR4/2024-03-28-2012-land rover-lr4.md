## 2012 Land Rover LR4

The 2012 Land Rover LR4 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its off-road capabilities and spacious interior. It is powered by a 5.0L V8 engine that produces 375 horsepower and 375 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

## Engine

**5.0L V8**

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from the engine, especially at startup or acceleration.
    - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Valve Seat Recession**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, or misfires.
    - Solution: Install new valve seats or replace the affected engine components.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    - Recalls: LR019329, LR019359

## Transmission

**6-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Shift Solenoid Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears.
    - Solution: Replace the failed solenoid.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: LR021284, LR021285
- **Torque Converter Shudder**
    - Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration.
    - Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

- **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Suspension sags or does not adjust properly.
    - Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    - Recalls: LR017014, LR020785

## Electrical

- **Battery Drain**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges rapidly when the vehicle is parked.
    - Solution: Investigate and repair the cause of the parasitic drain, such as a faulty module or accessory.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Electrical Module Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning electrical systems, such as navigation, climate control, or stability control.
    - Solution: Replace or repair the affected module.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    - Recalls: LR016782, LR016783

## Exterior

- **Door Handle Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Door handles do not open or break off.
    - Solution: Replace the door handle assembly.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    - Recalls: LR019017

## Interior

- **Climate Control Malfunction**
    - Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating does not work properly.
    - Solution: Repair or replace the climate control system components.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: LR022505, LR023579
- **Sunroof Drainage Clogged**
    - Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof.
    - Solution: Clean the sunroof drainage tubes and ensure proper drainage.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    - Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2012 Land Rover LR4 is a capable off-roader with a spacious and luxurious interior. However, it has experienced some reliability issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. These issues can be costly to repair, but the severity and frequency of the problems vary. If you are considering buying a used LR4, it is essential to factor the potential for expensive repairs into your decision.
