# 2016 Land Rover LR4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Land Rover LR4, buyers will find a highly capable luxury SUV renowned for its iconic styling, exceptional off-road prowess, and spacious interior. However, prospective owners should approach this luxury vehicle with eyes wide open regarding maintenance and potential mechanical complexities. Owner reports point to certain recurring trouble areas, particularly within the cooling system and electronic parking brake components, which require careful pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine delivers roughly 340 horsepower and robust low-end torque, giving the LR4 strong acceleration and towing capabilities. While generally smooth and powerful, this engine demands diligent cooling system maintenance to prevent catastrophic overheating.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant warning lights illuminate on the dashboard, sometimes accompanied by overheating or fluid pooling under the vehicle due to failing plastic cooling pipes.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling lines and plastic crossover pipes for leaks; replace degraded plastic components with updated parts.
- **Cost:** $18,397.76

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission shifts smoothly and pairs well with the vehicle's full-time four-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid checks and software updates help ensure long-term durability.

## Brakes

### Electronic Parking Brake and Hydraulic Brakes

Brake system complaints focus primarily on electronic parking brake module locking issues and hydraulic fluid leaks.

**Electronic Parking Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic parking brake module fails, locking the rear wheels in place or refusing to disengage, accompanied by warning messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic parking brake module and replace the unit if internal binding or electrical failure occurs.
- **Cost:** $1,000

**Brake Fluid Loss and Pedals Traveling to Floor**
- **Symptoms:** Low brake fluid warning illumination or sudden soft brake pedal travel, potentially leading to reduced braking performance.
- **Solution:** Thoroughly inspect brake lines, master cylinder, and calipers for leaks or hydraulic pressure loss.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V635000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Mounting Plate/Flange](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=LR4&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel outlet flange, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V444000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=LR4&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have driver side frontal air bags that may not inflate properly. An air bag that does not inflate properly may increase the risk of injury to the driver in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver side frontal air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Land Rover LR4 offers premium luxury and unmatched utility, but ownership costs can be high if major components fail. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a specialist familiar with British luxury vehicles, paying close attention to the cooling system, brake electronics, and service history.
