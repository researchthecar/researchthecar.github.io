# 2016 Land Rover LR4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Land Rover LR4 represents the final year of this boxy, highly capable luxury SUV lineage, offering a refined interior and robust off-road capability. Buyers eyeing a used LR4 should appreciate its commanding view and utility, but also approach its complex mechanical and electronic systems with thorough pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

This 3.0-liter supercharged V6 produces approximately 340 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for the heavy SUV. While generally potent and smooth, long-term maintenance requires vigilance regarding cooling and timing components.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and efficient shifting across various driving conditions. It is widely regarded as a durable unit in modern applications, provided regular fluid checks are observed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V635000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Mounting Plate/Flange](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=LR4&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel outlet flange, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V444000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=LR4&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have driver side frontal air bags that may not inflate properly. An air bag that does not inflate properly may increase the risk of injury to the driver in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver side frontal air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Land Rover LR4 combines rugged luxury with a strong supercharged powertrain, making it an appealing choice for enthusiasts. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive inspection by a marque specialist to catch any hidden leaks or electrical glitches before purchase.
