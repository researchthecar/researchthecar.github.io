**Introduction:**

The 2007 Land Rover LR3 is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its rugged off-road capabilities and comfortable on-road performance. It offers various engine and transmission options, including a 4.0L V6 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 4.4L V8 engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

While the LR3 has received praise for its off-road prowess, spacious interior, and luxurious appointments, it has also faced criticism for its fuel efficiency and reliability concerns.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: LR3 - 07E004

**4.4L V8**

* **Cylinder Head Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace cylinder heads
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: LR3 - 10V024, LR3 - 10V025

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Solenoid Pack Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, harsh shifting, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace solenoid pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: LR3 - 05V150

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension becomes inoperable, vehicle sags, reduced ground clearance
    * Solution: Replace air suspension compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: LR3 - 07E003, LR3 - 08V176, LR3 - 10V023

* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling front suspension, uneven tire wear, decreased handling
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Body Control Module (BCM) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, intermittent starting, lighting issues, instrument panel problems
    * Solution: Replace BCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the metal roof rack components
    * Solution: Replace roof rack components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Knob Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control knob becomes loose or falls off
    * Solution: Replace climate control knob
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Land Rover LR3 faces several issues, including engine failures, transmission problems, suspension malfunctions, and electrical glitches. The severity and cost of these issues can vary significantly. While some problems are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more serious and expensive to address. Potential LR3 owners should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
