# 2023 Land Rover Range Rover Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover delivers peerless luxury, sophisticated styling, and robust performance options. However, as a first-model-year vehicle following a complete redesign, buyers should approach the used market with careful inspection. Early owner reports highlight potential electrical issues, accelerated rear brake wear, and occasional powertrain hesitations that warrant attention during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six mild hybrid engine produces around 395 horsepower and provides smooth daily power delivery. It balances traditional Land Rover capability with modern efficiency, though early software anomalies have occasionally been reported by owners.

**Acceleration Lag or Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a noticeable delay or lack of normal response when attempting to accelerate from a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect software calibration and powertrain control modules for updates.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The potent 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 pushes roughly 523 horsepower for effortless, high-performance cruising and towing. It is a complex powerhouse that requires meticulous adherence to factory maintenance intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly across all permanent four-wheel-drive trims. While generally refined, early production software glitches can occasionally introduce shifting lags or unexpected power loss.

**Unexpected Powertrain Disengagement and Shifting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden losses of motive power while driving, alongside unexpected shifts into park or delays when engaging reverse gears.
- **Solution:** Check for manufacturer technical service bulletins and update or reprogram the transmission control module.

## Brakes

### Rear Service Brakes

The heavy vehicle curb weight places significant demand on braking components, with rear pads showing a tendency toward rapid wear on select long-wheelbase models.

**Accelerated Rear Brake Pad and Rotor Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle brake warning lights illuminate prematurely, indicating thin pads and worn rotors on the rear axle well before typical expected mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear brake thickness during a pre-purchase check and replace pads or rotors with updated factory components if excessive wear is found.

## Electrical

### Electrical Architecture & 12V Battery

The advanced electrical architecture powers a vast array of luxury features and driver assists, though early models are susceptible to complex software conflicts and battery drain issues.

**Complete Electrical Shutdown and Warning Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden dashboard warnings like 'Electric default detected' can occur, potentially leading to total loss of electrical power, steering assist, or braking capability.
- **Solution:** Perform comprehensive battery, charging system, and software module diagnostics at an authorized service center.

## Safety

### Rear Seating Mechanism

The luxury cabin features versatile power-operated seating configurations designed for maximum passenger comfort and cargo flexibility.

**Uncontrolled Folding of Rear Power Seats**
- **Symptoms:** Power-folding rear seats may activate without occupant sensors present, potentially catching passengers or items in the folding mechanism.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution when operating rear power folding seat controls and consult a dealer if sensor calibration is suspected.

- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Land Rover Range Rover is an exceptionally capable and lavish flagship SUV, but its status as a redesigned model year calls for vigilance. Prospective buyers should verify that all software updates have been applied, inspect the rear brakes carefully for premature wear, and test all electrical and powertrain functions thoroughly. Securing an extended warranty or comprehensive service history is strongly advised before purchase.
