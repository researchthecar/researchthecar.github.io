**Introduction:**

The 2005 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxury SUV known for its rugged capabilities and stylish design. It comes with a standard 4.4-liter V8 engine delivering 282 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The 4.2-liter V8 engine is also available, providing 305 horsepower and 299 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic transmission and a 5-speed automatic transmission. While the Range Rover is highly praised for its off-road performance and interior comfort, it has faced some reported issues over the years.

**Engine**

**4.4-liter V8 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Leaking coolant, overheating, loss of power
    * **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Noisy engine, reduced power, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace timing chain tensioner
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,000+
* **Oil Pump Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Low oil pressure, engine damage
    * **Solution:** Replace oil pump
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+

**4.2-liter V8 Engine**

* **VVT Solenoid Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Rough idle, reduced power, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace VVT solenoid
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
* **Throttle Body Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Stalling, rough idle, reduced power
    * **Solution:** Replace throttle body
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** No start, intermittent stalling, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace crankshaft position sensor
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering, overheating
    * **Solution:** Replace torque converter
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Valve Body Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, delayed engagement, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace valve body
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+

**5-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Sagging suspension, uneven ride height, check engine light
    * **Solution:** Replace air suspension compressor
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
* **Air Suspension Leak:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Sagging suspension, loss of ride height
    * **Solution:** Find and repair air suspension leak
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Loose steering, excessive vibration, uneven tire wear
    * **Solution:** Replace control arm bushings
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+ per side

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Battery dies quickly, electrical components malfunction
    * **Solution:** Determine and fix the source of the battery drain
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the cause
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Dimming lights, electrical issues, no start
    * **Solution:** Replace alternator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Starter Motor Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** No start, clicking sound, grinding noise
    * **Solution:** Replace starter motor
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Rear Hatch Struts Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Rear hatch won't stay open, sagging hatch
    * **Solution:** Replace rear hatch struts
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Gauges not functioning, check engine light, flickering
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace instrument cluster
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, overheating, foggy windows
    * **Solution:** Replace HVAC blower motor
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
* **Seat Switch Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Seats not adjusting, seat memory loss
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace seat switch
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+

**Summary**

The 2005 Land Rover Range Rover has some reported issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. While the off-road performance and luxurious interior are widely praised, the potential for costly repairs should be considered. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be significant and require extensive repairs. It's recommended that potential owners thoroughly research these issues and consider the associated costs before purchasing this vehicle.
