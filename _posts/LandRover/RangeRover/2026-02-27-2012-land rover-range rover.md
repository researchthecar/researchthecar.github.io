# 2012 Land Rover Range Rover

### Overview
The 2012 Land Rover Range Rover, a luxury SUV renowned for its off-road prowess and opulent interior, presents a mixed bag in terms of long-term reliability. While its robust construction and advanced features contribute to its appeal, certain model years and specific components have shown a propensity for issues that can impact ownership experience and costs. Owners often praise its commanding presence and capability, but potential buyers should be aware of common problem areas, particularly concerning the powertrain and electronics, which can require attention beyond routine maintenance. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding standard wear and tear.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L V8 Naturally Aspirated
**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light; potential engine damage if not addressed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, guides, and sprockets.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,500

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of hoses, water pump, or radiator as needed.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


### 5.0L V8 Supercharged
**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light; potential engine damage if not addressed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, guides, and sprockets.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,500

**Supercharger Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whining noise from the engine bay, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger belt and potentially idler pulleys.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (ZF 6HP26)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough gear changes, hesitation during acceleration, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, valve body repair/replacement, or full transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $800 - $3,500

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or cooler lines, slipping gears, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission pan gasket, cooler lines, or seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,000


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000

**Battery Drain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Parking Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, sensors not detecting obstacles, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of faulty parking sensors or control module.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Sunroof Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Water ingress into the cabin, damp carpets, mold growth.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sunroof drains, sealing of sunroof frame, or replacement of sunroof assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Interior

### Interior Electronics
**Heated Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** One or both heated seats not working, intermittent heating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of heating elements or seat control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Land Rover Range Rover, while offering a luxurious and capable driving experience, is not without its reliability concerns. The 5.0L V8 engines, both naturally aspirated and supercharged, are susceptible to timing chain tensioner issues, which can be costly to repair. The adaptive air suspension is another area prone to problems, with air spring leaks and compressor failures being common. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, also appear in owner reports. While the transmission is generally robust, issues like harsh shifting can arise. Owners should budget for potential repairs beyond standard maintenance, especially for these known problem areas, to ensure continued enjoyment of this premium SUV.
