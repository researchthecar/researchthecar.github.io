**Introduction:**

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxury SUV that offers impressive off-road capabilities and a premium interior. It is powered by a range of engines, including a 3.0L inline-six turbocharged engine with 395 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic and a 9-speed automatic.

**Engine**

**3.0L Inline-Six Turbocharged**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, reduced fuel economy, whistling noise from the engine
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle, reduced performance, engine warning lights
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delays in shifting, hard shifts, shuddering during shifts
    * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid flush, or replacement of transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle lowered or not responding to height adjustments
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Spring Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, loss of suspension firmness
    * Solution: Replace the leaking air spring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for a short period, warning lights on dashboard
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical component causing the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blank screen, unresponsive touch controls, system freezing
    * Solution: Software update, hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wiper blades not moving, intermittent operation
    * Solution: Replace the wiper motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise or rattling from the panoramic sunroof
    * Solution: Adjust or lubricate the sunroof mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover is a well-equipped SUV with impressive off-road capabilities. However, it does have some potential issues that can be expensive to fix. Engine turbocharger and timing chain tensioner failures, transmission shifting issues, and suspension air suspension compressor failures are some of the most common problems reported. Electrical issues, such as battery drain and infotainment system malfunctions, have also been reported. Interior issues are relatively minor, with panoramic sunroof noise being a common concern. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues should be considered when purchasing a 2022 Land Rover Range Rover.
