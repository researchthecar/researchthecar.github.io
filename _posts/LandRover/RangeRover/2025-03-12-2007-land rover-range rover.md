Alright, here's a breakdown of common issues and complaints for the 2007 Land Rover Range Rover, formatted as requested.

The 2007 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxury SUV known for its off-road capabilities and opulent interior. It comes with two engine options: a 4.4-liter V8 producing 305 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, and a supercharged 4.2-liter V8 generating 400 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the Range Rover's distinctive styling, comfortable ride, and go-anywhere attitude. Common complaints include poor fuel economy, frequent maintenance requirements, and high repair costs.

**Engine**

**4.4L V8**

*   **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise upon startup, especially when cold. Can lead to engine damage if not addressed.
    *   Solution: Replace timing chain tensioners, guides, and chains.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, overheating, white smoke from exhaust. Common leak points include radiator, hoses, and water pump.
    *   Solution: Replace damaged components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ depending on the source of the leak.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks down the side of the engine, burning oil smell.
    *   Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**4.2L Supercharged V8**

*   **Supercharger Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, whining noise from the supercharger, potential for complete failure.
    *   Solution: Rebuild or replace supercharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks (Same as 4.4L, but potentially exacerbated by supercharger heat)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, overheating, white smoke from exhaust. Common leak points include radiator, hoses, and water pump.
    *   Solution: Replace damaged components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ depending on the source of the leak.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise upon startup, especially when cold. Can lead to engine damage if not addressed.
    *   Solution: Replace timing chain tensioners, guides, and chains.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic (ZF 6HP26)**

*   **Valve Body Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping between gears.
    *   Solution: Replace or rebuild valve body.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Torque Converter Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, stalling, or complete loss of drive.
    *   Solution: Replace torque converter.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sags, "Suspension Fault" warning light, slow or no response to height adjustments.
    *   Solution: Replace air suspension compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Air Strut Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sags, hissing sound from struts, uneven ride height.
    *   Solution: Replace leaking air struts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per strut
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Height Sensors Failing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Erroneous readings, incorrect ride height, suspension fault warnings.
    *   Solution: Replace height sensors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per sensor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning. Often caused by parasitic draws from various modules.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and repair parasitic draw. This can involve replacing modules, repairing wiring, or updating software.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+ depending on the complexity of the issue.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Navigation/Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, system crashes, inability to input destinations, speaker issues.
    *   Solution: Software updates, component replacement (head unit, amplifier).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Transfer Case Control Module Issues**
     * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting between 4-high and 4-low, 4x4 system failure
     * Solution: Replace transfer case control module
     * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
     * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Tailgate Actuator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Tailgate not opening or closing properly, noisy operation.
    *   Solution: Replace tailgate actuator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, leaks, motor failure.
    *   Solution: Repair or replace sunroof motor, seals, or entire assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Land Rover Range Rover presents owners with a mixed bag. While it offers a luxurious driving experience and impressive off-road capability, it's plagued by a number of potential issues. Engine problems (particularly timing chain tensioners and coolant leaks), transmission issues (especially valve body problems), and air suspension failures are all relatively common and can lead to significant repair bills. Electrical gremlins and interior component failures further contribute to the ownership experience. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of the high cost of maintenance and repairs associated with this vehicle and should factor this into their purchasing decision.
