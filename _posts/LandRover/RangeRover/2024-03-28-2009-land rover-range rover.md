**Introduction**

The 2009 Land Rover Range Rover, a premium luxury SUV, offers a blend of off-road prowess and on-road refinement. Powered by a 4.2-liter supercharged V8 engine, it delivers 390 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The HSE and Supercharged trim levels are available. Drivers praise its off-road capabilities, luxurious interior, and powerful engine, while common complaints include high maintenance costs and a lack of technology features.

**Engine**

**4.2-liter Supercharged V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, overheating, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Piston Ring Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Overhaul engine
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blue smoke from exhaust on startup
    * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, rough shifting, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, loss of power, overheating transmission
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging, difficulty maintaining ride height, compressor noise
    * Solution: Replace air suspension components (e.g., air bags, compressor, lines)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Thumping or clunking noises when driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic drain, replace battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, slow charging, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Reseal or replace sunroof components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, failure to regulate temperature
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace defective components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate navigation directions, system freezing
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Land Rover Range Rover is a capable SUV with known issues in areas such as the engine, transmission, and suspension. While it offers a luxurious experience and impressive off-road capabilities, potential owners should be aware of the potential for costly repairs and high maintenance expenses.
