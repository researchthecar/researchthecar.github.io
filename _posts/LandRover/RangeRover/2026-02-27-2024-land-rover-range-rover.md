# 2024 Land Rover Range Rover Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a luxury SUV, the 2024 Land Rover Range Rover commands attention with its striking design, opulent interior, and formidable capability. However, used-car buyers should review owner-reported feedback carefully, as initial owner experiences highlight several electrical, powertrain, and visibility concerns that warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Mild Hybrid

The standard 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 mild-hybrid engine delivers robust output around 395 horsepower and smooth everyday performance. While generally responsive, early owner feedback highlights occasional electronic and fuel management module complaints.

**Powertrain Control Module and Fuel System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report sudden engine stalling, dashboard blackout, and unexpected loss of motive power while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified specialist inspect the Powertrain Control Module (PCM) and Fuel Pump Driver Module (FPDM) for diagnostic trouble codes or software anomalies.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The available 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 delivers authoritative acceleration with approximately 523 to 606 horsepower depending on the specific tune. It provides effortless passing power and high towing capacity for luxury buyers seeking peak performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly across all four wheels, offering refined and nearly imperceptible gear changes. It pairs well with both engine options, though prospective buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure crisp shifting behavior.

## Brakes

### Brake System

The heavy braking system provides strong stopping power, but owners have occasionally reported premature pad wear and unexpected pedal behavior.

**Premature Pad Wear and Pedal Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpectedly rapid rear brake pad wear at low mileages, alongside rare instances of altered brake pedal feel or temporary loss of braking capability.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and hydraulic actuators during a pre-purchase evaluation to ensure proper Bosch brake system operation.

## Electrical

### Electrical Control Systems

Complex onboard electronics govern everything from chassis dynamics to cabin convenience, making software stability paramount.

**Unintended Shifting into Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may unexpectedly display an 'Electrical Fault detected' warning message and shift into neutral independently while in motion, causing a loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Check for pending software updates and module bulletins related to powertrain and transmission communication lines.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Large panoramic glass surfaces and acoustic windshields enhance cabin ambiance but can be susceptible to environmental stress and road debris.

**Windshield Cracking and Moonroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report frequent windshield cracking and visual distortions, alongside rare instances where the moonroof glass fractures in sinusoidal wave patterns without impact.
- **Solution:** Examine glass surfaces carefully for pre-existing chips, stress cracks, or distorted optics prior to finalizing a purchase.

## Safety

### Power Seats

The plush cabin features flexible multi-row seating configurations governed by numerous convenience motors.

**Second-Row Seat Pinch Hazard**
- **Symptoms:** When utilizing power release buttons to access the third row, second-row seats may continue moving into place without an automatic pinch-release safety disengagement if an obstruction is present.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution and supervise passengers when operating power-folding seat mechanisms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V790000: Engine And Engine Cooling](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine mounted oil cooler hoses may not be secured properly, which can result in oil leaking onto the road. Oil leaking onto the road can cause a hazard for other road users, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the fasteners and the oil cooler hoses, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V872000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The body panels may not be bonded properly, reducing the structural strength of the vehicle. Reduced structural strength may not adequately protect passengers, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install two rivets and apply sealant, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V380000: Power Train:Clutch Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V450000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Filter/Screen](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the filter housing, oil filter, and o-rings, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V678000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the NFSM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V097000: Structure:Exterior Trim](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The panoramic sunroof side finisher trim may not be properly secured and can detach. A detached panoramic sunroof side trim can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the panoramic sunroof side finisher trim and secure it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V389000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The driver’s air bag clockspring connector may corrode, which can cause the air bag not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective lubricant gel to the driver’s air bag connector terminals, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Land Rover Range Rover offers unmatched luxury, prestige, and road presence, but used-car buyers should approach with vigilance. Given reports concerning electrical glitches, powertrain management modules, and glass durability, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly recommended.
