**Introduction:**

The 2017 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxurious SUV offering an opulent interior, impressive off-road capabilities, and exceptional performance. It's available with several engine and transmission options, including a 3.0L V6 diesel engine, a 5.0L V8 supercharged gasoline engine, and an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners appreciate its comfortable ride, refined handling, and advanced technological features. However, like any vehicle, it has faced some specific issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, abnormal engine noises
  * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, warning lights on the dashboard
  * Solution: Clean or replace the DPF
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
  * Recalls: LR066454

**5.0L V8 Supercharged Gasoline**

* **Supercharger Belt Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Squealing noises from the engine compartment, loss of power
  * Solution: Replace the supercharger belt
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $900+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid dripping from the transmission, hard shifting, slipping gears
  * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission fluid lines or seals
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: LR064813

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations felt through the vehicle, especially during acceleration
  * Solution: Replace the torque converter
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
  * Recalls: LR064918

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, difficulty maintaining ride height, warning lights on the dashboard
  * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor or other faulty components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunctions:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical issues, such as faulty sensors, flickering lights, or battery drain
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair the specific electrical fault
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
  * Recalls: None Found

* **In-Control Touch Pro Infotainment System Issues:**
  * Description/Symptoms: System freezes, touch screen unresponsive, navigation errors
  * Solution: Software updates, hardware replacements
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Doors fail to lock or unlock, door handles become unresponsive
  * Solution: Replace the door lock actuators or other faulty components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: LR064736

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the sunroof during driving, especially on uneven surfaces
  * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Issues:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air conditioning or heating, uneven temperature distribution
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair the fault in the climate control system
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2017 Land Rover Range Rover is a desirable SUV, it has faced some issues that have affected its reliability. The severity of the problems varies, with some being relatively minor and others potentially more costly to repair. Owners should be aware of these issues and factor them into their purchasing decisions.
