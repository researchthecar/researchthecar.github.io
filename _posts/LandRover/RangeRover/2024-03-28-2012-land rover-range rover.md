**Introduction:**
The 2012 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxury SUV that offers a sophisticated driving experience and renowned off-road capabilities. It is powered by a 5.0L V8 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, delivering 375 horsepower and 375 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle is known for its plush interior, advanced technology, and comfortable ride quality. However, like any vehicle, it has encountered certain issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**5.0L V8**

* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking gaskets, seals, or oil pan can cause oil drips or puddles under the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace faulty gaskets, seals, or oil pan.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Intake Manifold Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning intake manifold flaps can lead to reduced engine power, rough idle, or check engine light illumination.
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold or intake manifold flaps.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaky transmission seals or lines can result in low transmission fluid levels and slipping gears.
    * Solution: Replace damaged seals or lines and refill transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or rough shifting, clunking noises, or failure to shift gears properly.
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components, such as solenoids, gears, or torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air suspension components can cause vehicle to sag or lose height, resulting in reduced ride quality or handling issues.
    * Solution: Repair or replace air suspension compressor, bags, or lines.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains prematurely, preventing starting or causing electrical malfunctions.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty electrical components, such as parasitic drains or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows stop functioning or become inoperable.
    * Solution: Replace faulty window motor, regulator, or switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation accumulates inside headlight assemblies, impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies or use headlight sealant.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the vehicle's interior through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seals or repair sunroof mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Navigation System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system malfunctions, inaccurate directions, or screen freezes.
    * Solution: Update navigation software, replace navigation unit, or repair electrical connections.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2012 Land Rover Range Rover faces several issues ranging from minor inconveniences to more serious repairs. Engine oil leaks, intake manifold problems, and transmission fluid leaks are some of the reported concerns that require attention. Electrical issues, such as battery drain and power window failure, can impact the vehicle's functionality. While exterior and interior issues are less severe, they can affect the overall ownership experience. The cost to fix these issues can vary significantly depending on the severity of the problem and the availability of parts. It's important for owners to address these issues promptly to ensure the vehicle's reliability and maintain its value.
