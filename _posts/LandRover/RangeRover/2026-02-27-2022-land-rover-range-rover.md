# 2022 Land Rover Range Rover Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover represents the dawn of a fully redesigned generation, blending high-end luxury with advanced technological features and robust powertrain options. As a first-model-year luxury flagship vehicle, prospective used buyers should weigh its striking capability and opulent interior against the typical complexities associated with entirely new electronic architectures and sophisticated mechanical systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 MHEV

This mild-hybrid inline-6 engine produces approximately 395 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. It offers smooth power delivery and serves as the baseline powerplant for the lineup, generally balancing refinement with everyday driveability.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Sourced from BMW, this potent V8 delivers around 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. It provides exhilarating acceleration and robust towing capacity for buyers seeking top-tier performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with all available engines and standard all-wheel drive. It delivers seamless gear shifts and dependable long-term operation under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V252000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly welded second-row left seat frame may not adequately secure occupants during sudden braking or a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the second-row left seat frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V044000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine cam carrier and related components as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V324000: Latches/Locks/Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The second-row seat armrest storage compartment latch may fail, allowing the objects from within to be unsecured in the event of a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 201, "Occupant Protection in Interior Impact." - **What to do:** Dealers will replace the locking arm of the second-row armrest storage compartment, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V394000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Exterior lights that fail to illuminate can decrease visibility and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace or repair the rear lights as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V023000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display while in reverse decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rearview camera and add a new seal or replace the camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V947000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A rearview camera image that does not display can decrease the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V248000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Dc/Dc Converter](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V389000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The driver’s air bag clockspring connector may corrode, which can cause the air bag not to deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a protective lubricant gel to the driver’s air bag connector terminals, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Land Rover Range Rover is an exceptionally luxurious and capable flagship SUV that rewards buyers with unmatched comfort and prestige. Because it is the first year of a complete redesign, used-car shoppers should thoroughly inspect all electronic and mechanical functions before purchase and prioritize models with remaining warranty coverage or comprehensive service records.
