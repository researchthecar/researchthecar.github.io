**Introduction:**

The 2011 Land Rover Range Rover is a premium SUV known for its luxurious interior, capable off-road performance, and powerful engines. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine Options:**
    * 5.0L V8 (510 HP, 461 lb-ft)
    * 3.0L TDV6 Diesel (244 HP, 443 lb-ft)
* **Transmission Options:**
    * 6-speed automatic
    * 8-speed automatic

**Engine Issues:**

* **5.0L V8:**
    * **Water Pump Failure:** Leaks coolant, causing overheating and potential engine damage. **Solution:** Replace water pump. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $700 - $1,000+ **Recalls:** None Found
* **3.0L TDV6 Diesel:**
    * **Injector Failure:** Engine misfires, loss of power, and increased emissions. **Solution:** Replace faulty injectors. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+ **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission Issues:**

* **6-speed Automatic:**
    * **Delayed Shifting:** Slow or rough gear changes, especially during acceleration. **Solution:** Software update or transmission rebuild. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
    * **Torque Converter Lockup Problems:** Vehicle hesitates or shudders during acceleration. **Solution:** Replace torque converter. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,200 - $2,000+
* **8-speed Automatic:**
    * **Transmission Slipping:** Loss of power, erratic gear changes, or shuddering. **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension Issues:**

* **Air Suspension Failure:** Compressor or air struts fail, causing the vehicle to sag or ride abnormally. **Solution:** Replace faulty components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Rear Axle Seal Leaks:** Leaking oil from the rear axle seals. **Solution:** Replace axle seals. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+

**Electrical Issues:**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains prematurely, causing difficulty starting. **Solution:** Inspect electrical system for parasitic draw. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $700+
* **Electrical Faulty Wiring:** Electrical components may fail intermittently or not function at all. **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty wiring. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior Issues:**

* **Headlamp Condensation:** Condensation forms inside the headlamps, causing poor illumination. **Solution:** Replace headlamps. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

**Interior Issues:**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** Climate control system may fail to circulate air, adjust temperature, or display incorrect information. **Solution:** Inspect and repair failed components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Infotainment System Screen Flickers:** Touchscreen display may experience flickering or intermittent blackouts. **Solution:** Software update or hardware replacement. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The 2011 Land Rover Range Rover is a powerful and luxurious SUV with a range of engine and transmission options. While it offers impressive performance and comfort, it has some common issues to be aware of. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to fix, while electrical and suspension issues can affect vehicle reliability. The severity and expense of these issues can vary, and potential buyers should carefully consider these factors before purchasing.
