**Introduction**
The 2002 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxury SUV known for its off-road capabilities, stylish design, and high-end amenities. It is offered with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* 4.0L V8 (217 hp, 250 lb-ft torque) with 5-speed automatic transmission
* 4.6L V8 (220 hp, 290 lb-ft torque) with 4-speed automatic transmission
* 4.4L V8 (282 hp, 325 lb-ft torque) with 5-speed automatic transmission

Drivers appreciate the Range Rover's ruggedness, comfort, and prestige, but some have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**
**4.0L V8**
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idling, poor performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.6L V8**
* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**5-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, delayed gear shifts
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Hard Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerky or rough gear shifts
    * Solution: Flush transmission fluid, replace transmission filter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of ride height, sagging suspension
    * Solution: Replace air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for a period of time
    * Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin from the sunroof
    * Solution: Replace or repair sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Heated Seat Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heated seats do not work
    * Solution: Replace heating element or wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Land Rover Range Rover offers a luxurious and capable driving experience, but it is not without its issues. Owners have reported problems with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a used Range Rover.
