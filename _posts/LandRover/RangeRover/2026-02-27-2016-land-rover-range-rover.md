# 2016 Land Rover Range Rover Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Land Rover Range Rover delivers iconic luxury, incredible capability, and powerful engine options, making it a head-turning choice for used vehicle buyers. However, prospective buyers should approach this flagship SUV with careful inspection and an understanding of its complex electronics and mechanical systems. While it offers a peerless ride and opulent interior, maintenance costs and potential component failures can be substantial.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 Diesel

A 3.0-liter turbocharged diesel V6 engine producing approximately 254 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers strong low-end pulling power and high fuel efficiency, though routine maintenance is crucial for long-term health.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 Gas

A 3.0-liter supercharged gasoline V6 engine making around 340 to 380 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and responsive power delivery, serving as a popular daily-driver option.

### 5.0L Supercharged V8 Gas

A potent 5.0-liter supercharged V8 engine delivering 510 or 550 horsepower depending on the trim level. It offers blistering performance and immense capability, but demands close attention to cooling and timing components.

## Transmission

### ZF 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission paired across all powertrain options, known for smooth shifting and responsive gear selection. Routine fluid checks ensure longevity, though software updates are occasionally required for optimal performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V390000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The unlatched door may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of injury or a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the software, and inspect the KV latches, replacing them if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V325000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles were previously recalled and repaired under Recall 19V-390, however the repair may not have been completed properly. An unlatched door may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of injury or a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will update the software, and inspect the KV latches, replacing them if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V350000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seat belt Emergency Locking Retractor (ELR) may not lock as designed. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of the Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." If the driver brakes prior to a crash, the ELR will not lock at the correct g-force level, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the seat belt assembly, replacing it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V392000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The unlatched door may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect for any issues with the KV system, disabling the KV mechanisms if necessary, and update the KV Latch software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V387000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles were previously recalled and repaired under Recall 19V-392, however the repair may not have been completed properly. Unlatched doors may open while the vehicle is in motion, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect for any issues with the KV system, disabling the KV mechanisms if necessary, and update the KV Latch software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The passenger frontal air bag inflator initiator may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the passenger frontal air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V941000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a front passenger air bag inflator initiator that may fail to ignite during a crash, preventing the air bag from deploying. In the event of a crash of sufficient severity, the front passenger air bag may not deploy as required, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V942000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Land+Rover&model=Range+Rover&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seatbelt pretensioners that may not function properly in the event of a crash. If the front seat seatbelt pretensioners do not function properly, the seat occupants may not be adequately restrained in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Land Rover will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front seat belt assemblies, replacing them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Land Rover Range Rover remains a pinnacle of luxury and off-road prowess, but used buyers must weigh its impressive capability against the reality of complex upkeep. Prioritize models with comprehensive service histories and budget accordingly for potential maintenance. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is essential before sealing the deal.
