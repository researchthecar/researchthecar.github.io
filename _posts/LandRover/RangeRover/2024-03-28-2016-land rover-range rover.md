**Introduction**

The 2016 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxurious and capable full-size SUV. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a supercharged 5.0L V8 that produces 510 horsepower and 461 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0L turbodiesel V6 that produces 254 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. The Range Rover is also known for its spacious and well-equipped cabin, as well as its advanced safety features. 

However, like any vehicle, the Range Rover is not immune to problems. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific issues people are having with the vehicle:

**Engine**

**Supercharged 5.0L V8**

**Intake Manifold Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power and a rough idle.
* Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Turbodiesel V6**

**EGR Cooler Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: The EGR cooler can fail, causing coolant to leak into the exhaust system. This can lead to overheating and a loss of power.
* Solution: Replace the EGR cooler.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**ZF 8-Speed Automatic**

**Shifting Problems**
* Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting gears.
* Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Air Suspension Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system can fail, causing the vehicle to ride low or unevenly.
* Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor or air springs.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
* Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and repair the electrical drain.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
* Description/Symptoms: Condensation can build up inside the headlights.
* Solution: Replace the headlight assembly.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Issues**
* Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, lag, or reboot unexpectedly.
* Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment system.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Land Rover Range Rover is a reliable and well-built SUV. However, it is not immune to problems. Some of the most common issues include intake manifold failure, EGR cooler failure, shifting problems, air suspension failure, and electrical issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem.
