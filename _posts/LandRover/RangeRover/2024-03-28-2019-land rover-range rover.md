The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxurious and capable SUV that offers a variety of engine and transmission options. It is powered by a standard 3.0L V6 diesel engine that produces 254 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. Other available engines include a 3.0L V6 gasoline engine (340 horsepower, 332 lb-ft of torque), a 5.0L V8 gasoline engine (518 horsepower, 461 lb-ft of torque), and a 4.4L V8 diesel engine (335 horsepower, 516 lb-ft of torque). The Range Rover comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission, while a 9-speed automatic transmission is available with the 5.0L V8 engine.

Despite its high-end reputation, the 2019 Land Rover Range Rover has experienced a number of issues, including:

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6 Diesel Engine: EGR Cooler Failure**
   * Description/Symptoms: The EGR cooler can fail, causing coolant and exhaust gases to leak. This can lead to overheating, loss of power, and decreased fuel efficiency.
   * Solution: Replace the EGR cooler.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission: Torque Converter Shudder**
   * Description/Symptoms: The torque converter can shudder during acceleration, causing a vibration that can be felt throughout the vehicle.
   * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
   * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension: Compressor Failure**
   * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension compressor can fail, causing the vehicle to lose height and ride quality.
   * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery: Premature Failure**
   * Description/Symptoms: The battery can fail prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
   * Solution: Replace the battery.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
   * Recalls: N/A
* **Infotainment System: Software Bugs**
   * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can experience software bugs that cause it to freeze, crash, or display errors.
   * Solution: Update the software.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
   * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Headlights: Condensation**
   * Description/Symptoms: Condensation can form inside the headlights, causing them to fog up.
   * Solution: Replace the headlights.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control: AC Compressor Failure**
   * Description/Symptoms: The AC compressor can fail, causing the air conditioning system to stop working.
   * Solution: Replace the AC compressor.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2019 Land Rover Range Rover is a luxurious and capable SUV, but it has experienced a number of issues, including EGR cooler failure, torque converter shudder, air suspension compressor failure, software bugs, headlight condensation, and AC compressor failure. While some of these issues are relatively minor, others can be expensive to fix, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing this vehicle.
