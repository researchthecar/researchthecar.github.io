**Introduction:**

The 2009 Land Rover LR2 is a compact SUV that offers a blend of off-road capability and urban comfort. It is powered by a standard 3.2-liter inline-6 engine that produces 230 horsepower and 234 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The LR2 also features a Terrain Response system with four selectable modes to optimize performance on different surfaces. Despite its rugged nature, the LR2 is known for its comfortable interior and user-friendly features. However, it has also encountered some specific issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

**3.2L Inline-6**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise from the engine, especially during cold starts; reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Intake Manifold Runner Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, stalling, decreased fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Clean or replace the intake manifold runner control valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Oil Cooler O-Ring Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the oil cooler lines; low oil levels
    * Solution: Replace the O-ring on the oil cooler lines
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed gear engagement, RPMs increase without acceleration
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, transmission stuck in gear
    * Solution: Replace the faulty solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Transfer Case Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leak from the transfer case; low fluid levels
    * Solution: Replace the transfer case seal or gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension; poor handling
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
* **Rear Coil Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uneven ride height; reduced suspension travel
    * Solution: Replace the faulty coil springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly even after being replaced
    * Solution: Check and replace any faulty components causing the drain, such as lights or modules
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
* **Brake Light Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Brake lights not illuminating; cruise control not working
    * Solution: Replace the brake light switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunction
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Handle Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the tailgate handle; difficulty opening the tailgate
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300

**Interior**

* **Heater Core Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking into the cabin; fogging or moisture on the windshield
    * Solution: Replace the heater core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Steering Wheel Buttons Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel buttons unresponsive or sticking
    * Solution: Clean or replace the faulty buttons
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300

**Summary:**

The 2009 Land Rover LR2 faces a range of issues, with varying severity and cost. While the engine and transmission issues can be significant and expensive to repair, the other categories generally indicate minor or moderate problems. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider regular maintenance and repairs to ensure the reliability and longevity of their LR2.
