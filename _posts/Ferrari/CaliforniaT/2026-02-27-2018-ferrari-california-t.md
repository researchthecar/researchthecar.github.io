# 2018 Ferrari California T Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Ferrari California T blends open-top grand touring with turbocharged performance, powered by a sophisticated V8 engine mated to a dual-clutch transmission. As an exotic high-performance vehicle, prospective buyers should approach ownership with an understanding of specialized maintenance requirements and complex electronics. While owner-reported complaints are sparse for this model year, diligent pre-purchase inspections by a qualified Ferrari specialist remain essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 552 horsepower and 557 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration. It represents Ferrari's modern turbo era, offering strong performance but requiring meticulous adherence to service intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering wheel paddle shifters. It offers smooth operation in automatic mode and crisp engagement during spirited driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
