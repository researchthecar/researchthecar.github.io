# 2016 Ferrari California T Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ferrari California T combines iconic Italian grand touring performance with a twin-turbocharged V8 engine and a retractable hardtop. As with many high-performance exotic vehicles, shopping for a used model requires careful attention to maintenance history, electronics, and specialized mechanical systems. While official owner complaints for this model year are minimal, prospective buyers should still thoroughly inspect pre-owned examples for any lingering electrical quirks or mechanical wear.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 552 horsepower and 557 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration and a signature exhaust note. Regular servicing of the turbocharging systems and cooling components is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. Routine fluid changes and clutch health monitoring are vital to maintaining its crisp performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V811000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a low pressure fuel line that may not connect properly to the fuel pump feed pipe. The improper low pressure fuel line connection can result in a fuel leak in the engine compartment which may increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** FNA has notified owners, and dealers will replace the low pressure fuel line assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ferrari California T remains a thrilling and usable grand tourer for enthusiasts seeking open-top Italian performance. Because used exotic car ownership can involve high maintenance costs, buyers should insist on a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a certified Ferrari specialist to verify the health of the engine, transmission, and complex electronics.
