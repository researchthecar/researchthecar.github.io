# 2017 Ferrari California T Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ferrari California T offers an exhilarating open-air grand touring experience powered by a turbocharged V8 engine. Buyers shopping in the used market should evaluate service history and verify that all necessary manufacturer campaigns have been addressed.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 552 horsepower and 557 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration and strong mid-range performance. Regular maintenance is crucial to ensure long-term reliability of the forced-induction system.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear shifts via steering-column-mounted paddle shifters. Smooth operation depends on timely fluid services and proper clutch calibration.

## Safety

### Airbags

Owner reports highlight logistical delays associated with completing high-profile manufacturer airbag recalls.

**Takata Airbag Recall Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Owners reported experiencing prolonged delays when attempting to schedule and complete mandatory Takata airbag recall repairs through authorized dealerships.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized Ferrari dealer to check for open recall status and schedule the required component replacement free of charge.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=California+T&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2017 Ferrari California T, prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist and confirm that all safety recalls have been properly completed.
