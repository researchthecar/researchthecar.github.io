# 2024 Ferrari Purosangue

### Overview
The 2024 Ferrari Purosangue, a groundbreaking four-door, four-seater model from the iconic Italian marque, represents a significant departure from traditional Ferrari offerings. Engineered with a naturally aspirated V12 engine and an advanced all-wheel-drive system, it aims to blend supercar performance with everyday usability. As a new model, comprehensive long-term reliability data is still emerging.
