# 2024 Ferrari Purosangue Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Ferrari Purosangue represents a dramatic departure for the brand, introducing a high-performance V12-powered luxury utility vehicle to the market. Because this is an ultra-luxury vehicle produced in low volumes, prospective buyers should approach the used market with careful inspection protocols. While early ownership experiences are generally shaped by exotic performance and exclusivity rather than widespread consumer defect trends, understanding its bespoke powertrain options is essential for any potential buyer.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L V12 Engine

The Purosangue is powered by a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine producing approximately 715 horsepower and 528 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling high-RPM performance, though buyers should expect exceptionally high maintenance overhead and specialized servicing requirements typical of Ferrari's flagship twelve-cylinder powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through an 8-speed dual-clutch transaxle automatic transmission paired with an all-wheel-drive system. This unit provides lightning-fast gear changes suited to track-capable driving dynamics while maintaining highway cruising composure.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V479000: Electrical System:Wiring:Interior/Under Dash](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=Purosangue&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The fuse box’s power supply and the passenger-side footrest may contact each other, possibly resulting in a short circuit that activates the brake warning light and decreases the vehicle's braking ability. A loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will install a plastic spacer between the fuse box’s power supply and the passenger-side footrest, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Ferrari Purosangue is an extraordinary machine that commands a massive financial commitment both at purchase and during routine upkeep. With no widespread recurring defect patterns currently dominating owner complaint logs, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an authorized Ferrari specialist to verify proper mechanical and electronic health.
