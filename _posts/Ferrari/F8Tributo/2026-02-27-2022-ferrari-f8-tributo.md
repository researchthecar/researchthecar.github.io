# 2022 Ferrari F8 Tributo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ferrari F8 Tributo represents the pinnacle of mid-engine V8 Italian supercars, combining breathtaking performance with exotic styling. As a high-performance exotic vehicle, it is built with precision engineering, meaning maintenance and potential repairs require specialized knowledge and can involve substantial financial commitments. Buyers interested in a used F8 Tributo should carefully review service histories and ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an authorized specialist.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces an exhilarating 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It delivers blistering acceleration and track-ready performance while generally proving robust under normal exotic car operating conditions.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. It transfers massive power to the rear wheels smoothly and efficiently during aggressive driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F8+Tributo&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ferrari F8 Tributo is an extraordinary exotic supercar offering sensational performance and striking design. Because available reliability complaint data is minimal for this model year, prospective buyers should focus primarily on verifying comprehensive service records, confirming proper maintenance schedules, and performing a professional pre-purchase inspection to ensure long-term enjoyment.
