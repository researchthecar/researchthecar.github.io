# 2021 Ferrari F8 Tributo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ferrari F8 Tributo represents the pinnacle of mid-engine V8 supercar performance, delivering breathtaking power and precise handling. As an exotic Italian high-performance vehicle, prospective used buyers should prioritize comprehensive service history, professional pre-purchase inspections, and verification of any remaining manufacturer maintenance coverage over generalized reliability metrics.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and track-capable performance. Overall, this powerplant is robust when properly maintained, though high-performance driving requires strict adherence to factory service intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, tailored for aggressive driving dynamics. It is generally responsive and durable, provided the complex hydraulic and electronic systems have been inspected regularly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F8+Tributo&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2021 Ferrari F8 Tributo, standard statistical reliability reports take a back seat to individual vehicle provenance, meticulous service records, and professional mechanical inspections. Buyers should ensure all routine maintenance has been performed by authorized specialists to enjoy this exotic supercar with confidence.
