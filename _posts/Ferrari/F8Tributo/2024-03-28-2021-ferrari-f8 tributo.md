**Introduction:**

The 2021 Ferrari F8 Tributo is a mid-engined sports car produced by Ferrari. It is powered by a 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, which produces 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The F8 Tributo has a top speed of 211 mph and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 2.9 seconds.

**Engine**

* **3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased oil consumption, unusual noises coming from the engine.
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $7,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Fuel Injector Malfunction:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, hesitation upon acceleration, poor fuel economy.
        * Solution: Replace fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
    * **Clutch Slippage:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power during acceleration, shuddering during engagement.
        * Solution: Replace clutch assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery depletes prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replace battery or repair electrical fault.

        * Recalls: None Found

        * Description/Symptoms: Display freezes, unresponsive touchscreen, audio issues.
