# 2020 Ferrari F8 Tributo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Ferrari F8 Tributo represents the pinnacle of mid-engine Italian performance, featuring a twin-turbocharged V8 that delivers breathtaking speed and precise handling. For prospective used-car buyers, stepping into an exotic supercar requires careful attention to maintenance history, specialized servicing costs, and thorough pre-purchase inspections by certified technicians. While official owner-reported data for this exotic model remains sparse, understanding its complex powertrain and high-performance systems is essential for a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and sharp throttle response. It is a highly sophisticated powerplant requiring meticulous adherence to factory maintenance schedules to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, tailored for both track and road use. Buyers should verify proper clutch engagement and smooth operation during a test drive, as specialized gearbox services can be expensive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F8+Tributo&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
