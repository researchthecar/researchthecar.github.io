# 2019 Ferrari 488 Pista Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Ferrari 488 Pista is an uncompromising track-focused exotic car powered by a formidable twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As a low-volume, high-performance Italian supercar, buyers should expect unique maintenance requirements and specialized servicing costs typical of the marque.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is a highly sophisticated powerplant that requires meticulous adherence to Ferrari's scheduled maintenance intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes tailored for both aggressive track use and spirited road driving. Routine fluid checks and clutch wear inspections are critical for maintaining optimal shifting performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Pista&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Pista&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Ferrari 488 Pista is a premier exotic performance machine designed for enthusiasts seeking pinnacle track capabilities. Prospective buyers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections by a qualified Ferrari specialist and verify complete service history.
