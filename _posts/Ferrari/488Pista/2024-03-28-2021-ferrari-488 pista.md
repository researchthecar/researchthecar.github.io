**Introduction:**

The 2021 Ferrari 488 Pista is a high-performance sports car powered by a twin-turbocharged 3.9-liter V8 engine. It produces 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, making it one of the most powerful Ferraris ever produced. The 488 Pista is highly praised for its exceptional handling, blistering acceleration, and stunning design. However, like many high-performance vehicles, it has also been known to experience some issues.

**Engine**

**3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive smoke from the engine bay, loss of power, and a loud whistling noise.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected turbocharger(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall No. 22V-558

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Clutch Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, and a burning smell from the transmission.
    * Solution: Replacement of the clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle or electrical components malfunctioning after the car has been parked overnight.
    * Solution: Battery replacement or repair of any electrical faults.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **ECU Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, and dashboard warning lights illuminating.
    * Solution: Replacement of the ECU.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulating inside the headlight housing.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected headlight(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen not responding, navigation system not working, or audio system issues.
    * Solution: Firmware update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A clunking or knocking noise coming from the front of the vehicle, especially during turns.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected bushing(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Ferrari 488 Pista is a highly capable and desirable sports car, but it has been known to experience some issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem, but they can be significant for a vehicle of this caliber. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and budget accordingly for repairs or maintenance.
