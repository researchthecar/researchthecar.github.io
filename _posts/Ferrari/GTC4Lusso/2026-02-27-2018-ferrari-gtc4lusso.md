# 2018 Ferrari GTC4Lusso Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Ferrari GTC4Lusso is a high-performance grand tourer blending everyday usability with exotic performance, offering a choice between a twin-turbo V8 and a naturally aspirated V12. Prospective buyers exploring the used market should evaluate powertrain health and overall condition carefully, as maintenance and repair costs for exotic grand tourers demand dedicated budgeting.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 602 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and rear-wheel drive dynamics. Proper maintenance of the turbocharging systems and cooling components is essential for long-term reliability in used models.

### 6.3L Naturally Aspirated V12

The 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 generates roughly 680 horsepower and 514 lb-ft of torque, paired with an advanced all-wheel-drive system. This high-revving flagship engine provides a classic Ferrari acoustic experience alongside demanding specialized maintenance requirements.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters across both engine variants. Routine fluid changes and careful inspection of clutch pack operation are vital for maintaining smooth performance on pre-owned examples.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V089000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If a door cannot be opened with the external handle in the case of an emergency, it can increase the risk of injury for the driver or passengers.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will install new door locks and cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ferrari GTC4Lusso stands out as a versatile and thrilling luxury grand tourer, provided it has been meticulously maintained by previous owners. Buyers should secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a marque specialist to verify the condition of the engine, transmission, and electronics before finalizing a purchase.
