# 2020 Ferrari GTC4Lusso Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Ferrari GTC4Lusso represents a unique blend of grand touring luxury, practicality, and high-performance engineering from Maranello. Shopping for a used exotic grand tourer requires careful evaluation of its complex powertrain options and high-end electronics. While formal owner complaints in public databases are sparse for this low-volume supercar, prospective buyers should still approach shopping with a thorough inspection of mechanical and electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 602 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, powering the rear wheels. It provides blistering acceleration and a characteristic Ferrari exhaust note while utilizing modern forced-induction technology.

### 6.3L Naturally Aspirated V12

The flagship 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 produces around 680 horsepower and 514 lb-ft of torque, paired with Ferrari's advanced 4RM-S four-wheel-drive system. It offers a traditional high-revving supercar experience combined with all-weather capability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-column-mounted paddle shifters. It is engineered to handle high output efficiently, though proper maintenance history is essential for smooth long-term operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Ferrari GTC4Lusso is an extraordinary machine that combines everyday usability with supercar performance, whether chosen with the twin-turbo V8 or the iconic naturally aspirated V12. Because public complaint volumes are minimal, buyers must rely heavily on independent pre-purchase inspections by marque specialists, service history verification, and comprehensive checks of all onboard electronics.
