# 2019 Ferrari GTC4Lusso Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Ferrari GTC4Lusso is a high-performance grand tourer offering a choice of potent engine options and sophisticated all-wheel or rear-wheel-drive systems. As an exotic Italian performance car, shopping for a used model requires careful attention to maintenance history and specialized pre-purchase inspections. While formal owner complaint databases show a clean slate for this specific model year, prospective buyers should always budget for the high operating costs typical of exotic grand tourers.

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust horsepower and strong low-end torque for rapid acceleration. It provides a thrilling driving experience while serving as the rear-wheel-drive powertrain option for the lineup.

### 6.3L Naturally Aspirated V12

The 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine produces exceptional high-revving power and a characteristic exotic exhaust note. Paired with an advanced four-wheel-drive system, it represents the flagship performance tier for the model.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. It offers smooth operation during relaxed cruising alongside aggressive shifts in track modes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V089000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If a door cannot be opened with the external handle in the case of an emergency, it can increase the risk of injury for the driver or passengers.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will install new door locks and cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
