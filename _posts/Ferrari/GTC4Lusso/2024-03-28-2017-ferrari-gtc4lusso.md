**Introduction:**

The 2017 Ferrari GTC4Lusso is a high-performance grand tourer that combines luxury, style, and exceptional performance. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 6.3-liter V12 engine that produces 680 horsepower and 514 lb-ft of torque, paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission. The GTC4Lusso is praised for its thrilling driving experience, spacious cabin, and sleek exterior design. However, it has also been subject to several issues affecting different aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**6.3-liter V12**

**Engine Oil Leak:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking engine oil, resulting in oil drops under the vehicle or on the engine components.
Solution: Replacing the faulty engine oil pan or gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch**

**Gearbox Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping, or complete gearbox failure.
Solution: Replacing the faulty gearbox or internal components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle or complete electrical failure.
Solution: Inspecting the electrical system for parasitic drains, replacing the faulty battery, or updating software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Door handles failing to open or close properly, becoming loose, or breaking off.
Solution: Replacing the faulty door handles or internal mechanisms.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Navigation System Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning navigation system, including freezing, incorrect directions, or unresponsive touchscreen.
Solution: Updating the navigation software or replacing the faulty navigation unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the Ferrari GTC4Lusso offers an exhilarating driving experience and luxurious amenities, it has also been subject to a number of issues affecting its engine, transmission, electrical system, exterior, and interior components. These issues can range from relatively minor annoyances to more serious malfunctions that can impact the vehicle's performance and reliability. The cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the nature of the issue and the parts that need to be replaced.
