# 2017 Ferrari GTC4Lusso Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ferrari GTC4Lusso is a high-performance grand tourer combining V12 or V8 twin-turbo power with all-wheel drive luxury. While exotic vehicles of this caliber demand meticulous maintenance and specialized service, available owner feedback for this model year is relatively sparse.

---

## Engine

### 6.3L V12

This naturally aspirated 6.3-liter V12 engine produces approximately 680 horsepower and 514 lb-ft of torque, delivering thrilling performance and a signature exhaust note. Buyers should expect high routine maintenance costs and professional inspections prior to purchase.

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers around 602 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, offering robust low-end power and everyday usability. As with any high-output turbocharged exotic, comprehensive service records are essential for peace of mind.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering-column-mounted paddle shifters. Proper clutch calibration and fluid maintenance are vital for long-term reliability and smooth operation.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Owner reports indicate potential hurdles in swiftly completing manufacturer safety recall campaigns through authorized networks.

**Air Bag Recall Service Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience difficulty scheduling timely repair appointments at local dealerships for open air bag safety recalls.
- **Solution:** Contact authorized Ferrari service centers to coordinate recall scheduling and secure alternative transportation options if necessary.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V089000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If a door cannot be opened with the external handle in the case of an emergency, it can increase the risk of injury for the driver or passengers.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will install new door locks and cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
