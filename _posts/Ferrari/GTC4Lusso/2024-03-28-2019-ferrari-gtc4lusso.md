**Introduction**

The 2019 Ferrari GTC4Lusso is a high-performance grand tourer that combines practicality with luxury. It is powered by a 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine that produces 680 horsepower and 514 lb-ft of torque. Power is sent to all four wheels through a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission.

The GTC4Lusso is praised for its stunning design, luxurious interior, and blistering performance. However, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

6.3L V12

* **Intake Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear on intake valves can cause misfires, reduced power, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace worn intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning fuel injectors can lead to engine misfires, rough running, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

7-Speed Dual-Clutch

* **Clutch Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive clutch wear can cause slipping, difficulty shifting, and premature clutch failure.
    * Solution: Replace worn clutches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the transmission fluid can cause low fluid levels, overheating, and transmission failure.
    * Solution: Repair or replace leaking components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn lower control arm bushings can cause excessive play, vibration, and unstable handling.
    * Solution: Replace worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per wheel
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: A parasitic drain on the battery can lead to a dead battery and difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Identify and isolate the source of the drain and repair the faulty component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Front Bumper Damage**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front bumper is susceptible to damage from road debris and other obstacles.
    * Solution: Repair or replace damaged bumper.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment Screen Lag**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen can experience occasional lag or freezing.
    * Solution: Update software or replace faulty screen if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Ferrari GTC4Lusso is a high-performance grand tourer that offers stunning design, luxurious interior, and blistering performance. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the more common problems include intake valve failure, fuel injector failure, clutch wear, and transmission fluid leaks. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are generally infrequent and do not detract from the overall driving experience.
