**Introduction:**

The 2023 Ferrari SF90 Stradale is a high-performance hybrid sports car. It is powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 769 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a three-electric motor system that produces an additional 217 horsepower, resulting in a combined output of 986 horsepower and 800 lb-ft of torque. The SF90 Stradale is available with a seven-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the SF90 Stradale praise its incredible performance, advanced technology, and stunning design. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, increased emissions, whistling or whining noises
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping, delayed gear engagement
    * Solution: Replace or repair transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace or repair battery, check electrical system for parasitic drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, warning lights, loss of functionality
    * Solution: Repair or replace damaged electrical harnesses
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes, crashes, unresponsive to inputs
    * Solution: Software updates, system reset, hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Ferrari SF90 Stradale is a high-performance vehicle that has been praised for its performance, technology, and design. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
