# 2021 Ferrari SF90 Stradale Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ferrari SF90 Stradale represents the pinnacle of plug-in hybrid supercar performance, combining a twin-turbocharged V8 with electric motors to deliver astonishing output. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating such an advanced, ultra-low-volume exotic requires looking closely at bespoke powertrain engineering and complex electrical architectures. While widespread owner complaint patterns are sparse given the car's exclusivity, buyers should always verify service histories and remaining warranty coverage.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Hybrid

Featuring a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 paired with three electric motors producing roughly 986 total horsepower, this powertrain delivers hypercar-tier acceleration. Maintenance requires specialized technician care, and buyers should ensure all factory software updates and routine services have been strictly performed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

This rapid-shifting 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power to all four wheels while omitting a traditional reverse gear (relying instead on the electric motors). It offers lightning-fast gear changes suited for track or street driving, though proper clutch calibration and fluid maintenance are vital.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V136000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Stradale&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When a child seat is installed on the passenger seat (size M), the deployment of the air bag may impact the child seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Air bag impact with a child seat can increase the risk of injury to the occupant.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the size M passenger seat with a size XL passenger seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Purchasing a pre-owned 2021 Ferrari SF90 Stradale demands careful inspection by an authorized specialist, given its sophisticated hybrid drivetrain and exotic components. Although standard complaint data is minimal for this elite model, verifying a comprehensive dealer service record is essential to protect your investment.
