# 2023 Ferrari SF90 Stradale Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ferrari SF90 Stradale represents the pinnacle of plug-in hybrid supercar performance, pairing a twin-turbocharged V8 with electric motors. Because used-car reliability data for ultra-low-volume exotic hybrids relies heavily on specialized owner experiences and manufacturer maintenance schedules, buyers should approach the marketplace with careful inspection habits and ensure proper dealer service history.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8 with Tri-Motor Hybrid System

This high-output powertrain combines a 3.9-liter twin-turbo V8 with three electric motors to deliver approximately 986 horsepower. It offers blistering performance, though maintenance requires certified technician expertise and specialized diagnostic equipment.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

An 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission manages power delivery to the wheels. It provides lightning-fast gear changes suited for track and road driving alike.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V136000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Stradale&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** When a child seat is installed on the passenger seat (size M), the deployment of the air bag may impact the child seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Air bag impact with a child seat can increase the risk of injury to the occupant.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the size M passenger seat with a size XL passenger seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V698000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pipes, Hoses, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Stradale&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the turbocharger oil delivery pipes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ferrari SF90 Stradale is an extreme exotic hybrid focused on maximum performance rather than high-volume commuter durability. Used-car buyers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections by a qualified specialist, verify all hybrid system components, and confirm that routine maintenance intervals have been strictly followed.
