# 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale

### Overview
The 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale represents the pinnacle of hybrid supercar performance, combining a potent V8 engine with advanced electric motors. As a low-volume, high-performance exotic, its reliability is generally excellent, focusing on sophisticated systems rather than common automotive frailties. Owners typically experience exceptional build quality and cutting-edge technology. However, the complexity of its hybrid powertrain and bespoke components means that any issues, while rare, can be intricate and costly to resolve. Maintenance is specialized, and proactive care is crucial to preserving its peak performance and longevity. The SF90 Stradale is engineered for exhilarating driving, and with proper servicing, it delivers a remarkable ownership experience.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale exhibits a high level of reliability, characteristic of modern supercars with meticulous engineering. Major mechanical failures are exceptionally rare. The primary area where minor issues have been noted is within the complex hybrid electrical system, specifically related to control units or sensors, which can manifest as intermittent faults. These are typically resolved with software updates or component replacement. Other systems, including the V8 engine, dual-clutch transmission, suspension, exterior, and interior components, are generally robust and free from widespread problems. Owners should expect specialized maintenance and be prepared for potentially high repair costs should any rare issues arise, particularly concerning the hybrid powertrain.
