# 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale represents the pinnacle of hybrid performance from Maranello, combining a twin-turbocharged V8 engine with advanced electric motors for breathtaking speed. As an exotic plug-in hybrid supercar, it features cutting-edge technology and lightweight materials throughout its construction. Prospective used-car buyers should note that while official owner complaint volume is extremely low for this exclusive model, maintaining such a complex high-performance machine requires meticulous attention to service history and specialized dealership support.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Hybrid

A 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 paired with a plug-in hybrid electric system, producing a combined 986 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. This sophisticated powertrain delivers ferocious acceleration, though its immense complexity demands professional specialist maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

An 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission derived from Ferrari's Formula 1 experience, routing power exclusively to all four wheels. It offers lightning-fast gear changes and seamless integration with the hybrid drive units.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V136000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Stradale&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** When a child seat is installed on the passenger seat (size M), the deployment of the air bag may impact the child seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Air bag impact with a child seat can increase the risk of injury to the occupant.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the size M passenger seat with a size XL passenger seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V698000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pipes, Hoses, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Stradale&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the turbocharger oil delivery pipes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale is an engineering marvel that offers staggering performance and exclusivity.
