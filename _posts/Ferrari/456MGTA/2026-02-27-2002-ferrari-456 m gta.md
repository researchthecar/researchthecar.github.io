# 2002 Ferrari 456 M GTA

### Overview
The 2002 Ferrari 456 M GTA represents a pinnacle of grand touring performance from its era, blending a potent V12 engine with a more accessible automatic transmission. As a relatively low-production exotic, its reliability data is less extensive than mass-market vehicles. However, for its class, the 456 M GTA is generally considered a robust machine, with owners often prioritizing meticulous maintenance. The focus for this model is on the sophisticated V12 powertrain and its associated systems. While exotic cars inherently demand higher maintenance costs and specialized attention, the 456 M GTA's engineering is largely sound, with most reported issues stemming from age and the complexity of its high-performance components rather than fundamental design flaws. Owners should expect the need for specialized servicing.

---


## Engine

### 5.5L V12 (F116)
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks around hoses and radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of aged hoses, seals, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500

**Timing Belt Service**
- **Symptoms:** Preventative maintenance item; failure can be catastrophic.
- **Solution:** Scheduled replacement of timing belts, tensioners, and idlers.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000

**Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks onto exhaust manifolds, burning oil smell, potential for misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 4-Speed Automatic (GTA)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering, delayed engagement, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible fluid leaks, low fluid level, potential for transmission damage.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, gaskets, or transmission cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Dampers
**Electronic Damper Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, clunking noises, dashboard warning lights, inconsistent damping.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty dampers.

**Window Regulator Failure**

### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Ferrari 456 M GTA, while a magnificent V12 grand tourer, requires diligent ownership and specialized maintenance. The powertrain, particularly the 5.5L V12 engine and its automatic transmission, are generally robust but demand adherence to strict service intervals, especially for the timing belt. Cooling system integrity and potential gasket leaks are common age-related concerns. Electrical gremlins, particularly with window regulators and climate control, can arise. Suspension components, especially the adaptive dampers, may require attention over time. Overall, the 456 M GTA offers a rewarding ownership experience for those prepared for the associated costs and complexities of maintaining a high-performance Italian exotic.
