# 2016 Ferrari FF Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ferrari FF represents a unique chapter for the brand as its first production all-wheel-drive grand tourer, powered by a high-revving naturally aspirated V12 engine. For prospective used-car buyers, exploring an exotic grand tourer of this caliber requires careful consideration of mechanical complexity and specialized maintenance history. While owner-reported complaints for this model year are minimal, routine inspections by a qualified Ferrari specialist are essential before purchase to ensure long-term reliability.

---

## Engine

### 6.3L V12

The 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 produces approximately 651 horsepower and 504 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptional performance and high-revving thrills. This engine is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should expect significantly elevated service costs associated with exotic V12 ownership.

## Transmission

### 7-speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes and transfers power to Ferrari's proprietary 4RM all-wheel-drive system. Smooth operation depends heavily on timely fluid changes and software health, making a pre-purchase diagnostic scan critical.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=FF&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=FF&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ferrari FF is an extraordinary grand tourer that combines V12 performance with everyday practicality, provided it has been meticulously maintained. Prospective buyers should prioritize vehicles with a comprehensive service history and budget accordingly for specialized exotic car upkeep.
