# 2018 Ferrari 812 Superfast Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Evaluating a high-performance exotic grand tourer like the 2018 Ferrari 812 Superfast involves understanding its extraordinary engineering and exotic maintenance nature. While broad complaint databases show minimal widespread recurring owner defects for this model year, prospective buyers should always prioritize a rigorous pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist given the complexities of its high-output naturally aspirated V12 and dual-clutch transmission.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L Naturally Aspirated V12

The 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine produces approximately 789 horsepower and 530 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering performance and an iconic exhaust note. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers must budget for specialized servicing.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes tailored to the rear-wheel-drive layout of the 812 Superfast. Routine fluid checks and software updates by certified technicians are vital to maintaining its precision operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V614000: Visibility](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=812+Superfast&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The rear window could fully detach from the vehicle, creating a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear window, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=812+Superfast&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ferrari 812 Superfast remains an engineering masterpiece delivering immense V12 performance. Because official owner complaint data is sparse and exotic maintenance requires specialized expertise, buyers should invest in a thorough independent PPI to verify the condition of the powertrain and electronics before purchasing.
