# 2019 Ferrari 812 Superfast Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Ferrari 812 Superfast is an exotic front-engine, rear-wheel-drive grand tourer celebrated for its staggering performance and naturally aspirated V12 soundtrack. As a high-performance Italian flagship, shopping for a used model requires careful attention to maintenance history, electronic complexity, and specialized servicing costs. While low-mileage examples are common, buyers should prioritize comprehensive inspection records to ensure optimal mechanical and electrical health.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L Naturally Aspirated V12

This 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine produces approximately 789 horsepower and 530 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and a high-revving character. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though regular fluid checks and adherence to service intervals are essential for exotic V12 longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, tailored for high-performance driving. Overall reliability is strong, but prospective buyers should verify smooth clutch engagement and responsive shifting during a pre-purchase test drive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=812+Superfast&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V614000: Visibility](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=812+Superfast&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rear window could fully detach from the vehicle, creating a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear window, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Ferrari 812 Superfast offers monumental performance paired with exotic grand touring capability, making it a compelling choice for collectors and driving enthusiasts alike. Because formal complaint data for this low-volume exotic is sparse, buyers must rely heavily on independent specialist inspections, verified service history, and a thorough mechanical check before purchase.
