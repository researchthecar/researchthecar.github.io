**Introduction:**

The 2021 Ferrari 812 Superfast is a high-performance grand tourer that delivers exceptional speed and agility. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 6.5L V12 engine that produces an astounding 789 horsepower and 530 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, allowing for blistering acceleration and top speeds exceeding 200 mph. While widely praised for its thrilling performance and sleek design, the 812 Superfast has faced some specific issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**6.5L V12 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:** Cracked intake manifolds can lead to reduced engine power, misfiring, and excessive exhaust emissions. Solution: Replace the intake manifold and gaskets. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Oil Leakage:** Leaks from the oil pan, valve covers, or seals can result in oil loss and potential engine damage. Solution: Inspect and replace the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Fuel Pump Failure:** Malfunctioning fuel pumps can cause engine stalling, reduced performance, and difficulty starting. Solution: Replace the fuel pump and filter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Recall Number: 21V-165: Fuel tank may not meet crashworthiness requirements

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Clutch Slippage:** Worn or damaged clutch components can lead to reduced power transmission, grinding noises, and vibration. Solution: Replace the clutch assembly. Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,000+
* **Shifting Problems:** Difficulty shifting gears, delayed or failed shifts, or grinding noises during shifting indicate transmission issues. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Excessive battery discharge can cause premature battery failure and difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution: Inspect and identify the source of the parasitic drain and repair or replace the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Electrical System Malfunction:** Faulty wiring, loose connections, or damaged sensors can lead to various electrical problems, including instrument cluster issues, power window failure, and airbag warning lights. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Carbon Fiber Body Panel Defects:** Some owners have reported defects in the carbon fiber exterior panels, such as misaligned or loose panels. Solution: Repair or replace the affected panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Occasional glitches and software bugs can occur, causing the infotainment system to freeze, display errors, or become unresponsive. Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the system if necessary. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Seat Adjustment Issues:** Malfunctioning seat motors or faulty switches can affect the ability to adjust the seats for comfort and support. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the affected components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Issues with the air conditioning system, such as insufficient cooling or heating, can arise. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The Ferrari 812 Superfast is a highly capable and desirable sports car, but it has experienced some specific issues that owners have encountered. The engine and transmission issues are the most significant concerns, with intake manifold failures and clutch slippage being particularly costly to repair. Electrical and exterior problems are less common but can still be frustrating for owners. The infotainment system glitches and seat adjustment issues affect the vehicle's interior usability. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues reported for the 812 Superfast vary, and some matters are relatively minor, while others can be more significant and expensive to resolve.
