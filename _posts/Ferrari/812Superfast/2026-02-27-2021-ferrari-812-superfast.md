# 2021 Ferrari 812 Superfast Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ferrari 812 Superfast stands as an exotic, high-performance machine powered by a magnificent naturally aspirated V12 engine. For prospective buyers exploring the used exotic market, understanding baseline powertrain options and minor owner-reported quirks helps set realistic expectations for ownership and maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L V12

The 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 produces approximately 789 horsepower and 530 lb-ft of torque, delivering breathtaking performance. This high-revving powerhouse generally operates reliably when properly maintained, though routine servicing costs remain exceptionally high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes suited for track-capable acceleration. It provides seamless operation when functioning correctly, though prospective buyers should ensure thorough pre-purchase inspections of the clutch packs.

## Exterior

### Mirrors

Exterior components generally reflect exotic manufacturing standards, with isolated manual or feature documentation discrepancies reported by owners.

**Auto-Dimming Feature Discrepancy**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that while the owner's manual states the vehicle can activate or deactivate the auto-dimming rearview and side mirrors, the vehicle is not actually equipped with this feature.
- **Solution:** Verify mirror specifications directly with the vehicle build sheet or dealer, as manual documentation may contain inaccuracies.
