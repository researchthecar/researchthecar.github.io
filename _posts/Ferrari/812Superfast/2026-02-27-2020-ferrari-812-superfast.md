# 2020 Ferrari 812 Superfast Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Ferrari 812 Superfast stands at the pinnacle of naturally aspirated V12 performance, offering breathtaking acceleration and exotic styling. For prospective used-car buyers, researching this flagship grand tourer involves understanding its immense capabilities alongside the specialized maintenance associated with high-end Italian exotics. While widespread recurring mechanical complaint patterns are minimal, thorough pre-purchase inspections remain essential to ensure optimal health of its complex powertrain and electronics.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L V12

Features a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine producing approximately 789 horsepower and 530 lb-ft of torque. This masterpiece of engineering delivers ferocious high-RPM performance, though buyers should budget for meticulous, specialist-level maintenance.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Equipped with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission that provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. The unit is responsive and engaging, though proper service history is vital to ensure long-term clutch and actuator health.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V614000: Visibility](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=812+Superfast&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear window could fully detach from the vehicle, creating a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear window, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Ferrari 812 Superfast is an extraordinary machine that delivers a visceral driving experience matched by few modern automobiles. Buyers looking at the used market should prioritize examples with comprehensive dealer service records and perform a rigorous inspection by a qualified Ferrari specialist. Approaching ownership with a healthy maintenance budget ensures that this exotic V12 continues to deliver an uncompromised driving thrill.
