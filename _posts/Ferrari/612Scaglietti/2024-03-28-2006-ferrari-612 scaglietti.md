**Introduction:**
The 2006 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is a grand tourer produced by Ferrari. It features a naturally aspirated 5.7-liter V12 engine producing 540 horsepower and 434 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automated manual transmission. The 612 Scaglietti is known for its sleek design, exhilarating performance, and luxurious interior. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its potential issues.

**Engine**
**5.7L V12**

* **Oil pan gasket leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil seepage or a small puddle of oil beneath the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Intake manifold coolant leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, overheating, or coolant smell in the engine bay.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket or entire intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

* **Six-speed automated manual transmission shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder during gear changes, particularly in lower gears.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $15,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension control arm bushing wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling noises from the front suspension, poor handling, and excessive tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the worn control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Battery discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially when the car is left unused for extended periods.
    * Solution: Inspect for parasitic drain or faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
* **Ignition coil failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, poor performance, or difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace the affected ignition coil.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500 per coil

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Instrument cluster failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges or lights malfunctioning, inaccurate readings.
    * Solution: Replace the instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Summary:**
The 2006 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is a highly desirable vehicle with impressive performance and luxurious features. However, like any high-performance vehicle, it is susceptible to certain issues. The most significant issues include oil pan gasket leaks, intake manifold coolant leaks, transmission shudder, and battery discharge. While the cost of repairs can be substantial, the rarity and exclusivity of the 612 Scaglietti often outweigh these potential problems for enthusiasts and collectors.
