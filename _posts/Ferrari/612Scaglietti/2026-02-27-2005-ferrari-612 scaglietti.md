# 2005 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti

### Overview
The 2005 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti, a grand tourer designed for comfortable high-speed travel, combines V12 power with a more practical 2+2 seating arrangement. While generally built to Ferrari's exacting standards, as with any high-performance, low-volume exotic, certain areas can present unique challenges for owners. This analysis focuses on documented reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, highlighting potential areas for attention for prospective or current owners. The 612 Scaglietti utilizes a potent V12 engine and a sophisticated transaxle system, both of which are central to its performance but also potential sources of specialized repair needs.

---


## Engine

### 5.7L V12 (F133 F)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idle, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Coolant Leaks (Hose/Radiator)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, overheating, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses or radiator.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Stalling, difficulty starting, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty sensor.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Transmission

### F1 (Automated Manual) Transmission
**Hydraulic Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or jerky gear changes, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the hydraulic pump and potentially fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $5000

**Clutch Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting, grinding noises, clutch warning light.
- **Solution:** Adjustment or replacement of the clutch actuator.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

## Suspension

### Adaptive Dampers
**Electronic Control Module (ECM) Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Suspension warning lights, inconsistent damping, harsh ride.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the suspension ECM.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Damper Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced ride quality, visible fluid leaks from dampers.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking dampers.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per damper


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, door locks, interior lights, or infotainment system.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential repair or replacement of the BCM or related wiring.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $3000


### Infotainment System
**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Freezing, unresponsiveness, inaccurate GPS.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000

### Summary Verdict
The 2005 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is a capable grand tourer with a powerful V12 engine. While its core mechanicals are robust, owners should be aware of potential issues common to high-end, low-volume exotics. The F1 transmission, particularly its hydraulic system, can be a significant expense. Electrical gremlins related to the Body Control Module and infotainment system are also noted. Engine components like ignition coils and sensors can fail, requiring specialized attention. Suspension electronics and headlight sealing are other areas to monitor. Despite these potential challenges, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to issues, the 612 Scaglietti can remain a rewarding ownership experience.
