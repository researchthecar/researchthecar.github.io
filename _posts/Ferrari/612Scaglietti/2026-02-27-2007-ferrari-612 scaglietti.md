# 2007 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti

### Overview
The 2007 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti, a grand tourer known for its V12 power and luxurious appointments, generally exhibits strong mechanical reliability for its class. However, as with any high-performance exotic, specific areas can require attention. Owners often report satisfaction with the powertrain's robustness, but the complexity of its systems means that specialized maintenance and occasional component failures are not uncommon. This analysis focuses on documented recurring issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 5.7L V12
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Coolant Leaks (Hose/Radiator)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, overheating, sweet smell, puddles under the car.
- **Solution:** Replacement of damaged hoses or radiator.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000+

**Timing Belt Service**
- **Symptoms:** N/A (Preventative Maintenance)
- **Solution:** Scheduled replacement of timing belts and associated components (tensioners, idlers).
- **Cost:** $2500 - $4000+


## Transmission

### F1 (Automated Manual)
**Clutch Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, 'gearbox failure' warning light, jerky shifts.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the clutch actuator system.
- **Cost:** $3000 - $6000+

**Hydraulic Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or non-responsive gear changes, warning lights related to hydraulic pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000+

## Suspension

### Adaptive Dampers
**Electronic Damper Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, clunking noises, uneven handling, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty adaptive dampers.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500+ per corner


## Electrical

### General Electronics
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive buttons, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of head unit components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500+

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is a capable grand tourer with a generally robust V12 engine. However, its exotic nature and complex systems, particularly the F1 automated manual transmission and adaptive suspension, present potential reliability concerns. Owners should budget for specialized maintenance, including the critical timing belt service. Common issues revolve around the F1 transmission's hydraulic system and clutch actuators, as well as electronic components like window regulators and infotainment glitches. While the core mechanicals are strong, proactive maintenance and addressing warning lights promptly are crucial for long-term ownership satisfaction and to mitigate costly repairs.
