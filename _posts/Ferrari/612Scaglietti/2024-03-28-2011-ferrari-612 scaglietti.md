**Introduction:**

The 2011 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is a high-performance grand tourer with a naturally aspirated V12 engine. The 6.3-liter V12 produces 540 horsepower and 434 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 6-speed F1 paddle-shift transmission. It is praised for its exceptional handling, thrilling engine note, and luxurious interior, but some owners have reported certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **6.3L V12:**
    * **Sticking Valves:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, reduced fuel efficiency
        * Solution: Replace affected valves and valve guides
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Oil Leaks:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks under the vehicle, low oil levels
        * Solution: Replace leaking seals or gaskets
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed F1 Paddle-Shift:**
    * **Shifting Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace or repair defective clutch or solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible leak of fluid from the transmission area
        * Solution: Replace leaking seals or hoses
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, reduced handling
        * Solution: Replace worn bushings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive bounce or instability while driving
        * Solution: Replace defective shock absorbers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery loses charge, difficulty starting the vehicle
        * Solution: Replace battery or check for parasitic drain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Electrical Shorts:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, random electrical malfunctions
        * Solution: Identify and repair affected wiring or components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Carbon Fiber Bonnet Damage:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or warping on the carbon fiber bonnet
        * Solution: Replace affected bonnet
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Headlight Condensation:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlights
        * Solution: Replace or repair affected headlights
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Trim Peeling:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Trim on the steering wheel peeling or becoming discolored
        * Solution: Replace affected trim
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Infotainment System Glitches:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Software bugs or freezing, audio issues
        * Solution: Software updates or component replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2011 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is a highly capable and desirable vehicle, but owners should be aware of certain potential issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some minor problems requiring relatively inexpensive repairs, while others can be more significant and expensive to fix. Regular maintenance and inspections are recommended to catch any potential issues early on.
