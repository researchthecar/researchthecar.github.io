**Introduction:**

The 2007 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is a grand tourer manufactured by Ferrari. It was produced with two different engine options:

* **Engine:**
    * 5.7L V12
    * 6.0L V12
* **Transmission:**
    * 6-speed manual
    * 6-speed automated manual

The 5.7L V12 engine produces 540 horsepower and 434 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.0L V12 engine produces 588 horsepower and 434 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automated manual transmission.

The 612 Scaglietti is praised for its elegant design, powerful performance, and luxurious interior. However, like any vehicle, it has some known issues.

**Engine**

**5.7L V12**

* **No Issues Found**

**6.0L V12**

* **Connecting Rod Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noises from the engine, loss of power, and eventual engine seizure.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20,000 - $30,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automated Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Pack Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering of the clutch, difficulty shifting gears, or inability to engage gears.
    * Solution: Clutch pack replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission error codes, difficulty shifting gears, or complete loss of transmission functionality.
    * Solution: Mechatronic unit replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges prematurely, even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Identify and replace the faulty electrical component causing the drain (e.g., alternator, starter, lights, etc.).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Resealing or replacing the headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate or malfunctioning gauges, blank or flickering display.
    * Solution: Instrument cluster replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Ferrari 612 Scaglietti is generally a reliable and well-built vehicle, but it is not without its potential issues. Engine bearing failures, clutch pack issues, and mechatronic unit failures can be costly to repair. However, the severity of these issues and the cost to fix them are generally not excessive compared to other high-performance luxury vehicles.
