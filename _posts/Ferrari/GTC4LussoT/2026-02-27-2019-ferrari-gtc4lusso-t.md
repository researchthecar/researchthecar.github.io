# 2019 Ferrari GTC4Lusso T Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Ferrari GTC4Lusso T combines a practical four-seat shooting brake layout with the thrilling performance of a twin-turbo V8. As a high-end exotic grand tourer, prospective buyers should approach with careful inspection, recognizing that maintenance, electronics, and specialized systems require diligence and substantial upkeep budgets.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 602 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, sending power to the rear wheels. It delivers exhilarating acceleration and responsive power delivery, though owners should maintain rigorous servicing routines to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear shifts suited for grand touring. Routine fluid changes and careful clutch wear monitoring are essential for maintaining smooth operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V089000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso+T&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If a door cannot be opened with the external handle in the case of an emergency, it can increase the risk of injury for the driver or passengers.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will install new door locks and cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso+T&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso+T&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2019 Ferrari GTC4Lusso T offers an exotic blend of everyday usability and potent twin-turbo V8 performance.
