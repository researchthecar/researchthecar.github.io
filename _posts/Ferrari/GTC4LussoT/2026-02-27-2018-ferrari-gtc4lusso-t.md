# 2018 Ferrari GTC4Lusso T Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Ferrari GTC4Lusso T offers a unique blend of exotic performance and grand touring practicality, powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As with any high-end exotic vehicle, prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance history and ensure all safety recalls have been addressed by an authorized dealer. Publicly reported ownership complaints for this model year remain extremely limited.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 602 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, sending power to the rear wheels. It delivers ferocious acceleration while serving as the more efficiency-oriented powerplant in the GTC4Lusso lineup.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering-column-mounted paddle shifters. Regular fluid maintenance is critical to ensure smooth operation of the clutch packs over time.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety equipment reports center primarily around manufacturer recall fulfillment timelines rather than recurring mechanical failures.

**Takata Airbag Recall Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience extended wait times for parts availability when attempting to complete manufacturer safety recalls related to Takata airbag inflators.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized Ferrari dealer to schedule the recall repair and verify parts availability as soon as notices are received.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso+T&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso+T&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V089000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso+T&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If a door cannot be opened with the external handle in the case of an emergency, it can increase the risk of injury for the driver or passengers.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will install new door locks and cables, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=GTC4Lusso+T&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2018 Ferrari GTC4Lusso T is a sophisticated grand tourer that requires diligent upkeep and professional inspections prior to purchase. While owner complaints are sparse, verifying that all official recall campaigns have been completed is essential for peace of mind.
