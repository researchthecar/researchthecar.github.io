# 2022 Ferrari SF90 Spider

### Overview
The 2022 Ferrari SF90 Stradale Spider represents the pinnacle of hybrid hypercar technology, combining a potent V8 engine with an advanced electric powertrain. As a limited-production, high-performance vehicle, its reliability is generally excellent, reflecting meticulous engineering and premium componentry. Owners typically experience a high degree of satisfaction, with issues being rare and often related to the complex hybrid systems or specialized components. Maintenance is specialized and costly, as expected for a vehicle of this caliber. The focus is on delivering an unparalleled driving experience with robust performance, and for the most part, Ferrari achieves this with the SF90 Spider.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ferrari SF90 Spider demonstrates exceptional reliability for a hypercar, with most major mechanical components performing as expected. The complexity of its hybrid system is the primary area where minor electrical gremlins might surface, such as intermittent control unit faults or infotainment glitches. These are generally resolved with software updates. Given its exotic nature and cutting-edge technology, proactive and specialized maintenance is crucial. Overall, the SF90 Spider offers a robust and exhilarating ownership experience, with reliability largely meeting the high standards set by Ferrari.
