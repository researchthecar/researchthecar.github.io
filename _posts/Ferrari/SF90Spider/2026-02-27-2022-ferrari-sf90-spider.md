# 2022 Ferrari SF90 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ferrari SF90 Spider represents the pinnacle of plug-in hybrid supercar engineering, blending a twin-turbocharged V8 with three electric motors to deliver blistering performance. As with many ultra-exotic and complex hybrid vehicles, prospective buyers should approach the market with a clear understanding of maintenance realities and the importance of comprehensive pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Hybrid

The 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 works in tandem with electric motors to produce nearly 1,000 horsepower. This highly sophisticated powertrain offers staggering performance, though buyers should verify service histories given its exotic pedigree.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes derived from Ferrari's Formula 1 experience. It operates smoothly in automatic modes while offering immediate manual control via paddle shifters.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V136000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Spider&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** When a child seat is installed on the passenger seat (size M), the deployment of the air bag may impact the child seat. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Air bag impact with a child seat can increase the risk of injury to the occupant.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the size M passenger seat with a size XL passenger seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V698000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pipes, Hoses, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Spider&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the turbocharger oil delivery pipes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
