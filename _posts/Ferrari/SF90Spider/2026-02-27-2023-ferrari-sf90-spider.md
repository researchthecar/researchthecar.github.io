# 2023 Ferrari SF90 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ferrari SF90 Spider represents the pinnacle of open-top hybrid performance, combining a twin-turbocharged V8 engine with electric motors for staggering output. While prospective buyers are primarily focused on its breathtaking speed and exotic engineering, evaluating its powertrain options and monitoring owner-reported feedback helps ensure a smoother ownership experience for this high-end exotic.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Hybrid

This 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 works alongside a trio of electric motors to produce approximately 986 horsepower. It delivers blistering performance and sophisticated hybrid integration, requiring meticulous dealer maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission transfers immense power seamlessly to the rear wheels via an electronic differential, omitting a traditional mechanical reverse gear to save weight. It shifts with lightning speed, though proper specialized service is essential for optimal operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V698000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pipes, Hoses, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=SF90+Spider&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the turbocharger oil delivery pipes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ferrari SF90 Spider is an ultra-high-performance machine where complex hybrid systems and extreme power demand scrupulous service history verification. Buyers should prioritize examples with documented dealer maintenance and secure a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified exotic specialist before finalizing a purchase.
