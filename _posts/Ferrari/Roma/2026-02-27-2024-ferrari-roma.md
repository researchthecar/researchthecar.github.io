### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Ferrari Roma demonstrates strong technical reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components, the 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8 engine and the 8-speed dual-clutch transmission, with no widespread issues reported. The adaptive suspension system also appears robust. Minor concerns are primarily related to the electrical system, specifically occasional software glitches within the infotainment system and potential sensor calibration needs for ADAS features, both of which are generally manageable with software updates or recalibration. Exterior and interior components show no significant reported reliability concerns. Overall, the Roma offers a high level of dependability for a vehicle in its performance class, with most potential issues being minor and addressable.
