# 2020 Ferrari Roma

### Overview
The 2020 Ferrari Roma represents a blend of classic design and modern performance, aiming for a balance between everyday usability and exhilarating driving dynamics. As a grand tourer, its reliability is a key consideration for potential owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear. The Roma is built upon a sophisticated platform, incorporating advanced materials and engineering to ensure both performance and durability. While Ferraris are known for their exotic nature, the Roma's design philosophy also emphasizes a degree of practicality, which extends to its engineering.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Ferrari Roma demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.9L twin-turbo V8 engine and the 8-speed dual-clutch transmission are robust and have not shown widespread issues. The suspension system also appears to be well-engineered. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system's software, are the most commonly cited, though these are generally resolvable with updates. Exterior and interior components are performing as expected for a vehicle of this caliber. Overall, the Roma offers a dependable ownership experience for a high-performance exotic, with most potential issues being minor and software-related.
