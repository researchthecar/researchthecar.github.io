# 2021 Ferrari Roma Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ferrari Roma brings classic grand touring styling and modern twin-turbo performance to the entry-level Ferrari lineup. As with many exotic high-performance vehicles, evaluating pre-owned examples requires careful attention to electronics, specialized drivetrain maintenance, and complete service records to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 612 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating acceleration and robust performance, though buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals and professional inspections.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is shared with contemporary Ferrari models, offering lightning-fast shifts. Routine fluid changes and proper warm-up procedures are essential for longevity.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=Roma&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Ferrari Roma remains an enticing choice for luxury GT buyers seeking performance and everyday usability. Prospective owners should prioritize vehicles with documented dealer service history and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an exotic car specialist.
