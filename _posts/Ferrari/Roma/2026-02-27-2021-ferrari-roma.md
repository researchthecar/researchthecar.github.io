# 2021 Ferrari Roma

### Overview
The 2021 Ferrari Roma represents a blend of classic GT styling and modern performance, powered by Ferrari's acclaimed twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As a relatively new model, extensive long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, initial reports and the established reputation of Ferrari's powertrain components suggest a high level of engineering and build quality. Owners should expect a premium ownership experience, with the understanding that maintenance and potential repairs, while infrequent, can be costly due to the exotic nature of the vehicle and its specialized components. This analysis focuses on documented or commonly anticipated technical issues beyond routine maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ferrari Roma demonstrates strong initial reliability, benefiting from Ferrari's advanced engineering and robust powertrain. The twin-turbo V8 engine and 8-speed dual-clutch transmission are proven units with minimal reported issues. While the electrical system's infotainment may occasionally exhibit minor software glitches, these are typically resolved with updates. Other areas such as suspension, exterior, and interior components show no significant widespread problems. As with any high-performance luxury vehicle, potential repair costs can be substantial, emphasizing the importance of diligent maintenance and specialized servicing. Overall, the Roma is expected to offer a reliable and exhilarating driving experience for its owners.
