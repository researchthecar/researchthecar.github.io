### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ferrari Roma demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, consistent with Ferrari's reputation for building exceptional performance vehicles. The core powertrain components, including the 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8 engine and the 8-speed dual-clutch transmission, show no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the adaptive suspension, exterior elements, and interior materials are generally robust. The most commonly noted, albeit minor, concerns revolve around occasional software glitches within the infotainment system, which are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the Roma is a reliable exotic car, provided it receives diligent maintenance and care.
