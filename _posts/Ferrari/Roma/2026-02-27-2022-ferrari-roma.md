# 2022 Ferrari Roma Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ferrari Roma blends exotic styling with grand touring capability, powered by a sophisticated turbocharged V8 engine. As an ultra-luxury exotic car, prospective buyers should approach the used market with careful inspection, keeping in mind that maintenance and specialized repairs demand a significant financial investment.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 612 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance while requiring meticulous adherence to manufacturer maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes and smooth power delivery in daily driving conditions. It shares its architecture with advanced modern dual-clutch units found across high-end performance platforms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=Roma&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ferrari Roma is an alluring grand tourer offering breathtaking performance and everyday usability. Buyers evaluating a used model should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an authorized specialist to ensure all electronic and mechanical systems are in pristine condition.
