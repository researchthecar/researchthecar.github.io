**Introduction:**

The 2005 Ferrari 575 M Maranello is a high-performance grand tourer that combines elegant styling with a powerful engine. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 5.7-liter V12 engine, paired with a 6-speed manual or F1 automated manual transmission. With 508 horsepower and 434 lb-ft of torque, the 575 M Maranello offers impressive acceleration and handling. Owners praise the vehicle's distinctive design, thrilling driving experience, and premium materials. However, it is not without its drawbacks, including a stiff suspension and high maintenance costs.
