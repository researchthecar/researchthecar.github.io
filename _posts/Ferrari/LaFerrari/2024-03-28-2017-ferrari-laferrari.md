**Introduction:**

The 2017 Ferrari LaFerrari is a limited-production hybrid sports car with a naturally aspirated V12 engine paired with an electric motor. It produces 949 horsepower and 663 lb-ft of torque. Its sleek design and powerful performance have been widely praised, but some owners have reported issues with various components.

**Engine:**

**4.9L Naturally Aspirated V12**

* **Stuck Exhaust Valve:** Some engines have experienced stuck exhaust valves, leading to decreased power and performance. The valves become sticky due to carbon buildup.
    * Solution: Replace the exhaust valves and clean the exhaust system to prevent recurrence.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch (DCT)**

* **Transmission Failure:** A small number of vehicles have reported complete transmission failure. The cause is still under investigation, but it is believed to be related to a software glitch or a faulty component in the transmission.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $15,000 - $25,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Wear:** The front suspension bushings have been prone to premature wear, causing knocking and clunking noises.
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Discharge:** Some vehicles have experienced battery discharge issues, leading to difficulty starting or completely preventing the car from starting. The cause is still under investigation, but it is believed to be related to a faulty battery or a problem with the charging system.
    * Solution: Replace the battery and/or repair the charging system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Rear Wing Issue:** The rear wing on some vehicles has been known to malfunction, causing it to deploy or retract suddenly or unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the rear wing assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Navigation System Malfunction:** Some vehicles have experienced a malfunctioning navigation system, which can result in inaccurate or missing directions.
    * Solution: Update the navigation software and/or replace the navigation unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Ferrari LaFerrari is a high-performance vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components have all been subject to reported problems, some of which can be costly to repair. However, it's important to note that these issues are not widespread and affect only a small number of vehicles.
