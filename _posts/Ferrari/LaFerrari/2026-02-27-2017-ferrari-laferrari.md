# 2017 Ferrari LaFerrari Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ferrari LaFerrari is an ultra-exclusive hypercar combining a naturally aspirated V12 engine with an advanced hybrid system. As a low-volume exotic vehicle, shopping for a used model requires careful inspection by certified specialists, as maintenance and component costs are exceptionally high.

---

## Engine

### 6.3L V12 Hybrid

This powertrain pairs a 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 with an electric motor to produce a combined 950 horsepower. It is a high-strung, race-derived engine that demands meticulous specialist maintenance and offers intense performance.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is sent to the rear wheels via a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission derived from Ferrari's Formula 1 technology. It delivers lightning-fast gear changes suited for high-speed track and road driving.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=LaFerrari&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ferrari LaFerrari is a pinnacle of automotive engineering intended for serious collectors. Prospective buyers should prioritize comprehensive service records and professional pre-purchase inspections by factory-trained technicians to ensure optimal mechanical health.
