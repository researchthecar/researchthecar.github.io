**Introduction**

The 2015 Ferrari LaFerrari is a limited-production hybrid hypercar. It is powered by a 6.3-liter V12 engine and an electric motor, which combine to produce 950 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. The LaFerrari is only available with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. It is praised for its exceptional performance, handling, and exclusivity. However, it is also known for its high price and limited availability.

**Engine**
* **6.3-liter V12**

**Transmission**
* **7-speed Dual-Clutch**
