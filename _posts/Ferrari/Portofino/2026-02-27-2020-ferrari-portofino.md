# 2020 Ferrari Portofino Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Ferrari Portofino is a striking and capable grand touring convertible that brings everyday usability to the Prancing Horse lineup. Buyers looking at a pre-owned model will find an exotic machine designed for performance and comfort, though maintenance and upkeep require careful consideration. As with many high-end exotic sports cars, reviewing service records and checking all electronic and mechanical systems prior to purchase is essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 591 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is generally robust when properly maintained, but regular specialist servicing is critical to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes and smooth operation in automatic mode. Buyers should verify proper clutch engagement and smooth shifting during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=Portofino&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Ferrari Portofino offers a compelling mix of supercar performance and convertible daily-driving practicality. While major mechanical complaints are rare in owner reports, prospective buyers should prioritize vehicles with complete dealership service histories and perform a thorough inspection of all mechanical and electrical features.
