**Introduction**

The 2022 Ferrari Portofino M is a stunning convertible sports car that combines performance, style, and luxury. It features a 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 620 horsepower and 560 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The Portofino M offers a thrilling driving experience, with sharp handling and a top speed of over 199 mph. Its elegant exterior design and luxurious interior complement its performance capabilities. While the Portofino M is highly praised for its performance and aesthetics, some owners have reported various issues.

**Engine**

**3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, whistling sound
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust, overheating
    * Solution: Replacement of head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Clutch Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty changing gears, loss of power under acceleration
    * Solution: Replacement of clutch discs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Control Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to shift gears, transmission warning lights on dashboard
    * Solution: Replacement of transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorbers Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, excessive bouncing, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replacement of front shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting car
    * Solution: Battery replacement, diagnosis and repair of electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning electrical system, charging light on dashboard
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to open or close sunroof, excessive wind noise
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of sunroof mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning gauges, blank or flickering display
    * Solution: Replacement of instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the Ferrari Portofino M is a highly desirable sports car, it has experienced several issues affecting various components. The most common issues seem to be related to the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the repairs needed. It is important for owners to be aware of these issues and seek professional diagnosis and repair if any symptoms arise.
