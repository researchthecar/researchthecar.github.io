# 2023 Ferrari Portofino M Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Ferrari Portofino M represents the pinnacle of modern grand touring, pairing open-top versatility with the breathtaking performance of a twin-turbo V8. As an exotic high-performance vehicle, buyers should approach the market focusing heavily on official maintenance history, professional pre-purchase inspections, and the understanding that ownership costs reflect its exotic pedigree. While formal owner complaint data for this specific model year remains virtually non-existent, proper due diligence is always recommended when investing in an elite Italian grand tourer.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.855-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 612 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and classic Ferrari character. It is generally robust when subjected to regular, specialist-maintained service intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes while maintaining smooth operation during casual cruising. Buyers should ensure smooth engagement across all modes during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V810000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=Portofino+M&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The driveshaft screws may not have been tightened during production and may loosen. Driveshaft screws that loosen may result in a loss of vehicle traction and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will verify the tightening of the driveshaft screws and, if necessary, perform the tightening process, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Ferrari Portofino M is an exhilarating and sophisticated machine that offers world-class performance and luxury. Because public owner complaint volumes are extremely low, prospective buyers should prioritize inspecting service records, verifying remaining factory warranty coverage, and securing a comprehensive inspection from an authorized Ferrari specialist.
