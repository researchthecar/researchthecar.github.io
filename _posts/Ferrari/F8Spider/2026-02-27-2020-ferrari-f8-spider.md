# 2020 Ferrari F8 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Ferrari F8 Spider represents the pinnacle of mid-engine Italian performance, featuring a twin-turbocharged V8 engine that delivers breathtaking power and exhilarating open-top driving dynamics. As an exotic high-performance sports car, prospective buyers should approach ownership with an understanding of specialized maintenance requirements and the complexities associated with modern exotic powertrains. While formal owner complaint logs for this specific model year remain sparse, evaluating potential mechanical and electrical systems is crucial when considering a pre-owned purchase.

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbo V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and track-ready performance. Proper maintenance and regular servicing are essential to keep this high-strung exotic powerplant operating reliably.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, tailored for aggressive driving and precise control. It is a sophisticated automated manual system that requires careful inspection for clutch wear and software health during a pre-purchase evaluation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F8+Spider&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
