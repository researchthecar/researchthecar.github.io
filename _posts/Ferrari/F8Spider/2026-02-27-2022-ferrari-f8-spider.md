# 2022 Ferrari F8 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Ferrari F8 Spider is a high-performance exotic convertible powered by a mid-rear-mounted twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As an ultra-low-volume exotic sports car, comprehensive long-term reliability data and owner complaint logs are exceptionally scarce. Prospective buyers looking at pre-owned models should prioritize rigorous pre-purchase inspections by certified specialists and complete service history documentation.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and exotic performance. This high-output powerplant requires meticulous maintenance and premium fluids to maintain long-term reliability under enthusiastic driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, tailored for aggressive track use and smooth street driving. It is a highly complex electro-hydraulic unit that demands specialist service intervals and thorough testing during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F8+Spider&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Ferrari F8 Spider is a thrilling exotic convertible with immense performance capabilities and minimal documented owner complaints. Because standard automotive reliability databases have very limited data points for low-production exotic vehicles, buyers must rely heavily on professional PPIs, verified maintenance records, and remaining factory warranty coverage.
