# 2021 Ferrari F8 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Ferrari F8 Spider represents the pinnacle of mid-engine V8 drop-top performance, combining breathtaking Italian design with supercar capability. While ownership experiences for exotic cars of this caliber are typically shaped by meticulous maintenance schedules and specialized servicing rather than mass-market reliability trends, prospective buyers should still approach the pre-owned market with thorough inspection routines. Understanding the specific powertrain characteristics and checking for any lingering campaign or maintenance history will ensure a rewarding ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces an incredible 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and a signature exhaust note. While robust when properly maintained, it requires strict adherence to fluid change intervals and professional oversight due to its high-performance tuning.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, tailored specifically for rear-wheel-drive supercar dynamics. It is generally responsive and durable, though clutch wear patterns should be inspected during pre-purchase evaluations on any high-performance exotic.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F8+Spider&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a pre-owned 2021 Ferrari F8 Spider, prioritizing a comprehensive independent PPI (Pre-Purchase Inspection) by a certified marque specialist is essential. Because formal owner complaint data is sparse for this exclusive model, verifying service records, ensuring all campaign updates have been performed, and checking component health will provide the best peace of mind.
