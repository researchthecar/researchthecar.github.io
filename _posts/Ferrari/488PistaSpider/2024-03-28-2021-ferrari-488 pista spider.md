**Introduction**

The 2021 Ferrari 488 Pista Spider is a high-performance convertible sports car with a twin-turbocharged 3.9-liter V8 engine that produces 710 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The 488 Pista Spider is known for its impressive performance, handling, and styling. However, it is also known for some issues.

**Engine**
**3.9-liter V8 Engine**

* **Camshaft failure**
Description/Symptoms: The camshafts can fail, causing a loss of power and engine damage.
Solution: Replace the camshafts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger failure**
Description/Symptoms: The turbochargers can fail, causing a loss of power and engine damage.
Solution: Replace the turbochargers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Clutch failure**
Description/Symptoms: The clutch can fail, causing difficulty shifting gears and loss of power.
Solution: Replace the clutch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: None Found
