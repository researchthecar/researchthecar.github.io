**2007 Ferrari 599 GTB**

The 2007 Ferrari 599 GTB is a high-performance sports car known for its sleek design, powerful V12 engine, and advanced handling capabilities. It is available with a 6-speed manual or a 6-speed F1 automated manual transmission. The 599 GTB is praised for its thrilling driving experience, but it has also been known to experience certain issues.

**Engine**

**6.0L V12**

* **Valve Guide Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, reduced engine power, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace valve guides
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the engine, clicking or grinding noise when starting
    * Solution: Replace starter motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed F1 Automated Manual**

* **F1 Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power while shifting gears, transmission warning lights
    * Solution: Replace F1 pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noise from rear suspension, reduced handling performance
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, loss of electrical power, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the car due to low battery power
    * Solution: Diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain and repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Carbon Fiber Body Panel Delamination**
    * Description/Symptoms: Separation of carbon fiber layers causing bubbles or cracks in the exterior panels
    * Solution: Replace or repair affected body panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Belt Buckle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage or release seat belt
    * Solution: Replace seat belt buckle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Panel Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative climate controls, erratic temperature changes
    * Solution: Replace climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2007 Ferrari 599 GTB is a highly capable sports car that offers an exhilarating driving experience. However, it is not without its share of issues. The most prevalent concerns are related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not overly common and do not significantly detract from the car's overall enjoyment.
