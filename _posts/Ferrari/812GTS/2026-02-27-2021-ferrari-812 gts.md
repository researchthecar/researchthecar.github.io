# 2021 Ferrari 812 GTS

### Overview
The 2021 Ferrari 812 GTS represents the pinnacle of naturally aspirated V12 performance in a convertible grand tourer. As a limited-production model, extensive long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, based on initial owner feedback and the established reputation of Ferrari's V12 engines and sophisticated engineering, the 812 GTS is generally considered to be highly reliable for its class. Owners typically report exceptional performance and build quality, with issues being rare and often related to the complex electronic systems or specific, high-performance components rather than fundamental design flaws. Maintenance is specialized and costly, as expected for a vehicle of this caliber.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Ferrari 812 GTS exhibits a high level of mechanical and structural reliability, consistent with Ferrari's flagship models. The iconic V12 engine and dual-clutch transmission are robust. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the complex electrical systems, specifically the infotainment and ADAS, which can occasionally require software updates or sensor recalibration. These are not indicative of systemic failures but rather the nature of advanced automotive technology. Overall, for a supercar of this caliber, the 812 GTS offers impressive dependability, with most concerns revolving around specialized maintenance and the inherent costs associated with exotic vehicle ownership.
