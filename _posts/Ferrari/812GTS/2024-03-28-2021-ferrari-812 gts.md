**Introduction:**
The 2021 Ferrari 812 GTS is an Italian supercar with a naturally aspirated V12 engine and an innovative retractable hardtop. It produces 800 horsepower and 530 lb-ft of torque, delivering exhilarating performance. Despite its high price and niche market appeal, the 812 GTS has accrued a few issues reported by owners.

**Engine**
**6.5L V12**

* **Oil Leak from Oil Filter Housing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leakage around the oil filter housing, visible as a dark-colored stain on the engine block.
    * Solution: Replacement of oil filter housing gasket and cleaning of leaked oil.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, and loss of power.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty fuel injector.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**7-speed Dual-Clutch**

* **Clutch Plate Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty changing gears, and transmission shuddering.
    * Solution: Replacement of worn clutch plate.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning alternator, dim headlights, and difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replacement of defective alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components or software update.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Windshield Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sudden cracking of windshield, typically occurring at high speeds.
    * Solution: Replacement of windshield.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Freezing or unresponsive infotainment display, loss of navigation and entertainment functionality.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of faulty infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
Overall, the 2021 Ferrari 812 GTS has a limited number of reported issues. While some electrical and engine problems may arise, they are not widespread. The severity and cost of repairs can be high due to the vehicle's advanced components and specialized parts. Nonetheless, the 812 GTS remains a highly desirable and thrilling supercar for enthusiasts.
