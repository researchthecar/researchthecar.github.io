# 2022 Ferrari 812 GTS

### Overview
The 2022 Ferrari 812 GTS represents the pinnacle of naturally aspirated V12 performance and grand touring luxury. As a limited-production model, comprehensive long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, based on early reports and the established reputation of Ferrari's V12 engines and sophisticated engineering, the 812 GTS is expected to exhibit a high level of mechanical integrity. Owners should anticipate the typical considerations for a hypercar, focusing on specialized maintenance and the potential for high repair costs should any issues arise. The focus is on performance and exclusivity, with reliability generally being a strong suit for models of this caliber, though individual component failures can still occur.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Ferrari 812 GTS is a remarkable machine with a strong foundation of engineering excellence. While specific long-term failure data is limited due to its exclusivity and recent model year, early indications and Ferrari's track record suggest high mechanical reliability, particularly for the iconic V12 engine and dual-clutch transmission. The primary concerns for owners revolve around the potential for high repair costs for any component, the need for specialized maintenance, and minor electrical gremlins like infotainment glitches or sensor calibration issues, which are not uncommon in complex, high-performance vehicles. Overall, the 812 GTS is expected to be a reliable supercar for those who adhere to its stringent maintenance schedule and understand the inherent costs associated with exotic vehicle ownership.
