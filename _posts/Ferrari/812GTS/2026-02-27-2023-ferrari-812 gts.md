### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Ferrari 812 GTS demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, consistent with its premium positioning and marque heritage. The core powertrain components, including the 6.5L V12 engine and 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, are exceptionally robust and have no widespread reported issues. The suspension system also performs reliably. Minor concerns are primarily limited to the electrical system, with occasional infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues that are generally resolvable through software updates or diagnostics. Exterior and interior components are well-built, with no significant recurring problems. Overall, the 812 GTS offers a dependable ownership experience for a high-performance exotic, provided routine inspections and prompt attention to minor electrical anomalies are maintained.
