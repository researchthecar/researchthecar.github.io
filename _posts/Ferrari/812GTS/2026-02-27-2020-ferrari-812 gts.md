# 2020 Ferrari 812 GTS

### Overview
The 2020 Ferrari 812 GTS represents the pinnacle of grand touring performance, combining a potent naturally aspirated V12 engine with the exhilarating experience of open-top driving. As a limited-production model, its engineering is focused on delivering exceptional performance and a refined driving experience. While Ferraris are exotic vehicles requiring specialized care, the 812 GTS, in its initial production years, has demonstrated a commendable level of mechanical robustness, particularly concerning its powertrain. Owners should, however, be aware of the inherent complexities and costs associated with maintaining such a high-performance machine, especially regarding its advanced electronic systems and bespoke interior and exterior components.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Ferrari 812 GTS exhibits strong mechanical reliability, particularly with its iconic V12 engine and dual-clutch transmission, which are generally free from widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter concerns are within the complex electrical systems, such as the infotainment and ADAS, and the sophisticated retractable hardtop mechanism. These components, while advanced, can occasionally present glitches or require specialized attention. Routine maintenance and adherence to Ferrari's recommended service schedule are crucial for preserving the vehicle's performance and longevity. Given its exotic nature, repair costs for any issues, especially those involving specialized electronics or bodywork, are expected to be significant.
