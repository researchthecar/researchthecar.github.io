# 2016 Ferrari 488 GTB Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ferrari 488 GTB represents a high-performance evolution for the brand, pairing a twin-turbocharged V8 engine with exotic styling and sharp handling. For prospective buyers exploring the pre-owned market, evaluating maintenance history, structural integrity, and proper recall completion is essential for protecting your investment.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 661 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and responsive power delivery. While generally robust under proper maintenance, buyers should thoroughly inspect the turbochargers and cooling systems due to the intense operating environment.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery suited for both track and street driving. Pre-owned buyers should verify proper clutch engagement and smooth shifting behavior during a comprehensive test drive.

## Safety

### Airbags

Reports highlight potential risks concerning vehicle history, particularly regarding restored or rental units lacking proper safety equipment functionality.

**Airbag System Inoperability in Rebuilt Models**
- **Symptoms:** Owner reports indicate safety concerns regarding rental or rebuilt vehicles operating without functional airbag systems.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified Ferrari specialist inspect the supplemental restraint system to verify all components and sensors are present and operational.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V833000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V164000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software error, the driver may not be warned that the brake discs are critically worn. If the driver is not warned about the critical disc wear, brake disc damage may result, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will update the instrument panel node software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V881000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** This may cause a fuel vapor leak during engine start. A fuel vapor leak during engine start increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will replace the low pressure fuel line assembly connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
