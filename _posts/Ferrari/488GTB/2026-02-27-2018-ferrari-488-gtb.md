# 2018 Ferrari 488 GTB Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Ferrari 488 GTB is an exotic mid-engine sports car celebrated for its breathtaking performance and turbocharged power delivery. While owner complaints for this low-volume exotic are sparse, prospective buyers should thoroughly review maintenance records and inspect critical systems like braking and steering ergonomics prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 661 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and sharp throttle response. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though regular service by qualified technicians is essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes via paddle shifters and handles the engine's high output efficiently. Routine fluid changes and clutch health monitoring are recommended during pre-purchase inspections.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brakes

High-performance carbon-ceramic brakes provide immense stopping power but require careful monitoring of fluid levels and pad wear.

**Brake Fluid Low Warning and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** An instrument panel message indicating low brake fluid may appear, potentially leading to a complete loss of braking function.
- **Solution:** Have the hydraulic brake system, master cylinder, and fluid levels thoroughly inspected and serviced by a certified specialist.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Controls

The steering system provides razor-sharp feedback and quick ratios, paired with driver controls that require a brief adaptation period.

**Unconventional Horn Button Placement**
- **Symptoms:** The horn buttons are integrated into the steering wheel rim at the 9 and 3 o'clock positions rather than the center hub, which can cause confusion during emergency maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Familiarize yourself with the layout during a test drive to adapt to the unconventional placement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V833000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Ferrari 488 GTB remains a pinnacle of modern exotic engineering, combining everyday drivability with track-ready performance. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an independent Ferrari specialist to verify the health of the braking, electrical, and powertrain systems.
