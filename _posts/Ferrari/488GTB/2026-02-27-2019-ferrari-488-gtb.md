# 2019 Ferrari 488 GTB Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Evaluating a high-performance exotic like the 2019 Ferrari 488 GTB requires a focus on its twin-turbocharged V8 powertrain and sophisticated dual-clutch transmission. While formal owner complaints in public logs are exceptionally sparse for this model year, buyers should always verify maintenance histories and complete pre-purchase inspections. Exotic vehicle ownership demands diligent upkeep, particularly regarding complex hydraulic systems and high-performance wear items.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 661 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and responsiveness. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though regular servicing of cooling and forced-induction components is essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes suited for both track and street driving. Overall reliability is strong, but prospective buyers should inspect the clutch health and ensure fluid replacement intervals have been strictly followed.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Carbon-ceramic brakes offer immense stopping power, though component replacement costs can be substantial during routine maintenance.

**Brake Fluid Reservoir and Warning System**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may receive recall notices regarding potential brake fluid leaks that could trigger warning lights or affect braking performance.
- **Solution:** Authorized dealerships inspect and replace affected components or update software as specified by manufacturer recall campaigns.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V833000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Ferrari 488 GTB is an engineering masterpiece offering exhilarating performance paired with refined everyday usability for an exotic supercar. Because available complaint data is minimal and focused primarily on manufacturer notifications, your best safeguard is a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified Ferrari specialist. Budgeting carefully for specialized maintenance and insurance is key to a rewarding ownership experience.
