# 2017 Ferrari 488 GTB Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Ferrari 488 GTB, buyers are looking at a high-performance exotic known for its twin-turbocharged V8 power and exotic styling. While owner reports to federal safety regulators are relatively limited for this ultra-luxury tier, prospective buyers should still inspect complex systems like braking, cabin trim, and safety equipment thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2017 Ferrari 488 GTB is powered by a 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing approximately 661 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. This potent powerplant delivers blistering acceleration and robust overall engineering, though routine specialized maintenance is essential for longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed through a lightning-fast 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission derived from Ferrari's Formula 1 technology. It offers seamless gear changes and robust reliability under normal operating conditions, provided software and fluid services are maintained.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Carbon-ceramic brakes provide immense stopping power, but heavy track usage or rare electronic anomalies require careful professional inspection.

**Brake Pedal Failure During Track Use**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may fail completely and travel to the floor during high-stress track driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect the braking system, brake fluid, and master cylinder thoroughly, and upgrade components or replace worn parts if track events are planned.

**Unintended Brake Locking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's braking system may unexpectedly lock the brakes without any driver input, rendering the car immobile.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic stability and brake control modules evaluated by a qualified Ferrari technician.

## Interior

### Headliner and Sun Visor

Exterior and related cabin boundary trim components require cosmetic upkeep to maintain visibility and overall presentation.

**Sagging Headliner Impairing Visibility**
- **Symptoms:** The interior headliner may sag over time, causing the sun visor to drop down unexpectedly and interfere with the driver's forward visibility.
- **Solution:** Re-secure or replace the headliner upholstery and affected visor mountings at an interior specialty shop.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety systems rely on correct deployment hardware, making it crucial to verify that all manufacturer recalls have been completed.

**Airbag Recall Part Availability Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience difficulty scheduling timely recall repairs with dealerships due to ongoing replacement part supply constraints.
- **Solution:** Contact authorized Ferrari service centers repeatedly to track part availability and prioritize outstanding safety campaigns.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V833000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+GTB&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ferrari 488 GTB remains an exhilarating exotic choice, but ownership costs and specialized maintenance demands are exceptionally high. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an experienced exotic car technician, verifying brake health, interior trim condition, and the completion of any open safety recalls.
