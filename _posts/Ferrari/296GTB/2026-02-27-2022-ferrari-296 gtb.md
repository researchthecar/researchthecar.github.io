# 2022 Ferrari 296 GTB

### Overview
The 2022 Ferrari 296 GTB represents a significant technological leap for the Maranello marque, featuring a groundbreaking V6 hybrid powertrain. This innovative setup combines a potent twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 engine with an electric motor, delivering exceptional performance and efficiency. As a new model, long-term reliability data is still emerging, but initial reports and the brand's focus on meticulous engineering suggest a high level of build quality. The advanced hybrid system, while complex, is built upon Ferrari's extensive experience in motorsport and hybrid technology, aiming for both exhilarating driving dynamics and dependable operation.
