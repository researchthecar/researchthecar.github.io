# 2017 Ferrari F12 Berlinetta Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ferrari F12 Berlinetta represents the pinnacle of front-engine V12 grand touring performance, combining breathtaking power with Italian artistry. Because these exotic vehicles are produced in limited numbers and driven under specific conditions, shopping for a used model requires careful attention to maintenance history and component health. While formal owner complaints in public databases are sparse, buyers should always verify proper service intervals and professional inspections before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 6.3L Naturally Aspirated V12

The 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine produces approximately 730 horsepower and 509 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and an iconic exhaust note. This high-revving powerhouse is generally robust when subjected to regular maintenance and proper warm-up cycles, though upkeep costs remain exceptionally high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, transferring massive power seamlessly to the rear wheels. It is sophisticated engineering that requires strict adherence to fluid change intervals and diagnostic checks during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F12+Berlinetta&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F12+Berlinetta&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F12+Berlinetta&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
