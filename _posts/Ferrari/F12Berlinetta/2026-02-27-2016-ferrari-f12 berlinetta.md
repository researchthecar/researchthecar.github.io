### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Ferrari F12 Berlinetta demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, characteristic of a flagship supercar. The powerful 6.3L V12 engine and the responsive 7-speed dual-clutch transmission are robust and have no widespread reported issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential intermittent battery drain, especially with infrequent use. These issues are typically addressable through software updates or diagnostics for parasitic draws. Overall, the F12 Berlinetta offers a dependable ownership experience for a vehicle of its caliber, provided regular servicing and attention to its advanced electronic features.
