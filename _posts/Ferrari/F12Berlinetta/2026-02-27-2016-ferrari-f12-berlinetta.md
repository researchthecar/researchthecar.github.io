# 2016 Ferrari F12 Berlinetta Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ferrari F12 Berlinetta represents the pinnacle of front-mid-engine V12 grand touring performance, offering breathtaking power and exotic styling. For prospective used-car buyers, examining the reliability and maintenance history of these high-performance machines is crucial, as ownership involves specialized care and meticulous attention to mechanical health.

---

## Engine

### 6.3L V12

The 6.3-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine produces approximately 730 horsepower and 509 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance and high-revving thrills, though it requires strict adherence to maintenance schedules to ensure long-term mechanical stability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automated Manual

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. It is generally robust when operated properly, but clutch wear and software calibrations should be evaluated during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F12+Berlinetta&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V411000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F12+Berlinetta&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may malfunction, possibly causing the non-deployment or improper deployment of the air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners. In the event of a crash, air bags and/or seat belt pretensioners that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V536000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=F12+Berlinetta&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The brake fluid reservoir cap may not vent properly, creating a vacuum inside the brake fluid reservoir, and resulting in a brake fluid leak that may lead to a partial or total loss of brake function. A loss of brake function can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software to provide a new warning message for low brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
