# 2017 Ferrari 488 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Ferrari 488 Spider delivers breathtaking open-top performance powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As an exotic mid-engine sports car, it requires meticulous maintenance and specialized service. Buyers shopping for a pre-owned 488 Spider should review service history closely and verify proper operation of all electronic and mechanical systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 660 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. It offers exhilarating acceleration and modern responsiveness, though ongoing maintenance costs remain high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. It is generally robust when maintained properly, but replacement or major clutch service can be extremely costly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Spider&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V833000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Spider&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Ferrari 488 Spider is a thrilling exotic convertible that demands careful inspection before purchase. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive dealer service records and secure a pre-purchase inspection from an authorized Ferrari specialist.
