# 2019 Ferrari 488 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Ferrari 488 Spider delivers breathtaking open-top performance backed by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. While exotic supercars demand meticulous maintenance and specialized care, this model benefits from modern electronic controls and engineering refinement. Prospective buyers should always prioritize vehicles with a documented service history and professional pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 661 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. It offers ferocious acceleration and advanced thermal management, though it requires diligent upkeep and premium fluids.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. It is responsive and robust, though clutch wear should be verified during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V833000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Spider&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V090000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Spider&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel vapor separator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Ferrari 488 Spider is an engineering masterpiece offering thrilling dynamics and exotic appeal.
