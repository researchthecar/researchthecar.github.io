# 2016 Ferrari 488 Spider Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Ferrari 488 Spider delivers breathtaking open-top performance powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As an exotic mid-engine supercar, prospective buyers should approach ownership with an understanding that maintenance, specialized servicing, and component upkeep require dedicated financial preparation compared to mainstream vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 3.9L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 3.9-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 661 horsepower and 561 lb-ft of torque. It offers ferocious acceleration and advanced power delivery, though proper routine servicing is essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes via steering-column-mounted paddle shifters. It operates smoothly in automatic mode and responds instantly during aggressive driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V006000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Spider&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the passenger frontal air bag, these inflators may explode due to propellant degradation occurring after long-term exposure to absolute humidity and temperature cycling. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V833000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Spider&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Loss of braking ability increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake fluid reservoir cap and update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V164000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Ferrari&model=488+Spider&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software error, the driver may not be warned that the brake discs are critically worn. If the driver is not warned about the critical disc wear, brake disc damage may result, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Ferrari will notify owners, and dealers will update the instrument panel node software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Ferrari 488 Spider is a pinnacle of modern Italian engineering, offering thrilling performance and striking design. While formal owner complaints and recurring mechanical issues are minimal in available public logs, buyers must prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a certified exotic specialist and budget accordingly for high-end supercar maintenance.
