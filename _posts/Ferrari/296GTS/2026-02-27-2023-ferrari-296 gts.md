### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Ferrari 296 GTS exhibits strong initial reliability, with no significant widespread technical issues reported across its core mechanical components, including the advanced V6 hybrid powertrain and dual-clutch transmission. The suspension and exterior elements also appear robust. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system's software, are the most commonly noted, though these are typically resolved with updates. Given the exotic nature and cutting-edge technology, specialized servicing is essential. Overall, the 296 GTS is engineered to high standards, promising a reliable and thrilling ownership experience for those who adhere to recommended maintenance schedules.
