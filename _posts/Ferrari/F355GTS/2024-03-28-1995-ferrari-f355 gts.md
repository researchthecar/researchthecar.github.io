### Introduction to the 1995 Ferrari F355 GTS

The Ferrari F355 GTS is a mid-engined sports car produced by Ferrari from 1994 to 1999. It was the successor to the Ferrari 348 and was available in both Berlinetta (coupe) and GTS (targa) body styles. The F355 was powered by a 3.5-liter V8 engine. The engine was based on the engine used in the Ferrari 348, but it had been revised to produce more power. The F355 was also available with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission.

The F355 was a popular car, and it was praised for its performance, handling, and style. However, the car was also known for its high maintenance costs.

### **Engine**
**3.5L V8**

**Timing Belt Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, loss of power.

Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leaks, rough idle.

### **Summary**
The Ferrari F355 is a high-performance sports car that is known for its performance, handling, and style. However, the car is also known for its high maintenance costs. Some of the most common issues with the F355 include cylinder head gasket failure, timing belt failure, intake manifold gasket failure, shift linkage issues, transmission fluid leaks, alternator failure, starter failure, and HVAC system malfunction. The cost of fixing these issues can vary depending on the severity of the problem.

Overall, the Ferrari F355 is a reliable car, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
