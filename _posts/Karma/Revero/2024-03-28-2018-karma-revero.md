**2018 Karma Revero**

The 2018 Karma Revero is a luxury plug-in hybrid electric vehicle with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine and a single electric motor. The engine produces 235 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, while the electric motor adds 403 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. The Revero has a total system output of 638 horsepower and 630 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission and has a top speed of 125 mph. The EPA-estimated range is 53 miles on electric power alone and 390 miles combined.

People who like the Revero praise its stylish design, comfortable interior, and powerful performance. However, some have criticized its high price and limited trunk space.

**Engine/Transmission**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine with 6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Oil Leaks:** Oil leaks can occur from the valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket, or rear main seal. Symptoms include low oil levels, oil spots on the ground, and a burning smell. The fix involves replacing the leaking gasket or seal. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,000.
* **Timing Chain Failure:** The timing chain can fail prematurely, causing the engine to lose power or stall. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, reduced performance, and engine misfires. The fix involves replacing the timing chain and related components. The estimated cost to fix is $2,000 - $4,000.

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Problems:** The 12-volt battery can fail prematurely, causing the vehicle to lose power or not start. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical system malfunctions, and a warning light on the dashboard. The fix involves replacing the battery. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $400.
* **Wiring Harness Issues:** The wiring harness can develop issues, causing electrical problems throughout the vehicle. Symptoms can vary depending on the specific issue, but may include electrical system malfunctions, warning lights on the dashboard, and loss of power. The fix involves repairing or replacing the affected wiring harness. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $2,000.

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Rust:** The body panels can develop rust prematurely, especially in areas with high humidity or salt exposure. Symptoms include visible rust spots, bubbling paint, and weakened body panels. The fix involves repairing or replacing the affected body panels. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $2,000.

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag:** The infotainment system can experience lag or freezing. Symptoms include slow response times, difficulty using the touchscreen, and system crashes. The fix involves updating the software or replacing the infotainment unit. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,000.
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** The climate control system can malfunction, causing the heating or air conditioning to not work properly. Symptoms can vary depending on the specific issue, but may include inconsistent temperatures, fan noise, and warning lights on the dashboard. The fix involves repairing or replacing the affected components. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $2,000.

**Recalls**

* NHTSA Recall Number: 20V-236: Airbag Control Module Software Error
* NHTSA Recall Number: 20V-051: Fuel Pump Failure

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Karma Revero is a well-made vehicle, but it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The engine and transmission issues can be expensive to fix, and the electrical problems can cause a variety of issues throughout the vehicle. However, the Revero's stylish design, comfortable interior, and powerful performance make it a desirable choice for those looking for a luxury plug-in hybrid electric vehicle.
