# 2017 Nissan Rogue Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Nissan Rogue Sport, it helps to understand both its everyday practicality and the specific mechanical concerns reported by owners. While it offers a comfortable ride and nimble handling for city driving, prospective buyers should pay close attention to potential issues involving the continuously variable transmission, engine performance, and electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine (MR20DD)

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine (MR20DD) produces around 141 horsepower, offering adequate efficiency for daily commutes. However, buyers should carefully check service records for oil consumption and sensor performance.

**Excessive Oil Consumption and Piston Rings**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle emits blue smoke upon startup and rapidly burns through engine oil, requiring frequent top-offs.
- **Solution:** Requires mechanical inspection; severely worn piston rings may necessitate major engine repairs or replacement.
- **Cost:** $4,000 to $10,000

**Mass Air Flow Sensor and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine codes (P0101, P0102) accompanied by unexpected vehicle shutdown at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Clean or replace the faulty mass air flow sensor and inspect associated electrical connections.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired exclusively with front-wheel or all-wheel drive systems, the Xtronic CVT prioritizes fuel economy. Due to reported durability concerns, a thorough pre-purchase transmission test drive is strongly recommended.

**Transmission Failure and Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Brief acceleration delays, sudden jerking, or complete transmission failure leaving the vehicle immobilized.
- **Solution:** Have a professional technician inspect the transmission fluid condition and internal components; severe failures require unit replacement.

## Exterior

### Wiring Harness and Door Locks

Electrical complaints center primarily around accessory lighting, wiring harnesses, and door lock actuators that may require out-of-pocket repairs.

**Roof Wiring Harness Damage**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning map lights and visor vanity lights, with potential for severe electrical hazards if left unaddressed.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace damaged sections of the roof wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $1,952.06

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door locks stop working entirely across multiple doors.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed door lock actuators.

## Safety

### Forward Emergency Braking System

Advanced safety features add modern protection, though sensor sensitivity issues can occasionally trigger unintended driver alerts and braking actions.

**Phantom Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The emergency braking system unexpectedly activates and slams on the brakes even when there are no obstacles in front of the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the collision avoidance sensors and software calibrated or inspected by a qualified dealer or technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V093000: Electrical System:Ignition:Anti-Theft:Immobilizer/Proximity:Key/Sender](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue+Sport&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Driver contact with a key in the collapsed position could inadvertently shut off the vehicle while driving, increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, if the vehicle shuts off while driving, the air bags may not deploy in a crash as intended, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will insert a spacer into the key slot of the jackknife key, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When evaluating a used model, conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the transmission health, engine oil history, and safety sensors is essential. If these critical systems check out and have been well-maintained, it can serve as a budget-friendly and practical daily driver.
