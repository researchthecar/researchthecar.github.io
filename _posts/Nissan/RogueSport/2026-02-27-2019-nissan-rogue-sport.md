# 2019 Nissan Rogue Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Nissan Rogue Sport offers a compact footprint and practical cabin space, making it a popular choice for budget-conscious used-car buyers. However, prospective buyers should carefully review potential mechanical and safety system concerns before purchasing. Owner reports highlight several recurring problem areas ranging from powertrain operation to advanced driver-assistance features.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

Equipped with a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine producing around 141 horsepower, the Rogue Sport provides adequate daily commuting capability. While standard performance is typical for the class, buyers should verify engine health and maintenance records.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling in slow traffic or complete loss of motive power and acceleration while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check for fault codes (such as P0507) and inspect throttle body, fuel delivery, or cooling components.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable transmission routes power to either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive models. CVT longevity varies significantly, making a thorough pre-purchase transmission inspection essential.

**CVT Shudder and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report rapid-onset shaking, vibration, jerking during low-level acceleration, or complete transmission failure requiring major component replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition, road test for shudder symptoms, and consult professional transmission specialists for potential rebuilding or replacement.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety systems are designed to assist the driver, but unexpected activations can create hazardous driving situations.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system can engage unexpectedly while driving on open roads or highways without any obstacles ahead.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's collision sensors and software calibration inspected by a dealership or authorized service center.

### Seat Belt

Occupant protection systems should be carefully inspected to ensure proper latching and retraction before daily driving.

**Seat Belt Retractor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Occupants report problems with seat belts failing to retract properly or securing correctly during use.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies for mechanical wear or latch defects, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V093000: Electrical System:Ignition:Anti-Theft:Immobilizer/Proximity:Key/Sender](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue+Sport&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Driver contact with a key in the collapsed position could inadvertently shut off the vehicle while driving, increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, if the vehicle shuts off while driving, the air bags may not deploy in a crash as intended, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will insert a spacer into the key slot of the jackknife key, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Nissan Rogue Sport presents notable risks related to CVT transmission durability and unexpected forward collision braking incidents. Buyers should insist on a comprehensive mechanical inspection and a detailed test drive focusing on powertrain smoothness and driver-assistance behaviors before finalizing a purchase.
