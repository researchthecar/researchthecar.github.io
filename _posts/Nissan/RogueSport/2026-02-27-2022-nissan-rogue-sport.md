# 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport is a compact crossover that offers practical daily driving and nimble dimensions for urban environments. While overall owner satisfaction is generally steady, prospective used car buyers should review scattered reports concerning powertrain noises, electrical glitches, and unexpected driver-assistance activations.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2022 Rogue Sport is powered by a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine producing 141 horsepower. It provides adequate power for daily commuting, though routine monitoring of fluid levels is recommended based on owner feedback.

**Excessive Oil Consumption and Engine Cutting**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported the engine cutting off unexpectedly while driving, sluggish acceleration, oil burning without external leaks, and blue smoke emitting from the exhaust upon startup.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly and have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine internals or PCV system if oil consumption persists.

**Abnormal Engine Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note unusual noises emanating from the engine bay upon startup and during acceleration, though warning lights may not always illuminate.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose the source of the mechanical noise to ensure there is no premature wear on internal components.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The vehicle utilizes an Xtronic continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. While no prominent complaint-backed failures dominate this model year, prospective buyers should test drive the transmission thoroughly for smooth operation.

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Automatic Braking

Advanced safety features are designed to protect occupants, but sensitive collision sensors can occasionally result in false activations.

**Phantom Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's safety systems may trigger sudden, unexpected emergency braking events when no obstacles are present in the roadway.
- **Solution:** Have the collision avoidance sensors and camera calibration checked by a certified service center.

## Electrical

### Power Windows and Key Fob Locks

Electrical complaints center around convenience features like door locks and window controls.

**Door Lock and Window Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Key fobs may fail to properly lock or unlock specific doors, and power windows can occasionally roll down spontaneously or suffer from switch failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the door lock actuators, window regulators, and key fob programming at a dealership or local electrical specialist.

## Exterior

### Body Glass and Doors

Exterior structural and glass components generally hold up well, though isolated incidents of window breakage and door latch stiffness have been reported.

**Spontaneous Window Shattering and Door Latch Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Side windows may experience unexpected glass failure while driving at highway speeds, and passenger doors can occasionally fail to open from the inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged door latch assemblies and inspect window glass for stress fractures or improper installation alignment.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V093000: Electrical System:Ignition:Anti-Theft:Immobilizer/Proximity:Key/Sender](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue+Sport&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Driver contact with a key in the collapsed position could inadvertently shut off the vehicle while driving, increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, if the vehicle shuts off while driving, the air bags may not deploy in a crash as intended, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will insert a spacer into the key slot of the jackknife key, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport is a comfortable and efficient crossover, but used car shoppers should perform a thorough inspection prior to purchase. Pay close attention to engine health, window and door lock electronics, and the behavior of the driver-assist safety suite during a test drive to ensure everything operates as expected.
