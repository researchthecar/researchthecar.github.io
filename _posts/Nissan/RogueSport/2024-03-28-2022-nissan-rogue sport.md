## Introduction to the 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport

The 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport is a compact SUV known for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and intuitive technology. It's available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter inline-four rated at 141 horsepower and 147 lb.-ft. of torque, paired with a CVT, and a 2.5-liter inline-four rated at 181 horsepower and 181 lb.-ft. of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission.

While the Rogue Sport has received praise for its comfort and practicality, it has also faced some criticisms regarding its lackluster performance and uninspiring driving dynamics.

### Engine

**2.0-Liter Inline-Four**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine burns or leaks excessive oil, leading to decreased oil levels and potential engine damage
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of piston rings, valve seals, or other components; engine may need rebuilding in severe cases
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the timing chain tensioner can cause engine noise, rough idling, and potentially engine damage
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-Liter Inline-Four**

* **No Issues Found**

### Transmission

**CVT (2.0-Liter Engine)**

* **Premature Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: CVT fails prematurely, causing slipping, shuddering, or loss of power
    * Solution: CVT replacement or rebuilding
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: CVT failure recall #NTB22-038

**9-Speed Automatic (2.5-Liter Engine)**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or shifts erratically, causing delayed or harsh gear changes
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **No Issues Found**

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly or does not hold a charge, causing difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Battery replacement or repair of electrical components causing the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Sunroof Seal Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks through the sunroof seal, causing water damage to the interior
    * Solution: Replacement of the sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Infotainment System Glitch**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezes, reboots, or experiences connectivity issues
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment system module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to function properly, leading to uncomfortable cabin temperatures
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

### Summary

While the 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport offers a comfortable and practical ride, it has faced some reliability issues, including excessive oil consumption, transmission problems, and battery drain. These issues can be costly to fix, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. However, many of these issues can be addressed through recalls or software updates, mitigating some of the expenses for owners.
