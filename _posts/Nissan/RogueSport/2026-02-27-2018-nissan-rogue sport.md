## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may hesitate during acceleration or exhibit a jerking sensation, particularly at lower speeds or when coming to a stop.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module, or in more severe cases, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or internal component repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light may illuminate, accompanied by a burning smell or reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Check and top off transmission fluid, inspect for leaks, and ensure proper cooling system function. In some cases, transmission cooler may need cleaning or replacement.
- **Cost:** $250 - $800

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsive**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive to touch, or reboot unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the infotainment system, or a reset of the unit. In rare cases, the unit may need replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1,200


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The battery may drain prematurely, leading to starting issues. This can sometimes be linked to parasitic draws from certain electrical components.
- **Solution:** Test battery health, check for parasitic draws from modules like the infotainment or security system, and replace battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Nissan Rogue Sport generally presents a good reliability record, with its 2.0L engine and standard chassis components showing minimal widespread issues. The primary areas of concern tend to be the CVT transmission, which can exhibit hesitation or overheating issues, and certain electrical components like the infotainment system and potential battery drain. Interior issues are less common, with the blower motor being a notable, though not universal, concern. Addressing potential CVT maintenance and being aware of electrical quirks can contribute to a positive ownership experience.
