# 2021 Nissan Rogue Sport Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Nissan Rogue Sport offers a comfortable ride, modern tech features, and practical everyday utility for compact SUV shoppers. However, prospective used car buyers should carefully evaluate owner-reported concerns regarding driver assistance systems and electrical components before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine produces 141 horsepower and 147 lb-ft of torque, paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive. It provides adequate power for daily commuting, though routine maintenance of cooling systems and engine components is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The Xtronic CVT delivers smooth acceleration and optimized fuel economy across city and highway driving. Prospective buyers should inspect the transmission fluid condition and test drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure responsive operation.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators & Wiring

Electrical accessories generally function well, though specific components like door lock actuators can require out-of-pocket repairs.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door locks fail to secure or unlock properly, leaving doors vulnerable or stuck due to premature component wear.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed door lock actuator assemblies.
- **Cost:** $700

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Driver assistance features enhance everyday peace of mind, but sensor calibration issues can occasionally prompt unintended braking maneuvers.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision warning system may trigger without warning, causing the vehicle to unexpectedly apply sudden braking when no obstacles or hazards are present.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect sensor alignment, software calibration, and forward-facing camera mounts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V555000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A seat belt retractor that does not function as intended can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger seat belt and as necessary both outer rear seat belts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V525000: Structure:Body:Door:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The liftgate hinges can separate from the vehicle during a rear end crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 206, "Door Locks and Door Retention Components." Separated hinges compromise the protection offered by the liftgate, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left and right liftgate hinges, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V093000: Electrical System:Ignition:Anti-Theft:Immobilizer/Proximity:Key/Sender](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue+Sport&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Driver contact with a key in the collapsed position could inadvertently shut off the vehicle while driving, increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, if the vehicle shuts off while driving, the air bags may not deploy in a crash as intended, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will insert a spacer into the key slot of the jackknife key, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Nissan Rogue Sport is a sensible choice for budget-conscious drivers seeking compact SUV versatility. While powertrain options are generally dependable, used car shoppers should verify the proper operation of driver assistance features and door lock mechanisms during a pre-purchase inspection.
