# 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport

### Overview
The 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport is a compact SUV that offers a blend of practicality and a comfortable ride. It is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanical systems. Owners often praise its fuel efficiency and maneuverability in urban environments. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the Rogue Sport has demonstrated a solid track record for its model year, with most reported concerns being minor or related to typical usage rather than inherent design flaws. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Nissan Rogue Sport generally presents as a reliable compact SUV. The powertrain, consisting of the 2.5L engine and CVT transmission, is largely dependable, though some owners have noted occasional minor hesitations with the CVT. Electrical systems, particularly the infotainment unit, can sometimes exhibit minor connectivity glitches that are usually resolved with software updates. Beyond these isolated instances, the exterior, interior, and suspension components have shown good durability. Overall, the Rogue Sport is a solid choice for those seeking a practical and reasonably trouble-free vehicle, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
