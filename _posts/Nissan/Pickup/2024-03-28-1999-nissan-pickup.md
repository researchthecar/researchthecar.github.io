**Introduction:**

The 1999 Nissan Pickup, known as the Frontier outside North America, is a mid-size pickup truck. It was offered with three engine options: a 2.4-liter inline-four, a 3.3-liter V6, and a 4.0-liter V6. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual, a 4-speed automatic, and a 5-speed automatic. The Frontier was praised for its reliability, off-road capabilities, and comfortable interior. However, it was criticized for its lack of power and outdated design.

**Engine**

**2.4-liter Inline-Four**

- **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to frequent top-ups or engine failure.
  - Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**3.3-liter V6**

- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Gasket failure, resulting in coolant leaks, overheating, and decreased engine performance.
  - Solution: Intake manifold gasket replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**4.0-liter V6**

- **Cam Position Sensor Failure:** Faulty cam position sensor, causing engine misfires, rough idling, and difficulty starting.
  - Solution: Cam position sensor replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

- **Grinding Gears:** Difficulty shifting gears, particularly in cold weather or when under load.
  - Solution: Transmission fluid flush or replacement, or gearbox rebuild.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic**

- **Slipping Gears:** Transmission slips out of gear during acceleration or shifts harshly.
  - Solution: Transmission fluid flush or replacement, or transmission rebuild.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Automatic**

- **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

- **Leaf Spring Breakage:** Leaf springs can break under heavy load or rough road conditions.
  - Solution: Leaf spring replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per leaf spring
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:** Premature alternator failure, leading to battery drain or charging issues.
  - Solution: Alternator replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

- **Headliner Sagging:** Headliner material may sag or detach from the ceiling.
  - Solution: Headliner replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1999 Nissan Pickup has a mixed reliability record. Some issues, such as oil consumption and transmission problems, can be severe and expensive to repair. However, the vehicle also has its strengths, including reliability in other areas, off-road capability, and a comfortable interior. Potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase decision.
