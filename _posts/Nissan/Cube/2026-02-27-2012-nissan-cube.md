# 2012 Nissan Cube

### Overview
The 2012 Nissan Cube is a subcompact hatchback known for its distinctive, quirky styling and practical interior. From a reliability standpoint, it generally performs well, with owners reporting fewer major mechanical issues compared to some competitors in its class. The powertrain, while not the most powerful, is typically robust. Common areas of concern tend to be minor, often related to electrical components or the typical wear and tear expected in a vehicle of this age and segment. Overall, the Cube offers a reliable and economical choice for urban commuting and light-duty use, provided it has been maintained adequately.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)
**CVT Fluid Leaks/Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation, slipping, whining noises, potential overheating warnings.
- **Solution:** Regular fluid changes and inspection for leaks. In severe cases, transmission replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 (fluid service/minor repair), $3,000 - $5,000 (replacement)

## Suspension

### Standard Suspension
**Worn Struts/Shocks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, excessive body roll, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn struts and/or shocks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Faulty Window Regulators**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows failing to operate, slow movement, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator motor or assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400 per window

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500 (software update/minor repair), $500 - $1,500 (replacement)


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Paint Fading/Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Discoloration, loss of clear coat, especially on lighter colors and roof.
- **Solution:** Repainting affected areas or full vehicle respray.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3,000+

**Door Handle Durability**
- **Symptoms:** Cracked or broken exterior door handles.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected door handle.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Interior

### Upholstery and Trim
**Seat Fabric Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Fraying, tearing, or thinning of seat fabric, particularly on driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Seat cover installation or professional upholstery repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500 (covers), $300 - $1,000 (repair/replacement)

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Nissan Cube is a generally reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain. The 1.8L engine is known for its durability. The CVT transmission is the most common area for potential issues, with fluid leaks and overheating being reported, necessitating regular maintenance. Suspension components like struts can wear over time, as is typical for many cars. Electrical gremlins, such as faulty window regulators and infotainment glitches, are not uncommon. Exterior paint issues and interior fabric wear are also points to consider for a vehicle of this age. Overall, with diligent maintenance, the Cube should provide dependable service.
