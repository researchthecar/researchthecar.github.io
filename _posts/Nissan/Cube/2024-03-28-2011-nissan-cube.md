**Introduction:**

The 2011 Nissan Cube is a subcompact hatchback known for its boxy design and spacious interior. It came with a 1.8L 4-cylinder engine paired with either a 5-speed manual or a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

The Cube has been praised for its practicality, versatility, and fuel efficiency. However, it has also received criticisms for its sluggish performance, limited cargo capacity, and subpar handling.

**Engine**

**1.8L 4-Cylinder**

* **Engine Stalling Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine shuts off suddenly while driving or idling, causing loss of power and potential safety concerns.
    * Solution: Replacing the throttle position sensor, cleaning the throttle body, or updating the engine control module software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and possible engine damage if not addressed.
    * Solution: Replacing piston rings or valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Transmission Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy grinding or whining sounds during shifting, indicating potential problems with the gears or bearings.
    * Solution: Replacing worn gears or bearings, or overhauling the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **Transmission Slipping Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or loss of power during acceleration, accompanied by slipping or grinding noises.
    * Solution: Replacing the transmission fluid or rebuilding/replacing the CVT unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise, vibrations, or clunking over bumps, indicating worn or broken strut mounts.
    * Solution: Replacing the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking sounds over bumps, especially in the rear of the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replacing the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical glitches, such as flickering lights, power window malfunctions, or radio problems.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing or replacing faulty electrical components, wiring, or modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Window Regulator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows failing to operate smoothly or becoming stuck.
    * Solution: Replacing the window regulator motor or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Hatch Struts Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear hatch not opening or staying open due to worn or broken struts.
    * Solution: Replacing the rear hatch struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty Climate Control Panel:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control buttons not responding or malfunctioning, leading to erratic temperature regulation.
    * Solution: Replacing the climate control panel or reprogramming the HVAC module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windshield wipers not working properly or failing to activate.
    * Solution: Replacing the wiper motor or repairing the electrical connections.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2011 Nissan Cube has several notable issues, particularly related to the engine, transmission, and electrical system. Engine stalling and oil consumption issues can be significant concerns, while transmission problems can be costly to repair. Suspension and electrical issues are also common, although typically less severe.

Overall, while the Nissan Cube offers practicality and affordability, it's important to be aware of its potential reliability issues before making a purchasing decision. Repair costs can range from relatively minor to substantial, depending on the problem.
