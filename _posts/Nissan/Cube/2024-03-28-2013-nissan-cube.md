**Introduction:**

The 2013 Nissan Cube is a subcompact hatchback known for its boxy design and spacious interior. It features a standard 1.8L inline-4 engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The vehicle is praised for its practical design and fuel efficiency, but criticized for its lack of performance and poor handling.

**Engine**

**1.8L Inline-4**

* **Premature Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown head gasket, coolant leaks, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced performance, exhaust fumes
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Slipping or Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping during acceleration, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace CVT fluid and/or transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Sagging Rear Springs:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging задние пружины, reduced ground clearance, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace rear springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery discharge, engine not starting
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery being drained after prolonged periods of parking
    * Solution: Check and replace faulty electrical components (e.g., lights, stereo)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Handle Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Handle breaks, tailgate becomes difficult to open
    * Solution: Replace tailgate handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks not functioning correctly, door locks not engaging
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 14V413000, NHTSA Campaign ID: 19V509000

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard rattles or squeaks
    * Solution: Use foam or insulation to dampen vibrations
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: DIY cost or $50 - $100+ if having it done professionally
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Belt Buckle Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt buckle becomes rusted or corroded
    * Solution: Replace seat belt buckle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2013 Nissan Cube has several potential issues that can be costly to fix. The most significant issues include premature head gasket failure, CVT problems, and electrical concerns. While the vehicle has some appealing features, the numerous issues and potential expenses should be carefully considered before purchasing.
