# 2018 Nissan Kicks Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used compact crossover, understanding common owner-reported trouble spots helps set clear expectations. The model year brings budget-friendly styling and fuel efficiency, but various electrical, safety, and powertrain concerns have surfaced among drivers. Reviewing these reported areas closely will help you evaluate potential mechanical and electronic hiccups before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine generating approximately 125 horsepower and 115 lb-ft of torque, this setup prioritizes fuel efficiency for city commuting. While mechanically straightforward, electronic sensor glitches can occasionally disrupt smooth operation.

**Engine Stalling and Mass Air Flow Sensor Codes**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden engine stalls and loss of motive power while driving, frequently accompanied by illuminated check engine lights, ABS warnings, traction control lights, and P0101 mass air flow sensor trouble codes.
- **Solution:** Inspect, clean, or replace the mass air flow sensor, and check associated wiring harnesses or throttle body components.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired exclusively with front-wheel drive via a continuously variable transmission, this configuration delivers seamless power delivery and high gas mileage. Buyers should thoroughly test-drive the vehicle to ensure smooth acceleration without hesitation or unusual noises.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Issues related to ABS warnings and unexpected braking behaviors require careful inspection during a pre-purchase test.

**Brake System Warning Lights and ABS Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that all brake failure warning lights remain illuminated while driving and the anti-lock braking system stops functioning properly.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic scan the ABS module, inspect wheel speed sensors, and check hydraulic brake components.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Electronics

Electrical accessories generally function well, though infotainment bugs and minor lighting or camera display glitches have been reported.

**Audio System and Connectivity Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The audio head unit experiences connectivity issues with Android Auto or Apple CarPlay, causing media playback to skip or drop USB connections.
- **Solution:** Perform software resets, update infotainment firmware, or inspect USB ports and connecting cables.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features add modern protection, but radar sensitivity can cause false triggers or temporary system warnings.

**Front Radar and Emergency Braking Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Owners experience unexpected automatic emergency braking activations or dash warnings stating that the front radar is unavailable, frequently triggered during wet weather or normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front bumper radar sensor alignment, clean sensor surfaces, and check for software updates at a dealership.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Kicks&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

This compact crossover offers nimble handling and impressive fuel economy, making it an appealing choice for daily commuting. However, prospective buyers should carefully check for recurring engine sensor codes, verify proper operation of advanced safety radar systems, and ensure the electrical and braking components operate without warning lights. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a trusted mechanic is strongly recommended.
