**Introduction:**

The 2021 Nissan Kicks is a compact crossover SUV available with a single powertrain option: a 1.6-liter inline-four engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The engine produces 122 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque. Despite its modest powertrain, the Kicks is praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and agile handling. However, it has received some criticism for its cramped rear seat and lack of advanced safety features.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains prematurely, leaving it unable to start.
    * Solution: Replace defective battery or check for parasitic power draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical components malfunction due to faulty wiring or connectors.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace damaged wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windshield cracks spontaneously or from minor impacts.
    * Solution: Replace windshield.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard panels vibrate or rattle, especially on rough roads.
    * Solution: Apply insulation or tighten loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to regulate temperature or airflow.
    * Solution: Replace or repair climate control module or related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks fail to engage or disengage, or key FOB does not work.
    * Solution: Replace or repair door lock actuators or related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seatbelt Buckle Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seatbelt buckles fail to latch or release properly.
    * Solution: Replace seatbelt buckle assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Nissan Kicks is a reliable and affordable compact SUV. However, it has experienced some electrical and interior issues, which can be costly to repair. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor, while others can be more serious. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
