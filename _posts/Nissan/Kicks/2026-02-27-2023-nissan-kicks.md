# 2023 Nissan Kicks Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Nissan Kicks, buyers will find a compact crossover designed primarily for efficient urban commuting. Under the hood lies a modest 1.6-liter 4-cylinder engine paired with an Xtronic continuously variable transmission (CVT). While owner reports to federal safety regulators suggest potential areas to inspect—such as electrical issues, occasional engine stalling, and random rear glass shattering—overall ownership experiences vary. Prospective buyers should carefully evaluate these reported issues during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2023 Nissan Kicks is powered by a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine generating 122 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque. It prioritizes fuel economy over spirited performance for daily driving. Reliability is generally acceptable, though owners have flagged occasional electrical and stalling concerns.

**Engine Stalling and Starting Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the vehicle may unexpectedly stall while in motion or at a stoplight, or occasionally fail to crank when attempting to start.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the battery, charging system, throttle body, and related electronic engine sensors for faults.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is sent to the front wheels via an Xtronic continuously variable transmission (CVT). It helps maximize fuel efficiency in city driving, but buyers should ensure smooth operation during a test drive.

**Loss of Power and Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly fail to accelerate when pressing the gas pedal, leading to a loss of drive power.
- **Solution:** A dealership or transmission specialist must inspect the CVT assembly, fluid condition, and control modules for internal failure.

## Exterior

### Rear Window Glass

Exterior lighting and visibility components generally function as expected, though unprompted glass breakage has been reported by a subset of owners.

**Spontaneous Rear Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear windshield may suddenly shatter or pop without any visible impact or external force.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged rear window glass and inspect the defroster grid connections and tailgate frame alignment.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Driver-assistance technologies add modern safety layers to the crossover, though sensor alignment and calibration are critical to prevent false triggers.

**Unintended Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly slam on the brakes while driving forward without any obstacles present in front of it.
- **Solution:** Have the forward-facing camera and radar sensors recalibrated or inspected for software updates by a certified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Kicks&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing issue, the driver's air bag inflator may absorb moisture, and cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that deploys improperly may not protect the driver as intended during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front driver's air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Nissan Kicks serves as an affordable, highly efficient daily commuter with nimble handling. While most vehicles operate without major incident, used car shoppers should remain vigilant regarding reported issues like sudden engine stalling, unexpected automatic braking events, and rare rear windshield breakage. A thorough test drive and a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electronics and safety sensors will help secure a reliable purchase.
