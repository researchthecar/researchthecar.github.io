# 2022 Nissan Kicks Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Nissan Kicks, buyers will find an affordable subcompact crossover geared toward efficiency and urban commuting. While many owners enjoy its maneuverability and fuel economy, used-car shoppers should review potential owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain performance, forward collision systems, and body hardware before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

The Kicks comes equipped with a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing 122 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque. It prioritizes fuel efficiency over spirited acceleration for daily driving. Overall, it serves as a dependable commuter powerplant, though individual mechanical failures have occasionally been reported.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the vehicle unexpectedly decelerates while pressing the accelerator or loses power completely and stalls while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing by a qualified technician to check fuel delivery, ignition systems, or electronic engine controls.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed to the front wheels via an Xtronic continuously variable transmission designed to maximize fuel economy. Routine maintenance and fluid checks are vital to preserving long-term CVT longevity.

**Transmission Engagement Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced situations where the vehicle refuses to move forward when accelerating from a stop, though reverse gear may still function.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission thoroughly inspected by a professional service center to assess internal wear or hydraulic pressure issues.

## Exterior

### Hood Latches and Body Structure

Exterior body panels and latches generally hold up well, though isolated latch failures have been documented by owners.

**Unexpected Hood Opening**
- **Symptoms:** The hood may inadvertently fly open while driving at highway speeds, potentially shattering the windshield and obstructing driver visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hood latch mechanism for secure engagement and replace faulty latches immediately if any wear is detected.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance System

Advanced safety features aim to protect occupants, but sensitive radar and camera hardware can occasionally produce false triggers.

**Erratic Braking and Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system or warning lights may trigger unexpectedly, sometimes causing the vehicle to brake abruptly or steer erratically.
- **Solution:** Check sensor alignment, clean radar and camera areas, and have dealer software calibrations performed if false activations persist.

## Summary Verdict

The Nissan Kicks offers practical urban mobility and strong fuel efficiency, making it an attractive option on the used market. Buyers should conduct a thorough test drive, paying close attention to engine responsiveness, transmission engagement, and proper operation of driver-assistance features before finalizing a purchase.
