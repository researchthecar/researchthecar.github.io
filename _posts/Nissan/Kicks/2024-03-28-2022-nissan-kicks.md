**Introduction**

The 2022 Nissan Kicks is a subcompact crossover SUV known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and sporty handling. It comes equipped with a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). People appreciate the Kicks for its value, practicality, and maneuverability, but some have encountered issues with its reliability.

**Engine**

* **1.6-liter four-cylinder**

    * **Engine Stalling**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine suddenly shuts off while driving, especially during low-speed maneuvers or when idling.
        * Solution: Software update to reprogram the engine control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Cylinder Head Cover Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leakage from the cylinder head cover, leading to reduced oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacement of the cylinder head cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

    * **Jerking or Shuddering**
        * Description/Symptoms: Harsh or erratic shifting, particularly during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: CVT fluid replacement or software update.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mounts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises and clunking sounds when turning or going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges prematurely, making it difficult to start the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical components causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system does not regulate temperature properly or fails to function.
    * Solution: Replacement of the climate control module or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Radio Touchscreen Unresponsive**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment touchscreen becomes unresponsive or glitches intermittently.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the touchscreen unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Bulb Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both headlights fail to illuminate.
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlight bulb.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150 per bulb
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the Nissan Kicks offers a compelling package, it is not without its reliability issues. Several components, such as the engine, transmission, and electrical system, have experienced problems. The severity of these issues varies, but some can be costly to repair, especially for the Infotainment touchscreen or transmission. Overall, the Kicks requires regular maintenance and attention to potential problems to ensure trouble-free ownership.
