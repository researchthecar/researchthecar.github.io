**Introduction**

The 2021 Nissan NV200 is a compact cargo van offered with a single 2.0L 4-cylinder engine mated to a CVT. It has a horsepower of 151 hp and 174 lb-ft of torque. The NV200 is known for its spacious cargo area, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power and basic interior.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-cylinder**

* **Engine Misfire**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace faulty spark plugs or ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on engine startup
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **CVT Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty accelerating, hesitation during gear changes
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild CVT
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise and vibration on bumps, wandering steering
    * Solution: Replace front strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Check for parasitic drain, replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim lights, flickering electronics, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sliding Door Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door binds, does not open or close smoothly
    * Solution: Lubricate door mechanism, adjust hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle coming from dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten loose screws, install sound dampening material
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Steering Wheel Controls Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on steering wheel do not respond
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Nissan NV200 has a number of potential issues that can vary in severity and cost. Engine problems can be expensive to fix, such as timing chain failures. Transmission issues can also be costly. However, many other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to resolve. Overall, the NV200 is a reliable vehicle that offers good value for money.
