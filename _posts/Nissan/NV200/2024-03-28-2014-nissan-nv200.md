The 2014 Nissan NV200 is a versatile compact van that's well-suited for both personal and commercial use. Its key features include a spacious cabin, a powerful engine, and a low price point. The NV200 is available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine with 131 horsepower and 159 lb-ft of torque, and a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine with 133 horsepower and 150 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a five-speed manual transmission or a four-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter four-cylinder engine**
    * **Timing chain rattle:** A rattling noise may occur on startup or acceleration, caused by a faulty timing chain tensioner.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * **Valve cover gasket leak:** Oil may leak from the valve cover gasket, resulting in a burning oil smell and low oil levels.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
* **1.6-liter four-cylinder engine**
    * **No major issues found**

**Transmission**

* **Five-speed manual transmission**
    * **Grinding gears:** Difficulty shifting gears, especially when the transmission is cold.
        * Solution: Overhaul or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Four-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Slipping:** Inability to engage gears properly, resulting in rough shifting or a lack of power.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+

**Suspension**

* **No major issues found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Premature battery discharge, especially when the vehicle is parked.
        * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and repair electrical system faults.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Alternator failure:** Loss of charging power, leading to battery discharge and possible engine stalling.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Front bumper cracking:** Stress cracks may develop on the front bumper, especially in cold weather.
        * Solution: Replace the front bumper.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Climate control malfunctions:** Inability to adjust temperature or airflow, causing discomfort.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system, including replacing faulty actuators or sensors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Dashboard rattles:** Noises emanating from the dashboard or console, especially on rough roads.
        * Solution: Locate and dampen vibrating panels or components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary**

The 2014 Nissan NV200 is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has some potential issues that can be costly to repair. These issues include timing chain rattle, valve cover gasket leaks, and transmission problems. However, many of these issues can be avoided with regular maintenance and careful driving habits.
