# 2013 Nissan NV200

### Overview
The 2013 Nissan NV200 is a compact commercial van designed for urban delivery and utility. It is known for its fuel efficiency and maneuverability, making it a popular choice for businesses. Reliability is generally considered good, with a focus on practical functionality and straightforward engineering. While it doesn't typically suffer from widespread, catastrophic failures, like any vehicle, it can experience issues related to its powertrain, electrical systems, and specific components under heavy use. Owners and fleet managers often report satisfaction with its durability for its intended purpose, though maintenance is key to longevity.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Shuddering**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may hesitate during acceleration or exhibit a shuddering sensation, particularly at lower speeds or when engaging drive.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and replacement, potential recalibration of the CVT control module, or in severe cases, transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Power Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical components (lights, power windows, infotainment) may intermittently stop working or behave erratically.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the Body Control Module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminates, dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine, or complete loss of electrical power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $350 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch/Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Doors may be difficult to open or close, or power locks may not function correctly.
- **Solution:** Lubrication of latches, or replacement of door latches or power lock actuators.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from the vents, or air only coming from certain vents regardless of setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor or blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Nissan NV200 generally presents as a reliable work van, particularly for its intended urban use. The 2.0L engine and standard suspension components are robust. The most commonly cited areas for potential concern are the CVT transmission, which can exhibit hesitation or shuddering, and certain electrical components like the Body Control Module and alternator, which may experience premature failure. Interior HVAC blower motor issues are also reported. While these issues are not universal, they represent the most frequent non-wear-and-tear problems encountered. Regular maintenance, especially on the transmission fluid, is crucial for maximizing the lifespan and reliability of the NV200.
