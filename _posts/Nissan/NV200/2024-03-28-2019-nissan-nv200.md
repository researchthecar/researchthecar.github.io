**Introduction to the 2019 Nissan NV200**

The 2019 Nissan NV200 is a compact cargo van that offers a roomy interior, versatile seating options, and efficient performance. It is powered by a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT) that produces 131 horsepower and 142 pound-feet of torque.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder**
- **Engine Misfires:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine has rough idle, loss of power, and poor fuel economy.
    - Solution: Replace spark plugs and ignition coils.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, reduced power, and possible engine damage.
    - Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and adjust timing.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**
- **Slipping:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Vehicle hesitates on acceleration, loss of power, and rough shifting.
    - Solution: Replace or repair transmission.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Leaking Shock Absorbers:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Reduced suspension performance, knocking or rattling noises from suspension.
    - Solution: Replace shock absorbers.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per axle
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery discharge, and starting problems.
    - Solution: Replace alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    - Recalls: 19V-728
- **Faulty Battery Sensor:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light stays illuminated even after battery replacement.
    - Solution: Replace battery sensor.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rear Door Latch Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rear doors do not latch properly.
    - Solution: Replace rear door latch assembly.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Corrosion on Door Handles:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Pitting and rust on door handles.
    - Solution: Replace or refinish door handles.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per handle
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: No or weak airflow from vents.
    - Solution: Replace blower motor.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Dashboard Rattle:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Noisy rattling or buzzing from dashboard.
    - Solution: Inspect and tighten loose parts.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, while the 2019 Nissan NV200 is a reliable workhorse, it is not without its issues. The most common concerns involve engine misfires, timing chain tensioner failure, and slipping CVT transmission. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potentially serious problems, with repair costs varying widely. Additionally, owners have reported some minor electrical, exterior, and interior issues. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any problems that arise can help prolong the lifespan and minimize the cost of ownership for the 2019 Nissan NV200.
