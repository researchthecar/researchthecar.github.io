# 2020 Nissan NV200

### Overview
The 2020 Nissan NV200 is a compact commercial van designed for urban delivery and cargo transport. It prioritizes practicality, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. Reliability is generally considered a strong point for the NV200, with many owners reporting consistent performance and minimal unexpected repairs. Its straightforward mechanical design contributes to its dependable nature, making it a cost-effective choice for businesses. While specific engine and transmission options are limited, the powertrain generally holds up well under typical commercial use. Owners should still adhere to recommended maintenance schedules to ensure longevity and optimal performance.
