**Introduction:**

The 2005 Nissan X-Trail is a compact SUV powered by a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine that produces 165 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. It is known for its versatility, spacious interior, and affordability. However, it has received some complaints regarding reliability.

**Engine**

* **QR25DE 2.5L 4-Cylinder:**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, often requiring frequent top-ups.
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild engine.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * **Piston Ring Failure:** Pistons can develop premature wear, leading to compression loss and increased oil consumption.
        * Solution: Replace engine.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **RE4F03B 4-Speed Automatic:**
    * **Slipping Gears:** Transmission struggles to engage gears smoothly, resulting in hesitation or jerking.
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * **Torque Converter Failure:** Can cause loss of power, overheating, and transmission slipping.
        * Solution: Replace torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushings:** Premature wear can lead to squeaking, clunking, and decreased handling stability.
        * Solution: Replace bushings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains prematurely, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical components causing the drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure:** Alternator may fail to charge the battery, leading to electrical issues.
        * Solution: Replace alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:** Leaks can occur around the edges of the sunroof, particularly after heavy rain.
        * Solution: Replace or reseal sunroof gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:** Blower motor may stop working, causing lack of airflow from the ventilation system.
        * Solution: Replace blower motor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Plastic Dashboard Cracking:** Dashboard can develop cracks over time, especially in hot climates.
        * Solution: Replace dashboard.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The 2005 Nissan X-Trail has some notable issues, particularly related to its engine and transmission. The cost of repairs for these issues can be significant, ranging from hundreds to thousands of dollars. However, the X-Trail also offers a good balance of value and practicality for its price range.
