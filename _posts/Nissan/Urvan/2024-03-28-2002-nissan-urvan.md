## Introduction

The 2002 Nissan Urvan is a mid-size van that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It was known for its spacious interior, reliable performance, and affordable price. However, like all vehicles, the Urvan had its share of issues.

## Engine

- **ZD30DDTi 3.0L Diesel**
    - **Injector Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Leaking fuel, engine misfiring, loss of power
        - Solution: Replace injectors
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        - Recalls: None Found
- **TB45E 4.5L Petrol**
    - **Valve Spring Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, loss of power, rough idle
        - Solution: Replace valve springs
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        - Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

- **Zd30 Automatic Transmission**
    - **Torque Converter Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, shuddering, loss of power
        - Solution: Rebuild or replace torque converter
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        - Recalls: None Found
- **TB5 Manual Transmission**
    - **Synchronizer Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Difficult to shift gears, grinding noises
        - Solution: Replace synchronizers
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        - Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

- **Front Suspension Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises, poor handling, uneven tire wear
    - Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Suspension Leaf Spring Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity
    - Solution: Replace leaf springs
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

- **Alternator Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, electrical problems
    - Solution: Replace alternator
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Starter Motor Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, clicking noises
    - Solution: Replace starter motor
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

- **Rust on Body Panels**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rust spots appearing on body panels, especially around wheel arches
    - Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of rust
    - Recalls: None Found

## Interior

- **Dashboard Cracking**
    - Description/Symptoms: Cracks developing on the dashboard
    - Solution: Replace dashboard or use a dashboard cover
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2002 Nissan Urvan is overall a reliable and affordable vehicle. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners include engine injector failure, valve spring failure, torque converter failure, and suspension bushing failures. These issues can be costly to fix, but they are not uncommon for vehicles of this age and mileage.
