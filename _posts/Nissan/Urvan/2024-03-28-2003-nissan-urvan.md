**Introduction**

The 2003 Nissan Urvan is a full-size passenger van available with a single engine and transmission option. It is praised for its roomy interior and comfortable ride, but criticized for its outdated design and lack of modern safety features.

**Engine**

* **3.2L V6** (210 hp, 258 lb-ft torque)

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminates, electrical components malfunction due to insufficient power
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks slowly or does not start at all
    * Solution: Replace starter solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust Issues on Rear Tailgate**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion forms on the rear tailgate area, leading to rust and paint peeling
    * Solution: Remove rust, treat with rust inhibitor, and repaint
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights do not turn on or do not work consistently
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC/heater does not blow cold/hot air, controls do not respond correctly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Switches Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows do not go up or down properly or intermittently
    * Solution: Replace power window switch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2003 Nissan Urvan has a few common issues, including alternator failure, headlight switch failure, and climate control malfunctions. These issues can be costly to fix, with some repairs exceeding $800. However, the vehicle is generally reliable and offers a comfortable ride, making it a solid value for those in need of a spacious passenger van.
