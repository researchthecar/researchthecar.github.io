**Introduction:**

The 2012 Nissan Urvan is a full-size passenger van offered with a single engine and transmission option. The 4.2L V6 engine produces 251 horsepower and 271 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The Urvan is known for its spacious interior, comfortable seating, and reliable performance. However, it has also encountered some mechanical issues.

**Engine**

* **4.2L V6 Engine**
    * **Oil Leaking from Valve Cover Gasket:** Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning smell in the engine compartment and low oil levels. Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * **Coolant Leaks from Water Pump:** Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the water pump, leading to overheating and engine damage. Solution: Replace the water pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Slipping or Jerking:** Description/Symptoms: Transmission engages gears slowly or slips between gears, resulting in rough shifting and acceleration problems. Solution: Replace the transmission's internal components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorbers Malfunctioning:** Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged front shock absorbers cause poor handling, excessive bouncing, and reduced ride comfort. Solution: Replace the front shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Rear Leaf Springs Corrosion:** Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the rear leaf springs can weaken and cause them to break, leading to sagging or decreased load capacity. Solution: Replace the rear leaf springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Alternator fails, leading to a loss of power and electrical functions. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Battery Drain:** Description/Symptoms: Electrical drain on the battery, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system issue. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:** Description/Symptoms: Rust develops on body panels, particularly around the tailpipes, door hinges, and lower panels. Solution: Repair or replace the rusted body panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
* **Rear Bumper Sagging:** Description/Symptoms: Rear bumper sags or comes loose due to weak support brackets. Solution: Replace or repair the bumper's support brackets. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Description/Symptoms: Dashboard components rattle or buzz when driving over uneven surfaces. Solution: Apply sound deadening material or tighten loose components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Air Conditioner Blowing Warm Air:** Description/Symptoms: Air conditioner does not blow cold air, indicating a refrigerant leak or compressor failure. Solution: Repair or replace the leaking components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
* **Power Window Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Power window motors fail, causing windows to become inoperable. Solution: Replace the faulty power window motors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2012 Nissan Urvan is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some specific issues. The mechanical issues, such as oil and coolant leaks, can be costly to repair. The electrical problems can range from minor battery drains to alternator failures, requiring varying levels of expertise to diagnose and fix. The suspension and exterior issues are less severe and can be managed with regular maintenance. Overall, the cost of repairs can vary depending on the complexity of the issue and the labor involved.
