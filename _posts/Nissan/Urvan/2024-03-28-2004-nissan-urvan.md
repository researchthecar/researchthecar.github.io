**Introduction**

The 2004 Nissan Urvan is a full-size van known for its spacious interior and reliable performance. It is offered with a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine:**
    * 3.0L V6 (150 hp, 181 lb-ft torque)
* **Transmission:**
    * 5-speed manual
    * 5-speed automatic

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, rough idling, loss of power
    * **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**5-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission fluid leaks, slipping gears, difficulty shifting
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking component (e.g., gasket, seal)
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Vibration or shudder when accelerating
    * **Solution:** Replace torque converter
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Dropping or sagging rear end, uneven tire wear
    * **Solution:** Replace rear leaf springs
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Dim lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light
    * **Solution:** Replace alternator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Electrical Short Circuit:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Blown fuses, intermittent electrical problems
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty wiring or components
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
    * **Solution:** Replace door lock actuator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracking:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Cracks on the dashboard surface
    * **Solution:** Replace dashboard panel
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Failure of the HVAC system to function properly
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace faulty components within the HVAC system (e.g., blower motor, condenser)
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2004 Nissan Urvan is a reliable vehicle with moderate repair costs for common issues. While there are a few potential problems to watch out for, such as intake manifold gasket failure and transmission fluid leaks, these can be addressed with timely maintenance and repairs.
