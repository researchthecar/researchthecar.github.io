# 2004 Nissan Urvan

### Overview
The 2004 Nissan Urvan, a versatile light commercial vehicle, was designed for utility and durability. It offered a range of powertrain options to suit various commercial needs, from local deliveries to longer hauls. While generally considered a robust workhorse, like any vehicle of its age, certain components and systems have shown tendencies for specific issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers of this generation Urvan.

---


## Engine

### 2.7L TD27 Diesel
**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, white smoke from exhaust, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gasket and associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 3.2L QD32 Diesel
**Injector Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough running, loss of power, black smoke, starting problems.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the fuel injection pump.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, excessive smoke, oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Synchronizer Ring Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting into certain gears, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission disassembly and replacement of worn synchronizer rings.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1600


### 4-Speed Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, overheating transmission fluid.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1300

**Valve Body Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, harsh shifts, transmission not engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Cleaning, repair, or replacement of the valve body.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Leaf Spring Fatigue**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity, harsh ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn or broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, steering looseness.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light illuminated, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Wiring Harness Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning accessories, dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection, cleaning, and repair or replacement of corroded wiring sections.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Seals
**Rusting**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust on body panels, wheel arches, and undercarriage.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500+

**Door Seal Deterioration**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, wind noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of door seals.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Seat Upholstery Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Torn or worn seat fabric/vinyl.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat covers.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Nissan Urvan is a workhorse vehicle that, with proper maintenance, can offer reliable service. The primary areas of concern tend to be the diesel engines, particularly with issues related to the fuel system and turbochargers, and the transmission, where clutch wear and internal component wear are common. Suspension components like leaf springs and ball joints can also require attention due to the vehicle's utility nature. Electrical issues, such as alternator failure, and exterior rust are also points to consider. Overall, while not immune to problems, most issues are addressable with standard mechanical repairs.
