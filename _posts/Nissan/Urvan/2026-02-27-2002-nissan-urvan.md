# 2002 Nissan Urvan

### Overview
The 2002 Nissan Urvan, a versatile commercial van, was designed for utility and durability. While generally considered a robust vehicle for its intended purpose, like any vehicle of its age, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical points of concern reported by owners and technicians, excluding routine maintenance. Understanding these potential problems can help owners budget for repairs and ensure the longevity of their Urvan.

---


## Engine

### 2.7L TD27 Diesel
**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, white smoke from exhaust, coolant loss, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of head gasket, potential machining of cylinder head.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, especially in cold weather, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 3.2L QD32 Diesel
**Injector Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, engine stalling, fuel leaks.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the fuel injection pump.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whining or whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


### 4-Speed Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, overheating transmission fluid.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1100


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Leaf Spring Fatigue**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, increased bouncing, rattling noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn or broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises when turning, uneven tire wear, steering instability.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion and Shorts**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical failures, dashboard warning lights, non-functioning accessories.
- **Solution:** Inspection, cleaning, repair, or replacement of corroded or damaged wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $350 - $600


## Exterior

### Body Panels
**Rust and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust spots, particularly around wheel arches, door bottoms, and chassis.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000+


### Door Latches and Hinges
**Sticking or Seizing**
- **Symptoms:** Doors difficult to open or close, rattling noises.
- **Solution:** Lubrication, adjustment, or replacement of latches and hinges.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air or weak airflow from vents, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### Seat Upholstery
**Wear and Tear**
- **Symptoms:** Rips, tears, and thinning of fabric or vinyl, especially on driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat covers/upholstery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Nissan Urvan is a workhorse vehicle that, with proper maintenance, can offer reliable service. Common issues tend to be related to the diesel engines, particularly the TD27 and QD32 variants, with potential problems in head gaskets, glow plugs, and fuel injection systems. Transmission components like clutches and torque converters can also require attention. Suspension elements, especially leaf springs, may show wear due to load-bearing duties. Electrical gremlins and exterior rust are also points to consider for vehicles of this age. Overall, while not immune to age-related issues, the Urvan's robust construction means most problems are repairable, making it a viable option for those seeking a dependable commercial van.
