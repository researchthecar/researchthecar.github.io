**2019 Nissan Micra**

The 2019 Nissan Micra is a subcompact hatchback that offers a combination of affordability, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. It comes with a choice of a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine paired with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The engine delivers 109 horsepower and 107 pound-feet of torque. While the Micra has received positive feedback for its low price tag, spacious interior for its size, and easy handling, it has also faced some criticisms for its lack of power and somewhat dated interior design.

**Engine**

* **1.6-Liter Four-Cylinder**

Issue: **Excessive Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that their vehicles consume excessive amounts of oil, leading to a need for frequent oil top-ups.
Solution: The issue requires engine repair or replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**

Issue: **Gear Grinding**
Description/Symptoms: Drivers have experienced grinding noises and difficulty shifting gears, particularly when the transmission is cold.
Solution: Repair or replacement of the damaged gears is necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

Issue: **Shuddering or Hesitation**
Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported that the CVT transmission can exhibit shuddering or hesitation during acceleration or gear changes.
Solution: The issue may require software updates or transmission repair.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

Issue: **Squeaky or Clunking Noises**
Description/Symptoms: Some owners have complained about squeaky or clunking noises coming from the suspension, particularly over rough roads.
Solution: The issue usually requires replacing worn-out suspension components such as bushings or struts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

Issue: **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported that their vehicles experience battery drain, causing the battery to go dead and require replacement.
Solution: The issue can be traced to faulty electrical components, such as a parasitic draw from an accessory or module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

Issue: **Dashboard Warning Lights**
Description/Symptoms: Several owners have encountered various dashboard warning lights illuminating intermittently, often without any apparent cause.
Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the underlying electrical issue causing the warning lights is required.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause

**Exterior**

Issue: **Rust on Body Panels**
Description/Symptoms: A small number of owners have reported premature rust appearing on certain body panels, particularly around the wheel wells and lower doors.
Solution: Replacement of the affected body panels is usually necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

Issue: **Loose or Squeaky Interior Trim**
Description/Symptoms: Some owners have encountered loose or squeaky interior trim pieces, particularly in the dashboard and door panels.
Solution: Repair or replacement of the affected trim pieces is necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

Issue: **Air Conditioning Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Several owners have reported that their air conditioning systems have malfunctioned or failed to provide adequate cooling.
Solution: The issue may require repair or replacement of the compressor, condenser, or other components in the air conditioning system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number 20V232000

**Summary**

The 2019 Nissan Micra has received mixed reviews, with owners praising its affordability and practicality while also expressing concerns about its reliability and dated features. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as excessive oil consumption and transmission problems, can be more severe and costly. Potential buyers should carefully consider these potential issues and weigh them against the vehicle's strengths before making a decision.
