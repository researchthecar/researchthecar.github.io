# 2016 Nissan Micra

### Overview
The 2016 Nissan Micra, a subcompact hatchback, generally presents a respectable reliability record for its segment. Owners often praise its fuel efficiency and maneuverability in urban environments. While no vehicle is entirely free from potential issues, the Micra tends to avoid widespread, costly problems. Common areas of attention, if they arise, are typically related to minor electrical glitches or specific component wear rather than systemic failures. Overall, the 2016 model is considered a sensible choice for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing dependable, low-cost transportation, provided it has been maintained adequately.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Nissan Micra is generally a reliable vehicle, particularly for its price point. The petrol and diesel engines are robust, and the manual transmission is largely trouble-free. The CVT automatic can occasionally exhibit minor issues like hesitation, but these are usually rectifiable with maintenance. Electrical components, such as the infotainment system and power windows, are the most common areas for minor complaints, though these are not typically critical failures. Overall, the Micra offers good value with a low likelihood of major mechanical problems, making it a sound choice for economical motoring.
