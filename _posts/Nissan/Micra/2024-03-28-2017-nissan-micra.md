**Introduction:**

The 2017 Nissan Micra is a subcompact hatchback known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* 1.0L 3-cylinder, 73 hp, 79 lb-ft
* 1.6L 4-cylinder, 109 hp, 107 lb-ft

With a 5-speed manual or continuously variable transmission (CVT).

The Micra has received praise for its practical interior, low running costs, and peppy performance, while criticisms include its lack of rear legroom, limited safety features, and noisy cabin.

**Engine**

* **1.0L 3-cylinder**

    * **Cylinder Head Cracking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, and reduced engine performance.
        * Solution: Replace the cylinder head.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

    * **Oil Consumption Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and engine damage.
        * Solution: Repair or replace piston rings or oil seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **1.6L 4-cylinder**

    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Manual**

    * **Clutch Problems:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or grinding noises.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

* **CVT**

    * **Belt Pulley Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during acceleration or gear changes.
        * Solution: Replace the belt pulley.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the car is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system components or replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose dashboard components that rattle or vibrate during driving.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace loose parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Nissan Micra has some potential issues, particularly with the 1.0L engine and clutch in the 5-speed manual transmission. However, many of these issues are not widespread, and the car generally has a decent reliability record. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and solution required.
