**Introduction to the 2016 Nissan Micra**

The 2016 Nissan Micra is a subcompact hatchback that offers a combination of affordability, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. It is available with a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine producing 109 horsepower and 107 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a five-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

Owners generally appreciate the Micra's nimble handling, spacious interior for its size, and low fuel consumption. However, some have expressed concerns regarding its lack of power, noisy cabin, and limited cargo capacity.

**Engine**

**1.6-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine with Manual Transmission**

* **Engine Stalling:** Description/Symptoms: The engine suddenly shuts off while driving or idling, causing loss of power and potentially hazardous situations. Solution: Replacement of the crankshaft position sensor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Timing Chain Issues:** Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain can lead to engine damage. Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and associated components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000+
* **Oil Leaks:** Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the engine can occur from various seals or gaskets. Solution: Identification and replacement of the faulty seal or gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**1.6-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine with CVT**

* **CVT Shuddering:** Description/Symptoms: The transmission experiences a noticeable shuddering or vibration during acceleration or gear changes. Solution: Software update or replacement of the CVT. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* **CVT Slipping:** Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips or hesitates during gear changes, resulting in reduced power and poor acceleration. Solution: Replacement of the CVT. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shifting Difficulty:** Description/Symptoms: Resistance or difficulty when shifting gears, particularly when the transmission is cold. Solution: Lubrication of the shift linkage or replacement of the shift cables. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged strut mount bearings can cause clunking or squeaking noises when turning or driving over bumps. Solution: Replacement of the strut mount bearings. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Short Circuit:** Description/Symptoms: Sudden electrical malfunctions or power loss due to a short circuit in the wiring harness. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the affected wiring. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* **Battery Drain:** Description/Symptoms: Battery quickly drains or fails to hold a charge. Solution: Testing and replacement of the battery or faulty alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers fail to operate or become intermittent. Solution: Replacement of the wiper motor assembly. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Description/Symptoms: The heating or air conditioning system fails to function properly. Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the faulty climate control module or related components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
* **Cruise Control Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The cruise control system becomes inoperative. Solution: Inspection and replacement of the faulty cruise control switch or module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250

**Summary**

The 2016 Nissan Micra has received mixed reviews from owners. While its compact size, fuel efficiency, and affordable price are praised, some owners have expressed concerns regarding its limited power, noisy cabin, and potential for costly engine and transmission issues. The severity of these problems can vary from minor inconveniences to major expenses, and potential buyers should carefully consider the potential risks before making a purchase decision.
