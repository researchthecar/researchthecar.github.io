## 2021 Nissan 370Z

The 2021 Nissan 370Z is a sports car available with a 3.7-liter V6 engine paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 7-speed automatic transmission. The engine produces 332 horsepower and 276 lb-ft of torque. The 370Z is praised for its handling, performance, and affordability, but some criticize its cramped interior and lack of modern features.

### Engine
- **3.7L V6**
    - **Oil Consumption**
        - Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, low oil levels
        - Solution: Piston ring replacement
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        - Recalls: None Found
    - **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, illuminated check engine light
        - Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        - Recalls: None Found

### Transmission
- **6-Speed Manual**
    - **Grinding Gears**
        - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting
        - Solution: Replacement of the synchronizers or transmission rebuild
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        - Recalls: None Found
- **7-Speed Automatic**
    - **Shifting Delays**
        - Description/Symptoms: Delayed shifts, hesitation or jerking when shifting
        - Solution: Software update, valve body replacement, or transmission replacement
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
        - Recalls: None Found

### Suspension
- **No Issues Found**

### Electrical
- **Navigation System Malfunction**
        - Description/Symptoms: Navigation system does not work, screen freezes, GPS signal lost
        - Solution: Software update or replacement of the navigation module
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        - Recalls: None Found

### Exterior
- **No Issues Found**

### Interior
- **Climate Control Malfunction**
        - Description/Symptoms: Climate control not functioning properly, temperature not regulating
        - Solution: Replacement of the climate control module or actuators
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        - Recalls: None Found
- **Sun Visor Breakage**
        - Description/Symptoms: Sun visor breaks off easily
        - Solution: Replacement of the sun visor
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        - Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2021 Nissan 370Z has some potential issues to consider, particularly with the engine's oil consumption and the transmission's grinding gears and shifting delays. The electrical issues with the navigation system and climate control are also worth noting. However, the severity and cost of these issues vary, and many 370Z owners have not reported experiencing them. Overall, the 370Z remains a solid sports car, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
