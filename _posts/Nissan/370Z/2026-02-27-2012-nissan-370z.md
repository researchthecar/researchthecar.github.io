# 2012 Nissan 370Z

### Overview
The 2012 Nissan 370Z is a performance-oriented sports coupe known for its engaging driving dynamics and powerful V6 engine. As a model in its third year of production for the Z34 generation, it generally benefits from early production kinks being resolved. Owners often praise its sharp handling, responsive acceleration, and distinctive styling. While it's a reliable sports car, like any vehicle, it can experience specific issues that are worth noting for potential buyers or current owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problem areas and their potential resolutions.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6 (VQ37VHR)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Higher than expected oil levels dropping between changes, potentially leading to low oil warnings.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly and top up as needed. In some cases, internal engine issues might require more extensive repairs.
- **Cost:** $50 - $200 (for oil and filter) / Varies significantly for internal repairs

**Variable Valve Event and Lift (VVEL) Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, loss of power, rattling noise from the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the VVEL actuator or related components. This can be a labor-intensive repair.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Slight delays in gear engagement, occasional rough shifts, particularly at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module (TCM) can often resolve these issues. Fluid changes may also be beneficial.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Nissan 370Z is generally considered a reliable sports car, especially given its performance-oriented nature. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the VQ37VHR engine, specifically potential oil consumption and issues with the VVEL system, which can be costly to repair if they arise. The 7-speed automatic transmission may exhibit some minor shifting anomalies that can often be resolved with software updates or maintenance. The manual transmission and other systems like suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components have shown good durability. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning lights are key to ensuring long-term reliability for this engaging vehicle.
