### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Nissan 370Z is a mechanically sound sports car with a strong track record for its powertrain. The V6 engine and available transmissions are generally robust and reliable. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning the infotainment unit's occasional glitches and potential for intermittent battery drain. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient. Overall, the 370Z offers a reliable platform for enthusiasts, provided attention is paid to these specific electrical components and routine maintenance is performed.
