**Introduction to the 2017 Nissan 370Z:**

The 2017 Nissan 370Z is a rear-wheel-drive sports car available in coupe and convertible body styles. It features a powerful 3.7-liter V6 engine that produces 332 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. The 370Z offers a choice of a 6-speed manual transmission or a 7-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters. Drivers appreciate the 370Z's sharp handling and responsive steering, while some criticize its cramped interior and lack of modern features.

**Engine:**

* **3.7L V6 Engine with 6-Speed Manual Transmission:**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or ticking sounds, reduced power, and possible engine damage
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the valve cover onto the engine, resulting in smoke and an oil smell
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.7L V6 Engine with 7-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leak from the transmission pan or seals, causing low fluid levels and potential transmission damage
        * Solution: Inspect and repair the source of the leak and replace fluid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission:**
    * **Synchronizer Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially from second to third gear
        * Solution: Replace the worn synchronizers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Solenoid Valve Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or refusing to shift, check engine light illuminated
        * Solution: Replace the faulty solenoid valve
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise and vibration, unstable handling
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Mounts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise and vibration, especially over uneven surfaces
    * Solution: Replace the shock absorber mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Electrical Wiring Harness Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical faults, intermittent problems, or loss of power
    * Solution: Inspect and repair the damaged wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim lights, or electrical system failure
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Headlight Bulb Burnout:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or more headlights not illuminating
    * Solution: Replace the burned-out bulbs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Seat Heater Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not functioning or not heating evenly
    * Solution: Replace the faulty heating element or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Panel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control buttons not responding, incorrect temperature settings
    * Solution: Replace the climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Nissan 370Z has received generally positive reviews for its performance and handling, but some owners have reported issues with timing chain tensioner failure, transmission fluid leaks, suspension component wear, electrical problems, and interior malfunctions. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to significant. Overall, the 370Z is a reliable sports car, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues listed above.
