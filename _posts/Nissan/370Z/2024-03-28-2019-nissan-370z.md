**Introduction**

The 2019 Nissan 370Z is a popular sports car known for its sleek design, powerful engine, and agile handling. It comes with a 3.7L V6 engine and a choice of a 6-speed manual or 7-speed automatic transmission. The 370Z is praised for its responsive performance, sharp steering, and well-balanced chassis. However, it also has some drawbacks, including a cramped interior and limited visibility.

**Engine**

*3.7L V6 Engine*

**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from under the valve cover
Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from around the intake manifold
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

*6-Speed Manual Transmission*

**Shift Linkage Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting
Solution: Inspect for binding or worn components, replace linkage as needed
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

*7-Speed Automatic Transmission*

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Rear Subframe Bushing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noises and vibrations from the rear suspension, excessive camber changes
Solution: Replace worn bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Electrical Harness Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, loss of power, warning lights
Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace damaged wiring
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 19V471000

**Body**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Steering Wheel Buttons Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel buttons not responding or sticking
Solution: Replace faulty buttons
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**HVAC Malfunctions**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not functioning properly
Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Nissan 370Z has a few potential issues, but most are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. The most common complaints involve leaks from the engine and transmission, electrical harness problems, steering wheel button malfunctions, and HVAC malfunctions. However, it's important to note that these issues are not widespread and most owners report a positive experience with the 370Z.
