# 2017 Nissan 370Z

### Overview
The 2017 Nissan 370Z is a sports car known for its performance-oriented design and engaging driving experience. As a model in its second generation, it benefits from a mature platform. Reliability for the 2017 model year is generally considered good, with the VQ37VHR engine and its associated transmissions proving to be robust. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.7L V6 (VQ37VHR)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Higher than expected oil level drops between changes, potentially leading to low oil warnings.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly and top up as needed. In severe cases, internal engine inspection may be required.
- **Cost:** $50 - $500 (for monitoring/topping up, significantly more for internal repairs)

**Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, reduced engine power, rattling noise from the front of the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of VVT actuators and potentially related timing components.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and refill, software update, or internal transmission repair/rebuild.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Nissan 370Z demonstrates solid overall reliability, particularly with its well-regarded VQ37VHR engine and manual transmission. The automatic transmission can occasionally exhibit shifting anomalies. Some owners have noted oil consumption in the V6 engine, which warrants regular monitoring. Electrical gremlins, primarily with the infotainment system, are also a possibility. However, major mechanical failures are not commonly reported for this model year. With diligent maintenance and awareness of potential minor issues, the 370Z should provide a dependable and exhilarating driving experience.
