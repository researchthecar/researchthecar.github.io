**Introduction:**

The 2014 Nissan 370Z is a stylish and powerful sports coupe powered by a naturally aspirated 3.7-liter V6 engine. Available transmission options include a 6-speed manual or a 7-speed automatic. Despite its popularity, the 370Z has faced several issues over the years.

**Engine**
- **3.7L V6**
   - **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
     - Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the timing chain tensioner can cause timing chain slips, resulting in engine damage. Symptoms include excessive engine noise, loss of power, and check engine light illumination.
     - Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and associated components
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
     - Recalls: None Found
- **Oil Consumption:**
     - Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent oil top-offs. Symptoms include low oil level warnings, blue smoke from the exhaust, and engine damage if left unattended.
     - Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine rebuild
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
     - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
- **6-Speed Manual**
   - **Transmission Grind:**
     - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into third and fourth gears.
     - Solution: Repair or replacement of synchronizers and gears
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,800+
     - Recalls: None Found
- **7-Speed Automatic**
   - **Transmission Slippage:**
     - Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or hesitating during acceleration, causing a loss of power.
     - Solution: Valve body replacement or transmission rebuild
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
     - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
- **Alternator Failure:**
     - Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, dim headlights, and electrical accessories not working.
     - Solution: Alternator replacement
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
     - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Door Lock Malfunction:**
     - Description/Symptoms: Door locks failing to unlock or lock, causing difficulty entering or exiting the vehicle.
     - Solution: Lock actuator replacement
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
     - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
- **Dashboard Cracking:**
     - Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard, especially around the passenger airbag.
     - Solution: Dashboard replacement
     - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
     - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2014 Nissan 370Z has faced several issues, ranging from severe engine problems to minor electrical and interior concerns. The timing chain tensioner failure and oil consumption issues are particularly concerning, as they can lead to costly repairs or even engine damage. The transmission and electrical issues are also notable, causing performance and reliability issues. However, the suspension has been praised for its handling and agility.
