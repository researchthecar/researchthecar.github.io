**Introduction:**

The 2016 Nissan 370Z is a sports car powered by a 3.7-liter V6 engine that produces 332 horsepower and 276 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a 6-speed manual or 7-speed automatic transmission. The 370Z is praised for its handling, performance, and affordability. However, it also faces criticism for its lack of interior refinement and poor gas mileage.

**Engine**

**3.7L V6**

* **Excessive oil consumption:** Some 370Zs have been reported to consume excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups. This issue is caused by worn piston rings or valve seals.
    * Solution: Replace the affected parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing chain rattle:** Some 370Zs have experienced a rattling noise coming from the timing chain. This noise is caused by the timing chain stretching or skipping teeth.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Grinding gears:** Some 370Zs with the 6-speed manual transmission have reported experiencing grinding gears, especially when shifting into second or third gear. This issue is caused by worn synchronizers or a defective shift linkage.
    * Solution: Replace the affected parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-speed Automatic**

* **Erratic shifting:** Some 370Zs with the 7-speed automatic transmission have experienced erratic shifting, including delayed shifts, harsh shifts, and unexpected downshifts. This issue is caused by a faulty transmission control module (TCM) or a solenoid failure.
    * Solution: Reprogram the TCM or replace the solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension clunking:** Some 370Zs have reported a clunking noise coming from the front suspension. This noise is caused by worn ball joints or control arms.
    * Solution: Replace the affected parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear suspension sag:** Some 370Zs have experienced excessive rear suspension sag, causing the vehicle to sit lower in the rear. This issue is caused by weak rear springs or shocks.
    * Solution: Replace the rear springs or shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some 370Zs have reported a parasitic battery drain, causing the battery to discharge overnight. This issue is caused by a faulty alternator or a problem with the electrical system.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the underlying cause.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight condensation:** Some 370Zs have experienced condensation forming inside the headlight housings. This issue is caused by poor sealing or a faulty ventilation system.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight housings or repair the ventilation system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof rattle:** Some 370Zs have reported a rattling noise coming from the sunroof. This noise is caused by loose sunroof panels or a faulty sunroof motor.
    * Solution: Tighten the sunroof panels or replace the sunroof motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracks:** Some 370Zs have reported cracks forming on the dashboard. This issue is caused by excessive heat or UV exposure.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Seatbelt pretensioner malfunction:** Some 370Zs have reported a malfunctioning seatbelt pretensioner, which can fail to engage in an accident. This issue is caused by a faulty sensor or a problem with the seatbelt system.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair the seatbelt system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: Nissan Recall 17V-264

**Summary:**

The 2016 Nissan 370Z is a capable sports car with several performance and handling advantages. However, it is not without its issues. The engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, while the electrical, exterior, and interior issues are generally less severe and less expensive to fix. Overall, the issues with the 370Z are not very widespread, but they should be considered before purchasing the vehicle.
