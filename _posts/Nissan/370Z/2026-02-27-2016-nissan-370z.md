# 2016 Nissan 370Z

### Overview
The 2016 Nissan 370Z is a performance-oriented sports car known for its engaging driving dynamics and distinctive styling. As a model in its second generation, it benefits from a mature platform. Reliability for the 2016 model year is generally considered good, with owners reporting fewer significant mechanical issues compared to some competitors. The V6 engine and manual transmission are often highlighted for their robustness. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that may warrant attention, particularly concerning the infotainment system and some suspension components under heavy use. Overall, the 370Z offers a compelling package for enthusiasts seeking a blend of performance and dependability, provided routine maintenance is observed.

## Suspension

### Standard Sport Suspension
**Front Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn sway bar links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Bluetooth Connectivity Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent connection drops, failure to pair devices, audio skipping.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment unit, or potential head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**Backup Camera Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Camera not displaying an image, distorted image, or intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of the camera or its wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Nissan 370Z demonstrates solid overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.7L V6 engine and both transmission options are generally robust and well-regarded. Minor concerns are primarily related to the infotainment system's connectivity and occasional backup camera issues, which are not uncommon in vehicles of this era. Suspension components like sway bar links can wear over time, leading to noise, but are typically straightforward and relatively inexpensive to repair. With diligent maintenance, the 2016 370Z should provide a dependable and exhilarating driving experience for its owners.
