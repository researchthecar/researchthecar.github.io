### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Nissan Aprio is a generally dependable vehicle, particularly with its 1.6L engine and manual transmission. The most common reported issues tend to be related to the automatic transmission's longevity, with slipping gears being a concern. Suspension components, specifically bushings, can wear over time and lead to noise and handling degradation. Electrical gremlins, such as failing power window motors, are also noted. While not indicative of a fundamentally unreliable car, these potential issues suggest that buyers should inspect these areas carefully and budget for potential repairs, especially on higher-mileage examples.
