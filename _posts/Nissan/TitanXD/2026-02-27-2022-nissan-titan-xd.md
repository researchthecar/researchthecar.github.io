# 2022 Nissan Titan XD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Nissan Titan XD serves as a heavy-duty alternative to traditional half-ton trucks, offering a standard 5.6-liter Endurance V8 engine and a standard four-wheel-drive configuration. While it provides strong towing capacity and a comfortable cabin, used car buyers should carefully inspect available vehicle history records, as owner reports to NHTSA indicate potential areas of concern.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Endurance

The 5.6-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces approximately 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, paired with a heavy-duty frame. It offers robust performance for daily driving and towing, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

This 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth power distribution across various driving conditions and pairs exclusively with the four-wheel-drive system. Overall powertrain operation is generally solid, but a test drive is recommended to ensure seamless shifting.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V772000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Titan+XD&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Nissan Titan XD offers solid capability with its V8 powertrain and robust platform. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and test drive all vehicle systems to ensure everything operates smoothly before completing a purchase.
