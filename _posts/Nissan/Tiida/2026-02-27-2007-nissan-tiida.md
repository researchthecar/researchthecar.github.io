# 2007 Nissan Tiida

### Overview
The 2007 Nissan Tiida, known in some markets as the Nissan Versa, is generally considered a reliable compact car, particularly for its intended purpose as an economical daily driver. It offers a practical interior and decent fuel efficiency. While it doesn't typically suffer from major widespread mechanical failures, like any vehicle of its age, certain components may experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. The focus is on providing actionable data for technical reliability.

### 4-Speed Automatic (RE4F03B)
**Slipping or Delayed Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission may hesitate to engage gears, slip between gears, or exhibit rough shifting. Can be exacerbated by low transmission fluid levels or internal wear.
- **Solution:** Check and top up transmission fluid. If fluid is low, inspect for leaks. Internal repairs or transmission replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500 (fluid service/minor repair) to $2000 - $4000 (rebuild/replacement)


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Bushings and Ball Joints**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises from the front or rear suspension, especially over bumps. Uneven tire wear and vague steering feel can also occur.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings and ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per axle


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Faulty Window Regulators**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows may become slow, stop working, or make grinding noises. The regulator mechanism can fail.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350 per door

**Instrument Cluster Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or complete failure of gauges (speedometer, tachometer, fuel gauge), warning lights not illuminating or staying on, or flickering displays.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster. Sometimes a loose connection can be the cause.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The blower motor only works on certain speeds (usually high) or not at all. This is a common failure point in many vehicles.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Nissan Tiida is a generally dependable vehicle with a good track record for reliability. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the automatic transmission, specifically slipping or delayed shifting, and some electrical components like window regulators and instrument clusters. Suspension components, particularly bushings and ball joints, may require attention due to age and mileage. Interior issues are less common but can include HVAC blower motor resistor failures. Overall, with regular maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly, the Tiida should continue to provide reliable service. It's a practical choice for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing economy over luxury features.
