**Introduction**

The 2008 Nissan Tiida is a compact hatchback that offers a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 1.6L inline-4 engine with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission, and a 1.8L inline-4 engine with a 6-speed manual or CVT. The Tiida is known for its spacious interior, affordable price, and fuel-efficient performance. However, it has also been plagued by several notable issues.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4 Engine**

**Stuck Exhaust Valve Stems**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased oil consumption, hard starting, knocking noises
Solution: Replacement of affected valve stems
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**1.8L Inline-4 Engine**

**Overheating**
Description/Symptoms: High coolant temperature gauge readings, reduced engine power, steam or coolant leaks
Solution: Inspection and repair of cooling system components, including radiator, thermostat, and water pump
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 09V201000 (Radiator Replacement)

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Difficulty Shifting Gears**
Description/Symptoms: Stiff or clunky gear shifting, difficulty selecting gears
Solution: Adjustment or replacement of shifter linkage, lubrication of gear selector
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Torque Converter Shuddering**
Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering felt during acceleration or gear changes
Solution: Replacement of torque converter or transmission fluid flush
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Shock Absorber Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing or swaying of the front end, poor handling
Solution: Replacement of front shock absorbers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per shock absorber
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light illuminated
Solution: Replacement of alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Trim Panel Detachment**
Description/Symptoms: Loose or detached trim panels on the doors, windows, or roof
Solution: Reattachment or replacement of affected trim panels
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or intermittent climate control system, inconsistent temperature regulation
Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of climate control components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2008 Nissan Tiida has been met with mixed reviews, facing several reported issues despite its affordability and fuel efficiency. The most significant issues include engine overheating, transmission shuddering, and suspension failure, which can range in severity and cost of repair from hundreds to over a thousand dollars. While some of these issues have been subject to recalls, it is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing or owning a Tiida.
