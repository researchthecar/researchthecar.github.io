**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2009 Nissan Tiida is a subcompact hatchback available with a 1.8-liter 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and good fuel economy. However, it is known for its lack of power, especially with the automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**1.8-liter 4-cylinder Engine**

* **Oil Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of oil from the engine, visible oil stains on the ground or under the car.
    * Solution: Replace oil pan gasket or other leaking seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from the engine at startup, reduced engine power, illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard or impossible to shift gears, grinding noise when shifting.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace clutch, inspect or replace transmission linkage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifting, slipping gears, illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace or repair transmission solenoids or valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension, uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace strut mount.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, flickering gauges, dead battery, illuminated battery warning light.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Short in Steering Column:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power steering, flickering headlights, illuminated airbag warning light.
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty wiring in steering column.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Locks Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, door locks may not respond to key fob or switch.
    * Solution: Replace or repair door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ per door
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Airbag Warning Light Illumination:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Airbag warning light remains illuminated after the car has been started.
    * Solution: Reset airbag control module or replace faulty wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

The 2009 Nissan Tiida has some notable issues, particularly in the engine and transmission. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to major repairs. The estimated cost to fix these issues can be significant, so it's important to factor these potential expenses into your decision before purchasing a used Tiida.
