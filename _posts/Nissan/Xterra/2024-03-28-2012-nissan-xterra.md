**Introduction:**

The 2012 Nissan Xterra is a mid-size SUV powered by a 4.0L V6 engine that produces 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Known for its ruggedness, versatility, and off-road capabilities, the Xterra also offers a 6-speed manual transmission option for enthusiasts.

Despite its strengths, the 2012 Xterra has been associated with various issues across its different components.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6 with 5-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, engine performance issues.
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.0L V6 with 6-speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Overheating Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, or overheating warning light.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, uneven tire wear, poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace front suspension springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or popping noises, sagging rear end.
    * Solution: Replace rear leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharging, dimming lights, electrical failures.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Window Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative windows, noisy operation.
    * Solution: Replace window motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: Recall #12V135000

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust or corrosion on roof rack, loose or broken bolts.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace roof rack components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Fog Light Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fog lights fill with water or condensation.
    * Solution: Replace fog lights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors with power locks.
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or malfunctioning climate control system, including heating, ventilation, or air conditioning.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair electrical components or replace climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: Recall #13V183000

**Summary:**

The 2012 Nissan Xterra has been plagued by a number of issues, most commonly affecting the transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior components. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and its extent.
