# 2007 Nissan Xterra

### Overview
The 2007 Nissan Xterra is a capable mid-size SUV known for its off-road prowess and rugged construction. While generally considered reliable, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners and mechanics. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2007 model year across its various configurations. Understanding these common points of concern can help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and potential repairs.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L V6 (VQ40DE)
**Timing Chain Rattle**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling or ticking noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup or at idle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain tensioners, guides, and potentially the chain itself.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks around the oil cooler lines or housing.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking oil cooler lines or seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


### 2.5L 4-Cylinder (QR25DE)
**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, white smoke from exhaust, coolant loss, milky oil.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the head gasket and associated components.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2000


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (RE5R05A)
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed engagement, rough shifts between gears, or slipping sensation.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, solenoid replacement, or full transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2500

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, or unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator and motor assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Instrument Cluster Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering gauges, dead pixels on displays, or intermittent power loss to the cluster.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Nissan Xterra is a robust vehicle with a generally good reliability record, particularly for its intended off-road use. The most frequently cited technical issues revolve around the engine and transmission, with the 4.0L V6 engine susceptible to timing chain noise and the automatic transmission sometimes exhibiting harsh shifting. The 2.5L engine has had reports of head gasket issues. Electrical components like window motors and instrument clusters can also present problems. However, many owners report long-term satisfaction, especially when these known issues are addressed promptly. Regular maintenance and addressing early signs of trouble are key to ensuring the longevity of a 2007 Xterra.
