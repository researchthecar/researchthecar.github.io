**Introduction**

The 1993 Nissan D21 is a compact pickup truck that was known for its reliability and off-road capabilities. It was available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.4L inline-four, a 3.0L V6, and a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The D21 was praised for its rugged construction and affordable price, but it also had several issues that owners commonly reported.

**Engine**

* **2.4L Inline-Four**
    * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Blown head gasket, coolant leaks, overheating
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **3.0L V6**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive engine noise, loss of power, rattling
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking clutch fluid, difficulty shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: N/A
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Inability to shift gears properly, loss of power
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises when turning or braking, loose steering
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, lights dimming, electrical problems
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Rust Prone Frame**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust on the frame, especially in areas near the rear tires
    * Solution: Treat with rust inhibitor or replace frame
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks and warping on the dashboard, especially in areas exposed to sunlight
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or cover with dash mat
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 1993 Nissan D21 is a reliable and affordable pickup truck, but it does have some common issues that owners should be aware of. The severity and cost of these issues range from minor fixes to major repairs, but most of them can be addressed with relatively straightforward procedures.
