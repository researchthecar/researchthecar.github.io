**Introduction:**

The 2022 Nissan Versa is a subcompact sedan that comes with a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 122 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It has received praise for its spacious interior, good fuel economy, and affordability, but criticisms include its lack of power, noisy cabin, and uninspiring driving dynamics.

**Engine**

**1.6L Four-Cylinder Engine**

**Timing Chain Rattle:**
Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the engine, especially on cold starts.
Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioners.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V108000

**Transmission**

**CVT Transmission**

**Shuddering:**
Description/Symptoms: A vibration or shuddering felt during acceleration or deceleration.
Solution: Replace the transmission fluid or the entire transmission in severe cases.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: The battery draining quickly, even when the vehicle is turned off.
Solution: Replace the battery or check for electrical leaks.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Loose Trunk Lid:**
Description/Symptoms: The trunk lid not closing properly, allowing water to leak in.
Solution: Adjust the trunk hinges or replace the latch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattle:**
Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the dashboard while driving on rough roads.
Solution: Tighten loose screws or replace the dashboard panel.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System:**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezing, lagging, or not responding to inputs.
Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Nissan Versa has some reported issues, but the severity and cost of these issues vary. The most significant problems include timing chain rattle in the engine and shuddering in the CVT transmission. However, these issues are not widespread and seem to affect a limited number of vehicles. Overall, the Versa is a reliable and affordable subcompact sedan that offers good value for the money.
