**Introduction:**

The 2008 Nissan Versa is a subcompact sedan powered by a 1.8-liter, 122-horsepower engine mated to either a 4-speed automatic or 5-speed manual transmission. Praised for its fuel efficiency and affordability, the Versa has also received criticism for its lackluster performance and interior space.

**Engine**

* **1.8L 16-Valve DOHC 4-Cylinder Engine**

    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine performance, rough idling, poor fuel economy
        * Solution: Intake manifold cleaning
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Guide Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially at startup
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain guides and tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed acceleration
        * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, filter replacement, or transmission rebuild
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**

    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Struts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise and vibration when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces
    * Solution: Replacement of struts and mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Springs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken rear springs, leading to poor handling
    * Solution: Replacement of rear springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dimming lights
    * Solution: Battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system failure, discharged battery
    * Solution: Replacement of alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**
    * Description/Symptoms: Yellowing or cloudy headlights, reduced illumination
    * Solution: Headlight restoration or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Handles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sticking or broken door handles
    * Solution: Replacement of door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises and vibrations from the dashboard, especially on bumpy roads
    * Solution: Removal and tightening of dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies based on severity
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Cushions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uncomfortable seat cushions
    * Solution: Replacement of seat cushions
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2008 Nissan Versa is generally a reliable and affordable vehicle, it is not without its share of issues. The most common problems include intake valve carbon buildup, timing chain guide failure, suspension noise and vibration, and electrical system failures. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific vehicle and the extent of the damage. Regular maintenance and prompt repairs can help mitigate the risk of experiencing these issues and ensure the vehicle's longevity.
