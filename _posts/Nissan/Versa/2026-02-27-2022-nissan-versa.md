# 2022 Nissan Versa Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used compact sedan, understanding common owner-reported issues can help guide your inspection and purchasing decisions. This overview highlights patterns reported to federal safety regulators by vehicle owners to help you identify potential areas of concern during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine generates approximately 122 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate efficiency for daily commuting. While generally standard for the segment, some owners have reported throttle response and power management anomalies.

**Unexpected Limp Mode and Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle unexpectedly enters a low-power limp mode or experiences hesitation and sluggishness when the accelerator pedal is depressed.
- **Solution:** Owners note that restarting the vehicle after a brief waiting period can sometimes temporarily restore normal power, but persistent issues require professional diagnostic testing.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped on higher trims, the Xtronic CVT aims to maximize fuel economy through stepless gear ratios. Buyers should carefully evaluate CVT operation during a test drive, as transmission repairs can be costly.

**Transmission Slipping and Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have experienced transmission slipping, lurching during acceleration, unexpected stalling, or complete internal failure at higher mileages.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition and test drive thoroughly for smooth power delivery. Severe mechanical wear typically requires professional transmission overhaul or replacement.

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

The base 5-speed manual transmission offers a traditional clutch-pedal experience for drivers preferring direct gear selection. It is generally simpler mechanically than the automatic alternative.

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Automatic Braking

Advanced driver assistance features enhance modern safety profiles but rely heavily on cameras and radar that can occasionally misinterpret environmental data.

**Random Phantom Braking and Warning Activation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted instances where the vehicle's automated emergency braking system or rear parking collision warning activates unexpectedly without any obstacles present.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect sensor alignment, cleanliness, and software calibration if false collision warnings occur frequently.

## Steering

### Center Console Armrest

Cabin ergonomics play a role in driver comfort and control; test fitting the driving position during a pre-purchase inspection is recommended.

**Armrest Interference with Steering Movement**
- **Symptoms:** The positioning of the center console armrest can occasionally obstruct arm movement and physical steering control during sharp turns.
- **Solution:** Drivers can adjust seating position or avoid using the armrest during tight maneuvers to prevent physical interference.

## Exterior

### Rear Windshield

Exterior glass components are subject to environmental stresses and thermal changes, though spontaneous breakage remains relatively uncommon.

**Spontaneous Rear Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** In isolated reports, owners experienced the rear windshield shattering suddenly while driving in cold weather without any prior impacts or visible chips.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the rear glass panel by an auto glass professional is required.
