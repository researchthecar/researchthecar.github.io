**Introduction:**

The 2021 Nissan Versa is a subcompact sedan that offers a budget-friendly option for drivers. It comes with a 1.6-liter 4-cylinder engine that produces 122 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Nissan Versa is known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. However, certain issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine:**

* **1.6-liter 4-cylinder engine**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, especially in vehicles with high mileage.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Issues:** Premature stretching or failure of the timing chain.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **5-speed Manual Transmission:**
    * **Difficulty Shifting Gears:** Gears may be hard to shift, especially when the transmission is cold.
        * Solution: Replace or adjust shift linkage.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT):**
    * **Shuddering or Vibration:** Transmission may shudder or vibrate during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Update transmission software or replace CVT.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Draining:** Battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is turned off.
        * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Engine Control Module (ECM) Failure:** ECM may fail, causing engine problems and dashboard warning lights.
        * Solution: Replace ECM.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Windshield Cracking:** Windshield may crack or shatter easily.
        * Solution: Replace windshield.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Air Conditioning Issues:** Air conditioning may not blow cold air or may make excessive noise.
        * Solution: Repair or replace air conditioning compressor and/or related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the reported issues with the 2021 Nissan Versa range from minor inconveniences to more serious problems that may require costly repairs. Some issues, such as excessive oil consumption and timing chain issues, can be a significant concern for owners with high mileage vehicles. While the CVT transmission has received complaints of shuddering or vibration, no major reliability problems have been widely reported.
