**Introduction**

The 2014 Nissan Versa is a subcompact sedan known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and roomy interior. It's available with two engine options: a 1.6-liter inline-4 and a 1.8-liter inline-4 paired with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. While the Versa has received positive feedback for its value and practicality, it has also encountered several issues across various components.

**Engine**

**1.6-Liter Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.8-Liter Inline-4**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping out of gear, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking noise from front or rear suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature battery failure, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Taillight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation or moisture inside taillights
    * Solution: Replace taillights or seal taillights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks forming on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or have it repaired
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2014 Nissan Versa offers a budget-friendly and practical package, it has faced several issues across various components. Some of the more severe and costly repairs include intake manifold gasket failure, timing chain tensioner failure, and dashboard cracking. However, the cost of these repairs can vary significantly depending on the severity of the issue and the price of parts and labor in your area.
