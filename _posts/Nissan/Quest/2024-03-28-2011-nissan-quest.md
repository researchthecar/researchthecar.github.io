**Introduction**

The 2011 Nissan Quest is a minivan that offers a spacious cabin, comfortable seating, and a variety of features. It is available with a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission, producing 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. Some pros of the Quest include its stylish exterior, well-equipped interior, and user-friendly technology. However, it has been criticized for its subpar fuel economy and limited cargo space behind the third row.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake Valve Deposit Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon deposits accumulate on the intake valves, leading to reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Remove the intake manifold and clean the intake valves using specialized tools and chemicals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: A crack develops in the exhaust manifold, causing an exhaust leak that can be heard as a loud ticking sound.
    * Solution: Replace the exhaust manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation and Shuddering**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates when shifting gears or shudders during acceleration.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Alternator fails to generate sufficient power, causing battery discharge and electrical system issues.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle becomes loose or breaks, making it difficult to open the door.
    * Solution: Replace the door handle assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Air Conditioning Blower Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower motor stops working, resulting in lack of airflow in the cabin.
    * Solution: Replace the blower motor assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Lid Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Center console lid becomes jammed or broken, unable to open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replace the center console lid assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Nissan Quest has some notable issues, mainly related to the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. The most severe issue is the intake valve deposit buildup, which can cause significant engine problems. The estimated cost to fix the issues can range from $100 to over $4,000, depending on the severity and complexity of the repair. It's important to consider these potential issues when making a purchase decision and ensure proper maintenance to minimize the risk of costly repairs.
