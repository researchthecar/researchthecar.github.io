The 2011 Nissan Juke is a subcompact crossover SUV that was praised for its sporty handling, unique styling, and affordability. It was available with two engine options: a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated inline-four (117 horsepower, 111 lb-ft of torque) and a 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-four (188 horsepower, 177 lb-ft of torque). Transmission options included a 5-speed manual, a 6-speed manual, and a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

* **1.6L Naturally Aspirated Inline-Four**
    * **Engine Oil Leak:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket.
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found
* **1.6L Turbocharged Inline-Four**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Turbocharger fails due to internal damage.
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual:**
    * **Shifting Issues:** Difficulty shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replace shift linkage or transmission synchronizers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-Speed Manual:**
    * **Clutch Issues:** Clutch slips or fails prematurely.
        * Solution: Replace clutch assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **CVT:**
    * **Transmission Shudder:** Transmission shudders during acceleration or deceleration.
        * Solution: Software update or transmission replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 17V345000 (CVT Control Valve Malfunction)

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Radio Issues:** Radio malfunctions or becomes unresponsive.
        * Solution: Software update or radio replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains prematurely.
        * Solution: Inspect for parasitic draw and replace faulty components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Housing Moisture:** Headlight housings accumulate moisture.
        * Solution: Replace headlight housings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Squeaks:** Center console rattles or squeaks.
        * Solution: Tighten or replace center console mounting brackets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Seal Leak:** Sunroof seal allows water to leak into the cabin.
        * Solution: Replace sunroof seal.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Nissan Juke has some notable issues, particularly with the turbocharged engine's turbocharger and the CVT transmission. The estimated cost of repairs for these issues can be significant, ranging from $1,500 to over $2,500. However, the vehicle's affordability and sporty handling make it a compelling option for some buyers.
