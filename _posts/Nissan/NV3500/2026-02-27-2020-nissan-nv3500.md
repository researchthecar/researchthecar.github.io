### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Nissan NV3500 generally exhibits strong reliability, particularly concerning its powertrain, which is a critical factor for commercial vehicles. The 5.6L V8 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission have proven to be durable. While most systems are robust, minor electrical gremlins, such as intermittent accessory power, and occasional issues with door latches have been noted. These are typically not catastrophic failures and are often repairable with moderate cost. Overall, the NV3500 is a dependable workhorse, with its main potential concerns being minor and manageable, allowing for consistent operation in demanding work environments.
