# 2014 Nissan NV3500

### Overview
The 2014 Nissan NV3500 is a full-size commercial van known for its utility and cargo capacity. Reliability for this model year is generally considered good, with a focus on its robust powertrain and functional interior. Owners often praise its durability for commercial use. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems reported by owners and technicians, providing insights into potential repair areas and associated costs to help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions.

## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair parasitic draw, replace faulty battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Replace alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening from inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty door latch or lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per door


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or intermittent airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replace blower motor or blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


### Infotainment System (if equipped)
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, display freezing or showing errors.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Nissan NV3500 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly with its core powertrain components. The 5.6L V8 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are robust and typically do not present widespread issues beyond normal maintenance. The chassis and suspension also hold up well under typical usage. Areas that have seen some reported concerns include electrical components like the alternator and battery drain issues, as well as interior elements such as the HVAC blower motor and, on models equipped with them, the infotainment system. Exterior issues are less common but can involve door latch mechanisms. Overall, the NV3500 is a dependable work vehicle, with most potential problems being repairable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
