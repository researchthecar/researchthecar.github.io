**Vehicle Introduction**
The 2014 Nissan NV3500 is a full-size van available in multiple configurations. It offers a 5.6L V8 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Drivers appreciate its spacious interior, cargo capacity, and powerful engine. However, it has received some negative feedback regarding its fuel economy.

**Engine**
5.6L V8

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine burns excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the piston rings or replace the engine.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $8,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
5-Speed Automatic

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission fails to engage or disengage gears properly, causing the vehicle to hesitate, jerk, or not move.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Clunking Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A loud clunking noise is heard when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission mount.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Suspension**

* **Sagging Springs**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension springs sag or break, causing the vehicle to sit lower and affect handling.
    * Solution: Replace the rear suspension springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Leaking Shocks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The shocks leak fluid, decreasing ride quality and stability.
    * Solution: Replace the shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Test and identify the component draining the battery and repair or replace it.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails to charge the battery, leading to electrical problems such as dim lights and failure to start.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800

**Exterior**
* **Side Door Misalignment**
    * Description/Symptoms: The side door does not close properly or becomes misaligned.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the door hinges or latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system fails to regulate temperature or air flow.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace the faulty component, such as the blower motor, compressor, or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component

**Summary**
Overall, the 2014 Nissan NV3500 has received mixed reviews from owners. While it offers a powerful engine and spacious interior, it is prone to some potentially severe and expensive issues in areas such as the engine, transmission, and electrical system. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and consider the cost of potential repairs before purchasing this vehicle.
