# 2016 Nissan NV3500

### Overview
The 2016 Nissan NV3500 is a full-size commercial van designed for utility and cargo hauling. ## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery failure, vehicle not starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace faulty battery, check for parasitic draw from accessories or modules.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening from inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty door latch or lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per door


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or intermittent airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Nissan NV3500 demonstrates strong reliability in its core powertrain components, with the 5.6L V8 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission generally performing without widespread issues. The chassis and suspension also appear robust for its intended commercial use. However, owners may encounter some electrical gremlins, such as battery drain or infotainment system glitches, which are not uncommon in vehicles of this age and complexity. Exterior issues are typically limited to specific components like door latches. Interior concerns often revolve around the HVAC blower motor. Overall, the NV3500 is a dependable work vehicle, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
