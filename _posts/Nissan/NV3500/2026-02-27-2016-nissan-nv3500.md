# 2016 Nissan NV3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Nissan NV350und is a heavy-duty commercial van built to handle demanding work and passenger-transport duties. While it offers a robust platform for fleets and large families, used-car buyers should inspect powertrain components, climate control systems, and braking behavior closely during pre-purchase evaluations.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V6

The 4.0-liter V6 engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate baseline power for lighter commercial needs. It is generally regarded as a durable workhorse, though occasional sensor or catalytic converter issues have been reported by owners.

### 5.6L V8

The 5.6-liter V8 engine generates around 317 horsepower and 385 lb-ft of torque, offering strong towing and hauling capabilities. It delivers heavy-duty muscle for commercial applications, but buyers should listen for exhaust leaks and watch for stalling or emissions-related owner complaints.

**Catalytic Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminates, indicating a failure of one or both catalytic converters.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty catalytic converters to restore proper emissions compliance and engine performance.
- **Cost:** $2500

**Unexpected Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall while idling or driving at low speeds, occasionally failing to accelerate past low speeds afterwards.
- **Solution:** Inspect throttle bodies, fuel delivery components, and electronic control modules for faults.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic Transmission

This traditional 5-speed automatic transmission pairs with the V6 engine to handle commercial duty cycles. It features a straightforward design without complex continuously variable mechanisms, making it relatively dependable when fluid changes are maintained.

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission is matched to the V8 engine, providing closer gear ratios for optimized towing performance and highway cruising. Regular maintenance is recommended to ensure long-term durability under heavy loads.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Commercial vans require robust stopping power, but owner reports indicate potential concerns with pedal travel and front-end vibration during heavy braking events.

**Brake Pedal Travel and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** During hard or panic stops, the brake pedal may travel excessively toward the floor, and the front end may shake violently during high-speed braking.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, master cylinders, and ABS hydraulic units for wear or hydraulic pressure loss.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Body Electronics

electrical issues can occasionally disrupt dashboard readouts and warning indicators, requiring thorough diagnostic scans.

**Instrument Panel Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The speedometer, odometer, and check engine lights may malfunction simultaneously while warning lights illuminate erratically.
- **Solution:** Check instrument cluster wiring connections, body control modules, and vehicle grounding points.

## Exterior

### Climate Control and Seating

Interior comfort components generally hold up well, though rear-seat passengers may experience reduced climate control airflow.

**Insufficient Rear Air Flow**
- **Symptoms:** Airflow from the rear A/C vents may feel exceptionally weak, causing rear passenger areas to become uncomfortably warm.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear blower motors, ductwork obstructions, and blend door actuators.

The 2016 Nissan NV3500 is a capable choice for commercial fleets and large passenger transport, but buyers should perform a rigorous inspection.
