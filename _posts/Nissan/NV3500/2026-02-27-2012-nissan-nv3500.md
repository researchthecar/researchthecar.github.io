## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may not start after sitting for a period, dashboard warning lights may flicker.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace faulty battery, alternator, or parasitic draw component (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory).
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**Power Window Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows operate slowly, intermittently, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replace the power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Door Lock Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Central locking system fails to lock or unlock doors consistently.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty door lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, or intermittent airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replace the HVAC blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Nissan NV3500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain (engine and transmission), where widespread issues are not commonly reported. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are related to the electrical system, specifically battery drain and power window components, and some exterior and interior accessories like door lock actuators and the HVAC blower motor. These issues are generally repairable and do not indicate systemic flaws in the vehicle's core design. With proper maintenance, the NV3500 is expected to provide dependable service for commercial applications.
