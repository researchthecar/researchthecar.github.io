**2021 Nissan NV3500**

The 2021 Nissan NV3500 is a full-size van offered with a choice of two engines: a 4.0-liter V6 and a 5.6-liter V8. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The NV3500 is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and strong towing capacity. However, there have been some issues reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **4.0-liter V6**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to jump or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5.6-liter V8**
    * **Valvetrain noise:** Some owners have reported excessive valvetrain noise, such as ticking or chattering. This noise is typically caused by worn or damaged valve lifters.
        * Solution: Replace the valve lifters.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed automatic:**
    * **Shifting problems:** Some owners have reported shifting problems, such as hard or delayed shifts. These problems can be caused by a variety of issues, such as worn or damaged transmission components, a faulty transmission control module, or a software glitch.
        * Solution: Depending on the cause of the problem, the transmission may need to be repaired or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some owners have reported that their batteries are draining prematurely. This problem can be caused by a variety of issues, such as a faulty alternator, a parasitic drain, or a damaged battery.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the battery drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause of the problem
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Seat belt retractor failure:** Some owners have reported that the seat belt retractor has failed, causing the seat belt to become loose or inoperable.
        * Solution: Replace the seat belt retractor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate control issues:** Some owners have reported problems with the climate control system, such as the air conditioner not blowing cold air or the heater not working properly.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the climate control problem.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause of the problem
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Nissan NV3500 is a generally reliable vehicle, but there have been some issues reported with the engine, transmission, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but some of them can be quite expensive to repair.
