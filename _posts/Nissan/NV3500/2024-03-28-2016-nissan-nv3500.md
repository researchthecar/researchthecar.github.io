**Introduction**

The 2016 Nissan NV3500 is a full-size cargo van renowned for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and robust construction. It is offered with a choice of gasoline or diesel engines paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Despite its utility, the NV3500 has faced various issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**5.6L V8 Gasoline**

* **Intake manifold runner valve failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, and illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain guide failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, reduced power, and illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and guides
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.7L V6 Diesel**

* **Fuel injector failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, and illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Harsh shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or delayed gear engagement
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid and filter or repair transmission valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf spring breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride quality, sagging, and potential loss of control
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+ per side
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall 21V610; Nissan Recall NPR-DR155 (for vehicles manufactured before January 29, 2021)

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle failing to start or requiring frequent jump-starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, including alternators, batteries, and wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rusty brake rotors:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible rust on brake rotors, potentially causing excessive noise and reduced braking performance
    * Solution: Replace brake rotors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per rotor
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power window failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows failing to operate or moving slowly
    * Solution: Replace window motors or switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per window
    * Recalls: None Found
* **AC compressor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lack of cold air, insufficient cooling, or illuminated AC light
    * Solution: Replace AC compressor or related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Nissan NV3500 has faced several issues that can affect its performance, reliability, and overall ownership experience. While some concerns are relatively inexpensive to repair, others can be costly and require extensive repairs. The mentioned issues highlight the importance of thorough research and vehicle maintenance to minimize potential problems. Buyers should consider the severity and cost of these issues when making a purchasing decision.
