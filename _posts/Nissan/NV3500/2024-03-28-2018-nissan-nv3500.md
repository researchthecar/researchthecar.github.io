**Introduction:**

The 2018 Nissan NV3500 is a full-size commercial van available in multiple configurations. It offers a choice of two engines and one transmission option:

* 4.0L V6 (271 hp, 298 lb-ft) mated to a 5-speed automatic transmission
* 5.6L V8 (375 hp, 394 lb-ft) mated to a 7-speed automatic transmission.

The NV3500 is praised for its spacious cargo area, powerful engines, and versatility. However, it has also received some criticisms for its outdated interior, high fuel consumption, and limited safety features.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

* **Timing chain failure:** Premature wear of the timing chain can lead to engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain, tensioner, and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake manifold gasket leak:** Failure of the intake manifold gasket can cause coolant or oil leaks.
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket and related seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.6L V8**

* **Valve train noise:** Tappet noise or lifter noise may indicate issues with the valve train components.
    * Solution: Replace affected lifters, pushrods, or other valve train components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems:** Hesitation, slipping, or delayed shifts may indicate transmission issues.
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Overheating:** Excessive transmission fluid temperature can lead to transmission damage.
    * Solution: Check fluid level and condition, repair cooling system if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Harsh shifting:** Rough or abrupt shifting may indicate mechanical issues within the transmission.
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Leaking transmission fluid:** Leaks can occur from various seals or gaskets.
    * Solution: Replace leaking seals or gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise:** Worn or damaged components in the front suspension can create clunking or squeaking noises.
    * Solution: Replace or repair affected components, such as ball joints or sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension sag:** Overloaded vehicles can cause excessive suspension sag, impacting handling.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace suspension components as needed, consider upgrading to heavy-duty springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Parasitic drain on the battery can lead to premature failure.
    * Solution: Check for electrical faults, faulty components, or aftermarket accessories that draw power.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure:** Malfunctioning alternators can prevent proper charging of the battery.
    * Solution: Replace alternator and check electrical system for other issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door handle failure:** Faulty door handles may become difficult to open or remain stuck.
    * Solution: Replace door handles as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sliding door issues:** Sticking or malfunctioning sliding doors can be frustrating.
    * Solution: Inspect and adjust track system, lubricate moving parts, or replace door assembly if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Poor air conditioning performance:** Ineffective air conditioning may indicate a refrigerant leak or faulty compressor.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace air conditioning components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center console vibration:** Excessive vibration or rattling from the center console can be annoying.
    * Solution: Inspect and tighten loose components or replace console components if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Nissan NV3500 faces a range of issues, varying in severity and cost. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair but are not widespread. Suspension and braking systems appear to be relatively reliable. Electrical issues and exterior problems are minor inconveniences but can add up. Interior issues, such as poor air conditioning performance, can be frustrating. Overall, the NV3500's issues are not overly concerning, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems.
