# 2024 Nissan ARIYA Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Nissan Ariya, buyers will find a stylish and modern all-electric crossover offering multiple battery and powertrain configurations. While most drivers enjoy its quiet ride and spacious interior, early owner reports submitted to the NHTSA highlight a few critical electrical and safety concerns to watch out for during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 63 kWh Standard Range Electric Motor

This single-motor setup delivers approximately 214 horsepower and powers the front wheels, providing efficient performance for daily commuting. It offers a balanced entry-level electric experience for city and suburban drivers.

### 87 kWh Extended Range Electric Motor

This higher-capacity single-motor configuration produces around 238 horsepower to the front wheels and prioritizes driving range. It serves as a popular option for buyers seeking longer travel distances between charges.

**EV System Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden, unexpected shutdowns of the electric vehicle system while driving at highway speeds, resulting in a complete loss of power and acceleration.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis by a certified dealership to inspect powertrain software, high-voltage architecture, and inverter modules.

**Sluggish Initial Propulsion**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden EV system failures at startup, leaving the vehicle moving sluggishly and unable to perform normally.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electrical and propulsion control modules scanned for diagnostic trouble codes by a qualified technician.

### e-4ORCE Dual-Motor AWD System

Pairing dual electric motors with an advanced all-wheel-drive system, this option generates up to 389 horsepower depending on the trim. It delivers confident handling in inclement weather while maintaining smooth electric acceleration.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed-Gear Transmission

This direct-drive single-speed transmission delivers seamless, gearless power transfer across all driving conditions. It requires very little maintenance and contributes to the Ariya's refined driving dynamics.

## Electrical

### 12V Battery and Charging System

Electrical complaints center around auxiliary 12V battery failures and charging communication errors.

**12V Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that the 12-volt battery can prematurely fail to hold a charge, completely disabling the vehicle from starting and functioning despite a high-voltage main pack.
- **Solution:** Test the 12V battery health and replace it if it fails to hold a proper charge.

**Charging Session Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced issues where public or home chargers fail to stop charging on command, leaving the vehicle temporarily immobilized during charging sessions.
- **Solution:** Inspect the vehicle's charge port lock actuator and update charging software through a dealership if necessary.

## Safety

### Backup Camera and Seat Belts

Safety system reports highlight occasional glitches with driver-assist visibility and passenger restraint hardware.

**Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display screen remains black when shifting into reverse, rendering the rearview camera completely inoperable.
- **Solution:** Check camera wiring harnesses, software settings, or replace the camera assembly if hardware failure is confirmed.

**Stuck Seat Belt Buckle**
- **Symptoms:** Rear seat occupants have become trapped when the seat belt buckle fails to release upon pressing the release button.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the malfunctioning seat belt buckle assembly.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Nissan Ariya presents a comfortable and high-tech cabin with competitive electric driving ranges, but used-car buyers should review vehicle history and electrical health closely. Given early owner complaints regarding unexpected EV system shutdowns and auxiliary battery draining, ensure any prospective purchase undergoes a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on software updates and battery condition.
