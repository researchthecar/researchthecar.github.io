- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Starter Motor Issues**

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Nissan Tsuru demonstrates a strong track record for reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The 1.6L GA16DE engine and its associated manual and automatic transmissions are generally trouble-free. Suspension and exterior/interior components also tend to hold up well. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically with the alternator and starter motor, which can require replacement over time. These are not uncommon failures for vehicles of this age and type. Overall, the Tsuru remains a dependable option, with most potential issues being manageable and relatively affordable to repair.
