**Introduction**

The 1998 Nissan Tsuru is a compact sedan that was popular in Mexico. It is available with a 1.6-liter GA16DE engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 1.8-liter SR18DE engine paired with either a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed manual transmission. The Tsuru is praised for its comfortable ride, fuel efficiency, and reliability. However, there are some potential issues to be aware of.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter GA16DE**

* **Oil leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks can occur from the valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket, or rear main seal. Symptoms include oil dripping or pooling under the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking gasket or seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Timing chain noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling or ticking noise coming from the engine may indicate a stretched or broken timing chain. This can lead to engine damage if not repaired.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**1.8-liter SR18DE**

* **No issues found**

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual**

* **Clutch problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch may slip or chatter when shifting gears. This can make it difficult to drive the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch disc, pressure plate, and release bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+

**4-speed automatic**

* **Slipping gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip out of gear when driving, causing the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut mounts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn strut mounts can cause a clunking noise when driving over bumps. This can also lead to alignment problems.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Electrical**

* **Alternator problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail to charge the battery, leading to a loss of power and a dead battery. Symptoms include dimming headlights and difficulty starting the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
* **Starter problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The starter may fail to engage the flywheel, preventing the engine from starting. Symptoms include a clicking sound when trying to start the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: The body of the Tsuru can rust prematurely, especially in areas with high humidity or road salt.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the rusted panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of the damage

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard of the Tsuru can develop cracks over time, especially in hot climates.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

The 1998 Nissan Tsuru is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its potential issues. The most common problems are related to the engine (oil leaks and timing chain noise), transmission (slipping gears), and suspension (strut mounts). The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the age and condition of the vehicle.
