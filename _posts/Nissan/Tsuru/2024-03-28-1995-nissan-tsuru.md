**Introduction to the 1995 Nissan Tsuru**

The 1995 Nissan Tsuru is a compact sedan available with three engine options: a 1.6-liter GA16DE I4, a 1.8-liter SR18DE I4, and a 2.0-liter SR20DE I4. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic. The Tsuru was praised for its affordability, reliability, and spacious interior, but it faced some criticism for its lack of power and outdated design.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter GA16DE I4**

* **Head Gasket Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
>* Solution: Replace head gasket
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1500
>* Recalls: None Found

* **Oil Sludge Buildup**
>* Description/Symptoms: Reduced oil pressure, knocking noises, engine failure
>* Solution: Clean or replace oil passages, replace oil pump
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$1000+
>* Recalls: None Found

**1.8-liter SR18DE I4**

* **Timing Chain Guide Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises from engine, engine stalling, loss of power
>* Solution: Replace timing chain guide
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1000+
>* Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter SR20DE I4**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, leaking fluid
>* Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$800
>* Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
>* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed engagement, loss of power
>* Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000-$3000+
>* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, reduced stability, poor handling
>* Solution: Replace strut mounts
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500+
>* Recalls: None Found

* **Sway Bar Link Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Knocking noises, reduced stability during cornering
>* Solution: Replace sway bar links
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300
>* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, difficulty starting car, electrical system malfunctions
>* Solution: Replace alternator
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$600
>* Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting car, grinding noises
>* Solution: Replace starter
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500
>* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking/unlocking doors
>* Solution: Replace door lock assembly
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150-$300
>* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Adjuster Failure**
>* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty adjusting seat position
>* Solution: Repair or replace seat adjuster mechanism
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$250
>* Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Cracking**
>* Description/Symptoms: Cracks developing on dashboard
>* Solution: Replace dashboard
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1500+
>* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1995 Nissan Tsuru has a few known issues, but most are relatively inexpensive to fix. The most common issues include head gasket failure, clutch master cylinder failure, and transmission slipping. The Tsuru's reliability, affordability, and spacious interior make it a solid choice for budget-minded consumers.
