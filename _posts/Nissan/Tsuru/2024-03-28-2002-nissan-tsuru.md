**Introduction:**

The 2002 Nissan Tsuru is a subcompact sedan known for its affordability and reliability. It is powered by a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a four-speed automatic transmission. The Tsuru has received generally positive reviews for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and low maintenance costs. However, it has also been criticized for its outdated design, lack of modern features, and below-average fuel economy.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idling, increased emissions
  * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or ticking noises, loss of power
  * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Four-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission struggles to shift gears, delayed engagement, loss of power
  * Solution: Flush transmission fluid, replace transmission filter, or rebuild transmission
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing, rattling noises, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace front struts
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, excessive body roll, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, dead battery, battery warning light
  * Solution: Replace alternator
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the vehicle, clicking noises
  * Solution: Replace starter motor
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Surface rust on body panels, fenders, or doors
  * Solution: Sand down rust, apply rust treatment, or replace affected panels
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust temperature, blower fan inoperable
  * Solution: Replace climate control module
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Window Regulator Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Windows inoperable, difficulty raising or lowering windows
  * Solution: Replace window regulator motor
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Nissan Tsuru is a reliable and affordable vehicle, but it can experience some common issues. The most significant problems to watch for include intake manifold gasket failure, timing chain tensioner failure, and transmission slipping. While some of these issues can be costly to repair, the Tsuru generally has low maintenance costs and is still a good value for those seeking a budget-friendly sedan.
