The 2015 Nissan Tsuru is a sedan primarily sold in Mexico. It's known for its simplicity, affordability, and reliability, making it a popular choice for taxi fleets and budget-conscious buyers. It came equipped with a 1.6-liter inline-4 gasoline engine producing around 105 horsepower and 102 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. Drivers liked its low running costs, ease of maintenance, and durability. On the other hand, it lacked modern safety features, and its basic design and limited equipment were points of criticism.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4**

*   **Catalytic Converter Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The catalytic converter can fail prematurely, leading to poor fuel economy, a check engine light, and potentially failing emissions tests. This is often caused by overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace the catalytic converter.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oxygen Sensor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The oxygen sensor fails, causing the vehicle to run rich or lean. This leads to poor fuel economy and a check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replace the oxygen sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

*   **Clutch Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The clutch wears out prematurely. Symptoms include slipping clutch, difficulty shifting, and unusual noises when pressing the clutch pedal.
    *   Solution: Replace the clutch disc, pressure plate, and throw-out bearing.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Worn Shocks and Struts**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride quality, excessive bouncing, and poor handling. This is especially noticeable on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Replace the shocks and struts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ (depending on parts and labor)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Worn Bushings**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, especially when going over bumps.
    *   Solution: Replace the worn bushings (control arm bushings, sway bar bushings, etc.)
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+ (depending on which bushings need replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Alternator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery warning light on the dashboard, difficulty starting the engine, or a completely dead battery.
    *   Solution: Replace the alternator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Starter Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clicking noise when trying to start the engine, or the engine failing to crank.
    *   Solution: Replace the starter motor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rust formation is most common around the wheel wells, door sills, and undercarriage, especially in areas with high humidity or road salt usage.
    *   Solution: Repairing the rust damage and repainting the affected areas. Preventative rust proofing.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+ (depending on the severity)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking on the dashboard due to prolonged exposure to sunlight and heat.
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+ (depending on severity and method)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Nissan Tsuru is a basic, no-frills vehicle with common issues related to wear and tear due to its intended use as a budget-friendly car. The most concerning issues relate to engine and transmission components that may require replacement over time. Rust is also a factor, depending on the environment. The cost of repairs is generally affordable, reflecting the vehicle's simplicity and availability of parts. However, the lack of modern safety features and limited comfort should be taken into account when considering this vehicle.
