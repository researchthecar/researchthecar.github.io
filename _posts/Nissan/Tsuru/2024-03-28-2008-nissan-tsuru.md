**Introduction:**

The 2008 Nissan Tsuru is a subcompact sedan that was available with two engine options: a 1.6-liter 4-cylinder and a 1.8-liter 4-cylinder. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic. The Tsuru was praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and reliability, but it was also criticized for its lack of power and outdated design.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter 4-cylinder**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine stalls, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.8-liter 4-cylinder**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Symptoms: Oil leaks from valve cover
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Noisy engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Synchronizer Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Symptoms: Low transmission fluid levels, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid, repair leak
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Erratic shifting, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts:**
    * Symptoms: Noise, vibration while driving
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per axle
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Sagging rear end, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace rear leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per axle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine won't start
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tail Light Assembly Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Broken or burned out tail light
    * Solution: Replace tail light assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Symptoms: Cracks or warping on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioning Compressor Failure:**
    * Symptoms: No cold air from AC
    * Solution: Replace AC compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2008 Nissan Tsuru is a reliable and affordable vehicle. However, it does have some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most common issues include intake manifold gasket failure, crankshaft position sensor failure, valve cover gasket leak, and timing chain tensioner failure. Most of the issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. However, the timing chain tensioner failure can be a major repair that costs over $1000.
