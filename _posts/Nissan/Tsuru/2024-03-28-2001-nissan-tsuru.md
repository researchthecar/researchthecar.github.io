**Introduction:**

The 2001 Nissan Tsuru is a compact sedan known for its reliability and affordability. It is powered by a 1.6-liter GA16DE engine paired with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The GA16DE engine produces 105 horsepower and 105 lb-ft of torque. The Tsuru received positive reviews for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and budget-friendly price. However, it has some common issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter GA16DE Engine:**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, rough idling, decreased engine power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced power, misfires
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission:**

* **Synchronizer Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second or third gear
    * Solution: Replace synchronizer rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission:**

* **Solenoid Pack Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or refusing to shift gears
    * Solution: Replace solenoid pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking sound from the front suspension, rough ride
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sway Bar End Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noise from the front or rear suspension, unstable handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar end link
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminates, dimming lights, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks but does not start, grinding noise during starting
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or more tail lights do not illuminate properly
    * Solution: Replace tail light assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heater does not work properly, noisier-than-expected operation
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door lock does not work properly, door cannot be opened or closed
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2001 Nissan Tsuru is a reliable and affordable compact sedan with a few common issues. Engine and transmission issues can be a concern, particularly with the 1.6-liter GA16DE engine and 5-speed manual transmission. Suspension components, electrical components, and interior features can also experience problems. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific issue, but many repairs can be completed for a reasonable price. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a used 2001 Nissan Tsuru to make informed decisions about maintenance and repairs.
