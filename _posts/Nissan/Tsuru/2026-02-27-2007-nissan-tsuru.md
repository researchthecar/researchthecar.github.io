### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Nissan Tsuru stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain. The 1.6L engine and both manual and automatic transmissions are known for their durability and lack of common failures. Suspension and exterior/interior components also generally hold up well. The most frequently cited issues tend to be in the electrical system, specifically with the alternator and power window motors, which are relatively common for vehicles of this age and class. Overall, the Tsuru is a dependable choice with minimal widespread mechanical concerns, making it a practical and economical option for budget-conscious buyers.
