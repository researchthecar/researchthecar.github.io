**Introduction**

The 1996 Nissan Tsuru is a compact sedan known for its affordability, reliability, and fuel efficiency. It was available with various engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine:**
    * 1.6L Inline-4
    * 1.8L Inline-4
* **Transmission:**
    * 5-Speed Manual
    * 4-Speed Automatic

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning excessive amounts of oil, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacing worn piston rings or valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise, loss of power, or complete engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.8L Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, engine overheating, or rough idle.
    * Solution: Replacing the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, engine overheating, or white smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replacing the cylinder head gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, or loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacing the clutch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Synchronizer Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second or third gear.
    * Solution: Replacing worn synchronizers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, shuddering, or loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacing the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shifting issues, incorrect gear selection, or illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacing the TCM.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive body lean, uneven tire wear, or vibration.
    * Solution: Replacing worn suspension components (e.g., ball joints, tie rods, bushings).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sits lower than the front, reduced rear axle ground clearance.
    * Solution: Replacing or adjusting rear suspension springs or shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering lights, or dim headlights.
    * Solution: Replacing the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Draining:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery constantly going dead, even with a new battery.
    * Solution: Identifying and fixing parasitic draw (e.g., faulty wiring, accessories left on).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Wiring Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical malfunctions, such as lighting problems, power window failures, or engine performance issues.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing faulty wiring connections or harnesses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water entering the cabin through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replacing sunroof seals or adjusting sunroof alignment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, or doors unlocking unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Replacing door lock actuators or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty adjusting or maintaining desired temperature, AC not blowing cold air.
    * Solution: Recharging air conditioning system, replacing blend door actuator or other climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Adjustments Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty adjusting seats, seats not moving smoothly.
    * Solution: Replacing seat adjustment motors or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1996 Nissan Tsuru is a reliable vehicle with relatively few common issues. The most severe and costly issues involve engine and transmission component failures, such as timing chain failure or torque converter failure. However, many of these issues can be prevented through proper maintenance and care.
