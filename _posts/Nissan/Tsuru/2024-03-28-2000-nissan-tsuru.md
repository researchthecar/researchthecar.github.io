**Introduction to the 2000 Nissan Tsuru**

The 2000 Nissan Tsuru is a compact sedan that was introduced in 1991. It is powered by a range of gasoline engines, including a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 105 horsepower and 105 lb-ft of torque. The Tsuru is available with a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **1.6-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Oil consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption can lead to low oil levels, which can damage the engine. Symptoms include blue smoke from the exhaust, low oil pressure, and frequent oil changes.
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings and valve stem seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Grinding gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, particularly when shifting into reverse.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission synchronizers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Slipping gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage or disengage gears, resulting in loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission clutches or gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension**
    * **Strut mounts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise over bumps, especially when turning.
        * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator**
    * **Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, low battery power, warning lights on dashboard.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear bumper**
    * **Cracking or fading**
        * Description/Symptoms: Fine lines or cracks appear on the bumper, fading of color
        * Solution: Replace the rear bumper.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
    * **Air conditioning vent breakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vents become stuck or fall apart when adjusting
        * Solution: Replace air conditioning vents
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2000 Nissan Tsuru is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues that can be expensive to fix. These include oil consumption, transmission problems, and electrical failures. It is important to consider these potential issues before purchasing a used Tsuru.
