**Introduction to the 2015 Nissan Tsuru**

The 2015 Nissan Tsuru is a subcompact sedan that was popular in Mexico. It was available with a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine paired with either a five-speed manual or a four-speed automatic transmission. The Tsuru was known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and reliability. However, it was also criticized for its outdated design and lack of safety features.

**Engine**

* **1.6-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

    * **Engine Stalling**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine suddenly shuts off while driving or idling.
        * Solution: Replace the ignition switch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks around the valve cover gasket or rear main seal.
        * Solution: Replace the leaking gasket or seal.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

    * **Difficulty Shifting Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission is difficult to shift into gear, especially when cold.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid or clutch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Four-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Slipping Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips gears while driving, resulting in a loss of power.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**

    * **Strut Mounts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn strut mounts cause excessive noise and vibration over bumps.
        * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly even when the car is not running.
        * Solution: Replace the battery or identify any electrical components that are draining it.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Wiring Harness**
    * **Electrical Shorts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical shorts can cause a variety of issues, such as blown fuses, flickering lights, or even engine problems.
        * Solution: Inspect and repair the wiring harness.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**
    * **Dim Headlights**
        * Description/Symptoms: Headlights are not bright enough, especially at night.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs or adjust the headlights.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Body Panels**
    * **Rust**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rust can develop on the body panels, especially around the wheel wells.
        * Solution: Repair the rusted areas or replace the body panels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
    * **Cracked Dashboard**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard cracks over time, especially when exposed to direct sunlight.
        * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioning**
    * **Inoperative Air Conditioning**
        * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning system does not blow cold air.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the air conditioning compressor, condenser, or other components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Nissan Tsuru is an affordable and reliable subcompact sedan that has been popular in Mexico. However, it has a number of potential issues, including engine stalling, oil leaks, transmission slippage, electrical shorts, and rust. These issues can be costly to repair, especially in the case of a major component failure. Overall, the Tsuru is a fairly reliable vehicle but it is important to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing one.
