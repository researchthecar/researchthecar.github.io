**Introduction**
The 1993 Nissan 240SX is a rear-wheel-drive sports car available in two engine and transmission combinations:

* **Engine:** 2.4-liter inline-four, 155 horsepower and 160 lb-ft of torque
* **Transmission:** 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic

**Engine**

**2.4-liter Inline-Four**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking or rattling, reduced power output
Solution: Replace tensioner
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Synchronizer Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially while cold
Solution: Replace worn synchronizers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Valve Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or refusing to shift
Solution: Replace solenoid valve
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, vibration, or bumpiness while driving
Solution: Replace strut mounts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, sluggish performance
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine will not crank
Solution: Replace starter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels**
Description/Symptoms: Discoloration and perforation in metal body panels
Solution: Rust prevention or repair
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Door locks not functioning properly
Solution: Replace actuator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 1993 Nissan 240SX is known for its reliability and affordability, but it is not without its issues. The most common issues are minor electrical problems and suspension wear. However, more serious issues like engine timing chain tensioner or transmission synchronizer failures can occur, leading to costly repairs. Overall, the 240SX is a well-built car that can provide years of enjoyable driving with proper maintenance and repairs.
