**2017 Nissan NP300 Frontier**

The 2017 Nissan NP300 Frontier is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 2.5-liter four-cylinder that produces 152 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque. A 4.0-liter V6 is also available, which generates 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed manual and a seven-speed automatic. The NP300 Frontier has a payload capacity of up to 1,610 pounds and a towing capacity of up to 6,720 pounds.

Drivers appreciate the Frontier's rugged design, off-road capabilities, and spacious cabin. However, some common issues have been reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**
* **2.5-liter Four-Cylinder**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low engine oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine rebuild.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * **Timing Chain Rattle:** Noisy timing chain, especially during cold starts.
        * Solution: Timing chain replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
* **4.0-liter V6**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Leaking intake manifold gaskets, causing loss of power, rough idling, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Intake manifold gasket replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $900+
    * **Exhaust Manifold Bolt Failure:** Broken or loose exhaust manifold bolts, leading to exhaust leaks and decreased engine performance.
        * Solution: Exhaust manifold bolt replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Transmission**
* **Six-Speed Manual**
    * **Grinding Gears:** Difficulty shifting gears smoothly, especially when shifting into reverse.
        * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
* **Seven-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping:** Loss of power or delayed shifting due to transmission slipping.
        * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or transmission rebuild.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Suspension**
* **Leaf Spring Sagging:** Premature sagging of the rear leaf springs, leading to a lower ride height and decreased load-carrying capacity.
        * Solution: Leaf spring replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** Excessive battery drain, leading to a dead battery after short periods of inactivity.
        * Solution: Electrical system diagnosis and repair.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
* **Alternator Failure:** Worn-out alternator, unable to properly charge the battery.
        * Solution: Alternator replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+

**Exterior**
* **Rust Prone Frame:** Premature rusting of the frame, especially in areas exposed to salt or moisture.
        * Solution: Frame repair or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+

**Interior**
* **HVAC System Malfunction:** Faulty HVAC system, causing insufficient heating or cooling.
        * Solution: HVAC system diagnosis and repair.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200+
* **Center Console Lid Failure:** Broken or misaligned center console lid, affecting access to storage compartments.
        * Solution: Center console lid replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary**

The 2017 Nissan NP300 Frontier has a number of potential issues that can affect its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior systems. Some of these issues, such as oil consumption and excessive rust, can be severe and require costly repairs. However, other issues, such as minor electrical problems and interior inconveniences, are less serious and typically have lower repair costs. Overall, the NP300 Frontier has a mixed reliability record, with some owners experiencing significant problems while others enjoy relatively trouble-free ownership.
