**Introduction**

The 2019 Nissan NP300 Frontier is a mid-size pickup truck that has been on the market since 2004. It is powered by a 4.0-liter V6 engine that produces 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a 6-speed manual or 7-speed automatic transmission. The Frontier is available in three trim levels: S, SV, and SL.

People like the Frontier for its affordability, reliability, and off-road capabilities. However, some people have complained about its lack of features, poor fuel economy, and cramped interior.
