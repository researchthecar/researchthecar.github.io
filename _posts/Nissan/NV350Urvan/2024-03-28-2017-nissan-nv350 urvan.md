**Introduction:**

The 2017 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a full-size van offered in both passenger and cargo configurations. It features a powerful 5.6L V8 engine that produces 317 horsepower and 394 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 5-speed automatic transmission. The NV350 Urvan is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **5.6L V8**
    * **Leaking valve cover gaskets**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leakage from the valve covers, causing a burning smell and possible engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Faulty ignition coils**
        * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idling, and reduced engine power.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty ignition coils.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $150+ per coil
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Automatic**
    * **Hard shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears smoothly, especially from 1st to 2nd gear.
        * Solution: Replace the worn or damaged transmission components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Slipping gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips out of gear, causing a loss of power.
        * Solution: Overhaul or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty alternator**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging properly, dim headlights, and flickering dashboard lights.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on the undercarriage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust on the frame, suspension components, and exhaust system.
        * Solution: Treat and prevent rust with anti-rust coatings and regular maintenance.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of damage.
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty AC compressor**
        * Description/Symptoms: Weak or no airflow from the air conditioning system.
        * Solution: Replace the AC compressor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Power window failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Power windows getting stuck or operating erratically.
        * Solution: Replace the window motor or switches.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $350+ per window
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2017 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a capable and reliable van, but some owners have reported experiencing issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the extent of the problem. Regular maintenance and proper repairs are essential to minimize the risk of encountering these issues and ensure the van's longevity.
