# 2022 Nissan NV350 Urvan

### Overview
The 2022 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a commercial van designed for utility and reliability in demanding work environments. It typically features robust mechanical components aimed at longevity and ease of maintenance. While generally considered dependable, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard wear and tear, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and fleet managers. The NV350 Urvan is known for its practical design and straightforward engineering, contributing to its reputation as a workhorse vehicle. Understanding these potential issues can aid in proactive maintenance and cost management.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L QR25DE (Gasoline)
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


### 2.5L YD25DDTi (Diesel)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, black smoke from the exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, engine misfires, increased fuel consumption, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per injector


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200


### 5-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid under the vehicle, delayed shifting, overheating transmission.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets, fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Suspension

### Standard Leaf Spring Rear / Independent Front
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening doors from the inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door latch or actuator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, intermittent fan speed, unusual noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Nissan NV350 Urvan demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, particularly for its intended commercial use. The diesel engine option (YD25DDTi) shows potential for issues with the turbocharger and injectors, which are common concerns for turbocharged diesel engines and can be costly to repair. The manual transmission's clutch is a wear item, and the automatic transmission may experience fluid leaks. Suspension bushings and the alternator are other areas where owners might encounter problems. Exterior door mechanisms and interior HVAC blower motors are also noted as potential failure points. Overall, with diligent maintenance, especially concerning the diesel engine's fuel system and turbo, the NV350 Urvan should provide dependable service.
