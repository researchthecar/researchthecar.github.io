**Introduction:**

The 2021 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a versatile van offered in a range of configurations. It boasts a spacious interior, robust engine, and advanced safety features. While the vehicle offers many desirable qualities, it has also faced several specific issues according to owner reports.

**Engine**

* **2.5L Diesel Engine:**
    * **Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idling, reduced performance
        * Solution: Replace injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * **EGR Valve Malfunction:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Poor acceleration, higher-than-normal emissions
        * Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
* **3.5L Gasoline Engine:**
    * **Timing Chain Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on startup, reduced engine power
        * Solution: Replace timing chain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,000+

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shift Delay:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay during gear changes
        * Solution: Update transmission software
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * **Torque Converter Overheating:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorbers:**
    * **Premature Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, reduced handling
        * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, loss of electrical power
    * Solution: Replace or charge battery, check for electrical leaks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery fails to charge, electrical system issues
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors fail to lock or unlock electronically
    * Solution: Repair or replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy rattling from dashboard area
    * Solution: Apply foam or insulation to affected area
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Recalls:**

* None Found for 2021 Nissan NV350 Urvan

**Summary:**

The 2021 Nissan NV350 Urvan has faced several specific issues, including injector failure, EGR valve malfunction, timing chain failure, and suspension issues. The cost of repairs for these issues can range from minor to substantial, affecting the overall reliability of the vehicle. However, it's important to note that not all owners experience these problems, and the NV350 Urvan remains a popular choice for those in need of a spacious and versatile van.
