# 2015 Nissan NV350 Urvan

### Overview
The 2015 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a commercial van designed for utility and reliability, often used for cargo transport and passenger shuttling. Its robust construction and straightforward mechanicals generally contribute to a positive ownership experience. While specific widespread mechanical failures are not commonly reported for this model year, like any vehicle, individual components can experience issues over time. ### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Nissan NV350 Urvan generally exhibits good technical reliability, particularly with its petrol engine option. The diesel engine, while more powerful, has a few documented potential issues such as turbocharger and glow plug problems, which are not uncommon for diesel powertrains. Transmission reliability is decent, with the manual experiencing typical clutch wear. Electrical issues like battery drain are also noted but are often resolvable. Overall, the NV350 Urvan is considered a dependable workhorse, with most reported issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
