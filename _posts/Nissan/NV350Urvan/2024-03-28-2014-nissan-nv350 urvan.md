**Introduction to the 2014 Nissan NV350 Urvan**

The 2014 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a full-size van available in both passenger and cargo configurations. It features a 5.6-liter V8 engine producing 317 horsepower and 385 pound-feet of torque, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The NV350 Urvan has a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and a powerful engine. However, it also has some notable drawbacks, including poor fuel economy and limited safety features.

**Engine**

* **5.6L V8**
  * **Excessive oil consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, resulting in low oil levels and potentially leading to engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Timing chain issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain can stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or stall.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Automatic**
  * **Transmission slipping or shuddering**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips or shudders during gear changes, indicating a problem with the transmission fluid, solenoids, or other internal components.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front suspension makes a rattling or clunking noise when going over bumps or uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged suspension components, such as ball joints, bushings, or struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension springs sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension springs sag excessively, causing the vehicle to sit lower at the rear and affecting handling and stability.
    * Solution: Replace the rear suspension springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery drains excessively, even when the ignition is turned off.
    * Solution: Check for parasitic drains or a faulty alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's electrical system experiences intermittent or complete failure, affecting various electrical components such as lights, gauges, or the radio.
    * Solution: Troubleshoot the electrical system and replace any faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the affected components
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks malfunction, failing to open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuators or repair the associated electrical wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust on body panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: Surface rust appears on metal body panels, particularly in areas exposed to road salt or moisture.
    * Solution: Remove the rust and apply protective coatings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles or squeaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard develops rattles or squeaks over time, especially when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Identify and tighten loose components or apply sound-deadening material.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate control issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system fails to regulate temperature properly or blows cold air instead of hot air.
    * Solution: Inspect the climate control system for leaks, faulty sensors, or other issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the root cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Nissan NV350 Urvan has several common issues that can range in severity and cost. While the engine and transmission can experience major problems, the interior and exterior issues are generally less severe. Overall, the vehicle has a reputation for reliability but may require timely maintenance and repairs to address potential concerns.
