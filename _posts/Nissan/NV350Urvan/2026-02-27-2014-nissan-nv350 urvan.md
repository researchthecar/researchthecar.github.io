# 2014 Nissan NV350 Urvan

### Overview
The 2014 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a commercial van designed for utility and reliability, often used for passenger transport and cargo hauling. It is known for its robust construction and practical design, aiming to provide dependable service in demanding conditions. While generally considered a solid work vehicle, like any automotive product, it can experience specific issues that owners and fleet managers should be aware of. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated repair considerations for various configurations.

### 2.5L YD25DDTi (Diesel)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whining noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, engine misfires, increased fuel consumption, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,100


### 5-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid, rough shifting, overheating transmission.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets, fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Ball Joints and Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, poor handling, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints and suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Hinge Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Stiff or squeaky door operation, visible rust on hinges.
- **Solution:** Lubrication, rust treatment, or replacement of hinges.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Nissan NV350 Urvan demonstrates a generally reliable profile, particularly with its petrol engine option. The diesel variant, while offering better fuel economy, has shown some susceptibility to turbocharger and injector issues, which are not uncommon for diesel engines under heavy use. Transmission reliability is fair, with manual clutch wear being a typical concern for high-mileage vehicles, and automatic transmissions occasionally experiencing fluid leaks. Suspension components like ball joints and bushings can wear over time, leading to noise and handling degradation. Electrical issues are infrequent, with alternator failure being the most notable concern. Exterior and interior components are generally robust, though minor issues like door hinge rust can occur. Overall, the NV350 Urvan is a dependable workhorse, with most potential issues being manageable and addressable through timely maintenance and repair.
