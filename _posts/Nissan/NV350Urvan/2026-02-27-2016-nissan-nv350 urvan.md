# 2016 Nissan NV350 Urvan

### Overview
The 2016 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a commercial van designed for utility and cargo transport. Reliability is a key consideration for vehicles in this segment, and the NV350 Urvan generally performs well, focusing on durability for demanding work environments. While specific widespread mechanical failures are not commonly reported, like any vehicle, it can experience issues related to its powertrain, electrical systems, and components subjected to heavy use. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and fleet managers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Nissan NV350 Urvan demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, particularly for its intended commercial use. The gasoline engine option is robust with no significant widespread issues. The diesel engine, while more powerful, has a few documented potential concerns like turbocharger and glow plug failures, which are not uncommon for diesel powertrains. Transmission reliability is good, with manual clutches being a wear item. Suspension components, especially leaf springs, can require attention due to heavy loads. Electrical issues like alternator failure and battery drain are also noted. Overall, with proper maintenance, the NV350 Urvan is a dependable work vehicle.
