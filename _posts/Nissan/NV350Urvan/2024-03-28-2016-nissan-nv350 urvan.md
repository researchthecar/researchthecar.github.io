**Introduction:**
The 2016 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a full-size van that offers a spacious cargo area and a variety of engine and transmission options. It is powered by a 2.5-liter gasoline engine that produces 145 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 3.5-liter V6 that produces 261 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.6-liter V8 that produces 317 horsepower and 385 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 7-speed automatic transmission, a 5-speed manual transmission, and a 6-speed manual transmission. The NV350 Urvan is known for its reliability and durability, but there have been some reports of issues with certain components.

**Engine**
**2.5-Liter Gasoline Engine**
* **Timing chain failure:** The timing chain can prematurely stretch or break, resulting in engine damage.
  * Solution: Replace the timing chain and associated components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil consumption:** Some engines may consume excessive oil, leading to engine damage if not addressed.
  * Solution: Repair or replace the engine components responsible for oil consumption.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Slipping gears:** The transmission may slip out of gear while driving, resulting in a loss of power.
  * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front suspension creaking:** The front suspension may creak or groan when driving over uneven surfaces.
  * Solution: Lubricate the suspension components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Starter failure:** The starter may fail to engage the engine, preventing the vehicle from starting.
  * Solution: Replace the starter.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Door handles breaking:** The door handles may break or become loose, making it difficult to open the doors.
  * Solution: Replace the door handles.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Instrument cluster failure:** The instrument cluster may fail to display information, such as speed, fuel level, and odometer readings.
  * Solution: Replace the instrument cluster.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found
* **AC malfunction:** The air conditioning system may not effectively cool the cabin.
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair the AC system components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2016 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a generally reliable vehicle, but there are some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most serious issue is the timing chain failure, which can cause significant engine damage if not addressed. Other issues, such as transmission slippage and suspension creaking, are less severe but can still be costly to repair. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues associated with the NV350 Urvan are moderate, and most owners should be able to enjoy a reliable and long-lasting vehicle.
