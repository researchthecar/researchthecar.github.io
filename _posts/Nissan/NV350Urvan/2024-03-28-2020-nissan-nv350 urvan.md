**Introduction:**

The 2020 Nissan NV350 Urvan is a full-size van renowned for its spacious interior, versatility, and reliability. It is offered with a single engine and transmission option:

* 2.5L 4-cylinder gasoline engine generating 163 horsepower and 178 lb-ft of torque (paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission)

The NV350 Urvan has garnered praise for its roominess, comfortable seats, and smooth ride quality. However, some owners have reported certain issues with its major components.

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Electrical System**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to faulty door switches or an electrical parasitic draw.
* Solution: Replace defective door switches or diagnose and eliminate electrical sources causing the drain.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Doors**

* **Sliding Door Issue:** Owners have reported difficulties opening and closing the passenger-side sliding door due to binding or misalignment.
* Solution: Adjustment or replacement of door rollers, hinges, or tracks.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Windows**

* **Window Regulator Failure:** The window regulators may fail, causing the windows to become inoperable.
* Solution: Replacement of defective window regulators.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Instrument Panel**

* **Instrument Panel Illumination Issues:** Some users have experienced flickering or dimming of the instrument panel lights.
* Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty bulbs or electrical components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Nissan NV350 Urvan has received positive feedback for its reliability and practicality. However, it is not immune to occasional issues. The electrical system and exterior components have seen some reported problems, with battery drains and sliding door malfunctions being the most common. While these issues are not widespread, they can be inconvenient and require attention from a mechanic. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the severity of the issue and the need for part replacements.
