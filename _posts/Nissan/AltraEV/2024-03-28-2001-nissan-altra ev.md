## 2001 Nissan Altra EV

The 2001 Nissan Altra EV is an electric vehicle that was produced by Nissan from 2000 to 2002. The Altra EV was powered by a 66-kilowatt electric motor that produced 120 horsepower and 207 pound-feet of torque. The motor was powered by a 24-kilowatt-hour lithium-ion battery pack that gave the Altra EV a range of up to 100 miles on a single charge.

The Altra EV was praised for its smooth ride, comfortable interior, and low operating costs. However, the vehicle was also criticized for its limited range, slow acceleration, and high price.
