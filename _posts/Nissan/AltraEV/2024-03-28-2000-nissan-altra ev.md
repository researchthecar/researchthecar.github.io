**Introduction**

The 2000 Nissan Altra EV was a compact electric vehicle powered by a 45-kWh lithium-ion battery pack and an 80-kW electric motor. The Altra EV offered a range of up to 100 miles on a single charge. It was praised for its quiet operation, low running costs, and spacious interior. However, it also faced some issues that affected its reliability.

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Struts**
* Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the front suspension struts, leading to excessive noise, vibration, and reduced handling stability.
* Solution: Replacement of both front suspension struts.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
* Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 15V224000

**Rear Suspension**
* Description/Symptoms: Weak rear suspension springs, causing excessive body roll and instability during cornering.
* Solution: Reinforcement of the rear suspension springs.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Pack**
* Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery pack degradation, resulting in reduced range and performance.
* Solution: Replacement of the entire battery pack.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Charging System**
* Description/Symptoms: Faulty onboard charger, causing slow or intermittent charging.
* Solution: Replacement of the onboard charger.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlights**
* Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight assemblies, leading to reduced visibility at night.
* Solution: Replacement of the headlight assemblies.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per assembly
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**HVAC System**
* Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, causing inconsistent temperature regulation.
* Solution: Repair or replacement of the HVAC system components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2000 Nissan Altra EV faced several issues, particularly with its battery pack and suspension. These problems could significantly impact its reliability and ownership costs. The battery pack degradation issue is especially concerning, as it can render the vehicle unusable if not addressed promptly. However, the vehicle also received praise for its low running costs and spacious interior, making it a potentially appealing choice for those seeking an affordable and environmentally friendly driving experience.
