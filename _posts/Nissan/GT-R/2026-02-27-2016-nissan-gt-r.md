# 2016 Nissan GT-R Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Nissan GT-R is a high-performance supercar known for its blistering acceleration and advanced all-wheel-drive system. Powered by a hand-built twin-turbocharged V6 mated to a dual-clutch transmission, it delivers supercar performance that rivals much more expensive competitors. For used car buyers, finding a well-maintained example is crucial, as these vehicles are often driven hard and require meticulous maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V6 (VR38DETT)

This hand-built 3.8-liter twin-turbo V6 produces around 545 horsepower and 463 lb-ft of torque (higher in Nismo trims). It is generally robust and powerful, though high-performance driving requires strict adherence to maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (GR6)

The rear-mounted 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes. Buyers should inspect the transmission for smoothness and ensure fluid changes have been performed on schedule.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V795000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=GT-R&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." Due to the label missing the GVWR/GAWR information, the operator may overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will apply a corrected label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Nissan GT-R remains an incredible performance bargain for enthusiasts looking for supercar-level capabilities. While routine maintenance costs are high and pre-purchase inspections are vital, a well-cared-for model offers exhilarating driving dynamics and solid core mechanical reliability.
