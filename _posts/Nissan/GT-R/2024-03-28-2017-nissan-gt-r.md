**Introduction:**

The 2017 Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car known for its powerful engine, agile handling, and advanced technology. It is offered with a single engine option:

* 3.8L VR38DETT V6 engine, producing 565 horsepower and 467 lb-ft of torque
* Paired with a 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission

The GT-R receives praise for its impressive performance, but some owners have reported issues with various aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine:**

* **VR38DETT V6 Engine:**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** Failure of the turbochargers can result in loss of power, smoke from the engine bay, and increased oil consumption.
        * Solution: Replacement of the faulty turbochargers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil leaks:** Leaks can occur from various points, including the valve covers, oil pan, or turbocharger lines.
        * Solution: Identification and repair of the leak source
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Clutch pack failure:** Worn or damaged clutch packs can lead to slipping gears, difficulty shifting, and eventual transmission failure.
        * Solution: Replacement of the clutch pack
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission fluid leaks:** Leaks can occur from various seals or gaskets, resulting in loss of transmission fluid.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of leaking components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front suspension noise:** Noises, such as clunking or rattling, may be present during suspension travel.
    * Solution: Replacement of worn or damaged suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery drain:** Excessive battery drain may occur when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical faults causing the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure:** Failure of the alternator can result in insufficient power generation and battery drain.
    * Solution: Replacement of the faulty alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Clear coat peeling:** Peeling or cracking of the clear coat on the paint finish may occur.
    * Solution: Repainting or refinishing affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Infotainment system glitches:** Intermittent issues with the infotainment system, including freezing, crashing, or unresponsive touchscreens.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Nissan GT-R is known for its exceptional performance, but it has experienced some notable issues. Common engine problems include turbocharger failure and oil leaks, while the transmission may suffer from clutch pack failure or fluid leaks. Suspension noises and electrical issues have also been reported. The severity of these issues can vary, with some requiring significant repairs and others being relatively minor. While the GT-R is an impressive vehicle, potential owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider the estimated cost of repairs.
