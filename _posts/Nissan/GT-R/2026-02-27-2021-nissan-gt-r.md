# 2021 Nissan GT-R Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Nissan GT-R remains a high-performance supercar known for its formidable twin-turbocharged engine and advanced all-wheel-drive system. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating a model of this caliber requires looking past its track-ready capabilities to understand everyday driveability and maintenance demands. While factory support is specialized, understanding the core powertrain options helps ensure you select a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V6 (VR38DETT)

The standard VR38DETT 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces 565 horsepower and 467 lb-ft of torque, delivering explosive acceleration. It is hand-built by master technicians, requiring meticulous maintenance and premium fuel to ensure long-term durability.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V6 Nismo (VR38DETT)

The Nismo variant of the 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V6 ups output to 600 horsepower and 481 lb-ft of torque using GT3-derived turbochargers. This track-focused specification demands rigorous adherence to service intervals for optimal reliability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Transaxle

The rear-mounted 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transaxle provides lightning-fast gear changes and power distribution across the all-wheel-drive system. Buyers should expect specialized fluid changes and potential mechanical gear-clatter characteristics inherent to high-performance transaxles.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V402000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Spindle](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=GT-R&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The steering knuckle or rear axle could deform upon impact, such as from hitting a curb, resulting in a loss of steering control, or possible wheel separation. Both of these conditions can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front steering knuckle and rear axle housing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Nissan GT-R is an engineering marvel that offers supercar performance with everyday usability, provided it has been properly maintained. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive service histories and verify the condition of the powertrain. Always arrange for a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist before finalizing your purchase.
