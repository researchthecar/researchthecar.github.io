**Introduction:**

The 2012 Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car powered by a twin-turbocharged 3.8L V6 engine that produces 485 horsepower and 430 lb-ft of torque. It is known for its exceptional acceleration, handling, and overall performance. However, like any vehicle, it has had its share of issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **VR38DETT 3.8L V6**

  * **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the turbocharger, causing reduced engine power, excessive smoke, and unusual noises.
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

  * **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or slipping out of gear, resulting in loss of power and abnormal noises.
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Starter Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the starter motor, preventing the vehicle from starting.
    * Solution: Starter motor replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the alternator, leading to battery undercharging and electrical system problems.
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture collecting inside the headlight assemblies, causing fogging or condensation.
    * Solution: Replacing or resealing the headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Clear Coat Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Peeling of the clear coat on the exterior paint, particularly on the hood and roof.
    * Solution: Repainting the affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling noises coming from the dashboard.
    * Solution: Identifying and fixing any loose components or applying sound dampening material.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, leading to improper temperature or blowing settings.
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing the climate control module or actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Nissan GT-R is a high-performance sports car that has received praise for its performance and handling. However, it has experienced some issues, including turbocharger failures, transmission slipping, and various electrical and interior problems. While the severity and cost of these issues can vary, it is important for owners to be aware of them and factor them into their maintenance and ownership plans.
