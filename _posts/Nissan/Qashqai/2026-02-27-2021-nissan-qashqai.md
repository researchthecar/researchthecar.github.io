# 2021 Nissan Qashqai

### Overview
The 2021 Nissan Qashqai, a popular compact crossover, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting Nissan's efforts in building dependable vehicles. While no car is entirely immune to issues, the Qashqai for this model year has demonstrated good performance across its key systems. Owners typically report satisfaction with its day-to-day usability and the absence of widespread, recurring problems. However, as with any vehicle, specific components can occasionally experience faults, and it's prudent for owners to be aware of potential areas, particularly concerning the powertrain and certain electronic features. Regular maintenance remains crucial for ensuring long-term dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Nissan Qashqai is generally considered a reliable compact SUV. Most owners experience a trouble-free ownership period with no significant recurring faults. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted include occasional glitches with the infotainment system, such as screen unresponsiveness, and some reports of slight hesitations or jerking from the Xtronic CVT transmission, often rectifiable with software updates or fluid services. The petrol and diesel engines, along with the manual transmission, suspension, and interior components, have shown good durability. Overall, the Qashqai offers a dependable choice for its segment, provided routine maintenance is adhered to.
