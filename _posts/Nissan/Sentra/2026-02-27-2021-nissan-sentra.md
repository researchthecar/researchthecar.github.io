# 2021 Nissan Sentra Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Nissan Sentra is a stylish and fuel-efficient compact sedan that offers a comfortable cabin and modern driver-assistance features. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding advanced safety systems and steering components. While many drivers enjoy its daily commuting capability, verifying that all relevant safety recalls and mechanical inspections have been completed is strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing 149 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque, providing reliable and economical daily transportation.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped with Nissan's Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission routing power to the front wheels, prioritizing fuel economy for everyday commuting.

## Brakes

### Service and Parking Brakes

Brake system concerns involve sporadic warning lights, switch malfunctions, and unexpected brake engagement that warrant a thorough mechanical check.

**Unintended Brake Activation and Pedal Travel**
- **Symptoms:** The parking brake or emergency braking system independently activates while driving, or the brake pedal fails to bring the vehicle to a smooth stop.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the brake switch, anti-lock braking sensors, and parking brake actuator for faults.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Tie Rods

Steering system complaints center around unexpected stiffness and play in the steering gear, which require careful inspection during a used vehicle purchase.

**Steering Assist Failure and Play**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of power steering assist, stiff steering, or violent shaking and play in the steering rack at higher mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric steering rack, tie rods, and steering module for wear or mechanical failure, replacing affected components as needed.

## Electrical

### Battery and Door Locks

electrical issues such as intermittent battery drainage and unresponsive door locks are reported by a subset of owners.

**Battery Drain and Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle experiences unexpected battery drain overnight requiring jump starts, or door lock actuators and power windows fail to operate.
- **Solution:** Test the electrical charging system for parasitic draws and replace faulty door lock actuators or window switches.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety systems have generated owner complaints regarding false collision warnings and unexpected automatic braking events.

**Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system triggers sudden, unexpected stops while driving on open roads or highways without any obstacles present.
- **Solution:** Check forward collision sensors and update vehicle software modules according to manufacturer guidelines.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V461000: Steering:Linkages:Tie Rod Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Sentra&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A bent tie rod can break, causing a loss of steering control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the left and/or right tie rods as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V581000: Steering:Linkages:Tie Rod Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Sentra&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A bent tie rod can break and cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** As an interim repair, dealers will inspect and replace any bent or broken tie rods, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V628000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Sentra&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not properly display an image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rearview camera and harness for damage and replace them as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Nissan Sentra is a practical choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking modern styling and efficiency. However, because multiple owners have reported issues related to forward collision sensors, steering assist, and electrical components, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is essential. Buyers should verify that all open recalls have been addressed by a certified dealer before finalizing their purchase.
