# 2023 Nissan Sentra Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Nissan Sentra, buyers will find a modern compact sedan equipped with efficient powertrain options and updated tech features. While most owners enjoy a comfortable daily driver, a review of owner-reported feedback highlights a few recurring electrical, camera, and intermittent powertrain concerns that are worth noting during your inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2023 Nissan Sentra comes standard with a 2.0-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine producing 149 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque. Paired with front-wheel drive, it prioritizes fuel economy for everyday commuting, though buyers should watch for occasional reports of throttle response delays.

**Unexpected Stalling or Acceleration Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported occasional unexpected engine stalling while driving at moderate speeds or a noticeable hesitation and delay in acceleration after idling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the throttle body, fuel system, and electronic control unit for stored diagnostic trouble codes.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is sent to the front wheels via an Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) designed to maximize fuel efficiency. While generally smooth in daily operation, prospective buyers should thoroughly test the transmission's responsiveness during a test drive.

**Shifter Knob Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** The gear selector knob may pop off or loosen unexpectedly when shifting between gears.
- **Solution:** Inspect and secure or replace the shifter assembly components to ensure proper engagement.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Backup Camera

The electrical system manages the vehicle's advanced safety aids and cabin technology, though several owners have noted minor glitches involving camera displays and infotainment connectivity.

**Discolored Backup Camera Display**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera image may turn pink, purple, or fail entirely, making it difficult to see behind the vehicle while reversing.
- **Solution:** Inspect the camera harness and connections, or replace the backup camera unit if internal failure has occurred.

**Apple CarPlay Audio Static**
- **Symptoms:** A loud static noise occurs during phone calls and music playback when an iPhone is connected via Apple CarPlay, disrupting audio clarity.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates for both the smartphone and the vehicle's infotainment system, and inspect lightning or USB-C cables.

**Key Fob Low Battery Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard consistently displays a low battery warning for the key fob even after fresh batteries have been installed.
- **Solution:** Verify battery voltage, ensure correct fob battery orientation, and check the vehicle's receiver module if warnings persist.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Sentra&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing issue, the driver's air bag inflator may absorb moisture, and cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that deploys improperly may not protect the driver as intended during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front driver's air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Nissan Sentra is an attractive choice for used car buyers seeking solid fuel economy and modern styling. While owner complaints point to occasional backup camera discoloration, infotainment static, and minor powertrain hesitations, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure everything operates smoothly.
