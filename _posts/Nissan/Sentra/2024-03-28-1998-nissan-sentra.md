**Introduction:**

The 1998 Nissan Sentra is a compact sedan known for its reliability and fuel efficiency. It was available with two engine options: a 1.6-liter inline-4 and a 2.0-liter inline-4, both mated to either a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The 1.6-liter engine produces 115 horsepower and 108 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.0-liter engine generates 140 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque. The Sentra was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and low maintenance costs, but some owners reported minor issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Inline-4**

* **Oxygen Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check Engine Light illuminates, reduced fuel efficiency, engine stalls
    * Solution: Replace oxygen sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating engine
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Inline-4**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak, engine misfire
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shift Linkage Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace shift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, reduced ride comfort
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system issues
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Test and repair or replace components causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors do not lock or unlock properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per door
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Heater Core Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sweet smell inside vehicle, coolant leak
    * Solution: Replace heater core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $900+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows do not operate properly
    * Solution: Replace power window motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per window
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1998 Nissan Sentra is a reliable and affordable compact sedan. However, like any vehicle, it has specific issues that owners should be aware of. The most common engine issues include oxygen sensor failure and intake manifold gasket leaks. The transmission can experience shift linkage malfunctions and slippage, while the suspension may have strut mount failures. Electrical problems include alternator failure and battery drain. Exterior issues might involve door lock actuator failures, and the interior may have issues with the heater core and power window motors. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific parts affected and the extent of the damage. Regular maintenance and proper repairs can help minimize the likelihood of experiencing these problems and maintain the Sentra's performance and longevity.
