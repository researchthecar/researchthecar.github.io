**Introduction:**

The 2022 Nissan Sentra is a compact sedan that offers two engine options: a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine and a 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine. The 2.0-liter engine produces 149 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.6-liter turbocharged engine produces 191 horsepower and 177 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Sentra is praised for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and responsive handling, but some drivers complain about its lackluster acceleration and CVT transmission.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.6-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noises from engine bay
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT Transmission Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or slips when shifting gears
    * Solution: Reprogram or replace transmission control module (TCM)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from rear suspension
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix electrical components causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wipers stop working or move intermittently
    * Solution: Replace wiper motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes or responds slowly
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Nissan Sentra has some notable issues, but most are relatively minor and can be fixed for a moderate cost. The most severe issue is the turbocharger failure on the 1.6-liter turbocharged engine, which can be costly to repair. Overall, the Sentra is a reliable and affordable compact sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing.
