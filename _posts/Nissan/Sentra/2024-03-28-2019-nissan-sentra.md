**Interior**

* **Loose center console armrest**
    * Description/Symptoms: The center console armrest may become loose and rattle while driving.
    * Solution: Replace the armrest.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty door lock actuators**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door lock actuators may fail, causing the doors to lock or unlock randomly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Nissan Sentra has a relatively low number of reported issues, with the most common being the loose center console armrest and faulty door lock actuators. The cost of repairs for these issues is generally affordable.
