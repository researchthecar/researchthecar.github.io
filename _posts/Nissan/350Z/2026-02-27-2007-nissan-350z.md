# 2007 Nissan 350Z

### Overview
The 2007 Nissan 350Z, a popular sports car, is generally known for its robust mechanicals, particularly its V6 engine. However, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that owners and technicians have noted for potential issues. This analysis focuses on documented reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems, their symptoms, typical solutions, and estimated repair costs for various components and configurations. Understanding these potential pitfalls can help owners proactively address issues and maintain the performance and longevity of their 350Z.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35HR)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level checks required, blue smoke from exhaust under certain conditions.
- **Solution:** Engine rebuild or replacement, piston ring replacement.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $6,000+

**Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine Light, rough idle, rattling noise from the front of the engine, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of VVT actuators and potentially related timing components.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200

**Synchronizer Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild with replacement of synchronizer rings and other worn components.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000+


### 5-Speed Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement or transmission overhaul.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front control arm bushings or entire control arms.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**BCM (Body Control Module) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical gremlins, power window malfunctions, interior light issues, keyless entry problems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000+


## Exterior

### Standard Exterior
**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture or fogging inside headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Sealing headlight assemblies or replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500 (depending on solution)


## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Seat Heater Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both seat heaters not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of heating elements or wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Nissan 350Z is a mechanically sound sports car with a strong V6 engine. The most commonly reported issues revolve around the engine's oil consumption and VVT system, as well as potential wear and tear on the manual transmission's clutch and synchronizers, and automatic transmission's torque converter. Suspension components, particularly front bushings, can also require attention. Electrical gremlins, often linked to the BCM, and minor exterior and interior issues like headlight condensation and seat heater failures are less frequent but noted. Overall, with diligent maintenance and awareness of these potential problem areas, the 350Z remains a reliable and enjoyable performance vehicle.
