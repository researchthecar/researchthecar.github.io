# 2024 Nissan Altima Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Nissan Altima is a contemporary midsize sedan offering comfortable cabin appointments and efficient engine choices. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern safety tech and styling, but early adopter reports highlight a handful of noteworthy mechanical and electrical concerns. Prospective buyers should inspect powertrain components and safety systems carefully during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 182 horsepower, providing adequate daily driving power and solid fuel economy for commuters.

### 2.0L Variable Compression Turbo (VC-Turbo) 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers a more spirited 248 horsepower through innovative variable compression technology, offering enhanced passing performance.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The Xtronic CVT routes power to the front wheels (or all four wheels on select trims), prioritizing fuel efficiency with smooth simulated gear steps.

**Powertrain and Drive Shaft Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle makes a loud noise, shakes violently while driving at low speeds, and can become entirely undriveable.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect transmission housing bolts and drive shaft alignment; replace missing or sheared hardware and repair damaged surrounding components.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

The power steering system provides responsive handling, though physical feedback anomalies and stiffness have surfaced in some owner accounts.

**Steering Stiffness and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel exhibits intermittent vibration during highway driving or suddenly feels tight and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column, intermediate shaft, and wheel balance, and check for any applicable manufacturer service campaigns.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Exterior glass and body trim components generally offer solid fit and finish, though rare glass integrity issues have been documented.

**Spontaneous Rear Windshield Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Rear window glass shatters unexpectedly while parked or closing vehicle doors.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered rear glass and inspect surrounding window frame trim for alignment gaps or stress points.

## Interior

### Seat Heaters

Cabin electrical accessories manage comfort and infotainment functions, though isolated component failures have been reported.

**Excessive Heating Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Driver's seat warmer produces intense, uncontrollable heat at low settings, resulting in skin burns.
- **Solution:** Disconnect the seat heater immediately and have the heating element and control module inspected and replaced by a dealer.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Safety systems are designed to mitigate front-end collisions, though sensor calibration issues can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking.

**Erroneous Brake Activation**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes engage unexpectedly while driving at normal speeds, potentially causing the vehicle to slide or brake abruptly in wet conditions.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer or a specialized technician calibrate and inspect the forward collision sensors and braking module software.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V882000: Steering:Rack And Pinion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Altima&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control or steering lock-up can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Nissan Altima provides strong fuel economy and comfortable daily transportation, but buyers should remain vigilant during inspections. Particular attention should be paid to transmission housing hardware, steering response, and electrical accessories like seat warmers. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic is strongly recommended to catch any early mechanical or safety quirks before finalizing your purchase.
