# 2022 Nissan Altima Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Nissan Altima offers a comfortable ride, modern styling, and solid fuel economy, making it a popular choice in the midsize sedan market. However, used car buyers should carefully review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical anomalies, forward collision sensor sensitivity, and intermittent starting hurdles. While many drivers enjoy trouble-free commuting, thorough pre-purchase inspections are strongly advised to check electrical modules and driver-assistance systems before buying.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 188 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque. It provides dependable daily power and respectable fuel economy for standard commuting.

### 2.0L Variable Compression Turbo (VC-Turbo) Engine

The available 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine utilizes variable compression technology to deliver around 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It offers a punchy, responsive driving experience for buyers seeking extra performance.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The Xtronic CVT is paired across front-wheel-drive and available Intelligent All-Wheel-Drive trims. It prioritizes smooth power delivery and high fuel efficiency.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake components typically perform reliably under normal conditions, but proper post-service inspections are critical.

**Brake Responsiveness and Odors**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have occasionally noted unusual burning smells following brake services or delayed stopping response under specific circumstances.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, calipers, and hydraulic lines immediately if unusual odors or soft pedals occur.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Battery

Electrical complaints center around intermittent starting difficulties, dashboard warning illumination, and occasional power loss.

**Unexpected Stalling and Battery Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle unexpectedly loses power while driving or fails to start, accompanied by multiple dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the battery, charging system, and Body Control Module (BCM) connections for faults.

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Hardware

Cabin appointments are generally durable, but minor mechanical latch issues can occasionally affect door operation.

**Door Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors may fail to open from either the inside or outside despite successfully locking and unlocking.
- **Solution:** Inspect the interior door handle mechanisms and latch assembly, replacing faulty door cables or actuators as needed.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance System

Advanced safety systems generally protect occupants well, though a subset of owners experience overly sensitive collision avoidance sensors.

**Phantom Braking and Sensor Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system may trigger sudden, unexpected stops without obstructions, or the warning light may remain illuminated.
- **Solution:** Check sensors for alignment and debris; consult a dealership or specialist to update or recalibrate driver-assistance software.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Nissan Altima remains an appealing midsize sedan for commuters looking for fuel efficiency and comfort. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection that evaluates the electrical system, tests the automatic emergency braking sensors, and verifies proper mechanical operation of all doors and accessories.
