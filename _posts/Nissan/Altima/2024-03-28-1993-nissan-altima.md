**Introduction:**

The 1993 Nissan Altima is a mid-size sedan that was available with two engine options: a 2.4-liter inline-4 and a 3.0-liter V6. The inline-4 produced 150 horsepower and 156 lb-ft of torque, while the V6 produced 190 horsepower and 190 lb-ft of torque. Both engines were paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

The Altima was praised for its roomy interior, comfortable ride, and responsive handling. However, it was criticized for its lack of power and poor fuel economy.

## **Engine**

**2.4L Inline-4**

* **Leaking oil pan gasket:** Oil leaks from the oil pan gasket.
* **Valve cover gasket failure:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket.
* **Camshaft position sensor failure:** The camshaft position sensor fails, causing the engine to run rough.
* **Exhaust manifold gasket failure:** The exhaust manifold gasket fails, causing exhaust leaks.

**3.0L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** The intake manifold gasket fails, causing coolant and oil leaks.
* **Valve cover gasket failure:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket.
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner fails, causing the timing chain to skip.

## **Transmission**

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Shift solenoid failure:** The shift solenoid fails, causing the transmission to shift hard or not shift at all.
* **Torque converter failure:** The torque converter fails, causing the transmission to slip.

## **Suspension**

* **Strut failure:** The front struts fail, causing the vehicle to ride rough and handle poorly.
* **Sway bar link failure:** The sway bar links fail, causing the vehicle to sway excessively when cornering.

## **Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** The alternator fails, causing the battery to not charge.
* **Battery failure:** The battery fails, preventing the vehicle from starting.
* **Starter failure:** The starter fails, preventing the vehicle from starting.

## **Exterior**

* **Plastic bumper cracking:** The plastic bumper cracks easily.
* **Door handle failure:** The door handles break easily.

## **Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking:** The dashboard cracks easily.
* **Climate control failure:** The climate control system fails, preventing the AC or heat from working.

## **Summary**

The 1993 Nissan Altima has a number of potential issues, including oil leaks, transmission problems, and suspension failures. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the vehicle's condition. However, overall, the Altima is a reliable vehicle that is relatively affordable to repair.
