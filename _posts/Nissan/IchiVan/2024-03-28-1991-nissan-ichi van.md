**Introduction to the 1991 Nissan Ichi Van**

The 1991 Nissan Ichi Van was a compact van offered with a single engine and transmission option. It was praised for its spacious interior, versatility, and affordability, while some disliked its lack of power and outdated design.

**Engine**
* **1.3L Inline-4**
    * **Blown Head Gasket:** Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust. Solution: Replace head gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Timing Chain Rattle:** Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially at startup. Solution: Replace timing chain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Transmission**
* **4-Speed Manual**
    * **Grinding Gears:** Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting. Solution: Replace worn gears. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Leaking Transmission Fluid:** Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, transmission slipping or jerking. Solution: Replace transmission seals or lines. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Suspension**
* **Shock Absorber Wear:** Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride quality, bouncing or swaying of the vehicle. Solution: Replace shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per pair

**Electrical**
* **Alternator Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery not charging. Solution: Replace alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
* **Battery Discharge:** Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the engine. Solution: Test and replace faulty battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Exterior**
* **Rust on Body Panels:** Description/Symptoms: Rust spots or holes on exterior panels. Solution: Replace damaged panels or repair rust. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**
* **Dash Panel Cracking:** Description/Symptoms: Cracks or splits in the dash panel. Solution: Replace dash panel or repair cracks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not working properly. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace climate control components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800

**Summary**

The 1991 Nissan Ichi Van is a reliable and practical vehicle with a few common issues. While some issues like head gasket failure and transmission gear grinding can be costly to repair, most issues are relatively minor and affordable to fix. The overall cost of maintaining the Ichi Van is low, making it a suitable choice for budget-minded individuals.
