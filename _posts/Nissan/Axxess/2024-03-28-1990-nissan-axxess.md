**Introduction:**

The 1990 Nissan Axxess is a compact car offered with a single engine and transmission option. It is known for its affordability, reliability, and fuel efficiency. However, like most cars, it has some known issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine:**

**1.5L GA15DE I4**
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip, which can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**3-Speed Automatic (RE4F02A)**
* **Transmission shudder:** The transmission may shudder during shifts.
    * Symptoms: Vibration or shudder while shifting
    * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter or rebuild the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Faulty ignition switch:** The ignition switch may fail, causing the car not to start.
    * Symptoms: Car won't start
    * Solution: Replace the ignition switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior:**

* **HVAC blower motor failure:** The HVAC blower motor may fail, causing a loss of airflow in the cabin.
    * Symptoms: No airflow from the vents
    * Solution: Replace the HVAC blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1990 Nissan Axxess is generally a reliable car, but it does have some known issues that owners should be aware of. The most serious issue is the timing chain tensioner failure, which can lead to catastrophic engine damage. Other issues, such as the transmission shudder and HVAC blower motor failure, are less severe but can still be costly to repair. Overall, the Axxess is a good choice for those who need an affordable and reliable compact car but should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing.
