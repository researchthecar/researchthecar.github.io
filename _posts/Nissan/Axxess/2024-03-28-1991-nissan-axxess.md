The 1991 Nissan Axxess was a compact hatchback that offered a sporty driving experience. It was available with two engine options: a 1.6-liter inline-four and a 2.0-liter inline-four. Both engines were paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. The Axxess was praised for its handling and performance, but it was also criticized for its cramped interior and lack of features.
