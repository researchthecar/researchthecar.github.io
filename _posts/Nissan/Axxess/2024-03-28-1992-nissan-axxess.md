**Introduction:**

The 1992 Nissan Axxess is a subcompact car available with a single engine and transmission option. The 1.6L inline-4 engine produces 110 horsepower and 104 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 3-speed automatic transmission. The Axxess is praised for its fuel efficiency and affordability, but criticized for its underpowered engine and limited features.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4**

* **Blown Head Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the head gasket area, white smoke from the exhaust, overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the head gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cracked Intake Manifold:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leak, loss of power, rough idle.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**3-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, delayed or hard shifting, no movement in certain gears.
    * Solution: Replace the shift solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise when turning or going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical components not functioning properly, battery not charging.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No cranking when turning the key, clicking noise from the starter.
    * Solution: Replace the starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Rear Quarter Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust developing around the wheel wells and rear quarter panels.
    * Solution: Rust prevention treatment and body repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty Window Switches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows not opening or closing properly, intermittent operation.
    * Solution: Replace the window switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks developing on the dashboard surface.
    * Solution: Dashboard cover or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1992 Nissan Axxess has several common issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. While some issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly to resolve. Overall, the Axxess is generally a reliable vehicle for its age and price range, but it is important to be aware of potential issues and maintenance requirements.
