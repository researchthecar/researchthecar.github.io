# 2020 Nissan Murano Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Nissan Murano offers a comfortable, stylish ride with a standard V6 engine, but prospective buyers should review known owner complaints before purchasing. While it provides a well-appointed cabin, reports highlight specific areas of concern including forward collision system quirks, transmission hesitation, and lighting issues.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2020 Nissan Murano comes equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers smooth and adequate acceleration for daily driving, though buyers should watch for routine maintenance items.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped with an Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, it prioritizes fuel efficiency. However, used buyers should thoroughly test the transmission's responsiveness during a pre-purchase test drive.

**Transmission Hesitation and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report occasional hesitation when accelerating from a slow roll, as well as rare instances of premature CVT failure requiring major repairs.
- **Solution:** Consult a professional transmission specialist or dealer for diagnostic testing; fluid maintenance or unit replacement may be required.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical accessories and exterior lighting systems generally perform well, though headlight housing seals require inspection for moisture buildup.

**Headlight Moisture and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience heavy moisture intrusion inside the headlight assembly, leading to premature failure of the headlamp unit.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight housings for cracks or failing seals; replacement of the sealed headlight assembly may be necessary.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance occupant protection, but sensor calibration issues can occasionally lead to phantom braking events.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system can occasionally trigger false positives, unexpectedly applying brakes when no obstacles are present.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect and recalibrate the radar and camera sensors responsible for the forward collision system.

### Seat Belts

Standard seat belt restraints secure occupants effectively, though minor latch mechanisms can develop mechanical sticking over time.

**Sticky Seat Belt Latches**
- **Symptoms:** The driver or passenger seat belt buckles may become difficult to pull or fail to latch smoothly.
- **Solution:** Clean and lubricate the latch mechanism, or replace the seat belt buckle assembly if internal sticking persists.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V402000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Spindle](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Murano&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The steering knuckle or rear axle could deform upon impact, such as from hitting a curb, resulting in a loss of steering control, or possible wheel separation. Both of these conditions can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the front steering knuckle and rear axle housing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V170000: Steering:Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Murano&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The left and/or right-hand transverse link may not have been manufactured correctly, which could result in it separating from the ball joint. Ball joint separation can cause a loss of vehicle control, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the transverse link's lot codes, and if necessary, replace them and perform a vehicle alignment, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2020 Nissan Murano, conduct a thorough test drive to evaluate CVT operation and check all electronic safety features and lighting assemblies. While it offers a comfortable ride and strong V6 performance, verifying maintenance history and inspecting potential trouble spots will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
