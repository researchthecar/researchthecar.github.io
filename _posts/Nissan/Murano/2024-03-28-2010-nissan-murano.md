**Introduction:**

The 2010 Nissan Murano is a midsize SUV known for its stylish design, spacious interior, and impressive safety features. It offers a 3.5L V6 engine mated to a CVT transmission. Praised for its comfortable ride, advanced technology, and versatile cargo space, the Murano has some reported issues that buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**

  * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattles or knocks, check engine light illuminates, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, reduced engine performance, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Clean intake valves and throttle body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT Transmission**

  * **CVT Belt Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or hesitation during acceleration, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace CVT belt and pulleys
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid pooling under the vehicle, rough shifts, slipping gears
    * Solution: Fix fluid leak, replace transmission filter if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Struts/Shocks Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, poor handling, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace struts or shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noises, vibration while driving
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, electrical components malfunction
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system, replace battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Tailgate Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on the rear tailgate
    * Solution: Repair or replace tailgate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly or seal leaks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Lid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lid breaks or cracks, unable to open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace center console lid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Panel Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons don't work, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Replace climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2010 Nissan Murano has some reported issues, particularly with its timing chain tensioner, CVT transmission, and suspension components. While the severity and cost of repairs for some issues can be significant, many of the reported problems are relatively minor and affordable to fix. It's important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
