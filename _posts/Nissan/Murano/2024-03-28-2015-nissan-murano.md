**Introduction**

The 2015 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV that offers a combination of comfort, performance, and style. It is available with a choice of two engine options: a 3.5-liter V6 producing 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing 182 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a continuously variable transmission (CVT) and a six-speed automatic transmission. The Murano has been praised for its spacious interior, smooth ride, and advanced safety features. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of off-road capability and its somewhat bland styling.

**Engine**

**Summary**

The 2015 Nissan Murano has a number of potential issues, including timing chain tensioner failure, CVT slipping, and rear sway bar link failure. The severity of these issues can range from minor annoyances to major mechanical failures. The cost to fix these issues can also be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Overall, the Murano is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
