# 2024 Nissan Murano

### Overview
The 2024 Nissan Murano is a mid-size SUV known for its comfortable ride, stylish interior, and user-friendly technology. As a model that has been in production for several years with incremental updates, it generally benefits from a degree of established reliability. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear and tear. We will examine the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, exterior, and interior to provide a comprehensive overview of potential technical challenges.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional hesitation during acceleration or slight jerking sensations, particularly at lower speeds or when the transmission is cold.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or in severe cases, transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800 (for fluid service/software), $3,000 - $6,000 (for replacement)

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light illuminated, accompanied by a burning smell or reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair cooling system, check transmission fluid level and condition, or replace transmission.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500 (for cooling system repair), $3,000 - $6,000 (for replacement)

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen occasionally freezes, becomes unresponsive to touch, or restarts unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for software/reset), $800 - $1,500 (for replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery depletes prematurely, leading to starting issues. May be related to parasitic draw from certain modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing to identify parasitic draw, repair or replace faulty module, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600 (for diagnostics and module repair), $150 - $300 (for battery replacement)

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Nissan Murano generally presents a reliable profile, with its V6 engine and standard suspension systems showing no significant widespread technical concerns. The primary areas to monitor are the Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT), which can occasionally exhibit hesitation or overheating issues requiring attention, and the electrical system, where infotainment glitches and intermittent battery drain have been noted. Exterior and interior components are largely free from reported widespread technical defects. Owners should be aware of potential CVT maintenance needs and ensure electrical systems are functioning optimally to maintain the Murano's overall dependability.
