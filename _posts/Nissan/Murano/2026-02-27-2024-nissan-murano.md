# 2024 Nissan Murano Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Nissan Murano, prospective buyers will find a comfortable and well-appointed midsize crossover. While overall owner feedback is generally limited, a few early reports point to minor electrical quirks and powertrain behavior worth keeping in mind during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2024 Nissan Murano comes standard with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated powerplant delivers smooth acceleration and adequate power for daily commuting and highway cruising.

**Powertrain Vibration and Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected vehicle vibration and shuddering at highway speeds, which temporarily cleared after restarting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine mounts, fuel delivery, and powertrain control modules if symptoms recur.

**Cruise Control and Throttle Interaction**
- **Symptoms:** An owner noted unusual vehicle response when attempting to accelerate while resuming cruise control during a passing maneuver.
- **Solution:** Verify proper operation of the electronic throttle body and cruise control switches during a test drive.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The Murano utilizes an Xtronic continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It is designed to optimize fuel efficiency, though buyers should evaluate its operational smoothness during a thorough test drive.

## Suspension

### Handling and Stability

The suspension system is tuned for ride comfort, absorbing road imperfections well during daily commutes.

**High Crosswind Sensitivity**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may feel that the vehicle exhibits instability or requires constant correction when driving in strong wind conditions, even at reduced speeds.
- **Solution:** Ensure tires are properly inflated and have the wheel alignment and suspension components checked if handling feels loose.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

Electrical components generally operate smoothly, though isolated infotainment software glitches have been reported.

**Infotainment Resets and Clock Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may repeatedly reset during a short drive, occasionally affecting the backup camera display and causing the clock time to reset.
- **Solution:** Check for available software updates at a dealership or have the head unit inspected if frequent reboots occur.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V053000: Visibility:Glass, Side/Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Murano&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Detached glass panels can become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace both side quarter glass panels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Nissan Murano offers a refined ride and a strong V6 engine, making it an appealing option in the midsize crossover segment. While a small number of early owner complaints mention infotainment resets, handling sensitivity in high winds, and transient powertrain vibrations, these are largely isolated reports. Used car buyers should conduct a comprehensive test drive to check infotainment stability and high-speed road manners.
