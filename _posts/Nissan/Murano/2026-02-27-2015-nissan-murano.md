# 2015 Nissan Murano

### Overview
The 2015 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and distinctive styling. It was generally well-received for its blend of practicality and upscale features. Reliability for the 2015 model year is considered average to good, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from mechanics and owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35DE)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, or crankshaft seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell, white smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of radiator, hoses, water pump, or head gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200


## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough acceleration, delayed engagement, shuddering during operation.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or in severe cases, transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3500

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light, slipping gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Cooler line flush, replacement of transmission cooler, or transmission fluid service.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noise over bumps, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sway bar links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noise when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of strut mounts.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsive**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen not responding to input, system freezing during operation.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500


### Battery Drain
_No major issues reported for this configuration._

## Exterior

### Body Panels
**Paint Defects**
- **Symptoms:** Peeling or bubbling paint, especially on the roof or hood.
- **Solution:** Repainting affected areas.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+


### Sunroof
**Leaking**
- **Symptoms:** Water ingress into the cabin, especially during heavy rain or car washes.
- **Solution:** Cleaning and clearing sunroof drains, or replacement of seals.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or air only coming from certain vents.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Seat Material
**Excessive Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Tearing or excessive wrinkling of leather or fabric seats, particularly on the driver's side.
- **Solution:** Seat cover replacement or professional repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Nissan Murano presents a generally reliable ownership experience, with its V6 engine being a robust unit. The primary areas of concern tend to be the CVT transmission, which can exhibit issues if not properly maintained, and some electrical components like the infotainment system. Exterior paint and interior wear are also noted by some owners. Regular maintenance, especially for the transmission fluid, is crucial for longevity. While not immune to problems, most issues are repairable and not indicative of a fundamentally flawed vehicle.
