**Introduction:**

The 2012 Nissan Murano is a mid-size SUV that offers a combination of practicality and luxury. It features a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The V6 engine produces 265 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque. The Murano has received praise for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and ample cargo space. However, it has also been met with criticism for its uninspiring exterior design and lack of fuel efficiency.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Repair or replace piston rings, valve guides, or oil control valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged timing chain tensioner, causing a rattling noise at startup or acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: 12V384

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Shuddering or Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shudders or jerks during acceleration or shifting gears.
    * Solution: Re программирование of transmission control module or replacement of valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Upper Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or torn rear upper control arm bushings, causing abnormal tire wear, vibration, or noise.
    * Solution: Replace rear upper control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $750
* **Front Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking or damaged front struts, causing a bumpy ride, excessive bounce, or loss of stability.
    * Solution: Replace front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **ECU Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning engine control unit (ECU), causing engine performance issues, starting problems, or complete failure.
    * Solution: Replace ECU.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty alternator, leading to insufficient charging of the battery or electrical system failure.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Exhaust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature rust and corrosion on the exhaust system, potentially leading to exhaust leaks or failure.
    * Solution: Replace or repair rusted exhaust components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose sunroof panel or track, causing rattling or whistling noises during driving.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace sunroof bolts or tracks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary:**

The 2012 Nissan Murano is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues that owners should be aware of. The most significant concerns include oil consumption, timing chain tensioner failure, and CVT shuddering. Other issues like suspension bushings and electrical failures can also occur but are less severe. The cost of repairing these issues can be significant, so it's crucial to factor in maintenance costs when considering the Murano.
