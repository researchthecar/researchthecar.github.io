**Introduction**

The 2017 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV that offers a comfortable ride and a spacious interior. It is available with three different engine options: a 3.5L V6, a 2.5L inline-4, and a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4. The 3.5L V6 produces 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.5L inline-4 produces 182 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque. The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 produces 245 horsepower and 284 lb-ft of torque. All three engines are mated to a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil or coolant leaks, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
**2.5L Inline-4**

* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or knocking, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Turbocharger failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, smoke from exhaust, turbocharger noises
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT slipping or shuddering**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering during acceleration or shifting
    * Solution: Flush and replace CVT fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front strut mount failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front strut mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, electrical accessories do not work
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof rail trim fading**
    * Description/Symptoms: Roof rail trim fading or peeling
    * Solution: Replace roof rail trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof rattles when open or closed
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof tracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Nissan Murano is a comfortable and spacious SUV, but it does have some known issues. The most significant issues are with the turbocharger and the timing chain tensioner. However, most of the other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Murano is a reliable vehicle that offers good value for its price.
