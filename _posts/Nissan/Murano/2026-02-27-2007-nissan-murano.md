# 2007 Nissan Murano

### Overview
The 2007 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV that offered a comfortable ride and a distinctive styling for its time. It was primarily equipped with a V6 engine and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). While generally considered a decent vehicle, like any car of this age, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problems and their associated costs for owners considering or currently owning a 2007 Murano.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35DE)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets (e.g., valve cover, oil pan).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell, white smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of radiator, hoses, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Overheating and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation, slipping gears, jerky acceleration, transmission warning light, complete loss of drive.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or complete transmission rebuild/replacement.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**CVT Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low transmission fluid level, difficulty shifting, potential overheating.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, poor handling, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noise when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not working, interior lights flickering, dashboard warning lights illuminating randomly.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Paint and Body Panels
**Clear Coat Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Fading paint, peeling clear coat, especially on horizontal surfaces like the roof and hood.
- **Solution:** Repainting affected panels.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500 per panel


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding and Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced headlight visibility, aesthetic degradation.
- **Solution:** Headlight restoration kit or replacement of headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or air only coming from certain vents, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Infotainment System
**Navigation System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, inaccurate GPS location, system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the navigation unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Nissan Murano, while offering a unique style and comfortable ride, presents several potential reliability concerns, particularly with its CVT transmission, which is prone to overheating and failure, often requiring costly repairs. Engine oil and coolant leaks are also noted. Electrical issues, such as BCM malfunctions and alternator failures, can lead to various electronic gremlins. Exterior paint degradation and interior HVAC blower motor issues are common for vehicles of this age. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential expenses and ensure thorough pre-purchase inspections, especially focusing on the transmission's condition.
