# 2017 Nissan Murano Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used midsize SUV, the 2017 Nissan Murano stands out for its striking styling, comfortable Zero Gravity seats, and smooth highway ride. However, prospective buyers should approach with a healthy dose of caution due to notable recurring owner complaints concerning powertrain durability, forward-looking safety sensor glitches, and electrical system concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, this model offers strong acceleration and a confident highway drive. While generally robust mechanically, fuel delivery and throttle response should be checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Fuel/Propulsion Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall or hesitate when the accelerator pedal is pressed.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery and throttle body components; replace faulty sensors or fuel system parts as needed.

### Wiring Harness and Control Modules

Electrical complaints highlight localized wiring degradation and control module failures that can disable comfort features or lead to more severe safety concerns.

**Wire Harness and Heated Seat Electrical Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Air conditioning failure, headlight dimming, vehicle stalling, or localized burning smells and melted control module connectors from seat heaters.
- **Solution:** Inspect damaged wire harnesses and faulty connectors; replace affected wiring modules to eliminate fire hazards.

## Transmission

### Xtronic CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)

Paired exclusively with an Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) across all trims and front-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations, this unit delivers efficient cruising. However, it is the most critical area of concern for long-term reliability, as owners frequently report premature wear and failure symptoms.

**Transmission Slippage, Whining, and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of acceleration, high RPMs without speed increase, whining noises, jerking motions, or total transmission failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition, check for pending software updates or recalls, and replace or rebuild the transmission if internal failure has occurred.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance systems add modern convenience but can frustrate drivers if radar sensors experience calibration loss or false fault triggers.

**Unexpected Braking and Radar Obstruction**
- **Symptoms:** Sensors falsely report obstructions, causing sudden unexpected emergency braking or deactivating adaptive cruise control.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas, recalibrate forward-looking radar/sonar sensors, or replace failing sensor units.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Murano&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak on the circuit board may result in an electrical short, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the serial number on the ABS actuator, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Nissan Murano offers a comfortable cabin and refined daily drivability, but prospective buyers must perform a thorough evaluation. Pay special attention to the operation of the CVT transmission during a test drive, and verify that all safety sensors and electrical wiring harnesses are functioning correctly without warning lights.
