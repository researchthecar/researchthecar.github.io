# 2005 Nissan Murano

### Overview
The 2005 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV known for its distinctive styling and comfortable ride. It was generally well-received for its performance and interior amenities. However, like many vehicles of its era, it has certain areas that have shown more susceptibility to issues over time. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical problems reported by owners and technicians, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear. Understanding these potential weak points can help current and prospective owners anticipate and address necessary repairs, ensuring the longevity and reliability of the vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35DE)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Covers/Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets or other related seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, engine misfires, coolant leaks, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Solenoid Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor performance, check engine light with VVT codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty VVT solenoids.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Overheating and Fluid Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation, slipping, shuddering, or failure to engage gears, especially under load or after prolonged driving. Transmission fluid may appear burnt.
- **Solution:** Regular fluid changes with the correct CVT fluid, potential transmission cooler upgrade, or transmission rebuild/replacement.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000+

**Internal Component Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of drive, grinding noises, harsh shifting.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $5000+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noises when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, door locks, interior lights, or dashboard warning lights that come on and off randomly.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Paint and Body Panels
**Clear Coat Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** The clear coat on the roof, hood, or other exterior panels begins to flake or peel, especially in warmer climates.
- **Solution:** Repainting affected areas.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight lenses become opaque and yellowed, reducing light output and visibility.
- **Solution:** Restoration kits or replacement of headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on the highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


### Seat Heaters
**Malfunctioning Heating Elements**
- **Symptoms:** One or both seat heaters fail to produce heat.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the seat heating elements or associated wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2005 Nissan Murano offers a unique blend of style and utility, but potential buyers and owners should be aware of common issues. The CVT transmission is a significant concern, with overheating and internal failures being the most costly repairs. Engine-related issues like oil leaks and intake manifold gasket problems are also notable. Electrical gremlins, particularly those related to the BCM, can be frustrating. Exterior paint degradation and interior comfort system failures are also reported. Despite these potential drawbacks, many Muranos have provided reliable service with proper maintenance and timely repairs of these known weak points.
