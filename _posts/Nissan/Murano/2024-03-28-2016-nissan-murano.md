**Introduction:**

The 2016 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV that offers a comfortable and well-equipped interior. It is powered by a standard 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. The Murano is available with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive and comes with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

People who like the Murano appreciate its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive fuel economy. However, some common complaints include a lack of power from the V6 engine, a sluggish CVT, and a few reliability concerns.

**Engine**

* **3.5-Liter V6**

  * **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

  * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, particularly during startup, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

  * **Shuddering or Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or shuddering when accelerating, accompanied by a slipping sensation.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the CVT.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery quickly drains overnight or while parked.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or check for parasitic draws.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing or unresponsive, navigation issues, or audio system problems.
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Issues with air conditioning or heating, including temperature fluctuations or fan noise.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair specific component failure.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Nissan Murano has a few potential issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The cost of these repairs can be significant, but the Murano is generally a reliable vehicle. Overall, the Murano's spacious interior, comfortable ride, and good fuel economy make it a popular choice for families and those looking for a crossover SUV.
