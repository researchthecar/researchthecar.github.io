# 2016 Nissan Murano

### Overview
The 2016 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV known for its comfortable ride, stylish design, and spacious interior. It primarily came equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). This generation of the Murano generally offers a good balance of performance and fuel efficiency for its class. Reliability data suggests that while the powertrain is largely dependable, some owners have reported specific concerns, particularly with the CVT under certain conditions. Overall, the Murano is considered a solid choice for families and individuals seeking a comfortable and well-equipped vehicle, with most reported issues being manageable.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35DE)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets or seals (e.g., valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell, white smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hoses, radiator, water pump, or head gasket.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500+


## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle hesitates during acceleration, sudden jerks or lurches, rough shifting feel.
- **Solution:** Software update for the transmission control module, fluid flush and replacement, or in severe cases, transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $4000+

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light illuminated, reduced engine power, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Check and top off transmission fluid, inspect for leaks, clean or replace transmission cooler, or replace the transmission.
- **Cost:** $400 - $4000+

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsive, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation system freezing.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment system, hard reset, or replacement of the unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning light, accessories not working.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or components causing drain, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Nissan Murano presents a generally reliable package, with its V6 engine proving to be robust. The primary area of concern for this model year lies with the CVT transmission, which has seen reports of hesitation, jerking, and overheating, potentially leading to costly repairs. Electrical issues, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are also noted but are typically less severe. Exterior and interior components, along with the suspension, are largely free from widespread problems. Prospective buyers should pay close attention to the transmission's performance and maintenance history.
