# 2022 Nissan Murano Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Nissan Murano, prospective buyers will find a stylish and comfortable midsize crossover backed by a standard V6 powertrain. While owner reports to safety authorities are relatively light, used car shoppers should review available owner feedback regarding minor body leaks and electrical concerns to ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2022 Nissan Murano features a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated powerplant provides smooth acceleration and generally dependable everyday performance for daily commuting.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed through an Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It is designed to optimize fuel economy, though buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth operation.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Issues related to exterior glass and visibility are infrequent but worth noting during a visual walkaround.

**Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected cracking along the bottom of the front windshield, sometimes occurring after operating the defroster.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front windshield thoroughly for stress cracks or chips, and replace the glass if structural integrity is compromised.

### Body Seals and Water Leaks

Water leaks around body seals can lead to damp floorboards and should be checked closely during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Cabin Water Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Water pooling on the front and rear floorboards has been observed by owners during heavy rain or while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Check door seals, window gaskets, and roof drains for proper fitment and blockages, and dry any affected interior carpeting promptly.

## Safety

### Collision Notification Systems

Safety and telematics components should be tested to ensure all emergency notification features operate correctly.

**Automatic Collision Notification Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners reported that automatic emergency notification features or SOS buttons failed to activate or function as expected following a collision.
- **Solution:** Verify the functionality of onboard telematics and safety communication systems during your vehicle evaluation.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Nissan Murano offers a smooth ride and a capable V6 engine, making it a comfortable choice for used vehicle buyers. While isolated owner reports highlight potential concerns regarding cabin water leaks and windshield durability, conducting a detailed inspection before purchase will help secure a reliable example.
