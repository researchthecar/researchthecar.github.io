**Introduction:**

The 2014 Nissan Murano is a midsize SUV known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features. It comes with a choice of two engine options mated to a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Premature Valve Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rattling noise, engine knocking
    * Solution: Replace affected valve springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks from Oil Pressure Sensor:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine oil leak from the oil pressure sensor housing
    * Solution: Replace faulty oil pressure sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Hesitation during Acceleration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle hesitates or stumbles during acceleration
    * Solution: Update CVT software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
    * Recalls: NTSB ID: 10141788

**Suspension**

* **Strut Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or grinding noise when turning steering wheel
    * Solution: Replace faulty strut bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $350 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain due to Faulty BCM:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially when vehicle is not in use
    * Solution: Replace the Body Control Module (BCM)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate lock fails to engage, causing tailgate to pop open while driving
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles become corroded, causing difficulty in opening the doors
    * Solution: Repaint or replace affected door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Instrument cluster lights go out or malfunction
    * Solution: Replace the instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2014 Nissan Murano is a reliable vehicle with a few potential issues to watch out for. The most significant and costly issue is premature valve spring failure, which affects the 3.5L V6 engine. Other issues, such as electrical or interior component failures, are generally less severe and affordable to fix.
