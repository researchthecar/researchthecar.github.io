# 2019 Nissan Murano

### Overview
The 2019 Nissan Murano is a mid-size crossover SUV known for its comfortable ride, upscale interior, and distinctive styling. It primarily features a 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Reliability for the 2019 model year is generally considered good, with owners reporting fewer significant mechanical issues compared to some competitors. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen occasional reports of problems. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (VQ35DE)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, low oil level warnings, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell, steam from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of hoses, radiator, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable delay when accelerating, rough shifting feel, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid flush, or in severe cases, transmission replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3,500

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission warning light, reduced power, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid cooler inspection and repair/replacement, fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000


## Suspension

### Standard Suspension
**Strut Mount Bearing Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsive**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system glitches, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Climate Control System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**

- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Nissan Murano generally presents a good reliability record, particularly concerning its powertrain and exterior components. The most frequently cited issues revolve around the CVT transmission, which can exhibit hesitation or overheating, and some electrical gremlins like infotainment system glitches or battery drain. Minor concerns include potential oil or coolant leaks from the V6 engine and occasional suspension noises. While not indicative of widespread failure, these points suggest that regular maintenance, especially for the CVT, and prompt attention to electrical warnings are advisable for owners to ensure long-term dependability.
