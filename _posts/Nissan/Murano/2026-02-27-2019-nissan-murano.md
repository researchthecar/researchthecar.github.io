# 2019 Nissan Murano Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Nissan Murano offers a comfortable, stylish midsize crossover experience with a refined ride. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner-reported issues involving safety electronics, body hardware, and electrical components before making a purchase decision.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and adequate acceleration for daily driving. It is generally considered a robust and dependable mechanical unit within the lineup.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The Xtronic CVT is paired with the V6 engine in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. While it delivers strong fuel efficiency, buyers should inspect operation thoroughly during a test drive to ensure smooth engagement.

## Exterior

### Sunroof Drains and Lighting

Electrical concerns primarily center around cabin water intrusion from exterior drains and moisture buildup within exterior lighting housings.

**Sunroof Drain Clogging and Water Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin headliner and flooring, potentially risking damage to interior materials and nearby electrical modules.
- **Solution:** Clear or modify the drain tubes and inspect the headliner for hidden moisture accumulation.
- **Cost:** 500+

**Headlight Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Condensation or water pooling inside the sealed LED headlight assemblies, potentially causing lighting failure.
- **Solution:** Replace or reseal the affected headlight assembly.

### Panoramic Sunroof

The exterior body components are generally durable, though the panoramic sunroof glass has been reported by some owners to fracture unexpectedly.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud explosion-like sound while driving at highway speeds, followed by the panoramic glass panel fracturing or shattering.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and clean out all glass fragments from the headliner and interior.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Steering

Advanced driver assistance and safety systems occasionally exhibit erratic behaviors, such as unprompted emergency braking or steering pulls.

**Unintended Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The emergency braking system activates abruptly without an obstruction present, accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer check sensor alignment and update software if applicable.

**Erratic Steering Behavior**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may pull or turn independently while traveling at highway speeds without driver input.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column, electronic assist components, and front suspension alignment.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Murano&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Nissan Murano is an appealing used crossover choice thanks to its comfortable ride and strong V6 performance. However, shoppers should carefully inspect the panoramic sunroof for glass integrity, check for signs of past cabin water leaks from clogged drains, and verify that driver assistance electronics operate smoothly during a pre-purchase test drive.
