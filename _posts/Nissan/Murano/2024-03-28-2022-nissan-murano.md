**Introduction to the 2022 Nissan Murano**

The 2022 Nissan Murano is a midsize SUV powered by a 3.5L V6 engine that produces 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. It's offered with a single transmission option, a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Murano boasts a comfortable interior with ample space, a user-friendly infotainment system, and advanced safety features. However, it has received some criticism for its lack of engine power and lower fuel economy compared to some rivals.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine may consume excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the engine or its components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Piston Ring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of piston rings can cause loss of compression, increased oil consumption, and engine misfires.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes, resulting in delayed acceleration, rough shifting, or loss of power.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the CVT components, such as the transmission fluid, belts, or valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged struts can lead to poor ride quality, reduced handling, and uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the affected struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery may drain prematurely, resulting in difficulties starting or electrical malfunctions.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system, including wiring, alternator, or battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system may experience intermittent glitches, such as freezing, unresponsive touchscreen, or audio quality issues.
    * Solution: Software updates or hardware repairs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Exhaust System:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust system components, such as the muffler and tailpipe, may develop rust over time.
    * Solution: Replace the affected exhaust parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Moonroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Panoramic sunroof may leak during heavy rain or car washes.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair the sunroof seals or drainage system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks may malfunction, failing to lock or unlock properly.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the door lock mechanisms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Nissan Murano has received mixed reviews. While it offers a comfortable interior and user-friendly features, it has been criticized for its lack of engine power and lower fuel economy. Some issues, such as excessive oil consumption and CVT slippage, can be costly to repair. However, the vehicle has a relatively low number of severe issues reported, and most can be addressed through routine maintenance and repairs.
