# 2018 Nissan Murano Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Nissan Murano delivers stylish comfort and a smooth ride, but used-car buyers should carefully inspect certain mechanical and electronic systems. While owner feedback highlights its appeal as a comfortable commuter, reviewing recurring complaints regarding safety tech, structural glass, and powertrain responsiveness can help you avoid potential headaches down the road.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration for everyday driving. Overall, this V6 platform is generally robust, though sporadic owner reports have noted electrical or fire-related concerns tied to specific recalls.

## Transmission

### Xtronic CVT

Equipped with an Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT) paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive, this setup prioritizes fuel economy. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive the vehicle to check for hesitation or shuttering.

**Shuddering and Engagement Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a jerky or shuddering sensation throughout the cabin during acceleration, as well as occasional failure to properly engage at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the continuously variable transmission fluid condition and overall operation; transmission replacement or specialized service may be required if internal wear is present.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The available panoramic sunroof brings an open feel to the cabin, though rare reports of spontaneous glass breakage warrant careful visual inspection.

**Unexpected Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the panoramic sunroof front glass panel shattered unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass closely for any pre-existing stress fractures or damage prior to purchase, and keep the interior shade closed when driving as a precaution.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance peace of mind, but sensor reliability issues can cause unexpected warning lights and temporary system deactivation.

**Intermittent Warning Lights and System Disabling**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision warning and automatic emergency braking lights can illuminate intermittently, disabling cruise control and safety features while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a dealership or technician is recommended to check for sensor obstructions, software glitches, or faulty ABS/braking components affecting the safety suite.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Murano&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 19V807000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Murano&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A brake fluid leak on the circuit board may result in an electrical short, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS actuator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2018 Nissan Murano, prioritize a thorough test drive to evaluate transmission smoothness and ensure all driver-assistance electronics operate without triggering warning lights. Checking service records for past warranty work or safety updates will provide additional confidence in your purchase.
