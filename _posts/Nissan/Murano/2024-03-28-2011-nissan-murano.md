## 2011 Nissan Murano

The 2011 Nissan Murano is a midsize crossover SUV that offers a blend of style, comfort, and versatility. It features a spacious interior, a powerful engine, and a smooth ride.

**Engine**

* **3.5L V6**
    * **Cylinder Head Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, oil leaks, overheating, engine noise.
        * Solution: Replace cylinder head.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, decreased engine performance.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain and components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)**
    * **Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation, inconsistent gear shifting, reduced acceleration.
        * Solution: Replace CVT.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: No forward or reverse gears, slipping.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, vibration, poor ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dashboard warning lights.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: N/A
* **Headlight Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, flickering.
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Hinge Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on tailgate hinges.
    * Solution: Clean and lubricate hinges, replace if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation screen blank or unresponsive.
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
        * Recalls: N/A
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control does not respond, air conditioning not working.
    * Solution: Replace climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700
        * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2011 Nissan Murano is a reliable vehicle with a few common issues. The engine and transmission problems can be severe and costly to fix, but they are not widespread. The suspension, electrical, and interior issues are less severe and generally less expensive to repair.
