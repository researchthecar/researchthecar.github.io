# 2021 Nissan Murano Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Nissan Murano, buyers will find a stylish and comfortable midsize crossover equipped with a dependable powertrain. However, owner reports indicate a few recurring electrical and safety-related quirks worth keeping in mind during your pre-purchase inspection. While the driving experience is generally pleasant, reviewing these specific owner complaints helps ensure a well-informed buying decision.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2021 Nissan Murano comes standard with a 3.5-liter V6 engine generating around 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated powerplant is generally robust and provides smooth power delivery for daily commuting.

**Unintended Acceleration / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported occasional brief acceleration hesitation when merging, or instances where the vehicle momentarily continued accelerating after releasing the pedal.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electronic throttle body, accelerator pedal sensor, and engine control module for faults.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired with the V6 engine, the Xronic CVT delivers power smoothly to either the front wheels or the available all-wheel-drive system. It prioritizes fuel efficiency, though buyers should always test-drive to ensure smooth operation.

## Exterior

### Rear Windshield

Exterior glass components generally hold up well, though rare instances of spontaneous rear glass breakage have been reported.

**Spontaneous Rear Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear windshield shatters unexpectedly without any apparent impact from outside objects.
- **Solution:** Replace the rear glass window and inspect the window frame and defroster grid connections for stress points.

## Interior

### Dashboard and Instruments

Electrical system reports note occasional dashboard display faults and gauge malfunctions that can affect driver awareness.

**Instrument Cluster and Fuel Gauge Inaccuracies**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced erratic speedometer readings, blanking dashboard displays, or fuel gauges that cease to function properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect instrument cluster wiring and software calibration; software updates or module replacement may be required by a dealer.

### Sunroof Drainage

Cabin water intrusion stemming from panoramic sunroof drains has been flagged as a potential concern requiring routine clearing.

**Panoramic Sunroof Water Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the vehicle cabin due to clogged or failing panoramic sunroof drainage channels.
- **Solution:** Clear sunroof drain tubes and inspect seals for proper drainage and structural integrity.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance protection, though occasional sensor alignment or fault issues have been documented by owners.

**Persistent Crash Sensor Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** The crash sensor or forward collision warning light illuminates continuously while driving or parked, even when no obstructions are present.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer or a specialized shop recalibrate or replace faulty collision sensors and radar modules.

### Seat Belts

Rear seat belt mechanism reliability is critical for passenger safety; checking latch operation during an inspection is recommended.

**Rear Seat Belt Buckle Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The driver-side rear seat belt buckle fails to engage properly, and the release button does not pop up as intended.
- **Solution:** Inspect the affected seat belt assembly and replace the faulty buckle mechanism.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Nissan Murano is a comfortable and capable midsize crossover that offers a smooth ride and solid V6 performance. While owner complaints point to a few isolated electrical, sunroof drainage, and sensor issues, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help catch these items early. Overall, it remains a reasonable used vehicle choice when properly maintained.
