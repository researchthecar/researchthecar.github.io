**2002 Nissan Tsubame Overview**

The 2002 Nissan Tsubame is a mid-size sedan known for its sleek styling and comfortable interior. It came equipped with a 2.5-liter V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Despite its positive attributes, the vehicle has experienced several issues that have affected owner satisfaction.

**Engine**

* **2.5L V6 engine**
    * **Valve Seat Recall:** Premature valve seat wear can cause engine damage.
        * Solution: Valve seat replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID 15V154000, Nissan Recall ID P1512
    * **Exhaust Gas Control Valve Failure:** Malfunctioning exhaust gas control valve (EGRV) can cause poor engine performance and decreased fuel economy.
        * Solution: EGRV replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Transmission Solenoid Failure:** Solenoid malfunction can lead to delayed or harsh shifting, as well as premature transmission failure.
        * Solution: Solenoid replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:** Worn or damaged front suspension struts can cause reduced handling and stability, as well as excessive tire wear.
        * Solution: Strut replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200 per side
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Shock Absorber Failure:** Failed rear shocks can impact ride quality and stability.
        * Solution: Shock absorber replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600 per side
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Defective Fuel Pump:** Malfunctioning fuel pump can lead to engine stalling or difficulty starting.
        * Solution: Fuel pump replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Switch Failure:** Faulty ignition switch can prevent the vehicle from starting.
        * Solution: Ignition switch replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leakage:** Damaged or misaligned sunroof seals can allow water to enter the vehicle's interior.
        * Solution: Sunroof seal repair or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System Failure:** Malfunctioning climate control system can affect temperature regulation within the vehicle.
        * Solution: Climate control module repair or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:** Power window motor or switch failure can prevent the windows from operating properly.
        * Solution: Window motor or switch replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per window
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2002 Nissan Tsubame offers a comfortable and stylish driving experience, it has encountered a range of issues that can be costly to repair. The valve seat recall and transmission solenoid failure can lead to significant expenses, while other problems like suspension and electrical failures can impact the vehicle's overall performance and reliability. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a used Tsubame.
