## 1997 Nissan Tsubame


The 1997 Nissan Tsubame is a compact car that was only sold in Japan. It was available with a 1.5-liter inline-four engine and a four-speed automatic transmission. The Tsubame was praised for its fuel efficiency and affordability, but it was criticized for its lack of power and features.

**Engine**

* **1.5L Inline-Four**

    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner fails, causing the timing chain to skip and potentially damage the engine.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * **Piston Ring Failure:** The piston rings fail, causing the engine to burn oil and lose power.
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
    * **Recalls:**
        * None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:** Transmission fluid leaks from the transmission pan or lines.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission pan or lines.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure:** The shift solenoid fails, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all.
        * Solution: Replace the shift solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter fails, causing the transmission to slip or shudder.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * **Recalls:**
        * None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**

    * **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** The strut mount bearing fails, causing the strut to make noise and the vehicle to handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replace the strut mount bearing.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

* **Rear Suspension**

    * **Shock Absorber Failure:** The shock absorbers fail, causing the vehicle to ride rough and handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replace the shock absorbers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator fails, causing the battery to not charge and the vehicle to stall.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
* **Starter Failure:** The starter fails, preventing the vehicle from starting.
        * Solution: Replace the starter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Recalls:**
    * None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Lens Clouding:** The headlight lenses become cloudy over time, reducing light output.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight lenses.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Speakers Buzzing:** The dashboard speakers buzz at certain frequencies.
        * Solution: Replace the dashboard speakers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

## Summary

Overall, the 1997 Nissan Tsubame is a reliable and affordable car with a few common issues. The engine and transmission issues can be costly to fix, but the suspension and electrical issues are relatively minor. The interior issue is a nuisance, but it is not a major problem.
