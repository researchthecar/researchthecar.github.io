## Electrical

### Standard System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replace alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replace window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Clear Coat Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Fading paint, peeling clear coat, especially on horizontal surfaces.
- **Solution:** Repaint affected panels.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500 per panel

**Rust Formation**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion on wheel arches, rocker panels, and door bottoms, particularly in rust-prone climates.
- **Solution:** Rust repair and repainting.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+ depending on severity


## Interior

### Upholstery and Dash
**Dashboard Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Cracks appearing on the dashboard surface due to sun exposure and age.
- **Solution:** Dashboard cover or replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500

**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or intermittent airflow, often accompanied by a whining noise.
- **Solution:** Replace blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $450

The 2001 Nissan Tsubame is generally considered a reliable vehicle for its age, with no major widespread mechanical failures consistently reported. Proactive maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly can ensure continued dependable service from this practical compact car.
