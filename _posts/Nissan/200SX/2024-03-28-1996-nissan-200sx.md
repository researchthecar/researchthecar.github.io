**Introduction**

The 1996 Nissan 200SX is a sporty coupe that offers a blend of performance and affordability. It was available with a choice of two engines and two transmission options. The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated engine produced 140 horsepower and 130 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.4-liter supercharged engine generated 222 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque. Transmission choices included a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic.

Overall, the 200SX has received positive reviews for its handling, performance, and style. However, it has also been criticized for its cramped interior and lack of features.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Naturally Aspirated**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leak, engine overheating
    - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**2.4-Liter Supercharged**

* **Spark plug blowout**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, reduced power
    - Solution: Replace spark plugs and ignition coils (if necessary)
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced power, check engine light
    - Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and related components
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    - Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount bearing failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise, vibration
    - Solution: Replace strut mount bearings
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering lights
    - Solution: Replace alternator
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine won't crank
    - Solution: Replace starter
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaking**
    - Description/Symptoms: Water intrusion into the cabin
    - Solution: Replace or reseal sunroof components
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control fan motor failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: No air flow from vents
    - Solution: Replace fan motor
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard cracking**
    - Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard surface
    - Solution: Replace dashboard
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1996 Nissan 200SX generally receives positive reviews, but it does have some known issues that can be costly to repair. Common issues include spark plug blowout, timing chain tensioner failure, and transmission slipping. Overall, the severity of the issues can vary, and repair costs can be substantial.
