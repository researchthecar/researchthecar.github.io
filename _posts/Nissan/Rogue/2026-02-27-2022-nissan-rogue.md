# 2022 Nissan Rogue

### Overview
The 2022 Nissan Rogue is a popular compact SUV known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and good fuel economy. As a relatively new model year, widespread long-term reliability data is still accumulating. However, initial reports and analyses suggest a generally positive reliability trend for the Rogue. Owners often praise its user-friendly technology and practical features. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the 2022 Rogue appears to be a solid contender in its segment, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific component performance rather than systemic failures. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Nissan Rogue demonstrates a commendable level of reliability for its model year. The primary areas of concern, though not widespread, tend to be minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system and occasional transmission behavior like hesitation or shuddering, often resolvable with software updates or fluid services. The 1.5L VC-Turbo engine has shown good initial promise, and the suspension and exterior/interior components are generally robust. While the 12V battery can experience premature drain in some instances, overall, the Rogue is positioned as a dependable choice in the compact SUV market, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of major systemic flaws.
