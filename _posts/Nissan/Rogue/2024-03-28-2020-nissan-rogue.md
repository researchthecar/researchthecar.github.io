**Introduction:**

The 2020 Nissan Rogue is a compact SUV known for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and advanced safety features. It comes with a standard 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine and Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). Other engine and transmission options include a 3.5-liter V6 engine with a nine-speed automatic transmission. Owners appreciate the Rogue's comfort, reliability, and value, while some express concerns about its sluggish acceleration and cramped third-row seating.

**Engine**

**2.5-Liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Excess Oil Consumption:** Some owners report high oil consumption, leading to oil leaks and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Nissan extended the engine warranty to 10 years/120,000 miles for affected vehicles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
    * Recalls: NTSB ID 10192289

**3.5-Liter V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:** Drivers experience hesitation or jerking when shifting gears, especially during acceleration or hill climbing.
    * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control module (TCM) or replacing the valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Nine-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed Shifting:** Some vehicles exhibit delayed shifting, resulting in hesitation and reduced performance.
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: NTSB ID 10222112

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:** Owners complain of rattling or squeaking noises coming from the rear suspension, particularly over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replacing rear sway bar links or shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Electrical issues, such as faulty wiring or a defective battery, can lead to rapid battery drain.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the underlying electrical problem.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: NTSB ID 10197592

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Issues:** Some owners experience rattling or vibration from the roof rack, especially at higher speeds.
    * Solution: Installing a weatherstripping kit or tightening the roof rack mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Air Conditioner Malfunction:** Some vehicles experience issues with the air conditioner, such as insufficient cooling or strange noises.
    * Solution: Replacing the AC compressor or evaporator core.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Problems:** Glitches or freezes in the infotainment system can occur, leading to lost audio or navigation.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2020 Nissan Rogue is generally a reliable and well-rounded SUV, but it has some common issues that can affect its performance and user experience. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem. Owners should be aware of potential engine oil consumption, transmission hesitation or shifting problems, and electrical issues. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can help minimize the risk of these issues and ensure the longevity of the vehicle.
