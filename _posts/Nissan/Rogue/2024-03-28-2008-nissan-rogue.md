**Introduction**
The 2008 Nissan Rogue is a compact SUV that offers a spacious interior, versatile seating, and a fuel-efficient four-cylinder engine. It is powered by a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 170 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Rogue is praised for its comfortable ride, user-friendly infotainment system, and affordable price. However, it is criticized for its lack of power, uninspired handling, and poor fuel economy.

## **Engine**

### **2.5L Inline-4**

**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning smell and reduced engine oil levels.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise from the engine, reduced power, or engine stalling.
Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**

### **CVT**

**Transmission Slippage**
Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping during acceleration, inconsistent gear shifting, or loss of power.
Solution: Replace or rebuild the CVT transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

## **Electrical**

**Ignition Coil Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Misfiring or rough idling, reduced engine power, or difficulty starting the engine.
Solution: Replace the faulty ignition coil.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per coil
Recalls: None Found

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, even with a new battery.
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system components causing excessive battery drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

**Tail Light Assembly Moisture**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the tail light assemblies, causing condensation or water droplets.
Solution: Replace the affected tail light assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per assembly
Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

**Dashboard Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noise from the dashboard, especially during rough roads or high temperatures.
Solution: Apply sound deadening material to the affected areas or locate and secure loose components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**
Overall, the 2008 Nissan Rogue faces some significant issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. Valve cover gasket leaks, timing chain tensioner failures, and CVT transmission slippage can be costly to repair. Electrical problems, such as ignition coil failures and battery drain, can also be frustrating and expensive. The Rogue's exterior and interior issues are relatively minor, but they can still affect the overall reliability and enjoyment of the vehicle.
