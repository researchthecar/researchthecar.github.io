**Introduction**

The 2017 Nissan Rogue is a compact SUV that competes with vehicles like the Toyota RAV4, Honda CR-V, and Ford Escape. It's offered with a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine and a CVT continuously variable transmission. The Rogue is well-known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and high-tech features. However, it also has some issues that you should be aware of before buying one.

**Engine**

* **2.5L 4-cylinder**
    * **Valve body failure:** The valve body is a critical component of the transmission that controls the flow of fluid. If it fails, the transmission can't shift gears properly.
        * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping, or no power
        * Solution: Replace the valve body
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)**
    * **Premature failure:** The CVT can fail prematurely due to a variety of factors, including manufacturing defects, fluid leaks, and overheating.
        * Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, or no power
        * Solution: Replace the CVT
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front struts:** The front struts can wear out prematurely, causing the vehicle to ride rough and handle poorly.
    * Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, excessive vibration, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace the front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog light housing:** The fog light housing can crack or break, allowing moisture to enter the assembly.
    * Symptoms: Foggy or dim fog lights, water inside light housing
    * Solution: Replace the fog light housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control system failure:** The climate control system can fail, causing the vehicle to blow only hot or cold air.
    * Symptoms: No air flow, only hot or cold air, system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module or system components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Nissan Rogue has some significant issues that can be costly to repair. If you're considering buying one, it's important to be aware of these problems and factor them into your decision-making process. However, it's important to note that not all Rogues will experience these issues. Some owners have reported having very few problems with their vehicles. Ultimately, the decision of whether or not to buy a Rogue is a personal one.
