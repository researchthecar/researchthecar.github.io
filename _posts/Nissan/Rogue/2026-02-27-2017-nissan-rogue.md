# 2017 Nissan Rogue Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used compact SUV, the model offers a comfortable cabin and practical layout that attracts many buyers. However, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback carefully, particularly concerning drivetrain longevity and driver-assistance features. Researching past maintenance records and checking for completed updates can help mitigate potential ownership surprises.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder

This 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces around 170 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate everyday power for daily commuting. While generally straightforward mechanically, owners have occasionally reported unexpected stalling and electrical warning lights.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may unexpectedly stall while driving or idling at a stoplight, occasionally without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the throttle body, fuel system, or electrical sensors, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### Xtronic CVT

Equipped with an Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, this setup prioritizes fuel efficiency. However, the transmission is a frequent subject of owner complaints regarding premature wear, slipping, and costly replacements.

**Transmission Slipping and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience hesitation, severe slipping, loss of drive power, or complete transmission failure accompanied by burning fluid smells.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the Continuously Variable Transmission assembly.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power under normal conditions, though some drivers note spongy pedal feel and post-recall diagnostic issues.

**Brake Failure and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Brakes may feel spongy, accompanied by unusual ringing noises or persistent ABS warning lights following service.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake booster power supply, bleed hydraulic lines, and clear or resolve diagnostic trouble codes.

## Exterior

### Door Locks and Accessories

Cabin electronics generally function well, but minor electrical issues such as failing door lock actuators have been reported by owners.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power doors may fail to lock or unlock properly when using interior switches or key fobs.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty door lock actuator assembly.

## Safety

### Forward Collision System

Safety technologies like forward collision avoidance and airbags aim to protect occupants, but multiple owners have documented unintended sensor activations and warning light illuminations.

**Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system may engage unexpectedly ('phantom braking') while driving under normal conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect collision sensors, update software modules, or recalibrate the radar and camera systems.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V663000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) number 207, "Seating Systems," and 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." The insufficient welds can increase the risk of injury to the occupants in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the seat frames and replace any defective lower seat frames, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V244000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As a result, the passenger frontal air bag may be turned off and not deploy in the event of a crash. If the passenger frontal air bag does not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, the passenger is at an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the Air Bag Control Unit (ACU) and OCS Electronic Control Unit (ECU) in Altima, Maxima, Murano, Rogue, and Sentra vehicles, and replace the OCS ECU in LEAF, NV200, NV200 Taxi, Pathfinder, Infiniti Q50, JX35, and QX60. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V839000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A blown fuse may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a harness protector cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V875000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A corroded dash side harness connector increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply grease and repair any corroded connector as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V093000: Electrical System:Ignition:Anti-Theft:Immobilizer/Proximity:Key/Sender](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Driver contact with a key in the collapsed position could inadvertently shut off the vehicle while driving, increasing the risk of a crash. Additionally, if the vehicle shuts off while driving, the air bags may not deploy in a crash as intended, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will insert a spacer into the key slot of the jackknife key, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Purchasing a used model requires careful inspection, particularly regarding the health of the continuously variable transmission and advanced safety sensors. Buyers should prioritize vehicles with comprehensive service history records and perform a thorough test drive to verify smooth powertrain and braking operation.
