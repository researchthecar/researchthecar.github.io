## 2012 Nissan Rogue

The 2012 Nissan Rogue is a compact SUV that offers a spacious interior, versatile seating configurations, and a comfortable ride. It is available with two engine options: a 2.5-liter inline-four and a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four paired to a CVT transmission. The Rogue is praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and roomy cabin but criticized for its lackluster engine power and outdated infotainment system.

**Engine**

* **2.5-liter inline-four**
    * **Oil Consumption Issue**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage. Symptoms include a burning oil smell, reduced engine performance, and oil leaks.
        * Solution: Replacing piston rings, valve seals, or the entire engine.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the timing chain, which synchronizes the timing of the engine's valves and crankshaft. Symptoms include a rattling or knocking noise from the engine, rough idling, and reduced power.
        * Solution: Replacing the timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **CVT**
    * **Hesitation and Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Lag or hesitation during acceleration, rough shifting, or slipping gears.
        * Solution: Updating the transmission control software or replacing the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, leading to low fluid levels and potential transmission damage. Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears and a burning smell.
        * Solution: Replacing gaskets or seals to stop the leak.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged strut mounts, which support the suspension struts. Symptoms include clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, reduced ride comfort, and misalignment.
    * Solution: Replacing the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drainage, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Identifying and repairing electrical system faults, such as faulty relays or parasitic drain from electronic components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Weatherstripping Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Deteriorating or torn weatherstripping around windows or doors, causing wind noise or water leaks.
    * Solution: Replacing the weatherstripping.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises or rattling coming from the dashboard, particularly over rough roads.
        * Solution: Identifying and addressing the source of the rattling, such as loose screws or improperly secured components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Door Lock Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks that fail to unlock or lock properly, leading to difficulty entering or exiting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Repairing or replacing the door lock actuators.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2012 Nissan Rogue has several potential issues, ranging from engine and transmission problems to suspension and electrical faults. The most severe issues include excessive oil consumption, timing chain failure, and transmission hesitation/shifting problems, which can be costly to repair. However, many of the other issues, such as suspension noises and interior rattles, are relatively minor and less expensive to address.
