## 2010 Nissan Rogue

The 2010 Nissan Rogue is a compact SUV that has been well-received by consumers for its spacious interior and comfortable ride. It is offered with a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine and a CVT (continuously variable transmission).

People like the Rogue for its roomy interior, comfortable seats, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, some common complaints include a lack of power from the engine, poor fuel economy, and a noisy cabin.

### Engine

* **2.5L 4-cylinder**
    * **Piston ring failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace piston rings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain rattle:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup
        * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

* **CVT (continuously variable transmission)**
    * **Slipping:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or jerking during acceleration, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace or reprogram transmission control unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Shuddering:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering felt during acceleration or deceleration
        * Solution: Replace or reprogram transmission control unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Strut mount bearing failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension, especially on rough roads
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Battery issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dim headlights, flickering dashboard lights
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, flickering dashboard lights, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Sunroof seal failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin when sunroof is closed
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Instrument cluster black-out:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The instrument cluster suddenly turns off and becomes unresponsive
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC blower motor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from the vents, or only air flow at low speeds
    * Solution: Replace blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

Overall, the 2010 Nissan Rogue is a reliable and practical SUV for its class. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the more severe problems, such as piston ring failure and CVT transmission issues, can be costly to repair. Additionally, there have been some recalls for electrical problems and sunroof leaks.
