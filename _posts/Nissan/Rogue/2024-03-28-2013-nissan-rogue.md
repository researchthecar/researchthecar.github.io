**Introduction:**

The 2013 Nissan Rogue is a compact crossover SUV praised for its spacious interior and comfortable ride. It comes equipped with a standard 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

* **2.5L Four-Cylinder**
    * **Intake Valve Deposits:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Buildup of carbon deposits on intake valves can lead to reduced engine power, rough idling, and poor fuel economy.
        * Solution: Cleaning or replacing affected intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Noise:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from the timing chain, often heard during acceleration.
        * Solution: Replacing the faulty timing chain and tensioners
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **CVT**
    * **Slipping or Jerking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or jerking while shifting gears, especially at higher speeds or under load.
        * Solution: Replacing or repairing faulty transmission parts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged front strut mounts can cause knocking or rattling noises over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing the source of the drain, which may include faulty electronics or a parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **BCM Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Body control module (BCM) malfunction can lead to various electrical issues, such as flickering lights, inoperative windows, or door locks.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty BCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cover Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The plastic rear bumper cover can crack or split at mounting points.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected bumper cover
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard rattles or creaks, especially over rough roads.
    * Solution: Identifying and addressing the source of the rattle, which may require adding damping material or tightening loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

While the 2013 Nissan Rogue offers a comfortable and spacious interior, it has been plagued by some reliability concerns, particularly with its engine and transmission. Engine issues can be costly to repair, while transmission problems can significantly impact the vehicle's drivability. Suspension and electrical components have also been problematic, along with minor interior and exterior issues.
