**Introduction to the 2011 Nissan Rogue**

The 2011 Nissan Rogue is a compact SUV powered by a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine generating 170 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque. It is available with both front-wheel drive (FWD) and all-wheel drive (AWD), and offers two transmission options: a six-speed manual and a CVT automatic. The Rogue is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, it has received some criticism for its lack of power and mediocre fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise at startup, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption between oil changes
    * Solution: Repair or replace oil rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT Automatic**

* **Slipping Transmission:**
    * Symptoms: Loss of power, hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3000 - $5000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from rear suspension, unstable handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Locate and repair electrical component causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery warning light on dash
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Hood and Roof:**
    * Symptoms: Visible rust spots on hood and roof
    * Solution: Repair or replace damaged panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sun Visor Falling Out:**
    * Symptoms: Sun visor detaches from mounting point
    * Solution: Replace sun visor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Nissan Rogue has several potential issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical components. Timing chain tensioner and clutch failures can be expensive to repair, while CVT transmission problems can be catastrophic and costly. Other issues include rear sway bar link failure, battery drain, and rust on exterior panels. Overall, the Rogue's reliability is average, and owners should be prepared for potential repairs throughout the vehicle's lifespan.
