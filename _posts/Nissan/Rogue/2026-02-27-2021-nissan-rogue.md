# 2021 Nissan Rogue Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Nissan Rogue represents a heavily modernized generation featuring updated styling, a comfortable cabin, and advanced tech features. However, buyers should approach used models with attention to powertrain performance, driver assistance behavior, and electrical accessories. Reviewing service records and conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help mitigate potential ownership surprises.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L VC-Turbo 3-Cylinder

The 1.5-liter variable-compression turbocharged three-cylinder engine generates approximately 201 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque, offering improved fuel economy for daily commuting. While peppy, early production models have drawn owner complaints regarding driveability hiccups and cooling system concerns.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden engine hesitation, severe jerking during acceleration, illumination of the check engine light, and unexpected loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning, software updates, or replacement of failing fuel and ignition system components.

**Coolant Leaks and Head Gasket Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained loss of engine coolant, engine overheating, and white smoke or rough starting indicative of internal leaks.
- **Solution:** Pressure testing the cooling system, replacing leaking hoses/components, or performing major engine repairs if gasket failure is confirmed.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired with the standard powertrain, the Xtronic CVT delivers smooth power delivery and optimizes fuel efficiency across various driving conditions. Regular fluid maintenance is essential to preserving long-term CVT longevity.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system offers confident stopping power under normal conditions, though owners have reported isolated mechanical noises and caliper concerns.

**Premature Caliper Noise and Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Loud grinding noises originating from the wheel wells during low-speed braking, or premature wear on brake calipers.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers; replace seized or worn components as necessary.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Assist

The electric power steering system provides light, maneuverable handling at low speeds and solid highway feedback, though electronic driver aids can occasionally behave erratically.

**Steering Assist Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Driver assistance systems unexpectedly take control of the steering wheel, pulling sharply to one side or temporarily locking steering movement.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering gear, software calibration, and electronic sensors for faults.

## Electrical

### Door Locks and Infotainment

Electrical accessories, including power locks and infotainment screens, generally function well but have prompted occasional complaints regarding component wear and freezing displays.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door locks fail to respond to key fobs or interior switches, or lock and unlock randomly while driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuators.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance occupant protection, but sensor calibration issues can occasionally result in ghost braking or warning lights.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance system triggers false positive warnings and sudden unexpected braking events while driving.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas, check windshield alignment around camera mounts, and update system software at a dealership.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V957000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Abnormal wear inside the fuel pump may cause it to overheat and fail. Fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V286000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A loss of brake fluid may reduce braking performance, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the left and/or right rear brake calipers, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V186000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The wheel vibration may break the wheel studs and cause the wheel assembly to separate from the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the wheel nuts and replace any incorrect ones, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V068000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The fuel hose may not be secured in the proper position and the fuel tank lock ring may not be fully seated. An improperly secured fuel hose may leak, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, as necessary, reposition and secure the fuel hose and lock ring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V474000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." An insecurely anchored child seat can move during a sudden stop or crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rear seatbacks as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V527000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When the Sirius XM subscription is set to "Not Subscribed," it may cause the infotainment head unit to reboot, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces the driver's rear.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V666000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear seat belts for the left-hand and right-hand rear seats may not retract properly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 208, "Occupant Crash Protection," and 209, "Seatbelt Assemblies." Seat belts that do not retract properly may be difficult to operate, preventing their usage.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rear seat belts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V772000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Rogue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Nissan Rogue is a well-equipped compact SUV with modern styling and strong safety features, making it an attractive used option. Buyers should carefully evaluate potential issues concerning engine driveability, door lock actuators, and advanced driver assistance sensor behavior during a test drive. Verifying complete maintenance records and ensuring all applicable updates have been performed will help secure a reliable purchase.
