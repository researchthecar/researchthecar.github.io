**Introduction to the 2009 Nissan Rogue**

The 2009 Nissan Rogue is a compact SUV that is available with two engine options: a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine and a 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine with dual overhead camshafts (DOHC). Both engines are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

The Rogue has received positive reviews for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and good fuel economy. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine**

* **Excessive oil consumption:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's engine consumes excessive amounts of oil. This issue can lead to premature engine failure if not addressed.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain noise:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's engine makes a rattling noise when starting or accelerating. This issue is caused by a faulty timing chain.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve cover gasket leak:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's valve cover gasket leaks oil. This issue can cause oil to leak onto the engine, leading to a potential fire hazard.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously variable transmission (CVT)**

* **Premature failure:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's CVT has failed prematurely. This issue can occur due to a variety of factors, including manufacturing defects, improper maintenance, and overloading.
    * Solution: Replace the CVT.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shuddering:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's CVT shudders when shifting gears. This issue is caused by a faulty valve body.
    * Solution: Replace the valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Slipping:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's CVT slips when accelerating. This issue is caused by a worn or damaged belt.
    * Solution: Replace the belt.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Faulty ignition coil:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's ignition coil fails prematurely. This issue can cause the engine to misfire or stall.
    * Solution: Replace the ignition coil.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty alternator:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's alternator fails prematurely. This issue can cause the battery to drain and the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on undercarriage:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's undercarriage rusts prematurely. This issue can weaken the undercarriage and lead to structural damage.
    * Solution: Rustproof the undercarriage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Squeaky seats:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's seats squeak when they are adjusted or when the vehicle is in motion. This issue is caused by a loose or damaged spring.
    * Solution: Lubricate the spring or replace it if it is damaged.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cracked dashboard:** Some owners have reported that their Rogue's dashboard cracks prematurely. This issue is caused by exposure to sunlight or extreme temperatures.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2009 Nissan Rogue is a reliable SUV that offers a comfortable ride and good fuel economy. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components. These issues can range in severity from minor annoyances to major problems that can lead to expensive repairs. It is important to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a used Rogue.
