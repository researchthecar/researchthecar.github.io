**Introduction:**

The 2018 Nissan Rogue is a compact crossover SUV available with two engine options: a 2.5L four-cylinder producing 170 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque and a 2.0L four-cylinder turbocharged engine producing 225 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a continuously variable transmission (CVT) and a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Rogue is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and user-friendly technology. However, it has also received some criticism for its lackluster performance and uninspiring handling.

**Engine:**

* **2.5L Four-Cylinder**
    * **Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and oil seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * **Timing Chain Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain, leading to engine noise, reduced power, and potential engine damage
        * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **2.0L Four-Cylinder Turbocharged**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning or premature failure of the turbocharger, resulting in reduced engine power and performance
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger and related components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Transmission:**

* **CVT**
    * **Slipping or Shuddering:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips or shudders during gear changes, causing rough acceleration and loss of power
        * Solution: Replace transmission fluid or reprogram transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * **Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Complete transmission failure, causing loss of power and inability to drive
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears
        * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000
    * **Vibration:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive vibration during acceleration or deceleration
        * Solution: Replace torque converter or transmission mount
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system components causing the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
* **BCM Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning body control module (BCM) causing electrical system issues, such as flickering lights, inoperative electronics, or starting problems
    * Solution: Replace BCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Exterior:**

* **Rust on Liftgate:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust formation on the liftgate, particularly around the license plate area
    * Solution: Repair and repaint the liftgate or replace it
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Interior:**

* **Cracked Dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the plastic dashboard, especially near the air vents or passenger side airbag
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning touchscreen, software glitches, or connectivity problems
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment system components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+

**Summary:**

The 2018 Nissan Rogue is a generally reliable vehicle with a spacious interior and comfortable ride. However, it has experienced some significant issues, including oil consumption, timing chain failure, transmission problems, and electrical issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be expensive to repair. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a 2018 Rogue.
