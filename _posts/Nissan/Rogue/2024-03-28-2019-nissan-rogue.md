## 2019 Nissan Rogue

The 2019 Nissan Rogue is a compact crossover SUV that offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and a variety of advanced features. It is available with a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine or a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine, both of which are paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Rogue has received positive reviews for its handling, fuel efficiency, and safety features, but some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.5-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Oil consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
* **Symptoms:** Loss of oil between oil changes, visible smoke from the exhaust, engine knocking or rattling.
* **Solution:** Replace faulty piston rings or valve stem seals.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** A faulty turbocharger can lead to a loss of power and reduced performance.
* **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whistling or rattling noises from the turbocharger.
* **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Shuddering or slipping:** Some owners have reported shuddering or slipping while driving, especially during acceleration.
* **Symptoms:** Vibrations or shaking while driving, hesitation or slipping during acceleration.
* **Solution:** Update transmission software or replace faulty CVT components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaking strut mounts:** The rubber mounts on the struts can fail, causing a clunking or rattling noise when driving.
* **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noise over bumps, uneven tire wear.
* **Solution:** Replace the faulty strut mounts.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some owners have reported excessive battery drain, leading to a dead battery or starting issues.
* **Symptoms:** Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair electrical faults or replace a faulty battery.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the cause of the drain
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trim peeling:** The chrome or paint on the exterior trim pieces can peel or flake off.
* **Symptoms:** Peeling or flaking chrome or paint on the trim.
* **Solution:** Replace the affected trim pieces.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control issues:** The climate control system may malfunction, leading to temperature inconsistencies or a lack of air conditioning.
* **Symptoms:** Inconsistent cabin temperature, lack of air flow, or malfunctioning climate control buttons.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair electrical or mechanical faults in the climate control system.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the issue and repairs needed
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Nissan Rogue has received mixed reviews from owners, with some reporting significant issues while others have experienced few problems. The most common issues reported are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can be substantial. However, it is important to note that not all Rogues will experience these issues, and many owners have had positive experiences with the vehicle.
