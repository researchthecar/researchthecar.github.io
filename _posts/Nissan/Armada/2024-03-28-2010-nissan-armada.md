**Introduction**

The 2010 Nissan Armada is a full-size SUV that offers a spacious and comfortable interior, a robust engine, and impressive towing capabilities. It's powered by a 5.6L V8 engine that produces 317 horsepower and 385 lb-ft of torque. The Armada comes standard with a 5-speed automatic transmission.

Critics praise the Armada for its powerful engine, roomy cabin, and advanced safety features. However, some common issues have been reported by owners, which are detailed below.

## **Engine**

5.6L V8 Engine

- **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating
  - Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Valve Body Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, transmission slipping, loss of power
  - Solution: Replace valve body
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**

5-Speed Automatic Transmission

- **Solenoid Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, delayed shifting
  - Solution: Replace failed solenoids
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

- **Front Strut Mount Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, reduced handling
  - Solution: Replace strut mounts
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**

- **Battery Drain**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting
  - Solution: Diagnose and fix electrical drain source
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

- **Tailgate Corrosion**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on tailgate
  - Solution: Replace or repair tailgate
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

- **Cracked Dashboard**
  - Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard surface
  - Solution: Replace dashboard
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

While the 2010 Nissan Armada offers a capable engine and spacious cabin, it has been plagued by several significant issues in its engine, transmission, suspension, and exterior components. Some of these issues can be costly to repair and can significantly impact the vehicle's performance and reliability.
