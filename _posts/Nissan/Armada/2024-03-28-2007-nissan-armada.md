**2007 Nissan Armada**

The 2007 Nissan Armada is a full-size SUV that offers a spacious interior, powerful engine, and advanced features. It's available with two engine options: a 5.6-liter V8 that produces 317 horsepower and 385 lb-ft of torque and a 4.7-liter V8 that produces 261 horsepower and 324 lb-ft of torque. The 5.6-liter V8 comes with a 5-speed automatic transmission, while the 4.7-liter V8 is paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**5.6-liter V8**

* **Exhaust manifold cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust manifold cracks can cause a variety of issues, including a burning smell, reduced engine power, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: The cracked exhaust manifold must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **VVEL system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The VVEL system is responsible for controlling the timing of the intake valves. If it fails, it can cause the engine to run rough, stall, or lose power.
    * Solution: The VVEL system components must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.7-liter V8**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip when shifting gears, causing the vehicle to lose power or hesitate during acceleration.
    * Solution: The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear suspension springs breaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension springs can break, causing the rear of the vehicle to sag and the ride quality to deteriorate.
    * Solution: The broken springs must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: The cause of the battery drain must be diagnosed and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear liftgate handle failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear liftgate handle may fail, making it difficult or impossible to open the liftgate.
    * Solution: The liftgate handle must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may fail to regulate temperature or airflow.
    * Solution: The climate control module or other components must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat heater failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat heater may not work, even when activated.
    * Solution: The seat heater element or other components must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Nissan Armada has a number of potential issues that can affect its reliability. The most severe and costly issues include exhaust manifold cracking, transmission slipping, and rear suspension spring breakage. However, many of the issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable cost. Overall, the Armada is a reliable SUV that offers a good value for its price.
