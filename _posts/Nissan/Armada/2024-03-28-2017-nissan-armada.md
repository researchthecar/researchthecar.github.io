## The 2017 Nissan Armada: A Comprehensive List of Issues

The 2017 Nissan Armada is a full-size SUV that comes with a 5.6-liter V8 engine that produces 390 horsepower and 394 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Armada is known for its spacious cabin, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. However, it has also been plagued by a number of issues that have affected different components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **5.6L V8**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner may fail, leading to a loss of engine power and potential catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and inspect the timing chain for damage.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:** Some owners have experienced rough or delayed shifting, as well as slipping gears.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module (TCM) or replace the transmission solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:** The rear suspension can develop a clunking or rattling noise, particularly over rough roads.
        * Solution: Replace the rear suspension bushings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Sway Bar Link Failure:** The front sway bar links may fail, causing the vehicle to handle poorly and make a clicking or clunking noise when turning.
        * Solution: Replace the front sway bar links.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported a parasitic battery drain that can lead to a dead battery.
        * Solution: Inspect the electrical system for a faulty component, such as a faulty alternator or ignition switch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction:** The headlights may malfunction, causing them to flicker, dim, or fail completely.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly or repair the electrical wiring.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:** The exterior door handles may become stiff or break, making it difficult or impossible to open the doors.
        * Solution: Replace the door handles.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Liftgate Lock Failure:** The liftgate lock may fail, preventing the liftgate from opening or closing properly.
        * Solution: Replace the liftgate lock assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in poor heating or cooling performance.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module or blend door actuator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Nissan Armada has a mixed reputation. While it offers a comfortable and spacious cabin, as well as a powerful engine, it has also been plagued by a number of issues that affect various components of the vehicle. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Some issues, such as oil consumption and timing chain tensioner failure, can be costly and require major repairs. Other issues, such as exterior door handle failure and interior climate control malfunction, are less severe and less expensive to fix.
