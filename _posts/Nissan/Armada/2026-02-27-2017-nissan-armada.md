# 2017 Nissan Armada Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Nissan Armada offers robust full-size SUV capability and a strong V8 engine, but potential buyers should review common owner complaints before purchasing. While many owners enjoy its towing power and spacious interior, recurring issues involving electrical drains, brake pedal switch failures, and instrument cluster light malfunctions have been reported by drivers.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter Endurance V8 engine produces 390 horsepower and 394 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration and towing capability for this full-size SUV. The engine block and mechanical core generally prove durable, though minor accessory and electrical complaints occasionally surface.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed automatic transmission routes power to the rear wheels or available four-wheel-drive systems. While generally capable, some owners report rough shifting behavior and hesitation at lower speeds.

**Premature Shifting and Power Lag**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may shift into lower gears too early while driving at low speeds or going uphill, causing rpm fluctuation, delayed acceleration, or a momentary loss of power.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission fluid condition and check for updated powertrain control module software calibrations.

## Brakes

### Brake Pedal Switch

The brake system relies on electronic switches and sensors that can fail prematurely, affecting both brake light operation and vehicle starting capability.

**Brake Light Switch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Failure of the brake pedal switch can prevent the rear brake lights from illuminating and may cause push-button start systems to not recognize when the brake pedal is depressed, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty brake switch component with an updated replacement part.

## Electrical

### Instrument Panel and Dashboard Lighting

electrical issues in the cabin are among the more frequent owner grievances, particularly regarding dashboard illumination and intermittent parasitic battery drains.

**Dashboard and Speedometer Light Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sections of the instrument panel lights or speedometer backlighting blink intermittently or go dark entirely while driving, making it difficult to read gauges at night.
- **Solution:** Inspect instrument cluster wiring connections and replace failing LED modules or the instrument cluster assembly if necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Collision Warning Systems

Advanced safety and supplemental restraint systems generally function as intended, though occasional sensor faults can trigger warning lights on the dash.

**Air Bag Warning Light and BCI Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Side airbag warning lights may flash continuously, or the Blind Collision Intervention (BCI) system may display malfunction warnings that disable driver assistance features.
- **Solution:** Inspect side airbag sensor wiring harnesses and diagnostic trouble codes related to the collision avoidance modules.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Nissan Armada is a powerful and spacious used SUV option, but buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical accessories, brake switch operation, and transmission shift quality during a test drive. Verifying that all dashboard lights, safety sensors, and starting functions operate correctly can help avoid unexpected repair visits after purchase.
