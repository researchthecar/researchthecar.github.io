**Introduction:**
The 2023 Nissan Armada is a full-size SUV available with a single 5.6-liter V8 engine and a 7-speed automatic transmission. It boasts 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. The Armada is praised for its spaciousness, powerful engine, and comfortable interior. However, it has received some criticism for its poor fuel economy and handling.

**Interior:**

**Missing Seat Adjustment Mechanism**
- Description/Symptoms: Some models may have an issue with the seat adjustment mechanism. The passenger seat may not be able to move forward or backward, making it difficult to access the second row.
- Solution: Replace the faulty seat adjustment mechanism.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500
- Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2023 Nissan Armada has a limited number of reported issues. The only significant issue seems to be the potential for a faulty seat adjustment mechanism on the passenger seat. The estimated cost to fix this issue is relatively low, so it does not pose a significant concern.
