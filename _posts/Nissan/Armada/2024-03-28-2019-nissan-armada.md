**Introduction:**

The 2019 Nissan Armada is a full-size SUV known for its spaciousness, towing capabilities, and off-road performance. It is equipped with a standard 5.6L V8 engine paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. The Armada offers a comfortable and well-equipped interior with premium features, along with a rugged exterior that complements its off-road capabilities. However, the vehicle has received some reports of issues in various components.

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some vehicles may experience excessive battery drain, leaving the battery depleted and preventing the vehicle from starting.
    * Solution: The issue can be resolved by replacing the faulty battery or repairing any electrical components causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Certain electrical systems may malfunction, such as the headlights, taillights, or dashboard controls.
    * Solution: The affected electrical components need to be diagnosed and replaced or repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V507: Passenger Airbag Module Malfunction

**Exterior**

* **Exterior Trim Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some vehicles have reported issues with the exterior trim, such as loose or rattling trim pieces.
    * Solution: The affected trim pieces need to be inspected and replaced or tightened.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches or malfunctions, including freezing, displaying error messages, or losing connectivity.
    * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacements may be necessary to address the issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the reported issues for the 2019 Nissan Armada are relatively minor and do not seem to be widespread. The electrical and exterior issues are the most common, followed by the infotainment system issues. The estimated repair costs for these issues vary, but most are within a reasonable range.
