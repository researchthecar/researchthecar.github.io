**Introduction**

The 2022 Nissan Armada is a full-size SUV that seats up to eight passengers. It comes standard with a 5.6-liter V8 engine that produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. A seven-speed automatic transmission is the only transmission option. The Armada is available in four trim levels: S, SV, SL, and Platinum.

People like the Armada for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and comfortable ride. However, some people have reported having issues with the vehicle's engine, suspension, electrical system, and exterior.

**Engine**

* **5.6L V8**

**Engine Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The engine may experience catastrophic failure due to a faulty variable valve timing system. Symptoms include a sudden loss of power, a rattling noise from the engine, and illuminated check engine light.
Solution: Replace the variable valve timing system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience premature failure due to a faulty torque converter. Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, delayed engagement, and slipping gears.
Solution: Replace the torque converter and transmission fluid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Electrical System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may experience failure due to a faulty wiring harness. Symptoms include flickering lights, power outages, and inoperability of various electrical components.
Solution: Replace the wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

**Rust**
Description/Symptoms: The exterior body panels may develop rust due to inadequate corrosion protection. Symptoms include visible rust spots on the paint surface.
Solution: Repair or replace the affected panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

**Sunroof Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak water into the cabin due to a faulty seal around the sunroof frame. Symptoms include water dripping from the headliner or interior trim.
Solution: Replace the sunroof seal.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Nissan Armada is a reliable vehicle, but it is not immune to issues. The most serious issues reported by owners include engine failure, transmission failure, and electrical system failure. These issues can be expensive to fix, but they are relatively rare. The Armada's interior and suspension have received positive feedback, while the exterior has received some criticism due to rust issues.
