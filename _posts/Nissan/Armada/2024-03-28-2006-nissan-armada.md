**2006 Nissan Armada**

The 2006 Nissan Armada is a full-size SUV offered with a choice of a 5.6-liter V8 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It delivers 317 horsepower and 385 lb-ft of torque. The Armada is known for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and comfortable ride. However, it has also been plagued by some reported issues.

**Engine**

**5.6L V8**

* **VVEL System Failure:** Loss of power, rough idling, or stalling. Requires replacement of the VVEL system, costing around $1,500 - $2,500+.
* **Timing Chain Rattle:** Noise from the engine while running. May require replacement of the timing chain, costing around $700 - $1,500.

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:** Delayed or erratic shifting, slipping gears. May require repair or replacement of the transmission, costing around $2,000 - $4,000+.

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:** Clunking or squeaking sounds from the front suspension. May require replacement of the strut mounts, costing around $400 - $800+.
* **Rear Axle Leak:** Leakage of fluid from the rear axle. May require replacement of the axle seal or gasket, costing around $150 - $300.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains quickly even when the vehicle is parked. May require replacement of the alternator or battery, costing around $300 - $700+.
* **Electrical System Failure:** Various electrical components may malfunction, including lights, gauges, or the stereo. May require diagnosis and repair of the electrical system, costing around $200 - $1,000+.

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Gaps:** Uneven gaps between body panels. May require adjustment or replacement of the panels, costing around $500 - $2,000+.

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Climate control system fails to regulate temperature. May require replacement of the climate control module or blend door, costing around $500 - $1,500+.
* **Rear Seat Release Failure:** Inability to release the rear seats from the front. May require replacement of the release cable or latch, costing around $200 - $500+.

**Summary**

The 2006 Nissan Armada has faced some reported issues, particularly with its engine and transmission systems. These issues can range in severity and cost, with some requiring expensive repairs. However, it's important to note that not all Armadas will experience these problems, and many owners have been satisfied with their vehicles.
