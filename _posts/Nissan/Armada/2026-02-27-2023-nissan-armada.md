# 2023 Nissan Armada Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Nissan Armada is a full-size, three-row SUV known for its robust traditional truck-based capability and strong V8 performance. While it offers a comfortable cabin and serious towing power, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding occasional electrical glitches and unexpected body glass breakage. As with any used large SUV, a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains a smart step before bringing one home.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter Endurance V8 engine produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration and towing capabilities. It is a traditional, naturally aspirated powerplant generally known for dependable mechanical operation.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with either rear-wheel drive or four-wheel drive, the 7-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth shifts suited for highway cruising and heavy hauling. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Onboard electronics power a robust suite of convenience and safety features, though occasional isolated electrical glitches have been reported by drivers.

**Sudden Dashboard and Infotainment Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unexpected electrical anomalies where the dashboard and infotainment screens go completely dark while the vehicle is running and being shifted into park.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician check the battery connections, ground points, and body control modules for intermittent software or wiring faults.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body glass and panoramic features generally hold up well, though isolated glass breakage reports warrant a careful visual check during a used vehicle inspection.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the sunroof or moonroof glass suddenly shatters or explodes while parked, often producing a loud noise resembling a gunshot without any apparent external impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass closely for any pre-existing stress fractures and replace the damaged glass assembly if failure occurs.

### Liftgate Window

The rear hatch and associated windows provide standard utility, though rare spontaneous glass failures have been documented by vehicle owners.

**Rear Hatch Window Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced the rear hatch window unexpectedly shattering overnight or while parked without evidence of vandalism or physical impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered rear hatch window glass and check the window frame alignment and seals for abnormal pressure points.

- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." An overloaded vehicle can increase the risk of a crash.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Nissan Armada delivers formidable V8 performance and family-hauling utility, making it an appealing choice for used-car buyers seeking traditional SUV capability. While scattered reports highlight rare concerns regarding spontaneous glass breakage and isolated electrical anomalies, the vehicle remains generally solid. Buyers should perform a careful physical inspection of all glass surfaces and electronics to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
