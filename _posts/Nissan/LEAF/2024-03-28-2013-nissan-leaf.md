## 2013 Nissan LEAF

The 2013 Nissan LEAF is a compact electric car. It is powered by a single electric motor that produces 107 horsepower and 187 pound-feet of torque. The LEAF has a range of up to 107 miles on a single charge. It is praised for its smooth ride, ample cargo space, and advanced safety features. However, it is criticized for its short range, slow acceleration, and lack of all-wheel drive.

**Electric Motor**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** The strut mount bearing can fail, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps.
    * **Solution:** Replace the strut mount bearing.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Rear Suspension**

* **Trailing Arm Bushing Failure:** The trailing arm bushing can fail, causing a knocking noise when driving over bumps.
    * **Solution:** Replace the trailing arm bushing.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Electrical System**

* **Battery Degradation:** The battery can degrade over time, reducing the range of the vehicle.
    * **Solution:** Replace the battery.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $10,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**HVAC System**

* **Climate Control Module Failure:** The climate control module can fail, causing the HVAC system to malfunction.
    * **Solution:** Replace the climate control module.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Body Panels**

* **Loose Body Panels:** The body panels can become loose, causing wind noise and water leaks.
    * **Solution:** Tighten the body panels.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard**

* **Dashboard Rattles:** The dashboard can rattle, especially when driving over rough roads.
    * **Solution:** Secure the dashboard.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Seats**

* **Seat Covers Ripping:** The seat covers can rip, exposing the foam underneath.
    * **Solution:** Replace the seat covers.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Nissan LEAF is a reliable electric car with a few minor issues. The most common issues are related to the suspension and electrical system. Overall, the LEAF is a good choice for those looking for an affordable and environmentally friendly vehicle. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
