# 2017 Nissan LEAF Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Nissan LEAF is a popular, fully electric hatchback known for its efficiency and practicality as a daily commuter. While all-electric vehicles generally feature fewer moving parts than traditional gas-powered cars, prospective buyers should carefully review battery health, charging equipment, and ancillary systems when shopping in the used market. Being informed about how these electric drivetrains perform over time helps ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 80 kW AC Synchronous Electric Motor

Powered by an 80 kW AC synchronous electric motor producing 107 horsepower and 187 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain option provides punchy low-end acceleration and reliable, low-maintenance performance for city driving.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Direct Drive

Equipped with a single-speed fixed gear direct drive automatic transmission. This setup eliminates traditional gear shifting entirely, offering a smooth power delivery and high mechanical durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V253000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=LEAF&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing error within the air bag inflators, the passenger frontal air bag may not properly deploy in the event of a crash. In the event of a crash, if the front passenger air bag doesn't deploy properly, it can increase the risk of injury to passengers.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger air bag inflator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V296000: Equipment:Other:Owners/Service/Other Manual](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=LEAF&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 103, "Windshield Defrosting and Defogging Systems." Reduced defroster performance can limit visibility out of the windshield, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Nissan LEAF offers a straightforward and efficient electric driving experience with very few traditional mechanical complications. Buyers should prioritize checking the remaining battery capacity and ensuring all charging components function properly. Overall, it remains a sensible and economical choice for a used electric vehicle if the driving range fits your daily routine.
