### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Nissan Titan demonstrates solid mechanical reliability, particularly with its standard 5.6L V8 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission, which show no widespread reported issues. Suspension and exterior components are also generally robust. The primary areas of concern are within the electrical system, specifically intermittent infotainment glitches and potential premature battery drain, which can lead to starting problems. While these electrical issues are not endemic, they represent the most common non-wear-and-tear problems reported by owners. Overall, the Titan is a dependable truck, but owners should be aware of potential electrical system quirks and budget accordingly for any necessary repairs.
