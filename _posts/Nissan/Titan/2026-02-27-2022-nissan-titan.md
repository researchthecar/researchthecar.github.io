# 2022 Nissan Titan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Nissan Titan offers a straightforward ownership proposition anchored by a standard V8 engine and a nine-speed automatic transmission. As a modern used full-size truck, buyers generally experience traditional mechanical reliability, though prospective owners should still evaluate overall vehicle condition and check for any lingering component bulletins during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter Endurance V8 engine produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, providing robust towing and hauling capability. This powerplant is generally dependable, serving as the sole engine option across all trim levels for this model year.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a nine-speed automatic transmission paired to the V8 engine, this unit delivers smooth power delivery across both rear-wheel drive and four-wheel drive configurations. It provides reliable everyday shifting performance when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V457000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Titan&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The transmission parking pawl may not engage when the vehicle is shifted into park, which can result in a vehicle rollaway. A vehicle rollaway increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the transmission parking pawl pin, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V527000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Titan&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** When the Sirius XM subscription is set to "Not Subscribed," it may cause the infotainment head unit to reboot, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces the driver's rear.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Nissan Titan is a solid contender in the full-size truck market, benefiting from a standard V8 powertrain and conventional mechanical setups. Used-car buyers should perform a thorough test drive and mechanical inspection to ensure everything operates smoothly, but the truck generally lacks widespread, severe complaint trends.
