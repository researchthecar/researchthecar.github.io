**Introduction:**

The 2007 Nissan Titan is a full-size pickup truck powered by a 5.6-liter V8 engine mated to either a five-speed automatic transmission or a six-speed manual transmission. It features a spacious cabin, a capable suspension system, and a sturdy exterior design. Users praise the Titan for its towing and payload capabilities, but some have experienced concerns with certain components.

**Engine**

**5.6L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, engine overheating, possible engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace the crankshaft position sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive rattling noise coming from the engine, especially at startup.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain, tensioner, and guides.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Automatic:**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration felt while driving, especially at low speeds.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Hard Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts roughly or hesitates.
    * Solution: Flush the transmission fluid and replace the filter. In severe cases, the transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Manual:**

* **Difficulty Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission gears grind when shifting, especially when cold.
    * Solution: Replace the synchronizers in the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Sag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear springs lose tension, causing the vehicle to sag.
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the front suspension, especially when turning.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery light on, dim headlights, difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely even with no accessories on.
    * Solution: Inspect the electrical system for parasitic drain. Common culprits include the radio, lights, or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on the rear bumper.
    * Solution: Replace the rear bumper or have it repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Cable Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate does not open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate cable.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appear on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard or have it repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Heater Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters do not work or overheat.
    * Solution: Replace the seat heater element or wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Nissan Titan has received mixed reviews from owners. Some have reported significant issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical system, while others have had relatively trouble-free experiences. The severity and cost of these issues can vary widely depending on the specific problem and the condition of the vehicle.
