# 2023 Nissan Titan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Nissan Titan offers a capable full-size truck experience anchored by a standard V8 engine and a nine-speed automatic transmission. As a relatively recent model year, owner feedback remains sparse, but prospective buyers should still weigh standard pre-owned inspection practices. While current owner complaints are minimal, understanding the powertrain layout and standard inspection points will help ensure a confident used-truck purchase.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Engine

The 5.6-liter Endurance V8 engine produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, providing strong towing and acceleration capabilities. This powerplant serves as the sole engine option for the lineup, offering robust performance for daily driving and work duties.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The nine-speed automatic transmission pairs with the V8 engine across all trims, delivering smooth power delivery and optimized highway cruising. It provides reliable operation under normal driving conditions when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V671000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Titan&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The transmission parking pawl may not engage when the vehicle is shifted into park, which can result in a vehicle rollaway. A vehicle rollaway increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will perform the applicable repairs below, free of charge:  Model Year 2020-2022 Frontier vehicles: reprogram Transmission Control Module (TCM)  Model Year 2020-2022 Titan vehicles: reprogram TCM and Engine Control Module (ECM) . Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V273000: Tires:Bead](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Titan&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A rapid loss of tire pressure while driving can lead to a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace all four road tires, or inspect and replace the spare tire assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Nissan Titan presents a straightforward mechanical package with its robust V8 and modern nine-speed automatic transmission. Because available owner data is limited, buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on fluid conditions, electronics, and overall mechanical health before finalizing a deal.
