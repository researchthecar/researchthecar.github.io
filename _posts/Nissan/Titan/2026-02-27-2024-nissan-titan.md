# 2024 Nissan TITAN Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Nissan Titan serves as a straightforward, full-size pickup truck option for buyers looking for standard V8 capability without navigating overly complex turbocharged lineups. While owner complaints in early production periods are minimal, used-car buyers should still evaluate standard maintenance history and inspect potential wear points closely before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 5.6L V8 Endurance Engine

The 5.6-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine produces 400 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, providing strong towing capability and confident acceleration. It is a proven, traditional powerplant known for straightforward maintenance, though buyers should verify regular oil and fluid changes.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with the V8 engine, this 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear transitions and optimizes highway efficiency. It offers reliable operation across rear-wheel and four-wheel-drive configurations when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=TITAN&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing issue, the driver's air bag inflator may absorb moisture, and cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that deploys improperly may not protect the driver as intended during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front driver's air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V580000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=TITAN&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured ACU can prevent or delay air bags from deploying as intended, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ACU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Nissan Titan offers a robust and dependable mechanical package built around a proven V8 engine and multi-speed automatic transmission. Prospective used-car buyers can approach this model year with general confidence, provided they perform a standard pre-purchase inspection and verify the vehicle's service history.
