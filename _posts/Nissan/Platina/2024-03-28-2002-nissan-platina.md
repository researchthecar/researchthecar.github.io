**Introduction:**

The 2002 Nissan Platina is a compact sedan offered with two engine options: a 1.6-liter I4 and a 1.8-liter I4. Both engines are paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Platina is known for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and spacious interior. However, like many vehicles, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **1.6-liter I4 Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the engine, overheating, engine misfires
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or refusing to start, lack of power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
* **1.8-liter I4 Engine**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **4-speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, harsh shifting
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration felt when accelerating at low speeds
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noise over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Rear Sway Bar Links**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking noise when driving over rough roads
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery warning light, electrical issues
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Plastic Bumper Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the plastic bumpers
    * Solution: Replace bumper
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ depending on labor costs

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noise from the dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten loose screws or apply sound-dampening material
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Air Conditioning Blower Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing air, noise from the blower
    * Solution: Replace blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary:**

The 2002 Nissan Platina has some common issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of repairs vary depending on the specific issue. Overall, Platina owners should be prepared for potential repair costs, but many of the issues can be addressed with timely maintenance and repairs.
