**2003 Nissan Platina**

The 2003 Nissan Platina is a compact sedan offered with two engine options: a 1.6-liter 4-cylinder and a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

The Platina has received generally positive reviews for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and fuel efficiency. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, especially with the base engine, and its dated design.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter 4-cylinder**

* **Timing chain issues:** Occurs when plastic guides that guide the timing chain wear down and break, causing the chain to skip or break, potentially leading to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and guides.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Leaks coolant and vacuum, causing overheating and poor performance.
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**2.0-liter 4-cylinder**

* **No significant issues found.**

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

* **No significant issues found.**

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Slippage between gears:** Transmission may fail to shift properly, slipping between gears or refusing to engage.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure:** Worn strut mounts cause excessive noise and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** Alternator fails to charge battery, leading to electrical system problems.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
* **Power window motor failure:** Power window motor fails, preventing windows from being raised or lowered.
    * Solution: Replace power window motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** Headlights fill with condensation, impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**

* **Door lock actuator failure:** Door lock actuator fails, preventing doors from being locked or unlocked.
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
* **Climate control issues:** Climate control system may fail to blow air, adjust temperature, or defrost windows.
    * Solution: Varies depending on the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2003 Nissan Platina has some common issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. While some repairs can be relatively inexpensive, more significant issues such as timing chain failure or transmission rebuilds can be costly. Overall, the Platina is a reliable vehicle, but owners should be aware of these potential problems.
