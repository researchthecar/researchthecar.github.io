### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**CVT Fluid Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation during acceleration, whining noises, potential shuddering.
- **Solution:** Regular CVT fluid changes according to manufacturer recommendations, potential CVT unit replacement if severe damage occurs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 (fluid change), $2,000 - $4,000 (replacement)

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Nissan March demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly with its engine options. The primary area to monitor is the CVT transmission, where fluid maintenance is crucial to prevent premature wear and potential costly repairs. Other systems, including the suspension, electrical components, exterior, and interior, generally perform without significant reported issues. For owners, adhering to the recommended maintenance schedule, especially for the CVT fluid, will be key to ensuring long-term dependability and minimizing unexpected expenses. The March remains a sensible choice for budget-conscious drivers seeking a reliable city car.
