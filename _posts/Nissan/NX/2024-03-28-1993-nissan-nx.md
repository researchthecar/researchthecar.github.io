**Introduction**

The 1993 Nissan NX is a compact SUV that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 2.0-liter four-cylinder that produced 116 horsepower and 124 lb-ft of torque, while the optional engine was a 3.0-liter V6 that produced 160 horsepower and 183 lb-ft of torque. The NX was available with either a five-speed manual transmission or a four-speed automatic transmission.

The NX was praised for its sporty handling, comfortable ride, and spacious interior. However, it was also criticized for its lack of power, especially with the base engine, and its poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Four-Cylinder**
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning oil smell and potential engine damage if left unchecked.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Belt Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing belt tensioner can fail, causing the timing belt to skip or break, leading to catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing belt tensioner and timing belt.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.0L V6**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak from the intake manifold gasket, leading to overheating and potential engine damage if left unchecked.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Rear Main Seal Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the rear main seal, causing oil to drip onto the ground and potential engine damage if left unchecked.
        * Solution: Replace the rear main seal.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Five-Speed Manual**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **Four-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leak from the transmission pan gasket or a cracked case, leading to loss of hydraulic pressure and potential transmission damage.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission pan gasket or repair the cracked case.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque Converter Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The torque converter clutch can fail, causing shuddering or slipping while shifting.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts/Shocks Worn**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn front struts or shocks can lead to a rough ride, poor handling, and premature tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the front struts or shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Springs Sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear leaf springs can lead to a lower ride height, reduced load carrying capacity, and premature tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the rear leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator can fail, causing the battery to drain and the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The starter can fail, preventing the engine from starting.
    * Solution: Replace the starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Front and Rear Bumper Fade** 
    * Description/Symptoms: The front and rear bumpers can fade or discolor over time due to sun exposure.
    * Solution: Sand and repaint the bumpers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Plastic Trim Pieces Brittleness** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Plastic trim pieces can become brittle and crack over time due to sun and heat exposure.
    * Solution: Replace the cracked trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking** 
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard can crack or fade over time due to sun exposure.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard or use a dashboard cover.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Fabric Pilling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat fabric can pill or develop fuzz balls over time due to friction and wear.
    * Solution: Clean the fabric with a lint roller or brush.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1993 Nissan NX is a reliable and affordable compact SUV. However, it is not without its problems. The most common issues include valve cover gasket leaks, timing belt tensioner failures, intake manifold gasket leaks, transmission fluid leaks, and worn suspension components. The cost to fix these issues can range from a few hundred dollars to over a thousand dollars.
