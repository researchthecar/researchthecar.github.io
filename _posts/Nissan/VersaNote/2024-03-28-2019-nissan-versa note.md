**Introduction**

The 2019 Nissan Versa Note is a compact hatchback that offers a balance of practicality, fuel efficiency, and affordability. It comes with a standard 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine and a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Other engine and transmission options are not available. The Versa Note has received positive reviews for its spacious interior, comfortable seats, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, poor handling, and unrefined interior.
