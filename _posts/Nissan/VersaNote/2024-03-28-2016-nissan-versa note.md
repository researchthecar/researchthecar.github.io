**Introduction:**

The 2016 Nissan Versa Note is a subcompact hatchback available with two engine and transmission options: a 1.6L inline-4 with a 5-speed manual or CVT transmission, and a 1.6L inline-4 with a CVT transmission. It offers an affordable and fuel-efficient option for urban driving. Praised for its spacious interior, decent fuel economy, and compact size, some drawbacks include a sluggish engine and lack of features.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4 with 5-speed manual transmission**

* **Engine misfire:** Causes erratic engine operation, poor acceleration, and check engine light.
Description/Symptoms: Faulty ignition coils or spark plugs
Solution: Replacement of ignition coils or spark plugs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Oil leaks:** Leaks can occur from the valve cover gasket or oil pan.
Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks, low oil levels
Solution: Replacement of the leaking gasket or oil pan
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**1.6L Inline-4 with CVT transmission**

* **CVT transmission failure:** Can cause a loss of power or a shuddering sensation while driving.
Description/Symptoms: Sudden loss of power, shuddering while driving
Solution: Replacement of the CVT transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual transmission**

* **Difficult shifting:** May require excessive force or grinding to shift gears.
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding sounds
Solution: Repair or replacement of the transmission shifter or clutch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**CVT transmission**

* **CVT transmission failure:** Can cause a loss of power or a shuddering sensation while driving.
Description/Symptoms: Sudden loss of power, shuddering while driving
Solution: Replacement of the CVT transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure:** Can cause a knocking or clunking sound while driving over bumps.
Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking sounds, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replacement of the struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per strut
Recalls: None Found

* **Sway bar link failure:** Can cause a rattling noise while driving over bumps or turning.
Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise, loose or broken sway bar link
Solution: Replacement of the sway bar link
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** Can cause a loss of power or battery drain.
Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, flickering dash lights, dead battery
Solution: Replacement of the alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery drain:** Can occur due to a faulty electrical component or parasitic drain.
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dead battery
Solution: Identification and repair of the faulty electrical component
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door handle failure:** Can cause the door handle to become loose or stuck.
Description/Symptoms: Loose or stuck door handle
Solution: Replacement of the door handle assembly
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

* **Window motor failure:** Can prevent the window from rolling up or down.
Description/Symptoms: Inoperable window motor, window stuck up or down
Solution: Replacement of the window motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle:** Can cause a rattling or buzzing sound while driving.
Description/Symptoms: Rattle or buzzing sound coming from the dashboard
Solution: Removal of loose parts or installation of sound dampening material
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

* **Heater core leak:** Can cause a sweet or musty smell inside the vehicle.
Description/Symptoms: Sweet or musty smell, fogging on the windshield
Solution: Replacement of the heater core
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Nissan Versa Note has a moderate number of potential issues, with some being more significant and costly than others. CVT transmission failure is a major concern, while issues like dashboard rattles and window motor failure are less severe. The cost of repairs can vary widely, so it's important to factor in the potential expenses when considering this vehicle.
