# 2017 Nissan Versa Note Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Nissan Versa Note offers an affordable entry point for budget-conscious buyers seeking practical hatchback utility and decent fuel economy. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability concerns closely, as various mechanical and electrical issues have surfaced in everyday driving. Paying close attention to the powertrain and cabin electronics during a pre-purchase inspection can help avoid costly surprises down the road.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

Powered by a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine generating approximately 109 horsepower, the Versa Note provides basic, efficient transportation. While fuel economy is a strong point, overall powertrain durability can be mixed based on owner feedback.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may unexpectedly shut down or stall while driving at speed or operating in cold weather conditions.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes, inspect electronic sensors such as camshaft position sensors, and check the ECM or engine wiring harness where needed.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The continuously variable automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive maximizes fuel efficiency but is a frequent focal point for owner complaints regarding long-term reliability and premature wear.

**Transmission Slipping and Unexpected Deceleration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience transmission slipping, sudden downshifts, unexpected loss of speed, or severe revving under acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have the CVT thoroughly evaluated by a qualified transmission specialist; fluid condition checks and potential transmission replacement may be required.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators

Cabin electrical accessories, including door locks and ignition switches, can develop intermittent operational glitches as the vehicle ages.

**Power Door Lock Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Door locks fail to engage or disengage via the key fob, producing a buzzing noise and forcing manual operation from the inside.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuator mechanism inside the affected door panel.

## Interior

### Cowl and Cabin Drainage

Exterior body seals and drainage paths require routine cleaning to prevent environmental debris from causing interior water leaks.

**Passenger Compartment Water Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Water collects in the cowl area beneath the windshield wipers during heavy rain and floods the front or rear passenger-side floorboards.
- **Solution:** Clear blocked cowl drains and inspect cabin air filter seals to prevent water intrusion.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraints and basic occupant protection systems should be carefully inspected during a used car evaluation to confirm proper latching and sensor operation.

**Seat Belt Latch Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belt latches feel loose, become difficult to secure properly, or show signs of premature wear.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace damaged buckles or latch mechanisms to ensure occupant safety.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V551000: Electrical System:Ignition](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Versa+Note&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In these models, over time, the weight on the key ring and road conditions or some other jarring event may cause the ignition switch to move out of the run position to the accessory position, turning off the engine. If the vehicle stalls, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the production information on the ignition switch, and replace it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V275000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Versa+Note&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The ring plate that secures the air bag cushion to the passenger frontal air bag assembly may fail, possibly causing the air bag to deploy improperly in the event of a crash. In the event of a crash, if the passenger frontal air bag does not deploy as intended, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Nissan will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Nissan Versa Note remains an attractive option for budget-minded buyers needing straightforward hatchback utility, but it demands careful vetting. Prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing heavily on CVT transmission health, engine sensor performance, and potential interior water leaks. Buyers should approach with caution and verify service history before purchasing.
