# 2019 Nissan Versa Note Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Nissan Versa Note is a subcompact hatchback offering practical cargo space and efficient daily commuting capabilities. While overall owner feedback is relatively light, prospective used car buyers should still carefully inspect the electrical systems and powertrain behavior during a test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2019 Nissan Versa Note features a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine producing approximately 109 horsepower and 107 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant prioritizes fuel efficiency for city driving over brisk acceleration, offering straightforward and dependable mechanical behavior for everyday use.

### Power Accessories and Outlets

Issues related to interior power accessories and wiring can occasionally arise, requiring careful inspection of fuses and electrical connections.

**Electrical Short and Accessory Slowdown**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported intermittent power outlet failures, erratic window operation, and vehicle hesitation or stalling during operation.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the vehicle wiring harnesses, fuses, and window regulator motors to identify and repair any loose connections or short circuits.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed to the front wheels via an Xtronic continuously variable transmission, which is designed to maximize fuel economy. Buyers should closely evaluate CVT responsiveness and smoothness during a pre-purchase test drive, as these units require diligent fluid maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V654000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Versa+Note&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Nissan Versa Note serves as an affordable and economical hatch for budget-conscious drivers. Used car shoppers should perform a thorough mechanical and electrical inspection, paying close attention to transmission shift quality and accessory functionality before finalizing a purchase.
