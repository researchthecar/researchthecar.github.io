**Introduction:**

The 2017 Nissan Versa Note is a subcompact hatchback available in S, SV, and SR trims. It offers a single engine option, a 1.6-liter inline-four, paired with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The 1.6-liter engine produces 109 horsepower and 107 lb-ft of torque. The Versa Note is praised for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and affordability, but criticized for its lack of performance and uninspiring handling.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Inline-Four**

- **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, loss of coolant, white smoke from the exhaust
  - Solution: Replace the cylinder head gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Timing Chain Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or knocking, reduced engine power, difficulty starting
  - Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

- **Shifting Difficulty**
  - Description/Symptoms: Hard or grinding shifts, difficulty engaging gears
  - Solution: Replace the shifter assembly or transmission mounts
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

- **Transmission Shudder**
  - Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or judders during acceleration or deceleration
  - Solution: Flush and replace the transmission fluid or replace the CVT unit
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Strut Mount Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, reduced ride comfort
  - Solution: Replace the strut mounts
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Discharge**
  - Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, dim or flickering lights
  - Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic draw
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
  - Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

- **Headlight Condensation**
  - Description/Symptoms: Water droplets or fog inside the headlights
  - Solution: Replace the faulty headlights or seal air leaks
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Trunk Latch Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Trunk fails to open or close properly
  - Solution: Replace the trunk latch assembly
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Cracking**
  - Description/Symptoms: Cracks or warping on the dashboard
  - Solution: Replace the dashboard or use a dashboard cover
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Seat Heater Malfunction**
  - Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working or overheating
  - Solution: Replace the seat heater element or wiring harness
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Nissan Versa Note has some common issues, but most are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. The engine and transmission issues can be costly to resolve, but recalls have not been issued for these problems. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues should not deter potential buyers who value the vehicle's spaciousness, fuel efficiency, and affordability.
