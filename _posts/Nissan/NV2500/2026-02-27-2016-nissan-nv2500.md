# 2016 Nissan NV2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Nissan NV2500 is a capable full-size commercial van designed for heavy-duty work and cargo hauling. When shopping for a used model, evaluating powertrain health and checking for minor reported quirks like tire wear or mirror glare can help ensure a dependable work vehicle.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V6

The 4.0-liter V6 engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, providing standard pulling power for daily commercial duties. It offers generally solid reliability for working fleets when properly maintained.

### 5.6L V8

The 5.6-liter V8 engine generates around 317 horsepower and 385 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust capability for heavier payloads and towing. This engine is a popular choice among buyers needing maximum muscle for work applications.

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic

The 5-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels under varying load conditions. It is a traditional unit that requires regular fluid maintenance to ensure long-term durability in commercial service.

### 7-Speed Automatic

The 7-speed automatic transmission provides a wider gear spread to optimize both fuel efficiency and power delivery under heavy loads. It operates reliably under normal commercial operating conditions.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension and Tires

Front suspension components support the heavy front end of the van, making regular alignment checks important for tire longevity.

**Uneven Front Tire Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice that the front tires wear out unevenly or prematurely across the tread surface.
- **Solution:** Have a professional inspect front-end alignment, suspension components, and tire pressure, and adjust or replace parts as necessary.

## Interior

### Driver Side Mirror

Exterior mirrors provide vital rear visibility, though certain lighting conditions can occasionally create interior reflections.

**Dashboard Glare in Mirror**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a distracting reflection or glare of the dashboard lights in the driver-side mirror during nighttime driving.
- **Solution:** Adjust the mirror angle or use anti-glare window tint or dash covers to minimize interior light reflection.

The 2016 Nissan NV2500 is a sturdy choice for commercial buyers needing a reliable cargo hauler.
