## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent battery drain, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair parasitic draw, replace faulty battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Power Window Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating correctly or failing to move.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty power window actuator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening from inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty door latch or lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $250 - $550


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replace the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Nissan NV2500 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly with its powertrain. The 4.0L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are known for their durability. While most systems are robust, some owners have reported minor electrical issues such as battery drain and power window actuator failures, as well as occasional problems with door latches and HVAC blower motors. These issues are typically manageable and do not indicate systemic weaknesses across the vehicle. Overall, the NV2500 remains a solid choice for those seeking a dependable work van.
