**Introduction**

The 2016 Nissan NV2500 is a full-size commercial van available with two engine options: a 4.0L V6 and a 5.6L V8. Transmission options include a 5-speed automatic and a 7-speed automatic with a Cummins turbodiesel engine. Drivers appreciate the NV2500's spacious cargo area, versatile seating configurations, and powerful engine options. However, it has received some criticism for its outdated interior design and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

* **Oil Consumption**: Excessive oil consumption leading to engine damage. May be caused by worn piston rings or valve guides.
  * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Failure**: Premature failure of the timing chain can cause engine damage.
  * Solution: Timing chain replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**5.6L V8**

* **Spark Plug Failure**: Premature failure of spark plugs can cause misfires and engine performance issues.
  * Solution: Spark plug replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**: Difficulty shifting gears or slipping between gears. May be caused by worn transmission clutches or solenoids.
  * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation on Acceleration**: Transmission hesitating or jerking when accelerating. May be caused by a faulty torque converter or valve body.
  * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, torque converter or valve body replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage**: Rear leaf springs can break, leading to a loss of vehicle stability.
  * Solution: Leaf spring replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**: Premature failure of the alternator can lead to charging system problems and electrical failures.
  * Solution: Alternator replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Discharge**: Battery may discharge rapidly or fail to hold a charge. May be caused by a faulty battery, alternator, or parasitic drain.
  * Solution: Battery replacement, alternator repair, or parasitic drain diagnosis and repair
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Moisture**: Moisture buildup inside tail lights can occur, affecting visibility.
  * Solution: Tail light replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Rattling**: Door panels may rattle or vibrate during driving. May be caused by loose screws or clips.
  * Solution: Tightening screws or replacing clips
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
  * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC Malfunction**: Heating or air conditioning system may not function properly. May be caused by a faulty compressor, evaporator, or expansion valve.
  * Solution: HVAC system diagnosis and repair
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Nissan NV2500 has experienced some notable issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. While some issues can be relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be quite costly. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and its cause. However, it's important to consider these potential issues when evaluating the reliability and maintenance costs associated with this vehicle.
