**Introduction:**

The 2021 Nissan NV2500, an acclaimed commercial van, offers versatility and durability. It is available with either a 4.0L V6 or a 5.6L V8 engine, paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Owners appreciate its spacious cargo area, comfortable cabin, and robust construction. However, there have been some specific issues reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

* **Oil Leak from Valve Cover Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leakage of engine oil from the valve cover area, leading to decreased oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cam Phaser Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning cam phaser causing engine timing issues, resulting in reduced power and fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replacement of the cam phaser.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.6L V8**

* **Timing Chain Stretch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Elongation of the timing chain over time, leading to misalignment of engine components and potential catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or hesitation in gear shifting, resulting in reduced drivability.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of transmission components, such as solenoids or clutches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid discharge of the battery, even when the vehicle is turned off, leading to starting problems or a dead battery.
    * Solution: Troubleshooting and repair of electrical components, such as faulty wiring or parasitic drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Liftgate:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion and rust formation on the liftgate, affecting its integrity and appearance.
    * Solution: Replacement of the liftgate or repair of affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the tailgate hinge, causing the tailgate to sag or fall off.
    * Solution: Replacement of the tailgate hinge.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Radio Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or intermittent radio, affecting entertainment and information functions.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty radio components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Nissan NV2500 has some specific issues that are worth considering. While some issues, such as oil leaks and shifting problems, can be relatively minor and less expensive to fix, others, like engine failures or timing chain stretch, can be severe and costly. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and monitor their vehicles for any signs or symptoms.
