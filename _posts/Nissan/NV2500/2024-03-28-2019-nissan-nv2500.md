**2019 Nissan NV2500**

The 2019 Nissan NV2500 is a full-size van that can be configured to seat up to 12 passengers or to be used as a cargo van. The NV2500 is powered by a 5.6-liter V8 engine that produces 375 horsepower and 394 pound-feet of torque. A 7-speed automatic transmission is the only transmission option.

The NV2500 is known for its spacious interior, comfortable seats, and powerful engine. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as its poor fuel economy and lack of modern safety features.

**Engine**

**5.6-liter V8**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** This issue occurs when the valve cover gasket fails, allowing oil to leak out. Symptoms include a burning oil smell and a decrease in oil level. The solution is to replace the valve cover gasket. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $500+.
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** This issue occurs when the intake manifold gasket fails, allowing coolant and oil to leak out. Symptoms include a drop in coolant level and a decrease in engine performance. The solution is to replace the intake manifold gasket. The estimated cost to fix is $300 - $700+.
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** This issue occurs when the timing chain tensioner fails, allowing the timing chain to skip. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine and a decrease in engine performance. The solution is to replace the timing chain tensioner. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,000+.

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:** This issue occurs when the transmission has difficulty shifting gears. Symptoms include rough shifting, delayed shifting, and slipping gears. The solution is to replace the transmission control module or the transmission solenoid pack. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,500+.
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:** This issue occurs when the transmission develops a leak. Symptoms include a drop in transmission fluid level and a decrease in transmission performance. The solution is to replace the transmission fluid pan gasket or the transmission fluid lines. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $500+.

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Wear:** This issue occurs when the front suspension components, such as the ball joints, control arms, and struts, wear out prematurely. Symptoms include a decrease in handling and stability, as well as a rough ride. The solution is to replace the worn suspension components. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,500+.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** This issue occurs when the battery drains prematurely due to a faulty electrical component. Symptoms include a dead battery and a decrease in electrical performance. The solution is to identify and replace the faulty electrical component. The estimated cost to fix is $100 - $500+.
* **Alternator Failure:** This issue occurs when the alternator fails to generate electricity. Symptoms include a dead battery and a decrease in electrical performance. The solution is to replace the alternator. The estimated cost to fix is $300 - $700+.

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Rust:** This issue occurs when the body panels, such as the fenders, doors, and hood, rust prematurely. Symptoms include bubbling paint and rust spots. The solution is to replace the rusted body panels. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $2,000+.

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** This issue occurs when the climate control system fails to regulate temperature. Symptoms include a lack of heat or air conditioning, as well as a decrease in air flow. The solution is to replace the faulty climate control components. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,500+.
* **Door Lock Failure:** This issue occurs when the door locks fail to operate properly. Symptoms include difficulty locking or unlocking the doors, as well as a lack of security. The solution is to replace the faulty door lock actuators or the door lock control module. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $500+.

**Summary**

The 2019 Nissan NV2500 is a spacious and comfortable van, but it also has a number of potential issues. The most severe issues include engine problems, transmission problems, and suspension problems. These issues can be costly to fix, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing an NV2500.
