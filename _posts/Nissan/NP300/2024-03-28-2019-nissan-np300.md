**2019 Nissan NP300**

The 2019 Nissan NP300 is a mid-size pickup truck that is available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 2.5-liter four-cylinder that produces 165 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque. A 2.3-liter turbodiesel four-cylinder that produces 190 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque is also available. Transmission options include a six-speed manual, a seven-speed automatic, and a five-speed automatic.

The NP300 is praised for its powerful engines, spacious cabin, and rugged design. However, it has also been criticized for its stiff ride, lack of interior features, and high fuel consumption.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter four-cylinder**

* **Oil leak from valve cover:** Oil leaks from the valve cover, causing a loss of oil and potentially leading to engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip and potentially damaging the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.3-liter turbodiesel four-cylinder**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, resulting in a loss of power and decreased fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-speed manual**

* **Difficulty shifting gears:** Difficulty shifting gears, especially into first and second gear.
    * Solution: Adjust the clutch or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-speed automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission may slip or shudder when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Five-speed automatic**

* **No issues found**

**Suspension**

* **Leaking shock absorbers:** The shock absorbers can leak, causing a loss of damping and a rough ride.
    * Solution: Replace the shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Broken leaf springs:** The leaf springs can break, causing the vehicle to sag and potentially leading to handling problems.
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain quickly, even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Solution: Inspect the electrical system for any draws and repair or replace the faulty component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, preventing the battery from charging and causing the vehicle to stall.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on body panels:** Rust can develop on the body panels, especially around the wheel wells and tailgate.
    * Solution: Remove the rust and apply a protective coating.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cracked taillights:** The taillights can crack or break, allowing moisture and dirt to enter.
    * Solution: Replace the taillights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Noisy cabin:** The cabin can be noisy, especially at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Install sound insulation or replace the seals around the doors and windows.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty climate control system:** The climate control system may not function properly, causing the cabin to be too hot or too cold.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control unit or repair the wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Nissan NP300 is a capable pickup truck with a variety of engine and transmission options. However, it has been reported to have a number of issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. Some of these issues can be expensive to repair, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing a used NP300.
