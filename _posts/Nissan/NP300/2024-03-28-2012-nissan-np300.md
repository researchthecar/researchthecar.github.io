## 2012 Nissan NP300

The 2012 Nissan NP300 is a popular pickup truck known for its reliability, durability, and off-road capabilities. It is powered by a 2.5-liter diesel engine that produces 163 horsepower and 403 lb-ft of torque. The NP300 is available with either a 6-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission. 

People who love the Nissan NP300 appreciate its ruggedness, affordability, and spacious cabin. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.
