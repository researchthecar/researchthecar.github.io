**Introduction**

The 2017 Nissan NP300, also known as the Nissan Navara in some markets, is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a combination of rugged capability and modern amenities. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including a 2.3-liter diesel engine paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 7-speed automatic transmission. The 2.5-liter gasoline engine is equipped with a 6-speed manual transmission. People appreciate the NP300's spacious cabin, powerful engine options, and versatile features. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.3-liter Diesel Engine**

* **Faulty EGR Valve:**
    * Engine performance issues, black smoke from exhaust, decreased fuel efficiency
    * Replacement of EGR valve
    * $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Loss of power, excessive oil consumption, grinding or whining noises
    * Replacement of turbocharger
    * $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:**
    * Reduced engine power, poor fuel economy, increased emissions
    * Regeneration or replacement of DPF
    * $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-liter Gasoline Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding Noises When Shifting:**
    * Worn synchronizers or damaged gear teeth
    * Replacement of damaged components
    * $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Difficult to Shift Gears:**
    * Leaking hydraulic fluid, worn clutch
    * Repair or replacement of affected components
    * $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Hesitation or Jerking When Shifting:**
    * Faulty solenoids or torque converter
    * Repair or replacement of affected components
    * $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Worn clutches or damaged gears
    * Overhaul or replacement of transmission
    * $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Worn Shock Absorbers:**
    * Reduced ride comfort, poor handling
    * Replacement of shock absorbers
    * $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Weak Leaf Springs:**
    * Sagging or uneven suspension
    * Replacement of leaf springs
    * $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Faulty alternator or parasitic drain
    * Repair or replacement of affected components
    * $200 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical System Malfunctions:**
    * Faulty sensors or wiring
    * Diagnosis and repair of affected components
    * $250 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Frame:**
    * Corrosion on undercarriage components
    * Rust prevention measures, repair or replacement of affected parts
    * $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Weak leaf springs or overloaded vehicle
    * Reinforcement of suspension or load reduction
    * $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Malfunctions:**
    * Faulty actuators or wiring
    * Repair or replacement of door locks
    * $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC System Issues:**
    * Malfunctioning fans or controls
    * Repair or replacement of affected components
    * $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Nissan NP300 has received mixed reviews from owners. While it offers strong performance and versatility, some have experienced issues with its engine, transmission, and suspension components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can help to minimize the risk of breakdowns and ensure a more reliable ownership experience.
