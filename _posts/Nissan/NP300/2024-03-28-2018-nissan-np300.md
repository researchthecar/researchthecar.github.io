**Introduction:**

The 2018 Nissan NP300 is a compact pickup truck that is available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The most popular engine option is the 2.5-liter turbodiesel engine, which produces 161 horsepower and 297 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 2.4-liter gasoline engine and a 3.0-liter diesel engine.

The NP300 is known for its ruggedness and off-road capabilities. It also has a spacious cabin and a comfortable ride. However, some owners have reported some issues with the vehicle.
