## 2016 Nissan NP300

The 2016 Nissan NP300 is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its durability and off-road capabilities, but it has also been the subject of several complaints and recalls.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail, leading to a loss of power and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 18V846000
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors can fail, causing hard starting, rough idling, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Problems:** The exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve can become stuck open or closed, leading to poor engine performance and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V686000

**2.3-liter Diesel**

* **Timing Chain Failure:** The timing chain can fail, causing catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V308000

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Gearshift Linkage Failure:** The gearshift linkage can fail, making it difficult or impossible to shift gears.
    * Solution: Replace the gearshift linkage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:** The transmission can slip, causing rough shifting and a loss of power.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V007000

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:** The front suspension can develop a noise, such as a clunking or rattling sound.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace the front suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Inspect the electrical system for parasitic drains and repair as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator can fail, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to stall.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure:** The tailgate hinge can fail, causing the tailgate to drop or become inoperable.
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate hinge.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system can fail to provide heating or cooling, or it may blow only hot air.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace the relevant components of the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seatbelt Pretensioner Failure:** The seatbelt pretensioners can fail to deploy in the event of a crash.
    * Solution: Replace the seatbelt pretensioners.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V014000

**Summary**

The 2016 Nissan NP300 has been plagued by a number of issues across various components. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be severe and costly. The engine and transmission, in particular, have been the subject of several recalls and complaints. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and consider having their vehicles inspected regularly to prevent or address issues early on.
