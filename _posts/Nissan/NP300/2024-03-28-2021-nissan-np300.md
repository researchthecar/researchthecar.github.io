## The 2021 Nissan NP300

The 2021 Nissan NP300 is a popular pickup truck known for its ruggedness, reliability, and off-road capabilities. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including a 2.5-liter diesel engine with a 6-speed manual transmission and a 2.5-liter gasoline engine with a 5-speed manual transmission. The NP300 has a strong reputation among drivers for its durability and versatility, but there have been some reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, poor acceleration, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Turbocharger Boost Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke from exhaust, whistling noise
    * Solution: Repair or replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500
    * Recalls: N/A

**2.5-liter Gasoline**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or knocking noise, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $4000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clutch pedal feels spongy, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Synchromesh Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Repair or replace synchrohubs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Manual**

* **Input Shaft Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission noise, vibration while driving
    * Solution: Replace input shaft bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Rear Shock Absorbers Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Identify and repair electrical component causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on source of drain
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Frame and Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots, holes in metal
    * Solution: Rust treatment, replacement of affected parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity of rust
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate does not close or open properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Crack:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Crack in dashboard material
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Nissan NP300 is a reliable and capable pickup truck. However, some owners have reported issues with specific components, particularly the fuel injectors on the diesel engine and the timing chain on the gasoline engine. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are not widespread and should not deter potential buyers from considering the NP300.
