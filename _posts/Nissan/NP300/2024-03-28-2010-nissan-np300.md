**Introduction to the 2010 Nissan NP300**

The 2010 Nissan NP300 is a mid-size pickup truck offered in both single and double cab configurations. It is powered by a 2.5-liter inline-four diesel engine that produces 140 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The power is sent to the rear wheels through a five-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

* **2.5L Diesel Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, black smoke from exhaust, reduced power
    Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Transmission Noise**
    Description/Symptoms: Whining or grinding noise during gear changes
    Solution: Replace transmission bearings or synchronizers
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Strut Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Leaking struts, noise, reduced ride quality
    Solution: Replace struts
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    Recalls: N/A
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Leaf Spring Failures**
    Description/Symptoms: Broken or cracked leaf springs, loss of ride height
    Solution: Replace leaf springs
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle
    Solution: Inspect electrical components for parasitic draw
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
    Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, dimming lights, warning lights
    Solution: Replace alternator
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Hood Release Cable Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Hood will not open
    Solution: Replace hood release cable
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    Recalls: N/A
* **Tailgate Lock Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Tailgate will not open or close
    Solution: Replace tailgate lock
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Window Switch Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Windows will not operate
    Solution: Replace window switches
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2010 Nissan NP300 is a reliable and capable pickup truck. However, it does have some potential issues that owners should be aware of, particularly with the fuel injectors, transmission, and suspension. These issues can be costly to fix, so it is important to factor in the potential repair costs when considering purchasing this vehicle.
