The 2015 Nissan NP300 Navara is a popular pickup truck known for its ruggedness and versatility. Engine options for the 2015 model year varied depending on the market, but a common option was a 2.5-liter inline-4 diesel engine producing around 161 horsepower and 297 lb-ft of torque. Other engines include a 2.5-liter petrol engine. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual and a 7-speed automatic. Owners often praise the NP300 for its payload capacity and off-road capabilities, while some common complaints revolve around ride comfort and certain mechanical issues.

**Engine**

**2.5L Diesel (YD25DDTi)**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive black smoke, whistling noise from the turbocharger, engine warning light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Injector Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, misfires, poor fuel economy, engine knocking.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty injectors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Timing Chain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup; engine misfires; potential engine damage.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, sprockets, and tensioners.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**2.5L Petrol (QR25DE)**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning Oil, Low oil level, Blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Top off engine oil, or replace piston rings and valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

*   **Clutch Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clutch slippage, difficulty shifting gears, clutch pedal sticking or feeling spongy.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the clutch disc, pressure plate, and potentially the flywheel.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Automatic**

*   **Rough Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifts, especially between certain gears; transmission slipping.
    *   Solution: Software update or valve body replacement, or in some cases full transmission replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $4000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Leaf Spring Sagging/Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sitting lower than normal, reduced load-carrying capacity, rough ride.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Shock Absorber Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, excessive body roll when cornering, reduced handling stability.
    *   Solution: Replacement of shock absorbers.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Central Locking System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly, remote key not working consistently.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair of wiring issues, replacement of faulty actuators or control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Battery draining overnight or after a short period of inactivity, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and repair of parasitic drain, replacement of faulty battery.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust Issues (Particularly around wheel arches and body seams)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Visible rust forming on the body panels, especially in areas prone to moisture and road salt exposure.
    *   Solution: Rust repair and repainting.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2000+ (depending on severity)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Cracking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard surface due to sun exposure and temperature changes.
    *   Solution: Dashboard replacement or repair.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Nissan NP300, while a capable and popular truck, does have some common issues owners should be aware of. Engine issues, particularly with the diesel variants, can be costly, as can transmission problems. Suspension and electrical issues are also relatively common. Rust can be a significant concern, particularly in areas with harsh climates. Overall, potential buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle, paying close attention to these areas, and factor in potential repair costs. The severity and cost of the issues are moderate to high, with some repairs potentially exceeding $2000.
