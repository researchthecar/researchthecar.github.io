**Introduction**

The 2023 Nissan Kicks e-Power is a subcompact SUV that combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor to provide enhanced fuel efficiency. The engine generates 94 horsepower and 97 lb-ft of torque, while the electric motor contributes 129 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque. The Kicks e-Power is praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and stylish design, but it has also received criticism for its limited cargo space and lack of performance.
