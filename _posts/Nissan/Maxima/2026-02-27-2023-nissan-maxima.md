# 2023 Nissan Maxima Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2023 Nissan Maxima means looking at the final model year of this iconic sports sedan nameplate. Known for its distinct styling and potent V6 performance, it bridges the gap between a standard commuter and a more engaging daily driver. Prospective buyers should review standard pre-purchase inspection steps, as owner-reported data provides a helpful baseline for what to watch out for during a test drive.

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Gas Engine

The 2023 Nissan Maxima features a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 300 horsepower and 261 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated powerplant delivers strong acceleration and generally dependable everyday performance.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is routed through an Xtronic continuously variable transmission driving the front wheels. Buyers should test the CVT thoroughly during a used car inspection to ensure smooth operation without hesitation or slipping.
