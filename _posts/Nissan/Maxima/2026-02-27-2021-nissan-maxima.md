# 2021 Nissan Maxima Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Nissan Maxima offers a stylish blend of sport sedan styling and V6 performance, making it an appealing option for used car buyers seeking comfort and presence. While owner complaints are generally limited, prospective buyers should review specific mechanical and electrical functions during a pre-purchase inspection. Understanding its powertrain options and known owner reports helps ensure a confident shopping experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2021 Nissan Maxima comes equipped with a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 300 horsepower and 261 lb-ft of torque. This naturally aspirated powerplant delivers strong acceleration and generally dependable everyday performance.

**Engine Jerking and Oil Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle jerks and shakes while driving at low speeds, accompanied by a flashing oil pressure light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine diagnostics, oil pressure levels, and related sensors to identify the root cause.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is sent to the front wheels via a sport-tuned Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). Buyers should evaluate smooth operation and responsiveness during a test drive.

## Electrical

### Gauges and Infotainment

Electrical accessories and instrumentation generally operate reliably, though occasional reports note minor software or sensor lag.

**Delayed Coolant Temperature Gauge and CD Player Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic coolant temperature gauge experiences significant delays before activating, and the CD changer fails to function properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect electrical connections, software modules, and infotainment hardware, replacing faulty components as necessary.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body panels and glass components are securely styled, though isolated glass incidents have been reported by owners.

**Unexpected Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass sunroof panel shatters unexpectedly while driving under normal road conditions.
- **Solution:** Have the damaged glass assembly completely replaced and check the frame alignment for stress points.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Nissan Maxima is a compelling used sedan choice that pairs robust V6 power with distinctive styling. While isolated complaints highlight potential electronic quirks or rare glass issues, a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help secure a well-maintained example.
