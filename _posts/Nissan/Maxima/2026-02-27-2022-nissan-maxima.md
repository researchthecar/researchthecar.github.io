# 2022 Nissan Maxima Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Nissan Maxima, buyers will find a stylish, sport-oriented sedan equipped with a robust V6 engine. While owner-reported complaints are relatively few, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and inspect key areas like the brakes and electrical systems during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces around 300 horsepower and 261 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for daily driving. This powerplant generally maintains a solid reputation for durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### Xtronic CVT Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a continuously variable transmission paired with front-wheel drive, it delivers smooth power delivery and competitive highway fuel economy. Buyers should verify fluid maintenance history during inspection.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Service Brakes

Braking performance is vital for safety, and any reports of unexpected pedal travel should be thoroughly investigated.

**Brake Pedal Travel and Stopping Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a soft brake pedal feel or extended stopping distance, potentially causing difficulty bringing the vehicle to a full stop.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the brake pads, rotors, master cylinder, and hydraulic lines for leaks or premature wear.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems should be checked during nighttime test drives to ensure optimal visibility.

**Headlight Beam Irregularities**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice dark shaded spots or uneven light distribution within the illuminated field while operating headlights at night.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight housings and bulbs for proper alignment or manufacturing defects, and replace components if necessary.

### Panoramic Sunroof and Body Trim

Exterior and roof components should be inspected for proper fit and finish during a used vehicle evaluation.

**Sunroof Lining Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** The interior headliner or lining around the panoramic sunroof may begin to droop or hang down, obstructing the driver's view.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealer or upholstery specialist to have the sagging headliner re-secured or replaced under warranty if applicable.

The 2022 Nissan Maxima offers an engaging driving experience and strong V6 performance, though shoppers should remain vigilant. Conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes, exterior lighting, and interior trim to ensure everything is in proper working order.
