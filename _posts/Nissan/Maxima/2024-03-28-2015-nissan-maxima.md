**Introduction:**

The 2015 Nissan Maxima is a sporty sedan with a powerful engine and well-equipped interior. It is available with either a 3.5L V6 engine or a hybrid powertrain. The V6 engine produces 300 horsepower and 261 lb-ft of torque, while the hybrid system combines a 2.5L four-cylinder engine with an electric motor to generate 235 horsepower.

Drivers praise the Maxima's comfortable interior, responsive handling, and impressive acceleration. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Valve Body Issue:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, or refusing to shift gears
    - Solution: Replace the valve body
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Transmission Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, difficulty shifting gears, whining or grinding noises
    - Solution: Replace the transmission
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Control Arm Bushing Issue:**
- Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises when turning or going over bumps
- Solution: Replace the control arm bushings
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
- Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Discharge Issue:**
- Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, dim headlights, or warning messages related to battery voltage
- Solution: Replace the battery or alternator.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
- Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Panoramic Sunroof Issue:**
- Description/Symptoms: Sunroof won't open or close properly, makes rattling noises, or leaks water
- Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof assembly
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
- Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Nissan Maxima is a generally reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported issues with its engine, transmission, and other components. The severity and cost of these issues may vary depending on the specific problem and the severity of the damage. It is important to address any issues promptly to prevent more costly repairs in the future.
