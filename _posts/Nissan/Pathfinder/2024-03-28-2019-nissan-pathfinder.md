**2019 Nissan Pathfinder**

The 2019 Nissan Pathfinder is a three-row SUV available with a 284 horsepower, 3.5-liter V6 engine and a 9-speed automatic transmission. The Pathfinder is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features. However, there have been some issues reported with certain components.

**Engine**

* 3.5L V6 Engine

**None Found**

**Transmission**

* 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

**Transmission Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission may slip, jerk, or shudder. May also experience loss of power or difficulty shifting gears.
Solution: Replace transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Struts**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise, vibration, or harshness when driving over bumps or rough roads.
Solution: Replace suspension struts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains prematurely, causing difficulty starting or electrical system malfunctions.
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system issue causing drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**None Found**

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control system may not blow hot or cold air, or may malfunction intermittently.
Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty climate control components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Nissan Pathfinder is generally a reliable vehicle, there have been some reported issues with the transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The transmission failure issue can be particularly costly to fix, while the other issues are typically less expensive to address. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues should be considered when evaluating this vehicle.
