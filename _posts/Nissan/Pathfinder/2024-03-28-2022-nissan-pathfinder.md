**Introduction:**

The 2022 Nissan Pathfinder is a three-row SUV that offers a spacious cabin and versatile seating configurations. It is powered by a standard 3.5-liter V6 engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. AWD is available on all trims. Despite its positive attributes, the Pathfinder has faced some issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake Valve Deposit Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on intake valves, leading to rough idling, reduced power, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: De-carbonization of intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates to shift gears, particularly when accelerating.
    * Solution: Software update or transmission reprogramming.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random system crashes, touchscreen unresponsiveness, and audio issues.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises coming from the rear suspension, especially over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacing sway bar bushings or end links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the panoramic sunroof, especially during heavy rain.
    * Solution: Resealing or replacing sunroof assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Center Console Lid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of center console lid to open or close properly, potentially locking valuables inside.
    * Solution: Replacing center console lid assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

While the 2022 Nissan Pathfinder offers a comfortable and spacious interior, it has faced several issues that have impacted owner satisfaction. The most significant issues lie in engine valve buildup, transmission delays, and electrical glitches. While many of these issues can be resolved through software updates or relatively simple repairs, some, such as sunroof leaks and center console malfunctions, can be more costly to fix.
