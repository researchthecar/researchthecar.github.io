# 2016 Nissan Pathfinder Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Nissan Pathfinder offers a spacious, family-friendly interior and a comfortable ride, making it a popular choice in the midsize SUV segment. However, prospective buyers should approach this model year with careful consideration due to notable owner complaints regarding the powertrain, braking system, and visibility components. While many owners enjoy its everyday utility, checking service records for transmission and brake maintenance is strongly recommended before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2016 Nissan Pathfinder is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. While generally robust, some owners have reported unexpected power loss and stalling incidents.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shut off or lose power while driving, occasionally failing to restart and rendering hazard lights inoperable during incidents.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing of the electrical system, fuel delivery, and engine control modules is required to isolate the source of unexpected stalls.

## Transmission

### Xtronic Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Paired exclusively with the V6 engine, the Xtronic CVT delivers smooth acceleration and optimized fuel economy across front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. Long-term durability varies significantly, making a thorough pre-purchase transmission inspection essential.

**Transmission Failure and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden hesitation, sputtering under acceleration, complete loss of forward drive, or unexpected failure without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the CVT unit; severe failures typically necessitate transmission replacement or rebuilding.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The hydraulic braking system provides stopping power for normal driving conditions, but owner reports indicate recurring issues with pedal travel and braking responsiveness.

**Brake Pedal Softness and Failure to Respond**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may feel spongy, travel all the way to the floorboard, or fail to slow the vehicle adequately when depressed.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hydraulic system, master cylinder, brake lines, and pads; bleed or replace failing components as needed.

## Exterior

### Visibility and Sunroof

Exterior visibility components generally function as expected, though a subset of owners have experienced unexpected sunroof glass failure.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass sunroof may shatter or explode unexpectedly while driving under normal highway or paved road conditions.
- **Solution:** Clear glass debris from the interior cabin and replace the damaged sunroof assembly with a new glass panel.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V244000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Pathfinder&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As a result, the passenger frontal air bag may be turned off and not deploy in the event of a crash. If the passenger frontal air bag does not deploy as intended in the event of a crash, the passenger is at an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the Air Bag Control Unit (ACU) and OCS Electronic Control Unit (ECU) in Altima, Maxima, Murano, Rogue, and Sentra vehicles, and replace the OCS ECU in LEAF, NV200, NV200 Taxi, Pathfinder, Infiniti Q50, JX35, and QX60. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V420000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hood:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Pathfinder&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An unsecured hood could unexpectedly open while driving, obstructing the driver's view, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the bell crank assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2016 Nissan Pathfinder, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection with a focus on the CVT transmission and hydraulic brake system. While the vehicle offers great comfort and family utility, documented owner concerns regarding transmission hesitation and brake pedal feel warrant extra caution. Reviewing past maintenance records and verifying that all safety recalls have been addressed will help ensure a more reliable ownership experience.
