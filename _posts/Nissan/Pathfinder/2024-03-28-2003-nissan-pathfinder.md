**Introduction:**

The 2003 Nissan Pathfinder is a mid-size SUV available with a choice of a 3.5L V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It offers a balance of ruggedness, comfort, and spaciousness. However, like any vehicle, the Pathfinder has its share of common issues.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idle
  - **Solution:** Replacement of intake manifold gaskets
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
  - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Oil leaks from under the valve covers
  - **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
  - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Throttle Position Sensor Issues:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Erratic idling, poor acceleration, engine stalling
  - **Solution:** Replacement of throttle position sensor
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $250
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic:**

- **Transmission Slipping:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of power during acceleration, rough shifting
  - **Solution:** Transmission repair or rebuild
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
  - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Overheating:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Transmission fluid overheating, burning smell
  - **Solution:** Cooling system flush, transmission fluid replacement
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

- **Worn-Out Ball Joints:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Front-end clunking noises, uneven tire wear
  - **Solution:** Replacement of ball joints
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $900+
  - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Leaky Shocks and Struts:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive bouncing, unstable handling
  - **Solution:** Replacement of shocks and struts
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery not charging
  - **Solution:** Replacement of alternator
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
  - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Electrical Shorts:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Blown fuses, flickering lights, power loss
  - **Solution:** Electrical system inspection and repair
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

- **Cracked Windshield:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Cracks or chips in the windshield
  - **Solution:** Windshield replacement
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
  - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Fading Headlights:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Decreased headlight brightness
  - **Solution:** Headlight restoration or replacement
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

- **Broken Cup Holders:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Cup holders not holding drinks securely
  - **Solution:** Replacement of cup holders
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $50 - $150
  - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Dashboard Cracking:**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Cracks or warping on the dashboard
  - **Solution:** Dashboard replacement
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2003 Nissan Pathfinder is a reliable vehicle with some known issues that can be costly to repair. The most common and expensive problems are related to the transmission and engine. However, many of the issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix.
