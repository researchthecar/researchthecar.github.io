**Introduction to the 2013 Nissan Pathfinder:**

The 2013 Nissan Pathfinder is a midsize SUV offered with a variety of engine and transmission configurations. It features a powerful V6 engine and a spacious interior. The available 3.5-liter V6 engine delivers 260 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 3.8-liter V6 and a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine. Transmission options include a continuously variable transmission (CVT), a 5-speed automatic transmission, and a 6-speed manual transmission. The Pathfinder is praised for its comfortable interior and impressive fuel economy, but it has also been plagued by several issues.

**Engine**

**3.5-liter V6**
* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rattling noises, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V279000

**Transmission**

**CVT**
* **Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation during acceleration, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace CVT or CVT components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty changing gears, slipping
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**
* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise, vibration, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the vehicle, flickering lights
    * Solution: Repair or replace electrical components causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Brake Light Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Brake lights not illuminating, cruise control not functioning
    * Solution: Replace brake light switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V603000

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Visible rust spots on panels, especially underbody
    * Solution: Repair and prevent further rust
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity of rust
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
    * Solution: Repair or replace lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to turn on seat heaters or uneven heating
    * Solution: Repair or replace seat heater elements
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per seat
    * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC Blower Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to control fan speed, insufficient airflow
    * Solution: Repair or replace blower fan or related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Other Issues**

* **Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Nissan Pathfinder has several significant issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. While it offers a comfortable interior and good fuel economy, these issues can be costly to repair. The timing chain failure is especially concerning and can lead to catastrophic engine damage. The CVT transmission is also prone to failure, and the 5-speed automatic has been known to experience shifting issues.
