**Introduction:**

The 2016 Nissan Cabstar is a light commercial vehicle available with a range of diesel and gasoline engines paired with either a manual or automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable interior and impressive грузоподъёмность, making it popular for small businesses and contractors. Despite its strengths, the Cabstar has faced some issues in various areas.

**Engine**

* **2.3L dCi Diesel**
    * **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Valve Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine performance, increased fuel consumption, and emissions
        * Solution: Replacement of EGR valve
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.5L dCi Diesel**
    * **Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption
        * Solution: Injector replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300 per injector
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Gearshift Linkage Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replacement or repair of gearshift linkage
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found
* **7-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, reduced transmission performance
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of transmission fluid seals or lines
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive tire wear, noise and vibration during driving
    * Solution: Suspension component replacement or adjustment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Axle Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Groaning or humming noise from rear axle, vibration during acceleration or braking
    * Solution: Replacement of rear axle bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, electrical system malfunction
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery losing charge quickly, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical drain source
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to lock or unlock tailgate
    * Solution: Lock mechanism repair or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Handle Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles breaking or becoming loose
    * Solution: Handle replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Air Conditioning Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing cold air, decreased airflow
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of AC system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Radio Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: No sound, display issues, or other radio problems
    * Solution: Radio replacement or repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Nissan Cabstar has faced some issues in various areas, including engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and consider the cost of repairs before purchasing.
