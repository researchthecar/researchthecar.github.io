# 2024 Nissan Frontier Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Nissan Frontier continues as a capable midsize pickup truck, powered by a modern V6 engine paired with a responsive automatic transmission. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from contemporary styling and updated technology, appealing to buyers looking for a rugged yet daily-drivable truck. Because it is still early in its ownership lifecycle, long-term reliability patterns are still unfolding, though current owner feedback remains generally aligned with expectations for a modern truck.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 Direct Injection

This 3.8-liter V6 engine produces 310 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration and towing capability. It serves as the standard powerplant for the lineup, offering a balanced mix of performance and modern refinement.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and efficient gear shifts across various driving conditions, available in both rear-wheel and four-wheel drive configurations. It is designed to optimize fuel economy while maintaining responsive power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V154000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Frontier&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing issue, the driver's air bag inflator may absorb moisture, and cause the air bag to deploy improperly. An air bag that deploys improperly may not protect the driver as intended during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front driver's air bag assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V479000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Ball Joint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Frontier&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Ball joint separation can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front right-side lower link, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V582000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Frontier&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Improperly aimed headlights can result in insufficient illumination of the road, decreasing the driver's visibility and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the right-side headlight as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Nissan Frontier presents a solid option for used truck buyers seeking traditional midsize utility combined with a stout V6 powertrain. Prospective buyers should always conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and test drive to ensure all systems operate as expected.
