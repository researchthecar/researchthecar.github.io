# 2023 Nissan Frontier Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Nissan Frontier carries forward its robust platform, anchored by a modern V6 engine and a durable automatic transmission. While it offers traditional midsize truck utility and updated styling, prospective used-car buyers should review owner-reported feedback carefully. As an inherently mechanical machine, checking routine wear items and powertrain health during a pre-purchase inspection remains vital.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 Direct-Injection Engine

This 3.8-liter V6 engine produces around 310 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, paired with direct injection. It serves as the primary powerplant for the lineup, generally offering solid everyday performance and straightforward maintenance.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to either the rear wheels or the available four-wheel-drive system. It helps optimize highway cruising efficiency while supporting the truck's towing capabilities.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V273000: Tires:Bead](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Nissan&model=Frontier&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A rapid loss of tire pressure while driving can lead to a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace all four road tires, or inspect and replace the spare tire assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Nissan Frontier presents a dependable option in the midsize truck segment, backed by a strong standard powertrain. Buyers are encouraged to perform a thorough test drive and mechanical inspection to ensure all systems operate as expected.
