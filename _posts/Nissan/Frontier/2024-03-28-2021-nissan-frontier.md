**2021 Nissan Frontier**

The 2021 Nissan Frontier is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a powerful 3.8-liter V6 engine paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. It boasts a rugged exterior design and a comfortable interior with advanced technology features. Despite its strengths, the Frontier has faced some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **3.8-liter V6**
    * **Intake Valve Gasket Failure:** Premature failure of the intake valve gasket can cause oil leaks and decreased engine performance. The issue may be accompanied by a burning oil smell or a check engine light.
    * **Solution:** Replace the failed intake valve gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **9-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:** Some owners have reported issues with the transmission shifting smoothly, experiencing hesitation or jerky shifts.
    * **Solution:** Reprogramming the transmission control module (TCM) or replacing the valve body.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues:** Faulty electrical harnesses can cause a range of electrical problems, including intermittent power loss, flickering lights, or malfunctioning electronic components.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair or replace the affected harness.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Some owners have reported condensation forming inside the headlight assemblies, affecting visibility.
* **Solution:** Replace the affected headlights.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Faulty climate control systems may not regulate temperature effectively or may produce excessive noise.
* **Solution:** Diagnose and repair the failed component (e.g., blower motor, temperature control module).
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

While the 2021 Nissan Frontier offers a powerful and practical pickup option, it has faced some notable issues. The engine and transmission problems can be expensive to repair, while electrical and exterior issues can affect comfort and functionality. However, the lack of major suspension issues and the generally good interior quality are positive aspects to consider.
