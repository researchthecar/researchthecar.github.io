# 2007 Nissan Frontier

### Overview
The 2007 Nissan Frontier is a mid-size pickup truck known for its ruggedness and off-road capability. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, like any model, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding potential issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining common problems reported across its various engine and transmission configurations. Understanding these potential weak points can help owners anticipate and address issues proactively, ensuring the longevity and performance of their Frontier.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L QR25DE (4-cylinder)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil burning, potentially leading to low oil levels and engine damage if not monitored.
- **Solution:** Regular oil level checks and top-offs. In some cases, piston ring replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Timing Chain Rattle**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


### 4.0L VQ40DE (V6)
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Vacuum leaks, rough idle, potential check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, rough idle, stalling, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft position sensor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (RE5R05A)
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, or slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, solenoid replacement, or in severe cases, transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3,000+

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow movement, or falling glass.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Instrument Cluster Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Flickering gauges, dead gauges, or intermittent power loss to the cluster.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Tailgate Handle Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty opening or closing the tailgate, broken handle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the tailgate handle assembly.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Nissan Frontier presents a generally solid reliability record, particularly for its powertrain when properly maintained. The 4-cylinder engine has seen some reports of oil consumption and timing chain noise, while the V6 can experience intake manifold gasket leaks and camshaft sensor issues. Automatic transmission problems, such as harsh shifting, are the most frequently cited powertrain concern. Electrical gremlins like window regulators and instrument cluster malfunctions are not uncommon. Exterior and interior issues are typically minor, with tailgate handles and HVAC blower motors being the most noted. Overall, the Frontier is a dependable truck, but owners should be aware of these specific potential issues and perform regular checks, especially concerning oil levels and transmission performance.
