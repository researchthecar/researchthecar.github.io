## 2017 Nissan Frontier

The 2017 Nissan Frontier is a mid-size pickup truck that offers a choice between two engine options: a 2.5L inline-4 with 152 horsepower and 171 lb-ft of torque, and a 4.0L V6 with 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 5-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual for the 2.5L engine, and a 5-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual for the 4.0L engine.

The Frontier is praised for its ruggedness, affordability, and off-road capabilities. However, it also has some shortcomings, such as a dated interior, limited towing capacity, and poor fuel economy.

**Timing Chain Issues**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain can stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or stall.
Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**5-Speed Automatic**
**6-Speed Automatic**
**6-Speed Manual**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips or hesitates when shifting gears.
Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter, or overhaul the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Leaking Transmission Pan**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission pan develops a leak, causing transmission fluid to drip.
Solution: Replace the transmission pan gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**No Issues Found**

### Electrical

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is turned off.
Solution: Test the electrical system for parasitic drains and replace any faulty components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

**Tailgate Rust**
Description/Symptoms: The tailgate develops rust spots.
Solution: Sand and repaint the tailgate.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Interior

**Climate Control Malfunctions**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system fails to regulate temperature or airflow.
Solution: Replace the climate control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Dashboard Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard develops cracks over time.
Solution: Replace the dashboard.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Nissan Frontier is a reliable mid-size pickup truck with few major issues. However, it is important to be aware of the potential problems listed above, which can range in severity and cost from minor to expensive. Overall, the Frontier offers good value for money and is a capable choice for off-road adventures or everyday use.
