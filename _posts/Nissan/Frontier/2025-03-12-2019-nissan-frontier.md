The 2019 Nissan Frontier is a mid-size pickup truck known for its simplicity and ruggedness. It's powered by a 3.8L V6 engine producing 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission. There was no other engine option for 2019. Owners often appreciate its reliability and off-road capability, but some dislike its outdated design and fuel economy compared to newer trucks.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**

*   **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially upon startup. This can lead to timing chain failure, causing engine damage.
    *   Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and potentially the timing chain itself.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, check engine light illumination.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty camshaft position sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

*   **Torque Converter Shudder**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration felt during light acceleration or cruising speeds.
    *   Solution: Perform a transmission fluid flush or replace the torque converter.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Leaf Spring Sagging/Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the truck sitting lower than normal, poor ride quality, potential for cracking or breaking of leaf springs.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The heater or air conditioner only works on certain speeds, or not at all.
    *   Solution: Replace the blower motor resistor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust on Frame**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on the frame, particularly in areas exposed to road salt. Can lead to structural weakness.
    *   Solution: Rust repair, treatment, or frame replacement (in severe cases).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Seatbelt Latch Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The seatbelt latch failing to properly secure the seatbelt.
    *   Solution: Replace the seatbelt latch.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Nissan Frontier, while generally reliable, exhibits a few common issues. Engine problems like timing chain tensioner failure can be costly to repair. Suspension issues such as leaf spring sagging and electrical problems like the blower motor resistor are also common. Frame rust is a concern in certain climates. Overall, while the issues are not overwhelmingly severe, potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their purchasing decision. Costs for repairs can range from a few hundred dollars for minor electrical fixes to several thousand for engine or frame work.
