The 2016 Nissan NV1500 is a full-size cargo van known for its reliability and spacious interior. It comes with two engine options: a 4.0L V6 producing 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.6L V8 producing 317 horsepower and 385 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. Owners appreciate the NV1500's comfortable ride, high roof options, and ample cargo space. Common complaints include fuel economy and somewhat dated interior styling.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

*   **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup. Check engine light may illuminate. Can lead to serious engine damage if not addressed.
    *   Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner. It is recommended to replace the timing chain and guides as well.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**5.6L V8**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Leaf Spring Sagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the van sits lower than the front, especially when loaded. Affects ride quality and handling.
    *   Solution: Replace the rear leaf springs. Consider upgrading to heavier-duty springs if the van is frequently used for heavy loads.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

*   **Sliding Door Contact Plate Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The power sliding door may fail to open or close correctly. Interior lights that are activated when the sliding door opens may not work.
    *   Solution: Replace the sliding door contact plate.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

*   **Backup Camera Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Backup camera displays a blank screen or distorted image. The system may have an error message on the display.
    *   Solution: Replace the backup camera. Check wiring for damage or corrosion.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Roller Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sliding door becomes difficult to open or close. Noises such as grinding or squeaking are heard when operating the door.
    *   Solution: Replace the sliding door rollers and lubricate the track.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+

**Interior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Summary**
The 2016 Nissan NV1500 is generally a reliable van. The most significant potential issue is the timing chain tensioner failure in the 4.0L V6 engine, which can be a costly repair. Rear leaf spring sagging and electrical problems are also fairly common. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potentially major engine repairs.
