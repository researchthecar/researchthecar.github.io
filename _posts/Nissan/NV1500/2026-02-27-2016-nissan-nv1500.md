### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Nissan NV1500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 4.0L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are generally robust and do not present widespread common failures. Suspension and exterior components also hold up well under typical commercial use. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically related to battery drain and potential alternator failure, which are not uncommon for vehicles of this age and usage. Interior components are typically durable. Overall, the NV1500 is a dependable work vehicle with a good track record, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential electrical gremlins are addressed promptly.
