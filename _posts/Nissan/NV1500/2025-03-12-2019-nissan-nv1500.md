The 2019 Nissan NV1500 is a full-size cargo van known for its robust build and practicality. Engine options include a 4.0L V6 engine producing 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.6L V8 engine outputting 375 horsepower and 387 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed automatic transmission. Owners generally appreciate the van's spacious cargo area and powerful engine options. Common dislikes often revolve around fuel economy and the van's somewhat dated interior compared to competitors.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

*   **Timing Chain Rattle**
    *   Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the front of the engine, especially noticeable at startup or idle. This indicates potential wear or stretching of the timing chain.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**5.6L V8**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from various points on the engine, such as the valve covers, oil pan, or rear main seal. Visible oil drips on the ground or oil accumulation on engine components.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaking gaskets or seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ depending on the location of the leak.

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission (Both Engines)**

*   **Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough or jerky shifting between gears, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    *   Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement. In more severe cases, valve body replacement or transmission rebuild may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 for a fluid flush, $800 - $3,000+ for valve body replacement or rebuild.

**Suspension**

*   **Premature Bushing Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, especially when driving over bumps. Can lead to vague steering and reduced handling.
    *   Solution: Replacement of worn bushings, such as control arm bushings or sway bar bushings.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ depending on the specific bushings being replaced.

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle, especially after sitting for a few days.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of parasitic drain and repair of faulty components. This may include replacing the battery or alternator if they are faulty.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ depending on the source of the drain.

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the sliding side door. The door may stick, bind, or require excessive force to operate.
    *   Solution: Lubricating the tracks and rollers. In more severe cases, replacement of damaged rollers or cables.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+ depending on the severity of the issue and the parts needed.

**Interior**

*   **HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The HVAC blower motor stops working, resulting in no airflow from the vents.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the blower motor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary**
The 2019 Nissan NV1500 has some common issues across its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior and interior systems. The engine and transmission issues can be costly. While some issues are relatively minor, others, like timing chain problems or transmission issues, can be more severe and require substantial repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their purchasing decision.
