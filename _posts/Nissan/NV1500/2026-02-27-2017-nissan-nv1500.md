- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Nissan NV1500 demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain (engine and transmission), which are generally robust. The primary areas of concern tend to be electrical, with reports of battery drain and alternator failures being the most common technical issues. These are not necessarily indicative of a systemic flaw but rather components that may require attention over time, especially in a vehicle used for commercial purposes. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free of widespread complaints. Proactive electrical system checks and maintenance are recommended to ensure consistent operation.
