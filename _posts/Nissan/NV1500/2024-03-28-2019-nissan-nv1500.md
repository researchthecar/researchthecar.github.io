**Introduction**

The 2019 Nissan NV1500 is a full-size cargo van offered with two engine options: a 4.0L V6 and a 5.6L V8. The V6 engine produces 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, while the V8 produces 375 horsepower and 394 lb-ft of torque. The NV1500 is available with a 5-speed automatic transmission or a 7-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers appreciate the NV1500's spacious interior and powerful engines. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

* **Spark Plugs Failure:** The spark plugs can fail prematurely, causing misfires and loss of power. This issue can be fixed by replacing the spark plugs. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $300.
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip and damage the engine. This issue requires replacing the timing chain tensioner and timing chain. The estimated cost to fix is $1,000 - $1,500.

**5.6L V8**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** The valve cover gaskets can leak oil, causing a drop in oil levels and engine overheating. This issue can be fixed by replacing the valve cover gaskets. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $300.
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** The intake manifold gasket can leak coolant, causing overheating and reduced engine performance. This issue can be fixed by replacing the intake manifold gasket. The estimated cost to fix is $400 - $600.

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may experience difficulty shifting gears or may shift harshly. This issue can be caused by a faulty shift solenoid or a transmission fluid leak. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,000.

**7-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure:** The leaf springs can fail, causing the vehicle to sag and handle poorly. This issue can be fixed by replacing the leaf springs. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $700 per spring.
* **Shock Absorber Failure:** The shock absorbers can fail, causing the vehicle to bounce and ride rough. This issue can be fixed by replacing the shock absorbers. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $300 per shock.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is off. This issue can be caused by a faulty alternator or a parasitic draw on the battery. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $500.
* **Electrical Shorts:** The electrical system can experience shorts, causing electrical components to fail or malfunction. This issue can be difficult to diagnose and repair. The estimated cost to fix varies depending on the extent of the damage.

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dash Panel Rattle:** The dash panel can rattle, creating a noise while driving. This issue can be fixed by tightening or replacing the dash panel mounting hardware. The estimated cost to fix is $100 - $200.
* **Seatbelts Locking:** The seatbelts may lock prematurely, making it difficult to buckle the seatbelt. This issue can be fixed by replacing the seatbelt buckle. The estimated cost to fix is $100 - $200 per buckle.

**Summary**

The 2019 Nissan NV1500 has a few reported issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and costly. While the NV1500 is generally a reliable vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
