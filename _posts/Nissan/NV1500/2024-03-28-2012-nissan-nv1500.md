**Introduction:**

The 2012 Nissan NV1500 is a full-size commercial van offered in three trim levels (S, SV, and SL). It is powered by a 4.0L V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The NV1500 offers a spacious cargo area, powerful V6 engine, and various safety features. However, owners have reported some issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6 (261 hp, 281 lb-ft torque)**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling sounds from the engine, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 20V727000
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifting, slipping gears
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components (e.g., transmission solenoid, valve body)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaks, whining or grinding sounds, reduced transmission performance
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission seal or gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken rear suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace rear leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting, electrical issues
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rusty Body Panels (SV and SL Trims):**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots forming on body panels, especially around wheel arches and bumpers
    * Solution: Paint touch-ups or body panel replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 14V191000

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling sounds from the dashboard or glove box area
    * Solution: Tighten loose components or replace faulty parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Nissan NV1500 has some known issues that can be expensive to repair. Major issues include timing chain failure and rear leaf spring failure. Other issues, such as shifting problems and electrical issues, are also reported but generally less severe and costly. Overall, owners should be aware of these potential problems and budget accordingly for maintenance and repairs.
