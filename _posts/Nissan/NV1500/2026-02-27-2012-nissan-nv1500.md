## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may not start, battery dies prematurely.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair parasitic draw, replace faulty battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminates, dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Replace alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Nissan NV1500 demonstrates a generally strong reliability record, particularly in its powertrain (engine and transmission), where no significant widespread issues are reported. The primary areas of concern tend to be in the electrical system, with occasional reports of battery drain and alternator failures, which are common wear-and-tear items for vehicles of this age and mileage. Minor issues with exterior components like door lock actuators have also been noted. Overall, the NV1500 is a dependable work vehicle, with most potential problems being manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws.
