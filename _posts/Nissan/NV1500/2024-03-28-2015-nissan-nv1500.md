**Introduction to the 2015 Nissan NV1500**

The 2015 Nissan NV1500 is a full-size commercial van that offers a spacious cargo area and a powerful engine. It is available with a 4.0L V6 engine that produces 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 5-speed automatic transmission. The NV1500 is known for its durability, reliability, and affordability. However, it has some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

**Oil Leak**
Description/Symptoms: A leak can occur from the oil filter adapter, causing oil to accumulate around the engine.
Solution: Replace the oil filter adapter gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

**Hesitation and Jerking**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk during gear changes, especially when shifting into second or third gear.
Solution: Update the transmission control module (TCM) software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
Recalls: None Found

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

**Strut Bearing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The strut bearing can fail, causing a clunking noise when turning or hitting bumps.
Solution: Replace the strut bearings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension**

**Leaf Spring Breakage**
Description/Symptoms: The rear leaf springs can break, causing the vehicle to sag in the rear and become unstable.
Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
Solution: Inspect the electrical system for any parasitic draws.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the draw
Recalls: None Found

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The alternator can fail, causing the battery to not charge and the electrical system to eventually fail.
Solution: Replace the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Dashboard Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard can crack, especially around the air vents.
Solution: Replace the dashboard.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Nissan NV1500 is a generally reliable and affordable commercial van. However, it has some issues that owners should be aware of. The most common issues affect the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable price.
