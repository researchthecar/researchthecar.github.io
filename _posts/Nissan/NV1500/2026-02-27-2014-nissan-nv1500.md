**Power Window Actuator Failure**

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Nissan NV1500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 4.0L V6 engine and 5-speed automatic transmission are generally robust and capable of handling heavy workloads. While minor electrical issues like battery drain and power window problems can occur, and the HVAC blower motor is a known point of failure, these are not typically indicative of systemic design flaws. The suspension and exterior components are also reported to be durable. Overall, the NV1500 is a dependable choice for commercial use, with most potential issues being relatively straightforward and cost-effective to address, contributing to a positive ownership experience for its intended purpose.
