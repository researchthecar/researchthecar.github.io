**2016 Nissan NV1500**

The 2016 Nissan NV1500 is a full-size cargo van offered in three trim levels: S, SV, and SL. It is powered by a 4.0-liter V6 engine that produces 261 horsepower and 281 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The NV1500 has a spacious cargo area with a maximum cargo capacity of 234 cubic feet. It is also available with a variety of features, including Bluetooth connectivity, a rearview camera, and a navigation system.

Owners of the 2016 Nissan NV1500 have reported a number of issues with their vehicles. These issues include:

**Engine**

* **4.0-liter V6 engine**
  * **Intake valve deposits**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon deposits build up on the intake valves, which can cause the engine to run rough and lose power.
    * Solution: The intake valves need to be cleaned.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner fails, which can cause the timing chain to skip and damage the engine.
    * Solution: The timing chain tensioner and timing chain need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed automatic transmission**
  * **Shift solenoid failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The shift solenoid fails, which can cause the transmission to shift hard or not shift at all.
    * Solution: The shift solenoid needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension**
  * **Strut mount bearing failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The strut mount bearing fails, which can cause the front end of the vehicle to make a clunking noise and vibrate.
    * Solution: The strut mount bearing needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical system**
  * **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails, which can cause the battery to drain and the vehicle to stall.
    * Solution: The alternator needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body panels**
  * **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: The body panels rust, which can lead to holes forming in the panels.
    * Solution: The rusted panels need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per panel
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
  * **Cracked dashboard**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard cracks, which can be unsightly and distracting.
    * Solution: The dashboard needs to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall**

The 2016 Nissan NV1500 has a number of issues that can be costly to repair. Some of the issues, such as the intake valve deposits and timing chain tensioner failure, can even lead to catastrophic engine failure. If you are considering purchasing a 2016 Nissan NV1500, be sure to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic before you buy it.
