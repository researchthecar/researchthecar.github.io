**1999 Plymouth Prowler - Engine and Performance**

The 1999 Plymouth Prowler was a limited-edition sports car that featured a unique retro-futuristic design. It was powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine and came standard with a four-speed automatic transmission. The engine produced 214 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque. The Prowler received praise for its striking appearance and handling, but it was also criticized for its limited performance and high price.

**Engine - 3.5L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Camshaft Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, hard starting, reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace camshaft sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
* **Power Steering Pump Failure**
