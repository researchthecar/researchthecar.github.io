**Introduction**

The 1991 Plymouth Laser was a sporty coupe that was offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 2.0-liter four-cylinder that produced 135 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque. A 2.5-liter V6 was also available, which produced 165 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque. Both engines could be paired with a five-speed manual transmission or a four-speed automatic transmission.

The Laser was praised for its sporty handling and stylish design. However, it was also criticized for its lack of interior space and its poor fuel economy.
