# 2016 Audi A8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi A8 delivers a high-end luxury driving experience with powerful engine options and sophisticated interior tech. When shopping for a used model, buyers should carefully review service histories and inspect powertrain components to ensure long-term dependability.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0L supercharged V6 produces around 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, offering responsive acceleration and solid everyday efficiency for a luxury sedan. It is generally considered a dependable powerplant within Audi's lineup when properly maintained.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0L twin-turbo V8 churns out roughly 435 to 450 horsepower, delivering immense thrust and refined performance. Buyers should ensure consistent oil change intervals were followed to protect the turbochargers over high mileage.

### 6.3L W12

The flagship 6.3L W12 engine generates about 500 horsepower, offering ultra-smooth power delivery and supreme exclusivity. Maintenance and repair costs for this complex twelve-cylinder engine can be significantly higher than the standard V6 and V8 variants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with quattro all-wheel drive to provide effortless power delivery across all trims. It is widely regarded as a robust and reliable unit under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V035000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V057000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The fuel supply line for the high pressure fuel pump may become porous and leak fuel. Leaking fuel, in the presence of an ignition source, increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will install a fuel pressure damper, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V397000: Structure:Body:Hood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the seal contacts hot engine components, there could be an increased risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install a retaining plate to prevent the engine compartment seal from loosening. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi A8 is a feature-rich luxury cruiser that offers impressive performance and comfort. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with thorough service records and perform a thorough mechanical inspection before purchase.
