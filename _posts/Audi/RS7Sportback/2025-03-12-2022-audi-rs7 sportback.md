The 2022 Audi RS 7 Sportback is a high-performance luxury sedan known for its sleek design, powerful engine, and advanced technology. It comes standard with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle is praised for its impressive acceleration, comfortable ride, and luxurious interior. Some common dislikes include its high price tag, complicated infotainment system, and potential for expensive repairs.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbo V8**
*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the engine, loss of power, check engine light. Potential oil leaks around the turbocharger housing.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty turbocharger unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $8,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil spots under the car, burning oil smell, low oil level warning light. Oil may leak from valve cover gaskets, rear main seal, or turbocharger oil lines.
    *   Solution: Replacing the leaking gaskets or seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifts, particularly between 1st and 2nd gear. Hesitation when accelerating from a stop.
    *   Solution: Software update/reprogramming of the transmission control module (TCM). In some cases, valve body replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Transmission Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, inability to shift gears, warning messages on the dashboard, complete loss of drive.
    *   Solution: Transmission replacement or rebuild.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $6,000 - $10,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sitting unevenly, air compressor not working, "Suspension Fault" warning light.
    *   Solution: Replacing the air struts, air compressor, or ride height sensors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The MMI (Multi Media Interface) screen freezes, reboots unexpectedly, or becomes unresponsive.
    *   Solution: Software update, resetting the system, or replacing the MMI control unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Parking Sensors, Blind Spot Monitoring)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: False alarms or non-functioning parking sensors, blind spot monitoring system not working correctly.
    *   Solution: Replacing the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ per sensor
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Headlight LED Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Partial or complete failure of the LED daytime running lights or headlights.
    *   Solution: Replacing the headlight assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+ per headlight
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, excessive wind noise, water leaks.
    *   Solution: Adjusting or replacing the sunroof motor, track, or seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Rattles and Squeaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Annoying noises emanating from the dashboard, doors, or rear deck.
    *   Solution: Identifying the source of the noise and tightening or replacing loose components. Often difficult and time-consuming to diagnose.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Audi RS 7 Sportback is a performance-oriented vehicle that can be prone to expensive repairs. The most serious potential issues revolve around the engine (turbocharger failure, oil leaks) and transmission (rough shifting, potential failure). Electrical issues with the infotainment system and suspension malfunctions are also common. While the vehicle offers exhilarating performance and a luxurious experience, potential owners should be aware of the possibility of costly repairs. The severity and cost of repairs can vary greatly, but some issues like turbocharger or transmission failure can easily exceed $5,000-$10,000.
