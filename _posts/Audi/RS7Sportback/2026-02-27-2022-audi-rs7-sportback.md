# 2022 Audi RS7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi RS7 Sportback is a high-performance luxury fastback blending ferocious twin-turbo V8 power with everyday hatchback practicality and high-tech cabin amenities. For prospective used-car buyers, this flagship performance machine demands careful inspection due to its complex powertrain, high-strung electronics, and steep maintenance requirements typical of ultra-luxury German engineering.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. While generally robust, its complexity means that out-of-warranty mechanical repairs can be exceptionally costly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power efficiently to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system with rapid, smooth gear changes. Routine fluid changes are essential to maintain its performance and longevity under high-torque loads.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi RS7 Sportback is an exhilarating and luxurious grand tourer, but prospective buyers should approach with eyes wide open regarding potential running costs. Always secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an Audi specialist and verify service history before finalizing a purchase.
