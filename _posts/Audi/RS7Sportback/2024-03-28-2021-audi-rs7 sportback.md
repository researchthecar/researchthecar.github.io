**Introduction**

The 2021 Audi RS7 Sportback is a high-performance luxury hatchback that combines sleek styling, powerful performance, and advanced technology. It comes equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, producing 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. The RS7 Sportback boasts an impressive acceleration of 0-60 mph in 3.6 seconds, with a top track speed limited to 190 mph. Despite its sporty nature, the RS7 Sportback offers a comfortable and well-equipped interior with premium materials and advanced infotainment features.

**Engine**

* **4.0-liter Twin-Turbocharged V8**

    * **Intake Valve Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of intake valves leading to reduced engine performance, misfires, or engine stalling.
    Solution: Replacement of affected intake valves and associated components.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V813000

    * **Turbocharger Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning of turbochargers causing reduced boost pressure, loss of power, or excessive turbo noise.
    Solution: Replacement of faulty turbochargers.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+ per turbocharger
    Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**

    * **Transmission Control Module Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Faulty transmission control module leading to transmission malfunction, shifting problems, or loss of drive.
    Solution: Reprogramming or replacement of the transmission control module.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    Recalls: None Found

    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
    Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering experienced during acceleration or under load.
    Solution: Replacement of the torque converter.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    Description/Symptoms: Intermittent freezing, lagging, or display issues with the infotainment system.
    Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Wiring Harness Failures**
    Description/Symptoms: Faulty wiring harnesses leading to electrical malfunctions, component failures, or loss of power.
    Solution: Inspection and replacement of affected wiring harnesses.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ per harness
    Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
    Description/Symptoms: Water seepage or leaks through the sunroof during heavy rain.
    Solution: Inspection, resealing, or sunroof replacement.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Spoiler Malfunction**
    Description/Symptoms: Inoperative or slow-retracting rear spoiler.
    Solution: Replacement of rear spoiler motor or hydraulics.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete failure of the windshield wiper motor, leading to poor visibility during rain.
    Solution: Replacement of the wiper motor and associated components.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature regulation or failure of climate control settings.
    Solution: Inspection and repair or replacement of faulty components in the climate control system.
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Audi RS7 Sportback, while a powerful and luxurious performance vehicle, has exhibited several common issues that owners have reported. The most significant concerns lie in the engine, specifically with intake valve failures and turbocharger malfunctions, which can lead to costly repairs. Some transmission problems, including control module failures and torque converter shudder, have also been encountered. Electrical glitches and leaks in the exterior are other reported issues, though the severity of these problems varies. Interior issues, such as wiper motor failures and climate control malfunctions, are present but less common. Overall, the RS7 Sportback's reliability may be compromised by these recurring problems, which can impact the ownership experience and increase maintenance costs.
