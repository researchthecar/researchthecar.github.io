# 2021 Audi RS7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi RS7 Sportback is a high-performance luxury fastback blending staggering twin-turbo V8 power with everyday usability and striking styling. As a complex high-end performance vehicle, prospective buyers should approach used models with an understanding that maintenance, specialized care, and potential out-of-warranty repairs can carry substantial costs.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering straight-line acceleration. This powerhouse pairs robust performance engineering with advanced mild-hybrid technology, though buyers should verify complete service history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels the V8's massive output to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system with rapid, seamless gear changes. It offers a refined cruising character alongside aggressive manual paddle-shifting capability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V749000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The electrical connector can detach from the door crash sensor in either of the front doors. A detached connector can delay the activation of the restraint systems, including the side air bag, curtain air bag and/or seat-belt pretensioners during a side impact crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and as necessary, replace the crash sensors in both front doors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V339000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Cracks may cause engine damage and a stall or an oil leak, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi RS7 Sportback remains an elite luxury performance machine offering supercar-rivaling speed combined with executive comfort. Used car buyers should prioritize examples with documented dealer service records and budget accordingly for premium upkeep.
