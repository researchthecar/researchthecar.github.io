**2021 Audi Q3: Issue Report**

The 2021 Audi Q3 is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 2.5-liter five-cylinder engine with 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, and a 2.0-liter four-cylinder diesel engine with 184 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

People commonly enjoy the Q3's refined interior, ample cargo space, and responsive handling. However, it has received criticism for its high price point and lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, decreased power. Solution: Intake valve cleaning. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Timing Chain Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, loss of power. Solution: Timing chain replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $5000+
* **Coolant Leak from Water Pump:** Description/Symptoms: Visible coolant leak, overheating engine. Solution: Water pump replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

**2.5-Liter Five-Cylinder Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**2.0-Liter Four-Cylinder Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust. Solution: Turbocharger replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $4000+
* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:** Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption. Solution: DPF cleaning or replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, reduced power. Solution: Fuel injector replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+ per injector

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Hesitations or Slipping:** Description/Symptoms: Transmission does not shift smoothly, slipping occurs during acceleration. Solution: Transmission fluid flush, solenoid replacement, or rebuild. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Loss of gears, transmission doesn't engage. Solution: Mechatronic unit replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $5000+

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Dry Clutch Wear:** Description/Symptoms: Clutch shudder, slipping, difficulty engaging gears. Solution: Clutch replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Loss of gears, transmission doesn't engage. Solution: Mechatronic unit replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $5000+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Bushing Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noise from suspension, reduced ride comfort. Solution: Strut bushing replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues:** Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, such as malfunctioning lights, or power windows. Solution: Electrical harness repair or replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
* **Battery Discharge:** Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle. Solution: Battery replacement or charging system repair. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Clogging:** Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin during heavy rain. Solution: Sunroof drain cleaning. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Taillight Condensation:** Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the taillights. Solution: Taillight replacement or seal repair. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Description/Symptoms: System freezing, slow performance, software bugs. Solution: Software update or infotainment module replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
* **Steering Wheel Control Interruptions:** Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel controls become unresponsive or intermittent. Solution: Steering wheel control module repair or replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000+
* **Panoramic Sunroof Shade Wear:** Description/Symptoms: Sunshade material tears or detaches. Solution: Sunshade panel replacement. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

**Summary**

The 2021 Audi Q3 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some common issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The most severe and expensive problems include timing chain failure, transmission slipping, and mechatronic unit failures. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be costly and require major repairs. It's important for potential owners to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a Q3.
