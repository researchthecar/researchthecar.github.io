# 2022 Audi Q3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi Q3 brings luxury subcompact SUV buyers a refined cabin, standard Quattro all-wheel drive, and sharp styling. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern tech and safety features, though prospective buyers should evaluate how well its powertrain options suit their driving style.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. It offers a balanced blend of efficiency and everyday drivability for city commuting.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

The higher-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates roughly 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides punchier acceleration and confident passing power for highway driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

This traditional 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with the base engine, delivering smooth and predictable shifts under normal driving conditions.

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission

This quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with the higher-output engine, offering sporty responsiveness though low-speed urban crawling may occasionally feel abrupt.

## Recall Check

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V673000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A damaged vent pipe may allow fuel to spill out of the filler neck when the fuel cap is opened, increasing the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the internal vent pipe for damage and replace the charcoal canister and the fuel tank, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V701000: Visibility:Glass, Side/Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A broken rear window may result in loose pieces of glass and increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will check the manufacturing date of the vehicle's rear window and replace it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi Q3 remains a stylish and well-appointed option in the luxury subcompact crossover segment. While powertrain options offer distinct performance tiers, thorough pre-purchase inspections and test drives remain essential to ensure comfort with transmission behavior.
