**2020 Audi Q3**

The 2020 Audi Q3 is a compact luxury SUV that competes with the likes of the Mercedes-Benz GLA, BMW X1, and Volvo XC40. It is available with a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine producing 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Q3 also comes standard with Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system.

While the Q3 has received praise for its stylish design, luxurious interior, and powerful engine, it has also been plagued by numerous issues that have caused frustration for many owners.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged 4-cylinder Engine**

    * **Piston Ring Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, and engine knocking
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine at startup, decreased engine power, and check engine light
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed shifting, and loss of gears
        * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA 20V-085

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting, and electrical system failure
        * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or investigate other potential causes of the drain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
        * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or adjust the sunroof alignment
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
        * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slow response time, freezing, and glitches
        * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the head unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **Trim Rattles**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling trim panels in the cabin
        * Solution: Tighten or replace the affected trim panels
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2020 Audi Q3 has been plagued by a range of issues, from major engine and transmission problems to minor interior rattles. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be catastrophic and extremely costly. The severity and cost of these issues vary greatly depending on the problem, and some issues may be more common than others. However, the presence of numerous recalls and complaints from owners suggests that potential buyers should carefully consider the potential risks before purchasing a 2020 Audi Q3.
