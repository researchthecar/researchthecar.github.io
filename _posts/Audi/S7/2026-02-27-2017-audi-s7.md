# 2017 Audi S7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi S7 is a high-performance luxury sportback known for its powerful twin-turbo V8 engine and refined interior. While offering thrilling driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding powertrain behavior and electrical components to ensure a sound pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust horsepower and substantial torque, providing swift acceleration. Proper maintenance of the oil supply system is critical for long-term reliability.

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden turbocharger failure accompanied by oil supply issues and related dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspect turbochargers and oil strainers thoroughly; replace failed components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission pairs with quattro all-wheel drive for rapid gear changes. Smooth operation requires diligent fluid changes and attentive diagnostics during test drives.

**Transmission Hesitation and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected cutting out, warning lights, stalling in traffic, and harsh jerking behavior upon restarting.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning for transmission fault codes and software updates or hardware servicing by an Audi specialist.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Manages vehicle electronics and power distribution.

**Electrical Short and Burning Odor**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning lights illuminating simultaneously followed by a burning electrical smell and battery terminal issues.
- **Solution:** Inspect the battery terminals, charging system wiring harness, and electrical grounds for loose connections or shorts.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Provides exterior lighting and visibility features.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass panel shattering unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for stress fractures and replace the assembly if damage or structural weakness is found.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi S7 balances executive luxury with supercar performance, but its complex engineering demands careful evaluation. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the turbochargers, transmission responsiveness, and electrical health to avoid costly unexpected repairs.
