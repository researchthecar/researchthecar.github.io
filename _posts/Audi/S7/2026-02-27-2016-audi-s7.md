# 2016 Audi S7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Audi S7, buyers are drawn to its striking design and twin-turbo V8 performance. However, reviewing owner-reported data reveals certain reliability concerns, particularly regarding sudden engine stalls and transmission operation. Being aware of these potential mechanical hiccups can help prospective buyers properly evaluate a used model before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2016 Audi S7 features a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 450 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. While delivering exhilarating performance, buyers should watch closely for unexpected stalling or electrical-related engine shutdowns reported by some drivers.

**Sudden Engine Failure / Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported the vehicle losing engine power unexpectedly while driving at high speeds or in traffic, sometimes accompanied by multiple dashboard warning lights or failing to crank afterward.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to identify the root cause, which may involve electrical, fuel delivery, or internal mechanical components.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed through a quick-shifting 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive the transmission for smooth operation, as mechatronic unit failures can lead to expensive repairs.

**Mechatronic Unit and Shifting Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice random jerking when pulling away from a stop, harsh engagement when shifting into reverse, sudden transmission error codes, or complete loss of drive power.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit or transmission assembly may be necessary depending on the severity of internal wear.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi S7 offers thrilling luxury-sport performance, but used-car buyers should proceed with caution and verify service history. Pay close attention to transmission shift quality and ensure the engine runs smoothly during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid costly unexpected repairs.
