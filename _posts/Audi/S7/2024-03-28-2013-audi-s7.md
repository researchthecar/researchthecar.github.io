**Introduction**

The 2013 Audi S7 is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 420 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission (S tronic). The S7 is known for its sleek design, spacious interior, and responsive handling, but it has received some negative feedback regarding certain issues.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, burning of oil, blue or white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whining or whistling noises, increased oil consumption
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (S tronic)**

* **Clutch Plate Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering during gear changes, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Clutch plate replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission not shifting properly, loss of power, warning lights on the dashboard
    * Solution: Mechatronic unit replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises from front suspension, reduced handling and stability
    * Solution: Strut replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Faults:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random electrical issues, electronic malfunctions, warning lights on the dashboard
    * Solution: Electrical diagnostics and repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the interior from the panoramic sunroof
    * Solution: Sunroof cassette replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Audi S7 is a desirable luxury performance sedan, but it has been plagued by a number of issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. These problems can be costly to repair, and some can impact safety. While the S7 offers impressive performance and a comfortable interior, potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase decision.
