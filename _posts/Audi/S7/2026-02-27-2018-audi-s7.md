# 2018 Audi S7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi S7 blends executive luxury with twin-turbocharged performance, offering serious power for enthusiast buyers. While formal owner complaints for this model year are minimal, prospective buyers should always evaluate powertrain health and complex onboard electronics closely during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Turbocharged

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces roughly 450 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is generally robust when maintained on strict oil-change intervals, though proper turbo cool-down and maintenance history are essential.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes, though fluid maintenance is critical to ensure long-term clutch and mechatronic unit health.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi S7 remains a compelling luxury performance hatchback with very few reported widespread owner grievances in initial data. Buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection focusing on the twin-turbo V8 and dual-clutch transmission to ensure trouble-free ownership.
