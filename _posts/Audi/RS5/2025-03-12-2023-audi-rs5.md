The 2023 Audi RS5 is a high-performance coupe and Sportback known for its blend of luxury, sporty handling, and powerful engine. The RS5 is equipped with a 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It is paired exclusively with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. Owners often praise the RS5's exhilarating performance, sophisticated interior, and stylish design. Some common dislikes include the relatively firm ride, the price point, and limited rear passenger space.

**Engine**

**2.9L Twin-Turbo V6**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the engine, loss of power, check engine light.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty turbocharger.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil spots under the car, burning oil smell, low oil level warning. Often occurs near the valve cover gaskets or rear main seal.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**

*   **Rough Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or jerky shifts, especially between certain gears.
    *   Solution: Perform a transmission fluid flush and potentially reprogram the transmission control module (TCM). If the problem persists, valve body replacement or transmission rebuild may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 (fluid flush) - $4,000+ (transmission rebuild)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Adaptive Damper Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Warning light on the dashboard, ride quality feels uneven or overly stiff, suspension not adjusting properly to different driving modes.
    *   Solution: Diagnose the affected damper and replace it.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ per damper
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The MMI system freezes, becomes unresponsive, or crashes and restarts intermittently.
    *   Solution: Software update to the MMI system. In more severe cases, replacement of the MMI control unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1,500+ (MMI unit replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Various warning lights illuminating due to faulty sensors, such as parking sensors, tire pressure sensors, or blind-spot monitoring sensors.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ per sensor
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sunroof/Moonroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening/closing properly, getting stuck, or making unusual noises. Water leaks into the cabin.
    *   Solution: Clean and lubricate the sunroof tracks, repair or replace damaged components, or replace the entire sunroof assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 (cleaning/lubrication) - $2,000+ (sunroof assembly replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Seat Heater Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working, only working intermittently, or overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty seat heating element or control module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+ per seat
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Audi RS5 is a performance vehicle that offers a thrilling driving experience, but some potential issues can arise. Engine issues such as turbocharger failures and oil leaks can be costly. Transmission issues related to rough shifting can be mild or severe. Electrical issues with the infotainment system and sensors can be frustrating. While not all RS5s will experience these problems, it is important to be aware of them when considering purchasing or maintaining this vehicle. The severity of the issues ranges from minor inconveniences like infotainment glitches to major mechanical problems like turbo failure, resulting in a wide range of potential repair costs.
