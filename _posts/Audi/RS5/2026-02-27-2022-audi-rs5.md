# 2022 Audi RS5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi RS5 delivers blistering performance and everyday luxury, powered by a sophisticated twin-turbocharged powertrain. As a high-performance modern luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should focus on proper maintenance history and thorough pre-purchase inspections to ensure optimal mechanical and electrical health.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers thrilling acceleration and responsive power delivery, requiring diligent routine maintenance to ensure long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes paired with quattro all-wheel drive. It is generally robust when fluid change intervals are properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V102000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire and Loading Information." While referencing the B-pillar label, the operator may overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a corrected label on the B-pillar, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi RS5 is a formidable high-performance luxury vehicle combining daily usability with track-ready capability. Buyers should verify regular service intervals and conduct a comprehensive inspection to enjoy this sports coupe or sportback with peace of mind.
