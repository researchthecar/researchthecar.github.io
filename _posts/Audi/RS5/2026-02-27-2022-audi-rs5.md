# 2022 Audi RS5

### Overview
The 2022 Audi RS5, a high-performance variant of the A5 lineup, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its potent powertrain. It features a powerful 2.9L twin-turbocharged V6 engine paired with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. While the RS5 offers exhilarating performance, like any performance-oriented vehicle, it can experience specific issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining key areas such as the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, exterior, and interior. Owners generally report a positive ownership experience with a focus on performance and luxury, though attention to specific components is advised for long-term ownership.

---


## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of seals, gaskets, or oil pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whistling noises from the engine bay, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Inspection, repair, or replacement of turbocharger components.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable delays or rough shifts during acceleration or deceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi RS5 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability for a high-performance vehicle. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the potent powertrain, specifically potential oil leaks from the engine and occasional issues with the turbochargers. The transmission, while generally robust, can sometimes exhibit minor shifting anomalies. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system's software, are also noted. However, these issues are not universally reported and often fall within the expected range for performance cars. The suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread technical defects. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging symptoms are key to ensuring a positive and reliable ownership experience with the 2022 Audi RS5.
