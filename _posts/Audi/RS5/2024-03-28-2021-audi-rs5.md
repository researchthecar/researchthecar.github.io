**Introduction to the 2021 Audi RS5**

The 2021 Audi RS5 is a high-performance sports car with a 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine that produces 444 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque. It is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission and comes standard with Quattro all-wheel drive. The RS5 is known for its impressive performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technology features. However, owners have reported several issues with the vehicle, as detailed below.

**Engine**

* **2.9-liter Twin-Turbocharged V6**

  * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, misfires
    * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

  * **Shift Hesitation and Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitating or jerking during shifts
    * Solution: Software update or transmission replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**

  * **Clunking Noises**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking sounds from the front suspension while driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged suspension components (e.g., control arms, ball joints)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining rapidly, even with the key fob out of the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace faulty battery or investigate electrical system for current draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation accumulating inside the headlights
    * Solution: Replace or repair faulty headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or noise coming from the sunroof area
    * Solution: Adjust or repair sunroof mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Audi RS5 is a capable and enjoyable performance car. However, it is not without its issues. The engine and transmission are the most common trouble spots, with reported problems including carbon buildup on the intake valves and shift hesitation/jerking. Other concerns include suspension clunking noises, electrical battery drain, exterior headlight condensation, and interior sunroof rattles. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but some can be significant and impact the overall ownership experience.
