The 2022 Audi RS5 is a performance-oriented coupe and Sportback known for its stylish design, powerful engine, and engaging driving dynamics. Powered by a 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 engine producing 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, the RS5 delivers impressive acceleration and performance. The engine is exclusively paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Owners often praise the RS5 for its comfortable interior, refined ride quality (despite its performance focus), and distinctive aesthetics. Some criticisms include a relatively high price point and a less engaging driving experience compared to some competitors.

**Engine**

**2.9-Liter Twin-Turbo V6**

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported coolant leaks originating from the auxiliary water pump or associated hoses. Symptoms include low coolant levels, visible coolant leaks under the vehicle, and potential overheating in severe cases.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty auxiliary water pump and/or damaged coolant hoses.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing rough or delayed shifts, particularly when downshifting. This can be more noticeable in sportier driving modes.
    *   Solution: Software update and/or transmission fluid flush. In some cases, valve body replacement may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 (Software) - $2,000+ (Valve Body)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   No Issues Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Lagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The MMI infotainment system can occasionally freeze, lag, or become unresponsive. This includes issues with the touchscreen, navigation, and audio functions.
    *   Solution: Software updates from Audi can address some of these issues. A system reset may also provide temporary relief. Hardware replacement is rare but possible.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $1,500+ (Hardware Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: There are some reports of driver-assistance systems throwing false alarms due to faulty sensors. This can include pre-sense warnings, lane departure warnings, and parking sensor issues.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of the affected sensor(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ per sensor
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **LED Headlight Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The LED headlights can exhibit flickering, dimming, or complete failure. This is more common in areas with extreme weather conditions.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty headlight unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Creaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing rattling or creaking noises coming from the dashboard, door panels, or rear seats.
    *   Solution: Tightening of loose components, application of felt tape to contact points, or replacement of defective parts. This often requires careful diagnosis to pinpoint the source of the noise.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Sunroof malfunction**
     *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly
     *   Solution: Repair or replacement of sunroof motor or related parts
     *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$1500+
     *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Audi RS5 is a desirable performance car, but like any vehicle, it can have its share of issues. Based on available data, the most commonly reported problems revolve around the infotainment system, and minor engine coolant leaks. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potentially costly repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these common complaints and have the vehicle thoroughly inspected, especially focusing on the coolant system and the functionality of the infotainment system. While the costs associated with these repairs can vary, addressing them promptly can prevent further damage and ensure a more enjoyable ownership experience.
