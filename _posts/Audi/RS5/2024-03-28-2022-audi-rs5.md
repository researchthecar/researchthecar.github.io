**Introduction:**

The 2022 Audi RS5 is a high-performance luxury coupe with aggressive styling and a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine that produces 444 horsepower and 442 lb-ft of torque. The RS5 is available with an 8-speed automatic transmission and standard Quattro all-wheel drive.

Although the RS5 has many positive attributes, there are some common issues that owners have reported:

**Engine**

* **2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail prematurely, causing the timing chain to become loose and potentially jump over several teeth. This can result in a catastrophic engine failure.
        * **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $4,000+
        * **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V189000
    * **Fuel Pump Failure:** The high-pressure fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to lose power or stall.
        * **Solution:** Replacement of the fuel pump.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Clutch Pack Wear:** The clutch packs within the transmission can wear prematurely, leading to slipping, shuddering, or loss of power.
        * **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch packs and related components.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, especially when the vehicle is parked.
        * **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of electrical system faults, potentially including replacement of the battery.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $600+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system can experience various issues, including screen freezing, software glitches, and connectivity problems.
        * **Solution:** System updates, hardware replacement, or software reprogramming.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $1,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Paint Peel:** The paint can peel or chip prematurely on certain areas of the vehicle, such as the hood or trunk.
        * **Solution:** Refinishing of the affected areas.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Window Regulator Failure:** The window regulators can fail, causing the windows to become stuck or inoperable.
        * **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulators.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $400+ per window
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system can malfunction, leading to insufficient heating or cooling performance.
        * **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of electrical components, potentially including replacement of the climate control module.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Cracking Dashboard:** The dashboard can develop cracks or warping over time.
        * **Solution:** Dashboard replacement.
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,200 - $2,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Audi RS5 is a powerful and exciting vehicle, but it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most serious issues include timing chain tensioner failure and clutch pack wear. Electrical and infotainment system problems are also common, as well as some exterior and interior issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, and owners should budget accordingly for potential repairs.
