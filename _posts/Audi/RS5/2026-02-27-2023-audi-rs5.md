# 2023 Audi RS5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi RS5 delivers blistering performance and everyday luxury, powered by a robust twin-turbocharged V6 engine paired with a responsive automatic transmission. While luxury high-performance coupes and sportbacks of this caliber command close attention to routine maintenance, used buyers will find a generally well-engineered package backed by very few owner complaints.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L TFSI V6

This 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, offering immense power and rapid acceleration. It is generally robust when maintained on schedule, though premium fuel and strict oil change intervals are non-negotiable for longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The conventional 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and manages high torque loads effectively while distributing power through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It proves mechanically durable under normal operating conditions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi RS5 stands as a highly capable and thrilling choice for enthusiasts seeking a daily-drivable luxury sport vehicle. Prospective buyers should still prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify complete service history to ensure optimal mechanical health.
