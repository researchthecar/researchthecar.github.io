# 2021 Audi RS5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi RS5 delivers thrilling high-performance driving dynamics paired with everyday luxury, available in both coupe and Sportback body styles. Beneath its sculpted exterior lies a potent powertrain engineered for maximum grip and speed. While standard safety recalls and minor owner reports exist, a pre-purchase inspection remains vital to verify overall mechanical and electronic health before buying.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration across all driving conditions. This powerplant is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should watch for standard high-performance fluid maintenance intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power efficiently to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system while offering rapid, smooth gear changes. It suits both spirited driving and comfortable cruising, though routine fluid service is recommended to ensure long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V737000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harness for the rear lights could be missing the anti-abrasion protection in the area of the spare wheel well. Without the scuff protection, the wires may become damaged during a rear crash, disabling the doors from being opened from the outside, causing a loss of hazard lights, and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will apply scuff protection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi RS5 combines aggressive styling and exceptional performance into a versatile luxury package. Prospective buyers should ensure all manufacturer recalls have been addressed and obtain a thorough independent mechanical inspection to guarantee a reliable ownership experience.
