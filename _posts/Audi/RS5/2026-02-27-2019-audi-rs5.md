# 2019 Audi RS5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2019 Audi RS5 offers an exhilarating blend of luxury and high-performance engineering. While owner reports for this high-performance model are generally sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect specific luxury and tech features closely during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

This 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration. It is generally robust, though performance variants require diligent maintenance and premium fuel.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid shifts paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers strong reliability when routine fluid services are maintained.

## Electrical

### Emergency Call (SOS) System

Electrical reports indicate potential concerns with cabin communication features such as the SOS emergency call system.

**SOS System Malfunction and Cable Short**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an intermittent, loud SOS alert while driving accompanied by a diagnostic trouble code due to a cable short.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the wiring harness and replace the defective cable as necessary.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body components generally hold up well, though isolated glass incidents have been noted by owners.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof may spontaneously shatter while driving, producing a loud noise and scattering glass shards into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for pre-existing stress fractures and replace the glass assembly if failure occurs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V760000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a rear crash, the incorrect cover can cause the 12-volt power supply to be compromised, preventing the vehicle doors from being opened from the outside, or the hazard warning lights from functioning, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the positive terminal cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi RS5 is an impressive performance luxury vehicle with very few documented owner complaints on file. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and glass components to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
