**Introduction**

The 2014 Audi RS5 is a high-performance coupe that combines luxury and power. It is equipped with a 4.2L V8 engine that produces 450 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The RS5 is known for its impressive acceleration, handling, and overall driving experience. However, like any car, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

**4.2L V8**

* **Intake manifold flaps failing:** The intake manifold flaps can fail, causing a loss of power and decreased fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil leaks:** The RS5 can develop oil leaks from various areas, including the valve cover gaskets and oil pan gasket.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch**

* **Clutch slipping:** The clutch can slip, causing difficulty shifting and a loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise:** The front suspension can develop a rattling or knocking noise, especially over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace the front suspension components, such as the control arms or ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical system malfunctions:** The RS5 can experience various electrical issues, including battery drain, lighting problems, and infotainment system glitches.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the specific electrical component causing the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear spoiler rattle:** The rear spoiler can rattle or vibrate at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Adjust or tighten the spoiler mounting bolts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door lock actuators failing:** The door lock actuators can fail, resulting in difficulty locking or unlocking the doors.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof creaking:** The sunroof can develop a creaking or rattling noise when opening or closing.
    * Solution: Lubricate the sunroof tracks or adjust the sunroof alignment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2014 Audi RS5 is a well-regarded high-performance coupe, it is not immune to issues. The engine and transmission are generally reliable, but they can experience some problems that can be costly to fix. The suspension and electrical components may also require attention, but these issues are typically less severe. Overall, the RS5 is a solid car, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
