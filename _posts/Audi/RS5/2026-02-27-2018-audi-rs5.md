# 2018 Audi RS5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi RS5 offers high-performance luxury coupe styling paired with a twin-turbocharged V6 engine. Buyers looking at this model should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, keeping in mind that technology features rely on cellular networks that evolve over time.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces 443 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust performance and solid overall reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power to Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers smooth, quick shifts suited for both daily driving and spirited runs.

## Electrical

### 3G Telematics and Connectivity

Electrical and infotainment systems function well overall, though older vehicle communication hardware can be affected by telecom network transitions.

**Loss of 3G Connected Services**
- **Symptoms:** Certain built-in telematics and emergency services notifications become inactive due to cellular network shutdowns.
- **Solution:** Owners are generally informed by the manufacturer of the service discontinuation, as hardware upgrades are typically unavailable.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V760000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a rear crash, the incorrect cover can cause the 12-volt power supply to be compromised, preventing the vehicle doors from being opened from the outside, or the hazard warning lights from functioning, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the positive terminal cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi RS5 is a powerful and generally reliable luxury performance car. Used-car buyers should verify the operational status of all onboard electronics and telematics features, keeping in mind that older cellular network dependencies may impact certain connected services.
