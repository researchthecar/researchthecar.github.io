# 2021 Audi SQ5

### Overview
The 2021 Audi SQ5 is a performance-oriented compact luxury SUV that generally offers a high level of build quality and technological integration. As a performance variant, it commands a premium driving experience with a potent engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system. Reliability for the 2021 model year is largely positive, with owners reporting satisfaction with its powertrain and core systems. However, like many modern vehicles with advanced technology, there are a few areas where minor concerns have been noted, primarily related to infotainment and specific electronic components. Overall, the SQ5 is considered a dependable choice within its segment, balancing sportiness with practicality.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi SQ5 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain (3.0L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission). Most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal mechanical issues. The primary areas of concern, though not widespread, tend to be related to the optional adaptive air suspension, which can develop leaks or compressor issues, and occasional glitches within the infotainment system, often resolvable with software updates. Electrical issues like battery drain are also noted but are not endemic. Overall, the SQ5 is a dependable performance SUV, with potential buyers advised to be aware of the specific components that may require attention over time, especially if opting for the air suspension.
