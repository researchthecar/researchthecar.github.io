# 2020 Audi SQ5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi SQ5, buyers get a high-performance luxury crossover combining sporty driving dynamics with upscale cabin appointments. While owner reports to federal regulators highlight a few electronic and mechanical items to watch, prospective buyers should review these common owner experiences to make an informed decision.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (EA839)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine (EA839) delivers strong performance with around 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It generally provides brisk acceleration, though buyers should periodically inspect the cooling system for leaks.

**Water Pump and Vacuum System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks can develop from the water pump, potentially damaging the connected vacuum unit and triggering warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and cooling system for leaks, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers refined gear changes suited to both spirited driving and relaxed commuting.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior glass and trim require normal inspection during a used vehicle evaluation.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may unexpectedly fracture or implode while driving at highway speeds without an apparent external impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass panel for any existing stress cracks and consult insurance or dealers if replacement is needed.

## Interior

### SOS Emergency Calling System

On-board electronics manage various convenience and safety features, which can occasionally display software or module warnings.

**SOS System Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** A persistent warning light appears on the dashboard indicating that the emergency calling system has failed or is unavailable.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician can determine if a control module reset or hardware replacement is required.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance & Parking Sensors

Advanced safety systems provide helpful driver assistance, but sensor calibration is crucial for proper operation.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the pre-sense or rear parking sensors unexpectedly applied the emergency brakes when no obstacles were present.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer check sensor alignment and software calibration if false activation occurs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V947000: Electrical System:Body Control Module/Bcm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover on the gateway control module and seal an underbody seam as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V505000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Parts of the front seat frame and backrest adjuster may not have been welded properly. In the event of a rear-end crash, the backrest adjustment could break, possibly reducing the amount of occupant restraint, thus increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front seats, and, as necessary, replace the seatback frames, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi SQ5 offers an engaging blend of luxury and performance, though used car buyers should carefully inspect the cooling system, test all driver-assistance technologies, and verify that electronics operate without warning lights during a pre-purchase inspection.
