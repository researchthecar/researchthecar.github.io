The 2021 Audi SQ5 is a performance-oriented SUV known for its engaging driving dynamics, luxurious interior, and sleek styling. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise its powerful engine, comfortable ride, and upscale cabin. However, some criticisms include a somewhat firm ride (especially with larger wheels), and the infotainment system can be distracting.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks originating from the oil cooler lines or the rear main seal. Symptoms include visible oil drips under the vehicle, a burning oil smell, and low oil levels.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty oil cooler lines or rear main seal.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ (depending on the source and labor).

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks around the thermostat housing or water pump. Symptoms include low coolant levels, overheating, and visible coolant leaks.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the thermostat housing, water pump, or related seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)**

*   **Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rough or jerky shifting, particularly during low-speed maneuvers or when downshifting.
    *   Solution: Software update, transmission fluid flush, or in more severe cases, valve body replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2000+ (depending on the severity and required repairs)

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Issues (If Equipped)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: For vehicles equipped with the optional air suspension, some owners have reported issues with the air struts leaking or the air compressor failing. This can lead to a sagging suspension, uneven ride height, or a "suspension fault" warning light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty air struts or air compressor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $3000+ (per strut or compressor replacement)

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system (MMI) can experience glitches, freezes, or lag. This can affect navigation, audio, and other vehicle functions.
    *   Solution: Software update or, in some cases, replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1500+ (unit replacement)

*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Issues with sensors related to driver-assistance features (e.g., blind-spot monitoring, lane departure warning, parking sensors). Symptoms include error messages or malfunctioning systems.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ (per sensor).

**Exterior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported problems with the sunroof not opening or closing properly, or unusual noises during operation.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubrication of sunroof tracks, or replacement of faulty components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Interior**

*   **Rattling Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reports of rattling noises emanating from the dashboard or trim panels.
    *   Solution: Identification and tightening or replacement of loose components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

The 2021 Audi SQ5, while generally reliable and well-regarded, has some common issues owners have reported. The engine can develop oil and coolant leaks. The transmission can have harsh shifting issues. The air suspension, if equipped, can be problematic and expensive to fix. Electronic glitches within the infotainment system and sensor malfunctions have also been reported. Interior rattling and sunroof issues round out common concerns. Depending on the issue, repairs can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand. While many owners experience no issues, it's important to be aware of these potential problems when considering a used 2021 Audi SQ5.
