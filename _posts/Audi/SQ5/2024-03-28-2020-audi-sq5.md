The 2020 Audi SQ5 is a luxury performance SUV that offers an impressive blend of power, handling, and comfort. It comes standard with a 349-hp, 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission. The SQ5 also boasts a well-appointed interior, a spacious cargo area, and a host of advanced safety features.

However, like any vehicle, the 2020 Audi SQ5 has its fair share of issues. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific problems people are having with the vehicle:

**Engine**

* **3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine**
    * **Turbocharger failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of power and acceleration.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **High-pressure fuel pump failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The high-pressure fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to stall or hesitate.
        * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift erratically, hesitate to shift, or slip out of gear.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission control module or the transmission itself.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system can fail, causing the vehicle to sag or ride rough.
        * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor or the air springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
        * Solution: Replace the battery or the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical shorts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical shorts can occur, causing various electrical components to malfunction.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical short.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof can leak, allowing water to enter the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or the sunroof itself.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not work properly, causing the vehicle to become too hot or too cold.
        * Solution: Replace the climate control module or the climate control sensors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation system glitches**
        * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may not work properly, causing the vehicle to get lost or give incorrect directions.
        * Solution: Update the navigation system software or replace the navigation system module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2020 Audi SQ5 is a reliable and well-built SUV. However, it is not without its problems. The most common issues include turbocharger failure, high-pressure fuel pump failure, shifting problems, air suspension failure, battery drain, and sunroof leaks. The severity of these issues can vary, and some may be more expensive to fix than others. However, it is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a 2020 Audi SQ5.
