# 2022 Audi SQ5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi SQ5 delivers potent performance and upscale luxury, making it a compelling choice for shoppers seeking a sporty crossover. Because it is a relatively recent model year, available owner feedback is limited, but understanding its powertrain options and monitoring typical luxury vehicle electronics remain key parts of the shopping process.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces roughly 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It is generally robust, though buyers should ensure consistent maintenance histories.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid shifts paired with standard quattro all-wheel drive. It is a proven unit in Audi's performance lineup.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V947000: Electrical System:Body Control Module/Bcm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover on the gateway control module and seal an underbody seam as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V567000: Structure:Body:Tailgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly connected rear lid may decrease the structural protection of the vehicle during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** A dealer will replace the rear lid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi SQ5 is an appealing luxury performance crossover with strong mechanical underpinnings. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall condition and electronics functionality.
