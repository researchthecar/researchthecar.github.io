# 2016 Audi SQ5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury performance crossover, understanding potential trouble spots can help you make an informed decision. While owner reports reflect specific frustrations, they offer a helpful glimpse into how these vehicles age. Reviewing common areas of concern ensures you can spot potential issues during a test drive and budget for proper maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Supercharged

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine produces around 354 horsepower and 347 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration. It is generally robust, though cooling system components like the water pump warrant periodic inspection.

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The original engine water pump may fail prematurely, leading to coolant leaks and potential overheating risks.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system for leaks and replace the water pump with an updated OEM component if failure occurs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the standard all-wheel-drive system. It is known for refined shifting behavior and solid mechanical durability when serviced on schedule.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Rack

Issues related to steering feedback and assistance mechanisms should be checked carefully during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Intermediate Shaft Squeak and Power Steering Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a squeaking or creaking sensation in the steering wheel when turning, or potential loss of power steering assistance if moisture affects the rack and pinion unit.
- **Solution:** Inspect the intermediate shaft for wear and check the steering rack housing for moisture intrusion or mechanical failure.

## Exterior

### Door Latch and Locking System

Cabin electronics and convenience features generally perform well, though keyless entry behaviors can occasionally cause owner frustration.

**Unintended Door Locking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may automatically lock itself shortly after doors are closed, potentially locking keys or personal items inside.
- **Solution:** Have the key fob sensors, door lock actuators, and body control module diagnostics evaluated by a qualified technician.

### Side Mirrors

Exterior styling and visibility are generally good, though drivers should verify mirror positioning during test drives.

**Mirror Blind Spots**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience notable blind spots around the side mirrors when executing turns or lane changes.
- **Solution:** Adjust side mirrors carefully or add blind-spot auxiliary mirrors to improve visibility.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, this performance crossover offers a compelling blend of utility and luxury power. While owners have reported isolated concerns regarding cooling components, steering feedback, and locking electronics, a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help ensure you find a well-maintained example.
