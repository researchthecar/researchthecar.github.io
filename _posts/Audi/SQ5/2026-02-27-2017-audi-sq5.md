# 2017 Audi SQ5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury performance crossover, evaluating platform reliability and common owner-reported grievances helps ensure a confident purchase. While owner reports to federal databases are subjective and represent isolated experiences rather than guaranteed factory defects, reviewing these signals highlights areas that warrant a closer inspection during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Supercharged

This 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine delivers robust performance with approximately 354 horsepower and 346 lb-ft of torque. It generally offers strong acceleration, though cooling system components should be inspected routinely for leaks as mileage climbs.

**Cooling System and Water Pump Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported excessive engine bay heat accompanied by moisture or leaks around the water pump, occasionally triggering a 'Pull Over Immediately' warning message.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and cooling lines for coolant residue or seepage, and replace any failing components as needed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Equipped with a conventional 8-speed automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive, this setup delivers smooth, responsive gear changes. It is widely regarded as a durable unit when standard fluid maintenance intervals are observed.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Sensors

Electronic power steering components and sensors.

**Steering System Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced sudden steering system lockups or stiffness during operation, accompanied by dashboard sensor warnings indicating the vehicle is unsafe to drive.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the steering rack control module and seals for moisture intrusion or electrical faults.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, this performance crossover combines athletic driving dynamics with a well-appointed cabin, making it an appealing used luxury choice. Prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection, specifically checking the cooling system and steering electronics for any signs of past distress.
