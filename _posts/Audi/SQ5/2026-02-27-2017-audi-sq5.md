# 2017 Audi SQ5

### Overview
The 2017 Audi SQ5, a performance-oriented compact luxury SUV, generally presents a strong reliability profile. It is powered by a potent 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine, known for its robust performance and relatively good durability. The vehicle's advanced Quattro all-wheel-drive system and sophisticated suspension contribute to its sporty handling. While most owners report a positive experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen occasional attention. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 (3.0 TFSI)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Whistling noises from the engine bay, rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential for oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on the engine, burning oil smell, oil spots under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension (Optional)
**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'Suspension Malfunction' warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Similar to compressor failure, but often gradual; vehicle may slowly sag over time.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or seals.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200 per strut

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In some cases, head unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $1000


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery after sitting for a short period, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or faulty components. Battery replacement may be needed.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi SQ5 is a reliable performance SUV, with its core mechanical components, particularly the engine and transmission, demonstrating good durability. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the optional adaptive air suspension system, with potential failures of the compressor or struts. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and intermittent battery drain, are also noted but are not pervasive. Owners should be aware of potential PCV valve issues and oil leaks from valve cover gaskets on the 3.0L V6. Overall, with proactive maintenance and attention to these specific areas, the SQ5 should provide a rewarding ownership experience.
