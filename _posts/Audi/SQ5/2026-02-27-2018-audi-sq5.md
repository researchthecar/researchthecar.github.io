# 2018 Audi SQ5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi SQ5 delivers potent performance and luxury, pairing its turbocharged V6 engine with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. While many owners enjoy its refined driving dynamics, used-car buyers should review potential trouble spots, particularly regarding cabin water intrusion from drainage issues and select powertrain or steering concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (EA839)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine (EA839) produces approximately 354 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration. It provides strong performance, though buyers should routinely inspect for fluid leaks.

**Coolant Leak and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** A coolant warning light illuminates and engine temperatures begin rising while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling system components, top off fluid, and repair any leaks or damaged lines immediately to prevent severe engine damage.

**Oil Filter Housing Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks originating from the top of the engine bay around the filter housing.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic replace the faulty oil filter housing assembly and gaskets.
- **Cost:** $5000

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust during normal operation with no widespread recurring complaint patterns.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Rack

Steering performance is precise, but owners have documented occasional column noises and electronic steering warnings.

**Steering Malfunction and Alignment Shift**
- **Symptoms:** A 'Steering Malfunction' warning light illuminates on the dashboard, accompanied by an off-center or askew steering wheel that makes the vehicle difficult to control.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer or a specialist inspect the electronic steering system and replace the steering rack or column components as needed.

**Squeaking Noise**
- **Symptoms:** A noticeable squeaking sound emits from the steering column when turning slowly left or right, particularly after longer drives.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or service the steering column intermediate shaft and components.

## Electrical

### Comfort System Control Module

electrical issues are occasionally reported, most notably tied to water ingress in the rear cargo compartments from obstructed roof drains.

**Water Damage from Clogged Drains**
- **Symptoms:** Heavy rain leads to cabin water leaks that pool in the rear cargo/battery compartment, shorting out control modules and disabling key fobs or starting functions.
- **Solution:** Clear sunroof and roof drains regularly to prevent water accumulation, and replace any water-damaged control modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V947000: Electrical System:Body Control Module/Bcm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover on the gateway control module and seal an underbody seam as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V369000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A reduction of braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi dealers will bleed the rear brakes. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V416000: Wheels:Hub](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The detached trim may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will secure the wheel arch trim with an additional screw and nut, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V737000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The detached trim may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the four clips securing the forward part of the rear wheel arch trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V277000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Master Cylinder](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A short casting can cause a sudden loss of brakes, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will inspect the brake master cylinder, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi SQ5 is an appealing luxury performance SUV, but used-car shoppers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to the service history for coolant or oil leaks, test all electrical functions, and verify that the sunroof and roof drains are clean and free-flowing to avoid costly moisture damage.
