# 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron represents the high-performance evolution of Audi's flagship electric SUV, blending brisk acceleration and sharp handling with a luxurious cabin. As a relatively new and technically complex battery-electric vehicle, used shoppers should approach with the usual caveats for modern luxury EVs, keeping an eye on software stability and electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### Tri-Motor Electric Drive System

Powered by a tri-motor electric setup producing approximately 496 horsepower and 718 lb-ft of torque. This high-performance configuration delivers exceptional acceleration and all-wheel-drive capability with minimal mechanical maintenance requirements.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features a single-speed automatic gearbox paired with each axle's motor for seamless, shift-free power delivery. The system is mechanically straightforward and generally robust in everyday driving conditions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V240000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8+e-tron&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the pushrod screw joint as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron offers thrilling performance and high-end luxury for used EV shoppers. While widespread mechanical complaints are absent, buyers should thoroughly inspect the high-voltage battery health, charging hardware, and infotainment software prior to purchase.
