# 2017 Audi S3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi S3 offers a compelling blend of luxury, compact dimensions, and turbocharged performance for spirited drivers. While owner reports to NHTSA highlight a few scattered concerns regarding exterior visibility accessories and suspension vibrations, prospective buyers should carefully review powertrain and safety records before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration paired with Audi's standard quattro all-wheel-drive system. It generally proves robust under normal operating conditions, though routine maintenance is essential to ensure long-term mechanical longevity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear shifts designed to maximize performance. While generally responsive, owners have occasionally reported abrupt shifting behavior following unexpected jolts or road impacts.

**Transmission Engagement Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** After driving over minor road imperfections, the transmission may unexpectedly rev high, slam into a higher gear, illuminate warning lights, and violently jerk the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected immediately by a qualified technician or dealership to diagnose internal transmission codes and assess potential mechanical damage.

## Steering

### Suspension and Steering

The suspension system provides a firm, athletic ride quality suited for dynamic handling, though wheel balancing and alignment require regular attention.

**Highway Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may exhibit persistent vibrations or shaking during normal highway driving conditions at speeds between 65 and 75 mph.
- **Solution:** Check wheel balance, tire wear patterns, alignment specifications, and suspension components for premature wear or imbalance.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wipers

Exterior components are generally well-assembled, though minor accessory annoyances like wiper performance can occasionally affect visibility.

**Wiper Blade Chattering**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wiper blades may chatter, skip, or fail to sweep smoothly across the glass during rainy conditions.
- **Solution:** Replace the wiper inserts with quality replacements or clean the windshield thoroughly to remove chemical buildup.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety systems meet rigorous standards, but owners must verify that all active manufacturer recalls have been properly completed.

**Passenger Air Bag Warning Light and Recall Delays**
- **Symptoms:** The front passenger seat air bag warning light may fail to illuminate correctly regardless of whether a passenger is seated, and parts for safety recalls may face availability delays.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized dealership to check for open safety campaigns and schedule repairs as soon as replacement components become accessible.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V198000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the PODS sensor mat and update the PODS control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V196000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In a lower-speed crash situation, the air bag control unit software may deploy the passenger frontal air bag improperly if the front seat passenger is sitting on the edge of the seat or is laying in the seat with the seat reclined. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will update the air bag control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi S3 is an engaging and sophisticated sport sedan, but used car shoppers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to transmission shift quality during a test drive, verify that highway speeds do not induce abnormal vibrations, and confirm that all relevant safety recalls have been resolved.
