**Introduction:**

The 2022 Audi S3 is a compact sports sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter TFSI engine that produces 306 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission. Other engine options include a 2.5-liter TFSI engine with 394 horsepower and a 3.2-liter VR6 engine with 444 horsepower. The S3 is known for its sharp handling, responsive acceleration, and luxurious interior. However, there have been some reliability concerns reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI Engine**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the timing chain tensioner can cause the timing chain to jump or break, leading to catastrophic engine failure. Symptoms may include a rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, or an illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and, in severe cases, the timing chain and gears.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Clutches Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: When attempting to accelerate or under load, the transmission may slip, causing a loss of power and a burning smell.
    * Solution: Replacement of the clutch pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning Control Units:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electronic control units, such as the engine control unit or the transmission control unit, may malfunction, causing intermittent or permanent problems with engine performance, transmission operation, or other vehicle systems.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of the affected control unit(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The MMI infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, including freezing, lagging, or displaying incorrect information.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Audi S3 is a capable and enjoyable sports sedan. However, it has some reliability concerns that potential buyers should be aware of, particularly with the timing chain tensioner in the 2.0L TFSI engine and the clutches in the 7-speed S tronic transmission. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, and it is recommended to have a thorough pre-purchase inspection before acquiring an S3.
