**2011 Audi S3**

The 2011 Audi S3 is a high-performance variant of the A3 hatchback. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine that produces 256 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a six-speed manual or a seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. The S3 comes standard with all-wheel drive.

The S3 is praised for its performance, handling, and luxurious interior. However, it is also known to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idle, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Cleaning the intake valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, loss of power, and check engine light.
        * Solution: Replacing the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual**
    * **Gear Grinding**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially when cold.
        * Solution: Replacing the transmission fluid or rebuilding the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seven-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, jerking, or refusing to shift gears.
        * Solution: Replacing the mechatronic unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * **Premature Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, vibration, and reduced handling.
        * Solution: Replacing the control arm bushings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Xenon Headlights**
    * **Ballast Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not illuminating or flickering.
        * Solution: Replacing the ballast.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery**
    * **Premature Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the car, electrical system issues.
        * Solution: Replacing the battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof**
    * **Leaking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin.
        * Solution: Replacing the sunroof seals or adjusting the sunroof alignment.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Wheels**
    * **Curb Damage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cosmetic damage to the wheels.
        * Solution: Repairing or replacing the wheels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of the damage.
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel**
    * **Buttons Not Working**
        * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on the steering wheel not responding.
        * Solution: Replacing the steering wheel control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC**
    * **Climate Control Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Climate control not functioning properly, temperature not adjusting.
        * Solution: Resetting the climate control module or replacing it.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Audi S3 is a high-performance car with some known issues. The most severe and expensive issue is the timing chain tensioner failure, which can lead to catastrophic engine damage. Other common problems include carbon buildup on the intake valves, transmission issues, and suspension problems. Overall, the S3 is a reliable car, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues and consider the cost of repairs before purchasing one.
