**Introduction:**

The 2016 Audi S3 is a high-performance sedan powered by a 2.0L TFSI turbocharged engine, producing 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It features a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system and comes with a six-speed manual or a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The S3 is praised for its sharp handling, responsive engine, and luxurious interior. However, there have been several reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Removal and cleaning of intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, and potential catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Reverse Gear Popping Out:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reverse gear disengages while driving.
    * Solution: Replacement of reverse gear synchronization.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the transmission housing.
    * Solution: Replacement of transmission housing seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking and rattling noises during suspension movement.
    * Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining rapidly, resulting in a no-start condition.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical fault causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the fault
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Replacement of headlights with updated seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the sunroof during driving.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of sunroof tracks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 Audi S3 has a range of reported issues that can vary in severity and cost to fix. Some issues, such as the timing chain tensioner failure and reverse gear popping out, can be potentially serious and expensive to repair. Others, like the sunroof rattle and battery drain, may be less severe and less costly to resolve. Overall, it is crucial for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to schedule regular maintenance and repairs to minimize their likelihood and impact.
