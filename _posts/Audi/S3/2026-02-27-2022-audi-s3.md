# 2022 Audi S3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi S3 delivers punchy performance and sophisticated everyday luxury, leveraging a turbocharged four-cylinder engine and a rapid dual-clutch transmission. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern engineering and advanced technology suites, though prospective buyers should still evaluate maintenance histories and common platform quirks. Reviewing owner feedback and keeping an eye out for minor electronic or mechanical inconsistencies can help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 306 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It is widely shared across the Volkswagen-Audi performance lineup and generally offers solid mechanical reliability when maintained on schedule.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. Routine fluid changes are vital to maintain smooth operation and longevity in high-performance driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V224000: Child Seat:Vehicle Latch Anchor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." An incorrectly marked child restraint anchorage may cause confusion during child seat installation, resulting in a child seat being improperly secured and increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace all four fitting sleeves, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V515000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The retention force of the seat belt tensioner on the driver and front passenger seats may be inadequate to properly restrain the occupants during a crash. Improperly restrained occupants have an increased risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver and front passenger seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V652000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An incorrectly folded air bag can tear, prohibiting it from deploying properly during a crash, increasing the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V823000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi S3 remains an appealing choice for shoppers seeking a balance of luxury and sports-sedan performance. While powertrain options are robust, thorough pre-purchase inspections and service record checks are always recommended before finalizing a used vehicle purchase.
