# 2016 Audi S3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Audi S3, buyers are generally drawn to its refined performance, luxury interior, and sporty handling. While many owners enjoy trouble-free driving, reviewing owner experiences and known technical bulletins helps highlight areas that require careful pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and sporty performance. Routine maintenance is critical, as some owners have reported oil consumption and overheating concerns.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission channels power to Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system, offering rapid and precise gear shifts. Prospective buyers should verify proper clutch engagement and smooth operation during a test drive.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body components are generally durable, though isolated structural glass incidents have been reported.

**Sudden Sunroof Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may shatter or explode unexpectedly while driving without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any pre-existing stress cracks, and replace the glass assembly if structural compromise is suspected.

## Safety

### Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS)

Safety system complaints primarily center around passenger airbag warnings and occupancy sensor faults.

**Passenger Airbag Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger airbag warning light illuminates, and the information screen displays a system malfunction, causing the passenger airbag to remain deactivated.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the occupant detection sensor wiring and control module, and ensure all relevant safety recalls or software updates are addressed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V474000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An improper electrical contact may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) to falsely detect a malfunction and deactivate the passenger air bag. A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the connector and reroute the cable, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V198000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the PODS sensor mat and update the PODS control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V543000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During air bag deployment, the front passenger air bag may explode or deploy improperly. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi S3 offers an impressive blend of luxury and performance, but buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to vehicle maintenance history, engine oil levels, and electronic safety systems to ensure a reliable ownership experience.
