# 2024 Audi S3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi S3 delivers thrilling performance and sharp handling, backed by a potent turbocharged powertrain. While early owner feedback includes isolated complaints regarding windshield wiper noise and intermittent electronic sensor errors, the vehicle generally presents as a modern, high-performance compact sedan for prospective used-car buyers.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 306 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It serves as the standard powerplant for the model, balancing everyday drivability with spirited performance.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive, the quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear changes and responsive power delivery. It is well-matched to the energetic character of the turbocharged engine.

## Brakes

### Driver Assistance Sensors

Advanced safety and driver assistance features offer robust protection, though isolated electronic glitches have been reported by owners.

**Random Sensor and Warning Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience random warning messages from front and rear parking sensors, accompanied by illuminated ABS or SOS system failure lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified dealer or technician scan for diagnostic trouble codes and perform necessary software updates or sensor recalibrations.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wipers

Exterior visibility components generally function as intended, though minor owner reports highlight occasional wiper noise.

**Noisy Wiper Blades**
- **Symptoms:** Wiper blades make a loud scratching sound during operation in rainy conditions, which can impair visibility and increase driving stress.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiper blade inserts for wear or debris, and replace the wiper blades as needed.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi S3 remains an engaging and technologically advanced choice for enthusiasts seeking a compact luxury sport sedan. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test all electronics, driver assistance sensors, and wiper operations during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything works properly.
