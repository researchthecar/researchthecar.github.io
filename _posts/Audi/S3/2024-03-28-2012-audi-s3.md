**Introduction:**

The 2012 Audi S3 is a high-performance hatchback that combines sporty dynamics with premium amenities. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with either a 6-speed manual or 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission. Praised for its agile handling, refined interior, and impressive powertrain, the S3 has also faced some criticisms for its firm ride and limited rear legroom.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle consumes excessive amounts of oil between oil changes, typically over 1 quart per 1,000 miles.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Timing chain tensioner fails, causing the timing chain to skip or break, resulting in engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or slips during gear changes, especially in low gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noise from the front suspension over bumps or uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replace front suspension bushings or control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear suspension sags or squats prematurely, especially when carrying heavy loads.
    * Solution: Replace rear springs or shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Solution: Replace battery or identify and repair electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating, ventilation, or air conditioning system fails to function properly.
    * Solution: Repair or replace HVAC components, such as blower motor or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlight lenses.
    * Solution: Replace headlight housing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Hatchback Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hatchback latch fails to properly secure the hatchback, causing it to open unexpectedly.
    * Solution: Replace hatchback latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezes, crashes, or displays incorrect directions.
    * Solution: Update navigation software or replace navigation unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof leaks water or moisture into the cabin during heavy rain or car washes.
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal or drain tube.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Audi S3 is a well-built and reliable vehicle with a number of appealing features. However, it has experienced some issues, primarily related to engine oil consumption, timing chain problems, and electrical glitches. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not widespread and should not deter potential buyers. The severity and frequency of these issues vary, and it is important to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic before purchase to assess its overall condition and address any potential concerns.
