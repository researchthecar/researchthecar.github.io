**Introduction:**

The 2019 Audi S3 is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It features a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine that produces 288 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, mated to a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The S3 is praised for its agile handling, responsive steering, and comfortable interior, while some drivers find its ride quality a bit firm.

### **Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

- **Turbocharger Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling or whining sounds from the engine
  - Solution: Replacement of turbocharger
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle or knocking sounds, reduced fuel efficiency
  - Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced power, increased fuel consumption
  - Solution: Intake valve cleaning or replacement
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

- **Clutch Pack Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration or deceleration
  - Solution: Replacement of clutch pack
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission warning light illuminated, failure to shift gears, sudden loss of power
  - Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**

- **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
  - Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Bouncy or unstable ride, leaking shock absorber fluid
  - Solution: Replacement of shock absorbers
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $900
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Overnight battery discharge, failure to start the vehicle
  - Solution: Identification and repair of electrical drain
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Electrical System Malfunction:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illuminated, various warning lights, electrical component failures
  - Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system issues
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

- **Door Handle Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Door handle fails to open or close properly
  - Solution: Replacement of door handle
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Headlight Condensation:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight lens
  - Solution: Replacement of headlight lens
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**

- **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Noises and vibrations originating from the dashboard
  - Solution: Inspection and tightening of dashboard components
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $200
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Infotainment System Lag:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Slow or unresponsive infotainment system
  - Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment module
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2019 Audi S3 generally has favorable reviews, but there are some potential issues to be aware of. Engine problems such as turbocharger and timing chain issues can be costly to repair, and transmission issues with the dual-clutch automatic may also be expensive. Suspension and electrical issues are less severe and typically less expensive to fix. Overall, the S3 is a fun and capable vehicle, but buyers should be prepared for the possibility of encountering some of the issues listed above.
