**Introduction:**

The 2015 Audi S3 is a high-performance compact sedan powered by a turbocharged 2.0L engine. It boasts 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with a standard 6-speed manual or an optional 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The S3 is known for its athletic handling, luxurious interior, and impressive acceleration. However, it has also faced its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement or engine rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased emissions
    * Solution: Intake valve cleaning or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Shift Linkage Issues:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into 5th and 6th gears
    * Solution: Shift linkage adjustment or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission Clutch Pack Issues:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Clutch slipping, shuddering, or difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Clutch pack replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling sounds from the front suspension, especially over rough roads
    * Solution: Bushing replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** 
    * Description/Symptoms:
    Quickly drained battery, especially if the vehicle is left unused for extended periods
    * Solution: Electrical system inspection and potential battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Taillight Condensation:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Water or moisture buildup inside the rear taillights
    * Solution: Taillight replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow or unresponsive infotainment system, especially when navigating or using other apps
    * Solution: Software update or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Panoramic Sunroof Squeaks and Rattles:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the panoramic sunroof, especially when driving over uneven roads
    * Solution: Sunroof adjustment or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Despite its performance and luxury offerings, the 2015 Audi S3 has faced some notable issues. The engine has been plagued by oil consumption and carbon buildup problems, while the transmission has had its fair share of clutch pack failures. Suspension issues, electrical gremlins, and interior squeaks have also been reported. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some requiring costly repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchasing decision.
