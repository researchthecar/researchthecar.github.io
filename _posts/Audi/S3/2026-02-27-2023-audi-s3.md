# 2023 Audi S3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi S3 delivers punchy performance and daily usability, combining luxury and speed into a compact package. As a relatively new model year, complaint data is sparse, reflecting a stable baseline for buyers browsing the used market.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 306 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It generally offers solid reliability when properly maintained, though regular premium fuel and timely oil changes are essential.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear changes paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. Routine fluid maintenance is critical to ensuring long-term durability and smooth operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V901000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." Missing weight information may result in unintentional overloading of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi dealers will install an overlay label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi S3 presents a clean slate for used car buyers with minimal reported owner complaints. Prospective owners should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to ensure peace of mind.
