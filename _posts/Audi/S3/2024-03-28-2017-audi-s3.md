**2017 Audi S3**

The 2017 Audi S3 is a high-performance compact sedan that offers a blend of luxury, sportiness, and practicality. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque and is paired with a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission.

The S3 has received praise for its powerful engine, sharp handling, and comfortable interior. However, it has also been criticized for its firm ride and high price.

**Engine**

* **2.0L TFSI Engine**

    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, audible whining or grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, misfiring
        * Solution: Remove and clean intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

    * **Gear Grinding**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace damaged gears or synchro rings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Seven-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission**

    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, jerking, or not engaging
        * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Clutch Pack Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration
        * Solution: Replace clutch pack
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**

    * **Strut Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front of the vehicle, reduced ride comfort
        * Solution: Replace struts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension**

    * **Shock Absorber Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride comfort, excessive body roll, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dim headlights, flickering electronics
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after a period of non-use
    * Solution: Find and repair source of parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace or repair headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water entering the cabin through the sunroof
    * Solution: Re-seal sunroof
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, system crashes, audio problems
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Audi S3 is a highly capable performance sedan, but it is not without its issues. Some common problems include turbocharger failure, carbon buildup on intake valves, transmission problems, and suspension wear. These issues can be costly to repair, so it is important to factor them into the cost of ownership. However, despite these issues, the S3 remains a desirable choice for those seeking a compact luxury sedan that delivers on performance and handling.
