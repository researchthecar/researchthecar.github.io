# 2019 Audi S3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi S3 delivers sharp handling and brisk performance from its turbocharged four-cylinder engine and dual-clutch transmission. Because this vehicle relies on owner-submitted complaints rather than widespread mechanical failures, prospective buyers should focus on a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and electronic health.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 288 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It offers strong acceleration and robust performance for daily driving and sporty applications alike.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S-Tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid maintenance is essential to preserving smooth shifting behavior over high mileage.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety equipment generally performs well, though standard manufacturer recall campaigns require verification during purchase.

**Recall Remedy Delays and Scheduling**
- **Symptoms:** Owners experienced delays in receiving recall notifications or scheduling parts availability at local dealerships for airbag safety campaigns.
- **Solution:** Check the vehicle identification number (VIN) with an authorized dealer to verify if all safety recall campaigns have been fully completed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V198000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S3&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the PODS sensor mat and update the PODS control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi S3 is an engaging and sophisticated sport sedan offering strong powertrain performance. Buyers should carefully verify service records and ensure all open safety recalls have been addressed by a certified dealer before finalizing a purchase.
