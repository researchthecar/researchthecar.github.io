## 2014 Audi S3

The 2014 Audi S3 is a high-performance compact car that offers a blend of sporty driving dynamics, luxurious amenities, and advanced technology. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a six-speed manual or a six-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The S3 comes standard with Quattro all-wheel drive, which provides excellent traction and handling in all weather conditions.

People who like the 2014 Audi S3 praise its powerful engine, sharp handling, comfortable interior, and high-quality materials. However, some people find the S3's ride to be too firm, and its infotainment system can be difficult to use.

### Engine

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

- **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
    - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

- **No Issues Found**

**Six-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

- **Mechatronic unit failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: The mechatronic unit is the brain of the dual-clutch automatic transmission. It can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all.
    - Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

- **No Issues Found**

### Electrical

- **Battery drain**
    - Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain excessively, even when the car is parked.
    - Solution: Replace the battery or check for electrical faults.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

- **Headlight condensation**
    - Description/Symptoms: Condensation can build up inside the headlights, impairing visibility.
    - Solution: Replace the headlights or reseal them.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

### Interior

- **Sunroof creaking**
    - Description/Symptoms: The sunroof can creak or rattle when closed.
    - Solution: Lubricate the sunroof rails or replace the sunroof motor.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2014 Audi S3 is a well-rounded performance car that offers a lot of value for its price. However, it is not without its issues. The most serious issue is the timing chain tensioner failure, which can lead to catastrophic engine damage. Other common issues include the mechatronic unit failure in the dual-clutch automatic transmission, battery drain, headlight condensation, and sunroof creaking. Overall, the severity of the issues with the 2014 Audi S3 is moderate, but the cost to fix them can be expensive.
