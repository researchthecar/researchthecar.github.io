# 2016 Audi A5 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi A5 Quattro combines timeless coupe and convertible styling with a sophisticated interior and standard all-wheel drive. As a used vehicle purchase, buyers should weigh its luxury appeal against the typical maintenance and mechanical vigilance required for German luxury performance cars.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It offers a solid balance of efficiency and punchy acceleration, though proper oil maintenance is critical for longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed conventional automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust when serviced at recommended intervals.

### 6-Speed Manual

The 6-speed manual transmission offers a more engaging driving experience for enthusiast drivers. It is a rarer find in this model year and requires standard clutch maintenance.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi A5 Quattro is an appealing luxury cruiser that delivers confident handling and refined style. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with thorough service documentation and budget for premium maintenance costs to ensure long-term reliability.
