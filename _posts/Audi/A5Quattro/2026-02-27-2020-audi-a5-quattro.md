# 2020 Audi A5 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi A5 Quattro, buyers will find a stylish and technologically advanced luxury vehicle equipped with a capable powertrain. While the mechanical and structural aspects generally hold up well, owners have highlighted some notable electronic frustrations. Reviewing vehicle history records and checking that all software updates have been properly applied will help ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and refined power delivery for daily driving while paired with Audi's standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and works seamlessly with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid maintenance is key to preserving its smooth operation over high mileage.

## Electrical

### Multi-Media Interface (MMI) Infotainment System

The MMI touchscreen and digital cockpit interface manage most vehicle settings, audio, and safety camera feeds, though owner reports indicate occasional software instability.

**Random MMI Screen Reboots and Shutdowns**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment touchscreen and MMI system unexpectedly shut down or enter an endless reboot loop while driving, causing temporary loss of the backup camera, navigation, and audio controls.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized dealer or service center to check for available software patches and infotainment updates designed to stabilize the system.

### Backup Camera and Driver Assistance

Advanced safety features and camera systems rely heavily on central electronic modules to deliver real-time parking and visibility assistance.

**Backup Camera Interruption During MMI Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** When the infotainment system randomly restarts while the vehicle is placed in reverse, the backup camera display temporarily goes blank, potentially creating a visibility hazard.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a technician to ensure all infotainment control modules are running the latest software versions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V737000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harness for the rear lights could be missing the anti-abrasion protection in the area of the spare wheel well. Without the scuff protection, the wires may become damaged during a rear crash, disabling the doors from being opened from the outside, causing a loss of hazard lights, and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will apply scuff protection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A5 Quattro offers an engaging driving dynamic, strong engine performance, and luxury features that appeal to used-car buyers. Prospective owners should verify that the electrical and infotainment software updates have been completed to mitigate reported display reboot issues. Overall, a pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and regular service history will provide the best peace of mind.
