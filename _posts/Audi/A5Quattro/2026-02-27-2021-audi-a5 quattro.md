### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi A5 Quattro demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission performing exceptionally well. The Quattro all-wheel-drive system is a robust feature. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning occasional infotainment glitches and, less frequently, intermittent battery drain issues that require professional diagnosis. These electrical concerns are not indicative of systemic failure but rather isolated incidents that can be resolved with software updates or component replacement. Overall, the A5 Quattro offers a dependable and premium ownership experience for its segment.
