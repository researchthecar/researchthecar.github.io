**Introduction:**

The 2010 Audi A5 Quattro is a sleek and sporty luxury coupe available with a range of engine and transmission options. It boasts a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 (211 hp, 258 lb-ft) as standard, with a 3.2L V6 (265 hp, 243 lb-ft) and a 4.2L V8 (354 hp, 325 lb-ft) offered as upgrades. The A5 Quattro comes with a 6-speed manual or 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. Praised for its stylish design, responsive handling, and comfortable interior, it has received some criticism for its high price and limited rear-seat space.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** Intake valves can accumulate carbon deposits causing reduced engine performance and fuel efficiency. Cleaning the valves can resolve the issue.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**3.2L V6**
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, leading to skipped timing and potential engine damage. Replacement of the tensioner is necessary.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**4.2L V8**
* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**
* **Shift Linkage Wear:** The shift linkage can wear, causing difficulty shifting or popping out of gear. Replacement of the linkage is typically required.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** The mechatronic unit, which controls gear shifting, can fail, leading to transmission problems. Replacement of the unit is necessary.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 15V397000

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:** The front lower control arm bushings can wear out prematurely, causing knocking noises, poor handling, and uneven tire wear. Replacement of the bushings is the solution.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues:** Electrical harnesses can experience problems, leading to various electrical malfunctions. The specific location and cause of the issue can vary. Diagnosis and repair or replacement of the affected harness is required.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Misalignment:** The trunk lid may not align properly, causing a cosmetic issue and potential water leaks. Adjustment of the hinges or latch can resolve the problem.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Display Issues:** The navigation system display can fail or experience glitches, affecting its functionality. Software updates or replacement of the display unit may be necessary.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2010 Audi A5 Quattro is a sporty and luxurious coupe with a range of engine and transmission options. However, it has some issues, including carbon buildup on intake valves (2.0L engine), timing chain tensioner failure (3.2L engine), mechatronic unit failure (8-speed automatic transmission), and front lower control arm bushing failure. While the severity of these issues varies, they can be costly to repair, ranging from as low as $100 to over $4,000. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into the cost of ownership.
