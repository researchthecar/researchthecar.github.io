# 2017 Audi A5 Quattro

### Overview
The 2017 Audi A5 Quattro, a stylish and sporty coupe and convertible, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on performance and premium features. While it shares many components with other Audi models of its era, its specific reliability profile is influenced by its powertrain and the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Owners often praise its driving dynamics and interior quality. However, like any vehicle, certain areas may require attention over time, particularly concerning the advanced technology and powertrain components. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TFSI (Turbocharged Direct Injection)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil consumption between oil changes, potential for oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Engine internal inspection, piston ring replacement, or PCV valve replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, check engine light, potential for oil leaks or whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi A5 Quattro presents a generally reliable profile, with its strengths lying in its sophisticated engineering and premium feel. The most commonly cited technical concerns revolve around the 2.0L TFSI engine, specifically oil consumption and PCV valve issues, which are not uncommon for turbocharged direct-injection engines of this era. The S tronic dual-clutch transmission has a potential for mechatronic unit failures, which can be a significant repair. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and parking sensors, are also noted but often resolvable with software updates or component replacement. The Quattro system and standard suspension components have proven robust. Overall, while not immune to issues, the A5 Quattro offers a good balance of performance and dependability, provided potential powertrain and electronic concerns are addressed proactively.
