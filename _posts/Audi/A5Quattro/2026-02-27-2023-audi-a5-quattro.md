# 2023 Audi A5 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A5 Quattro delivers sleek styling, agile handling, and standard all-wheel drive, making it an appealing luxury sport option. While generally well-regarded, early owner feedback highlights a few isolated technical and mechanical concerns that prospective buyers should keep in mind during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 12-volt mild-hybrid system. It provides responsive acceleration and respectable fuel economy for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear changes and routes power efficiently to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers sporty shifting characteristics suited to the A5's performance character.

## Suspension

### Rear Wheels and Suspension

Suspension components are generally robust, though minor noises may occasionally require professional diagnosis.

**Rear Thumping Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an unusual or loud thumping noise originating from the rear of the vehicle at slow speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the rear suspension components and wheel assemblies to isolate the source of the noise.

## Electrical

### Pre Sense and Driver Assistance

Advanced safety features enhance protection, but complex sensor suites can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking events.

**Phantom Braking and Pre Sense Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the automated emergency braking system engaged unexpectedly without obstruction, alongside sudden dashboard warning messages for Audi pre sense.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized dealer to check for software updates or sensor recalibrations addressing driver assistance system sensitivity.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi A5 Quattro remains a sophisticated and enjoyable luxury choice, provided you inspect it thoroughly. When shopping for a used model, verify that all driver assistance sensors are properly calibrated and check the suspension for any unusual noises during a test drive.
