# 2022 Audi A5 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A5 Quattro delivers a refined driving experience, blending stylish design with capable all-wheel-drive performance and upscale technology. As a relatively recent model year, it generally maintains a strong standing among luxury buyers seeking modern amenities and dynamic handling. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, reviewing potential powertrain options and staying attentive to general vehicle condition during a pre-purchase inspection remains wise.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It pairs standard Quattro all-wheel drive with balanced efficiency and performance, though routine maintenance is essential for long-term health.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

Serving as the base powerplant in select markets, this lower-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder generates around 201 horsepower. It offers a smooth, efficient power delivery suited for comfortable cruising and everyday commuting.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear shifts while efficiently routing power to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers a responsive and engaging drive, though prospective buyers should test-shift thoroughly across various speeds to ensure smooth operation.
