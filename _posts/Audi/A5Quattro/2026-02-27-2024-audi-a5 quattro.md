### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Audi A5 Quattro presents itself as a reliable luxury vehicle with a strong emphasis on performance and technology. Early indications and Audi's track record suggest that major mechanical failures are uncommon for both the 2.0L turbocharged engine options and the 7-speed S tronic transmission. While the suspension and exterior components are generally robust, minor electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system's software, have been noted, though these are typically resolvable with updates. Overall, the A5 Quattro is a dependable choice for those seeking a premium driving experience, with reliability expected to be a strong suit, provided routine maintenance is adhered to.
