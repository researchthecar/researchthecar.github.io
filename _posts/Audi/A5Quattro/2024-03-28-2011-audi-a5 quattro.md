**Introduction:** The 2011 Audi A5 Quattro is a luxury coupe that offers a blend of performance and refinement. It is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The A5 Quattro delivers 211 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Despite its positive attributes, the vehicle has been known to exhibit certain issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine:**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Intake manifold cleaning and decarbonization.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Control Arms:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking sounds from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+ per arm
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Subframe:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud banging noise when driving over bumps or rough roads.
    * Solution: Reinforce or replace the subframe.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Replace or seal affected headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains excessively, even when parked.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair any parasitic draws on the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin from the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replace or repair sunroof drain tubes or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Trunk Lid Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on the inside of the trunk lid.
    * Solution: Replace or repair affected trunk lid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Creaks and Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the center console area.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace loose screws or clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not functioning properly.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair any faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:** The 2011 Audi A5 Quattro has been praised for its performance, handling, and luxurious interior. However, it has also been plagued by a range of issues, including carbon buildup in the engine, suspension problems, electrical malfunctions, and interior noises. These issues can vary in severity and cost to fix. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before making a purchase.
