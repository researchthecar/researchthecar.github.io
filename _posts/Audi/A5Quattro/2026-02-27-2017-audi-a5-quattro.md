# 2017 Audi A5 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi A5 Quattro offers a blend of luxury, refined styling, and confident all-weather performance. Prospective buyers exploring the used market should carefully review maintenance records and verify completion of any relevant safety updates.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 220 horsepower and provides lively acceleration paired with standard all-wheel drive. It is a generally capable powerplant, though owners have reported isolated mechanical and electronic control component issues requiring dealership intervention.

**Engine Management and Camshaft Component Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light illuminated early in ownership, prompting component replacements including the crankshaft, throttle body, and cam adjuster.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose fault codes and inspect internal components; replace worn or failing engine management parts as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifts that complement the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid maintenance is key to ensuring long-term durability in this luxury application.

### 6-Speed Manual

The available 6-speed manual transmission offers an engaging driving experience for enthusiasts who prefer direct gear control. Overall mechanical reliability is solid when the clutch is inspected for wear prior to purchase.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V002000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the coolant pump overheats, it can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will update the software so that the power supply to the coolant pump is deactivated if the pump becomes blocked with debris, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V229000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A blocked pump or a short-circuited pump can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi A5 Quattro is an appealing luxury coupe and cabriolet option, provided buyers prioritize thorough pre-purchase inspections and verify service histories. While owner-reported complaints indicate potential for isolated engine control component troubles, diligent maintenance helps ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
