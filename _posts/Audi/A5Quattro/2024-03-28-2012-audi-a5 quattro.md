**Introduction**

The 2012 Audi A5 Quattro is a luxury coupe that combines performance with style. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 211 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels. The A5 Quattro also offers a range of other engine and transmission options, including a 3.0-liter V6 engine with 272 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, and a six-speed manual transmission.

Drivers love the A5 Quattro's sporty handling, comfortable interior, and sleek exterior design. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, and decreased fuel economy.
Solution: Intake valve cleaning or replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**3.0-Liter V6 Engine**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine operation, especially during startup and acceleration.
Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Hesitation or Delay**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or delay in shifting gears.
Solution: Software update or transmission control module replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling sound while driving over rough surfaces.
Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery drain, even when the vehicle is not in use.
Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical fault causing drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Spoiler Adhesive Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Rear spoiler detaching from the vehicle.
Solution: Replacement of rear spoiler and adhesive.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Plastic Trim Pieces Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling plastic trim pieces in the dashboard or door panels.
Solution: Removal of trim pieces and application of soundproofing or adhesive.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Audi A5 Quattro is a reliable vehicle that offers a combination of performance and luxury. However, it has some known issues that can be costly to repair. The most common issues affect the engine, transmission, and suspension. While these issues can be expensive to fix, they are not common and should not deter buyers from considering this vehicle.
