**Introduction**

The 2015 Audi A5 Quattro is a compact luxury coupe known for its sleek design, powerful performance, and advanced technology. It is available with three engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder, a 3.0L supercharged V6, and a 3.0L TDI diesel V6. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic, and a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. The A5 is praised for its handling, responsive steering, and well-equipped interior, but it has received some complaints about its reliability.

**Engine**

**2.0T FSI quattro**
* **High-pressure fuel pump failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting, stalling, and reduced engine power. The faulty pump must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Carbon buildup on intake valves:** Symptoms include reduced engine performance and fuel efficiency. The intake valves must be cleaned or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**3.0T FSI quattro**
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Symptoms include engine noise, loss of power, and potentially catastrophic engine damage. The tensioner and timing chain must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Water pump failure:** Symptoms include coolant leaks and overheating. The water pump must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**3.0TDI quattro**
* **Diesel particulate filter (DPF) clogging:** Symptoms include reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption, and potential engine damage. The DPF must be cleaned or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Injector failure:** Symptoms include rough idling, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption. The faulty injector(s) must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per injector

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**
* **Synchronizer wear:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, especially into third gear. The synchronizer rings must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**8-speed Tiptronic Automatic**
* **Valve body failure:** Symptoms include harsh shifting, slipping gears, and loss of power. The valve body must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Mechatronic unit failure:** Symptoms include transmission juddering, slipping, and potential catastrophic transmission damage. The mechatronic unit must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+

**7-speed S tronic**
* **Clutch pack failure:** Symptoms include slipping gears, loss of power, and potential catastrophic transmission damage. The clutch pack must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Shift fork failure:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, especially into higher gears. The shift fork must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension strut failure:** Symptoms include clunking noises, poor handling, and reduced ride comfort. The faulty strut(s) must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per strut
* **Rear suspension spring failure:** Symptoms include a sagging rear end and reduced ride height. The faulty spring(s) must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per spring

**Electrical**

* **Electrical harness damage:** Symptoms can vary depending on the location of the damage, but may include electrical malfunctions, warning lights, and potential fire hazards. The damaged harness must be repaired or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Battery drain:** Symptoms include slow or no engine starts. The battery may need to be replaced or the electrical system may need to be diagnosed for parasitic drains. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuator failure:** Symptoms include difficulty locking or unlocking the doors from the inside or outside. The faulty actuator(s) must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per actuator
* **Heated seat module failure:** Symptoms include inoperable heated seats. The faulty module(s) must be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per seat

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle:** Symptoms include a rattling or vibrating sound from the dashboard area. The cause can vary, but may require removal and disassembly of the dashboard for repairs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Climate control malfunction:** Symptoms can vary depending on the nature of the malfunction, but may include inoperable climate control features, incorrect temperatures, or excessive noise. The faulty component(s) must be diagnosed and replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**

While the 2015 Audi A5 Quattro offers impressive performance and luxury, it has been plagued by a significant number of issues with its engine, transmission, and other components. These issues can be costly to repair, with some exceeding $5,000 in estimated expenses. Owners should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing or owning this vehicle.
