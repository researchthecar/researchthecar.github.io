# 2019 Audi A5 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi A5 Quattro delivers sleek styling, engaging driving dynamics, and a refined interior. However, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding occasional electrical, steering, and body integrity issues before purchasing a pre-owned model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing lively acceleration and respectable efficiency. It is generally robust when properly maintained with timely oil and filter changes.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear shifts paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid services are essential to ensure long-term mechanical durability and smooth engagement.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers a direct and engaging connection for enthusiast drivers. It is a mechanically straightforward unit, though clutch wear depends heavily on previous driving habits.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Issues related to sudden steering stiffness require prompt diagnostic attention to ensure vehicle safety.

**Steering Malfunction / Loss of Assist**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a sudden 'Steering Malfunction' warning message accompanied by a dramatic increase in steering effort, making the vehicle difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis typically requires checking steering control modules and replacing faulty electronic power steering components if necessary.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and SOS System

Driver aids and camera visibility systems should be thoroughly tested during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Rearview Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** An 'SOS System Deactivated' warning can appear upon shifting into reverse, rendering the rearview camera temporarily inoperable.
- **Solution:** Software resets or module replacement may be needed to restore proper backup camera and telematics functionality.

## Exterior

### Door Sills and Body Drainage

Body seals and drainage paths require periodic checks to prevent moisture intrusion.

**Water Accumulation in Door Sills**
- **Symptoms:** Water can collect inside rear door wells or sills, producing a noticeable sloshing sound during braking or acceleration after heavy rain.
- **Solution:** Clear blocked door drain plugs and check weatherstripping seals to ensure proper water evacuation.

## Safety

### Auto Start/Stop and Driver Assistance

Electronic safety systems and convenience features can occasionally display erratic behavior reported by drivers.

**Auto Start/Stop and Pre-Sense Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have noted unexpected engine cutoffs or overly sensitive pre-sense collision braking activation during routine driving maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's sensor calibration and software modules inspected and updated by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi A5 Quattro offers a compelling mix of luxury, performance, and everyday usability. While most mechanical systems prove durable, used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical components, steering responsiveness, and body seals to ensure everything operates as intended.
