**Introduction**

The 2017 Audi A5 Quattro is a luxury coupe that offers a sophisticated driving experience. It comes with a standard 2.0L inline-4 engine that produces 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It is mated to a 7-speed dual-clutch S tronic transmission that sends power to all four wheels. Other available engine options include a 3.0L V6 with 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0L TDI diesel engine with 190 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque.

The A5 Quattro is praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it also has some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L inline-4 engine**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner may fail, causing the timing chain to skip and damage the engine.
        * Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.0L V6 engine**
    * **Valve carbon buildup:** Carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, reducing engine performance.
        * Symptoms: Reduced power, hesitation, check engine light
        * Solution: Clean the intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0L TDI diesel engine**
    * **Diesel particulate filter (DPF) regeneration issues:** The DPF may not regenerate properly, causing a loss of power and increased emissions.
        * Symptoms: Reduced power, check engine light, increased fuel consumption
        * Solution: Replace the DPF
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission**
    * **Clutch pack failure:** The clutch packs may wear out prematurely, causing slipping and loss of power.
        * Symptoms: Slipping gears, loss of power, check transmission light
        * Solution: Replace the clutch pack
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension control arm bushings:** The front suspension control arm bushings may wear out prematurely, causing clunking noises and poor handling.
        * Symptoms: Clunking noises, poor handling, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to not start.
        * Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle
        * Solution: Replace the battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** Condensation may build up inside the headlights, impairing visibility.
        * Symptoms: Foggy or wet headlights
        * Solution: Replace the headlights
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system software bugs:** The infotainment system may experience software bugs, causing glitches and freezing.
        * Symptoms: System freezes, navigation errors, audio issues
        * Solution: Update the software
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Audi A5 Quattro is a well-regarded luxury coupe with some reported issues. The most serious concerns are related to the engine and transmission, which can be expensive to repair. The vehicle also has some minor electrical and interior issues. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues should be carefully considered before purchasing this particular model.
