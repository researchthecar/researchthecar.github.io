## 2021 Audi A5 Quattro

The 2021 Audi A5 Quattro is a luxury compact car available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four producing 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 producing 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

The A5 Quattro is praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it has received some criticism for its firm ride and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail prematurely, leading to reduced engine power and increased oil consumption. Symptoms include excessive smoke from the exhaust, decreased performance, and illuminated check engine light.
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V588000

**3.0-liter Turbocharged V6**

* **Valve Seat Recession:** The valve seats in the cylinder head can become recessed over time, leading to reduced compression and engine performance. Symptoms include rough idle, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
Solution: Replace cylinder head
Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Clutch Pack Failure:** The clutch packs within the transmission can fail, resulting in gear slippage and difficulty shifting. Symptoms include delayed or harsh shifting, slipping gears, and illuminated transmission warning light.
Solution: Replace clutch pack
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V596000

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Suspension Malfunction:** The adaptive suspension can malfunction, leading to a stiff orbouncy ride. Symptoms include excessive body roll, uneven tire wear, and illuminated suspension warning light.
Solution: Diagnose and repair suspension components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The vehicle's battery can discharge prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the car. Symptoms include dim headlights, slow cranking, and illuminated battery warning light.
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** The headlights can develop condensation inside the lens, causing poor visibility at night.
Solution: Replace headlight assembly
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system can experience glitches, including freezing, slow response, and connectivity issues.
Solution: Software update or hardware replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Audi A5 Quattro is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some significant issues with its engine, transmission, and suspension. The cost to fix these issues can be substantial, making it important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a used A5 Quattro.
