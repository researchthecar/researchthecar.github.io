**Introduction:**

The 2014 Audi TT RS is a high-performance sports car powered by a turbocharged 2.5L inline-five engine that delivers 400 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a six-speed manual or a seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission. The TT RS is acclaimed for its blistering performance, precise handling, and luxurious interior, but it has also been subject to a few reported issues.

### **Engine**

**2.5L inline-five TFSI**

* **Misfiring:**
    * Symptoms: Rough idle, reduced power, or engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs, coils, or fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger failure:**
    * Symptoms: Loss of boost pressure, reduced power, or engine knocking
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**Six-speed manual**

* **Difficulty shifting:**
    * Symptoms: Gears grind or refuse to engage
    * Solution: Adjust or replace shift linkage or clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch**

* **Shift hesitation or jerks:**
    * Symptoms: Transmission delays or stutters during shifting
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

### **Electrical**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Symptoms: Battery goes dead prematurely
    * Solution: Test battery, charging system, or electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical power steering failure:**
    * Symptoms: Loss of power steering assistance
    * Solution: Replace power steering motor or module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

### **Interior**

* **Door panel rattles:**
    * Symptoms: Rattles or vibrations from door panels
    * Solution: Adjust door panel mounting points or add sound deadening material
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

While the 2014 Audi TT RS is generally a reliable and impressive sports car, it has faced some issues with its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. Some of these issues can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others may be more serious and require significant repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their purchasing decision.
