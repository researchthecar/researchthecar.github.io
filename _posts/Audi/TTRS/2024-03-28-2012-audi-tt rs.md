**Introduction:**
The 2012 Audi TT RS is a high-performance sports car known for its powerful engine and sharp handling. Offered with a single engine and transmission option, the TT RS delivers 360 horsepower and 343 lb-ft of torque. The 2.5L TFSI engine is paired with a six-speed dual-clutch S tronic transmission. Despite its impressive features, the Audi TT RS has encountered various issues reported by owners.

**Engine:**
* **2.5L TFSI Engine - Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some 2.5L TFSI engines may exhibit excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage. Symptoms include oil drips or leaks, blue smoke from the exhaust, and frequent oil level sensor alerts.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the affected engine components, including valve stems, seals, or pistons.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**
* **Six-Speed S tronic Transmission - Clutch Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The six-speed S tronic transmission may experience clutch slipping or shuddering, especially during shifts. This can result in jerky acceleration or hesitation when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch plates and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**
* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**
* **Headlight Flickering or Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights may flicker or malfunction, including dimmed output, intermittent failures, or complete loss of illumination.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty headlight assemblies or control modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**
* **No Issues Found**

**Interior:**
* **Dashboard or Center Console Squeaks or Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various dashboard or center console components may exhibit squeaks or rattles due to loose fitment or broken clips.
    * Solution: Realign or tighten the affected components or replace broken clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Radio or Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The radio or navigation system may encounter malfunctions, including frozen displays, loss of functionality, or error messages.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the affected modules.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 2012 Audi TT RS offers impressive performance, it has faced various reported issues that can be costly to repair. The most significant concerns involve excessive oil consumption in the 2.5L TFSI engine and clutch problems with the six-speed S tronic transmission. Electrical issues, interior squeaks or rattles, and radio/navigation malfunctions are also notable concerns. Overall, the severity of these issues varies, and the costs to fix can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand.
