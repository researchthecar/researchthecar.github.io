**2016 Audi Q5**

The 2016 Audi Q5 is a compact luxury SUV. It comes standard with a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 220 horsepower and 258 pound-feet of torque. Optional engine choices include a 3.0-liter supercharged V6 that produces 352 horsepower and 346 pound-feet of torque, and a 2.0-liter diesel engine that produces 190 horsepower and 295 pound-feet of torque. All engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Q5 comes standard with front-wheel drive, and all-wheel drive is available as an option.

The Q5 is praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it is criticized for its lack of rear-seat legroom and cargo space.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased exhaust smoke, oil leaks
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle, loss of power, stalling
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idle
    * Solution: Clean intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Supercharged V6**

* **Supercharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, strange noises from the engine bay
    * Solution: Replace supercharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle, loss of power, stalling
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased exhaust smoke, oil leaks
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine performance, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Clean or replace DPF
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitations or Jerks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifts, especially when upshifting
    * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid change, or transmission rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, difficulty accelerating, slipping gears
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises from the front suspension, reduced handling stability
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of ride height, rough ride, suspension warning lights
    * Solution: Replace air springs or air compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical issues, such as flickering lights, power window failure, infotainment system issues
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair specific electrical component
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery, diagnose and repair parasitic battery drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rails Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Black paint on the roof rails peeling or fading
    * Solution: Replace or repaint roof rails
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight housings
    * Solution: Replace or reseal headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or creaking noises from the sunroof
    * Solution: Lubricate or adjust sunroof tracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Control Panel Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control features not working properly, such as temperature or fan speed control
    * Solution: Replace HVAC control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Audi Q5 is a reliable and well-built vehicle, but it is not without its problems. The most common issues reported are related to the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The severity of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem, but many of them can be costly to repair. Potential owners should be aware of these issues before purchasing a Q5.
