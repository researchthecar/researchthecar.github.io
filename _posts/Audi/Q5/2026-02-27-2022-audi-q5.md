# 2022 Audi Q5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi Q5 blends luxury, sharp handling, and modern technology into a popular compact luxury SUV package. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating the powertrain choices and overall build quality is key to making a smart investment. Because owner-reported data provides an early look into real-world ownership experiences, checking for any recurring complaints helps ensure a trouble-free garage addition.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It offers a strong balance of everyday responsiveness and efficiency for daily driving.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid (55 TFSI e)

This plug-in hybrid powertrain combines a 2.0-liter turbo four-cylinder with an electric motor to generate a combined 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It delivers brisk acceleration alongside electric-only driving range.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly through the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It provides rapid gear shifts and refined highway cruising behavior.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V898000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A high-voltage battery that overheats increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software as the final remedy, anticipated to be available in the second quarter of 2025. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V080000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A high-voltage battery that overheats increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software , free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V294000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The screws can loosen, allowing oil to leak and increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the screws as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi Q5 remains a compelling choice in the luxury compact SUV segment, offering refined powertrain options and sophisticated styling. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and test drive all electronic and mechanical systems to ensure everything operates as expected.
