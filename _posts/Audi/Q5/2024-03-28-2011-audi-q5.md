**Introduction:**

The 2011 Audi Q5 is a compact luxury crossover SUV that offers a refined driving experience and ample cargo space. It is available with a choice of two engines and two transmission options. The 2.0T engine produces 211 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.2L V6 engine generates 270 horsepower and 243 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a standard eight-speed Tiptronic transmission. Drivers appreciate the Q5's comfortable ride, spacious interior, and impressive handling, but some have encountered issues with various components.

**Engine**

**2.0T Engine**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on intake valves reduces engine performance, causing rough idling, hesitation, and reduced fuel economy.
    * Solution: Walnut shell blasting to remove carbon deposits.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of timing chain tensioner can cause rattling noises, chain skipping, and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.2L V6 Engine**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking valve cover gasket allows oil to seep out, causing oil consumption and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Coolant Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty coolant pump reduces coolant circulation, leading to overheating and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace coolant pump and associated hoses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Tiptronic Transmission**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty mechatronic unit can cause shifting problems, transmission slipping, and loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Ball Joint Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn ball joints result in loose or clunking noises, uneven tire wear, and reduced stability.
    * Solution: Replace ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ (per wheel)
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Suspension Bushings Deterioration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Deteriorated bushings can cause excessive noise, vibration, and reduced handling performance.
    * Solution: Replace suspension bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ (per wheel)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Dashboard Lighting Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning light bulbs in the instrument cluster cause limited visibility of vital gauges.
    * Solution: Replace faulty light bulbs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Gremlins:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, such as power windows malfunctioning, door locks failing, and dashboard lights flickering.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair specific electrical faults.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside headlight housings causes poor visibility and potential electrical damage.
    * Solution: Replace headlight housing or repair seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clogged sunroof drain plugs prevent water from draining properly, leading to water leaks inside the vehicle.
    * Solution: Clean or replace sunroof drain plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2011 Audi Q5 offers a comfortable and refined driving experience, it has been plagued by several reliability issues. The engine and transmission can face costly repairs, while the suspension and electrical systems can also cause problems. While some issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be severe and can put a significant dent in your wallet. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
