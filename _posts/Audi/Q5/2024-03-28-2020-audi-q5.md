**Introduction:**

The 2020 Audi Q5 is a compact luxury SUV known for its sleek design, premium cabin, and advanced technology. It's available with a choice of two gasoline engines: a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder and a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6. Transmission options include a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic (DCT) and an 8-speed automatic. While the Q5 offers a comfortable and enjoyable driving experience, several specific issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

- **Fuel Injector Failure:** Leaking or malfunctioning fuel injectors can cause rough idling, poor performance, and increased fuel consumption. Requires replacement of injectors. **$500 - $1,000+**
- **Timing Chain Tensioner Issues:** A faulty timing chain tensioner can lead to excessive noise and eventually skipped or broken timing chain. Requires replacement of tensioner and related components. **$1,500 - $2,500+**
- **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** Excessive carbon buildup on intake valves can reduce engine efficiency, causing reduced power and fuel economy. Requires cleaning or replacement of valves. **$500 - $1,500**
- **Recalls:**
  - NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V340000: Fuel pressure sensor may fail, leading to engine stall.

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

- **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** A faulty valve cover gasket can cause oil leaks and smoke from the engine. Requires replacement of gasket. **$500 - $1,000**
- **Exhaust Manifold Cracking:** Cracks in the exhaust manifold can lead to exhaust leaks and reduced engine performance. Requires replacement of manifold. **$1,000 - $2,000+**
- **Fuel Pump Failure:** A faulty fuel pump can cause difficulty starting the engine or loss of power while driving. Requires replacement of fuel pump. **$500 - $1,500**

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (DCT)**

- **Shifting Problems:** Rough shifting, delayed gear changes, or inability to engage gears. May require replacement of clutch components or transmission control module. **$1,000 - $3,000+**
- **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** The mechatronic unit controls the DCT operation and can experience electrical or mechanical failures. Requires replacement of unit. **$2,000 - $4,000+**
- **Recalls:**
  - NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V498000: Clutch failure may lead to loss of power.

**8-Speed Automatic**

- **Torque Converter Shudder:** Vibration or shuddering felt during acceleration or gear changes. May require replacement of torque converter. **$1,000 - $2,000**
- **Transmission Slipping:** Inability to transmit power effectively, resulting in loss of power or difficulty shifting gears. Requires rebuild or replacement of transmission. **$3,000 - $5,000+**

**Suspension**

- **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** Worn strut mount bearings can cause clunking or rattling noises and affect handling. Requires replacement of mounts. **$500 - $1,000+**
- **Sway Bar Link Failure:** Broken or damaged sway bar links can cause increased body roll and reduce handling stability. Requires replacement of links. **$200 - $500+**

**Electrical**

- **Infotainment System Malfunctions:** Freezing, crashing, or unresponsive infotainment systems. May require software updates or hardware replacements. **$100 - $1,000+**
- **Headlight Wiring Harness Issues:** Faulty wiring harnesses can lead to intermittent or failed headlights. Requires replacement of wiring harness. **$500 - $1,000+**
- **Battery Discharge:** Battery may discharge prematurely due to parasitic drain or faulty alternator. Requires diagnostics and repair of faulty components. **$500 - $1,500+**
- **Recalls:**
  - NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V619000: Insufficiently tightened battery may lead to fire.

**Exterior**

- **Roof Rail Noise:** Wind noise from roof rails can be excessive at higher speeds. Requires installation of wind deflectors or modification of rails. **$100 - $500+**

**Interior**

- **Sunroof Rattling:** Excessive noise or rattling from the panoramic sunroof. Requires adjustment or replacement of sunroof tracks or seals. **$500 - $1,000+**
- **Dashboard Cracking:** Cracks may develop on the dashboard over time. Requires replacement of dashboard. **$1,000 - $2,000+**
- **Door Panel Buzzing:** Buzzing or rattling noises from door panels. May require adjustment or replacement of door panel clips. **$100 - $500**

**Summary:**

While the 2020 Audi Q5 is generally a reliable vehicle, owners have reported several specific issues that can affect its performance, comfort, and safety. The severity of these issues varies, and repair costs can be significant. Owners should be aware of potential problems and factor them into their decision-making process when considering this vehicle.
