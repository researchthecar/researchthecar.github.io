### 2018 Audi Q5

The 2018 Audi Q5 is a compact luxury SUV that offers a blend of performance, style, and comfort. It comes with a choice of two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder and a 3.0L turbocharged V6. Both engines are mated to a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (S tronic). The Q5 has a spacious cabin with plenty of room for passengers and cargo, and it comes with a variety of high-tech features, including a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and an 8.3-inch infotainment system.

**Likes:**
- Powerful and efficient engines
- Sharp handling and comfortable ride
- Luxurious and spacious interior
- High-tech features

**Dislikes:**
- Expensive
- Some interior materials feel cheap
- Not as fuel-efficient as some competitors

### **Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noise from the engine, usually on cold starts.
Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
Recalls: 20V439000

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

**Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, and increased emissions.
Solution: Clean the intake valves.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission (S tronic)**

**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or refusing to shift gears.
Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,000
Recalls: 20V425000

### **Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

### **Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly or fails to start the vehicle.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

### **Interior**

**Climate Control System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to adjust temperature, or fan speed is inoperable.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the issue with the climate control system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Driver's Seat Lumbar Support Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Lumbar support in the driver's seat fails to adjust.
Solution: Repair or replace the lumbar support mechanism.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2018 Audi Q5 is a generally reliable and well-built vehicle, but it has some potential issues that buyers should be aware of. The most common problems are related to the engine and transmission, and these can be expensive to fix. However, overall, the Q5 is a good choice for buyers who are looking for a luxury SUV with strong performance, a comfortable ride, and a high-tech interior.
