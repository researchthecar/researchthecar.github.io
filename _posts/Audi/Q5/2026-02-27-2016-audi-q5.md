# 2016 Audi Q5

### Overview
The 2016 Audi Q5 is a compact luxury SUV that generally offers a good balance of performance, comfort, and practicality. It is known for its upscale interior, refined driving dynamics, and available Quattro all-wheel-drive system. While it's a well-built vehicle, like any automotive model, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This data focuses on common technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2016 model year across its various configurations. Understanding these potential issues can help prospective buyers and current owners with maintenance planning and repair cost estimations.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TFSI (Turbocharged Direct Injection)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil burning between oil changes, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Can be due to worn piston rings, valve stem seals, or PCV system issues. Requires diagnosis and potential internal engine repairs.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000+


### 3.0L TFSI (Supercharged)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $350 - $700

**Water Pump Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, whining noise from the front of the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump and thermostat assembly.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic (0B5 / DL501 - S tronic)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, slipping gears, transmission warning light, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,500

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky acceleration, delayed engagement, shuddering during shifts.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Audi Q5 is a generally reliable luxury SUV, particularly praised for its build quality and driving experience. The most commonly reported technical issues tend to revolve around the gasoline engines, specifically the 2.0L TFSI and 3.0L TFSI variants, with concerns like PCV valve failures and oil consumption being notable. The S tronic dual-clutch transmission can also present issues, primarily with the mechatronic unit and clutch packs. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches, are also present but typically less severe. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. While repairs for some of these issues can be costly, proactive maintenance and addressing early symptoms can mitigate significant problems.
