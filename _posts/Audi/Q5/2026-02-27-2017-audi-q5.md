# 2017 Audi Q5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury compact SUV, understanding common owner-reported concerns can help you make an informed decision. While many drivers enjoy refined performance and comfortable driving dynamics, a subset of owners have shared experiences regarding cooling system leaks, fuel delivery components, and steering concerns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers a balance of efficiency and responsive power for daily driving. Buyers should regularly check for common fluid leaks and ensure routine maintenance has been performed.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report low coolant levels and visible fluid puddles, often originating from the thermostat housing or water pump area.
- **Solution:** Inspect the thermostat housing and surrounding cooling components for leaks; replace failed housings or seals as needed.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience fuel delivery problems or start difficulties, sometimes linked to fuel pump flange or pump mechanism wear.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel pump and flange assembly inspected by a qualified technician and replace faulty components.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine offers robust acceleration and smooth power delivery across the power band. It is generally well-regarded but requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and prompt gear changes tailored to luxury driving dynamics. Regular fluid changes help maintain optimal operation over the vehicle's lifespan.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

Issues related to the electromechanical steering system can cause sudden stiffness, requiring careful inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Electromechanical Steering Stiffness**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience intermittent stiffness or increased resistance when turning left or right.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering rack assembly for moisture intrusion or electrical faults, and replace the electromechanical unit if failure is confirmed.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Camera System

Electrical accessories such as backup cameras and cabin electronics should be thoroughly tested during a used car inspection.

**Backup Camera Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display may intermittently show a black screen or fail to display the proper view when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Check camera wiring harnesses, software modules, and display connections for faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V001000: Visibility:Sun/Moon Roof Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If this happens, the corrosion can weaken the canister and the inflator could fracture without air bag deployment, propelling fragments into the passenger compartment, striking and causing serious injury to vehicle occupants. An inflator rupture may result in metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the inflator and either coat it or replace it, as necessary, after removing the surrounding foam, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V002000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the coolant pump overheats, it can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will update the software so that the power supply to the coolant pump is deactivated if the pump becomes blocked with debris, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V297000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing defect within the gearbox, despite selecting the Park position, the vehicle may still unexpectedly roll. Despite selecting 'Park', if the parking brake is not applied, the vehicle may rollaway, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the geabox in the affected vehicles, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V229000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A blocked pump or a short-circuited pump can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V298000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump flange if cracks are present. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When evaluating a used model, conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection that focuses on the cooling system, steering components, and electronics can save you from unexpected repair bills. While owner complaints highlight specific areas of concern, proper maintenance history remains the best indicator of long-term dependability.
