# 2014 Audi Q5

### Overview
The 2014 Audi Q5 is a compact luxury SUV that generally offers a good balance of performance, comfort, and practicality. It's known for its upscale interior, refined driving dynamics, and available Quattro all-wheel-drive system.  While it's a well-built vehicle, like any car, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This data focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. The Q5 offers a range of powertrain options, each with its own set of potential, though not universal, reliability considerations.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TFSI (Turbocharged Direct Injection)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil consumption, potential for oil leaks, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Piston ring replacement, PCV valve replacement, or other internal engine repairs.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Timing Chain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine, particularly on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Timing chain and tensioner replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


### 3.0L TFSI (Supercharged)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, whistling noise from the engine, potential oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Audi Q5 is a generally reliable luxury SUV, with most owners reporting satisfaction. The most commonly cited technical issues revolve around the gasoline engines, specifically the 2.0L TFSI experiencing oil consumption and timing chain concerns, and the 3.0L TFSI having potential PCV valve issues. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and tail light problems, are also noted but are typically less severe. The diesel engine and transmissions have a strong track record with fewer reported problems. Overall, while not immune to issues, the Q5 offers a solid ownership experience, especially when routine maintenance is diligently performed and potential engine concerns are addressed proactively.
