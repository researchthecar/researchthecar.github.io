**Introduction**

The 2014 Audi Q5 is a compact luxury SUV known for its blend of performance and everyday practicality. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including a 225-hp 2.0T engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a 272-hp 3.0T engine with an 8-speed automatic or 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. Drivers praise the Q5's spacious cabin, comfortable ride, and athletic handling. However, like any vehicle, the 2014 Audi Q5 has encountered some issues.

**Engine**

**2.0T Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced performance, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Manual cleaning of the intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0T Engine**

* **Timing Chain Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, rattling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, rough shifting, loss of gears
    * Solution: Mechatronic unit replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**All Models**

* **Rear Air Suspension Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear suspension sagging, reduced ride height
    * Solution: Replacement of air suspension bags
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**All Models**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Alternator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**All Models**

* **Taillight Wiring Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Taillights not functioning, brake lights not illuminating
    * Solution: Replacement of wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**All Models**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water intrusion into the cabin, mold growth
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Trim Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Decorative trim around the center console peeling off
    * Solution: Replacement of trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2014 Audi Q5 has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While it offers many desirable features and a premium driving experience, the vehicle has been plagued by several common issues. The most significant and costly issues are related to the timing chain and transmission, which can result in hefty repair bills. Other problems include suspension leaks, electrical issues, and interior trim concerns. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchase decision.
