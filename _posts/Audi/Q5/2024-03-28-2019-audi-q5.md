**Introduction:**

The 2019 Audi Q5 is a luxury compact SUV that offers a blend of performance, comfort, and technology. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0L four-cylinder engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, delivering 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The Q5 is praised for its sophisticated interior, sharp handling, and generous cargo space, but some owners have reported certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Timing Chain Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear on the timing chain, resulting in rattling or screeching noises, decreased engine performance, or engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, tensioner, and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to frequent oil top-ups or low oil warnings.
    * Solution: Replacing piston rings, valve seals, or other internal engine components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerky or delayed gear changes, slipping gears, or difficulty selecting gears.
    * Solution: Software updates, valve body replacement, or transmission rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electronic control unit for the transmission fails, causing transmission malfunctions or complete failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air suspension system leaks or malfunctions, causing uneven ride height or suspension collapse.
    * Solution: Replacement of air springs, compressor, or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wear on the front control arm bushings, causing knocking or rattling noises, vibration, or misalignment.
    * Solution: Replacement of the control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains excessively when parked, leading to difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system components, such as faulty modules or wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning touchscreen, unresponsive buttons, or audio system problems.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies, affecting visibility.
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlight assemblies or resealing of the units.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Camera Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera malfunctions, resulting in a black screen or distorted image.
    * Solution: Replacement of the camera or wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks through the panoramic sunroof, causing damage to the headliner or electronics.
    * Solution: Adjustment of the sunroof, replacement of gaskets, or reinstallation of the sunroof.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Automatic Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: HVAC system malfunctions, resulting in improper temperature or air distribution.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of HVAC components, such as actuators or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Audi Q5 offers a premium driving experience, but some owners have encountered various issues ranging in severity and cost. Engine problems, transmission malfunctions, and electrical issues can be significant and expensive to repair. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider preventive maintenance or extended warranties to mitigate the financial burden of repairs.
