The 2024 Audi Q5 is a popular luxury compact SUV known for its comfortable ride, upscale interior, and standard all-wheel drive. Engine options include a 40 TFSI 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder producing 201 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, a 45 TFSI 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder producing 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, and a 55 TFSI e plug-in hybrid powertrain combining a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder with an electric motor producing a combined 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. All engines are paired with a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. Owners generally appreciate the Q5's refined driving experience and technology features, but some have expressed concerns about infotainment system glitches and occasional reliability issues.

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Lagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The Audi MMI (Multi Media Interface) system may freeze, become unresponsive, or exhibit significant lag when performing tasks such as navigating menus, using Apple CarPlay/Android Auto, or adjusting settings.
    *   Solution: Software updates from Audi can sometimes address these issues. In some cases, a complete system reset or hardware replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1200+ (hardware replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Parking Sensors, Driver Assist Features)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Erroneous warnings, failure to activate, or inconsistent performance of parking sensors, lane keep assist, adaptive cruise control, or other driver-assist features.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of the specific sensor or module causing the problem. Replacement or recalibration may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ per sensor/module
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Unexplained rattling or squeaking noises emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or rear cargo area.
    *   Solution: Identifying the source of the noise, which may involve tightening loose trim pieces, applying felt tape to contact points, or replacing defective components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+ (depending on the severity and repair)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the sunroof, excessive wind noise when closed, or water leaks.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating sunroof tracks, replacing worn seals, or repairing/replacing the sunroof motor or mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Tail Light Condensation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the tail light housings.
    *   Solution: Replacing the affected tail light housing assembly. Some owners attempt to seal the existing housing, but this is not always a permanent fix.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+ per tail light
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2024 Audi Q5 exhibits a range of issues, with electrical problems related to the infotainment system and driver-assist features being the most frequently reported. Interior rattles and exterior condensation are also noted. The severity of these issues varies, but some can be costly to repair, particularly those requiring hardware replacement. Overall, prospective buyers should be aware of these potential problems and consider them when evaluating the vehicle.
