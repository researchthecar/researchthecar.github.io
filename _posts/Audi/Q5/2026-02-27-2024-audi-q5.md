# 2024 Audi Q5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Audi Q5, buyers can generally expect a refined luxury crossover experience backed by strong engineering. However, owner reports submitted to safety authorities highlight a few specific quirks and operational concerns—particularly regarding advanced driver-assistance features and electrical behavior—that are worth keeping in mind during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 produces roughly 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. It is a widely used and generally robust powerplant in modern Audi lineups, though regular maintenance is essential for long-term health.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid (55 TFSI e)

Pairing a 2.0-liter turbo-4 with an electric motor, this plug-in hybrid option generates a combined 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque while offering electric-only driving range. It combines strong performance with improved efficiency, though it introduces added electrical complexity.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It delivers rapid gear changes and responsive performance under varied driving conditions.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is strong and confident, though routine wear items should be carefully verified on any pre-owned model.

**Premature Rotor Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have experienced faster-than-expected wear on brake rotors under normal driving conditions within the first year of ownership.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pad thickness and rotor condition during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure they meet service standards.

## Electrical

### Power Liftgate Sensor

Exterior convenience features like power liftgates add daily usability, though sensor sensitivity can occasionally catch users off guard.

**Accidental Liftgate Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The hands-free kick sensor or key fob proximity can inadvertently trigger the tailgate to close while loading or unloading cargo from the rear.
- **Solution:** Be mindful of key fob placement and foot positioning near the rear bumper when accessing the cargo area.

## Safety

### Forward Collision / Pre-Sense System

Advanced safety systems provide helpful driver aids, but sensitive programming can occasionally cause unexpected interventions.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the Audi Pre-Sense collision avoidance system occasionally triggers sudden, hard braking for no apparent reason, creating potential rear-end collision risks with following traffic.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer check for software updates related to driver-assistance modules, and note that settings may need to be adjusted or reset periodically.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V080000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A high-voltage battery that overheats increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software , free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V294000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The screws can loosen, allowing oil to leak and increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the screws as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi Q5 remains a compelling luxury SUV choice, blending comfort, modern technology, and capable powertrain options. While owner complaints point to occasional overly sensitive collision avoidance warnings and minor convenience system quirks, a thorough test drive and professional inspection will help ensure any prospective vehicle is in top shape.
