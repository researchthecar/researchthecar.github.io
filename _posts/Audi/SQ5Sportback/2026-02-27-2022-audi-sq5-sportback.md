# 2022 Audi SQ5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi SQ5 Sportback combines coupe-like styling with a potent turbocharged V6 engine and standard Quattro all-wheel drive. For used car buyers, this sporty luxury crossover offers engaging performance and high-end interior technology, though prospective owners should thoroughly evaluate available models for maintenance history and any lingering electronic or powertrain quirks reported by early adopters.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and passing power. It is generally regarded as a strong and smooth performer, though regular premium fuel and meticulous maintenance are vital for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system, offering quick gear changes and refined cruising behavior. This traditional torque-converter automatic has proven reliable under normal operating conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V947000: Electrical System:Body Control Module/Bcm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover on the gateway control module and seal an underbody seam as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V567000: Structure:Body:Tailgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly connected rear lid may decrease the structural protection of the vehicle during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** A dealer will replace the rear lid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi SQ5 Sportback remains an appealing choice for buyers seeking a blend of utility, luxury, and sport-sedan performance. While formal complaint data for this model year is minimal, shoppers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify service records to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
