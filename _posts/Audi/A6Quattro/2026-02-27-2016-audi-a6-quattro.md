# 2016 Audi A6 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi A6 Quattro delivers a refined driving experience, cutting-edge interior technology, and confident all-weather performance. Prospective buyers evaluating a used model should review the available powertrain configurations and inspect the vehicle thoroughly, keeping in mind that owner-reported experiences can vary.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced mix of efficiency and power. It serves as a competent entry-level option for daily commuting.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0L supercharged V6 engine generates roughly 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and strong passing power. It is a popular choice for buyers seeking enhanced performance paired with Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission is paired with the V6 engine, delivering smooth and responsive gear shifts. It is generally well-regarded for durability under normal driving conditions.

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission is typically paired with the 2.0L engine, offering quick shifts and engaging performance. Buyers should ensure smooth operation during test drives, as dual-clutch units require attentive maintenance.
