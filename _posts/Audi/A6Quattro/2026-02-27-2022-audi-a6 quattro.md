### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one or more corners, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, connectivity issues (Bluetooth, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto), navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### Driver Assistance Systems
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts from adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors; system deactivation.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or recalibration of sensors, replacement of faulty sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi A6 Quattro demonstrates a high level of reliability, with most major mechanical and electrical systems performing as expected. The powertrain, including both the 2.0L and 3.0L engines and the 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. Potential concerns are primarily concentrated in optional features like the Adaptive Air Suspension, where leaks can occur, and in the complex electrical systems, particularly the infotainment and driver assistance sensors, which may experience occasional software glitches or sensor malfunctions. These electrical issues are often resolvable with software updates. Overall, the A6 Quattro is a dependable luxury sedan, with most reported issues being minor or related to optional equipment.
