# 2020 Audi A6 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi A6 Quattro, prospective buyers will find a luxury sedan renowned for its refined driving dynamics, upscale cabin, and advanced technology suite. However, owner reports submitted to safety authorities highlight several recurring electronic and electrical quirks that shoppers should investigate carefully during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering a balance of everyday efficiency and competent power for daily commuting.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine generates around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration paired with Audi's signature Quattro all-wheel-drive system.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear changes across all trim levels equipped with Quattro all-wheel drive, though software-related electronic glitches have occasionally been reported by owners.

**Motive Power Loss and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected loss of motive power accompanied by illuminated brake and transmission warning lights while driving.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician to check transmission control modules and software integrity.

## Electrical

### MMI Software and Door Actuators

Electrical architecture complaints frequently center on software glitches, infotainment connectivity hiccups, and occasional complexities tied to the 48-volt mild-hybrid system.

**Camera Display and Door Latch Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The 360-degree or backup camera display may intermittently show a black screen or cancel symbol, while door latches can occasionally fail to open on the first pull.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of affected door actuator mechanisms by a dealership.

**48V Mild-Hybrid System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden electrical system failure warnings, leading to unexpected vehicle stalling and multiple dashboard fault lights due to starter-alternator issues.
- **Solution:** Dealer diagnosis and replacement of failing 48V electrical components or associated control modules.

## Safety

### Pre-Sense and Driver Assistance

Advanced safety and driver-assistance features provide strong protection but rely heavily on sensitive bumper and windshield sensors that can require periodic calibration.

**Intermittent Forward Collision and Camera Errors**
- **Symptoms:** The Audi pre-sense system may operate intermittently, occasionally triggering false emergency braking events or displaying rear environmental camera fault codes.
- **Solution:** Recalibration or cleaning of sensor arrays and cameras by a service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V153000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Moisture may enter the starter generator and cause an electrical short circuit. A short circuit in the starter generator increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will replace the starter generator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V274000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may not properly deploy through the instrument panel tear seam. In the event of a crash, the passenger air bag may not properly deploy and inflate, and small plastic parts may enter the cabin, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the instrument panels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect brake fluid can reduce braking ability and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the brake fluid reservoir cap and, if necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A6 Quattro remains a sophisticated and comfortable luxury choice, but potential buyers should pay close attention to its complex electrical systems and mild-hybrid components during a pre-purchase inspection. Verifying that all software updates and recall services have been properly performed will help ensure a smoother ownership experience.
