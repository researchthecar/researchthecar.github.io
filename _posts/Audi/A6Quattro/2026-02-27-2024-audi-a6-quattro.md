# 2024 Audi A6 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi A6 Quattro delivers a sophisticated blend of luxury, cutting-edge technology, and confident all-weather performance. As a relatively new model on the used market, it generally benefits from modern engineering and high-end materials. However, prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate its advanced powertrain options and complex electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering responsive acceleration for daily driving. It balances efficient cruising with respectable power delivery, making it a dependable choice for everyday use.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine generates around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and effortless highway passing. Paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive, this option delivers smooth, confident performance for spirited drivers.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with the 2.0L engine, delivering rapid gear changes and responsive performance. It offers smooth operation in daily traffic while maintaining quick shifts during dynamic driving.

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed traditional automatic transmission is mated to the 3.0L V6 engine, providing refined and seamless gear changes. It suits the luxury cruiser character of the A6 Quattro exceptionally well with reliable, everyday shifting behavior.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi A6 Quattro remains a compelling luxury sedan option for used car buyers seeking refined performance and advanced features. Because this model year is still relatively new, thorough pre-purchase inspections remain essential to ensure all complex onboard systems and powertrain components are in proper working order.
