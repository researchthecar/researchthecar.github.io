**Introduction**

The 2007 Audi A6 Quattro is a luxury sedan that offers a blend of performance and comfort. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.2-liter V6, a 4.2-liter V8, and a 5.2-liter V10. The 3.2-liter V6 produces 255 horsepower and 247 lb-ft of torque, while the 4.2-liter V8 produces 350 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The 5.2-liter V10 produces an impressive 435 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. All engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2007 Audi A6 Quattro have praised the vehicle's performance, handling, and interior quality. However, some owners have reported experiencing a number of issues with the vehicle, including engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems.

**Engine**

**3.2-liter V6**

* **Timing Chain Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or fail completely.
Solution: Replacing the timing chain and related components is necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Intake Manifold Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold may crack or warp, causing vacuum leaks and engine performance issues.
Solution: Replacing the intake manifold is necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**4.2-liter V8**

* **Valve Stem Seal Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The valve stem seals may fail, causing oil to leak into the combustion chamber and resulting in blue smoke from the exhaust.
Solution: Replacing the valve stem seals is necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Summary**

The 2007 Audi A6 Quattro is a reliable and well-regarded luxury sedan. However, some owners have experienced a number of issues with the vehicle, including engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems. While some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a 2007 Audi A6 Quattro.
