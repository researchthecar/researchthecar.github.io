# 2017 Audi A6 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Audi A6 Quattro, prospective buyers will find a refined luxury sedan that balances engaging performance with sophisticated technology. While it generally offers a comfortable ride and capable handling, owners have reported certain recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that warrant a careful inspection before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces around 252 horsepower and delivers a strong blend of efficiency and everyday performance. Overall, it is a robust powerplant, though routine checks of cooling components and engine mounts are recommended.

**Engine Mount and Cooling System Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid-damped engine mounts can collapse and leak fluid, leading to metal-on-metal conditions and vibrations. Owners have also reported coolant leaks, water pump failures, and stuck thermostats causing the engine to overheat.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine mounts for fluid leaks or physical collapse, and check the cooling system components for signs of leaks or thermostat malfunction.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine generates approximately 333 horsepower, providing smooth and brisk acceleration across the power band. It is generally well-regarded for durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, smooth gear changes paired with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid services are essential to maintaining long-term shift quality.

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed traditional automatic transmission provides seamless power delivery for models paired with the V6 engine configuration. It is known for smooth operation and strong reliability under normal driving conditions.

## Steering

### Power Steering Rack

Issues related to the steering system can severely impact vehicle control and require prompt professional attention.

**Steering Rack and Control Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden loss of power steering assist, heavily restricted steering wheel movement, or a locked steering wheel while driving. Multiple generic error codes can also populate due to power steering control module glitches.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the steering rack assembly and power steering control module for diagnostic trouble codes or mechanical wear.

## Safety

### Blind Spot and Collision Avoidance Systems

Advanced driver assistance features enhance highway safety, though intermittent sensor faults should be verified during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Intermittent Blind Spot Warning Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger-side blind spot monitoring system may operate intermittently and fail to register surrounding vehicles or tractor-trailers on a random basis, often without generating diagnostic trouble codes.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealer or specialist to check sensor alignment and diagnostic parameters, keeping in mind that intermittent faults can be difficult to capture without active codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V035000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V955000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Additionally, certain 2017 Audi Q7, A4 Sedan, A4 Allroad, and 2018 Audi Q5 vehicles are being recalled because the seat belt pretensioners may not activate properly. In the event of a crash, if the air bags and/or the seat belt pretensioners do not inflate or function properly, the vehicle occupants have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen and Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected air bags and seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi A6 Quattro delivers an upscale driving experience, impressive powertrain choices, and advanced luxury features. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly evaluate the vehicle's steering components, engine mounts, and electronic safety aids during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid unexpected repair bills.
