**Introduction**

The 2002 Audi A6 Quattro is a mid-size luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience, a comfortable interior, and quattro all-wheel drive for enhanced stability and performance. It is powered by a 3.0-liter V6 engine paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The A6 Quattro offers a well-rounded package with impressive handling, a spacious cabin, and a range of standard features. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 FSI**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on startup, decreased engine power, and possible engine failure.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Runner Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, rough idling, and stalling.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold runner control valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Shuddering:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or shuddering during acceleration or gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace the CVT fluid and filter, or in severe cases, the entire CVT.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises when turning, vibrations, and premature tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the lower control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **CEL Illumination Due to Oxygen Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check Engine Light (CEL) illuminates due to faulty oxygen sensor readings.
    * Solution: Replace the oxygen sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Control Unit (ECU) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and instrument cluster malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replace the ECU.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight assembly.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof will not open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Audi A6 Quattro offers a luxurious and well-equipped experience, but it is not without potential issues. While some concerns, such as timing chain tensioner failure and electrical control unit failure, can be costly to repair, others like sunroof motor failure are less severe. Overall, the reliability of the A6 Quattro is moderate, but it is essential to weigh the potential costs of repairs against the vehicle's overall benefits when considering purchasing one.
