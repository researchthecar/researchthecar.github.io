# 2023 Audi A6 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2023 Audi A6 Quattro means looking at a modern luxury sedan that balances sophisticated engineering with strong performance. While early owner feedback points to isolated powertrain concerns, the vehicle generally maintains the high standards expected from Audi's luxury lineup. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the drivetrain and transmission operation during a test drive to ensure everything is in proper working order.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering a responsive and efficient drive. It provides smooth power delivery suited for daily commuting while maintaining balanced luxury performance.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The upgraded 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine generates about 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It delivers brisk acceleration and robust passing power for a more dynamic driving experience.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission sends power smoothly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes, though buyers should watch for any unexpected warning codes during evaluation.

**Transmission Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly displays a transmission malfunction warning on the highway and begins to decelerate, requiring the car to be pulled over and towed.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnosed by a qualified repair shop to retrieve transmission diagnostic trouble codes and perform necessary component repairs or replacements.

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission is paired with specific powertrain configurations to provide refined and seamless gear changes. It is generally robust and well-matched to highway cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi A6 Quattro offers a refined luxury experience with strong powertrain choices and advanced technology. Because owner reports note isolated transmission issues, a pre-purchase inspection focusing on powertrain diagnostics is strongly recommended for peace of mind.
