# 2019 Audi A6 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi A6 Quattro delivers luxury, advanced technology, and confident all-weather performance. However, used car buyers should approach this model year with caution due to several recurring electrical and powertrain complaints. Being informed about common mechanical and electrical pain points can help prospective owners spot potential issues during a test drive and budget for possible repairs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It offers balanced efficiency and everyday drivability for base trims, though buyers should still watch out for general accessory leaks.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 produces 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for higher trims. While smooth and powerful, this powerplant has been associated with costly coolant leaks and rare internal wear issues.

**Coolant Leaks and Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaking into the vacuum hose system, leading to warnings or component degradation.
- **Solution:** Replace the failing water pump, vacuum lines, solenoids, and associated sensors.
- **Cost:** $5,000

**Catastrophic Internal Engine Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Metal debris found in the engine, resulting in severe internal mechanical failure and stalling.
- **Solution:** Requires a complete engine rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $12,000

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly through the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. While shift quality is generally crisp, owners have occasionally reported electrical-related transmission warnings and erratic behavior.

**Unintended Neutral Shift and Drivetrain Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden transmission failure warnings, unexpected shifting into neutral, or rolling backward while in drive.
- **Solution:** Diagnose software control modules or internal transmission components at an authorized service center.

## Steering

### Power Steering & Assist

The electronic power steering system provides light, adjustable feedback at varying speeds. Intermittent electronic faults can occasionally disable power assist, requiring greater physical effort from the driver.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel becomes heavy and difficult to turn accompanied by electronic power steering warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect electronic steering control modules and harness connections for faults.

## Electrical

### Starter-Alternator / Electrical System

The 48-volt mild-hybrid electrical architecture powers accessory systems and the belt-alternator starter. Failures within this system can cause sudden electrical shutdowns and lengthy waits for replacement parts.

**Starter-Generator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Electrical system failure warnings, sudden loss of motive power, and failure to restart due to a malfunctioning starter-alternator.
- **Solution:** Replace the starter-generator unit under applicable service actions or manufacturer recalls.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

The vehicle relies on a comprehensive array of cameras and radar sensors for driver assistance features. Glitches in these systems can lead to nuisance warnings or temporary loss of visibility aids.

**Erratic Collision Avoidance and Backup Camera Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Random activation of emergency brakes, erratic parking sensor warnings, and blank rearview camera screens.
- **Solution:** Reprogram camera modules, clean sensor lenses, or replace faulty control units.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V153000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Moisture may enter the starter generator and cause an electrical short circuit. A short circuit in the starter generator increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will replace the starter generator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect brake fluid can reduce braking ability and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the brake fluid reservoir cap and, if necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V125000: Lane Departure:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The driver may be unaware that the lane departure warning system is deactivated and rely on a system that is not functional, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi A6 Quattro offers a refined interior, strong engine choices, and sophisticated driving dynamics, but potential buyers should proceed carefully. electrical issues—specifically related to the 48-volt starter-generator system—and occasional coolant or powertrain issues require thorough pre-purchase inspections. Securing an extended warranty or comprehensive service history is highly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
