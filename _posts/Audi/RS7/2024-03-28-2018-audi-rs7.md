**Introduction:**

The 2018 Audi RS7 is a high-performance sportback coupe powered by a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine mated to an eight-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. It produces 560 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, offering exhilarating acceleration and handling dynamics. While the RS7 is praised for its luxurious cabin, advanced technology, and sleek design, there are a number of issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbo V8**

- **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Accumulation of carbon deposits on the intake valves can lead to reduced engine performance, rough idle, and increased emissions.
   - Solution: Removal of carbon deposits through chemical cleaning or walnut shell blasting.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

- **Turbocharger Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning turbochargers can cause a loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, and unusual noises.
   - Solution: Replacement of faulty turbochargers.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**

- **Shifting Hesitations:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Intermittent hesitations or delays during gear changes, particularly when accelerating or downshifting.
   - Solution: Software updates or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Air Suspension Malfunction:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Failure of the air suspension system can lead to uneven ride height, reduced handling, and potential safety hazards.
   - Solution: Replacement of faulty air suspension components, including compressors, sensors, or struts.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge when the vehicle is parked, leading to difficulty starting.
   - Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical components causing the drain, such as faulty modules or wiring.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Headlight Moisture Intrusion:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Accumulation of moisture inside the headlight assemblies, impairing visibility at night.
   - Solution: Replacement of faulty headlight seals or entire headlights.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Infotainment System Glitches:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Intermittent freezing, crashing, or incorrect functionality of the MMI infotainment system.
   - Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment module.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Audi RS7 is a thrilling performance vehicle, but it is not without its issues. While some issues, such as infotainment glitches, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, like engine and transmission problems, can be more severe and costly. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these issues before making a purchase and factor them into their decision-making process.
