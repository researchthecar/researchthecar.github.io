# 2016 Audi RS7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi RS7 combines breathtaking performance with high-end luxury, making it an enticing option for used-car buyers looking for a fast, sophisticated four-door coupe. However, prospective buyers should approach high-performance luxury vehicles with care, as complex engineering can lead to expensive maintenance and repairs down the road. Reviewing owner reports provides helpful insight into areas that may require extra attention during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbo

Equipped with a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing around 560 to 605 horsepower, this engine delivers phenomenal acceleration. While generally robust when properly maintained, it is a highly complex performance powerplant requiring diligent upkeep and premium servicing.

**Turbocharger Failure and Oil Screen Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported potential turbocharger failure, sometimes linked to issues with the oil feed screen supplying the turbochargers.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the turbochargers and oil feed lines during a pre-purchase inspection. Replace clogged oil screens or failed turbos as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Paired with a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission and Quattro all-wheel drive, gear changes are smooth and rapid. No widespread recurring powertrain complaints were reported in available owner data.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The electrical architecture is sophisticated, controlling numerous comfort and performance features, though occasional glitches have been reported.

**Auto Start-Stop Premature Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's auto start-stop system may engage prematurely while the car is still slowing to a stop, causing the engine to shut off while in motion and making steering heavy.
- **Solution:** Check for any applicable manufacturer software updates or diagnostic trouble codes affecting the engine management and start-stop systems.

## Exterior

### Window Glass

Exterior styling and trim are generally durable, though minor window glass defects can occasionally occur.

**Side Window Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Front driver and passenger side window glass may begin to delaminate over time.
- **Solution:** Inspect side windows for separation or bubbling, and replace affected glass if visibility or appearance is compromised.
- **Cost:** $1100

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Interior materials are luxurious and well-appointed, but physical components like seat belt latches should be thoroughly tested.

**Rear Seat Belt Buckle Latching Difficulty**
- **Symptoms:** Rear passenger seat belt buckles may fail to click or lock securely without excessive force.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear seat belt buckles for proper latching function and debris, and replace the buckle assembly if the mechanism fails to lock reliably.

### Air Bags

Standard safety equipment is comprehensive, though buyers should always confirm that all past safety recall campaigns have been completed.

**Air Bag Component Availability**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience delays in scheduling manufacturer safety recall repairs due to parts availability.
- **Solution:** Check the NHTSA VIN lookup tool to verify if safety recalls remain open and contact an authorized dealer to schedule necessary repairs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V096000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." If the operator were to load the vehicle based on information on the tire information label, they could overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the tire information label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi RS7 is an extraordinary performance machine that offers supercar-level speed alongside everyday luxury. When shopping for a used model, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an Audi specialist is strongly recommended to check the condition of the turbochargers, electronics, and overall mechanical health. Budgeting for higher-than-average maintenance costs will help ensure a more rewarding ownership experience.
