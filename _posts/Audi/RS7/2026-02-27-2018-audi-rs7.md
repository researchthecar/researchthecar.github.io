# 2018 Audi RS7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi RS7 delivers breathtaking performance paired with a luxurious cabin, making it an appealing choice for enthusiasts seeking executive-level speed. Because this high-performance luxury fastback operates with advanced twin-turbocharged engineering and complex electronics, used buyers should approach inspections with care. While widespread mechanical failures are limited in owner reports, maintenance costs for performance hardware remain exceptionally high.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 560 to 605 horsepower depending on the trim level. It offers blistering acceleration and robust highway performance, though proper maintenance history is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with quattro all-wheel drive to deliver rapid, smooth gear changes. It is generally robust when fluid changes are performed on schedule, handling the high torque output of the V8 engine effectively.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V096000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS7&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." If the operator were to load the vehicle based on information on the tire information label, they could overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the tire information label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi RS7 remains an exhilarating and sophisticated luxury performance car for used buyers who prioritize power and prestige. Prospective owners should secure a thorough pre-purchase inspection from an Audi specialist and budget generously for ongoing maintenance and premium upkeep.
