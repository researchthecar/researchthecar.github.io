**Introduction:**

The 2016 Audi A3 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan powered by a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a six-speed dual-clutch transmission and is available with Quattro all-wheel drive. The A3 is praised for its stylish design, well-appointed interior, and impressive performance. However, there have been some issues reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** Excessive carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause a loss of power, hesitation during acceleration, and increased fuel consumption. The solution is to clean the intake valves manually. Estimated cost: $300 - $600+.
**Recalls:** N/A

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Jerky Shifting:** Some owners have reported experiencing jerky or hesitant shifting, particularly at low speeds. The issue may be resolved by updating the transmission software or replacing the transmission control module. Estimated cost: $200 - $800+.
* **Premature Clutch Wear:** Excessive clutch wear can occur, leading to a loss of power, slipping gears, and difficulty shifting. The solution is to replace the clutch assembly. Estimated cost: $1,000 - $1,500+.
**Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V380000

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some A3s have experienced a parasitic battery drain that can lead to the battery losing power over time. The issue is typically caused by a faulty component or wiring harness. The solution is to diagnose and replace the affected component. Estimated cost: $100 - $500+.
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Some owners have reported experiencing glitches and freezing issues with the infotainment system. The solution may be to update the system software or replace the infotainment unit. Estimated cost: $500 - $1,500+.
**Recalls:** N/A

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:** Some A3s have experienced leaks from the sunroof, leading to water damage inside the vehicle. The solution is to replace the sunroof seal or adjust the sunroof alignment. Estimated cost: $200 - $500+.
* **Door Lock Failure:** Some owners have reported experiencing issues with the door locks not locking or unlocking properly. The issue may be resolved by replacing the door lock actuator or reprogramming the door lock control module. Estimated cost: $100 - $400+.
**Recalls:** N/A

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Some A3s have experienced a rattling noise coming from the dashboard. The issue may be resolved by tightening or replacing loose screws or components. Estimated cost: $100 - $300+.
* **HVAC Issues:** Some owners have reported experiencing issues with the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system, such as inconsistent temperature control or excessive noise. The solution may involve repairing or replacing faulty components within the HVAC system. Estimated cost: $200 - $800+.
**Recalls:** N/A

**Summary:**

The 2016 Audi A3 Quattro has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While some owners have been pleased with their vehicles, others have encountered various issues. The severity of the issues can range from minor annoyances to major problems that can affect the vehicle's performance or safety. Repair costs can be substantial, depending on the nature of the issue. It is important for prospective buyers to be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
