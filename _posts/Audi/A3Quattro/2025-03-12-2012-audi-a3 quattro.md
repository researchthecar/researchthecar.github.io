The 2012 Audi A3 Quattro is a compact luxury car known for its sporty handling, upscale interior, and available all-wheel drive. In 2012, the A3 Quattro was equipped with a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 200 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed S-tronic dual-clutch transmission. Other engine options for the 2012 Audi A3 included a 2.0L TDI diesel engine and a naturally aspirated 2.5L inline-5 engine (found in the RS3, which wasn't sold in the US in 2012). Many owners appreciate the A3's fuel efficiency, nimble handling, and premium feel. Common complaints revolve around potential maintenance costs associated with European cars and specific issues related to the DSG transmission.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (TFSI)**

*   **High Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, often exceeding 1 quart every 1,000 miles. This may be caused by faulty piston rings or excessive wear on the valve stem seals.
    *   Solution: Replacement of piston rings and/or valve stem seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Diverter Valve Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost pressure, reduced engine performance, and potential check engine light illumination.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the diverter valve.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission (DSG)**

*   **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, hesitation, clunking noises, failure to engage gears, and potential complete transmission failure.
    *   Solution: Replacement or repair of the mechatronic unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Clutch Pack Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Slipping during acceleration, jerky shifting, and difficulty engaging gears.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the clutch packs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $3,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises from the front suspension, uneven tire wear, and vague steering.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the control arm bushings or the entire control arm.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per control arm
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Ignition Coil Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough running engine, check engine light illumination.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty ignition coil(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per coil (depending on if you replace all 4)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Window Regulator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Window failing to move up or down, or moving erratically.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the window regulator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof getting stuck, making excessive noise, or leaking water.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating the sunroof tracks, repairing or replacing the sunroof motor, or replacing the sunroof seal.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Navigation System Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, system rebooting, or complete failure of the navigation unit.
    *   Solution: Resetting the system, software update, or replacement of the navigation unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 (reset) - $1,000+ (replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Audi A3 Quattro offers a blend of performance and luxury. However, potential buyers should be aware of some common issues. The most significant concerns revolve around the engine's oil consumption and the DSG transmission's reliability, both of which can lead to costly repairs. Suspension and electrical issues are also potential concerns, though generally less severe. Overall, a thorough inspection and maintenance history review are crucial when considering a used 2012 Audi A3 Quattro.
