# 2023 Audi A3 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A3 Quattro blends agile handling, a premium interior, and standard all-wheel drive into a compact luxury package. Prospective used-car buyers looking at this model will find a modern driving experience supported by advanced powertrain technology. Because this vehicle is still relatively new to the pre-owned market, long-term complaint trends are minimal, but reviewing its core mechanical options remains a vital step for any smart purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 201 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers brisk acceleration and solid efficiency, representing a reliable modern powertrain choice for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear changes while channeling power to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It provides a sporty and responsive driving dynamic suited for both city commutes and highway cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V901000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." Missing weight information may result in unintentional overloading of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi dealers will install an overlay label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2023 Audi A3 Quattro is an appealing choice for used-car buyers seeking luxury, efficiency, and confident all-weather traction. As always, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a verified maintenance history are recommended before finalizing your purchase.
