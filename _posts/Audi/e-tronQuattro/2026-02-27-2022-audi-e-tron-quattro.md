# 2022 Audi e-tron Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi e-tron Quattro represents a luxurious and technologically advanced entry into the electric vehicle space, offering refined driving dynamics and a quiet cabin. As a used buy, prospective owners should review available vehicle history and ensure all software updates have been completed. While modern EVs generally feature fewer moving parts than traditional internal combustion vehicles, assessing the condition of high-voltage components and onboard electronics remains important.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motors (355 hp / 414 lb-ft)

This dual-motor electric setup delivers 355 horsepower and 414 lb-ft of torque, with a temporary boost function up to 402 horsepower. It provides smooth, rapid acceleration and standard all-wheel-drive capability for confident daily driving.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

The single-speed automatic transmission delivers power seamlessly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system without gear-shift interruptions. It is generally robust and requires minimal routine maintenance compared to multi-gear transmissions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V240000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+Quattro&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the pushrod screw joint as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi e-tron Quattro offers a compelling blend of luxury, comfort, and zero-emission performance for used-car shoppers. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, charging hardware, and software versions to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
