## 2020 Audi e-tron Quattro

The 2020 Audi e-tron Quattro is a all-electric SUV powered by two electric motors.  It has a combined output of 402 horsepower and 490 lb-ft of torque. The e-tron has a range of up to 204 miles on a single charge and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 5.5 seconds.

The e-tron has received praise for its luxurious interior, spacious cabin, and comfortable ride. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's infotainment system, charging system, and suspension.

### **Suspension**

**Air Suspension Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to sag and become difficult to control.
Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor or air springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**

**Infotainment System Glitches**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, such as freezing, rebooting, or displaying incorrect information.
Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: 20V-321

**Charging System Issues**
Description/Symptoms: The charging system may experience issues, such as slow charging, intermittent charging, or failure to charge.
Solution: Replace the charging port or charging cable.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: 20V-556

### **Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

### **Interior**

**Panoramic Sunroof Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof may leak water into the cabin.
Solution: Replace the sunroof seals or adjust the sunroof alignment.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

Overall, the 2020 Audi e-tron Quattro is a reliable and well-built vehicle. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's infotainment system, charging system, and suspension. These issues can be costly to fix, so it is important to factor in the potential cost of repairs when considering purchasing this vehicle.
