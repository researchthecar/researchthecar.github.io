# 2023 Audi e-tron Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi e-tron Quattro represents an upscale entry into the luxury electric SUV segment, offering refined styling, a quiet cabin, and standard dual-motor all-wheel drive. As a modern electric vehicle, prospective buyers looking at the pre-owned market should still evaluate overall build quality and verify proper charging functionality. Reviewing owner feedback and remaining factory warranties can provide helpful peace of mind when shopping for a used luxury EV.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motors (355 hp / 414 lb-ft)

The dual asynchronous electric motors generate 355 horsepower and 414 lb-ft of torque, with a temporary boost to 402 horsepower during sport mode. This powertrain delivers smooth, instantaneous acceleration suited for luxury daily driving.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

A fixed-ratio single-speed automatic transmission transfers power seamlessly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system without traditional gear shifts. The layout is mechanically straightforward and generally free of multi-gear complexity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V240000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+Quattro&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the pushrod screw joint as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi e-tron Quattro blends traditional luxury appointments with zero-emission driving. Used-car buyers will find a quiet and well-equipped electric SUV, though inspecting the high-voltage battery health and ensuring all software updates are current remains a sensible step during purchase evaluation.
