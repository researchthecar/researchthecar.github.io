**Introduction:**

The 2022 Audi e-tron Quattro is an all-electric SUV with a dual-motor electric powertrain. It offers a refined driving experience, spacious interior, and advanced technology features. However, like any vehicle, it can encounter certain issues.

**Electric Motor**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**12-Volt Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The 12-volt auxiliary battery drains prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle or operating accessories.
Solution: Replacement of the 12-volt battery and potential repair of electrical components causing excessive drainage.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Charging System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete failure of the vehicle's charging system, preventing the battery from charging properly.
Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty charging components, such as the onboard charger, charging cable, or battery cooling system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
Recalls: NHTSA 22V-470, NHTSA 22V-800

**Exterior**

**Roof Rails Corrosion**
Description/Symptoms: Corrosion or discoloration on the aluminum roof rails, leading to a visual defect.
Solution: Replacement of affected roof rails.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Sunroof Mechanism Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Inability to open or close the panoramic sunroof, or noisy operation due to faulty mechanisms.
Solution: Repair or replacement of sunroof components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
Recalls: None Found

**Touchscreen Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Unresponsiveness, lag, or flickering of the central touchscreen display.
Solution: Firmware updates or potential replacement of the touchscreen unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Audi e-tron Quattro is generally a reliable vehicle, but some specific issues have been reported. Electrical issues related to the 12-volt battery and charging system are the most common concerns. Exterior corrosion on the roof rails and interior issues with the sunroof mechanism and touchscreen display have also been encountered. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost of repairs can range from minor to significant.
