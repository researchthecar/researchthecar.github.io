# 2021 Audi e-tron Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi e-tron Quattro brings luxury electric driving to the market with refined styling and a smooth ride. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability trends carefully, particularly regarding high-voltage electrical architecture and auxiliary systems.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motor Powertrain (355 hp)

The base dual-motor electric setup produces 355 horsepower (up to 402 hp on boost) with standard electric Quattro all-wheel drive. This setup provides brisk, silent acceleration and robust everyday performance, though owners should monitor the complex high-voltage electronics.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

A direct single-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the dual motors without traditional gear-shifting hesitation. The unit is generally low-maintenance, though integrated with the vehicle's broader powertrain control modules.

## Electrical

### High-Voltage Electrical and Charging System

Electrical architecture controls major vehicle functions, but complex software and high-voltage modules have generated notable owner complaints.

**Electrical System Malfunction and Drive Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of forward propulsion accompanied by dashboard warnings requiring the vehicle to be safely stopped.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic evaluation by an authorized dealer or EV technician, which may involve software updates or component replacement.

**Charging Socket Seal Moisture Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture buildup in the high-voltage charging socket can trigger electrical fault warnings and potential short circuits.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of the faulty seal or charging interface component.
- **Cost:** $1,045.50

### Multi Media Interface (MMI) Displays

Cabin displays govern essential driver feedback and comfort controls, though interface bugs can temporarily disrupt visibility.

**Screen Blanking and Interface Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment and instrument cluster screens go blank during operation, resulting in a loss of display for speed and climate controls.
- **Solution:** Rebooting the system or having the dealership update MMI software and interface modules.

## Exterior

### Glass and Sunroof

Exterior glass components provide scenic cabin light, but rare spontaneous breakage events require costly structural glass replacement.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may rupture outward unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds without direct impact.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the sunroof glass assembly and clearing of glass debris.

## Safety

### Braking and Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety systems assist in accident avoidance, though software calibrations occasionally trigger unexpected interventions.

**Partial Brake Response and Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report instances of unexpected deceleration or perceived initial inconsistency in brake pedal travel.
- **Solution:** Check for software calibrations and consult dealer service bulletins regarding regenerative braking software.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V240000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+Quattro&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the pushrod screw joint as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi e-tron Quattro offers a compelling blend of luxury and electric performance, but potential buyers should thoroughly check vehicle history and remaining warranty coverage. Pay close attention to high-voltage electrical service records and infotainment software functionality during a pre-purchase inspection.
