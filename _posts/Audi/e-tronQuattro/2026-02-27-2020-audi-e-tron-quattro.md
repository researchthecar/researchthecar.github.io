# 2020 Audi e-tron Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi e-tron Quattro, buyers can expect a refined, all-electric luxury crossover offering dual-motor all-wheel drive and strong performance. While overall complaint volume in public records is quite low, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate build quality, electronics, and potential water ingress points during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motors (355 hp)

This dual-motor electric setup produces 355 horsepower (up to 402 horsepower on temporary boost) with instant torque distributed via electric quattro all-wheel drive. The powertrain delivers smooth, silent acceleration and generally proves robust in daily driving.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

A single-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to both front and rear axles without traditional gear shifting. This unit has very few moving parts and typically requires minimal maintenance compared to internal combustion gearboxes.

## Interior

### Interior Electronics & Sunroof

Owner reports indicate potential vulnerability with sunroof seals leading to moisture ingress near cabin electronics.

**Sunroof Water Leaks Affecting Electronics**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks through the sunroof assembly and can drip onto sensitive interior electronic components.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof drainage channels and seals; repairs or adjustments may be necessary to prevent water intrusion.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V569000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Separation of the rotor and shaft may cause a reduction in braking assistance and a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Electronic Stability Control (ESC), increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake booster, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V867000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery Thermal:Management:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A high-voltage battery that overheats increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install onboard diagnostic software to monitor battery module performance, and replace affected battery modules where necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi e-tron Quattro combines luxury EV performance with a generally calm reliability profile in early owner feedback. Buyers should prioritize a thorough inspection of the cabin electronics and sunroof seals to ensure no prior moisture damage exists before finalizing a purchase.
