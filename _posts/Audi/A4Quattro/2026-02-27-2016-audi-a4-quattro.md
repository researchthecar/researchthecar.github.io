# 2016 Audi A4 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi A4 Quattro is a refined and capable luxury compact sedan, offering standard all-wheel drive and a premium interior. For prospective used-car buyers, understanding the vehicle's mechanical layout and potential maintenance items helps ensure a satisfying ownership experience. While many examples run strong, checking service history and being aware of known trouble spots can save you from unexpected repair bills.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It offers a strong blend of performance and efficiency, though buyers should watch for oil consumption and timing chain wear in higher-mileage examples.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and prompt shifts paired with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust when fluid changes are maintained on schedule.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional 6-speed manual gearbox provides an engaging driving experience for enthusiasts. It requires a healthy clutch assembly for optimal performance.

The 2016 Audi A4 Quattro remains an appealing choice in the pre-owned luxury market due to its timeless styling, engaging driving dynamics, and confident all-wheel-drive traction.
