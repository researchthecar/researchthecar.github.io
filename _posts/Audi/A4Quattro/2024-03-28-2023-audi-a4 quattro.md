**Introduction**

The 2023 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience, advanced technology, and a comfortable cabin. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder and a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6. The 2.0-liter engine produces 201 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.0-liter engine generates 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

Owners appreciate the A4's sharp handling, responsive steering, and well-appointed interior. However, some have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and other components.

**Engine**

**2.0T**

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from the engine, especially at startup or idle; loss of power
Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
Recalls: N/A

- **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, stalling; illuminated Check Engine light
Solution: Remove intake manifold and clean valves
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: N/A

**3.0T**

- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic**

- **Clutch Pack Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping transmission, shuddering or grinding noises
Solution: Replace clutch pack
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

- **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noise from the suspension; uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: N/A

- **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of damping, bouncing or swaying of the vehicle
Solution: Replace shock absorbers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after short periods of non-use
Solution: Test and replace faulty electrical components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: N/A

- **Faulty Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) Sensor:**
Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate or intermittent tire pressure readings; illuminated TPMS warning light
Solution: Replace faulty sensor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

- **Windshield Wiper Linkage Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Wipers do not move smoothly or become stuck
Solution: Replace wiper linkage
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

- **Infotainment System Glitch:**
Description/Symptoms: Frozen or unresponsive touchscreen, Bluetooth connectivity issues
Solution: Reset or update infotainment system
Estimated Cost to Fix: Less than $100
Recalls: N/A

- **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging:**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin during heavy rain

**Summary**

The 2023 Audi A4 Quattro is generally considered a reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components. The severity of the issues varies, with some requiring costly repairs. However, it is important to note that not all vehicles experience these problems.
