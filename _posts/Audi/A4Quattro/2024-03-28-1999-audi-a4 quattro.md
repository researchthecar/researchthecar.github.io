**Introduction:**

The 1999 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact, luxury sedan powered by a 1.8L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 150 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or an optional 5-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. The A4 Quattro is lauded for its handling, performance, and luxurious interior but criticized for its firm ride and limited rear headroom.

**Engine**

* **1.8L Turbocharged Inline-4**
    * **Failed Ignition Coils:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires or stalls, poor performance
        * Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Coolant Thermostat Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating or running too cold, poor fuel economy
        * Solution: Replace faulty thermostat
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Manual Transmission:**
    * **Shift Linkage Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace worn shift linkage components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5-speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Torque Converter Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, lack of power
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Solenoid Malfunctions:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting erratically or not at all
        * Solution: Replace faulty solenoid(s)
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of electrical power, battery drain
    * Solution: Replace faulty alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Wiring Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights failing or flickering
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Panel Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not responding or malfunctioning
    * Solution: Replace faulty control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1999 Audi A4 Quattro has a reputation for reliability and performance, but it is not immune to certain issues. While some problems can be costly to fix, such as a torque converter failure, others are relatively minor and can be addressed at a reasonable cost. Overall, the A4 Quattro is a well-rounded vehicle that offers a comfortable and engaging driving experience.
