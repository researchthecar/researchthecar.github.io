# 2019 Audi A4 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Audi A4 Quattro, buyers generally find a refined and technologically advanced luxury sedan that balances everyday drivability with confident all-weather performance. While owner reports to safety and highway regulators are relatively sparse, evaluating specific mechanical components and potential electronic or safety system quirks will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (TFSI)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration paired with the standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Overall, this powertrain is generally robust, though minor accessory component wear like motor mount leaks can occasionally surface.

**Leaking Motor Mount**
- **Symptoms:** A routine dealer inspection may reveal fluid leaking from the passenger-side motor mount, which can accelerate deterioration if left unaddressed.
- **Solution:** Have the motor mounts inspected during routine service intervals and replace any failing units promptly to prevent excessive engine movement.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, smooth gear shifts and works seamlessly with the Quattro all-wheel-drive setup. It is a sophisticated unit that requires regular fluid maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Parking Sensors

Advanced safety and driver assistance features provide modern peace of mind, though occasional sensor calibration sensitivity or sudden automatic braking activations have been noted by some drivers.

**Aggressive Braking and Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the automatic emergency braking system engaged too abruptly, or error messages flashed indicating a parking aid malfunction.
- **Solution:** Check for any applicable software updates at an authorized service center and ensure sensor lenses are clean and free of debris.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi A4 Quattro is a compelling used luxury car choice, offering strong performance, a premium interior, and standard all-wheel drive. Prospective buyers should carefully verify vehicle service records, inspect engine mounts, and test all driver-assistance features during a pre-purchase inspection to minimize the risk of unexpected repair bills.
