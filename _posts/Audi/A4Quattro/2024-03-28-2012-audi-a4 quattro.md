**Introduction:**

The 2012 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan renowned for its sophisticated design, impeccable handling, and impressive performance. It features a 2.0L TFSI turbocharged inline-4 engine that generates 211 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission and Quattro all-wheel drive. Drivers appreciate its comfortable interior, advanced technology features, and sporty driving dynamics. However, like other vehicles, the A4 Quattro has encountered some issues that owners and mechanics have reported.

**Engine:**

**2.0L TFSI Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, stalling, check engine light
Solution: Intake valve cleaning, potentially replacing valves
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
Recalls: 19V-378

**Transmission:**

**8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**

* **Gear Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, shifting problems, check engine light
Solution: Transmission fluid replacement, solenoid repair, potential transmission rebuild
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
Recalls: 18V-819

**Suspension:**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Rearview Camera Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Camera screen goes blank, distorted images, no display
Solution: Camera replacement, wiring inspection
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Draining**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting, loss of power
Solution: Electrical system diagnosis, battery replacement, alternator inspection
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior:**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Water intrusion during rain or car wash
Solution: Sunroof seal replacement, drain tube cleaning
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Audi A4 Quattro has some reported issues, but the severity and cost of these issues vary. The most common problems involve carbon buildup on intake valves and transmission gear slippage. While electrical issues and sunroof leaks have also been reported, they appear to be less prevalent. It's important to note that these issues may not affect all vehicles and that regular maintenance and proper usage can help prevent or mitigate these problems.
