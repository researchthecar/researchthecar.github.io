# 2024 Audi A4 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi A4 Quattro is a refined luxury sport sedan offering strong performance, advanced technology, and confident all-weather handling. As a nearly new model year, the A4 Quattro generally benefits from modern engineering and minimal widespread owner complaints in early service. Prospective used buyers should still carefully inspect individual vehicles and review maintenance records to ensure reliable long-term ownership.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, representing the standard high-output powertrain for the Quattro lineup.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, smooth gear changes paired with Audi's legendary Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers a sporty driving dynamic while maintaining composure during daily commutes.

## Suspension

### Tires

Factory or replacement tires can experience unexpected tread or sidewall separation, though such incidents are rare owner-reported events.

**Rear Tire Sidewall Separation**
- **Symptoms:** A driver experienced an unexpected tire failure on the highway when the outside sidewall detached from the rest of the tire.
- **Solution:** Inspect tires regularly for wear or structural damage and replace compromised tires immediately.
