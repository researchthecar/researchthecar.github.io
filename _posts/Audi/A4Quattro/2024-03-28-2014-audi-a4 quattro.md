**2014 Audi A4 Quattro**

The 2014 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience with a premium interior. It is powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, producing 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The A4 Quattro is known for its comfortable ride, responsive handling, and advanced technology features. However, like any vehicle, it has had its share of issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking noise from the engine, especially on cold starts
    * Solution: Replacing timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating engine
    * Solution: Replacing water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replacing transmission fluid seals or lines
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration from low speeds
    * Solution: Replacing torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noises from the front suspension, poor handling
    * Solution: Replacing control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking shock absorber oil, reduced ride quality
    * Solution: Replacing rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replacing battery, checking for parasitic drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replacing headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per assembly
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Module Communication Faults**
    * Description/Symptoms: Warning lights, malfunctioning components
    * Solution: Software updates, module replacements
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots or corrosion on the trunk lid
    * Solution: Replacing trunk lid or rust repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Interior Trim Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or loose interior trim panels
    * Solution: Tightening screws, replacing trim clips
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seatbelt Pretensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seatbelt failing to retract properly or staying locked up
    * Solution: Replacing seatbelt pretensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 20V541

**Summary**

The 2014 Audi A4 Quattro has had a variety of issues reported by owners. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, such as interior trim rattles, others can be more severe and costly, such as engine timing chain tensioner failure or transmission fluid leaks. It is important to be aware of potential issues before purchasing this vehicle and to budget for possible repairs.
