**Introduction**

The 2009 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan known for its well-rounded performance, comfortable interior, and advanced technology. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 211 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption requiring frequent top-offs.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and engine seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Premature failure of the timing chain tensioner, leading to engine noise and potential catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:** Carbon accumulates on intake valves, reducing engine performance and fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Cleaning the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Jerking and Hesitation:** Transmission judders or hesitates during shifts, especially when accelerating.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Failure:** Deteriorated control arm bushings cause excessive suspension noise and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure:** Premature failure of the xenon headlight ballast, causing intermittent or no headlight illumination.
    * Solution: Replace headlight ballast.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**Exterior**

* **Condensation in Tail Lights:** Moisture accumulation in the tail lights due to faulty seals.
    * Solution: Replace tail light seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Switch Failure:** The sunroof switch may fail, preventing the sunroof from opening or closing.
    * Solution: Replace sunroof switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary**

The 2009 Audi A4 Quattro is generally reliable, but it is not without its issues. Engine oil consumption, timing chain tensioner failure, CVT problems, and suspension component failures are some of the more severe issues reported. The cost of repairs can be significant, but many issues can be addressed through regular maintenance or proactive replacements.
