# 2021 Audi A4 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Audi A4 Quattro, buyers will find a refined luxury sedan known for its sophisticated styling, agile handling, and standard all-wheel-drive capability. While owners generally praise its driving dynamics and interior comfort, a small number of driver-submitted complaints highlight potential issues ranging from electrical glitches to powertrain concerns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 201 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, featuring mild-hybrid technology. It offers a smooth power delivery and balanced fuel efficiency for daily driving.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

The higher-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates roughly 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. It provides brisk acceleration while maintaining solid long-term mechanical reliability when properly maintained.

**Powertrain Tremors and Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle begins to tremble, transmission and floor shake, RPMs spike higher than normal, and strange noises or jerking occur during initial acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealer inspect the transmission control module, engine mounts, and drivetrain components for wear or software updates.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with Quattro all-wheel drive, offering rapid gear shifts and responsive performance. Routine fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term durability and smooth operation.

## Suspension

### Factory Tires and Wheels

Suspension components are generally robust, though owners should carefully inspect original equipment tires for signs of uneven wear or structural degradation.

**Premature Tire Belt Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual hopping or vibration while driving, often attributed by owners to shifted internal tire belts even at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire tread and internal structure regularly, and replace affected tires promptly to restore ride smoothness.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Infotainment

Electrical complaints primarily center on the digital cockpit and instrument displays experiencing intermittent stability issues.

**Digital Dashboard Display Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The main instrument cluster screen crashes, goes blank, displays a red triangle, or fails to boot up properly, leaving the driver without a speedometer or fuel gauge.
- **Solution:** Visit an authorized dealer to check for software patches, module resets, or necessary hardware replacements.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi A4 Quattro is a compelling luxury sedan option for used-car buyers who value performance and modern technology. Prospective owners should thoroughly check the digital instrument cluster and test drive the vehicle to ensure smooth acceleration and shifting. A pre-purchase inspection by an independent Audi specialist is recommended to verify overall vehicle health.
