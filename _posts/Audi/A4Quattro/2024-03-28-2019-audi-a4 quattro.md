**Introduction**

The 2019 Audi A4 Quattro is the latest iteration of Audi's popular luxury sedan. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* 2.0-liter TFSI® 4-cylinder engine with 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a seven-speed S tronic® dual-clutch transmission
* 3.0-liter TFSI® V6 engine with 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed Tiptronic® automatic transmission

The Audi A4 Quattro is known for its sophisticated design, comfortable interior, and impressive performance. However, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter TFSI® 4-cylinder engine**
* **Intake manifold issues**
  * Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel economy
  * Solution: Replace intake manifold
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Water pump failure**
  * Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating
  * Solution: Replace water pump
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter TFSI® V6 engine**
* **Oil leaks**
  * Symptoms: Oil pooling under the vehicle, low oil levels
  * Solution: Replace oil pan gasket or other leaking components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger failure**
  * Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust
  * Solution: Replace turbocharger
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-speed S tronic® dual-clutch transmission**
* **Clutch slippage**
  * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed acceleration
  * Solution: Replace clutch pack
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic unit failure**
  * Symptoms: Transmission error messages, limp mode operation
  * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Eight-speed Tiptronic® automatic transmission**
* **Shifting problems**
  * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, hard shifts
  * Solution: Replace or repair transmission components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount bearing failure**
  * Symptoms: Noises from suspension, reduced ride quality
  * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Control arm bushing wear**
  * Symptoms: Alignment issues, premature tire wear
  * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
  * Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, dimming headlights
  * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment system issues**
  * Symptoms: Freezing, crashing, or unresponsive infotainment system
  * Solution: Update software or replace hardware
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Dented or scratched body panels**
  * Symptoms: Visible damage to body panels
  * Solution: Repair or replace body panels
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of damage
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Loose or damaged weatherstripping**
  * Symptoms: Wind noise, water leaks
  * Solution: Replace weatherstripping
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof rattles**
  * Symptoms: Noises from sunroof while driving
  * Solution: Adjust or lubricate sunroof
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **NVH (noise, vibration, harshness) issues**
  * Symptoms: Excessive road noise, vibrations, or harsh ride quality
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair noise or vibration source
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the issue
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Audi A4 Quattro is a well-received luxury sedan with a variety of engine and transmission options. However, it is not immune to issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the affected parts. It is important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and some may be more common with certain engine or transmission configurations.
