# 2020 Audi A4 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi A4 Quattro, buyers will find a refined and technologically advanced luxury sedan. However, owner reports highlight a few recurring electrical and cabin issues that potential buyers should inspect closely before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

Equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 261 horsepower, paired with a 12-volt mild-hybrid system. This powertrain delivers brisk acceleration and strong fuel efficiency, and it generally has a solid reputation for mechanical durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Features a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission mated to Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid, smooth gear changes, though prospective owners should test drive to ensure seamless engagement across all gears.

## Electrical

### MMI Infotainment System

The vehicle relies heavily on touchscreens and digital displays for multimedia and vehicle controls, which can occasionally experience software instability.

**Infotainment System Spontaneous Rebooting**
- **Symptoms:** The MMI center display screen and dashboard screens randomly go black and reboot every few minutes while driving, temporarily disabling navigation, audio, and the backup camera.
- **Solution:** Often requires a software update performed by a dealership or a module reset, though some owners report persistent behavior requiring component replacement.

### Backup Camera and Driver Assistance

Active safety features and camera systems generally perform well, but are occasionally impacted by underlying MMI software glitches.

**Backup Camera Loss During Reboot**
- **Symptoms:** When the infotainment system reboots spontaneously while reversing, the backup camera and parking assistance displays suddenly disappear, creating a safety hazard.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's infotainment software checked for the latest manufacturer patches.

## Interior

### Driver Seat Mechanism

The cabin is crafted from premium materials, but physical adjustment hardware should be checked during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Seat Track Bracket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A metal bracket on the seat corner can snap unexpectedly while driving, causing the seat to detach from its track on one side.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat frame and tracks for stability; broken components require seat frame or track replacement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A4 Quattro is a sophisticated luxury sedan offering great performance and all-weather capability. Buyers should thoroughly test the infotainment system and backup camera functionality during a test drive to ensure the MMI unit does not suffer from recurring reboots.
