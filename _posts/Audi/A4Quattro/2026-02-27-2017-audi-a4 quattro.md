## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive, or reboot unexpectedly. Navigation system may lose signal.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $150 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may drain prematurely, leading to starting issues. Can be related to parasitic draw from modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi A4 Quattro is a generally reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. The Quattro all-wheel-drive system is robust. The most commonly reported technical concerns tend to be related to the electrical system, specifically intermittent infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronics. These issues are typically addressable through software updates or component diagnosis. Overall, the A4 offers a dependable luxury sedan experience with a good track record for its class, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
