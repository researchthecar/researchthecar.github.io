# 2022 Audi A4 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A4 Quattro balances luxury, sharp handling, and a refined interior with standard all-wheel drive. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern technology and updated styling, making it an appealing choice for used-car buyers seeking a premium compact sedan. While early owner feedback points to a generally solid ownership experience, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and verify that all software updates and routine services have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It pairs standard Quattro all-wheel drive with balanced power delivery and generally dependable everyday performance.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

Serving as the entry-level powerplant, this 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder generates around 201 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. It prioritizes fuel efficiency while maintaining smooth power delivery suitable for daily commuting.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and works seamlessly with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Proper fluid maintenance is key to preserving its smooth operation over high mileage.

## Recall Check

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V368000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
