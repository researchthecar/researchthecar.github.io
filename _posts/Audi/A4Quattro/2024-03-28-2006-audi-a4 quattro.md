**Introduction:**

The 2006 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan known for its performance, handling, and refinement. It is powered by a variety of engines, including a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four, a 3.2L V6, and a 4.2L V8. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, a 6-speed automatic, and a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **High-pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or loss of power under acceleration, hard starting, fuel leaks
    * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced engine power, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioners
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.2L V6**

* **Intake Valve Deposits**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, misfiring, rough idling
    * Solution: Clean the intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Valve Body Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, slipping gears, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting problems, illuminated check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **Belt Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power during acceleration, shuddering
    * Solution: Replace the CVT belt
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises over bumps, reduced ride quality
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing or creaking noises over bumps, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace the lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies after being parked for an extended period
    * Solution: Replace the battery drain source (e.g., alternator, modules)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Regulators**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow or inoperative power windows
    * Solution: Replace the power window regulators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Center Console Buttons**
    * Description/Symptoms: Stickiness or failure of center console buttons
    * Solution: Replace the center console buttons
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2006 Audi A4 Quattro is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems are related to the engine, transmission, and electrical system. While some of these issues can be costly to fix, many of them are relatively minor and inexpensive. Overall, the A4 Quattro is a solid choice for a luxury sedan, but buyers should be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase.
