**Introduction**

The 2004 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan praised for its handling, performance, and interior quality. It features quattro all-wheel drive and is available with a 1.8L turbocharged inline-4 or 3.0L V6 engine, paired with either a 5-speed manual or 5-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**1.8L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption may occur due to piston ring issues. Symptoms include decreased engine oil levels and blue smoke from the exhaust.
Solution: Piston ring replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6**

* **Timing Belt Tensioner Failure:** The timing belt tensioner can fail, causing the timing belt to skip or break. Symptoms include engine noise, rough idling, and loss of power.
Solution: Timing belt and tensioner replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* No Issues Found

**5-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Lockup Issue:** The torque converter may fail to lock up properly, resulting in poor fuel economy and acceleration. Symptoms include shuddering or slipping during gear changes.
Solution: Torque converter replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Failure:** The front control arm bushings can wear out prematurely, leading to excessive play and poor handling. Symptoms include rattling or clunking noises and uneven tire wear.
Solution: Control arm bushing replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail due to overheating or electrical issues. Symptoms include flickering lights, difficulty starting the engine, and a battery warning light.
Solution: Alternator replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Ignition Coil Failure:** Ignition coils can fail, causing misfires and rough engine running. Symptoms include a check engine light, engine vibration, and reduced power.
Solution: Ignition coil replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per coil
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **HID Headlight Ballast Failure:** The HID headlight ballasts can fail, causing the headlights to flicker or not work correctly. Symptoms include dim or flickering headlights.
Solution: Ballast replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700 per ballast
Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging:** The sunroof drain plugs can become clogged, allowing water to accumulate in the sunroof cassette and leak into the interior. Symptoms include water dripping inside the car and a musty odor.
Solution: Cleaning or replacing drain plugs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction:** The navigation system may become unresponsive or display incorrect information. Symptoms include a blank screen or frozen display.
Solution: Software update or navigation unit replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Switch Failure:** The power window switches can fail, preventing the windows from being operated. Symptoms include windows not functioning or only working intermittently.
Solution: Window switch replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250 per switch
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 Audi A4 Quattro is generally a reliable vehicle, but like any car, it has potential issues. Some common problems include oil consumption in the 1.8L engine, timing belt tensioner failure in the 3.0L engine, and torque converter lockup issues in the 5-speed automatic transmission. Suspension components such as the control arm bushings may fail, leading to poor handling. Electrical issues include alternator failure, ignition coil failure, and HID headlight ballast failure. Interior issues include navigation system malfunction and power window switch failure. Exterior issues include clogged sunroof drain plugs and potential HID headlight ballast failure. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific part and labor costs.
