**Introduction**

The 2016 Audi A4 Quattro is a compact luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience and an array of advanced features. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0L inline-four engine and comes standard with a six-speed manual transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a turbocharged 1.8L inline-four engine with a six-speed automatic transmission, as well as a supercharged 3.0L V6 engine with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

Despite its overall reliability, the 2016 Audi A4 Quattro has experienced various reported issues across different components. These issues range in severity and cost to repair.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Carbon Buildup**
  * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, rough idling
  * Solution: Intake valve cleaning
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **1.8L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, decreased engine performance, oil leaks
  * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **3.0L Supercharged V6**

* **Water Pump Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks, reduced engine performance
  * Solution: Replacement of water pump
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, clutch pedal spongy or not engaging
  * Solution: Replacement of clutch master cylinder
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage**
  * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or slipping during gear changes, loss of power
  * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, replacement of transmission solenoid
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Gear shifting issues, loss of power, transmission warning lights
  * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
  * Description/Symptoms: Noises from the front suspension, decreased handling and stability
  * Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
  * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting the vehicle
  * Solution: Replacement of battery, check for current draw
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Connector Corrosion**
  * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, warning lights illuminated
  * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of electrical connectors
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
  * Description/Symptoms: Fogging or moisture inside the headlight assemblies
  * Solution: Replacement of headlights
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or wind noise from the panoramic sunroof
  * Solution: Adjustment of sunroof alignment
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Audi A4 Quattro offers an upscale driving experience but has faced a number of issues across various components. While most of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, some, such as transmission and engine failures, can be costly and severe. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
