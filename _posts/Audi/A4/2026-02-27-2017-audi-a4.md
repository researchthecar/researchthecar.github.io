# 2017 Audi A4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi A4 delivers refined driving dynamics, a luxurious interior, and advanced technology that make it an appealing choice in the used compact luxury sedan market. However, prospective buyers should review common owner-reported complaints regarding cooling system leaks, electrical glitches, and transmission behavior to ensure a well-maintained example is selected.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration and balanced efficiency. While generally robust, buyers should watch closely for cooling system leaks and ignition-related quirks.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant warning lights and visible fluid pooling, often originating around the thermostat or water pump housing.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and thermostat housing assembly for cracks or gasket failures, and replace faulty cooling components.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling or rough running conditions, sometimes accompanied by spark plug or ignition misfire issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnose ignition coils and spark plugs, and check for software updates related to the start-stop system.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes paired with front-wheel drive or quattro all-wheel drive. Routine fluid maintenance is critical to ensuring long-term operational smoothness.

**Gear Shifting and Drive Mode Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears while driving or unexpected shifts into sport mode during inclement weather conditions.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition, scan for diagnostic trouble codes, and perform software recalibration or mechatronic unit inspection if necessary.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Body Electronics

**Headlight Housing Stress Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Development of stress cracks in the headlamp lenses, which can obscure light projection and reduce night visibility.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected headlight housing assemblies.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Seat Belt Sensors

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning lights or erratic passenger seat occupancy sensor behavior triggering unexpected warning messages.
- **Solution:** Inspect passenger detection sensor wiring harnesses and replace faulty seat sensor modules as needed.

**Seat Belt Chime and Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Continuous seat belt warning lights and chimes activating even when the passenger seat is unoccupied.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt buckle switch and recalibrate or replace the sensor assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi A4 is a rewarding and sophisticated used luxury sedan, but thorough pre-purchase inspections are essential. Pay close attention to cooling system components, electronics functionality, and service records to avoid unexpected repair bills.
