# 2017 Audi A4

### Overview
The 2017 Audi A4 is a premium compact executive car known for its sophisticated design, advanced technology, and engaging driving dynamics. It offers a comfortable and refined experience, with a focus on luxury and performance. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and reliability, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective and current owners of the 2017 A4, across its various powertrain configurations.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TFSI (190 hp)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level checks required, potential blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Engine internal inspection, potential piston ring replacement or PCV valve update.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic (DL382)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission warning lights, loss of drive.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**

### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, car won't start.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of faulty modules or accessories causing drain, battery replacement if necessary.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi A4 presents a generally reliable package, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. The most frequently cited technical concerns revolve around the 2.0L TFSI engine's potential for oil consumption and issues with the S tronic dual-clutch transmission, specifically the mechatronic unit and clutch packs. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and parasitic battery drain, are also noted. However, these issues are not universal and often depend on maintenance history and specific vehicle usage. For owners prioritizing a refined driving experience, the A4 delivers, but awareness of these potential repair areas is advisable.
