# 2017 Audi A4

The 2017 Audi A4 is a compact luxury sedan that offers a refined driving experience and a well-appointed interior. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four, a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged diesel inline-four. Transmission options include a six-speed manual, an eight-speed automatic, and a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic.

The A4 has received praise for its comfortable ride, responsive handling, and high-quality interior. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of rear-seat legroom and its relatively high price.

## Engine

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000-$3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner fails, causing the timing chain to skip or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500-$2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**2.0L Turbocharged Diesel Inline-Four**

* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The DPF becomes clogged, restricting exhaust flow and causing reduced engine power.
    * Solution: Replace the DPF.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500-$2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: The torque converter shudders during gear changes, causing a noticeable vibration.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000-$1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Clutch Pack Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch packs wear out prematurely, causing slipping and shuddering during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch packs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000-$3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front control arm bushings wear out, causing clunking noises and reduced handling precision.
    * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$700+
    * Recalls: One Recall (NHTSA ID: 20V681000)

## Electrical

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the car is parked.
    * Solution: Diagnose the source of the drain and repair or replace the affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain
    * Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

* **Sunroof Drain Tube Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof drain tubes become clogged, causing water to leak into the cabin.
    * Solution: Unclog the drain tubes or replace them if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Interior

* **Central Infotainment Screen Glitching**
    * Description/Symptoms: The central infotainment screen experiences glitches, freezes, or becomes unresponsive.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the screen if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2017 Audi A4 is a well-rounded compact luxury sedan with a number of desirable features. However, it is not without its issues. Some of the most common problems include excessive oil consumption, timing chain tensioner failure, and torque converter shudder. The severity of these issues can vary, but they can all be costly to repair. Overall, the A4 is a solid choice for those who value quality and performance, but it's important to be aware of its potential issues before making a purchase.
