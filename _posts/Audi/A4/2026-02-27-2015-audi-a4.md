# 2015 Audi A4

### Overview
The 2015 Audi A4, a popular compact executive car, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on premium features and driving dynamics. While it's a well-engineered vehicle, like any car, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. Understanding these potential weak points can help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and repairs, ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of their A4.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TFSI (CCTA/CBFA)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, check engine light, oil consumption, whistling noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Low oil level between changes, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Can be due to worn piston rings or PCV system issues. Requires diagnosis and potential engine internal repairs or PCV replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500+

**Timing Chain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, check engine light, rough running.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, guides, and related seals.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Audi A4 is a generally reliable vehicle, particularly with its automatic transmissions and standard suspension setups. The most common concerns tend to revolve around the 2.0L TFSI engine, specifically issues with the PCV valve, oil consumption, and timing chain wear, which can be costly to repair. The S tronic dual-clutch transmission has also seen reports of mechatronic unit failures. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and tail light issues, are also noted but are typically less severe. Despite these potential drawbacks, many owners report satisfaction with the A4's performance and comfort, provided these specific components are well-maintained or addressed promptly.
