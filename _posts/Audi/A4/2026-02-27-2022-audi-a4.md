# 2022 Audi A4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A4 blends luxury, advanced technology, and confident performance into a polished compact executive sedan. Buyers shopping for a used model will find a refined driving experience, though evaluating powertrain configurations and maintaining standard service intervals remains key to long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 40 TFSI 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 40 TFSI features a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generating approximately 201 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. It delivers smooth, efficient power for daily driving while maintaining strong reliability under proper maintenance.

### 45 TFSI 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 45 TFSI utilizes a higher-output tune of the 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine, producing roughly 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. This option pairs strong acceleration with solid engineering, making it a favorite for performance-minded buyers.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear changes across all trim levels and works in tandem with quattro all-wheel drive. Regular fluid changes are vital to preserving its responsiveness and long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V368000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. ## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi A4 stands out as a sophisticated and capable choice in the used luxury sedan market. Prospective buyers should inspect service records carefully, confirm proper operation of powertrain options, and prioritize well-maintained examples for peace of mind.
