# 2019 Audi A4

### Overview
The 2019 Audi A4 is a well-regarded compact luxury sedan known for its refined interior, engaging driving dynamics, and advanced technology. Generally, it presents a strong reliability profile, with owners reporting satisfaction with its performance and build quality. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the A4 tends to avoid widespread, costly problems. Common areas of attention, if they arise, are typically related to specific electronic components or, less frequently, powertrain elements. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its longevity and optimal performance, as with any premium vehicle. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related concerns.

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, low oil level warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic (Dual-Clutch Automatic)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough shifting, delayed engagement, or jerky movements during acceleration or deceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or potential internal component repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Audi A4 demonstrates good overall reliability, aligning with expectations for a premium German sedan. The powertrain, including both engine and transmission options, is generally robust, though some owners have reported isolated instances of oil leaks on the more powerful 2.0L engine variant and occasional transmission hesitation with the S tronic dual-clutch. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and potential battery drain, are the most commonly cited non-wear-and-tear issues. Suspension and interior/exterior components are largely free of widespread problems. With diligent maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging electrical concerns, the 2019 A4 should provide a dependable luxury driving experience.
