# 2019 Audi A4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi A4 balances sharp handling, premium interior craftsmanship, and a refined ride, making it a compelling choice in the used luxury compact sedan market. While owner-reported complaints are relatively few, prospective buyers should review available maintenance records carefully and inspect potential problem areas like active safety sensors and engine mounts during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and everyday drivability. It is a generally robust powerplant, though routine inspections of engine mounts and ancillary systems are recommended.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

This lower-output tune of the 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder delivers around 188 horsepower, prioritizing fuel efficiency while maintaining smooth power delivery. It pairs well with front-wheel or Quattro all-wheel-drive configurations for daily commuting.

### Engine Mounts

Supports and dampens engine vibrations to maintain cabin refinement.

**Leaking Motor Mount**
- **Symptoms:** Owners or technicians may notice fluid leaking from the engine mount during routine inspections, which can lead to rapid deterioration if left unaddressed.
- **Solution:** Have the compromised engine mount replaced by a qualified technician to prevent excessive engine movement and vibration.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and power delivery across Quattro all-wheel-drive models. Proper fluid service intervals are key to maintaining its longevity and smooth shift quality.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features designed to detect obstacles and help prevent frontal collisions.

**Abrupt Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The Audi Pre Sense system may occasionally trigger overly sensitive or unexpected emergency braking events while driving.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized dealer or specialist to check for software updates or sensor recalibrations if false activations occur.

**Parking Aid Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may receive dashboard error messages indicating that the front parking aid or obstacle detection systems are malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Have the parking sensors and associated control modules inspected and serviced by a qualified repair facility.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi A4 offers an upscale driving experience and solid overall performance, but used car shoppers should remain vigilant. Ensure that advanced safety systems function correctly and that a thorough mechanical inspection is performed to catch potential issues like worn engine mounts before finalizing your purchase.
