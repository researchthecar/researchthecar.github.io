## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** The MMI infotainment system may experience occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or minor glitches.
- **Solution:** Software updates or a system reset are typically effective. In rare cases, component replacement may be needed.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported intermittent battery drain issues, leading to a dead battery.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the parasitic draw is required. This could involve faulty modules, aftermarket accessories, or the battery itself.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Audi A4 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain and fundamental mechanical systems. The 2.0L TFSI engines and the S tronic transmission have proven to be robust with no widespread issues reported. Suspension components also appear to hold up well under normal driving conditions. The primary areas of concern, though not pervasive, lie within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain. These issues are generally addressable through software updates or targeted diagnostics. Overall, the 2018 A4 is a dependable choice, with potential buyers advised to be aware of the minor electrical quirks that may arise.
