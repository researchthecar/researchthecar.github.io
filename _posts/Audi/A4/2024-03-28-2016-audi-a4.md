**2016 Audi A4 Introduction**

The 2016 Audi A4 is a luxury sport sedan that offers a blend of performance and sophistication. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The A4 is praised for its responsive handling, comfortable interior, and advanced technology. However, owners have reported various issues that affect different components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased power output.
Solution: Intake manifold removal and cleaning.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Clutch Slippage:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power during acceleration, slipping gears.
Solution: Clutch replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when turning or driving over rough roads.
Solution: Control arm replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Control Module (ECM) Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, warning lights on dashboard.
Solution: ECM replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead prematurely, difficulty starting.
Solution: Battery replacement or repair of electrical draw.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Clogs:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking sunroof, water stains on headliner.
Solution: Cleaning or replacement of sunroof drains.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: None Found

* **Exterior Trim Pieces Falling Off:**
Description/Symptoms: Chrome trim around windows or bumpers becomes loose.
Solution: Replacement of trim pieces.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
Description/Symptoms: Navigation issues, touchscreen unresponsive, audio quality issues.
Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Audi A4 has received mixed reviews from owners. While it is praised for its performance, handling, and comfort, it has been plagued by various issues that can be costly to repair. The most severe issues include clutch slippage, ECM failure, and electrical drain. However, many of the issues reported are relatively minor and can be resolved for a reasonable cost.
