**Introduction:**

The 2007 Audi A4 is a compact executive car available in sedan, convertible, and Avant (wagon) body styles. It offers a range of engine options, including a turbocharged 2.0T inline-four and a 3.2L V6. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, a 6-speed Tiptronic automatic, and a Multitronic CVT. The A4 is known for its luxurious interior, impressive performance, and advanced technology. However, like any vehicle, it has some reported issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**2.0T Inline-Four**

- **Intake Manifold Flaps Failure:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The plastic intake manifold flaps can break and fall into the engine, causing reduced performance, increased fuel consumption, and a potential engine seizure.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
-- **Recalls:** None Found

- **Oil Filter Housing Leak:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The oil filter housing may develop a leak, causing oil to leak out and potentially lead to engine damage.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil filter housing and gasket.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800

**3.2L V6**

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to slip and potentially leading to engine damage.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and associated components.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
-- **Recalls:** None Found

- **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The valve cover gasket may leak, causing oil to leak out.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the valve cover gasket.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $700

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

- **No Issues Found**

**6-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**

- **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The mechatronic unit, which controls the transmission's hydraulics and electronics, can fail, causing problems with shifting and potentially leaving the vehicle inoperable.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the mechatronic unit.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $3,500+
-- **Recalls:** 07V119000, 06V303000

**Multitronic CVT**

- **Transmission Slippage:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission may slip or hesitate while driving, causing loss of power and acceleration.
-- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the CVT transmission.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $4,000+
-- **Recalls:** 07V163000, 06V350000

**Suspension**

- **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The front control arm bushings may wear out, causing clunking noises, poor handling, and uneven tire wear.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the control arm bushings.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800

- **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The rear suspension may sag over time, leading to decreased ride height and handling issues.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the rear shocks or springs.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500

**Electrical**

- **Ignition Coil Failure:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** One or more ignition coils may fail, causing misfires, rough idling, and reduced engine performance.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty coils.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300 per coil

- **Battery Drain:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
-- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of the electrical system to identify the source of the drain.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

- **Fog Light Housing Condensation:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The fog light housings may develop condensation inside them, which can lead to reduced visibility and burned-out bulbs.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the fog light housings.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400

- **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The door lock actuators may fail, preventing the doors from locking or unlocking properly.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty actuators.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800

**Interior**

- **Heater Core Leak:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The heater core may develop a leak, causing coolant to leak into the passenger compartment and create a sweet smell.
-- **Solution:** Replacement of the heater core.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500

- **Carpet Staining:**
-- **Description/Symptoms:** The carpeting may develop stains or discoloration over time, especially in areas exposed to moisture.
-- **Solution:** Professional carpet cleaning or replacement.
-- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600

**Summary:**

The 2007 Audi A4 is a well-regarded vehicle with impressive performance and luxurious appointments. However, it does have some known issues that owners should be aware of. The most severe and potentially expensive issues include engine timing chain tensioner failure, transmission mechatronic unit failure, and heater core leaks. Regular maintenance and inspections can help mitigate these issues and extend the vehicle's lifespan.
