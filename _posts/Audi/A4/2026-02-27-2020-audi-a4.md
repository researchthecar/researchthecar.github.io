# 2020 Audi A4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi A4, buyers will find a refined and technologically advanced luxury compact sedan that balances performance with everyday comfort. While mechanical powertrains are generally robust, prospective owners should pay close attention to onboard electronics and minor cabin hardware. Reviewing maintenance records and testing all electronic features during a pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. It is generally reliable when maintained with regular synthetic oil changes.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

Offering a more efficiency-focused tune, this 2.0-liter turbo-four puts out roughly 201 horsepower and provides smooth, responsive power for daily commuting. The platform has a solid mechanical track record with proper scheduled servicing.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear shifts across all driving conditions. It requires timely fluid changes to maintain optimal clutch engagement and long-term durability.

## Electrical

### MMI Infotainment and Display System

The cabin technology suite relies heavily on touchscreen and digital interfaces, which can occasionally experience software-driven glitches or reboots.

**Spontaneous MMI Screen Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The central MMI infotainment screen and digital dash displays periodically shut down, go black, and reboot while driving, resulting in temporary loss of navigation, radio, and backup camera functionality.
- **Solution:** Often resolved by applying factory software updates at a dealership, though persistent module failures may require hardware replacement.

## Interior

### Driver Seat Frame and Tracks

Interior appointments are upscale and durable, though isolated mechanical failures in seat adjustment hardware can occur.

**Driver Seat Bracket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A metal support bracket on the seat frame can snap unexpectedly during normal driving, causing the corner of the seat to detach from its sliding track.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat assembly for structural cracks or looseness; damaged frames require professional repair or seat frame replacement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A4 remains a compelling choice in the pre-owned luxury market, offering engaging driving dynamics and a high-end interior. Buyers should thoroughly test the infotainment system and check for any history of software resets or seat hardware concerns during their test drive. With a clean inspection and verified service history, this sedan serves as a comfortable and sophisticated daily driver.
