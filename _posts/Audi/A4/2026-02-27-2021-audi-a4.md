# 2021 Audi A4

### Overview
The 2021 Audi A4 is a well-regarded compact luxury sedan, known for its refined interior, engaging driving dynamics, and advanced technology. Reliability for the 2021 model year is generally strong, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality engineering. Most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected issues. Common areas of concern, while not widespread, tend to be minor and often related to specific electronic features or the complex nature of modern turbocharged engines. However, for the most part, the A4 delivers a dependable and premium experience, with proactive maintenance being the key to long-term satisfaction. The available powertrains offer a good balance of performance and efficiency.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or minor display errors within the MMI infotainment system.
- **Solution:** Software updates are typically available from Audi dealerships to resolve these issues. A system reset may also help.
- **Cost:** $0 - $200 (if out of warranty or for advanced diagnostics)


### Driver Assistance Systems
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent warnings or deactivation of advanced driver-assistance features (e.g., adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist) due to sensor obstruction or minor calibration issues.
- **Solution:** Ensure sensors are clean. Dealer recalibration or sensor cleaning may be required.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi A4 demonstrates commendable reliability, with most owners experiencing a trouble-free ownership period. The powertrain and core mechanical components are robust. The few reported issues are primarily concentrated in the sophisticated electronic systems, such as the infotainment interface and driver assistance sensors, often manifesting as minor software glitches or requiring sensor recalibration. These are generally addressable through dealer service and software updates. With regular maintenance and attention to potential electronic quirks, the 2021 A4 is a dependable choice in the luxury sedan segment.
