**Introduction:**

The 2022 Audi e-tron GT is a fully electric luxury sedan with a sleek and sporty design. It offers impressive performance with its dual electric motors producing a combined 469 horsepower and 490 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle boasts a stylish interior, advanced technology features, and a comfortable ride. However, like many vehicles, the e-tron GT is not without its share of issues.

**Electrical:**

**Battery Drain Issue**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain when the vehicle is parked.
Solution: Software update or battery replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Glitches, freezes, or blackouts of the infotainment screen.
Solution: Software update or hardware replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Climate Control Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control, inability to adjust temperature or airflow.
Solution: Software update or climate control module replacement.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

**Door Handle Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Door handles fail to open or close properly.
Solution: Replacement of door handle assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

**Creaking or Rattling Sounds**
Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard, center console, or other interior components.
Solution: Identification and tightening or replacement of loose parts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Heated Seat Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Heated seats malfunction or become inoperable.
Solution: Replacement of heated seat element or wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Audi e-tron GT is a desirable electric sedan, but it is not without its issues. Electrical problems, particularly battery drain and infotainment system glitches, seem to be the most common concerns. Exterior door handle malfunctions and interior noises can also be frustrating. The severity of these issues varies, and some may be covered under warranty. However, the cost of repairs can range from minor to significant, especially for major electrical or component replacements.
