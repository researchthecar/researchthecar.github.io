# 2023 Audi e-tron GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi e-tron GT is a high-performance luxury electric vehicle sharing its core architecture with the Porsche Taycan. It delivers blistering acceleration, sharp handling, and a sophisticated cabin, making it an appealing choice for buyers looking to step into the electric grand touring segment. While initial owner feedback and safety reporting point to a robust overall build, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history carefully.

---

## Engine

### Dual Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors (469 hp / 522 hp with overboost)

The standard e-tron GT utilizes dual electric motors powering all four wheels with smooth, instantaneous torque delivery. This powertrain offers exceptional performance and refinement suited for daily driving or long-distance touring.

### RS Dual Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors (590 hp / 637 hp with overboost)

The high-performance RS e-tron GT variant ups the ante with more aggressive dual-motor output and track-ready calibration. It provides exhilarating acceleration while maintaining everyday electric vehicle drivability.

## Transmission

### 2-Speed Automatic Transmission

A unique two-speed automatic transmission manages power delivery, using a short first gear for launch acceleration and a long second gear for efficiency at highway speeds. The system operates seamlessly during normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V229000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V228000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will, analyze the battery data and replace the high-voltage battery modules as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V465000: Service Brakes, Air:Supply:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A failed brake circuit can cause longer brake pedal travel and reduced braking performance, extending the distance required to stop and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front axle brake hoses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V726000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software as the final remedy free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V227000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat cushion, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi e-tron GT brings supercar performance and luxury cruiser comfort together in an advanced electric package. Because early models rely heavily on complex electrical systems and high-voltage architectures, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an EV-certified technician is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
