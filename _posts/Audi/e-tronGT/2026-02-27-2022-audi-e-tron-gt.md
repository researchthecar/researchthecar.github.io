# 2022 Audi e-tron GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi e-tron GT is a high-performance electric luxury sedan that shares its underlying architecture with the Porsche Taycan. As an early model year for this platform, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and ensure that manufacturer service campaigns have been properly addressed.

---

## Engine

### Dual Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors (Standard e-tron GT)

The standard e-tron GT features dual permanent magnet synchronous motors producing 469 horsepower (up to 522 hp with overboost) and 464 lb-ft of torque. These electric motors deliver rapid acceleration and all-wheel-drive capability with minimal mechanical maintenance requirements.

### Dual Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors (RS e-tron GT)

The RS e-tron GT features upgraded dual permanent magnet synchronous motors generating 590 horsepower (up to 637 hp with overboost) and 612 lb-ft of torque. This high-output powertrain offers exceptional straight-line performance while maintaining standard EV reliability characteristics.

## Transmission

### 2-Speed Automatic Transmission

Both e-tron GT variants utilize a unique two-speed automatic transmission on the rear axle, designed to optimize efficiency at cruising speeds and launch performance off the line. This specialized EV gearbox generally operates smoothly, though buyers should verify proper shifting operation during a test drive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V935000: Suspension:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loose retaining ring may allow air to escape, compromising vehicle handling and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the air suspension struts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V084000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." An improperly secured child seat increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reroute and secure the center rear seat belt buckle wiring harness and, as necessary, replace a damaged buckle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V085000: Equipment:Other:Owners/Service/Other Manual](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect tire pressure information could result in improperly inflated tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will insert a corrected tire pressure table into the owner's manual, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V927000: Suspension:Front:Macpherson Strut](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An air suspension strut failure may cause a loss of vehicle handling and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the suspension strut and replace it, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V229000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi e-tron GT offers thrilling performance and striking design, but as an early production luxury electric vehicle, thorough pre-purchase inspections are strongly advised. Buyers should verify that all software updates and safety recalls have been completed by an authorized dealer.
