**Introduction:**

The 2023 Audi TTS Quattro is a high-performance sports coupe powered by a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a quattro all-wheel-drive system. This model delivers 302 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It is known for its agile handling, premium interior, and stylish design. However, like any vehicle, it has faced some issues reported by owners.

**Engine:**

**2.0L TFSI Inline-4 Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** Carbon deposits can accumulate on the intake valves, causing reduced power, increased fuel consumption, and rough idling.
  * Solution: Valve cleaning or replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V795000

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, resulting in excessive timing chain noise and potential engine damage.
  * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**7-speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Jerky or Rough Shifting:** Some owners have experienced jerky or rough shifting, particularly during low-speed maneuvers.
  * Solution: Software updates or transmission valve body replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Squeaky Control Arms:** The control arms can develop a squeak when worn or damaged.
  * Solution: Control arm replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported excessive battery drain, leading to a dead battery.
  * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing faulty electrical components or electronics.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Condensation in Headlights:** Moisture can accumulate inside the headlights, causing fogging or condensation.
  * Solution: Headlight replacement or repair.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Loose Audio System:** The audio system may become loose, resulting in rattling or buzzing noises.
  * Solution: Tightening or replacing loose components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Audi TTS Quattro has faced some reported issues, including engine valve issues, transmission shifting problems, suspension squeaks, electrical battery drains, and exterior headlight condensation. While some of these issues can be minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as engine timing chain failures, can be more severe and costly. Overall, the reported issues with the TTS Quattro are not widespread and do not significantly detract from its overall reliability or performance.
