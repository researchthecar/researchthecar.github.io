# 2021 Audi TTS Quattro

### Overview
The 2021 Audi TTS Quattro is a performance-oriented compact sports car that generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality engineering. It is equipped with a potent 2.0-liter turbocharged engine paired with a sophisticated dual-clutch automatic transmission, both of which are designed for spirited driving while maintaining a good level of durability. Owners typically report a positive ownership experience with few major mechanical concerns. However, as with any performance vehicle, proactive maintenance is key to ensuring long-term reliability. The Quattro all-wheel-drive system contributes to its dynamic capabilities and generally performs without significant issues. Overall, the TTS Quattro offers a compelling blend of performance and dependability for its segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi TTS Quattro demonstrates commendable reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas of concern are minor, infrequent electrical glitches, particularly within the infotainment system, which are typically resolved with software updates. The performance-oriented nature of the TTS means that while major failures are rare, consistent maintenance and attentive driving habits are recommended to preserve its optimal condition. Overall, it is a dependable choice for enthusiasts seeking a blend of performance and practicality.
