**2016 Audi TTS Quattro**

The 2016 Audi TTS Quattro is a high-performance sports car that is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual or a six-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The TTS Quattro comes standard with all-wheel drive.

The TTS Quattro is a fun and rewarding car to drive. It has a powerful engine, sharp handling, and a comfortable ride. However, it is also a relatively expensive car, and it has some reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, smoke from the exhaust, low oil levels on the dipstick
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings or the entire engine
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: #17V359000
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, loss of power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **Six-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, shuddering
        * Solution: Replace the clutch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Audi TTS Quattro is a fun and rewarding car to drive. However, it is also a relatively expensive car, and it has some reliability issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be quite expensive to fix. Overall, the TTS Quattro is a good car, but it is important to be aware of its potential problems before buying one.
