**Introduction:**
The 2015 Audi TTS Quattro is a high-performance sports car that combines sleek design with impressive power. It features a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 265 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The TTS Quattro comes with a standard six-speed manual transmission or an optional six-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. It is praised for its agile handling, responsive engine, and stylish exterior, but criticized for its firm ride and limited rear-seat space.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacing piston rings and valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on startup, followed by reduced engine power and potential catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain tensioner and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises, and reduced clutch engagement.
    * Solution: Replacing the clutch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed S Tronic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission malfunction, shifting problems, and potential loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacing the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clicking or clunking noises from the suspension, especially over uneven roads.
    * Solution: Replacing the strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical system malfunctions, and drained battery.
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing the source of the battery drain (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic drain).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight housing, impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Replacing the headlight assembly or repairing the seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Multi-Function Steering Wheel Button Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on the steering wheel not functioning properly, including volume control, cruise control, and phone controls.
    * Solution: Replacing the multi-function steering wheel unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 2015 Audi TTS Quattro offers impressive performance and styling, it has been reported to experience several issues. Engine problems such as oil consumption and timing chain tensioner failure can be costly to repair. Transmission issues with the S tronic unit are also a potential concern. Overall, the severity and potential cost of these issues should be considered before purchasing a used TTS Quattro.
