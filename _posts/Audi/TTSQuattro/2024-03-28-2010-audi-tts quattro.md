**2010 Audi TTS Quattro**

The 2010 Audi TTS Quattro is a high-performance sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience with its 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine and Quattro all-wheel drive system. The TTS comes with a 6-speed manual transmission as standard, while a 6-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission is available as an option. Despite its impressive capabilities, the TTS has encountered several issues that have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the timing chain tensioner can lead to rattling noises, engine misfires, and reduced power output.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall #19V477000

* **Intake Manifold Flap Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning or broken intake manifold flaps can cause a loss of power, rough idling, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold flaps.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**6-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The mechatronic unit is responsible for controlling the transmission's gears. Its failure can result in loss of gears, hesitation, or complete transmission failure.
    * Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall #18V656000

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged lower control arm bushings can lead to excessive vibration, uneven tire wear, and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the lower control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Draining Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can prematurely discharge due to faulty electrical components or software glitches.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the underlying electrical issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

* **Xenon Headlight Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Issues with the xenon headlights can include flickering, bulb failure, or reduced illumination.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected headlight components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Lenses:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The fog light lenses can become discolored or foggy, reducing their effectiveness.
    * Solution: Replacement of the fog light lenses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may experience glitches, resulting in inconsistent temperature outputs or fan speeds.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

* **Navigation System Errors:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may experience software errors, leading to incorrect directions or map glitches.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the navigation system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2010 Audi TTS Quattro offers an exhilarating driving experience but is not without its issues. Some of the reported problems, such as the timing chain tensioner failure and mechatronic unit failure, can be severe and expensive to repair. However, most other issues are relatively minor and do not significantly impact the overall ownership experience.
