**Introduction:**

The 2018 Audi TTS Quattro boasts a potent 2.0L TFSI engine that delivers 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. Paired with a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission, this coupe accelerates from 0-60 mph in just 4.6 seconds. However, despite its impressive performance, the TTS Quattro has been plagued by several issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L TFSI Engine**
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, hesitation during acceleration
        * Solution: Intake manifold removal and cleaning, walnut blasting of valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noise from engine, reduced engine power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner and related components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed S tronic Transmission**
    * **Clutch Pack Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears at high torque
        * Solution: Clutch pack replacement, transmission fluid flush
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting problems, check transmission light
        * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Magnetic Ride Suspension**
    * **Magnetic Ride Control Module Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Suspension becomes excessively firm or soft, handling issues
        * Solution: Replacement of magnetic ride control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Magnetic Ride Damper Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Suspension becomes unbalanced, uneven ride quality
        * Solution: Replacement of affected damper
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per damper
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Milage Indicator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Incorrect mileage displayed on instrument cluster
    * Solution: Replacement of instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Resealing of headlights, replacement of headlight assemblies if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system components causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle does not operate correctly, difficult to open or close door
    * Solution: Replacement of door handle sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Spoiler Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear spoiler does not move or moves erratically
    * Solution: Replacement of rear spoiler motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Door Release Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fuel door does not open when release is pressed
    * Solution: Replacement of fuel door release solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Pixel Damage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Pixels on instrument cluster become dim or disappear
    * Solution: Replacement of instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Blower Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system does not blow air or blows insufficiently
    * Solution: Replacement of climate control blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Drain Tube Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin during heavy rain
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of sunroof drain tube
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Audi TTS Quattro has faced a range of issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior components. These issues can be relatively minor, such as sensor failures and door handle problems, or more severe, such as carbon buildup on intake valves and timing chain tensioner failure. The cost of repairs can be significant, with some issues requiring a replacement of major parts. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to seek prompt repairs to avoid further damage or safety concerns.
