## 2017 Audi TTS Quattro

The 2017 Audi TTS Quattro is a high-performance sports coupe powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with 292 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a standard six-speed manual transmission or an optional seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. The Quattro all-wheel-drive system is standard.

The TTS Quattro is known for its sleek styling, powerful engine, and sharp handling, but it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0T**
    * **Piston Rings**
        * Description/Symptoms: The piston rings can fail, leading to excessive oil consumption and blue smoke from the exhaust.
        * Solution: Replace the piston rings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual**
    * **Shift Linkage**
        * Description/Symptoms: The shift linkage can wear out, causing difficulty shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replace the shift linkage.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seven-Speed S tronic**
    * **Clutch Pack**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clutch pack can wear out prematurely, leading to slipping and shuddering during shifts.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch pack.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Premature Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery can fail prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
        * Solution: Replace the battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**
    * **Overcharging**
        * Description/Symptoms: The alternator can overcharge, damaging electrical components.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield**
    * **Stress Cracks**
        * Description/Symptoms: The windshield can develop stress cracks, requiring replacement.
        * Solution: Replace the windshield.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console**
    * **Trim Panel**
        * Description/Symptoms: The trim panel on the center console can become loose or rattle.
        * Solution: Reattach or replace the trim panel.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster**
    * **Backlight Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The backlight on the instrument cluster can fail, making it difficult to read the gauges.
        * Solution: Replace the instrument cluster.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2017 Audi TTS Quattro has some reported issues, but overall it is a reliable and high-performance sports coupe. The most severe and expensive issues are related to the piston rings and clutch pack. However, many of the other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix.
