## 2014 Audi TTS Quattro

The 2014 Audi TTS Quattro is a compact, high-performance sports car that offers exhilarating driving dynamics, a luxurious interior, and advanced technology. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine that produces 265 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a standard six-speed manual transmission or an optional six-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The TTS Quattro comes in both coupe and convertible body styles.

While the 2014 Audi TTS Quattro is a well-rounded sports car, it has had some reported issues. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific problems people are having with the vehicles:

**Engine**

* **2.0T Engine:**
    * **Intake Valve Deposits:** Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause a loss of power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
    * **Solution:** Cleaning the intake valves.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **All Engines:**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** A faulty timing chain tensioner can lead to a rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, and eventually engine failure.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the timing chain tensioner.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Recalls:** N/A

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Difficulty:** Some owners have reported difficulty shifting gears, especially when the transmission is cold.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the shift linkage or synchronizers.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Six-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Clutch Pack Failure:** Premature wear of the clutch packs can cause slipping, shuddering, and difficulty shifting gears.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the clutch packs.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension:**
    * **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** A faulty strut mount bearing can cause a clunking noise from the front suspension, especially when turning or hitting bumps.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the strut mount bearings.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Rear Suspension:**
    * **Lower Control Arm Bushing Deterioration:** The lower control arm bushings can prematurely deteriorate, leading to excessive rear wheel camber and uneven tire wear.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the lower control arm bushings.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have experienced excessive battery drain, especially when the vehicle is parked for extended periods.
    * **Solution:** Identifying and fixing the source of the electrical drain.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Varies depending on the cause of the issue
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Condensation can form inside the headlight assemblies, especially in humid conditions.
    * **Solution:** Replacing the headlight assemblies.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+ per headlight
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction:** Some owners have experienced issues with the navigation system, including freezing or incorrect navigation instructions.
    * **Solution:** Updating the software or replacing the navigation system.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Sunroof Rattle:** The panoramic sunroof can sometimes rattle when closed.
    * **Solution:** Adjusting or replacing the sunroof seals.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2014 Audi TTS Quattro is a reliable and enjoyable sports car. However, it has had some reported issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost to fix them can be significant. It is important to do thorough research and consider the potential costs of ownership before purchasing a used 2014 Audi TTS Quattro.
