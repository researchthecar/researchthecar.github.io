# 2023 Audi S7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi S7 Sportback blends executive luxury with high-performance driving dynamics, featuring a potent twin-turbo V6 engine and a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system. As a modern luxury sport sedan, prospective used-car buyers should consider the complexity of its advanced electronics and high-performance powertrain components. Reviewing owner experiences provides helpful context on what to monitor during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6 (444 hp)

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration paired with a smooth power delivery. It is generally robust when maintained on strict service intervals, though routine monitoring of high-pressure fuel and turbo systems is recommended for used buyers.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission provides quick gear changes and seamless power distribution to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is well-matched to the high-output V6 engine and typically demonstrates dependable operation if fluid changes are kept up to date.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi S7 Sportback is a sophisticated performance machine offering immense power and everyday usability. While major recurring mechanical complaints are minimal for this model year, buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is performed by a qualified specialist to verify the health of its advanced powertrain and electronic systems.
