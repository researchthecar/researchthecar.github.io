# 2022 Audi S7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi S7 Sportback is a high-performance luxury fastback combining striking design with sophisticated tech and potent twin-turbo power. For used-car buyers looking at this upscale model, evaluating the powertrain options and overall vehicle condition is essential for a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It provides robust acceleration and everyday drivability, though prospective buyers should maintain thorough service records.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes while pairing with quattro all-wheel drive. It is a durable unit under normal operating conditions when fluid maintenance schedules are followed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi S7 Sportback offers exhilarating performance and luxury appointments for prospective used-car buyers. As with any high-end luxury vehicle, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verified maintenance history are highly recommended.
