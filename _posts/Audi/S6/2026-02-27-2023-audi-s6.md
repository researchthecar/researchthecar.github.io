# 2023 Audi S6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi S6 blends executive luxury with high-performance capability, driven by a potent twin-turbo setup. As a relatively new luxury performance vehicle on the used market, it generally commands careful inspection by prospective buyers due to complex electronics and high-strung powertrain technology. While major recurring defect trends remain sparse in owner reports, buyers should approach luxury performance models with a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration. This powerplant utilizes modern direct-injection and turbo technology that requires strict adherence to maintenance schedules for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic traditional automatic transmission pairs with quattro all-wheel drive to deliver smooth yet rapid gear changes. It is generally robust under normal driving conditions, though fluid changes are vital for maintaining longevity in high-performance applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi S6 is a sophisticated and powerful luxury sport sedan that offers an engaging drive alongside everyday refinement. Because it combines advanced engineering with complex digital and mechanical systems, buyers should verify service records and secure a professional inspection before purchase.
