# 2015 Audi S6

### Overview
The 2015 Audi S6, a high-performance variant of the A6 sedan, is equipped with a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, delivering exhilarating acceleration and a refined driving experience. This generation of the S6 is generally considered reliable, benefiting from Audi's engineering prowess. However, like any performance vehicle, it has specific areas that owners and technicians should be aware of. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (CWGD)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level checks required, blue smoke from exhaust under acceleration.
- **Solution:** Engine internal inspection, piston ring replacement, or PCV system check.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger inspection and potential replacement.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,500 per turbo

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, potential for increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic (DL501)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough engagement, delayed shifts.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, 'suspension fault' warning message, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per strut

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension fault.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**MMI Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio cutting out.
- **Solution:** Software updates, MMI control unit reset or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Lighting Control Module
**Headlight Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Adaptive headlights not functioning, flickering, or failing to turn on.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the lighting control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Audi S6 offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury, with a generally solid reliability record. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its high-performance powertrain and optional adaptive air suspension. Owners may encounter issues such as increased oil consumption and turbocharger problems with the 4.0L V8, and potential failures of the mechatronic unit or clutch packs in the S tronic transmission. The adaptive air suspension, while providing excellent ride quality, can be prone to leaks or compressor issues. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the MMI infotainment system, are also noted. Despite these potential issues, with diligent maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs, the 2015 S6 can remain a reliable and enjoyable performance sedan.
