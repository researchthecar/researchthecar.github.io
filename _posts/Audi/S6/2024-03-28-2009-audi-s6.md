**Introduction**

The 2009 Audi S6 is a high-performance sedan that offers a refined driving experience with plenty of power and features. It comes equipped with a 5.2-liter V10 engine paired with a six-speed automatic transmission, delivering 430 horsepower and 391 lb-ft of torque. The S6 is known for its luxurious interior, advanced technology, and sporty handling. However, like any vehicle, it has some issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **5.2L V10**

* **Intake Manifold Flaps Sticking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sticking intake manifold flaps can cause reduced engine power, hesitation during acceleration, and a rough idle.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold flaps.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks can occur from faulty seals or gaskets, leading to fluid loss and potential transmission damage.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking seals or gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per seal/gasket
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn front control arm bushings can cause clunking noises, excessive tire wear, and decreased handling.
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Navigation System Crashing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may crash or become unresponsive intermittently.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the navigation unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation can accumulate inside the headlights.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600 per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Squeaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises may come from the center console when the vehicle is driven over rough surfaces.
    * Solution: Tighten or apply lubricant to the affected parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2009 Audi S6 has some potential issues, but they are not widespread or severe. The most common issues are related to the intake manifold flaps, transmission fluid leaks, and front control arm bushings. While these repairs can be costly, they are generally not major concerns. The S6 remains a well-regarded performance sedan that offers a blend of luxury, power, and handling.
