# 2016 Audi S6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Audi S6, buyers are drawn to its potent performance, upscale cabin, and everyday practicality. However, because it is a high-performance luxury sedan packed with advanced engineering, staying informed on potential mechanical and electrical hiccups is essential. Reviewing owner feedback and known common problem areas can help you evaluate prospective vehicles and negotiate a smarter purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2016 Audi S6 comes standard with a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 450 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. While delivering exhilarating acceleration and refined power delivery, this complex powerplant requires diligent maintenance and can be expensive to repair out of warranty.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report unexpected engine stalling and sudden loss of motive power while driving, occasionally accompanied by turbo-related error warnings.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is necessary to inspect the turbocharger system, oil screen filters, and crankcase ventilation components.

**Crankcase Ventilation Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Faulty crankcase ventilation components can lead to pressure issues, oil leaks, or rough running conditions.
- **Solution:** Replace the crankcase ventilation valve and associated breather hoses.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed to Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system via a 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. It offers lightning-fast gearchanges, but prospective owners should thoroughly test drive the vehicle to check for any mechatronic or shifting anomalies.

**Harsh Downshifts and Mechatronic Unit Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience hesitation, delayed engagement, or violent jerking when coming to a stop or during downshifts.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing of the transmission control module and mechatronic unit, potentially requiring software updates, fluid services, or component replacement.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension Struts

The adaptive air suspension delivers a comfortable ride and adjustable handling dynamics, but replacement air struts can be costly when age-related wear sets in.

**Air Strut Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging corners, unexpected ride height changes, or suspension warning messages on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Replace worn or leaking air suspension struts.

## Brakes

### Front Brake Calipers

The high-performance braking setup provides strong stopping power suited for the S6's capabilities, though routine inspections of lines and calipers are advised.

**External Crossover Tube Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Brake fluid leaks can develop due to corrosion on the external steel crossover tubes of performance brake calipers, often accelerated by trapped moisture under protective sleeves.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines and calipers regularly, and replace damaged brake calipers or lines immediately.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi S6 is a thrilling luxury sport sedan that blends high-end comfort with formidable V8 performance. However, due to its complex engineering, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an Audi specialist. Verifying service history, checking for any turbocharger or transmission quirks, and budgeting for potential luxury maintenance costs will help ensure a more satisfying ownership experience.
