**Introduction:**

The 2001 Audi S6 is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a 4.2L V8 engine paired with a 5-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. It boasts 340 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. The S6 is appreciated for its refined interior, ample power, and sharp handling, but it is also known for certain issues.

**Engine**

* **4.2L V8**
    * **Intake Manifold Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, reduced engine power
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, reduced engine power, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioners
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Tiptronic Automatic:**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible fluid leak, slipping gears, delayed shifts
        * Solution: Repair or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, slipping gears, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoids
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noises, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Sway Bar End Links:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, decreased stability
    * Solution: Replace end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after short periods of parking
    * Solution: Repair or replace battery, check for parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Inoperative Window Switches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows fail to operate
    * Solution: Replace window switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dash Cluster Pixel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Displayed characters become distorted or faded
    * Solution: Replace pixel display
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Heated Seat Element Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heated seats are not working
    * Solution: Repair or replace heating element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2001 Audi S6 is a performance-oriented sedan with several potential issues. While some issues, such as suspension bushings and heated seat elements, are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others, like intake manifold failure and timing chain tensioner failure, can be more severe and costly. Owners should be aware of these issues and budget for potential repairs.
