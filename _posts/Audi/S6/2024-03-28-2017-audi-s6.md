# 2017 Audi S6 Issues


The 2017 Audi S6 is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine that produces 450 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. The S6 is also equipped with a variety of advanced features, including a sport-tuned suspension, a heads-up display, and a Bang & Olufsen sound system.

Despite its impressive performance and features, the 2017 Audi S6 has been plagued by a number of issues. These issues range from minor annoyances to serious mechanical problems.

## Engine
- **4.0L V8 Engine**
    - **Carbon Buildup**
        - Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause a loss of power, poor fuel economy, and rough idling.
        - Solution: The intake valves must be cleaned to remove the carbon buildup.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        - Recalls: None Found
    - **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause the timing chain to skip, which can damage the engine.
        - Solution: The timing chain tensioner must be replaced.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        - Recalls: None Found


## Transmission
- **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    - **Shifting Issues**
        - Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift roughly or hesitate to shift.
        - Solution: The transmission control module may need to be reprogrammed or the transmission may need to be replaced.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        - Recalls: None Found


## Suspension
- **Adaptive Air Suspension**
    - **Air Spring Leaks**
        - Description/Symptoms: The air springs can develop leaks, which can cause the suspension to sag.
        - Solution: The air springs must be replaced.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 per air spring
        - Recalls: None Found


## Electrical
- **Electrical System**
    - **Battery Drain**
        - Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain even when the car is turned off.
        - Solution: The parasitic drain must be found and repaired.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        - Recalls: None Found


## Exterior
- **No Issues Found**


## Interior
- **Interior**
    - **Cracked Dashboard**
        - Description/Symptoms: The dashboard can crack over time.
        - Solution: The dashboard must be replaced.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        - Recalls: None Found


## Summary
The 2017 Audi S6 is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. However, it has been plagued by a number of issues, including carbon buildup on the intake valves, timing chain tensioner failure, and electrical problems. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing a used S6.
