## 2014 Audi S6

The 2014 Audi S6 is a performance-oriented sedan that combines a potent engine with a refined interior and advanced technology. It is powered by a 4.0-liter turbocharged V8 engine that produces 420 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. Paired with the engine is a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission.

People like the S6 for its powerful engine, sophisticated interior, and sharp handling. However, it has also been known to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **4.0L Turbocharged V8 Engine**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing a rattling noise and potentially catastrophic engine damage. 
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** The intake manifold gasket can fail, leading to vacuum leaks, reduced engine power, and increased emissions.
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Fuel injectors can fail, causing rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, and decreased fuel efficiency.

**Transmission**

* **Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission (S tronic)**
    * **Clutches Failure:** The clutches can wear prematurely, resulting in transmission slippage, shuddering, and difficulty shifting gears.
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** The mechatronic unit, which controls the transmission, can fail, causing a variety of transmission problems, including loss of power, difficulty shifting gears, and limp mode.

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:** The air suspension compressor can fail, leading to a loss of ride height and poor handling.
* **Rear Control Arm Bushing Failure:** The rear control arm bushings can prematurely fail, causing excessive rear-end vibration and noise.

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Failure:** The electrical harness can develop faults, causing a variety of electrical problems, including loss of power, instrument panel malfunctions, and airbag deployment issues.
* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, especially when the vehicle is parked for extended periods.
* **Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure:** The xenon headlight ballasts can fail, causing intermittent or complete headlight failure.

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging:** The sunroof drain plugs can become clogged, leading to water leaking into the cabin.
* **Trunk Lid Release Failure:** The trunk lid release can fail, making it difficult or impossible to open the trunk.

**Interior**

* **Airbag Sensor Failure:** The airbag sensor can fail, causing the airbag warning light to illuminate and potentially preventing the airbags from deploying in the event of an accident.
* **Dashboard Rattling:** The dashboard can develop a rattle or squeak, especially when driving on rough roads.
* **Navigation System Malfunctions:** The navigation system can experience glitches or become unresponsive, requiring a software update or replacement.

**Summary**

The 2014 Audi S6 is a powerful and luxurious sedan, but it has been known to have some significant issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can be high, and some problems may require extensive labor. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and to consider the potential cost of ownership.
