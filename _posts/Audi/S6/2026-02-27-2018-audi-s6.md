# 2018 Audi S6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Audi S6, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbo V8. While these German performance cars offer incredible speed and refinement, prospective owners should review potential owner-reported areas of concern regarding visibility, braking performance, and electrical issues.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2018 Audi S6 features a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 450 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. This engine delivers effortless, rapid acceleration but requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed to Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system via a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. It provides lightning-fast gear changes, though buyers should verify smooth operation during test drives.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking systems on luxury sedans frequently generate substantial dust and require attentive servicing.

**Excessive Brake Dust and Stopping Distance Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Heavy accumulation of brake dust on wheels accompanied by owner-perceived impacts on stopping distance.
- **Solution:** Regularly inspect brake pads and rotors for wear, and consider alternative aftermarket pad formulations if dust is a persistent nuisance.

## Steering

### Charging and Steering Systems

Complex electrical architectures in modern luxury vehicles can occasionally manifest warning messages related to low battery voltage.

**Battery Drain and Steering Fault Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** A dead battery can cause failure to start and trigger secondary dashboard warnings such as a steering system fault.
- **Solution:** Test the health of the battery and charging system, and replace weak batteries to clear electrical fault codes.

## Exterior

### Windshield

Owner reports highlight occasional fragility with the front glass under minor road debris impacts.

**Windshield Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the windshield shattered unexpectedly after minor impacts from small rocks or road debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield thoroughly for existing chips or cracks, and replacement of the glass by an auto glass specialist if failure occurs.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi S6 is an exhilarating luxury performance sedan, but potential buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the battery health, brake wear, and overall electrical and glass condition to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
