# 2022 Audi S6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi S6 delivers high-performance luxury powered by a sophisticated twin-turbocharged mild-hybrid V6 engine. For used-car buyers, this luxury sport sedan offers impressive refinement and modern tech, though prospective owners should carefully review vehicle history and maintenance records given the complex nature of its powertrain and electronic systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6 (444 hp)

This 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 utilizes a 48-volt mild-hybrid system to produce approximately 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and refined power delivery, though proper maintenance is crucial for long-term durability.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (444 hp / 591 hp)

A potent 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 is available in select global markets or related performance variants, offering immense power and effortless passing capability. It is a highly complex performance powerplant requiring diligent upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers crisp, responsive gear shifts suited for both relaxed cruising and aggressive driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi S6 is a sophisticated and powerful luxury sport sedan that balances daily drivability with high-end performance. Buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to enjoy this complex vehicle with confidence.
