# 2022 Audi S6

### Overview
The 2022 Audi S6, a performance-oriented variant of the A6 sedan, offers a compelling blend of luxury, technology, and potent performance. Powered by a robust twin-turbocharged V8 engine, it delivers exhilarating acceleration and a refined driving experience. Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system ensures confident handling in various conditions. The S6 is equipped with advanced driver-assistance features and a sophisticated infotainment system, reflecting Audi's commitment to innovation. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and performance, like any complex vehicle, some specific areas may warrant attention based on owner feedback and technical reports.

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or hose.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi S6 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain components. The twin-turbo V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have not shown significant widespread issues. The primary areas of concern, based on available data, are related to the adaptive air suspension, which can experience leaks, and occasional electrical gremlins within the infotainment system or premature 12V battery failure. These issues, while not universal, are the most commonly cited potential reliability concerns for this model year. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are recommended to ensure optimal performance and longevity.
