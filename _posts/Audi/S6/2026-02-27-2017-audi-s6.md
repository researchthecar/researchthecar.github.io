# 2017 Audi S6

### Overview
The 2017 Audi S6 is a high-performance luxury sedan known for its potent V8 engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system. As a performance-oriented model, it generally exhibits strong build quality and reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain components. However, like many complex luxury vehicles, it can be susceptible to specific issues, particularly in its advanced electronic systems and some suspension elements, which may require specialized attention and potentially higher repair costs. Owners should be aware of these potential areas to ensure continued optimal performance and longevity of their S6.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8
**Turbocharger Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty turbocharger actuator or wastegate.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, increased oil consumption, potential engine misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic (Dual-Clutch Automatic)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough acceleration, shuddering during shifts.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant compressor noise, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or replacement of the MMI control unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings from adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors; system deactivation.
- **Solution:** Calibration or replacement of faulty sensors (e.g., radar, cameras).
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi S6 offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury, with its core powertrain generally proving robust. However, potential owners should be mindful of the complexity inherent in its advanced systems. Issues with the dual-clutch transmission's mechatronic unit and clutch packs, as well as potential failures in the adaptive air suspension components, represent the most significant mechanical concerns. Electrically, infotainment glitches and ADAS sensor problems are not uncommon. While the engine itself is strong, associated components like turbo actuators and PCV valves can require attention. Regular maintenance and prompt addressing of warning lights are crucial for maintaining the S6's reliability and performance.
