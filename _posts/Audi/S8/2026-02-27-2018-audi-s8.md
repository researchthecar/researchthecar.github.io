# 2018 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi S8 represents the pinnacle of executive luxury and high-performance engineering, blending effortless long-distance cruising with supercar-rivaling acceleration. Beneath its understated exterior lies a sophisticated powertrain designed for dynamic performance. While standard owner complaint filings for this specific model year remain sparse, prospective buyers should always approach high-performance luxury sedans with a thorough inspection of maintenance history, electrical complexity, and suspension integrity to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense power, pushing upwards of 605 horsepower depending on the trim level. This high-output powerplant offers exhilarating performance, though prospective buyers should maintain rigorous oil change intervals and monitor cooling and turbo components closely.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Power is routed through a responsive 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive. The gearbox provides smooth daily cruising shifts alongside rapid-fire gear changes under aggressive acceleration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V397000: Structure:Body:Hood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the seal contacts hot engine components, there could be an increased risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install a retaining plate to prevent the engine compartment seal from loosening. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
