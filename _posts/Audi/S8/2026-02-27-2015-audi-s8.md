# 2015 Audi S8

### Overview
The 2015 Audi S8 is a high-performance luxury sedan that generally offers a strong level of reliability, especially considering its potent powertrain. Owners typically praise its build quality and the sophisticated engineering that underpins its dynamic capabilities. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the S8 tends to avoid widespread, recurring problems that plague less robust designs. The focus for this model year is on maintaining its performance-oriented systems and ensuring the advanced technology features function as intended. Regular maintenance is key to preserving the S8's exceptional driving experience and preventing minor concerns from escalating into significant repairs.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light illuminated, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**MMI Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000


### Lighting Systems
**LED Headlight Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or complete failure of LED headlights, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of headlight control modules or entire headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500 per side

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Blend Door Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or airflow direction, clicking noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty blend door actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


### Seat Adjustments
**Power Seat Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Failure of power seats to adjust forward, backward, or recline.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat motor or control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Audi S8 is a robust performance sedan with a strong reliability record for its powertrain. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the advanced adaptive air suspension system, which can develop leaks or compressor problems, and certain electrical components within the infotainment and lighting systems. Interior climate control and power seat components can also experience failures. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are not typically indicative of systemic flaws across the entire model range. Regular servicing and prompt attention to warning lights are crucial for maintaining the S8's luxury and performance.
