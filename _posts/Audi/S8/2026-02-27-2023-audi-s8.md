# 2023 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi S8 delivers flagship luxury paired with thrilling twin-turbo V8 performance. As a high-end luxury sedan equipped with advanced electronics, complex powertrain engineering, and sophisticated driver-assistance systems, prospective buyers should review available maintenance records closely and consider remaining factory warranty coverage before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 563 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It offers immense acceleration and refined power delivery, though proper maintenance and premium fluid changes are critical for long-term longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It shifts crisply under hard acceleration while remaining remarkably smooth during relaxed highway cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V084000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** During air bag assembly, the front-passenger occupant protection flap of the air bag may not have been folded correctly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." In a crash, an incorrectly folded air bag can deploy with too much force, increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi S8 is a formidable luxury performance sedan that combines everyday comfort with supercar-level straight-line speed. While reported owner complaints are minimal, its high-tech cabin and potent powertrain mean that out-of-warranty repairs can be expensive. Buyers are advised to prioritize well-maintained examples with complete service history.
