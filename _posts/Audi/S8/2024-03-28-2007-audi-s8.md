**Introduction:**

The 2007 Audi S8 is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine that produces 450 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a six-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. Praised for its impressive performance, comfortable interior, and advanced technology, the S8 has also faced some reliability concerns, as outlined below.

**Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, reduced engine power, fuel economy, and hesitation.
    * Solution: Intake valve cleaning.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Faulty Fuel Pressure Sensor:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, hard starts, reduced engine power.
    * Solution: Sensor replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Timing belt failure, engine rattle, engine damage.
    * Solution: Timing chain tensioner replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Hesitation:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifting.
        * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, programming update.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission failure, no shifting.
    * Solution: Mechatronic unit replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 12V037000

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension sagging, reduced ride height.
    * Solution: Compressor replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, electrical system issues.
    * Solution: Battery replacement, electrical system diagnosis.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Faulty Xenon Headlights:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Flickering or dim headlights.
    * Solution: Headlight assembly replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID: 18V168000; 18V169000

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rail Trim Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trim peeling and fading.
    * Solution: Trim replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation software errors, screen freezing.
    * Solution: Software update, system replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not cooling properly, heating system issues.
    * Solution: Component replacement, software update.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $700+

**Summary:**

The 2007 Audi S8 is a powerful and luxurious sedan, but it has faced some reliability concerns, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. Issues such as carbon buildup, timing chain tensioner failure, mechatronic unit failure, and navigation system malfunctions can be expensive to fix. While the S8 offers a thrilling driving experience, it may not be suitable for those who prioritize reliability or want to avoid potentially costly repairs.
