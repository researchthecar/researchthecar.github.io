**Introduction to the 2020 Audi S8**

The 2020 Audi S8 is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers an exhilarating driving experience. It comes standard with a powerful 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 563 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, the S8 can accelerate from 0-60 mph in just 3.8 seconds. Despite its impressive performance, the S8 also offers a comfortable and refined ride, making it an ideal daily driver.

**Engine**

4.0-liter TFSI® V8

* **Turbocharger Wastegate Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate may fail, causing loss of power and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V260000

**Transmission**

8-speed Tiptronic®

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Air Suspension Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The adaptive air suspension system may malfunction, causing the vehicle to sag or become unstable.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Solution: Replacement of the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak water into the cabin, causing damage to interior components.
    * Solution: Replacement of the sunroof seal or other repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, including freezing or loss of functionality.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Audi S8 is a high-performance sedan that offers an impressive combination of power, comfort, and refinement. However, the vehicle has experienced some issues, including turbocharger wastegate failure, suspension malfunctions, electrical problems, and interior glitches. The severity of these issues varies, but some of them can be costly to repair. Potential buyers should consider these issues before purchasing an S8.
