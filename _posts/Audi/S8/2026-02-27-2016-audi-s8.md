# 2016 Audi S8

### Overview
The 2016 Audi S8, a high-performance luxury sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. Powered by a potent twin-turbocharged V8 engine, it offers exhilarating performance coupled with sophisticated technology. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any complex vehicle, certain areas can occasionally present challenges. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, check engine light, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty wastegate actuator or the complete turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**PCV Valve Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential oil leaks, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000

**Parasitic Battery Drain**

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Audi S8 is a robust performance sedan with a generally positive reliability record. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the complex adaptive air suspension system, particularly air spring leaks and compressor issues, and occasional electrical gremlins like parasitic battery drain. The powerful 4.0L V8 engine, while generally dependable, can experience issues with wastegate actuators or PCV valves. Most other systems, including the transmission, infotainment, exterior, and interior components, show good reliability with no widespread significant problems reported. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning lights are recommended for optimal long-term ownership.
