# 2016 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2016 Audi S8, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury sedan that pairs opulent comfort with twin-turbocharged V8 power. While these flagship German performance cars offer thrilling driving dynamics and sophisticated amenities, keeping one on the road requires vigilance regarding its complex electronic and mechanical systems. Prospective owners should review vehicle history thoroughly and budget for specialized maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers an impressive 520 horsepower and 481 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless acceleration for this luxury sedan. This powerplant is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should watch for oil and coolant line leaks as mileage climbs. Strict adherence to synthetic oil change intervals is critical for longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Power is routed through an eight-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive, offering seamless gear shifts and responsive performance. The transmission is typically durable, but fluid changes are essential to prevent harsh shifting or long-term wear.

## Steering

### Steering Angle and Alignment

Issues related to steering alignment and electronic integration are occasionally reported by owners experiencing cascading sensor faults.

**Off-Center Steering After System Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported multiple system error warnings appearing simultaneously upon startup, accompanied by the steering wheel being significantly off-center while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning is required to clear fault codes, recalibrate steering angle sensors, and check electronic power steering modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V057000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The fuel supply line for the high pressure fuel pump may become porous and leak fuel. Leaking fuel, in the presence of an ignition source, increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will install a fuel pressure damper, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V397000: Structure:Body:Hood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the seal contacts hot engine components, there could be an increased risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install a retaining plate to prevent the engine compartment seal from loosening. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi S8 is an exhilarating performance flagship that delivers immense luxury and speed, but it demands careful inspection before purchase. While major powertrain mechanical failures are uncommon, complex electrical and steering sensor anomalies can occasionally arise. Buyers should secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a qualified specialist to ensure long-term reliability and enjoyment.
