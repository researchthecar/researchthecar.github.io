### Overview
The 2021 Audi S8, a high-performance luxury sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential concerns for prospective and current owners.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks/Compressor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, "Suspension Malfunction" warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking air struts/bags, replace faulty air compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen unresponsiveness, system freezing, intermittent connectivity issues with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600


### Driver Assistance Systems
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings from adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors; system deactivation.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or recalibration of sensors, replacement of faulty sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi S8 demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, with its powerful 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission being robust and largely issue-free. The primary areas where owners might encounter concerns are the sophisticated adaptive air suspension, which can develop leaks or compressor issues over time, and the advanced electrical systems, particularly the infotainment unit which may experience software glitches, and driver assistance sensors that can occasionally malfunction. Interior components like seat actuators and heating elements can also present intermittent issues. Despite these potential, albeit not widespread, concerns, the S8 offers a generally dependable and luxurious driving experience for its class.
