# 2021 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi S8 is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a potent twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As a sophisticated flagship model loaded with advanced electronics, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate both powertrain performance and overall electrical health when shopping on the used market.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 563 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It delivers effortless acceleration and refined power delivery suited for a high-end luxury flagship.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission pairs with quattro all-wheel drive to manage high torque output smoothly. It provides prompt gear shifts and cruising efficiency appropriate for a luxury performance sedan.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Owner reports indicate occasional sudden electrical or battery warning lights upon startup, which represent isolated incidents rather than widespread proven defects.

**Electrical System Warning Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Multiple warning lights illuminate in rapid sequence upon startup, accompanied by an electrical system and battery malfunction alert advising not to drive the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnostics scanned by a qualified service center to determine if the issue stems from a failing battery, software glitch, or charging component.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V111000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An engine control module malfunction may cause the engine to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the engine control module connector for a missing sealing pin, and install it, if necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V201000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An instrument panel display that fails to show critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi S8 offers formidable performance and luxury, with minimal documented complaints outside of an isolated electrical warning report. Used car buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the complex electronics and powertrain health before finalizing a purchase.
