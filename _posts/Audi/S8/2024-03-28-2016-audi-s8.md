**Introduction:**

The 2016 Audi S8 is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine, paired with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. The V8 engine delivers an impressive 520 horsepower and 481 pound-feet of torque, propelling the S8 from 0-60 mph in just 3.9 seconds. Known for its exceptional handling, luxurious interior, and advanced technology, the S8 is praised for its speed, comfort, and overall refinement. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced some issues.

**Engine**

* **4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
    * **Oil Consumption Issue**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Remove intake valves for cleaning.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delayed shifts, rough shifting, or loss of gears.
        * Solution: Update transmission control unit software or replace transmission components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Air Suspension**
    * **Air Spring Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride height, uneven suspension, and potential compressor failure.
        * Solution: Replace leaking air springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 per spring

**Electrical**

* **MMI Infotainment System**
    * **Software Glitches**
        * Description/Symptoms: Freezing, slow performance, or loss of functionality.
        * Solution: Update infotainment software or replace components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* **Xenon Headlights**
    * **Ballast Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights.
        * Solution: Replace faulty ballast.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per ballast

**Exterior**

* **Door Handles**
    * **Sticking or Malfunctioning**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening doors, electrical issues with buttons.
        * Solution: Replace door handle assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof**
    * **Leakage Issue**
        * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin during rain.
        * Solution: Replace or reseal sunroof seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Summary**

The 2016 Audi S8 is a high-performance luxury sedan with exceptional capabilities. However, it has experienced some issues, including oil consumption issues in the engine, shifting problems in the transmission, and suspension issues. These problems can be costly to repair, and some have required multiple repairs. If you are considering purchasing a 2016 Audi S8, it is important to be aware of these potential issues and factor in the cost of potential repairs into your purchase decision.
