# 2020 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a luxury performance flagship like the 2020 Audi S8 brings incredible twin-turbo V8 power and executive-level comfort, but prospective used-car buyers should still approach with proper mechanical mindfulness. While comprehensive owner complaint data is relatively light for this model year, keeping a close eye on complex powertrain electronics and high-end accessories is always recommended when evaluating high-performance European sedans. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by an Audi specialist remains the best way to verify vehicle health before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is a sophisticated, highly complex performance engine that requires meticulous maintenance history to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs standard quattro all-wheel drive with smooth, rapid gear shifts suited for both relaxed cruising and aggressive driving. It generally proves robust when fluid changes are performed on schedule.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V111000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An engine control module malfunction may cause the engine to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the engine control module connector for a missing sealing pin, and install it, if necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
