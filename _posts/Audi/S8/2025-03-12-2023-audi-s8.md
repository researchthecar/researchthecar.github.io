Here's a breakdown of common issues and complaints reported for the 2023 Audi S8, categorized and formatted as requested.

The 2023 Audi S8 is a high-performance luxury sedan known for its powerful engine, advanced technology, and refined interior. It comes equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 563 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. People like the S8 for its impressive acceleration, comfortable ride (thanks to the adaptive air suspension), and luxurious features. Some common dislikes include the high price tag and the complex infotainment system which can be distracting to use while driving.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noises from the engine, loss of power, check engine light. Can be caused by oil starvation or component failure within the turbocharger.
    *   Solution: Turbocharger replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil spots under the car, burning oil smell, low oil level warning light. Common leak areas include valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, and rear main seal.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the leaking gasket or seal.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+ depending on the leak location.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable rough shifts between gears, especially at lower speeds. Hesitation or delay when accelerating from a stop or changing gears.
    *   Solution: Software update or transmission valve body replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sits unevenly, suspension warning lights illuminate, compressor failure.
    *   Solution: Replacing the air struts, compressor, or air lines as needed.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+ per corner, depending on the failed component.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The MMI (Multi Media Interface) system freezes, reboots unexpectedly, or becomes unresponsive.
    *   Solution: Software update or replacement of the MMI control unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Issues with parking sensors, blind-spot monitoring, or adaptive cruise control, resulting in error messages or incorrect readings.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+ per sensor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof is slow to open or close, gets stuck, or makes excessive noise. Water leaks may also occur.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating the sunroof tracks, or replacing the sunroof motor or seals.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Seat Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Seat fails to adjust properly, motor makes grinding noises, or seat becomes stuck in one position.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the seat motor or entire seat track assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Audi S8, while a high-performing luxury vehicle, can be prone to several issues. The most concerning problems are related to the engine (turbocharger failures and oil leaks), transmission (harsh shifting), and air suspension. Electrical issues with the infotainment system and sensors are also relatively common. The cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars for minor fixes to several thousand dollars for major component replacements like turbochargers or air suspension struts. These potential issues, combined with the vehicle's already high price tag, should be considered before purchasing a 2023 Audi S8.
