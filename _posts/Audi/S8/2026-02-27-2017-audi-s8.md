# 2017 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi S8 represents the pinnacle of luxury performance, offering a twin-turbocharged V8 engine that delivers immense power paired with refined driving dynamics. Used car buyers looking at this high-performance flagship should appreciate its sophisticated engineering while remaining mindful that complex luxury German sedans often require diligent maintenance and can incur high repair costs when components fail.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 605 horsepower and 517 lb-ft of torque in the Plus trim, providing breathtaking acceleration. This direct-injection powerplant is robust overall, but prospective owners should maintain strict service intervals to ensure long-term reliability of its turbochargers and complex ancillaries.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system with smooth, rapid gear changes. It is generally durable under high-output driving conditions, provided the fluid is inspected and serviced regularly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V397000: Structure:Body:Hood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the seal contacts hot engine components, there could be an increased risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install a retaining plate to prevent the engine compartment seal from loosening. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi S8 is an exhilarating, ultra-luxurious performance sedan that rewards careful ownership with world-class comfort and speed. Because there are no dominant recurring complaint trends in the available owner data, buyers should focus on performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection, reviewing comprehensive service records, and budgeting for the higher operating costs typical of high-end European flagships.
