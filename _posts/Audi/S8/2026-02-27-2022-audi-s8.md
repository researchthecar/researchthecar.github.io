## Suspension

### Active Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, warning lights on the dashboard, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut(s).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per strut


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation system errors, connectivity issues (Bluetooth/Apple CarPlay/Android Auto).
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### Driver Assistance Systems
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts from adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors; system deactivation.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or recalibration of sensors, replacement of faulty sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi S8 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 4.0L twin-turbo V8 and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have not shown widespread issues. However, like many luxury vehicles with advanced systems, potential concerns lie in the sophisticated electronics and suspension. Owners may encounter issues with the active air suspension, specifically air strut leaks, and occasional glitches within the infotainment system or driver assistance sensors. These are not uncommon for vehicles with such complex technology. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are recommended to ensure continued optimal performance and longevity of the S8.
