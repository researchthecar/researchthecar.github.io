# 2022 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi S8 delivers high-performance luxury powered by a twin-turbo V8 engine paired with an advanced automatic transmission. While it offers sophisticated engineering and blistering speed, prospective buyers should review available owner reports and inspect major mechanical systems thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 563 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It provides effortless acceleration and refined power delivery typical of Audi's flagship performance sedan.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes and refined cruising behavior suited to a luxury performance flagship.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V084000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** During air bag assembly, the front-passenger occupant protection flap of the air bag may not have been folded correctly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." In a crash, an incorrectly folded air bag can deploy with too much force, increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2022 Audi S8 remains a premier choice for buyers seeking executive luxury combined with sports-car performance. A comprehensive professional inspection prior to purchase is always recommended to ensure optimal mechanical and electronic health.
