# 2014 Audi S8

### Overview
The 2014 Audi S8, a high-performance luxury sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. Powered by a potent V8 engine and equipped with advanced Quattro all-wheel-drive, it offers exhilarating performance. While most owners report a satisfying ownership experience, like any complex vehicle, certain areas can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs for the 2014 model year.

---


## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, check engine light, potential oil leaks, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of turbochargers and associated lines.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000+

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'air suspension malfunction' warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**MMI Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio cutting out, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or potential replacement of the MMI control unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000+


### Lighting System
**LED Headlight Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Individual LED segments failing, flickering headlights, 'check headlight' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty LED headlight modules.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500 per side

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Audi S8 is a robust performance sedan with a generally positive reliability record. The primary areas that may require attention are the adaptive air suspension components, particularly air strut leaks and compressor issues, and occasional glitches within the MMI infotainment system. Engine-related issues, while not extremely common, can be costly to repair, especially concerning turbochargers. Electrical components like LED headlights can also be expensive if they fail. Overall, for a vehicle of its complexity and performance level, the S8 offers good long-term reliability when properly maintained, but potential owners should be aware of the higher cost associated with repairs for its advanced systems.
