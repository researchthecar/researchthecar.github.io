# 2024 Audi S8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi S8 represents the pinnacle of luxury and high-performance engineering, pairing a twin-turbocharged V8 engine with a refined automatic transmission and sophisticated electronic systems. As a relatively new luxury performance vehicle, widespread chronic mechanical failures are scarce in owner reports, though prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate complex cabin electronics and powertrain health during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 563 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration with effortless refinement. While robust under normal operating conditions, buyers should verify service records due to the high-strung nature and thermal complexity of modern high-performance German V8 engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the quattro all-wheel-drive system with rapid, seamless gear changes. It is generally well-matched to the engine's power output, though routine fluid maintenance is essential for long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi S8 is an extraordinarily capable, luxurious super-sedan backed by strong powertrain engineering. Because complaints are minimal for this model year, buyers can approach with confidence while keeping in mind that routine maintenance and out-of-warranty repairs for high-end luxury vehicles remain inherently expensive.
