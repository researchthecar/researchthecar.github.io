# 2018 Audi TT RS Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi TT RS Quattro delivers exhilarating performance through its iconic turbocharged five-cylinder engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system. While overall owner complaint volume is very low for this high-performance sports car, prospective buyers should still carefully inspect the fuel system and standard mechanical components during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged 5-Cylinder

The 2.5-liter turbocharged five-cylinder engine produces approximately 394 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, offering thrilling acceleration and a signature exhaust note. It is generally robust, though owners should monitor for rare fuel system and EVAP component leaks.

**Fuel System and Evaporative Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel leaks out of the excess drain hole instead of entering the vehicle during refueling, posing a potential fire hazard.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel tank, EVAP canister, and associated lines, and replace any failed components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission channels power efficiently to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system with rapid, seamless gear changes. It is a sophisticated unit that requires routine fluid maintenance to ensure long-term durability.
