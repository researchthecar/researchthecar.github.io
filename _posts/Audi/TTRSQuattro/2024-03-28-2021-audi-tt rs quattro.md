**2021 Audi TT RS Quattro**

The 2021 Audi TT RS Quattro is a high-performance sports car that offers an exhilarating driving experience. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.5-liter inline-5 engine that produces 394 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The TT RS Quattro boasts impressive acceleration, handling, and braking capabilities. It also features a comfortable and well-equipped interior.

However, there have been some reported issues with the 2021 Audi TT RS Quattro. These issues range from minor inconveniences to more serious problems.

**Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and whistling noises.
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**
    * **Clutch Pack Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, jerking, and slipping.
        * Solution: Replace clutch pack.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, such as flickering lights, power window failures, and starting difficulties.
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty electrical harnesses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Convertible top failing to open or close properly, leaks, and wind noise.
    * Solution: Repair or replace convertible top mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trim on the center console peeling off or fading.
    * Solution: Replace center console trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2021 Audi TT RS Quattro is a highly capable and enjoyable sports car, it has been reported to have some issues, including turbocharger failures, transmission clutch pack failures, electrical harness issues, convertible top malfunctions, and peeling center console trim. The severity and cost of these issues vary, and some may be more common than others. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase.
