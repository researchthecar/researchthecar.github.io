**Introduction:**

The 2014 Audi TT RS Quattro is a high-performance sports car powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-five engine producing 400 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a six-speed manual or a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (S tronic). The TT RS is praised for its blistering acceleration, precise handling, and luxurious interior. However, it is not without its flaws.

**Engine**

* **2.5L Turbocharged Inline-Five**
    * **Misfiring Under Load:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires when accelerating or under heavy load, causing hesitation and loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils or spark plugs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Whining or whistling noises from the turbocharger, loss of boost, reduced engine power.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V340000
        * Issue: Potential for a fuel pump hose to leak.
        * Description/Symptoms: Fuel leak, engine stalling.

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual**
    * **Shifting Difficulty:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises when shifting.
        * Solution: Adjust or replace transmission linkage components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Seven-Speed S tronic**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips between gears, error messages on dashboard.
        * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Identify the source of parasitic drain and rectify it.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin from the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Steering Wheel Buttons Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel buttons fail to respond, intermittent function.
    * Solution: Replace the steering wheel buttons or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Summary:**

The 2014 Audi TT RS Quattro is a thrilling sports car with impressive performance. However, it is not immune to issues. The most significant concerns include potential engine misfiring, turbocharger failure, and transmission problems. Interior and exterior issues are generally less severe and less costly to fix. While the TT RS is a desirable vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and consider the associated repair costs.
