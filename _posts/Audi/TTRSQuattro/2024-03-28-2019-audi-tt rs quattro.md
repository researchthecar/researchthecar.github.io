**Introduction:**

The 2019 Audi TT RS Quattro is a high-performance sports car known for its thrilling driving experience. It features a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-five engine paired with a seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission, producing 400 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. The vehicle boasts sharp handling, a luxurious interior, and a sleek exterior.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Turbocharged Inline-Five**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or knocking noises from the engine at startup, especially when cold
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, or abnormal turbocharger noises
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger wastegate actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch**

* **Clutch Pack Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, shuddering during gear changes, or difficulty engaging certain gears
    * Solution: Replacement of clutch pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Ballast Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both headlights intermittently flickering or failing to turn on
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight ballast
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

No Issues Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment Screen Lag and Freezing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow response or freezing of the infotainment system, difficulty using touch controls
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2019 Audi TT RS Quattro is a high-performance vehicle with many desirable features, it has been reported to have several issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can be significant, and owners may experience reduced performance, increased maintenance costs, or safety concerns.
