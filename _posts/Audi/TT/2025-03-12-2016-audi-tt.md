The 2016 Audi TT is a sporty coupe and convertible known for its sleek design, nimble handling, and high-quality interior. The standard engine for the 2016 TT is a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 producing 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission. Many owners appreciate the TT's responsive engine, sharp handling, and stylish interior. However, some criticisms include the limited rear seat space (in the coupe), firm ride, and potential for expensive repairs.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (220 hp, 258 lb-ft)**

*   **Water Pump Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, warning light on the dashboard. The water pump housing, often made of plastic, can crack and leak.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the water pump, thermostat, and possibly coolant flush.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Ignition Coil Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel economy, and engine warning light.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty ignition coil(s). It's often recommended to replace all ignition coils at the same time.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission**

*   **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, failure to shift, warning light on the dashboard. The Mechatronic unit is the transmission control unit.
    *   Solution: Replacement or rebuild of the Mechatronic unit. Fluid change is recommended.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Clutch Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Slipping during acceleration, jerky engagement, difficulty shifting. Premature wear of the dual clutches.
    *   Solution: Clutch replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Worn Suspension Bushings**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, particularly when going over bumps. Reduced handling precision.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the worn bushings.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+ (depending on which bushings need replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Car is dead after sitting for a short period of time.
    *   Solution: Have the battery tested and replaced if necessary. If the battery tests ok, have the vehicle inspected for excessive parasitic draw to find the component draining the battery.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Infotainment System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing, slow response, failure to connect to Bluetooth, and screen malfunctions.
    *   Solution: Software updates, component replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Headlight LED Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Flickering or complete failure of the LED daytime running lights or headlights.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the headlight assembly (LEDs are often integrated).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Virtual Cockpit Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Pixelation, flickering, or complete failure of the virtual cockpit display.
    *   Solution: Software updates or replacement of the virtual cockpit unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Audi TT, while sporty and stylish, can present owners with some potential issues. The most concerning problems revolve around the engine (water pump, ignition coils), transmission (Mechatronic unit, clutch wear), and electrical systems (battery drain, infotainment system). Repairs can be expensive, particularly those involving the transmission and electrical components. The severity of issues ranges from minor inconveniences to more serious mechanical failures. Owners should be diligent with maintenance and be prepared for potentially costly repairs.
