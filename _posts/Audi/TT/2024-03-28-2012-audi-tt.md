**2012 Audi TT**

The 2012 Audi TT is a sleek and sporty two-seater coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is available with a 2.0-liter TFSI engine and a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission. People who own the Audi TT appreciate its stylish design, powerful engine, and responsive handling. However, they have reported some issues with the vehicle, which we will discuss in this report.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter TFSI Engine**

* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to slip and resulting in catastrophic engine damage.
* **Water pump failure:** The water pump can fail, leading to coolant leaks and overheating.
* **Intake manifold runner flap failure:** The intake manifold runner flaps can fail, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual Transmission:**

* **Clutch failure:** The clutch can fail, resulting in difficulty shifting gears and a loss of power.

**6-speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission:**

* **Mechatronic unit failure:** The mechatronic unit is a critical component that controls the transmission's operation. It can fail, leading to shifting problems and transmission failure.

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension strut mount failure:** The front suspension strut mount can fail, causing a knocking noise and reduced handling.
* **Rear suspension control arm bushing wear:** The rear suspension control arm bushings can wear out, leading to a loose-feeling suspension and reduced stability.

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely due to a faulty electrical component.
* **Electrical harness failure:** The electrical harness can fail, causing various electrical system malfunctions.

**Exterior**

* **Windshield wiper linkage failure:** The windshield wiper linkage can fail, causing the wipers to stop working.

**Interior**

* **Heated seat element failure:** The heating element in the seats can fail, leaving the seats cold.

**Summary**

Overall, the 2012 Audi TT has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While some owners have experienced severe and costly engine and transmission issues, others have not reported any major problems. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the severity of the issue and the specific parts that need to be replaced.
