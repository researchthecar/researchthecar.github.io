# 2017 Audi TT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi TT delivers sharp styling, engaging handling, and a high-tech cabin, making it an appealing option for used sports car buyers. Prospective purchasers should carefully inspect the vehicle's powertrain options and ensure all routine maintenance has been documented, as owner reports highlight a few select areas requiring attention.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing spirited acceleration. It is generally robust when maintained with regular oil changes, though buyers should watch for standard direct-injection carbon buildup over time.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear shifts paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid changes are critical to maintaining longevity and preventing clutch pack wear.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V076000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=TT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the fuel tank may become damaged by the fuel tank heat shield bracket . A damaged fuel tank may leak fuel, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a protective cap on the fuel tank heat shield bracket, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi TT remains an exciting and stylish choice for used car buyers who prioritize performance and interior luxury. While major recurring mechanical failure patterns are limited in owner data, thorough pre-purchase inspections and verification of service history are recommended to ensure trouble-free ownership.
