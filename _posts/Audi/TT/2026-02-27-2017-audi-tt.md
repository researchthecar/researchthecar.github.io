### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi TT demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, with most major mechanical systems like the engine and transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The 2.0L TFSI engine and its associated S tronic or manual transmissions are generally robust. Suspension components are also typically durable. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is the infotainment system, which can occasionally exhibit software glitches requiring updates or resets. Exterior and interior components are generally well-built and hold up well over time. Overall, the 2017 TT is a reliable choice for those seeking a sporty and stylish vehicle, provided standard maintenance is performed and minor electrical quirks are addressed.
