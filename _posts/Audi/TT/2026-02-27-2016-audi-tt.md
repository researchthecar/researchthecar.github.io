# 2016 Audi TT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Audi TT means evaluating a stylish, modern sports car known for its engaging driving dynamics and upscale cabin. While official owner complaint volumes are low, prospective buyers should still carefully inspect core mechanical components and interior trim details during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for the TT. It is generally regarded as a robust powerplant, provided routine oil change intervals are strictly maintained.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission offers rapid gear changes and power delivery to the standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid maintenance is critical to ensuring the longevity and smooth operation of this complex gearbox.

## Interior

### Front Seat Trim Housing

Owner reports indicate potential vulnerabilities with the plastic trim housing surrounding the upper front seats where side airbags are integrated.

**Loose or Broken Seat Airbag Trim**
- **Symptoms:** The plastic housing and trim pieces on the sides of the front seats near the headrest pop open, break, or pull away from the seat structure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat trim during a pre-purchase evaluation and have damaged plastic housings or clips replaced by a qualified technician.
- **Cost:** $2700

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V076000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=TT&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the fuel tank may become damaged by the fuel tank heat shield bracket . A damaged fuel tank may leak fuel, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a protective cap on the fuel tank heat shield bracket, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V543000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=TT&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During air bag deployment, the front passenger air bag may explode or deploy improperly. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi TT offers compelling styling and sporty performance, backed by a relatively quiet owner complaint history. Buyers should prioritize a thorough inspection of the vehicle's interior trim, especially around the front seat structures and airbag housings, while maintaining standard powertrain checkups.
