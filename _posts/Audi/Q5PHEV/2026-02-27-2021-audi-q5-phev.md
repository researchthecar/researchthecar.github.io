# 2021 Audi Q5 PHEV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi Q5 Plug-In Hybrid (PHEV) delivers strong performance, a refined cabin, and efficient daily driving capability. However, used car buyers should carefully review potential issues related to electrical stability, sunroof integrity, and powertrain behaviors. While many owners enjoy its luxury and tech features, attention to historical complaints can help prevent unexpected repair frustrations.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Plug-In Hybrid

Paired with an electric motor to produce a combined 362 horsepower, this plug-in hybrid powertrain offers brisk acceleration and solid fuel economy. Overall mechanical reliability is generally favorable, though proper maintenance is critical.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This dual-clutch automatic transmission manages power delivery across the all-wheel-drive system with rapid, smooth gear changes. Buyers should verify proper operation during test drives, particularly at lower speeds.

### Drivetrain & Suspension Handling

Suspension and drivetrain components generally provide a composed, comfortable ride quality, though low-speed maneuverability can occasionally exhibit minor binding behaviors.

**Low-Speed Shudder and Binding**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may shudder, buck, or jitter when executing tight turns or driving at very low speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the all-wheel-drive system and consult relevant service bulletins for recommended fluid changes or adjustments.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

The exterior styling is highlighted by a large panoramic glass roof, which has been associated with rare reports of spontaneous glass failure.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass panel may crack or explode unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds without any apparent external impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for pre-existing stress fractures and ensure clean track seals; replacement requires a full glass assembly swap.

## Interior

### Gateway Control Module & Sensors

The electrical architecture is advanced and heavily reliant on integrated control modules, making it sensitive to moisture intrusion if drainage paths become blocked.

**Water Damage to Control Modules**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights illuminate, digital dashboard goes black, windows roll down spontaneously, and the vehicle may lose motive power.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof drains to prevent water accumulation in the trunk area, and replace damaged gateway modules if water intrusion has occurred.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)

Safety technologies provide extensive driver protection features, though sensor calibration and phantom braking alerts remain points to monitor.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly trigger automatic emergency braking or register seat belt and collision warning glitches during normal driving.
- **Solution:** Verify that all camera and radar sensors are properly calibrated, especially following any front-end repairs or windshield replacements.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi Q5 PHEV is a compelling luxury hybrid that offers brisk performance and efficient daily commuting. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for any history of water intrusion around the trunk modules, verify proper functioning of the electronics and driver assistance features, and check the panoramic sunroof for peace of mind.
