# 2022 Audi Q5 PHEV

### Overview
The 2022 Audi Q5 PHEV, a plug-in hybrid variant of the popular compact luxury SUV, aims to blend performance with efficiency. It typically features a turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an electric motor, offering a combined output that provides brisk acceleration and the ability to travel on electric power alone for shorter distances. The Q5 PHEV is built on the Volkswagen Group's MLB Evo platform, sharing its underpinnings with other Audi models. This platform generally contributes to a refined driving experience, good handling, and a solid build quality. As a PHEV, its reliability is influenced by both its internal combustion engine and its complex hybrid powertrain, including the battery system and electric motor.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi Q5 PHEV demonstrates strong reliability across its core mechanical components, including the engine, transmission, and suspension, with no widespread issues reported for these systems. The hybrid-specific components also appear robust. Overall, the Q5 PHEV offers a reliable ownership experience, with most potential issues being minor and software-related, which are often addressed under warranty or through simple fixes.
