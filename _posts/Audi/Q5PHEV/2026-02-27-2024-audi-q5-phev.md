# 2024 Audi Q5 PHEV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi Q5 PHEV brings luxurious efficiency and smooth plug-in hybrid performance to the compact luxury SUV segment. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding early-model electrical glitches, advanced driver-assistance system behaviors, and powertrain transitions. While many owners enjoy its comfort and efficiency, checking for open recall statuses on electrical and hybrid components is crucial before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Plug-In Hybrid

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs with an electric motor to deliver around 362 total system horsepower, offering brisk acceleration alongside electric efficiency. Buyers should inspect hybrid system health and ensure software updates are current.

**Powertrain Transition Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may temporarily lose accelerator response or display a drive system malfunction warning when transitioning from electric battery power to gasoline engine power.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer inspect powertrain control modules and ensure all relevant software updates are applied.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly through the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It generally operates seamlessly, though proper fluid maintenance is key to long-term durability.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

Responsible for ride height adjustment, damping comfort, and load leveling.

**Front Ride Height Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** The front end of the vehicle may sit lower than the rear after sitting, triggering an air suspension failure warning upon startup before eventually leveling out.
- **Solution:** Have the air struts, compressor, and leveling sensors inspected for leaks or electronic faults.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and MMI Display

Handles interior cabin tech, displays, and accessory power management.

**Infotainment System Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** The MMI touchscreen infotainment system may unexpectedly turn off during normal driving and require several minutes to reboot, occasionally forgetting user profiles and prompting for a PIN code.
- **Solution:** Restart the vehicle or visit a dealership to check for software patches addressing MMI stability issues.

## Safety

### Lane Keep Assist and Collision Avoidance

Manages driver-assistance features, automated emergency braking, and camera/radar sensing systems.

**Erratic Lane Correction**
- **Symptoms:** Lane Keep Assist may erroneously activate during normal driving at moderate speeds, causing unexpected steering inputs toward side streets or adjacent lanes.
- **Solution:** Have sensor calibrations and steering software checked by a certified technician.

**Collision Warning Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance and lane departure warning systems may repeatedly sound alarms and flash warnings indicating a system malfunction.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor areas and have the dealership run diagnostic scans for intermittent software glitches.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi Q5 PHEV offers a sophisticated blend of luxury, fuel economy, and plug-in capability. While powertrain performance is generally well-regarded, used-car buyers should pay close attention to reported electrical glitches, advanced driver-assistance sensor behavior, and high-voltage battery recall status. Thoroughly verifying service history and confirming that all recall remedies have been completed will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
