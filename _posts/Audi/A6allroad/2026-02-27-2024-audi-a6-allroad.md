# 2024 Audi A6 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi A6 allroad combines rugged wagon utility with luxury performance, appealing to buyers looking for versatility and high-end features. As a relatively new model year with limited owner complaints on file, prospective buyers can generally approach it with confidence, though standard luxury maintenance expectations apply.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6

Equipped with a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. This powerplant delivers smooth, responsive acceleration and solid overall reliability for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Features a responsive 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routing power to Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers quick shifts and refined operation under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi A6 allroad stands out as a sophisticated and capable luxury wagon with minimal reported trouble spots in its initial ownership period. Buyers should still prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection and review maintenance histories to ensure long-term peace of mind.
