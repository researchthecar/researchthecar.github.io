# 2020 Audi A6 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi A6 allroad, buyers will find a sophisticated luxury wagon that combines raised ground clearance with refined road manners. While the driving experience and build quality earn high marks, prospective owners should review owner-reported electrical and powertrain concerns before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (335 hp / 369 lb-ft)

The 2020 Audi A6 allroad comes standard with a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system. This powerplant delivers brisk acceleration and smooth power delivery, though the complex electrical architecture has been a focal point for some owner complaints.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed through a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive. It offers rapid gear changes and strong highway efficiency, though buyers should verify smooth operation during low-speed test drives.

## Electrical

### 48-Volt Mild Hybrid Electrical System

The electrical architecture supports advanced vehicle accessories and the mild-hybrid powertrain, but certain owner reports indicate vulnerability to sudden system warnings.

**Electrical System and Starter-Generator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report receiving sudden dashboard warnings such as 'Electrical system malfunction' and 'Starting malfunction,' which can be followed by a loss of vehicle functions or engine shutdown while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician to check the 48-volt system and starter-generator components, which may require software updates or hardware replacement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A6 allroad offers a compelling mix of luxury, utility, and performance for used-car buyers. While it generally provides a comfortable ride, prospective owners should carefully check service records, verify the status of any electrical or hybrid system updates, and ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is performed.
