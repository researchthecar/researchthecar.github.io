# 2021 Audi A6 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Audi A6 allroad, buyers are drawn to its luxurious cabin, rugged wagon versatility, and refined turbocharged performance. However, owner reports indicate potential problem areas concerning engine cooling system leaks, instrument cluster electrical glitches, and overly sensitive driver-assistance features. Reviewing these specific systems can help prospective buyers spot trouble before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine providing efficient daily driving performance, though it is less common in this wagon configuration.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

A 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine paired with a mild-hybrid system, delivering robust power and strong acceleration suitable for a luxury wagon.

**Coolant Leaks and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported low coolant warning lights, visible fluid leaks, engine overheating, and in severe cases, engine seizure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the thermostat housing, water pump, and oil cooler bracket for leaks. Replace worn cooling components and refill the system with proper coolant.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

A 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission that offers quick gear shifts paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Virtual Cockpit

Manages cabin displays, driver instrumentation, and vehicle data systems, though software glitches and module failures have been reported by owners.

**Instrument Panel Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The digital virtual cockpit display randomly turns off, reboots, chimes loudly, or displays an instrument cluster malfunction error.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a dealer or technician to check for software updates or replace failing instrument panel modules.
- **Cost:** $3,737.07

## Exterior

### Door Latches and Closures

Cabin hardware and latch mechanisms require routine inspection to ensure proper physical operation and security.

**Doors Fail to Latch Fully**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle doors require excessive force to close properly and may not latch securely upon entry, sometimes without triggering an open-door warning light.
- **Solution:** Inspect and adjust or replace the door latch mechanisms.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Pre-Sense

Includes advanced driver assistance systems like predictive control and automated emergency braking, which can occasionally exhibit overly sensitive behavior in adverse weather.

**Unintended Hard Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The Audi Pre-sense safety system can falsely trigger during heavy rain or when backing out of driveways, causing the vehicle to brake suddenly.
- **Solution:** Check for available sensor calibrations or software updates at a qualified service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect brake fluid can reduce braking ability and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the brake fluid reservoir cap and, if necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V201000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An instrument panel display that fails to show critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi A6 allroad is a sophisticated and capable luxury wagon, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the engine cooling system for signs of thermostat or bracket leaks, verify that all digital instrument displays operate smoothly without rebooting, and test the driver-assistance features to ensure they are properly calibrated.
