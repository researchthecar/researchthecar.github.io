# 2022 Audi A6 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A6 allroad combines rugged wagon styling with luxury amenities and standard quattro all-wheel drive. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern engineering and advanced technology, though used car buyers should still evaluate vehicle maintenance history and remaining factory warranty coverage.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (335 hp / 369 lb-ft)

This turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 engine produces 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers strong, smooth acceleration and solid overall durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

A 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power to the standard quattro all-wheel drive system. It provides rapid gear shifts and refined highway cruising behavior.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2022 Audi A6 allroad is a sophisticated luxury wagon with strong performance and versatile capability. Because complaint data is sparse for this model year, buyers should focus on verifying a complete service history and conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection.
