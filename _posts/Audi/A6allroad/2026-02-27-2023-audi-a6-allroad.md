# 2023 Audi A6 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A6 allroad combines rugged wagon styling with luxury performance and standard Quattro all-wheel drive. As a relatively new luxury model, it offers buyers sophisticated tech and strong turbocharged power. When shopping for a used unit, review service history and verify remaining factory warranty coverage.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (335 hp / 369 lb-ft)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It delivers smooth, rapid acceleration suitable for both highway cruising and foul-weather driving with solid long-term reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs standard with Quattro all-wheel drive. Regular fluid maintenance is essential to ensure smooth shifting and longevity over high mileage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6+allroad&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi A6 allroad remains a compelling choice for luxury wagon buyers seeking versatility and performance. While current owner reports are minimal, prospective buyers should still perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and confirm service records before finalizing a purchase.
