# 2024 Audi Q8 e-tron Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi Q8 e-tron Sportback delivers a striking design, upscale interior, and smooth electric performance. As a relatively new luxury EV option on the pre-owned market, prospective buyers should carefully review vehicle history records and ensure all software updates have been completed.

---

## Engine

### Dual Motor Electric Propulsion System

The Q8 e-tron Sportback utilizes a dual-motor, all-wheel-drive electric propulsion setup generating approximately 355 horsepower and 414 lb-ft of torque (or up to 402 horsepower in boost mode). It offers brisk acceleration and refined cruising, though long-term battery degradation and high-voltage electrical performance remain key areas for buyer inspection.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is sent to all four wheels via a fixed-ratio, single-speed automatic transmission designed for seamless, shift-free acceleration. This direct-drive system is mechanically straightforward and generally trouble-free.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V428000: Service Brakes, Air:Supply:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8+e-tron&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Reduced braking ability can cause longer brake pedal travel or extend the distance required to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the brake pressure line as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi Q8 e-tron Sportback is a sophisticated luxury electric crossover with strong performance and modern tech. Because owner reporting data is still developing for this model year, buyers should prioritize certified pre-owned options, verify remaining factory warranty coverage, and perform a thorough inspection of the high-voltage electrical systems before purchasing.
