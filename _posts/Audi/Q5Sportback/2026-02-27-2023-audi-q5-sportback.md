# 2023 Audi Q5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Audi Q5 Sportback, buyers can expect a stylish and tech-forward luxury crossover coupe. Because this model year is relatively new, owner feedback is still developing, though early driver reports highlight a few electronic quirks and advanced safety system sensitivities.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

The standard 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and refined daily driveability for most owners.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts paired with quattro all-wheel drive. While generally smooth, owners should watch for occasional electronic powertrain messages.

**Transmission Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden loss of acceleration accompanied by a dashboard warning requiring the vehicle to be safely stopped.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic scanning by a dealership or qualified mechanic to identify underlying software or mechatronic faults.

## Brakes

### Electronic Parking Brake

Issues related to the electronic parking brake system can occasionally immobilize the vehicle on inclines, requiring specialized service assistance.

**Parking Brake Failure to Disengage**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic parking brake may fail to release when shifting from park into gear after being parked on an incline, sometimes triggering brake failure warnings.
- **Solution:** Vehicle may require professional towing to a service center for diagnostic inspection and system resetting.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Digital Dash

Cabin electronics and digital displays offer modern functionality but have exhibited occasional startup delays and connectivity drops in early owner reports.

**Display Screen and Connectivity Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The digital dashboard or central infotainment screens may fail to power on promptly at startup, or smartphone integration may repeatedly disconnect during navigation and calls.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets performed by an authorized service center can often resolve intermittent display and connectivity glitches.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features enhance highway safety, though isolated reports indicate overly sensitive collision avoidance reactions.

**Inadvertent Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision detection system may falsely interpret traffic situations and unexpectedly engage automatic emergency braking, causing abrupt deceleration.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians check sensor calibration and inspect for software updates addressing sensor sensitivity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V753000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5+Sportback&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The engine connecting rod bearings may become damaged, which can result in engine failure. A damaged engine can result in engine failure and a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace an affected engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V294000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5+Sportback&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The screws can loosen, allowing oil to leak and increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the screws as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi Q5 Sportback blends luxury appeal with strong performance, but prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the electronics, parking brake functionality, and driver assistance systems during a test drive. Verifying that all software updates have been applied can help mitigate these reported early-model concerns.
