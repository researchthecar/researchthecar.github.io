# 2022 Audi Q5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi Q5 Sportback combines coupe-like styling with the practical luxury of a compact crossover. For prospective used-car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and general owner sentiment is crucial for a confident purchase. While formal complaint data remains sparse, evaluating standard mechanical configurations and keeping an eye on routine luxury vehicle maintenance will help guide your inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

The standard 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It offers a smooth power delivery suited for daily commuting while maintaining competitive fuel efficiency for its class.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid (55 TFSI e)

The plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with an electric motor to generate a combined 362 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This option delivers brisk acceleration alongside electric-only driving capability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term shifting smoothness.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V368000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5+Sportback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V947000: Electrical System:Body Control Module/Bcm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5+Sportback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover on the gateway control module and seal an underbody seam as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5+Sportback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V753000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5+Sportback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The engine connecting rod bearings may become damaged, which can result in engine failure. A damaged engine can result in engine failure and a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace an affected engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V294000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q5+Sportback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The screws can loosen, allowing oil to leak and increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the screws as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
