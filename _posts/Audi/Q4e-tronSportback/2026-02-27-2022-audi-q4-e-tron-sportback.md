# 2022 Audi Q4 e-tron Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi Q4 e-tron Sportback is a stylish, all-electric crossover that brings luxury and everyday usability together. Because available complaint data for this model year is minimal, prospective buyers should focus on standard pre-owned inspection practices, ensuring software updates are current and checking the high-voltage battery health.

---

## Engine

### Q4 e-tron 40 Electric Motor

The single-motor rear-wheel-drive powertrain produces approximately 201 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque. It offers efficient everyday performance and generally dependable operation in early owner reports.

### Q4 e-tron 50 / 55 quattro Dual Motor

The dual-motor all-wheel-drive powertrain delivers around 295 horsepower, providing brisk acceleration and confident traction. This setup forms the core of the higher-tier performance options for the lineup.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

A smooth single-speed direct-drive automatic transmission routes power seamlessly to the wheels without traditional gear shifts. It features a straightforward mechanical layout known for low maintenance needs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V823000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q4+e-tron&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V189000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q4+e-tron&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** During deployment, the head curtain air bag could deform the door seal and expose the metal inlay within the seal, which could tear the air bag. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." In the event of a crash, a torn air bag cannot protect occupants as intended.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install cloth tape in the driver and front passenger B-pillar area, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q4+e-tron&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V361000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q4+e-tron&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrect headlight control module software may cause the parking lights not to operate as intended when the headlights are turned on and the turn signals are activated, which can reduce.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the headlight control module with a new data set, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V120000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q4+e-tron&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The instrument panel may not properly display the transmission gear lever indicator. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 102, "Transmission Shift Lever Sequence, Starter Interlock, and Transmission Braking Effect." Failure to display the gear position may result in a vehicle rollaway.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the brake control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
