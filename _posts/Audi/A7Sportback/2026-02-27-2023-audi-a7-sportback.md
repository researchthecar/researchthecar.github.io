# 2023 Audi A7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A7 Sportback combines striking fastback styling with sophisticated luxury and robust performance options. As a modern luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should focus on proper maintenance history and electronic system checks when evaluating a used model.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing strong, smooth acceleration. It represents the primary mainstream powerplant for the A7 lineup, blending refined daily driveability with modern mild-hybrid efficiency.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine delivers around 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque as an efficient base option. It offers balanced performance and respectable fuel economy for everyday commuting.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power smoothly through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes and responsive performance suited to the sportback's dynamic character.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi A7 Sportback remains an appealing choice for used luxury buyers looking for advanced technology and distinctive design. Prioritizing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verifying service records will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
