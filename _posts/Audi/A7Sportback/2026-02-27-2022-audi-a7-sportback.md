# 2022 Audi A7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A7 Sportback combines striking design, luxury, and advanced technology in a sleek four-door coupe package. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and keep in mind that owner experiences can vary regarding vehicle electronics and equipment.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (TFSI)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering strong, smooth acceleration. It provides a refined driving experience typical of luxury performance sedans.

### 2.0L Turbo 4-Cylinder (TFSI)

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generating approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque serves as an efficient base option. It balances responsive daily driveability with competitive fuel economy.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers a sporty yet composed feel during normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V898000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A high-voltage battery that overheats increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software as the final remedy, anticipated to be available in the second quarter of 2025. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
