**Introduction**

The 2020 Audi A7 Sportback is a stylish and sporty liftback that combines luxury and performance. It is powered by a standard 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine that produces 340 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a standard 7-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine and a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 engine. The A7 Sportback is praised for its sleek design, comfortable interior, and impressive performance. However, it has also been reported to have some issues, which are detailed below.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Turbocharged V6 Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors can fail, causing the engine to misfire, run rough, and have reduced power.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to slip and the engine to lose power.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Engine**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine**
    * **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The valve stem seals can fail, causing the engine to burn oil and produce blue or white smoke from the exhaust.
        * Solution: Replace the valve stem seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Slippage:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears, causing the car to lose power or jerk.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may fail, causing the car to lose power, the lights to turn off, or the dashboard to malfunction.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the car to have difficulty starting or preventing it from starting at all.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof drain plugs can clog, causing water to leak into the cabin.
    * Solution: Unclog the drain plugs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the car to be too hot or too cold.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **MMI System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The MMI infotainment system may lag or freeze, making it difficult to use.
    * Solution: Update the MMI software or replace the MMI unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Audi A7 Sportback is a reliable and enjoyable car, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported are related to the engine, including fuel injector failure, timing chain tensioner failure, and valve stem seal failure. Transmission issues, such as transmission slippage, have also been reported. Electrical problems, such as electrical system failure and battery drain, can also occur. Interior issues include climate control malfunction and MMI system lag. Exterior issues are relatively minor, with the most common being sunroof drain plugs clogging. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but it is important to be aware of them before purchasing an A7 Sportback.
