# 2019 Audi A7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi A7 Sportback delivers striking fastback styling, a luxurious cabin, and advanced technology paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. However, used car buyers should approach this model year with care, as early examples of this generation exhibit notable electrical, powertrain, and cooling system complaints. Reviewing service histories for resolved TSBs and previous component replacements is highly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

Equipped with a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. While generally smooth and capable, early production models can experience cooling system and electrical integration glitches.

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may overheat unexpectedly, illuminating warning lights on the dashboard during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Have the cooling system inspected and replace the failed water pump with updated OEM components.

**Engine Stalling and Starting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may stall unexpectedly while driving accompanied by engine malfunction warnings, fuel pump errors, or starter battery failure.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electrical charging and fuel delivery systems; replace faulty starter batteries, belt starter generators, or fuel pumps as required.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Features a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with Quattro all-wheel drive. It offers lightning-fast gear changes, though some owners report low-speed throttle lag or shifting harshness.

**Throttle Lag and Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a noticeable delay or hesitation when pressing the accelerator, or unexpected jerking sensations during take-off.
- **Solution:** Check for available transmission control module software updates or consult a specialist to inspect clutch engagement adaptation.

## Electrical

### 48V Mild Hybrid System & Electronics

The electrical architecture relies heavily on complex software and a 48-volt mild-hybrid setup, which has been a frequent source of sudden warning lights and charging faults.

**Belt Starter Generator (BSG) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Electrical components begin to fault sequentially, multiple warning lamps illuminate on the dashboard, and the vehicle may ultimately shut off or fail to restart.
- **Solution:** Replace the failing belt starter generator unit and ensure relevant software flashes are up to date.

### Backup Camera and Pre-Sense Systems

Advanced safety systems and driver aids provide great security when functioning, but intermittent glitches in cameras and collision-avoidance sensors can impair daily usability.

**Intermittent Camera and Autonomous Braking Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display may go blank intermittently, or driver assistance features may inappropriately trigger sudden braking maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Perform manufacturer software updates and inspect camera wiring harnesses or sensor calibration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect brake fluid can reduce braking ability and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the brake fluid reservoir cap and, if necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V125000: Lane Departure:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The driver may be unaware that the lane departure warning system is deactivated and rely on a system that is not functional, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi A7 Sportback is a technologically advanced luxury cruiser that impresses with its performance and style. However, prospective buyers should carefully verify the vehicle's maintenance history, specifically checking whether the 48V mild hybrid starter-generator, water pump, and relevant software updates have been addressed. Prioritizing a pre-purchase inspection by an independent Audi specialist can help avoid costly out-of-pocket electrical and cooling repairs.
