# 2021 Audi A7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Audi A7 Sportback, buyers can expect a striking combination of luxury, advanced technology, and refined performance. However, reviewing owner-reported feedback helps highlight potential electronic and powertrain quirks that are worth keeping on your radar during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Turbocharged

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces roughly 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering strong, smooth acceleration. Buyers should monitor for reported cooling system leaks and electronic drivability hiccups during inspections.

**Coolant Leak and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** A water pump design failure can allow coolant to leak into the vacuum lines, preventing proper cooling and leading to immediate engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and vacuum lines for signs of leakage, and replace the faulty water pump assembly if compromised.

**Sudden Deceleration**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine deceleration while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by a brief unresponsive period when pressing the gas pedal.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning for powertrain control module codes and software updates may be required.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission comes paired with quattro all-wheel drive, delivering lightning-fast gear changes and efficient power delivery. Regular fluid maintenance is essential to preserving long-term shift quality.

## Interior

### Virtual Cockpit and Dashboard

Advanced digital displays and complex wiring networks power the vehicle's cabin features, though occasional software glitches and warning lights have been noted by owners.

**Virtual Cockpit Blackout**
- **Symptoms:** The digital dashboard display goes completely black except for a warning exclamation point, causing a temporary loss of visibility for speed and engine temperature.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer or a specialist inspect instrument cluster software and wiring harnesses for faults.

**Electrical System Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** A yellow electrical system warning light illuminates, which can escalate to a red warning light and precede a total loss of vehicle electrical functions.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the charging system, battery health, and primary control modules immediately upon warning activation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect brake fluid can reduce braking ability and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the brake fluid reservoir cap and, if necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi A7 Sportback remains a compelling luxury fastback choice, provided prospective buyers thoroughly verify the condition of its cooling system and complex electronics. A comprehensive inspection by an Audi specialist is strongly recommended prior to purchase.
