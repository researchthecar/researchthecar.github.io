# 2020 Audi A7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Audi A7 Sportback blends stunning fastback styling with sophisticated technology and robust performance. For prospective used car buyers, understanding both powertrain capabilities and minor reported user concerns helps ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (335 hp)

This turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system. It offers brisk acceleration and refined power delivery suitable for daily driving and highway cruising.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission sends power smoothly to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It delivers crisp gear shifts and dependable everyday operation.

## Electrical

### Charging Pad

Owner reports indicate minor convenience system quirks, particularly with accessory charging functions.

**Wireless Phone Charger Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** The built-in wireless charging pad causes connected smartphones to overheat and enter thermal protection mode.
- **Solution:** Avoid using the built-in wireless charging pad if overheating persists, or utilize a traditional wired USB connection for charging.

### Mild Hybrid Electrical System

Electrical architecture relies on complex mild-hybrid technology that should be thoroughly evaluated during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Electrical System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden electrical system failures while driving, potentially triggering multiple warning lights and requiring a safe pull-over and tow.
- **Solution:** Have the 48-volt mild-hybrid electrical components and battery modules inspected and diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V159000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The score line on the passenger air bag may not have been manufactured correctly. An incorrectly manufactured air bag may not deploy properly, and could also eject plastic debris into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the passenger air bag score line, and repair it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V274000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The passenger air bag may not properly deploy through the instrument panel tear seam. In the event of a crash, the passenger air bag may not properly deploy and inflate, and small plastic parts may enter the cabin, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the instrument panels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V601000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect brake fluid can reduce braking ability and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the brake fluid reservoir cap and, if necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A7 Sportback is a luxurious and capable grand tourer with generally favorable owner feedback. Buyers should verify proper operation of the advanced electrical and mild-hybrid systems during a test drive to ensure trouble-free ownership.
