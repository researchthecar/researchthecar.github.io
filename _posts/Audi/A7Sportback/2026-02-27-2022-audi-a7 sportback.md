# 2022 Audi A7 Sportback

### Overview
The 2022 Audi A7 Sportback is a luxury four-door coupe that blends striking design with advanced technology and performance. As a premium vehicle, it generally exhibits a high level of build quality and engineering. Reliability data for the 2022 model year, being relatively new, is still emerging, but initial reports suggest a solid performance. Owners can expect a refined driving experience, with the A7 offering a comfortable and technologically advanced cabin. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the A7 Sportback aims to provide a dependable ownership experience, focusing on sophisticated powertrain options and a well-integrated electronic system. Maintenance will be typical for a luxury vehicle, with a focus on premium fluids and parts.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi A7 Sportback demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain and core mechanical components. The engine and transmission options, including the 2.0L Turbo and 3.0L V6, along with the 7-speed S tronic, have shown no significant widespread issues. The suspension and exterior/interior components also appear robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential sensor malfunctions for driver assistance features. These are generally manageable with software updates or component recalibration/replacement. Overall, the A7 Sportback is a dependable luxury vehicle, with its reliability rating being positively influenced by its sophisticated engineering and build quality.
