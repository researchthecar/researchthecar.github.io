# 2024 Audi A7 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi A7 Sportback combines striking design, high-end luxury, and advanced technology in a sleek four-door package. As a relatively new luxury model on the used market, it benefits from contemporary engineering and refined powertrain options, though buyers should still carefully inspect individual vehicle condition and service histories.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It offers a balance of efficient daily driving performance and smooth power delivery.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine generates around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, featuring mild-hybrid technology. It provides robust acceleration and a refined driving character suited to the luxury grand tourer profile.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission pairs with Quattro all-wheel drive to deliver rapid, seamless gear changes. It supports both spirited driving and relaxed highway cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi A7 Sportback presents a compelling option for used luxury car buyers seeking sophisticated style and strong performance. Because available owner complaint data is minimal for this model year, prospective buyers should focus on a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verification of maintenance records.
