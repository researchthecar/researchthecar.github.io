# 2018 Audi A6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Audi A6, buyers can expect a refined executive sedan offering sophisticated engineering and a luxurious cabin. While formal owner complaints and safety reports for this model year are limited, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate service history, electronic systems, and any open safety campaigns during their pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing balanced daily efficiency and punchy performance. It is a generally dependable powerplant, though routine maintenance of the turbocharger and ignition components is essential.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 delivers roughly 340 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque for robust, seamless acceleration. This engine is well-regarded for its longevity, but buyers should monitor cooling system integrity and supercharger belt condition as the mileage climbs.

### Electrical & Fuel System

Electrical and fuel delivery systems generally operate reliably, though isolated incidents highlight the importance of inspecting wiring harnesses and fuel lines.

**Unexplained Vehicle Fire**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the vehicle spontaneously caught fire near the rear gas tank area while parked and turned off for several hours.
- **Solution:** Thoroughly inspect the vehicle's fuel lines, electrical harnesses, and undercarriage components during a pre-purchase inspection for any signs of wear or previous damage.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission offers rapid, seamless gear changes paired with Quattro all-wheel drive. Regular fluid changes are critical to ensuring smooth engagement and long-term mechanical durability.

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed traditional automatic transmission provides refined shifting dynamics suited for front-wheel drive and select all-wheel drive configurations. It is robustly built, though timely maintenance prevents rough shifting over time.

## Safety

### Airbags

Safety equipment is comprehensive, but buyers must verify that all manufacturer safety campaigns and airbag-related recalls have been successfully resolved.

**Recall Part Availability Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience difficulty scheduling or completing required safety recall repairs due to reported shortages of replacement parts.
- **Solution:** Check the NHTSA database using the vehicle's VIN to verify open safety recalls and confirm part availability with a certified dealer before finalizing purchase.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V035000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi A6 remains a compelling choice for used luxury sedan buyers thanks to its powerful engine lineup and upscale driving dynamics. Although isolated severe incidents and recall part delays have been noted by individual owners, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verification of service records will help ensure a confident buying experience.
