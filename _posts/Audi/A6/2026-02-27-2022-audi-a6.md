# 2022 Audi A6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A6 delivers a refined luxury driving experience with advanced technology and a sophisticated cabin. When shopping for a used model, understanding the available powertrain options and monitoring common owner-reported areas can help ensure a satisfying purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient daily performance. It provides a balanced entry point into the A6 lineup with solid overall reliability when properly maintained.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine generates around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is paired with a mild-hybrid system and is generally dependable, though routine upkeep is essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel drive or Quattro all-wheel drive configurations, offering rapid and precise gear changes. It delivers a sporty driving dynamic while maintaining highway cruising efficiency.

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

This traditional 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission is typically paired with the V6 engine and Quattro all-wheel drive. It offers smooth, refined shifting characteristics suited to luxury touring.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V368000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V861000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A gateway control module shutdown will suddenly reduce engine power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V155000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge:Fuel Level Float/Sensor/Sending Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An inaccurate fuel reading may cause an engine stall due to lack of fuel, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new sensor in the tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
