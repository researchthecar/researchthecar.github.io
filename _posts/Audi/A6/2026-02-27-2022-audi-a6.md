# 2022 Audi A6

### Overview
The 2022 Audi A6 is a luxury sedan known for its refined driving experience, advanced technology, and premium interior. As a mid-cycle model, it benefits from Audi's continuous refinement of its platforms. Reliability for the 2022 model year is generally strong, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality and engineering. Owners typically report a positive ownership experience with fewer unexpected issues compared to some competitors. However, like any complex vehicle, specific components can occasionally present challenges, though widespread problems are uncommon. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining key systems across its available powertrains.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi A6 demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The 2.0L and 3.0L turbocharged engines, along with the 7-speed S tronic transmission, are robust and have not shown significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are with the optional Adaptive Air Suspension, which can experience compressor malfunctions, and occasional software glitches within the infotainment system. Electrical components, such as the 12V battery, can also be a point of failure, though this is not unique to the A6. Overall, the 2022 A6 offers a dependable luxury vehicle experience with a strong track record for its main systems.
