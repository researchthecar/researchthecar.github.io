**2015 Audi A6 Overview**

The 2015 Audi A6 is a luxury sedan known for its refined performance, advanced technology, and sophisticated design. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* **Engine Options:**
    * 2.0T Inline-4 (220 hp, 258 lb-ft)
    * 3.0T Supercharged V6 (333 hp, 325 lb-ft)
    * 3.0 TDI Clean Diesel V6 (240 hp, 428 lb-ft)
* **Transmission Options:**
    * 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission
    * 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission (3.0T only)

While the A6 generally receives praise for its exceptional driving experience, it has also been prone to various issues.

**Engine**

**2.0T Inline-4**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rattling or knocking noises
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0T Supercharged V6**

* **Supercharger Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replacement of supercharger actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0 TDI Clean Diesel V6**

* **Emissions Control System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Illuminated "Check Engine" light, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of emissions control components (e.g., EGR valve, DPF filter)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: 19V-095 and 19V-306

**Transmission**

**8-speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or jerking, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
    * Recalls: 19V-306

**7-speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Clutch Pack Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes, loss of drive
    * Solution: Replacement of clutch pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension lowering unexpectedly, reduced handling and stability
    * Solution: Replacement of air suspension pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Inspection and replacement of faulty electrical components (e.g., alternator, wiring)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Resealing or replacement of headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow or unresponsive infotainment system display
    * Solution: Software update or reset
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not functioning properly
    * Solution: Inspection and repair of climate control components (e.g., compressor, actuators)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Audi A6 is a capable luxury sedan, but it has faced various issues that can be costly to repair. The severity and cost of these problems can range from minor inconveniences like infotainment lag to major expenses like transmission failures.
