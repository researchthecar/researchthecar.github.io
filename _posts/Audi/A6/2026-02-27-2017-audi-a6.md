# 2017 Audi A6

### Overview
The 2017 Audi A6 is a luxury sedan known for its refined driving experience, premium interior, and advanced technology. As a mid-size executive car, it generally offers a good balance of performance and comfort. Reliability for the 2017 model year is generally considered strong, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, like any complex vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention than others. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TFSI (Turbocharged Direct Injection)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level checks required, potential blue smoke from exhaust under acceleration.
- **Solution:** Piston ring replacement, PCV valve update.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


### 3.0L TFSI (Supercharged V6)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, check engine light, potential oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### 7-Speed S tronic (Dual-Clutch - AWD)
**Mechatronic Unit Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,500


## Suspension

### Standard/Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, compressor running excessively, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or lines, compressor repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**MMI Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio cutting out, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, control module reset or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000


### Lighting Systems
**LED Headlight Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or complete failure of LED headlights, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of headlight control modules or LED units.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500 per side

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi A6 generally presents a solid reliability record, particularly for its powertrain and core components. Owners should be aware of potential issues with oil consumption on the 2.0L TFSI engine and mechatronic unit problems in the S tronic dual-clutch transmission. The adaptive air suspension, while offering superior comfort, can be a source of costly repairs if leaks develop. Electrical gremlins, primarily related to the MMI infotainment system and LED headlights, are also noted but often resolvable with software updates or module replacements. Overall, the A6 is a dependable luxury vehicle, but proactive maintenance and awareness of these specific potential issues can help mitigate unexpected costs.
