# 2017 Audi A6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Audi A6, buyers will find a refined and technologically sophisticated luxury sedan. While many owners enjoy its strong performance and upscale interior, reviewing owner-reported complaints highlights a few specific areas—particularly concerning steering components and engine hardware—that warrant careful inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces roughly 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced mix of efficiency and daily drivability. Buyers should inspect closely for fluid leaks and cooling system performance during pre-purchase evaluations.

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

This 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine generates approximately 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration. While generally capable, prospective buyers should monitor for cooling system and engine mount wear.

**Engine Mount and Cooling Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported hydraulic fluid leaks from failed fluid-damped engine mounts, causing the motor to sit low, alongside thermostat and water pump failures leading to engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replace the collapsed hydraulic engine mounts and faulty thermostat or water pump assemblies.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with Quattro all-wheel drive on 2.0T models, delivering rapid and smooth gear changes. Routine fluid maintenance is essential for long-term reliability.

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

This traditional torque-converter automatic transmission comes standard on 3.0T Quattro models, offering refined shifts and durability. It remains a solid option for everyday luxury commuting.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Control Module

The electronic power steering system provides responsive handling under normal conditions, but steering rack failures have been documented by owners.

**Power Steering Failure and Rack Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden loss of power steering assistance, locked steering wheels, and recurring error codes populating from the power steering control module.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering rack and control module, and replace faulty assemblies as necessary.

## Electrical

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Driver assistance features add modern convenience and protection, though sensor reliability can occasionally be inconsistent.

**Intermittent Blind Spot and Lane Departure Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger-side blind spot warning and lane departure systems occasionally fail to detect surrounding vehicles, displaying intermittent sensor warnings.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan for diagnostic trouble codes and recalibrate or replace affected radar sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V035000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V955000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Additionally, certain 2017 Audi Q7, A4 Sedan, A4 Allroad, and 2018 Audi Q5 vehicles are being recalled because the seat belt pretensioners may not activate properly. In the event of a crash, if the air bags and/or the seat belt pretensioners do not inflate or function properly, the vehicle occupants have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen and Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected air bags and seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi A6 delivers strong luxury performance, comfortable handling, and versatile powertrain choices. However, used car buyers should pay close attention to potential steering rack concerns and engine mount or cooling system wear during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid unexpected repair bills.
