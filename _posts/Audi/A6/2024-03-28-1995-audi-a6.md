**Introduction**

The 1995 Audi A6 offers a combination of refinement and performance, making it a desirable choice among premium sedans. It is powered by a 2.8-liter V6 engine that produces 172 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine with 138 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.6-liter V6 with 158 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, both paired with a 5-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

**2.8-liter V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leakage, rough idling, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Belt Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screeching or whining noise from the engine, timing belt skipping or breaking
    * Solution: Replace timing belt tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.6-liter V6**

No Issues Found

**2.0-liter 4-cylinder**

No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or not shifting properly
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Manual**

No Issues Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when driving over bumps, reduced steering response
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Tail Light Circuit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both tail lights not working
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace tail light circuit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Fogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation inside headlight lenses
    * Solution: Reseat headlights or replace seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Trim Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from dashboard, especially on rough roads
    * Solution: Tighten or replace loose dashboard trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1995 Audi A6 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it does have some potential issues to look out for. The most common problems are related to the engine (specifically the 2.8-liter V6) and suspension. While some of these issues can be costly to fix, the A6 remains a well-regarded vehicle for its combination of luxury and performance.
