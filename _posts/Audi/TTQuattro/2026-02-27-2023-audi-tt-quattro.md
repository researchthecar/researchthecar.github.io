# 2023 Audi TT Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi TT Quattro delivers sporty performance and sharp handling wrapped in a timeless design, paired with standard all-wheel drive. As a modern luxury sports car, prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate its powertrain configurations and overall condition during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure long-term mechanical health.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It is generally regarded as a robust powerplant when maintained with timely oil changes and quality fluids.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and power to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid service is vital to maintaining smooth operation over high mileage.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=TT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi TT Quattro remains an engaging and stylish choice for used sports car buyers. Routine maintenance and a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help secure a trouble-free ownership experience.
