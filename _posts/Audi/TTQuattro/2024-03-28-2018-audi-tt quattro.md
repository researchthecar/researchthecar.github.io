**Introduction to the 2018 Audi TT Quattro**

The 2018 Audi TT Quattro is a sports car available with a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes with a standard 6-speed manual transmission and an optional 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (S tronic). The TT Quattro is appreciated for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and stylish design, but criticized for its small back seat and limited trunk space.

**Engine**
- **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
    - **Turbocharger failure:** Symptoms include reduced engine power, increased oil consumption, and excessive smoke from the exhaust. The turbocharger may need to be replaced.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    - **Fuel injector failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting, rough idling, and decreased fuel efficiency. The faulty injector(s) may need to be replaced.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    - **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, especially at startup. The tensioner may need to be replaced.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
- **No Recalls Found**

**Transmission**
- **6-speed Manual**
    - **Grinding gears:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting or grinding noises when shifting gears. The transmission may need to be repaired or replaced.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    - **Clutch failure:** Symptoms include slipping or shuddering when engaging the clutch. The clutch may need to be replaced.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
- **7-speed S tronic**
    - **Shift solenoid failure:** Symptoms include harsh shifting or difficulty shifting gears. The solenoid may need to be replaced.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - **Mechatronic unit failure:** Symptoms include loss of power, transmission slipping, or inability to shift gears. The mechatronic unit may need to be replaced.
      - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
- **No Recalls Found**

**Suspension**
- **Strut failure:** Symptoms include excessive bouncing, uneven tire wear, and poor handling. The struts may need to be replaced.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
- **Control arm bushing failure:** Symptoms include clunking or rattling noises when going over bumps. The bushings may need to be replaced.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
- **No Recalls Found**

**Electrical**
- **Battery drain:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the car or a dead battery after a period of time. The cause may need to be diagnosed and repaired, such as a faulty alternator or parasitic drain.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
- **Electrical harness issues:** Symptoms may vary depending on the harness affected, but could include problems with lighting, gauges, or electronic systems. The harness may need to be repaired or replaced.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
- **No Recalls Found**

**Exterior**
- **Headlight condensation:** Symptoms include moisture accumulation inside the headlight housing. The headlight may need to be resealed.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
- **No Recalls Found**

**Interior**
- **Infotainment system glitches:** Symptoms include crashing, freezing, or unresponsive touchscreen. The software may need to be updated or the system may need to be reset.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
- **Trim panel rattles:** Symptoms include annoying noises coming from the dashboard or door panels. The panels may need to be adjusted or secured.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
- **No Recalls Found**

**Summary**

The 2018 Audi TT Quattro is a performance-oriented sports car with a few potential issues to watch out for. Engine problems, such as turbocharger failures and fuel injector issues, can be costly to repair. Transmission issues, particularly with the 7-speed S tronic, are also a concern. Electrical problems and suspension components may also require attention. However, the overall severity and cost of these issues are not excessive, and the TT Quattro remains a desirable choice for those seeking a stylish and enjoyable driving experience.
