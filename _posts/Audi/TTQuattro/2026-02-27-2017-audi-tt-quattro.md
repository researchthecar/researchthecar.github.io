# 2017 Audi TT Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi TT Quattro blends sharp sports car handling, upscale interior tech, and standard all-wheel drive into a stylish package. While it offers engaging performance for daily driving or weekend cruising, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history and maintenance records as these complex German sports cars age.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides strong acceleration and respectable efficiency, though routine maintenance and timely fluid changes are critical for long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs standard with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid services are essential to maintain smooth operation over time.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V076000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=TT&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the fuel tank may become damaged by the fuel tank heat shield bracket . A damaged fuel tank may leak fuel, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a protective cap on the fuel tank heat shield bracket, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi TT Quattro is a compelling used luxury sports car choice for buyers seeking style and performance. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by an Audi specialist is strongly recommended to ensure all mechanical and electrical systems are in good working order.
