# 2018 Audi TT Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi TT Quattro delivers sharp handling, distinctive styling, and punchy turbocharged performance backed by a sure-footed all-wheel-drive system. For used-car buyers, this luxury sports car offers an engaging daily drive and high-end cabin tech, though it requires diligent maintenance. Reviewing common owner feedback helps highlight what to watch out for during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering lively acceleration and reasonable efficiency. It is generally robust when timely oil changes are performed, but routine monitoring of cooling and ignition components is recommended.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 6-speed S-Tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and power delivery matched to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Prospective buyers should test drive the vehicle thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement and proper shifting behavior across all modes.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V076000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=TT&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the fuel tank may become damaged by the fuel tank heat shield bracket . A damaged fuel tank may leak fuel, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a protective cap on the fuel tank heat shield bracket, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi TT Quattro is a compelling option for buyers seeking a stylish and sporty luxury coupe or roadster. While formal owner complaints are sparse for this model year, maintaining a disciplined service history is key to avoiding costly unexpected repairs. Always conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection before finalizing your purchase.
