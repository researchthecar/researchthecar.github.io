**Introduction**

The 2000 Audi TT Quattro is a compact sports car that offers a blend of performance and style. It features a turbocharged 1.8L engine that produces 225 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque, paired with a quattro all-wheel drive system. Other engine options include a naturally aspirated 1.8L engine with 180 horsepower and 173 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual and a 6-speed automatic. The TT Quattro is praised for its agile handling, responsive engine, and stylish design. However, it has also been criticized for its cramped interior and high cost of ownership.

**Engine**

**1.8L Turbocharged**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Runner Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, idling issues, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runner control
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.8L Naturally Aspirated**

* No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Synchro Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting into certain gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace synchronizer rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, erratic shifting, no engagement
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises from the suspension, misalignment
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $450

**Electrical**

* **Window Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working, intermittent operation
    * Solution: Replace window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
* **Ignition Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the car, no power to accessories
    * Solution: Replace ignition switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery discharge
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on door handles, difficulty opening doors
    * Solution: Replace door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Display Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering display, inaccurate temperature readings
    * Solution: Replace climate control display
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $450
* **Heater Core Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No heat from the vents, foul odor
    * Solution: Replace heater core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800

**Summary**

The 2000 Audi TT Quattro is a reliable and enjoyable sports car but it is not without its issues. The most common problems are related to the turbocharger, transmission, and suspension. These issues can be costly to repair, but the overall ownership experience is generally positive.
