**Introduction**
The 2023 Audi TT Quattro is a compact sports car available with a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine and a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The engine produces 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft. of torque and achieves a 0-60 mph time of 5.3 seconds. Despite its impressive performance, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Other**
**Recalls**
NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V934000
Component: Brake System, Hydraulic
Description: The rear brake calipers may have been insufficiently bled by the factory, potentially leaving residual air in the system that could reduce braking effectiveness. Audi will bleed the rear brakes for free.

**Summary**
The 2023 Audi TT Quattro has not reported any significant issues across its major components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior systems. However, there has been one recall for the brake system. Overall, the vehicle seems to be reliable, but it is essential to be aware of the potential issues before making a purchase.
