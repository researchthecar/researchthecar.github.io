**Introduction:**

The 2016 Audi TT Quattro is a compact sports car powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a six-speed manual transmission or a six-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (S tronic). The TT Quattro is praised for its sharp handling, stylish design, and premium interior, but it has also faced criticism for its cramped rear seats and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Fuel Pump Failure:** The fuel pump can fail unexpectedly, causing the engine to stall or misfire. **Symptoms:** Loss of power, difficulty starting, rough engine idle. **Solution:** Replace the fuel pump. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to become loose and skip, which can result in catastrophic engine damage. **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine, loss of power, misfiring. **Solution:** Replace the timing chain and tensioner. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2500+ **Recalls:** NHTSA Recall Number: 20V-437, 19V-167

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Six-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic (S tronic)**

* **Clutch Pack Failure:** The clutch packs in the transmission can fail, causing slipping, shuddering, or difficulty shifting gears. **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting, hesitation during acceleration, loss of power. **Solution:** Replace the clutch pack. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2000+ **Recalls:** NHTSA Recall Number: 20V-437

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle. **Symptoms:** Dead battery, slow cranking, flickering lights. **Solution:** Inspect the electrical system for parasitic drains and replace the battery if necessary. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture can accumulate inside the headlights, causing condensation and reduced visibility. **Symptoms:** Foggy or cloudy headlights. **Solution:** Replace the headlight assembly. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1000+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Freezing:** The infotainment system can freeze or become unresponsive, requiring a restart. **Symptoms:** Black screen, delayed response, frozen controls. **Solution:** Update the software or replace the infotainment unit. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2000+

**Summary:**

The 2016 Audi TT Quattro is a well-rounded sports car with impressive performance and handling. However, it has faced some issues with the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior components. The most severe issues include fuel pump failure, timing chain tensioner failure, and clutch pack failure, which can result in expensive repairs. Overall, the TT Quattro is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing one.
