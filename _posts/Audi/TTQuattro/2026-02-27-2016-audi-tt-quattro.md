# 2016 Audi TT Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi TT Quattro combines sharp handling, modern technology, and distinctive styling into a compelling compact sports car package. Buyers shopping for a used model should evaluate the vehicle's powertrain options and ensure all mechanical and electrical systems are thoroughly inspected before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration paired with standard all-wheel drive. It is generally responsive and well-suited to the car's dynamic character, though proper maintenance history is essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and power delivery to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid changes are critical to maintaining smooth operation over high mileage.
