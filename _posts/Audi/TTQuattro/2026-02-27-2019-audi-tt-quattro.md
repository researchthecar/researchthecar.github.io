# 2019 Audi TT Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi TT Quattro offers sharp handling, distinctive styling, and punchy turbocharged performance paired with a standard all-wheel-drive system. For used-car shoppers, evaluating the powertrain options and overall vehicle condition is key to enjoying this sporty coupe or roadster.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, representing the standard powerplant for this model year.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term smooth operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V076000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=TT&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the fuel tank may become damaged by the fuel tank heat shield bracket . A damaged fuel tank may leak fuel, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a protective cap on the fuel tank heat shield bracket, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi TT Quattro is an engaging and stylish driver's car with robust mechanical foundations. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify proper maintenance history and overall mechanical health before finalizing a purchase.
