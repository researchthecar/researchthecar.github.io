**Introduction**

The 2022 Audi TT Quattro is a compact luxury sports car known for its sleek design, powerful engine, and sporty handling. It is available with a single engine and transmission option:

- 2.0L TFSI inline-4 engine with a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission
- 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque

Drivers appreciate the TT Quattro's agile handling, responsive steering, and luxurious interior, but some have reported issues with its engine, suspension, and electrical components.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI Inline-4**

- **Turbocharger Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, or unusual noises from the engine bay.
  - Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
- **Fuel Injector Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, or decreased fuel efficiency.
  - Solution: Replacement or cleaning of the fuel injectors.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**7-Speed S Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic**

- **Clutch Pack Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering of the transmission during gear changes.
  - Solution: Replacement of the clutch pack.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
- **Mechatronic Unit Malfunction:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping, or sudden loss of power.
  - Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

- **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises while turning or braking, reduced steering precision.
  - Solution: Replacement of the control arm bushings.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
- **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Excessive bouncing or instability while driving, reduced ride comfort.
  - Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+

**Electrical**

- **Battery Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, difficulty starting the car, or reduced electrical performance.
  - Solution: Replacement of the battery.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
- **Wiring Harness Corrosion:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, intermittent issues, or warning lights on the dashboard.
  - Solution: Inspection of the wiring harness and replacement of damaged wiring.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

- **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

- **Navigation System Bugs:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate navigation, freezing or crashing of the system.
  - Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
- **Power Window Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the windows, intermittent operation.
  - Solution: Replacement of the window regulator or motor.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+

**Summary**

While the 2022 Audi TT Quattro is a well-regarded sports car, it has been reported to have some potential issues. The most severe and expensive of these concern the transmission, potentially costing over $4,000 to repair. Other concerns include engine turbocharger failures, suspension wear, electrical glitches, and minor interior issues. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a TT Quattro.
