# 2023 Audi Q8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi Q8 blends striking coupe-like styling with upscale luxury and everyday utility, making it a compelling choice in the midsize luxury SUV segment. As a relatively new model on the used market, it generally presents a modern driving experience backed by advanced technology and a refined cabin. Buyers looking at a pre-owned 2023 model should still carefully evaluate individual vehicle condition, service history, and any remaining factory warranty coverage.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing strong and smooth acceleration. It is paired with a 48-volt mild-hybrid system to enhance efficiency and seamless stop-start operation.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers quick, refined shifts and distributes power smoothly through Audi's legendary Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is well-matched to the V6 powerplant for effortless highway cruising and dynamic driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi Q8 remains a sophisticated and technologically advanced luxury SUV option for used car buyers. While powertrain options offer robust performance and refinement, prospective owners should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service records to confirm overall vehicle health.
