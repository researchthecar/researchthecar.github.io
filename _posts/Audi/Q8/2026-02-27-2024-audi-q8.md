# 2024 Audi Q8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2024 Audi Q8, buyers will find a luxury SUV offering high-end styling and advanced technology. Because early-production owner reports highlight several electronic, braking, and door latch anomalies, prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and ensure all software updates have been completed before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The standard 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and capable acceleration. This powertrain is generally robust, though early owner feedback points to occasional peripheral charging and electrical integration quirks.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions with few mechanical complaints reported.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Owner reports indicate occasional concerns regarding brake vibration and premature wear on rotors.

**Brake Vibration and Premature Rotor Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience severe shuddering or vibration through the brake pedal, with rotors warping prematurely under standard operating conditions.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the brake pads and rotors, replacing warped components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical complaints center on auxiliary battery and charging system performance.

**Battery and Charging Relay Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden 12V battery or relay failures can leave the vehicle immobilized and stranded.
- **Solution:** Test the 12V battery health and charging system relays, replacing faulty electrical components if necessary.

## Exterior

### Door Latches

Some owners have experienced interior and exterior door handle and latch inconsistencies.

**Door Latch and Handle Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Doors may fail to securely latch when closed or require multiple pulls on the interior handle to fully unlatch.
- **Solution:** Inspect door latch mechanisms and actuator cables, adjusting or replacing faulty assemblies as needed.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Driver assistance sensors occasionally trigger unintended braking events during routine turns.

**Pre-Sense False Positive Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking or Pre-Sense system may incorrectly interpret turning maneuvers as hazards and abruptly slam on the brakes.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to check for sensor calibration updates or software patches related to the driver assistance systems.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi Q8 delivers strong luxury credentials and performance, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to brake condition, electronic charging systems, and driver assistance sensor behavior during a test drive to ensure everything operates as intended.
