# 2022 Audi Q8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi Q8 is a luxury midsize coupe-SUV that combines striking styling with strong performance and high-end technology. Buyers shopping for a used luxury model will find a vehicle that offers a refined driving experience and a high-tech cabin, though prospective owners should always evaluate overall condition and maintenance history carefully.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (335 hp / 369 lb-ft)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It provides smooth, responsive acceleration suitable for daily driving and highway cruising.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless gear changes and routes power effectively to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2022 Audi Q8 presents a compelling option for used luxury SUV buyers seeking sophisticated design, robust performance, and a comfortable ride.
