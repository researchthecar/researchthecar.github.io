# 2018 Audi A7 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi A7 Quattro blends sleek fastback styling with sophisticated technology and strong performance. As a luxury vehicle packed with advanced electronics and turbocharged powertrain options, prospective buyers should carefully weigh its dynamic capabilities against potential ownership costs as the vehicle ages.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Supercharged TFSI

This supercharged 3.0-liter V6 engine produces approximately 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth power and responsive acceleration. It generally offers solid performance, though routine maintenance and complex auxiliary systems require diligent care.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed traditional automatic transmission pairs with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system to provide refined gear shifts and highway cruising efficiency. It is generally robust compared to dual-clutch units, provided fluid changes are maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V035000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V370000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A7&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Stress or wear of the body-sensing mat within the front passenger seat may cause the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) control module to malfunction. If the PODS module malfunctions, the front passenger air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will install a PODS system repair kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi A7 Quattro remains an attractive choice for luxury car buyers looking for distinct design and performance. While formal complaint trends for this model year are minimal, standard luxury car upkeep and thorough pre-purchase inspections are strongly recommended.
