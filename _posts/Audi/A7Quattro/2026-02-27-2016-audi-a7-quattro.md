# 2016 Audi A7 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi A7 Quattro blends striking fastback styling with luxury performance and standard all-wheel drive. As a used purchase, prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and remain aware of common mechanical complexities typical of high-end German luxury vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Supercharged TFSI

This 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine generates approximately 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. It offers strong, responsive acceleration and is generally considered robust, though routine maintenance is essential to avoid oil leaks or cooling system wear.

### 3.0L V6 Turbocharged TDI Diesel

This 3.0-liter turbocharged diesel V6 produces around 240 horsepower and an impressive 428 lb-ft of low-end torque. It delivers excellent fuel efficiency and effortless cruising capability, though emissions equipment requires attentive upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

This traditional torque-converter 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with the TDI diesel engine, offering smooth and responsive shifts. It provides a reliable power delivery experience when fluid changes are maintained on schedule.

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with the supercharged gasoline V6, delivering rapid gear changes. Buyers should check for smooth engagement at low speeds during a pre-purchase test drive.
