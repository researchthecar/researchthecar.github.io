# 2022 Audi A8 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A8 Quattro represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining advanced technology, refined interior comfort, and sophisticated engineering. As a flagship luxury sedan, prospective used-car buyers can generally expect a well-appointed driving experience, though its complex electronic systems and high-performance powertrains require careful inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

A 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers smooth, effortless power delivery suited for a luxury flagship.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

A potent 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine generating around 453 horsepower and 487 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust, high-performance acceleration for drivers seeking maximum power.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission featuring steering wheel paddle shifters and permanent Quattro all-wheel drive. It provides seamless, rapid gear changes that complement the vehicle's refined performance character.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V084000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** During air bag assembly, the front-passenger occupant protection flap of the air bag may not have been folded correctly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." In a crash, an incorrectly folded air bag can deploy with too much force, increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
