# 2024 Audi A8 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi A8 Quattro represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining advanced technology, refined interior comfort, and confident all-wheel-drive performance. As a flagship luxury sedan, it offers sophisticated powertrain choices designed to deliver effortless acceleration and smooth highway cruising. Prospective used-car buyers looking at this model year will find a vehicle that generally benefits from modern engineering refinements, though luxury vehicle ownership always warrants careful attention to electronic and mechanical complexity.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, utilizing a mild-hybrid system for seamless power delivery. It serves as the standard powerplant for the A8 Quattro, offering a balanced blend of responsive performance and everyday refinement.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The potent 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine generates around 443 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration for enthusiast drivers. It provides substantial passing power while maintaining the quiet, composed demeanor expected of a flagship sedan.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system across all available engine options. It features rapid, nearly imperceptible gear changes and manual shift capability via steering wheel paddles.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
