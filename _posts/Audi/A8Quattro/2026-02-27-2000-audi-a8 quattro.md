# 2000 Audi A8 Quattro

### Overview
The 2000 Audi A8 Quattro, a luxury sedan known for its sophisticated all-wheel-drive system and comfortable ride, presents a mixed bag in terms of long-term reliability. While its core mechanical components, particularly the Quattro system, are generally robust, certain model years and specific components have documented areas of concern. Owners often praise the A8's build quality and performance, but potential buyers should be aware of common electrical gremlins and potential issues with aging suspension and powertrain components. Regular maintenance is crucial for mitigating many of these potential problems, especially given the complexity of the vehicle's systems.

---


## Engine

### 4.2L V8 (AQD, AVP, AXB, AXC)
**Timing Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine power loss, engine stalling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing belt, tensioners, and idler pulleys. If failure occurs, potential for catastrophic engine damage requiring rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil drips under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 2.8L V6 (ACK, AHA, APG)
**Timing Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine power loss, engine stalling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing belt, tensioners, and idler pulleys. If failure occurs, potential for catastrophic engine damage requiring rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1300

**PCV System Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential for oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve and associated hoses.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (01V)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid under the vehicle, rough shifting, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets, or transmission cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'air suspension fault' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200 per corner for springs, $800 - $1500 for compressor

**Height Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent ride height, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty height sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### Standard Coil Spring Suspension
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, door locks, interior lights, dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### Instrument Cluster
**Pixelated Display / Dead Pixels**
- **Symptoms:** Missing or unreadable information on the instrument cluster display.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


### Ignition Switch
**Failure to Start / Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Car won't start, electrical components cutting out intermittently.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ignition switch.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators
**Failure of Central Locking**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking with remote or key, individual door lock failure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door lock actuators.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400 per door


### Headlight Leveling System
**Malfunctioning Xenon Headlights**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights not adjusting height, flickering, or not turning on.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of headlight level sensors, ballasts, or bulbs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Interior

### Climate Control Unit
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Blower fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### Sunroof Mechanism
**Sticking or Non-Functioning Sunroof**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof struggles to open/close, makes grinding noises, or stops working entirely.
- **Solution:** Lubrication of tracks, or replacement of motor or mechanism components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2000 Audi A8 Quattro offers a luxurious experience but requires diligent maintenance and awareness of potential issues. The V8 and V6 engines, while powerful, are susceptible to timing belt failures if not serviced on schedule, and oil leaks are common as the vehicles age. The adaptive air suspension, a hallmark of the A8, can be a significant source of repair costs due to leaks and sensor failures. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the Body Control Module and instrument cluster, are also frequently reported. While the Quattro system itself is generally reliable, the complexity of the A8's integrated systems means that repairs can be costly. Prospective buyers should prioritize vehicles with a comprehensive service history and budget for potential repairs, especially concerning the engine, suspension, and electrical systems.
