**2017 Audi A8 Quattro: Specific Issues and Estimated Repair Costs**

The 2017 Audi A8 Quattro is a luxury sedan available with a choice of three engines: a 3.0-liter supercharged V6 producing 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing 450 horsepower and 444 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.3-liter W12 producing 500 horsepower and 463 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic and an 8-speed double-clutch automatic.

The A8 is praised for its spacious interior, advanced technology, and impressive performance, but some owners have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**

* **3.0-liter supercharged V6**
    * **Fuel injector failure:** Injectors can become clogged or damaged, causing engine misfires, rough idling, and reduced power.
    * Solution: Replace faulty injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: #19V895
* **4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8**
    * **Timing chain failure:** Chains can stretch or break, leading to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: #19V896
* **6.3-liter W12**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Tiptronic automatic**
    * **Shifting problems:** Transmission may hesitate, slip, or refuse to shift properly.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: #19V897
* **8-speed double-clutch automatic**
    * **Clutch pack failure:** Clutches may wear out prematurely, causing shuddering, slipping, and difficulty engaging gears.
    * Solution: Replace clutch pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: #19V898

**Suspension**

* **Front air suspension failure:** Air bags can leak or fail, causing the vehicle to sag or ride unevenly.
* Solution: Replace air bags and related components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: #19V899

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Battery can discharge prematurely due to an electrical fault.
* Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical fault.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: #19V900

**Exterior**

* **LED headlight failure:** Headlights may flicker, become dim, or fail completely.
* Solution: Replace faulty headlights.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: #19V901

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system glitches:** System may freeze, become unresponsive, or display incorrect information.
* Solution: Update software or replace infotainment module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: #19V902
* **Climate control malfunction:** System may not adjust temperature or airflow properly.
* Solution: Replace faulty sensors or actuators.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 Audi A8 Quattro is a highly regarded luxury sedan with impressive performance and advanced features. However, it has experienced some notable issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. Repair costs for these issues can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Some issues, such as timing chain failure, can have severe consequences and require costly repairs.
