# 2020 Audi A8 Quattro

### Overview
The 2020 Audi A8 Quattro, a flagship luxury sedan, generally exhibits a high level of build quality and technological integration. As expected from a premium vehicle, it offers a refined driving experience with advanced features. Reliability data for this model year indicates a solid performance, with most reported issues being relatively minor or specific to certain advanced systems. Owners generally appreciate the A8's sophisticated powertrain options and comfortable ride. While no vehicle is entirely immune to problems, the A8 Quattro demonstrates a commendable track record for its class, particularly concerning its core mechanical components. Attention to specific electronic features and advanced driver-assistance systems is recommended for long-term ownership.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks/Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one or more corners, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of leaking air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, connectivity issues with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500


### Driver Assistance Systems
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings from adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors; system deactivation.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or recalibration of sensors, replacement of faulty sensors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Audi A8 Quattro generally presents a strong reliability profile, especially for its powertrain and core mechanicals. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the advanced adaptive air suspension system, which can develop leaks or malfunctions, and occasional glitches within the sophisticated infotainment and driver assistance electronics. These electrical and suspension-related concerns, while not universal, represent the most common non-routine repairs. Overall, the A8 Quattro is a well-built luxury sedan, and with proactive maintenance and awareness of its complex systems, it offers a dependable ownership experience for its segment.
