**Introduction:**

The 2016 Audi A8 Quattro is a luxurious sedan powered by a 3.0L supercharged V6 engine producing 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. It features an 8-speed Tiptronic transmission and Quattro all-wheel drive. The A8 is praised for its comfortable ride, advanced technology, and spacious interior. However, it has received some complaints regarding reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Supercharged V6 (CKUA)**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the timing chain tensioner can cause stretched or broken timing chains, resulting in a loss of engine power or catastrophic engine damage. Symptoms include excessive engine noise, rough idling, or difficulty starting the engine.
        * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and possibly the timing chains.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on intake valves and combustion chambers can lead to reduced engine performance, hesitation, and knocking.
        * Solution: Walnut shell blasting or chemical cleaning of the intake valves and combustion chambers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Tiptronic (DL808)**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the mechatronic unit, the electronic control module for the transmission, can cause transmission slipping, gear engagement issues, or loss of drive.
        * Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: 17V-482

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the air suspension compressor can result in a loss of air pressure in the suspension system, causing the vehicle to sag or ride low.
        * Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery discharge can lead to a dead battery or difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Inspection of the electrical system for parasitic drains or battery replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800

**Exterior**

* **Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the xenon headlight ballast can cause the headlights to malfunction or fail to illuminate.
        * Solution: Replacement of the headlight ballast.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Interior**

* **MMI Touchpad Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction of the MMI touchpad can result in difficulty or intermittent use of the infotainment system.
        * Solution: Replacement of the MMI touchpad.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Summary:**

The 2016 Audi A8 Quattro has experienced some reliability concerns, particularly with timing chain issues in the 3.0L supercharged V6 engine and mechatronic unit failures in the 8-speed Tiptronic transmission. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can be significant.
