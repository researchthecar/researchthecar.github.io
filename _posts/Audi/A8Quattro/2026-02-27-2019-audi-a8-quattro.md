# 2019 Audi A8 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi A8 Quattro represents the pinnacle of luxury and technology for Audi's flagship sedan lineup. As a sophisticated used luxury purchase, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate its complex powertrain offerings and advanced electronic systems. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, maintaining a high-end European flagship requires diligence regarding service history and potential out-of-warranty repair costs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and responsive performance. It serves as the standard powerplant for the A8 Quattro, balancing everyday drivability with respectable efficiency for a large luxury sedan.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust performance with approximately 453 horsepower and 487 lb-ft of torque. It provides effortless acceleration for buyers seeking a more muscular driving dynamic in their flagship luxury vehicle.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system across all available engine variants. It is known for quick, refined shifts that complement the serene driving character of the A8.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V111000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An engine control module malfunction may cause the engine to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the engine control module connector for a missing sealing pin, and install it, if necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V415000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A failure of the seat track rivet increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger seat tracks, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
