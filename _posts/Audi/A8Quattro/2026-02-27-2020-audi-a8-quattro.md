# 2020 Audi A8 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi A8 Quattro, buyers will find a technologically advanced luxury flagship offering sophisticated ride quality and potent powertrain options. While initial owner reports suggest a generally high-end driving experience, prospective buyers should review available reliability feedback concerning electrical and powertrain systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership journey.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, utilizing a mild-hybrid system for smooth operation. It provides brisk acceleration for the large luxury sedan while maintaining refined daily drivability.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers substantial performance with roughly 453 horsepower, offering effortless highway passing power. This high-output option suits buyers prioritizing brisk acceleration and robust dynamic capability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system across all available engine configurations. It generally delivers smooth, responsive gear changes suited to the flagship sedan's luxury character.

## Electrical

### 48V Electrical System

The vehicle relies heavily on advanced electrical architecture, including a 48V mild-hybrid system designed to enhance efficiency and smooth out start-stop operations.

**Belt-Driven Starter Generator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a battery warning light, sudden loss of vehicle power, and a start system malfunction that can disable the vehicle and prevent restarting.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle diagnosed by a qualified technician to determine if replacement of the 48V belt-driven starter generator is necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V111000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An engine control module malfunction may cause the engine to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the engine control module connector for a missing sealing pin, and install it, if necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A8 Quattro is a sophisticated luxury sedan that combines impressive performance with a high-tech cabin. Prospective buyers should carefully evaluate the vehicle's complex electrical systems and verify maintenance history, as electrical issues involving the mild-hybrid architecture have been reported by some owners.
