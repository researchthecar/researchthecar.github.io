# 2017 Audi A8 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi A8 Quattro represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining sophisticated engineering, a lavish interior, and confident all-weather performance. As a flagship luxury sedan, it offers sophisticated powertrain choices and advanced technology features. Used car buyers looking at this model should weigh its high-end driving dynamics and comfort against potential ownership costs and the complexity associated with modern luxury vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Supercharged

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine produces around 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and responsive acceleration. It serves as the base powerplant for the A8 Quattro, delivering a balanced blend of everyday drivability and efficiency.

### 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbocharged

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generates an impressive 450 horsepower and 444 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless power delivery and rapid acceleration. This powerplant caters to enthusiasts seeking elevated performance from their luxury flagship.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes and pairs standard with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is widely praised for its refined shifting character and durability under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V035000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V397000: Structure:Body:Hood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the seal contacts hot engine components, there could be an increased risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install a retaining plate to prevent the engine compartment seal from loosening. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V178000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Worn bearings can cause the turbine wheel to contact the turbocharger housing or cause the shaft to break, causing an engine stall and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the oil strainer and perform an oil change, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
