# 2017 Audi A8 Quattro

### Overview
The 2017 Audi A8 Quattro, a flagship luxury sedan, generally offers a high level of build quality and technological integration. As a premium vehicle, it aims to provide a refined driving experience with robust performance. While many owners report satisfaction, like any complex automobile, certain areas can experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2017 model year, specifically examining its engine and transmission variants, suspension, electrical systems, and interior/exterior components.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L TFSI Supercharged V6
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Supercharger Belt Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Whining noise from the engine bay, reduced acceleration, potential loss of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger drive belt.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### 4.0L TFSI Twin-Turbo V8
**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on the engine, burning oil smell, potential for oil to drip onto exhaust components.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic (ZF 8HP)
**Mechatronic Unit Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Torque Converter Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, suspension warning light, bouncy ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air struts or air springs.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, suspension warning light, constant running of compressor.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System Glitches
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts randomly.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the MMI control unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### LED Headlight Malfunctions
**Adaptive Lighting Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights not adjusting correctly, warning lights related to lighting system.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty headlight modules or control units.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000 per headlight

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi A8 Quattro is a sophisticated luxury sedan with a generally good reliability record, especially considering its advanced technology. The most commonly reported technical issues tend to be concentrated in the adaptive air suspension system, with potential for leaks or compressor failures. Engine-specific concerns, particularly for the V8 variant, can include oil leaks and ignition coil issues. Transmission problems, while not pervasive, can arise with the mechatronic unit or torque converter. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and LED headlight malfunctions, are also noted. Owners should be aware of these potential areas and factor in the higher cost of repairs associated with premium components and complex systems.
