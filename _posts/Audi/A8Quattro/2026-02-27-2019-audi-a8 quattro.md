## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one or more corners, suspension warning light illuminated.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Air Strut Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Gradual loss of ride height, particularly after sitting for extended periods.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut(s).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200 per strut


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or unexpected reboots of the MMI system.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600


### Driver Assistance Systems
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings or deactivation of adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or recalibration of sensors. Replacement if damaged.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Audi A8 Quattro demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain components. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the sophisticated adaptive air suspension system, with potential for compressor or strut leaks, and occasional glitches within the advanced electrical and infotainment systems. These are not uncommon for vehicles with such complex technology. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are recommended to mitigate potential problems and ensure the continued luxury and performance of the A8.
