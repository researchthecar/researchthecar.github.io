# 2021 Audi A8 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Audi A8 Quattro, buyers will find a flagship luxury sedan that balances sophisticated engineering with opulent comfort. While owner reports to federal regulators are relatively limited for this model year, potential shoppers should still carefully inspect the vehicle's complex electrical architecture, fuel system, and glass components during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (TFSI)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers smooth, effortless acceleration suited for a luxury sedan while generally maintaining dependable mechanical operation.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 (TFSI)

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers a robust 453 horsepower and 487 lb-ft of torque for high-performance luxury driving. Buyers should watch for occasional fuel system component leaks reported by some owners.

**Fuel Pump Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A strong gasoline odor inside or outside the vehicle, often pointing to a compromised fuel pump seal from manufacturing.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel pump and replace the faulty seal or assembly as needed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system across all trims. Most drivers experience refined shifts, though isolated reports note occasional engagement delays.

**Shift Delay and Engagement Jolt**
- **Symptoms:** A noticeable delay when shifting gears from a complete stop, followed by a sudden recoil, jump, or loud noise.
- **Solution:** Check for transmission software updates or have the adaptive learning values reset by a specialist.

## Electrical

### Electrical System and Alternator

The A8 relies heavily on advanced electronics and a mild-hybrid architecture, making a thorough battery and charging system check essential.

**Sudden Electrical Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden and rapid failure of all electrical systems while driving, potentially leaving the vehicle temporarily disabled.
- **Solution:** Inspect the alternator, battery management system, and belt starter generator (BSG) charging components.

## Exterior

### Glass and Sunroof

The expansive glass panels offer an airy cabin feel, though rare instances of spontaneous glass breakage have been documented by owners.

**Moonroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud pop followed by the rear moonroof or center glass suddenly exploding outward and scattering glass shards.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for any structural stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V111000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Engine Control Module (Ecu/Ecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An engine control module malfunction may cause the engine to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the engine control module connector for a missing sealing pin, and install it, if necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2021)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi A8 Quattro delivers top-tier luxury, advanced technology, and refined performance. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electrical charging system, fuel seals, and panoramic glass to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
