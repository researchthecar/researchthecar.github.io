# 2023 Audi A8 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A8 Quattro represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining advanced technology, refined interior comfort, and sophisticated engineering. As a flagship full-size luxury sedan, it is designed for buyers seeking a seamless driving experience, though its complex mechanical and electronic systems require careful evaluation when purchasing pre-owned.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, utilizing mild-hybrid technology for smooth power delivery. It offers refined performance suited for daily luxury driving with generally solid reliability.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generates around 453 horsepower and 479 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and high-end performance. It provides effortless passing power at the expense of increased complexity and fuel consumption.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission pairs with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system to provide smooth, rapid gear shifts. It is well-matched to both engine options and generally operates reliably under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V084000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** During air bag assembly, the front-passenger occupant protection flap of the air bag may not have been folded correctly. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." In a crash, an incorrectly folded air bag can deploy with too much force, increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi A8 Quattro is a sophisticated luxury sedan offering robust powertrain options and supreme comfort. Prospective used car buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, as electronic and mechanical complexity is inherent to high-end flagship sedans.
