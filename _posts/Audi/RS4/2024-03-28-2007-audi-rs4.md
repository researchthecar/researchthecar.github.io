**Introduction**

The 2007 Audi RS4 is a high-performance wagon that packs a 4.2-liter V8 engine under the hood, producing 420 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. The RS4 comes standard with a six-speed manual transmission and all-wheel drive. It offers a thrilling driving experience with precise handling and blistering acceleration.

**Engine**

**4.2-liter V8**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, and increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on engine startup and timing chain skipping
    * Solution: Replacing timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption between oil changes
    * Solution: Replacing piston rings or valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding When Shifting Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second gear
    * Solution: Replacing synchronizers or transmission rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose steering, excessive tire wear, and clunking noises
    * Solution: Replacing control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Strut Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leak from the strut, reduced ride comfort, and uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replacing strut assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Replacing battery or diagnosing electrical system for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Heated Seat Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heated seats not working
    * Solution: Replacing heating element or control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the rear bumper, especially at the ends
    * Solution: Replacing or repairing rear bumper
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose dashboard components causing rattling noises
    * Solution: Tightening dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
* **Door Panel Squeak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises from door panels
    * Solution: Using felt tape or lubricant to eliminate friction
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+

**Summary**

The 2007 Audi RS4 offers impressive performance and handling, but it also has some potential issues that buyers should be aware of. The engine can experience carbon buildup, timing chain tensioner failure, and oil consumption. The transmission may exhibit grinding gears, especially in second gear. Suspension issues include control arm bushing failure and strut leaks. Electrical problems can include battery drain and heated seat failure. Some exterior components, like the rear bumper, have been known to crack. Interior rattles and door panel squeaks are also common issues. Repair costs for these problems can vary significantly depending on the severity of the problem and the specific parts that need to be replaced.
