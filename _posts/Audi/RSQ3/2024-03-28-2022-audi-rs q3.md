**Introduction**
The 2022 Audi RS Q3 is a high-performance version of the Q3 compact SUV. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.5-liter five-cylinder engine that produces 394 horsepower and 354 pound-feet of torque. The RS Q3 is available with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. 

Drivers appreciate its excellent driving dynamics, impressive performance, and luxurious appointments. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**
- **2.5-liter turbocharged inline-five**
 **Knocking or ticking noises**
  - Description/Symptoms: Abnormal noises coming from the engine when idling or under load
  - Solution: Replace defective engine components, such as camshaft tensioner or piston rings
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
- **Seven-speed dual-clutch automatic (S tronic)**
 **Delayed or rough shifting**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or a "jerky" transmission response
  - Solution: Reprogram transmission control module or replace transmission components
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **Front suspension**
 **Strut mount bearing failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Abnormal noises or clunking sounds when turning or driving over rough roads
  - Solution: Replace defective strut mount bearing
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
- **Electrical system**
 **Battery drain issues**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly or vehicle struggles to start
  - Solution: Test and replace defective electrical components, such as alternator, battery, or parasitic draw
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Sunroof**
 **Leaks or rattling noises**
  - Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin or annoying rattling sounds coming from the sunroof
  - Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof components, such as seals or tracks
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
- **Infotainment system**
 **Software glitches or freezes**
  - Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues with navigation, audio, or connectivity
  - Solution: Update or replace infotainment system software
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
Overall, the 2022 Audi RS Q3 is a capable and highly enjoyable vehicle. However, some owners have encountered issues with engine noises, transmission problems, and electrical concerns. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but it is important for potential buyers to be aware of them before making a purchase.
