**Introduction**

The 2016 Audi S5 is a sporty coupe with a sophisticated interior and advanced performance capabilities. It is powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 engine that delivers 354 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The S5 is available with either a 6-speed manual or an 8-speed automatic transmission. It features a quattro all-wheel-drive system and a sport-tuned suspension for enhanced handling and stability.

**Engine**

**3.0-Liter V6 Engine**

* **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace high-pressure fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or ticking noise, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, clutch pedal spongy or does not return
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, harsh shifting, loss of gears
    * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noise when turning or going over bumps
    * Solution: Replace front control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Subframe Mounting Point Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or popping noise from the rear of the vehicle, alignment issues
    * Solution: Reinforce rear subframe mounting points
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting or stalling
    * Solution: Update software or replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rearview Camera Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera screen blank or distorted
    * Solution: Replace camera module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Spoiler Adhesion Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear spoiler detaches from the trunk
    * Solution: Re-adhere or replace rear spoiler
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Central Information Display Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Display screen unresponsive or flickering
    * Solution: Replace display screen
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Steering Wheel Buttons Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on steering wheel become stuck or unresponsive
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel buttons or module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Audi S5 is a performance-oriented coupe with a powerful engine and sophisticated interior. However, it has some potential issues that buyers may encounter, including engine and transmission problems, suspension issues, and electrical and interior malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues vary, so it is important to research and consider them before purchasing a used S5.
