# 2020 Audi S5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi S5, buyers are generally drawn to its striking styling, upscale interior, and punchy performance. While owner complaints for this model year are relatively modest in volume, prospective buyers should thoroughly evaluate the vehicle's onboard technology and infotainment systems during a test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 2020 Audi S5 is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing approximately 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant delivers brisk acceleration and robust performance, generally proving durable when routine maintenance intervals are strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Power is sent to Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system via an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. This gearbox offers smooth, rapid shifts under normal driving conditions and pairs well with the V6 engine's power delivery.

## Electrical

### MMI Infotainment System

The electrical architecture supports advanced cabin tech, though owner reports highlight recurring software instability in the central display.

**Infotainment System Constant Rebooting**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the central Multi Media Interface (MMI) screen randomly restarts every few minutes while driving, which can temporarily disable navigation, audio, and connected smartphone features.
- **Solution:** Owners should consult a dealership for potential software updates, though diagnosis and repair paths may vary as fixes are investigated.

### Parking and Driver Assistance Sensors

Advanced safety and driver assistance features rely heavily on stable vehicle software to maintain seamless operation during low-speed maneuvers.

**Backup Camera and Sensor Loss During MMI Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** When the infotainment system restarts unexpectedly while reversing, drivers may temporarily lose access to the backup camera and parking proximity sensors.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician to check for pending software patches or control module updates addressing MMI stability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V737000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harness for the rear lights could be missing the anti-abrasion protection in the area of the spare wheel well. Without the scuff protection, the wires may become damaged during a rear crash, disabling the doors from being opened from the outside, causing a loss of hazard lights, and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will apply scuff protection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi S5 blends luxury and performance, but potential buyers should pay close attention to electronic stability during a pre-purchase inspection. Verifying that the infotainment and camera systems operate without unexpected interruptions will help ensure a frustration-free ownership experience.
