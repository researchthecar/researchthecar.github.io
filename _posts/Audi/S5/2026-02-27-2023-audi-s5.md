# 2023 Audi S5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi S5 delivers strong luxury performance powered by its turbocharged V6 engine and responsive automatic transmission. While initial owner feedback is generally limited, prospective used-car buyers should review individual vehicle histories carefully and perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (349 hp)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for the S5. It is generally a robust power plant, though isolated owner reports note minor electrical or sensor-related fuel monitoring glitches.

**Fuel Gauge Inaccuracy**
- **Symptoms:** The fuel gauge may remain stuck or unresponsive, incorrectly displaying fuel levels and leading to unexpected engine stalls due to running out of fuel.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the fuel sending unit or instrument cluster and replace malfunctioning components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes while distributing power to Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. No widespread or recurring transmission defects have been highlighted by early owner complaints.

## Suspension

### Tires

Factory-equipped performance tires offer high levels of grip but require attentive maintenance due to their low profile.

**Tire Tread Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Low-profile tires may experience sudden tread separation at highway speeds, potentially causing handling instability or rim damage.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire age, tread depth, and inflation pressures regularly, and replace damaged or worn tires promptly.

## Safety

### Air Bags

The supplemental restraint system is designed to protect occupants in a collision, though isolated incidents highlight potential diagnostic concerns.

**Failure to Deploy in Frontal Impact**
- **Symptoms:** Airbags may fail to deploy during significant frontal collisions, increasing the risk of occupant injury.
- **Solution:** Ensure the supplemental restraint system (SRS) is professionally inspected if any warning lights illuminate on the dashboard.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi S5 is an appealing luxury sport model with potent performance and a well-engineered powertrain. While available complaint data points to a few isolated issues, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection remains the best way to ensure a prospective vehicle is in top condition.
