# 2016 Audi S5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Audi S5 means balancing spirited performance with luxury refinement, but prospective buyers should keep an eye on potential electrical and steering glitches. While complaints for this model year are relatively sparse, reviewing specific component reliability can help you spot issues before they become costly headaches.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6

The 3.0-liter supercharged V6 produces approximately 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. It offers strong, responsive acceleration paired with solid overall durability, though regular maintenance is key.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission is paired with Quattro all-wheel drive, offering an engaging and enthusiast-focused driving experience. It generally proves mechanically robust for daily use.

### 7-Speed S-Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic provides lightning-fast shifts and smooth power delivery across various driving conditions. Buyers should verify proper clutch engagement during test drives to ensure long-term reliability.

## Steering

### Electronic Steering Rack

Owner reports indicate occasional sensitivity in the electronic power steering system, particularly regarding moisture exposure.

**Electronic Steering Rack Error**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden electronic steering rack error warning, often following exposure to wet or rainy driving conditions, which can impact power steering assistance.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the steering rack connections, module, and wiring harness for moisture intrusion or electrical faults.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi S5 delivers compelling performance and upscale comfort, but used car shoppers should thoroughly check the vehicle's electrical systems and steering components during a pre-purchase inspection. Prioritizing well-maintained examples with complete service history will help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.
