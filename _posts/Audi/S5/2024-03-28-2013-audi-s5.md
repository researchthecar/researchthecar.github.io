**Introduction**

The 2013 Audi S5 is a high-performance coupe that combines sleek styling with impressive driving dynamics. It is powered by a supercharged 3.0L V6 engine that produces 333 horsepower and 347 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed manual or 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. The S5 is known for its responsive handling, luxurious interior, and advanced technology features. However, like all vehicles, it has had its share of issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6 Engine (Supercharged)**
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, misfires
        * Solution: Intake valve cleaning
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, reduced engine power
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **7-Speed S Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Shifting problems, transmission slipping
        * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: 14V333

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Components (Lower Control Arms, Tie Rods):**
    * **Premature Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from suspension
        * Solution: Replacement of worn components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains rapidly even when the car is off
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: 12V216
* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights or taillights fail to work
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from sunroof when closed
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of sunroof components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Creaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling noises from door panels
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of door panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: HVAC system fails to function properly, blowing hot or cold air
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of HVAC system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Audi S5 is a well-regarded performance coupe, but it has had its fair share of issues reported by owners. The most significant issues include engine carbon buildup, timing chain tensioner failure, and mechatronic unit failure in the transmission. While some issues, such as door panel creaks or sunroof rattles, are relatively minor, others, such as engine and transmission problems, can be expensive to repair. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues can vary, and it is important to weigh these factors when considering purchasing a used S5.
