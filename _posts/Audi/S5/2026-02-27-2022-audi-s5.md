# 2022 Audi S5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi S5 delivers strong performance and luxurious daily drivability, combining punchy turbocharged power with a refined interior. While overall owner feedback for this model year is limited regarding major structural flaws, buyers should always verify service history and perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection when shopping in the pre-owned luxury sport segment.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It generally offers solid reliability when properly maintained with timely oil changes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. It is a robust unit for daily driving and spirited performance when serviced according to factory schedules.
