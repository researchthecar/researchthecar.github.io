# 2021 Audi S5

### Overview
The 2021 Audi S5, a performance-oriented variant of the A5 lineup, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality engineering. It is equipped with a potent turbocharged V6 engine paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission, offering exhilarating performance without significant widespread mechanical concerns. Owners typically report satisfaction with the vehicle's build quality, advanced technology features, and sporty driving dynamics. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the S5 for this model year demonstrates a commendable level of dependability, with most reported problems being minor and easily addressed. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its performance and longevity.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi S5 is a reliable performance sedan with a strong mechanical foundation. The 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and generally free from significant issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain issues. These are typically resolvable with software updates or professional diagnosis. Overall, the S5 offers a dependable and enjoyable driving experience, with its reliability rating being a positive aspect for potential buyers.
