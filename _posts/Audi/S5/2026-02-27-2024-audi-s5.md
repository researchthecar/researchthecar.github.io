# 2024 Audi S5

The 2024 Audi S5, a performance-oriented variant of the A5 lineup, generally presents a strong profile in terms of technical reliability. This analysis focuses on documented technical concerns beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of potential reliability hotspots for prospective and current owners. The emphasis is on widespread or notable technical faults rather than common wear and tear.

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Audi S5 demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, with its core powertrain and mechanical systems showing minimal reported issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced relate to the sophisticated electrical systems, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential calibration needs for driver assistance sensors. These are generally manageable through software updates or recalibration, with associated costs being relatively low. For the most part, the S5 offers a robust and dependable ownership experience, aligning with Audi's reputation for quality engineering in its performance models. Owners should remain vigilant for software-related updates and ensure proper maintenance of sensor systems.
