**Introduction:**

The 2021 Audi S5 is a high-performance luxury coupe that boasts a powerful 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine delivering 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and Quattro all-wheel drive, it offers exhilarating driving dynamics. While it excels in performance and comfort, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine:**

* **Oil Consumption Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage
    * Solution: Replacement of piston rings and valve guides
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation, slipping, or jerking during gear changes
    * Solution: Software update or transmission fluid replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises such as clunking, squeaking, or rattling when driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mount bearing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Issues with the display, navigation, or audio functions of the MMI infotainment system
    * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: 21V345

**Exterior:**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight housing
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, leading to inconsistent temperature or air flow
    * Solution: Replacement of climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Audi S5 has received positive reviews for its performance and comfort. However, it has also experienced some issues affecting the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior components. The severity of these issues vary, with some requiring costly repairs. Before purchasing a used S5, it's advisable to thoroughly inspect the vehicle and consider the potential repair costs associated with these issues.
