# 2019 Audi S5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Audi S5, buyers are treated to a luxury performance coupe and sportback blending aggressive styling with everyday usability. While owner reports to safety regulators are relatively limited for this model year, prospective purchasers should still perform a thorough inspection, particularly checking cooling system components and onboard electronics during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (EA839)

The 2019 Audi S5 features a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a smooth power delivery. This EA839 powerplant is generally robust, though buyers should monitor the cooling system for potential leaks around auxiliary valves.

**Coolant Pump and Valve Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warnings regarding coolant levels appear upon startup, accompanied by visible coolant residue or leakage around the mechanical coolant pump switching valve on the cylinder head.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and switching valve for leaks, and replace failed components with updated factory parts as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Equipped with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission distributing power via Quattro all-wheel drive, this setup delivers quick shifts and refined cruising. No widespread recurring mechanical complaints have been prominently reported for this transmission.

## Suspension

### Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS)

Suspension and wheel monitoring systems generally maintain good road manners, though sensor operation should be checked.

**TPMS Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The tire pressure monitoring system fails to alert the driver promptly when tire pressure drops significantly, leading to potential tire damage.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire pressures manually during routine maintenance and have the TPMS sensors and control module diagnosed if warnings fail to trigger.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Odometer

Electrical architecture supports advanced driver displays and infotainment, requiring careful verification of digital modules and history records during purchase.

**Mileage Discrepancy / Odometer Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistencies discovered between physical vehicle condition, registration records, and displayed mileage.
- **Solution:** Verify vehicle history thoroughly using comprehensive third-party vehicle history reports and professional pre-purchase inspections.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V114000: Suspension:Front:Shock Absorber](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Loose shock absorbers may impair vehicle steering, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the shock absorber forks, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi S5 remains an appealing luxury performance choice, offering strong turbo-V6 performance and refined driving dynamics. Used car buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the cooling system and verify vehicle history documentation carefully before finalizing a purchase.
