**Introduction:**

The 2017 Audi S5 is a high-performance luxury coupe that combines sleek styling with impressive driving dynamics. It features a powerful 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine mated to an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission. With 354 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, the S5 delivers exhilarating acceleration and responsive handling. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its share of potential issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

* **Turbocharger Lag:** Some drivers experience hesitation or delay in acceleration due to turbocharger lag.
  * Solution: Installing an aftermarket turbo upgrade can improve spool-up time.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
* **Ignition Coil Failure:** Ignition coils can fail, causing misfires and rough idling.
  * Solution: Replacing the faulty ignition coils.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250 per coil
* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** Over time, carbon deposits can accumulate on the intake valves, leading to reduced engine performance and fuel efficiency.
  * Solution: Engine decarbonization service or cleaning the intake valves.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic**

* **Hesitation or Jerking Shifts:** Some drivers have reported occasional hesitation or jerking during gear changes.
  * Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the valve body.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Leakage can occur from the transmission pan gasket or cooler lines.
  * Solution: Replacing the faulty gasket or lines and topping up the transmission fluid.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400

**Suspension**

* **Uneven Tire Wear:** Improper alignment or worn suspension components can lead to uneven tire wear.
  * Solution: Tire rotation and alignment, replacing worn components such as ball joints or bushings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500 per component

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have experienced excessive battery drain due to malfunctioning control modules or parasitic electrical draws.
  * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing or replacing the faulty components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture or condensation can accumulate inside the headlights, affecting visibility.
  * Solution: Replacing the headlight housing or using moisture-absorbing solutions.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800 per headlight

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Issues:** Some drivers have reported glitches or freezes with the navigation system.
  * Solution: Updating the navigation system software or replacing the navigation module.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary**

The 2017 Audi S5 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle overall, but like all cars, it is not immune to potential issues. The most common problems reported by owners include turbocharger lag, ignition coil failures, transmission hesitation, and excessive battery drain. While some of these issues can be minor inconveniences, others may require more expensive repairs. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any symptoms can help minimize the likelihood and severity of these problems.
