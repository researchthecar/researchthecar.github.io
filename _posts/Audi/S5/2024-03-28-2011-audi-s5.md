**Introduction**

The 2011 Audi S5 is a high-performance sports car that combines athletic handling with luxurious amenities. It features a 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine that produces 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a six-speed manual or seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. The S5 is available in coupe and convertible body styles. 

Drivers praise the S5 for its powerful engine, sharp handling, and stylish design. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6**
    * **Carbon buildup on intake valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, misfires
        * Solution: Intake valve cleaning
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Water pump failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating
        * Solution: Replace water pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-speed manual**
    * **Grinding gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially when cold
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $4000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic**
    * **Shifting issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifts, hesitation, loss of power
        * Solution: Software update or transmission replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $5000+
        * Recalls: 18V648 (Transmission control module software update)

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or grinding noises when turning or driving over uneven surfaces, excessive tire wear
    * Solution: Replace worn suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, especially after short trips
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing faulty electrical components or parasitic drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water enters the cabin through the sunroof or its drain tubes
    * Solution: Replace or repair sunroof components, clear sunroof drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Electrical issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning window switches, climate control issues, dashboard warning lights
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Steering wheel buttons malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on the steering wheel don't respond or become intermittent
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel buttons or associated wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2011 Audi S5 is a desirable sports car, it has experienced some reliability issues. Engine and transmission problems have been reported, as well as suspension wear, electrical issues, and interior malfunctions. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor while others can be costly to repair.
