# 2018 Audi S5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi S5 delivers a compelling blend of luxury, sophisticated technology, and strong turbocharged performance. However, prospective buyers should approach the used market with a clear understanding of potential mechanical and electrical hiccups reported by owners. While many drivers enjoy its upscale cabin and robust powertrain, keeping a close eye on maintenance history and known trouble spots can save you from unexpected repair bills.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (EA839)

The 2018 Audi S5 is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 (EA839) producing approximately 354 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and refined power delivery, though owners should monitor closely for cooling system and water pump longevity.

**Coolant Leaks and Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant levels in the expansion tank, small fluid leaks on the engine, and potential vacuum system contamination leading to overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump, coolant reservoir, and associated hoses; replace faulty components immediately to prevent secondary engine damage.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Equipped with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission routing power through Quattro all-wheel drive, this setup provides quick, smooth gear changes. Routine fluid changes and software checks help maintain long-term reliability.

**Unexpected Rollaway / Gearbox Disengagement**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly disengage from Park, resulting in a rollaway if the parking brake is not properly engaged.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission shift linkage and parking brake mechanism inspected by a qualified technician.

## Suspension

### Suspension Springs

The multi-link suspension setup provides sharp handling and a compliant ride, but owners should watch for premature component wear on rough roadways.

**Broken Coil Spring**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the suspension warning light and potential clunking noises or uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Inspect the coil springs and replace any fractured suspension components in matched axle pairs.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Camera Systems

Advanced electronics enhance the cabin experience, though intermittent software glitches in driver assistance and camera systems have been noted by owners.

**Backup Camera and Display Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview or top-view camera system blacks out, freezes, or defaults to a graphic display mode instead of the live camera feed.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates or faulty camera modules and wiring harnesses.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim are generally well-built, though panoramic sunroof panels can occasionally be vulnerable to stress fractures.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden loud pop or bang resembling a gunshot, followed by severe cracking or shattering of the glass panel while driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment issues or stress points.

## Safety

### Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS)

Standard safety equipment is comprehensive, but electrical connections within the passenger detection system require prompt attention if warning lights appear.

**Airbag Warning Light / Faulty PODS Connection**
- **Symptoms:** An airbag warning message appears on the dash, and the passenger airbag may unexpectedly switch off even when occupied.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair the PODS wiring connection or perform necessary dealer recall service.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V547000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Oxidation on the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) connecting cable may cause the software to misdiagnose a malfunction and disable the passenger air bag. A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install cable ties to the harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi S5 is an exhilarating and luxurious daily driver, but used car shoppers should prioritize finding a model with a documented maintenance history. Watch out for potential cooling system leaks, electrical camera glitches, and suspension wear during your pre-purchase inspection to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
