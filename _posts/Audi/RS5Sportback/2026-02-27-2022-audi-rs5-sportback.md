# 2022 Audi RS5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi RS5 Sportback delivers exhilarating performance wrapped in a practical, aggressive four-door hatchback body style. Under the hood, it packs a potent twin-turbocharged engine mated to a quick-shifting automatic transmission and quattro all-wheel drive. For used car buyers, this high-performance luxury machine offers thrilling driving dynamics and daily usability, though prospective owners should budget for premium maintenance costs typical of German performance vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L V6 Twin-Turbocharged TFSI

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces roughly 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, offering rapid acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though high-performance driving demands diligent fluid checks and adherence to service intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and seamless power distribution to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. It matches the engine's high output well, maintaining refined cruising manners alongside aggressive downshifts in dynamic mode.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V102000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire and Loading Information." While referencing the B-pillar label, the operator may overload the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a corrected label on the B-pillar, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi RS5 Sportback is an impressive blend of everyday practicality and supercar-level performance. Because available owner complaint data is sparse for this model year, buyers can approach the market with relative confidence, provided they review maintenance history and secure a thorough pre-purchase inspection from an Audi specialist.
