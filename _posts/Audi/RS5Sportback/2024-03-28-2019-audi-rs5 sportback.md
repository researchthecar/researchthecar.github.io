**Introduction**

The 2019 Audi RS5 Sportback is a high-performance sports car that combines elegance and speed. It comes with a standard 2.9L twin-turbo V6 engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission, delivering 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. The RS5 is praised for its stylish design, luxurious interior, and impressive driving dynamics. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.9L V6**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**: Premature failure of the turbochargers, resulting in reduced engine power and whistling noises.
        * Solution: Replacement of turbochargers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Rattle**: Abnormal rattling noises from the timing chain, indicating excessive wear or stretching.
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain and related components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Hesitation**: Intermittent hesitation or delayed gear changes, particularly during acceleration or downshifting.
        * Solution: Software updates, valve body replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Dampers Failure**: Premature wear or failure of the adaptive dampers, leading to poor ride quality and handling issues.
        * Solution: Replacement of adaptive dampers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**: Occasional freezing, lagging, or unresponsive behavior of the infotainment system.
        * Solution: Software updates
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Issues**: Intermittent electrical problems, such as flickering lights, malfunctioning sensors, or dashboard warnings.
        * Solution: Inspection and repair/replacement of electrical harness
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panel Gap Issues**: Irregular gaps between exterior panels, resulting in a misaligned or uneven appearance.
        * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of body panels
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**: Inconsistent or malfunctioning climate control system, resulting in improper heating or cooling.
        * Solution: Replacement of sensors, actuators, or control unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Ventilation Issues**: Insufficient or ineffective ventilation for the rear seats.
        * Solution: Inspection and repair/replacement of ventilation system
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Audi RS5 Sportback offers impressive performance and luxury, it has reported issues in several areas. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being relatively minor and affordable to fix, while others can be more serious and expensive. Turbocharger failure, timing chain rattle, and suspension issues are among the more costly and concerning problems. However, it's important to note that these issues may not affect all RS5 Sportbacks, and many owners have reported a positive experience with the vehicle.
