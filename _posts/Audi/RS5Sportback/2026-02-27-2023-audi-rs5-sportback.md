# 2023 Audi RS5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi RS5 Sportback delivers high-performance driving dynamics paired with everyday usability, blending aggressive styling with luxury appointments. As a relatively modern performance vehicle, it generally benefits from solid contemporary engineering, though prospective buyers should evaluate powertrain health and overall vehicle maintenance history carefully.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6

This 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces approximately 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers robust performance and strong reliability when maintained with regular fluid changes, though buyers should watch for standard high-performance wear.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers quick, refined shifts and distributes power effectively through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust under normal operating conditions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi RS5 Sportback is an exciting and capable luxury performance car that offers strong powertrain options. While widespread recurring defect patterns are limited, used-car buyers should always perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify mechanical integrity and maintenance compliance.
