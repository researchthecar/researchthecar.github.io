# 2019 Audi RS5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi RS5 Sportback is a high-performance luxury liftback powered by a twin-turbo V6 engine paired with a quick-shifting automatic transmission. While it offers thrilling driving dynamics and striking styling, used car buyers should evaluate potential electrical and body integrity quirks reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration and strong everyday performance. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should watch for standard high-performance maintenance costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the Quattro all-wheel-drive system with smooth gear changes. This transmission is well-regarded for handling high power outputs reliably under normal operating conditions.

## Electrical

### Emergency Call (SOS) System

Electrical complaints center primarily around software and wiring glitches in connectivity features like the emergency call system.

**Intermittent SOS Malfunction and Cable Short**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an intermittent loud SOS alert while driving, accompanied by diagnostic code B18BD11 due to a potential cable short.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the wiring harness and replace the defective cable as necessary.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body components are generally durable, though rare reports of glass breakage warrant careful inspection.

**Unexpected Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof may shatter spontaneously while driving, producing a loud noise and scattering glass shards into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass panel for any preexisting stress fractures and replace the assembly if structural failure occurs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V760000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a rear crash, the incorrect cover can cause the 12-volt power supply to be compromised, preventing the vehicle doors from being opened from the outside, or the hazard warning lights from functioning, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the positive terminal cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi RS5 Sportback combines luxury and supercar-level performance, making it an exciting used car option. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the vehicle's electrical accessories and exterior glass integrity during a pre-purchase inspection.
