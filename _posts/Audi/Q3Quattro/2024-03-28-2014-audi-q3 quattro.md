**Introduction**
The 2014 Audi Q3 Quattro is a compact luxury SUV that offers a comfortable ride, sporty handling, and ample cargo space. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 200 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The Q3 also offers a quattro all-wheel drive system for enhanced traction.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, check engine light
  * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission control module (TCM) failure:** The TCM can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all.
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hard shifting, check engine light
  * Solution: Replace the TCM.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front control arm bushings wear:** The front control arm bushings can wear out prematurely, causing clunking noises and poor handling.
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises when turning or braking, vibration in the steering wheel
  * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely due to a fault in the electrical system.
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after short periods of time, difficulty starting the vehicle
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical fault.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the fault
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight condensation:** The headlights can develop condensation on the inside, affecting visibility.
  * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlights, reduced headlight output
  * Solution: Replace the affected headlights.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per headlight
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate latch failure:** The tailgate latch can fail, causing the tailgate to open unexpectedly.
  * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate opens unexpectedly, rattling noises when driving
  * Solution: Replace the tailgate latch.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof drain tube clogging:** The sunroof drain tubes can become clogged, leading to water leaks in the cabin.
  * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin, musty smell
  * Solution: Clear the drain tubes.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
The 2014 Audi Q3 Quattro is a reliable SUV, but it is not without its issues. The timing chain tensioner failure is a serious issue that can lead to catastrophic engine damage, but it is relatively rare. The other issues listed are less severe and less expensive to fix. Overall, the Q3 is a solid choice for those looking for a comfortable and sporty compact SUV, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
