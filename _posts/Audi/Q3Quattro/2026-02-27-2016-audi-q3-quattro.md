# 2016 Audi Q3 Quattro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Audi Q3 Quattro is a compact luxury crossover offering nimble handling, standard all-wheel drive, and an upscale cabin. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating the powertrain options and overall vehicle condition is essential to ensure long-term satisfaction without unexpected mechanical setbacks.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 200 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily driving with a balanced focus on performance and efficiency.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

This 6-speed traditional automatic transmission pairs with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system to deliver smooth power delivery and stable traction across various road conditions.
