# 2019 Audi A4 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi A4 allroad blends luxury, everyday practicality, and rugged styling into a compelling wagon package. For prospective used-car buyers, it offers a sophisticated interior and standard Quattro all-wheel drive, though shoppers should carefully evaluate overall mechanical history and maintenance records as vehicles age.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, though routine maintenance is crucial for long-term dependability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with standard all-wheel drive. Regular fluid changes are recommended to maintain smooth operation over time.

## Electrical

### Electrical Gauges and Charging

Electrical complaints are infrequent but can involve gauge and charging system anomalies.

**Electrical Failure and Check Engine Light**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an illuminated check engine light accompanied by electrical gauge or surge charger failures.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the specific electrical fault and replace failed components as necessary.

## Safety

### Pre Sense Collision Avoidance

Braking hardware is generally robust, though advanced driver-assistance features can occasionally exhibit erratic behavior.

**Unexpected Pre Sense Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The Audi Pre Sense system may unexpectedly and violently activate for a brief duration, causing sudden vehicle deceleration.
- **Solution:** Inspect sensor alignment and check for software updates or diagnostic trouble codes at an authorized service center.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi A4 allroad is a refined and capable luxury wagon, but used buyers should review service history and watch for minor electrical or driver-assistance quirks. A pre-purchase inspection by an independent specialist is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
