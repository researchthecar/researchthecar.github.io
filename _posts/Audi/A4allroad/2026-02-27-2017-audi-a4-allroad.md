# 2017 Audi A4 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Audi A4 allroad combines rugged wagon styling with refined luxury and standard Quattro all-wheel drive, making it an appealing choice for used-car buyers looking for both utility and driving dynamics. Powered by a turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a dual-clutch automatic transmission, it offers a nimble and responsive ride. Prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and inspect the vehicle thoroughly, as pre-owned luxury European wagons can incur higher-than-average maintenance costs as they age.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It is generally regarded as smooth and efficient, though proper maintenance and timely oil changes are critical for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs effectively with the standard Quattro all-wheel-drive system. While responsive and engaging, routine fluid services are essential to maintain shift quality and prevent premature wear.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V675000: Structure:Body:Door:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The sharp edges of the speaker trim increase the risk of injury to vehicle occupants.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the speaker cover aluminum trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V642000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles may have been programmed with new software that inadvertently makes the side marker lights inoperative in conjunction with the parking lights or the headlights. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment.".
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will upload revised software to correct the issue, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V955000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Additionally, certain 2017 Audi Q7, A4 Sedan, A4 Allroad, and 2018 Audi Q5 vehicles are being recalled because the seat belt pretensioners may not activate properly. In the event of a crash, if the air bags and/or the seat belt pretensioners do not inflate or function properly, the vehicle occupants have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen and Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected air bags and seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V836000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A passenger air bag module mounting screw may not have been sufficiently tightened, possibly affecting the deployment of the air bag in the event of a crash. If the air bag does not deploy correctly in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air bag mounting screw, tightening or replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V547000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Oxidation on the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) connecting cable may cause the software to misdiagnose a malfunction and disable the passenger air bag. A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install cable ties to the harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2017 Audi A4 allroad is a versatile and upscale wagon that offers a compelling blend of practicality and performance. While standard powertrain options are robust when properly maintained, buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals and budget for potential luxury vehicle repair costs. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by an independent European car specialist is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
