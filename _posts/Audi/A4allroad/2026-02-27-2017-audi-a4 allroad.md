# 2017 Audi A4 allroad

### Overview
The 2017 Audi A4 allroad is a premium compact wagon known for its blend of luxury, performance, and all-weather capability. Built on the B9 platform, it shares many components with the standard A4 sedan but features increased ground clearance, standard quattro all-wheel drive, and a more rugged exterior. This generation saw significant updates in technology and interior refinement. Reliability for the 2017 model year is generally considered good, with owners reporting fewer significant mechanical issues compared to some previous generations. However, as with any complex vehicle, certain areas may warrant attention.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi A4 allroad generally exhibits good reliability, particularly in its powertrain (2.0L TFSI engine and 7-speed S tronic transmission) and chassis components. The standard quattro all-wheel drive system is robust. The most commonly reported, though not widespread, issues tend to be related to the electrical system, specifically minor glitches within the infotainment interface and occasional battery drain concerns that require diagnostic attention. Exterior and interior components are typically well-built and hold up well. Overall, it's a dependable choice for those seeking a capable and luxurious all-weather wagon, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
