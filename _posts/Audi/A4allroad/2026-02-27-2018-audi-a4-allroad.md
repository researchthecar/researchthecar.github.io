# 2018 Audi A4 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Audi A4 allroad, buyers can generally expect a refined wagon combining luxury, utility, and standard Quattro all-wheel drive. While the vehicle offers impressive performance and interior comfort, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback concerning various electronic and safety systems. Performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection helps ensure all features operate as intended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for daily driving. It offers a smooth power delivery suited for both city commutes and highway cruising.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless gear shifts paired with Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Regular fluid maintenance is recommended to ensure long-term shifting refinement.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Camera System

Electrical components generally function well, though occasional owner reports highlight infotainment or camera glitches.

**Intermittent Rear View Camera**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that the backup camera occasionally fails to display an image when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of camera modules by a dealership may be necessary to resolve intermittent display faults.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Exterior panels and trim hold up well to weather, though glass integrity should be verified during an inspection.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported stress cracks developing across the windshield without any direct impact from road debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield thoroughly before purchase and replace it through an auto glass specialist if structural cracks are present.

## Safety

### Air Bags and Pre-Sense System

Advanced safety systems provide high levels of protection, but sensor calibrations should be verified for proper operation.

**Unexpected Pre-Sense Activation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected activation of the Audi pre-sense safety system, causing seatbelts to tighten or the vehicle to brake suddenly.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect sensor calibrations and check for any applicable software updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V675000: Structure:Body:Door:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The sharp edges of the speaker trim increase the risk of injury to vehicle occupants.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the speaker cover aluminum trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V836000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A passenger air bag module mounting screw may not have been sufficiently tightened, possibly affecting the deployment of the air bag in the event of a crash. If the air bag does not deploy correctly in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air bag mounting screw, tightening or replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V547000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Oxidation on the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) connecting cable may cause the software to misdiagnose a malfunction and disable the passenger air bag. A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install cable ties to the harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi A4 allroad remains an appealing choice for buyers seeking a capable luxury wagon with standard all-wheel drive. While most mechanical systems prove reliable, prospective owners should carefully check electrical accessories, driver assistance sensors, and glass condition. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection remains the best safeguard against unexpected repair costs.
