# 2022 Audi A4 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A4 allroad blends wagon versatility with luxury execution, featuring standard quattro all-wheel drive and a ruggedized stance. For prospective used-car buyers, researching common owner experiences helps set expectations regarding potential maintenance items. While powertrain options are generally refined, reviewing component-specific feedback ensures a well-informed purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration for daily driving. It pairs standard mild-hybrid technology with responsive power delivery, representing a sophisticated and generally reliable powerplant for this luxury wagon.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and optimizes both performance and fuel efficiency. Paired exclusively with quattro all-wheel drive, it offers smooth highway cruising and responsive downshifts when driven with enthusiasm.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi A4 allroad remains a compelling choice for shoppers seeking a luxury wagon with standard all-wheel drive and refined driving dynamics. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to ensure long-term satisfaction.
