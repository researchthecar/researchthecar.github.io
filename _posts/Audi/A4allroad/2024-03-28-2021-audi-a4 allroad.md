**Introduction:**

The 2021 Audi A4 allroad is a luxurious station wagon that combines sporty handling with rugged off-road capabilities. It features a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine with 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

Owners of the A4 allroad praise its refined interior, spacious cargo area, and versatile nature. However, some have reported encountering various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Engine oil levels drop rapidly, requiring frequent top-ups.
   * Solution: Replace faulty piston rings or valve stem seals.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks during gear shifts.
   * Solution: Update transmission software or replace clutch packs.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Leak:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Strut assembly leaks fluid, causing a loss of damping and noise.
   * Solution: Replace faulty strut assembly.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per strut
   * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Malfunctioning Infotainment System:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezes, crashes, or displays errors.
   * Solution: Update software or replace faulty module.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Paint Peeling on Door Handles:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Paint peels off the plastic door handles, exposing bare plastic.
   * Solution: Remove and repaint the door handles.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per door handle
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof rattles at high speeds or on rough roads.
   * Solution: Replace or adjust sunroof tracks.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
   * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
   * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system blows only hot or cold air, or fails to adjust temperature.
   * Solution: Replace faulty blend door or actuator.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Audi A4 allroad is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not immune to issues. Some owners have reported problems with engine oil consumption, transmission hesitation, suspension leaks, electrical malfunctions, and interior rattles. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be significant. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
