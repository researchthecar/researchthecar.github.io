**Introduction:**
The 2017 Audi A4 allroad is a luxury station wagon powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine producing 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT). The A4 allroad is praised for its spacious and comfortable interior, refined performance, and all-wheel drive capability. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as a high price tag and a firm ride.

**Engine/Transmission**

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Abnormal engine noise, reduced performance, and potential catastrophic engine damage if not addressed.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 21V590

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering, difficulty shifting, and eventual loss of drivability.
    * Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit, which is the transmission's control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery drain, especially when parked, requiring frequent battery replacements.
    * Solution: Identification and repair of electrical faults or replacement of faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin around the sunroof, causing damage to interior components.
    * Solution: Replacement of the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Bugs:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues with the infotainment system, including audio cutouts, navigation glitches, and touchscreen responsiveness.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2017 Audi A4 allroad is a well-built vehicle with excellent performance and features. However, there are a few notable issues that can be expensive to repair, such as timing chain tensioner failure, mechatronic unit failure, and battery discharge. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their ownership costs.
