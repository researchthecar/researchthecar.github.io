# 2021 Audi A4 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi A4 allroad combines rugged wagon styling with refined luxury and standard Quattro all-wheel drive. As shoppers consider a used model, understanding powertrain options and potential owner-reported electronic or driver-assist quirks helps ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers brisk acceleration and strong efficiency, making it a well-rounded choice for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and complements the Quattro all-wheel-drive system smoothly.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Owner feedback points to occasional unwarranted interventions by driver-assistance suites, which should be inspected during a test drive.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The emergency braking system occasionally deploys unexpectedly while the vehicle is stationary or moving slowly, activating the seat belt tensioners and applying the brakes.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's sensor calibration and software checked by a qualified technician.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi A4 allroad is a stylish and capable luxury wagon with a robust powertrain lineup. Buyers should thoroughly test the driver-assistance and safety systems during pre-purchase inspections to verify proper sensor calibration.
