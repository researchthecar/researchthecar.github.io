# 2023 Audi A4 allroad Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A4 allroad blends wagon practicality with luxury and standard quattro all-wheel drive. As a relatively recent model year, owner-reported reliability data is still maturing, making pre-purchase inspections and careful verification of remaining factory warranty coverage essential for prospective buyers.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers brisk acceleration and solid everyday efficiency, with no widespread structural reliability trends reported by owners.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear changes and power delivery through the standard quattro system. It generally operates smoothly under normal driving conditions, though buyers should test shift quality thoroughly during a test drive.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and MMI System

Owner reports indicate occasional software glitches within the MMI infotainment system affecting smartphone integration.

**Intermittent Apple CarPlay and Bluetooth Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The Apple CarPlay function may randomly become unavailable in wired or wireless modes, simultaneously causing Bluetooth hands-free calling to fail and preventing phone connection.
- **Solution:** Owners report consulting local dealerships for diagnostic evaluation, which may involve software updates or resetting the infotainment module.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A4+allroad&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi A4 allroad is a well-rounded and capable luxury wagon with minimal significant complaints logged so far. Buyers should verify proper operation of all infotainment and smartphone integration features during a test drive, and ensure any remaining manufacturer warranty transfers smoothly.
