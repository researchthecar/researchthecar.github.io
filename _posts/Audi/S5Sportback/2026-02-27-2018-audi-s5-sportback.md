# 2018 Audi S5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi S5 Sportback blends sophisticated styling with robust performance, making it a compelling choice for luxury sport sedan buyers. However, used-car shoppers should carefully review maintenance records and watch out for specific recurring mechanical and electrical issues reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo V6 (EA839)

The 2018 Audi S5 Sportback is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine (EA839) producing approximately 354 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. While delivering brisk acceleration and refined power delivery, buyers should monitor the cooling system closely for potential water pump leaks.

**Coolant Leak and Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant levels in the expansion tank, small coolant leaks on the engine, and potential vacuum system contamination leading to overheating.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed water pump, coolant reservoir, and affected hoses/vacuum lines to prevent further engine damage.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Power is routed through an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. This transmission generally offers smooth and responsive shifts under normal driving conditions.

## Suspension

### Coil Springs / Suspension

Suspension components provide a balanced mix of athletic handling and compliant daily ride quality, though individual mechanical parts can occasionally suffer premature wear.

**Broken Suspension Spring**
- **Symptoms:** Suspension warning light illuminates on the dashboard, often accompanied by a broken coil spring segment.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the damaged coil spring to restore proper ride height and handling stability.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Camera Systems

Advanced electronics and driver assistance features add modern convenience, but intermittent glitches in camera and sensor modules have been noted by some owners.

**Intermittent or Failing Rearview/Top View Cameras**
- **Symptoms:** The backup or top-view camera display blacks out, freezes, or reverts to a graphic mode instead of showing live surroundings.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection of the camera module, wiring harness, or software update may be required to resolve display errors.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior styling is sleek and aerodynamic, though rare instances of spontaneous glass failure on factory sunroofs warrant a careful visual check during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A loud popping sound resembling a gunshot occurs while driving, followed by the sunroof glass suddenly shattering.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for structural stress.

## Safety

### Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS)

Safety systems are comprehensive, but prospective buyers should verify that all occupant detection recalls and warning light concerns have been properly resolved.

**PODS Fault and Airbag Warning**
- **Symptoms:** An airbag warning message appears on the dashboard, and the passenger airbag may unexpectedly switch off even when occupied.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer inspect the wiring connection and address any related safety recalls or sensor harnesses.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V547000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Oxidation on the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) connecting cable may cause the software to misdiagnose a malfunction and disable the passenger air bag. A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install cable ties to the harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi S5 Sportback is an exhilarating and luxurious daily driver that commands attention. While major powertrain failures are relatively uncommon, buyers should pay close attention to cooling system integrity, camera and electrical quirks, and verify that all safety recalls—particularly concerning occupant detection systems—have been addressed.
