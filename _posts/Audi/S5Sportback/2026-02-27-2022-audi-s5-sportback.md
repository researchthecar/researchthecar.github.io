# 2022 Audi S5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi S5 Sportback blends striking styling with strong performance from its turbocharged V6 engine and standard all-wheel-drive system. For used-car buyers looking at this luxury performance hatchback, understanding the available powertrain options and typical owner-reported experiences is key to making a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and refined power delivery. It is generally robust, though buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals and service history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission provides smooth and quick gear shifts tailored for both sporty driving and cruising. It pairs well with the Quattro all-wheel-drive system and maintains a solid reputation for durability when properly serviced.
