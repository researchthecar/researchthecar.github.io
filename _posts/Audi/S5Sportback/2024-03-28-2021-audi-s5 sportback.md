**2021 Audi S5 Sportback**

The 2021 Audi S5 Sportback is a high-performance hatchback with a sleek design and luxurious interior. It offers a choice between a 349-horsepower 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine and a 349-horsepower 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine, both mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The S5 Sportback is praised for its handling, performance, and premium appointments, but some owners have reported issues with certain components.

### **Engine**
**3.0L Turbocharged V6 Engine**

**Intake Valve Deposits**
Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on intake valves can lead to reduced engine performance, increased emissions, and fuel economy issues.
Solution: Cleaning the intake valves and decarbonizing the engine.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6 Engine**

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of one or both turbochargers, resulting in decreased engine power, hesitation, and check engine light illumination.
Solution: Replacing the faulty turbochargers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**
**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Hesitation, rough shifting, or slipping gears.
Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid changes, or transmission rebuilds.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3,000+
Recalls: NHTSA 20V535 - Transmission May Shift into Neutral While Driving

### **Suspension**
**Front Suspension Control Arms**
Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged control arms can cause clunking noises, alignment issues, and reduced handling.
Solution: Replacing the faulty control arms.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
Recalls: None Found

### **Electrical**
**Infotainment System Glitches**
Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or permanent failure of the infotainment system, resulting in loss of audio, navigation, and other features.
Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: NHTSA 20V226 - Infotainment System May Not Function Properly

### **Exterior**
**Sunroof Rattling**
Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may rattle or vibrate while driving.
Solution: Adjusting or replacing the sunroof assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**
**Center Console Trim Peeling**
Description/Symptoms: The trim around the center console may peel or delaminate.
Solution: Replacing the trim piece.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2021 Audi S5 Sportback is generally a reliable vehicle, but some owners have reported issues with certain components, particularly the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of repairs vary depending on the issue and the extent of the damage. It's important for owners to stay up-to-date on recalls and regular maintenance to minimize the risk of costly repairs.
