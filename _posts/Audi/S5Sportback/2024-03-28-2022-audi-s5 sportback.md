**Introduction:**

The 2022 Audi S5 Sportback is a performance-oriented hatchback available with a single engine and transmission option. It boasts a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine producing 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and quattro all-wheel drive. Despite its high-performance capabilities, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine:**

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-offs or leading to engine damage. Solution: Audi has updated the engine oil control rings and pistons under warranty for affected vehicles. Estimated Cost to Fix: Covered under warranty. Recalls: NHTSA ID: 22V-542
* **Turbocharger Failure:** Description/Symptoms: A faulty turbocharger can cause a loss of power, decreased fuel economy, or excessive exhaust smoke. Solution: The turbocharger is typically replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Timing Chain Rattle:** Description/Symptoms: A loose or stretched timing chain can result in a rattling noise from the engine. Solution: Requires replacement of the timing chain and associated components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Transmission:**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Hesitations:** Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported occasional hesitations or delayed shifts while driving. Solution: Updating the transmission control software often resolves the issue. Estimated Cost to Fix: Covered under warranty. Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Power Window Failure:** Description/Symptoms: One or more power windows may fail to operate or become inoperable. Solution: Replacement of the window motor or control module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, such as freezing or touchscreen malfunctions. Solution: Software updates or replacement of the system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior:**

* **Paint Clear Coat Peeling:** Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported peeling or flaking of the paint clear coat, particularly in areas exposed to sunlight. Solution: Refinishing the affected areas. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior:**

* **Sunroof Seal Leaks:** Description/Symptoms: The sunroof seal may deteriorate or fail, causing water leaks into the cabin. Solution: Replacement of the sunroof seal. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Dashboard Creaks and Rattles:** Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced creaks and rattles from the dashboard or interior trim. Solution: Disassembly and tightening of loose components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may fail to regulate temperature or airflow properly. Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty components, such as the compressor or actuators. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary:**

The 2022 Audi S5 Sportback has some reported issues that can affect the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. While some of these issues are covered under warranty, others can be costly to fix. The severity and cost of these issues vary, so it's important for prospective buyers to be aware of them before purchasing a vehicle.
