# 2022 Audi S5 Sportback

### Overview
The 2022 Audi S5 Sportback, a performance-oriented variant of the A5 Sportback, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality engineering. Powered by a potent turbocharged V6 engine and typically paired with an advanced all-wheel-drive system, it offers a compelling blend of sportiness and practicality. Owners generally report a positive ownership experience with few recurring major mechanical faults. However, as with any high-performance vehicle, attention to maintenance schedules is crucial to ensure long-term dependability. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard wear and tear, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi S5 Sportback demonstrates a high level of reliability, with its core mechanical components, including the engine and transmission, showing no significant widespread issues. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. Adhering to the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule is key to preserving the S5 Sportback's performance and longevity. Overall, it is considered a dependable performance sedan for its class.
