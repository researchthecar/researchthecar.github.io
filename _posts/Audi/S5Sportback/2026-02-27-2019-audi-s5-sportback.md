# 2019 Audi S5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Audi S5 Sportback, buyers are generally drawn to its striking design, luxurious interior, and turbocharged performance. While owner-reported complaints are relatively limited, prospective owners should pay close attention to cooling system integrity and powertrain maintenance history during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (EA839)

The 2019 Audi S5 Sportback is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine (EA839) producing roughly 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust performance, though buyers should monitor the cooling system for potential leaks around the water pump and valves.

**Coolant Pump / Valve Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning messages regarding coolant levels or visible coolant residue near the mechanical coolant pump switching valve.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and switching valve for leaks, and replace failed coolant components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission routing power to the quattro all-wheel-drive system, this setup is generally robust and smooth. Routine fluid changes help maintain long-term reliability.

## Suspension

### Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS)

Suspension and wheel components are built to handle sporty driving dynamics, though routine checks of tire pressures and sensors remain important.

**TPMS Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Failure of the tire pressure monitoring system to alert the driver to dangerously low tire pressure.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire pressures manually during routine checks and have the TPMS sensors diagnosed if warning lights fail to operate correctly.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Odometer

Electrical systems on modern luxury vehicles are complex. Buyers should verify proper operation of all digital displays, sensors, and vehicle history records.

**Mileage Discrepancies**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistencies between actual vehicle condition, registration records, and reported odometer readings.
- **Solution:** Verify vehicle history reports and cross-check odometer records prior to purchase.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V114000: Suspension:Front:Shock Absorber](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Loose shock absorbers may impair vehicle steering, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the shock absorber forks, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi S5 Sportback offers an appealing blend of everyday practicality and high-end performance. Prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the cooling system and verify all vehicle history records to ensure a reliable ownership experience.
