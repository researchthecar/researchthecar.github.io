# 2021 Audi S5 Sportback

### Overview
The 2021 Audi S5 Sportback, a performance-oriented variant of the A5 Sportback, offers a compelling blend of sporty dynamics and everyday practicality. Powered by a potent turbocharged V6 engine and equipped with Audi's renowned Quattro all-wheel-drive system, it delivers exhilarating performance. The S5 Sportback generally exhibits strong build quality and a high level of technological integration, reflecting Audi's premium positioning. Reliability for the 2021 model year is largely positive, with owners reporting satisfaction with its powertrain and core systems. However, as with any performance vehicle, specific components may require attention over time, though widespread critical failures are not commonly cited in owner feedback and professional reviews.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi S5 Sportback demonstrates a strong reliability profile, particularly concerning its core powertrain and mechanical components. The 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and well-regarded. While the majority of systems operate without significant fault, minor electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches or occasional sensor issues with driver assistance features, have been noted by some owners. These are generally resolved with software updates or recalibration. Overall, the S5 Sportback is a dependable performance sedan, with potential issues being relatively minor and manageable, aligning with expectations for a premium, performance-oriented vehicle.
