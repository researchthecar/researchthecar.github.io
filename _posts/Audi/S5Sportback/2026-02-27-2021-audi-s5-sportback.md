# 2021 Audi S5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi S5 Sportback blends striking four-door coupe styling with robust turbocharged performance and standard Quattro all-wheel drive. As a luxury sport compact, it offers everyday practicality alongside strong enthusiast appeal. Prospective buyers looking at the used market will find a generally well-built vehicle, though reviewing maintenance history and outstanding recalls is always recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration. It is paired exclusively with an all-wheel-drive system and provides a refined, responsive driving experience for daily commutes and spirited drives alike.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

An 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission handles gear shifts, offering smooth operation in traffic and quick responses in manual mode. It complements the V6 powerplant well and has generally proven dependable under normal operating conditions.

## Suspension

### Suspension System

The adaptive suspension setup balances ride comfort and agile handling, though owners should verify that all factory campaigns have been completed.

**Suspension Component Recall Notice**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may receive recall notices regarding suspension components while awaiting replacement parts availability at dealerships.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized Audi dealer inspect the vehicle and perform any necessary recall repairs once parts become available.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V737000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harness for the rear lights could be missing the anti-abrasion protection in the area of the spare wheel well. Without the scuff protection, the wires may become damaged during a rear crash, disabling the doors from being opened from the outside, causing a loss of hazard lights, and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will apply scuff protection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi S5 Sportback is an appealing choice for used-car buyers seeking luxury, technology, and strong performance. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, verifying service records and ensuring safety recalls have been addressed will help secure a trouble-free ownership experience.
