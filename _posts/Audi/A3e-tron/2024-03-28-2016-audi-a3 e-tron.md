**Introduction:**

The 2016 Audi A3 e-tron is a plug-in hybrid vehicle that combines a 1.4L turbocharged gasoline engine with an electric motor for a combined output of 204 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It is available with a 6-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission. The A3 e-tron is praised for its fuel efficiency, quick acceleration, and comfortable ride. However, it has also been known to experience some issues.

**Engine**

* **1.4L TFSI**
    * **Valve Stem Seal Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed S tronic**
    * **Clutch Pack Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, difficulty shifting
        * Solution: Replace clutch pack
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-speed Manual**
    * **No Issues Found**

* **No Issues Found**

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Crossbars Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on crossbars
    * Solution: Replace crossbars
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: System freezes, slow response times
    * Solution: Software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Audi A3 e-tron offers excellent fuel efficiency and performance, but it has been known to experience some issues with its engine, transmission, electrical system, and exterior components. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost of repairs can be significant. It is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing or leasing an A3 e-tron.
