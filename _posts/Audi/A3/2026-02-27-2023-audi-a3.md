# 2023 Audi A3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A3 offers a refined entry into luxury compact sedans, combining upscale technology, agile handling, and efficient powertrain engineering. As a relatively recent model year, owner feedback remains limited, but prospective buyers should still evaluate its core mechanical systems and cabin electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

The standard 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 201 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It provides smooth power delivery for daily commuting with generally solid reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with front-wheel drive or Quattro all-wheel drive. Regular fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term shifting smoothness and durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V901000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." Missing weight information may result in unintentional overloading of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi dealers will install an overlay label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi A3 presents a compelling option for used-car buyers seeking modern luxury, fuel efficiency, and confident road manners. Because owner-reported issues are minimal for this model year, shoppers are advised to perform a standard test drive, verify service history, and check all onboard electronics to ensure everything operates as expected.
