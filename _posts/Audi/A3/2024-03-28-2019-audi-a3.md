**2019 Audi A3**

The 2019 Audi A3 is a luxury subcompact car available with a choice of engines and transmissions:

* 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine with 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission
* 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine with 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission

Appreciated aspects of the A3 include its stylish design, comfortable interior, and peppy performance. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (184 hp)**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup:** Carbon deposits accumulate on the intake valves, leading to reduced engine performance, rough idling, and potential engine damage. Solution involves cleaning or replacing the intake valves. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * **Thermostat Failure:** The thermostat may fail, causing the engine to overheat or run cold. Symptoms include coolant leaks, temperature gauge fluctuations, or engine performance issues. Solution involves replacing the thermostat. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (228 hp)**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail prematurely, resulting in a noisy engine, reduced performance, and potential engine damage. Solution involves replacing the timing chain and tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**
    * **Shifting Issues:** Some owners have reported experiencing rough shifting, delayed gear changes, or transmission slipping. Solution may involve software updates or transmission repairs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported experiencing battery drain issues, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution may involve replacing the battery or diagnosing electrical system faults. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:** The sunroof may develop leaks, allowing water to enter the cabin. Solution involves replacing or repairing the sunroof seals. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system may experience glitches or freeze, causing issues with navigation, audio, or other functionality. Solution may involve software updates or hardware repairs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing issues with heating, ventilation, or air conditioning. Solution may involve replacing sensors or actuators. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Summary**

While the 2019 Audi A3 offers a refined driving experience, it has been plagued by some reliability issues. Engine problems, such as intake valve carbon buildup and thermostat failure, can be costly to repair. The transmission has also been known to experience shifting issues, while the electrical system may face battery drain. Interior and exterior issues, such as sunroof leaks and infotainment system glitches, can further add to the expense and inconvenience. Owners should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing or owning the 2019 Audi A3.
