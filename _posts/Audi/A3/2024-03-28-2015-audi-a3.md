**Introduction**

The 2015 Audi A3 is a luxury subcompact sedan that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It is powered by a 1.8-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 170 horsepower and 199 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a 6-speed manual or a 6-speed automatic transmission.

The Audi A3 has received critical acclaim for its performance, handling, and luxurious interior. However, it has also been subject to some common issues that have affected owner satisfaction.

**Engine**

* **1.8-liter Turbocharged Inline-4**
    * **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption
        * Solution: Cleaning or replacement of intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, engine misfires, and reduced fuel efficiency
        * Solution: Replacement of fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Grinding gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into first or second gear
        * Solution: Replacement of transmission fluid or clutch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting hesitation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delay or hesitation when shifting gears
        * Solution: Software update or replacement of transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering when shifting gears
        * Solution: Replacement of transmission fluid or transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery loses charge prematurely, often overnight
    * Solution: Replacement of battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical gremlins**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent problems with various electrical systems, such as lights, windows, or radio
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of specific electrical component
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise coming from the sunroof while driving
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door panel rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise coming from the door panels while driving
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of door panel clips
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Audi A3 is a well-regarded luxury subcompact but has experienced some common issues that can affect owner satisfaction. These issues range from minor annoyances, such as interior rattles, to more serious problems, such as engine carbon buildup. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific model and year, so it is important to do your research before purchasing.
