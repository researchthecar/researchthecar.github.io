# 2022 Audi A3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A3 offers buyers a refined subcompact luxury sedan experience with modern technology and efficient powertrain options. When shopping for a used model, understanding the mechanical options and monitoring minor owner-reported concerns can help ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (40 TFSI)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 201 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, equipped with a mild-hybrid system. It offers a balanced blend of responsive daily performance and strong fuel efficiency for the A3 lineup.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and pairs with front-wheel or Quattro all-wheel drive. It provides a sporty driving dynamic while requiring regular scheduled fluid maintenance to ensure longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V224000: Child Seat:Vehicle Latch Anchor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." An incorrectly marked child restraint anchorage may cause confusion during child seat installation, resulting in a child seat being improperly secured and increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace all four fitting sleeves, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V515000: Seat Belts:Pretensioner](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The retention force of the seat belt tensioner on the driver and front passenger seats may be inadequate to properly restrain the occupants during a crash. Improperly restrained occupants have an increased risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver and front passenger seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V652000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An incorrectly folded air bag can tear, prohibiting it from deploying properly during a crash, increasing the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V823000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
