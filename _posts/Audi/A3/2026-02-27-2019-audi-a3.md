# 2019 Audi A3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Audi A3, buyers will find a refined and tech-forward luxury compact car that offers engaging driving dynamics. While owner reports to federal regulators highlight a few areas to watch—such as intermittent powertrain warnings and electrical accessories—prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect any pre-owned model and verify that all necessary safety campaigns have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque in front-wheel-drive configurations. It provides smooth, efficient power delivery for daily commuting, though routine maintenance is essential for long-term health.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

A higher-output variant of the 2.0-liter turbocharged engine generating around 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque paired with Quattro all-wheel drive. It offers brisk acceleration and solid performance, though owners have occasionally reported fuel system related drivability concerns.

**Fuel System Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden engine stalling while driving accompanied by check engine light codes such as P0148.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealer diagnose fuel pressure and delivery components while fault codes are active.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S Tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts across front-wheel-drive and Quattro trims. While generally crisp and responsive, prospective buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure smooth clutch engagement.

**Transmission Failure Warning and Shifting Erraticism**
- **Symptoms:** A 'Transmission Failure' warning message illuminates on the dashboard, and the transmission may inadvertently shift modes.
- **Solution:** Scan transmission control modules for diagnostic trouble codes and update software or inspect internal mechatronics components.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

The vehicle's complex electrical architecture powers advanced lighting and interior electronics. Owners should verify that all exterior lighting and driver aids operate correctly during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Headlight Assembly Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Premature failure of the headlight assembly units, resulting in non-functional running lights or main beams.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected headlight assembly unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V198000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the PODS sensor mat and update the PODS control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V815000: Tires:Pressure Monitoring And Regulating Systems](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The tire pressure monitoring system may not detect an air pressure loss in adequate time when deflating occurs among all four tires simultaneously. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 138, "Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems." An undetected sudden loss of pressure in all four tires.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the TPMS software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V110000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A3&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the suction pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi A3 is a stylish and capable luxury car, but used buyers should exercise due diligence. Be sure to check the vehicle service history, test the powertrain thoroughly for smooth shifting and reliable power delivery, and confirm that all open safety recalls have been addressed by a franchised dealer.
