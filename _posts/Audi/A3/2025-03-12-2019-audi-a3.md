Here's a breakdown of common issues and complaints for the 2019 Audi A3, organized by category and engine/transmission options.

The 2019 Audi A3 is a compact luxury car appreciated for its stylish design, agile handling, and well-appointed interior. Engine options include a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder producing either 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque (40 TFSI) or 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque (45 TFSI). All trims come standard with a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. Common likes include the car's responsive acceleration, comfortable ride, and high-quality materials. Common dislikes include the limited rear seat space and the sometimes-complex infotainment system.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI (40 TFSI - 184 HP)**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, requiring more frequent oil top-offs between scheduled services. Blue smoke from the exhaust can sometimes be observed.
    *   Solution: In some cases, a software update addressing the engine management system may reduce oil consumption. In more severe instances, a piston ring replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 (software update) - $3,000+ (piston ring replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**2.0L TFSI (45 TFSI - 228 HP)**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic**

*   **Hesitation and Jerky Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners experience hesitation when accelerating from a stop or during low-speed maneuvers. Jerky shifts, particularly between lower gears, have also been reported.
    *   Solution: A software update to the transmission control module (TCM) may resolve the issue. In some cases, a clutch replacement may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 (software update), $1,500 - $3,000+ (clutch replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The MMI infotainment system can occasionally freeze, crash, or become unresponsive. This can affect navigation, audio, and other vehicle functions.
    *   Solution: A software update to the MMI system may resolve the issue. In some cases, a complete system reset or replacement of the MMI unit may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 (software update), $1,000+ (MMI unit replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Faulty Ambient Lighting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The ambient lighting system may flicker, malfunction, or stop working altogether. This could be due to a faulty LED module or wiring issue.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty LED module or repair any damaged wiring.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+, depending on the extent of the issue and if more than one light is out.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Rattle**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may rattle, especially when driving on uneven surfaces.
    *   Solution: Inspection and lubrication of the sunroof tracks and seals. In some cases, adjustment or replacement of the sunroof components may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Headliner Sagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The headliner material may start to sag or detach from the roof, particularly around the edges.
    *   Solution: Re-glue the headliner material or replace the entire headliner assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 Audi A3 has a mix of potential issues. The most significant concern appears to be the possibility of higher oil consumption in the lower HP version of the 2.0L engine, which could lead to substantial repair costs if piston ring replacement is necessary. While the transmission and electrical issues are also present, they are less critical. The rest of the potential issues are minor, but all should be considered when purchasing the vehicle.
