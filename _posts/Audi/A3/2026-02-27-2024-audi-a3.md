# 2024 Audi A3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2024 Audi A3 means considering a refined compact luxury sedan that balances nimble handling with modern technology. While overall owner feedback is limited, buyers should inspect the electrical and charging systems carefully to ensure accessories and battery management operate without premature drain issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (40 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 201 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers smooth power delivery and balanced efficiency for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

A quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission channels power to either the front wheels or Quattro all-wheel drive, providing responsive gear changes during spirited driving.

## Electrical

### 12V Battery and Charging System

Electrical complaints center around battery state-of-charge management during infrequent use.

**Premature 12V Battery Depletion**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the check engine light illuminates if the vehicle sits unridden or is driven for less than 20 minutes a day due to low 12V battery charge.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or technician to verify battery health and software state, keeping in mind that occasional trickle charging or longer drive cycles may be required for power management systems.
