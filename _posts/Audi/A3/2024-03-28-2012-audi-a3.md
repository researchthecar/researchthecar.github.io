**2012 Audi A3**

The 2012 Audi A3 is a compact luxury hatchback that offers a variety of engine and transmission options. The 2.0L TFSI engine produces 200 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.5L TFSI engine produces 300 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, a 6-speed automatic, and a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic.

The A3 is praised for its stylish exterior, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it has also been criticized for its high price and lack of standard features.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, loss of power, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Remove intake manifold and clean or replace intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L TFSI**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* No Issues Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, transmission slipping, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: 13V189000

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, vehicle won't start
    * Solution: Replace battery, inspect for parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* No Issues Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trim on center console peeling or chipping
    * Solution: Replace center console trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Audi A3 is a reliable vehicle with relatively few reported issues. The most common problems are related to the engine (carbon buildup on intake valves) and transmission (shift solenoid failure, mechatronic unit failure). These issues can be costly to repair, but they are not widespread. Overall, the 2012 Audi A3 is a good choice for those who want a stylish and refined compact luxury hatchback.
