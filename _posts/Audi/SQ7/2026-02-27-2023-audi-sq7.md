# 2023 Audi SQ7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi SQ7 is a high-performance luxury SUV combining everyday practicality with sports-car acceleration. As a relatively recent model year, owner feedback remains sparse, and prospective buyers should weigh its advanced tech and powerful engineering against typical modern luxury vehicle maintenance considerations.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and robust capability. It is generally praised for its refined power delivery, though maintenance and servicing costs associated with high-output German performance engines tend to be elevated.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the quattro all-wheel-drive system across varied driving conditions. It operates transparently in everyday traffic while offering responsive manual shifts when dynamic performance is desired.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ7&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ7&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi SQ7 is a formidable luxury performance SUV that currently lacks significant recurring complaint patterns from early owners. Buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to ensure peace of mind.
