# 2024 Audi SQ7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2024 Audi SQ7 offers luxury performance and twin-turbo V8 capability, but it is always wise to inspect newer models thoroughly for early-production quirks. As a high-performance luxury SUV, it combines everyday practicality with immense power, though prospective buyers should review available maintenance histories and test all onboard electronics prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

This potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, delivering explosive acceleration for a large luxury SUV. It generally offers robust performance, though buyers should ensure consistent oil service intervals were maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive, this setup provides quick and smooth gear changes. The transmission is engineered to handle high torque loads reliably under normal driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Console USB-C Ports

Issues reported by owners indicate potential concerns with cabin charging ports and internal wiring connections.

**USB-C Connector Push-In and Shorting**
- **Symptoms:** USB-C ports on the front and second-row center consoles may push inward during use, potentially leading to electrical shorts and overheating circuit boards.
- **Solution:** Inspect the console USB ports for physical looseness or damage, and have a qualified technician replace faulty assemblies if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ7&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ7&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
