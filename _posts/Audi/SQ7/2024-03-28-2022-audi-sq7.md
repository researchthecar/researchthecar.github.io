**2022 Audi SQ7**

The 2022 Audi SQ7 is a performance-oriented SUV powered by a 503-horsepower 4.0-liter V8 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable and luxurious interior, impressive performance, and state-of-the-art technology. However, it also has its fair share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **4.0L V8**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors can fail, causing the engine to run rough, misfire, or stall.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifts, or it may slip between gears.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may malfunction, causing the vehicle to sag or lose ride height.
    * Solution: Replace the air suspension struts or compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge prematurely, leading to starting problems.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or troubleshoot the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, such as freezing, navigation errors, or connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Grill Trim Loosening**
    * Description/Symptoms: The chrome trim around the grille may loosen or detach.
    * Solution: Reattach or replace the trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof may leak water or produce wind noise.
    * Solution: Inspect and reseal the sunroof or replace the sunroof motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Audi SQ7 is a competent performance SUV, but it has a history of reported issues, particularly with the fuel injectors, transmission, and air suspension. These issues can be costly to repair, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Owners may also experience problems with the electrical system, exterior trim, and interior components, such as the panoramic sunroof.
