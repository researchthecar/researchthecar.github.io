# 2022 Audi SQ7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi SQ7 delivers high-performance luxury and everyday utility, anchored by a twin-turbo V8 engine and a responsive automatic transmission. As a relatively new and complex luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history and remaining factory warranty coverage.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces roughly 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It provides blistering acceleration for the performance SUV, though ongoing maintenance demands close attention to complex turbocharging and cooling systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth shifts and pairs standard Quattro all-wheel drive with high torque loads. It generally operates reliably under normal driving conditions when fluid changes are maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi SQ7 is a compelling luxury performance choice for buyers seeking V8 power and upscale features. Ensure any pre-owned model undergoes a comprehensive mechanical inspection to check for potential high-end maintenance requirements.
