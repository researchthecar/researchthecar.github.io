The 2022 Audi SQ7, a high-performance luxury SUV, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality engineering. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, examining key areas such as the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, exterior, and interior. The SQ7's sophisticated systems require diligent attention, but widespread critical failures are not a common theme based on available data.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Compressor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'suspension fault' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Gradual or sudden loss of ride height, uneven stance, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking air springs.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per spring


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, connectivity issues (Bluetooth/Apple CarPlay/Android Auto).
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor Calibration Issues**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings from adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors.
- **Solution:** Recalibration of sensors, potential cleaning or replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi SQ7 demonstrates commendable reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have not shown significant widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter technical concerns are related to the sophisticated adaptive air suspension system, which can experience compressor malfunctions or air spring leaks, and certain electrical components, such as infotainment system glitches or ADAS sensor calibration needs. These issues, while requiring attention and potential cost, are generally manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws across the vehicle. Overall, the SQ7 offers a reliable luxury performance SUV experience, provided owners are aware of and prepared for potential maintenance on its advanced systems.
