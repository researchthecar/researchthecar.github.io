**Introduction**

The 2012 Audi TTS is a performance-oriented sports car known for its sleek design, handling, and premium amenities. It is powered by a 265 horsepower 2.0-liter TFSI engine mated to a 6-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 340 horsepower 2.5-liter TFSI engine with a 7-speed S tronic transmission and a 268 horsepower 2.0-liter TDI diesel engine with a 6-speed manual transmission. Drivers appreciate the TTS's agility, responsive acceleration, and luxurious interior. However, it has also been subject to several issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on intake valves leads to reduced airflow, engine misfires, and power loss.
  * Solution: Intake valve cleaning and walnut blasting.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Worn timing chain tensioner can result in a rattling sound from the engine, reduced power, and potential catastrophic engine damage.
  * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L TFSI**

* **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Faulty high-pressure fuel pump can lead to loss of power, engine stalling, and difficulty starting.
  * Solution: Replacement of high-pressure fuel pump.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L TDI**

* **Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) Cooler Issues:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Failure of EGR cooler can lead to decreased engine performance, reduced fuel efficiency, and engine overheating.
  * Solution: Replacement of EGR cooler.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: 14V223, 14V224, 14V540

**Transmission**

**6-Speed S tronic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Faulty mechatronic unit can lead to transmission slipping, gear changes not occurring, and transmission failure.
  * Solution: Replacement of mechatronic unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Clutch Pack Wear:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Worn clutch packs can result in slipping, shuddering, and difficulty changing gears.
  * Solution: Replacement of clutch packs.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed S tronic**

* **Harsh Shifting:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Delay or jolt in gear changes, especially at lower speeds.
  * Solution: Software update or replacement of transmission control module.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Bushing Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Worn or torn rear suspension bushings can cause clunking noises, reduced stability, and uneven tire wear.
  * Solution: Replacement of rear suspension bushings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Steering Rack Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Power steering failure, heavy steering, and warning lights on dashboard.
  * Solution: Replacement of electrical steering rack.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle.
  * Solution: Replacement of battery or investigation of parasitic drain.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Condensation:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Moisture build-up inside fog lights, leading to reduced visibility.
  * Solution: Inspection and replacement of fog lights if necessary.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or creaking noises from the panoramic sunroof while driving.
  * Solution: Lubrication or adjustment of sunroof components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Audi TTS is a high-performance vehicle with potential issues that can be costly to repair. Engine issues, such as carbon buildup and timing chain tensioner failure, are common. Transmission problems, like mechatronic unit failure and clutch pack wear, can also be expensive to fix. Electrical issues, including electrical steering rack failure and battery discharge, have been reported. Additionally, suspension bushing failure and interior rattles can affect the vehicle's ride quality and comfort. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and consider regular maintenance and repairs to minimize their impact.
