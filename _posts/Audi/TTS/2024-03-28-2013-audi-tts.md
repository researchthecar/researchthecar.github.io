**2013 Audi TTS**

The 2013 Audi TTS is a high-performance sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine that produces 265 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual or optional six-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The TTS comes standard with Quattro all-wheel drive.

Owners of the 2013 Audi TTS generally appreciate its powerful engine, agile handling, and luxurious interior. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI I4**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Clean or replace the intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, poor performance, and possible engine damage
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Synchronizer Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second and third gears
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts harshly, delays in gear changes, and possible loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noise from the front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the front suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Short Circuits**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random electrical failures, flickering lights, and possible fire hazard
    * Solution: Identify and repair or replace the affected section of the electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking inside the vehicle through the sunroof
    * Solution: Clean or replace the sunroof drain plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaks and Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises from the dashboard, especially on rough roads
    * Solution: Tighten loose panels or apply sound dampening materials
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, incorrect navigation, or audio system issues
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2013 Audi TTS is a capable and enjoyable sports car, it has encountered several issues that can be costly and inconvenient to repair. The severity of these issues varies, with some being more common and affecting a wider range of vehicles. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing a used TTS and factor in the potential costs of repairs.
