**2015 Audi TTS**

The Audi TTS is a high-performance sports car that combines luxury, performance, and technology. It's available as a coupe or a roadster, and in both rear-wheel drive and Quattro all-wheel drive configurations. The TTS features a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 292 horsepower and 280 pound-feet of torque. It's paired with a six-speed manual or a six-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission (DCT).

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of piston rings and/or valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and/or engine warning lights.
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, accompanied by a grinding noise.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of transmission components, such as the synchronizers or shift forks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed DCT**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage a gear or a sudden loss of power while driving.
    * Solution: Replacement of the clutch pack or transmission controller.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $2,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Premature Suspension Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive play or noise in the suspension, resulting in poor handling and stability.
    * Solution: Replacement of suspension components, such as control arms or bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200 per corner
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or permanent electrical malfunctions, causing various issues ranging from engine problems to inoperable features.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of specific electrical components or wiring harnesses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Loose or Misaligned Body Panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor fit and finish of body panels, resulting in gaps or misalignments.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of body panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per panel
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Squeaky Dashboard**
    * Description/Symptoms: Persistent squeaking or rattling noises from the dashboard area.
    * Solution: Installation of soundproofing materials or adjustment of dashboard components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Climate Control**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning temperature control, leading to excessive heat or cold in the cabin.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Vehicle Reliability Summary**

The 2015 Audi TTS has a mixed reliability record. Its engine and transmission issues can be severe and costly to repair. However, other issues, such as suspension and electrical problems, are less common and generally less expensive to fix. Overall, the TTS may not be the most reliable choice for a high-performance sports car, and potential buyers should be prepared for the possibility of significant repair costs.
