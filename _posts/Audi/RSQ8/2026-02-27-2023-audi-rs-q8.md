# 2023 Audi RS Q8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi RS Q8 combines supercar-level performance with luxury SUV practicality, featuring a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As a high-performance modern vehicle, prospective used-car buyers should weigh its exceptional engineering against the high maintenance costs and complexity typical of German luxury performance vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance paired with mild-hybrid technology, though buyers should expect demanding maintenance schedules and high fueling costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts while efficiently managing the high torque output of the V8 engine across all driving modes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi RS Q8 is an immensely capable and thrilling performance SUV that commands attention on the road. Used-car buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a specialist, maintain a healthy budget for upkeep, and verify service history to enjoy this flagship luxury vehicle with confidence.
