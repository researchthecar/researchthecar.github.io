# 2021 Audi RS Q8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi RS Q8 is a high-performance luxury SUV combining immense power with cutting-edge technology. Prospective buyers exploring the used market should review owner-reported experiences carefully, keeping in mind that high-performance platforms involve complex mechanical and electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating acceleration, though prospective buyers should maintain rigorous adherence to maintenance schedules given the high-stress nature of turbocharged performance engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid shifts paired standard with quattro all-wheel drive. It is well-matched to the power output of the V8 engine, offering both relaxed cruising and responsive performance.

## Steering

### Power Steering & Steering Control

Owner reports indicate rare potential for electrical anomalies impacting steering assist.

**Loss of Power Steering via Electrical Fault**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an electrical system malfunction warning, leading to a complete shutdown and sudden loss of steering capabilities.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle towed to an authorized service center for diagnostic evaluation and replacement of affected electrical components or alternators.

## Electrical

### Charging System & Electrical Architecture

The complex electrical network supports numerous luxury and performance features, though isolated owner complaints note critical charging system faults.

**Electrical System Malfunction and Charging Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle displays sudden electrical errors prompting immediate stopping, potentially resulting in extended wait times for parts backorders.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection by a qualified technician and replacement of faulty charging components such as the alternator.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V339000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Cracks may cause engine damage and a stall or an oil leak, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V201000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An instrument panel display that fails to show critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi RS Q8 offers supercar-level performance wrapped in a practical SUV body. While overall mechanical complaints remain limited, buyers should verify the health of the vehicle's electrical and charging systems prior to purchase.
