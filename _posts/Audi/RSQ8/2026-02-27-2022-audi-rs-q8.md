# 2022 Audi RS Q8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi RS Q8 delivers blistering performance from its twin-turbocharged V8 engine, combining supercar capabilities with luxury SUV practicality. As a high-performance flagship, prospective buyers should approach a used model with careful inspection of its advanced powertrain and complex electronic systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It offers immense power delivery and robust mechanical design, though maintenance requires strict adherence to scheduled intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission manages the high power output with smooth, rapid shifts and standard quattro all-wheel drive. It is generally robust under normal operating conditions when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi RS Q8 is an exceptionally engineered performance vehicle that demands diligent pre-purchase inspections and adherence to specialized maintenance routines. Buyers should prioritize examples with complete service histories to minimize the risk of expensive out-of-warranty repairs.
