The 2022 Audi RS Q8 is a high-performance SUV blending practicality with exhilarating performance. It comes equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, producing a robust 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners praise its luxurious interior, stunning acceleration, and confident handling. However, some criticisms include its high price tag, firm ride quality, and relatively poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**4.0L Twin-Turbo V8**

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the engine, loss of power, check engine light. Can be caused by oil starvation or bearing failure.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the failed turbocharger(s). Inspect oil lines and ensure proper lubrication.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $7,000+ (depending on if one or both turbos need replacing and if a dealer or independent shop does the work)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warning. Common leak areas include valve cover gaskets and turbocharger oil lines.
    *   Solution: Replace leaking gaskets and seals. Ensure proper torque specifications are followed during reassembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,500+ (depending on the location and severity of the leak)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifts, especially between 1st and 2nd gear, or 2nd and 3rd. Hesitation when accelerating from a stop.
    *   Solution: Software update for the transmission control module (TCM). In some cases, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or valve body replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 (software update) - $3,000+ (valve body replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sitting unevenly, compressor not activating, error message on the dashboard related to suspension. Can be caused by a leak in the air struts or failure of the air compressor.
    *   Solution: Identify and repair air leaks, replace faulty air struts or compressor. Recalibrate the suspension system.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+ (depending on the component needing replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, system freezes, random reboots, loss of functionality (navigation, audio, etc.).
    *   Solution: Software update for the infotainment system. If the problem persists, hardware replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1,500+ (hardware replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Parking Sensors, Blind Spot Monitoring)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: False alerts, system not functioning, error messages on the dashboard. Often caused by a faulty sensor or wiring issue.
    *   Solution: Diagnose the faulty sensor or wiring. Replace the affected sensor or repair the wiring.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Adaptive Headlight Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Headlights not leveling properly, error message on the dashboard, headlights aiming too low or high.
    *   Solution: Check the headlight leveling sensors and motors. Replace faulty components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof failing to open or close, getting stuck, making excessive noise.
    *   Solution: Lubricate the sunroof tracks and mechanism. Replace faulty sunroof motor or repair damaged tracks.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Audi RS Q8, while a performance-oriented and luxurious SUV, has some common issues. The most severe and costly issues are related to the turbochargers, air suspension, and potential valve body problems in the transmission. Electrical glitches, specifically with the infotainment system and sensors, are also reported. While not all vehicles will experience these issues, potential buyers should be aware of these possibilities and factor in potential repair costs when considering ownership. The issues range from moderate to severe, with repair costs varying accordingly.
