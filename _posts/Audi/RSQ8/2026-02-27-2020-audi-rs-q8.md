# 2020 Audi RS Q8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Audi RS Q8 delivers incredible performance and luxury, combining supercar power with everyday SUV utility. As a high-performance flagship, prospective used-car buyers should carefully evaluate its complex powertrain and advanced technology systems before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is generally robust, though its high-output nature requires diligent maintenance and premium fuel.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with quattro all-wheel drive for quick, seamless gear changes across various driving conditions. It is well-matched to the V8's power output and requires regular fluid changes to ensure long-term durability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+Q8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi RS Q8 is an exceptionally capable high-performance SUV that offers thrilling dynamics and luxury. Buyers should prioritize thorough pre-purchase inspections and verify service records to ensure the complex mechanical and electrical systems remain in top condition.
