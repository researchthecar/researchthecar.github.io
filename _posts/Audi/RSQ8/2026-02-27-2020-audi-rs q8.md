# 2020 Audi RS Q8

### Overview
The 2020 Audi RS Q8 is a high-performance luxury SUV that blends potent power with sophisticated technology. As a relatively new model in the performance SUV segment, its long-term reliability is still being established. However, initial data suggests a strong foundation, with the advanced powertrain and chassis systems generally performing well. Owners often praise its dynamic driving capabilities and premium interior. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the RS Q8 appears to be a robust offering, with most reported concerns being minor or related to the complexities inherent in a vehicle of this caliber. Regular maintenance and adherence to Audi's service schedule are crucial for ensuring optimal performance and longevity.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Audi RS Q8 demonstrates good initial reliability, particularly concerning its powerful 4.0L twin-turbo V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, which have not shown widespread issues. The primary areas where owners have reported concerns are the adaptive air suspension, with potential for leaks or malfunctions, and the sophisticated infotainment system, which can occasionally suffer from software glitches. Premature 12V battery failure has also been noted by a small number of owners. While these issues are not endemic, they represent the most common deviations from expected performance. Overall, the RS Q8 is a reliable performance SUV, but potential buyers should be aware of the costs associated with maintaining its advanced suspension and electronic systems.
