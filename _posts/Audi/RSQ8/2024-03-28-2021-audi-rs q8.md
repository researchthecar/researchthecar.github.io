**2021 Audi RS Q8**

The 2021 Audi RS Q8 is a high-performance SUV that combines luxury and sportiness. It comes standard with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 591 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. Other engine options include a 3.0-liter turbodiesel V6 and a 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6.

People like the Audi RS Q8 for its powerful engine, spacious interior, and luxurious features. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle, including:

**Engine**

**4.0-liter Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbochargers can fail prematurely, causing a loss of power and other performance issues. Solution: Replace the failed turbochargers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Oil Pump Failure:** The oil pump can fail, causing a loss of oil pressure and engine damage. Solution: Replace the failed oil pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, causing a delay in acceleration or deceleration. Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Malfunction:** The infotainment system may malfunction, causing the screen to freeze or become unresponsive. Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Climate Control Failure:** The climate control system may fail, causing the temperature inside the vehicle to become too hot or too cold. Solution: Repair or replace the faulty climate control component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary**

The 2021 Audi RS Q8 is a powerful and luxurious SUV, but it has some potential issues that buyers should be aware of. The most common issues include turbocharger failure, oil pump failure, shifting problems, electrical system failure, and infotainment system malfunction. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but they should be considered before purchasing an RS Q8.
