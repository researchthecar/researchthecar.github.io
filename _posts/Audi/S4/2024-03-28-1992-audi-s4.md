**Introduction:**

The 1992 Audi S4 is a high-performance variant of the Audi 90 quattro. It is powered by a 2.2L turbocharged inline-5 engine that produces 227 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The S4 is available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. It features a Torsen center differential and quattro all-wheel drive. Drivers appreciate the S4's handling, performance, and luxury features. However, it has a few known issues that should be considered before purchasing.

**Engine**

**2.2L Turbocharged Inline-5**

* **Head Gasket Failure:** 
Symptoms: White smoke from the exhaust, coolant loss, overheating. 
Solution: Replace the head gasket. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: NHTSA ID Number: 05V444000

* **Turbocharger Failure:** 
Symptoms: Loss of power, blue or black smoke from the exhaust, whistling or howling noise. 
Solution: Replace the turbocharger. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

* **Oil Leaks:** 
Symptoms: Oil spots under the car, low oil level on the dipstick. 
Solution: Replace leaking gaskets or seals. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Synchronizer Wear:** 
Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting. 
Solution: Replace the worn synchronizers. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure:** 
Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears, overheating. 
Solution: Replace the torque converter. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

* **Solenoid Failure:** 
Symptoms: Shifting problems, delayed shifts, harsh shifts. 
Solution: Replace the faulty solenoid. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** 
Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise over bumps, reduced handling. 
Solution: Replace the strut mounts. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

* **Ball Joint Failure:**
Symptoms: Clunking or popping noise when turning, uneven tire wear. 
Solution: Replace the ball joints. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues:** 
Symptoms: Electrical components malfunctioning, intermittent electrical problems. 
Solution: Repair or replace the damaged wiring harness. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

* **Alternator Failure:** 
Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery discharge, warning light on the dashboard. 
Solution: Replace the alternator. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Rust:**
Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, wheel wells, and undercarriage. 
Solution: Repair or replace rusted parts. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of rust

* **Headlight Lenses Fogging:** 
Symptoms: Headlight lenses becoming cloudy or hazy, reduced visibility at night. 
Solution: Replace the headlights. 
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
Symptoms: A/C not blowing cold air, heater not working properly.
Solution: Repair or replace the climate control components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

* **Power Window Failure:**
Symptoms: Window not operating properly, window falling off the track.
Solution: Repair or replace the window motor or regulator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

* **Dashboard Pixelation:**
Symptoms: Pixels on the dashboard display fading or becoming illegible.
Solution: Replace the dashboard cluster.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Summary**

Overall, the 1992 Audi S4 is a capable and desirable performance vehicle. However, it is not without its share of issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the age, mileage, and maintenance history of the vehicle. Potential buyers should carefully consider the potential costs and availability of parts before purchasing.
