# 2015 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2015 Audi S4, a performance-oriented variant of the A4 sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its performance capabilities. It features a potent supercharged V6 engine and a sophisticated quattro all-wheel-drive system. While it offers an engaging driving experience, like any performance car, it can be susceptible to specific issues related to its high-strung powertrain and advanced systems. Owners should be aware of potential concerns primarily centered around the engine's forced induction system and certain electronic components. However, with proper maintenance, the S4 can provide years of spirited driving.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 (TFSI)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, whistling noise from the engine, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Supercharger Belt Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whining noise from the front of the engine, potential for belt breakage.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger drive belt and potentially tensioner/idler pulleys.
- **Cost:** $200 - $450

**Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine performance, rough idle, misfires, decreased fuel economy.
- **Solution:** Intake manifold removal and walnut blasting or chemical cleaning of intake valves.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic (Dual-Clutch Automatic)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough shifting, shuddering during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Software Glitches**

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Audi S4 is a capable performance sedan with a generally good reliability record. The primary areas of concern revolve around the sophisticated 3.0L supercharged engine, particularly potential issues with the PCV system, supercharger belt, and carbon buildup. The S tronic dual-clutch transmission can also present costly repairs if the mechatronic unit or clutch packs fail. While the manual transmission is robust, the automatic requires diligent maintenance. Electrical gremlins, especially with the infotainment system, can occur. However, many owners report satisfaction with the vehicle's performance and overall build quality, provided regular maintenance is performed and potential issues are addressed promptly.
