**Introduction:**
The 2000 Audi S4 is a high-performance sports sedan that debuted with a 2.7L twin-turbocharged V6 engine, producing 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It was available with a 6-speed manual or 5-speed Tiptronic transmission. The S4 is known for its responsive handling and refined interior, but has a reputation for potential reliability issues.

**Engine**
* **2.7L Twin-Turbocharged V6**

**Intake Manifold Flap Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Plastic flaps in the intake manifold become brittle and break, causing a loss of power and rough idling.
Solution: Replace intake manifold.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Turbochargers can fail prematurely, leading to reduced power and oil leaks.
Solution: Replace turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **6-Speed Manual**

**Synchro Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Synchronizer rings can wear prematurely, causing difficulty shifting gears, especially at high speeds.
Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
Recalls: None Found

* **5-Speed Tiptronic**

**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The Mechatronic unit, which controls shifting, can malfunction, causing erratic shifting or even loss of gears.
Solution: Replace Mechatronic unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3000 - $5000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
Description/Symptoms: Control arm bushings can wear prematurely, leading to clunking noises and reduced handling stability.
Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Axle Carrier Bushings**
Description/Symptoms: Bushing failure can cause a clunking sound when accelerating or braking, and can lead to unstable handling.
Solution: Replace rear axle carrier bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The ballast that powers the xenon headlights can fail, causing the headlights to dim or not work at all.
Solution: Replace ballast.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Discharge**
Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely due to a parasitic draw, often caused by a failing alternator or starter.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the parasitic draw.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The blower motor that controls air flow can fail, causing no air to blow through the vents.
Solution: Replace blower motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Power Seat Failures**
Description/Symptoms: Electric motors that adjust the power seats can fail, causing the seats to become inoperable.
Solution: Replace failed motor or entire seat.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Rear Spoiler Vibration**
Description/Symptoms: The rear spoiler can vibrate at high speeds, creating a buzzing noise.
Solution: Install a spoiler support bracket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2000 Audi S4 has a number of potential issues that can range in severity and cost. Engine and transmission problems can be particularly expensive to repair. However, with proper maintenance and attention to potential issues, the S4 can still provide a thrilling and rewarding driving experience.
