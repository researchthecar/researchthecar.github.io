## 2017 Audi S4

The 2017 Audi S4 is a high-performance sedan that combines style, speed, and luxury. It features a turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 engine that produces 354 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels through Audi's Quattro all-wheel drive system.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Turbo**

- **Hesitation or Stalling**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The engine may hesitate or stall during acceleration or while idling.
  - **Solution:** Replace the fuel injector.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
- **Turbocharger Failure**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and excessive smoke.
  - **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

- **Jerking or Shuddering**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission may jerk or shudder during gear changes.
  - **Solution:** Update the transmission software or replace the transmission fluid.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $400+
- **Overheating**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission may overheat, causing a loss of power and warning lights to illuminate.
  - **Solution:** Replace the transmission cooler or flush the transmission fluid.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

- **Rear Suspension **
  - **Noise and Vibration**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** The rear suspension may make a clunking or rattling noise during driving.
    - **Solution:** Replace the rear suspension components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
- **Front Suspension**
  - **Premature Wear**
    - **Description/Symptoms:** The front suspension components may wear prematurely, leading to poor handling and increased tire wear.
    - **Solution:** Replace the affected components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The battery may drain prematurely while the vehicle is parked.
  - **Solution:** Replace the battery or repair electrical system connections.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+
- **Infotainment System Issues**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, freezing, or connectivity problems.
  - **Solution:** Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+
  - **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V327000

**Exterior**

- **Headlight Condensation**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights.
  - **Solution:** Replace the headlight assembly or ventilate the headlights.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
  - **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

- **Climate Control Malfunction**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing insufficient heating or cooling.
  - **Solution:** Repair or replace the climate control unit.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600+
- **Sunroof Noise**
  - **Description/Symptoms:** The sunroof may make a whistling or clicking noise during operation.
  - **Solution:** Align or replace the sunroof components.
  - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $700+

**Summary**

The 2017 Audi S4 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but it has some notable issues. The most significant concern is the turbocharger failure, which can be expensive to repair. Other common problems include hesitation or stalling, transmission jerking or overheating, and rear suspension noise. However, many of these issues can be addressed with regular maintenance and repairs. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary, so it's important to consider individual vehicle history and maintenance records before purchasing a used 2017 Audi S4.
