**2013 Audi S4**

The 2013 Audi S4 is a high-performance sedan known for its refined interior, potent engine, and exceptional handling. It is equipped with a 3.0L supercharged V6 engine that delivers 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a 6-speed manual or an optional 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT). The S4 has received high praise for its well-rounded performance, but it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

- **3.0L Supercharged V6 Engine**
--- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
----- **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive engine noise, loss of power, and illuminated check engine light.
----- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain tensioner.
----- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
----- **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

- **6-speed Manual Transmission**
--- **No Issues Found**
- **7-speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)**
--- **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
----- **Description/Symptoms:** Rough shifting, loss of gears, and transmission fault codes.
----- **Solution:** Replacement of mechatronic unit.
----- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
----- **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

- **Electrical Control Unit (ECU) Failure:**
--- **Description/Symptoms:** Engine misfires, electrical malfunctions, and communication errors.
--- **Solution:** Replacement of ECU.
--- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
--- **Recalls:** None Found

- **Battery Drain:**
--- **Description/Symptoms:** Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle.
--- **Solution:** Testing and replacement of faulty electrical components.
--- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
--- **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

- **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

- **Climate Control Malfunction:**
--- **Description/Symptoms:** Inoperative AC, heating, or ventilation systems.
--- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of climate control module.
--- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
--- **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2013 Audi S4 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but it is not immune to issues. The most severe and expensive problems are related to the engine and transmission, particularly the timing chain tensioner and mechatronic unit failures. While some electrical and interior issues have been reported, they appear to be less common. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and the potential costs associated with them before making a decision.
