# 2001 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2001 Audi S4, a performance-oriented variant of the B5 generation A4, is renowned for its potent twin-turbocharged V6 engine and quattro all-wheel-drive system. While offering exhilarating driving dynamics, this model is also known for specific reliability concerns that are common for its era and performance focus. Owners often face issues related to the complex powertrain, particularly the twin-turbo setup and associated cooling systems. Electrical gremlins and interior wear are also points of attention. Despite these potential drawbacks, the S4 remains a desirable car for enthusiasts, with many issues being well-documented and repairable by knowledgeable technicians. Regular and diligent maintenance is crucial for mitigating many of these common problems.

---


## Engine

### 2.7L Twin-Turbo V6 (APB)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, blue smoke from exhaust, whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Replace turbochargers, often with upgraded units.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $5000+

**PCV System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, increased oil consumption, boost leaks.
- **Solution:** Replace the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) system components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Coolant Flange Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, sweet smell of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replace the plastic coolant flange, often with an aluminum upgrade.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Camshaft Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks around the timing belt area, potential for oil contamination of the timing belt.
- **Solution:** Replace camshaft seals, often done during timing belt service.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual (01E)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replace clutch kit (disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Synchronizer Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding when shifting into certain gears, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replace transmission.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000+


### 5-Speed Automatic (01V)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering, slipping, delayed engagement, transmission fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Replace or rebuild torque converter, potentially transmission service.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Valve Body Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed shifts, failure to shift.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace valve body.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1800


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replace worn control arm bushings or entire control arms.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Shock Absorber/Strut Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, reduced handling, visible leaks.
- **Solution:** Replace shock absorbers or struts.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not working, slow operation, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replace window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idle, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300 per coil

**ABS Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** ABS warning light illuminated, loss of ABS function.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace ABS control module.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Exterior

### Body Panels & Trim
**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture buildup inside headlight housings.
- **Solution:** Seal headlight housings, or replace if severely damaged.
- **Cost:** $50 - $200

**Paint Fade/Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of clear coat, fading of paint color, especially on roof and hood.
- **Solution:** Repaint affected panels.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Headliner Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Fabric lining of the roof detaches and sags.
- **Solution:** Re-glue or replace headliner material.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replace HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Pixelated Instrument Cluster Display**
- **Symptoms:** Missing or garbled segments on the LCD display of the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2001 Audi S4 is a performance icon with a complex powertrain that demands attention. Its twin-turbo V6, while powerful, is prone to issues like turbocharger failure, PCV system problems, and coolant leaks, necessitating specialized knowledge and potentially significant repair costs. The manual transmission is generally robust but subject to clutch wear, while the automatic can experience torque converter and valve body issues. Suspension components, particularly control arm bushings, are common wear items. Electrical gremlins, such as window regulators and ABS modules, and interior wear like sagging headliners and pixelated displays, are also frequently reported. Despite these challenges, the S4 offers a thrilling driving experience for those willing to invest in its upkeep.
