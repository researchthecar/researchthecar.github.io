# 2024 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2024 Audi S4 represents a compelling blend of performance and luxury, building upon Audi's reputation for sophisticated engineering. As a performance-oriented variant of the A4 sedan, the S4 typically features a potent turbocharged V6 engine, a sport-tuned suspension, and an array of advanced technology. Reliability for the 2024 model year is generally strong, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality and durability. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the S4 is designed with robust components and advanced manufacturing processes to minimize common problems. Owners can expect a refined driving experience with a focus on long-term dependability, though as with any high-performance vehicle, diligent maintenance is key to preserving its optimal condition.

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Audi S4 demonstrates a high level of reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically related to occasional infotainment software glitches and potential sensor calibration needs for ADAS features. These are generally manageable and often resolvable with software updates or professional recalibration. Overall, the S4 is a dependable performance sedan, provided routine maintenance is performed. Its robust build quality and advanced engineering contribute to a positive ownership experience with minimal expected major mechanical failures.
