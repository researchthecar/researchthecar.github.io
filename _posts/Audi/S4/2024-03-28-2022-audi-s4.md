**2022 Audi S4**

The 2022 Audi S4 is a high-performance sedan that offers a blend of luxury, sportiness, and practicality. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine that produces 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. Other available engine options include a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine with 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque.

Drivers appreciate the S4's powerful engine, responsive handling, and spacious interior. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

* **3.0-liter turbocharged V6**
    * **Intake valve carbon buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Over time, carbon buildup can accumulate on the intake valves, reducing airflow and engine performance. Symptoms include reduced power, hesitation, and rough idling.
        * Solution: The intake valves must be cleaned or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting hesitation**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control module or replacing the transmission may be necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive air suspension**
    * **Air spring leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Air springs can develop leaks, causing the suspension to sag or become uneven.
        * Solution: Replacing the faulty air springs is required.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 per air spring
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Premature battery failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may fail prematurely, causing electrical problems or difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replacing the battery is necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body panels**
    * **Paint defects**
        * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported premature paint fading or peeling.
        * Solution: Reparing or repainting the affected body panels may be necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center console**
    * **Creaking or rattling noises**
        * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced creaking or rattling noises coming from the center console.
        * Solution: Tightening or replacing the affected components may be necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 Audi S4 is a well-rounded performance sedan with impressive features. However, owners have reported issues with various components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be costly to repair.
