# 2019 Audi S4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi S4 is a high-performance sport sedan that combines luxury with potent twin-turbocharged power. While early owner feedback suggests engaging driving dynamics and solid overall engineering, prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and conduct thorough pre-purchase inspections to check for mechanical or electronic anomalies.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L V6 Turbocharged

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces roughly 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration. It is generally robust, though buyers should listen closely for any unusual top-end engine noises during a test drive.

**Valvetrain Noise and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience unusual ticking or knocking noises emanating from the top end of the motor, potentially indicating rocker arm or valvetrain wear.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the valvetrain components and replace any worn parts as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and power to Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. It is widely regarded as a durable unit when maintained with regular fluid changes.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide strong stopping power, but electronic glitches involving driver-assistance integration should be monitored.

**Unexpected Brake Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden brake engagement accompanied by an illuminated ABS warning light and pre-sense safety system messages.
- **Solution:** Inspect the anti-lock braking system sensors, brake modules, and software calibration for faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi S4 is an appealing used sport sedan offering impressive performance and luxury appointments. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive maintenance records and perform a detailed inspection focusing on the engine valvetrain and electronic braking systems.
