# 2020 Audi S4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Audi S4 delivers exhilarating performance and upscale luxury, but prospective buyers should keep an eye out for potential electronic issues. While overall mechanical durability is typically strong, owner complaints highlight recurring software glitches that affect daily usability.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

Powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 generating 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, this engine provides brisk acceleration and refined power delivery. It is generally robust when maintained properly with timely fluid changes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive, offering smooth and responsive gear shifts. This unit is well-regarded for durability under normal driving conditions.

## Electrical

### MMI Infotainment and Virtual Cockpit

Electrical complaints center heavily on infotainment software glitches and occasional instrument cluster display failures.

**Infotainment System Rebooting**
- **Symptoms:** The main MMI screen and virtual cockpit repeatedly shut down and restart every few minutes while driving, disabling audio, navigation, and hands-free calling.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module replacements performed by a dealership can sometimes resolve persistent reboot loops.

**Instrument Cluster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster fails to boot up, resulting in a completely black or blank screen while the vehicle is running.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing and potential replacement of the instrument cluster unit.

### Backup Camera and Display System

Safety system concerns are primarily tied to software instability affecting driver visibility aids like the rearview camera.

**Backup Camera Feed Interruption**
- **Symptoms:** When shifting into reverse, the rearview camera feed turns off or fails to display properly due to ongoing infotainment system reboots.
- **Solution:** Updating the vehicle software or addressing the underlying MMI stability issue usually restores proper camera function.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi S4 is a compelling sport sedan that combines luxury with strong performance, but buyers should thoroughly test the infotainment and electrical systems during a pre-purchase inspection. Checking for updated software versions can help mitigate common screen-reboot and camera-display frustrations.
