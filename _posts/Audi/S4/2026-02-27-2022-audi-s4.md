# 2022 Audi S4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi S4 blends refined everyday usability with sports-sedan performance, anchored by a turbocharged V6 engine and standard quattro all-wheel drive. For used-car buyers, exploring its powertrain options and monitoring any emerging owner-reported glitches will help ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (349 hp)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong acceleration. It is generally robust, though buyers should verify maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission provides smooth and quick gear changes suited to both commuting and spirited driving. Routine fluid changes are recommended for long-term durability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi S4 offers a compelling mix of luxury and performance for used-car shoppers. While current owner feedback highlights few widespread mechanical defects, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and service history review remain essential steps before buying.
