# 2022 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2022 Audi S4, a performance-oriented variant of the A4 sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality engineering. Powered by a potent turbocharged V6 engine, it offers exhilarating performance coupled with a refined driving experience. Owners typically report satisfaction with the vehicle's build quality, technology integration, and dynamic capabilities. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the S4 has demonstrated a solid track record, with most reported concerns being minor and easily addressed. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential mechanical and electrical vulnerabilities.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi S4 demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, with its core powertrain components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential sensor malfunctions for driver assistance features. These are generally resolvable through software updates or component recalibration/replacement. The suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from reported widespread problems. Overall, the S4 offers a dependable performance sedan experience, with most potential issues being minor and manageable, especially within the warranty period.
