# 2002 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2002 Audi S4, a performance-oriented variant of the B6 generation, is renowned for its potent twin-turbocharged V8 engine and quattro all-wheel-drive system. While offering exhilarating driving dynamics, this model is known to have specific reliability concerns that owners should be aware of. The complexity of its powertrain, particularly the twin-turbo setup and associated systems, can lead to costly repairs if not meticulously maintained. Common areas of concern include the engine's timing chain system, turbocharger issues, and various electrical gremlins. The suspension, while robust for performance driving, can also experience wear over time. Prospective buyers should carefully consider the potential for maintenance and repair costs associated with this enthusiast vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 4.2L Twin-Turbo V8 (253 kW)
**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the rear of the engine, check engine light, potential catastrophic engine failure if ignored.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chains, tensioners, guides, and seals. This is a labor-intensive job.
- **Cost:** $3000 - $6000+

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling or hissing sounds from the engine bay, excessive smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbochargers and associated hoses/lines.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $5000+

**PCV System Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, increased oil consumption, potential for vacuum leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the PCV valve and associated hoses.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 6-Speed Automatic (Tiptronic)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter and potentially the transmission.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or entire control arms.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Shock Absorber/Strut Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, reduced handling, visible leaks from shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of shock absorbers or struts.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, grinding noises when operating windows.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on the highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idle, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per coil

### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Audi S4 is a desirable performance car with a powerful V8 engine and engaging driving dynamics. However, its complexity translates to potential reliability concerns, particularly with the engine's timing chain system and turbochargers, which can incur significant repair costs. Transmission issues, especially with the automatic, and wear on suspension components are also noted. Electrical gremlins, such as window regulators and ignition coils, can also arise. While the exterior and interior are generally robust, potential buyers must be prepared for higher-than-average maintenance and repair expenses. Thorough pre-purchase inspection and a history of diligent maintenance are crucial for ownership.
