# 2021 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2021 Audi S4, a performance-oriented variant of the A4 sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its potent powertrain. It features a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, delivering exhilarating performance. Owners typically praise its sophisticated interior, advanced technology, and engaging driving dynamics. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the S4 has demonstrated a solid track record for its model year, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific component failures rather than widespread systemic problems. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its long-term dependability and performance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi S4 presents a strong case for reliability within the luxury performance sedan segment. Its core mechanical components, the 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, are robust and have not shown significant widespread issues. The chassis and suspension systems also appear to be well-engineered for durability. Minor concerns are primarily concentrated in the electrical system, specifically with occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well. Overall, the 2021 S4 is a dependable choice, provided owners address potential electrical quirks and maintain it according to Audi's recommendations.
