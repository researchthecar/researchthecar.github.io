# 2021 Audi S4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi S4 delivers robust performance through its turbocharged V6 engine and responsive all-wheel-drive system, making it a compelling choice for sport sedan enthusiasts. While early owner feedback indicates generally strong mechanical operation, prospective buyers should always review service histories and conduct a thorough test drive to ensure all electronics and powertrain systems perform correctly.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine produces 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and refined power delivery. It is generally regarded as a robust powertrain option, provided regular maintenance schedules are strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth and quick gear changes suited for both daily commuting and spirited driving. Routine fluid checks and timely servicing help maintain its long-term reliability.

### Drive System and Powertrain Electronics

Electrical concerns are minimal, though owners should monitor the complex onboard software and warning indicators for proper operation.

**Drive System Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an unexpected loss of vehicle power accompanied by an illuminated check engine light and a dashboard message reading "Drive System Malfunction - Please Contact Service."
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle scanned by a qualified technician or dealership to retrieve diagnostic trouble codes and update or repair the affected electronic control modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi S4 combines luxury, technology, and strong performance, backed by a generally favorable initial reliability outlook. Used-car shoppers should prioritize models with comprehensive service records and verify that all software updates and open campaigns have been properly addressed by a certified professional.
