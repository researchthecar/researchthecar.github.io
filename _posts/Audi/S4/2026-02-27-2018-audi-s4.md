# 2018 Audi S4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Audi S4 delivers robust performance and sophisticated luxury, but prospective used-car buyers should carefully evaluate its mechanical history. While owner reports suggest it offers an engaging driving experience, a few notable engine and safety-related concerns have surfaced in driver feedback. Thoroughly inspecting service records and verifying maintenance history will help ensure a more reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (EA839)

The 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 354 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing rapid acceleration. While powerful and refined, buyers should monitor for cooling system and internal valvetrain concerns reported by owners.

**Water Pump and Coolant System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks and damage to adjacent vacuum lines or the thermostat, which can eventually lead to overheating and potential engine failure without immediate warning.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and vacuum lines for coolant intrusion or leaks, and replace failing components promptly.

**Rocker Arm Bearing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** An audible knocking noise from the motor caused by internal rocker arm needle bearings becoming loose, potentially circulating metal shavings through the engine.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose internal engine noises immediately and replace damaged rocker arms or affected assemblies.

**PCV Housing and Line Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive crankcase pressure buildup that can dislodge the oil cap and spray oil into the engine bay, creating a potential fire hazard near the turbocharger.
- **Solution:** Inspect the PCV housing and connected lines for cracking or clogging, and replace faulty parts to restore proper pressure regulation.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifting paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive. It generally proves durable when routine fluid maintenance is maintained.

## Suspension

### Suspension Springs and Mounts

Suspension components generally absorb road imperfections well, though isolated owner reports note premature spring wear.

**Broken Rear Springs and Worn Mounts**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual noises from the rear suspension or altered vehicle ride height, potentially compromising handling if left unaddressed.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear coil springs and mounting hardware during routine maintenance and replace any fractured springs or deteriorating mounts.

## Safety

### Pre Sense Auto-Braking

Advanced safety features enhance driver awareness, but sensor calibration issues can occasionally lead to false system triggers.

**Erroneous Forward Collision Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's pre-sense auto-braking and collision warning system unexpectedly activates while driving, causing sudden and unexpected vehicle slowing.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates or sensor calibration issues at an authorized service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V547000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Oxidation on the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) connecting cable may cause the software to misdiagnose a malfunction and disable the passenger air bag. A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install cable ties to the harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=S4&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi S4 is an appealing sport sedan that combines luxury with strong turbocharged performance. However, used-car shoppers should prioritize models with comprehensive service documentation, paying close attention to cooling system components, internal engine health, and suspension condition. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by an Audi specialist is strongly recommended.
