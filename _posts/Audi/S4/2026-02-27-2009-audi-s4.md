# 2009 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2009 Audi S4, a performance-oriented variant of the A4, is known for its potent V8 engine and engaging driving dynamics. While generally well-regarded for its sporty capabilities, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding reliability. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. We will examine the powertrain, suspension, electrical systems, and other key areas to offer a comprehensive reliability overview.

---


## Engine

### 4.2L V8 (Naturally Aspirated)
**Timing Chain Guides**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the rear of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain guides, tensioners, and potentially the chains themselves.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $4000

**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil usage between changes, potentially leading to low oil levels.
- **Solution:** Can be due to worn piston rings or valve stem seals; requires engine disassembly for repair.
- **Cost:** $3000 - $6000+

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, potential oil leaks, whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### 6-Speed Tiptronic Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Valve Body Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission fault lights, erratic behavior.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the transmission valve body.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the MMI unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow movement, or falling down.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Audi S4, powered by its robust 4.2L V8, offers exhilarating performance but comes with potential reliability considerations, primarily concerning the engine's timing chain guides and oil consumption, which can be costly to address. Automatic transmission models may experience issues with the torque converter or valve body. While the suspension and interior are generally solid, optional adaptive dampers can be a point of failure. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and window regulators, are also noted. Despite these potential issues, many owners find the S4's driving experience justifies the investment in its upkeep, especially if proactively maintained.
