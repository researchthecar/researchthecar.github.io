# 2013 Audi S4

### Overview
The 2013 Audi S4, a performance-oriented variant of the A4 sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its potent powertrain. It features a supercharged 3.0-liter V6 engine known for its strong performance and relatively good durability. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any performance car, there are specific areas that warrant attention. Common concerns tend to revolve around the engine's cooling system and potential oil consumption, as well as some transmission-specific issues, particularly with the S tronic dual-clutch automatic. However, many of these issues are manageable with proactive maintenance and addressing them promptly. Overall, the S4 offers a compelling blend of luxury, sportiness, and dependable performance when properly cared for.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 (TFSI)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, check engine light, potential oil leaks, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Low oil level warnings, need for frequent top-ups between oil changes.
- **Solution:** Can be related to PCV issues, piston ring wear, or valve stem seals. Diagnosis is key; may require internal engine repairs.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000+

**Supercharger Belt Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whining noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger drive belt and potentially tensioner/pulley.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### 7-Speed S tronic (Dual-Clutch Automatic)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000+

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, rough gear changes, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Audi S4 offers a strong performance package with a generally reliable 3.0L supercharged V6 engine. The most common concerns are related to the PCV system and potential oil consumption, which can be costly if not addressed. The manual transmission is robust, but the S tronic automatic can experience issues with its mechatronic unit and clutch packs, representing significant repair expenses. Suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components are typically free of widespread problems. Proactive maintenance, especially regarding oil levels and PCV system checks, is crucial for long-term reliability. Despite potential transmission and engine-related costs, the S4 remains a desirable performance sedan for enthusiasts who are prepared for its specific maintenance needs.
