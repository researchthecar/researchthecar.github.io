**2023 Audi S4**

The 2023 Audi S4 is a high-performance compact sedan that delivers a thrilling driving experience. It is equipped with a powerful 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine that produces 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The S4 comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive, providing excellent traction and handling. Despite its impressive performance, the S4 also offers a comfortable and luxurious interior, making it a well-rounded choice for both daily commuting and weekend getaways.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Turbocharged V6**

 **Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, abnormal noises, smoke from the engine bay
Solution: Replace failed turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

 **Engine Oil Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the engine, low oil levels, oil warning light
Solution: Replace leaking gaskets or seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Transmission**

* **Eight-Speed Automatic**

 **Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay in gear changes, loss of power during acceleration
Solution: Replace worn or damaged transmission components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Frozen display, unresponsive touchscreen, audio issues
Solution: Software updates, hardware replacement if necessary
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

Solution: Identify and repair the source of the electrical drain

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Noisy rattling or vibrations from the panoramic sunroof

**Summary**

The 2023 Audi S4 is a powerful and luxurious compact sedan with a few minor but manageable issues. The potential engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, but they are not widespread. Overall, the S4 is a reliable and well-rounded choice for discerning drivers.
