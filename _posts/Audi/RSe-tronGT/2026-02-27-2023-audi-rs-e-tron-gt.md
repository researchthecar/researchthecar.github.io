# 2023 Audi RS e-tron GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi RS e-tron GT represents the pinnacle of high-performance all-electric luxury, offering supercar-level acceleration and striking grand tourer styling. As a relatively low-volume flagship EV, used-car buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, focusing on specialized electronic systems, high-voltage battery health, and software reliability.

---

## Engine

### Dual-Motor Electric Powertrain

Features dual permanent-magnet synchronous electric motors producing an estimated 590 horsepower (up to 637 hp with overboost) and 612 lb-ft of torque. This electric powertrain delivers blistering acceleration and all-wheel-drive capability with minimal routine mechanical maintenance required.

## Transmission

### 2-Speed Automatic Transmission

Utilizes a unique two-speed automatic transmission on the rear axle, pairing a short first gear for explosive launches with a long second gear for efficient highway cruising. It operates smoothly and requires no traditional clutch service.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V613000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Liquid accumulation in the high voltage battery can cause electrical arcing, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will test the battery for leakage and replace the battery as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V229000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V228000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will, analyze the battery data and replace the high-voltage battery modules as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V465000: Service Brakes, Air:Supply:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A failed brake circuit can cause longer brake pedal travel and reduced braking performance, extending the distance required to stop and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front axle brake hoses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V726000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software as the final remedy free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V227000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat cushion, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi RS e-tron GT is an extraordinarily fast and sophisticated luxury electric vehicle that offers a thrilling driving experience. While standard mechanical wear is minimal compared to gas-powered vehicles, prospective buyers should verify software update statuses and ensure all high-voltage electrical systems have been professionally inspected prior to purchase.
