# 2024 Audi RS e-tron GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi RS e-tron GT stands as a high-performance all-electric luxury sedan blending striking styling with blistering acceleration. As a relatively low-volume and advanced electric vehicle, prospective buyers should carefully weigh complex high-voltage electrical ecosystems against standard luxury car maintenance. While official owner feedback is sparse, early reports underscore the critical importance of inspecting advanced driver assistance technologies.

---

## Engine

### Dual Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors

This high-performance electric powertrain utilizes dual permanent magnet synchronous motors producing up to 637 horsepower with overboost. The setup delivers instantaneous acceleration and standard all-wheel drive, representing a robust and mechanically straightforward alternative to traditional internal combustion engines.

## Transmission

### 2-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a specialized 2-speed automatic transmission designed to balance low-end launch acceleration with high-speed efficiency. This unique gearbox operates seamlessly under normal driving conditions, requiring minimal routine maintenance compared to conventional multi-gear transmissions.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Advanced safety features are designed to protect occupants, though complex sensor suites warrant careful pre-purchase verification.

**Active Safety System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle safety features including emergency braking, cross traffic warning, and forward collision warning may fail to engage when needed, potentially leading to low-speed collisions.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect sensor alignment, software calibration, and control modules for diagnostic fault codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V228000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will, analyze the battery data and replace the high-voltage battery modules as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V465000: Service Brakes, Air:Supply:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A failed brake circuit can cause longer brake pedal travel and reduced braking performance, extending the distance required to stop and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front axle brake hoses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V726000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software as the final remedy free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi RS e-tron GT offers extraordinary performance and cutting-edge luxury, but buyers should approach with normal due diligence regarding advanced electronics and driver assistance features. Given the scarcity of widespread complaint data, ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection of all active safety systems and software updates before finalizing a purchase.
