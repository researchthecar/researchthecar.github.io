# 2022 Audi RS e-tron GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi RS e-tron GT represents a high-performance pinnacle in the luxury electric vehicle segment, delivering striking design and blistering acceleration. For used-car buyers considering this flagship EV, evaluating its advanced dual-motor setup and cutting-edge electrical systems is essential. While owner feedback is limited, understanding the core powertrain options and potential early-production electronics quirks will help you navigate the pre-owned market with confidence.

---

## Engine

### Dual Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motors (Front & Rear)

The RS e-tron GT utilizes dual permanent magnet synchronous motors paired with a 93.4 kWh battery pack, generating an impressive 637 horsepower with overboost. This sophisticated electric propulsion system delivers blistering acceleration and refined, silent power delivery suited for high-performance driving.

## Transmission

### 2-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through an innovative 2-speed automatic transmission on the rear axle, which optimizes both launch acceleration and high-speed efficiency. This specialized EV gearbox generally operates smoothly, though buyers should verify seamless shifting behavior during a test drive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V842000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Charging:Cable/Cord:Onboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated outlet or charging cable can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will supply a new 220V/240V compact/portable charging cable with an incorporated temperature sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V229000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V228000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery modules. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will, analyze the battery data and replace the high-voltage battery modules as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V465000: Service Brakes, Air:Supply:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A failed brake circuit can cause longer brake pedal travel and reduced braking performance, extending the distance required to stop and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front axle brake hoses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V726000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high-voltage battery may experience a short circuit within the battery module. A short circuit in a high-voltage battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install advanced diagnostic software as the final remedy free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V227000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat cushion, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS+e-tron+GT&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
