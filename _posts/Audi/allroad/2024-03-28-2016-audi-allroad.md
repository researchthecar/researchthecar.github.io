**Introduction**

The 2016 Audi allroad is a mid-size luxury station wagon that combines the ruggedness of an SUV with the sleekness of a sedan. It comes equipped with a 3.0-liter TFSI V6 engine that produces 272 horsepower and 324 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an eight-speed Tiptronic transmission and Quattro all-wheel drive. Other engine and transmission options for the allroad include:

* 3.0-liter TDI V6 diesel engine with 240 horsepower and 428 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed Tiptronic transmission
* 2.0-liter TFSI inline-four engine with 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission

Owners generally appreciate the allroad's comfortable ride, spacious interior, and impressive performance, but some have encountered various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**3.0-Liter TFSI V6**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, and hesitation during acceleration.
 - Solution: Intake valve cleaning or replacement
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 - Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Rattling noises from the engine at startup or acceleration, reduced engine power, and illuminated check engine light.
 - Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
 - Recalls: None Found
* **PCV Valve Failure:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the engine, rough idle, and reduced engine power.
 - Solution: Replacement of the PCV valve
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Thermostat Failure:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating or underheating, inconsistent temperature readings, and reduced fuel efficiency.
 - Solution: Replacement of the thermostat
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**3.0-Liter TDI V6**

* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging:**
 - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased exhaust emissions, and illuminated DPF warning light.
 - Solution: DPF cleaning or replacement
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
 - Recalls: None Found

**2.0-Liter TFSI Inline-Four**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, and hesitation during acceleration.
 - Solution: Intake valve cleaning or replacement
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
 - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Tiptronic**

* **Shift Hesitation or Jerking:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough gear changes, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
 - Solution: Software updates or transmission fluid replacement
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
 - Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Difficulty Shifting Gears:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging or disengaging gears, grinding noises during shifting.
 - Solution: Replacement of the clutch or other transmission components
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
 - Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed S tronic**

* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Gear changes accompanied by lurching or shuddering, illuminated check engine light, and reduced engine power.
 - Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
 - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leaks:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, reduced ride height, and illuminated suspension warning light.
 - Solution: Replacement of air springs or air bags
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ per corner
 - Recalls: 16V280

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after sitting for a period of time, difficulty starting the vehicle.
 - Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system, including alternator or battery replacement
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
 - Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, glitches, or loss of functionality.
 - Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
 - Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Buildup of moisture inside the headlight assemblies.
 - Solution: Replacement of headlights
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per headlight
 - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Paint Peeling:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Peeling clear coat on the rear bumper, primarily on Silver vehicles.
 - Solution: Replacement or repainting of the rear bumper
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
 - Recalls: 16V696

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Rattling:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Noises or vibrations coming from the panoramic sunroof.
 - Solution: Adjustment or replacement of sunroof components
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
 - Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Trim Scratches:** 
 - Description/Symptoms: Scratches or scuffs on the piano black trim around the gear selector.
 - Solution: Replacement of center console trim
 - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Audi allroad is a reliable vehicle, but it has encountered some issues that owners should be aware of. While most issues are not overly expensive to fix, some repairs, such as transmission or suspension problems, can be costly. Potential buyers should consider the severity and cost of potential issues when making their purchase decision.
