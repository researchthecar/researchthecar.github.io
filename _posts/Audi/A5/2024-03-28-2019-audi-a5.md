**Introduction:**

The 2019 Audi A5 is a midsize luxury coupe and convertible offered with a range of engine and transmission options. Its sleek design, premium interior, and performance-oriented handling have made it a popular choice among enthusiasts. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**2.0L TFSI (248 hp)**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups or premature oil changes.
    * Solution: Diagnose the cause (worn piston rings, valve seals, or turbocharger), and replace affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
* **Valve Seat Failure:** A known issue on some 2.0L TFSI engines, this can lead to decreased performance, engine noise, and potential catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replace affected valve seats and related valve components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: 19V468, 20V121, 20V175, 20V533

**Transmission**

**7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch:**

* **Clutches Wearing Out Prematurely:** Some owners have experienced premature clutch wear, resulting in transmission shuddering or slipping.
    * Solution: Replace the worn clutches and inspect other transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** A complex electronic-hydraulic component that controls the transmission's functions, some units have failed, causing transmission malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Squeaky or Rattling Suspension:** Some owners have reported squeaking or rattling noises from the suspension, particularly over bumps or rough roads.
    * Solution: Inspect and lubricate suspension components, replace worn or damaged bushings or ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have experienced excessive battery drain, causing premature failure or difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnose the cause (parasitic drain, faulty battery, or alternator), and repair or replace affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Some owners have reported condensation forming inside the headlight assemblies, reducing visibility and potentially damaging electrical components.
    * Solution: Replace affected headlight assemblies or reseal them.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per assembly

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof Creaks or Rattles:** Some owners have experienced creaking or rattling noises from the panoramic sunroof, particularly when open or tilted.
    * Solution: Inspect and lubricate sunroof components, or adjust the sunroof alignment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Audi A5 has received positive reviews for its performance and luxury. However, certain engine and transmission issues can be costly to repair. While electrical, suspension, exterior, and interior issues are generally less severe and expensive, they can still affect overall ownership satisfaction. It's important to consider these potential issues when making a purchasing decision.
