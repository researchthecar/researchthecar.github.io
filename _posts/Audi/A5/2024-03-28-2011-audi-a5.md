**Introduction**

The 2011 Audi A5 is a luxury coupe that offers a refined driving experience. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0L TFSI inline-4, a 3.2L FSI V6, and a 3.0L TDI V6. The A5 is praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it has been known to experience some issues, which are listed below.

**Engine**

- **2.0L TFSI**
  - **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    - Description/Symptoms: Causes loss of power, rough idle, and poor fuel economy
    - Solution: Clean or replace intake valves
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  - **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rattling or whining noise from engine, loss of power
    - Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced power
    - Solution: Replace high-pressure fuel pump
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
- **3.2L FSI**
  - **Ignition Coil Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idle, reduced power
    - Solution: Replace ignition coils
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
- **3.0L TDI**
  - **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased fuel consumption, engine warning light
    - Solution: Clean or replace DPF
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
  - **Fuel Injector Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idle, reduced power
    - Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
- **All Engines**
  - **Oil Leaks**
    - Description/Symptoms: Oil on engine or ground, burning oil smell
    - Solution: Replace leaking seals or gaskets
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+

**Transmission**

- **CVT Transmission**
  - **Premature Wear of Belt or Pulleys**
    - Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering during gear changes, loss of power
    - Solution: Replace belt or pulleys
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
- **Tiptronic Transmission**
  - **Mechatronic Control Unit Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Jerky shifting, loss of gears, transmission warning light
    - Solution: Replace mechatronic control unit
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  - **Torque Converter Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering during gear changes, overheating
    - Solution: Replace torque converter
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

- **All Suspensions**
  - **Strut Mount Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from suspension, uneven tire wear
    - Solution: Replace strut mounts
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Electrical**

- **All Electrical Systems**
  - **Battery Drain**
    - Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after extended periods of time, need to jump-start
    - Solution: Identify and fix parasitic drain
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - **Electrical Component Failures**
    - Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning headlights, taillights, interior lights
    - Solution: Replace failed component
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

- **None Found**

**Interior**

- **Ceiling Headliner Sagging**
    - Description/Symptoms: Headliner fabric detaches from roof
    - Solution: Reattach headliner fabric with adhesive
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary**

The 2011 Audi A5 is a reliable vehicle overall, but it has been known to experience some issues. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and costly. It is important to be aware of these issues before purchasing an A5 so that you can budget accordingly for potential repairs.
