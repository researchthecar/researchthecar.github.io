# 2016 Audi A5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Audi A5, buyers are typically drawn to its timeless styling, refined interior, and engaging performance. While owner complaints for this model year are relatively sparse, prospective buyers should review available maintenance records and inspect critical powertrain and steering components closely before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced blend of efficiency and daily pep. It is generally robust when maintained on schedule, though buyers should verify service history regarding timing components.

**Drive Chain Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The drive chain may break unexpectedly, leading to permanent engine damage and requiring a complete engine replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect the timing chain and tensioners for wear; replace the drive chain assembly if necessary.

**Limp Mode and Check Engine Light**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly enters limp mode accompanied by an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes with an OBD-II scanner to identify sensor or internal engine issues.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

This conventional 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with Quattro all-wheel drive, delivering smooth and responsive shifts. It is generally considered dependable compared to older dual-clutch units.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers a connected driving experience for enthusiasts, though it is harder to find on the used market. Regular fluid checks help maintain long-term shifting crispness.

## Steering

### Electromechanical Steering Rack

Steering components generally offer precise handling, but complete rack failures have been reported by some owners.

**Steering Rack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The electromechanical steering rack can fail completely without advanced warning, making the vehicle difficult or dangerous to steer.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty steering rack assembly.

## Electrical

### MMI Infotainment System

Electrical accessories provide good functionality, though occasional screen or infotainment glitches are worth checking during a test drive.

**Screen Stays Black**
- **Symptoms:** The MMI infotainment screen remains black upon vehicle startup and fails to turn on.
- **Solution:** Reset the system or inspect electrical fuses and connections; replacement units may be required if internal failure occurs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V622000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Contact corrosion within the electrical connector for the auxiliary heater may cause the heater wires to overheat or melt. The melted wires can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the auxiliary heater and, as necessary, update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V002000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the coolant pump overheats, it can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will update the software so that the power supply to the coolant pump is deactivated if the pump becomes blocked with debris, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V229000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A blocked pump or a short-circuited pump can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi A5 is an appealing luxury coupe and cabriolet option, but used car buyers should prioritize a pre-purchase inspection that covers the engine timing components, steering system, and electronics. Verifying a consistent maintenance history will significantly reduce the risk of unexpected repair bills.
