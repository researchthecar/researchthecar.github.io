# 2016 Audi A5

### Overview
The 2016 Audi A5, a stylish and sporty coupe, convertible, and liftback, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on premium features and driving dynamics. While it excels in performance and design, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2.0L TFSI and 3.0L Supercharged V6 engines, as well as the Multitronic CVT and S tronic dual-clutch transmissions. Understanding these potential issues can help prospective and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and repairs.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L TFSI (Turbocharged)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil burning, low oil level warnings, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Piston ring replacement, PCV valve replacement, or engine rebuild depending on severity.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500


### 3.0L Supercharged V6
**Supercharger Belt Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Squealing noises from the engine bay, loss of supercharger boost, reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger drive belt and potentially tensioner.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**PCV System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, potential for increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the positive crankcase ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### Multitronic CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)
**Transmission Shudder/Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation during acceleration, jerky shifts, shuddering sensation.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or potential transmission unit replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3,500

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


### S tronic (7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, gear selection issues, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Clutch Pack Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, rough engagement, delayed acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch packs.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000

**Infotainment System Glitches**

**Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS, Parking)**

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Audi A5 is a desirable vehicle that offers a blend of performance and luxury. While its core mechanical components are generally robust, potential owners should be aware of specific issues that can arise, particularly concerning the 2.0L TFSI engine's oil consumption and turbocharger health, and potential transmission problems with both the CVT and S tronic units. Electrical gremlins, especially with the infotainment system, can also occur. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to early symptoms are crucial for mitigating these potential costs and ensuring a reliable ownership experience. Despite these points, the A5 remains a strong contender in its segment for those who prioritize style and driving engagement.
