**Introduction:**

The 2021 Audi A5 is a luxury compact car offered with the following engine and transmission options:

* 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 with a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission (248 horsepower, 273 lb-ft torque)
* 3.0L turbocharged V6 with an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission (349 horsepower, 369 lb-ft torque)
* 3.0L turbocharged V6 with a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission (349 horsepower, 369 lb-ft torque)

The A5 is praised for its stylish exterior, well-appointed interior, and engaging driving dynamics. However, it has also received criticism for its high price, limited rear legroom, and lack of some advanced safety features.

---

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Valve Cover Leak:** Oil leaks from the valve cover, causing a burning oil smell and potential engine damage. Solution: Replace valve cover gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **Intake Manifold Runner Issues:** Malfunctioning intake manifold runners affect engine performance and cause engine warning lights. Solution: Replace intake manifold. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * **Recall:** None Found

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**

* **Turbocharger Issues:** Turbocharger failure leads to reduced engine power and poor performance. Solution: Replace turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **Coolant Leak:** Coolant leaks from the water pump or hoses, resulting in overheating. Solution: Replace leaking parts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * **Recall:** None Found

---

**Transmission**

**7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Clutch Pack Failure:** Clutch packs in the transmission wear prematurely, causing slipping, shuddering, and transmission failure. Solution: Replace clutch packs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Issues:** Mechatronic unit malfunctions affect transmission shifting and lead to gear changes, limp mode, and loss of power. Solution: Replace or repair mechatronic unit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * **Recall:** None Found

**8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Hesitations:** Hesitations during gear shifts, especially when downshifting, indicate software or mechanical issues. Solution: Reprogramming or replacement of transmission components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **Torque Converter Shudder:** Vibrations during acceleration are caused by a failing torque converter. Solution: Replace torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recall:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Damping System Failure:** Faulty adaptive damping system leads to poor ride quality and handling, especially on rough roads. Solution: Replace or repair damping system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **Strut Mount Bearing Noise:** Worn or faulty strut mount bearings produce noises during turns and over bumps. Solution: Replace strut mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * **Recall:** None Found

---

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Issues:** Faulty electrical harnesses can cause various electrical problems, such as flickering lights, malfunctioning electronics, and battery drain. Solution: Repair or replace electrical harnesses. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:** Software glitches and hardware failures affect the operation of the infotainment system. Solution: Update software or replace system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * **Recall:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunctions:** Faulty door handles can become stuck or fail to operate properly. Solution: Replace door handles. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * **Recall:** None Found

---

**Interior**

* **Power Seat Motor Issues:** Power seat motors can fail, preventing seat adjustments. Solution: Replace seat motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **Steering Wheel Controls Malfunction:** Buttons or switches on the steering wheel may fail to respond or function properly. Solution: Repair or replace steering wheel controls. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * **Recall:** None Found

---

**Summary:**

The 2021 Audi A5 has a variety of potential issues, particularly related to the engine, transmission, and electrical components. Repairs can be expensive, ranging from a few hundred dollars to thousands of dollars. However, many of these issues are relatively rare, and the A5 remains a generally reliable choice for luxury car buyers.
