# 2022 Audi A5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A5 delivers a compelling mix of sharp styling, refined luxury, and engaging performance across its coupe, cabriolet, and sportback body styles. For used car buyers, understanding how its powertrain and electronic systems hold up over time is essential for making a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

The base 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 produces around 201 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth power delivery for daily commuting. It is generally regarded as a reliable option when properly maintained with timely oil changes.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

The higher-output 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 generates approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and spirited performance. Like its lower-output counterpart, it maintains a solid reputation for everyday mechanical durability.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 (S5)

The potent 3.0L turbocharged V6 pumps out 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering high-performance thrills for enthusiast drivers. It is a robust powerplant, though buyers should expect higher maintenance complexity compared to the standard four-cylinder engines.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with four-cylinder models and quattro all-wheel drive, offering lightning-fast gear changes. Regular fluid service is critical to ensuring long-term shifting smoothness and mechanical health.

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The traditional 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission comes mated to the V6 engine, providing refined and seamless shifts. It is known for strong durability under spirited driving conditions.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi A5 presents itself as a sophisticated and mechanically sound choice for used luxury car buyers. With a robust lineup of engines and well-matched transmissions, serious recurring mechanical complaints are minimal. Prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall condition and electronic functionality.
