# 2022 Audi A5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi A5 Sportback combines sleek styling, engaging driving dynamics, and a refined interior into a practical luxury package. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern technology and engineering, though used car buyers should still evaluate standard mechanical health and maintenance history. Reviewing powertrain specifications and potential ownership patterns helps ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

The 40 TFSI features a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 201 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, paired with front-wheel drive or quattro all-wheel drive. It offers a balanced blend of efficiency and daily drivability for standard commuting.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

The 45 TFSI utilizes a higher-output 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 generating around 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, standard with quattro all-wheel drive. This option delivers brisk acceleration and a more spirited driving experience for enthusiasts.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes across both 40 TFSI and 45 TFSI powertrain variants. Regular fluid maintenance is essential to ensure long-term shifting smoothness and mechanical durability.
