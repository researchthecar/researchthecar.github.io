## 2020 Audi A5 Sportback

The 2020 Audi A5 Sportback is a luxury sedan that combines stylish design and high performance. It is available with a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, or a 3.0L turbocharged V6 engine that produces 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Both engines come standard with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission and quattro all-wheel drive. The A5 Sportback is praised for its refined interior, ample cargo space, and responsive handling. Some drawbacks include its high price and limited rear headroom.

## Engine
**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
* **Engine Stalling:** Engine randomly stalls while driving, particularly when downshifting.
* **Solution:** Software update and replacement of the throttle body.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number 20V668000
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Premature failure of the timing chain tensioner can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and associated components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $5,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**
* **No Issues Found**

## Transmission
**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**
* **Transmission Shuddering:** Transmission shudders during acceleration, upshifting, or downshifting.
* **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the transmission clutches.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Recalls:** None Found

## Suspension
* **No Issues Found**

## Electrical
* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains rapidly even when the vehicle is not in use.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the battery and investigation into potential electrical system leaks.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $750
* **Recalls:** None Found

## Exterior
* **Door Lock Failure:** Door locks fail to engage intermittently, leaving the vehicle vulnerable to intrusion.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the door lock actuators.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

## Interior
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** Climate control system malfunction results in inadequate heating or cooling.
* **Solution:** Replacement of the climate control module or actuators.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
* **Recalls:** None Found

## Summary

The 2020 Audi A5 Sportback is a well-rounded vehicle, but not without its shortcomings. The engine and transmission issues can be severe and costly to fix, while the electrical and exterior problems are less pressing. Potential buyers should carefully consider these issues and the associated repair costs before purchasing an A5 Sportback.
