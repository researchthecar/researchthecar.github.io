# 2018 Audi A5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Audi A5 Sportback, buyers will find a stylish and technologically advanced luxury liftback that combines everyday utility with engaging performance. While owner feedback highlights satisfying driving dynamics and a premium cabin, prospective owners should pay attention to recurring owner reports regarding cooling system components and sporadic electrical glitches.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 252 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration and respectable efficiency. Buyers should inspect the engine bay thoroughly for coolant leaks and historical maintenance records.

**Cooling System and Oil Cooler Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owner complaints note coolant loss, unexpected drops in coolant levels, and coolant mixing into the engine oil due to plastic component and oil cooler degradation.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling lines, thermostat housing, and oil cooler for leaks or contamination; replace failed plastic fittings or gaskets as necessary.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may occasionally shut off without warning while cornering at very low speeds.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery, electrical sensors, and engine control modules to identify the root cause of low-speed stalling.

### MMI Infotainment and Coolant Pump Electronics

Electrical complaints center on infotainment stability and accessory sensor faults, which can cause intermittent driver distractions or dashboard warnings.

**MMI Screen Random Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The main infotainment display screen randomly reboots every few minutes, disrupting navigation and Apple CarPlay usage.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates or check for failing infotainment control modules at an authorized service center.

**Coolant Pump Electrical Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning lights illuminate, the vehicle may enter limp mode, and starting issues can arise due to coolant pump electrical faults.
- **Solution:** Inspect the coolant pump electrical components and replace the pump assembly if an internal short or electrical failure is diagnosed.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, smooth gear changes paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. Overall, this transmission is generally robust when maintained with regular fluid changes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V547000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Oxidation on the Passenger Occupant Detection System (PODS) connecting cable may cause the software to misdiagnose a malfunction and disable the passenger air bag. A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will install cable ties to the harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V836000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A passenger air bag module mounting screw may not have been sufficiently tightened, possibly affecting the deployment of the air bag in the event of a crash. If the air bag does not deploy correctly in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air bag mounting screw, tightening or replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V893000: Suspension:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The front suspension fasteners may not have been sufficiently tightened during vehicle production, possibly resulting in a front wheel becoming loose. A loose front wheel may increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Volkswagen has notified owners, and dealers will inspect the front suspension fasteners, and replace them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V822000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles were built with head curtain air bag assemblies that may not unfold properly. As such, these vehicle fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." In the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the head curtain air bag, if the air bag does not deploy.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will modify the head curtain air bags so that they deploy properly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V675000: Structure:Body:Door:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The sharp edges of the speaker trim increase the risk of injury to vehicle occupants.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will replace the speaker cover aluminum trim, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Audi A5 Sportback remains a compelling used luxury option thanks to its striking design and balanced powertrain. However, used car buyers should carefully verify the condition of the cooling system and test all infotainment and electrical features during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid unexpected repair bills.
