# 2020 Audi A5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Audi A5 Sportback, buyers can generally expect a stylish and well-appointed luxury vehicle with solid foundational engineering. However, prospective owners should pay close attention to potential electronics glitches, particularly concerning the infotainment and MMI systems, as owner reports highlight frequent rebooting issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering lively acceleration and respectable efficiency. It is generally robust when maintained with timely oil changes.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Stronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system, this 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers quick, seamless gear shifts. Regular fluid service is recommended to maintain optimal shift quality over long-term ownership.

## Electrical

### MMI Infotainment System

The interior electronics manage major comfort, audio, and navigation interfaces, though certain software iterations have proven prone to unexpected instability.

**Infotainment Random Reboot and Software Loop**
- **Symptoms:** The central MMI touchscreen and dashboard display randomly shut down or continuously reboot every few minutes, causing temporary loss of navigation, audio, and vehicle settings.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets performed by an authorized dealership may resolve the issue, though some owners report recurring glitches.

### Backup Camera and Driver Assistance

Driver assistance features rely heavily on stable vehicle software to maintain continuous operation of safety cameras and proximity sensors.

**Backup Camera Failure During MMI Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** When the MMI system unexpectedly crashes and reboots while reversing, the backup camera screen goes blank, temporarily disabling parking sensors and rearview visibility.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership for any available software patches or recall service actions addressing MMI stability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V737000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The wiring harness for the rear lights could be missing the anti-abrasion protection in the area of the spare wheel well. Without the scuff protection, the wires may become damaged during a rear crash, disabling the doors from being opened from the outside, causing a loss of hazard lights, and increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Audi will notify owners, and dealers will apply scuff protection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V295000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the lock nuts and, if necessary, the associated bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V874000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=A5&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A disabled passenger air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the connecting cable and either the heating mat or entire seat cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi A5 Sportback combines striking design with engaging driving dynamics, making it an appealing used luxury choice. While mechanical powertrains are generally dependable, buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electronics and infotainment functions during a pre-purchase test drive to ensure the MMI system operates reliably.
