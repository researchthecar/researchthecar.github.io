**Introduction to the 2019 Audi A5 Sportback**

The 2019 Audi A5 Sportback is a stylish and sporty luxury hatchback that offers a comfortable and luxurious interior, a powerful engine, and a responsive driving experience. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine with 248 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine with 349 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine with 444 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. The A5 Sportback also offers a choice of a six-speed manual transmission, a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, and an eight-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its impressive performance and features, the 2019 Audi A5 Sportback has been plagued by a number of issues reported by owners. These issues range from minor annoyances to more serious problems that can affect the vehicle's safety and performance.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idling, stalling, and increased fuel consumption
    - Solution: Cleaning the intake valves manually or using a chemical cleaner
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased engine noise, and increased oil consumption
    - Solution: Replacing the turbocharger
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Water Pump Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, and engine damage
    - Solution: Replacing the water pump
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**3.0-liter Turbocharged V6 Engine**

* **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, stalling, and difficulty starting
    - Solution: Replacing the high-pressure fuel pump
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, loss of power, and engine damage
    - Solution: Replacing the timing chain tensioner
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding or difficulty shifting gears**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or grinding noises when shifting gears
    - Solution: Replacing the clutch or transmission
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting delays or hesitation**
    - Description/Symptoms: Delay or hesitation when shifting gears, especially when accelerating or decelerating
    - Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the mechatronic unit
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Jerking or shuddering during shifts**
    - Description/Symptoms: Jerking or shuddering when shifting gears, especially when upshifting
    - Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the torque converter
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

* **Premature wear of suspension components**
    - Description/Symptoms: Noises from the suspension, such as clunking or rattling, and reduced handling performance
    - Solution: Replacing the worn suspension components
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dim headlights, and reduced electrical functionality
    - Solution: Identifying and repairing the source of the battery drain, such as a faulty electrical component
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Electrical system malfunctions**
    - Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, such as flickering lights, inoperative electronics, and engine stalling
    - Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the faulty electrical component or wiring
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation**
    - Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation inside the headlight lenses
    - Solution: Replacing the headlight lenses or using a desiccant
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Rear trunk lid alignment issues**
    - Description/Symptoms: Misalignment of the rear trunk lid, causing it to sit unevenly or protrude
    - Solution: Adjusting the trunk lid alignment
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $250+
    - Recall: NHTSA Campaign ID: 21V-539

**Interior**

* **Seat heater malfunction**
    - Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters do not work or work intermittently
    - Solution: Replacing the heating elements or control module
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Roof lining sagging**
    - Description/Symptoms: Sagging or loose headliner material
    - Solution: Repairing or replacing the roof lining
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2019 Audi A5 Sportback is a stylish and sporty luxury hatchback that offers a comfortable and luxurious interior, a powerful engine, and a responsive driving experience. However, it has been plagued by a number of issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, suspension problems, electrical problems, exterior problems, and interior problems. Some of these issues are minor annoyances, while others can be more serious and affect the vehicle's safety and performance. The cost to fix these issues can vary widely depending on the severity of the problem and the location of the repairs.
