# 2023 Audi A5 Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi A5 Sportback combines sleek coupe-like styling with everyday hatchback practicality and dynamic performance. As a relatively modern luxury vehicle, owner feedback is generally limited, though prospective buyers should review available owner reports concerning driver assistance electronics and occasional suspension or tire concerns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (45 TFSI)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, paired with standard quattro all-wheel drive. It offers a balanced blend of responsive acceleration and refinement for daily driving.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (40 TFSI)

This lower-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers around 201 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing efficiency while retaining lively performance. It serves as the entry-level powertrain option for the lineup.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, smooth gear changes and power delivery across all driving modes. It is standard across the A5 Sportback lineup when paired with quattro all-wheel drive.

## Suspension

### Rear Suspension and Wheels

The suspension provides a secure, planted ride quality typical of German luxury sport sedans, though minor mechanical noises can occasionally arise.

**Rear Thumping Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an unusual loud thumping or knocking noise originating from the rear of the vehicle at slow speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic or dealership inspect the rear suspension components and wheel assemblies to isolate the source of the noise.

## Safety

### Pre Sense and Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features enhance highway safety, but sensitive camera and radar suites can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected interventions based on owner reports.

**Pre Sense Malfunction and False Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected high-pitched warning chimes, dashboard error messages regarding Audi Pre Sense or AEB malfunctions, or sudden unwanted automated braking events.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to check for software updates or sensor recalibrations if warning lights or false braking events occur.
