# 2022 Audi SQ8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi SQ8 combines striking coupe-like SUV styling with ferocious twin-turbo V8 performance and a luxurious, tech-forward cabin. As a high-performance luxury vehicle, prospective used buyers should anticipate higher-than-average maintenance costs and thoroughly review service history. While widespread mechanical failures are rare in early ownership, monitoring complex electrical systems and powertrain health is vital.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2022 Audi SQ8 features a potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing roughly 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling acceleration and robust performance, though regular premium maintenance and diligent oil change intervals are crucial for long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Power is routed through an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission paired with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system. The transmission shifts smoothly during daily driving and responds sharply in dynamic drive modes, generally proving robust when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V806000: Equipment:Electrical:Infotainment:Video (Touch)Screen/Monitor/Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment main unit may become damaged internally when the vehicle is shut off, resulting in an inoperative rearview camera display the next time the vehicle is started. - **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment main unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
