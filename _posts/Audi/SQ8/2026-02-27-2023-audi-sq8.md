# 2023 Audi SQ8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi SQ8 delivers high-performance luxury with a twin-turbo V8 engine and sporty handling. As a relatively new and high-end model, used-car buyers should carefully inspect individual vehicles for mechanical and electrical health, keeping in mind that ownership costs and premium maintenance requirements remain high.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless acceleration and robust performance. Overall reliability for this high-output powertrain is generally solid, though routine maintenance is complex and expensive.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid gear shifts paired with Quattro all-wheel drive. It is a durable unit well-suited to handling the high torque output of the V8 engine.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi SQ8 is an exceptionally capable luxury performance SUV. Because owner complaint data is sparse for this recent model year, buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify complete service history before purchasing.
