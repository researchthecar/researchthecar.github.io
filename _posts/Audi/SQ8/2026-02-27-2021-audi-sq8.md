# 2021 Audi SQ8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi SQ8 is a high-performance luxury SUV offering robust twin-turbo V8 power and sophisticated styling. While owner-reported complaints are sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and verify that all necessary manufacturer campaigns have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

This potent 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers around 500 horsepower and strong low-end torque. It provides exhilarating performance balanced with sophisticated everyday drivability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes suited to high-performance driving. It channels power effectively across all driving modes.

## Electrical

### MMI Infotainment System

Owner reports note occasional temporary freezes or glitches in the MMI infotainment and software systems.

**Infotainment System Freezes**
- **Symptoms:** The MMI infotainment screen or system may suddenly stop working, particularly around the time of automated software updates.
- **Solution:** Restarting the vehicle or resetting the MMI system typically restores normal operation; persistent software glitches may require a dealer visit for updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V606000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the middle-rear seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V034000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rear axle alignment may not have been inspected after the repairs for Recall 21V-295 (42L1) were performed. Rear axle misalignment may cause premature or uneven tire wear, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear axle, adjust the alignment as necessary, and replace any prematurely or unevenly worn tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V339000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Cracks may cause engine damage and a stall or an oil leak, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V742000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A blank rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V201000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An instrument panel display that fails to show critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument panel module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi SQ8 is generally well-regarded for its exceptional performance and luxury features, with very few owner-submitted complaints on record. Buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify that all software updates and recall campaigns are fully up to date.
