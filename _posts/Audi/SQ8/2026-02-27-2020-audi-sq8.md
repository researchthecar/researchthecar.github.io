# 2020 Audi SQ8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Audi SQ8 delivers high-performance luxury with a twin-turbocharged V8 engine and a sophisticated interior. As a high-end European performance SUV, potential buyers should carefully evaluate service history, as complex electronics and powertrain components can be costly to maintain outside of warranty.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

Equipped with a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing approximately 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. This engine provides rapid acceleration, though routine maintenance and premium fuel requirements are high.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

Features an 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission paired with quattro all-wheel drive. It offers smooth, quick gear shifts suited for both relaxed cruising and spirited driving.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Audi SQ8 is an exhilarating luxury performance SUV that combines athletic handling with everyday utility. Used-car shoppers should prioritize models with comprehensive service records and thoroughly inspect all onboard electronic systems before purchase.
