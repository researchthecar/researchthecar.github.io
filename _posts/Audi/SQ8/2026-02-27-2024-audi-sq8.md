# 2024 Audi SQ8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi SQ8 is a high-performance luxury SUV that combines aggressive styling with potent twin-turbo power. While buyers are naturally drawn to its flagship blend of comfort and athleticism, early-production models occasionally attract owner reports regarding complex cabin and body electronics. Prospective shoppers should thoroughly inspect vehicle systems during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generates approximately 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It is a sophisticated powerhouse that requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the quattro all-wheel-drive system across all driving modes. It shifts crisply under hard acceleration while remaining refined during daily commuting.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Infotainment Screens

Cabin displays and screens are heavily integrated, meaning intermittent software glitches can temporarily impact multiple interior controls.

**Screen Blackouts and Cluster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster and central infotainment displays may randomly black out, rendering climate and vehicle controls temporarily inoperable.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module replacements performed by an authorized service center may resolve intermittent display dropouts.

## Exterior

### Door Locks and Electronic Handles

Electrical system concerns on early examples center on complex body control modules and electronic door mechanisms.

**Electronic Door Lock Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that electronic door handles can trap occupants inside or lock them out of the vehicle due to electrical glitches.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection by a certified dealership or technician is required to check software modules and door latch assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi SQ8 remains an exhilarating luxury performance choice, though early owner feedback highlights potential quirks within its advanced electronic architecture. Buyers should prioritize models with verified software updates and ensure all digital displays and power door features function seamlessly before purchasing.
