**Introduction:**

The 2019 Audi RS3 is a high-performance compact sports car renowned for its exhilarating driving experience. It features a potent 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-5 engine, capable of generating an impressive 401 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Paired with the standard 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, the RS3 accelerates from 0 to 60 mph in a mere 3.5 seconds. Other engine or transmission options are not available for the 2019 model year. Despite its exceptional performance, the RS3 has faced some specific issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Turbocharged Inline-5 Engine**

* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, engine hesitations, reduced performance
    * Solution: Replacing spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Unusual engine noise, reduced engine power, possible engine damage
    * Solution: Replacing timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Clutch Pack Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering during gear shifts, especially under hard acceleration or deceleration
    * Solution: Replacing clutch pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking sounds from the front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replacing lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen display freezing, unresponsive buttons, software glitches
    * Solution: Updating software, replacing touchscreen module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing electrical drain source (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic draw)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
    * Solution: Realigning sunroof, replacing sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Instrument Cluster Display Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Partial or complete failure of the instrument cluster display
    * Solution: Replacing instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Summary:**

While the 2019 Audi RS3 offers an exhilarating driving experience, it has not been immune to specific issues. Engine problems such as spark plug failure and timing chain tensioner issues have been reported. The transmission can develop clutch pack wear, and the suspension may experience control arm bushing failures. Electrical issues include infotainment system malfunctions and battery drain. Interior concerns include sunroof leakage and instrument cluster display issues. The severity of these issues varies; however, some repairs can be costly, such as transmission clutch pack replacements or electrical component replacements. It's important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to have them addressed promptly to ensure the safety and performance of their vehicle.
