# 2019 Audi RS3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Audi RS3 delivers exhilarating performance thanks to its potent turbocharged inline-five engine, making it a thrilling choice for driving enthusiasts. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability concerns regarding cooling system components and fuel delivery before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-5

The 2019 Audi RS3 features a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-five engine producing roughly 394 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It offers blistering acceleration and a distinct exhaust note, though buyers should monitor cooling system health closely.

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported engine temperature fluctuations and coolant loss, sometimes resulting in premature water pump failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system for leaks and replace the water pump if failure occurs.

**Fuel System Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty filling the fuel tank due to nozzle clicking off repeatedly, alongside potential fuel leaks while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel tank and associated lines for blockages or leaks, and replace damaged components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

Equipped with a quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with standard Quattro all-wheel drive, this setup delivers rapid gear changes and strong grip. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety equipment includes advanced airbag systems and pre-sense driver assistance features, though owners have reported dashboard warning lights linked to safety campaigns.

**Airbag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag and pre-sense warning lights remaining active on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized dealer to check for open safety recalls or faulty sensors requiring replacement.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V198000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS3&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the PODS sensor mat and update the PODS control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Audi RS3 is a high-performance sport sedan that offers exceptional speed and driving dynamics, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the cooling and fuel systems. Checking for completed safety recall service is also recommended before finalizing a purchase.
