**2018 Audi RS3**

The 2018 Audi RS3 is a high-performance hatchback powered by a turbocharged 2.5-liter inline-five engine that produces 400 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a standard 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The RS3 is known for its impressive performance, sharp handling, and luxurious interior. However, it has also faced criticism for its high price and stiff ride.

**Engine**

* **2.5L Inline-Five Turbo**

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, leading to issues with temperature regulation, airflow, or fan speed.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID Number: 20V445000
* **Infotainment System Screen Flickering**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system screen may flicker or experience display issues.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment system display.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Audi RS3 is a well-made vehicle with impressive performance and handling. However, it has experienced some issues with its climate control system and infotainment system. While these issues are not widespread, they can be costly to repair.
