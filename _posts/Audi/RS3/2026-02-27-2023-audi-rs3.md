# 2023 Audi RS3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi RS3 delivers breathtaking performance from its iconic turbocharged five-cylinder engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive system. As a high-performance compact sport sedan, it attracts enthusiastic drivers who value speed, agility, and premium daily drivability. While current owner feedback and complaint data are sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should always evaluate mechanical condition and maintenance history thoroughly.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged 5-Cylinder Engine

This turbocharged 2.5-liter five-cylinder engine produces approximately 394 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering distinctive exhaust notes and blistering acceleration. It is widely praised for robust engineering, though high-performance driving demands diligent adherence to maintenance intervals.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and power delivery tailored to the all-wheel-drive layout. Routine fluid changes are vital to maintain smooth operation during aggressive driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V901000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS3&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." Missing weight information may result in unintentional overloading of the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Audi dealers will install an overlay label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi RS3 remains an exhilarating choice for enthusiasts seeking supercar-rivaling acceleration in a practical sedan package. Because early owner complaints are minimal, buyers can approach well-maintained examples with confidence. A pre-purchase inspection by an Audi specialist is strongly recommended to verify overall vehicle health.
