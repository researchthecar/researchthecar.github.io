# 2022 Audi RS3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi RS3 is a high-performance sport sedan known for its iconic turbocharged five-cylinder engine and agile handling. As a relatively recent model year, it attracts driving enthusiasts looking for modern technology and blistering acceleration. While overall complaint data is sparse, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain health and overall maintenance history before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged 5-Cylinder

The 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-five engine produces approximately 401 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling performance and a distinctive exhaust note, with generally solid long-term reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power to Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear shifts and crisp operation, though prospective buyers should inspect for smooth engagement during test drives.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V823000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS3&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi RS3 remains a compelling choice for enthusiasts seeking a potent luxury sport sedan. With minimal reported reliability complaints on file, it stands as an exciting daily driver or weekend toy. Buyers are advised to prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of the high-performance powertrain and electronics.
