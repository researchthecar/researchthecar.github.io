# 2017 Audi RS3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2017 Audi RS3 offers an entry point into high-performance compact luxury, anchored by its distinctive and potent turbocharged 5-cylinder engine. While enthusiast drivers are drawn to its impressive acceleration and agile handling, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history and maintenance records. As with many specialized performance cars, keeping up with routine upkeep is essential to ensure long-term dependability.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged 5-Cylinder

The 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-5 engine produces approximately 400 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque, paired standard with quattro all-wheel drive. It delivers exhilarating performance and a characteristic exhaust note, though buyers should ensure consistent maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S-tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S-tronic dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and power delivery suited for spirited driving. Routine fluid changes are critical to maintaining smooth operation over the vehicle's lifespan.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V198000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=RS3&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated passenger air bag increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the PODS sensor mat and update the PODS control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Audi RS3 is a thrilling performance machine that blends daily usability with track-ready capability. Because no widespread recurring mechanical failure trends dominate owner complaint logs for this model year, buyers should focus heavily on a pre-purchase inspection, verifying service records, and checking for signs of prior modification or aggressive driving.
