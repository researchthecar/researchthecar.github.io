**Introduction:**

The 2022 Audi RS3 is a high-performance compact sedan powered by a turbocharged 2.5-liter inline-five engine and a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. With 401 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, the RS3 offers thrilling acceleration and impressive handling dynamics. However, like any vehicle, the RS3 has encountered a few issues reported by owners.

**Engine:**

**2.5-Liter Turbocharged Inline-Five Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gradual power loss and reduced fuel efficiency. The engine may also experience hesitation or rough idling.
    * Solution: Removing the intake manifold and cleaning the valves with a specialized chemical solution.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Hesitation in Gear Changes:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may delay or hesitate when shifting gears, particularly from first to second gear.
    * Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the clutch pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, display glitches, or lose connectivity.
    * Solution: Updating the system software or replacing the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Loose Weatherstripping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The weatherstripping around the windows or doors may come loose, allowing water or wind noise to enter the cabin.
    * Solution: Replacing the weatherstripping with new seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Headliner Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The headliner may produce a rattling or buzzing noise while driving.
    * Solution: Inspecting for loose wires, clips, or other potential sources of vibration and securing them.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Audi RS3 is a well-regarded performance vehicle, but it has encountered a few minor issues. The engine and transmission problems, while potentially serious, can be resolved with reasonable repair costs. The electrical and exterior issues are more common and can be addressed with less expense. The interior issue with the headliner is relatively minor and can be fixed without major disruption. Overall, the RS3 has a good reliability record with most issues being manageable and relatively low-cost to resolve.
