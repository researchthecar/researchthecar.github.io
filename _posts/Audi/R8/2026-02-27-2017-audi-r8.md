# 2017 Audi R8

### Overview
The 2017 Audi R8, a second-generation supercar, is renowned for its exhilarating performance and advanced engineering. Powered by a naturally aspirated V10 engine and available with a sophisticated Quattro all-wheel-drive system, it offers a compelling blend of everyday usability and track-ready capability. Reliability for the 2017 model year is generally strong, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality in its flagship sports car. While exotic cars inherently have higher maintenance costs than mainstream vehicles, the R8's robust construction and well-engineered components contribute to a positive ownership experience for those seeking a high-performance machine. Owners can expect a reliable platform, provided regular maintenance is performed by qualified technicians.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Audi R8 demonstrates commendable reliability for a high-performance supercar. The core powertrain components, including the potent 5.2L V10 engine and the efficient 7-speed S tronic transmission, are exceptionally robust. While the majority of systems show no significant issues, minor electrical gremlins such as infotainment system glitches and occasional battery drain have been noted, though these are typically resolvable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. The suspension and exterior/interior components are generally problem-free. Overall, the R8 offers a reliable and exhilarating driving experience, with maintenance costs being the primary consideration for potential owners.
