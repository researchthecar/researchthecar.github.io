# 2022 Audi R8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi R8 is a high-performance exotic sports car that blends everyday usability with thrilling supercar performance. As a late-model entry, it benefits from years of refinement, offering a refined cabin and sophisticated engineering. While owner complaints for this model year are exceptionally low, prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate exotic maintenance costs and overall vehicle history.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10 FSI

The 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine produces approximately 562 to 602 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque depending on the tune. It is renowned for its thrilling high-revving character and robust mechanical build, though maintenance requires specialized care.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes and power to the quattro all-wheel-drive system or rear-wheel-drive setup. It offers smooth operation in traffic alongside aggressive performance in manual mode.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V225000: Power Train:Manual Transmission:Lubricant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=R8&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Clutch slippage can cause a loss of drive power and increase the risk of a crash. A transmission oil leak in the presence of hot exhaust components can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the transmission oil level, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi R8 is a remarkably solid and reliable exotic sports car with virtually no major recurring complaint patterns reported by owners. Buyers looking for an everyday-usable supercar will find it to be an exciting choice, provided they budget for premium maintenance and a pre-purchase inspection.
