**Introduction**

The 2012 Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car powered by a 4.2L V8 engine that delivers 430 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. It features a quattro all-wheel-drive system and is available with either a 6-speed manual or a 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic transmission. The R8 has received praise for its exceptional handling, power, and distinctive design. However, owners have reported a number of issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **4.2L V8**
    * **Valve train carbon buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Buildup of carbon deposits on the intake valves, leading to reduced engine performance, rough idling, and hesitation.
        * Solution: Removal of the intake manifold and cleaning of the valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Piston ring failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Broken or worn piston rings, causing excessive oil consumption and smoke from the exhaust.
        * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $15,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed manual**
    * **Clutch issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the clutch, causing slipping or difficulty shifting.
        * Solution: Clutch replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **7-speed S tronic dual-clutch automatic**
    * **Mechatronic unit failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction of the mechatronic unit, which controls the transmission, leading to shifting problems, loss of power, and error codes.
        * Solution: Replacement of the mechatronic unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

    * **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

    * **Battery drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature discharge of the battery, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replacement of the battery or identification and repair of a parasitic drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

    * **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

    * **Steering wheel peeling**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and peeling of the leather on the steering wheel.
        * Solution: Replacement of the steering wheel.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2012 Audi R8 is a high-performance vehicle with a number of potential issues. Engine problems, such as valve train carbon buildup and piston ring failure, can be serious and expensive to fix. Transmission issues, including clutch and mechatronic unit failures, can also be costly. Electrical problems, such as battery drain, can be frustrating and inconvenient. Interior issues, such as peeling of the steering wheel, are less severe but can still be annoying. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicles inspected regularly by a qualified mechanic to ensure safety and reliability.
