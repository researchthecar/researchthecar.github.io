# 2021 Audi R8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Audi R8 is an exotic supercar that blends everyday usability with a high-revving naturally aspirated V10 engine. As a low-volume luxury performance vehicle, shopping for a used R8 means looking past standard commuter metrics and focusing heavily on specialized maintenance, powertrain health, and professional pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10 FSI

The 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 produces between 562 and 602 horsepower depending on the trim level (such as the standard or Performance variants). It delivers blistering performance and an iconic exhaust note, though buyers should budget for high-tier premium fuel, specialized servicing, and routine fluid checks.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power swiftly to either the rear wheels or the rear-biased Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes suitable for both track driving and highway cruising, requiring regular fluid changes to maintain peak operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V225000: Power Train:Manual Transmission:Lubricant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=R8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Clutch slippage can cause a loss of drive power and increase the risk of a crash. A transmission oil leak in the presence of hot exhaust components can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the transmission oil level, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Audi R8 remains a thrilling and exotic choice for enthusiasts seeking supercar performance with refined daily drivability. Because owner complaint data and official service records for low-volume supercars can be sparse, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an experienced Audi Sport specialist is essential before finalizing any used purchase.
