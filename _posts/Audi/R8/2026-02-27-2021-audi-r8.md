# 2021 Audi R8

### Overview
The 2021 Audi R8, a celebrated supercar, generally exhibits strong mechanical and electrical reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and performance focus. Owners typically report a satisfying ownership experience with minimal unexpected issues. The V10 engine, while potent, is a well-established unit with a solid track record. However, as with any high-performance vehicle, attention to specialized maintenance and potential for higher repair costs on certain components are factors to consider. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related reliability concerns.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi R8 demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with its status as a premium performance vehicle. Major mechanical and electrical systems, including the iconic V10 engine and the S tronic transmission, are well-regarded for their durability. Owners generally do not report significant recurring issues outside of expected maintenance. While the R8 is built to high standards, potential owners should be aware that specialized maintenance and parts for high-performance vehicles can incur higher costs compared to mainstream cars. Overall, the 2021 R8 is a dependable choice for those seeking exhilarating performance with a solid reliability record.
