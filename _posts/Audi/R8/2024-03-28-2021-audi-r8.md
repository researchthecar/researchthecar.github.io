**Introduction**

The 2021 Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car that offers an exhilarating driving experience. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine that produces 562 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. The R8 is available with either a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission. It features a lightweight aluminum body, a sophisticated suspension system, and a sleek and aggressive design. While the R8 is known for its impressive performance and handling, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**
* 5.2-liter V10

**Transmission**
* 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission
* 6-speed manual transmission

**Suspension**
* Front and rear independent suspension with double wishbones
* Magnetic ride dampers

**Electrical**
* Infotainment system
* Navigation system
* Audio system

**Exterior**
* Carbon fiber body panels
* LED headlights and taillights
* 20-inch aluminum wheels

**Interior**
* Leather-wrapped seats
* Alcantara headliner
* Carbon fiber trim

**Engine**
* 5.2-liter V10
    * **Exhaust manifold cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: The exhaust manifolds can crack, causing a loss of power and a decrease in fuel efficiency.
        * Solution: Replace the cracked exhaust manifolds.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Valve train noise**
        * Description/Symptoms: The valve train can make excessive noise, such as tapping or rattling.
        * Solution: Adjust the valve clearances or replace the noisy components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience difficulty shifting gears, or it may slip out of gear.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* 6-speed manual transmission
    * **No issues found**

**Electrical**
* **Infotainment system crash**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may crash or freeze, causing a loss of access to navigation, audio, and other features.
    * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Body panel alignment issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The body panels may not be aligned properly, resulting in gaps or unevenness.
    * Solution: Adjust the body panels or replace damaged parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Squeaky door panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door panels may squeak or rattle when opening or closing the doors.
    * Solution: Apply lubricant to the door hinges or replace the door panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car that offers an exhilarating driving experience. However, there have been some issues reported with the vehicle, including exhaust manifold cracking, valve train noise, shifting problems, infotainment system crashes, body panel alignment issues, and squeaky door panels. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but some of them can be significant. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
