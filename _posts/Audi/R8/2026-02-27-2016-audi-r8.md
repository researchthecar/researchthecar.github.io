# 2016 Audi R8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Evaluating a used 2016 Audi R8 requires balancing supercar performance with exotic ownership expectations. As a high-performance machine powered by naturally aspirated V10 engineering, it delivers thrilling dynamics and everyday usability relative to its class. Prospective buyers should focus on proper maintenance history and comprehensive pre-purchase inspections to identify any underlying mechanical or electronic wear.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10 FSI

The 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine produces approximately 540 to 610 horsepower depending on the tune, delivering high-revving performance and exotic exhaust notes. It is generally robust when maintained properly, though routine servicing costs are exceptionally high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed S tronic dual-clutch transmission provides rapid, seamless gear changes and power delivery to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid changes are critical to maintaining smooth operation and longevity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V543000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=R8&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During air bag deployment, the front passenger air bag may explode or deploy improperly. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front-passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi R8 stands out as a usable everyday supercar backed by formidable V10 performance. While severe recurring mechanical failure patterns are limited in owner feedback, the complexity and high cost of exotic car maintenance demand careful vetting. Buyers should prioritize examples with complete service records and professional pre-purchase inspections.
