# 2020 Audi R8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Navigating the pre-owned market for an exotic supercar like the Audi R8 requires a close look at its high-performance powertrain options and overall upkeep. While formal owner complaints are exceptionally sparse for this model year, buyers should always conduct thorough independent inspections to evaluate mechanical health, routine maintenance history, and prior usage.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10 FSI

This naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine produces between 532 and 602 horsepower depending on the specific tune, delivering blistering acceleration and an iconic exhaust note. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should budget for premium maintenance costs typical of exotic European sports cars.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed S tronic Dual-Clutch Automatic

This dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast gear shifts via steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters while routing power to the quattro all-wheel-drive system. Routine fluid changes are critical to ensuring longevity and smooth operation under aggressive driving.

### Dealer Delivery and Transaction Handling

Administrative and transactional concerns during purchase are non-mechanical hurdles that emphasize the importance of working with reputable exotic car dealerships.

**Purchase Delivery Dispute**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported purchasing the vehicle through a dealership fair and square, but experienced issues with the dealership failing to deliver the car.
- **Solution:** Resolve delivery terms directly through dealership management, legal consultation, or corporate intervention if purchase contracts are unfulfilled.

## Summary Verdict

The Audi R8 remains an exhilarating choice for enthusiasts seeking exotic performance paired with everyday usability. Because available owner data is minimal, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist to verify the condition of the engine, transmission, and electronics.
