The 2022 Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car offered as a Coupe or Spyder (convertible). It is powered by a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine producing either 562 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque in the RWD models or 602 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque in the Performance models. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. Owners generally praise the R8 for its stunning design, thrilling performance, and surprisingly comfortable ride for a supercar. Some criticisms include the outdated infotainment system and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

**5.2L V10 (562 hp / 602 hp)**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported oil leaks from various points in the engine. Symptoms include oil spots under the car, burning oil smell, and low oil levels.
    *   Solution: Identifying and replacing the leaking gasket or seal.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+, depending on the location of the leak and labor costs.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks can occur from hoses, the water pump, or the radiator. Symptoms include a sweet smell, visible coolant leaks, and overheating.
    *   Solution: Replacing the leaking component (hose, water pump, radiator).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $800+, depending on the location and the cost of parts and labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Jerking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported harsh or jerky shifting, particularly at lower speeds.
    *   Solution: In some cases, a software update of the TCU (Transmission Control Unit) may resolve the issue. If not, it could indicate a problem with the clutch packs or mechatronics unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update: $100 - $300. Clutch pack replacement or mechatronics unit repair/replacement: $2,000 - $8,000+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Magnetic Ride Control Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The magnetic ride control (if equipped) can sometimes exhibit issues, such as uneven ride quality or failure of the dampers. This may result in a bouncy ride or warning lights on the dashboard.
    *   Solution: Replacing the affected magnetic ride damper(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+ per damper, including labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can be prone to freezing, crashing, or exhibiting other glitches.
    *   Solution: Software updates can sometimes resolve the issue. In some cases, the infotainment unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update: $0 - $200. Infotainment unit replacement: $800 - $2,500+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (e.g., Parking Sensors, TPMS)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Parking sensors, tire pressure monitoring system (TPMS) sensors, or other sensors may malfunction, leading to inaccurate readings or warning lights.
    *   Solution: Replacing the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ per sensor, including labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Headlight/Taillight LED Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Individual LEDs within the headlights or taillights may fail, resulting in dimming or complete loss of illumination.
    *   Solution: Replacing the affected headlight or taillight assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+ per headlight/taillight, including labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Seat Motor Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The seat adjustment motors can sometimes fail, preventing the seat from moving in certain directions.
    *   Solution: Replacing the faulty seat motor or seat assembly
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+ per motor/seat, including labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Audi R8, while an exceptional sports car, does have some common issues. The most frequently reported issues include oil and coolant leaks in the engine, harsh shifting from the transmission, and electrical system glitches. The cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars for minor fixes to several thousand for major component replacements. It's crucial to have a qualified mechanic inspect any potential R8 purchase thoroughly, and factor in these potential maintenance costs when considering ownership.
