**Introduction:**

The 2022 Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car powered by a 5.2-liter V10 engine that produces 562 horsepower and 406 pound-feet of torque. It is available with a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. Critics praise the R8 for its blistering acceleration, sharp handling, and luxurious interior. However, some owners have encountered various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**5.2L V10 Engine**

* **Intake Valve Failure:** Intake valves can prematurely fail, leading to a loss of compression and engine misfires.
    * Solution: Replace the affected intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Camshaft Sensor Malfunction:** The camshaft sensor can malfunction, causing the engine to run erratically or fail to start.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty camshaft sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Clutch Pack Failure:** The clutch packs can fail prematurely, leading to slipping or hesitation during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replace the worn-out clutch packs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:** The mechatronic unit, which controls the transmission, can malfunction, causing shifting problems or transmission failure.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some vehicles may experience excessive battery drain, leading to difficulty starting the car.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty battery or repair the underlying electrical problem.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system can experience occasional glitches, such as freezing or displaying incorrect information.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Control Panel Malfunctions:** Some vehicles may experience malfunctions with the control panel, such as non-responsive buttons or incorrect display information.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty control panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing incorrect temperature or fan operation.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the underlying issue, such as a faulty temperature sensor or actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2022 Audi R8 is a performance-oriented sports car, but it is not immune to certain issues. Engine and transmission problems appear to be the most commonly reported concerns, with potential costs ranging from $3,000 to $6,000+. Electrical glitches and interior malfunctions are less severe and typically less expensive to resolve. Overall, prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues before considering purchasing an R8.
