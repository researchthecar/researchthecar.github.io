**2017 Audi R8**

The 2017 Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car that is available with two engine options: a 5.2-liter V10 engine that produces 540 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.2-liter V10 Plus engine that produces 610 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The R8 is praised for its thrilling performance, sharp handling, and luxurious interior. However, it is also criticized for its high price and limited practicality.

**Engine**

* **5.2-liter V10 Engine**
    * **Valve Spring Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The valve springs can fail, causing the valves to become stuck open or closed. This can lead to engine damage.
        * Solution: The valve springs need to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5.2-liter V10 Plus Engine**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
    * **Clutch Pack Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clutch pack can fail, causing the transmission to slip or shudder.
        * Solution: The clutch pack needs to be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain quickly, even when the car is not in use.
    * Solution: The cause of the battery drain needs to be diagnosed and fixed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation can form inside the headlights.
    * Solution: The headlights need to be resealed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can experience glitches, such as freezing or crashing.
    * Solution: The infotainment system software needs to be updated.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if under warranty, otherwise $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 Audi R8 is a high-performance sports car that is generally reliable. However, there are a few issues that owners should be aware of, including valve spring failure, clutch pack failure, battery drain, headlight condensation, and infotainment system glitches. The severity of these issues varies, but some of them can be costly to fix.
