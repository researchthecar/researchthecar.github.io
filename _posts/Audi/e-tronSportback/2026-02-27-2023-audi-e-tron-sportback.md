# 2023 Audi e-tron Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi e-tron Sportback is a sleek and luxurious all-electric crossover that blends coupe-like styling with everyday utility. For used-car buyers interested in transitioning to electric mobility, it offers refined driving dynamics, a quiet cabin, and substantial battery power. Reviewing available owner reports highlights very few recurring mechanical or electrical complaints for this model year, though buyers should still carefully inspect the high-voltage battery system and onboard electronics prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### Dual Electric Motors (355 hp)

This dual-motor electric propulsion setup generates around 355 horsepower and provides standard Quattro all-wheel drive. It delivers swift, smooth acceleration with minimal reported mechanical complications.

### Dual Electric Motors S-Line (402 hp)

This high-output dual-motor configuration increases output to roughly 402 horsepower in boost mode for enhanced performance. It maintains strong overall reliability while offering quicker response times.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

A direct-drive single-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless power distribution to the all-wheel-drive system without traditional gear changes. It has proven mechanically robust in daily driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+Sportback&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V240000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+Sportback&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the pushrod screw joint as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi e-tron Sportback presents a solid option in the used luxury electric vehicle market, backed by an absence of widespread owner complaints. Prospective buyers should verify remaining factory warranty coverage, test all touchscreen and driver-assistance features, and ensure the high-voltage battery health is professionally evaluated.
