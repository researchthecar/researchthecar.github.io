**Introduction:**

The 2021 Audi e-tron Sportback is an all-electric SUV that offers a luxurious and environmentally friendly driving experience. It boasts a dual electric motor setup providing 402 horsepower and 490 lb-ft of torque, enabling it to accelerate from 0-60 mph in 5.5 seconds. The e-tron Sportback is lauded for its sleek design, spacious interior, and impressive range of up to 240 miles on a single charge. However, like any vehicle, it has faced some issues since its release.

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

**Reduced Ride Quality**
Description/Symptoms: Complaints of a harsh and uncomfortable ride, especially over bumps and uneven surfaces.
Solution: Replacing the front suspension components, such as the shock absorbers or struts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Reports of excessive battery drain even when the vehicle is parked and turned off.
Solution: Updating the software or installing a battery saver.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+
Recalls: 23V923

**Infotainment System**

**Screen Freezing**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen may freeze or become unresponsive intermittently.
Solution: Rebooting the infotainment system or updating the software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Body Panels**

**Misaligned Body Panels**
Description/Symptoms: Issues with the fit and finish of body panels, such as uneven gaps and misaligned doors.
Solution: Realignment or replacement of affected body panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control System**

**Malfunctioning Air Conditioning**
Description/Symptoms: Reports of the air conditioning system failing to cool the cabin effectively.
Solution: Recharging or replacing the refrigerant, or repairing the compressor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Other**

**Software Bugs**

**Various Software Glitches**
Description/Symptoms: Minor software glitches that may cause occasional issues with features such as navigation or driver assistance systems.
Solution: Software updates and bug fixes.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Audi e-tron Sportback is a well-received vehicle, it has faced some issues, particularly with its suspension and electrical systems. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor software glitches that can be resolved with updates, and others requiring more costly repairs such as suspension component replacement or air conditioning repairs. It's worth noting that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and individual experiences may vary.
