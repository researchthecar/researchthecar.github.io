# 2022 Audi e-tron Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi e-tron Sportback delivers a sleek coupe-like roofline paired with a quiet, luxurious, and technologically advanced electric cabin. For used-car buyers considering this high-end EV, reviewing known powertrain specifications and monitoring initial owner feedback can help ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### Dual-Motor Electric Powertrain (55 quattro)

The 55 quattro utilizes dual asynchronous electric motors generating around 355 horsepower (up to 402 horsepower with boost mode) and 414 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and confident all-weather capability.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

A direct-drive single-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery seamlessly to all four wheels, offering smooth and maintenance-free operation compared to traditional multi-gear transmissions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+Sportback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V240000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=e-tron+Sportback&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the pushrod screw joint as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi e-tron Sportback remains an appealing option in the used luxury electric vehicle market, offering strong performance and refined styling. Prospective buyers should still verify overall vehicle history and ensure all software updates have been properly applied before finalizing a purchase.
