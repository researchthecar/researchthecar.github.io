# 2023 Audi Q7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Audi Q7 delivers a refined luxury midsize SUV experience with sophisticated interior tech, comfortable ride quality, and capable handling. While early ownership feedback is generally positive, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and monitor comfort accessories during test drives.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient daily performance for the base luxury trim.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine generates around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration and towing capability for highway cruising.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

The eight-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission pairs with both engine options and standard quattro all-wheel drive, delivering smooth, responsive gear changes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Audi Q7 remains a strong contender in the luxury three-row SUV segment, balancing performance, comfort, and advanced features. Used car buyers should perform a thorough inspection of electronics and drivetrain components to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
