# 2016 Audi Q7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Audi Q7 means looking into a luxury three-row SUV known for its refined road manners and upscale cabin. While limited owner feedback specifically highlights major mechanical trends for this model year, prospective buyers should always carefully inspect basic functional components and safety equipment during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Supercharged V6 (TFSI)

This 3.0-liter supercharged V6 engine produces approximately 333 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for the large SUV. It is generally regarded as a strong and responsive powerplant, though routine maintenance of cooling and supercharger components is vital.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6 Diesel (TDI)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged diesel V6 offers strong low-end torque and impressive highway fuel economy. Buyers should verify proper maintenance history regarding emissions equipment and fuel systems on diesel variants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

This conventional 8-speed automatic transmission works in tandem with Audi's quattro all-wheel-drive system to deliver smooth and prompt gear changes. It has a reputation for durability when fluid changes are performed at recommended intervals.

## Safety

### Rear Passenger Seat Belt

Owner reports indicate occasional issues with secondary safety restraints, such as rear seat belt latches failing to secure properly.

**Rear Seat Belt Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rear passenger seat belt fails to click or secure properly into its buckle.
- **Solution:** Have the seat belt buckle assembly inspected by a qualified technician and replaced if the internal latching mechanism is defective.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V035000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Audi has notified owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Audi Q7 offers a comfortable and capable luxury driving experience, backed by robust powertrain options. Because used vehicle condition largely depends on prior maintenance, a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, safety restraints, and mechanical wear is strongly recommended.
