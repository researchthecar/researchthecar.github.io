# 2024 Audi Q7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a modern luxury midsize SUV like the 2024 Audi Q7 brings cutting-edge technology, sophisticated styling, and refined performance into focus. While early owner feedback points to minor cabin electronic quirks, the vehicle generally maintains a strong initial ownership posture backed by robust powertrain engineering. Prospective buyers should review the available engine options and check for any localized electronic updates during a standard pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient daily drivability. This base powerplant provides adequate acceleration and balances performance with everyday fuel economy for the Q7 lineup.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine delivers around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque for stronger, more effortless acceleration. It is a smooth and capable option well-suited for towing and highway cruising in this luxury SUV platform.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The high-performance 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine pushes output to roughly 500 horsepower for the SQ7 variant. It provides exhilarating acceleration while retaining daily composure, though buyers should anticipate higher running and maintenance costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with all available engines and distributes power seamlessly via Quattro all-wheel drive. It offers refined gear changes and generally proves reliable during early ownership phases.

## Electrical

### Overhead Console

Owner reports indicate occasional glitches within overhead console electronics, particularly concerning telematics and emergency communication features.

**Emergency Call System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The emergency call feature located in the overhead console may malfunction or throw error messages.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer inspect the overhead console module and perform any necessary software resets or component replacements.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V868000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 214, "Side Impact Protection." An improperly mounted air bag may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the air bag as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V251000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A deactivated front passenger air bag will not deploy during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger seat occupant detection system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V900000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi Q7 presents a compelling option for used luxury SUV buyers, combining powerful engine choices with a refined interior and confident handling. While isolated owner reports highlight minor electrical inconveniences like overhead console telematics faults, widespread mechanical failures remain rare. Thoroughly testing all cabin electronics and verifying maintenance history will help ensure a satisfying purchase.
