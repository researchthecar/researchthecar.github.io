# 2022 Audi Q7 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Audi Q7 delivers a refined luxury midsize SUV experience with advanced technology, comfortable seating, and strong performance capabilities. As a relatively recent model year, owner reports are sparse, but prospective buyers should still evaluate its sophisticated powertrain options and electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This base 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 261 horsepower and 273 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate efficiency and pep for daily driving, though some buyers prefer the stronger six-cylinder options for heavier loads.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

The available 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 engine generates around 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. It offers brisk acceleration and robust towing capability, representing a popular choice for luxury SUV buyers.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Reserved for the high-performance SQ7 variant, this 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 pushes out 500 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance accompanied by a muscular exhaust note.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Tiptronic Automatic

An 8-speed Tiptronic automatic transmission comes standard across all Q7 and SQ7 variants, distributing power smoothly through Audi's Quattro all-wheel-drive system. It generally shifts imperceptibly under normal conditions and matches well with both four-cylinder and multi-cylinder engine options.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V825000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=Q7&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rear-view camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit parameters, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Audi Q7 stands out as a sophisticated, comfortable, and well-rounded luxury SUV choice. Because it is still a newer vehicle on the used market, verified owner complaint data is minimal, but shoppers should verify proper operation of all onboard electronics and powertrain systems before finalizing a purchase.
