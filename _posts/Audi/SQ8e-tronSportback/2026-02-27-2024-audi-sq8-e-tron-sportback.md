# 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron Sportback represents the pinnacle of electric performance from the brand, offering dual-motor and tri-motor layout options with robust power outputs. As a relatively new and highly sophisticated electric vehicle, used car buyers should carefully evaluate its advanced powertrain and complex electrical architectures. While ownership data is still emerging, being aware of available configurations helps in assessing individual vehicle condition.

---

## Engine

### Tri-Motor Electric Drive System

Features a high-performance tri-motor electric setup delivering approximately 496 horsepower and instant torque. This system provides exceptional acceleration while requiring specialized maintenance and inspection of high-voltage cooling systems.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Automatic Transmission

Utilizes a direct-drive single-speed automatic transmission paired with Quattro all-wheel drive. It offers smooth, seamless power delivery without traditional gear-shift hesitation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V240000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Audi&model=SQ8+e-tron&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the pushrod screw joint as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Audi SQ8 e-tron Sportback is an advanced luxury electric crossover with impressive performance credentials. Prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the high-voltage battery health, charging systems, and overall electronics.
