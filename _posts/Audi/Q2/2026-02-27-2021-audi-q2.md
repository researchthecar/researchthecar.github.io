### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Audi Q2 generally exhibits good technical reliability, with most major mechanical components like the engine and transmission performing as expected. Other categories such as suspension, exterior, and interior components show no widespread significant problems. Overall, the Q2 is a dependable choice within its segment, provided routine maintenance is adhered to and potential minor electrical quirks are managed.
