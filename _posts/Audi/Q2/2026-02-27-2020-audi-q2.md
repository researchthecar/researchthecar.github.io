### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Audi Q2 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability across its core mechanical components, including engines and transmissions, with no widespread critical failures reported for any of its available configurations. The chassis and exterior elements also appear robust. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment software glitches that are often resolvable with updates, and a possibility of premature battery failure, which is not uncommon in modern vehicles. Overall, the Q2 is a dependable choice for its segment, provided potential buyers are aware of and prepared for minor electrical system quirks.
