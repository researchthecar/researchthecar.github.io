# 2022 Audi Q2

### Overview
The 2022 Audi Q2, a compact premium SUV, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting Audi's commitment to quality engineering. It offers a blend of sophisticated design, advanced technology, and a refined driving experience. While specific widespread issues are not commonly reported for this model year, like any vehicle, individual components can experience problems. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns across key systems, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear. The Q2 is available with a range of efficient petrol and diesel engines, paired with either manual or automatic transmissions, catering to diverse driver preferences. Its robust build quality and integrated safety features contribute to its appeal in the competitive small SUV segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Audi Q2 demonstrates a commendable level of technical reliability, with no significant widespread issues reported across its core mechanical and electrical systems. The engines and transmissions, including both petrol and diesel options and the S tronic dual-clutch gearbox, are generally robust. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system's software, are the most frequently cited, though these are typically resolved with updates. The suspension, exterior, and interior components also appear to hold up well under normal usage. Overall, the Q2 is a reliable choice within its segment, provided it receives regular maintenance.
