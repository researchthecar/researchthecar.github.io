**Introduction**

The 2021 Audi A1 is a subcompact hatchback. Engine options include a 1.0-liter three-cylinder with 114 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque, a 1.5-liter four-cylinder with 148 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0-liter four-cylinder with 197 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. All models are equipped with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT).

The A1 is praised for its comfortable ride, stylish interior, and responsive handling. However, it is criticized for its limited rear seat space and lack of advanced safety features.
