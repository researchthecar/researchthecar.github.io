**Introduction:**

The 1991 Audi Coupe Quattro is a high-performance sports car powered by a turbocharged 2.3-liter inline-5 engine producing 220 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission and all-wheel drive. It has garnered praise for its responsive handling, stylish design, and comfortable interior. However, the vehicle has also faced some reliability concerns over the years.

**Engine**

* **2.3L Inline-5 Turbocharged**

    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning oil smell and engine overheating. Replace the gasket. $200 - $500
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail due to oil starvation or excessive heat. Replace the turbocharger. $1500 - $2500+
    * **Knock Sensor Failure:** The knock sensor may fail, leading to incorrect engine timing and reduced performance. Replace the knock sensor. $150 - $300

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**

    * **Clutch Slipping:** The clutch may slip under load, resulting in difficulty shifting gears or low power transmission. Replace the clutch. $500 - $1000

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushings:** Premature wear of the lower control arm bushings can cause excessive vibration, poor handling, and misalignment. Replace the bushings. $400 - $700
* **Shock Absorber Failure:** Shock absorbers may fail, resulting in a rough ride, poor handling, and uneven tire wear. Replace the shock absorbers. $600 - $1200

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to lose power. Replace the alternator. $500 - $800
* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to a faulty component, such as a parasitic draw or a failing starter. Diagnose and repair the electrical fault. $150 - $500

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak:** The sunroof seal can fail, allowing water to enter the cabin. Replace or repair the sunroof seal. $200 - $400
* **Headlight Switch Failure:** The headlight switch may fail, resulting in inoperable headlights. Replace the headlight switch. $100 - $200

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:** The instrument cluster may fail, affecting gauges and warning lights. Replace the instrument cluster. $300 - $600
* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:** The HVAC blower motor may fail, resulting in a lack of ventilation or heating. Replace the blower motor. $200 - $400

**Summary**

The 1991 Audi Coupe Quattro is a performance-oriented vehicle with potential reliability issues. Issues can range from minor electrical concerns to major engine and transmission failures, and repair costs can be significant. While enthusiast owners may appreciate its engaging driving dynamics, potential buyers should be aware of the potential maintenance and repair costs associated with this high-performance sports car.
