**Introduction**

The 2004 Saleen S7 is an American high-performance sports car manufactured by Saleen Automotive. It is powered by a 7.0L V8 engine that produces 550 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque. The S7 is available with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission. It is known for its impressive performance and aggressive styling. However, it is also known for its high price and limited production numbers.

**Engine**

**7.0L V8**

* **Valve Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Symptoms include a loss of power, misfires, and a rattling noise from the engine.
    * Solution: The faulty valve springs must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Crankshaft Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Symptoms include a knocking noise from the engine, loss of oil pressure, and engine seizure.
    * Solution: The crankshaft and bearings must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch pedal may feel spongy or slip, making it difficult to shift gears.
    * Solution: The clutch assembly must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the transmission fluid lines or seals can cause the transmission to slip or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: The leaking components must be replaced or repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged control arm bushings can cause a clunking noise from the front of the vehicle when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: The control arm bushings must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: A parasitic drain on the battery can cause the battery to go dead after the vehicle has been parked for an extended period of time.
    * Solution: The source of the parasitic drain must be identified and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation can build up inside the headlight lenses, causing them to fog up.
    * Solution: The headlight lenses must be resealed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The HVAC system may not blow air or may blow air at an incorrect temperature.
    * Solution: The HVAC system may need to be repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2004 Saleen S7 is a high-performance sports car that offers an exciting driving experience, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that it may encounter. The engine and transmission issues can be particularly costly to repair, and the suspension and electrical issues can affect the vehicle's performance and reliability. These issues should be considered when purchasing a used S7 and factoring in the cost of potential repairs.
