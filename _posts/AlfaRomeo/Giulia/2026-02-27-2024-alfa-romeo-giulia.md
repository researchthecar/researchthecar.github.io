# 2024 Alfa Romeo Giulia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Alfa Romeo Giulia delivers engaging driving dynamics, sharp steering, and striking Italian styling, making it a distinct choice in the luxury sport sedan segment. Buyers shopping on the used market will want to weigh its captivating performance and upscale interior against typical luxury maintenance requirements and occasional electrical quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk daily acceleration and balanced power delivery, though prospective owners should stay on top of scheduled maintenance.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6 (Quadrifoglio)

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio model, this Ferrari-derived 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 generates a robust 505 horsepower. It transforms the sedan into a track-capable machine, requiring meticulous upkeep and premium operating costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission features quick, responsive shifts and pairs smoothly with both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V667000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the radio, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V845000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Headlights that fail can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the wheel arch liner and repair the wiring harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Alfa Romeo Giulia remains an exciting option for driving enthusiasts who prioritize style and handling over conventional segment choices. While standard powertrain options are robust, thorough pre-purchase inspections and verification of remaining factory warranty coverage are strongly recommended for used buyers.
