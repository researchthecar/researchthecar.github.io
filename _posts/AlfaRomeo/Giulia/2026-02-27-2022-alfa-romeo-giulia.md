# 2022 Alfa Romeo Giulia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Alfa Romeo Giulia delivers engaging driving dynamics, sharp Italian styling, and a driver-focused cabin that sets it apart from traditional luxury sport sedans. For used car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and monitoring owner-reported quirks are key to a confident purchase. While it offers strong performance, prospective owners should review vehicle history records carefully.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk daily acceleration and balanced efficiency, though routine maintenance is essential for long-term health.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6

Reserved for the high-performance Quadrifoglio model, this Ferrari-derived 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 pushes out 505 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers thrilling track-ready performance but demands meticulous upkeep and higher maintenance budgets.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with both engine options, delivering quick shifts and smooth power delivery across rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V667000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the radio, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
