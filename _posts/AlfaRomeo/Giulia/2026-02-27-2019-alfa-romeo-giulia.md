# 2019 Alfa Romeo Giulia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Alfa Romeo Giulia delivers striking Italian styling and engaging driving dynamics that set it apart in the compact luxury sport sedan segment. However, prospective buyers should approach the pre-owned market with care, as owner reports highlight potential electrical, fuel system, and braking concerns. Keeping up with proper maintenance and checking for unresolved recall campaigns are crucial steps when considering a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration and spirited performance. It serves as the standard powerplant across most Giulia trims, balancing everyday efficiency with dynamic capability.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Pressure Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected engine stalling, hesitation upon acceleration, or illuminated check engine and electronic throttle control warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel pressure sensors, fuel delivery module, and associated wiring or relays. Perform any necessary factory recall services.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, rapid gear shifts and pairs exceptionally well with the turbocharged engine. It is generally regarded as a robust unit in modern luxury applications, though overall reliability depends on regular fluid maintenance.

## Brakes

### Service and Parking Brakes

The braking system provides strong stopping power, but electronic and hydraulic complaints suggest potential risks that warrant a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

**Braking Malfunctions and Parking Brake Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported extended braking distances, unresponsive brake pedals, or the electronic parking brake engaging independently and failing to disengage.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hydraulic brake system, electronic parking brake actuator, and related control modules for fault codes or mechanical wear.

## Safety

### Airbags and Restraints

While equipped with comprehensive passive safety systems, owners have occasionally flagged concerns regarding restraint system responses during impacts.

**Airbag Non-Deployment Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** In certain frontal or side collision incidents, owners noted that the supplemental airbags failed to deploy as expected.
- **Solution:** Verify that all airbag sensors, wiring harnesses, and the Restraints Control Module are functioning properly with no active diagnostic trouble codes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V551000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may be operated without enough fuel in the tank causing a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo will notify owners, and dealers will update the BCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V148000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Adaptive Cruise Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The driver's inability to deactivate the ACC can increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo has notified owners, and dealers will update the BSM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V382000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An unexpected decrease in brake performance increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the brake system module (BSM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V943000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair, the brake booster connection, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V586000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel delivery module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Alfa Romeo Giulia is an exciting and rewarding sedan to drive, but it demands careful evaluation on the used market. Prospective owners should secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a marque specialist, verify all open recalls have been completed, and budget for potential electronic or fuel system maintenance.
