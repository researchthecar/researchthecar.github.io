# 2017 Alfa Romeo Giulia Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Alfa Romeo Giulia offers engaging driving dynamics, striking Italian styling, and sharp handling that appeals to enthusiast buyers. However, used car shoppers should approach early model years with caution due to recurring owner complaints regarding electrical issues, fuel pump issues, and intermittent engine stalling. Thoroughly inspecting service records and verifying that all software updates have been completed is strongly advised before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The standard 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. While generally praised for its performance, owners have reported occasional fuel pump failures and electronic throttle issues.

**Fuel Pump and Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience sudden engine stalling while driving at highway or city speeds, accompanied by fuel system warnings.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump and fuel control module.

**Electronic Throttle Control and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Acceleration may fail to respond, followed by a 'Service Electronic Throttle Control' warning message and potential engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnose electronic throttle body components and update or replace control modules as necessary.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6 (Quadrifoglio)

The high-performance Quadrifoglio trim features a Ferrari-derived 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 generating 505 horsepower. It delivers thrilling track-ready performance, though complex maintenance requires vigilant upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, rapid gear changes across all trims and is generally robust. Rare owner reports note unexpected shifting behavior into neutral while driving.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly drop into neutral gear while in motion on the highway or during intersection turns.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission control module software, wiring harnesses, and shift actuators.

## Brakes

### Electronic Parking Brake & Service Brakes

Braking systems provide exceptional stopping power, though some owners have reported electrical wiring corrosion affecting electronic parking brake functionality.

**Unexpected Parking Brake Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic parking brake may engage spontaneously while the vehicle is parked or in motion and refuse to disengage.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake wiring harnesses for corrosion and replace faulty parking brake actuators or control modules.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical system issues, including sensor warnings and lighting glitches, are among the more frequent owner complaints for early production models.

**Headlight Moisture and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Front headlights may experience condensation buildup, leading to premature assembly failure.
- **Solution:** Replace compromised headlight assemblies and check seals.

### Sunroof

Exterior body complaints primarily center around rare instances of panoramic glass roof failures and trim alignment.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass sunroof may unexpectedly fracture or explode outward while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass assembly and inspect for frame alignment or stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V636000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These engines may misfire causing the catalytic converter to overheat, possibly resulting in damage to the nearby wiring and engine components. Heat damage to the engine compartment wiring or components can increase the risk of a fire or it can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles for diagnostic codes, and update the engine control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V828000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Brake fluid leaking onto the exhaust can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will install a bracket on the brake fluid line to prevent contact with the coolant hose clamp. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V148000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Adaptive Cruise Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's inability to deactivate the ACC can increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo has notified owners, and dealers will update the BSM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V382000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An unexpected decrease in brake performance increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the brake system module (BSM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V586000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Giulia&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel delivery module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Alfa Romeo Giulia remains a compelling choice for drivers prioritizing style and handling over traditional luxury-segment predictability. Prospective buyers should carefully review service history, verify electrical and fuel pump integrity, and ensure all previous owner complaints have been properly resolved by a qualified technician.
