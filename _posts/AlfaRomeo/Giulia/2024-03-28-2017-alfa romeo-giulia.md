## 2017 Alfa Romeo Giulia

The 2017 Alfa Romeo Giulia is a compact luxury sports sedan that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a standard turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 with 505 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. The Giulia is praised for its agile handling, responsive steering, and stylish design, but it has also been criticized for its high price and some reliability concerns.

### **Engine**

- **2.0-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
    - **Misfiring**
        - Description/Symptoms: The engine misfires, causing a rough idle, loss of power, and increased emissions.
        - Solution: Replace the faulty ignition coil or spark plug.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        - Recalls: None Found
  

- **2.9-Liter Twin-Turbocharged V6**
    - **Turbocharger Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger fails, resulting in a loss of power, increased exhaust smoke, and whistling noises from the engine bay.
        - Solution: Replace the failed turbocharger.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        - Recalls: None Found


### **Transmission**

- **Eight-Speed Automatic**
    - **Transmission Slipping**
        - Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips out of gear, causing a loss of power and erratic shifting.
        - Solution: Repair or replace the transmission.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        - Recalls: None Found


### **Suspension**

- **No Issues Found**


### **Electrical**

- **Battery Drain**
    - Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    - Solution: Replace the battery or troubleshoot the electrical system to identify the source of the drain.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found


### **Exterior**

- **No Issues Found**


### **Interior**

- **Infotainment Screen Malfunctions**
    - Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen malfunctions, causing intermittent or complete loss of functionality.
    - Solution: Replace the infotainment unit.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found


### **Summary**

The 2017 Alfa Romeo Giulia is a thrilling sports sedan with some notable reliability concerns. The engine and transmission issues can be expensive to repair, while the electrical and interior issues can be frustrating. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues should be considered when purchasing or owning a Giulia.
