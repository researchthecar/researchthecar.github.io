# 2023 Alfa Romeo Tonale Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Alfa Romeo Tonale introduces the Italian brand to the compact luxury plug-in hybrid SUV segment, blending distinctive styling with electrified performance. As a relatively new model sharing its architecture with the Dodge Hornet, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate both its powertrain options and early owner-reported quirks. While stylish and well-equipped, pre-owned shoppers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly and review maintenance histories.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged Plug-In Hybrid Inline-4

The 1.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder plug-in hybrid engine pairs with an electric motor to produce a combined 285 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and all-electric driving capability, though buyers should verify battery health and charging equipment operation on used models.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A smooth-shifting 6-speed automatic transmission works in tandem with the hybrid system to route power to all four wheels. It generally operates seamlessly, though complex hybrid powertrains warrant a thorough test drive to check for shifting smoothness and seamless transitions between electric and gas power.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V623000: Electrical System:Propulsion System: Cables:Fastener/Connector](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The 12-volt battery positive cable and/or high voltage connector cable may not be tightened properly, which can overheat the electrical connections and result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the cable connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V696000: Back Over Prevention:Warnings:External/Pedestrian Alert](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 141, "Minimum Sound Requirements for Hybrid and Electric Vehicles." Failure to alert pedestrians when backing up increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear pedestrian siren connection and, if necessary, secure and/or replace the connector or siren, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V175000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." A vehicle with an incorrect maximum capacity weight value may be overloaded, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will add an overlay label to the tire placard information, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V246000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The display module may not show the rearview camera image. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software or replace the display as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Alfa Romeo Tonale offers an enticing mix of plug-in hybrid efficiency and sharp Italian design for used-car buyers. Because formal complaint data for this model year is minimal, shoppers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician and ensure all dealer-recalled software updates have been properly completed.
