# 2024 Alfa Romeo Tonale Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Alfa Romeo Tonale brings striking Italian styling and electrified performance to the compact luxury crossover segment. As a newer addition to the market sharing its underlying architecture with the Dodge Hornet, prospective used-car buyers should weigh its dynamic character against potential early-production electronics and powertrain teething pains reported by initial owners.

---

## Engine

### 1.3L Turbocharged Inline-4 Plug-in Hybrid

This 1.3-liter turbocharged plug-in hybrid powertrain produces an estimated 285 total system horsepower paired with an electric motor and all-wheel drive. It offers brisk acceleration and electric-only driving capability, though buyers should verify proper hybrid system function during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed traditional automatic transmission handles power delivery for the plug-in hybrid setup, routing torque to the front wheels while the rear axle is driven electrically. It generally delivers smooth shifts, though complex power-split transitions warrant a thorough test drive.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V623000: Electrical System:Propulsion System: Cables:Fastener/Connector](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The 12-volt battery positive cable and/or high voltage connector cable may not be tightened properly, which can overheat the electrical connections and result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the cable connections as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V696000: Back Over Prevention:Warnings:External/Pedestrian Alert](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 141, "Minimum Sound Requirements for Hybrid and Electric Vehicles." Failure to alert pedestrians when backing up increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear pedestrian siren connection and, if necessary, secure and/or replace the connector or siren, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V175000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." A vehicle with an incorrect maximum capacity weight value may be overloaded, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will add an overlay label to the tire placard information, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V209000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Missing rim and tire size information could lead to an incorrect wheel being installed, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will apply a corrected label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V752000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Tonale&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of brake function increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reinforce the brake pedal arm, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Alfa Romeo Tonale remains an intriguing choice for buyers seeking distinctive design and plug-in efficiency in a compact luxury package. Because it is a relatively recent model, verifying that all manufacturer campaigns have been completed and conducting a careful inspection of the electronics and hybrid system will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
