**Introduction**

The 2012 Alfa Romeo Giulietta is a stylish and sporty compact hatchback that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 1.4-liter turbocharged gasoline engine that produces 168 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to a six-speed manual or six-speed automatic transmission. The Giulietta is praised for its handling, agile chassis, and responsive steering. However, it has received some criticism for its cramped interior and lack of rear legroom.

**Engine**

**1.4-liter Turbocharged Gasoline Engine**

* **Issue:** Premature turbocharger failure
* Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost pressure, decreased engine performance, check engine light
* Solution: Replace turbocharger
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: N177, N181

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* No Issues Found

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Issue:** Hesitation and rough shifting
* Description/Symptoms: Slow gear changes, slipping gears, shuddering
* Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* Recalls: N182

**Suspension**

* **Issue:** Premature shock absorber wear
* Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride comfort, excessive body roll, suspension noise
* Solution: Replace shock absorbers
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Issue:** Battery drain
* Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains overnight, difficulty starting the car
* Solution: Replace or repair alternator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Issue:** Loose door handles
* Description/Symptoms: Door handles do not open smoothly, door handles rattle
* Solution: Tighten door handles
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Issue:** Faulty climate control unit
* Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate temperature readings, fan not blowing properly
* Solution: Replace climate control unit
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 Alfa Romeo Giulietta is a fun and engaging vehicle to drive, but it has some potential reliability issues. The most severe and costly problems are related to the turbocharger and transmission, which can require expensive repairs. However, most issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Giulietta is a solid choice for drivers who value performance and style, but it is important to be aware of its potential drawbacks.
