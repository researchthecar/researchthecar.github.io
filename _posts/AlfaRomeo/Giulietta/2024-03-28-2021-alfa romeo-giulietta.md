**Introduction**

The 2021 Alfa Romeo Giulietta is a compact luxury sedan that offers a blend of performance and style. It is available with a range of four-cylinder engine options and a choice of manual or automatic transmissions. Despite its appealing features, the Giulietta has been plagued by a number of issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **1.4L MultiAir Turbocharged**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and failure of the timing chain tensioner can lead to a stretched or broken timing chain, resulting in engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and possibly the timing chain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **1.6L JTDM Diesel**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction or failure of the turbocharger can cause a loss of power, increased fuel consumption, or a rattling noise.
        * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **1.8L TBI**
    * **Fuel Injector Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty fuel injectors can lead to rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, or decreased fuel economy.
        * Solution: Replacement of the affected fuel injector(s).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per injector
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**
    * **Shift Linkage Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or grinding noises during shifts.
        * Solution: Replacement of the shift linkage components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Issues with the TCM can cause transmission errors, shifting problems, or hesitation.
        * Solution: Reprogramming or replacement of the TCM.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged struts can cause clunking noises, uneven tire wear, or a rough ride.
    * Solution: Replacement of the front suspension struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800 per strut
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, even after replacing it.
    * Solution: Inspection for parasitic current draw and repair of faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Gaps**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or excessive gaps between body panels can affect the vehicle's appearance.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of body panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Issues with the climate control system can lead to malfunctioning heating, cooling, or air flow.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Infotainment System Bugs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Software glitches, screen freezing, or connectivity issues.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2021 Alfa Romeo Giulietta has faced a range of issues that affect its reliability and performance. Engine problems, transmission malfunctions, suspension issues, and electrical gremlins can lead to costly repairs. Interior issues, such as climate control malfunctions and infotainment bugs, further detract from the ownership experience. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can significantly impact the vehicle's value and desirability.
