**Introduction:**

The 2015 Alfa Romeo Giulietta is a compact hatchback that offers a sporty driving experience. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 1.4-liter turbocharged gasoline engine with 120 horsepower and 152 lb-ft of torque, a 1.8-liter turbocharged gasoline engine with 170 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged gasoline engine with 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed manual transmission and a six-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the Giulietta praise its handling, performance, and styling. However, some people have reported issues with the car's reliability.

**Engine**

**1.4-liter Turbocharged Gasoline Engine**

* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, stalling
    * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, reduced engine power, stalling
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.8-liter Turbocharged Gasoline Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, smoke from exhaust, whistling sound
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil level, engine knocking
    * Solution: Repair or replace piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Gasoline Engine**

* **Connecting Rod Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking sound from engine, reduced engine power, stalling
    * Solution: Replace connecting rod bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil pressure, engine damage
    * Solution: Replace oil pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear Shift Difficulty:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise
    * Solution: Replace or repair shift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Valve Body Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, slipping gears, no reverse gear
    * Solution: Replace or repair valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from transmission, low fluid level
    * Solution: Replace or repair leaking seals or gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from rear suspension
    * Solution: Replace or repair rear suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains overnight, difficulty starting car
    * Solution: Replace or repair battery, identify and fix source of drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wipers Streaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windshield wipers leave streaks or smudges
    * Solution: Replace or repair windshield wipers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Panel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Instrument panel goes black or displays incorrect information
    * Solution: Replace or repair instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sun Visor Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sun visor droops or falls down
    * Solution: Replace or repair sun visor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 Alfa Romeo Giulietta has some significant reliability issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. Owners may experience engine problems such as carbon buildup on intake valves, timing chain tensioner failure, turbocharger failure, and oil consumption. Transmission problems include gear shift difficulty, valve body failure, and transmission fluid leaks. Other issues include rear suspension noise, battery drain, and instrument panel failure. These issues can be expensive to repair, so it's important to consider the potential costs before purchasing a Giulietta.
