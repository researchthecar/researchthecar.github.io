## Introduction

The 2017 Alfa Romeo 4C is a mid-engine, rear-wheel drive sports car manufactured by Alfa Romeo. It is available with a single engine and transmission option:

* 1.75L turbocharged inline-four engine producing 237 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque
* Six-speed dual-clutch transmission (TCT)

The 4C is known for its lightweight carbon fiber monocoque chassis, responsive handling, and aggressive styling. However, it has also received criticism for its cramped interior, limited practicality, and high price.

## Engine

**1.75L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

**Turbocharger Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust, whistling or grinding noises from the turbocharger
* Solution: Replace turbocharger
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
* Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, rough idle, hesitation or stalling upon acceleration
* Solution: Walnut blasting or chemical cleaning of intake valves
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

**Six-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (TCT)**

**Clutch Pack Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Slipping or shuddering during gear changes, difficulty engaging gears, loss of power
* Solution: Replace clutch pack
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Erratic gear changes, transmission stuck in limp mode, check engine light
* Solution: Replace TCM
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
* Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

**No Issues Found**

## Electrical

**Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly when parked, electrical components malfunction
* Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Headlight Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: One or both headlights fail to illuminate
* Solution: Replace headlight assembly
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

**Clearcoat Peeling**
* Description/Symptoms: Clearcoat layer on paint peels or flakes off
* Solution: Repaint affected panels
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
* Recalls: None Found

## Interior

**Climate Control Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to regulate temperature, excessive noise or vibration
* Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2017 Alfa Romeo 4C is a niche sports car with a number of potential issues to consider. Engine and transmission problems can be expensive to repair, while electrical and exterior issues can range from minor inconveniences to costly repairs.

The severity of these issues can vary depending on the specific model year and mileage of the vehicle. If you are considering purchasing a 4C, it is important to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic to identify any potential problems before making a decision.
