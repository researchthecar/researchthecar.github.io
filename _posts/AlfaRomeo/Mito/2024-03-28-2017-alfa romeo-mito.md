**Introduction:**
The 2017 Alfa Romeo Mito is a subcompact hatchback featuring a 1.4L turbocharged gasoline engine paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. Praised for its stylish design, agile handling, and sporty performance, the Mito has faced some common issues reported by owners.

**Engine**
* **1.4L Turbocharged Gasoline Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Turbocharger malfunctions, resulting in reduced power, excessive smoke, and engine noise.
        * **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Loose timing chain causes rattling or grinding noises, potentially leading to catastrophic engine failure.
        * **Solution:** Timing chain tensioner replacement
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**
* **6-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Difficulty Shifting Gears:** Hard or impossible to shift gears, especially into higher gears.
        * **Solution:** Transmission overhauling or replacement
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**
* **Rear Shock Absorber Leakage:** Leaking shock absorbers reduce suspension effectiveness, causing a rough ride and potential handling problems.
        * **Solution:** Shock absorber replacement
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+ per shock absorber
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**
* **Body Control Module Failure:** Malfunctioning BCM causes electrical issues such as non-responsive buttons, inoperative lights, and battery drain.
        * **Solution:** BCM replacement
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**
* **Door Handle Breakage:** Plastic door handles can break, making it difficult to open the doors.
        * **Solution:** Door handle replacement
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300+ per handle
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**
* **Air Conditioning System Failure:** Malfunctioning HVAC system leads to poor airflow, insufficient heating or cooling, and strange noises.
        * **Solution:** AC compressor or condenser replacement
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**
While the Alfa Romeo Mito offers a stylish and sporty driving experience, it has faced some notable issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical components. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be severe and costly. Potential owners should carefully consider the reliability concerns before making a purchase decision.
