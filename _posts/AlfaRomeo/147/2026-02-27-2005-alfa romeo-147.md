# 2005 Alfa Romeo 147 Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2005 Alfa Romeo 147, a popular Italian compact car, offered a blend of sporty styling and engaging driving dynamics. While generally appreciated for its design and handling, like many vehicles of its era, it has specific areas where reliability concerns have been noted by owners and mechanics. This analysis focuses on common technical issues reported, excluding routine maintenance and general wear and tear, to provide a clearer picture of its long-term dependability. Understanding these potential pitfalls can help prospective owners make informed decisions and current owners prepare for necessary repairs.

## Engine

### 1.9L JTDm Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration or DPF replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**EGR Valve Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, black smoke from exhaust, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 1.6L Twin Spark Petrol
**Camshaft Variator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise on startup, loss of low-end torque, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft variator unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Ignition System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, poor acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of spark plugs, ignition coils, or related wiring.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


### 2.0L Twin Spark Petrol
**Camshaft Variator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise on startup, loss of low-end torque, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the camshaft variator unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Ignition System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, poor acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of spark plugs, ignition coils, or related wiring.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, stiff pedal.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000


### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, stiff pedal.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000


### Selespeed (Automated Manual)
**Hydraulic System Leaks/Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Failure to shift gears, error messages on the dashboard, slow gear changes.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic pump, accumulator, or related lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear changes, inability to select gears, dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gear selection sensors or control unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Wishbone Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn wishbone bushings or complete wishbone assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Rear Shock Absorbers**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, visible leaks from shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent issues with lights, wipers, central locking, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


### Window Regulators
**Power Window Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Windows not operating, slow movement, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Exterior

### Body Panels & Paint
**Rust on Wheel Arches and Sills**
- **Symptoms:** Visible corrosion, bubbling paintwork, especially on older models or those in humid climates.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and repair, repainting.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Interior

### Air Conditioning System
**AC Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No cold air, unusual noises from the AC system.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the AC compressor and potentially a system flush.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500


### Dashboard Display
**Pixelated Display**
- **Symptoms:** Missing segments or complete failure of the central information display on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the display unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2005 Alfa Romeo 147 presents a mixed reliability profile. While its petrol Twin Spark engines can suffer from variator and ignition issues, and the diesel JTDm engines are prone to DPF and EGR problems, these are often addressable with proper maintenance and timely repairs. The Selespeed transmission is a notable area for potential expense due to its complex hydraulic system. Suspension components like wishbone bushings are common wear items. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the BCM and window regulators, can also arise. Rust can be a concern in certain environments. Despite these potential issues, many owners find the driving experience and distinctive styling outweigh the reliability concerns, provided they are prepared for the associated maintenance costs.
