# 2002 Alfa Romeo 147 Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2002 Alfa Romeo 147, a popular Italian hatchback, offered a blend of sporty styling and engaging driving dynamics. While generally appreciated for its design and handling, like many vehicles of its era, it has specific areas where reliability concerns have been noted by owners and mechanics. This analysis focuses on common technical issues reported, excluding routine maintenance and general wear. Understanding these potential pitfalls can help prospective owners and current owners manage expectations and maintenance costs for this distinctive car.

## Engine

### 1.9L JTD Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whining noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, engine warning light illumination.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 1.6L Twin Spark Petrol
**Cam Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine stoppage, significant internal engine damage if belt breaks.
- **Solution:** Timely replacement of the cam belt and tensioners is crucial; failure can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
- **Cost:** $500 - $800

**Water Pump Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, visible corrosion around the pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### 2.0L Twin Spark Petrol
**Cam Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine stoppage, significant internal engine damage if belt breaks.
- **Solution:** Timely replacement of the cam belt and tensioners is crucial; failure can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
- **Cost:** $500 - $800

**Water Pump Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, visible corrosion around the pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, heavy clutch pedal.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement (kit includes pressure plate, friction disc, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000

**Gearbox Synchro Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty selecting specific gears.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the gearbox, focusing on synchro rings.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### Selespeed (Automated Manual)
**Hydraulic System Leaks/Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to select gears, 'check Selespeed' warning light, slow gear changes.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic pump, accumulator, actuators, or control unit.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500+

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear changes, 'check Selespeed' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty sensors (e.g., gear position sensor).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Wishbone Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tyre wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn front wishbone bushings or the entire wishbone assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Rear Shock Absorber Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, reduced damping, visible fluid leaks from shock absorbers.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical gremlins, such as non-functioning lights, wipers, central locking, or dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Window Regulators
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows failing to operate, windows dropping unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per window


### Airbag System
**Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag warning light stays illuminated on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify faulty sensor, wiring, or control module; repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Exterior

### Bodywork
**Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion, particularly around wheel arches, sills, and door bottoms.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and repair, potentially involving panel replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+ (depending on severity)


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced light output, aesthetic degradation.
- **Solution:** Restoration kits or replacement of headlight units.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $200


### Dashboard Trim
**Peeling/Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Soft-touch dashboard materials can degrade and peel over time, especially in warmer climates.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of affected trim pieces.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Alfa Romeo 147 presents a mixed reliability picture. While its engines, particularly the Twin Spark variants, require diligent maintenance (especially cam belt changes), they can be robust. The JTD diesel engines are generally more reliable but can suffer from turbo or EGR issues. The Selespeed transmission is a known weak point, often requiring significant investment to maintain. Electrical gremlins, particularly related to the BCM and window regulators, are not uncommon. Suspension components like bushings can wear, and rust is a concern on older examples. Despite these potential issues, the 147 remains a desirable car for those who appreciate its driving character and Italian flair, provided they are prepared for potentially higher maintenance costs than some rivals.
