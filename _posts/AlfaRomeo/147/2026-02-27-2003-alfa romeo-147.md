# 2003 Alfa Romeo 147 Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2003 Alfa Romeo 147, a popular Italian compact car, offered a blend of sporty styling and engaging driving dynamics. While generally appreciated for its design and handling, like many vehicles of its era, it has specific areas where reliability can be a concern. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems encountered by owners of various engine and transmission configurations. Understanding these potential pitfalls can help prospective buyers and current owners manage expectations and maintenance costs.

## Engine

### 1.9L JTD Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whining noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, engine warning light illumination.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 1.6L Twin Spark Petrol
**Camshaft Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine stoppage, catastrophic engine damage if belt breaks.
- **Solution:** Timely replacement of the timing belt and tensioners is crucial.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Water Pump Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks from the front of the engine, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump.
- **Cost:** $250 - $450


### 2.0L Twin Spark Petrol
**Camshaft Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine stoppage, catastrophic engine damage if belt breaks.
- **Solution:** Timely replacement of the timing belt and tensioners is crucial.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Water Pump Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks from the front of the engine, overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump.
- **Cost:** $250 - $450

**Ignition System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, reduced engine performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of spark plugs, ignition coils, or related wiring.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, heavy clutch pedal.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement (kit includes pressure plate, friction disc, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000

**Gearbox Synchro Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty selecting gears.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the gearbox.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


### Selespeed (Automated Manual)
**Hydraulic System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Failure to shift gears, error messages on the dashboard, pump noise.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic pump, accumulator, or lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000+

**Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear changes, inability to select gears, dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of relevant sensors (e.g., gear position sensor).
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Wishbone Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tyre wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn wishbone bushings or the entire wishbone assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Rear Shock Absorbers**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, visible leaks from shock absorbers.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $250 - $450


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical components malfunctioning randomly (lights, wipers, windows), dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Window Regulators
**Failure to Operate**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not moving up or down, grinding noises from door panels.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Exterior

### Bodywork
**Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion appearing on wheel arches, sills, and around windows.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and repair, potentially repainting affected areas.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced light output, poor aesthetics.
- **Solution:** Polishing or replacement of headlight units.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### Air Conditioning System
**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No cold air, unusual noises from the engine bay when AC is on.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the AC compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000


### Dashboard Illumination
**Pixelated Displays**
- **Symptoms:** Missing segments or complete failure of the multi-information display (MID) or odometer.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the instrument cluster.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2003 Alfa Romeo 147 presents a mixed reliability picture. While its engines, particularly the Twin Spark variants, can be prone to specific issues like timing belt and water pump problems, and the diesel engines may face turbo or EGR valve concerns, these are often manageable with timely maintenance. The Selespeed transmission is a notable area of potential expense due to its complex hydraulic system. Suspension components like bushings can wear, and electrical gremlins, especially related to the BCM, are not uncommon. Rust can also be a concern depending on the vehicle's history and climate. Despite these potential issues, many owners find the driving experience and distinctive styling outweigh the reliability concerns, provided a diligent maintenance schedule is followed.
