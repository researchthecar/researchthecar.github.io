# 2007 Alfa Romeo 159 Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2007 Alfa Romeo 159, while celebrated for its striking Italian design and engaging driving dynamics, presents a mixed reliability profile. Owners often praise its road presence and handling, but certain mechanical and electrical components have been noted for potential issues. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into areas that may require attention for owners of this model. Understanding these potential pitfalls can help in proactive maintenance and managing ownership costs.

## Engine

### 1.9L JTDm Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration or DPF cleaning/replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**EGR Valve Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, black smoke from exhaust, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### 6-Speed Q-Tronic Automatic
**Occasional Gear Selection Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation or difficulty shifting between gears, particularly noticeable at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter change, or potential internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Wishbone Bushings Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn wishbone bushings or complete wishbone assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Body Control Module (BCM) Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent issues with lights, windows, central locking, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module diagnosis and potential replacement.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

**Sensor Failures (e.g., ABS/VDC)**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated ABS or VDC warning lights, loss of traction control functionality.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Alfa Romeo 159 offers a compelling blend of style and driving pleasure, but potential owners should be aware of its reliability quirks. Diesel variants, particularly the 1.9L and 2.4L JTDm, are more prone to issues related to the DPF and EGR systems, which can be costly to repair. The Q-Tronic automatic transmission may also experience occasional gear selection problems. On the electrical front, BCM glitches and sensor failures are not uncommon. Suspension components, specifically front wishbone bushings, can wear prematurely. While petrol engines and manual transmissions generally fare better, proactive maintenance and a thorough pre-purchase inspection are highly recommended to mitigate potential repair costs and ensure a more enjoyable ownership experience.
