# 2012 Alfa Romeo 159 Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2012 Alfa Romeo 159, while celebrated for its striking design and engaging driving dynamics, presents a mixed picture regarding long-term reliability. Owners often praise its Italian flair and performance, particularly with the diesel engines. However, like many European vehicles of its era, it can be susceptible to specific electrical gremlins and some mechanical components may require more attention than their Japanese or American counterparts. Maintenance history and diligent servicing are crucial for mitigating potential issues and ensuring a positive ownership experience. This data focuses on common reported faults beyond standard wear and tear.

## Engine

### 1.9L JTDm Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**EGR Valve Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, black smoke from exhaust, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 2.4L JTDm Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke, oil leaks around the turbo.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

### 6-Speed Q-Tronic Automatic
**Occasional Gear Shifting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation during shifts, rough gear changes, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter change, software update, or internal repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Wishbone Bushings Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front wishbone bushings or complete wishbone assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, grinding noises from door panel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Sensor Malfunctions (ABS/ESP)**
- **Symptoms:** ABS and ESP warning lights illuminated, potential loss of ABS/ESP functionality.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty wheel speed sensors or related modules.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or head unit repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Alfa Romeo 159 offers a compelling blend of style and performance, but potential buyers should be aware of its specific reliability quirks. Diesel engines, particularly the 1.9L and 2.4L JTDm, are prone to DPF and EGR issues, while the automatic transmission can sometimes exhibit shifting problems. Electrical gremlins, such as window regulators and sensor faults, are also noted. Suspension components, specifically front wishbone bushings, may require attention. While the petrol engines and manual transmissions are generally more robust, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a commitment to regular, specialized maintenance are highly recommended to ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
