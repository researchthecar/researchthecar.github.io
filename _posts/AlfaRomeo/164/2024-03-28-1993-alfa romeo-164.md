**Introduction:**

The 1993 Alfa Romeo 164 is a mid-size luxury sedan that was produced from 1992 to 1997. It was available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder, a 2.5-liter V6, and a 3.0-liter V6. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual, a 4-speed automatic, and a 6-speed manual. The 164 was praised for its stylish design, spacious interior, and handling, but it was also criticized for its high price and reliability issues.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter 4-Cylinder**

* **Blown Head Gasket**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the head gasket, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Belt Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Skipped or broken timing belt, engine won't start or runs poorly
    * Solution: Replace timing belt
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-Liter V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the intake manifold gasket, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-Liter V6**

* **Oil Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil pressure, engine damage
    * Solution: Replace oil pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, stalling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace camshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Synchronizer Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping out of gear, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping when shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking struts, rough ride, loss of handling
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, loss of handling
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, loss of electrical power
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, clicking or grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working, stuck or slow windows
    * Solution: Replace window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks not working, stuck or slow locks
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not working, blowing hot or cold air
    * Solution: Replace climate control module or actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Lights Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard lights not working, dim or flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace bulbs or circuit board
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1993 Alfa Romeo 164 is a stylish and fun-to-drive sedan, but it comes with a number of potential reliability issues. Some of the most common problems include blown head gaskets, timing belt failures, intake manifold gasket failures, and transmission slipping. These issues can be expensive to fix, and they can significantly reduce the value of the car. However, if you're willing to take the risk, the 164 can be a rewarding car to own.
