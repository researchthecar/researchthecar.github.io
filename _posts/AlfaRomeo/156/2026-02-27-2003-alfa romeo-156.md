# 2003 Alfa Romeo 156

### Overview
The 2003 Alfa Romeo 156, a stylish Italian sedan, offered a compelling blend of performance and design. While it garnered praise for its driving dynamics and distinctive aesthetics, like many vehicles of its era, it presented certain reliability considerations. Owners often reported a mixed bag of experiences, with some enjoying trouble-free ownership while others encountered specific recurring issues. The 156's mechanical complexity, particularly with its engines and electrical systems, meant that maintenance and repairs could sometimes be more involved and costly than for its German or Japanese rivals. However, for enthusiasts, the unique character and driving pleasure often outweighed these potential drawbacks.

---


## Engine

### 1.9L JTD Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or rebuild.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### 2.0L Twin Spark Petrol
**Cam Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine shutdown, severe internal engine damage.
- **Solution:** Immediate replacement of cam belt, tensioners, and idlers. Potential engine rebuild if failure occurred.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 (preventative), $3000+ (if failed)

**Ignition System Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, poor fuel economy, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of spark plugs, ignition coils, or wiring.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 2.4L JTD Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or rebuild.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, poor acceleration, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Transmission

### Manual Gearbox
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


### Selespeed (Automated Manual)
**Hydraulic System Leaks/Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to select gears, error messages on the dashboard, pump failure.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic pump, accumulator, actuators, or lines. Fluid replacement.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500+

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear changes, 'Selespeed System Failure' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of relevant sensors (e.g., gear position sensor).
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Bushings and Ball Joints**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn bushings and ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Front Wishbone Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Knocking sounds, poor handling, premature tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front wishbones (often the lower ones).
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion and Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functioning lights, dashboard warnings, starting issues.
- **Solution:** Inspection, repair, or replacement of damaged wiring sections. Cleaning of connectors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800+


### ECU (Engine Control Unit)
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine running issues, starting problems, immobilizer faults.
- **Solution:** ECU repair or replacement and reprogramming.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### Window Regulators
**Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not working or operating slowly.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator motor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Exterior

### Bodywork
**Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion on wheel arches, sills, and door bottoms.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and repair, panel replacement if severe.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000+ (depending on severity)


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced light output, poor aesthetics.
- **Solution:** Polishing and sealing, or replacement of headlight units.
- **Cost:** $50 - $200


## Interior

### Climate Control Unit
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $50 - $150


### Dashboard Pixels
**LCD Display Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Faded or missing pixels on the central display (e.g., odometer, radio info).
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the LCD unit.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2003 Alfa Romeo 156 is a car that requires attentive ownership. While its engines, particularly the Twin Spark, can be prone to specific issues like cam belt failure if maintenance is neglected, and the diesel variants may face turbo or EGR problems, these are often well-documented and manageable with proactive care. The Selespeed transmission is a notable area for potential expense. Electrical gremlins and suspension wear are also common themes. Despite these potential reliability concerns, many owners find the driving experience and unique Italian charm make the 156 a rewarding car to own, provided they are prepared for potentially higher maintenance costs and are diligent with servicing.
