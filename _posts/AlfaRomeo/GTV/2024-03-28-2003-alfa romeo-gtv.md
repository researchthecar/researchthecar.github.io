**Introduction:**

The 2003 Alfa Romeo GTV is a stylish Italian sports car with a 3.2L V6 engine producing 240 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. Known for its sleek design and handling, it also has drawbacks like cramped interior space and limited reliability.

**Engine**

* **3.2L V6**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise on startup, reduced engine performance
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, engine overheating
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from under valve cover
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
        * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Gear Shift Linkage Issue:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loose or stiff gear shifting
        * Solution: Adjust or replace gear shift linkage
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise over bumps, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace front suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+ per strut
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking from rear suspension, reduced stability
    * Solution: Replace worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery repeatedly dies or lacks power
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair any faulty components causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the underlying issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Broken Taillight Assembly:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or missing taillight
    * Solution: Replace taillight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faded Headlights:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced light output from headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs or polish headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Bezel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or loose center console bezel
    * Solution: Replace center console bezel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Panel Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable climate control buttons or display
    * Solution: Replace or repair climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2003 Alfa Romeo GTV has some potential issues that can affect its reliability and performance. While some issues like timing chain tensioner failure can be costly to fix, others are relatively affordable to address. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues vary, and it's important to factor them into consideration when purchasing or maintaining this vehicle.
