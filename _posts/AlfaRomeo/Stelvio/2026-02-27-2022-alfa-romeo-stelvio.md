# 2022 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Alfa Romeo Stelvio offers engaging driving dynamics and distinctive Italian styling, making it an appealing option in the compact luxury SUV segment. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should review available owner reports concerning electrical features and safety recall notifications. While overall complaint volume remains relatively low, checking service records and verifying that safety recalls have been completed is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It serves as the standard powerplant across most trims and is generally paired with an all-wheel-drive system.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio trim, this 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 generates a robust 505 horsepower. It delivers thrilling track-ready performance, though buyers should expect higher maintenance complexity compared to the standard four-cylinder engine.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is utilized across the lineup, offering quick and smooth gear shifts. It complements both engine options well and generally maintains a solid reputation for operational refinement.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Rearview Camera

Electrical and camera systems generally operate as intended, though isolated owner reports indicate occasional display or sensor glitches.

**Rear Camera and Proximity Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that the rearview camera system stops functioning entirely, while front parking sensors intermittently trigger false warnings.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a dealership or qualified technician may be required to inspect camera modules, wiring harnesses, or software calibration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V667000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the radio, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Alfa Romeo Stelvio blends sporty handling with upscale luxury, though used car buyers should approach with normal due diligence. Ensure all open safety recalls are addressed by an authorized dealer prior to purchase, and thoroughly test all electronic features and driver-assistance cameras during a test drive.
