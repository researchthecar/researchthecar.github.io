# 2023 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Alfa Romeo Stelvio delivers engaging Italian styling and sharp handling to the compact luxury SUV segment. While it offers an exciting driving dynamic, prospective buyers should review available reliability data and owner experiences carefully when shopping in the used market.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This base turbocharged 4-cylinder engine produces around 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It balances performance with efficiency, though regular maintenance is critical for long-term reliability.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio trim, this Ferrari-derived twin-turbo V6 pushes out 505 horsepower. It transforms the Stelvio into a true performance machine but demands meticulous upkeep and higher running costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This responsive ZF 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options, delivering quick shifts and smooth power delivery across all trims.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V857000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V667000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the radio, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
