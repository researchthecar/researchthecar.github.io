# 2020 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Alfa Romeo Stelvio offers engaging driving dynamics and distinct Italian styling, but buyers should approach with careful inspection. While owner reports to NHTSA are relatively few for this model year, specific complaints point to potential electrical, powertrain, and interior control quirks that are worth keeping in mind during a used car test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It generally offers solid performance, though owners have occasionally reported unexpected check engine lights and stalling incidents.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6 (Quadrifoglio)

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio trim, this Ferrari-derived 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 pushes out an impressive 505 horsepower. It delivers thrilling sports-car performance, but prospective buyers should anticipate higher ongoing maintenance requirements.

### Warning Lights and Electronics

Electrical systems control many core vehicle functions, making diagnostic scans essential when evaluating a pre-owned model.

**Spurious Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** The check engine light and various unrelated warning indicators illuminate on the dashboard during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or specialist scan diagnostic trouble codes to isolate sensor faults or software glitches.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired across the lineup, the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, smooth shifts and responsive power delivery. Overall, the transmission mechanicals are robust, though cabin controls connected to it warrant inspection.

**Gear Shifter Button Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The plastic actuator button on the gear selector breaks off or pops out, and the internal button can get stuck, preventing the driver from shifting gears.
- **Solution:** Replace the gear shift assembly or broken actuator components.

## Safety

### Advanced Safety Systems

Safety technologies assist with driver awareness, though prospective owners should verify that all driver-assist features and restraint systems are fully functional.

**Lane Keeping and Airbag Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected system interventions associated with lane keeping assist during collisions, alongside instances where airbags failed to deploy as expected.
- **Solution:** Inspect safety system sensors and have the vehicle's supplemental restraint system professionally inspected if any prior damage is suspected.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V878000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Pressure Sensor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. A fuel leak also increases the risk of fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the fuel line sensor housing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V309000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Hard braking may cause the Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) Hydraulic Control Unit (HCU) rotor shaft to separate, a reduction of braking assist, and a loss of ABS. The loss of braking assistance and ABS functionality can reduce overall braking performance, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ABS HCU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V382000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An unexpected decrease in brake performance increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the brake system module (BSM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V943000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair, the brake booster connection, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V667000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the radio, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Alfa Romeo Stelvio rewards drivers with sharp handling and distinctive style, but used car buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to interior shifter durability and electronic sensor status to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
