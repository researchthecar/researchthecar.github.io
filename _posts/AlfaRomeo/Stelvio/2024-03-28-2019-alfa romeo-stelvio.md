The 2019 Alfa Romeo Stelvio is a luxury SUV powered by a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine with 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Stelvio is praised for its sleek design, agile handling, and responsive engine. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:** Carbon deposits accumulate on the intake valves, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel efficiency.
  * Solution: Replace the intake valves or clean them manually.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to slip and potentially damaging the engine.
  * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Failed fuel injectors can cause rough idling, misfiring, and poor fuel economy.
  * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Shudder:** A shuddering sensation may be felt when shifting gears, especially at low speeds.
  * Solution: Update the transmission control module software or replace the transmission solenoids.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Delayed Shifting:** The transmission may delay shifting gears, causing hesitation and sluggish performance.
  * Solution: Update the transmission control module software or replace the transmission valve body.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to a faulty alternator or parasitic electrical draw.
  * Solution: Replace the alternator or diagnose and repair the electrical system.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system may experience software glitches, causing freezes, crashes, and loss of functionality.
  * Solution: Update the infotainment system software.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture may accumulate inside the headlight assemblies, leading to poor illumination.
  * Solution: Replace the headlights with updated versions.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Rear Hatch Seal Leak:** Water may leak into the cargo area through a faulty rear hatch seal.
  * Solution: Replace the rear hatch seal.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Rear Seat Latch Failure:** The rear seat latches may fail, preventing the seat from being properly secured.
  * Solution: Replace the faulty rear seat latches.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing inadequate heating or cooling.
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty climate control components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Summary**

While the 2019 Alfa Romeo Stelvio offers a compelling combination of performance and style, it has been plagued by several issues in key areas such as the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. The cost to fix these issues can range from minor to significant, potentially impacting the overall ownership experience. Prospective buyers should carefully consider these issues before making a purchase decision.
