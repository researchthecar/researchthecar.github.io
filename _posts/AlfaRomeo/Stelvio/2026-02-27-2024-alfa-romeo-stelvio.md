# 2024 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Alfa Romeo Stelvio delivers engaging Italian styling and sharp handling to the luxury compact SUV segment. For prospective used-car buyers, reviewing its foundational powertrain options and monitoring owner-reported experiences help ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving while maintaining balanced efficiency for the luxury class.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio trim, this 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 generates an impressive 505 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers exhilarating sports-car performance at the expense of higher running and maintenance costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across the Stelvio lineup, delivering quick, refined gear shifts and reliable power delivery to either the rear wheels or all four wheels.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V667000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the radio, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
