**Introduction**

The 2020 Alfa Romeo Stelvio is a compact luxury SUV known for its sporty handling and premium Italian styling. It is available with a choice of two engines and two transmission options:

* Base 2.0L 4-cylinder turbocharged engine with 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque
* Quadrifoglio 2.9L V6 twin-turbocharged engine with 505 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque
* 8-speed automatic transmission for both engines

While the Stelvio has been praised for its performance and design, it has also been criticized for its reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L 4-cylinder Turbocharged**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive engine noise, especially at startup or acceleration; possible loss of power
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Cylinder Head Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace cylinder head
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.9L V6 Twin-Turbocharged**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, or delayed shifting
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, poor ride quality
    * Solution: Replace front suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, electrical components malfunctioning
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic drain; may require new battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, glitches, or software issues
    * Solution: Software update or module replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors fail to lock or unlock properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per actuator
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin from sunroof
    * Solution: Inspect and seal sunroof assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working properly or unevenly
    * Solution: Replace seat heater element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per seat
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Panoramic Sunroof Shade Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shade gets stuck or torn
    * Solution: Repair or replace panoramic sunroof shade
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Alfa Romeo Stelvio is a performance-oriented SUV with a number of potential issues that can be costly to repair. The most common issues reported include timing chain tensioner failure, cylinder head failure, and electrical problems. While the Stelvio offers a luxurious and sporty driving experience, its reliability concerns may be a deterrent for some potential buyers.
