# 2021 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2021 Alfa Romeo Stelvio, a stylish Italian compact luxury SUV, offers a compelling blend of performance and design. While generally well-received for its driving dynamics and upscale interior, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported concerns. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability issues, excluding routine maintenance and common wear-and-tear items. We will examine the performance and potential pitfalls of its powertrain options, electrical systems, and other key components to provide a comprehensive reliability overview for potential owners.

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, low oil level warnings, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals, gaskets, or oil pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (ZF)
**Hesitation/Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Rough shifting, delayed engagement, occasional jerking during gear changes.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and fill, or internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Alfa Romeo Stelvio presents a generally solid reliability profile, particularly in its suspension, exterior, and interior components, where no significant widespread issues are reported. The primary areas of concern revolve around the 2.0L turbocharged engine, with potential for oil leaks and turbocharger problems, and the 8-speed automatic transmission, which can occasionally exhibit shifting irregularities. Electrical systems, especially the infotainment unit and battery performance, have also seen some user-reported glitches. While not as statistically robust as some competitors, addressing these potential issues proactively can contribute to a more reliable ownership experience for this performance-oriented Italian SUV.
