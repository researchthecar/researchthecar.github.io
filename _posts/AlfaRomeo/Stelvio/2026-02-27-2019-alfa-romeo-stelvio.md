# 2019 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury compact SUV, the Alfa Romeo Stelvio offers striking Italian styling and engaging driving dynamics. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding fuel system concerns, electrical glitches, and unexpected mechanical behavior to make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The standard 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, paired with standard all-wheel drive. It offers brisk acceleration and competitive efficiency, though buyers should watch for reported fuel delivery and stalling concerns.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbo V6

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio trim, the 2.9-liter twin-turbocharged V6 pumps out an impressive 505 horsepower. It delivers thrilling sports-car performance for the SUV segment, but demands meticulous maintenance and thorough inspection before purchase.

### Fuel System

Components related to fuel delivery and engine management can occasionally experience performance hiccups that warrant attention.

**Fuel Pump Failure and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected engine stalling while driving at highway speeds or in parking lots, accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel pump and system integrity, and check for open manufacturer recalls.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unexpectedly shifts into neutral while operating, creating a loss of power and potential safety hazard.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized dealership to verify if software updates or recall service bulletins apply to your vehicle's VIN.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is widely praised for smooth, rapid shifts and well-spaced gear ratios. It pairs well with both engine options, though prospective buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure responsive engagement across all gears.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Battery System

Electrical architecture supports advanced cabin technology and vehicle controls, but sensitive electronics require careful diagnostic checks.

**Battery Drain and Key Fob Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report random alarm activations, key fobs failing to unlock doors, and complete vehicle no-start conditions due to electrical issues.
- **Solution:** Test the battery health, inspect charging systems, and check software modules for updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V309000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Hard braking may cause the Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) Hydraulic Control Unit (HCU) rotor shaft to separate, a reduction of braking assist, and a loss of ABS. The loss of braking assistance and ABS functionality can reduce overall braking performance, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ABS HCU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V635000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Hoses/Lines/Piping/Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of coolant can cause the engine to overheat and the vehicle to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the coolant hose, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V551000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may be operated without enough fuel in the tank causing a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo will notify owners, and dealers will update the BCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V148000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Adaptive Cruise Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The driver's inability to deactivate the ACC can increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo has notified owners, and dealers will update the BSM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V382000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An unexpected decrease in brake performance increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the brake system module (BSM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V943000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Brake pedal detachment can cause a loss of brake function and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair, the brake booster connection, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V586000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel delivery module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
