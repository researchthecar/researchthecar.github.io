# 2021 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Alfa Romeo Stelvio offers spirited driving dynamics and striking Italian styling, making it a standout in the compact luxury SUV segment. While driver reports are relatively limited, prospective used-car buyers should still review known powertrain configurations and monitor real-world owner feedback regarding electronics and cooling systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard powerplant across most of the lineup. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving, though owners should inspect service history carefully for any sporadic electronic or warning light issues.

**Check Engine Warning and 'Do Not Drive' Message**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported the check engine warning light unexpectedly illuminating while driving between 35 and 45 MPH, accompanied by a 'Do Not Drive' message on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic or dealership run diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint the underlying engine or sensor fault.

### 2.9L Twin-Turbocharged V6

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio trim, this Ferrari-derived 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 churns out an impressive 505 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance for driving enthusiasts, though it requires meticulous maintenance and premium upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired across all trims with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, this ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, smooth shifts and responsive gear selection. It is generally regarded as a robust unit in modern luxury applications, with few recurring owner complaints noted.

## Electrical

### Battery and Infotainment System

Electrical architecture complaints generally center around intermittent screen blackouts and charging stability.

**Infotainment Screen Shutdown and Charging Flaws**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced the main infotainment screen shutting down unexpectedly due to battery or charging inconsistencies, which can disrupt navigation, GPS, and other electronic displays.
- **Solution:** Have the battery, charging system, and software versions tested by a technician to ensure proper voltage output and module communication.

## Interior

### Heater Core and Climate Conduits

Cabin climate components are mostly reliable, though rare heater core leaks can introduce coolant vapors into the interior.

**Coolant Leak into Cabin Vents**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant may leak from the heater core into the air conduits, producing a heavy mist and smoke-like hazardous vapors inside the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the heater core for leaks and replace the faulty core assembly along with flushing the climate ventilation system if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V878000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Pressure Sensor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. A fuel leak also increases the risk of fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the fuel line sensor housing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V309000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Hard braking may cause the Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) Hydraulic Control Unit (HCU) rotor shaft to separate, a reduction of braking assist, and a loss of ABS. The loss of braking assistance and ABS functionality can reduce overall braking performance, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ABS HCU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V221000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An improperly crimped clamp may allow the side curtain air bag cushion and inflator to separate, causing an incomplete deployment or preventing the deployment of the air bag. An incomplete deployment or non-deployment of the side curtain air bag increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the side air bag inflatable curtain assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V512000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Incorrect tire information can result in under-inflated tires or installing the incorrect-size tires, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V510000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A seat belt buckle switch sensor may be improperly connected, preventing the front seat air bag from deploying as intended. A front seat air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will repair the connection, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V667000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the radio, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Alfa Romeo Stelvio rewards drivers with engaging handling and distinctive luxury, but buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to potential electrical quirks, battery health, and engine warning indicators to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
