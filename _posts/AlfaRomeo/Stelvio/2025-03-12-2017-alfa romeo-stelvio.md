The 2017 Alfa Romeo Stelvio marked the model's debut, introducing Italian style and performance to the compact SUV segment. A popular configuration features a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the Stelvio's engaging driving dynamics, distinctive styling, and powerful engine. Criticisms commonly revolve around reliability concerns, electrical glitches, and higher maintenance costs compared to its competitors.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged I4**

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported higher than normal oil consumption, requiring frequent top-offs between oil changes. Symptoms can include the low oil level warning light illuminating, and a noticeable decrease in oil level on the dipstick.
    *   Solution: There isn't a single definitive fix. Some dealers have performed oil consumption tests. The issue may be addressed by updated PCV valve, revised oil seals, or in more severe cases, engine replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies greatly. Oil consumption tests are often free. PCV valve replacements could cost $100 - $300. Oil seal repair $1,000+, Engine replacement $5,000 - $10,000+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Turbocharger Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, unusual noises (whining, whistling) from the engine, and potentially the check engine light illuminating. The turbocharger may fail due to oil starvation, internal component failure, or wastegate issues.
    *   Solution: Turbocharger replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**ZF 8HP50 8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rough or jerky shifting, particularly during low-speed maneuvers. Hesitation or delayed engagement when shifting between gears can also occur.
    *   Solution: Software updates to the transmission control module (TCM) have been known to fix this issue. In severe cases, valve body replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software updates may be covered under warranty or cost $100-$200. Valve body replacement can cost $1,500-$3,000+.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Premature Bushing Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, especially when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces. This can indicate worn bushings in the control arms or sway bar links.
    *   Solution: Replacement of worn bushings or entire control arms/sway bar links.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Bushing replacement $200 - $500 per corner. Control arm/sway bar link replacement $300 - $800 per corner.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can experience freezing, lagging, screen blackouts, or random reboots. Connectivity issues with Bluetooth or Apple CarPlay/Android Auto are also common complaints.
    *   Solution: Software updates are usually the first step. In some cases, replacement of the head unit may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software updates may be free or cost $100-$200. Head unit replacement can cost $800-$2000+.
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Various sensors (oxygen sensors, wheel speed sensors, etc.) can fail, leading to check engine lights and other warning messages.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of the faulty sensor and replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500+ per sensor, depending on location and type.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Door Handle Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Exterior door handles can become loose or fail to operate properly.
    *   Solution: Door handle replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$600+ per door handle.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof malfunctions, including failure to open or close completely, sticking, or unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Sunroof repair or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Alfa Romeo Stelvio, while praised for its driving dynamics and styling, has potential reliability concerns. Engine issues like excessive oil consumption and turbocharger failures can be costly. Electrical glitches, particularly with the infotainment system, are also common complaints. Suspension and interior issues are less prevalent but should be considered. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in the potential for higher maintenance costs compared to other vehicles in its class. The severity of issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potentially major repairs costing thousands of dollars.
