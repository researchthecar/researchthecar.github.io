# 2018 Alfa Romeo Stelvio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Alfa Romeo Stelvio offers engaging handling and distinctive Italian styling, but potential used-car buyers should approach with caution due to a notable history of electrical, fuel system, and stalling concerns. While it delivers strong performance and driving dynamics, owners frequently report complex electronic glitches and drivetrain hiccups that require careful pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This standard 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 280 horsepower and 306 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It forms the core powertrain for the vast majority of Stelvio models on the used market.

### 2.7L Twin-Turbocharged V6 (Quadrifoglio)

Exclusive to the high-performance Quadrifoglio trim, this Ferrari-derived 2.9-liter twin-turbo V6 pumps out an impressive 505 horsepower. It delivers supercar performance but demands strict maintenance adherence and potentially higher upkeep costs.

### Fuel System & Stalling

Issues surrounding fuel delivery and high-pressure pumps can lead to sudden loss of power or engine stalls, presenting safety concerns on busy roadways.

**Fuel Pump Failure and Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling, loss of motive power while driving, and illuminated check engine or warning lights.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing of the fuel delivery system and potential replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump or related components.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with an all-wheel-drive system across most trims, this ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and responsive shifts. It is generally robust mechanically, though occasional electronic integration issues can affect overall powertrain refinement.

## Brakes

### Brake System & ABS Module

Brake and lighting control modules can occasionally exhibit faults, sometimes exacerbated by moisture intrusion near the body control module.

**ABS Module Malfunction and Lighting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** ABS warnings, compromised braking efficiency, or unexpected failure of critical exterior lighting components like headlights and brake lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect the ABS module and body control modules for water intrusion or failure, and replace faulty lighting assemblies or harnesses.

## Exterior

### Body Harness and Ground Straps

electrical issues are among the most commonly reported headaches for early Stelvio models, frequently traced to grounding issues or battery drains.

**Ground Strap Corrosion and Electrical Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Battery drains, melting ground straps, intermittent accessory failures, and various warning lights appearing on the dash.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace damaged ground straps, wiring harnesses, or the battery as needed to restore proper electrical continuity.

### Panoramic Sunroof

A subset of owners have experienced unexpected structural glass breakage on panoramic sunroof models during regular highway driving.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof shatters unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by a loud pop and falling glass.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and clear all glass debris from the interior cabin and track mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V635000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Hoses/Lines/Piping/Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A loss of coolant can cause the engine to overheat and the vehicle to stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the coolant hose, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V636000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** These engines may misfire causing the catalytic converter to overheat, possibly resulting in damage to the nearby wiring and engine components. Heat damage to the engine compartment wiring or components can increase the risk of a fire or it can cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles for diagnostic codes, and update the engine control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V551000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The vehicle may be operated without enough fuel in the tank causing a stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo will notify owners, and dealers will update the BCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V148000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Adaptive Cruise Control](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The driver's inability to deactivate the ACC can increase the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo has notified owners, and dealers will update the BSM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V823000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A reduction in braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Alfa Romeo will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the braking system, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V205000: Equipment:Electrical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Electrical malfunctions such as windshield wipers that may not work in the rain and lights that may not work at night increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will install additional sealing protection to prevent water from entering the body control module and its connectors. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V203000: Structure:Body:Hatchback/Liftgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Corrosion at the connectors may cause the liftgate to unintentionally open while moving at speeds of less than 3 miles per hour, allowing cargo to fall out of the vehicle, creating a road hazard and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will install additional protection against water leaking into the liftgate wiring connectors and switch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V147000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Alfa+Romeo&model=Stelvio&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 104, "Windshield Wiping and Washing Systems." Limited wiper function can reduce the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the wiper motor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Alfa Romeo Stelvio rewards drivers with sharp handling, brisk turbocharged performance, and distinct Italian flair, but prospective buyers should proceed with eyes wide open. Persistent owner complaints regarding fuel pump failures, electrical issues, and ground strap corrosion mean a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable specialist is essential. Look for well-documented service history to ensure all campaign updates and preventative repairs have been completed.
