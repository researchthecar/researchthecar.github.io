# 2007 Alfa Romeo GT Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2007 Alfa Romeo GT, a stylish Italian coupe, offers a compelling blend of performance and design. While generally appreciated for its aesthetics and driving dynamics, like many vehicles of its era, it has specific areas that warrant attention regarding long-term reliability. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential mechanical and electrical concerns. Owners can use this data to anticipate and address potential problems, ensuring a more dependable ownership experience. The GT was available with a range of potent engines and manual transmissions, each with its own set of potential reliability considerations.

## Engine

### 1.9L JTDm Diesel
**Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated DPF warning light, reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption.
- **Solution:** Forced regeneration, DPF cleaning, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, black smoke from exhaust, loss of power, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 2.4L JTDm Diesel

## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual (M32)
**Bearing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Whining or grinding noises from the gearbox, especially at speed or when accelerating/decelerating.
- **Solution:** Gearbox rebuild with upgraded bearings or replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000


### 6-Speed Manual
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** N/A
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** Varies


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Wishbone Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Knocking or clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn wishbone bushings or complete wishbone assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Rear Spring Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, potential for broken springs.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear springs.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning lights, power windows, central locking, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Window Regulators
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, or unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Exterior

### Body Panels & Paint
**Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion typically found on wheel arches, sills, and around the rear window.
- **Solution:** Rust removal and repair, repainting.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced light output, aesthetic degradation.
- **Solution:** Polishing and sealing, or replacement of headlight units.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400


## Interior

### Climate Control Unit
**Faulty Actuators/Fans**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent temperature control, air not blowing from vents, unusual noises from dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty actuators or blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Seat Bolster Wear
**Wear and Tear**
- **Symptoms:** Visible wear and tear on the fabric or leather of the driver's seat bolster due to ingress/egress.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat covers.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Alfa Romeo GT presents a mixed reliability profile. Diesel variants are more prone to issues related to the DPF and EGR systems, common in many modern diesels. Manual transmissions, particularly the M32 gearbox, can suffer from bearing wear. Suspension components like wishbone bushings and rear springs are also noted areas for potential attention. Electrical gremlins, often linked to the BCM or window regulators, can occur. Exterior rust, especially in common areas, and interior wear on seats are also points to consider. Despite these potential issues, many problems are addressable with proper maintenance and timely repairs, allowing owners to enjoy the GT's distinctive character.
