# 2007 Alfa Romeo Spider

### Overview
The 2007 Alfa Romeo Spider, a stylish Italian roadster, offers a compelling blend of performance and design. While generally appreciated for its aesthetics and driving dynamics, like many European vehicles of its era, it can present specific reliability concerns. Owners often report a mixed bag of experiences, with some enjoying trouble-free ownership while others encounter issues, particularly with certain electronic components and powertrain elements. Regular maintenance is crucial for this model to mitigate potential problems and ensure longevity. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights for potential buyers and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 2.2L JTS (185 hp)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 3.2L JTS V6 (260 hp)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $350 - $700

**Thermostat Housing Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the thermostat housing and gasket.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Gearbox Grinding**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty engaging gears, grinding noise when shifting.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of internal gearbox components or synchronizers.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


### Selespeed (Automated Manual)
**Hydraulic Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or non-responsive gear changes, error messages on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Selespeed hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000

**Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear selection, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the Selespeed actuator.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Shock Absorber Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, visible fluid leaks on shock absorbers.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical components malfunctioning randomly (e.g., lights, windows, central locking).
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


### Window Regulators
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow or jerky movement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen displays errors, freezes, or becomes unresponsive.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Exterior

### Soft Top Mechanism
**Motor/Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power convertible top fails to operate, slow operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the convertible top motor or hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1300

**Leaking Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Water ingress into the cabin when raining.
- **Solution:** Replacement of door and window seals.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


### Headlight Condensation
**Moisture Buildup**
- **Symptoms:** Condensation visible inside headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Sealing of headlight units or replacement if severe.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Interior

### Airbag Warning Light
**Sensor/Wiring Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag warning light remains illuminated on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty airbag sensors or wiring harness.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### HVAC Blower Motor
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from the vents, or intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Alfa Romeo Spider is a car that rewards passionate drivers but requires attentive ownership. While the engines and transmissions can be robust when properly maintained, specific issues like oil leaks and clutch wear are not uncommon. The electrical system, particularly the Body Control Module and infotainment, can be a source of frustration with intermittent faults. The convertible top mechanism also presents potential repair costs. Owners should be prepared for potentially higher maintenance and repair costs compared to more mainstream brands, but for those who appreciate its unique character and driving experience, it can be a rewarding choice. Regular servicing and prompt attention to any warning lights are highly recommended.
