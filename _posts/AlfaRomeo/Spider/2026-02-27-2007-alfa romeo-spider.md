# 2007 Alfa Romeo Spider Reliability Guide

### Overview
The 2007 Alfa Romeo Spider, a stylish Italian roadster, offers a compelling blend of performance and design. While generally appreciated for its driving dynamics and aesthetics, like many European vehicles of its era, it can present specific reliability concerns. Owners often praise its engaging handling and distinctive styling. However, potential buyers should be aware of common issues that may arise, particularly concerning the powertrain and certain electronic components. Regular maintenance is crucial for mitigating these potential problems and ensuring the longevity of this sporty convertible. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related issues.

## Engine

### 2.2L JTS (185 hp)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive oil consumption between scheduled changes, potentially leading to low oil levels and engine damage.
- **Solution:** Regularly check oil levels and top up as needed. In some cases, piston ring replacement may be required.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup; check engine light may illuminate.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 3.2L JTS V6 (260 hp)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Similar to the 2.2L, excessive oil consumption can occur.
- **Solution:** Regular oil level checks and top-ups. Potential for more extensive engine work if neglected.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Actuators**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, loss of power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of VVT solenoids and actuators.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1400


### 6-Speed Automatic (Q-Tronic)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid, rough shifting, potential overheating.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets; transmission fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Wishbone Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn wishbone bushings or complete wishbone assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, lights, central locking, or dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Parking Sensors
**Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts or failure to detect obstacles.
- **Solution:** Cleaning sensors, checking wiring, or replacing faulty sensor units.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


## Exterior

### Convertible Roof Mechanism
**Hydraulic Leaks/Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or inoperative roof operation, fluid leaks under the car.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, pump, or cylinders.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1800


## Interior

### Climate Control Unit
**Faulty Actuators**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or airflow direction.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blend door actuators.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Alfa Romeo Spider is a desirable sports car with a distinct personality. While it offers an engaging driving experience, potential owners should be prepared for a higher likelihood of certain mechanical and electrical issues compared to more mainstream brands. Common concerns include engine oil consumption, timing chain wear on the 2.2L, and potential issues with the automatic transmission. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the Body Control Module and convertible roof mechanism, are also noted. Proactive maintenance and addressing issues promptly are key to enjoying this Italian roadster. It's recommended to have a pre-purchase inspection by a specialist familiar with Alfas.
