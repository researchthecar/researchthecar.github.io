**Introduction:**

The 1996 Mercury Sable is a mid-size sedan offered with a range of engine and transmission options. It was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive performance, but it also had its share of reliability concerns.

**Engine:**

* **3.0L V6**
    * **Upper intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace upper intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $900+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **EGR valve failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, rough idle, reduced power
        * Solution: Replace EGR valve
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.8L V6**
    * **Ignition coil failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, engine stalling, poor fuel economy
        * Solution: Replace ignition coil
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil pump failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of oil pressure, engine noises, engine failure
        * Solution: Replace oil pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slow or delayed gear shifts, lack of response
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises, slipping
        * Solution: Replace clutch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front strut failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, reduced ride quality, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear shock failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, reduced ride quality, rear-end bouncing
    * Solution: Replace rear shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, starting problems, battery drain
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the vehicle, flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Headlight switch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to turn on headlights, intermittent headlight operation
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exterior door handle failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening doors from the outside
    * Solution: Replace exterior door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Power window failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to roll up or down windows
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Heater core failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No heat from heater, sweet smell in cabin
    * Solution: Replace heater core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1996 Mercury Sable had several reliability issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The estimated cost to fix these issues can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to over $3,000. However, the Sable also offered a spacious and comfortable cabin, making it a popular choice for families.
