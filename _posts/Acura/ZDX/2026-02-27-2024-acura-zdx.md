# 2024 Acura ZDX

### Overview
The 2024 Acura ZDX represents a significant step into the electric vehicle market for Acura, built on GM's Ultium platform. As a first-year model, comprehensive long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, initial assessments and the established nature of the underlying platform suggest a generally robust performance. Early indications point towards strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain and core electrical systems, leveraging proven EV technology. Potential areas for monitoring, as with any new model, include software integration and specific electronic components. Overall, the ZDX is positioned to offer a dependable electric driving experience, building on Acura's reputation for quality.
