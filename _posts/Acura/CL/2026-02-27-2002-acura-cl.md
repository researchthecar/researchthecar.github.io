# 2002 Acura CL

### Overview
The 2002 Acura CL, a sporty coupe, aimed to blend performance with Acura's reputation for reliability. While generally well-regarded, like any vehicle of its age, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding potential issues. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into the mechanical and electrical components that may require attention. Understanding these potential weak points can help owners budget for and address repairs proactively, ensuring the longevity and enjoyment of their 2002 CL.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Inline-4 (Type S)
**Timing Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine shutdown, loss of power, potential for severe internal engine damage if belt breaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing belt, tensioner, and idler pulleys. Inspect water pump for leaks.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking seals (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket, crankshaft seal).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


### 3.2L V6 (Base)
**Timing Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine shutdown, loss of power, potential for severe internal engine damage if belt breaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing belt, tensioner, and idler pulleys. Inspect water pump for leaks.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Automatic Transmission Issues (Torque Converter)**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the automatic transmission, often involving torque converter replacement.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic
**Torque Converter Shudder/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vibration or shuddering, especially at lower speeds or during acceleration; potential for slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter, sometimes requiring transmission fluid flush or transmission overhaul.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Solenoid Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed gear engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty shift solenoids.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 5-Speed Manual
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** N/A
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** Varies


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Ball Joints and Tie Rod Ends**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints and tie rod ends.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Premature Strut/Shock Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, excessive body roll, fluid leaks from struts/shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn struts and/or shocks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Gremlins**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows malfunctioning, interior lights acting erratically, door locks not working, dashboard warning lights illuminating without cause.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the Body Control Module.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Paint and Clear Coat
**Clear Coat Peeling/Fading**
- **Symptoms:** Visible peeling or chalky appearance of the paint, especially on the roof, hood, and trunk.
- **Solution:** Professional repainting or touch-up work.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced headlight visibility, hazy or yellowed appearance of the plastic lenses.
- **Solution:** Headlight restoration kit or replacement of headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $50 - $300


## Interior

### HVAC Blower Motor
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or air only coming from certain vents, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Leather Seats
**Cracking and Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Visible cracks, tears, or excessive wear on leather seating surfaces, particularly on the driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Leather repair kits or professional upholstery repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800+


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2002 Acura CL offers a generally reliable driving experience, especially with the manual transmission. However, owners should be aware of potential issues common to vehicles of this era, particularly concerning the timing belt (critical for engine longevity), automatic transmission torque converter, and certain electrical components like the Body Control Module. Exterior paint and interior wear are also common cosmetic concerns. Proactive maintenance, especially timely timing belt replacement, and addressing early signs of transmission trouble can significantly mitigate repair costs and ensure the continued enjoyment of this sporty coupe.
