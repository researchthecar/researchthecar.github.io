# 2023 Acura Integra

### Overview
The 2023 Acura Integra represents a return to form for the beloved nameplate, offering a blend of sporty performance, premium features, and Acura's renowned reliability. Built on a proven platform shared with the Honda Civic Si, the Integra benefits from extensive engineering and testing. Early data suggests a strong start for the model year, with owners generally reporting positive experiences. The focus on driver engagement, coupled with thoughtful interior design and modern technology, makes the Integra a compelling option in the compact luxury sedan segment. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the 2023 Integra appears to be off to a solid start in terms of dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Acura Integra is demonstrating strong initial reliability, aligning with Acura's reputation for dependable vehicles. The powertrain, utilizing the well-regarded 2.0L turbocharged engine and either a CVT or manual transmission, has shown no significant widespread issues. Suspension and exterior components are also performing well. The primary area with minor reported concerns is the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the Integra offers a reliable and enjoyable ownership experience for its segment, with minimal expected long-term mechanical concerns beyond standard maintenance.
