### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Acura Integra demonstrates strong initial reliability, aligning with Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. The powertrain, including both the 2.0L turbocharged engine and its associated CVT or manual transmission options, appears robust with no significant widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally holding up well. The most commonly noted, albeit minor, concerns revolve around the infotainment system, which can occasionally exhibit software glitches requiring updates or resets. Overall, the Integra is shaping up to be a dependable choice in its segment, with potential buyers able to expect a reliable ownership experience, provided they address any minor electronic quirks that may arise.
