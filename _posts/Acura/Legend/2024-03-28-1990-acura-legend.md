**1990 Acura Legend**

The 1990 Acura Legend is a mid-size luxury sedan offered with two engine and transmission options:

* 2.5L V6 with 4-speed automatic transmission (162 hp, 164 lb-ft)
* 3.2L V6 with 4-speed automatic transmission (200 hp, 206 lb-ft)


**Engine**
**2.5L V6**
* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.2L V6**
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leaks, rough idle, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**4-speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to shift smoothly, delayed or erratic shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged struts, reduced ride comfort, handling issues
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per corner

* **Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or deteriorated bushings, rattling noises, steering vibrations
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per side

**Electrical**
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system failure, battery discharge, warning lights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

* **Power Window Regulator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative power windows, clicking noises
    * Solution: Replace power window regulator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per window

**Exterior**
* **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks from sunroof, water damage to interior
    * Solution: Repair or replace sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**
* **Climate Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative climate control, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+


**Summary**

The 1990 Acura Legend is known for its reliability and luxury features. However, it does have some common issues to watch out for, including cylinder head gasket failure, intake manifold gasket failure, and electrical issues. The severity and cost of these repairs vary, but they can be costly if not addressed promptly.
