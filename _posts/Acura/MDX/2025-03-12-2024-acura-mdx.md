The 2024 Acura MDX is a popular luxury SUV known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and upscale features. It typically receives positive reviews for its build quality and reliability. The standard engine is a 3.5-liter V6 producing 290 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. There is also an Acura MDX Type S trim level with a turbocharged 3.0-liter V6 engine, producing 355 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. Some owners appreciate the MDX's blend of practicality and luxury, while others may find the infotainment system to be somewhat complex or the styling to be too conservative.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

*   **Engine Oil Dilution**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report an increasing oil level on the dipstick and a gasoline smell in the oil. This can be caused by excessive short trips. It could also be an issue with the piston rings or fuel injectors.
    *   Solution: Changing driving habits to include longer trips, replacing the fuel injectors or piston rings. If the oil level rises, changing the oil to prevent potential engine damage.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150 (Oil Change). $800 - $1500+ (Fuel Injectors/Piston Rings)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Turbocharged V6**
    *No Issues Found*

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh or Jerky Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing harsh or jerky shifting, particularly between lower gears. This may be more noticeable during acceleration or deceleration.
    *   Solution: A software update to the transmission control module (TCM). In some cases, replacement of valve body is needed.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 (Software Update). $800 - $1200+ (Valve Body Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*No Issues Found*

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system, including the navigation, audio, and climate controls, may occasionally freeze, crash, or become unresponsive.
    *   Solution: Restarting the system (sometimes requires turning the vehicle off and on), software updates from Acura, or in rare cases, replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Restart or Software Update). $500-$1500+ (Infotainment Unit Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Wireless Apple CarPlay/Android Auto Connectivity Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty connecting to or maintaining a stable connection with Apple CarPlay or Android Auto wirelessly.
    *   Solution: Ensuring the phone's software is up-to-date, resetting the infotainment system, or having the dealership check for software compatibility issues.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software updates/reset). (Unknown cost for compatibility issues)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*No Issues Found*

**Interior**

*   **Rattling Noises from Dashboard/Trim**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported experiencing rattling noises emanating from the dashboard or other interior trim panels while driving, especially on uneven road surfaces.
    *   Solution: Identifying the source of the rattle and securing the loose panel or component, which may involve adding padding or tightening fasteners.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50-$300+ (depending on the complexity of the repair)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2024 Acura MDX appears to have relatively few widespread, serious issues. The most frequently reported issues relate to the infotainment system's reliability and occasional transmission concerns. Engine issues are minimal. Repair costs seem to vary significantly, with software fixes being inexpensive and more extensive repairs potentially costing several hundreds or thousands of dollars. It's important to note that the information presented here is based on currently available data and may not reflect the experience of all owners. Prospective buyers should always perform their own due diligence and consider getting a pre-purchase inspection from a trusted mechanic.
