# 2017 Acura MDX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Acura MDX is a popular midsize luxury crossover known for its spacious interior, family-friendly utility, and comfortable ride. However, used-car buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain performance and electronics, as owners have reported recurring concerns regarding engine driveability, transmission shifting anomalies, and sensitive driver-assistance warnings.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 290 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth acceleration for daily driving. While generally durable, a subset of owners have experienced unexpected stalling and power loss incidents.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden engine stalls while driving at various speeds, accompanied by unexpected losses of motive power and illumination of warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel system, electronic throttle body, and diagnostic trouble codes to determine the root cause of the stall.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with the V6 engine to optimize highway fuel economy. Some drivers, however, report occasional rough shifting behavior and unexpected gear changes.

**Unintended Neutral Shift and Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift into neutral while driving at highway speeds or hesitate and shudder when transitioning between lower gears.
- **Solution:** Check for available manufacturer software updates or consult a transmission specialist for diagnostic evaluation of valve body and shift control modules.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power for a heavy crossover, though routine maintenance is essential to prevent rotor warping and judder.

**Brake Pedal Vibration and Stopping Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a distinct vibration or judder in the front brakes when applying stopping pressure, as well as occasional delayed deceleration from low speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake rotors for warping, check brake pads for wear, and bleed the hydraulic brake lines if air is suspected.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical components generally perform reliably, though exterior lighting housings can occasionally experience seal failures leading to moisture buildup.

**Tail Light Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Water enters the inner rear tail light assemblies, causing internal condensation and eventual electrical failure of the lighting elements.
- **Solution:** Replace affected tail light assemblies and ensure proper sealing to prevent future moisture intrusion.

### Roof Structure Paint

Exterior body panels hold up well overall, but specific sections near the roof glass and sunroof frame are prone to cosmetic paint delamination.

**Paint Peeling and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** The paint on the roof panel between the sunroof and front windshield begins to bubble, peel, and flake off, potentially exposing metal to rust.
- **Solution:** Consult a professional auto body shop for repounding, sealing, and repainting affected roof sections.

## Safety

### AcuraWatch Driver Assist System

The safety suite includes forward collision warnings and lane keeping aids designed to protect occupants. Owners note that sensitivity issues and sensor glitches can cause temporary system dropouts.

**Radar Sensor and CMBS Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights for lane keep assist, collision mitigation braking, and adaptive cruise control illuminate intermittently, temporarily disabling safety features.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera and radar sensors for obstructions, verify windshield alignment after replacements, and check software calibration settings.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V060000: Fuel System, Other:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Reduced fuel pump performance can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will update the fuel injection engine control unit (FI-ECU) software and, if necessary, replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V256000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Loss of the taillights reduces the vehicle's visibility, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Acura will notify owners, and dealers will modify the tailgate lid lights and install updated gaskets and a wiring sub-harness or will replace both tailgate lid lights. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V777000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." A reduction of braking performance increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Owners will be notified, and Honda or Acura dealers will bleed the brakes, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V751000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A damaged engine may run improperly or stall while driving, increasing the risk of a fire, crash, or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair, or replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V259000: Exterior Lighting](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Vehicle lights that fail to illuminate can decrease visibility and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the wiring and install a fuse harness, replace the lid light, and repair the body dust sealer, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V365000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=MDX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Failure of rear suspension components, such as the rear control arm, can cause a loss of vehicle handling and control, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the rear subframe and install a rear subframe reinforcement kit and, as necessary, repair or replace the rear subframe components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Acura MDX offers compelling family luxury and refined road manners, making it an attractive secondhand option. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough test drive to verify smooth transmission shifts, ensure all driver-assistance systems operate without warning lights, and inspect the vehicle for any signs of mechanical or cosmetic neglect.
