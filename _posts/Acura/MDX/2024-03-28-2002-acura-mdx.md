**Introduction**

The 2002 Acura MDX is a midsize luxury SUV known for its comfort, practicality, and performance. It was offered with a 3.5L V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The MDX received positive reviews for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and advanced features. However, it also had some common issues that affected certain aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
* **Valve Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tapping noise from engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace valve springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, slipping gears, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Torque Converter Lockup Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration during gear changes
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noises, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises, reduced cornering stability
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunction
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't crank, clicking noise
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Seal Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking sunroof, water damage to interior
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from dashboard area
    * Solution: Inspect and tighten loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
* **Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate navigation, frozen screen
    * Solution: Update or replace navigation system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Window Regulator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window doesn't roll up or down, clicking noise
    * Solution: Replace window regulator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary**

The 2002 Acura MDX is a solid SUV with some common issues to be aware of. The most severe and costly issue is cylinder head gasket failure, which can require a major engine repair. Transmission problems, suspension issues, and electrical failures can also be costly. However, many of the other issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the MDX is a reliable and practical vehicle for everyday use.
