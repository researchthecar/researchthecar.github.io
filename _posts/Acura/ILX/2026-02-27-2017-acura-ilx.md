# 2017 Acura ILX

### Overview
The 2017 Acura ILX is a compact luxury sedan that offers a blend of sporty styling and premium features. It is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly for its powertrain. However, like any car, it has had some reported issues that owners should be aware of. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical problems, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear. The ILX primarily came with a single engine and transmission configuration, simplifying the data analysis for these core components. Overall, the ILX presents a solid option for those seeking an entry-level luxury sedan with a good track record for dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Acura ILX demonstrates strong reliability in its core mechanical components, with its 2.4L engine and 8-speed dual-clutch transmission generally free from widespread issues. Owners have reported some electrical concerns, primarily related to infotainment system glitches and occasional premature battery failure. Exterior issues like clear coat peeling and headlight condensation have also been noted. Interior problems are less common, though AC compressor issues can arise. Overall, the ILX is a dependable choice, but potential buyers should be aware of the possibility of minor electrical and cosmetic repairs.
