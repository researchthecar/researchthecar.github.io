# 2020 Acura ILX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Acura ILX offers buyers a generally dependable compact sedan option with straightforward powertrain configurations. While owner-reported complaints are relatively few, prospective buyers should review specific system concerns noted in driver feedback to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder

Equipped with a 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine generating approximately 201 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant is generally regarded as durable and reliable for daily driving needs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Features an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive, providing responsive gear changes and solid overall mechanical reliability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system performance is typically robust, with only isolated complaints concerning pedal firmness.

**Brake Pedal Softness**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice the brake pedal gradually sinking toward the floor while holding pressure at a stoplight.
- **Solution:** Inspection of the brake master cylinder and hydraulic system for pressure loss or leaks.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical system complaints are sparse, though isolated electronic module glitches have been reported by some owners.

**Smart Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported random alarm triggering at all hours, headlights failing to turn off, and dashboard controls shorting out.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of the faulty smart integration unit or body control module.

## Exterior

### Rear Window Glass

Exterior visibility components are generally sturdy, though rare instances of spontaneous rear glass breakage have been documented.

**Spontaneous Rear Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear windshield has been reported to burst or shatter unexpectedly, sometimes while the rear defroster is active in cold weather.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the rear window glass panel.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=ILX&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V859000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Master Cylinder](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=ILX&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Contaminated brake fluid may cause a seal inside the brake master cylinder to deform, which can cause an internal brake fluid leak and reduce brake function. Reduced brake function can extend the distance required to stop, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake master cylinder, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Acura ILX proves to be a dependable choice for used car buyers seeking a practical and efficient compact sedan. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential electrical glitches, rear glass breakage, and brake pedal feel, the vehicle's overall track record suggests solid everyday reliability.
