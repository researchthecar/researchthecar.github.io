### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Acura ILX is a reliable choice within its segment, with the powertrain and core mechanical components demonstrating strong durability. The most frequently reported issues are minor and typically relate to the infotainment system's software or occasional battery drain, which are not uncommon for modern vehicles.
