### Overview
The 2015 Acura ILX, a compact luxury sedan, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting Acura's reputation for dependable vehicles. ## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Bluetooth Connectivity Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent connection drops, inability to pair devices, audio stuttering.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment system, or in some cases, replacement of the module.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the engine, dimming headlights, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Acura ILX is generally a reliable vehicle with few significant mechanical or electrical issues reported by owners. The powertrain, suspension, and exterior components are largely trouble-free. The most commonly cited concerns revolve around the infotainment system's Bluetooth connectivity and, less frequently, premature battery failure. Some instances of AC compressor issues have also been noted. Overall, the ILX offers a dependable ownership experience, with potential repairs typically being manageable in cost and scope, aligning with Acura's reputation for quality.
