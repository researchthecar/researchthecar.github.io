# 2021 Acura ILX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Acura ILX combines sharp styling and a nimble front-wheel-drive platform, making it an appealing option for buyers seeking an entry-level luxury sedan. While it generally boasts solid mechanical underpinnings, owner feedback submitted to safety regulators points to a few isolated electronic, lighting, and powertrain hiccups worth keeping in mind during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder

The 2021 Acura ILX features a naturally aspirated 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine producing around 201 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant is generally regarded as durable and provides spirited performance for daily commuting.

**Unintended Acceleration / Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may continue to accelerate or experience unexpected throttle behavior accompanied by clicking noises during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic throttle body, accelerator pedal sensor, and powertrain control module inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The transmission is an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic paired exclusively with front-wheel drive. It offers quick shifts and engaging performance, though software updates may occasionally be needed to ensure smooth operation.

**Unexpected Deceleration and Shifting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly decelerate without warning, fail to shift into higher gears causing high engine revs, and trigger multiple dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Scan transmission control modules for diagnostic trouble codes and check transmission fluid condition and software calibration.

## Suspension

### Suspension and Driveshaft

The suspension delivers a firm, sporty ride quality, though individual component wear should be checked prior to purchase.

**Premature Component Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual noises or structural failure detected within the suspension or driveshaft components during normal driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Perform a thorough undercarriage and suspension inspection on a lift to identify worn bushings, joints, or fractured components.

## Exterior

### Headlight Assembly

Owner reports highlight occasional moisture sealing vulnerabilities in exterior lighting fixtures.

**Water Intrusion and Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture and standing water collect inside the front headlight housings, potentially leading to short circuits and failure of lighting components.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seals and replace compromised headlight assemblies if water damage has affected the internal electronics.

### Windshield Glass

Visibility components generally perform well, though rare manufacturing defects in glass optics have been noted.

**Optical Distortion**
- **Symptoms:** Road lines and external objects appear curved or distorted when viewed through certain sections of the windshield.
- **Solution:** Have the windshield inspected by an auto glass specialist; replacement may be required if optical imperfections impair visibility.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Acura ILX is a stylish and generally dependable compact sedan, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the condition of the headlight housings for moisture intrusion, verify proper transmission shifting behavior, and check the front suspension and steering components to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
