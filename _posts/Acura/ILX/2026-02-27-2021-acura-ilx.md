# 2021 Acura ILX

### Overview
The 2021 Acura ILX, while a competent entry-level luxury sedan, exhibits a mixed reliability profile. It generally offers a solid ownership experience, particularly in its powertrain, which has proven to be quite robust. However, like many vehicles in its class, it's not entirely immune to issues, with some owners reporting concerns primarily related to the infotainment system and occasional minor electrical glitches. The build quality is generally good, contributing to a positive interior experience for most users. Suspension components have also held up well under typical driving conditions. Overall, the ILX is a dependable choice, but potential buyers should be aware of the specific areas where minor issues have been noted.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Acura ILX is a generally reliable vehicle, especially concerning its powertrain, which benefits from proven Honda engineering. The 2.4L engine and 8-speed DCT are known for their durability. While the suspension and exterior components are typically trouble-free, some owners have encountered issues with the infotainment system, ranging from minor software glitches to more significant unresponsiveness. Premature battery failure has also been noted by a small percentage of owners. Despite these minor electrical concerns, the ILX offers a dependable and comfortable driving experience for its segment, with most common repairs being relatively affordable.
