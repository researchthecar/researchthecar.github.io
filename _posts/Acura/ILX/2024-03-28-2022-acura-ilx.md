**2022 Acura ILX**

The 2022 Acura ILX is a compact luxury sedan powered by a 2.4-liter inline-four engine that produces 201 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque paired with an eight-speed dual-clutch transmission. The ILX is praised for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and high-tech features. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as its lackluster acceleration and cramped rear seats.

    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing the temperature to fluctuate or the fan to stop working.

    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Sunroof**

**Summary**

The 2022 Acura ILX is a reliable vehicle with minimal reported issues. The most common problems are minor interior issues, such as climate control malfunctions and loose trim. While these issues are not severe or costly to fix, they can be annoying to deal with. Overall, the ILX is a solid choice for those looking for a compact luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and feature-rich experience.
