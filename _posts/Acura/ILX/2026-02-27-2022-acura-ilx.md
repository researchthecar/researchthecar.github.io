# 2022 Acura ILX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2022 Acura ILX gives buyers access to a compact sport sedan known for its straightforward powertrain and comfortable interior. Because this model year sees very few widespread owner complaints registered, it generally presents a clean slate on the secondhand market. Prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall condition, focusing particularly on cosmetic exterior finishes and electronics.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 201 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque, pairing smooth power delivery with Honda's historically strong reliability reputation. It serves as the sole engine option for this model year, offering daily drivers dependable performance without complex turbocharger maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

An 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission with a torque converter transfers power to the front wheels, delivering quick shifts and responsive acceleration. This transmission option is standard across all trims and is generally robust when subjected to regular fluid maintenance.

## Electrical

### Keyless Ignition and Security

Electrical and security systems operate reliably, though used buyers should always ensure all key fobs are accounted for at purchase.

**Key Fob and Access Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Some drivers have reported suspicion regarding spare key handling or unusual vehicle behavior attributed to electronic access.
- **Solution:** Verify that all key fobs are accounted for and consider reprogramming or replacing keys through a certified dealer if security is a concern.

## Exterior

### Paint

Exterior body components and paint finishes generally hold up well, though isolated cosmetic flaws can occur.

**Paint Chipping**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature paint chipping in isolated spots on the vehicle exterior shortly after purchase.
- **Solution:** Inspect the body panels carefully during a test drive and consult a professional detailer or body shop for touch-ups or protective film if chips are found.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Acura ILX stands out as a dependable and low-stress choice for used-car shoppers seeking a practical daily driver with sporty styling. Given the sparse volume of reported owner issues, standard pre-purchase checks for cosmetic paint wear and proper key fob programming are typically all that is required. Buyers who value a proven powertrain and straightforward features will find this sedan to be a sensible garage addition.
