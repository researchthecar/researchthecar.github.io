# 2018 Acura ILX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Acura ILX, buyers will find a stylish and generally dependable entry-luxury sedan that shares its mechanical roots with Honda. While owner feedback points to a relatively quiet reliability record overall, some drivers have logged complaints concerning fuel system operation and engine stalling. Reviewing service records and verifying that any open campaign work has been completed can help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder

The 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 201 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. Paired with a direct-injection layout, this powerplant is generally known for strong long-term durability when properly maintained.

### Fuel System

Owner reports highlight occasional fuel delivery hiccups and unexpected warning lights, though these remain isolated relative to overall sales volume.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Pump Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalls, erratic fuel gauge readings, or sudden loss of power while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel pump assembly and electrical connections, and ensure all manufacturer campaigns are addressed.

**Electrical Warning Lights and Drivability Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained dashboard warning lights illuminating alongside unusual mechanical vibrations or steering feedback concerns.
- **Solution:** Perform a comprehensive diagnostic scan to check for stored trouble codes within the powertrain control modules and related wiring.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission features a torque converter for smooth low-speed operation combined with rapid gear changes. It delivers a responsive driving dynamic and requires regular fluid changes to maintain optimal shift quality over time.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V052000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=ILX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel gauge displays that the vehicle has more gas than it actually does, the vehicle may run out of gas and stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Acura will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, as necessary, replace the fuel tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V424000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=ILX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A separated driveshaft can cause the vehicle to have a loss of drive or allow the vehicle to move even while in Park, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Acura will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and, as necessary, replace the driveshaft and half shaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=ILX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V859000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Master Cylinder](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=ILX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Contaminated brake fluid may cause a seal inside the brake master cylinder to deform, which can cause an internal brake fluid leak and reduce brake function. Reduced brake function can extend the distance required to stop, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake master cylinder, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Acura ILX offers a compelling mix of refined performance and everyday usability for used-car shoppers. While minor complaints exist regarding the fuel system and unexpected stalling incidents, the vehicle maintains a solid foundation. Prospective buyers should inspect service history, check for pending safety campaigns, and perform a thorough test drive before purchasing.
