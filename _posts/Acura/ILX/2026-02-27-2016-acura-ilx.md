# 2016 Acura ILX

### Overview
The 2016 Acura ILX, a compact luxury sedan, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. It offers a refined driving experience with a focus on comfort and technology. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen occasional reports of issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Some drivers have reported occasional hesitation or jerky shifts, particularly at lower speeds or during initial acceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module are often the first step. In more severe cases, internal transmission inspection and repair may be necessary.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional reports of the infotainment screen freezing, becoming unresponsive, or requiring a reset.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the infotainment system are typically available. In rare cases, the unit may need to be replaced.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have experienced unexpected battery drain, leading to starting issues. This can sometimes be linked to parasitic draw from certain modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the parasitic draw is crucial. This might involve checking modules, wiring, or aftermarket accessories. Battery replacement may be needed if the original is compromised.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Acura ILX is generally considered a reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The powertrain, including the 2.4L engine and 8-speed DCT, is robust, though some minor transmission hesitation has been noted. Electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, are the most frequently reported issues, but these are often resolvable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread problems. Overall, the ILX offers a dependable ownership experience for its segment, with potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures.
