# 2003 Acura RSX

### Overview
The 2003 Acura RSX, a popular compact sports coupe, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially for its era. It was offered with two distinct engine and transmission configurations, catering to different performance preferences. While the RSX is known for its engaging driving dynamics and sporty appeal, like any vehicle, it has certain areas that owners have reported issues with over time. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical concerns, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of its long-term dependability. The data presented aims to assist potential buyers and current owners in understanding potential reliability aspects.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L K20A3 (Base Model)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $150 - $350

**VTEC System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power at higher RPMs, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of solenoid, filter, or related wiring.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 2.0L K20A2 (Type-S Model)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Low oil level between changes, blue smoke from exhaust under acceleration.
- **Solution:** Can sometimes be managed with specific oil, but may require piston ring replacement in severe cases.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000+

**Timing Chain Tensioner**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine, especially on startup.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and potentially the chain and guides.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual (Base Model)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Synchronizer Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding when shifting into certain gears, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Internal transmission repair or rebuild, replacement of synchronizer rings.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500+


### 6-Speed Manual (Type-S Model)
**Shifter Cable Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loose shifter feel, difficulty engaging gears, sloppy shifting.
- **Solution:** Adjustment or replacement of shifter cables.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


### 5-Speed Automatic (Optional on Base)
**Transmission Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed engagement, harsh shifting, loss of power.
- **Solution:** Fluid and filter change, potential internal repairs or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Ball Joints/Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints and suspension bushings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Rear Wheel Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Humming or grinding noise from the rear that increases with speed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the rear wheel bearing assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500


## Exterior

### Standard Exterior
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Standard Interior
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $50 - $150

**Seat Fabric Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Tearing or thinning of seat fabric, especially on driver's side bolster.
- **Solution:** Seat cover replacement or professional upholstery repair.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2003 Acura RSX is a solid choice for a sporty and reliable compact car, particularly if well-maintained. The K-series engines are generally robust, though some oil consumption or timing chain tensioner issues can arise on higher-mileage examples, especially the Type-S. Manual transmissions are typically durable, but clutch wear is a common maintenance item. Automatic transmissions are less common and can be more prone to issues. Suspension components like bushings and bearings are typical wear items for a car of this age. Electrical gremlins are infrequent but can include window motors or alternators. Overall, the RSX offers a good balance of performance and dependability, with most potential issues being manageable and relatively affordable to address.
