# 2018 Acura NSX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Acura NSX is an exotic hybrid supercar that blends everyday drivability with track-ready performance. As a high-performance modern vehicle, prospective used buyers should evaluate its advanced powertrain and specialized systems carefully during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6 Hybrid

A 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine paired with a three-electric-motor hybrid system, producing an estimated 573 horsepower and 476 lb-ft of torque. It offers blistering acceleration and sophisticated thermal management, though specialized maintenance requires trained technicians.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission featuring an integrated direct-drive electric motor. It delivers rapid, seamless gear shifts suited for both relaxed cruising and aggressive driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V600000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel tanks, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Acura NSX is a technologically advanced hybrid supercar that generally maintains strong build quality. Buyers should ensure all hybrid and powertrain systems are thoroughly inspected by a qualified specialist before purchase.
