# 2022 Acura NSX

### Overview
The 2022 Acura NSX represents the pinnacle of hybrid supercar performance, combining a potent twin-turbocharged V6 engine with a sophisticated three-motor Sport Hybrid Super Handling All-Wheel Drive system. This advanced powertrain is engineered for both exhilarating performance and remarkable efficiency. As a low-volume, high-performance vehicle, the NSX is built with precision and utilizes cutting-edge technology. While generally reliable, like any complex machine, specific areas can occasionally present challenges. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and enthusiasts.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6 Hybrid
**Hybrid System Battery Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric-only range, decreased overall power output, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Battery pack replacement.
- **Cost:** $8,000 - $15,000

**Turbocharger Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of boost pressure, reduced engine power, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty turbocharger actuator or associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


## Transmission

### 9-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, delayed gear engagement, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Acura NSX, with its advanced hybrid powertrain and sophisticated engineering, generally exhibits strong reliability for a supercar. The primary documented concerns revolve around the longevity of the hybrid battery pack and potential issues with turbocharger actuators, which can be costly to repair. Transmission control module glitches are also noted, though often resolvable with software updates. Other systems like suspension, electrical components, exterior elements, and the interior show a high degree of reliability with no widespread issues reported. Owners should be aware of the potential for significant repair costs associated with the hybrid system and performance-oriented engine components.
