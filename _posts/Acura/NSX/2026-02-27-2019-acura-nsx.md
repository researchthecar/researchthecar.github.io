# 2019 Acura NSX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Acura NSX is a high-performance hybrid supercar that blends everyday drivability with advanced twin-turbocharged engineering. As a low-volume exotic sports car, it generally sees specialized care from owners, resulting in very few reported common owner complaints in public databases. Prospective buyers looking at a used 2019 model should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the complex hybrid powertrain and electronics.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6 Hybrid

The 2019 Acura NSX features a 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine paired with a three-electric-motor hybrid system, producing a combined 573 horsepower. This sophisticated powerplant delivers blistering performance while maintaining impressive composure during daily driving.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

Power is routed to the all-wheel-drive system via a 9-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission with paddle shifters. The gearbox provides lightning-fast shifts suited for both track use and relaxed cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Acura NSX stands out as a remarkably reliable exotic supercar choice for used-car buyers, backed by Honda's engineering pedigree and low complaint volumes. While mechanical issues are rare, prospective owners should ensure that routine maintenance and specialized hybrid system checks are up to date before finalizing a purchase.
