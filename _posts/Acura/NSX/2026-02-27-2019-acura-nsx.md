# 2019 Acura NSX

### Overview
The 2019 Acura NSX represents a pinnacle of hybrid supercar engineering, blending a twin-turbocharged V6 engine with a sophisticated three-electric-motor Sport Hybrid Super Handling All-Wheel Drive system. This advanced powertrain is designed for both exhilarating performance and remarkable efficiency. As a low-volume, high-performance vehicle, the NSX is built with precision and utilizes cutting-edge technology. While generally considered reliable for its class, the complexity of its hybrid system and high-performance components means that any potential issues, though infrequent, can be specialized and require expert attention. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Acura NSX demonstrates strong technical reliability, particularly considering its complex hybrid powertrain and high-performance nature. Major mechanical failures in the engine, transmission, and suspension are not widely reported, indicating robust engineering and manufacturing. The primary area where minor, infrequent issues have been noted is within the electrical system, specifically occasional glitches with the infotainment interface. These are typically resolved with software updates or resets and do not represent systemic failures. Overall, the NSX is a reliable supercar, with its advanced technology contributing to a generally positive ownership experience regarding technical dependability.
