# 2017 Acura NSX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Acura NSX is a technologically advanced hybrid supercar featuring a twin-turbocharged V6 paired with electric motors and a dual-clutch transmission. While widely praised for its everyday drivability and performance, used buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding fuel system components and auxiliary equipment.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L Twin-Turbocharged V6 Hybrid

The 2017 Acura NSX utilizes a 3.5-liter twin-turbocharged V6 combined with a three-motor Sport Hybrid SH-AWD system, generating a combined 573 horsepower. This complex powertrain delivers exhilarating performance, though buyers should verify fuel system health.

**Fuel Pump Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving on the highway due to fuel pump module failure.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump module through a qualified service center.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

Power is sent to the wheels via a responsive 9-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission integrated with a direct-drive electric motor. It provides lightning-fast shifts suited for both spirited driving and daily commuting.

## Brakes

### Carbon Ceramic Rotors

High-performance stopping hardware requires careful inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Rotor Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have noted cracking on the carbon fiber brake rotors under normal operating conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake rotors regularly for signs of thermal stress or cracking and replace as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The complex electrical architecture requires a healthy battery to support its many onboard convenience and hybrid control systems.

**Battery and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dead batteries can result in unexpected electrical power loss and difficulty operating electronic door latches.
- **Solution:** Test the 12V battery and charging system regularly to prevent unexpected electronic glitches.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V599000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Road vibrations may cause the metal screws that secure the printed circuit board for the high mounted center brake light to loosen and contact the circuitry, possibly resulting in the brake light fuse blowing and the brake lights not functioning. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS).
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the center high mount brake light, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V600000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the left and right fuel tanks, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=NSX&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Acura NSX is an impressive hybrid supercar that blends exotic performance with everyday usability. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the fuel system, carbon ceramic brakes, and electrical components to ensure everything operates smoothly.
