# 2020 Acura NSX

### Overview
The 2020 Acura NSX represents a pinnacle of hybrid supercar engineering, blending a potent twin-turbocharged V6 engine with a sophisticated three-motor Sport Hybrid Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) system. This advanced powertrain is designed for both exhilarating performance and remarkable efficiency. Reliability for the 2020 model year is generally strong, reflecting Acura's commitment to quality and durability in its halo sports car. Owners typically report a high degree of satisfaction with the vehicle's mechanical and electrical systems, with most issues being minor or related to specific user experiences rather than widespread manufacturing defects. The NSX's complex nature means that while major failures are rare, specialized knowledge and parts may be required for any necessary repairs.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Acura NSX demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with its premium sports car positioning. Major mechanical and powertrain failures are exceptionally rare. The most commonly cited, albeit minor, issues tend to be related to the infotainment system's software, which can usually be resolved with updates. The complex hybrid system and advanced drivetrain have proven robust. Owners should expect excellent long-term durability, provided regular maintenance is performed by qualified technicians. The NSX is a well-built machine where significant reliability concerns are not a common theme among owners.
