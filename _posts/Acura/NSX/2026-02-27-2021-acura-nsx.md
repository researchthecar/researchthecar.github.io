# 2021 Acura NSX

### Overview
The 2021 Acura NSX represents a pinnacle of hybrid supercar engineering, blending a potent twin-turbocharged V6 engine with a sophisticated Sport Hybrid Super Handling All-Wheel Drive system. This advanced powertrain is designed for both exhilarating performance and remarkable efficiency. Reliability for the 2021 model year generally reflects Acura's commitment to quality, with a focus on the complex hybrid components and the high-performance nature of the vehicle. While the NSX is a low-volume exotic, its engineering is derived from proven Acura and Honda technologies, contributing to a generally robust ownership experience. Owners can expect a vehicle that requires diligent maintenance due to its complexity, but major mechanical failures are not widespread.
