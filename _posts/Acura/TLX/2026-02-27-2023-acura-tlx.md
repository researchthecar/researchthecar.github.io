# 2023 Acura TLX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Acura TLX offers a compelling mix of sharp styling and engaging performance, though buyers should review owner-reported concerns closely. While many drivers enjoy its dynamic handling and premium cabin, early feedback highlights a few notable areas of owner dissatisfaction ranging from electrical glitches to brake and security vulnerabilities. As with any used luxury sport sedan, a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains vital.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four produces 272 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard engine for most TLX trims. It offers a responsive, peppy daily driving experience with solid reliability expectations.

### 3.0L Turbocharged V6

Reserved for the high-performance Type S, this 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 generates 355 horsepower and 354 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust acceleration and pairs exclusively with Acura's Super Handling All-Wheel Drive system.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic

The 10-speed automatic transmission comes standard across all 2023 TLX models, shifting smoothly to balance fuel efficiency and power delivery. It is generally regarded as a durable unit with few widespread mechanical complaints.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system feedback centers on occasional premature wear and unusual pedal feedback, which require physical inspection during a test drive.

**Brake Rotor Wear and Inconsistent Pedal Feel**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted premature brake rotor warping causing vibrations, alongside an inconsistent or sticky brake pedal feel accompanied by unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect brake pads, rotors, and hydraulic lines, replacing components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Anti-Theft and Tracking Systems

Electrical system reports highlight concerns regarding vehicle security, anti-theft tracking reliability, and occasional infotainment connectivity issues.

**Vulnerability to Theft and Tracking Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that anti-theft tracking features failed, car alarms did not sound, and vehicles were driven away despite security measures due to computer cloning vulnerabilities.
- **Solution:** Consult an authorized dealership or security specialist for available software updates or additional physical security enhancements.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V950000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel tank assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V391000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment" and 135, "Light Vehicle Brake Systems." A brake pedal that shifts out of position can prevent the driver from applying the brakes as intended, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the brake pedal assembly as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Acura TLX is a stylish and sporty option in the used luxury sedan market, but potential buyers should exercise care. Pay special attention to brake condition and verify the operational status of all electrical and security features during your test drive. Conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help ensure you find a reliable, well-maintained example.
