# 2022 Acura TLX

### Overview
The 2022 Acura TLX is a luxury sport sedan that generally offers a high level of reliability, reflecting Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. It is equipped with sophisticated technology and performance-oriented powertrains, including a potent 2.0L turbocharged engine and an available 3.0L turbocharged V6 for the Type S model. The ZF-sourced 10-speed automatic transmission is also a key component. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any complex vehicle, some specific areas have seen isolated reports. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for the 2022 TLX.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Acura TLX demonstrates strong overall reliability, aligning with Acura's reputation. The powertrain, including both the 2.0L turbo-four and the 3.0L turbo-V6 (Type S), along with the 10-speed automatic transmission, has shown no significant widespread mechanical failures. Suspension components are also generally robust. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning occasional infotainment system glitches and, in some instances, premature battery drain. These are not typically indicative of systemic powertrain or chassis weaknesses. For potential owners, addressing software updates for the infotainment and monitoring battery health are prudent steps. Overall, the TLX is a dependable luxury sedan with a solid track record for its model year.
