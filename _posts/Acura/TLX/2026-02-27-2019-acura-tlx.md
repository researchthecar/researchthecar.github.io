# 2019 Acura TLX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Acura TLX offers a compelling blend of luxury, technology, and sporty driving dynamics, making it an attractive option on the used market. Buyers have access to two capable engine choices paired with refined transmission options. While many owners enjoy trouble-free motoring, used car shoppers should review service histories carefully, as some models have accumulated complaints regarding electrical quirks, fuel system components, and occasional powertrain behavior.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder

A naturally aspirated 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine producing approximately 206 horsepower, paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. It provides brisk acceleration and efficient everyday performance for daily commuting.

### 3.5L V6

A robust 3.5-liter V6 engine generating around 290 horsepower, paired with a smooth 9-speed automatic transmission. It delivers strong passing power and is frequently paired with Acura's Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) system.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

An 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission featuring a torque converter for smooth low-speed takeoffs, standard on 4-cylinder front-wheel-drive models.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed conventional automatic transmission paired with V6 models, designed to optimize highway fuel economy and power delivery.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift into neutral while driving or fail to restart after the auto idle stop/start function engages.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission control modules, software calibrations, and mechanical linkages for faults.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Parking Brake

Braking hardware and electronic parking brake actuators designed for secure stopping power.

**Electronic Parking Brake and Emissions Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warning messages illuminate unexpectedly for the electronic parking brake and emissions systems.
- **Solution:** Scan diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint faulty sensors, software glitches, or wiring harness concerns.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Electrical architecture managing vehicle accessories, starting systems, and battery health.

**Battery Drain Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start intermittently due to unexpected parasitic electrical draw draining the battery.
- **Solution:** Test battery health, check for parasitic draws from accessory modules, and replace components as necessary.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Lighting systems encompassing headlights, taillights, and associated control modules.

**Headlight Lens Stress Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight lenses develop internal crazing or stress cracks from the inside without external impacts.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected headlight assemblies to restore proper light output and appearance.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Driver assistance suite utilizing radar and camera sensors to monitor traffic and enhance occupant safety.

**Front-Facing Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Internal failure of the front windshield camera triggers warning lights and disables features like automatic emergency braking and lane keeping assist.
- **Solution:** Inspect diagnostic trouble codes and replace the front-facing camera assembly if internal hardware failure is confirmed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V215000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V060000: Fuel System, Other:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Reduced fuel pump performance can result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will update the fuel injection engine control unit (FI-ECU) software and, if necessary, replace the fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V298000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Belts And Associated Pulleys](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Teeth separation from the timing belt may result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicle and replace any affected timing belt free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V751000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A damaged engine may run improperly or stall while driving, increasing the risk of a fire, crash, or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair, or replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=TLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Acura TLX is generally a dependable pre-owned luxury sedan backed by robust engine options and comfortable ride quality. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive the vehicle to verify smooth transmission operation and check all driver assistance technologies, lighting assemblies, and electrical accessories for proper function. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection can help uncover any lingering maintenance or electrical needs before finalizing a purchase.
