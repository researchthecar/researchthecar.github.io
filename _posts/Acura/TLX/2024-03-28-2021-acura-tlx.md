**2021 Acura TLX**

The 2021 Acura TLX is a luxury sedan that offers a combination of style, performance, and practicality. It comes with three engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four, a 2.4-liter naturally aspirated inline-four paired with two electric motors, and a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 paired with three electric motors. The transmission options are a 10-speed automatic for the 2.0-liter and 3.0-liter engines and an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic for the 2.4-liter engine.

Critics praise the TLX for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and powerful engines. However, some complain about its lack of interior space and expensive options.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning excessive amounts of oil, causing low oil levels and potential engine damage
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4-liter Naturally Aspirated Inline-Four + 2 Electric Motors**

* **Hybrid System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check Hybrid System warning light illuminates, reduced power, or engine stalling
    * Solution: Software update or hybrid system component replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter Turbocharged V6 + 3 Electric Motors**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, or loud noises
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation, slipping, or rough shifting
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Clutch Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes
    * Solution: Clutch replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Squeaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise coming from the rear suspension when driving over uneven surfaces
    * Solution: Lubricating or replacing the rear suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, flickering lights, or dead battery
    * Solution: Battery replacement or electrical system repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air conditioning or heating system, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Refrigerant recharge, climate control system repair, or software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow response times, freezing, or crashing
    * Solution: Software update or infotainment system replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buildup of moisture inside the headlight housing
    * Solution: Headlight replacement or resealing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2021 Acura TLX offers many appealing features, it is not immune to certain issues. Engine and transmission problems, such as excessive oil consumption and turbocharger failure, can be costly to repair. However, most issues are minor and can be resolved without major expenses. Overall, the TLX remains a reliable and impressive vehicle for those seeking a blend of performance and luxury.
