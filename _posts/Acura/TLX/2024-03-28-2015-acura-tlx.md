**Introduction:**

The 2015 Acura TLX is a mid-size luxury sedan that comes with two engine options: a 2.4L 4-cylinder producing 206 horsepower and 182 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.5L V6 producing 290 horsepower and 267 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT). The TLX has received praise for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling, but it has also been criticized for its cramped rear seat and lack of standard driver-assist features.

**Engine**

* **2.4L 4-Cylinder**
    * **Timing Chain Rattle:**
        * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the engine bay when starting or revving the engine.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.5L V6**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed DCT**
    * **Transmission Hesitation and Jerking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or jerks when shifting gears, especially during acceleration and deceleration.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software and/or replace the transmission control module (TCM).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Excessive Body Roll:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle leans excessively during cornering or sudden lane changes.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bars or install aftermarket suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or identify and fix the source of the drain (e.g., parasitic draw from accessories).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the fog lights.
    * Solution: Replace the fog lights or add silica gel packs to absorb moisture.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof makes a rattling noise when open or closed.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof tracks or weatherstripping.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Blind Spot Monitoring Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The blind spot monitoring system does not alert the driver to vehicles in the blind spots.
    * Solution: Replace the radar sensors or update the software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Acura TLX has a relatively low number of reported issues. However, some of the issues, such as the transmission hesitation and battery drain, can be significant and costly to repair. While the TLX offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience, potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
