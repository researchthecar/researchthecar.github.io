This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers of the 2015 model year.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Acura RLX demonstrates a strong overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain components, which are generally robust. The primary areas of concern tend to be within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit experiencing software glitches and occasional premature 12V battery failures. These issues, while not pervasive, can be inconvenient and incur repair costs. Beyond these electrical quirks, the RLX offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal widespread mechanical problems reported for its engine, transmission, suspension, exterior, and interior.
