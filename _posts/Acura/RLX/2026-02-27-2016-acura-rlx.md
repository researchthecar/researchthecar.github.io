# 2016 Acura RLX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Acura RLX, buyers are looking at a sophisticated flagship luxury sedan known for its spacious interior and advanced technology features. While overall owner-reported complaints are relatively sparse, evaluating powertrain options and minor electrical or mechanical quirks can help ensure a smooth ownership experience. Prospective buyers should inspect steering and electrical functions thoroughly during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Direct-Injection

The 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 310 horsepower and 272 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and responsive acceleration. This direct-injection powerplant generally maintains a strong reputation for durability and long-term reliability under proper maintenance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the front-wheel-drive layout, offering predictable gear shifts. It is generally a dependable unit, provided regular fluid changes are maintained.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (Sport Hybrid)

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission is integrated into the Sport Hybrid model alongside an electric motor system to optimize efficiency and performance. Buyers should test drive hybrid variants meticulously to ensure seamless clutch engagement and electric transition.

## Steering

### Rear Wheel Steering System

The precision all-wheel steer system enhances agility but relies heavily on complex electronic control modules.

**Rear Wheel Alignment and Response**
- **Symptoms:** During tight maneuvers or after sharp turns in parking lots, the computer-controlled rear wheels may fail to respond accurately to driver input, temporarily sticking in an improper angle.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the four-wheel steering computer module, actuators, and software calibration to ensure proper alignment and response.

## Exterior

### Trunk Latch and Release Mechanism

Cabin and exterior electronics generally hold up well, though occasional sensor or latch glitches can occur.

**Trunk Unlocking Independently**
- **Symptoms:** After manually closing the trunk, the latch may unexpectedly unlock by itself, leaving the cargo area unsecured.
- **Solution:** Inspect the trunk latch assembly, actuator, and electronic switch for failure, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag Deployment

Safety equipment is comprehensive, but prospective buyers should always verify collision history and airbag readiness.

**Inconsistent Supplemental Restraint Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** In certain moderate to severe frontal impacts, specific supplemental restraints such as knee airbags may deploy while steering wheel modules fail to trigger as expected.
- **Solution:** Ensure that the vehicle has no history of unresolved airbag recalls and have the SRS diagnostic system scanned for stored fault codes.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Acura RLX offers a compelling mix of luxury, performance, and advanced technology for used-car shoppers. While isolated owner reports point toward potential complexities with advanced steering electronics and minor electrical accessories, major powertrain mechanical failures are uncommon. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, steering responsiveness, and maintenance history will help secure a reliable luxury sedan.
