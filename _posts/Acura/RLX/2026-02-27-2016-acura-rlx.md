# 2016 Acura RLX

### Overview
The 2016 Acura RLX is a luxury sedan that aimed to blend performance with advanced technology. It was offered with a sophisticated hybrid powertrain and a standard V6 engine, providing a comfortable and refined driving experience. As a luxury vehicle, the RLX typically features premium materials and a host of electronic amenities. Reliability for the 2016 model year is generally considered good, with owners reporting fewer significant mechanical issues compared to some competitors. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from owners and technicians, particularly concerning its complex hybrid system and certain electronic features.

**Hybrid System Malfunctions**

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes, or reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Acura RLX generally exhibits good reliability, particularly with its standard V6 engine and transmission. The primary areas of concern tend to be associated with the more complex Sport Hybrid SH-AWD powertrain, where issues with the hybrid system components or the dual-clutch transmission can arise. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and keyless entry sensor problems, are also noted. Exterior issues like headlight condensation and interior concerns with the climate control system are less common but have been reported. Overall, the RLX is a dependable luxury sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of the specific complexities of the hybrid variant and its associated repair costs.
