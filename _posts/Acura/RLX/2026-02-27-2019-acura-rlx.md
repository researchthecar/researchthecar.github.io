# 2019 Acura RLX

### Overview
The 2019 Acura RLX is a luxury sedan known for its comfortable ride, advanced technology, and refined interior. As a flagship model, it generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. The RLX typically comes equipped with a potent V6 engine and a sophisticated hybrid powertrain in its Sport Hybrid SH-AWD variant, both designed for smooth performance and efficiency. Owners generally report a positive ownership experience with fewer significant mechanical issues compared to some competitors. However, like any vehicle, specific components can occasionally experience problems, though widespread, recurring failures are uncommon for this model year.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Acura RLX demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with Acura's brand promise. Major mechanical issues are rare, and most reported problems are minor and related to the advanced electronic systems, such as the infotainment. The powertrain, both the standard V6 and the hybrid variant, is generally robust. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its longevity. Overall, the RLX is a dependable luxury sedan with a strong track record, making it a solid choice for buyers prioritizing comfort and technology without significant reliability concerns.
