# 2019 Acura RLX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Acura RLX represents a sophisticated, low-volume luxury flagship offering advanced technology and robust engineering. While owner complaints in public databases are sparse due to its limited sales numbers, prospective buyers should still evaluate standard powertrain configurations and remain mindful of complex electronics and advanced driver-assistance systems typical of modern luxury sedans.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

This naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 310 horsepower and 272 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as highly durable and reliable under routine maintenance schedules.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

This hybrid powertrain pairs the 3.5-liter V6 with a three-motor Sport Hybrid SH-AWD system, generating a combined 377 horsepower. It offers enhanced performance and traction, though its intricate electrical components require specialized care.

## Transmission

### 10-speed automatic transmission

A smooth-shifting 10-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel-drive models. It provides refined gear selection and good highway efficiency.

### 7-speed dual-clutch transmission

A quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch transmission integrated directly with the Sport Hybrid electric motor system. It delivers responsive power delivery suited for enthusiast drivers.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V298000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Belts And Associated Pulleys](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Teeth separation from the timing belt may result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicle and replace any affected timing belt free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RLX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Acura RLX is a solid choice for used-car buyers seeking a comfortable, well-equipped luxury sedan without the usual European maintenance headaches. Because available owner data is extremely limited, shoppers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics, hybrid systems, and mechanical wear before finalizing a purchase.
