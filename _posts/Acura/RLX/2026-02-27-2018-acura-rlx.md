### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Acura RLX demonstrates strong overall reliability, consistent with Acura's reputation. The powertrain, suspension, and most interior/exterior components are robust and rarely present significant issues. The primary areas to monitor are the infotainment system, which can experience occasional software glitches, and the possibility of premature battery failure, a concern common across many modern vehicles. These are generally manageable issues with reasonable repair costs. For potential buyers, the RLX offers a dependable luxury sedan experience with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
