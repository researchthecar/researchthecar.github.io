# 2018 Acura RLX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Acura RLX represents a sophisticated, full-size luxury flagship known for its refined comfort and advanced technology. While generally holding a reputation for solid Japanese engineering, prospective buyers should review available owner reports concerning early electrical glitches and isolated engine concerns when evaluating a used model.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

This 3.5-liter naturally aspirated V6 engine produces around 310 horsepower and 272 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and confident acceleration. It is paired with a front-wheel-drive layout and offers a generally dependable mechanical baseline for everyday driving.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

This Sport Hybrid variant pairs a 3.5-liter V6 with a three-motor electric system to generate a combined 377 horsepower, channeling power through a Super Handling All-Wheel Drive system. It provides spirited performance alongside enhanced traction, though buyers should verify the health of the complex hybrid electronics.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic

This 10-speed automatic transmission comes mated to the standard V6 engine, offering responsive and smooth gear changes. Routine fluid maintenance is key to keeping this complex multi-speed unit shifting reliably over high mileage.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

This 7-speed dual-clutch transmission is integrated directly into the Sport Hybrid powertrain layout, featuring an integrated electric motor. It delivers quick, engaging shifts, though prospective buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement across all operating temperatures.

## Interior

### Dashboard and Starting Electronics

Owner feedback highlights occasional electrical system quirks, though widespread harness failures are uncommon.

**Cluster Warning Lights and Starting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle failed to start and multiple warning lights illuminated unexpectedly across the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the battery, charging system, and main electrical grounds for faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RLX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Acura RLX is a well-equipped luxury sedan that blends comfort with strong performance options. While isolated owner complaints point to potential electrical and starting anomalies, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
