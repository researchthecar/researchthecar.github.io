# 2020 Acura RLX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Acura RLX represents the final year of Acura's flagship luxury sedan, offering a blend of advanced technology and comfort. While generally regarded as a low-volume, well-engineered luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should review available owner reports and inspect key mechanical and electronic systems closely during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

This naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 engine produces approximately 310 horsepower and 272 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and reliable power delivery. It is a proven mechanical unit that forms a dependable foundation for the vehicle.

### 3.5L V6 Hybrid

Paired with a three-motor Sport Hybrid Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) system, this powertrain generates 377 total system horsepower. It delivers brisk performance alongside improved efficiency, though its complex electronics require thorough diagnostic checks.

**Unexpected Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience sudden, unintended acceleration at low speeds while navigating parking lots or side streets.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the electronic throttle body, throttle actuator, and powertrain control module for stored fault codes.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic

This 10-speed automatic transmission is paired with the standard front-wheel-drive V6 model, providing quick and seamless gear changes. Regular fluid changes are recommended to maintain long-term durability.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Integrated with the hybrid powertrain, this 7-speed dual-clutch transmission features an integrated electric motor to optimize power delivery. It generally offers crisp shifts, but proper maintenance is key to avoiding clutch wear.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electric power steering system provides responsive handling under normal conditions, though electronic faults can occasionally trigger steering resistance.

**Steering Wheel Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may suddenly become heavy or difficult to turn while driving, accompanied by multiple dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering motor, wiring harnesses, and control module connections for potential faults or power supply issues.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RLX&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Acura RLX is a sophisticated luxury sedan with limited production numbers, making well-maintained examples desirable for buyers seeking comfort and value. While isolated owner reports note rare electronic or powertrain anomalies, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help ensure the vehicle's complex hybrid and steering systems are in top condition.
