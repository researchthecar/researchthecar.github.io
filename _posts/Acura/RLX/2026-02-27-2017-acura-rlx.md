# 2017 Acura RLX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2017 Acura RLX means exploring a luxury sedan known for its comfort, high-end feature list, and advanced technology. While owner reports to federal regulators are relatively scarce for this model year, prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate individual vehicle histories, especially regarding potential mechanical and handling concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6

The 3.5-liter V6 engine delivers smooth power and refined performance typical of a luxury flagship sedan. While generally durable, isolated owner reports highlight severe internal component failures that require thorough pre-purchase mechanical inspections.

**Connecting Rod Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden and catastrophic engine failure while driving at speed, potentially accompanied by a loss of power and smoke or fire if hot oil contacts the exhaust.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection and potentially a complete engine replacement depending on the extent of internal damage.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes tailored to the luxury cruiser character of the vehicle. No widespread recurring defect trends have been heavily documented by owners for this gearbox.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Wheels

Brake and wheel assemblies require standard routine maintenance, with occasional owner reports of high-speed vibrations necessitating a balanced check.

**High-Speed Wheel Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may feel a severe vibration originating from the driver-side wheel area, which tends to become more pronounced at higher driving speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels for balance issues, tire wear anomalies, and brake rotor condition to locate the source of the vibration.

## Steering

### Front Suspension and Steering

Suspension components generally maintain ride comfort, though individual owner feedback notes potential noise and alignment quirks as vehicles age.

**Steering Pull and Creaking Noises**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may pull to one side while driving at various speeds, accompanied by creaking sounds when turning the steering wheel left or right.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the suspension bushings, steering components, and alignment settings to identify worn parts.
