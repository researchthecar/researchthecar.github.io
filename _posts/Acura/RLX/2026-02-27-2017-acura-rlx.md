# 2017 Acura RLX

### Overview
The 2017 Acura RLX is a luxury sedan known for its comfortable ride and advanced technology. As a premium offering from Acura, it generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting the brand's reputation for building durable vehicles. The RLX, particularly in its hybrid variants, showcases sophisticated engineering aimed at balancing performance with fuel efficiency. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any complex vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Acura RLX demonstrates a high level of reliability, consistent with Acura's brand promise. The powertrain, including the sophisticated hybrid system and its associated transmission, appears robust with no widespread reported issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally trouble-free. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit experiencing occasional glitches, and a possibility of premature battery failure. The interior, while generally well-built, can sometimes present minor climate control system malfunctions. Overall, the RLX is a dependable luxury sedan, with potential concerns being relatively minor and manageable.
