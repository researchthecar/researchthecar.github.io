# 2006 Acura TSX

### Overview
The 2006 Acura TSX, a compact executive car, is generally recognized for its sporty driving dynamics and premium features. Built on the Honda Accord platform, it inherits much of the reliability associated with Honda engineering. This generation of the TSX is known for its robust powertrain and well-built interior, making it a solid choice for those seeking a blend of performance and dependability. While no vehicle is entirely free from potential issues, the 2006 TSX has a reputation for being a reliable performer with a lower-than-average likelihood of major mechanical problems when properly maintained. Owners often praise its engaging handling and comfortable ride.

## Engine

### 2.4L K24A2 I4
**Oil Leaks (VTEC Solenoid/Valve Cover Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on engine components, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the VTEC solenoid gasket or valve cover gasket.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350

**Timing Chain Tensioner Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and potentially the chain and guides.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

## Suspension

### Standard Independent Suspension
**Worn Ball Joints/Tie Rod Ends**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints and/or tie rod ends.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Rear Shock Absorber Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, visible fluid leaks on shock bodies.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Power Issues/Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Random electrical gremlins, dashboard warning lights illuminating without apparent cause, issues with power windows or locks.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 Acura TSX is a reliable vehicle with a strong reputation, particularly for its powertrain. While common issues like oil leaks from the VTEC solenoid or valve cover gasket, and potential wear on the timing chain tensioner can occur, they are generally manageable. Suspension components like ball joints and tie rods may require attention with age and mileage, as can the alternator. Electrical gremlins related to the BCM are less common but can be complex to diagnose. Overall, the TSX offers a dependable ownership experience, with most reported issues being repairable and not indicative of systemic failure. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its continued reliability.
