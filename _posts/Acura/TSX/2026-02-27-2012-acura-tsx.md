# 2012 Acura TSX

### Overview
The 2012 Acura TSX is generally considered a reliable vehicle, building on Acura's reputation for quality and durability. It was offered with two distinct powertrain options, both known for their solid performance and longevity when properly maintained. The interior and exterior components are typically well-built, contributing to a positive ownership experience. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the TSX tends to avoid widespread, costly problems, making it a sensible choice for many buyers seeking a dependable luxury sedan. Owners generally report satisfaction with its driving dynamics and overall build quality.

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Acura TSX stands out as a highly reliable vehicle with a strong track record. Major mechanical failures are uncommon, and most reported issues are minor and easily addressed. Both the engine and transmission options are robust and designed for longevity. The electrical, suspension, exterior, and interior components also demonstrate good durability. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience with minimal unexpected repair costs, provided regular maintenance is performed. It remains a solid choice for those seeking a well-built and reliable luxury sedan.
