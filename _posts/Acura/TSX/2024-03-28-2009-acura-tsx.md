**Introduction:**

The 2009 Acura TSX is a compact sport sedan that offers a balance of performance and luxury. It is powered by a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine that generates 201 horsepower and 172 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission. The TSX is praised for its sharp handling, comfortable interior, and generous standard features. However, it has also been reported to have some issues in certain areas.

**Engine**

**2.4L Engine**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and failure of the timing chain tensioner, causing excessive noise, engine misfires, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Harsh Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or rough shifting, especially during downshifts or in cold weather.
    * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module (TCM) or replace the transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding Noises:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy grinding sound when shifting gears, particularly when shifting to 2nd or 3rd gear.
    * Solution: Replace the synchronizers or the entire transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clicking or clunking noise from the front suspension, especially when turning or going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the front strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle or a dead battery after sitting for a period of time.
    * Solution: Identify and fix the source of the battery drain, such as a faulty alternator, parasitic draw, or battery issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Panel Gap Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven or inconsistent gaps between the body panels, affecting aesthetics.
    * Solution: Adjust the body panels or replace them if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not functioning properly, such as insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control actuators or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2009 Acura TSX has a mix of minor and potentially severe issues. The timing chain tensioner failure and suspension problems can be costly to repair, while other issues like harsh shifting or electrical problems can be addressed for a lower cost. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and consider the estimated repair costs when making a purchase decision.
