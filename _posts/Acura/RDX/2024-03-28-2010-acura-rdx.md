**Introduction**

The 2010 Acura RDX is a compact luxury SUV powered by a 3.5L V6 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It boasts 240 horsepower and 234 lb-ft of torque. The RDX received praise for its spacious interior, refined ride, and powerful engine, but some drivers expressed concerns about its fuel economy and somewhat stiff suspension.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the intake manifold gasket, leading to overheating or poor engine performance.
    * Solution: Replacing the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning smell or reduced oil levels.
    * Solution: Replacing the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Harsh Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting gears abruptly or hesitating, causing discomfort to drivers.
    * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control module or replacing transmission parts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Premature Transmission Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission failing prematurely due to internal component wear or overheating.
    * Solution: Rebuilding or replacing the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noises coming from the front suspension, especially over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacing worn suspension components such as struts, springs, or control arms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear shocks failing, leading to a bumpy or unstable ride.
    * Solution: Replacing the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **ECU Programming Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine control unit (ECU) not programmed correctly, resulting in engine performance issues or warning lights.
    * Solution: Reprogramming or replacing the ECU.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely, leading to starting problems.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing electrical system issues or replacing the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin from the sunroof.
    * Solution: Replacing or repairing the sunroof seals or mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation forming inside the headlight housings.
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing the headlight seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cracked Dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard over time.
    * Solution: Replacing the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control not functioning properly or blowing hot air instead of cold air.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the climate control system or replacing faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2010 Acura RDX has several common issues, particularly with its transmission, engine, and suspension. Symptoms can range from mild annoyances like noises or leaks to more severe problems like premature transmission failure. While the cost of repairs can vary significantly, some issues can be quite expensive to fix. Despite these issues, the RDX remains a popular choice due to its performance, comfort, and premium features.
