# 2022 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2022 Acura RDX is a strong contender in the luxury compact SUV segment, known for its upscale interior, engaging driving dynamics, and advanced technology. Built on Acura's proprietary 'Global Light Truck' platform, it shares its powertrain with other Honda/Acura products, contributing to its overall reliability. The RDX generally receives positive marks for its build quality and the performance of its turbocharged engine and smooth 10-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise its comfortable ride and quiet cabin, making it a suitable choice for daily commuting and longer journeys. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the 2022 RDX demonstrates a commendable level of dependability for its class.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Acura RDX generally exhibits strong reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission performing commendably. The most frequently cited issues tend to be related to the infotainment system, which can experience occasional glitches, and a few reports of premature battery failure. These are not typically indicative of systemic powertrain or chassis weaknesses. Overall, the RDX is a dependable luxury SUV, and potential buyers can expect a solid ownership experience with a focus on addressing minor electrical quirks if they arise.
