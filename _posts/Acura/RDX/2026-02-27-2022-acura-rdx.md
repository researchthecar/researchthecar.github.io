# 2022 Acura RDX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Acura RDX, buyers will find a stylish and feature-rich luxury compact crossover. However, reviewing owner-submitted feedback reveals a few recurring areas of concern—most notably unexpected rear glass shattering and brake noise or premature wear—that deserve a close inspection during your pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The RDX comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing around 272 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission. Overall, this powertrain provides lively acceleration, though isolated owner reports note electronic or power delivery glitches.

**Engine Power Loss and Limp Mode**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly lose power during acceleration, dropping speeds and running rough while refusing to rev.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing at a qualified service center to identify potential fuel, sensor, or electronic throttle body faults.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 10-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the front wheels or Acura's Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) system. It generally shifts crisply and exhibits solid long-term dependability with no widespread mechanical failure trends.

## Brakes

### Brake Rotors and Pads

Issues related to premature brake wear, grinding noises, and vibrating rotors have been reported by several owners early in the vehicle's lifespan.

**Premature Wear and Unusual Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience abnormal screeching sounds, rough contact noises, or steering wheel vibrations during braking, sometimes at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for premature wear or grooving, and replace or resurface components as needed.

## Exterior

### Rear Hatch Glass

Exterior complaints center heavily around spontaneous rear glass breakage, which can catch owners by surprise when parked.

**Spontaneous Rear Window Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear windshield or hatch glass shuddenly shatters while the vehicle is parked or unattended, without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Have the rear glass replaced and inspect the window frame and defroster wiring for stress points or defects.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features like collision mitigation generally assist drivers well, but a few owners report overly sensitive false triggers from the automated braking systems.

**False Positive Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may incorrectly trigger the automatic emergency braking system when no obstruction is present, causing sudden deceleration.
- **Solution:** Ensure sensor calibrations are up to date and have dealer software updates applied if available.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V064000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, failing to suppress the air bag as intended. An air bag that deploys unintentionally during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Acura RDX is an engaging and well-appointed luxury crossover, but potential buyers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to the condition of the brakes for early wear and check the rear window glass carefully. Verifying that all software updates for driver-assist systems have been performed will help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
