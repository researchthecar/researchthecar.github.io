# 2014 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2014 Acura RDX is a compact luxury SUV known for its comfortable ride, potent V6 engine, and upscale interior. It generally presents a strong reliability record, reflecting Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. Owners often praise its performance and fuel efficiency for its class. While most RDX models from this year are expected to perform well with routine maintenance, there are a few areas where owners have reported specific concerns that are worth noting for potential buyers or current owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard wear and tear.

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Acura RDX is a reliable vehicle with a strong overall mechanical foundation. The 3.5L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. The suspension system also holds up well. The most commonly reported issues tend to be in the electrical system, specifically with the infotainment's Bluetooth connectivity and the keyless entry door handle sensors. These are typically fixable with software updates or component replacements. Exterior and interior components are also largely free of widespread problems. With diligent maintenance and attention to potential electrical quirks, the 2014 RDX should provide dependable service.
