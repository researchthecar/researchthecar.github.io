### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Acura RDX demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, with its core powertrain components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically related to occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. These issues are generally addressable through software updates or component replacement. Exterior and interior components, along with the suspension, appear to be robust and free from common complaints. Overall, the RDX is a dependable choice for luxury SUV buyers seeking a well-built and reliable vehicle.
