# 2017 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2017 Acura RDX is a compact luxury SUV that generally offers a good balance of performance, comfort, and reliability. It is powered by a capable V6 engine paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. The RDX is known for its upscale interior, user-friendly technology, and a comfortable ride, making it a solid choice in its segment. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, there are a few areas where potential issues have been noted, primarily related to the infotainment system and occasional minor mechanical concerns. Overall, the RDX is considered a dependable vehicle with a strong track record.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Acura RDX is a reliable luxury SUV with a strong overall mechanical foundation. The 3.5L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. Potential concerns are primarily concentrated in the electrical system, specifically with the infotainment unit experiencing freezing or Bluetooth connectivity issues, and some reports of premature battery failure. Exterior and interior components are largely robust. With proactive maintenance and attention to the noted electrical quirks, the 2017 RDX should provide dependable service for its owners.
