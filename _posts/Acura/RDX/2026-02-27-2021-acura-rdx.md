# 2021 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2021 Acura RDX is a popular luxury compact SUV known for its blend of performance, comfort, and technology. Generally, the RDX has demonstrated good reliability, reflecting Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. Owners often praise its refined interior, potent turbocharged engine, and engaging driving dynamics. While most RDX models experience few significant problems, like any vehicle, there can be isolated reports of issues that are worth noting for potential buyers. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into specific components and potential repair costs.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, low oil level warnings, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of seals or gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling or hissing sounds from the engine bay, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential repair or replacement of the turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable delays or rough shifts during acceleration or deceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates for the transmission control module, or in severe cases, internal transmission repairs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Acura RDX is a generally reliable vehicle with a strong powertrain and well-built interior. The most commonly reported technical issues tend to be related to the infotainment system's responsiveness and occasional minor oil leaks or turbocharger concerns, which are not excessively prevalent. Transmission performance is typically smooth, though some owners have noted occasional hesitation. Electrical issues like battery drain can occur but are often resolvable with diagnostics. Overall, the RDX offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic failures.
