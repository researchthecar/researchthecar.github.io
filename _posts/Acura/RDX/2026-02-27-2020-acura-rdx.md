# 2020 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2020 Acura RDX is a compact luxury SUV that generally offers a strong reliability record, reflecting Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. It is powered by a potent 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission, a combination that provides a good balance of performance and efficiency. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, there are a few areas that have seen occasional reports of issues. These tend to be minor and often related to specific electronic components or software glitches rather than fundamental mechanical failures. Overall, the RDX is considered a reliable choice in its segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Acura RDX is a highly reliable vehicle with a strong overall performance. The powertrain, suspension, and most interior/exterior components are robust and rarely present issues. The primary areas that have seen some owner reports are related to the infotainment system, which can experience occasional glitches requiring software updates or resets, and in rarer cases, premature battery failure. These issues are generally not indicative of major mechanical flaws and are often resolvable with standard maintenance or minor repairs. For a luxury compact SUV, the RDX offers excellent long-term dependability.
