# 2012 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2012 Acura RDX, a compact luxury SUV, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. It was offered with a potent turbocharged four-cylinder engine, providing a good balance of performance and efficiency for its class. The RDX is known for its comfortable ride, upscale interior, and sporty handling, making it an attractive option for those seeking a premium compact SUV. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, there are a few areas that have seen occasional attention from owners and technicians. This data focuses on documented, non-routine issues.

---


## Engine

### 2.3L Turbocharged I4 (K23A1)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, excessive smoke.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of the turbocharger assembly, checking oil lines for blockages.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets and seals, such as valve cover gasket or oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (5AT)
**Transmission Shudder/Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking or shuddering during acceleration, delayed gear engagement.
- **Solution:** Software update for transmission control module, fluid flush and replacement, or potential internal transmission repairs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Power Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Window not operating, slow window movement, clicking noises when operating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Bluetooth Connectivity Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty pairing devices, intermittent connection, no audio output.
- **Solution:** Software update for the infotainment system, or potential replacement of the Bluetooth module.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400


## Exterior

### Standard Exterior
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, headliner staining.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes, inspection for any water damage.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Acura RDX is a reliable compact luxury SUV with a generally positive ownership experience. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the turbocharged engine's components, such as the turbocharger itself and potential oil leaks from seals. The transmission can occasionally exhibit shuddering, often addressable with software updates or fluid services. Minor electrical issues like power window motors and Bluetooth connectivity can occur. The suspension may require attention for worn bushings over time, and interior issues like clogged sunroof drains are not uncommon. Overall, the RDX offers good durability, but owners should be aware of these potential, though not universal, repair areas.
