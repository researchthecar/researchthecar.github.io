# 2024 Acura RDX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2024 Acura RDX, buyers will find a luxury compact SUV that blends engaging driving dynamics with modern styling and advanced technology. Because the 2024 model year is still relatively new, owner feedback is limited, but early driver reports highlight a few distinct areas to watch out for during a pre-purchase inspection. Being aware of these initial owner concerns can help you evaluate a prospective vehicle more effectively.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2024 Acura RDX is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing 272 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving and highway passing while maintaining balanced efficiency.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is sent through a 10-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or Acura's Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) system. This transmission offers smooth gear changes and helps optimize fuel economy across various driving conditions.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Service Brakes

Owner reports note potential brake pedal travel concerns requiring immediate inspection.

**Brake Pedal Travel to Floor**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the brake pedal traveled all the way to the floorboard, resulting in a delayed stopping response or failure to slow down adequately.
- **Solution:** Have the brake system inspected immediately for hydraulic pressure loss, master cylinder performance, or air in the lines.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Trim

Reflective steering wheel trim can occasionally create distracting sunlight glare.

**Glare from Decorative Trim**
- **Symptoms:** The decorative aluminum trim on the steering wheel can catch bright sunlight at certain angles, producing a strong and distracting light reflection for the driver.
- **Solution:** Apply an anti-glare film or consult aftermarket styling solutions if the reflection proves bothersome.

## Electrical

### Front Power Seats

An isolated owner report highlights a severe electrical incident involving a front passenger seat.

**Seat Wiring or Component Electrical Fire**
- **Symptoms:** In rare instances, the front passenger seat area experienced an electrical fire while the vehicle was parked and turned off.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's interior electrical harnesses and seat adjustment modules inspected for signs of short circuits or chafing.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

A few owners have reported unexpected sunroof glass breakage during normal driving.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners experienced loud noises resembling an explosion followed by the panoramic sunroof glass shattering unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the panoramic glass assembly for stress cracks or impact damage, and consult a dealer regarding replacement options under warranty if applicable.

### Side Mirrors

Driver-side mirror vibration at moderate speeds has been noted in owner reports.

**Excessive Mirror Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's side mirror exhibits noticeable shaking or vibrating while driving at speeds above 35 mph.
- **Solution:** Check mirror mounting hardware or have the mirror assembly replaced by a dealership.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Isolated issues with rear seatbelt locking mechanisms have been documented.

**Stuck Rear Seatbelt Buckle**
- **Symptoms:** The rear seatbelt buckle locking mechanism may malfunction, preventing passengers from easily unlatching the seatbelt.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear seatbelt assemblies and replace faulty buckle mechanisms as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V114000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear box assembly may not have been tightened properly, allowing water to enter the assembly and cause corrosion and binding gears. Corroded and binding steering gears can result in a loss of steering control and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the electric power steering rack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Acura RDX offers a compelling mix of luxury, cargo versatility, and confident performance. While early owner feedback highlights a few noteworthy complaints—such as rare instances of brake pedal travel issues, sunroof glass breakage, and isolated electrical concerns—many vehicles remain solid daily drivers. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes, glass, and electrical systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
