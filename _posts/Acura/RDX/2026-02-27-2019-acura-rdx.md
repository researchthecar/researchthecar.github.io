# 2019 Acura RDX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Acura RDX, buyers will find a stylish and engaging compact luxury crossover powered by a punchy turbocharged engine. While offering nimble handling and an upscale interior, this model year has accumulated notable owner complaints concerning specific structural and powertrain issues that prospective owners should inspect closely before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 272 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for daily driving. While generally robust, early model year variants have reported occasional head gasket and fuel pump concerns.

**Head Gasket Failure and Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a distinct startup sputter, choppy shudder as turbo boost climbs, or engine overheating due to coolant leaking into cylinder heads.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the engine block and replacement of the head gasket or cylinder components if failure occurs.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may stall unexpectedly, fail to start, or shut down while driving due to internal fuel pump failure.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump assembly.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and efficient gear shifts across front-wheel-drive and Super Handling All-Wheel Drive (SH-AWD) configurations. No widespread recurring failure patterns are heavily concentrated in owner complaint clusters for this unit.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

Electrical complaints primarily involve minor software glitches and infotainment reboots affecting smartphone integration.

**Screen Freezing and Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display screen may freeze, fail to connect to smartphones, or unexpectedly reboot while starting or driving the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates or reset the infotainment system at a dealership.

## Exterior

### Rear Glass Window

Exterior complaints center heavily on spontaneous rear glass breakage, which can startle occupants and requires costly glass replacement.

**Spontaneous Rear Windshield Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The rear windshield or back window can shatter or implode suddenly while driving, when closing a door, or while parked, often related to stress near the rear defroster grid.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered rear glass assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V314000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If the fuel pump fails, the engine can stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V215000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V158000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A manufacturing issue with the front seat belts may cause the seat belt buckle channel to interfere with the release button, preventing the seat belt buckle from latching. An unlatched seat belt cannot properly restrain the seat occupant during a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver and front passenger seat belt buckle release buttons or the buckle assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V332000: Air Bags:Sensor:Occupant Classification:Front Passenger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat weight sensor may crack and short circuit, which can cause the air bags to deploy unintentionally during a crash. Air bags that deploy unintentionally during a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat weight sensors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Acura RDX offers a compelling blend of luxury, performance, and everyday utility, but used car buyers must exercise caution. Pay close attention to service history regarding potential head gasket leaks, fuel pump reliability, and verify whether the rear window has already been addressed, as spontaneous shattering remains a notable owner complaint.
