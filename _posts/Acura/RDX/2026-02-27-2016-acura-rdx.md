# 2016 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2016 Acura RDX is a compact luxury SUV known for its blend of performance, comfort, and reliability. It offers a refined driving experience with a potent V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission. The interior is well-appointed with quality materials and user-friendly technology. Safety features are comprehensive, contributing to its appeal as a family vehicle. Overall, the RDX generally receives positive marks for its build quality and dependability, making it a solid choice in its segment. Owners often praise its comfortable ride and responsive handling, though some minor electrical and interior quirks have been noted over time.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In some cases, replacement of the head unit may be required.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery failure or dead battery without apparent cause.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from components like the infotainment system or other modules. Replacement of faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**Climate Control Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Airflow not directing correctly (e.g., only blowing out of defroster vents), or temperature not changing.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty HVAC actuator motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Acura RDX is a highly reliable vehicle with a strong track record. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission, is robust and generally free from significant issues. Suspension components also hold up well. The primary areas where minor concerns have been reported are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues, and some interior components like climate control actuators. These issues are not widespread but are worth noting. Overall, the RDX offers excellent long-term dependability, with most owners experiencing minimal problems beyond routine maintenance.
