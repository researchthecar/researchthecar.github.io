# 2016 Acura RDX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Acura RDX is a popular luxury crossover known for its smooth performance and comfortable cabin. While generally dependable, used-car buyers should review potential owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain refinement, electrical accessories, and safety technology before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 279 horsepower and 252 lb-ft of torque, providing strong and smooth acceleration. This powerplant is generally robust, though occasional reports note minor fuel or throttle response anomalies.

**Acceleration Hesitation and Surge**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a hesitation, sputtering, or surging sensation during acceleration at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel system, throttle body, and ignition components for faults.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It delivers composed gear changes, but buyers should test drive thoroughly to check for potential torque converter shudder.

**Rough Shifting and Torque Converter Judder**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note jerky upshifts and downshifts, or a noticeable judder vibration from the torque converter at highway or cruising speeds.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition; software updates or fluid flushes may alleviate symptoms, though severe judder may require professional transmission service.

## Brakes

### Brake System & Collision Mitigation

The service brakes and driver-assist safety suites generally provide reliable stopping power, though sensors can occasionally produce false alerts.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic collision mitigation or forward braking system intermittently activates without clear obstructions or traffic ahead.
- **Solution:** Ensure radar and camera sensors are clean and properly calibrated; consult a dealer if false triggers persist.

## Electrical

### Electrical Accessories and Battery Draw

Electrical architecture is generally sound, but intermittent accessory drains and module issues are worth inspecting on pre-owned models.

**Battery Drain via HandsFreeLink**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle battery experiences unexpected drainage, sometimes linked to the Bluetooth HandsFreeLink module failing to shut down properly.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic electrical draws and update or disconnect the faulty Bluetooth module if it fails to sleep.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V182000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing error, in the event of a crash necessitating deployment of the driver frontal air bag, these inflators may explode. An explosion of an inflator within the driver frontal air bag module may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver, front seat passenger or other occupants resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Honda will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver's air bag inflator with an alternate inflator, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V061000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2016)**

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Acura RDX offers strong V6 performance and upscale comfort, making it a solid contender in the used luxury crossover segment. Buyers should perform a thorough test drive to evaluate transmission smoothness and ensure all electrical features and driver aids operate properly.
