# 2015 Acura RDX

### Overview
The 2015 Acura RDX is a compact luxury SUV known for its blend of performance, comfort, and reliability. It offers a refined driving experience with a potent V6 engine and a smooth automatic transmission, making it a strong contender in its segment. Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles generally extends to the RDX, with owners reporting a high level of satisfaction. While no vehicle is entirely free from potential issues, the 2015 RDX demonstrates a solid track record for dependability, with most reported concerns being minor or related to typical usage rather than inherent design flaws. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability data, excluding routine maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Acura RDX generally exhibits strong reliability, aligning with Acura's reputation for building durable vehicles. The powertrain, including the 3.5L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission, is robust and rarely presents significant issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are typically trouble-free. The most commonly cited concerns, though not widespread, relate to the infotainment system experiencing occasional glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. These issues are generally manageable with software updates or component replacement. Overall, the 2015 RDX is a dependable choice for a luxury compact SUV, with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
