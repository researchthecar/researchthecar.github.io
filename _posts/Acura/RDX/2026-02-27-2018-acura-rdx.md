# 2018 Acura RDX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Acura RDX offers a refined driving experience, solid build quality, and a naturally aspirated V6 engine that makes it a popular choice in the used luxury compact SUV market. However, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints regarding powertrain performance and select electrical or body accessories. While overall mechanical durability is generally favorable when properly maintained, checking service history for transmission and torque converter updates is strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 Engine

The 2018 Acura RDX is powered by a 3.5-liter V6 engine producing approximately 279 horsepower and 252 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant is generally regarded as robust and durable, providing smooth acceleration for everyday driving.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience unexpected starting issues or stalling related to fuel delivery components.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel system and replace the faulty fuel pump or associated motor components.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the V6 engine, the 6-speed automatic transmission is available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It delivers smooth gear changes overall, though buyers should verify proper transmission operation during a test drive.

**Torque Converter and Slipping Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice harsh shifting, delayed engagement, dashboard warning lights, or transmission slipping into neutral.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission control module and torque converter, and perform recommended software updates or hardware repairs.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

The electrical system operates most auxiliary features reliably, though minor issues with exterior lighting seals have been reported by owners.

**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture or water accumulation inside the headlight housing, which can reduce nighttime illumination.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seals for stress fractures and replace the housing assembly if moisture intrusion persists.

### Sunroof

Exterior body panels and trim hold up well over time, but potential buyers should inspect glass surfaces, including the optional panoramic or standard sunroof.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden loud pop or explosion sound followed by the sunroof glass fracturing while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass and frame for stress points, and replace the glass assembly if damage occurs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V858000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Acura&model=RDX&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Acura RDX remains a compelling used luxury SUV choice thanks to its strong V6 performance and comfortable interior. Buyers should thoroughly check the vehicle's maintenance records, paying close attention to transmission shift quality and torque converter operation. Performing a pre-purchase inspection will help ensure any hidden or developing mechanical issues are addressed before purchase.
