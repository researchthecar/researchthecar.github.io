**Introduction:**

The 2007 Saab 9-3 is a premium sedan offered with a choice of engines and transmissions:

* **2.0T 4-cylinder:** 210 hp, 221 lb-ft of torque
* **2.8T V6:** 255 hp, 258 lb-ft of torque
* **6-speed manual transmission**
* **5-speed automatic transmission**

Praised for its stylish design, handling, and safety features, the Saab 9-3 has also been criticized for its high maintenance costs and electrical issues.

**Engine**

**2.0T 4-cylinder**

* **Turbocharger failure:** Premature turbocharger failure is a common issue, leading to reduced engine power and increased oil consumption.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+.
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.8T V6**

* **Timing chain rattle:** The timing chain can stretch and rattle, requiring replacement.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and tensioners.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual**

* **Clutch failure:** The clutch can prematurely wear out, resulting in difficulty shifting gears.
    * Solution: Replacement of the clutch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200.
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slip:** The transmission may slip and lose power during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replacement or rebuild of the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount bearing failure:** The strut mount bearings can wear out, leading to a clunking noise and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Key fob malfunctions:** The key fob may malfunction due to electrical issues, preventing the car from starting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the key fob battery or reprogramming.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Ignition switch failure:** The ignition switch may fail, preventing the car from starting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the ignition switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700.
    * Recalls: 06V170000 (Ignition Key Release)

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** Condensation may form inside the headlights, impairing visibility.
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Trunk release failure:** The trunk release button may fail, making it difficult to open the trunk.
    * Solution: Replacement of the trunk release button or wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof drain blockage:** The sunroof drains can become clogged, leading to water leaks into the cabin.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing the sunroof drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Saab 9-3 has potential reliability issues, particularly with its turbocharger, transmission, and electrical system. While some repairs are relatively inexpensive, others can be costly. The severity and cost of repairs depend on the specific issue encountered. Overall, the Saab 9-3 is a well-built and stylish vehicle, but owners should be aware of the potential maintenance expenses.
