**Introduction:**

The 2010 Saab 9-5 is a mid-size luxury sedan that was praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and impressive handling. It was available with four engine and transmission options:

* 2.0T FWD with a 6-speed manual or 6-speed automatic (210 hp / 221 lb-ft)
* 2.0T XWD with a 6-speed automatic (220 hp / 258 lb-ft)
* 2.8T XWD with a 6-speed automatic (300 hp / 295 lb-ft)
* 3.0T XWD with a 6-speed automatic (227 hp / 406 lb-ft)

**Engine**

**2.0T**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or knocking noises, reduced engine power, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost pressure, reduced engine power, smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.8T**

* **Water Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, coolant leaks
    * Solution: Replace water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0T**

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic transmission behavior, shift delays, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Check charging system, replace battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Fogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Foggy appearance inside headlight housing
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters do not work, either one seat or all seats
    * Solution: Repair or replace seat heating element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Radio Display Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Display screen goes blank or shows distorted images
    * Solution: Repair or replace radio display
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2010 Saab 9-5 has several potential issues, primarily related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences and others being more serious and costly. The estimated cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars to over $2,500, depending on the issue and the specific parts affected. Overall, while the 9-5 is a stylish and comfortable vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of its potential reliability issues before purchasing.
