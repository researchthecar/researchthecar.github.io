**Introduction**

The 2006 Saab 09-May is a compact sedan that was introduced as the replacement for the Saab 9-3. It was available with two engine options: a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 and a 2.3L inline-4. Both engines came standard with a 5-speed manual transmission, while a 5-speed automatic transmission was offered as an option. The 2.0L turbocharged engine produced 210 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.3L engine produced 173 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque.

The 09-May was praised for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and powerful engine options. However, it was also criticized for its high price, limited rear seat space, and poor fuel economy.
