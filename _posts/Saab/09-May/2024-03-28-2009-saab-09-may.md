**Introduction**

The 2009 Saab 9-5 is a midsize luxury sedan powered by a 2.3 liter 4-cylinder engine producing 220 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It was offered with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 9-5 was praised for its comfortable interior, sporty handling, and distinctive styling, but it was criticized for its lack of modern features and reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.3L 4-Cylinder**

    * **Turbo Oil Leak**
    Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the turbocharger
    Solution: Replace the turbocharger oil seal
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**

    * **Shifter Issues**
    Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    Solution: Replace the shifter linkage or transmission synchronizers
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

* **6-Speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the transmission pan or lines
    Solution: Replace the leaking component
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Bushings**
Description/Symptoms: Abnormal tire wear, clunking or squeaking noises from the rear suspension
Solution: Replace the rear suspension bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim lights, engine stalling
Solution: Replace the alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 16V566000, NHTSA Campaign Number 09V219000

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Water droplets inside the headlight assemblies
Solution: Replace headlight assembly
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly
Solution: Replace the sunroof switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300

**Summary**

The 2009 Saab 9-5 has a number of potential issues, including oil leaks, transmission problems, and electrical failures. These issues can be costly to repair, especially the transmission issues. However, the 9-5's comfortable interior, sporty handling, and distinctive styling make it an appealing choice for those seeking a used luxury sedan.
