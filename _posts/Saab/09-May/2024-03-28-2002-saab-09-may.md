**Introduction:**

The 2002 Saab 9-3 is a compact luxury sedan available with three engine options: a 2.0L I4, a 2.0T I4 turbocharged engine, and a 2.0T I4 turbocharged Viggen engine. The 2.0L I4 engine produces 150 horsepower and 143 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.0T I4 turbocharged engine produces 185 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque. The 2.0T I4 turbocharged Viggen engine produces 225 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque. All engines are paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Saab 9-3 for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and unique styling. However, they dislike its high fuel consumption, limited rear seat space, and expensive parts.

**Engine**

**2.0L I4 Engine**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the cylinder head gasket, overheating engine
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leak, rough idle, poor performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0T I4 Turbocharged Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, whistling or screeching noises
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Wastegate Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overboosting, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace wastegate solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
