**Introduction:**

The 2007 Saab 9-7x was a mid-size SUV that shared its platform with the Chevrolet Trailblazer, Buick Rainier, Isuzu Ascender, and GMC Envoy. The 9-7x was offered with two engine choices: a 4.2L I6 or a 5.3L V8. Both engines were paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The 4.2L engine produced 275 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, while the 5.3L engine produced 303 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque.

Owners praised the 9-7x for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and powerful engines. However, they also complained about its poor fuel economy, unreliable transmission, and lack of modern features.

**Engine**

**4.2L I6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak from the intake manifold, potential for overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, potential for catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
**5.3L V8**

* **Piston Ring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings and hone the cylinders.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the valve cover, potential for spark plug misfires.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to engage gears or slipping out of gears while driving.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations during acceleration, loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the front suspension, uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the front lower control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, excessive bouncing, potential for instability.
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Starter Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the vehicle, clicking noise when attempting to start.
    * Solution: Replace the starter motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on the rear bumper, potential for holes developing.
    * Solution: Replace the rear bumper or have it repaired by a body shop.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Interior**

* **Center Console Lid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to open or close the center console lid, broken latch.
    * Solution: Replace the center console lid or latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard, especially on the passenger side.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard or have it repaired by an upholstery shop.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Summary**

The 2007 Saab 9-7x has a number of potential issues, including engine problems, transmission problems, suspension problems, electrical problems, and interior problems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. While the 9-7x offers a comfortable ride and spacious interior, its reliability issues and lack of modern features may be a deterrent for some buyers.
