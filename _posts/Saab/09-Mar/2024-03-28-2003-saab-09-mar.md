**Introduction**

The 2003 Saab 09-Mar is a compact car known for its sporty handling and unique design. It was available with a choice of two engines and two transmissions:

* **Base Engine:** 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 (150 hp, 220 lb-ft)
* **Turbo Engine:** 2.3L turbocharged inline-4 (200 hp, 258 lb-ft)
* **Transmissions:** 5-speed manual, 5-speed automatic

**Engine**

**Base Engine (2.0L turbo)**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the intake manifold area, overheating
  * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Boost Leak**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increase in exhaust smoke
  * Solution: Replace or repair damaged boost hose
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Turbo Engine (2.3L turbo)**

* **Turbocharger Actuator Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Overboosting, loss of power
  * Solution: Replace turbocharger actuator
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption**
  * Description/Symptoms: Low oil levels, excessive oil burning
  * Solution: Piston ring replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual**

* **Shift Linkage Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
  * Solution: Replace shift linkage
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
  * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from the transmission pan or seals
  * Solution: Replace seals or transmission pan
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Solenoid Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, hesitation
  * Solution: Replace solenoid
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, excessive bouncing
  * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery warning light on
  * Solution: Replace alternator
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Switch Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flickering or not working
  * Solution: Replace headlight switch
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening doors
  * Solution: Replace door handles
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
  * Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing cold, heater not blowing hot
  * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Leak**
  * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin when sunroof is closed
  * Solution: Replace sunroof seals
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2003 Saab 09-Mar has some potential issues, most are relatively minor and affordable to fix. The most severe issue is the turbocharger actuator failure on the 2.3L turbo engine, which can be costly to repair. Overall, the Saab 09-Mar is a reliable and enjoyable car that provides a unique and sporty driving experience.
