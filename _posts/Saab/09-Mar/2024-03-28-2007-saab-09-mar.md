**Introduction:**

The 2007 Saab 9-3 is a compact executive car that was available with a range of engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 that produced 210 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. A more powerful 2.8-liter turbocharged V6 was also available, producing 250 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Transmission choices included a 5-speed manual, 6-speed automatic, and a 5-speed automatic with Saab's Sentronic semi-automatic system.

The Saab 9-3 was praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it was also criticized for its high price and lack of reliability.
