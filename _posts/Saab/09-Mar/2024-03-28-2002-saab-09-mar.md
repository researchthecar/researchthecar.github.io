**Introduction**
The 2002 Saab 9-3 is a luxury compact car that offers a blend of performance and Swedish design. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 (185 horsepower, 184 pound-feet of torque) and a 2.0-liter turbocharged V6 (210 horsepower, 221 pound-feet of torque). Front-wheel drive is standard, while all-wheel drive is available as an option. The 9-3 is praised for its handling, comfortable interior, and stylish design, but it has also been criticized for its lack of interior space and some reliability issues.

## **Engine**

### **2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4**
* **Oil Leaks from Cam Phaser Bolts:**
    * Oil leaks from the cam phaser bolts at the rear of the engine.
    * Replace the cam phaser bolts.
    * $500 - $1,000+
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Coolant leaks from the intake manifold gasket.
    * Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * $300 - $600+
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * The turbocharger fails, leading to a loss of power and excessive smoke.
    * Replace the turbocharger.
    * $1,500 - $2,500+
**Recalls:**
* NHTSA Campaign Number: 02V224000
    * Issue: Fuel Tank Leakage
* NHTSA Campaign Number: 04V181000
    * Issue: Throttle Position Sensor

### **2.0-liter Turbocharged V6**
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * The timing chain tensioner fails, causing a rattling noise and potential engine damage.
    * Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Oil Pan Gasket Leak:**
    * Oil leaks from the oil pan gasket.
    * Replace the oil pan gasket.
    * $300 - $500+
* **EGR Valve Carbon Buildup:**
    * Carbon buildup on the EGR valve causes performance problems and reduced fuel economy.
    * Clean or replace the EGR valve.
    * $200 - $400+
**Recalls:**
* None Found

## **Transmission**

### **5-Speed Manual**
* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * The clutch master cylinder fails, leading to a loss of clutch pressure and the inability to shift gears.
    * Replace the clutch master cylinder.
    * $300 - $600+
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
    * Transmission fluid leaks from the transmission pan or seals.
    * Replace the leaking components and refill the transmission fluid.
    * $200 - $500+

### **4-Speed Automatic**
* **Solenoid Pack Failure:**
    * The solenoid pack fails, causing shifting problems and potential transmission damage.
    * Replace the solenoid pack.
    * $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * The torque converter shudders when shifting gears.
    * Replace the torque converter.
    * $1,500 - $2,500+

## **Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts Wear:**
    * The strut mounts wear out, causing a knocking noise and reduced handling.
    * Replace the strut mounts.
    * $200 - $400+ per axle
* **Rear Control Arm Bushings Rear:**
    * The rear control arm bushings wear out, leading to a clunking noise and poor handling.
    * Replace the rear control arm bushings.
    * $300 - $500+ per axle

## **Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failures:**
    * Electrical components such as the alternator, starter, or battery may fail prematurely.
    * Replace the faulty electrical component.
    * $500 - $2,000+
* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * The ignition coils fail, causing misfires and reduced engine performance.
    * Replace the ignition coils.
    * $200 - $400+
* **ECM Failure:**
    * The engine control module (ECM) fails, leading to various engine problems.
    * Replace the ECM.
    * $1,000 - $2,000+

## **Interior**

    * Replace the radio display.
    * $200 - $400+
* **Climate Control Unit Failure:**
    * The climate control unit fails to operate correctly.
    * Replace the climate control unit.

**Summary**

The 2002 Saab 9-3 has some potential reliability issues that can be costly to repair. The most severe issues include engine and transmission problems, which can lead to significant expenses. However, the 9-3 is also known for its handling, comfort, and design, and many owners find the benefits outweigh the potential drawbacks.
