The 1999 Saab 09-Mar is a compact executive car that offers a smooth ride, responsive handling, and a comfortable interior. It is powered by a 2.3-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 185 horsepower and 206 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.3-liter turbocharged inline-4**

- **Engine misfires:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power
    - Solution: Replace spark plugs, ignition coils, or fuel injectors
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Turbocharger failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive exhaust smoke
    - Solution: Replace turbocharger
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Oil leaks:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Oil puddles under the vehicle
    - Solution: Replace oil pan gasket or other leaking component
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual**

- **Grinding gears:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears
    - Solution: Replace or repair synchronizers
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**4-speed automatic**

- **Slipping gears:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power during acceleration
    - Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Strut mounts:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps
    - Solution: Replace strut mounts
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Control arm bushings:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loose steering, uneven tire wear
    - Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Electrical system issues:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, flickering gauges
    - Solution: Inspect and replace faulty components
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Battery drain:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Dead battery
    - Solution: Replace battery, test for parasitic drain
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Door lock actuators:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly
    - Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Window switches:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Windows not operating properly
    - Solution: Replace window switches
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1999 Saab 09-Mar is known for its engaging driving dynamics but can suffer from certain reliability issues. Engine problems, such as misfires and turbocharger failures, can be costly to fix. Transmission problems, while less common, can also be expensive to address. Other issues like suspension and electrical problems are typically less severe and less expensive to resolve. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their purchasing decision.
