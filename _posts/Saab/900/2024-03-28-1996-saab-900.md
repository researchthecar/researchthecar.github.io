**Introduction:**

The 1996 Saab 900 is a compact executive car known for its distinctive design, innovative features, and sporty handling. It was available with a range of engine and transmission options, including naturally aspirated and turbocharged gasoline engines, and manual or automatic transmissions. The 900 was praised for its safety features, comfort, and performance, but also received criticism for its cramped interior and high maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**2.0 L 16-Valve Naturally Aspirated** (133 hp, 136 lb-ft torque)
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500
    * Recalls: 96V089000
* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from valve cover, oil pan, or other engine components
    * Solution: Replace or seal leaking components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$500

**2.0 L 16-Valve Turbocharged** (185 hp, 200 lb-ft torque)
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger and related components, including oil supply lines
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000-$2,000
* **Water Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks
    * Solution: Replace water pump and associated components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**
* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replace clutch components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800
* **Synchronizer Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding noises when shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace worn synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500

**4-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage or slips gears
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500-$2,500
* **Electrical Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission control module (TCM) malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace TCM or reprogram software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Foggy or water-filled headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight lenses and seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400
* **Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, especially around wheel arches
    * Solution: Repair and treat rust areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$2,000+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged struts, resulting in poor handling and suspension performance
    * Solution: Replace struts and mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600-$1,000
* **Bushings Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn bushings cause loose steering, suspension noise, or vibration
    * Solution: Replace worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery fails to charge, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400-$600
* **Electrical Wiring Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical gremlins, such as flickering lights, gauges not working, or intermittent power issues
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$1,000+

**Interior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks or becomes loose
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200
* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard develops cracks or warps
    * Solution: Replace or repair dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000

**Summary**

The 1996 Saab 900 has several potential issues, including engine leaks, transmission slippage, electrical problems, and suspension wear. While some issues are relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be costly. The severity and cost of these issues should be considered when purchasing or maintaining a used 900.
