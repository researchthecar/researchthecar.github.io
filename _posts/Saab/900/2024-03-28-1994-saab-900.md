**1994 Saab 900**

The 1994 Saab 900 is a compact executive car available in sedan and convertible body styles. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter I4 engine that produces 185 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque. The 900 offers both a five-speed manual and a four-speed automatic transmission.

The Saab 900 is praised for its sporty handling and comfortable interior, but it has been criticized for its lack of power and reliability issues.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged I4**

**Blown Head Gasket**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the engine, overheating, loss of power
Solution: Replace head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling sound, blue smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

**Synchro Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise
Solution: Replace synchronizers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic**
**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Delayed or no shifting, slipping gears
Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system issues, battery not charging
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rear Window Seal Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into trunk, fogging up rear window
Solution: Replace rear window seal
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning fans, poor airflow, no heat or air conditioning
Solution: Replace climate control module or components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1994 Saab 900 has a number of potential issues that can range in severity and cost. The most common problems seem to be related to the engine, transmission, and electrical system. While some repairs may be relatively minor and inexpensive, others can be quite costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
