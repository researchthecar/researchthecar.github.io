## 1996 Saab 9000

The Saab 9000 is a mid-size sedan that was produced from 1984 to 1998. For the 1996 model year, the 9000 was offered with two engine and transmission options:

* 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 (150 hp, 188 lb-ft torque) with a 5-speed manual transmission
* 2.3L turbocharged V6 (170 hp, 229 lb-ft torque) with a 4-speed automatic transmission

The 9000 was praised for its comfortable interior, sporty handling, and safety features. However, it was also criticized for its high price and poor fuel economy.

**Summary**
