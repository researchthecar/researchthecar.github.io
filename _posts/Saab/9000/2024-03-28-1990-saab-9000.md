**Introduction:**
The 1990 Saab 9000 was a popular midsize sedan known for its turbocharged performance, comfortable interior, and safety features. It was powered by a 2.1L or 2.3L turbocharged inline-four engine, paired with either a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The 9000 was praised for its acceleration and handling but was criticized for its cramped back seat and outdated design.

**Engine**

**2.1L Turbo**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks from the intake manifold gasket, leading to overheating or engine damage.
   - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   - Recalls: N/A
- **Head Gasket Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks from the head gasket, leading to overheating or engine damage.
   - Solution: Replace head gasket.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
   - Recalls: N/A

**2.3L Turbo**
- **Turbocharger Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling or whining noises, excessive smoke from exhaust.
   - Solution: Replace or rebuild turbocharger.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   - Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**
- **Clutch Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch, vibration or shuddering during engagement.
   - Solution: Replace clutch.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   - Recalls: N/A

**4-Speed Automatic**
- **Transmission Failure:**
   - Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hard shifts, no response from transmission.
   - Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
   - Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

   - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per strut

   - Solution: Replace control arm bushings.

**Summary:**
Overall, the 1990 Saab 9000 exhibits various issues, with engine and transmission problems being the most common and costly. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor in the repair costs when considering this vehicle. The severity of the issues ranges from minor annoyances to major breakdowns, and the cost of repairs can be significant, especially for more complex problems.
