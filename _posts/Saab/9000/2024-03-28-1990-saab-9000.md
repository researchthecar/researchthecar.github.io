**Introduction:**
The 1990 Saab 9000 is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its advanced engineering and safety features. It was available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four (145 horsepower, 162 lb-ft torque) and a 2.3-liter naturally aspirated inline-four (135 horsepower, 151 lb-ft torque). Both engines could be paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $750 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks From Vacuum Pump:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil pooling beneath the vehicle, loss of oil pressure
    * Solution: Replace vacuum pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**2.3-Liter Naturally Aspirated Inline-Four**

* No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Synchro Ring Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding or crunching noises
    * Solution: Replace synchro rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200+
* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clutch pedal feels spongy or sinks to the floor
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle hesitates or stalls when accelerating
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Torque Converter Vibration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration when shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
* **Rear Control Arm Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or knocking noise from rear of vehicle
    * Solution: Replace rear control arm mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, electrical issues
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Re-seal sunroof
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks developing on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary:**

The 1990 Saab 9000 is a reliable vehicle with some common issues. The engine and transmission issues are typically costly to fix, while the suspension and electrical issues are generally more affordable. Some interior and exterior issues can also arise. It's important to address these issues promptly to prevent further damage and ensure the vehicle's safety and performance.
