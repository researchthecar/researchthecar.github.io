**Introduction**

The 1998 Saab 9000 is a mid-size luxury sedan offered with a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its comfortable ride, safety features, and quirky styling. However, like all vehicles, it has certain issues that owners may encounter.

**Engine**

* **B204 2.0L Turbo**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks near the intake manifold, overheating, engine misfire
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive oil consumption, whistling sound from turbo
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **B234 2.3L Turbo**
    * **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced engine power
        * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace piston rings or engine rebuild
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **B235 2.3L Turbo 5-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, difficulty shifting, shuddering
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque Converter Lock-Up Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, overheating, reduced fuel economy
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **B235 2.3L Turbo 5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting, grinding noise
        * Solution: Replace clutch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Input Shaft Bearing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Whining or grinding noise from transmission, difficulty shifting
        * Solution: Replace input shaft bearing
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorbers**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, poor handling, leaking shocks
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies after short periods of time, flickering lights, electronic problems
    * Solution: Check for faulty alternator, starter, or wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Gremlins**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, such as power window failures, door locks malfunctioning, etc.
    * Solution: Difficult to diagnose, may require extensive electrical system check
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, intermittent headlights, flickering
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tail Light Bulbs Burn Out**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tail lights not working, frequent bulb replacements
    * Solution: Replace tail light bulbs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogged**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin through sunroof, headliner gets wet
    * Solution: Clean or replace sunroof drain plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working, flickering, or inaccurate
    * Solution: Repair or replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1998 Saab 9000 has a range of issues ranging from minor inconveniences to more serious mechanical problems. The severity and cost of the issues can vary significantly depending on the specific issue and the condition of the vehicle. Regular maintenance and timely repairs can help to alleviate some of the potential issues and extend the life of the vehicle.
