**Introduction**

The 2017 Lamborghini Aventador is a mid-engine sports car produced by Lamborghini. It is powered by a naturally-aspirated 6.5L V12 engine paired with a 7-speed single-clutch automated manual transmission. The Aventador is known for its sharp styling, powerful engine, and impressive performance. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

* **6.5L V12**

    * **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace faulty piston rings and/or valve seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the oil pan, valve covers, or other engine components have been reported.
        * Solution: Replace faulty gaskets or seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty fuel injectors can lead to engine misfires, rough idling, and reduced power.
        * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Single-Clutch Automated Manual**

    * **Clutch Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: The single-clutch transmission can experience premature clutch wear, leading to slipping and loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace worn clutch assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Shifting Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported difficulty shifting gears or delayed shifts.
        * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control unit (TCU) or replacing faulty sensors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Faults**
        * Description/Symptoms: Electrical issues, such as malfunctioning sensors, wiring harness faults, or battery problems have been reported.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the fault
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Panel Alignment**
        * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have experienced misaligned roof panels, leading to water leaks or wind noise.
        * Solution: Adjust or replace roof panel mounts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Electronics Glitches**
        * Description/Symptoms: Occasional glitches or failures of the dashboard electronics have been reported, including malfunctioning navigation or audio systems.
        * Solution: Software updates or replacement of faulty components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Lamborghini Aventador is a high-performance sports car with impressive capabilities. However, it is not immune to issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. Excessive oil consumption, oil leaks, and clutch wear are common concerns. Electrical faults and alignment issues have also been reported, but they are less severe and less costly to fix. Overall, the Aventador is a well-built and reliable vehicle, but potential owners should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
