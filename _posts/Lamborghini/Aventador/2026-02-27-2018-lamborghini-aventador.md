# 2018 Lamborghini Aventador Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lamborghini Aventador is an exotic V12 supercar known for its striking design and blistering performance. When shopping for a used model, buyers should keep in mind that exotic vehicles require meticulous maintenance and specialized service. While formal owner complaint data is sparse for this ultra-low-volume supercar, prospective owners should thoroughly inspect the powertrain and electrical systems prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L Naturally Aspirated V12

Featuring a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 producing around 730 to 740 horsepower, this engine delivers iconic exotic sound and extreme performance. It requires premium fuel, precise maintenance intervals, and expert care to remain reliable.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Independent Shift Rod (ISR) Automated Manual

The 7-speed ISR automated manual transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes suited for track-capable driving. Buyers should inspect clutch wear and shift smoothness, as replacement and service costs for exotic gearboxes are exceptionally high.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V391000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Aventador&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The engine may stall unexpectedly during certain driving conditions such as when the accelerator pedal is released below 2000 rpms while the transmission is automatically down shifting to a lower gear. An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will install updated engine software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

Purchasing a used 2018 Lamborghini Aventador is an emotional decision centered on exotic styling and uncompromising V12 performance. Because mechanical and electrical upkeep demand specialized expertise and high budgets, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an authorized specialist is essential.
