# 2017 Lamborghini Aventador Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lamborghini Aventador is an exotic supercar powered by a massive naturally aspirated V12 engine, delivering breathtaking performance and unmistakable presence. While high-end exotics like the Aventador are typically maintained with great care by their owners, prospective buyers should approach the used market with attention to service history and potential mechanical quirks.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L V12

The 2017 Lamborghini Aventador features a 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine producing approximately 700 to 740 horsepower depending on the specific trim. This high-revving powerhouse delivers incredible performance, though buyers should expect substantial maintenance costs and careful monitoring of operating temperatures.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed ISR (Independent Shift Rods) Automated Manual

Power is routed through a 7-speed ISR automated manual transmission designed for lightning-fast gear changes. The transmission is robust under proper use, but clutch wear can be significant depending on driving habits and city mileage.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V391000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Aventador&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The engine may stall unexpectedly during certain driving conditions such as when the accelerator pedal is released below 2000 rpms while the transmission is automatically down shifting to a lower gear. An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will install updated engine software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V073000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Aventador&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Gasoline contact with an ignition source such as a hot exhaust system can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will upgrade the fuel tank's evaporative emissions system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lamborghini Aventador is an uncompromising hypercar built for pure thrills rather than daily commuting. Used buyers should prioritize vehicles with comprehensive service records and professional pre-purchase inspections to minimize the risk of expensive exotic car repairs.
