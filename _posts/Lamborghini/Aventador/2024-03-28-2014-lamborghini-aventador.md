**2014 Lamborghini Aventador**

The 2014 Lamborghini Aventador is a high-performance sports car that is powered by a massive 6.5-liter V12 engine that produces 700 horsepower. The Aventador is available with either a 7-speed single-clutch automated manual transmission or a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. It is a popular choice among sports car enthusiasts due to its incredible performance and head-turning looks. However, some owners have reported experiencing certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* 700 horsepower 6.5-liter V12 engine

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **7-speed single-clutch automated manual transmission**

**Difficult Shifting:**
* Description/Symptoms: Drivers may experience difficulty shifting gears, especially when shifting from first to second gear. The transmission may also grind or jerk when shifting.
* Solution: Replacing the clutch and flywheel is often necessary to resolve this issue.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
* Recalls: None Found

* **7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission**

**Transmission Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience catastrophic failure, resulting in a loss of power and the inability to drive the vehicle.
* Solution: Replacing the transmission is the only way to resolve this issue.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $25,000 - $30,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
* Solution: Replacing the battery is typically the most effective way to solve this problem.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Carbon Fiber Damage:**
* Description/Symptoms: The carbon fiber body panels may become cracked or damaged, especially in areas exposed to UV rays or road debris.
* Solution: Repairing or replacing the damaged panels is the best way to fix this issue.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Rear Visibility:**
* Description/Symptoms: The low roofline and small rear window significantly limit rear visibility.
* Solution: There is no known solution for this design flaw.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: Not applicable
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2014 Lamborghini Aventador is a high-performance sports car that has received praise for its performance and aesthetics. However, some owners have reported experiencing issues with the transmission and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing an Aventador.
