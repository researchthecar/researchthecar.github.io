# 2016 Lamborghini Aventador Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Lamborghini Aventador is an exercise in exotic supercar ownership, where breathtaking performance and dramatic styling take center stage. Because this high-end vehicle is produced in limited volumes, standard owner-reported complaint databases often capture very few recurring mechanical trends, though prospective buyers should still approach inspection with thorough due diligence. Maintaining an exotic V12 flagship requires specialized servicing, meticulous pre-purchase inspections, and a healthy budget for upkeep.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L V12

The 6.5-liter naturally aspirated V12 engine produces roughly 700 to 740 horsepower depending on the specific trim level. It delivers breathtaking acceleration and classic supercar acoustics, though it demands rigorous professional maintenance and premium fueling.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed ISR Independent Shift Rod Automated Manual

The 7-speed Independent Shift Rod (ISR) automated manual transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes tailored for high-performance driving. Buyers should inspect the clutch wear and overall shifting smoothness closely during a pre-purchase evaluation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V391000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Aventador&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The engine may stall unexpectedly during certain driving conditions such as when the accelerator pedal is released below 2000 rpms while the transmission is automatically down shifting to a lower gear. An engine stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will install updated engine software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V461000: Wheels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Aventador&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A wheel detachment can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will replace the centering bolts with ones that have thread-locking fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V073000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Aventador&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Gasoline contact with an ignition source such as a hot exhaust system can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will upgrade the fuel tank's evaporative emissions system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lamborghini Aventador remains an ultimate automotive statement, blending extreme V12 performance with iconic exotic styling. While broad complaint trends are scarce due to low production volumes, buyers must prioritize a comprehensive mechanical inspection by a qualified exotic specialist to ensure all systems and drivetrain components are in top condition.
