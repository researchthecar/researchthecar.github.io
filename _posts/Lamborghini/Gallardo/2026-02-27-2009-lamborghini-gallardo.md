# 2009 Lamborghini Gallardo

### Overview
The 2009 Lamborghini Gallardo, a mid-engine V10 supercar, is renowned for its exhilarating performance and striking design. While generally considered a robust exotic car, like any high-performance vehicle, it can experience specific issues that owners should be aware of. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2009 model year. Owners can use this data to anticipate and address potential repairs, ensuring the continued optimal performance and longevity of their Gallardo.

---


## Engine

### 5.0L V10 (LP500-4)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, engine overheating, low coolant level warning.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Clutch Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, 'clutch error' warning light, inability to engage gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the clutch actuator unit.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


### 5.2L V10 (LP560-4)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of gaskets, seals, or oil pan.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

**E-gear System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear changes, transmission warning lights, failure to select gears.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of E-gear control unit or related sensors.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $5000

### 6-Speed E-gear (Automated Manual)
**Hydraulic Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or jerky gear changes, inability to shift, 'transmission failure' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the E-gear hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $1800 - $3500

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent shifting problems, dashboard warning lights related to transmission.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty sensors.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Lamborghini Gallardo, across its V10 engine variants and transmission options (manual and E-gear), demonstrates a generally solid reliability profile for an exotic supercar. The most frequently cited technical concerns revolve around the E-gear transmission system, particularly hydraulic pump and sensor issues, and occasional coolant or oil leaks from the V10 engines. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and window regulator problems, are also noted but are not typically indicative of systemic failures. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free from widespread reported issues. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to any warning lights are crucial for owners to mitigate potential repair costs and maintain the exceptional performance of this iconic Lamborghini.
