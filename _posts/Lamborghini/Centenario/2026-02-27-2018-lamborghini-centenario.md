# 2018 Lamborghini Centenario

### Overview
The 2018 Lamborghini Centenario, a limited-production hypercar celebrating the 100th birthday of Ferruccio Lamborghini, represents the pinnacle of automotive engineering and exotic design. Built as a coupe and roadster, it features a naturally aspirated V12 engine derived from the Aventador, tuned for even higher performance. Its aggressive, aerodynamic bodywork is constructed from carbon fiber. As a vehicle of extreme exclusivity and performance, the Centenario is not typically evaluated on the same reliability metrics as mass-produced vehicles. Data is scarce due to its limited production run and owner base, which often prioritizes performance and collectibility over long-term, everyday usability. However, the underlying mechanicals are based on proven Lamborghini architecture, suggesting a high degree of robustness for its intended purpose.
