# 2017 Lamborghini Centenario Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lamborghini Centenario is an ultra-exclusive hypercar built to celebrate the birth centenary of company founder Ferruccio Lamborghini. Available in extremely limited coupe and roadster numbers, it pairs dramatic carbon-fiber styling with exotic high-performance engineering. For used-car buyers and collectors, vehicles of this caliber are typically maintained through specialized factory channels rather than evaluated on traditional high-volume reliability metrics.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L V12

This naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine produces approximately 750 horsepower, delivering blistering performance and an iconic exhaust note. It is a highly specialized powerplant requiring meticulous dealer maintenance and high-octane fuel.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed ISR Single-Clutch Automated Manual

The 7-speed Independent Shifting Rod (ISR) automated manual transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes tailored for track-capable driving. Buyers should inspect clutch wear and system calibration during a pre-purchase evaluation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V244000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Centenario&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of 49 CFR Part 567, "Certification." An overloaded vehicle may increase the risk of suspension or tire failure, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will install a corrected certification label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lamborghini Centenario is a blue-chip collector vehicle where traditional mass-market reliability data does not apply. Prospective buyers should prioritize comprehensive service records, documented dealer maintenance histories, and thorough professional inspections before acquisition.
