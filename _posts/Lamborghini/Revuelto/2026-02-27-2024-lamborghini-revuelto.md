# 2024 Lamborghini Revuelto Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lamborghini Revuelto marks an electrifying new era for the brand's flagship V12 supercar, blending hybrid technology with traditional exotic performance. As a brand-new model, its complex powertrain and advanced electronics represent cutting-edge engineering. Buyers looking at the pre-owned market should weigh its extreme performance against the inherent complexities of a first-year exotic hybrid platform.

---

## Engine

### 6.5L V12 Plug-In Hybrid

The Revuelto features a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine paired with three electric motors, producing a combined 1,001 horsepower. This sophisticated powerplant delivers blistering performance, though its intricate hybrid architecture requires specialized servicing.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Power is managed by a transverse 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission mounted behind the V12 engine. It offers lightning-fast gearchanges suited for track driving and effortless highway cruising alike.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V765000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Revuelto&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Inoperative windshield wipers can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger wiper system connecting rod, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V945000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pipes, Hoses, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Revuelto&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The oil circuit connection flange screws may have been improperly tightened during assembly, which could cause the oil line to leak. An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will Inspect and repair the flange screws as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V477000: Structure:Body:Door:Hinge And Attachments](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Revuelto&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Door spring failure can cause the doors to close unexpectedly, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the spherical pins as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V053000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Revuelto&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2024 Lamborghini Revuelto is an engineering tour de force that commands attention on any road or track. Maintain realistic expectations regarding upkeep and specialized maintenance for a high-end exotic.
