# 2024 Lamborghini Revuelto

### Overview
The 2024 Lamborghini Revuelto represents a significant leap forward for the brand, introducing a groundbreaking plug-in hybrid V12 powertrain. As a brand-new model, comprehensive long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, initial reports and the inherent complexity of its advanced systems suggest a focus on meticulous maintenance and specialized servicing. The Revuelto combines a naturally aspirated 6.5-liter V12 engine with three electric motors, delivering unprecedented performance. While Lamborghini's engineering is renowned for its robustness, the integration of such sophisticated hybrid technology introduces new potential areas for technical scrutiny. Owners should expect a premium ownership experience, which includes a commitment to understanding and addressing the unique needs of this hypercar.

## Engine

### 6.5L Naturally Aspirated V12 Hybrid
**Hybrid System Integration**
- **Symptoms:** Potential for software glitches affecting power delivery or charging; rare instances of battery thermal management anomalies.
- **Solution:** Software updates, diagnostic checks by authorized technicians, potential battery module recalibration.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500 (Software/Diagnostics), $5,000+ (Battery Module Repair/Replacement)

**High-Voltage Battery Pack**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric range, intermittent power loss in EV mode, dashboard warnings related to battery performance.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing of battery management system, potential cell balancing or module replacement by specialized technicians.
- **Cost:** $10,000 - $30,000+

- **Solution:** Ensure regular charging of the high-voltage battery, check for parasitic draws, replace battery if performance degrades.

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Lamborghini Revuelto, being a first-year model with highly advanced hybrid technology, requires a proactive approach to maintenance. While core mechanical components like the V12 engine and DCT transmission are built to Lamborghini's high standards, the complexity of the hybrid system presents the primary area for potential technical attention. Early adopters should be aware of the possibility of software-related issues and the critical importance of the high-voltage battery system. Specialized servicing by authorized Lamborghini technicians is paramount for ensuring optimal performance and longevity. Overall, reliability is expected to be strong, but owners must embrace the premium service requirements associated with such a cutting-edge vehicle.
