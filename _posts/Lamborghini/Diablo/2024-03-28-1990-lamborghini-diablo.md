**Introduction**

The 1990 Lamborghini Diablo is an iconic supercar powered by a naturally aspirated V12 engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. It boasts an impressive 485 horsepower and 428 lb-ft of torque. The Diablo is known for its aggressive styling, blistering performance, and luxurious interior. However, like any performance vehicle, it has its fair share of potential issues.

**Engine**

* **V12 Engine**
    * **Valve Guide Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn valve guides cause excessive oil consumption, resulting in blue smoke from the exhaust.
        * Solution: Replace valve guides and seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * **Timing Belt Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A failed timing belt can cause catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace timing belt and tensioners according to maintenance schedule.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from gaskets, seals, or hoses.
        * Solution: Replace or tighten gaskets, seals, or hoses.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Difficulty Shifting Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gears may be difficult to engage or pop out of gear.
        * Solution: Replace or repair shifter linkage, worn synchronizers, or damaged gears.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Strut Mount Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn strut mounts can cause excessive noise, vibration, and poor handling.
        * Solution: Replace worn strut mounts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Shock Absorber Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failed shock absorbers cause a bouncy ride and poor handling.
        * Solution: Replace shock absorbers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A failing alternator can cause electrical system problems, including dimming lights and difficulty starting.
        * Solution: Replace alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Electrical Gremlins**
        * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, such as malfunctioning gauges or power window failures.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair specific electrical components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Bulbs Burn Out Frequently**
        * Description/Symptoms: Headlight bulbs may burn out prematurely.
        * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs with higher-quality ones or upgrade the headlight wiring.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system may not function properly.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace HVAC components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**

While the Lamborghini Diablo is a desirable and thrilling supercar, it has its share of potential issues. Some of these issues can be severe and expensive to fix, such as a catastrophic engine failure due to timing belt failure. Other issues, such as electrical gremlins or headlight bulb burnouts, are less severe but can still be frustrating for owners. Overall, it is essential to factor in the potential costs of maintenance and repairs when considering purchasing a used Diablo.
