**1992 Lamborghini Diablo**

The 1992 Lamborghini Diablo is a mid-engine sports car produced by Lamborghini from 1990 to 2001. It was Lamborghini's flagship model during its production run and is considered one of the most iconic supercars of all time. The Diablo was available with two different engine options: a 5.7-liter V12 and a 6.0-liter V12. Both engines were paired with a 5-speed manual transmission.

The Diablo was praised for its stunning design, powerful performance, and handling. However, it was also criticized for its high price, lack of comfort, and poor reliability.

**Engine**

**5.7-Liter V12**

- **Valve Timing Issue**
  - Description/Symptoms: The engine may experience a loss of power, rough idling, and increased emissions.
  - Solution: Replace the timing belt and tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Head Gasket Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: The engine may overheat, lose coolant, and experience a loss of compression.
  - Solution: Replace the head gasket.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Connecting Rod Bearing Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: The engine may experience a knocking noise, loss of power, and eventually catastrophic failure.
  - Solution: Replace the connecting rod bearings.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $7,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**6.0-Liter V12**

- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

- **Clutch Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: The clutch may slip, chatter, or become difficult to engage.
  - Solution: Replace the clutch disk, pressure plate, and release bearing.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Transmission Gear Grinding**
  - Description/Symptoms: The transmission may grind when shifting gears.
  - Solution: Replace the worn gears or synchronizers.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: The battery may fail to charge, causing the vehicle to stall.
  - Solution: Replace the alternator.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Starter Motor Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may fail to start.
  - Solution: Replace the starter motor.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Window Regulator Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: The power windows may fail to operate properly.
  - Solution: Replace the window regulator motor or switch.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 1992 Lamborghini Diablo is a high-performance supercar with a stunning design and impressive performance. However, it is also known for its high price, lack of comfort, and poor reliability.

The most common issues with the Diablo include engine valve timing issues, head gasket failures, clutch failure, and electrical problems. These issues can be expensive to repair, so potential buyers should be aware of them before making a purchase.
