# 2022 Lamborghini Urus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2022 Lamborghini Urus, buyers can expect ultra-luxury high performance backed by a twin-turbo V8 powertrain. While these exotic super-SUVs deliver blistering speed and striking styling, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate service history and electronics operation during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces roughly 641 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering immense acceleration. Routine maintenance and thorough electrical inspections are crucial for long-term reliability at this performance tier.

**Cold Start Ignition Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that on cold starts, pressing the brake and start button may fail to crank the engine, sometimes requiring multiple attempts before starting.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the starter, ignition switch, and electrical charging system.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The responsive 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission smoothly handles high torque outputs across all driving modes. It generally operates reliably if routine fluid changes are maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V804000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The infotainment system may become damaged, which can result in an inoperative rearview camera display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the infotainment unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V903000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Lamborghini Urus combines supercar performance with everyday SUV usability, though ownership demands robust maintenance budgets. Buyers should ensure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection is performed, paying close attention to engine starting behavior and onboard electronics.
