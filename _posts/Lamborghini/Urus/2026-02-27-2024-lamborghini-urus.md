# 2024 Lamborghini Urus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Lamborghini Urus represents the pinnacle of high-performance luxury SUVs, blending exotic supercar DNA with everyday versatility. Powered by a potent twin-turbocharged V8 engine paired with a responsive automatic transmission, it delivers breathtaking acceleration and composed handling. As a relatively new and ultra-luxury model on the pre-owned market, prospective buyers should focus on proper maintenance history and professional pre-purchase inspections to ensure optimal mechanical health.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine pushes out roughly 657 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque in standard and S variants. It provides ferocious acceleration and robust everyday performance, though buyers should expect high maintenance costs typical of exotic Italian powertrains.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast shifts while maintaining smooth operation during routine driving. It routes power seamlessly to the all-wheel-drive system, exhibiting strong reliability when properly serviced.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V348000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hood:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A hood that opens while driving can detach and become a road hazard, or obstruct the driver's view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the hood, and install a striker support plate if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V903000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
