# 2023 Lamborghini Urus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Lamborghini Urus, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury SUV sharing its foundational platform with other high-end VW Group offerings. While owner reports for this model year are very sparse, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate exterior paint and structural finishes during pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces roughly 641 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. It provides robust performance, though buyers should expect high maintenance and fueling costs typical of exotic super-SUVs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission handles power delivery seamlessly across all-wheel-drive configurations. It offers crisp shifts suited for both relaxed cruising and aggressive track driving.

## Exterior

### Body Panels and Paint Finish

Issues involving exterior finish quality and body panels require careful visual inspection.

**Paint Blistering and Bubbling**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported noticing air bubbles forming beneath the painted exterior surfaces.
- **Solution:** Consult a qualified body shop or dealer to inspect paint integrity and address any underlying factory finish flaws.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V348000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hood:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A hood that opens while driving can detach and become a road hazard, or obstruct the driver's view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the hood, and install a striker support plate if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V903000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Lamborghini Urus remains a formidable and exotic choice in the luxury performance SUV segment, backed by robust powertrain options. Because available owner complaint data is extremely limited, buyers should prioritize a thorough professional inspection, verify service history, and check for any outstanding manufacturer recalls before finalizing a purchase.
