# 2020 Lamborghini Urus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lamborghini Urus blends supercar performance with everyday SUV practicality, utilizing a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As a high-performance luxury vehicle, prospective used-car buyers should anticipate significant maintenance and running costs. While formal owner complaints are relatively scarce for this model year, careful pre-purchase inspections are strongly recommended given the complexity of its powertrain and onboard electronics.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 641 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It delivers explosive acceleration and robust performance, though regular high-tier maintenance is critical to avoid costly mechanical complications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides rapid, smooth gear changes suited for both aggressive driving and relaxed cruising. It pairs smoothly with the all-wheel-drive system and generally proves durable when fluid changes are maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V713000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel line and quick connector, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V903000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lamborghini Urus is an exhilarating luxury performance SUV that demands meticulous upkeep. Buyers should secure a comprehensive inspection from a qualified specialist before purchasing to ensure all mechanical and electrical systems are in optimal condition.
