# 2019 Lamborghini Urus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Lamborghini Urus combines supercar performance with the practical layout of a luxury SUV, driven by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As an exotic high-performance vehicle, prospective buyers should approach the used market with an understanding that specialized maintenance and specialized care are essential. While major recurring defect trends are limited in owner reports, monitoring powertrain health and complex onboard electronics is vital for a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The Urus features a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing approximately 641 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It delivers blistering acceleration and robust power, though buyers should verify complete service history given the high-stress nature of turbocharged exotic engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

Power is routed through an 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission paired with an all-wheel-drive system. It offers crisp, rapid shifts suited for track-capable performance as well as smooth daily cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V713000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Lamborghini will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel line and quick connector, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V903000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Lamborghini Urus remains an imposing and dynamic choice for buyers seeking elite performance paired with everyday SUV utility. Because extensive owner complaint datasets are relatively sparse, thorough pre-purchase inspections by a certified technician familiar with exotic platforms are strongly recommended to ensure long-term peace of mind.
