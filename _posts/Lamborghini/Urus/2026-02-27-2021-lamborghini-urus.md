# 2021 Lamborghini Urus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Lamborghini Urus combines supercar performance with the practical utility of a luxury SUV. For used-car buyers looking at this high-performance Italian machine, understanding its foundational powertrain and potential minor quirks is essential before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

Features a 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 641 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. It delivers explosive acceleration and robust performance, though it requires meticulous maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

Equipped with an 8-speed ZF automatic transmission paired with an all-wheel-drive system. It provides rapid, smooth gear changes suited for both daily driving and aggressive track use.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V603000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, and replace as necessary, the affected seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V336000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Cracks may cause engine damage and a stall or an oil leak, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V903000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Urus&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the parking assist software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Lamborghini Urus is a formidable luxury super-SUV backed by robust powertrain options. Prospective buyers should always conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist to ensure proper maintenance history and overall mechanical health.
