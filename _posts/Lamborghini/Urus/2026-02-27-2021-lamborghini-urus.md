### Overview
The 2021 Lamborghini Urus, a high-performance luxury SUV, generally exhibits strong mechanical and electrical reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. Owners typically report satisfaction with its robust powertrain and sophisticated systems. However, as with any complex vehicle, specific components can occasionally present challenges, often related to advanced technology or high-stress operation. This analysis focuses on documented, non-routine issues that may arise, excluding standard maintenance or expected wear and tear.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light illuminated, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, connectivity issues (Bluetooth, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto), navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning light, dead battery after short periods of inactivity.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, potential replacement of battery or faulty module causing drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Lamborghini Urus demonstrates a high level of reliability for a performance-oriented luxury SUV. The powertrain, including the 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the adaptive air suspension, which can occasionally develop leaks, and the sophisticated electrical system, particularly the infotainment unit which may experience software glitches. Battery drain issues, though not common, can also occur. Overall, the Urus offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential issues being manageable and typical for vehicles with advanced technology and high performance.
