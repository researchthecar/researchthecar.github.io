# 2018 Lamborghini Huracan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Lamborghini Huracan is an exotic supercar offering high-revving performance and striking design. While exotic vehicles require specialized maintenance and meticulous care, owners enjoy a remarkably visceral driving experience backed by a robust V10 powertrain.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10

The 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 produces approximately 573 to 631 horsepower depending on the specific variant, delivering blistering acceleration and an iconic exhaust note. This powerplant is generally robust and mechanically durable when subjected to proper routine servicing and warm-up cycles.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery across all driving modes. Buyers should verify clutch wear and fluid maintenance history, as servicing exotic dual-clutch units can be costly.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V466000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In temperatures below 41°F, the rearview camera may not display due to a software error in the infotainment system. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V081000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Improperly adjusted headlights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install the missing blanking caps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V826000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software to deactivate the headlight functions for Tourist Mode and the Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2018 Lamborghini Huracan, prospective buyers should prioritize comprehensive service history records and a pre-purchase inspection by a qualified exotic specialist. While routine operating and maintenance costs are high, the core mechanical engineering is solid.
