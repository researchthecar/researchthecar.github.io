### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Lamborghini Huracan demonstrates strong mechanical reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components, the 5.2L V10 engine and the dual-clutch transmission. Issues are generally infrequent and tend to be concentrated in the sophisticated electrical systems, such as infotainment glitches and lighting control module malfunctions. These are not uncommon for high-performance vehicles with advanced electronics. Suspension and exterior/interior components are largely robust. While the Huracan is a reliable supercar, owners should budget for potential, albeit rare, electrical repairs. Overall, it offers a dependable platform for exhilarating driving experiences, provided regular specialized maintenance is performed.
