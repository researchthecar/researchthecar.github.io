# 2020 Lamborghini Huracan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Lamborghini Huracán delivers breathtaking exotic performance backed by naturally aspirated V10 power and lightning-fast dual-clutch transmissions. While prospective buyers are primarily focused on high-speed capability and exotic styling, everyday usability and maintenance costs remain critical considerations. The available data shows minimal structural complaints for this model year, reflecting its high-end engineering pedigree, though routine inspections and careful pre-purchase evaluations are always recommended for high-performance supercars.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L Naturally Aspirated V10

This high-revving 5.2-liter V10 engine produces around 602 to 631 horsepower depending on the specific variant and delivers ferocious acceleration. It is generally robust and well-regarded for its mechanical durability, though it demands meticulous maintenance and premium fluids.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides rapid gear changes and seamless power delivery across all driving modes. It operates reliably under spirited driving conditions, provided the clutch system and transmission fluids are serviced according to factory schedules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V466000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In temperatures below 41°F, the rearview camera may not display due to a software error in the infotainment system. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not display an image reduces the driver's rear view, increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V081000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Improperly adjusted headlights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install the missing blanking caps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V220000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Fluid/Lubricant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Clutch slippage can cause a loss of drive power and increase the risk of a crash. A transmission oil leak in the presence of hot exhaust components can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the transmission oil level, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V826000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software to deactivate the headlight functions for Tourist Mode and the Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Lamborghini Huracán represents the pinnacle of modern supercar engineering, combining aggressive performance with impressive core mechanical stability. With virtually no widespread reliability complaints reported for this model year, it stands out as a solid option among exotic sports cars. Buyers should still prioritize a thorough professional inspection and verify comprehensive service history before purchasing.
