# 2017 Lamborghini Huracan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Lamborghini Huracan is an exotic high-performance sports car celebrated for its thrilling naturally aspirated V10 performance and exotic styling. For used-car buyers entering the supercar market, understanding routine maintenance, potential component quirks, and mechanical health is essential before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10 Engine

The 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine produces approximately 580 to 610 horsepower depending on the specific variant. It is generally robust and renowned for its high-revving character, though proper maintenance and regular fluid services are vital for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear shifts and power delivery to the all-wheel-drive or rear-wheel-drive system. Buyers should inspect clutch wear levels and ensure smooth shifting behavior during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V081000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Improperly adjusted headlights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install the missing blanking caps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V826000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software to deactivate the headlight functions for Tourist Mode and the Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Lamborghini Huracan offers exhilarating exotic performance backed by strong engineering foundations. Prospective buyers should always prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified exotic vehicle specialist to verify mechanical and electronic health.
