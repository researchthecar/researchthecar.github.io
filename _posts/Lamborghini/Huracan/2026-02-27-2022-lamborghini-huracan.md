# 2022 Lamborghini Huracan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Lamborghini Huracan represents the pinnacle of naturally aspirated V10 performance, offering exotic styling and breathtaking driving dynamics for prospective buyers. As a high-end supercar, researching historical owner feedback provides valuable context regarding potential maintenance considerations before making a purchase. While formal complaint data for this model year is minimal, understanding its engineering and typical supercar ownership traits helps ensure a confident buying experience.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L Naturally Aspirated V10

The 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine produces upwards of 600 to 630 horsepower depending on the specific trim level, delivering blistering acceleration and a signature exhaust note. This power plant is generally robust when subjected to proper routine maintenance, though prospective buyers should anticipate premium servicing costs typical of exotic Italian sports cars.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery in both automatic and manual paddle-shift modes. It is well-matched to the high-revving V10 engine, offering reliable performance if fluid changes and clutch wear are monitored according to factory schedules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V220000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Fluid/Lubricant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Clutch slippage can cause a loss of drive power and increase the risk of a crash. A transmission oil leak in the presence of hot exhaust components can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and correct the transmission oil level, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V422000: Structure:Body:Door:Handle:Exterior](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The exterior driver-side door handle may not be assembled properly, which can prevent the opening of the driver-side door from the outside. A person may become trapped which, in the event of an emergency, may increase their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair the driver-side door handle, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V826000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software to deactivate the headlight functions for Tourist Mode and the Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
