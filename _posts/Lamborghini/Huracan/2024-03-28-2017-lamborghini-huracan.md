**Introduction:**

The 2017 Lamborghini Huracan is an Italian supercar known for its sleek design and powerful performance. It features a naturally aspirated V10 engine paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, delivering 602 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. The Huracan is praised for its thrilling driving experience, handling capabilities, and exotic styling, but it has also faced several issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

**V10 5.2L**

* **Valve Timing Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or rough running, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replace faulty solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall #22V-059

* **Oil Leak from Oil Filter Housing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil dripping from engine compartment, low oil levels
    * Solution: Replace faulty oil filter housing seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch**

* **Clutch Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, shuddering during shifts
    * Solution: Replace worn clutch components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall #22V-059

* **Transmission Control Module Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission warning lights, erratic shifting patterns
    * Solution: Reprogram or replace TCM
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride quality, bouncing or uneven handling
    * Solution: Replace faulty shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

* **Squeaky Suspension:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises from suspension components
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace worn parts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, inability to start vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Gremlins:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues with lights, electronics, or sensors
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair specific issues
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors locking or unlocking spontaneously
    * Solution: Replace faulty door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace or seal headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500

**Interior**

* **Instrument Cluster Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges or displays not functioning properly
    * Solution: Replace faulty instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500

* **Navigation System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezing, losing GPS signal
    * Solution: Update or replace navigation software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Summary:**

The 2017 Lamborghini Huracan has experienced various issues across its major components, but these issues vary in severity and cost. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, while suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior issues tend to be less expensive. It's important for Huracan owners to be aware of these potential issues and budget accordingly for maintenance and repairs.
