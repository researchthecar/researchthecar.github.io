# 2016 Lamborghini Huracan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Lamborghini Huracan is an exotic supercar offering high performance and striking design, powered by a naturally aspirated V10 engine matched to a dual-clutch transmission. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating a high-end vehicle of this caliber involves looking closely at overall maintenance history, professional inspections, and proper upkeep rather than relying strictly on widespread owner complaint trends.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10 Engine

This vehicle features a 5.2-liter naturally aspirated V10 engine producing approximately 602 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance and high-revving character, though routine maintenance costs remain exceptionally high.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear shifts across all-wheel-drive trims. Buyers should verify clutch wear and fluid service history during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V081000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Improperly adjusted headlights can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install the missing blanking caps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V826000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software to deactivate the headlight functions for Tourist Mode and the Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Lamborghini Huracan is an uncompromising exotic supercar defined by its vocal V10 engine and rapid dual-clutch transmission. While standard owner complaint data is sparse, prospective buyers should budget carefully for specialized maintenance, insurance, and professional pre-purchase inspections.
