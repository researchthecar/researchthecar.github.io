# 2023 Lamborghini Huracan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a modern exotic like the 2023 Lamborghini Huracan means balancing exhilarating performance with high-end maintenance realities. While owner reports are limited, buyers should carefully review electrical systems and dealer service histories when considering a used model.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L V10

The 2023 Lamborghini Huracan features a naturally aspirated 5.2-liter V10 engine, producing approximately 631 horsepower depending on the specific trim. This high-revving powerhouse is renowned for its thrilling performance and character, though specialized maintenance is required.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Power is routed through a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission featuring paddle shifters and all-wheel or rear-wheel drive depending on the variant. It delivers lightning-fast gear changes suited for both track and street driving.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster

Owner reports indicate potential vulnerability in digital display hardware and associated vehicle software.

**Instrument Cluster Failure**
- **Symptoms:** While driving on the highway, the entire digital instrument cluster may go completely black, causing a loss of speed and gauge visibility.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a dealer or qualified specialist to diagnose electrical faults, module failures, or software glitches.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V826000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Lamborghini&model=Huracan&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Incorrectly adjusted headlights can reduce the visibility of oncoming traffic, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software to deactivate the headlight functions for Tourist Mode and the Adaptive Front-lighting System (AFS), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Lamborghini Huracan remains an iconic supercar choice for enthusiasts seeking uncompromising V10 performance. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and confirm remaining warranty coverage.
