## 2001 Mazda Millenia

The 2001 Mazda Millenia is a midsize luxury sedan that was introduced in 1995. It was available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including:

* 2.3L V6 engine with 170 horsepower and 160 lb-ft of torque
* 2.5L V6 engine with 170 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque
* 3.0L V6 engine with 215 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque
* 5-speed manual transmission
* 4-speed automatic transmission

The Millenia was praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it was also criticized for its high price and lack of features.

**Engine**

**2.3L V6 Engine**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the intake manifold, engine overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L V6 Engine**

* **Timing belt failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, catastrophic engine damage if the timing belt breaks
    * Solution: Replace the timing belt and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6 Engine**

* **Valve cover gasket leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gaskets, burning oil smell, misfires
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping gears, difficulty shifting, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

* **Strut mounts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling sound from the front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Rear Suspension**

* **Rear shock absorbers**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking shock absorbers, bouncy or unstable rear end
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting the engine, electrical system failure
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on the body panels, especially around the wheel arches
    * Solution: Repair or replace the affected body panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Power window failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working, stuck windows
    * Solution: Replace the power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary**

The 2001 Mazda Millenia is a reliable and well-built vehicle. However, it is not without its problems. Some of the most common issues include intake manifold gasket failure, timing belt failure, and transmission slipping. The severity of these issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. Overall, the Millenia is a good choice for those looking for a comfortable and stylish midsize sedan, but it is important to be aware of its potential problems before buying one.
