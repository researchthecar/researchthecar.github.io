### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mazda 2 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its powertrain and core mechanical systems demonstrating exceptional durability. The most frequently noted, albeit minor, issues tend to be related to the infotainment system's software, which can occasionally exhibit glitches. These are typically resolved with software updates or resets and do not represent a significant threat to the vehicle's overall functionality or longevity. For a subcompact car, the 2022 model offers a commendable balance of driving pleasure and dependable performance, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious and discerning buyers alike.
