- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light illumination, difficulty starting.

### Summary Verdict
The 2011 Mazda 2 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The 1.5L engine and both transmission options (manual and automatic) are robust and rarely present significant issues beyond normal maintenance. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are within the electrical system, specifically with power window motors and the alternator. Suspension and exterior/interior components are typically durable for the vehicle's age and class. Overall, the 2011 Mazda 2 is a dependable choice, with potential issues being relatively minor and manageable.
