### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mazda 2 stands out as a reliable subcompact car, with its powertrain and chassis components demonstrating excellent durability. The most frequently cited, albeit uncommon, issues relate to the infotainment system, which can experience occasional software glitches requiring updates or resets. Battery longevity is generally good, but rare instances of premature failure have been noted. Overall, the Mazda 2 offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential issues being minor and easily rectified, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious buyers prioritizing reliability.
