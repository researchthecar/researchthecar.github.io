### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mazda 3 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its Skyactiv-G engines and Skyactiv-Drive transmissions demonstrating excellent durability. The majority of reported issues are minor and infrequent, such as occasional infotainment system glitches or minor paint chipping, which are not indicative of systemic problems. Mazda's engineering prowess is evident in the robust build quality and the absence of widespread mechanical failures across all engine and transmission options. Owners can generally expect a dependable and enjoyable driving experience with minimal unexpected repair costs beyond standard maintenance.
