## **2023 Mazda MX-30 EV**

The 2023 Mazda MX-30 EV is an all-electric compact crossover SUV. It is powered by a single electric motor that produces 143 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque. The MX-30 EV has an estimated range of 100 miles on a full charge.

People like the MX-30 EV for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and engaging driving dynamics. However, some people have complained about its limited range and lack of all-wheel drive.
