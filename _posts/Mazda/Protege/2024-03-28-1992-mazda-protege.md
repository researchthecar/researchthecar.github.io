**Introduction:**

The 1992 Mazda Protege was a compact car available with three engine options: a 1.6L inline-four with 103 horsepower and 97 lb-ft of torque, a 1.8L inline-four with 125 horsepower and 127 lb-ft of torque, and a 1.8L V6 with 130 horsepower and 135 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic. The Protege was known for its sporty handling, responsive steering, and relatively low price point. However, it was criticized for its lack of power, poor fuel economy, and cramped interior.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-Four**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant and oil leaking from the intake manifold, causing overheating and engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold gasket.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
* **Recalls:** None Found

**1.8L Inline-Four**

**Timing Belt Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** The timing belt can break or skip, resulting in catastrophic engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replace the timing belt and water pump according to the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**1.8L V6**

**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Oil leak from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning smell and possible engine damage.
* **Solution:** Replace the valve cover gasket.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

**Clutch Master Cylinder Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Inability to shift gears, clutch pedal feels spongy or sinks to the floor.
* **Solution:** Replace the clutch master cylinder.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
* **Recalls:** N/A

**4-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slippage**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, delayed engagement, and slipping between gears.
* **Solution:** Rebuild or replace the transmission.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Electrical system failure, battery not charging.
* **Solution:** Replace the alternator.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Inability to lock or unlock doors using the power locks.
* **Solution:** Replace the door lock actuator.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**Window Switch Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Inability to operate the power windows.
* **Solution:** Replace the window switch.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 1992 Mazda Protege has a number of potential issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. While the cost of repairs for some issues is relatively low, major issues such as transmission slippage and timing belt failure can be expensive to fix. Overall, the severity and cost of potential issues should be considered when purchasing or owning a Protege.
