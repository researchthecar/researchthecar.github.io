# 2000 Mazda Protege

### Overview
The 2000 Mazda Protege, a compact car known for its sporty handling and affordability, generally presented a decent reliability record for its era. While not without its quirks, many owners found it to be a dependable daily driver. Common areas of concern tended to be related to age and mileage, particularly with certain powertrain components and electrical systems. However, for its time, the Protege offered a good balance of performance and practicality, making it a popular choice for budget-conscious buyers. Regular maintenance was key to ensuring its longevity, as with most vehicles of this vintage.

**No Major Issues**

### 2.0L DOHC (FS-DE)
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** N/A


### 4-Speed Automatic
**Slipping Gears**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation during shifts, engine revs without acceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, potential internal repairs or rebuild.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings, sway bar links, or bushings.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


## Electrical

### Wiring Harness & Components
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Power Window Motors**
- **Symptoms:** Windows not operating, slow movement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of individual power window motors or regulator assemblies.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350 per window


## Exterior

### Body Panels & Paint
**Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion on wheel arches, rocker panels, and door bottoms.
- **Solution:** Rust repair and repainting, rustproofing treatments.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2000 Mazda Protege is a reasonably reliable compact car, particularly with its manual transmission and 1.6L engine. Owners should be aware of potential issues with automatic transmissions, oil leaks on the 2.0L engine, and age-related wear on suspension components and electrical parts like alternators and power window motors. Rust can also be a concern in certain climates. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing common wear items, the Protege can remain a dependable vehicle, offering good value for its age.
