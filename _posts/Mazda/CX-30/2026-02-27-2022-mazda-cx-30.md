# 2022 Mazda CX-30 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mazda CX-30 delivers upscale interior appointments, nimble handling, and standard all-wheel drive, making it a popular subcompact crossover choice for used-car buyers. While it generally offers solid mechanical foundations, owner reports highlight a few recurring concerns regarding electrical glitches, safety feature warnings, and premature component wear. Prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, focusing on electronics functionality and steering or suspension smoothness during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

The standard naturally aspirated 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine produces 186 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, offering adequate daily acceleration. It is generally regarded as reliable, though occasional sensor or pressure warnings have been noted by owners.

**Low Oil Pressure Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an unexpected low oil pressure warning light illuminated on the dashboard while driving at highway speeds, even with regular maintenance intervals.
- **Solution:** Check oil levels immediately, inspect for leaks, and have the pressure sensor and oil pump evaluated by a qualified mechanic.

### 2.5L Turbo SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

The available turbocharged 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine generates up to 250 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque on premium fuel, providing much quicker acceleration and passing power. It pairs smoothly with the standard all-wheel-drive system.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The traditional 6-speed automatic transmission avoids the droning and reliability worries of some competing CVTs, delivering crisp and responsive shifts across all trims. It is generally considered a dependable powertrain component.

## Suspension

### Wheel Bearings

Issues related to premature wheel bearing wear can impact steering feel and create noticeable cabin noise.

**Premature Wheel Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a humming, grinding noise, steering wheel vibration, or slight steering pull while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the affected wheel bearing inspected and replaced if worn.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Connectivity

Electrical and infotainment glitches, including backup camera failures and connectivity errors, are among the more frequent owner grievances.

**Infotainment System Glitches and Bricking**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen or Connect system may become unresponsive, freeze, or fail entirely following battery discharge or system errors.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reset, update software at a dealership, or replace the battery if it fails to hold a proper charge.

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Exterior glass reports indicate occasional optical distortions in factory windshields that can cause driver discomfort.

**Distorted or Wavy Windshield**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience visual distortion, double vision, or eye strain when looking through certain areas of the front windshield.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield for optical defects and have it replaced if distortion impairs safe visibility.

## Safety

### Lane Keep Assist and Forward Collision

Advanced safety systems generally assist the driver well, but occasional software glitches can cause erratic steering corrections.

**Steering Assist and Lane Keep Irregularities**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that lane assist features can pull aggressively or make steering difficult if road markings are faded or uneven.
- **Solution:** Adjust driver assistance settings through the infotainment menu or have system sensors recalibrated by a professional.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V275000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-30&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Reduced braking ability can increase the distance required to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the ABS HCU, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mazda CX-30 is an appealing used crossover that offers a premium feel and engaging driving dynamics. While most mechanical systems prove robust, buyers should carefully test all electronics, safety features, infotainment functions, and steering responsiveness during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything operates as intended.
