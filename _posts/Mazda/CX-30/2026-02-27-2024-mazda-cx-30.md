# 2024 Mazda CX-30 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Mazda CX-30, buyers will find a stylish, tech-forward subcompact crossover that largely impresses with its refined driving dynamics and upscale interior. However, as with any modern vehicle packed with driver-assistance electronics and advanced safety features, owners have flagged a handful of recurring electronic, sensor, and structural concerns. Reviewing owner feedback and remaining factory coverage can help prospective buyers spot potential issues before bringing one home.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces 191 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate everyday power for daily commutes. It is generally regarded as a reliable, straightforward powerplant with few mechanical complaints.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 2.5-liter turbocharged engine bumps output up to 250 horsepower (on premium fuel), offering lively acceleration and robust passing power. Like its naturally aspirated counterpart, it maintains a solid reputation for mechanical durability.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped across all trims paired with standard i-Activ all-wheel drive, the traditional 6-speed automatic transmission trades endless gear-ratios for smooth, predictable shifting. It avoids many of the common reliability pitfalls plaguing modern CVTs and dual-clutch units.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Cabin Electronics

Cabin electronics manage most climate and entertainment functions, but complex software can occasionally require resets or updates to resolve glitches.

**Keyless Entry and Electrical Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may encounter unexpected error messages such as keyless entry system malfunctions or intermittent loss of cabin function controls.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates at a certified service center or check vehicle battery health and key fob signal integrity.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim are generally well-assembled, though rare reports of glass failure require careful inspection of the roof panel.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass sunroof may suddenly fracture or explode outward while driving under normal conditions without external impact or debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass assembly for stress points and have the panel replaced under warranty or comprehensive insurance.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Radar

Advanced safety systems add great peace of mind, though sensitive radar and camera hardware can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected braking inputs.

**Unintended Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally apply the brakes aggressively or turn on automatic braking while accelerating or cruising when no visible obstacles are present.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership inspect the front radar sensors, camera alignment, and software calibration to ensure proper operation.

**Front Radar Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** An i-ACTIVSENSE partially disabled front radar malfunction warning message illuminates on the instrument cluster while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Clean any debris or blockage from the front emblem/sensor area and consult a dealer for diagnostic evaluation if the warning persists.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V649000: Lane Departure: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-30&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Automatic braking, steering assist, and headlight control systems that do not function as intended can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the forward sensing camera as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V357000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-30&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Air bags that do not deploy in the event of a crash increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the SAS unit with improved software or replace the unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mazda CX-30 stands out as a sophisticated and generally dependable choice in the subcompact crossover segment, pairing robust engine choices with a conventional automatic transmission. While owner complaints highlight occasional issues with driver-assistance radar sensors and isolated electrical or sunroof concerns, most foundational mechanical systems remain sound. Buyers should verify all electronics and safety tech function properly during a test drive and check for remaining factory warranty coverage.
