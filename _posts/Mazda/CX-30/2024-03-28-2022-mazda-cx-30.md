**Introduction**

The 2022 Mazda CX-30 is a compact SUV that offers a refined driving experience, premium interior appointments, and advanced safety features. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a six-speed automatic transmission, producing 186 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque. The CX-30 is praised for its agile handling, stylish design, and comfortable cabin. However, it has received some criticism for its limited cargo space and lack of advanced driver-assistance features as standard.

**Engine**

* **G25T 2.5L SkyActiv-G I4**

    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning fuel injectors can cause misfiring, rough idling, stalling, and reduced engine power.
        * Solution: Replace affected fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Exhaust Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Stuck exhaust valves can lead to poor engine performance, decreased fuel economy, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Clean or replace affected exhaust valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **AW6A-EL FWD 6-Speed Automatic**

    * **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loose gaskets or seals can cause transmission fluid to leak, leading to slipping gears, delayed shifts, and whining noises.
        * Solution: Replace or reseal affected gaskets or seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery may discharge prematurely due to parasitic drain from faulty electrical components.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty components or replace battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Washer Fluid Reservoir Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the windshield washer fluid reservoir can cause leaks and reduced wiper fluid capacity.
    * Solution: Replace affected reservoir.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system may experience occasional lag or freezing during operation.
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Button Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control buttons may become unresponsive or malfunction.
    * Solution: Replace affected climate control panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Mazda CX-30 has a few reported issues that are primarily related to the engine, transmission, electrical system, and certain interior components. The severity of these issues varies, but most are relatively minor and can be addressed with routine maintenance or repairs. The estimated cost to fix these issues ranges from minimal to moderate, depending on the specific problem and parts required.
