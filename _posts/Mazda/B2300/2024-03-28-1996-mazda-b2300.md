**Introduction**

The 1996 Mazda B2300 is a compact pickup truck available in regular and extended cab configurations. It is powered by a 2.3-liter inline-four engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The B2300 is known for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and affordable price. However, it has some common issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

**2.3L Inline-Four**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Symptoms include coolant or oil leaks, overheating, and rough idling. Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost: $200 - $400
* **Timing belt failure:** Symptoms include engine failure or a noisy engine. Solution: Replace the timing belt. Estimated Cost: $400 - $600+
* **Crankshaft position sensor failure:** Symptoms include intermittent starting problems or engine stalling. Solution: Replace the crankshaft position sensor. Estimated Cost: $100 - $200+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Shifting problems:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears or gears grinding. Solution: Replace the transmission shift linkage or rebuild the transmission. Estimated Cost: $200 - $800+

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping transmission:** Symptoms include difficulty accelerating or shifting gears. Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission. Estimated Cost: $800 - $1500+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension ball joint failure:** Symptoms include clunking or popping noises when turning or driving over bumps. Solution: Replace the ball joints. Estimated Cost: $200 - $400+
* **Rear suspension leaf spring failure:** Symptoms include sagging in the rear of the truck or a rough ride. Solution: Replace the leaf springs. Estimated Cost: $300 - $500+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** Symptoms include dim headlights or a dead battery. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost: $300 - $500+
* **Starter failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the engine or a grinding noise when starting. Solution: Replace the starter. Estimated Cost: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Rust on the frame and body:** Symptoms include visible rust or holes in the frame or body panels. Solution: Repair or replace the rusted areas. Estimated Cost: $500 - $2000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking:** Symptoms include cracks or breaks in the dashboard. Solution: Replace the dashboard. Estimated Cost: $500 - $1000+
* **Plastic trim pieces breaking:** Symptoms include broken or loose plastic trim pieces in the interior. Solution: Replace the broken trim pieces. Estimated Cost: $50 - $200+

**Summary**

The 1996 Mazda B2300 is a generally reliable and affordable compact pickup truck. However, it has some common issues that can be expensive to repair, such as timing belt failure, transmission problems, and rust. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their purchase decision.
