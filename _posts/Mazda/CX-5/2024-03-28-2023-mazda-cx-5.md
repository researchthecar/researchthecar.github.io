**Introduction:**

The 2023 Mazda CX-5 is a popular compact crossover SUV known for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and reliable performance. It offers two engine options and two transmission options. The base engine is a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated inline-4 with 191 horsepower and 187 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The optional engine is a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 with 256 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque, mated to a six-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Naturally Aspirated Inline-4**

* **Carbon Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, decreased fuel economy
    * Solution: Clean carbon deposits from intake valves and combustion chamber
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200-$1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-liter Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, excessive smoke from exhaust, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000-$3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Knock Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace knock sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, transmission slipping, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000-$1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace front lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after sitting, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and fix electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Variable depending on source of drain
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight lens
    * Solution: Seal headlight housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sudden cracks appearing on windshield
    * Solution: Replace windshield
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400-$800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty adjusting temperature, air conditioning not working
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400-$800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Mazda CX-5 has a few reported issues, mainly affecting the engine, transmission, and electrical components. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and require significant repairs. Overall, the CX-5 remains a reliable and well-received vehicle, but prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
