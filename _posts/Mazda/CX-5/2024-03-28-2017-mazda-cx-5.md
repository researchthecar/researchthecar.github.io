## 2017 Mazda CX-5

The 2017 Mazda CX-5 is a compact SUV available with two engine options and two transmission options. The 2.0-liter SKYACTIV-G engine produces 155 horsepower and 150 lb.-ft of torque, while the 2.5-liter SKYACTIV-G engine produces 187 horsepower and 186 lb.-ft of torque. Both engines can be paired with a six-speed automatic transmission or a six-speed manual transmission.

The CX-5 is praised for its stylish design, high-quality interior, and fun-to-drive nature. However, it is criticized for its small cargo capacity and lack of some advanced safety features.

### Engine
**2.0-liter SKYACTIV-G**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause reduced engine power, fuel economy, and responsiveness.
    * Solution: Cleaning the intake valves and ports.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty fuel injectors can cause engine misfires, rough idling, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
* **Timing Chain Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A noisy timing chain can indicate excessive wear or stretching, leading to engine damage if left unaddressed.
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**2.5-liter SKYACTIV-G**

* **No Issues Found**

### Transmission
**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitation or Delay**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears, causing rough driving and loss of power.
    * Solution: Updating the transmission control software or replacing the transmission solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the transmission fluid can lead to low fluid levels, causing transmission damage if not addressed promptly.
    * Solution: Replacing the leaking seal or gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

### Suspension

* **Struts and Shock Absorbers**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn-out struts or shock absorbers can cause rough ride quality, poor handling, and premature tire wear.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected struts or shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+ for all four
* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn-out lower control arm bushings can cause excessive tire wear, misalignment, and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replacing the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: A parasitic drain on the battery can lead to premature battery discharge, making it difficult to start the vehicle.
    * Solution: Isolating and eliminating the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Infotainment System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctions in the infotainment system can include unresponsive touchscreen, audio problems, or navigation glitches.
    * Solution: Updating the software or replacing the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA ID: 21V286000 - Potential for Engine Control Module (ECM) Failure

### Exterior

* **Windshield Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield can crack or chip more easily than expected.
    * Solution: Replacing the windshield.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
* **Paint Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Minor paint defects, such as flaking or bubbling, have been reported in some cases.
    * Solution: Repairing or repainting the affected area.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

### Interior

* **Plastic Trim Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some interior plastic trim pieces may rattle or vibrate during driving.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing the affected trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
* **Seat Belt Buckle Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat belt buckles may malfunction or become difficult to unlatch.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected seat belt buckle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

### Summary

The 2017 Mazda CX-5 is a reliable and fun-to-drive vehicle. However, it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. These issues range in severity and cost, but many can be easily addressed through regular maintenance and repairs. Overall, the CX-5 is a solid choice for those seeking a compact SUV that combines style, performance, and practicality.
