# 2022 Mazda CX-5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mazda CX-5 is widely praised for its upscale interior, engaging handling, and sleek styling. However, used car buyers should review common owner-reported trouble spots, particularly concerning exterior lighting assemblies, electrical accessories, and occasional reports of body glass issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

A 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing approximately 187 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque. It offers dependable daily performance and respectable fuel efficiency for routine commuting.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

A 2.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generating up to 250 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque on premium fuel. This option provides brisk acceleration and robust passing power for highway driving.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with standard all-wheel drive across all trims. It is generally regarded as responsive and mechanically durable compared to CVT alternatives.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting Assembly

Lighting systems generally perform well, though some owners have experienced premature failures and noisy cooling fans within the LED headlamp assemblies.

**Headlight Cooling Fan and Assembly Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loud whirring, buzzing, or humming noises coming from the front headlights, followed by premature LED headlight failure or adaptive lighting motor malfunction.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies for unusual noises upon vehicle unlock and replace faulty headlight units if cooling fans or motors fail.

### Sunroof Glass

Exterior body panels and glass are largely trouble-free, though isolated incidents of spontaneous sunroof glass breakage have been reported.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping or gunshot-like sounds while driving, followed by the sunroof glass suddenly shattering and dropping shards into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for alignment or stress points.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mazda CX-5 remains a compelling choice in the compact SUV segment, combining sharp driving dynamics with a refined cabin. While most mechanical systems prove durable, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the LED headlights for unusual noises and verify the condition of exterior glass during a pre-purchase inspection.
