### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Mazda CX-5 stands out as a highly reliable vehicle, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission demonstrating excellent durability. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning the infotainment unit's occasional glitches and, less frequently, premature battery failure. These are generally manageable with software updates or standard component replacement. Beyond these electrical quirks, the CX-5 offers a robust and dependable ownership experience, making it a strong contender in the compact SUV segment for those prioritizing long-term reliability.
