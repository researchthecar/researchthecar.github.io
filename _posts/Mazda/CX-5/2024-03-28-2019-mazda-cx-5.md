**Introduction**

The 2019 Mazda CX-5 is a compact SUV known for its sporty handling, upscale interior, and impressive fuel economy. It is offered with two engine options: a 2.5L naturally aspirated inline-four producing 187 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.5L turbocharged inline-four producing 250 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its popularity, the 2019 Mazda CX-5 has faced some reported issues, which we will explore in detail below.

**Engine**

**2.5L Naturally Aspirated Inline-Four**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** A few owners have experienced premature turbocharger failure, resulting in a loss of power and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Delayed Shifts:** Some owners have reported delayed gear shifts, especially when shifting into higher gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:** Some owners have experienced excessive noise coming from the rear suspension, particularly over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension components, such as shock absorbers or bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** A small number of owners have reported excessive battery drain, even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system for potential leaks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:** Some owners have experienced premature failure of the windshield wiper motor.
    * Solution: Replace wiper motor assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** A few owners have reported intermittent glitches with the infotainment system, such as freezing or slow response times.
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 Mazda CX-5 remains a popular SUV, it has faced some reported issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of these issues varies, and some are limited to a small number of vehicles. The estimated cost to repair these issues can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars, depending on the nature of the problem. Potential buyers should be aware of these reported issues and consider their severity when making a decision about purchasing this vehicle.
