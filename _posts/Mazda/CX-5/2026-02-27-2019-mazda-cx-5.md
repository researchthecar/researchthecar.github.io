# 2019 Mazda CX-5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mazda CX-5 is widely praised for its upscale interior, engaging driving dynamics, and sleek styling. However, prospective buyers should review some notable owner-reported reliability concerns—most notably regarding the electrical system and certain engine issues. Armed with this information, you can thoroughly inspect a used model before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder (Naturally Aspirated)

This standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 187 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, offering responsive daily driving performance and decent fuel economy.

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder (Turbocharged)

Available on higher trims, the turbocharged 2.5-liter engine pumps out up to 250 horsepower on premium fuel, providing significantly stronger acceleration and passing power.

### Cylinder Head / Coolant System

Some owners have reported oil or coolant leaks stemming from porous cylinder head castings, leading to burning smells and expensive mechanical repairs.

**Cylinder Head Oil / Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** An acrid burning smell, visible smoke from the engine compartment, or low oil/coolant levels without an obvious external source.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis and potential cylinder head replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $2000

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission generally delivers smooth shifts, though a small number of owners have experienced premature internal component failures and rough low-speed operation.

**Internal Transmission Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Whining noises during acceleration, vehicle jerking, rough low-speed driving, or sudden failure to engage in gear.
- **Solution:** Inspection by a qualified technician; may require transmission overhaul or complete unit replacement.

## Electrical

### Mazda Connect Infotainment Screen

The infotainment touchscreen can suffer from digitization glitches, commonly referred to as 'ghost touch,' which disrupt normal audio and navigation functions.

**Ghost Touch / Phantom Inputs**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen registers random inputs without physical contact, causing menus to cycle, radio stations to change, and unprompted phone calls to be placed.
- **Solution:** Dealer service or replacement under applicable warranty extensions/settlements.

## Exterior

### Mirrors and Sunroof

Minor exterior durability gripes include occasional failures in the power-folding mirror mechanism and isolated reports of spontaneous sunroof glass breakage.

**Side Mirror and Sunroof Operation**
- **Symptoms:** Auto-folding side mirrors failing to open fully, or sunroof glass fracturing unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Inspect mirror motors and mechanisms; replace damaged sunroof glass and assemblies if affected.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V497000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A software error in the powertrain control module (PCM) may cause the engine to stall. An unexpected stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mazda will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the PCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V875000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mazda CX-5 is a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking style, safety, and driving engagement. While most examples prove dependable, shoppers should specifically test the infotainment system for phantom screen touches and have a pre-purchase inspection check for any signs of engine oil leaks or transmission hesitation.
