### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Mazda CX-5 demonstrates a high level of reliability across its core mechanical components, including the engine and transmission options. The naturally aspirated and turbocharged 2.5L engines, paired with the 6-speed automatic transmission, are robust and have a proven track record. Suspension and exterior elements also appear to be free from significant widespread issues. The primary area where minor concerns have been noted is within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the CX-5 is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a well-built and reliable compact SUV.
