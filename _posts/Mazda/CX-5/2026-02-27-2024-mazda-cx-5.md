# 2024 Mazda CX-5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mazda CX-5 is widely praised for its upscale interior, engaging handling, and sleek styling. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues involving electrical components, exterior glass, and select mechanical systems. While many drivers enjoy a trouble-free ownership experience, staying informed about these specific areas can help you evaluate a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

This 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces 187 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, offering reliable daily driving performance and decent efficiency for everyday commutes.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This turbocharged variant boosts output up to 256 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque (on premium fuel), delivering punchy acceleration while maintaining the vehicle's composure.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with standard all-wheel drive, providing smooth, predictable shifts and a more connected driving feel than many CVT competitors.

## Suspension

### Shock Absorbers

Suspension components typically offer a stable and comfortable ride, though individual component leaks can occur early in the vehicle's lifespan.

**Premature Shock Fluid Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Rear shock absorbers may develop fluid leaks prematurely, potentially leading to a compromised ride quality.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the leaking shock absorber units.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems generally perform well, though adaptive headlight motors can develop premature noise and failure.

**Headlight Fan Motor and Assembly Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report a persistent buzzing or humming noise coming from the headlights, followed by premature fan motor or assembly failure.
- **Solution:** The affected headlight assembly or internal fan motor must be replaced.
- **Cost:** $19,223.93

### Sunroof

Body panels and exterior trims are mostly durable, but a small number of owners have experienced unexpected sunroof glass breakage.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden explosive or gunshot-like sound occurs while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by wind noise as the sunroof glass shatters unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and clear out any remaining debris from the track.

## Interior

### Seats

The cabin is well-appointed and comfortable, though occasional reports note loose seat frames or cabin noises.

**Loose or Rocking Driver Seat**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's seat shifts, rocks back and forth, or feels loose during braking and cornering maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat mounting hardware and track mechanisms, tightening or replacing components as necessary.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mazda CX-5 remains a strong contender in the compact SUV segment, offering sharp driving dynamics and an upscale cabin. While most owners experience reliable daily operation, buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's exterior lighting systems, sunroof, and seat stability during a pre-purchase test drive. Checking these specific components can help ensure a worry-free used car purchase.
