# 2015 Mazda CX-5

### Overview
The 2015 Mazda CX-5 is generally considered a reliable compact SUV, praised for its engaging driving dynamics and fuel efficiency. Mazda's Skyactiv technology, which underpins the CX-5's powertrain and chassis, aims for a balance of performance and economy. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems reported by owners and technicians, providing insights into potential areas of concern for the 2015 model year across its various configurations.

## Electrical

### Body Control Module
**Intermittent Power Loss to Accessories**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported issues with certain electrical accessories (e.g., power windows, infotainment system) intermittently losing power or malfunctioning. This can sometimes be linked to the body control module.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the body control module.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Infotainment System
**Touchscreen Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional reports of the touchscreen becoming unresponsive or freezing, requiring a system reset.
- **Solution:** Software update or system reset. In rare cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Mazda CX-5 stands out as a reliable choice in the compact SUV segment. Its Skyactiv powertrains and chassis are generally robust, with owners reporting few significant mechanical failures outside of routine maintenance. The most commonly cited issues tend to be in the electrical system, specifically related to the infotainment touchscreen and occasional glitches with the body control module, though these are not universally experienced. Overall, the CX-5 offers a dependable ownership experience with a low probability of major, costly repairs, making it a solid recommendation for those seeking a practical and enjoyable SUV.
