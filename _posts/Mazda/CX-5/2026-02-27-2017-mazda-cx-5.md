# 2017 Mazda CX-5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Mazda CX-5 is widely praised for its premium interior styling, engaging driving dynamics, and sleek exterior design. However, used car buyers should carefully review common owner-reported complaints, particularly regarding electrical glitches and powertrain durability issues outside of warranty coverage. While NHTSA complaints highlight several recurring mechanical and electronic grievances, a pre-purchase inspection can help catch many of these potential problem areas before buying.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

The 2.5-liter naturally aspirated SkyActiv-G four-cylinder engine produces approximately 187 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque. It generally offers dependable daily performance, though rare owner reports cite sudden overheating or stalling incidents.

**Engine Stalling / Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Engine overheat warning lights suddenly illuminating on the dash or unexpected stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling system integrity, check thermostat operation, and diagnose underlying mechanical or electronic faults.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed SkyActiv-Drive Automatic

The conventional six-speed automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, known for quick, smooth shifts and solid mechanical reliability without widespread major complaint clusters.

### Rear Differential

All-wheel-drive models feature a rear differential that can experience premature mechanical failure in some instances, resulting in costly out-of-pocket repairs if outside the warranty period.

**Rear Differential Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Abnormal whining or popping noises coming from the rear of the vehicle, often accompanied by metal shavings found in the gear oil.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the rear differential assembly.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power, but owners have reported premature pad wear and occasional rear caliper binding.

**Brake Drag and Premature Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Premature wear on brake pads and rear calipers seizing, causing excessive heat, a burning smell, and wheel drag.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake calipers for proper sliding operation, replace warped pads, and service or replace seized calipers.

## Electrical

### Infotainment Touchscreen

The electronic architecture is generally robust, but the central infotainment system is prone to touch-screen anomalies.

**Ghost Touch and Radio Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen registers phantom inputs, randomly changing radio stations, adjusting volume, or opening menus without driver contact.
- **Solution:** Update the infotainment system firmware at a dealership or replace the digitizer screen assembly.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Body Structure

Exterior body components are generally durable, though isolated reports note spontaneous sunroof glass shattering.

**Exploding Sunroof Glass**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping noise followed by the sunroof glass suddenly shattering outward or inward while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and clear out any remaining glass fragments from the track mechanism.

### Paint Finish

The exterior finish looks striking when new, but certain factory paint colors are prone to premature stone chipping and flaking.

**Paint Chipping and Flaking**
- **Symptoms:** Paint chipping easily upon minor impacts from road debris, particularly on premium multi-stage colors like Soul Red.
- **Solution:** Apply touch-up paint or utilize protective film wraps to prevent further chipping.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V744000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-5&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Contact with the muffler may damage the trailer wiring harness, potentially resulting in the trailer lighting being inoperative, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mazda will notify owners, and dealers will reroute the trailer hitch harness away from the muffler, replacing the harness if it is damaged, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mazda CX-5 remains a compelling compact SUV choice thanks to its upscale driving experience and comfortable cabin. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test the infotainment system for phantom touch inputs, listen closely for any rear differential noises during a test drive, and inspect the vehicle's paint and brake components. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help ensure these potential issues are addressed before finalizing your purchase.
