**Introduction:**

The 1990 Mazda B2600 is a compact pickup truck offered with a single engine and transmission option. Its 2.6L I4 engine produces 112 horsepower and 145 lb-ft of torque, which is sent to the rear wheels through a 5-speed manual transmission. The B2600 is known for its versatility, affordability, and reliable performance. However, it has also encountered some common issues.

**Engine**

* **2.6L I4**
    * **Head gasket failure:** Symptoms include overheating, white smoke from the exhaust, and coolant leaks. Solution: Replace the head gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Timing belt failure:** Symptoms include sudden loss of power, engine noise, and misfiring. Solution: Replace the timing belt and water pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

* **5-speed manual**
    * **Synchronizer issues:** Symptoms include grinding or difficulty shifting gears. Solution: Replace the synchronizers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension wear:** Symptoms include excessive tire wear, uneven ride, and clunking or rattling noises. Solution: Replace worn ball joints, tie rods, or bushings. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** Symptoms include dim or flickering lights, battery discharge, and charging system warnings. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Starter motor failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the engine, grinding noises, or clicking sounds. Solution: Replace the starter motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Exterior**

* **Rust issues:** Symptoms include rust spots on the body panels, fenders, or frame. Solution: Repair and prevent further rust by applying rust protectant. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of the damage
* **Rear differential seal leak:** Symptoms include oil leaks from the rear axle. Solution: Replace the differential seal. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Interior**

* **Loose or broken door handles:** Symptoms include difficulty opening or closing the doors. Solution: Replace the door handles. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **HVAC system failure:** Symptoms include no air flow, weak air flow, or strange noises from the vents. Solution: Inspect and repair or replace the HVAC components as needed. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the specific issue

**Summary:**

The 1990 Mazda B2600 is a generally reliable vehicle with a few common issues. Issues with the engine, suspension, and electrical components can be costly to repair, while interior and exterior problems tend to be less severe. Regular maintenance and preventive measures can help extend the life of the B2600 and minimize potential problems.
