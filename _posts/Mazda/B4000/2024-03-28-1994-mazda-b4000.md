**Introduction**

The 1994 Mazda B4000 is a mid-size pickup truck that was available with three engine options: a 2.3L inline-four, a 2.6L V6, and a 4.0L V6. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic. The B4000 was praised for its comfortable ride, impressive towing capacity, and affordable price tag. However, it also had some drawbacks, such as its lackluster fuel economy and dated interior.

**Engine**

* **2.3L Inline-Four**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idling
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.6L V6**
    * **Timing Belt Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, catastrophic engine damage
        * Solution: Replace timing belt and tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4.0L V6**
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks, burning oil smell
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, difficulty shifting
        * Solution: Replace clutch kit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears
        * Solution: Repair or replace leaking component
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, turn signals not working
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard material
    * Solution: Replace or repair dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 1994 Mazda B4000 is a reliable pickup truck with some potential issues. The most serious problems are the timing belt failure on the 2.6L V6 and the alternator failure. These repairs can be expensive, but most other issues are relatively minor and affordable to fix.
