**Introduction:**

The 2002 Mazda B4000 is a compact pickup truck offered with two engine options: a 2.3-liter inline-four and a 3.0-liter V6. Both engines are paired with a five-speed manual transmission or an optional four-speed automatic transmission. The B4000 was praised for its ruggedness, payload capacity, and off-road capabilities. However, it also faced criticism for its dated interior and limited safety features.

**Engine**

**2.3-Liter Inline-Four**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idling, reduced engine power.
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 02V385000

**3.0-Liter V6**

**Timing Belt Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, rough idling, ticking noise from engine.
Solution: Replace timing belt and tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Grinding Gears:**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting.
Solution: Replace worn gears or synchronizers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Slipping Gears:**
Description/Symptoms: Car hesitates or stalls when shifting gears, loss of power.
Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Lower Ball Joint Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noise when turning, uneven tire wear.
Solution: Replace ball joint.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Leaf Springs Overloading:**
Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced payload capacity.
Solution: Replace leaf springs or reinforce them with additional leaves.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Corrosion:**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, flickering lights, electrical system malfunction.
Solution: Clean or replace battery terminals and cables.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not functioning properly, insufficient airflow.
Solution: Replace climate control unit or repair electrical connections.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Bed Liner Peeling:**
Description/Symptoms: Protective spray-on bed liner peeling off or cracking.
Solution: Reapply the bed liner or replace it.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2002 Mazda B4000 is a reliable and capable pickup truck. However, it is not without its issues, such as potential engine and transmission problems, suspension overloading, and interior electrical malfunctions. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from $200 to over $2,500.
