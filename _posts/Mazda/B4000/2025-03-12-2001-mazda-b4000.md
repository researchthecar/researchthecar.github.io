The 2001 Mazda B4000 is a compact pickup truck based on the Ford Ranger platform. This year model was available with a 2.5L inline-4 cylinder engine or a more powerful 4.0L V6. The 4.0L V6 produced around 160 horsepower and 220 lb-ft of torque. Owners generally appreciate the B4000 for its reliability, affordability, and compact size, making it easy to maneuver and park. Common complaints often revolve around its relatively basic interior, limited rear seat space in extended cab models, and fuel economy, especially with the 4.0L engine.

**Engine**

**4.0L V6**

*   **Timing Chain Rattle**
    *   Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the front of the engine, especially on cold starts. This indicates wear on the timing chain tensioners and guides. If left unaddressed, can lead to timing chain failure.
    *   Solution: Replace the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+

*   **Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks around the valve cover gaskets, leading to oil burning smells and visible oil accumulation on the engine.
    *   Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300

**Transmission**

**Automatic Transmission (5R55E)**

*   **Slipping/Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips between gears, or shifts harshly, particularly between 2nd and 3rd gear. This can be caused by worn valve body components or internal clutch pack wear.
    *   Solution: Replacing the valve body or a full transmission rebuild.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2500+

**Suspension**

*   **Ball Joint Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive play in the front suspension, clunking noises when going over bumps, and uneven tire wear. Can lead to dangerous handling.
    *   Solution: Replace the upper and lower ball joints.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Electrical**

*   **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers stop working intermittently or completely.
    *   Solution: Replace the windshield wiper motor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Exterior**

*   **Door Handle Breakage**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The exterior door handles, especially the driver's side, become brittle and break due to age and repeated use.
    *   Solution: Replace the broken door handle.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150

**Interior**

*   **Cracked Dashboard**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The dashboard develops cracks, especially in areas exposed to sunlight.
    *   Solution: Dashboard replacement is ideal, but can be expensive. A dashboard cover is a more affordable option.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100 (cover) or $500 - $1000+ (replacement)

**Summary**

The 2001 Mazda B4000, while generally a reliable truck, exhibits a few common issues related to its age. The timing chain rattle and valve cover gasket leaks on the 4.0L V6 are relatively common and can be moderately expensive to fix. Transmission issues, while less frequent, can be more costly to address. Suspension components like ball joints are also prone to wear and require periodic replacement. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor repair costs into their purchase decision.
