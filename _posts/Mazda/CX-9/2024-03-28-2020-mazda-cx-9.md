**Introduction:**

The 2020 Mazda CX-9 is a midsize SUV with seating for up to seven passengers. It's powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 250 horsepower and 320 lb-ft. of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed automatic transmission and available all-wheel-drive.

The CX-9 is praised for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and responsive handling. However, it's not without its flaws, which are detailed below.

**Engine**

**2.5 liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 21V389000
* **Carbon Buildup on Intake Valves**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, knocking, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Clean intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Shift Solenoids**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed or no gear engagement
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Dashboard Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable gauges, flickering lights, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace dashboard module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Blown Fuse for Power Outlet**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power outlet not working
    * Solution: Replace blown fuse
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wipers not working or intermittent
    * Solution: Replace wiper motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cracked Headlight Lenses**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation inside headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight lenses
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per lens
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Element Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heater not working in one or more seats
    * Solution: Replace seat heater element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per seat
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable climate control buttons, inconsistent temperature
    * Solution: Replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2020 Mazda CX-9 is a solid midsize SUV with a few notable issues. The most common engine problems are fuel injector failure and carbon buildup on intake valves. The transmission can also suffer from torque converter failure and faulty shift solenoids. Electrical issues are not uncommon, including dashboard malfunctions and blown fuses. Some owners have also reported problems with the windshield wiper motor and cracked headlight lenses. The interior can be plagued by seat heater element malfunctions and climate control issues.

Overall, the severity and cost of the issues facing the 2020 Mazda CX-9 can vary. However, it's important to be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
