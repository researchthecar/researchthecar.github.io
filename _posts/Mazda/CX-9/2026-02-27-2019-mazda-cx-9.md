# 2019 Mazda CX-9 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mazda CX-9 blends sharp handling, a refined interior, and strong turbocharged performance into a compelling three-row crossover package. While generally praised for driving dynamics and comfort, used-car shoppers should be aware of a few notable recurring trouble spots documented by owners. Specifically, potential engine coolant leaks and infotainment display screen cracking require careful inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### SkyActiv-G 2.5L Turbo 4-Cylinder

The 2.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces up to 250 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque when using premium fuel. It provides punchy low-end acceleration well-suited for a midsize SUV, though buyers should verify cooling system integrity.

**Coolant Leak at Cylinder Head**
- **Symptoms:** Engine overheating, white smoke from the tailpipe, or low coolant levels without an obvious external puddle.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cylinder head and turbo coolant lines; replacement of the cylinder head or gaskets may be required depending on severity.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional six-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the turbocharged engine across both front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It lacks widespread reported mechanical failures among owner complaint logs.

## Steering

### Steering Column

The steering system offers crisp response and solid road feel, with minor mechanical noises serving as the primary reported grievance.

**Steering Column Clicking Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Audible clicking or popping noises when turning the steering wheel from side to side.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering hardware and tighten or replace fasteners according to technical service guidelines.

## Electrical

### Infotainment Display Screen

Cabin electronics are generally robust, but the central infotainment touchscreen is susceptible to internal delamination and spider cracking.

**Internal Screen Cracking and Ghost Touch**
- **Symptoms:** Spider-web cracks appearing internally on the glass, unresponsive touchscreen zones, or erratic 'ghost touch' behavior.
- **Solution:** Replace the infotainment display unit, often covered under manufacturer service bulletins or extended goodwill in certain cases.

### Backup Camera and Display

Driver assistance features generally operate reliably, though screen failures can compromise active safety aids like the rearview camera.

**Distorted or Frozen Rearview Camera Image**
- **Symptoms:** Rearview camera image freezes or becomes severely distorted due to internal screen damage, impairing visibility when reversing.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the infotainment display panel to restore a clear backup camera feed.

## Exterior

### Body Trim and Glass

Exterior styling remains striking, particularly with specialty paint options, though plastic body moldings require occasional fastener checks.

**Wheel Well Trim Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Plastic wheel arch molding or trim clips breaking loose and detaching while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace broken retaining clips or install new wheel well trim molding pieces.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V875000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-9&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V770000: Tires:Tread/Belt](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-9&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A detached tire tread can decrease vehicle stability and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mazda will notify owners, and dealers will inspect all four tire serial numbers, replacing the tires as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mazda CX-9 is an appealing used family SUV that offers engaging driving dynamics and a premium cabin feel. Buyers should carefully check the vehicle history and maintenance records for any signs of engine coolant loss or cylinder head issues, and thoroughly test the infotainment screen for internal cracks or touch malfunctions. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
