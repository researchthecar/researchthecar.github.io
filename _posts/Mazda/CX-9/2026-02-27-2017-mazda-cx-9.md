# 2017 Mazda CX-9 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Mazda CX-9, buyers are generally drawn to its stylish design, upscale cabin, and engaging driving dynamics. However, owner reports submitted to safety authorities indicate certain recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that prospective buyers should investigate carefully before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2017 Mazda CX-9 is powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 227 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque on regular fuel. While praised for its low-end grunt and punchy acceleration, used buyers should carefully inspect this engine for signs of coolant consumption or related head issues.

**Coolant Leak and Cylinder Head Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report detecting a sweet coolant smell, engine overheating, and unexpected vehicle shutdowns due to coolant leaks originating around the cylinder head.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the cylinder head area; repairs may involve replacing compromised engine components.

**Fuel System and Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough engine idling, illuminated check engine lights (such as code P0192), difficulty starting, and a noticeable loss of power during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning and potential replacement of failing fuel pressure sensors or fuel pump assemblies.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive, the 6-speed automatic transmission offers smooth, responsive shifts and generally solid long-term durability without widespread recurring complaint patterns.

## Suspension

### Suspension System

Suspension components generally hold up well, though wear items can develop noises over time.

**Front and Rear Suspension Clunking Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers frequently hear knocking or clunking sounds from the front and rear suspension when going over uneven pavement, hitting bumps, braking, or starting from a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension bushings, struts, control arms, and mounting hardware for wear or looseness, and replace worn components.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Electronics

Electrical accessories provide modern convenience features, but touchscreens and related modules can occasionally exhibit glitchy behavior.

**Infotainment Screen Failure and Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display screen intermittently goes black—disabling access to the backup camera—or develops internal 'spider' cracks across the display panel.
- **Solution:** May require software updates, system resets, or replacement of the infotainment display unit.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Body Glass

Exterior trim and glass generally look sharp, though rare spontaneous sunroof failures have been documented by owners.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass panel can fracture or shatter unexpectedly while driving at highway speeds without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the shattered glass assembly and thorough cleaning of glass shards from the interior.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Mazda CX-9 remains a compelling choice for a midsize three-row crossover thanks to its refined driving dynamics and upscale interior styling. However, prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing specifically on the engine coolant system, cylinder head integrity, and proper functioning of electronic displays.
