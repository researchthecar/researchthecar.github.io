## 2012 Mazda CX-9

The 2012 Mazda CX-9 is a three-row SUV with seating for up to seven passengers. It is powered by a 3.7-liter V6 engine that produces 273 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The CX-9 is available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations.

The CX-9 has received praise for its stylish design, comfortable interior, and powerful engine. However, it has also been criticized for its high fuel consumption and limited cargo space.

### **Engine**
- **3.7L V6**
    - **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that their CX-9s are consuming excessive amounts of oil. This can lead to engine damage if not addressed.
        - Solution: The solution to this issue is to replace the piston rings. This is a major repair that can cost $2,000 to $3,000.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        - Recalls: None Found
    - **Timing Chain Failure**
        - Description/Symptoms: The timing chain can fail on the 3.7L V6 engine, which can cause catastrophic engine damage.
        - Solution: The solution to this issue is to replace the timing chain and related components. This is a major repair that can cost $3,000 to $5,000.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
        - Recalls: None Found

### **Transmission**
- **Six-Speed Automatic**
    - **Transmission Slipping**
        - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that their CX-9s' transmissions are slipping. This can cause the vehicle to hesitate or jerk when shifting gears.
        - Solution: The solution to this issue is to rebuild or replace the transmission. This is a major repair that can cost $2,500 to $4,000.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
        - Recalls: None Found

### **Suspension**
- **No Issues Found**

### **Electrical**
- **Battery Drain**
    - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that their CX-9s are experiencing battery drain. This can cause the battery to die, leaving the vehicle stranded.
    - Solution: The solution to this issue is to identify and repair the source of the battery drain. This can be a difficult and time-consuming process.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

### **Exterior**
- **Rear Bumper Sagging**
    - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that the rear bumper on their CX-9s is sagging. This can be caused by a number of factors, including the weight of the bumper itself and the impact of road hazards.
    - Solution: The solution to this issue is to replace the rear bumper. This is a relatively minor repair that can cost $500 to $1,000.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

### **Interior**
- **Navigation System Malfunctions**
    - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that the navigation system on their CX-9s is malfunctioning. This can cause the system to freeze, reboot, or display incorrect information.
    - Solution: The solution to this issue is to update the navigation system software. This can be done at a Mazda dealership.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100
    - Recalls: None Found
    - **Panoramic Sunroof Rattle**
        - Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that the panoramic sunroof on their CX-9s rattles when it is open. This can be a very annoying problem.
        - Solution: The solution to this issue is to adjust the sunroof tracks. This is a relatively minor repair that can cost $100 to $200.
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        - Recalls: None Found

### **Summary**

The 2012 Mazda CX-9 has a number of potential issues, including excessive oil consumption, timing chain failure, transmission slipping, battery drain, rear bumper sagging, navigation system malfunctions, and panoramic sunroof rattle. Some of these issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, while others are major and can cost thousands of dollars to repair. Overall, the CX-9 is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
