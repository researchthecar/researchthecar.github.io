# 2018 Mazda CX-9 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Mazda CX-9 is a stylish, three-row midsize crossover known for its upscale interior, engaging handling, and punchy turbocharged engine. While it offers strong family appeal, used-car buyers should review specific mechanical and electrical pain points reported by owners, particularly concerning engine cooling and infotainment durability.

---

## Engine

### Skyactiv-G 2.5T 4-Cylinder

Powered by a 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 producing 250 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque, this engine delivers strong low-end acceleration. However, buyers should carefully check service history for any past coolant leak issues.

**Cylinder Head Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle overheats unexpectedly, low coolant warnings appear, and coolant can mix with engine oil, potentially leading to catastrophic engine failure.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection and replacement of the affected cylinder head or related exhaust components.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Mated exclusively to a 6-speed automatic transmission available in front-wheel or all-wheel drive, the powertrain generally operates smoothly and lacks widespread mechanical failure trends.

## Electrical

### Infotainment Display

Electrical complaints center primarily on display screens and occasional auxiliary systems.

**Screen Delamination and Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Internal 'spider-web' cracks or frosting appear under the touch panel, causing ghost touches or preventing proper navigation visibility.
- **Solution:** Replace the infotainment touchscreen unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V875000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-9&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V403000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-9&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The passenger frontal air bag may not deploy, increasing the risk of injury. Inoperable turn signals prevent the driver from signaling to other drivers, increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** Mazda will notify owners, and dealers will repair the wiring harnesses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Mazda CX-9 is an enjoyable and premium family hauler, but shoppers need to verify its mechanical condition. Prioritize models with a documented service history, specifically ensuring that engine cooling and cylinder head integrity have been checked, and inspect the infotainment screen for internal bubbling or cracking before purchasing.
