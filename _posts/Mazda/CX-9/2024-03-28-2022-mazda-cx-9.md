**Introduction:**
The 2022 Mazda CX-9 is a midsize SUV available with two engine and transmission options:

* 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 with 6-speed automatic transmission (250 horsepower, 320 lb-ft of torque)
* 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 with 6-speed automatic transmission (227 horsepower, 310 lb-ft of torque)

Drivers appreciate its stylish design, responsive handling, and premium interior. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 (250 horsepower)**
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace the fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 (227 horsepower)**
* No Issues Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Problems:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficult or delayed gear changes
        * Solution: Update transmission software, replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noises from the rear suspension
    * Solution: Replace sway bar bushings or end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly
    * Solution: Identify and fix electrical component drawing excessive power
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlights
    * Solution: Replace the headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: System freezes, touchscreen unresponsive
    * Solution: Update software, replace infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
Overall, the 2022 Mazda CX-9 is a generally reliable vehicle. However, the issues mentioned in this list may occur in some models. The severity and cost of these issues vary, ranging from minor inconveniences to more significant repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
