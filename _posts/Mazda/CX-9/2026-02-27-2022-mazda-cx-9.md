# 2022 Mazda CX-9

### Overview
The 2022 Mazda CX-9 is a family-oriented three-row SUV that generally receives positive marks for its driving dynamics, upscale interior, and standard all-wheel-drive. From a reliability standpoint, the CX-9 has historically performed well, and the 2022 model year continues this trend. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the CX-9 demonstrates a solid build quality and thoughtful engineering. Owners can expect a dependable experience with the common areas of concern being relatively minor and infrequent, often related to specific electronic features or less common mechanical quirks rather than widespread systemic failures. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or connectivity issues with Apple CarPlay/Android Auto.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500


### Battery
**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may drain faster than expected, leading to starting issues, particularly if the vehicle sits for extended periods or with frequent short trips.
- **Solution:** Check for parasitic draws, ensure all accessories are off, consider a battery tender, or replace the battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mazda CX-9 stands out as a reliable choice in the three-row SUV segment. Its powertrain, consisting of a robust 2.5L turbocharged engine and a smooth 6-speed automatic transmission, shows no significant widespread issues. The suspension and exterior components are also generally trouble-free. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, premature battery drain. These issues are typically resolvable with software updates or standard battery maintenance. Overall, the CX-9 offers a dependable ownership experience with a strong track record for durability and fewer major mechanical problems compared to many competitors.
