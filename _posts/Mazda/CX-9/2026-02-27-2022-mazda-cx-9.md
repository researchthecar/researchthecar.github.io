# 2022 Mazda CX-9 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mazda CX-9 combines sharp styling, engaging handling, and a well-appointed cabin, making it a compelling choice in the midsize three-row SUV segment. Overall, owner feedback indicates that major powertrain mechanical failures are uncommon, though buyers should review scattered reports concerning driver-assistance electronics, trim fasteners, and climate-related washer performance.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Turbocharged Skyactiv-G 4-Cylinder

This 2.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces up to 250 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque when using 93 octane fuel. It provides strong low-end response for daily driving and generally maintains a solid reputation for mechanical durability.

## Transmission

### Skyactiv-Drive 6-Speed Automatic

Paired with either front-wheel drive or i-Activ all-wheel drive, this traditional six-speed automatic transmission trades some high-speed gear ratios for responsive shifting and dependable mechanical operation.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Driver Aids

Cabin electronics generally function well, though occasional software glitches in lighting controls and driver assistance aids have been documented.

**Cluster Brightness and Lane Keep Assist Inoperable**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that the instrument panel lighting may not adjust correctly between excessively dim or bright settings, and the lane keep assist feature may fail to engage.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealer or specialist to check for instrument cluster software updates and sensor module functionality.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wipers and Fasteners

Exterior panels and visibility components hold up well, though winter weather performance of washer nozzles and secure fastening of trim pieces warrant inspection.

**Freezing Washer Sprayers and Loose Cowl Fasteners**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wiper sprayers can struggle to function properly in sub-freezing temperatures when road salt and snow create heavy windshield grime, and cowl panel or header bolts may loosen over time.
- **Solution:** Clear out winter debris, use low-temperature rated washer fluid, and periodically check exterior trim fasteners during routine maintenance.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Active safety features provide helpful driver aids, though sensor calibration sensitivity can occasionally trigger false warnings.

**Phantom Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the forward collision warning and automatic emergency braking engage unexpectedly while utilizing adaptive cruise control when no obstacles are present.
- **Solution:** Have the radar sensor and camera alignment inspected by a qualified technician, and ensure software updates are current.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mazda CX-9 remains a stylish and generally reliable choice for used SUV shoppers, supported by a strong standard turbocharged engine and traditional automatic transmission. While isolated owner complaints highlight concerns with electronic driver aids, sensor sensitivity, and minor trim fasteners, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure everything operates as expected.
