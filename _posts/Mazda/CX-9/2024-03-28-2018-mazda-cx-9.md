**Introduction to the 2018 Mazda CX-9**

The 2018 Mazda CX-9 is a midsize SUV that comes with a 2.5L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It offers a spacious cabin, advanced safety features, and impressive performance. The 2.5L turbocharged engine produces 227 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. Some reviewers have praised the CX-9 for its handling, comfortable interior, and responsive engine, while others have criticized its limited cargo space and lack of certain features.

**Engine**

* **2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**

  * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear of the timing chain tensioner can lead to a rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning fuel injectors can cause rough idle, stalling, or reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Replacement of the faulty fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Door Trim Panel Detachment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear door trim panel may detach from the door frame, particularly in colder climates.
    * Solution: Reattachment of the trim panel using adhesive or clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hairline cracks may develop on the dashboard, especially in areas exposed to direct sunlight.
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Switch Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door lock switch may become inoperable, preventing the door from being locked or unlocked.
    * Solution: Replacement of the faulty door lock switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Mazda CX-9 has a mix of positive and negative reviews. While it is praised for its performance and handling, it also has some issues that owners have reported, such as engine timing chain tensioner failure, fuel injector problems, battery drain, and interior cracking. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Potential buyers should do thorough research and consider the potential risks before purchasing this vehicle.
