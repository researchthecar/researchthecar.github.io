**Introduction:**

The 2015 Mazda CX-9 is a mid-size, three-row SUV that offers a combination of style, performance, and practicality. It comes standard with a 2.5L turbocharged engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission, producing 250 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. Known for its sharp handling and comfortable interior, the CX-9 has also been praised for its fuel efficiency. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

**Engine**

**2.5L Turbocharged Engine**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, loss of power
Solution: Replace failed fuel injector(s)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, hesitation upon acceleration
Solution: Intake valve cleaning or replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shift Solenoid Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, transmission slipping
Solution: Replace faulty shift solenoid(s)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission Fluid Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of transmission fluid, transmission overheating
Solution: Identify and repair source of leak, replace lost fluid
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Strut Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noises when going over bumps, reduced handling
Solution: Replace worn or damaged strut(s)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Leaf Spring Breakage**
Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven ride
Solution: Replace broken leaf spring
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery dies frequently, difficulty starting
Solution: Identify and resolve source of drain, replace battery if necessary
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Sunroof Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into cabin through sunroof
Solution: Inspect and seal sunroof seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Interior Dashboard Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Rattles or noises from dashboard
Solution: Remove and reinstall dashboard components, tighten loose screws
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not functioning properly
Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty component(s)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2015 Mazda CX-9 is a generally reliable vehicle. However, it has experienced some common issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and suspension components. While the severity of these issues can vary, some, such as fuel injector failure and transmission fluid leaks, can be costly to repair. Potential owners should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a CX-9 of this model year.
