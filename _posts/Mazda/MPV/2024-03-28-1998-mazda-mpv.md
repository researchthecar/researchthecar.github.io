## 1998 Mazda MPV

The 1998 Mazda MPV is a minivan that was available with two engine options: a 2.5L V6 and a 3.0L V6. Both engines were paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The MPV was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful V6 engines. However, it was also criticized for its poor fuel economy and lack of standard safety features.

### Engine

**2.5L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6**

* **Timing Belt Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, catastrophic engine damage
    * Solution: Replace timing belt
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, hard starting
    * Solution: Replace ignition coil(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission will not shift properly, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Front Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise, vibration, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, electrical problems, battery will not charge
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Rear Bumper Sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear bumper hangs low, rattles
    * Solution: Replace bumper mounting brackets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 1998 Mazda MPV is a reliable minivan with a spacious interior and powerful V6 engines. However, it is important to be aware of potential issues such as transmission slipping, suspension problems, and electrical failures. While these issues are not common, they can be costly to repair.
