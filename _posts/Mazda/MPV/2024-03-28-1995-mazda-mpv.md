**Introduction of the 1995 Mazda MPV**

The 1995 Mazda MPV is a mid-size minivan that was a popular choice for families due to its spacious interior and comfortable ride. It came with two engine options: a 2.5L V6 producing 170 horsepower and 160 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0L V6 producing 190 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options included a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed manual. Drivers praised the MPV's reliability, safety features, and fuel efficiency. However, some complaints were voiced about its lack of power and subpar handling.

**Engine**

**2.5L V6**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
  Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
- **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
  Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks around the valve cover
  Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls:
- None Found

**3.0L V6**
- **Timing Belt Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Broken timing belt can cause catastrophic engine damage
  Solution: Replace timing belt and associated components
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
Recalls:
- None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**
- **Transmission Slipping**
  Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage or slips gears
  Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
- **Torque Converter Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering, overheating
  Solution: Replace torque converter
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
Recalls:
- None Found

**5-Speed Manual**
- **Clutch Failure**
  Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
  Solution: Replace clutch assembly
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
Recalls:
- None Found

**Suspension**

- **Struts and Shocks Wear**
  Description/Symptoms: Bouncy or rough ride, poor handling
  Solution: Replace struts and shocks
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls:
- None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain**
  Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly
  Solution: Inspect and replace faulty components (e.g., alternator, starter)
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls:
- None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rust**
  Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, especially around wheel wells and undercarriage
  Solution: Repair rust damage and apply rust protection
  Estimated Cost to Fix: Depends on the severity of damage
Recalls:
- None Found

**Interior**

- **Door Panel Rattling**
  Description/Symptoms: Door panels vibrate or make noise when driving
  Solution: Tighten or replace door panel clips
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
- **Dashboard Cracks**
  Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard material
  Solution: Replace or repair dashboard
  Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls:
- None Found

**Summary**

The 1995 Mazda MPV is a reliable and practical minivan with a spacious interior and a comfortable ride. However, it has some known issues such as intake manifold gasket failure, timing belt failure, and suspension wear. These problems can be costly to fix, so it's important to factor in potential repair expenses when considering this vehicle.
