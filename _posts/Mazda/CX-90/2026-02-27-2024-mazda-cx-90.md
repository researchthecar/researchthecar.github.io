# 2024 Mazda CX-90 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mazda CX-90 enters the three-row crossover market with a sophisticated interior, dynamic handling, and a choice of modern powertrains, including an inline-six turbo and a plug-in hybrid. However, early production models have drawn notable owner complaints concerning unexpected steering resistance, electrical glitches, and powertrain drivability hiccups. Prospective used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history records and ensure all pertinent dealer software updates or mechanical service campaigns have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Turbocharged I4

The 3.3-liter turbocharged inline-six engine delivers smooth power and strong towing capability. While generally robust, early examples have experienced electronic management glitches and occasional power loss complaints.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected loss of engine power, illumination of warning lights, and entry into limp mode while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose fault codes and perform any required software updates or component replacements.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall or display hybrid and electrical malfunction messages.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel and electrical systems for underlying diagnostic trouble codes and apply appropriate manufacturer updates.

### 2.5L Plug-In Hybrid Powertrain

The 2.5-liter plug-In hybrid powertrain pairs a four-cylinder engine with an electric motor for efficient daily driving. Complex hybrid electronics have prompted some owner reports regarding warning alerts and starting concerns.

**Electrical and Hybrid System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights illuminate for hybrid system faults, sometimes preventing the vehicle from starting or causing sudden deceleration.
- **Solution:** Check battery control modules and hybrid system software for updates or necessary component servicing.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission works seamlessly with the longitudinal engine layout to deliver quick shifts and efficient cruising. It maintains a solid mechanical reputation with minimal significant owner complaints.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension and Components

The independent suspension setup balances ride comfort and cornering stability, but isolated noises from control arms have been noted.

**Suspension Clunking Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear clunking or knocking noises from the front lower control arm area during suspension travel.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension bushings, ball joints, and control arms for premature wear or looseness.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Assist

The electronic power steering system provides responsive handling, though sticky steering sensations have been reported by several owners.

**Sticky Steering and Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel develops a sticky or resistant feel during turns, fails to return to center automatically, and may require excessive corrective force.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering gear assembly and steering rack for binding; consult a dealer for authorized mechanical repairs or software calibration.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Advanced vehicle electronics manage everything from safety assists to powertrain controls, though auxiliary battery and module warnings have surfaced in early models.

**Low Voltage Battery and Warning Alerts**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected electrical system warnings appear, potentially draining the battery or forcing the vehicle into reduced-power modes.
- **Solution:** Test the battery state of charge, check charging system output, and ensure all relevant electrical recalls are addressed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V429000: Steering:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of vehicle control increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten or replace the steering and suspension bolts, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V553000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Warnings:External/Pedestrian Alert](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 141, "Minimum Sound Requirements for Hybrid Electric Vehicles." Inaccurate external warning sounds may confuse pedestrians about the direction of an approaching vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the AVAS ISB amplifier software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V718000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software error, the rearview, front, and/or side camera images may not appear on the display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A camera that does not display an image can reduce the driver's view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the view monitor control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V719000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Due to a software error, the engine and electric motor may shut down unexpectedly, which can result in a loss of drive power. A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the powertrain control module and engine control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V022000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Loss of power steering assist while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the spring engaging the worm gear and reapply grease to the gear teeth, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V349000: Forward Collision Avoidance: Automatic Emergency Braking](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Unexpected braking increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the vehicle control module (VCM) with updated software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V816000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Management System/Energy Control Module (Bms/Becm):Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine may not restart after the idling stop feature (i-stop) turns off the engine due to a software issue. An engine that fails to restart increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the powertrain control module (PCM) and the battery energy control module (BECM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V814000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-90&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** During vehicle startup, the defroster, seat belt warning, 360° view monitor functions, and the PHEV high-voltage battery cooling system may not function properly. An ESU that does not function properly, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the Dash ESU with improved software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mazda CX-90 is an appealing choice for shoppers seeking a premium three-row SUV with engaging driving dynamics. While its powertrain options are generally well-regarded, buyers should pay close attention during a test drive to check for steering resistance or electrical warnings. Verifying that a certified technician has performed all open updates will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.
