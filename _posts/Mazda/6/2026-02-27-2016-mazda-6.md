This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2016 model year across its various configurations. Understanding these potential issues can help owners with proactive maintenance and informed repair decisions.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Freezing/Lag**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may become unresponsive, freeze, or exhibit lag during operation.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or in rare cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery failure or dead battery, sometimes linked to parasitic draw from certain modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mazda 6 demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly with its Skyactiv powertrain and chassis components. The most frequently cited, though not widespread, issues tend to be in the electrical system, specifically concerning the infotainment unit's responsiveness and occasional battery drain problems. These are generally manageable with software updates or targeted diagnostics. Other areas like the engine, transmission, suspension, exterior, and interior show a high degree of dependability, with no significant recurring technical faults reported. Owners can expect a robust and enjoyable driving experience with diligent attention to potential electrical quirks.
