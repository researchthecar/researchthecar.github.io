The 2022 Mazda 3 Sport is a popular compact car known for its sporty handling, upscale interior, and attractive styling. The model you specified has a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing 191 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 2.0-liter four-cylinder and a turbocharged 2.5-liter four-cylinder. A six-speed manual transmission is also available on select trims. Owners generally praise the car's engaging driving experience, premium feel, and fuel efficiency. Common complaints include a somewhat cramped rear seat, limited cargo space, and potential for road noise.

**Engine**

**2.5L Naturally Aspirated Four-Cylinder**
*   **Engine Hesitation/Stalling**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported hesitation during acceleration or occasional stalling, particularly when the engine is cold or at low speeds.
    *   Solution: The issue can be resolved with a software update to the PCM (Powertrain Control Module) to correct fuel trim and throttle response parameters.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+ (Depending on dealer charges for software update; potentially covered under warranty).
        Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported harsh or jerky shifting, particularly between 1st and 2nd gear, or hesitation when accelerating from a stop.
    *   Solution: A transmission control module (TCM) software update may improve shift quality. In some cases, a transmission fluid flush and fill is recommended or needed.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+ (Software Update); $150 - $300+ (Fluid Change).
    Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Lag**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can occasionally freeze, lag, or become unresponsive. This can affect navigation, audio, and other functions.
    *   Solution: Restarting the infotainment system by holding down the power button usually resolves the issue temporarily. A software update can be obtained. In rare cases, the infotainment unit may need to be replaced if the issue persists.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+ (Software Update); $500 - $1500+ (Replacement).
        Recalls: None Found
*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that the battery drains quickly, especially if the car sits for an extended period.
    *   Solution: Check for parasitic draws by testing each fuse to determine which circuit is causing the drain. The car should also have its charging system tested to make sure it's operating correctly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **LED Headlight Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The LED headlights on some 2022 Mazda 3 Sports have been reported to flicker or fail prematurely.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty headlight unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+ (depending on labor costs and dealership pricing).
    Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Buzzing Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rattling or buzzing noises coming from the dashboard or door panels, especially on rough roads.
    *   Solution: The source of the noise needs to be identified and the affected components properly secured or replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ (depending on the severity and source of the rattle).
        Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Mazda 3 Sport with the 2.5L engine and 6-speed automatic transmission is generally a reliable vehicle, but owners have reported issues ranging from minor software glitches to more significant concerns with the infotainment system and transmission. The severity of these issues varies, with some being easily addressed through software updates, while others require more costly repairs. Addressing these issues promptly can help maintain the vehicle's reliability and driving experience.
