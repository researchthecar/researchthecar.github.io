**Vehicle Introduction**

The 2016 Mazda 3 Sport is a compact hatchback available with two engine options: a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine and a 2.5L 4-cylinder engine. The 2.0L engine produces 155 horsepower and 150 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.5L engine produces 184 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual transmission, a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 6-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters.

People like the Mazda 3 Sport for its sporty handling, attractive design, and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle, including engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical problems.

**Engine**

**2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **Knocking Noise on Startup**
    * Description/Symptoms: A knocking noise is heard coming from the engine when starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $900+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes excessive amounts of oil.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty Shifting Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The gearshift lever is difficult to move, especially when shifting into reverse.
    * Solution: Replace the shift linkage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slips out of gear while driving.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters**

* **Paddle Shifters Not Working**
    * Description/Symptoms: The paddle shifters do not engage the transmission when used.
    * Solution: Replace the paddle shifter switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $350+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to stall.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty component causing the drain, such as a defective module or wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the vehicle through the sunroof during heavy rain.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Paint Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The paint on the exterior of the vehicle peels and flakes off.
    * Solution: Repaint the affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system does not work properly, resulting in inconsistent temperature or airflow.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty climate control module or sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Crashing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system randomly crashes or restarts.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the faulty component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Mazda 3 Sport has received mixed reviews from owners. While it has been praised for its sporty handling and attractive design, it has also been plagued by several issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical problems. The severity of these issues can vary, and some owners have reported having few or no problems at all. The cost of repairs can also be significant, so it's important to factor this into your decision-making process when considering this vehicle.
