### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mazda 3 Sport exhibits strong overall reliability across its powertrain and major systems. The naturally aspirated and turbocharged Skyactiv-G engines, along with both automatic and manual transmissions, have shown no significant widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. The most commonly noted, albeit minor, concerns relate to occasional infotainment system glitches, typically resolvable with software updates. This suggests that the 2022 Mazda 3 Sport is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a well-built and reliable compact car.
