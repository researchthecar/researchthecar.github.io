# 2021 Mazda 3 Sport

### Overview
The 2021 Mazda 3 Sport, known for its premium interior and engaging driving dynamics, generally presents a strong reliability profile. Mazda's commitment to quality engineering is evident in its powertrain and chassis components. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 2021 Mazda 3 Sport demonstrates a commendable track record, with most reported concerns being minor or isolated incidents rather than widespread defects. Owners can expect a dependable vehicle with a focus on driver enjoyment and long-term satisfaction, provided regular maintenance is performed.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mazda 3 Sport is a highly reliable vehicle, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The Skyactiv-G engines and Skyactiv-Drive transmissions have proven to be robust and dependable. Minor electrical glitches with the infotainment system are the most commonly cited, but these are typically resolved with software updates. Exterior paint can be somewhat susceptible to chipping, which is a common concern across many modern vehicles. Overall, the 2021 Mazda 3 Sport offers excellent long-term reliability, making it a sound choice for buyers prioritizing a premium feel and dependable performance.
