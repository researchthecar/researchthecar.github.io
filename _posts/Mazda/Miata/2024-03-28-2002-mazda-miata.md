**Introduction:**

The 2002 Mazda Miata is a beloved sports car known for its handling, performance, and affordability. It is offered with a single engine option and two transmission options. The 1.8L VVT engine produces 142 horsepower and 125 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual and a 4-speed automatic.

**Engine**

**1.8L VVT**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Increased oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacing valve stem seals or repairing piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine, loss of power, or engine failure.
    * Solution: Replacing the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, accompanied by grinding noises.
    * Solution: Replacing or repairing the synchronizers in the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Soft or spongy clutch pedal, difficulty engaging gears.
    * Solution: Replacing the clutch master cylinder.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Solenoid Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, delayed shifting, or transmission slipping.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the transmission, low transmission fluid levels.
    * Solution: Replacing the leaking seals or gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, clunking or knocking sounds during turns.
    * Solution: Replacing the strut mount bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminated, poor battery charging.
    * Solution: Replacing the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced power.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty ignition coil.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal components under the vehicle, compromising structural integrity.
    * Solution: Rust treatment or replacement of affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on the extent of rust damage
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working or malfunctioning.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty heating element or control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioner Compressor Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor air conditioning performance, warm air blowing from vents.
    * Solution: Replacing the compressor or related AC components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2002 Mazda Miata is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues that can be costly to fix. The engine is prone to excessive oil consumption and timing chain problems, while the transmission can experience grinding gears or solenoid failures. In addition, the vehicle may also face suspension issues such as strut mount bearing failures and electrical concerns like alternator or ignition coil failure. Rust on the undercarriage is a potential problem that should be addressed to prevent further damage. Overall, the severity of these issues and the associated repair costs can vary widely depending on the specific problem encountered.
