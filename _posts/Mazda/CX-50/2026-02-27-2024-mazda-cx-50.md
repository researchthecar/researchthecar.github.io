# 2024 Mazda CX-50 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Mazda CX-50 brings rugged styling and engaging driving dynamics to the compact crossover segment. While overall owner satisfaction remains strong, early production models feature a handful of reported electrical, steering, and structural issues that prospective used-car buyers should inspect carefully.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

The base 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 187 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate everyday power for daily commutes.

### 2.5L SkyActiv-G Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The upgraded turbocharged 2.5-liter engine generates up to 256 horsepower and 320 lb-ft of torque on premium fuel, offering significantly punchier acceleration for passing and highway driving.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The traditional six-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options, delivering crisp and predictable shifts compared to the CVTs common in competing crossovers.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is solid, but owners should monitor pad wear patterns and rotor condition closely during service intervals.

**Uneven Brake Pad and Rotor Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Inner brake pads wear out significantly faster than outer pads, or premature cracking develops on the rear brake rotors.
- **Solution:** Perform a thorough manual inspection of both inner and outer brake pads and rotors during routine tire rotations.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Steering assembly components generally perform well, though alignment drift and isolated hardware faults have been documented.

**Steering Column Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may lose center alignment or loosen unexpectedly, with rare reports of steering column component detachment due to stripped hardware.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering hardware and bolts for proper torque specs and replace any stripped fasteners immediately.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

Electrical complaints primarily center around premature original equipment battery failure and parasitic power drain concerns.

**Premature Battery Failure and Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle displays low battery warnings or experiences sudden complete battery failure, sometimes causing electrical glitches and dashboard error lights.
- **Solution:** Have the battery and charging system tested; replace the weak factory battery with a heavy-duty aftermarket unit if drain issues persist.

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Active safety features enhance protection but can occasionally experience temporary sensor deactivation in congested driving conditions.

**Blind Spot Monitoring Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** Blind spot monitoring and rear cross-traffic alert systems stop functioning or turn off temporarily during extended highway driving or heavy traffic.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to check for software updates or sensor recalibration if driver-assist features drop out intermittently.

## Exterior

### Liftgate and Glass

Body hardware and glass components require close inspection for alignment issues, door detent wear, and stress-related windshield cracks.

**Power Liftgate and Windshield Stress Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Power liftgate anti-pinch mechanism fails to reverse properly during closure, or stress cracks develop along the driver's side A-pillar windshield.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield for stress fractures originating from the pillars and test power liftgate safety sensors for proper resistance response.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V167000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-50&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached trailer hitch assembly can create a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the trailer hitch bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Mazda CX-50 is a compelling choice for used-car shoppers seeking an upscale, great-handling crossover with distinct utility. While most mechanical systems prove reliable, prospective buyers should verify that the battery, steering hardware, and driver-assistance sensors have been thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection.
