## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Power Window Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Slow or inoperative power windows.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Keyless Entry Fob Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Remote key fob may stop working intermittently or entirely.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or replacement of the key fob, or potential issues with the receiver module.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Soft Top Leaks/Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Water ingress through the soft top seals or fabric wear over time.
- **Solution:** Sealant application, repair of small tears, or replacement of the soft top.
- **Cost:** $150 - $800 (depending on repair vs. replacement)

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mazda MX-5 Miata is a highly reliable sports car with a strong track record. The powertrain (2.0L engine and 6-speed transmissions) is exceptionally robust, with no significant widespread issues reported. Similarly, the suspension and interior components generally hold up well. The most commonly cited issues tend to be minor electrical glitches, such as power window motors or keyless entry fobs, and potential wear or leaks in the soft top. These are typically manageable repairs and do not detract significantly from the overall dependability of the vehicle. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring its longevity.
