### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mazda MX-5 Miata is a highly reliable sports car with very few reported widespread mechanical issues. The powertrain (2.0L Skyactiv-G engine and 6-speed transmissions) is exceptionally robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and rare instances of battery drain, both generally resolvable with software updates or diagnostics. Exterior paint can be prone to minor chipping, a common trait for low-profile vehicles. Interior-wise, road noise is a characteristic rather than a fault. Overall, the 2016 MX-5 Miata offers a dependable and enjoyable ownership experience, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
