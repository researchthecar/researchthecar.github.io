### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Mazda MX-5 Miata stands out as a highly reliable sports car. Across all major categories, including its robust 2.0L engine and well-regarded manual and automatic transmissions, there are no widespread or commonly reported mechanical failures. The suspension, electrical systems, exterior components like the body and tops, and interior elements also show a strong track record. This indicates that the 2013 MX-5 Miata is a dependable choice for enthusiasts seeking a pure driving experience with minimal risk of significant, non-wear-related issues. Routine maintenance is key to preserving its performance, but major component failures are uncommon for this model year.
