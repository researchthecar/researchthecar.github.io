**Introduction:**
The 2015 Mazda MX-5 Miata is a two-seater sports car praised for its handling, performance, and affordability. It is powered by a 2.0-liter Skyactiv-G engine delivering 155 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed manual or six-speed automatic transmission. Owners appreciate its lively driving experience, fuel efficiency, and sleek design. Some drawbacks include limited cargo space and a cramped cabin.

**Engine**
**2.0-liter Skyactiv-G**
- **Oil Leaks:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Leaks can occur from the camshaft cover gasket or oil filter housing.
  - Solution: Replace the gasket or filter housing.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle, loss of power, potential catastrophic engine damage.
  - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and tensioner guide.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**Six-Speed Manual**
- **Shifting Difficulties:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, grinding when shifting.
  - Solution: Replace the shift cables or lubricate the transmission.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Transmission Noise:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Whining or grinding noise while driving.
  - Solution: Replace the transmission input shaft bearing or repair the transmission case.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic**
- **Torque Converter Shudder:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration or gear changes.
  - Solution: Flush and refill the transmission fluid or replace the torque converter.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Transmission Slipping:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, sluggish acceleration, RPMs increasing without corresponding speed gain.
  - Solution: Replace the transmission clutches or rebuild the transmission.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $4000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
- **Battery Drain:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery loses power over time, preventing the vehicle from starting.
  - Solution: Replace the battery or check for parasitic drain.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Infotainment System Malfunctions:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes, audio cuts out, or navigation system errors.
  - Solution: Update the infotainment system software or replace the unit.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Convertible Top Issues:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Convertible top not opening or closing properly, water leaks.
  - Solution: Repair or replace the convertible top motor or hydraulic system.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Bumper Cracks:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Cracks or damage to the rear bumper near the exhaust tips.
  - Solution: Replace the rear bumper.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
- **HVAC System Malfunctions:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly, fan speed variations.
  - Solution: Replace the HVAC blower motor or check for electrical issues.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Power Window Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Power window stuck in place or operating intermittently.
  - Solution: Replace the window motor or switch.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**
While the 2015 Mazda MX-5 Miata is a well-loved sports car, it is not without its issues. The most serious problems involve engine timing chain tensioner failure and transmission shifting difficulties, which can be costly to repair. Other issues, such as convertible top and rear bumper problems, can also be inconvenient but are generally less expensive to fix. Overall, the MX-5 Miata is a reliable vehicle for its class, but potential owners should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
