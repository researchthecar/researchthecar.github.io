**Introduction:**

The 2022 Mazda MX-5 Miata is a compact sports car renowned for its sharp handling, responsive performance, and stylish design. It is offered with a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine paired with either a six-speed manual or automatic transmission. The engine generates 181 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque. The Miata has been praised for its engaging driving experience, excellent fuel economy, and value for money. However, it has a few drawbacks, including a cramped interior and limited cargo space.

**Engine:**

**2.0-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Issue: Excessive oil consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine burns excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replace engine piston rings or entire engine in severe cases.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Issue: Difficulty shifting into gear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gears may grind or be difficult to engage, especially when the transmission is cold.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace clutch and/or transmission linkage components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Issue: Erratic shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may hesitate or shift abruptly between gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace solenoids within the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Issue: Noises from front suspension**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking, clunking, or rattling noises during suspension travel.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace worn or damaged sway bar end links, ball joints, or struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Issue: Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery discharges quickly when parked.
    * Solution: Identify and repair parasitic draws, such as faulty alternator or interior lights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V-123: Battery drain due to faulty rear camera system

**Exterior:**

* **Issue: Faulty LED headlights**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights may flicker, dim, or fail prematurely.
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Issue: Malfunctioning infotainment system**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezes, system becomes unresponsive, or audio functions are disrupted.
    * Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Mazda MX-5 Miata is a well-received sports car with few significant issues. However, it is important to be aware of potential engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical concerns. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they should be considered when evaluating the vehicle's reliability and ownership costs.
