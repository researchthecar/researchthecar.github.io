# 2020 Mazda CX-3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Mazda CX-3, buyers will find a subcompact crossover known for its nimble handling, upscale interior feel, and efficient powertrain. Reviewing available owner reports helps provide a complete picture of long-term ownership expectations alongside routine maintenance needs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 148 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, offering a dependable and efficient daily driving experience.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional six-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and responsive shifts. It is well-matched to the four-cylinder engine, generally proving durable in everyday use.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Owner reports highlight unpredictable external factors affecting vehicle operation, though standard safety systems perform as designed during impacts.

**Medical Incidents While Driving**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported experiencing a medical event behind the wheel resulting in a crash.
- **Solution:** Ensure drivers are medically cleared for operation, and have the vehicle inspected by a qualified professional for collision damage if an incident occurs.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V487000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display properly by showing a flickering or distorted image. A rearview camera that does not display properly can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Mazda CX-3 presents as a stylish and efficient option for used subcompact crossover buyers. While standard owner feedback remains limited, prospective buyers should conduct a thorough test drive and pre-purchase inspection to ensure all mechanical and safety systems are in good condition.
