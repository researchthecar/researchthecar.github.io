# 2016 Mazda CX-3

### Overview
The 2016 Mazda CX-3, a subcompact crossover SUV, generally presents a solid reliability record, reflecting Mazda's reputation for building dependable vehicles. It is powered by Mazda's Skyactiv-G 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine, known for its efficiency and smooth operation. This engine is paired with either a Skyactiv-Drive 6-speed automatic transmission or, in some markets, a 6-speed manual. The CX-3's chassis is based on Mazda's Skyactiv-Body technology, contributing to its agile handling and structural integrity. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally experience issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Freezing/Lag**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive, or exhibit lag during operation. This can affect navigation, audio, and connectivity features.
- **Solution:** Software updates from the dealership or a system reset can often resolve these issues. In rare cases, a component replacement might be necessary.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500 (for potential software updates or module replacement)


### Battery
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported the need to replace the battery sooner than expected, leading to starting issues or complete power loss.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the battery. Ensuring proper charging system function is also important.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 (for battery replacement)

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Mazda CX-3 is a generally reliable vehicle with a strong mechanical foundation. The Skyactiv-G engine and transmissions have proven to be robust, with no significant widespread issues reported for these core components. The chassis and suspension also appear to hold up well under normal driving conditions. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. Exterior and interior components have largely met owner expectations without significant recurring problems. Overall, the CX-3 offers a dependable ownership experience, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
