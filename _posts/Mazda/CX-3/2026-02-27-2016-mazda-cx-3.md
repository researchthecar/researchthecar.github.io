# 2016 Mazda CX-3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Mazda CX-3 is a stylish and nimble subcompact crossover that brings engaging driving dynamics to the segment. However, used car buyers should carefully evaluate owner-reported concerns regarding electrical glitches, exterior lighting moisture, and engine fuel delivery. Reviewing service history and verifying that all safety recalls have been completed will help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter SkyActiv-G four-cylinder engine produces approximately 146 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate pep for daily commuting. It is generally praised for solid fuel economy, though occasional fuel system and electrical complaints have been reported by owners.

**Fuel System Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may abruptly lose power and stall while driving at highway speeds, failing to restart afterward.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel delivery system and replace failing fuel pump components or related sensors as needed.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive six-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, this setup delivers smooth shifts and reliable operation under normal driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

Cabin electronics are modern and feature-rich, but the touchscreen interface can occasionally develop touch-registration anomalies.

**Ghost Touches and Screen Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen center display registers unprompted inputs, causing the system to scroll through contacts, place random phone calls, or change audio channels.
- **Solution:** Update the infotainment firmware or replace the failing touchscreen digitizer panel.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical and lighting components generally function well, though some owners have noted moisture buildup inside exterior housings.

**Headlight Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight assemblies develop interior moisture over time, leading to dimmed and diffused nighttime visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seals for leaks and replace the headlight housing units if moisture intrusion persists.

## Interior

### Driver Seat

The interior offers comfortable seating and clean design, though mechanical seat height adjusters can occasionally lose their position.

**Seat Height Adjustment Sinking**
- **Symptoms:** The manual driver's seat height adjuster slowly loses pressure or slips downward while driving, requiring frequent re-pumping.
- **Solution:** Inspect the manual height adjustment mechanism and replace faulty seat frame components if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V644000: Structure:Body:Hatchback/Liftgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** On the affected vehicles, the lift gate support struts may corrode, possibly causing the struts to break and/or the lift gate to drop unexpectedly. If the lift gate falls unexpectedly, it may hit the user, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Mazda dealers will replace the both lift gate supports, free of charge, however parts are not currently available. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V203000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the steering knuckle separates from the strut assembly, it can cause a loss of steering control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Mazda will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the lower mounting nuts and bolts that secure the front strut assembly to the steering knuckle. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V487000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display properly by showing a flickering or distorted image. A rearview camera that does not display properly can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Mazda CX-3 is an appealing choice for buyers seeking sharp handling and a premium cabin feel in a small package. While major mechanical failures are relatively infrequent, prospective buyers should thoroughly test the infotainment screen, check for moisture inside the headlights, and ensure all open safety recalls have been addressed by a dealer.
