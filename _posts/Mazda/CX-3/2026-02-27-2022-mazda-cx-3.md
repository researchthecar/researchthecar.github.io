# 2022 Mazda CX-3

### Overview
The 2022 Mazda CX-3 is a subcompact crossover known for its stylish design, engaging driving dynamics, and upscale interior for its class. Mazda's reputation for building reliable vehicles generally extends to the CX-3. For the 2022 model year, it continues to offer a refined experience with a focus on driver enjoyment. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the CX-3 has historically demonstrated strong performance in terms of mechanical durability and overall dependability. Owners typically report satisfaction with its powertrain and core components, contributing to a positive ownership experience. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Mazda CX-3 demonstrates a high level of technical reliability across its core mechanical components, including the engine and transmission. The 2.0L Skyactiv-G engine and the 6-speed automatic transmission are robust and have a proven track record. Minor electrical issues, primarily related to the infotainment system's responsiveness or connectivity, are the most commonly cited, though these are typically resolved with software updates and are not indicative of major system failures. Other categories such as suspension, exterior, and interior components show no significant widespread problems. Overall, the 2022 CX-3 is a dependable choice for consumers seeking a stylish and reliable subcompact SUV.
