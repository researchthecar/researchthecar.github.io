# 2019 Mazda CX-3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mazda CX-3 is a stylish and engaging subcompact crossover, known for its nimble handling and upscale interior feel. While overall owner satisfaction remains relatively high, prospective buyers should review a few recurring mechanical and electrical pain points reported by drivers, ranging from steering stiffness to fuel and windshield concerns.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter SkyActiv-G four-cylinder engine produces 148 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate pep for city commuting paired with strong fuel economy. It generally proves dependable, though isolated fuel and propulsion complaints have surfaced among owners.

**Motive Power Loss and Fuel Delivery**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected losses of motive power while driving, occasionally accompanied by low oil pressure warnings or fuel gauge inaccuracies causing vehicles to run out of fuel prematurely.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel system and oil pressure diagnostics checked by a qualified mechanic; address any applicable manufacturer safety campaigns.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a traditional six-speed automatic transmission available in front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, this gearbox is widely praised for crisp, responsive shifting and lacks widespread mechanical failure trends.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Resistance

Issues related to steering feel and resistance have been noted by multiple drivers, particularly during highway driving.

**Stiff or Binding Steering Wheel**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that the steering wheel can exhibit unusual resistance or feel stuck, particularly at highway speeds, creating a hazard for oversteering or difficulty maintaining a smooth path.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering assembly, intermediate shaft, and steering linkage for binding or electronic faults.

## Electrical

### Electrical System and Controls

Electrical complaints center primarily around minor interior switch durability and occasional battery power loss.

**Center Console Switch and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Console switches (such as the sport mode toggle) can unseat or fall out of their sockets, and some owners experience random battery drains or door lock electrical failures.
- **Solution:** Re-secure loose console trim clips and test the battery and charging system for parasitic draws.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior complaints occasionally highlight fragile glass components, including sudden sunroof shattering or unprovoked windshield cracking.

**Windshield Stress Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Front windshields have been reported to develop long stress cracks spontaneously from the top edge without direct impact from road debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield with a certified glass component, ensuring proper sealing to alleviate frame stress.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V875000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V487000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display properly by showing a flickering or distorted image. A rearview camera that does not display properly can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mazda CX-3 is a compelling used subcompact SUV that delivers sharp styling and nimble driving dynamics. While major mechanical catastrophes are uncommon, buyers should thoroughly inspect the steering feel for any stiffness, check for proper electrical switch operation, and confirm that all open safety recalls have been completed by a dealership.
