# 2019 Mazda CX-3

### Overview
The 2019 Mazda CX-3 is generally considered a reliable subcompact SUV, praised for its engaging driving dynamics and upscale interior for its class. Mazda's Skyactiv technology, including its engines and transmissions, has a reputation for durability. Owners often report a positive ownership experience with fewer unexpected repairs compared to some competitors. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the CX-3 tends to perform well in terms of long-term dependability, making it a solid choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking a stylish and fun-to-drive crossover. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its continued reliability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Mazda CX-3 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain components. The 2.0L Skyactiv-G engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are robust and rarely present significant issues beyond normal maintenance. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are the infotainment system, which can experience occasional software glitches, and the battery, with some reports of premature failure. Exterior paint's susceptibility to chipping is a minor aesthetic concern. Overall, the CX-3 offers a dependable ownership experience with a low likelihood of major mechanical failures, making it a commendable choice in the subcompact SUV segment.
