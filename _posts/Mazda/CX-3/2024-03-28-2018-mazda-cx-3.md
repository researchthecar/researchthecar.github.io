**Introduction to the 2018 Mazda CX-3**

The 2018 Mazda CX-3 is a subcompact crossover SUV that offers a sporty and refined driving experience. It is available with a choice of two engine options: a 2.0-liter inline-four producing 146 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.5-liter inline-four producing 184 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The CX-3 is known for its agile handling, responsive steering, and comfortable interior. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as limited rear-seat space, a small cargo area, and a lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter inline-four**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **2.5-liter inline-four**
    * **Fuel Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The fuel pump fails, causing the engine to stall while driving.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Intake Valve Deposit Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Deposits build up on the intake valves, reducing engine performance and fuel efficiency.
        * Solution: Clean the intake valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **Six-speed automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission fluid leaks, causing a loss of power and shifting issues.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front struts**
    * **Premature Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: The front struts wear prematurely, causing a rough ride and poor handling.
        * Solution: Replace the front struts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Premature Battery Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery fails prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
        * Solution: Replace the battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator**
    * **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails, preventing the battery from charging and leading to electrical system problems.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Headlights**
    * **Headlight condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Condensation builds up inside the headlights, impairing visibility.
        * Solution: Replace the headlights.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
    * **Dashboard Panel Warping**
        * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel warps, causing a gap between the panel and the windshield.
        * Solution: Replace the dashboard panel.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

Overall, the 2018 Mazda CX-3 is a reliable vehicle with a few common issues. The most significant problems include fuel pump failures, transmission fluid leaks, and premature wear of the front struts. While these issues can be expensive to fix, they are not widespread and should not deter potential buyers from considering the CX-3.
