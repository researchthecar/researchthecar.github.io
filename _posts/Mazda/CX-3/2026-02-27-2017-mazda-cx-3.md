# 2017 Mazda CX-3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned Mazda CX-3, buyers generally find a stylish subcompact crossover equipped with nimble handling and an upscale interior. However, prospective owners should review owner-reported complaints regarding certain fuel system and electrical quirks before making a purchase. While many drivers enjoy its fuel efficiency and driving dynamics, being aware of these potential trouble spots helps ensure a more informed buying decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Skyactiv-G 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 146 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque. Paired exclusively with front-wheel or all-wheel drive, this powerplant provides respectable fuel economy and generally dependable day-to-day operation.

### Fuel System

Delivers fuel from the tank to the direct injection system. Owner complaints note intermittent stalling concerns that require professional diagnostic inspection.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle unexpectedly stalls while driving at highway speeds or before the low fuel warning indicator illuminates.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis typically involves checking fuel pressure, testing the fuel pump module, and replacing faulty components if failure is confirmed.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The traditional six-speed automatic transmission features manual shift modes and sport settings. It is widely praised for responsive shifting and solid long-term mechanical reliability without widespread major failure trends.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment System

Manages vehicle power distribution, lighting, and interior electronics. Occasional owner complaints highlight stubborn power-down glitches and auxiliary accessory quirks.

**Ignition Shut-Off Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced situations where the vehicle fails to shut off completely, draining the battery while emitting continuous warning chimes.
- **Solution:** Inspect the ignition switch assembly, key fob detection sensors, and central electronic modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Windshield Washer Reservoir

Holds washer fluid for maintaining outward visibility. Minor owner reports note occasional reservoir leaks requiring replacement.

**Fluid Reservoir Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Washer fluid drains out completely shortly after being poured into the tank.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield washer fluid tank and associated hoses for cracks or punctures, and replace the reservoir if damaged.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V487000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display properly by showing a flickering or distorted image. A rearview camera that does not display properly can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The used vehicle provides responsive handling and an engaging driving experience, but buyers should perform a thorough inspection of the fuel system and electrical accessories. Ensuring that all electronic modules and fuel delivery components operate properly will help secure a trouble-free ownership experience.
