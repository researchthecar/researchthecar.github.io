# 2021 Mazda CX-3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Mazda CX-3, buyers will find a subcompact crossover known for its nimble handling, upscale interior feel, and efficient powertrain. While overall owner satisfaction tends to be high, prospective buyers should review specific mechanical feedback—particularly regarding steering performance—to ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Skyactiv-G 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 148 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque, paired exclusively with front-wheel or all-wheel drive. It offers dependable daily performance and strong fuel economy for routine commuting.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Skyactiv-Drive Automatic

The traditional six-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, responsive gear changes compared to the CVTs found in many competing models. This gearbox has proven generally robust and well-matched to the vehicle's engine options.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

Owner reports indicate occasional steering feedback inconsistencies that warrant a thorough test drive.

**Steering Wheel Resistance**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note unexpected resistance or sticking sensations in the steering wheel, particularly noticeable during minor corrections at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspection by a qualified technician is recommended; potential remedies may involve servicing or lubricating internal steering components such as the worm gear.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V487000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=CX-3&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rearview camera image may not display properly by showing a flickering or distorted image. A rearview camera that does not display properly can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Mazda CX-3 remains an attractive option for used car buyers seeking style and efficiency in a small crossover. Because owner complaints highlight potential steering resistance at highway speeds, a careful test drive focusing on steering feel is strongly advised before finalizing any purchase.
