**Introduction:**
The 2016 Mazda CX-3 is a subcompact crossover SUV powered by a 2.0L inline-4 engine and available with either a 6-speed automatic or manual transmission. The engine produces 146 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque. Praised for its stylish design, fuel efficiency, and engaging driving dynamics, the CX-3 has some noted drawbacks, including a cramped rear seat and limited cargo space.

**Engine**
**2.0L Inline-4**

* **Check Engine Light Illuminating**
    * Description/Symptoms: The check engine light may illuminate due to a faulty mass airflow sensor.
    * Solution: Replace the mass airflow sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil may leak from the valve cover gasket.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation and Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk during gear changes.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: 17V191000
* **Delayed Engagement**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may delay in engaging when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: 19V704000

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts Failing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front strut mounts may fail, causing excessive noise and vibration.
    * Solution: Replace the front strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Sway Bar End Links Failing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear sway bar end links may fail, causing clunking noises while driving.
    * Solution: Replace the rear sway bar end links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Keyless Entry System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The keyless entry system may fail to unlock the doors or start the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the keyless entry module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The exterior door handles may break or become loose.
    * Solution: Replace the door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper motor may fail, preventing the wipers from working.
    * Solution: Replace the windshield wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Lid Breaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The plastic center console lid may break off.
    * Solution: Replace the center console lid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Squeaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may squeak due to loose panels or components.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the loose panels or components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2016 Mazda CX-3 has some common issues affecting various components. While the majority of the issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, the transmission problems can be more costly and potentially more serious. Overall, the CX-3 is generally a reliable vehicle with issues that are not widespread.
