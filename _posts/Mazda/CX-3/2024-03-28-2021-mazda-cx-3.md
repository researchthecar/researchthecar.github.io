**2021 Mazda CX-3**

The 2021 Mazda CX-3 is a subcompact crossover SUV that offers a comfortable and sporty ride. It comes with a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 148 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The CX-3 is known for its stylish design, responsive handling, and fuel efficiency. However, it has received some complaints about reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter Skyactiv-G I4 Engine**

  * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Skyactiv-Drive Automatic Transmission**

  * **Delayed or Rough Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough gear changes, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Transmission software update or solenoid replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arms:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear on the control arm bushings, causing clunking or rattling noises.
    * Solution: Control arm replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing faulty electrical components, such as the alternator or parasitic drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windshield may crack spontaneously or with minor impacts.
    * Solution: Windshield replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard panels rattle or vibrate, especially on rough roads.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing loose trim clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Mazda CX-3 has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While it offers a pleasant driving experience, some owners have encountered mechanical issues that can be costly to repair. The engine and transmission issues are the most concerning, as they can affect the vehicle's performance and longevity. If considering this model, it is recommended to research the specific issues and consult with a trusted mechanic before making a purchase decision.
