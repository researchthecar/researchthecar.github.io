# 2007 Mazda RX-8

### Overview
The 2007 Mazda RX-8, known for its unique rotary engine and sporty handling, presents a mixed bag in terms of long-term reliability. While its performance and driving dynamics are highly praised, certain mechanical components, particularly the rotary engine, require diligent maintenance and can be prone to specific issues if not properly cared for. Owners often report satisfaction with the car's handling and unique character, but the specialized nature of the Renesis engine means that potential buyers should be aware of its maintenance needs and potential repair costs. This analysis focuses on common reported issues beyond standard wear and tear.

---


## Engine

### 1.3L Renesis Rotary (Naturally Aspirated)
**Apex Seal Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of compression, rough idle, difficulty starting (especially when hot), reduced power, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Engine rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000+

**Eccentric Shaft Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Knocking or rattling noise from the engine, especially under load; potential for catastrophic engine failure.
- **Solution:** Engine rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $7,000+

**Cooling System Issues (Overheating)**
- **Symptoms:** Engine overheating, coolant leaks, potential for blown coolant seals leading to engine damage.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace radiator, water pump, thermostat, hoses; flush cooling system; address coolant seal issues if present.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000

**Ignition System Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, check engine light, poor acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replace spark plugs, ignition coils, and potentially ignition wires.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Synchronizer Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mazda RX-8 is a performance-oriented vehicle with a unique rotary engine that demands specific care. While the chassis, suspension, and general electrical/exterior/interior components are generally reliable, the Renesis engine is the primary area of concern. Apex seal wear, bearing failures, and cooling system issues are the most significant potential problems, often requiring costly rebuilds. The manual transmission can also experience clutch and synchronizer wear. Prospective buyers should prioritize vehicles with a documented maintenance history, especially regarding engine oil changes and proper warm-up/cool-down procedures. Regular compression tests are highly recommended before purchase.
