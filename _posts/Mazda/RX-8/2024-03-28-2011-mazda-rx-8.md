**Introduction:**

The 2011 Mazda RX-8 is a sporty coupe known for its unique rotary engine, stylish design, and agile handling. It was offered with a single engine and transmission combination:

- 1.3L RENESIS Rotary Engine (232 hp, 159 lb-ft torque)
- 6-Speed Manual or 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

**Engine**

- **Apex Seal Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idling, increased oil consumption
  - Solution: Replace apex seals
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Oil Pump Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Low oil pressure, engine overheating
  - Solution: Replace oil pump
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

- **6-Speed Manual:**
  - **Difficulty Shifting:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Gears grinding, not engaging properly
    - Solution: Replace worn synchronizers
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
  - **Clutch Wear:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, slipping
    - Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200

- **6-Speed Automatic:**
  - **Transmission Slippage:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, hesitation when shifting
    - Solution: Overhaul transmission or replace torque converter
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
  - **Solenoid Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Check Engine light, transmission shifting erratically
    - Solution: Replace faulty solenoid
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Suspension**

- **Strut Mount Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, reduced handling, uneven tire wear
  - Solution: Replace strut mounts
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per corner
- **Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise, reduced handling
  - Solution: Replace worn bushings
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300 per side

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, Check Engine light
  - Solution: Replace alternator
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
- **Starter Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the vehicle
  - Solution: Replace starter motor
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Exterior**

- **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors
  - Solution: Replace actuator
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per door
- **Headlight Condensation:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight assembly
  - Solution: Remove condensation, seal leaks
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $100+

**Interior**

- **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working, Check Engine light illuminated
  - Solution: Replace instrument cluster
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
- **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: No air flow from vents, whistling noise
  - Solution: Replace blower motor
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary:**

The 2011 Mazda RX-8 has a few known issues, but the severity and cost of these problems vary depending on the specific issue. Engine issues, such as apex seal failure, can be costly to repair, while electrical problems, such as alternator failure, are typically less expensive. Overall, the RX-8 is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues listed above before purchasing.
