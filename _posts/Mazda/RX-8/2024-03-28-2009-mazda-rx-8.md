**Introduction:**

The 2009 Mazda RX-8 is a compact sports car known for its unique rotary engine and sporty handling. It is offered with a 1.3-liter naturally aspirated Renesis rotary engine producing 232 horsepower and 159 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**Renesis 1.3L Rotary**

* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Apex Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Severe engine knocking, loss of compression, oil burning
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace engine
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, poor fuel economy, engine hesitation
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Shift Linkage Adjustment:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Adjust shift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, fluid leak
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leak, rough shifting, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace transmission seals or gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor handling, bouncy ride, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery discharge, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't crank, clicking noises
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Quarter Panel Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots and bubbling on quarter panels
    * Solution: Patch or replace quarter panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation buildup inside headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Audio Head Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No sound, display malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace audio head unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows don't open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace window motor or switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2009 Mazda RX-8 can experience a range of issues, with engine problems being the most severe and costly. Issues with the rotary engine can be expensive to fix, while transmission and suspension problems are typically less problematic. Electrical and exterior issues are not uncommon, but are generally less serious and less expensive to address. Interior issues are relatively minor, with audio head unit failures and power window failures being the most common. Overall, the RX-8 can be a rewarding car to own for enthusiasts who are willing to deal with potential reliability issues and maintenance costs.
