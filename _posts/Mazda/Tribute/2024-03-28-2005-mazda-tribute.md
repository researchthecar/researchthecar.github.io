**Introduction**

The 2005 Mazda Tribute is a compact SUV that has been discontinued. It is available with three engine options: a 2.3L inline-four, a 3.0L V6, and a 2.3L inline-four with a turbocharger. The base 2.3L inline-four produces 153 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.0L V6 produces 200 horsepower and 192 lb-ft of torque. The turbocharged 2.3L inline-four produces 242 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque.

All three engines are available with either a five-speed manual or a four-speed automatic transmission.

The Tribute is generally well-regarded for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and comfortable ride. However, it has received some complaints about its lack of power and poor handling.

**Engine**

* **2.3L Inline-Four**

  * **Intake Manifold Runner Control Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold runner control valve can fail, causing the engine to lose power and stall.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold runner control valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The EGR valve can fail, causing the engine to run rough and emit excessive exhaust gas.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **3.0L V6**

  * **No Issues Found**

* **2.3L Inline-Four with Turbocharger**

  * **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail, causing the engine to lose power and emit excessive smoke.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200-$1800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2005 Mazda Tribute is a reliable and fuel-efficient SUV that has received some complaints about its lack of power and poor handling. The most common issues with the Tribute include intake manifold runner control valve failure, EGR valve failure, transmission slipping, alternator failure, starter failure, climate control malfunction, power window failure, and dashboard cracks. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most can be repaired for a reasonable price.
