**Introduction:**

The 2011 Mazda Tribute is a compact SUV that was introduced in 2000. It shares a platform and powertrain options with the Ford Escape. The Tribute is available with a choice of two engines: a 2.3-liter four-cylinder engine with 153 horsepower and 152 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0-liter V6 engine with 203 horsepower and 199 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to a five-speed manual or a five-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2011 Mazda Tribute praise its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and comfortable ride. However, some owners have experienced issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems.

**Engine**

**2.3-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Intake manifold runner control valve failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Causes the engine to run rough and may cause a loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold runner control valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter V6 Engine**

* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Results in a rattling noise from the engine and may cause the engine to fail.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficult shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Drivers may experience difficulty shifting gears, especially when the transmission is cold.
    * Solution: Replace the shift linkage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Five-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip out of gear, especially when accelerating or shifting.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front strut failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front struts may fail, causing a loss of stability and a rough ride.
    * Solution: Replace the front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, resulting in a loss of electrical power and a dead battery.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Solution: Identify and repair the cause of the battery drain, such as a faulty electrical component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust may develop on the body panels, especially on older vehicles.
    * Solution: Treat the rust with a rust remover and apply a protective coating.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak water into the interior of the vehicle, causing damage to the upholstery and electronics.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty audio system**
    * Description/Symptoms: The audio system may malfunction, causing speakers to cut out or the system to shut down completely.
    * Solution: Replace the audio system components, such as the head unit or speakers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Air conditioning failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air conditioning system may fail, resulting in a lack of cooling or heating.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the cause of the failure, such as a refrigerant leak or faulty compressor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2011 Mazda Tribute is a generally reliable vehicle, but some owners have experienced issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical systems, and interior components. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the problem and the necessary repairs. It is important to note that the frequency of these issues may not represent all Tribute models, and many vehicles may not experience any major problems.
