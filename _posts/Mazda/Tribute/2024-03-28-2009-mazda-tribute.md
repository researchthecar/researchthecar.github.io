**Introduction:**

The 2009 Mazda Tribute is a compact SUV that offers a versatile and economical option. It comes with a choice of two engine options: a 2.5L inline-4 and a 3.0L V6. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. While praised for its sporty handling and comfortable interior, some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.5L Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, low oil levels, oil smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace piston rings or engine rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed or rough gear changes
    * Solution: Replace transmission filter and fluid, or major transmission repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Sway Bar End Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front suspension, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Short Circuit:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, intermittent electrical problems, warning lights on dashboard
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate won't open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No airflow from heater or air conditioner, strange noises from blower motor
    * Solution: Replace blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Seat Heaters Inoperative:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Front seat heaters don't work
    * Solution: Replace faulty seat heater element or wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2009 Mazda Tribute has received mixed reviews with some reliability issues reported. The most common issues include intake manifold gasket leaks, oil consumption, and transmission slippage. Repairs for these issues can be costly, ranging from $300 to $4,000 or more. However, it's important to note that not all vehicles will experience these problems. The Tribute offers a comfortable and sporty driving experience when in good condition.
