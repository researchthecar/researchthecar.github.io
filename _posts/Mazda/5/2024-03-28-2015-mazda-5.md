**2015 Mazda 5**

The 2015 Mazda 5 is a compact minivan that offers seating for up to six passengers. It is powered by a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 157 horsepower and 163 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission.

Owners appreciate the Mazda 5's comfortable interior, versatile seating arrangements, and responsive handling. However, some have reported issues with the vehicle's durability and reliability.

**Engine**

**2.5L Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine burns excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups. May lead to engine damage if not addressed.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or slip while shifting gears, causing a loss of power or acceleration.
    * Solution: Transmission repair or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Premature Tire Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven tire wear or rapid tire wear on all four corners, indicating suspension alignment issues.
    * Solution: Suspension component replacement and alignment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature rust or corrosion on the vehicle's undercarriage, especially in areas exposed to moisture.
    * Solution: Undercarriage repair or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not function properly, leading to insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Climate control module repair or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Mazda 5 has received mixed reviews in terms of reliability. While some owners have reported minor issues, others have encountered more significant problems, such as excessive oil consumption and transmission failures. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
