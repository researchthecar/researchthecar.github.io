**Introduction:**

The 2011 Mazda CX-7 is a midsize SUV known for its sporty handling and upscale interior. It comes with two engine options and two transmission options:

* **Engine Options:**
    * 2.5L Inline-4 (161 hp, 161 lb-ft of torque)
    * 2.3L Turbocharged Inline-4 (244 hp, 258 lb-ft of torque)
* **Transmission Options:**
    * 6-speed Manual Transmission
    * 6-speed Automatic Transmission

While the CX-7 has received praise for its performance and features, it has also been plagued by several issues that have affected its reliability.

**Engine**

**2.5L Inline-4**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle burns excessive oil, causing low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and oil seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Timing Chain Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the engine during startup or acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor engine performance, increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**2.3L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, smoke from the engine, loud noises.
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, decreased fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into reverse.
    * Solution: Replace or repair clutch, transmission gears.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Car hesitates or slips when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noise from the front end.
    * Solution: Replace strut mount.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely or has difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Check for electrical shorts or faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle becomes loose or difficult to operate.
    * Solution: Replace door handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appear on the dashboard.
    * Solution: Replace dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2011 Mazda CX-7 has a range of issues that affect its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. While some of these issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be quite costly. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can significantly impact the reliability and longevity of the vehicle.
