**Introduction:**

The 1993 Mazda B2200 is a mid-size pickup truck that was produced from 1989 to 1998. It was available with either a 2.2L inline-four engine producing 110 horsepower and 135 lb-ft of torque or a 2.6L inline-four engine producing 125 horsepower and 150 lb-ft of torque. Both engines were paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

The B2200 was praised for its reliability, durability, and affordability. However, it was also criticized for its lack of power and refinement.

**Engine**

**2.2L I4**

* **Blown Head Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaking from the engine, overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vacuum leak, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Belt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, catastrophic engine damage if belt breaks while driving
    * Solution: Replace timing belt and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.6L I4**

* **No Major Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replace clutch kit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Synchronizer Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second or third gear
    * Solution: Replace synchronizer rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the pan or seals
    * Solution: Replace or repair leaking components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting erratically or not shifting at all
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Coil Spring Sag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride height, premature tire wear
    * Solution: Replace coil springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per axle
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ball Joint Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling suspension, difficulty steering
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 per joint
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering lights, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace ignition coil
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Brown or orange spots on body panels, especially on fenders and rocker panels
    * Solution: Sand and repaint affected areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Differential Seal Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Diff fluid leaking from the rear differential
    * Solution: Replace differential seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks forming on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or have it repaired
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Fabric Tearing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rips or tears in the seat fabric
    * Solution: Replace or repair seat upholstery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1993 Mazda B2200 is generally a reliable and durable pickup truck. However, it does have some common issues that can be expensive to fix, such as head gasket failure and transmission clutch failure. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues should be considered before purchasing or owning a B2200.
