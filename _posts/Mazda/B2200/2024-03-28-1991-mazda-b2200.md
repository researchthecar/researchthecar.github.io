**Introduction:**

The 1991 Mazda B2200 is a compact pickup truck that offers a blend of functionality and affordability. It is powered by a 2.2-liter inline-four engine, paired with a five-speed manual transmission as standard. The B2200 has a payload capacity of 1,500 pounds and a towing capacity of 2,000 pounds. It is known for its reliability, fuel efficiency, and comfortable cabin. However, like any vehicle, it has its fair share of issues.

**Engine**

**2.2-Liter Inline-Four**

* **Premature Valve Seat Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, engine knocking, and reduced power.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected valve seats and valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Five-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially when cold.
    * Solution: Replacing or repairing the worn synchronizers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping sounds when turning or driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacing the worn ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per joint.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Leaf Spring Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride height and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replacing the sagging leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting, and battery drain.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Solenoid Sticking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks but does not start.
    * Solution: Replacing the solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No airflow from the vents.
    * Solution: Replacing the blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400.
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Seat Recliner Mechanism Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty adjusting the seat position.
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing the recliner mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1991 Mazda B2200 is a reliable and practical pickup truck with relatively few major issues. The most common issues are related to the engine and suspension, which can be expensive to repair. However, the cost of repairs is generally lower than that of other vehicles in its class.
