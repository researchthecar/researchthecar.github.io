**Introduction:** The 1994 Mazda RX-7 is a legendary sports car renowned for its unique rotary engine and exceptional handling. Powered by a 1.3-liter twin-turbocharged rotary engine, it generates 255 horsepower and 217 lb-ft of torque. The RX-7 is highly acclaimed for its agile handling and aggressive appearance but has also been known to experience certain mechanical issues.

**Engine**

**1.3L Twin-Turbo Rotary Engine**

* **Apex Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of compression, rough idling, excessive smoking
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil usage
    * Solution: Frequent oil changes, rebuild of the oil control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost pressure, engine knocking
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear Grinding:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, erratic shifting
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises over bumps, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace the bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Control Arm Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Shorts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, erratic electrical behavior
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the short circuit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Surface rust, corrosion
    * Solution: Apply rust-proofing treatment, repair rusted areas
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling door panels
    * Solution: Tighten or adjust the door panel connections
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative air conditioning, heating, or ventilation
    * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1994 Mazda RX-7 is a desirable sports car with a cult following, but it is not without its known issues. The rotary engine requires specialized maintenance and can be prone to apex seal failure. The transmission can experience issues with both the manual and automatic options. The suspension bushings and electrical system can also cause problems. While these issues can be costly to repair, the overall experience and legacy of the RX-7 continue to attract enthusiasts.
