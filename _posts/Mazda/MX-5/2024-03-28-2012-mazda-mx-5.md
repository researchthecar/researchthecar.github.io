**Introduction**

The 2012 Mazda MX-5 is a compact, two-seater sports car that is known for its handling, performance, and affordability. It is available with a 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 167 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque. The MX-5 is available with either a six-speed manual transmission or a six-speed automatic transmission.

People like the MX-5 for its fun-to-drive nature, its affordability, and its styling. However, some people complain about the MX-5's small size, its lack of power, and its cramped interior.
