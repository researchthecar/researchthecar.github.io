## 2013 Mazda MX-5

The 2013 Mazda MX-5 is a lightweight, rear-wheel-drive sports car available with a 2.0-liter Skyactiv-G engine producing 167 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission.

People like the MX-5's sharp handling, responsive steering, and its affordable price tag. However, some find the interior to be cramped and the ride to be harsh.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Skyactiv-G Engine**

* **Stuck piston rings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace pistons and rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into 2nd or 3rd gear.
    * Solution: Replace synchronizers or gear set.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Delayed shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Sagging rear springs**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear of the vehicle appears lower than the front, leading to poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace rear springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminates, electrical systems malfunction.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks do not work properly or become inoperative.
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Rattling dashboard**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard vibrates or rattles while driving.
    * Solution: Apply sound deadening material or tighten loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2013 Mazda MX-5 is a fun-to-drive sports car, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems affect the engine, transmission, and electrical system, with estimated repair costs ranging from $200 to $3,000+. While these issues can be frustrating, they are not widespread and should not deter potential buyers from considering the MX-5.
