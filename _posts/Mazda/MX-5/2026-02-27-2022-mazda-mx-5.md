# 2022 Mazda MX-5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Mazda MX-5 continues to be a celebrated lightweight sports car, offering engaging handling and a responsive powertrain. While owner feedback is generally positive, prospective buyers should review a few reported concerns regarding manual transmission synchronizers and unexpected driver-assistance activations based on NHTSA owner complaints.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Skyactiv-G 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 181 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque, providing lively performance for the lightweight roadster. This powerplant is generally regarded as robust and reliable in daily driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission is praised for its short throws and mechanical feel, though some owners have noted durability concerns with the synchronizers.

**Manual Transmission Synchronizer Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported erratic gear shift operation, difficulty changing gears, and complete transmission failure due to failing synchros.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection and potential transmission rebuild or replacement of the affected synchronizer components.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission features steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters for a sportier feel, offering a smooth alternative to the manual gearbox with solid dependability.

## Electrical

### Roof and Alarm System

Electrical integration manages convenience features and top operation, with rare owner reports of interlocks preventing vehicle mobility.

**Retractable Roof and Alarm Interference**
- **Symptoms:** The rooftop mechanism fails to remain sealed in the open or closed position, which in turn causes the vehicle's alarm system to disarm and prevents vehicle movement.
- **Solution:** Inspect the convertible top position sensors, wiring harnesses, and related control modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Seats and LATCH System

The cabin prioritizes driver ergonomics and comfort, though specific configuration discrepancies should be verified during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Missing Child Seat LATCH Hooks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note that child safety seat LATCH hooks are not present in the vehicle despite being referenced in the user manual.
- **Solution:** Consult with a local dealership to verify proper child restraint installation options and factory specifications.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features assist with driver awareness, though occasional sensor phantom activations have been reported by vehicle owners.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may suddenly and without warning activate its emergency braking system while driving at speed in clear conditions.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's sensor alignment and software calibration inspected by a qualified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V695000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=MX-5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A software error in the air bag control module may cause the front air bags to deploy with excessive force during a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that deploys with excessive force can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Sophisticated Air Bag Sensor (SAS) control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Mazda MX-5 remains an appealing choice for driving enthusiasts, combining responsive handling with an energetic engine. While most owners experience trouble-free motoring, used-car buyers should thoroughly test the manual transmission shifting and verify proper operation of driver-assistance sensors and convertible top electronics during a pre-purchase inspection.
