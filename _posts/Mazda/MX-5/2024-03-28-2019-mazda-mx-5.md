**Introduction to the 2019 Mazda MX-5**

The 2019 Mazda MX-5 is a sporty and stylish roadster that delivers a thrilling driving experience. It is available with a naturally aspirated 2.0-liter Skyactiv-G engine that produces 181 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque, or a 2.5-liter Skyactiv-G engine that produces 184 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque. Both engines can be paired with a 6-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission.

The MX-5 is known for its responsive handling, sharp steering, and comfortable interior. However, like any vehicle, it has some issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**2.0L Skyactiv-G Engine**

* **Knocking Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A knocking noise is heard from the engine when accelerating or under load.
    * Solution: Replacing the engine timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5L Skyactiv-G Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear Shift Linkage Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second and fourth gears.
    * Solution: Replacing the transmission gear shift linkage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear Lower Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises and vibrations from the suspension, especially over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacing the rear lower control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replacing the battery or checking for parasitic electrical drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Trim Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the dashboard trim.
    * Solution: Installing additional padding or applying adhesive to secure the trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Mazda MX-5 is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues that owners have experienced. The most common problems include knocking noises from the engine, gear shift linkage issues with the manual transmission, and rear lower control arm bushing issues with the suspension. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most can be resolved for a reasonable price.
