### Overview
The 2021 Mazda MX-5 Miata is a highly regarded sports car celebrated for its engaging driving dynamics and reliable performance. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, examining common areas of potential concern for the specific engine and transmission configurations offered for the 2021 model year. The MX-5's reputation for dependability is largely upheld, with few widespread mechanical or electrical issues reported by owners.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Mazda MX-5 Miata demonstrates excellent technical reliability across its core mechanical components, including the 2.0L Skyactiv-G engine and both manual and automatic transmissions. Suspension systems are robust, and exterior elements like the soft top have not shown significant widespread issues. The interior generally holds up well. The most commonly noted, albeit minor, concerns relate to occasional glitches within the infotainment system, typically resolvable with software updates. Overall, the MX-5 is a dependable choice for enthusiasts seeking a fun-to-drive vehicle with a strong track record for reliability.
