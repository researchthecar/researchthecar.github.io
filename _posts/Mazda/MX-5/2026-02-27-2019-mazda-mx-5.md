# 2019 Mazda MX-5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Mazda MX-5 Miata continues to be celebrated as an engaging, lightweight sports car with enthusiastic driving dynamics. For used-car buyers, the mechanical foundation is generally robust, though limited owner reports highlight minor electrical, steering, and paint concerns worth noting during an inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L SkyActiv-G 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 181 horsepower and 151 lb-ft of torque, offering lively performance and strong overall reliability. It is the sole engine option available for this model year.

**Fuel System Component Availability**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced delays waiting for parts related to fuel system campaigns.
- **Solution:** Verify that all open manufacturer campaigns have been completed by an authorized dealer using the VIN.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The crisp 6-speed manual transmission pairs rear-wheel drive with direct mechanical feedback and proves durable under normal operation.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The available 6-speed automatic transmission offers smooth shifts for relaxed cruising, supported by standard powertrain reliability.

## Steering

### Steering Angle Sensor and Power Steering

Issues related to steering electronics and sensors are occasionally reported by owners.

**Steering Angle Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of warning lights such as the ABS and brake lights, alongside intermittent power steering loss.
- **Solution:** Have the steering angle sensor and electronic control modules inspected or replaced by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Paint Finish

Exterior finishes are generally attractive, though specific premium paint options may show road wear more readily.

**Excessive Paint Chipping**
- **Symptoms:** Accumulation of small chips and blemishes on the front bumper and hood areas.
- **Solution:** Consider applying a protective paint protection film (PPF) to vulnerable front-end surfaces.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Cabin safety components generally function as intended, though isolated mechanical wear can occur.

**Seat Belt Retraction Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belts fail to retract or tighten properly on their own, requiring manual feeding into the housing.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt mechanisms for binding or wear, and replace the assembly if the retractor fails to function correctly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V875000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=MX-5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V695000: Air Bags: Air Bag/Restraint Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mazda&model=MX-5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A software error in the air bag control module may cause the front air bags to deploy with excessive force during a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that deploys with excessive force can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Sophisticated Air Bag Sensor (SAS) control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Mazda MX-5 is a rewarding pre-owned purchase that combines engaging handling with dependable core mechanics. Buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection checking the steering electronics, paint condition, and verifying that all recall services have been properly performed.
