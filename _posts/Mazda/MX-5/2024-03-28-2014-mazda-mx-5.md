**2014 Mazda MX-5**

The 2014 Mazda MX-5 is a two-seat roadster that is known for its handling and performance. It is powered by a 2.0L inline-four engine that produces 155 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a six-speed manual or automatic transmission. The MX-5 is available in two trim levels: Sport and Grand Touring.

People who like the MX-5 praise its handling, performance, and affordability. However, some people have complained about the MX-5's lack of cargo space and its relatively low horsepower.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-Four**

* **Valve Spring Failure:** **Symptoms** - Excessive engine noise or misfire at high RPMs. **Solution** - Replace valve springs. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** **Symptoms** - Engine rattle or knocking at startup. **Solution** - Replace timing chain tensioner. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $500 - $1,000
* **Oil Pressure Sensor Failure:** **Symptoms** - Oil pressure light comes on at idle. **Solution** - Replace oil pressure sensor. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $100 - $200

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Gears:** **Symptoms** - Difficulty shifting gears, especially into second gear. **Solution** - Replace or repair transmission. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $1,500 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** **Symptoms** - Noise or vibration when driving over bumps. **Solution** - Replace strut mounts. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $300 - $600
* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:** **Symptoms** - Clunking or rattling noise from the rear of the vehicle. **Solution** - Replace rear sway bar links. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $100 - $200

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** **Symptoms** - Battery goes dead after a period of time. **Solution** - Diagnose and repair electrical system. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $200 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Tear:** **Symptoms** - Hole or tear in the convertible top. **Solution** - Replace or repair convertible top. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **HVAC Fan Failure:** **Symptoms** - HVAC fan does not blow air. **Solution** - Replace HVAC fan motor. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $200 - $400
* **Instrument Cluster Failure:** **Symptoms** - Gauges or warning lights do not function. **Solution** - Replace instrument cluster. **Estimated Cost to Fix** - $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2014 Mazda MX-5 is generally a reliable and fun-to-drive vehicle. However, there are a few potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most serious issues include valve spring failure, timing chain tensioner failure, and grinding gears. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic before purchasing.
