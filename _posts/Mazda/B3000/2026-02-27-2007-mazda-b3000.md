# 2007 Mazda B3000

### Overview
The 2007 Mazda B3000, a compact pickup truck, is generally considered a reliable vehicle, especially given its age. It shares many components with its Ford Ranger counterpart, benefiting from a proven platform. Owners often praise its durability and straightforward mechanicals. However, like any vehicle of this vintage, certain areas may require attention. This analysis focuses on common reported issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential mechanical and electrical concerns that owners might encounter. The B3000's robust construction contributes to its longevity, making it a practical choice for those seeking an affordable and dependable workhorse or daily driver.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L V6
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, engine misfires, rough idle, sweet smell from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Spark Plug Blowout**
- **Symptoms:** Engine misfires, loss of power, check engine light, audible popping sound from engine.
- **Solution:** Requires re-threading the spark plug hole with a Heli-Coil or Time-Sert, or potentially replacing the cylinder head.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200+


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** N/A
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** Varies


### 5-Speed Automatic (5R44E/5R55E)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, delayed engagement, harsh shifting.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter and potentially transmission fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, steering wander.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Leaf Spring Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced rear ride height, poor load-carrying capacity, rougher ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of sagging leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement, grinding noise when operating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Frame Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust on the frame rails, especially in areas with harsh winters and road salt.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, repair, or frame replacement (if severe).
- **Cost:** $200 - $2000+

**Tailgate Latch Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty opening or closing the tailgate, latch sticking.
- **Solution:** Lubrication or replacement of the tailgate latch mechanism.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on the highest setting, or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $200

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door locks not working consistently or at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the door lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Mazda B3000 is a generally dependable compact truck with a good reputation for reliability. The most common issues tend to be related to the 3.0L V6 engine, specifically intake manifold gasket leaks and potential spark plug blowout, which can be costly to repair. Automatic transmission torque converters can also be a point of failure. Suspension components like ball joints and leaf springs may require attention due to age and use. Electrical issues such as alternator failure and window regulator problems are not uncommon. Exterior rust, particularly on the frame, is a concern in certain climates. Interior issues are typically minor, like HVAC blower motor resistors or door lock actuators. Overall, with proper maintenance and addressing these potential issues, the B3000 can remain a reliable vehicle.
