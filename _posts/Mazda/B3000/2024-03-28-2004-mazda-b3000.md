**Introduction:**

The 2004 Mazda B3000 is a compact pickup truck available with four-wheel drive or two-wheel drive. It features a 3.0-liter V6 engine that produces 220 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque, and it can be paired with either a five-speed manual transmission or a four-speed automatic transmission. The B3000 is known for its comfortable interior, reliable engine, and off-road capabilities. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **3.0-liter V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idling, stalling, reduced power
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Five-Speed Manual**

**Transmission Gear Grinding**
* Description/Symptoms: Grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty shifting gears
* Solution: Replace or repair transmission gears
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

* **Four-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping gears, delayed shifting, erratic shifting
* Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery light illuminates, dimming lights, loss of electrical power
* Solution: Replace alternator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Tailgate Rust**
* Description/Symptoms: Rust developing on tailgate, leading to holes and weakened metal
* Solution: Replace tailgate or repair rust
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Airbag Deployment Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Airbag fails to deploy in a collision
* Solution: Replace airbag module
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
* Recalls: 04V271000

**Summary:**

The 2004 Mazda B3000 is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported are related to the intake manifold gasket, transmission gears, alternator, and tailgate rust. The severity of these issues can range from minor to severe, and the cost of repairs can be significant. However, the B3000 remains a popular choice for those seeking a capable and affordable compact pickup truck.
