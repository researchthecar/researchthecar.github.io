**Introduction:**

The 2017 Chevrolet City Express is a compact cargo van offered with either a 2.0L I4 engine or a 2.4L I4 engine. Both engines are mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 2.0L engine produces 131 horsepower and 141 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.4L engine produces 181 horsepower and 173 lb-ft of torque. The City Express is known for its maneuverability, fuel efficiency, and affordability but has received criticism for its lack of advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**2.0L I4 Engine**

**Coolant Leak from Intake Manifold Gasket**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the intake manifold gasket, causing a decrease in coolant levels and overheating.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
Recalls: None Found

**2.4L I4 Engine**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shift Problems**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or slip during shifting, especially when cold.
Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter. In some cases, the transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $4,000
Recalls: None Found

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is turned off.
Solution: Check for parasitic drains, such as a faulty alternator or ignition switch. Replace any faulty components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Wind Noise from Roof Rail**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise comes from the roof rail area.
Solution: Install wind deflectors or adjust the roof rail position.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system does not blow air or only blows hot air.
Solution: Replace the blower motor or climate control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Chevrolet City Express has received reports of a few issues, including coolant leaks, shift problems, battery drains, wind noise, and climate control malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix while others can be more serious and costly. Overall, the City Express is a reliable and affordable cargo van, but owners should be aware of potential issues and budget accordingly.
