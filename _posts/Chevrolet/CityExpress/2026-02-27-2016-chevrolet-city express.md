# 2016 Chevrolet City Express

### Overview
The 2016 Chevrolet City Express is a compact cargo van designed for urban utility and light commercial use. It shares its platform with the Nissan NV200, inheriting much of its mechanical DNA. This van is generally considered a straightforward and relatively reliable work vehicle, particularly for its intended purpose of hauling goods in city environments. Its focus on practicality and fuel efficiency makes it an attractive option for small businesses. While it doesn't boast luxury features, its core components are designed for durability under typical commercial driving conditions. Owners often praise its maneuverability and ease of parking in tight urban spaces.
