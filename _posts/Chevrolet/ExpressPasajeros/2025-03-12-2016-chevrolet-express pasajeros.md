The 2016 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros is a full-size passenger van known for its spacious interior and workhorse capabilities. Engine options for this model year include the 4.8L Vortec V8 (280 hp, 295 lb-ft of torque), the 6.0L Vortec V8 (342 hp, 373 lb-ft of torque), and the 6.6L Duramax Turbo Diesel V8 (260 hp, 525 lb-ft of torque). All engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Owners often appreciate the van's reliability, towing capacity, and generous cargo space. Common dislikes include its dated design, lower fuel economy, and less refined ride compared to newer vans.

**Engine**

**4.8L Vortec V8**

*   **No Issues Found**

**6.0L Vortec V8**

*   **No Issues Found**

**6.6L Duramax Turbo Diesel V8**

*   **Fuel Injector Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel economy, black smoke from exhaust.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injector(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+ (per injector, depending on location and labor)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic (All Engines)**

*   **Torque Converter Shudder**
    *   Description/Symptoms: A shaking or vibrating sensation, particularly at lower speeds during light acceleration.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the torque converter with an updated design. Transmission fluid flush and fill may also be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Leaf Spring Sagging/Cracking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the van sitting lower than normal, reduced ride height, poor handling, potentially visible cracks in leaf springs.
    *   Solution: Replacement of leaf spring pack.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ (per side)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Power door locks failing to lock or unlock, erratic operation, clicking sounds from within the door.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty door lock actuator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+ (per door)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Side Door Roller Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficult or impossible to open or close the side doors. Grinding or binding noises when the side door is operated.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the roller assembly or the entire side door depending on the severity of the damage.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150-$500+ (per door)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Summary**

The 2016 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros generally proves to be a reliable work van, but it has issues to look out for. The most concerning issues revolve around the torque converter shutter and fuel injector failure, which can lead to costly repairs. Sagging or cracking leaf springs and failing door lock actuators are also concerns to be aware of.
