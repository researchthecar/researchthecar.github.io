The 2022 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros (Passenger Van) is a full-size van known for its durability, spaciousness, and towing capacity. It's a popular choice for large families, shuttle services, and commercial applications. Engine options include a 4.3L V6 (276 hp, 298 lb-ft of torque), a 6.6L V8 (401 hp, 464 lb-ft of torque), and a 2.8L Duramax Turbo-Diesel (181 hp, 369 lb-ft of torque). The 4.3L V6 is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, while the 6.6L V8 and the 2.8L Duramax Turbo-Diesel are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. People appreciate the Express Pasajeros for its workhorse capabilities, simple design making it easy to repair, and robust chassis. Common dislikes include its outdated design, fuel economy, and relatively basic interior features compared to newer vans on the market.

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Leaf Spring Sag**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the van sits lower than normal, especially when loaded. Can lead to reduced ride quality and handling.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaf springs with new ones, possibly with a heavier-duty option for increased load capacity. Installation of air bags can also help.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Battery drains overnight or within a short period of time, preventing the van from starting. Could be due to a parasitic draw from an aftermarket accessory, a faulty module, or a failing battery.
    *   Solution: Diagnose the source of the parasitic draw using a multimeter. Disconnect aftermarket accessories one by one to see if the drain stops. Test the battery and alternator. Replace faulty components as needed.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Sliding Door Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sliding door is difficult to open or close, makes excessive noise, or gets stuck. Could be due to worn rollers, a misaligned track, or a damaged latch mechanism.
    *   Solution: Inspect the rollers and track for wear or damage. Lubricate the rollers and track. Adjust the alignment of the track. Replace worn or damaged parts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Blower motor stops working, resulting in no air flow from the vents. Could be due to a faulty blower motor, resistor, or wiring.
    *   Solution: Test the blower motor and resistor. Replace faulty components. Check the wiring for damage and repair as needed.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen freezes, becomes unresponsive, or crashes intermittently. Can be a software or hardware issue.
    *   Solution: Try resetting the system. Check for software updates and install them. If the problem persists, the unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1000+ (replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros generally appears to be a reliable vehicle. The most common complaints are regarding suspension sagging, electrical drain issues, sliding door performance, and some interior components. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to potentially more costly repairs, but overall the number of issues reported is low. Costs for repairs can vary based on the specific problem and the location where the repairs are performed.
