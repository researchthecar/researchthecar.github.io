**Introduction:**

The 2017 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros is a full-size passenger van powered by a single engine option paired with two transmission choices. It offers a spacious interior and commendable towing capacity. While praised for its reliability and versatility, it has also faced some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **6.6L V8 Gasoline**
    * **Check Engine Light Due to Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The check engine light illuminates, and the vehicle may experience rough idling, starting problems, or decreased fuel efficiency.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injector(s).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the intake manifold gasket area, causing overheating and potentially leading to engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or slips during gear changes, or the vehicle may fail to shift gears.
        * Solution: Replace or repair the faulty transmission components, such as the solenoid or valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Axle Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A grinding or humming noise coming from the front end of the vehicle, especially during turns or while driving at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Replace the failing front axle bearing assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery fails to charge or discharges quickly, leading to electrical system issues or starting problems.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Module Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks malfunction, failing to lock or unlock properly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty door lock module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate hinge breaks, causing the tailgate to drop or become misaligned.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty tailgate hinge.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Loose Exterior Trim:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The exterior trim pieces, such as side moldings or fender skirts, become loose or detached.
    * Solution: Reattach or replace the loose trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel or instrument cluster rattles or makes noise when driving on rough roads.
    * Solution: Inspect and tighten the dashboard components or apply padding to reduce vibrations.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Defroster Vent Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The defroster vents fail to properly direct airflow, causing fogging or icing on the windshield.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the faulty defroster vent assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros has reported several issues, ranging from minor annoyances to more severe mechanical failures. While some issues may be addressed under warranty, others can be costly to repair. Overall, the vehicle's reliability may be compromised by these issues, but its spaciousness and versatility remain attractive features.
