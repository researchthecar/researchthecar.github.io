**2011 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros**

The 2011 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros is a full-size passenger van offered in two engine options: a 4.8L V8 and a 6.0L V8. The 4.8L V8 produces 280 horsepower and 296 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.0L V8 produces 323 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

The Express Pasajeros is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engines. However, it has received criticism for its below-average fuel economy, outdated design, and lack of modern safety features.

**Engine**

**4.8L V8**

* **Engine Misfire**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs or ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.0L V8**

* **Valve Cover Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak, engine noise
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid and filter, or rebuild/replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering during acceleration, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Ball Joint Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises during turns, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front suspension ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace rear leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No cranking when turning the key
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks or becomes loose
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Taillight Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or more taillights not working
    * Solution: Replace taillight bulb
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power window stuck or not working
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Panel Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard panel
    * Solution: Replace dashboard panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros has moderate severity issues with its suspension, electrical system, and exterior components. Costs to fix the issues typically range from $200 to $800+, with more severe issues costing up to $2,500+. The engine and transmission issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for under $1,000. Overall, the Express Pasajeros is a reliable van but can be expensive to maintain over time.
