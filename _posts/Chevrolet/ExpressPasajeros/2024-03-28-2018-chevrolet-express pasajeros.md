**Introduction:**

The 2018 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros is a full-size van available with either a 4.3L V6 engine or a 6.6L V8 engine, coupled with either a 6-speed automatic transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission. Despite its spacious interior and reliable performance, the vehicle has encountered several issues in various components.

**Engine**

**4.3L V6 Engine**

* **Oil Consumption:** Some vehicles experience excessive oil consumption, leading to a decrease in oil levels and potential engine damage.
* **Timing Chain Issues:** The timing chain may stretch or fail, causing engine misfires and potential catastrophic engine failure.
* **Exhaust Manifold Leak:** The exhaust manifold may crack or leak, leading to exhaust fumes entering the cabin and reduced engine performance.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.6L V8 Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors may fail, causing engine misfires, rough idling, and reduced fuel efficiency.
* **Glow Plug Issues:** The glow plugs may fail to activate, leading to difficult engine starting in cold weather.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Delayed Shifting:** The transmission may experience delayed or harsh shifting, causing drivability issues.
* **Slipping Gears:** The transmission may slip out of gear, leading to a loss of power and safety concerns.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-770

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:** The shift solenoids may fail, causing erratic shifting and potential transmission damage.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:** The front strut mounts may fail, causing a knocking or clunking noise and reduced handling performance.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Issues:** The rear leaf springs may sag or break, leading to reduced load capacity and handling problems.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some vehicles experience excessive battery drain, leading to difficulty starting and potential electrical system failure.
* **Alternator Issues:** The alternator may fail to charge the battery, leading to electrical problems and a dead battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-175

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:** The door handles may become loose or fall off, affecting the ability to open and close the doors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust Issues:** Some vehicles experience premature rusting on the undercarriage and body panels.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Issues:** The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system may malfunction, causing poor temperature control and ventilation problems.
* **Instrument Panel Illumination Failure:** The instrument panel illumination may fail, making it difficult to read gauges and controls.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $350
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros has encountered several issues in various components, ranging from engine problems to interior malfunctions. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and potentially costly. It's important for potential buyers and current owners to be aware of these issues and consider the potential financial implications before making a decision about this vehicle.
