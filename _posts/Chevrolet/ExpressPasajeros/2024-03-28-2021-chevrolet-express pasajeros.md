## 2021 Chevrolet Express Passenger Van

The 2021 Chevrolet Express Passenger Van is a full-size passenger van that offers spacious seating for up to 15 passengers. It is available with two engine options: a 4.3L V6 and a 6.6L V8. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Express Passenger Van is known for its comfortable ride, ample cargo space, and reliable performance. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

**4.3L V6**
**No Issues Found**

**6.6L V8**
**Engine Stalling**
Description/Symptoms: The engine may stall while driving or idling, resulting in a loss of power and potential safety hazard.
Solution: Replacement of the fuel injector assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: NHTSA 21V-392

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip out of gear, causing a loss of power and difficulty accelerating.
Solution: Replacement of the transmission control module (TCM).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Rear Leaf Springs**
**Sagging or Breaking**
Description/Symptoms: The rear leaf springs may sag or break, resulting in a lowered ride height and reduced handling.
Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
Solution: Replacement of the body control module (BCM) or electrical components that are causing the drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Sliding Door Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The sliding door may fail to open or close properly, creating difficulty accessing the passenger compartment.
Solution: Repair or replacement of the door mechanism.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may fail to heat or cool the cabin properly.
Solution: Replacement of the blend door actuator or other HVAC components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2021 Chevrolet Express Passenger Van offers a spacious and reliable driving experience, it has been reported to have some issues. The most common issues include engine stalling, transmission slipping, and rear leaf spring problems. The severity of these issues varies, and some may require costly repairs. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and address them promptly to ensure the safety and reliability of their vehicle.
