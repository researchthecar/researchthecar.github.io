## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights flickering, loss of power to accessories, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty wiring harnesses, grounds, or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start after sitting, battery dies prematurely.
- **Solution:** Identify parasitic draw from accessories or faulty charging system components.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


## Exterior

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The V6 and diesel engines, along with the available automatic transmissions, are robust and have few documented widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are within the electrical system, specifically related to intermittent power loss and battery drain, and some exterior components like door latches. Interior issues are less common, with the HVAC blower motor being a notable, though not universal, concern. Overall, for a vehicle designed for heavy-duty use, its reliability is commendable, with most potential issues being repairable at moderate costs.
