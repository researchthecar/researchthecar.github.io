**Introduction**

The 2016 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros is a full-size, heavy-duty van available in various configurations for both passenger and cargo transportation. The van is powered by a standard 6.0L V8 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. Optional powertrain options include a 4.8L V8 engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission and a 6.6L Duramax turbodiesel V8 engine paired with an Allison 1000 6-speed automatic transmission. People generally appreciate the Express Pasajeros' spacious cabin, strong powertrain, and versatility. However, it has received criticism for its outdated interior, poor fuel economy, and limited safety features.

**Engine**

**Summary**

The 2016 Chevrolet Express Pasajeros has several reported issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. While many of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, some, such as turbocharger failure or transmission solenoid failure, can be quite costly. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and have their vehicles regularly inspected and maintained to address them promptly.
