**Introduction**

The 2013 Chevrolet Cruze is a compact car that offers a comfortable ride, good fuel economy, and a spacious interior. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 1.4L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, a 1.8L naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 2.0L turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

While the Cruze is a popular choice for drivers, it has also been known to have some issues. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific problems that people have been having with the vehicles:

**Engine**

* **1.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Coolant Leak:** The coolant reservoir may crack and leak, causing the engine to overheat.
    * **Timing Chain Issues:** The timing chain may stretch or break, leading to catastrophic engine failure.
* **1.8L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** The intake manifold gasket may fail, causing a loss of power and poor fuel economy.
* **2.0L Turbocharged Diesel 4-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail, leading to a loss of power and increased emissions.

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may shift erratically or refuse to shift into gear.
* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure:** The clutch may wear prematurely, causing difficulty shifting gears.

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Mounts:** The front suspension strut mounts may wear out prematurely, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps.
* **Rear Suspension Springs:** The rear suspension springs may sag or break, leading to poor handling and a rough ride.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to a faulty alternator or parasitic electrical drain.
* **Electrical System Malfunctions:** The electrical system may experience various malfunctions, such as flickering lights or power window failures.

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Gaps:** The body panels may have uneven gaps or fitment issues.
* **Headlight Condensation:** The headlights may develop condensation on the inside, impairing visibility.

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Issues:** The HVAC system may blow hot or cold air intermittently or fail to function properly.
* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:** The dashboard may develop squeaks or rattles over time.
* **Power Window Failures:** The power windows may fail intermittently or completely.

**Summary**

The 2013 Chevrolet Cruze has received mixed reviews from drivers. While it offers some positive attributes, such as a comfortable ride and good fuel economy, it has also been plagued by a number of issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical malfunctions. The severity and cost of the repairs can vary depending on the specific problem. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before purchasing a Cruze.
