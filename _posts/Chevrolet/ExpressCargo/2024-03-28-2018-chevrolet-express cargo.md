**Introduction:**

The 2018 Chevrolet Express Cargo is a full-size cargo van that is known for its spacious cargo area and towing capabilities. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 4.3L V6, a 6.0L V8, and a 6.6L V8. The 4.3L V6 produces 276 horsepower and 298 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.0L V8 produces 341 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque. The 6.6L V8 is the most powerful option, producing 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque. All engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2018 Chevrolet Express Cargo generally praise its spacious cargo area, powerful engines, and towing capabilities. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.3L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, and reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve cover gasket leak:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover gaskets
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.0L V8**

* **Active fuel management (AFM) system issues:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, and reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Disable the AFM system or replace the lifters
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil leaks:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the engine gaskets or seals
    * Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.6L V8**

* **Turbocharger failure:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, and loud noises from the engine
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Diesel particulate filter (DPF) problems:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, and black smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Clean or replace the DPF
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission slipping:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, hesitation during acceleration, and rough shifting
    * Solution: Replace the transmission or transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque converter shudder:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noises from the front suspension
    * Solution: Replace the worn suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension sag:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear of the vehicle sags excessively when loaded
    * Solution: Replace the rear springs or shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use
    * Solution: Find and fix the source of the parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator fails to charge the battery, causing the vehicle to stall
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on the body panels:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots or corrosion on the body panels
    * Solution: Repair or replace the affected body panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate issues:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate does not open or close properly, or the lock fails
    * Solution: Replace or repair the tailgate components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises and vibrations coming from the dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC system issues:** 
    * Description/Symptoms: The HVAC system does not blow cold or hot air, or the fan does not work
    * Solution: Replace or repair the affected HVAC components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Chevrolet Express Cargo has received mixed reviews from owners. While some owners praise its spacious cargo area, powerful engines, and towing capabilities, others have reported issues with various components of the vehicle. Some of the more severe issues, such as transmission slipping and turbocharger failure, can be expensive to repair. However, many of the other issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for less than $1,000.
