Understanding these potential problem areas can help fleet managers and owners proactively address maintenance and minimize downtime.

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights flicker, accessories fail to operate intermittently, engine may stall.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair loose or corroded wiring harnesses, check battery terminals and connections, test alternator output.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Faulty Alternator**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminates, dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Replace the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Failure**

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Chevrolet Express Cargo is a dependable vehicle for commercial applications, with its powertrain generally proving robust. The most commonly cited issues tend to be related to the electrical system, particularly intermittent power loss and alternator failures, which can be exacerbated by heavy use. Exterior components like door latches can also wear out. Interior issues are less frequent but can include HVAC blower motor problems. Overall, with diligent maintenance and attention to potential electrical gremlins, the Express Cargo offers solid reliability for its intended purpose.
