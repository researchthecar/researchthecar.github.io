**2017 Chevrolet Express Cargo**

The 2017 Chevrolet Express Cargo is a full-size van offered with a variety of engine and transmission configurations. It is a popular choice for businesses and families alike due to its spacious interior, powerful engines, and rugged construction. However, like any vehicle, the Express Cargo has its share of issues.

**Engine**

* **6.0L Vortec V8**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:** This issue causes a coolant leak and can lead to overheating. Symptoms include a drop in coolant levels, a sweet smell coming from the engine, and white smoke coming from the exhaust. The solution is to replace the intake manifold gaskets. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * **Timing chain failure:** This issue can cause the engine to lose power or stall. Symptoms include a rattling noise coming from the engine, a loss of power, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the timing chain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
* **4.8L Vortec V8**
    * **No issues found**
* **Duramax 6.6L Turbo Diesel**
    * **Injector failure:** This issue can cause the engine to run rough and have white smoke coming from the exhaust. Symptoms include a loss of power, a rough idle, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
* **Turbocharger failure:** This issue can cause the engine to lose power and have black smoke coming from the exhaust. Symptoms include a loss of power, a loud whistling noise, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000

**Transmission**

* **6-speed automatic**
    * **No issues found**
* **8-speed automatic**
    * **Shift shudder:** This issue can cause the transmission to shudder when shifting gears. Symptoms include a vibration felt through the vehicle, a delay in shifting gears, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the transmission fluid and filter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **4-speed automatic**
    * **Slipping gears:** This issue can cause the transmission to slip out of gear when driving. Symptoms include a loss of power, a grinding noise, and a check engine light. The solution is to rebuild or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension:**
    * **Strut failure:** This issue can cause the front of the vehicle to sag and make it difficult to handle. Symptoms include a clunking noise, a loss of ride height, and uneven tire wear. The solution is to replace the struts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Rear suspension:**
    * **Leaf spring failure:** This issue can cause the rear of the vehicle to sag and make it difficult to handle. Symptoms include a clunking noise, a loss of ride height, and uneven tire wear. The solution is to replace the leaf springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure:** This issue can cause the vehicle to not start. Symptoms include a dead battery, a clicking noise when trying to start the vehicle, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* **Alternator failure:** This issue can cause the vehicle to lose power and eventually stall. Symptoms include a dimming of the headlights, a flickering of the interior lights, and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Exterior**

* **Door handle failure:** This issue can cause the door handle to break off or become difficult to operate. Symptoms include a loose or broken door handle and a difficulty opening the door. The solution is to replace the door handle. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
* **Rear door hinge failure:** This issue can cause the rear door to sag or come loose. Symptoms include a misaligned or loose rear door and a difficulty opening or closing the door. The solution is to replace the rear door hinge. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking:** This issue can cause the dashboard to crack or warp. Symptoms include a cracked or warped dashboard and a rattling noise coming from the dashboard. The solution is to replace the dashboard. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Seat belt buckle failure:** This issue can cause the seat belt buckle to stick or not latch properly. Symptoms include a difficulty buckling or unbuckling the seat belt and a check engine light. The solution is to replace the seat belt buckle. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* **Air conditioning failure:** This issue can cause the air conditioning system to not blow cold air. Symptoms include a lack of cold air coming from the vents and a check engine light. The solution is to diagnose and repair the air conditioning system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Summary**

The 2017 Chevrolet Express Cargo is a reliable and powerful vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The most common issues include intake manifold gasket failure, timing chain failure, and electrical problems. The severity of these issues can range from minor to major, and the cost to fix them can be significant. However, overall, the Express Cargo is a good choice for businesses and families alike.
