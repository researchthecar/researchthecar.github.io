The 2007 Chevrolet Corsa is a subcompact hatchback that was praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and sporty handling. It was offered with a 1.4L 4-cylinder or a 1.8L 4-cylinder engine, paired with either a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. Some common dislikes include limited interior space and a lack of available features.

**Engine**

* **1.4L 4-cylinder**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Causes coolant and oil leaks, overheating, and reduced engine performance.
    * **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $450+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **1.8L 4-cylinder**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Results in a rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, and possible engine damage.
    * **Solution:** Replace timing chain tensioner.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $750+
    * **Recall:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed manual**
    * **Synchro failure:** Causes difficulty shifting gears, especially in cold weather.
    * **Solution:** Replace damaged synchronizer ring.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $300+
    * **Recall:** None Found
* **4-speed automatic**
    * **Transmission slipping:** Occurs when gears fail to engage properly, resulting in loss of power and acceleration.
    * **Solution:** Overhaul or replace transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
    * **Recall:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front struts:** May experience premature wear, leading to clunking noises and reduced handling.
* **Solution:** Replace worn struts.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $600+ for a pair
* **Recall:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** Causes battery to not charge, resulting in loss of electrical power.
* **Solution:** Replace alternator.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $400+
* **Recall:** None Found
* **Starter motor failure:** Prevents engine from starting.
* **Solution:** Replace starter motor.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $350+
* **Recall:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog light bulbs burning out:** May be due to faulty wiring or excessive heat.
* **Solution:** Replace burnt-out bulbs.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $25 - $50 for a pair
* **Recall:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle:** Caused by loose components or poor assembly.
* **Solution:** Identify and tighten loose parts or use noise-dampening materials.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
* **Recall:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Chevrolet Corsa has several potential issues that can arise, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical components. While some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be costly and require extensive repairs. Overall, it is important to consider these potential issues before purchasing this vehicle and to keep up with regular maintenance to ensure a reliable driving experience.
