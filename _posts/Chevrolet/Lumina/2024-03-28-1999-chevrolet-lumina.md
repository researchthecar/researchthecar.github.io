**Introduction:**

The 1999 Chevrolet Lumina is a mid-size sedan that comes with three engine options: a 3.1L V6 producing 160 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque, a 3.4L V6 producing 185 horsepower and 215 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.8L V6 producing 200 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque. The 3.1L engine comes with a 4-speed automatic transmission, while the 3.4L and 3.8L engines come with a 4-speed automatic or 5-speed manual transmission. The Lumina is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and decent fuel economy. However, it has also been plagued by various issues that affect its overall reliability and performance.

**Engine**

**3.1L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, poor engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine overheating, coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.4L V6**

* **Timing Chain Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Lower Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, poor engine performance
    * Solution: Replace lower intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.8L V6**

* **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Loss under Acceleration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle loses power when accelerating, check engine light
    * Solution: Update engine control software or replace throttle position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: 99V123000

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, no engagement, harsh shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Transmission Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or noise when shifting or accelerating
    * Solution: Replace transmission mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises from rear suspension
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace rear suspension components (e.g., sway bar, links)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No crank or click when starting
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery draining, check battery light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not functioning properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles breaking or becoming difficult to open
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Trunk Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing trunk
    * Solution: Replace trunk latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working
    * Solution: Replace seat heater element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC Control Panel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control not responding or malfunctioning
    * Solution: Replace HVAC control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Center Console Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or squeaking noises from center console
    * Solution: Tighten or replace console components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1999 Chevrolet Lumina has a variety of issues that can affect its reliability and performance. While some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Major concerns include engine gasket failures, transmission failure, and suspension component failures. Electrical and interior problems are also prevalent. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific vehicle and its maintenance history. Overall, the Lumina's reliability is below average, and owners may need to be prepared for unexpected repairs.
