**Introduction:**

The 2013 Chevrolet Matiz is a subcompact hatchback powered by a single 0.8L S-TEC II inline-four engine. With 51 horsepower and 47 lb-ft of torque, it is paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. The Matiz is praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and compact size, while criticisms include limited power and a cramped interior.
