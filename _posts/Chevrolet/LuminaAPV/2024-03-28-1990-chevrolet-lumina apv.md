**Introduction:**

The 1990 Chevrolet Lumina APV was a popular minivan that offered a spacious cabin and a reliable engine. It came with a 160-horsepower 3.1L V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**3.1L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, reduced engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve cover gasket leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from valve cover area
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace transmission solenoid pack or overhaul transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery not charging, warning light on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No crank, clicking noise when turning key
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on body panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on fenders, doors, or rear hatch
    * Solution: Repair or replace affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on extent of damage
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard surface
    * Solution: Cover with dash mat or replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Window regulator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows not rolling up or down properly
    * Solution: Replace window regulator motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1990 Chevrolet Lumina APV is a generally reliable vehicle with some common issues to watch out for. Engine issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while transmission problems can be more costly. Suspension and electrical issues are infrequent but can still arise. Exterior rust is a concern, and interior components can develop cracks or fail over time.
