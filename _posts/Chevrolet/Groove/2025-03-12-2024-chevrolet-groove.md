The 2024 Chevrolet Groove is a subcompact SUV known for its affordability and fuel efficiency. It is powered by a 1.5-liter inline-4 engine producing around 110 horsepower and 108 lb-ft of torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). Many appreciate its budget-friendly price point and decent fuel economy for city driving. Downsides often mentioned include its limited cargo space and somewhat underwhelming engine performance, especially when fully loaded.

    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing a jerking or hesitant feeling during acceleration, particularly at lower speeds. This can sometimes be accompanied by a slight delay in response when pressing the accelerator.

    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen occasionally freezes, becomes unresponsive, or reboots unexpectedly. This can affect access to navigation, audio controls, and other vehicle settings.
    *   Solution: Software update or infotainment system replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Backup Camera Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The backup camera displays a distorted image, freezes, or fails to activate when the vehicle is in reverse.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the backup camera.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

    *   Description/Symptoms: Moisture builds up inside the headlight housing, creating condensation and potentially reducing visibility.

    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Annoying rattling or squeaking noises emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or rear cargo area while driving, especially on rough roads.

    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2024 Chevrolet Groove, while economical, appears to have some potential issues. The most concerning seem to revolve around the CVT transmission, with reports of jerking and unusual noises. Electrical glitches, particularly with the infotainment system and backup camera, are also notable. Interior rattles, while not a mechanical issue, can be bothersome for owners. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to potentially costly repairs. As this is a newer model, it's advisable for potential buyers to carefully research owner experiences and consider an extended warranty.
