**Introduction:**
The 1990 Chevrolet C35 is a heavy-duty pickup truck available in various configurations. It offers a choice of two engine options, a 7.4L V8 and a 6.6L V8 diesel. Both come paired with either a 4-speed automatic or a 5-speed manual transmission. The C35 is known for its towing and hauling capabilities but has been met with mixed reviews regarding its reliability and comfort.

**Engine**
- **7.4L V8**
    - **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, rough idle, loss of power.
        - **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gaskets.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,000+
        - **Recalls:** None Found
- **6.6L V8 Diesel**
    - **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Hard starting, black smoke, loss of power.
        - **Solution:** Replace failed fuel injectors.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
        - **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**
- **4-Speed Automatic**
    - **Transmission Slipping:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, slipping gears.
        - **Solution:** Repair or replace transmission.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
        - **Recalls:** None Found
- **5-Speed Manual**
    - **Shifter Cable Wear:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, loose or sloppy gear lever.
        - **Solution:** Replace shifter cables.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
        - **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**
- **Front Suspension:**
    - **Ball Joint Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Clunking or popping sound while turning, uneven tire wear.
        - **Solution:** Replace ball joints.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
        - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Rear Suspension:**
    - **Leaf Spring Sagging:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Rear end sitting low, reduced towing capacity, uneven tire wear.
        - **Solution:** Replace leaf springs.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
        - **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**
- **Alternator Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Dimming lights, dead battery, electrical problems.
        - **Solution:** Replace alternator.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
        - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Starter Solenoid Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Engine won't crank or cranks slowly.
        - **Solution:** Replace starter solenoid.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
        - **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**
- **Hood Latch Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Hood won't stay closed or opens unexpectedly.
        - **Solution:** Replace hood latch.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
        - **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**
- **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** No air flow from vents, reduced heating or cooling capacity.
        - **Solution:** Replace blower motor.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
        - **Recalls:** None Found
- **Cruise Control Switch Failure:**
        - **Description/Symptoms:** Cruise control not working or intermittent.
        - **Solution:** Replace cruise control switch.
        - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
        - **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**
The 1990 Chevrolet C35 has been met with mixed reviews regarding reliability. Some common issues include intake manifold gasket failure, fuel injector failure, transmission slipping, and suspension problems. However, the severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific component and the condition of the vehicle.
