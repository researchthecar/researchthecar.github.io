**1992 Chevrolet C35**

The 1992 Chevrolet C35 is a heavy-duty pickup truck that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It was powered by a 7.4L V8 engine that produced 230 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque. The C35 was also available with a 6.5L diesel engine that produced 190 horsepower and 360 lb-ft of torque. Both engines were mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission.

The C35 was a popular choice for commercial and recreational use due to its durability and towing capacity. However, it was not without its problems.

**Engine**

**7.4L V8**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Valve cover gasket leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from under valve covers
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.5L Diesel**

* **Fuel injection pump failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injection pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Slipping gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips out of gear while driving
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension ball joint failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear leaf spring failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, decreased ride height
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, grinding noise when trying to start
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on body panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rusty spots on body panels, especially around wheel wells and lower doors
    * Solution: Repair rust or replace body panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cracked windshield**
    * Description/Symptoms: Spider cracks or chips in windshield
    * Solution: Replace windshield
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Broken climate control knobs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or missing climate control knobs
    * Solution: Replace climate control knobs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty instrument cluster**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working or inaccurate readings
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1992 Chevrolet C35 is a reliable and capable truck, but it is not without its problems. The most common issues reported with this vehicle include engine intake manifold gasket failure, transmission slipping gears, and rust on body panels. The severity of the issues ranges from minor (broken climate control knobs) to major (fuel injection pump failure). The cost to fix these issues can also vary greatly, from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Overall, the 1992 Chevrolet C35 is a good choice for those who need a heavy-duty truck that can handle tough jobs, but it should be inspected carefully before purchasing to avoid any major problems.
