**Introduction:**

The 2012 Chevrolet Orlando is a versatile crossover SUV that offers ample space and functionality. It comes standard with a 2.4L inline-4 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.4L Inline-4**

* **Engine Knocking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise emanating from the engine, particularly during acceleration.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the engine's fuel injector, spark plugs, or timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or difficulty shifting between gears.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid change or flush, solenoid replacement, or complete transmission overhaul.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Assembly Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, uneven tire wear, or a noticeable drop in the vehicle's ride height.
    * Solution: Replacement of the front strut assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery losing power quickly, even when parked.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components, such as parasitic drains or alternator issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Taillight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the taillights, causing a cloudy or foggy appearance.
    * Solution: Replacement of the taillight lenses or bulbs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heating, ventilation, or air conditioning system not functioning properly.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty HVAC components, such as the blower motor, temperature sensors, or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $900+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Chevrolet Orlando has a few common issues that can affect its performance, reliability, and overall satisfaction. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others may be more serious and require more extensive repairs.
