# 2017 Chevrolet Suburban Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Chevrolet Suburban is a massive and capable full-size SUV that offers immense passenger and cargo capacity, making it a popular choice for large families and towing duty. When shopping for a used model, buyers should carefully evaluate the available powertrain options and keep an eye out for known comfort and accessory quirks that can pop up over time. Taking the time to inspect potential used models thoroughly will help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 5.3L V8 EcoTec3

The standard 5.3-liter V8 engine produces 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, featuring active fuel management and direct injection. It balances acceptable everyday performance with reasonable fuel efficiency for a large utility vehicle.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission comes paired with the standard V8 engine to deliver smooth shifting and reliable power delivery across various driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V645000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Vacuum](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Suburban&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As the vacuum level drops, the brake assist decreases, increasing braking effort, extending the distance required to stop the vehicle, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the Electronic Brake Control Module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V761000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Wheel Speed Sensor/Tone Ring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Suburban&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Unintended activation of the driveline-protection system will cause unintended braking on the wheel on the opposite side of the failed sensor, causing the vehicle to pull to one side unexpectedly, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the EBCM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V033000: Visibility:Rearview Mirrors/Devices:Exterior](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Suburban&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." If equipped with the incorrect right-side rear-view mirror, the driver may misjudge the distance of other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the convex mirror with a non-convex mirror and provide a new page for the owner's manual with the customer notification letter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Suburban&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V289000: Power Train:Transfer Case (4-Wheel Drive)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Suburban&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A component missing from the drivetrain transfer case may cause the front and/or rear wheels to lock up without warning to the driver. Front or rear wheel lockup increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the transfer case assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

Overall, the 2017 Chevrolet Suburban remains a compelling choice for buyers who need maximum hauling and seating capability.
