**2019 Chevrolet Suburban**

With three engine options, including a 5.3L V8 (355 hp, 383 lb-ft of torque), a 6.2L V8 (420 hp, 460 lb-ft of torque), and a 3.0L turbodiesel V6 (277 hp, 460 lb-ft of torque), and three transmission options, including a 6-speed automatic, an 8-speed automatic, and a 10-speed automatic, the 2019 Chevrolet Suburban offers a range of power and performance options. It is praised for its spacious interior, towing capacity, and off-road capabilities. However, it has also been reported to have a few problems.

**Engine**

**5.3L V8**

* **Intake Valve Sticking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, hesitation during acceleration, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake valves and/or intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise from engine, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.2L V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Bolt Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust leak, rattling noise
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifold bolts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Turbodiesel V6**

* **DEF Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, check engine light, reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace DEF injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, increased fuel consumption, check engine light
    * Solution: Regenerate or replace DPF
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Harsh Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or shuddering during gear changes
    * Solution: Transmission fluid change, software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration when accelerating from a stop
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delay or hesitation when shifting gears
    * Solution: Transmission fluid change, software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uneven ride height, noise from rear axle
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Black screen, frozen display, unresponsive buttons
    * Solution: Software update, system replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture build-up inside headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly, temperature fluctuations
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Adjustment Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seats not adjusting properly or getting stuck
    * Solution: Replace seat motor or control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Chevrolet Suburban is a generally reliable vehicle, however, it has been reported to have some potential issues. The most common problems seem to be with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While some issues can be minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important to consider these potential problems when deciding whether to purchase a 2019 Chevrolet Suburban.
