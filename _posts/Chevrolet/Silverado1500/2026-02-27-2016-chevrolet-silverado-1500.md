# 2016 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 offers strong capability and a comfortable ride, making it a popular choice in the used full-size truck market. However, prospective buyers should thoroughly review maintenance records, as this model year is associated with several well-documented mechanical and electrical concerns. Pay close attention to powertrain performance and steering functionality during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 4.3L EcoTec3 V6

A 4.3-liter V6 engine producing approximately 285 horsepower and 305 lb-ft of torque, offering dependable baseline capability for light-duty tasks with generally solid long-term durability.

### 5.3L EcoTec3 V8

A widely chosen 5.3-liter V8 engine delivering 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, balancing strong towing performance with active fuel management technology that requires careful attention to valve lifter reliability.

**Active Fuel Management (AFM) Lifter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine check lights, severe engine stalling, or tapping noises accompanied by collapsed valve lifters damaging the camshaft and catalytic converters.
- **Solution:** Replace failing valve lifters, damaged camshafts, and affected exhaust components.
- **Cost:** $10,000+

### 6.2L EcoTec3 V8

A high-output 6.2-liter V8 engine generating 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, paired with premium capability while sharing similar cylinder-deactivation components as the 5.3L.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission (6L80)

A 6-speed automatic transmission utilized across various trims, known to occasionally develop torque converter shudder and premature internal wear requiring major service.

**Torque Converter Failure and Transmission Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Hard shifting, delayed engagement into first gear, severe drivetrain shudder, or total loss of drive with metal shavings discovered in transmission fluid.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replace the transmission and failed torque converter components.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission (8L90)

An 8-speed automatic transmission providing refined highway cruising ratios, though some owners report rough shifting behavior and slippage.

**Transmission Slippage and Harsh Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic gear selection, sudden slipping under acceleration, and illuminated transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Perform professional transmission diagnosis, software updates, or internal component replacement.

## Brakes

### Brake Hydraulic and Assist System

Braking system components that generally provide solid stopping power but can experience vacuum pump or pedal responsiveness faults in isolated incidents.

**Brake Pedal Failure and Vacuum Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal becoming hard or inoperable, unexpected activation of brake assist, and abnormal ABS pulsing during low-speed stops.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake booster vacuum pump system and replace faulty hydraulic components or sensors.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Electronic power steering system that can experience intermittent assist failures or warning lights linked to electrical connectivity issues.

**Power Steering Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Stiff steering wheel, unexpected loss of power steering function, and 'Service Stabilitrak' warning messages displayed on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty electronic steering components, damaged wiring harnesses, or steering rack assemblies.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant restraint anchor points that require routine inspection to ensure secure mounting to the vehicle frame.

**Seat Belt Anchor and Cable Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Driver or passenger seat belt anchor cables breaking loose or separating from the vehicle frame unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged seat belt assemblies and secure mounting anchors to factory specifications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V578000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Either condition may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will add adhesive to the nut and reinstall the nut with increased tightness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V291000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the pretensioner cable becomes fatigued or breaks, the driver may not be properly restrained in the event of a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the pretensioner cable and seat side-shield, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V645000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Vacuum](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As the vacuum level drops, the brake assist decreases, increasing braking effort, extending the distance required to stop the vehicle, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the Electronic Brake Control Module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V761000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Wheel Speed Sensor/Tone Ring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Unintended activation of the driveline-protection system will cause unintended braking on the wheel on the opposite side of the failed sensor, causing the vehicle to pull to one side unexpectedly, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the EBCM, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V256000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Inadequate welds may allow the control arm to separate from the bushing, compromising steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the left and right front upper control arms and realign the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V069000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Pedals And Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Either condition may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will add adhesive to the brake pedal pivot nut and reinstall with an increased torque, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V437000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+1500&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** While being previously remedied for recall 16V-651, the affected vehicles may not have received the complete software update necessary to remedy the recall condition. Without the update, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Chevrolet Silverado 1500 is a capable and versatile truck, but used-car buyers should approach with informed caution. Key areas such as V8 engine lifters, transmission durability, and power steering electronics warrant a careful inspection and a thorough test drive before purchase. Verifying a complete service history can significantly reduce the risk of encountering costly unexpected repairs.
