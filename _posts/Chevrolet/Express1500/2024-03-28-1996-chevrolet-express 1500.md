**Introduction:**

The 1996 Chevrolet Express 1500 is a full-size van that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It offered a spacious interior, versatile seating arrangements, and a powerful engine lineup. However, like all vehicles, it had some common problems reported by owners.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**
**Horsepower:** 255 hp
**Torque:** 330 lb-ft

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 96V102000

**6.5L V8 Diesel**
**Horsepower:** 190 hp
**Torque:** 360 lb-ft

* **Injection Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, loss of power, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace injection pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4L60E Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration when accelerating from a stop or shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 96V126000

**4L80E Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises when turning, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Leaf Spring Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sits low in the rear, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after sitting for a period of time
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty component causing the drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical problems, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks off or becomes loose
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks developing on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or repair with aftermarket cover
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **HVAC Blend Door Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No heat or cold air, inconsistent temperature control
    * Solution: Replace blend door actuator or servo
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1996 Chevrolet Express 1500 had several common problems, including intake manifold gasket failure, injection pump failure, and torque converter shudder. While some of these issues can be costly to repair, the Express 1500 is still a reliable and versatile van for those who need a spacious and powerful hauler.
