**Introduction**

The 2011 Chevrolet Aveo5 is a subcompact hatchback known for its budget-friendly price and fuel-efficient performance. It offers a choice of two engine and transmission options: a 1.6L engine with a 5-speed manual transmission and a 1.6L engine with a 4-speed automatic transmission. While the Aveo5 received praise for its affordability and practicality, it has also been plagued by several reliability issues.

**Engine**

**1.6L Engine**

**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the top of the engine near the spark plugs
Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Shifting Difficulty**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, especially from first to second
Solution: Replace shift forks and synchronize
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Transmission Fluid Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from the transmission pan
Solution: Replace transmission pan gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Noise**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension
Solution: Replace sway bar end links or struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Electrical System Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Various electrical issues, such as flickering lights, power window failures, and dead batteries
Solution: Varies depending on the specific issue
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Lock Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Doors unlocking or locking spontaneously
Solution: Replace door lock actuators
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard
Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Chevrolet Aveo5 has been impacted by numerous reliability issues that can range from minor annoyances to more significant and expensive repairs. The most prevalent issues include valve cover gasket leaks, transmission fluid leaks, suspension noise, and electrical system problems. While the Aveo5 remains an affordable and practical choice, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing.
