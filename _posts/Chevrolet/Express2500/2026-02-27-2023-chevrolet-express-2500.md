# 2023 Chevrolet Express 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Chevrolet Express 2500 continues its long-running role as a sturdy, traditional full-size cargo and passenger van designed for heavy-duty commercial use. Because it shares much of its mechanical DNA with past model years, buyers can generally expect straightforward utility and familiar mechanical options. However, as an aging platform, it lacks modern driver aids and refined interior comforts found in newer competitors, making it a specialized choice strictly for fleet or work-duty applications.

---

## Engine

### 4.3L V6

This 4.3-liter V6 engine produces approximately 276 horsepower and 305 lb-ft of torque, paired with a modern multi-speed automatic transmission. It serves as the baseline option suited for lighter commercial delivery duties.

### 6.6L V8

The heavy-duty 6.6-liter V8 engine generates roughly 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque, offering robust towing and hauling capability for demanding commercial applications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission manages power delivery for the V6 engine, providing balanced gear ratios for city driving and highway cruising.

### 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

A heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with the 6.6L V8 engine, engineered to handle heavy payloads and frequent towing without complaint.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V876000: Equipment Adaptive/Mobility:Wheelchair Restraints/Securement:Latch/Anchor:](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Express+2500&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The retractors may not lock, preventing the wheelchair from being properly secured. An unsecured wheelchair can move during transit, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Chevrolet Express 2500 remains a reliable, old-school workhorse for buyers prioritizing sheer hauling capacity and straightforward mechanical design over modern tech. While current owner complaint data is sparse, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect any prospective work van for signs of commercial wear and tear before purchasing.
