The 2018 Chevrolet Express 2500 is a full-size cargo van known for its dependability and utility. It's a popular choice for businesses and individuals needing a spacious and reliable workhorse. Engine options include a 4.3L V6 (276 hp, 298 lb-ft torque), a 6.0L V8 (341 hp, 373 lb-ft torque), and a 2.8L Duramax Diesel (181 hp, 369 lb-ft torque). The van generally receives praise for its robust build, payload capacity, and ease of customization. However, its outdated design, fuel economy, and somewhat basic interior are often cited as drawbacks.

**Engine**

**4.3L V6**
* **No Issues Found**

**6.0L V8**
* **Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption requiring frequent top-offs between oil changes. This can lead to engine damage if not monitored closely.
    * Solution: Monitor oil levels frequently. In some cases, piston ring replacement may be required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Lifter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Ticking noise from the engine, rough running, and potential misfires.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected lifters, potentially including camshaft replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.8L Duramax Diesel**
* **Glow Plug Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting in cold weather, rough idle, and potential engine misfires.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty glow plug(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough running engine, misfires, reduced power, and potential fuel leaks.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injector(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**All Transmissions**
* **Harsh Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Abrupt and jerky gear changes, particularly between 1st and 2nd gear.
    * Solution: Fluid flush and replacement, valve body repair or replacement, or transmission replacement in severe cases.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Leaf Spring Sagging/Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the van sagging, reduced load-carrying capacity, and potential clunking noises.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaf spring(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery dying overnight or within a short period of time, requiring frequent jump starts.
    *   Solution: Identify the source of the parasitic draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replace it.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **ABS Sensor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: ABS warning light illuminated on the dashboard, loss of ABS functionality, and potential wheel lockup during braking.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty ABS sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Side Door Roller Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening and closing the sliding side door, grinding noises, and potential door misalignment.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty door roller(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower motor stops working, resulting in no air conditioning or heat.
    * Solution: Replace the blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Chevrolet Express 2500, while a reliable work van, does have some common issues. The 6.0L V8 engine seems to be more prone to problems such as oil consumption and lifter failure, which can be costly to repair. Electrical issues like battery drain and ABS sensor failure can also be annoying and require diagnosis. The sliding door roller failure and HVAC blower motor failure are common issues that do not generally cost much to repair. It is recommended to pay close attention to the engine, transmission, and suspension components when purchasing this vehicle, and have it thoroughly inspected by a qualified mechanic to prevent issues with the vehicle.
