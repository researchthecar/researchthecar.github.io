# 2022 Chevrolet Express 2500

### Overview
The 2022 Chevrolet Express 2500 is a workhorse van designed for commercial use, prioritizing utility and durability. It offers robust powertrain options and a straightforward design, which generally contributes to a solid reliability record for its class. While specific high-tech features are minimal, the focus on essential functionality means fewer complex systems are prone to failure. Owners can expect a dependable vehicle for demanding tasks, though like any commercial vehicle, regular maintenance is crucial for optimal performance and longevity. The van's construction is geared towards resilience in commercial environments, making it a practical choice for businesses.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Chevrolet Express 2500 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly given its commercial application. The powertrain options, including the 4.3L V6 and 6.6L V8 engines paired with the 8-speed automatic transmission, are generally robust and have not shown widespread common failures. Similarly, the suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components are reported to be reliable, with no significant recurring issues documented. This suggests that the Express 2500 is a dependable choice for businesses and individuals requiring a no-frills, durable van. Adherence to recommended maintenance schedules will be key to maintaining this high level of reliability over the vehicle's lifespan.
