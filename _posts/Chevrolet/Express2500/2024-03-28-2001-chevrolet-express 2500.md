**Introduction:**
The 2001 Chevrolet Express 2500 is a full-size van known for its spacious interior and powerful engine options. It offers two engine options, a 5.3L V8 and a 6.0L V8, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**5.3L V8**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Causes coolant and oil leaks, leading to overheating and engine damage.
- Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
- Recalls: None Found

**6.0L V8**
- **Lifter Failure:** Noisy engine, loss of power, and potential engine damage.
- Solution: Replace lifters and camshaft if necessary.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
- Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic:**
- **Torque Converter Failure:** Causes shuddering and loss of power during acceleration.
- Solution: Replace torque converter.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
- Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:** Causes clunking and poor handling.
- Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
- Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:** Loss of electrical power, battery discharge.
- Solution: Replace alternator.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
- Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rust on Frame:** Can lead to structural damage and safety concerns.
- Solution: Sandblasting and rust prevention measures.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on extent of rust
- Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Cracking:** Causes unsightly appearance.
- Solution: Replace dashboard.
- Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
- Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
While the 2001 Chevrolet Express 2500 offers a spacious interior and powerful engine options, it is not without its issues. Engine problems can be costly to fix, especially with the 6.0L V8. Suspension and electrical issues can also be a concern. However, the severity of these issues can vary, and many vehicles perform reliably with proper maintenance.
