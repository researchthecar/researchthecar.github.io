**Introduction**

The 1998 Chevrolet Express 2500 is a full-size van known for its spacious interior, sturdy build, and towing capabilities. It is offered with a choice of two engines: a 5.7L V8 and a 6.0L V8. Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic transmission and a 5-speed manual transmission. Owners appreciate the vehicle's practicality and reliability, while some have experienced issues with various components.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Spark Plug Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: 02V328000

**6.0L V8**

* **Valve Spring Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Loss of power, ticking noise from engine
    * Solution: Replace valve springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: 15V507000
* **Lifter Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Noisy engine, lifter ticking, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace lifters
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: 15V508000

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, inconsistent shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission filter, fluid, and/or transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Failure to Engage Reverse:**
    * Symptoms: Unable to engage reverse gear
    * Solution: Replace reverse gear solenoid or transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Synchro Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace synchros
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Clunking noises while turning or driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Sagging rear end, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine, electrical issues
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine cranks but won't start
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rusty Fender Wells:**
    * Symptoms: Rust, corrosion on fender wells
    * Solution: Repair or replace fender wells
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cracked Plastic Interior Trim:**
    * Symptoms: Broken or cracked plastic pieces inside the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace interior trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Lock Mechanism Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, door handle doesn't work properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator or mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1998 Chevrolet Express 2500 is a generally reliable vehicle with a spacious interior and powerful engines. However, some owners may encounter issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific part failure. Regular maintenance and professional repairs are recommended to mitigate potential issues and extend the vehicle's lifespan.
