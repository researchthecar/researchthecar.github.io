The 2022 Chevrolet Express 2500 is a full-size van primarily used for commercial purposes, known for its robust build and ample cargo space. Engine options include a 4.3L V6 (276 hp, 298 lb-ft of torque) and a 6.6L V8 (401 hp, 464 lb-ft of torque). A 2.8L Duramax Turbo-Diesel engine was offered until 2021, but not in 2022. The 2022 Express comes with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners generally appreciate the van's straightforward design, ease of maintenance, and towing capacity. Common dislikes include the outdated interior, relatively poor fuel economy, and lack of modern safety features.

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly, especially when the van is not driven for a few days. This can result in difficulty starting the vehicle or a completely dead battery.
    *   Solution: Diagnosing the source of the parasitic draw. Common causes include aftermarket accessories that were improperly installed, a faulty body control module (BCM), or a malfunctioning diode in the alternator. The solution involves identifying and repairing or replacing the defective component and its wiring.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+, depending on the complexity of the electrical issue and the cost of parts and labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system (if equipped) can freeze, become unresponsive, or crash unexpectedly. This may affect navigation, audio, and other functions.
    *   Solution: Performing a system reset or updating the infotainment software to the latest version. In some cases, the head unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $500+ (head unit replacement).
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Side Door Latch Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the side doors, particularly the sliding door. The latch mechanism may become sticky or fail to engage properly.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating the door latch mechanism. If the latch is damaged, it may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+, depending on whether the latch needs cleaning or replacement.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Chevrolet Express 2500 appears to be a relatively reliable vehicle, with few widespread, significant problems reported. The most concerning issues seem to be related to electrical components. The infotainment and battery drain problems can be quite disruptive. These issues can range in severity from minor inconveniences to more costly repairs, depending on the root cause and required fixes.
