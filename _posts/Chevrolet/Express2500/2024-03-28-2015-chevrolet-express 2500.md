## 2015 Chevrolet Express 2500

The 2015 Chevrolet Express 2500 is a full-size van available in passenger and cargo configurations. It offers a choice of two engines: a 4.8L V8 and a 6.0L V8. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic and an 8-speed automatic. The Express 2500 is praised for its spacious interior and powerful engines, but it is criticized for its outdated design and poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

**4.8L V8**
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Spark Plug Thread Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs and repair or replace cylinder head
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
* **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle, loss of power, no start
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and gears
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign Number 15V375000: Crankshaft Oil Seal Leak

**6.0L V8**
* **Valve Stem Seal Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Camshaft Lifter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Ticking noise from engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace camshaft lifters
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* **Intake Manifold Runner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifting, no shifting
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**8-Speed Automatic**
* **Clutch Pack Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping, shuddering, no power transfer
    * Solution: Replace clutch pack
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Control Module Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No shifting, erratic shifting, transmission overheating
    * Solution: Replace control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, rough ride
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* **Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor handling, bouncing ride
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Identify and fix source of drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors won't lock or unlock
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Rear Bumper Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on rear bumper
    * Solution: Replace or repair rear bumper
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Panel Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Controls won't work, temperature not regulating
    * Solution: Replace climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Seat Belt Buckle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt won't buckle or unbuckle
    * Solution: Replace seat belt buckle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+

**Summary**

The 2015 Chevrolet Express 2500 has a range of engine and transmission issues, as well as problems with its suspension, electrical system, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, but some can be quite costly to repair. Overall, the Express 2500 has a moderate reliability rating.
