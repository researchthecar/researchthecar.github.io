## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming lights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replace alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Power Window Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Windows not operating, slow operation, or getting stuck.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace window motor, regulator, or switch.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Rust on Body Panels**
- **Symptoms:** Visible corrosion, bubbling paint, especially in wheel wells and lower body sections.
- **Solution:** Rust repair and repainting, or replacement of affected panels.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+

**Door Latch/Lock Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening or closing.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or replace door latch/lock actuator.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


## Interior

### Interior Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or air only comes out at certain speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace HVAC blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Seat Wear and Tear**
- **Symptoms:** Torn upholstery, broken seat adjustment mechanisms (beyond normal wear).
- **Solution:** Repair or replace seat components or upholstery.
- **Cost:** $100 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Chevrolet Express 2500 demonstrates good overall reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain components across all engine and transmission options. The V8 engines and automatic transmissions are generally robust and do not present widespread common failures. Potential issues tend to be more isolated to specific systems like the electrical components (alternator, power windows) and exterior elements (rust, door latches), particularly on higher mileage units or those exposed to harsh environments. Interior components like the HVAC blower motor can also be a point of concern. Proactive maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly can significantly extend the life and maintain the functionality of this dependable work van.
