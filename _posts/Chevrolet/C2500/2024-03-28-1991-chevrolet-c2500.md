**Introduction:**

The 1991 Chevrolet C2500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck available in various configurations. It features a powerful 5.7L V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic or 5-speed manual transmission. Drivers appreciate its towing capacity, ruggedness, and spacious interior. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Distributor Cap and Rotor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace distributor cap and rotor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Pedal Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sticking, chattering, slipping
    * Solution: Adjust or replace clutch linkage/cable
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front End Ball Joint Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises while steering, shaking or wandering
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging, harsh ride, uneven wear on tires
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine does not crank or starts slowly
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Wiring Harness Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Flickering lights, power loss, engine issues
    * Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rusty Tailgate:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots or holes on tailgate
    * Solution: Repair or replace tailgate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard due to sun damage or temperature changes
    * Solution: Cover with dash cover or replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1991 Chevrolet C2500 is a capable pickup truck, but it has its share of potential issues. Engine problems, such as intake manifold gasket failure and distributor cap/rotor failure, can be costly to fix. Transmission issues, like torque converter failure in the automatic transmission, are also significant concerns. The suspension can experience issues with worn ball joints and broken leaf springs. Electrical problems, wiring harness damage in particular, can lead to various electrical malfunctions. Exterior and interior issues are relatively minor but can still impact the vehicle's appearance and functionality. The severity of these issues varies, and repair costs can be substantial. It's important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing or owning a 1991 Chevrolet C2500.
