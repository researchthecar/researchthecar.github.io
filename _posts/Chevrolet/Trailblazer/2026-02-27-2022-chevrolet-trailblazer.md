# 2022 Chevrolet Trailblazer Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Chevrolet Trailblazer offers buyers a stylish subcompact crossover option with flexible cargo room and modern technology. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding electronic issues, particularly related to the gear shifter and park sensor systems. Familiarizing yourself with these common trouble spots can help you navigate a used model inspection more effectively.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L Turbocharged I3

A 1.2-liter turbocharged inline-three engine producing around 137 horsepower, typically paired with front-wheel drive. It offers efficient city driving dynamics but can feel strained under heavy loads.

### 1.3L Turbocharged I3

A slightly larger 1.3-liter turbocharged inline-three producing 155 horsepower, available with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It provides punchier acceleration for daily commuting and highway driving.

**Engine Overheating and Cooling System Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle displays engine warning lights or overheats on the highway due to coolant control valve or water pump failures.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty water pumps or coolant control valves as needed.

## Transmission

### CVT (Continuously Variable Transmission)

A continuously variable transmission used on front-wheel-drive 1.2L and 1.3L models, prioritizing fuel economy with smooth, gearless acceleration.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 9-speed automatic transmission paired exclusively with all-wheel-drive models, offering traditional stepped gear ratios and robust towing capability for its class.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Master cylinder, brake lines, and pedal mechanisms providing stopping power.

**Brake Pedal Binding**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal intermittently sticks or feels unusual when depressed during driving.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect and repair the brake pedal assembly or hydraulic components.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Electric power steering assist system providing responsive handling.

**Intermittent Power Steering Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard displays a 'Service Power Steering' or 'Service ESC' warning light, occasionally causing heavy steering resistance until the vehicle is restarted.
- **Solution:** Check electronic control modules and power steering connections for faults.

## Electrical

### Shifter and Park Sensor Assembly

Electrical switches and sensors governing cabin accessories, door locks, and gear selection.

**Shift to Park Warning Message**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle displays a 'Shift to Park' error message on the dashboard even when the gear selector is fully in park, preventing the car from shutting off or locking.
- **Solution:** Adjust or replace the transmission control shifter harness or switch assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 to $600

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators

Power door lock mechanisms responsible for remote and cabin switch vehicle security.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door locks fail to lock or unlock properly, requiring manual intervention or actuator replacement.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuator motors.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Camera and radar-based driver assistance safety suites.

**False Positive Collision Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Adaptive cruise control or forward collision systems occasionally misinterpret surroundings and trigger sudden, unexpected braking on the highway.
- **Solution:** Inspect radar sensors and software calibrations at a dealership.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V440000: Equipment:Mechanical:Accessory Scissor/Screw/Bottle Jack/Lift:Stand](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trailblazer&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fractured jack can cause the vehicle to collapse, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the jack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a pre-owned 2022 Chevrolet Trailblazer, verify that all electronic systems and safety features operate correctly during your test drive. Pay special attention to the gear shifter's communication with the ignition, as the 'Shift to Park' error is a well-documented nuisance among owners. Overall, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help you secure a reliable and enjoyable subcompact SUV.
