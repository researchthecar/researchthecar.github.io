**Introduction:**

The 2004 Chevrolet W5500HD Tiltmaster is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for construction, hauling, and towing applications. It is available with various engine and transmission options, including:

* 6.6L Duramax Diesel V8 (360 hp, 650 lb-ft torque)
* 7.8L Vortec V8 (300 hp, 455 lb-ft torque)

**Engine**

**6.6L Duramax Diesel V8**

* **Turbocharger Actuator Failure:**
Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace turbocharger actuator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V447000

* **Fuel Injector Seal Leakage:**
Symptoms: Leaking fuel near injectors, rough idle, reduced fuel efficiency
Solution: Replace fuel injector seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500
Recalls: None Found

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
Symptoms: Illuminated check engine light, reduced engine power, rough idle
Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 19V704000

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from seals or gaskets
Solution: Replace leaking seals or gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failures:**
Symptoms: Broken or cracked leaf springs, sagging suspension
Solution: Replace leaf springs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
Recalls: None Found

* **Shock Absorber Leaks:**
Symptoms: Worn or leaking shock absorbers, reduced handling
Solution: Replace shock absorbers
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery not charging
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
Symptoms: Battery draining quickly while parked
Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical circuit causing the drain
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Brake Light Switches:**
Symptoms: Brake lights not functioning, cruise control inoperable
Solution: Replace brake light switches
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V445000

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Controls:**
Symptoms: Steering wheel controls not functioning properly
Solution: Replace steering wheel control module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

* **Gauge Cluster Malfunction:**
Symptoms: Gauges not working properly, displaying incorrect information
Solution: Repair or replace gauge cluster
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2004 Chevrolet W5500HD Tiltmaster has some common issues in the engine, suspension, electrical, and interior components. While some issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Overall, the vehicle's reliability is average, with a mix of minor and moderate issues.
