**Introduction of the 2008 Chevrolet W5500HD Tiltmaster**

The 2008 Chevrolet W5500HD Tiltmaster is a heavy-duty commercial truck built for towing and hauling heavy loads. It is powered by a 6.6L Duramax turbo-diesel V8 engine that produces 360 horsepower and 660 lb-ft of torque. The W5500HD Tiltmaster is available with a 6-speed Allison automatic transmission. It has a payload capacity of up to 19,500 pounds and a towing capacity of up to 36,000 pounds.

People who like the W5500HD Tiltmaster praise its powerful engine, heavy-duty construction, and spacious cab. However, some people complain about the truck's high price, poor fuel economy, and lack of advanced safety features.
