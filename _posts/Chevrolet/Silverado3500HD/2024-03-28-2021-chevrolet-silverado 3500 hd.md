**Introduction:**

The 2021 Chevrolet Silverado 3500 HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck designed for towing and hauling. It is available with various engine and transmission options, including a 6.6L V8 gasoline engine, a 6.6L V8 Duramax turbodiesel engine, and a 10-speed automatic transmission. The Silverado 3500 HD is praised for its powerful engines, spacious interior, and impressive towing capacity but criticized for its rough ride and lack of certain advanced features.

**Engine**

**6.6L V8 Gasoline**

**Intake Valve Sticking:**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, rough idle
* Solution: Replace or clean intake valves
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200+
* Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number 21V-274

**6.6L V8 Duramax Turbodiesel**

**Turbocharger Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, loud whistling noise, excessive smoke
* Solution: Replace turbocharger
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic**

**Shifting Problems:**
* Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, slipping gears
* Solution: Update transmission software, replace transmission components
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,500+
* Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number 21V-275

**Suspension**

**Rear Leaf Spring Delamination:**
* Description/Symptoms: Noisy or rough ride, reduced load capacity
* Solution: Replace leaf springs
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, slow engine cranks
* Solution: Inspect and replace faulty electrical components
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Tailgate Latch Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Tailgate does not open or close properly
* Solution: Replace tailgate latch
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction:**
* Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent or weak airflow, temperature fluctuations
* Solution: Update software, replace climate control module
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 Chevrolet Silverado 3500 HD has several reported issues, mainly affecting the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of the issues varies from minor to potentially major, and the cost to fix can be significant. However, it is important to note that not all vehicles experience these problems, and the overall reliability of the truck is still within industry standards.
