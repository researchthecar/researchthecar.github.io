**Introduction:**

The 2021 Chevrolet Cavalier is a compact sedan that comes with a 1.5 liter EcoTec DI 4-cylinder engine paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission or an available Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT). The engine produces 155 horsepower and 184 lb-ft of torque. The Cavalier is known for its sporty handling, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. However, it has received mixed reviews regarding its engine performance and infotainment system.

**Engine**

**1.5 liter EcoTec DI 4-cylinder Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, rough idle, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Pressure Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Illuminated check engine light, low oil pressure warning, engine stalling
    * Solution: Replace oil pressure sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Gear Grinding:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise when shifting
    * Solution: Synchronizer replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, difficulty accelerating
    * Solution: CVT replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, misalignment
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Sway Bar End Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise when turning, reduced stability
    * Solution: Replace sway bar end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficult starting, weak battery
    * Solution: Replace battery or fix electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Wiring Harness Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working properly
    * Solution: Replace headlight wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle does not open door reliably
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: System is slow to respond, freezes
    * Solution: Software update or infotainment system replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Chevrolet Cavalier has mixed reviews regarding its reliability. Some common issues include engine oil pressure sensor failure, CVT transmission slipping, suspension wear, and electrical problems. The severity of these issues varies, and some can be costly to repair. However, many owners report that they are satisfied with the vehicle's handling, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior.
