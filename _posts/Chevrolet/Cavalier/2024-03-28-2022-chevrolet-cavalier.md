### 2022 Chevrolet Cavalier

The 2022 Chevrolet Cavalier is a compact sedan that offers a comfortable and fuel-efficient ride. It is powered by a 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 177 horsepower and 203 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). The Cavalier is also available with a 1.4-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 155 horsepower and 177 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a six-speed manual transmission or a CVT.

**Likes:**

* Comfortable interior
* Fuel-efficient engine
* Affordable price

**Dislikes:**

* CVT transmission can be sluggish
* Cramped rear seat
* Lack of standard features

**Cracked Dashboard**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may develop cracks near the defroster vents.

### Summary

The 2022 Chevrolet Cavalier is generally a reliable vehicle. However, there have been some reported issues with the CVT transmission, which can lead to lagging acceleration. The cracked dashboard issue is also a concern, but it is not as widespread as the transmission problem. Overall, the severity of the issues is relatively minor, and the cost to fix them is reasonable.
