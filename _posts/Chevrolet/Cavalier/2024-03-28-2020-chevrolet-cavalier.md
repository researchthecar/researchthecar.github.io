**2020 Chevrolet Cavalier**

The Chevrolet Cavalier is a compact sedan that comes available with two engine and transmission options:

* **1.4L Turbocharged I-4 Engine** with a continuously variable transmission (CVT)
* **1.5L Turbocharged I-4 Engine** with a 6-speed automatic transmission

People who like the Cavalier praise it for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and spacious interior. However, some common complaints include its lack of power, cheap interior materials, and cramped back seat.

**Engine**

**1.4L Turbocharged I-4 Engine**

* **Loss of Power**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sudden loss of power while driving, particularly when accelerating or going uphill.
    * Solution: Replacing the turbocharger assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.5L Turbocharged I-4 Engine**

* **Check Engine Light and Engine Stalling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check Engine light illuminates, and the engine stalls intermittently.
    * Solution: Reprogramming the engine control unit (ECU) and replacing the fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **Hesitation and Shuddering**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation and shuddering while shifting or accelerating.
    * Solution: Replacing or reconditioning the CVT assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Premature Front Sway Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises and clunking from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replacing the front sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, such as flickering lights, power window failure, or dashboard malfunctions.
    * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the faulty electrical component(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Center Console Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the center console panel.
    * Solution: Replacing the center console assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Panel Loose**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door panels becoming loose and rattling.
    * Solution: Adjusting and tightening the door panel clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2020 Chevrolet Cavalier has a few notable issues that can be costly to repair, particularly the transmission and electrical system problems. However, these issues do not appear to be widespread, and most owners report having a positive experience with the vehicle.
