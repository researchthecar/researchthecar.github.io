## 2012 Chevrolet Impala

The 2012 Chevrolet Impala is a full-size sedan offered with a choice of two engines: a 3.6L V6 producing 303 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.3L V8 producing 303 horsepower and 318 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic transmission for the V6 engine and a 6-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission for the V8 engine.

The Impala is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engines. However, it has also been plagued with a number of issues that have affected owner satisfaction.

### Engine

**3.6L V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, performance issues, check engine light
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: None Found

**5.3L V8**

**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
* Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks, engine noise, check engine light
* Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
* Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission (V6, V8)**

**Transmission Shift Solenoid Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, transmission slipping, check engine light
* Solution: Replace transmission shift solenoid
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

**No Issues Found**

### Electrical

**Alternator Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, dim lights, check engine light
* Solution: Replace alternator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
* Recalls: 14V359000

### Exterior

**No Issues Found**

### Interior

**Center Console Lid Latch Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Center console lid won't stay closed
* Solution: Replace center console lid latch
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* Recalls: None Found

**Window Regulator Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Windows won't roll up or down
* Solution: Replace window regulator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: 14C030000

### Summary

The 2012 Chevrolet Impala has been plagued with a number of issues that affect various components of the vehicle. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and require significant repairs. Overall, these issues have led to decreased owner satisfaction and a lower reliability rating for the Impala.
