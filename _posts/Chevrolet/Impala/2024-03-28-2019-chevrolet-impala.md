**2019 Chevrolet Impala**

The 2019 Chevrolet Impala is a full-size sedan offered with three engine options: a 2.5L 4-cylinder, a 3.6L V6, and a 5.3L V8. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic and an 8-speed automatic.

The Impala is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and strong performance from the V6 and V8 engines. However, some common issues have been reported.

**Engine**

* **2.5L 4-Cylinder**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Leaking coolant causing overheating and engine damage. Requires intake manifold replacement. $900 - $1,200+
    * **Timing Chain Failure:** Chain stretches or breaks, causing catastrophic engine damage. Requires engine replacement. $5,000+
* **3.6L V6**
    * **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption leading to engine damage. Requires engine overhaul or replacement. $3,000+
* **5.3L V8**
    * **Active Fuel Management System Failure:** Disables cylinders to save fuel but can cause engine knocking and power loss. Requires replacement of AFM lifter system. $1,000 - $2,000+

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:** Hard shifting, delayed shifting, or slipping. Requires transmission rebuild or replacement. $2,000+
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak:** Leak from transmission pan or cooler lines. Requires fluid refill and repair of leak source. $300 - $600

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts:** Excessive wear or failure causing noise and vibration. Requires replacement of strut mounts. $500 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drainage:** Battery drains excessively when the vehicle is parked. Requires diagnosis and repair of electrical fault. $200 - $600+
* **Body Control Module Failure:** Controls electrical systems, but can fail causing various electrical problems. Requires BCM replacement. $1,000 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture buildup inside the headlight housing. Requires replacement of headlight assembly. $500 - $800+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaks and Rattles:** Noises coming from the dashboard due to loose or misaligned components. Requires disassembly and tightening or replacement of components. $200 - $600+
* **Center Console Lid Misalignment:** Lid does not close properly, causing rattling noises. Requires adjustment or replacement of console lid. $300 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2019 Chevrolet Impala has some common issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues can vary, and some require expensive repairs. Overall, the Impala is a spacious and comfortable sedan, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing one.
