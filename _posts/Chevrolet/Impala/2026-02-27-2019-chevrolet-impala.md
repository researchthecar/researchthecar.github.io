# 2019 Chevrolet Impala Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Chevrolet Impala offers a spacious cabin, comfortable ride, and straightforward tech features, making it a popular choice for a used full-size sedan. While it generally provides reliable daily transportation, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints regarding electrical glitches, intermittent powertrain concerns, and occasional accessory failures. Taking time for a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure any underlying issues are caught early.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder

This naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine produces around 197 horsepower and 191 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It offers efficiency for daily commutes, though overall power is modest for a large sedan.

### 3.6L V6

This robust V6 engine delivers 305 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque, matched to a 6-speed automatic transmission. It provides brisk acceleration and generally solid reliability for long-distance driving.

**Reduced Engine Power Mode**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden check engine light and reduced engine power, limiting vehicle speed during wet weather conditions.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes and replace faulty wiring harnesses, throttle bodies, or sensors as needed.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the front wheels under normal conditions, though some units have exhibited mechanical shudders or electronic shifter sensor issues.

**Torque Converter Shudder and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a persistent shudder or vibration during acceleration that can lead to internal transmission failure.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the torque converter or rebuild/replace the entire transmission unit.
- **Cost:** $5870.68

**'Shift to Park' Message Glitch**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle displays a 'Shift to Park' warning on the dashboard even when the shifter is fully in the park position, sometimes preventing the car from turning off.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the shifter microswitch or assembly.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Cabin Electronics

Cabin electrical systems generally function well, though intermittent audio glitches and accessory fuse failures have been reported by some owners.

**Loss of Audio and Chimes**
- **Symptoms:** Interior audio, including radio, turn signal clicks, and door open warnings, cuts out completely.
- **Solution:** Reset or update the radio module, or replace faulty amplifier components.

## Safety

### Air Bag Sensors

Safety equipment is robust overall, but proper operation of seat-mounted occupant detection sensors should be verified during a pre-purchase test drive.

**Passenger Seat Occupant Sensor Fault**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger airbag warning light illuminates or the airbag deactivates unexpectedly because internal wiring or sensors rub against the seat frame.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat wiring harness and replace faulty occupant detection sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V576000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Impala&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A reduction of braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the vehicle's brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Chevrolet Impala remains a comfortable and capable used sedan option, particularly when equipped with the strong V6 engine. While most vehicles operate without major incident, buyers should carefully check for transmission shifting behavior, functional interior electronics, and working safety sensors. A comprehensive inspection by an independent mechanic prior to purchase is strongly recommended.
