# 2016 Chevrolet Impala Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Chevrolet Impala is a full-size sedan known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride quality, and composed handling. While it generally serves as a dependable commuter or family cruiser, prospective buyers should review certain recurring trouble spots reported by owners, including electrical quirks, climate control failures, and occasional transmission or steering concerns. A thorough pre-purchase inspection can help verify that major powertrain and electronic systems are functioning properly.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 196 horsepower and 186 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient performance for daily commuting. It is paired with a six-speed automatic transmission and generally maintains a dependable mechanical track record when properly serviced.

### 3.6L V6 Engine

The 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers a robust 305 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for highway passing. It is a widely used GM powerplant, though buyers should monitor for standard maintenance upkeep and occasional accessory drive wear.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The six-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery across engine options. While most units operate smoothly, some owners have experienced slipping, unexpected gear loss, or stalling issues requiring professional diagnostic attention.

**Transmission Slipping and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the transmission loses gear engagement, leading to sudden revving, failure to accelerate, or engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission fluid condition, valve body operation, and internal components; rebuild or replacement may be necessary for severe mechanical failures.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The electric power steering system provides light and responsive handling under normal conditions, though intermittent electronic or mechanical failures have been reported by some owners.

**Power Steering Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a sudden loss of power steering assistance, making the steering wheel extremely heavy and difficult to turn, often accompanied by a warning light.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic power steering module, wiring connections, and steering rack assembly for faults; replace failed components as needed.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Climate Control

Cabin electronics and climate controls generally function well, but evaporator core leaks and occasional radio or infotainment glitches have been noted by vehicle owners.

**Air Conditioning Evaporator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The air conditioning system blows warm air due to refrigerant leaks from the evaporator core, often requiring dashboard removal for repairs.
- **Solution:** Pressure test the A/C system to locate refrigerant leaks and replace faulty evaporator core components.
- **Cost:** $1,700

## Safety

### Air Bag and Restraints

Safety systems include comprehensive airbag coverage and seat belt sensors, though intermittent warning lights can occasionally appear due to sensor or wiring faults.

**Airbag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates continuously on the instrument cluster, indicating a fault within the supplemental restraint system.
- **Solution:** Scan the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) module for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect seat belt pretensioners or sensors.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Chevrolet Impala stands out as a comfortable, spacious full-size sedan that delivers a pleasant driving experience. While many owners enjoy trouble-free motoring, used car shoppers should carefully inspect the vehicle for potential issues such as A/C evaporator leaks, power steering faults, and transmission performance anomalies. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verifying service records will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
