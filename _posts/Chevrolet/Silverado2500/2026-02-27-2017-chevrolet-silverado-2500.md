# 2017 Chevrolet Silverado 2500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Chevrolet Silverado 2500, prospective buyers will find a heavy-duty truck known for its robust towing capacity and rugged utility. However, reviewing owner complaints highlights specific areas that warrant close inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation, particularly regarding braking systems, powertrain behavior, and electrical accessories.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L V8 Engine

The 6.0-liter gasoline V8 engine produces roughly 360 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, offering dependable workhorse performance for heavy-duty tasks.

### 6.6L Duramax V8 Turbodiesel

The 6.6-liter Duramax turbodiesel V8 delivers massive torque—up to 910 lb-ft in later iterations—making it a preferred choice for heavy hauling, though emissions and fuel system components require vigilant maintenance.

**Fuel Injector and Emissions Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated dashboard warning lights, reduced engine power, and trouble codes related to fuel delivery or NOx sensors.
- **Solution:** Diagnose failed sensors or injectors and replace affected components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the 6.0L gas engine, the 6-speed automatic provides predictable shifting for daily driving and towing, though routine fluid checks are advised.

### Allison 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

The Allison 6-speed automatic transmission is paired with the Duramax diesel engine, renowned for heavy-duty durability, though some owners report shifting anomalies or shudder over time.

**Transmission Shudder and Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering or vibration during acceleration, rough gear engagement, or hesitation and slipping while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified transmission specialist inspect the unit for internal wear, perform a fluid flush, or service the torque converter.

## Brakes

### Brake System

The braking hardware is engineered to handle heavy loads, but certain owner reports indicate unexpected pedal stiffness and assist failures.

**Brake Pedal Stiffness and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal suddenly becomes extremely hard to depress, leading to significantly increased stopping distances or complete loss of normal brake assist.
- **Solution:** Inspect the vacuum pump, brake booster, and hydraulic lines for leaks or failures, and replace faulty components.

## Steering

### Steering Assembly

Steering components generally provide solid feedback, though unexpected losses of power assist have been documented by drivers.

**Steering Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden stiffness in the steering wheel or complete loss of power steering assist while turning or maneuvering at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the power steering pump, electronic assist modules, and wiring connections for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Dash Electronics

Electrical accessories manage complex towing and cabin functions, with occasional reports of sensor and lighting synchronization glitches.

**Headlight and Display Glitch**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights and navigation screens intermittently switch to daylight mode during nighttime driving, causing automatic headlights to turn off unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Check ambient light sensors and update or reset the infotainment and body control modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V567000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 209, "Seat Belt Assemblies." The seat belt retractors may not perform as intended during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace both front seat belt retractor assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V328000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The engine block heater cord or the terminals that connect the heater cord to the block heater may short circuit and fail. A short-circuit may damage the engine components and increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** GM will contact owners, dealers will replace the engine block heater, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V496000: Equipment:Electrical:Engine Block Heater](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short-circuit can increase the risk of a fire. Vehicles that have this feature disabled as a remedy to the safety condition will be contacted and offered replacement block heaters and heater cords under a separate customer satisfaction program, free of charge.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace engine block heaters and heater cords. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V047000: Fuel System, Diesel:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Silverado+2500&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the fuel tank and fuel pump module, replacing them as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Chevrolet Silverado 2500 is a capable and powerful heavy-duty truck well-suited for demanding work and towing duties. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's braking system, steering assist functionality, and transmission behavior during a test drive. Verifying service records for the diesel emissions and fuel systems can also help ensure long-term reliability.
