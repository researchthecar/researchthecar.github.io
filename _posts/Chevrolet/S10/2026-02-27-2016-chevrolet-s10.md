### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Chevrolet S10 demonstrates a generally solid reliability profile. The powertrain, including both the gasoline and diesel engine options and their associated transmissions, appears robust with no widespread critical failures reported. Suspension and exterior components are also typically durable. The most notable area for potential concern, though not widespread, is the electrical system, with some reports of intermittent power issues that require professional diagnosis and repair. Overall, the S10 is a dependable choice for a compact pickup, provided standard maintenance is performed and any minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
