**Introduction:**

The 1996 Chevrolet S10 is a compact pickup truck with various engine and transmission options. With its powerful engines and robust chassis, it is known for its capability and durability.

**Engine**

* **2.2L SOHC 4-cylinder**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, rough idling
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4.3L V6**
    * **Timing Chain Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Rattling noise on startup, loss of power, engine stalling
        * Solution: Replace timing chain assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Valve Seal Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace intake valve seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-speed Automatic (4L60E)**
    * **Delayed Engagement:**
        * Symptoms: Transmission delays in shifting from park to drive or reverse
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoid or valve body
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Overheating:**
        * Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks, burning smell, slipping gears
        * Solution: Replace transmission cooler or transmission assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5-speed Manual (NV3500)**
    * **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Soft or no clutch pedal, difficulty shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Synchronizer Gear Wear:**
        * Symptoms: Grinding noise when shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace synchronizer gears
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension:**
    * **Ball Joint Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, loose steering
        * Solution: Replace ball joints
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension:**
    * **Leaf Spring Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride
        * Solution: Replace leaf springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Battery fails to hold charge, vehicle won't start
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Dim headlights, flickering dashboard lights, vehicle stalls
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Wiring Harness Damage:**
        * Symptoms: Electrical issues, blown fuses, flickering lights
        * Solution: Inspect and repair damaged wiring
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust Issues:**
        * Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, frame, and components
        * Solution: Repair or replace affected areas
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Headlights won't turn on or off
        * Solution: Replace headlight switch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Doors won't lock or unlock with power locks
        * Solution: Replace door lock actuator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
        * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Blend Door Failure:**
        * Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating doesn't blow in the desired temperature
        * Solution: Replace blend door actuator or motor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1996 Chevrolet S10 is a capable pickup truck with a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its durability but can experience various issues, including engine leaks, transmission problems, suspension wear, and electrical faults. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue, but some can be costly.
