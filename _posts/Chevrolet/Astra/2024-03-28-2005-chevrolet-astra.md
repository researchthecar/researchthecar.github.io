**Introduction:** The 2005 Chevrolet Astra is a popular compact car known for its sporty handling and spacious interior. It was available with two engine options: a 2.2L Ecotec I4 and a 2.4L Ecotec I4. Both engines came with a standard 5-speed manual transmission, while a 4-speed automatic transmission was optional.

**Engine**

**2.2L Ecotec I4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leakage of coolant, overheating, low coolant levels
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or knocking noise from engine, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

**2.4L Ecotec I4**

* **No Major Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shift Linkage Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Repair or replace shift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: N/A

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, delayed shifts, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace failed solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking noise from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+ per pair
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system failure, battery not charging, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A

* **Ignition Coil Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, poor engine performance, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per coil
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture build-up inside headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlights with vented versions
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+ per pair
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure to heat or cool cabin, erratic fan speeds
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2005 Chevrolet Astra is generally a reliable and affordable compact car, but it is not without its issues. The most common issues found with the vehicle involve the timing chain tensioner, intake manifold, and transmission solenoids. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are relatively infrequent. Overall, the Astra is a well-rounded vehicle with a comfortable interior, sporty handling, and reasonable fuel economy.
