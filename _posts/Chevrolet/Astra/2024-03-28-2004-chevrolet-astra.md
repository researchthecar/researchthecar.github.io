**Introduction**

The 2004 Chevrolet Astra is a compact car available with a 2.2L Ecotec inline-4 engine paired with a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The Ecotec engine produces 143 horsepower and 155 lb-ft of torque. Drivers praise the Astra for its sporty handling and fuel efficiency, but criticize its cramped interior and lack of features.

**Engine**

**2.2L Ecotec I4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, misfires, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: 05V160000
* **EGR Valve Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Synchronizer Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace the worn synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty accelerating, delayed shifting, engine revving high
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, vibrations, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, reduced braking effectiveness
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Assembly Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation, flickering lights
    * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Airbag Clockspring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Airbag warning light illuminated, horn not working
    * Solution: Replace the airbag clockspring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: 04V514000
* **Power Window Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows not working, intermittent operation
    * Solution: Replace the power window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2004 Chevrolet Astra has several potential issues, but most are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. The most severe problems, such as transmission slipping or engine gasket failure, can be more costly to repair. Overall, the Astra is a reliable and affordable vehicle with some common but manageable issues.
