**Introduction:** The 2022 Chevrolet Traverse is a three-row SUV that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and strong towing capacity. It is available with two engine options: a 3.6-liter V6 with 310 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder with 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission. The Traverse is praised for its generous passenger and cargo space, powerful engines, and smooth ride. However, it has been criticized for its fuel economy, dated interior, and lack of some advanced safety features.

**Engine**
**3.6L V6**
- **Intake manifold failure:** Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold can crack, leading to coolant leaks, overheating, and engine damage. Solution: Replacing the intake manifold. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ Recalls: N/A
- **Valve cover gasket leak:** Description/Symptoms: The valve cover gasket can leak oil, resulting in smoke, burning oil smell, and engine performance issues. Solution: Replacing the valve cover gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ Recalls: N/A
**2.0L Turbocharged I4**
- **No significant issues found**

**Transmission**
**9-Speed Automatic**
- **Hesitation or surging during gear changes:** Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or surge when shifting gears, causing a rough or delayed response. Solution: Updating the transmission control module software. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V-156

**Suspension**
- **Front strut mount failure:** Description/Symptoms: The front strut mounts can fail, leading to a clunking or rattling noise when driving over bumps or rough roads. Solution: Replacing the front strut mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ Recalls: N/A
- **Rear shock absorber failure:** Description/Symptoms: The rear shock absorbers can wear out prematurely, resulting in a bumpy ride, poor handling, and increased stopping distance. Solution: Replacing the rear shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**
- **Battery drain:** Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain quickly when the vehicle is parked, even with all accessories turned off. Solution: Identifying and fixing the source of the battery drain (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic draw). Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ Recalls: N/A
- **Electrical short circuit:** Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience electrical problems, such as flickering lights, inoperative gauges, or even a complete loss of power. Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the short circuit. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+ Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**
- **Headlight condensation:** Description/Symptoms: Moisture may accumulate inside the headlight assemblies, causing condensation and poor visibility at night. Solution: Replacing the headlight assemblies or resealing them. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ Recalls: N/A
- **Door handle malfunction:** Description/Symptoms: The exterior door handles can stick, making it difficult to open or close the doors. Solution: Replacing the door handles. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ Recalls: N/A

**Interior**
- **Dashboard rattle:** Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle or squeak when driving on rough roads or at certain speeds. Solution: Identifying and padding or tightening the loose components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+ Recalls: N/A
- **Climate control issues:** Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may blow cold air when set to heat or vice versa, or fail to regulate temperature properly. Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the faulty component (e.g., thermostat, blend door actuator). Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ Recalls: N/A

**Summary:** The 2022 Chevrolet Traverse has a number of potential issues, including engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is recommended to research common problems before purchasing this vehicle and to schedule regular maintenance to prevent and address any issues promptly.
