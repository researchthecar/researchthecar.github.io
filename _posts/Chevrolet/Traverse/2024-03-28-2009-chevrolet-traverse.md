**2009 Chevrolet Traverse**

The 2009 Chevrolet Traverse is a three-row crossover SUV known for its spacious interior, versatile seating arrangements, and powerful engine options. It came with two engine options: a 3.6L V6 and a 5.3L V8, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Drivers have generally praised the Traverse's comfortable ride, functional design, and towing capabilities. However, like any vehicle, it has had its share of issues.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

**Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage
Solution: Piston ring replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant and/or oil leaks, rough idling, decreased power
Solution: Intake manifold gasket replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**5.3L V8**

*No Issues Found*

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed or hard shifting, slipping gears
Solution: Transmission overhaul or replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
Recalls: 14V415000, 14V416000, 15V567000

**Overheating**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission overheating, burning smell, loss of power
Solution: Transmission cooler replacement, transmission fluid flush, or transmission replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Lower Control Arms**
Description/Symptoms: Worn bushings, causing clunking or rattling noises, poor handling
Solution: Control arm replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Shock Absorbers**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking or worn shock absorbers, causing reduced ride quality, poor handling
Solution: Shock absorber replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*No Issues Found*

**Exterior**

*No Issues Found*

**Interior**

**Dashboard Panel Warping**
Description/Symptoms: Dashboard panel warping or cracking over time
Solution: Dashboard panel replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not functioning correctly, such as temperature not adjusting or air flow issues
Solution: Climate control module or actuator replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Chevrolet Traverse has been a popular and generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its potential issues. The most common concerns are related to oil consumption and transmission problems. The severity of these issues can vary, and so can the cost to fix them. However, with proper maintenance and addressing any issues promptly, the Traverse can still provide a satisfying ownership experience for many years to come.
