**Introduction**

The 2011 Chevrolet Traverse is a midsize SUV that was offered with a choice of two engines and two transmissions. The base engine was a 3.6L V6 that produced 281 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, while the optional engine was a 3.0L V6 that produced 264 horsepower and 223 lb-ft of torque. The 3.6L V6 came standard with a 6-speed automatic transmission, while the 3.0L V6 was paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. The Traverse was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and strong V6 engines. However, it was also criticized for its lack of fuel efficiency and its dated interior.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

* **Intake manifold cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold can crack, causing a loss of power, rough idling, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: 14V449000
* **Oil consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume excessive oil, leading to low oil levels and engine damage.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the engine.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift hard, slip, or hesitate.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission or repair the valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: 14V430000
* **Transmission failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may fail completely, leaving the vehicle unable to move.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: 14V456000

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front struts can fail, causing the vehicle to bounce excessively and handle poorly.
    * Solution: Replace the struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the battery and diagnose the cause of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Window regulator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The window regulators can fail, causing the windows to become stuck.
    * Solution: Replace the window regulators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: 14V380000

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle due to loose components.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace the loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Chevrolet Traverse is a spacious and comfortable SUV with powerful V6 engines. However, it has a number of potential issues, including intake manifold cracking, oil consumption, transmission problems, and suspension failure. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but they can be a significant inconvenience for owners.
