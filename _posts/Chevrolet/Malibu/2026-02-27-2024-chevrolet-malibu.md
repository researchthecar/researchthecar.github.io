# 2024 Chevrolet Malibu Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Chevrolet Malibu represents the final model year for this long-running midsize sedan. For used car buyers, it offers familiar styling and daily drivability, though early owner feedback points to notable electrical issues and camera glitches. Prospective owners should review vehicle history reports carefully and ensure any outstanding safety recalls have been resolved.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

Equipped with a 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine producing approximately 163 horsepower, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). This setup prioritizes fuel economy for daily commuting, though some drivers experience hesitation during initial takeoff.

**Acceleration Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where pressing the accelerator after a stop results in a delayed response before the vehicle begins to move.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the throttle body, fuel delivery system, and powertrain control module for software updates or faults.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

The front-wheel-drive CVT is designed for smooth, efficient cruising, but can occasionally exhibit harsh engagement during cold weather operations.

**Hard Shifting and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences unexpected jerking and harsh engagement during cold starts, alongside occasional temporary losses in driving power.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scan required to check for hidden trouble codes and software updates for the transmission control module.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Backup Camera System

Cabin electronics manage infotainment, climate controls, and driver assistance feeds, though camera reliability has been a frequent pain point for owners.

**Backup Camera and Display Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera may display a black screen, freeze, or fail entirely when the vehicle is placed in reverse, occasionally accompanied by infotainment screen glitches during wet weather.
- **Solution:** Check for applicable dealer software flashes or recall remedies, and have faulty display modules inspected or replaced if necessary.

## Exterior

### Trunk Seals

Exterior body seals and panels generally fit tightly, though occasional weatherstripping defects can allow moisture into cargo areas.

**Water Pooling in Trunk**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have discovered water accumulating inside the trunk area due to compromised weatherstripping seals.
- **Solution:** Inspect trunk weather seals for damage or improper alignment, and replace faulty gaskets to prevent moisture intrusion.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Chevrolet Malibu can serve as an affordable commuter option, but buyers should approach with attentive inspection habits. Particular attention should be paid to the electronic display functions, backup camera operation, and any signs of moisture intrusion in the trunk. Verifying that all safety recalls have been completed by a certified dealer is strongly recommended before purchase.
