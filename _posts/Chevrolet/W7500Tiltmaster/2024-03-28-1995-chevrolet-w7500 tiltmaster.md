**Introduction:**

The 1995 Chevrolet W7500 Tiltmaster is a heavy-duty commercial truck known for its durability and towing capabilities. It comes standard with a 7.4L gasoline V8 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 6.5L turbocharged diesel V8 engine and a 5-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

**7.4L Gasoline V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil and coolant leaks, reduced engine power, overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Distributor Cap and Rotor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No spark, engine misfires, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace distributor cap and rotor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.5L Turbocharged Diesel V8**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive smoke, turbocharger whine
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Fuel Injection Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, no fuel pressure, hard starting
    * Solution: Replace fuel injection pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power under acceleration, delayed shifting, grinding noises
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering during acceleration, loss of power, overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Synchronizer Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting into certain gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken leaf springs, rough ride
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+ per axle
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy or unstable ride, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+ per shock
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No crank, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust and Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Surface rust or holes in body panels, frame, or undercarriage
    * Solution: Preventative measures (rustproofing, undercoating) or repairs if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on the extent of rust
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bulbs burning out frequently, poor illumination
    * Solution: Replace headlights or upgrade to brighter bulbs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard surface
    * Solution: Replace or cover dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Windows not operating properly, switches not functioning
    * Solution: Repair or replace power window motor or switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per window
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1995 Chevrolet W7500 Tiltmaster is a reliable and durable truck with a few common issues to be aware of. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, while exterior and interior issues are generally less severe and less expensive to fix. Overall, the vehicle remains a solid choice for heavy-duty hauling and towing.
