**2015 Chevrolet Spark**

The 2015 Chevrolet Spark is a subcompact hatchback offered with two engine options: a 1.2-liter four-cylinder engine and a 1.4-liter four-cylinder engine. Both engines are paired with a five-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT).

**Engine**

* **1.2-Liter Engine**
    * **Intake Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine may stall or run rough at idle, poor fuel economy, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **1.4-Liter Engine**
    * No Issues Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace clutch or transmission components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **CVT**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid loss, slipping gears
        * Solution: Repair or replace transmission oil cooler
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts**
    * **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when driving over bumps, reduced handling
        * Solution: Replace strut mounts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension**
    * No Issues Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Battery Discharge**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, slow starting
        * Solution: Replace battery, check for parasitic drain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlights**
    * **Headlight Switch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not turning on, intermittent flickering
        * Solution: Replace headlight switch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handles**
    * **Door Handle Cable Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening doors, broken door handle
        * Solution: Replace door handle cable
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
        * Recalls: 14V726

**Interior**

* **Dashboard**
    * **Instrument Cluster Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard lights malfunctioning or not working
        * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: 16V385

**Summary**

The 2015 Chevrolet Spark has several known issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific problem encountered. However, these issues can impact the vehicle's reliability and performance. Owners should be aware of these potential issues when considering purchasing or maintaining this vehicle.
