# 2022 Chevrolet Spark Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Chevrolet Spark offers budget-conscious shoppers an affordable, compact ride suited primarily for city commuting. While used-car buyers generally appreciate its efficiency and straightforward nature, it is always wise to inspect various vehicle systems thoroughly. Reviewing owner reports can provide helpful context on what to watch for during a used vehicle test drive.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2022 Chevrolet Spark is equipped with a 1.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing around 98 horsepower and 94 lb-ft of torque. This small powerplant focuses heavily on fuel efficiency and provides adequate pep for urban driving conditions.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Power is typically routed through a continuously variable transmission, offering seamless acceleration suited for daily commuting. Buyers should evaluate transmission responsiveness and listen for unusual whines during a test drive.

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 5-speed manual transmission is also available on select base trims for drivers who prefer a direct shifting experience. This simpler gearbox option is generally robust, provided the clutch has been maintained properly.

## Electrical

### Wipers and Stability Control Electronics

Electrical systems manage comfort and safety accessories; checking all switches and features during a test drive is recommended.

**Electrical Glitches in Wet Weather**
- **Symptoms:** An owner experienced erratic behavior with the windshield wipers, stability control, and other electrical functions while driving through rain.
- **Solution:** Check battery connections, ground points, and module connectors for moisture intrusion or corrosion.

## Exterior

### Hood Latch and Structure

Exterior body panels and latches generally hold up well, though physical inspection of latches is recommended.

**Unexpected Hood Opening**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that the hood unexpectedly flew up while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hood latch mechanism and secondary safety catch to ensure they secure properly without looseness.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety equipment is designed to protect occupants, though rare component non-deployment reports highlight the importance of checking airbag indicator lights.

**Driver Air Bag Failure to Deploy**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that side airbags deployed during a high-speed rollover crash, but the driver steering wheel airbag did not.
- **Solution:** Have the supplemental restraint system (SRS) inspected and diagnostic trouble codes scanned if any warning lights are present.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Chevrolet Spark remains an appealing, economical choice for budget-minded drivers. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential concerns regarding electrical glitches, hood latches, and airbag performance, these owner reports are not proof of widespread structural defects. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and test all vehicle features before finalizing a purchase.
