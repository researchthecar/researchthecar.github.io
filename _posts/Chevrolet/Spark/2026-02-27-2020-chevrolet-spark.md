# 2020 Chevrolet Spark Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Chevrolet Spark is an economical subcompact city car known for its budget-friendly nature and impressive fuel efficiency. While generally simple and straightforward mechanically, used-car buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical anomalies, unexpected power loss, and minor body or interior trim quirks before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L I4 LV7 Engine

The 2020 Spark features a 1.4-liter naturally aspirated inline-four engine producing 98 horsepower and 94 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for city commutes and decent fuel economy, though occasional complaints cite sudden powertrain stalling or power loss.

**Unexpected Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the vehicle may suddenly lose motive power while driving at various speeds, though it can sometimes be restarted afterward.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the powertrain control module, fuel delivery system, and throttle body components for faults.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Equipped with an electronic continuously variable transmission designed to maximize city fuel efficiency. It operates smoothly under normal conditions, though proper fluid maintenance is critical for long-term durability.

### 5-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 5-speed manual transmission is available on base trims for drivers preferring direct gear selection. It is a mechanically simple unit with a generally dependable track record.

## Electrical

### Battery Connections and Wiring

The electrical architecture is generally adequate, but owners should inspect wiring harnesses and battery terminals for secure connections during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Intermittent Electrical Disconnection**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden electrical loss or failure to maintain power until battery terminals or cables are adjusted or secured.
- **Solution:** Inspect and clean battery terminals, ensure tight cable connections, and check ground points for corrosion.

## Interior

### Cargo Area Cover

Interior ergonomics are straightforward, though minor convenience features like the rear cargo cover can occasionally prove troublesome for daily usability.

**Trunk Divider Unlocking Randomly**
- **Symptoms:** The rear cargo shelf or divider may unlatch or pop open unexpectedly while driving, obstructing visibility through the rear window.
- **Solution:** Check the mounting clips and securing mechanisms, or secure the divider manually to prevent unexpected movement.

The 2020 Chevrolet Spark serves as an affordable and nimble option for tight urban environments, but used-car shoppers should remain thorough during evaluations. Prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection checking the battery connections, electrical components, and overall mechanical health to ensure a dependable driving experience.
