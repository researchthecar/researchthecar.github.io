**Introduction:**

The 2022 Chevrolet Spark is a subcompact hatchback that offers a standard 1.4L I-4 engine with 98 horsepower and 123 lb-ft of torque. The Spark is available with either a 5-speed manual transmission or a continuously variable transmission (CVT). It's known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability in urban areas, but criticism includes lackluster performance, cramped interior, and poor sound insulation.

**Engine**

**1.4L I-4**

* **Engine Knock:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking noise from the engine under acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace defective piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Shifter Wobble:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive play in the shifter, making it difficult to engage gears.
    * Solution: Replace shifter assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping when accelerating.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, even with the engine off.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Paint Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Paint peeling and blistering on the tailgate.
    * Solution: Sand and repaint the tailgate.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cracked Instrument Cluster:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the instrument cluster, making it difficult to read.
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Chevrolet Spark has a few common issues, but most are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. The most significant issue is transmission slipping in the CVT, which can be costly to repair. However, overall, the Spark is a reliable and affordable vehicle for urban commuters.
