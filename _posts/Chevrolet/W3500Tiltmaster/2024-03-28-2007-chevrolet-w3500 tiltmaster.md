**Introduction:**

The 2007 Chevrolet W3500 Tiltmaster is a heavy-duty pickup truck that comes standard with a 6.6L Duramax V8 engine and a six-speed automatic transmission. It offers impressive towing and payload capabilities, making it popular for commercial and agricultural use.

The W3500 Tiltmaster is available with two other engine options: a 6.0L Vortec V8 and a 8.1L Vortec V8. The 6.0L Vortec V8 produces 360 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, while the 8.1L Vortec V8 produces 340 horsepower and 455 lb-ft of torque.

Despite its rugged capabilities, the W3500 Tiltmaster has a comfortable and spacious interior, with amenities like a six-speaker audio system, air conditioning, and power windows. However, it has been criticized for its lack of modern safety features and its high fuel consumption.

**Engine**

**6.6L Duramax V8**

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idle, reduced power, increased emissions
    * Solution: Replace faulty injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: #06009

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling or screeching noises, excessive smoke
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, slipping, or delayed engagement
    * Solution: Replace faulty solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken rear end, rough ride
    * Solution: Replace faulty leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Front Shock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy or unstable ride, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replace faulty shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical system malfunctions, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace faulty alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Wiring Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical problems, loss of function in specific components
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, one or both headlights not working
    * Solution: Replace faulty bulbs or headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, door locks not working
    * Solution: Replace faulty door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges not working or displaying incorrect readings
    * Solution: Replace faulty instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Chevrolet W3500 Tiltmaster is a capable heavy-duty truck with impressive towing and payload capabilities. However, it has a number of potential issues that can be costly to repair, such as injector failure, turbocharger failure, and rear leaf spring failure. It also lacks modern safety features and has high fuel consumption.
