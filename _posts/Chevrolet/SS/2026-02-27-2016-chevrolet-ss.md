# 2016 Chevrolet SS Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Chevrolet SS is a rear-wheel-drive performance sedan powered by a robust V8 engine, offering a blend of muscle-car dynamics and everyday practicality. While enthusiast owners generally praise its performance and comfort, prospective buyers should review known owner reports regarding electronic steering assist warnings, sensor water intrusion, and seat belt tensioner hardware. As with any high-performance vehicle, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L V8 LS3

The 2016 Chevrolet SS comes standard with a potent 6.2-liter LS3 V8 engine producing 415 horsepower and 415 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling acceleration and a classic V8 exhaust note, though higher-mileage examples should be checked for valvetrain noise.

**Collapsed Lifter**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an audible ticking or knocking noise from the engine bay, commonly associated with valve train wear.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnosis by a qualified technician and replacement of the affected lifters and potentially the camshaft.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The available 6-speed manual transmission provides a direct, engaging shifting experience for enthusiasts. It is generally robust when maintained properly, though clutch wear depends heavily on prior driving habits.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission features tap-shift controls for manual gear selection. It offers smooth cruising capability, though buyers should verify crisp shifts during a test drive.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering Rack

The electric power steering system provides adjustable weight and responsive handling, but owners have reported intermittent stiffness requiring inspection of the rack's internal components.

**Power Steering Stiffness and Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden steering stiffness accompanied by a 'service power steering' warning message on the dash.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering rack and electrical connections for faults, and verify whether applicable manufacturer service campaigns or recalls have been performed.

## Electrical

### Proximity and Parking Sensors

Driver assistance features enhance everyday usability, but exterior sensors are susceptible to moisture intrusion and environmental wear over time.

**Side Detection and Park Assist Errors**
- **Symptoms:** The driver information center displays 'Service Side Detection System' or 'Park Assist Unavailable' warnings due to sensor moisture exposure.
- **Solution:** Inspect sensor modules and wiring harnesses for water intrusion, corrosion, or physical damage, and replace faulty sensors as needed.

### Infotainment and Display System

The MyLink infotainment system handles audio, navigation, and vehicle settings, though sporadic electronic glitches and display blanks have been noted by owners.

**Backup Camera and Screen Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display may go blank, freeze, or fail to show the rearview camera image when reversing.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic checks may require updating software, repairing wiring connections, or replacing the complete radio/display assembly.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs as expected, though proper installation and hardware integrity of seat belt tensioners should be verified during inspections.

**Tensioner Cable Exposure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that seat belt tensioner cables or associated protective covers become loose, exposed, or floppy following previous repairs.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the seat belt mounting points and secure or replace loose trim and covers.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V638000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=SS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The repair may not have been performed properly. If the tensioner cable separates, the driver may not be properly restrained in the event of a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles to determine whether the new seat trim was installed and replace the trim as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V801000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=SS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Corrosion of the connector between the electric power steering module and the torque sensor connector may cause a loss of electric power steering assist. A greater steering effort would be needed to control the vehicle if power steering is lost, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the steering gear assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V254000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=SS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Corrosion of the connector between the electric power steering module and the torque sensor connector may cause a loss of electric power steering assist. A greater steering effort would be needed to control the vehicle if power steering is lost, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the steering gear assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=SS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V382000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=SS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Corrosion of the connector between the electric power steering module and the torque sensor connector may cause a loss of electric power steering assist. A greater steering effort would be needed to control the vehicle if power steering is lost, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the steering gear assembly, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V437000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=SS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** While being previously remedied for recall 16V-651, the affected vehicles may not have received the complete software update necessary to remedy the recall condition. Without the update, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V518000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=SS&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the tensioner cable separates, the driver may not be properly restrained in the event of a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the tensioner cable assembly, replacing any cable that is damaged. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Chevrolet SS remains a highly desirable performance sedan combining V8 muscle with everyday comfort. While mechanical basics are generally solid, buyers should carefully check for electrical issues, sensor warnings, and steering assist operation. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable mechanic is essential to catch any underlying issues before purchase.
