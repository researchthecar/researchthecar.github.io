# 2017 Chevrolet SS Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Chevrolet SS, buyers are looking at a modern performance sedan equipped with a robust V8 powerplant and rear-wheel drive. While generally praised for its enthusiast appeal and everyday utility, owner reports submitted to safety regulators highlight a few scattered mechanical and electrical concerns worth noting during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L V8 LS3

The 6.2L V8 LS3 engine produces approximately 415 horsepower and 415 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration and a traditional pushrod V8 design. It is generally robust and well-regarded for durability, though routine maintenance is essential for high-performance use.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The available 6-speed manual transmission pairs directly with the V8 engine for a traditional, engaging driver experience. It is mechanically straightforward, but buyers should check the clutch condition and shift quality during a test drive.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and delivers smooth cruising performance. Regular fluid changes help maintain longevity, though performance-driven models should be inspected for signs of wear.

## Suspension

### Rear Wheel Bearings

Suspension components are generally robust, though individual bearing wear can occur.

**Sealed Wheel Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature failure of the rear sealed wheel bearings, which can lead to humming noises or wheel play.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed wheel bearing assembly.
- **Cost:** $1,142

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is strong, though noise complaints are occasionally reported under light pedal application.

**Brake Squeal**
- **Symptoms:** Applying the brakes may produce a noticeable and persistent squealing noise during normal operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors, and apply appropriate anti-squeal shims or replace pads with alternative compounds.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering responsiveness is normally sharp, but unexpected assistance losses have been reported by a small number of owners.

**Power Steering Interruption**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced unexpected losses of power steering functionality, making the vehicle difficult to control at speed.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering components, wiring connections, and fluid levels or replace faulty steering assemblies as needed.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical complaints are infrequent, but intermittent power drops can pose unexpected drivability concerns.

**Intermittent Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience sudden losses of electrical power and ignition while driving, followed by an immediate restart.
- **Solution:** Diagnose battery terminals, ignition switches, and main electrical harnesses for loose connections or faults.

## Interior

### Interior Trim and Visibility

Exterior and body elements are largely trouble-free, with minor cosmetic design complaints being the primary note.

**Reflective Chrome Trim Glare**
- **Symptoms:** Bright sunlight can reflect off shiny chrome interior trim surrounding the navigation and shifter areas into the driver's eyes.
- **Solution:** Apply a non-reflective matte vinyl wrap or film to tone down the shiny trim surfaces.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Air Bags

Safety systems generally function as intended, though occasional warning lights related to restraint wiring have been noted.

**Seatbelt Tensioner Cable Fault**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light may illuminate due to faults within the seatbelt tensioner cable assembly.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seatbelt assembly and replace the faulty tensioner cable or related components.

## Summary Verdict

The Chevrolet SS is a compelling performance sedan that combines everyday practicality with V8 muscle. While owner-reported complaints point to occasional electrical glitches, wheel bearing wear, and steering assist concerns, the vehicle does not suffer from widespread systemic powertrain failures. Prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the suspension, steering, and electrical components to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
