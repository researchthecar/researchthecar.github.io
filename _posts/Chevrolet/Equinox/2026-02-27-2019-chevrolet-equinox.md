# 2019 Chevrolet Equinox Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Chevrolet Equinox is a compact crossover offering comfortable ride quality, practical cabin space, and competitive fuel economy with its turbocharged engine lineup. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability trends carefully, particularly regarding brake performance and electrical system anomalies. While many drivers enjoy its daily usability, addressing known mechanical quirks early can prevent costly repairs down the road.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged I4

The base 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 170 horsepower and 203 lb-ft of torque, paired with a front- or all-wheel-drive system. It offers a balance of efficiency for daily commuting, though owners should monitor for vacuum pump and fuel system concerns.

**Brake Vacuum Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden loss of brake assist, resulting in a stiff, hard-to-depress brake pedal and warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replace the vacuum pump and inspect related camshaft components for potential shrapnel damage.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden engine stalls while driving or unexpected shutdowns during auto-stop events.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery and powertrain control modules; replace faulty sensors or fuel pump components as necessary.

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The optional 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four generates around 252 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing much stronger acceleration and increased towing capacity compared to the base engine. It generally offers robust performance when properly maintained.

### 1.6L Turbo-Diesel I4

The 1.6-liter turbo-diesel engine produces roughly 137 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, prioritizing highway fuel economy and low-end pulling power. It represents a less common option in the used market.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission is typically paired with the base 1.5L engine and diesel variants, offering smooth power delivery under normal driving conditions.

**Transmission Slippage and Abrupt Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden vehicle deceleration, harsh gear changes, slipping during acceleration, or delayed engagement from a stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition, check the valve body and internal accumulator, or repair/replace transmission assemblies if internal wear is present.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with the 2.0-liter turbo engine, providing refined gear selection and improved highway cruising efficiency.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

The service brake system provides reliable stopping power under normal conditions, though recurring reports of vacuum pump failures warrant careful inspection prior to purchase.

**Brake Assist Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal becomes difficult to depress, leading to extended stopping distances and illuminated warning messages.
- **Solution:** Replace the mechanical vacuum pump and associated brake booster components.

## Electrical

### Body Electrical and Infotainment

Electrical systems control cabin amenities and safety features, though occasional reports highlight vulnerability to water intrusion in the rear cargo area.

**Water Leak into Rear Compartment Fuse Box**
- **Symptoms:** Water pooling in the spare tire well leading to corrosion or electrical glitches in rear-mounted fuse panels.
- **Solution:** Identify and seal leak sources around the liftgate or tail lamps, and replace damaged electrical connectors.

### Power Train Shifter

The drivetrain components transfer power effectively to the wheels, though the shifter mechanism is prone to microswitch failures that trigger annoying parking warnings.

**Shift to Park Error Message**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to recognize that it has been placed in park, preventing the ignition from turning off or draining the battery.
- **Solution:** Replace the shifter microswitch assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V668000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Missing bolts on the start-stop accumulator endcap could result in a transmission oil leak and may progress to a loss of propulsion, increasing the risk of a crash. A transmission fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the start-stop transmission accumulator and replace it if any bolts are missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V576000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A reduction of braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the vehicle's brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V667000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of the ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V184000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the insufficient welds may result in an increased risk of injury for an occupant seated in the rear-right position.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear-right seatback frame, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Chevrolet Equinox offers a versatile cabin and pleasant road manners, making it a popular choice in the compact SUV segment. Used car buyers should pay close attention to brake vacuum pump operation and test all electronic features thoroughly during a pre-purchase inspection. Verifying that safety and powertrain updates have been addressed will help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.
