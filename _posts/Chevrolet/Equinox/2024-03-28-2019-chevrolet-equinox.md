**Introduction**

The 2019 Chevrolet Equinox is a popular midsize SUV known for its spacious interior, impressive fuel economy, and user-friendly infotainment system. It's powered by a 1.5L turbocharged four-cylinder engine mated to a six-speed automatic transmission, producing 170 horsepower and 203 lb-ft of torque. Other engine and transmission options include:

* 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine with nine-speed automatic transmission (252 horsepower, 260 lb-ft of torque)

**Engine**

**1.5L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Carbon buildup on intake valves:** Symptoms include rough idling, misfires, and reduced engine performance. Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting hesitation:** Symptoms include delayed or unresponsive shifting. Solution: Reprogramming transmission control module or replacing transmission solenoid. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+

* **High-pitched whining noise:** Symptoms are a noticeable whining noise coming from the transmission. Solution: Replacing transmission input shaft bearing. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Suspension**

* **Front strut mount failure:** Symptoms include clunking or knocking noises from the front suspension, especially during turns. Solution: Replacing strut mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

* **Rear suspension sagging:** Symptoms include uneven tire wear, poor handling, and reduced ride quality. Solution: Replacing rear springs or shocks. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Symptoms include a dead battery after the vehicle has been parked. Solution: Testing and replacing faulty components, such as the alternator or battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+

**Exterior**

* **Roof rack noise:** Symptoms include wind noise or whistling sounds while driving at highway speeds. Solution: Adjusting or replacing roof rack crossbars. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

* **Tailgate unlocking issue:** Symptoms include difficulty opening the tailgate due to a malfunctioning electronic lock. Solution: Replacing tailgate actuator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Loose center console:** Symptoms include a rattling or loose center console. Solution: Tightening or replacing console mounts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

* **Infotainment screen freezing:** Symptoms include the infotainment screen becoming unresponsive or frozen. Solution: Rebooting the system or updating software. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+

**Summary**

The 2019 Chevrolet Equinox has received positive reviews for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and advanced features. However, it has experienced some issues related to engine carbon buildup, transmission shifting, suspension components, and electrical system. The severity of these issues and the associated repair costs vary, but some can be costly to address, such as intake valve cleaning or transmission replacement.
