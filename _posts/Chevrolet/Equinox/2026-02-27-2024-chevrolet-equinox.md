# 2024 Chevrolet Equinox Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Chevrolet Equinox is a popular compact crossover that offers practical daily driving and modern technology features. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints concerning powertrain performance, braking concerns, and electronic glitches. While many owners enjoy their vehicles, checking service records and conducting a thorough test drive can help ensure you select a reliable pre-owned model.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged I4

Equipped with a 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-four engine producing approximately 175 horsepower, this option prioritizes fuel efficiency for daily commuting. Overall reliability is generally acceptable, though owners have noted occasional fuel system and drivability concerns.

**Fuel Pump Failure and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report check engine lights, sudden engine hesitation, and diminished capacity or engine stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the fuel system and address any diagnostic trouble codes associated with fuel pressure.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive models, the 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth daily gear shifts. Buyers should test drive the vehicle thoroughly to verify crisp and responsive engagement across all gears.

**Transmission Shaking and Lurching**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may shake or lurch violently under heavy acceleration, such as merging onto highways.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition and update software modules if recommended by manufacturer bulletins.

## Suspension

### Tires and Ride Quality

The suspension system absorbs road imperfections effectively, but proper tire maintenance is essential to prevent unwanted cabin vibrations.

**Vibrations at Highway Speeds**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice unusual vibrations or shaking coming through the vehicle while traveling at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have wheel balancing, tire wear patterns, and suspension alignment inspected by a professional.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power under normal conditions, but occasional owner reports highlight unexpected pedal feel issues that require prompt professional inspection.

**Brake Pedal Responsiveness Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced instances where the brake pedal offers no resistance or fails to slow the vehicle adequately.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines, master cylinder, and electronic brake modules immediately if pedal feel becomes spongy or unresponsive.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Connectivity

Cabin electronics manage vital climate and entertainment functions, though periodic software glitches can affect device integration.

**Bluetooth and Infotainment Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Paired smartphones frequently lose Bluetooth connections, and contact synchronization features may fail.
- **Solution:** Perform software updates for the infotainment system or reset connection pairings.

## Exterior

### Windshield Visibility

Exterior visibility components are generally well-designed, though specific interior finishes can create notable glare under direct sunlight.

**Dashboard Glare on Windshield**
- **Symptoms:** Bright sunlight can cause strong reflections from dashboard materials onto the windshield, restricting forward visibility.
- **Solution:** Use a dashboard cover or polarized sunglasses to minimize distracting reflections.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety systems are designed to protect occupants, but sensitive collision avoidance sensors can occasionally trigger unintended braking alerts.

**Phantom Braking Events**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally apply the brakes aggressively without warning or apparent road obstructions.
- **Solution:** Ensure camera and radar sensors are clean and check for any available manufacturer software updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V869000: Structure:Body:Door](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The door strikers may fracture, resulting in the door opening unexpectedly. A door opening while the vehicle is being driven can increasing the risk of injury or a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace all four door strikers and attaching bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Chevrolet Equinox is a versatile compact SUV with appealing features, but used-car buyers should perform careful checks before purchasing. Pay close attention during your test drive to engine performance, transmission shifting smoothness, and brake responsiveness. Reviewing maintenance records and ensuring all software updates have been applied will help secure a dependable vehicle.
