**2022 Chevrolet Equinox**

The 2022 Chevrolet Equinox is a compact SUV that seats up to five passengers and is offered with a choice of two engines and three transmission options. The base engine is a 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder that produces 170 horsepower and 203 lb-ft of torque, while the optional engine is a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder that produces 252 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. The 1.5-liter engine is available with a six-speed automatic transmission or a continuously variable automatic transmission (CVT), while the 2.0-liter engine is available with a nine-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2022 Chevrolet Equinox have reported a number of issues with their vehicles, including:

**Engine**

**1.5-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Intake valve deposits:** Carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, causing the engine to run rough and lose power. The solution is to clean the intake valves. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of power. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated cost to fix: $1,500 - $2,500

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **No issues found**

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Shuddering:** The transmission can shudder when shifting gears. The solution is to update the transmission software. Estimated cost to fix: Free under warranty
* **Slipping:** The transmission can slip gears, causing a loss of power. The solution is to replace the transmission. Estimated cost to fix: $3,000 - $5,000

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting issues:** The transmission may shift gears harshly or not shift gears at all. The solution is to replace the transmission control module. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000

**Nine-Speed Automatic**

* **No issues found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure:** The front struts can fail, causing the vehicle to bounce or sway. The solution is to replace the struts. Estimated cost to fix: $600 - $1,200

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start. The solution is to diagnose and repair the electrical system. Estimated cost to fix: Varies depending on the issue
* **Infotainment system issues:** The infotainment system may freeze, crash, or otherwise malfunction. The solution is to update the software or replace the infotainment system. Estimated cost to fix: Free under warranty

**Exterior**

* **Roof rack leaks:** The roof rack can leak water into the vehicle's interior. The solution is to seal the roof rack. Estimated cost to fix: Free under warranty

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle:** The dashboard may rattle or squeak when driving over bumps. The solution is to apply padding or adhesive to the dashboard. Estimated cost to fix: Varies depending on the method used
* **Seat cushion failure:** The seat cushion can collapse or sag, causing discomfort for passengers. The solution is to replace the seat cushion. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $600

**Overall Summary**

The 2022 Chevrolet Equinox has a number of issues that can affect its reliability and performance. The severity of the issues and the cost to fix them vary depending on the issue. Some issues, such as the intake valve deposits, can be relatively inexpensive to fix, while others, such as a transmission failure, can be very expensive to repair.
