# 2022 Chevrolet Equinox Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Chevrolet Equinox, prospective buyers should carefully review owner-reported complaints and powertrain specifications. While this compact SUV offers modern styling and practical daily drivability, some owners have flagged issues ranging from electrical glitches to fuel and transmission concerns. Keeping these areas in mind during a pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged I4

The 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 170 horsepower and 203 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard power plant for the Equinox. It delivers efficient performance for daily commuting, though buyers should watch for fuel system and stalling complaints.

**Fuel Pump Failure and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden engine stalling, loss of motive power while driving, or a rattling noise accompanied by a dashboard warning light due to fuel pump or module failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the defective fuel pump module or associated components as required.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, the 6-speed automatic transmission generally offers smooth gear changes. Buyers should verify proper transmission operation and inspect for any fluid leaks during a test drive.

**Transmission Fluid Loss and Shifting Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Catastrophic fluid loss from a broken line clamp or sudden failure to recognize gear positions upon startup, triggering multiple warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission lines, clamps, and fluid levels; repair or replace damaged components and address software faults if present.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Stability Control

Responsive steering is vital for handling; ensure the steering column and front end feel tight during evaluation.

**Steering Column Popping and Wheel Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Popping noises in the steering column during turns, accompanied by wheel vibration, grinding noises, or sudden jerking with stability control lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components, intermediate shaft, and stability control sensors for wear or binding.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and OnStar Module

Electrical accessories govern key convenience and communication features; checking module responsiveness is recommended.

**Infotainment Blank Screen and OnStar Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The radio screen goes completely blank, refusing to power on or respond, rendering the backup camera, USB charging ports, and OnStar services inoperable.
- **Solution:** Reset or replace the faulty OnStar/infotainment control module.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance peace of mind but can occasionally exhibit sensor or software anomalies.

**Inadvertent Electronic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic braking system unexpectedly engages and locks up while driving at normal speeds, causing a sudden jolt and rapid deceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the collision avoidance sensors and brake control module for fault codes or required recalibrations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V612000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The tires may have been manufactured with an insufficient amount of curative agent, which could cause tread separation. Tread separation can result in a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the tire DOT numbers, and replace any affected tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V359000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly welded seat frame may not adequately restrain the driver in a crash, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the driver's seat and replace the cushion frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V724000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A seat with a missing or loose bolt may not properly restrain the occupant in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the driver's seat and replace the seat cushion frame, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V013000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The fuel pump module may not consistently provide sufficient fuel to the engine, resulting in an engine stall. An engine stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V339000: Child Seat:Vehicle Latch Anchor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Equinox&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The four rear-seat lower child seat LATCH anchorage bars may have excessive powder coating, which could prevent a child seat from being installed with the anchorage bars. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." The inability to install a child seat.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the latch anchorage bars, and remove and replace the finish, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Chevrolet Equinox is a versatile compact SUV with comfortable road manners, but buyers should perform a thorough inspection prior to purchase. Pay close attention to fuel pump operation, electrical module performance, and transmission fluid lines to ensure long-term reliability.
