# 2024 Chevrolet Blazer EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Chevrolet Blazer EV enters the mid-size electric crossover market with striking styling and multiple powertrain configurations. As a first-model-year EV built on GM's Ultium platform, it introduces advanced tech and spacious accommodations. However, early adopters have reported various electronic, software, and driver-assistance issues that prospective used-car buyers should weigh carefully.

---

## Engine

### Ultium Single Motor FWD

This front-wheel-drive electric motor setup delivers around 220-240 horsepower, offering efficient everyday driving dynamics and a strong driving range for daily commuting.

### Ultium Dual Motor AWD

The dual-motor all-wheel-drive configuration produces approximately 288 horsepower, providing enhanced traction and brisk acceleration suitable for diverse weather conditions.

### Ultium Performance Motor AWD (SS)

Serving as the performance flagship, this high-output dual-motor setup generates up to 557 horsepower for rapid acceleration, though buyers should verify software health and charging capabilities on used models.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive

A single-speed direct-drive transmission delivers seamless power delivery without traditional gear shifts, though early software integration has occasionally triggered powertrain warning lights.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Cluster

The cabin is heavily reliant on large digital screens and software controls, making software stability paramount for seamless daily operation.

**Infotainment Screen and Display Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment screen or digital instrument cluster may go blank or randomly reboot while driving, temporarily disabling controls.
- **Solution:** Update the vehicle software to the latest manufacturer release or perform a system reset at a certified service center.

## Exterior

### Windshield Visibility

Exterior lighting and visibility components generally function well, though cabin trim reflections can occasionally hinder outward visibility in bright sunlight.

**Excessive Dashboard Glare**
- **Symptoms:** Glossy interior trim pieces and dashboard surfaces reflect brightly onto the windshield during sunny conditions, obstructing visibility.
- **Solution:** Utilize aftermarket dashboard mats or anti-reflective films to minimize windshield reflections.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver-assistance systems add modern safety features, but sensitivity calibration issues can occasionally result in false collision warnings or unexpected braking events.

**Unexpected Automatic Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may falsely detect obstacles or stationary objects, triggering sudden, unexpected automatic emergency braking while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer check for sensor misalignment, perform necessary calibration, and apply any available software updates.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V869000: Structure:Body:Door](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Blazer+EV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The door strikers may fracture, resulting in the door opening unexpectedly. A door opening while the vehicle is being driven can increasing the risk of injury or a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace all four door strikers and attaching bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V320000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Blazer+EV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rear drive unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V487000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Blazer+EV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A lower control arm fracture can cause loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the right front lower control arm as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V433000: Parking Brake](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Blazer+EV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The rear parking brake wiring harness may become damaged or corroded, resulting in unintended activation of the parking brake or loss of the parking brake function. Unintended activation of the parking brake while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the harness and, if necessary, reroute the harness and install anti-abrasive tape. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V031000: Parking Brake](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Blazer+EV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The rear parking brake wiring harness may become damaged or corroded, resulting in unintended activation of the parking brake or loss of the parking brake function. Unintended activation of the parking brake while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the chassis harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Chevrolet Blazer EV brings appealing styling and strong electric performance to the used market, but as a first-year EV model, it exhibits characteristic early adopter software and sensor quirks. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test all driver-assistance features, infotainment displays, and charging capabilities. Verifying that all open software updates and recalls have been completed by a certified dealership will help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.
