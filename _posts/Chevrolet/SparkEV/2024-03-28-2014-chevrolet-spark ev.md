**Introduction:**

The 2014 Chevrolet Spark EV is a fully electric city car that offers impressive efficiency and a peppy driving experience. It features a single electric motor that delivers 100 horsepower and 428 pound-feet of torque through a fixed-ratio gearbox. The Spark EV is praised for its agility and affordability, but some owners have encountered various issues with different components of the vehicle.

## **Electric Motor**
* **None Found**

## **Exterior**
* **Paint Bubbling**
Description/Symptoms: The paint on the roof, hood, and trunk lid can develop bubbles or blisters.
Solution: Replacing the affected body panels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Door Handle Corrosion**
Description/Symptoms: The door handles can corrode prematurely, especially in humid climates.
Solution: Replacing the corroded door handles.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**
* **Infotainment System Lag**
Description/Symptoms: The touchscreen infotainment system can experience lag and delayed response.
Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+ depending on the fix required.
Recalls: None Found

* **Airbag Control Module Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The airbag control module may malfunction, causing the airbags to not deploy in the event of an accident.
Solution: Replacing the airbag control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: 14V559000

## **Electrical**
* **Battery Issues**
Description/Symptoms: The traction battery can lose capacity over time, reducing the vehicle's range and performance.
Solution: Replacing the traction battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
Recalls: 14V545000

* **Charging Port Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The charging port can fail to connect properly or may not charge the battery efficiently.
Solution: Repairing or replacing the charging port.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Wiring Harness Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The electrical wiring harness can become damaged or loose, causing electrical problems throughout the vehicle.
Solution: Inspecting and repairing or replacing the damaged wiring harness.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ depending on the extent of damage.
Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

The 2014 Chevrolet Spark EV has experienced some issues that have affected owners' satisfaction. While the severity and cost of repairs vary depending on the issue, it's important to consider these potential problems when considering purchasing this vehicle. The most serious issues include battery problems, airbag control module malfunctions, and electrical wiring harness issues, which can be expensive and impact the safety and reliability of the car. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
