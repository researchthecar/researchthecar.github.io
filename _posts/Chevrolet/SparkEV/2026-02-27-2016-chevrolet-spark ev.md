# 2016 Chevrolet Spark EV

### Overview
The 2016 Chevrolet Spark EV is a compact electric vehicle known for its urban maneuverability and efficiency. As an all-electric model, it bypasses many traditional internal combustion engine concerns, focusing instead on battery and electric motor performance. Reliability data for the Spark EV generally indicates a robust powertrain, with most reported issues stemming from the electrical system or specific component failures rather than widespread design flaws. Owners often praise its low running costs and ease of use, though like any vehicle, it has had isolated incidents that warrant attention for potential buyers.

## Electrical

### Battery Pack
**Reduced Range/Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable decrease in driving range over time, slower charging speeds.
- **Solution:** Battery diagnostics and potential replacement under warranty or out-of-pocket.
- **Cost:** $5,000 - $15,000+

**Charging System Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to charge the vehicle, error messages related to charging port or onboard charger.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of charging port, onboard charger, or related wiring.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Electrical system malfunctions, dashboard warning lights, difficulty starting accessory systems.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Chevrolet Spark EV demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its electric powertrain. The primary areas of concern revolve around the longevity and performance of the high-voltage battery pack, which can experience degradation over time, and occasional issues with the charging system or the 12V auxiliary battery. Interior electronics, specifically the infotainment system, may also present minor software-related problems. However, these are not typically widespread failures and often manageable with software updates or component replacement. For an electric vehicle of its era, the Spark EV offers a generally dependable experience, with most potential issues being electrical in nature rather than mechanical.
