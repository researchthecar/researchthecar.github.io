**Introduction:**

The 2005 Chevrolet T7500 is a heavy-duty commercial truck offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. It's known for its powerful engines, rugged construction, and towing capabilities. However, like any vehicle, it has some reported issues.

**Engine**

**6.6L Duramax V8**
* **Injector Failure:** Premature failure of the fuel injectors, leading to poor engine performance, loss of power, and decreased fuel efficiency.
* Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illumination, rough idling, black smoke from exhaust, decreased fuel economy.
* Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Allison 1000 5-Speed Automatic**
* **Torquesense Converter Issues:** Failure of the torquesense converter, resulting in loss of torque, slipping, or shuddering during gear changes.
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, lack of power, whining or grinding noise during gear changes.
* Solution: Replace or repair the torquesense converter.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Leaf Spring Failures:** Premature failure of the leaf springs, leading to a loss of ride quality, handling issues, and potential safety hazards.
* Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, sagging rear end, reduced load capacity.
* Solution: Replace the faulty leaf springs.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** Excessive battery drain caused by faulty electrical components, such as the alternator, starter, or wiring harness.
* Description/Symptoms: Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the truck.
* Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty electrical component.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Mirror Vibration:** Excessive vibration of the side mirrors, causing difficulty in maintaining a clear view.
* Description/Symptoms: Mirrors vibrating excessively, especially at highway speeds.
* Solution: Replace mirror brackets or adjust mirror alignment.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Failure of the air conditioning or heating system, resulting in uncomfortable cabin temperatures.
* Description/Symptoms: Blowing hot air instead of cold, inadequate heating, fan not working.
* Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty component, such as the compressor, condenser, or heater core.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $750+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2005 Chevrolet T7500 is a capable work truck, but it has some reported issues with fuel injectors, transmission torquesense converters, leaf springs, and electrical components. The severity of the issues can vary, but some can be costly to repair. Overall, the T7500 is a reliable truck for its intended purpose, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before investing.
