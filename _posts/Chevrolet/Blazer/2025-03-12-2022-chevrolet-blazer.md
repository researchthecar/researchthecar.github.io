The 2022 Chevrolet Blazer is a mid-size SUV that offers a blend of sporty styling and practicality. Available engine options include a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.6L V6 engine that generates 308 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. People often praise the Blazer for its sharp looks, comfortable ride, and user-friendly infotainment system. Common complaints revolve around its relatively small cargo space for its class, and the fuel economy of the V6 engine.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

*   **Engine Misfires**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Check engine light illuminated, rough idling, and noticeable lack of power. The misfire can be caused by faulty spark plugs or ignition coils.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty spark plugs and/or ignition coils.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil level warning light comes on frequently. Visible blue smoke from the exhaust, especially on startup.
    * Solution: Perform an engine oil consumption test to determine the rate of oil usage. May require piston ring replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

*   **Timing Chain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, rattling noise from the engine, especially at startup.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**9-Speed Automatic (Both Engine Options)**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Noticeable clunking or jerking during gear changes, hesitation when accelerating, and potential for transmission slipping.
    *   Solution: Software update to the transmission control module (TCM). If the problem persists, valve body replacement might be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update $100 - $200, valve body replacement $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Visible leak underneath the vehicle, especially near the transmission. Low transmission fluid level.
    *   Solution: Replacing the transmission pan gasket or sealing the transmission cooler lines.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The touchscreen freezes, becomes unresponsive, or restarts unexpectedly. Bluetooth connectivity issues.
    *   Solution: Software update for the infotainment system. Replacement of the infotainment unit may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update - often free at the dealership. Infotainment unit replacement $500 - $1200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The vehicle battery drains overnight, preventing starting.
    *   Solution: Diagnosing parasitic draw and replacing any faulty modules that are causing the drain.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+ (depending on the source of the drain)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rear Liftgate Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The power liftgate fails to open or close properly, or makes unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Replace the liftgate struts or the liftgate motor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Summary:**

The 2022 Chevrolet Blazer has several potential issues ranging from engine and transmission problems to electrical glitches. Engine issues such as excessive oil consumption and timing chain problems can be quite costly to repair. Transmission issues can also be costly. Electrical problems, such as infotainment system failures, can be a major source of frustration and may require expensive component replacements. The frequency and severity of these issues vary, but potential buyers should be aware of these common complaints and ensure a thorough inspection of the vehicle before purchase.
