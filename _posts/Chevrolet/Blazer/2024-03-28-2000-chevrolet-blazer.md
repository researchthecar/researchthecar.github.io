**Introduction:**

The 2000 Chevrolet Blazer is a mid-size SUV available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It offers a spacious interior, a powerful engine lineup, and a rugged exterior. However, like many vehicles, it has its fair share of common issues.

**Engine**

**4.3L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: 00V092000
* **Head gasket failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+

**5.7L V8**

* **Valve lifter failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tapping or clicking noise from engine
    * Solution: Replace valve lifters
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* **Throttle body failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, difficulty accelerating
    * Solution: Replace throttle body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Transmission**

**4L60E 4-speed automatic transmission**

* **Torque converter shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration when shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Transmission slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * Recalls: 03V174000

**Suspension**

* **Ball joint failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noise when turning or braking, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per side
* **Strut failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil, bouncing or rough ride
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+ per pair

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Starter failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting engine, grinding noise when starting
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Rust and corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on body panels, bubbling paint
    * Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on severity

**Interior**

* **Cracked dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or splits in dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **Broken window regulators:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or inability to open or close windows
    * Solution: Repair or replace window regulators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per window

**Summary**

Overall, the 2000 Chevrolet Blazer is a capable SUV with a strong engine lineup and a spacious interior. However, it is not without its issues. Engine and transmission problems can be costly to fix, and owners should be prepared for potential expenses. Other issues, such as rust and cracked dashboards, while less severe, can still detract from the overall driving experience.
