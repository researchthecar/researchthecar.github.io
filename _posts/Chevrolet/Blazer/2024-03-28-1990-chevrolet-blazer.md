## 1990 Chevrolet Blazer

The 1990 Chevrolet Blazer was a popular SUV that offered a combination of versatility, off-road capability, and comfort. It was powered by a variety of engine options, including a 4.3L V6, a 5.7L V8, and a 6.2L V8. Transmission options included a 4-speed automatic or a 5-speed manual.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**
