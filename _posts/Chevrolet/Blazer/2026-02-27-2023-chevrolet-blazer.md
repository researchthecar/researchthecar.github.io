# 2023 Chevrolet Blazer Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Chevrolet Blazer offers sharp styling, engaging handling, and a comfortable cabin, making it an appealing choice in the midsize crossover market. However, prospective buyers should review owner complaints regarding powertrain behavior and advanced driver assistance systems before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The standard 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing a solid balance of everyday responsiveness and efficiency for daily commuting.

**Engine Stalling and Cooling Issues**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalling while driving, accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes like P0128 and continuous running of the cooling fan after shutdown.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose the cooling system, thermostat, and engine management sensors.

### 3.6L V6

The available 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers 308 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, offering stronger passing power and a higher towing capacity well-suited for highway driving.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The nine-speed automatic transmission pairs with both engine options across front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations, aiming to optimize fuel economy and smooth power delivery.

**Transmission Shudder and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice intermittent acceleration hesitation, transmission shuddering during gear changes, or failure to shift out of first gear.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership for diagnostic evaluation, potential software updates, or component replacements such as the valve body.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Parking Brake

Braking components provide confident stopping power under normal conditions, though electronic parking brake anomalies have been reported.

**Parking Brake Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic rear parking brake motor can occasionally lock the rear wheels, preventing the vehicle from moving until restarted or serviced.
- **Solution:** Have the parking brake actuator motor and control module inspected and repaired or replaced by a qualified technician.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Cabin electronics manage vital driver information and entertainment features, though occasional software glitches can affect screen reliability.

**Instrument Cluster Screen Blanking**
- **Symptoms:** The digital odometer and speedometer display screen may intermittently crash and go completely blank while driving.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates for the infotainment and instrument cluster modules or consult a dealer for hardware diagnostics.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Driver assistance features add modern security layers but can occasionally misinterpret road environments.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance and automatic emergency braking systems may activate abruptly without an actual obstruction present.
- **Solution:** Inspect sensor alignments, clear debris from camera and radar zones, and check for applicable manufacturer service bulletins.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V042000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Blazer&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left-side half-shaft assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V172000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Blazer&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the sun gears, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Chevrolet Blazer is a stylish and capable midsize SUV, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough test drive to check for transmission smoothness, responsive electronics, and proper operation of driver assistance features. Verifying service history and ensuring all open software updates or recalls have been addressed will help secure a more dependable ownership experience.
