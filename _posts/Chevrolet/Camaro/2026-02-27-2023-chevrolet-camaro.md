# 2023 Chevrolet Camaro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Chevrolet Camaro, buyers are looking at the final stretch of this iconic muscle car's modern production run. It offers aggressive styling, sharp handling, and a range of potent engine options from turbocharged fours to roaring V8s. While overall owner satisfaction is high, a handful of owner-reported concerns regarding powertrain quirks, electrical glitches, and safety systems have surfaced in early reliability tracking.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The entry-level 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 produces 275 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers a lighter front end for nimble handling, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history.

### 3.6L V6

The naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 generates 335 horsepower and 284 lb-ft of torque. It serves as a balanced middle-ground option combining everyday drivability with traditional sports car performance.

### 6.2L V8

The potent 6.2-liter V8 punches out 455 horsepower and 455 lb-ft of torque in standard SS trim, delivering thrilling straight-line performance alongside a muscular exhaust note.

**Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported catastrophic engine failure linked to internal bearing issues, requiring major repairs or complete engine replacement.
- **Solution:** Consult a certified dealer for diagnostic checks and potential warranty replacement if bearing failure symptoms occur.

**Intermittent Slow Crank**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may exhibit a slow crank or fail to start on the first attempt after being driven and turned off for 20 to 30 minutes.
- **Solution:** Inspect starting system components, starter heat shielding, and battery health, as owner forums note similar intermittent thermal-soak concerns.

### 6.2L Supercharged V8

At the top of the lineup, the supercharged LT4 V8 in the ZL1 churns out an incredible 650 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque, built for serious track capability and high-speed performance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual gearbox features active rev matching and offers an engaging, hands-on driving experience across various trims.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic is paired with specific engine configurations to deliver quick shifts, though prospective buyers should test-drive thoroughly for smooth engagement.

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

Available on V8 models, the 10-speed automatic provides rapid gear changes and optimized power delivery for both street and track use.

## Exterior

### Trunk Release

Electrical accessories and comfort features generally function well, though isolated component failures can occasionally occur.

**Electronic Trunk Release Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic trunk release fails to operate, preventing the trunk from opening via either the key fob or the interior push button.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership inspect the latch mechanism and wiring harness, as faulty actuators may require replacement.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Advanced safety features and restraint systems are designed to protect occupants, but owners have reported critical anomalies involving deployment timing.

**Inadvertent Roof Rail Air Bag Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Passenger and driver side roof rail airbags may deploy unexpectedly while simply driving around a corner without any impact or collision.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to an authorized dealer to inspect the airbag control module and sensors for faults.

**Failure to Deploy in Collisions**
- **Symptoms:** Frontal airbags may fail to deploy during a front-end collision, accompanied by dashboard warning messages or app notifications indicating an airbag system fault.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified service center is required to identify sensor or module faults within the supplemental restraint system.

The 2023 Chevrolet Camaro remains a thrilling choice for driving enthusiasts seeking classic muscle car performance mixed with modern cockpit tech.
