**Introduction:**

The 1999 Chevrolet Venture is a minivan offered with various engine and transmission options, including a 3.4L V6 with a 4-speed automatic transmission, a 3.8L V6 with a 4-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.4L V6 with a 5-speed manual transmission. The Venture was praised for its spacious interior, comfortable seats, and versatile cargo space. However, it has been known to experience some issues over time.

## **Engine**
### **3.4L V6**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, poor performance
  - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Throttle Body Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, idling issues, decreased fuel efficiency
  - Solution: Replace throttle body
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350
  - Recalls: 00V189000

### **3.8L V6**
- **Head Gasket Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
  - Solution: Replace head gaskets
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800
  - Recalls: 03V022001

## **Transmission**
### **4-Speed Automatic**
- **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Visible fluid leaks, slipping gears, delayed shifting
  - Solution: Identify and seal leak source, replace lost fluid
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, slipping gears, delayed engagement
  - Solution: Replace faulty shift solenoid
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found

### **5-Speed Manual**
- **Clutch Disc Wear:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, slipping gears, grinding noises
  - Solution: Replace clutch disc and pressure plate
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**
- **Front Sway Bar Link Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises when turning, loose handling
  - Solution: Replace sway bar link
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**
- **Ignition Coil Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, engine misfires, decreased fuel efficiency
  - Solution: Replace ignition coil
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Battery Drain:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical drain, replace battery if necessary
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**
- **Power Side Mirror Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Inoperable side mirror, clicking noises
  - Solution: Replace side mirror motor or switch
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**
- **Dashboard Rattling:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loose dashboard panels, rattling noises when driving over bumps
  - Solution: Secure loose panels with clips or adhesives
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

The 1999 Chevrolet Venture experiences a range of issues that vary in severity and cost to fix. Some common concerns include engine and transmission problems, electrical issues, and exterior and interior components failure. While some issues may be minor and relatively inexpensive to resolve, others can be more complex and costly. However, despite these issues, the Venture remains a popular choice for drivers seeking a spacious and versatile minivan.
