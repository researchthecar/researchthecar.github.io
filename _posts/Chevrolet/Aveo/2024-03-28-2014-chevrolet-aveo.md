**Introduction:**

The 2014 Chevrolet Aveo, a subcompact sedan, is powered by a 1.6L inline-4 ECOTEC engine that produces 108 horsepower and 105 lb-ft of torque. Paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission, the Aveo offers a balance of fuel efficiency and responsiveness. Owners appreciate the vehicle's affordability, compact size, and fuel efficiency, while common complaints include limited interior space and a lack of modern features.

**Engine**

* **1.6L ECOTEC**
    * **Head Gasket Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, hesitation, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder during acceleration
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $1800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts:**
    * **Premature Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, poor handling
        * Solution: Replace front struts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery:**
    * **Premature Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, electrical component malfunctions
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlights:**
    * **Foggy Lenses:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced visibility due to foggy or yellowed lenses
        * Solution: Polish or replace headlights
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Locks:**
    * **Malfunction:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, doors opening while driving
        * Solution: Repair or replace door lock assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard:**
    * **Rattling:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Annoying rattling noises from dashboard
        * Solution: Identify and secure loose components
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2014 Chevrolet Aveo is a reliable and affordable subcompact car with a few common issues. Engine head gasket failures and intake manifold problems are the most serious issues to watch out for, while electrical and interior issues are generally minor. The cost of repairs varies widely, but overall, the Aveo is a budget-friendly vehicle with manageable maintenance costs.
