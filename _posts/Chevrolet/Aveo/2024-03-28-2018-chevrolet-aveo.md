**Introduction:**

The 2018 Chevrolet Aveo is a subcompact sedan that is no longer in production. It was available with a 1.5-liter four-cylinder engine rated at 107 horsepower and 104 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a six-speed manual or a six-speed automatic transmission. The Aveo received praise for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and practicality, but was criticized for its lack of power and uninspired interior.

**Sun Visor Clip Breakage**

Description/Symptoms: Sun visor clips break off, causing the visor to fall down
Solution: Visor clip replacement or visor assembly replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100

**Summary:**

The 2018 Chevrolet Aveo has a relatively low number of reported issues, with no major mechanical problems. The most common issues are minor interior issues such as seat cracking and sun visor clip breakage. Overall, the Aveo is a reliable and affordable vehicle for its class.
