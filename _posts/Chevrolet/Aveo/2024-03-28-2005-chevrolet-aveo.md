The 2005 Chevrolet Aveo is a subcompact car available with a 1.6L DOHC I4 engine paired with either a 5-speed manual transmission or 4-speed automatic transmission. It offers up to 103 horsepower and 107 lb-ft of torque. The Aveo is praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and nimble handling. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power, noisy cabin, and cramped interior.

**Engine**

* **1.6L DOHC I4**
  * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, performance issues
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Timing Chain Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking sound from engine
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
  * **Shifter Cable Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, popping out of gear
    * Solution: Replace shifter cable bushing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Clutch Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic**
  * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, shifting issues
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid pan gasket or lines
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, no engagement in certain gears
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace front suspension struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Springs Sagging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity
    * Solution: Replace rear suspension springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic drain, replace battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights
    * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs or wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Plastic Trim Fading**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature fading of exterior plastic trim
    * Solution: Use a plastic protectant or replace trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Hinge Squeaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise when opening or closing doors
    * Solution: Lubricate door hinges
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $50
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Fabric Tearing**
    * Description/Symptoms: Torn seat fabric, especially on side bolsters
    * Solution: Repair or replace seat fabric
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Creaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling sound from dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten loose components or install anti-rattle material
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2005 Chevrolet Aveo is a budget-friendly subcompact car with decent reliability. However, it is prone to certain issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure, timing chain noise, and transmission fluid leaks. These issues can be costly to repair, especially if they are not addressed promptly. The Aveo's relatively low purchase price may outweigh its maintenance expenses for some buyers, but it is important to be aware of these potential problems before making a decision.
