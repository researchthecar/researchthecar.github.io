**Introduction to the 2023 Chevrolet Tracker**

The 2023 Chevrolet Tracker is a compact SUV that comes standard with a 1.5L inline-4 engine producing 138 horsepower and 163 lb-ft of torque, mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission. It's also available with a 1.3L turbocharged 3-cylinder engine generating 155 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque, paired with a CVT. The Tracker has received praise for its affordability, spacious interior, and fuel efficiency. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power and somewhat cramped rear seat.

**Engine**

**1.5L Inline-4 Engine with 6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Low Power Output**
  * Description/Symptoms: Inability to accelerate as expected, especially at highway speeds or with hills
  * Solution: Engine software updates to improve performance
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+
  * Recalls: None Found

**1.3L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder Engine with CVT**

* **Turbo Lag**
  * Description/Symptoms: Delayed response when accelerating, resulting in hesitation or lack of power
  * Solution: Throttle body replacement or software update
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Hesitation and Jerking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts roughly or hesitates during gear changes
  * Solution: Transmission fluid flush or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**

* **Premature Wear**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, slipping gears, or humming noises
  * Solution: CVT replacement or rebuild
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Electrical System Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, inoperable accessories, or check engine light illumination
  * Solution: Battery replacement, alternator repair, or wiring harness inspection
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V199000

**Exterior**

**Door Panel Gap**
  * Description/Symptoms: Uneven or excessive gaps between door panels and body
  * Solution: Door panel adjustment or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattle**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noises coming from the dashboard, especially over uneven roads
  * Solution: Dashboard panel adjustment or replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Chevrolet Tracker is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. While some issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be costly, such as CVT problems. However, most of the issues are not widespread and can be addressed under warranty or with regular maintenance.
