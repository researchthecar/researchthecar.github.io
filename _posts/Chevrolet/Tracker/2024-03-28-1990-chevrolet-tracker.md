## 1990 Chevrolet Tracker

The 1990 Chevrolet Tracker is a compact SUV that was available with a 1.6-liter inline-four engine and a 5-speed manual transmission. It was praised for its affordability, versatility, and off-road capabilities. However, it was also criticized for its lack of power and cramped interior.

### Engine

**1.6L Inline-Four**

**Head Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Blown head gasket, coolant leaks, overheating
* Solution: Replace head gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, vacuum leaks, rough idle
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
* Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**5-Speed Manual**

**Synchronizer Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
* Solution: Replace synchronizers
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission Mount Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission vibration, noise, and leaks
* Solution: Replace transmission mount
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

**Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
* Description/Symptoms: Clunking or squeaking noises when turning or driving over bumps
* Solution: Replace control arm bushings
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: None Found

**Rear Leaf Spring Breakage**
* Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven ride height
* Solution: Replace leaf springs
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
* Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

**Alternator Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical problems
* Solution: Replace alternator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
* Recalls: None Found

**Starter Motor Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start
* Solution: Replace starter motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

**Rust**
* Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, especially around the wheel wells and undercarriage
* Solution: Repair or replace rusty panels
* Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of the damage
* Recalls: None Found

### Interior

**Dashboard Cracking**
* Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard
* Solution: Replace dashboard
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: None Found

**HVAC Fan Motor Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: No airflow from vents
* Solution: Replace fan motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* Recalls: None Found

## Summary

Overall, the 1990 Chevrolet Tracker is a reliable and affordable vehicle. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems include head gasket failure, intake manifold gasket leaks, and suspension component wear. These issues can be costly to repair, but they are not typically severe and can be avoided with regular maintenance.
