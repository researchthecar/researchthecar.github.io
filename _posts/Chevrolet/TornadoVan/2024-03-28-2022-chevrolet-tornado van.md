The 2022 Chevrolet Tornado Van is a versatile commercial vehicle offered in multiple configurations to meet various business needs. The van is available with a range of engine and transmission options, including gasoline and diesel powertrains. The interior provides ample cargo space and various amenities for driver comfort. While the Tornado Van is generally praised for its spaciousness and functionality, it has been reported to have some issues in certain areas.

**Engine**

* **2.8L Duramax Turbo-Diesel Engine**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive smoke, reduced power, turbocharger whistling noises
        * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 20V727000

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during gear shifts
        * Solution: Transmission fluid change, transmission control module update, or transmission rebuild
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sudden drop in ride height, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Leaf spring replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light illuminated, difficulty starting the vehicle
        * Solution: Alternator replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Door Handle Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening the rear doors
        * Solution: Door handle replacement or adjustment
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Belt Buckle Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt buckle does not hold or release properly
        * Solution: Seat belt buckle replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V344000

**Summary**

While the 2022 Chevrolet Tornado Van offers versatility and spaciousness, it has experienced a few issues related to its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical components, and interior features. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to more significant repairs that may affect the vehicle's performance and safety. The estimated costs to fix these issues vary depending on the specific problem and the complexity of the repair. It is recommended that owners and prospective buyers research these issues and be aware of potential costs when considering purchasing or maintaining a 2022 Tornado Van.
