# 2022 Chevrolet Corvette Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Chevrolet Corvette delivers world-class supercar performance at an accessible price point, anchored by its mid-engine layout. While owners generally praise its thrilling dynamics and everyday usability, early-model electronic glitches and mechanical quirks have surfaced in owner reports. Prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, paying close attention to convertible top mechanisms and transmission behavior.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L LT2 V8

The 6.2-liter naturally aspirated LT2 V8 engine produces 490 to 495 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration. Overall, the engine mechanicals are robust, though occasional electronic sensor and module glitches have been reported by owners.

**Powertrain and Control Module Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden hesitation, jerking, or warning lights such as 'Rear Axel System Off' and illuminated check engine lights.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a qualified dealer to diagnose powertrain control modules, update software, or inspect related sensors.

**Body Control Module (BCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** An erratic BCM can cause instrument gauges like oil pressure to drop to zero and result in odometer mileage discrepancies.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer inspect the BCM and electrical connections, and replace or reprogram the module if necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes across all trims. While most units perform well, rare instances of early mechanical failure have been documented.

**Premature Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have experienced sudden transmission failure requiring complete unit replacement at very low mileage.
- **Solution:** Consult a Chevrolet dealer for diagnostic evaluation and potential replacement under factory warranty.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides immense stopping power, though heavy street or track use can occasionally induce rotor hot spots and vibration.

**Brake Rotor Pulsation and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Pulsating and vibrating brake pedal feedback, particularly noticeable when rotors and pads reach high operating temperatures.
- **Solution:** Have the brake pads and rotors inspected, resurfaced, or replaced as needed.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

The electrical architecture is complex, supporting numerous comfort and performance systems, but can occasionally suffer from parasitic drains or instrument panel blackouts.

**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained battery drain leading to a no-start condition and error codes, occasionally requiring the negative terminal to be disconnected to reset.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the vehicle for parasitic electrical draws and update software or replace faulty control modules.

## Exterior

### Convertible Hardtop Mechanism

The power-retractable hardtop adds open-air versatility to the Corvette, but owners should carefully verify proper operation of the cables and motors.

**Retractable Top Cable and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Cables or wires snapping and retractable headrests breaking, preventing the folding hardtop from opening or closing properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace broken retaining cables, wires, or retractor mechanisms through a dealership service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V091000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Corvette&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An unexpected loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left or right rear half-shaft assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Chevrolet Corvette remains an incredible performance bargain, but used-car buyers should perform a detailed inspection before purchasing. Pay close attention to the functionality of the retractable hardtop mechanism, verify that all software updates and electronic modules are operating without error, and check service records for any past powertrain or transmission work.
