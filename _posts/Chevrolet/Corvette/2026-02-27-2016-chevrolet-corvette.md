# 2016 Chevrolet Corvette Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Chevrolet Corvette delivers world-class sports car performance, pairing aggressive styling with impressive V8 power. However, prospective buyers should review common owner-reported issues ranging from drivetrain shudders to recurring wheel cracks. Understanding these potential problem areas can help ensure you select a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 6.2L LT1 V8

The standard 6.2-liter LT1 V8 engine produces 455 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, providing blistering acceleration and robust everyday reliability.

**Fuel Odor and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report smelling fuel around the vehicle or noticing leaks near the fuel tank or pump module flange area.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel pump module and lines, and replace damaged or leaking components.

### 6.2L LT4 Supercharged V8

Found in the high-performance Z06 trim, the supercharged LT4 V8 pumps out an immense 650 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque for track-ready capability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 7-speed manual features Active Rev Match for seamless downshifts, offering an engaging driving experience with generally sturdy mechanical reliability.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed paddle-shift automatic provides quick gear changes, though certain units are prone to torque converter shudder issues that require specialized service.

**Transmission Shudder and Harsh Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a shudder or vibration resembling driving over rumble strips, especially at highway speeds, alongside erratic shifting or hesitation.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a certified technician to perform recommended transmission fluid flushes or service bulletins.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

The Corvette's high-performance suspension and low-profile tires deliver exceptional cornering grip but leave factory wheels vulnerable to cracking over harsh impacts.

**Inner Wheel Bead Cracks**
- **Symptoms:** Rear wheel rims develop hidden cracks on the inner bead lip, leading to slow air leaks and complete loss of tire pressure.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for structural fractures and replace damaged rims as necessary.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

The steering system provides sharp and direct feedback, though intermittent electronic assist failures have been reported by some owners.

**Loss of Power Steering Assist**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers suddenly lose electric power steering assist while driving, accompanied by dashboard warnings, making the steering extremely heavy until the ignition is cycled.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic power steering module and wiring harnesses for faults, replacing failing components as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Corvette&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V437000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Corvette&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** While being previously remedied for recall 16V-651, the affected vehicles may not have received the complete software update necessary to remedy the recall condition. Without the update, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Chevrolet Corvette is an exhilarating and generally capable sports car, but buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to the 8-speed automatic for transmission shudders, inspect the wheels carefully for hidden inner-rim cracks, and verify that the fuel system and power steering operate without fault.
