**2017 Chevrolet Corvette**

The 2017 Chevrolet Corvette is a high-performance sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience. It comes standard with a 6.2-liter V8 engine that produces 455 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 6.2-liter supercharged V8 that produces 650 horsepower and 650 lb-ft of torque, and a 7.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 that produces 505 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. The Corvette is available with a 7-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the Corvette praise its powerful engine, sharp handling, and stylish design. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle, including:

**Engine**

* **LT1 6.2L V8 Engine**
    * **Valve Spring Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and failure of the valve springs can cause a loss of engine power and performance, resulting in a rough idle and misfiring.
        * Solution: Replacement of the affected valve springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **LT4 6.2L Supercharged V8 Engine**
    * **Fuel Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning fuel pumps can lead to insufficient fuel delivery, resulting in engine stalling, power loss, and difficulty starting.
        * Solution: Replacement of the faulty fuel pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Water Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty water pumps can result in coolant leaks and overheating issues, potentially leading to engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacement of the defective water pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear or failure of the clutch can cause difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or chatter.
        * Solution: Replacement of the affected clutch components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * **Synchro Gear Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged synchro gears can result in difficulty engaging specific gears, grinding noises, or gear slippage.
        * Solution: Replacement of the faulty synchro gears.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty shift solenoids can cause delayed or erratic gear shifts, as well as transmission slipping or hesitation.
        * Solution: Replacement of the affected shift solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaks in the transmission fluid lines or seals can lead to fluid loss and transmission slippage or failure.
        * Solution: Repair or replacement of the damaged components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A faulty alternator can result in inadequate charging of the vehicle's battery, leading to electrical system malfunctions and difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the defective alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery drain can occur due to faulty electrical components or parasitic power draw, resulting in a weak or dead battery.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the underlying electrical issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight assemblies can lead to fogging or dimming of the headlights, affecting visibility.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Paint Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature peeling or flaking of the paint can occur on certain areas of the vehicle, affecting the overall appearance.
    * Solution: Repainting of the affected panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+ per panel
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or splits in the dashboard material can develop over time, affecting the aesthetics and potentially causing rattles or squeaks.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected dashboard panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Issues with the heating, ventilation, and air conditioning system can include faulty controls, blower motor problems, or refrigerant leaks.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the specific HVAC component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Chevrolet Corvette is a high-performance sports car with exceptional driving dynamics. However, some owners have experienced various issues, including engine and transmission problems, electrical malfunctions, and interior concerns. While some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and require costly repairs. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a 2017 Corvette.
