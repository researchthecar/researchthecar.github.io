**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Issue:** Worn or failed ball joints
Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling noise when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces, difficulty steering.
Solution: Replace ball joints.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Suspension Issue:** Leaking or blown shock absorbers
Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, excessive bouncing, unstable handling, uneven tire wear.
Solution: Replace shock absorbers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Issue:** Failed alternator
Description/Symptoms: Dimming or flickering lights, electrical system malfunctioning, battery not charging.
Solution: Replace alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Issue:** Weak or dead battery
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dim or flickering lights, electrical system failure.
Solution: Replace battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Issue:** Burned-out headlights
Description/Symptoms: Inoperable headlights, reduced visibility at night.
Solution: Replace headlight bulbs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $20 - $50+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issue:** Broken or malfunctioning climate control
Description/Symptoms: Inoperable air conditioning, heating, or ventilation system.
Solution: Replace climate control unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Issue:** Failed power window motor
Description/Symptoms: Inoperable power windows.
Solution: Replace power window motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1994 Chevrolet C3500HD is a reliable and capable heavy-duty pickup truck. Some common issues to look out for include wear and tear on suspension components, potential alternator and battery failures, and minor electrical and interior issues. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, and regular maintenance and repairs can help keep the vehicle in good condition.
