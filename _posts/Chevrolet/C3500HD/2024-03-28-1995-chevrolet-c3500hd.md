**Introduction**

The 1995 Chevrolet C3500HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck. It is powered by a 7.4L Vortec V8 engine that produces 290 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, and it is paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The C3500HD has a payload capacity of 6,190 lbs and a towing capacity of 11,900 lbs.

People like the C3500HD for its strong engine, spacious interior, and comfortable ride. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

**Engine**

* **7.4L Vortec V8 Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the intake manifold gasket can cause coolant and oil to leak, leading to overheating and engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacing the intake manifold gasket costs around $500 - $1,000+.
    * **Valve Stem Seal Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the valve stem seals can cause the engine to smoke and consume excessive oil.
        * Solution: Replacing the valve stem seals costs around $400 - $800+.
    * **Cracked Cylinder Head**
        * Description/Symptoms: A cracked cylinder head can cause coolant and oil to mix, leading to overheating and engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacing the cylinder head costs around $2,000 - $3,000+.
* **Recalls**
    * None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: The torque converter can shudder when the transmission shifts gears.
        * Solution: Replacing the torque converter costs around $1,000 - $1,500+.
    * **Failed Transmission Rebuild**
        * Description/Symptoms: A transmission rebuild that was not done correctly can cause the transmission to slip or fail completely.
        * Solution: Replacing the transmission costs around $2,500 - $4,000+.
    * **Broken Input Shaft**
        * Description/Symptoms: A broken input shaft can prevent the transmission from shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replacing the input shaft costs around $1,500 - $2,000+.
* **Recalls**
    * None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Worn Ball Joints**
        * Description/Symptoms: Worn ball joints can cause the front end of the vehicle to shake or wander.
        * Solution: Replacing the ball joints costs around $300 - $500+.
    * **Failed Shocks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failed shocks can cause the vehicle to ride rough and handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replacing the shocks costs around $200 - $400+.
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Leaking Shock Absorbers**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking shock absorbers can cause the rear of the vehicle to sag and handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replacing the shock absorbers costs around $200 - $400+.
    * **Broken Leaf Springs**
        * Description/Symptoms: Broken leaf springs can cause the rear of the vehicle to sag and handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replacing the leaf springs costs around $500 - $1,000+.
* **Recalls**
    * None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A failed alternator can prevent the vehicle from starting or can cause the battery to drain.
    * Solution: Replacing the alternator costs around $300 - $500+.
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A failed starter can prevent the vehicle from starting.
    * Solution: Replacing the starter costs around $200 - $400+.
* **Battery Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: A failed battery can prevent the vehicle from starting.
    * Solution: Replacing the battery costs around $100 - $200+.
* **Recalls**
    * None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: The body of the C3500HD is prone to rusting, especially in areas where salt is used to melt ice on the roads.
    * Solution: Preventing rust requires regular cleaning and waxing, and repairing existing rust can be expensive.
* **Recalls**
    * None Found

**Interior**

* **Faulty Door Locks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door locks can fail to lock or unlock, making it difficult to enter or exit the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replacing the door locks costs around $100 - $200+.
* **Broken Window Regulators**
    * Description/Symptoms: The window regulators can break, causing the windows to become stuck in the up or down position.
    * Solution: Replacing the window regulators costs around $200 - $400+.

**Summary**

The 1995 Chevrolet C3500HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck with a strong engine and a spacious interior. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be expensive to repair.
