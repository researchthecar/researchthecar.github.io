**Introduction:**

The 2013 Chevrolet Cheyenne pickup truck is a full-size, heavy-duty vehicle known for its powerful engines and rugged capabilities. It is offered with a choice of petrol and diesel engines, and various transmission options. The Cheyenne has received praise for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive towing and hauling abilities. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its potential issues.

**Engine**

**6.6L Duramax Diesel**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** Symptoms include loss of power, black smoke from the exhaust, and reduced fuel economy. The turbocharger needs to be replaced, costing around $3,000 - $4,000+.
* **Fuel Injector Issues:** Clogged or faulty fuel injectors can cause rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, and reduced engine power. The fuel injectors need to be cleaned or replaced, costing around $500 - $1,000+ per injector.
* **EGR System Malfunction:** A malfunctioning EGR (Exhaust Gas Recirculation) system can lead to increased emissions, reduced engine performance, and potential engine damage. The EGR valve and/or cooler may need to be replaced, costing around $1,000 - $2,000+.

**6.0L Vortec Petrol**

* **AFM (Active Fuel Management) System Issues:** Symptoms include rough idling, engine vibration, and increased fuel consumption. The AFM system may need to be disabled or repaired, costing around $500 - $1,500+.
* **Valve Spring Failure:** Broken or weak valve springs can cause a ticking noise from the engine and reduced power. The valve springs need to be replaced, costing around $1,000 - $2,000+.

**Transmission**

**Allison 1000 6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:** Symptoms include delayed or rough shifting, and hesitation during gear changes. The transmission may need to be serviced or rebuilt, costing around $2,000 - $4,000+.
* **Torque Converter Failure:** A failing torque converter can cause shuddering or vibration while driving, especially when changing gears. The torque converter needs to be replaced, costing around $1,500 - $2,500+.

**Allison 3000 6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Failure:** Major transmission failures can occur, causing complete loss of power or inability to shift gears. The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced, costing upwards of $5,000+.

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure:** Broken or weakened leaf springs can cause sagging in the rear of the vehicle and reduced ride comfort. The leaf springs need to be replaced, costing around $500 - $1,000+.
* **Rear Differential Noise:** A whining or howling noise from the rear differential can indicate worn or damaged bearings. The differential may need to be serviced or rebuilt, costing around $1,000 - $2,000+.

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include flickering lights, dashboard warning lights, and difficulty starting the vehicle. The alternator needs to be replaced, costing around $500 - $1,000+.

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Handle Breakage:** The tailgate handle may become weak or break, making it difficult to open or close the tailgate. The handle needs to be replaced, costing around $200 - $400+.

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Button Malfunctions:** Some users have reported issues with the steering wheel buttons not working properly or becoming loose. The steering wheel buttons may need to be replaced, costing around $200 - $500+.
* **Climate Control System Issues:** Users have encountered problems with the climate control system, including malfunctioning buttons, faulty temperature sensors, and insufficient heating or cooling. The climate control components may need to be repaired or replaced, costing around $500 - $1,500+.

**Summary**

The 2013 Chevrolet Cheyenne is a capable and popular pickup truck, but it is not immune to potential issues. The most common problems reported by owners include turbocharger failures, fuel injector issues, transmission problems, and suspension concerns. The severity and cost of these issues vary, and it is important to have any problems addressed promptly to minimize the potential for further damage or costly repairs.
