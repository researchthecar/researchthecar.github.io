**Introduction**

The 2016 Chevrolet Cheyenne is a full-size pickup truck that is available with a 4.3L V6, 5.3L V8, or 6.2L V8 paired with either a 6-speed or 10-speed automatic transmission. It is praised for its powerful engines, towing capabilities, and comfortable interior, but some owners have experienced various issues.

**Engine**

**4.3L V6**
* **AFM Lifter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Active Fuel Management (AFM) lifters may fail, causing a tapping noise and reduced engine power.
    * Solution: Replace AFM lifters.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA ID Number 19V-454

**5.3L V8**
* **Piston Slap**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from the engine, particularly during cold starts and acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace pistons and/or piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.2L V8**
* **Valve Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rough idle, and check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace valve springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed shifting, hesitation, or lack of power.
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may not shift properly, causing jerking or shuddering.
    * Solution: Replace TCM.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking or noisy shock absorbers.
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Leaf Spring Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or broken leaf springs.
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains excessively, leading to starting issues.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty component(s) causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Faulty Instrument Cluster**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges may malfunction or display incorrect information.
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fender Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on the fenders.
    * Solution: Replace fenders.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate Hinge Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate may not close or open properly.
    * Solution: Replace tailgate hinges.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system may not function properly, causing temperature issues.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty component(s) in the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Wiper Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wiper switch may not work or may cause wipers to operate intermittently.
    * Solution: Replace wiper switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Chevrolet Cheyenne has experienced several issues that can be costly to repair. Engine problems, such as AFM lifter failure and piston slap, are common. The transmission can also have issues, including shift solenoid failure and TCM failure. Suspension components such as shock absorbers and leaf springs may also need replacement. Electrical problems with battery drain and faulty instrument clusters can be frustrating. Exterior issues like fender rust and tailgate hinge failure are also present. While the interior may be generally comfortable, issues like climate control malfunction and wiper switch failure can occur.
