# 2019 Chevrolet Trax Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Chevrolet Trax is a subcompact crossover SUV that offers nimble handling and city-friendly dimensions, making it a popular choice for budget-conscious commuters. However, used car buyers should approach this model year with careful inspection, as owner reports point to notable recurring complaints involving electrical accessories, steering functionality, and cooling system leaks. While parts availability is generally strong due to shared GM architecture, proactive maintenance and a thorough pre-purchase inspection are highly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged I4

The 2019 Chevrolet Trax comes standard with a 1.4-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine generating approximately 138 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque. While adequately powered for urban driving, this engine has received owner complaints regarding coolant leaks and premature water pump wear.

**Coolant Leaks and Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant levels, rough engine running, or visible puddles underneath the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and cooling lines for cracks or leaks; replace failed components as necessary.
- **Cost:** $1,800

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is sent through a 6-speed automatic transmission available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It provides smooth everyday shifting, with limited widespread powertrain mechanical complaints reported in owner feedback.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Owner complaints regarding the service brakes highlight intermittent warning lights, unexpected pedal stiffness, and pulling sensations during stops.

**Brake Assist Warnings and Pedal Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Service Brake Assist warning messages displayed on the dashboard, vehicle pulling to one side, or temporary pedal hesitation.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake system sensors, battery health, and hydraulic lines; clear fault codes or replace malfunctioning sensors.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Rack

Issues related to steering stiffness and mechanical binding during cold ambient temperatures have been noted by owners, occasionally requiring major component replacement.

**Steering Column Freezing or Stiffening**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel becomes difficult to turn, locks up, or freezes during cold weather starts.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and rack assembly; lubrication or full column replacement may be required depending on wear.

## Electrical

### Climate Control Fan Resistor

Electrical accessories, particularly climate control blower motor resistors and wiring connectors, are prone to thermal degradation and early failure.

**Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Climate control fan fails to operate on certain speed levels or stops working entirely, accompanied by potential burning odors.
- **Solution:** Replace the burnt climate control fan resistor and inspect electrical connectors for thermal damage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V312000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Ball Joint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trax&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A partial separation of the lower-control arm from the vehicle could cause the attached front wheel to toe outward, impacting the vehicle's handling and steering and increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the left and right front lower-control arms, replacing them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V774000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trax&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The Sensing Diagnostic Module (SDM) on these vehicle may not have been turned off of "manufacturing mode," preventing the air bags from deploying in the event of a crash. If the air bags do not deploy in the event of a crash, it can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the SDM module to the correct mode, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Chevrolet Trax presents a mixed ownership picture. While its compact footprint and fuel-efficient turbocharged engine make it attractive for daily commuting, buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history reports and perform a detailed pre-purchase inspection. Particular attention should be paid to the cooling system, steering responsiveness in colder climates, and the operational integrity of interior electronics and climate control components.
