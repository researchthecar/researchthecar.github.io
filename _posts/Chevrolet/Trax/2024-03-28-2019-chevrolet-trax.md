**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2019 Chevrolet Trax is a subcompact crossover SUV available with one engine and two transmission options:

* 1.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine (153 hp, 177 lb-ft torque)
* 6-Speed Automatic Transmission
* Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

Drivers appreciate the Trax's fuel efficiency, affordability, and spacious interior. Some complaints include its lack of power, noisy cabin, and limited advanced safety features.

**Engine**

**1.4L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine**

* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, misfires, rough idling
    * Solution: Intake valve cleaning
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Fuel Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Fuel pump replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of turbo boost, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Wastegate actuator replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Timing chain tensioner replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, overheating
    * Solution: Torque converter replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+

**Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)**

* **CVT Pulley Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding noise from transmission, loss of power
    * Solution: CVT pulley replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mounts Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from front suspension, rough ride
    * Solution: Strut mount replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Identify and repair electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $400+
* **Electrical Harness Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, loss of power
    * Solution: Electrical harness repair or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Ignition coil replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per coil

**Exterior**

* **Front Bumper Fascia Detachment**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bumper fascia becoming loose or detached
    * Solution: Fascia repair or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Trim Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trim panels vibrating or rattling
    * Solution: Trim panel adjustment or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Summary:**

The 2019 Chevrolet Trax has some common issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. While some issues are relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be costly. Owners may want to consider extended warranty coverage to protect against potential repairs.
