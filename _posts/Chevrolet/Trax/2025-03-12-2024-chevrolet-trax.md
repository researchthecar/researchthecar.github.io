The 2024 Chevrolet Trax is a subcompact SUV powered by a 1.2-liter turbocharged inline-3 engine producing 137 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. There are no other engine or transmission options available. People generally like the Trax for its affordability, spacious interior for its class, and stylish design. However, some dislike its modest engine power and lack of available all-wheel drive.

**Engine**

**1.2L Turbocharged Inline-3**

*   **Excessive Engine Vibration**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported excessive vibration, especially at idle and low speeds. This can sometimes be felt through the steering wheel, seats, and pedals.
    *   Solution: The dealer should inspect the engine mounts, balance shaft, and engine for any signs of damage or wear. A software update to adjust the engine's idle speed may also help reduce vibration.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Fuel Injector Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported issues with the fuel injectors, leading to rough idling, misfires, and reduced fuel economy. In severe cases, it can cause the engine to stall.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injector(s). It's recommended to replace all injectors if one fails.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

*   **Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported hard or jerky shifting, especially between lower gears.
    *   Solution: A transmission fluid flush and software update may improve the shifting performance. In some cases, replacement of valve body may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system occasionally freezes, becomes unresponsive, or crashes, requiring a restart. This can affect navigation, audio, and other connected features.
    *   Solution: Check for available software updates for the infotainment system. Performing a "hard reset" (disconnecting the battery for a short time) can sometimes resolve the issue. If the problem persists, the dealer may need to replace the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software update) - $1,000+ (Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Backup Camera Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The backup camera image may be distorted, flickering, or completely black.
    *   Solution: Check the camera's connection and wiring for any damage. If the wiring is fine, the camera unit may need to be replaced.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

*   **Loose Interior Trim**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported that certain interior trim pieces, such as door panels or dashboard components, come loose or rattle.
    *   Solution: The dealer can refasten or replace the loose trim pieces.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Rattling Noise from Dashboard**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Owners have reported annoying rattling noises coming from inside the dashboard while driving, especially on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Diagnose and secure the source of the rattle, which may involve tightening loose screws, adding foam padding, or replacing damaged parts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2024 Chevrolet Trax, while generally praised for its value and interior space, does have some reported issues. Engine vibration and infotainment system problems appear to be the most frequently cited concerns. While many issues are relatively minor and can be addressed with software updates or minor repairs, some, like fuel injector problems, can be more costly. Overall, most issues seem to be minor, however, some engine and transmission issues can become costly.
