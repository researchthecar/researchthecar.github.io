# 2016 Chevrolet Trax Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Chevrolet Trax offers buyers an affordable subcompact SUV option with a practical footprint and elevated seating position. However, used-car shoppers should approach this model year with care due to recurring owner complaints regarding engine performance, electrical glitches, and body paint durability. Thoroughly inspecting the powertrain, safety restraint systems, and service history is recommended before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.4L Turbocharged I4

The 2016 Chevrolet Trax is powered by a 1.4-liter turbocharged inline-four engine producing 138 horsepower and 148 lb-ft of torque. While providing adequate pep for city commuting, this engine option is tied to notable owner reports concerning turbocharger longevity and cooling system leaks.

**Loss of Motive Power and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling, hesitation, and sudden losses of motive power while accelerating or driving at speed, frequently accompanied by the check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes, inspect the turbocharger system, and replace failing components such as the turbocharger assembly or purge valves as necessary.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant levels trigger temperature warnings on the dashboard, caused by leaks originating from cooling system components or reservoirs.
- **Solution:** Pressure test the cooling system, locate the leak source, and replace damaged hoses, clamps, or the coolant reservoir.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed automatic transmission channels power to either the front wheels or an available all-wheel-drive system. Buyers should check for smooth shifting and verify that the transmission control modules operate without intermittent communication faults.

**Transmission Communication Loss and Gear Hunting**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience sudden loss of communication with the transmission, resulting in a check engine light, failure to restart after being turned off, or erratic gear hunting on the highway at high RPMs.
- **Solution:** Scan transmission control modules for diagnostic codes, inspect wiring harnesses, and repair or replace faulty control modules.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators

Electrical complaints center heavily around convenience features and body control modules, with door lock actuators being a notable failure point.

**Power Door Lock Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power door lock actuators fail, leaving specific doors unable to be locked or unlocked via the key fob or interior switches, occasionally trapping passengers inside.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuator assemblies within the affected doors.

### Paint and Body Structure

Exterior structural integrity is generally acceptable, though cosmetic paint degradation has been a frequent frustration for owners.

**Paint Peeling and Flaking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle paint begins to bubble, peel, or flake off body panels such as the liftgate and roof, particularly on specific factory color finishes.
- **Solution:** Repaint affected body panels after proper surface preparation, as factory warranty coverage for cosmetic paint defects may have expired.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Airbags

Safety restraint components require careful pre-purchase verification to ensure all seat belts, buckles, and airbag systems are fully intact and operational.

**Seat Belt and Latch Deficiencies**
- **Symptoms:** Owners and passengers report missing, cut, or malfunctioning seat belt clips and assemblies, compromising occupant safety.
- **Solution:** Inspect all seating positions for proper seat belt functionality and replace damaged or missing seat belt assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V652000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm:Lower Ball Joint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trax&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A partial separation of the lower-control arm from the vehicle could cause the attached front wheel to toe outward, impacting the vehicle's handling and steering and increasing the risk of crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the left and right front lower-control arms, replacing them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trax&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V422000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trax&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) numbers 208 "Occupant Crash Protection" and 114 "Theft Protection." Without audible indicators, the driver may not fasten their seatbelt, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will update the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V437000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trax&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** While being previously remedied for recall 16V-651, the affected vehicles may not have received the complete software update necessary to remedy the recall condition. Without the update, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V257000: Equipment:Electrical:Radio/Tape Deck/Cd Etc.](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Trax&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 114 "Theft Protection." Without audible indicators, the driver may leave the key in the ignition, increasing the risk of theft.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will update the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Chevrolet Trax presents a mixed ownership picture, blending nimble city driving dynamics with a higher-than-average volume of powertrain and electrical complaints. Prospective buyers should prioritize vehicles with comprehensive service records, test drive thoroughly to check for transmission hesitation or engine sputtering, and confirm that all safety recalls and structural items have been fully addressed.
