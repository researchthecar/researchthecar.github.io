## 1998 Chevrolet K1500 Suburban

The 1998 Chevrolet K1500 Suburban is a full-size SUV that was available with a range of engine and transmission options. It was popular for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and towing capabilities. However, like any vehicle, it had its share of issues.

The K1500 Suburban was offered with three engine options: a 5.7L V8, a 6.5L turbodiesel V8, and a 7.4L V8. The 5.7L V8 produced 255 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.5L turbodiesel V8 produced 195 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. The 7.4L V8 produced 290 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque.

The K1500 Suburban was also available with three transmission options: a 4-speed automatic, a 5-speed manual, and a 6-speed manual. The 4-speed automatic was the most popular option, while the 5-speed manual was only available with the 5.7L V8 engine. The 6-speed manual was only available with the 7.4L V8 engine.
