### Standard Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Issues**

- **Cost:** $100 - $500

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery depletes faster than expected, requiring frequent jump starts.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic draw, check alternator output, and inspect battery health.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600

The 2017 Chevrolet LCF 3500HD demonstrates good overall reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain components.
