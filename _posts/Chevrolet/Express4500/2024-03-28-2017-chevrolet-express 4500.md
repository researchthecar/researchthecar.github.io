**Introduction**

The 2017 Chevrolet Express 4500 is a full-size van offered with a choice of two engine options: a 6.0L V8 with 341 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque or a 6.6L V8 with 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Express 4500 is known for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and towing capabilities. However, it has also been reported to have several issues.

**Engine**

* **6.0L V8**
    * **Intake Manifold Bolt Failure:** The intake manifold bolts can fail, causing a vacuum leak and reduced engine performance.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold bolts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V128000
* **6.6L V8**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** The fuel injectors can fail, causing misfires, reduced engine performance, and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure:** The TCM can fail, causing transmission problems such as erratic shifting, loss of power, and inability to shift gears.
        * Solution: Replace the TCM.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Mount Failure:** The front suspension strut mount can fail, causing a clunking noise, reduced handling, and uneven tire wear.
        * Solution: Replace the front suspension strut mounts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:** The rear leaf springs can break or crack, causing a rattling noise, reduced ride quality, and impaired handling.
        * Solution: Replace the rear leaf springs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator can fail, causing the battery to discharge and the vehicle to lose power.
        * Solution: Replace the alternator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge:** The battery can discharge prematurely due to a parasitic drain or a faulty electrical component.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the parasitic drain or faulty component.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:** The door handles can become loose or break, making it difficult to open the doors.
        * Solution: Replace the door handles.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Window Regulator Failure:** The window regulators can fail, causing the windows to get stuck or fall out of the tracks.
        * Solution: Replace the window regulators.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system can malfunction, causing the AC or heater to not work properly.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty component within the climate control system.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the faulty component
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattle:** The dashboard can rattle due to loose components or poor insulation.
        * Solution: Disassemble and tighten or insulate the loose components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Chevrolet Express 4500 has been reported to have several issues affecting various components. The most severe issues include engine intake manifold bolt failure, fuel injector failure, transmission control module failure, and rear leaf spring breakage. The cost to fix these issues can range from a few hundred dollars to over $1,000. While the Chevrolet Express 4500 offers spaciousness and powerful engines, it is important for potential buyers to be aware of the potential issues that may arise.
