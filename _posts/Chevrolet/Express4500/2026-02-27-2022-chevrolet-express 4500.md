# 2022 Chevrolet Express 4500

### Overview
The 2022 Chevrolet Express 4500 is a heavy-duty full-size van designed for commercial use, emphasizing utility and cargo capacity. Reliability for this model year generally aligns with expectations for a work vehicle, with a focus on robust powertrains and durable construction. While specific component failures are not widespread, potential areas for attention can arise with high mileage and demanding operational conditions. Owners should be aware of common areas that may require maintenance or repair over the vehicle's lifespan, particularly concerning the powertrain and electrical systems, though overall dependability is considered strong for its class.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Chevrolet Express 4500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 6.6L V8 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission are generally robust and well-suited for the demands of commercial use. Suspension components are designed for durability. Minor electrical gremlins, such as intermittent power issues, and HVAC blower motor failures are the most commonly cited, though not to a degree that significantly impacts overall dependability. For a work van, the Express 4500 offers a dependable platform with manageable potential repair costs, provided regular maintenance is performed.
