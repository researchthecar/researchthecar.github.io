**Introduction:**

The 2015 Chevrolet Express 4500 is a full-size cargo van offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The standard engine is a 6.0L V8 producing 342 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque. Optional engine choices include a 6.6L V8 diesel producing 450 horsepower and 910 lb-ft of torque, a 6.0L V8 gasoline producing 366 horsepower and 388 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.0L Vortec L96 V8 gasoline producing 357 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque. Paired with the engines are either a 6-speed automatic transmission, an 8-speed automatic transmission, or a 10-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the Chevrolet Express 4500 appreciate its spacious cargo area, powerful engine options, and towing capacity. However, they have also reported experiencing various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8 Gasoline**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Symptoms include coolant leaks, engine overheating, and reduced engine performance. Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Lifter Failure:** Symptoms include engine knocking or ticking, decreased engine performance, and reduced fuel efficiency. Solution: Replace lifters. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Oil Pressure Sensor Failure:** Symptoms include intermittent or no oil pressure readings on the dashboard gauge, engine overheating, and engine damage. Solution: Replace oil pressure sensor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**6.6L V8 Diesel**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Symptoms include hard starting, poor engine performance, and black smoke from the exhaust. Solution: Replace fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Turbocharger Failure:** Symptoms include decreased engine power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, and loud noises from the engine compartment. Solution: Replace turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Emission Control System Issues:** Symptoms include illuminated "Check Engine" light, reduced engine performance, and increased emissions. Solution: Repair or replace emissions control components. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the specific issue

**6.0L V8 Gasoline (L96)**

* **Piston Ring Failure:** Symptoms include excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, and decreased engine performance. Solution: Overhaul or replace engine. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Timing Chain Failure:** Symptoms include engine rattling noises, decreased engine performance, and engine failure. Solution: Replace timing chain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure:** Symptoms include slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, and overheating. Solution: Replace torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Symptoms include wet spots under the vehicle, low transmission fluid levels, and decreased transmission performance. Solution: Repair or replace leaking transmission components. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the location and severity of the leak

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, delayed shifts, and hard shifts. Solution: Repair or replace transmission components. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the specific issue
* **Overheat Protection Mode:** Symptoms include transmission overheating and the vehicle entering a limp mode. Solution: Replace faulty transmission components or sensors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Failure:** Symptoms include sagging or broken leaf springs, decreased ride height, and handling issues. Solution: Replace leaf springs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Shock Absorber Failure:** Symptoms include poor ride quality, excessive bouncing, and handling issues. Solution: Replace shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, dead batteries, and rapid battery discharge. Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system issues. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause of the drain
* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include dim headlights, flickering dashboard lights, and difficulty starting the vehicle. Solution: Replace alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Starter Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, grinding noises, and no response from the starter. Solution: Replace starter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Rust and Corrosion:** Symptoms include rust spots on the body panels, undercarriage, and frame. Solution: Repair or replace rusted components. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of the rust
* **Door Hinge Failure:** Symptoms include loose or sagging doors, difficulty opening or closing doors, and broken hinges. Solution: Repair or replace door hinges. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracks:** Symptoms include cracks or splits in the dashboard material. Solution: Replace dashboard. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Seat Belt Pretensioner Failure:** Symptoms include failure of the seat belt to tighten during a collision. Solution: Replace seat belt pretensioners. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Air Conditioning Malfunction:** Symptoms include weak or no airflow from the air conditioning system, warm air blowing, and compressor noise. Solution: Repair or replace air conditioning components. Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the specific issue

**Recalls**

* **NHTSA Campaign Number:** 16V645000 - Rear Differential Carrier Bolts May Fracture
* **NHTSA Campaign Number:** 17V159000 - Brake Rotor Fracture
* **NHTSA Campaign Number:** 19V060000 - Side Door Latch May Fail
* **NHTSA Campaign Number:** 20V164000 - Center Seat Belt Buckle Anchor Failure

**Summary**

The Chevrolet Express 4500 is a versatile and capable cargo van, but it has been known to experience various issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior components. The severity of these issues varies, but some of them can be costly to repair. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
