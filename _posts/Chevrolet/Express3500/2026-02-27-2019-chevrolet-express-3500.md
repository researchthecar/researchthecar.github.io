# 2019 Chevrolet Express 3500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a heavy-duty commercial van built for towing, hauling, and fleet service. While owner feedback is sparse, commercial vehicles often endure heavy workloads that make thorough pre-purchase inspections essential for buyers.

---

## Engine

### 4.3L V6

A 4.3-liter V6 engine producing approximately 276 horsepower and 298 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard entry-level powerplant. It provides adequate power for lighter utility duties, though fleet buyers often opt for heavier-duty V8 options.

### 6.0L V8

A robust 6.0-liter V8 engine generating around 341 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque, designed for heavy towing and hauling. This tried-and-true block is known for long-term durability in commercial applications.

### 2.8L Duramax Turbo-Diesel 4-Cylinder

A 2.8-liter turbo-diesel engine outputting roughly 181 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with an automatic transmission. It offers enhanced low-end torque and improved fuel economy for high-mileage commercial routes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission paired with select gasoline engines to optimize highway cruising and fuel delivery. Regular fluid maintenance is recommended to ensure smooth long-term operation under load.

### 6-Speed Heavy-Duty Automatic Transmission

A heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission designed to handle the high torque output of the V8 and diesel powerplants. It features durable gearsets built to withstand rigorous commercial use.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake Booster

Owner reports indicate potential hydraulic booster issues can impact brake pressure and steering effort.

**Hydraulic Booster Fluid Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid leaking from the hydraulic power brake booster, resulting in a stiffer steering feel and increased braking distances.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hydraulic booster assembly and replace the leaking unit to restore proper brake assist and steering function.
