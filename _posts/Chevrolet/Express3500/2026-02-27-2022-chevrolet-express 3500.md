### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights flickering, accessories not functioning intermittently, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of wiring harness, battery connections, alternator, or control modules. Repair or replacement of faulty components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Chevrolet Express 3500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission, which are generally robust for commercial duty. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, specifically concerning intermittent power losses and infotainment system glitches. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and require professional diagnosis. Exterior and interior components, along with the suspension, appear to be holding up well under typical usage. Proactive checks of electrical connections and software updates for the infotainment system are recommended for optimal long-term performance.
