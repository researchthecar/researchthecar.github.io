**Introduction:**

The 2007 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a versatile full-size van offering robust performance and spacious accommodations. It boasts a 6.0L V8 engine producing 323 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Praised for its hauling and towing capabilities, the Express also faces some common issues to be aware of.

**Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak from the intake manifold, overheating engine
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, hard starting, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, slipping gears, delayed engagement
        * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, solenoid replacement if necessary
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace rear leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, clicking noise
    * Solution: Replace starter motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light, dim lights, electrical problems
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken or loose door handle
    * Solution: Replace door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Air Conditioning Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of cold air, warm air blowing
    * Solution: Replace air conditioning compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Instrument Panel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning gauges, warning lights, no display
    * Solution: Replace instrument panel cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2007 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a capable vehicle with some common issues. Engine problems, such as intake manifold leaks and crankshaft sensor failures, can occur. Transmission issues can include shifting difficulties. Suspension issues, like rear leaf spring failure, may affect ride quality. Electrical problems, including starter and alternator failures, can cause difficulties. Occasional exterior and interior issues can arise. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost of repairs can range from minor to moderate.
