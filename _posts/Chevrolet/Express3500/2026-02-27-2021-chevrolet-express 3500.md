### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Chevrolet Express 3500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components, which are well-established. The primary areas of concern tend to be within the electrical system, with reports of intermittent power issues and infotainment glitches. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and require professional diagnosis. For the most part, the Express 3500 lives up to its reputation as a dependable vehicle for commercial and heavy-duty use, provided regular maintenance is performed and potential electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
