**Introduction:**
The 2005 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a popular full-size passenger van. It is available with multiple engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 5.3L V8 that produces 295 horsepower and 335 lb-ft of torque. The other available engine option is a 6.0L V8 that produces 325 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Chevrolet Express 3500 has a spacious interior and a comfortable ride, but it is not as fuel-efficient as some other full-size vans.

**Engine**
**5.3L V8**
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Oil or coolant leaks, reduced engine performance, check engine light. Replace intake manifold gaskets. $500 - $1000+
* **Piston Ring Failure:** Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust, reduced engine power. Replace piston rings. $1500 - $2500+

**6.0L V8**
* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Oil leaks, burning oil smell, check engine light. Replace valve cover gaskets. $300 - $500+
* **Head Gasket Failure:** Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust. Replace head gaskets. $1500 - $2500+
* **Spark Plug Failure:** Engine misfires, rough idle, reduced power. Replace spark plugs. $100 - $300+
* **Fuel Pump Failure:** Engine stalling, difficulty starting, loss of power. Replace fuel pump. $500 - $1000+

**Transmission**
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Leaks from transmission pan, reduced transmission fluid levels, slipping gears. Replace transmission pan gasket or seal. $200 - $500+
* **Shift Solenoid Failure:** Transmission slipping, hard shifts, delayed shifts. Replace shift solenoid. $300 - $600+
* **Torque Converter Failure:** Shuddering, slipping, overheating. Replace torque converter. $800 - $1500+

**Suspension**
* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:** Noise, vibration, bouncing, reduced steering control. Replace front suspension struts. $500 - $1000+
* **Rear Suspension Leaf Spring Breakage:** Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity. Replace leaf springs. $300 - $700+

**Electrical**
* **Battery Failure:** Difficulty starting, electrical problems, dead battery. Replace battery. $100 - $300+
* **Alternator Failure:** Dimming lights, flickering gauges, reduced electrical power. Replace alternator. $400 - $800+
* **Starter Failure:** Engine will not crank, clicking noise. Replace starter. $300 - $600+

**Exterior**
* **Door Hinge Pin Failure:** Door sagging, difficulty opening or closing doors. Replace door hinge pins. $200 - $400+
* **Tailgate Hinge Failure:** Tailgate sagging, difficulty opening or closing tailgate. Replace tailgate hinges. $100 - $300+

**Interior**
* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:** Loose or broken dashboard components. Tighten or replace dashboard components. $100 - $300+
* **Window Regulator Failure:** Difficulty opening or closing windows. Replace window regulator. $300 - $600+

**Summary:**
The 2005 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a generally reliable vehicle, but it does have some common issues. The most severe and expensive issues are engine and transmission problems. However, these issues are not as common as some of the less severe issues, such as electrical problems and interior squeaks and rattles. Overall, the Chevrolet Express 3500 is a good choice for those who need a spacious and comfortable full-size van.
