The 2014 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a full-size van known for its durability and cargo capacity, often used for commercial purposes and passenger transport. Engine options include a 4.8L V8 (280 hp, 295 lb-ft of torque), a 6.0L V8 (342 hp, 373 lb-ft of torque), and a 6.6L Duramax Diesel V8 (260 hp, 525 lb-ft of torque). The available transmissions are the 4L80E 4-speed automatic, and the 6L90 6-speed automatic. Owners appreciate the van's practicality and robust build, but common complaints revolve around fuel economy, dated interior, and some recurring mechanical issues.

**Engine**

**4.8L V8**

*   **Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption between oil changes.
    *   Solution: Replacing valve stem seals can help reduce the amount of oil leaking past the valve. Replace the PCV Valve as a maintenance item.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**6.0L V8**

*   **Active Fuel Management (AFM) Lifter Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Ticking noise, misfires, and potential engine damage due to AFM lifter failure, leading to flat cams.
    *   Solution: Replace failed lifters with updated parts or perform an AFM delete.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption between oil changes.
    *   Solution: Replacing valve stem seals can help reduce the amount of oil leaking past the valve. Replace the PCV Valve as a maintenance item.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**6.6L Duramax Diesel V8**

*   **Fuel Injector Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, black smoke, decreased fuel economy, and potential engine damage.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Glow Plug Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Hard starting in cold weather, excessive white smoke during start-up.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty glow plugs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4L80E 4-speed automatic**

*   **Slipping/Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping between gears, harsh or delayed shifts.
    *   Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**6L90 6-speed automatic**

*   **Torque Converter Shudder**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration at low speeds, especially during light acceleration.
    *   Solution: Replace the torque converter or perform a transmission fluid flush.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Delayed or Harsh Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Delays in shifting gears, hard or jerky shifts.
    *   Solution: Valve body replacement or transmission rebuild/replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $4,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Leaf Spring Sagging/Broken Leaf Springs**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear end sagging, reduced load capacity, clunking noises.
    *   Solution: Replace leaf springs.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Worn Ball Joints/Tie Rod Ends**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Loose steering, clunking noises, uneven tire wear.
    *   Solution: Replace ball joints and/or tie rod ends.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Door Lock Actuator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Door locks failing to function, requiring manual operation.
    *   Solution: Replace faulty door lock actuator(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+ per door
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Alternator Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dimming headlights, vehicle stalling.
    *   Solution: Replace the alternator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust on Rocker Panels and Wheel Arches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rust formation on rocker panels and wheel arches.
    *   Solution: Rust removal and repair, panel replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Driver Seat Frame Cracks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Driver seat frame crack which cause the seat to rock back and forth when accelerating or braking.
    * Solution: Replace the seat frame or seat assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Chevrolet Express 3500, while reliable for its intended purpose, exhibits some common issues that potential buyers should be aware of. Engine issues, especially oil consumption and AFM lifter failure (for the 6.0L), can lead to significant repair costs. Transmission problems, such as torque converter shudder and harsh shifting, are also potential concerns. Suspension and electrical issues can be more manageable in terms of cost, but still need to be addressed to maintain the vehicle's reliability and safety. Overall, a thorough inspection before purchase is recommended.
