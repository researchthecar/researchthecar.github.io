**Introduction:**

The 2023 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a full-size van with a powerful engine and rugged construction. It offers a spacious interior with multiple seating configurations and a variety of engine and transmission options. Owners praise its durability and towing capabilities, but some have reported issues related to certain components.

**Engine Options:**

* 6.6L V8 gasoline engine (401 horsepower, 464 lb-ft of torque)
* 6.0L V8 gasoline engine (341 horsepower, 373 lb-ft of torque)
* 6.6L Duramax turbo-diesel V8 engine (445 horsepower, 910 lb-ft of torque)

**Transmission Options:**

* 6-speed automatic transmission
* 10-speed automatic transmission

**Engine**

**6.6L V8 Gasoline Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Exhaust Manifold Cracks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust leaks, rattling noises
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifolds
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.0L V8 Gasoline Engine**

* **Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.6L Duramax Turbo-Diesel V8**

* **Diesel Particulate Filter (DPF) Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, DPF warning light
    * Solution: Clean or replace DPF
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, reduced power, misfires
    * Solution: Replace injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping out of gears, difficulty shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Hesitations:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitating to shift gears, delayed acceleration
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive tire wear, uneven ride, clunking noises
    * Solution: Replace suspension components (e.g., ball joints, bushings)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Faults:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical components malfunctioning, engine not starting
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Taillight Assembly Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tail lights or brake lights not working
    * Solution: Replace rear taillight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Gaps:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gaps or misalignments in the dashboard panels
    * Solution: Adjust or replace dashboard panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Chevrolet Express 3500 has a few potential issues that owners should be aware of. Engine-related problems can be costly to repair, while transmission issues can affect the vehicle's performance. The suspension, electrical system, and interior have also been reported to have issues. Overall, the severity and cost of the reported issues vary depending on the specific problem encountered.
