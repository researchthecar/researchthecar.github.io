**Introduction:**

The 2015 Chevrolet Express 3500 is a full-size van offered with various engine and transmission options. The Vortec 6.0L V8 engine produces 324 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque. The Vortec 6.6L V8 engine produces 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic and an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Express 3500 is known for its spacious interior, cargo capacity, and towing capabilities, while some drawbacks include its fuel consumption and handling.

**Engine**

**Vortec 6.0L V8**
* **Intake manifold failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, and reduced fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
* **Exhaust manifold leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, ticking, or whistling noises.
    * Solution: Replace the exhaust manifold gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Vortec 6.6L V8**
* **Fuel injector failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, and difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Replace the failed fuel injector(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per injector
* **EGR valve failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine performance issues, increased emissions.
    * Solution: Clean or replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Emissions sensor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the failed sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000

**8-Speed Automatic**
* **Torque converter shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension squeak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or creaking noises from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar links or bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Rear leaf spring failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, excessive body roll.
    * Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, electrical system issues.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
* **Starter failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, clicking noises.
    * Solution: Replace the starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Exterior**

* **Rear door handle failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Handle doesn't open the door properly.
    * Solution: Replace the door handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Interior**

* **Door lock actuator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors don't lock or unlock properly.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per door
* **Climate control issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Summary:**

The 2015 Chevrolet Express 3500 has a range of reported issues, but many of them are not major concerns and can be fixed at a reasonable cost. The most severe issues include intake manifold failure on the Vortec 6.0L V8 engine and transmission shifting problems on the 6-speed automatic transmission. Overall, the Express 3500 is a reliable vehicle with a spacious and versatile interior, making it suitable for both commercial and personal use.
