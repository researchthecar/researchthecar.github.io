# 2024 Chevrolet Equinox EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Chevrolet Equinox EV is a modern all-electric crossover offering substantial driving range and contemporary styling. As a relatively new EV platform, early owner feedback highlights several prominent areas of concern, primarily involving advanced safety systems, interior visibility, and digital display stability. Buyers interested in a used model should closely inspect these electronic and driver-assistance features during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### Single Motor Electric Propulsion

The standard front-wheel-drive configuration utilizes a single permanent magnet synchronous motor delivering approximately 213 horsepower and 236 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth, efficient acceleration suitable for daily commuting with generally solid reliability, though early adopters have reported occasional high-voltage warnings.

### Dual Motor Electric Propulsion

The available dual-motor all-wheel-drive system increases output to approximately 288 horsepower and 333 lb-ft of torque. This option adds enhanced traction in adverse weather conditions while maintaining the platform's overall electric drivetrain architecture.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Fixed Gear Transmission

The single-speed direct-drive transmission routes power from the electric motor(s) directly to the drive wheels without traditional gear shifts. This setup inherently features fewer moving parts than a conventional internal combustion transmission, resulting in minimal mechanical complications.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

While standard stopping performance is generally adequate, a small number of owners have flagged concerning electronic or hydraulic brake response delays.

**Brake Responsiveness Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where pressing the brake pedal in low-speed or parking scenarios resulted in delayed or insufficient deceleration.
- **Solution:** Have the brake system hydraulic lines, regenerative braking software, and electronic brake actuators thoroughly inspected by a qualified professional.

## Steering

### Turn Signal Stalk

The steering column multi-function switch has exhibited mechanical sensitivity issues during normal turning maneuvers.

**Unintended Signal Activation**
- **Symptoms:** After completing a right turn, the turn signal stalk may overcorrect or bounce, automatically engaging the left turn signal for a few flashes.
- **Solution:** Inspect and adjust or replace the multi-function switch assembly if the mechanical self-canceling mechanism fails to operate smoothly.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster & Infotainment

The vehicle relies heavily on large digital displays for driving metrics and controls. Some owners have experienced unexpected screen blackouts and software reboots while driving.

**Display Screen Failure and Rebooting**
- **Symptoms:** The digital dashboard or infotainment screens may suddenly go completely black or freeze, temporarily cutting off the speedometer, battery status, and climate controls before rebooting.
- **Solution:** Software updates performed by a dealership can often resolve display stability issues, though persistent hardware faults may require module replacement.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Dashboard Visibility

Exterior visibility can be compromised under specific lighting conditions due to interior design reflections against the windshield.

**Dashboard Glare**
- **Symptoms:** Bright sunlight reflects intensely off the dashboard material, creating severe glare on the inside of the windshield and impairing forward visibility.
- **Solution:** Using a dashboard cover or polarizing sunglasses can help mitigate the reflection during bright daytime driving.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features are designed to enhance safety, but erratic activation of the automatic emergency braking system has been a notable complaint among owners.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience phantom braking, unexpectedly and abruptly applying the brakes while driving at highway speeds or through intersections without any apparent obstacles.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician check sensor alignment, radar calibration, and ensure all relevant software recalls or updates are applied.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Chevrolet Equinox EV presents an appealing option for used electric vehicle buyers seeking modern range and features, but it requires careful evaluation. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test the driver-assistance systems for any unexpected braking behavior, verify that all digital displays and software are functioning without stutter or failure, and check for interior glare during sunny test drives. Ensuring all manufacturer software updates have been performed will go a long way toward securing a reliable ownership experience.
