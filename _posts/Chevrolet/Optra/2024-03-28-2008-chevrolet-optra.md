**Introduction:**

The 2008 Chevrolet Optra was a compact sedan offered with a 2.0L Ecotec engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission. It was known for its affordability, spacious interior, and comfortable ride. However, some owners reported encountering issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* 2.0L Ecotec Engine

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant or oil leak, rough idle, loss of power.
* **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* 4-Speed Automatic Transmission

**Transmission Solenoid Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, check engine light.
* **Solution:** Replace faulty solenoid.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $900+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Noises while turning, uneven tire wear, steering drift.
* **Solution:** Replace worn bushings.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Battery failure, lights dimming, slow cranking.
* **Solution:** Identify and fix cause of power drain.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Water inside headlights, diminished visibility.
* **Solution:** Seal headlight housing or replace faulty unit.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

**HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** No air flow from vents, noisy blower motor.
* **Solution:** Replace blower motor.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Instrument Panel Shut Off:**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Dashboard lights and gauges turn off while driving.
* **Solution:** Diagnose cause of electrical fault and repair.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $600+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2008 Chevrolet Optra had some reported issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components. Some issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure and transmission solenoid issues, could be more severe and costly to fix. However, most of the reported issues are relatively minor and can be addressed with regular maintenance and timely repairs.
