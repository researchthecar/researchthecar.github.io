**Introduction:**

The 2004 Chevrolet Tornado is a compact pickup truck that offered a 2.2L Ecotec I4 engine paired with either a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. Despite its affordability and practicality, the Tornado has been plagued by various issues that have caused significant headaches for owners.

**Engine**

**2.2L Ecotec I4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of coolant, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace or repair synchronizers and shift forks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, no engagement, delayed shifting
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Clunks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from front suspension over bumps
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts or lower ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

* **Rear Axle Seal Leakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from rear axle
    * Solution: Replace axle seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty electrical components, such as alternator, battery, or starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

* **Electrical Short:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, flickering lights, overheating wires
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical short
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **Hood Release Cable Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Unable to release hood latch
    * Solution: Replace hood release cable
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from dashboard over bumps
    * Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

* **Seat Insulation Deterioration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Foam insulation under seats deteriorating and breaking down
    * Solution: Replace seat insulation
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Summary:**

The 2004 Chevrolet Tornado is a vehicle marred by a multitude of issues that can negatively impact its reliability and resale value. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others, such as transmission failure and electrical shorts, can be extremely costly and time-consuming to repair. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
