**Introduction:**

The Bolt EV is known for its spacious interior, advanced technology features, and impressive driving range. However, it has also been met with some complaints regarding various issues.

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Clunking**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension when driving over uneven surfaces or making sharp turns.
Solution: Replace or repair faulty suspension components, such as the struts, control arms, or sway bar links.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension Sagging**
Description/Symptoms: The rear of the vehicle sags, causing a noticeable tilt or uneven ride height.
Solution: Replace or repair the rear suspension springs or shock absorbers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Pack Cooling Issues**
Description/Symptoms: The battery pack overheats, causing reduced performance or even vehicle shutdown.
Solution: Replace or repair the faulty battery pack cooling system components, such as fans or thermal management modules.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $8000 - $12000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V035000

**Battery Pack Discharge**
Description/Symptoms: The battery pack drains rapidly, resulting in reduced driving range or sudden vehicle shutdown.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the parasitic battery drain, which could be due to faulty modules, sensors, or electrical components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers fail to operate or move erratically.
Solution: Replace the faulty windshield wiper motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Center Console Creaking**
Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling noises coming from the center console when driving on rough roads.
Solution: Tighten or replace loose or worn components within the console assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Chevrolet Bolt EV has experienced some issues related to battery pack cooling, excessive battery drain, and suspension components. These issues can result in expensive repairs, particularly for replacing the battery pack. However, the frequency and severity of these problems vary among individual vehicles.
