# 2022 Chevrolet Colorado Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used midsize truck, evaluating historical complaint patterns helps highlight areas requiring closer inspection during a pre-purchase test drive. While owners report generally satisfying everyday utility, certain recurring annoyances and mechanical hiccups—particularly concerning electrical systems, steering mechanism quirks, and braking feedback—warrant attention before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L I4 Engine

The base 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces 200 horsepower and 191 lb-ft of torque, serving as a basic entry-level powerplant for light-duty driving. It provides adequate efficiency for fleet or commuter use, though heavy towing strains its capability.

### 3.6L V6 Engine

The popular 3.6-liter V6 engine delivers 308 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque, offering a smooth power band and robust acceleration. It strikes a balanced compromise between daily drivability and capable midsize towing.

### 2.8L Duramax Turbo-Diesel I4

The 2.8-liter Duramax turbo-diesel generates 181 horsepower and an impressive 369 lb-ft of low-end torque. It is a favored option for buyers prioritizing high towing capacity and long-distance fuel economy.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired primarily with the base four-cylinder engine, this traditional 6-speed automatic provides predictable shifting for daily driving tasks.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Matched with the V6 engine, this 8-speed automatic aims to optimize fuel economy and acceleration, though buyers should verify smooth operation during test drives.

### 6-Speed Automatic (Diesel)

A heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission designed to handle the high torque output of the Duramax diesel engine option.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide strong stopping power under normal conditions, but isolated reports of high pedal resistance require vigilance.

**Stiff Pedal Feel**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden moments where the brake pedal feels extraordinarily hard to depress, resembling a brick underfoot during low-speed parking maneuvers.
- **Solution:** Have the brake booster, vacuum lines, and hydraulic components inspected by a qualified technician.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Shifter Linkage

Steering and column linkages generally perform well, though minor mechanical interlock failures can trap keys in the ignition.

**Stuck Key and Gear Shift Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note difficulty removing ignition keys unless the gear shifter is cycled repeatedly between gears or the battery is temporarily disconnected.
- **Solution:** Adjust or replace worn steering column release mechanisms and shift cables.

## Electrical

### Ignition and Shifter Interlock

Electrical system concerns focus predominantly on ignition switch communication quirks and shifter recognition issues.

**Key Stuck in Ignition**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report being unable to remove the key from the ignition switch because the vehicle's computer fails to register that the transmission is fully in Park.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty shifter assemblies or adjustment sensors within the steering column.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V375000: Seats:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chevrolet&model=Colorado&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly welded seat frame may not properly restrain the seat occupant, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front passenger seat and replace the cushion frame, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

Overall, this truck offers solid utility and diverse powertrain configurations suited to varying workloads. While owners have noted specific frustrations regarding ignition key release mechanisms and occasional brake pedal stiffness, thorough pre-purchase inspections can help identify these concerns before purchase.
