**Introduction:**

The 2017 Chevrolet Silverado 3500, a heavy-duty pickup truck, is known for its towing and hauling capabilities. Engine options include a 6.0L V8 gas engine (360 horsepower, 380 lb-ft), a 6.6L V8 Duramax diesel engine (445 horsepower, 910 lb-ft), and an optional 6.6L V8 Duramax High-Output diesel engine (445 horsepower, 950 lb-ft). Transmission options are a 6-speed automatic for the gas engine and an Allison 10-speed automatic for the diesel engines. The Silverado 3500 is praised for its power and durability but criticized for its fuel economy and interior quality.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8 Gas Engine**

* **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, rough engine idle
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Fuel Injectors:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, decreased fuel efficiency, hard starting
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.6L V8 Duramax Diesel Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased exhaust smoke, whistling sound
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.6L V8 Duramax High-Output Diesel Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes, delayed acceleration
    * Solution: Replace torque converter clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Allison 10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises over bumps, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains overnight, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery or troubleshoot electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots on the rear bumper
    * Solution: Replace or repaint rear bumper
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Overheating:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Center console becomes hot to the touch, electronic components malfunctioning
    * Solution: Repair or replace center console wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Chevrolet Silverado 3500 has a range of issues, some of which can be costly to repair, such as engine and transmission problems. While it offers impressive power and capability, potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in potential repair costs.
