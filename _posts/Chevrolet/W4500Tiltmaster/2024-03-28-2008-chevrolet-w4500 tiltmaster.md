**Introduction:**

The 2008 Chevrolet W4500 Tiltmaster is a heavy-duty commercial truck with a gross vehicle weight rating (GVWR) of 45,000 pounds. It is powered by a 6.6L Duramax turbodiesel engine and an Allison 6-speed automatic transmission. The W4500 Tiltmaster is known for its towing and hauling capabilities, as well as its comfortable cab and advanced features. However, it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

**6.6L Duramax Turbodiesel**

**Exhaust Gas Recirculation (EGR) System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The EGR valve or cooler may fail, leading to reduced engine power, increased emissions, and a check engine light.
Solution: Replace the EGR valve or cooler.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 13V671000

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail due to oil starvation or contamination, resulting in reduced boost pressure, decreased engine performance, and a whining noise.
Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Allison 6-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or shudder when shifting, leading to a loss of power or a delayed response.
Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Leaf Spring Breakage**
Description/Symptoms: The leaf springs may break under heavy loads, resulting in a loss of stability and control.
Solution: Replace the leaf springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Steering**

**Power Steering Pump Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The power steering pump may fail, leading to a loss of power steering assist and difficulty turning the steering wheel.
Solution: Replace the power steering pump.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, leading to a loss of power and a dead battery.
Solution: Replace the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Battery Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may fail prematurely due to electrical issues or excessive use.
Solution: Replace the battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Mirrors Corrosion**
Description/Symptoms: The mirrors may corrode and become damaged over time.
Solution: Replace the mirrors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Cabin Noise**
Description/Symptoms: The cabin may experience excessive noise from wind or road vibration.
Solution: Install sound deadening materials.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2008 Chevrolet W4500 Tiltmaster is a capable commercial truck, but it has been reported to have some issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. While the W4500 Tiltmaster offers strong performance and features, potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
