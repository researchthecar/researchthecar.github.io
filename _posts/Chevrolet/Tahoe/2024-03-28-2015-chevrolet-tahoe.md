**2015 Chevrolet Tahoe**

The 2015 Chevrolet Tahoe is a full-size SUV offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 5.3L V8 producing 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 6.2L V8 producing 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.0L Duramax diesel V6 producing 269 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque. The Tahoe is available with a six-speed automatic transmission, an eight-speed automatic transmission, or a ten-speed automatic transmission.

The Tahoe is known for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and towing capability. However, it has also received criticism for its fuel economy and ride quality.

**Engine**

* **5.3L V8 with Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Valve stem seal failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **AFM lifter failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, reduced power
        * Solution: Replace AFM lifters
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 16V387000
* **5.3L V8 with Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Valve stem seal failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6.2L V8 with Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **3.0L Duramax Diesel V6 with Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Turbocharger failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, increased fuel consumption, black smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Torque converter shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration during acceleration or shifting
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **Ten-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear shocks failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride quality, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replace rear shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Front control arm bushings failure**
       * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from front end, uneven tire wear
       * Solution: Replace front control arm bushings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
       * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle will not start, electrical components malfunctioning
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate latch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tailgate will not open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V385000

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard material
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center console lid failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Center console lid will not open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace center console lid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Heater core failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blowing cold air, fogging windows
    * Solution: Replace heater core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Chevrolet Tahoe is a large and powerful SUV with a spacious interior and good towing capability. However, it has been known to have some reliability issues, particularly with its engine and suspension components. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the severity of the issue and the location of the mechanic.
