**2001 Chevrolet C6500 Kodiak**

The 2001 Chevrolet C6500 Kodiak is a commercial-grade pickup truck with a maximum payload capacity of up to 17,500 pounds. Its available engine options include the 7.4-liter gasoline V8 (290 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque) and the 6.6-liter Duramax diesel V8 (300 horsepower and 520 lb-ft of torque). Transmission choices consist of an Allison 1000 5-speed automatic for the gasoline engine and an Allison 3000 6-speed automatic for the diesel engine.

People generally praise the C6500 Kodiak for its durability, towing capacity, and off-road capabilities. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as poor fuel economy and a cramped cabin.

**Engine**

**7.4-liter Gasoline V8 with Allison 1000 5-speed Automatic**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, engine overheating, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Spark Plug Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced performance, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.6-liter Duramax Diesel V8 with Allison 3000 6-speed Automatic**

* **Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, rough idle, reduced power, increased emissions
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, black smoke from exhaust, whistling or whining noise
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Allison 1000 5-speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during shifts, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaks from transmission pan or gaskets, slipping or rough shifting
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission pan or gaskets, top up transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Allison 3000 6-speed Automatic**

* **Valve Body Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping
    * Solution: Replace valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, transmission not shifting into all gears
    * Solution: Replace solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, excessive body roll, instability while cornering
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per pair
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, dim or flickering lights, dead battery
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, difficulty starting, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Bedliner Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the plastic bedliner
    * Solution: Replace bedliner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust on Frame and Body**
    * Description/Symptoms: Surface rust or corrosion on metal components
    * Solution: Sand and repaint affected areas, use rust-resistant coating
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window not functioning properly, stuck or slow movement
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Instrument Cluster Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges not functioning or providing inaccurate readings
    * Solution: Replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2001 Chevrolet C6500 Kodiak is a durable and capable pickup truck, but it is not without its issues. Some of the more notable problems include intake manifold gasket failure, injector failure, torque converter failure, valve body failure, leaf spring breakage, and rust on the frame and body. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem and its extent.
