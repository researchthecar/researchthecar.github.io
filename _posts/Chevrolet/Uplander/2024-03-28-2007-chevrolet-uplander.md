**Introduction:**

The 2007 Chevrolet Uplander is a minivan available in LS, LT, and LTZ trim levels. It offers a choice of two engine options: a 3.5L V6 and a 3.9L V6. The 3.5L V6 produces 200 horsepower and 220 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.9L V6 generates 240 horsepower and 245 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Some positives of the Uplander include its spacious cabin, versatile seating, and comfortable ride. However, it has been criticized for its subpar handling and dated interior design.

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and engine damage.
    * Solution: Piston ring replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cam Phaser Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, and reduced fuel economy.
    * Solution: Cam phaser replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.9L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, and reduced power.
    * Solution: Intake manifold gasket replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission slipping out of gear during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front end, poor handling, and uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Front strut replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or complete headlight failure.
    * Solution: Headlight switch replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, battery drain, and difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Alternator replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative windshield wipers, especially during heavy rain or snowfall.
    * Solution: Wiper motor replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof.
    * Solution: Sunroof seal replacement or adjustment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or inability to operate one or more power windows.
    * Solution: Window motor or switch replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Chevrolet Uplander has several potential issues, including engine oil consumption, transmission slipping, suspension strut failure, and electrical problems. These issues can be costly to repair, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. While the Uplander offers a roomy cabin and comfortable ride, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase.
