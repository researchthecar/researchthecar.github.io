**Introduction**

The 1999 Chevrolet W5500 Tiltmaster is a heavy-duty commercial truck designed for hauling and construction applications. It is available with a range of engine and transmission options.

The W5500 Tiltmaster is praised for its powerful engines and towing capacity. However, it has also been criticized for its uncomfortable ride and lack of modern features.

**Engine**

* **6.6L Duramax Diesel Engine**

- **Fuel Injector Failure:** Injectors can clog or fail, leading to reduced power, rough idling, and increased emissions.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty fuel injectors.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
- **Recalls:** NHTSA Campaign ID: 09V239000

* **7.4L Vortec V8 Gasoline Engine**

- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing coolant leaks and a decrease in engine performance.
- **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold gasket.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1000+

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual Transmission**

- **Clutch Failure:** The clutch can wear out, leading to slippage and difficulty shifting gears.
- **Solution:** Replace the clutch and pressure plate.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1000 - $1500+

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

- **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** The transmission can develop leaks from the pan, torque converter, or lines.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking components.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1500+

**Suspension**

* **Front Leaf Springs:** The front leaf springs can break or sag over time, leading to a rough ride and poor handling.
- **Solution:** Replace worn or damaged leaf springs.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+

* **Rear Airbags:** The rear airbags can leak or fail, resulting in reduced load-carrying capacity and an unstable ride.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty airbags.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1200+

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Wiring Harness Damage:** The electrical wiring harness can become damaged or frayed, causing electrical issues throughout the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace damaged wiring.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:** The headlight switch can fail, causing the headlights to stay on or not come on when needed.
- **Solution:** Replace the headlight switch.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:** The HVAC blower motor can fail, leading to a lack of air conditioning or heating.
- **Solution:** Replace the blower motor.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $500+

* **Power Window Failure:** The power window motors can fail, making it difficult to operate the windows.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed motors.
- **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+

**Summary**

The 1999 Chevrolet W5500 Tiltmaster has a range of issues that can affect its performance, reliability, and comfort. While some issues, such as headlight switch failure, are relatively minor, others, like fuel injector failure and transmission fluid leaks, can be more expensive to fix. It is important to weigh the potential costs and benefits before purchasing this vehicle.
