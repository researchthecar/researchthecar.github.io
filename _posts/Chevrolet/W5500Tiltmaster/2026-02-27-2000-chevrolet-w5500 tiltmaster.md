### Standard System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming headlights, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Wiring Harness Issues**

### Summary Verdict
The 2000 Chevrolet W5500 Tiltmaster demonstrates reasonable reliability for a commercial vehicle of its era. The gasoline V6 and V8 engines are generally dependable with no significant widespread issues reported. The Isuzu diesel engine, while more powerful and efficient, can present issues like injector seal leaks and turbocharger problems, which are not uncommon for this type of powertrain and require attention. Transmission options, both automatic and manual, are typically robust. Electrical system components, such as the alternator and wiring harnesses, can experience failures, particularly with age and mileage. Rust and corrosion are potential concerns, especially for trucks operated in challenging environments. Overall, with proper maintenance and addressing specific diesel engine concerns, the W5500 Tiltmaster can remain a reliable workhorse.
