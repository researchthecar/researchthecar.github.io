**Introduction:**

The 2015 Chevrolet Silverado 2500 HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck available with two engine options: a 6.0L V8 or a 6.6L Duramax diesel V8. The 6.0L V8 produces 360 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.6L Duramax produces 445 horsepower and 910 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

Owners praise the Silverado 2500 HD for its powerful engine, towing and hauling capabilities, and comfortable interior. However, it is not without its faults, which we will detail below.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8**

**Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, reduced power
Solution: Replace fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
Recalls: NHTSA 19V585000

**6.6L Duramax V8**

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, black smoke from exhaust, whining noise from turbocharger
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $3500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

**Shifting Problems**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, delayed shifts
Solution: Replace transmission fluid and filter, or rebuild/replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $5000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Noise, vibration, and steering instability
Solution: Replace worn suspension components (e.g., control arms, ball joints, tie rods)
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, flickering gauges, battery not charging
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks off or becomes inoperable
Solution: Replace door handle
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: NHTSA 19V592000

**Interior**

**HVAC System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heater not working properly
Solution: Diagnose and repair HVAC system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 Chevrolet Silverado 2500 HD is a capable and powerful truck, but it has several known issues that can be costly to repair. Engine and transmission problems are the most severe and expensive, while suspension and electrical issues are more common but less severe. Overall, it is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before purchasing a Silverado 2500 HD.
