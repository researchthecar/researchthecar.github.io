**Introduction**

The 2007 Chevrolet Silverado 2500 HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck offered with a range of engine and transmission options. It features a 6.0L V8 engine with 360 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, or an optional 6.6L Duramax diesel V8 with 365 horsepower and 660 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 6-speed manual, 6-speed automatic, or Allison 5-speed automatic. The Silverado 2500 HD is praised for its towing and hauling capabilities, but some owners have reported issues with its engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idle, engine overheating
Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Piston Ring Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace piston rings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: 07V262000

**6.6L Duramax Diesel V8**

* **Fuel Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, poor engine performance, increased emissions
Solution: Replace fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, whistling or whining noise
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: 08V090000

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
Solution: Replace clutch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears
Solution: Repair or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
Recalls: 07V030000

* **Torque Converter Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during acceleration
Solution: Replace torque converter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Allison 5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts
Solution: Replace shift solenoid
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: 07V251000

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Bushing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, poor handling
Solution: Replace suspension bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Axle Seal Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking differential fluid
Solution: Replace rear axle seal
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions, dim lights
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, battery dies prematurely
Solution: Test and replace faulty electrical components
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Bed Liner Peeling**
Description/Symptoms: Bed liner material peeling off
Solution: Replace bed liner
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate Handle Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Tailgate handle breaks or becomes loose
Solution: Replace tailgate handle
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Cushion Stitching Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Stitching on seat cushions comes undone
Solution: Repair or replace seat cushions
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard material
Solution: Replace dashboard
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Chevrolet Silverado 2500 HD is a capable workhorse with strong towing and hauling capabilities, but it has been plagued by a number of issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The cost of repairs can vary widely depending on the severity of the issue, but some common problems can range from a few hundred dollars to upwards of $3,000+. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their decision-making process.
