**Introduction:**

The 2023 Ford Bronco Sport is a compact SUV that combines rugged off-road capability with everyday practicality. It is available with two engine options: a 1.5L EcoBoost inline-3 engine producing 181 horsepower and 190 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0L EcoBoost inline-4 engine producing 250 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic transmission for the 1.5L engine and a 10-speed SelectShift® automatic transmission for the 2.0L engine.

**Engine**

**1.5L EcoBoost Inline-3 Engine**

* **Stalling at Low RPMs**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine stalls when the vehicle is decelerating or coming to a stop.
    * Solution: Replacement of the throttle body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**2.0L EcoBoost Inline-4 Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rattling noises, excessive smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, or delayed shifts.
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission repairs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**10-Speed SelectShift® Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations or shuddering felt during acceleration or shifting.
    * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Breakage of the rear leaf spring, resulting in a loud noise and a loss of handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the leaf spring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

* **Front Strut Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or clunking noises coming from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replacement of the front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains quickly when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

* **Electrical Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues with lights, gauges, or other electrical systems.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Paint Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Peeling paint on the hood, bumpers, or other exterior surfaces.
    * Solution: Repainting the affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

* **Door Lock Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the door locks to lock or unlock.
    * Solution: Replacement of the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the climate control system to heat or cool the cabin.
    * Solution: Replacement of the blend door actuator or other climate control components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

* **Interior Panel Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibrating or rattling noises coming from the interior panels.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing loose components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
    * TSBs: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Ford Bronco Sport is a capable and versatile SUV with some notable issues. The most common problems are associated with the engine, transmission, and suspension. While these issues can be costly to fix, most of them are relatively minor and can be resolved with routine maintenance or repairs. Overall, the Bronco Sport is a well-rounded vehicle that offers a good balance of off-road capability and everyday practicality.
