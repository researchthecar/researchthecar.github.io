**Introduction:**

The 2023 GMC Canyon is a mid-size pickup truck available with a choice of two engines and two transmissions. The standard engine is a 2.5-liter four-cylinder with 200 horsepower and 191 lb-ft of torque. The optional engine is a 3.6-liter V6 with 308 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque. Both engines can be paired with either a six-speed automatic transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Canyon for its rugged good looks, spacious interior, and powerful engines. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **Timing chain failure:** The timing chain can stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or stall.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.6-liter V6**

* **Valve spring failure:** The valve springs can break, causing the engine to make a ticking noise and lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the valve springs and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Flush the transmission fluid and replace the transmission filter. In severe cases, the transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **No issues found.**

**Suspension**

* **No issues found.**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain excessively even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Inspect the electrical system for any parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on frame:** Some owners have reported rust forming on the vehicle's frame, particularly in areas exposed to salt and moisture.
    * Solution: Clean and apply a rust-resistant coating to the affected areas. In severe cases, the frame may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system freezing:** The infotainment system may freeze or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Reset the infotainment system by holding down the power button for 10 seconds.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 GMC Canyon is a capable and dependable pickup truck, but it has been known to experience some issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. While the severity of these issues can vary, some of them can be costly to repair. It's important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a Canyon so that you can make an informed decision.
