# 2016 GMC Sierra Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 GMC Sierra is a capable and popular full-size pickup truck known for its refined interior, strong towing capability, and comfortable ride. When shopping for a used model, buyers will find a robust lineup of powertrain options ranging from efficient V6 engines to powerful V8s. While overall owner satisfaction is high, used truck shoppers should review specific mechanical areas and check service records to ensure long-term dependability.

---

## Engine

### 4.3L V6 EcoTec3

This 4.3-liter V6 engine produces approximately 285 horsepower and 305 lb-ft of torque. It offers reliable daily drivability and respectable capability for light-duty truck buyers.

### 5.3L V8 EcoTec3

The 5.3-liter V8 engine generates around 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, serving as the most popular choice for balanced towing and everyday performance.

### 6.2L V8 EcoTec3

This high-output 6.2-liter V8 delivers 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, paired with strong hauling capability for demanding truck owners.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with standard engine options, providing smooth gear changes and proven mechanical durability.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission offered on higher trims and specific V8 configurations, optimizing fuel efficiency and power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V084000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standards (FMVSS) number 114, "Theft Protection", and/or 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unbelted driver is at a greater risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will update the radio software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V830000: Air Bags:Frontal:Sensor/Control Module-Inactive](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, the mounting stud used to secure the air bag sensing and diagnostic Module (SDM) may be incorrectly positioned, and as a result, the SDM housing could fracture. If the SDM housing fractures, water may get into SDM causing it to malfunction and hinder the proper deployment of the air bags in the event of a crash, thereby increasing.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will have the mounting stud repositioned and the SDM replaced, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2016 GMC Sierra remains a strong contender in the used full-size truck market, offering a comfortable cabin, strong towing numbers, and diverse powertrain choices.
