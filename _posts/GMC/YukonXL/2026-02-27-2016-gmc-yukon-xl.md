# 2016 GMC Yukon XL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 GMC Yukon XL offers generous passenger and cargo space alongside strong towing capabilities, making it a popular choice for large families and road trippers. Buyers exploring the used market should weigh its utility and comfortable ride against potential mechanical complexities, particularly concerning its powertrain offerings and electrical accessories.

---

## Engine

### 5.3L V8 EcoTec3

The 5.3L V8 EcoTec3 engine produces approximately 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, utilizing active fuel management and direct injection. It provides balanced performance and towing capability for daily driving, though buyers should monitor oil consumption and valvetrain operation.

### 6.2L V8 EcoTec3

The larger 6.2L V8 EcoTec3 engine delivers around 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and increased towing capacity. It provides strong overall performance but requires premium fuel and careful maintenance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the 5.3L engine options to deliver smooth shifting and dependable highway cruising. Routine fluid maintenance is recommended to help ensure long-term durability.

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with higher-tier models and the 6.2L engine, offering refined gear selection and improved efficiency. Prospective buyers should test drive thoroughly to check for smooth engagement across all gears.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon+XL&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V437000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon+XL&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** While being previously remedied for recall 16V-651, the affected vehicles may not have received the complete software update necessary to remedy the recall condition. Without the update, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V256000: Suspension:Front:Control Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon+XL&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Inadequate welds may allow the control arm to separate from the bushing, compromising steering and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the left and right front upper control arms and realign the vehicle, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 GMC Yukon XL remains a capable and spacious full-size SUV well-suited for heavy hauling and large families. While powertrain options provide strong performance, used car shoppers should conduct a thorough inspection of the engine operation, transmission shifting, and electrical systems before purchasing.
