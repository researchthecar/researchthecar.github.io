**Introduction**

The 2023 GMC Yukon XL is a full-size SUV offering a powerful V8 engine and a spacious interior. It comes standard with a 6.2L V8 engine producing 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, while a 3.0L Duramax turbo-diesel V6 engine producing 277 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque is available as an option. Both engines are paired with a 10-speed automatic transmission.

While owners appreciate the Yukon XL's powerful engine and roomy interior, some have encountered various issues with the vehicle, ranging from engine and transmission problems to electrical and suspension concerns.

**Engine**

**6.2L V8 Engine**

* **Piston ring failure:** Premature wear of the piston rings can lead to excessive oil consumption and engine knocking.
* **Solution:** Engine rebuild or replacement
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $4,000 - $10,000+

**3.0L Duramax V6 Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can prematurely fail, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel economy.
* **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,500 - $4,000

**Recalls:**
* NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V706000: Driveshaft corrosion may cause loss of power

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting issues:** Transmission may experience rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears, or refusing to shift.
* **Solution:** Transmission repair or replacement
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $5,000+

**Recalls:**
* None Found

**Suspension**

**Rear suspension:**

* **Sagging rear springs:** The rear suspension springs can sag prematurely, resulting in decreased ride height and handling issues.
* **Solution:** Spring replacement
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000

**Electrical**

**Electrical system:**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is parked.
* **Solution:** Electrical system diagnosis and repair
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* No Issues Found

**Interior**

* **Center console trim peeling:** The trim on the center console may peel or become discolored.
* **Solution:** Trim replacement
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400

**Summary**

While the 2023 GMC Yukon XL offers a powerful engine and a spacious interior, it has faced several issues affecting the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. These issues range in severity from minor inconveniences to potentially major repairs. Costs to fix these issues can be substantial, potentially reaching thousands of dollars. It is worth considering these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
