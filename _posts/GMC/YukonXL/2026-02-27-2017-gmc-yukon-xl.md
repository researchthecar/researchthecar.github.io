# 2017 GMC Yukon XL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 GMC Yukon XL is a large, capable full-size SUV that offers generous passenger and cargo space alongside strong towing capability. While popular for family hauling and road trips, used-car buyers should carefully inspect powertrain options and electronics, as owners have logged various owner-reported complaints regarding transmission performance and interior accessories.

---

## Engine

### 5.3L V8 ECOTEC3

The 5.3L V8 EcoTec3 engine produces approximately 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, paired with active fuel management and direct injection. It provides reliable daily performance when properly maintained, though buyers should monitor oil consumption and lifter operation.

### 6.2L V8 ECOTEC3

The larger 6.2L V8 EcoTec3 delivers 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and increased towing capacity. It is a powerful option that generally performs well, though it requires premium fuel and close attention to drivetrain health.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission comes paired with the standard 5.3L engine, offering smooth shifting and reliable highway cruising. Routine fluid changes are recommended to ensure long-term durability.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with select configurations, including the 6.2L V8, providing tighter gear ratios for improved power delivery. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly to check for smooth shifting behavior across all gears.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V651000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon+XL&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, certain driving conditions may cause the air bag sensing and diagnostic module (SDM) software to activate a diagnostic test. During this test, deployment of the frontal air bags and the seat belt pretensioners would not occur in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will reflash the SDM software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 GMC Yukon XL remains a spacious and versatile choice for buyers needing three rows of seating and heavy towing capacity. Prospective owners should perform a thorough test drive checking transmission behavior and electrical accessories to ensure everything operates as expected.
