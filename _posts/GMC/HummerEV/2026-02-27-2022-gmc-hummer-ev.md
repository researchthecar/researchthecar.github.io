# 2022 GMC Hummer EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 GMC Hummer EV is an ultra-heavy, high-performance electric supertruck showcasing immense capability and cutting-edge battery technology. As an early model year for an ambitious new platform, prospective used buyers should approach with careful consideration regarding early production software and electrical integration.

---

## Engine

### Ultium Drive Tri-Motor Electric Powertrain

Features a high-output multi-motor electric configuration producing around 1,000 horsepower, delivering massive acceleration and torque. Reliability for this early platform relies heavily on thermal management and inverter software stability.

## Transmission

### 1-Speed Direct-Drive Automatic Transmission

A specialized single-speed direct-drive system paired with the multi-motor architecture. This design minimizes mechanical complexity compared to traditional multi-gear setups, though seamless integration with electric motor torque delivery remains essential.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V141000: Exterior Lighting:Tail Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Hummer+EV&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." A taillight that fails to illuminate or illuminates incorrectly may reduce the visibility of the vehicle, or communicate incorrect information to other drivers, increasing the risk of a.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace as necessary, the left and/or right rear taillight assemblies. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V771000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Hummer+EV&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high voltage battery pack enclosure may not be properly sealed, allowing water to enter the pack. Water accumulation in the high voltage battery pack can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V367000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Hummer+EV&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the high-voltage battery pack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 GMC Hummer EV is a technological marvel that offers unmatched road presence and electric performance. Because it represents the introductory year of a complex new vehicle architecture, buyers are advised to verify that all manufacturer updates have been completed.
