# 2023 GMC Hummer EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 GMC Hummer EV represents an ultra-heavy, high-tech entry into the electric truck and SUV market, showcasing massive power outputs and unique features like CrabWalk. As a first-year production model of a complex EV architecture, prospective buyers should carefully weigh its cutting-edge capabilities against potential early-production software and electrical teething issues.

---

## Engine

### Ultium Drive Triple-Motor System

The triple-motor Ultium electric drive system pushes an estimated 1,000 horsepower, delivering staggering acceleration for the heavy chassis. It provides immense capability, though early software integration requires careful monitoring by buyers.

## Transmission

### 1-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

A specialized single-speed direct-drive gearbox handles the immense torque output from the Ultium electric motors. This system features fewer moving parts than a traditional multi-gear transmission, supporting a streamlined and direct power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V771000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Hummer+EV&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The high voltage battery pack enclosure may not be properly sealed, allowing water to enter the pack. Water accumulation in the high voltage battery pack can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V786000: Seat Belts:Front:Anchorage](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Hummer+EV&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The left or right front seat belt buckle attachment bolts may not be tightened properly. An improperly tightened seat belt buckle may not restrain an occupant as intended, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the left and right front seat belt buckle attachment bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 GMC Hummer EV is an engineering marvel that offers unmatched presence and electric performance, but early adopters should anticipate potential software glitches typical of first-year EV platforms. Buyers are advised to verify that all manufacturer updates have been completed prior to purchase.
