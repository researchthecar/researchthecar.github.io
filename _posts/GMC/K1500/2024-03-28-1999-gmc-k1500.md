**Introduction:**

The 1999 GMC K1500 is a full-size pickup truck offered with various engine and transmission options. It features a powerful V8 engine, robust suspension, and a spacious interior. Drivers appreciate its versatile capabilities and rugged design. However, like any vehicle, it has some common issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **5.3L Vortec V8**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, loss of engine power
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * **Valve cover gasket leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from valve covers
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets and bolts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * **P0420 catalytic converter efficiency code:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, reduced fuel economy
        * Solution: Replace catalytic converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
* **6.0L Vortec V8**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, loss of engine power
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * **Timing chain failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle, loss of power, stalling
        * Solution: Replace timing chain and gears
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
        * Recalls: 01V043000
    * **Engine knock:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Knocking sound from engine, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace engine bearings or pistons
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+

**Transmission**

* **4L60E automatic transmission**
    * **Overdrive gear slipping:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power in overdrive gear
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
        * Recalls: 99V069000
    * **No reverse gear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission does not engage in reverse
        * Solution: Replace transmission reverse gear planetary
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
    * **Shift solenoid failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoids
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* **NV3500 manual transmission**
    * **Clutch failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
        * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
    * **Transmission noise:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Grinding or whining noise from transmission
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission bearings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+

**Suspension**

* **Front upper control arm rust and failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corroded control arms, loss of suspension support
    * Solution: Replace front upper control arms
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: 99V126000
* **Leaf spring breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Broken leaf springs, uneven ride, decreased stability
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
* **Shock absorber failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride comfort, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dimming headlights
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Battery terminal corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting vehicle, electrical malfunctions
    * Solution: Clean or replace battery terminals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+
* **Headlight switch failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, intermittent lighting
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Exterior**

* **Fuel door cable failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening fuel door
    * Solution: Replace fuel door cable
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from dashboard
    * Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard mounting screws
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
* **Seat belt retractor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belt does not retract fully
    * Solution: Replace seat belt retractor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: 99V017000

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1999 GMC K1500 has some common issues that affect its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior components. While the severity and cost of these issues vary, owners should be aware of them to make informed decisions about maintenance and repairs. Regular inspections and timely repairs can help extend the vehicle's lifespan and minimize the impact of these issues.
