**Introduction:**

The 2004 GMC Envoy XL is an SUV available with a choice of two engine options and two transmission options. The 4.2L V6 engine produces 275 hp and 275 lb-ft of torque, while the 5.3L V8 engine produces 290 hp and 325 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic transmission and a 6-speed automatic transmission.

The Envoy XL is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engines. However, there have been several reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.2L V6 Engine**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idle, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing chain failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Valve cover gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks, engine noise
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.3L V8 Engine**

* **Active fuel management system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace or repair AFM solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Knock sensor failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, reduced performance
    * Solution: Replace knock sensors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Repair or rebuild transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Torque converter failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission control module failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting problems, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shift solenoid failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic shifting, delayed or no shifts
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Control arm failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arms
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Shock absorber failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, loss of handling
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies frequently, electrical issues
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors cannot be locked or unlocked
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Roof rack crossbar failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Crossbars detach or break
    * Solution: Replace crossbars
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control panel failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heater does not work, buttons not functioning
    * Solution: Replace climate control panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Dashboard rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise from dashboard while driving
    * Solution: Locate and secure loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2004 GMC Envoy XL has a number of reported issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, and suspension issues. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor annoyances and others requiring major repairs. The cost of repairs can also be significant, so it is important to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing this vehicle.
