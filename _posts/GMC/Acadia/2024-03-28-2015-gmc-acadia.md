**2015 GMC Acadia**

The 2015 GMC Acadia is a midsize crossover SUV available with three engine options: a 3.6L V6 (281 horsepower, 272 lb-ft of torque), a 2.5L inline-4 (194 horsepower, 188 lb-ft of torque), and a 3.6L V6 with direct injection (310 horsepower, 271 lb-ft of torque). Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic or a 9-speed automatic.

Owners praise the Acadia for its spacious cabin, comfortable seats, and user-friendly infotainment system. However, some have reported issues with various components.

**Engine**

* **3.6L V6**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating engine
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.5L inline-4**
    * **Oil consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Low oil levels, engine knocking
        * Solution: Replace piston rings and oil pan gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
        * Recalls: 14V460, 16V182
* **3.6L V6 with direct injection**
    * **Fuel injector failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, reduced power
        * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: 15V580

**Transmission**

* **6-speed automatic**
    * **Transmission slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **9-speed automatic**
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, delayed shifts
        * Solution: Update transmission software, replace transmission valve body
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: 15V955

**Suspension**

* **Front struts leaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, reduced ride comfort
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: 16V512

**Electrical**

* **Power window switch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working
    * Solution: Replace power window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: 15V571

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises from the dashboard area
    * Solution: Tighten loose screws, apply vibration-dampening material
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center console lid sticking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the center console lid
    * Solution: Lubricate or replace center console lid latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear liftgate struts weak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Liftgate not staying up
    * Solution: Replace liftgate struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 GMC Acadia has been subject to a number of issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While some of these issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly to repair. Overall, the Acadia is a reliable and versatile vehicle, but it is important to be aware of potential issues before making a purchase decision.
