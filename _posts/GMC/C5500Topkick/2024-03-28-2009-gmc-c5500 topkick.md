**2009 GMC C5500 Topkick**

The 2009 GMC C5500 Topkick is a heavy-duty commercial vehicle with a GVWR of 55,000 lbs. It is powered by a 6.6L Duramax diesel engine and a 6-speed Allison automatic transmission, producing 360 horsepower and 765 lb-ft of torque. Popular features of the Topkick include its payload and towing capacities, but it has received criticism for its cab comfort and lack of modern amenities.

    * Recalls: N/A
* **Turbocharger Failure:**

### Summary

The 2009 GMC C5500 Topkick has a few notable issues, particularly with its engine and transmission.
