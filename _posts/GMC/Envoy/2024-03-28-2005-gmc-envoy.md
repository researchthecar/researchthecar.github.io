**Introduction:**

The 2005 GMC Envoy is a midsize SUV known for its ruggedness and spacious interior. It offers two engine options: a 4.2L V6 and a 5.3L V8, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Envoy has been praised for its towing capacity, comfortable seats, and off-road capabilities. However, like any vehicle, it has some reported issues.

**Engine**

**4.2L V6**

- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, engine overheating, loss of power.
Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: #06V460000

**5.3L V8**

- **Active Fuel Management (AFM) Lifter Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Ticking or knocking noises, reduced engine performance, misfires.
Solution: Replace lifters and upgrade engine controller software to disable AFM.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

- **Torque Converter Shudder:**
Description/Symptoms: Vibrations when accelerating or shifting.
Solution: Replace torque converter.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Upper Control Arm Ball Joint Wear:**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, vibration, misalignment.
Solution: Replace upper control arms.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Ignition Lock Cylinder Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Key does not turn or ignition switch becomes stuck.
Solution: Replace ignition lock cylinder.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: #04V488000

**Exterior**

- **Fog Light Moisture Buildup:**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside fog lights.
Solution: Inspect and clean fog light assemblies.
Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A

**Interior**

- **Power Window Switch Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Power windows do not operate or operate intermittently.
Solution: Replace power window switch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

- **Instrument Cluster Backlight Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Dim or intermittent instrument cluster подсветка.
Solution: Replace instrument cluster or repair backlight.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2005 GMC Envoy has some reported issues, particularly with the engine (AFM lifter failure), transmission (torque converter shudder), and electrical (ignition lock cylinder failure). The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to major repairs that can be expensive. However, it's important to note that not all Envoys experience these issues, and the vehicle offers a reliable and capable combination of performance and comfort for its intended use.
