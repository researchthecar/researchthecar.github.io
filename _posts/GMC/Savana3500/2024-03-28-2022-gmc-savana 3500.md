**Introduction of the Vehicle**

The 2022 GMC Savana 3500 is a full-size van available with two engine options: a 6.6L V8 and a 6.0L V8. The 6.6L V8 produces 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque, while the 6.0L V8 produces 360 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers praise the Savana 3500 for its powerful engine, spacious cabin, and rugged durability. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle, affecting various components.

**Engine**

* **6.6L V8**

  * **Intake Manifold Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or leaks in the intake manifold, leading to reduced engine performance, increased emissions, and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall ID: 22V-157
  * **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty fuel injectors cause rough idling, loss of power, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+ per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

* **6.0L V8**

  * **No Major Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**

  * **Torque Converter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes, transmission overheating, and potential loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter and transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,800 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Springs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure or sagging of the rear leaf springs, leading to uneven handling and reduced load-carrying capacity.
    * Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+ per axle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Insufficient charging or premature failure of the alternator, leading to battery drainage and potential electrical problems.
    * Solution: Replacement of the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge when the vehicle is parked, indicating a potential electrical drain.
    * Solution: Tracing and repairing the source of the electrical drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Cracked Headlights**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature cracking or clouding of the headlight lenses, reducing visibility and appearance.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+ per assembly
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cruise Control Intermittently Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control not engaging or disengaging properly, leading to inconsistent driving.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the cruise control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Noisy Interior**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive road noise and wind noise entering the cabin, creating an uncomfortable driving experience.
    * Solution: Installation of sound dampening materials or replacement of faulty weatherstripping.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2022 GMC Savana 3500 has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While some owners have experienced major issues, others have reported minimal problems. The most common issues reported include intake manifold failure, torque converter failure, and cracked headlights. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific component failure and the extent of the repairs required.
