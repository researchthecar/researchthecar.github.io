**Introduction:**

The 2021 GMC Savana 3500 is a full-size van that offers a powerful V8 engine and a spacious interior. It's available with several engine and transmission options, including a 6.0L V8 paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission. Reviewers praise the Savana's spacious cargo area and comfortable seats, but criticize its outdated interior and lack of standard safety features.

**Engine:**

**6.0L V8**

* **Oil Pressure Sensor Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine oil pressure warning light illuminates, loss of engine power
    * Solution: Replace oil pressure sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust fumes enter the cabin, poor engine performance
    * Solution: Repair or replace exhaust manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission struggles to shift gears, hesitant or jerky shifts
    * Solution: Replace transmission valve body, flush transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, transmission slipping or overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension:**

* **Front and Rear Shock Absorbers:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar Links:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises over bumps, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, warning light illuminates
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles break or become difficult to operate
    * Solution: Replace door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Side Mirror Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Mirrors crack or become damaged in car washes
    * Solution: Replace side mirrors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard cracks or becomes discolored
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Lid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Center console lid breaks or does not open properly
    * Solution: Replace center console lid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 GMC Savana 3500 has several potential issues, including engine oil leaks, transmission hesitation, suspension concerns, and electrical problems. These issues can be costly to fix, but most are not considered major and should not prevent you from considering the Savana. Overall, the Savana is a reliable and capable van that is suitable for a variety of applications.
