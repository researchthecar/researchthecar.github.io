## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replace alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Power Window Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Windows not operating, slow operation, or getting stuck.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace window motor, regulator, or switch.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air conditioning or heat, or intermittent airflow from vents.
- **Solution:** Replace HVAC blower motor and resistor.

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 GMC Savana 3500 demonstrates solid overall reliability, particularly in its powertrain. The V8 engines and automatic transmissions are generally robust and designed for heavy use. The most commonly reported issues tend to be electrical, such as alternator failures and power window problems, which are not uncommon in vehicles of this age and type. Interior components, specifically the HVAC blower motor, can also require attention. Suspension and exterior components are typically durable. With proactive maintenance and attention to these specific electrical and HVAC concerns, the Savana 3500 can remain a dependable vehicle for demanding tasks.
