**Introduction**

The 2007 GMC Savana 3500 is a full-size van produced by General Motors. It is powered by a 6.0L Vortec LY6 V8 engine mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission, delivering 323 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque. The Savana 3500 is known for its spacious interior, reliable performance, and durability. However, like any vehicle, it has its fair share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **6.0L LY6 V8 Engine**

  * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Active Fuel Management (AFM) System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, noisy operation
    * Solution: Disable or repair AFM system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

  * **Slipping Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, transmission shuddering
    * Solution: Replace transmission or repair internal components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Torque Converter Lock-up Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, transmission overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after short periods of inactivity
    * Solution: Identify and repair electrical drain (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic draw)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the root cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Hood Latch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hood latches fail to engage or release, causing the hood to pop open
    * Solution: Replace hood latches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Belt Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belts retract too slowly or fail to latch
    * Solution: Replace seat belt components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400
    * Recalls: 10V-179

**Summary**

Overall, the 2007 GMC Savana 3500 is a reliable workhorse when properly maintained. However, it is not immune to certain issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the severity of the problem. Regular maintenance and timely repairs are crucial for extending the life of this vehicle.
