# 2017 GMC Savana 3500

### Overview
The 2017 GMC Savana 3500 is a full-size van designed for commercial and heavy-duty use, prioritizing utility and cargo capacity. Reliability for this model year generally aligns with expectations for a work vehicle, with most reported issues stemming from specific component failures rather than widespread design flaws. Owners often report satisfaction with its robust powertrain options and durable construction, suitable for demanding applications. However, like any vehicle, certain areas may require attention over time, particularly concerning electrical systems and specific engine components under heavy use. Regular maintenance is crucial for maximizing the longevity and performance of the Savana 3500.

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights flickering, loss of power to accessories, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty wiring harnesses, grounds, or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start after sitting, battery dies prematurely.
- **Solution:** Identify parasitic draw from faulty components or accessories, replace battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 GMC Savana 3500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The available V8 engines and the 6-speed automatic transmission are generally robust and well-suited for the demanding nature of a heavy-duty van. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are within the electrical system, with some reports of intermittent power problems and battery drain. These are often repairable with targeted diagnostics. The exterior and interior components are typically durable, reflecting the van's utilitarian design. Overall, the Savana 3500 is a dependable choice for commercial use, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential electrical gremlins are addressed promptly.
