### Summary Verdict
The 2018 GMC Savana 3500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The V8 engines and automatic transmission are generally robust and well-suited for the demands of a commercial van. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning intermittent power losses and potential battery drain, which are not uncommon in vehicles of this type and age. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free of widespread reported defects. With diligent maintenance and attention to potential electrical gremlins, the Savana 3500 is expected to provide dependable service.
