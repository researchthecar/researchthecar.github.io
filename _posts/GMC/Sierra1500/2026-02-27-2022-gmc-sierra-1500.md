# 2022 GMC Sierra 1500 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 GMC Sierra 1500 offers strong capability, modern styling, and an updated interior, making it a competitive choice in the full-size truck market. However, used-car buyers should carefully review maintenance records and previous repairs, as owner reports highlight potential reliability concerns ranging from powertrain issues to electrical and body hardware glitches.

---

## Engine

### 2.7L Turbo High-Output I4

This turbocharged 4-cylinder engine produces around 310 horsepower and 430 lb-ft of torque, providing strong low-end response for daily driving and light towing while balancing fuel efficiency.

### 5.3L EcoTec3 V8

A traditional small-block V8 pushing 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, offering reliable towing performance when properly maintained, though early production units require attention to potential mechanical wear.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle unexpectedly stalls while driving uphill or at highway speeds, sometimes without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis by a certified repair facility to identify root causes such as fuel delivery or sensor failures.

**Engine Knocking and Seizure**
- **Symptoms:** Loud knocking noises develop in the motor, accompanied by metal debris in the oil pan, low oil pressure warnings, and eventual engine failure.
- **Solution:** Often requires complete engine replacement or extensive internal rebuilding.

### 6.2L EcoTec3 V8

A high-output V8 delivering 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, tailored for maximum towing capacity and spirited acceleration.

### 3.0L Duramax Turbo-Diesel I6

A refined diesel option generating 277 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, prized by owners for exceptional fuel economy and strong low-end pulling power.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired primarily with the 2.7L turbo engine, this 8-speed automatic provides smooth shifting dynamics under normal operating conditions.

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

A sophisticated 10-speed automatic used across V8 and diesel trims to optimize fuel economy and power delivery, though select owners have reported valve body and mechanical locking issues.

**Valve Body and Rear Wheel Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden locking of the rear wheels while driving or shifting, transmission failure, and gear engagement hesitation accompanied by diagnostic trouble codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty valve body components or internal transmission parts by a dealership, often under warranty.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Trailer Control

Hydraulic braking systems designed for heavy towing workloads, though rare component failures require immediate professional inspection.

**Brake System Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Spongy brake pedal feel, failure of the truck to stop adequately, and warning messages on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Replace the master cylinder, gasket, and brake fluid reservoir.

## Electrical

### Telematics and Control Modules

Manages onboard connectivity and safety communication systems, with occasional reports of module failure requiring replacement.

**Telematics Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of OnStar connectivity, navigation services, and emergency communication features due to a failing control module.
- **Solution:** Replace the Telematics Control Module.
- **Cost:** $645

## Exterior

### Front Grille Deflector

Exterior trim elements subject to wind forces at high speeds, which have prompted manufacturer recalls due to detachment risks.

**Grille Deflector Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Front chrome or trim deflector loosens at highway speeds and detaches, potentially striking the windshield or damaging vehicle paint.
- **Solution:** Ensure all recall updates or mounting bracket reinforcements are properly installed by a dealer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V463000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The accessory sport bar contains a high-mounted brake light that may not function. As such, these vehicles may fail to conform to Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "lamps, reflective devices, and associated equipment." An inoperative high-mounted brake light can reduce visibility to other drivers and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the installation of the accessory bar and provide the correct vehicle wiring, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V903000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The daytime running lights (DRLs) may not deactivate when the headlights are on. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." DRLs that do not deactivate as intended may result in a glare, reducing visibility, and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 24V797000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Rear wheel lock-up can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install new transmission control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V060000: Structure:Body:Bumpers:Active Shutters/Grill](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached front grille deflector may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reinforce or repair all attachment points on the chrome front grille deflector, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V274000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Engine failure increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the engine, and as necessary, repair or replace the engine. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V083000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Sierra+1500&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Rear wheel lock up increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the vehicles and update the transmission control software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 GMC Sierra 1500 is a capable and comfortable modern truck, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough mechanical inspection before purchasing. Pay close attention to service history, verifying whether transmission valve body updates, brake system checks, and exterior trim recall repairs have been completed. Selecting a model with remaining powertrain warranty coverage offers extra peace of mind.
