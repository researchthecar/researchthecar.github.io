**Introduction:**

The 1996 GMC Savana 1500 is a full-size van available with multiple engine and transmission configurations. The base engine is a 4.3L V6 producing 190 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 5.0L V8 (200 horsepower, 300 lb-ft of torque), a 5.7L V8 (250 horsepower, 350 lb-ft of torque), and a 6.5L V8 diesel (170 horsepower, 380 lb-ft of torque). Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic (4L60E) and a 5-speed manual.

Owners appreciate the Savana's spacious interior, powerful engines, and versatility. However, some have encountered issues with various components.

**Engine**

**4.3L V6**
**Oil Leak From Rear Main Seal:**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the rear of the engine. Engine oil level decreases and can cause engine damage if not addressed.
Solution: Replace rear main seal.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**5.0L V8**
**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaks coolant into the cylinders, causing overheating and reduced engine power. May also result in white smoke from the exhaust.
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**5.7L V8**
**Head Gasket Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaks coolant into the cylinders, causing overheating and reduced engine power. May also result in white smoke from the exhaust and milky oil.
Solution: Replace head gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**6.5L V8 Diesel**
**Injector Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, white smoke from the exhaust, reduced power, and poor fuel economy.
Solution: Replace faulty injectors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+ per injector
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4L60E Automatic**
**Slipping:**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage gears properly, causing delayed or no acceleration.
Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Faulty Alternator:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery does not charge properly, leading to electrical system failure and difficulty starting the vehicle.
Solution: Replace alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Rusty Door Hinges:**
Description/Symptoms: Door hinges rust and seize, making it difficult to open and close the doors.
Solution: Lubricate or replace hinges.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Blower motor does not work, leading to poor ventilation and heating/cooling performance.
Solution: Replace blower motor resistor or blower motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1996 GMC Savana 1500 has some common issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. Engine issues range from leaks to gasket failures, while transmission issues include slipping and overheating. Electrical and interior problems are less common but can still occur. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue and the extent of damage.
