**Introduction**

The 2002 GMC Savana 1500 is a full-size van offered in various configurations, ranging from cargo to passenger models. It features a powerful engine with good towing capacity and a spacious interior. However, like any vehicle, it comes with its fair share of issues.

**Engine**

* **4.8L V8**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Leaks coolant, overheating, engine misfire
        * **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gasket
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
        * **Recalls:** 02V177000
    * **Power Steering Pump Failure**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty steering, whining noise from pump
        * **Solution:** Replace power steering pump
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **5.3L V8**
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Oil leak from valve covers
        * **Solution:** Replace valve cover gaskets
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Oil Pressure Sensor Failure**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** False low oil pressure readings, engine damage in severe cases
        * **Solution:** Replace oil pressure sensor
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **6.0L V8**
    * **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfire
        * **Solution:** Replace cylinder head gaskets
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
        * **Recalls:** 03V053000

**Transmission**

* **4L60-E 4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Failure**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Shifting issues, shuddering, stalling
        * **Solution:** Replace torque converter
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Transmission Seal Leak**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Fluid leaks from transmission seals
        * **Solution:** Replace transmission seals
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **4L80-E 4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Input Shaft Bearing Failure**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Grinding noises, difficulty shifting
        * **Solution:** Replace input shaft bearing
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Output Shaft Seal Leakage**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Fluid leaks from transmission rear seal
        * **Solution:** Replace output shaft seal
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Ball Joint Wear**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Clunking noises, steering instability
    * **Solution:** Replace ball joints
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Stabilizer Bar Link Wear**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Knocking noises, reduced handling
    * **Solution:** Replace stabilizer bar links
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Dimming lights, difficulty starting
    * **Solution:** Replace alternator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $600+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Dead battery after extended periods
    * **Solution:** Diagnose and repair cause of battery drain
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Fuse Panel Malfunction**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Blown fuses, electrical malfunctions
    * **Solution:** Replace fuse panel
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Lens Cracked**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Broken or cracked tail light lenses
    * **Solution:** Replace tail light assembly
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Sliding Door Roller Failure**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty opening or closing sliding doors
    * **Solution:** Replace sliding door rollers
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Panel Failure**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Malfunctioning climate control system
    * **Solution:** Replace climate control panel
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Dashboard Rattling**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Noises from dashboard trim
    * **Solution:** Tighten or replace loose trim pieces
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 GMC Savana 1500 has several potential issues to consider. While it offers a powerful engine and spacious interior, it comes with a range of concerns, particularly in the engine and transmission components. Some issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure and torque converter failure, can be costly to repair. Owners should be prepared for potential maintenance expenses to keep their vehicle running reliably.
