**Introduction:**

The 2014 GMC Savana 1500 is a full-size cargo van known for its spacious interior, powerful engine, and reliable drivetrain. It comes standard with a 4.8L V8 engine and a 6-speed automatic transmission, but a 6.0L V8 engine and an optional 4-speed automatic transmission are available.

People like the Savana 1500 for its versatility, durability, and strong towing capacity. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.8L V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, engine overheating, decreased performance
>* Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
>* Recalls: None Found

**6.0L V8**

* **Piston Ring Failure:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, reduced engine power, blue or white smoke from tailpipe
>* Solution: Replace piston rings and/or pistons
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
>* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed Shifting:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or slipping, delayed gear engagement
>* Solution: Replace transmission fluid and filter, repair or replace solenoid pack
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
>* Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder during acceleration or deceleration
>* Solution: Replace torque converter
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
>* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Premature Ball Joint Wear:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises while turning or going over bumps, loose or wobbling steering
>* Solution: Replace ball joints
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+ per side
>* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunction, flickering lights, battery not charging
>* Solution: Replace alternator
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
>* Recalls: 14V-410

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Undercarriage:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on metal parts under the vehicle
>* Solution: Treat with rust inhibitor, sand or sandblast and repaint affected areas
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
>* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattles:**
>* Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard, especially on rough roads
>* Solution: Tighten or replace loose components
>* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
>* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 GMC Savana 1500 is a versatile and capable van with potential issues that range from minor electrical glitches to more severe engine and transmission problems. While some repairs, such as dashboard rattles, are relatively inexpensive, others, like piston ring failure, can be costly. It's important to consider these potential issues when making a purchase decision and to keep up with regular maintenance to prevent or minimize them.
