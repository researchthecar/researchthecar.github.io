### Summary Verdict
The 2014 GMC Savana 1500 demonstrates a generally solid reliability record, particularly with its engine options, where no widespread issues are commonly reported. The primary areas of concern tend to be the transmission, specifically the older 4-speed automatic, which can experience torque converter problems. Electrical components like the alternator and power window regulators are also noted as potential failure points. However, these are often repairable with moderate cost. For owners prioritizing longevity, regular maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging symptoms, especially related to the transmission and electrical systems, are recommended. Overall, it remains a dependable choice for its intended purpose.
