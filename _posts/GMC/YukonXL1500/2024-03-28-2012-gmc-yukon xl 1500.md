**Introduction:**

The 2012 GMC Yukon XL 1500 is a full-size SUV offered with one engine and transmission option: a 5.3L V8 engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The V8 engine produces 320 horsepower and 335 lb-ft of torque. It has a spacious interior, comfortable seats, and a powerful engine. However, it also has some known issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

**5.3L V8**

* **Active Fuel Management Valve Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The AFM valve can fail, causing the engine to run rough and stall.
    * Solution: Replace the AFM valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: 13V010000

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold gasket can leak, causing a rough idle and decreased performance.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears or may shift erratically.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission solenoid pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: 12V317000

* **Transmission Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may leak fluid, which can cause slipping and decreased performance.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the leaking seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front suspension strut can fail, causing a clunking noise and reduced ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace the front suspension strut.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear shock absorbers can fail, causing a rough ride and decreased handling.
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator can fail, causing the battery to not charge and the vehicle to stall.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: 15V495000

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Solution: Test and repair or replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Tailgate Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate handle can break or become difficult to operate.
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper motor can fail, leaving the wipers inoperable.
    * Solution: Replace the windshield wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, causing improper heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headliner Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The headliner material can sag and become detached.
    * Solution: Replace the headliner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 GMC Yukon XL 1500 has several known issues that can affect its reliability and performance. The most common issues include engine valve failure, intake manifold gasket leaks, transmission shifting problems, suspension strut failures, electrical problems, tailgate handle failure, and interior climate control malfunctions. While the cost of these repairs can vary, they can be significant, and some may compromise the safety or reliability of the vehicle. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in the cost of potential repairs when considering this vehicle.
