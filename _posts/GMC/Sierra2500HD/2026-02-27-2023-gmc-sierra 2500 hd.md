### Summary Verdict
The 2023 GMC Sierra 2500 HD demonstrates solid reliability, especially concerning its powertrain. The 6.6L Duramax diesel engine, while generally robust, has a few reported issues related to the Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) system and glow plugs, which are not uncommon for modern diesel trucks. The gasoline engine and the Allison transmission are performing very well with no significant widespread problems. Electrical issues are primarily limited to infotainment system glitches, which are often resolved with software updates. Overall, the Sierra 2500 HD is a dependable choice for heavy-duty applications, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
