**Introduction:**

The 2016 GMC Sierra 2500 HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck that comes in a variety of configurations. It is powered by a choice of gasoline or diesel engines, and can be paired with a six-speed automatic or a ten-speed automatic transmission. The Sierra 2500 HD is known for its powerful engines, rugged construction, and spacious cabin. However, it has also been plagued by a number of issues.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8 Gasoline**

* **Fuel injector failure:** The fuel injectors can fail, causing the engine to run poorly or stall.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Duramax 6.6L V8 Diesel**

* **Emission system failure:** The emission system can fail, causing the engine to lose power and emit excessive exhaust emissions.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the emission system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 19V459000

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems:** The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears or may slip out of gear.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission control module or the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Ten-Speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation on takeoff:** The transmission may hesitate when taking off from a stop.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if under warranty, otherwise $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front end vibration:** The front end of the vehicle may vibrate at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Replace the front wheel bearings or the front axle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain prematurely.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear brake lights not working:** The rear brake lights may not work properly.
    * Solution: Replace the brake light switch or the brake light assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V413000

**Interior**

* **Radio display screen failure:** The radio display screen may fail or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Replace the radio display screen.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2016 GMC Sierra 2500 HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck that is known for its powerful engines and rugged construction. However, it has also been plagued by a number of issues, including engine problems, transmission problems, and electrical problems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem.
