### 6.6L Duramax Turbo-Diesel V8 (L5P)
**DEF System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light, reduced engine power, rough idling, stalling.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of Diesel Exhaust Fluid (DEF) tank, pump, or injector.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Turbocharger Lag/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of the turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 GMC Sierra 2500 HD demonstrates solid reliability, particularly with its gasoline engine and 10-speed automatic transmission, which show no widespread reported issues. The Duramax diesel engine, while powerful, has some documented potential concerns related to the DEF system and turbocharger, which are not uncommon for modern diesel powertrains and can be costly to repair if they arise. Electrical components, specifically the infotainment system, may experience occasional glitches. Overall, the Sierra 2500 HD is a dependable choice for heavy-duty needs, with most potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws across the entire model range.
