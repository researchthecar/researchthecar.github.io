# 2024 GMC Sierra 2500 HD Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 GMC Sierra 2500 HD is a heavy-duty pickup built to handle demanding towing and hauling workloads. While early owner feedback is generally limited, buyers evaluating a used model should examine the heavy-duty powertrain options and basic electrical systems to ensure they meet their trailering and reliability expectations.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L V8 Gas (L8T)

The standard 6.6-liter V8 gasoline engine produces approximately 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque. It provides dependable, conventional heavy-duty power without the added complexity of a diesel emissions system.

### 6.6L Duramax V8 Turbo-Diesel (L5P)

The optional 6.6-liter Duramax turbo-diesel V8 delivers around 470 horsepower and 975 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a heavy-duty Allison transmission for maximum towing capability.

**Engine Block Heater Concern**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported an issue shortly after plugging in the engine block heater to a standard outlet.
- **Solution:** Inspect the block heater cord, wiring harness, and connection points for damage or shorts.

## Transmission

### Allison 10-Speed Automatic

This 10-speed automatic transmission is standard across both gas and diesel engine configurations. It is designed to optimize power delivery and engine braking during heavy towing.
