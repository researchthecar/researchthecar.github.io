**Introduction:**

The 1996 GMC P3500 is a full-size pickup truck known for its robust performance and powerful engine options. It is powered by a 7.4L V8 engine that produces 300 horsepower and 454 lb-ft of torque. The P3500 comes equipped with a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed manual transmission. It features a spacious cabin, a sturdy exterior, and a towing capacity of up to 13,200 pounds. While the P3500 offers impressive capabilities, it has some reported issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine:**

**7.4L V8 Engine**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Leakage of coolant and oil, rough idling, engine overheating
* Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**TCC Solenoid Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, delayed shifting, hard shifting
* Solution: Replacement of the TCC solenoid
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Synchronizer Ring Wear:**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises during shifting
* Solution: Replacement of the synchronizer rings
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

**Front Ball Joint Wear:**
* Description/Symptoms: Loose steering, clunking noises during turns
* Solution: Replacement of the ball joints
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per ball joint
* Recalls: None Found

**Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
* Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, decreased towing capacity
* Solution: Replacement of the leaf springs
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per leaf spring
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

**Generator Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, battery not charging
* Solution: Replacement of the generator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

**Air Conditioning Compressor Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Reduced cooling performance, no cool air
* Solution: Replacement of the air conditioning compressor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

**Door Handle Breakage:**
* Description/Symptoms: Door handle not opening or closing properly
* Solution: Replacement of the door handle
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100+ per door handle
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1996 GMC P3500 is a capable pickup truck with some reported issues. While some components, such as the intake manifold gasket and transmission solenoids, may experience failures, many of the issues are related to wear and tear or heavy usage. Overall, the severity of the issues is moderate, with repair costs ranging from low to medium. Regular maintenance and proper care can help extend the life of the vehicle and mitigate the potential for costly repairs.
