## 1997 GMC T7500

The 1997 GMC T7500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck with a GVWR of 26,000 lbs. It features a 7.4L V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The T7500 has a towing capacity of 12,000 lbs. and a payload capacity of 5,900 lbs. Many drivers praise the truck for its power and towing capabilities. However, it is known to have some common issues.

### Engine
**7.4L V8 Engine**
* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Gaskets deteriorate over time, causing coolant or oil leaks and overheating.
* Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, overheating engine.
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets.
* Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $800+
* Recalls: None found

### Transmission
**4-speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Transmission slipping:** Transmission may slip gears when shifting, resulting in loss of power or inability to move.
* Symptoms: Slipping gears, difficulty shifting, delayed shifting.
* Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
* Estimated cost to fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None found

### Suspension
* **Leaf spring breakage:** Leaf springs can crack or break, causing the truck to sag and ride rough.
* Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride.
* Solution: Replace leaf springs.
* Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $800 per spring

### Electrical
* **Battery drain:** Vehicles may experience a battery drain due to faulty components or wiring.
* Symptoms: Battery drains overnight or after extended periods of inactivity.
* Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components.
* Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500+

### Exterior
* **Door handle failure:** Door handles may become loose or break, making it difficult to open doors.
* Symptoms: Loose or broken door handles.
* Solution: Replace door handles.
* Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $200 per handle

### Interior
* **Cruise control failure:** Cruise control may malfunction or become inoperable.
* Symptoms: Cruise control does not engage or disengages randomly.
* Solution: Replace cruise control module or repair wiring.
* Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $500
* Recalls: 04V170000

### Summary
The 1997 GMC T7500 is a capable heavy-duty pickup truck with strong towing and payload capacities. However, it is known to have some common issues with the engine (intake manifold gasket failure), transmission (slipping gears), and electrical system (battery drain). The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the extent of the problem and the necessary repairs.
