The 2022 GMC Savana 4500 is a full-size cargo van known for its robust capabilities and utilitarian design. It's favored for its ample cargo space, durability, and relatively simple mechanicals compared to more modern vans. Engine options for the 2022 Savana 4500 include a 4.3L V6 engine producing 276 horsepower and 298 lb-ft of torque and a 6.6L V8 engine producing 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a heavy-duty 6-speed automatic transmission. Many appreciate the Savana's straightforward design for its ease of maintenance and repair, while some criticize its dated interior and relatively poor fuel economy.

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Leaf Spring Sag**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle noticeably lower than the front, especially when loaded. Can cause uneven tire wear and reduced handling performance.
    *   Solution: Replace the rear leaf springs. Consider heavy-duty or reinforced leaf springs for increased load capacity and durability.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+ depending on parts and labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery is dead or significantly drained, especially after sitting for a few days. Can be caused by a parasitic draw from a faulty module or accessory.
    *   Solution: Identify the source of the parasitic draw by using a multimeter to test each circuit. Repair or replace the faulty component.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ depending on the complexity of the issue.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Malfunctioning Sensors**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Intermittent or constant triggering of warning lights such as ABS, Traction Control, or Check Engine Light.
    *   Solution: Scan the vehicle's computer for Diagnostic Trouble Codes (DTCs). Replace the faulty sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per sensor replaced.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Side Door Latch Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing the side cargo doors. Latch mechanism may become sticky or require excessive force to operate.
    *   Solution: Lubricate the latch mechanism with silicone spray. If the problem persists, replace the latch assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+ depending on if lubrication works or if replacement is needed.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The HVAC blower motor stops working, resulting in no airflow from the vents. May only fail on certain speed settings initially.
    *   Solution: Replace the blower motor. Inspect the blower motor resistor for damage and replace it if necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 GMC Savana 4500 appears to have relatively few widespread, significant issues. The most commonly reported problems relate to suspension, electrical systems, and door latch mechanisms. The severity of these issues ranges from minor inconveniences to more impactful problems that could affect the vehicle's reliability and safety. Repair costs vary depending on the specific problem, but generally fall within a moderate range.
