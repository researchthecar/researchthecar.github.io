## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights flicker, accessories fail to operate intermittently.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair faulty wiring, loose connections, or failing relays. Battery and alternator checks are also recommended.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminates, dimming headlights, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Replace the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Doors may not lock or unlock properly, or may not stay closed.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the door latch mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Lighting
**Headlight Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture buildup inside headlight assemblies.
- **Solution:** Seal any cracks or faulty seals in the headlight housing. Replacement of the headlight assembly may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air or reduced airflow from vents, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replace the blower motor and/or blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


### Seating
**Seat Adjustment Mechanism Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty adjusting or locking driver/passenger seats in position.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or replace the seat adjustment rails and locking mechanisms.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 GMC Savana 4500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain components, which are generally robust and well-suited for demanding commercial use. The V8 engines and automatic transmissions have a good track record. Areas that may require attention over time include the electrical system, with potential for intermittent power issues and alternator failures. Exterior components like door latches and interior systems such as the HVAC blower motor are also noted as potential points of concern. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing minor electrical or mechanical quirks promptly, the Savana 4500 can be a dependable work vehicle.
