### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights flickering, accessories not functioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair wiring harnesses, check battery terminals and connections, test alternator output.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Faulty Alternator**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dim headlights, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replace the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 GMC Savana 4500 demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission, which are generally robust for commercial duty. The chassis and suspension are built to withstand heavy use. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically related to the alternator and intermittent power issues, which are not uncommon in vehicles with extensive electrical loads. Exterior and interior components are typically durable. Overall, the Savana 4500 is a dependable work vehicle, with most potential issues being manageable and repairable, especially with proactive maintenance.
