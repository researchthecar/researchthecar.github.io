**Introduction:**

The 2016 GMC Savana 4500 is a full-size van that is available with various engine and transmission options. The available engine options include:

* 6.0L V8 gasoline engine (345 horsepower and 429 lb-ft of torque)
* 6.6L V8 Duramax turbo-diesel engine (397 horsepower and 765 lb-ft of torque)

The available transmission options include:

* 6-speed automatic transmission
* 10-speed automatic transmission (6.6L Duramax turbo-diesel engine only)

The 2016 GMC Savana 4500 is known for its spacious interior, powerful engine options, and towing and hauling capabilities. However, some owners have experienced issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**6.0L V8 Gasoline Engine**

* **Leaking intake manifold:** Leaks at the gasket, causing coolant and oil leaks. Replace intake manifold gaskets. $500 - $800+

**6.6L V8 Duramax Turbo-Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel injector failure:** Injectors fail, causing engine misfires, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption. Replace fuel injectors. $2,000 - $2,500+
* **Turbocharger failure:** Turbocharger fails, resulting in loss of power and increased fuel consumption. Replace turbocharger. $3,000 - $4,000+

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting problems:** Transmission slips or hesitates when shifting. Rebuilt or replace transmission. $2,000 - $3,000+

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No issues found**

**Suspension**

* **Rear leaf spring breakage:** Leaf springs break, causing the rear of the vehicle to sag. Replace leaf springs. $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Battery drains quickly due to parasitic power draw. Check and replace faulty components. $100 - $500+
* **Alternator failure:** Alternator fails, causing the battery to discharge. Replace alternator. $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Rust on body panels:** Body panels rust prematurely, especially on the rear doors and tailgate. Repair or replace rusted panels. $500 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracks:** Dashboard develops cracks, especially on the passenger side. Replace dashboard. $500 - $1,000+
* **HVAC system failure:** HVAC system fails, causing poor air conditioning or heating. Repair or replace faulty components. $300 - $800+

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 GMC Savana 4500 is a capable vehicle that offers spaciousness, power, and hauling abilities. However, some owners have encountered issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. The severity and cost of the repairs vary, but some issues, such as fuel injector failure or turbocharger failure, can be costly to fix.
