## Suspension

### Heavy-Duty Chassis
**Leaf Spring Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity, rougher ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, poor handling, excessive body roll.
- **Solution:** Replacement of shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 GMC Savana 4500 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The available V8 gasoline and Duramax diesel engines, along with the robust Allison and 6L90 transmissions, are well-regarded for their durability in commercial applications. Potential areas for concern, common to heavy-duty vans, include suspension components like leaf springs and shock absorbers due to constant load-bearing, and electrical systems which can be complex with various upfits. Routine maintenance and inspection of these areas are recommended to ensure continued dependable service.
