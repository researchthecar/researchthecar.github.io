## 2017 GMC Savana 4500

The 2017 GMC Savana 4500 is a full-size van offered with a range of engine and transmission options. It is known for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and impressive towing capacity. However, like any vehicle, it has also been subject to various reported issues.

**Engine**

* **6.0L V8**
    * **Engine Stalling:** Engine stalls while driving, often due to a faulty fuel pump.
    * Symptoms: Sudden loss of power, engine warning light
    * Solution: Replace fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID 21T002
* **6.6L Duramax Diesel V8**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Turbocharger fails, resulting in reduced engine power and increased smoke from the exhaust.
    * Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slippage:** Transmission slips when shifting gears, causing rough or delayed shifting.
    * Symptoms: Slipping gears, shuddering, check engine light
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Delayed Gear Shifts:** Transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears.
    * Symptoms: Slow or erratic shifting, check engine light
    * Solution: Update transmission control module software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Springs:** Premature wear or breakage of leaf springs.
* Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride, tire alignment issues
* Solution: Replace leaf springs
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Symptoms: Dead battery, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Identify and repair source of drain (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic draw)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Lighting Issues:** Malfunctioning headlights, taillights, or turn signals.
    * Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, intermittent operation
    * Solution: Replace bulbs, repair wiring, or update electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Locks Malfunctioning:** Power door locks fail to operate properly.
    * Symptoms: Doors won't lock or unlock, key fob issues
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators or door lock control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Side Mirrors Vibrating:** Side mirrors vibrate excessively at high speeds.
    * Symptoms: Blurry or distorted view from side mirrors
    * Solution: Replace side mirror assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Failure:** Heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system malfunctions, resulting in inadequate temperature control.
    * Symptoms: Poor airflow, insufficient heating or cooling, leaks
    * Solution: Repair or replace HVAC components (e.g., blower motor, condenser)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise Control Deactivation:** Cruise control randomly deactivates while driving.
    * Symptoms: Cruise control light blinks or turns off
    * Solution: Update software or replace cruise control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 GMC Savana 4500 has a number of reported issues affecting various components. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more complex and costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a vehicle of this model year.
