**2022 GMC Savana 4500**

The 2022 GMC Savana 4500 is a full-size cargo van offered with various engine and transmission options. With a 6.6L V8 engine producing 401 horsepower and 464 lb-ft of torque, this van boasts impressive hauling and towing capabilities. Despite its workhorse nature, the Savana 4500 also features comfortable seating, advanced safety features, and a user-friendly infotainment system.
