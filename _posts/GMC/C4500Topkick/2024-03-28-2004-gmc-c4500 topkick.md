**Introduction:**

The 2004 GMC C4500 Topkick is a heavy-duty commercial truck offered with a 300-horsepower 8.1L V8 engine mated to a 6-speed Allison automatic transmission. It boasts a payload capacity of up to 17,500 pounds and a towing capacity of 24,500 pounds. While many owners praise its reliability and durability, others have reported concerns with certain components.

**Engine**

* **8.1L V8**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idling, and reduced engine performance
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Allison**

**Valve Body Issues**
* Description/Symptoms: Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, and transmission slipping
* Solution: Replace or repair valve body
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500

**Suspension**

**Leaf Spring Breakage**
* Description/Symptoms: Broken leaf springs, sagging suspension, and reduced ride comfort
* Solution: Replace leaf springs
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Steering**

**Steering Rack Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Loss of steering control, difficulty turning, and excessive play in steering wheel
* Solution: Replace steering rack
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500

**Electrical**

**Alternator Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, dim headlights, and electrical system issues
* Solution: Replace alternator
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**Exterior**

**Tailgate Hinge Corrosion**
* Description/Symptoms: Rusted tailgate hinges, difficulty opening and closing tailgate
* Solution: Replace tailgate hinges
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Interior**

**Climate Control Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, lack of heating or cooling
* Solution: Repair or replace climate control module
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600

**Recall History:**

* None Found

**Summary:**

The 2004 GMC C4500 Topkick has some reported issues, but the severity and cost of these issues vary widely. Intake manifold gasket failure and valve body issues can be relatively inexpensive to fix, while steering rack failure and transmission problems can be costly. Overall, the C4500 is a capable heavy-duty truck, but potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
