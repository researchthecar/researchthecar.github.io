# 2021 GMC Yukon Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 GMC Yukon offers a spacious cabin, impressive towing capabilities, and advanced technology features that make it a compelling choice in the full-size SUV market. However, prospective buyers should review owner complaints carefully, as this model year exhibits a notable cluster of electrical, engine, and powertrain issues ranging from fuel pump failures to unexpected transmission shifts.

---

## Engine

### 5.3L V8 Engine

The standard 5.3-liter V8 engine produces 355 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, providing solid everyday utility and towing capability for most families.

**Loss of Motive Power and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle experiences sudden loss of power, fails to accelerate properly, or stalls unexpectedly while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing by a qualified mechanic or dealership is required to address underlying fuel module or electrical faults.

### 6.2L V8 Engine

The available 6.2-liter V8 engine generates 420 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration and robust performance for heavy-duty hauling.

**Electrical Warning Lights and Drivetrain Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of warning lights on the dashboard accompanied by abnormal vehicle shaking or loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Inspect powertrain components and electronic control modules for fault codes and update software as needed.

### 3.0L Duramax Turbo-Diesel I6

The 3.0-liter Duramax turbo-diesel inline-six produces 277 horsepower and 460 lb-ft of torque, offering excellent fuel economy and strong low-end torque for long-distance cruising.

**Fuel Pump and Propulsion Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start or experiences sudden propulsion loss while in motion without immediate warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty fuel pump modules or control units as diagnosed by a certified service center.

### Fuel System

Manages fuel delivery from the tank to the engine to ensure consistent performance across all driving conditions.

**Fuel Pump Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine cranks but fails to start, requiring towing to a repair facility.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed fuel pump module and associated control hardware.

## Transmission

### 10-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 10-speed automatic transmission pairs across all engine options to optimize gear ratios for fuel efficiency and smooth highway performance.

**Abrupt Failure and Shifter Defects**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission slips, experiences sudden mechanical failure, or unexpectedly shifts into neutral or park while operating.
- **Solution:** Inspect the valve body, transfer case actuator, or internal transmission components; replacement or major rebuilding may be necessary.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension System

Provides adjustable ride height and damping control to enhance passenger comfort and load leveling.

**Cold Weather Air Suspension Fault**
- **Symptoms:** Suspension fails to operate properly in sub-freezing temperatures, causing excessive bouncing and an unstable ride.
- **Solution:** Inspect air struts, compressors, and control lines for moisture or mechanical failure during dealer service visits.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Body Control Modules

Controls cabin electronics, displays, and accessory power distribution throughout the vehicle.

**Infotainment Freeze and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of infotainment system functionality in cold weather or low battery voltage triggering safety restraint warnings.
- **Solution:** Reboot or replace faulty control modules, or replace weakened batteries.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Provides overhead natural light and ventilation with an integrated power sunshade.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof glass suddenly explodes or shatters while driving without any external impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V455000: Steering:Steering Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A software issue may cause a loss of power steering assist at startup or while driving. Increased steering effort, particularly at lower speeds, increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V036000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Driveshaft failure can cause a sudden loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left and/or right rear driveshaft assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V579000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Electric:Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Overall braking performance may be compromised, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will recalibrate the brake system control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V811000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** If an incorrect bolt was used, the seat belt may not be properly attached to the vehicle. If a seat belt assembly is not properly attached to the vehicle, the seat belt may not properly restrain an occupant in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the suspect bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V064000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V735000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The attachment bolts for the front seats may not have been properly reinstalled after the repair was completed. If an occupied front seat has loose or missing attachment bolts, it may move during a crash, increasing the risk of injury to the seated occupant.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the seat attachments for both front seats and install attachment bolts as needed, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V739000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The fuel pump power control module may cause the fuel pump to fail or operate intermittently, which can interrupt fuel flow to the engine, and cause an unexpected engine stall. An engine stall increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel pump power control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V421000: Air Bags:Srs Malfunction Warning Lamp/Light](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Yukon&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The air bag malfunction indicator light may fail to warn the driver of a problem with the air bag, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 GMC Yukon is a capable and versatile full-size SUV, but used car buyers should perform a thorough inspection of its electrical systems, transmission operation, and fuel pump components before purchasing. Verifying service history and ensuring any remaining warranty coverage is active can provide valuable peace of mind against potential mechanical and electronic repair bills.
