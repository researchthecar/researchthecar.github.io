# 2000 GMC Yukon

### Overview
The 2000 GMC Yukon, a full-size SUV, was generally considered a robust vehicle for its era, particularly favored for its towing capacity and spacious interior. It offered a choice of powerful V8 engines designed for demanding use. While many owners reported good long-term reliability, like any vehicle of this age, certain components are more prone to issues than others. Common areas of concern often relate to the powertrain, particularly with higher mileage, and some electrical components can develop faults. However, with regular maintenance, the Yukon can remain a dependable option for many.

---


## Engine

### 5.3L V8 Vortec
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, rough idle, engine misfires, sweet smell of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Oil Cooler Line Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks around the front of the engine, low oil pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500


### 4.8L V8 Vortec
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, rough idle, engine misfires, sweet smell of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


### 6.0L V8 Vortec
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, rough idle, engine misfires, sweet smell of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Transmission

### 4L60-E Automatic
**Torque Converter Clutch (TCC) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping, shuddering, failure to engage or disengage, P0740 code.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the transmission, or specific TCC solenoid replacement.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed shifting, slipping, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, installation of an auxiliary transmission cooler.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Ball Joint Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, steering looseness.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Worn Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Squeaking or creaking noises, vague steering feel, instability.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or sway bar bushings.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows, locks, or interior lights malfunctioning intermittently or failing completely.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels
**Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Corrosion on fenders, rocker panels, and wheel wells, especially in rust-belt regions.
- **Solution:** Rust repair and repainting, or replacement of affected panels.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+


### Headlight Lenses
**Clouding/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced light output, hazy appearance.
- **Solution:** Headlight restoration kit or replacement of headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400


## Interior

### HVAC Blower Motor
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, or intermittent fan speed.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### Seat Actuators
**Power Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats fail to move forward, backward, or adjust height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2000 GMC Yukon is a capable and spacious SUV that can offer good reliability if well-maintained. Key areas to inspect and potentially address include the intake manifold gaskets on V8 engines, potential transmission issues like torque converter problems, and common suspension wear items such as ball joints and bushings. Electrical gremlins, particularly related to the BCM, can also arise. Exterior rust is a concern in certain climates. Overall, it's a solid choice for those seeking a large, older SUV, but buyers should be aware of these potential repair points and factor them into ownership costs.
