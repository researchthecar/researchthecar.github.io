## Electrical

### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion/Abrasion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, warning lights, power loss to components.
- **Solution:** Inspection, cleaning of connections, repair or replacement of damaged sections.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming headlights, engine stalling due to lack of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels
**Rust/Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust formation, particularly on lower body panels, wheel arches, and frame components, especially in areas with harsh climates.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500+


### Headlights/Taillights
**Condensation/Clouding**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture ingress leading to fogging inside lenses, reduced light output, potential bulb failure.
- **Solution:** Sealing repair or replacement of light assemblies.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air circulation from vents, or intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


### Dashboard Gauges
**Intermittent Functionality**
- **Symptoms:** Fuel gauge, speedometer, or tachometer not reading accurately or intermittently failing.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of wiring or replacement of gauge cluster.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 GMC W5500 Forward demonstrates a generally reliable profile for its intended commercial use. Major powertrain issues are not commonly reported, suggesting the available engine and transmission options are well-suited for the vehicle's demands. Potential concerns tend to lie in areas common to many vehicles of this age and type, such as electrical system integrity (wiring harness corrosion) and exterior bodywork susceptible to rust in certain environments. Interior components like the HVAC blower motor and dashboard gauges can also present issues over time. Proactive maintenance and inspection, particularly for electrical connections and body rust, are recommended to ensure continued dependable operation.
