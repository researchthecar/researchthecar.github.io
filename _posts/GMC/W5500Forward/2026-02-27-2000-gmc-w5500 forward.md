## Electrical

### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion and Shorts**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical failures, warning lights, non-functional accessories.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair/replace damaged wiring sections. Clean corroded connectors.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming headlights, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replace alternator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Exterior

### Body Panels
**Rust and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust, particularly around wheel wells, door bottoms, and frame.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment and repair, panel replacement if severe.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500+


### Headlights/Taillights
**Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture buildup inside light housings.
- **Solution:** Seal housing or replace unit.
- **Cost:** $50 - $200 per unit


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air or reduced airflow from vents.
- **Solution:** Replace blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $400


### Dashboard Gauges
**Intermittent Functionality**
- **Symptoms:** Gauges (speedometer, tachometer, fuel) not working or providing erratic readings.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace instrument cluster or relevant sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800
