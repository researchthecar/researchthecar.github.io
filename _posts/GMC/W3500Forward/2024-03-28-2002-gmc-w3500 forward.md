**Introduction**

The 2002 GMC W3500 Forward is a heavy-duty pickup truck powered by the 6.6L Duramax LB7 V8 engine that produces 300 horsepower and 520 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 4-speed automatic transmission (4L85E). This vehicle is known for its robust towing and hauling capabilities but has also been affected by several known issues.

**Engine**

* **6.6L Duramax LB7 V8 Engine**

**Injector Sticking/Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Fuel injectors may stick or fail, resulting in poor engine performance, increased emissions, and decreased fuel economy.
* Solution: Replace the faulty injectors.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail prematurely, leading to reduced engine power and increased exhaust smoke.
* Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-speed 4L85E Automatic Transmission**

**Transmission Slippage**
* Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting, causing delayed acceleration or difficulty maintaining speed.
* Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
* Solution: Identify and fix the source of the drain, such as a faulty accessory or electrical connection.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in inconsistent or ineffective heating or cooling.
* Solution: Repair or replace the faulty climate control module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2002 GMC W3500 Forward is a capable and reliable heavy-duty truck. However, it has been subject to several common issues, primarily related to the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, so potential buyers should thoroughly research and inspect any used examples they consider purchasing.
