**2016 GMC Terrain**

The 2016 GMC Terrain is a compact crossover SUV with a modern design, spacious cabin, and advanced safety features. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 2.4L I-4 and a 3.6L V6, paired with a six-speed or nine-speed automatic transmission, respectively. The Terrain offers a comfortable ride, impressive fuel economy, and responsive handling. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.4L I-4**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine burns excessive oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

* **Timing Chain Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise is heard from the engine on startup, acceleration, or deceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioners.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or slips when shifting gears, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Replace or reprogram the transmission control module (TCM).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Nine-Speed Automatic**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A clunking or rattling noise is heard from the front suspension over bumps or uneven roads.
    * Solution: Replace the lower control arms or sway bar end links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical systems may malfunction, including the headlights, taillights, or power windows.
    * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Liftgate Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear liftgate fails to open or close properly, or leaks water into the cargo area.
    * Solution: Replace or adjust the liftgate latch or weatherstripping.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument Panel Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The instrument panel displays incorrect information or malfunctions completely.
    * Solution: Replace or reprogram the instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Heater and Air Conditioning Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The heating or air conditioning system does not function properly, or emits unpleasant odors.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components, such as the blower motor, compressor, or evaporator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 GMC Terrain has received mixed reviews from owners. While some have praised its comfort, fuel efficiency, and safety features, others have experienced various issues, including engine problems, transmission issues, suspension noise, and electrical failures. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific problem.
