# 2024 GMC Terrain Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 GMC Terrain brings modern styling and everyday practicality to the compact SUV segment, but early owner reports highlight some notable mechanical and electrical concerns. While many drivers enjoy its comfortable ride and features, prospective buyers should review potential powertrain and safety system glitches documented in early ownership logs.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 1.5L turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 175 horsepower and 203 lb-ft of torque, offering adequate efficiency for daily commuting. It serves as the standard powerplant for the lineup, though buyers should watch for reported fuel delivery concerns.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling while driving, sometimes accompanied by high pressure fuel pump issues or loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the high-pressure fuel system and replace failing fuel pumps or sensors as needed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with standard front-wheel drive or available all-wheel drive, the 9-speed automatic transmission aims to balance smooth highway cruising with responsive gearing.

**Transmission Fluid Leaks and Internal Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced transmission fluid leaks from areas like the torque converter seal, as well as sudden gear loss or erratic shifts requiring major component replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect the transmission housing and seals for leaks; severely damaged units may require professional rebuild or complete replacement.

**Acceleration Lag and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may exhibit hesitation or a noticeable delay when pressing the accelerator pedal before successfully engaging a gear.
- **Solution:** Check for available powertrain control module updates or software recalibrations at an authorized service center.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Electronic power steering provides light effort at low speeds and firm road feel on the highway, but occasional electrical faults can temporarily disable assist functions.

**Service Power Steering Warning**
- **Symptoms:** A dashboard warning may illuminate alongside a sudden loss of power steering assistance, making the steering wheel difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic power steering module and electrical connections diagnosed by a professional technician.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and HVAC Electronics

Cabin electronics manage major comfort and safety controls, though screen blackouts and climate control anomalies have occasionally been reported.

**Infotainment Screen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** The central display screen may intermittently go blank or fail, disrupting access to key vehicle controls and safety functions.
- **Solution:** Perform a system restart or visit a dealership to check for software patches and module updates.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features add peace of mind, though sensor calibration and erratic software behavior remain points of observation in early feedback.

**Erratic Sensor Activation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that adaptive cruise control or automatic emergency braking can occasionally activate falsely, triggering warning lights and sudden vehicle deceleration.
- **Solution:** Clean sensor lenses and camera housings, and have a dealer inspect calibration settings if false alerts persist.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V869000: Structure:Body:Door](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=GMC&model=Terrain&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The door strikers may fracture, resulting in the door opening unexpectedly. A door opening while the vehicle is being driven can increasing the risk of injury or a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace all four door strikers and attaching bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 GMC Terrain offers modern styling and practical everyday utility, but potential buyers should perform a thorough inspection given the early reports of powertrain leaks, fuel pump issues, and electronic glitches. Securing a remaining factory warranty or an extended service plan can provide valuable peace of mind.
