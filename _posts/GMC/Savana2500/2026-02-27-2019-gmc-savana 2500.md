## Exterior

### Body Panels and Doors
**Door Latch/Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking properly, difficulty opening from inside or outside.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty door latch or lock actuator assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, or intermittent fan speed operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 GMC Savana 2500 demonstrates good overall reliability, especially concerning its core powertrain components. The 4.3L V6 and 6.0L V8 engines, along with the 6-speed automatic transmission, are generally dependable for commercial applications. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, owners may encounter isolated electrical issues such as intermittent power loss, and exterior concerns like faulty door latches. Interior components, particularly the HVAC blower motor, can also be a point of failure. These issues are typically repairable with moderate cost. Proactive maintenance and addressing minor concerns promptly can ensure the continued longevity and operational efficiency of the Savana 2500.
