**Introduction:**

The 1997 GMC Savana 2500 is a versatile full-size van that offers spacious cargo capacity and a range of engine and transmission options. The popular 6.5L V8 engine delivers ample power and torque of 190 horsepower and 365 lb-ft, respectively. The Savana 2500 boasts a comfortable and utilitarian interior, making it suitable for both work and family use. However, like all vehicles, it has some known issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **6.5L V8 Engine**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and/or oil leaks from the intake manifold; engine overheating; rough idle
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and/or oil leaks into the cylinders; white smoke from exhaust; engine overheating
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
**Transmission**

* **4L80E Automatic Transmission**
    * **Transmission Slippage**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to engage properly, causing slipping and loss of power
        * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5L90E Automatic Transmission**
    * **No Known Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension**
    * **Ball Joint Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loose or noisy ball joints; premature tire wear
        * Solution: Replace ball joints
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per side
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension**
    * **Leaf Spring Sagging**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the van sits lower than the front; reduced handling and braking performance
        * Solution: Replace leaf springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim headlights; difficulty starting the vehicle; electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains excessively even when the vehicle is parked
    * Solution: Test and replace faulty components, such as relays or switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks fail to open or close properly
    * Solution: Replace or repair door lock mechanisms
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on body panels, especially around wheel wells and the undercarriage
    * Solution: Repair or replace affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of damage

**Interior**

* **HVAC Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system fails to heat or cool the cabin effectively
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components, such as the heater core or blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Fabric Tearing**
    * Description/Symptoms: Tears or holes in seat fabric caused by normal wear and tear or excessive use
    * Solution: Repair or replace seat covers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1997 GMC Savana 2500 is a capable vehicle with a number of engine and transmission options. However, it has some known issues that can lead to costly repairs. Engine and transmission problems are the most severe and expensive, with estimated repair costs ranging from $1,000 to over $3,000. Suspension and electrical issues are also common, with estimated repair costs between $200 and $1,000+. Interior issues, such as HVAC malfunctions, can be annoying but are generally less expensive to fix. It's important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and factor them into their purchase decision.
