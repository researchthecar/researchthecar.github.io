**Introduction to the 2016 GMC Savana 2500**

The 2016 GMC Savana 2500 is a full-size cargo van available in multiple configurations, including various roof heights and wheelbases. It is powered by a standard 4.8-liter V8 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission, producing 285 horsepower and 296 lb-ft of torque. Optional engine choices include a 6.0-liter V8 engine and a 6.6-liter Duramax turbo-diesel V8 engine.

Commonly praised aspects of the Savana 2500 include its spacious cargo area, versatile configurations, and powerful engine options. However, some critics have noted its outdated interior, limited fuel economy, and rough ride quality.

**Engine**

**4.8-liter V8**

* **Cylinder Misfire**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, loss of power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace faulty spark plugs, ignition coils, or fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Low oil levels, blue smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Repair or replace piston rings, valve seals, or PCV valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.0-liter V8**

* **No Issues Found**

**6.6-liter Duramax V8**

* **Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idle, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace faulty injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2500 - $4000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V446000
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of boost pressure, decreased engine performance
    * Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, slipping gears, no engagement
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace broken leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, electrical components fail
    * Solution: Test and replace faulty battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuse Box Failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower motor inoperative, lights not working
    * Solution: Repair or replace fuse box
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors won't lock or unlock, keys don't work
    * Solution: Replace faulty door lock actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Windshield Washer Fluid Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No windshield washer fluid coming out
    * Solution: Replace faulty pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard material
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Door Panel Trim Loose**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling door panels
    * Solution: Reattach or replace door panel trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 GMC Savana 2500 has some potential issues, but many are relatively minor and can be resolved at a reasonable cost. However, some more severe issues, such as engine injector failure or turbocharger failure, can be expensive to repair. Overall, the Savana 2500 is a generally reliable vehicle with a spacious and versatile interior, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
