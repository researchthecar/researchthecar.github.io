## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard lights flickering, accessories not responding, engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair wiring harnesses, check battery terminals and connections, test alternator and voltage regulator.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Faulty Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine light illumination, poor engine performance, incorrect fuel economy readings.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and replace faulty sensors (e.g., O2 sensor, MAF sensor, crankshaft position sensor).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

## Interior

### Seating and Upholstery
**Accelerated Wear on High-Use Areas**
- **Symptoms:** Tearing or thinning of fabric/vinyl on driver's seat bolsters, armrests, and steering wheel.
- **Solution:** Professional upholstery repair or replacement, use of seat covers for protection.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500


### HVAC System
**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, or intermittent airflow, regardless of fan speed setting.
- **Solution:** Replace the HVAC blower motor and potentially the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $250 - $700

The 2022 GMC Savana 2500 demonstrates good mechanical reliability, with its V8 engines and automatic transmission being robust for commercial applications.
