The 2012 GMC Savana 2500 is a full-size van that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 4.8L V8 that produces 285 horsepower and 296 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options include a 5.3L V8 with 310 horsepower and 334 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.0L V8 with 352 horsepower and 373 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed automatic and a six-speed manual.

The Savana 2500 has a spacious interior and a comfortable ride. It also offers a variety of features, including air conditioning, power windows and locks, and a stereo system. However, the Savana 2500 has also been known to have some issues.

**Engine**

**4.8L V8**

- **Intake manifold gasket failure:** The intake manifold gasket can fail, causing coolant and oil to leak. This can lead to overheating and engine damage.
    - Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    - Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V652000

- **Timing chain failure:** The timing chain can fail, causing the engine to lose power and stall. This can be a catastrophic failure that requires a complete engine rebuild.
    - Solution: Replace the timing chain.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000
    - Recalls: None Found

**5.3L V8**

- **Piston ring failure:** The piston rings can fail, causing the engine to lose compression and power. This can lead to increased oil consumption and engine damage.
