## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Intermittent Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional loss of power to various accessories or dashboard lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of wiring harness issues, fuse box problems, or faulty relays. Replacement of affected components.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery depletes faster than expected, leading to starting issues.
- **Solution:** Identification of parasitic draws from modules or accessories. Repair or replacement of faulty components causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 GMC Savana 2500 demonstrates good overall reliability, especially in its powertrain and chassis. The V8 engines and automatic transmissions are generally robust and capable of high mileage with proper maintenance. The most common areas for potential concern lie within the electrical system, with reports of intermittent power issues and battery drain that may require diagnosis and repair. The HVAC system's blower motor is another component that can occasionally fail. For owners, focusing on regular maintenance and being attentive to any electrical anomalies will contribute significantly to the longevity and dependable operation of this workhorse van.
