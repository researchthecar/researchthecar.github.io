**Introduction:**

The 2021 GMC Sierra 3500 HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its powerful engines and rugged capabilities. It offers a range of engine and transmission options, including:

* 6.6L V8 gasoline engine with 401 hp and 464 lb-ft of torque
* 6.6L V8 turbodiesel engine with 445 hp and 910 lb-ft of torque
* 10-speed Allison automatic transmission (standard)

Drivers appreciate the Sierra 3500 HD's towing capacity, payload capability, and off-road performance. However, some owners have reported specific issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**6.6L V8 Gasoline Engine**

* **Fuel injector failure:** Fuel injectors can become clogged or fail, causing rough idling, reduced engine power, and increased fuel consumption.
  * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Exhaust manifold cracking:** The exhaust manifold can develop cracks, leading to exhaust leaks and reduced engine performance.
  * Solution: Replace the exhaust manifold
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**6.6L V8 Turbodiesel Engine**

* **Injector nozzle failure:** Injector nozzles can become clogged or fail, causing engine misfires, reduced power, and increased emissions.
  * Solution: Replace the injector nozzles
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per injector
  * Recalls: None Found

* **DEF system issues:** The diesel exhaust fluid (DEF) system can experience problems with the DEF injector, pump, or sensors, leading to reduced engine power and limp mode.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the specific component
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**10-Speed Allison Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift quality issues:** Some owners have experienced rough shifting, delayed shifts, or slipping gears.
  * Solution: Transmission software updates or component replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf spring breakage:** The rear leaf springs can break, leading to reduced stability and handling.
  * Solution: Replace the leaf springs
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Shock absorber failure:** The shock absorbers can fail, resulting in a bumpy ride and reduced handling.
  * Solution: Replace the shock absorbers
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per shock
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** Some owners have reported excessive battery drain, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the specific electrical component causing the drain
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment system issues:** The infotainment system can experience software glitches, connectivity problems, or screen freezing.
  * Solution: Software updates or component replacement
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door handle failure:** The exterior door handles can become loose or fail to operate properly.
  * Solution: Replace the door handle
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per handle
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Tailgate issues:** The tailgate can become misaligned or difficult to open or close.
  * Solution: Adjust the tailgate latch or replace the tailgate components
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat heater issues:** The seat heaters can malfunction or fail to provide adequate warmth.
  * Solution: Replace the seat heater element or wiring
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per seat
  * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate control issues:** The climate control system can experience problems with the compressor, evaporator, or sensors, leading to insufficient cooling or heating.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the specific component
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 GMC Sierra 3500 HD is a capable heavy-duty pickup truck with powerful engines and off-road prowess. However, some owners have encountered specific issues with the vehicle, primarily involving the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the individual case and the specific parts affected.
