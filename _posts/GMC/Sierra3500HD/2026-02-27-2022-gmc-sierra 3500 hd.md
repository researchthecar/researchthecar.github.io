### Summary Verdict
The 2022 GMC Sierra 3500 HD demonstrates commendable reliability, especially within its core heavy-duty functions. The powertrain, including both the Duramax diesel and gasoline V8 engines paired with the robust Allison transmissions, is a strong point with no significant widespread issues reported. Minor concerns are primarily isolated to the optional Multi-Flex tailgate and occasional infotainment system glitches, which are generally resolvable with software updates or simple maintenance. Overall, the Sierra 3500 HD is a dependable choice for demanding applications, with its major components proving to be durable and reliable for its intended purpose.
