**Introduction:**
The 1991 GMC C3500HD is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its ruggedness and hauling capabilities. It is powered by a 7.4L V8 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed manual transmission. The C3500HD is renowned for its high torque but is criticized for its poor fuel economy and lack of modern features.

**Engine**
* **7.4L V8 with 4-speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Leaks coolant and causes overheating. Requires replacement of the manifold gasket. $300 - $500+
    * **Head Gasket Failure:** Blows out, causing coolant and oil mixing. Requires head gasket replacement and possible cylinder head resurfacing. $1200 - $2500+
* **7.4L V8 with 5-speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Throttle Position Sensor Malfunction:** Causes erratic engine performance and stalling. Requires TPS replacement. $150- $250+

**Transmission**
* **4-speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder:** Vibrations felt during acceleration. Requires torque converter replacement. $1000 - $1500+
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure:** Transmission shifts erratically or refuses to shift. Requires solenoid replacement. $200 - $400+
* **5-speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Clutch Failure:** Slipping or shuddering during engagement. Requires clutch replacement. $500 - $800+

**Suspension**
    * **Leaf Spring Breakage:** Occurs due to heavy loads or road damage. Requires leaf spring replacement. $600 - $1000+ per axle

**Electrical**
    * **Alternator Failure:** No charging of the battery. Requires alternator replacement. $300 - $600+

**Exterior**
    * **Tailgate Corrosion:** Rusting and pitting occur at the bottom of the tailgate. Requires tailgate replacement or repair. $500 - $1200+

**Interior**
* **Heater/AC Blower Motor Failure:** No air flow from vents. Requires blower motor replacement. $200 - $400+

**Summary:**
The 1991 GMC C3500HD is a reliable workhorse with known engine and transmission issues. While the repairs can be costly, the vehicle's durability and towing capacity make it a popular choice for heavy-duty applications. Owners should be prepared for potential repairs, especially as the vehicle ages.
