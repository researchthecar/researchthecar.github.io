**Introduction**

The 2002 Pontiac Aztek was a compact SUV that offered a unique and versatile design. It was available with a 3.4L V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Aztek received both praise for its practicality and criticism for its unconventional styling.

**Engine**

* **3.4L V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Leaks coolant or oil, causing overheating or engine damage, rough idling, and misfiring.
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $700+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic**

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed shifting, slipping, and loss of gears.
* Solution: Replace solenoid pack.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Worn or damaged struts lead to poor handling, excessive bouncing, and uneven tire wear.
* Solution: Replace front struts.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Low or no charging, battery draining, and electrical problems.
* Solution: Replace alternator.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Panel Corrosion**
* Description/Symptoms: Rust and corrosion on door panels, leading to paint bubbling and peeling.
* Solution: Remove and repair or replace door panels.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
* Description/Symptoms: Cracking and warping of dashboard plastic due to UV exposure.
* Solution: Replace dashboard.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Pontiac Aztek had several issues, ranging in severity from minor cosmetic problems to major engine or transmission failures. The cost to fix these issues could be substantial, especially for the more significant problems. However, it's important to note that the Aztek was a unique and versatile vehicle that offered a lot of practicality, which may outweigh the potential repair costs for some owners.
