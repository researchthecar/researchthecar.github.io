**Introduction**

The 2009 Pontiac G3 Wave is a stylish and sporty compact sedan known for its responsive handling and comfortable interior. It is powered by a 2.2L Ecotec inline-four engine rated at 155 horsepower and 150 lb-ft of torque paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The G3 Wave offers a smooth and fuel-efficient driving experience, making it a popular choice among value-oriented consumers. However, like all vehicles, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

**2.2L Ecotec Inline-Four**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine burns excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and engine damage if not addressed.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings or engine rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or slips when shifting gears, causing rough or delayed acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module, valve body, or transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noise from the front suspension when turning or hitting bumps.
    * Solution: Replace strut bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly even when the car is not in use, leading to dead batteries and potential starting issues.
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic draw and fix accordingly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles become loose or break, making it difficult to open doors.
    * Solution: Replace door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Window Regulator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows fail to operate or become stuck in an open position.
    * Solution: Replace window regulators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2009 Pontiac G3 Wave is generally a reliable and affordable vehicle, it is not immune to certain issues. The most common problems reported by owners include excessive oil consumption, shifting issues, and strut bearing failure. Depending on the severity of the issue, repairs can be costly, with some requiring major engine or transmission work. However, many of the issues are relatively minor and can be addressed with reasonable repair costs.
