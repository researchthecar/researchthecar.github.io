The 2007 Pontiac Wave5 is a subcompact hatchback known for its fuel efficiency and affordability. It comes standard with a 1.6L inline-4 engine producing approximately 103 horsepower and 107 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic transmission. Many owners appreciate its nimble handling and spacious cargo area for its class, but some dislike its basic interior and somewhat unrefined engine.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4**

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the thermostat housing or water pump, leading to overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace the thermostat housing and/or water pump, and replace the coolant.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

*   **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume an excessive amount of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage if not monitored.
    *   Solution: In some cases, piston ring replacement might be necessary; However, a full engine replacement might be a better option as well if internal components have been damaged.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

*   **Shifting Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifting, slipping between gears.
    *   Solution: Replace the transmission fluid, or potentially a transmission rebuild or replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Worn Struts/Shocks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, poor handling, excessive body roll.
    *   Solution: Replace the worn struts and/or shocks.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

*   **Rust on Strut Mounts**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on the strut mounts and potentially failing
    *   Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **ABS Sensor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: ABS light illuminated, ABS not functioning.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty ABS sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

*   **Power Window Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Power windows failing to operate, either intermittently or completely.
    *   Solution: Replace the window motor or switch.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Rust**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Rust forming on the rocker panels or around the wheel wells
    *   Solution: Cut out the affected area and replace with new metal, then repaint.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Broken Door Handles**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Interior door handles breaking.
    *   Solution: Replace the broken door handle.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Pontiac Wave5 is generally a reliable and affordable vehicle, but owners should be aware of potential issues such as coolant leaks, excessive oil consumption, transmission problems, and electrical malfunctions. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to more costly repairs. Regular maintenance and inspections are essential to mitigating these risks. The average cost of repairs varies, but some issues, like rust repair or engine work, can be relatively expensive.
