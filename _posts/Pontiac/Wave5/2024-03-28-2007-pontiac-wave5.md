**Introduction:** The 2007 Pontiac Wave5 is a 4-door compact sedan powered by a 2.2L Ecotec inline-4 engine that produces 148 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

**2.2L Ecotec Inline-4**

* **Timing Chain Rattle:** Occurs during start-up or acceleration; caused by a worn timing chain tensioner or guide.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain, tensioner, and guides.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:** Hesitation, delay, or hard shifting between gears; caused by faulty shift solenoids or a damaged torque converter.
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoids or torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noises:** Clunking or banging sounds from the rear suspension; caused by worn control arm bushings or sway bar links.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings or sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Premature Tire Wear:** Uneven or rapid tire wear on the front or rear tires; caused by a misalignment or faulty suspension components.
    * Solution: Inspect and adjust suspension, replace worn components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800 depending on the components replaced
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains quickly even when the vehicle is not in use; caused by a faulty alternator or electrical components.
    * Solution: Test electrical system, replace alternator or other faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Short:** Blown fuses, sparks, or smoke from electrical wiring; caused by a faulty wiring harness or electrical module.
    * Solution: Inspect wiring harness, replace faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Quarter Panels:** Premature rust formation on the rear quarter panels; caused by poor body panel seals or inadequate protection.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair rust, apply undercoating for future protection.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Fan Issues:** Blower motor not working or intermittent operation; caused by a faulty blower motor or resistor.
    * Solution: Replace blower motor or resistor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Actuators:** Power door locks not working or intermittent operation; caused by a faulty door lock actuator.
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300 each
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:** The 2007 Pontiac Wave5 has several reported issues, including engine timing chain problems, transmission shifting issues, suspension noises, electrical drains and shorts, exterior rust, and interior HVAC and door lock malfunctions. The severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the issue and the extent of the damage.
