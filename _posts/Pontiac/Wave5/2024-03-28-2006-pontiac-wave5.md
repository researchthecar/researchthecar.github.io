**Introduction**

The 2006 Pontiac Wave5 is a compact SUV offered with a single engine and transmission option. It boasts a 2.2-liter Ecotec inline-four engine that produces 144 horsepower and 152 lb-ft of torque. Power is sent to the front wheels via a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Wave5 is known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior, but it has received mixed reviews for its performance and handling.

**Engine**

* **Engine:** 2.2-liter Ecotec inline-four

    * **Head Gasket Failure:** Leaking coolant, overheating, and white smoke from the tailpipe. Requires replacement of the head gasket. $1,000 - $1,500. Recall: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Rattles or grinding noises from the engine. Requires replacement of the timing chain tensioner. $500 - $800. Recall: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Transmission:** 4-speed automatic

    * **Shift Solenoid Failure:** Harsh or delayed shifting. Requires replacement of the shift solenoid. $200 - $400. Recall: 06037
    * **Torque Converter Failure:** Slipping or shuddering when shifting. Requires replacement of the torque converter. $1,000 - $1,500. Recall: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Assembly Failure:** Noises or vibrations when driving over bumps. Requires replacement of the front strut assemblies. $500 - $800. Recall: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:** Loss of ride comfort, bouncing or swaying. Requires replacement of the rear shock absorbers. $300 - $500. Recall: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Battery discharge, dimming lights. Requires replacement of the alternator. $500 - $800. Recall: None Found
* **Battery Failure:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, dim lights. Requires replacement of the battery. $100 - $200. Recall: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Latch Failure:** Tailgate does not open or close properly. Requires replacement of the tailgate latch. $200 - $300. Recall: None Found
* **Headlight Bulb Failure:** Dim or flickering headlights. Requires replacement of the headlight bulbs. $50 - $100. Recall: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Noises or vibrations from the dashboard. Requires tightening or replacing loose components. $100 - $200. Recall: None Found
* **Seatbelt Sensor Malfunction:** Airbag warning light illuminates erroneously. Requires calibration or replacement of the seatbelt sensor. $300 - $500. Recall: None Found
* **Heater Core Failure:** Coolant leak inside the vehicle, foggy windows. Requires replacement of the heater core. $1,000 - $1,500. Recall: None Found

**Summary**

The 2006 Pontiac Wave5 faces several issues that vary in severity and cost to fix. The most common and expensive issues involve the engine and transmission, including head gasket failure, timing chain tensioner failure, shift solenoid failure, and torque converter failure. Other concerns include suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems. While these issues can be costly, the Wave5 is still an affordable and practical vehicle for certain buyers.
