**Introduction:**

The 2005 Pontiac Montana is a popular minivan that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and ample cargo space. It is powered by a standard 3.5-liter V6 engine that produces 200 horsepower and 220 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Other available engine options include a 3.9-liter V6 with 240 horsepower and a 3.4-liter V6 with 185 horsepower. Owners appreciate the Montana's versatile seating arrangements, user-friendly infotainment system, and roomy cargo area. However, some common issues have been reported over the years.

**Engine:**

* **3.5-Liter V6:**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** The intake manifold gasket may deteriorate, causing coolant and oil leaks. Symptoms include engine overheating, reduced coolant levels, and rough idling. The solution is to replace the gasket. Estimated cost to fix: $300 - $500.
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:** Fuel injectors can become clogged or malfunction, leading to engine misfires, rough running, and decreased fuel efficiency. The fuel injectors should be tested and replaced if necessary. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $300 per injector.
* **3.9-Liter V6:**
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** The valve cover gasket can develop leaks, resulting in oil seepage. The gasket should be replaced to prevent excessive oil loss. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $350.
* **3.4-Liter V6:**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission:**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Slipping:** The transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes, causing rough or delayed shifts. The issue can be fixed by replacing the torque converter or transmission clutch pack. Estimated cost to fix: $1,000 - $2,000.
    * **Solenoid Issues:** Transmission solenoids can fail, resulting in erratic shifting, delayed engagement, or a lack of response. The solenoids should be replaced to restore proper transmission function. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $500.

**Suspension:**

* **Strut/Shock Failure:** The struts or shocks may fail, leading to reduced ride quality, handling problems, and uneven tire wear. The struts or shocks should be replaced in pairs. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $800.
* **Control Arm Bushings:** Control arm bushings can deteriorate over time, causing clunking noises, vibrations, and impaired handling. The bushings should be replaced to restore ride quality. Estimated cost to fix: $150 - $300.

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Issues:** The battery may fail prematurely, resulting in difficulty starting the vehicle or electrical malfunctions. The battery should be tested and replaced if found to be defective. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $200.
* **Alternator Failure:** The alternator may fail to properly charge the battery, leading to electrical problems and even vehicle stalling. The alternator should be tested and replaced if necessary. Estimated cost to fix: $250 - $400.

**Exterior:**

* **Door Handle Breakage:** Exterior door handles can break or become loose, causing difficulty opening the doors. The door handles should be inspected and replaced if found to be damaged. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $200.

**Interior:**

* **HVAC System Malfunction:** The HVAC system may fail to properly heat or cool the cabin. The issue can be caused by a number of components, such as the blower motor, evaporator core, or blend door actuator. The specific component that requires replacement should be identified and repaired. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $600.
* **Dashboard Squeaks/Rattles:** The dashboard may develop squeaks or rattles, especially over rough roads. The specific source of the noise should be identified and remedied, such as by adding cushioning material or tightening loose screws. Estimated cost to fix: $100 - $250.

**Summary:**

The 2005 Pontiac Montana has some common issues that affect different components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the required repair. Overall, the Montana is a reliable minivan, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues that have been reported.
