## 2006 Pontiac Matiz

The 2006 Pontiac Matiz is a subcompact hatchback that was offered with a 0.8L SOHC 3-cylinder engine and a 5-speed manual transmission. It produces 51 horsepower and 51 lb-ft of torque. The Matiz was praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. However, it was criticized for its lack of power, cramped interior, and poor handling.

**Engine**

**0.8L SOHC 3-Cylinder Engine**

* **Blown Head Gasket**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Failed Timing Belt**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, rattling noise from the engine
    * Solution: Replace timing belt
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Worn Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension, loose steering
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Failed Ignition Switch**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine will not start, key may not turn in the ignition
    * Solution: Replace ignition switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Battery**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the engine
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Body Panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: Paint bubbling or peeling, rust spots on the body
    * Solution: Repair rusted areas, repaint affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the extent of the damage
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Broken Window Regulators**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window does not roll up or down properly
    * Solution: Replace window regulator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Door Locks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks do not lock or unlock properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2006 Pontiac Matiz is a budget-friendly vehicle with generally minor issues that are relatively inexpensive to repair. However, some owners have reported more serious problems such as a blown head gasket and failed timing belt, which can be costly to fix. Overall, the Matiz is a reliable and fuel-efficient car for those seeking affordable transportation.
