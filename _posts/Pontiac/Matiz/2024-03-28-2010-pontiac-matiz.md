**Introduction to the 2010 Pontiac Matiz**

The 2010 Pontiac Matiz is a subcompact hatchback that was offered with a single engine option. The 1.0-liter 4-cylinder engine produces 67 horsepower and 66 pound-feet of torque. The Matiz is available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission.

The Matiz is praised for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and maneuverability. However, it has received criticism for its lack of power, cramped interior, and poor crash test ratings.

**Engine**

**1.0-liter 4-cylinder**

* **Oil leak from valve cover gasket**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil is seeping from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning oil smell and low oil levels.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing belt failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing belt can fail prematurely, causing catastrophic engine damage. Symptoms include rough idling, loss of power, and a loud ticking noise.
    * Solution: Replace the timing belt and all associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-speed manual transmission**

* **Clutch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch can wear out prematurely, causing slippage and difficulty shifting.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed automatic transmission**

* **Transmission fluid leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission fluid can leak from the pan gasket or other seals, causing low fluid levels and transmission failure.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the leaking component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front strut failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front struts can fail, causing a rough ride and uneven tire wear.
    * Solution: Replace the front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can discharge prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the battery drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation can accumulate inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Remove the headlight assemblies and allow them to dry out.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $50
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering wheel peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The steering wheel cover can peel or crack over time.
    * Solution: Replace the steering wheel cover.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2010 Pontiac Matiz is a budget-friendly vehicle that has some common issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The cost of these repairs can range from minor to significant, and it is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems.
