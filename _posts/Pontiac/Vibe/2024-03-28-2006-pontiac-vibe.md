**Introduction:**

The 2006 Pontiac Vibe is a compact crossover SUV jointly developed by General Motors and Toyota. It was offered with two engine options and a single transmission option. The standard engine is a 1.8L inline-4 producing 126 horsepower and 122 lb-ft of torque, while the optional engine is a 2.4L inline-4 producing 164 horsepower and 160 lb-ft of torque. All Vibes come standard with a 5-speed manual transmission.

Drivers appreciate the Vibe's spacious interior, versatile design, and impressive fuel economy. However, some common issues have been reported with this vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.8L Inline-4**

**Oil Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaks may occur from the valve cover gasket or oil pan.
Solution: Replace the faulty gasket or oil pan.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
Recalls: N/A

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Failure can cause vacuum leaks, rough idling, and reduced engine performance.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $450+
Recalls: N/A

**2.4L Inline-4**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

**Shifter Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Shifter may become difficult to shift or may pop out of gear.
Solution: Replace the shifter linkage or transmission mount.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $350+
Recalls: N/A

**Clutch Pedal Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Pedal may feel soft or spongy, or may become stuck.
Solution: Inspect and adjust the clutch system, or replace the clutch master or slave cylinder.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Starter Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Starter may fail to engage or may make grinding noises.
Solution: Replace the starter motor or solenoid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: N/A

**Alternator Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Alternator may fail to charge the battery or may make whining noises.
Solution: Replace the alternator or voltage regulator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

**Door Lock Actuator Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Door locks may not engage or disengage properly.
Solution: Replace the faulty door lock actuator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

**Center Console Lid Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Lid may rattle or become loose.
Solution: Tighten or replace the lid hinge.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
Recalls: N/A

**Steering Wheel Control Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel controls may not function properly.
Solution: Replace the multifunction switch or steering wheel module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2006 Pontiac Vibe has a range of issues that can vary in severity and cost to fix. Common engine issues include oil leaks and intake manifold gasket failure, while the transmission may experience shifter and clutch pedal issues. Electrical problems such as starter and alternator issues have also been reported. Additionally, some exterior and interior components have been known to malfunction. Overall, the Vibe's reliability issues are not severe, but some repairs can be costly.
