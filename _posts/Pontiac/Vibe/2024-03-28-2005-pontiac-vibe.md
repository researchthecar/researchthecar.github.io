**Introduction:**

The 2005 Pontiac Vibe is a compact SUV that is powered by a 1.8L ECOTEC inline-4 engine that produces 132 horsepower and 125 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable interior, ample cargo space, and agile handling. However, it has some reliability issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine:**

**1.8L ECOTEC Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Symptoms include coolant leaks, overheating, and rough idling. Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Symptoms include rattling noises from the engine, especially during startup. Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
* **Exhaust Manifold Warping:** Symptoms include a ticking or tapping noise from the engine, as well as performance issues. Solution: Replace the exhaust manifold. Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1000+

**Transmission:**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, and hesitation during acceleration. Solution: Repair or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2500+

**Suspension:**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** Symptoms include clunking noises from the front end, especially when turning or going over bumps. Solution: Replace the strut mount bearings. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:** Symptoms include clunking noises from the rear end, especially when going over bumps or making turns. Solution: Replace the rear sway bar links. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator Failure:** Symptoms include dimming lights, difficulty starting the engine, and battery discharge. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* **Ignition Switch Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the engine, intermittent electrical problems, and stalling. Solution: Replace the ignition switch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
* **Power Window Motor Failure:** Symptoms include non-functioning power windows. Solution: Replace the power window motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior:**

* **Door Handle Breakage:** Symptoms include loose or broken door handles. Solution: Replace the door handle. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Rear Hatch Struts Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty opening or closing the rear hatch. Solution: Replace the rear hatch struts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Symptoms include rattling noises from the dashboard area. Solution: Identify the source of the rattle and secure it. Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
* **Seatbelt Buckle Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty securing the seatbelt. Solution: Replace the seatbelt buckle. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

**Summary:**

While the Pontiac Vibe offers a comfortable and practical driving experience, it has a number of reliability issues that can lead to repairs and maintenance costs. The most severe and expensive issues include transmission slippage, timing chain tensioner failure, and exhaust manifold warping. Potential buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle before purchasing and consider the potential costs of repairs.
