**Introduction:**

The 2007 Pontiac Vibe is a compact SUV manufactured by General Motors. It features a reliable 1.8L 4-cylinder engine and comes with either a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic transmission. The Vibe is known for its practicality, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues that owners have experienced.

**Engine**

**1.8L 4-Cylinder**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
  * Solution: Replace piston rings or install oil control rings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Transmission Noise:** Grinding or whining noises when shifting gears.
  * Solution: Replace worn gears or bearings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping Gears:** Failure to engage gears properly, resulting in sudden loss of power.
  * Solution: Replace transmission solenoid pack.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushings:** Worn bushings lead to excessive noise, suspension vibration, and decreased handling.
  * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Premature failure of the alternator, resulting in battery drainage and electrical system issues.
  * Solution: Replace alternator.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leakage:** Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof, causing mold and mildew buildup.
  * Solution: Replace sunroof seal or re-seat sunroof.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 08V389000

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:** Annoying noises coming from the dashboard, especially during hot or cold weather.
  * Solution: Apply foam insulation or dielectric grease to reduce vibrations.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: DIY project under $50

**Summary:**

The 2007 Pontiac Vibe generally has a good reliability record. However, it is not without its issues. The most severe and costly concerns revolve around engine oil consumption, transmission slipping, and sunroof leakage. While interior and exterior issues may be less significant, they can still be annoying and affect the overall driving experience. Fortunately, most of these repairs can be completed within a reasonable budget.
