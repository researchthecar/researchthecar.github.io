**Introduction**

The 1990 Dodge Ram 50 was a compact pickup truck that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The standard engine was a 2.4L inline-four, which produced 103 horsepower and 133 lb-ft of torque. A 2.6L V6 was also available, which produced 110 horsepower and 150 lb-ft of torque.
