**Introduction:**

The 1997 Dodge Neon is a compact sedan that was available with two engine options: a 2.0L SOHC 4-cylinder and a 2.0L DOHC 4-cylinder. Both engines were paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 3-speed automatic transmission. The Neon was offered in base, Sport, and Highline trim levels. It was praised for its low price, fuel efficiency, and sporty handling. However, it was also criticized for its cramped interior and lack of power.

## **Engine**

### **2.0L SOHC 4-Cylinder**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, rough idle, decreased engine performance
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Head Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
* Solution: Replace head gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

### **2.0L DOHC 4-Cylinder**

**Timing Belt Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls, no-start condition (catastrophic failure)
* Solution: Replace timing belt and tensioner
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**

### **5-Speed Manual**

**Shift Cable Bushing Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, popping out of gear
* Solution: Replace shift cable bushings
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* Recalls: None Found

### **3-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Fluid Leak**
* Description/Symptoms: Fluid leak, slipping gears, delayed engagement
* Solution: Replace transmission fluid seal or gasket
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

### **Front Suspension**

**Strut Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise, poor handling, uneven tire wear
* Solution: Replace struts
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per strut
* Recalls: None Found

### **Rear Suspension**

**Sway Bar Link Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise, clunking over bumps
* Solution: Replace sway bar link
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per link
* Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**

### **All Versions**

**Headlight Switch Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, intermittent operation
* Solution: Replace headlight switch
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* Recalls: None Found

**Ignition Cylinder Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, key gets stuck in the ignition
* Solution: Replace ignition cylinder
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

### **All Versions**

**Door Handle Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks off or becomes loose
* Solution: Replace door handle
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150 per handle
* Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

### **All Versions**

**Power Window Motor Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Window does not move or moves slowly
* Solution: Replace power window motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250 per window
* Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

The 1997 Dodge Neon is a reliable and affordable compact car. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems include intake manifold gasket failure, head gasket failure, timing belt failure, shift cable bushing failure, and transmission fluid leak. These issues range from minor inconveniences to more serious problems that can require expensive repairs.
