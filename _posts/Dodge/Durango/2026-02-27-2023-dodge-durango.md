# 2023 Dodge Durango Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Dodge Durango, buyers will find a muscular midsize SUV known for its strong utility and diverse powertrain lineup ranging from a practical V6 to high-output V8 engines. While many owners enjoy its towing capacity and aggressive styling, owner-submitted reports highlight a few recurring concerns regarding electrical components, braking systems, and fuel delivery. Reviewing these areas can help prospective buyers perform a more targeted pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine produces approximately 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering standard daily-driving capability and decent efficiency. It serves as the baseline engine for lower trims like the GT and SXT.

### 5.7L HEMI V8

The 5.7-liter HEMI V8 pushes out 360 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque, providing robust towing capacity and strong mid-range acceleration. It is a popular choice for buyers needing heavier utility.

**Fuel Delivery Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to accelerate adequately when pressing the gas pedal, sometimes without triggering immediate dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect the fuel pump and pressure sensors, replacing faulty components as necessary.

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 delivers an impressive 475 horsepower for high-performance SRT models, offering aggressive acceleration and track-inspired dynamics. It is built for enthusiasts seeking maximum power.

### 6.2L Supercharged HEMI V8

The top-tier 6.2-liter supercharged V8 powers the SRT Hellcat variant with a staggering 710 horsepower, providing elite performance capabilities. It is a rare, highly specialized option for speed enthusiasts.

## Transmission

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission pairs across all engine options, delivering smooth, responsive gear changes and optimized highway fuel economy. Overall mechanical durability is generally well-regarded.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Brake system reliability is paramount, and a few owners have flagged severe pedal drops and vacuum boost pump failures.

**Brake Pedal Failure and Vacuum Boost Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal goes straight to the floor, resulting in sudden and severe loss of stopping power.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake master cylinder and vacuum boost pump immediately, and replace failing hydraulic components.
- **Cost:** $1,400

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Lighting

electrical issues involving infotainment software glitches and exterior LED turn signals have been reported by some owners.

**UConnect Software and Exterior Lighting Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Radio displays frequent service error messages, screen blacks out, or LED front turn signals fail prematurely requiring extensive assembly replacement.
- **Solution:** Have dealership software updates flashed or replace faulty integrated lighting modules as needed.
- **Cost:** $1,400

## Exterior

### Windshield Glass

Exterior trim and glass should be carefully inspected for premature wear, such as edge bubbling or seal degradation.

**Windshield Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Bubbles and separation forming along the corners and top edge of the windshield glass.
- **Solution:** Consult an auto glass specialist to evaluate whether the windshield requires replacement under warranty.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V640000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Durango&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the instrument panel mounted shifter boot, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Dodge Durango remains an appealing, versatile SUV with strong engine choices and impressive capability. However, used car buyers should carefully test the brakes, inspect electrical accessories, and ensure all infotainment systems and exterior lights operate correctly before finalizing a purchase.
