### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Dodge Durango generally presents a reliable profile across its powertrain and core mechanical systems. The V6 and V8 engines, along with the 8-speed automatic transmission, have proven robust with no significant widespread mechanical failures reported. The suspension system also appears to be durable. The primary areas of concern are within the electrical system, specifically related to the Uconnect infotainment system experiencing occasional glitches and a possibility of premature battery failure. These are not typically indicative of systemic design flaws but rather common electronic or component-specific issues that can arise in modern vehicles. Overall, the 2018 Durango is a solid choice for those seeking a capable SUV, with potential buyers advised to be aware of the minor electrical quirks.
