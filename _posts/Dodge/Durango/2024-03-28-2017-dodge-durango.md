The 2017 Dodge Durango is a full-size SUV that offers a variety of powertrain options. It comes standard with a 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. A 5.7-liter V8 engine with 360 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque and an upgraded 6.4-liter V8 which produces 475 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque is also available. The Durango comes with either an eight-speed automatic or a nine-speed automatic transmission, depending on the engine choice.

The Durango has received generally positive reviews, with critics praising its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine options. However, it has also been criticized for its subpar fuel economy and lack of some features that are available on competing SUVs.
