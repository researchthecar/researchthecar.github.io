This analysis breaks down reported issues across key automotive systems, providing insights into potential areas of attention for prospective buyers and current owners. ### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Dodge Durango demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly within its powertrain options. The Pentastar V6 and HEMI V8 engines, along with the 8-speed automatic transmission, are generally robust and well-regarded. The primary areas that may require attention are related to the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential intermittent battery drain issues, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronics. Exterior and interior components, as well as suspension, show no widespread significant issues. Overall, the Durango is a dependable SUV for its segment, with most potential concerns being minor and addressable through software updates or targeted diagnostics.
