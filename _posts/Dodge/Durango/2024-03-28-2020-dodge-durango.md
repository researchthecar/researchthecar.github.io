**Introduction:**

The 2020 Dodge Durango is a versatile SUV that offers a powerful lineup of engine options, including a 3.6L V6 with 293 hp and 260 lb-ft of torque, a 5.7L V8 with 360 hp and 390 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.2L V8 with 475 hp and 470 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic for the V6 and V8 engines, and a 10-speed automatic for the 6.2L V8. The Durango is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and excellent towing capabilities, but it has received some criticism for its dated infotainment system and excessive wind noise at higher speeds.

**Engine**
* **3.6L V6**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
        * Description/Symptoms: High oil consumption, requiring frequent top-offs.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5.7L V8**
    * **Valve Lifter Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noisy ticking or tapping sound from the engine, loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace faulty lifters.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **6.2L V8**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**
* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Delayed or Rough Shifting:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or shifts roughly between gears.
        * Solution: Reprogram transmission control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: TSB 21-076-19
* **10-Speed Automatic**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**
* **Air Suspension Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Suspension system malfunctions, causing uneven ride height or a bouncy ride.
        * Solution: Replace faulty air suspension components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Electrical Short in Lighting System:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Headlights, taillights, or other lights flicker or fail intermittently.
        * Solution: Repair or replace faulty wiring.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Rear Bumper Cover Cracking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or splits in the plastic rear bumper cover.
        * Solution: Replace bumper cover.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Dashboard Creaking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noises or creaks coming from the dashboard.
        * Solution: Apply lubricant or tighten screws.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: DIY repair, or $100 - $200+ for professional installation
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2020 Dodge Durango offers many appealing features, it has experienced some issues, particularly related to its engine and electrical systems. The severity of the issues varies, with some being minor annoyances (e.g.,dashboard creaking) and others being more serious (e.g., excessive oil consumption). The cost of repairs can also range from a few hundred dollars to over $2,000 depending on the issue. Overall, prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues before making a purchase decision.
