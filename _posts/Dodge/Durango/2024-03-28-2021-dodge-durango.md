**Introduction:**

The 2021 Dodge Durango is a full-size SUV offered with a wide range of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 3.6L V6 producing 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, while the optional engine choices include a 5.7L V8 producing 360 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque, and a 6.4L V8 producing 475 horsepower and 470 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include an 8-speed automatic for the V6 and V8 engines, and a 10-speed automatic for the 6.4L V8. The Durango is praised for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and robust towing capabilities. However, it has also been met with some common issues.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

**Intake Manifold Issues**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Rough idle, stalling, loss of power
* **Solution:** Replace intake manifold
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:** N/A

**5.7L V8**

**Valve Seat Recession**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Engine knocking, reduced power, increased oil consumption
* **Solution:** Replace cylinder heads
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $5,000 - $10,000+
* **Recalls:** 22V-842

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Shifting Problems**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Hesitation or jerking during gear changes
* **Solution:** Software update, transmission fluid flush, or transmission replacement
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $5,000+
* **Recalls:** 21V-239

**Suspension**

**Rear Suspension Noise**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Squeaking or rattling noise coming from the rear suspension when driving over bumps
* **Solution:** Replace rear suspension bushings or shock absorbers
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $1,000+
* **Recalls:** N/A

**Electrical**

**Fuel Pump Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Vehicle stalls or fails to start, loss of power
* **Solution:** Replace fuel pump
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $800 - $1,200+
* **Recalls:** N/A

**Exterior**

**Door Handle Issues**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Door handles become difficult to operate or break off
* **Solution:** Replace door handles
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** N/A

**Interior**

**Windshield Wiper Stalk Failure**
* **Description/Symptoms:** Windshield wipers stop working or work intermittently
* **Solution:** Replace windshield wiper stalk
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+
* **Recalls:** N/A

**Summary:**

The 2021 Dodge Durango has received positive feedback for its performance and features, but it has also faced some reliability issues. The most severe issues reported include valve seat recession in the 5.7L V8 engine and shifting problems in the 8-speed automatic transmission. Other common issues include fuel pump failure, problems with door handles and windshield wiper stalks, and rear suspension noise. It is important to note that not all Durangos are affected by these issues, and the overall severity and cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific problem encountered.
