Alright, here is a comprehensive list of common issues and complaints reported by owners of the 2017 Dodge Durango, categorized by major component and engine/transmission option. The 2017 Dodge Durango is a popular SUV known for its spacious interior, towing capacity, and available V8 engine. Engine options include the 3.6L Pentastar V6 (290-295 hp, 260 lb-ft torque), the 5.7L Hemi V8 (360 hp, 390 lb-ft torque), and the 6.4L Hemi V8 (475 hp, 470 lb-ft torque) which was only available on the SRT trim. All engine options are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. People generally like the Durango's comfortable ride, ample cargo space, and powerful engine options. Common dislikes include the dated infotainment system, subpar fuel economy, and some reliability concerns.

**Engine**

**3.6L Pentastar V6**

*   **Tick Noise / Misfire**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report a ticking noise coming from the engine, often accompanied by a misfire. This can be caused by worn rocker arms, lifters, or camshaft issues.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis by a mechanic to pinpoint the source of the noise. Replacing affected rocker arms, lifters, or camshaft.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+ depending on the extent of the damage.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Cooler Housing Leak**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant and/or oil leaking from the oil cooler housing. Often visible as a puddle under the vehicle.
    *   Solution: Replacing the oil cooler housing and gaskets. An updated design of the housing is available that addresses the weakness of the original part.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    *   Recalls: None Found

**5.7L Hemi V8**

*   **Hemi Tick**
    *   Description/Symptoms: A distinct ticking or tapping sound emanating from the engine, often described as the "Hemi tick". The sound may vary with engine temperature or load. Some sources suggest the lifters or exhaust manifolds are the culprit.
    *   Solution: In some instances, the issue resolves itself over time. In more severe cases, replacement of the affected lifters or exhaust manifold gaskets may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+ depending on the underlying cause and repair needed.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Exhaust Manifold Bolt Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: A ticking or exhaust leak sound, often louder on cold starts, due to broken exhaust manifold bolts.
    *   Solution: Replacing the broken bolts and potentially the exhaust manifold gasket.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000
    *   Recalls: None Found

**6.4L Hemi V8**
*   **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (All Engines)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or jerky shifts, especially between certain gears. Hesitation when accelerating or downshifting.
    *   Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid flush and replacement, or valve body replacement. In some cases, a full transmission rebuild or replacement is necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 (software update) - $4,000+ (transmission replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Torque Converter Shudder**
    *   Description/Symptoms: A shaking or shuddering sensation, particularly at lower speeds or when the torque converter is locking up.
    *   Solution: Transmission fluid flush with updated fluid, torque converter replacement, or transmission replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 (fluid flush) - $3,000+ (torque converter replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Load Leveling Shock Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear suspension, especially when loaded or towing. Reduced ride quality and stability.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the rear load leveling shocks (if equipped).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Uconnect System Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Freezing, lagging, crashing, or rebooting of the Uconnect infotainment system. Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    *   Solution: Software updates, system reset, or replacement of the Uconnect module.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update) - $1000+ (module replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Power Liftgate Malfunction**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Power liftgate not opening or closing properly. Liftgate getting stuck or making unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis of the liftgate motor, struts, sensors, and latch. Replacement of the faulty components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Water Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin, often around the windshield, sunroof, or liftgate seals.
    *   Solution: Replacing the affected seals or resealing the windshield/sunroof.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard Cracking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard, particularly in hot climates.
    *   Solution: Dashboard replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Dodge Durango is a capable SUV, but owners have reported some common issues. Engine issues, particularly with the 3.6L V6 and 5.7L V8, can lead to costly repairs. Transmission problems, especially rough shifting and torque converter shudder, are also relatively frequent. Electrical glitches with the Uconnect system and power liftgate are common annoyances. Overall, while the Durango offers a lot of features and performance, potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor in potential repair costs. The severity and cost of repairs vary, but addressing these issues can be significant.
