# 2022 Dodge Durango Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Dodge Durango continues its reputation as a muscular and capable midsize SUV, offering strong towing capabilities and spacious three-row seating. However, used car buyers should carefully review owner-reported issues concerning electrical components, exterior lighting, and powertrain performance. While many drivers enjoy its performance and utility, keeping an eye on these specific trouble spots during a pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a more reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine produces 293 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing reliable baseline performance for daily driving and light utility needs.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle engine may shut off unexpectedly while driving at moderate speeds without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine components, fuel supply, and coolant lines for potential failures or obstructions.

### 5.7L HEMI V8

The 5.7-liter HEMI V8 generates 360 horsepower and 390 lb-ft of torque, offering robust towing capability and strong acceleration for enthusiasts.

**Unexpected Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Engine experiences sudden power loss or catastrophic failure while in transit.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic perform a thorough diagnostic check of the engine management and fuel delivery systems.

### Fuel Pump

The fuel delivery components ensure a steady supply of gasoline to the engine under various driving conditions, though occasional electrical relay and pump failures have been reported.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to crank or stalls unexpectedly in traffic due to fuel delivery interruption.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty fuel pump or associated electrical relays.

## Transmission

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic

The TorqueFlite 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear shifts across all trims and drivetrains, contributing to both performance and fuel efficiency.

**Shifter and Engagement Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to shift into drive or unexpectedly drops into neutral while idling or accelerating.
- **Solution:** Inspect the shifter assembly and transmission internal components for mechanical wear or improper heat treatment.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

The vehicle's electrical and lighting systems generally operate well, though owners have reported occasional issues with LED daytime running lights and moisture buildup in rear lamp assemblies.

**Headlight and Daytime Running Light Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Driver-side daytime running lights, headlights, or rear racetrack light bars flicker, accumulate moisture, or stop working entirely.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected lamp assembly or seal housing units to prevent moisture intrusion.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V140000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Durango&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." An ESC malfunction indicator light that fails to illuminate during an electronic stability control problem can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ABS control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V154000: Steering:Rack And Pinion](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Durango&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Difficulty steering, or a loss of steering control, can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V866000: Power Train:Manual Transmission:Column Shift Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Durango&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An improperly engaged shifter assembly may slip out of the "drive" position, resulting in a sudden loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the shifter assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V115000: Structure:Body:Hatchback/Liftgate](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Durango&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The rear spoiler may not be aligned properly with the roof, resulting in damage to the spoiler during liftgate use. A damaged rear spoiler can detach and become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rear spoiler, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V415000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Durango&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 126, "Electronic Stability Control Systems." Driving with a disabled electronic stability control system can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ABS control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V436000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Durango&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the radio software or the software will be OTA, "Over-The-Air," free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Dodge Durango is a powerful and spacious SUV choice for families and towers alike, but prospective buyers should conduct a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to exterior lighting assemblies for moisture issues and verify proper operation of the transmission shifter and electrical fuel systems during a test drive.
