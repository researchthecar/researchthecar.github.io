**Introduction:**

The 1992 Dodge D350 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its powerful engine options and rugged capabilities. It was available with three different engine choices: a 5.2L V8, a 5.9L V8, and a 7.2L V8. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual, a 4-speed automatic, and a 6-speed manual.

Drivers appreciate the D350's impressive towing and payload capacities, as well as its spacious cabin and comfortable ride. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components over the years.

**Engine**

**5.2L V8**

* **Valve Stem Seals Leaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption and blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks and/or poor engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5.9L V8**

* **Cylinder Head Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, and loss of power
    * Solution: Replace cylinder head
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.2L V8**

* **Oil Pan Gasket Leaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the bottom of the engine
    * Solution: Replace oil pan gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electronic Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fuel system malfunctions, poor engine performance, and reduced fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace electronic fuel injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Slave Cylinder Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or clutch not engaging
    * Solution: Replace clutch slave cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gears slipping out of engagement during driving
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Lockup Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle shuddering or loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear axle sagging or rubbing on tires
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor handling, excessive bounce, and possible tire wear
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Starter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine not cranking over
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, and electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working or intermittent operation
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Corrosion:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust and/or pitting on the tailgate
    * Solution: Repair or replace tailgate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Lock Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioning Compressor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lack of cold air from the AC vents
    * Solution: Replace AC compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 1992 Dodge D350 has a mixed record regarding reliability and durability. While it offers impressive capabilities and a comfortable interior, some owners have faced issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the availability of parts. It's important to consider these potential issues when evaluating this vehicle for purchase or long-term ownership.
