## 2003 Dodge Ram 3500

The 2003 Dodge Ram 3500 is a heavy-duty pickup truck that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The 5.9L Cummins turbo diesel engine is particularly popular among truck enthusiasts, while the 4-speed automatic transmission is known for its durability. The Ram 3500 is praised for its towing and hauling capabilities, but some owners have reported issues with various components.

## Engine

### 5.9L Cummins Turbo Diesel

**Injection Pump Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, difficulty starting
* Solution: Replace injection pump
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
* Recalls: None Found

### 6.8L Hemi V10

**Cylinder Head Crack**
* Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
* Solution: Replace cylinder head
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

### 4-Speed Automatic

**Torque Converter Shudder**
* Description/Symptoms: Vibration while shifting gears, particularly at low speeds
* Solution: Replace torque converter
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

### 6-Speed Manual

**No Issues Found**

## Suspension

**Rear Axle Seal Leakage**
* Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from rear axle
* Solution: Replace axle seal
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

**Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after extended periods of non-use
* Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic drain, repair or replace faulty components
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Fuse Box Overheating**
* Description/Symptoms: Electrical issues, blown fuses, burning smell
* Solution: Replace fuse box
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

**Rear Tailgate Latch Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Tailgate will not open or close properly
* Solution: Replace tailgate latch
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

## Interior

**HVAC Fan Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Blower motor will not operate
* Solution: Replace blower motor
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2003 Dodge Ram 3500 is a capable truck but has some notable issues to be aware of. Engine and transmission problems can be severe and costly, while suspension and electrical issues are generally less severe and expensive to fix. Overall, the Ram 3500 remains a popular choice for those seeking a heavy-duty workhorse, but potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues it may face.
