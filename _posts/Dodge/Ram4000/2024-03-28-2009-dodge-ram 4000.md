## 2009 Dodge Ram 4000

The 2009 Dodge Ram 4000 is a heavy-duty pickup truck known for its powerful engines and sturdy construction. It offers various engine and transmission options to meet different needs. Owners appreciate its towing and hauling capabilities, spacious cabin, and impressive power. However, like any vehicle, it has some reported issues that owners should be aware of.

### Engine

**5.7L Hemi V8**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Leaking coolant, rough idling, and decreased engine performance
    * Replace the gasket
    * $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Valve Seat Recession:**
    * Engine knocking or tapping, especially when cold
    * Requires valve seat repair or replacement
    * $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**6.7L Cummins I6 Turbo Diesel**

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * Engine power loss, reduced fuel economy
    * Replace the EGR valve
    * $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Engine performance issues, excessive smoke, and whistling noises
    * Replace the turbocharger
    * $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

### Transmission

**4-Speed Automatic (45RFE)**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Delayed engagement, gear slippage while driving
    * Rebuild or replace the transmission
    * $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Solenoid Failure:**
    * Erratic shifting, transmission stuck in one gear
    * Replace the failed solenoid
    * $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**6-Speed Automatic (68RFE)**

* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Vibration or shuddering during acceleration or gear changes
    * Replace the torque converter
    * $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Valve Body Failure:**
    * Transmission slipping, delayed shifts, or overheating
    * Repair or replace the valve body
    * $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

### Suspension

**Front Suspension**

* **Ball Joint Failure:**
    * Clunking or creaking noises, uneven tire wear
    * Replace the ball joints
    * $200 - $400 per wheel+
    * Recalls: N/A

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Battery drains rapidly when parked
    * Diagnose and repair electrical draw
    * $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Vehicle fails to start, dim headlights, charging system issues
    * Replace the alternator
    * $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

### Exterior

* **Rust on Frame and Body:**
    * Corrosion on various components, especially in areas exposed to salt or moisture
    * Repair or replace rusted parts
    * Varies depending on the extent of damage
    * Recalls: N/A

### Interior

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * No air flow from vents
    * Replace the blower motor
    * $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Instrument Cluster Failure:**
    * Gauges not working or displaying incorrect information
    * Repair or replace the instrument cluster
    * $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: N/A

## Summary

The 2009 Dodge Ram 4000 is a capable and powerful pickup truck, but it has some reported issues that owners should be aware of. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly, especially for the engine and transmission. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage. Regular maintenance and inspections can help prevent some issues and extend the life of the vehicle.
