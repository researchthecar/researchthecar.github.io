## 2008 Dodge Atos

The 2008 Dodge Atos is a compact city car produced by Dodge. It is powered by a 1.1-liter 4-cylinder engine mated to a 5-speed manual transmission. The Atos is known for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and maneuverability. However, it has also been known to experience some issues over time.

* **Seat Adjustment Lever**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat adjustment lever may break, making it difficult or impossible to adjust the driver's seat.
    * Solution: Replace the seat adjustment lever.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Window Switches**
    * Description/Symptoms: The window switches may fail, preventing the windows from being opened or closed.
    * Solution: Replace the window switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150 per switch
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2008 Dodge Atos is a reliable and affordable city car. However, it has been known to experience some issues over time, particularly with its seat adjustment lever and window switches. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but most are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable price.
