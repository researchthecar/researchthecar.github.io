**Introduction:**

The 2009 Dodge Atos is a subcompact hatchback that was offered with a single engine and transmission option. The 1.1-liter, inline-4 engine produces 62 horsepower and 71 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. Drivers like the Atos for its fuel efficiency and affordability, while common criticisms include its lack of power and cramped interior.

**Summary:**

The 2009 Dodge Atos has relatively few reported issues, with the most common problems being related to the interior. While the power window failure is a known issue, it is not particularly severe or expensive to fix. Overall, the Atos is a reliable and budget-friendly vehicle that is suitable for everyday use.
