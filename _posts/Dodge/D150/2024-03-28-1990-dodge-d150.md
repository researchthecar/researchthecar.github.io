**Introduction**

The 1990 Dodge D150 is a popular full-size pickup truck. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.9L V6, a 5.2L V8, and a 5.9L V8. The D150 is also available with either a manual or automatic transmission.

People like the D150 for its spacious interior, strong engine, and comfortable ride. However, there are some common issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

* **3.9L V6**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Cracked cylinder head**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace cylinder head
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5.2L V8**
    * **Timing chain failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from engine, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace timing chain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5.9L V8**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Manual transmission**
    * **Shifter linkage failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace shifter linkage
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Clutch failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace clutch
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Automatic transmission**
    * **Transmission fluid leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid under vehicle
        * Solution: Replace transmission fluid pan gasket or seal
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque converter failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping when shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front ball joints**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when turning or going over bumps
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear leaf springs**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical problems
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle won't start
    * Solution: Replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust on body panels, frame
    * Solution: Repair rust
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard lights not working**
    * Description/Symptoms: No illumination on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace bulbs or wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Cruise control not working**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control doesn't engage or hold speed
    * Solution: Repair or replace cruise control switch or module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1990 Dodge D150 is a popular full-size pickup truck with a variety of engine and transmission options. It has a spacious interior and a strong engine. However, it does have some common issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure, timing chain failure, and transmission fluid leaks. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can be high.
