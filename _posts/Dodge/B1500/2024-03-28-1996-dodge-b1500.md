**Vehicle Introduction**

The 1996 Dodge B1500 is a full-size van that offers a spacious cabin, versatile cargo area, and various engine and transmission options. Available engine options include the 3.9L V6, 5.2L V8, and 5.9L V8. For transmissions, buyers can choose between a 3-speed automatic, 4-speed automatic, or 5-speed manual. Known for its ruggedness, towing capabilities, and practicality, the B1500 has also gained some popularity among enthusiasts for its potential as a conversion van. However, it has some common issues to watch out for.

**Engine**

**3.9L V6**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaks, overheating, reduced engine power
  - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
  - Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from valve cover area
  - Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
  - Recalls: None Found

**5.2L V8**
- **Crankshaft Position (CKP) Sensor Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, check engine light, loss of power
  - Solution: Replace CKP sensor
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Ignition Coil Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, loss of power, check engine light
  - Solution: Replace ignition coil
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200 per coil
  - Recalls: None Found

**5.9L V8**
- **Head Gasket Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust, oil in coolant
  - Solution: Replace head gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Water Pump Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, squealing noise, check engine light
  - Solution: Replace water pump
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**3-Speed Automatic**
- **Slipping Gears**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips out of gear, difficulty engaging gears
  - Solution: Replace transmission or rebuild
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**
- **Overdrive Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission stuck in lower gears, no power in overdrive
  - Solution: Replace overdrive solenoid or transmission control module (TCM)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
  - Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual**
- **Grinding Gears**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise
  - Solution: Replace clutch or synchronize
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $900
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Front Ball Joint Wear**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when turning or braking, uneven tire wear, poor handling
  - Solution: Replace ball joints
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per side
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Rear Leaf Spring Sag**
  - Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, reduced load capacity
  - Solution: Replace leaf springs
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light, dim headlights, electrical issues
  - Solution: Replace alternator
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Power Window Motor Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Power windows inoperative
  - Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per window
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Rust**
  - Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, frame, and other areas
  - Solution: Rust repair, rust prevention coating
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Paint Peeling**
  - Description/Symptoms: Paint peeling or chipping
  - Solution: Repaint affected areas
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dash Panel Cracking**
  - Description/Symptoms: Cracks forming on dashboard
  - Solution: Replace or repair dash panel
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Door Panel Sagging**
  - Description/Symptoms: Door panels loose or sagging
  - Solution: Replace or repair door panel mounting clips
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1996 Dodge B1500 has a few common issues to consider, particularly its propensity for engine gasket failures, transmission problems, and electrical issues. The cost of repairs can range from minor to significant, depending on the severity and the specific parts affected. Potential buyers and owners should be aware of these issues and budget accordingly for repairs.
