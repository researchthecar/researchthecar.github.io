**Introduction:**

The 1992 Dodge Colt is a compact car available with two engine options: a 1.5L inline-four and a 1.8L inline-four. The 1.5L engine produces 85 horsepower and 94 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.8L engine produces 105 horsepower and 115 lb-ft of torque. Both engines can be paired with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 3-speed automatic transmission. The Colt is praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and maneuverability, but it is criticized for its lack of power and dated interior.

**Engine**

**1.5L Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**1.8L Engine**

* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may stall or hesitate during acceleration. There may be a check engine light on.
    * Solution: Replace the crankshaft position sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch pedal may feel spongy or become difficult to shift. There may be a burning smell.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**3-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shudder or vibrate during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front of the vehicle may feel unstable or there may be a clunking noise when going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Starter Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may not start or may only start intermittently.
    * Solution: Replace the starter solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Lens Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The lenses on the fog lights may crack or become discolored.
    * Solution: Replace the fog light lenses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power window switches may become intermittent or stop working altogether.
    * Solution: Replace the power window switch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1992 Dodge Colt is a reliable and affordable compact car, but it does have some common issues that owners should be aware of. The most significant issues include crankshaft position sensor failure, clutch failure, and torque converter shudder. However, the cost of these repairs is relatively low, making the Colt a good value overall.
