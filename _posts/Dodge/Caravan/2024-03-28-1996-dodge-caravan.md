**Introduction**

The 1996 Dodge Caravan is a popular minivan known for its spacious interior, versatile seating configurations, and economical V6 engine. It is powered by a 3.0L V6 engine producing 150 horsepower and 165 lb-ft of torque paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include:

* 2.4L I4 engine producing 128 horsepower and 153 lb-ft of torque
* 3.3L V6 engine producing 180 horsepower and 212 lb-ft of torque
* 5-speed manual transmission

Drivers appreciate the Caravan's comfortable ride, ample cargo space, and affordable price point. However, some common problems have been reported over the years.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, engine overheating, reduced performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
    
**2.4L I4 Engine**
  
* **Timing Belt Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls or loses power, rattling or grinding noises from the engine
    * Solution: Replace timing belt and related components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
    
**3.3L V6 Engine**
  
* **Oil Leaks from Valve Cover Gaskets:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover area, visible oil stains
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed or rough shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid and filter, adjust shift linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Sway Bar Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension, unstable handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Shock Absorber Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy rear suspension, rattling or banging sounds
    * Solution: Replace shock absorber mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering lights, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery even after charging
    * Solution: Identify and repair source of parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, especially around wheel wells and fenders
    * Solution: Rust removal and treatment, may require panel replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on severity, can be $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
    
**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks in the dashboard, particularly on the passenger side
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Air Conditioner Evaporator Core Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Ineffective air conditioning, unpleasant odors from vents
    * Solution: Replace evaporator core
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 1996 Dodge Caravan is a generally reliable vehicle. However, some common issues such as intake manifold gasket failure, timing belt failure, and rust can be expensive to repair. It is recommended to have these issues addressed promptly to prevent further damage and ensure the vehicle's longevity. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and its severity.
