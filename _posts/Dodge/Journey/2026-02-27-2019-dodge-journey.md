# 2019 Dodge Journey Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Dodge Journey, buyers will find a budget-friendly midsize crossover that offers flexible seating and straightforward utility. However, reviewing owner experiences reveals a collection of recurring complaints centered around electrical accessories, rearview cameras, and occasional powertrain hiccups. While it serves as an affordable family transport option, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect electronics and mechanical components prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine produces 173 horsepower and 166 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. It provides basic, commuter-friendly performance but can feel underpowered when the vehicle is fully loaded with passengers and cargo.

**Engine Stalling and Ignition Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall, experience ignition failure, or trigger dashboard warning lights related to crankshaft sensors and ignition coils.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty ignition coil packs, spark plugs, or crankshaft position sensors as needed.

## Transmission

### 4-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 4-speed automatic transmission paired with the base 4-cylinder engine. It offers simple mechanical operation, though gear selection is limited compared to modern multi-speed units.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed automatic transmission typically paired with available front-wheel-drive or all-wheel-drive configurations. While it offers better highway efficiency than the base 4-speed, owners have occasionally reported durability and shifting concerns.

**Transmission Clunking and Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sporadic clunking noise or harsh engagement when shifting between reverse and drive.
- **Solution:** Inspect motor mounts and transmission components; replace worn mounts or service the transmission control module if symptoms persist.

**Premature Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may suffer sudden mechanical failure, resulting in a complete loss of drive capability while on the road.
- **Solution:** Perform a complete transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $7,471.96

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Backup Camera

Electrical complaints frequently involve infotainment glitches, such as unresponsive Bluetooth connectivity and malfunctioning rearview camera displays.

**Backup Camera Failure and Distortion**
- **Symptoms:** The rear backup camera screen may display a blank black or blue image, show an inverted view, flicker, or display an error message.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses, software modules, or replace the camera assembly as required.

**Bluetooth Initialization Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's infotainment system may fail to connect to Bluetooth for hands-free calling and media streaming.
- **Solution:** Power cycle the vehicle or pull and reset the radio fuse to reinitialize the system.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Airbags

Safety-related owner complaints highlight potential issues with occupant sensor functionality and interior restraint hardware wear.

**Passenger Seatbelt Sensor and Trim Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger seatbelt sensor may stop functioning correctly, accompanied by plastic trim pieces breaking off the seat frame.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the faulty seatbelt sensor and damaged anchor trim components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V552000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Journey&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Cracks in the microprocessor may cause the vehicle's rearview camera image not to display properly. A rearview image that does not display properly reduces the driver's view behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Dodge Journey remains an accessible choice for buyers seeking three-row seating on a strict budget. However, prospective owners should pay close attention during test drives, specifically verifying that the backup camera, infotainment electronics, and transmission operate smoothly without hesitation or warning lights. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic is strongly recommended.
