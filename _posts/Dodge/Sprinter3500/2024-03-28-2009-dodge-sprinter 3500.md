**2009 Dodge Sprinter 3500**

The 2009 Dodge Sprinter 3500 is a versatile commercial van known for its spacious interior, powerful engines, and rear-wheel drive configuration. It offers various engine and transmission options, including the 2.7L I4 turbodiesel engine with 154 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, and the 3.0L V6 turbodiesel engine with 188 horsepower and 325 lb-ft of torque. Transmission choices include a 5-speed automatic and a 6-speed manual.

Drivers appreciate the Sprinter 3500's durability, reliability, and cargo capacity, but some have encountered issues with various components over the years.

**Engine**

**2.7L I4 Turbodiesel**

* **Fuel Injection System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed gear changes, slipping gears
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Breakage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear, clunking noises
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harness Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Short circuits, blown fuses, component malfunctions
    * Solution: Repair or replace electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Door Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening rear doors, door opens unexpectedly
    * Solution: Replace door latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative heating or air conditioning, erratic temperature control
    * Solution: Replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative windshield wipers
    * Solution: Replace wiper motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2009 Dodge Sprinter 3500 has faced some issues, particularly with its fuel injection system, rear leaf springs, electrical harness, and climate control. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are not widespread and the Sprinter 3500 remains a reliable and capable commercial van for many drivers.
