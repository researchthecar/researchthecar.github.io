**2000 Dodge Viper**

The 2000 Dodge Viper is a high-performance sports car that was produced from 1999 to 2010. It was available with a 8.0L V10 engine rated at 450 horsepower and 490 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. Other engine and transmission options were not available for the 2000 model year.

The Viper is known for its aggressive styling, powerful engine, and impressive performance. However, it also has some drawbacks, such as its limited interior space, poor fuel economy, and high cost.
