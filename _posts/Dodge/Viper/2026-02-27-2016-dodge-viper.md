# 2016 Dodge Viper Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Dodge Viper is an iconic American supercar, known for its uncompromising performance and massive 8.4-liter V10 engine. While built for the track and enthusiast driving, used-car buyers should evaluate powertrain health and overall vehicle maintenance carefully. Because these vehicles are frequently driven hard, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is essential.

---

## Engine

### 8.4L V10

Equipped with a naturally aspirated 8.4-liter V10 engine producing 645 horsepower and 600 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling raw power and generally robust mechanical durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Tremec TR6060

Paired exclusively with a robust 6-speed manual transmission feeding power to the rear wheels. It offers direct, mechanical engagement suited for high-performance driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V736000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Viper&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the driver's air bag wires are pinched, the air bag may not deploy properly in the event of a crash necessitating air bag deployment, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dodge will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver's frontal air bag and clockspring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V750000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Viper&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A defective vibration weld on the passenger airbag chute may cause the passenger air bag door to detach during the air bag deployment. If the air bag door detaches during deployment, there is an increased the risk of injury to vehicle occupants.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger side upper panel assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Dodge Viper is a raw, specialized performance machine with no widespread recurring defect patterns in current reporting. Prospective buyers should focus on verifying proper routine maintenance, checking for track-use wear, and ensuring a comprehensive mechanical inspection before purchase.
