# 2020 Dodge Challenger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Dodge Challenger, buyers are looking at a classic muscle car available in a wide range of powerful trims, from efficient V6 daily drivers to supercharged V8 icons. While these vehicles offer thrilling performance and distinct styling, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback regarding specific mechanical and electrical glitches. Taking the time to inspect potential trouble spots during a pre-purchase test drive can help ensure a dependable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 (Pentastar)

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces around 303 horsepower and serves as the standard engine for entry-level models paired with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It offers a balanced mix of everyday drivability and reasonable fuel economy for daily commuting.

### 5.7L V8 (HEMI)

The 5.7-liter HEMI V8 pushes out up to 375 horsepower, providing classic muscle car acceleration and an unmistakable exhaust note. It is a robust, time-tested power plant frequently chosen by enthusiasts seeking traditional V8 performance.

### 6.4L V8 (392 HEMI)

The 392 cubic-inch V8 delivers a substantial 485 horsepower, offering track-ready capability straight from the factory. This engine is tailored for buyers prioritizing aggressive acceleration and high-performance thrills.

### 6.2L Supercharged V8 (Hellcat / Redeye)

The supercharged 6.2-liter HEMI V8 generates anywhere from 717 to nearly 800 horsepower in high-end configurations. It demands careful inspection due to the immense stress placed on its powertrain components during aggressive driving.

**Clutch and Driveline Stress**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported loud snapping noises or mechanical failures when pressing the clutch pedal to shift gears or come to a stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect the clutch assembly, transmission components, and driveshaft thoroughly for premature wear or component failure.

**Cooling Fan Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine cooling fans can suffer mechanical separation, causing damage to the radiator and rendering the vehicle inoperable.
- **Solution:** Check the electric cooling fans and radiator housing for structural integrity and ensure no contact or vibration issues exist.

### Wiring and Climate Control

Electrical complaints on the Challenger are generally isolated, but owners have reported occasional wiring harness chafing, climate control leaks, and minor body control glitches.

**Evaporator Core Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** The air conditioning system stops blowing cool air due to a refrigerant leak originating from the evaporator core.
- **Solution:** Test the AC system for proper cooling performance and inspect for hidden refrigerant leaks.

**Headlight Wiring Harness Rubbing**
- **Symptoms:** Headlight wiring can rub against the frame, causing bare metal-to-metal contact that blows out the headlight and malfunctions turn signals without warning.
- **Solution:** Inspect front wiring harnesses for secure routing and protective loom insulation to prevent chafing.

**Spontaneous Trunk Opening**
- **Symptoms:** The trunk lid may unlatch and open automatically while the vehicle is in motion.
- **Solution:** Check the trunk latch mechanism, key fob buttons, and body control module for intermittent electrical signals.

## Transmission

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic

The ZF-derived 8-speed automatic transmission is widely praised for its smooth shifting and responsive performance across V6 and V8 trims. It generally proves durable under normal operating conditions.

### Tremec 6-Speed Manual

The 6-speed manual transmission is paired exclusively with select V8 engines, offering a connected, old-school shifting experience for purists. Buyers should inspect the clutch disc and linkage for signs of aggressive use.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

The suspension setup provides a comfortable highway ride while maintaining composure in corners, though buyers should verify factory emergency tire equipment.

**Lack of Spare Tire**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles often come equipped with an emergency inflator kit rather than a traditional spare tire, leaving drivers stranded in the event of a severe sidewall blowout.
- **Solution:** Verify whether the vehicle includes a temporary spare tire package or consider purchasing an aftermarket spare for long-distance travel.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Rack

Steering operation is normally precise and responsive, though cold-weather bearing stiffness or binding has been noted in isolated reports.

**Cold Weather Steering Lockup**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may completely lock up or bind during initial turns in cold weather, accompanied by a grinding noise.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering shaft universal joints, bearings, and rack assembly for binding or lubrication breakdown.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V516000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Challenger&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** During manufacturing, the windshield may not have been properly bonded to the vehicle, allowing it to detach in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 212, "Windshield Mounting." A windshield that separates from the vehicle during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will remove and replace the front windshield urethane sealant, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Dodge Challenger remains an appealing choice for buyers seeking bold styling, comfortable cruising, and high-horsepower performance. While most mechanical systems prove reliable, shoppers should thoroughly evaluate high-performance powertrains for signs of clutch or cooling wear, and check electrical accessories like the air conditioning and wiring harnesses. Conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help secure a well-maintained example.
