# 2022 Dodge Challenger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Dodge Challenger, buyers will find a modern muscle car that blends iconic retro styling with everyday usability. While owner feedback points to a few isolated mechanical and body concerns, the available powertrain options remain a major draw for enthusiasts looking for classic performance.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces around 303 horsepower and 268 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard entry-level engine paired with rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It offers smooth everyday power delivery and generally dependable operation for daily driving.

**Accessory Belt Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported a whining noise coming from the accessory belt area after coming to a stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect the drive belt, pulleys, and tensioner, and replace components as necessary.

**Fuel Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A fuel leak has been reported originating from the gas tank area toward the engine.
- **Solution:** Thoroughly inspect fuel lines and tank connections, and replace damaged fuel system components immediately.

### 5.7L HEMI V8

The 5.7-liter HEMI V8 pushes out up to 375 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, providing traditional muscle car performance with strong mid-range acceleration. It is a proven, robust pushrod engine choice for buyers seeking classic V8 sound and capability.

### 6.4L HEMI V8

The 6.4-liter HEMI V8 delivers an impressive 485 horsepower and 475 lb-ft of torque for the R/T Scat Pack trim. It provides track-ready thrills while maintaining reasonable reliability under normal driving conditions.

### 6.2L Supercharged HEMI V8

The supercharged 6.2-liter HEMI V8 powers the high-output SRT Hellcat models, generating staggering horsepower outputs well north of 700. This specialized engine is built for maximum performance, requiring diligent maintenance and careful inspection by prospective buyers.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The TorqueFlite 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and pairs seamlessly with all engine options across the lineup. It is widely regarded as a durable and refined gearbox in modern modern performance applications.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The Tremec 6-speed manual transmission is available exclusively on select V8 trims for drivers who prefer a traditional three-pedal setup. It offers direct mechanical engagement and solid overall durability when properly maintained.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Suspension and wheel assemblies generally hold up well, though routine monitoring of tire health is advised.

**Slow Tire Air Leak**
- **Symptoms:** A slow leak can cause tire pressure to drop consistently over a few days despite routine checks and patches.
- **Solution:** Inspect the wheel rim, valve stem, and tire tread for punctures or damage, and repair or replace the tire as needed.

## Exterior

### Security Alarm

Electrical complaints are infrequent, though sporadic sensor or alarm glitches can occasionally occur.

**Random Alarm Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle alarm may trigger spontaneously for no apparent reason.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or technician check the door latches, hood switch, and security module sensitivity settings.

### Sideview Mirrors

Exterior panels and trim hold up well, though care should be taken when cleaning delicate surfaces like mirror glass.

**Scratch-Prone Mirror Glass**
- **Symptoms:** The exterior sideview mirror glass can scratch easily during routine washing, gradually obstructing visibility.
- **Solution:** Use dedicated automotive glass cleaners and soft microfiber towels, and replace mirror glass if visibility becomes impaired.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Standard safety systems operate as expected, with only minor convenience grievances reported by owners regarding belt retraction.

**Slow Seat Belt Retraction**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's seat belt can fail to retract quickly upon exit, causing it to slip outside the door frame.
- **Solution:** Inspect the retractor mechanism and clean the webbing if friction is causing sluggish retraction.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Dodge Challenger is a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking bold styling and diverse performance tiers, ranging from efficient V6 daily drivers to potent supercharged V8s. While a small number of isolated owner complaints touch on minor trim, electrical, and belt noises, the vehicle demonstrates reasonable overall dependability. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on fluid lines, tires, and mechanical components.
