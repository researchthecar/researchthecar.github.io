# 2019 Dodge Charger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Dodge Charger continues to offer a compelling mix of classic muscle car styling, spacious interior comfort, and strong rear-wheel-drive performance. Prospective buyers looking at used models will find a wide spectrum of engine configurations ranging from economical V6 options to high-output HEMI V8 powerplants. While the Charger is generally robust, used-car shoppers should be mindful of reported owner complaints concerning electrical issues, exterior lighting failures, and occasional powertrain or steering issues.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

This naturally aspirated V6 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard power plant for GT and SXT models. It balances everyday drivability with decent fuel economy for a full-size sedan.

### 5.7L HEMI V8

The 5.7L HEMI V8 delivers 370 horsepower and 395 lb-ft of torque, providing traditional muscle car performance on R/T trims. It is generally robust but can occasionally experience valvetrain or lifter wear.

**Internal Engine and Valvetrain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected internal engine failures, often involving lifters and valvetrain components requiring major repairs.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection, replacement of worn lifters, or potentially a complete internal engine rebuild.

### 6.4L HEMI V8

Displacing 6.4 liters, this powerful V8 churns out 485 horsepower and 475 lb-ft of torque for the Scat Pack trim. It offers thrilling acceleration paired with stiffened suspension tuning.

### 6.2L Supercharged HEMI V8

Found in the high-performance SRT Hellcat, this supercharged engine pushes out an incredible 707 horsepower. It is built for extreme performance enthusiasts and demands meticulous maintenance.

## Transmission

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired across virtually all engine options, this ZF-derived 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and responsive shifts. It is widely praised for both smooth everyday cruising and aggressive performance driving.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides ample stopping power, though some owners have noted vibration or unexpected engagement of electronic braking features.

**Brake Responsiveness and Shaking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced delayed brake response or vehicle vibrations when applying brakes at higher speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors for warping, and the automatic braking sensor systems.

## Steering

### Power Steering

Steering feel is typically direct and weighted well for a large sedan, but electronic steering assist issues have been reported by some owners.

**Power Steering Loss or Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden reduction or total loss of power steering assistance while driving, making the vehicle difficult to maneuver.
- **Solution:** Check electronic power steering sensors, wiring harnesses, and replace faulty steering components.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical complaints for the Charger occasionally highlight exterior lighting problems and wiring connector issues.

**Headlight Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights fail to activate or illuminate properly while driving, posing visibility risks at night.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight bulbs, wiring connectors, and control modules, replacing damaged components as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V512000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Charger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of a back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will update body control module software to disable stealth mode when the vehicle is shifted into "Reverse", free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V203000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Charger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The instrument cluster may not properly illuminate driver warnings. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 101, "Control and Displays." Failure of the instrument cluster to properly illuminate may prevent the driver from receiving warnings about the vehicle's systems, increasing.
- **What to do:** Chrysler has notified owners, and dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V758000: Service Brakes](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Charger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A damaged tire can suddenly lose air pressure and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** FCA US will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front wheels and inspect and replace the front tires, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V246000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Charger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Failure to provide the air bag warning information increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Chrysler has notified owners, and dealers will replace the driver and passenger sun visors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V198000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain:Inflator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Charger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An inflator rupture may result in sharp metal fragments striking occupants, resulting in injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace both side curtain air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Dodge Charger is an exciting and practical full-size sedan that caters well to both daily drivers and performance enthusiasts. While most mechanical systems prove durable, used car buyers should thoroughly check the vehicle's electrical components, lighting systems, and steering functionality during a pre-purchase inspection. Opting for a model with a clean service history will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
