**1999 Dodge Ram 2500**

The 1999 Dodge Ram 2500 is a full-size pickup truck with several engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 318 cubic-inch V8, with a 5.9-liter V8 and a 5.9-liter V10 available as upgrades. Transmission options included a 5-speed manual, a 4-speed automatic, and a 5-speed automatic.

The Ram 2500 was known for its strong engines, rugged design, and towing capabilities. However, it also had some issues that owners reported.

**Engine**

* **5.9-liter V8**
  * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Symptoms: Coolant leaking, overheating, poor engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Cylinder head cracking**
    * Symptoms: Coolant leaking, overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace cylinder heads
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **5.9-liter V10**
  * **Spark plug failure**
    * Symptoms: Misfiring, rough idle, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Exhaust manifold bolts breaking**
    * Symptoms: Exhaust leak, rattling noise
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifold bolts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-speed manual**
  * **Clutch failure**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, slipping gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **4-speed automatic**
  * **Transmission slipping**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, slipping gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **5-speed automatic**
  * **Torque converter failure**
    * Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Leaf spring breakage**
    * Symptoms: Sagging suspension, rough ride, loss of control
    * Solution: Replace leaf springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Ball joint failure**
    * Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises, loose steering, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace ball joints
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure**
    * Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting engine, battery discharge
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure**
    * Symptoms: Engine not starting, clicking noises
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on body panels**
    * Symptoms: Discoloration, bubbling paint, holes in panels
    * Solution: Repair or replace affected panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate latch failure**
    * Symptoms: Tailgate not closing or opening properly
    * Solution: Replace tailgate latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking**
    * Symptoms: Cracks in dashboard material
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power window failure**
    * Symptoms: Window not working, slow window operation
    * Solution: Replace window motor or switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1999 Dodge Ram 2500 is a popular pickup truck that is known for its strong engines, rugged design, and towing capabilities. However, it has some issues that can be expensive to repair. Some of the most common problems include intake manifold gasket failure, cylinder head cracking, spark plug failure, and transmission slipping. While the severity of these issues can vary, it is important to be aware of them before purchasing a Ram 2500.
