**Introduction:**

The 1998 Dodge Ram 2500 Van was a popular choice for commercial and personal use. It was powered by a 5.9L V8 engine, mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission. The van offered a spacious interior and a towing capacity of up to 10,000 pounds. While the Ram 2500 Van was praised for its power and durability, it has also been plagued by several common issues.

**Engine:**

* **5.9L V8**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, rough idle, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, white smoke from exhaust, overheating
        * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Slipping Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Suspension Coil Springs**
    * **Sagging Springs**
        * Description/Symptoms: Lowered ride height, reduced handling
        * Solution: Replace coil springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Alternator**
    * **Early Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge, electrical system problems
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Door Locks**
    * **Sticking Locks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing doors
        * Solution: Lubricate or replace door lock mechanisms
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **HVAC System**
    * **Blower Motor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: No airflow from vents
        * Solution: Replace blower motor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Blend Door Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Temperature control issues
        * Solution: Replace blend door actuator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1998 Dodge Ram 2500 Van has been plagued by several major issues, including intake manifold gasket failure, cylinder head gasket failure, and transmission problems. These issues can be costly to repair, and they can often lead to further problems down the road. While the Ram 2500 Van is a popular choice for commercial and personal use, it is important to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing one.
