# 2024 Dodge Hornet

### Overview
The 2024 Dodge Hornet is a new entry into the compact SUV market, offering a blend of sporty styling and performance, particularly with its available powertrains. As a first-year model, comprehensive long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, initial reports and analysis of its underlying platform (shared with Alfa Romeo) suggest a generally solid mechanical foundation. Owners can expect a modern vehicle with integrated technology and a focus on driving dynamics. While specific widespread issues are not yet prevalent, as with any new vehicle, minor teething problems are possible and will become clearer with more time on the road and accumulated mileage. ## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen may become unresponsive, navigation issues, Bluetooth connectivity problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset of the system.
- **Cost:** $0 - $150 (for potential dealer diagnostic/update if not covered under warranty)


### 12V Battery
**Early Battery Drain (Hybrid Models)**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, especially after prolonged periods of inactivity or when the hybrid system is not actively charging the 12V battery.
- **Solution:** Ensure regular driving to maintain charge, check for parasitic draws, battery replacement if faulty.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 (for battery replacement)

### Summary Verdict
The 2024 Dodge Hornet, being a new model, exhibits a generally positive initial reliability outlook. The core mechanical components, including the 2.0L Turbo and 1.3L Plug-in Hybrid powertrains, along with their respective automatic and dual-clutch transmissions, have not shown widespread issues. Suspension systems are also reported as robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that can typically be resolved with software updates, and a potential for 12V battery drain in hybrid models, which may require attention if persistent. Exterior and interior components are currently free from significant reported problems. As the vehicle matures, ongoing monitoring will be crucial to identify any emerging trends.
