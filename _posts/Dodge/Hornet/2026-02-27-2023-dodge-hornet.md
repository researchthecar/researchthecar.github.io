# 2023 Dodge Hornet

### Overview
The 2023 Dodge Hornet is a new entry into the compact SUV market, offering a blend of sporty styling and practical utility. As a first-year model, initial reliability data is still emerging, but early reports suggest a generally solid performance for its powertrain options. The vehicle aims to provide a compelling package for buyers seeking a more engaging driving experience in its segment. While specific long-term durability is yet to be fully established, the available engines and transmissions have shown promise in early assessments, with most reported issues being minor or related to initial software calibrations. Owners should remain vigilant for any unusual noises or performance changes, though widespread critical failures have not been a significant concern.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Dodge Hornet, being a new model, exhibits a promising start in terms of reliability. Powertrain components, including both the 2.0L Turbo and 1.3L Turbo engines paired with their respective automatic transmissions, have not shown significant widespread issues. The suspension and exterior components are also reporting no major concerns. The primary area with minor reported issues is the electrical system, specifically the infotainment unit, which can occasionally experience software glitches requiring updates or resets. Overall, the Hornet appears to be a reliable option for its segment, with most potential issues being minor and addressable through software or routine checks. Long-term durability will be a key factor to monitor as more data becomes available.
