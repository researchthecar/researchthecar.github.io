**1996 Dodge Ram 1500 Van**

The 1996 Dodge Ram 1500 Van is a full-size van that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 3.9L V6 that produced 175 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options included a 5.2L V8 that produced 220 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque, and a 5.9L V8 that produced 245 horsepower and 335 lb-ft of torque. The Ram 1500 Van was available with either a four-speed automatic transmission or a five-speed manual transmission.

People who like the 1996 Dodge Ram 1500 Van often praise its spacious interior, powerful engine options, and comfortable ride. However, some people have complained about the van's poor fuel economy and limited towing capacity.

**Summary**

The 1996 Dodge Ram 1500 Van is a reliable vehicle that has been praised for its spacious interior, powerful engine options, and comfortable ride. However, it does have some potential issues that should be considered before purchasing one. The most common issues include:

* Engine: Intake manifold gasket failure, exhaust manifold gasket failure, ignition coil failure, cylinder head gasket failure
* Transmission: Transmission slipping, clutch failure

The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but they should be taken into consideration when making a decision about whether or not to purchase a 1996 Dodge Ram 1500 Van.
