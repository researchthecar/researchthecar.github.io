**Introduction:**
The 2004 Dodge Ram 1500 is a full-size pickup truck known for its powerful engine options and rugged design. It comes equipped with a standard 3.7-liter V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission, while also offering a 4.7-liter V8 and a 5.9-liter V8 engine with a 5-speed automatic transmission. People appreciate its comfortable interior and impressive towing and payload capabilities but have reported some issues with its various components.

**Engine**
- **3.7-liter V6 Engine**
    **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, rough idling
    Solution: Replace intake manifold
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    Recalls: N/A
- **4.7-liter V8 Engine**
    **No Issues Found**
- **5.9-liter V8 Engine**
    **Head Gasket Failure:**
    Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    Solution: Replace head gasket
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**
- **4-speed Automatic Transmission**
    **Torque Converter Problems:**
    Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, rough shifting, shuddering
    Solution: Replace torque converter
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    Recalls: N/A
- **5-speed Automatic Transmission**
    **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
    Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, no shifting, stuck in one gear
    Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**
- **Rear Leaf Springs:**
    Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear
    Solution: Replace leaf springs
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
    Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**
- **Alternator Failure:**
    Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, warning light, dim headlights
    Solution: Replace alternator
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**
- **Body Rust:**
    Description/Symptoms: Corrosion on body panels, frame, undercarriage
    Solution: Repair or replace rusted areas
    Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies greatly depending on extent of damage
    Recalls: N/A

**Interior**
- **HVAC Control Panel Failure:**
    Description/Symptoms: Buttons not working, temperature control issues
    Solution: Replace HVAC control panel
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**
The 2004 Dodge Ram 1500 has received mixed reviews regarding its reliability. While some owners report minor issues such as electrical and interior problems, others have experienced more severe issues with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the issue at hand, but overall, the Ram 1500 can be a reliable workhorse with proper maintenance.
