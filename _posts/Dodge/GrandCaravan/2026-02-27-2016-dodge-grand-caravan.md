# 2016 Dodge Grand Caravan Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Dodge Grand Caravan remains a popular choice for budget-conscious families needing versatile cargo and passenger space. Powered by a dependable V6 engine paired with a traditional automatic transmission, it offers familiar drivability and substantial hauling capability. However, used car buyers should inspect the vehicle carefully, as common trouble spots include sliding door actuator failures, electrical issues, and occasional powertrain concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 (283 hp / 260 lb-ft)

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces 283 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for a minivan. This widely used powerplant is generally robust, though occasional reports note stalling and tick issues requiring mechanical attention.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected engine stalling while driving or coming to a stop, occasionally accompanied by a check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fault codes, inspect electronic throttle bodies or sensors, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a 6-speed automatic transmission, the vehicle delivers smooth highway cruising. Buyers should thoroughly test-shift the transmission during a pre-purchase inspection to rule out slipping or delayed engagement.

**Transmission Slipping and Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unintended shifting, hard engagement, sudden loss of gear engagement, or transmission pump failure leading to a loss of drive power.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace internal transmission components, torque converters, or the transmission assembly.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators & Latches

The electrical system oversees numerous convenience features like power sliding doors and instrumentation, which can develop annoying glitches over time.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sliding doors or power liftgates fail to lock, unlock, or open via remote or manual switches.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuators or integrated latch assemblies.

### Exterior Lighting & Body Trim

Exterior panels and lighting assemblies generally hold up well, though rear lamp sealing issues warrant a close look during a walkaround inspection.

**Tail Light Water Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Moisture accumulation inside the rear tail light housings or LED racetrack lighting, potentially causing short circuits.
- **Solution:** Seal, dry out, or replace the affected tail light assemblies.

## Safety

### Seating & Restraints

Cabin features emphasize utility with Stow 'n Go seating, but interior trim and headrest components require verification of proper operation.

**Active Headrest Deployment**
- **Symptoms:** Active headrests deploy unexpectedly without a rear-end collision, causing potential discomfort or driver distraction.
- **Solution:** Reset or replace the deployed active headrest mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V824000: Equipment](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Grand+Caravan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Chrysler (FCA US LLC) is recalling various Dodge, Chrysler, and RAM vehicles equipped with Kidde Plastic-Handle or Push Button 'Pindicator' Fire Extinguishers. These extinguishers may become clogged, preventing the extinguisher from discharging as expected or requiring excessive force to activate the extinguisher.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 16V461000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Grand+Caravan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the transmission loses hydraulic pressure, the vehicle may lose motive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the transmission pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V300000: Seats:Mid/Rear Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Grand+Caravan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The nuts on the third row seatbacks were incorrectly torqued and may become loose. If the third row seatback becomes loose, it may separate from the seat frame, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will re-torque the third row seatback nuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V595000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Hood:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Grand+Caravan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the affected vehicles, the bolts that attach the hood striker may loosen and, as a result, the hood may open unexpectedly while driving. If the hood opens while driving, the driver's vision may be obstructed, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will install new hood striker bolts with the proper torque, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V278000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Grand+Caravan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V876000: Equipment Adaptive/Mobility:Wheelchair Restraints/Securement:Latch/Anchor:](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Dodge&model=Grand+Caravan&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The retractors may not lock, preventing the wheelchair from being properly secured. An unsecured wheelchair can move during transit, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Dodge Grand Caravan remains an attractive budget-friendly option for families who prioritize maximum interior utility and seating flexibility. While its engine and transmission offer adequate performance, prospective buyers must carefully check all electronic accessories, power sliding doors, and transmission shifting behavior. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection will help catch common wear items before purchase.
