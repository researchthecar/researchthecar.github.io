**Introduction**

The 1992 Dodge Grand Caravan is a popular minivan that is known for its spacious interior and versatile seating arrangements. It is powered by a 3.0L V6 engine that produces 142 horsepower and 171 lb-ft of torque. The Grand Caravan is available with either a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 5-speed manual transmission.

People like the Grand Caravan for its comfortable ride, roomy interior, and affordable price. However, the vehicle has been known to have a number of issues, including:

**Engine**

- **3.0L V6 Engine - Excessive Oil Consumption:** Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume excessive amounts of oil, resulting in low oil levels and potential engine damage. Solution: The engine may need to be rebuilt or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+ Recalls: None Found
- **3.0L V6 Engine - Timing Chain Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail, causing the engine to stall or run erratically. Solution: The timing chain and related components will need to be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000 Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

- **4-Speed Automatic Transmission (A604):** Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or shudder during gear changes, especially when shifting from 1st to 2nd gear. Solution: The transmission may need to be repaired or replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000 Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Control Arm Bushings:** Description/Symptoms: The control arm bushings may wear out, causing the front end of the vehicle to shake or vibrate. Solution: The control arm bushings will need to be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600 Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:** Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use. Solution: The source of the battery drain will need to be identified and repaired. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500 Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Door Latch Failure:** Description/Symptoms: The door latches may fail, causing the doors to open unexpectedly. Solution: The door latches will need to be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Cracking:** Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may crack or warp over time. Solution: The dashboard may need to be replaced. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600 Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1992 Dodge Grand Caravan is a spacious and affordable minivan that has been known to have a number of issues, including excessive oil consumption, timing chain failure, and transmission problems. The severity and cost of the issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the condition of the vehicle.
