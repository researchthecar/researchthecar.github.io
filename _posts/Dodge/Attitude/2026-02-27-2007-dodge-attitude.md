# 2007 Dodge Attitude

### Overview
The 2007 Dodge Attitude, a subcompact car, generally presented a mixed bag of reliability. While its core mechanical components often proved durable for the era, certain areas were more prone to issues. Owners reported a range of concerns, particularly with the electrical system and some engine-related components, depending on the specific powertrain. The interior and exterior, while generally holding up, could show signs of wear. Overall, the Attitude offered a budget-friendly option, but potential buyers should be aware of common problem areas to ensure a well-maintained vehicle.

### 4-Speed Automatic
**Slipping Gears**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting between gears, especially under load.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or potential rebuild if internal damage is present.
- **Cost:** $250 - $700 (fluid/filter), $1500 - $3500 (rebuild)


## Suspension

### Standard Suspension
**Worn Ball Joints and Tie Rod Ends**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Faulty Window Regulators**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows failing to operate, slow operation, or making grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator motor and mechanism.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per window

**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light illuminated, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**Paint Fading/Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Discoloration or peeling of the paint, particularly on horizontal surfaces exposed to sun.
- **Solution:** Repainting or touch-up work.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000+

**Headlight Haze**
- **Symptoms:** Cloudy or yellowed headlights reducing visibility.
- **Solution:** Headlight restoration kit or replacement.
- **Cost:** $50 - $150 (restoration), $200 - $400 (replacement)


## Interior

### Upholstery and Trim
**Worn Seat Fabric**
- **Symptoms:** Tears, thinning, or discoloration of seat upholstery.
- **Solution:** Seat cover installation or professional upholstery repair/replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $500

**Dashboard Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Cracks appearing on the dashboard surface, especially in hot climates.
- **Solution:** Dashboard cover or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Dodge Attitude is a budget-friendly subcompact that can offer decent value if well-maintained. Its 1.6L engine and manual transmission are generally reliable. However, potential buyers should be cautious of the automatic transmission, which can develop slipping issues. Electrical gremlins, such as faulty window regulators and alternators, are also noted concerns. Exterior paint and interior wear are common for vehicles of this age and class. Addressing these potential issues proactively can lead to a more dependable ownership experience.
