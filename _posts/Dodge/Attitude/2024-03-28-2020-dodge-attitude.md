**Introduction**

The 2020 Dodge Attitude is a budget-friendly sedan offered with a 1.2-liter 3-cylinder engine paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. Praised for its affordability and fuel efficiency, the Attitude has received criticism for its lack of power and outdated interior.

**Engine**

**1.2-Liter 3-Cylinder**

* **Oil Consumption:** Excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace or repair piston rings or other internal engine components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Failure:** Premature failure of the timing chain, resulting in engine misfires, reduced power, and stalling.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Grinding Gears:** Difficulty shifting gears, accompanied by a grinding noise.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the transmission synchronizers and other related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut/Shock Mount Failure:** Worn or damaged strut or shock mounts, causing a rattling noise and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the strut or shock mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+ per axle
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar Link Failure:** Failure of the sway bar links, leading to excessive body roll and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per link
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Overloads:** Overloading of the electrical system, resulting in blown fuses or damage to electrical components.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair any faulty wires or electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:** Rapid battery drain when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Test and replace the battery or identify and repair any parasitic electrical draws.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:** Failure of the door handles, making it difficult or impossible to open the doors.
    * Solution: Replace the door handle assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per handle
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Trunk Lid Seal Failure:** Failure of the trunk lid seal, allowing water or dust to enter the trunk.
    * Solution: Replace the trunk lid seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Issues:** Malfunctioning climate control system, resulting in inconsistent or ineffective heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the climate control unit, blower motor, or other related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure:** Failure of the power window motors, switches, or wiring.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the affected components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per window
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2020 Dodge Attitude has several common issues, primarily affecting the engine, transmission, and suspension. While the issues can be costly to repair, they are generally not severe and can be resolved. However, it is essential for owners to be aware of these issues and address them promptly to avoid potential damage or further expenses.
