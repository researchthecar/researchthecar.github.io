# 2017 Dodge Attitude

### Overview
The 2017 Dodge Attitude, a subcompact sedan, generally presents a mixed bag in terms of long-term reliability. While it offers a budget-friendly entry into the automotive market, certain areas have shown more propensity for issues than others. Owners have reported a range of concerns, primarily centered around the powertrain and some electrical components. However, for its class and price point, the Attitude can be a dependable option if well-maintained, with many common problems being addressable with routine servicing and timely repairs. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture for potential owners and current drivers.

---


## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant smell, visible puddles under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, radiator, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $150 - $350


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)
**Hesitation or Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of acceleration, jerky movements during acceleration or deceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement; potential transmission rebuild or replacement in severe cases.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2500+

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Low transmission fluid level, warning lights, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, gaskets, or transmission lines.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600


## Suspension

### Standard Suspension
**Worn Ball Joints/Tie Rod Ends**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, loose steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Faulty Power Window Regulators**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of power window motor and regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $450

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Dodge Attitude, while an economical choice, exhibits some common reliability concerns typical of its segment. The engine and transmission are the most frequent sources of reported issues, with cooling system leaks, ignition coil failures, and CVT-related problems being notable. Suspension components like ball joints and tie rods can also wear prematurely. Electrical gremlins, particularly with power windows and the infotainment system, are not uncommon. Exterior and interior components generally fare better, with no widespread critical failures documented. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor in the possibility of repairs, especially concerning the powertrain, when considering this vehicle.
