# 2008 Dodge Attitude

### Overview
The 2008 Dodge Attitude, a subcompact car, generally presented a mixed bag of reliability. While it offered an affordable entry point and decent fuel economy, certain areas required attention. Owners reported a few recurring issues, particularly concerning the powertrain and some electrical components. However, many of the reported problems were not widespread, suggesting that individual vehicle maintenance and specific production batches might have played a significant role. Overall, for its segment and price point, the Attitude could be a reasonable choice, but potential buyers should be aware of the common trouble spots to ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---


## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seals and gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $150 - $350

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant puddles under the car, steam from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of radiator, hoses, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


### 4-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking or rough shifts between gears, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter change, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $250 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard Suspension
**Worn Ball Joints/Tie Rod Ends**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, loose steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension components.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Faulty Alternator**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, dead battery.
- **Solution:** Alternator replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500

**Power Window Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Windows not rolling up or down, slow operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window motor or regulator.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** N/A


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2008 Dodge Attitude, while budget-friendly, exhibits some common reliability concerns typical of its class. The engine can be prone to oil and coolant leaks, requiring attention to seals and cooling system components. Transmissions, both manual and automatic, may experience wear issues like clutch problems or rough shifting. Suspension components such as ball joints and tie rods are also noted for wear. Electrically, alternators and power window regulators are potential failure points. While not overwhelmingly problematic, these issues suggest that regular inspections and prompt repairs are advisable for owners of the 2008 Attitude to maintain its functionality and longevity.
