# 2021 Polestar Polestar 1 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Polestar 1 is a low-volume, high-performance plug-in hybrid GT car that pairs a turbocharged and supercharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine with dual rear electric motors. Built with a carbon-fiber-reinforced polymer body and premium components, it offers a rare combination of electrified performance and exotic styling. Because of its exclusivity and complex powertrain architecture, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history and ensure access to specialized service technicians.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged & Supercharged Inline-4 Hybrid

This 2.0-liter internal combustion engine utilizes both a turbocharger and a supercharger to deliver strong baseline output before combining with the electric drive system. It provides high-specific-output performance for the front wheels, though its mechanical complexity demands strict adherence to maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission routes power from the internal combustion engine while working in tandem with the hybrid architecture. It generally delivers smooth gear changes suited to grand touring, though maintenance history should be verified on pre-owned models.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V683000: Seat Belts:Front:Retractor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Polestar&model=Polestar+1&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V926000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Polestar&model=Polestar+1&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Defective high-voltage battery cells may cause the battery to overheat when fully charged. The high-voltage battery overheating increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery cells, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V361000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Polestar&model=Polestar+1&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Defective high-voltage battery cells may cause the battery to overheat when fully charged. The high-voltage battery overheating increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery pack and restore the electric driving range as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Polestar 1 is an exotic, low-volume performance hybrid that appeals strongly to collectors and enthusiasts seeking unique design and advanced engineering. Given its niche production and complex dual-powertrain setup, buyers should prioritize professional inspections and verified service records to ensure long-term peace of mind.
