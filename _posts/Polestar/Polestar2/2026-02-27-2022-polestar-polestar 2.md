# 2022 Polestar Polestar 2

### Overview
The 2022 Polestar Polestar 2, an all-electric performance sedan, has generally demonstrated strong reliability, reflecting its modern engineering and focused design. As an electric vehicle, its powertrain architecture differs significantly from traditional internal combustion engine cars, leading to fewer mechanical components prone to wear. Early models have shown a good track record, with most reported issues being minor or related to software. The focus on build quality and advanced technology contributes to a positive ownership experience, though like any new technology, some software glitches and accessory-related concerns have been noted. Overall, the Polestar 2 presents a compelling option for those seeking a reliable and technologically advanced electric vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Polestar Polestar 2 exhibits a high level of reliability, particularly in its electric powertrain and core mechanical components. The primary areas where minor issues have been observed are within the electrical system, specifically related to software glitches in the infotainment and occasional battery drain, which are often resolvable with software updates. The absence of widespread problems in the engine, transmission, suspension, exterior, and interior components indicates robust engineering and quality control. Owners can generally expect a dependable driving experience with minimal unexpected repairs outside of standard maintenance. The vehicle's strong reliability score makes it a solid choice in the premium electric vehicle segment.
