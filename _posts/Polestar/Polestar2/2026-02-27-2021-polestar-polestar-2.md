# 2021 Polestar Polestar 2 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Polestar 2 is an all-electric fastback that combines sleek Scandinavian design with engaging driving dynamics. As an early model year for this EV, prospective buyers should pay close attention to software stability, infotainment behavior, and known hardware quirks reported by owners. While it offers strong performance and a unique alternative to traditional luxury EVs, thoroughly verifying maintenance records and software update history is recommended.

---

## Engine

### Single Motor Electric Drive

Single-motor electric propulsion variant powering the front or rear wheels depending on the model year, offering efficient everyday driving range and smooth power delivery with minimal mechanical upkeep.

### Dual Motor Electric Drive

High-performance dual-motor electric setup providing all-wheel drive and substantial horsepower and torque for quick acceleration and dynamic handling capabilities.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Single-speed direct-drive automatic transmission designed for electric vehicles, delivering seamless, gearless acceleration and requiring very little routine maintenance.

### Axles and CV Joints

Drivetrain components responsible for transferring power from the electric motors to the wheels.

**Clicking Noises and Rear Axle Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Audible clicking, tapping, or ticking noises originating from the rear of the vehicle, particularly noticeable when braking at low speeds or turning sharply.
- **Solution:** Inspect rear axles and CV joints for wear; replace affected components if excessive play or mechanical failure is found.

## Electrical

### Back Over Prevention

Driver assistance systems centered around rear visibility and parking sensors, which have frequently been impacted by software-related glitches.

**Backup Camera Failure and Black Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment touchscreen goes blank, freezes, or displays a 'camera unavailable' error message when shifting into reverse, rendering the backup camera non-functional.
- **Solution:** Install the latest over-the-air (OTA) software updates or visit a service center to resolve underlying infotainment bugs.

### Charging System

Electrical architecture managing high-voltage power distribution and vehicle charging components.

**On-Board Charger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle fails to charge when plugged into standard AC home or public chargers under normal operating conditions.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection and replacement of the faulty on-board charger unit by a certified service center.

## Summary Verdict

The Polestar 2 is an appealing choice for buyers looking to enter the used electric vehicle market with distinct style and strong performance. Prospective owners should carefully verify that all software updates have been successfully applied and check for any history of infotainment reboots, backup camera glitches, or drivetrain noises. A pre-purchase inspection focusing on the charging system and axle components will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
