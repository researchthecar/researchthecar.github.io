**Introduction:**

The 2022 Polestar Polestar 2 is a fully electric luxury sedan that boasts an impressive 408 horsepower and 487 lb-ft of torque. It offers a captivating blend of performance, technology, and sustainability. However, like any vehicle, it has faced its share of issues.

**Electric Motor**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Squeaky Front Suspension**
* Description/Symptoms: Noises from the front end of the vehicle when driving over bumps or uneven surfaces.
* Solution: Replacement of front suspension components, such as struts or bushings.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Uneven Tire Wear**
* Description/Symptoms: Premature and uneven wear on tires, particularly on the inner or outer edges.
* Solution: Inspection and alignment of the suspension system, possible replacement of alignment-related components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Excessive battery discharge when the vehicle is parked, resulting in difficulty starting or accessory failure.
* Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system components, possible software updates.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* Recalls: NHTSA ID: 22V-922, NHTSA ID: 22V-906

**Headlight Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: Dimming or flickering headlights, complete headlight failure.
* Solution: Replacement of faulty headlight components or software updates.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Panel Gap Misalignment**
* Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent spacing between body panels, particularly around doors, trunk, or hood.
* Solution: Adjustment or replacement of body panels or door hinges.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Issues**
* Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning or erratic climate control system, including heating, ventilation, or air conditioning.
* Solution: Replacement of faulty sensors or actuators, software updates.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System Glitches**
* Description/Symptoms: Lagging, freezing, or unresponsive infotainment system, including navigation, Bluetooth, or media playback.
* Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment module.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: NHTSA ID: 22V-403, NHTSA ID: 22V-340

**Summary:**

The 2022 Polestar Polestar 2 has encountered several issues, including suspension, electrical, and cosmetic problems. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. It is important to address any issues promptly to maintain optimal performance and safety.
