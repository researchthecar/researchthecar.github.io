# 2016 BMW 550i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 550i xDrive offers a blend of luxury and high-performance twin-turbo V8 power. However, prospective buyers should approach this performance-focused model with diligence, as high-output luxury European vehicles often require vigilant maintenance and can incur significant ownership costs as they age.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally swift acceleration. Buyers should prioritize thorough mechanical inspections and meticulous service records due to the complex nature of this high-performance powerplant.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and delivers seamless, rapid shifts paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust when fluid maintenance intervals are respected.

The 2016 BMW 550i xDrive is an enticing luxury sedan for buyers seeking V8 performance and all-weather capability.
