# 2016 BMW 428i xDrive Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 428i xDrive Gran Coupe, equipped with the N20 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine and an 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission, generally presents a strong reliability profile. This model year benefits from BMW's refined engineering, offering a blend of performance and luxury. While most owners report a positive experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen occasional attention. Understanding these potential issues can help owners with proactive maintenance and informed repairs, ensuring the longevity and enjoyment of this sporty four-door coupe. The xDrive all-wheel-drive system adds to its capability, particularly in adverse weather conditions.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating, low coolant level warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat assembly.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,000

**PCV Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, increased oil consumption, whistling noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the crankcase ventilation valve (PCV) assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, warning lights on the dashboard, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 428i xDrive Gran Coupe is a generally reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain. The robust 2.0L turbocharged engine and the highly regarded 8-speed automatic transmission are known for their durability. Potential concerns primarily revolve around the engine, specifically the timing chain, water pump, and PCV system, which can require attention over time. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain and infotainment system quirks, are also noted but not universally experienced. The chassis, exterior, and interior components tend to hold up well. With diligent maintenance and addressing any emerging issues promptly, the 428i xDrive Gran Coupe should provide a satisfying ownership experience.
