# 2016 BMW 428i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 428i xDrive Gran Coupe combines sporty styling with all-weather capability and practical four-door access. Prospective used car buyers generally find this vehicle engaging to drive, though it is important to carefully inspect service records and potential mechanical wear points common to modern luxury European cars.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a strong balance of performance and efficiency. Buyers should verify regular maintenance history, particularly regarding timing chain guides on early iterations.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This smooth 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp shifts and pairs well with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust when fluid changes are observed.
