# 2009 BMW 335i xDrive

### Overview
The 2009 BMW 335i xDrive, equipped with its potent twin-turbocharged inline-six engines and xDrive all-wheel-drive system, offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics, like many performance-oriented vehicles of its era, it has specific areas that warrant attention from an ownership perspective. This data focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for prospective and current owners. Understanding these potential pitfalls can help in proactive maintenance and budgeting for repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (N54)
**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Wastegate Rattle**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine bay, especially on deceleration or at low RPMs.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger wastegate actuators or entire turbochargers.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000+

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump (HPFP) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Stalling while driving, long crank times, check engine light, loss of power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks, rough idle, blue smoke from exhaust, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Water Pump Failure (Electric)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, check engine light, reduced engine performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat assembly.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Idrive Controller Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Unresponsive iDrive system, erratic screen behavior, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the iDrive controller unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 BMW 335i xDrive is a capable all-wheel-drive performance sedan. Its primary reliability concerns are concentrated within the engine, particularly with the N54 twin-turbocharged powerplant. Issues such as fuel injector failure, turbocharger wastegate problems, and high-pressure fuel pump failures are relatively common and can be costly to repair. The electric water pump is another component that may require attention. While the transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior systems are generally robust, owners should be aware of the potential for these specific engine-related repairs. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for mitigating these issues and ensuring the longevity of the vehicle.
