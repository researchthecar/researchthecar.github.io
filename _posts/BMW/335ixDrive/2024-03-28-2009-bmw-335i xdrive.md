**Introduction**

The 2009 BMW 335i xDrive is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a 3.0L N54 inline-6 twin-turbocharged engine producing 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a 6-speed manual transmission, while a 6-speed automatic transmission is optional. The xDrive all-wheel-drive system provides enhanced traction in all weather conditions.

Drivers praise the 335i xDrive for its impressive acceleration, handling, and luxurious interior. However, it has also been known for having some issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L N54 Engine**

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced power
Solution: Replace high-pressure fuel pump
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
Recalls: N/A

**Wastegate Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise coming from the turbochargers
Solution: Replace wastegates
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Transmission Fluid Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the transmission housing
Solution: Replace transmission seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: N/A

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Mechatronics Unit Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, hard shifts, loss of gears
Solution: Replace mechatronics unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

**Control Arm Bushings**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking or popping noises when turning or going over bumps
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

**Brake Light Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Brake lights not working, cruise control not working
Solution: Replace brake light switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

**Windshield Washer Pump Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Windshield washer fluid not spraying
Solution: Replace windshield washer pump
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

**Sunroof Control Module Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not operating properly
Solution: Replace sunroof control module
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: N/A

**Summary**

The 2009 BMW 335i xDrive is a high-performance sedan with potential issues in the engine, transmission, and other components. While some issues, such as the high-pressure fuel pump failure, can be costly to repair, others are relatively minor. It is important for potential owners to be aware of these issues and factor them into their purchase decision.
