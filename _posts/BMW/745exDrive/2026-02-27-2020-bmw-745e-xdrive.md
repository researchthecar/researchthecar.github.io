# 2020 BMW 745e xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 745e xDrive brings plug-in hybrid efficiency to BMW's flagship luxury sedan lineup, blending a turbocharged inline-six engine with an electric motor and standard all-wheel drive. As a sophisticated luxury vehicle laden with complex electronics and advanced powertrain components, prospective used-car buyers should approach with careful inspection. While initial owner reports show very few specific mechanical complaints, maintaining a high-end European plug-in hybrid out of warranty requires diligence regarding its specialized electrical systems and overall upkeep.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-In Hybrid

This powertrain pairs a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine with an integrated electric motor, producing a combined output of around 389 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers a smooth, powerful driving experience with the capability for electric-only driving, though buyers should verify the health of both the internal combustion components and the high-voltage battery system.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features an integrated electric motor and routes power seamlessly to the intelligent xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is widely regarded as a responsive and durable unit in modern BMW applications, contributing to both refined cruising and efficient power delivery.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V589000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745e&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without an audible warning, the driver may forget to buckle the seat belt, increasing their risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW 745e xDrive is a technologically advanced luxury sedan that offers an appealing mix of performance and plug-in hybrid efficiency. Because owner complaint data for this specific model year is sparse, buyers are advised to prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified BMW technician, paying close attention to the high-voltage battery status, electronics, and service history.
