# 2022 BMW 745e xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 745e xDrive offers a sophisticated plug-in hybrid luxury experience, combining a turbocharged inline-six engine with an electric motor for seamless performance and efficiency. As a high-end flagship sedan, buyers looking at the used market should evaluate both its advanced powertrain options and general vehicle systems to ensure long-term peace of mind.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-in Hybrid (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six plug-in hybrid engine produces approximately 389 combined horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as a smooth and robust powertrain option for this flagship sedan.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is widely praised for its quick, refined shifts and strong overall durability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V683000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745e&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A short-circuit in the battery cell can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the affected battery cell module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 745e xDrive is a technologically advanced luxury sedan that combines robust engineering with plug-in hybrid efficiency. Used-car buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's complex electronic and hybrid systems prior to purchase, but standard powertrain components remain solid.
