# 2018 BMW 420i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury sport coupe or convertible, owner feedback and safety reports provide essential insights into what to expect behind the wheel. While these vehicles deliver engaging driving dynamics and refined styling, prospective buyers should review key system areas including cooling and occupant safety to ensure a well-maintained vehicle.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (B48) produces approximately 181 horsepower and 214 lb-ft of torque, offering a balanced blend of efficiency and everyday performance. Routine monitoring of cooling system components is recommended for reliable long-term ownership.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected puddles of fluid underneath the vehicle accompanied by illuminated coolant warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect hoses, lines, and the water pump for leaks, and replace any compromised cooling system components.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected losses of motive power or engine stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery and cooling system pressure issues; replace faulty pumps or hoses as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid gear changes and pairs effectively with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It generally exhibits solid reliability when maintained with regular fluid service.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant safety systems generally operate as intended, though occasional latch or sensor irregularities have been noted by owners.

**Seat Belt Retraction and Alert Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted instances where seat belts failed to retract properly or failed to restrain as needed, alongside false seatbelt warning alerts.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies, buckle sensors, and retraction mechanisms, and replace faulty components.

Overall, this vehicle offers a rewarding driving experience complemented by upscale cabin appointments. Prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection, specifically checking the cooling system and electronic components, to ensure trouble-free motoring.
