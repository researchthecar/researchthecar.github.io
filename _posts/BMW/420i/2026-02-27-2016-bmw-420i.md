# 2016 BMW 420i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 420i offers a blend of luxury and sporty handling, powered by turbocharged four-cylinder engine options paired with refined automatic or manual transmissions. While overall owner satisfaction is generally high, used car buyers should evaluate maintenance histories carefully, as European luxury vehicles demand diligent upkeep to avoid unexpected mechanical and electrical snags as mileage climbs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48/N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 181 horsepower and 199 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration and respectable efficiency. It is generally robust when maintained properly, though early versions warrant a check on timing chain guides.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes and pairs exceptionally well with the powertrain. It enjoys a strong reputation for long-term durability across BMW's lineup.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission offers an engaging, traditional shifting experience for enthusiast drivers. It is mechanically sturdy, though clutch wear depends heavily on prior driving habits.
