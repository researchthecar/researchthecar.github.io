**Introduction**

The 1995 BMW 740iL is a luxurious sedan powered by a 4.0L V8 engine paired with a 5-speed automatic transmission. It produces 282 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The 740iL is known for its comfortable cabin, refined ride, and powerful engine. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of potential issues.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8**

* **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, rough idle
  * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
* **Oil Filter Housing Gasket Leak:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the top of the engine
  * Solution: Replace oil filter housing gasket
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from various seals or gaskets
  * Solution: Identify and replace leaking component
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, or delayed engagement when shifting gears
  * Solution: Replace torque converter
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+

**Suspension**

* **Rear Air Suspension Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle sagging, rough ride quality
  * Solution: Replace air suspension components (e.g., compressor, bags, sensors)
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500 per side

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains rapidly, even when the vehicle is not in use
  * Solution: Identify and replace faulty component (e.g., alternator, parasitic draw)
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Headlights or other electrical components not functioning
  * Solution: Replace headlight switch
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Exterior**

* **Fog Light Wiring Harness Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Fog lights not working
  * Solution: Replace fog light wiring harness
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Display Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Display on the climate control panel becomes dim or inoperable
  * Solution: Replace climate control display unit
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Cruise Control Intermittent Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Cruise control not engaging or disengaging correctly
  * Solution: Replace cruise control switch or sensor
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250

**Summary**

The 1995 BMW 740iL is a luxurious sedan with a powerful engine and comfortable ride. However, it is not without its potential issues. Some of the more severe and costly issues include valve stem seal failure, transmission fluid leaks, and rear air suspension failure. Other issues, such as headlight switch failure and climate control display failure, are less severe and less expensive to fix. Overall, the 740iL is a reliable vehicle with a good track record, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
