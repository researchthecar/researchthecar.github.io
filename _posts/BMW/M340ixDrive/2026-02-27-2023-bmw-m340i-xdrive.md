# 2023 BMW M340i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M340i xDrive blends impressive performance with daily drivability, featuring a robust turbocharged inline-six engine and a smooth automatic transmission. While it represents a modern, highly capable sport sedan, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback and keep up with routine maintenance to ensure long-term dependability.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its strong performance and strong mechanical reputation among modern luxury sport sedans.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and works in tandem with the rear-biased xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It delivers quick, refined shifts and generally proves durable under normal operating conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M340i xDrive is an exceptional high-performance sport sedan that generally exhibits solid build quality and refined engineering. Buyers should inspect any prospective vehicle thoroughly, verify service records, and conduct a comprehensive test drive to ensure everything operates as expected.
