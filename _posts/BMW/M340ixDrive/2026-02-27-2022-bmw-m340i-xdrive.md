# 2022 BMW M340i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW M340i xDrive delivers exhilarating performance, sharp handling, and a sophisticated cabin, making it a compelling choice for sport sedan enthusiasts. As owners log miles, however, a handful of isolated mechanical and electrical complaints have emerged in owner reports. Buyers evaluating a used model should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to check for early signs of component wear.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine (B58) produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and strong overall reliability. It pairs smoothly with the all-wheel-drive system, though buyers should watch for occasional cooling component wear.

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported coolant reservoir leaks and water pump failures leading to potential engine overheating risks.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system for leaks, and replace faulty water pumps or coolant reservoirs as needed.

**Starter Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may require multiple attempts to start while all other electrical components turn on normally.
- **Solution:** Test the starting system and replace a faulty starter motor if ignition failure persists.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless shifts and pairs effectively with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It generally exhibits strong durability under normal driving conditions.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering system complaints are rare, but intermittent electronic assist failures have been noted in isolated owner reports.

**Intermittent Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A power steering failure warning message illuminates on the dashboard, making the steering wheel very difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Restart the vehicle or have the electronic steering control modules diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Safety

### Air Bag Wiring

Safety system components generally perform well, though occasional wiring harness faults can trigger warning lights.

**Passenger Restraint System Warning**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard displays a warning message regarding a passenger restraint system malfunction due to a wiring issue.
- **Solution:** Inspect and repair or replace the affected passenger knee airbag wiring harness.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW M340i xDrive combines thrilling turbocharged performance with everyday luxury, though prospective buyers should remain mindful of reported cooling system, starter, and electrical glitches. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable technician will help ensure these isolated issues are absent before finalizing a purchase.
