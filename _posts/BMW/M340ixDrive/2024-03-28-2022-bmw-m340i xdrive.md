**2022 BMW M340i xDrive**

The 2022 BMW M340i xDrive is a high-performance sedan powered by a 3.0-liter inline-six engine producing 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The M340i is praised for its sporty handling, powerful engine, and luxurious interior. However, it has faced some issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter Inline-Six**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, rough idle, decreased engine power
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leak from Valve Cover Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil seeping from the area around the valve cover
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering felt during acceleration or at low speeds
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the cause of the battery drain (e.g., parasitic draw)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Glitches, slow response, or freezing of the infotainment system
    * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 BMW M340i xDrive is a capable and enjoyable vehicle, but it has faced some issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. While some of these issues may not be severe or costly to fix, they can still cause inconvenience to owners. Potential buyers should research these issues and consider their severity and potential cost before making a purchase decision.
