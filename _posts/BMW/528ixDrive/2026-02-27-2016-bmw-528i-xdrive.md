# 2016 BMW 528i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury sedan, understanding the common mechanical and electrical tendencies of the vehicle is essential. The 2016 BMW 528i xDrive offers a sophisticated driving experience with capable all-wheel drive, but prospective buyers should review owner-reported issues involving electrical accessories, cooling system components, and safety restraint warnings. While many owners enjoy refined performance, keeping an eye out for these specific recurring trouble spots can help ensure a smoother ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing a responsive balance of power and efficiency for daily driving. It is generally robust, though buyers should monitor the cooling and fuel systems closely for signs of wear.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Sweet-smelling fluid under the vehicle, low coolant warning lights, or engine overheating due to leaking plastic cooling components.
- **Solution:** Inspect all plastic coolant hoses, fittings, and the expansion tank; replace any compromised components immediately to prevent engine damage.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power seamlessly to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, offering quick gear changes and strong highway fuel economy with minimal widespread mechanical complaints.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical accessories on this generation can occasionally develop frustrating issues, particularly with exterior lighting seals.

**Headlight Condensation and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Visible moisture buildup or condensation inside the headlight housing during colder months, eventually leading to electrical short circuits and headlight failure.
- **Solution:** Check headlight housing seals for integrity or replace the headlight assembly if internal moisture has damaged the electronics.
- **Cost:** $4000

## Safety

### Air Bag and Restraint System

Safety systems generally perform well, though occasional sensor faults within the cabin harness can trigger warning lights.

**Passenger Restraint System Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning light on the dashboard and passenger restraint system malfunction messages.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the supplemental restraint system codes, inspect seatbelt tensioner wiring, and replace faulty sensors or connectors.

## Recall Check

- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver's seat belt assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=528i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V718000: Child Seat](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=528i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles may fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." If the child seat is not properly attached to the lower anchors, it can increase the child's risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, repair the inboard lower anchor on the left rear seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V608000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=528i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An improperly sealed electrical connector on the water pump may be exposed to water and short circuit. An electrical short increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the water pump and plug connector as necessary, and install a protective shield, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 528i xDrive is a comfortable and well-appointed luxury sedan, but potential buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the cooling system, headlight housing seals, and electrical safety warnings. Prioritizing a well-documented maintenance history will significantly mitigate unexpected repair expenses.
