**Introduction**

The 2016 BMW 528i xDrive is a mid-size luxury sedan powered by a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine producing 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 528i is praised for its refined driving experience, comfortable interior, and advanced safety features. However, it has received criticism for its expensive maintenance costs and lack of certain desirable amenities.

**Engine**

* **2.0L 4-cylinder**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, loss of power, and rough idling
        * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, ticking noises, and possible engine damage
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic**
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, harsh shifts, and transmission slipping
        * Solution: Replace shift solenoids
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery fails to hold a charge, difficulty starting the car
    * Solution: Replace battery and inspect charging system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows inoperative
    * Solution: Replace window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ per assembly
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system freezes, inaccurate directions
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rearview Camera Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera displays a blank screen or distorted image
    * Solution: Replace rearview camera
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 BMW 528i xDrive has a range of potential issues, including engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior problems. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to repair, while others can be more costly and affect the vehicle's performance and reliability. While the 528i offers a refined driving experience and advanced features, it is important to be aware of its potential maintenance issues before making a purchase decision.
