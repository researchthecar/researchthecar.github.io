# 2016 BMW 650i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 650i Gran Coupe combines sleek styling, luxurious interior accommodations, and forceful twin-turbo performance into an executive four-door package. While it offers a refined driving experience, used-car buyers should approach luxury German grand tourers of this vintage with careful inspection, as specialized maintenance and complex systems can lead to expensive ownership costs over time.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (N63TU)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration. This updated N63TU engine features revised internal components compared to earlier iterations, but still demands rigorous maintenance schedules and proactive monitoring for cooling and oil system health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission features steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters and delivers smooth, rapid gear changes across various drive modes. It is widely regarded as a robust and dependable unit in modern BMW applications when serviced appropriately.

The 2016 BMW 650i Gran Coupe is a compelling luxury purchase for drivers prioritizing high-end features and V8 performance, but prospective buyers should secure a thorough pre-purchase inspection from a marque specialist.
