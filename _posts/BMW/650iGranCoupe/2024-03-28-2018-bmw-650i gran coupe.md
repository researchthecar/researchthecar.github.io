**2018 BMW 650i Gran Coupe**

The 2018 BMW 650i Gran Coupe is a luxurious and performance-oriented vehicle that boasts a powerful engine, sleek design, and spacious interior. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine that produces 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to the rear wheels.

Owners of the 650i Gran Coupe have praised its thrilling performance, comfortable ride, and stylish exterior. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle that are worth considering.

**Engine**

* **4.4L Twin-Turbo V8**
    * **Oil Leak:** Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning oil smell and low oil levels. Requires replacement of the gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found


**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Issues:** Transmission hesitates or jerks when shifting gears. May require software updates or transmission replacement.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension:**
    * **Control Arm Bushings:** Premature wear on control arm bushings, causing vibrations and clunking noises. Requires replacement of bushings.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
* **Rear Suspension:**
    * **Rear Axle Bushings:** Worn rear axle bushings lead to knocking noises and decreased stability. Requires replacement of bushings.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400+


**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Battery drains quickly, even when the car is off. May require battery replacement or repairs to electrical components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Electrical Faults:** Intermittent electrical issues, such as malfunctioning lights or dashboard displays. May require software updates or repairs to electrical components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found


**Exterior**

* **Sunroof:** Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof seals. Requires replacement of seals or repairs to the sunroof mechanism.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+


**Interior**

* **Headliner:** Sagging or detaching headliner material. Requires replacement of the headliner.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $600+
* **Center Console:** Loose center console with rattling noises. Requires tightening or replacement of console components.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $300+


**Summary**

The 2018 BMW 650i Gran Coupe is a desirable vehicle with a captivating performance and luxurious interior. However, it has experienced some issues with its engine, transmission, and suspension. These issues can range in severity and cost to fix, so it is essential to consider these potential problems before purchasing this vehicle.
