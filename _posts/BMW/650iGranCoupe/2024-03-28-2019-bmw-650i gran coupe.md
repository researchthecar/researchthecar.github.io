**Introduction:**

The 2019 BMW 650i Gran Coupe is a four-door luxury coupe with a sporty demeanor and sleek design. It features a powerful 4.4L twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. The 650i Gran Coupe comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive, while all-wheel drive is available as an option.

**Engine:**

* **4.4L V8 Twin-Turbocharged**

- **Fuel Injector Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idling, decreased fuel economy
  - Solution: Replace faulty fuel injector(s)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Turbocharger Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from exhaust, whistling or squealing noises
  - Solution: Replace or rebuild turbocharger
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Slippage**
  - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power or delayed acceleration, erratic shifting
  - Solution: Recondition or replace transmission
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Torque Converter Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during shifts, overheating
  - Solution: Replace torque converter
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

- **Front Strut Assembly Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Noises from front suspension, uneven tire wear, poor handling
  - Solution: Replace front strut assembly
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $900+ (per side)
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, bouncing or swaying
  - Solution: Replace rear shock absorber(s)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+ (per side)
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

- **Electrical System Malfunction**
  - Description/Symptoms: Electrical components not functioning properly, warning lights illuminated
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

- **Headlight Condensation**
  - Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation inside the headlight assembly
  - Solution: Replace headlight assembly
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+ (per side)
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

- **Center Console Panel Separation**
  - Description/Symptoms: Separation between center console panel and dashboard
  - Solution: Replace or repair center console panel
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found
- **Sunroof Rattle**
  - Description/Symptoms: Rattle or noise from the sunroof
  - Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof mechanism
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 BMW 650i Gran Coupe is a reliable and high-performing vehicle. However, it is not immune to certain mechanical and electrical issues. The most severe and expensive to fix issues are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and budget accordingly for repairs if necessary.
