# 2019 BMW 650i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW 650i Gran Coupe combines sleek styling with robust performance from its twin-turbo V8 engine. As a used luxury purchase, buyers should review service history carefully and check for isolated owner-reported powertrain and electronic system glitches.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing rapid acceleration for the luxury cruiser. Routine maintenance and diligent fluid checks are essential to ensure long-term mechanical health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission offers smooth, quick shifts and pairs seamlessly with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained.

**Starter Motor Mounting Bolts**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may produce strange noises during starter engagement due to mounting bolts backing out.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect, secure, or replace the starter motor mounting hardware.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance System

Advanced safety systems provide helpful driver assistance, though occasional sensor false positives can be reported by owners.

**Abrupt Braking False Positives**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly brake or stop abruptly while driving due to perceived obstructions.
- **Solution:** Inspect sensor alignments, clear debris from camera and radar areas, and check for relevant software updates.
