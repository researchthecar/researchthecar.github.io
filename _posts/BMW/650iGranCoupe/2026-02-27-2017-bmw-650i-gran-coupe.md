# 2017 BMW 650i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a pre-owned 2017 BMW 650i Gran Coupe means weighing sleek styling and high-performance luxury against potential mechanical and electrical complexities. While powertrain options offer robust power, buyers should carefully inspect secondary systems such as trunk and latch mechanisms during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (N63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating acceleration, though it requires meticulous maintenance and close monitoring of oil levels and cooling components as the vehicle ages.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission utilizes steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters and offers smooth, rapid gear shifts. It is generally robust when paired with regular fluid services.

## Exterior

### Trunk Release Mechanism

Electrical accessories and locking mechanisms generally function well, though intermittent issues with latches and actuators can occasionally arise.

**Trunk Release and Access Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the trunk fails to open electronically, creating challenges because the emergency release handle and seat-folding levers are located entirely inside the trunk area.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic trunk latch actuator, wiring harness, and emergency mechanical release cables for proper operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=650i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 650i Gran Coupe delivers impressive performance and luxury, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough mechanical and electrical inspection. Paying close attention to complex cabin electronics, locking mechanisms, and regular engine maintenance history will help ensure a more reliable ownership experience.
