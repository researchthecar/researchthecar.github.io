# 2024 BMW X6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW X6 continues to offer a striking coupe-SUV profile combined with powerful engine options and luxury appointments. For shoppers exploring the used market, understanding the early ownership feedback for this model year helps identify potential areas of concern, particularly regarding brake-system-related recalls and notices reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (xDrive40i)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine utilizes a mild-hybrid system to produce approximately 375 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. It offers a smooth power delivery and serves as the standard powerplant for the lineup.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (M60i)

This high-performance 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 features mild-hybrid assistance and generates around 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. It provides robust acceleration and a commanding driving experience for enthusiasts.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters and crisp gear changes paired across all trim levels. It is generally regarded as smooth and responsive during daily driving.

## Brakes

### Integrated Brake System

Owner reports highlight safety notices and warning messages tied to brake system components, emphasizing the importance of verifying open recall status.

**Integrated Brake System Malfunction and Recall Notice**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported receiving recall notifications regarding the integrated brake system, accompanied by dashboard warning lamps and error messages indicating potential brake control issues.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized BMW dealer inspect the vehicle and perform any necessary recall remedies as parts become available.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V471000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The knee air bag (driver and/or passenger) may not have been manufactured correctly, which can result in the knee air bag not deploying as intended. A knee air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the knee air bag (driver and/or passenger), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V622000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger knee air bag may not deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger knee air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V104000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V739000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V422000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Electric:Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2024 BMW X6, prospective buyers should carefully review vehicle history reports and confirm that all manufacturer recalls have been addressed by a certified dealer. While the powertrain options deliver strong performance and refinement, verifying the status of brake system components is essential for peace of mind.
