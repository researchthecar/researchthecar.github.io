# 2019 BMW X6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 BMW X6, buyers will find a stylish and powerful luxury coupe-SUV that blends athletic performance with high-end features. While owner feedback highlights a few notable mechanical and safety concerns, evaluating overall condition and maintenance history remains key to a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces around 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, offering strong, smooth acceleration for daily driving. It provides a balanced combination of everyday efficiency and responsive luxury performance for used buyers.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The potent 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 churns out roughly 445 to 523 horsepower depending on the trim, delivering exceptionally brisk acceleration. Buyers seeking high-performance thrills should budget for attentive maintenance and potentially higher running costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This widely used 8-speed automatic transmission features quick, seamless shifts and is paired with the X6's standard all-wheel-drive system. It is generally regarded as robust and refined, supporting both relaxed cruising and aggressive driving.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake

Reports concerning brake operation are minimal, though electronic engagement glitches can occasionally occur.

**Unexpected Parking Brake Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** The electronic parking brake may engage independently while the vehicle is in motion, forcing the driver to pull over.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electronic control modules and parking brake actuator inspected by a qualified technician.

## Interior

### Seat-Mounted Airbags

Safety systems generally perform as designed in collisions, though isolated airbag deployment incidents have been reported by owners.

**Side Airbag Thermal Burn**
- **Symptoms:** Deployment of the seat-mounted side airbag during an impact can potentially rupture and release hot gases, causing skin burns.
- **Solution:** Verify if any applicable safety recalls or component inspections have been performed by an authorized dealer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V680000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** If a rear upper control arm breaks, it can affect vehicle handling and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW dealers will replace both rear upper control arms, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V614000: Wheels:Lugs/Nuts/Bolts/Studs](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The wheel bolts on these vehicles may not have been properly tightened. The improperly tightened wheel bolts may loosen and break, affecting vehicle handling and control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace and tighten all of the wheel bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V823000: Child Seat](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The lower anchor bars may become damaged over time when used with an ISOFIX-type, rigid-style connector, child restraint system. Damaged lower anchor bars may increase the child's risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will weld a reinforcing bracket to the lower anchor bars and the vehicle body, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW X6 delivers engaging luxury performance and a striking design, but prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance records and perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Paying close attention to mechanical and electrical electronics helps ensure a dependable ownership experience.
