# 2021 BMW 740i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW 740i xDrive blends flagship luxury, advanced technology, and confident all-weather performance. As a high-end German luxury sedan, prospective used car buyers should carefully weigh its sophisticated engineering and overall comfort against potential long-term maintenance costs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more robust and dependable modern powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup delivers smooth, rapid gear shifts and solid long-term durability when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V576000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A starter motor that overheats can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW 740i xDrive remains a strong contender for luxury sedan shoppers, offering refined performance and robust powertrain options. Buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is performed to catch any hidden wear before finalizing their purchase.
