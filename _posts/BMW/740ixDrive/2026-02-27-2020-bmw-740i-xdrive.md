# 2020 BMW 740i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 740i xDrive offers a blend of luxury, advanced technology, and capable all-weather performance. For used-car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and monitoring common owner-reported areas can help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more robust and reliable modern powerplants, offering smooth power delivery and competitive efficiency.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is celebrated for quick, refined shifts and strong overall durability in daily driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V576000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A starter motor that overheats can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW 740i xDrive stands as a sophisticated luxury sedan featuring a robust powertrain combination. Prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, verify service history, and conduct a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection to ensure long-term reliability.
