## 2018 BMW 740i xDrive

The 2018 BMW 740i xDrive is a luxury sedan that offers a powerful engine, a comfortable ride, and a spacious interior. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine that produces 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

People who like the 2018 BMW 740i xDrive praise its powerful engine, comfortable ride, and spacious interior. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

**Engine**

* **3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine**
    * **Oil leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks can occur from the valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket, or rear main seal. Symptoms include a burning oil smell, low oil levels, and oil spots on the ground.
        * Solution: The leaking gasket or seal must be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain can stretch or break, which can cause the engine to lose power or stall. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, difficulty starting the engine, and reduced engine performance.
        * Solution: The timing chain and related components must be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift roughly, slip out of gear, or refuse to shift. Symptoms include difficulty accelerating, jerking or shuddering during shifting, and a lack of response from the transmission.
        * Solution: The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to ride low or unevenly. Symptoms include a rough ride, difficulty steering, and a lack of response from the suspension system.
        * Solution: The air suspension system may need to be repaired or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical system problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may experience issues such as battery drain, flickering lights, or power window failures. Symptoms include a dead battery, intermittent electrical problems, and a lack of response from electrical components.
        * Solution: The faulty electrical component must be repaired or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak, rattle, or fail to open or close properly. Symptoms include water leaks, wind noise, and a lack of response from the sunroof system.
        * Solution: The sunroof may need to be repaired or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument cluster issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The instrument cluster may experience issues such as flickering, dimming, or display failures. Symptoms include difficulty reading the gauges, intermittent display problems, and a lack of response from the instrument cluster.
        * Solution: The instrument cluster may need to be repaired or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation system issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may experience issues such as freezing, crashing, or displaying incorrect directions. Symptoms include difficulty using the navigation system, intermittent system problems, and a lack of response from the navigation system.
        * Solution: The navigation system may need to be updated or replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 BMW 740i xDrive is a luxury sedan that offers a powerful engine, a comfortable ride, and a spacious interior. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
