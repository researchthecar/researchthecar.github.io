# 2019 BMW 740i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW 740i xDrive offers a blend of luxury, advanced technology, and confident all-weather performance. As a flagship luxury sedan, prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance history and mechanical health to ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine (B58) produces approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as a smooth and robust powerplant with a strong reputation for reliability in modern BMW lineups.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes paired with xDrive all-wheel drive. This gearbox is known for refined operation and durability when properly maintained.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V465000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 740i xDrive stands out as a sophisticated luxury cruiser featuring refined powertrain options. Buyers are advised to prioritize well-documented service records and a pre-purchase inspection to verify overall vehicle health.
