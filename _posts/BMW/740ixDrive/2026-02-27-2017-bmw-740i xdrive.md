# 2017 BMW 740i xDrive

### Overview
The 2017 BMW 740i xDrive, a flagship luxury sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting BMW's engineering prowess. It is equipped with a potent turbocharged inline-six engine paired with an advanced automatic transmission, both designed for performance and efficiency. While the vehicle offers a sophisticated driving experience with advanced technology and premium materials, like any complex luxury car, certain areas may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, water pump, or thermostat housing.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**PCV System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve or system.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**iDrive Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio cutting out, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module reset; in some cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, warning lights on startup.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or components causing excessive power draw.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 740i xDrive is a well-built luxury vehicle with a generally positive reliability record. The powertrain, consisting of the 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission, is robust. Potential areas of concern are primarily related to the advanced adaptive air suspension system, which can experience leaks or compressor issues, and some electrical gremlins within the iDrive infotainment system or occasional battery drain. Addressing these issues promptly can ensure continued enjoyment of this sophisticated sedan. Routine maintenance is crucial for longevity, but major mechanical failures are not commonly reported beyond these specific components.
