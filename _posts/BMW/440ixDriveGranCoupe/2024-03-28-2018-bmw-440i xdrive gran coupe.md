**2018 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe**

The 2018 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe is a stylish and sporty four-door coupe that offers a powerful performance and a luxurious interior. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine that produces 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The 440i xDrive comes with standard all-wheel drive and a host of performance features, such as adaptive suspension and M Sport brakes.

The 440i xDrive has received positive reviews for its potent engine, sharp handling, and comfortable interior. However, it has also been criticized for its firm ride quality and high price.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six Engine**

    * **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine power loss, rough idling, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace wastegate actuator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: NHTSA ID Number 20V146
    * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, loss of power, fuel leaks
        * Solution: Replace fuel pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shifting issues, loss of gear
        * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Suspension**

    * **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when turning, poor handling, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**

    * **Premature Discharge**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, dead battery
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Motor**

    * **Failed Starter Solenoid**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks but does not start, clicking noise
        * Solution: Replace starter motor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handles**

    * **Recessed Button Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Door handles do not open when pressed
        * Solution: Replace door handle assemblies
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700 per door
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control**

    * **HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: No air flow from vents, noise from blower motor
        * Solution: Replace blower motor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System**

    * **Touchscreen Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: Unresponsive touchscreen, flickering display
        * Solution: Update software or replace touchscreen
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe is a well-rounded performance coupe that offers a potent engine, sharp handling, and a luxurious interior. However, it has experienced some issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension, which can be costly to repair. Overall, the severity of the issues and their associated costs should be considered before purchasing this vehicle.
