# 2017 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2017 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe, equipped with the B58 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission, generally presents a strong reliability profile. This model year benefits from BMW's refined engineering, particularly in its powertrain. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 440i xDrive Gran Coupe has demonstrated a good track record for its core mechanical components. Owners typically report satisfaction with its performance and build quality. However, as with any premium vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of the possibility of more complex and costly repairs compared to less performance-oriented or luxury brands, especially concerning its advanced electronic systems and turbocharged powertrain.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe is a reliable vehicle, particularly in its powertrain. The 3.0L B58 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and generally free from significant issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are within the complex electrical systems, such as the iDrive infotainment and potential battery drain, and less commonly, exterior lighting issues like headlight condensation. Interior components and suspension systems have shown good durability. While maintenance costs can be higher than average for a luxury performance car, its overall reliability is commendable, making it a solid choice for those seeking a blend of performance and practicality, provided regular maintenance is performed and potential electrical gremlins are addressed promptly.
