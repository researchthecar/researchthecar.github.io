# 2018 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe offers a compelling blend of luxury, hatchback practicality, and strong turbocharged performance. While it is generally regarded as a well-engineered luxury sport vehicle, used car buyers should remain mindful of owner-reported issues involving engine cooling lines, occasional electrical glitches, and seatbelt mechanisms. Conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection helps ensure these luxury machines have been properly maintained.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

Powered by BMW's renowned 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 (B58) engine, producing approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. This engine is widely praised for its robust performance and refined power delivery, though proper cooling system maintenance is critical.

**Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained fluid pooling underneath the vehicle accompanied by an illuminated coolant warning light.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling lines, hoses, and water pumps for leaks; replace damaged hoses or failing components as necessary.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of motive power while driving or idling, sometimes preceded by coolant or water pump pressure issues.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic diagnose the cooling and fuel delivery systems, and replace faulty sensors or pumps.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a responsive 8-speed ZF automatic transmission featuring xDrive all-wheel drive, offering quick shifts and smooth operation. It is generally robust and dependable when routine fluid checks are observed.

## Exterior

### Body Electronics

Complex onboard electronics control many luxury convenience features, which can occasionally exhibit intermittent software glitches.

**Random Electrical and Window Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Spontaneous unlocking of doors, windows or sunroof opening on their own, or gear shift service warnings while parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect body control modules and software integrations, and perform necessary software updates or module replacements.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs well, though occasional complaints target seatbelt retraction and warning sensor malfunctions.

**Seat Belt Retraction and Alert Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Driver's seatbelt fails to retract smoothly or secure properly, or the warning chime sounds even when buckled.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seatbelt buckle sensor and retractor assembly, and replace faulty components if safety function is impaired.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V507000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the vehicle has an incompatible vehicle-sensitive locking mechanism, the seat belt may not restrain the seat occupant in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary replace, the front seat belt assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 BMW 440i xDrive Gran Coupe is an exhilarating and versatile luxury sport sedan option for used car shoppers. While the B58 engine and ZF transmission combo are mechanically strong, buyers should carefully check for cooling system leaks and electrical quirks. Prioritize models with comprehensive service records and get a pre-purchase inspection from a BMW specialist to drive with confidence.
