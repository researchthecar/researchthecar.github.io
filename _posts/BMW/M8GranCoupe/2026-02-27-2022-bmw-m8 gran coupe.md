### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW M8 Gran Coupe demonstrates a high level of technical reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The formidable 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 and the 8-speed M Steptronic transmission are robust and have not shown widespread issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior/interior materials are generally well-regarded. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional software glitches in the infotainment and potential sensor calibration needs for ADAS. These are not indicative of systemic failures but rather common complexities found in modern, technology-laden vehicles. Overall, the M8 Gran Coupe offers a dependable ownership experience for a high-performance luxury car, provided routine maintenance is followed and minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
