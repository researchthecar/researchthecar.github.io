# 2023 BMW M8 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M8 Gran Coupe represents the pinnacle of modern luxury performance, blending aggressive track-ready capability with four-door executive comfort. Powered by a potent twin-turbo V8 paired with sophisticated all-wheel-drive systems, it delivers staggering performance for buyers seeking high-end thrills. Because this high-performance flagship sits at the cutting edge of BMW engineering, prospective used buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history, maintenance records, and remaining factory warranty coverage before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (S63)

This high-output 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces around 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque in Competition specification. It delivers ferocious acceleration and robust power delivery, though proper routine maintenance and premium fuel are essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features Drivelogic shift-speed settings and routes power seamlessly to the rear-biased M xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It provides rapid gear changes under hard acceleration while remaining smooth during daily driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M8 Gran Coupe is an exceptionally fast and luxurious grand tourer that commands attention on the road. Without a notable pattern of recurring owner complaints reported for this model year, it remains a compelling option for enthusiasts in the secondhand market. Buyers should prioritize securing a clean pre-purchase inspection from a marque specialist and verify the vehicle's service history to ensure trouble-free ownership.
