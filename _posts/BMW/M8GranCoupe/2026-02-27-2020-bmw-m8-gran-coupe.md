# 2020 BMW M8 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M8 Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury four-door powered by a potent twin-turbo V8 engine paired with an advanced all-wheel-drive system and automatic transmission. While it delivers blistering performance and daily drivability, prospective buyers should review available owner reports regarding engine performance and vehicle visibility during adverse weather.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

The 2020 BMW M8 Gran Coupe features a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 600 to 617 horsepower depending on the trim. It offers immense power delivery and robust build quality, though proper monitoring of cooling and electronic systems is recommended.

**Unintended Acceleration and Throttle Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported rare instances where the vehicle experienced sudden, unintended full-throttle acceleration, driving RPMs up to 7,000–8,000 without input from the accelerator pedal.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic throttle body, accelerator pedal sensor, and engine control unit (ECU) thoroughly inspected by a qualified technician.

**Coolant Leak Affecting Injectors**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience engine misfires and a noticeable loss of power due to coolant leaking from the reservoir tank directly onto the fuel injectors.
- **Solution:** Inspect the coolant expansion tank and surrounding lines for leaks, and replace damaged fuel injectors or leaking reservoirs as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Steptronic Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through an 8-speed M Steptronic automatic transmission paired with the M xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It delivers quick, precise shifts under heavy acceleration and smooth behavior during cruising.

## Exterior

### Windshield Defroster

Exterior styling and glass components generally hold up well, though climate control visibility management in heavy rain requires working cabin filtration and defroster systems.

**Windshield Fogging**
- **Symptoms:** The windshield may become excessively foggy while driving in rainy conditions at highway speeds, with the defroster failing to clear the glass immediately.
- **Solution:** Check climate control system settings, ensure the air conditioning compressor engages to dry incoming air, and inspect cabin air filters.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V164000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M8+Gran+Coupe&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The sensors in the front seat belt buckles may inaccurately detect a belted occupant as unbelted, possibly causing the air bag and seat belt pretensioners to deploy improperly in the event of a crash. If the air bag and/or seat belt pretensioners deploy improperly in the event of a crash, there is an increased risk of injury to the driver and front.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt buckles, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V883000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M8+Gran+Coupe&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The transmission wiring harness can become damaged, resulting in an electrical short circuit. An electrical short circuit can cause the transmission to shift to neutral resulting in a loss of propulsion, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace any damaged transmission harnesses. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M8 Gran Coupe is an engineering marvel that blends supercar performance with luxury sedan practicality. While isolated owner complaints highlight rare electronic throttle concerns, coolant leaks near injectors, and windshield defogging issues, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a specialized technician will help ensure a solid ownership experience.
