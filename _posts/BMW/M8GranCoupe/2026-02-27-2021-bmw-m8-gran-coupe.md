# 2021 BMW M8 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW M8 Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury four-door that pairs supercar-level acceleration with everyday usability. While early owner feedback and complaint data are sparse for this exclusive flagship, prospective buyers should approach with the typical due diligence required for modern, highly complex German performance machines.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 producing roughly 600 to 617 horsepower depending on the trim, this engine delivers ferocious performance. It is a sophisticated, high-output powerplant requiring strict adherence to maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features Drivelogic shift settings and distributes power through an M-tuned xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It offers lightning-fast gearchanges and robust handling of the engine's massive torque.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M8&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M8 Gran Coupe remains a thrilling and opulent choice on the used market, though its heavy reliance on advanced electronics and high-strung mechanics warrants a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Buyers are advised to secure a comprehensive service history and look for remaining warranty coverage to mitigate potential luxury repair costs.
