**Introduction**

The 2006 BMW 325xi is a compact luxury sedan known for its handling, performance, and comfort. It comes with a 3.0-liter inline-6 engine producing 215 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque, paired with either a 6-speed manual or a 6-speed automatic transmission.

Commonly praised for its powerful engine, responsive handling, and comfortable interior, the 325xi has also received criticism for its high maintenance costs and some interior design choices.

**Engine**

* **N52B30 (3.0L Inline-6)**
    * **Vanos Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, rough idle, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace Vanos solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the top of the engine
        * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400-$600+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Coolant Thermostat Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, coolant leaks
        * Solution: Replace coolant thermostat
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **GM A5S390R (6-speed Automatic)**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, loss of gears, check engine light
        * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500-$2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leak from the pan or lines
        * Solution: Repair leak, replace fluid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Torque Converter Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, shuddering, overheating
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000-$2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idle, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace ignition coil(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical problems, battery drain
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the car
    * Solution: Test and replace battery or troubleshoot electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150-$300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin
    * Solution: Clean or replace sunroof drain plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Buttons Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buttons on the steering wheel stop working or become intermittent
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel buttons or wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Trim Panel Squeaky**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises from the center console area
    * Solution: Tighten or replace trim panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50-$150+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2006 BMW 325xi offers strong performance and handling, it has a few moderate to severe issues that can be costly to fix, particularly with the transmission and electrical system. Drivers may also experience some minor interior and exterior issues.
