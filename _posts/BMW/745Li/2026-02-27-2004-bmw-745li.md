# 2004 BMW 745Li

### Overview
The 2004 BMW 745Li, a flagship luxury sedan, offered a blend of advanced technology and powerful performance. While generally well-regarded for its comfort and driving dynamics, like many vehicles of its era with complex systems, it is not without its potential reliability concerns. Owners should be aware of common issues that can arise, particularly with the advanced electronics and powertrain components. This data focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related problems that may require attention beyond routine maintenance, providing insights for potential buyers and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L V8 (N62)
**Cooling System Leaks (Valley Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the valley pan gasket and associated seals.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $2,500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets and/or oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**VANOS System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, loss of power, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of VANOS solenoids and seals.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (GM 5L40-E / ZF 6HP26)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of transmission seals, cooler lines, or transmission pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive (Active Roll Stabilization)
**Hydraulic Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid leaks from active sway bar actuators, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking seals or actuators.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500 per side

## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of iDrive controller/head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent issues with lights, windows, locks, or other electronic accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


### Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure
_No major issues reported for this configuration._

## Exterior

### Door Handle Microswitches
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not unlocking or locking remotely, interior lights not activating when opening doors.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the microswitch within the door handle assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400 per handle


### Sunroof Mechanism
**Sticking or Failure to Operate**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof does not open, close, or tilt properly.
- **Solution:** Lubrication, adjustment, or replacement of sunroof motor/mechanism.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Interior

### HVAC Blower Motor Resistor
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


### Seat Actuator Malfunction
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats do not move or move erratically.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat actuator motors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per seat

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 BMW 745Li is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but its advanced technology comes with potential reliability challenges. Common issues tend to center around the complex V8 engine's cooling and oil systems, the sophisticated automatic transmission, and various electrical components, including the iDrive system and body control modules. While the suspension and exterior/interior components are generally less problematic, specific systems like the Adaptive Drive can be costly to repair. Prospective buyers should budget for potential maintenance and repairs, especially concerning the engine and electronics, as these are the most frequent areas of concern.
