# 2002 BMW 745Li

### Overview
The 2002 BMW 745Li, part of the E65 generation, represented a significant technological leap for BMW's flagship sedan. It introduced the iDrive infotainment system and a powerful V8 engine. While offering a luxurious and technologically advanced driving experience, this generation is known for its complex electronics and sophisticated, albeit sometimes temperamental, mechanical systems. Owners should be prepared for potential maintenance costs associated with its advanced features and high-performance components. Reliability can be a mixed bag, with some owners experiencing fewer issues than others, often depending on maintenance history and the specific components that may have been subject to recalls or common failure points.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L V8 (N62B44)
**Valve Stem Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke on startup, oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of hoses, water pump, thermostat, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks from the bottom of the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-speed Automatic (GM 5L40-E / ZF 6HP26)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission warning lights, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement or repair of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**Mechatronic Unit Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, slipping gears, failure to engage.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive / Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air struts.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200 per corner

**Active Roll Stabilization (ARS) Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll, warning lights related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the ARS pump.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500


### Standard Suspension
**No widespread issues reported.**

## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, system reboots, unresponsive controls.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical gremlins, lights not working, power windows issues, central locking problems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement or reprogramming of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

## Exterior

### Door Handle Mechanism
**Sticking or Broken Door Handles**
- **Symptoms:** Door handles not retracting or opening the door.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the door handle mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per handle

## Interior

### HVAC Blower Motor Resistor
**HVAC Fan Speed Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on certain speeds or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300


### Seat Actuator Failure
**Power Seat Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving or only moving in one direction.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat actuators or control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per seat

### Summary Verdict
The 2002 BMW 745Li is a complex luxury sedan with a sophisticated V8 engine and advanced technology, particularly the iDrive system. While it offers a premium driving experience, potential buyers should be aware of common issues such as valve stem seal leaks, cooling system problems, and transmission control module failures. The complex electrical system, including the iDrive and various control modules, can also be a source of concern and costly repairs. Suspension components, especially on models with Adaptive Drive, can also require significant investment. Regular and meticulous maintenance is crucial to mitigate these potential issues. Overall, while not the most reliable vehicle in its class, its luxury and performance can be rewarding if potential ownership costs are factored in.
