# 2016 BMW 428i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 428i xDrive combines sporty handling and confident all-weather traction with a luxury coupe or gran coupe cabin. Prospective buyers evaluating a used model should carefully inspect the vehicle's powertrain and supporting systems, as owner reports highlight a few key mechanical and electrical areas to monitor during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque. It offers a strong balance of everyday responsiveness and efficiency, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with ZF

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid shifts paired with xDrive all-wheel drive. It is generally regarded as a robust unit in modern BMW applications when serviced properly.
