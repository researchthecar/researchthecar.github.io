**Introduction**

The 2016 BMW 428i xDrive is a compact luxury coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. It's powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 240 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 428i xDrive is praised for its agile handling, responsive steering, and comfortable interior, but it's also known for certain issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Vacuum Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking vacuum pump, reduced engine power, engine stalling
        * Solution: Replace vacuum pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,500
        * Recalls: 20V-648
    * **Timing Chain Guide Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive engine noise, reduced engine power, engine failure
        * Solution: Replace timing chain guide
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **ZF Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Gear slipping, difficulty shifting, transmission fault codes
        * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500
        * Recalls: 18V-741

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Repeated battery drain leading to starting issues
    * Solution: Identify and fix electrical component drawing excessive power
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the underlying issue
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, premature bulb failure
    * Solution: Replace headlights or faulty wiring
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof leaking water into the cabin
    * Solution: Clean or replace sunroof drain plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment Screen Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Black or unresponsive screen, system glitches
    * Solution: Replace infotainment screen or update software
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Folding Mechanism Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to fold or unfold rear seats
    * Solution: Replace rear seat folding mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 BMW 428i xDrive has several reported issues, including engine problems, transmission failures, electrical glitches, and some interior and exterior issues. While some of these issues can be minor and inexpensive to fix, others, such as engine failures and transmission problems, can be significantly more costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before making a purchase decision.
