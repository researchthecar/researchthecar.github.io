**Vehicle Introduction**

The 2005 BMW X3 is a compact luxury SUV known for its agile handling, spacious interior, and available potent inline-6 engines.

* **Engine Options:**
    * 2.5L inline-6 (2.5i) - 184 hp, 175 lb-ft torque
    * 3.0L inline-6 (3.0i) - 225 hp, 214 lb-ft torque
* **Transmission Options:**
    * 5-speed manual
    * 6-speed automatic

**Engine**

**2.5L Inline-6 (2.5i)**

* **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L Inline-6 (3.0i)**

* **Cooling System Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Overheating, low coolant levels
    * Solution: Replace faulty components (e.g., thermostat, radiator)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaks from the transmission
    * Solution: Seal leaks or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Squeaky Front Sway Bar Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or creaking noises from the front suspension when going over bumps
    * Solution: Replace bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially when the car is parked
    * Solution: Identify and repair electrical component causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
