# 2024 BMW 430i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 430i xDrive offers a blend of sporty handling, refined interior luxury, and all-weather capability. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from BMW's modern engineering and technology suite, though used car buyers should carefully review service histories and ensure all software updates are current.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque. It provides a strong balance of everyday efficiency and responsive power for the all-wheel-drive platform.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the xDrive system to deliver quick, smooth shifts and optimized gearing. It is widely regarded as one of the most reliable and refined transmissions in its class.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V863000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as a hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the cylinder head cover as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 430i xDrive is a sophisticated and capable luxury vehicle with a solid powertrain foundation. Prospective buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection and verify standard maintenance records to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
