## 2017 BMW 430i xDrive

The 2017 BMW 430i xDrive is a luxury sport coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-four engine that produces 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters. The 430i xDrive comes standard with all-wheel drive, which provides excellent traction in all weather conditions.

The 430i xDrive is known for its agile handling, responsive steering, and comfortable ride. It is also a relatively fuel-efficient car, with an EPA-estimated 24 mpg in the city and 34 mpg on the highway.

However, like any car, the 430i xDrive is not without its problems. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific issues that people are having with the vehicles:

### Engine

**B48 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail prematurely, resulting in a loss of power. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. The estimated cost to fix is $2,000 - $3,000+.
* **High-pressure fuel pump failure:** The high-pressure fuel pump can fail, resulting in a loss of fuel pressure. The solution is to replace the fuel pump. The estimated cost to fix is $1,000 - $2,000+.
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, resulting in a loss of timing. The solution is to replace the timing chain tensioner. The estimated cost to fix is $1,500 - $2,500+.

### Transmission

**ZF 8HP45 Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission shudder:** The transmission can shudder when shifting gears. The solution is to replace the transmission fluid and filter. The estimated cost to fix is $300 - $500+.
* **Transmission slipping:** The transmission can slip when shifting gears. The solution is to replace the transmission clutch packs. The estimated cost to fix is $2,000 - $3,000+.
