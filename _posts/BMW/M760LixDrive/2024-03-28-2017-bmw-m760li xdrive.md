**2017 BMW M760Li xDrive**

The 2017 BMW M760Li xDrive is a full-size luxury sedan powered by a 6.6-liter V12 engine that produces 601 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed Steptronic Sport automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

The M760Li is known for its powerful performance and luxurious amenities, but it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **6.6-liter V12**

**Valve Steam Seal Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks, decreased engine performance, increased exhaust emissions
Solution: Replace valve steam seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Steptronic Sport**

**Gearbox Control Unit Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, delayed or rough shifting, loss of power
Solution: Replace gearbox control unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension**

**Air Spring Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, decreased handling and stability
Solution: Replace air springs
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **iDrive System**

**Software Bugs**
Description/Symptoms: System crashes, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues
Solution: Software updates or module replacements
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Carbon Ceramic Brakes**

**Glazing**
Description/Symptoms: Brakes may squeal or grind during use
Solution: Resurface brake rotors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic Sunroof**

**Seal Failures**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaks, wind noise
Solution: Replace sunroof seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 BMW M760Li xDrive has been reported to have a range of issues affecting various components, including the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix can be significant. It is important for owners of this vehicle to be aware of these potential problems and to have them addressed promptly to ensure optimal performance and safety.
