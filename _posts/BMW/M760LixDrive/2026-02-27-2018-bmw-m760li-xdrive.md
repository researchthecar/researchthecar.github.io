# 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive represents the pinnacle of the 7 Series lineup, featuring a twin-turbocharged V12 engine and standard all-wheel drive. As an ultra-luxury performance flagship, it offers immense power and sophisticated engineering, but prospective used-car buyers should approach with caution due to the extreme complexity and maintenance costs associated with its flagship powertrain and advanced onboard electronics.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.6-liter twin-turbo V12 engine produces approximately 600 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and immense acceleration. While robustly engineered, it represents a highly complex powerplant where routine maintenance and potential out-of-warranty repairs demand a substantial financial commitment.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the all-wheel-drive system to provide exceptionally smooth and rapid gear changes. It is generally reliable under normal operation, though proper fluid maintenance is critical for long-term durability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V377000: Engine And Engine Cooling](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M760Li&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If oil leaks onto the front brake discs, a greater distance may be needed to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the engine oil cooler line, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive is an extraordinary engineering achievement offering unmatched V12 performance and luxury. However, used buyers must carefully evaluate service history, as upkeep and potential mechanical or electrical repairs on a twelve-cylinder flagship can be extraordinarily expensive.
