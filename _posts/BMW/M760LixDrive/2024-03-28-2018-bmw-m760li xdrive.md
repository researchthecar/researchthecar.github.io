**Introduction:**

The 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive is the ultimate iteration of the 7 Series, offering a potent twin-turbo V12 engine that produces 601 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The M760Li xDrive is praised for its blistering performance, luxurious interior, and cutting-edge technology, but it is not without its drawbacks.

**Engine**

* **Twin-Turbo V12**

 **Engine Misfires Under Load**
Description/Symptoms: The engine may intermittently misfire under heavy acceleration, causing loss of power and rough running.
Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils or spark plugs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

 **Shifting Hesitation**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears, particularly during low-speed maneuvers.
Solution: Update transmission control software or replace faulty shift solenoids.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Air Suspension**

 **Air Spring Leaks**
Description/Symptoms: Air springs may leak over time, causing the suspension to sag or become uneven.
Solution: Replace faulty air springs.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+ per corner
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery may prematurely drain, causing difficulty starting or a complete loss of power.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system components responsible for the drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Faults**
Description/Symptoms: Various electrical faults may occur, including flickering lights, malfunctioning gauges, or intermittent system failures.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty electrical components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+ depending on the fault
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

 * **Factory Wheels**
 
 **Curb Scuffs**
Description/Symptoms: The factory wheels are prone to curb scuffs due to their low-profile tires.
Solution: Repair or replace damaged wheels.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ per wheel
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation Display**
Description/Symptoms: The navigation display may experience glitches or become unresponsive.
Solution: Update the navigation software or replace the display unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers an exhilarating driving experience. However, it is not without its issues. While most are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, some can be more severe and costly. Nevertheless, the overall reliability and ownership experience remain fairly positive.
