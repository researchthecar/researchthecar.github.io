# 2021 BMW M760Li xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW M760Li xDrive represents the pinnacle of executive luxury and performance, featuring a potent twelve-cylinder engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive dynamics. As a low-volume, ultra-luxury flagship, prospective used-car buyers should anticipate high maintenance costs and specialized service requirements. While complaints for this specific model year are virtually non-existent, buyers should still approach flagship German luxury vehicles with a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12 (600 hp)

The 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 600 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and immense acceleration. This sophisticated powerplant is exceptionally complex, requiring meticulous maintenance and specialized technician care to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive provides seamless, rapid gear changes that complement the massive power output of the V12 engine. It is generally robust and well-matched to the vehicle's high-performance luxury cruiser character.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M760Li&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M760Li xDrive is an extraordinary engineering achievement offering unmatched luxury and staggering V12 performance. Because there are no recurring owner complaints or widespread issue trends flagged for this model year, it presents as a solid pre-owned prospect for luxury enthusiasts. However, owners must budget significantly for routine upkeep, premium fuel, and specialized maintenance.
