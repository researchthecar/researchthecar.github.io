# 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive

### Overview
The 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive represents the pinnacle of BMW's luxury sedan offerings, combining potent performance with opulent comfort. Powered by a formidable V12 engine and featuring advanced xDrive all-wheel-drive, this model is engineered for a refined driving experience. Reliability data for this high-performance variant, while generally strong due to BMW's engineering, can be influenced by the complexity of its advanced systems. ## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks/Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'air suspension malfunction' warning light, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking air springs, hoses, or the air compressor. Regular checks of the system can help identify leaks early.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**iDrive Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness to touch or controls, intermittent audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential replacement of the head unit or related modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure/Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, battery warning lights, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnose and repair parasitic drain sources, or replace the battery. Ensure all accessories are turned off when the vehicle is parked.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 BMW M760Li xDrive is a highly capable luxury sedan with a strong overall reliability record, particularly concerning its potent V12 engine and advanced transmission. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are recommended to ensure continued optimal performance and longevity of this exceptional vehicle.
