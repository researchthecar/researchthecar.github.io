# 2020 BMW M760Li xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M760Li xDrive represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance, anchored by a formidable V12 engine. For prospective used-car buyers, this flagship sedan offers incredible prestige and effortless power, though maintaining such an advanced and complex German performance machine requires careful diligence and a healthy maintenance budget.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbo V12

This 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 600 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and effortless acceleration. It is a highly complex power plant requiring meticulous maintenance history to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs seamlessly with the all-wheel-drive xDrive system, offering lightning-fast gear changes and refined highway cruising. It is generally robust, provided fluid changes are maintained according to schedule.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M760Li&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M760Li xDrive is an extraordinary engineering achievement offering unmatched V12 refinement and luxury. Used-car buyers should prioritize finding an example with a comprehensive service history and budget accordingly for higher-than-average upkeep.
