**Introduction:**

The 2019 BMW M760Li xDrive is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V12 engine producing 601 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It comes with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The M760Li is praised for its exceptional performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technology features. However, it also has some reported issues.

**Engine**

**V12 Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbochargers may fail, resulting in reduced engine power and increased exhaust smoke.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty turbochargers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Cracked Exhaust Manifold:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The exhaust manifold may develop cracks, leading to engine noise and reduced performance.
    * Solution: Replace the cracked exhaust manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifting, or it may fail to shift gears properly.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the faulty transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to ride low or unevenly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to have difficulty starting.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system issue causing the battery drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, freezing, or other issues.
    * Solution: Update the system software or replace faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may leak water into the cabin, causing water damage.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the sunroof seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Ambient Lighting Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The ambient lighting may malfunction, not illuminating properly.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty ambient lighting components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Panoramic Sunroof Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof may make excessive noise when operated.
    * Solution: Lubricate or adjust the sunroof mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

The 2019 BMW M760Li xDrive has some notable issues, particularly with its engine and transmission. The cost of repairs can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to have their vehicles regularly inspected by a qualified mechanic.
