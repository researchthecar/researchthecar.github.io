**Introduction:**

The 2020 BMW M760Li xDrive is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V12 engine. With 601 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, it offers blistering acceleration and top-speed performance. However, as with any vehicle, there have been some reported issues with various components of the M760Li xDrive.

**Engine**

* **Twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V12**

    * **Oil leaks from turbochargers**
        * Description/Symptoms: Visible oil residue around the turbochargers accompanied by a burning oil smell.
        * Solution: Replace leaking turbochargers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, rough idling, and reduced performance.
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic**

    * **Shifting issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough gear shifts, especially during acceleration.
        * Solution: Update transmission control module software or replace transmission solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive M Suspension**

    * **Air suspension strut failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, reduced ride height, and rough ride.
        * Solution: Replace faulty air suspension strut.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+ per strut
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight housing.
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic sunroof rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the sunroof during driving.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2020 BMW M760Li xDrive is an impressive vehicle, it has had reports of some issues related to its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. The severity of these issues can vary, but some can be costly to repair. Overall, it is important for potential owners to be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their decision-making process.
