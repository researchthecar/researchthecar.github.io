# 2016 BMW 550i GT xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 550i GT xDrive combines high-performance luxury with versatile fastback utility, anchored by a potent V8 engine and all-wheel drive. While it offers an exceptionally smooth ride and upscale cabin, prospective buyers should approach with careful consideration regarding its complex powertrain and associated long-term maintenance needs. Reviewing service history and performing a pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist are strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63)

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless acceleration. It is a high-performance, complex powerplant requiring meticulous maintenance to prevent oil consumption and cooling system concerns over time.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and smooth, rapid gear changes paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust, though regular fluid changes are advised to maintain long-term shifting refinement.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 550i GT xDrive is a powerful and luxurious touring vehicle that rewards attentive ownership. Due to its sophisticated engineering and potential for costly out-of-warranty repairs, buyers should prioritize well-maintained examples with comprehensive service records.
