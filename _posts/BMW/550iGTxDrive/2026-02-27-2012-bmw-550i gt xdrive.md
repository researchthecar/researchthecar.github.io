# 2012 BMW 550i GT xDrive

### Overview
The 2012 BMW 550i GT xDrive, a luxurious and powerful Gran Turismo model, offers a compelling blend of performance and comfort. Powered by a robust twin-turbocharged V8 engine, it delivers exhilarating acceleration and a refined driving experience. The xDrive all-wheel-drive system enhances traction and stability in various conditions. While generally well-regarded for its engineering and premium features, like any complex vehicle, it has specific areas that have seen more attention from owners regarding reliability. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (N63B44O0)
**Turbocharger Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke from exhaust, oil smell, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger seals and lines, potentially turbocharger replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, check engine light, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Engine misfires, rough running, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 BMW 550i GT xDrive is a capable luxury vehicle, but its powerful 4.4L twin-turbo V8 engine is the primary area of concern for potential reliability issues. Owners have reported issues with turbocharger oil leaks, fuel injectors, and ignition coils, which can be costly to repair. The advanced Adaptive Drive suspension system, while enhancing performance, can also be a source of expensive repairs if malfunctions occur. Other systems like the transmission, electrical components (particularly the iDrive), and general interior/exterior elements appear to be relatively robust. Prospective buyers should be prepared for potentially higher maintenance costs associated with the engine and advanced suspension features.
