# 2021 BMW M240i xDrive

### Overview
The 2021 BMW M240i xDrive, part of the F22 generation's final model year, is generally regarded as a robust performance coupe. It benefits from BMW's extensive engineering and a focus on driving dynamics. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the M240i xDrive typically exhibits strong reliability, particularly in its powertrain. Owners often praise its engaging driving experience and solid build quality. However, as with any performance-oriented vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of specific areas that might require attention over time, though widespread critical failures are uncommon for this model year. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its long-term performance and dependability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW M240i xDrive is a highly reliable performance vehicle, with its core powertrain components (3.0L B58 engine and 8-speed ZF transmission) demonstrating excellent durability. Most reported issues are minor and related to the electrical system, such as occasional infotainment glitches or potential battery drain, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronics. These are generally manageable with software updates or standard battery replacement. The suspension, exterior, and interior components are also typically robust. Overall, the M240i xDrive offers a strong reliability record for its class, provided it has been maintained according to BMW's recommendations.
