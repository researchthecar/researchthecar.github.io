# 2021 BMW M240i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW M240i xDrive offers an exhilarating blend of luxury and performance, anchored by a robust powertrain. While used-car buyers generally praise its dynamic capabilities and strong engineering, keeping up with routine maintenance and pre-purchase inspections remains key to ensuring long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Known as the B58, this powerplant is widely regarded by enthusiasts and mechanics as exceptionally reliable and smooth for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission routes power seamlessly to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It offers crisp, rapid shifts and has built a strong reputation for durability under spirited driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V554000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M240i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During a crash, a seat belt retractor that does not function as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the seat belt retractors (including the seat belts) and, if necessary, replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V342000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M240i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The support may separate from the roof liner and fall into the passenger compartment, increasing the risk of injury during a crash necessitating the deployment of the head air bag.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and repair or replace the roof liner as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M240i xDrive stands out as a solid choice for a used luxury performance coupe, backed by a well-regarded powertrain. Prospective buyers should still prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall vehicle health and maintenance history.
