**2017 BMW M240i xDrive** is a compact sports coupe with a 3.0-liter inline-six engine that produces 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The M240i is praised for its lively performance, sharp handling, and luxurious interior. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**B58B30 3.0L Inline-Six**
  * **Timing Chain Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and failure of the timing chain can lead to engine damage or catastrophic failure. Symptoms may include noisy engine operation, reduced power, and illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain, tensioner, and other related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the high-pressure fuel pump can lead to difficulty starting the vehicle or loss of power while driving. Symptoms may include rough idling, hesitation on acceleration, and illuminated check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**ZF 8HP50 8-Speed Automatic**
  * **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, slipping gears, or inability to engage certain gears.
    * Solution: Replacement of transmission solenoids, torque converter, or complete transmission rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Non-Leather Seat Cracking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Cracking or splitting of the vinyl or fabric seat material, particularly on the bolsters.
  * Solution: Replacement of the affected seat upholstery.

**Summary**

The 2017 BMW M240i xDrive is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has been reported with certain issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the extent of the problem. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicles serviced regularly to prevent or mitigate them.
