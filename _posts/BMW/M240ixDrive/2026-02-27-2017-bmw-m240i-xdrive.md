# 2017 BMW M240i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 BMW M240i xDrive blends high performance with everyday usability, featuring a potent turbocharged inline-six engine and standard all-wheel drive. While generally praised for its driving dynamics and solid powertrain engineering, used-car buyers should review owner-reported cosmetic, wheel, and electrical concerns typical of modern luxury sports coupes.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 2017 M240i xDrive is powered by the B58 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine, generating approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant is widely regarded as robust and reliable compared to its predecessors, offering strong performance with very few widespread mechanical complaints.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic with Paddle Shifters

Power is routed through a smooth and responsive ZF 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive. This gearbox is known for quick shifts and strong long-term durability when properly maintained.

### 6-Speed Manual

A traditional 6-speed manual transmission was also available for purists seeking direct engagement with the B58 engine. It offers solid mechanical feel and remains a sought-after option on the used market.

## Suspension

### Wheels

Suspension and wheel components are generally sturdy, though low-profile tires and large factory alloy wheels can occasionally leave rims vulnerable to harsh road impacts.

**Factory Rim Fractures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected cracking or fracturing of factory aluminum rims leading to sudden tire pressure loss.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for cracks or structural damage during a pre-purchase inspection and replace compromised rims.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster Lighting

Electrical systems are complex, with occasional reports of minor lighting or display quirks that warrant a thorough test drive in varying lighting conditions.

**Unilluminated Gauge Dials in Dim Light**
- **Symptoms:** Speedometer and tachometer gauges may lack proper backlighting under daytime dim lighting conditions, making numerals difficult to read.
- **Solution:** Check instrument cluster illumination settings and verify proper operation of ambient light sensors.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety systems generally perform well, though isolated sensor anomalies can occasionally trigger warning indicators.

**Passenger Airbag Malfunction Light**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger airbag warning light may illuminate persistently on the dashboard, indicating a potential fault in the occupant detection system.
- **Solution:** Scan the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) computer for diagnostic trouble codes to identify faulty sensors or wiring harnesses.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW M240i xDrive offers an exhilarating driving experience backed by a generally reliable powertrain. While owner reports highlight isolated concerns regarding wheel durability and interior electronics, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic will help ensure the vehicle is in top condition.
