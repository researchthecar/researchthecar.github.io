# 2019 BMW M240i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW M240i xDrive pairs exhilarating performance with a reputation for solid foundational engineering. As a used purchase, prospective buyers generally find that its powertrain options offer robust performance with fewer recurring mechanical headaches than older generation performance models, making it an appealing choice for enthusiasts looking for everyday usability.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 (B58) engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its strong performance, smooth power delivery, and generally solid reliability compared to its predecessor.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features quick, responsive shifts and pairs with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust and requires standard maintenance to ensure long-term durability.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission offers a traditional engaging driving experience for purists. It maintains a strong reputation for mechanical durability when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M240i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW M240i xDrive stands out as a compelling used performance option due to its strong powertrain lineup and lack of widespread recurring mechanical complaints. Prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall condition and maintenance history.
