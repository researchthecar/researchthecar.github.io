**Introduction**

The 2021 BMW M240i xDrive is a high-performance coupe powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-6 engine producing 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and xDrive all-wheel drive. The M240i is praised for its thrilling driving experience, luxurious interior, and impressive technology features. However, it has also faced some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **B58B30** (3.0L I6)
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, loud whistling noise.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, stalling, difficulty starting.
        * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delays in shifting, rough or jerky shifts, transmission slipping.
        * Solution: Replace or repair faulty transmission components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive M Suspension**
    * **Strut Bearing Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noise when turning or driving over bumps, reduced handling performance.
        * Solution: Replace the strut bearings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, such as flickering lights, power window failures, or battery drain.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue.
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Trim Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or detached rear bumper trim, gaps or misalignment.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the bumper trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Frozen or unresponsive touchscreen, audio playback issues, Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Assessment**

Overall, the 2021 BMW M240i xDrive has received positive reviews, but it has not been immune to issues. Some of the reported problems, such as the turbocharger failure, can be costly to repair. However, it's important to note that these issues may not affect all vehicles and that the M240i remains a highly capable and enjoyable sports car.
