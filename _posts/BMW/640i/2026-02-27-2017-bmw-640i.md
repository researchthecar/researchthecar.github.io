# 2017 BMW 640i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Navigating the market for a used luxury car involves balancing performance with potential ownership hurdles. This reliability summary highlights what to look out for when evaluating this vehicle to help you make an informed purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55/B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine typically produces around 315 to 320 horsepower and strong low-end torque. It is generally regarded as one of BMW's smoother and more reliable modern powerplants, though proper maintenance remains crucial.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission, this setup delivers seamless gear shifts and excellent highway efficiency. The gearbox is widely praised for durability in normal operating conditions.

## Interior

### Trunk and Seat Release Mechanism

Electrical and latch-related complaints are generally isolated, but minor interior convenience glitches should be tested thoroughly.

**Trunk Release Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the trunk fails to open, trapping cargo inside. Because the emergency release and seat-folding levers are located entirely within the rear of the trunk, accessing the interior cargo space can become extremely difficult during a failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic trunk latch and manual release cables during a pre-purchase check to ensure proper mechanical and electrical operation.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

Overall, this model offers a compelling blend of luxury and performance, with standard powertrain options proving relatively robust. Buyers should verify all electronics and interior release mechanisms are fully functional before finalizing a purchase.
