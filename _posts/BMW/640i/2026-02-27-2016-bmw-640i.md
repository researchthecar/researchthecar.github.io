# 2016 BMW 640i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 640i delivers a blend of luxury and performance, anchored by a robust turbocharged inline-six engine. While owner-reported complaints are minimal, prospective buyers should review available maintenance records and inspect typical luxury vehicle wear points during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Gas Engine (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as a smooth and dependable powerplant for daily driving when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Steptronic

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless shifts and is paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It is generally robust and requires standard fluid maintenance over its lifespan.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 640i presents a solid pre-owned luxury option with a well-regarded powertrain. Buyers should prioritize finding a model with a documented service history and perform a thorough inspection of mechanical and electronic systems.
