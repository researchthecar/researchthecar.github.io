**Introduction**

The 2019 BMW 740e xDrive is a luxury sedan that combines a 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine with a 12 kWh battery and electric motor for a total of 322 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 740e is known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and advanced technology features. However, it also has some reported issues.

**Engine**
* **3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Engine**
    * **Intake Valve Deposits**
        * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves can cause reduced engine performance, rough idle, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Cleaning the intake valves is necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail due to oil starvation or excessive wear, leading to reduced engine power and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacing the turbocharger is required.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifts, slipping gears, or refusing to shift.
        * Solution: Repairing or replacing the transmission components, such as the valve body or solenoids, may be necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
* **Battery Discharge**
        * Description/Symptoms: The 12V battery may discharge quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
        * Solution: Replacing the battery or identifying and fixing electrical drains.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical components, such as the infotainment system, headlights, or power windows, may experience malfunctions.
        * Solution: Diagnosing and repairing or replacing the faulty electrical components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the component.
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**
* **Dashboard Creaks and Rattles**
        * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard can develop creaks and rattles, particularly over rough roads.
        * Solution: Applying sound deadening material or tightening loose components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Trim Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: The center console trim can become loose or misaligned.
        * Solution: Realigning or replacing the trim.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 BMW 740e xDrive offers a comfortable and technologically advanced driving experience, it has some potential issues. The reported problems with the engine, transmission, and electrical system can be costly to repair. It is essential to be aware of these issues and factor in potential repair costs when considering this vehicle.
