# 2016 BMW 328i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 328i offers an engaging driving experience paired with a luxury interior, featuring a capable turbocharged four-cylinder engine. While it delivers strong performance and daily usability, prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance history and inspect potential wear areas across powertrain and body systems.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent fuel economy, though proper maintenance is critical for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers swift, smooth shifts and pairs well with rear-wheel or all-wheel-drive configurations. It is generally robust when fluid maintenance schedules are maintained.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers a direct, engaging connection for enthusiasts. It is relatively uncommon in this model year but holds up well under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V608000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An improperly sealed electrical connector on the water pump may be exposed to water and short circuit. An electrical short increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the water pump and plug connector as necessary, and install a protective shield, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 328i remains an appealing choice for used-car shoppers seeking luxury and driving dynamics. Prioritizing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verifying service records will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
