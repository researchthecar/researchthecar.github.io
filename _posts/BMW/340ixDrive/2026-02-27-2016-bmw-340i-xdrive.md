# 2016 BMW 340i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 340i xDrive blends luxury, all-weather capability, and the renowned B58 turbocharged inline-six engine, making it a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking performance and refinement. While early production of this generation established a strong reputation for powertrain reliability compared to its predecessor, prospective owners should still carefully evaluate service history and watch for minor electrical or component wear common in German sports sedans of this vintage.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its smooth power delivery, robust performance, and significantly improved reliability over older BMW powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system delivers rapid, smooth gear changes and optimal power distribution. It is generally considered highly durable under normal driving conditions.

The 2016 BMW 340i xDrive offers an appealing mix of athletic performance, all-weather traction, and a notably dependable powertrain anchored by the B58 engine.
