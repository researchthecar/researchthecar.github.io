# 2016 BMW 535i GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 535i Gran Turismo blends luxury, spaciousness, and performance into a unique fastback design. While it offers a comfortable ride and robust powertrain options, used-car buyers should approach this model with careful inspection due to potential electrical and mechanical complexities typical of high-end German vehicles of this era.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as one of BMW's more reliable modern six-cylinder powerplants, though regular maintenance is essential.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, quick shifts and pairs exceptionally well with the inline-6 engine. It has a strong reputation for durability when fluid changes are observed.
