# 2017 BMW 535i GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 BMW 535i Gran Turismo blends luxury, spaciousness, and performance into a unique fastback design. As a used vehicle, prospective buyers should carefully weigh its driving dynamics and feature-rich cabin against the potential complexities of maintaining a high-end German luxury car. Reviewing service history and assessing the condition of its powertrain and electronics are key steps for any potential purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and responsive power delivery. While generally robust compared to earlier iterations, it requires diligent maintenance regarding cooling and oil changes to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear shifts and efficient highway cruising. It is widely regarded as a durable unit in modern BMW lineups when serviced properly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=535i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 535i Gran Turismo offers a compelling mix of comfort and performance for used-car shoppers who prioritize interior space and luxury. While standard powertrain options are generally refined, buyers must approach luxury European ownership with a readiness for thorough maintenance checks. Always prioritize vehicles with documented service histories.
