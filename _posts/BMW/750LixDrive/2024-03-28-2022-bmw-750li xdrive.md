The 2022 BMW 750Li xDrive is a luxurious sedan powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The vehicle is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported various issues with their vehicles.

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the exhaust, unusual noises from the engine
    * Solution: Replace the affected turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, hesitation or jerking during acceleration
    * Solution: Replace or repair the transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Adaptive Air Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, loss of damping, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replace or repair the air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system, replace the battery if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment system malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing, audio system not working, navigation system not responding
    * Solution: Update the software, replace the affected components if necessary
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replace the affected headlights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat adjustment failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust the seat position, intermittent movement or malfunctioning controls
    * Solution: Replace or repair the affected seat components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2022 BMW 750Li xDrive is a capable and luxurious sedan, it is not immune to potential issues. Some owners have reported turbocharger failures, transmission slipping, suspension problems, and electrical malfunctions. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but it is important for potential buyers to be aware of them before making a purchase decision.
