**Introduction**

The 2014 BMW 750Li xDrive is a luxury sedan with a powerful V8 engine and a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission. It has a spacious interior, comfortable seats, and plenty of high-tech features. However, like any car, it has its share of problems.

Some of the most common issues with the 2014 BMW 750Li xDrive include:

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Oil leaks:** Oil leaks are a common problem on the 4.4L twin-turbocharged V8 engine. The leaks can occur from a variety of places, including the valve covers, oil pan, and rear main seal.
  * Description/Symptoms: You may notice oil spots under your car or on your driveway. You may also see a drop in your oil level.
  * Solution: The solution to an oil leak is to replace the leaking part.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:** The 8-speed automatic transmission can sometimes slip. This can cause the car to hesitate or jerk when shifting gears.
  * Description/Symptoms: You may notice the transmission slipping when you are accelerating or shifting gears.
  * Solution: The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension issues:** The air suspension system can sometimes fail. This can cause the car to ride rough or low.
  * Description/Symptoms: You may notice the car riding rough or low. You may also hear a noise from the suspension.
  * Solution: The air suspension system may need to be repaired or replaced.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery can sometimes drain prematurely. This can be caused by a variety of factors, including a faulty alternator, a parasitic drain, or a weak battery.
  * Description/Symptoms: You may notice that your car battery is dead or has a low charge.
  * Solution: The battery may need to be replaced or the alternator may need to be repaired.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** The headlights can sometimes develop condensation inside them. This can be caused by a faulty seal or a leak in the headlight housing.
  * Description/Symptoms: You may notice condensation inside your headlights.
  * Solution: The headlights may need to be replaced or the seals may need to be repaired.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation system issues:** The navigation system can sometimes malfunction. This can cause the system to freeze or give you incorrect directions.
  * Description/Symptoms: You may notice that the navigation system is not working properly.
  * Solution: The navigation system may need to be updated or replaced.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 BMW 750Li xDrive is a great car, but it does have its share of problems. Some of the most common issues include oil leaks, transmission slipping, suspension issues, electrical problems, and interior issues. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can be high. However, if you are considering buying a 2014 BMW 750Li xDrive, you should be aware of these potential problems.
