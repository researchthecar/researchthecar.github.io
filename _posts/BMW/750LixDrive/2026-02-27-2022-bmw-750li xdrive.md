## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, loss of ride height, 'air suspension malfunction' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent connectivity issues (Bluetooth, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto).
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $700


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle, 'battery low' warnings.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the parasitic draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 750Li xDrive generally presents a reliable profile, with its robust 4.4L twin-turbo V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission showing no significant widespread issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are the adaptive air suspension, with potential for leaks, and the complex electrical system, which can sometimes suffer from infotainment glitches or parasitic battery drain. Interior components like climate control actuators can also be a source of repair. While these issues are not endemic, they represent the most common technical concerns beyond standard maintenance for this luxury sedan. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are recommended.
