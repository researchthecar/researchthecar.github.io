# 2019 BMW 750Li xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW 750Li xDrive represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining a potent twin-turbo V8 engine with a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system and plush interior appointments. For prospective used-car buyers, this flagship sedan offers incredible performance and prestige, though maintenance and potential mechanical complexities require careful consideration. Reviewing common owner feedback helps ensure you find a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and rapid acceleration. While powerful, this complex powerplant requires meticulous maintenance history and premium care to avoid costly upkeep down the road.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Paired standard with xDrive all-wheel drive, this 8-speed automatic provides seamless and rapid gear changes tailored to various driving modes. It is generally robust, though regular fluid checks are recommended during routine servicing.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=750Li&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 750Li xDrive is an exceptionally refined and powerful luxury sedan that delivers an uncompromising driving experience. Used-car shoppers should prioritize finding models with a documented service history and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify the health of its high-tech systems.
