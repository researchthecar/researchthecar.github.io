# 2015 BMW 228i

### Overview
The 2015 BMW 228i, part of the F22 generation, is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly praised for its engaging driving dynamics and premium interior. It typically came equipped with a potent 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine, paired with either a ZF 8-speed automatic or a 6-speed manual transmission. While BMW vehicles are known for their performance, this model year has demonstrated a solid track record regarding major mechanical failures. Owners often report satisfaction with the powertrain and overall build quality. However, as with any performance-oriented luxury car, proactive maintenance is key to ensuring long-term dependability and avoiding potential issues that can arise from spirited driving or deferred servicing.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20B20)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, increased oil consumption, whistling noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 BMW 228i presents a strong case for reliability within the luxury sport coupe segment. While the potent 2.0L turbocharged engine is generally robust, potential owners should be aware of the possibility of timing chain wear and PCV valve issues, which are not uncommon in this engine family and can be costly to repair if neglected. The ZF 8-speed automatic and 6-speed manual transmissions have proven to be very durable. Other systems like suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components show no widespread significant problems, indicating good overall build quality. Regular maintenance and addressing any early warning signs promptly are crucial for maximizing the longevity and enjoyment of this performance-oriented BMW.
