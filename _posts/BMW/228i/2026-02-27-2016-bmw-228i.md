# 2016 BMW 228i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 BMW 228i, buyers are generally drawn to its engaging rear-wheel-drive dynamics, nimble handling, and strong turbocharged performance. While formal complaint volumes in public logs remain relatively modest, evaluating powertrain options and keeping an eye on common vehicle systems will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It offers quick acceleration and good fuel efficiency, though buyers should monitor overall maintenance history closely.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, quick shifts and well-suited gear ratios for daily driving and spirited runs. It is generally robust when paired with regular fluid checks.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission appeals to enthusiasts seeking direct engagement with the drivetrain. It provides positive shift throws, though clutch wear heavily depends on prior driving habits.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V608000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=228i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An improperly sealed electrical connector on the water pump may be exposed to water and short circuit. An electrical short increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the water pump and plug connector as necessary, and install a protective shield, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 228i combines agile handling with turbocharged performance, making it an appealing choice for driving enthusiasts. Prospective buyers should thoroughly review service histories and conduct a pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of the engine and drivetrain before finalizing a purchase.
