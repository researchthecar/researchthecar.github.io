# 2012 BMW 118i

### Overview
The 2012 BMW 118i, part of the E87/F20 generation, is generally considered a reliable compact executive car, particularly praised for its engaging driving dynamics and premium interior. It typically came equipped with efficient petrol engines, offering a good balance of performance and economy. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 118i model year.

---


## Engine

### 1.6L N43B16 (Petrol)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per coil

**Valvetronic System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor performance, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of Valvetronic actuator or related sensors.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


### 2.0L N43B20 (Petrol)
**Fuel Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough running, increased fuel consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per injector

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 BMW 118i offers a generally robust ownership experience with its petrol engines and transmissions. The most commonly cited technical issues revolve around the ignition system and Valvetronic components for the 1.6L and 2.0L petrol engines, as well as potential mechatronic unit or torque converter problems in the automatic transmission. Electrical gremlins, particularly related to sensors and the electric water pump, can also arise. However, these are not universal and often depend on maintenance history and mileage. With diligent maintenance and prompt attention to early warning signs, the 118i can remain a reliable and enjoyable vehicle.
