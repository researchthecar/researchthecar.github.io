# 2016 BMW 328d xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 328d xDrive combines diesel efficiency with all-wheel-drive capability, appealing to buyers looking for a commuter car with plenty of low-end torque. While it offers solid highway fuel economy and engaging driving dynamics, potential buyers should carefully evaluate diesel-specific components and electronic systems before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Diesel 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter turbocharged diesel engine produces approximately 180 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It is known for exceptional highway fuel economy and strong low-end pulling power, though modern diesel emissions equipment requires attentive maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is widely regarded as a responsive and durable unit in modern BMW applications.
