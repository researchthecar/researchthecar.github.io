# 2017 BMW 328d xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 328d xDrive, buyers get a combination of diesel efficiency and all-wheel-drive capability. However, owners have reported several recurring mechanical and electrical concerns that prospective buyers should keep in mind during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Diesel 4-Cylinder (N47)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder engine delivers strong low-end torque and high fuel efficiency. Buyers should watch closely for cooling system leaks and fuel delivery issues during routine inspections.

**Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Low coolant warnings, sweet smells, or visible puddles beneath the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Inspect hoses, connections, and the water pump, then replace failing components to prevent overheating.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine hesitation, rough running, or sudden stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the fuel delivery and electrical systems, and replace malfunctioning fuel pumps or sensors.

**Head Gasket Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss without external leaks, white exhaust smoke, or oil mixing with coolant.
- **Solution:** Perform a combustion leak test and replace the head gasket and associated seals if compromised.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, engine sputtering under load, or failure to maintain fuel pressure.
- **Solution:** Test fuel rail pressure and replace the failing high-pressure fuel pump or lift pump.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with xDrive

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system to provide smooth, rapid gear changes. It generally maintains a solid reliability record with few widespread owner complaints.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake Cable and Clips

Service brakes generally perform well, though the parking brake retaining clips are prone to failure and can allow cables to sag.

**Brake Cable Dislodgement**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual grinding noises from the underside of the vehicle or a loose parking brake mechanism.
- **Solution:** Inspect the parking brake cable mounting clips above the driveshaft and replace broken plastic retainers.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment functions adequately overall, but occasional issues with seat belt latches and restraint warnings have been reported by owners.

**Seat Belt Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty buckling seat belts, intermittent warning chimes, or failure of the latch mechanism to lock securely.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt assembly and buckle components, replacing the latch mechanism as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V755000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The melting intake manifold can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the EGR cooler as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V907000: Fuel System, Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The EGR cooler may leak internally, causing coolant to mix with diesel engine soot, which could result in smoldering particles and a melting intake manifold. A melting intake manifold increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the EGR cooler and inspect the intake manifold, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V586000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-pressure fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 328d xDrive offers an appealing mix of efficiency, confident handling, and all-weather capability. However, used car buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection focusing on the cooling system, fuel delivery components, and undercarriage brake lines to avoid costly surprises down the road.
