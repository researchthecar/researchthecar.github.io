**Introduction**

The 2017 BMW 328d xDrive is a luxury compact sedan powered by a 2.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-4 diesel engine. It produces 180 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic Steptronic Sport transmission. Other engine options include a 2.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-4 gasoline engine (241 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque) and a 3.0L TwinPower Turbo inline-6 gasoline engine (320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque). The 328d is praised for its fuel efficiency, handling, and interior amenities, but criticized for its firm ride and lack of engine power.

**Engine**

* **2.0L TwinPower Turbo Inline-4 Diesel**

    * **Diesel Fuel Injection Pump Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, engine misfires, decreased fuel economy
    Solution: Replace fuel injection pump
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    Recalls: None Found
    
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, turbocharger whining noises, blue smoke from exhaust
    Solution: Replace turbocharger
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    Recalls: None Found
    
* **2.0L TwinPower Turbo Inline-4 Gasoline**

    * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, stalling, difficulty starting
    Solution: Replace fuel pump
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    Recalls: 17V-394, 20V-234

* **3.0L TwinPower Turbo Inline-6 Gasoline**

    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Steptronic Sport Transmission**

    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    Description/Symptoms: Shifting problems, loss of gears, transmission shuddering
    Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
    Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: Cosmetic rattle from center console
Solution: Inspect and tighten trim
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100

* **Heated Seat Element Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Heated seats not warming up
Solution: Replace heating elements
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500

**Summary**

The 2017 BMW 328d xDrive is generally a reliable vehicle, but does have some potential issues with the diesel engine, transmission, and interior. The severity of the issues vary, with some being minor cosmetic concerns and others potentially more expensive repairs. It's recommended to regularly maintain and service the vehicle to minimize the risk of encountering future problems.
