# 2016 BMW 535d xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 535d xDrive combines luxury performance with a robust diesel powertrain, offering exceptional highway fuel economy and all-weather capability. While general owner feedback reflects a refined driving experience, used-car buyers should approach luxury diesel models with careful inspection of emissions equipment and auxiliary systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L TwinPower Turbo Diesel I6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 diesel engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong low-end pulling power and high efficiency. It is generally robust mechanically, though maintaining its complex emissions control systems is crucial for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the high-torque diesel engine, providing quick shifts and optimized cruising efficiency. It is widely regarded as a durable unit when standard fluid service intervals are observed.
