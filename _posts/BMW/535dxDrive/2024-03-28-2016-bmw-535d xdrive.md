## 2016 BMW 535d xDrive

The 2016 BMW 535d xDrive is a luxury sedan powered by a 3.0-liter inline-six turbocharged diesel engine that produces 255 horsepower and 413 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 535d xDrive has a starting price of $56,400.

People like the 535d xDrive for its powerful engine, comfortable interior, and luxurious features. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior.

### Engine

**3.0L Inline-Six Turbocharged Diesel**

**Injector Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, decreased power, black smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharger Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling noise from engine
Solution: Replace turbocharger
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Fluid Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking onto ground, slipping gears
Solution: Repair or replace transmission fluid pan gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Torque Converter Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering, slipping gears
Solution: Replace torque converter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

**Front Suspension**

**Control Arm Bushing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from front suspension, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

**Electrical System**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains quickly, difficulty starting
Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

**No Issues Found**

### Interior

**Climate Control**

**AC Compressor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing cold air
Solution: Replace AC compressor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 BMW 535d xDrive is a well-regarded luxury sedan, but it is not without its problems. The most severe and expensive issues reported include injector failure, turbocharger failure, and torque converter failure. However, many of the other issues reported are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the 535d xDrive is a solid choice for buyers looking for a powerful and luxurious sedan.
