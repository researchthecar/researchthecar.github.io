# 2007 BMW 325i

### Overview
The 2007 BMW 325i, part of the E90 generation, is generally regarded as a solid vehicle with a good balance of performance and luxury. However, like any complex German automobile, it has specific areas that have shown a propensity for issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems owners might encounter. Understanding these potential weaknesses can help in proactive maintenance and informed purchasing decisions for this model year.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L N52B30 I6
**Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil smell in the engine bay, visible oil on the engine block, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the valve cover gasket and potentially the valve cover itself.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil spots under the vehicle, oil smell, low oil level warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Eccentric Shaft Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check Engine Light, rough idle, poor performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the eccentric shaft sensor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Cooling System Leaks (Water Pump/Thermostat)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, steam from engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (GM GA6HP19Z)
**Mechatronic Sleeve Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fluid leaks, harsh shifting, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the mechatronic sleeve and seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, rough engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000

## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Front Control Arm Bushings Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front control arms or bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, reduced handling, visible leaks from shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Power Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Window not moving up or down, grinding noise when operating window.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**LCM (Light Control Module) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning headlights, taillights, or interior lights; flickering lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the Light Control Module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**ABS/DSC Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** ABS and DSC warning lights illuminated on the dashboard, loss of ABS/DSC functionality.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the ABS/DSC control module.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

**Sunroof Drain Clog**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential electrical issues from water damage.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sunroof drains.
- **Cost:** $100 - $300

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 BMW 325i (E90) offers a compelling driving experience but requires attention to specific potential failure points. The N52 engine, while generally robust, is prone to oil leaks from gaskets and has a known issue with its electric water pump and thermostat. Automatic transmissions can develop leaks and torque converter problems. Electrically, issues with window regulators, light modules, and ABS/DSC modules are documented. Suspension components like control arm bushings and shocks may require replacement over time. Regular maintenance and addressing these common issues proactively are key to maintaining the reliability and enjoyment of this BMW model.
