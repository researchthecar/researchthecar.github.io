# 2004 BMW 325i

### Overview
The 2004 BMW 325i, part of the E46 generation, is generally regarded as a solid performer with a good balance of sportiness and luxury. However, like many vehicles of its era, it has specific areas that have shown a propensity for issues over time. Owners often praise its driving dynamics and build quality, but attention to certain components is recommended for long-term reliability. This analysis focuses on common reported problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential repair costs and solutions for the 2.5L inline-six engine and its associated transmissions.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L M54B25 Inline-6
**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gasket, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected gasket(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Cooling System (Water Pump, Thermostat, Hoses)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, heater not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of failed components (water pump, thermostat, hoses, expansion tank).
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**CCV (Crankcase Ventilation) System**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust, vacuum leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the CCV valve and associated hoses.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (GM A5S360R/A5S325Z)
**Harsh Shifting / Delayed Engagement**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, difficulty engaging gears, slipping.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, valve body repair, or transmission rebuild/replacement.
- **Cost:** $500 - $3000+


### 5-Speed Manual (Getrag 420G)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting, worn clutch pedal.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front lower control arms with integrated bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Rear Shock Mounts**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling or clunking noise from the rear, especially over uneven surfaces.
- **Solution:** Replacement of rear shock mounts.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**ABS/DSC Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** ABS and/or DSC warning lights illuminated on the dashboard, loss of ABS/DSC function.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the ABS/DSC control module.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, window getting stuck.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Exterior

### Body and Trim
**Headlight Lens Hazing/Yellowing**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced headlight visibility, yellowed or cloudy plastic lenses.
- **Solution:** Restoration kits or replacement of headlight assemblies.
- **Cost:** $100 - $400

**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential damage to electronics.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Pixelated LCD Display (Instrument Cluster/Radio)**
- **Symptoms:** Missing or unreadable segments on the digital displays.
- **Solution:** Repair of the LCD ribbon cable or replacement of the unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2004 BMW 325i (E46) offers a compelling driving experience but requires diligent maintenance and awareness of common issues. The M54 engine is robust but prone to oil leaks and cooling system problems, which are manageable with timely repairs. Transmission issues, particularly with the automatic, can be costly if not addressed early. Suspension components like control arm bushings and electrical gremlins such as ABS module failures are also notable. While interior and exterior issues like display pixelation and headlight hazing are common for the age, they are generally less critical. Overall, the 325i can be a reliable car if potential problems are proactively diagnosed and repaired, with an estimated average repair cost for significant issues ranging from $400 to $1200, excluding major component failures.
