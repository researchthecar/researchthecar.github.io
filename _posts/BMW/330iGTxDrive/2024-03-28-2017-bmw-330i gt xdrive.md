**Introduction:**

The 2017 BMW 330i GT xDrive is a four-door, five-passenger luxury gran turismo powered by a 2.0 liter TwinPower Turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and xDrive all-wheel drive. The 330i GT is known for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and stylish design. However, it has received some criticism for its high price and limited cargo space.

**Engine**

* **2.0 liter TwinPower Turbocharged inline-four**

    * **Oil Leaks from Valve Cover**
        * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket onto the engine. Symptoms include a burning oil smell, low oil levels, and smoke from the engine.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Cracking**
        * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold can develop cracks, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel economy. Symptoms include a rough idle, misfires, and decreased responsiveness.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed Automatic**

    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, resulting in delayed or rough shifts. Symptoms include hesitation, juddering, and slipping.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control unit or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Draining**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, leaving the car unable to start. Symptoms include difficulty starting or extended cranking.
        * Solution: Replace the battery or identify and repair electrical issues causing the drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Short**
        * Description/Symptoms: An electrical short can occur, causing electrical malfunctions or even a fire. Symptoms include flickering lights, blown fuses, and smoke or burning smells.
        * Solution: Identify and repair the source of the short circuit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the location and severity of the short
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Control Knob Malfunction**
        * Description/Symptoms: The control knob for the infotainment system may fail to respond or become intermittent. Symptoms include difficulty navigating menus or selecting functions.
        * Solution: Replace or repair the control knob.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2017 BMW 330i GT is a well-liked vehicle overall, it does have some reported issues, particularly related to the engine and transmission. The severity of the issues can range from minor inconveniences to major repairs, and the cost to fix can be significant. If you are considering purchasing a 330i GT, it is important to be aware of these potential issues and factor them into your decision.
