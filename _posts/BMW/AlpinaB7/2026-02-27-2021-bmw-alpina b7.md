## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant running of the compressor, vehicle may sag.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent system reboots.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the parasitic draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Actuator Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Air not directing correctly (e.g., only blowing from defroster), inconsistent temperature.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty HVAC blend door actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Seat Adjustments
**Motor or Control Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving, memory functions failing.
- **Solution:** Replacement of seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW Alpina B7 is a highly reliable luxury sedan, benefiting from BMW's extensive engineering and Alpina's performance enhancements. The powertrain, including the formidable 4.4L V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. Potential concerns are primarily related to the sophisticated adaptive air suspension system, which can experience leaks or compressor failures, and occasional electrical gremlins such as infotainment glitches or parasitic battery drain. Interior components like climate control actuators and seat motors can also require attention. Despite these isolated issues, the B7 offers a premium ownership experience with a strong overall reliability record for its class.
