# 2007 BMW Alpina B7

### Overview
The 2007 BMW Alpina B7, a high-performance variant of the 7 Series, offers a blend of luxury and potent power. Based on the E65 generation, it features a hand-built Alpina engine and enhanced chassis components. While generally well-regarded for its performance and exclusivity, like any specialized vehicle, it can present specific reliability concerns that owners should be aware of. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Supercharged V8 (E65)
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks around hoses and radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, radiator, water pump, or thermostat.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, or crankshaft seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

**Supercharger Belt Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, whining noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the supercharger drive belt.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (GM 5L40-E/5HP19)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or seals, rough shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission pan gasket, seals, or torque converter.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

**Solenoid Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of transmission solenoids.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Active Roll Stabilization (ARS) Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid leaks from hydraulic actuators, clunking noises over bumps, reduced handling stability.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking ARS actuators or hydraulic lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2500

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, 'air suspension inactive' warning message, loss of ride height adjustment.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor and potentially air springs.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1800


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**iDrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of iDrive controller/head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow window movement, grinding noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not illuminating, flickering lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 BMW Alpina B7 is a rare and powerful machine. While its core powertrain and chassis are robust, owners should anticipate potential costs associated with its specialized components. Issues with the cooling system, oil leaks, and the adaptive suspension are the most commonly cited concerns. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the iDrive system, can also arise. Given its performance focus and limited production, maintenance and repair costs can be higher than standard BMW models. Regular, specialized servicing is crucial for maintaining its performance and reliability.
