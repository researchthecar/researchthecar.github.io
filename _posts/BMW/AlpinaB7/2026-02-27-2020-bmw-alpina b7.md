## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant running of the compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or unexpected reboots of the iDrive system.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for software/diagnostics)


### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent warnings or failures of features like adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors.
- **Solution:** Cleaning, recalibration, or replacement of faulty sensors (e.g., radar, cameras, ultrasonic).
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000 per sensor/module

### Climate Control System
**Actuator Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Uneven temperature distribution, inability to control specific vents, unusual noises from the dashboard.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 BMW Alpina B7 is a highly reliable vehicle, especially considering its performance-oriented nature and complex systems. The powertrain (4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 and 8-speed automatic transmission) exhibits excellent durability with no widespread reported issues. The primary areas that may require attention are the adaptive air suspension, where leaks or compressor issues can arise over time, and certain electrical components, such as infotainment software glitches or ADAS sensor malfunctions. Interior climate control actuators can also be a point of failure. Overall, with diligent maintenance and addressing these specific potential issues, the Alpina B7 offers a robust and dependable ownership experience for a luxury performance sedan.
