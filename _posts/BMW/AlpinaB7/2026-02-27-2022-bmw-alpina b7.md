# 2022 BMW Alpina B7

### Overview
The 2022 BMW Alpina B7 represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance, blending a potent V8 engine with Alpina's bespoke tuning and luxurious appointments. As a high-performance luxury sedan, its reliability is generally strong, benefiting from BMW's robust engineering and Alpina's meticulous refinement. Owners typically experience a high level of satisfaction, with the vehicle designed for long-term durability and driving pleasure. However, as with any complex, high-performance vehicle, specific areas can occasionally present challenges, though widespread systemic failures are uncommon. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or unexpected reboots of the iDrive system.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system resets. In rare cases, module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500 (for software updates/diagnostics)


### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent warnings or deactivation of features like adaptive cruise control, lane keeping assist, or parking sensors.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sensors, recalibration, or replacement of faulty sensors/modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW Alpina B7 is a highly reliable luxury performance sedan, with its core powertrain components demonstrating excellent durability. The most commonly cited potential issues, though not widespread, tend to be related to the advanced adaptive air suspension system, which can develop leaks over time, and occasional electronic glitches within the sophisticated infotainment and ADAS systems. These are generally manageable with proper diagnostics and repair. For owners who prioritize performance, luxury, and a refined driving experience, the Alpina B7 offers a compelling package with a strong overall reliability record, provided that potential suspension and electronic nuances are addressed proactively.
