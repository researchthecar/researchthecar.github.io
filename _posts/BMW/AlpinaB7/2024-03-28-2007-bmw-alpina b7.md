**Introduction**

The 2007 BMW Alpina B7 is a high-performance luxury sedan based on the BMW 7 Series. It is powered by a 4.4-liter V8 engine tuned by Alpina to produce 500 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. The B7 is available with either a six-speed automatic transmission or a six-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

* **4.4-Liter V8**

    * **Fuel Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience a sudden loss of power or may fail to start altogether.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may make a rattling noise when started or accelerated.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience reduced power and fuel economy.
        * Solution: Clean the intake valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

    * **Transmission Slippage**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * **Torque Converter Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience vibrations or shuddering when accelerating.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery may drain prematurely.
    * Solution: Test and replace the faulty component causing the drain (e.g., alternator, starter, etc.).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture may accumulate inside the headlights.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may malfunction or freeze.
    * Solution: Update or replace the navigation system software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2007 BMW Alpina B7 is a powerful and luxurious sedan, but it has a few known issues that can be expensive to repair. The most common issues affect the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific part that fails. Overall, the B7 is a well-built vehicle with a strong reputation for reliability, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues before purchasing one.
