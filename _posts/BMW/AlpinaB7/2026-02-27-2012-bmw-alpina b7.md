# 2012 BMW Alpina B7

### Overview
The 2012 BMW Alpina B7 represents a high-performance iteration of the 7 Series, blending luxury with potent power. As a specialized model, its reliability data often reflects the underlying BMW platform with Alpina's unique enhancements. While generally well-regarded for its engineering, specific areas can experience issues common to high-output vehicles and complex luxury car systems. Owners should be aware of potential concerns related to its advanced powertrain and sophisticated electronics, though many components demonstrate robust build quality. Maintenance is crucial for preserving its performance and longevity.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger unit(s).
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Pipes)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant loss, sweet smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking coolant hoses and pipes.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP90)
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, transmission warning light, inability to select gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement or reprogramming of the TCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Mechatronics Unit Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronics unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Active Roll Stabilization Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll in corners, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of stabilizer bar actuators or hydraulic pump.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, suspension warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (iDrive)
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** iDrive screen becomes unresponsive or freezes, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies frequently, electrical components malfunction intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 BMW Alpina B7 is a powerful and luxurious sedan, but its complexity means potential for specific issues, particularly with the high-performance engine, advanced transmission, and sophisticated electronic systems. Turbocharger issues, transmission control module failures, and problems with the adaptive suspension are the most commonly cited concerns. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and parasitic battery drain, can also occur. While the exterior and interior generally hold up well, proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning lights are essential for owners to mitigate potential repair costs and ensure the continued reliability of this exclusive vehicle.
