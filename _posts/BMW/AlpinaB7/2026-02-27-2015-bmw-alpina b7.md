## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per corner

**Air Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant running of compressor, vehicle may sag.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (iDrive)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $700


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Climate Control System
**Actuator Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Air not directing correctly (e.g., only blowing from defroster), inconsistent temperature.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty HVAC blend door actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 BMW Alpina B7 is a highly capable and luxurious performance sedan. Its core powertrain and transmission are exceptionally robust, with no widespread issues reported. The primary areas that may require attention are the adaptive air suspension, which can develop leaks or compressor issues over time, and the complex electrical system, particularly the infotainment unit which may experience software glitches. Interior climate control actuators can also be a point of failure. Despite these potential issues, which are not uncommon for high-end vehicles of this era, the B7 offers a generally reliable ownership experience when properly maintained. Proactive checks of the suspension and software updates can mitigate many potential problems.
