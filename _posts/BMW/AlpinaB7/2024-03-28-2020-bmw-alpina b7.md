**Introduction:**

The 2020 BMW Alpina B7 is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine that produces 600 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission and comes standard with all-wheel drive. The B7 offers a blend of performance, luxury, and practicality. It is praised for its powerful engine, responsive handling, and well-appointed interior. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine:**

* **4.4L Twin-Turbo V8:**

   * **Valve Stem Seal Failure:**
   Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust
   Solution: Replace valve stem seals
   Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission:**

   * **No Issues Found**

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
   Symptoms: System freezes, navigation issues, audio problems
   Solution: Software update or hardware replacement
   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   Recalls: None Found

* **Power Window Failure:**
   Symptoms: Windows refuse to open or close
   Solution: Replace window motor or switch
   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Power Trunk Lid Malfunction:**
   Symptoms: Trunk won't open or close properly
   Solution: Replace trunk lid motor or switch
   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Sunroof Switch Failure:**
   Symptoms: Sunroof won't open or close
   Solution: Replace sunroof switch
   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
   Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The reported issues with the 2020 BMW Alpina B7 are primarily related to electrical and interior components. The valve stem seal failure in the V8 engine is a more serious and expensive issue to repair. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues are relatively minor and do not detract significantly from the overall appeal of this high-performance luxury sedan.
