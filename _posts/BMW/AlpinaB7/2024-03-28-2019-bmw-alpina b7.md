**2019 BMW Alpina B7**

The 2019 BMW Alpina B7 is a high-performance luxury sedan based on the BMW 7 Series. It is powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 600 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. The B7 comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

People who like the B7 praise its powerful engine, luxurious interior, and impressive handling. However, some people find the B7 to be too expensive and its ride quality to be too firm.

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, increased oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust pipe
  * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: NHTSA 20V280
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, loss of power
  * Solution: Replace the fuel injector
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque Converter Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping during gear changes, loss of power
  * Solution: Replace the torque converter
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle will not raise or lower, may sit lower than normal
  * Solution: Replace the air suspension compressor
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, may not start after sitting for a few days
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair the cause of the battery drain
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control System Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system does not work properly
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair the cause of the failure
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 BMW Alpina B7 is a reliable and well-built vehicle. However, like all vehicles, it is not without its potential issues. The most common issues reported by owners include turbocharger failure, fuel injector failure, and torque converter failure. These issues can be expensive to repair, so it is important to be aware of them before purchasing a B7.
