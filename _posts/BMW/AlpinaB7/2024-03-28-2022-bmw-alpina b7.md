**Introduction:**
The 2022 BMW Alpina B7 is a high-performance luxury sedan that boasts refined styling, a powerful engine, and advanced technology. It comes equipped with a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, producing 600 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. The B7 is known for its thrilling driving experience, plush interior, and cutting-edge features. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of reported issues.

**Engine**
* **4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (B58B44T2)*
    * **Turbocharger Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased exhaust smoke, excessive oil consumption, and audible turbocharger noises.
        * Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
    * **Fuel Pump Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced fuel efficiency, and illuminated check engine light.
        * Solution: Replace defective fuel pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,800+

**Transmission**
* **8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP76)**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations and clunking noises during gear changes, especially at low speeds.
        * Solution: Replace faulty torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
    * **Shift Solenoid Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or hard shifting, transmission slipping, and illuminated check engine light.
        * Solution: Replace defective shift solenoid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Suspension**
* **Adaptive Air Suspension:**
    * **Air Compressor Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Suspension sags or fails to adjust properly, reduced ride comfort, and illuminated suspension warning lights.
        * Solution: Replace faulty air compressor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,200+
    * **Air Strut Leak:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Suspension sags on one or more corners of the vehicle, reduced ride comfort, and noticeable hissing sounds.
        * Solution: Replace leaking air strut(s).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,800+ per strut

**Electrical**
* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start or experiences electrical issues after sitting for extended periods.
    * Solution: Replace defective battery or check for parasitic electrical drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+
* **Rearview Camera Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera display remains blank or displays distorted images.
    * Solution: Replace faulty camera module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**Exterior**
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture build-up inside the headlight housing, reducing visibility during inclement weather.
    * Solution: Replace defective headlight housing(s).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+ per headlight

**Interior**
* **Navigation System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system experiences freezing, lagging, or incorrect route guidance.
    * Solution: Update software or replace faulty navigation module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+

**Summary:**
While the 2022 BMW Alpina B7 is a highly capable luxury sedan, it has its fair share of reported issues. Some of the more prevalent problems include turbocharger and fuel pump failures in the engine, torque converter and shift solenoid issues in the transmission, air suspension problems in the suspension, and electrical issues such as battery discharge and rearview camera failure. The cost of repairs for these issues can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars, depending on the severity of the problem. Overall, it is important to be aware of these potential issues before purchasing a 2022 BMW Alpina B7 and to factor in the potential cost of repairs.
