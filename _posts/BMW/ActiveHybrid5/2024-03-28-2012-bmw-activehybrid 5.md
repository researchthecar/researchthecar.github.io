**Introduction to the 2012 BMW ActiveHybrid 5**

The 2012 BMW ActiveHybrid 5 combines a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-6 engine with an electric motor, delivering a combined output of 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, the ActiveHybrid 5 offers a smooth and efficient driving experience. While praised for its sporty handling and comfortable interior, it has been criticized for its fuel economy not living up to expectations and its high maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail prematurely, resulting in reduced engine power and increased oil consumption.
    * Description/Symptoms: Significant loss of power, smoke from the exhaust, abnormal noises from the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** The transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes, causing rough or delayed shifting.
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, erratic transmission behavior, loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the transmission solenoids or clutches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushings Worn:** The control arm bushings may wear out prematurely, leading to excessive vibration and noise when driving over bumps.
    * Description/Symptoms: Shaking or vibrating while driving, especially on uneven surfaces, reduced handling precision.
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500 per corner
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** The 12-volt battery may discharge prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the car, electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replace or repair the battery or charging system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:** The iDrive infotainment system may experience malfunctions such as freezing, glitches, or connectivity issues.
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable navigation, audio, or other features, unresponsive screen, software errors.
    * Solution: Software update or hardware replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 BMW ActiveHybrid 5 is a powerful and sophisticated vehicle, but it is not without its issues. The turbocharger, transmission, and suspension can experience problems, which can be costly to repair. Additionally, the infotainment system may malfunction. However, the severity of these issues is relatively low, and the overall cost of ownership is comparable to other luxury vehicles in its class.
