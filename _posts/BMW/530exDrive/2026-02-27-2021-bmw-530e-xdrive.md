# 2021 BMW 530e xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW 530e xDrive combines luxury and plug-in hybrid efficiency with all-wheel-drive capability. For used-car buyers, evaluating the powertrain options and monitoring potential electrical or fuel system concerns is recommended during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Plug-In Hybrid

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs electric assistance with a traditional gas engine for balanced performance and efficiency. It delivers smooth power delivery suited for daily commuting, though proper maintenance of both systems is essential.

**Heavy Smoke From Vehicle**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience sudden heavy white smoke emitting from the vehicle while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle thoroughly inspected by a qualified technician to identify the source of the smoke, which may involve fuel or electrical system faults.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear shifts and optimizes both performance and fuel economy across various driving conditions. It is generally robust and well-regarded in modern BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=530e&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V601000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=530e&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The debris could result in a short-circuit, increasing the risk of fire or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will inspect and, if necessary, replace the battery module free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW 530e xDrive offers an appealing blend of advanced hybrid technology and refined luxury. Buyers should ensure a comprehensive mechanical and electrical inspection is performed to catch any underlying issues before purchase.
