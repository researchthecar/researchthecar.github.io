# 2016 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe blends sleek styling, luxurious interior appointments, and responsive performance. For used-car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and potential owner-reported areas of concern can help guide a smart purchase decision.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces approximately 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It offers smooth power delivery and generally solid reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system to provide quick, refined shifts. It is widely regarded as a robust and dependable unit in modern BMW lineups.
