# 2017 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe blends luxury, performance, and striking styling into a four-door fastback design. While it offers a refined driving experience and a potent powertrain, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle maintenance histories and perform a thorough inspection of comfort and utility features before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces approximately 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is generally praised for its smooth power delivery and robust performance in daily driving scenarios.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and seamless gear shifts. It is well-regarded for durability when properly maintained with fluid changes.

## Exterior

### Trunk Release Mechanism

Electrical accessories and trunk mechanisms generally function reliably, though occasional latch or switch glitches have been documented by vehicle owners.

**Trunk Release Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported issues with the trunk failing to open electronically, which can restrict access to cargo and emergency release handles located within the trunk space.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic trunk latch assembly, wiring harness, and release switches for proper function and replace failed actuators or fuses as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe is a sophisticated luxury touring option with strong mechanical foundations. Buyers should verify the functionality of all interior electronics and convenience features during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
