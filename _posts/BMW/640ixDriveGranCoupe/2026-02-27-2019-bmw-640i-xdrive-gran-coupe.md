# 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe means looking at a stylish, high-performance luxury vehicle with a striking silhouette and robust powertrain options. While owner reports to federal safety regulators are minimal for this model year, buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify the operation of onboard technology, electronics, and standard mechanical components.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and responsive acceleration. It is generally regarded as a robust and dependable powerplant when properly maintained with timely fluid changes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup offers quick, seamless gear changes and efficient highway cruising. It has a strong reputation for durability in modern BMW applications.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Suspension and wheel equipment generally perform well, though tire selection requires careful attention depending on regional weather.

**Summer Tire Limitations**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle equipped with summer performance tires experiences severely reduced traction and drivability in snowy or icy winter conditions.
- **Solution:** Replace factory summer tires with dedicated winter tires or high-quality all-season tires if operating in cold-weather climates.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera

Owner reports note potential failures in driver assistance hardware.

**Inoperable Backup Camera**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display fails to activate when shifting into reverse, leaving the driver without visual assistance.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the camera module, wiring harness, and display unit for faults or blown fuses.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe combines luxury, executive styling, and strong performance, backed by a limited number of formal owner complaints. Prospective buyers should prioritize verifying all electronic features, such as parking cameras, and ensure the vehicle is equipped with appropriate tires for their local climate before finalizing a purchase.
