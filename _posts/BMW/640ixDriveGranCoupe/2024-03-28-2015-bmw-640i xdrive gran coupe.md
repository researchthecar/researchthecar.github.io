**Introduction:**

The 2015 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe is a four-door sports car with a sleek design and powerful performance. It comes with a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine that produces 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and an all-wheel drive system. Other engine and transmission options include a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine with 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers and critics alike have praised the BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe for its stylish exterior, spacious interior, and impressive handling. However, the vehicle has also been reported to have some issues in certain areas.

**Engine**

* **N55 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six**
    * **Fuel Injector Failure:**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel efficiency
        * **Solution:** Replace faulty fuel injectors
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,200+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure:**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased exhaust emissions, whining or rattling noises
        * **Solution:** Replace turbocharger wastegate assembly
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
        * **Recalls:** None Found
* **N63 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
    * **Timing Chain Failure:**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Engine rattling, rough idling, decreased engine power
        * **Solution:** Replace timing chain and related components
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $3,000 - $5,000+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shift Delay:**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Delayed or hesitant gear shifts
        * **Solution:** Software update or transmission fluid change
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
        * **Recalls:** None Found
    * **Torque Converter Failure:**
        * **Description/Symptoms:** Shuddering or vibration during acceleration, slipping gears
        * **Solution:** Replace torque converter
        * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,800+
        * **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * **Solution:** Identify and repair faulty electrical component (e.g., parasitic drain)
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Headlight Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** One or both headlights stop working
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty headlight ballast or bulb
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Slow response or freezing of the infotainment system
    * **Solution:** Software update
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** Free
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Air conditioning or heating not working properly
    * **Solution:** Replace faulty climate control module or actuator
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe is generally a reliable and well-regarded vehicle. However, it has been reported to have some potential issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. These issues vary in severity and cost to fix, but many can be resolved through software updates or repairs by a qualified mechanic. It is important to research the specific issues and their potential solutions before purchasing or owning this vehicle.
