**2014 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe**

The 2014 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers a spacious and stylish interior, a potent engine, and advanced handling capabilities. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine that produces 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and xDrive all-wheel drive. The 640i xDrive Gran Coupe is praised for its refined driving dynamics, well-appointed cabin, and impressive performance, but it is also known for having a few notable issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6**

* **Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the engine, rough idling, poor acceleration, stalling
    * Solution: Replacement of the fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, loss of boost, check engine light
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger wastegate actuator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **High-Pressure Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, reduced fuel economy, rough idling
    * Solution: Replacement of the high-pressure fuel injector
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per injector
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Delays or Hesitations:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slow or delayed gear changes, hesitation when accelerating
    * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid flush, or transmission replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Clunking or Grinding Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Unusual noises when shifting gears
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or worn bushings, clunking or rattling noise over bumps
    * Solution: Replacement of the control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor ride quality, excessive body roll, leaking shock absorbers
    * Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per shock
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replacement of the battery or alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation inside the headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per assembly
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Trim Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Chrome or black trim around the door handles peels or fades
    * Solution: Replacement of the door handle trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per door
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Front Bumper Lip Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Lower front bumper lip is easily damaged due to its low ground clearance
    * Solution: Replacement or repair of the front bumper lip
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Squeaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noise when turning the steering wheel
    * Solution: Lubrication or replacement of steering wheel components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **iDrive System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Glitches, freezes, or unresponsive iDrive infotainment system
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of iDrive components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Coupe is a well-rounded luxury performance vehicle, but it is not without its issues. Some of the more common problems include fuel pump failure, shifting delays or hesitations, front control arm bushing failure, battery discharge, and door handle trim peeling. While these issues can be frustrating and costly to fix, the severity and frequency vary depending on the specific vehicle and its maintenance history.
