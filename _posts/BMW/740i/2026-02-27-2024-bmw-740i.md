# 2024 BMW 740i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 740i represents the pinnacle of modern luxury, offering advanced technology, striking presence, and refined powertrains. As a relatively new luxury flagship in the used marketplace, buyers generally evaluate it for its high-end feature set, ride quality, and long-term sophistication. While early owner feedback in public complaint logs is sparse, understanding its powertrain options and baseline maintenance expectations remains essential for a confident luxury purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 375 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. Known in the industry as the B58, this powerplant is widely praised for its smooth power delivery, robust engineering, and strong overall reliability compared to past German luxury flagships.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs seamlessly with the inline-six engine, delivering quick shifts and effortless highway cruising. It is a well-regarded ZF unit known for durability and refined operation across various BMW models.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V700000: Electrical System:Adas:Driver Monitoring:Camera/Sensor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The ground connection to the steering wheel may not have been attached correctly, resulting in an inoperative hands-on detection system. An inoperative hands-on detection system may fail to detect when the driver's hands are not on the steering wheel and therefore not engage the emergency stopping assistant system, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reattach the ground connection as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V104000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V697000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** In addition, the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Dynamic Stability Control (DSC) system may not function. Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V739000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V422000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Electric:Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.
