# 2021 BMW 740i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW 740i represents the pinnacle of luxury, offering advanced technology, premium comfort, and a sophisticated powertrain. For used car buyers, this flagship sedan delivers a refined driving experience, though prospective owners should carefully evaluate its complex electronic systems and maintenance history.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque. Known as the B58, this powerplant is widely regarded by enthusiasts and mechanics for its robust build quality, smooth power delivery, and strong reliability profile compared to previous generations.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission utilizes a torque converter setup paired with steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters. It delivers seamless gear changes and pairs exceptionally well with the inline-six engine while maintaining solid long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V576000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A starter motor that overheats can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW 740i is a remarkably sophisticated luxury sedan featuring a highly praised powertrain and refined driving dynamics. While major mechanical failures are rare, buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive service histories and budget for the higher upkeep costs typically associated with flagship German luxury vehicles.
