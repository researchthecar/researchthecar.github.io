# 2020 BMW 740i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 740i represents the pinnacle of executive luxury, blending impressive performance with advanced comfort and technology. For used-car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and monitoring common owner-reported trouble spots is key to a satisfying ownership experience. While modern luxury flagships require attentive maintenance, approaching a used purchase with informed expectations can help secure a well-maintained example.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. Known as the B58, this powerplant is generally regarded as smooth, responsive, and notably reliable for a modern luxury vehicle.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission offers seamless gear changes and pairs effectively with the inline-six engine. It enjoys a strong reputation for durability and refinement across various driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V576000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A starter motor that overheats can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2020 BMW 740i offers a sophisticated driving dynamic and a plush cabin, making it an appealing choice on the pre-owned market. Buyers should prioritize comprehensive service history checks and a pre-purchase inspection by a qualified BMW specialist to ensure long-term reliability.
