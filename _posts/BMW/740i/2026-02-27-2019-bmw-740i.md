# 2019 BMW 740i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW 740i represents the pinnacle of luxury, combining stately road manners with advanced technology. As a flagship sedan, it offers sophisticated engineering and impressive comfort, though prospective used-car buyers should carefully inspect each vehicle's service history and electronic features to ensure trouble-free ownership.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth power and strong overall reliability for everyday driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, seamless shifts paired with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive setups, known for durability and refined performance.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V465000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 740i is a sophisticated luxury sedan that generally offers robust mechanical performance from its powertrain options. Buyers looking at a used model should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and general vehicle history to secure a dependable luxury experience.
