# 2023 BMW 740i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 740i represents the pinnacle of modern luxury and technology for the brand's flagship sedan. As a relatively new model year on the used market, it generally benefits from modern engineering and advanced powertrain options, though prospective buyers should still carefully review vehicle history and remaining factory warranties.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine (B58) produces approximately 375 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its smooth power delivery, impressive efficiency, and robust long-term reliability relative to older BMW performance powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features ZF gearsets, offering quick and seamless shifts across all driving modes. It is paired standard with the inline-6 engine and generally exhibits very high reliability in daily driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V860000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The telematics control unit may not transmit the vehicle location information to emergency responders. Emergency response may be delayed without the vehicle location information, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the telematics control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V414000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front seat length adjustment system may not have been welded properly. An improperly welded front seat may not properly restrain an occupant, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front seats and if necessary, replace the seat front structure including the seat length adjustment system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V479000: Electrical System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The Interaction Bar, located on the dashboard, may not activate the windshield defrosting/defogging system and/or the hazard warning lights. In addition, those control buttons may not illuminate when pressed.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the Interaction Bar, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V644000: Visibility/Wiper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Windshield wiper failure can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the windshield wiper system, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V700000: Electrical System:Adas:Driver Monitoring:Camera/Sensor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The ground connection to the steering wheel may not have been attached correctly, resulting in an inoperative hands-on detection system. An inoperative hands-on detection system may fail to detect when the driver's hands are not on the steering wheel and therefore not engage the emergency stopping assistant system, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reattach the ground connection as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V104000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V697000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** In addition, the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Dynamic Stability Control (DSC) system may not function. Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V739000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=740i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW 740i is an exceptionally sophisticated luxury sedan that combines refined performance with modern amenities. Because early-adopter luxury vehicles can occasionally feature complex electrical or mechanical issues, buyers should prioritize well-maintained examples with a documented service history and remaining warranty coverage.
