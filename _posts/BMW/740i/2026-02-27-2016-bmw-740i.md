# 2016 BMW 740i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury flagship sedan like the 2016 BMW 740i, it helps to understand both its sophisticated engineering and the specific issues reported by owners. While owner feedback highlights advanced features and strong performance, a few recurring mechanical and electrical issues have surfaced over time. Reviewing these common trouble spots can help prospective buyers perform a more thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

The 2016 BMW 740i is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine producing approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. Known for delivering smooth and efficient power to the luxury sedan, the engine itself is generally robust, though peripheral cooling system components warrant careful attention.

**Radiator Shutter / Active Air Flap Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience engine malfunction warnings or engine overheating due to upper and lower radiator blinds failing to open or close properly.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the faulty upper and lower radiator shutter actuators or blinds as needed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive, this setup delivers seamless gear changes and optimized highway efficiency. It is widely regarded as one of the most reliable units in its class with few widespread mechanical complaints.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension

The air suspension provides a plush, highly customizable ride quality typical of a luxury flagship, but complex air struts and compressors can wear out and require costly repairs as the vehicle ages.

**Front Air Suspension Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The front end of the vehicle remains lowered, which can cause the tires to rub against the inner fenders or body components while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the air suspension system for leaks or compressor failure, and replace faulty struts or control modules.

## Electrical

### Parking Distance Control & Infotainment

The cabin is heavily reliant on advanced electronics and software integrations, which can occasionally experience bugs, sensor glitches, or infotainment freezes.

**Parking Sensor and Audio System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic Park Distance Control (PDC) system can act flakily or fail entirely, and the audio control system may occasionally freeze and stop responding to inputs.
- **Solution:** Check for available software updates or replace malfunctioning control modules and parking sensors.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

The exterior features sleek styling and large glass panels, though rare structural glass incidents have been reported by vehicle owners.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass can unexpectedly fracture or shatter inward while driving without any prior impact.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for stress points and replace the shattered glass panel under comprehensive insurance or warranty if applicable.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Driver Assists

Advanced safety features and sensor suites provide high levels of occupant protection, though occasional sensor recalibrations or harness checks may be necessary.

**Phantom Seat Belt Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may display false fasten seat belt warnings or activate blind spot warning lights intermittently while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt buckle wiring harnesses for looseness or corrosion and secure or replace faulty sensors.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 740i offers impressive luxury, powerful performance, and advanced technology that make it an attractive pre-owned option. However, buyers should carefully inspect complex systems such as the active cooling flaps, air suspension, and onboard electronics. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable BMW technician will help ensure a satisfying and trouble-free ownership experience.
