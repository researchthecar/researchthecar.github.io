# 2021 BMW 745Le xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW 745Le xDrive represents the pinnacle of luxury plug-in hybrid engineering, offering a sophisticated blend of electrified efficiency and traditional flagship comfort. As a complex luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate the condition of its powertrain, onboard electronics, and hybrid components. While owner-reported complaints for this specific model year remain limited, reviewing potential trouble areas helps ensure a confident pre-owned purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-In Hybrid

This powertrain pairs a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine with an integrated electric motor to produce a combined 389 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It delivers seamless acceleration and respectable efficiency, though its high-tech nature requires meticulous maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features an integrated electric motor and routes power through an intelligent xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is widely praised for smooth, rapid gear changes and durable overall performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745Le&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745Le&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V601000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745Le&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The debris could result in a short-circuit, increasing the risk of fire or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will inspect and, if necessary, replace the battery module free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V598000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745Le&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** During engine start, the engine management software may cause loss of braking assist. Loss of braking assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine management software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
