# 2020 BMW 745Le xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 745Le xDrive offers a sophisticated plug-in hybrid luxury experience, combining a turbocharged inline-six engine with electric propulsion for refined performance and efficiency. As a high-end flagship sedan, shopping for a used model involves balancing advanced technology and comfort against potential complex electrical and powertrain maintenance costs as the vehicle ages.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-In Hybrid

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine is paired with an electric motor to deliver a combined output of approximately 389 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth, effortless acceleration and solid overall reliability, though routine maintenance on high-complexity hybrid systems is essential.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system with seamless gear changes. It is widely regarded as a durable and responsive unit in modern BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745Le&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745Le&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V598000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745Le&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** During engine start, the engine management software may cause loss of braking assist. Loss of braking assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine management software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW 745Le xDrive combines luxury and efficiency, but buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history and remaining battery health. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a specialist familiar with BMW plug-in hybrid systems is strongly recommended.
