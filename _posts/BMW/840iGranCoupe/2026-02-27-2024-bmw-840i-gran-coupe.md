# 2024 BMW 840i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 840i Gran Coupe is a luxurious and high-performing four-door flagship that combines striking design with impressive long-distance cruising capabilities. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from mature engineering and advanced technology features. Prospective used-car buyers should review vehicle history and ensure remaining factory warranty coverage is verified, as typical for modern high-end European luxury vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's most reliable and smooth modern powerplants, offering strong performance paired with respectable efficiency.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear changes and is paired with either rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. This gearbox has proven exceptionally durable and refined across numerous BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 840i Gran Coupe presents a solid choice for used luxury car buyers seeking style, comfort, and robust powertrain performance. With no widespread recurring mechanical defects reported at this stage, routine maintenance history remains the primary focal point during inspection.
