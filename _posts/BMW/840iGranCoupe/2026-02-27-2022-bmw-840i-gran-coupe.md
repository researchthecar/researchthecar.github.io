# 2022 BMW 840i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 840i Gran Coupe is a striking luxury four-door that blends high-end performance with executive style. Powered by BMW’s respected inline-six engine, it offers smooth acceleration and refined grand touring capabilities for used-car buyers looking for exclusivity and modern tech.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It is generally praised for its robust performance, smooth power delivery, and solid reputation for long-term mechanical reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission utilizes a torque converter setup paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It offers crisp, rapid gear shifts and is widely considered one of the most reliable and refined transmissions in its class.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 840i Gran Coupe stands out as a luxurious and mechanically sound pre-owned choice, supported by a strong powertrain. Prospective buyers should still conduct a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and electronic health before finalizing a purchase.
