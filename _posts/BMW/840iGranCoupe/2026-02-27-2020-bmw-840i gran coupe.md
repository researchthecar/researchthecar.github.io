The 2020 BMW 840i Gran Coupe, powered by the robust B58 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine, generally exhibits strong reliability. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring its longevity, but significant widespread mechanical failures are uncommon for this model year.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 BMW 840i Gran Coupe is a reliable luxury vehicle, with its core powertrain components (engine and transmission) showing excellent durability. The most commonly reported issues tend to be related to the sophisticated electrical and infotainment systems, which can sometimes experience software glitches or battery-related problems. These are generally manageable with software updates or component replacement. The suspension, exterior, and interior components have proven to be robust. Overall, for a high-performance luxury coupe, the 840i Gran Coupe offers a commendable level of reliability, provided regular maintenance is performed and potential electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
