### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 840i Gran Coupe demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The robust 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the highly regarded ZF 8-speed automatic transmission are known for their durability and performance. While major mechanical failures are rare, potential owners should be mindful of occasional electrical gremlins, such as minor infotainment system glitches or intermittent battery drain, which are not uncommon in modern luxury vehicles with complex electronic systems. These issues are generally addressable through software updates or targeted diagnostics. Overall, the 840i Gran Coupe offers a reliable and enjoyable ownership experience, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential minor electrical issues are addressed promptly.
