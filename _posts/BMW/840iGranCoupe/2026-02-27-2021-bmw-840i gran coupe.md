The focus for this analysis is on potential mechanical and electrical components that might deviate from expected performance, excluding routine maintenance. Overall, the 840i Gran Coupe is considered a dependable luxury sports sedan, with a low incidence of significant mechanical failures.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW 840i Gran Coupe demonstrates commendable reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission are well-regarded for their durability and performance. While the mechanical aspects are solid, minor electrical gremlins, such as occasional infotainment system glitches and rare instances of battery drain, are the most commonly cited issues. These are generally manageable through software updates or diagnostics. Suspension and exterior/interior components show no significant widespread problems. Overall, the 840i Gran Coupe offers a reliable luxury driving experience, with potential issues being minor and infrequent, making it a strong contender in its segment.
