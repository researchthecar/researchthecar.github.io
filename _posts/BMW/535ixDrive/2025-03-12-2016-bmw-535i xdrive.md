The 2016 BMW 535i xDrive is a mid-size luxury sedan known for its blend of performance, comfort, and technology. It is powered by a 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine, producing 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options for the 2016 5 Series include a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 in the 528i and a 4.4L turbocharged V8 in the 550i. The 535i xDrive provides a comfortable ride, agile handling, and a well-appointed interior. Some common criticisms include the iDrive infotainment system's learning curve and potentially high maintenance costs. It also lacks the sporty feel of some of its competitors.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)**

*   **Oil Leaks from Valve Cover Gasket**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking down the side of the engine, burning oil smell, low oil levels.
    *   Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Oil Filter Housing Gasket Leak**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leak near the front of the engine, low oil levels, oil dripping.
    *   Solution: Replace the oil filter housing gasket.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant low message. Car is overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace leaking coolant lines or coolant tank.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Jerky or harsh shifting between gears, hesitation when accelerating, especially at lower speeds.
    *   Solution: Perform a transmission fluid flush and filter replacement. In more severe cases, valve body replacement or mechatronic unit repair may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Front Thrust Arm Bushing Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vibrations at highway speeds, especially when braking. Clunking sound from the front suspension.
    *   Solution: Replace the front thrust arm bushings or the entire thrust arm assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Battery Drain Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Car fails to start, frequent jump starts needed, "Increased Battery Discharge" warning on the iDrive screen.
    *   Solution: Diagnose parasitic drain, replace faulty modules, or replace the battery. It may be necessary to register the new battery with the car's computer.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **iDrive System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, system rebooting, loss of functionality, navigation issues.
    *   Solution: Software update, replacement of the iDrive control unit in severe cases.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Door Handle LED Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The LED lights in the door handles stop working.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the affected door handle.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per handle
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof not opening or closing properly, getting stuck, or making excessive noise.
    *   Solution: Lubrication of sunroof tracks, repair or replacement of sunroof motor or cables.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 BMW 535i xDrive can present owners with a range of issues, from minor electrical problems to more significant engine and transmission concerns. Oil leaks and suspension component failures are common, with repairs potentially costing several hundred to over a thousand dollars. Electrical issues with the iDrive system and battery drain can also be frustrating. While the car offers a luxurious driving experience, potential buyers should be aware of these common problems and budget accordingly for maintenance and repairs.
