# 2016 BMW 535i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 535i xDrive combines luxury, all-weather confidence, and strong performance, making it an appealing option on the used market. Buyers looking at this model should familiarize themselves with potential maintenance items common to German luxury sedans of this era, particularly concerning powertrain options and electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 (N55) produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth and responsive power. This engine is generally robust compared to its predecessors, though proper maintenance history is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Steptronic

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs seamlessly with the all-wheel-drive system, offering quick shifts and refined operation. It is widely regarded as a durable unit in modern BMW lineups when fluid changes are observed.
