**2012 BMW 535i xDrive**

The 2012 BMW 535i xDrive is a luxury sedan that offers a powerful engine and a comfortable interior. It comes with a 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine that produces 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and xDrive all-wheel drive. The 535i xDrive has a starting price of $50,700.

Drivers appreciate the 535i xDrive's responsive handling, powerful engine, and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

* **N55 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six Engine**
    * **Oil Filter Housing Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the oil filter housing gasket can contaminate the serpentine belt, causing it to slip and fail. Symptoms include a burning oil smell, loss of power, and a Check Engine Light.
        * Solution: Replace the oil filter housing gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the valve cover gasket can allow oil to leak onto the exhaust manifold, causing a burning oil smell and smoke.
        * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Turbocharger failure can lead to reduced engine power, increased oil consumption, and a Check Engine Light.
        * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+

**Transmission**

* **ZF 8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Mechatronic Sleeve Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A failure in the mechatronic sleeve can cause the transmission to shift harshly or slip out of gear.
        * Solution: Replace the mechatronic sleeve.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Torque Converter Lock-Up Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: A failure in the torque converter lock-up clutch can cause the transmission to shudder or vibrate during acceleration.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Bulb Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the headlight bulbs.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight bulbs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Tail Light Bulb Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature failure of the tail light bulbs.
    * Solution: Replace the tail light bulbs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+

**Interior**

* **Idrive Display Screen Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The iDrive display screen may become unresponsive or display distorted images.
    * Solution: Replace the iDrive display screen.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Climate Control System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not regulate temperature properly or fail to turn on.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Summary**

The 2012 BMW 535i xDrive is a reliable luxury sedan with a powerful engine and comfortable interior. However, it has been known to experience certain issues, most notably engine oil leaks and transmission problems. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they should be considered when purchasing a used 535i xDrive.
