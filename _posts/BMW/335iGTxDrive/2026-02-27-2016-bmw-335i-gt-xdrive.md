# 2016 BMW 335i GT xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 335i GT xDrive combines athletic performance with expanded cargo utility and standard all-wheel drive. As with many German luxury vehicles of this vintage, prospective buyers should review owner-reported feedback carefully, as maintenance and minor electrical or mechanical issues can surface as the vehicle ages.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 (N55) produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque, offering strong, smooth acceleration. It is generally regarded as more reliable than its N54 predecessor, though proper maintenance and oil change intervals remain critical.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides rapid, seamless gear changes paired with standard xDrive all-wheel drive. It is widely considered durable and responsive when serviced appropriately.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 335i GT xDrive offers a compelling mix of performance, all-weather capability, and hatchback versatility. Buyers should prioritize finding well-documented examples with a clear service history to help mitigate potential out-of-pocket repair costs.
