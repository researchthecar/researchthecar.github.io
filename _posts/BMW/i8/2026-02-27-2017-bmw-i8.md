# 2017 BMW i8

### Overview
The 2017 BMW i8 is a groundbreaking plug-in hybrid sports car that blends performance with efficiency. Its advanced powertrain, featuring a turbocharged 1.5-liter 3-cylinder gasoline engine paired with an electric motor, delivers exhilarating acceleration and impressive fuel economy. The i8's unique construction, utilizing carbon-fiber reinforced plastic (CFRP) for its passenger cell, contributes to its lightweight design and rigid chassis. While generally lauded for its innovative technology and driving dynamics, like any complex vehicle, it can experience specific issues related to its hybrid systems and advanced electronics. This report focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance.

---


## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder (Gasoline) + Electric Motor
**Hybrid Battery Cooling System**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating warnings, reduced electric range, reduced overall performance.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty cooling fan, pump, or coolant lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of the turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

## Electrical

### Hybrid System Electronics
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, potential module reset or replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**12V Auxiliary Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical system warnings, inability to start the vehicle (even with a charged hybrid battery).
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW i8 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core mechanical components and chassis. The primary areas of concern revolve around the sophisticated hybrid system's cooling and electronics, as well as potential infotainment glitches. While the advanced technology is a hallmark of the i8, it also introduces complexity that can lead to more costly repairs if issues arise. Owners should be mindful of the hybrid battery cooling system and ensure regular software updates for the infotainment. Overall, for a vehicle of its technological advancement, the i8 offers a commendable reliability record, with most reported issues being manageable.
