# 2017 BMW i8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 BMW i8 is an exotic plug-in hybrid sports car featuring a futuristic carbon-fiber architecture and striking scissor doors. It pairs a turbocharged 1.5-liter 3-cylinder gasoline engine with an electric motor to deliver distinctive performance and efficiency. Used buyers should inspect the complex hybrid powertrain and specialized body structures thoroughly, as maintenance on exotic platforms can require specialized care.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged Inline-3 Hybrid

A 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-3 engine paired with an electric motor, producing a combined 357 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. This innovative hybrid powertrain offers brisk performance and efficient cruising, though proper maintenance of both its combustion and electrical cooling systems is essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed automatic transmission handling power exclusively for the rear wheels in this all-wheel-drive hybrid setup. It shifts smoothly and integrates well with the electric motor's power delivery, showing generally dependable operation in normal driving conditions.

### 2-Speed Automatic Transmission

A dedicated 2-speed automatic transmission managing power delivery exclusively for the front electric motor. It operates seamlessly in the background to balance efficiency and instant electric torque.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=i8&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW i8 offers head-turning exotic styling and advanced hybrid technology for used-car buyers looking to stand out. While powertrain options are generally robust, shoppers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection covering the unique hybrid electronics, carbon-fiber chassis components, and specialized drivetrain systems.
