# 2019 BMW i8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW i8 offers striking design and advanced plug-in hybrid technology, blending a turbocharged three-cylinder engine with an electric motor. As a low-volume exotic sports car, its unique drivetrain and carbon-fiber construction require specialized knowledge for maintenance. Prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history and mechanical condition prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid

This 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-three engine works in tandem with an electric motor to produce a combined output of 369 horsepower. It provides high-revving performance and strong efficiency, though its complex hybrid cooling and turbo systems require attentive maintenance.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery for the gasoline engine, while a separate 2-speed automatic manages the electric motor. This dual-transmission setup provides seamless power blending across all driving modes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V652000: Equipment:Electrical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=i8&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Capacitors within the TurboCord Portable Chargers may fail, possibly resulting in a shock hazard or a fire. An electrical shock or a fire can increase the risk of injury or death.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the charging cable, and replace it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V599000: Hybrid Propulsion System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=i8&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A shut down of high-voltage electrical power would result in a loss of propulsion, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the EME module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW i8 is a high-tech exotic sports car that demands careful inspection before buying. While complaint data is sparse for this model year, specialized hybrid repairs and maintenance can be costly. Buyers should ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified BMW i-certified technician.
