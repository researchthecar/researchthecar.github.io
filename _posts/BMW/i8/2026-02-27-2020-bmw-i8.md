# 2020 BMW i8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW i8 represents the final year of production for the brand's pioneering plug-in hybrid sports car, combining striking carbon-fiber architecture with electrified performance. As with many low-volume exotic plug-in hybrids, prospective used-car buyers should thoroughly review maintenance histories and ensure all relevant campaigns have been performed by a certified dealer.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder Plug-In Hybrid

This 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-three engine works in tandem with an electric motor to produce a combined 369 horsepower. It offers brisk acceleration and solid efficiency for an exotic sports car, though buyers should verify proper maintenance of both the fuel and high-voltage cooling systems.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery for the gasoline engine, while a separate 2-speed automatic manages the electric motor. This dual-transmission setup generally operates smoothly, though comprehensive diagnostic checks during a pre-purchase inspection are advised.

## Electrical

### High-Voltage Electrical System

Components related to the high-voltage architecture require specialized care and attention to ensure long-term reliability.

**High-Voltage Electrical Inspection and Service**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may receive recall notices regarding high-voltage electrical components requiring dealer inspection or service.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized BMW dealer inspect the high-voltage system and perform any necessary campaign repairs.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V601000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=i8&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The debris could result in a short-circuit, increasing the risk of fire or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will inspect and, if necessary, replace the battery module free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW i8 is an exotic, technologically advanced plug-in hybrid that demands careful vetting by any potential used-car buyer. While standard complaint volume is low, verifying complete dealer service records and confirming that all specialized electrical recalls have been addressed is essential before purchase.
