# 2016 BMW i8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW i8 blends futuristic design with plug-in hybrid performance, offering an exotic look and sports-car handling. While generally well-engineered, buyers should thoroughly inspect the high-voltage electrical architecture and overall maintenance history before purchasing a pre-owned model.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder Hybrid

This 1.5-liter turbocharged inline-3 engine works in tandem with an electric motor to produce a combined 357 horsepower and 420 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, though prospective buyers should have the complex hybrid powertrain inspected by a specialist.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The rear wheels are driven by a conventional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with the gasoline engine. It shifts smoothly and integrates well with the hybrid driving modes, though regular fluid maintenance is recommended for longevity.

### 2-Speed Automatic Transmission

A dedicated 2-speed automatic transmission handles power delivery for the front electric motor. It operates quietly in the background and requires minimal maintenance, though complex electronic controls govern its synchronization with the rear drivetrain.

## Suspension

### Tires and Traction

The suspension and wheel setup relies on specialized high-performance tires to handle the unique weight distribution of a plug-in hybrid sports car. Proper tire maintenance and replacement are crucial for safe handling.

**Rapid Tread Wear and Reduced Wet Traction**
- **Symptoms:** Performance tires may wear out quickly and exhibit reduced grip or slippage on wet roadways.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire tread depth regularly and replace high-performance summer tires as soon as wear patterns become uneven or compromised.

## Electrical

### High-Voltage Battery Temperature Sensors

The electrical system manages a sophisticated high-voltage battery and plug-in hybrid architecture. Internal sensor failures can occasionally trigger warning lights and safety shutdowns.

**NTC Temperature Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights may appear while driving, potentially followed by a loss of power or a high-voltage battery shutdown.
- **Solution:** Have the high-voltage battery assembly and its internal cell module temperature sensors diagnosed and replaced by a certified technician.

The 2016 BMW i8 offers an exciting driving experience and exotic styling, but its advanced hybrid technology requires careful scrutiny. Potential buyers should prioritize a pre-purchase inspection focusing on the high-voltage electrical components and battery health to avoid unexpected repair costs.
