# 2021 BMW M2 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW M2 is a high-performance sports coupe celebrated for its engaging driving dynamics and potent turbocharged engines. As a modern performance car, it delivers thrilling track-ready capabilities alongside everyday usability. Prospective buyers looking at a used model should note that maintenance costs for German luxury performance vehicles can run higher than average, making a thorough pre-purchase inspection essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (S55)

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-6 (S55) produces approximately 405 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque, powering the M2 Competition. It is a robust performance engine built for high-output demands, though proper cooling and timely maintenance are critical for longevity.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter single-turbocharged inline-6 (N55) delivers about 365 horsepower and 343 lb-ft of torque, found in the standard M2 variants. It offers responsive power delivery and generally solid reliability when serviced according to factory schedules.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission provides an engaging, hands-on driving experience for purists. It is generally durable, though clutch wear heavily depends on previous driver habits.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery. Routine fluid services are important to ensure long-term mechanical health.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V554000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M2&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During a crash, a seat belt retractor that does not function as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the seat belt retractors (including the seat belts) and, if necessary, replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V666000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M2&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A damaged connection between the fuel line and the fuel rail could lead to a fuel leak and, in the presence of an ignition source, increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fuel injectors and if a damping component is missing, the injectors and the fuel rail will be replaced, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M2 remains a compelling choice for driving enthusiasts seeking sharp handling and exhilarating performance in a compact package. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, buyers should prioritize finding an example with a documented service history. Budgeting for premium maintenance and upkeep will help ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
