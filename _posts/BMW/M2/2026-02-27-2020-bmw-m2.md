# 2020 BMW M2 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M2 delivers thrilling performance and sharp handling, making it a favorite for driving enthusiasts looking for a compact luxury sports coupe. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should review service histories carefully to ensure proper maintenance. While overall owner satisfaction is high, checking specific mechanical and electrical systems during a pre-purchase inspection can help avoid unexpected surprises.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbo Inline-6 (S55)

This high-performance 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-6 produces around 405 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. It offers robust power delivery for spirited driving, though regular maintenance and cooling system checks are essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and direct power delivery. Buyers should verify smooth operation during low-speed maneuvering and parking.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission appeals to purists seeking an engaging, hands-on driving experience. Clutch condition and shift crispness should be inspected prior to purchase.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M2&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V666000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M2&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A damaged connection between the fuel line and the fuel rail could lead to a fuel leak and, in the presence of an ignition source, increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fuel injectors and if a damping component is missing, the injectors and the fuel rail will be replaced, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M2 stands out as a rewarding and capable sports coupe for daily driving or weekend track use. Prioritizing a thorough pre-purchase inspection and confirming a consistent maintenance history will provide the best foundation for a confident used car purchase.
