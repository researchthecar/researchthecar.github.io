**Introduction**

The 2021 BMW M2 is a performance-oriented coupe powered by a twin-turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine. It is available with a six-speed manual transmission or a seven-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The engine produces 405 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque.

Owners appreciate the M2's excellent handling, powerful engine, and engaging driving experience. However, some have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-Six**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, check engine light, blue smoke from the exhaust.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the oil pan gasket or valve cover gasket.
    * Solution: Replace the gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Grinding Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noise.
    * Solution: Replace the synchronizers or gearset.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle battery drains prematurely.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or identify and fix the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Clearcoat Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clearcoat layer on the paint peels or flakes off.
    * Solution: Repaint the affected area.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower motor stops working or makes excessive noise.
    * Solution: Replace the blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard cracks or becomes distorted.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 BMW M2 is a high-performance vehicle that has some potential issues. The engine and transmission have experienced some problems, but these are not particularly severe or expensive to fix. The suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components have also had some reported issues, but these are relatively minor. Overall, the M2's issues are not a major concern and most owners should be happy with the car's performance and reliability.
