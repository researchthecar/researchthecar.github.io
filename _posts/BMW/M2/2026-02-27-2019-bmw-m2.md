# 2019 BMW M2 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW M2 delivers thrilling performance and sharp handling, making it a favorite among driving enthusiasts. As a high-performance luxury sports coupe, prospective buyers should review its mechanical and electronic systems carefully to ensure a well-maintained driving experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (S55)

The S55 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-6 produces approximately 405 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque, offering relentless power delivery. It is a robust performance engine, though diligent maintenance and oil change intervals are crucial for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes suited for both track and street driving. It offers smooth operation when properly maintained, though buyers should verify proper clutch engagement during test drives.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission caters to purists seeking direct mechanical engagement. It features rev-matching technology and a satisfying shift action, making it a sought-after option for enthusiasts.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M2&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW M2 is a potent and engaging sports coupe that rewards diligent upkeep. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on powertrain health and service history to enjoy its performance safely.
