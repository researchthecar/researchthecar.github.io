# 2016 BMW M2

### Overview
The 2016 BMW M2, a performance-oriented compact coupe, is generally regarded as a robust and reliable vehicle, especially considering its high-performance nature. Built on the F22 2 Series platform, it inherits a solid mechanical foundation. The N55 engine, a well-established turbocharged inline-six, has proven its mettle, though like any performance engine, it demands diligent maintenance. Transmission options, primarily the manual and the M-DCT dual-clutch automatic, are also known for their durability when properly cared for. Owners typically report satisfaction with the M2's build quality and the absence of widespread, recurring mechanical failures. However, as with any enthusiast vehicle, specific components might require attention over time, particularly those subjected to hard driving.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW M2 demonstrates strong reliability, particularly for a vehicle in its performance class. The core mechanical components, including the potent N55 engine and the available manual and M-DCT transmissions, are generally dependable. Owners rarely encounter significant, systemic failures. Routine maintenance is crucial for preserving its performance and longevity. While no model is entirely immune to issues, the M2 stands out for its robust engineering and solid build quality, making it a reliable choice for driving enthusiasts who adhere to recommended service schedules.
