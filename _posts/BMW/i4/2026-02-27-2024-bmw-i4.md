# 2024 BMW i4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW i4 is an all-electric luxury gran coupé that blends traditional BMW driving dynamics with modern electric propulsion. Available in single-motor rear-wheel-drive and dual-motor all-wheel-drive configurations, it offers strong acceleration and impressive cabin tech. As a relatively new EV offering from the brand, early-production data indicates a clean slate regarding major mechanical and electrical failures, though prospective buyers should always test all infotainment and charging functionalities prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### eDrive35 Single Motor Electric Drive

This entry-level electric motor setup powers the rear wheels with approximately 281 horsepower. It offers smooth, linear power delivery and robust everyday efficiency for daily commuting.

### eDrive40 Single Motor Electric Drive

This mid-tier rear-wheel-drive electric motor delivers around 335 horsepower with brisk acceleration. It is widely praised for its balance of strong range and satisfying power output.

### M50 Dual Motor Electric Drive

This high-performance dual-motor setup provides all-wheel drive and generates up to 536 horsepower. It delivers explosive acceleration and athletic handling backed by BMW M tuning.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct-Drive Transmission

A single-speed fixed gear transmission routes power seamlessly to the wheels across all i4 variants. It requires minimal maintenance and ensures instant torque delivery without traditional shift hesitation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V135000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=i4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An overheated battery module increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-voltage battery module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V611000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=i4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Reduced structural integrity may result in damage to the high voltage battery during a crash, increasing the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the left-side longitudinal beam as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V395000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Motor:Controller:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=i4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW i4 stands out as a highly capable and refined luxury electric vehicle with minimal reported owner complaints. Buyers can approach the market with confidence, keeping standard used-EV checks in mind such as battery health diagnostics and software verification.
