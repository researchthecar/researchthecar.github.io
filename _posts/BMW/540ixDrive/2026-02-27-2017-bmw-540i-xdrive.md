# 2017 BMW 540i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 540i xDrive, buyers get a sophisticated executive sedan known for strong performance and luxury. However, reviewing owner feedback highlights several recurring areas that require close inspection. Potential shoppers should pay attention to electrical glitches, sensor warnings, and cooling system reports.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine (B58) produces approximately 335 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth power delivery and strong overall reliability compared to older BMW powerplants. Cooling system components should still be monitored closely for leaks as the vehicle ages.

**Coolant Hose Deterioration and Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant hoses may degrade from internal pressure, leading to fluid leaks and low coolant warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspect coolant lines regularly for signs of wear or leakage, and replace compromised hoses.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with xDrive

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the intelligent xDrive all-wheel-drive system to deliver quick, smooth shifts and confident traction in various weather conditions. It generally has a solid reputation for durability when routine maintenance is performed.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Steering response is precise and comfortable, though power adjustment mechanisms can experience internal sensor failures.

**Steering Wheel Adjustment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The power-adjustable steering wheel fails to return to its set position or adjust properly.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty steering column hall effect sensor integrated into the steering column assembly.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster

The digital cockpit provides clear readouts but can occasionally exhibit software or display glitches.

**Instrument Cluster Display Shaking or Blanking Out**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster may shake, flicker, or go completely black while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electrical connections and cluster software diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Headlights and Daytime Running Lights

Electrical system concerns center mostly on lighting modules and instrument cluster display behavior.

**Daytime Running Light Dimming or Melting**
- **Symptoms:** Daytime running lights may appear dim, discolored, or fail entirely due to melted connections between the headlight housing and LED modules.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies for discoloration or melting; replacement of the housing and LED module may be necessary.

## Safety

### Airbags and Passenger Restraints

Safety systems generally protect occupants well, though sensor faults within passenger seat mats are relatively common owner complaints.

**Passenger Restraint and Airbag Warning Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning lights and passenger seatbelt or occupant mat sensor errors can appear intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the restraint control module and replace faulty seat mat pads or wiring harness connections as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V243000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Prior exposure to excessive levels of humidity within the driver and passenger head air bag inflators may affect the proper deployment of the head air bags. In the event of a crash of sufficient severity, deployment of the head air bag may be impaired and/or the igniter could separate from the inflator, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the left or right head air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 540i xDrive combines luxury, impressive performance, and all-weather capability, but buyers should approach with a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to cooling system hoses, headlight assembly connections, and interior electronic warnings to avoid unexpected out-of-pocket expenses.
