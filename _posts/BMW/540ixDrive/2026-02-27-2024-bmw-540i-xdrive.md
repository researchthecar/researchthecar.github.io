# 2024 BMW 540i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 BMW 540i xDrive, prospective buyers will find a refined luxury sedan equipped with modern technology and robust performance. While generally praised for its sophisticated driving dynamics and feature-rich cabin, early-production models have accumulated a small number of owner reports highlighting potential hydraulic brake concerns and driver-assistance sensor behavior. As with any late-model luxury vehicle, reviewing service histories and ensuring all campaign remedies have been applied is essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine generates approximately 375 horsepower and 398 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. This powerplant is widely regarded as smooth, powerful, and mechanically robust in modern BMW applications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes and power distribution to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is a proven unit known for quick response times and long-term durability when properly maintained.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Owner reports highlight occasional warning messages and safety campaign notices related to the hydraulic brake and electronic stability control systems.

**Brake System Malfunction and Recall Involvement**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported warning messages regarding brake fluid or potential electronic stability control and brake system failures, sometimes intersecting with manufacturer recall notices.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized service center inspect the brake hydraulic system and ensure all applicable manufacturer safety recall remedies have been properly executed.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

The safety category encompasses driver-assistance features, with isolated owner complaints regarding rear collision mitigation responsiveness.

**Rear Automatic Braking Inoperation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted instances where the rear automatic braking or collision avoidance systems failed to engage when backing out of parking spaces.
- **Solution:** Inspect backup sensors and camera alignment; consult a dealer for potential software updates governing driver assistance features.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V714000: Steering:Linkages:Knuckle:Spindle:Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A sudden increase in required steering effort increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering spindle's double universal joint, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V422000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Electric:Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 540i xDrive offers a compelling blend of luxury, technology, and performance. While early owner feedback points to minor concerns regarding brake system warnings and driver-assistance sensor behavior, these are largely addressable through standard dealer service campaigns. Buyers should verify that all recall work has been completed prior to purchase.
