**Introduction:**

The 2021 BMW 540i xDrive is a luxurious and sporty sedan that delivers a potent driving experience. Powered by a robust 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine, the 540i xDrive generates 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It's paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and xDrive all-wheel drive, ensuring exceptional performance in all conditions. The interior boasts a blend of comfort and technology, while the exterior exudes a sleek and refined aesthetic.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six**

**Valve Stem Seals Leaking**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust during acceleration
Solution: Replace valve stem seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension**

**Control Arm Bushings Degraded**
Description/Symptoms: Noises from the front suspension, vibrations while driving
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Rear Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Wiper Motor Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: Wipers not operating correctly, intermittent operation
Solution: Replace wiper motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Rearview Camera Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Rearview camera not displaying an image or distorted image
Solution: Replace rearview camera
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Sunroof Rattling**
Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the sunroof while driving
Solution: Inspect and lubricate sunroof tracks, adjust or replace sunroof components as necessary
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Dashboard Creaks**
Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling sounds from the dashboard
Solution: Remove and refit interior trim panels, apply soundproofing materials
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2021 BMW 540i xDrive is a reliable and well-engineered vehicle. However, some minor issues have been reported, such as valve stem seal leaks in the engine, control arm bushing degradation in the front suspension, and electrical glitches with the wiper motor and rearview camera. The severity of these issues is generally moderate, and most can be resolved at a reasonable cost.
