**Electrical**

**Battery**

**Premature Battery Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery capacity decreases rapidly, affecting driving range.
Solution: Battery replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $15,000 - $20,000+
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 19V821

**12-Volt Battery**

**Rapid Discharge:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, causing problems starting the vehicle or using electronics.
Solution: Battery replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Window Seals**

**Water Leakage:**
Description/Symptoms: Water enters the cabin from around the window seals, causing leaks.
Solution: Replacement of damaged seals
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard**

**Plastic Trim Peeling:**
Description/Symptoms: Plastic trim on the dashboard starts to peel and separate.
Solution: Replacement of affected trim pieces
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 BMW i3 is generally a well-built vehicle, with only minor electrical and interior issues reported. The most significant issue is premature battery failure, which can be costly to fix. Overall, the severity of the issues is moderate, and the cost of repairs is generally reasonable.
