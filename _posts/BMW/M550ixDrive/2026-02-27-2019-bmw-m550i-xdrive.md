# 2019 BMW M550i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW M550i xDrive offers high-performance luxury powered by a robust twin-turbo V8 engine paired with a smooth automatic transmission. While it delivers thrilling dynamics and executive comfort, prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and inspect complex mechanical and electrical systems before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 456 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. It delivers tremendous acceleration and passing power, though buyers should watch for potential cooling system wear and oil leaks typical of high-output German performance engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and distributes power seamlessly through the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust and well-matched to the V8's powerband, though regular fluid changes are recommended to ensure long-term durability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M550i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW M550i xDrive is an exceptionally fast and well-appointed luxury sedan that rewards diligent maintenance. Buyers should prioritize examples with comprehensive service records and budget for specialized upkeep to keep this high-performance machine running reliably.
