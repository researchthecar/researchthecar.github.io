**Introduction:**

The 2019 BMW M550i xDrive is a high-performance sedan powered by a 4.4-liter turbocharged V8 engine. It delivers 456 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. Praised for its potent engine, responsive handling, luxurious interior, and high-tech features, the M550i xDrive has received mixed feedback on its firm ride quality and limited rear legroom.

**4.4-Liter Turbocharged V8**

**Summary:**

The 2019 BMW M550i xDrive is a well-rounded performance car with a few notable issues. The most severe concerns include timing chain tensioner failure and transmission fluid leaks, which can be costly to repair. Electrical issues such as headlight malfunction and rearview camera overload can also be frustrating. While the interior is generally comfortable, the wiper motor and radio can experience glitches. Overall, the M550i xDrive offers a high level of performance and luxury, but it is not immune to potential reliability concerns.
