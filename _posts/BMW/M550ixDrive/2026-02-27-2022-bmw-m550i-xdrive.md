# 2022 BMW M550i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW M550i xDrive offers a compelling blend of high-performance luxury and everyday drivability, driven by a potent V8 engine. Prospective used-car buyers should carefully inspect this high-performance sport sedan during a pre-purchase inspection, keeping an eye out for any isolated owner-reported anomalies.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Equipped with a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, paired with standard xDrive all-wheel drive. This powertrain delivers blistering acceleration and robust overall performance, though ongoing maintenance history is vital for high-output BMW engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Features an 8-speed sport automatic transmission with paddle shifters that delivers crisp, rapid gear changes. This transmission is widely regarded as smooth and durable under normal driving conditions.

## Suspension

### Wheels

Factory alloy wheels and suspension components generally provide sharp handling, though low-profile tires and large wheels can occasionally leave rims susceptible to road stress.

**Cracked Wheels**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle owners have reported experiencing cracked wheels under normal driving conditions without any impact with curbs, debris, or significant road hazards.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for structural cracks during a pre-purchase examination and replace any damaged wheels as necessary.

The 2022 BMW M550i xDrive stands out as a powerful and luxurious option for enthusiasts seeking executive sedan performance.
