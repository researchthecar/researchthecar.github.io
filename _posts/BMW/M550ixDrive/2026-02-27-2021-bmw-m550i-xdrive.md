# 2021 BMW M550i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 BMW M550i xDrive, buyers can generally expect a high-performance luxury sedan backed by robust engineering. While owner reports are minimal, inspecting critical wear items like powertrain mounts or flexible couplings remains a wise step during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing rapid acceleration and effortless passing power. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should verify regular oil service history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission pairs with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system to deliver quick, smooth shifts and responsive performance. It proves highly durable in daily driving conditions.

**Cracked Guibo (Flex Disc)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible cracks on the powertrain flexible disc can develop, which may risk disrupting power transmission to the wheels and affecting vehicle control.
- **Solution:** Inspect the guibo during routine service and replace the flexible disc immediately if any structural cracking is observed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V598000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M550i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the reduced rearview image view can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, program the rearview camera with updated software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M550i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M550i xDrive offers a compelling blend of luxury and V8 performance with very few widespread complaints reported by owners. Prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the driveshaft couplings and general mechanical integrity.
