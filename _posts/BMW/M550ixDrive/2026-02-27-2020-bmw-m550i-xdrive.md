# 2020 BMW M550i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M550i xDrive offers a potent blend of luxury and high-performance twin-turbo V8 power. As with many sophisticated modern performance vehicles, used car buyers should inspect the drivetrain and electronic systems carefully to ensure proper operation.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (N63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, offering breathtaking acceleration. It requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability under spirited driving.

**Auto Stop-Start Abruptness**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience an abrupt or harsh stop when the automatic fuel-saving start-stop system activates as the car slows down.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the engine management software and vehicle computer modules for any available updates or calibration adjustments.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive provides lightning-fast and smooth gear changes. This transmission is generally robust, though regular fluid changes are recommended for longevity.

### xDrive All-Wheel Drive and Differential

The all-wheel drive system distributes power efficiently across all four wheels, though high-torque loads can place stress on mechanical differential components.

**Differential and Drivetrain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice unexpected mechanical failure or unusual noises originating from the front differential or rear drivetrain components.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front and rear differential units, drive axles, and fluid conditions for any signs of premature wear or leakage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V598000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M550i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the reduced rearview image view can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, program the rearview camera with updated software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2020 BMW M550i xDrive is an exhilarating performance sedan that rewards attentive ownership. Prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the drivetrain, differential components, and powertrain electronics.
