**Introduction**

The 2022 BMW 220i Gran Coupe is a compact luxury sedan that offers a blend of style, performance, and comfort. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine that produces 189 horsepower and 199 lb-ft of torque. Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, the 220i Gran Coupe delivers a smooth and responsive driving experience. The vehicle also features a spacious and well-equipped interior, making it a comfortable choice for daily commuting or long road trips. However, the 220i Gran Coupe does have some potential issues that buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4**

  * **Leaking oil pan gasket:** The oil pan gasket may develop a leak, leading to oil leaking out of the engine. This can cause engine damage if not fixed promptly.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

  * **Shifting issues:** The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifting, especially during downshifts.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Electrical system malfunctions:** The vehicle's electrical system may experience malfunctions, including issues with the radio, navigation, or climate control.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the affected electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Paint defects:** The vehicle's paint may develop swirls or scratches easily.
    * Solution: Touch up or repaint the affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of the damage
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door lock malfunction:** The door locks may fail to engage or disengage properly.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 BMW 220i Gran Coupe is a solid choice for those seeking a compact luxury sedan with a sporty driving experience. However, it's important to be aware of the potential issues mentioned above. The severity and cost of the issues vary, but they should be considered when making a purchasing decision.
