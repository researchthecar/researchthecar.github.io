# 2022 BMW 220i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2022 BMW 220i Gran Coupe, part of the F44 generation, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting BMW's commitment to quality engineering. This model, often equipped with the B48 engine and a ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, is designed for a balance of performance and efficiency. While specific widespread mechanical failures are uncommon for this model year, like any vehicle, individual experiences can vary. Owners generally report satisfaction with the powertrain and core systems. Attention to routine maintenance is key to ensuring long-term dependability. The Gran Coupe's design integrates modern technology, which, while generally robust, can occasionally present minor electronic quirks, though these are not typically systemic.
