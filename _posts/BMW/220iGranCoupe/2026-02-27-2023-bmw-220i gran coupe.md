# 2023 BMW 220i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2023 BMW 220i Gran Coupe, part of the F44 generation, generally presents a strong reliability profile, building on BMW's reputation for engineering quality. This model primarily utilizes a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission. While specific widespread issues are not heavily documented for this relatively new model year, like any vehicle, individual components can experience problems. Owners should be aware of potential, though not common, issues that can arise with modern turbocharged engines and complex electronic systems. Overall, the 220i Gran Coupe is considered a dependable choice within its segment, with most reported concerns being isolated incidents rather than systemic flaws. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring long-term reliability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 BMW 220i Gran Coupe demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues.
