# 2013 BMW 740Li xDrive

### Overview
The 2013 BMW 740Li xDrive, a full-size luxury sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its advanced technology and performance-oriented nature.  Owners often praise its comfortable ride, powerful engine options, and sophisticated interior.  However, like any complex luxury car, it is not immune to potential issues.  This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into specific components that may require attention.  The xDrive all-wheel-drive system adds to its capability but also introduces potential complexity.  Overall, the 740Li xDrive offers a compelling blend of luxury and performance, with a reliability record that, while not flawless, is competitive within its segment.

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** PCV valve replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Water pump replacement.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 BMW 740Li xDrive is a luxurious and powerful sedan with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its advanced technology, particularly the engine's turbocharger and cooling system components, and the sophisticated electrical systems like the iDrive and potential battery drain issues. The adaptive suspension can also be a source of costly repairs if it malfunctions.  However, many owners report satisfaction with the vehicle's overall dependability when routine maintenance is performed diligently.  Prospective buyers should be aware of the potential for higher repair costs associated with these luxury components.
