# 2015 BMW Alpina B7L xDrive

### Overview
The 2015 BMW Alpina B7L xDrive represents a pinnacle of performance and luxury, combining Alpina's bespoke tuning with BMW's engineering prowess. As a high-performance variant of the 7 Series, it features a potent V8 engine and sophisticated xDrive all-wheel-drive system. Reliability for such a specialized vehicle is generally strong, benefiting from BMW's robust platform and Alpina's meticulous enhancements. However, due to its complexity and performance focus, certain components may require more specialized attention and potentially higher maintenance costs compared to standard luxury sedans. Owners can expect a rewarding driving experience, but proactive maintenance and awareness of potential high-performance-related wear are advisable.

### Adaptive M Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, warning lights on the dashboard, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 BMW Alpina B7L xDrive is a highly capable and generally reliable luxury performance sedan. The powertrain, including the 4.4L twin-turbo V8 and 8-speed automatic transmission, is robust. Potential areas for attention are primarily related to the advanced suspension system, specifically air spring leaks, and occasional electrical gremlins within the complex iDrive infotainment system or battery drain issues. These are not uncommon for vehicles with such advanced technology and performance tuning. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are key to ensuring long-term dependability and enjoying the unique driving experience this Alpina offers.
