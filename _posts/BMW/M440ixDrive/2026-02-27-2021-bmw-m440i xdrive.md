### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW M440i xDrive is a highly reliable performance coupe. The powertrain, including the 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission, is exceptionally robust with no widespread reported issues. Similarly, the suspension and exterior components are generally trouble-free. The primary areas to be aware of are potential minor electrical glitches, such as infotainment system software bugs or occasional 12V battery drain, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with extensive electronic features. These are typically manageable with software updates or battery replacement. Overall, the M440i xDrive offers a dependable and exhilarating driving experience with a strong reliability record.
