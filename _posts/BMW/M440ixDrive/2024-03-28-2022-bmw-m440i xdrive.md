**Introduction**

The 2022 BMW M440i xDrive is a high-performance sport sedan that bridges the gap between the standard 4 Series and the full-blown M4. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine producing 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The M440i is lauded for its potent engine, sharp handling, and luxurious interior, but criticisms include its stiff ride and higher price tag compared to competitors.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Reports indicate higher-than-usual oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and potentially engine damage.
    * Solution: Inspect and replace piston rings as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitation:** Some drivers experience delayed or hesitant shifting, particularly during acceleration.
    * Solution: Reprogramming the transmission control unit (TCU).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Occasional reports of the infotainment system freezing, crashing, or displaying errors.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:** Isolated incidents of door handles becoming loose or failing to open properly.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty door handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls:
    * NHTSA Reference Number: 22E012: Insufficient Door Hinge Strength

**Interior**

* **Seat Lumbar Support Failure:** Malfunctioning lumbar support on driver seat, causing back discomfort.
    * Solution: Replacing the seat lumbar support motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Summary**

The 2022 BMW M440i xDrive is a capable performer, but some issues have been reported. While the engine and transmission are largely reliable, the infotainment system and exterior have faced occasional problems. The severity of the issues varies, and repair costs can be significant in some cases. Owners should be aware of these potential issues and consider the associated costs before making a purchase decision.
