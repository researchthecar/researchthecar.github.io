# 2022 BMW M440i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW M440i xDrive blends high performance with everyday luxury, driven by its robust turbocharged inline-six engine and intelligent all-wheel-drive system. Prospective used-car buyers generally find this model to offer an engaging driving experience paired with modern technology. While official owner-reported complaint data is sparse for this model year, maintaining a high-performance luxury vehicle requires careful pre-purchase inspection and diligence regarding routine servicing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded by enthusiasts and technicians as one of BMW's most reliable and refined modern powertrains.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission comes paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, delivering quick gear changes and dependable long-term operation under spirited driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V267000: Visibility:Sun/Moon Roof Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-Operated Window Systems." A sunroof that closes without the key fob present inside the vehicle can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the roof function control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
