# 2023 BMW M440i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M440i xDrive blends high performance with everyday usability, featuring a robust turbocharged inline-six engine and standard all-wheel drive. As a relatively new luxury performance vehicle on the used market, it generally shows strong initial quality with very few widespread owner complaints logged. Prospective buyers should still verify maintenance histories and perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to ensure all complex electronics and drivetrain components are functioning as expected.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Known in the industry as the B58, this powerplant has earned a strong reputation for impressive performance and overall reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed sport automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup delivers crisp, rapid shifts. It is widely regarded as one of the smoothest and most dependable automatic transmissions in its class.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M440i xDrive is a compelling choice for used luxury car buyers seeking a blend of sports car performance and modern daily drivability. With a lack of recurring owner complaints or major mechanical red flags, it stands out as a solid used prospect. Buyers are advised to prioritize clean vehicle history reports and professional inspections to secure a trouble-free ownership experience.
