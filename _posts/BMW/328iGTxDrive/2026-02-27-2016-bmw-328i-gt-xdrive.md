# 2016 BMW 328i GT xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 328i GT xDrive combines hatchback utility, standard all-wheel drive, and luxury features into a distinct grand touring package. Prospective buyers evaluating used models should review standard maintenance records and powertrain histories to ensure long-term dependability.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, though buyers should verify regular oil change maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Steptronic

This 8-speed automatic transmission comes paired with standard xDrive all-wheel drive. It is widely praised for smooth, prompt shifts and strong long-term mechanical reliability.

The 2016 BMW 328i GT xDrive offers versatile cargo space and confident all-weather performance. While major powertrain concerns are minimal in owner reports, careful pre-purchase inspections and detailed service histories remain essential for a confident used luxury purchase.
