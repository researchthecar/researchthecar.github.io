# 2016 BMW 328i GT xDrive

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 328i GT xDrive, part of the F34 generation, is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly known for its blend of practicality and sporty driving dynamics. It typically features a potent turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an efficient automatic transmission and the brand's signature xDrive all-wheel-drive system. While most owners report a positive experience, like any complex German luxury vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from repair shops. Understanding these potential issues can help owners with proactive maintenance and budgeting for repairs, ensuring the longevity and enjoyment of their 328i GT xDrive.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20/B48)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, increased oil consumption, whistling noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Water Pump Leaks/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating, low coolant level warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat assembly.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,200

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 328i GT xDrive offers a solid reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern revolve around the engine, specifically potential issues with the timing chain, PCV valve, and water pump, which can be costly to repair if they fail. However, these are not universal problems and often occur after significant mileage. The transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components are generally robust and do not present widespread reliability concerns. Regular maintenance and addressing early warning signs, particularly for the engine, are key to ensuring a dependable ownership experience with this versatile BMW model.
