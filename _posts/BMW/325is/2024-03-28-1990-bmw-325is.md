**Introduction**

The 1990 BMW 325is is a high-performance variant of the E30 3 Series. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 2.5-liter inline-6 engine that produces 170 horsepower and 160 lb-ft of torque. The 325is was available with a 5-speed manual transmission or a 4-speed automatic transmission. Its well-balanced handling, responsive steering, and comfortable interior are praised, but it is criticized for its lack of modern amenities and safety features.

**Engine**

**2.5-Liter Inline-6**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, combustion gases in the cooling system
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blue smoke from exhaust, oil consumption
    * Solution: Replace valve seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Transmission Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration felt when shifting or accelerating, noisy transmission
    * Solution: Replace transmission mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, car not moving properly
    * Solution: Replace clutch packs or torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings Worn:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing noises from suspension, imprecise steering
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Strut/Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, Poor handling, Leaking fluid
    * Solution: Replace struts or shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, loss of electrical power
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Weak battery, car not starting
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Plastic Trim Fade:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wearing and fading of plastic trim around windows and bumpers.
    * Solution: Replace trim or use exterior trim restorer.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: No airflow from vents, excessive noise
    * Solution: Replace blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Heater Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heater not warming up
    * Solution: Replace seat heater element
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1990 BMW 325is is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, but it does have some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The most common issues involve the engine, transmission, and suspension, with some electrical and interior problems also being reported. The severity and cost of the issues vary, so it is important to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic before purchasing.
