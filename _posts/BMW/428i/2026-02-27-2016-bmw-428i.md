# 2016 BMW 428i

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 428i, part of the F32 generation, is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly praised for its engaging driving dynamics and premium interior. It typically features a potent 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with either a manual or automatic transmission. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from a reliability standpoint. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. The focus is on specific components and their reported problems, offering estimated repair costs and solutions where applicable.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20/B48)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gasket, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected gasket(s).
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential whistling noise, engine misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, warning lights on the dashboard, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of battery, alternator, or parasitic drain source.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 428i offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury, with a generally solid reliability record. The most commonly cited issues revolve around the engine, specifically the timing chain, PCV valve, and potential oil leaks, which are not uncommon for turbocharged engines of this era. Electrical gremlins, such as battery drain and infotainment glitches, can also occur. However, the transmission and suspension systems are typically robust. With proactive maintenance and addressing potential issues like timing chain wear early, the 428i can provide a rewarding ownership experience. Owners should be aware of these potential points of concern and budget accordingly for any necessary repairs.
