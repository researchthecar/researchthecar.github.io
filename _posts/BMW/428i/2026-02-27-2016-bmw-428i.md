# 2016 BMW 428i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 428i blends athletic handling with sophisticated styling, appealing strongly to enthusiasts looking for a dynamic daily driver. As a used vehicle, it requires careful inspection of its powertrain and auxiliary systems to ensure long-term reliability. While offering engaging performance, prospective buyers should review maintenance records and budget for routine European car upkeep.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque. It delivers lively acceleration and respectable efficiency, though early production versions require monitoring of the timing chain assembly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and pairs well with the engine's power band. It is generally robust when fluid maintenance intervals are respected.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission offers a traditional, engaging shifting experience for enthusiast drivers. It is a rarer find in this model lineup and typically holds up well with normal clutch maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V608000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=428i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** An improperly sealed electrical connector on the water pump may be exposed to water and short circuit. An electrical short increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the water pump and plug connector as necessary, and install a protective shield, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 428i is a rewarding luxury sport coupe and convertible option for used car buyers who prioritize driving dynamics. Prioritize finding a well-documented example with a thorough mechanical pre-purchase inspection to minimize the risk of unexpected repair costs.
