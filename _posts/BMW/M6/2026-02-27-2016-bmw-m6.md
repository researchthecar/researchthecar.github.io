# 2016 BMW M6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW M6 is a high-performance luxury grand tourer offering exhilarating power and striking styling. While it delivers exceptional driving dynamics, prospective buyers should approach used models with care, keeping in mind that high-performance German luxury vehicles require meticulous maintenance and can be expensive to repair.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (S63)

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces around 560 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. Known for complex plumbing and high thermal loads, it requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission offers rapid, race-inspired gear changes suited for high-performance driving. Routine fluid changes are essential to maintain smooth operation over high mileage.

## Electrical

### Headliner and Roof Electrical

Electrical architecture controls comfort features, lighting, and advanced cabin electronics throughout the vehicle.

**Headliner Electrical Malfunction and Smoke**
- **Symptoms:** Smoke may suddenly appear near the headliner or windshield area, potentially leading to rapid electrical fire hazards.
- **Solution:** Immediately inspect vehicle wiring harnesses, fuses, and roof-mounted electronic modules for shorts or damage.

## Safety

### Airbags and Restraint Systems

Safety components include advanced airbag deployment and restraint systems designed to protect occupants in a collision.

**Passenger Restraint and Airbag Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience warning messages indicating a passenger restraint or airbag system failure, sometimes accompanied by a loss of power to the passenger side.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle's Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) modules and wiring harnesses to diagnose and replace faulty sensors or seat mat components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V094000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M6&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The third brake light may become loose, possibly allowing the light to detach from the vehicle. If the third brake light detaches from the vehicle, it would no longer provide the required lighting to following traffic, or it may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the third brake light and properly secure it with new fasteners. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW M6 is a thrilling grand tourer, but owner reports highlight potential concerns regarding electrical safety and airbag restraint warnings. Buyers should secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from a specialist familiar with high-performance BMW platforms before finalizing a purchase.
