# 2018 BMW M6 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2018 BMW M6, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury grand tourer with substantial power and advanced technology. While overall ownership reports for this model year are limited, prospective buyers should inspect advanced electronic and suspension systems carefully, as repairs on high-performance BMW components can be expensive.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 2018 BMW M6 features a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing around 600 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. This high-output powerplant delivers thrilling acceleration, though routine maintenance is critical for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Power is routed through a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission with paddle shifters. It provides rapid gear changes suited to the vehicle's sporting character, though prospective buyers should verify smooth operation during test drives.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Suspension & VDM Module

The adaptive suspension relies on complex electronic controls to manage ride quality and handling performance.

**Virtual Dynamics Management Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a sudden loss of suspension balance, incorrect ride height, and bouncy or hazardous driving dynamics.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the electronic control modules and replace the failed Virtual Dynamics Management module.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V094000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M6&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The third brake light may become loose, possibly allowing the light to detach from the vehicle. If the third brake light detaches from the vehicle, it would no longer provide the required lighting to following traffic, or it may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the third brake light and properly secure it with new fasteners. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 BMW M6 remains a potent and luxurious grand tourer, but its complex engineering demands thorough pre-purchase inspections. Buyers should prioritize maintenance history and electronic system diagnostics to avoid unexpected repair costs.
