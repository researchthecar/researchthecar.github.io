# 2024 BMW X5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW X5 is a sophisticated and technologically advanced luxury midsize SUV, offering a blend of strong performance, upscale interior amenities, and multiple refined powertrain configurations. However, owner reports and early field data point to notable electrical glitches, complex software-driven systems, and specific mechanical items that buyers should inspect carefully. While many owners enjoy its driving dynamics, reviewing maintenance records and ensuring all applicable safety recalls have been addressed is essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (sDrive40i / xDrive40i)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 375 horsepower, offering smooth power delivery and strong everyday acceleration. It serves as the standard powerplant for the 40i models, balancing performance with respectable efficiency.

**Engine Stalling and Software Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden engine hesitation, warning errors on the dashboard, and unexpected engine stalling while operating the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a dealer or specialist to update engine control module software or replace failing sensors.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-In Hybrid (xDrive50e)

The xDrive50e plug-in hybrid pairs a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six with an electric motor to deliver a combined 483 horsepower and notable electric-only driving range. While potent and efficient, its complex electrical architecture requires careful diagnostic oversight.

**High-Voltage System and Drivetrain Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Owners experience sudden loss of power accompanied by drivetrain malfunction or high-voltage system error messages, occasionally leaving the vehicle stranded.
- **Solution:** Dealer diagnosis of the high-voltage battery control electronics, software updates, or replacement of affected hybrid components.

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (M60i)

The high-performance 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 churns out an impressive 523 horsepower, providing effortless acceleration and a muscular exhaust note for enthusiast drivers. It is a complex, high-output engine that demands strict adherence to maintenance intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ubiquitous 8-speed automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast, smooth gear changes across all trim levels and seamlessly integrates with the available all-wheel-drive systems. It is generally robust, though complex electronic controls can occasionally present software-related drivability concerns.

**Transmission Disengagement and Revving**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may unexpectedly disengage while maneuvering, causing the engine to rev freely in drive mode and resulting in a temporary loss of propulsion.
- **Solution:** Transmission control module reprogramming, valve body service, or replacement of failing internal mechatronics units.

## Brakes

### Integrated Brake System

The electronic braking system provides confident stopping power, but relies heavily on sophisticated sensors and integrated control modules that are subject to specific recall campaigns.

**Brake System Malfunctions and Unexpected Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights on the dashboard regarding brake failure, alongside reports of unexpected braking behavior or reduced braking assist.
- **Solution:** Perform manufacturer safety recall repairs, replace faulty wheel speed sensors, or service the integrated brake module.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Emergency Call System

The vehicle's extensive electronic architecture powers dual curved display screens and advanced connectivity features, though early models have experienced localized software glitches and module failures.

**Emergency Call System Malfunction and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Persistent dashboard error messages regarding the emergency call system, instrument cluster screen blackouts, and excessive battery drain caused by control modules failing to sleep.
- **Solution:** Reprogramming or replacing the telematics control unit (TCU), updating instrument cluster software, and replacing drained batteries.

## Exterior

### Glass and Sunroof

Exterior styling features large glass surfaces and panoramic sunroof options that dramatically open up the cabin, though rare instances of spontaneous glass breakage have been documented.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof panels may suddenly fracture or explode while driving or parked, showering the cabin with glass shards.
- **Solution:** Complete replacement of the panoramic glass assembly and thorough cleaning of interior trim pieces.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V471000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The knee air bag (driver and/or passenger) may not have been manufactured correctly, which can result in the knee air bag not deploying as intended. A knee air bag that does not deploy as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the knee air bag (driver and/or passenger), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V622000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger knee air bag may not deploy as intended. An air bag that does not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger knee air bag, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V104000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V138000: Structure:Interior Panels:Dashboard](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The instrument panel may not have been manufactured properly, which can result in improper air bag deployment or the panel separating during air bag deployment. Instrument panel separation during air bag deployment can result in pieces of the panel striking occupants.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the instrument panel, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V327000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**

**[Recall 24V697000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** In addition, the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and Dynamic Stability Control (DSC) system may not function. Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.

**[Recall 24V739000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V422000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Electric:Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X5&modelYear=2024)**

- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW X5 remains a top contender in the luxury SUV segment thanks to its refined driving manners, high-end cabin, and strong powertrain choices. However, prospective buyers should verify that all open recalls—particularly those involving the integrated braking system—have been resolved by a certified dealer. Prioritize models with complete service histories and thoroughly test all electronic features, driver aids, and infotainment systems during a pre-purchase inspection.
