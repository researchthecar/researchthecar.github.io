# 2021 BMW 230i

### Overview
The 2021 BMW 230i, part of the F23 generation's final model year, is generally regarded as a reliable compact luxury coupe. It typically features a robust 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission, known for its smooth operation and good fuel economy. The vehicle's build quality is characteristic of BMW's premium offerings, with a focus on driver engagement and a well-appointed interior. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 230i has demonstrated a solid track record, with most reported problems being minor or related to typical maintenance rather than widespread design flaws. Owners generally praise its performance and handling.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW 230i presents a strong case for reliability within its segment. The powertrain, consisting of the 2.0L turbocharged engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is well-proven and generally free from significant defects. While minor electrical glitches, particularly with the infotainment system, can occur and battery life can vary, these are not indicative of systemic failures. The chassis, suspension, exterior, and interior components have also shown good durability. Overall, the 230i is a dependable choice for those seeking a sporty and luxurious compact coupe, with most potential issues being manageable and not prohibitively expensive to address.
