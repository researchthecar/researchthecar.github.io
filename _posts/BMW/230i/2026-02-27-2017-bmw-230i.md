# 2017 BMW 230i

### Overview
The 2017 BMW 230i, part of the F22 generation, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its performance-oriented nature. It offers a blend of sporty driving dynamics and premium features. The 230i is powered by BMW's robust turbocharged four-cylinder engine, known for its efficiency and power delivery. Transmission options typically include a smooth-shifting automatic and, in some markets, a manual. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, certain components can occasionally present issues. This analysis focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related reliability concerns.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 230i demonstrates strong overall reliability, with its core mechanical components like the engine and transmissions showing no significant widespread issues. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience, provided regular maintenance is performed. The vehicle's robust engineering contributes to its reputation as a reliable performance coupe.
