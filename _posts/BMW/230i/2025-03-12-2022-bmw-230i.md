The 2022 BMW 230i is a sporty and compact coupe that offers a blend of performance and luxury. It is praised for its agile handling, quick acceleration, and stylish design. Some owners appreciate its premium interior and advanced technology features. However, some have expressed concerns about the firm ride, limited rear seat space, and the cost of maintenance. The standard engine is a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 producing 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The 230i is only offered with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Higher trim models such as the M240i feature a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine that produce 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Inline-4**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported oil leaks, particularly around the valve cover gasket or oil filter housing. Symptoms include the smell of burning oil, visible oil drips under the car, and low oil level warnings.
    *   Solution: Replacing the valve cover gasket or oil filter housing gasket.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

*   **Rough Shifting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rough or delayed shifting, particularly during lower gears.
    *   Solution: Software update, or in more severe cases, valve body replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 (Software Update) - $2000+ (Valve Body Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Premature Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Owners have noticed premature wear of suspension bushings, leading to clunking noises, vague steering feel, and decreased ride comfort.
    * Solution: Replacement of worn bushings, such as control arm bushings or sway bar bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+ (depending on the specific bushing and labor rates)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can occasionally freeze, become unresponsive, or experience glitches in the display.
    *   Solution: Software updates or, in rare cases, replacement of the infotainment unit.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (Software Update) - $1500+ (Infotainment Unit Replacement)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Various)**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctions in various sensors, such as the parking sensors or blind spot monitoring system, can occur. These can manifest as false alarms, error messages, or complete failure of the system.
    * Solution: Diagnosis of the specific sensor failure and replacement of the faulty sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ (depending on the sensor and labor rates)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rattling or squeaking noises emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or rear deck.
    *   Solution: Locating and addressing the source of the noise, potentially by tightening fasteners, adding insulation, or replacing worn clips.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+ (depending on the severity and source)
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Seat Creaking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported creaking noises coming from the seats.
    *   Solution: Tightening or lubricating the seat frame or adjustment mechanism.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 BMW 230i appears to have some common issues, including engine oil leaks, rough shifting, and electrical glitches. Most issues are not considered severe but should be addressed to maintain optimal performance and prevent further damage. The cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the specific problem and the extent of the damage.
