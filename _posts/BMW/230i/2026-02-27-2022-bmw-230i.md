# 2022 BMW 230i

### Overview
The 2022 BMW 230i, part of the second generation G42 model, generally presents a strong reliability profile, building on BMW's reputation for engineering quality. It is equipped with a potent yet efficient turbocharged four-cylinder engine and a well-regarded automatic transmission. Owners can expect a refined driving experience with solid build quality. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the 230i appears to have avoided widespread, systemic problems in its initial production years. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring its long-term performance and durability, as with any luxury performance vehicle.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 230i demonstrates commendable reliability for its class. The core mechanical components, including the 2.0L turbocharged engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, are robust and have not shown significant widespread failure trends. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to infotainment software, are the most commonly cited, but these are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the 230i is a solid choice for those seeking a blend of performance and dependability, provided regular maintenance is performed.
