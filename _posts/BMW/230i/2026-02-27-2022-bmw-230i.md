# 2022 BMW 230i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 230i delivers an engaging driving experience with responsive handling and a refined cabin, making it a compelling entry point into modern luxury sport coupes. As a relatively recent model year, owner feedback indicates a generally solid initial ownership experience with minimal widespread mechanical complaints. Prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and electronic health.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering lively acceleration and respectable fuel economy. It is generally regarded as a robust modern powerplant for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers swift, smooth gear changes and pairs exceptionally well with the turbocharged engine. It has built a strong reputation for durability across numerous BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V636000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Relay](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=230i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter relay may corrode, causing the relay to overheat and short circuit. A short circuit in the starter relay may increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=230i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 230i stands out as a dependable and entertaining choice for used sport coupe buyers, backed by proven powertrain engineering and minimal early owner grievances. While the lack of major reported complaint trends is reassuring, standard used-car due diligence—such as reviewing maintenance records and checking for any unperformed recall updates—remains essential.
