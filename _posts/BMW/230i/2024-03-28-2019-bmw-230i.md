**Introduction:**

The 2019 BMW 230i is a compact coupe that delivers a thrilling driving experience with its turbocharged engine and sharp handling. It boasts a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, paired with a standard 8-speed automatic transmission.

The 230i has been praised for its responsive powertrain, agile handling, and premium interior. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.0-Liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

**Fuel Injector Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Stalling, rough idling, reduced engine power
Solution: Replace fuel injectors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic:**

**Harsh Shifting:**
Description/Symptoms: Jerky or rough shifts, especially when downshifting
Solution: Software update
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (covered under warranty)
Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Infotainment System Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezes, unresponsive, or displays errors
Solution: Software update or component replacement
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation:**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside the headlight assemblies
Solution: Replace headlight assemblies
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: N/A

**Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Wipers stop working or move slowly
Solution: Replace wiper motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

**Faulty Climate Control Unit:**
Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating system not functioning properly
Solution: Replace climate control unit
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2019 BMW 230i offers a compelling driving experience, but it is not without its faults. The reported issues are generally manageable and do not affect the overall reliability of the vehicle. However, some issues, such as the fuel injector failure and climate control unit failure, can be costly to repair.
