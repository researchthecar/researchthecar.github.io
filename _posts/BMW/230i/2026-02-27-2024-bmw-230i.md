# 2024 BMW 230i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2024 BMW 230i gives buyers access to a modern luxury sport coupe or gran coupe known for lively performance and engaging driving dynamics. This model year generally benefits from mature powertrain engineering, though used car shoppers should always evaluate vehicle history, maintenance records, and proper upkeep before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers a strong balance of everyday efficiency and responsive acceleration, maintaining solid baseline reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and pairs seamlessly with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It is widely regarded as a durable and refined unit in modern BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V770000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=230i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear may not have been tightened properly, which can allow it to loosen. A loose steering gear can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 230i presents an attractive option for used car buyers seeking luxury and sporty performance. Because owner experiences can vary, a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verification of service history remain essential steps for any prospective buyer.
