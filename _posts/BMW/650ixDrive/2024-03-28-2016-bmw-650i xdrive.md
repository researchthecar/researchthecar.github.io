**Introduction**

The 2016 BMW 650i xDrive is a high-performance luxury coupe powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. It features an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 650i is praised for its impressive performance, handling, and luxurious interior. However, like any vehicle, it has also experienced some reported issues by owners.

**Engine**

* **4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Some vehicles consume excessive engine oil, requiring frequent top-ups or potentially leading to engine damage.
        * Solution: BMW has issued a technical service bulletin (TSB) to address this issue, which may involve replacing the engine valve stem seals or performing other repairs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: A prematurely worn or stretched timing chain can result in a variety of problems, including decreased engine performance, engine rattling noises, and potential engine failure.
        * Solution: Replacing the affected timing chain requires specialized labor and is typically a major repair.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking During Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears, particularly during low-speed maneuvers.
        * Solution: Replacing transmission fluid or updating the transmission software may resolve the issue. In some cases, a more thorough repair may be necessary.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Premature Gearbox Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: In some vehicles, the transmission may fail prematurely, leading to a loss of power or inability to shift gears.
        * Solution: Replacing the affected transmission or rebuilding it is required.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Axle Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear axle bushings may prematurely wear, leading to a clunking or knocking noise from the rear of the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replacing the worn bushings typically resolves the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Faults**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some vehicles have reported various electrical faults, such as flickering lights, malfunctioning electronics, or battery drain.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the specific electrical issue is required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies based on the specific issue
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture may enter the headlight housing, causing condensation buildup inside the lenses.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected headlight assemblies usually resolves the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000 per headlight
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Washer Nozzle Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield washer nozzles may fail to spray fluid properly.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected nozzles is typically the solution.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may rattle or squeak when opened or closed.
    * Solution: Adjusting, lubricating, or replacing the sunroof components may resolve the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Loose Interior Trim Pieces**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some interior trim pieces, such as door panels or dashboard elements, may occasionally become loose or detached.
    * Solution: Reattaching or replacing the affected trim pieces is typically the remedy.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 BMW 650i xDrive has received generally positive feedback, but some owners have reported certain issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but it's important for potential buyers to be aware of them before making a purchase.
