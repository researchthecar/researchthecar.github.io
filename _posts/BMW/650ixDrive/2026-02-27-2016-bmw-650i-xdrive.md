# 2016 BMW 650i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 650i xDrive offers high-performance luxury with robust twin-turbo V8 power and confident all-wheel-drive capability. While it delivers an engaging driving experience and a high-end cabin, used car buyers should approach high-performance German luxury with careful inspection of maintenance records and complex engine support systems.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (N63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. It provides effortless, rapid acceleration but requires meticulous maintenance and strict adherence to oil service intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission features steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters and delivers smooth, rapid gear changes tailored to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust when fluid maintenance is kept up to date.
