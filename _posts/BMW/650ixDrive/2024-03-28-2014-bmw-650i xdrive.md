**Introduction:**

The 2014 BMW 650i xDrive is a high-performance luxury coupe that delivers exceptional power and handling. It features a potent 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that generates 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. The 650i xDrive comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive, providing a balance of performance and stability.

While the BMW 650i xDrive has earned praise for its impressive driving dynamics and luxurious amenities, it has also been the subject of several reported issues affecting various components.

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine**

**Oil Leak from Valve Covers**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil from the valve covers, indicated by visible oil pooling or drips.
Solution: Replacing the valve cover gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Intake Valve Cleaning**
Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves, leading to reduced engine performance and increased fuel consumption.
Solution: Cleaning the intake valves using a walnut blasting process or chemical cleaning.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Transmission Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Complete transmission failure, resulting in a loss of power and inability to shift gears.
Solution: Replacing the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely even after being fully charged, often due to a parasitic draw.
Solution: Diagnosing and fixing the source of the parasitic draw, such as a faulty module or accessory.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Soft-Close Trunk Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The trunk lid fails to close automatically or opens unexpectedly.
Solution: Replacing the soft-close trunk actuator or other related components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2014 BMW 650i xDrive has been praised for its impressive performance and luxury, but it has also experienced some reliability concerns. The engine and transmission issues can be severe and costly to repair, potentially affecting the owner's satisfaction with the vehicle. While the suspension, exterior, and interior have not been reported to have major issues, the reported electrical and interior problems may still cause inconvenience or require attention. Potential buyers should be aware of these reported issues and consider them when evaluating the overall reliability of the 2014 BMW 650i xDrive.
