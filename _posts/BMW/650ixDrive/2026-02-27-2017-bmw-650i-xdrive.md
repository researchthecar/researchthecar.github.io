# 2017 BMW 650i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a 2017 BMW 650i xDrive, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury gran coupe powered by a potent twin-turbo V8 engine paired with all-wheel drive. While these vehicles offer impressive power and plush interior appointments, they are complex machines requiring meticulous maintenance. Prospective owners should review available owner feedback carefully and budget for potential upkeep associated with luxury European performance platforms.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine (445 hp)

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, offering rapid acceleration. While powerful, the complex turbocharging and auxiliary systems require strict adherence to maintenance schedules to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and integrates with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system to deliver smooth, responsive gear changes. It generally proves durable under normal driving conditions when fluid maintenance is kept up to date.

## Interior

### Trunk Release and Seat Folding Mechanism

electrical issues can occasionally impact convenience features such as trunk access and seat release mechanisms.

**Trunk Release Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience a total failure of the electronic trunk release mechanism, leaving no straightforward way to access the trunk area.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic release switch, fuses, and latch actuator assembly, and replace faulty electrical components as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=650i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 650i xDrive blends striking styling with robust V8 performance and all-wheel-drive capability. Buyers should verify all electrical accessories and convenience features during a pre-purchase inspection. Prioritizing a well-documented maintenance history will help ensure a more satisfying ownership experience.
