### Overview
The 2017 BMW 740Le xDrive, a plug-in hybrid variant of the flagship 7 Series, combines luxury, performance, and efficiency. As a high-end luxury sedan, its reliability is generally expected to be strong, with a focus on advanced technology and premium materials. ## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Suspension Leaks/Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'air suspension malfunction' warning light, unusual noises from the suspension system.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace leaking air springs, struts, or the air compressor. Regular inspection of air lines and connections is recommended.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Electrical

### Hybrid Battery System
**Reduced Electric Range/Charging Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable decrease in electric-only driving range, failure to charge the battery fully, 'hybrid system malfunction' warning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a BMW specialist to identify battery degradation or charging system faults. Potential battery module replacement or charging system component repair.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $7000+


### Infotainment System
**iDrive Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness to touch or controls, intermittent audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets. In some cases, replacement of the iDrive control unit or display may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 740Le xDrive demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its advanced hybrid system and air suspension. Issues with the hybrid battery, while not extremely common, can be costly to repair. Similarly, the air suspension system can develop leaks or compressor failures over time. Electrical gremlins within the iDrive system are also a possibility, often resolvable with software updates. Beyond these specific points, the engine and transmission are robust. Owners should be mindful of the specialized nature of hybrid and air suspension repairs, opting for qualified technicians.
