# 2016 BMW 428i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 428i Gran Coupe blends sleek styling with everyday utility, making it an appealing option on the used market. Buyers looking at this luxury four-door should carefully evaluate its mechanical history and maintenance records to ensure long-term dependability. While it offers engaging driving dynamics, understanding its powertrain options and typical ownership experiences is essential for a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, though prospective buyers should verify regular maintenance history, particularly regarding timing chain guides.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid shifts and pairs well with the standard rear-wheel-drive or optional xDrive all-wheel-drive systems. It is generally robust and well-regarded for both performance and everyday drivability.
