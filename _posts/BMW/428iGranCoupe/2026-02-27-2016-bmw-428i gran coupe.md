# 2016 BMW 428i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 428i Gran Coupe, part of the F36 generation, is generally considered a reliable vehicle, particularly praised for its engaging driving dynamics and premium interior. It typically features a potent 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from repair shops. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20/B48)
**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $2,500

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gasket, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected gasket(s).
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential whistling noise.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### General Electrical System
**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, warning lights on the dashboard.

- **Cost:** $800 - $1,200

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning or not working.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of battery, alternator, or parasitic drain source.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 428i Gran Coupe offers a compelling blend of performance and practicality. Its primary reliability concerns, based on available data, tend to center around the engine, specifically the timing chain, PCV valve, and potential oil leaks, which are not uncommon for turbocharged engines of this era. The electric water pump is another component that has shown a propensity for failure, leading to overheating issues. The 8-speed automatic transmission and other major systems like suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally robust. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to early warning signs are crucial for ensuring long-term reliability and avoiding more costly repairs.
