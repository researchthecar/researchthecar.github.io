# 2017 BMW M240i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 BMW M240i combines strong performance and everyday drivability, driven by a respected turbocharged inline-six engine. While owner-reported complaints are relatively sparse, buyers should review the available powertrain options and keep an eye out for minor equipment quirks.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 2017 M240i features the 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six (B58) engine, producing approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant is widely praised for its robust performance, smooth power delivery, and strong overall reliability profile.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a responsive 8-speed ZF automatic transmission featuring paddle shifters, this drivetrain offers crisp gear changes and solid highway efficiency. It is generally considered a durable and trouble-free unit in well-maintained examples.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 6-speed manual transmission is available for purists seeking direct engagement. It matches the performance character of the inline-six nicely and exhibits good mechanical durability.

## Suspension

### Factory Wheels

Suspension and wheel components are generally robust, though low-profile factory rims warrant careful inspection.

**Rim Fractures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported unexpected wheel rim fracturing on original factory rims, leading to sudden tire pressure loss.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels closely for structural cracks during a pre-purchase inspection and replace any damaged rims.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster Lighting

Electrical systems are generally dependable, with only minor visibility quirks reported for interior instrumentation.

**Unlit Gauges in Dim Daylight**
- **Symptoms:** Speedometer and tachometer gauges can become difficult to read or lack proper backlighting under daytime dim lighting conditions.
- **Solution:** Check instrument cluster illumination settings and verify proper gauge function during varying ambient light conditions.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety systems operate reliably overall, though isolated sensor warnings may occasionally require professional diagnosis.

**Passenger Airbag Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** A persistent passenger airbag malfunction warning light illuminates on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) sensor or seat occupancy mat to identify the fault code.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW M240i is an engaging and generally reliable luxury sport coupe. Prospective buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection focusing on the wheels, interior electronics, and safety warning indicators to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
