# 2019 BMW M240i

### Overview
The 2019 BMW M240i, a performance-oriented compact luxury coupe, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting BMW's engineering prowess. It is powered by a potent turbocharged inline-six engine known for its robust performance and relatively good durability. Transmission options, typically an automatic or a manual, are also well-regarded. While the M240i is built with quality components, like any performance vehicle, it can encounter specific issues, particularly related to its high-performance nature and advanced systems. Owners should be aware of potential areas such as the cooling system, turbocharger, and certain electronic modules, though widespread catastrophic failures are uncommon. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its long-term reliability.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks around hoses or radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of turbocharger or related components.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 BMW M240i is a reliable performance car with a strong mechanical foundation. The 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine is generally robust, though potential issues with the cooling system, turbocharger, and PCV valve should be monitored. Both the 8-speed automatic and 6-speed manual transmissions are well-built and rarely present significant problems. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system's software, can occur but are usually resolvable with updates. Overall, the M240i offers a compelling blend of performance and dependability, provided it receives consistent maintenance and any emerging issues are addressed promptly.
