# 2019 BMW M240i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW M240i delivers thrilling performance backed by a potent turbocharged inline-six engine and refined transmission options. For used-car buyers, this luxury sport coupe generally presents a solid ownership proposition when well-maintained, though prospective owners should carefully inspect mechanical and electrical systems prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded by enthusiasts and mechanics as one of BMW's more robust and reliable modern powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, smooth shifts and pairs exceptionally well with the inline-six engine across rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It maintains a strong reputation for durability under normal driving conditions.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission caters to driving purists seeking a more engaging, hands-on experience. It is mechanically robust, though overall availability is rarer on the used market.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M240i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW M240i is an appealing choice for enthusiasts seeking a blend of everyday usability and high-end performance. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive service histories and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to ensure long-term reliability.
