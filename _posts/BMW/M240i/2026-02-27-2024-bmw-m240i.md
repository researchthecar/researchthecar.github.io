It typically features a robust turbocharged inline-six engine and a sophisticated automatic transmission, designed for both spirited driving and daily usability. Reliability for this model generally aligns with BMW's premium standards, focusing on durable powertrain components and advanced technology. While specific long-term data for the 2024 model year is still emerging, historical trends and initial owner feedback suggest a strong mechanical foundation. ### Summary Verdict
The 2024 BMW M240i demonstrates strong mechanical reliability, particularly with its acclaimed turbocharged inline-six engine and robust automatic transmission. Early data suggests these core components are performing well, with no widespread issues reported. The primary areas where minor concerns have surfaced are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery drain, which are not uncommon in modern vehicles with complex electronics. Suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally holding up well. Overall, the M240i is a reliable performance coupe, provided routine maintenance is adhered to. Potential owners should be aware of the possibility of minor electrical gremlins and factor in the typical premium maintenance costs associated with a performance BMW.
