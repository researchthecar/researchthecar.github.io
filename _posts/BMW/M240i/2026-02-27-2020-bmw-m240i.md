# 2020 BMW M240i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M240i is a high-performance luxury coupe and convertible known for its potent powertrain and engaging driving dynamics. As a pre-owned purchase, it offers a strong blend of everyday usability and sports-car performance, though buyers should evaluate condition and maintenance history carefully.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine (B58) produces approximately 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as robust and powerful, representing one of BMW's more reliable modern powerplant offerings.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick and smooth shifts, pairing exceptionally well with the inline-six engine across both rear-wheel drive and xDrive all-wheel drive configurations.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission caters to enthusiast drivers seeking a more connected shifting experience, available on rear-wheel-drive variants.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M240i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M240i stands out as a compelling choice for used performance car buyers due to its highly regarded powertrain options and strong driving dynamics. Prospective owners should prioritize vehicles with documented service histories and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection.
