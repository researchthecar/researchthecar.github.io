# 2016 BMW 535i GT xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 535i GT xDrive combines luxury, spaciousness, and all-weather capability, appealing to buyers looking for a distinct grand tourer. When shopping for a used model, understanding its powertrain options and monitoring for potential electronic or fluid-related issues will help ensure a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as one of BMW's more robust modern powerplants, though proper maintenance of cooling and oil systems is critical for longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, delivering smooth and responsive shifts. It is widely praised for durability and refined performance across various driving conditions.
