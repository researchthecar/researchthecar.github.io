# 2017 BMW 535i GT xDrive

### Overview
The 2017 BMW 535i GT xDrive, part of the F07 generation, is a luxury Gran Turismo that blends the practicality of a wagon with the driving dynamics of a sedan. It is equipped with BMW's renowned xDrive all-wheel-drive system, enhancing its stability and performance in various conditions. Under the hood, it typically features a potent turbocharged inline-six engine paired with an automatic transmission. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, like any complex machine, it can experience specific issues as it ages. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust, potential oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased oil consumption, boost leaks.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000+

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, various electrical components malfunctioning or not working.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw, battery replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**iDrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of the iDrive control unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 535i GT xDrive generally offers good reliability, particularly with its robust powertrain. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the engine, specifically the PCV valve, water pump, and turbocharger, which can require attention as the vehicle accumulates mileage. Electrical issues like battery drain and iDrive system glitches are also noted, though often resolvable with software updates or component replacement. The transmission, suspension, exterior, and interior components are typically solid. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging symptoms are key to ensuring long-term dependability for this luxury performance vehicle.
