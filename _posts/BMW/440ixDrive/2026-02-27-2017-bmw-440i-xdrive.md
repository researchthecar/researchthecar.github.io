# 2017 BMW 440i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 BMW 440i xDrive offers an engaging blend of luxury and performance, centered around its acclaimed turbocharged inline-six engine. While generally praised for its driving dynamics and solid build quality, prospective buyers should review common owner-reported trouble spots such as cooling system leaks and electrical or restraint system warnings.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Engine (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine (B58) produces approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more robust modern powerplants, though ancillary plastic cooling components require periodic inspection.

**Coolant Leak from Plastic Housings**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report low coolant warnings, visible fluid under the vehicle, or engine overheating due to failures in plastic components like the oil filter housing or thermostat.
- **Solution:** Inspect plastic coolant lines, fittings, and the oil filter housing regularly; replace degraded components with updated parts.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with BMW's xDrive all-wheel-drive system, offering lightning-fast and seamless gear changes. It has a strong reputation for durability with standard fluid maintenance.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Restraint System

Safety features generally perform well, though electronic sensor faults in the cabin restraint system can occasionally trigger dashboard warnings.

**Passenger Occupancy Sensor Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning lights and intermittent messages regarding passenger restraint system or seat occupancy sensor failures.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified shop or dealer to reset or replace faulty occupancy sensors or wiring harnesses.

### Seat Belt

Seat belts and tensioners provide standard protection, but isolated mechanical wear can affect latch retention.

**Seat Belt Latch and Tensioner Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The driver or passenger seat belt may fail to remain securely latched at its anchor point or trigger warning chimes.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace worn tensioners or latch mechanisms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V115000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat-mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 440i xDrive, prioritize vehicles with thorough maintenance records, particularly concerning cooling system health and electrical operations. While owner reports highlight occasional coolant leaks and interior sensor glitches, the robust B58 engine and smooth drivetrain make it a rewarding choice if properly inspected prior to purchase.
