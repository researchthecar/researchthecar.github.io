# 2018 BMW 440i xDrive

### Overview
The 2018 BMW 440i xDrive, equipped with the potent B58 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, generally presents a strong reliability profile. This model year benefits from BMW's mature engineering, with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system adding to its robust performance. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 440i xDrive has demonstrated a commendable track record, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. Owners generally report satisfaction with the vehicle's build quality and long-term durability, though like any performance-oriented luxury car, proactive maintenance is key to ensuring optimal operation and longevity. Potential areas for attention are typically minor and related to specific electronic features or less common component failures.

### Summary Verdict
The 2018 BMW 440i xDrive is a reliable performance coupe, with its core powertrain (3.0L B58 engine and ZF 8-speed transmission) being exceptionally robust. Most reported issues are minor and related to the electrical system, such as infotainment glitches or occasional battery drain, and some exterior lighting components. Interior and suspension components generally perform well without widespread problems. While proactive maintenance is always recommended for a luxury performance vehicle, the 440i xDrive offers a solid ownership experience with a low probability of major mechanical failures.
