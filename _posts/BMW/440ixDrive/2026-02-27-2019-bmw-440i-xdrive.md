# 2019 BMW 440i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 BMW 440i xDrive, buyers are generally looking at a luxury sport machine powered by BMW's renowned B58 inline-six engine. While overall owner satisfaction is high regarding performance and refinement, reviewing owner-submitted complaints provides helpful insight into potential trouble spots. Prospective buyers should inspect the cooling systems and powertrain electronics closely during pre-purchase evaluations.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine (B58) produces approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, offering robust performance and smooth power delivery. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more reliable modern powerplants, though proper maintenance of its cooling system is essential.

**Coolant Leaks and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may receive dashboard warnings regarding low coolant levels or experience engine overheating while parked or driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system hoses, reservoir, and water pump for leaks. Replace any compromised components and refill with proper coolant.

**Pressure Control Valve (PCV) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A loud high-pitched squealing noise from the engine bay accompanied by white smoke billowing from the exhaust.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected under applicable extended warranty coverage or replace the faulty PCV valve assembly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup delivers quick shifts and solid traction in varied weather conditions. It typically proves dependable when routine fluid services are maintained.

**Drivetrain Malfunction Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden 'Drivetrain malfunction' warning message on the dashboard, sometimes accompanied by a temporary loss of acceleration while driving.
- **Solution:** Scan the vehicle's computer for stored diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint sensor, software, or mechanical faults.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body panels and trim are generally well-assembled, though occasional reports highlight glass integrity concerns regarding the factory sunroof option.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** A sudden, violent explosion or shattering sound from the glass sunroof panel while driving under normal conditions.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the sunroof frame for any structural stress or alignment issues.

## Safety

### Airbags and Restraints

The safety systems in this luxury sport model include comprehensive airbag deployment configurations and active restraint monitors, though sensor warnings require prompt professional diagnosis.

**Passenger Restraint System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent warning messages on the display screen regarding airbag or seat belt malfunctions, potentially affecting deployment readiness.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the seat belt sensors, wiring harnesses, and airbag modules for faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V465000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V533000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger air bag inflator may have been incorrectly manufactured, potentially affecting the air bag deployment performance in the event of a crash. If the front passenger air bag does not deploy properly in the event of a crash, the occupant has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 440i xDrive offers an appealing mix of high performance, everyday luxury, and all-weather capability anchored by its strong inline-six engine. While owners have reported isolated issues involving coolant leaks, drivetrain warnings, and SRS sensor messages, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist can help ensure you find a reliable example.
