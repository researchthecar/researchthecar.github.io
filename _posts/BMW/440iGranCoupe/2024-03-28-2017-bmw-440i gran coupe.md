## 2017 BMW 440i Gran Coupe

The 2017 BMW 440i Gran Coupe is a four-door luxury coupe that offers a blend of style, performance, and practicality. It is available with two engine options: a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine producing 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, and a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine producing 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. The 440i Gran Coupe is praised for its sporty handling, comfortable interior, and sleek design. However, it has received some criticism for its lack of rear seat headroom and cargo space.

### Engine

**Turbocharged 2.0-liter Four-Cylinder**

* **High-pressure fuel pump failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The high-pressure fuel pump can fail, causing a loss of power and eventual engine stalling.
    * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake valve carbon buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves can reduce engine performance and fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Clean the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Turbocharged 3.0-liter Inline-Six**

* **Turbocharger wastegate failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate can fail, causing a loss of boost pressure and reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger wastegate.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Mechatronic unit failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The mechatronic unit, which controls the transmission's shifting, can fail, causing jerky shifts and loss of power.
    * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Rear suspension bushing failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension bushings can wear out prematurely, causing a knocking or clunking noise when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the worn bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Check for electrical leaks and replace the battery if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Sunroof seal failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof seal can fail, causing water leaks into the interior.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Central console trim peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The trim on the central console can peel or bubble.
    * Solution: Replace the damaged trim.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear seatbelt latch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear seatbelt latch can fail to engage, preventing the seatbelt from being used.
    * Solution: Replace the faulty latch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: 19V-632

## Summary

Overall, the 2017 BMW 440i Gran Coupe is a reliable and well-built vehicle. However, some specific issues have been reported, including high-pressure fuel pump failure, intake valve carbon buildup, and mechatronic unit failure. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, but it is important to be aware of them before purchasing a used 440i Gran Coupe.
