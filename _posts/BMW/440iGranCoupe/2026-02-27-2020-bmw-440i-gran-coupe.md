# 2020 BMW 440i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 440i Gran Coupe combines sleek styling with strong performance from its signature inline-six engine. Prospective used-car buyers looking at this luxury four-door should review available maintenance history and remain aware of owner-reported issues involving engine cooling and braking components.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged I6

The 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is generally well-regarded for performance, though owners should monitor cooling system health closely.

**Cooling System Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a low coolant warning light illuminated on the dashboard accompanied by an engine overheating message.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system for leaks, verify coolant levels, and replace any compromised hoses or components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel or xDrive all-wheel drive offers crisp, responsive gear shifts and maximizes fuel efficiency. It is a highly refined unit with a strong reputation for long-term reliability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Owner feedback highlights rare instances of unexpected brake pedal behavior at low speeds, making a pre-purchase brake inspection essential.

**Brake Pedal Failure to Stop**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may fail to stop as expected when depressing the brake pedal at low speeds, without illuminating any warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have the braking system thoroughly inspected by a qualified professional to check for hydraulic pressure loss, brake pad wear, or master cylinder faults.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety equipment generally performs well, but buyers must verify that all active safety campaigns have been addressed.

**Knee Airbag Deployment Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Defective knee airbags may fail to deploy properly in the event of a frontal collision.
- **Solution:** Check vehicle recall records using your VIN to ensure that any necessary safety recall repairs have been completed by an authorized dealer.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW 440i Gran Coupe is an engaging and luxurious pre-owned choice, anchored by a potent inline-six engine and smooth transmission. While overall owner complaints are relatively low, buyers should carefully verify cooling system integrity, brake responsiveness, and open recall status during a pre-purchase inspection.
