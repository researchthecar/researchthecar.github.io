### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW 440i Gran Coupe demonstrates strong technical reliability, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing no significant widespread issues. Overall, the 440i Gran Coupe offers a dependable ownership experience for a performance luxury vehicle, provided routine maintenance is adhered to and any minor electrical anomalies are addressed promptly.
