# 2022 BMW 440i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2022 BMW 440i Gran Coupe, equipped with the B58 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, generally presents a strong reliability profile. This model benefits from BMW's mature powertrain technology, which has seen continuous refinement. Owners typically report a satisfying ownership experience with fewer unexpected mechanical issues compared to some competitors. However, as with any modern luxury vehicle, attention to specific electronic features and occasional software glitches can be noted. The Gran Coupe's blend of performance and practicality is a key selling point, and its reliability data reflects a well-engineered product.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 440i Gran Coupe demonstrates commendable reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The B58 engine and ZF 8-speed transmission are robust and well-regarded. The primary areas where minor issues have been observed are within the electrical system, specifically related to infotainment software glitches and occasional sensor calibration needs for driver assistance features. These are generally manageable and often resolvable with software updates or recalibration. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components show no significant widespread problems. Overall, the 440i Gran Coupe is a reliable choice for a luxury performance sedan, provided routine maintenance is performed and potential minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
