# 2019 BMW 440i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury sport sedan, the 2019 BMW 440i Gran Coupe offers an enticing mix of athletic handling, sleek styling, and strong powertrain performance. However, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback and known mechanical tendencies before making a purchase. While driver reports represent individual experiences rather than guaranteed factory flaws, keeping an eye out for specific trouble spots can help ensure a more confident ownership journey.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 2019 BMW 440i Gran Coupe features a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine (the acclaimed B58) producing roughly 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is generally praised for its smooth power delivery and robust long-term durability, though proper cooling system maintenance remains crucial.

**Coolant Leaks and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted low coolant warnings appearing on the instrument cluster, sometimes accompanied by engine overheating risks.
- **Solution:** Inspect the coolant expansion tank, hoses, and connections for signs of weeping or leaks, and top off or replace damaged components as necessary.

**Drivetrain Malfunction and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden dashboard warnings for drivetrain malfunctions, occasionally leading to a loss of throttle response or engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle's computer for stored diagnostic trouble codes relating to the pressure control valve, fuel delivery, or electronic sensors.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through a responsive 8-speed ZF automatic transmission paired with either rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. This gearbox is widely regarded as one of the best in the industry, offering quick shifts and solid mechanical reliability.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

The exterior styling incorporates sleek lines and optional glass sunroof panels, which require careful inspection for weatherstripping and alignment.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced sudden, violent shattering of the glass sunroof panel while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by a loud pop.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for pre-existing micro-fractures or stress points, and replace the glass assembly if structural compromise is detected.

## Safety

### Airbags and Restraints

Safety electronics monitor multi-stage airbags and active seat belts, though sensor fault messages can occasionally trigger warning lights.

**Airbag and Seat Belt Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent warning messages for the passenger restraint system, airbag off, or service airbag may illuminate on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt tensioners, wiring harnesses, and occupant detection sensors, and have the safety restraint module professionally diagnosed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V465000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V533000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger air bag inflator may have been incorrectly manufactured, potentially affecting the air bag deployment performance in the event of a crash. If the front passenger air bag does not deploy properly in the event of a crash, the occupant has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 440i Gran Coupe is a rewarding daily driver backed by a powerhouse engine and crisp handling dynamics. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential cooling system hiccups, electronic sensor alerts, and rare sunroof issues, regular service history can mitigate most risks. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a BMW specialist to verify engine health and electrical integrity.
