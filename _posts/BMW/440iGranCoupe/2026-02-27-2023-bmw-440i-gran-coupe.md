# 2023 BMW 440i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 440i Gran Coupe blends striking four-door coupe styling with engaging performance, anchored by BMW's well-regarded turbocharged inline-six powertrain. While overall owner feedback and reliability tracking remain sparse for this model year due to its relative newness, prospective buyers can generally expect a refined driving experience supported by modern engineering. As with any luxury performance vehicle, inspecting service history and verifying proper maintenance remains crucial.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is widely considered one of BMW's more robust and dependable modern powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with standard rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive, this 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, refined shifts and contributes significantly to overall efficiency and performance. It generally exhibits strong reliability in daily driving conditions.
