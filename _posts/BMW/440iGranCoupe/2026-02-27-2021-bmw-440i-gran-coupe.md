# 2021 BMW 440i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury car, evaluating owner feedback and known mechanical options helps set realistic expectations. This report summarizes reported owner experiences and powertrain options to help you make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering robust performance and smooth power delivery. It is widely regarded as a reliable and potent powerplant in modern BMW lineup configurations.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup delivers quick, seamless gear changes. It is generally noted for strong durability under normal driving conditions.

## Safety

### Seat Belt

Issues related to seat belt mechanisms and webbing wear have been noted in owner complaints.

**Seat Belt Presenter and Retractor Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the automated seat belt presenter arm mechanism can feel flimsy, and the belt webbing or chest strap may fray or shred prematurely. Additionally, some owners experienced repeated failures in the seat belt retractor assembly.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt webbing for signs of wear or shredding and ensure the automatic presenters function properly. Damaged assemblies or retractors will typically need replacement.
