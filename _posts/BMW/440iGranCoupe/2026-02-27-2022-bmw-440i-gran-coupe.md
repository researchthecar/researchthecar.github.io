# 2022 BMW 440i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2022 BMW 440i Gran Coupe means enjoying sleek styling and potent turbocharged performance, but buyers should review known owner reports and active service campaigns carefully. While early owner feedback covers a range of mechanical and electrical concerns, careful pre-purchase inspections remain the best tool for identifying potential issues before sealing the deal.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and impressive refinement. It is widely regarded as a mechanically durable powerplant within modern BMW lineups, though buyers should verify all open campaigns have been addressed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission provides quick, smooth gear changes paired with standard rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It generally offers solid long-term dependability when properly maintained.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may occasionally shift into neutral independently while driving at various speeds without illuminating warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose transmission control modules and software calibration.

### Rear Differential

The drivetrain components transfer power effectively to the wheels, but owners have reported occasional leaks requiring significant repairs.

**Rear Differential Fluid Leak and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid leaks developing around the differential housing, potentially leading to premature internal component wear and requiring complete unit replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect the differential seals and housing for leaks; replace the differential assembly if internal damage has occurred.

## Safety

### Backup Camera and Restraint Systems

Safety and driver-assistance features provide modern protection, though electronic glitches can occasionally interfere with camera and restraint operations.

**Rear Vision Camera Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera may deactivate when the park assist system is turned off, leaving the driver without a display image while reversing.
- **Solution:** Inspect software integration and camera module connections for faults.
