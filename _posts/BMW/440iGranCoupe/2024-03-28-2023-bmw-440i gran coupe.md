**Vehicle Introduction:**

The 2023 BMW 440i Gran Coupe is a stylish and sporty four-door coupe. It is powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine that produces 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Power is sent to all four wheels through an eight-speed automatic transmission.
