# 2017 BMW 440i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2017 BMW 440i Gran Coupe, part of the F36 generation, is generally regarded as a reliable and well-built vehicle, particularly known for its potent inline-six engine and engaging driving dynamics. This model year benefits from BMW's extensive engineering and quality control. While no car is entirely immune to issues, the 440i Gran Coupe typically exhibits strong performance with a focus on driver experience. Owners often praise its blend of sportiness and practicality. Reliability data suggests that with proper maintenance, this model can offer a dependable ownership experience, with most reported issues being minor or related to specific component wear rather than widespread design flaws. The powertrain, in particular, has a solid reputation.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 440i Gran Coupe (F36) demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly with its acclaimed B58 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the robust ZF 8-speed automatic transmission. While minor electrical gremlins, such as occasional infotainment glitches or battery drain issues, can occur, they are not indicative of systemic failures. The mechanical components, suspension, exterior, and interior generally hold up well, reflecting BMW's premium build quality. With diligent maintenance, especially fluid changes for the transmission and regular battery checks, the 440i Gran Coupe is expected to provide a dependable and enjoyable ownership experience, making it a solid choice for those seeking performance and luxury.
