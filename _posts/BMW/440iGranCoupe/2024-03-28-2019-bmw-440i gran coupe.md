## 2019 BMW 440i Gran Coupe

The 2019 BMW 440i Gran Coupe is a four-door luxury sports car that offers a combination of performance, style, and practicality. It is powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine that produces 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to the rear wheels.

The 440i Gran Coupe is also available with a 429-horsepower, 3.0-liter inline-six engine in the M440i model.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter TwinPower Turbocharged Inline-Six**

* **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:** The high-pressure fuel pump can fail, causing the engine to lose power or stall. Symptoms include a lack of power, rough idling, and stalling. The solution is to replace the high-pressure fuel pump. Estimated cost to fix: $1,000 - $1,500+ Recalls: N/A
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure:** The turbocharger wastegate can fail, causing the engine to overboost and potentially damage the engine. Symptoms include excessive smoke from the exhaust, a loss of power, and a rattling noise from the turbocharger. The solution is to replace the turbocharger wastegate. Estimated cost to fix: $1,500 - $2,000+ Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The vehicle's battery can drain prematurely. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, dim headlights, and a dead battery. The solution is to replace the battery. Estimated cost to fix: $200 - $400+ Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:** The infotainment system can experience glitches, such as freezing, crashing, or losing connectivity. Symptoms include a black screen, unresponsive buttons, and lost navigation data. The solution is to update the infotainment system software or replace the infotainment system module. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,500+ Recalls: N/A

### Summary

The 2019 BMW 440i Gran Coupe is a well-rounded luxury sports car that offers a thrilling driving experience and a comfortable and stylish interior. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that can affect the vehicle. Some of the issues, such as the high-pressure fuel pump failure, turbocharger wastegate failure, and battery drain, can be costly to fix. Overall, the 440i Gran Coupe is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to do your research before purchasing one to make sure that you are aware of the potential problems and costs associated with it.
