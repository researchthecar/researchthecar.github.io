# 2010 BMW 335i

### Overview
The 2010 BMW 335i, known for its potent performance and sporty handling, offers a compelling package for driving enthusiasts. It was available with a choice of robust inline-six engines and either a manual or automatic transmission, catering to different driver preferences. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics, like many performance-oriented vehicles of its era, it has specific areas that have shown a propensity for issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (N54)
**High-Pressure Fuel Pump (HPFP) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Stalling, rough idle, engine misfires, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the fuel pump.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Issues (Wastegate Rattle/Failure)**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine bay, loss of power, boost leaks.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or wastegate actuator repair.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000 per turbo

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, poor fuel economy, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per injector

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks, rough idle, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the PCV valve system.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)
**Turbocharger Issues (Wastegate Rattle)**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine bay, particularly at low RPMs.
- **Solution:** Often a software update or replacement of the turbocharger assembly if severe.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2500

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, poor fuel economy, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per injector


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement (clutch kit, flywheel, labor).
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission (Steptronic)
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering, slipping, rough shifting, transmission fluid leaks.
- **Solution:** Torque converter replacement or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3500

**Valve Body Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Valve body repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or entire control arms.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Strut Mount Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noises when turning, rough ride.
- **Solution:** Replacement of strut mounts.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Water Pump Failure (Electric)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Idrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the iDrive control unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not turning on, flickering headlights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2010 BMW 335i is a performance-oriented luxury sedan that, while offering an engaging driving experience, is not without its potential reliability concerns. The N54 engine, in particular, is known for issues with its high-pressure fuel pump, turbochargers, and fuel injectors, which can lead to significant repair costs. The N55 engine is generally more reliable but can still experience turbocharger wastegate rattle and injector issues. Transmission issues, particularly with the automatic, and suspension components like control arm bushings are also noted. Electrical gremlins, such as water pump failures and iDrive system glitches, can also arise. Despite these potential drawbacks, many owners find the driving pleasure of the 335i outweighs the risks, provided regular maintenance and prompt attention to emerging issues.
