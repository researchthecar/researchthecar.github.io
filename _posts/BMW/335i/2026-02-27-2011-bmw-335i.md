# 2011 BMW 335i

### Overview
The 2011 BMW 335i, part of the E90/E92/E93 generation, is renowned for its potent performance, particularly with its turbocharged inline-six engines. While offering an engaging driving experience, like many performance-oriented vehicles, it has specific areas that have seen more frequent attention from owners and technicians. This data focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related issues that have been commonly reported, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. The vehicle's complexity, especially in its powertrain and electronics, means that while generally well-built, certain components require diligent maintenance and awareness of potential failure points.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (N54)
**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Turbocharger Wastegate Rattle/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the engine bay, especially on deceleration; loss of boost pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger wastegates or the entire turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump (HPFP) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Stalling while driving, long crank times, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks, rough idle, increased oil consumption, whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve system.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, rattling noise from turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electronic wastegate actuator or turbocharger.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500

**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idle, check engine light, reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear (Accelerated)**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch kit replacement (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission (ZF 6HP19/26)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission slipping, failure to engage gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front or rear control arm bushings.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Strut Mount Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noises when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of strut mounts.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Water Pump Failure (Electric)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, check engine light, reduced engine performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat assembly.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

**Idrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the iDrive control unit/display.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights flickering or not turning on.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2011 BMW 335i offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury, but potential buyers should be aware of its specific reliability considerations. The twin-turbocharged inline-six engines, particularly the N54, are prone to issues with fuel injectors, the high-pressure fuel pump, and turbocharger components. The electric water pump is also a common failure point. Automatic transmissions can experience issues with the mechatronic unit and torque converter. While the suspension and interior are generally robust, some owners report accelerated wear on control arm bushings and strut mounts. Electrical gremlins, such as iDrive system glitches and headlight ballast failures, can also occur. Despite these potential concerns, many 335i models, when properly maintained, provide a rewarding ownership experience. Diligent pre-purchase inspections and a budget for potential repairs are highly recommended.
