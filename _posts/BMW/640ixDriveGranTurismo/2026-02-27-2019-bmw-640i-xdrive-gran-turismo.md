# 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Turismo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Turismo offers a luxurious blend of executive sedan comfort and hatchback versatility, powered by BMW's refined turbocharged inline-six engine. While owner complaints for this low-volume model are sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect complex electrical systems, advanced driver assistance features, and powertrain mounts during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as one of BMW's more robust and reliable modern powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed ZF automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid gear shifts paired with standard xDrive all-wheel drive. It is widely considered dependable with proper fluid maintenance.

**Starter Motor Mounting Bolt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A strange noise occurs when engaging the starter as the starter motor shifts out of position.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the starter mounting bolts, secure or replace them, and check the starter and flywheel for alignment damage.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety and driver-assistance systems provide cutting-edge protection, though sensor sensitivity can occasionally cause unexpected behavior.

**Abrupt Phantom Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle unexpectedly stops abruptly while driving on the freeway or city streets, accompanied by control panel obstruction warnings despite clear surroundings.
- **Solution:** Have the collision avoidance sensors, camera alignment, and software modules inspected and recalibrated by a certified technician.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Turismo is a sophisticated and capable grand tourer with very few reported owner complaints in early data. Buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection focusing on the starter assembly, drivetrain components, and advanced safety sensor calibration to ensure trouble-free ownership.
