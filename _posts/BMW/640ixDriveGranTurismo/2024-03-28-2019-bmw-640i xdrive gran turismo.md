## 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Turismo

The 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Turismo is a luxury sedan that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. It comes standard with a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine that produces 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 640i can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 5.3 seconds and has a top track speed of 155 mph.

The 640i has received positive reviews for its performance, handling, and interior. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

**Engine**

* **3.0L Turbocharged Inline-Six Engine**
    * **Oil Filter Housing Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil can be seen around the oil filter housing.
        * Solution: Replace the oil filter housing gasket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to slip and the engine to lose power.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift hard or jerkily, or it may refuse to shift gears altogether.
        * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arms**
    * **Ball Joint Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: The ball joints in the front lower control arms can wear out, causing the front end of the vehicle to feel loose or unstable.
        * Solution: Replace the lower control arms.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or investigate for parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Short**
    * Description/Symptoms: An electrical short can cause a variety of problems, including blown fuses, flickering lights, and inoperative accessories.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture can accumulate inside the headlights, causing them to fog up or become cloudy.
   * Solution: Replace the headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof can leak water into the vehicle's interior.
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seals or adjust the sunroof alignment.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not work properly, blowing hot or cold air instead of the desired temperature.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module or actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rearview Camera Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rearview camera may fail to display an image or may display a distorted image.
    * Solution: Replace the rearview camera.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 BMW 640i xDrive Gran Turismo is a well-rounded luxury sedan that offers a powerful engine, comfortable ride, and spacious interior. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior. The severity of the issues can range from minor annoyances to major problems that can affect the safety and reliability of the vehicle. The cost to fix these issues can also vary depending on the severity of the problem and the parts that need to be replaced. Overall, the 640i is a solid choice for luxury sedan buyers, but potential owners should be aware of the potential issues that they may encounter.
