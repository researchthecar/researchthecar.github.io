# 2017 BMW 328d Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used BMW 328d, buyers get access to a remarkably efficient turbodiesel luxury sedan that excels on long highway commutes. While these vehicles offer refined road manners and strong low-end torque, prospective owners should pay close attention to cooling system longevity, electrical accessories, and routine maintenance history. Being diligent about pre-purchase inspections helps catch potential component wear before it turns into an expensive shop visit.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Diesel 4-Cylinder (N47/B47)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder diesel engine delivers around 180 horsepower and robust torque, making it a favorite for highway efficiency. Routine fluid maintenance is critical to ensure long-term reliability for this diesel powerplant.

**Coolant Leaks and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note recurring coolant loss, low coolant warning messages, and potential engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspect hoses, radiator fittings, and the water pump for leaks; replace damaged seals or failed components immediately.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected engine hesitation, difficulty starting, or complete stalling while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery and electrical systems; replace failing fuel pumps or sensors as indicated by diagnostic trouble codes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This widely used 8-speed automatic transmission offers smooth, responsive shifts and pairs exceptionally well with the diesel engine's power band. It generally proves dependable when routine fluid services are maintained.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake Cable and Clips

The braking hardware provides strong stopping power, though minor hardware failures like retaining clips can require simple physical inspections.

**Parking Brake Cable Drooping**
- **Symptoms:** A grinding noise coming from the center-underside of the vehicle, caused by the parking brake cable contacting the driveshaft due to broken plastic clips.
- **Solution:** Inspect the underside retaining clips and replace broken plastic brackets to secure the parking brake cable away from the driveshaft.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Restraints

Safety systems generally protect occupants well, but restraint sensor warnings should be checked immediately during pre-purchase evaluations.

**Seat Belt Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty latching seat belts or intermittent warnings regarding the passenger restraint system.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt buckles and wiring harnesses; replace faulty latch mechanisms or sensors as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V755000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The melting intake manifold can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the EGR cooler as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V907000: Fuel System, Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The EGR cooler may leak internally, causing coolant to mix with diesel engine soot, which could result in smoldering particles and a melting intake manifold. A melting intake manifold increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the EGR cooler and inspect the intake manifold, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V586000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-pressure fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 328d remains an attractive choice for drivers seeking high fuel economy combined with luxury car handling. While owners have flagged issues relating to cooling components, parking brake retaining clips, and electrical sensors, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection can catch most common trouble spots early. Prospective buyers who verify proper maintenance records can look forward to a comfortable and efficient daily driver.
