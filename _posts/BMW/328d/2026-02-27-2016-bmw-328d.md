# 2016 BMW 328d Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 328d offers buyers an entry point into luxury diesel performance, combining robust low-end torque with excellent highway fuel economy. When shopping for a used model, prospective owners should carefully review service records and pay close attention to engine and electrical accessories, as owner reports highlight a few common mechanical and system quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbo Diesel 4-Cylinder (180 hp / 280 lb-ft)

The 2.0-liter TwinPower Turbo diesel 4-cylinder engine produces approximately 180 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque. It is generally praised for efficiency, though buyers should verify proper emissions and fuel system maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes while pairing with either rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It is widely regarded as one of the most reliable and responsive units in its class.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V755000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The melting intake manifold can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the EGR cooler as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V185000: Power Train:Driveline:Constant Velocity Joint](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The constant-velocity joint which transfers power between the transfer case and the rear drive shaft, may become damaged, possibly resulting in loss of power to the rear wheels. A loss of power to the rear wheels can affect vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the constant-velocity joint and inspect the transfer case, replacing it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V907000: Fuel System, Diesel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The EGR cooler may leak internally, causing coolant to mix with diesel engine soot, which could result in smoldering particles and a melting intake manifold. A melting intake manifold increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the EGR cooler and inspect the intake manifold, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V586000: Fuel System, Diesel:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=328d&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Fuel pump failure can cause an engine stall while driving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-pressure fuel pump, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, the 2016 BMW 328d is a compelling option for commuters seeking high fuel economy paired with luxury driving dynamics. While major mechanical failures are infrequent in owner reports, thorough pre-purchase inspections and verified maintenance histories remain essential for a confident used luxury car purchase.
