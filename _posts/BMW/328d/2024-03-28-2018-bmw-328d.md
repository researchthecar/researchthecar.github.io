**Introduction:**

The 2018 BMW 328d is a compact luxury sedan powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four diesel engine. It produces 180 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The 328d is praised for its fuel efficiency and performance, but it also has some reported issues.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four Diesel**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, decreased fuel efficiency, rough idling
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **EGR Valve Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced power, increased emissions, check engine light
    * Solution: Clean or replace EGR valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from exhaust, whistling noise
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering, slipping, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak on the bottom of the transmission, slipping gears, harsh shifting
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission fluid leak
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission not shifting properly, check engine light, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3000 - $4000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise, excessive vibration, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Struts/Shocks Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, excessive body roll, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace struts/shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery warning light, vehicle not starting
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Top not opening or closing properly, leaks, binding
    * Solution: Repair or replace convertible top
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Paint Scratches/Dents:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Not a manufacturing defect
    * Solution: Repair or replace panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks/Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard when driving
    * Solution: Add insulation or replacement dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, GPS not working properly
    * Solution: Update software or replace navigation system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2018 BMW 328d has some potential issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. While the severity of these issues can vary, they can be costly to repair. It is important to research and consider these issues before purchasing a 328d to ensure that you are prepared for any potential expenses.
