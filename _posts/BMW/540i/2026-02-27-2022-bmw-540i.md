# 2022 BMW 540i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 540i blends luxury and strong performance, but prospective used-car buyers should review owner-reported reliability trends. While owner feedback highlights advanced features and smooth driving dynamics, some drivers have logged concerns regarding electrical glitches, powertrain behavior, and sensor vulnerabilities. A thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended to ensure all systems and safety features function correctly.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces around 335 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque, offering robust and smooth acceleration. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more dependable modern powerplants, though proper maintenance remains essential.

### Climate Control and Accessories

Electrical and climate components generally operate smoothly, though occasional cabin accessory noises have been reported.

**High-Pitched AC Noise**
- **Symptoms:** A loud, high-pitched noise emanates from the vehicle whenever the heating or air conditioning system operates or when the engine reaches higher temperatures.
- **Solution:** Have the air conditioning compressor and associated climate control components inspected and replaced if worn or faulty.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless shifts and is paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It generally maintains a strong reputation for durability in daily driving conditions.

## Safety

### Driver Assistance Sensors

Advanced safety features enhance driver awareness, but sensor sensitivity can occasionally cause operational inconveniences.

**Faulty Parking and Collision Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior proximity sensors malfunction easily, resulting in false warnings or a complete failure of backup camera and parking assistance features.
- **Solution:** Inspect individual sensors for damage or alignment issues, and have faulty units recalibrated or replaced by a qualified technician.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 540i remains a compelling luxury sport sedan choice due to its refined inline-six performance and upscale cabin. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential sensor sensitivities and accessory noises, these issues do not overshadow the vehicle's overall appeal. Buyers should verify service history and perform a comprehensive inspection prior to purchase.
