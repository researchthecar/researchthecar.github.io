# 2020 BMW 540i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 540i blends executive luxury, impressive driving dynamics, and advanced technology. While it is generally a sophisticated and well-engineered luxury sedan, used car buyers should remain mindful of owner-reported issues involving powertrain components, electrical glitches, and rare safety concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 2020 BMW 540i features a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine (the B58) producing approximately 335 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant is widely praised for its smooth power delivery and robust overall durability.

**Oil Pump Component Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported potential concerns regarding plastic internal components within the oil pump, which could theoretically risk oil pressure drops.
- **Solution:** Inspect the engine and oil pressure system during routine maintenance; consult a certified technician if warning lights appear.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 540i utilizes a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission paired with either rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It is known for quick, smooth shifts and high reliability under normal operating conditions.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking components provide strong stopping power suited for a performance luxury sedan, though routine inspection of pads and lines is recommended.

**Brake Responsiveness Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Isolated driver complaints mention unexpected stopping hesitation or prolonged stopping distances in heavy traffic scenarios.
- **Solution:** Have the brake pads, rotors, and hydraulic fluid professionally inspected for wear or pressure loss.

## Interior

### Audio and Interior Electronics

Electrical architecture is advanced and feature-rich, though rare cabin water leaks and sensor brightness fluctuations have been noted by some owners.

**Speaker Water Incursion**
- **Symptoms:** Water drainage issues can lead to moisture accumulation in the front doors, potentially damaging optional premium midrange speakers.
- **Solution:** Check door seals and drainage paths during inspections, and replace damaged speaker components if water intrusion has occurred.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V598000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the reduced rearview image view can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, program the rearview camera with updated software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V046000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A broken output shaft can lead to a loss of drive power or a rollaway condition in a parked vehicle (if the parking brake is not engaged), increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the output shaft(s), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V598000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=540i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** During engine start, the engine management software may cause loss of braking assist. Loss of braking assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine management software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW 540i is a compelling choice for used-car shoppers seeking high performance and luxury comfort. Prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the engine bay, door seals, and electrical systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
