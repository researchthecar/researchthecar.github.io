# 2020 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe is a striking luxury four-door that blends high-end comfort with robust performance. While early owner feedback in public complaint logs is exceptionally sparse for this low-volume luxury model, buyers should still approach a pre-owned German grand tourer with standard diligence regarding its advanced electronics and turbocharged powertrain.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. Widely regarded as one of BMW's most reliable modern powerplants, it offers smooth power delivery and solid everyday durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed ZF automatic transmission handles gear selection, pairing quickly with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally celebrated for crisp, seamless shifting and dependable long-term operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V164000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The sensors in the front seat belt buckles may inaccurately detect a belted occupant as unbelted, possibly causing the air bag and seat belt pretensioners to deploy improperly in the event of a crash. If the air bag and/or seat belt pretensioners deploy improperly in the event of a crash, there is an increased risk of injury to the driver and front.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt buckles, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V576000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A starter motor that overheats can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the vehicle software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2020 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe presents a very clean slate in terms of documented owner complaints, supported by a world-class powertrain. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and maintenance history to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
