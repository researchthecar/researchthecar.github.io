# 2021 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe blends luxury, striking styling, and everyday performance into a striking four-door package. While it offers sophisticated powertrain technology and agile handling for its size, prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and remain mindful of the complex electronics and high-end maintenance typical of flagship German luxury grand tourers.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged I4

The 3.0L Turbocharged I4 option is not a standard configuration for this flagship model; the 840i is powered by BMW's acclaimed 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine producing around 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It delivers robust, smooth acceleration paired with respectable efficiency for a grand touring car, though routine maintenance remains vital for long-term health.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque, offering strong, smooth power delivery across the rev range. Known for its refinement and durability in modern BMW lineups, it serves as a highly capable and balanced powerplant for the all-wheel-drive Gran Coupe.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with the standard xDrive all-wheel-drive system to deliver rapid, seamless gear changes. It is widely regarded as one of the smoothest and most responsive automatic gearboxes on the market, contributing significantly to both performance and highway cruising comfort.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V761000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The connection between the 48-Volt battery and the starter-generator may not have been assembled properly, which can cause increased electrical resistance at the connections. Increased electrical resistance can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the connection between the 48-Volt battery and the starter-generator assembly, replacing it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
