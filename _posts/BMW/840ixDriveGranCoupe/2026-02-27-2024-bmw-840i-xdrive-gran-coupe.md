# 2024 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe blends luxury, striking design, and smooth performance into an executive four-door package. As a relatively new luxury model on the pre-owned market, it benefits from modern engineering and advanced technology features. Prospective used-car buyers should approach luxury performance vehicles with standard diligence regarding upkeep costs and specialized maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. Known as the B58, it is widely regarded by enthusiasts and mechanics for its robust build quality, smooth power delivery, and strong reliability profile relative to other modern luxury powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission utilizes a torque converter setup paired with xDrive all-wheel drive. It offers crisp, rapid gear changes and has demonstrated solid mechanical durability in various high-end BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
