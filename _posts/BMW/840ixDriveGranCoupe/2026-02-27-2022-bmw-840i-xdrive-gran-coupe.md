# 2022 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe blends striking four-door coupe styling with luxurious comfort and impressive performance. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern engineering and advanced technology features, making it an appealing option for used luxury car buyers seeking grand touring capability.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. Known widely as the B58, this powerplant is generally regarded by enthusiasts and mechanics as smooth, powerful, and notably reliable for a modern luxury vehicle.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed ZF automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup delivers rapid, seamless gear changes. It is widely praised for its durability and responsiveness in daily driving and spirited cruising alike.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe offers a refined driving experience backed by a robust powertrain. Buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service history, but the overall mechanical outlook for this luxury grand tourer remains favorable.
