**Introduction**

The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe is a four-door luxury sedan powered by a 3.0-liter inline-six engine producing 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It comes equipped with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 840i is praised for its sleek design, powerful engine, and well-equipped interior. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter Inline-Six**

No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

**Rough Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience rough or jerky shifts, especially during low-speed maneuvers.
Solution: Software updates or transmission repairs may be necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Adaptive Suspension**

**Leaking Shock Absorbers**
Description/Symptoms: The shock absorbers may leak fluid, resulting in a loss of suspension damping and a rough ride.
Solution: Shock absorber replacement is required.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+ per shock
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Infotainment System**

**Freezing or Black Screen**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze or display a black screen, rendering it unresponsive.
Solution: Software updates or hardware replacements may be necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Door Handles**

**Recessed Door Handle Buttons**
Description/Symptoms: The buttons on the door handles may become recessed, making it difficult to open the doors.
Solution: Replacement door handles are required.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per door
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control**

**Blower Motor Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The blower motor may fail, resulting in no airflow through the vents.
Solution: Blower motor replacement is required.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Panoramic Sunroof**

**Rattling or Wind Noise**
Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof may rattle or produce excessive wind noise while driving.
Solution: Adjustments or repairs to the sunroof mechanism may be necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2022 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe is a well-liked vehicle, it has some reported issues with its transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior components. The severity of the issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. Potential owners should be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
