# 2021 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2021 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe, equipped with the B58 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission, generally presents a strong reliability profile. This model benefits from BMW's mature powertrain technology and robust build quality. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the 840i xDrive Gran Coupe has demonstrated a commendable track record, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific component failures rather than widespread systemic problems. Owners generally report a satisfying ownership experience with this luxurious four-door coupe, appreciating its blend of performance, comfort, and advanced technology.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe is a reliable vehicle, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission are well-regarded for their durability. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, in some instances, premature battery failure. These are not typically indicative of a systemic flaw but rather isolated component issues that can occur in modern, technology-laden vehicles. Overall, the 840i xDrive Gran Coupe offers a dependable luxury driving experience with a low probability of major mechanical failures.
