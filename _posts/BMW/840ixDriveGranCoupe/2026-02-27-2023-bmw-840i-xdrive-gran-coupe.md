# 2023 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury vehicle featuring sophisticated engineering and advanced technology. As a relatively new luxury model on the pre-owned market, prospective buyers generally evaluate its robust powertrain options and overall build quality while keeping an eye on complex electronics and standard maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth power delivery. This powerplant is widely regarded as one of BMW's more reliable modern offerings for daily driving and grand touring.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup offers rapid, seamless shifts and optimized gearing. It is generally robust and well-matched to the inline-six engine's power band.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW 840i xDrive Gran Coupe combines striking design with strong performance capabilities. Buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, verify service records, and ensure all electronic systems operate as intended during a pre-purchase inspection.
