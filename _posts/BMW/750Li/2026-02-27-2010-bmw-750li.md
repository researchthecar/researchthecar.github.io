# 2010 BMW 750Li

### Overview
The 2010 BMW 750Li, a flagship luxury sedan, offers a potent blend of performance and advanced technology. Powered by a robust V8 engine and featuring a sophisticated suspension system, it aims for a refined driving experience. However, like many high-performance luxury vehicles of its era, it can present specific reliability concerns that owners should be aware of. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas and their associated costs to help prospective and current owners make informed decisions.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (N63)
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, increased oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust, turbo warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbochargers, often recommended to replace both.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $6,000

**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, misfires, check engine light, poor fuel economy.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Valve Stem Seal Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive blue smoke on startup or deceleration, oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals, often requiring engine disassembly.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,500

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks from hoses or radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, radiator, water pump, or thermostat.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6HP26)
**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, transmission warning light, inability to select gears, limp mode.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Active Roll Stabilization (ARS) Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll, warning lights related to suspension, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of ARS hydraulic pump, actuators, or related sensors.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation errors, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of the iDrive controller or head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent issues with lights, windows, locks, or other electronic accessories.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,300


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical gremlins.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential damage to electronics.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drains.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air conditioning or heat, or intermittent fan operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor and resistor.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2010 BMW 750Li is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but its advanced technology and high-performance engine come with potential reliability challenges. The 4.4L twin-turbo V8 is prone to issues like turbocharger failure, fuel injector problems, and valve stem seal degradation, which can be costly to repair. The transmission, while generally robust, can suffer from mechatronic unit or torque converter failures. Electrical systems, particularly the iDrive infotainment and body control modules, can also experience glitches. Owners should budget for potential repairs, especially concerning the engine and complex electronic systems. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for mitigating these issues and maintaining the vehicle's performance and longevity.
