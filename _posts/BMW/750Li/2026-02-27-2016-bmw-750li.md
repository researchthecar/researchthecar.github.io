# 2016 BMW 750Li Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a 2016 BMW 750Li requires careful attention to its advanced luxury systems and high-performance powertrain options. While formal complaint reports for this model year are limited, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history records, particularly concerning historical recall campaigns and complex electronics.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust power, generating approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. While offering effortless acceleration, it is a complex powerplant that demands diligent maintenance history to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the V8 engine, offering quick and refined gear shifts. It is generally robust, though routine fluid checks are recommended for high-mileage examples.

## Safety

### Airbags

Safety equipment on the flagship sedan includes comprehensive airbag systems, though historical recall campaigns require verification during purchase.

**Takata Airbag Inflator Recall Status**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may receive recall notices regarding inflator components with delayed part availability for necessary repairs.
- **Solution:** Contact an authorized BMW dealership to verify if the specific vehicle has had all applicable Takata airbag recalls completed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V173000: Air Bags:Frontal:Sensor/Control Module-Inactive](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=750Li&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have an air bag control module that may randomly reset due to an electrical short circuit as a result of residual metallic particles from the manufacturing process. In the event of a crash while the air bag module is resetting, the air bags may not deploy, increasing the risk of occupant injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 750Li delivers exceptional luxury and performance, but its sophisticated engineering makes a pre-purchase inspection and verified service history essential. Buyers should verify that all safety recalls, particularly regarding airbag components, have been properly addressed by a certified dealer before finalizing a purchase.
