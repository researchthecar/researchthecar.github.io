# 2016 BMW 750Li

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 750Li, a flagship luxury sedan, generally offers a high level of refinement and technology. As with many high-performance vehicles, its complexity can sometimes lead to specific areas requiring attention. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance. While the powertrain is robust, some electrical and suspension components have shown a propensity for issues, particularly as the vehicle ages. Owners should be aware of these potential areas to ensure continued optimal performance and longevity of this sophisticated automobile. The interior and exterior generally hold up well, reflecting the premium build quality expected from BMW.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63TU)
**Turbocharger Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke from exhaust, oil smell, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger seals and lines, potential turbocharger replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced acceleration.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils and spark plugs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Fuel Injector Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, engine misfires, poor fuel economy, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200

## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, "air suspension malfunction" warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Active Roll Stabilization Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll during cornering, "suspension malfunction" warning.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of active anti-roll bar components.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (iDrive)
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or replacement of control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 750Li is a luxurious and powerful sedan, but its advanced technology and performance can lead to specific reliability concerns. The 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 engine, while potent, is susceptible to issues like turbocharger oil leaks and ignition coil failures, which can be costly to repair. The sophisticated suspension system, particularly the adaptive drive components, may require attention due to potential failures in the air suspension compressor or active roll stabilization. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and parasitic battery drain, are also noted. Despite these potential issues, the transmission and the interior/exterior generally exhibit good reliability. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for owners of the 2016 750Li.
