# 2019 BMW 750Li Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW 750Li represents the pinnacle of luxury, offering advanced technology, supreme comfort, and a powerful engine. When shopping for a used luxury flagship like this, buyers should carefully evaluate the vehicle's powertrain options and monitor for complex electronic or mechanical issues typical of high-end German sedans.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (N63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 445 to 523 horsepower depending on the specific model year tune and offers robust performance. Buyers should ensure thorough maintenance records are available, as high-performance turbocharged engines require meticulous upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid gear shifts while optimizing fuel economy across various driving modes. It is generally regarded as a durable unit when serviced properly at recommended intervals.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=750Li&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 750Li delivers exceptional luxury and performance, but prospective buyers should approach with a healthy awareness of potential high maintenance costs. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist is strongly recommended before finalizing any purchase.
