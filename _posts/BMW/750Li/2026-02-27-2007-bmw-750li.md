# 2007 BMW 750Li

### Overview
The 2007 BMW 750Li, a flagship luxury sedan, offers a potent combination of performance and comfort. Powered by a robust V8 engine and sophisticated drivetrain, it aims for a premium driving experience. However, like many high-performance luxury vehicles of its era, it can present specific reliability concerns that owners should be aware of. While generally well-engineered, certain components, particularly those related to its advanced technology and powertrain, may require attention over time. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential repair areas and associated costs for the 2007 750Li.

---


## Engine

### 4.8L V8 (N62B48)
**Valve Stem Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke on startup or deceleration, oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks from hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking components (water pump, thermostat, hoses, radiator).
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

**Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks visible from the bottom of the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-speed Automatic (GM 6L45/6L50)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or cooler lines, potential for slipping gears or harsh shifting.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals, pan gasket, or cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, error codes related to transmission control.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission solenoid pack.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,100


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Active Roll Stabilization (ARS) Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Fluid leaks from hydraulic lines or actuators, potential for reduced handling stability.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking ARS components.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, 'air suspension malfunction' warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,500


## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of iDrive controller or head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical components malfunctioning intermittently (lights, windows, locks, sensors).
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 BMW 750Li is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but its advanced technology and high-performance V8 engine can lead to specific reliability concerns. Common issues include leaks from valve stem seals and oil pan gaskets, as well as potential problems with the cooling system. The transmission, while generally robust, can experience leaks or solenoid failures. The sophisticated suspension and electrical systems, particularly the iDrive and adaptive drive components, are also areas where owners may encounter issues requiring costly repairs. Regular and thorough maintenance is crucial for mitigating these potential problems. Prospective buyers should be prepared for higher maintenance and repair costs compared to less complex vehicles.
