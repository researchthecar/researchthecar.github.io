# 2016 BMW 228i xDrive

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 228i xDrive, part of the F22 generation, is generally regarded as a reliable performance coupe. It combines sporty handling with a premium interior and the added security of all-wheel drive. While most owners report a positive experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from a reliability standpoint. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20/B48)
**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential stalling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Water Pump Leaks/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, low coolant level warning, visible corrosion around the pump.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat assembly.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, potential module reset or replacement.
- **Cost:** $100 - $600

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery, warning lights on dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty module or battery.
- **Cost:** $200 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 228i xDrive is a solid choice for those seeking a blend of performance and practicality. Its powertrain, particularly the 8-speed automatic transmission, is robust. The primary areas to monitor are the engine's PCV system and water pump, which can develop issues over time. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are also noted but often resolvable with software updates or targeted repairs. Overall, with proactive maintenance and attention to these specific components, the 228i xDrive offers a reliable and enjoyable driving experience.
