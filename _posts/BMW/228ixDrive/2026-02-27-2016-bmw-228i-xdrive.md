# 2016 BMW 228i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 228i xDrive combines sporty handling, standard all-wheel drive, and punchy turbocharged performance into a compact luxury coupe. For used car shoppers, understanding the powertrain specifications and keeping an eye on routine maintenance items is key to a satisfying ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and solid fuel economy, though buyers should verify regular oil change history and monitor timing chain guides as the vehicle ages.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with ZF

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid shifts and pairs well with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust when maintained properly with periodic fluid service.
