**Introduction**

The 2016 BMW M235i xDrive is a high-performance sport compact car that boasts a powerful engine and excellent handling. It comes with a 3.0-liter inline-six engine that produces 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. Drivers appreciate its agile performance and luxurious interior, but some have reported various issues.

**Engine**

**3.0-liter Inline-Six**

- **Oil Leak from Oil Pan Gasket:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Engine oil leaks from the oil pan gasket, causing low oil levels.
  - Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $250-$400
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner fails, leading to rattling noises, engine misfires, and reduced performance.
  - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000-$1,500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

- **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaks from the transmission housing, resulting in slipping gears and difficulty shifting.
  - Solution: Replace the transmission housing gasket.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500
  - Recalls: None Found

- **TCM Module Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The transmission control module (TCM) fails, causing transmission issues such as delayed shifting or sudden gear changes.
  - Solution: Replace the TCM module.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Rear Suspension Bushing Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension bushings wear out prematurely, causing excessive noise and vibration.
  - Solution: Replace the rear suspension bushings.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300-$500
  - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery Discharge:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle.
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair the underlying electrical issue.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $50-$300
  - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Headlight condensation:**
  - Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlight housing.
  - Solution: Replace the headlight assembly.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000
  - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Navigation System Malfunction:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The navigation system glitches or malfunctions, failing to provide accurate directions.
  - Solution: Update the navigation software or replace the navigation unit.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Power Window Switch Failure:**
  - Description/Symptoms: The power window switch fails to operate properly, causing windows to become stuck open or closed.
  - Solution: Replace the power window switch.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100-$200
  - Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 BMW M235i xDrive is a performance-oriented vehicle with some reported issues. The most severe and costly issues include timing chain tensioner failure and transmission fluid leaks. Regular maintenance and repairs can help mitigate these problems, but owners should be aware of potential expenses.
