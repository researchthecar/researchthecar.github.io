# 2020 BMW M235i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M235i xDrive delivers engaging performance and daily drivability through its upscale compact footprint. Prospective used-car buyers looking at this sporty luxury model will find a well-appointed machine, though keeping a close eye on maintenance history remains essential for a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 301 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, paired with standard all-wheel drive. It offers brisk acceleration and solid day-to-day efficiency when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and power distribution suited to both spirited driving and commuting. Overall reliability is generally strong with routine fluid checks.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V091000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M235i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." If the sunroof continues to close after the vehicle is shut off, it may increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the sunroof electronic coding software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M235i xDrive stands out as a rewarding, high-performing luxury compact car for enthusiasts. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical health before finalizing a deal.
