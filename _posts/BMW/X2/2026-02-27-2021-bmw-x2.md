# 2021 BMW X2 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW X2 offers a sporty and distinctive entry into the luxury subcompact SUV segment, pairing sharp handling with responsive powertrain options. For used-car buyers, looking into this model means balancing its engaging driving dynamics and upscale interior against typical luxury maintenance expectations. Reviewing known powertrain specifications and monitoring common owner-reported areas helps ensure a well-informed purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (sDrive28i / xDrive28i)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving while maintaining reasonable efficiency, and it generally offers solid mechanical reliability when properly maintained.

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (M35i)

This high-output 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine churns out roughly 302 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque for the performance-oriented M35i trim. It delivers aggressive acceleration and a sportier exhaust note, though buyers should expect higher overall running costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission

Paired with the higher-output M35i performance model, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and robust gear management. It is generally well-regarded for durability under spirited driving conditions.

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Featured on the sDrive28i and xDrive28i models, this 8-speed automatic unit offers paddle shifters and responsive gear changes tailored for efficiency and performance. Regular fluid inspections help ensure long-term smooth operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During a crash, a seat belt retractor that does not function as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the seat belt retractors (including the seat belts) and, if necessary, replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW X2 stands out as a stylish, fun-to-drive luxury crossover with dependable powertrain options. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with complete service records and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and cosmetic condition.
