# 2022 BMW X2 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW X2 combines sporty handling with coupe-like styling in a compact luxury package. As a relatively recent model, buyer feedback and complaints remain sparse, allowing shoppers to focus on standard maintenance and thorough pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (sDrive28i / xDrive28i)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving while maintaining respectable fuel efficiency across both front-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations.

### 2.0L TwinPower Turbocharged Inline-4 (M35i)

The high-output 2.0-liter turbocharged engine in the M35i delivers around 302 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque for a much sportier driving dynamic. It offers robust performance but requires diligent adherence to maintenance schedules.

### Fuel / Propulsion System

Manages fuel delivery and propulsion oversight, supporting overall powertrain operation.

**Drivetrain Malfunction / Limp Mode**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an unexpected drivetrain malfunction warning on the dashboard, placing the vehicle into a reduced-power 'limp' mode and limiting acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle computer for stored diagnostic trouble codes to pinpoint the faulty sensor, fuel component, or software glitch.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission

This conventional 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with higher-output models, offering smooth and rapid gear changes. It is generally regarded as robust and well-matched to the vehicle's power delivery.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

Found on select variants, this 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers snappy gear shifts and efficient power transfer. Drivers should expect a slightly more mechanical feel during low-speed maneuvering compared to torque-converter automatics.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW X2 is generally well-regarded for its peppy performance and engaging driving dynamics. While isolated powertrain complaints exist, overall vehicle reliability appears stable. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify service history.
