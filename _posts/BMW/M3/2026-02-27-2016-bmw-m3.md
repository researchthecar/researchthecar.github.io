# 2016 BMW M3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW M3 delivers thrilling performance powered by its iconic twin-turbocharged inline-six engine, offering sharp handling and track-ready capabilities in a practical four-door sedan package. While widely praised for its driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review owner-reported mechanical tendencies and maintenance history carefully to avoid unexpected ownership costs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (S55)

The 3.0-liter S55 twin-turbocharged inline-six produces around 425 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque, providing explosive acceleration. It is a high-performance powerplant that requires diligent maintenance and careful monitoring for enthusiast-specific mechanical quirks.

**Crank Hub Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that the engine crank hub can slip or spin out of alignment, potentially leading to catastrophic engine timing failure and leaving the vehicle disabled.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection, retiming, or installation of an aftermarket pinned/keyed crank hub capture solution.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes tailored for both spirited driving and daily commuting. It generally offers robust performance when serviced according to factory recommendations.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers a direct, engaging connection for purists seeking traditional three-pedal performance. It is a durable unit well-suited for high-horsepower applications.

### Suspension and Drivetrain Mounts

Components related to ride quality and handling stability that may experience premature wear under aggressive driving conditions.

**Knocking Noises and Drivetrain Vibrations**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear knocking sounds from the front or side when driving over bumps, accompanied by unusual vibrations during acceleration and braking.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension bushings, control arms, and differential mountings for wear, replacing damaged components as necessary.

### Rear Axle and Differential

Manages power delivery to the rear wheels, requiring periodic fluid services to maintain smooth operation.

**Rear Axle Clunking and Jolting**
- **Symptoms:** Some drivers notice a distinct clunking or jolting sensation emanating from the rear axle area during low-speed maneuvers or gear engagement.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the differential bushings, fluid condition, and axle shafts to determine if component play or wear is present.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V713000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A loss of vehicle propulsion can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify vehicle owners, and dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V653000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Reuse of the existing bolts may cause the bolts to loosen. Loose rear sub-frame bolts may adversely affect vehicle handling and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear sub-frame bolts with new bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V782000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M3&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have a driveshaft with a slip-joint that may not have been filled with grease. If the slip-joint fails, there would be a loss of power to the rear axle and therefore the vehicle would stop moving, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the driveshaft, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW M3 is an exceptional performance sedan, but its high-strung nature means buyers must prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an independent specialist. Pay close attention to documented maintenance records and watch out for enthusiast-reported mechanical concerns such as crank hub integrity before finalizing a purchase.
