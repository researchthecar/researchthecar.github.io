# 2017 BMW M3 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2017 BMW M3 offers an exhilarating driving experience paired with high-performance engineering. Buyers should review owner-reported feedback regarding early electrical glitches and drivetrain noises to ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged I6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 425 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque. It delivers thrilling acceleration and robust track capability while requiring diligent maintenance. Fuel system components should be carefully inspected during routine ownership.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear shifts tailored for high-performance driving. Routine fluid changes are critical to maintaining smooth operation over high mileage.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission provides an engaging, driver-focused experience for enthusiasts. Proper clutch maintenance is essential for longevity under aggressive driving conditions.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical complaints are rare, but unexpected battery or power distribution failures can occasionally disable the vehicle.

**Complete Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start and exhibits a total lack of power to all onboard electronics.
- **Solution:** Inspect battery connections, main power distribution fuses, and the starter wiring harness.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V713000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M3&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A loss of vehicle propulsion can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify vehicle owners, and dealers will replace the driveshaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V653000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M3&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Reuse of the existing bolts may cause the bolts to loosen. Loose rear sub-frame bolts may adversely affect vehicle handling and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the rear sub-frame bolts with new bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW M3 is a powerhouse sports sedan that rewards keen drivers with exceptional performance. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive PPI focusing on the electrical system and drivetrain components.
