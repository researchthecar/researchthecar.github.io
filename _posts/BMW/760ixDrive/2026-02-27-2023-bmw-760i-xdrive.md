# 2023 BMW 760i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 760i xDrive represents the pinnacle of the brand's luxury sedan lineup, offering executive-level comfort, cutting-edge technology, and effortless performance. As a relatively new model in the used marketplace, prospective buyers generally benefit from modern engineering and advanced driver-assistance systems. While owner-reported complaints are sparse given its modern vintage, reviewing powertrain specifications and keeping an eye on complex onboard electronics remains a wise strategy for shoppers.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (S68)

This robust 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 536 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 48V mild-hybrid system. It offers immense power and refined operation, though ongoing maintenance costs typical of high-performance luxury German engines should be anticipated by used buyers.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission channeling power to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, it delivers seamless gear shifts and optimizes power delivery across driving conditions.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW 760i xDrive is an exceptionally engineered luxury flagship that generally provides a trouble-free initial ownership experience.
