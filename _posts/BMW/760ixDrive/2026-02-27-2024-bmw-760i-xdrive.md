# 2024 BMW 760i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 760i xDrive represents the pinnacle of the brand's luxury flagship lineup, blending ultra-smooth performance with cutting-edge cabin technology. As a relatively new model year with a high-end powertrain, prospective buyers generally enjoy a sophisticated driving experience backed by modern engineering. While owner reports to safety regulators are minimal for this production run, buyers should still carefully inspect complex electronics and powertrain systems during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (S68)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine features mild-hybrid technology, producing around 536 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. It delivers effortless acceleration and refined power delivery suited for a luxury flagship.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission pairs with the standard xDrive all-wheel-drive system for seamless gear changes. It provides responsive shifting dynamics that match the high-output V8 engine.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V714000: Steering:Linkages:Knuckle:Spindle:Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=760i+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A sudden increase in required steering effort increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering spindle's double universal joint, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V739000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=760i+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V422000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Electric:Control Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=760i+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.
