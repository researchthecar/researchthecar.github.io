## 2017 BMW Alpina B6 xDrive Gran Coupe

The 2017 BMW Alpina B6 xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury sedan that combines the performance of a sports car with the comfort and practicality of a four-door sedan. It is powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 600 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to all four wheels. The B6 xDrive Gran Coupe has a 0-60 mph time of 3.6 seconds and a top speed of 205 mph.

Despite its impressive performance, the B6 xDrive Gran Coupe has received some complaints from owners. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific issues people are having with the vehicles:

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbochargers can fail prematurely, causing a loss of power and performance. The symptoms include a lack of boost, reduced engine power, and increased exhaust smoke. The solution is to replace the failed turbocharger. The estimated cost to fix is $2,000 - $3,000+.
* **Fuel injector failure:** The fuel injectors can fail, causing a loss of power and performance. The symptoms include a rough idle, hesitation during acceleration, and increased fuel consumption. The solution is to replace the failed fuel injectors. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,000+.

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems:** The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, causing a loss of power and performance. The symptoms include slipping gears, delayed shifts, and harsh shifts. The solution is to rebuild or replace the transmission. The estimated cost to fix is $2,500 - $5,000+.

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start. The symptoms include a weak battery, dim headlights, and a loss of power. The solution is to replace the battery. The estimated cost to fix is $200 - $400+.
* **Electrical system failure:** The electrical system may fail, causing a loss of power and performance. The symptoms include a loss of power, flickering lights, and a dead battery. The solution is to diagnose and repair the failed electrical component. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $2,000+.

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system glitches:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, causing it to crash, freeze, or restart. The symptoms include a black screen, a loss of audio, and a loss of navigation functionality. The solution is to update the infotainment software. The estimated cost to fix is $0 - $500+.
* **Climate control problems:** The climate control system may not function properly, causing the interior of the vehicle to become too hot or too cold. The symptoms include a lack of heat or air conditioning, uneven temperatures, and a noisy blower motor. The solution is to diagnose and repair the failed climate control component. The estimated cost to fix is $500 - $1,500+.

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 BMW Alpina B6 xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury sedan that offers an impressive driving experience. However, it has received some complaints from owners regarding engine and transmission issues. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost to fix can be expensive.
