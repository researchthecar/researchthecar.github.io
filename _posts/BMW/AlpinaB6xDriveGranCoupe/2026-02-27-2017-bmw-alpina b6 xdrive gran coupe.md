# 2017 BMW Alpina B6 xDrive Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2017 BMW Alpina B6 xDrive Gran Coupe represents a pinnacle of performance and luxury, blending potent Alpina-tuned V8 power with the practicality of a four-door coupe. As a high-performance variant of the 6 Series Gran Coupe, it benefits from extensive engineering enhancements by Alpina. Reliability data for such exclusive models is often less voluminous than for mainstream vehicles. However, based on available owner feedback and technical assessments, the B6 xDrive generally exhibits strong build quality and mechanical integrity, reflecting its premium positioning.
