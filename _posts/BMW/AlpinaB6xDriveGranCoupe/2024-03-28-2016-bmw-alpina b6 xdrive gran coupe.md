**Introduction:**

The 2016 BMW Alpina B6 xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine delivering 540 horsepower and 538 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The B6 Gran Coupe is praised for its muscular engine, luxurious interior, and agile handling. However, it also faces some reliability concerns.

**Engine:**

* **N63B44TU 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** Loss of power, engine warning light, increased oil consumption
        * Replace turbochargers
        * $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
    * **Valve Stem Seal Wear:** Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from exhaust
        * Replace valve stem seals
        * $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission:**

* **8-Speed ZF 8HP70**
    * **Mechatronic Sleeve Failure:** Transmission shuddering, harsh shifts, loss of gears
        * Replace mechatronic sleeve
        * $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
        * Diagnose and repair electrical drain
        * $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **iDrive Malfunction:** Screen freezes, system unresponsive
        * Software update or hardware replacement
        * $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior:**

* **Door Handle Failure:** Handles become loose or stuck
        * Adjust or replace door handles
        * $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: N/A

**Interior:**

* **Infotainment Screen Glare:** Screen difficult to see in bright conditions
        * Install anti-glare screen protector
        * $100 - $200
        * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2016 BMW Alpina B6 xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury vehicle with known issues in its engine, transmission, electrical system, and exterior. The engine and transmission issues can be significant and expensive to repair. While the interior and suspension have no major issues, minor concerns can still arise. Overall, the B6 Gran Coupe's reliability is a concern, and potential buyers should consider its potential maintenance costs before purchasing.
