# 2022 BMW 430i xDrive Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2022 BMW 430i xDrive Gran Coupe, equipped with the B48 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine and an 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission, generally exhibits strong reliability. This model benefits from BMW's extensive engineering and rigorous testing. While no vehicle is entirely immune to potential issues, the 430i xDrive Gran Coupe has demonstrated a solid track record, particularly concerning its powertrain. Owners can expect a premium driving experience with a focus on performance and comfort, supported by a robust mechanical foundation. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring long-term dependability, but significant widespread mechanical or electrical faults are uncommon for this model year.
