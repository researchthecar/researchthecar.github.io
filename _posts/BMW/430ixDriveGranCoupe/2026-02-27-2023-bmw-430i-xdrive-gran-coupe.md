# 2023 BMW 430i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 430i xDrive Gran Coupe offers a stylish blend of utility, luxury, and all-weather capability, powered by BMW's well-regarded turbocharged four-cylinder engine. As a relatively recent model year, it typically benefits from modern tech features and refined driving dynamics, though used car buyers should still inspect the vehicle thoroughly for any early mechanical or electrical anomalies.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (255 hp / 295 lb-ft)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration while maintaining decent efficiency. It generally offers solid long-term dependability when routine maintenance schedules are closely followed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

Paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, this 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, rapid shifts and seamless power delivery. It is widely considered one of the most reliable and responsive automatic units on the market.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
