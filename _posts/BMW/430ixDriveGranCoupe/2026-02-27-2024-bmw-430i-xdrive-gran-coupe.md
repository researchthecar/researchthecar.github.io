# 2024 BMW 430i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 430i xDrive Gran Coupe blends sleek four-door coupe styling with standard all-wheel drive and BMW's refined driving dynamics. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern engineering and advanced technology features. Prospective buyers looking at a used 2024 model will want to evaluate overall condition and remaining factory warranty coverage.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque. It offers a responsive and efficient driving experience suitable for daily commuting and spirited driving alike.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid gear changes paired with standard xDrive all-wheel drive. It is widely regarded as one of the most refined and reliable automatic gearboxes in its class.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V863000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as a hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the cylinder head cover as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 430i xDrive Gran Coupe is a stylish and capable luxury vehicle with a strong powertrain setup. Because it is a late-model used car, thorough inspection and verification of service history are recommended prior to purchase.
