# 2012 BMW Alpina B7 xDrive

### Overview
The 2012 BMW Alpina B7 xDrive, a high-performance luxury sedan, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, especially considering its potent powertrain and advanced technology. Built upon the robust BMW 7 Series platform and enhanced by Alpina's engineering expertise, the B7 xDrive offers a compelling blend of power, comfort, and handling. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the B7 xDrive demonstrates a solid track record for its core mechanical components. Owners typically report satisfaction with its performance and build quality, with most maintenance falling into expected service intervals for a vehicle of this caliber. However, as with any complex luxury car, potential buyers should be aware of the higher costs associated with specialized parts and labor.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Air Suspension Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running frequently.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air strut or air spring. Seal replacement for compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Active Roll Stabilization Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights on the dashboard, reduced handling stability, clunking noises during cornering.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of active anti-roll bar components or hydraulic lines.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000+


## Electrical

### Infotainment System (iDrive)
**iDrive Controller Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Unresponsive controller, freezing screen, intermittent functionality.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of the iDrive controller unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical gremlins (lights flickering, windows not working).
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, battery replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 BMW Alpina B7 xDrive is a remarkably well-engineered vehicle with a strong reputation for reliability in its core powertrain and drivetrain. The V8 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and capable of high mileage with proper maintenance. However, as a complex luxury performance sedan, it is not without potential issues, particularly in its advanced suspension and electrical systems. Owners should anticipate potential costs related to air suspension components and the sophisticated iDrive system. While major engine or transmission failures are uncommon, proactive maintenance and awareness of these specific areas can ensure a more predictable ownership experience. Overall, it offers good reliability for its class, but maintenance and repair costs will be higher than average.
