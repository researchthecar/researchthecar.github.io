# 2013 BMW Alpina B7 xDrive

### Overview
The 2013 BMW Alpina B7 xDrive, a high-performance luxury sedan, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting its premium engineering and build quality. As a specialized model, it benefits from Alpina's enhancements to BMW's already robust platform. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the B7 xDrive has a reputation for solid mechanicals and sophisticated systems. Owners typically report satisfaction with its performance and long-term durability, though as with any complex luxury vehicle, potential owners should be aware of the costs associated with specialized maintenance and potential repairs, particularly concerning its advanced powertrain and electronics.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Active Roll Stabilization Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights on the dashboard, noticeable body roll during cornering, or stiffness in ride quality.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty hydraulic actuators, pumps, or control modules.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**iDrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or errors with navigation, audio, or connectivity features.
- **Solution:** Software updates, rebooting the system, or in some cases, replacement of the head unit or related modules.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


### Sensors and Modules
**Parking Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts or failure of parking sensors to detect obstacles.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of individual faulty sensors.

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 BMW Alpina B7 xDrive is a highly reliable luxury performance sedan. Its core powertrain components, the 4.4L twin-turbo V8 engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission, are exceptionally robust and rarely present significant issues. The primary areas where owners might encounter problems are related to its advanced electronic systems, such as the iDrive infotainment and the Adaptive Drive suspension. These can sometimes experience glitches or component failures, leading to repair costs typical of high-end luxury vehicles. Overall, with proper maintenance, the B7 xDrive offers a dependable and exhilarating ownership experience, with most issues being isolated rather than widespread.
