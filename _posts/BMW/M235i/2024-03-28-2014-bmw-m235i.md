**Introduction**

The 2014 BMW M235i is a high-performance coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-6 engine that produces 322 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque. The M235i is available with a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission.

The M235i has been praised for its agile handling, responsive steering, and powerful engine. However, it has also been criticized for its firm ride, lack of rear-seat space, and high price.

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Engine**

* **Intake Valve Deposits**
    * Description/Symptoms: Over time, carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, which can lead to decreased engine performance and fuel economy. The symptoms of intake valve deposits may include rough idling, hesitation on acceleration, and decreased power.
    * Solution: The intake valves must be cleaned to remove the carbon deposits.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 250 - 350+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger can fail due to a variety of reasons, including oil starvation, excessive heat, or a faulty wastegate. The symptoms of turbocharger failure may include loss of power, blue smoke from the exhaust, and a whining noise.
    * Solution: The turbocharger must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 900 - 1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty Shifting Gears**
    * Description/Symptoms: The driver may experience difficulty shifting gears, especially into reverse.
    * Solution: The transmission may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 1000 - 1800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may leak fluid, which can cause the transmission to slip or fail.
    * Solution: The transmission fluid leak must be repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 400 - 600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift gears erratically or refuse to shift gears altogether.
    * Solution: The transmission may need to be reprogrammed or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 500 - 1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front suspension may make a clunking or rattling noise when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: The front suspension components, such as the control arms or bushings, may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 400 - 800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: The battery may need to be replaced or the electrical system may need to be inspected for a parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 200 - 400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Faults**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience electrical faults, such as flickering lights, malfunctioning gauges, or dead batteries.
    * Solution: The electrical system may need to be inspected and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 200 - 600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: The convertible top may not open or close properly, or it may leak water.
    * Solution: The convertible top may need to be repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 800 - 1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Gaps**
    * Description/Symptoms: There may be gaps between the dashboard panels, which can make the interior look cheap.
    * Solution: The dashboard panels may need to be adjusted or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: 200 - 400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 BMW M235i is a high-performance coupe that offers a thrilling driving experience. However, it has been known to have some issues, including intake valve deposits, turbocharger failure, difficulty shifting gears, transmission fluid leaks, and electrical faults. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can be significant. Overall, the M235i is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues that it may have.
