# 2023 BMW M340i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M340i delivers thrilling performance and everyday refinement, anchored by one of the industry's most celebrated modern powertrains. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from mature engineering, though used buyers should still carefully evaluate any vehicle's history.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The B58 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its robust build quality, impressive power delivery, and strong overall reliability profile.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, smooth shifts and is paired with either rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It has proven to be mechanically durable in demanding performance applications.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M340i stands out as a high-performance sport sedan with a strong reputation for powertrain durability. Prospective buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify overall condition.
