# 2022 BMW M340i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 BMW M340i, buyers will find a performance-focused sport sedan renowned for its smooth inline-six power and engaging driving dynamics. While owner reports to NHTSA are relatively sparse, they do highlight a few electrical and mechanical areas that warrant a careful pre-purchase inspection. Knowing the available engine and drivetrain configurations helps ensure you select the right model for your driving needs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six (B58) engine produces roughly 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its robust performance and strong reliability track record, though standard cooling and starting components should be checked.

**Water Pump and Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss leading to potential engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system for leaks and replace the failing water pump or reservoir as needed.

**Starter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle takes multiple attempts to crank, with electrical systems powering on but the ignition failing to engage.
- **Solution:** Test the starting system and replace the faulty starter motor.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, rapid shifts across both rear-wheel-drive and xDrive all-wheel-drive configurations. It is generally very reliable in daily driving and spirited use alike.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Issues with steering assist are uncommon but reported by some owners.

**Intermittent Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A power steering failure warning message appears on the dash, and the steering wheel becomes heavy and difficult to turn.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic steering system scanned for diagnostic trouble codes and update software or replace failing modules if necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag Wiring

Airbag and restraint systems generally function as intended, though occasional wiring faults can trigger warning lights.

**Passenger Restraint System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard displays a restraint system warning due to a short in the wiring harness.
- **Solution:** Inspect the airbag wiring harness and repair or replace the affected components at an authorized facility.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW M340i offers an exceptional blend of luxury and sports-sedan performance. While isolated owner reports note issues involving the starter, cooling system, and electrical harnesses, these do not indicate widespread systemic flaws. Buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection before purchase to catch any hidden leaks or electrical glitches.
