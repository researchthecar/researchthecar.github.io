# 2021 BMW M340i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used BMW M340i, buyers generally find a thrilling, high-performance sport sedan centered around a powerful inline-six engine and refined transmission options. While owner reports to federal regulators highlight a few mechanical and electrical areas to inspect closely during a test drive, the vehicle maintains a strong reputation for performance and luxury.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58TU)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six (B58TU) engine delivers approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and impressive refinement. It is widely regarded as one of the brand's more dependable modern powerplants, though proper maintenance remains vital.

**Thermoplastic Oil Pump Component Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported concerns regarding internal plastic components within the variable-displacement oil pump, which could potentially risk oil pressure regulation.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the oil pump design and check for any active diagnostic trouble codes or oil pressure warnings during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Burning Odor and Drivetrain Error Codes**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an intermittent burning smell over time, eventually accompanied by dashboard drivetrain error codes.
- **Solution:** Trace the source of any burning odors immediately to prevent potential fluid leaks or electrical shorts; professional diagnostic scanning is recommended.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides rapid, seamless gear changes across rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It is generally robust, though electronic shift controls should be verified during inspection.

**Unexpected Gear Selection Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected gear shifts or erratic behavior between reverse, drive, and park at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a dealership or specialist shop and inspect the electronic gear selector switch and linkage.

## Steering

### Front Steering and Suspension Assembly

Front steering and suspension components provide sharp, engaging handling characteristic of a sport sedan, but front-end wear should be checked during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**Creaking Noises and Stiff Steering Feel**
- **Symptoms:** A low creaking noise may develop from the front wheels when traveling at slow speeds over small bumps, eventually accompanied by increased steering stiffness.
- **Solution:** Inspect front suspension bushings, ball joints, and steering components for wear, lubrication loss, or binding.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V554000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During a crash, a seat belt retractor that does not function as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the seat belt retractors (including the seat belts) and, if necessary, replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V761000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The connection between the 48-Volt battery and the starter-generator may not have been assembled properly, which can cause increased electrical resistance at the connections. Increased electrical resistance can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the connection between the 48-Volt battery and the starter-generator assembly, replacing it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V298000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An improperly installed seat belt assembly may not restrain an occupant as expected, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the front seat belt buckle assembly connection and, if necessary, replace it, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V211000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The seat frame may not have been welded properly to the seat rails. An improperly welded seat frame may not properly restrain the occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat frame and back rest, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M340i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The BMW M340i offers an exceptional blend of luxury and high-output performance, making it an enticing option on the used market. Prospective buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the front suspension, electronics, and oil system components to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
