**Introduction:**

The 2015 BMW Alpina B7L is a high-performance sedan with an impressive 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 540 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. It is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission with paddle shifters. The B7L is known for its luxurious interior, impressive performance, and sleek exterior styling. However, there have been some reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Throttle Position Sensor Failure:** Malfunctioning throttle position sensors can cause engine hesitation, stalling, or difficulty starting. Requires sensor replacement. **$500 - $1,200+**
* **Turbo Wastegate Malfunction:** Turbo wastegates may fail, causing overboosting and potential engine damage. Requires replacement of the wastegate. **$2,500 - $4,000+**
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Faulty fuel injectors can affect engine performance, leading to misfires, rough idling, or power loss. Requires injector replacement. **$1,200 - $2,000+**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:** Some owners have reported issues with rough or delayed gear shifting. Transmission fluid flushes or software updates are recommended. **$200 - $400+**
* **Transmission Solenoid Failure:** Faulty solenoids can affect transmission performance, leading to erratic shifting or slipping gears. Requires solenoid replacement. **$600 - $1,500+**

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Suspension Control Failure:** Malfunctioning sensors or actuators in the adaptive suspension system can affect ride quality or stability. Requires diagnosis and repair or module replacements. **$600 - $2,000+**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some B7Ls have experienced excessive battery drain, potentially due to faulty electronic modules or parasitic power draw. Requires battery testing and electrical system diagnostics. **$200 - $600+**
* **Infotainment System Malfunction:** Issues with the infotainment system (iDrive) have been reported, including screen freezes, software glitches, or loss of functionality. Requires software updates or component replacements. **$500 - $1,500+**

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:** Some B7Ls have experienced headlight condensation, affecting visibility and potentially causing electrical issues. Requires headlight seal replacements or modifications. **$300 - $600+**

**Interior**

* **Heated/Cooled Seat Failure:** Malfunctioning seat heating or cooling modules can affect passenger comfort. Requires seat component testing and repair. **$500 - $1,200+**

**Summary:**

While the 2015 BMW Alpina B7L is a highly capable and impressive vehicle, there have been some reported issues. These issues range in severity and cost to repair. Owners should be aware of potential problems and consider extended warranties or preventative maintenance to minimize the likelihood of costly repairs.
