**Introduction:**

The 2012 BMW Alpina B7L is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine producing 540 horsepower and 540 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with a ZF 8-speed automatic transmission and features all-wheel drive. The B7L is praised for its impressive performance, spacious interior, and luxurious appointments, but it has also been subject to some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke from the engine, loud whistling noises.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Transmission Slippage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delayed gear changes, loss of power under acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid or rebuild the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uneven ride height, excessive noise from the suspension.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the air suspension components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sway Bar End Link Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the suspension, reduced handling stability.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar end links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, reduced electrical power.
    * Solution: Replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlights, reduced visibility at night.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air conditioning or heating system.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module or diagnose and repair the specific component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2012 BMW Alpina B7L offers impressive performance and luxury, it has been subject to some reported issues. These include turbocharger failure, transmission slippage, suspension leaks, electrical issues, headlight condensation, and interior climate control problems. The severity and cost of these issues can vary significantly depending on the specific part that needs repair or replacement.
