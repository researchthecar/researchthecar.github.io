# 2023 BMW Z4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW Z4 delivers an engaging driving experience with sharp handling and refined powertrain options. As a relatively recent model year with very few reported owner complaints, it generally maintains a strong initial reliability outlook for used luxury sports car buyers.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (sDrive30i)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers a balanced mix of efficiency and responsive performance for daily driving.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M40i)

This robust 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine generates around 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating acceleration and is widely praised for its smooth power delivery and durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features quick, smooth shifts and pairs seamlessly with both engine options. It is known for strong mechanical refinement in modern BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V821000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The weld seam of the integrated transmission control unit's cover may not have been manufactured properly, which could allow automatic transmission oil to leak into the transmission control unit. Transmission oil that leaks into the control unit can lead to a seizure of the transmission gears, causing a sudden loss of drive power and increasing the risk of.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the transmission mechatronics module and the automatic transmission fluid, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW Z4 stands out as a mechanically sound option in the used luxury roadster market, backed by strong engine and transmission choices. Prospective buyers should still conduct a standard pre-purchase inspection to ensure overall vehicle health.
