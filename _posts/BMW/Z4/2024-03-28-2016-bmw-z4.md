**Introduction:**

The 2016 BMW Z4 is a compact luxury roadster available in two engine and transmission configurations: an sDrive30i and an sDrive35i. The sDrive30i features a 3.0-liter inline-six engine that produces 255 horsepower and 220 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The sDrive35i features a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six engine that produces 335 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission. The Z4 is praised for its handling, performance, and stylish design, while some drivers may find its ride too firm and its interior cramped.

**Engine**

**sDrive30i**

* **Water pump failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating engine, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake valve deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced performance, rough idling, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Clean or replace the intake valves
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel injector issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, poor performance, increased emissions
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**sDrive35i**

* **Turbocharger failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: 16V-784
* **High-pressure fuel pump failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic (sDrive30i)**

* **Shifting issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifts, slipping gears
    * Solution: Update transmission software, replace transmission fluid, or rebuild or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission fluid leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from the transmission, reduced transmission fluid levels
    * Solution: Fix the leak and replace the transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch (sDrive35i)**

* **Clutch wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced clutch engagement, slipping gears, shuddering
    * Solution: Replace the clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front strut mount failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises, reduced ride quality
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical system failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical components not working, loss of power, warning lights
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the vehicle, weak battery, reduced electrical power
    * Solution: Replace the battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible top issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks, slow opening or closing, malfunctioning
    * Solution: Repair or replace the convertible top components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Plastic trim rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises and vibrations from plastic trim pieces
    * Solution: Secure or replace the trim pieces
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation system issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, inaccurate navigation, connection problems
    * Solution: Update software or replace the navigation unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center console creaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling sounds from the center console
    * Solution: Tighten or replace console components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Heated seat failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Heated seats not working or overheating
    * Solution: Repair or replace the heating elements
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 BMW Z4 is a well-rounded luxury roadster with generally positive reviews from owners. However, it is not without its issues. The most common problems reported by owners include water pump failure, turbocharger failure, and transmission shifting issues. While many of these issues can be costly to repair, they are relatively rare and do not affect the majority of Z4 owners.
