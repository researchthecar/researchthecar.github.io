# 2020 BMW Z4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Navigating the used market for a 2020 BMW Z4 means looking at a dynamic, open-top roadster engineered for performance and engaging handling. While owner-submitted complaints for this model are relatively sparse, prospective buyers should review available powertrain configurations and remain mindful of isolated electronic or steering-related owner reports logged with safety authorities.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (sDrive30i)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for daily driving. This powerplant generally balances efficiency with responsive power delivery, making it a popular choice for everyday roadster enthusiasts.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M40i)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine generates roughly 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering thrilling performance and rapid acceleration. This robust inline-6 is well-regarded by enthusiasts for its smooth power delivery and strong overall reliability profile.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission paired across the lineup provides quick, refined shifts and seamless power transfer in both automatic and manual paddle-shifted modes. It is widely praised for its durability and smooth operation in varied driving conditions.

## Steering

### Steering and Assistance Systems

Steering complaints are infrequent but note occasional environmental noises and driver-assist sensitivity.

**Steering Noise and Lane Departure Assist Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported squeaking or grinding noises when moisture affects the steering area, as well as instances where lane departure intervention abruptly pulls the steering wheel.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the steering linkage and seals for moisture intrusion, and consult a dealer regarding driver-assistance system calibration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V850000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Headlight failure can reduce the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the headlight control units, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V700000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The failed weld may allow fuel to leak, increasing the risk of a fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V598000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** During engine start, the engine management software may cause loss of braking assist. Loss of braking assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine management software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V516000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices and Associated Equipment." Should the headlamps be adjusted horizontally, there could be increased glare and reduced visibility for oncoming drivers, or reduced visibility for the vehicle operator, thereby increasing.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the headlamp vertical and horizontal adjustment plugs, correcting them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V636000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Relay](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Z4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter relay may corrode, causing the relay to overheat and short circuit. A short circuit in the starter relay may increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2020 BMW Z4 offers an engaging driving experience backed by capable powertrain choices and solid overall construction.
