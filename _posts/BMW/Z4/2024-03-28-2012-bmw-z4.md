**Introduction:**

The 2012 BMW Z4 is a compact luxury roadster that offers a thrilling driving experience. It is powered by a 3.0L inline-six engine producing 335 horsepower and 317 lb-ft of torque. The Z4 is available with a six-speed manual transmission or a six-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the BMW Z4 praise its powerful engine, agile handling, and stylish design. However, some common complaints include a cramped interior, a firm ride, and a high price tag.

**Engine**

* **N54 3.0L Inline-Six**
    * **Vanos System Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The variable valve timing system (Vanos) fails, resulting in reduced engine performance and increased fuel consumption.
        * Solution: Replace the Vanos solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The high-pressure fuel pump fails, causing the engine to stall or lose power.
        * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Water Pump Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The water pump fails, leading to overheating and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the water pump and thermostat.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual Transmission:**
    * **No Issues Found**
* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Mechatronic Unit Failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: The mechatronic unit, which controls the transmission, fails, causing shifting problems or transmission failure.
        * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The front strut mount fails, causing clunking noises and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the front strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear springs break, resulting in a sagging rear end and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace the rear springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges rapidly, even when the vehicle is turned off.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or identify and fix the electrical drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Faults:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical components, such as lights, wipers, or the infotainment system, fail intermittently.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the specific electrical fault.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The convertible top fails to operate or leaks water.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the convertible top motor or hydraulic system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Moisture:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlights.
    * Solution: Inspect the headlight seals and replace them if necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system freezes, becomes inaccurate, or displays incorrect information.
    * Solution: Update or replace the navigation system software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard rattles or makes noises when driving over rough roads.
    * Solution: Apply foam tape or other padding to eliminate the rattling.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 BMW Z4 is a fun-to-drive roadster, but it has some reliability issues that can be costly to repair. The most common issues include Vanos system failures, high-pressure fuel pump failures, water pump failures, mechatronic unit failures, and convertible top problems. These issues can range in severity from minor annoyances to major mechanical breakdowns that could leave you stranded. Overall, the Z4's reliability is average, and it is advisable to budget for potential repairs before purchasing one.
