# 2016 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe combines striking styling with high-performance luxury, featuring a powerful V8 engine and sophisticated all-wheel-drive dynamics. Used-car shoppers looking at this luxury four-door should carefully evaluate service history and mechanical condition, as high-performance German luxury vehicles demand diligent maintenance to avoid expensive surprises.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration. While potent, it requires meticulous oil change intervals and vigilant monitoring of cooling and turbo components to maintain long-term health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic

Equipped with an 8-speed sport automatic transmission featuring paddle shifters, power is smoothly delivered to the intelligent xDrive all-wheel-drive system. This gearbox generally proves robust, though fluid maintenance is recommended for seamless operation over high mileage.
