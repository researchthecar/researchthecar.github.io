# 2019 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury vehicle featuring powerful twin-turbo V8 engineering and sophisticated all-wheel-drive dynamics. While initial owner reports are relatively sparse, prospective buyers should review available powertrain options and monitor vehicle electronics and safety systems during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust horsepower and torque, offering exceptional acceleration for this luxury gran coupe. Routine maintenance and thorough pre-purchase inspections are strongly advised due to the complex nature of high-output turbocharged German engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission pairs with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system to provide smooth, rapid gear changes. It is generally a robust unit, though peripheral starter components require standard periodic checks.

**Starter Motor Mounting Bolt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle produces a strange noise when the starter is engaged, caused by the starter motor shifting out of position.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the starter motor mounting bolts, secure or replace them as needed, and verify proper starter alignment.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety and driver-assistance features enhance protection on the road, though sensitive radar and camera hardware can occasionally report false obstructions.

**Abrupt Unintended Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected abrupt stopping while driving on the freeway or city streets, occasionally accompanied by control panel obstruction warnings despite clear surroundings.
- **Solution:** Inspect the forward-facing camera and radar sensors for alignment, cleanliness, or software glitches, and consult a dealer for any applicable sensor recalibrations.
