The 2016 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 650i Gran Coupe is praised for its ample power, luxurious interior, and sharp handling, but criticized for its high fuel consumption and limited rear-seat space.

**Brakes**

* **Premature Front Brake Pad Wear**
Description/Symptoms: Excessive wear on the front brake pads, causing premature replacement.
Solution: Replace the brake pads.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Issues**
Description/Symptoms: Water leaks, rattling, or inoperable sunroof.
Solution: Replace or repair the sunroof mechanism.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: 15V-845

**Interior**

* **iDrive Software Glitches**
Description/Symptoms: Intermittent touchscreen failures, navigation errors, or Bluetooth connectivity issues.
Solution: Update the iDrive software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+

**Summary**

The 2016 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe is a well-rounded luxury sedan with potent performance and a comfortable interior. However, it is not without its issues, including premature brake pad wear, sunroof problems, and occasional electrical glitches. While the costs of these issues can range from minor to moderate, it's important to be aware of them before purchasing a used 650i Gran Coupe.
