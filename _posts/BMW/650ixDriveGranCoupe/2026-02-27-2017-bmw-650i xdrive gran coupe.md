# 2017 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2017 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe, a luxurious four-door coupe, offers a blend of performance and comfort. Powered by a potent V8 engine and featuring BMW's xDrive all-wheel-drive system, it provides an engaging driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its build quality and driving dynamics, like any vehicle, it has specific areas that potential owners should be aware of regarding long-term reliability. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential component failures and their associated costs for the 2017 model year.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63TU2)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased oil consumption, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of turbocharger(s) or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Fuel Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor fuel economy, engine misfires, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe is a powerful and luxurious vehicle with a generally solid mechanical foundation. The 4.4L twin-turbo V8 engine, while potent, can be prone to issues like turbocharger problems, ignition coil failures, and fuel injector issues, which can be costly to repair. The transmission is typically reliable. Suspension components, particularly the adaptive systems, can experience malfunctions. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and battery drain, are also noted. Exterior and interior components are generally robust, with minor issues like headlight condensation being the most common. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the reliability of this high-performance grand tourer.
