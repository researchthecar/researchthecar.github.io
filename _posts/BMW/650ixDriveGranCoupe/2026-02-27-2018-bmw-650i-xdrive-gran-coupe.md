# 2018 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury cruiser, evaluating a model's mechanical background helps ensure long-term satisfaction. This guide outlines known owner-reported issues and core powertrain options for the vehicle to help you make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust horsepower and substantial torque, offering brisk acceleration typical of high-performance luxury tourers. Buyers should budget for diligent maintenance history checks, as modern turbocharged V8s require meticulous oil change intervals to remain reliable over time.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission provides seamless gear changes and power delivery routed through an intelligent all-wheel-drive system. It is generally robust, though regular fluid inspections help maintain smooth operation.

## Interior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Cabin appointments are upscale and durable, though mechanized features like roof shades require periodic inspection.

**Sunroof Shade Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof curtain or shade fails to retract properly, sags downward, and can block rear visibility or drop into the cabin area.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a service center to inspect the sunroof shade mechanism and repair or replace the failing track or fabric components.

## Safety

### Air Bag

Airbag and occupant safety systems generally function as intended, though isolated sensor faults can occur.

**Passenger Seat Occupant Sensor Fault**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger airbag warning light illuminates unexpectedly on the dashboard and remains lit, indicating a potential fault in the occupant detection system.
- **Solution:** Have the dealership inspect the passenger seat sensor mat; owners have reported needing full mat replacements to resolve the warning light.
- **Cost:** $2,600 to $3,000

Overall, this luxury Gran Coupe offers impressive performance and refined comfort, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect complex cabin electronics and safety sensor systems prior to purchase. Verifying a thorough maintenance history can prevent unexpected out-of-pocket expenses.
