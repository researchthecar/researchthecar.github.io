**Introduction:**

The 2014 BMW 650i Gran Coupe is a luxurious and sporty sedan that offers impressive performance and style. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine that produces 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. The car is available with either a six-speed manual transmission or an eight-speed automatic transmission. It also features a spacious and well-equipped interior, along with a number of advanced driver assistance features.

The 650i is highly praised for its powerful engine, sophisticated handling, and comfortable cabin. However, it has also been criticized for its high price tag and lack of fuel efficiency.

**Engine**

**4.4-liter Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Oil Leak from Oil Pan Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leak from the oil pan gasket can be seen under the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250-$500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from the engine, especially when starting or accelerating.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200-$1800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

* No Issues Found

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Delayed Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update the transmission software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 19V363000

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise from the rear suspension, especially over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace the rear suspension bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600-$1000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly or fails to hold a charge.
    * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and fix electrical drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Xenon Headlamp Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Xenon headlamp may flicker or fail to illuminate.
    * Solution: Replace the xenon headlamp assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800-$1200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* No Issues Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Screen Glitch:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system screen may freeze or become unresponsive.
    * Solution: Reset the infotainment system or update the software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0-$200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Navigation System Freezing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system may freeze or become unusable.
    * Solution: Update the navigation system software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 BMW 650i Gran Coupe is a reliable vehicle overall. However, it has been reported to have some common issues, such as oil leaks, timing chain tensioner failure, and suspension bushing failure. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to moderate. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicles serviced regularly to prevent or address them promptly.
