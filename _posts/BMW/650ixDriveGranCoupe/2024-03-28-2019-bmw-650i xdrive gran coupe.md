## 2019 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe

The 2019 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe is a stylish and powerful luxury sedan. It comes standard with a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. A six-speed manual transmission is standard, while an eight-speed automatic transmission is optional. The 650i xDrive Gran Coupe can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 4.6 seconds and has a top speed of 155 mph.

Owners of the 650i xDrive Gran Coupe generally praise its performance, handling, and luxurious interior. However, there have been some reports of issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

### Engine

- **4.4L Twin-turbocharged V8**

  - **Timing chain issues:** The timing chain can stretch or break, causing the engine to lose power or fail. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, reduced power, and difficulty starting the engine. The solution is to replace the timing chain and tensioner. Estimated cost to fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
  - **Valve stem seal failure:** The valve stem seals can fail, causing oil to leak into the combustion chambers. Symptoms include blue smoke from the exhaust, increased oil consumption, and poor engine performance. The solution is to replace the valve stem seals. Estimated cost to fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  - **Turbocharger failure:** The turbochargers can fail, causing a loss of power and increased exhaust emissions. Symptoms include a whistling or screeching noise from the engine, reduced power, and black smoke from the exhaust. The solution is to replace the turbocharger. Estimated cost to fix: $2,500 - $5,000+

### Transmission

- **Six-speed Manual Transmission**

  - **Clutch issues:** The clutch can wear out or fail, causing difficulty shifting gears or slipping gears. Symptoms include a grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty finding gears, and a slipping clutch. The solution is to replace the clutch disc and pressure plate. Estimated cost to fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  - **Transmission mount failure:** The transmission mount can fail, causing the transmission to move around excessively. Symptoms include vibration, noise, and difficulty shifting gears. The solution is to replace the transmission mount. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+

- **Eight-speed Automatic Transmission**

  - **Mechatronic unit failure:** The mechatronic unit is a computer-controlled hydraulic valve body that controls the transmission. It can fail, causing the transmission to malfunction. Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, and a loss of power. The solution is to replace the mechatronic unit. Estimated cost to fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  - **Torque converter failure:** The torque converter can fail, causing the transmission to slip or shudder. Symptoms include a shuddering sensation when accelerating, slipping gears, and a loss of power. The solution is to replace the torque converter. Estimated cost to fix: $2,500 - $4,000+

### Suspension

- **Front suspension bushings:** The front suspension bushings can wear out or fail, causing the vehicle to handle poorly and make noise. Symptoms include a clunking or rattling noise from the front of the vehicle, poor handling, and excessive tire wear. The solution is to replace the front suspension bushings. Estimated cost to fix: $500 - $1,000+
