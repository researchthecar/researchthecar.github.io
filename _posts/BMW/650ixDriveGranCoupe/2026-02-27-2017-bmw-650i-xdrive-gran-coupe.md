# 2017 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe, buyers are met with a striking luxury sedan that combines aggressive performance with high-end cabin amenities. While the available data shows minimal reported owner complaints, luxury performance vehicles of this tier require meticulous maintenance history checks and careful inspection of body and electrical electronics.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers robust output around 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and strong highway performance, though prospective buyers should budget for higher-than-average maintenance costs and strict service intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed sport automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, this setup provides quick, smooth gear changes and confident traction in various weather conditions. Routine fluid checks are recommended to ensure long-term durability.

## Exterior

### Trunk Release and Seating Access

Cabin features are generally robust, but localized issues with secondary cargo access have been reported by individual owners.

**Trunk Release Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience an inability to open the trunk electrically, trapping access when secondary manual release handles and rear seat folding levers are located exclusively inside the trunk area.
- **Solution:** Inspect the trunk electronic release switch, wiring harness, and latch mechanism for failure. Consult a qualified technician to service or replace malfunctioning release actuators.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=650i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 650i xDrive Gran Coupe remains an enticing option for enthusiasts seeking luxury and power. Because luxury performance parts and electronics can be expensive to repair, always prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify a complete service record before finalizing your purchase.
