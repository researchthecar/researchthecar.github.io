The 2024 BMW 228i Gran Coupe is a stylish and sporty entry-level luxury sedan. The model is primarily powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It's generally liked for its agile handling, attractive design, and upscale interior. However, some common dislikes include its relatively small rear seat, limited cargo space, and the sometimes-firm ride quality.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket. This can manifest as a burning smell, visible oil drips under the car, or low oil levels.
    *   Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks often originate from the plastic thermostat housing. This can lead to overheating, low coolant levels, and a sweet, antifreeze-like smell.
    *   Solution: Replace the thermostat housing with an updated design (if available, some aftermarket options are more durable) and thermostat.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Aisin 8-Speed Automatic (AWTF-8G45)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported occasional rough shifting, particularly when cold, or hesitation when accelerating.
    *   Solution: A software update may resolve minor issues. In more severe cases, valve body replacement or transmission replacement may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update - $100-$300, Valve body replacement - $1500 - $3000, Transmission replacement $5000 - $8000+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Premature Bushing Wear**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing premature wear of the front control arm bushings, leading to clunking noises from the front suspension, particularly over bumps.
    *   Solution: Replace the worn control arm bushings. Replacing the entire control arm may be recommended depending on the extent of the wear.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Bushing replacement - $300 - $600, Control Arm replacement $500-$1000+ per side.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The iDrive infotainment system may occasionally freeze, lag, or exhibit other glitches.
    *   Solution: Try a system reboot first. If the issue persists, a software update from the dealer may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Software update - $100-$300, Hardware replacement $1,000+ if necessary
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Parking Sensors, Blind Spot Monitoring)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Erroneous warnings or non-functional sensors related to parking assist or blind spot monitoring systems.
    *   Solution: Diagnose the specific sensor and wiring. Replace the faulty sensor and repair any damaged wiring.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per sensor
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

*   **Rattling Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reports of rattling noises emanating from the dashboard or door panels, particularly on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Locate and address the source of the rattle. This may involve tightening loose trim pieces or applying sound-deadening material.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+ depending on complexity and labor involved.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2024 BMW 228i Gran Coupe appears to have a mix of minor and potentially more significant issues. The most common complaints revolve around engine oil and coolant leaks, transmission performance, electrical glitches, and suspension component wear. While some problems can be resolved with relatively inexpensive fixes like software updates or gasket replacements, others, such as valve body failures or sensor replacements, can be more costly. Overall, these issues range from minor annoyances to potentially significant expenses, warranting a thorough inspection before purchase.
