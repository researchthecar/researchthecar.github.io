# 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe offers a stylish entry point into the brand's luxury lineup with standard all-wheel drive and modern tech. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers will find a generally solid mechanical baseline supported by limited owner complaint data. As with any modern turbocharged luxury vehicle, keeping up with routine maintenance and thoroughly inspecting the vehicle's electrical and powertrain systems during a test drive is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (228i xDrive)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration for daily driving and generally maintains a dependable reputation when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, this 8-speed automatic delivers quick, seamless shifts and contributes to respectable fuel economy. It is widely regarded as a robust and refined transmission option in BMW's modern lineup.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V513000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=228i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-pressure fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=228i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe presents a balanced option for used car buyers seeking luxury and efficiency without widespread reported mechanical flaws. While available data indicates a quiet initial ownership period, buyers should still perform a pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to ensure long-term reliability.
