# 2021 BMW 228i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2021 BMW 228i Gran Coupe, part of the F44 generation, offers a blend of sporty aesthetics and practical four-door coupe styling. It is primarily equipped with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine, paired with an 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission. This powertrain configuration is generally well-regarded for its performance and efficiency. Reliability data for the 2021 model year indicates a solid performance, with most reported issues being minor and not indicative of widespread systemic problems. Owners generally appreciate the vehicle's driving dynamics and premium interior, though like any vehicle, some isolated component concerns have been noted.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW 228i Gran Coupe demonstrates good overall reliability, consistent with BMW's reputation for building durable vehicles. The powertrain, consisting of the 2.0L turbocharged engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, is robust and has not shown significant widespread failure patterns. The primary areas where minor issues have been reported are within the electrical system, specifically concerning the infotainment unit's occasional glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain issues. These are generally manageable with software updates or diagnostics. The suspension, exterior, and interior components have proven reliable, with no major concerns surfacing in owner feedback or technical reports for this model year.
