# 2024 BMW 228i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 228i Gran Coupe blends sporty styling with everyday luxury and efficiency. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from mature powertrain engineering, though buyers should still evaluate overall vehicle condition during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration. It is generally regarded as a reliable modern powerplant when maintained with regular oil changes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The responsive 8-speed automatic transmission pairs well with front-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive configurations. It delivers smooth, quick shifts and maintains a strong reputation for dependability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V711000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=228i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The bolts that connect the front body structure to the vehicle chassis may not have been tightened properly. A loose front body structure may not adequately protect occupants in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and if necessary, tighten the front structure bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 228i Gran Coupe presents a solid used-car proposition with standard modern luxury and efficient powertrain options. Prospective buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify overall mechanical and cosmetic health.
