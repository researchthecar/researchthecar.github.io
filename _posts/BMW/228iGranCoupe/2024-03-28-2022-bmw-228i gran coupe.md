**Introduction:**

The 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe is a premium subcompact executive sedan that boasts a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and offers an optional xDrive all-wheel drive system. The 228i is renowned for its sporty handling, luxurious interior, and advanced technology features. Common complaints include a stiff ride on certain road surfaces and a lack of standard features in the base trim.

**Engine:**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

**Fuel Pump Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting, reduced power, check engine light
* Solution: Replace fuel pump
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slippage:**
* Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or slipping during gear changes, check engine light
* Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

**Front Suspension Struts:**
* Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noises over bumps, poor handling, uneven tire wear
* Solution: Replace struts
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, electrical components malfunctioning
* Solution: Replace battery or identify electrical drain
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior:**

**Climate Control Malfunction:**
* Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not blowing, temperature fluctuations
* Solution: Replace HVAC actuator or control module
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe is a generally reliable vehicle with few significant issues reported by owners. The most common issues relate to the fuel pump and transmission, but these are not widespread. The estimated cost of repairs for these issues can be substantial, so those considering purchasing a used model should have a thorough inspection performed. Overall, the 228i is a well-built and enjoyable car that offers a high level of performance and luxury.
