# 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe, powered by the B48 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine, generally exhibits strong reliability, a hallmark of BMW's engineering. This model combines sporty performance with the practicality of a four-door coupe. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected issues, like any modern vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen occasional attention. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential weak points and their typical resolutions. The integration of advanced technology and the robust powertrain contribute to its overall dependable nature, though vigilance with certain electrical and engine components is advised.

---


## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or the water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises from the engine bay, increased oil consumption, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the turbocharger or related seals and lines.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 228i Gran Coupe is a generally reliable vehicle, particularly concerning its powertrain and chassis components. The 2.0L turbocharged engine and 8-speed automatic transmission have proven robust. The most frequently cited issues tend to be related to the electrical system, specifically infotainment software glitches and occasional battery drain, which are not uncommon in modern, tech-laden vehicles. Minor coolant leaks and potential turbocharger issues, while not pervasive, are areas to monitor. Overall, with proactive maintenance and attention to potential electrical quirks, the 228i Gran Coupe offers a dependable and enjoyable driving experience.
