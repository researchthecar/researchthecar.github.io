# 2022 BMW 320i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 BMW 320i, buyers will find a luxury sport sedan known for engaging driving dynamics and modern technology. While overall owner feedback is limited, reviewing available powertrain options and occasional owner-reported quirks can help ensure a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 180 to 255 horsepower depending on specific market tunes, offering a strong balance of efficiency and power. It is generally regarded as a robust powerplant, though routine maintenance is essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the standard rear-wheel or all-wheel drive setups, this ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers exceptionally smooth and fast shifts. It is widely praised for its durability and responsiveness in daily driving.

## Steering

### Tires

Factory or replacement tire durability can vary based on road conditions and driving habits.

**Tire Failure and Sidewall Damage**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden tire blowouts or unexpected sidewall cuts while operating at highway speeds, resulting in temporary handling concerns.
- **Solution:** Inspect tires regularly for wear or damage and replace compromised tires promptly with quality replacements.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior glass components generally hold up well, though rare stress fractures can occur.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may unexpectedly shatter or explode outward while driving or parked.
- **Solution:** Have the sunroof assembly inspected and replace the glass panel if structural stress or damage is detected.

The 2022 BMW 320i offers an upscale driving experience with solid core powertrain options. Used car buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection focusing on tires, exterior glass, and general mechanical health to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
