The 2022 BMW 320i is an entry-level luxury sedan known for its sporty handling, comfortable ride, and upscale interior. The 320i is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing 180 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. Other engine options available for the 2022 3 Series include the 330i's 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder (255 hp, 295 lb-ft), the M340i's 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six (382 hp, 369 lb-ft), and the plug-in hybrid 330e (288 hp combined). All engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners generally appreciate the 320i's fuel efficiency, agile driving dynamics, and premium feel. Common dislikes include the relatively small rear seat, the cost of optional features, and the potential for expensive maintenance and repairs.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder (B48)**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the valve cover gasket, oil filter housing gasket, or the oil pan gasket. Can result in burning oil smell or visible oil drips.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaking gasket or seal.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+ depending on location and labor.
    * Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks around the expansion tank, hoses, or radiator. Can lead to overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace the leaking components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+ depending on location and labor.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report occasional rough shifting, particularly at lower speeds, or hesitation when accelerating.
    *   Solution: In some cases, a transmission software update can resolve the issue. In more severe cases, valve body replacement or transmission overhaul may be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 (software update) - $3000+ (valve body/overhaul)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

No Issues Found

**Electrical**

*   **iDrive System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The iDrive infotainment system can experience occasional glitches, freezing, or slow response times.
    *   Solution: Restarting the system or performing a software update can sometimes resolve the issue.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (restart) - $200+ (software update if not covered by warranty)
    *   Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty sensors**
    *   Description/Symptoms: May affect safety systems.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty sensors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Tail Light Cracking/Condensation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the tail light lens or condensation buildup inside the tail light housing.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the tail light assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+ per tail light
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Creaking/Rattling Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reports of creaking or rattling noises emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or rear deck.
    *   Solution: Diagnosing and tightening loose trim pieces or applying felt tape to contact points.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+ depending on the location and complexity.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 BMW 320i, while generally a reliable and enjoyable vehicle, does have some common issues to be aware of. The most frequently reported problems revolve around minor engine leaks, transmission quirks, electrical glitches in the iDrive system, and some exterior/interior trim issues. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to potentially costly repairs, with estimated costs ranging from a few hundred dollars for minor fixes to several thousand for more extensive repairs like transmission overhauls or engine gasket replacements. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision, ensuring they have a pre-purchase inspection performed and consider extended warranty options.
