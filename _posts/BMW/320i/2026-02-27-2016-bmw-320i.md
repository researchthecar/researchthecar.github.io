# 2016 BMW 320i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 320i offers an entry point into the brand's popular compact luxury sedan lineup, blending rear-wheel-drive or all-wheel-drive dynamics with daily drivability. While generally praised for its sharp handling and refined interior, used car buyers should approach with a clear understanding of its mechanical tendencies. Reviewing common owner feedback helps highlight what areas to inspect closely during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20/B48)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 180 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque, providing responsive power for daily commuting. Routine maintenance and diligent oil change intervals are critical to ensuring long-term durability for this powerplant.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick and seamless gear changes while optimizing fuel economy across various driving conditions. It is widely regarded as a robust unit, though fluid checks remain recommended during routine service.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The available 6-speed manual transmission offers a more engaging, driver-focused experience for enthusiasts seeking direct control. It is less common in the marketplace but generally holds up well with normal clutch operation.
