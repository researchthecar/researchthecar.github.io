# 2016 BMW 220i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 220i offers buyers a sporty, rear-wheel-drive luxury coupe experience backed by BMW's driver-focused engineering. While it generally delivers an engaging ride and refined interior, used car shoppers should review maintenance histories closely to ensure previous owners kept up with routine services.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides a strong balance of everyday efficiency and spirited acceleration for the 220i.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and optimizes fuel economy across driving conditions. It is widely regarded as a robust and refined unit in modern BMW applications.
