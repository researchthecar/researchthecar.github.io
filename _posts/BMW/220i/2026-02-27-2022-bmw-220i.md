### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 220i demonstrates commendable reliability, with no significant systemic flaws reported across its primary mechanical and electrical systems. Overall, the 220i offers a dependable ownership experience, aligning with BMW's reputation for producing well-built vehicles. Routine maintenance is essential, but major component failures are uncommon for this model year.
