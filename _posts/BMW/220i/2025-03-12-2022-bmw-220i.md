The 2022 BMW 220i is a compact coupe offering a sporty driving experience and premium features. The engine options available include the 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 cylinder engine, and the 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 cylinder with xDrive. The 220i is the base model and comes with the 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 cylinder engine producing 181 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque. Many owners appreciate the 220i's sharp handling, stylish design, and comfortable interior. Common criticisms include the relatively small rear seats, limited cargo space, and some find the technology interface a bit complex.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4 Cylinder (B48)**
*   **Rough Idle/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, and occasional stalling, especially when the engine is cold.
    *   Solution: The issue is typically related to fuel injectors, ignition coils, or a vacuum leak. Inspection and replacement of faulty components are required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-4 Cylinder with xDrive (B48)**
*   **Rough Idle/Hesitation**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, and occasional stalling, especially when the engine is cold.
    *   Solution: The issue is typically related to fuel injectors, ignition coils, or a vacuum leak. Inspection and replacement of faulty components are required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (Aisin)**
* **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported harsh or jerky shifting, particularly between lower gears. Others have noted hesitation when accelerating from a stop or while attempting to pass.
    * Solution: In some cases, a software update may resolve the issue. In more severe cases, replacement of the valve body within the transmission may be required. In extreme situations, a full transmission replacement may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

*   **iDrive System Freezing/Rebooting**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The iDrive infotainment system can freeze, become unresponsive, or randomly reboot. This can affect navigation, audio, and other vehicle functions.
    *   Solution: A software update may fix the issue. In some cases, a replacement of the iDrive control unit is required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,500+
    *   Recalls: None Found
*   **Sensor Malfunctions (Parking Sensors, etc.)**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Failure of parking sensors or other sensor systems can occur, resulting in error messages or incorrect readings.
    *   Solution: Replacement of faulty sensors.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

*   **Rattling/Squeaking Noises**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Some owners report rattling or squeaking noises coming from the dashboard, door panels, or other interior trim pieces.
    *   Solution: Locating and tightening loose fasteners or applying felt tape to contact points to reduce friction.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 BMW 220i appears to have a mix of minor electrical and interior issues along with some potential engine and transmission concerns. The rough idle/hesitation issues and the harsh shifting transmission issues have been reported by some owners. The iDrive system freezing, while not mechanically critical, can be a frustrating issue. Overall, while some issues exist, they do not appear to be widespread across all vehicles. Repair costs can vary greatly depending on the specific problem and the chosen repair method.
