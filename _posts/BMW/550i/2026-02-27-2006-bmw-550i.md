# 2006 BMW 550i

### Overview
The 2006 BMW 550i, part of the E60 generation, is a luxury executive sedan known for its potent V8 engine and engaging driving dynamics. While generally well-regarded for its performance and comfort, like any vehicle of this age and complexity, it has specific areas that have shown a propensity for issues. Owners often praise its powerful engine and refined interior, but potential buyers should be aware of common electronic glitches and potential maintenance costs associated with its advanced systems. This data focuses on documented, non-wear-and-tear related reliability concerns.

---


## Engine

### 4.8L V8 (N62B48)
**Valve Stem Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke from exhaust, especially on startup or deceleration; oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,000

**Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks visible from the bottom of the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Cooling System Leaks (Hoses, Radiator, Water Pump)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking components.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (GM 6L45/6L50)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Rough shifting, slipping gears, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets, fluid flush.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500

**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, inability to select gears, transmission fault codes.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**iDrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows not operating, slow operation, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Xenon headlights not turning on or flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 BMW 550i offers a compelling blend of performance and luxury, but its advanced engineering comes with potential reliability considerations. The 4.8L V8 engine, while powerful, is prone to issues like valve stem seal wear and cooling system leaks, which can be costly to repair. The automatic transmission can also develop leaks or mechatronic unit problems. Electrically, the iDrive system and components like window regulators and headlight ballasts are known to fail. While the suspension, exterior, and interior are generally robust, owners should budget for potential repairs, particularly concerning the powertrain and electronics, to maintain this sophisticated vehicle.
