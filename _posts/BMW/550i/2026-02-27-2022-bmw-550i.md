# 2022 BMW 550i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury executive sedan, the 2022 BMW 550i promises potent performance and upscale comfort. However, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding powertrain electronics, sensor reliability, and body glass integrity. While owner-reported complaints highlight specific areas to inspect closely during a test drive, a thorough pre-purchase inspection remains essential to ensuring long-term satisfaction.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (M550i xDrive)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, providing explosive acceleration paired with standard all-wheel drive. While generally robust, owners should verify proper cooling system operation and monitor for any accessory noise during pre-purchase inspections.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission features steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters and smooth, rapid gear changes tailored to the M550i's performance profile. Overall reliability is typically strong, though buyers should ensure all software updates and routine fluid maintenance have been completed.

## Exterior

### Glass Roof

The exterior glass components provide an open-air cabin feel, but rare instances of unprovoked shattering have been reported by owners.

**Sudden Glass Roof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass may fracture or explode unexpectedly while driving without any prior visible chips or external impacts.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass assembly for stress fractures and replace the sunroof panel under comprehensive insurance or warranty where applicable.

## Safety

### Driver Assistance Sensors

Advanced safety sensors provide crucial parking and collision avoidance support, though bumper sensors can occasionally prove sensitive to impacts or environmental wear.

**Faulty Parking and Collision Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience improper or missing warnings from exterior proximity sensors, sometimes triggered by minor external damage or internal component failure.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the bumper-mounted sensors and camera calibration, replacing any malfunctioning hardware as necessary.

### Airbags and Restraints

Occupant detection systems keep track of seat positioning and passenger presence to manage airbag deployment safely.

**Passenger Restraint Warning Message**
- **Symptoms:** A persistent warning message regarding the passenger restraint system illuminates on the dashboard, often linked to seat or floor wiring harnesses.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scans are required to isolate the affected sensor or connector under the seat, followed by component replacement or harness repair.

The 2022 BMW 550i delivers exhilarating V8 performance and premium luxury, but buyers must remain vigilant about potential electronic issues, driver-assistance sensor calibration, and auxiliary system quirks. Reviewing service records and conducting a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help mitigate risks and ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
