# 2016 BMW 550i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 550i offers strong luxury and performance credentials, anchored by powerful V8 engine options. While owner feedback in consumer databases highlights typical luxury car electrical and accessory nuances, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain health, maintenance history, and overall condition during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (445 hp)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration. Buyers should prioritize diligent maintenance records and check for cooling system or oil leak histories.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and pairs well with rear-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It is generally robust when fluid changes are maintained over its lifespan.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=550i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V718000: Child Seat](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=550i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles may fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." If the child seat is not properly attached to the lower anchors, it can increase the child's risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, repair the inboard lower anchor on the left rear seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 550i is an engaging and powerful executive sedan for buyers willing to stay ahead of routine maintenance and complex luxury upkeep. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified BMW specialist is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
