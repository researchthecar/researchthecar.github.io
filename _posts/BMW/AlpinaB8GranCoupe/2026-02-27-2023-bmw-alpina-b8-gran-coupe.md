# 2023 BMW Alpina B8 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW Alpina B8 Gran Coupe is an ultra-high-performance luxury vehicle blending everyday comfort with breathtaking power. Because it is a low-volume, highly specialized luxury model, wide-scale complaint trend data remains sparse, but buyers should anticipate high maintenance and upkeep costs typical of exotic German performance cars.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This potent 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces around 612 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and effortless passing power. While robustly engineered, it requires strict adherence to maintenance intervals and premium fluids.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an Alpina-calibrated 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, it delivers rapid, seamless gear changes and confident power delivery in all driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=Alpina+B8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW Alpina B8 Gran Coupe offers extraordinary exclusivity, luxury, and performance. Used-car buyers should thoroughly review service histories and secure a pre-purchase inspection from a qualified specialist, keeping in mind that parts, servicing, and insurance will command a significant premium.
