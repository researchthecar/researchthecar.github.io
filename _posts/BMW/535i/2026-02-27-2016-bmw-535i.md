# 2016 BMW 535i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 BMW 535i, buyers will find a luxury sedan that balances executive comfort with strong turbocharged performance. While luxury European vehicles require diligent maintenance and can be expensive to repair outside of warranty, understanding the typical powertrain options and minor owner-reported quirks helps you select a well-maintained example for your garage.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. It provides smooth, responsive acceleration and is generally considered robust when routine oil change intervals are strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless gear changes across rear-wheel and all-wheel drive configurations. It is widely praised for its durability and refined shift quality in everyday driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V718000: Child Seat](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=535i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles may fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." If the child seat is not properly attached to the lower anchors, it can increase the child's risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, repair the inboard lower anchor on the left rear seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=535i&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 535i is a compelling choice for used-car buyers seeking a refined, powerful executive sedan. Prospective owners should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify complete service records to ensure long-term reliability and avoid unexpected luxury repair costs.
