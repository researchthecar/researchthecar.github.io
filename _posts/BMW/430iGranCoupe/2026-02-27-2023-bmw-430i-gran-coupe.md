# 2023 BMW 430i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 430i Gran Coupe combines sleek four-door coupe styling with engaging driving dynamics and modern luxury. As a relatively new model on the used market, it generally benefits from modern engineering refinements and advanced powertrain technology. Prospective buyers should review available vehicle history carefully and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to ensure all onboard systems and mechanical components are operating as intended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque, delivering a strong balance of everyday efficiency and responsive acceleration. It is widely regarded as a robust and dependable powerplant within BMW's modern lineup.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission features manual shift modes and pairs effectively with both rear-wheel drive and xDrive all-wheel drive configurations. It is known for quick gear changes and strong long-term reliability under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

The 2023 BMW 430i Gran Coupe presents itself as an appealing used luxury choice, offering strong performance and everyday usability without widespread mechanical complaints recorded at this early stage. Buyers should prioritize well-maintained examples with comprehensive service records and verify that all software updates and routine maintenance have been properly performed.
