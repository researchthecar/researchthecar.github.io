# 2017 BMW 430i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 430i Gran Coupe, buyers will find a stylish and engaging luxury sport vehicle powered by BMW's reliable B46 turbocharged four-cylinder engine. While the driving dynamics and powertrain earn praise, prospective owners should review common owner-reported grievances regarding cooling system components and interior safety sensors.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 (B46)

The 2017 BMW 430i features a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine (B46) generating approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It offers a smooth balance of power and efficiency, though plastic cooling system components warrant regular inspection as the vehicle ages.

**Coolant Leaks from Plastic Housing**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience low coolant warnings on the dashboard, and an inspection reveals coolant leaking from plastic engine components such as the oil filter housing, thermostat, or water pump connections.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed plastic housing, gaskets, or electric water pump components with updated parts.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an eight-speed ZF automatic transmission paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive, this gearbox is widely regarded as smooth, responsive, and durable with no major recurring mechanical defect trends reported by owners.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Seat Occupancy System

Safety equipment generally performs well, though sensor faults within the passenger restraint system can trigger dashboard warnings.

**Passenger Restraint System Warning**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates intermittently or stays on constantly, often accompanied by a message indicating a passenger seat occupancy sensor failure.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the passenger restraint system and replace faulty seat occupancy sensors or wiring harnesses.

### Seat Belt Assembly

Seat belt mechanical components should be thoroughly checked during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure secure latching and tensioning.

**Seat Belt Latch Disconnection**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's or passenger's seat belt unexpectedly detaches from its anchor point or the tensioner fails to maintain proper restraint during normal vehicle operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt assembly and replace damaged latches or tensioner anchor points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V115000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat-mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
