## 2019 BMW 430i Gran Coupe

The 2019 BMW 430i Gran Coupe is a stylish and sporty four-door coupe that slots between the 3 Series and 5 Series in BMW's lineup. It's powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 255 horsepower and 295 pound-feet of torque. The 430i Gran Coupe is available with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive and comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**
* **B46 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: Burning excessive amounts of oil, leading to low oil levels and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace piston rings, valve stem seals, or the entire engine.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $10,000+
    * **Timing Chain Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure or stretching of the timing chain, leading to engine noise, power loss, and potential engine failure.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain, tensioner, and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty fuel injectors, leading to rough idling, poor fuel economy, and potential engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the affected fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-203 - Fuel Pump Failure

**Transmission**
* **ZF 8HP50 8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibrations during acceleration, often due to a worn torque converter.
        * Solution: Replace the torque converter or the entire transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * **Shifting Problems**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or delayed shifts.
        * Solution: Relearn transmission adaptations or replace transmission solenoids.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Suspension**
* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge, especially when the vehicle is parked.
        * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty modules or components (e.g., alternator, starter motor).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Infotainment System Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning touchscreens, frozen displays, or connectivity problems.
        * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA Campaign Number: 18V-699 - Airbag Control Unit Failure

**Exterior**
* **Headlight Condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlights.
        * Solution: Replacing headlight assemblies or venting the headlights.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA Campaign Number: 21V-114 - Headlight Glare

**Interior**
* **Sunroof Rattle**
        * Description/Symptoms: Noises from the sunroof when it is closed.
        * Solution: Adjusting or replacing sunroof seals or guides.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
* **Climate Control Malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: Faulty air conditioning or heating systems.
        * Solution: Replacing sensors, actuators, or the climate control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**
The 2019 BMW 430i Gran Coupe is a stylish and powerful vehicle, but it has had some issues reported. The most common problems relate to the engine, including excessive oil consumption, timing chain issues, and fuel injector failure. Other issues include transmission shudder, battery drain, and sunroof rattle. The severity of these issues varies, but some can be costly to fix.

Overall, the 430i Gran Coupe has received mixed reviews from owners. Some praise its performance, handling, and style, while others criticize its reliability and high repair costs.
