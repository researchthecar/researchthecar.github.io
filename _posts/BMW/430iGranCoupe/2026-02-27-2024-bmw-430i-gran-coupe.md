# 2024 BMW 430i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 430i Gran Coupe combines sleek styling with everyday practicality, offering buyers an engaging driving dynamic paired with modern luxury features. As a relatively new model year with minimal reported owner complaints, it generally presents a clean slate for used-car shoppers. However, prospective buyers should still perform standard pre-purchase inspections to ensure proper maintenance and electronics functionality.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It offers a refined balance of responsive power delivery and respectable fuel economy for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid shifts across rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It is widely regarded as one of the most reliable and responsive automatic gearboxes in its class.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V863000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as a hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the cylinder head cover as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
