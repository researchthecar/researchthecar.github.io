# 2022 BMW 430i Gran Coupe

The 2022 BMW 430i Gran Coupe, part of the G26 generation, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting BMW's commitment to engineering quality. This powertrain is typically paired with an 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission, which is also a well-regarded unit. While specific widespread mechanical failures are uncommon for this model year, like any vehicle, individual experiences can vary.
