# 2020 BMW 745e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW 745e blends luxury flagship styling with plug-in hybrid efficiency, offering an advanced powertrain for used-car shoppers who want both performance and electrified driving. While early owner feedback points to a quiet ride and sophisticated tech, buyers considering a used luxury hybrid should approach with normal due diligence regarding complex electronics and high-voltage systems.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-in Hybrid

This powertrain pairs a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine with an electric motor to produce a combined 389 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and smooth transitions, though buyers should verify the health of the high-voltage battery system during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Managed by an 8-speed automatic transmission with integrated electric motor capability, power delivery is seamless across all driving modes. This ZF-sourced unit is generally robust, provided routine fluid maintenance has been performed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V589000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745e&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." Without an audible warning, the driver may forget to buckle the seat belt, increasing their risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW 745e is a sophisticated luxury plug-in hybrid that rewards prospective buyers with comfort and strong performance. As with any high-end modern vehicle, a comprehensive mechanical and electronic inspection by a specialist is highly recommended before finalizing your purchase.
