# 2022 BMW 745e

### Overview
The 2022 BMW 745e, a plug-in hybrid variant of the flagship 7 Series, aims to blend luxury, performance, and efficiency. It typically features a sophisticated powertrain combining a turbocharged gasoline engine with an electric motor. As a luxury vehicle, the 745e is equipped with advanced technology and premium materials, contributing to a refined driving experience. Reliability for this model year generally aligns with BMW's reputation for producing well-engineered vehicles, though specific components, especially those related to the hybrid system and advanced electronics, warrant attention. Owners can expect a high level of comfort and cutting-edge features, but as with any complex vehicle, understanding potential issues is key to long-term satisfaction.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, compressor running frequently.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200 per corner

**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per side


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, intermittent connectivity issues (Apple CarPlay/Android Auto).
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $800


### Hybrid Battery System
**Reduced Range/Performance**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable decrease in electric-only range, reduced overall power output, battery warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential battery module repair or replacement (under warranty if applicable).
- **Cost:** $3000 - $10000+


### 12V Auxiliary Battery
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical systems malfunctioning, 'battery discharge' warnings, difficulty starting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the 12V auxiliary battery.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 745e demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. However, as a technologically advanced plug-in hybrid, potential issues tend to arise in the complex electrical and hybrid systems, as well as the adaptive suspension. Owners should be aware of possible software glitches in the infotainment, potential degradation of the hybrid battery over time, and the possibility of air spring leaks in the adaptive suspension. While the engine and transmission are robust, proactive maintenance and attention to warning lights related to the hybrid system are recommended. The cost of repairs for these specialized components can be significant, especially outside of warranty coverage.
