# 2022 BMW 745e Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 745e represents a sophisticated plug-in hybrid flagship sedan, combining a turbocharged inline-six engine with an electric motor for seamless luxury cruising. As with many modern high-end European hybrids, shopping for a used model involves balancing complex powertrain technology against long-term maintenance considerations. While official owner complaint logs for this specific model year remain sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect both the conventional mechanical systems and the high-voltage electrical components thoroughly prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-In Hybrid

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six pairs with an integrated electric motor to deliver around 389 total horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque. It offers robust performance and refined power delivery, though proper maintenance of both the cooling and hybrid systems is essential for long-term durability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features integrated electric motor capability and delivers exceptionally smooth, quick gear shifts. It is generally robust when fluid changes are maintained according to schedule.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V683000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=745e&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A short-circuit in the battery cell can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the affected battery cell module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 745e offers an impressive blend of ultra-luxury comfort and hybrid efficiency, making it an enticing pre-owned luxury option. Because reported owner complaints are minimal for this model year, buyers can approach the vehicle with cautious optimism while prioritizing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified BMW technician.
