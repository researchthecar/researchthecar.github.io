# 2007 BMW 760Li

### Overview
The 2007 BMW 760Li, a flagship luxury sedan, offers a potent combination of performance and opulence. Powered by a formidable V12 engine, it represents the pinnacle of BMW's engineering for its era. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics and comfort, like any high-performance vehicle, it has specific areas that owners should be aware of regarding long-term reliability. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential repair costs and solutions for owners of this sophisticated machine.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L V12 (N73B60)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils. It's often recommended to replace them in pairs or all at once for optimal performance.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil, plus labor

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets. This can be labor-intensive due to the engine's complexity and placement.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500+

**Cooling System Issues (Water Pump, Hoses)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, low coolant level, heater not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump, thermostat, and/or coolant hoses. Regular coolant flushes are also recommended.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000+


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (GM 6L80/6L90)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid, rough shifting, slipping gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of leaks, often involving seals or the transmission pan gasket. Regular fluid and filter changes are crucial.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800+

**Solenoid Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, failure to shift into certain gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty transmission solenoids. This often requires dropping the transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200+


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive (Active Roll Stabilization)
**Active Roll Stabilization Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights on the dashboard related to suspension, clunking noises, reduced handling stability.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty active roll stabilization actuator. This is a complex and expensive component.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000+


### Standard Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, especially after sitting, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air struts. Leaks can also occur at hose connections.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500+ per corner


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**iDrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation issues, audio problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets. In some cases, replacement of the iDrive controller or head unit may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000+

**Battery Drain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, dead battery, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find parasitic drain sources (e.g., faulty modules, door lock actuators). Battery replacement may also be needed.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800+

**Headlight/Taillight Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning headlights or taillights, flickering, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the adaptive headlight or taillight control modules.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700+

## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential damage to electronics.
- **Solution:** Clearing of sunroof drain tubes. Regular maintenance is key.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Seat Actuator Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats not moving correctly or at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat motors or control modules.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800+

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 BMW 760Li is a complex luxury vehicle with a powerful V12 engine. While it offers an exceptional driving experience, potential owners should be prepared for higher maintenance and repair costs compared to less complex vehicles. Key areas to monitor include the engine's ignition system and potential oil leaks, transmission shifting issues, and the sophisticated suspension and electrical systems, particularly the iDrive. Regular and proactive maintenance, especially fluid changes and system checks, is crucial for mitigating costly repairs and ensuring the longevity of this high-performance sedan. Despite these potential issues, many owners find the ownership experience rewarding due to the vehicle's performance and luxury.
