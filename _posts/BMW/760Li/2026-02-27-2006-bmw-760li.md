# 2006 BMW 760Li

### Overview
The 2006 BMW 760Li, a flagship luxury sedan, is known for its potent V12 engine and opulent interior. While offering a refined driving experience, like many high-performance luxury vehicles of its era, it can present specific reliability challenges. Owners should be aware of potential issues primarily related to its complex powertrain and advanced electronic systems. Maintenance and repair costs can be significant due to the specialized nature of the components and the V12 engine's intricate design. However, with diligent maintenance, many of these concerns can be mitigated, allowing for continued enjoyment of this executive saloon.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L V12 (N73B60)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Coolant Leaks (Expansion Tank, Hoses)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell, low coolant level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of cracked expansion tank or leaking hoses.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**VANOS System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, reduced engine performance, rattling noises on startup.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of VANOS solenoids or related components.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500


## Transmission

### 6-speed Automatic (GM 5L40-E / 6HP26)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from transmission pan or cooler lines, harsh shifting, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, gaskets, or cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1200

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive (Active Roll Stabilization)
**Hydraulic Leaks/Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights, clunking noises, reduced handling stability, body roll.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, seals, or the active stabilizer bar pump.
- **Cost:** $2000 - $4000


### Standard Air Suspension (Rear)
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Rear end sagging, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200 per side


## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Door Lock Actuators
**Failure of Actuators**
- **Symptoms:** Doors not locking or unlocking, intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door lock actuators.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per door


### Headlight Ballast/Bulb Failure
**Xenon Headlight Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not working, flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of ballast or xenon bulb.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per side

## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Sunroof Drain Clogs**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential damage to electronics.
- **Solution:** Cleaning of sunroof drains.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**Heated Seat Element Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Heated seats not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of heating elements or seat modules.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 BMW 760Li is a complex luxury vehicle where reliability is highly dependent on meticulous maintenance and prompt attention to issues. The V12 engine, while powerful, is prone to specific failures like ignition coil problems and oil leaks, which can be costly to repair. The advanced suspension and electrical systems, particularly the iDrive, also present potential points of failure. Owners should budget for specialized maintenance and be prepared for potentially high repair bills, especially for powertrain and electronic components. Despite these potential drawbacks, a well-maintained 760Li can still offer a luxurious and powerful driving experience.
