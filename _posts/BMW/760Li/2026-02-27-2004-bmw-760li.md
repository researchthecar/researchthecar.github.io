# 2004 BMW 760Li

### Overview
The 2004 BMW 760Li, a flagship luxury sedan, is powered by a potent V12 engine, offering a refined and powerful driving experience. While generally well-regarded for its performance and comfort, like any complex luxury vehicle of its era, it has specific areas that may require attention. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential issues owners might encounter with its sophisticated systems. Understanding these potential pitfalls can help in proactive maintenance and managing ownership costs for this high-performance German automobile.

---


## Engine

### 6.0L V12 (N73B60)
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils. Often recommended to replace in sets or all at once for optimal performance.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per coil

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of degraded gaskets. This can be labor-intensive due to engine complexity.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Cooling System Issues (Water Pump, Hoses)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, temperature gauge fluctuations.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the water pump, thermostat, and any cracked or brittle hoses.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


## Transmission

### 6-speed Automatic (GM 5L40-E / 6HP26)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid under the vehicle, rough shifting, slipping gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of leaks, often from the transmission pan gasket or cooler lines. Fluid and filter change may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

**Solenoid Pack Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh shifting, delayed engagement, failure to shift into certain gears.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the transmission solenoid pack.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1500


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive (Active Roll Stabilization)
**Active Roll Stabilization System Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll in corners, warning lights on the dashboard related to suspension, clunking noises.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty hydraulic actuators, pumps, or control modules within the system.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $4000+


### Standard Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks / Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, constant compressor running, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or the air compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2000


## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, navigation system errors, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or module resets. In some cases, replacement of the head unit or control module may be required.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent issues with lights, windows, locks, or other electronic accessories. Warning lights may appear without clear cause.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and reprogramming or replacement of the Body Control Module.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1500


### Parking Sensors
**Faulty Parking Sensors**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts, no alerts when obstacles are present, parking assist system warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of individual faulty parking sensors.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500 per sensor

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 BMW 760Li is a complex luxury vehicle with a powerful V12 engine. While it offers a premium experience, potential owners should be aware of specific reliability concerns, particularly with the engine's ignition system and potential oil leaks, as well as the sophisticated suspension and electrical systems. Transmission issues, though not universal, can also arise. Proactive maintenance, especially concerning the cooling system and regular fluid changes, is crucial. The cost of repairs can be significant due to the complexity and specialized nature of the components. Despite these potential issues, with diligent care and maintenance, the 760Li can remain a rewarding vehicle.
