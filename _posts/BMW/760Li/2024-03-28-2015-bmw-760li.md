**Introduction:**

The 2015 BMW 760Li is an exquisite full-size luxury sedan powered by a potent 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine, producing 535 horsepower and 550 lb-ft of torque. It features an eight-speed automatic transmission, providing a refined and responsive driving experience. While the 760Li boasts an array of advanced features and amenities, it has faced its fair share of issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**6.0L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, decreased performance
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitations:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent hesitation during gear shifts
    * Solution: Fluid flush, software update, or transmission replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or uneven ride height, loss of air pressure
    * Solution: Replace faulty air struts or other components in the suspension system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Sway Bar End Links:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from rear suspension, wobbly handling
    * Solution: Replace worn or damaged end links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery draining prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical issues, such as faulty alternator or parasitic drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **iDrive System Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent display issues, software glitches
    * Solution: Software updates, hardware replacements
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Lid Closing Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty closing or opening trunk
    * Solution: Adjust trunk lid hinges or replace latch mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture inside headlight housing
    * Solution: Replace headlight housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature control, fan noise
    * Solution: Replace faulty actuators or climate control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Drainage Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking from sunroof, interior water damage
    * Solution: Clean or replace clogged drainage tubes
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2015 BMW 760Li is a luxurious vehicle that has encountered various issues. Some issues, such as fuel injector failures and air suspension leaks, can be expensive to repair. However, other issues, like rear sway bar end links and sunroof drainage issues, are relatively minor. Overall, the severity and cost of repairs vary depending on the specific issue encountered.
