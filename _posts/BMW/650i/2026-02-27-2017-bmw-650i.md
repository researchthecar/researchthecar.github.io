# 2017 BMW 650i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 650i, buyers are typically drawn to its luxurious styling, upscale cabin, and potent V8 performance. While high-end luxury grand tourers offer immense comfort and prestige, they also demand diligent maintenance history and careful inspection of their complex mechanical and electrical systems to avoid costly surprises.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, providing rapid acceleration and effortless highway cruising. Buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive service records, as this high-performance powerplant requires meticulous upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, smooth shifts and optimizes both performance and fuel efficiency across various driving conditions. It is generally robust, though routine fluid checks are recommended during pre-purchase inspections.

## Exterior

### Trunk Release Mechanism

Electrical complaints for this model are relatively sparse, but accessory systems and locking mechanisms should be thoroughly tested before purchase.

**Trunk Release Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the trunk fails to open electronically, complicating access when rear seat release levers are also located inside the trunk area.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic trunk release switch, wiring harness, and latch actuator for proper operation. Replace faulty components as needed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=650i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 650i is a sophisticated grand tourer offering strong V8 performance and a lavish interior experience. Prospective buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and mechanical health, ensuring the vehicle has been well-maintained by previous owners.
