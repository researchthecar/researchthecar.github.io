# 2016 BMW 650i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 650i is a high-performance luxury grand tourer offering powerful V8 performance and a well-appointed cabin. As with many high-end European performance cars, prospective used buyers should approach with careful inspection, as maintenance and potential mechanical repairs can be costly over time.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. Buyers should keep up with rigorous maintenance schedules to manage heat and turbocharger longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid shifts paired with paddle shifters. It is generally robust and well-regarded for both refinement and durability when properly serviced.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 650i is an engaging and luxurious GT car, but it requires a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a dedicated maintenance budget. Buyers should verify service history and check for any signs of fluid leaks or electrical issues before purchasing.
