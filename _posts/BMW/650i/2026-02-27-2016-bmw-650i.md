# 2016 BMW 650i

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 650i, a luxurious grand tourer, generally offers a high level of performance and comfort. As a premium vehicle, its reliability is a key consideration for potential owners. While the 650i is built with quality components, like any complex machine, it can experience specific issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2016 model year. Understanding these potential issues can help owners prepare for and address them proactively, ensuring the continued enjoyment of this sophisticated vehicle.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63TU)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced power, increased oil consumption, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Fuel Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor fuel economy, engine misfires, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Active Roll Stabilization Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Increased body roll during cornering, warning lights on the dashboard related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of active anti-roll bars and related sensors.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $4,000


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**iDrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio cutting out, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, control unit reset, or replacement of the iDrive controller or head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure/Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 650i is a powerful and luxurious vehicle that, for the most part, offers solid reliability. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the sophisticated twin-turbo V8 engine, specifically the turbochargers, ignition coils, and fuel injectors, which can require attention and incur significant repair costs. The advanced adaptive suspension system can also present issues with its active components. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the iDrive infotainment system and potential battery drain, are also noted. However, the transmission and core exterior/interior components are generally robust. Proactive maintenance and addressing early signs of trouble in the engine and electrical systems are recommended for owners to maintain the 650i's performance and longevity.
