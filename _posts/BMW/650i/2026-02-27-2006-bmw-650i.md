# 2006 BMW 650i

### Overview
The 2006 BMW 650i, a luxury grand tourer, offers a blend of performance and comfort. Powered by a robust V8 engine, it generally provides a satisfying driving experience. However, like many complex luxury vehicles of its era, it is not immune to certain mechanical and electrical gremlins that can arise with age and mileage. Owners should be aware of potential issues, particularly concerning the powertrain and electronic systems, which can require specialized attention and potentially significant repair costs. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any developing problems are crucial for maintaining the 650i's performance and longevity.

---


## Engine

### 4.8L V8 (N62B48)
**Valve Stem Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke on startup or deceleration, oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,000

**Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks detected under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Cooling System Leaks (Hoses, Radiator, Water Pump)**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking components.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1,800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (GM 6L45/6L80)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or cooler lines.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, lines, or pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed engagement, rough shifts, or slipping between gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter service, or potential rebuild/replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $4,000+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,000

**Active Roll Stabilization (Optional)**
- **Symptoms:** Error messages, reduced handling performance, potential leaks from hydraulic components.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of hydraulic pump, lines, or active stabilizers.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500+


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**iDrive System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Windows not moving up or down, grinding noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of window regulator motor and mechanism.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Headlight Ballast Failure**
- **Symptoms:** One or both Xenon headlights not working.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the headlight ballast.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2006 BMW 650i is a capable luxury coupe, but potential buyers should be prepared for maintenance costs associated with its advanced technology and performance-oriented V8 engine. Common issues revolve around the valve stem seals, oil leaks, and cooling system components, which can be costly to repair. Transmission issues, while not universal, can also arise and require significant investment. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the iDrive system, are also a possibility. Despite these potential drawbacks, with diligent maintenance and prompt repairs, the 650i can remain a rewarding vehicle to own.
