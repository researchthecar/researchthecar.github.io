# 2007 BMW 750i

### Overview
The 2007 BMW 750i, a flagship luxury sedan, offers a potent combination of performance and advanced technology. Powered by a robust V8 engine and featuring sophisticated electronic systems, it aims to deliver a premium driving experience. However, like many high-performance luxury vehicles of its era, the 750i can present specific reliability concerns that owners should be aware of. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics and comfort, certain components, particularly within the powertrain and electronics, have been noted for potential issues that may require attention over time. Regular maintenance and prompt addressing of any emerging problems are crucial for maintaining the longevity and optimal performance of this sophisticated automobile.

---


## Engine

### 4.8L V8 (N62B48)
**Valve Stem Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke from exhaust, especially on startup or deceleration; oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $4,500

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant loss, visible leaks from hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking components (water pump, thermostat housing, hoses, radiator).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500

**Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil drips under the vehicle, oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,000


## Transmission

### 6-speed Automatic (GM 6L45/6L50)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or cooler lines; rough shifting or slipping.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals, pan gasket, or cooler lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering, slipping, or harsh shifting, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive (Active Roll Stabilization)
**Active Roll Stabilization Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights on the dashboard, clunking noises, reduced handling stability.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic lines, pumps, or active anti-roll bars.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $5,000+


### Standard Chassis
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No Major Issues
- **Cost:** No Major Issues


## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, audio issues, navigation problems.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of iDrive controller or head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical gremlins including lights not working, power windows issues, central locking problems.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to find the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, short circuit) and repair.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800

**Condensation**

### Climate Control System
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air conditioning or heat, or intermittent operation.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


### Sunroof Mechanism
**Sunroof Motor/Mechanism Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof not opening or closing, or operating slowly.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the sunroof motor or associated mechanisms.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 BMW 750i is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but its complexity means potential for costly repairs. Key areas to monitor include the V8 engine's valve stem seals and cooling system, the automatic transmission's torque converter and fluid leaks, and various electrical components like the iDrive system and body control modules. The advanced suspension systems, while enhancing driving dynamics, can also be a source of expensive repairs if issues arise. Prospective buyers should be prepared for higher maintenance costs compared to less complex vehicles and ensure thorough pre-purchase inspections, focusing on these known problem areas. Regular and diligent maintenance is paramount to mitigating these risks and enjoying the premium experience this BMW offers.
