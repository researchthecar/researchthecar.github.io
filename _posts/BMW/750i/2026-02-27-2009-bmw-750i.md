# 2009 BMW 750i

### Overview
The 2009 BMW 750i, part of the F01 generation, represented a significant technological leap for BMW's flagship sedan. It was equipped with potent V8 engines and advanced features, aiming for a blend of luxury, performance, and cutting-edge technology. While generally well-regarded for its driving dynamics and opulent interior, like many complex luxury vehicles of its era, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. Understanding these potential reliability concerns is crucial for prospective buyers and current owners to manage maintenance costs and ensure long-term satisfaction. This analysis focuses on documented common problems beyond routine maintenance.

---


## Engine

### 4.8L V8 (N62B48)
**Valve Stem Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke on startup, oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks from hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking components (water pump, thermostat, hoses, radiator).
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500

**Oil Pan Gasket Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the oil pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (GM 6L80/90)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Rough shifting, slipping gears, warning lights, low transmission fluid level.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, transmission cooler lines, or transmission pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,000

**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, failure to engage gears, transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive System
**Active Roll Stabilization (ARS) Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, reduced handling stability, warning lights related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hydraulic lines or active anti-roll bars.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,000 per side

**Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging, inability to adjust ride height, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive controls, audio issues, navigation system errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, module reset, or replacement of iDrive controller or head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,500


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning lights, power windows, door locks, or other accessories; warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


### Parking Sensors
**Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** False alerts or failure of the parking assist system to detect obstacles.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty parking sensors.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300 per sensor

## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Headliner Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Fabric detaching from the headliner board, especially in hot climates.
- **Solution:** Re-gluing or replacement of the headliner.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 BMW 750i is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but its advanced technology and complex systems can lead to specific reliability concerns. The V8 engine, while potent, is prone to oil leaks and cooling system issues. The transmission can experience leaks and mechatronic unit problems. Electrically, the iDrive system and body control modules are common sources of glitches and faults. Suspension components, particularly those related to the adaptive systems, can be costly to repair if they fail. Owners should budget for potential repairs, especially concerning the engine, transmission, and electrical systems, and ensure regular maintenance by qualified technicians to mitigate these risks.
