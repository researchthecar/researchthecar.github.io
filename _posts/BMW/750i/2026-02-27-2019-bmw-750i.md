# 2019 BMW 750i

### Overview
The 2019 BMW 750i, a flagship luxury sedan, offers a blend of potent performance and sophisticated comfort. Powered by a robust V8 engine, it aims for a high level of driver engagement and passenger luxury. As with any high-performance luxury vehicle, understanding potential reliability concerns is crucial for owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into common problem areas and their potential resolutions for the 2019 model year.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63TU3)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Increased oil level warnings, blue smoke from exhaust, potential for fouled spark plugs.
- **Solution:** Regular oil level checks, potential PCV valve replacement, or in severe cases, engine internal inspection and repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,500

**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, boost pressure warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of turbochargers or associated plumbing.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $5,000+

**Ignition Coil/Spark Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils and spark plugs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, suspension warning light, uneven ride height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500

**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic ride height adjustments, suspension warning messages.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty suspension height sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, navigation errors, connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or resets, potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1,000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Dead battery after short periods of inactivity, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, followed by repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1,200

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 BMW 750i is a complex luxury vehicle with a powerful V8 engine. While the transmission and exterior/interior components generally show good reliability, potential owners should be aware of engine-related issues such as oil consumption and turbocharger concerns, which can be costly to repair. The adaptive air suspension and electrical systems, particularly the infotainment and potential battery drain, are also areas where issues have been reported. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are recommended to mitigate potential repair costs and ensure the longevity of this high-performance sedan.
