# 2016 BMW 750i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 BMW 750i, buyers will find a luxurious flagship sedan packing immense performance and advanced technology. However, owning a high-end luxury vehicle of this vintage requires diligence regarding maintenance history and potential electrical or active aerodynamic system glitches. Reviewing owner complaints highlights a few recurring areas to inspect closely before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63)

The 2016 BMW 750i is powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. This potent powerplant offers effortless acceleration, though complex thermal management systems require careful upkeep.

**Active Radiator Grille Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The active grille shutters can become stuck in the closed position, which may increase the risk of engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspect the upper and lower grill shutter actuators and replace faulty active air flap assemblies.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The vehicle utilizes an 8-speed sport automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive or rear-wheel drive, delivering smooth and rapid gear changes. No prominent recurring transmission failures are highlighted in current owner feedback.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Rims

Suspension components provide a supremely plush ride, but large low-profile wheel packages demand attention for potential cracking.

**Spontaneous Wheel Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Factory wheels may develop hairline cracks under normal driving conditions, resulting in sudden tire pressure loss or air leaks.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheels thoroughly for structural damage and replace compromised rims.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster

Electrical architecture is extensive in this flagship sedan, though major display failures remain isolated in owner reports.

**Blank Instrument Panel**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster intermittently goes entirely blank, preventing the driver from viewing speed, fuel levels, and warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Inspect software versions, module connections, and power supply feeds to the instrument panel.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Owner reports indicate occasional warnings from the active occupant protection and seatbelt pre-tensioning systems.

**Active Protection Restriction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard displays warning messages stating that Active Protection is restricted and seatbelt pre-tensioning may not function properly.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician is required to clear sensor codes and service the active safety module.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 750i remains a sophisticated luxury cruiser offering advanced features and robust V8 performance. While major mechanical breakdowns are not overwhelmingly frequent, prospective buyers should verify the functionality of active cooling grilles, electrical displays, and safety systems during a pre-purchase inspection.
