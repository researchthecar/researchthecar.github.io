# 2016 BMW 750i

### Overview
The 2016 BMW 750i, representing the G11/G12 generation, is a luxury sedan known for its advanced technology and powerful performance. As a flagship model, it generally offers a high level of build quality and reliability. However, like any complex vehicle, certain areas may experience issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear, to provide a clear picture for owners and potential buyers. The focus is on specific components and their reported problems, along with estimated repair costs where available.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (N63B44O2)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased oil consumption, exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coils.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Fuel Injector Problems**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor fuel economy, engine misfires.
- **Solution:** Replacement of defective fuel injectors.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one corner, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or air compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000 per corner for springs, $500 - $1,000 for compressor.

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 BMW 750i is a sophisticated luxury vehicle that generally performs well. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to its powerful twin-turbo V8 engine, specifically issues with turbochargers, ignition coils, and fuel injectors, which can lead to significant repair costs. The advanced adaptive air suspension can also be a source of expense if air springs develop leaks. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and potential battery drain, are also noted. However, the transmission and exterior/interior components are largely reported as reliable. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any warning signs are crucial for maintaining the longevity and performance of this high-end sedan.
