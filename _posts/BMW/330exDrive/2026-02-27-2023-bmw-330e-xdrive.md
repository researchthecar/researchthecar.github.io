# 2023 BMW 330e xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 330e xDrive delivers an appealing blend of plug-in hybrid efficiency and confident all-weather handling. For used car buyers, researching early reliability trends helps set expectations for this sophisticated electrified luxury sedan.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 Plug-In Hybrid

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine is paired with an electric motor to generate a combined 288 horsepower and 310 lb-ft of torque. It offers responsive acceleration and refined daily drivability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features integrated electric motor capability and paddle shifters, routing power smoothly through the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is generally praised for quick shifts and overall mechanical refinement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V747000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330e&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** After shifting into reverse, the rearview camera software may cause a portion of the rearview image to be obstructed. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An obstructed rearview image can reduce the driver's visibility of what is behind the vehicle, increasing.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the rearview camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330e&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW 330e xDrive is a sophisticated luxury plug-in hybrid that combines performance with efficiency. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of both the conventional powertrain and hybrid electrical systems.
