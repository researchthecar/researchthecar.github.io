# 2019 BMW M5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 BMW M5, buyers are looking at a high-performance luxury sedan renowned for its twin-turbo V8 power and dynamic capabilities. While it delivers exhilarating driving dynamics, prospective owners should review owner-reported complaints concerning cooling system components and powertrain behavior. Performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help catch potential mechanical issues early.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (S63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing supercar-level acceleration. Proper maintenance and attentive monitoring of the cooling and fuel systems are essential for long-term reliability.

**Coolant Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported coolant leaks originating from components such as the coolant expansion tank, which can lead to low coolant levels and potential overheating warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system and replace damaged tanks, hoses, or seals as necessary.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Steptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission features Drivelogic settings and pairs with an all-wheel-drive system to handle high torque loads. Overall operation is typically smooth, though electronic or mechanical faults can occasionally trigger limp-mode operation.

**Transmission Malfunction Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced sudden transmission malfunction warnings resulting in reduced vehicle speed while driving at high speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the transmission control module and fluid inspected by a qualified specialist to diagnose fault codes and update software.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V883000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The transmission wiring harness can become damaged, resulting in an electrical short circuit. An electrical short circuit can cause the transmission to shift to neutral resulting in a loss of propulsion, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace any damaged transmission harnesses. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V513000: Fuel System, Other:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The high pressure fuel pump may not have been properly attached to the engine, which may cause the fuel pump flange to crack, resulting in a fuel leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M5&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW M5 is a formidable performance sedan that demands careful evaluation before purchase. Buyers should prioritize service history records checking for past cooling system repairs and transmission diagnostics to ensure a dependable ownership experience.
