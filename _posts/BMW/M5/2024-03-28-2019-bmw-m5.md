**Introduction**

The 2019 BMW M5 is a high-performance sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine that produces 600 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. The M5 is praised for its impressive acceleration, sharp handling, and luxurious interior. However, it has also received some criticism for its high price, stiff ride, and excessive fuel consumption.

**Engine**

**Twin-Turbocharged 4.4-Liter V8**

* **Turbocharger Wastegate Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling sound is heard from the engine bay when accelerating at low speeds.
    * Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger wastegate actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
* **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, hesitation during acceleration, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Removal of the cylinder head and cleaning of the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leak from the transmission pan.
    * Solution: Replacement of the transmission pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
* **Shifting Hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Occasional hesitation or delay when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Dampers Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension becomes excessively stiff or soft, poor ride quality.
    * Solution: Replacement of the adaptive dampers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $2,500
* **Sway Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise when driving over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replacement of the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain at Rest**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains excessively when the vehicle is parked.
    * Solution: Replacement of the battery or repair of the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Navigation System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system freezes or displays incorrect directions.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the navigation unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment Screen Flickering**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment screen flickers or goes blank.
    * Solution: Replacement of the infotainment screen.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system does not adjust the temperature effectively or produces an excessive amount of noise.
    * Solution: Replacement of the climate control unit or repair of the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Summary**

Overall, the 2019 BMW M5 is a powerful and well-built performance sedan. However, it has had some issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, and interior. While the severity and cost of these issues vary, some can be significant and require significant repairs. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and consider the cost of ownership when making a purchase decision.
