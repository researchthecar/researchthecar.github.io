# 2022 BMW M5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW M5 delivers breathtaking performance paired with high-end luxury, making it a thrilling choice for used performance-car buyers. While it generally commands a strong reputation for powertrain engineering, shoppers should carefully inspect luxury vehicles of this caliber for minor aesthetic and build-quality quirks. As with any high-performance German sport sedan, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (S63)

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 600 to 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. It offers ferocious acceleration and robust overall engineering, though routine maintenance is critical to long-term health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Steptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission features Drivelogic shift modes and pairs with an M-specific all-wheel-drive system. It delivers rapid, crisp gear changes with strong reliability under normal operating conditions.

## Exterior

### Windshield

Exterior glass and body panels are generally well-assembled, though occasional isolated reports of stress-related windshield issues have been noted by owners.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** The front windshield may develop a crack near the top center without any external impact, spreading symmetrically outward.
- **Solution:** Have the windshield inspected by a certified glass technician and replaced if structural integrity is compromised.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW M5 remains an exceptionally powerful and well-regarded luxury sport sedan for enthusiasts seeking top-tier performance. Current owner feedback points to very few systemic mechanical issues outside of isolated cosmetic or glass concerns. Buyers should prioritize finding a well-documented model with a clean service history and budget accordingly for high-performance maintenance costs.
