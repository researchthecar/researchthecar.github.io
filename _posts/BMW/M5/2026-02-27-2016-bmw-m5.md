# 2016 BMW M5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 BMW M5 offers a thrilling high-performance luxury experience, combining executive comfort with twin-turbocharged V8 power. While widespread mechanical failures are not heavily logged in early owner complaints, buyers should still approach high-performance German engineering with a careful eye toward maintenance history, potential interior wear, and complex electrical systems.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (S63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 (S63) engine produces approximately 560 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. Proper and timely maintenance is critical to ensuring the longevity of this high-output performance powerplant.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (M-DCT)

The 7-speed M Dual-Clutch Transmission features Drivelogic settings for lightning-fast gear changes. Buyers should verify smooth operation during both aggressive driving and low-speed parking maneuvers.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim

Cabin materials are generally luxurious, though prolonged sun exposure can occasionally cause trim adhesive to fail.

**Dashboard Leather Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** The leather upholstery on the dashboard begins to pull away or detach due to failing adhesive.
- **Solution:** Re-gluing or replacing the affected leather dash panel through an upholstery specialist.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M5&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V718000: Child Seat](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M5&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles may fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 225, "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." If the child seat is not properly attached to the lower anchors, it can increase the child's risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, repair the inboard lower anchor on the left rear seat, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW M5 remains an exhilarating sport sedan choice for enthusiasts who prioritize speed and luxury. Because owner complaint data is sparse and high-performance repairs can be costly, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a qualified BMW specialist and verify a complete service history.
