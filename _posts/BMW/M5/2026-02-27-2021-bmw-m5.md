# 2021 BMW M5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 BMW M5, buyers gain access to a high-performance luxury sedan equipped with twin-turbocharged V8 power. While owner feedback is generally limited, prospective buyers should still inspect complex electronics, lighting, and powertrain systems during a pre-purchase evaluation.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (S63)

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers immense horsepower and torque, providing supercar-rivaling acceleration. Proper maintenance and timely fluid changes are critical to ensuring long-term reliability under high-performance driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Steptronic Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission features Drivelogic shift modes and pairs with an M xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid gear changes and durable everyday drivability when properly maintained.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Reverse Lighting

Involves cabin electronics, lighting, and driver assistance visibility features.

**Dim Backup Camera and Reverse Lights**
- **Symptoms:** The backup camera image and reverse lights are excessively dim and limited, making nighttime visibility difficult.
- **Solution:** Verify if software updates or aftermarket auxiliary enhancements can improve visibility, though dealership service may consider it operating as designed.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V598000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M5&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the reduced rearview image view can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, program the rearview camera with updated software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M5 delivers exhilarating performance wrapped in an executive sedan package, but its advanced technology and high-output engine require careful inspection. Buyers should perform a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on electronics and lighting features to ensure everything operates correctly.
