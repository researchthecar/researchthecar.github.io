**Introduction**

The 2012 BMW M5 is a high-performance sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine that produces 560 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The M5 is known for its blistering acceleration, precise handling, and luxurious interior. However, it does have some known issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbo V8**

* **Intake Manifold Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intake manifold cracking can cause a loss of power, rough idling, and odd noises.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Turbocharger failure can result in a lack of power, blue smoke from the exhaust, and overheating.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
    * **Clutch Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clutch wear can lead to slipping, shuddering, and difficulty shifting gears.
        * Solution: Replace the clutch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Suspension System Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Adaptive suspension system failure can cause a loss of ride quality, uneven tire wear, and handling problems.
    * Solution: Replace the adaptive suspension system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drain can result in a dead battery and difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the battery drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system malfunctions can cause a variety of issues, including loss of power, intermittent functionality of components, and warning lights.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the specific electrical system fault.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation inside the headlights can occur in humid environments.
    * Solution: Replace the headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system issues can include slow performance, incorrect information, and freezing.
    * Solution: Update the navigation system software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue.
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Bluetooth Connectivity Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bluetooth connectivity problems can prevent the vehicle from connecting to phones and audio devices.
    * Solution: Reset the Bluetooth system or update the software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2012 BMW M5 is a high-performance sedan with known issues that can be costly to repair. The most common issues include intake manifold cracking, turbocharger failure, and suspension system failures. While these issues can be a concern, it is important to note that not all M5s will experience them. With proper maintenance and care, the M5 can provide many years of driving enjoyment.
