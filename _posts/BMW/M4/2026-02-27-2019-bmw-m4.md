# 2019 BMW M4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW M4 delivers thrilling performance backed by a potent twin-turbocharged inline-six engine and driver-focused engineering. For prospective used-car buyers, researching the available powertrain options and maintaining meticulous service records are key steps to ensuring a rewarding ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (S55)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 425 horsepower and 406 lb-ft of torque in standard form, or up to 444 horsepower in the Competition package. Known for its ferocious power delivery and track capability, it requires strict adherence to maintenance schedules to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

This quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers lightning-fast gear changes and aggressive track performance. Buyers should look for smooth engagement during test drives, as proper fluid maintenance is critical for longevity.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers an engaging, hands-on driving experience paired with rev-matching technology. It is favored by purists who prioritize direct mechanical connection over automated shifting.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW M4 is a high-performance machine that rewards enthusiastic driving, provided it has been properly maintained. Buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive service history and conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection before buying.
