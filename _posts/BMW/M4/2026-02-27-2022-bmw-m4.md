# 2022 BMW M4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 BMW M4, buyers are looking at a high-performance coupe known for its aggressive styling and potent twin-turbocharged engines. Because owner reports and documented complaints are minimal for this model year, prospective buyers should focus on standard pre-purchase inspections to verify overall mechanical health and previous driving history.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged I6 (Base / Competition)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six produces approximately 473 horsepower in standard form and 503 horsepower in Competition trim. It delivers blistering acceleration while generally maintaining solid reliability under normal operating conditions.

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged I6 (Competition xDrive / CSL)

Generating up to 503 horsepower (and higher in limited CSL variants), this high-output inline-six pairs with all-wheel drive or track-tuned setups. Buyers should verify proper maintenance intervals, as these high-performance powerplants experience demanding driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

Available exclusively on base rear-wheel-drive variants, this traditional manual gearbox appeals to enthusiast drivers. Buyers should inspect the clutch condition and overall shift feel during a test drive.

### 8-Speed M Steptronic Automatic Transmission

Paired with Competition and xDrive models, this quick-shifting automatic transmission manages high torque loads effectively. Regular fluid changes are recommended to ensure long-term durability.

## Safety

### Visibility

Exterior lighting and trim components generally hold up well, though aftermarket cosmetic modifications should be scrutinized for safety and legality.

**Aftermarket Reflector Inserts**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported aftermarket reflector inserts replacing factory safety reflectors with dull black components, potentially reducing vehicle visibility.
- **Solution:** Inspect the vehicle to ensure factory-compliant reflective safety devices are installed.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW M4 presents as a thrilling yet mechanically sound option in the used luxury performance market, backed by very few owner complaints. Prospective buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection, verify maintenance records, and check for any aftermarket modifications.
