# 2021 BMW M4

### Overview
The 2021 BMW M4, a high-performance coupe, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting BMW's engineering prowess. However, as with any performance-oriented vehicle, specific areas can be prone to issues, particularly those related to its potent powertrain and advanced technology. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond standard maintenance. Owners can expect a robust driving experience, but awareness of potential, albeit infrequent, issues in the engine, transmission, and electrical systems is advisable for proactive ownership and to ensure the longevity of this enthusiast-focused machine.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (S58)
**Cooling System Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant loss, overheating, visible leaks around hoses and radiator.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty hoses, seals, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Turbocharger Lag/Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced power, delayed acceleration, unusual noises from the turbo.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential repair or replacement of turbocharger components.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Steptronic Automatic
**Harsh Shifting/Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable jolts or hesitations during gear changes, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Software update or transmission fluid flush and recalibration.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### 6-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear (Premature)**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch assembly replacement.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset.
- **Cost:** $0 - $200


### Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems (ADAS)
**Sensor Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** False warnings from parking sensors, adaptive cruise control, or lane keeping assist.
- **Solution:** Sensor cleaning, recalibration, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW M4 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability for a performance vehicle. The core powertrain, particularly the 3.0L twin-turbo inline-6 engine, is robust, though occasional cooling system leaks and turbocharger issues have been noted. Both the automatic and manual transmissions are generally dependable, with minor software or clutch-related concerns being the most common. Electrical systems, especially the infotainment and ADAS, can experience occasional glitches requiring software updates or sensor recalibration. Exterior and interior components are largely free from widespread issues. Overall, the M4 offers a reliable platform for enthusiasts, with potential issues being manageable and not indicative of systemic flaws.
