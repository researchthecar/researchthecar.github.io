# 2020 BMW M4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M4 represents the final year of the F82 generation, delivering high-performance driving dynamics powered by its twin-turbocharged inline-six engine. While highly praised for its track capability and aggressive styling, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle history, maintenance records, and potential wear associated with high-performance coupes.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (S55)

The S55 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged inline-six produces between 405 and 444 horsepower depending on the package. It offers exhilarating power delivery, though diligent maintenance and routine fluid changes are vital for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear changes suited for spirited driving. Buyers should test for smooth engagement in stop-and-go traffic during a pre-purchase inspection.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers an engaging, direct connection for enthusiast drivers. It is a robust unit provided the clutch has not been abused by previous owners.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension and Wheelbase

The suspension setup is stiffly tuned for agile handling, though owners should watch for component wear.

**Front End Instability**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported unexpected front-end lifting and wheelbase handling instability while turning at moderate speeds without warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician thoroughly inspect the front suspension components, steering geometry, and wheel alignment.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V666000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M4&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A damaged connection between the fuel line and the fuel rail could lead to a fuel leak and, in the presence of an ignition source, increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fuel injectors and if a damping component is missing, the injectors and the fuel rail will be replaced, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M4 is a thrilling performance machine that rewards enthusiastic drivers with sharp handling and potent engine options. Because owner-reported issues are sparse but can relate to high-stress driving dynamics, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly advised.
