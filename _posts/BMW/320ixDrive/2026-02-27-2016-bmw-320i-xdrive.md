# 2016 BMW 320i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 320i xDrive offers a blend of all-weather capability and European driving dynamics, powered by a turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with an all-wheel-drive system. While it provides an engaging daily commute, used car buyers should carefully review service records and inspect vehicle components closely, as owner reports highlight occasional mechanical and electrical hiccups that require attention.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (N20/B48)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 180 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque. It offers a balance of efficiency and punchy low-end power, though proper maintenance is critical to long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and power to the xDrive system. It is generally regarded as a robust and dependable unit across various driving conditions.
