# 2017 BMW 320i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 320i xDrive, buyers get an entry-level luxury sport sedan equipped with standard all-wheel drive. While it offers engaging driving dynamics and refined everyday comfort, potential owners should review owner-reported areas of concern regarding cooling components, powertrain accessories, and auxiliary systems before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 180 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque, providing balanced efficiency and everyday performance. Routine maintenance is critical for long-term reliability.

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report engine overheating alerts and visible coolant puddles under the vehicle due to fluid leaks within the cooling system.
- **Solution:** Inspect hoses, fittings, and the radiator for leaks; replace failing components and refill the coolant system.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall while driving or experience difficulty starting up.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery and electrical systems; replace faulty fuel pumps or sensors as needed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The standard 8-speed ZF automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid shifts paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, generally maintaining high mechanical durability if fluid changes are performed.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake Cable Assembly

Braking components provide strong stopping power, but secondary parking brake hardware requires inspection for loose or broken retaining clips.

**Parking Brake Cable Dislodgement**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice a grinding noise coming from the center underside of the vehicle as the parking brake cable drops onto the driveshaft.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace broken retaining clips that secure the parking brake cable assembly to the vehicle underbody.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 320i xDrive provides a compelling mix of luxury, handling, and all-weather capability.
