# 2020 BMW M850i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M850i xDrive is a high-performance luxury grand tourer blending effortless speed with everyday comfort. Powered by a potent twin-turbo V8 engine paired with an advanced all-wheel-drive system, it delivers exhilarating driving dynamics and a remarkably refined cabin. Prospective buyers should review its mechanical and electronic systems carefully, as ownership costs for high-end luxury performance vehicles can be substantial.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (523 hp)

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, providing blistering acceleration. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though owners should anticipate high costs for routine upkeep and fluid changes typical of high-output BMW powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission features paddle shifters and integrates seamlessly with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid, smooth gear changes under hard acceleration and refined shifting during relaxed cruising.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V164000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The sensors in the front seat belt buckles may inaccurately detect a belted occupant as unbelted, possibly causing the air bag and seat belt pretensioners to deploy improperly in the event of a crash. If the air bag and/or seat belt pretensioners deploy improperly in the event of a crash, there is an increased risk of injury to the driver and front.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the front seat belt buckles, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M850i xDrive remains a compelling choice for buyers seeking a luxurious, high-performance grand tourer with everyday usability. While major powertrain issues are uncommon, prospective owners should budget carefully for premium maintenance, fuel, and potential out-of-warranty repairs. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified BMW specialist is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
