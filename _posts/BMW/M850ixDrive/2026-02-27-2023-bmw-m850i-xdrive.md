# 2023 BMW M850i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M850i xDrive blends high-performance grand touring capability with luxury, anchored by a powerful twin-turbo V8 engine and an all-wheel-drive system. Used-car shoppers looking at this high-end luxury coupe or convertible will find a vehicle that generally delivers strong performance without widespread preliminary owner complaint patterns in available federal tracking data. As with any heavily computerized modern BMW flagship, buyers should thoroughly inspect vehicle electronics and mechanical systems prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration and refined cruising capability. It is a sophisticated high-performance powerplant requiring strict adherence to maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission features steering wheel-mounted paddle shifters and pairs with the rear-biased xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It offers smooth daily shifts as well as aggressive performance gear changes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M850i xDrive presents a compelling option for luxury performance buyers, backed by robust powertrain options and limited initial complaint history. Prospective buyers should still prioritize a professional pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of the complex electronics, suspension, and high-output engine components.
