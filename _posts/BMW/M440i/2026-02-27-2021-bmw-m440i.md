# 2021 BMW M440i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 BMW M440i, buyers are typically looking at a high-performance luxury coupe or convertible powered by BMW's robust turbocharged inline-six engine. While early owner reports are relatively sparse, prospective buyers should still inspect key mechanical systems and verify that any dealer service actions or campaigns have been completed.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine (B58) produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration and respectable efficiency. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more reliable modern powerplants, though routine maintenance and cooling system checks remain essential.

**Cooling System Concern**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden cooling system failures or service action notices regarding auxiliary water pump assemblies.
- **Solution:** Inspect the water pump and cooling system thoroughly, and ensure all factory service actions and campaigns are up to date.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and power distribution to either the rear wheels or xDrive all-wheel drive system. It generally proves dependable under spirited driving conditions with regular fluid inspections.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Standard braking components provide strong stopping power suited for the vehicle's performance capabilities.

**Brake Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected brake system warnings or reduced braking performance.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the brake pads, rotors, sensors, and hydraulic lines for wear or faults.

## Exterior

### Windshield

Exterior glass and body panels generally maintain a solid fit and finish, though occasional glass stress issues can occur.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** A large crack may appear across the glass unexpectedly, sometimes accompanied by popping sounds while parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect the glass thoroughly prior to purchase and check with insurance or a professional auto glass specialist for replacement if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V554000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During a crash, a seat belt retractor that does not function as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the seat belt retractors (including the seat belts) and, if necessary, replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V761000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The connection between the 48-Volt battery and the starter-generator may not have been assembled properly, which can cause increased electrical resistance at the connections. Increased electrical resistance can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the connection between the 48-Volt battery and the starter-generator assembly, replacing it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V176000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Thorax](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** In certain side-impact crashes, the head-thorax air bag may not deploy as intended. An air bag that deploys incorrectly can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, at no charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M440i blends luxury and everyday drivability with thrilling performance. While limited owner complaints point to potential cooling system attention and rare glass concerns, the car remains an exciting option if given a thorough pre-purchase inspection and a verified service history.
