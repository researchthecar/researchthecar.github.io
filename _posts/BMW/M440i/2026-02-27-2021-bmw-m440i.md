# 2021 BMW M440i

### Overview
The 2021 BMW M440i, part of the G22 generation, is a high-performance coupe that blends luxury with sporty dynamics. Powered by a potent turbocharged inline-six engine, it offers exhilarating acceleration and engaging handling. As a relatively new model, widespread long-term reliability data is still emerging. However, initial reports and the inherent engineering of BMW's B58 engine suggest a strong foundation. Owners generally praise its performance, technology, and build quality. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the M440i appears to be a robust offering in its segment, with most reported concerns being minor or related to specific user experiences rather than systemic flaws. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring its longevity and optimal performance.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW M440i demonstrates strong mechanical reliability, particularly with its acclaimed B58 engine and ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, which have proven robust in other BMW applications. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the complex electrical systems, specifically the infotainment software and driver assistance sensors, which can sometimes exhibit glitches or require recalibration. These are generally not indicative of major mechanical failures but rather typical of advanced automotive technology. Exterior and interior components, along with suspension, have shown no significant widespread problems. Overall, the M440i is considered a reliable performance vehicle, provided it receives consistent maintenance and any minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
