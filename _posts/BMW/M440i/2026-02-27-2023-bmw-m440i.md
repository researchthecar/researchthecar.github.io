# 2023 BMW M440i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M440i delivers thrilling performance and modern luxury, anchored by a robust inline-six engine and refined transmission options. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from BMW's latest powertrain technology and advanced electronics. When shopping for a used model, buyers can generally expect a solid ownership experience, though verifying service history and inspecting for electronic or accessory quirks remains wise.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's most reliable and smooth modern powerplants, offering strong performance paired with respectable efficiency.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed ZF automatic transmission provides quick, seamless shifts across both rear-wheel-drive and xDrive all-wheel-drive configurations. It is known for strong durability and responsive behavior under dynamic driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M440i stands out as an appealing choice for used luxury performance car buyers, combining everyday drivability with potent engineering. While major mechanical failures are rarely reported for this model year, prospective buyers should always perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and review maintenance records to ensure optimal condition.
