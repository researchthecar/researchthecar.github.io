# 2023 BMW 330i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 330i stands out as a strong contender in the luxury sport sedan segment, offering sharp handling and refined performance. For used car buyers looking at this model year, understanding the powertrain layout and monitoring early owner feedback can help guide a confident purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It provides a balanced mix of brisk acceleration and daily efficiency, generally proving reliable in modern BMW applications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers swift, smooth shifts across rear-wheel and all-wheel drive configurations. It is widely regarded as one of the most dependable and responsive automatic units in the industry.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW 330i presents an appealing option for used luxury sedan buyers, backed by a robust powertrain and strong overall performance. Prospective buyers should still perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to ensure all vehicle systems are in top condition.
