# 2007 BMW 330i

### Overview
The 2007 BMW 330i, part of the E90 generation, is generally regarded as a solid performer with a good balance of sportiness and luxury. It was offered with a potent naturally aspirated inline-six engine and a choice of manual or automatic transmissions. While BMWs of this era are known for their driving dynamics, like any vehicle, they can experience specific issues as they age. This data focuses on common, documented problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. Understanding these potential issues can help in proactive maintenance and budgeting for repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L N52B30 Inline-6
**Valve Cover Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Oil smell in the engine bay, visible oil on the engine exterior, blue smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the valve cover gasket and potentially the valve cover itself if warped.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil consumption, whistling noises from the engine, potential for oil leaks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $450

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual (Getrag GS6-37BZ)
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


### 6-Speed Automatic (ZF 6HP19)
**Mechatronic Sleeve Seal Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fluid leaks, potential for transmission slippage or harsh shifting.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the mechatronic sleeve and seals.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front lower control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Strut Mount Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Popping or creaking noises when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the front strut mounts.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Power Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Window not moving up or down, clicking noises from the door panel.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window regulator assembly.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** HVAC fan only works on the highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.

- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**Sunroof Drain Clog**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks into the cabin, musty smell, potential damage to electronics.
- **Solution:** Clearing of the sunroof drainage tubes.
- **Cost:** $100 - $200

**iDrive Controller Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent or complete failure of the iDrive controller buttons or scroll wheel.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the iDrive controller unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2007 BMW 330i (E90) offers an engaging driving experience, but like many premium vehicles of its age, it is not without potential issues. The naturally aspirated 3.0L inline-six engine is generally robust, but common concerns include oil leaks from the valve cover gasket and PCV system, as well as potential failure of the electric water pump. The automatic transmission can sometimes exhibit issues with the mechatronic sleeve or torque converter. Suspension components like control arm bushings and strut mounts are also known to wear over time, leading to noise and handling degradation. Electrical gremlins, such as power window regulators and HVAC resistors, can occur. Interior issues like clogged sunroof drains are also noted. Despite these potential points of attention, with diligent maintenance and addressing issues promptly, the 2007 330i can remain a reliable and enjoyable vehicle.
