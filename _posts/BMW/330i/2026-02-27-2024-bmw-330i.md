# 2024 BMW 330i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 330i continues to offer a compelling mix of dynamic performance and daily luxury, anchored by its modern powertrain options. As a relatively new model year on the pre-owned market, it benefits from contemporary engineering and advanced technology features. Prospective buyers exploring used models should review standard inspection practices, keeping in mind that owner-reported feedback remains limited for this recent production cycle.

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and refined power delivery, establishing itself as a robust daily driver option.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission offers smooth, rapid shifts paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive configurations. It is widely praised for its responsiveness and overall mechanical durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V770000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear may not have been tightened properly, which can allow it to loosen. A loose steering gear can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
