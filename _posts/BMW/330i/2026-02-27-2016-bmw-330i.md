# 2016 BMW 330i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 330i represents a solid entry in the compact luxury sport sedan segment, offering a balance of performance and daily drivability. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should carefully review service records and perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to account for potential age-related wear.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (B48) produces approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It generally provides smooth power delivery and decent efficiency, though routine maintenance is crucial for longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, refined shifts and pairs well with the engine options. It is widely regarded as a robust and dependable unit when properly maintained.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW 330i can be an enjoyable and rewarding pre-owned luxury car for buyers who prioritize driving dynamics. Prioritize examples with comprehensive maintenance histories, and budget for ongoing upkeep typical of a German luxury vehicle.
