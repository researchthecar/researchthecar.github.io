# 2021 BMW M850i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW M850i xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury four-door that blends formidable twin-turbo V8 power with sophisticated grand touring comfort. As a flagship model, it delivers blistering acceleration and refined road manners, though prospective buyers should approach its complex electronics and high-strung powertrain with careful mechanical scrutiny. Independent owner feedback for this model year is generally light, reflecting its low-volume exclusivity, but standard luxury car maintenance and diagnostic diligence remain essential.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (523 hp / 553 lb-ft)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 produces 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, delivering immense power across the rev range. This engine is generally robust when maintained meticulously, though its high complexity and thermal loads mean routine servicing is critical.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and integrates with the rear-biased xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It offers rapid, smooth gear changes and has proven highly dependable in high-output BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V062000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The rotor and shaft within the integrated brake system may not have been welded properly and could separate during a hard braking event, possibly resulting in a loss of the Antilock Brake System (ABS) and braking assistance. A loss of ABS and a reduction in braking assistance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the integrated brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
