# 2023 BMW M850i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M850i xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury four-door that blends effortless speed with everyday usability. Buyers looking at a pre-owned model will find a remarkably robust vehicle backed by minimal reported owner complaints in its initial years on the market, though prospective owners should always budget for the higher-tier maintenance typical of modern high-output German luxury cars.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (523 hp)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and smooth power delivery. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though its complex turbocharging and cooling systems require diligent fluid monitoring.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and seamlessly distributes power to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is widely regarded as one of the best torque-converter automatics on the market for both responsiveness and long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M850i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M850i xDrive Gran Coupe presents a very solid profile for used luxury car buyers, supported by a lack of widespread recurring owner complaints. Prospective purchasers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection to verify mechanical and electronic health before finalizing a deal.
