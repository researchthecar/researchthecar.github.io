# 2019 BMW 520i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury executive sedan, the 2019 BMW 5-Series offers sophisticated styling, advanced technology, and refined performance. However, prospective buyers should carefully review owner-reported complaints spanning various mechanical and electrical domains to ensure long-term satisfaction and avoid unexpected repair costs.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces smooth power and dependable efficiency for everyday driving. Regular maintenance of cooling and filtration components is key to ensuring long-term reliability.

**Oil Filter Housing Degradation**
- **Symptoms:** Plastic oil filter housing components can degrade over time, potentially leading to fluid leaks or engine performance concerns.
- **Solution:** Inspect the plastic oil filter housing during routine maintenance and replace it with an updated unit if signs of wear or cracking appear.
- **Cost:** Expensive job

**Engine Stalling and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances of rough idle, black smoke emission, overheating, and sudden engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the fuel delivery, cooling system, and electrical sensors to identify the root cause of stalling.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine delivers robust horsepower and torque for enthusiastic drivers. It is generally regarded as a strong and durable powertrain choice within the lineup.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides rapid, seamless gear changes across all driving modes. It is widely praised for refinement and overall mechanical durability.

**Inadvertent Gear Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted instances where actuating the electronic shifter while in motion inadvertently shifts the transmission into neutral.
- **Solution:** Exercise caution when interacting with the electronic gear selector lever and ensure software is up to date.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Camera System

Electrical architecture supports numerous advanced driver assistance and convenience features, though occasional software glitches can affect displays.

**Intermittent Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display occasionally goes black when shifting into reverse, hindering rearward visibility.
- **Solution:** Update the vehicle software or inspect the camera wiring harness and display module connections.

## Safety

### Passenger Restraint System

Safety equipment includes comprehensive airbag and restraint arrays designed to protect occupants during collisions.

**Passenger Restraint Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Warning messages regarding the passenger restraint system appear on the instrument cluster, indicating a potential sensor or airbag fault.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer or a specialized technician diagnose the specific airbag or seat belt sensor fault code and replace faulty components.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 5-Series remains a compelling choice for used luxury sedan buyers thanks to its engaging driving dynamics and premium features. While most mechanical systems prove robust, prospective owners should thoroughly inspect potential cooling, electrical, and restraint system concerns prior to purchase.
