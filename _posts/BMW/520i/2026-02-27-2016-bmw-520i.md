# 2016 BMW 520i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 520i offers a blend of luxury, refinement, and balanced everyday performance for used-car buyers looking for an executive sedan. While it generally provides a comfortable ride and upscale interior amenities, prospective buyers should carefully review service histories and check for any lingering mechanical quirks typical of turbocharged luxury vehicles of this vintage.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (B48) produces approximately 181 horsepower and 214 lb-ft of torque. It offers smooth power delivery and decent efficiency, representing a reliable modern powerplant when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless gear changes and pairs well with the engine. It is widely regarded as a robust and dependable unit across various driving conditions.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, the 2016 BMW 520i remains an attractive choice for those seeking a premium driving experience, provided it has been well-maintained by previous owners. Buyers are advised to prioritize examples with comprehensive service records and to perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection before finalizing a deal.
