# 2018 BMW 520i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 BMW 5 Series offers a sophisticated blend of luxury, comfort, and advanced technology. While generally well-regarded, used car buyers should pay attention to recurring owner reports involving electrical issues, cooling system hiccups, and passenger seat occupant sensor malfunctions.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (B48) delivers around 180 to 248 horsepower depending on the specific tune, offering a smooth power delivery. It is generally reliable with standard maintenance, though owners must monitor for occasional cooling system leaks.

**Coolant Leak and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may display high coolant temperature warnings and indicate that coolant needs to be replenished unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Inspect cooling lines, water pump, and radiator components for leaks; replace damaged hoses or failing water pumps as needed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The widely used 8-speed ZF automatic transmission provides quick and seamless gear shifts. It has a strong reputation for durability when fluid changes are performed at recommended intervals.

## Safety

### Passenger Occupant Sensor

Safety systems generally perform well, though the front passenger seat sensor can trigger intermittent warnings.

**Restraint System Malfunction and Seat Belt Warning**
- **Symptoms:** A warning light illuminates on the dashboard, and error messages pop up regarding the passenger restraint system or seat belt sensors failing to recognize an occupant.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the passenger seat occupancy sensor mat or module; replace the faulty sensor assembly.
- **Cost:** $2800

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 BMW 5 Series is a comfortable and capable luxury sedan that rewards proactive maintenance. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the service history, test all electronic systems and safety warnings, and ensure the cooling components have been inspected for any signs of leakage.
