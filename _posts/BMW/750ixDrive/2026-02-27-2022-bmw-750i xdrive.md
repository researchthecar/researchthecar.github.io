### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, connectivity issues (Bluetooth, Apple CarPlay/Android Auto), navigation errors.

### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, battery warning lights, electrical components malfunctioning due to low voltage.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, potential battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 750i xDrive demonstrates strong reliability in its core powertrain components, with the 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 and 8-speed automatic transmission generally performing without widespread issues. However, potential owners should be aware of isolated concerns within the electrical system, particularly concerning infotainment software glitches and intermittent battery drain, which can lead to starting problems. The adaptive air suspension, while offering superior comfort, has a documented susceptibility to air spring leaks, which can be a significant repair cost. Exterior and interior components are largely free from common defects. Overall, the 750i xDrive offers a reliable luxury experience, but vigilance regarding electrical and suspension systems is advised for long-term ownership.
