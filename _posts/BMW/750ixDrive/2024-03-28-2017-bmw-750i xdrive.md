The 2017 BMW 750i xDrive is a luxury sedan powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 750i is known for its spacious interior, advanced technology features, and powerful performance. However, it also has some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **N63B44T2 V8 Engine**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** The turbochargers may fail, causing a loss of power and increased exhaust smoke.
        * Solution: Replace the turbochargers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * **Fuel injector failure:** The fuel injectors may fail, causing rough idling, misfires, and reduced fuel economy.
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * **Oil leaks:** The engine may develop oil leaks from various gaskets and seals.
        * Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets and seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

* **ZF 8HP75 eight-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting problems:** The transmission may experience shifting problems, such as delayed shifts, hard shifts, or slipping gears.
        * Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure:** The air suspension system may fail, causing the vehicle to sag or ride unevenly.
        * Solution: Replace the air suspension components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **Control arm bushings worn:** The control arm bushings may wear out, causing clunking or rattling noises from the suspension.
        * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The vehicle may experience a battery drain due to a faulty electrical component or a parasitic draw.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical issue.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Electrical module failure:** The electrical modules in the vehicle may fail, causing various electrical issues.
        * Solution: Replace the faulty electrical module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** The headlights may develop condensation inside due to a faulty seal.
        * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Interior**

* **Trim panel rattles:** The interior trim panels may rattle or squeak due to loose fasteners or poor fitment.
        * Solution: Tighten the fasteners or replace the trim panels.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Infotainment system glitches:** The infotainment system may experience glitches or freezing.
        * Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the infotainment unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Recalls**

* NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V415000
    * Issue: The fuel level sensor may fail, providing inaccurate fuel level readings.
* NHTSA Campaign Number: 17V416000
    * Issue: The front seat belt buckle may not latch properly, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.

**Summary**

The 2017 BMW 750i xDrive has a number of reported issues, including turbocharger failures, fuel injector issues, and transmission problems. However, it is important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues. The severity and cost of the repairs vary depending on the specific issue encountered.
