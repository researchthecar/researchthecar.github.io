**Introduction:**

The 2019 BMW 750i xDrive is a luxury sedan powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine paired to an 8-speed automatic transmission. It produces 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. The 750i xDrive offers a smooth and powerful driving experience, along with a well-appointed interior and advanced technology features. However, it has faced its share of issues since its release.

**Engine**

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Carbon buildup on the intake valves reduces airflow, leading to reduced engine power and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Remove and clean the intake valves.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger wastegate actuator malfunctions, causing reduced turbo boost pressure and decreased engine performance.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger wastegate actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Shock:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A harsh or delayed shift between gears, accompanied by a jerking motion.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Suspension Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunction of the adaptive suspension system, leading to a rough or unbalanced ride.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair or replacement of faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **iDrive System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The iDrive infotainment system experiences crashes, freezes, or intermittent connectivity issues.
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of the iDrive control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is parked and turned off.
    * Solution: Identification and repair of the source of the parasitic drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drainage Tube Clogging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clogged sunroof drainage tubes lead to water leaks into the cabin.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing the sunroof drainage tubes.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Panel Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control panel malfunctions, causing issues with temperature control and air conditioning.
    * Solution: Replacement of the climate control panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2019 BMW 750i xDrive has faced several issues, including intake valve deposits, turbocharger wastegate actuator failure, shift shock, suspension failure, electrical malfunctions, sunroof drainage tube clogging, and climate control panel failure. While some issues are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix, others can be more severe and costly. Potential owners should be aware of these potential issues before purchasing this vehicle.
