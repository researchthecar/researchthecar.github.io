# 2016 BMW 750i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 750i xDrive offers flagship luxury, advanced technology, and authoritative performance from its twin-turbo V8. For used-car buyers, while the driving experience is exceptional, the complex engineering requires careful inspection and diligent maintenance history review to avoid unexpected ownership costs.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 445 horsepower and 480 lb-ft of torque, delivering swift acceleration. It is a high-output, thermally stressed engine that demands strict adherence to maintenance intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, offering seamless and rapid gear changes. This transmission is generally robust when serviced properly.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2016 BMW 750i xDrive, prioritize models with comprehensive service records and professional pre-purchase inspections. While major recurring issues may not dominate the owner complaint record, the advanced electronics and high-performance powertrain call for a healthy maintenance budget.
