# 2020 BMW 750i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 BMW 750i xDrive, buyers will find a flagship luxury sedan offering exceptional performance, high-end comfort, and advanced technology. While initial owner feedback highlights few widespread mechanical failures, prospective purchasers should carefully evaluate complex luxury electronics and powertrain health during pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 Engine

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless acceleration. It is generally robust, though proper maintenance and regular fluid changes are vital for long-term turbocharger and timing chain longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features ZF planetary gearsets paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, delivering seamless and quick shifts. It is widely regarded as one of the most reliable and refined transmissions in the luxury segment.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V513000: Fuel System, Other:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=750i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The high pressure fuel pump may not have been properly attached to the engine, which may cause the fuel pump flange to crack, resulting in a fuel leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the high pressure fuel pumps, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW 750i xDrive is an impressively engineered luxury sedan that balances immense power with refined daily drivability. Because owner-reported complaints are sparse and major recurring mechanical defects are not heavily documented in early feedback, buyers should focus on verifying service history and ensuring all advanced technology features operate correctly.
