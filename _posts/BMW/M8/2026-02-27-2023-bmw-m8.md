# 2023 BMW M8 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M8 stands as a formidable high-performance luxury cruiser, blending blistering twin-turbo V8 power with upscale cabin appointments and sophisticated grand touring capabilities. As a relatively fresh luxury performance vehicle, it generally benefits from modern engineering and advanced powertrain tech, though prospective buyers should still carefully evaluate complex electronics and high-performance maintenance costs.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8

This high-output 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque in Competition specification, offering ferocious acceleration paired with sophisticated valvetrain technology. It is a robust powerhouse when properly maintained, though its high-strung performance nature demands diligent fluid changes and premium fuel.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed M Sport Automatic

An 8-speed M Sport automatic transmission routes power smoothly and aggressively to the rear-biased M xDrive all-wheel-drive system. This unit is well-matched to the engine's power band, delivering rapid gear shifts under hard driving and refined cruising manners on the highway.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M8&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M8 delivers world-class luxury and supercar-rivaling performance. Because this model year is still relatively young and complex, buyers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections, verify service histories, and budget for the higher ongoing maintenance costs typical of elite German performance cars.
