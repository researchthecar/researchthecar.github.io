**Introduction:**

The 2022 BMW M760i xDrive is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V12 engine. It produces an impressive 600 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. Praised for its thrilling performance, luxurious interior, and advanced technology, complaints have surfaced regarding certain aspects of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**Twin-Turbocharged 6.6L V12**

* **Intake Valve Deposit Buildup**
   * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine performance, increased fuel consumption, rough idle.
   * Solution: Clean or replace intake valves.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
   * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noises from the engine, performance issues.
   * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Control Module Failure**
   * Description/Symptoms: Gear shifting issues, transmission slipping, check engine light.
   * Solution: Reprogram or replace transmission control module.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Control Arm Bushing Wear**
   * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or clunking noises while driving, uneven tire wear.
   * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
   * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to start, slow engine crank.
   * Solution: Replace battery or check for parasitic drain.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Corrosion**
   * Description/Symptoms: Door handles seize or become difficult to operate.
   * Solution: Replace door handles.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag**
   * Description/Symptoms: Slow response times, unresponsive touchscreen, software glitches.
   * Solution: Software updates.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free of charge
   * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
   * Description/Symptoms: System fails to regulate temperature or airflow.
   * Solution: Reprogram or replace climate control module.
   * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
   * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 BMW M760i xDrive offers exhilarating performance but has experienced some reliability issues. Engine problems can be costly to fix, with intake valve deposit buildup and timing chain tensioner failures being the most significant concerns. The transmission control module may also malfunction, leading to transmission issues. Suspension components can wear prematurely, and electrical problems such as battery discharge have been reported. Minor interior issues like infotainment lag and climate control malfunctions may arise. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues vary, and not all vehicles will experience these problems.
