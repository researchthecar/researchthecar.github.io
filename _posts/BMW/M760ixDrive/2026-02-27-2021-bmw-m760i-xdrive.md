# 2021 BMW M760i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW M760i xDrive stands at the pinnacle of luxury performance, featuring a formidable V12 powerplant designed for effortless high-speed cruising. For prospective used-car buyers, this flagship sedan offers unmatched prestige and advanced engineering, though upkeep and complexity require careful inspection.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

This 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 produces approximately 601 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration with supreme refinement. While mechanically robust when properly maintained, buyers should anticipate high maintenance overhead due to the dense engine bay and complex auxiliary systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with xDrive all-wheel drive, this 8-speed automatic provides seamless and rapid gear changes suited to the V12's immense power output. It is generally reliable under normal operation, provided fluid changes are performed according to schedule.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M760i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V379000: Child Seat:Tether: Connector/Clip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M760i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The top child seat tether anchor on the rear middle seat may not be readily accessible. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 225, Section 6.1(b) "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." A child restraint system placed on the vehicle's rear middle seat without utilizing the three-point.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will install a top tether anchor cover at the rear middle seating position. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW M760i xDrive is an extraordinary engineering achievement offering supreme comfort and blistering V12 performance. While formal owner complaints are exceptionally sparse, prospective buyers must remain mindful of the inherently high operational and maintenance costs associated with a flagship luxury European performance vehicle.
