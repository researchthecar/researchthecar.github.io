**Introduction:**

The 2017 BMW M760i xDrive is a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V12 engine mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. With 601 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, the M760i can accelerate from 0-60 mph in under 4 seconds. It also features a sophisticated all-wheel-drive system for improved handling and stability. The M760i is praised for its potent engine, comfortable ride, and luxurious interior, but criticisms include its high price and thirsty fuel economy.

**Engine**

* **Twin-turbocharged 6.6-liter V12 (N74B66)**
    * **Turbocharger failure:** Premature failure of the turbochargers can cause reduced engine power, excessive smoke, and engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace faulty turbochargers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,000+ per turbocharger
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Fuel injector issues:** Faulty fuel injectors can cause rough idling, misfiring, and reduced fuel efficiency.
        * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $900+ per injector
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic (8HP76)**
    * **Transmission slipping:** In some cases, the transmission may slip or shudder while shifting, resulting in poor performance.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the transmission valve body.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive M suspension:**
    * **Air suspension failure:** Malfunctioning air struts or air compressor can lead to an uneven ride height or suspension collapse.
        * Solution: Replace faulty air struts or compressor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+ per axle
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery issues:** Some vehicles have experienced premature battery failure, resulting in electrical problems and difficulty starting.
        * Solution: Replace faulty battery.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Software glitches:** Occasional software glitches can affect various electronic systems, such as the infotainment system or driver assistance features.
        * Solution: Update or reset the vehicle's software.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of the issue
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Plastic trim deformation:** Certain plastic trim pieces, particularly around the wheel arches, can deform or dislodge over time.
        * Solution: Replace or repair affected trim pieces.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Panoramic sunroof rattling:** Some vehicles have experienced rattling or noise from the panoramic sunroof.
        * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof tracks and seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment system lag:** The infotainment system can sometimes experience lag or slow response times.
        * Solution: Update or reset the vehicle's software.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the severity of the issue
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2017 BMW M760i xDrive is a highly capable and luxurious vehicle, it is not without its potential issues. The most common problems seem to be related to the turbochargers, fuel injectors, transmission, and air suspension. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, so potential buyers should be aware of the potential expenses associated with ownership.
