# 2019 BMW M760i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW M760i xDrive sits at the pinnacle of the 7 Series lineup, offering flagship luxury paired with an immensely powerful V12 engine. For prospective used-car buyers, this ultra-high-end sedan delivers exceptional performance and refinement, though ownership demands careful attention to maintenance and potential repair costs associated with its complex engineering.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 600 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and immense acceleration. It is a highly complex powerplant that requires rigorous maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, providing seamless and rapid gear changes. This transmission is generally robust and well-matched to the high torque output of the V12 engine.

## Safety

### Passenger Seat Restraint Sensor

Safety equipment on the M760i xDrive is extensive, though occasional owner reports highlight minor electronic sensor glitches within the cabin.

**Seat Bottom Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Warning lights regarding the seatbelt and restraint systems intermittently appear on the dashboard while an occupant is seated in the front passenger seat.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the passenger seat bottom sensor and replace the faulty occupancy detection component as needed.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW M760i xDrive is an extraordinary luxury sedan offering blistering performance and supreme comfort, backed by limited owner complaints outside of isolated interior sensor glitches. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable specialist to ensure all complex electronic and mechanical systems are in top condition.
