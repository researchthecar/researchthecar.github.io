## 2021 BMW M760i xDrive

The 2021 BMW M760i xDrive is a high-performance luxury sedan that slots in above the M5 in BMW's lineup. It features a twin-turbocharged 4.4-liter V8 engine that produces 617 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. Other engine and transmission options are not available.

The M760i is praised for its impressive power, luxurious interior, and advanced technology. However, it is criticized for its high price and stiff ride quality.

### Engine

**4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Spark plug failure:** Misfires, rough idling, reduced power. Solution: Replace spark plugs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
* **Fuel injector failure:** Hard starting, engine hesitation, reduced power. Solution: Replace fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Turbocharger failure:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, unusual noises. Solution: Replace turbocharger. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* **No Recalls Found**

### Transmission

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission shudder:** Jerking or vibration during gear changes. Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission components if necessary. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* **No Recalls Found**

### Suspension

* **Air suspension failure:** Suspension becomes stiff or unresponsive, vehicle may sag. Solution: Replace air suspension components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Strut failure:** Knocking or clunking noises, poor handling. Solution: Replace struts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **No Recalls Found**

### Electrical

* **Battery drainage:** Battery drains prematurely, vehicle may not start. Solution: Replace battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Infotainment system glitches:** Touchscreen unresponsive, software crashes. Solution: Update software or replace infotainment system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Electrical harness failure:** Various electrical issues such as flickering lights, power window failure. Solution: Replace electrical harness. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Recall Number: 22V-803** - Electrically Assisted Steering Motor Bolt May Not Be Properly Tightened
* **Recall Number: 22V-117** - Electric Coolant Pump May Not Be Properly Secured

### Exterior

* **Headlight condensation:** Moisture builds up inside headlights. Solution: Replace headlights. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Taillight failure:** Taillights fail to illuminate or function improperly. Solution: Replace taillights or repair wiring harness. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
* **No Recalls Found**

### Interior

* **Climate control issues:** Climate control system fails to maintain desired temperature, air conditioning or heating does not work properly. Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace climate control system components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Steering wheel buttons malfunction:** Buttons on the steering wheel become unresponsive or sticky. Solution: Replace steering wheel buttons or control module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Power seat failure:** Power seats fail to adjust or move properly. Solution: Repair or replace power seat motors or control module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* **No Recalls Found**

### Summary

The 2021 BMW M760i xDrive is a powerful and luxurious performance sedan with advanced features. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that have been reported by owners. Some of the more common and costly issues include spark plug failure, fuel injector failure, turbocharger failure, transmission shudder, and air suspension failure. Before purchasing a used M760i, it is recommended to have it thoroughly inspected by a qualified mechanic to identify any potential problems.
