# 2020 BMW M760i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M760i xDrive stands as an ultra-luxury flagship sedan powered by an immense V12 engine, offering extraordinary performance paired with high-end refinement. For prospective used-car buyers, this high-performance luxury cruiser demands rigorous pre-purchase inspections due to the profound complexity of its drivetrain and high-end electronics. While formal complaint volume remains low, ownership upkeep and potential maintenance liabilities on a specialized twelve-cylinder BMW require careful consideration.

---

## Engine

### 6.6L Twin-Turbocharged V12

The 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 600 horsepower and 627 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless and monumental acceleration. Buyers should expect intense maintenance complexity and specialized servicing requirements typical of low-volume high-performance BMW powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, the 8-speed automatic transmission provides exceptionally smooth and rapid gear changes. This transmission is generally robust, though proper fluid maintenance is critical for long-term health.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M760i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V379000: Child Seat:Tether: Connector/Clip](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M760i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The top child seat tether anchor on the rear middle seat may not be readily accessible. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 225, Section 6.1(b) "Child Restraint Anchorage Systems." A child restraint system placed on the vehicle's rear middle seat without utilizing the three-point.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will install a top tether anchor cover at the rear middle seating position. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M760i xDrive is an engineering marvel that rewards drivers with unmatched V12 prestige and effortless power, but it comes with the complex ownership overhead typical of ultra-luxury performance cars. Buyers should prioritize a comprehensive inspection by a marque specialist and verify the service history before purchase.
