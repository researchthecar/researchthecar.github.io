# 2024 BMW 230i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 230i xDrive offers a blend of sporty handling, all-weather confidence, and refined everyday luxury. As a relatively recent model year, owner feedback and formal complaint histories are sparse, pointing to a generally solid ownership experience thus far. Buyers looking at this luxury sport coupe can typically expect strong performance paired with modern engineering, though keeping up with routine maintenance remains essential.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, with a generally favorable reliability outlook in modern BMW applications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Paired standard with xDrive all-wheel drive, this 8-speed automatic unit delivers quick, smooth shifts and robust durability under normal driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V770000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=230i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear may not have been tightened properly, which can allow it to loosen. A loose steering gear can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 230i xDrive presents a very clean initial reliability profile with no widespread complaint patterns reported by owners. Prospective buyers should still conduct a standard pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to ensure long-term peace of mind.
