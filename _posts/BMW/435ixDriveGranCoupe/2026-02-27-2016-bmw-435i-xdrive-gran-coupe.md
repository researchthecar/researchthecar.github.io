# 2016 BMW 435i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 435i xDrive Gran Coupe blends sleek four-door styling with athletic performance, making it an appealing option for used luxury car buyers. Equipped with standard all-wheel drive and a potent turbocharged inline-six engine, it delivers a dynamic driving experience. While it offers strong luxury credentials, used buyers should carefully inspect service records and common wear items to ensure long-term reliability.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as robust and powerful, though buyers should watch for routine oil leaks and cooling system wear as mileage climbs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission with Paddle Shifters

The 8-speed ZF automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and pairs seamlessly with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is widely considered exceptionally reliable with proper fluid maintenance.
