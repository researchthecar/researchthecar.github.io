# 2016 BMW 328i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 BMW 328i xDrive, buyers will find a sporty and capable luxury sedan equipped with all-wheel drive. While the vehicle offers engaging driving dynamics and upscale comfort, prospective owners should review potential mechanical and electrical areas to ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (N20/B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 240 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent fuel economy, though buyers should watch for routine maintenance items typical of modern German turbo engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, offering crisp, smooth shifts and durable long-term performance.
