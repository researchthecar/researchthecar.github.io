# 2018 BMW M6 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2018 BMW M6 Gran Coupe means buying into high-performance luxury, featuring a twin-turbo V8 engine and a sophisticated chassis setup. While owner reports for this high-end model are relatively sparse, buyers should remain mindful of the complex electronics and advanced suspension systems that underpin its sporty ride.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8

The 2018 BMW M6 Gran Coupe is powered by a 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing approximately 560 to 600 horsepower. This high-output powerplant delivers thrilling acceleration, though proper maintenance and warm-up routines are critical for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Power is routed through a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission designed for rapid shifts under aggressive driving. It offers smooth operation in daily driving mode alongside sharp, immediate responses when pushed.

## Suspension

### Virtual Dynamics Management Module

Advanced adaptive suspension components help maintain ride quality and handling stability, though electronic control units can occasionally experience faults.

**Suspension Balance and Height Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may suddenly lose its suspension balance and ride height, resulting in a bouncy and hazardous driving experience.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic suspension modules and related wiring thoroughly inspected, and replace the faulty Virtual Dynamics Management module if required.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V094000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M6&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The third brake light may become loose, possibly allowing the light to detach from the vehicle. If the third brake light detaches from the vehicle, it would no longer provide the required lighting to following traffic, or it may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the third brake light and properly secure it with new fasteners. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 BMW M6 Gran Coupe offers exhilarating performance and striking styling, making it a compelling pre-owned luxury buy. Prospective owners should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the complex electronic, suspension, and powertrain systems to ensure trouble-free ownership.
