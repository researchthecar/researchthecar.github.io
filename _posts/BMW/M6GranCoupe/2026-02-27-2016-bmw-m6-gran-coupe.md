# 2016 BMW M6 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 BMW M6 Gran Coupe, buyers are greeted by a high-performance luxury sedan powered by a formidable twin-turbo V8 engine. While driver reports indicate thrilling performance and upscale amenities, used vehicle shoppers should carefully inspect complex electrical systems and safety restraints, as owner complaints point to occasional electronic and airbag malfunctions.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 Engine (S63)

This 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine generates approximately 560 horsepower and 500 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration. Proper maintenance and cooling system checks are essential for long-term health when purchasing this performance model.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

Equipped with a quick-shifting 7-speed dual-clutch transmission featuring Drivelogic, this setup provides lightning-fast gear changes tailored to spirited driving. Buyers should test-shift thoroughly across all modes to ensure smooth clutch engagement.

## Electrical

### Body Electrical and Wiring

The complex electrical architecture demands a thorough pre-purchase inspection to rule out any underlying wiring or module anomalies.

**Electrical System Short or Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Isolated reports have noted unexpected electrical failures, with extreme owner accounts citing smoke or fire originating near upper cabin areas.
- **Solution:** Inspect all visible wiring harnesses, fuses, and modules for signs of wear, corrosion, or aftermarket modification.

## Safety

### Passenger Restraint and Airbag System

Owner feedback highlights rare concerns regarding airbag and restraint system warnings that require professional diagnostic scans.

**Passenger Restraint Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported warning messages indicating passenger restraint or airbag system malfunctions, sometimes accompanied by a loss of power to the passenger side.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the supplemental restraint system (SRS) module and inspect seat sensors or wiring harnesses for faults.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V094000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M6&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The third brake light may become loose, possibly allowing the light to detach from the vehicle. If the third brake light detaches from the vehicle, it would no longer provide the required lighting to following traffic, or it may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the third brake light and properly secure it with new fasteners. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 BMW M6 Gran Coupe offers breathtaking luxury and supercar performance, but its high-complexity systems require diligent maintenance history checks. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electronics, safety restraints, and powertrain health to ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
