# 2017 BMW M6 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 BMW M6 Gran Coupe is a high-performance luxury four-door combining striking design with aggressive twin-turbo V8 power. As with many high-end European performance cars, prospective buyers should carefully review maintenance records and inspect potential trouble spots before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (S63)

The 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 560 to 600 horsepower, delivering immense acceleration and dynamic performance. Buyers should ensure regular maintenance intervals were strictly followed to preserve long-term engine health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes suited to the high-output V8 engine. Proper fluid changes and smooth operation during test drives are essential for verifying its condition.

## Brakes

### High-Mount Brake Light and Trim

Exterior body fittings and lighting assemblies generally hold up well, though occasional trim and seal inspections are recommended.

**Rear Third Brake Light Assembly Loosening**
- **Symptoms:** The rear third brake light can become loose and detach from its mounting position, potentially leading to water leaks into the cabin and trunk areas.
- **Solution:** Inspect the mounting nuts behind the interior trim, secure or replace loose hardware, and check for any moisture accumulation or mold in the affected areas.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V094000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The third brake light may become loose, possibly allowing the light to detach from the vehicle. If the third brake light detaches from the vehicle, it would no longer provide the required lighting to following traffic, or it may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the third brake light and properly secure it with new fasteners. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M6&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW M6 Gran Coupe offers breathtaking luxury and supercar-rivaling performance for the discerning enthusiast. While major mechanical failures are not widely reported in standard owner complaint logs, meticulous pre-purchase inspections and verified service histories remain vital due to the complex engineering and high cost of upkeep.
