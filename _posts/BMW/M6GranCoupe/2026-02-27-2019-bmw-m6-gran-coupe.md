# 2019 BMW M6 Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW M6 Gran Coupe represents the pinnacle of high-performance luxury, combining aggressive styling with ferocious twin-turbo V8 power. As a complex, high-end German performance machine, prospective buyers should approach used models with careful consideration of maintenance history and potential upkeep costs.

---

## Engine

### 4.4L Twin-Turbo V8 (S63)

The S63 4.4-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces around 560 to 600 horsepower, delivering staggering acceleration and high-end performance. Proper maintenance and timely servicing are critical to ensuring the longevity of this sophisticated powerplant.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers lightning-fast gear changes suited for both track driving and relaxed cruising. It requires regular fluid changes to maintain smooth operation over high mileage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V094000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M6&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The third brake light may become loose, possibly allowing the light to detach from the vehicle. If the third brake light detaches from the vehicle, it would no longer provide the required lighting to following traffic, or it may become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the third brake light and properly secure it with new fasteners. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
