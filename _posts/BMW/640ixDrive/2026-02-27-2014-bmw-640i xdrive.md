# 2014 BMW 640i xDrive

### Overview
The 2014 BMW 640i xDrive, a luxurious grand tourer, generally offers a high level of performance and comfort. It is equipped with a potent turbocharged inline-six engine and an advanced xDrive all-wheel-drive system, aiming to provide a refined driving experience. While BMW vehicles are known for their engineering prowess, like any complex machine, some areas may experience specific issues over time. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and prospective buyers of this model year.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)
**Turbocharger Wastegate Actuator**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light, reduced engine power, rattling noise from the turbo area.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the wastegate actuator or the entire turbocharger assembly.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, oil leaks, blue smoke from exhaust, increased oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, check engine light with temperature warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 BMW 640i xDrive is a well-engineered vehicle with a generally strong reliability record. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the turbocharged engine, specifically the wastegate actuator, PCV valve, and electric water pump, which can require attention over time. The transmission is robust, and the suspension, while featuring advanced systems, can occasionally develop issues with active components. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the iDrive system and potential battery drain, are also noted. Despite these potential issues, with proper maintenance and timely repairs, the 640i xDrive can remain a dependable and enjoyable luxury vehicle.
