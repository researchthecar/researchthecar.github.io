# 2016 BMW 640i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 640i xDrive blends luxury grand touring with BMW's robust turbocharged inline-six powerplant and confident all-wheel drive. While it offers refined performance and sophisticated styling, prospective used-car buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle maintenance history and electronics complexity before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more durable modern powerplants, though proper maintenance and oil change intervals are critical.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features ZF gearsets paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, delivering seamless and quick shifts. This transmission is generally reliable under normal driving conditions when fluid maintenance is observed.
