# 2017 BMW 640i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 640i xDrive, buyers can generally expect a luxurious grand tourer with strong performance credentials. While official owner-reported data for this specific configuration is sparse, evaluating potential electrical quirks and powertrain health during a pre-purchase inspection remains a wise approach.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55/B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It delivers smooth power delivery and is generally considered more reliable than the V8 alternatives in the lineup.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with xDrive all-wheel drive to provide rapid, refined shifts. It is widely praised for durability and smooth operation when maintained properly.

## Exterior

### Trunk Release Mechanism

Electrical accessories and trunk mechanisms should be thoroughly tested during a pre-purchase inspection to catch rare latch or switch failures.

**Trunk Latch and Access Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The trunk may fail to open electronically, creating difficulty accessing the rear storage area when interior folding seat release levers are also located inside the trunk.
- **Solution:** Inspect the trunk latch wiring harness, electronic actuator, and manual release options to ensure proper functionality.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 640i xDrive offers a compelling blend of luxury and performance, underpinned by a generally robust inline-six engine and transmission pairing. Used car buyers should prioritize a thorough test of all onboard electronics and trunk features to ensure trouble-free ownership.
