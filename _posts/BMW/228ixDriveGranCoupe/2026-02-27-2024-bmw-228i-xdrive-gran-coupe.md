# 2024 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe brings all-wheel-drive capability and sleek styling to the brand's entry-level luxury sedan lineup. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from modern engineering and advanced technology features. Prospective used-car buyers should review available owner reports and inspect the vehicle thoroughly to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and decent efficiency, representing the standard powerplant for this model.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and rapid gear changes paired with xDrive all-wheel drive. It is a well-regarded unit that generally offers solid long-term dependability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V711000: Structure:Body](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=228i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The bolts that connect the front body structure to the vehicle chassis may not have been tightened properly. A loose front body structure may not adequately protect occupants in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and if necessary, tighten the front structure bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
