# 2022 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe offers buyers an entry point into BMW's luxury lineup with standard all-wheel drive and sporty styling. As a relatively recent model year, owner feedback and formal complaint histories remain quite limited, indicating a generally stable ownership experience so far. Used car shoppers evaluating this compact sedan should still inspect the vehicle thoroughly and review service history to ensure long-term mechanical peace of mind.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. This powerplant is generally regarded as robust and refined, though proper maintenance and timely oil changes remain vital for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission comes paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, delivering quick and smooth gear changes. It is widely considered one of the more dependable automatic transmissions in its class, requiring standard fluid maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V513000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=228i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the high-pressure fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=228i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe presents a solid option for used luxury car buyers, backed by a scarcity of recurring mechanical complaints in its early years. While powertrain options are smooth and well-regarded, prospective buyers should always perform a pre-purchase inspection and verify vehicle history before finalizing a purchase.
