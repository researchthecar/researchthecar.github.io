## 2022 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe

The 2022 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe is a premium compact sedan that combines sleek styling with a luxurious interior and engaging driving dynamics. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The 228i xDrive Gran Coupe offers a thrilling driving experience with precise handling, responsive acceleration, and a well-tuned suspension. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of issues.

No Issues Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Hesitation and Jerking During Gear Shifts**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or jerk when shifting gears, particularly during low-speed maneuvers.
    * Solution: Software update may resolve the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
* **Loss of Power or Stalling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may lose power intermittently or cause the vehicle to stall, especially when accelerating from a stop.
    * Solution: Replacement of torque converter, transmission control module, or transmission may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
