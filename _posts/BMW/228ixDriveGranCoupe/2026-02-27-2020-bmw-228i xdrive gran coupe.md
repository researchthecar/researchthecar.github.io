# 2020 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2020 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe, part of BMW's compact luxury sedan lineup, offers a blend of sporty performance and practical four-door coupe styling. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission and xDrive all-wheel drive. This model aims to provide a premium driving experience with advanced technology and a refined interior. Reliability data for this specific model year and configuration suggests a generally solid performance, with most reported issues being minor or related to typical vehicle usage rather than widespread manufacturing defects. Owners generally appreciate its dynamic handling and upscale features.

### Summary Verdict
The 2020 BMW 228i xDrive Gran Coupe demonstrates good overall reliability, consistent with BMW's reputation for building durable vehicles. With routine maintenance and attention to potential electrical quirks, the 228i xDrive Gran Coupe is expected to provide a reliable luxury driving experience.
