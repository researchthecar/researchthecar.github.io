**2022 BMW 430i**

The 2022 BMW 430i is a luxury sports coupe that offers a blend of performance, style, and comfort. It is powered by a turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission that sends power to the rear wheels. Other engine and transmission options available for the 430i include a turbocharged 3.0-liter six-cylinder engine with 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, and an eight-speed Sport Automatic transmission with paddle shifters.

The 430i has received praise for its stylish design, responsive handling, and comfortable interior. However, there have been some issues reported with the vehicle, which are detailed below:

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from the engine, especially at idle
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.0-liter turbocharged six-cylinder engine**
    * **High-pressure fuel pump failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or difficulty starting, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Eight-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Shifting issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifts, delayed shifts, or slipping gears
        * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid change, or transmission replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Eight-speed Sport Automatic transmission**
    * **Transmission shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shudder during acceleration or deceleration
        * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid change, or transmission replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies quickly, even when the car is not in use
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair any electrical faults, replace the battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment system glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: System freezing, crashing, or displaying errors
    * Solution: Software update, system reset, or infotainment system replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door locks not working properly, doors not opening or closing
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuator or latch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center console trim peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Trim on the center console peeling or bubbling
    * Solution: Replace the trim
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 BMW 430i is a capable and stylish sports coupe, but it has been plagued by some issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of the issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and costly. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing a 430i and should consider the estimated repair costs when evaluating the overall value of the vehicle.
