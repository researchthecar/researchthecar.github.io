# 2024 BMW 430i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 430i brings sleek styling and athletic performance to the luxury sport coupe and convertible market. Equipped with refined powertrain engineering, it offers a compelling blend of daily drivability and engaging dynamics for prospective used car buyers.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque. It offers a smooth power delivery and strong efficiency, establishing itself as a robust and dependable powerplant for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless shifts and pairs exceptionally well with the inline-4 engine across rear-wheel and all-wheel drive configurations. It is widely regarded as one of the most reliable and refined gearboxes in its class.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V863000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source, such as a hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the cylinder head cover as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 430i presents a solid option for used luxury car buyers, backed by a proven powertrain and modern features. As with any newer luxury vehicle, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended to ensure complete mechanical peace of mind.
