# 2017 BMW 430i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 BMW 430i, buyers generally find a stylish and engaging luxury sport vehicle known for sharp handling and strong performance. However, reviewing owner experiences highlights specific areas to monitor, particularly concerning cooling system components and interior safety sensors.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine (B48) produces approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and solid efficiency. It is generally robust, though buyers should watch for plastic cooling component degradation.

**Coolant Leak and Oil Filter Housing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report warning messages regarding low coolant levels, sometimes accompanied by complete coolant loss due to degradation of composite plastic components around the oil filter housing and thermostat.
- **Solution:** Inspect the oil filter housing and cooling connections for leaks; replace damaged plastic housings, gaskets, or thermostats with updated parts.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid shifts and pairs exceptionally well with the powertrain. It enjoys a strong reputation for durability with no widespread recurring complaint patterns.

## Safety

### Passenger Air Bag Occupancy Sensor

Safety systems generally perform reliably, though cabin sensor faults can trigger warning lights.

**Restraint System Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated air bag warning lights and sporadic dashboard messages indicating a passenger restraint system or seat occupancy sensor failure.
- **Solution:** Have the seat occupancy sensor mat and associated wiring harnesses inspected and replaced if faulty.

### Seat Belt Assembly

Seat belts and tensioners require proper physical inspection during a pre-purchase evaluation to ensure secure latching.

**Seat Belt Anchorage Disconnection**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's seat belt unexpectedly detaches from its fixed mounting point near the door side during movement.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt tensioners, anchor bolts, and mounting points; replace faulty mounting hardware or tensioner assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V115000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat-mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 430i offers compelling luxury and performance, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect the engine bay for signs of coolant leaks around plastic housing units and verify that all safety restraint warnings and seat belt anchors operate correctly.
