# 2023 BMW 430i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW 430i offers a blend of luxury and performance, underpinned by modern engineering and refined styling. While early owner feedback highlights strong overall build quality and driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review available vehicle history and inspect electronic features during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as a robust and efficient powerplant for daily driving.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp and smooth shifts across rear-wheel and all-wheel drive configurations. It is widely praised for its reliability and responsiveness.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW 430i is a solid luxury purchase with well-regarded powertrain options. Buyers should conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection and test all vehicle systems to ensure everything operates as expected.
