# 2021 BMW 430i

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or errors with the iDrive system, navigation, or connectivity features.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system resets. In rare cases, module replacement may be necessary.

### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery discharge, requiring jump starts or battery replacement sooner than expected. May be linked to parasitic draw from electronic modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, potentially involving disabling modules or replacing faulty components. Battery replacement if necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800
