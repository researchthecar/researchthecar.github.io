# 2022 BMW 430i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 BMW 430i, buyers will find a stylish and performance-oriented luxury coupe or convertible backed by punchy turbo-four power. While owner feedback is relatively limited, prospective buyers should review specific mechanical complaints and outstanding manufacturer campaigns before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 294 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration and strong efficiency. It is generally regarded as a robust and refined powerplant in modern BMW applications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive, offering lightning-fast and smooth shifts. It is widely praised for its durability and responsiveness in daily driving.

**Unexpected Shifting to Neutral**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the transmission may independently shift into neutral while driving at various speeds without illuminating warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the transmission control module, software calibration, and mechanical linkages for faults.

### Rear Differential

The rear differential handles power distribution to the rear wheels (or all four in xDrive models), requiring periodic fluid inspections to maintain long-term longevity.

**Rear Differential Fluid Leak and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience fluid leaks from the rear differential assembly, which can eventually require a complete unit replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect the rear differential housing for active leaks or unusual whining noises, and replace worn seals or the entire differential unit if internal damage is present.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Park Assist

Advanced safety and driver assistance electronics help maneuver the vehicle, though software glitches can occasionally impact visibility features.

**Rear Vision Camera Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera may deactivate itself when the park assist system is turned off, leaving the driver without a reversing image.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates at a dealership or specialized service center to resolve intermittent camera module dropouts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V267000: Visibility:Sun/Moon Roof Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-Operated Window Systems." A sunroof that closes without the key fob present inside the vehicle can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will reprogram the roof function control unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V584000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An audible chime that does not properly alert the driver to fasten their seat belt increases the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the receiver audio module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V636000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Relay](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter relay may corrode, causing the relay to overheat and short circuit. A short circuit in the starter relay may increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=430i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 430i provides an engaging driving experience and premium luxury features, but used car shoppers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Ensure that all standard maintenance history is documented and verify that any open starter or electrical safety recalls have been properly addressed by a certified dealer.
