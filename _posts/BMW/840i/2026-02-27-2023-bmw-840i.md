# 2023 BMW 840i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 BMW 840i, buyers are looking at a high-end luxury grand tourer that combines striking design with impressive long-distance cruising capabilities. Because this model year has limited owner complaint data and benefits from mature engineering of BMW's core powertrain platforms, it generally presents a clean slate for prospective second-hand buyers. However, as with any sophisticated modern luxury vehicle, thorough pre-purchase inspections remain essential to ensure all electronic and mechanical systems are functioning properly.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and refined power delivery. Widely regarded as one of BMW's most reliable modern powerplants, it pairs strong performance with generally favorable long-term durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers rapid, smooth shifts and adapts seamlessly to both relaxed cruising and spirited driving. It is widely praised for its solid engineering and robust reliability profile across numerous luxury applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V099000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." Unintentionally closing the windows or sunroof without the key present can increase the risk of injury to occupants inside the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software for the Electronic Control Unit (ECU), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW 840i stands out as a sophisticated luxury option with a strong powertrain and limited reported trouble spots for its model year. Buyers should still prioritize a comprehensive mechanical and electronic inspection to protect against unexpected luxury repair costs.
