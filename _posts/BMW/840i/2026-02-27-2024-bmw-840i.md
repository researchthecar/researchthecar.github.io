# 2024 BMW 840i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 840i represents the pinnacle of modern luxury grand touring, blending striking design with impressive performance. As a relatively new luxury model in the pre-owned marketplace, it generally benefits from modern engineering and advanced technology suites, though prospective buyers should still inspect complex onboard electronics and powertrain health carefully.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its smooth power delivery, robust engineering, and strong overall reliability profile.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features ZF-sourced planetary gearsets with manual paddle shifters. It offers quick, refined shifts and pairs seamlessly with both rear-wheel drive and xDrive all-wheel drive configurations.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 840i is an exceptional luxury cruiser offering a refined driving experience and a powerful inline-six engine. Because it is a modern high-end vehicle, buyers are encouraged to verify service history and thoroughly test all electronic features before finalizing a purchase.
