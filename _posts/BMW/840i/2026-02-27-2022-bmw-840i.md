# 2022 BMW 840i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 840i stands as a luxurious and high-performing grand tourer, offering sophisticated styling and impressive long-distance cruising comfort. For prospective used-car buyers, evaluating its powertrain options and general build quality is essential to ensuring a satisfying ownership experience. While owner-reported complaints are minimal for this model year, careful inspection of electronics and standard pre-purchase checks remain recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. Known widely as the B58, this powerplant is generally regarded by enthusiasts and technicians for its robust reliability, smooth power delivery, and strong overall performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Paired with the inline-six engine, the 8-speed ZF automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless shifts and contributes to strong fuel efficiency. This gearbox enjoys a strong reputation for durability across numerous BMW applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 840i offers a compelling blend of luxury, cutting-edge technology, and a well-regarded powertrain. Because used luxury vehicles require diligent maintenance and comprehensive pre-purchase inspections, buyers should verify service records and test all onboard electronic systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
