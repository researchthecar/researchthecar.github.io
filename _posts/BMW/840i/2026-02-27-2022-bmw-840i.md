# 2022 BMW 840i

### Overview
The 2022 BMW 840i, a luxurious grand tourer, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting BMW's engineering prowess. It is equipped with a potent turbocharged inline-six engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, offering a blend of performance and refinement. Owners typically report a satisfying ownership experience with minimal unexpected mechanical problems. However, as with any high-performance vehicle, proactive maintenance is key to ensuring long-term dependability. While major powertrain issues are uncommon, attention to electrical systems and specific interior features can help maintain the vehicle's premium feel and functionality over time. Overall, the 840i is a solid choice for those seeking a reliable and engaging luxury coupe.

### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may discharge prematurely, leading to starting issues, especially if the vehicle is not driven regularly or if aftermarket electronics are installed.
- **Solution:** Check for parasitic draw, update software, replace battery if necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 (for battery replacement)

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 840i demonstrates commendable reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have a proven track record. While the suspension and exterior/interior elements generally hold up well, minor electrical gremlins, such as occasional infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues, are the most commonly cited concerns. These are typically manageable through software updates or routine maintenance. Overall, the 840i offers a dependable luxury driving experience, provided owners stay on top of recommended service intervals and address any minor electrical anomalies promptly.
