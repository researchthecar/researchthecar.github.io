# 2017 BMW 440i

### Overview
The 2017 BMW 440i, part of the F32 generation, is generally regarded as a reliable vehicle, particularly praised for its potent powertrain and engaging driving dynamics. It typically features a robust inline-six engine and a well-regarded automatic transmission. While most owners report a positive experience, like any performance-oriented luxury vehicle, there are specific areas where potential issues have been noted. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture for prospective and current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)
**Coolant Leaks (Water Pump/Thermostat)**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant level dropping, visible coolant residue, engine overheating warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty water pump or thermostat housing.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1000

**PCV System Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, potential whistling noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve or assembly.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsiveness, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset of the iDrive system.
- **Cost:** $0 - $200 (for diagnostics/updates if not covered)


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, electrical components malfunctioning intermittently, battery warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the battery, diagnosis of parasitic draw.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 BMW 440i offers a strong blend of performance and luxury, with its core mechanical components, particularly the B58 engine and ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, demonstrating good reliability. The most commonly reported technical issues tend to be related to the cooling system (water pump/thermostat) and the PCV system, which are not uncommon for turbocharged engines. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment glitches and occasional battery drain, are also noted but often resolvable with software updates or battery replacement. Overall, the 440i is a dependable choice for a performance coupe, provided potential buyers are aware of and prepared for these specific, albeit not overwhelmingly frequent, potential repairs.
