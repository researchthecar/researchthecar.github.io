# 2017 BMW 440i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury sport model, prospective buyers frequently look closely at powertrain refinement and everyday cabin electronics. Reviewing owner feedback and technical service data helps highlight specific areas requiring attention, such as cooling components and interior occupant detection sensors, ensuring a more confident purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering a responsive blend of efficiency and everyday performance. While generally robust, buyers should monitor plastic cooling system components for age-related leaks.

**Coolant Leaks and Housing Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice dashboard warnings regarding low coolant levels, accompanied by potential fluid leaks originating from plastic engine components or the oil filter housing.
- **Solution:** Inspect plastic coolant lines, thermostats, and the oil filter housing for cracking; replace failed components and refill with proper coolant.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine delivers 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, praised for smooth power delivery and strong performance. It is widely considered a durable and dependable powerplant within the lineup.

### Climate and Convertible Top Controls

Electrical accessories function well overall, but complex mechanisms like retractable hardtops require careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Retractable Hardtop and Climate Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard warnings stating that the retractable top is not fully locked, or abnormal rattling noises and reduced cooling from the air conditioning vents.
- **Solution:** Check convertible top microswitches and alignment mechanisms, or inspect climate control blower motors and refrigerant levels.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides quick, smooth gear shifts and pairs well with both rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive layouts. It boasts a strong reputation for long-term mechanical reliability.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers an engaging driving experience for enthusiast buyers. It requires standard clutch maintenance but presents no widespread mechanical flaws.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Seat Belt Sensors

Safety restraint systems generally perform reliably, though occasional electrical warnings related to seat sensors have been reported by owners.

**Passenger Occupancy and Restraint Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning lights and dashboard messages indicating a passenger restraint system or seat occupancy sensor failure.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the seat occupancy mat or wiring harness and replace faulty sensor components as needed.

**Seat Belt Anchor Disconnection**
- **Symptoms:** The driver or passenger seat belt may unlatch or detach unexpectedly from its fixed lower mounting point during vehicle operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt tensioners and anchor mounting points for secure attachment and replace defective hardware if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V115000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat-mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
