# 2019 BMW 440i Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury sport coupe or convertible, reviewing owner-reported feedback can help you spot potential trouble spots before making a purchase. While driver complaints represent individual experiences rather than guaranteed defects, paying attention to recurring powertrain and electrical notes helps ensure a smoother ownership journey.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (430i)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, offering lively performance for daily driving. It is generally robust, though owners should monitor for occasional cooling system leaks and valve issues.

**Coolant Leaks and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** A low coolant warning message appears on the dashboard, potentially leading to engine overheating if left unaddressed.
- **Solution:** Inspect the cooling system hoses and reservoir for leaks, and top off or replace faulty components as necessary.

**Pressure Control Valve (PCV) Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A loud high-pitched noise emanates from the engine bay, accompanied by white smoke billowing from the exhaust.
- **Solution:** Have the pressure control valve replaced. Note that BMW has provided extended warranty coverage for this specific component on certain models.

**Drivetrain Malfunction Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** A drivetrain malfunction warning appears on the instrument cluster, resulting in a sudden loss of acceleration power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning is required to identify faulty sensors, valves, or fuel system components triggering the limp mode.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (440i)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth power delivery and strong acceleration. This B58 powerplant is widely regarded by enthusiasts as one of the brand's more reliable modern engines.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides rapid, smooth gear changes and pairs seamlessly with both rear-wheel drive and xDrive all-wheel drive configurations. It exhibits strong overall durability with very few widespread owner complaints.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers an engaging, direct shifting experience for purists selecting rear-wheel-drive variants. It is mechanically sturdy, provided the clutch has not been abused by previous drivers.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior panels and trim hold up well over time, but isolated incidents of spontaneous glass breakage on sunroof-equipped models have been documented.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic or standard glass sunroof shatters or explodes spontaneously while driving, producing a loud noise resembling a gunshot.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and clear out any remaining glass fragments from the track and interior cabin.

## Safety

### Airbags and Seat Belts

Safety systems generally perform as expected, though occasional dashboard warnings related to occupant detection sensors and restraint connections have been reported.

**Airbag and Restraint System Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** An 'Air Bag Off', 'Service Air Bag', or 'Passenger restraint system: Malfunction' warning message illuminates intermittently on the dashboard display.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt tensioners, sensors, and airbag wiring harnesses to locate and resolve faulty electrical connections.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V352000: Air Bags:Knee Bolster](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An improperly folded and assembled air bag may not deploy as intended, increasing risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the knee air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V465000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V533000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=440i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger air bag inflator may have been incorrectly manufactured, potentially affecting the air bag deployment performance in the event of a crash. If the front passenger air bag does not deploy properly in the event of a crash, the occupant has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the front passenger air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

This luxury sport vehicle delivers impressive performance and refined driving dynamics, particularly when equipped with the inline-6 engine option. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the cooling system, electrical connections, and service history to ensure a reliable ownership experience.
