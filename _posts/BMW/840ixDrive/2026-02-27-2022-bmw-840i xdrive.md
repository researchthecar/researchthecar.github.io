### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive generally presents a strong reliability profile, with its core powertrain (3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 and 8-speed automatic transmission) showing no widespread issues. The suspension system also appears robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential false alerts from parking sensors. These are typically resolvable through software updates or component servicing. Exterior and interior components have largely been free of significant reported problems. Overall, the 840i xDrive is a reliable choice within its segment, provided routine maintenance is performed and minor electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
