# 2024 BMW 840i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 840i xDrive blends high-end luxury with potent performance, making it an enticing option for used luxury grand tourer buyers. Because it represents a recent model year, available owner feedback is still developing, though powertrains benefit from BMW's refined engineering experience. Prospective buyers should carefully evaluate vehicle condition and service history when considering a pre-owned model.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine (B58) produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's most reliable and robust modern powerplants, offering strong performance paired with respectable daily drivability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission channels power seamlessly through the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is known for quick, crisp shifts and durable long-term performance across various driving modes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW 840i xDrive is a sophisticated luxury cruiser powered by a well-regarded powertrain combination. With limited reported complaints at this stage, used buyers can approach the model with reasonable confidence, provided they ensure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and verify regular maintenance records.
