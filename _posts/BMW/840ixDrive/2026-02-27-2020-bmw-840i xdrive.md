The 2020 BMW 840i xDrive, a luxurious grand tourer, generally exhibits strong reliability, reflecting BMW's engineering prowess. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for owners and prospective buyers. ### Summary Verdict
The 2020 BMW 840i xDrive is a highly reliable vehicle, particularly in its core mechanical components like the engine and transmission. The 3.0L turbocharged inline-six and the 8-speed automatic transmission are well-regarded for their durability. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the sophisticated electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and potential battery drain issues, which are not uncommon in modern luxury vehicles with extensive electronics. These are generally manageable with software updates or component replacement. Overall, the 840i xDrive offers a dependable ownership experience for a performance-oriented luxury car.
