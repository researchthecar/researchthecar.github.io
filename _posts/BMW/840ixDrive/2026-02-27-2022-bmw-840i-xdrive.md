# 2022 BMW 840i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive blends striking grand touring design with impressive performance from its turbocharged inline-six engine. As a relatively recent luxury model, it generally enjoys a modern electronic and mechanical foundation, though buyers should still carefully inspect the vehicle's complex onboard tech and powertrain components prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 368 lb-ft of torque. It is widely regarded as one of BMW's more robust and dependable modern powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission paired with xDrive all-wheel drive, it delivers swift, seamless gear changes and respectable long-term reliability under normal driving conditions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V644000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=840i&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An overheated starter motor can ignite nearby combustible material in the engine compartment, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive offers a compelling mix of luxury and performance, backed by a robust powertrain. Prospective buyers should perform a standard pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of the electronics, suspension, and drivetrain.
