**2022 BMW 840i xDrive**

The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive is a high-performance luxury coupe that delivers exhilarating driving dynamics and a captivating appearance. It is powered by a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine that generates 335 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. Paired with an 8-speed Steptronic Sport transmission and xDrive all-wheel drive, the 840i xDrive accelerates from 0-60 mph in 4.6 seconds. Its sleek design, spacious interior, and advanced technology features make it a highly desirable vehicle.

## **Engine**

- **B58 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6**

  - **Turbocharger Failure**
    - Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, check engine light, abnormal exhaust smoke
    - Solution: Turbocharger replacement
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

  - **Fuel Injector Leak**
    - Description/Symptoms: Fuel smell, poor engine performance, check engine light
    - Solution: Fuel injector replacement
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Transmission**

- **8-Speed Steptronic Sport Transmission**

  - **Shifting Issues**
    - Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, delayed shifting, slipping between gears
    - Solution: Transmission software update, transmission rebuild
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-254

## **Suspension**

- **No Issues Found**

## **Electrical**

- **Battery Discharge**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery fails to hold a charge, frequent jump-starts
    - Solution: Battery replacement
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

- **No Issues Found**

## **Interior**

- **Climate Control Malfunction**
    - Description/Symptoms: Inconsistent temperature, weak airflow, malfunctioning AC/heater
    - Solution: Climate control module replacement
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Panoramic Sunroof Leak**
    - Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
    - Solution: Sunroof seal replacement
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    - Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

The 2022 BMW 840i xDrive is a well-engineered vehicle that offers exceptional performance and luxury. However, it is not immune to certain issues that can affect its reliability. The most common issues reported by owners include turbocharger failure, fuel injector leaks, and battery discharge. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are typically not major in severity and can be addressed through proper maintenance and repairs.
