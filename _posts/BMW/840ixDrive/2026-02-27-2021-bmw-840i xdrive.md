## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen may freeze, become unresponsive, or exhibit minor graphical glitches. Navigation system may occasionally lose signal.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or in rare cases, replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500 (depending on software fix vs. hardware replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may drain faster than expected, leading to starting issues. This can sometimes be related to parasitic draw from electronic modules.
- **Solution:** Testing for parasitic draw, replacing faulty modules, or replacing the battery.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 BMW 840i xDrive demonstrates a high level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components.
