# 2014 BMW 640i Gran Coupe

### Overview
The 2014 BMW 640i Gran Coupe, a luxurious four-door coupe, generally offers a strong reliability record, particularly for its powertrain. It is equipped with a potent 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine, known for its smooth power delivery and robust engineering. The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is also highly regarded for its efficiency and durability. While the vehicle excels in performance and comfort, like any complex luxury car, it can experience specific issues, primarily related to its advanced electronic systems and some components within the engine's ancillary systems. Owners should be aware of potential, though not widespread, concerns with certain sensors and seals. Overall, the 640i Gran Coupe is a reliable choice for those seeking a blend of performance and practicality in the luxury segment.

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)
**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gasket, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the affected gasket.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**PCV Valve Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, increased oil consumption, whistling noise from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the Positive Crankcase Ventilation (PCV) valve assembly.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Water Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, coolant leaks, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the electric water pump and thermostat.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 BMW 640i Gran Coupe demonstrates good overall reliability, with its core powertrain components being particularly robust. The 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. Potential issues tend to be concentrated in ancillary engine components like the water pump and PCV valve, and some electrical gremlins within the infotainment system and various sensors. Minor oil leaks can also occur. While not immune to the complexities of a modern luxury vehicle, the 640i Gran Coupe offers a dependable ownership experience when routine maintenance is performed and potential issues are addressed promptly. It is a solid choice for those prioritizing performance and luxury.
