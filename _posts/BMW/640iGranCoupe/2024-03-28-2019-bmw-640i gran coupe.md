## 2019 BMW 640i Gran Coupe

The 2019 BMW 640i Gran Coupe is a stylish and sporty sedan that offers a comfortable ride and plenty of performance. It is powered by a 3.0L turbocharged inline-6 engine that produces 335 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. The engine is mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Some of the things people like about the 640i Gran Coupe include its sleek design, comfortable interior, and powerful engine. However, some people have also reported issues with the vehicle, including:

**Engine**

**3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure:** The turbocharger can fail, causing a loss of power and a decrease in fuel economy. The turbocharger will need to be replaced. $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Oil leaks:** The engine may develop oil leaks from the valve cover gasket or oil pan gasket. The gaskets will need to be replaced. $500 - $1,000+

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting problems:** The transmission may shift roughly or may not shift gears at all. The transmission may need to be replaced. $2,500 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure:** The strut mounts can fail, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps. The strut mounts will need to be replaced. $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to not start. The battery will need to be replaced. $200 - $400+
* **Electrical system failure:** The electrical system may fail, causing a variety of problems, such as loss of power, inoperative lights, or a dead battery. The electrical system will need to be diagnosed and repaired. $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof leaks:** The sunroof may leak water into the cabin. The sunroof seal will need to be replaced. $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system issues:** The infotainment system may freeze or crash. The infotainment system will need to be updated or replaced. $500 - $2,000+

**Summary**

The 2019 BMW 640i Gran Coupe is a reliable vehicle, but it has been known to have some issues. The most common issues include turbocharger failure, oil leaks, and shifting problems. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
