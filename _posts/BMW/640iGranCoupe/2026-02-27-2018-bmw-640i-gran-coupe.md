# 2018 BMW 640i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used luxury cruiser, evaluating how the powertrain and key comfort systems hold up over time is essential. This model offers strong performance and sophisticated engineering, but prospective buyers should keep an eye on potential minor electrical glitches, interior trim durability, and specific sensor concerns reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

Powered by a smooth 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine producing approximately 320 horsepower, this powertrain provides brisk acceleration and refined cruising capabilities. Overall mechanical durability is generally solid when properly maintained.

**Unexpected Engine Revving**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the engine revs unexpectedly while the brake pedal is depressed, causing unintended forward movement.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic throttle body, software calibrations, and brake-throttle override systems inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a responsive 8-speed automatic transmission available in both rear-wheel drive and xDrive all-wheel drive configurations, this gearbox delivers quick, seamless shifts and contributes to strong overall efficiency.

**Driveshaft Flex Disc Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Routine inspections may reveal multiple cracks in the driveshaft flex disc (guibo), which can risk complete failure if left unaddressed.
- **Solution:** Inspect the flex disc regularly during oil changes and replace the component immediately if cracking or wear is observed.

## Interior

### Panoramic Sunroof Shade

The cabin features high-end materials and advanced technology, though certain comfort accessories like motorized shades require attentive care.

**Sunroof Curtain Sagging and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof sunshade or curtain may fail to retract properly, sagging downward and obstructing rear visibility or passenger space.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof shade mechanism and cassette tracks; broken clips or tensioners typically require assembly replacement.

## Safety

### Passenger Airbag Sensor

Advanced safety systems provide robust occupant protection, though sensitive seat sensors can occasionally trigger dashboard warnings requiring professional service.

**Passenger Restraint Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger airbag malfunction or restraint warning light illuminates unexpectedly on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing at a dealership or specialist is required; replacing a faulty seat occupancy sensor mat typically resolves the fault.
- **Cost:** $2,600 to $3,000

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V465000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the crankshaft sensor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The vehicle combines striking styling with strong performance, making it an appealing choice in the pre-owned luxury market. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the driveshaft flex disc, interior trim components, and electronic sensor systems to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
