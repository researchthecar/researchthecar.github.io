### Infotainment System
**Occasional Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** The iDrive infotainment system may occasionally freeze, become unresponsive, or display errors. This can affect navigation, audio, and connectivity features.
- **Solution:** Software updates or a system reset are often effective. In rare cases, a module replacement might be necessary.

### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported premature battery failure or unexplained battery drain, leading to starting issues or electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Ensure all accessories are turned off when the vehicle is parked. Regular battery testing and replacement as needed. Check for parasitic draws.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600
