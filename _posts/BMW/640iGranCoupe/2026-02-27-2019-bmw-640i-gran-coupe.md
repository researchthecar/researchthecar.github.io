# 2019 BMW 640i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 BMW 640i Gran Coupe blends striking four-door coupe styling with luxury and performance. For used-car buyers, researching typical owner experiences helps set expectations regarding maintenance and potential electronic or equipment glitches.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 335 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and robust power delivery. It is generally regarded as a robust powertrain option for daily driving when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, refined shifts and pairs with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive. It is a widely used and generally dependable unit across BMW's lineup.

## Suspension

### Factory Tires

Original equipment tires and wheel assemblies.

**Summer Tire Winter Traction Limitations**
- **Symptoms:** Factory-equipped summer performance tires lose traction significantly in freezing temperatures and snow, making winter driving unsafe without appropriate replacements.
- **Solution:** Swap summer performance tires for dedicated winter tires or all-season tires if driving in cold, snowy climates.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera System

Assists with rear visibility during parking maneuvers.

**Inoperable Backup Camera**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may find that the back-over prevention camera fails to activate or display an image when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or qualified technician inspect the camera wiring, module, and display unit for faults or required software resets.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW 640i Gran Coupe is a sophisticated luxury vehicle offering strong performance and comfort. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check all electronics, driver aids, and tire equipment to ensure everything is in proper working order before purchase.
