# 2017 BMW 640i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2017 BMW 640i Gran Coupe means blending striking four-door coupe styling with BMW's smooth inline-six performance. While this luxury cruiser offers an engaging drive and high-end cabin, used buyers should carefully inspect all mechanical and electrical accessories before purchasing, as premium European maintenance and potential electronic issues can become costly over time.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55/B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 315 to 320 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is widely praised for its smooth power delivery and responsive acceleration, though routine maintenance is crucial to long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive, the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp shifts and excellent gear spacing. It is generally considered robust and dependable when serviced according to schedule.

## Exterior

### Trunk Release and Access

Electrical accessories and convenience features are generally advanced, but complex wiring harnesses and actuators require thorough pre-purchase checks.

**Trunk Latch Failure and Inaccessible Release**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience a total failure of the electronic trunk release mechanism, leaving the cargo area locked and inaccessible. Because manual fold-down seat releases are located inside the trunk area, passengers may be unable to reach them from the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electronic trunk latch and wiring harness for faults, and have a qualified technician repair or replace the failed actuator mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V914000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=640i&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles have seat- mounted side air bag inflator initiators that may fail to ignite during a crash. If the air bag inflator initiator fails to ignite, the seat-mounted air bag will not deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag modules, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 BMW 640i Gran Coupe is a luxurious and capable grand tourer, anchored by a smooth powertrain. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection to verify the condition of all electronics, latch mechanisms, and fluid lines to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
