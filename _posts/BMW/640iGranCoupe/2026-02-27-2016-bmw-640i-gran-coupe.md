# 2016 BMW 640i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 640i Gran Coupe combines striking styling with strong performance, making it an appealing choice for used luxury car buyers. While it offers impressive driving dynamics and a well-appointed cabin, prospective buyers should thoroughly evaluate the vehicle's maintenance history and powertrain health before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 engine produces approximately 315 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It is generally regarded as one of BMW's smoother powerplants, though proper maintenance and cooling system checks are essential for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, rapid gear changes and pairs exceptionally well with the inline-6 engine. It has a strong reputation for durability when routine fluid changes are observed.
