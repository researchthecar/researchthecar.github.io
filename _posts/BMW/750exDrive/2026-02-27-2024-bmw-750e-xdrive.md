# 2024 BMW 750e xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 750e xDrive represents the pinnacle of modern luxury and plug-in hybrid engineering. Offering sophisticated powertrain technology and an opulent cabin, it attracts buyers looking for advanced features and eco-conscious performance. As a relatively recent model year, long-term reliability trends are still unfolding, and prospective used car buyers should inspect the complex electrical and hybrid systems carefully.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Plug-In Hybrid

This plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-6 engine with an electric motor to generate an impressive combined output of approximately 483 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk acceleration and refined cruising, though its advanced hybrid architecture requires diligent maintenance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with xDrive All-Wheel Drive

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with BMW's intelligent xDrive all-wheel-drive system, this setup delivers seamless gear changes and confident all-weather traction. It is widely regarded as smooth and responsive in daily driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V714000: Steering:Linkages:Knuckle:Spindle:Arm](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=750e+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A sudden increase in required steering effort increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering spindle's double universal joint, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V739000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=750e+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Either of these scenarios can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the integrated brake module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V096000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=750e+xDrive&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The electrical wiring harness for the air conditioning system may become damaged during replacement of the cabin air filter. A damaged wiring harness can short-circuit, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the air conditioning system’s electrical wiring harness and add a retaining strap/bracket to the harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
