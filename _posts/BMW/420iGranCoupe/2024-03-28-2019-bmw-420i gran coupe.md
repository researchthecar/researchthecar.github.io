**Introduction**

The 2019 BMW 420i Gran Coupe is a luxury four-door coupe that offers a sporty driving experience and a spacious interior. It is powered by a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 184 horsepower and 221 lb-ft of torque, mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission. The 420i Gran Coupe is praised for its handling, performance, and overall comfort, but some owners have reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
**Timing Chain Issues:**
Description/Symptoms: Rattling or grinding noise during startup or acceleration, followed by a loss of engine power.
Solution: Replace timing chain and related components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Eight-Speed Automatic**
**Transmission Slipping:**
Description/Symptoms: Jerking or hesitation during gear changes, loss of power or acceleration.
Solution: Replace or repair transmission components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery depletes quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty electrical components (e.g., alternator, starter, lighting system).
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Motor Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Sunroof fails to open or close, or makes loud noises when operating.
Solution: Replace sunroof motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2019 BMW 420i Gran Coupe is a generally reliable vehicle, some owners have encountered issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity of these issues can vary, with some requiring expensive repairs. However, these issues do not seem to be widespread, and the majority of owners report a positive experience with the vehicle.
