# 2016 BMW 420i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 420i Gran Coupe combines sleek four-door coupe styling with sporty driving dynamics. As a used vehicle purchase, prospective buyers should review available maintenance records and inspect the powertrain and electrical systems thoroughly to ensure long-term reliability.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48/N20)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 180 horsepower and 200 lb-ft of torque. It offers a responsive and efficient drive, though timely maintenance is vital to prevent long-term wear.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid gear shifts matched to rear-wheel drive or xDrive all-wheel drive configurations. It is generally robust when fluid changes are performed at recommended intervals.
