# 2024 BMW 330i xDrive Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW 330i xDrive offers a compelling blend of luxury, technology, and all-weather capability, powered by BMW's renowned turbocharged inline-four engine. As a relatively new model in the used market, it generally benefits from modern engineering refinements and advanced feature sets, though buyers should always inspect modern luxury vehicles thoroughly for complex electronic integration.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B48)

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine produces approximately 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and solid efficiency. It is generally regarded as a robust and reliable powerplant in BMW's modern lineup.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and smooth, rapid gear changes paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system. It is widely praised for both refinement and long-term durability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V770000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear may not have been tightened properly, which can allow it to loosen. A loose steering gear can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=330i&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
