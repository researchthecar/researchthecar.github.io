**2017 BMW 330i xDrive**

The 2017 BMW 330i xDrive is a luxury sedan powered by a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It produces 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. The 330i xDrive is known for its agile handling, responsive engine, and comfortable interior.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**

  * **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, increased oil consumption, smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, reduced engine power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**

  * **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifts, slipping gears
    * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Mechatronic Unit Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, loss of gears, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace the mechatronic unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front end, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
  * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle
  * Solution: Replace the battery or diagnose and fix any electrical drains
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging**
  * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through the sunroof
  * Solution: Clean or replace the drain plugs
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Center Console Trim Panel Creaking**
  * Description/Symptoms: Creaking or rattling noises from the center console
  * Solution: Tighten or replace the trim panel
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 BMW 330i xDrive is a reliable and enjoyable vehicle that experiences some common issues. While the engine and transmission problems can be costly to repair, they are not widespread. The majority of issues are minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the 330i xDrive provides good value for its price range.
