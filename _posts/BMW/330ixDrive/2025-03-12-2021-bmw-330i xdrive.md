The 2021 BMW 330i xDrive is a popular luxury sedan known for its engaging driving dynamics, comfortable interior, and stylish design. The 330i xDrive comes standard with a turbocharged 2.0-liter inline-4 engine producing 255 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options available for the 2021 3 Series include the 330e plug-in hybrid with a combined output of 288 horsepower and the M340i with a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-6 producing 382 horsepower. Common praises for the 330i xDrive include its balanced handling, fuel efficiency, and premium features. Common complaints often revolve around the cost of maintenance, some infotainment system complexity, and the availability of certain features as options rather than standard equipment.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (B46/B48)**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks can occur from various locations, including the valve cover gasket, oil filter housing gasket, and oil pan gasket. Symptoms include visible oil leaks, burning oil smell, and low oil levels.
    *   Solution: Replacing the leaking gasket is the typical solution.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+ depending on the location and complexity of the repair.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Coolant Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks from the expansion tank, radiator hoses, or water pump. This can lead to overheating.
    *   Solution: Replace the affected parts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+ depending on the component and labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP)**
* **Harsh Shifting/Hesitation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Some owners have reported experiencing harsh or delayed shifting, particularly during acceleration or deceleration. This can manifest as a noticeable "jerk" or hesitation between gears.
    * Solution: In some cases, a software update can resolve the issue. In others, a transmission fluid flush or replacement of the valve body may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $3,000+ depending on the solution.
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Glitches**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Reports of the iDrive system freezing, lagging, or malfunctioning. This can include issues with the navigation, audio, and climate control functions.
    *   Solution: Software updates are often released to address these issues. A system reset might also be necessary. In rare cases, hardware replacement could be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (software updates) to $1,000+ (hardware replacement).
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Sensor Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Issues with parking sensors, adaptive cruise control sensors, or other driver-assistance system sensors. These malfunctions can cause warning lights, inaccurate readings, or system failures.
    *   Solution: Diagnose the specific sensor and replace it if necessary. Recalibration of the system may also be required.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+ depending on the sensor and labor.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof may get stuck, make excessive noise when opening or closing, or leak.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating the sunroof tracks. Replacing the sunroof motor or other components may be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1000+ depending on the issue.
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 BMW 330i xDrive is generally a reliable vehicle. Some owners experience minor issues, particularly with the engine oil leaks and infotainment system glitches. Transmission issues are not common, but when they do occur, it can be a major issue. While some problems can be resolved with software updates or minor repairs, others may require more extensive and costly fixes. Prospective buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their purchasing decision.
