# 2003 BMW 745i

### Overview
The 2003 BMW 745i, a flagship luxury sedan, offered a potent combination of performance and advanced technology for its era. Powered by a robust V8 engine and sophisticated automatic transmission, it aimed to deliver a refined driving experience. However, like many complex luxury vehicles from this period, the 745i is known to have specific areas that require attention due to its intricate systems and high-performance nature. Owners should be aware of potential issues, particularly concerning the electronics, cooling system, and transmission, which can contribute to significant repair costs if not maintained properly. Despite these potential drawbacks, when well-maintained, the 745i can still offer a rewarding ownership experience.

---


## Engine

### 4.4L V8 (N62B44)
**Cooling System Leaks (Valley Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the valley pan gasket and associated seals.
- **Cost:** $1,200 - $2,500

**Valve Stem Seals**
- **Symptoms:** Blue smoke on startup or deceleration, oil consumption.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve stem seals, often requiring cylinder head removal.
- **Cost:** $3,000 - $5,000+

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets, Oil Pan Gasket)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks, burning oil smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,500


## Transmission

### 6-speed Automatic (GM 5L40-E / ZF 6HP26)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, harsh shifting, low transmission fluid level.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair of leaks, often at the cooler lines or mechatronic sleeve.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200

**Mechatronic Unit Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, 'limp mode', transmission warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the mechatronic unit.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Drive (Active Roll Stabilization)
**Active Roll Stabilization Leaks/Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises, reduced handling stability, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hydraulic actuators or pump.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000+


### Standard Air Suspension (Rear)
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Rear end sagging, uneven ride height, compressor running excessively.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1,200 per side


## Electrical

### iDrive System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, non-responsive controls, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or replacement of head unit components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Body Control Module (BCM)
**BCM Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical gremlins, lights not working, power windows issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1,800


### Xenon Headlight Ballast Failure
**Headlight Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** One or both headlights not turning on, flickering.
- **Solution:** Replacement of headlight ballasts.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per side

## Interior

### Standard Interior
**Headliner Sagging**
- **Symptoms:** Fabric detaching from the headliner board.
- **Solution:** Re-gluing or replacement of the headliner.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Pixelated LCD Displays (Instrument Cluster/iDrive)**
- **Symptoms:** Faded or missing pixels on displays.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the affected display unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2003 BMW 745i is a complex luxury vehicle with a higher potential for costly repairs compared to less sophisticated cars. Key areas of concern include the engine's cooling system and valve stem seals, potential transmission issues, and various electrical components, particularly the iDrive system and body control modules. Suspension, especially the adaptive systems, can also be a source of significant expense. While the exterior and basic interior components are generally robust, specific interior elements like displays and headliners can degrade over time. Prospective buyers should budget for potential maintenance and repairs, as well-maintained examples are crucial for a reliable ownership experience.
