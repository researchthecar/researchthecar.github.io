# 2021 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe offers lively performance and a well-appointed interior for buyers seeking a compact luxury sport sedan. While early owner feedback and data show standard mechanical soundness for its powertrain, prospective used-car shoppers should review available service histories and inspect the vehicle thoroughly for minor electrical or interior trim quirks.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (301 hp)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 301 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong acceleration paired with standard all-wheel drive. It is generally considered robust, provided routine maintenance schedules are strictly followed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed sport automatic transmission provides quick and smooth shifts across all driving modes. It is a proven unit in BMW's modern lineup and typically exhibits strong long-term dependability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V554000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M235i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these assemblies fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." During a crash, a seat belt retractor that does not function as intended increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the seat belt retractors (including the seat belts) and, if necessary, replace the retractors, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V091000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M235i&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." If the sunroof continues to close after the vehicle is shut off, it may increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the sunroof electronic coding software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
