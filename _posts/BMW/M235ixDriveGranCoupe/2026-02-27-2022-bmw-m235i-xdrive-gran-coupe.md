# 2022 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe offers sporty performance and standard all-wheel drive, blending everyday usability with athletic styling. When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should review available maintenance records and inspect the cabin for signs of previous liquid spills, as interior electronics can be sensitive to moisture near the console.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 301 horsepower and 331 lb-ft of torque, delivering strong acceleration. It is paired exclusively with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system and generally provides dependable performance when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The eight-speed automatic transmission delivers quick and smooth shifts tailored to the sporty character of the Gran Coupe. This gearbox is generally robust, though proper care and fluid maintenance are recommended for long-term reliability.

### Shift Selector and Sensors

Cabin electronics and center console controls are generally durable, but sensitive components can be impacted by accidental liquid spills.

**Transmission Sensor Moisture Damage**
- **Symptoms:** A liquid spill in the center console cupholders can cause internal sensors underneath the gear shifter to get wet, triggering dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspect the center console area for sticky residue or liquid damage, and have a qualified technician replace any compromised shifter sensors.
