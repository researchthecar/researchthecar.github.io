**Introduction**

The 2022 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe is a compact luxury sports car that combines performance and practicality. It features a 3.0L turbocharged inline-six engine that delivers 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission and standard xDrive all-wheel drive. Despite its impressive performance, some owners have reported specific issues with their vehicles, falling into the following categories:

**Engine**

**None Found**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Jerky Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates and shifts abruptly during gear changes.
    * Solution: Software updates or transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
* **Premature Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails prematurely, causing loss of power or difficulty shifting gears.
    * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Noisy Struts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Struts make clunking or rattling noises over rough roads.
    * Solution: Replacement of struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Infotainment system freezes, crashes, or malfunctions.
    * Solution: Software updates or component replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains excessively, causing difficulties starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and replacement of faulty components or battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Creaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof creaks or rattles when opening or closing.
    * Solution: Lubrication or replacement of sunroof components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

While the 2022 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe offers exciting performance and a luxurious interior, it has faced some reliability concerns. The most severe issues include premature transmission failure and electrical glitches, which can be costly to repair. Owners should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing or owning this vehicle.
