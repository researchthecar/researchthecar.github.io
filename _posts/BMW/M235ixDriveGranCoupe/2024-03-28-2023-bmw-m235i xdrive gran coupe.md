**Introduction:**

The 2023 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe is a high-performance four-door coupe powered by a turbocharged 3.0-liter inline-six engine. The engine produces 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive. The M235i is praised for its athletic handling, potent engine, and luxurious interior, but has also received some criticism for its cramped rear seats and limited cargo space.
