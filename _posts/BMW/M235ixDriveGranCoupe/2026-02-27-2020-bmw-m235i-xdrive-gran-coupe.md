# 2020 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe delivers sharp performance, modern technology, and the everyday practicality of an all-wheel-drive four-door setup. For used car buyers, understanding the vehicle's powertrain configurations and monitoring standard maintenance items will help ensure a rewarding ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (301 hp)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 301 horsepower and 332 lb-ft of torque, offering strong acceleration and responsive power delivery. Overall, it is a robust powerplant, though routine maintenance and quality fluids remain essential for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with xDrive

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system, this setup delivers quick, smooth shifts and confident traction in various weather conditions. It is generally reliable when fluid changes are performed at recommended intervals.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V091000: Visibility:Power Window Devices And Controls](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M235i&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 118, "Power-operated Window Systems." If the sunroof continues to close after the vehicle is shut off, it may increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the sunroof electronic coding software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 BMW M235i xDrive Gran Coupe is an engaging and capable compact luxury sedan that balances performance with everyday usability. Prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection and verify service history to ensure optimal mechanical condition.
