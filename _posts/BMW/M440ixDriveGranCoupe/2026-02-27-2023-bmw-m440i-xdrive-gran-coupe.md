# 2023 BMW M440i xDrive Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 BMW M440i xDrive Gran Coupe blends practical four-door usability with robust performance from its turbocharged inline-six engine and standard all-wheel-drive system. As a relatively recent model year, it benefits from BMW's latest powertrain engineering refinements, offering strong luxury credentials and dynamic driving characteristics. Prospective used-car buyers looking at this performance-oriented liftback will find a well-built luxury vehicle, though routine inspection of modern electronics and high-performance wear items remains wise.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (B58)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with a mild-hybrid system. The B58 engine family is widely regarded by industry experts and owners as exceptionally robust and reliable for a high-performance luxury powertrain.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission features paddle shifters and integrates with the xDrive all-wheel-drive system to deliver rapid, smooth gear changes. This ZF-sourced unit is renowned for both durability and refined shifting behavior across various driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V118000: Electrical System:Body Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=M440i&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Doors that do not lock as intended while the vehicle is in motion may open during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the ECU software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 BMW M440i xDrive Gran Coupe presents a solid choice for used-car shoppers seeking high-end performance, modern technology, and everyday usability. Its proven powertrain options enjoy a strong reputation for durability. Buyers should still ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection is performed to verify the condition of high-performance components and complex onboard electronics.
