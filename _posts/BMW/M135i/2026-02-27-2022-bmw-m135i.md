# 2022 BMW M135i

### Overview
The 2022 BMW M135i, a potent all-wheel-drive hatchback, generally presents a strong reliability profile, reflecting BMW's engineering prowess. It is equipped with a robust 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine, known for its performance and relative durability. The xDrive all-wheel-drive system and the 8-speed Steptronic automatic transmission are also well-regarded for their smooth operation and longevity. While specific widespread mechanical failures are uncommon for this model year, like any performance vehicle, diligent maintenance is key to ensuring optimal long-term operation. Owners generally report satisfaction with the vehicle's build quality and technological integration, with most issues being minor and related to user experience rather than core mechanical integrity.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 BMW M135i demonstrates commendable reliability, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The 2.0L turbocharged engine and the 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have a good track record. While the electrical system, specifically the infotainment, can occasionally exhibit minor software glitches, these are typically resolved with updates and do not indicate fundamental reliability concerns. The chassis, suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally well-built and hold up well. Overall, the M135i is a reliable performance hatchback, provided it receives regular maintenance as recommended by the manufacturer. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience with a low probability of significant mechanical failures.
