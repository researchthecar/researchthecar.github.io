# 2019 BMW X4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 BMW X4, buyers will find a stylish coupe-SUV that pairs luxury with athletic performance. While owner feedback is generally manageable, prospective buyers should review available maintenance histories and inspect the vehicle for common electrical and mechanical quirks reported by early adopters.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Engine

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine delivers robust everyday power and efficiency for base trims. It offers dependable performance when properly maintained with timely fluid changes.

### 3.0L Turbocharged I6 Engine

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine provides exhilarating acceleration and performance for higher-tier M40i models. It is generally robust, though performance-oriented driving styles mean buyers should verify thorough service records.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission delivers swift, refined shifts across all trim levels and drivetrain configurations. It is widely regarded as a durable unit in modern BMW applications.

## Electrical

### Emergency Call and Telematics System

Electrical complaints center largely on advanced cabin tech and accessory modules rather than core powertrain wiring.

**Emergency Call System Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** An 'Emergency call system malfunction' warning message appears on the dash, often linked to faults in the emergency battery hardware, GPS antenna, or microphone circuit.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealer inspect the roof aerial cover, telematics control unit, and backup battery connections to replace faulty hardware.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Driver assistance features add safety and convenience, though sensor anomalies can occasionally cause unexpected alerts.

**Random Collision Detection Activation**
- **Symptoms:** The collision mitigation system triggers warnings or unexpectedly applies the brakes at highway speeds without any obstacles present.
- **Solution:** Check the windshield-mounted camera and front sensor alignment or have the dealer scan for software updates and sensor calibration issues.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V453000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A reduction of braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the rear brake circuit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V152000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The instrument panel casing may not have been tightened properly to the passenger air bag. In the event of a crash necessitating air bag deployment, the air bag could damage the instrument panel and cause small pieces of the panel to strike the driver or other occupants, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the instrument panel casing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V563000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front seatback frames may have been improperly welded, allowing the seatback to unexpectedly fold forward or rearward. In the event of a crash, there is an increased risk of injury if the seatback folds forward or rearward.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front seats, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V684000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." The lack of an image in the back-up camera display increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will update the back-up camera software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V521000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front passenger seat occupant classification system may have been improperly assembled, which could result in the misclassification of the seat as unoccupied, and deactivate the front air bag. A deactivated front air bag will not deploy in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat foam, which includes the occupant classification system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V291000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The instrument panel assembly may have been improperly manufactured, possibly affecting the deployment of the passenger's frontal air bag. If the front passenger air bag does not deploy properly in the event of a crash, the occupant has an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the instrument panel assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 BMW X4 offers engaging driving dynamics and luxury appeal, but used buyers should remain vigilant. Prioritize models with comprehensive dealer service records, and ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection checks the electrical systems and driver assistance sensors.
