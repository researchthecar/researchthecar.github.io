# 2021 BMW X4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 BMW X4, buyers will find a stylish coupe-SUV that pairs luxury with athletic performance. While general owner feedback points to a rewarding driving experience, prospective buyers should review available owner reports regarding powertrain components and interior details to make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (xDrive30i)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing balanced daily performance. It is a common baseline option for the lineup and generally offers solid drivability when properly maintained.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M40i)

This 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six (B58) delivers around 382 horsepower and 365 lb-ft of torque for robust performance. It is widely regarded as a strong and smooth powerplant, though buyers should verify service history.

**Premature Oil Pump Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle owners have reported concerns regarding abnormal engine noises or potential oil pump vulnerabilities on certain B58-family configurations.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified BMW technician to check oil pressure and overall engine health.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission with xDrive

This smooth 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with BMW's intelligent xDrive all-wheel-drive system to deliver quick, refined shifts. It is generally robust, though occasional all-wheel-drive drivetrain complaints have been noted by owners.

**Transfer Case Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a drivetrain malfunction warning message on the dashboard, occasionally accompanied by mechanical binding or noises from the all-wheel-drive system.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a professional technician is required; severe cases may necessitate transfer case fluid service or component replacement.
- **Cost:** $20,000

## Suspension

### Suspension System

The suspension is tuned for sporty handling, which translates to a firm ride quality that some occupants may find uncomfortably stiff on poorly maintained roads.

**Excessive Ride Stiffness**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers and passengers may report an overly harsh ride, shaking, or discomfort over rough road surfaces.
- **Solution:** Verify that tire pressures are correct and check dampers and bushings for premature wear or stiffness.

## Safety

### Rear Seat Belts

The cabin features premium materials and intuitive technology, though physical wear items like seat belts warrant a manual check during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Seat Belt Fraying and Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have observed rear seat belts fraying or separating unexpectedly near anchor points during normal use.
- **Solution:** Inspect the rear seat belts for signs of wear and have damaged assemblies replaced immediately by a dealer.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V598000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the reduced rearview image view can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, program the rearview camera with updated software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V096000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** When shifting into Reverse, a small portion of the rearview image may be obscured, or the screen may not illuminate. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." If the driver relies on only the rearview camera image, the missing or reduced rearview image view.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rearview camera software and update it if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V199000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Fuel may leak through the failed weld, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V598000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** During engine start, the engine management software may cause loss of braking assist. Loss of braking assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine management software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V211000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The seat frame may not have been welded properly to the seat rails. An improperly welded seat frame may not properly restrain the occupant during a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat frame and back rest, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V636000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Relay](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter relay may corrode, causing the relay to overheat and short circuit. A short circuit in the starter relay may increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V056000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The engine starter may overheat and catch fire due to wear on an internal component. Fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine starter, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 BMW X4 combines luxury appeal with engaging driving dynamics, but used-car buyers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to the all-wheel-drive drivetrain, verify the condition of interior safety restraints, and ensure a professional pre-purchase evaluation is completed before finalizing your purchase.
