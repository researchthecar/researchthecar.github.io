# 2024 BMW X4 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 BMW X4 is a stylish coupe-styled luxury crossover that combines athletic handling with modern cabin technology. Because it is still relatively new to the used market, long-term reliability data is limited, though initial owner feedback highlights a mix of strong powertrain performance and occasional isolated incidents.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (xDrive30i)

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 248 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides a balanced blend of everyday efficiency and responsive acceleration for daily driving.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (M40i)

This robust 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six generates around 382 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance while maintaining respectable refinement for a luxury sport crossover.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed ZF automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the xDrive all-wheel-drive system across all trims. It is widely regarded in the automotive industry for quick, precise shifts and long-term durability.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Owner reports indicate rare instances of unexpected glass breakage overhead.

**Sunroof Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear a loud shattering noise coming from the overhead sunroof area while moving at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Have the sunroof assembly inspected by a professional technician and replace any shattered glass under warranty if applicable.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V770000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=BMW&model=X4&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The steering gear may not have been tightened properly, which can allow it to loosen. A loose steering gear can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the steering gear, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 BMW X4 remains a compelling option for used luxury SUV buyers looking for dynamic performance and distinctive styling. While early reports show minimal widespread mechanical defects, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the exterior glass and sunroof during a pre-purchase evaluation.
