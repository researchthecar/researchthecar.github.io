**Introduction**

The 2015 BMW X4 is a luxury compact SUV that slots between the X3 and X6 in BMW's lineup. It features a sloping roofline that gives it a sporty appearance. The X4 is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder, a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six, and a 3.0-liter diesel inline-six. The X4 is also available with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive.

People who like the X4 praise its stylish design, powerful engines, and sporty handling. However, some people find the X4's interior to be cramped, and its sloping roofline can make it difficult to get in and out of.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder**

* **Turbocharger failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel injector failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The fuel injectors may fail, causing a rough idle, misfires, and decreased fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six**

* **High-pressure fuel pump failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The high-pressure fuel pump may fail, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0-liter diesel inline-six**

* **Emissions system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The emissions system may fail, causing the vehicle to fail emissions tests.
    * Solution: Replace the emissions system components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-speed automatic transmission**

* **Transmission control module failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission control module may fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not at all.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission fluid leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may leak fluid, causing a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut mount failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The strut mount may fail, causing a clunking noise and a decrease in handling performance.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock absorber failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The shock absorbers may fail, causing a decrease in handling performance and a rough ride.
    * Solution: Replace the shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may fail, causing the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, causing the battery to not charge and the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both headlights may fail.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tail light failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or both tail lights may fail.
    * Solution: Replace the tail light.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard may rattle due to a loose trim panel or a faulty screw.
    * Solution: Tighten the trim panel or screw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat adjustment failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat may not adjust properly due to a faulty switch or motor.
    * Solution: Replace the switch or motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2015 BMW X4 is a stylish and sporty SUV, but it is not without its problems. The X4 has been known to suffer from a variety of issues, including engine, transmission, and suspension problems. Some of these issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to factor in the cost of potential repairs when considering purchasing an X4.
