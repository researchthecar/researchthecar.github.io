# 2016 BMW 435i Gran Coupe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 BMW 435i Gran Coupe combines sleek four-door styling with engaging driving dynamics and luxury appeal. Powered by BMW's renowned turbocharged inline-six engine, it delivers a spirited performance while maintaining daily drivability. Used car buyers looking at this model should evaluate the maintenance history carefully, as luxury European performance vehicles demand consistent upkeep to remain trouble-free.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 (N55)

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 300 lb-ft of torque. It is generally praised for its smooth power delivery and robust performance, though regular fluid maintenance is critical for longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Sport Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission offers quick, refined shifts and pairs exceptionally well with the inline-six engine. It has built a reputation for solid reliability in modern BMW applications when properly maintained.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission caters to enthusiast drivers seeking direct mechanical engagement. It is a rarer find in the Gran Coupe body style but offers straightforward mechanical durability.
