**Introduction:**

The 1991 Oldsmobile Silhouette is a front-wheel-drive minivan powered by a standard 3.1L V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable and spacious cabin, along with a variety of amenities. However, like all vehicles, it has its share of potential problems.

**Engine**

**3.1L V6**

* **Head gasket failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Slipping gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle fails to accelerate properly, hesitation during shifting
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Worn shock absorbers:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bumpy ride, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Failed sway bar:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive body roll during cornering
    * Solution: Replace sway bar
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, difficulty starting, battery warning light
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Starter problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine fails to crank
    * Solution: Replace starter or fix electrical connections
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust around wheel wells:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust spots visible on wheel wells
    * Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Defective door handles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles break or become loose
    * Solution: Replace door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Cracked dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appear on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard or repair cracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Power window failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows fail to operate
    * Solution: Repair or replace window motors or switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1991 Oldsmobile Silhouette is prone to various issues that can affect its reliability and performance. However, many of these issues are not severe and can be resolved at a reasonable cost. The most common problems include head gasket failure, transmission slipping, worn shock absorbers, electrical issues, and rust around wheel wells. Owners may also experience cracked dashboards and power window failures. By addressing these potential problems promptly, owners can help maintain their Silhouette in good working condition.
