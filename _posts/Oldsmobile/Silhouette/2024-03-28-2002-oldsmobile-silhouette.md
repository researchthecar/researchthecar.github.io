**Introduction:**
The 2002 Oldsmobile Silhouette is a minivan praised for its spacious cabin, powerful engine, and comfortable ride. It came with two engine options: a 3.4L V6 and a 3.8L V6, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. However, despite its desirability, it has several known issues that potential buyers should be aware of.

**Engine**
**3.4L V6**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, performance issues
	- Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
- **Power Steering Pump Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Loss of power steering assist, whining noises
	- Solution: Replace power steering pump
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**3.8L V6**
- **No Major Engine Issues Found**

**Transmission**
**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
- **Transmission Slipping:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
	- Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
- **Torque Converter Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: shuddering, slipping, overheating
	- Solution: Replace torque converter
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+
- **No Other Transmission Issues Found**

**Suspension**
- **Front Strut Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Leaking struts, clunking noises, poor handling
	- Solution: Replace front struts
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+ per strut
- **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, poor handling, uneven tire wear
	- Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per shock absorber

**Electrical**
- **Electrical Shorts:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, intermittent electrical problems
	- Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical shorts
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on location and severity
- **Alternator Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, dimming lights, dashboard warnings
	- Solution: Replace alternator
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
- **Starter Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Engine will not crank, clicking noises
	- Solution: Replace starter
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Exterior**
- **Power Mirror Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Mirror does not adjust, jammed
	- Solution: Replace power mirror motor
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+ per mirror
- **No Other Exterior Issues Found**

**Interior**
- **Plastic Trim Rattle:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling plastic trim on dashboard or door panels
	- Solution: Secure or replace loose trim
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies
- **Dashboard Odometer Failure:**
	- Description/Symptoms: Odometer display is dim or malfunctioning
	- Solution: Repair dashboard odometer or replace instrument cluster
	- Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Summary:**
The 2002 Oldsmobile Silhouette is a capable minivan with a comfortable and spacious interior. However, it is not without a number of potential issues. The engine and transmission can develop problems that can be expensive to repair, while the suspension and electrical systems can also have their fair share of problems. The cost of repairs can vary depending on the severity of the issue and the availability of parts. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
