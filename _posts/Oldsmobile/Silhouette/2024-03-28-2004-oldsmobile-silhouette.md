**Introduction**
The 2004 Oldsmobile Silhouette is a minivan that was available with two different engine and transmission options. 

# **Engine**
## **3.4L V6**
* **Engine Internal Damage:** Excessive oil consumption leading to overheating, reduced power, and knocking noises.
  * Cause: Faulty piston rings.
  * Solution: Engine replacement or major overhaul.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
  * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign ID 11V232000
## **3.8L V6**
* **No Issues Found**
# **Transmission**
## **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
* **Transmission Failure:** Slipping, jerking gears, or refusing to engage.
  * Cause: Internal component failure (e.g., clutch packs, torque converter).
  * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500+
  * Recalls: None Found
## **5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
* **No Issues Found** 
# **Suspension**
* **Strut Failure:** Leaking fluid, reduced handling, and uneven tire wear.
  * Cause: Faulty strut seals.
  * Solution: Replacement of struts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1,000+ per axle
* **Sway Bar Bushing Deterioration:** Rattling or knocking noises when driving over bumps.
  * Cause: Worn or damaged sway bar bushings.
  * Solution: Replacement of bushings.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
# **Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** Excessive battery discharge leading to difficulties starting the vehicle.
  * Cause: Faulty alternator or parasitic drain from electronic components.
  * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of specific issue.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
* **Power Window Motor Failure:** Windows refusing to open or close.
  * Cause: Faulty motor or electrical connection.
  * Solution: Replacement of motor or repair of wiring.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150-$300+ per window
# **Exterior**
* **Side Mirror Wobble:** Loose or vibrating side mirrors.
  * Cause: Worn mirror mount or broken retaining clips.
  * Solution: Tightening or replacement of mount or clips.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50-$200+
# **Interior**
* **Radio Failure:** Display issues, loss of audio, or system malfunctions.
  * Cause: Faulty radio unit or electrical connection.
  * Solution: Replacement of radio unit or repair of wiring.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500+
* **Headliner Sagging:** Drooping or falling headliner fabric.
  * Cause: Weak adhesive or deteriorated fabric.
  * Solution: Replacement of headliner.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$400+
# **Summary**
The 2004 Oldsmobile Silhouette exhibits some significant issues, particularly with the engine (internal damage) and transmission (failure). These problems can be costly to repair, ranging from hundreds to thousands of dollars. Other issues, such as suspension and electrical problems, are more manageable in terms of severity and cost. While the Silhouette offers spaciousness and convenience, it's important to be aware of these potential issues before considering a purchase.
