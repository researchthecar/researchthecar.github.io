**Introduction**

The 1997 Oldsmobile LSS is a high-performance sedan that made its name as a NASCAR pace car. It featured a powerful V8 engine and impressive handling capabilities, but it also had some issues that owners have reported. The LSS was available with two engine options: a 5.7L V8 and a 6.5L V8, both paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

* **5.7L V8**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Causes coolant and/or oil leaks, leading to overheating and engine damage.
    * **Solution:** Replace intake manifold gaskets.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **6.5L V8**
    * **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure:** Failure of the head gasket can cause coolant and oil mixing, leading to overheating and engine damage.
    * **Solution:** Replace cylinder head gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Slipping Gears:** Transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting, especially under load.
    * **Solution:** Rebuild or replace transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Can cause electrical system issues, including dim headlights, battery draining, and no-start conditions.
* **Solution:** Replace alternator.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:** Headlights may not turn on or may flicker intermittently.
* **Solution:** Replace headlight switch.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Switch Failure:** Power windows may not operate properly or may get stuck.
* **Solution:** Replace power window switch.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 1997 Oldsmobile LSS has some potential issues, including engine gasket failures, transmission problems, and electrical issues. The severity of these issues can range from minor inconveniences to major engine damage, and the cost of repairs can be substantial. However, the LSS remains a capable and stylish performance sedan that is still enjoyed by many enthusiasts.
