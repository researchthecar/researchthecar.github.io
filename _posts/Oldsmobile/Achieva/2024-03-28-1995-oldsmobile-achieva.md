**Introduction**

The 1995 Oldsmobile Achieva is a mid-size sedan offered with three engine options: a 2.3L Quad 4, a 3.1L V6, and a 3.4L V6. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual, a 4-speed automatic, and a 4-speed overdrive automatic. The Achieva is praised for its spacious interior and smooth ride, but criticized for its lackluster performance and dated styling.

**Engine**

* **2.3L Quad 4**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, rough idle
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant and oil leaks, overheating, engine knocking
        * Solution: Replace head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.1L V6**
    * **Ignition Module Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: No spark, no start, rough idle
        * Solution: Replace ignition module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Power Steering Pump Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Heavy steering, whining noise
        * Solution: Replace power steering pump
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.4L V6**
    * **Alternator Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dead battery, no power to electronics
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **5-Speed Manual**
    * **Clutch Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping, difficulty shifting gears
        * Solution: Replace clutch kit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking fluid, slipping gears
        * Solution: Replace seals, add transmission fluid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **4-Speed Overdrive Automatic**
    * **Solenoid Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, no overdrive
        * Solution: Replace solenoid
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace front struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+ per pair
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies overnight
    * Solution: Test electrical system for parasitic draw
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Wiring Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, shorts
    * Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Antenna Mast Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Antenna breaks off
    * Solution: Replace antenna mast
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on dashboard
    * Solution: Replace dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window does not operate
    * Solution: Replace power window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC or heat does not work properly
    * Solution: Repair or replace climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1995 Oldsmobile Achieva has a range of issues, including engine gasket failures, transmission fluid leaks, suspension problems, electrical malfunctions, and interior issues. While some issues are minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be costly and require major repairs. Overall, the Achieva's reliability is average, with some serious issues to be aware of before purchasing one.
