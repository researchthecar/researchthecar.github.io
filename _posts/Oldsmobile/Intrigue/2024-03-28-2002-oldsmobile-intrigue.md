**Introduction to the 2002 Oldsmobile Intrigue**

The 2002 Oldsmobile Intrigue was a midsize sedan offered with two engine and transmission options: a 3.5L V6 with a 4-speed automatic transmission and a 3.8L V6 with a 5-speed automatic transmission. The 3.5L V6 produced 215 horsepower and 220 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.8L V6 produced 240 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque.

The Intrigue was praised for its comfortable interior, spacious trunk, and powerful engines. However, it was criticized for its dated exterior design, lack of features, and poor fuel economy.

---

**Engine**

**3.5L V6**

* **Intake Gasket Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
  * Solution: Replace intake gasket
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattle or knocking on startup, loss of power
  * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $2000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**3.8L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

---

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, difficulty shifting gears, check engine light
  * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises over bumps, uneven tire wear
  * Solution: Replace strut mounts
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
  * Recalls: None Found

---

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
  * Description/Symptoms: Car won't start, dead battery overnight
  * Solution: Diagnose and fix source of battery drain
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on cause
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Motor Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working, grinding noises from window motors
  * Solution: Replace faulty window motor
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Exterior door handle breaks, door cannot be opened from outside
  * Solution: Replace door handle
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working
  * Solution: Repair or replace faulty wiring or seat heater element
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Oldsmobile Intrigue has several issues that can be costly to repair, particularly engine and transmission problems. The severity of the issues varies, but some, such as intake gasket failure and timing chain tensioner failure, can lead to catastrophic engine damage if not addressed promptly. While the Intrigue offers a comfortable and spacious interior, its dated design, lack of features, and poor fuel economy are major drawbacks.
