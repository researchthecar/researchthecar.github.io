**Introduction:**

The 1998 Oldsmobile Regency is a full-size luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and powerful driving experience. It comes standard with a 3.8L V6 engine rated at 205 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque. A 3.8L supercharged V6 producing 240 horsepower and 280 lb-ft of torque and a 4.0L V8 with 250 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque are optional. Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed manual for the supercharged V6. The Regency is praised for its spacious cabin, smooth ride, and powerful engines, but it is criticized for its high fuel consumption and lack of modern amenities.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**
- **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Intake manifold gasket fails, causing coolant and oil leaks and rough running.
    - Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: 00V135000

**3.8L Supercharged V6**
- **Supercharger failure:** The supercharger can fail, resulting in a loss of power and engine damage.
    - Solution: Replace the supercharger.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: 05V252000

**4.0L V8**
- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**
- **Transmission slipping:** The transmission can slip, causing a loss of power and rough shifting.
    - Solution: Replace the transmission.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: 01V118000

**5-Speed Manual**
- **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

- **Strut mount failure:** The strut mounts can fail, causing a clunking sound and decreased stability.
    - Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail, causing electrical problems and a loss of power.
    - Solution: Replace the alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Tail light failure:** The tail lights can fail, reducing visibility at night.
    - Solution: Replace the tail light assembly.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Power window switch failure:** The power window switches can fail, preventing the windows from being operated.
    - Solution: Replace the power window switches.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary**

While the 1998 Oldsmobile Regency has some common issues, such as intake manifold gasket failure, transmission slipping, and alternator failure, these issues can be expensive to repair. Additionally, the vehicle lacks modern amenities and has high fuel consumption, making it a less desirable option for some buyers.
