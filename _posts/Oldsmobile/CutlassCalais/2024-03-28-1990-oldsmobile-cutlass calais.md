**Vehicle Introduction:** The 1990 Oldsmobile Cutlass Calais was a popular midsize coupe and sedan. It was known for its comfortable ride, stylish design, and affordable price. The Cutlass Calais came with three engine options: a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine, a 3.1-liter V6 engine, and a 3.4-liter V6 engine. The four-cylinder engine produced 110 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.1-liter V6 produced 120 horsepower and 160 lb-ft of torque. The 3.4-liter V6 was the most powerful engine, producing 150 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque. All three engines were paired with a four-speed automatic transmission.

 Solution: Replace head gasket
 Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+

**Transmission**
- 4-speed automatic transmission
 **Transmission slipping**
 Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping gears, difficulty shifting, shuddering
 Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
 Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
 Recalls: None Found
