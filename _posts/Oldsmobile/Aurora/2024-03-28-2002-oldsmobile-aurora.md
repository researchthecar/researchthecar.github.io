**Introduction**

The 2002 Oldsmobile Aurora is a full-size luxury sedan known for its powerful Northstar engine lineup and stylish exterior. Available engine options include:

* 4.0L V8 (Northstar V8 LD8) - 250 hp / 260 lb-ft torque
* 4.0L V8 (Northstar V8 L47) - 295 hp / 290 lb-ft torque

Transmission options include a 4-speed automatic transmission (4T80-E) and a 5-speed manual transmission (Getrag F40-6).

While praised for its performance, comfort, and styling, the Aurora has received criticism for its reliability issues.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8 (Northstar V8 LD8)**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating
Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Water Pump Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating
Solution: Replace water pump
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**4.0L V8 (Northstar V8 L47)**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating
Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission (4T80-E)**

* **Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough shifting
Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Manual Transmission (Getrag F40-6)**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Squealing, clunking noises, uneven tire wear
Solution: Replace control arm bushings
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Power Window Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Inoperative power windows
Solution: Replace power window switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Inoperative headlights
Solution: Replace headlight switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Major Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Heater Core Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, fogging on windshield
Solution: Replace heater core
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Oldsmobile Aurora has several potential issues that can be costly to fix, particularly with the engine and transmission. While the vehicle offers impressive performance and a luxurious interior, its reliability problems should be carefully considered before purchasing.
