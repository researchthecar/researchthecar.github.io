**Introduction**

The 1992 Oldsmobile Cutlass Supreme is a mid-size sedan that offers a comfortable ride and decent performance. It is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, including a 3.1L V6 with a 4-speed automatic transmission, a 3.4L V6 with a 4-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.8L V6 with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Cutlass Supreme has a spacious interior and a large trunk, making it a good choice for families or anyone who needs a practical and reliable car. However, it does have some issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine Issues**

* **3.1L V6**
    * **Intake manifold gasket failure:** This is a common problem on the 3.1L V6 engine. The gasket can fail, causing coolant to leak and the engine to overheat.
    * **Symptoms:** Coolant leak, engine overheating
    * **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **3.4L V6**
    * **Head gasket failure:** This is a serious problem that can cause the engine to overheat and fail.
    * **Symptoms:** Coolant leak, engine overheating, white smoke from the exhaust
    * **Solution:** Replace the head gasket.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $2,000
    * **Recalls:** None Found
* **3.8L V6**
    * **No issues found.**

**Transmission Issues**

* **4-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Slipping gears:** This is a common problem on the 4-speed automatic transmission. The transmission may slip gears, causing the car to hesitate or lose power.
    * **Symptoms:** Transmission slipping, hesitation, loss of power
    * **Solution:** Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500
    * **Recalls:** None Found
