## 1991 Oldsmobile Cutlass Supreme

The 1991 Oldsmobile Cutlass Supreme is a mid-size sedan that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 3.1-liter V6 that produced 140 horsepower and 185 lb-ft of torque. A 3.8-liter V6 was also available, which produced 170 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque. The top-of-the-line engine was a 5.0-liter V8 that produced 190 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque.

Transmission options included a three-speed automatic, a four-speed automatic, and a five-speed manual. The Cutlass Supreme was also available with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive.

The Cutlass Supreme was praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and powerful engines. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle's reliability.

**Engine**

**3.1-Liter V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, engine misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve cover gasket leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from valve covers
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
