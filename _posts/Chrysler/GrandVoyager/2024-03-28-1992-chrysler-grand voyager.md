**Introduction:**

The 1992 Chrysler Grand Voyager is a popular minivan that was offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The 3.0L V6 engine produced 142 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.3L V6 engine produced 150 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque. Both engines were paired with a 3-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Grand Voyager for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and reliable performance. However, there are some common issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** This can cause coolant and oil leaks, as well as overheating. The intake manifold gasket must be replaced. $500 - $1000+
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** This can cause the timing chain to skip, which can lead to engine damage. The timing chain tensioner and guides must be replaced. $1000 - $1500+

**3.3L V6**

* **Valve cover gasket leak:** This can cause oil leaks. The valve cover gaskets must be replaced. $200 - $600+

**Transmission**

* **3-speed automatic transmission failure:** This can cause a variety of symptoms, including slipping, shuddering, and difficulty shifting gears. The transmission must be rebuilt or replaced. $1500 - $2500+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension:** The front suspension components can wear out over time, leading to a variety of symptoms, including noise, vibration, and poor handling. The worn components must be replaced. $500 - $1500+
* **Rear suspension:** The rear suspension components can also wear out over time, leading to similar symptoms. The worn components must be replaced. $500 - $1500+

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure:** The battery can fail over time, especially in cold weather. The battery must be replaced. $100 - $200+
* **Alternator failure:** The alternator can fail over time, which can lead to a variety of electrical problems. The alternator must be replaced. $200 - $400+

**Exterior**

* **Door handles:** The door handles can break or become loose over time. The door handles must be replaced. $100 - $300+
* **Tailgate:** The tailgate can rust or become damaged over time. The tailgate must be replaced. $500 - $1000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard:** The dashboard can crack or become discolored over time. The dashboard must be replaced. $500 - $1000+
* **Seats:** The seats can become worn or torn over time. The seats must be repaired or replaced. $300 - $800+
* **HVAC system:** The HVAC system can fail over time, leading to a variety of problems, including poor heating or cooling performance. The HVAC system must be repaired or replaced. $500 - $1500+

**Summary:**

The 1992 Chrysler Grand Voyager is a reliable minivan with a spacious interior and comfortable ride. However, there are some common issues that owners should be aware of, including intake manifold gasket failure, timing chain tensioner failure, transmission failure, and suspension wear. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to repair them can range from $100 to $2500+.
