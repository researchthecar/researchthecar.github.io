# 2024 Chrysler Voyager Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Chrysler Voyager continues to serve budget-conscious minivan buyers with practical utility and standard family features. As a relatively new model-year vehicle, its reliability profile is primarily shaped by modern powertrain technology and foundational vehicle systems. Used-car shoppers looking at this model should review available history reports and verify proper operation during a test drive.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine generating approximately 287 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque. This well-known powerplant offers smooth everyday acceleration and generally steady long-term performance across Chrysler's minivan lineup.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features a 9-speed automatic transmission designed to optimize highway fuel efficiency and power delivery. Buyers should evaluate shifting smoothness through all gears during a test drive to ensure proper operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V238000: Visibility:Windshield:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Voyager&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The windshield may not have been properly bonded to the vehicle, allowing it to detach during a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 212, "Windshield Mounting." A windshield that detaches from the vehicle during a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the windshield, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V240000: Traction Control System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Voyager&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An inactive traction control system can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the TCS software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V888000: Seats:Barriers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Voyager&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The seat limiter may not have been installed, allowing the seat to move too far forward. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A seat positioned too close to the air bag can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and install a seat limiter as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V302000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Voyager&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." An air bag with insufficient pressure retention increases the risk of ejection and injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the side curtain air bags, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V876000: Equipment Adaptive/Mobility:Wheelchair Restraints/Securement:Latch/Anchor:](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Voyager&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The retractors may not lock, preventing the wheelchair from being properly secured. An unsecured wheelchair can move during transit, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 26V189000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Voyager&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." Air bags with insufficient pressure retention increase the risk of ejection and injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the side curtain air bags, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V358000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Voyager&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The unintended connection may prevent the air bags from deploying, increasing the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the spring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
