## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Uconnect Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In rare cases, head unit replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1200


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may not start after sitting for a period, or battery dies prematurely.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories. Battery replacement may be needed.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Chrysler 300 is generally a reliable vehicle, particularly with its standard 3.6L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission, which have proven robust. The HEMI V8, while powerful, also shows good reliability. The most commonly reported issues tend to be in the electrical system, specifically with the Uconnect infotainment system experiencing occasional glitches and a possibility of intermittent battery drain. These are typically fixable with software updates or diagnostics. Other areas like suspension, exterior, and interior components show good durability. Overall, the 300 offers a dependable full-size sedan experience, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
