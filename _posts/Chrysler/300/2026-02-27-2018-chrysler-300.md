# 2018 Chrysler 300 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Chrysler 300 offers a classic full-size sedan experience with spacious comfort, bold styling, and strong available engine performance. However, used-car buyers should pay close attention to potential electronic and infotainment glitches, as well as drivetrain complaints. Reviewing service records and thoroughly testing all onboard technology during a test drive is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6

The 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 produces approximately 292 to 300 horsepower and serves as the standard engine for most models. It offers a smooth power delivery and balanced fuel economy for daily driving.

### 5.7L Hemi V8

The 5.7-liter Hemi V8 delivers a robust 363 horsepower, providing strong acceleration and traditional muscle-car performance. It is a popular choice for buyers seeking extra passing power and V8 rumble.

## Transmission

### TorqueFlite 8-Speed Automatic

Paired with both engine options, the ZF-sourced 8-speed automatic transmission generally delivers smooth gear changes. Some owners have reported low-speed harshness or shifting irregularities that require professional diagnosis.

**Transmission Shifting and Jolt Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience hard shifts into second gear, jolt sensations at lower speeds during acceleration, or a feeling of the transmission slipping in and out of gear.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission fluid levels, check for software updates, and diagnose internal component wear.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Screen Delamination

Electrical and infotainment grievances are among the more frequently reported issues, particularly regarding screen degradation and backup camera functionality.

**Screen Delamination and Radio Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen LCD display can become delaminated, causing erratic behavior such as random beeping, ghost inputs, and failure of climate control or navigation features.
- **Solution:** Replace the affected infotainment display unit.

**Backup Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** When shifting into reverse, the infotainment screen remains blank or black, preventing the rearview camera image from displaying.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring connections or replace the rearview camera assembly and display module as needed.

## Exterior

### Interior Door Panels

Cabin fit and finish are generally comfortable, though isolated issues with door panel security have been documented.

**Door Panel Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The interior trim and paneling on the vehicle doors can become unsecured or warp away from the door frame.
- **Solution:** Resecure or replace the affected door panel clips and assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V280000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=300&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the incorrect park lock rod is installed, the transmission may not shift into 'PARK' and keep the vehicle from moving, increasing the risk of unintended vehicle movement and the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will install the correct park lock rod, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V332000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=300&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are being recalled to address a defect that could prevent the cruise control system from disengaging. If, when using cruise control, there is a short circuit within the vehicle's wiring, the driver may not be able to shut off the cruise control either by depressing the brake pedal or manually turning the system off once it has been engaged.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the software, and perform a software flash on the engine or powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V524000: Electrical System:Wiring:Front Underhood](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=300&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Chrysler will notify owners, and dealers will replace the powertrain control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V198000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain:Inflator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=300&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** An inflator rupture may result in sharp metal fragments striking occupants, resulting in injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace both side curtain air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Chrysler 300 remains a comfortable and powerful choice for used sedan buyers, but prospective owners should inspect the vehicle carefully. Pay close attention to the touchscreen display for signs of delamination or bubbling, verify that the backup camera operates correctly, and test drive the vehicle to ensure smooth transmission operation across all gears.
