# 2023 Chrysler Pacifica Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Chrysler Pacifica delivers strong family-friendly utility, versatile seating configurations, and available all-wheel drive or plug-in hybrid capability. However, owners have reported noteworthy electrical and powertrain concerns that potential buyers should evaluate carefully. Reviewing service records and verifying that active recall repairs have been completed is strongly advised.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 (Pentastar)

A naturally aspirated 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 287 horsepower paired with a traditional 9-speed automatic transmission, offering standard front-wheel drive or available all-wheel drive.

### 3.6L V6 Plug-In Hybrid (PHEV)

A 3.6-liter V6 plug-in hybrid powertrain utilizing an eFlite electrically variable transmission to deliver efficiency and electric-only driving range, though some hybrid-specific electrical and propulsion complaints have been documented.

**Loss of Motive Power and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine shutdowns accompanied by instrument cluster messages like 'Stop Safely - Vehicle Will Shut Off Soon' while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic evaluation by a dealership, which may involve powertrain control module updates or component replacements.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

A standard 9-speed automatic transmission paired with the Pentastar V6 gas engine, providing smooth highway cruising and gear selection.

### eFlite Electrically Variable Transmission

An electronically managed hybrid transmission designed for the plug-in hybrid variant, though select owner complaints note intermittent shifting anomalies and limp mode activations.

**Shifter and Parking Mechanism Defects**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may display 'Service Shifter' warnings, enter limp mode, or abruptly shift into park while moving at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a certified dealer to inspect the gear selector, wiring harness, or transmission control module for faults.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Shifter

Manages vehicle starting, interior electronics, and advanced driver assistance interfaces, occasionally prone to intermittent sensor faults.

**Dashboard Warning Lights and Starting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine lights, unexpected vehicle shut-offs during auto-stop events, and dashboard alerts prompting the driver to service the shifter.
- **Solution:** Inspect the battery health, key fob battery, and associated wiring connectors or CAN bus communication lines.

## Exterior

### Seating

Cabin seating infrastructure featuring Stow 'n Go capabilities, though physical latch mechanisms require careful inspection for wear.

**Seat Back Collapse and Headrest Adjustment**
- **Symptoms:** Second-row seat backs may unlatch or recline unexpectedly, and headrests may fail to lock securely into place.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat latch mechanisms and replace broken hinge or locking components as necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Occupant protection infrastructure that monitors safety restraints and side-curtain deployment systems.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates on the dashboard, signaling a potential fault within the supplemental restraint system.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the airbag control module for diagnostic trouble codes and repair faulty wiring or sensors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V010000: Electrical System:Wiring:Connectors/Plugs/Receptacles](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An internal transmission wiring connector may short circuit, resulting in an unexpected engine shutdown. An unexpected engine shutdown can cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the Power Inverter Module (PIM) software and, if necessary, update the instrument panel cluster (IPC) and the electronic control module (ECM) software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V500000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the fuel line as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V753000: Electrical System:12V/24V/48V Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short may occur due to an incorrectly installed 12-volt battery isolator post, resulting in a fire while parked or driving. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will correct the isolator to fit the anti-rotation stud, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V199000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Clockspring/Spiral Cassette](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The steering column control module may not be welded properly, which can prevent the driver's air bag from deploying in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An air bag that does not deploy in a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the steering column control module, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V436000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the radio software or the software will be OTA, "Over-The-Air," free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V793000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An improperly secured connector for the side curtain air bags may prevent the air bags from deploying. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 214, "Side Impact Protection" and 226, "Ejection Mitigation." An air bag that does not deploy during a crash increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the connector, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V388000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing error, the rearview camera image may not display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V302000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Chrysler&model=Pacifica&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." An air bag with insufficient pressure retention increases the risk of ejection and injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the side curtain air bags, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Chrysler Pacifica is a feature-rich and comfortable choice for families, but prospective buyers should inspect the electrical systems and powertrain behavior closely during a test drive. Verifying service history and ensuring that all open recalls have been completed by a certified dealer will help mitigate potential ownership risks.
