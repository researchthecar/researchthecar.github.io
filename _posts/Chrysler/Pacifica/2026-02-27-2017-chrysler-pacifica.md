# 2017 Chrysler Pacifica

### Overview
The 2017 Chrysler Pacifica, a popular minivan, generally offers a solid ownership experience with a focus on family utility and comfort. It was introduced as a replacement for the Town & Country and offers a refined ride and a spacious interior. While most owners report satisfaction, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where potential issues have been noted. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into common problems, their symptoms, potential solutions, and estimated repair costs for various components and powertrain options.

---


## Engine

### 3.6L Pentastar V6
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level warnings.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking seals or gaskets (e.g., valve cover gasket, oil pan gasket).
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Overheating, visible coolant puddles, sweet smell, low coolant level.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of hoses, water pump, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic (948TE)
**Shuddering or Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Rough shifting, delayed engagement, vibration during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Software updates, transmission fluid flush and fill, or internal component repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1500

**Transmission Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of drive, grinding noises, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $5000+

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen fails to respond, system reboots unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Software updates, hard reset, or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1000


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start, battery dies prematurely.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty modules or components causing drain.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Power Sliding Doors
**Malfunctioning Doors**
- **Symptoms:** Doors fail to open/close, get stuck, or operate erratically.
- **Solution:** Adjustment of sensors, lubrication of tracks, or replacement of motor/actuator.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Interior

### Climate Control
**Blower Motor Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No air or intermittent airflow from vents, strange noises from dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### Seat Actuators
**Power Seat Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power seats fail to move forward/backward or adjust height.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty seat motor or switch.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Chrysler Pacifica is a competent minivan with a generally good reliability record. The most frequently reported issues tend to revolve around the 9-speed automatic transmission, particularly concerning shifting quality and occasional failures, as well as some electrical gremlins like infotainment system glitches and battery drain. Engine-related problems are less common but can include oil and coolant leaks on the standard V6. Exterior components like power sliding doors can also experience malfunctions. While these issues can be frustrating and costly to repair, they are not universal, and many Pacifica owners enjoy trouble-free operation. Regular maintenance and addressing early warning signs are crucial for mitigating potential problems.
