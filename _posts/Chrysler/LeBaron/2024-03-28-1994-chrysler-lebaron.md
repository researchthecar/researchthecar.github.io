**Introduction:**

The 1994 Chrysler LeBaron is a front-wheel-drive luxury coupe that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It was praised for its comfortable interior, sleek design, and responsive handling. However, it also had its share of issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Can cause coolant and oil leaks, overheating, and engine damage. Requires replacement of the intake manifold gasket. $400 - $900+
- **Head Gasket Failure:** Can cause coolant and oil leaks, overheating, and engine damage. Requires replacement of the head gaskets. $1,000 - $2,000+

**3.3L V6**
- **Throttle Position Sensor Issues:** Can cause rough idling, stalling, and poor fuel economy. Requires replacement of the throttle position sensor. $150 - $300+
- **Exhaust Manifold Cracking:** Can cause exhaust leaks and loss of power. Requires replacement of the exhaust manifold. $300 - $700+
- **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure:** Can cause hard starting, stalling, and engine misfires. Requires replacement of the camshaft position sensor. $100 - $250+

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic A604**
- **Slipping Gears:** Can cause the transmission to lose power or gears to slip out of place. Requires transmission rebuild or replacement. $1,500 - $3,000+
- **Torque Converter Shudder:** Can cause the transmission to shudder during acceleration. Requires replacement of the torque converter. $700 - $1,500+

**Suspension**

- **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** Can cause clunking or grinding noises, and uneven tire wear. Requires replacement of the strut mount bearings. $200 - $400+
- **Sway Bar Link Failure:** Can cause clunking or rattling noises when turning. Requires replacement of the sway bar links. $100 - $250+

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:** Can cause the battery to drain, difficulty starting the engine, or electrical system malfunctions. Requires replacement of the alternator. $300 - $700+
- **Battery Cable Corrosion:** Can cause difficulty starting the engine or electrical system malfunctions. Requires cleaning or replacement of the battery cables. $50 - $150+
- **Lighting Switch Failure:** Can cause the headlights, taillights, or other lights to malfunction. Requires replacement of the lighting switch. $100 - $250+

**Exterior**

- **Sunroof Drain Plugs Clogging:** Can cause water to leak into the cabin. Requires cleaning or replacement of the drain plugs. $50 - $150+
- **Door Handle Failure:** Can cause difficulty opening the doors. Requires replacement of the door handles. $50 - $150+

**Interior**

- **Dashboard Panel Cracking:** Can cause the dashboard to crack or break. Requires replacement of the dashboard panel. $200 - $400+
- **Power Window Failure:** Can cause difficulty raising or lowering the windows. Requires replacement of the power window motor or switch. $100 - $350+
- **Seat Belt Retractor Failure:** Can cause the seat belt to fail to retract properly. Requires replacement of the seat belt retractor. $150 - $300+

**Summary:**

The 1994 Chrysler LeBaron has a variety of issues that can range in severity and cost from minor repairs to major engine or transmission problems. While it is a comfortable and stylish car, potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing.
