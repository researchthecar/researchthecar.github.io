**Introduction:**

The 1990 Chrysler LeBaron is a mid-size luxury sedan that was available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine was a 2.5L inline-4 with 100 horsepower and 137 lb-ft of torque, while the optional engines included a 3.0L V6 with 141 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque, and a turbocharged 2.2L inline-4 with 160 horsepower and 220 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options included a three-speed automatic, a four-speed automatic, and a five-speed manual.

Owners of the 1990 Chrysler LeBaron appreciate its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and stylish exterior. However, the vehicle has also been known to experience a number of issues over the years.

**Engine**

**2.5L Inline-4**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Causes coolant and oil leaks, overheating, and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head gasket failure:** Causes coolant and oil leaks, overheating, and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the head gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.0L V6**

* **Valve cover gasket leaks:** Causes oil leaks and engine deposits.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing belt failure:** Can cause catastrophic engine damage if not replaced on schedule.
    * Solution: Replace the timing belt.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.2L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Turbocharger failure:** Causes loss of power and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel injector failure:** Causes engine misfires and poor performance.
    * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Three-Speed Automatic**

* **Slipping:** Causes the transmission to lose power or refuse to engage gears.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Four-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting problems:** Causes the transmission to shift gears too late or not at all.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission control module or solenoid pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Five-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch failure:** Causes the clutch to slip or chatter.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure:** Causes the vehicle to bounce or handle poorly.
    * Solution: Replace the struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway bar link failure:** Causes clunking noises and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** Causes the battery to discharge and the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure:** Causes the vehicle to not start.
    * Solution: Replace the starter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical short:** Causes blown fuses, flickering lights, and electrical system failure.
    * Solution: Find and repair the short circuit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door handle failure:** Causes the door handle to break or become stuck.
    * Solution: Replace the door handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Window regulator failure:** Causes the window to become stuck or fall out of the door.
    * Solution: Replace the window regulator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard cracking:** Causes the dashboard to crack or warp.
    * Solution: Replace the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat upholstery wear:** Causes the seat upholstery to become torn or worn.
    * Solution: Replace the seat upholstery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate control failure:** Causes the climate control system to malfunction or fail completely.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module or components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1990 Chrysler LeBaron has a number of potential issues, including engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems. The severity of these issues varies, but some can be costly to repair. Overall, the LeBaron is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of its potential issues before purchasing one.
