## Introduction

The 1995 Chrysler New Yorker was a full-size luxury sedan known for its spacious cabin, comfortable ride, and powerful engine options. Available engine options included the 3.5L EGA V6 with 214 horsepower and 222 lb-ft of torque, and the 4.0L EGH V8 with 253 horsepower and 284 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options consisted of a 4-speed automatic for the V6 and a 4-speed automatic with overdrive for the V8.

Owners praised the New Yorker for its comfortable and roomy interior, smooth ride, and powerful V8 engine. However, some complaints included poor fuel economy, a lack of modern features, and questionable reliability.

## Engine

### 3.5L EGA V6

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, engine overheating, reduced engine power
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

### 4.0L EGH V8

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, reduced engine power, engine stalling
Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
Estimated Cost to Fix: $700 - $1,200+

**Head Gasket Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, engine overheating, milky-white oil
Solution: Replace head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+

## Transmission

### 4-Speed Automatic

**Transmission Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or slips while shifting, lack of power
Solution: Replace transmission clutch packs and/or torque converter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

### 4-Speed Automatic with Overdrive

**Overdrive Solenoid Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission stuck in lower gears, lack of power
Solution: Replace overdrive solenoid
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifting erratically, transmission not shifting into gear
Solution: Replace TCM
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

## Suspension

**Strut Mount Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Noises over bumps, reduced steering stability
Solution: Replace strut mounts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

## Electrical

**Alternator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, flickering lights, electrical system failure
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Starter Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Engine cranks but does not start
Solution: Replace starter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

## Exterior

**Door Lock Actuator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking with remote or manual controls
Solution: Replace door lock actuator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

## Interior

**Power Window Switch Failure**
Description/Symptoms: Windows not rolling up or down
Solution: Replace power window switch
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

## Summary

The 1995 Chrysler New Yorker offers a comfortable and spacious ride, but it is known for some potential reliability issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. While some repairs can be relatively inexpensive, others, such as replacing the head gasket or performing major transmission work, can be quite costly. Overall, this vehicle may require more frequent and potentially expensive repairs compared to other vehicles in its class.
