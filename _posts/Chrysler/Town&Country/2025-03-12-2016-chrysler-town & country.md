The 2016 Chrysler Town & Country is a minivan known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and Stow 'n Go seating, which allows the second- and third-row seats to fold completely into the floor. It came standard with a 3.6-liter Pentastar V6 engine producing 283 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Owners appreciate the convenience and versatility of the van for family transportation. However, some common complaints include issues with the power sliding doors, electrical glitches, and premature wear of some components.

**Engine**

**3.6L Pentastar V6**

*   **Oil Filter Housing Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks around the oil filter housing, often dripping onto the ground or causing a burning smell.
    *   Solution: Replace the oil filter housing. Some redesigned housings are available to address this issue.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Rocker Arm Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Ticking noise from the engine, misfires, and potential engine damage. This issue affects rocker arms with part number 5184296AC and earlier revisions.
    *   Solution: Replace the failed rocker arm(s) and lifter(s).
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+ depending on the extent of the damage and number of rockers replaced.
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Camshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, rough idling, and check engine light activation with codes related to camshaft position.
    *   Solution: Replace the faulty camshaft position sensor.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic (62TE)**

*   **Rough Shifting/Slipping**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifts between gears, or the transmission slipping out of gear. Can be accompanied by unusual noises.
    *   Solution: Diagnosis is key; could involve valve body replacement, solenoid replacement, or a complete transmission rebuild/replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 (solenoid replacement) - $3000+ (transmission rebuild/replacement).
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Torque Converter Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibrations, especially during acceleration or while maintaining highway speeds.
    *   Solution: Replace the torque converter and flush the transmission fluid.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Rear Suspension Sagging**
    *   Description/Symptoms: The rear of the vehicle sits noticeably lower than the front, especially when loaded with passengers or cargo. Can cause handling issues.
    *   Solution: Replace the rear coil springs or install aftermarket air-assist suspension to compensate for the sag.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

*   **Power Sliding Door Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Power sliding doors failing to open or close properly, intermittent operation, or getting stuck.
    *   Solution: Check the door wiring harness for breaks or shorts, lubricate the door tracks and rollers, or replace the door motor/actuator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 (lubrication/wiring repair) - $800+ (motor/actuator replacement).
    *   Recalls: None Found

*   **Radio/Uconnect System Malfunctions**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Radio not turning on, touchscreen freezing or becoming unresponsive, Bluetooth connectivity problems, or navigation issues.
    *   Solution: Software updates may resolve some issues. In other cases, the radio unit needs replacement.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (software update if available) - $800+ (radio unit replacement).
    *   Recalls: 2016 Chrysler Town & Country Radio Uconnect recall. Recall Number 18V252000

*   **TIPM (Totally Integrated Power Module) Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Multiple electrical issues including, but not limited to, fuel pump relay failure (causing stalling), wipers not working, horn not working, or airbag light illuminating.
    *   Solution: Replace the TIPM.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

*   **Power Liftgate Problems**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Liftgate failing to open or close properly, intermittent operation, or getting stuck.
    *   Solution: Check the liftgate struts, latch mechanism, wiring harness, or replace the liftgate motor/actuator.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 (lubrication/wiring repair) - $600+ (motor/actuator replacement).
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

*   **Stow 'n Go Seat Latch Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Difficulty latching or unlatching the Stow 'n Go seats, making it hard to fold them into the floor.
    *   Solution: Lubricate the latch mechanism or replace the latch assembly.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300+
    *   Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Chrysler Town & Country is a versatile minivan, but owners have reported a range of issues. Engine issues, particularly oil leaks and rocker arm failures, can be costly to repair. Transmission problems, electrical glitches with the power sliding doors and TIPM, and suspension sagging are also prevalent. While some issues are minor and easily fixed, others can be significant and expensive, making careful inspection and a pre-purchase inspection advisable.
