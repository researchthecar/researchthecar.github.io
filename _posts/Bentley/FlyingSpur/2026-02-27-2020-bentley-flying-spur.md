# 2020 Bentley Flying Spur Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Bentley Flying Spur represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, blending effortless performance with handcrafted opulence. As a complex, highly computerized flagship sedan, prospective buyers should approach the pre-owned market with an understanding that maintenance and specialized repairs demand a substantial financial commitment. While owner reports to federal regulators for this model year remain virtually nonexistent, evaluating such an advanced machine still requires careful inspection of its sophisticated powertrain and onboard electronics.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L W12 Twin-Turbocharged

This twin-turbocharged 6.0-liter W12 engine produces approximately 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth and rapid acceleration. It is a highly complex power plant requiring meticulous adherence to maintenance schedules to ensure long-term reliability.

### 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbocharged

The twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 engine generates roughly 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering a slightly more agile driving dynamic while maintaining robust power delivery. This option is generally regarded as a strong and capable performer in the luxury segment.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Paired with all-wheel drive, this 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and seamless power delivery across all driving conditions.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V452000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Flying+Spur&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." A reduction in the quality of the rearview camera image can reduce the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will modify the top view camera holder to improve the water draining ability, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V104000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Storage:Tank Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Flying+Spur&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The fuel tank may not have been welded properly during manufacturing, which could result in a fuel leak. A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify the owner, and a dealer will replace the fuel tank, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
