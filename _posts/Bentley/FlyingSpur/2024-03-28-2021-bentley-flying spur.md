**Introduction:**

The 2021 Bentley Flying Spur is a luxurious sedan that combines performance and style. It is available with a choice of two engine options: a 4.0-liter V8 and a 6.0-liter W12. The V8 produces 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, while the W12 generates 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission.

People who love the Bentley Flying Spur appreciate its spacious cabin, powerful engine, and luxurious amenities. However, some owners have reported issues with various components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.0-Liter V8**

* **Valve Stem Seal Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption and blue smoke from the exhaust
    * Solution: Replace the valve stem seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**6.0-Liter W12**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or clicking noise from the engine, reduced power output
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,500 - $6,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**
    * **Clutch Pack Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping or shuddering transmission
        * Solution: Replace the clutch pack
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $8,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Uneven ride height, loss of air pressure in the suspension system
    * Solution: Repair or replace the air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after a period of time, even when the vehicle is not in use
    * Solution: Replace the battery and investigate for any parasitic drains
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin through the sunroof
    * Solution: Replace the sunroof seal or adjust the sunroof alignment
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not working properly, uneven temperature distribution, excessive noise
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Bentley Flying Spur is a magnificent vehicle, but it is not without its issues. Some of the problems reported by owners can be costly to repair, including engine and transmission failures. However, many of the issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable price. Overall, the Flying Spur is a solid choice for those who are looking for a luxurious and powerful sedan.
