# 2023 Bentley Flying Spur Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Bentley Flying Spur represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering supreme comfort, handcrafted interior finishes, and effortless performance. As a high-end luxury vehicle, it is equipped with highly sophisticated electronic architectures and complex mechanical systems. Buyers in the used market should look for well-maintained examples with complete service histories to ensure these intricate systems continue to operate seamlessly.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

A 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing approximately 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque. It offers brisk performance and a more athletic exhaust note while maintaining refined power delivery.

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged W12

A massive 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged W12 engine generating around 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. Known for its ultra-smooth power delivery and effortless acceleration, it is a masterclass in engineering.

### 2.9L V6 Hybrid

A 2.9-liter turbocharged V6 plug-in hybrid powertrain delivering roughly 536 combined horsepower. This option provides silent electric driving capability alongside traditional combustion performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

An 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with all-wheel drive across the lineup. It delivers rapid, nearly imperceptible gear shifts suited for both relaxed cruising and dynamic driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V314000: Structure:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Flying+Spur&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An incorrectly installed retention bracket can result in the entertainment screen becoming a projectile during a crash, increasing the risk of occupant injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the retention brackets in both front seats, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Bentley Flying Spur is an extraordinary luxury sedan that combines exquisite craftsmanship with commanding performance. Because formal owner complaint data is sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should focus on verifying a comprehensive dealer service history and conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to safeguard their investment.
