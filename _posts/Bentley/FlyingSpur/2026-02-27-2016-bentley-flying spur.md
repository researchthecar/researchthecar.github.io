Owners can expect a robust vehicle, but awareness of potential, albeit infrequent, issues in specific systems can aid in proactive maintenance and informed ownership.

## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, uneven ride height, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to adjust ride height, constant running of compressor, warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor unit.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, navigation errors, Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In some cases, module replacement may be required.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500 (depending on severity and need for module replacement)


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle, frequent battery replacement.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replacement of the faulty component.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Bentley Flying Spur demonstrates a high level of reliability, particularly concerning its potent W12 and V8 engines and the smooth 8-speed automatic transmission. The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are the adaptive air suspension, with potential for leaks or compressor problems, and the sophisticated electrical system, which can occasionally suffer from infotainment glitches or parasitic battery drain. These issues, while not endemic, are more common in complex luxury vehicles. Overall, the Flying Spur offers a dependable ownership experience for those who adhere to recommended maintenance schedules and are prepared for the potential costs associated with specialized luxury vehicle repairs.
