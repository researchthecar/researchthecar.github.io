# 2014 Bentley Mulsanne

### Overview
The 2014 Bentley Mulsanne represents the pinnacle of luxury and performance, offering a bespoke driving experience. As a high-end, hand-built vehicle, its reliability is generally strong, focusing on sophisticated engineering and premium materials. However, like any complex machine, certain areas can experience issues, often related to its advanced systems or the sheer complexity of its construction. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for owners and enthusiasts of this exceptional automobile.

---


## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V8
**Turbocharger Actuator Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, turbo warning light illumination, inconsistent boost pressure.
- **Solution:** Replacement or recalibration of turbocharger wastegate actuators.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500

**Oil Cooler Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil leaks around the oil cooler lines, potential for oil level drops, engine warning light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking oil cooler lines or seals.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900


## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic
**Transmission Control Module (TCM) Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting, delayed engagement, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Software update for the TCM or replacement of the module.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one or more corners, uneven ride height, 'suspension fault' warning.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air springs or associated lines.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000 per corner

**Ride Height Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Incorrect vehicle ride height, suspension warning lights, inconsistent suspension behavior.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ride height sensors.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or reboots intermittently.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment control unit.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


### Keyless Entry System
**Door Handle Sensor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Keyless entry system fails to unlock or lock doors when touching handles.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty door handle sensors.
- **Cost:** $300 - $500 per handle

**Actuator Failure**

### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Bentley Mulsanne is a testament to automotive craftsmanship, with a generally robust mechanical and electrical foundation. The primary areas that may require attention are related to its complex air suspension system, particularly air spring leaks and sensor issues, and potential glitches within the advanced infotainment and keyless entry electronics. Engine and transmission components, while sophisticated, are typically reliable, though issues like turbo actuator malfunctions or TCM glitches can occur. Owners should expect that repairs on such a high-end vehicle will be costly, reflecting the specialized parts and labor involved. Regular, professional maintenance is crucial for preserving the Mulsanne's exceptional performance and longevity.
