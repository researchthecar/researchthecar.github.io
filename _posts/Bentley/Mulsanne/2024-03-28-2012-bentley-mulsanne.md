**Introduction:**

The 2012 Bentley Mulsanne is a luxurious full-size sedan powered by a 6.75-liter V8 engine that produces 505 horsepower and 752 lb-ft of torque. It features a sumptuous interior, advanced technology, and impressive performance.

However, despite its premium status, the Mulsanne has faced some reliability issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **6.75-liter V8**
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, reduced power, and potential engine damage
        * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Crankshaft bearing failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking sound from engine, loss of oil pressure, and potential engine failure
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-speed automatic transmission**
    * **Torque converter shudder:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering during acceleration
        * Solution: Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension compressor failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to adjust ride height, uneven ride, or suspension sagging
    * Solution: Replacement of air suspension compressor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment system glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, navigation errors, or audio distortion
    * Solution: Software update or component replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical harness issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical faults, flickering lights, or component failures
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Navigation system updates:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Outdated maps or navigation software
    * Solution: Subscription to over-the-air updates
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2012 Bentley Mulsanne, while an opulent vehicle, has experienced some reliability concerns, including engine problems, transmission issues, suspension failures, electrical faults, and outdated navigation systems. The severity and cost of repairs can be significant, so it's essential to factor in these potential expenses when considering purchasing or maintaining this luxury sedan.
