# 2017 Bentley Mulsanne Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Bentley Mulsanne stands as an ultra-luxury flagship sedan, offering supreme hand-crafted comfort and immense power from its twin-turbocharged V8 engine. For prospective used-car buyers, vehicles in this tier demand meticulous pre-purchase inspections and documented service histories due to the extreme complexity of their electronics and mechanical systems. While widespread mechanical failures are rare given low production volumes and pampered use, individual component repairs can carry substantial costs.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This venerable 6.75-liter V8 engine produces approximately 505 horsepower and 752 lb-ft of torque (or up to 530 hp in Speed trims), delivering effortless, authoritative acceleration. It is generally robust when properly maintained, but regular servicing is vital to avoid expensive engine bay complications.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

Paired with a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission, power is sent seamlessly to the rear wheels. The ZF-sourced unit is widely regarded as durable and refined, though fluid changes are essential to maintain its seamless operation over high mileage.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V033000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Mulsanne&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the rear seat belt anchor may detach from the vehicle. As such, these vehicles fail to comply to Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." In the event of a crash, if the rear seat belt buckle detaches, the seat occupant would have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will improve the strength of the rear seat belt mounting by adding an additional reinforcement panel to the body structure, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Shopping for a used 2017 Bentley Mulsanne requires focusing heavily on professional inspection reports and verifying maintenance records rather than relying on common failure trends. Because factory complaint data is extremely sparse for this model year, buyers should instead budget carefully for routine luxury upkeep, premium insurance, and specialized labor rates to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
