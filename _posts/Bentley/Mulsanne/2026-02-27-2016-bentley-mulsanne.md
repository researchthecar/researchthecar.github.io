# 2016 Bentley Mulsanne Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Bentley Mulsanne represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury motoring, offering hand-built craftsmanship and effortless performance. For prospective used-car buyers, researching a flagship luxury sedan of this caliber requires careful attention to its complex engineering and high maintenance overhead. While major powertrain mechanical failures are rare, keeping a vehicle of this sophistication operating at its best demands meticulous adherence to service schedules.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This legendary 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 505 horsepower and a massive 752 lb-ft of torque. It provides effortlessly smooth, authoritative acceleration suited for an ultra-luxury sedan, though routine servicing costs are exceptionally high.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes that complement the effortless power delivery of the V8 engine. It is generally robust and well-matched to the vehicle's grand touring character.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V033000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Mulsanne&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the rear seat belt anchor may detach from the vehicle. As such, these vehicles fail to comply to Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." In the event of a crash, if the rear seat belt buckle detaches, the seat occupant would have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will improve the strength of the rear seat belt mounting by adding an additional reinforcement panel to the body structure, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Bentley Mulsanne remains an extraordinary statement of prestige and engineering. Buyers exploring the used market should prioritize vehicles with comprehensive, documented service histories. Independent pre-purchase inspections by marque specialists are strongly advised to verify the condition of the complex mechanical and electronic systems before finalizing a purchase.
