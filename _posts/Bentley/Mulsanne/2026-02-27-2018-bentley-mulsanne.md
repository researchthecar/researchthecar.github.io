# 2018 Bentley Mulsanne Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Bentley Mulsanne represents the pinnacle of British ultra-luxury motoring, offering hand-built craftsmanship and effortless performance. For prospective used-car buyers, ultra-luxury flagships of this caliber require careful inspection, as specialized maintenance and complex systems demand significant upkeep. While major complaint trends are minimal, keeping a close eye on service history is essential when evaluating these sophisticated vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 6.75L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This legendary 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 505 horsepower and 752 lb-ft of torque in standard form, delivering immense and silent thrust. It is renowned for its robust mechanical build, though routine servicing requires specialist expertise and can be exceptionally costly.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission routes power seamlessly to the rear wheels, perfectly complementing the engine's vast torque reserve. It generally operates reliably when properly maintained, though fluid changes should be kept strictly on schedule.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V632000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Mulsanne&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** When reverse gear has been selected, the rearview camera display may be obstructed or may not be fully visible within two seconds. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rearview Mirrors." If the full camera view is obstructed or delayed and the driver does not check their.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the software in the infotainment system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V033000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Mulsanne&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the rear seat belt anchor may detach from the vehicle. As such, these vehicles fail to comply to Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." In the event of a crash, if the rear seat belt buckle detaches, the seat occupant would have an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will improve the strength of the rear seat belt mounting by adding an additional reinforcement panel to the body structure, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Bentley Mulsanne is an extraordinary machine that prioritizes peerless luxury and effortless power above all else. Used-car buyers should prioritize examples with comprehensive, documented service records and perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to ensure all mechanical and electronic systems are in top condition.
