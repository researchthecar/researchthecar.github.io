**Introduction**

The 2002 Bentley Azure is a luxury convertible that combines opulence with performance. It is powered by a 6.75-liter V8 engine that produces 414 horsepower and 435 lb-ft of torque. The Azure is available with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

Drivers praise the Azure for its powerful engine, luxurious interior, and classic styling. However, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

* **6.75-liter V8**

    * **Intake manifold gasket failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing chain tensioner failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-speed automatic**

    * **Transmission slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, hesitation when accelerating
        * Solution: Repair or replace transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension struts**
    * **Leaking struts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Wobbling, uneven tire wear
        * Solution: Replace leaking struts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension springs**
    * **Broken springs**
        * Description/Symptoms: Sagging rear end, rough ride
        * Solution: Replace rear suspension springs
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery**
    * **Premature battery failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, loss of electrical power
        * Solution: Replace battery
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator**
    * **Alternator failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, battery discharge
        * Solution: Replace alternator
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible top**
    * **Convertible top hydraulics failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty raising or lowering convertible top
        * Solution: Repair or replace convertible top hydraulics
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate control**
    * **Climate control failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning AC, heater, or ventilation
        * Solution: Repair or replace climate control unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard**
    * **Cracked dashboard**
        * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or warping on dashboard surface
        * Solution: Replace dashboard
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2002 Bentley Azure is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but it is not immune to issues. The most common problems reported include engine gasket failures, timing chain tensioner failures, transmission slipping, and suspension component failures. Electrical and interior issues are also not uncommon. The cost of repairs can be significant, ranging from hundreds to thousands of dollars. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing an Azure.
