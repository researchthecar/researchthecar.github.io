**Introduction**
The 2000 Bentley Azure is a luxury convertible that was produced from 1999 to 2002. It was based on the Continental R and was powered by a 6.75-liter V8 engine. The Azure was available with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

People who like the Bentley Azure appreciate its powerful engine, luxurious interior, and classic styling. However, some people have reported problems with the vehicle's reliability and high cost of maintenance.

**Engine**
* **6.75-liter V8 Engine**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000-$3000
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Oil Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine may develop oil leaks from the valve covers, oil pan, or rear main seal.
        * Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Slipping Gears**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip gears, causing the vehicle to lose power or jerk.
        * Solution: Replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5000-$7000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
* **Front Suspension Struts**
        * Description/Symptoms: The front suspension struts can wear out, causing the vehicle to ride rough or handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replace the front suspension struts.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000-$1500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Shock Absorbers**
        * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension shock absorbers can wear out, causing the vehicle to ride rough or handle poorly.
        * Solution: Replace the rear suspension shock absorbers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000-$1500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain**
        * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to start or run poorly.
        * Solution: Test the battery and replace it if necessary. Also, check for any electrical parasitic drains such as malfunctioning interior lights.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200-$500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Shorts**
        * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle may experience electrical shorts, causing various electrical components to malfunction.
        * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty electrical components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$2000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Headlight Condensation**
        * Description/Symptoms: The headlights may develop condensation inside the lenses.
        * Solution: Replace the headlights or repair the seals.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Navigation System Malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: The navigation system may malfunction, causing it to provide inaccurate directions or display errors.
        * Solution: Update the navigation system software or replace the navigation unit.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500-$1500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2000 Bentley Azure is a luxurious and powerful vehicle, but it is not without its problems. Some of the most common issues reported by owners include timing chain tensioner failure, oil leaks, slipping gears, and electrical shorts. The cost to fix these problems can be significant, so it is important to factor in the cost of maintenance before purchasing a used Azure.
