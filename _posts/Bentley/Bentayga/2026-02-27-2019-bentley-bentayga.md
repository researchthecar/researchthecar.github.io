# 2019 Bentley Bentayga Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a pre-owned 2019 Bentley Bentayga means stepping into a world of ultra-luxury performance, bespoke craftsmanship, and powerful engine options ranging from a refined V8 to a commanding W12. Because these ultra-luxury SUVs involve complex engineering and high-end materials, buyers should approach the market with careful inspection habits. While formal owner complaints remain minimal for this model year, maintaining a vehicle of this caliber requires diligent service history checks and awareness of potential out-of-warranty repair costs.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and a throaty exhaust note. It balances daily drivability with strong performance, though prospective buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals to ensure longevity.

**Fuel Odor**
- **Symptoms:** An abnormal fuel smell may be detected inside the cabin while parked or attempting to start the vehicle, sometimes without any dashboard warning lights illuminating.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel lines, seals, and connections for any leaks or vapor escapes.

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged W12

The flagship 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged W12 engine generates a massive 600 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque for effortless, ultra-smooth power delivery. It provides supreme refinement and immense capability, though its complex layout demands meticulous professional servicing.

### 3.0L V6 Plug-In Hybrid

This hybrid powertrain combines a 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 with an electric motor to deliver efficiency alongside traditional luxury performance. It offers a smooth electric-only driving experience around town, though buyers should verify battery health and charging system functionality.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers seamless gear changes that match the effortless power delivery of both the V8 and W12 engines. It is generally robust and well-regarded for reliability, provided the fluid has been inspected and maintained according to schedule.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V154000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The third-row upper seat belt attachment may not stay connected to the vehicle in the event of a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." If the seat belt is not properly secured in the event of a crash, the occupant will not be properly.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will replace the upper seat belt attachment bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V321000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel line and quick connector, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V092000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the side curtain air bags may not properly inflate. An air bag that does not properly inflate in the event of a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will replace the curtain air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V592000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the flange and fuel supply module wiring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
