# 2017 Bentley Bentayga Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Bentley Bentayga is an ultra-luxury performance SUV offering immense power and opulence. While early owner feedback includes a few scattered mechanical and electronic concerns, prospective buyers should approach this complex vehicle with an understanding of potentially high maintenance and diagnostic costs typical of ultra-luxury European brands.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L W12 Twin-Turbocharged

The 6.0-liter W12 twin-turbocharged engine generates approximately 600 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering immense acceleration for this luxury SUV. It is a highly sophisticated powerplant requiring meticulous maintenance, and any underlying mechanical or fuel system issues can involve costly specialist labor.

**Fuel Line Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice an abnormally strong fuel odor while operating the vehicle due to a fractured fuel line.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a qualified technician and replace the damaged fuel line immediately.

**Auto Start-Stop Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may stall unexpectedly during low-speed maneuvers or when coming out of a parking space due to a malfunctioning auto start-stop system.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership technician to inspect the powertrain control module and restart software parameters.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission paired with standard all-wheel drive, this unit manages the massive power output of the Bentayga. While no major widespread recurring transmission mechanical failures dominate owner reports, proper fluid maintenance is critical for long-term longevity.

## Steering

### Lane Assist

Issues reported in this category focus on advanced driver-assistance system behavior rather than traditional hydraulic or mechanical steering failures.

**Unexpected Steering Maneuvers**
- **Symptoms:** The lane assist system may occasionally initiate unintended or overly aggressive steering corrections toward oncoming traffic.
- **Solution:** Have the driver assistance sensors, calibration, and software checked by a certified Bentley technician.

## Exterior

### Windshield Defrosting and Ventilation

Exterior visibility components generally function well, though specific climate control design traits can occasionally impact front window clarity.

**Windshield Fogging**
- **Symptoms:** The front windshield may persistently steam up in humid weather conditions, severely reducing forward visibility.
- **Solution:** Check the cabin air filter operation, climate control routing, and defroster system functionality.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V816000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** During the assembly process, the affected vehicles may not have had the seat mechanism and instrument panel joints properly tightened. If the seat mechanism and instrument panel joints were not tightened, they can loosen, increasing the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and tighten the seat mechanism and instrument panel joints, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V592000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the flange and fuel supply module wiring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Bentley Bentayga provides unmatched road presence and performance, but ownership demands significant financial preparation for routine upkeep and potential electronic or fuel system diagnostics. Buyers should secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an independent specialist familiar with high-end Volkswagen Group platforms before finalizing a purchase.
