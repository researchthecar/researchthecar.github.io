# 2018 Bentley Bentayga Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Bentley Bentayga is a high-performance luxury SUV offering a blend of immense power and upscale craftsmanship. While overall complaint volume in public records is quite low, buyers should still approach a pre-owned luxury vehicle of this complexity with careful inspection routines.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L W12 Twin-Turbo

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged W12 engine produces roughly 600 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless acceleration. It is a complex, high-output powerplant that requires meticulous adherence to maintenance schedules.

### 4.0L V8 Twin-Turbo

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine offers around 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, providing a slightly more agile driving dynamic. This option balances impressive luxury performance with strong daily drivability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, seamless gear changes suited for all-wheel-drive luxury cruising. Routine fluid service is essential to maintain long-term reliability under high torque loads.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking systems provide strong stopping power for this heavy luxury SUV, though consumable components can wear quickly.

**Premature Brake Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Owners may experience unexpectedly rapid wear on front and rear brake pads without prior warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pad thickness and rotor condition regularly during routine service intervals and replace components as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

- **Issue:** When reverse gear has been selected, the rearview camera display may be obstructed or may not be fully visible within two seconds. - **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the software in the infotainment system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V154000: Seat Belts:Rear/Other](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The third-row upper seat belt attachment may not stay connected to the vehicle in the event of a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 210, "Seat Belt Assembly Anchorages." If the seat belt is not properly secured in the event of a crash, the occupant will not be properly.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will replace the upper seat belt attachment bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V092000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the side curtain air bags may not properly inflate. An air bag that does not properly inflate in the event of a crash can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will replace the curtain air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V592000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the flange and fuel supply module wiring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Bentley Bentayga remains an exclusive and powerful choice for used luxury SUV buyers, though maintenance and consumable costs are exceptionally high. Prospective owners should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to verify the health of mechanical and braking systems.
