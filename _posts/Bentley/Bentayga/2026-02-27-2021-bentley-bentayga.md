# 2021 Bentley Bentayga Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Bentley Bentayga delivers ultra-luxury and commanding performance, blending handcrafted comfort with formidable powertrains. As a high-end luxury vehicle, potential buyers should carefully weigh the prestige and refinement against complex mechanical systems and potentially expensive maintenance when purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration with refined daily drivability. This powerplant is generally robust, though complex luxury engineering demands meticulous maintenance adherence.

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged W12

The flagship 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged W12 engine generates around 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque for effortless, ultra-smooth high-speed cruising. It provides immense power delivery, though its intricate design requires specialized servicing.

### 3.0L V6 Plug-In Hybrid

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 plug-in hybrid option combines a gasoline engine with an electric motor to deliver around 443 total system horsepower. It offers efficient short-distance electric driving while maintaining traditional Bentley luxury.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the permanent all-wheel-drive system across all engine trims. It delivers seamless gear changes that complement the vehicle's luxurious ride quality.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V605000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicles Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front passenger seat belt assemblies, and if necessary, replace the seat belts free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V975000: Equipment:Other:Owners/Service/Other Manual](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The owner's manual incorrectly states that the air bag system will not deploy when the "Passenger Airbag On" symbol is illuminated. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." The incorrect information may change how occupants position themselves in relation.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 21V276000: Suspension:Rear:Axle:Non-Powered Axle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A broken lock nut may misalign the rear axle, causing a loss of control and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify owners, and dealers will replace the lock nuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V647000: Electronic Stability Control (Esc):Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Incorrect software for the chassis control module may cause the Electronic Stability Control (ESC) system to malfunction. A malfunctioning ESC system can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the chassis control module with the correct software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V257000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** An unlocked seat rail could move in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury to occupants.
- **What to do:** Dealers will check and reset the 2nd row seat rail positions to ensure proper engagement of the seat rails, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V743000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera screen that displays a blank image reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V592000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the flange and fuel supply module wiring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Bentley Bentayga remains an exceptionally capable and lavish ultra-luxury SUV for used car buyers seeking ultimate comfort and prestige. Prospective owners should ensure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to verify the condition of its sophisticated powertrain and onboard electronics.
