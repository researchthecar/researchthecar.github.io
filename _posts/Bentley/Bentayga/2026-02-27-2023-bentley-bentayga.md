# 2023 Bentley Bentayga Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Bentley Bentayga represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury performance, combining hand-crafted interior appointments with formidable powertrain choices. As a high-end luxury vehicle, prospective buyers should focus on proper service history and the complexity of its advanced electronics and powertrain options. Evaluating a pre-owned model requires careful inspection of its mechanical and technical systems to ensure everything operates to factory standards.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers approximately 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless acceleration. It offers a balance of spirited performance and refined cruising dynamics suited for daily driving.

### 3.0L V6 Plug-In Hybrid

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 plug-in hybrid powertrain pairs electric assistance with traditional combustion for efficiency and smooth power delivery. It is an appealing option for buyers seeking reduced emissions without sacrificing luxury features.

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged W12

The flagship 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged W12 engine generates an immense 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque for effortless, ultra-smooth thrust. This iconic powerplant offers supreme refinement but commands higher maintenance attention.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission routes power seamlessly to the permanent all-wheel-drive system across all engine variants. It is designed to provide instantaneous gear changes while maintaining utmost cabin serenity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V386000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An unlocked seat rail can move in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury to occupants.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V592000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the flange and fuel supply module wiring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Bentley Bentayga remains an exclusive and capable ultra-luxury SUV with robust powertrain choices. While available owner feedback data shows minimal recurring mechanical complaints, buyers should still conduct a thorough pre-purchase inspection emphasizing electronics and drivetrain health.
