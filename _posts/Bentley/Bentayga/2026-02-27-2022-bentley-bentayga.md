# 2022 Bentley Bentayga Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Bentley Bentayga represents the pinnacle of ultra-luxury performance and craftsmanship, offering an array of robust engine choices ranging from turbocharged V8 and W12 powerplants to efficient hybrid configurations. As an ultra-luxury vehicle, buyers shopping on the used market should expect sophisticated engineering that requires meticulous maintenance and specialized servicing. While current owner reporting shows a very clean slate with minimal recurring mechanical complaints, prospective buyers should always verify service histories and perform thorough pre-purchase inspections given the complex electronic and powertrain architectures typical of modern ultra-luxury SUVs.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk performance and a sporty exhaust note. It is generally regarded as a strong and responsive powerplant for daily driving.

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged W12

The massive 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged W12 engine delivers an immense 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque for effortless, ultra-smooth acceleration. While exceptionally powerful, engine bay packaging is tight, which can increase labor time and costs for routine maintenance.

### 3.0L V6 Plug-In Hybrid

The 3.0-liter turbocharged V6 plug-in hybrid option combines traditional combustion power with an electric motor for enhanced efficiency and silent low-speed cruising. It balances ultra-luxury amenities with modern electrified powertrain technology.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission is paired across the Bentayga lineup with permanent all-wheel drive. It delivers seamless gear changes that complement the vehicle's refined ride quality.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V975000: Equipment:Other:Owners/Service/Other Manual](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The owner's manual incorrectly states that the air bag system will not deploy when the "Passenger Airbag On" symbol is illuminated. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." The incorrect information may change how occupants position themselves in relation.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V257000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An unlocked seat rail could move in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury to occupants.
- **What to do:** Dealers will check and reset the 2nd row seat rail positions to ensure proper engagement of the seat rails, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V743000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera screen that displays a blank image reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the infotainment unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V386000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An unlocked seat rail can move in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury to occupants.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 23V592000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Bentayga&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the flange and fuel supply module wiring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
