**Introduction:**

The 2004 Bentley Arnage is a stately luxury sedan powered by a potent 6.75-liter V8 engine mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission. It boasts an impressive 400 horsepower and 610 lb-ft of torque. The Arnage is renowned for its opulent cabin, refined ride, and classic British styling. However, like any vehicle, it has its share of potential issues.

**Engine**

**6.75L V8**

- **Oil Leaks:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Visible oil leaks from gaskets, seals, or oil pan.
    - Solution: Replace faulty gaskets, seals, or oil pan.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Intake Manifold Issues:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, reduced power, or stalling.
    - Solution: Replace intake manifold.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

- **Shifting Problems:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or lurching.
    - Solution: Repair or replace transmission components as needed.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

- **Air Suspension Leaks:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Sagging suspension, uneven ride height, or warning lights.
    - Solution: Repair or replace air suspension components (e.g., air bags, compressor).
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Alternator Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, or warning lights.
    - Solution: Replace alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Battery Drain:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead quickly, especially after sitting for a period.
    - Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace source of battery drain (e.g., faulty components, parasitic draw).
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Fog Light Malfunctions:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Fog lights do not illuminate or turn on erratically.
    - Solution: Replace faulty fog lights or wiring.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Seat Control Issues:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Seat controls fail to adjust seats or memories are lost.
    - Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical faults, or replace seat control modules.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **HVAC System Malfunctions:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Climate control not working properly, temperature fluctuations, or strange noises.
    - Solution: Repair or replace HVAC components (e.g., blower motor, compressor, actuators).
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2004 Bentley Arnage is a luxury vehicle known for its comfort and refinement, but it can be susceptible to some potential issues. Many of the problems are related to the engine or transmission, which can be expensive to repair. Additionally, there are some common electrical and suspension issues that can arise. Overall, the Arnage requires regular maintenance and attention to its complex systems to ensure optimal performance and reliability.
