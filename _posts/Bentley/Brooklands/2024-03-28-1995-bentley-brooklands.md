**Introduction**

The 1995 Bentley Brooklands is a luxury grand tourer that was produced by Bentley Motors Limited. It is powered by a 6.75-liter turbocharged V8 engine that produces 300 horsepower and 488 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Brooklands was praised for its luxurious interior, powerful engine, and timeless design. However, it was also criticized for its high price and lack of modern amenities.

**Engine**

* **Turbocharger failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, engine overheating, black smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 99V098000
* **Intake manifold gasket failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leak, engine overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing belt failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine failure, no start
    * Solution: Replace timing belt
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Automatic transmission failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No power to wheels, erratic shifting, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Air suspension failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Suspension sags, uneven ride height, loss of control
    * Solution: Repair or replace air suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, electrical components malfunction
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: No power to electrical components, battery dies while driving
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights do not illuminate, reduced visibility at night
    * Solution: Replace headlight bulbs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Instrument cluster failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gauges do not work, incorrect readings
    * Solution: Repair or replace instrument cluster
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1995 Bentley Brooklands is a classic luxury grand tourer that is known for its powerful engine, luxurious interior, and timeless design. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that can arise with this vehicle. Many of the issues are related to the engine and transmission, which can be expensive to repair. Additionally, the electrical system and suspension can also experience problems. Overall, the severity of the issues and the cost of repairs can vary depending on the specific issue and the condition of the vehicle.
