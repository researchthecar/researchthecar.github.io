## Suspension

### Adaptive Air Suspension
**Air Spring Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may sag on one corner, unusual noises from suspension, warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking air spring or associated seals.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to raise or lower the vehicle, constant running of the compressor, suspension warning lights.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the air suspension compressor.
- **Cost:** $700 - $1200


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Screen freezing, unresponsive touch controls, Bluetooth connectivity issues, navigation errors.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or potential module replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $800


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dying prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory) and repair or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Bentley Continental GT demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components (W12 and V8 engines, 8-speed automatic transmission). The primary areas where owners might encounter issues are related to the sophisticated adaptive air suspension, with potential for leaks or compressor problems, and the advanced electrical systems, which can sometimes exhibit infotainment glitches or battery drain issues. These are not uncommon for high-end vehicles with complex technology. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs can mitigate most potential problems, ensuring the continued enjoyment of this exceptional grand tourer.
