# 2018 Bentley Continental Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a luxury grand tourer like the 2018 Bentley Continental means weighing effortless performance against exotic maintenance realities. While formal owner complaints are sparse for this model year, buyers should approach ultra-luxury pre-owned purchases with careful inspections of hand-built assemblies and complex onboard electronics.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 542 horsepower and 568 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and refined cruising. It provides a slightly more agile front-end feel compared to the heavier W12 powerplant while maintaining high standards of reliability for the brand.

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged W12

The flagship 6.0L twin-turbocharged W12 generates roughly 626 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque for immense, effortless power delivery. This complex engine is durable when properly serviced, but routine maintenance and potential component access can involve premium labor costs.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid, seamless gear changes suited to both relaxed touring and aggressive driving. It pairs smoothly with both engine options, though prospective buyers should verify crisp engagement during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V863000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Hose, Piping, And Connections](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Continental&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A power steering fluid leak may cause a loss of vehicle control, or a hazard to following traffic, increasing the risk of a crash. In addition, the fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify the owners, and dealers will replace the PAS cooler pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Bentley Continental remains a pinnacle of grand touring luxury, backed by robust powertrain options and minimal reported owner grievances. Prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist to ensure all electronic systems, auxiliary components, and mechanical assemblies are in top condition before purchase.
