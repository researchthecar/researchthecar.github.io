# 2017 Bentley Continental Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Bentley Continental represents an ultra-luxury grand tourer that pairs hand-crafted British craftsmanship with immense performance capabilities. As an exotic pre-owned purchase, it delivers a striking presence and effortless long-distance cruising comfort. However, buyers should remember that maintenance, specialized labor, and replacement parts reflect its original six-figure price tag.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 521 to 582 horsepower depending on the tune, offering rapid acceleration and relatively brisk throttle response. This engine is generally robust, though routine servicing requires specialized knowledge and premium fluids.

### 6.0L Twin-Turbocharged W12

The 6.0-liter twin-turbocharged W12 outputs between 582 and 633 horsepower, delivering turbine-like power delivery and effortless high-speed cruising. While mechanically stout, engine bay packaging is extremely tight, meaning minor component failures can lead to intensive labor hours.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission channels power seamlessly to the all-wheel-drive system with rapid and refined shifts. It is widely regarded as a highly durable unit across various luxury applications, provided fluid changes are maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V863000: Steering:Hydraulic Power Assist:Hose, Piping, And Connections](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Bentley&model=Continental&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A power steering fluid leak may cause a loss of vehicle control, or a hazard to following traffic, increasing the risk of a crash. In addition, the fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Bentley will notify the owners, and dealers will replace the PAS cooler pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Bentley Continental is a masterpiece of luxury and performance, built to high engineering standards. While major mechanical failures are uncommon, prospective buyers must budget appropriately for comprehensive pre-purchase inspections and the higher-than-average upkeep costs associated with ultra-luxury ownership.
