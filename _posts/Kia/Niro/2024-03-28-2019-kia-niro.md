**Introduction to the 2019 Kia Niro**

The 2019 Kia Niro is a compact SUV that offers a hybrid and plug-in hybrid option. The hybrid model is powered by a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine and an electric motor, producing a combined 139 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque. The plug-in hybrid model uses the same engine and electric motor but has a larger battery pack, allowing for up to 26 miles of all-electric driving. It also produces more power, with a combined output of 179 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque.

The Niro is praised for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, and other components.

**Engine**

**1.6L Four-Cylinder Engine (Hybrid and Plug-In Hybrid)**

* **Knocking or rattling noise from the engine**
    * Description/Symptoms: A knocking or rattling noise may be heard from the engine, especially during acceleration.
    * Solution: The issue may be caused by a faulty engine bearing or piston. The engine may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Excessive oil consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent topping up.
    * Solution: The issue may be caused by worn piston rings or a faulty valve stem seal. The engine may need to be rebuilt or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**CVT (All Models)**

* **Slipping or hesitation during shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip or hesitate during gear changes, especially when accelerating or decelerating.
    * Solution: The issue may be caused by a faulty CVT fluid filter or a problem with the transmission control module. The fluid may need to be changed or the module may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery may drain prematurely, especially if accessories such as headlights or air conditioning are left on.
    * Solution: The issue may be caused by a faulty battery, alternator, or electrical component. The battery or alternator may need to be replaced or the electrical system may need to be diagnosed and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Electrical system failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's electrical system may fail, causing various components to malfunction, including the engine, lights, and infotainment system.
    * Solution: The issue may be caused by a faulty electrical component or a loose connection. The electrical system may need to be diagnosed and repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Exterior**

* **Liftgate latch failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The liftgate may not open or close properly due to a faulty latch.
    * Solution: The liftgate latch may need to be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall ID 21V719000

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience glitches, including freezing, blackouts, or navigation errors.
    * Solution: The infotainment system may need to be updated or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
* **Interior trim rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: The interior trim may rattle or squeak due to loose components or poor assembly.
    * Solution: The loose components may need to be tightened or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500

**Summary**

The 2019 Kia Niro is a reliable and fuel-efficient SUV, but it has experienced some issues, including engine knocking, transmission slipping, electrical system failures, and liftgate latch problems. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences and others requiring costly repairs. Before purchasing a Niro, it's important to be aware of these potential problems and factor them into your decision.
