# 2022 Kia Niro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Kia Niro, buyers will find a versatile and highly efficient compact crossover that appeals to commuters and fuel-conscious drivers alike. While the vehicle generally offers solid day-to-day utility, prospective owners should review owner-reported issues involving the electrical system, hybrid powertrain components, and lighting fixtures.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Kappa GDI 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

The 1.6-liter direct-injection four-cylinder engine pairs with an electric motor to produce around 139 horsepower in hybrid configurations. It provides exceptional fuel economy for daily driving, though owners should monitor cooling and emissions system sensors.

**Catalytic Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle triggers a persistent check engine light with diagnostic trouble code P0420, indicating catalyst system efficiency below threshold due to internal disintegration.
- **Solution:** Inspect internal catalyst condition and replace the catalytic converter assembly if structural failure is confirmed.

**Inverter Coolant Crystallization**
- **Symptoms:** An intermittent warning message pops up on the dashboard screen instructing the driver to "Refill Inverter Coolant."
- **Solution:** Flush and service the cooling system to remove crystallized coolant that interferes with system sensors.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels while maximizing fuel efficiency. It operates reliably under normal conditions, provided regular maintenance schedules are observed.

## Electrical

### 12V and Lithium-Ion Battery System

The onboard electrical architecture supports complex hybrid systems and safety tech, but owners should watch for parasitic drains and battery health during colder months.

**Battery Drain and Swelling**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle fails to start or unlock after sitting overnight, particularly in colder temperatures, sometimes accompanied by a swollen lithium-ion battery.
- **Solution:** Test the electrical draw and replace the affected 12V or hybrid battery pack if internal swelling or cell failure is detected.

## Exterior

### Service Brakes and Lighting

The braking system provides dependable stopping power, though electrical connection faults can occasionally trigger false warning lights or intermittent exterior lighting failures.

**Brake Light Warning and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience recurring dashboard warnings for brake lights even after replacing the physical bulbs multiple times, often caused by loose wiring harnesses.
- **Solution:** Inspect the rear bulb sockets and wiring harnesses for loose connections or intermittent power delivery, and repair or secure faulty wiring.

### Sunroof and Trim

Exterior panels and trim are generally well-assembled, though isolated incidents involving glass stress and interior trim glare have been reported.

**Sunroof Glass Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass can experience unexpected shattering or loud spontaneous stress noises while parked or stored.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for stress cracks and replace the glass panel if structural failure occurs.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V534000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Internal:Clutches/Bands:Actuators/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Fluid may leak into the printed circuit board inside the hydraulic clutch actuator, which can result in an engine compartment fire. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the hydraulic clutch actuator as necessary and install a new fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Niro presents as an efficient and practical used hybrid crossover option, backed by strong fuel economy and everyday utility. While most mechanical systems prove dependable, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's electrical health, battery condition, cooling sensors, and brake light wiring prior to purchase.
