# 2019 Kia Niro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Kia Niro offers buyers an efficient hybrid crossover option designed for strong fuel economy and daily practicality. While it generally serves as a sensible commuter vehicle, used car shoppers should review its powertrain variations and monitor for any reported owner complaints during test drives.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L GDI 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

This 1.6-liter direct-injection four-cylinder engine pairs with an electric motor to produce a combined 139 horsepower. It is known for delivering excellent fuel efficiency in everyday driving conditions.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers direct power transfer and crisp gear shifts. Buyers should test drive the vehicle to ensure smooth operation across low-speed stop-and-go conditions.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V534000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Internal:Clutches/Bands:Actuators/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Fluid may leak into the printed circuit board inside the hydraulic clutch actuator, which can result in an engine compartment fire. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the hydraulic clutch actuator as necessary and install a new fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Kia Niro remains an attractive choice for fuel-conscious used car buyers thanks to its hybrid efficiency and practical layout. Thoroughly inspect the vehicle's maintenance history and verify proper operation of all systems before finalizing a purchase.
