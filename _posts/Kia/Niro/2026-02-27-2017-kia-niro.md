# 2017 Kia Niro Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Kia Niro introduces a dedicated hybrid platform aimed at delivering high fuel efficiency in a practical crossover package. While it offers impressive economy and a comfortable ride, used car buyers should evaluate both the conventional hybrid powertrain and common owner-reported gripes before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L GDI 4-Cylinder Hybrid Engine

This 1.6-liter direct-injection four-cylinder engine is paired with an electric motor to produce a combined 139 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque. It is generally praised for exceptional fuel economy, though buyers should verify regular maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

A 6-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers power to the front wheels, offering a more engaging driving feel than traditional CVTs found in competing hybrids. Buyers should test drive the vehicle to ensure smooth shifting behavior across all gears.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V666000: Hybrid Propulsion System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The increased electrical resistance can overheat the rear seat which is above the PRA, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the Power Relay Assembly (PRA) for damage. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V257000: Hybrid Propulsion System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the HCA caps for leaked oil and replace the HCA assembly if needed, or replace the HCA cap with additional sealant. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V836000: Hybrid Propulsion System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Overheated rear seats increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the PRA and replace either the PRA or the Main Relay, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V534000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Internal:Clutches/Bands:Actuators/Solenoids](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Fluid may leak into the printed circuit board inside the hydraulic clutch actuator, which can result in an engine compartment fire. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the hydraulic clutch actuator as necessary and install a new fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Kia Niro is an appealing choice for shoppers prioritizing fuel economy and daily usability. A thorough pre-purchase inspection and test drive of the hybrid system and transmission will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
