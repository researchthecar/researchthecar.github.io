**Introduction to the 2021 Kia Niro**

The 2021 Kia Niro is a compact crossover SUV available in three powertrain options: a 1.6L 4-cylinder gas engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission, a 1.6L 4-cylinder turbocharged gas engine with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission, and a 1.6L 4-cylinder gas-electric hybrid with a 6-speed dual-clutch transmission. The gas engine produces 132 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque, while the turbocharged engine produces 188 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque. The Niro is praised for its fuel efficiency, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with certain components of the vehicle.

**Engine**

**1.6L 4-cylinder Gas Engine**

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
Description/Symptoms: The engine consumes an excessive amount of oil, leading to frequent top-ups and potential engine damage.
Solution: Replacing piston rings and valve seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**1.6L 4-cylinder Turbocharged Gas Engine**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic Transmission**

**Slipping Gears**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission fails to engage gears or slips out of gear while driving.
Solution: Replacing the transmission fluid or rebuilding the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**7-speed Dual-clutch Transmission**

**Clunky Shifting**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission shifts gears harshly or with a clunking noise.
Solution: Replacing the transmission fluid or updating the transmission software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
Recalls: None Found

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Loose Seat Cushions**
Description/Symptoms: The seat cushions become loose and shift around while driving.
Solution: Tightening the bolts that hold the cushions in place.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Kia Niro has generally positive reviews from owners. However, a few reported issues can be costly to repair. The excessive oil consumption issue with the 1.6L gas engine is the most severe, potentially leading to engine damage if not addressed promptly. Other issues, such as the battery failure and seat cushion issue, are less severe but can still be inconvenient and costly to fix.
