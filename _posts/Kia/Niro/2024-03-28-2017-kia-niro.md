**Introduction to the 2017 Kia Niro**

The 2017 Kia Niro is a compact crossover SUV that offers a unique blend of fuel efficiency and practicality. It features a standard 139-horsepower, 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. Alternatively, a 181-horsepower, 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine is available, paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission. The Niro has received praise for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive fuel economy. However, like any vehicle, it has not been without some reported issues.

**Engine**

* **1.6L Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **Hesitation on Acceleration**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine may hesitate or lag when accelerating from a stop or at low speeds.
        * Solution: Software update or throttle body cleaning
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume an excessive amount of oil, requiring frequent top-offs.
        * Solution: Piston ring replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **1.6L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder Engine**
    * **No reported issues**

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic**
    * **Rough Shifting**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift gears roughly or jerkily.
        * Solution: Transmission fluid change or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch**
    * **Clutch Chatter**
        * Description/Symptoms: The clutch may make a chattering noise when engaging or disengaging gears.
        * Solution: Clutch replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No reported issues**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to fail to start.
    * Solution: Electrical system inspection and repair
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation may form inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Headlight replacement or resealing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Crashing**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may occasionally crash or freeze.
    * Solution: Software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 (covered under warranty)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Kia Niro is generally a reliable and well-received vehicle. However, some owners have reported issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem. It is important to note that not all vehicles will experience these issues, and many owners are satisfied with their Niro's performance.
