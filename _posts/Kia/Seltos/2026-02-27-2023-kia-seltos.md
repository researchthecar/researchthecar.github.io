### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Kia Seltos presents itself as a reliable option in the subcompact SUV market. Major mechanical failures are not commonly reported for its engine or transmission options (2.0L naturally aspirated, 1.6L turbocharged, CVT, and 7-speed DCT). The suspension and exterior components also appear robust. The most frequently noted, albeit minor, issues tend to be related to the infotainment system, such as occasional software glitches or connectivity problems, which are often resolvable with software updates. Overall, the Seltos offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal widespread technical concerns, making it a practical choice for daily commuting and general use.
