# 2023 Kia Seltos Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Kia Seltos is a stylish and feature-packed compact SUV that appeals to daily commuters and small families alike. While it offers a comfortable ride and modern technology, prospective used-car buyers should note a handful of recurring owner complaints, particularly regarding electrical anomalies and intermittent engine or transmission behavior.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

This naturally aspirated 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine produces 146 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting with respectable fuel economy.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers a livelier 175 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque for quicker acceleration, though used buyers should monitor fluid levels closely.

**Engine Stalling and Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalls while driving, illuminated dashboard warning lights, and high rates of oil consumption requiring frequent top-offs between oil changes.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect engine compression, check for diagnostic trouble codes, and perform software updates or necessary mechanical repairs.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Paired with the base engine, this continuously variable transmission prioritizes fuel efficiency for everyday driving.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

Linked to the turbocharged engine, this 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers quick shifts but can exhibit low-speed operational quirks.

**Erratic Shifting and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced hesitation, jerking, or unusual shifting behavior when moving between gears or accelerating from a stop.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition, ensure control modules have the latest software calibrations, and inspect clutch actuation components.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Instrument Cluster

The electrical architecture supports modern cabin tech, but sporadic glitching in displays and gauges has been noted by some owners.

**Display Glitches and Speedometer Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The touchscreen interface may occasionally display gibberish, become unresponsive, or the analog speedometer and gauges may drop to zero unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Perform a system reset, check vehicle fuses, or visit a dealership to update or replace faulty instrument cluster modules.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Dashboard Glare

Exterior glass and trim are generally robust, though cabin design can produce distracting reflections under direct sunlight.

**Windshield Glare and Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note a significant, hazardous reflection of the dashboard plastics onto the front windshield during sunny conditions, alongside occasional spontaneous windshield cracks.
- **Solution:** Use a dark dash mat to cut reflections or replace damaged glass through professional auto glass services.

## Safety

### Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist

Advanced safety features enhance peace of mind, though sensor calibration must be maintained to prevent false triggers.

**False Activation**
- **Symptoms:** Sensors and camera systems may trigger sudden, unintended emergency braking or flash warning lights while driving under normal conditions.
- **Solution:** Ensure camera and radar lenses are clean, and have a dealer inspect calibration settings for the collision avoidance systems.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V531000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Fluid/Lubricant:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Seltos&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An electric oil pump assembly that overheats increases the risk of a vehicle fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the electric oil pump controller, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V099000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Seltos&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Engine damage can cause a vehicle stall or fire, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Kia Seltos remains an appealing pre-owned subcompact SUV thanks to its bold styling, practical interior space, and flexible powertrain choices. While most examples provide dependable daily service, buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for potential electrical glitches, sensor behavior, and fluid consumption history before purchasing.
