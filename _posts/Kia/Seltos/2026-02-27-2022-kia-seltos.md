# 2022 Kia Seltos Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Seltos is a stylish and versatile compact SUV that appeals to daily commuters and small families alike, offering nimble handling and a practical cabin. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported reliability data carefully, particularly regarding unexpected engine behavior and electrical glitches. While many drivers enjoy its feature-packed interior and efficient performance, taking the time to inspect powertrain health and service history is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The base 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 146 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. It prioritizes fuel efficiency but requires careful monitoring for potential oil consumption or drivability concerns.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine stalling, hesitation, and a sudden loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to inspect fuel delivery, ignition components, and electronic sensor signals.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates 175 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque, offering livelier acceleration for highway driving. It pairs with an all-wheel-drive system and delivers a more engaging driving dynamic.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

The continuously variable automatic transmission (IVT) is paired with the base 2.0L engine to optimize fuel economy in front-wheel or all-wheel-drive configurations.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired with the 1.6L turbocharged engine, providing quick shifts but occasionally showing sensitivity to prolonged heavy traffic conditions.

**Transmission Overheating and Clutch Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted dashboard warnings regarding transmission overheating and temporary clutch engagement issues during heavy traffic or stop-and-go driving.
- **Solution:** Allow the transmission to cool down, and have a dealer inspect the dual-clutch module and fluid condition.

## Interior

### Dashboard and Warning Lights

Electrical architecture manages convenience features and driver-assistance systems, though occasional software glitches and sensor miscommunications have been reported by owners.

**Erratic Instrument Cluster and Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden illumination of multiple warning lights on the dashboard alongside erratic behavior from the infotainment or navigation systems.
- **Solution:** Inspect battery health, grounding points, and software module calibration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V099000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Seltos&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Engine damage can cause a vehicle stall or fire, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Seltos offers attractive styling, good cargo space, and modern technology, making it an appealing option in the crowded compact SUV segment. However, used car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on engine operation, electrical systems, and transmission performance. Verifying service records and checking for completed safety updates will help ensure a more dependable ownership experience.
