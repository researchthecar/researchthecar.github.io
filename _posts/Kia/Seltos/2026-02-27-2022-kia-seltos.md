# 2022 Kia Seltos

### Overview
The 2022 Kia Seltos is a subcompact SUV that has generally received positive reviews for its value, practicality, and available all-wheel drive. From a technical reliability standpoint, the Seltos demonstrates a solid performance, with most reported issues being minor and infrequent. Owners generally appreciate its straightforward design and the availability of different powertrain options, which contribute to its overall appeal. While no vehicle is entirely without potential problems, the Seltos appears to be a dependable choice in its segment, with a focus on addressing any emerging concerns promptly through dealer service. The data reflects a vehicle that is holding up well under typical usage.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Kia Seltos presents itself as a reliable option within the subcompact SUV market. Its powertrain options, including the naturally aspirated 2.0L and the turbocharged 1.6L engines, paired with either a CVT or a 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission, have shown good durability with no widespread critical failures reported. The chassis and suspension components are also holding up well. Minor electrical gremlins, primarily related to the infotainment system's responsiveness or connectivity, are the most commonly cited issues, typically resolvable with software updates. Exterior and interior components are generally robust. Overall, the Seltos offers a dependable ownership experience with minimal significant mechanical or electrical concerns.
