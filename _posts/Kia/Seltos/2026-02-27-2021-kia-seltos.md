### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Kia Seltos generally exhibits good reliability across its main mechanical and electrical systems. The powertrain options, including both the naturally aspirated and turbocharged engines paired with their respective transmissions (CVT and 7-speed DCT), have not shown significant widespread failure patterns. Minor electrical quirks, primarily related to the infotainment system's responsiveness or connectivity, are the most commonly cited issues, typically resolvable with software updates. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components are performing well, with no significant recurring problems reported. Overall, the Seltos is a dependable choice in its class, offering a low likelihood of major mechanical distress for owners.
