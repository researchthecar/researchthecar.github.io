### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Kia Seltos demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly with its naturally aspirated engine and CVT transmission. The primary area of concern, though minor for most, lies with the 7-speed DCT, which can occasionally exhibit slight hesitations. Electrical issues are generally limited to minor infotainment glitches that are often resolved with software updates. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components show no significant widespread problems. For owners of the DCT-equipped Seltos, being aware of potential transmission behavior and ensuring timely software checks can contribute to a smoother ownership experience. Overall, the Seltos is a dependable choice in its segment.
