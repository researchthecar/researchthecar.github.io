# 2019 Kia Cadenza Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Kia Cadenza is a full-size sedan known for its spacious and upscale cabin, smooth ride, and strong feature set. For used car shoppers evaluating reliability, looking closely at powertrain options and reported owner complaints can help identify potential areas to inspect before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L V6 GDI

This 3.3-liter direct-injection V6 engine produces around 290 horsepower and 253 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and responsive acceleration for daily driving. It is generally regarded as a durable powerplant, though owners should always check service history for regular oil changes.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels and aids in highway fuel economy. Overall, this gearbox has a solid reputation for reliability when properly maintained.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body and glass components are generally durable, though isolated incidents involving sunroof glass have been reported by owners.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported a sudden loud noise resembling a gunshot, followed by shattering of the sunroof glass while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any stress fractures and have damaged glass replaced by a professional shop.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V137000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Cadenza&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The electrical circuit in the Hydraulic Electronic Control Unit (HECU) may short-circuit, which can cause a fire in the engine compartment. An engine compartment fire can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace certain fuses in the electrical junction box. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Kia Cadenza offers a refined driving experience and comfortable interior for used car buyers. While overall complaint volume is relatively low, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's glass, electronics, and service history to ensure everything is in top working order.
