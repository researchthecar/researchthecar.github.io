**2016 Kia Cadenza**

The 2016 Kia Cadenza is a full-size sedan that offers a comfortable and spacious interior, a powerful engine, and a host of standard features. It comes with a 3.3-liter V6 engine that produces 293 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque, paired with an available eight-speed automatic transmission. All-wheel drive is also available. The Cadenza has received positive reviews for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and impressive performance. However, some owners have reported several issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.3-liter V6 Engine**
    * **Ticking noise:** A ticking noise may be heard from the engine, which can be caused by worn valve lifters. The solution is to replace the valve lifters.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting issues:** The transmission may hesitate or shudder during shifts, which can be caused by a faulty valve body or torque converter. The solution is to replace the faulty component.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation:** Condensation may accumulate inside the headlight assemblies, which can reduce visibility at night. The solution is to seal the headlights or replace the affected units.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattles:** The dashboard may rattle or creak over rough roads, which can be caused by loose components or poor insulation. The solution is to identify the source of the noise and tighten or replace the affected components.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500+
* **Recalls:** None Found
