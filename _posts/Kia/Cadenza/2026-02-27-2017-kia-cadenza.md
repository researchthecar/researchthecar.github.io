### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**

- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories, potential replacement of faulty module or battery.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Kia Cadenza is generally considered a reliable vehicle, with its powertrain and core mechanical components demonstrating good durability. The most frequently cited issues tend to be concentrated in the electrical system, specifically concerning the infotainment unit and occasional battery drain problems. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and require diagnostic attention. Other areas like the engine, transmission, suspension, exterior, and interior components have largely avoided widespread complaints, suggesting good build quality and component longevity in those aspects. Owners should be aware of potential electrical quirks and ensure proper maintenance to maximize the vehicle's lifespan.
