# 2016 Kia Cadenza Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Kia Cadenza offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and strong standard features for shoppers looking at full-size used sedans. While most owners enjoy its smooth road manners and luxury-adjacent feel, prospective buyers should review a few recurring owner complaints regarding windshield sealing, suspension wear, and potential engine concerns. Evaluating maintenance history closely can help you find a well-kept example.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L V6 GDI

The 2016 Kia Cadenza comes equipped with a 3.3-liter direct-injection V6 engine producing around 293 horsepower. This powertrain provides brisk acceleration and refined power delivery, though used buyers should verify proper cooling system health and service records.

**Engine Overheating and Coolant Intrusion**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported repeated engine overheating, unexpected coolant loss, and white smoke emitting from the exhaust.
- **Solution:** Professional diagnostic testing is required to check for internal head gasket failure or coolant leaks, potentially requiring major engine service or replacement.

**Loose Head Bolts**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note severe engine failure associated with loosened head bolts within the engine block.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection and repair, which may involve machining or replacing the affected engine components.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is sent to the front wheels via a smooth 6-speed automatic transmission. This transmission generally operates reliably without major recurring owner complaints.

## Suspension

### Front Struts

Suspension complaints are relatively minimal, though high-mileage models may experience component wear like front strut failure.

**Premature Strut Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear a loud pop or grinding noise from the front end, followed by the vehicle sitting lower on one side due to failed suspension components.
- **Solution:** Replace the worn-out front struts and associated mounting hardware.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Seals

Exterior complaints center primarily around windshield seal degradation and glass issues.

**Windshield Leaks and Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced wind noise, sudden cracking, and water leaking into the cabin from the top and sides of the front windshield due to failing seals.
- **Solution:** Inspect windshield seals and have the windshield professionally resealed or replaced if leaks or cracks are present.

### Sunroof

The interior is generally well-appointed and durable, though rare reports of spontaneous sunroof glass breakage have been noted.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report hearing a loud noise while driving at highway speeds, followed by the sunroof glass suddenly shattering and scattering glass inside the cabin.
- **Solution:** Have the sunroof glass replaced and check the assembly frame for any structural stress or alignment defects.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V158000: Visibility:Windshield](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Cadenza&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During manufacturing, the front windshield may not have been properly bonded to the vehicle, allowing it to detach. A front windshield that detaches from a vehicle can increase the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the front windshield glass. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V652000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Cadenza&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The Hydraulic Electronic Control Unit (HECU) may experience an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HECU fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Kia Cadenza is an appealing option for used car buyers seeking a spacious, comfortable sedan with plenty of features. While most mechanical systems prove dependable, shoppers should carefully inspect the vehicle for windshield seal leaks, suspension wear, and proper engine cooling performance during a pre-purchase inspection.
