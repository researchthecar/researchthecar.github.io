## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Occasional freezing, unresponsiveness, or Bluetooth connectivity issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates or system reset. In some cases, replacement of the head unit may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $100 - $800

**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Premature battery failure or unexplained drain when the vehicle is off.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw, repair faulty modules or wiring.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2013 Kia Forte Koup demonstrates solid reliability across its powertrain and chassis components. The most frequently cited issues tend to be minor electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and occasional battery drain, which are generally manageable and not indicative of systemic failures. Owners can expect a dependable driving experience with proper maintenance, with the caveat to be aware of potential infotainment quirks. Overall, it represents a reliable choice within its segment for those seeking a stylish and budget-friendly coupe.
