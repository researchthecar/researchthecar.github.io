### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Kia Forte Koup is generally considered a reliable vehicle, especially for its class and price point. Major mechanical failures are uncommon, and most reported issues are minor, such as occasional infotainment system glitches. Both the 2.0L and 1.6L Turbo engines, along with the manual and automatic transmissions, have demonstrated good durability. Owners should be aware of potential infotainment system quirks, but these are typically resolvable with software updates. Overall, the Forte Koup presents a solid choice for those seeking a sporty and dependable compact coupe.
