# 2017 Kia Forte Koup Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Kia Forte Koup offers a sporty two-door design and an engaging driving dynamic for budget-conscious buyers. Because owner complaint data for this specific model year is sparse, prospective shoppers should evaluate individual vehicle condition carefully through a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

This 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 147 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque. It serves as a dependable, fuel-efficient base powerplant for daily commuting.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

This turbocharged 1.6-liter engine generates around 201 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque, powering the sportier SX trim. It offers spirited performance, though buyers should verify regular maintenance intervals.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional 6-speed manual transmission paired with select trims, offering direct mechanical control and a more engaging driving experience.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission that delivers smooth, predictable shifts for everyday driving conditions.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

A 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission paired with the turbocharged engine, providing quick gear changes and responsive acceleration.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V031000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Forte+Koup&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Circuit damage may result in deactivated air bags that will not deploy in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ACU, and either update the software or replace the unit, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Kia Forte Koup presents few documented owner complaints, making a physical inspection and vehicle history check your best tools for evaluating a used model. Ensure all routine maintenance has been performed, especially on turbocharged variants.
