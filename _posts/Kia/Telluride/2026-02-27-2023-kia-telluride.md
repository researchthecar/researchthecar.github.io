# 2023 Kia Telluride Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Kia Telluride has built a strong reputation as a comfortable, feature-packed three-row midsize SUV, but prospective buyers of a pre-owned model should note several recurring complaints. Owners have reported issues ranging from drivetrain binding to electrical glitches and body trim concerns. While many components perform reliably, being aware of these common trouble spots can help you inspect a used model more thoroughly before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 GDI

Powered by a 3.8-liter V6 direct-injection engine producing around 291 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and capable acceleration for family driving.

**Crankshaft Timing Codes**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine light illuminates with diagnostic trouble codes P0016 or P0017, occasionally accompanied by engine performance issues.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the timing components and sensors, and perform any necessary repairs or warranty service.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a conventional 8-speed automatic transmission available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations, offering smooth and responsive gear shifts.

### AWD Coupler

Issues with the all-wheel drive system components can lead to binding or noise during tight low-speed maneuvers.

**All-Wheel Drive Coupler Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual noise, shaking, or a skipping/thumping sensation when making slow-speed turns, along with potential difficulty accelerating.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty AWD coupler assembly with updated components.

## Suspension

### Rear Shocks

The rear suspension setup provides a comfortable ride quality, though rear shock components have occasionally required early replacement.

**Rear Shock Absorber Clunking**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or thumping noises coming from the rear suspension over bumps or when rear passengers board.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace worn or faulty rear shock absorbers.

## Electrical

### Digital Instrument Cluster

Advanced digital displays manage driver instrumentation and infotainment, though intermittent display failures have been reported by some owners.

**Instrument Cluster Blank Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The digital driver cluster screen fails to power on or goes completely black upon starting the vehicle, removing access to gauges and warnings.
- **Solution:** Have the dealership inspect the digital display unit and associated wiring harness or software module.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior glass and trim components generally hold up well, but owners have noted susceptibility to windshield cracking.

**Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields developing stress cracks spontaneously or easily chipping upon minimal impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged windshield glass.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V035000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing error, the wire harnesses for the second row seat side air bags may become damaged, causing them not to deploy in the event of a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace, or secure the air bag wire harnesses, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V298000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 101, "Control and Displays," and 138, "Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems." A blank instrument cluster display will not show critical information, such as tire pressure, warning messages, and warning lights, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V214000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The intermediate shaft and right front driveshaft may not be fully engaged, which can result in vehicle roll away while in PARK. A vehicle roll away increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic parking brake software and replace any damaged intermediate shafts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V407000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front power seat motor may overheat due to a stuck power seat slide knob, which can result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a bracket for the power seat switch back covers and replace the seat slide knobs, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V494000: Structure:Exterior Trim](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A detached trim piece can become a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the belt molding trim assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V430000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The front power seat motor may overheat due to a stuck power seat slide knob or an improper recall 24V407 repair, which can result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install an electronic fuse assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Telluride remains an appealing option in the used midsize SUV market due to its comfort, family-friendly packaging, and strong feature list. Buyers should prioritize a pre-purchase inspection that checks the all-wheel drive system for binding during tight turns, verifies proper operation of the digital instrument cluster, and inspects the rear suspension and windshield for existing wear.
