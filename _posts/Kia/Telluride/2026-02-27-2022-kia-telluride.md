# 2022 Kia Telluride Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Telluride has established itself as a popular midsize three-row SUV known for its comfortable interior, smooth ride, and strong value. However, used car buyers should review owner-reported issues and known service bulletins carefully. While many owners praise the vehicle's versatility, common problem areas reported to safety regulators include electrical glitches, windshield and sunroof durability concerns, and intermittent engine or drivetrain complaints. Understanding these patterns can help prospective buyers inspect a used model thoroughly before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 Engine

The Telluride features a naturally aspirated 3.8-liter V6 engine producing approximately 291 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, offering confident acceleration for daily driving and towing. Overall, the powertrain is generally robust, though buyers should routinely check for fluid leaks and idle stability during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Oil Filter Housing Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Oil leaks or pooling around the engine bay due to cracking in the plastic oil filter housing.
- **Solution:** Inspect the oil filter housing for hairline cracks and replace the plastic assembly with an updated unit if leaking.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel drive or available all-wheel drive, this unit delivers smooth and timely gear shifts. It has a relatively solid reputation among owners with very few widespread mechanical failures reported.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Backup Camera

Electrical accessories, including infotainment touchscreens, warning indicators, and power locking mechanisms, are common areas for minor owner complaints.

**Infotainment Reboot and Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The central display screen may intermittently freeze, reboot, or lose backup camera visibility due to software glitches or water intrusion.
- **Solution:** Update the infotainment system software or replace faulty camera assemblies and wiring harnesses.

## Exterior

### Door Locks and Actuators

Door lock mechanisms require functional actuators to ensure safety features operate dependably.

**Child Safety Lock and Door Lock Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Error messages on the dashboard regarding door locks or failure of the child safety locks to engage properly.
- **Solution:** Replace malfunctioning door lock actuators or reprogram the body control module as required.

### Windshield and Glass

Exterior glass components, including windshields and optional sunroofs, have generated complaints regarding susceptibility to stress cracks and sudden failures.

**Spontaneous Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Windshields developing stress cracks across the glass surface without any visible impact from road debris.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield for edge defects and replace the windshield if spontaneous cracking occurs.

**Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud popping noises followed by the sunroof glass suddenly shattering while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for structural alignment.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety restraint systems are vital components that should be thoroughly tested during a used vehicle inspection to ensure proper latching and retraction.

**Seat Belt Latch and Retractor Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty latching front seat belt buckles or rear seat belts failing to retract smoothly into their housings.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace faulty buckles or retractors under warranty or service guidelines.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 101, "Control and Displays." **[Recall 22V509000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Impaired anti-lock braking or traction control systems can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the spare tire, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V626000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse and a wiring harness extension. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V214000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The intermediate shaft and right front driveshaft may not be fully engaged, which can result in vehicle roll away while in PARK. A vehicle roll away increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic parking brake software and replace any damaged intermediate shafts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V407000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front power seat motor may overheat due to a stuck power seat slide knob, which can result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a bracket for the power seat switch back covers and replace the seat slide knobs, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V430000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The front power seat motor may overheat due to a stuck power seat slide knob or an improper recall 24V407 repair, which can result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install an electronic fuse assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, the 2022 Kia Telluride remains an attractive choice in the used midsize SUV market due to its comfort, features, and strong engine performance. Prospective buyers should perform a detailed pre-purchase inspection focusing on the condition of the windshield and glass, proper operation of all electrical accessories and safety features, and checking for any engine fluid leaks. Verifying maintenance history and ensuring all open recalls have been completed will greatly reduce the risk of unexpected repair bills.
