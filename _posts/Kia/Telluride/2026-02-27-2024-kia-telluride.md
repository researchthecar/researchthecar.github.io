# 2024 Kia Telluride Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Kia Telluride continues to be a highly popular choice in the midsize three-row SUV segment, praised for its upscale interior, spacious cabin, and smooth ride. However, prospective used-car buyers should weigh these strong comfort and utility traits against a handful of reported electrical, body trim, and safety system concerns that have drawn attention from owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 Engine

The 2024 Kia Telluride is powered by a 3.8-liter V6 engine producing approximately 291 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, providing confident acceleration for daily driving and towing. While generally robust, buyers should remain vigilant regarding isolated reports of oil consumption and sudden engine stalls.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle burns a noticeable amount of oil between changes without visible external leaks on the ground.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels frequently and consult a dealer for diagnostic testing if consumption is rapid.

**Engine Stalling and Shifter Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may stall at low speeds accompanied by warning lights and a message prompting the driver to shift to park or neutral.
- **Solution:** Have the powertrain control module and related sensors inspected by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Mated to the V6 engine is an 8-speed automatic transmission available with front-wheel drive or torque-vectoring all-wheel drive, delivering smooth and refined gear changes suited for family road trips.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Infotainment Screens

Owner complaints highlight occasional electronic glitches involving the central infotainment screen and digital instrument panels going dark while driving.

**Blank Dashboard and Infotainment Displays**
- **Symptoms:** Digital instrument clusters or center touchscreen displays go completely black intermittently, making the speedometer and safety notifications invisible.
- **Solution:** Software updates or complete display unit replacements may be necessary, though some owners report diagnostic delays at dealerships.

## Exterior

### Body Structure and Door Trim

Exterior body structure concerns primarily center around loose or detaching trim pieces that have been the subject of safety recalls.

**Detaching Door Belt Molding**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior door window trim or belt molding pieces loosen and detach from the vehicle while driving, creating a road hazard.
- **Solution:** Participate in manufacturer recall campaigns to secure or replace affected trim pieces, though parts availability may cause wait times.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Brakes

Advanced safety and driver-assistance suites provide strong protection but occasionally trigger complaints regarding false positive braking or unexpected electronic behavior.

**Unexpected Braking or Acceleration Anomalies**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may experience unexpected forward collision braking activations or rare instances of unintended acceleration on inclines.
- **Solution:** Have driver-assistance sensor calibrations checked and software inspected by an authorized service center.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V077000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash. A hole in the engine block increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the engine sub-assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V214000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The intermediate shaft and right front driveshaft may not be fully engaged, which can result in vehicle roll away while in PARK. A vehicle roll away increases the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic parking brake software and replace any damaged intermediate shafts as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V407000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The front power seat motor may overheat due to a stuck power seat slide knob, which can result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a bracket for the power seat switch back covers and replace the seat slide knobs, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V494000: Structure:Exterior Trim](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached trim piece can become a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the belt molding trim assemblies as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V430000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Telluride&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The front power seat motor may overheat due to a stuck power seat slide knob or an improper recall 24V407 repair, which can result in a fire while parked or driving. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install an electronic fuse assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Kia Telluride remains a compelling, highly capable family SUV with a well-earned reputation for comfort and features. Used-car shoppers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history reports, verify that all open safety recalls—particularly those concerning exterior trim—have been addressed, and ensure all electronic instrument displays and driver-assistance systems operate properly during a pre-purchase test drive.
