**Introduction:**

The 2022 Kia Telluride is a mid-size SUV that offers a spacious cabin, powerful engines, and impressive technology features. It comes with two engine options: a 291-horsepower V6 with an 8-speed automatic transmission and a 355-horsepower V6 with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Owners praise the Telluride for its comfortable ride, ample seating, and advanced safety features. However, certain issues have been reported with specific components.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buildup of carbon deposits on intake valves can lead to reduced engine performance, rough idling, and increased emissions.
    * Solution: Cleaning or replacing intake valves and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption may require frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Engine inspection, potential replacement of piston rings or valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Harsh Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may exhibit rough or delayed shifts, especially during acceleration or deceleration.
    * Solution: Software updates, clutch pack replacement, or transmission rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy rear shocks, particularly over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharge occurs even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Electrical system inspection, replacement of faulty components (e.g., battery, alternator).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Wiper blades may fail to operate or move erratically.
    * Solution: Replacement of wiper motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Creaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard may creak or rattle during driving, particularly on rough roads.
    * Solution: Tightening or replacing dashboard components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Seat Belt Buckle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear seat belt buckle may not latch or unlatch properly.
    * Solution: Replacement of seat belt buckle assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number 22V-066

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Kia Telluride is a reliable vehicle with a good track record. However, like any vehicle, it may experience some issues with specific components. The majority of the issues reported are relatively minor and can be fixed relatively inexpensively. However, some issues, such as oil consumption and transmission problems, can be more severe and costly to resolve.
