# 2022 Kia Rio Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Rio is a practical and economical subcompact car, offering efficient daily transportation for budget-conscious buyers. While generally seen as a straightforward commuter vehicle, used-car shoppers should be aware of a handful of owner-reported electrical, visibility, and mechanical complaints gathered from federal safety databases.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Inline-4 Gamma II Engine

The 2022 Kia Rio comes equipped with a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated inline-4 engine producing approximately 120 horsepower and 112 lb-ft of torque. This powerplant prioritizes fuel economy over high performance, making it a dependable and straightforward choice for everyday city driving when properly maintained.

**Engine Knocking and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unusual knocking noises coming from the engine bay alongside abnormal operating temperatures.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a qualified technician or dealership to diagnose internal engine components such as connecting rods.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Power is sent to the front wheels via an Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT) designed to optimize fuel efficiency. This automatic transmission option provides smooth acceleration in traffic, though buyers should test-drive thoroughly to ensure responsive operation.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera System

Safety features include standard driver aids and backup systems, though intermittent electrical displays have been noted by some owners.

**Intermittent Rearview Camera Display**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera may intermittently activate while driving at various speeds or display a blank or distorted image when shifting into reverse.
- **Solution:** Check the camera wiring harness, display connections, and backup trigger switch for faults.

## Exterior

### Windshield Wiper Switch

Electrical accessories provide basic convenience features, though isolated owner complaints highlight potential switch or sensor glitches.

**Intermittent Wiper Operation**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield wipers may struggle to move or fail to operate on initial intermittent settings, working only on higher speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the wiper switch assembly and motor linkage, replacing faulty components as necessary.

## Safety

### Power Windows

Exterior components are generally standard for the class, with power accessories requiring basic verification during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Window Auto-Reverse Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic window mechanism may fail to stop when lightly engaged, rolling all the way up and potentially trapping objects or hands.
- **Solution:** Have the power window regulator, motor, and safety auto-reverse sensors inspected and calibrated or replaced.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V304000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Rio&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A bolt in the steering column may not be secured properly, which can cause the steering column to detach from the steering rack and result in a loss of steering control. Loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the lower steering column u-joint retention bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Rio remains an appealing option for budget-minded used-car buyers looking for high fuel economy and simple daily operation. While most owners experience trouble-free driving, prospective buyers should carefully inspect electrical accessories, window mechanisms, and powertrain performance during a test drive to ensure everything is in proper working order.
