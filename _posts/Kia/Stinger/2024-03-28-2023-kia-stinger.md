**Introduction to the 2023 Kia Stinger**

The 2023 Kia Stinger is a sleek and powerful sports sedan that offers an impressive combination of style, performance, and affordability. It is available with a choice of two engine options: a turbocharged 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing 300 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, and a twin-turbocharged 3.3-liter V6 engine with 368 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission.

While the Stinger is generally well-received by owners and critics alike, there have been some reported issues with certain aspects of the vehicle. These issues range in severity from minor inconveniences to more significant problems that may require costly repairs.

**Engine**

**Turbocharged 2.5-liter Four-Cylinder Engine**

* **Engine Misfires**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Twin-Turbocharged 3.3-liter V6 Engine**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning excessive amounts of oil, low oil levels on dipstick
    * Solution: Replace piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifting, slipping gears
    * Solution: Reflash transmission control module or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Squeaky Control Arms**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noise from suspension over bumps
    * Solution: Replace control arms
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Shortages**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, flickering lights, loss of power
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening rear doors from the inside
    * Solution: Replace door handle assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaks into the cabin through sunroof
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2023 Kia Stinger is a desirable vehicle, it is not without its drawbacks. The reported issues, while not widespread, can be costly to repair in some cases. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential issues and factor them into their decision-making process.
