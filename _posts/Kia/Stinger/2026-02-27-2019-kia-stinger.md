# 2019 Kia Stinger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Kia Stinger is a performance-oriented fastback sedan that delivers engaging driving dynamics and luxury-tier styling. While widely praised for its power and handling, used buyers should carefully inspect specific areas—most notably the braking system and high-pressure fuel components—to ensure trouble-free ownership.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing around 255 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering solid everyday efficiency and punchy performance.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbo V6

A potent 3.3-liter twin-turbocharged V6 putting out 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, providing rapid acceleration and robust grand-touring capability.

**High-Pressure Fuel Pump Concern**
- **Symptoms:** Potential fuel delivery issues or related dashboard warnings tied to high-pressure fuel pump operation.
- **Solution:** Verify that all relevant manufacturer recall campaigns and software updates concerning the high-pressure fuel system have been properly executed.

**Oil Pressure Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated oil pressure warning lights despite normal actual oil pressure levels verified by a mechanic.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty oil pressure sensor unit.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission paired with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, delivering smooth and quick shifts suited to the Stinger's sporty character.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

High-performance braking systems provide strong stopping power but have shown a tendency toward premature rotor vibration and heat warping under normal driving conditions.

**Brake Rotor Warpage and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel shudder, pedal vibration, and wobbling sensations when applying the brakes at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Resurface or replace the front brake rotors and pads with upgraded aftermarket or revised OEM components.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Trim

Exterior panels and glass features generally hold up well, though owners have reported occasional sunroof rattles and isolated incidents of glass stress fractures.

**Sunroof Shattering and Rattling**
- **Symptoms:** Acoustic rattling noises from the sunroof assembly while driving, or rare instances of sudden glass breakage.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof alignment and seals for loose hardware, and replace shattered glass panels if damaged.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V518000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** While driving, an engine compartment fire may occur in the area where the Anti-lock Brake Hydraulic Electronic Control Unit (HECU) is located. An engine compartment fire can increase the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will install a new fuse kit in the electrical junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V634000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine control unit software, inspect, and replace the fuel pump as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V169000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger:Hoses/Plumbing](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire while driving.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe and hose assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Kia Stinger is an enticing pre-owned sport sedan, provided prospective buyers check for common wear items like warped brake rotors and ensure all engine-related recalls have been completed by a certified dealer.
