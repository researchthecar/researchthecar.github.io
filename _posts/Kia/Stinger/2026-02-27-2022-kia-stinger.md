## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Intermittent Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Screen may freeze, become unresponsive, or display errors. Navigation system may occasionally lose signal.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or potential replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $100 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Discharge**
- **Symptoms:** Battery may drain unexpectedly, leading to starting issues. Can be related to parasitic draw from certain modules.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis of parasitic draw, replacement of faulty module, or battery replacement.
- **Cost:** $200 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Kia Stinger presents itself as a reliable performance sedan, with its core mechanical components (engine and transmission) showing strong durability. The primary areas of concern, based on available data, lie within the electrical system, specifically with potential infotainment system glitches and occasional battery drain issues. These are not typically catastrophic failures but can be inconvenient and require professional diagnosis. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components are generally free from widespread reported defects. Overall, the Stinger offers a good reliability record for its class, provided potential electrical quirks are addressed promptly.
