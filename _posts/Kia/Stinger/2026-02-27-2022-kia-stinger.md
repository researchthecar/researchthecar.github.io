# 2022 Kia Stinger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Stinger delivers an engaging driving experience with a sleek fastback design and strong engine options. While it offers impressive performance, prospective buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding exterior glass, electrical accessories, and powertrain components to ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque for base trims. Owner reports indicate occasional performance hesitations, though it generally provides a responsive daily driving experience.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The 3.3L twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers a robust 368 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque for GT trims. It offers brisk acceleration, though buyers should verify proper engine management and electronic control operation.

**Acceleration Hesitation and ECM Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to accelerate properly even at full throttle, creating a safety hazard in traffic.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the electronic control module (ECM) and associated engine management sensors.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with both rear-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It generally shifts smoothly and provides reliable operation under normal driving conditions.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Electronic Control Unit (HECU)

Braking performance is generally strong, particularly on GT trims equipped with performance brakes, though rare hydraulic control unit failures have been reported.

**HECU Failure and Brake Damage**
- **Symptoms:** ABS and braking system failure accompanied by burning components, warped rotors, and damaged calipers.
- **Solution:** Inspect the HECU, anti-lock braking system components, and brake hardware, replacing failed modules and damaged brakes.
- **Cost:** $4500

## Exterior

### Sunroof Assembly

Exterior trim and panels hold up well, but a small number of owners have reported incidents involving spontaneous glass breakage on the sunroof.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud bang heard while driving followed by the sunroof glass unexpectedly shattering without impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for stress or defects.

## Interior

### HVAC Blower Motor

Electrical accessories and climate control components generally function well, though occasional blower motor and wiring harness failures have been noted by owners.

**Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Complete loss of air conditioning and heater blower function, creating discomfort and potential safety issues in extreme temperatures.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the blower motor and related wiring harnesses as necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V169000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger:Hoses/Plumbing](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire while driving.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe and hose assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Stinger is an appealing choice for buyers seeking sport sedan performance and luxury features. While major mechanical failures are infrequent, prospective owners should perform a detailed pre-purchase inspection checking the electrical system, sunroof glass, and braking components to ensure trouble-free ownership.
