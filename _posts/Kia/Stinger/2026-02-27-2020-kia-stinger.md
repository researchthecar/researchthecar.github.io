# 2020 Kia Stinger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2020 Kia Stinger, buyers can generally expect an engaging grand tourer with strong performance credentials. However, reviewing owner-submitted complaints highlights a few recurring mechanical and electrical areas worth monitoring during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 255 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate entry-level performance. It generally offers reliable daily driving dynamics when properly maintained.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbo V6

The potent 3.3-liter twin-turbocharged V6 delivers 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque for robust acceleration. Buyers should closely inspect the fuel delivery systems and check service records for any past recall or drivability repairs.

**Fuel Pump and Stalling Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine hesitation, fuel starvation symptoms, and occasional engine stalling.
- **Solution:** Inspect fuel system components and verify if all relevant software updates or pump replacements have been performed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and responsive shifts across rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It maintains a generally solid reputation with few widespread mechanical complaints reported by owners.

## Brakes

### Brake Rotors and Pads

The high-performance braking system offers strong stopping power, though some owners experience rotor vibration issues over time.

**Brake Vibration and Rotor Depths**
- **Symptoms:** Braking can produce severe steering wheel shaking and cabin vibrations caused by uneven pad deposits on the rotors.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for uneven wear, and have rotors resurfaced or replaced as necessary.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering

Steering components generally provide sharp handling, but electrical anomalies can occasionally affect power assist.

**Power Steering Stiffening and Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel may suddenly become extremely heavy, stiff, or difficult to control while driving, or experience unprompted movement while idling.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electronic power steering (EPS) module, wiring connections, and related diagnostic codes.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The cabin features extensive electronics, though isolated wiring and fuse glitches have been documented by vehicle owners.

**Signal-Induced Electrical Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Engaging the right-hand turn signal can occasionally cause the vehicle's entire electrical system to shut down.
- **Solution:** Inspect the multi-function switch, fuse blocks, and body control modules for short circuits or faulty wiring.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V862000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A software error in the instrument cluster may cause an inaccurate fuel gauge reading. An inaccurate fuel gauge may result in the vehicle unexpectedly running out of fuel and stalling, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V422000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace the MDPS Assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V560000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Insufficient fuel supply to the engine can result in a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the fuel pump and replace the jet nozzle housing assembly, if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V518000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** While driving, an engine compartment fire may occur in the area where the Anti-lock Brake Hydraulic Electronic Control Unit (HECU) is located. An engine compartment fire can increase the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will install a new fuse kit in the electrical junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V634000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the engine control unit software, inspect, and replace the fuel pump as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V169000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger:Hoses/Plumbing](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire while driving.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe and hose assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Kia Stinger is a compelling sports sedan that blends luxury features with strong performance, particularly in V6 guise. While most owners enjoy trouble-free driving, prospective buyers should carefully check the steering assist system, brake rotor condition, and electrical functions during a thorough test drive and pre-purchase inspection.
