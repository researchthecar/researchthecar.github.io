# 2023 Kia Stinger Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Kia Stinger serves as an exciting, high-performance sport sedan that delivers dynamic handling and punchy turbocharged power. For used car shoppers, it represents a compelling grand tourer, though buyers should review owner-reported cosmetic and electrical quirks during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces approximately 300 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque in later model years, offering brisk acceleration for daily driving. It provides a balanced blend of efficiency and responsive power delivery for everyday commuters.

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

The potent 3.3-liter twin-turbo V6 generates 368 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, turning the Stinger into a true performance machine. It delivers thrilling acceleration and robust highway passing power while maintaining general mechanical durability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission handles gear selection across all Stinger trims, paired with either rear-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. It offers smooth, quick shifts and generally operates reliably without widespread mechanical complaints.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Exterior body components are generally stylish and robust, though glass integrity on equipped sunroofs warrants a careful check.

**Sunroof Glass Fracture**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the sunroof glass shatters unexpectedly while driving, accompanied by a loud pop.
- **Solution:** Requires replacement of the shattered sunroof assembly and thorough cleaning of glass shards from the interior cabin.

### Door Latch

Interior appointments offer a premium feel and driver-focused ergonomics, though physical latches should be inspected for secure operation.

**Unintended Door Opening**
- **Symptoms:** An owner noted an alarming incident where the driver's door unlatched and opened unexpectedly while navigating a highway curve.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty door latch mechanisms or locking cables if proper engagement fails.

## Interior

### Cabin Electronics

The electrical architecture supports numerous comfort and tech features, though occasional component glitches have been documented by vehicle owners.

**Cabin Electrical Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience intermittent electrical failures affecting window switches and various cabin accessories after service or during normal operation.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing by a qualified technician is necessary to isolate loose harnesses, faulty body control modules, or switch assemblies.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Safety equipment includes a comprehensive suite of modern supplemental restraint systems, though isolated incidents of non-deployment during severe impacts have been reported.

**Failure of Airbags to Deploy**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported that frontal and side airbags failed to deploy during a significant high-speed collision and spin-out.
- **Solution:** Professional diagnostic inspection of the airbag control module, impact sensors, and wiring harness is required.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V169000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger:Hoses/Plumbing](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Stinger&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire while driving.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe and hose assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Kia Stinger remains a highly appealing choice for used car buyers seeking luxury-sport styling and turbocharged performance. While isolated owner complaints highlight potential concerns regarding sunroof glass, cabin electronics, and latch mechanisms, a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
