# 2017 Kia Optima Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Kia Optima offers a comfortable ride, spacious cabin, and attractive styling, making it a popular choice in the midsize sedan market. However, prospective buyers should approach used models with caution, particularly regarding powertrain reliability and recurring electrical concerns. While many owners enjoy trouble-free commuting, a notable portion of drivers have reported significant engine issues and unexpected component failures.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces around 185 horsepower and serves as the standard powerplant for base trims. Buyers should carefully check service records for oil consumption habits and prior warranty engine replacements.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling, hesitation, and sudden loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a certified technician; repairs may involve addressing wiring, fuel delivery, or internal engine components.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** The engine rapidly consumes oil between regular service intervals without visible external leaks.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels frequently and consult a dealer regarding ongoing manufacturer warranty extensions or class-action settlement guidelines.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine pairs with a dual-clutch transmission to prioritize fuel economy while generating approximately 178 horsepower. It provides a nimble driving dynamic, though routine maintenance is essential for longevity.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers a sportier experience with around 245 horsepower for high-end trims like the SX. Buyers looking at this option should inspect the turbocharger and cooling systems thoroughly.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional six-speed automatic transmission is paired with standard four-cylinder models and is generally robust. Routine fluid changes help maintain smooth shifting over high mileage.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The seven-speed dual-clutch transmission comes mated to the 1.6L turbo engine, offering quick shifts for efficiency-minded buyers. Smooth low-speed operation relies heavily on proper software calibration and clutch health.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering components generally hold up well, though occasional electronic power steering assist failures have been reported by owners.

**Loss of Power Steering Assist**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel suddenly becomes heavy and difficult to turn while driving, accompanied by dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Restarting the vehicle often temporarily clears the symptom, but professional diagnostic scanning is required to service faulty steering sensors or modules.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical complaints center primarily around intermittent lighting failures and warning indicator glitches that require thorough diagnosis.

**Headlight and Tail Light Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights or taillights intermittently shut off while driving at night, or turn signals malfunction unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Inspect bulb sockets, wiring harnesses, and related switches; replace damaged components as needed.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment is critical to verify during a pre-purchase inspection, particularly regarding occupant restraint functionality.

**Seat Belt Latch and Retractor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Driver or passenger seat belts fail to click securely into the latch or completely lock up and refuse to retract.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace faulty buckle mechanisms or retractor units.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V907000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Optima&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Kia has notified owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuel pipe, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V161000: Hybrid Propulsion System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Optima&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** These vehicles are equipped with a Voltage Protection Device (VPD), designed to protect the high voltage battery module, that may activate inadvertently. If the VPD activates while the vehicle is being operated in Electric Vehicle (EV) mode, there may be a loss of power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will remove the VPD switch and install two newly designed Battery Management Systems (BMS) that contain Overvoltage Protection Devices (OPDs), free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V594000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Optima&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the trunk latch base subassembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Kia Optima can be an affordable and feature-rich used sedan option, but buyers must proceed with diligence. Prioritize vehicles with comprehensive service history records confirming regular oil changes and completed manufacturer recalls. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by an independent mechanic is strongly recommended to check for engine oil consumption and electrical integrity before finalizing a purchase.
