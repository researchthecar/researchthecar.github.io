**Introduction:**
The 2017 Kia Optima is a midsize sedan available with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 2.4L inline-4 that produces 185 horsepower and 178 lb-ft of torque. Optional engines include a 2.0L turbocharged inline-4 that produces 245 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.3L V6 that produces 290 horsepower and 252 lb-ft of torque. All engines are paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Optima is praised for its spacious interior, stylish exterior, and impressive fuel economy. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of driving excitement and some interior materials.

**Engine**
**2.4L Inline-4**
* **Excessive oil consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may consume excessive amounts of oil, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Repair or replace piston rings and/or valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may stretch or break prematurely, causing engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4**
* **No major issues found**

**3.3L V6**
* **Knocking or ticking noises:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may make knocking or ticking noises during acceleration or under load.
    * Solution: Replace hydraulic lifters.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**
**6-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may hesitate or slip when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Rebuild transmission or replace defective components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Exterior**
* **Hood flutter:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The hood may flutter or shake at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Adjust hood hinges or install hood struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Climate control issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not function properly, leading to uneven temperatures or excessive noise.
    * Solution: Repair or replace defective components in the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Power window failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power windows may fail to operate or become stuck.
    * Solution: Repair or replace window motors or switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2017 Kia Optima has been known to experience some issues, especially with its engine and transmission. While not all vehicles will experience these problems, they are worth being aware of before purchasing. The estimated cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars to over $3,000, depending on the severity of the issue. It's important to consult with a qualified mechanic if you experience any of these problems.
