**Introduction:**

The 2010 Kia Rondo is a compact minivan that offers a spacious interior, versatile seating options, and a fuel-efficient four-cylinder engine. It is available with a 2.4L inline-four engine paired to a 4-speed automatic transmission or a 2.7L V6 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic transmission.

While the Rondo is generally reliable, some owners have encountered issues with certain components.

**Engine**

**2.4L Inline-Four**

* **Oil Leak:** Oil may leak from the timing cover or oil pan gasket.
    * Solution: Replace the timing cover or oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Knocking Noise:** A knocking noise from the engine may indicate worn piston rings.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Delays:** The transmission may experience delayed shifts or difficulty shifting into gear.
    * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and filter or replace the transmission control module (TCM).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:** The strut mount bearing may wear out, causing clunking or rattling noises.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mount bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Leak:** The rear shock absorbers may leak fluid, affecting ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Headlight Wiring Harness Failure:** The headlight wiring harness may fail, causing the headlights to malfunction.
    * Solution: Replace the headlight wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to a faulty ignition switch or alternator.
    * Solution: Replace the ignition switch or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:** The door handle may break or become loose, making it difficult to open the door.
    * Solution: Replace the door handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Bumper Crack:** The rear bumper may crack or break under impact.
    * Solution: Replace the rear bumper.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing the air conditioner or heater to fail.
    * Solution: Replace the climate control module or blend door actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Window Switch Failure:** The window switches may fail, causing the windows to become inoperable.
    * Solution: Replace the window switch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2010 Kia Rondo is a reliable vehicle with a few potential issues. Some engine and transmission issues can be costly to repair, but the suspension, electrical, exterior, and interior problems are generally less severe and less expensive to address.
