**Introduction:**

The 2012 Kia Rondo is a compact minivan available in two engine and transmission options: a 2.4L inline-4 with a 6-speed automatic transmission or a 2.0L inline-4 with a 6-speed manual transmission. It offers a comfortable and spacious interior, along with a 2.4L engine that delivers decent power. However, it is known for its mediocre fuel economy and some reliability concerns.

**Engine**

**2.4L Inline-4 with 6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Cracking intake manifold gaskets can cause coolant and oil leaks, leading to overheating and reduced performance.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold and gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause excessive noise and may lead to timing chain failure.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Oil Pump Failure:** In some cases, the oil pump may fail, leading to insufficient oil pressure and engine seizure.
    * Solution: Replace the oil pump and inspect engine bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA ID: 12V455000: Timing Chain Tensioner Failure
    * NHTSA ID: 14V194000: Crankshaft Oil Leakage

**2.0L Inline-4 with 6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Failure:** Faulty shift solenoids can cause erratic shifting, transmission slipping, and check engine light illumination.
    * Solution: Replace the shift solenoid pack.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Transmission Fluid Leak:** Leaks from the transmission pan, cooler lines, or seals can cause fluid loss and transmission damage.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair the source of the leak.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **Recalls:**
    * NHTSA ID: 13V326000: Transmission Control Module Software Update

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:** Worn or failed strut mounts can cause noise, vibration, and reduced ride quality.
    * Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Sway Bar Link Failure:** Broken or worn sway bar links can lead to clunking noises and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** A faulty alternator can lead to insufficient charging or complete loss of electrical power.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Battery Drain:** Parasitic battery drain can result from faulty components or poor wiring.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace the source of the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Handle Failure:** The tailgate handle may break, making it difficult to open or close the tailgate.
    * Solution: Replace the tailgate handle.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
* **Headlight Condensation:** Moisture can accumulate inside the headlight assemblies, leading to fogging or dimming.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace the headlight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Heater Core Failure:** A faulty heater core can prevent the heater from working, leading to discomfort during cold weather.
    * Solution: Replace the heater core.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Power Window Switch Failure:** Malfunctioning power window switches can prevent windows from properly rolling up or down.
    * Solution: Replace the power window switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:** Broken or worn door lock actuators can cause doors to lock or unlock erratically.
    * Solution: Replace the door lock actuators.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Summary:**

The 2012 Kia Rondo has some potential reliability issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical components. The cost of repairs can range from minor to significant, depending on the severity of the problem. However, its spacious interior and reasonable price point may appeal to those seeking a budget-friendly minivan.
