**2016 Kia Rondo**

The 2016 Kia Rondo is a compact minivan known for its spacious interior, versatility, and affordable price tag. It is powered by a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2016 Kia Rondo generally appreciate the vehicle's spaciousness, comfort, and fuel efficiency. However, some common issues have been reported.

**Engine**

* **2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine**

  * **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine may consume excessive oil, leading to frequent oil top-ups and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace piston rings and valve seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

  * **Shift Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may hesitate or delay when shifting gears, especially during acceleration.
    * Solution: Update transmission control module (TCM) software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (covered under warranty)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front MacPherson Struts:**
    * **Strut Bearing Failure:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension, especially when turning or going over bumps.
      * Solution: Replace strut bearings.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery:**
    * **Premature Battery Failure:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Battery may fail prematurely, resulting in starting problems or electrical issues.
      * Solution: Replace battery.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
      * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator:**
    * **Alternator Failure:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Electrical system may malfunction, leading to battery discharge or engine stalling.
      * Solution: Replace alternator.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Cover:**
    * **Fading:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Rear bumper cover may fade or discolor over time.
      * Solution: Replace or refinish rear bumper cover.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard:**
    * **Dashboard Rattle:**
      * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard may rattle or make a vibrating noise, especially when driving on rough roads.
      * Solution: Tighten or replace loose components in dashboard.
      * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
      * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Kia Rondo has a few common issues, including excessive oil consumption, shift hesitation, strut bearing failure, premature battery failure, and alternator failure. These issues can range in severity and cost to fix. While some issues are covered under warranty, others can be more expensive to repair. Overall, the Rondo is a reliable and practical vehicle for families and those who need a spacious and versatile minivan.
