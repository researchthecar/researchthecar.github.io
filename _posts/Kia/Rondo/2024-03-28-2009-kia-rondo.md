**2009 Kia Rondo**

The 2009 Kia Rondo is a compact MPV available with two engine options: a 2.4-liter inline-four and a 2.7-liter V6. Both engines are paired with a four-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the 2009 Kia Rondo generally appreciate its spacious interior, versatility, and affordability. However, they have also reported several issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.4-liter Inline-Four**
    * **Intake Manifold Issue:** The intake manifold can crack, causing vacuum leaks and performance issues.
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.7-liter V6**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip and potentially damage the engine.
        * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and timing chain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Four-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping:** The automatic transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorbers:** The rear shock absorbers can fail, causing a bumpy ride and reduced handling.
        * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorbers.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to a faulty alternator or parasitic draw.
        * Solution: Diagnose and repair the underlying cause of the battery drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Lock Actuator:** The tailgate lock actuator may fail, preventing the tailgate from opening.
        * Solution: Replace the tailgate lock actuator.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC Blower Motor Failure:** The HVAC blower motor may fail, resulting in a lack of air circulation.
        * Solution: Replace the HVAC blower motor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2009 Kia Rondo has been plagued by several issues, including engine problems such as intake manifold cracking, transmission slipping, suspension failures, electrical issues, and interior concerns. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to potential engine damage. The cost to fix the issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the labor rates in your area.
