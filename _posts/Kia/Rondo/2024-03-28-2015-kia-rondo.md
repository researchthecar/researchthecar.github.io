**Introduction**

The 2015 Kia Rondo is a compact MPV offered with a single engine and transmission option. The 2.0-liter GDI engine delivers 164 horsepower and 159 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Drivers appreciate the Rondo's spacious interior and comfortable ride, while some find fault with its bland styling and modest performance.

## Engine

**2.0-Liter GDI**

* **Engine Stalling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random engine stalling at low speeds or idle
    * Solution: Replace the throttle position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the valve cover gasket or oil pan
    * Solution: Replace the affected gasket or oil pan
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or delayed response
    * Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the valve body
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rapid battery discharge even when the vehicle is parked
    * Solution: Replace the defective battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Shorts**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent power loss or malfunctioning electrical components
    * Solution: Locate and repair the short circuit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

* **Door Handle Failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening doors due to broken or loose door handles
    * Solution: Replace the door handle
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tail Light Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the taillights
    * Solution: Replace the taillights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **No Issues Found**

## Summary

The 2015 Kia Rondo generally experiences minor issues, with the most common problems affecting the engine, transmission, and electrical components. While some issues can be costly to repair, overall, the vehicle has a relatively low cost of ownership compared to competitors.
