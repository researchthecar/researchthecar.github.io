**Introduction**

The 2014 Kia Rondo is a compact minivan that offers spacious cabin, comfortable seating, and a smooth ride. It comes standard with a 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine that produces 175 horsepower and 169 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. A 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine producing 164 horsepower and 140 lb-ft of torque is optional, along with a five-speed automatic transmission.

Despite its overall reliability, the 2014 Kia Rondo has had some reported issues. Here is a comprehensive list of the specific issues people are having with the vehicle, categorized by major components:

**Engine**

* **2.4-liter Four-Cylinder Engine:**
    * **Intake Valve Timing Control Solenoid Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Stalling or rough idling, especially at low speeds. Solution: Replacement of the intake valve timing control solenoid. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * **Power Steering Pump Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Whining noise from the power steering pump, loss of power steering assist. Solution: Replacement of the power steering pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * **Oxygen Sensor Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, poor fuel economy. Solution: Replacement of the oxygen sensor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
* **2.0-liter Four-Cylinder Engine:**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking or rattling noise, especially during cold starts. Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Shifting Problems:** Description/Symptoms: Rough or delayed shifting, slipping gears. Solution: Transmission fluid flush, software update, or transmission overhaul. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $2,000+
* **Five-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Torque Converter Clutch Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration during acceleration. Solution: Replacement of the torque converter clutch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the front suspension, uneven tire wear. Solution: Replacement of the front struts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Rear Shock Absorbers Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Rough ride, bouncing or swaying of the rear end. Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:** Description/Symptoms: Weak or dead battery, especially after short trips. Solution: Inspection of the electrical system for parasitic drain, alternator testing and replacement if necessary. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $600+
* **Headlight Switch Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, flickering or dimming. Solution: Replacement of the headlight switch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Windshield wipers not working, intermittent operation. Solution: Replacement of the windshield wiper motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250+
* **Power Window Motor Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Power windows not working, slow or intermittent operation. Solution: Replacement of the window motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Failure:** Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters not working, heating unevenly. Solution: Replacement of the seat heater elements or control module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Dashboard Rattle:** Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noises from the dashboard. Solution: Identification and tightening of loose components, or replacement of the dashboard. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Summary**

While the 2014 Kia Rondo has proven to be a generally reliable vehicle, it has experienced some specific issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and other components. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more serious and expensive. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential issues and to have their vehicles regularly serviced and inspected to address any problems early on.
