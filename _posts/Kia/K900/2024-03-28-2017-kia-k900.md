**Introduction**

The 2017 Kia K900 is a full-size luxury sedan that offers a potent 3.3L turbocharged V6 engine paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. This engine delivers 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque. The K900 boasts a spacious cabin, advanced technology features, and a host of safety and driver-assist systems. However, it also has its fair share of issues that owners have reported.

**Engine**

**3.3L Turbocharged V6**

* **Engine Stalling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine unexpectedly stalls while driving or idling.
    * Solution: Replacing the fuel filter, intake manifold, or fuel pump may resolve the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A damaged or faulty turbocharger can result in reduced engine power, whistling noises, or increased oil consumption.
    * Solution: Replacing the turbocharger is typically necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip gears or hesitate when shifting, causing power loss or rough shifting.
    * Solution: Fluid replacement or transmission repair/replacement may be required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut or Shock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Worn or faulty struts or shocks can lead to a rough ride, excessive body roll, and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replacing the affected struts or shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges quickly even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Identifying and repairing or replacing the faulty electrical component causing the drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rust on Wheel Wells:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature rust development in the wheel well areas.
    * Solution: Rust repair and repainting the affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Lag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The touchscreen infotainment system may experience delays, freezing, or unresponsive behavior.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacing the infotainment unit may be necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Kia K900 has several potential issues that owners have encountered. These issues range from relatively minor inconveniences like infotainment system lag to more serious concerns such as engine stalling or transmission slipping. The severity and cost of the repairs can vary widely depending on the specific problem. Regular maintenance and timely repairs are recommended to address these issues and maintain the vehicle's performance and reliability.
