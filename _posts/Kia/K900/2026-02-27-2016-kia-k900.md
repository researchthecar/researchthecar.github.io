# 2016 Kia K900 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Kia K900 is a luxury flagship sedan known for its spacious interior and smooth ride quality. While offering substantial value and comfort on the used market, prospective buyers should review available owner reports regarding electrical accessories, lighting, and interior trim materials to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6

The 3.8-liter V6 engine produces approximately 311 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque, offering smooth and adequate power for daily driving. This powertrain option is generally considered reliable, though owners should verify that all factory safety campaigns have been completed.

### 5.0L V8

The 5.0-liter V8 engine delivers a robust 420 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque for strong acceleration and effortless highway passing. Maintenance on this larger powerplant can be slightly more demanding, but it remains a solid choice for buyers seeking V8 performance.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels across both engine configurations. This gearbox operates reliably under normal conditions with proper fluid maintenance.

## Exterior

### Headlights and Lighting

Owners have reported intermittent lighting issues that can impair night visibility.

**Headlight Flickering and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights operated in auto mode may flicker or shut off completely while driving at night.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or qualified technician to inspect the lighting control modules and wiring harnesses.
- **Cost:** $700

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim

Interior material degradation, specifically dashboard warping, has been noted by some drivers.

**Dashboard Bubbling and Rippling**
- **Symptoms:** The leather dashboard material may begin to bubble, ripple, or tear around the head-up display and passenger areas.
- **Solution:** Inspect the dashboard condition prior to purchase; severely damaged trim may require professional upholstery repair or dashboard replacement.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V051000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K900&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The Hydraulic Electronic Control Unit (HECU) module could malfunction and cause an electrical short, which could result in an engine compartment fire. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse for the HECU circuit board, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V210000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K900&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Loss of headlight illumination may reduce visibility and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace the multi-function switch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V652000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K900&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The Hydraulic Electronic Control Unit (HECU) may experience an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the HECU fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Kia K900 provides high-end luxury features and strong engine choices at an accessible price point on the used market. Buyers should carefully inspect the vehicle's electrical lighting systems and interior leather dashboard trim during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid unexpected repair costs.
