### Overview

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Kia K900 demonstrates a generally strong reliability record, particularly concerning its powertrain. The 3.8L V6 and 5.0L V8 engines, along with the 8-speed automatic transmission, are robust and do not show significant widespread issues. However, potential owners should be aware of isolated concerns within the electrical and suspension systems. The infotainment system can occasionally exhibit software glitches, and the adaptive air suspension may develop leaks over time, leading to costly repairs. These issues are not universal but represent the most commonly cited technical problems for this model year. Overall, the K900 offers a reliable luxury experience with a few specific points to monitor.
