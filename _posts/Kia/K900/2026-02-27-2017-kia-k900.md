# 2017 Kia K900

### Overview
The 2017 Kia K900, a full-size luxury sedan, aimed to offer a premium experience with a focus on comfort and advanced features. It was primarily offered with a potent V6 engine, paired with an automatic transmission. While generally considered a reliable vehicle, like any car, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical problems beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.8L V6
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning light, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, or crankshaft seals.
- **Cost:** $250 - $600


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic
**Hesitation or Shifting Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky shifts, delayed engagement, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, or potential internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing or Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, freezes, or restarts randomly.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Premature Battery Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting the vehicle, battery warning light, electrical components malfunctioning.
- **Solution:** Testing and replacement of the battery, or diagnosis of parasitic draw.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Trim
**No Major Issues**
- **Symptoms:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Solution:** No widespread issues reported.
- **Cost:** No widespread issues reported.


## Interior

### Leather Upholstery
**Premature Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Cracking or excessive wear on leather seats, especially on driver's side.
- **Solution:** Professional cleaning, conditioning, or seat cover replacement.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600


### Climate Control System
**Malfunctioning A/C or Heater**
- **Symptoms:** Inconsistent temperature, weak airflow, or complete failure of heating or cooling.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair/replacement of blower motor, blend door actuators, or refrigerant recharge.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Kia K900 generally presents a good reliability record for its class, with the powertrain components (engine and transmission) being relatively robust. The most commonly cited issues tend to be related to the electrical system, particularly the infotainment unit, and some instances of premature wear on interior leather. Minor coolant and oil leaks from the V6 engine have been reported, but these are often manageable repairs. Owners should be mindful of potential battery drain issues and ensure regular maintenance of the climate control system. Overall, the K900 offers a comfortable and feature-rich experience, and with proper care, it can be a dependable luxury sedan.
