# 2020 Kia K900 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Kia K900 represents a rare and luxurious flagship sedan offering high-end comfort and powerful engine options. For used-car shoppers considering this sophisticated cruiser, understanding its powertrain lineup and monitoring key components can help ensure a smooth ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Twin-Turbocharged V6

This 3.3-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 365 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration. It serves as the primary powerplant for the K900, balancing refined luxury performance with modern forced-induction capability.

### 5.0L Naturally Aspirated V8

The 5.0-liter V8 engine delivers a robust 420 horsepower and 376 lb-ft of torque for effortless power delivery. Known for its traditional smooth performance, this larger displacement option suits buyers prioritizing classic flagship sedan character.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels or available all-wheel-drive system. It offers seamless gear changes designed to complement the vehicle's luxury ride quality.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V169000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger:Hoses/Plumbing](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K900&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** An oil leak in the presence of hot engine components can increase the risk of an engine compartment fire while driving.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the left turbocharger oil feed pipe and hose assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Kia K900 is a well-equipped luxury sedan backed by robust powertrain options. Prospective buyers should inspect the vehicle thoroughly, but overall, it remains a compelling alternative to traditional European flagships.
