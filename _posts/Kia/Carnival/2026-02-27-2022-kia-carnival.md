# 2022 Kia Carnival Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Kia Carnival brings a modern, SUV-inspired aesthetic to the minivan segment, backed by a generous feature set and a robust powertrain. However, prospective buyers should carefully review owner-reported concerns regarding body hardware, sliding door mechanisms, and fuel system components. While it offers strong family utility and smooth highway performance, checking for completed dealer service and active recall updates is essential.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 GDI

Powered by a 3.5-liter V6 direct-injection engine producing around 290 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, this powerplant offers brisk acceleration and confident highway passing. Overall, it provides dependable daily performance, though buyers must remain mindful of fuel system service bulletins.

**Crossover Fuel Pipe Leak**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a strong fuel odor or raw fuel leaks originating from the high-pressure crossover fuel pipe connection to the fuel rail, presenting a potential fire hazard.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected by a dealer to ensure any applicable fuel pipe recalls or replacements have been properly performed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired exclusively with the V6 engine, the 8-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth and predictable shifts suited for family hauling. No widespread or recurring mechanical complaint patterns stand out for this transmission.

## Exterior

### Sliding Doors and Latches

The interior offers versatile seating and amenities, though automated convenience features require careful verification during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Power Sliding Door Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Power sliding doors may fail to respond to buttons, reverse improperly against obstacles, or refuse to latch securely, triggering continuous dashboard warnings.
- **Solution:** Inspect door sensors, wiring harnesses, and latch assemblies for wear or obstruction, and have damaged components adjusted or replaced.

### Body Hardware and Windshield

Exterior panels and trim elements are generally durable, but prospective owners should check glass integrity and exterior weatherstripping.

**Windshield Cracking**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances of front windshields developing sudden, extensive cracks across the glass from minor impacts.
- **Solution:** Inspect the windshield thoroughly before purchase and replace it through an experienced auto glass professional if compromised.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V908000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Electronic Lock/Latch Actuator](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A power sliding door that fails to latch may open while moving, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the passenger-side power sliding door release actuator's drum and holder inside the remote controller assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V277000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Fuel Injection System:Fuel Rail](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A damaged fuel rail tube may leak fuel, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect, and if necessary, replace the multi-port injection fuel rail, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V179000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Water accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and verify whether the vehicle is equipped with a Genuine Kia accessory trailer tow hitch assembly. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V236000: Structure:Body:Door](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The auto-reverse feature for the power sliding doors (PSD) may not activate in all situations. A power sliding door that does not auto-reverse can close on an occupant, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the power sliding door control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V025000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached roof molding can create a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the roof molding and replace or secure it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V232000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The vehicle presents an appealing option for families seeking minivan utility with contemporary styling. Buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical inspection, verifying that fuel system recalls have been completed and confirming that sliding doors and electronic convenience items operate without hesitation.
