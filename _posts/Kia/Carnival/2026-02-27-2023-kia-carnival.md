# 2023 Kia Carnival Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Kia Carnival presents a modern and spacious option for families seeking a versatile multi-purpose vehicle. However, owner reports highlight several recurring concerns, particularly regarding power-operated sliding door malfunctions, fuel system odors, and seat belt hardware performance. Potential buyers should carefully inspect these areas during a test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 3.5L V6 GDI

The 3.5-liter gasoline direct-injection V6 engine produces 290 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, offering strong and smooth acceleration for highway cruising and hauling. It provides dependable everyday performance, though owners have reported occasional fuel odor concerns requiring inspection.

**Fuel Odor and Leak**
- **Symptoms:** An abnormally strong fuel smell is noticeable inside or around the vehicle during operation or after startup.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a qualified service center or dealership to inspect fuel injection lines and related components for leaks.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the front wheels, balancing highway efficiency with responsive gear changes.

## Exterior

### Sliding Door Latch and Sensors

Electrical components related to convenience and body features generally perform well, though automated sliding doors are a frequent source of owner grievances.

**Automatic Sliding Door Pinch Sensor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power sliding doors fail to reverse direction upon encountering resistance, continuing to close or jamming during operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect door track alignment, clear debris, and have the pinch sensors and latch mechanisms diagnosed by a technician.

### Sliding Door Structure and Actuators

Exterior body hardware is generally modern and robust, but motorized entry points require careful pre-purchase checks.

**Sliding Door Jamming**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger-side sliding door gets stuck in open or closed positions, preventing normal vehicle access.
- **Solution:** Lubricate tracks and inspect mechanical cables and the door actuator motor for binding or failure.

## Safety

### Seat Belts and Pretensioners

Safety equipment meets standard modern requirements, though specific complaints point to latching and retraction faults in the rear seating rows.

**Rear Seat Belt Retraction and Latch Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Second or third-row seat belts fail to retract properly, or buckles fail to latch securely during sudden stops.
- **Solution:** Have the seat belt assembly and pretensioners inspected and replaced by a dealer if latching mechanisms fail.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V179000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Water accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and verify whether the vehicle is equipped with a Genuine Kia accessory trailer tow hitch assembly. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V236000: Structure:Body:Door](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The auto-reverse feature for the power sliding doors (PSD) may not activate in all situations. A power sliding door that does not auto-reverse can close on an occupant, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the power sliding door control module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V025000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A detached roof molding can create a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the roof molding and replace or secure it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V232000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Carnival&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Kia Carnival is a comfortable and highly functional family hauler backed by a competent V6 powertrain. While most owners enjoy its utility, used car buyers should pay special attention to the operation of the power sliding doors, check for any fuel odors, and verify that all seat belts latch securely before purchasing.
