**Introduction:**
The 2022 Kia EV6 is an all-electric crossover that offers impressive performance and a spacious interior. It is available in two trim levels: Wind and GT-Line. The Wind trim is powered by a single electric motor that produces 225 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, while the GT-Line trim features a dual-motor setup that cranks out 320 horsepower and 446 lb-ft of torque. Both trims come standard with a 77.4 kWh lithium-ion battery pack that provides an estimated range of 310 miles for the Wind trim and 274 miles for the GT-Line trim.
