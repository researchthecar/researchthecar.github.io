# 2024 Kia Niro EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Kia Niro EV, buyers will find a modern, efficient electric crossover that generally offers solid daily usability. While owner reports to safety authorities are relatively light, a few electrical and seating concerns have surfaced that prospective buyers should keep on their radar during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Electric Motor (201 hp)

The Niro EV features a front-mounted permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing 201 horsepower and 288 lb-ft of torque, powered by a 64.8-kWh battery pack. This powertrain option provides brisk acceleration and reliable electric propulsion for everyday commuting.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Power is sent to the front wheels via a single-speed direct drive automatic transmission. This simpler gearless setup offers smooth, seamless acceleration and typically requires minimal maintenance compared to traditional multi-speed gearboxes.

## Electrical

### Body Electrical and Accessories

Minor convenience features and connectivity systems can occasionally experience software hiccups or slow mechanical response times.

**Slow Folding Mirrors and App Connectivity Errors**
- **Symptoms:** Power-folding side mirrors may operate sluggishly, and companion smartphone application features can display remote command errors.
- **Solution:** Inspect mirror motors for binding or electrical resistance and check software modules or reset connected vehicle services.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Owner reports indicate occasional electronic or sensor glitches affecting seat belt monitoring systems.

**Seat Belt Warning Light Illumination**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted instances where the seat belt warning light illuminates unexpectedly on the instrument cluster while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the seat belt sensors, wiring harness, and restraint control module for intermittent faults.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V057000: Power Train:Driveline:Driveshaft](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro+EV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the drive shaft, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V024000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro+EV&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The floor wiring assembly beneath the front passenger seat may become damaged, which can prevent the front air bags and seat belts from deploying properly or cause an unintended side curtain air bag deployment. Air bags and seat belts that deploy improperly or an air bag deploying unintentionally can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect, replace and reroute the floor wiring assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Kia Niro EV is a compelling used electric vehicle choice, backed by an efficient powertrain and modern features. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test all interior electronics, power accessories, and safety warning indicators to ensure everything operates as expected.
