**Introduction:**

The 2022 Kia Niro EV is an all-electric compact crossover SUV that offers impressive range, a comfortable interior, and a user-friendly infotainment system. It comes with a single electric motor that produces 201 horsepower and 265 lb-ft of torque. The Niro EV is known for its fuel efficiency, practicality, and spacious interior. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Electric Motor**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

**Rear Suspension Noise**
Description/Symptoms: A rattling or clunking noise coming from the rear suspension when driving over bumps or rough roads.
Solution: The rear suspension components may need to be inspected, tightened, or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The Niro EV's battery draining rapidly, even when the vehicle is parked and turned off.
Solution: The issue may be caused by a software bug or a faulty battery. The software may need to be updated or the battery may need to be replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Wiper Blade Operation**
Description/Symptoms: The wiper blades may not move or operate properly during rain or snow.
Solution: The wiper motor or wiring harness may need to be inspected and replaced if necessary.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Center Console Trim Issue**
Description/Symptoms: The center console trim may become loose or rattle while driving.
Solution: The trim may need to be tightened or replaced.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2022 Kia Niro EV is a popular electric vehicle, but it has also experienced some issues. The most common issues reported by owners include rear suspension noise, battery drain issues, wiper blade operation problems, and center console trim issues. While some of these issues can be minor and easily fixed, others can be more severe and expensive to repair. Overall, the severity and cost of the issues reported on the Niro EV vary, so it is important to research potential problems before purchasing the vehicle.
