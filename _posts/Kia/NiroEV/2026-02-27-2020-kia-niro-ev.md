# 2020 Kia Niro EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Kia Niro EV is a practical and efficient all-electric crossover offering substantial driving range and everyday usability. As a battery-electric vehicle, it replaces traditional internal combustion powertrains with a dedicated electric motor and lithium-ion battery system, making it an appealing option for used EV shoppers looking to transition away from gasoline.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (201 hp)

The Niro EV features a 201-horsepower permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing 291 lb-ft of torque, driving the front wheels. This electric propulsion system provides smooth, immediate acceleration and generally strong reliability with fewer moving parts than a traditional engine.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Reduction Gearbox

Power is transmitted to the front wheels via a single-speed reduction gear transmission. This direct-drive setup operates without traditional gear shifting, contributing to a seamless driving experience and minimal mechanical maintenance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V899000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery:Thermal Management:Fluid/Coolant](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro+EV&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Coolant contacting the EPCU circuit board may cause a loss of drive power, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the EPCU as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V426000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro+EV&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The circuit board for the rearview camera may become damaged and cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Kia Niro EV presents a solid used-car proposition for buyers interested in an affordable electric vehicle with minimal mechanical complexity. While ongoing owner vigilance regarding high-voltage battery health and charging equipment is recommended, the lack of widespread powertrain complaints suggests a dependable daily driver.
