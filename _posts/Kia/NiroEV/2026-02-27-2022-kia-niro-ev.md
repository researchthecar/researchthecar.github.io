# 2022 Kia Niro EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Niro EV is an all-electric compact crossover that offers efficient everyday driving with a practical hatchback layout. Buyers exploring the used market will find a generally modern electric platform, though evaluating battery health, charging equipment, and basic electrical systems is always recommended during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor

The Niro EV features a permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing approximately 201 horsepower and 291 lb-ft of torque. This powertrain provides brisk acceleration and high efficiency, generally proving reliable with few moving parts compared to internal combustion engines.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Reduction Gear Direct Drive

Power is sent to the front wheels via a single-speed reduction gear transmission. This direct-drive system eliminates traditional shifting hesitation and has proven mechanically straightforward and dependable.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V358000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Shock Protection](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro+EV&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The high voltage battery safety plug may overheat and melt, which can cause a loss of drive power or fire. A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash, and a fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the safety plug, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V426000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro+EV&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The circuit board for the rearview camera may become damaged and cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Niro EV presents a solid option for shoppers seeking an affordable, efficient electric crossover. While overall mechanical and electric stability is high, prospective buyers should thoroughly test all onboard electronics, charging ports, and climate functions to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
