# 2021 Kia Niro EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Kia Niro EV offers a practical and efficient fully electric driving experience for everyday commuters and small families. While it benefits from a clean layout and modern features, used car buyers should always review available owner feedback regarding electrical and charging behavior. A pre-purchase inspection focused on high-voltage components and auxiliary electronics will help ensure a confident buying decision.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Motor (201 hp)

This model features a single permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing approximately 201 horsepower and 291 lb-ft of torque, powered by a 64 kWh lithium-ion battery pack. It provides responsive acceleration and strong overall efficiency for daily driving.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Reduction Gearbox

A single-speed reduction gear transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels without traditional gear shifts. This setup is mechanically simple and generally requires very little maintenance over the vehicle's lifespan.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V426000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Niro+EV&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The circuit board for the rearview camera may become damaged and cause the rearview camera image not to display. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's view of what is behind the vehicle.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Kia Niro EV is a compelling choice for buyers transitioning to electric mobility, blending strong everyday performance with efficient packaging. Prospective owners should verify the health of the electrical systems and ensure all software updates have been properly applied during a test drive.
