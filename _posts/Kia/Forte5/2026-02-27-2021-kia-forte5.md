### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Kia Forte5 demonstrates good overall reliability, with no major widespread mechanical issues reported for its engine or transmission options. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, as with many vehicles, potential battery issues over time. These are generally manageable and not indicative of systemic design flaws. The exterior and interior components have also shown good durability. With routine maintenance and attention to potential electrical quirks, the 2021 Forte5 is expected to be a dependable vehicle for its owners.
