**Introduction**

The 2022 Kia Forte5 is a compact hatchback powered by a standard 147-hp, 2.0L inline-4 engine paired with a CVT or a 195-hp, 1.6L turbocharged inline-4 engine paired with a 7-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT). It's praised for its stylish exterior, spacious interior, and advanced safety features, while common complaints include firm ride quality and limited rear-seat legroom.

**Engine**

**2.0L Inline-4**

* **Misfiring under load**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stumbles or hesitates under acceleration, accompanied by check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils, spark plugs, or fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
* **Timing chain noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or grinding noise from the engine when starting or accelerating.
    * Solution: Replace faulty timing chain or tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200+

**1.6L Turbocharged Inline-4**

* **Turbocharger failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive exhaust smoke, or oil leaks.
    * Solution: Replace or rebuild faulty turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+
* **High-pressure fuel pump failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling or difficulty starting, accompanied by check engine light.
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+

**Transmission**

**CVT**

* **Shuddering or hesitation during shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or shudders when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace faulty valve body.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+

**7-Speed DCT**

* **Clutch shuddering or slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering or slipping during acceleration or shifting.
    * Solution: Replace faulty clutch packs or update transmission software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2000+
* **Shifting delays or hard shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission takes too long to shift or shifts abruptly.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace faulty solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Loose or worn sway bar links**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the suspension, especially over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replace faulty sway bar links.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly when the car is parked.
    * Solution: Replace faulty battery or alternator, or find and fix any parasitic power draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+
* **Electrical system malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random electrical problems, such as flickering lights, malfunctioning gauges, or power window issues.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty electrical components or wiring.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies widely depending on the issue

**Interior**

* **Loose or rattling interior trim**
    * Description/Symptoms: Interior panels or trim pieces rattle or squeak over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Tighten or replace loose or damaged trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* **HVAC system problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control, poor airflow, or excessive noise.
    * Solution: Repair or replace faulty HVAC components, such as the blower motor, compressor, or expansion valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000+

**Exterior**

* **Fog light malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fog lights not working or flickering.
    * Solution: Replace faulty fog light bulbs, wiring, or switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Recalls**

* NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-123
    * Description: Incorrectly programmed airbag control module may prevent airbags from deploying in a crash.
    * Solution: Update airbag control module software.
* NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-169
    * Description: Faulty brake booster vacuum hose may cause loss of braking assist.
    * Solution: Replace faulty brake booster vacuum hose.

**Summary**

While the 2022 Kia Forte5 offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and advanced safety features, it has several known issues related to its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor annoyances and others requiring significant repairs. The cost of addressing these issues can range from relatively low to quite expensive, so it's important for potential buyers to be aware of them before making a purchase decision.
