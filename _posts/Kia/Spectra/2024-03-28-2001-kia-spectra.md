**Introduction:**

The 2001 Kia Spectra is a compact sedan that was praised for its affordable price, roomy interior, and fuel efficiency. It was available with a choice of two engines and two transmission options:

* **Engine Options:**
    * 1.8L Inline-4 (126 hp, 131 lb-ft)
    * 2.0L Inline-4 (138 hp, 140 lb-ft)
* **Transmission Options:**
    * 5-speed Manual Transmission
    * 4-speed Automatic Transmission

People appreciate the Spectra's easy handling, comfortable seats, and spacious trunk. However, they note that the interior is basic and the performance is underwhelming.

**Engine**

**1.8L Inline-4**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket and associated seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Exhaust leak, whistling or rattling noise
    * Solution: Replace exhaust manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Inline-4**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**5-speed Manual Transmission**

* **Shifter Linkage Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loose or sloppy shifter
    * Solution: Replace shifter linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**4-speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic or delayed shifting, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounting Plate Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when turning, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace strut mounting plates
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, especially when parked
    * Solution: Test and replace defective electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery warning light on, dim headlights, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handle breaks or sticks
    * Solution: Replace door handle assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Power Window Switch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Window doesn't respond to switch, intermittent window operation
    * Solution: Replace power window switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Blower Resistor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blower speeds not working correctly, no heat or A/C
    * Solution: Replace HVAC blower resistor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2001 Kia Spectra is a reliable and affordable vehicle with some common issues. The majority of the issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. However, potential buyers should be aware of the more severe issues, such as head gasket failure and transmission problems, before purchasing.
