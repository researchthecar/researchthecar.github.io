# 2022 Kia Sorento

### Overview
The 2022 Kia Sorento, a popular three-row SUV, generally presents a strong reliability profile, building on Kia's reputation for quality. It offers a range of powertrain options, including efficient hybrid and plug-in hybrid variants alongside traditional gasoline engines. Owners generally report satisfaction with its features, comfort, and practicality. While most systems perform as expected, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where minor concerns have been noted by a segment of owners. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for prospective and current owners.

### Infotainment System
**Occasional glitches or unresponsiveness**

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Kia Sorento demonstrates good overall reliability, with most systems performing commendably. The primary areas of concern, though not widespread, involve occasional transmission hesitation with the DCT, minor infotainment system glitches, and a few reports of premature 12V battery failure. These issues are generally addressable through software updates or component replacement and do not indicate systemic flaws across the model range. The various engine options, including the hybrid and PHEV variants, have not shown significant common problems. For potential buyers, the Sorento remains a solid choice, with these minor points being the most frequently cited technical concerns.
