# 2022 Kia Sorento Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Sorento offers a compelling package for used-car shoppers, combining a modern interior, flexible three-row seating, and an array of available engine and transmission choices. While many owners enjoy its comfort and features, prospective buyers should review owner complaints regarding powertrain hesitation, unexpected electronic warnings, and occasional body or electrical glitches. A thorough pre-purchase inspection and test drive can help ensure any potential mechanical or electrical concerns are identified before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder

A naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine making around 191 horsepower, providing dependable baseline performance for daily commuting without standout reliability complications.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

A robust 2.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing approximately 281 horsepower, delivering strong acceleration but requiring careful verification of turbo health.

**Turbocharger Failure and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may hesitate or stall momentarily at stops before resuming power, eventually illuminating the check engine light when the turbocharger fails.
- **Solution:** Inspect turbocharger operation, check for diagnostic codes, and replace the turbo assembly if internal failure is confirmed.

### 1.6L Turbo Hybrid / Plug-In Hybrid

A fuel-efficient electrified powertrain combining a 1.6-liter turbocharged engine with an electric motor, offering excellent efficiency alongside smooth power delivery.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 8-speed automatic transmission paired with standard gasoline engines, generally offering smooth gear shifts when operating correctly.

**Transmission Slipping and Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission may slip, jerk intermittently, or fail to accelerate properly, occasionally limiting vehicle speed or causing a loss of reverse gear.
- **Solution:** Update transmission control modules, check fluid condition, or perform mechanical repairs if internal component wear is present.

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

A quick-shifting wet dual-clutch automatic transmission mated to the 2.5L turbocharged engine, providing sporty gear changes and efficient power transfer.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission utilized in hybrid variants, providing reliable and seamless transitions between electric and gas power.

## Steering

### Steering System

Provides speed-sensitive steering assist, though intermittent electronic indicator warnings and mechanical noise have been reported by some drivers.

**Electronic Power Steering Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The Electronic Power Steering (EPS) warning light may flash intermittently, accompanied by clicking noises or temporary stiffness when turning.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering electrical connections, update control software, or replace faulty steering column components.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Manages core vehicle electronics, charging systems, and accessory features, though certain sensor faults can trigger unexpected limp modes.

**Idle Stop-And-Go and Acceleration Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** The Auto Start-Stop system warning light may flash, leading to sudden loss of propulsion, throttle failure, or an inability to restart the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnose software control modules, test battery and charging systems, and replace faulty sensors or wiring harnesses as needed.

## Exterior

### Sunroof

Enhances cabin light and ventilation with a large glass panel, though rare spontaneous breakage reports warrant careful pre-purchase inspection.

**Panoramic Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof may spontaneously fracture or explode while driving or parked, creating a loud noise and showering glass into the cabin.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for stress or alignment issues.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V202000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The oil supply pipe to the turbocharger may crack, which could result in an oil leak in the engine compartment. An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the turbocharger oil supply pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V509000: Tires:Temporary/Emergency Spare Tire](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Impaired anti-lock braking or traction control systems can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the spare tire, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V703000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the vehicle, and, as necessary, replace the fuse and/or tow hitch harness module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V760000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V610000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not properly display an image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera housing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V649000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) blower motor wiring harness may be inadequate, which can result in a fire. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the wiring harness and blower motor resistor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Sorento remains a versatile choice for families seeking modern styling and practical three-row capability. While most vehicles provide trouble-free service, used-car buyers should pay close attention during test drives to verify smooth transmission operation, responsive engine acceleration, and proper functioning of electronic and safety systems. Ensuring a comprehensive mechanical inspection prior to purchase will help mitigate potential risks.
