# 2016 Kia Sorento Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Kia Sorento is a popular midsize crossover offering spacious seating and a comfortable ride, but used car buyers should approach certain powertrain and body configurations with caution. Owner complaints highlight significant concerns regarding engine reliability, specific paint adhesion failures on white pearl models, and electrical or shifting anomalies. Careful pre-purchase inspections and a thorough review of maintenance records are strongly recommended before buying.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder

The base 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine produces around 185 horsepower, providing adequate daily driving performance. However, this engine family has attracted notable owner complaints concerning cooling system leaks and internal head gasket wear.

**Head Gasket Failure and Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report engine overheating, loss of coolant, and white smoke from the exhaust due to head gasket failure.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis, head gasket replacement, or potential engine rebuild/replacement.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles unexpectedly lose motive power or stall while driving at highway speeds, accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing of fuel, ignition, and emission systems; software updates or sensor replacements may be required.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 240 horsepower, offering stronger acceleration and mid-range torque. Buyers should ensure regular oil change intervals were maintained to prevent turbocharger complications.

### 3.3L V6

The 3.3-liter V6 engine delivers a smooth 290 horsepower, making it an excellent choice for towing and highway passing. It represents a robust powertrain option for the Sorento when properly serviced.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the various engine choices to deliver smooth gear changes under normal operating conditions. Some owners have reported shift quality issues that may require software updates.

**Erratic Shifting and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice hard jerking motions when accelerating from a stop or transitioning between first and second gear.
- **Solution:** Transmission control module reprogramming or mechanical inspection of internal components.

## Brakes

### Power Train and Shifter

The drivetrain setup distributes power reliably to the front or all four wheels, though mechanical interlock components around the shifter can experience premature wear.

**Shifter Stuck in Park**
- **Symptoms:** The gear selector gets stuck, preventing the driver from shifting out of park without manually engaging the shift lock release override.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty shifter assemblies, brake-shift interlock switches, or associated control modules.

## Exterior

### Structure and Paint

Exterior panels generally hold up well, though aesthetic cosmetic defects like paint peeling on specific factory color codes have been a persistent owner grievance.

**Paint Peeling and Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** The clear coat and factory paint—particularly on Snow White Pearl models—begin to bubble, flake, and peel away in large sheets from the hood, roof, and doors.
- **Solution:** Professional repainting of affected body panels, as touch-up paint is generally insufficient for large-scale delamination.

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical systems control numerous convenience and safety features, with lighting components occasionally exhibiting intermittent flickering or connection loss.

**Headlight and Tail Light Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights or tail lights intermittently flicker, go out completely, or fail to operate properly despite functional fuses and bulbs.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring harnesses, sockets, and headlight switch assemblies for corrosion or loose connections.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V907000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Kia has notified owners, and dealers will inspect and replace the fuel pipe, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V446000: Seat Belts:Front:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the front passenger seat belt cannot be latched, an occupant sitting in the front passenger seat has an increased risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace the front passenger seat belt buckle cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V770000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's seatback frame may have been produced with inadequate weld joints and, as a result, the seatback may not function as designed in the event of a rear-end collision. In the event of a crash, the driver seatback frame could break and fail to restrain a seat occupant, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the welds and replace the driver seatback frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V862000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, " Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." If the trailer brake lights stay illuminated while being used, other drivers may be confused, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace the trailer tow hitch harness, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V480000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A component of the rear suspension may not be properly welded. The improper weld may break, affecting the rear suspension alignment, thereby increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the welds at the rear suspension cross-member, replacing the rear cross-member assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V089000: Vehicle Speed Control:Accelerator Pedal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Due to an improperly soldered component in the accelerator pedal assembly, the driver may not be able to accelerate the vehicle. If the vehicle is unable to be accelerated, there is an increased risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace the accelerator assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V612000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Lever And Linkage:Column Shift](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A vehicle rollaway increases the risk of a crash and injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the automatic transmission shift lever assembly components, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V703000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the vehicle, and, as necessary, replace the fuse and/or tow hitch harness module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Kia Sorento offers compelling family utility and strong V6 performance, but buyers must remain vigilant about potential mechanical hurdles. Pay close attention to engine coolant levels, test drive thoroughly to check transmission shift quality, and carefully inspect Snow White Pearl models for paint delamination. Securing a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an independent mechanic is essential to verifying vehicle health.
