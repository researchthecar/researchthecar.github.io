# 2021 Kia Sorento Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Kia Sorento represents a complete redesign year for the popular midsize SUV, bringing sharper styling, an updated interior, and a variety of new powertrain options. As with many first-year redesigns, used-car buyers should weigh the appeal of its modern tech and improved driving dynamics against potential early-production quirks. Reviewing owner experiences and known technical specifications helps prospective buyers identify which configurations offer the smoothest ownership journey.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder

A naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing around 191 horsepower, serving as the base powerplant for lower trims. It offers dependable daily-driver performance, though buyers seeking more robust acceleration often look toward the turbocharged or electrified options.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

A turbocharged 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine generating roughly 281 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for higher trims. Routine maintenance and timely oil changes are critical to ensuring the long-term health of this direct-injection turbocharged platform.

### 1.6L Turbocharged Hybrid / Plug-in Hybrid

A 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder paired with an electric motor system, delivering high fuel efficiency and responsive low-end torque. This electrified option appeals to commuters looking to maximize fuel economy, though buyers should verify the health of the hybrid battery components on pre-owned models.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

An 8-speed traditional automatic transmission mated to the non-turbo and turbocharged 2.5L engines, designed for smooth gear shifts and highway efficiency. Keeping up with fluid service intervals helps maintain crisp shifting behavior.

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

An 8-speed wet dual-clutch automatic transmission paired specifically with the 2.5L turbocharged engine for faster, sportier shifts. Drivers should familiarize themselves with the unique engagement characteristics of dual-clutch systems in stop-and-go traffic.

### 6-Speed Automatic

A 6-speed traditional automatic transmission utilized in the hybrid and plug-in hybrid variants, optimized for seamless power blending between gas and electric sources. It provides a conventional shifting feel compared to continuously variable alternatives.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V519000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V703000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the vehicle, and, as necessary, replace the fuse and/or tow hitch harness module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V760000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V649000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) blower motor wiring harness may be inadequate, which can result in a fire. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the wiring harness and blower motor resistor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Kia Sorento is a versatile and stylish midsize SUV that offers plenty of configuration choices to suit different driving needs. While its first-year status warrants a thorough pre-purchase inspection of its electronics and mechanical systems, well-maintained models generally provide strong value, comfort, and modern features for used-car shoppers.
