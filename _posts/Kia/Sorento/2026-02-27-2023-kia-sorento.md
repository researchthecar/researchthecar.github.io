# 2023 Kia Sorento Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Kia Sorento offers a compelling package for used-car shoppers, blending contemporary styling, a feature-rich interior, and flexible seating configurations. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding powertrain performance, electrical glitches, and miscellaneous body components. While many drivers enjoy its fuel efficiency and everyday usability, checking service history and confirming that all relevant recall work has been performed is essential.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The base 2.5-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine produces 191 horsepower, providing adequate power for daily commuting and respectable fuel economy for a midsize SUV.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners report unexpected engine stalling while driving on highways or city streets, accompanied by dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect the fuel delivery, ignition, and sensor systems to diagnose underlying electrical or mechanical faults.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 2.5-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine generates 281 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, offering significantly stronger acceleration and passing power.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the base engine, this 8-speed conventional automatic transmission delivers smooth and reliable gear changes during everyday driving.

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

Exclusive to models with the turbocharged engine, this 8-speed wet dual-clutch transmission offers rapid shifts but has drawn owner reports concerning erratic behavior and mechanical breakdowns.

**Transmission Shifting and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted harsh bucking, failure to shift properly, getting stuck in lower gears, or complete transmission failure requiring replacement.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid levels and condition; severe mechanical failures may require professional transmission overhaul or replacement.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides adequate stopping power for this midsize crossover, but front rotors and pads may require attention sooner than expected under heavy use.

**Brake Pulsation and Premature Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note abnormal brake pedal vibration, pulsating sensations during stopping, and premature wear on rotors and pads.
- **Solution:** Resurface or replace brake rotors and install new brake pads when pulsation or excessive wear is detected.

## Steering

### Steering Column

The electric power steering system provides light and adjustable feedback for parking and highway cruising, though early production columns have generated noise complaints.

**Steering Squeaks and Assist Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced abnormal squealing noises from the steering column, stiffness, or temporary loss of smooth steering assist.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or replace worn steering column components and inspect electronic power steering assist connections.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & HVAC

The Sorento's cabin relies heavily on complex electronics and infotainment integration, which can occasionally experience software or hardware malfunctions.

**Warning Lights and Component Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Random dashboard warning lights, communication errors with advanced driver assistance systems, and sudden HVAC system failures have been reported.
- **Solution:** Update software modules or replace faulty sensors and blower motor resistors as diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Glass and Sunroof

Exterior glass and trim components generally hold up well, though rare reports of spontaneous breakage warrant careful inspection of large glass surfaces before purchase.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Isolated incidents where side windows or the panoramic sunroof glass shattered or imploded unexpectedly without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged glass panels and inspect window/sunroof seals and frames for stress points.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V579000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A detached air bag warning label will not warn occupants about the risks of air bag deployment, increasing the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the driver and front passenger sun visors, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V610000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera that does not properly display an image can reduce the driver's rear view, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the rearview camera housing, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V649000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) blower motor wiring harness may be inadequate, which can result in a fire. A fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the wiring harness and blower motor resistor, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Kia Sorento is a versatile and well-equipped crossover that appeals to families and commuters alike. While many owners experience trouble-free driving, used-car buyers should thoroughly test-drive the vehicle to check for transmission smoothness, steering noises, and brake performance. Verifying service records and ensuring all open recalls have been addressed will help secure a dependable ownership experience.
