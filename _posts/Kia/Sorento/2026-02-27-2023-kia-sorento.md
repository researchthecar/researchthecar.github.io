# 2023 Kia Sorento

### Overview
The 2023 Kia Sorento, a popular three-row SUV, generally presents a strong reliability profile, building on Kia's reputation for quality. It offers a range of powertrain options, including efficient hybrid and plug-in hybrid variants alongside traditional gasoline engines. Owners generally report satisfaction with its features, comfort, and practicality. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the Sorento's common concerns tend to be minor and addressable, with most owners experiencing a dependable ownership experience. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into specific components and their potential reliability.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Kia Sorento demonstrates commendable reliability across its various powertrain options. While minor electrical glitches, particularly with the infotainment system, and occasional premature 12V battery failures have been noted, these are not indicative of systemic powertrain or chassis weaknesses. The engine and transmission options, including the hybrid and PHEV variants, are generally robust. The exterior and interior components also appear to hold up well under typical usage. Overall, the Sorento is a solid choice for families seeking a dependable and feature-rich SUV, with most potential issues being relatively minor and manageable.
