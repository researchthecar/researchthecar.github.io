# 2018 Kia Sorento Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Kia Sorento is a popular midsize SUV offering three rows of seating and a comfortable ride, making it an attractive option for families. However, used car buyers should approach certain model years with care. NHTSA complaints highlight several recurring problem areas, most notably concerning engine performance, electrical glitches, and steering concerns. Being aware of these potential trouble spots will help you thoroughly inspect a prospective vehicle before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

A 2.4-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine producing around 185 horsepower, serving as the base powerplant. Buyers should verify regular oil maintenance and check for engine-related recalls.

**Excessive Oil Consumption and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note unexpected engine stalling, activation of warning lights, and high rates of oil consumption requiring frequent top-offs between oil changes.
- **Solution:** Check service history for completed recall updates such as the Knock Sensor Detection System (KSDS) and consult a mechanic if oil consumption is abnormal.

### 3.3L V6 Engine

A 3.3-liter V6 engine generating approximately 290 horsepower, providing stronger towing capabilities and smoother acceleration for highway driving.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of motive power while driving, accompanied by exhaust issues or warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician diagnose powertrain control modules and exhaust systems for proper operation.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with lower engine trims, generally known for steady shifting behavior.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed automatic transmission matched with V6 models, designed to optimize fuel economy and highway cruising efficiency.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Power steering components help maintain vehicle control, though some owners have experienced mechanical resistance.

**Stiff or Sticky Steering**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel becomes stiff, ratchety, or sticks when navigating curves or driving on expressways for extended periods.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and intermediate shaft for mechanical binding or component wear.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting & Headlights

The electrical system has reported glitches involving exterior lighting controls and random battery drains.

**Headlight and Tail Light Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights or running lights randomly turning off while driving or failing to illuminate when using the auto switch or turn signals.
- **Solution:** Inspect lighting switches, wiring harnesses, and related fuses for loose connections or electrical shorts.

### Structure and Paint

Exterior body panels and factory paint finishes require careful visual inspection for peeling or corrosion spots.

**Paint Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Factory paint, particularly on White Pearl vehicles, begins peeling or delaminating on the hood, roof, and front fenders.
- **Solution:** Inspect the vehicle's exterior finish closely and inquire with local dealers regarding potential manufacturer paint warranty coverages.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V656000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the steering column separates, it can cause a loss of steering ability, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace the cowl crossbar, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V784000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The side curtain air bags on these vehicles have a component that may detach during deployment of the inflatable curtain air bag. If the component detaches during deployment, it may enter the passenger compartment and be a projectile within the vehicle cabin, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver and passenger side curtain air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V703000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sorento&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the vehicle, and, as necessary, replace the fuse and/or tow hitch harness module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2018 Kia Sorento, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is essential. Pay close attention to engine health, oil consumption records, and proper operation of all electrical features and exterior lighting. Verifying that all safety recalls have been completed by a certified dealership will help ensure a safer and more reliable ownership experience.
