**Introduction:**

The 2019 Kia Sportage is a compact SUV available with either a 2.4L inline-four or a 2.0L turbocharged inline-four engine, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and a variety of safety features. However, some owners have reported issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.4L Inline-Four Engine**

**Engine Stalling/Hesitation:**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine stalls or hesitates while driving, especially when accelerating.
* Solution: Replace faulty fuel filter, fuel pump, or mass airflow sensor.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine**

**Timing Chain Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine makes rattling or ticking noise, poor performance, and reduced fuel economy.
* Solution: Replace failed timing chain components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shifting Problems:**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks during shifting, delayed engagement, or slipping gears.
* Solution: Replace faulty valve body, transmission fluid, or transmission filter.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is parked.
* Solution: Replace faulty battery, alternator, or parasitic drain.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Windshield Washer Fluid Leak:**
* Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from the windshield washer reservoir or nozzle.
* Solution: Replace faulty reservoir or nozzle.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Heater/AC Malfunction:**
* Description/Symptoms: Heater or air conditioner does not work or blows warm air.
* Solution: Replace faulty blower motor, compressor, or blend door actuator.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2019 Kia Sportage offers many great features, it has experienced some issues, including engine stalling, timing chain failure, transmission shifting problems, and battery drain. The severity and cost of these issues vary, with some being relatively minor while others can be quite costly. It is recommended that potential buyers research these issues and consider them when making a decision about whether to purchase this vehicle.
