**2007 Kia Sportage**

The 2007 Kia Sportage is a compact SUV available with a 2.0L 4-cylinder or 2.7L V6 engine. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual or 4-speed automatic. The Sportage is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordable price. However, it has also been reported to have several issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L 4-cylinder**
    * **Cylinder Head Gasket Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating engine, white smoke from exhaust
        * Solution: Replace cylinder head gasket
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.7L V6**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling or ticking noise, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-speed Automatic**
    * **Torque Converter Shudder**
        * Description/Symptoms: Vibration or shuddering while driving, especially at low speeds
        * Solution: Replace torque converter
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from suspension, especially over bumps
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway Bar Link Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from suspension, especially while cornering
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Power Window Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows inoperative
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights inoperative or flickering
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Moisture**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation inside tail lights
    * Solution: Reseal or replace tail lights
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Bumper Cover Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Paint peeling or bubbling on bumper cover
    * Solution: Repaint or replace bumper cover
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or vibration from dashboard
    * Solution: Install damping material or tighten loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system inoperative or blowing hot air
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2007 Kia Sportage has several potential issues, including engine and transmission problems, suspension issues, electrical problems, exterior issues, and interior issues. The severity of these issues ranges from minor annoyances to major repairs that can cost thousands of dollars. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues before purchasing a used Sportage.
