# 2024 Kia Sportage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Kia Sportage offers buyers a bold exterior design, a roomy and tech-forward interior, and a choice of efficient powertrain options. While many owners enjoy its modern styling and daily drivability, some early adopters have reported recurring vehicle owner complaints regarding electrical glitches, spontaneous panoramic sunroof shattering, and premature brake wear.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 187 horsepower and 178 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate everyday power. Overall, it serves as a straightforward option for daily commutes, though select owners have experienced unexpected hesitation and drivability concerns.

**Engine Hesitation and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected engine hesitation, severe bucking, and intermittent stalling while driving or attempting to park.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic or dealership inspect powertrain control modules, throttle body operation, and fuel delivery components for diagnostic trouble codes.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Hybrid / Plug-In Hybrid Engine

The 1.6-liter turbocharged hybrid and plug-in hybrid options pair forced induction with electric motors to deliver improved fuel economy and responsive low-end torque. These setups add complexity over the base engine but are generally favored for their efficiency and refined power delivery.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Matched to the non-hybrid 2.5L engine, this traditional 8-speed automatic generally delivers smooth and predictable shifts under normal driving conditions.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the 1.6L turbocharged hybrid configurations, this 6-speed automatic transmission works seamlessly with the electric motor setup to optimize efficiency.

## Brakes

### Brake Rotors

The braking system provides adequate stopping power for daily driving, but early-model rotor wear and warping have been noted by several vehicle owners.

**Premature Rotor Warping**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note steering wheel vibration and pulsating feedback when applying the brakes, often occurring at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Have the brake rotors inspected, resurfaced, or replaced with higher-durability aftermarket pads and rotors if premature wear recurs.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Onboard electronics manage most cabin and driver-assist features, though intermittent locking malfunctions and dashboard warning lights have been reported by some owners.

**Random Dashboard Warning Lights and Gear Shifter Lockout**
- **Symptoms:** All dashboard warning lights illuminate simultaneously, occasionally accompanied by an inability to shift the vehicle out of park.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scans should be performed by a qualified technician to check for software glitches, loose battery grounds, or failing control modules.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior glass and body trim components are generally modern in design, though the panoramic sunroof has drawn multiple owner complaints regarding spontaneous breakage.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced the panoramic glass roof exploding or shattering loudly while driving without any preceding impact from road debris or projectiles.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for stress fractures and work with a dealership to replace the damaged glass under warranty where applicable.

## Safety

### Forward Collision-Avoidance

Driver assistance technologies add a layer of active protection, though occasional false collision warnings and inadvertent emergency braking events have been documented.

**Inappropriate Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automated emergency braking or collision warning system occasionally triggers false alerts or unexpected hard braking during normal transit.
- **Solution:** Clean the windshield camera and radar sensor areas, and consult a dealer to ensure all ADAS software is fully updated.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V025000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sportage&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A detached roof molding can create a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the roof molding and replace or secure it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V422000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sportage&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The circuit board in the motor-driven power steering assembly may short circuit, resulting in a loss of power steering assist. A loss of power steering assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the motor-driven power steering electric power pack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V874000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sportage&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 101, "Controls and Displays." An instrument panel screen that fails to display critical safety information, such as the speedometer or warning lights, can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the tow hitch harness control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Kia Sportage is a compelling compact SUV choice thanks to its striking appearance, comfortable interior, and diverse powertrain lineup. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for common early-ownership grievances, such as brake rotor vibration, electrical warning light anomalies, and the condition of the panoramic sunroof. Prioritizing models with clean pre-purchase inspections and verified service history will help ensure a more confident ownership experience.
