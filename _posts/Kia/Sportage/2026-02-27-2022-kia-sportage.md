# 2022 Kia Sportage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Sportage offers a stylish compact crossover package with practical everyday usability and a solid suite of modern features. However, prospective used buyers should look closely at maintenance history and powertrain integrity, as owner reports highlight notable concerns ranging from sudden engine failures to electrical glitches and body glass issues.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

A 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing roughly 181 horsepower and 175 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate daily acceleration, though buyers should verify regular oil maintenance to mitigate potential internal wear.

**Timing Chain and Internal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may start shaking, emit unusual noises, illuminate the check engine light, or experience catastrophic engine failure due to a jumped timing chain.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis, with severe cases necessitating a complete short block or engine replacement.
- **Cost:** $11,843

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

A 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generating approximately 240 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. It delivers punchier performance for sportier trims, requiring diligent upkeep on turbo components and oil changes.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive. It offers smooth, reliable shifting under normal driving conditions without widespread major mechanical complaints.

## Brakes

### Parking Brake

Standard braking systems handle daily stopping duties reliably, though mechanical parking brake components can occasionally require service.

**Parking Brake Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The parking or emergency brake may unexpectedly stop functioning, compromising vehicle security when parked on inclines.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer or mechanic run diagnostics and replace the failed brake components.

## Electrical

### Battery Terminal and Fuse Block

Manages vehicle charging and power distribution, though certain terminal designs have drawn owner reports regarding electrical connectivity.

**Positive Terminal Fuse Block Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Issues with the positive battery terminal and fusible link assembly can lead to unexpected electrical power loss, starting failures, or intermittent accessory behavior.
- **Solution:** Inspect the terminal connections and replace the fusible link assembly or battery terminal components as necessary.

### Ignition and Security

Steering column and ignition security mechanisms require strict adherence to recommended anti-theft service updates.

**Vulnerability to Theft**
- **Symptoms:** Certain vehicle configurations have been targeted due to well-known security vulnerabilities, potentially leading to attempted or successful theft.
- **Solution:** Ensure all manufacturer anti-theft software updates and physical security countermeasure campaigns have been properly installed.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior glass and panoramic roof options enhance cabin light but require vigilance regarding rare stress fracture reports.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The sunroof glass can suddenly explode or shatter while driving at highway speeds, sending glass fragments into the cabin without prior impact or warning.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged sunroof glass assembly and inspect the surrounding frame for structural stress.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance features designed to prevent collisions, though sensor sensitivity can occasionally cause phantom braking events.

**Unintended Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle's safety system may erroneously activate the brakes without warning or reason while driving at normal speeds in regular traffic conditions.
- **Solution:** Have the forward collision avoidance sensors and software calibrated or inspected by a qualified dealer or technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V703000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sportage&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the vehicle, and, as necessary, replace the fuse and/or tow hitch harness module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Sportage is a comfortable and feature-packed compact crossover, but used car shoppers should perform thorough due diligence. Inspect service records carefully for engine health and ensure all safety and security updates have been addressed by a certified dealer before purchasing.
