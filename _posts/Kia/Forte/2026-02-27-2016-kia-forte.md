# 2016 Kia Forte Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Kia Forte is an affordable compact car offering a smooth ride and decent feature set, making it a popular choice for budget-conscious used-car buyers. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding engine performance, electrical glitches, and headlight operation before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 1.8L I4

The 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 145 horsepower and is paired with efficient transmissions. It provides adequate daily commuting performance, though some owners have reported unexpected stalls and power losses.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Car runs fine at lower speeds, but when traveling over 50 mph and then decelerating or stopping at a traffic light, the engine stalls out.
- **Solution:** Diagnose throttle body, sensors, or fuel delivery components and replace faulty parts as needed.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly loses power while driving on highways or main roads, accompanied by dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect powertrain control modules, fuel system, and ignition components.

### 2.0L I4

The 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine generates around 173 horsepower, offering stronger acceleration than the base engine. It generally maintains standard reliability when properly maintained.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission provides direct gear control for base trims. It is mechanically simple and generally dependable.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth shifts in city and highway driving. While mostly trouble-free, some drivers experience hesitation during low-speed acceleration.

**Acceleration Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable lag or delay in response when pressing the accelerator pedal, particularly between 10 mph and 35 mph.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition, update control modules, or inspect valve body operation.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Electrical complaints center heavily on exterior lighting and switch malfunctions, which can severely compromise nighttime visibility.

**Headlight Failure and Flickering**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights fail to turn on, flicker constantly while driving, or shut off unexpectedly when switching to high beams.
- **Solution:** Inspect the multi-function headlight switch, wiring harnesses, and relay connectors for failure.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V622000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Forte&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the trunk latch assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Kia Forte is a practical used compact car, but buyers should conduct a thorough inspection before purchasing. Pay special attention to engine performance, transmission responsiveness, and the proper operation of all exterior lights to avoid unexpected repair bills.
