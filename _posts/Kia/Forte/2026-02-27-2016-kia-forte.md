# 2016 Kia Forte Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Kia Forte offers budget-conscious compact car buyers a practical daily driver with respectable fuel economy and decent tech features. When shopping for a used model, understanding the available powertrain options and potential historical complaints can help you find a reliable commuter car.

---

## Engine

### 1.8L 4-Cylinder

The 1.8-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 145 horsepower and 130 lb-ft of torque, powering base trims. It provides adequate fuel efficiency for daily commuting with generally straightforward mechanical reliability.

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder

The larger 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine generates around 173 horsepower and 154 lb-ft of torque for higher trims. It offers livelier acceleration while maintaining a conventional, reliable design suitable for everyday driving.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The conventional 6-speed manual transmission is paired with base engine options for a direct driving feel. It features a simple mechanical build that typically requires minimal maintenance beyond standard clutch service.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, predictable gear shifts across most Forte trims. It stands as a durable option for buyers seeking effortless daily driving without complex CVT mechanics.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V622000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Forte&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the trunk latch assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Kia Forte is a sensible choice for used car shoppers looking for an affordable, straightforward compact sedan or hatchback. By carefully inspecting the powertrain option that fits your driving needs and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection, you can secure a dependable and cost-effective daily driver.
