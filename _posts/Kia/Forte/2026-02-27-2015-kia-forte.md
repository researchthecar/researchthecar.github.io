### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Kia Forte demonstrates solid reliability, with most major mechanical systems performing as expected. The primary area of concern noted is with the infotainment system, which can experience occasional glitches requiring software updates or, in more severe cases, replacement. Beyond this, owners can generally expect a dependable vehicle with minimal unexpected repair costs, making it a sensible choice in the compact car segment. Routine maintenance is key to ensuring long-term performance and avoiding issues related to neglect.
