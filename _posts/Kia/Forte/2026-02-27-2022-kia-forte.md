# 2022 Kia Forte Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Kia Forte, buyers will find a stylish compact car with generally modern features. While many owners enjoy daily commuting without major hurdles, driver-submitted complaints highlight a few specific areas to check during a pre-purchase inspection, particularly concerning intermittent engine hesitation, transmission engagement quirks, and electrical accessories.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 147 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque, offering efficient, straightforward performance for everyday driving. Overall, it is a mechanically simple unit, though buyers should verify smooth idle and acceleration during a test drive.

**Intermittent Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** At low and moderate speeds, the engine may intermittently and slightly stall while a rapid alarm sounds without dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic inspect throttle body operation, fuel delivery, and engine control modules for pending diagnostic trouble codes.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

Available on sportier trims like the GT, the 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder generates 201 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque for a much more spirited driving experience. This engine is generally robust, but prospective owners should check for proper turbo operation and regular oil change history.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Paired with the base 2.0L engine, this Intelligent Variable Transmission is designed for optimized fuel economy during daily commutes. While generally smooth, buyers should thoroughly test reverse and drive engagement.

**Gear Shifter and Reverse Engagement Delay**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience instances where the vehicle fails to respond immediately or properly when shifting into reverse gear.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission linkage, shift cable adjustments, and fluid condition, or have software updates applied by a dealer if applicable.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

Exclusive to the GT trim, this 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts for a sporty feel. Buyers should evaluate low-speed stop-and-go driving behavior for any unusual shudder or hesitation.

## Interior

### Air Conditioning and Climate Control

Electrical and climate accessories generally function well, though climate control durability warrants a thorough check before finalizing a purchase.

**AC Condenser and Compressor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The air conditioning system may blow warm air or experience complete system failure due to a broken air compressor or condenser defect.
- **Solution:** Inspect the climate control system operation across all fan speeds and temperature settings, and check for refrigerant leaks.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

The supplemental restraint systems are designed to protect occupants in collisions, though owner feedback underscores the importance of verifying sensor health.

**Airbag Non-Deployment Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Some owner reports indicate that frontal or side airbags failed to deploy during specific impact scenarios, accompanied by a lack of warning lights.
- **Solution:** Verify that safety restraint sensors and diagnostic modules are fully functional with no active warning indicators on the instrument cluster.

### Seat Belts

Seat belts are vital safety equipment; buyers should test each buckle click and retraction tension during a pre-purchase test.

**Random Seat Belt Release**
- **Symptoms:** The front driver-side seat belt may spontaneously unlatch or release while the vehicle is in motion.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt buckle assembly and latch mechanism for debris or mechanical wear, and replace the buckle assembly if faulty.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V304000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Forte&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A bolt in the steering column may not be secured properly, which can cause the steering column to detach from the steering rack and result in a loss of steering control. Loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will tighten the lower steering column u-joint retention bolt, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Forte is an appealing compact sedan offering strong fuel economy, contemporary styling, and a choice of efficient or sporty powertrains. While most drivers experience trouble-free operation, used-car buyers should carefully inspect the climate control system, test transmission engagement across all gears, and ensure all safety equipment and seat belts are fully operational. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection will help secure a reliable vehicle.
