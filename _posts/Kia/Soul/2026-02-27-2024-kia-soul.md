# 2024 Kia Soul Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Kia Soul continues to offer its iconic boxy styling, practical interior packaging, and value-oriented feature list. However, early owner reports indicate several emerging electrical and component-level concerns that prospective buyers should keep in mind. While many vehicles remain problem-free, checking for software updates and verifying proper operation of electronics during a pre-purchase test drive is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder

The standard 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 147 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting. It offers dependable mechanical performance overall, though occasional electronic management glitches have been reported by owners.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report unexpected engine stalling while operating the vehicle, requiring multiple restart attempts on the roadway.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose fault codes, update powertrain control modules, or inspect fuel and ignition systems.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Paired with the 2.0-liter engine, this Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT) uses a chain-belt design intended to simulate traditional gear shifts and improve fuel efficiency. It operates smoothly in daily traffic when properly maintained.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Infotainment

Cabin electronics provide modern connectivity features, though occasional screen blanking and digital glitches have been noted by owners.

**Blank Dashboard or Infotainment Screen**
- **Symptoms:** The digital instrument cluster or central infotainment display fails to power on or remains blank upon vehicle startup.
- **Solution:** Check system fuses, perform software resets, or have the dealer update and replace faulty display units.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems generally function well, but low-beam performance has drawn criticism from drivers navigating unlit roads.

**Headlight Dimness**
- **Symptoms:** Low beam headlights appear excessively dim during nighttime driving, whether set to automatic or manual control.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight assemblies, wiring harnesses, and control switches for proper voltage and alignment.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)

Advanced safety suites enhance driver awareness, but sensor communication drops can temporarily disable active assistance features.

**Intermittent ADAS Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Safety features like forward collision avoidance and lane departure assist disable simultaneously without warning during operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera and radar sensors for obstructions, and consult a dealer for software recalibration.

### Horn and Power Accessories

Interior switches and accessory components provide ergonomic convenience, though parts backorders for certain replacement items have been reported.

**Horn Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The horn fails to sound when pressed, posing a safety hazard during regular driving.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty horn assembly or relay under warranty if parts availability permits.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Kia Soul remains an appealing, budget-friendly compact crossover with great utility and a comfortable ride. Prospective buyers should thoroughly test all electronics, including the infotainment screens, headlights, horn, and safety features. Ensuring all software updates have been applied by an authorized dealer will help mitigate reported early-ownership quirks.
