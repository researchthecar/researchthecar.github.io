# 2021 Kia Soul Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Kia Soul is a distinctively styled, practical compact crossover that appeals to budget-conscious buyers seeking versatile cargo space and modern tech features. However, prospective buyers should review powertrain reliability carefully, as certain model years face significant engine-related complaints and safety campaigns. Researching maintenance history and verifying completed recall work are essential steps before purchasing a used model.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces around 147 horsepower, paired with an intelligent variable transmission. While it offers solid fuel economy and adequate daily drivability, buyers should verify that piston and engine-related service campaigns have been properly addressed.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling, loss of motive power while driving at highway speeds, and illuminated warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have an authorized dealer inspect the vehicle for applicable piston ring or engine control recalls and perform necessary repairs or component replacements.

**Engine Misfire**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, engine misfires, and dashboard warning lights related to spark plug or cylinder performance.
- **Solution:** Inspect ignition coils, spark plugs, and cylinder compression, and replace faulty ignition components as needed.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates roughly 201 horsepower, providing a more spirited driving experience paired with a dual-clutch transmission. It delivers strong acceleration but requires diligent maintenance to ensure long-term turbocharger health.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

The Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT) is designed to maximize fuel efficiency while simulating traditional gear shifts for a smoother feel. Most drivers find it adequate for daily commuting, though early powertrain complaints highlight occasional shifting hesitation.

**Power Train Stalling and RPM Fluctuation**
- **Symptoms:** Engine RPM flutters unexpectedly, transmission delays engagement into low gear from a stop, and the vehicle may exhibit stuttering or bucking at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Check transmission software calibration and fluid condition, and consult a qualified technician for powertrain diagnostics.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission is paired exclusively with the turbocharged engine option, offering quick and crisp gear changes. Buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement in stop-and-go traffic.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance under normal conditions is responsive and secure. Rare owner reports point to sudden pedal firmness or reduced stopping responsiveness during sudden panic stops.

**Brake Pedal Response Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal fails to respond adequately during emergency braking maneuvers, potentially impacting collision avoidance systems.
- **Solution:** Have the braking system, master cylinder, and anti-lock brake actuators thoroughly inspected by a professional technician.

## Steering

### Steering Column

The electronic power steering system is light and maneuverable for city driving. However, owners have occasionally reported wandering highway tracking or minor column noises.

**Steering Drift and Knocking**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel develops a noticeable drift or wandering sensation on straight roads, or produces a knocking sound when turning the ignition.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column intermediate shaft, alignment components, and electronic power steering software.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The electrical system powers a wide array of infotainment, lighting, and safety features. While generally robust, isolated complaints note intermittent accessory and lighting glitches.

**Dashboard and Accessory Lighting Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights, windshield wipers, and dashboard or infotainment displays intermittently fail while driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect wiring harnesses, fuses, and body control modules for loose connections or software glitches.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Paint

The exterior styling is a primary selling point for the Soul, but isolated manufacturing defects involving roof paint adhesion and glass panels warrant a careful physical inspection.

**Sunroof Shattering and Paint Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** The glass sunroof spontaneously shatters while parked or driving, or large sections of exterior paint peel away from the roof panel.
- **Solution:** Replace damaged glass panels or have affected body panels professionally repraised and sealed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V259000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The piston oil rings may not have been properly heat-treated, which may result in engine damage. A damaged engine may stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V099000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Engine damage can cause a vehicle stall or fire, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Kia Soul presents an appealing package for used car buyers desiring distinctive styling, flexible cargo capability, and modern technology. While most vehicles provide reliable daily transportation, prospective owners must carefully verify service histories and confirm that all safety recalls—particularly those concerning engine reliability—have been completed by a certified dealer. Conducting a pre-purchase inspection is strongly recommended.
