# 2019 Kia Soul Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Kia Soul is a popular compact hatchback known for its distinctive styling and practical cabin space. However, used car buyers should approach this model year with care due to notable owner complaints regarding engine reliability, steering column vulnerabilities, and electrical concerns. Thoroughly inspecting the vehicle's maintenance history and confirming whether safety campaigns have been addressed is strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Naturally Aspirated I4

The base 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine produces around 130 horsepower, providing adequate power for daily commuting. It is paired with manual or automatic transmissions, though buyers should watch for general engine longevity concerns shared across this model's powertrain lineup.

### 2.0L Naturally Aspirated I4

The 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine outputs approximately 161 horsepower and serves as the volume-selling option for the Soul. While generally peppy and efficient, it accounts for the majority of owner-reported drivability and oil consumption complaints.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling, hesitation during acceleration, or a complete loss of motive power while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a qualified technician; repairs may involve addressing fuel delivery components, software updates, or mechanical engine repairs depending on the root cause.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Unexplained low oil levels accompanied by illuminated check engine lights or unusual engine noise between scheduled oil changes.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels closely and consult a mechanic for professional diagnosis and potential warranty remedies.

### 1.6L Turbocharged I4

The turbocharged 1.6-liter engine delivers around 201 horsepower for a sportier driving dynamic, paired exclusively with a dual-clutch automatic transmission. It offers brisk performance but requires diligent maintenance to avoid turbocharger and fueling complications.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual

A traditional 6-speed manual transmission was available on base trims, offering straightforward mechanical engagement and low long-term complexity.

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the 2.0L engine to deliver smooth, traditional gear changes without the complexity of modern CVTs.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission is mated exclusively to the turbocharged engine, providing rapid shifts but requiring attentive driving habits in stop-and-go traffic to preserve clutch life.

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brakes and ABS

Brake system complaints include rare failures of the master cylinder and hydraulic control units that require immediate professional intervention.

**Master Cylinder and HECU Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal traveling all the way to the floor without adequate stopping power, or ABS and electronic stability control warning lights illuminating.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hydraulic system and replace faulty master brake cylinders or hydraulic electronic control unit components.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Motor Driven Power Steering

Steering complaints frequently center around physical wear items like the motor-driven power steering flexible coupling as well as security vulnerabilities involving the ignition cylinder and steering column.

**Steering Coupling Wear and Column Vulnerability**
- **Symptoms:** Loose steering feel, abnormal vibrations, or knocking noises originating from the steering wheel area.
- **Solution:** Replace the worn steering column rubber coupling or repair steering column damage. Note that certain components carry extended manufacturer warranty coverage.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Fan Controls

Electrical system reports highlight localized issues ranging from battery drain to cabin fan control switch overheating.

**Blower Motor Switch and Wiring Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Failure of the air conditioning system, unusual burning smells, or wiring harnesses turning black due to excessive heat at the fan control switch.
- **Solution:** Inspect the wiring harness and replace the blower motor switch or affected electrical connectors.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V031000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Circuit damage may result in deactivated air bags that will not deploy in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ACU, and either update the software or replace the unit, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Kia Soul presents a mixed reliability picture for used vehicle shoppers. While its distinctive styling and practical packaging remain attractive, potential buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain health, verify steering component conditions, and ensure all relevant safety recalls have been completed by a certified dealer before purchasing.
