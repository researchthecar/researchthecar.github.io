# 2023 Kia Soul Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Kia Soul offers distinct styling and a practical cabin layout, but prospective buyers should review reported owner concerns. Several drivers have documented powertrain behaviors, electrical glitches, and advanced driver-assistance anomalies. While many vehicles operate reliably, checking service records and verifying that all software updates and safety recalls have been performed is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 147 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate daily acceleration. It is paired exclusively with an Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT) driving the front wheels.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may unexpectedly stall while driving, accompanied by loss of engine power, fluctuating RPMs, and momentary illumination of warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning for stored trouble codes, inspecting fuel and ignition systems, and updating engine control module software if applicable.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

The continuously variable automatic transmission utilizes a chain-belt drive system designed to maximize fuel efficiency across urban and highway driving conditions.

**Transmission Hesitation or Locking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected hesitation, engagement delays, or abnormal locking behavior during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect transmission fluid levels, check for related powertrain control module updates, and perform a comprehensive system diagnostic.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Manages exterior illumination including headlights, taillights, and daytime running lights.

**Headlight Performance Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported inadequate illumination or intermittent headlight failure during night driving conditions.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlamp assemblies, wiring harnesses, and bulbs, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Integrates camera and sensor technology to detect potential frontal impacts and assist with emergency braking.

**Unexpected Autonomous Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly slow down on its own and activate hazard and brake lights under normal driving conditions without an obstruction.
- **Solution:** Check forward-facing camera and radar sensor calibration, clear any obstructions, and ensure relevant software updates are applied.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V298000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard numbers 101, "Control and Displays," and 138, "Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems." A blank instrument cluster display will not show critical information, such as tire pressure, warning messages, and warning lights, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the instrument cluster software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V531000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Fluid/Lubricant:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An electric oil pump assembly that overheats increases the risk of a vehicle fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the electric oil pump controller, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V830000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing error, the side curtain air bags may deploy unexpectedly. An air bag that deploys unexpectedly can increase the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace both side curtain air bag modules as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V099000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Engine damage can cause a vehicle stall or fire, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2023 Kia Soul, prioritize models with documented maintenance histories and verified completion of safety recalls. While the vehicle offers solid packaging and efficiency, a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on electrical systems and powertrain operation is strongly advised.
