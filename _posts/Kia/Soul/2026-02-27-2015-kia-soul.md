## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V750000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2015)**
- **Issue:** An engine compartment fire can occur while driving. An engine compartment fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the engine compartment for fuel and/or engine oil leaks, perform an engine test and make any repairs, including engine replacement, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V120000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2015)**
- **Issue:** Piston damage may result in an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. The leaking oil may contact the exhaust, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will upgrade the Catalytic Overheating Protection Engine Control Unit logic to prevent overheating of the catalytic converter. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V608000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2015)**
- **Issue:** If the pinion gear separates, steering will be lost, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and secure the pinion plug or replace the steering gear assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V123000: Vehicle Speed Control:Accelerator Pedal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2015)**
- **Issue:** If the vehicle has a bent or broken accelerator pedal, the driver may have difficulty accelerating the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will add a supporting rubber underneath the pedal stopper, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
