# 2022 Kia Soul Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia Soul offers a distinctive boxy design, practical cargo space, and an engaging feature set that appeals to budget-conscious daily commuters. While it generally provides a comfortable ride and modern technology, prospective used-car buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain reliability. Owner reports highlight critical engine and transmission concerns that require thorough pre-purchase inspections before making a commitment.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 147 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque, delivering adequate daily performance and decent fuel economy. However, buyer reports indicate potential risks regarding unexpected stalling and internal component wear that warrant close attention during a test drive.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling, activation of warning lights, and a sudden loss of motive power while driving at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic checks by a qualified technician are necessary to identify failing sensors, software glitches, or internal mechanical wear.

**Internal Engine Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Abnormal knocking noises from the rod bearings accompanied by catastrophic engine damage and potential engine block breach.
- **Solution:** Requires professional mechanical inspection and potentially complete engine replacement if internal component failure has occurred.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

The Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT) is paired with the 2.0L engine to maximize fuel efficiency and provide smooth cruising. Used car buyers should carefully test the transmission across various speeds to check for hesitation, slipping, or unusual noise.

**Transmission Failure and Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Fluctuating RPMs, sudden loss of acceleration capability on the highway, and complete transmission mechanical failure.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation by a transmission specialist, which may involve rebuilding or replacing the transmission unit.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging System

The electrical architecture supports modern safety and infotainment amenities, though localized battery drain issues have been noted by owners.

**Premature Battery Discharge**
- **Symptoms:** Persistent battery discharge warnings displayed on the dashboard leading to a dead battery that requires frequent jump-starts.
- **Solution:** Have the battery, charging system, and parasitic draw tested by a qualified automotive technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V099000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Hard Parts Internal/Mechanical](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Engine damage can cause a vehicle stall or fire, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the engine as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia Soul remains a popular choice for practical daily driving, but prospective buyers must approach with caution due to documented powertrain complaints involving engine stalls and transmission failures. Always secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an independent mechanic and verify service history records before finalizing a purchase.
