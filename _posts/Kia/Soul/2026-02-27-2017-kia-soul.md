# 2017 Kia Soul Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Kia Soul remains a popular choice for used-car buyers seeking a distinctive, practical hatchback with generous interior packaging. However, prospective buyers should approach certain powertrain configurations and model years with caution due to prominent owner complaints involving engine durability and safety accessories. Paying close attention to service records and prior maintenance can help mitigate potential risks.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

The base 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine generates around 130 horsepower, providing adequate efficiency for daily commuting. Buyers should carefully verify maintenance history and oil consumption habits.

**Excessive Oil Consumption and Engine Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note sudden knocking sounds, unexpected deceleration, and severe internal oil consumption without visible external leaks, risking total engine seizure.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels frequently and consult a qualified technician for oil consumption testing or piston ring inspection.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly stall while driving at speed or lose motive power when attempting to accelerate from a stop.
- **Solution:** Check diagnostic trouble codes and inspect fuel delivery, ignition, and sensor components.

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine produces approximately 161 horsepower, offering brisker acceleration compared to the base engine. It shares similar ownership vigilance requirements regarding oil consumption and electrical sensors.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated warning lights accompanied by sudden engine shutdown during highway or city driving.
- **Solution:** Perform thorough diagnostic testing on engine management sensors and software updates if applicable.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The performance-oriented 1.6-liter turbocharged engine pumps out 201 horsepower for a more spirited driving experience. Routine maintenance with quality synthetic oil is vital for longevity.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual gearbox offers direct gear control on select trims. It generally displays dependable mechanical operation when paired with a healthy clutch.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic provides smooth gear changes and reliable daily drivability. Routine fluid changes help maintain long-term durability.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers quick shifts paired with the turbocharged engine option. Buyers should test-drive thoroughly at low speeds to ensure smooth engagement.

**Low-Speed Juddering**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may shudder or lurch during startup and low-speed maneuvering.
- **Solution:** Have a certified specialist inspect the clutch actuation system and update transmission control modules.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Standard disc brakes offer respectable stopping power, but drivers should monitor brake pad wear and ABS sensor functionality closely.

**Brake Pedal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may feel unresponsive or fail to slow the vehicle adequately during sudden stops.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines, master cylinder, and ABS sensors for wear or hydraulic pressure loss.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Electric power steering provides light effort at low speeds and firm feedback on the highway, though occasional electrical assist dropouts have been reported.

**Loss of Power Assist**
- **Symptoms:** Steering wheel suddenly becomes very heavy and difficult to turn, accompanied by a warning light.
- **Solution:** Inspect electronic power steering components and wiring harnesses for faults.

## Exterior

### Paint Finish

Exterior body panels feature unique styling, though specific paint colors have exhibited premature delamination and clear coat failure.

**Paint Peeling and Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Factory paint begins chipping and peeling away in large flakes, particularly around the roof and hood areas.
- **Solution:** Consult a professional auto body shop for repainting options or check with local dealer goodwill policies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V031000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Circuit damage may result in deactivated air bags that will not deploy in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ACU, and either update the software or replace the unit, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Kia Soul offers distinctive styling, a versatile cabin, and practical daily utility. However, used car buyers must carefully inspect vehicle history reports for engine oil consumption issues, electrical sensor faults, and paint condition. Securing a pre-purchase inspection from an independent mechanic is highly recommended before finalizing any purchase.
