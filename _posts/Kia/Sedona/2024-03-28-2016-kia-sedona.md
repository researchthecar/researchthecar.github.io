**Introduction:**

The 2016 Kia Sedona is a minivan offered with a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 3.3-liter V6, which produces 276 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. A 3.8-liter V6 is also available, which produces 336 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque. This engine is paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. Both engines are offered in front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations.

People who like the Sedona praise its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive fuel economy. However, some people have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, and other components.

**Engine**

**3.3-liter V6**

* **Oil Consumption:** The 3.3-liter V6 engine has been known to consume excessive amounts of oil. This can lead to engine damage if it is not addressed.
    * Solution: The oil consumption issue can be fixed by replacing the piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:** Some owners have reported problems with the six-speed automatic transmission shifting smoothly.
    * Solution: The shifting problems can be fixed by replacing the transmission fluid and filter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported that their Sedona's battery is draining prematurely.
    * Solution: The battery drain issue can be fixed by replacing the battery or the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Wind Noise:** Some owners have reported that they experience excessive wind noise when driving their Sedona.
    * Solution: The wind noise issue can be fixed by replacing the weatherstripping around the windows and doors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Problems:** Some owners have reported problems with the climate control system not working properly.
    * Solution: The climate control problems can be fixed by replacing the climate control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Kia Sedona is a reliable vehicle with few reported issues. However, some owners have reported problems with the engine, transmission, and other components. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem.
