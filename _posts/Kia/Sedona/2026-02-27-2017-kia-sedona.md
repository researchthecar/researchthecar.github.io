# 2017 Kia Sedona Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Kia Sedona offers a comfortable, spacious cabin and smooth road manners, making it a budget-friendly option for family hauling. However, used car buyers should approach with a measure of caution. Owner reports highlight recurring mechanical and electrical concerns, notably regarding engine performance and sliding door latch mechanisms.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L V6

Equipped with a 3.3-liter V6 engine producing approximately 276 horsepower, providing solid acceleration and towing capability for a minivan. While generally robust, buyers should monitor for unexpected warning lights, stalling, or cooling system issues.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report unexpected vehicle deceleration, illumination of warning lights, and engine stalling while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electrical system, fuel delivery components, and sensors. Diagnostic scans may reveal failing components requiring replacement.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels. It generally operates reliably with standard fluid maintenance, though routine inspection for leaks or shifting delays is recommended.

## Exterior

### Sliding Door Latch

The cabin features practical seating configurations, though power sliding door components are prone to latching difficulties over time.

**Door Latch and Closure Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sliding doors fail to latch completely, triggering a dashboard warning light or unexpectedly opening while the vehicle is parked.
- **Solution:** Inspect tracks for obstructions and have the door lock actuators or power latch mechanisms adjusted or replaced by a technician.

### Exterior Paint

Exterior panels hold up decently, but specific paint finishes have shown tendencies to flake or peel prematurely under normal environmental exposure.

**Paint Peeling and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Paint begins to chip, flake, or peel prematurely, particularly around the rear glass and door panels on certain color options like white pearl.
- **Solution:** Consult an auto body shop for professional repainting or touch-ups to prevent further clear coat failure and body corrosion.

## Safety

### Air Bag

Safety equipment generally performs well, but intermittent warning lights related to passenger modules require prompt diagnosis.

**Air Bag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates unexpectedly upon vehicle startup, indicating a potential fault in the supplementary restraint system.
- **Solution:** Take the vehicle to a dealership or specialist to pull diagnostic trouble codes and replace faulty airbag modules or wiring harnesses.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V338000: Structure:Body:Door](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sedona&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The PSD may not auto-reverse when its closing is obstructed. If the door closes on an occupant, there is an increased risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the PSD module software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V031000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sedona&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Circuit damage may result in deactivated air bags that will not deploy in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ACU, and either update the software or replace the unit, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V725000: Exterior Lighting:Turn Signal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sedona&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The Smart Junction Box (SJB) software logic may not properly interpret the signals received from the multifunction switch, which could cause the turn signal to activate in the opposite direction of what the driver intended. A turn signal activating in the opposite direction of what was intended by the driver increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the SJB software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V109000: Electrical System:Wiring](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sedona&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If a child is in a child seat in the front seat, a broken ODS wiring harness will not prevent the front passenger air bag from deploying in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of injury to the child.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will remove the wire harness clip to prevent the wiring harness from breaking. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Kia Sedona is a capable and feature-packed family mover, but prospective buyers should inspect it thoroughly before purchasing. Pay close attention to engine performance, sliding door latches, and the condition of the exterior paint during a pre-purchase inspection to avoid unexpected repair bills.
