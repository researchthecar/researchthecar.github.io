**Introduction:**

The 2002 Kia Sedona is a minivan available with a 3.5-liter V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission. Known for its spacious cabin and reliable performance, the Sedona has received mixed reviews regarding its handling and fuel efficiency.

**Engine:**

**3.5L V6**

* **Engine Ticking Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A ticking or rattling noise coming from the engine.
    * Solution: Replacing the hydraulic lifters.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The transmission slipping or hesitating during gear changes.
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension sagging or drooping, especially under heavy loads.
    * Solution: Replacing the rear springs or shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Power Window Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: One or more power windows not functioning.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty window motor or switch.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Rear Bumper Damage:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear bumper prone to damage from minor impacts due to its low height.
    * Solution: Replacing the damaged bumper.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard, particularly on the passenger side.
    * Solution: Replacing the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Latch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear seat latches failing to properly engage, posing a safety risk.
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty latches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 02V043000, 04V067000

**Summary:**

The 2002 Kia Sedona has a limited number of major issues, most of which are relatively common problems found in many other vehicles. While the engine and transmission concerns can be costly to repair, the other issues are generally less severe and more affordable to fix.
