# 2019 Kia Sedona Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Kia Sedona, prospective buyers will find a spacious and feature-rich minivan that generally offers comfortable family transport. However, reviewing common owner-reported trouble spots can help you avoid unexpected headaches. The most notable areas of concern involve the power sliding doors and occasional powertrain hesitation, meaning a thorough test drive checking all utility features is highly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L V6 GDI

Powered by a 3.3-liter direct-injection V6 engine producing around 276 horsepower and 248 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth acceleration for daily driving. Overall mechanical reliability is generally acceptable, though buyers should watch for oil-related concerns reported by some owners.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported high oil consumption rates leading to low oil levels and potential engine wear at higher mileages.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly between changes and consult a certified mechanic if oil loss is excessive; some cases may require extensive engine evaluation or replacement.

**Oil Pressure Switch Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the red oil pressure warning light on the dashboard accompanied by oil seepage from the switch.
- **Solution:** Inspect the oil pressure switch for leaks and replace the component immediately if failure is detected.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission delivering power to the front wheels, offering responsive gear selection. While generally robust, a subset of drivers experience occasional shift quality anomalies.

**Shifting Hesitation and Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may hesitate, lurch forward unexpectedly, or experience harsh downshifts while accelerating or slowing down.
- **Solution:** Check for software updates or control module recalibrations at a dealership, and inspect transmission fluid condition.

## Electrical

### Power Sliding Doors

The cabin offers flexible seating and modern conveniences, though the power sliding door mechanisms require careful verification during a pre-purchase inspection.

**Door Latching and Sensor Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Sliding doors fail to latch completely on their own, trigger persistent door-ajar warning chimes, or refuse to open/close via power buttons.
- **Solution:** Inspect sliding door actuators, alignment tracks, and latches, and replace faulty door sensors or motors as needed.

## Interior

### Climate Control Panel

Electrical accessories generally function well, though rare localized melting issues around the manual climate control interface have been documented by owners.

**Melting Control Knobs**
- **Symptoms:** Heating and air conditioning control knobs or surrounding panel areas show signs of melting or thermal distortion.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged climate control panel assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V031000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sedona&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Circuit damage may result in deactivated air bags that will not deploy in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ACU, and either update the software or replace the unit, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V207000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Sedona&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." A seat belt that cannot be properly used increases the risk of injury in the event of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia has notified owners, and dealers will inspect the seat belt installation, and reinstall the seat belt buckle to the correct side if necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Kia Sedona is a capable and comfortable family hauler, but used car shoppers should perform targeted checks before purchasing. Pay close attention to the operation of the power sliding doors, test the transmission responsiveness across various speeds, and verify that all engine fluid levels and electrical accessories are in proper working order.
