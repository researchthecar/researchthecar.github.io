# 2023 Kia K5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Kia K5 stands out as a stylish, modern midsize sedan that brings aggressive styling and strong feature lists to the daily driver market. However, prospective used-car buyers should review potential owner-reported trouble areas before purchasing. While many drivers enjoy its comfortable ride and tech-forward cabin, early feedback highlights concerns ranging from fuel system behavior and powertrain hesitations to occasional body trim and electrical sensor faults.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Turbocharged Inline-4

This standard 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 180 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It offers a balance of efficiency and everyday drivability, though some owners have reported fuel delivery and hesitation concerns.

### 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4 (GT)

Reserved for the high-performance GT trim, this 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 pushes out a robust 290 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque. Mated to an 8-speed wet dual-clutch transmission, it provides brisk acceleration for driving enthusiasts.

### Fuel

Owner reports indicate occasional quirks regarding fuel odors, pump operation, and tank expansion behaviors requiring dealer assessment.

**Fuel Tank and Pump Concerns**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unusual fuel odors, potential tank expansion symptoms, or unexpected fuel pump failures leading to drivability disruptions.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the fuel system lines, pump assembly, and any applicable manufacturer service campaigns.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Standard and performance transmissions provide smooth power delivery in most conditions, though isolated reports highlight hesitation and rough shifting behaviors.

**Shifting Hesitation and Gear Slip**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected downshifting, sudden drops into neutral while driving, rough gear transitions, or erratic RPM flares.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership or transmission specialist for software updates, diagnostic scans, or potential component replacements if internal wear is found.

## Exterior

### Trim and Glass

Body hardware generally holds up well, though some drivers have experienced loose bumper clips, unclipped trim, and spontaneous sunroof glass failures.

**Exterior Trim Detachment and Sunroof Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Front splash guards, bumper clips, or window and trunk trim pieces partially detaching while driving; rare reports of panoramic sunroof glass shattering spontaneously.
- **Solution:** Inspect exterior clips and weatherstripping for loose fasteners. For sunroof concerns, check for impact damage and verify glass integrity.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Electronic safety suites provide valuable protection but can occasionally trigger false warnings or cluster warning lights due to sensor miscommunication.

**Sensor and Warning Light Clusters**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden activation of forward collision-avoidance warnings, blind-spot assist alerts, or multiple dashboard sensor lights illuminating simultaneously.
- **Solution:** Clean exterior camera and radar sensor zones, and have the dealership recalibrate or inspect the driver assistance modules if warning lights persist.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V760000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V149000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The side curtain air bags may have been installed incorrectly, causing them not to deploy properly in the event of a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the side curtain air bags, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V493000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Detached trim pieces can create a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the C-pillar trim assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V794000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A melted fuel tank can leak, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the check valve. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Kia K5 is an appealing used sedan option thanks to its striking design, engaging powertrain choices, and generous equipment levels. While the majority of vehicles operate reliably, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle history, test-drive to check for transmission smoothness, and ensure all relevant dealer inspections or service campaigns have been addressed.
