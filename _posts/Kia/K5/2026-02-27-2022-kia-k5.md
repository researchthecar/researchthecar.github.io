# 2022 Kia K5

### Overview
The 2022 Kia K5, a mid-size sedan, generally presents a strong reliability profile, building on Kia's reputation for producing dependable vehicles. It offers a range of powertrain options, including efficient turbocharged engines and a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. Owners generally report satisfaction with the K5's build quality and performance. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the K5 has demonstrated a good track record for its model year, with most reported concerns being minor or isolated incidents rather than widespread defects. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of concern for prospective and current owners.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Kia K5 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability across its major mechanical and electrical systems. Owners can generally expect a trouble-free ownership experience, with few documented widespread issues affecting the engine, transmission, or suspension. The most frequently noted, albeit minor, concern relates to occasional infotainment system glitches, which are typically resolved with software updates. Overall, the K5 is a solid choice for those seeking a dependable mid-size sedan, with its robust engineering contributing to its positive reliability standing for the 2022 model year.
