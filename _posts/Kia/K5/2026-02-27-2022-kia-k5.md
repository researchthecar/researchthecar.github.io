# 2022 Kia K5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Kia K5 is a stylish and feature-packed midsize sedan offering bold styling and a comfortable ride. While many owners enjoy its modern tech and punchy powertrains, used car buyers should research common mechanical complaints, including reported issues with melting fuses, brake rotor wear, and fuel system concerns. Keeping an eye on active service campaigns and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-4 produces roughly 180 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It offers a balance of efficiency and everyday drivability, though buyers should watch out for reported fuel system and stalling glitches.

**Fuel Leak and Odor**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report a strong fuel odor inside the cabin or visible fuel leaks around the rear seat and tank area.
- **Solution:** Have the fuel system and tank components inspected by a qualified technician, and check for applicable manufacturer recalls.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling, illumination of warning lights, and temporary loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnose electrical or fuel delivery faults and ensure engine control modules have the latest software updates.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (GT)

Exclusive to the high-performance GT trim, this 2.5-liter turbocharged engine pumps out 290 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque. It provides brisk acceleration and spirited performance, though models driven hard should be inspected closely for powertrain wear.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

Standard on most trims, this conventional 8-speed automatic delivers smooth and responsive gear shifts. It generally proves dependable, provided regular fluid maintenance is observed.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

Paired exclusively with the 2.5L turbo engine in the GT trim, this quick-shifting dual-clutch transmission enhances performance. Buyers should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth engagement from a stop.

## Brakes

### Brake Rotors and System

The braking system provides adequate stopping power, but multiple owners have noted premature rotor warping requiring early replacement.

**Warped Brake Rotors**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle shaking or steering wheel vibration at high speeds or when applying the brakes.
- **Solution:** Replace warped brake rotors and inspect brake pads for uneven wear.

## Steering

### Power Steering

The electric power steering system offers light and direct feedback, though rare failures in the steering motor have been reported.

**Steering Motor Noise and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** High-pitched rattling or squeaking noises when turning the steering wheel, along with increased steering effort.
- **Solution:** Inspect the power steering motor assembly and replace faulty components as necessary.
- **Cost:** Dependent on warranty coverage

## Electrical

### Electrical System and Multi-Fuse Panel

Electrical accessories and cabin electronics are generally robust, though the multi-fuse panel has drawn complaints for overheating issues.

**Melting Multi-Fuse and A/C Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Air conditioning stops working intermittently, accompanied by burning smells or a melting fuse box under the hood.
- **Solution:** Inspect the multi-fuse panel for heat damage and replace faulty fuse blocks or wiring components.

## Safety

### Air Bag and Seat Belts

Safety equipment includes comprehensive airbag coverage and advanced driver assists, though sensor warnings require prompt professional diagnosis.

**Airbag Warning and Seat Belt Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated airbag warning lights on the dashboard or issues involving side curtain airbag sensors.
- **Solution:** Have the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) scanned for diagnostic trouble codes and serviced by a certified technician.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V519000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V447000: Lane Departure: Lane Keep: Steering Assist](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** If a communication fault occurs while driving around a curve with Lane Following Assist (LFA) engaged, the steering assist level may not adjust properly, causing a sudden increase in the steering effort required. A sudden increase in steering effort can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the R-MDPS software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V760000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V149000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The side curtain air bags may have been installed incorrectly, causing them not to deploy properly in the event of a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and reinstall the side curtain air bags, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V794000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A melted fuel tank can leak, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the check valve. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Kia K5 stands out as a compelling used midsize sedan choice thanks to its striking design, comfortable cabin, and strong feature list. While most mechanical systems prove reliable, prospective buyers should verify that fuel system recalls and electrical fuse inspections have been completed. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes, electrical fuse panel, and steering components will provide extra peace of mind.
