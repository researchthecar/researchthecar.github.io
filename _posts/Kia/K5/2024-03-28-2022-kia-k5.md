**Introduction:**
The 2022 Kia K5 is a midsize sedan that offers a blend of style, performance, and technology. It comes with two engine options: a 1.6L turbocharged four-cylinder or a 2.5L naturally aspirated four-cylinder, paired with either an 8-speed automatic transmission or an 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. The K5 has received praise for its sleek design, spacious interior, and advanced features but has also been criticized for its stiff ride and some reliability issues.

**Engine**
- **1.6L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
    - **Turbo Actuator Failure:**
        - Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, decreased acceleration, illuminated check engine light
        - Solution: Replacement of turbo actuator
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        - Recalls: None Found
- **2.5L Naturally Aspirated Four-Cylinder**
    - **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**
- **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    - **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
        - Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears, difficulty shifting
        - Solution: Repair or replacement of transmission fluid pan gasket
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        - Recalls: None Found
- **8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission:**
    - **Clutch Friction Material Wear:**
        - Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or slipping when engaging gears, difficulty shifting, noise from transmission
        - Solution: Replacement of clutch assembly
        - Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **Front Suspension:**
    - **No Issues Found**
- **Rear Suspension:**
    - **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
- **Battery Drain:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting vehicle
    - Solution: Replacement of defective battery or repair of electrical components
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Headlight Malfunction:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Dim or flickering headlights, headlights not turning on
    - Solution: Replacement of headlight bulbs or electrical components
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Paint Peeling (Specific to GT-Line trim):**
    - Description/Symptoms: Peeling or flaking paint on the rear bumper
    - Solution: Refinishing of affected area
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: 22V-916
- **No Other Issues Found**

**Interior**
- **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Failure of climate control system, fluctuating temperatures
    - Solution: Replacement of climate control unit or electrical components
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Inoperable windshield wipers, intermittent wiper operation
    - Solution: Replacement of windshield wiper motor
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2022 Kia K5 has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While some owners have experienced minor issues such as battery drain and paint peeling, others have reported more significant problems with the engine, transmission, and interior components. The severity of the issues varies, with some requiring only minor repairs while others may lead to costly replacements. The estimated cost of repairs can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
