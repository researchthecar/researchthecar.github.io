# 2024 Kia K5 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Kia K5, buyers will find a modern midsize sedan that generally offers striking styling and a comfortable ride. While owner feedback is relatively limited given its young age, some early reports note electrical glitches, interior trim concerns, and occasional mechanical wear items. As with any newer used vehicle, checking for completed factory updates and remaining warranty coverage is essential.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 180 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque, providing a responsive daily driving experience. This engine balances efficiency with punchy low-end power, though occasional electrical warnings have been reported by owners.

**Electrical Warning Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported random electrical warning lights illuminating on the dashboard while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the vehicle computer for diagnostic trouble codes and inspect software calibrations and wiring harnesses.

### 2.5L Turbocharged Inline-4

Exclusive to high-performance trims like the GT, the 2.5-liter turbocharged inline-4 outputs a robust 290 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque. It delivers spirited acceleration and strong passing power for enthusiasts seeking a sportier midsize sedan.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the 1.6L engine, this traditional 8-speed automatic provides smooth and predictable gear changes for daily commuting. It generally operates reliably with routine fluid maintenance.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission

Found behind the 2.5L GT engine, this wet dual-clutch transmission offers rapid and direct gear shifts. Buyers should ensure proper shifting behavior during a test drive, as dual-clutch units can feel slightly different at low speeds than torque-converter automatics.

## Brakes

### Brake Rotors and Pads

Braking performance is normally strong, but heavy use or manufacturing variations can occasionally lead to early pad and rotor wear.

**Premature Wear and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience steering wheel shake or vehicle vibration when slowing down from highway speeds due to uneven brake pad wear or warped rotors.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for premature wear, and replace or resurface components as needed.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Backup Camera

The central electronics and touchscreen systems generally function well, though isolated incidents of display failure have been noted.

**Screen Blackout and Loss of Camera**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment display may go completely blank, disabling the backup camera and creating a safety hazard while reversing.
- **Solution:** Have the dealership inspect the infotainment head unit, check for available software updates, or replace the display module if hardware failure is confirmed.

## Exterior

### Trim and Pillars

Exterior body panels and lighting hold up well, though minor trim attachment issues have been reported by some owners.

**Loose or Detaching Chrome Pillars**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior chrome pillar trim pieces or C-pillar garnish may loosen and detach while driving at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Visit a dealer to secure or replace loose exterior trim clips and moldings.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V493000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Detached trim pieces can create a road hazard for other vehicles, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the C-pillar trim assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V794000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=K5&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A melted fuel tank can leak, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the check valve. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Kia K5 is an appealing used sedan choice thanks to its bold styling, comfortable interior, and strong warranty background. While early owners have pointed out minor issues regarding trim pieces, infotainment screens, and brake wear, widespread mechanical failures are uncommon. Prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the electronics, brakes, and exterior trim during a pre-purchase test drive.
