**Introduction**

The 2015 Kia Soul EV is an all-electric subcompact crossover SUV. It is powered by a single electric motor that produces 109 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque. The Soul EV has a range of up to 93 miles on a single charge. It is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and peppy acceleration. However, it has been criticized for its limited range, slow charging times, and lack of some features.

**Electrical**

* **12-Volt Battery Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The 12-volt battery can fail prematuerly, causing the vehicle to lose power.
    * Solution: Replace the 12-volt battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The electrical system can malfunction, causing a variety of problems, such as loss of power, flickering lights, and inoperative gauges.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Charging System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The charging system can malfunction, preventing the vehicle from charging.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the charging system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Window Regulator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The window regulators can fail, causing the windows to become stuck or inoperable.
    * Solution: Replace the window regulator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system can malfunction, causing the air conditioning or heating to not work properly.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the climate control system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can malfunction, causing the screen to become unresponsive or the system to freeze.
    * Solution: Update or replace the infotainment system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2015 Kia Soul EV has a few known issues, primarily with the electrical system and interior components. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can range from minor to major. It is important to note that not all Soul EVs will experience these issues, and many owners report having a positive experience with the vehicle.
