**2021 Kia Soul EV**

The 2021 Kia Soul EV is an all-electric subcompact SUV that offers a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and an impressive range of up to 243 miles. It comes with a single 201-horsepower electric motor and a 64-kWh battery pack.

**Suspension**

* **Premature Strut Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wear and tear on the front struts, leading to a bumpy ride and reduced handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Management System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Occasional errors in the battery management system, resulting in reduced performance or loss of power.
    * Solution: Software update or battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Heater Fan Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperative heater fan, leading to poor cabin heating.
    * Solution: Replacement of the heater fan motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Breakage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Weak or faulty door handles that can break off during use.
    * Solution: Replacement of the door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaks around the windshield or sunroof, causing water to enter the cabin.
    * Solution: Resealing or replacing the affected components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles**
    * Description/Symptoms: Annoying noises coming from loose or misaligned dashboard components.
    * Solution: Tightening or adjusting the affected components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seatbelt Retractor Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seatbelts that fail to retract properly, creating a safety hazard.
    * Solution: Replacement of the seatbelt retractors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
    * Recalls: Number: 20V578000

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Kia Soul EV is a generally reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some common issues, particularly with its suspension, electrical system, and interior components. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be significant. It is recommended to research potential problems and consider extended warranties or service contracts for added peace of mind.
