### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Kia Soul EV demonstrates good reliability, particularly in its core electric powertrain components. The electric motor and single-speed transmission are generally robust. The primary areas of concern revolve around the battery pack's long-term degradation, which is typical for EVs of this era, and occasional issues with the charging system and infotainment software. Exterior and suspension components have not shown significant widespread problems. Owners should be aware of potential battery health over time and ensure software is up-to-date for the best user experience. Overall, it remains a dependable choice for urban electric mobility.
