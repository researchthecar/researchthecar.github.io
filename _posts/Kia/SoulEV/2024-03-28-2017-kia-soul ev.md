**2017 Kia Soul EV Overview**

The 2017 Kia Soul EV is an all-electric compact crossover SUV powered by a single electric motor producing 109 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque. It offers a driving range of up to 111 miles on a single charge. Despite its small size, the Soul EV is known for its spacious interior, generous cargo space, and agile handling. However, owners have reported some issues with the vehicle, primarily related to battery and electrical components.

**Electric Motor**

**Battery Management System Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The battery management system may fail, leading to inaccurate battery level readings, reduced performance, and eventual vehicle shutdown.
Solution: Replacement of the battery management system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**High-Voltage Battery Cell Degradation**
Description/Symptoms: The high-voltage battery cells may degrade over time, reducing the vehicle's driving range and overall performance.
Solution: Replacement of the battery pack.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $10,000 - $15,000+
Recalls: None Found

**12-Volt Battery Discharge**
Description/Symptoms: The 12-volt battery may discharge prematurely, causing difficulty starting the vehicle or power loss to various electrical systems.
Solution: Replacement of the 12-volt battery.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
Description/Symptoms: Moisture can accumulate inside the headlight assemblies, leading to fogging or condensation during temperature changes.
Solution: Replacement of the affected headlight assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Rear Seat Belt Lockout**
Description/Symptoms: The rear seat belt buckles may become stuck or malfunction, preventing the use of the rear seats.
Solution: Replacement of the affected seat belt buckle.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300
Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 18V845

**Summary**

While the 2017 Kia Soul EV offers practical features and a spacious interior, it has faced reliability issues related to the battery and electrical components. Battery management system malfunctions and battery cell degradation can significantly impact the vehicle's performance and range. Electrical problems, such as 12-volt battery discharge, can cause inconvenience and safety concerns. Additionally, issues with exterior lighting and rear seat belt lockouts have been reported. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can be a source of frustration for owners.
