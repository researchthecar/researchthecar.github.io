**Electrical:**

* **12V Battery Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery fails prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle or electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Battery replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture builds up inside the headlight assemblies, causing impaired visibility.
    * Solution: Headlight assembly replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Tail Light Seal Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seal around the tail lights fails, allowing moisture to enter and cause corrosion or electrical issues.
    * Solution: Tail light assembly replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Dashboard Panel Gaps:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gaps and unevenness in the dashboard panel, affecting aesthetics and potentially leading to creaks or rattles.
    * Solution: Panel adjustment or replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Panoramic Sunroof Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise or whistling when the panoramic sunroof is open.
    * Solution: Adjust sunroof alignment or replace seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary:**

The 2022 Kia Soul EV has received mixed reviews from owners. While many appreciate its spaciousness, comfort, and affordability, some have encountered issues with the 12V battery, headlight condensation, exterior seals, and interior panel gaps. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can potentially impact the vehicle's reliability and driving experience.
