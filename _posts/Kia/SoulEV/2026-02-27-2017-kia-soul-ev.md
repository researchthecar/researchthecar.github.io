# 2017 Kia Soul EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Kia Soul EV, buyers will find a quirky, practical electric hatchback that offers zero-emission driving paired with the iconic Soul styling. While electric vehicles generally feature fewer moving parts than their gas-powered counterparts, this model year has accumulated owner reports regarding electrical system and powertrain behavior that prospective buyers should inspect closely.

---

## Engine

### 109-hp Electric Motor & 27 kWh Lithium-ion Battery

The 2017 Kia Soul EV utilizes a 109-horsepower electric motor paired with a 27 kWh lithium-ion polymer battery pack, delivering 210 lb-ft of torque. It provides sprightly low-end acceleration suitable for city commuting, though its driving range is modest compared to newer electric vehicles.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Power is sent to the front wheels via a single-speed direct-drive transmission. This simplified setup eliminates traditional gear shifting, offering smooth power delivery and minimal mechanical maintenance.

**Unintended Rolling in Park**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may roll or fail to remain securely stationary when the gear selector is placed in the PARK position.
- **Solution:** Have the parking pawl and linkage inspected by a qualified technician, and always utilize the mechanical parking brake on inclines.

## Electrical

### Electrical System & Battery Management

The electrical architecture manages both high-voltage propulsion and 12-volt accessory systems, though owner reports highlight critical system faults that require careful diagnostic verification.

**Unexpected Vehicle Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported sudden vehicle shutdown or unexpected entry into 'Limp Mode' accompanied by abnormal battery voltage warnings, requiring a complete restart.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the high-voltage battery management system and check for pending software updates or failing battery cell modules at a certified service center.

**Brake Application Electrical Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** Applying the service brakes occasionally triggers a complete vehicle electrical shutdown, loss of power steering, and temporary brake firmness before mechanical backup brakes engage.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pedal sensors, regenerative braking components, and main electrical harnesses for faults or loose connections.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V031000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Circuit damage may result in deactivated air bags that will not deploy in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ACU, and either update the software or replace the unit, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V218000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A battery electrical short-circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update and/or replace the battery management system and software, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V724000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Unintended vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Kia Soul EV is an appealing entry-level electric vehicle for short-distance commuters, but prospective buyers must approach with diligence. Given the owner reports regarding unexpected electrical shutdowns and powertrain anomalies, a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the high-voltage battery health, charging functionality, and brake-electrical integration is essential before finalizing a purchase.
