**Introduction:**

The 2016 Kia Soul EV is an electric hatchback with a single-motor, front-wheel drive configuration. The electric motor produces 109 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque. The Soul EV is known for its affordable price, spacious interior, and advanced technology features. However, it has also received some criticism for its limited driving range and slow acceleration.

### **Electrical**

* **12V Battery Failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, loss of electrical power, and warning lights on the dashboard. Solution: Replace the 12V battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Electrical Harness Short:** Symptoms include intermittent electrical problems, such as flickering lights, power window failure, or airbag warning lights. Solution: Repair or replace the affected electrical harness. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Charging Port Issues:** Symptoms include difficulty charging the vehicle or intermittent charging. Solution: Inspect and repair the charging port or charging cable. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

### **Exterior**

* **Door Handle Malfunction:** Symptoms include difficulty opening or closing the door handle. Solution: Replace the door handle assembly. Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+
* **Headlight Condensation:** Symptoms include moisture buildup inside the headlight housing. Solution: Replace the headlight assembly. Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
* **Windshield Washer Failure:** Symptoms include inability to spray windshield washer fluid. Solution: Replace the windshield washer motor or pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+

### **Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Symptoms include difficulty adjusting temperature, fan speed, or air distribution. Solution: Inspect and repair the climate control system. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Infotainment System Issues:** Symptoms include freezing, lagging, or loss of functionality. Solution: Update or reset the infotainment system software. Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $200+
* **Interior Panel Squeaks and Rattles:** Symptoms include annoying noises coming from the interior panels. Solution: Identify and tighten loose panels or apply sound-dampening material. Estimated Cost to Fix: Free - $100+

### **Summary**

The 2016 Kia Soul EV has received mixed reviews regarding reliability. While some owners have reported relatively few issues, others have experienced more significant problems, particularly with the electrical system and interior components. The cost to fix these issues can vary widely depending on the severity of the issue and the location of the repair. Overall, it is important to consider potential repair costs when evaluating the Soul EV as a potential purchase.
