# 2019 Kia Soul EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Kia Soul EV offers a distinctive electric driving experience, but prospective used-car buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding powertrain stability, electrical systems, and steering components. While electric vehicles eliminate many traditional internal combustion issues, attention must be paid to battery longevity, charging infrastructure compatibility, and reported electrical or drive unit anomalies.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Electric Motor

The Soul EV utilizes an electric motor paired with a high-voltage lithium-ion battery pack, delivering smooth, instant torque suited for city commuting. Overall reliability for the electric drive unit is generally favorable, though supporting electrical systems warrant inspection.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Power is sent to the front wheels via a straightforward single-speed reduction gear transmission. This design lacks traditional multi-gear shifting complexity, resulting in a low rate of mechanical failure.

## Steering

### Motor Driven Power Steering (MDPS)

The power steering system relies on an electric motor setup that can experience wear in internal flexible components over time.

**Steering Coupling Wear and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a loose steering wheel, noticeable vibrations, or a distinct knocking sound when turning.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the worn steering column flexible rubber coupling.

## Electrical

### Charging and Climate Controls

Electrical architecture supports high-voltage propulsion, auxiliary accessories, and climate management systems, which occasionally display localized component sensitivities.

**Climate Control Harness Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Fan control switches may overheat, resulting in discoloration of the wiring harness and failure of the air conditioning system.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fan control switch and wiring harness, and replace damaged electrical components or climate control units.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V031000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Circuit damage may result in deactivated air bags that will not deploy in a crash. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the ACU, and either update the software or replace the unit, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V218000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A battery electrical short-circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update and/or replace the battery management system and software, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V724000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Unintended vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2019 Kia Soul EV, prospective owners should verify the state of health of the high-voltage battery and ensure all steering columns and electrical accessories operate properly. A thorough pre-purchase inspection by an EV-certified technician will help confirm that drive units and charging systems are free of underlying defects.
