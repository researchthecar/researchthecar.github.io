# 2016 Kia Soul EV Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Kia Soul offers quirky styling and practical cargo space, but used-car buyers should approach certain model years and powertrain combinations with caution. Owner complaints heavily highlight engine performance troubles, including unexpected stalling, loss of motive power, and premature catalytic converter failures. Additionally, cosmetic paint peeling on factory white finishes and occasional electrical glitches have been reported by owners.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L GDI 4-Cylinder

The 1.6-liter Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) four-cylinder engine produces approximately 130 horsepower, providing adequate around-town performance. However, this engine family has attracted notable complaints regarding oil consumption, stalling, and premature exhaust catalyst wear.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience unexpected engine stalling, illumination of warning lights, and sudden loss of motive power while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation is required; repairs often involve addressing fuel delivery issues or replacing faulty sensors and catalytic converters.

**Catalytic Converter Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Check engine lights illuminate, accompanied by high engine revving, smoke from the exhaust, and engine knocking.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged catalytic converter and address underlying causes of excessive heat or oil consumption.

### 2.0L GDI 4-Cylinder

The larger 2.0-liter GDI four-cylinder engine offers around 164 horsepower for livelier acceleration. It shares many architectural traits with the smaller 1.6L, so buyers should verify service records for oil consumption and previous warranty repairs.

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Electric Motor (Soul EV)

The electric powertrain variant utilizes an AC synchronous electric motor paired with a lithium-ion polymer battery pack, delivering smooth, silent torque. It generally avoids the traditional internal combustion exhaust and oil consumption complaints.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The conventional 6-speed manual transmission offers a direct shifting feel for base trims. It is generally mechanically robust, with few widespread complaint patterns.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs smoothly with the gasoline engines for daily commuting. Regular fluid changes help ensure long-term durability.

### Single-Speed Reduction Gear (Soul EV)

The single-speed direct drive transmission used in the EV model delivers seamless power delivery without traditional gear shifting. It requires minimal maintenance compared to multi-speed gearboxes.

## Exterior

### Paint and Body Structure

Exterior body components are generally durable, though specific cosmetic paint defects affect vehicle appearance over time.

**Factory Paint Peeling and Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Factory paint, particularly on white vehicles, begins bubbling, cracking, and peeling off flat surfaces such as the hood, roof, and side mirrors.
- **Solution:** Repainting or professional clear-coat refinishing of affected body panels is required to restore protection.

### Exterior Lighting and Switches

Electrical system concerns mostly center on minor accessory glitches and lighting anomalies rather than total system failures.

**Intermittent Headlight Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights randomly flicker, turn off and on without warning, or fail to illuminate properly during night driving.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight wiring harnesses, switches, and relays; replace faulty electrical components as needed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V389000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The parking mechanism may become damaged if the driver moves the gear shift lever into Park 'P' before the vehicle comes to a complete stop. Unintended vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will update the electronic control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V608000: Steering](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the pinion gear separates, steering will be lost, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Kia will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and secure the pinion plug or replace the steering gear assembly, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V218000: Electrical System:Propulsion System:Traction Battery](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A battery electrical short-circuit increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update and/or replace the battery management system and software, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V724000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Gear Position Indication (Prndl)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Kia&model=Soul+EV&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Unintended vehicle movement can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the electronic control unit software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used model, prospective buyers should carefully review service histories, paying special attention to engine health, oil consumption records, and exhaust components. A pre-purchase inspection by an independent mechanic is strongly recommended to catch potential mechanical or electrical concerns before finalizing a purchase.
