# 2017 Kia Soul EV

### Overview
The 2017 Kia Soul EV is a compact electric vehicle known for its distinctive styling and practical urban utility. As an electric vehicle, its powertrain differs significantly from traditional internal combustion engine cars, focusing on battery and electric motor performance. Reliability data for the 2017 model year generally indicates a solid performance, with owners reporting fewer mechanical issues compared to many gasoline-powered counterparts. The primary focus for electric vehicles is often on battery longevity and charging system integrity, areas where the Soul EV has shown commendable durability. However, like any vehicle, specific components can experience issues, though widespread problems are not commonly cited for this model year.

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Kia Soul EV demonstrates good overall reliability, particularly in its electric powertrain components. While battery degradation is an expected long-term factor for any EV, specific issues with the motor and transmission are rare. The most common electrical concerns tend to be related to the charging system or infotainment software glitches, which are generally manageable. Exterior and interior components have shown robust performance with no significant widespread complaints. For prospective buyers, understanding the battery's health and warranty status is crucial, but the 2017 Soul EV is generally considered a dependable choice in the used electric vehicle market.
