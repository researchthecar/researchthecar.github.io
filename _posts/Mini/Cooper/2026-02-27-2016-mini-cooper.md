# 2016 Mini Cooper Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Mini Cooper offers a blend of iconic styling and engaging driving dynamics. However, prospective buyers should review powertrain options and common owner-reported issues carefully to ensure long-term satisfaction and avoid unexpected repair bills.

---

## Engine

### 1.5L Turbocharged 3-Cylinder

This base 1.5-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine produces approximately 134 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque. It provides peppy performance for daily driving while maintaining strong fuel efficiency for used-car buyers.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The larger 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine delivers around 189 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque in Cooper S trims. It offers spirited acceleration and robust power delivery, though proper maintenance remains vital for longevity.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission engages driving enthusiasts with direct control over gear shifts. It generally offers straightforward mechanical reliability when maintained with regular fluid changes.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

A conventional 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth shifts suited for commuting. Buyers should test-operate all gears to ensure seamless engagement without hesitation or slipping.

### 8-Speed Steptronic Automatic Transmission

Found on select higher trims, the 8-speed automatic transmission optimizes power delivery and highway efficiency. It provides refined gear changes but can be costly to service if internal mechanical faults develop.

- **What to do:** BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver's seat belt assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V222000: Exterior Lighting:Brake Lights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mini&model=Cooper&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." If the brake light does not illuminate when a turn signal is activated, other drivers may be confused, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** MINI will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the vehicle to correct the brake lighting, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V887000: Steering:Gear Box (Other Than Rack And Pinion)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mini&model=Cooper&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A gearbox malfunction may cause a loss of vehicle control and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** BMW will replace the steering gearbox, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V628000: Air Bags:Frontal:Passenger Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Mini&model=Cooper&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Due to a manufacturing error, the passenger frontal air bag may not deploy properly in a low speed crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." In the event of a vehicle crash, an air bag that does not deploy properly increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** MINI and BMW will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger frontal air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
