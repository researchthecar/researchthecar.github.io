## 2021 Mini Cooper Clubman

The 2021 Mini Cooper Clubman is a 5-door station wagon with a unique personality. It's offered in three different trim levels: Classic, Signature, and Iconic. Under the hood, there are two engine options: a 1.5-liter 3-cylinder engine with 134 horsepower and 162 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine with 189 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque. Both engines can be paired with either a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed automatic transmission.

Pros:
- Distinctive retro-inspired design
- Premium cabin materials
- Fun-to-drive handling

Cons:
- Less cargo space than some rivals
- Expensive for a subcompact car
- Mediocre fuel economy

### Engine

**1.5-liter 3-cylinder engine**

**Oil Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the valve cover or oil pan can leave visible puddles of oil under the vehicle and lead to low oil levels.
Solution: Replace the leaking gaskets or seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner may fail, causing the timing chain to slip and leading to engine damage. Symptoms include rattling noises, reduced engine power, and illuminated check engine light.
Solution: Replace the timing chain and tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**2.0-liter 4-cylinder engine**

**Turbocharger Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The turbocharger may fail, causing reduced engine power, black smoke from the exhaust, and increased oil consumption.
Solution: Replace the turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 20V-137

### Transmission

**6-speed manual transmission**

**No Issues Found**

**8-speed automatic transmission**

**Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaks from the transmission housing could lead to low fluid levels and transmission damage. Symptoms include slipping gears and delayed shifting.
Solution: Repair or replace the leaking seals or gaskets.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+

### Suspension

**No Issues Found**

### Electrical

**Infotainment System Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze, crash, or fail to respond to inputs.
Solution: Software update or system reset.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: NHTSA Recall Number: 22V-120

### Exterior

**No Issues Found**

### Interior

**Climate Control Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may not function properly, leading to inconsistent cabin temperatures or inoperative heating and cooling.
Solution: Repair or replace faulty components, such as the control module or sensors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

### Summary

Overall, the 2021 Mini Cooper Clubman has some potential issues that require attention. The engine and transmission issues can be serious and expensive to fix. However, the majority of other issues are relatively minor and less costly to repair. When considering this vehicle, it is crucial to be aware of these potential problems and factor in the cost of repairs when making a purchase decision.
