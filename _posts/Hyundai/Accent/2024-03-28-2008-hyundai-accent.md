**Introduction to the 2008 Hyundai Accent**

The 2008 Hyundai Accent is a subcompact car available with two engine options: a 1.6L inline-4 producing 110 horsepower and 107 lb-ft of torque, and a 1.5L inline-4 producing 92 horsepower and 89 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic.

Owners typically praise the Accent's fuel efficiency, affordability, and reliability. However, complaints have been raised regarding its cramped interior, lack of features, and noisy ride.

**Engine**

**1.6L Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating
    * Solution: Replacement of intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine stalling, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replacement of crankshaft position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.5L Inline-4**

* **Engine Stalling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine shuts off unexpectedly, especially when idling
    * Solution: Software update or replacement of idle air control valve
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Master Cylinder Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of clutch pressure
    * Solution: Replacement of clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $400+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall #14V595000

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting between gears, loss of power
    * Solution: Replacement of transmission or transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounting Plate Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or knocking sounds from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mounting plate
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $300+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall #14V595000

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Identification and repair of electrical component causing drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Moisture**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside headlight housings
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Trim Panel Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling trim panels on dashboard
    * Solution: Tightening or replacement of trim panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2008 Hyundai Accent is a generally reliable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. Engine and transmission problems can be costly, with some requiring major repairs. Suspension and electrical issues are less severe but can still affect the overall driving experience. Interior rattles and exterior moisture buildup are relatively minor problems but can be annoying for owners.
