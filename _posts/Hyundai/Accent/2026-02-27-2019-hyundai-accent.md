# 2019 Hyundai Accent Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Hyundai Accent, buyers will find a compact sedan known for solid fuel economy and daily drivability. However, owner reports submitted to safety authorities indicate certain recurring areas of concern, particularly regarding engine performance and safety systems. Reviewing these specific trouble spots can help prospective buyers perform targeted pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Gamma GDI 4-Cylinder

The 2019 Accent is powered by a 1.6-liter direct-injection four-cylinder engine producing 120 horsepower paired with front-wheel drive. While generally efficient for daily commuting, buyers should watch for owner-reported complaints regarding oil consumption and unexpected power loss.

**Loss of Motive Power and Cylinder Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden loss of engine power, unexpected stalling while driving on highways, and illumination of the check engine light accompanied by cylinder misfires.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic perform a comprehensive diagnostic scan, compression test, and cylinder inspection to identify mechanical failure.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** The engine consumes engine oil rapidly between scheduled oil change intervals, potentially leading to low oil levels if not monitored frequently.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly between changes and consult a technician to check for internal wear or leaks if consumption persists.

## Transmission

### Smartstream Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Power is routed to the front wheels via a continuously variable automatic transmission designed to maximize fuel efficiency. This IVT option delivers smooth everyday acceleration when properly maintained.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A traditional six-speed manual transmission was available on base trims for drivers preferring a direct shift feel. It offers straightforward mechanical reliability without complex automatic control modules.

## Steering

### Front Suspension and Steering Alignment

Suspension components absorb routine road imperfections, but alignment quirks and front-end noises have been noted by some owners.

**Vehicle Pulling and Suspension Noises**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may pull consistently to one side despite correct alignment specifications, or produce clunking sounds from the front suspension during sharp turns.
- **Solution:** Inspect suspension bushings, control arms, and steering components for wear or improper alignment geometry.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

The safety restraint systems provide standard occupant protection, though electronic module alerts require careful inspection before purchase.

**Airbag Warning Light and Module Faults**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning indicator light remains illuminated on the instrument cluster, and diagnostic trouble codes such as B162000 may be stored in the control module.
- **Solution:** Have the Supplemental Restraint System (SRS) scanned by a dealer or specialist to diagnose faulty modules or wiring harness issues.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V619000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the trunk latch base, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Hyundai Accent presents itself as an economical commuter choice, but potential buyers should exercise caution. Given owner reports regarding engine power loss, oil consumption, and airbag warning lights, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an independent mechanic is strongly advised to verify vehicle condition.
