**Introduction:**

The 2021 Hyundai Accent is a compact sedan that comes with a single engine and transmission option. It is powered by a 1.6-liter inline-four engine that produces 120 horsepower and 113 pound-feet of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The Accent is praised for its fuel efficiency, affordability, and roomy interior, but its lack of power and interior quality have been criticized.

**Engine**

**1.6-liter Inline-Four**

* **Oil Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine oil leaks from various gaskets and seals. May cause a burning oil smell, low oil levels, and engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace faulty gaskets and seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise coming from the timing chain when starting the engine.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission may slip, hesitate, or refuse to shift gears.
    * Solution: Re-flash the transmission control module (TCM) or replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $3500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission fluid leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the transmission case or seals.
    * Solution: Replace the leaking seals or gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension over bumps and rough roads.
    * Solution: Replace or lubricate worn-out suspension components such as struts, shocks, or bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear of the vehicle appears lower than the front, affecting handling and alignment.
    * Solution: Replace or reinforce rear suspension components such as springs or shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery quickly drains even when the engine is off.
    * Solution: Test and replace the battery or diagnose electrical system for parasitic drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Wiring Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions, such as non-working lights or sensors.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace the faulty wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulates inside the headlight assembly.
    * Solution: Replace or reseal the headlight assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Paint Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Paint peeling or chipping on the exterior of the vehicle.
    * Solution: Repaint or touch up the affected areas.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard while driving.
    * Solution: Tighten or lubricate loose components or replace faulty trim pieces.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Window Switches Malfunctioning:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power window switches not functioning correctly.
    * Solution: Replace faulty window switches or electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Hyundai Accent has several reported issues that can affect different components of the vehicle. Engine oil leaks and timing chain noise are some of the major concerns, while the transmission may also experience shifting problems or fluid leaks. Suspension issues such as noise and sagging can also occur. Electrical problems such as battery drain and wiring harness issues are also reported. In the exterior, headlight condensation and paint peeling are the most common issues. Interior problems include dashboard squeaks and rattles, as well as malfunctioning window switches. While these issues can be expensive to fix, they are generally not catastrophic in nature.
