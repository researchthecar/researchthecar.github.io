# 2020 Hyundai Accent Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Hyundai Accent offers a straightforward, economical daily driver experience with practical fuel efficiency for budget-conscious used car shoppers. While overall owner satisfaction remains standard for the subcompact segment, prospective buyers should review available owner reports concerning occasional electrical stalls, steering assists, and exterior trim components.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Smartstream 4-Cylinder Engine (120 hp)

The 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 120 horsepower and 113 lb-ft of torque. It prioritizes fuel economy and daily commuting efficiency, though limited owner complaints note unexpected engine stalls.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the vehicle may unexpectedly shut off when coming to a complete stop or slowing down.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnostic evaluation of the fuel and electrical systems to identify the root cause of the stall.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT) Automatic

The continuously variable automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels while maximizing fuel economy. It generally operates smoothly under normal driving conditions without widespread major mechanical complaints.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional six-speed manual gearbox provides direct driver engagement on base trims. It is mechanically simple and exhibits a solid track record with minimal owner-reported failure rates.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Steering component feedback indicates rare instances of electric power steering assist loss, which requires immediate attention if encountered.

**Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have experienced a sudden loss of power steering assistance while navigating turns, resulting in heavier steering effort.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering motor, wiring connections, and control modules for faults.

## Exterior

### Exterior Door Handles

Body hardware generally holds up well, though minor durability issues like loose or detaching exterior door handles have been reported.

**Door Handle Detachment**
- **Symptoms:** Exterior passenger door handles may unfasten or detach when attempting to open the vehicle doors.
- **Solution:** Reattach or replace the affected door handle assembly clips and mounting hardware.

## Safety

### Airbags and Restraints

Safety system components generally protect occupants well during impacts, though occasional electrical sensor or airbag warning light issues have been documented by owners.

**Airbag Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning indicator illuminates on the dashboard, pointing to a potential fault within the supplemental restraint system.
- **Solution:** Have the dealership or a qualified technician scan the airbag diagnostic codes and replace faulty components.
- **Cost:** 1700.00 usd

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V619000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the trunk latch base, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V069000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioner(s) may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, striking vehicle occupants and resulting in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V218000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver and passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. Exploding seat belt pretensioners can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V354000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioner(s) with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Hyundai Accent is a sensible, economical choice for everyday driving, backed by reasonable fuel economy and straightforward mechanics. Used car buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the electrical system, steering responsiveness, and safety sensors to ensure trouble-free ownership.
