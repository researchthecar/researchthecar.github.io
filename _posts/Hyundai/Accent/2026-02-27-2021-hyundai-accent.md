# 2021 Hyundai Accent Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Hyundai Accent, buyers will find a practical and fuel-efficient subcompact sedan. While owner-reported complaints are relatively limited, prospective owners should review powertrain performance and specific hardware quirks before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 120 horsepower and 113 lb-ft of torque, offering reliable daily commuting efficiency. Overall, this powerplant is generally dependable, though isolated owner reports note unexpected power fluctuations.

**Motive Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected temporary losses of motive power while driving at highway speeds without prior warning lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the fuel injection system, throttle body, and electronic engine management sensors.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Paired with the 1.6L engine, the Intelligent Variable Transmission provides smooth acceleration and helps maximize fuel economy for daily driving. It maintains a solid reputation among owners with very few reported mechanical failures.

## Suspension

### Wheel Lug Nuts and Studs

Suspension and wheel assembly hardware generally hold up well, though wheel fastener durability requires careful attention during routine maintenance.

**Swollen or Fracturing Lug Nuts**
- **Symptoms:** Lug nuts and wheel studs can seize, swell, or fracture during routine tire rotations or seasonal tire changes.
- **Solution:** Replace affected factory lug nuts with stronger aftermarket alternatives and inspect wheel studs for thread damage during tire services.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V937000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Master Cylinder](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Reduced braking performance can cause longer brake pedal travel or extend the distance required to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake master cylinder, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V069000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioner(s) may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, striking vehicle occupants and resulting in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V311000: Steering:Column](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A bolt in the steering column may not be secured properly, which can cause the steering column to detach from the steering rack and result in a loss of steering control. Loss of steering control can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten the lower steering column u-joint retention bolt, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V354000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Accent&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioner(s) with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Hyundai Accent stands out as an economical and sensible daily driver for used car buyers. While isolated reports concerning intermittent power loss and stubborn wheel hardware warrant a pre-purchase inspection, the vehicle presents a manageable ownership experience overall.
