# 2016 Hyundai Accent Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Hyundai Accent, buyers will find a generally straightforward subcompact car, though certain mechanical and safety concerns deserve careful attention. While it offers efficient daily transportation, prospective owners should thoroughly inspect the vehicle's powertrain and electrical systems prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L GDI 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2016 Accent comes standard with a 1.6-liter Gamma Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) four-cylinder engine producing around 137 horsepower. While generally efficient, some owners have reported severe internal failures and oil consumption issues.

**Engine Failure and Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may fail completely, experience internal oil leaks, or cause sluggish acceleration and rough idling due to fouled catalytic converters and O2 sensors.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace damaged emission components, or perform engine replacement if internal failure has occurred.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a smooth-shifting 6-speed automatic transmission, this powertrain delivers predictable daily driving dynamics with few widespread reliability complaints.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The available 6-speed manual transmission offers a direct connection for drivers who prefer shifting their own gears, maintaining a solid track record.

## Brakes

### ABS and Traction Control Module

Dashboard warning lights coupled with speedometer failure point to intermittent electrical or sensor glitches.

**Intermittent ABS and Speedometer Failure**
- **Symptoms:** In warm weather, the ABS, traction control, and emergency brake warning lights illuminate simultaneously while the speedometer stops working.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheel speed sensors, wiring harnesses, and the ABS module for faults.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering Column

Issues involving sudden power steering loss can severely compromise vehicle safety.

**Steering Lockup / Loss of Control**
- **Symptoms:** The Electronic Stability Program (ESP) warning light illuminates and the steering wheel suddenly freezes or becomes extremely difficult to turn in either direction.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and EPS motor; replace faulty steering components as necessary.

## Safety

### Air Bag System

Restraint system warnings and sensor faults require immediate diagnostic verification to ensure occupant safety.

**Airbag Warning Light and Deployment Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The SRS airbag warning light illuminates intermittently, or passenger seat occupancy sensors fail, potentially preventing airbags from deploying properly in a collision.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the SRS module, repair loose seat sensor wiring, or replace malfunctioning airbag components.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Hyundai Accent is an economical choice for budget-minded buyers, but a pre-purchase inspection is vital. Be sure to check the steering column operation, verify that all dashboard safety and ABS warning lights function normally, and ensure the engine has been properly maintained without oil consumption or hesitation symptoms.
