# 2004 Hyundai XG350

### Overview
The 2004 Hyundai XG350, a mid-size luxury sedan, aimed to offer premium features at a competitive price point. Powered by a robust V6 engine and typically paired with an automatic transmission, it provided a comfortable and smooth driving experience. While generally considered a reliable vehicle for its era, like any car, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical concerns, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of its long-term dependability.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6 (G6CU)
**Timing Belt Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden engine shutdown, loss of power, ticking or rattling noises from the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing belt, tensioners, and idler pulleys. In case of failure, potential damage to valves and pistons may require more extensive engine repair.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200

**Oil Leaks (Valve Cover Gaskets)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil on the engine, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of valve cover gaskets.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Automatic (F5A51)
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking during gear changes, delayed engagement, engine revving without acceleration.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, solenoid replacement, or in severe cases, transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $300 - $2500+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Control Arm Bushings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering feel, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of control arm bushings or the entire control arm assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Electrical Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Malfunctioning power windows, door locks, interior lights, or dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


### Alternator
**Premature Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, difficulty starting the engine.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Exterior

### Standard Body
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** N/A
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** Varies


## Interior

### Standard Interior
**HVAC Blower Motor Resistor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Fan only works on highest setting or not at all.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor resistor.
- **Cost:** $100 - $250

### Summary Verdict
The 2004 Hyundai XG350 offers a generally dependable ownership experience, particularly concerning its powertrain and exterior. However, potential buyers and current owners should be aware of common issues such as timing belt maintenance, potential transmission quirks, and specific electrical component failures like the BCM and alternator. Suspension components, particularly bushings, may also require attention as the vehicle ages. Addressing these known issues proactively can help maintain the XG350's comfort and reliability.
