# 2020 Hyundai Veloster Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Hyundai Veloster offers a unique three-door layout and sporty styling, appealing to buyers looking for an engaging compact hatchback. While it brings plenty of personality to the road, prospective used-car buyers should review owner complaints regarding various electrical, powertrain, and body issues. Evaluating service history and conducting a thorough pre-purchase inspection can help ensure a reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder

The base 2.0-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 147 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate daily driving performance, though owners should watch for occasional ignition or mechanical noise concerns.

**Engine Knocking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience an abnormal knocking noise originating from the engine block.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine internals, pistons, and valve train to determine the source of the noise.

**Spark Plug Misfire**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may misfire, leading to rough running or reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace worn spark plugs or related ignition components.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The available 1.6-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates 201 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque. It offers a much punchier driving experience for enthusiasts, though proper maintenance of turbo components and oil levels is essential.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder (N)

Exclusive to the high-performance N trim, this 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder pushes out up to 275 horsepower. It is built for aggressive driving, making a meticulous pre-purchase inspection vital for used models.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission is paired with select trims and provides a direct, engaging connection for drivers who prefer shifting their own gears.

**Reverse Lockout Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The manual shifter reverse lockout mechanism may fail to reset properly when returning to neutral, potentially restricting gear selection.
- **Solution:** Inspect the shifter assembly and replace any worn or binding lockout components.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, traditional gear shifts for daily commuting convenience.

### 7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 7-speed dual-clutch transmission offers quick, automated gear changes for sportier trims, though buyers should ensure it shifts smoothly during a test drive.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission

Available on the Veloster N, this 8-speed wet dual-clutch transmission is engineered to handle high-performance driving dynamics and rapid shifts.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

Steering system complaints highlight potential electronic power steering dropouts that require physical effort to maneuver the vehicle until restarted.

**Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The power steering warning light may illuminate, followed by a complete loss of power steering assistance.
- **Solution:** Have the electronic power steering module, sensors, and wiring thoroughly diagnosed by a professional.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Door Latches

Electrical and body accessory reports indicate occasional quirks with switchgear operation and cold-weather door latch resistance.

**Turn Signal Switch Light Deactivation**
- **Symptoms:** Activating the turn signal with excessive force can inadvertently rotate the switch to the off position, disabling exterior lights.
- **Solution:** Exercise care when operating the turn signal stalk, or have the switch assembly inspected if it feels overly loose.

**Door Latch Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** In sub-freezing temperatures, exterior doors may fail to open from the outside.
- **Solution:** Apply proper weatherstrip lubricant or inspect door latch mechanisms for moisture buildup.

### Glass and Trim

Exterior body reports focus primarily on rare glass panel vulnerabilities and environmental wear on wheel finishes.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass roof panel may shatter spontaneously when exposed to high ambient temperatures and direct sunlight.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass roof panel and inspect the surrounding frame for alignment issues.

## Interior

### Seating

Cabin comfort is generally well-received, though isolated reports point to component failures within the driver's seat adjustments.

**Driver Seat Lumbar Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The internal lumbar support mechanism in the driver seat may crack or break, causing the seat to feel sunken and unsupported.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat frame internals and replace broken lumbar adjustment components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V301000: Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Veloster&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The piston oil rings may not have been properly heat-treated, which could result in engine damage. A damaged engine may stall, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and, if necessary, replace the engine, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Hyundai Veloster remains a stylish and fun-to-drive hatchback choice, but buyers should perform a detailed inspection of its mechanical and electrical systems. Pay close attention to steering operation, manual transmission components, and potential cabin or glass wear during your test drive to ensure a trouble-free purchase.
