# 2016 Hyundai Elantra GT Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Hyundai Elantra GT offers practical hatchback versatility, a comfortable ride, and decent cargo space, making it an appealing option for budget-conscious used car shoppers. However, prospective buyers should exercise caution, as owner reports highlight notable concerns regarding engine reliability, stalling, and electrical or steering hiccups.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine produces around 173 horsepower and provides adequate daily performance for the hatchback. While generally efficient, this engine has attracted owner complaints regarding unexpected stalling and hesitation.

**Engine Stalling and Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may suddenly stall while driving at various speeds or experience a sudden loss of motive power accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the underlying cause, which may involve checking oil pressure, sensors, or fuel delivery components, and replace faulty parts as needed.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth, reliable shifts under normal driving conditions. It pairs well with the 4-cylinder engine and has fewer major complaints compared to the vehicle's engine electrical systems.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The 6-speed manual transmission gives drivers more direct control and typically offers solid mechanical durability. Routine clutch maintenance is expected over higher mileages.

## Brakes

### Brake

Braking performance is adequate when maintained, but vehicles operated in regions with winter road salt are susceptible to brake line and backing plate corrosion.

**Brake Line and Component Rust**
- **Symptoms:** Brake lines rust out leading to fluid leaks, spongy pedal feel, or total brake failure accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines and backing plates regularly for corrosion, and replace rusted lines or components immediately.

## Steering

### Steering and Subframe

Steering response is generally crisp, though rare power steering lockups and front-end rust merit a thorough pre-purchase inspection.

**Power Steering and Control Arm Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Power steering warning lights illuminate, steering wheel locks up unpredictably, or control arms rust out from the subframe.
- **Solution:** Inspect the front subframe and control arms for structural rust, and service or replace failed electronic power steering components.

## Exterior

### Latch

Cabin convenience features and latches generally hold up, though mechanical wear can occasionally disable trunk access.

**Trunk and Door Latch Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Trunk fails to open from the inside or via electronic releases, and emergency interior releases may become inoperable.
- **Solution:** Inspect the trunk latch mechanism and linkage cables, and replace broken actuators or latch assemblies.

## Safety

### Air Bag

Safety equipment functions as intended in most scenarios, but steering-column electrical components like clocksprings are prone to wearing out over time.

**Clockspring and Airbag Warning**
- **Symptoms:** Airbag warning light illuminates on the dashboard, horn stops working, and steering wheel controls may fail.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty steering wheel clockspring unit to restore proper electrical continuity to the airbag and horn.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V307000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Elantra+GT&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag inflator may rupture during deployment. An air bag inflator that ruptures may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or passengers, increasing the risk of injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the air bag inflator, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Hyundai Elantra GT is a practical and versatile hatchback choice, but buyers should approach with a degree of caution. A thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on the engine's operating behavior, steering components, and undercarriage rust protection is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
