**2015 Hyundai Elantra GT**

The 2015 Hyundai Elantra GT is a compact hatchback that offers a blend of practicality and style. It comes with a choice of two engine options: a 1.8-liter inline-four with 145 horsepower and 130 lb-ft of torque, or a 2.0-liter inline-four with 173 horsepower and 154 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed manual for the 1.8-liter engine and a six-speed automatic for both engines.

Owners of the Elantra GT generally praise its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and fuel-efficient performance. However, there have been some reported issues with certain components.

**Engine**

* **1.8-liter Inline-Four**

  * **Fuel injector failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, decreased fuel efficiency
    * Solution: Replace fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * **Valve cover gasket leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the engine, visible smoke from the engine bay
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
* **2.0-liter Inline-Four**

  * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual**

  * **Slipping clutch**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clutch engages and disengages erratically, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace clutch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* **Six-Speed Automatic**

  * **Shift solenoid failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts erratically or fails to shift, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Power window failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Power windows do not operate or operate intermittently
    * Solution: Replace power window motor or switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Battery drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges rapidly, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Check parasitic draws and replace or repair faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause

**Exterior**

* **Headlight condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlight housings
    * Solution: Replace headlight housings or install moisture vents
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Interior**

* **Dash panel warping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dash panel buckles or deforms due to heat exposure
    * Solution: Replace dash panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2015 Hyundai Elantra GT has a mix of positive and negative reviews. While it offers a spacious interior and efficient performance, it has been plagued by several recurring issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can be a concern for potential buyers.
