**Introduction:**

The 2019 Hyundai Sonata is a midsize sedan powered by a standard 2.5-liter inline-four engine with 191 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque. It's also available with a turbocharged 1.6-liter inline-four engine producing 180 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a standard 8-speed automatic and an optional 7-speed dual-clutch transmission. The Sonata has received positive reviews for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and user-friendly technology but has been criticized for its lackluster acceleration and uninspiring handling.

**Engine**

**2.5-Liter Inline-Four Engine**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the valve cover gasket, causing a burning oil smell and low oil levels.
    * Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $450
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A rattling noise coming from the engine, especially during startup or acceleration.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.6-Liter Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine**

* **Turbocharger Actuator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power and a check engine light illuminated.
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **High-Pressure Fuel Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, misfiring, and reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Replace the high-pressure fuel pump.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Harsh Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or lurching during gear changes, especially in low gears.
    * Solution: Software update or transmission fluid flush.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission**

* **Dual-Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, and a check engine light illuminated.
    * Solution: Replace the dual-clutch unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Rear Shock Absorber Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises coming from the rear suspension, especially when going over bumps.
    * Solution: Replace the rear shock absorber mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery draining prematurely, leading to difficulty starting the vehicle.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace faulty electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: Recall number: 19V671000 - Battery drain due to malfunctioning smart trunk system
* **Infotainment System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system freezing, lagging, or displaying errors.
    * Solution: Software update or system reset.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system not working properly or producing strange noises.
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace faulty components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Seat Position Sensor Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat position sensor failing to detect the correct seat position.
    * Solution: Replace the seat position sensor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2019 Hyundai Sonata has some common issues that can affect its reliability and performance. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be more serious and costly. However, it's important to note that not all Sonatas will experience these problems, and the vehicle has generally received positive reviews from owners and critics alike.
