# 2020 Hyundai Sonata Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Hyundai Sonata arrives with striking coupe-like styling, a feature-packed interior, and a comfortable ride. However, prospective used-car buyers should review owner complaints regarding engine performance, fuel system integrity, and electrical accessories before purchasing. While many owners enjoy its fuel efficiency and modern tech, monitoring known problem areas can save you from unexpected repair bills.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

A naturally aspirated 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing around 191 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, serving as the standard powerplant for the base trims. It offers dependable daily driving performance and decent fuel economy, though owners have occasionally reported stalling and oil consumption issues.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

A turbocharged 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine pushing out approximately 180 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque, providing stronger low-end acceleration for upper trims. This engine is generally responsive, but buyers should keep an eye out for potential fuel system and powertrain integration concerns reported in early model years.

Core engine components experience occasional owner complaints regarding unexpected stalling and fuel delivery concerns.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may unexpectedly stall while driving or fail to maintain a steady idle after coming to a stop, often accompanied by warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a qualified technician is required to identify faulty sensors, software glitches, or fuel delivery interruptions.

### Fuel

Fuel system complaints center around fuel pump malfunctions and gasoline odors originating from the rear seating area.

**Fuel Leak or Odor**
- **Symptoms:** A strong gasoline smell inside the cabin or around the rear seats, sometimes accompanied by a rapidly dropping fuel gauge.
- **Solution:** Inspect the fuel tank, lines, and fuel pump assembly for leaks or structural defects, and replace damaged components.

**Fuel Pump Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start or experiences sudden fuel starvation while driving, triggering check engine lights and electrical warnings.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning fuel pump module inside the fuel tank.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An eight-speed automatic transmission paired with most Sonata models, delivering smooth shifts under normal conditions but occasionally prone to hesitation or engagement complaints.

**Transmission Hesitation and Slippage**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed acceleration after coming to a stop, jerking, or temporary slipping when attempting to accelerate at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Check transmission fluid condition, update control modules, or consult a dealer for mechanical inspection and potential rebuild if internal wear is present.

## Steering

### Steering Column

Steering complaints indicate that mechanical noises can develop within the steering column over time.

**Steering Wheel Noise**
- **Symptoms:** A raspy, rubbing, or whining noise emanating from the steering wheel area when turning left or right.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering column and replace the internal steering bearing as necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical system reports highlight isolated issues involving fuse box degradation and climate control failures.

**Multifuse Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Sudden loss of cabin climate control airflow due to a melted multifuse and terminal pins inside the fuse box.
- **Solution:** Inspect the under-hood fuse box, replace the melted multifuse, and repair damaged wiring harnesses.

### Back Over Prevention

Safety equipment complaints note occasional intermittent or complete failures of the rearview camera display.

**Backup Camera Display Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen displays a blank black screen when shifting into reverse, preventing visibility behind the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Check camera connections, update infotainment software, or replace the backup camera unit.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V213000: Electrical System:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Sonata&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Unintended vehicle movement increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will reprogram the RSPA software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V122000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Sonata&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." The incorrect tire information may mislead the vehicle owner to install the wrong tire size, potentially affecting vehicle handling, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will install new label stickers over the tire pressure label and in the owner's manual, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V196000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Sonata&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the trunk latch and replace the trunk latch base, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V796000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Sonata&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A melted fuel tank can leak, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the check valve. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Hyundai Sonata is a compelling used midsize sedan offering striking design and a comfortable cabin, but buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to potential fuel odor issues, steering column noises, and transmission shifting smoothness during a test drive to ensure a reliable ownership experience.
