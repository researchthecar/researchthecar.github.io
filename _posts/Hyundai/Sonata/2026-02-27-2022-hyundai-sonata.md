# 2022 Hyundai Sonata Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Hyundai Sonata offers a stylish, tech-forward option in the midsize sedan market, but potential buyers should review owner complaints regarding powertrain and fuel system concerns. While it remains a popular commuter choice with strong modern features, used car shoppers should pay close attention to transmission performance and engine reliability during test drives.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine produces around 191 horsepower, providing adequate daily driving power and respectable fuel economy for everyday commuters.

### 1.6L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The turbocharged 1.6-liter 4-cylinder engine generates roughly 180 horsepower and enhanced low-end torque, offering a more responsive driving experience in the higher trims.

### Fuel System

Issues related to the fuel delivery and injection components have prompted owner complaints regarding fuel odors and unexpected stalling risks.

**Engine Stalling and Fuel Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported sudden engine stalling, loss of motive power while driving, and strong fuel odors or leaks around the fuel injectors.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the fuel system and replacement of faulty components or injectors by a certified technician.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission paired with most Sonata models delivers smooth highway cruising, though some owners report low-speed shift quality concerns.

**Harsh Shifting and Gear Stutter**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience rough shifting between first and second gear, hard downshifts when slowing down, and occasional acceleration hesitation.
- **Solution:** May require dealership software reprogramming or mechanical inspection under remaining warranty coverage.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

Electrical complaints center around occasional battery drain issues and intermittent climate control system faults.

**Battery Drain and HVAC Blower Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The 12-volt battery can drain prematurely due to faulty integrated modules, and the HVAC blower motor may become inoperable without warning.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty batteries or integrated system modules and repair blower motor connections as needed.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features enhance overall protection, though sensor calibration issues can cause unexpected braking interventions.

**Unintended Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system can occasionally engage briefly for no apparent reason while driving forward or backing up.
- **Solution:** Have dealership technicians inspect and recalibrate the radar and camera sensors responsible for collision avoidance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V524000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Sonata&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V746000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Sonata&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V796000: Fuel System, Gasoline](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Sonata&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A melted fuel tank can leak, increasing the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the check valve. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Sonata is a feature-rich midsize sedan that generally provides a comfortable ride and modern amenities, but prospective buyers should carefully inspect the transmission and fuel system. Taking an extended test drive to verify smooth gear shifts and checking for any active recall or service history will help ensure a reliable purchase.
