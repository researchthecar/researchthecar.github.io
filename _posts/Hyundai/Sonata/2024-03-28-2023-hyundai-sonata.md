**Introduction:**

The 2023 Hyundai Sonata is a four-door sedan available with three engine options: a 2.5L GDI I4, a 1.6L Turbo GDI I4, and a 2.5L Turbocharged GDI I4. The 2.5L GDI I4 engine produces 191 hp and 181 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.6L Turbo GDI I4 engine produces 180 hp and 195 lb-ft of torque. The 2.5L Turbocharged GDI I4 engine is the most powerful option, producing 290 hp and 311 lb-ft of torque. All engines come standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

The Sonata offers a spacious interior, a comfortable ride, and a host of standard features. However, it has also received some complaints about its interior quality, lack of horsepower in the base engine, and its fuel economy.

**Engine**

**2.5L GDI I4**

* **Engine Stalling:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** The engine may suddenly stall while driving, especially at low speeds or when coming to a stop.
    * **Solution:** The issue can be fixed by updating the engine control module (ECM) software.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $50 - $200+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** The fuel injectors may fail, causing the engine to run rough, hesitate, or stall.
    * **Solution:** The fuel injectors will need to be replaced.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $400 - $800+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**1.6L Turbo GDI I4**

* **No Issues Found**

**2.5L Turbocharged GDI I4**

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** The turbocharger may fail, resulting in a loss of power and increased fuel consumption.
    * **Solution:** The turbocharger will need to be replaced.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission may slip or hesitate when shifting gears.
    * **Solution:** The transmission fluid may need to be replaced or the transmission may need to be rebuilt.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $3,000+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Exterior Trim Issues:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** The exterior trim, such as the bumpers or side mirrors, may become loose or fall off.
    * **Solution:** The trim will need to be replaced.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** The dashboard may rattle or make noises while driving.
    * **Solution:** The dashboard panel will need to be removed and reattached with sound deadening material.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $50 - $150+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

* **Center Console Noise:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** The center console may rattle or make noises while driving.
    * **Solution:** The center console will need to be removed and reassembled with sound deadening material.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $250+
    * **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary**

The 2023 Hyundai Sonata has a few potential issues, but most of them are minor and relatively inexpensive to fix. The most serious issue is the turbocharger failure, which can be costly to repair. Overall, the Sonata is a reliable and affordable vehicle that offers a comfortable and spacious interior.
