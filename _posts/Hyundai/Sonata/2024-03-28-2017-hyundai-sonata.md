**Introduction to the 2017 Hyundai Sonata**

The 2017 Hyundai Sonata is a mid-size sedan available with a choice of three engines:

* 2.4L GDI 4-cylinder (185 hp, 178 lb-ft torque)
* 1.6L Turbo GDI 4-cylinder (178 hp, 195 lb-ft torque)
* 2.0L Turbo GDI 4-cylinder (245 hp, 260 lb-ft torque)

Transmission options include a 6-speed automatic, 8-speed automatic, and 7-speed dual-clutch automatic. The Sonata is praised for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and advanced safety features. However, it has also received some criticism for its lackluster handling and uninspired exterior design.

## Engine

**2.4L GDI 4-cylinder**

* **Engine Knock**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or tapping sound coming from the engine, especially when accelerating.
    * Solution: Replacement of engine bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, rough idle, or loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacement of fuel injectors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.6L Turbo GDI 4-cylinder**

* **Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups.
    * Solution: Replacement of piston rings or engine rebuild.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbo GDI 4-cylinder**

* **Turbocharger Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, whistling or screeching sounds from the engine bay.
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Transmission

**6-speed Automatic**

* **Hesitation and Jerking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or jerks when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, software updates, or transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fails to shift or shifts erratically.
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush or transmission replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7-speed Dual-Clutch Automatic**

* **Vibrations and Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission vibrates or produces excessive noise during shifts.
    * Solution: Replacement of clutches or transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Suspension

* **Front Strut Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Replacement of front struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $900+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear end of the vehicle sags or bounces excessively.
    * Solution: Replacement of rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Electrical

* **Electrical System Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical components, such as lights, radio, or windows, malfunction or fail to operate.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Interior

* **Center Console Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Center console vibrates or rattles during driving.
    * Solution: Tightening of console mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Steering Wheel Squeak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel squeaks when turning.
    * Solution: Lubrication of steering wheel column.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
    * Recalls: None Found

## Exterior

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or condensation inside headlight lenses.
    * Solution: Resealing or replacement of headlights.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2017 Hyundai Sonata has received mixed reviews from owners and critics. While it offers a spacious and comfortable interior, it has been plagued by several issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and interior. The severity of these issues varies, with some being minor inconveniences and others potentially costly repairs. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before purchasing a Sonata.
