# 2016 Hyundai HD65

### Overview
The 2016 Hyundai HD65 is a commercial light-duty truck known for its robust construction and utility. Designed for demanding work environments, it prioritizes durability and operational efficiency. Reliability data for this model year indicates a generally solid performance, with most reported issues being isolated incidents rather than widespread systemic failures. Owners and fleet managers can expect a dependable vehicle, provided regular maintenance is performed. The focus of this analysis is on common mechanical and electrical components that have seen specific reports of concern, excluding routine wear and tear.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L J2 Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling noises from the engine bay.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**Glow Plug Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, especially in cold weather; check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 6-Speed Manual
**Synchronizer Ring Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty engaging certain gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission disassembly and replacement of worn synchronizer rings.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2000


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Wiring Harness
**Corrosion and Shorts**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical component failures (lights, indicators, dashboard), warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspection, cleaning of corroded connections, repair or replacement of damaged wiring.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


### Alternator
**Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Battery warning light illuminated, dimming headlights, weak battery.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $900

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Hyundai HD65 demonstrates good overall reliability for a commercial vehicle. The most frequently reported issues tend to be related to the powertrain, specifically turbocharger problems and clutch wear in manual transmissions, which are not uncommon in heavy-duty applications. Electrical system issues, particularly wiring harness corrosion, can also arise and require attention. Suspension components like leaf springs may experience wear over time. However, the exterior and interior generally hold up well. With proactive maintenance and addressing minor issues promptly, the HD65 remains a capable and dependable workhorse.
