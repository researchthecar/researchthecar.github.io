# 2016 Hyundai HD78

### Overview
The 2016 Hyundai HD78 is a commercial light-duty truck known for its robust construction and utility. Designed for various commercial applications, it prioritizes durability and operational efficiency. Reliability data for the HD78 generally indicates a solid performance for its class, with most reported issues being minor or related to specific component wear rather than systemic failures. Owners and fleet managers often cite its straightforward mechanicals as a contributing factor to its dependable nature in demanding work environments. Regular maintenance is key to maximizing its lifespan and preventing common issues.

---


## Engine

### 3.9L Cummins ISF3.8 Diesel
**Glow Plug Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting in cold weather, engine warning light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300

**EGR Valve Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased emissions, rough idling.
- **Solution:** Cleaning or replacement of the EGR valve.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 5-Speed Manual (M5S-580)
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Clutch replacement (clutch kit).
- **Cost:** $600 - $1200


### 6-Speed Manual (M6S-750)
**Synchronizer Ring Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding noise when shifting gears, difficulty engaging gears.
- **Solution:** Transmission internal repair, replacement of synchronizer rings.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


## Suspension

### Standard Leaf Spring Suspension
**Leaf Spring Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven ride height, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of broken leaf springs.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per axle

**Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Excessive bouncing, poor handling, visible fluid leaks from shocks.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400 per pair


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Wiring Harness Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical faults, non-functional lights or accessories.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of corroded wiring sections.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Exterior

### Body Panels and Frame
**Rust and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust spots, particularly on wheel arches, door sills, and frame rails, especially in areas with harsh climates.
- **Solution:** Rust treatment, panel repair, or replacement.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500+


## Interior

### Cabin Components
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, or intermittent airflow.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400

**Seat Upholstery Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Tearing or excessive wear on driver's seat.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seat cover.
- **Cost:** $150 - $350


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Hyundai HD78 demonstrates good overall reliability for a commercial vehicle. The primary areas of concern tend to be related to the powertrain, specifically clutch wear in manual transmissions and potential issues with the EGR system or glow plugs in the diesel engine. Suspension components like leaf springs and shock absorbers can also require attention due to the nature of commercial use. Electrical issues, such as alternator failure, are not uncommon but are generally repairable. Exterior rust can be a factor in certain environments. Interior wear is typical for a work vehicle. With consistent maintenance, the HD78 is a dependable choice for its intended applications.
