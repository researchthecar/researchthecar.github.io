# 2009 Hyundai Entourage

### Overview
The 2009 Hyundai Entourage, a family-oriented minivan, generally offers a decent level of reliability for its class and model year. While not without its potential issues, many owners report satisfactory ownership experiences. Common areas of concern tend to be related to the powertrain and certain electrical components, though these are not universally experienced. Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity of this vehicle. The Entourage was designed with practicality and passenger comfort in mind, and for many, it fulfills these roles effectively. However, potential buyers should be aware of specific recurring problems that may require attention.

---


## Engine

### 3.8L V6
**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil spots under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets (e.g., valve cover, oil pan).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Knocking/Ticking Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Audible knocking or ticking sounds from the engine, especially on startup or under load.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential repair of internal engine components, lifters, or timing chain issues.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500+


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerking or rough gear changes, transmission slipping out of gear, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter service, solenoid replacement, or full transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $400 - $2500+


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Struts/Shocks**
- **Symptoms:** Bouncy ride, excessive body roll, clunking noises over bumps.
- **Solution:** Replacement of struts and/or shocks.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800

**Sway Bar Links**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises, especially when turning or going over uneven surfaces.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn sway bar links.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Electrical

### General Electrical System
**Power Window Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows failing to operate, slow operation, or getting stuck.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of window regulators, motors, or switches.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500

**Infotainment System Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Radio/navigation system freezing, unresponsiveness, or audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software updates, or replacement of the head unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2009 Hyundai Entourage is a practical family vehicle with a generally acceptable reliability record for its age. The most common concerns revolve around potential engine oil leaks and transmission issues, particularly harsh shifting. Suspension components like struts and sway bar links can also wear over time, leading to noise and ride quality degradation. Electrical gremlins, such as power window problems and infotainment system glitches, are not uncommon. While these issues can be costly to repair, they are often manageable with timely maintenance and addressing problems as they arise. Overall, it can be a reliable choice if well-maintained, but potential buyers should factor in the possibility of these repairs.
