**Introduction:**

The 2022 Hyundai Veloster N is a sporty compact hatchback that offers impressive performance with its 2.0L turbocharged inline-four engine paired with a six-speed manual transmission. It boasts 250 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque. Despite its commendable qualities, the Veloster N has faced several issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Inline-Four Engine**
    * **Hesitation on Acceleration:** Engine hesitates or stutters during acceleration, particularly at low speeds.
        * Solution: Software update to recalibrate the engine control unit (ECU).
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Rough Idle:** Engine idles rough or stalls after starting.
        * Solution: Replacing the fuel injectors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Manual Transmission**
    * **Difficulty Shifting:** Gears are hard to engage or shift smoothly.
        * Solution: Replacing the shift linkage or transmission fluid.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Stiff Suspension:** Vehicles equipped with the Performance Package experience excessive stiffness, leading to a harsh ride.
        * Solution: Replacing the factory suspension with aftermarket components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery drains quickly or fails to recharge.
        * Solution: Replacing the battery or addressing electrical system issues.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches:** Infotainment system freezes, crashes, or displays errors.
        * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacements.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Trunk Latch Issue:** Trunk latch malfunctions, preventing the trunk from opening or closing properly.
        * Solution: Replacing the trunk latch.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Spoiler Vibration:** Rear spoiler vibrates excessively at high speeds.
        * Solution: Installing a spoiler support bracket.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Door Panel Noises:** Door panels rattle or squeak when driving over rough surfaces.
        * Solution: Installing sound-dampening material or tightening screws.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $100+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** Climate control system fails to adjust temperature or blow air properly.
        * Solution: Replacing the climate control module or HVAC components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2022 Hyundai Veloster N has experienced several issues across various components. While some issues are minor and can be easily resolved, others, such as engine hesitations and suspension stiffness, can be more serious and impact the overall driving experience. The cost of repairs varies widely, but most issues can be addressed for under $500. It is important to note that some issues may require more extensive repairs, increasing the overall cost.
