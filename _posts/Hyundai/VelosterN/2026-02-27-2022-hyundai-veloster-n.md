# 2022 Hyundai Veloster N Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Hyundai Veloster N, buyers should familiarize themselves with owner-reported concerns gathered from driver feedback. While this high-performance hatchback delivers engaging driving dynamics, some drivers have reported electronic power steering issues and isolated powertrain quirks. Reviewing these potential areas can help prospective buyers inspect a used model more thoroughly before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 275 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque, offering strong performance for spirited driving. Overall mechanical reliability is generally solid, though isolated owner reports note rare component failures like fuel injector issues.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission features rev-matching capabilities and provides a direct, engaging connection for driving enthusiasts. It is generally robust when properly maintained.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT)

The 8-speed wet dual-clutch automatic transmission delivers rapid gear shifts and includes paddle shifters and an 'N Grin Shift' boost mode. Buyers should verify proper shift quality and responsiveness during a test drive.

## Steering

### Electronic Power Steering System

The steering system relies on an electronic power assist setup integrated with advanced driver assistance features. Some owners have experienced unexpected steering assist drops or warning messages.

**Steering Assist Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that electronic power steering assist can suddenly fail while driving at highway speeds, resulting in heavy steering effort and warning lights on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic scanning of the electronic power steering module and potentially replacing faulty EPS components or fuses.

**Lane Departure Warning Intermittent Faults**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners experience unexpected warning messages regarding the lane departure warning system or driver attention monitor coinciding with steering resistance.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera sensors, software calibration, and related wiring harnesses for proper function.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V746000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Veloster+N&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V528000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Veloster+N&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update engine control module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Veloster N is a thrilling hot hatchback that appeals strongly to driving enthusiasts. While it generally offers dependable performance, prospective buyers should carefully check the electronic power steering system and driver assistance warning lights during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything operates correctly.
