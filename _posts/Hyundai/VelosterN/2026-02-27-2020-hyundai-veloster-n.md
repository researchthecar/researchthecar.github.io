# 2020 Hyundai Veloster N Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Hyundai Veloster N is a performance-focused hatchback known for its engaging driving dynamics and punchy turbocharged powertrain. While overall major mechanical complaints remain relatively sparse for this model year, prospective buyers should review available owner feedback regarding specific electronic and steering systems. As with any sport compact, verifying proper maintenance and checking for signs of hard driving or modifications is essential.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces 275 horsepower and 260 lb-ft of torque when equipped with the Performance Package. It provides strong acceleration and lively performance, though buyers should ensure oil changes were performed regularly.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The standard 6-speed manual transmission features rev-matching capabilities and a direct shift feel. It is generally robust, making it a popular choice for driving enthusiasts.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission

Introduced for quick shifts and track readiness, this 8-speed wet dual-clutch automatic offers rapid gear changes. Buyers looking at DCT models should test drive thoroughly to ensure smooth operation across all gears.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering (EPS)

The electronic power steering system provides adjustable weight and responsive feedback, though isolated owner reports mention unexpected assist losses.

**Power Steering Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported that the electric power steering can fail suddenly while driving, accompanied by a warning light and heavy steering effort.
- **Solution:** Cycling the vehicle's ignition may temporarily restore function, but a diagnostic inspection by a professional technician is recommended to identify faulty EPS components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V528000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Veloster+N&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update engine control module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Hyundai Veloster N offers thrilling driving dynamics and distinctive styling for enthusiast buyers. While complaint volume is low, prospective owners should thoroughly test the electric power steering and inspect the vehicle for any history of modification or neglect. A pre-purchase inspection by a qualified mechanic will help ensure the car remains a reliable and exciting daily driver or weekend toy.
