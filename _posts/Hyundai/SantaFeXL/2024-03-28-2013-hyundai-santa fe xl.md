**Introduction:**

The 2013 Hyundai Santa Fe XL is a mid-size SUV that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and a variety of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 2.4L four-cylinder that produces 175 horsepower and 168 lb-ft of torque. The optional engines are a 3.3L V6 with 290 horsepower and 252 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder with 264 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed automatic and a five-speed automatic.

While the Santa Fe XL has received positive reviews for its value and practicality, it has also been reported to have some common issues.

**Engine**
* **2.4L Four-Cylinder**
    * **Intake Manifold Gasket Leak:** Oil and coolant can leak from the intake manifold gasket, causing overheating and engine damage.
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip and potentially damaging the engine.
    * **Valve Cover Gasket Leak:** Oil may leak from the valve cover gasket, causing oil loss and engine damage.
* **3.3L V6**
    * **Cylinder Head Gasket Leak:** Coolant may leak from the cylinder head gasket, causing overheating and engine damage.
    * **Oil Pan Gasket Leak:** Oil may leak from the oil pan gasket, causing oil loss and engine damage.
* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
    * **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger may fail, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel economy.

**Transmission**
* **Six-Speed Automatic**
    * **Slipping:** The transmission may slip or shudder when shifting, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel economy.
* **Five-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, causing a loss of power and reduced fuel economy.

**Suspension**
* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:** The rear sway bar link may fail, causing a rattling noise and reduced stability.
* **Lower Ball Joint Failure:** The lower ball joint may fail, causing the wheel to come loose and potentially causing an accident.

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to not start.
* **Headlight Malfunction:** The headlights may malfunction, causing reduced visibility and safety concerns.
* **Power Window Failure:** The power windows may fail to operate, causing inconvenience and safety concerns.

**Exterior**
* **Liftgate Latch Failure:** The liftgate latch may fail, causing the liftgate to open unintentionally and potentially causing injury.
* **Rear Bumper Corrosion:** The rear bumper may corrode prematurely, causing cosmetic damage and reducing the value of the vehicle.

**Interior**
* **HVAC System Failure:** The HVAC system may fail, causing a loss of heating or cooling and reduced comfort.
* **Cruise Control Failure:** The cruise control may fail, causing the vehicle to not maintain a set speed and reducing safety.

**Summary:**

The 2013 Hyundai Santa Fe XL has been reported to have some common issues, including engine leaks, transmission problems, suspension failures, electrical malfunctions, and exterior and interior issues. While the severity and cost of these issues can vary, it is important to be aware of them before purchasing or owning this vehicle.
