**Introduction:**

The 2017 Hyundai Santa Fe XL is a three-row SUV that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and a variety of features. It is available with a choice of two engines: a 3.3-liter V6 with 290 horsepower and 252 lb-ft of torque, or a 2.4-liter four-cylinder with 185 horsepower and 178 lb-ft of torque. Transmission options include a six-speed automatic for the V6 and a six-speed manual or six-speed automatic for the four-cylinder.

Owners generally praise the Santa Fe XL for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and long list of standard features. However, some have reported issues with the engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior.

**Engine**

* **V6 3.3L**
    * **Cylinder Head Warping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Warping of the cylinder head can cause coolant leaks, overheating, and engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacement of the cylinder head.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: N/A

* **2.4L I4**
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the timing chain tensioner can cause the timing chain to jump or break, resulting in catastrophic engine damage.
        * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner and associated components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
        * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V353000

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission may slip out of gear, causing hesitation or loss of power.
        * Solution: Replacement or rebuild of the transmission.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: N/A
* **Six-Speed Manual**
    * **No Known Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: A clunking noise may be heard from the front suspension when driving over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of the front shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Wiring Harness Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Faulty wiring harnesses can cause a variety of electrical problems, such as flickering lights, power window failure, and engine stalling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the affected wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Tailgate Liftgate Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The tailgate liftgate may fail to open or close properly.
    * Solution: Replacement of the tailgate liftgate assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Panel Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard panel may develop cracks over time.
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard panel.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2017 Hyundai Santa Fe XL has received mixed reviews from owners. While some report a reliable and comfortable experience, others have encountered significant issues with the engine, transmission, and electrical system. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but they can be a potential concern for prospective buyers.
