**Interior**

* **HVAC System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The HVAC system may malfunction, causing the temperature control to become unresponsive or the system to blow only cold air.
    * Solution: Replace the HVAC module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Lag**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience lag or become unresponsive, especially when using Android Auto or Apple CarPlay.
    * Solution: Update the system software or replace the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Hyundai Santa Fe XL has a good reliability record with relatively few reported issues. The most common interior problems are related to the HVAC system and the infotainment system. These issues can be moderately expensive to fix, but they are not considered severe or widespread.
