This analysis focuses on documented issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for the 2015 Santa Fe XL across its various configurations.

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Hyundai Santa Fe XL demonstrates solid reliability, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. The most frequently cited issues are related to the electrical system, specifically intermittent battery drain and occasional infotainment system glitches, which are generally manageable and not indicative of systemic failures. Owners can expect a dependable vehicle with minimal unexpected repair costs beyond standard maintenance. Regular servicing and prompt attention to any minor electrical anomalies will contribute to the long-term durability of the Santa Fe XL.
