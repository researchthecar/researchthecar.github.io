# 2019 Hyundai Santa Fe XL Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Hyundai Santa Fe XL offers three-row family capability and generous cargo space, making it an appealing choice on the used market. However, prospective buyers should review known owner complaints regarding specific mechanical and body components before making a purchase. Thoroughly inspecting service records and verifying the completion of safety campaigns will help ensure a more reliable ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L V6

The 3.3L V6 engine produces approximately 290 horsepower and 252 lb-ft of torque, providing strong pulling power for the three-row crossover. While generally capable, owners have flagged isolated incidents involving major internal failures and oil consumption.

**Engine Failure and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported experiencing check engine lights followed by unexpected engine stalling and the need for a complete engine replacement.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the engine diagnostics and oil consumption patterns; severe cases may require component rebuilding or replacement.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Owners note increased oil consumption after major internal engine work, such as timing chain replacements.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels frequently between changes and consult a professional technician if rapid fluid loss persists.
- **Cost:** 3500

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the V6 engine to deliver smooth, predictable gear changes across front-wheel and all-wheel drive models. This gearbox generally maintains a solid reputation with few recurring owner complaints.

## Electrical

### Battery and Charging

Electrical complaints focus primarily on power accessories and battery drain concerns.

**Premature Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may experience unexpected dead batteries, particularly in colder weather or short periods after purchase.
- **Solution:** Test the charging system and battery health, replacing weak units with correctly specified cold-cranking amp batteries.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Owner reports highlight vulnerabilities in the exterior glass components, particularly concerning panoramic roofs.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a sudden, loud cracking sound while driving as the panoramic glass panel shatters and scatters glass throughout the cabin.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for any micro-fractures before purchase and replace the assembly if structural weakness or damage is identified.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V056000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe+XL&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module could malfunction and cause an electrical short, which could result in an engine compartment fire. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the ABS multi-fuse, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Hyundai Santa Fe XL is a spacious family vehicle, but used car shoppers should carefully evaluate its mechanical health. Pay close attention to engine performance, verify that all safety recalls have been addressed, and inspect features like the panoramic sunroof for any signs of stress. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by an independent mechanic is strongly recommended.
