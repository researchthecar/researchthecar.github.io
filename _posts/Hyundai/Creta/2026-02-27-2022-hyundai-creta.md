### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Hyundai Creta demonstrates solid reliability across most of its key systems. The naturally aspirated and turbocharged petrol engines, along with the manual and CVT transmissions, have shown no significant widespread issues. The primary area of note is the 7-speed DCT, which has seen some reports of minor hesitation or jerky shifts, often resolvable with software updates. Electrical components, particularly the infotainment system, may occasionally require a reset or update. Overall, the Creta is a dependable choice in its segment, with most potential issues being minor and manageable, contributing to a positive ownership experience.
