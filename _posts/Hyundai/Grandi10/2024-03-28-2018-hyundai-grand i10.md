**Introduction**

The 2018 Hyundai Grand i10 is a compact hatchback that offers a range of engine and transmission options. It is powered by a 1.2-liter gasoline engine that produces 83 horsepower and 114 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with either a 5-speed manual or a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Grand i10 is known for its affordability, fuel efficiency, and spacious interior. However, it has been plagued by several issues that have affected its reliability.

**Engine**

**1.2L Gasoline Engine**

**Engine Overheating**
Description/Symptoms: The engine temperature gauge rises to the red zone, and the vehicle loses power.
Solution: Replace the thermostat and flush the cooling system.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: NHTSA ID: 18V413000

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual Transmission**

**Difficulty Shifting Gears**
Description/Symptoms: The gearshift lever becomes difficult to move, and the vehicle may pop out of gear.
Solution: Replace the shift linkage or the transmission mounts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Electrical System Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's electrical system fails, causing the loss of power to the engine, lights, and other components.
Solution: Replace the battery or the alternator.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattle**
Description/Symptoms: The dashboard rattles when the vehicle is driven over rough roads.
Solution: Secure the loose components or apply sound dampening material.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2018 Hyundai Grand i10 has a range of issues that can affect its reliability. The severity of the issues varies from minor annoyances, such as dashboard rattles, to serious problems, such as engine overheating and electrical system failure. The cost of repairs can also be significant, ranging from $200 to $1,000 or more. Overall, the Grand i10's reliability is below average, and potential buyers should be aware of the potential issues before purchasing a used vehicle.
