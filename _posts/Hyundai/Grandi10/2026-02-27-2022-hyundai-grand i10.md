### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Hyundai Grand i10 generally exhibits good reliability, particularly in its powertrain and chassis components. The 1.2L Kappa MPI engine and its associated manual and automatic transmissions have proven to be robust. Suspension systems are also largely trouble-free. The most commonly reported, though not widespread, issues tend to be minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system and, as with many vehicles, occasional battery failures. These are typically manageable and do not indicate systemic flaws. Overall, the Grand i10 offers a dependable ownership experience for its class, with most potential issues being minor and cost-effective to resolve.
