**2021 Hyundai Grand i10**

The 2021 Hyundai Grand i10 is a subcompact hatchback that has been available in India since 2021. It is powered by a 1.2-liter Kappa engine that produces 82 horsepower and 114 Nm of torque. The engine is mated to a 5-speed manual transmission or a 5-speed automatic transmission. The Grand i10 is available in three variants: Magna, Sportz, and Asta. The Magna variant comes with features such as power windows, air conditioning, and a music system. The Sportz variant adds features such as alloy wheels, fog lamps, and a rear spoiler. The Asta variant is the top-of-the-line variant and comes with features such as a touchscreen infotainment system, automatic climate control, and a sunroof.

The Grand i10 has been praised for its spacious cabin, fuel efficiency, and easy-to-drive nature. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of power and features.

**Interior**

* **Loose Door Panels**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door panels may become loose, causing a rattling noise.
    * Solution: Replacement of the door panel clips.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Power Window Switches**
    * Description/Symptoms: The power window switches may become faulty, causing the windows to operate intermittently or not at all.
    * Solution: Replacement of the power window switches.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $100 per switch
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2021 Hyundai Grand i10 is a reliable and fuel-efficient subcompact hatchback. However, it has been reported to have some minor issues, such as loose door panels and faulty power window switches. The severity of these issues is generally low, and the cost to fix them is typically affordable.
