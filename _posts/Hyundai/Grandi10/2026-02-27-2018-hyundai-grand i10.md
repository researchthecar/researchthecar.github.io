### Summary Verdict
The 2018 Hyundai Grand i10 demonstrates strong reliability across its core mechanical systems, including the engine and transmission options (1.0L and 1.2L petrol engines with manual or automatic transmissions). Suspension, exterior, and interior components are also generally robust. The most commonly cited, though not widespread, issue relates to potential electrical gremlins, specifically occasional battery drain, which can be resolved through diagnosis and repair. Overall, the Grand i10 is a dependable choice for budget-conscious buyers seeking a reliable city car with minimal risk of major mechanical failures.
