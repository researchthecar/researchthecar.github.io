**2016 Hyundai H300**

The 2016 Hyundai H300 is a full-size passenger van that offers a spacious interior and a powerful engine. It is available with a 2.5-liter diesel engine that produces 130 horsepower and 229 lb-ft of torque and can be paired with a 5-speed manual or 6-speed automatic transmission. The H300 is known for its reliability and affordability, but it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine**

* **2.5-liter Diesel Engine**
    * **Fuel Injector failure**
        * Loss of power, black smoke from exhaust, rough idle
        * Replacement of fuel injectors
        * $400 - $800+
    * **EGR Valve failure**
        * Check engine light, poor fuel economy
        * Replacement of EGR valve
        * $300 - $500+
    * **Turbocharger failure**
        * Loss of power, whistling noise from engine
        * Replacement of turbocharger
        * $1,000 - $1,500+

**Transmission**

* **5-speed Manual**
    * **Difficulty shifting gears**
        * Worn or damaged synchronizers
        * Repair or replacement of synchronizers
        * $500 - $1,000+
* **6-speed Automatic**
    * **Slipping transmission**
        * Worn or damaged clutches
        * Overhaul or replacement of transmission
        * $1,500 - $3,000+
    * **Shifting problems**
        * Faulty solenoid or transmission control module
        * Replacement of solenoid or control module
        * $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise**
    * Worn or damaged ball joints or struts
    * Replacement of ball joints or struts
    * $300 - $600+

**Electrical**

* **Battery drain**
    * Faulty alternator or parasitic draw
    * Replacement of alternator or diagnosis and repair of parasitic draw
    * $300 - $600+
* **Electrical glitches**
    * Faulty wiring or electrical components
    * Diagnosis and repair of faulty components
    * $100 - $300+

**Exterior**

* **Rust on body panels**
    * Lack of proper corrosion protection
    * Repair or replacement of rusted panels
    * $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard rattle**
    * Loose or damaged dashboard components
    * Tightening or replacement of components
    * $100 - $300+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2016 Hyundai H300 is a reliable vehicle, but it has been reported to have some issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to repair them can be significant. It is important to research and consider the potential risks before purchasing this vehicle.
