**2017 Hyundai H300 Overview:**

The 2017 Hyundai H300 is a compact van available with two engine options: a 2.5-liter CRDi inline-four diesel engine and a 2.4-liter Theta II inline-four gasoline engine. Both are mated to a 6-speed manual transmission. The H300 is praised for its spacious interior, fuel efficiency, and affordable price. However, owners have reported several issues with various components.

**Engine**

**2.5-liter CRDi Diesel**

* **EGR Valve Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Excessive engine smoke, reduced power, and check engine light illumination.
    * **Solution:** Replace the EGR valve.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Loss of power, black smoke from the exhaust, and whistling noises.
    * **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500

**2.4-liter Theta II Gasoline**

No engine issues reported.

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises, and shuddering during acceleration.
    * **Solution:** Replace the clutch assembly.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,000

**Suspension**

* **Rear Leaf Spring Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Sagging rear end, uneven tire wear, and clunking noises.
    * **Solution:** Replace the leaf springs.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $400

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Harnesses:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Intermittent electrical issues, such as flickering lights, radio malfunctions, and starting problems.
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace damaged electrical harnesses.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $700
* **Alternator Failure:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, difficulty starting the vehicle, and battery warning light illumination.
    * **Solution:** Replace the alternator.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $800

**Exterior**

No exterior issues reported.

**Interior**

* **Heater/AC Malfunction:**
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Poor heating or cooling performance, strange noises, and intermittent operation.
    * **Solution:** Repair or replace damaged components in the heater/AC system.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $600

**Other**

* **DPF Filter Blockage:**
    * **Engine Type:** 2.5-liter CRDi Diesel
    * **Description/Symptoms:** Reduced engine power, increased fuel consumption, and excessive smoke from the exhaust.
    * **Solution:** Clean or replace the DPF filter.
    * **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000

**Summary**

The 2017 Hyundai H300 has several common issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and electrical system. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be costly and require major repairs. However, it's important to note that not all H300s will experience these problems, and overall, it remains a reliable and practical vehicle for many drivers.
