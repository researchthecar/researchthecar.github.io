### Summary Verdict
The 2020 Hyundai EX6 generally exhibits good reliability, with its core powertrain and chassis components performing well. The most frequently cited technical concerns revolve around the 7-speed Dual-Clutch Transmission (DCT), which can sometimes exhibit minor shifting anomalies, and occasional electrical gremlins such as infotainment system freezes or intermittent battery drain. These issues are typically addressable through software updates or targeted repairs. For the most part, owners can expect a dependable vehicle with minimal unexpected technical problems beyond standard wear and tear. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any emerging symptoms are recommended to ensure continued optimal performance.
