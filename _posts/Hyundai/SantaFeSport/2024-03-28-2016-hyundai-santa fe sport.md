**Introduction:**

The 2016 Hyundai Santa Fe Sport is a mid-size SUV powered by a 2.4L inline-4 engine producing 185 horsepower and 178 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Santa Fe Sport is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and impressive safety features. However, some owners have reported experiencing certain issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.4L Inline-4**

* **Intake Manifold Issue:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, loss of power, increased emissions
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall ID 20V626000

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slips, delays when shifting gears, shuddering
    * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid flush
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises when turning or going over bumps
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, difficulty starting vehicle
    * Solution: Inspect and replace faulty battery or electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* **Electrical Short:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blowing fuses, flickering lights, loss of power
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical short
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall ID 21V284000

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Small cracks appearing on the windshield
    * Solution: Replace windshield
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on insurance coverage

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Crash:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezes, goes black, or restarts unexpectedly
    * Solution: Software update, system reset
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free if under warranty, otherwise $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

While the Hyundai Santa Fe Sport has generally positive reviews, some owners have experienced issues with various components. These issues include engine intake manifold problems, transmission shifting hesitation, suspension strut mount noise, electrical problems, exterior windshield cracking, and interior infotainment system crashes. The severity and cost of these issues vary depending on the specific problem.
