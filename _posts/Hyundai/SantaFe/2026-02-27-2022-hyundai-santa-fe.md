# 2022 Hyundai Santa Fe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Hyundai Santa Fe offers stylish design, a comfortable interior, and modern tech, making it a popular choice in the midsize SUV segment. However, prospective buyers should review certain recurring mechanical and electrical concerns documented by owners. While many drivers enjoy its everyday versatility and feature list, checking service history and remaining powertrain coverage is recommended before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Naturally Aspirated I4

This base 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces 191 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for daily commuting and routine driving.

### 2.5L Turbocharged I4

The available 2.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates 281 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration and strong passing power.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light, engine misfires, rough running, or fuel delivery issues.
- **Solution:** Requires replacement of faulty fuel injectors.

**Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Engine suddenly loses power or stalls while driving or attempting to start.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic inspection required; software updates or component replacement may be needed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The conventional 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with non-turbo models to deliver smooth and predictable gear changes.

**Shifter or Park Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Dashboard displays a shifter malfunction warning message and prevents shifting out of park or neutral.
- **Solution:** Dealer inspection and replacement of faulty shifter assembly or software reprogramming.

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission offers quick shifts on turbocharged trims but requires careful attention to service history.

**Transmission Shudder and Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Hesitation, jerking, or shuddering during gear shifts, along with potential loss of motive power.
- **Solution:** Requires professional diagnosis, clutch adjustment, or transmission replacement under warranty.

## Exterior

### Power Windows and Accessories

Cabin electronics and convenience features generally function well, though occasional component failures have been reported.

**Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Power windows suddenly drop into the door panel and fail to stay up or operate correctly.
- **Solution:** Replace the failed window regulator and motor assembly.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced driver assistance systems provide added protection but can occasionally trigger false warnings or unexpected interventions.

**Unexpected Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The automatic emergency braking system engages unexpectedly while driving with no apparent obstacles ahead.
- **Solution:** Have the dealer check sensor calibration and update driver-assist software.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V524000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and tighten, or replace the fuel pipe as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V197000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The oil supply pipe to the turbocharger may crack, which could result in an oil leak in the engine compartment. An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the turbocharger oil supply pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V746000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V181000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Water accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse and wire extension kit as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V879000: Back Over Prevention: Sensing System: Camera](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview image that does not display reduces the driver's visibility and increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the rearview camera, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Santa Fe is a feature-packed midsize SUV that appeals to families and daily drivers alike. While most owners enjoy its comfort and performance, buyers should verify the condition of the powertrain and electronics, particularly concerning fuel injectors, transmission operation, and driver-assist sensors. Purchasing a certified pre-owned model or one with remaining factory warranty coverage can offer extra peace of mind.
