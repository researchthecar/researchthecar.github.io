# 2022 Hyundai Santa Fe

### Overview
The 2022 Hyundai Santa Fe generally presents a strong profile for reliability, building on Hyundai's reputation for producing dependable vehicles. This mid-size SUV offers a comfortable ride, practical interior, and a good range of features, making it a popular choice for families and individuals alike. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected problems, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen occasional reports of issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential areas of attention for the 2022 model year across its various configurations.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Hyundai Santa Fe demonstrates commendable reliability, with the majority of its systems performing without significant reported issues. The naturally aspirated 2.5L engine and its associated automatic transmission are particularly robust. The turbocharged variant's dual-clutch transmission has seen some minor reports of shifting anomalies, though these are generally addressable with software updates. Electrical components, primarily the infotainment system, may occasionally exhibit minor glitches, but these are not widespread and typically resolved with simple fixes. Overall, the Santa Fe is a solid choice for consumers prioritizing dependability, with few common mechanical or electrical faults to deter potential buyers.
