# 2019 Hyundai Santa Fe Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Hyundai Santa Fe offers a comfortable cabin, generous features, and practical everyday utility for family buyers. However, prospective buyers should approach the used market with a degree of caution due to recurring owner complaints involving engine performance, body paint durability, and auxiliary electrical systems. Careful service record checks and a thorough pre-purchase inspection are strongly recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L GDI 4-Cylinder

A naturally aspirated 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine producing around 185 horsepower, providing baseline daily performance. Buyers should closely monitor oil consumption and verify whether engine-related service campaigns or recalls have been performed.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may unexpectedly stall while operating, fail to restart immediately, or experience severe oil consumption and reduced motive power.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a certified technician; repairs may involve addressing oil consumption or software updates under applicable warranty extensions.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

A turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine generating approximately 235 horsepower, offering more responsive acceleration and stronger passing power.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An eight-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, designed to balance highway efficiency with smooth shifting behavior.

**Shifter or Park Defect**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience hesitation when shifting or observe warning messages related to the transmission and gear selector functions.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission control modules and perform recommended software updates or linkage adjustments.

## Exterior

### Factory Paint and Exterior Trim

Exterior body finishes generally maintain a modern appearance, though certain factory paint colors have demonstrated susceptibility to premature peeling.

**Paint Peeling and Corrosion**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature bubbling, chipping, and large sheets of factory paint peeling away from metal body panels like the hood.
- **Solution:** Professional repainting or body shop repair, which may involve out-of-pocket costs if factory warranty coverage has expired.
- **Cost:** $1,000+

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance and Seat Belts

Advanced safety and passive restraint systems provide good occupant protection, but sensor calibration and occasional warning light anomalies require attention.

**Unexpected Autonomous Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The collision avoidance system may occasionally trigger unwarranted heavy braking or flash warning alerts when no immediate obstruction is present.
- **Solution:** Inspect forward sensors, calibrate windshield cameras, and check for software updates at a dealership.

**Seat Belt Buckle Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belt buckles may fail to latch securely or trigger warning lights on the instrument cluster.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace faulty seat belt buckle assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V715000: Air Bags:Side/Window](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The Side Curtain Air Bag (SCAB) mounting bolts may interfere with and damage the air bags during deployment. In the event of a crash, if the air bags become damaged during deployment they may not inflate properly, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will install a protective cover over the SCAB installation bolts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V181000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Fe&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Water accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse and wire extension kit as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Hyundai Santa Fe remains an attractive used midsize SUV choice thanks to its comfortable ride and comprehensive feature list. However, prospective buyers should verify that all open engine and recall services have been completed, inspect the vehicle for exterior paint defects, and ensure the powertrain operates smoothly during a test drive.
