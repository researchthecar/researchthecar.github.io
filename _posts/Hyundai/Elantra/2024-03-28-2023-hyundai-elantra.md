**Introduction:**

The 2023 Hyundai Elantra is a compact sedan that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The base engine is a 2.0-liter inline-four producing 147 horsepower and 132 lb-ft of torque, paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT). An optional 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-four produces 195 horsepower and 195 lb-ft of torque, coupled with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission (DCT).

The Elantra is praised for its fuel-efficient engines, spacious interior, and advanced safety features. However, it is criticized for its uninspiring performance and lack of premium cabin materials.

**Engine:**

**2.0-liter Inline-Four with CVT:**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Increased oil consumption, resulting in frequent oil top-ups or oil leaks.
    * Solution: Replacement of piston rings or engine overhaul.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy engine operation, particularly during start-up and acceleration.
    * Solution: Replacement of timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**1.6-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four with DCT:**

* **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, excessive turbo boost pressure, or check engine light.
    * Solution: Replacement of turbocharger wastegate actuator or assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**CVT:**

* **Hesitation and Jerking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitation or jerking during gear changes, especially while accelerating.
    * Solution: Software updates, transmission fluid flush, or CVT replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**DCT:**

* **Clutch Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power and acceleration, transmission shuddering, or difficulty shifting.
    * Solution: Clutch replacement or transmission overhaul.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $5,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise over bumps, especially on one side.
    * Solution: Replacement of strut mount bearing.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drained overnight or struggles to start.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system component causing drain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Short:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Blown fuses, malfunctioning electrical components, or check engine light.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system wiring or component.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Windshield Washer Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Washer fluid leaking from the base of the windshield or hoses.
    * Solution: Replacement or repair of washer fluid pump or hoses.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Door Lock Mechanism Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty locking or unlocking doors, door handle malfunctioning.
    * Solution: Replacement or repair of door lock mechanism.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent screen freezing, navigation errors, or audio issues.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Plastic Trim Panel Creaks and Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Annoying squeaking or rattling noises coming from plastic trim panels in the cabin.
    * Solution: Removal and adjustment or replacement of trim panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2023 Hyundai Elantra offers fuel-efficient engines and a spacious interior, but it is not without its issues. The engine and transmission categories have the most significant concerns, including excessive oil consumption, turbocharger failures, and CVT hesitation. Electrical issues and infotainment glitches are also present. Suspension and exterior issues are generally minor but can add to the overall inconvenience. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but some can be quite costly and require extensive repairs.
