# 2022 Hyundai Kona

### Overview
The 2022 Hyundai Kona generally presents itself as a reliable subcompact SUV, offering a good balance of features, fuel efficiency, and practicality for its segment. Hyundai's commitment to quality is evident in many aspects of the Kona's design and engineering. While no vehicle is entirely without potential concerns, the 2022 model year has demonstrated a solid track record. Owners typically report satisfaction with its maneuverability in urban environments and its user-friendly infotainment system. The available powertrain options cater to different driving preferences, from efficient base engines to more spirited turbocharged variants. Overall, the Kona is a competitive offering in its class, with a focus on delivering a dependable ownership experience.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Hyundai Kona is a strong contender in the subcompact SUV market, exhibiting good overall reliability. Major mechanical issues with the engine or transmission options (2.0L MPI Atkinson with 6-speed automatic, and 1.6L Turbo GDI with 7-speed dual-clutch) are not commonly reported. The suspension system also appears robust. The most frequently noted, though not widespread, concerns tend to be minor electrical glitches within the infotainment system, typically resolvable with software updates. Exterior and interior components generally hold up well, with no significant durability issues surfacing. For potential buyers, the Kona represents a dependable choice with a low probability of encountering significant mechanical problems.
