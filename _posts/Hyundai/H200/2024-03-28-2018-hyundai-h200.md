**Introduction:**

The 2018 Hyundai H200 is a compact MPV that is known for its spacious interior and affordable price tag. It is available with a choice of a 2.4-liter gasoline engine or a 2.5-liter diesel engine, both paired with a 6-speed manual transmission.

People generally like the H200 for its practicality, fuel efficiency, and low maintenance costs. However, there have been some issues reported with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**2.4-Liter Gasoline**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, leading to a rattling noise from the engine and potentially catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-Liter Diesel**

* **Engine Stalling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The engine may stall while driving, especially during acceleration.
    * Solution: Update the engine control module (ECM) software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (covered under warranty)
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clutch may slip or fail, making it difficult or impossible to shift gears.
    * Solution: Replace the clutch assembly.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Coil Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The coil springs can fail, resulting in a loss of ride height and handling issues.
    * Solution: Replace the coil springs.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, preventing the battery from charging properly.
    * Solution: Replace the alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Inspect the electrical system for parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the cause
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The door handles may break or become loose, making it difficult to open the doors.
    * Solution: Replace the door handles.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **HVAC System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) system may not perform properly, resulting in insufficient heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the HVAC system components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the issue
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2018 Hyundai H200 is a reliable vehicle with relatively minor issues. The most common problems reported are with the timing chain tensioner on the gasoline engine and the clutch on the manual transmission. These issues can be expensive to fix, but they are generally covered under warranty.
