**Introduction:**

The 2016 Hyundai H200 is a compact van that is available with two engine options: a 2.5-liter gasoline engine and a 2.5-liter diesel engine. Both engines are paired with a 5-speed manual transmission. The gasoline engine produces 162 horsepower and 158 lb-ft of torque, while the diesel engine produces 136 horsepower and 278 lb-ft of torque. The H200 is known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordable price. However, it has also been reported to have several issues.

**Engine Issues**

**2.5-liter Gasoline Engine**

* **Intake manifold cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold may crack, causing a loss of power and fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing chain tensioner failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner may fail, causing the timing chain to skip teeth and potentially leading to catastrophic engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.5-liter Diesel Engine**

* **EGR valve failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The EGR valve may fail, causing the engine to run poorly and emit excessive emissions.
    * Solution: Replace the EGR valve.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
