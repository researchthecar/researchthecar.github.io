**Introduction:**

The 2017 Hyundai H200 is a versatile commercial vehicle available with a variety of engine and transmission options. It offers a spacious cargo area, a comfortable cabin, and a range of features to enhance its functionality. However, like any vehicle, the H200 has some reported issues that owners may encounter.

## **Engine**

**2.5L Diesel Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard starting, rough idling, poor performance, increased fuel consumption
    * Solution: Replace faulty fuel injector(s) 
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $750+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, whistling or squealing sounds from engine bay
    * Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.4L Gasoline Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

## **Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Clutch Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Slipping or difficulty engaging gears, poor acceleration, burning smell
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty or delays when shifting gears, rough gear changes, transmission slipping
    * Solution: Overhaul or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise or clunking sounds from suspension, uneven tire wear, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace faulty strut(s)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+ per strut
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting vehicle, dim headlights, instrument panel malfunction
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty electrical components, such as alternator or battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Headlight Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dim or inoperative headlights
    * Solution: Replace faulty headlight assembly or bulb
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Exterior**

* **Rear Bumper Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sagging or detachment of rear bumper
    * Solution: Repair or replace bumper mounting brackets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Interior**

* **Dashboard Squeaks and Rattles:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises coming from the dashboard
    * Solution: Apply sound dampening material or tighten loose components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $250+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning air conditioning or heater
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair or replace faulty components, such as compressor, evaporator, or blower motor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

## **Summary**

While the 2017 Hyundai H200 offers many positive attributes, it does have some reported issues that owners may encounter. The severity of these issues varies, and not all vehicles will experience them. Some repairs can be relatively inexpensive, while others may be more costly. It's important for owners to be aware of potential problems and to have them diagnosed and repaired promptly to avoid further damage or inconvenience.
