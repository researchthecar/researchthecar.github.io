# 2023 Hyundai Tucson Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Hyundai Tucson is a stylish, feature-packed compact SUV that appeals to everyday commuters and families alike. While it offers a comfortable ride, modern technology, and a well-appointed interior, prospective used car buyers should carefully review recurring owner feedback regarding specific powertrain, electrical, and driver-assistance systems. Being aware of these reported trouble spots can help you perform a targeted inspection and ensure any remaining factory warranty coverage transfers smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L 4-Cylinder Engine

The standard 2.5-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine produces approximately 187 horsepower and 178 lb-ft of torque, offering adequate power for daily commuting and reliable everyday efficiency.

### 1.6L Turbocharged Hybrid/Plug-in Hybrid Engine

The 1.6-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder hybrid and plug-in hybrid powertrains pair with an electric motor to deliver around 226 to 261 combined horsepower, combining brisk acceleration with enhanced fuel economy.

### Fuel System

Issues related to fuel delivery components can occasionally lead to unexpected misfires and stalling.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly loses power, stutters, and stalls on the highway or during acceleration, illuminating check engine lights.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic diagnose misfire codes and replace any failed fuel injectors.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the base 2.5L engine, this conventional 8-speed automatic provides smooth, predictable gear shifts suited for daily driving.

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

Used alongside the 1.6L turbocharged hybrid configurations, this 6-speed automatic transmission delivers refined gear selection tailored for efficiency.

## Steering

### Steering Rack and Column

Steering components must be thoroughly inspected during a pre-purchase check to ensure precise control and safety.

**Steering Column and Rack Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual grinding noises when turning the wheel or, in severe reports, a loss of steering response.
- **Solution:** Inspect steering components immediately for mechanical looseness or component failure and replace worn parts.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior glass features require careful inspection for stress cracks or prior damage.

**Spontaneous Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass shatters or explodes suddenly while driving at highway speeds without prior impact.
- **Solution:** Replace the damaged glass panel and inspect the frame for stress points.

## Safety

### Forward Collision-Avoidance System

Advanced safety systems add protective measures but can occasionally experience erratic sensor triggers.

**Unexpected Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The forward collision avoidance system triggers sudden emergency braking without warning or visible obstacles present.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera and radar sensors for alignment issues or software updates at a dealership.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V526000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Fluid/Lubricant:Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Tucson&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An electric oil pump assembly that overheats increases the risk of a vehicle fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the electric oil pump controller, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V893000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Tucson&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Moisture accumulation may cause the trailer lighting to fail or an electrical short circuit. Nonfunctioning trailer lights can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a cap of the wiring, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Hyundai Tucson is a versatile and modern compact SUV, but used car shoppers should perform a thorough mechanical and electronic inspection before purchasing. Pay close attention to vehicle service records, test drive the steering and powertrain thoroughly, and verify that all safety recall campaigns have been completed by an authorized dealer.
