**Introduction:**

The 2016 Hyundai Tucson is a compact SUV offering a 2.0L 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. It offers a well-equipped interior, spacious seating, and a user-friendly infotainment system. While praised for its value and practicality, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **2.0L 4-Cylinder**
    * **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Some vehicles experience excessive oil consumption, leading to low oil levels and possible engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings and valve stem seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Hesitations/Jerking:** Issues with the transmission control module (TCM) can cause hesitation while shifting gears or abrupt jerking movements.
    * Solution: Replace or reprogram the TCM.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Prematurely Worn Bushings:** Rear suspension bushings can wear out prematurely, leading to knocking or rattling noises over bumps.
* Solution: Replace the affected bushings.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Faulty Backup Camera:** Some vehicles experience issues with the backup camera, resulting in a blurry or distorted image or no image at all.
* Solution: Replace the backup camera.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Leaks:** Vehicles equipped with a sunroof may experience water leaks, potentially damaging the interior.
* Solution: Inspect and seal the sunroof assembly.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 17V488

**Interior**

* **Loose Interior Trim Panels:** Panels on the dash and doors may become loose or rattle over time.
* Solution: Secure the panels or replace the clips.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2016 Hyundai Tucson has received generally positive reviews but has been subject to a few significant issues that can be costly to repair. The excessive oil consumption and transmission problems are particularly noteworthy, although the frequency of these issues varies. Potential buyers should research these issues and consider the potential financial implications before purchasing.
