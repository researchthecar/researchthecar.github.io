# 2019 Hyundai Tucson

### Overview
The 2019 Hyundai Tucson generally presents itself as a reliable compact SUV, offering a good balance of features, comfort, and practicality for its segment. Hyundai's commitment to quality is evident in many aspects of the vehicle's design and engineering. While most owners report a positive ownership experience with minimal unexpected repairs, like any vehicle, there are specific areas where some owners have encountered issues. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential problem areas for the 2019 Tucson across its various configurations.

### 2.4L GDI (Theta III)
**Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported higher than expected oil consumption, potentially leading to low oil levels if not monitored.
- **Solution:** Regular oil level checks and top-offs. In some cases, engine internal inspection or repair may be necessary.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** The infotainment screen may occasionally freeze or become unresponsive to touch inputs.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system resets, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have reported intermittent battery drain issues, leading to a dead battery.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from specific components, battery replacement, or alternator check.
- **Cost:** $150 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2019 Hyundai Tucson is generally a dependable vehicle with a solid reliability record. The most frequently cited issues, though not universal, tend to be related to the infotainment system's responsiveness and, for the 2.4L engine, potential oil consumption. Transmission issues are infrequent but can occur. Electrical gremlins like battery drain are also noted by a small percentage of owners. Overall, the Tucson offers a good value proposition for those seeking a practical and reasonably reliable compact SUV, provided potential buyers are aware of and prepared for these minor concerns.
