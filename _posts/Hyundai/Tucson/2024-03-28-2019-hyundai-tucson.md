**Introduction:**

The 2019 Hyundai Tucson is a compact SUV that comes with a variety of engine and transmission options. It has a spacious cabin and offers good fuel efficiency. However, it has some issues that owners should be aware of.

**Engine:**

* **2.4L GDI 4-cylinder:**
    * **Premature engine failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, knocking sounds, and decreased engine performance.
        * Solution: Engine replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $9,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **1.6L Turbocharged GDI 4-cylinder:**
    * **Turbocharger failure:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, whistling sounds, and increased oil consumption.
        * Solution: Turbocharger replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **6-speed automatic:**
    * **Transmission slipping:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed engagement, and jerking.
        * Solution: Transmission repair or replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **8-speed automatic:**
    * **No issues found.**

**Suspension:**

* **Front suspension noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling sounds from the front suspension.
    * Solution: Strut or spring replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear suspension sag:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sits lower in the rear than the front.
    * Solution: Shock or spring replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Battery drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly and requires jump-starting.
    * Solution: Identify and repair electrical component causing drain.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Headlight failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flicker or do not turn on.
    * Solution: Headlight bulb or wiring replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $300
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Sunroof leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water enters the cabin through the sunroof when it rains.
    * Solution: Sunroof seal replacement or adjustment.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Wind noise:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive wind noise from around the windows or roof.
    * Solution: Adjust or replace weatherstripping.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Climate control issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A/C or heating system does not work properly.
    * Solution: AC compressor or heater core replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard warping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard becomes wavy or distorted, especially in hot weather.
    * Solution: Dashboard replacement.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Hyundai Tucson has some significant issues with its engine, transmission, and suspension. These issues can be costly to repair and can affect the reliability and safety of the vehicle. However, it also has some positive features and reasonable fuel efficiency.
