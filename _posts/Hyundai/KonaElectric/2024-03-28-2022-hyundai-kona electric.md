**2022 Hyundai Kona Electric**

The 2022 Hyundai Kona Electric is a subcompact crossover SUV powered by a single electric motor that produces 201 horsepower and 291 lb-ft of torque. The Kona Electric is praised for its spacious interior, advanced safety features, and impressive range. However, it has received criticism for its limited cargo space and lack of all-wheel drive.

**Electric Motor**

* **Battery Drain During Parking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Kona Electric owners have reported that their vehicles are experiencing battery drain while parked, even when not in use.
    * Solution: A software update from Hyundai is required to fix the issue.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free of charge
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **No Transmission Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **No Suspension Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Display Screen Glitching**
    * Description/Symptoms: Kona Electric drivers have encountered issues with their display screens glitching, freezing, or going black.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the display screen by Hyundai is necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **12-Volt Battery Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The Kona Electric's 12-volt battery may experience premature failure, causing the vehicle to lose power or not start.
    * Solution: Replacement of the 12-volt battery is required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Roof Rack Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Kona Electric owners have reported excessive noise caused by the roof rack while driving at highway speeds.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of the roof rack by Hyundai is required.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free of charge
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V-817

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Panel Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof on some Kona Electric models may experience rattling, water leaks, or binding.
    * Solution: Replacement of the sunroof panel by Hyundai is necessary.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Free of charge
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2022 Hyundai Kona Electric has received a fair amount of complaints regarding its electrical system and display screen issues. These issues can be relatively minor but may require software updates or part replacements. The roof rack noise and sunroof panel problems affect the exterior and interior of the vehicle. Overall, the severity and cost of these issues vary, and some can be resolved at no cost to the owner.
