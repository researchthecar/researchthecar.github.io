**Introduction**

The 2021 Hyundai Kona Electric is an all-electric subcompact SUV that offers a spacious interior, ample cargo space, and an estimated range of up to 258 miles on a single charge. It is powered by a single electric motor that produces 201 horsepower and 291 lb-ft of torque.

**Exterior**

**Panoramic Sunroof Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The panoramic sunroof may fail to open or close properly, or it may make unusual noises while operating.
Solution: Replace the sunroof assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2000+

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Lag**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience lag or delays when responding to inputs.
Solution: Update the infotainment software.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Free (if under warranty)

**Heater/AC Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The heater or air conditioning system may not function properly, resulting in inadequate heating or cooling.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the faulty component, such as the blower motor, compressor, or refrigerant lines.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Hyundai Kona Electric has a good reliability record. The few issues that have been reported are relatively minor and can be fixed at a reasonable cost.
