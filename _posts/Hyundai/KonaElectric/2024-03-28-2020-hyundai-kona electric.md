**Introduction:**

The 2020 Hyundai Kona Electric is an all-electric subcompact crossover SUV known for its sleek design, advanced tech features, and impressive driving range. Powering the Kona Electric is a single synchronous permanent magnet electric motor, paired with a single-speed automatic transmission.

**Electrical:**

**High Voltage Battery Issue:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery can overheat and catch fire.
Solution: Replace the battery with a modified one.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: 22V-062

**Exterior:**

**Missing Headlight Trim:**
Description/Symptoms: Trim around the headlights can detach and fall off.
Solution: Replace the trim.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Interior:**

**Squeaky Dashboard:**
Description/Symptoms: Dashboard creaks and rattles over bumps.
Solution: Apply lubricant or replace the dashboard.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Loose Front Seat:**
Description/Symptoms: Front seat shakes or moves when driving over rough roads.
Solution: Tighten the seat bolts or replace the seat.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2020 Hyundai Kona Electric has a limited number of reported issues. The electrical issue and missing headlight trim are the most concerning, but they can be resolved with cost-effective repairs. The interior issues may cause minor annoyances, but they are not typically severe or costly to fix.
