# 2022 Hyundai Kona Electric Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Hyundai Kona Electric, buyers generally find an efficient and nimble zero-emission crossover well-suited for daily commuting. However, owner reports submitted to safety authorities highlight a few recurring electronic and charging-related glitches that are worth keeping on your radar during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### Permanent Magnet Synchronous Electric Motor (201 hp)

The 2022 Kona Electric features a front-mounted permanent magnet synchronous electric motor producing 201 horsepower and 291 lb-ft of torque, powered by a 64-kWh lithium-ion battery pack. This powertrain provides brisk acceleration and strong overall reliability with minimal moving parts compared to internal combustion engines.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Direct Drive Transmission

Power is sent to the front wheels via a simple single-speed direct drive reduction gear transmission. This gearbox lacks traditional multi-gear shifting components, contributing to a smooth power delivery and straightforward long-term mechanical durability.

## Electrical

### 12V Battery and Charging System

Electrical architecture handles high-voltage propulsion as well as standard 12V accessory functions, with occasional reports centering on auxiliary battery depletion and EV control system warnings.

**12V Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported that vehicle systems fail to shut down completely after the car is turned off, leading to a dead 12V battery and occasionally disabling instrument panel displays like the speedometer.
- **Solution:** Diagnose parasitic electrical draws at a qualified service center, update vehicle software modules, or replace the depleted 12V battery if it has been compromised by deep discharges.

**EV System Startup Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience sudden 'Check Vehicle System' warning messages upon startup, preventing the vehicle from remaining powered on and drivable.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle towed to an authorized dealer to read diagnostic trouble codes and perform necessary software resets or inverter/battery management system repairs.

## Exterior

### Charging Port Door

Cabin and exterior access points are generally ergonomic, though the charging port door latch can occasionally stick.

**Charging Port Latch Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The push-to-open door covering the exterior charging port can lock shut and refuse to open, restricting access to plug in the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Repeatedly press the unlock button to cycle the actuator or have a technician inspect and replace the faulty electronic latch mechanism.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Kona Electric offers an engaging, low-maintenance driving experience backed by a solid electric motor and single-speed transmission design. Prospective buyers should carefully verify the health of the 12V battery system and ensure all electronic door latches and charging components operate smoothly during a test drive.
