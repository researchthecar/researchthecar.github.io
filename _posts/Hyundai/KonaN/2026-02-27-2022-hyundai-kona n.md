### Overview
The 2022 Hyundai Kona N is a performance-oriented compact SUV that blends sporty driving dynamics with the practicality of a crossover. While its enthusiast appeal is high, potential buyers often inquire about its long-term reliability. This analysis focuses on documented technical issues beyond standard maintenance, aiming to provide a clear picture of the vehicle's dependability. ### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Hyundai Kona N demonstrates a generally strong reliability profile, particularly concerning its core powertrain components. The 2.0L turbocharged engine has not shown widespread issues. The primary area of concern is the 8-speed dual-clutch transmission, which may experience overheating under very aggressive driving conditions, potentially leading to performance issues and requiring software updates or fluid services. Electrical systems, specifically the infotainment screen, can occasionally exhibit minor glitches that are typically resolved with software updates. Other categories like suspension, exterior, and interior components are largely free of significant reported problems. Overall, the Kona N offers a reliable performance experience, with the transmission being the most notable component to monitor for potential issues.
