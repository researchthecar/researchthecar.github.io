# 2023 Hyundai Kona N Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Hyundai Kona N is a high-performance subcompact crossover designed for enthusiast drivers, combining sporty handling with everyday utility. While it offers engaging driving dynamics, prospective buyers should review owner-reported experiences closely to understand potential quirks. As with any performance-focused model, checking service history and verifying proper operation of all mechanical and electronic systems is recommended during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 276 horsepower and 289 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration for the performance crossover. It provides strong track-ready capability, though owners should be mindful of reported moisture sensitivity around engine sensors.

**Knock Sensor Moisture Sensitivity**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report a flashing check engine light, sudden power loss, or engine limp mode shortly after washing the vehicle or exposing the engine bay to water.
- **Solution:** Inspection and potential replacement of the knock sensor or affected wiring harness by a qualified technician.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission

The 8-speed wet dual-clutch automatic transmission features steering-wheel paddle shifters and dedicated performance modes for quick, precise shifts. It transfers power smoothly to the front wheels, though proper maintenance is crucial for long-term durability.

## Steering

### Steering and Drivetrain

Steering and handling components designed for sporty cornering response.

**Jerking and Binding During Low-Speed Turns**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may exhibit a jerking or jumping sensation when pulling out from a stop or executing sharp turns at low speeds, sometimes accompanied by suspension noises.
- **Solution:** Have the front differential, steering components, and suspension geometry inspected by a professional to isolate the source of binding.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V528000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Kona+N&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update engine control module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Hyundai Kona N delivers an exciting driving experience with punchy turbocharged power and sharp handling. While a few owners have noted isolated concerns regarding moisture-sensitive engine sensors and low-speed driveline behavior, a thorough inspection can help ensure the vehicle is in solid condition before purchase.
