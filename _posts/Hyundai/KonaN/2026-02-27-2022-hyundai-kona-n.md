# 2022 Hyundai Kona N Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2022 Hyundai Kona N means looking at a high-performance crossover geared toward enthusiast drivers. While owner reports to regulatory bodies highlight a few isolated powertrain and electrical hiccups, prospective buyers should carefully inspect each vehicle's history, condition, and mechanical operation before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 276 horsepower and 289 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration for this performance crossover. It generally offers spirited performance, though individual owner reports note occasional sensor-related driving hesitations.

**Limp Mode and Acceleration Stumble**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report random stumbles during highway acceleration and sudden entry into limp mode, sometimes accompanied by a check engine light or knock sensor codes.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose stored diagnostic trouble codes, inspect the knock sensor, and check the fuel and ignition systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Automatic

The 8-speed wet dual-clutch automatic transmission features steering-wheel paddle shifters and dedicated performance modes tailored for dynamic driving. It delivers quick gear changes, though buyers should verify smooth operation across all driving conditions during a test drive.

## Electrical

### Horn and Battery

Electrical accessories and onboard convenience features generally function as expected, though isolated component wear has been reported.

**Horn Failure and Battery Discharge**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden horn non-functionality alongside premature battery drainage.
- **Solution:** Test the battery state of health, inspect the charging system, and check the horn switch and fuses for continuity.
- **Cost:** Out-of-pocket costs varied depending on battery replacement parts.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V746000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Kona+N&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V528000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump:Control/Drive Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Kona+N&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A loss of drive power can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update engine control module software. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Kona N is an engaging and powerful performance crossover that appeals to enthusiast drivers. Buyers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection focusing on engine performance, electronic sensors, and overall mechanical condition to ensure a satisfying ownership experience.
