# 2017 Hyundai H400

### Overview
The 2017 Hyundai H400, a light commercial vehicle, generally exhibits a solid reliability record, particularly for its intended purpose of cargo transport and commercial use. While no vehicle is entirely immune to issues, the H400 has demonstrated a commendable level of durability. Owners and fleet managers often report satisfaction with its robust build and functional design. Common areas of attention, as with many vehicles in this class, tend to be related to powertrain components under heavy use and certain electrical accessories. However, widespread, critical failures are not a dominant theme in owner feedback, suggesting a well-engineered product for its segment. Regular maintenance is key to maximizing its lifespan and performance.

---


## Engine

### 2.5L CRDi Diesel
**Turbocharger Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, black smoke from exhaust.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the turbocharger unit.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**Glow Plug Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Difficulty starting, especially in cold weather; check engine light illumination.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty glow plugs.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission
**Clutch Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping clutch, difficulty shifting gears, burning smell.
- **Solution:** Replacement of clutch kit (clutch disc, pressure plate, release bearing).
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid, rough shifting, overheating.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals and gaskets, fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Worn Ball Joints/Tie Rod Ends**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn suspension components.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


## Electrical

### Standard Electrical System
**Alternator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Dimming headlights, battery warning light, electrical system malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the alternator.
- **Cost:** $600 - $1100

**Power Window Regulator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Window not moving up or down, grinding noise when operating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power window regulator mechanism.
- **Cost:** $250 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Hyundai H400 is a generally reliable light commercial vehicle. The 2.5L CRDi diesel engine is robust, though turbocharger and glow plug issues can arise, particularly with high mileage or less diligent maintenance. Both manual and automatic transmissions are functional, with clutch wear being the primary concern for manuals and fluid leaks for automatics. Suspension components like ball joints and tie rod ends may require attention over time. Electrical issues, such as alternator failure and power window regulator problems, are not uncommon but are typically repairable. Exterior and interior components have shown good durability. Overall, the H400 offers good value with manageable potential repair costs for its class.
