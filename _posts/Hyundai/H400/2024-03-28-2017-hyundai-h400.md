## 2017 Hyundai H400

The 2017 Hyundai H400 is a versatile cargo van offering ample cargo space, reliable performance, and a comfortable interior. It comes equipped with a 2.5L diesel engine paired with a 6-speed manual transmission. While the H400 generally receives positive feedback for its spaciousness and fuel efficiency, some owners have reported specific issues with the vehicle, as outlined below.

### Engine

- **Fuel injector failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting the engine, rough idling, reduced power, and illuminated check engine light.
  - Solution: Replace faulty fuel injectors.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

- **Shifting problems**
  - Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises, and gear slippage.
  - Solution: Repair or replace transmission components as needed.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

- **Strut failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Noises from the front end, uneven tire wear, and poor handling.
  - Solution: Replace faulty struts.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

- **Battery drain**
  - Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, slow engine crank, and dimmed lights.
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical system issues causing the drain.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

- **Door handle issues**
  - Description/Symptoms: Door handles may stick or break, making it difficult to open doors.
  - Solution: Replace faulty door handles.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Interior

- **Air conditioning malfunction**
  - Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning system may not blow cold air or fail to function at all.
  - Solution: Repair or replace faulty compressor, condenser, or other components.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Other

- **Brake pedal vibration**
  - Description/Symptoms: Vibration felt in the brake pedal during braking.
  - Solution: Diagnose and repair brake system components causing the vibration.
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
  - Recalls: None Found

### Summary

Overall, the 2017 Hyundai H400 offers reliable performance and ample cargo space. However, some owners have encountered specific issues with the fuel injectors, transmission, struts, electrical system, door handles, air conditioning, brake pedal vibration, and other components. The severity of these issues varies, and the estimated cost to repair them ranges from $100 to $2,000+ depending on the specific problem. It's important to note that the issues mentioned here are not exhaustive and may not affect all H400 models.
