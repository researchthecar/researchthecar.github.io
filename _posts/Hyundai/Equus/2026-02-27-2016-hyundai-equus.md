# 2016 Hyundai Equus Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a pre-owned 2016 Hyundai Equus, buyers will find a luxurious flagship sedan that offers a quiet cabin and a smooth ride. While generally comfortable, prospective owners should review owner-reported feedback regarding electrical quirks, suspension components, and engine exhaust soot when evaluating a potential purchase.

---

## Engine

### 5.0L V8 GDI

Powered by a 5.0-liter V8 engine producing around 429 horsepower, this engine delivers robust acceleration and smooth performance appropriate for a luxury flagship sedan.

**Excessive Exhaust Soot**
- **Symptoms:** Heavy black soot builds up quickly on the rear tailpipes, bumper, and trunk lid, causing staining and chrome pitting that requires chemical cleaners to remove.
- **Solution:** Inspect the exhaust system and combustion health; regular detailing may be required to prevent permanent cosmetic damage.

**Unexpected Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** The engine may abruptly stall or shut off while driving at highway speeds, resulting in a loss of power steering and power brakes.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic perform a comprehensive diagnostic scan of the powertrain control module and fuel injection systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels, offering refined gear changes that complement the vehicle's luxury demeanor.

## Suspension

### Air Suspension

The sophisticated air suspension system provides a plush ride quality but can be costly to service if air springs or control blocks degrade.

**Air Block and Airbag Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Air suspension components and air blocks may fail repeatedly, causing the vehicle to sag or ride harshly even after recent part replacements.
- **Solution:** Inspect the air struts, compressor, and valve blocks for leaks, and consider aftermarket or updated OEM revisions if failures persist.

## Brakes

### Wheels and Brakes

Braking hardware is generally robust, though wheel finish degradation can occasionally impact brake pad and rotor operation.

**Powder Coating Delamination**
- **Symptoms:** Flaking powder coating or finish from the wheel rims can detach and lodge between the brake pads and rotors, extending stopping distances and compromising braking performance.
- **Solution:** Inspect wheel finish integrity during routine maintenance and clean or replace affected wheels and brake hardware.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Owner complaints highlight intermittent exterior lighting anomalies that can affect night visibility.

**Headlight and Turn Signal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights set to automatic may intermittently flicker on and off at night, or both headlights and turn signals can become entirely inoperable.
- **Solution:** Inspect the lighting control modules, fuses, and headlight assemblies for electrical faults or loose connections.

The 2016 Hyundai Equus provides high-end luxury and V8 performance at a fraction of its original price, but prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the air suspension, electrical lighting functions, and engine exhaust areas before finalizing a purchase.
