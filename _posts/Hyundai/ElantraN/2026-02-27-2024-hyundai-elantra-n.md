# 2024 Hyundai Elantra N Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Hyundai Elantra N is a high-performance sport compact sedan known for its track-ready capability and aggressive styling. When shopping for a used model, buyers will find a heavily enthusiast-focused vehicle backed by modern features. While early owner reports are sparse, reviewing standard powertrain options and minor accessory functions can help ensure a smooth purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

This 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 276 horsepower and 289 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and dynamic performance. It is generally praised for its responsiveness, though buyers should verify proper maintenance history on sporty models.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers a direct, engaging connection for driving enthusiasts. It features rev-matching capabilities and standard front-wheel-drive dynamics.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed wet dual-clutch automatic transmission provides lightning-fast gear changes and includes steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters. It suits both spirited driving and daily commuting.

## Electrical

### Horn

Electrical accessories generally function well, though isolated component failures can occur.

**Horn Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The horn fails to produce any sound when pressed, preventing the driver from warning nearby motorists.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the horn assembly, fuse, and wiring, and replace the faulty components as necessary.
