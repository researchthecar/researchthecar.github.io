**Introduction:**

The 2023 Hyundai Elantra N is a sport-tuned sedan that offers thrilling performance with its 2.0-liter turbocharged engine producing 276 horsepower and 289 lb-ft of torque. It is available with either a 6-speed manual transmission or an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. The Elantra N boasts impressive handling and sharp steering, making it a popular choice for enthusiasts. However, like any vehicle, it has experienced some issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Engine**

* **Knocking Noise During Acceleration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A knocking or rattling noise when accelerating
    * Solution: Check spark plugs and/or replace engine mount
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual Transmission**

* **Difficulty Shifting Gears:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard to shift into certain gears or gears popping out
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly or adjust linkage
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Shuddering:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering or vibration when shifting gears or accelerating
    * Solution: Reprogram transmission software or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $5000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Excessive Body Roll:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Car leans excessively in corners or on uneven surfaces
    * Solution: Install stiffer suspension components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely after sitting
    * Solution: Replace faulty battery or diagnose parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Dented Rear Bumper:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rear bumper dents easily from minor impacts
    * Solution: Replace bumper
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Vibration:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Steering wheel vibrates at certain speeds or during acceleration
    * Solution: Road force balance tires or replace steering components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment**

* **Connectivity Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Apple CarPlay or Android Auto not connecting or disconnecting randomly
    * Solution: Update software or replace head unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2023 Hyundai Elantra N is a popular choice for driving enthusiasts, it has experienced some issues that potential buyers should be aware of. Most of the issues are minor and can be resolved with relatively low costs, such as battery drain, connectivity issues, and steering wheel vibration. However, more serious problems like transmission shuddering or difficulty shifting gears could be more costly to repair. Overall, it's important to research all vehicles thoroughly and consider the potential issues before making a purchasing decision.
