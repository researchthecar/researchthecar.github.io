**Introduction:**

The 2012 Hyundai Genesis Coupe is a rear-wheel-drive sports coupe powered by a 3.8L V6 engine with 348 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque. It comes standard with a six-speed manual transmission or an optional eight-speed automatic transmission. The Genesis Coupe offers a compelling combination of performance and style, but some owners have reported various issues.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**

**Knocking Noise:**
* Description/Symptoms: A persistent knocking noise from the engine, especially under load.
* Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* Recalls: #13V319000

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

**Gear Grinding:**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears or grinding noises when shifting.
* Solution: Replace the clutch or transmission fluid.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $1,200+

**Eight-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Delay:**
* Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delay when shifting gears.
* Solution: Update the transmission control module software.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: Free under warranty
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Rear Suspension Squeak:**
* Description/Symptoms: A squeaking or creaking noise from the rear suspension, especially over bumps.
* Solution: Lubricate suspension components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $150

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: The battery discharges excessively when the vehicle is parked.
* Solution: Replace the defective battery or alternator.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $600+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Dashboard Rattle:**
* Description/Symptoms: Rattles or vibrations coming from the dashboard.
* Solution: Tighten or replace dashboard components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Center Console Creaking:**
* Description/Symptoms: Creaking or popping noises from the center console area.
* Solution: Tighten or reinforce the console mounting points.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation:**
* Description/Symptoms: Moisture buildup inside the headlights.
* Solution: Replace or seal the headlights.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2012 Hyundai Genesis Coupe has received generally positive reviews for its performance, handling, and styling. However, some owners have experienced issues with engine knocking, transmission gear grinding and shift delays, suspension squeaks, electrical battery drain, and various interior rattles and creaks. While the severity of the issues can vary, some of them can be costly to repair.
