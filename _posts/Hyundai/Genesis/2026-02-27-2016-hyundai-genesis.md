# 2016 Hyundai Genesis Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Hyundai Genesis offers a luxurious cabin, refined ride quality, and strong value as a pre-owned executive sedan. However, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate potential mechanical and electrical hiccups documented by owners, particularly concerning steering mechanics, exterior lighting, and powertrain sensors.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 Engine

The standard 3.8-liter V6 engine produces around 311 horsepower and 293 lb-ft of torque, delivering smooth power and generally dependable daily performance, though buyers should watch for minor sensor leaks.

**Oil Pressure Switch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The oil pressure warning light illuminates intermittently while idling or stopped, often caused by a faulty switch that fails to read accurate pressure.
- **Solution:** Replace the defective oil pressure switch.

### 5.0L V8 Engine

The optional 5.0-liter V8 engine pushes output to 420 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque, providing effortless acceleration and robust highway performance with standard rear-wheel drive.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission routes power reliably to either the rear wheels or the H-Trac all-wheel-drive system, experiencing very few mechanical complaints from owners.

## Steering

### Steering Rack Assembly

Steering complaints center around electronic power steering rack binding, which can create a stiff or unresponsive feel returning to center.

**Steering Wheel Sticking and Failure to Return**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel sticks after completing a turn and fails to self-center, requiring the driver to manually force the wheel back.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the electric power steering rack assembly.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting and Switchgear

Electrical concerns are mostly localized to multifunction switch shorts affecting exterior lighting and accessory power modules.

**Headlight Flickering or Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights flicker or temporarily shut off when the turn signal stalk is engaged while the headlights are set to auto mode.
- **Solution:** Replace or repair the multifunction steering column switch assembly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V359000: Parking Brake:Indicator Light](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Genesis&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the parking brake indicator is not illuminated when the parking brake is applied, the driver may operate the vehicle with the parking brake partially engaged, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the parking brake switch, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V160000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Antilock/Traction Control/Electronic Limited Slip:Control Unit/Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Genesis&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The Anti-Lock Brake System (ABS) module could malfunction and short circuit. An electrical short circuit inside the ABS module could cause an engine compartment fire while parked or driving.
- **What to do:** Hyundai will notify owners, and dealers will replace the ABS module fuse, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V107000: Electrical System:Starter Assembly:Solenoid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Genesis&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Water may enter the starter solenoid and cause an electrical short, which can result in an engine compartment fire while parked or driving. An engine compartment fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a remedy relay kit in the engine junction box, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Hyundai Genesis delivers high-end luxury features and competitive performance at a fraction of its original price, making it an attractive used purchase. Buyers should thoroughly inspect the steering feel, test all exterior lighting functions, and verify proper engine sensor operation prior to purchase.
