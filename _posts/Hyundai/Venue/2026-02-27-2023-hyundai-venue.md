# 2023 Hyundai Venue Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Hyundai Venue is a subcompact crossover aimed at budget-conscious commuters looking for high fuel economy and maneuverability. While generally modern and well-equipped for its class, early-mileage owner reports highlight a few recurring electronic, lighting, and tire concerns that potential buyers should keep in mind.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Smartstream 4-Cylinder Engine

The Venue is powered by a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine generating approximately 121 horsepower and 113 lb-ft of torque. It prioritizes fuel efficiency over spirited acceleration and serves as a dependable standard commuter powertrain.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Power is sent to the front wheels via an Intelligent Variable Transmission designed to optimize fuel economy in city driving. This continuously variable design operates smoothly under normal driving conditions.

## Suspension

### Factory Tires and Wheels

Suspension hardware remains sturdy, but early-model factory tire wear and isolated sidewall issues warrant a close physical inspection during a used vehicle pre-purchase check.

**Premature Tire Wear and Sidewall Separation**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have experienced premature tread wear or outer sidewall separation on factory-equipped tires at relatively low mileages.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire health regularly and replace worn or damaged tires with quality aftermarket alternatives if sidewall defects occur.

## Electrical

### Infotainment and Horn System

Electrical reports highlight occasional infotainment display glitches and recurring horn failures, which require prompt inspection for safety reasons.

**Infotainment Screen Blanking and Horn Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported the touchscreen infotainment display going completely black or becoming too dim to read, as well as instances of the horn failing unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Visit a dealership to check for applicable software updates for the infotainment system or replace the faulty horn assembly.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

The suite of active safety features generally aids everyday driving, though occasional sensor warnings and alert system complaints have been logged.

**Warning Light Illumination and Forward Collision Alerts**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted unexpected illumination of multiple safety system warning lights, including forward collision avoidance, lane keep assist, and blind spot monitoring.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's diagnostic trouble codes scanned by a qualified technician to reset or service the affected sensors.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Hyundai Venue is an affordable and practical choice for city drivers, backed by a simple powertrain. Prospective buyers should thoroughly check the electronics, test the horn and infotainment system, and inspect the condition of the tires during a test drive to ensure everything is in proper working order.
