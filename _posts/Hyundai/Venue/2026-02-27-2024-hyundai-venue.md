# 2024 Hyundai Venue Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Hyundai Venue is a compact front-wheel-drive crossover powered by a modest 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine matched with an Intelligent Variable Transmission. As a relatively new model on the used market, it offers nimble city driving and contemporary styling, but prospective buyers should review initial owner feedback regarding minor electrical quirks and accessory failures.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L 4-Cylinder Engine (Smartstream G1.6)

The 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 121 horsepower and 113 lb-ft of torque. It provides efficient daily commuting performance, though early owner reports note occasional startup sputtering and minor electrical interaction issues.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

The continuously variable transmission routes power exclusively to the front wheels. It is designed for optimal fuel economy in urban settings and generally operates smoothly without widespread mechanical failure reports.

## Electrical

### Horn Assembly

Owner feedback highlights recurring failures with the vehicle's horn assembly, often requiring component replacement.

**Inoperative Horn**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the horn intermittently or completely stops working due to potential moisture corrosion at the interface.
- **Solution:** Have a dealership or technician inspect the horn assembly and replace faulty low-pitch horn components under warranty if applicable.

### Instrument Cluster and Dashboard

Isolated reports indicate intermittent dashboard blanking and electrical system warnings during operation.

**Dashboard Display and Electronic Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The dashboard screen or instrument cluster may go blank, display erratic warning lights, or cause the speedometer to drop to zero while driving.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electrical system and software modules diagnosed by a qualified technician to identify potential wiring or module glitches.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V308000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Exhaust System:Emission Control:Gas Recirculation Valve (Egr Valve)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Venue&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An electrical short circuit may occur in the exhaust gas recirculation (EGR) valve assembly, which can result in a loss of drive power. A loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the exhaust gas recirculation valve assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Hyundai Venue is an economical and compact choice for city drivers, backed by standard powertrain configurations that generally perform reliably. Used car shoppers should thoroughly test all electrical accessories—especially the horn and instrument cluster—to ensure no underlying glitches exist from previous owner reports.
