This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability data, excluding routine maintenance and normal wear and tear, to provide a clear picture of potential mechanical and electrical concerns.

### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Hyundai Venue demonstrates a generally strong reliability record, particularly concerning its powertrain. The 1.6L engine and CVT transmission have not shown significant widespread issues. Suspension components are also holding up well. The most commonly reported, though not necessarily widespread, concerns tend to be with the infotainment system, which can experience occasional glitches, and in rare cases, premature battery failure. These electrical issues are typically resolvable with software updates or component replacement. Overall, the Venue is considered a reliable option in its segment, with most potential problems being minor and manageable.
