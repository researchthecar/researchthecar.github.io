# 2021 Hyundai Venue Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used subcompact crossover like this model, buyers generally find a practical and efficient daily driver suited for city commuting. While most systems perform reliably, reviewing owner-reported pain points in areas like electronics and safety hardware can help you spot potential trouble spots before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Smartstream 4-Cylinder Engine

Equipped with a 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine producing around 121 horsepower, this powerplant prioritizes fuel economy over swift acceleration. Overall mechanical durability is generally sound, though minor electronic sensor glitches occasionally surface.

**Check Engine Light and Intermittent Rough Idle**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted persistent check engine lights accompanied by a rough idle and occasional loss of driving power.
- **Solution:** Have a professional run a thorough diagnostic scan on engine sensors, ignition components, and the fuel system to isolate the underlying fault.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Paired exclusively with front-wheel drive, this continuously variable transmission design is tuned for smooth highway cruising and optimal fuel efficiency.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Braking performance is adequate for normal daily driving when well-maintained, though mechanical wear and pedal feel issues should be checked during a test drive.

**Soft Pedal and Crunching Noises**
- **Symptoms:** The brake pedal may feel unusually hard or travel excessively toward the floorboard, accompanied by crunching sounds.
- **Solution:** Inspect the brake pads, rotors, master cylinder, and ensure no obstructions are interfering with pedal travel.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Steering response is typically light and nimble for city driving, though intermittent electronic power assist glitches have been documented.

**Stiff Steering Wheel**
- **Symptoms:** The steering wheel can suddenly become firm and difficult to maneuver during turns, often accompanied by a warning light.
- **Solution:** Scan the power steering control module for fault codes and inspect the electronic steering motor connections.

## Electrical

### Horn Assembly

Electrical system concerns typically center around accessory glitches and minor component failures rather than major wiring harnesses.

**Intermittent or Inoperable Horn**
- **Symptoms:** Owners report that the horn frequently stops working for days at a time or fails completely.
- **Solution:** Inspect the horn switch, relay, and wiring harness, and replace faulty components as necessary.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally functions as intended, though active recalls regarding seat belt latch mechanisms require careful verification.

**Seat Belt Disconnection and Warning Light**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers note that front seat belts can inadvertently unlatch while driving, and warning lights may stay illuminated.
- **Solution:** Check with a certified dealership to ensure all relevant safety recall inspections and latch replacements have been performed.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V796000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Venue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioner(s) may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner could project metal fragments into the vehicle, striking vehicle occupants and resulting in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat belt pretensioners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V458000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Venue&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioners with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

This vehicle serves as an economical and easy-to-park option for daily commuting, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to functional checks on the horn, infotainment stability, brake pedal feel, and verify that any open seat belt safety recalls have been fully addressed by a dealer.
