## 2023 Hyundai Venue

The 2023 Hyundai Venue is a subcompact SUV that offers a combination of affordability, versatility, and technology. It's available with either a 1.6-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a six-speed manual or an Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT), or a 1.5-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission. The 1.6-liter engine produces 121 horsepower and 113 lb-ft of torque, while the 1.5-liter turbocharged engine produces 159 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque.

Owners generally praise the Venue for its spacious interior, user-friendly infotainment system, and impressive fuel economy. However, some have reported issues with the vehicle's reliability and build quality.

### Engine
**1.6-Liter Four-Cylinder**

**Valve Cover Oil Leak**
Description/Symptoms: Oil may leak from the valve cover gasket, causing an oil burning smell and low oil levels.
Solution: Replace the valve cover gasket.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**1.5-Liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

**No Issues Found**

### Transmission
**Six-Speed Manual**

**Grinding Noises**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may make grinding noises when shifting gears, especially when cold.
Solution: Replace the shift linkage or transmission synchronizers.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**IVT**

**Jerking or Shuddering**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may jerk or shudder during acceleration or deceleration.
Solution: Update the transmission software or replace the transmission valve body.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

**Seven-Speed Dual-Clutch**

**Clutch Slipping**
Description/Symptoms: The clutch may slip, causing a loss of power and difficulty shifting gears.
Solution: Replace the clutch.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

### Suspension
**No Issues Found**

### Electrical
**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge quickly when the vehicle is parked for extended periods.
Solution: Identify and fix the source of the battery drain, such as a faulty alternator or parasitic drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

### Exterior
**No Issues Found**

### Interior
**Sunroof Creaking**
Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may creak or rattle when opened or closed.
Solution: Lubricate the sunroof tracks or replace the sunroof assembly.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2023 Hyundai Venue is a generally reliable and affordable subcompact SUV, but it has some reported issues that can affect its drivability and overall ownership experience. The most significant issues include oil leaks, transmission problems, and electrical gremlins. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem and the age and mileage of the vehicle.
