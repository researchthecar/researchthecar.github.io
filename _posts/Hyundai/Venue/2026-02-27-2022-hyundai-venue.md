# 2022 Hyundai Venue Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Hyundai Venue, prospective buyers will find a compact crossover powered by an efficient engine paired with a modern automatic transmission. While generally economical and easy to maneuver, owner complaints submitted to safety agencies highlight a few recurring mechanical and electrical issues worth noting before purchasing. Reviewing service records and conducting a thorough test drive can help ensure any potential steering, electrical, or safety concerns have been properly addressed.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Smartstream 4-Cylinder Engine

The 1.6-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces approximately 121 horsepower and 113 lb-ft of torque, offering adequate efficiency for daily commuting. Overall, this powerplant has proven generally dependable for routine driving duties.

## Transmission

### Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT)

Equipped with an Intelligent Variable Transmission (IVT) routing power to the front wheels, this setup delivers smooth acceleration and respectable fuel economy. The transmission option is generally well-regarded for daily driving comfort.

## Steering

### Steering Wheel Assembly

Owner reports indicate occasional concerns with steering alignment and vibration that require professional inspection.

**Steering Pull and Vibration**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported the vehicle unexpectedly pulling to one side or experiencing steering wheel vibrations at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a certified technician inspect the wheel alignment, steering components, and suspension geometry.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment System

Minor electrical quirks involving the infotainment display and horn can occasionally occur.

**Infotainment Screen and Horn Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The radio display or rear-view camera screen may occasionally fail to turn on at startup, and intermittent horn non-functionality has been noted.
- **Solution:** Restarting the vehicle can temporarily reset display glitches, while faulty horn components require dealer diagnosis and part replacement.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety component concerns primarily center around parts availability for active recall repairs.

**Seat Belt Pretensioner Part Availability**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced delays in getting safety recall repairs completed due to temporary shortages of replacement seat belt pretensioner parts.
- **Solution:** Contact a local dealership to check for open safety recalls and verify replacement part availability.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V458000: Seat Belts:Front](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Venue&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** In the event of a crash, the front driver-side and/or passenger-side seat belt pretensioners may explode upon deployment. An exploding seat belt pretensioner can project metal fragments into the vehicle, strike vehicle occupants, and result in injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will secure the seat belt pretensioners with a cap, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Venue is an affordable, fuel-efficient subcompact crossover that suits city driving well. While owner feedback highlights isolated complaints regarding steering alignment, minor electrical glitches, and safety recall part delays, these issues do not overshadow the vehicle's overall daily utility. Used car buyers should verify that all open safety recalls have been completed and ensure a thorough test drive before finalizing their purchase.
