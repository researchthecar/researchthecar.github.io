# 2022 Hyundai Santa Cruz Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Hyundai Santa Cruz bridges the gap between a compact crossover and a traditional pickup truck, offering unique versatility and modern styling. However, used-car buyers should review owner-reported reliability concerns closely, particularly regarding powertrain behavior, electrical issues, and certain safety recalls. While many owners enjoy its car-like handling and innovative bed design, keeping an eye on transmission performance and potential engine-related issues is recommended during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

This base 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine produces 191 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed traditional automatic transmission. It offers adequate power for daily commuting and routine errands with straightforward mechanical reliability.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The upgraded 2.5-liter turbocharged engine pumps out 281 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, mated to an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission. It provides brisk acceleration and strong towing capability, though performance-focused buyers should remain mindful of reports tied to the dual-clutch setup.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed dual-clutch transmission delivers quick, sporty gear changes on turbocharged trims, but a subset of owners have experienced unexpected shifting anomalies and power loss.

**Loss of Motive Power and Shifting Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report sudden loss of motive power at highway speeds, hesitation upon acceleration, or getting stuck in first gear during cold starts.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning by a dealership is required; software updates, clutch adjustments, or complete transmission replacement may be necessary.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Infotainment Systems

Cabin electronics and driver assistance modules generally function well, though intermittent screen and sensor glitches have been noted by some drivers.

**Backup Camera and Electronic Display Failures**
- **Symptoms:** The backup camera display intermittently goes black or remains stuck on the screen after shifting into drive.
- **Solution:** Inspect camera wiring harnesses and software modules; dealerships may update firmware or replace faulty camera units.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior panels and lighting are modern and functional, though rare reports of spontaneous sunroof glass breakage warrant careful inspection.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The glass sunroof panel may unexpectedly shatter or explode while driving without any impact from external debris.
- **Solution:** Replace the shattered glass panel and inspect the surrounding frame for stress or defects.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V197000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Gasoline:Turbo/Supercharger](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Cruz&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The oil supply pipe to the turbocharger may crack, which could result in an oil leak in the engine compartment. An oil leak in the presence of an ignition source such as hot engine or exhaust components, can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the turbocharger oil supply pipe, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V746000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Cruz&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Loss of drive power increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the transmission, as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V038000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Cruz&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A detached roof molding could become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace, or secure the roof moldings, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V181000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Cruz&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Water accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse and wire extension kit as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Hyundai Santa Cruz presents an appealing option for buyers seeking crossover comfort with open-bed utility. Prospective owners should verify that all open recalls—particularly those involving trailer hitches or electrical harnesses—have been addressed. Conducting a thorough test drive focusing on smooth transmission shifts and checking electronic accessories will help ensure a reliable ownership experience.
