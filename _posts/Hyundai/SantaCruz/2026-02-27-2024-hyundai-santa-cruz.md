# 2024 Hyundai Santa Cruz Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Hyundai Santa Cruz blends the utility of an open bed with the comfort and driving dynamics of a crossover SUV. While many owners praise its versatility and modern interior technology, early adopter reports point to a few recurring areas of concern. Prospective buyers should carefully review powertrain responsiveness and electronic safety systems during a test drive to ensure everything operates smoothly.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

A 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine producing 191 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. It serves as the standard powerplant for base trims, prioritizing everyday efficiency.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

A turbocharged 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine delivering 281 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, matched with an 8-speed wet dual-clutch transmission. It provides brisk acceleration and stronger towing capability for higher trims.

**Sudden Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected drops in vehicle speed and acceleration while navigating highways or steep inclines, occasionally accompanied by delayed downshifts.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic evaluation by a certified technician is required to check throttle body function, turbocharger operation, and powertrain control module software.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed conventional automatic transmission paired with the naturally aspirated engine. It offers smooth, predictable gear changes suited for daily commuting.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission

An 8-speed wet dual-clutch automatic transmission mated to the turbocharged engine. It shifts quickly under power but can occasionally exhibit low-speed hesitation typical of DCT designs.

**Intermittent Engagement Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners have experienced moments where the transmission behaves as if it shifted into neutral, resulting in a temporary loss of drive before abruptly engaging.
- **Solution:** Software updates or clutch recalibration performed by a dealership can sometimes resolve shifting anomalies.

## Steering

### Suspension and Steering Components

Independent front and rear suspension tuned for a comfortable, car-like ride quality.

**Creaking Suspension Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may hear abnormal creaking or groaning sounds originating from the suspension components when accelerating from a complete stop.
- **Solution:** Inspect bushings, joints, and mounting hardware for premature wear or lack of lubrication.

## Exterior

### Tailgate and Glass

Exterior body panels, glass elements, and cargo bed features designed for utility and styling.

**Unintended Tailgate Release**
- **Symptoms:** The tailgate may occasionally unlatch or lower while driving or operating the vehicle, or dislodge from its hinges during loading.
- **Solution:** Inspect tailgate latch mechanisms, alignment, and key fob proximity settings.

## Safety

### Advanced Driver Assistance Systems

Comprehensive suite of active safety features designed to assist with collision mitigation and lane centering.

**Over-Sensitive Lane Keeping and Collision Avoidance**
- **Symptoms:** The lane keeping assist or forward collision avoidance systems may occasionally trigger unprompted steering corrections or brief emergency braking maneuvers during normal traffic flow.
- **Solution:** Adjust driver assistance sensitivity settings through the infotainment menu or consult a dealership to calibrate sensor modules.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V412000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Cruz&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The circuit board in the motor-driven power steering assembly may short circuit, resulting in a loss of power steering assist. A loss of power steering assist can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the motor-driven power steering electric power pack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Hyundai Santa Cruz is an appealing alternative to traditional midsize trucks, offering everyday comfort and clever utility. While reported issues largely center around intermittent electronic driver-assistance quirks and rare powertrain hesitation, most vehicles operate dependably. Used car buyers should verify that all software updates have been applied and thoroughly test drive the vehicle to evaluate transmission behavior and electronic systems.
