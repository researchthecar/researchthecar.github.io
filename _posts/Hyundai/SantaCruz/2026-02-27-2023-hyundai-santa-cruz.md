# 2023 Hyundai Santa Cruz Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Hyundai Santa Cruz brings a unique blend of compact SUV comfort and open-bed utility to the market. While its distinctive styling and versatile layout attract many buyers, used-car shoppers should review owner reports concerning powertrain hiccups, electrical glitches, and unexpected driver-assistance activations.

---

## Engine

### 2.5L Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder

This base 2.5-liter engine produces 191 horsepower and 181 lb-ft of torque, offering adequate power for daily commuting and basic utility tasks while prioritizing fuel efficiency.

### 2.5L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder

The turbocharged 2.5-liter engine pumps out a robust 281 horsepower and 311 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration and strong towing capability, though it is tied to more complex mechanical components.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly drops in speed, limiting acceleration to low speeds (such as 20 mph) without immediate warning lights.
- **Solution:** Pull over, shut off the engine, and restart the vehicle to reset the system, then have a dealer inspect for underlying engine management faults.

**Fuel Injector Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine lights, rough running, and stored error codes indicating fuel injector malfunctions.
- **Solution:** Have the faulty fuel injectors diagnosed and replaced by a qualified service center.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the base naturally aspirated engine, this traditional 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and reliable gear changes for everyday driving.

### 8-Speed Wet Dual-Clutch Transmission

Working alongside the turbocharged engine, this 8-speed dual-clutch transmission offers quick shifts and sporty performance, but requires careful attention to reported shifting anomalies.

**Shifting Failure and Sluggishness**
- **Symptoms:** Transmission fails to advance through gear ranges, displays low fluid warnings with code P193B-79, or causes sluggish acceleration.
- **Solution:** Cycling the ignition off and back on may temporarily restore operation, but persistent issues require professional inspection and potential component replacement.

## Electrical

### Electrical System

The electrical architecture supports numerous advanced displays and comfort features, though intermittent harness and display glitches have been reported by some owners.

**Dashboard Display Fading**
- **Symptoms:** Instrument cluster lights or dashboard screens randomly fade or go black, temporarily disabling associated safety features.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealership to check for software updates or wiring harness inspections, as this is often an intermittent concern.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety suites provide helpful driver-assist capabilities, though occasional false-positive collision warnings and sudden braking events require attentive monitoring.

**Unintended Emergency Braking**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle suddenly issues audible warnings and applies the emergency brakes while driving at highway speeds without obstacles present.
- **Solution:** Have dealer technicians inspect and recalibrate the radar and camera sensors responsible for collision avoidance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V038000: Structure:Body:Roof And Pillars](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Cruz&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A detached roof molding could become a road hazard, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace, or secure the roof moldings, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V181000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Santa+Cruz&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Water accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse and wire extension kit as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Hyundai Santa Cruz presents an appealing lifestyle vehicle choice for buyers wanting car-like driving dynamics with open-bed practicality. While major mechanical failures are not universal, prospective buyers should thoroughly test drive models to check transmission smoothness, verify that all electrical features and driver aids operate properly, and ensure any open recall items have been addressed by a dealer.
