# 2022 Hyundai Palisade

### Overview
The 2022 Hyundai Palisade is a popular three-row SUV known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and value. From a technical reliability standpoint, the Palisade generally performs well, with most owners reporting a positive experience. The powertrain, consisting of a robust V6 engine and an 8-speed automatic transmission, is a strong point, offering smooth power delivery and decent fuel economy for its class. While no vehicle is entirely without potential issues, the Palisade's reported problems are typically minor and not widespread across the model year. Common areas of concern, though infrequent, tend to be related to infotainment glitches and occasional minor interior trim fitment issues. Overall, the 2022 Palisade is considered a reliable choice for families seeking a well-equipped and practical SUV.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Hyundai Palisade demonstrates strong reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The 3.8L V6 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission are robust and have not shown significant widespread issues. While minor electrical glitches, primarily with the infotainment system, and occasional interior trim fitment concerns have been noted by a small percentage of owners, these are generally not critical failures and are often resolvable with software updates or minor adjustments. The suspension, exterior, and other interior systems are performing as expected for a vehicle in this class. Overall, the 2022 Palisade is a reliable choice with a low probability of major mechanical problems.
