# 2022 Hyundai Palisade Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Hyundai Palisade is a well-regarded midsize three-row SUV offering comfortable seating, upscale features, and strong overall practicality. However, used car buyers should carefully review potential owner complaints regarding engine performance, electrical glitches, and safety equipment. While many owners enjoy trouble-free driving, keeping an eye out for known mechanical and structural issues can help prevent unexpected repair bills down the road.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 GDI

Equipped with a 3.8-liter direct-injected V6 producing roughly 291 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, this powertrain provides robust acceleration and towing capacity for a three-row SUV. While generally durable, buyers should monitor for occasional reports of high oil consumption and unexpected power loss.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** The engine consumes oil at an abnormally high rate, requiring frequent top-offs between scheduled oil changes.
- **Solution:** Perform diagnostic oil consumption tests; consult dealership for potential internal engine service or warranty coverage.

**Loss of Motive Power**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle suddenly loses engine power while driving, making it difficult to maintain normal speeds and creating a hazardous traffic situation.
- **Solution:** Inspect powertrain control modules, fuel delivery systems, and engine sensors for faults.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An 8-speed traditional automatic transmission pairs with the V6 engine, offering smooth shifts across front-wheel-drive and all-wheel-drive configurations. No major recurring transmission mechanical failures dominate owner complaints for this model year.

## Suspension

### Rear Suspension Shocks

The suspension provides a compliant, family-friendly ride quality, though some owners experience premature wear or recurring issues with rear self-leveling shocks.

**Premature Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Unusual clunking or gurgling noises coming from the rear suspension, accompanied by a bumpy ride or self-leveling shock failure.
- **Solution:** Replace worn or leaking rear shock absorbers and level sensors.

## Electrical

### Third-Row Power Seats

Interior appointments are generally comfortable and premium, but power-folding third-row seat mechanisms have drawn scrutiny regarding obstruction sensing.

**Obstruction Detection Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The third-row power-folding seats fail to stop when encountering resistance from objects or passengers in the seat.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat motor limit switches and consult dealership for any available software updates or mechanism adjustments.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuators

Electrical complaints for this model year largely center on convenience accessories, wiring harnesses, and locking mechanisms that may require intermittent troubleshooting.

**Child Safety Lock Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rear door child safety locks make a clicking noise and frequently fail to lock properly, triggering a warning message on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replace faulty door lock actuators and inspect wiring harnesses.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs well in crash tests, though specific component recalls and owner complaints regarding seat belt mechanisms require attention before purchasing.

**Seat Belt Sticking and Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Seat belts become stuck, tangled, or bound up inside their assembly, making them difficult or impossible to fasten properly.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle inspected under applicable manufacturer recalls to repair or replace affected seat belt assemblies.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V353000: Electrical System: Instrument Cluster/Panel](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A disabled display screen will not show critical information, such as the meters, gauges, and warning lights, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the instrument cluster assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V633000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse and wire extension kit, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V180000: Visibility/Wiper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Windshield wiper failure can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver-side windshield wiper arm, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V607000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Seat belt buckles that fail to latch may not properly restrain occupants in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat belt buckle assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V034000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The side curtain air bags for third-row occupants may deploy improperly in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." Improper side curtain air bag deployment increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective film over the rear window and remove padding, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Palisade remains a compelling used SUV choice thanks to its spacious cabin, upscale features, and strong V6 performance. Prospective buyers should verify that all open safety recalls—particularly concerning seat belts—have been completed, and thoroughly inspect the suspension, engine oil consumption history, and interior electronics during a pre-purchase test drive.
