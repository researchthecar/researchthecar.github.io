# 2021 Hyundai Palisade Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Hyundai Palisade is a well-regarded midsize three-row SUV offering comfortable passenger accommodation, strong standard features, and a smooth driving demeanor. Powered by a dependable V6 engine mated to an eight-speed automatic transmission, it provides a family-friendly package that appeals to many used car shoppers. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding certain electrical accessories, safety equipment, and structural components to ensure a fully sorted vehicle before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.8L V6 Engine

The Palisade is equipped with a 3.8-liter V6 engine producing approximately 291 horsepower and 262 lb-ft of torque, delivering robust acceleration and confident towing capability. Overall, the powertrain is mechanically stout, though isolated owner reports note unexpected electrical or drivability glitches.

**Loss of Motive Power and Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle may unexpectedly lose motive power, experience engine stalling at low speeds or idle, and illuminate dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Requires diagnostic testing by a qualified technician, which may involve inspecting electrical connections, replacing faulty sensors, or addressing fuel delivery components.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

An eight-speed automatic transmission routes power smoothly to either the front wheels or the available all-wheel-drive system. It offers refined gear shifts and generally maintains a strong track record for long-term mechanical reliability.

## Exterior

### Door Lock Actuator

Electrical accessories generally function well, but comfort and convenience systems can occasionally develop component-level glitches such as door lock actuator failures.

**Door Lock Actuator Failure and Child Safety Warnings**
- **Symptoms:** Repeated clicking sounds from door locks, continuous locking and unlocking cycles, and a 'Child Safety Lock Failure' message appearing on the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replace the malfunctioning door lock actuator assembly.

### Sunroof and Glass

Exterior body panels and lighting are generally durable, though vehicles equipped with the panoramic sunroof require careful inspection of the tracks and glass integrity.

**Panoramic Sunroof Jamming or Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** Sunroof glass may begin opening unevenly with grinding noises, fail to open or close completely due to broken tracks or guides, or shatter spontaneously.
- **Solution:** Inspect sunroof tracks, cables, and wind deflector assemblies; replace damaged components or complete glass panels as necessary.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

The Palisade provides comprehensive safety equipment, though owners have reported specific concerns regarding seat belt latch reliability and remedy availability for ongoing recall campaigns.

**Seat Belt Buckle Unlatching**
- **Symptoms:** Front and second-row seat belt buckles may independently unlatch while driving, or replacement parts for open safety recalls may face extended backorders.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat belt assemblies and replace faulty buckle mechanisms as required by manufacturer recalls or service advisories.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V840000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Fluid](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Reduced braking performance can extend the distance required to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the brake master cylinder, and flush the brake fluid, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V627000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The windshield wiper motor may become inoperative, causing the wipers to fail. Windshield wiper failure can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver-side windshield wiper arm, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V633000: Trailer Hitches](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Debris and moisture accumulation on the tow hitch harness module printed circuit board (PCB) may cause an electrical short, which can result in a fire. A fire while parked or driving can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a new fuse and wire extension kit, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V180000: Visibility/Wiper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Windshield wiper failure can reduce visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver-side windshield wiper arm, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V607000: Seat Belts: Rear/Other:Buckle Assembly](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** Seat belt buckles that fail to latch may not properly restrain occupants in a crash, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the seat belt buckle assemblies, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V034000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Palisade&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The side curtain air bags for third-row occupants may deploy improperly in a crash. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 226, "Ejection Mitigation." Improper side curtain air bag deployment increases the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install a protective film over the rear window and remove padding, as necessary free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Hyundai Palisade stands out as a spacious and capable used family SUV with strong overall performance and a comfortable cabin. While the core engine and transmission options are dependable, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect the vehicle for functioning door locks, proper sunroof operation, and completed safety recall work regarding seat belts.
