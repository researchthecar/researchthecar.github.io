# 2024 Hyundai Nexo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Hyundai Nexo is an advanced hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicle that offers zero-emission driving paired with the practicality of a crossover. As a specialized alternative-fuel vehicle available in limited markets, buyers must weigh its innovative technology against a sparse complaint record and the unique infrastructure requirements of hydrogen fueling. Because owner data is limited, prospective shoppers should thoroughly inspect the fuel cell system and verify local hydrogen refueling availability before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 95 kW Fuel Cell Stack / 120 kW Electric Motor

The hydrogen fuel cell system pairs a 95 kW fuel cell stack with a 135-horsepower (101 kW) electric motor, supplemented by a battery pack to drive the front wheels with 161 total system horsepower. This specialized powertrain delivers smooth, silent electric operation, though long-term reliability heavily depends on proper maintenance of the fuel cell stack and cooling systems.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Reduction Gear Transmission

A direct-drive single-speed reduction gear transmission transfers power efficiently from the electric motor to the front wheels without traditional gear shifting. This simplified design typically requires minimal maintenance and offers highly reliable daily operation.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V761000: Fuel System, Other:Storage:Tank Assembly:Pressure Relief Devices](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Nexo&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** The thermally activated pressure relief device (TPRD) may break and cause a hydrogen gas leak, which can result in a fire while parked. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the TPRD, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Hyundai Nexo presents an intriguing, eco-friendly option for buyers living in regions with established hydrogen infrastructure. With virtually no widespread complaint patterns reported for this model year, mechanical reliability appears stable, though specialized components require certified technician service. Prospective buyers should confirm local hydrogen station access and secure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection.
