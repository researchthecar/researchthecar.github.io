# 2024 Hyundai Nexo

### Overview
The 2024 Hyundai Nexo represents a significant step forward in hydrogen fuel cell technology, offering a zero-emission driving experience with impressive range and performance. As a relatively new technology in the mainstream automotive market, its long-term reliability is still being established. However, based on initial data and the maturity of Hyundai's fuel cell systems, the Nexo is engineered for durability. Key areas of focus for reliability include the fuel cell stack, hydrogen storage system, electric powertrain, and associated control electronics. While specific widespread component failures are not yet extensively documented, potential areas for attention, as with any advanced vehicle, involve the complex integration of these systems and their long-term performance under various environmental conditions.
