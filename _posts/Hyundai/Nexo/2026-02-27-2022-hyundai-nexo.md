# 2022 Hyundai Nexo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Hyundai Nexo is an innovative hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicle that offers zero-emission driving paired with the practicality of a crossover. As a specialized alternative-fuel vehicle, prospective buyers should carefully weigh its unique powertrain against local hydrogen fueling infrastructure and overall service availability.

---

## Engine

### Hydrogen Fuel Cell Stack and Electric Motor System

The Nexo utilizes a 95-kW fuel cell stack paired with a 120-kW electric motor, producing 161 horsepower and 291 lb-ft of torque. This hydrogen fuel cell system delivers smooth electric propulsion, though its long-term reliability heavily depends on specialized dealer support.

## Transmission

### Single-Speed Reduction Gearbox

Power is sent to the front wheels via a simple single-speed reduction gear transmission. This direct-drive setup is inherently mechanically straightforward and typically requires minimal maintenance.

## Safety

### Thermally Activated Pressure Relief Device

Components related to hydrogen storage and pressure regulation require strict adherence to manufacturer maintenance and inspection guidelines.

**TPRD Concern**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported safety concerns regarding the Thermally Activated Pressure Relief Device on the hydrogen storage system.
- **Solution:** Have the hydrogen storage and pressure relief systems inspected by an authorized Hyundai hydrogen service facility.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V761000: Fuel System, Other:Storage:Tank Assembly:Pressure Relief Devices](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Nexo&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The thermally activated pressure relief device (TPRD) may break and cause a hydrogen gas leak, which can result in a fire while parked. A vehicle fire increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the TPRD, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Hyundai Nexo presents a technologically advanced driving experience, but prospective buyers must verify access to hydrogen refueling stations and specialized service technicians before purchasing. While powertrain issues are rare in owner reports, careful pre-purchase inspection of the fuel system is essential.
