# 2016 Hyundai Azera Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2016 Hyundai Azera, buyers will find a well-equipped and comfortable full-size sedan that generally offers a smooth ride. However, review owner reports carefully, as some drivers have flagged issues ranging from steering stickiness to engine and cooling concerns.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Lambda II GDI V6

The 3.3-liter direct-injected V6 engine produces approximately 293 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque, providing robust acceleration for the Azera. It is generally robust, though owners should monitor fluid levels and cooling system integrity closely.

**Oil Consumption and Overheating**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported low oil levels triggering pressure warning lights, as well as engine overheating leading to failure.
- **Solution:** Check oil levels regularly and inspect the cooling system for leaks or thermostat issues.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission with Shiftronic

Paired exclusively with the V6 engine, this traditional 6-speed automatic delivers smooth gear shifts in everyday driving. It generally proves dependable, though routine fluid maintenance is recommended.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering System

Issues related to steering feel and potential binding have been reported by owners during highway travel.

**Steering Sticky or Locking Up**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience the steering wheel sticking or temporarily locking when making minor adjustments during straight-line highway driving, requiring extra force to correct.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electronic steering gear and column components for binding or failure.

## Exterior

### Panoramic Sunroof

Exterior body and trim components are generally durable, though panoramic glass panels warrant close inspection.

**Panoramic Glass Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic sunroof glass can explode or shatter spontaneously while driving at highway speeds, creating a loud noise and scattering glass fragments.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof glass for pre-existing stress fractures and replace the shattered assembly if failure occurs.

## Safety

### Airbag Control Module

Safety equipment generally performs well, but sporadic electronic module failures can disable airbags.

**Airbag Warning Light and Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light illuminates unexpectedly on the dashboard, and diagnostic scans indicate the internal module has failed or erased itself.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty airbag control module to restore proper airbag system functionality.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V619000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Azera&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the trunk latch base, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Hyundai Azera offers strong performance and a comfortable cabin, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Pay particular attention to the electronic steering feel, cooling system health, and functional safety electronics before finalizing a purchase.
