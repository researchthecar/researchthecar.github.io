**Introduction**

The 2011 Hyundai Azera is a midsize sedan that offers a spacious cabin, smooth ride, and powerful engine. It comes with a standard 3.3-liter V6 engine that produces 293 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. Other engine options include a 3.8-liter V6 producing 333 horsepower and 291 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. People like the Azera for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and powerful engine. However, some have reported issues with the transmission and suspension.

**Engine**

**3.3-liter V6 Engine**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, misfiring, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Oil Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from engine block or valve covers
    * Solution: Replace leaking gaskets or seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.8-liter V6 Engine**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or ticking noises from engine, reduced acceleration
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shuddering or Slipping**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shuddering or slipping during shifting
    * Solution: Replace transmission fluid or solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Delayed Engagement**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission delays when engaging into gear
    * Solution: Replace transmission control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Gear Grinding**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grating or grinding noises when shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace worn gears or synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mount Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from front suspension, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Poor handling, excessive bouncing over bumps, oil leaking from shocks
    * Solution: Replace rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical Short in Headlight Wiring**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights flickering or not working, electrical burning smell
    * Solution: Repair or replace wiring harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: 14V824000
* **Faulty Battery Sensor**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check battery light illuminates despite new battery
    * Solution: Replace battery sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture or fog inside headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replace headlight seals or housing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or buzzing noise from dashboard area
    * Solution: Tighten loose components or apply sound deadening material
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Center Console Squeak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise from center console when shifting or driving
    * Solution: Tighten or lubricate components within center console
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2011 Hyundai Azera offers a spacious and comfortable cabin, along with a powerful engine. However, some owners have reported issues with the transmission, suspension, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues can vary, but some, such as timing chain tensioner failure or transmission problems, can be costly to repair. It's important for potential buyers to be aware of these potential issues before making a purchasing decision.
