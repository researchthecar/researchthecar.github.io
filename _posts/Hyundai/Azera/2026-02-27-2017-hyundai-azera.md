# 2017 Hyundai Azera Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Hyundai Azera offers a spacious, comfortable cabin and a smooth ride, representing the final model year for Hyundai's full-size sedan in the U.S. market. While the powertrain is generally robust, prospective used-car buyers should review recurring owner complaints regarding steering stiffness and minor body or interior component failures.

---

## Engine

### 3.3L Lambda II GDI V6

The 3.3-liter direct-injection V6 engine produces 293 horsepower and 255 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth and ample acceleration for this full-size sedan. Overall, this engine has a reputation for solid mechanical durability when routine fluid maintenance is observed.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic with Shiftronic

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission delivers power smoothly to the front wheels and generally avoids the complexity and complaints associated with newer multi-speed or dual-clutch units. It stands as a reliable, low-drama component of the Azera's drivetrain.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering

Owner reports highlight occasional steering stiffness at highway speeds, which requires careful inspection during a test drive.

**Steering Wheel Sticking or Locking**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that the steering wheel can briefly stick, bind, or feel heavy and difficult to adjust while maintaining highway speeds, requiring extra physical force to stay in lane.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check the electric power steering motor, steering column assembly, or related sensors for faults.

## Exterior

### Trunk Latch and Release Mechanism

Minor convenience issues involving trunk access have been noted by a small number of owners.

**Trunk Failure to Open or Secure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced situations where the trunk fails to open via the key fob, interior button, or manual release, or alternatively fails to latch securely shut.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the trunk latch actuator mechanism or associated electronic release switches as needed.

### Panoramic Sunroof

While rare, spontaneous glass breakage is a documented safety concern for vehicles equipped with panoramic sunroofs.

**Spontaneous Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The panoramic glass roof may spontaneously shatter or explode outward while driving under normal conditions.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the panoramic glass assembly and thorough cleaning of the surrounding track and headliner.

## Interior

### Seats

Isolated complaints regarding seat stability point to the need for a thorough interior inspection before purchase.

**Loose Rear Seats**
- **Symptoms:** Rear passenger seats may feel loose, unstable, or improperly anchored.
- **Solution:** Have a professional inspect the seat mounting brackets and re-torque or replace anchoring hardware.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V619000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Trunk Lid:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Hyundai&model=Azera&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The trunk latch may become damaged, preventing the opening of the trunk from the inside. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 401, "Internal Trunk Release." A person inside the trunk compartment may become trapped, increasing their risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the trunk latch base, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Hyundai Azera provides a quiet, well-appointed ride with a dependable powertrain. When shopping for a used model, pay close attention to the steering feel during highway test drives and verify that all interior features, including trunk locks and the panoramic roof, are fully functional.
