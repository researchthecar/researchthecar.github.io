# 2015 Hyundai Azera

The 2015 Hyundai Azera is a full-size sedan known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and upscale features. ### Battery Drain
**Intermittent Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle fails to start after sitting for a period, indicating a parasitic draw.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the draw (e.g., faulty module, aftermarket accessory). Repair or replacement of the faulty component.
- **Cost:** $150 - $500

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Hyundai Azera stands out as a reliable choice in the large sedan segment. Its powertrain, including the 3.3L V6 engine and 6-speed automatic transmission, is generally robust with no significant widespread mechanical failures reported. The suspension and exterior components also exhibit good durability. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically concerning occasional infotainment system glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain. These issues are typically addressable through software updates or targeted repairs. Overall, the 2015 Azera offers a dependable ownership experience with a strong emphasis on comfort and refinement, making it a solid used car option for those seeking value and reliability.
