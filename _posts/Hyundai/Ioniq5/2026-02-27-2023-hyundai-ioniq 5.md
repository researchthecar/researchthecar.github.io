# 2023 Hyundai Ioniq 5

### Overview
The 2023 Hyundai Ioniq 5 is a highly acclaimed electric vehicle known for its innovative design, spacious interior, and strong performance. As an EV, its mechanical complexity differs significantly from traditional internal combustion engine vehicles. Reliability data for the 2023 model year generally indicates a robust platform, with most reported issues being minor or related to software. Owners often praise its build quality and the advanced technology integrated into the vehicle. While no vehicle is entirely without potential problems, the Ioniq 5 has demonstrated a commendable level of dependability for its inaugural years on the market, positioning it as a strong contender in the growing EV segment.

### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Hyundai Ioniq 5 exhibits strong reliability, particularly in its core electric powertrain and battery systems, with no widespread critical failures reported. The most common concerns tend to be software-related, such as infotainment glitches, which are often resolvable with updates. Minor issues like 12V battery premature failure and occasional charging interruptions have been noted but are not indicative of systemic problems. Overall, the Ioniq 5 is considered a dependable electric vehicle, with most potential issues being minor and manageable. Its robust construction and advanced technology contribute to a positive ownership experience regarding reliability.
