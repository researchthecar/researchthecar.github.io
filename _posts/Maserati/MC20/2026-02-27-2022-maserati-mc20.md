### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Maserati MC20, with its potent Nettuno V6 engine and advanced dual-clutch transmission, exhibits strong initial reliability in its core mechanical components. No widespread issues have been reported for the engine or transmission. The suspension system also appears robust. Potential areas for attention, though not widespread, include minor electrical gremlins such as infotainment system glitches and the need for recalibration of ADAS sensors. The exotic carbon fiber construction and premium interior materials are holding up well. As with any high-performance supercar, diligent adherence to the manufacturer's maintenance schedule is paramount for ensuring long-term dependability and optimal performance.
