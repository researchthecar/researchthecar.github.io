# 2023 Maserati MC20 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Maserati MC20 is a striking mid-engine supercar powered by an innovative twin-turbo V6 engine and an advanced dual-clutch transmission. Because it operates in a low-volume exotic tier, formal owner complaint data is exceptionally sparse. Prospective buyers should approach the MC20 with standard exotic-car mindfulness, ensuring a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Nettuno Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter Nettuno twin-turbocharged V6 utilizes Formula 1-derived pre-chamber combustion technology to produce 621 horsepower and 538 lb-ft of torque. It provides blistering performance for the MC20, though its complex architecture requires meticulous maintenance by certified technicians.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

An 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission routes power to the rear wheels, offering rapid, seamless gear changes suited for both track and street driving. It generally operates smoothly, but buyers should test all drive modes and check for any low-speed clutch shudder during a pre-purchase inspection.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V537000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=MC20&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." A vehicle with an incorrect maximum capacity weight value may be overloaded, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install corrected labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=MC20&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Maserati MC20 remains an exotic machine defined by breathtaking performance and striking Italian design rather than traditional commuter reliability metrics. With minimal owner complaints recorded, buyers are advised to focus on service history, professional inspections, and remaining warranty coverage when evaluating a pre-owned model.
