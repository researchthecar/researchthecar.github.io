# 2024 Maserati MC20 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Maserati MC20 represents a dramatic mid-engine flagship for the brand, pairing exotic Italian styling with high-performance engineering. Because it occupies an ultra-low-volume, exotic super-sports car segment, typical owner feedback and complaint data are exceptionally scarce. Prospective buyers should approach shopping with the standard due diligence required for exotic vehicles, verifying service history and ensuring a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a qualified specialist.

---

## Engine

### Nettuno 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The MC20 is powered by a proprietary 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged 'Nettuno' V6 utilizing F1-derived pre-chamber combustion technology, generating approximately 621 horsepower and 538 lb-ft of torque. This innovative high-output engine delivers blistering supercar performance, though buyers should expect exotic-tier maintenance costs and complexities.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Dual-Clutch Automatic

Power is routed to the rear wheels via an 8-speed dual-clutch automatic transmission designed to deliver lightning-fast gear changes. The gearbox contributes heavily to the car's track-ready dynamics while remaining refined during everyday street driving.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V537000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=MC20&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." A vehicle with an incorrect maximum capacity weight value may be overloaded, which can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install corrected labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=MC20&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Maserati MC20 remains a rare and potent exotic choice with virtually no widespread recurring complaint patterns reported in standard public databases. For used-car buyers, the primary considerations will revolve around specialized maintenance, insurance, and verifying pristine provenance rather than common mechanical defects.
