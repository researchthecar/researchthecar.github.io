# 2024 Maserati GranTurismo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Maserati GranTurismo marks a fresh chapter for the iconic luxury grand tourer, bringing striking Italian styling and powerful twin-turbo V6 performance to the modern luxury market. As an exotic high-performance coupe, it offers breathtaking acceleration and refined grand touring capabilities, though buyers should expect the typical ownership complexities associated with high-end Italian exotics. Prospective purchasers are encouraged to thoroughly inspect vehicle electronics and powertrain options prior to purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Nettuno)

The Nettuno 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 utilizes advanced pre-chamber combustion derived from Formula 1 technology, producing robust horsepower outputs ranging from roughly 483 hp to over 550 hp depending on the trim level. This high-strung performance engine delivers exhilarating acceleration, though it requires meticulous maintenance and premium fueling to remain in top operating condition.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

An 8-speed ZF torque-converter automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the wheels, balancing lightning-fast manual paddle shifts with refined low-speed cruising manners. It is generally regarded as a robust and dependable unit well-suited to handle the high torque output of the twin-turbo V6.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V679000: Electrical System:Instrument Panel:Fuel Gauge](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An unexpected engine stall while driving increases the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the BCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V892000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 111, "Rear Visibility." An inoperative rearview camera display reduces the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the CVPAM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 25V244000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Without a tire information label, the vehicle may be overloaded, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the labels, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V722000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamp, Reflective Devices and Associated Equipment." Improperly aimed headlights can result in insufficient illumination of the road, reducing the driver's visibility, and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati North America will inspect and, if necessary, install one or two caps to cover the horizontal headlight aim adjustment mechanism, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
