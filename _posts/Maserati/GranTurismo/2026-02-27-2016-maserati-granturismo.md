# 2016 Maserati GranTurismo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Maserati GranTurismo delivers exotic Italian styling and a Ferrari-derived V8 soundtrack, making it an alluring choice for used luxury GT shoppers. Beneath its sweeping lines lies a high-performance grand tourer designed more for weekend cruising than appliance-like reliability, meaning potential buyers should budget accordingly for maintenance and upkeep.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L V8 (454 hp, 384 lb-ft)

Powered by a naturally aspirated 4.7-liter V8 producing roughly 454 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, mated exclusively to a rear-wheel-drive layout. This Ferrari-derived powerplant offers thrilling performance and an unmistakable exhaust note, though routine services and exotic maintenance intervals require specialized care.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed ZF Automatic

Equipped with a traditional 6-speed ZF automatic transmission sending power to the rear wheels. This torque-converter automatic is generally sturdier and smoother in everyday traffic than older automated manual gearboxes, offering dependable grand touring operation when maintained properly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V461000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may have a defective power supply capacitor that can cause a deactivation or inadvertent deployment of the air bag. A deactivated air bag increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V744000: Latches/Locks/Linkages:Doors:Latch](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the door latch was not heat treated, in the event of a crash, the door latch may fail allowing the door to open, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the passenger side door latch assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V849000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak in the presence of an ignition source can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the main delivery fuel line, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Maserati GranTurismo remains a compelling and charismatic grand tourer for buyers seeking exotic flair and sonorous V8 performance without daily commuter demands. While formal owner complaint trends are minimal, prospective owners must carefully inspect service history and prepare for the higher upkeep costs associated with an Italian luxury performance car.
