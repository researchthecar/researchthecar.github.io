# 2017 Maserati GranTurismo Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Maserati GranTurismo represents the final years of the iconic Pininfarina-styled coupe and convertible generation, offering exotic Italian styling and a Ferrari-derived V8 soundtrack. While these grand tourers deliver an unforgettable driving experience, prospective buyers should approach them with a clear understanding of high maintenance costs and potential mechanical quirks typical of exotic Italian vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L V8

The 4.7-liter naturally aspirated V8 produces approximately 454 horsepower and 384 lb-ft of torque, delivering rapid acceleration and a signature exhaust note. Overall, this Ferrari-derived powerplant is mechanically robust when properly maintained, though regular servicing and premium upkeep are mandatory.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed ZF Automatic

The traditional 6-speed ZF torque-converter automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the rear wheels while offering reliable everyday drivability compared to earlier automated manual setups.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V461000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=GranTurismo&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The air bag Electronic Control Unit (ECU) may have a defective power supply capacitor that can cause a deactivation or inadvertent deployment of the air bag. A deactivated air bag increases the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the air bag ECU, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Maserati GranTurismo is a dramatic and relatively robust exotic grand tourer, especially benefiting from the mature 6-speed ZF automatic transmission and Ferrari-derived V8. Buyers should prioritize comprehensive service records and budget generously for ongoing maintenance and premium upkeep.
