# 2018 Maserati Quattroporte Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Navigating the used market for a luxury Italian sedan like this requires keeping an eye on powertrain performance and potential electronic or throttle quirks. While owner feedback is sparse, inspecting how the vehicle handles acceleration and drivetrain response during a test drive is an essential part of the buying process.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers robust horsepower and strong torque for dynamic performance. Buyers should ensure proper service history and check electronic throttle responsiveness during a test drive.

**Uncommanded Acceleration**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may occasionally continue to accelerate after the driver lifts their foot off the gas pedal, particularly when driving in aggressive modes.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the electronic throttle body, accelerator pedal sensor, and engine control module for faults or software updates.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8

The potent 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine provides high-end performance with substantial horsepower and torque outputs. Routine maintenance and careful cooling system checks are vital for long-term ownership.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This smooth-shifting 8-speed automatic transmission channels power efficiently to the rear wheels or all-wheel-drive system. Overall, it provides refined gear changes when maintained properly.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V347000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the welds fracture, it can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the sub frame for incomplete welds, replacing the sub frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V834000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak within the engine compartment increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will install a new fuel delivery line kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The vehicle offers engaging performance and striking Italian design, but luxury sedan ownership demands careful pre-purchase inspections and diligence regarding electronics and throttle behavior. Prioritize models with complete service records to ensure a more reliable ownership experience.
