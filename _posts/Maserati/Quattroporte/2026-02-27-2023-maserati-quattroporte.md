# 2023 Maserati Quattroporte Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Maserati Quattroporte blends Italian luxury and performance with striking design, though prospective buyers should approach its high-end technology and mechanical systems with careful inspection. Because owner-reported feedback is limited for this model year, verifying a comprehensive service history and performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection remain essential steps for any used-car buyer.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine produces approximately 345 horsepower, offering brisk acceleration and distinctive exhaust notes. It provides a balanced entry point to the Quattroporte lineup, though routine maintenance is crucial for long-term health.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8

The high-performance 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 delivers around 580 horsepower for exceptional straight-line speed and thrilling driving dynamics. Buyers should expect higher running and maintenance costs compared to the V6 variants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed ZF automatic transmission delivers smooth, rapid shifts across all trim levels and pairs effectively with both rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. While generally robust, electronic controls can occasionally present isolated shifting anomalies.

**Neutral Gear Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected transmission behavior, such as the vehicle shifting into neutral gear unexpectedly while operating.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician or dealership inspect the transmission control module and software calibration.

## Brakes

### Electronic Brake System

High-performance braking hardware provides strong stopping power suited for luxury sports sedans, though electronic sensor integration requires careful diagnostic oversight.

**Electronic Brake Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported warning messages or sudden electronic brake system malfunctions occurring alongside transmission issues.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic scanning at an authorized service center is recommended to check for electrical faults or sensor failures.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Maserati Quattroporte remains an exotic and engaging luxury sedan choice, but buyers should remain vigilant regarding complex electronics and powertrain controls. Ensuring that all software updates have been applied and securing a professional pre-purchase inspection will help mitigate potential ownership risks.
