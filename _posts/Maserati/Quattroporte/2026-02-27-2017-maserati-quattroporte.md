# 2017 Maserati Quattroporte Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2017 Maserati Quattroporte means stepping into the world of Italian luxury, exotic engine notes, and striking design. Because available owner data is exceptionally sparse for this model year, buyers should rely on thorough pre-purchase inspections and verifying proper maintenance history to navigate potential luxury ownership costs.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6

This Ferrari-derived 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 produces around 404 to 424 horsepower depending on the trim, delivering brisk acceleration. While thrilling to drive, maintenance and repair bills for this exotic powerplant can be significantly higher than mainstream luxury sedans.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Displacing 3.8 liters, this potent twin-turbo V8 pushes out over 520 horsepower for top-tier performance trims. It rewards drivers with incredible power delivery, but demands diligent upkeep and careful mechanical scrutiny from prospective buyers.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission shifts smoothly and pairs well with both engine options for responsive power delivery. It is generally a robust unit, though fluid changes should be monitored during pre-purchase inspections.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V610000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump resistor cable assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V856000: Power Train:Driveline:Differential Unit](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The rear differential unit pinion nut may not have been properly tightened, allowing the pinion nut to loosen. A loose pinion gear may bind, possibly resulting in the rear wheels locking up and increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the rear differential unit pinion nut torque, and, as necessary, replace the rear differential unit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V840000: Tires:Sidewall](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** A loss of air may result in tire failure or a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V173000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Due to an engine software problem, at slow speeds such as stop-and-go traffic, the transmission may unexpectedly shift into neutral or park, or the engine may shut off. If the transmission shifts into neutral or park unexpectedly, or if the engine shuts down without warning, it can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will re-flash the software for the engine control module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V839000: Back Over Prevention: Camera System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** If the back-up camera is inoperable, it may be more difficult for the operator to see people or objects behind the vehicle, increasing the risk of injury or crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will update the ETM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V046000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** Adjusting the front seats may cause the seat wiring harness to rub, possibly resulting in an electrical short. An electrical short can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles, replacing the seat wiring harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
