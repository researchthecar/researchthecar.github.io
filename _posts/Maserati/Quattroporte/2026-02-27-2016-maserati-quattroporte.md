# 2016 Maserati Quattroporte Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2016 Maserati Quattroporte means balancing Italian luxury, striking design, and potent performance against the realities of high-end exotic car maintenance. While ownership brings considerable prestige and driving engagement, buyers should approach the marketplace with careful inspection habits and awareness of potential electrical and auxiliary system quirks typical of high-end European sedans of this vintage.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine delivers robust horsepower and strong torque for dynamic performance. Generally durable when properly maintained, prospective buyers should verify regular service intervals and fluid changes.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8

The flagship 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 offers exceptional power output and exhilarating acceleration. Routine professional inspections are recommended to ensure optimal health of the turbocharging and cooling systems.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth, responsive gear changes suited to both relaxed cruising and spirited driving. It is widely regarded as a robust and dependable unit across various luxury applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V264000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During the assembly process, the rear tie-rod to hub assembly attaching bolt may not have been properly tightened. An improperly tightened rear tie-rod to hub carrier assembly attaching bolt may allow the tie-rod to separate from the hub carrier, resulting in a loss of control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the left and right rear tie-rod to hub carrier assembly attaching bolts to verify that they are properly tightened, replacing the rear tie-rod to hub carrier assembly as necessary, free. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V610000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Fuel Pump](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A stall can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the fuel pump resistor cable assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V046000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Adjusting the front seats may cause the seat wiring harness to rub, possibly resulting in an electrical short. An electrical short can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles, replacing the seat wiring harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V122000: Vehicle Speed Control:Accelerator Pedal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Quattroporte&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the floor mat moves and becomes trapped between the accelerator and the vehicle carpet, it may result in very high vehicle speeds, which could cause a crash, serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver-side floor mat and possibly the accelerator pedal cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
