# 2016 Maserati Ghibli Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Maserati Ghibli offers Italian luxury and a stirring exhaust note, but prospective buyers should approach with caution. While owner-reported complaints for this model year cover a relatively small sample, they highlight specific areas of concern including sudden powertrain disruptions and interior trim vulnerabilities.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Base / S)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6, developed with Ferrari, produces approximately 345 hp in base trim and up to 404 hp in the S variant. Buyers should maintain rigorous service records, as proper upkeep is critical for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission is widely praised for its smooth and quick gear shifts across all driving modes. It generally offers solid mechanical dependability when paired with routine fluid changes.

### Powertrain Control

Electrical and powertrain control components require careful diagnostic checks during pre-purchase inspections.

**Motive Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported unexpected losses of motive power while driving at highway speeds without prior warning indicators.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the powertrain control modules and electrical harness connections.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Brake system wear should be carefully evaluated during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure safe stopping performance.

**Brake Squeaking and Reduced Stopping Response**
- **Symptoms:** Abnormal squeaking noises accompanied by perceived decreases in stopping efficiency at various speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads, rotors, and calipers for excessive wear or glazing, and replace components as needed.

## Exterior

### Sun Visors

Cabin hardware and trim pieces can occasionally suffer from premature wear or mechanical loosening.

**Sagging Sun Visor**
- **Symptoms:** The sun visor can loosen internally and drop down unexpectedly while driving, obstructing the driver's forward view.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty sun visor assembly with an updated design.

## Interior

### Dashboard Trim and Airbags

Interior material degradation around safety equipment underscores the importance of a thorough physical inspection.

**Dashboard Leather Peeling**
- **Symptoms:** Factory-installed dashboard leather can peel away, raising concerns regarding proper airbag deployment aesthetics and clearance.
- **Solution:** Consult an interior trim specialist to secure or replace loose dashboard leather components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V840000: Tires:Sidewall](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A loss of air may result in tire failure or a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the affected tires, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V046000: Seats:Front Assembly:Power Adjust](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** Adjusting the front seats may cause the seat wiring harness to rub, possibly resulting in an electrical short. An electrical short can increase the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the vehicles, replacing the seat wiring harness, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V264000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** During the assembly process, the rear tie-rod to hub assembly attaching bolt may not have been properly tightened. An improperly tightened rear tie-rod to hub carrier assembly attaching bolt may allow the tie-rod to separate from the hub carrier, resulting in a loss of control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the left and right rear tie-rod to hub carrier assembly attaching bolts to verify that they are properly tightened, replacing the rear tie-rod to hub carrier assembly as necessary, free. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V122000: Vehicle Speed Control:Accelerator Pedal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the floor mat moves and becomes trapped between the accelerator and the vehicle carpet, it may result in very high vehicle speeds, which could cause a crash, serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will replace the driver-side floor mat and possibly the accelerator pedal cover, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a used 2016 Maserati Ghibli, prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection performed by a specialist familiar with Italian luxury vehicles. Verifying service history and checking for unresolved powertrain or electrical complaints will help mitigate unexpected ownership costs.
