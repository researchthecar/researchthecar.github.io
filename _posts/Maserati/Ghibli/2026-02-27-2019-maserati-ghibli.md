# 2019 Maserati Ghibli Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Maserati Ghibli, buyers are drawn to its exotic styling, Ferrari-built engines, and luxury cabin. However, luxury Italian performance demands careful inspection and diligence regarding historical maintenance. While owner-reported complaints for this model year are relatively sparse, prospective buyers should still thoroughly test-drive the vehicle to ensure mechanical and electronic systems operate seamlessly.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Base/S)

Equipped with a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 producing between 345 and 424 horsepower depending on the tune, this Ferrari-derived powerplant offers robust acceleration and a distinctive exhaust note. Buyers should prioritize comprehensive service records, as proper maintenance is vital for longevity.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic

Paired with an 8-speed ZF automatic transmission, gear shifts are typically crisp and refined. This transmission is generally robust, but any hesitation or erratic shifting during a test drive warrants professional evaluation.

### Powertrain Control System

The electrical architecture controls numerous comfort and drivetrain features, making diagnostic scans essential before purchase.

**Inadvertent Shifting and Engine Stall**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shift into Neutral or Park at low speeds, or the engine may shut off entirely.
- **Solution:** Consult a specialist to diagnose electrical software glitches, sensor faults, or powertrain control module issues.

## Suspension

### Rear Suspension

Suspension components provide sporty handling, but owners should inspect for premature wear.

**Rear-End Sway**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience the vehicle's rear end swaying or losing composure when driving over road dips, particularly at higher speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the rear suspension components, bushings, alignment, and shocks for wear or looseness.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Maserati Ghibli offers thrilling performance and distinctive Italian flair, but prospective buyers should approach with caution. Ensure a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a technician familiar with exotic or luxury European vehicles to catch any hidden suspension or electrical anomalies.
