# 2021 Maserati Ghibli Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Maserati Ghibli delivers Italian style, luxury, and distinctive performance to the midsize luxury sport sedan segment. While powertrain options offer robust output and engaging dynamics, prospective used-car buyers should weigh the typical maintenance overhead and luxury vehicle ownership costs associated with Italian performance marques.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Base / Modena)

Ferrari-built 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 producing approximately 345 to 424 horsepower depending on the tune. This engine provides brisk acceleration and a characteristic exhaust note, requiring diligent maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8 (Trofeo)

High-performance 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 generating around 580 horsepower for the top-tier Trofeo trim. It transforms the sedan into a high-speed cruiser but demands meticulous upkeep and premium operating costs.

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Hybrid (Modena / GT)

Mild-hybrid 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder paired with an e-Booster system, rated at roughly 332 horsepower. This powertrain balances efficiency with responsive low-end torque for daily driving.

## Transmission

### ZF 8-Speed Automatic

ZF eight-speed torque-converter automatic transmission featuring aluminum paddle shifters. It delivers smooth shifts and reliable operation across all Ghibli powertrain configurations.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V872000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. A fuel leak also increases the risk of fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel line sensor housing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V703000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V613000: Tires](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Tires that are inflated to the incorrect pressure could lead to a loss of vehicle stability and control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the placard and update the tire pressure monitoring system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V269000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. A fuel leak also increases the risk of fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel line sensor housing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Maserati Ghibli is an appealing choice for buyers seeking exotic flair and strong performance in a daily-drivable luxury sedan. Standard pre-purchase inspections are highly recommended to verify the health of the complex electronics and high-output powertrains before finalizing a purchase.
