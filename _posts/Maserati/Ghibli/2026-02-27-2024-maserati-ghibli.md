# 2024 Maserati Ghibli Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Maserati Ghibli continues to offer Italian luxury, exotic exhaust notes, and striking styling for discerning buyers. While early production units of past model years had their share of electrical and mechanical growing pains, newer iterations benefit from refined engineering. Prospective buyers exploring the used market should evaluate powertrain performance and general electronics thoroughly before finalizing a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Modena)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 generates approximately 424 horsepower and 428 lb-ft of torque, delivering brisk acceleration. It provides strong performance, though buyers should expect premium maintenance costs.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8 (Trofeo)

This potent 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 pushes out around 580 horsepower and 538 lb-ft of torque for maximum performance. It transforms the sedan into a high-speed cruiser, though fuel economy and upkeep are substantial.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic

This widely used 8-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, smooth shifts and pairs effectively with both rear-wheel and all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally proves dependable with routine fluid changes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Maserati Ghibli remains an emotional choice for luxury sedan shoppers who prioritize exclusivity and performance over mainstream reliability. Maintenance and insurance costs will be high, so a pre-purchase inspection by a specialist is strongly advised.
