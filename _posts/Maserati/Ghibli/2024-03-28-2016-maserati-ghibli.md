**Introduction**

The 2016 Maserati Ghibli is a luxury sports sedan that offers a captivating blend of performance, style, and Italian heritage. It is available with a choice of three gasoline-powered engines: a 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 with 345 horsepower (S Q4 trim), a 3.8L twin-turbocharged V8 with 523 horsepower (S Q4, SQ4 GranSport), and a 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 with 404 horsepower (S Q4 GranLusso). All trims are equipped with an 8-speed automatic transmission and all-wheel drive.

While the Ghibli has received praise for its sleek design, responsive handling, and powerful engines, owners have also reported some issues with various components.

**Engine**

**3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6 (345 HP)**
* **Intake Manifold Runner Control Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic engine performance, loss of power, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold runner control
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.8L Twin-Turbocharged V8 (523 HP)**
* **Exhaust Valve Damage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loud engine noise, reduced engine power, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replace exhaust valves and valve guides
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**
* **Shift Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hesitation or delayed gear shifts, shuddering, check engine light illumination
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $1,800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squealing or clunking noise from front suspension, vibration, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace front lower control arms
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies after sitting for an extended period, slow engine cranks
    * Solution: Diagnose and resolve electrical system parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the source of the drain
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water condensation inside headlight assembly, reduced visibility at night
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Cassette Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof does not open or close properly, squealing or grinding noise
    * Solution: Replace sunroof cassette
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: 20V-495

**Summary**

While the 2016 Maserati Ghibli offers a captivating driving experience, potential buyers should be aware of the various issues that owners have reported. The severity of the issues varies, with some being relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, while others can be more costly and affect the overall performance of the vehicle. It is recommended to thoroughly research and consider the potential repair costs before purchasing a used Ghibli.
