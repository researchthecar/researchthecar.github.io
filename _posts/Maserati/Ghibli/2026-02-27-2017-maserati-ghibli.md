# 2017 Maserati Ghibli

### Overview
The 2017 Maserati Ghibli, a luxury sports sedan, offers a blend of Italian styling and performance. While generally appreciated for its driving dynamics and premium interior, like many high-performance vehicles, it can present specific reliability concerns. Owners often report issues related to the powertrain, particularly with certain engine and transmission configurations. Electrical systems and infotainment can also be a source of frustration. However, for those who prioritize a distinctive driving experience and are prepared for potential maintenance, the Ghibli remains a compelling option. This analysis focuses on documented common issues beyond standard maintenance, providing insights into potential ownership costs and areas to monitor.

---


## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6 (350 hp)
**Turbocharger Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Loss of power, whistling noises, increased exhaust smoke.
- **Solution:** Turbocharger replacement or repair.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,500

**Oil Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible oil stains under the vehicle, burning oil smell, low oil level.
- **Solution:** Replacement of gaskets, seals, or oil pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP70)
**Harsh Shifting / Jerking**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable clunks or hesitations during gear changes, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, software update, or internal transmission repair.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,200

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Reddish fluid under the vehicle, burning smell, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of seals, transmission lines, or transmission pan.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Air Suspension Leaks (if equipped)**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicle sagging on one side, uneven ride height, 'air suspension fault' warning.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of air springs, lines, or compressor.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,500


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing / Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system reboots randomly, features not working.
- **Solution:** Software updates, system reset, or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
_No major issues reported for this configuration._

## Exterior

### Door Handles
**Sticking or Malfunctioning Handles**
- **Symptoms:** Door handles retract slowly or not at all, difficulty opening doors.
- **Solution:** Lubrication or replacement of the door handle mechanism.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


## Interior

### Climate Control
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No air coming from vents, inconsistent fan speed, unusual noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blower motor or resistor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600

### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Maserati Ghibli, while offering a desirable blend of performance and luxury, exhibits some common reliability concerns. The twin-turbocharged V6 engines can be prone to turbocharger issues and oil leaks, and the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission may experience harsh shifting or leaks. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and battery drain, are also reported. Exterior door handle mechanisms and interior climate control components can also require attention. Owners should be prepared for potentially higher maintenance costs compared to more mainstream luxury brands, with specific attention needed for powertrain and electrical systems. Regular servicing and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the Ghibli's performance and longevity.
