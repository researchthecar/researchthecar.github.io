**Introduction:**

The 2021 Maserati Ghibli is a luxury sports sedan that embodies Italian craftsmanship and performance. It is available with two engine options, a 3.0L V6 with a twin-turbocharger and a 3.8L V8 with a twin-turbocharger. The 3.0L engine produces 345 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, while the 3.8L engine generates 580 horsepower and 538 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Ghibli is acclaimed for its stylish design, powerful engines, and luxurious cabin, but it has also been reported to have some issues.

**Engine:**

**3.0L V6 Twin-Turbocharger**

* **Timing Belt Tensioner Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The timing belt tensioner may fail, causing the timing belt to slip or break, resulting in catastrophic engine damage. Symptoms include a rattling noise from the engine, difficulty starting the vehicle, and a loss of power.
Solution: Replace the timing belt tensioner and timing belt.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**3.8L V8 Twin-Turbocharger**

* **Connecting Rod Bearing Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The connecting rod bearings may fail, causing the crankshaft to seize and damage the engine. Symptoms include a knocking noise from the engine, reduced engine power, and an illuminated check engine light.
Solution: Replace the engine.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $15,000 - $20,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Delays:**
Description/Symptoms: The transmission may experience delays in shifting gears, especially during acceleration or deceleration. Symptoms include slow acceleration, rough shifting, and a loss of power.
Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The torque converter may fail, causing the transmission to slip or lose power. Symptoms include difficulty engaging gears, shuddering during acceleration, and a loss of power.
Solution: Replace the torque converter.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The front suspension struts may fail, causing the vehicle to lose stability and ride comfort. Symptoms include excessive bouncing, a noisy suspension, and uneven tire wear.
Solution: Replace the front suspension struts.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per strut
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical:**

* **Electrical System Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: The electrical system may malfunction, causing various electrical components to fail or behave erratically. Symptoms include flickering lights, power window problems, and a dead battery.
Solution: Diagnose the electrical system and replace the faulty components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: Varies depending on the faulty component
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: The windshield wiper motor may fail, causing the wipers to stop working or operate erratically. Symptoms include wipers not operating at all, intermittent wiping, or wiper blades chattering.
Solution: Replace the windshield wiper motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Infotainment System Bug:**
Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience software bugs, causing it to freeze, display incorrect information, or lose connectivity. Symptoms include a black screen, glitchy navigation, and Bluetooth pairing issues.
Solution: Update the infotainment system software or replace the unit.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2021 Maserati Ghibli is a luxurious and powerful sports sedan, it has been reported to have several issues, particularly with the engine and transmission. The severity of these issues can vary, with some being relatively minor and others potentially catastrophic. The estimated cost to fix the identified issues can also be substantial, especially for engine and transmission repairs. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues and consider the potential costs of repairs when making a purchase decision.
