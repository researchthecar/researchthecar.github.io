**Introduction**

The 2014 Maserati Ghibli is a mid-size luxury sports sedan that offers impressive performance and stylish design. It is powered by a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine that produces 345 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque. The Ghibli also comes with a six-speed automatic transmission and rear-wheel drive.

Owners of the 2014 Maserati Ghibli generally appreciate its luxurious interior, sporty handling, and powerful engine. However, some owners have reported experiencing various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**3.0L Twin-Turbocharged V6**

* **Cam Phaser Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: The cam phaser can fail, causing engine timing issues that result in rough idling, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
  * Solution: Replace the cam phaser.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Air Suspension Malfunction:**
  * Description/Symptoms: The air suspension system can malfunction, causing the vehicle to ride lower than normal. This can lead to reduced handling and instability at higher speeds.
  * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty components within the air suspension system.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain Issue:**
  * Description/Symptoms: The vehicle's battery may drain quickly, even when not in use. This can be caused by various electrical components drawing excessive power.
  * Solution: Diagnose and replace faulty electrical components, such as the alternator or starter.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Moisture can accumulate inside the headlights, causing poor visibility during wet weather conditions.
  * Solution: Inspect and replace damaged headlight seals or modules.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:**
  * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard can develop a rattle or vibration, especially at higher speeds.
  * Solution: Tighten or replace loose dashboard components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Maserati Ghibli is a performance-oriented luxury sedan that has received positive reviews for its handling and engine performance. However, some owners have experienced issues with various components, including the cam phaser, air suspension, battery, headlights, and dashboard. The severity and cost of these issues can vary, and owners should be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a Ghibli.
