# 2022 Maserati Ghibli Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2022 Maserati Ghibli, buyers are drawn to its striking Italian styling, upscale cabin, and exhilarating performance. As an exotic luxury sport sedan, maintenance and ownership considerations differ significantly from mainstream vehicles. Reviewing available powertrain configurations and typical owner-reported experiences helps prospective buyers determine if this premium Italian sedan fits their lifestyle and budget.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (GT / Modena)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces approximately 345 hp in the GT and 424 hp in the Modena trim, delivering brisk acceleration paired with a distinctive exhaust note. While generally robust, buyers should prioritize comprehensive mechanical inspections and verified maintenance history due to the high-performance nature of the engine.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8 (Trofeo)

Exclusive to the high-performance Trofeo trim, this 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 pumps out an impressive 580 horsepower for blistering straight-line performance. This specialty exotic powerplant requires meticulous upkeep and professional servicing to ensure long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

Equipped across all Ghibli trims, the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers lightning-fast and smooth gear shifts. It is widely regarded in the automotive industry for both refined daily driving and aggressive spirited performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V269000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. A fuel leak also increases the risk of fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel line sensor housing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Maserati Ghibli offers a compelling blend of exotic prestige, luxury, and performance for used-car buyers seeking something unique. Because specific owner complaints and widespread recurring mechanical defects are minimal in current reporting, buyers should focus heavily on verifying professional maintenance records, conducting thorough pre-purchase inspections, and budgeting accordingly for premium upkeep.
