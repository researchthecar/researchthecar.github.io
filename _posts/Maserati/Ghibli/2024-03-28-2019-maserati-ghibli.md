**Introduction to the 2019 Maserati Ghibli**

The 2019 Maserati Ghibli is a mid-size luxury sedan renowned for its sleek design, potent performance, and exclusive Italian craftsmanship. It comes equipped with a turbocharged 3.0L V6 engine delivering 345 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Ghibli is praised for its exhilarating driving experience, well-appointed interior, and stylish exterior. However, like any vehicle, it has certain issues that owners have encountered.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Turbocharged Engine**

* **Fuel Injector Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfiring, rough idling, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replacement of faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Turbocharger Boost Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, overboost, check engine light
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of turbocharger system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Hesitation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed gear changes, rough shifts
    * Solution: Transmission fluid flush, software update
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping, loss of power, overheating
    * Solution: Replacement of torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2000 - $3000+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Wear:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noises, vibration, poor handling
    * Solution: Replacement of control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Bouncy ride, excessive body roll
    * Solution: Replacement of rear shock absorbers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery discharges quickly, vehicle fails to start
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical system issue
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Central Control Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning infotainment system, electrical component issues
    * Solution: Replacement of central control unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1500+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Lens Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks or chips in headlight lenses
    * Solution: Replacement of headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the vehicle interior through the sunroof
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of sunroof seals
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Seat Belt Tensioner Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat belts not retracting properly, check engine light
    * Solution: Replacement of seat belt tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: NHTSA Recall #19V528

**Summary**

The 2019 Maserati Ghibli is a desirable vehicle that can provide an enjoyable driving experience. However, potential buyers should be aware of the various issues that have been reported by owners, ranging from minor annoyances to more costly repairs. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost of repairs can be significant. Therefore, it is important to factor these potential expenses into the overall cost of ownership and consider extended warranty options for peace of mind.
