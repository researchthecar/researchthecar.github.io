**Introduction:**

The 2017 Maserati Ghibli is a stylish and powerful luxury sedan. It offers a thrilling driving experience with its high-performance engines and responsive handling. The Ghibli is available with a variety of engine and transmission options, allowing buyers to tailor the car to their specific needs.

However, despite its impressive performance and features, the Ghibli has been plagued by a number of reliability issues. These issues range from minor annoyances to more serious problems that can affect the safety and performance of the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **3.0L V6 Twin-Turbo**
    * **Blown Turbochargers**
        * Description/Symptoms: Excessive smoke, loss of power, loud noises from the engine
        * Solution: Replace the turbochargers
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Intake Manifold Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idling, stalling
        * Solution: Replace the intake manifold
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **3.8L V8 Twin-Turbo**
    * **Valve Train Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine knocking, reduced power, poor fuel economy
        * Solution: Replace the camshaft and lifters
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $3,500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Fuel Injector Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, engine misfires, stalling
        * Solution: Replace the fuel injectors
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
        * Description/Symptoms: Leaking fluid, slipping gears, difficulty shifting
        * Solution: Replace the transmission fluid and seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Gear Shift Issues**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, gears slipping out of place
        * Solution: Replace the gear shift lever or transmission control module
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the power drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Infotainment System Malfunctions**
        * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, blackouts, various software glitches
        * Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Top may not open or close properly, leaks when closed
    * Solution: Repair or replace the convertible top mechanism
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Rattle**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or vibration from the sunroof when it is open or closed
    * Solution: Adjust or replace the sunroof guide rails
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Buzzing or rattling noises from the rear seat area
    * Solution: Inspect and repair the loose or damaged components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Maserati Ghibli has a number of potential reliability issues that can affect its performance, safety, and comfort. While some of these issues are minor annoyances, others can be more serious and costly to repair. Owners should be aware of these issues and consider the potential costs of repairs before purchasing a Ghibli.
