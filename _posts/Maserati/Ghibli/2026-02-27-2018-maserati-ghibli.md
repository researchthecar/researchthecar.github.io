# 2018 Maserati Ghibli Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2018 Maserati Ghibli delivers Italian style and exhilarating twin-turbo performance, making it an enticing option for used-car buyers looking for an exotic badge on a budget. However, like many high-end European luxury sedans, prospective owners must carefully weigh maintenance costs and potential mechanical complexities before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Base / S)

This Ferrari-built 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces roughly 345 hp in base form and 424 hp in the S trim, offering strong acceleration and a stirring exhaust note. Routine maintenance is critical, and buyers should expect higher-than-average servicing costs for this high-performance Italian engine.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic

Managed by a smooth-shifting 8-speed ZF torque-converter automatic transmission, power is sent reliably to the rear wheels or intelligent Q4 all-wheel-drive system. This gearbox is generally robust and widely used across the luxury sector, offering crisp gear changes and solid long-term reliability.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes and Wheels

High-performance braking systems provide strong stopping power but require attentive maintenance and inspection of both pads and rotors.

**Brake and Tire Interaction**
- **Symptoms:** An owner reported unusual vehicle behavior while driving over 40 mph and depressing the brake pedal, noting concerns involving high-performance tire and brake setups.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the braking system components, brake pads, rotors, and tire wear patterns to ensure proper alignment and stopping performance.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V347000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the welds fracture, it can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the sub frame for incomplete welds, replacing the sub frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V834000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Ghibli&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak within the engine compartment increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will install a new fuel delivery line kit, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Maserati Ghibli offers a compelling blend of luxury, prestige, and performance for the used market. While available owner feedback highlights minor system checks, prospective buyers should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a specialist and budget accordingly for premium upkeep.
