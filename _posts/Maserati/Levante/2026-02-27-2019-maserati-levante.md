# 2019 Maserati Levante Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a luxury performance SUV like the 2019 Maserati Levante brings undeniable Italian styling and stirring exhaust notes, but prospective buyers should approach with eyes open regarding potential electronic stability. While owner complaints for this model year are relatively few in number, the reports highlight frustrating electrical glitches that can cascade into powertrain and control system disruptions. Ensuring a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is strongly advised.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6, sourced from Ferrari, produces between 345 and 424 horsepower depending on the trim level and delivers brisk acceleration. It provides a thrilling driving experience, though complex engine management systems require diligent maintenance.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the vehicle unexpectedly shuts off while stopped or moving, accompanied by rolling backwards on hills or a sudden loss of drive power.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis by a qualified technician is required to check for underlying electrical issues, software calibration faults, or fuel delivery issues.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8

The high-performance 3.8-liter twin-turbo V8 churns out 550 or 590 horsepower in GTS and Trofeo trims, offering blistering straight-line speed. Buyers should expect high running costs and complex mechanical upkeep.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission shifts smoothly and quickly, matching the sporty character of the Levante across all driving modes. It is generally regarded as a robust unit, though proper fluid maintenance is essential.

## Steering

### Steering Gear and Assist

The electronic power steering system provides responsive handling, though faults in related electrical modules can compromise steering assist.

**Loss of Power Steering Assist**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have noted severe temporary losses of power steering assist and vehicle control at highway speeds, requiring substantial physical effort to manage the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Have the steering gear assembly, drive components, and associated electronic sensors thoroughly inspected and replaced if mechanical failure is found.

## Electrical

### Instrument Cluster and Infotainment

Complex vehicle architecture can lead to occasional software glitches, sensor communication errors, and intermittent accessory failures.

**Electrical System and Instrument Panel Chaos**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced erratic behavior where the instrument cluster goes haywire, triggering check engine lights, power steering loss, air bag warning lights, and inadvertent parking brake engagement.
- **Solution:** Inspect body control modules, wiring harnesses, and battery health, as software updates or module replacements are frequently needed.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Standard safety equipment includes multiple airbags and active driver aids, though routine checks of occupant restraint hardware are recommended.

**Rear Seat Belt Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rear passengers may find that the seat belt clicks into the buckle mechanism but fails to properly secure and latch.
- **Solution:** Inspect the rear seat belt buckle assembly and replace the faulty latch mechanism.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V521000: Exterior Lighting:Headlights](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Levante&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." Affecting the visibility of oncoming traffic can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will adjust the headlights, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Maserati Levante offers an enticing mix of exotic pedigree and everyday SUV utility, but prospective buyers must weigh these attributes against potential electrical and control system quirks reported by some owners. While mechanical drivetrains are generally stout, diagnostic complexities mean repairs can be expensive. Prioritize well-documented service histories and professional inspections before finalizing a purchase.
