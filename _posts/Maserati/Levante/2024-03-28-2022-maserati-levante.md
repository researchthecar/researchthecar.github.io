**Introduction:**

The 2022 Maserati Levante is a luxury SUV that offers an exhilarating driving experience with its powerful engines and refined interior. The Levante is available with two engine options: a 3.0L V6 twin-turbocharged engine and a 3.8L V8 twin-turbocharged engine. Transmission options include an 8-speed ZF automatic transmission. Critics praise the Levante's stylish design, comfortable cabin, and impressive performance. However, it has received some criticism for its high price and occasional reliability issues.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Twin-Turbocharged Engine**

* **Fuel Pump Failure:** The fuel pump may malfunction, causing the engine to stall or fail to start. Replacing the fuel pump is the solution, with an estimated cost of $500-$800+
* **Turbocharger Boost Leak:** A boost leak can occur in the turbocharger system, resulting in decreased engine performance. Replacing the affected components, such as hoses or clamps, is the solution. Estimated cost: $300-$600+
* **Timing Chain Issues:** The timing chain may prematurely stretch or wear, leading to engine performance issues and potential catastrophic failure. Timing chain replacement is required, costing an estimated $1500-$2500+

**3.8L V8 Twin-Turbocharged Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** The transmission may experience leaks from seals or gaskets, resulting in fluid loss and potential transmission damage. Replacing or repairing the leaking component is the solution, with an estimated cost of $200-$600+
* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may exhibit harsh or delayed shifts, slipping gears, or refusing to shift altogether. Transmission repairs or replacements may be necessary, costing an estimated $1500-$3000+

**Suspension**

* **Front Shock Absorber Failure:** The front shock absorbers may prematurely fail, causing a bouncy or rough ride. Replacing the shock absorbers is the solution, with an estimated cost of $400-$800+ per side
* **Rear Air Suspension Malfunction:** The rear air suspension system may malfunction, leading to uneven ride height, instability, or decreased comfort. Repairing or replacing the air suspension components is required, costing an estimated $1000-$2000+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely due to a faulty component or parasitic draw. Diagnostic testing is necessary to identify the cause and implement the appropriate repair or replacement, which may cost an estimated $200-$500+
* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, freezes, or connectivity problems. Software updates or hardware replacements may be needed, costing an estimated $500-$1200+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing inconsistent temperature settings or failure to blow air. Repairing or replacing the affected components, such as the blower motor or temperature sensors, is the solution, with an estimated cost of $200-$600+
* **Navigation System Updates:** The navigation system may require frequent updates to remain current. Updating the system costs an estimated $100-$300+, depending on the method used

**Summary:**

The 2022 Maserati Levante is a performance-oriented SUV with some reported issues that may affect its reliability and ownership experience. While some issues can be resolved with minor repairs, others may require significant costs to address. Potential buyers should be aware of these potential problems and factor them into their decision-making process.
