**2018 Maserati Levante**

The 2018 Maserati Levante is a mid-size luxury SUV that offers a range of engine and transmission options. The base model features a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine producing 345 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. A more powerful 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 590 horsepower and 538 lb-ft of torque is also available, mated to a 10-speed automatic transmission.

The Levante has received praise for its sleek design, powerful engines, and luxurious interior. However, it has also been criticized for its high price, poor fuel economy, and occasional reliability issues.

### Engine

**3.0-liter Twin-Turbocharged V6**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine rattling noise, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Oil Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine oil in the turbo piping
    * Solution: Inspect turbocharger for leaks, replace seals or turbo as needed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.8-liter Twin-Turbocharged V8**

* **Valve Spring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, reduced power, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace valve springs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**8-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shift Solenoid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard shifting, no shift, check engine light
    * Solution: Replace shift solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque Converter Shudder:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Vibration during low-speed acceleration
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**10-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Transmission Fluid Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid dripping
    * Solution: Replace transmission pan gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Delayed Shifts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates to shift gears
    * Solution: Update transmission software, replace shift solenoids
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noise from the front suspension, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery goes dead after extended periods of non-use
    * Solution: Test battery and charging system, replace battery if needed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Ignition Coil:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfire, reduced power
    * Solution: Replace ignition coil
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Sunroof Leak:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water leaking into the cabin when sunroof is closed
    * Solution: Inspect sunroof seal, replace or repair as needed
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Infotainment System Crashing:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen goes black, system restarts frequently
    * Solution: Update infotainment software, reset or replace system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: HVAC system not working properly, temperature not regulating
    * Solution: Test HVAC system, replace faulty components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2018 Maserati Levante offers a powerful and luxurious driving experience, but it is not without its reliability issues. Engine problems, transmission issues, and electrical problems are among the most common. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to factor in the potential cost of repairs before purchasing this vehicle.
