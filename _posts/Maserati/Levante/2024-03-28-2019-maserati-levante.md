**Introduction:**

The 2019 Maserati Levante is a luxury SUV that offers a blend of performance and refinement. It is available with a range of powertrain options, including a 3.0L V6 turbocharged engine, a 3.8L twin-turbocharged V8 engine, and a 3.0L V6 diesel engine. The Levante comes standard with an 8-speed automatic transmission, and all-wheel drive is standard on all models.

Owners appreciate the Levante's stylish design, comfortable interior, and powerful performance. However, there have been some issues reported with certain components.

**Engine**

**3.0L V6 Turbocharged**

* **Intake manifold failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold can crack, causing vacuum leaks and reduced engine performance.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque converter shudder**
    * Description/Symptoms: The torque converter can experience shuddering or vibration during acceleration.
    * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front suspension noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive noise from the front suspension, particularly over bumps.
    * Solution: Replacement of worn or damaged components in the suspension system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random electrical problems, such as power window malfunctions, instrument panel failures, or battery drain.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of specific electrical components as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment system bugs**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze or experience glitches, requiring a reboot.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2019 Maserati Levante offers a thrilling driving experience, it has some potential issues that owners should be aware of. The engine and transmission-related problems can be costly to fix, while the electrical glitches and interior issues may not be as severe but can still be annoying. Overall, the issues with the Levante are comparable to other luxury SUVs, but they should be considered when making a purchase decision.
