# 2021 Maserati Levante Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2021 Maserati Levante combines Italian flair, luxury accommodations, and athletic performance with the utility of an SUV. For used car buyers, navigating Italian luxury ownership means balancing the appeal of its potent engine lineup and refined road manners against higher-than-average maintenance considerations and potential electrical or powertrain quirks common in high-end European vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6 (Base, S)

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 develops roughly 345 to 424 horsepower depending on the tune, offering spirited acceleration and a distinct exhaust note. Overall reliability is generally acceptable with diligent maintenance, though premium service costs apply.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8 (GTS, Trofeo)

A powerful 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing upwards of 550 to 580 horsepower, delivering supercar-rivaling performance in an SUV package. Buyers should expect high running costs and require meticulous maintenance history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with all-wheel drive across all trims, delivering rapid, smooth shifts and responsive manual control via paddle shifters. It is generally robust, though routine fluid inspections are recommended.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V872000: Fuel System, Gasoline:Delivery:Hoses, Lines/Piping, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Levante&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** A fuel leak may cause an engine stall, increasing the risk of a crash. A fuel leak also increases the risk of fire in the presence of an ignition source.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the fuel line sensor housing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V703000: Seat Belts](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Levante&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 208, "Occupant Crash Protection." An unsecured child restraint system can increase the risk of injury during a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect and replace the seat belt assemblies, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Levante&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Maserati Levante offers a thrilling driving experience and distinctive styling that separates it from mainstream luxury crossovers. Prospective buyers should prioritize models with comprehensive service records and budget accordingly for premium maintenance and upkeep.
