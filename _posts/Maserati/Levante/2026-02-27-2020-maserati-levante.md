# 2020 Maserati Levante Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Maserati Levante offers an alluring blend of Italian styling and spirited performance for luxury SUV buyers. However, shopping for a used luxury vehicle requires careful attention to potential electrical and powertrain issues reported by owners. While complaints are relatively scarce, prospective buyers should thoroughly inspect vehicle history and electrical functionality before purchasing.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

This 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 delivers robust performance and distinctive exhaust notes. Buyers should verify regular maintenance history and monitor for any recurring electronic warnings.

**Unexpected Engine Shutdown**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly shut down while driving in traffic and trigger the check engine light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the electrical or fuel system faults causing the stall.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8

The high-output 3.8-liter twin-turbo V8 provides exhilarating acceleration for GTS and Trofeo trims. Routine maintenance and careful inspection are recommended for these complex performance powerplants.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and responsive gear shifts across all driving modes. It generally proves dependable, provided the transmission fluid is maintained according to schedule.

## Electrical

### Main Electrical System

The electrical architecture supports numerous luxury and convenience features but can occasionally experience critical failures reported by vehicle owners.

**Total Electrical Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle can completely lose electrical power while driving on the highway and fail to restart afterwards.
- **Solution:** Inspect the battery, alternator, main wiring harness, and power distribution modules for faults.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Maserati Levante delivers strong performance and distinctive luxury appeal, but used car buyers must approach with cautious optimism. Given the potential severity of reported electrical and engine shutdown issues, a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection by a knowledgeable specialist is strongly recommended.
