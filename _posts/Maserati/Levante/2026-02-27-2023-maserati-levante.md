# 2023 Maserati Levante Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Maserati Levante delivers exotic styling, engaging exhaust notes, and Italian luxury to the midsize luxury SUV segment. Buyers shopping for a used Levante should weigh its strong performance credentials against the high costs of maintenance and ownership typical of Italian exotic brands.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The base 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 produces approximately 345 horsepower and 369 lb-ft of torque, offering brisk acceleration and everyday drivability. It provides a balanced entry point into the Levante lineup while maintaining signature Maserati performance character.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8

The potent 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 churns out upwards of 550 to 580 horsepower depending on the Modena/Trofeo tune, delivering supercar-rivaling straight-line speed. This high-performance engine is complex and requires diligent adherence to maintenance schedules.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission routes power smoothly to the intelligent all-wheel-drive system with quick, responsive shifts. It is widely regarded as a robust and refined unit in modern luxury applications.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V098000: Back Over Prevention:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Levante&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." A rearview camera image that does not display decreases the driver's visibility, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Maserati Levante is an expressive choice for buyers seeking distinctive Italian flair and robust twin-turbo performance in an SUV package. Prospective owners should ensure a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection and budget accordingly for premium maintenance and upkeep.
