The 2020 Maserati Levante is a luxury SUV known for its Italian styling, powerful engine options, and engaging driving dynamics. Available engines include a 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 producing either 345 or 424 horsepower and 369 or 428 lb-ft of torque respectively, as well as a 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 making either 550 or 590 horsepower and 538 or 538 lb-ft of torque. All engines are paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. Owners often praise the Levante for its distinctive exhaust note, comfortable seats, and sporty handling. However, common complaints include concerns about reliability, electrical glitches, and the cost of maintenance.

**Engine**

**3.0L Twin-Turbo V6**

*   **Oil Leaks from Turbocharger Oil Lines**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks observed near the turbochargers, potentially causing smoke and a burning smell. Can lead to low oil levels and potential engine damage if left unaddressed.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the turbocharger oil lines with updated parts that are less prone to leaking.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500+

*   **High Pressure Fuel Pump (HPFP) Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idling, difficulty starting, and potential loss of power. Diagnostic codes related to fuel pressure regulation.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the high-pressure fuel pump and potentially related fuel system components.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $2500+

**3.8L Twin-Turbo V8**

*   **Oil Leaks**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks at various engine locations, including valve cover gaskets, oil pan gasket, and front crankshaft seal. Noticeable oil consumption and visible oil drips.
    *   Solution: Replacing the leaking gaskets and seals with updated or higher-quality parts.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2000+ (depending on location and severity)

**Transmission**

**ZF 8HP75 8-Speed Automatic Transmission (All Engines)**

*   **Harsh Shifting/Jerking**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Abrupt or jerky shifts, particularly between lower gears. Hesitation or delayed response when accelerating.
    *   Solution: Software update of the transmission control module (TCM). If the problem persists, valve body replacement or full transmission replacement might be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 (Software Update) to $3000 - $8000+ (Transmission Replacement).
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

*   **Air Suspension Compressor Failure**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Vehicle sitting lower than normal, uneven ride height, "Suspension Fault" warning light. The compressor fails to inflate the air springs properly.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the air suspension compressor. Inspecting and replacing any leaking air struts or lines is highly recommended.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $3000+

**Electrical**

*   **Infotainment System Freezing/Crashing**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Infotainment screen freezes, unresponsive touch controls, system restarts, and potential loss of functionality (navigation, audio, climate control).
    *   Solution: Software updates to the infotainment system. In some cases, replacement of the infotainment unit might be necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 (Software Update) to $1000 - $3000+ (Unit Replacement)
Recalls: None Found
*   **Sensor Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) sensor failures, causing inaccurate or no tire pressure readings on the dashboard display. Faulty parking sensors, leading to incorrect proximity alerts or complete failure.
    *   Solution: Replacement of the faulty sensors. In some cases, recalibration of the sensor system might be needed.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300 per sensor

**Exterior**

*   **Sunroof Issues**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Sunroof malfunctions, including failure to open or close, unusual noises during operation, and water leaks.
    *   Solution: Cleaning and lubricating the sunroof tracks and mechanism. If the motor or other components are damaged, replacement is necessary.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1000+

**Interior**

*   **Dashboard/Trim Rattles**
    *   Description/Symptoms: Annoying rattles and squeaks emanating from the dashboard, door panels, or other interior trim pieces, especially when driving on rough roads.
    *   Solution: Identifying and securing the loose trim pieces with foam padding or specialized adhesive. Some owners may attempt DIY fixes, while others may require professional assistance.
    *   Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+ (depending on severity and professional labor)

**Summary**

The 2020 Maserati Levante, while offering a luxurious experience, has some common issues to be aware of. Engine oil leaks and high-pressure fuel pump failures are the most prevalent engine-related issues, with costs ranging from $800 to $2500+. Transmission harsh shifting can be addressed through software updates, but may require more significant repairs. Air suspension failures and electrical glitches are common complaints, with repair costs potentially exceeding $3000+. The sunroof and interior trim can also present problems. While some issues may be minor and inexpensive to address, others could lead to costly repairs, impacting the overall ownership experience.
