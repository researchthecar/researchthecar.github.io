# 2018 Maserati Levante Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The Maserati Levante brings Italian luxury and performance to the midsize luxury SUV segment. While used car buyers are often drawn to its distinctive styling and powerful Ferrari-derived engines, ownership requires careful consideration of potential electrical and mechanical quirks reported by some owners.

---

## Engine

### 3.0L Twin-Turbo V6

The 3.0-liter twin-turbocharged V6 engine, sourced from Ferrari, produces between 345 and 424 horsepower depending on the trim level. It offers robust performance, though buyers should budget for premium maintenance costs typical of high-performance Italian powertrains.

### 3.8L Twin-Turbo V8

The powerful 3.8-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine powers the top-tier GTS and Trofeo models, delivering exceptional horsepower and speed. It provides thrilling performance alongside high complexity and maintenance demands.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission delivers crisp, smooth shifts across all driving modes and is paired with an all-wheel-drive system. It is generally regarded as a durable unit in modern luxury applications.

## Suspension

### Front Suspension and Control Arms

The suspension system provides dynamic handling but should be inspected regularly for wear on bushings and control arms.

**Front Lower Control Arm Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience unexpected vehicle handling instability, pulling, or suspension noise, which can impact control at highway speeds.
- **Solution:** Inspect front suspension components, including lower control arms and bushings, for premature wear or looseness.

## Electrical

### Center Console and Infotainment

Electrical complaints are infrequent but can involve complex interior electronics and infotainment stability.

**Console Overheating and Smoke**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported rare instances of smoke or fire originating near the center console and cup holder area, as well as infotainment display overheating and shutting down.
- **Solution:** Have the vehicle's electrical wiring harness and console electronics thoroughly inspected by a qualified technician.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V347000: Structure:Frame And Members](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Maserati&model=Levante&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** If the welds fracture, it can cause a loss of vehicle control, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Maserati will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the sub frame for incomplete welds, replacing the sub frame, as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

When shopping for a pre-owned Maserati Levante, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by a marque specialist is essential. While severe complaints are uncommon, verifying the condition of the electrical systems and front suspension will help ensure a more reliable ownership experience.
