# 2020 Aston Martin Vantage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Aston Martin Vantage delivers exotic performance and striking design, powered by a potent twin-turbocharged V8 engine. As a high-end luxury sports car, prospective buyers should approach used models with an understanding that specialized maintenance and servicing costs will be significantly higher than standard vehicles.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

This Mercedes-AMG-sourced 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces roughly 503 horsepower and 505 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration. It is generally robust when properly maintained, though buyers should verify complete service history.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission delivers rapid shifts and refined cruising capability. It is well-regarded for durability in high-performance applications.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V604000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=Vantage&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** The front seat air bag fasteners may not have been tightened correctly. Loose fasteners can cause the air bag to improperly deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will examine and, if necessary, secure the front seat air bag fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Aston Martin Vantage is an exhilarating exotic sports car built for enthusiasts seeking performance and exclusivity. While major recurring defect patterns are not heavily represented in owner reports, buyers should budget carefully for premium maintenance, specialized labor, and pre-purchase inspections.
