# 2019 Aston Martin Vantage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Aston Martin Vantage delivers exotic performance and striking design, making it an enticing option for used-car buyers seeking a high-end sports car. Because this model year represents the introduction of a major generation powered by an AMG-sourced twin-turbo V8, prospective owners should carefully evaluate both powertrain characteristics and build quality. While individual experiences vary, keeping up with specialized maintenance is essential for a smooth ownership journey.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces approximately 503 horsepower and 505 lb-ft of torque, offering robust acceleration and responsive power delivery. Derived from Mercedes-AMG, it is generally robust but requires meticulous adherence to service intervals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The ZF 8-speed automatic transmission routes power to the rear wheels, providing quick, refined gear changes suited for both daily driving and spirited track use. It is a proven unit in modern luxury sports cars, provided it is maintained according to manufacturer standards.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V806000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=Vantage&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The locking nut fasteners on the passenger air bag may not be tightened properly. In the event of a crash necessitating passenger air bag deployment, the air bag may not deploy properly, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air bag fasteners, tightening them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V604000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=Vantage&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front seat air bag fasteners may not have been tightened correctly. Loose fasteners can cause the air bag to improperly deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will examine and, if necessary, secure the front seat air bag fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Aston Martin Vantage combines exotic styling with modern powertrain engineering, though buyers should budget for specialized maintenance and thorough pre-purchase inspections. Approaching this luxury sports car with a focus on comprehensive service records will help ensure a satisfying and reliable ownership experience.
