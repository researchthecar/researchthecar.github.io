## Introduction to the 2021 Aston Martin Vantage

The 2021 Aston Martin Vantage is a high-performance sports car known for its sleek design, powerful engine, and luxurious interior. It is available with two engine options: a 4.0L twin-turbocharged V8 and a 5.2L naturally aspirated V12. Both engines are mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The V8 engine produces 503 horsepower and 505 lb-ft of torque, while the V12 engine produces 592 horsepower and 505 lb-ft of torque.

Owners of the 2021 Vantage appreciate its impressive acceleration, responsive handling, and stylish design. However, they have also reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**4.0L V8**

* **Oil Leaks:** Oil may leak from the oil pan gasket or valve cover gasket. Symptoms include oil spots under the car and decreased oil levels. Solution: Replace the faulty gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Engine Misfires:** The engine may misfire due to faulty spark plugs or ignition coils. Symptoms include rough idling, hesitation during acceleration, and decreased performance. Solution: Replace the faulty spark plugs or ignition coils. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**5.2L V12**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** The transmission may slip out of gear, especially during hard acceleration or deceleration. Symptoms include loss of power, rough shifting, and a shuddering sensation. Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission. Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000+
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:** Transmission fluid may leak from the transmission pan gasket or lines. Symptoms include fluid spots under the car and decreased fluid levels. Solution: Replace the faulty gasket or lines. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

* **No Issues Found**

**Exterior**

* **Faulty Headlights:** The headlights may fail to illuminate properly or may flicker erratically. Symptoms include reduced visibility at night and warning lights on the dashboard. Solution: Replace the faulty headlights. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Interior**

* **Ventilation Fan Noise:** The ventilation fan may produce excessive noise, especially at higher speeds. Symptoms include loud fan sounds from the dashboard. Solution: Replace the faulty fan motor. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary**

Overall, the 2021 Aston Martin Vantage is a high-performance sports car that delivers an exhilarating driving experience. However, owners have reported some issues with the vehicle, particularly with the engine, transmission, and electrical systems. The severity of these issues may vary depending on the specific vehicle and its maintenance history. While some repairs may be relatively minor, others can be costly and time-consuming.
