# 2017 Aston Martin DB11 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Aston Martin DB11 represents a striking evolution of the brand's iconic grand tourer lineage, offering exotic styling combined with high-performance powertrains. As a high-end luxury sports car, prospective buyers should approach the marketplace with an understanding that ownership involves meticulous maintenance, specialized servicing, and careful pre-purchase inspections to identify any lingering electrical or mechanical quirks.

---

## Engine

### 5.2L Twin-Turbocharged V12

This 5.2-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine produces approximately 600 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless acceleration and high-speed cruising capability. It is a complex, high-output powerplant that requires attentive upkeep and premium maintenance to ensure long-term reliability.

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Sourced through partnership, the 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 generates around 503 horsepower and offers a slightly lighter, more nimble driving dynamic. This powerplant is generally robust, though standard luxury-tier maintenance schedules must be strictly observed.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

Paired with both engine options, the 8-speed ZF automatic transmission provides quick, smooth gear shifts suited for both relaxed touring and aggressive driving. It is a widely utilized and dependable unit in modern luxury applications when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V604000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB11&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The front seat air bag fasteners may not have been tightened correctly. Loose fasteners can cause the air bag to improperly deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will examine and, if necessary, secure the front seat air bag fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V411000: Tires:Pressure Monitoring And Regulating Systems](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB11&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 138, "Tire Pressure Monitoring Systems." If the driver is not warned that a tire is under-inflated, continued use may result in tire failure, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will update the TPMS software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V065000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB11&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's frontal air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
