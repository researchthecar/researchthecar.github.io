# 2019 Aston Martin DB11 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Aston Martin DB11 blends breathtaking grand touring style with formidable performance, offering buyers a choice of refined twin-turbocharged powerplants. While exotic grand tourers demand meticulous maintenance and higher-than-average upkeep budgets, shoppers looking at the DB11 will find an exhilarating driving experience paired with sophisticated luxury. As with any high-end British hand-built automobile, prospective owners should prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection from an Aston Martin specialist to ensure all systems are operating at their peak.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The Mercedes-AMG-sourced 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 503 horsepower and 505 lb-ft of torque. It offers responsive power delivery and is generally regarded as a robust powertrain option for daily grand touring.

### 5.2L Twin-Turbocharged V12

Aston Martin's in-house 5.2-liter twin-turbocharged V12 churns out roughly 600 to 630 horsepower depending on the tune. This flagship engine delivers effortless, soaring acceleration suited for high-speed cruising.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The rear-mid-mounted 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission shifts smoothly during relaxed cruising while offering sharp responses in manual paddle-shift mode.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V806000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB11&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The locking nut fasteners on the passenger air bag may not be tightened properly. In the event of a crash necessitating passenger air bag deployment, the air bag may not deploy properly, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air bag fasteners, tightening them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V604000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB11&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** The front seat air bag fasteners may not have been tightened correctly. Loose fasteners can cause the air bag to improperly deploy, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will examine and, if necessary, secure the front seat air bag fasteners, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
