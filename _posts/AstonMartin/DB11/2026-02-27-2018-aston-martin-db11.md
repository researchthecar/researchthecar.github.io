# 2018 Aston Martin DB11 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

Shopping for a used 2018 Aston Martin DB11 offers entry into a world of breathtaking grand touring performance, sublime styling, and exotic presence. Powered by either a roaring twin-turbo V12 or a potent AMG-sourced twin-turbo V8, the DB11 delivers effortless velocity and luxurious comfort for long-distance cruising. Because these are low-volume ultra-luxury hand-built grand tourers, prospective buyers should focus on proper maintenance history, electronic system functionality, and inspecting mechanical components thoroughly before purchase.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 produces approximately 503 horsepower and 513 lb-ft of torque, sourced through a partnership with Mercedes-AMG. It offers brisk acceleration and slightly better weight distribution, serving as a robust and responsive entry point into DB11 ownership.

### 5.2L Twin-Turbo V12

The in-house designed 5.2-liter twin-turbocharged V12 generates roughly 600 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, delivering exceptionally smooth, effortless power. It is an iconic grand touring engine providing supreme refinement and high-speed authority.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed ZF Automatic Transmission

The rear-mounted 8-speed ZF automatic transmission provides quick, seamless gear changes and excellent highway cruising efficiency. It pairs smoothly with both engine options, requiring routine fluid checks to maintain long-term reliability.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V806000: Air Bags](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB11&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The locking nut fasteners on the passenger air bag may not be tightened properly. In the event of a crash necessitating passenger air bag deployment, the air bag may not deploy properly, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the air bag fasteners, tightening them as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V065000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB11&modelYear=2018)**
- **Issue:** The driver's frontal air bag may unexpectedly deploy due to insufficient grounding of the steering components if an electrostatic discharge occurs and the air bag clockspring is broken. If the driver air bag inadvertently deploys, it can increase the risk of injury and a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will add sufficient grounding to the steering components, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Aston Martin DB11 remains a captivating luxury grand tourer blending exotic appeal with everyday usability. While official owner complaint volumes are extremely low given its niche production numbers, buyers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections by marque specialists to verify the condition of complex electronics, powertrain components, and overall maintenance records.
