**Introduction**

The 2017 Aston Martin DB11 is a stunning grand tourer with a potent 5.2-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing 600 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. It is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The DB11 received praise for its sleek design, exhilarating performance, and comfortable interior. However, some owners have reported various issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

**5.2L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:** The intake manifold can crack, leading to reduced engine power and poor fuel efficiency. Solution: Replacing the intake manifold. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+. Recalls: 18V-836
* **Fuel Injector Failure:** Fuel injectors can fail, causing misfires, rough idling, and reduced engine performance. Solution: Replacing the fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+ per injector. Recalls: None Found
* **Turbocharger Wastegate Failure:** The wastegate in the turbochargers can fail, affecting boost pressure regulation. This can lead to decreased performance and potential engine damage. Solution: Replacing the wastegate. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+. Recalls: 18V-836

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

* **Shift Flare:** During gear changes, the transmission may experience a noticeable jerk or delay. Solution: Updating the transmission software. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+. Recalls: None Found
* **Delayed Shifting:** In some cases, the transmission may hesitate or delay shifting gears. Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the transmission control module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+. Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:** The front suspension struts can develop leaks, leading to poor handling and reduced ride comfort. Solution: Replacing the struts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+ per strut. Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Sway Bar Link Failure:** The rear sway bar links can fail, causing clunking noises and unpredictable handling. Solution: Replacing the links. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+ per link. Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Infotainment System Malfunction:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, crashes, or connectivity issues. Solution: Updating the software or replacing the infotainment module. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+. Recalls: 18V-836
* **Electrical Harness Faults:** Faulty wiring or connections in the electrical harness can lead to various electrical issues, such as flickering lights, battery drain, or malfunctioning sensors. Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the faulty wiring. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+. Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Body Panel Fitment Issues:** Some owners have reported misaligned or uneven body panels, affecting the vehicle's appearance. Solution: Adjusting or replacing the affected panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+. Recalls: None Found
* **Convertible Top Leaks:** Convertible models may experience leaks around the convertible top when it rains. Solution: Replacing the seals or adjusting the top's alignment. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+. Recalls: 18V-836

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Rattle:** Rattles or squeaks may be heard from the dashboard or other interior components. Solution: Identifying and tightening or replacing the loose parts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+. Recalls: None Found
* **HVAC Malfunction:** The heating, ventilation, or air conditioning system may not function properly, resulting in temperature issues or poor airflow. Solution: Diagnosing and repairing the faulty component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+. Recalls: 18V-836

**Summary**

While the Aston Martin DB11 is a highly capable and desirable grand tourer, it has experienced various issues affecting its engine, transmission, suspension, electrical system, exterior, and interior components. Some of these issues can be costly to repair, especially those related to the engine and transmission. However, the severity and frequency of these issues vary, and not all vehicles are affected. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and consider obtaining an extended warranty for added peace of mind.
