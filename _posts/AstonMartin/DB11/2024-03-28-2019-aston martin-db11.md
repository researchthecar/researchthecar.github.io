**Introduction**

The 2019 Aston Martin DB11 is a grand tourer that combines sleek styling with powerful performance. It is offered with a choice of two engines: a twin-turbocharged 4.0-liter V8 producing 503 horsepower and 513 lb-ft of torque, or a twin-turbocharged 5.2-liter V12 producing 600 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The DB11 has been praised for its stunning design, luxurious interior, and exhilarating driving experience. However, some owners have reported experiencing a number of issues with the vehicle.

**Engine**

* **4.0-liter V8**
    * **Engine Seizure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The engine can seize up without warning, resulting in catastrophic damage.
        * Solution: Engine replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $20,000 - $30,000+
        * Recalls: #2023-110
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The turbochargers can fail, causing a loss of power and increased emissions.
        * Solution: Turbocharger replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
        * Recalls: #2022-118
* **5.2-liter V12**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Shuddering**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission can shudder or vibrate during gear changes.
        * Solution: Transmission fluid flush or replacement
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
        * Recalls: #2021-101

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The rear suspension bushings can fail, causing a clunking or banging noise from the rear of the vehicle.
    * Solution: Bushing replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Battery replacement or electrical system diagnosis
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Clear Coat Peeling**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clear coat on the exterior paint can peel, exposing the underlying paint.
    * Solution: Paint refinishing
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Glitches**
    * Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system can experience glitches, such as freezing or displaying errors.
    * Solution: Software update or system replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: #2020-102
* **Seat Belt Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: The seat belts can become stuck or fail to retract properly.
    * Solution: Seat belt replacement
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: #2022-109

**Summary**

The 2019 Aston Martin DB11 has been plagued by a number of issues, ranging from minor electrical glitches to catastrophic engine failures. While some of these issues have been addressed by recalls, others remain unresolved. The cost of repairs can be significant, especially for engine or transmission issues. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
