### Summary Verdict
The 2023 Aston Martin DB11 demonstrates strong technical reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components, which are generally robust. The V12 and V8 engines, along with the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, have proven dependable. Minor electrical gremlins, such as occasional infotainment system glitches or intermittent battery drain, are the most commonly cited issues, though these are not pervasive. The suspension, exterior, and interior components generally meet the high standards expected of a luxury grand tourer. Owners should be aware of potential electrical system quirks and ensure regular battery maintenance. Overall, the DB11 offers a reliable ownership experience for a vehicle in its class, provided routine checks and prompt attention to any minor electrical anomalies.
