### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Aston Martin DB11 demonstrates strong technical reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components, which are largely sourced from reputable partners. The V12 and V8 engines, along with the ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, are robust and have a proven track record. Suspension systems are also generally trouble-free. The most commonly reported, albeit minor, issues tend to be related to the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less frequently, intermittent battery drain. These are not indicative of systemic failure but rather typical of complex modern vehicle electronics. Overall, the DB11 offers a high level of reliability for a luxury performance car, with most potential issues being minor and manageable.
