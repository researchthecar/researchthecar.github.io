**Introduction:**
The 2018 Aston Martin DB11 is a grand tourer with a front-mounted engine and rear-wheel drive. It is powered by a twin-turbocharged V8 engine or a twin-turbocharged V12 engine, and is available with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The DB11 is praised for its performance, handling, and luxurious interior, but it has also faced some issues.

**Engine**
- **V8 Engine**
  - **Intake Valve Problems:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and tear on the intake valves can cause a loss of power, reduced fuel economy, and increased emissions.
    - Solution: Replace the affected intake valves.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000
    - Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V156000
- **V12 Engine**
  - **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    - Description/Symptoms: A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause the timing chain to jump or break, resulting in catastrophic engine damage.
    - Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner and/or timing chain.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    - Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 19V471000

**Transmission**
- **8-Speed Automatic Transmission**
  - **Shifting Problems:**
    - Description/Symptoms: The transmission may have difficulty shifting gears, resulting in delayed or rough shifts, or it may get stuck in a particular gear.
    - Solution: Re-flash the transmission control module or replace the transmission.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $4,000
    - Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**
- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**
- **Battery Drain:**
    - Description/Symptoms: The battery may discharge quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    - Solution: Replace the faulty battery or alternator.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found
- **Electrical System Malfunctions:**
    - Description/Symptoms: Various electrical components, such as lights, windows, or infotainment system, may malfunction or fail intermittently.
    - Solution: Diagnose and repair the affected electrical components.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
- **Panel Gaps and Misalignments:**
    - Description/Symptoms: The body panels may have gaps or misalignments that affect the overall appearance of the vehicle.
    - Solution: Adjust the body panels and/or replace damaged panels.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
- **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    - Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may experience occasional glitches, such as freezing, crashing, or displaying incorrect information.
    - Solution: Update the infotainment software or replace the infotainment unit.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**
The 2018 Aston Martin DB11 has faced some issues with its engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. While some issues are minor and can be easily resolved, others can be more severe and costly. It is important for owners to be aware of these potential problems and to schedule regular maintenance and inspections to prevent or address them promptly.
