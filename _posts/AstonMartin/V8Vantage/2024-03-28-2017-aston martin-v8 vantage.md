**Introduction:**

The 2017 Aston Martin V8 Vantage is a high-performance sports car powered by a 4.7-liter V8 engine paired with a six-speed manual or an eight-speed automatic transmission. The V8 produces 430 horsepower and 361 lb-ft of torque. The Vantage is praised for its sleek design, powerful engine, and impressive handling, but it has also been met with criticism for its cramped interior and limited storage space.

**Engine**

**4.7-liter V8**

**Intake Manifold Cracking**
Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold may develop cracks over time, leading to reduced engine performance and a potential vacuum leak. The check engine light may also illuminate.
Solution: Replace the intake manifold.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**Six-Speed Manual**

**Clutch Slippage**
Description/Symptoms: The clutch may begin to slip under heavy acceleration or when climbing hills. This can result in a loss of power and difficulty shifting gears.
Solution: Replace the clutch disc and pressure plate.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Trunk Lid Release Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The trunk lid release button may fail, preventing the trunk from being opened.
Solution: Replace the trunk lid release button or the latch mechanism.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction**
Description/Symptoms: The climate control system may malfunction, resulting in inconsistent heating or cooling. The fan may also stop working.
Solution: Diagnose and replace the faulty component within the system, such as the blower motor or control panel.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Aston Martin V8 Vantage is a high-performance sports car with impressive performance and handling. However, it does have some notable issues, including intake manifold cracking and clutch slippage. The interior is also known to be cramped and have limited storage space. The severity of these issues can vary depending on the individual vehicle and the cost of repairs can be substantial.
