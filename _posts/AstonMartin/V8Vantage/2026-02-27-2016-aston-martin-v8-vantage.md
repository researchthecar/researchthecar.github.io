# 2016 Aston Martin V8 Vantage Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Aston Martin V8 Vantage delivers exotic styling and a thrilling driving experience, backed by hand-built craftsmanship. For prospective used-car buyers, understanding its powertrain options and potential quirks is essential when evaluating these low-volume British sports cars.

---

## Engine

### 4.7L V8

This naturally aspirated 4.7-liter V8 engine produces approximately 420 to 430 horsepower depending on the specific spec, offering robust performance and a distinctive exhaust note. It is generally robust, though routine maintenance and specialized servicing are vital for long-term health.

## Transmission

### 7-Speed Sportshift II Automated Manual

The 7-speed Sportshift II automated manual transmission provides rapid gear changes for a more track-focused feel. Buyers should inspect the clutch wear and overall responsiveness closely during a pre-purchase inspection.

### 6-Speed Manual

The traditional 6-speed manual transmission offers a pure, engaging connection for enthusiast drivers. It is mechanically straightforward, though clutch condition remains a key focal point for used examples.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V846000: Latches/Locks/Linkages](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=V8+Vantage&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The affected vehicles are equipped with interior door unlocking mechanisms that may not function when the vehicle has been locked from the outside. As such, these vehicles fail to conform to Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 206, "Door Locks and Door Retention Components." ## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Aston Martin V8 Vantage remains an alluring and capable luxury sports car option for enthusiasts willing to budget for specialized maintenance. Thoroughly inspecting the mechanical components and service history before purchase will help ensure a rewarding ownership experience.
