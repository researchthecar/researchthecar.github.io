The 2014 Aston Martin V8 Vantage is a luxury sports car that combines breathtaking performance with elegant styling. It is powered by a 4.7-liter V8 engine that generates 436 horsepower and 380 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 7-speed automated manual transmission. The V8 Vantage is praised for its thrilling driving experience, sharp handling, and sophisticated interior. However, like all vehicles, it is not without its issues.

**Engine**

**4.7L V8**

* **Valve Timing Control Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, reduced power, and engine warning light illumination.
    * Solution: Replacement of the variable valve timing (VVT) actuator and associated sensors.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine vacuum leaks, rough idle, and decreased fuel efficiency.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**7-Speed Automated Manual**

* **Shifting Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, gear slippage, or delayed shifts.
    * Solution: Reprogramming of the transmission control module (TCM) or replacement of the transmission fluid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Squeaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise from the front suspension when driving over uneven surfaces.
    * Solution: Replacement of the suspension bushings or ball joints.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Shock Absorber Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking shock absorbers, reduced ride quality, and increased noise.
    * Solution: Replacement of the rear shock absorbers.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, difficulty starting the vehicle, and electrical system malfunctions.
    * Solution: Replacement of the battery or alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Errors**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning infotainment system, touchscreen glitches, and Bluetooth connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the infotainment module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation or moisture buildup inside the headlight assemblies.
    * Solution: Replacement of the headlight assemblies or installation of a moisture barrier.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Rattling**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling noise from the sunroof when closed or opened.
    * Solution: Adjustment or replacement of the sunroof seals or mechanisms.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Button Malfunctions**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning steering wheel buttons, such as cruise control or volume controls.
    * Solution: Replacement of the steering wheel buttons or control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Air Conditioning Vent Failures**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable air conditioning vents, excessive noise, or poor airflow.
    * Solution: Replacement of the vent motors or adjustment of the air conditioning system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

While the 2014 Aston Martin V8 Vantage is a highly capable and desirable sports car, it is not immune to certain issues. The most common problems reported by owners relate to engine timing control, intake manifold gaskets, suspension squeaking, and electrical system issues. The severity and cost of these issues vary, but it is important for potential buyers to be aware of them before making a purchasing decision.
