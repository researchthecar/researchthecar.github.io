**Introduction:**

The 2005 Aston Martin Vanquish is a high-performance sports car powered by a 5.9-liter V12 engine. It offers an exhilarating driving experience with 460 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque. Despite its impressive capabilities, the Vanquish has encountered various issues reported by owners.

**Engine**

**5.9-liter V12**

* **Intake Manifold Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracking of the intake manifold, resulting in vacuum leaks, rough idling, and loss of power.
    * Solution: Replacement of the intake manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **VVT Solenoid Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the variable valve timing (VVT) solenoid, causing engine performance issues, reduced power, and increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Replacement of the VVT solenoid.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automated Manual Transmission (AMT)**

* **Gearbox Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical or mechanical failure of the AMT gearbox, leading to shifting problems, loss of gears, or complete failure.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the gearbox and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $5,000 - $10,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 08V261000

**Suspension**

* **Lower Control Arm Bushings Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Premature wear and failure of the rear lower control arm bushings, causing knocking sounds and poor handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent electrical issues, including battery drain, flickering lights, or failure of electronic components.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the affected electrical components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Clear Coat Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Peeling or fading of the clear coat on the exterior paint, particularly on the hood and roof.
    * Solution: Removal of the old clear coat and reapplication of a new layer.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Failure of the climate control system, resulting in ineffective heating or cooling.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the climate control unit and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2005 Aston Martin Vanquish is a powerful and desirable sports car, but it faces several issues that can be costly to repair. The engine and transmission issues are particularly significant, potentially leading to expensive repairs or even catastrophic failure. The electrical, suspension, exterior, and interior issues are also common but generally less severe and expensive to fix. Overall, potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in potential repair costs when considering purchasing a used Vanquish.
