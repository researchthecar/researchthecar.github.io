**Introduction**

The 2003 Aston Martin Vanquish is a grand tourer that features a naturally aspirated 5.9-liter V12 engine. It produces 460 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque. The Vanquish comes standard with a 6-speed manual transmission. A 6-speed automatic transmission is available as an option. The Vanquish is praised for its stunning design, powerful engine, and luxurious interior. However, it is also known for its high price tag and expensive maintenance costs.

**Engine**

**5.9L V12**

**Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain tensioner can fail, causing the timing chain to skip or break. This can lead to catastrophic engine damage.
Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

**No Issues Found**

**6-Speed Automatic**

**Valve Body Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The valve body can fail, causing the transmission to shift erratically or not shift at all.
Solution: Replace the valve body.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The front lower control arm bushings can fail, causing the front suspension to become loose and unstable.
Solution: Replace the front lower control arm bushings.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery can drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle stranded.
Solution: Replace the battery or find and fix the source of the drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Heated Seat Control Module Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The heated seat control module can fail, causing the heated seats to stop working.
Solution: Replace the heated seat control module.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2003 Aston Martin Vanquish is a high-performance grand tourer that offers a thrilling driving experience. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that can affect the vehicle. The most serious issues include timing chain tensioner failure and transmission valve body failure. These issues can be expensive to fix, so it is important to factor in the cost of maintenance when considering purchasing a Vanquish.
