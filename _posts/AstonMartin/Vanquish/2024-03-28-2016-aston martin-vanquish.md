**Introduction to the 2016 Aston Martin Vanquish**

The 2016 Aston Martin Vanquish is a British-made grand tourer. It is powered by a 5.9-liter V12 engine that produces 568 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Vanquish has a top speed of 190 mph and can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in 3.6 seconds.

The Vanquish is a popular car among enthusiasts, but it is not without its problems. Some of the most common issues with the car include:

**Engine**

**5.9-Liter V12**

* **Valve Stem Seal Failure:** Leaking oil into combustion chamber, causing smoking and poor performance. **Replace valve stem seals.** **$2,000 - $4,000** Recalls: NHTSA ID# 19V485000
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** Rattling noise on startup, check engine lights, loss of power. **Replace timing chain tensioners.** **$1,500 - $2,500**

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**No Issues Found**

**Suspension**

* **Front Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:** Squeaking noise, poor handling. **Replace bushings or control arms.** **$500 - $1,000**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** Battery dies prematurely. **Replace battery, check for parasitic draw.** **$200 - $500**
* **Ignition Switch Failure:** Key won't turn, no power to car. **Replace ignition switch.** **$500 - $1,000** Recalls: NHTSA ID# 15V208000

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Actuator Failure:** Door locks stop working, keyless entry inoperable. **Replace actuator.** **$200 - $400**
* **Window Regulator Failure:** Window gets stuck or falls out of track. **Replace regulator.** **$500 - $1,000**

**Interior**

* **Infotainment System Software Bugs:** Touchscreen glitches, slow response times. **Update software.** **$0 - $200**
* **Ventilation Fan Failure:** Fan stops working, poor air circulation. **Replace fan.** **$300 - $500**

**Summary**

The 2016 Aston Martin Vanquish is a powerful and stylish grand tourer, but it is not without its problems. The car has a number of potential issues, including engine valve stem seal failure and electrical problems. The cost to fix these issues can be significant, so it is important to factor this into the cost of ownership.
