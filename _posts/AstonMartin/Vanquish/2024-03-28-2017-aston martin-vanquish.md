**Introduction:**

The 2017 Aston Martin Vanquish is a high-performance grand tourer known for its sleek design and powerful engine. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 5.9-liter V12 engine that produces 568 horsepower and 465 lb-ft of torque, paired with an 8-speed Touchtronic III automatic transmission. The Vanquish is highly praised for its stunning aesthetics, exhilarating driving experience, and luxurious interior, but it has also been subject to a number of issues.

**Engine**

- **V12**
    - **Oil Leaks:** Several owners have reported oil leaks from the V12 engine, particularly around the valve covers and oil pan. Symptoms include visible oil on the ground or engine compartment, as well as a burning oil smell. The repair typically involves replacing the leaking gaskets or seals.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

- **8-Speed Touchtronic III Automatic:**
    - **Shifting Problems:** Owners have experienced hesitation, slipping, or rough shifting during gear changes. This may be caused by faulty solenoids or other internal components.
    - **Solution:** Replacement of the faulty components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $2,000 - $4,000+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

- **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

- **Battery Drain:** Some Vanquish owners have reported excessive battery drain when the vehicle is parked, leading to a dead battery. The cause can vary, such as faulty modules or electrical accessories.
    - **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of the underlying electrical issue.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

- **Trunk Lid Misalignment:** A number of owners have encountered issues with the alignment of the trunk lid, resulting in gaps or uneven closure.
    - **Solution:** Adjustment or replacement of the trunk lid hinges or latches.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $300 - $800+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

- **Climate Control Malfunction:** Some Vanquish owners have reported problems with the climate control system, including inadequate cooling or heating performance. The issue could be caused by faulty sensors, actuators, or the HVAC module.
    - **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of the affected components.
    - **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000+
    - **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

The 2017 Aston Martin Vanquish is a desirable vehicle, but it is not without its issues. Common problems include oil leaks from the V12 engine, shifting problems with the 8-speed automatic transmission, electrical battery drain, trunk lid misalignment, and climate control malfunctions. While some of these issues are relatively minor, others can be more costly to repair. It is recommended that potential buyers be aware of these potential issues and factor in the potential cost of repairs when considering a Vanquish.
