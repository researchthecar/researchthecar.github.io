**Introduction:**

The 2014 Aston Martin Vanquish is a high-performance grand tourer known for its sleek design, luxurious interior, and powerful engine. It is powered by a naturally aspirated 5.9-liter V12 engine that produces 560 horsepower and 457 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 6-speed Touchtronic 2 automatic transmission. The Vanquish is praised for its agility, handling, and performance, but it has also been criticized for its high fuel consumption and limited practicality.

**Engine**

**5.9L V12 Naturally Aspirated**

* **ECU Mapping Error:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine may run poorly, stall, or experience power loss at high RPM.
    * Solution: Update ECU software.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Intake Valve Deposit Buildup:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, rough idle, or increased fuel consumption.
    * Solution: Clean intake valves and replace affected parts as needed.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Oil Leak from Oil Pan Gasket:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaks from the oil pan, leaving a puddle under the vehicle.
    * Solution: Replace oil pan gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $700
    * Recalls: N/A

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Touchtronic 2 Automatic**

* **Delayed Gear Shifts:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission lags or hesitates when shifting gears.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace solenoids.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Clunking Noise When Shifting:**
    * Description/Symptoms: A noticeable clunking sound occurs during gear shifts.
    * Solution: Replace transmission mount or affected gear.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1500
    * Recalls: N/A

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not in use.
    * Solution: Find and repair the source of the power drain (e.g., parasitic battery drain).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Electrical Short in Cabin:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical components in the cabin, such as the infotainment system or lighting, may malfunction or stop working.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair the electrical short.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800+
    * Recalls: N/A

**Exterior**

* **Clear Bra Peeling:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The clear protective film on the exterior paint may peel or bubble.
    * Solution: Replace the clear bra.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
    * Recalls: N/A

**Interior**

* **Headliner Sagging:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The headliner fabric may sag or detach from the roof.
    * Solution: Replace the headliner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1200
    * Recalls: N/A
* **Trim Panel Rattle:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Plastic trim panels in the interior may rattle or vibrate while driving.
    * Solution: Secure or replace loose trim panels.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: N/A

**Summary:**

The 2014 Aston Martin Vanquish is a high-performance vehicle with impressive handling and performance. However, it does have some issues, including engine mapping errors, intake valve deposits, and electrical glitches. The severity of these issues varies, and the cost to fix them can be significant. Overall, while the Vanquish is an exciting and desirable car, potential owners should be aware of its potential issues and associated costs before making a purchase decision.
