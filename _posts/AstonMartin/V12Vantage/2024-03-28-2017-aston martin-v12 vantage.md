**Introduction**

The 2017 Aston Martin V12 Vantage is a high-performance sports car powered by a 6.0-liter V12 engine. It is offered with a 6-speed manual or 7-speed automated manual transmission. The V12 Vantage is praised for its thrilling engine, sharp handling, and sleek design. However, it also has some drawbacks, including limited rear-seat space and a high price tag.

**Engine**

* **V12**

  * **Valve Train Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive valve noise, loss of power, and poor fuel economy.
    * Solution: Replace faulty valves or timing chain.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $4,000 - $6,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
  * **Oil Leak from Rear Main Seal**
    * Description/Symptoms: Oil leaking from the rear of the engine.
    * Solution: Replace rear main seal.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-Speed Manual**

  * **Grinding Noises When Shifting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears and grinding noises.
    * Solution: Replace worn transmission components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **7-Speed Automated Manual**

  * **Shifting Delays and Hesitations**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission hesitates or delays when shifting.
    * Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Strut Mounts**
  * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises while driving over bumps.
  * Solution: Replace worn strut mounts.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Electrical System Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard warning lights illuminate, flickering lights, or complete electrical failure.
  * Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty electrical components.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation**
  * Description/Symptoms: Water or condensation inside the headlight housing.
  * Solution: Replace headlight housing or seal.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Malfunctions**
  * Description/Symptoms: GPS not working, screen freezing or glitching.
  * Solution: Update navigation software or replace unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Air Conditioning Failure**
  * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning not blowing cold air.
  * Solution: Recharge refrigerant or replace AC compressor.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

Overall, the 2017 Aston Martin V12 Vantage is a high-performance sports car that offers thrilling driving experiences. However, it is not without its issues. While most of the reported problems are relatively minor and can be resolved with routine maintenance, some can be more costly to fix. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific problem and the affected component.
