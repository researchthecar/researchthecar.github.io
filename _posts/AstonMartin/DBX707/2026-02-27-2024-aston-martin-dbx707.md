# 2024 Aston Martin DBX707 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Aston Martin DBX707 is an ultra-luxury high-performance SUV offering breathtaking power and dynamic capability. As a relatively new and exotic performance platform, buyers should carefully evaluate powertrain health, electronics, and overall vehicle condition during a pre-purchase inspection.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine delivers an impressive 697 horsepower and 663 lb-ft of torque. Sourced via AMG partnership, it provides explosive acceleration, though ongoing maintenance requires diligent professional servicing.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Wet Clutch Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission utilizes a wet clutch setup tailored for rapid shifts and high torque handling across all-wheel-drive configurations. It generally operates smoothly under heavy loads when properly maintained.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V712000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pipes, Hoses, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX707&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of oil and oil pressure could lead to the engine stalling or seizing, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the hoses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V200000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX707&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the rear lower suspension arm can cause a loss of vehicle control, damage the brake line or other suspension components, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will inspect the lower rear suspension arms for cracks, and as necessary, replace the lower rear arms, and replace the bolts for the torque reaction links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Aston Martin DBX707 is a powerhouse luxury SUV built for extreme performance. While widespread complaint patterns are minimal, prospective buyers are strongly advised to secure a comprehensive inspection by an exotic car specialist before purchase.
