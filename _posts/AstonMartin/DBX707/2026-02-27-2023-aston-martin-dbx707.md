# 2023 Aston Martin DBX707 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Aston Martin DBX707 is an ultra-luxury high-performance SUV offering breathtaking power and handcrafted elegance. As an exotic vehicle produced in limited numbers, prospective used-car buyers should weigh its immense capabilities against specialized maintenance requirements and limited real-world complaint history.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine, sourced from Mercedes-AMG, produces 697 horsepower and 663 lb-ft of torque. It delivers explosive acceleration and robust performance, though buyers should expect high running costs typical of hand-built exotic powertrains.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Wet-Clutch Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed wet-clutch automatic transmission manages the massive torque output smoothly while offering rapid gear changes. It provides refined cruising behavior alongside aggressive shifts in dynamic driving modes.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V200000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX707&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the rear lower suspension arm can cause a loss of vehicle control, damage the brake line or other suspension components, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will inspect the lower rear suspension arms for cracks, and as necessary, replace the lower rear arms, and replace the bolts for the torque reaction links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Aston Martin DBX707 remains a rare and exceptionally fast luxury SUV for buyers seeking exclusivity and supercar performance. Because owner complaint data is sparse and specialized servicing requires factory-trained technicians, a thorough pre-purchase inspection by an Aston Martin specialist is strongly recommended.
