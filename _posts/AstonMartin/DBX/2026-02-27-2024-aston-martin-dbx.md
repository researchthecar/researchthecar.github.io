# 2024 Aston Martin DBX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Aston Martin DBX is a high-performance luxury SUV offering exceptional power and prestige. While official owner complaint volume is very low, buyers should carefully evaluate individual vehicle history and ensure comprehensive pre-purchase inspections.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine produces robust horsepower and strong torque, delivering exhilarating performance. It is a sophisticated powertrain shared with high-end performance applications, requiring diligent maintenance for long-term reliability.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid gear changes suited to the SUV's dynamic capabilities. It pairs seamlessly with the all-wheel-drive system for refined power delivery.

## Exterior

### Underbody Shield

Underbody shielding helps protect sensitive mechanical components from road debris but may be susceptible to snagging on low obstacles.

**Underbody Panel Damage**
- **Symptoms:** An underbody protective panel can catch on road debris, resulting in a tear and exposing underlying wiring and components.
- **Solution:** Inspect the underbody panel for secure fastening and replace damaged shielding if torn.
- **Cost:** Out-of-pocket dealer replacement cost

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V249000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Hoses/Lines/Piping/Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of oil and oil pressure can result in the engine stalling or seizing, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the hoses for the oil cooler, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V255000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall or loss of headlights and/or power steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the fuses and cables for damage and replace them as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V200000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the rear lower suspension arm can cause a loss of vehicle control, damage the brake line or other suspension components, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will inspect the lower rear suspension arms for cracks, and as necessary, replace the lower rear arms, and replace the bolts for the torque reaction links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Aston Martin DBX is a rare and capable luxury performance SUV with minimal reported owner complaints. Buyers should prioritize a thorough mechanical and structural inspection to verify underbody condition and overall upkeep.
