# 2022 Aston Martin DBX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Aston Martin DBX introduces luxury buyers to the brand's first-ever SUV, combining exotic performance with everyday utility. Powered by potent Mercedes-AMG-derived engines paired with smooth automatic transmissions, it delivers the driving dynamics expected of an ultra-luxury marque. Prospective used-car buyers will find a sophisticated vehicle that requires careful independent pre-purchase inspection due to complex electronics and high maintenance overhead.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

This hand-built 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 produces approximately 542 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque, offering blistering acceleration and a signature exhaust note. It is generally robust, though standard high-performance maintenance and fluid checks are critical for longevity.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission delivers quick, seamless shifts suited for both relaxed cruising and aggressive driving. It operates reliably when maintained with regular fluid services.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V255000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall or loss of headlights and/or power steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the fuses and cables for damage and replace them as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V200000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the rear lower suspension arm can cause a loss of vehicle control, damage the brake line or other suspension components, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will inspect the lower rear suspension arms for cracks, and as necessary, replace the lower rear arms, and replace the bolts for the torque reaction links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Aston Martin DBX remains a striking and capable ultra-luxury SUV for buyers seeking exclusivity and performance. Because formal owner complaint data is sparse, shoppers should prioritize a comprehensive mechanical and electronic inspection by a qualified specialist before purchasing.
