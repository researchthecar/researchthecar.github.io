# 2023 Aston Martin DBX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Aston Martin DBX brings high-performance luxury and exotic styling to the ultra-luxury SUV segment, powered by potent AMG-sourced engines. As an exotic low-volume vehicle, finding comprehensive long-term reliability trends can be challenging, though buyers should anticipate high maintenance and specialized servicing costs. When shopping for a pre-owned DBX, a thorough inspection by an experienced marque specialist is essential to ensure proper mechanical and electronic function.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

The 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8, supplied by Mercedes-AMG, produces approximately 542 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque in standard tune. This powerplant delivers brisk acceleration paired with a distinctive exhaust note, though routine maintenance requires specialized care and premium fluids.

### 3.0L Turbocharged Inline-6 Mild Hybrid

The 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-6 mild hybrid engine is paired with a 48-volt electrical system, offering smooth power delivery and enhanced efficiency for the DBX lineup. It represents an accessible entry point to the model range while maintaining refined luxury performance.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Torque Converter Automatic

The 9-speed automatic transmission provides quick, seamless shifts and manages high torque loads effectively across all driving modes. It contributes to both responsive performance during spirited driving and relaxed cruising on the highway.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V249000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Cooling System:Hoses/Lines/Piping/Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of oil and oil pressure can result in the engine stalling or seizing, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the hoses for the oil cooler, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V255000: Electrical System:Wiring:Fuses And Circuit Breakers](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** An engine stall or loss of headlights and/or power steering can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will inspect the fuses and cables for damage and replace them as necessary. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 26V200000: Suspension:Rear](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DBX&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** Failure of the rear lower suspension arm can cause a loss of vehicle control, damage the brake line or other suspension components, and increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will inspect the lower rear suspension arms for cracks, and as necessary, replace the lower rear arms, and replace the bolts for the torque reaction links, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.
