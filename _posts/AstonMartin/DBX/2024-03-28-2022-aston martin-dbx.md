**Introduction to the 2022 Aston Martin DBX**

The 2022 Aston Martin DBX is a high-performance luxury SUV that combines the brand's signature style with exceptional power and handling. Offered with a single engine and transmission option, the DBX delivers an exhilarating driving experience.

**Engine**

* **4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8** - 550 horsepower, 516 lb-ft of torque

**Transmission**

* **9-speed Automatic**

**Engine**

* **4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8**

**Engine Misfiring**
* Description/Symptoms: Engine experiencing rough idling, hesitation, and loss of power.
* Solution: Replace faulty ignition coils or spark plugs.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Fuel Pump Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Vehicle stalling or difficulty starting.
* Solution: Replace the fuel pump.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200+

**Transmission**

* **9-speed Automatic**

**Harsh Shifting**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or jerking during gear changes.
* Solution: Update transmission software or replace transmission components.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+

**Slippage**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping out of gear while driving.
* Solution: Replace worn clutch packs or torque converter.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000+

**Suspension**

* **Adaptive Air Suspension**

**Air Bag Leak**
* Description/Symptoms: Suspension losing air pressure, causing the vehicle to ride low.
* Solution: Replace faulty air bag or suspension component.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**Strut Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Vehicle bouncing excessively or making loud noises over bumps.
* Solution: Replace worn struts.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery draining rapidly when the vehicle is parked.
* Solution: Replace faulty electrical components or update software.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing or becoming unresponsive.
* Solution: Update software or replace infotainment system.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Loose Headliner**
* Description/Symptoms: Headliner material becoming loose or falling down.
* Solution: Reattach or replace headliner.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+

**Door Lock Malfunction**
* Description/Symptoms: Door locks failing to engage or disengage.
* Solution: Replace door lock actuators or wiring.
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+

**Safety Features**

* **None Found**

**Summary**

The 2022 Aston Martin DBX is a highly capable luxury SUV with exceptional performance and refinement. However, it is not without its issues. Engine misfiring, fuel pump failure, and suspension problems are among the most commonly reported complaints. While these issues can be costly to fix, they are generally not considered severe enough to detract significantly from the overall driving experience.
