# 2017 Aston Martin Rapide

### Overview
The 2017 Aston Martin Rapide S represents a pinnacle of luxury grand touring, blending potent performance with exquisite craftsmanship. As a high-performance vehicle, its reliability is generally strong, reflecting meticulous engineering and premium componentry. However, like any complex machine, certain areas can experience issues over time, particularly those related to its advanced powertrain and sophisticated electronic systems. This analysis focuses on documented technical reliability concerns, excluding routine maintenance and expected wear and tear, to provide a clear picture for owners and prospective buyers.

---


## Engine

### 5.9L V12
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500


## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic (ZF 8HP70)
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or cooler lines, shifting problems, slipping gears.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals, lines, or transmission pan gasket.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1000

**Software Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Erratic shifting patterns, delayed engagement, harsh shifts.
- **Solution:** Software update or recalibration of the transmission control module.
- **Cost:** $150 - $300


## Suspension

### Adaptive Dampers
**Electronic Damper Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride quality, clunking noises over bumps, suspension warning light.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty adaptive damper units.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing/Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive or freezes, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software reset or update; potential replacement of head unit.
- **Cost:** $200 - $1500


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, difficulty starting the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify the source of the drain (e.g., faulty module, accessory) and repair.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800


## Exterior

### Door Handles
**Pop-out Door Handle Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Door handles fail to retract or extend properly, difficulty opening doors.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the door handle mechanism.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Interior

### Climate Control System
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, inconsistent fan speed, unusual noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2017 Aston Martin Rapide S generally exhibits good reliability for a vehicle of its caliber and complexity. The V12 engine and ZF automatic transmission are robust, though specific issues like ignition coil failures and transmission fluid leaks can occur. Owners should be aware of potential electronic gremlins within the infotainment and adaptive suspension systems, which can be costly to repair. Exterior and interior components, such as door handles and climate control, can also present isolated issues. Proactive maintenance and prompt attention to warning signs are crucial for maintaining the Rapide S's performance and longevity.
