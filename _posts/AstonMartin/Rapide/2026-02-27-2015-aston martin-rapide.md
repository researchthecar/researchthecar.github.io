# 2015 Aston Martin Rapide

### Overview
The 2015 Aston Martin Rapide, a luxurious four-door grand tourer, generally presents a strong reliability record for its segment. While exotic vehicles inherently require more specialized and potentially costly maintenance than mainstream cars, the Rapide's core mechanicals have proven robust. Owners typically report satisfaction with its performance and build quality. However, as with any high-performance luxury vehicle, potential buyers should be aware of the specialized nature of repairs and the associated costs, particularly concerning its potent V12 engine and sophisticated electronic systems. Regular, diligent maintenance by qualified technicians is paramount to ensuring long-term dependability and enjoying the Rapide's exceptional driving experience.

---


## Engine

### 5.9L V12
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light illumination, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per coil

**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and repair/replacement of leaking hoses, gaskets, or radiator.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Timing Chain Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, particularly on startup; potential for reduced performance.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $2500 - $5000+


## Transmission

### Touchtronic II 6-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from the transmission pan or lines, potential for slipping gears, harsh shifting.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals, gaskets, or lines.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering during acceleration, slipping, or delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000+


## Suspension

### Adaptive Damping System
**Shock Absorber Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, excessive bouncing, clunking noises over bumps, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty adaptive shock absorbers.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500 per corner

**Control Arm Bushing Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or rattling noises, imprecise steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn control arm bushings or complete control arms.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000 per axle


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, displays errors, or freezes.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500+


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, car struggles to start, electrical components malfunction.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $1000+

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Aston Martin Rapide is a highly desirable luxury sedan with a generally good reliability record for its class. The primary areas of concern, as expected for a V12-powered exotic, revolve around potential engine and transmission issues, particularly related to fluid leaks and component wear over time. Electrical gremlins, especially within the infotainment system, can also arise. While routine maintenance is essential and can be costly, major mechanical failures are not overwhelmingly common. Owners should prioritize sourcing specialized technicians for repairs and factor in the higher cost of parts and labor associated with maintaining a vehicle of this caliber. Overall, with proper care, the Rapide offers a reliable and exhilarating ownership experience.
