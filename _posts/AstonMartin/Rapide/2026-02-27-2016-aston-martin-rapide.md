# 2016 Aston Martin Rapide Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Aston Martin Rapide is a high-performance luxury four-door sports sedan blending striking British design with a hand-built V12 heart. While low-volume exotic cars like the Rapide do not yield massive complaint databases compared to mainstream vehicles, buyers should approach ownership with an understanding of specialized maintenance costs, complex electronics, and tight packaging typical of front-engine, rear-wheel-drive grand tourers.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L V12

The Rapide features a naturally aspirated 6.0-liter V12 engine producing roughly 550 horsepower and 457 lb-ft of torque. It delivers exhilarating performance and a signature exhaust note, though routine servicing requires specialist care and premium fluids.

## Transmission

### Touchtronic III 8-speed Automatic

Equipped with an advanced ZF 8-speed automatic transmission, the Rapide offers lightning-fast gear changes and smooth highway cruising. This gearbox is generally robust, but software calibrations and fluid condition should be verified by a qualified technician during pre-purchase inspections.

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Aston Martin Rapide remains an exotic yet usable daily-capable grand tourer for buyers who prioritize prestige, hand-crafted interior appointments, and V12 performance. Because official owner complaint data and large-scale reliability metrics are sparse for ultra-luxury low-volume models, a rigorous pre-purchase inspection by an authorized Aston Martin specialist is essential to verify mechanical health and avoid unexpected luxury repair bills.
