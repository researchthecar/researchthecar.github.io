**Introduction:**

The 2020 Aston Martin DBS is a handcrafted grand tourer with a powerful V12 engine. It boasts 715 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. Despite its impressive performance, the DBS has received some negative reviews regarding reliability.

**Engine**

**5.2L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

* **Leaking oil pump seal:** Oil leaks from the engine, causing a drop in oil level and potential engine damage. **Solution:** Replace the oil pump seal. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,000
* **Intake manifold gasket failure:** Vacuum leaks occur due to a faulty gasket. Symptoms include rough idling and decreased engine performance. **Solution:** Replace the intake manifold gasket. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500
* **Turbocharger wastegate actuator failure:** Turbocharger boost pressure is not regulated properly, resulting in hesitation and reduced power. **Solution:** Replace the wastegate actuator. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,000

**Transmission**

**8-Speed ZF Automatic**

* **Harsh shifting:** Transmission shifts roughly during acceleration or deceleration. **Solution:** Update transmission software or replace mechatronics unit. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000
* **Delayed gear engagement:** Transmission hesitates to engage gears, especially from a standstill. **Solution:** Inspect and clean transmission fluid filter. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:** Electrical system fails due to a faulty alternator. Symptoms include dim headlights, battery drain, or no start. **Solution:** Replace the alternator. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,000 - $1,500
* **Infotainment system glitches:** Touchscreen display freezes, crashes, or becomes unresponsive. **Solution:** Software update or hardware replacement. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
* **Exterior lighting failures:** Headlights, taillights, or turn signals malfunction due to faulty bulbs or wiring. **Solution:** Replace bulbs or repair wiring. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500

**Exterior**

* **Panel gap irregularities:** Uneven gaps between body panels. **Solution:** Realign or replace body panels. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000
* **Paint defects:** Minor paint imperfections or chipping. **Solution:** Touch-up or respray affected areas. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500

**Interior**

* **Climate control malfunction:** Air conditioning or heating system does not function properly. **Solution:** Inspect and repair or replace HVAC components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $2,000
* **Trims and buttons may rattle or become loose:** Trims or buttons may become loose or rattle while driving. **Solution:** Tighten or replace affected components. **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $100 - $500

**Summary:**

While the 2020 Aston Martin DBS offers impressive performance, it has faced some reliability issues. Engine problems, such as oil leaks and intake manifold gasket failures, are the most concerning. Transmission issues, electrical glitches, and exterior defects have also been reported. The cost of repairs can be significant, ranging from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars.
