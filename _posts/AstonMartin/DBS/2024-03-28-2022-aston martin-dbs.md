**Introduction:**

The 2022 Aston Martin DBS is a high-performance grand tourer that embodies the brand's legacy of excellence. Equipped with a potent 5.2L twin-turbocharged V12 engine, it delivers an exhilarating driving experience with 725 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. The DBS is available with either an 8-speed automatic transmission or a 6-speed manual transmission.

Owners of the DBS appreciate its sleek design, luxurious interior, and thrilling performance. However, like any vehicle, it is not without its issues. Here is a comprehensive list of problems reported by owners:

**Engine**

**5.2L Twin-Turbocharged V12**

**Turbocharger Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, excessive smoke, rattling or hissing noises.
Solution: Replace faulty turbocharger.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $6,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Fuel Injector Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: Rough idle, hesitation during acceleration, increased fuel consumption.
Solution: Replace or clean clogged fuel injectors.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**8-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slippage:**
Description/Symptoms: Loss of power during acceleration, difficulty shifting gears.
Solution: Repair or replace faulty transmission components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery depletes quickly when parked, even with all accessories turned off.
Solution: Identify and repair electrical circuit causing the drain.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Window Seal Leakage:**
Description/Symptoms: Water or wind noise seeping into the cabin through window seals.
Solution: Replace or adjust window seals.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Climate Control Malfunction:**
Description/Symptoms: AC or heater not functioning properly, temperature not regulated correctly.
Solution: Diagnose and repair faulty climate control components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
Recalls: None Found

**Infotainment System Glitches:**
Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, software errors, audio issues.
Solution: Update or replace software, repair or replace faulty components.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2022 Aston Martin DBS offers impressive performance and luxury, it has experienced a fair share of issues in several key areas. The engine problems, such as turbocharger failure and fuel injector malfunctions, can be costly to repair. Transmission slippage in the 8-speed automatic is another notable issue. Electrical battery drain and interior climate control malfunctions can also be frustrating for owners. While some issues are minor, others can impact the vehicle's performance and safety.
