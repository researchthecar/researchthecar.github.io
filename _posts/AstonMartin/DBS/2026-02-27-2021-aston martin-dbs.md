### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Aston Martin DBS Superleggera demonstrates strong overall reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components (5.2L Twin-Turbo V12 engine and 8-speed automatic transmission), where no widespread issues are reported. The suspension system also appears robust. The primary areas where minor concerns have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches and, less commonly, intermittent battery drain issues. These are generally resolvable through software updates or diagnostics. Exterior and interior components are largely free from significant reported defects. Owners should prioritize regular servicing and be aware of potential electrical gremlins, though these do not detract from the vehicle's overall impressive engineering and performance.
