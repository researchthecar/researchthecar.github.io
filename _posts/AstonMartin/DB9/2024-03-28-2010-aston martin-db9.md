**Introduction:**

The 2010 Aston Martin DB9 is a luxury grand tourer renowned for its sleek design, powerful performance, and opulent interior. It is available with a 6-speed manual or a 6-speed automatic transmission. The 6.0-liter V12 engine produces 470 horsepower and 443 lb-ft of torque, delivering blistering acceleration and an intoxicating exhaust note. While the DB9 is adored for its exceptional driving experience, it has not been immune to certain mechanical issues.

**Engine**

**6.0L V12**

* **Intake Manifold Leakage:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Loss of engine power, rough idling, and increased fuel consumption.
  * Solution: Replacement of intake manifold gaskets.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Exhaust Manifold Cracking:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rattles or ticking noises, reduced engine performance, and possible exhaust fumes in the cabin.
  * Solution: Replacement of cracked exhaust manifolds.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch, and loss of power.
  * Solution: Replacement of clutch assembly.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Jerking or hesitation during gear changes, loss of power, and possible transmission warning lights.
  * Solution: Transmission rebuild or replacement.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,500 - $7,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Valve Body Issues:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rough shifting, delayed gear changes, and transmission warning lights.
  * Solution: Replacement of valve body.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Broken Front Control Arms:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or thumping noises over bumps, uneven tire wear, and altered steering feel.
  * Solution: Replacement of damaged control arms.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Shock Absorber Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Rough ride quality, poor handling, and premature tire wear.
  * Solution: Replacement of worn or damaged shock absorbers.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, weakened headlights, and electrical malfunctions.
  * Solution: Identification and repair of parasitic drain.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Faulty Instrument Cluster:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate or intermittent gauges, warning lights, or display failures.
  * Solution: Replacement of instrument cluster.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Loose Side Mirrors:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Vibrating or shaking side mirrors.
  * Solution: Tightening of mirror base or replacement of mirror assembly.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Multimedia Screen Failure:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Black or unresponsive screen, freezing, or loss of functionality.
  * Solution: Replacement of multimedia unit.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
  * Recalls: None Found
* **Power Window Malfunction:**
  * Description/Symptoms: Inoperable windows, slow or delayed operation, or window failing to remain open.
  * Solution: Repair or replacement of window motors, switches, or wiring.
  * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
  * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the Aston Martin DB9 is a desirable grand tourer, it has not been without its share of issues. Engine problems, transmission failures, and suspension concerns have been reported, along with electrical malfunctions and interior electronic glitches. Although the severity of the issues can vary, some repairs can be costly. Potential buyers need to be aware of these potential issues and factor in the potential cost of maintenance.
