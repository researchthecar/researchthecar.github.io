**Introduction**

The 2005 Aston Martin DB9 is a grand tourer that was praised for its beautiful design, powerful engine, and luxurious interior. It was offered with a 6.0-liter V12 engine mated to a 6-speed automatic or 6-speed manual transmission.

**Engine**

**6.0L V12**

* **Spark Plug Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, rough idling, engine hesitation
    * Solution: Replace spark plugs
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfires, engine hesitation, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace ignition coils
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300
* **Throttle Position Sensor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Erratic engine behavior, loss of power, stalling
    * Solution: Replace throttle position sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Fluid Leaks**
    * Description/Symptoms: Fluid leaking from transmission pan or other areas
    * Solution: Repair or replace damaged seals or gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
* **Shifting Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears
    * Solution: Adjust shift linkage, repair or replace transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000

**6-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Wear**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500
* **Synchronizer Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grating noises when shifting, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Repair or replace synchronizers
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mounts Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises over bumps
    * Solution: Replace strut mounts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Rear Suspension Bushings Worn**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or groaning noises from rear suspension
    * Solution: Replace bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely
    * Solution: Test and repair electrical system for parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* **Headlight Switch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Exterior**

* **Convertible Top Window Scratched**
    * Description/Symptoms: Scratches on convertible top window
    * Solution: Replace window
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+

**Interior**

* **Navigation System Outdated**
    * Description/Symptoms: Navigation system does not have the latest map data
    * Solution: Update navigation system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
* **Climate Control Not Functioning**
    * Description/Symptoms: Climate control system not regulating temperature or airflow properly
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair climate control system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+

**Summary**

The 2005 Aston Martin DB9 is a luxurious and powerful vehicle that can be a joy to own. However, it is important to be aware of the potential issues that can arise. The most common problems are related to the engine, transmission, and suspension. While the cost of repairs can be high, they are generally not overly severe and most owners should be able to afford the necessary maintenance and repairs.
