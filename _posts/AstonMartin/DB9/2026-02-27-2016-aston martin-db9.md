### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Aston Martin DB9 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability, particularly in its core powertrain components. The robust 5.9L V12 engine and the ZF automatic transmission are generally trouble-free. However, as with many luxury vehicles featuring advanced technology, some electrical components, such as the infotainment system and power window mechanisms, can occasionally present issues. Climate control actuator malfunctions are also a possibility. These are not indicative of systemic failure but rather the complexities inherent in a high-end GT. Regular maintenance and prompt attention to any minor electrical gremlins are key to ensuring a consistently excellent ownership experience.
