# 2015 Aston Martin DB9

### Overview
The 2015 Aston Martin DB9, a grand tourer known for its potent V12 engine and luxurious appointments, generally exhibits strong mechanical reliability. As a high-performance, low-volume exotic, its ownership experience is often characterized by meticulous maintenance and specialized care. While individual component failures can occur, widespread systemic issues are uncommon. Owners typically report satisfaction with the vehicle's core engineering, though the cost of ownership, particularly for repairs and specialized servicing, remains a significant consideration. This analysis focuses on documented reliability trends beyond routine maintenance, highlighting potential areas of concern for prospective or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 5.9L V12
**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Misfires, rough idling, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $600 per coil

**Coolant Leaks (Hoses/Pipes)**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant loss, sweet smell, engine overheating, puddles under the vehicle.
- **Solution:** Replacement of damaged coolant hoses or pipes.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Touchtronic Automatic
**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Slipping gears, shuddering during acceleration, delayed engagement, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Rebuilding or replacement of the torque converter.
- **Cost:** $2,500 - $5,000

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from transmission seals or lines, shifting problems, overheating.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals or lines.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,200


## Suspension

### Adaptive Dampers
**Electronic Damper Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, clunking noises over bumps, warning lights on the dashboard related to suspension.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty adaptive dampers.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $2,500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, displays errors, or freezes.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $800 - $2,000


### Battery Drain
**Parasitic Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies unexpectedly, car won't start after sitting for a short period.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair of faulty modules or circuits causing the drain.
- **Cost:** $300 - $900

### Climate Control System
**HVAC Blower Motor Failure**
- **Symptoms:** No airflow from vents, intermittent fan speed, unusual noises from the dashboard.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blower motor.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

### Summary Verdict
The 2015 Aston Martin DB9 demonstrates a commendable level of reliability for a luxury exotic sports car. The V12 engine and automatic transmission are generally robust, though specific components like ignition coils, torque converters, and coolant hoses can require attention. Electrical gremlins, particularly with the infotainment system and parasitic battery drain, are not uncommon and can be costly to resolve. Suspension components, especially the adaptive dampers, may also present issues. While not plagued by widespread systemic failures, owners should be prepared for the high cost of specialized maintenance and potential repairs inherent to a vehicle of this caliber. Regular, expert servicing is key to maintaining its reliability.
