**Introduction:**

The 2012 Aston Martin DB9 is a luxurious grand tourer powered by a naturally aspirated V12 engine. It offers an exhilarating driving experience and boasts a sleek and captivating design. However, like any vehicle, it can experience certain issues.

**Engine:**

* **5.9L V12**

    * **Intake Valve Sticking:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, rough idle, engine misfires
        * Solution: Replace affected intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Engine Overheating:**
        * Description/Symptoms: High coolant temperature, reduced engine performance
        * Solution: Inspect and repair cooling system components (e.g., water pump, thermostat)
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission:**

* **Touchtronic 2 Six-Speed Automatic**

    * **Shifting Issues:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed shifts, harsh shifting
        * Solution: Software update, transmission fluid replacement, or transmission rebuild
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $5,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **Graziano Six-Speed Manual**

    * **Clutch Wear:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Slipping clutch, difficulty engaging gears
        * Solution: Replace clutch assembly
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension:**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical:**

* **Electrical Failures:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent power loss, malfunctioning components (e.g., lights, gauges)
    * Solution: Inspect and repair or replace affected electrical components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior:**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation within headlight assemblies
    * Solution: Replace affected headlight assemblies
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior:**

* **Center Console Squeaking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Annoying squeaking noise from the center console area
    * Solution: Tighten or lubricate console components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

While the 2012 Aston Martin DB9 is a highly capable and desirable vehicle, it can experience certain issues related to its engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the exact nature of the problem. However, overall, the DB9 remains a reliable grand tourer with a strong track record of performance and luxury.
