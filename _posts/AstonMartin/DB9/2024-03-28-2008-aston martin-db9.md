**Introduction**

The 2008 Aston Martin DB9 is a grand tourer available with two engine options (V12 & V8) and three transmission options (6-speed automatic, 6-speed manual, and Touchtronic 2 6-speed automatic). It offers a blend of luxury, performance, and style. Enthusiasts praise its impressive engine power, sleek design, and responsive handling. However, it has some known issues that potential buyers should consider.

**Engine**

**V12**

* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises such as rattling or whining from the engine, especially during start-up or acceleration.
    * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain tensioner.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Cracked Exhaust Manifold:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking exhaust gases, resulting in a burning smell or a drop in engine performance.
    * Solution: Replace the cracked exhaust manifold.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**V8**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough gear changes, slipping gears, or refusal to shift into certain gears.
    * Solution: Repair or rebuild the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Torque Converter Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission overheating, loss of drive, or shuddering during gear changes.
    * Solution: Replacement of the torque converter.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Manual**

* **No Issues Found**

**Touchtronic 2 6-Speed Automatic**

* **Electronic Control Unit Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission malfunction, such as gear selection problems or error messages.
    * Solution: Replacement of the electronic control unit.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Knocking or clunking noises from the front suspension, unstable handling.
    * Solution: Replacement of the strut mounts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery losing charge rapidly, difficulty starting the car.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of the electrical system, potentially replacing the alternator or battery.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Electrical Harness Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning electrical components, such as lights, gauges, or power windows.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the affected wiring harness.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Sticky Dashboard:**
    * Description/Symptoms: The dashboard material becoming sticky and tacky, especially in warm weather.
    * Solution: Replacement of the dashboard.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Malfunction:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Air conditioning or heating not working properly, temperature control issues.
    * Solution: Repair or replacement of the climate control module or related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2008 Aston Martin DB9 is a desirable grand tourer, but it does have some potential issues that buyers should be aware of. Engine problems, transmission issues, electrical glitches, and interior problems are the most prevalent. The severity and cost of these issues can vary widely, so it's essential to have the vehicle inspected by a qualified mechanic before purchasing.
