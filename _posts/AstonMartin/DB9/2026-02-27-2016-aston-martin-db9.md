# 2016 Aston Martin DB9 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Aston Martin DB9 represents the final flourish for the iconic grand tourer, offering hand-crafted luxury and a sonorous V12 engine. Because these vehicles are built in limited numbers and primarily serve as weekend or collector cars, comprehensive owner complaint data is sparse. Used-car buyers looking at a 2016 model should focus on thorough pre-purchase inspections by marque specialists, verifying routine maintenance history, and checking for any electrical or auxiliary system quirks typical of low-volume exotic cars.

---

## Engine

### 6.0L V12 Engine

This naturally aspirated 6.0-liter V12 produces approximately 510 horsepower and 457 lb-ft of torque, delivering effortless acceleration and a signature exhaust note. The hand-built powerplant is generally robust when properly maintained, though regular servicing and fluid checks are critical for exotic ownership.

## Transmission

### Touchtronic III 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with the V12, the rear-mid-mounted ZF 8-speed automatic transmission provides smooth and rapid gear changes via paddle shifters. It is widely regarded as a responsive and durable unit for grand touring duties.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 17V796000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission:Control Module (Tcm/Pcm/Tecm)](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB9&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the park pawl does not engage and the vehicle is stopped on a slope and exited without the parking brake being applied, the vehicle may unexpectedly roll, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Aston Martin will notify owners, and dealers will update the TCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Aston Martin DB9 is a compelling choice for enthusiasts seeking timeless British styling and V12 performance. While formal complaint data is minimal, prospective buyers should always prioritize a professional inspection by an Aston Martin specialist to ensure all mechanical and electrical systems are in peak condition before purchase.
