# 2024 Aston Martin DB12 Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Aston Martin DB12 is a high-performance grand tourer that succeeds the DB11, offering immense power and luxury. As an ultra-luxury exotic vehicle, prospective used buyers should prioritize comprehensive pre-purchase inspections and verify service history to ensure proper maintenance.

---

## Engine

### 4.0L Twin-Turbocharged V8

Powered by a Mercedes-AMG-sourced 4.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 producing approximately 671 horsepower and 590 lb-ft of torque. This engine delivers rapid acceleration and robust performance typical of modern exotic grand tourers.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Equipped with a ZF 8-speed torque-converter automatic transmission routing power to the rear wheels. It provides smooth cruising gear shifts as well as aggressive performance responses when driven dynamically.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V712000: Engine And Engine Cooling:Engine:Oil/Lubrication:Pipes, Hoses, And Fittings](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB12&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** A sudden loss of oil and oil pressure could lead to the engine stalling or seizing, increasing the risk of a crash or fire.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the hoses, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 25V001000: Back Over Prevention:Display Function](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Aston+Martin&model=DB12&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard (FMVSS) number 111, "Rear Visibility." An obstructed rearview camera image can reduce the driver's rear visibility, increasing the risk of a crash or injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Aston Martin DB12 represents the pinnacle of British grand touring with exceptional powertrain performance. Because owner-reported data for this model year is minimal, buyers should rely on professional mechanical inspections and certified pre-owned histories.
