# 2012 Aston Martin Virage

### Overview
The 2012 Aston Martin Virage represents a pinnacle of British luxury grand touring, blending potent performance with exquisite craftsmanship. As a relatively low-volume, high-performance vehicle, its reliability data is less extensive than mass-produced cars. However, owners generally report a high level of satisfaction, with the Virage being built to exacting standards. The focus is on delivering a refined and powerful driving experience, and while individual component failures can occur, widespread systemic issues are uncommon. Maintenance and potential repairs, as expected with a vehicle of this caliber, can be costly and require specialized attention.

---


## Engine

### 5.9L V12
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, engine overheating, low coolant level warning light.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of hoses, seals, or radiator. Professional diagnosis is recommended.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1500

**Ignition Coil Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idling, misfires, check engine light, reduced engine power.
- **Solution:** Replacement of faulty ignition coil(s).
- **Cost:** $300 - $700 per coil


## Transmission

### ZF 6-Speed Automatic
**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid, slipping gears, delayed shifting, potential overheating.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of seals, transmission pan gasket, or cooler lines. Fluid flush and refill.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1200

**Torque Converter Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at low speeds, slipping, delayed engagement, unusual noises.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the torque converter. This is a labor-intensive repair.
- **Cost:** $1500 - $3000


## Suspension

### Adaptive Dampers
**Electronic Damper Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** Harsh ride, excessive body roll, suspension warning light, inconsistent damping.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and replacement of faulty adaptive damper units. Requires specialized Aston Martin diagnostic tools.
- **Cost:** $1000 - $2500 per corner


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Glitches/Freezing**
- **Symptoms:** Infotainment screen becomes unresponsive, displays errors, or freezes.
- **Solution:** Software update or potential replacement of the head unit. Often requires dealer intervention.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2000


### General Electrical System
**Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Battery dies prematurely, electrical components malfunction intermittently.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis to identify parasitic draw from modules or accessories. Battery replacement may be necessary.
- **Cost:** $300 - $800

### Summary Verdict
The 2012 Aston Martin Virage is a sophisticated grand tourer with a generally strong reliability record for its class. While not immune to issues, particularly those related to complex electronic systems and high-performance components, widespread failures are rare. Common concerns tend to be related to potential coolant leaks from the V12 engine, transmission fluid leaks, and occasional electronic glitches within the infotainment system or adaptive suspension. Repairs, when needed, are typically expensive and require specialized knowledge and parts. Owners should prioritize regular, professional maintenance to ensure the longevity and optimal performance of this exclusive vehicle.
