**Introduction to the 1990 Aston Martin Virage**

The 1990 Aston Martin Virage, a luxurious grand tourer, was powered by a 5.3-liter V8 engine producing 330 horsepower and 330 lb-ft of torque. It was paired with a 5-speed manual transmission as standard and offered a 5-speed ZF automatic as an option. The Virage was lauded for its elegant design, refined interior, and impressive performance, but it also faced several issues.

**Engine**

**5.3-Liter V8**

* **Excessive Oil Consumption**
    * Description/Symptoms: Burning oil excessively, leading to increased oil consumption and engine smoke
    * Solution: Replacing piston rings or performing an engine rebuild
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $10,000+
* **Fuel Injector Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting or running the engine, rough idling, poor fuel economy
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200
* **Water Pump Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, overheating engine
    * Solution: Replacing the water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800
* **Ignition Coil Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Misfiring, rough running engine, difficulty starting
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty ignition coil
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping clutch
    * Solution: Replacing the clutch assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
* **Gearbox Noise**
    * Description/Symptoms: Grinding or whining noises when shifting gears
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing damaged gears or bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500

**5-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power or hesitation during acceleration
    * Solution: Replacing or rebuilding the transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $5,000
* **Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Gear selection issues, delay in gear engagement
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises from the rear suspension, poor handling
    * Solution: Replacing the worn bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
* **Front Shocks/Struts Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking shock absorbers or struts, reduced ride quality, poor handling
    * Solution: Replacing the faulty shocks/struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,500

**Electrical**

* **Battery Discharge**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly without apparent reason
    * Solution: Identifying and fixing electrical components causing parasitic drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to recharge the battery, dim lights, electrical system issues
    * Solution: Replacing the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200

**Exterior**

* **Weatherstripping Deterioration**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking windows, wind noise
    * Solution: Replacing the weatherstripping
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600
* **Chrome Trim Pitting**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust or corrosion on chrome trims
    * Solution: Polishing or replacing the affected trims
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replacing the dashboard or repairing the cracks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
* **Climate Control Issues**
    * Description/Symptoms: Malfunctioning climate control system, inability to regulate temperature
    * Solution: Repairing or replacing the climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,200

**Summary**

The 1990 Aston Martin Virage faces several issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, and suspension. The cost of repairs can vary significantly depending on the severity of the issue and the expertise of the mechanic. While the Virage offers a luxurious and performance-oriented driving experience, it is essential to be aware of its potential issues and budget accordingly.
