**Introduction:**

The 1997 Aston Martin DB7 was a grand tourer that debuted with a 3.2-liter inline-6 engine and a 5-speed manual transmission. Later, a V12 engine option became available. The DB7 was praised for its sleek design, luxurious interior, and strong performance. However, like any vehicle, it had its share of issues.

**Engine**

**3.2L Inline-6**

* **Head gasket failure:** Symptoms include coolant leaking, overheating, and white smoke from the exhaust. Solution: Replace the head gasket. Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
* **Oil leaks:** Symptoms include visible oil leaks from the engine. Solution: Repair or replace the leaking seals or gaskets. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Coolant leaks:** Symptoms include coolant leaking from the radiator, hoses, or water pump. Solution: Repair or replace the leaking component. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Timing chain tensioner failure:** Symptoms include rattling noises from the engine. Solution: Replace the timing chain tensioner. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+

**6.0L V12**

* **Spark plug failure:** Symptoms include misfiring, rough idling, and reduced power. Solution: Replace the spark plugs. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Fuel injector failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting, rough idling, and reduced power. Solution: Replace the fuel injectors. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Alternator failure:** Symptoms include flickering lights, battery warning light, and difficulty starting. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Power steering pump failure:** Symptoms include heavy steering, whining noises, and leaks. Solution: Replace the power steering pump. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+

**Transmission**

**5-Speed Manual**

* **Clutch failure:** Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, slipping, or shuddering. Solution: Replace the clutch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
* **Transmission fluid leak:** Symptoms include leaks from the transmission case. Solution: Repair or replace the leaking gasket or seal. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Torque converter failure:** Symptoms include slipping, shuddering, or overheating. Solution: Replace the torque converter. Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
* **Solenoid failure:** Symptoms include shifting issues, harsh shifts, or limp mode. Solution: Replace the faulty solenoid. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure:** Symptoms include excessive bouncing, uneven tire wear, and poor handling. Solution: Replace the struts. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+ per axle
* **Control arm bushing failure:** Symptoms include clunking noises, vibration, and poor handling. Solution: Replace the bushings. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per axle
* **Sway bar link failure:** Symptoms include clunking noises and poor handling. Solution: Replace the sway bar links. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+ per side

**Electrical**

* **Battery failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting, flickering lights, and battery warning light. Solution: Replace the battery. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* **Ignition switch failure:** Symptoms include difficulty starting the car. Solution: Replace the ignition switch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Alternator failure:** Symptoms include flickering lights, battery warning light, and difficulty starting. Solution: Replace the alternator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* **Headlight switch failure:** Symptoms include flickering or inoperable headlights. Solution: Replace the headlight switch. Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+

**Exterior**

* **Rust:** Symptoms include body corrosion and bubbling paint. Solution: Repair or replace rusted panels. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $5,000+ depending on the extent of the damage
* **Sunroof leaks:** Symptoms include water leaking into the cabin from the sunroof. Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof seals. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
* **Door lock failure:** Symptoms include difficulty locking or unlocking the doors. Solution: Repair or replace the door lock actuators. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per door
* **Window regulator failure:** Symptoms include difficulty raising or lowering the windows. Solution: Repair or replace the window regulator. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+ per window

**Interior**

* **Climate control failure:** Symptoms include inoperable air conditioning, heating, or ventilation. Solution: Repair or replace the climate control module or components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+

**Other**

* **Radio failure:** Symptoms include no sound, distorted sound, or inoperable buttons. Solution: Repair or replace the radio. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
* **Cruise control failure:** Symptoms include inoperable cruise control buttons or system not engaging. Solution: Repair or replace the cruise control module or components. Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+

**Summary:**

The 1997 Aston Martin DB7 had its fair share of issues, particularly with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical components. While some issues are relatively minor and inexpensive to fix, others can be costly and require extensive repairs. The severity and cost of the issues vary depending on the specific model year and engine/transmission combination. It's important for potential owners to be aware of these potential problems before purchasing a used DB7 and to factor in the cost of potential repairs.
