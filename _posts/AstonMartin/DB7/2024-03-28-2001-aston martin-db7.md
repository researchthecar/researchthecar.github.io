## 2001 Aston Martin DB7

The 2001 Aston Martin DB7 is a grand tourer that comes with a naturally aspirated 6.0L V12 engine, producing 420 horsepower and 400 lb-ft of torque. The engine is paired with a 6-speed manual or 5-speed automatic transmission. The DB7 is praised for its timeless design, luxurious interior, and thrilling driving experience. However, it has its share of issues to be aware of.

**Engine**

* **6.0L V12**
    * **Valve Stem Seal Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Blue smoke from the exhaust, excessive oil consumption
        * Solution: Replace valve stem seals
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Crankshaft Position Sensor Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Stalling, difficulty starting
        * Solution: Replace crankshaft position sensor
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **6-speed Manual**
    * **Shift Linkage Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, grinding noises
        * Solution: Replace shift linkage
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found
* **5-speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leak from the pan or hoses
        * Solution: Replace leaking parts
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery drains quickly, even when the vehicle is not running
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical drain (faulty alternator, starter, etc.)
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Harness Short**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical malfunctions (lights, gauges, power windows, etc.)
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair short in electrical harness
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Corrosion**
    * Description/Symptoms: Door handles become corroded and fail to open doors
    * Solution: Replace door handles
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per handle
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Switchgear Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Electrical switches (power windows, locks, etc.) become faulty
    * Solution: Replace faulty switches
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500 per switch
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Dashboard Plastic Discoloration**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dashboard plastic becomes faded or discolored over time
    * Solution: Replace dashboard panel
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found

## Summary

The 2001 Aston Martin DB7 is a desirable grand tourer that offers a thrilling driving experience. However, it comes with several potential issues that can be costly to repair. The engine and transmission issues can be severe and require major overhauls. Electrical problems can also cause significant headaches and expenses. While the exterior and interior issues are less severe, they can still diminish the ownership experience. Potential buyers should be aware of these issues and factor in the potential costs of repairs when considering the DB7.
