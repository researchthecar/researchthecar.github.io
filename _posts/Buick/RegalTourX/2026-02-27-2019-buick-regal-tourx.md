# 2019 Buick Regal TourX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2019 Buick Regal TourX, buyers will find a stylish and versatile wagon that blends European driving dynamics with everyday practicality. While overall owner satisfaction is generally positive, evaluating specific mechanical systems and known owner complaints can help you navigate a smarter purchase. Paying close attention to powertrain electronics, routine maintenance history, and cabin components will ensure you select a reliable pre-owned example.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 250 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing brisk acceleration for daily driving. It is generally robust, though buyers should watch for occasional cold-weather crankcase pressure issues.

**PCV System and Rear Main Seal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A high-pitched squealing noise during very cold weather, followed by oil leaks caused by excessive crankcase pressure blowing out seals without triggering dashboard warning lights.
- **Solution:** Inspect the PCV system and replace damaged rear main or crankcase seals.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with front-wheel-drive models to deliver smooth and efficient gear shifts. Regular fluid maintenance helps ensure long-term durability.

### 水素 / 8-Speed Automatic (AWD)

An 8-speed automatic transmission is utilized in all-wheel-drive configurations, providing power distribution across varying road conditions. It offers refined operation when properly maintained.

### Shift-to-Park Microswitch

Electrical complaints center primarily around console shifter microswitches failing to register the correct gear position.

**Intermittent Shift to Park Message**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle displays a persistent 'Shift to Park' message on the dashboard even when the gear selector is fully in the park position, preventing accessories and lights from shutting off properly.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty shift position switch assembly within the console shifter.

## Interior

### Driver Seat Mechanism

Cabin hardware is generally comfortable, though rare structural failures in seat adjustment tracks have been reported by owners.

**Seat Track Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's seat suddenly breaks and slides completely forward during braking, then snaps backward, creating a hazardous driving situation.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat track locking mechanism and replace damaged seat frame components.

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Buick Regal TourX is an appealing choice for buyers seeking distinctive wagon utility, but inspecting common trouble spots is essential.
