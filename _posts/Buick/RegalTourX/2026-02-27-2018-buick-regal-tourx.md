# 2018 Buick Regal TourX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2018 Buick Regal TourX, buyers will find an appealing blend of European wagon styling and practical cargo space. While overall owner feedback is relatively modest, prospective purchasers should inspect specific areas such as the powertrain, electrical systems, and brakes to ensure long-term reliability.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces 250 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing lively performance for the wagon. It is generally robust, though owners occasionally report throttle-related power restrictions.

**Reduced Engine Power Mode**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report the check engine light illuminating alongside a 'Reduced Engine Power' warning message, leading to unexpected acceleration difficulties.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the powertrain control module and inspect throttle wiring connections.

## Transmission

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

This 8-speed automatic transmission pairs with standard all-wheel drive to deliver smooth shifts. Aside from occasional shifter sensor glitches, it operates reliably.

**'Shift to Park' Message and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle displays a persistent 'Shift to Park' warning on the driver information center despite being fully in park, which can prevent locking and drain the battery.
- **Solution:** Inspect and replace the shifter microswitch or assembly as necessary.

## Brakes

### Brake Hoses

Components within the braking system generally perform adequately, though individual hose failures highlight the importance of thorough pre-purchase inspections.

**Front Brake Hose Crimp Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have experienced sudden failure of the front passenger brake hose at the crimp fitting.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake lines and hoses thoroughly during routine maintenance and replace any compromised components immediately.

## Electrical

### Backup Camera and Assistance Systems

electrical issues in this vehicle tend to center around driver-assistance accessories and convenience features rather than core charging hardware.

**Intermittent Backup Camera and Grid Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera display frequently fails to show guide lines, or the backup and cross-traffic warning systems intermittently stop working.
- **Solution:** Check camera software, wiring harnesses, and rear module connections for faults.

## Summary Verdict

The 2018 Buick Regal TourX offers distinctive styling and utility as a used vehicle purchase, but buyers should exercise due diligence. Prioritize a comprehensive pre-purchase inspection focusing on the brakes, electrical accessories like the backup camera, and proper operation of the shifter mechanism.
