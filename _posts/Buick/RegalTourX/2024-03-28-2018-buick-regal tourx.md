**Introduction:**

The 2018 Buick Regal TourX is a mid-size wagon that is based on the Regal sedan. The TourX is offered with a choice of two engines: a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder or a 3.6L V6. Both engines are mated to an 8-speed automatic transmission. The Regal TourX has a spacious cabin and a comfortable ride, but it suffers from some reliability issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
    * **Intake Valve Deposits:** Carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, leading to a loss of power and fuel economy. The solution is to clean the intake valves. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200 - $400.
    * **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** The timing chain tensioner can fail, which can cause the timing chain to skip and damage the engine. The solution is to replace the timing chain tensioner. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $1,000 - $2,000.
* **3.6L V6**
    * **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **8-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may experience shifting problems, such as slipping gears or refusing to shift. The solution is to replace the transmission control module. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $1,000 - $2,000.
    * **Torque Converter Failure:** The torque converter may fail, which can cause the transmission to slip or shudder. The solution is to replace the torque converter. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $1,500 - $2,500.

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is not in use. The solution is to replace the battery. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $100 - $200.
* **Electrical Gremlins:** The electrical system may experience a variety of gremlins, such as flickering lights, inoperative gauges, and malfunctioning power windows. The solution is to diagnose and repair the specific electrical problem. The estimated cost to fix this issue can vary widely depending on the specific problem.

**Exterior**

* **No Issues Found**

**Interior**

* **Seat Heaters:** The seat heaters may not work properly. The solution is to replace the seat heater elements. The estimated cost to fix this issue is $200 - $400.
* **Climate Control Malfunction:** The climate control system may not work properly, such as blowing hot air when cold air is selected. The solution is to diagnose and repair the specific climate control problem. The estimated cost to fix this issue can vary widely depending on the specific problem.

**Summary:**

The 2018 Buick Regal TourX has a number of reliability issues, including intake valve deposits, a failing timing chain tensioner, shifting problems with the transmission, battery drain, electrical gremlins, faulty seat heaters, and problems with the climate control system. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost to fix them can be significant. Overall, the 2018 Buick Regal TourX is not a reliable vehicle, and it is not recommended to purchase one.
