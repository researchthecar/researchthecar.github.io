# 2007 Buick Terraza

### Overview
The 2007 Buick Terraza, a minivan based on the GM U platform, offered a comfortable ride and practical interior space. While generally considered a decent family vehicle for its time, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical problems that go beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential reliability concerns for prospective buyers or current owners. Understanding these potential weak points can help in proactive maintenance and budgeting for repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.5L V6
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating, sweet smell of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Timing Chain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the timing chain, tensioners, and guides.
- **Cost:** $800 - $1500


### 3.9L V6
**Intake Manifold Gasket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, engine overheating, sweet smell of coolant.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold gaskets.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700

**Variable Valve Timing (VVT) Solenoid Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, poor performance, check engine light with VVT codes.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the VVT solenoid.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


## Transmission

### 4-Speed Automatic (4T65-E)
**Torque Converter Clutch (TCC) Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Shuddering at certain speeds, slipping, delayed engagement.
- **Solution:** Rebuild or replacement of the torque converter and potentially the transmission.
- **Cost:** $1200 - $2500

**Transmission Fluid Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Leaking fluid from seals or lines, low fluid level, shifting problems.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of leaking seals, lines, or cooler.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noise when turning the steering wheel, especially at low speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the strut mount bearings.
- **Cost:** $250 - $450

**Worn Ball Joints/Tie Rod Ends**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking noises over bumps, vague steering, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn ball joints and/or tie rod ends.
- **Cost:** $400 - $700


## Electrical

### Body Control Module (BCM)
**Intermittent Power Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Various electrical components (lights, power windows, locks) acting erratically or failing completely.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and potential reprogramming or replacement of the BCM.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1000


### Power Sliding Door Actuators
**Failure to Open/Close**
- **Symptoms:** Sliding doors not operating correctly, slow operation, or complete failure.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the power sliding door actuator motor or assembly.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800 per door


## Exterior

### Body Panels & Trim
**Rust on Rear Wheel Arches**
- **Symptoms:** Visible rust and corrosion on the lower rear body panels.
- **Solution:** Rust repair and repainting.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700


## Interior

### HVAC System
**Blend Door Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or direct airflow (e.g., only hot air or cold air, air only coming from defroster).
- **Solution:** Replacement of the HVAC blend door actuator.
- **Cost:** $200 - $400


### Infotainment System
**Radio/Display Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent screen flickering, unresponsiveness, or complete failure of the radio or display.
- **Solution:** Repair or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2007 Buick Terraza, while offering practicality, exhibits some common issues that owners should be aware of. The engines, particularly the V6 options, can suffer from intake manifold gasket leaks and, in some cases, timing chain wear. Transmission issues, especially related to the torque converter clutch, are also reported. On the electrical front, Body Control Module (BCM) problems and power sliding door actuator failures can be costly. Suspension components like strut mounts and ball joints may require attention over time. Interior comfort can be affected by HVAC blend door failures. While not plagued by widespread catastrophic failures, these potential issues suggest that thorough pre-purchase inspections and a budget for potential repairs are advisable for this model.
