**Introduction:**

The 2014 Buick LaCrosse is a midsize luxury sedan with a comfortable and spacious interior. It is available with a choice of two gasoline engines: a 3.6L V6 with 303 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque or a 2.4L 4-cylinder with 182 horsepower and 172 lb-ft of torque. Both engines come standard with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The LaCrosse is praised for its refined ride, luxurious amenities, and advanced safety features. However, it has received some criticism for its limited cargo space and unintuitive infotainment system.

**Engine**

**3.6L V6**

**Timing Chain Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The timing chain may fail, causing engine damage. Symptoms include a rattling or ticking noise from the engine, a loss of power, and a check engine light.
Solution: Replace the timing chain, guides, and tensioners.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**2.4L 4-Cylinder**

**No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**

**Shift Solenoid Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The shift solenoid may fail, causing delayed or harsh shifts. Symptoms include difficulty shifting gears, slipping gears, and a check engine light.
Solution: Replace the shift solenoid.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely. Symptoms include difficulty starting the vehicle, dimmed headlights, and a battery warning light.
Solution: Diagnose and repair the source of the drain, which may include replacing the battery, alternator, or cables.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Window Regulator Failure**
Description/Symptoms: The window regulator may fail, causing the windows to become inoperable. Symptoms include windows that are slow or difficult to operate, or that fail to open or close completely.
Solution: Replace the window regulator motor.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**No Issues Found**

**Interior**

**Sunroof Noise**
Description/Symptoms: The sunroof may squeak or rattle when open or closed.
Solution: Lubricate the sunroof tracks and adjust the glass panel.
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2014 Buick LaCrosse is a reliable and well-made vehicle, but it has a few potential issues that buyers should be aware of. The most serious issue is the timing chain failure on the 3.6L V6 engine, which can be expensive to repair. Other issues, such as the shift solenoid failure and window regulator failure, are less severe and less costly to fix. Overall, the LaCrosse is a solid choice for those looking for a comfortable and luxurious midsize sedan.
