**2016 Buick LaCrosse**

The 2016 Buick LaCrosse is a midsize luxury sedan that offers a comfortable and refined ride. It is powered by a standard 3.6-liter V6 engine that produces 304 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque, paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission. Other available engine options include a 2.5-liter four-cylinder and a 3.6-liter V6 with eAssist. The LaCrosse is praised for its spacious interior, quiet cabin, and comfortable seats, but it has received some criticism for its bland exterior styling and uninspiring driving dynamics.

**Engine**

**3.6-liter V6**

**Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
* Description/Symptoms: Oil or coolant leaks, engine overheating, rough idle
* Solution: Replace intake manifold gaskets
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 18V769000

**Transmission**

**Eight-Speed Automatic Transmission**

**Shift Solenoid Issues**
* Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or shuddering, delayed or harsh shifts
* Solution: Replace shift solenoids
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Control Arm Bushings Wear**
* Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noises while driving over bumps, imprecise steering
* Solution: Replace worn bushings
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per side
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain**
* Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
* Solution: Identify and repair source of battery drain (e.g., faulty parasitic draw)
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Headlight Condensation**
* Description/Symptoms: Water droplets or condensation inside headlight housings
* Solution: Seal or replace headlight housings
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500 per headlight
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Center Console Trim Rattle**
* Description/Symptoms: Loose or rattling trim around center console
* Solution: Tighten or replace trim clips
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $200
* Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2016 Buick LaCrosse is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has experienced some common issues related to its engine, transmission, and suspension. The intake manifold gasket failure and shift solenoid issues can be expensive to repair, while other issues like battery drain and trim rattles are less severe. Overall, the severity of the issues is moderate, but some repairs can be costly.
