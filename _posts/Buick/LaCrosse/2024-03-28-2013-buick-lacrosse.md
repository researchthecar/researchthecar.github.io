**Introduction:**

The 2013 Buick LaCrosse is a full-size sedan powered by a standard 3.6-liter V6 engine with 288 horsepower and 270 lb-ft of torque, paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. It also offers an optional 2.4-liter four-cylinder engine with 182 horsepower and 172 lb-ft of torque, mated to a six-speed automatic transmission. The LaCrosse is praised for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and refined driving experience, while criticisms include its uninspiring handling and fuel economy.

**Engine**

* **3.6-liter V6**
    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Deposits of carbon accumulate on the intake valves, restricting airflow and causing a decrease in engine performance and fuel efficiency. Symptoms may include rough idling, hesitation upon acceleration, and reduced power output.
        * Solution: Removal of the intake manifold and cleaning of the intake valves.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.4-liter Four-Cylinder**
    * **Timing Chain Stretch**
        * Description/Symptoms: Over time, the timing chain can stretch and cause a discrepancy in the timing between the camshaft and crankshaft. Symptoms may include a rattling noise from the engine, especially during startup or acceleration, and a reduction in engine power.
        * Solution: Replacement of the timing chain and related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic**
    * **Delayed Shift Response**
        * Description/Symptoms: The transmission hesitates or delays when shifting gears, resulting in rough or jerky shifts.
        * Solution: Software updates or replacement of the transmission control module.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $600+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Transmission Fluid Leak**
        * Description/Symptoms: A leak in the transmission fluid can cause slipping and decrease in power transfer. Symptoms may include difficulty shifting gears, a burning smell, and low fluid levels.
        * Solution: Replacement of seals or gaskets, or more extensive transmission repairs.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **No Issues Found**

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery drains prematurely, even when the vehicle is parked and turned off.
    * Solution: Diagnosis and repair of electrical components that are drawing excessive power.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Sunroof Panel Creaking**
    * Description/Symptoms: The sunroof panel makes creaking or rubbing noises when opened or closed.
        * Solution: Lubrication of the sunroof rails or replacement of the sunroof panel.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: The climate control system fails to maintain desired temperature or fan speed.
        * Solution: Replacement of climate control module or sensors.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Windshield Wiper Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The windshield wipers stop working or operate intermittently.
        * Solution: Replacement of the wiper motor or related components.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2013 Buick LaCrosse generally has moderate reliability, with some notable issues affecting the engine, transmission, electrical system, and interior. While the costs to fix these issues can be significant, the majority of them are relatively minor and can be addressed with routine maintenance and repairs.
