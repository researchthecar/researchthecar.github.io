# 2016 Buick LaCrosse

### Overview
The 2016 Buick LaCrosse, a mid-size luxury sedan, generally offers a comfortable and refined driving experience. It was available with two primary powertrain options: a standard 3.6L V6 engine and an optional eAssist mild-hybrid system. While the LaCrosse is known for its quiet cabin and smooth ride, like any vehicle, it has specific areas where owners have reported issues. This analysis focuses on common mechanical and electrical concerns reported by owners and technicians, excluding routine maintenance and standard wear and tear. Understanding these potential problems can help prospective buyers and current owners make informed decisions regarding maintenance and repairs.

---


## Engine

### 3.6L V6 (LGX)
**Coolant Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Visible coolant puddles under the vehicle, low coolant level warning, engine overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of leaking gaskets, hoses, or water pump.
- **Cost:** $300 - $700

**Carbon Buildup**
- **Symptoms:** Rough idle, reduced engine power, decreased fuel efficiency, check engine light.
- **Solution:** Professional intake manifold cleaning or walnut blasting.
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


### eAssist (2.4L Hybrid)
**Battery Pack Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Reduced electric assist, reduced fuel economy, warning lights related to the hybrid system.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the hybrid battery pack.
- **Cost:** $2,000 - $4,000


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6T70/6T75)
**Harsh Shifting / Slipping**
- **Symptoms:** Jerky gear changes, delayed engagement, slipping between gears, transmission warning light.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid flush and filter replacement, solenoid replacement, or transmission rebuild.
- **Cost:** $500 - $2,500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mounts / Bearings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises when turning or going over bumps, uneven tire wear.
- **Solution:** Replacement of worn strut mounts and bearings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Screen Freezing / Unresponsiveness**
- **Symptoms:** Touchscreen becomes unresponsive, system freezes, audio issues.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $1,000

### Summary Verdict
The 2016 Buick LaCrosse is generally a reliable vehicle, particularly the powertrain when maintained properly. The most commonly reported issues revolve around the 3.6L V6 engine experiencing coolant leaks and carbon buildup, and the transmission sometimes exhibiting harsh shifting. The eAssist hybrid model has had some reports of battery pack issues. Electrical gremlins, such as infotainment system glitches and battery drain, are also noted. Interior and exterior components are less prone to significant problems, with blend door actuators being a minor concern in the climate control system. Overall, addressing potential coolant leaks and transmission behavior proactively can contribute to a positive ownership experience.
