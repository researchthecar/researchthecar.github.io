## 2003 Buick LeSabre

The 2003 Buick LeSabre is a full-size sedan known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and affordable price. It comes standard with a 3.8L V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission. Other available engine and transmission options include:

- 3.8L V6 with 5-speed automatic transmission (205 hp, 230 lb-ft torque)
- 3.8L Supercharged V6 with 4-speed automatic transmission (260 hp, 280 lb-ft torque)

Drivers enjoy the LeSabre's smooth ride, quiet cabin, and ample storage space. However, it has been criticized for its lack of handling and performance, as well as its dated interior design.

### Engine

**3.8L V6**
- **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, rough idling, decreased engine performance
  - Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1000
  - Recalls: None Found

**3.8L Supercharged V6**
- **Supercharger Belt Tensioner Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Whining or squealing noise from engine, decreased performance, potential engine damage if not addressed
  - Solution: Replace supercharger belt tensioner
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

### Transmission

**4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
- **Transmission Fluid Leak**
  - Description/Symptoms: Leaking transmission fluid, slipping gears, hesitation during acceleration
  - Solution: Replace transmission fluid pan gasket or seal
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
  - Recalls: None Found

**5-Speed Automatic Transmission**
- **Solenoid Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Harsh or delayed shifting, slipping gears, loss of power
  - Solution: Replace failed solenoid
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

- **Strut Mount Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise from suspension, uneven tire wear, reduced handling
  - Solution: Replace strut mounts
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
  - Recalls: None Found

- **Sway Bar Link Wear**
  - Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noise when driving over bumps, reduced cornering stability
  - Solution: Replace worn sway bar links
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
  - Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

- **Alternator Failure**
  - Description/Symptoms: Dimming or flickering lights, battery light illuminated, difficulty starting engine
  - Solution: Replace alternator
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

- **Plastic Trim Discoloration**
  - Description/Symptoms: Plastic trim on bumpers, fenders, or side skirts fading or becoming discolored
  - Solution: None available, replacement trim pieces may also discolor
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A

### Interior

- **Climate Control Malfunction**
  - Description/Symptoms: Failure of climate control system to heat or cool properly, fan noise or vibration
  - Solution: Repair or replace faulty components in climate control system (e.g., blower motor, control module)
  - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
  - Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2003 Buick LeSabre is a spacious and comfortable sedan with a few common issues. The most severe issues are related to the engine, such as the intake manifold gasket failure and supercharger belt tensioner failure. These problems can lead to engine damage if not addressed promptly. Other issues, such as transmission fluid leaks, suspension wear, and electrical failures, are less severe but still require attention to maintain the vehicle's reliability. Overall, the LeSabre has a moderate level of reliability and repair costs are generally affordable.
