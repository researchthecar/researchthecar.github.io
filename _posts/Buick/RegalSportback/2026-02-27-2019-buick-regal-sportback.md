# 2019 Buick Regal Sportback Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Buick Regal Sportback, buyers will find a stylish and versatile hatchback offering European driving dynamics. However, reviewing owner-submitted complaints and known mechanical tendencies can help you spot potential trouble spots before signing a purchase agreement. While many owners enjoy daily driving comfort, being aware of recurring electrical and drivetrain quirks will ensure you make an informed investment.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine generates approximately 250 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, providing spirited acceleration for the Sportback. Overall reliability is generally sound, though owners should monitor for occasional pressure regulation or seal issues in cold climates.

**PCV and Crankcase Seal Failure**
- **Symptoms:** A high-pitched squealing noise during freezing weather, followed by an oil leak caused by excessive internal crankcase pressure blowing out seals.
- **Solution:** Inspect the PCV system and replace damaged rear main or crankcase seals.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with front-wheel-drive configurations, this 9-speed automatic transmission generally delivers smooth shifts and efficient highway cruising. No widespread, major mechanical failure trends are heavily documented in owner complaints for this unit.

### 8-Speed Automatic Transmission

Used in all-wheel-drive variants, this 8-speed automatic works alongside the turbo engine to distribute power effectively. It maintains a relatively quiet reliability profile in daily driving conditions.

### Power Train Shifter

Components related to gear selection can occasionally present electrical or mechanical switch communication bugs that confuse vehicle power-down sequences.

**"Shift to Park" Message Defect**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle displays a persistent "Shift to Park" message even when the gear selector is fully in the park position, preventing accessories and lights from shutting off and draining the battery.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the faulty shift position switch assembly according to manufacturer service bulletins.

## Suspension

### Wheels and Tires

Suspension and wheel assemblies handle road imperfections well, though sensitive wheel-balance tolerances require careful maintenance to prevent cabin vibrations.

**Highway Speed Vibrations**
- **Symptoms:** Noticeable vehicle rattling, shaking mirrors, and wheel vibration at highway speeds that are difficult to resolve through standard wheel balancing.
- **Solution:** Check tires for out-of-round conditions, ensure precise wheel balancing, or replace defective tires.

## Interior

### Driver Seat Frame

Interior cabin seating structures are typically sturdy, though isolated failures of adjustment tracks can occur under heavy stress.

**Seat Track Breakage**
- **Symptoms:** The driver's seat suddenly breaks and slides forward uncontrollably during braking maneuvers, creating a safety hazard.
- **Solution:** Inspect seat adjustment tracks and replace broken seat frame components or mechanisms.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 18V576000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Foundation Components:Disc:Caliper](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Regal&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** A reduction of braking performance can increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will bleed the vehicle's brake system, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 19V762000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Regal&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Seats with improper welds may not provide adequate protection in the event of a crash, increasing the risk of an injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the crossbar welds on the front seats. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V465000: Service Brakes, Hydraulic:Power Assist:Vacuum](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Regal&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** 135, "Light vehicle brake systems." A loss of power brake assist can extend the distance required to stop the vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the EBCM software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The Buick Regal Sportback is a comfortable and practical European-influenced cruiser, but used car buyers should perform thorough checks before purchasing. Pay close attention to electrical gear-shifter messages, test drive for any high-speed vibrations, and verify that all safety recalls have been completed by a certified dealer.
