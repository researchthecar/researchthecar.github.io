**Introduction:**

The 2019 Buick Regal Sportback is a stylish and sporty sedan that offers a comfortable and well-equipped interior. It comes with a 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine that produces 250 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail, leading to a loss of power and increased engine noise.
* **Description/Symptoms:** The engine produces reduced power, makes excessive noise, and may illuminate the check engine light.
* **Solution:** Replace the turbocharger.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $1,500 - $2,500+
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Transmission**

**Nine-Speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Issues:** The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifting, especially during acceleration.
* **Description/Symptoms:** The transmission hesitates or jerks while shifting gears.
* **Solution:** Update the transmission software or replace the transmission solenoids.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $500 - $1,500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Failure:** The front or rear struts can fail, leading to a rough ride and reduced handling.
* **Description/Symptoms:** The vehicle bounces excessively, makes clunking noises, and may pull to one side.
* **Solution:** Replace the struts.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $600 - $1,200
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery can drain prematurely, even when the vehicle is turned off.
* **Description/Symptoms:** The battery dies regularly, especially after a period of inactivity.
* **Solution:** Identify and repair the electrical drain, which may involve replacing faulty components or updating software.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $1,000
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Exterior**

* **Rear Tailgate Seal Failure:** The tailgate seal can fail, allowing water to leak into the cargo area.
* **Description/Symptoms:** Water enters the trunk, causing musty odors and potential electrical issues.
* **Solution:** Replace the tailgate seal.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $200 - $500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Interior**

* **Windshield Wiper Control Failure:** The windshield wiper control module can fail, causing the wipers to malfunction or fail completely.
* **Description/Symptoms:** The wipers fail to operate or operate intermittently.
* **Solution:** Replace the windshield wiper control module.
* **Estimated Cost to Fix:** $250 - $500
* **Recalls:** None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2019 Buick Regal Sportback has several common issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. The severity of these issues can range from minor to major, and the cost to fix them can vary significantly. Owners should be aware of these potential problems and consider them when purchasing or maintaining their vehicle.
