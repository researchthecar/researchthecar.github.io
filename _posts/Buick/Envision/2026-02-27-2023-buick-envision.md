# 2023 Buick Envision Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2023 Buick Envision, buyers will find a stylish and comfortable compact crossover with modern tech features. However, owner reports submitted to federal safety regulators highlight a few distinct areas worth noting, including electrical glitches, braking concerns, and occasional powertrain behavior. As with any modern vehicle packed with advanced driver-assistance systems and electronics, a thorough pre-purchase inspection is recommended.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine produces around 228 horsepower and is paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. It offers a balanced blend of efficiency and daily drivability for this crossover.

**Unintended Neutral Shift**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported experiences where the vehicle appeared to unexpectedly shift into neutral or experience jolting during acceleration and deceleration.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the transmission control module and powertrain software for diagnostic trouble codes or necessary updates.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed automatic transmission comes standard across the lineup, routing power smoothly to either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive depending on the configuration.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system provides standard anti-lock braking and electronic stability assistance, though a small number of owners have flagged sudden pedal feel inconsistencies.

**Brake Pedal Response Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Some owners experienced unexpected reductions in braking resistance or delayed stopping response when pressing the brake pedal.
- **Solution:** Inspect the hydraulic brake system, brake booster, and associated electronic brake modules for faults or fluid leaks.

## Electrical

### Telematics and OnStar Module

The electrical architecture supports advanced infotainment and connected vehicle services, though module failures can occasionally disrupt cabin tech.

**Telematics Module Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Failure of the OnStar or telematics module can result in loss of GPS, inability to connect to remote services, and intermittent battery drain.
- **Solution:** Replace or reprogram the faulty telematics control module as diagnosed by a dealer or technician.

## Safety

### Park Assist and Driver Aids

Advanced safety features enhance driver awareness, but buyers should verify that all promised driver-assistance hardware is fully operational.

**Inoperable Park Assist Features**
- **Symptoms:** Certain vehicles were delivered or operated with temporarily non-functional park assist features due to industry-wide supply chain constraints.
- **Solution:** Check with a dealership to see if retrofits or missing semiconductor components have been installed under available manufacturer notices.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V903000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Envision&modelYear=2023)**
- **Issue:** The daytime running lights (DRLs) may not deactivate when the headlights are on. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." DRLs that do not deactivate as intended may result in a glare, reducing visibility, and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Buick Envision is generally a comfortable and well-equipped choice for compact luxury SUV buyers, but it is not entirely immune to initial quality hiccups. Prospective buyers should carefully test all electrical systems, infotainment features, and brake responsiveness during a pre-purchase test drive, and verify whether any safety features were impacted by previous component shortages.
