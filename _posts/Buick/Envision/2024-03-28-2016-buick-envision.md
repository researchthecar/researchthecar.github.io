**Introduction:**

The 2016 Buick Envision is a compact SUV available with two engine and transmission options: a 2.5L 4-cylinder paired with a 6-speed automatic and producing 182 horsepower and 172 lb-ft of torque, and a 3.6L V6 paired with a 6-speed automatic and producing 301 horsepower and 272 lb-ft of torque. The Envision is praised for its comfortable interior, spacious cargo area, and refined handling, but some owners have reported issues with its powertrain and other components.

**Engine**

**2.5L 4-cylinder**

* **Oil Leaks:** Oil leaks can occur from various gaskets and seals, causing oil loss and potential engine damage.
    * Solution: Replace faulty gaskets or seals.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Timing Chain Problems:** The timing chain may stretch or break over time, leading to engine noise, reduced power, and potential catastrophic engine failure.
    * Solution: Replace timing chain and related components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.6L V6**

* **Valve Cover Gasket Leaks:** Leaks from the valve cover gasket can cause oil consumption and engine smoke.
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gasket.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-speed Automatic**

* **Shifting Problems:** The transmission may hesitate, slip, or refuse to shift gears, leading to drivability issues.
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut and Shock Wear:** The struts and shocks may wear prematurely, causing excessive bouncing and reduced ride comfort.
    * Solution: Replace struts and shocks.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+ per side
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Lower Control Arm Bushing Failure:** The lower control arm bushings may fail, leading to clunking or squeaking noises, and reduced alignment stability.
    * Solution: Replace lower control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain prematurely, leading to starting problems or electrical system failures.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical drain source (e.g., faulty alternator, parasitic draw).
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Infotainment System Issues:** The infotainment system may experience glitches, freezes, or display problems.
    * Solution: Software updates or hardware replacements.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $0 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Tail Light Condensation:** Water may accumulate inside the tail lights, causing condensation or moisture buildup.
    * Solution: Replace taillight assemblies.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400 per side
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Sunroof Drain Blockages:** The sunroof drains may become blocked, leading to water leaks inside the vehicle.
    * Solution: Clear or replace sunroof drains.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Steering Wheel Control Issues:** The steering wheel controls may become unresponsive or intermittently work.
    * Solution: Replace steering wheel control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

* **Climate Control Problems:** The climate control system may malfunction, blowing warm air when cold is selected or vice versa.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair electrical or mechanical issues within the system.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2016 Buick Envision has received mixed reviews due to its reported issues. The engine, transmission, and suspension components have been the most problematic areas, with some repairs being costly and time-consuming. However, it is worth noting that not all Envision owners have experienced these issues, and many continue to enjoy the vehicle's comfort and performance.
