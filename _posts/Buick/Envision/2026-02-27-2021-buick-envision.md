# 2021 Buick Envision Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used Buick Envision, prospective buyers will find a stylish and comfortable compact crossover with a refined cabin and modern tech. Reviewing owner feedback and vehicle reliability records helps highlight potential trouble spots—such as powertrain behavior and accessory malfunctions—so you can make an informed purchasing decision.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces around 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate pep for daily driving. It generally offers smooth operation, though buyers should monitor for occasional fuel system sensor or pump concerns.

**Fuel Pump Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a check engine light or performance hesitation related to fuel delivery components.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician diagnose the fault codes and replace faulty fuel system components as needed.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission pairs with the standard turbo engine to optimize highway efficiency. While generally smooth, some owners experience erratic shifting or momentary loss of drive power.

**Transmission Slipping and Shifting Delays**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported instances where the transmission unexpectedly slips out of drive, shifts into neutral, or hesitates before jerking forward during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Check for available software updates from the dealer or have the transmission inspected for internal wear and fluid degradation.

## Suspension

### Factory Tires

Suspension and wheel assemblies maintain ride comfort, but certain factory-equipped tire batches have shown tendencies toward early wear.

**Premature Tire Wear and Damage**
- **Symptoms:** Original equipment tires may exhibit early tread pitting, delamination, or unexpected blowouts at relatively low mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect tire tread depth and condition regularly, and replace tires showing signs of structural breakdown.

## Exterior

### Interior Lighting System

Cabin electrical architecture controls lighting, infotainment, and driver aids. Minor glitches involving persistent interior illumination have been noted by some owners.

**Unresponsive Interior Lights**
- **Symptoms:** Interior rear lights may spontaneously turn on while driving and fail to turn off automatically after the vehicle is shut down.
- **Solution:** Inspect body control module settings and associated door switches for electrical faults.

## Interior

### HVAC Defrost Housing

Exterior visibility relies heavily on properly functioning climate and defroster systems, which can occasionally experience internal housing actuator failures.

**Climate Control Defrost Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Windshield and side windows fail to clear properly when defrost mode is selected, accompanied by a climate system warning message on the dash.
- **Solution:** Dealer inspection may require replacing the internal HVAC case housing components.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V421000: Air Bags:Srs Malfunction Warning Lamp/Light](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Envision&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The air bag malfunction indicator light may fail to warn the driver of a problem with the air bag, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

**[Recall 22V903000: Exterior Lighting:Lighting Control Module:Software](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Envision&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The daytime running lights (DRLs) may not deactivate when the headlights are on. As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 108, "Lamps, Reflective Devices, and Associated Equipment." DRLs that do not deactivate as intended may result in a glare, reducing visibility, and increasing the risk.
- **What to do:** Verify recall completion by VIN with NHTSA, the manufacturer, or a dealer before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

Overall, the vehicle presents a comfortable and well-equipped option for used compact SUV shoppers, but it warrants a careful inspection. Pay close attention during a test drive to ensure smooth transmission engagement, proper climate control operation, and healthy tire condition before finalizing your purchase.
