## 2023 Buick Envision

The 2023 Buick Envision is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable and luxurious driving experience. It is available with two engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four and a 2.5-liter naturally aspirated inline-four. Both engines are paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission.

People like the Envision for its spacious and well-appointed interior, its smooth ride, and its powerful engine options. However, some drivers have reported issues with the vehicle's engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system.

**Engine**

**2.0-Liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Intake Valve Deposits:** Carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, causing reduced engine power and fuel economy. Solution: Cleaning the intake valves is necessary, which can cost $200 - $400.
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure:** A faulty timing chain tensioner can cause the timing chain to slip, resulting in engine damage. Solution: Replacing the timing chain tensioner and timing chain, which can cost $1,500 - $2,500+.
* **Turbocharger Failure:** The turbocharger can fail due to various reasons, such as oil starvation or bearing wear. Solution: Replacing the turbocharger, which can cost $2,000 - $3,000+.

**2.5-Liter Naturally Aspirated Inline-Four**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**Nine-Speed Automatic Transmission**

* **Shifting Issues:** The transmission may experience rough or delayed shifts, especially when shifting from Park to Drive. Solution: Updating the transmission software or replacing the transmission control module. Cost: $200 - $1,000+
* **Torque Converter Shudder:** The torque converter may shudder when the vehicle is accelerating or under load. Solution: Replacing the torque converter, which can cost $1,000 - $2,500+.

**Suspension**

* **Rear Suspension Noise:** Some drivers have reported a clunking or rattling noise coming from the rear suspension. Solution: Replacing the rear sway bar end links or bushings, which can cost $200 - $400+.
* **Front Suspension Strut Failure:** The front suspension struts can fail prematurely, causing a decrease in ride comfort and handling. Solution: Replacing the front struts and mounts, which can cost $600 - $1,000+ per side.

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:** The battery may drain quickly, even when the vehicle is parked and turned off. Solution: Fixing the faulty components or accessories causing the drain, which can vary in cost depending on the specific issue.
* **Electrical Shorts:** Electrical shorts can occur, causing various electrical malfunctions. Solution: Identifying and repairing the source of the short, which can be time-consuming and costly.

**Exterior**

* **Windshield Cracking:** The windshield may crack easily due to rock chips or other impacts. Solution: Replacing the windshield, which can cost $400 - $800+
* **Headlight Condensation:** Condensation may build up inside the headlights, impairing visibility. Solution: Replacing the headlights or resealing them, which can cost $200 - $600+ per headlight.

**Interior**

* **Infotainment Screen Malfunctions:** The infotainment screen may freeze, lag, or become unresponsive. Solution: Updating the software or replacing the infotainment system, which can cost $500 - $1,000+.
* **Climate Control Issues:** The climate control system may malfunction, causing inconsistent temperatures or fan speeds. Solution: Replacing the climate control module or actuators, which can cost $200 - $600+.

**Summary**

The 2023 Buick Envision is a well-rounded SUV with a comfortable and luxurious interior. However, it has been reported to have some issues with its engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The severity of these issues can vary, and the cost of repairs can be significant. It is important for potential buyers to be aware of these issues before making a purchase decision.
