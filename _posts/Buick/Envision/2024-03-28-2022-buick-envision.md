**Introduction**
The 2022 Buick Envision is a compact SUV that offers a comfortable ride and plenty of features. It comes with a standard 2.0-liter turbocharged 4-cylinder engine that produces 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Buyers can also choose an available 2.5-liter naturally aspirated 4-cylinder engine that produces 194 horsepower and 190 lb-ft of torque, which is paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission. The Envision is available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations.

Owners of the 2022 Buick Envision have reported a number of issues with their vehicles. These issues range from minor annoyances to more serious problems.

**Engine**
**2.0-Liter Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**
* **Excessive Oil Consumption:** Some owners have reported that their engines are consuming excessive amounts of oil. This can lead to engine damage if not corrected.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to replace the piston rings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-229
* **Timing Chain Failure:** Some owners have reported that their timing chains have failed, which can cause the engine to seize.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to replace the timing chain and associated components.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number: 22V-100

**2.5-Liter Naturally Aspirated 4-Cylinder**
* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**
**9-Speed Automatic**
* **Shifting Problems:** Some owners have reported that their transmissions are shifting hard or erratically.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to reprogram the transmission control module.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Failure:** Some owners have reported that their transmissions have failed completely.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to replace the transmission.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $3,000 - $5,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**6-Speed Automatic**
* **No Issues Found**

**Suspension**
* **Leaking Struts:** Some owners have reported that their struts are leaking fluid. This can lead to a loss of ride quality and handling.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to replace the struts.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**
* **Battery Drain:** Some owners have reported that their batteries are draining prematurely. This can make it difficult to start the vehicle.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to diagnose the cause of the battery drain and repair it.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Shorts:** Some owners have reported that their vehicles have experienced electrical shorts. This can lead to a variety of problems, including fires.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to diagnose the cause of the electrical short and repair it.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**
* **Paint Defects:** Some owners have reported that their vehicles have paint defects, such as peeling or chipping.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to have the paint repaired or replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Wind Noise:** Some owners have reported that their vehicles experience excessive wind noise. This can be annoying and make it difficult to hear conversations.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to have the vehicle inspected for any sources of wind noise and have them repaired.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**
* **Climate Control Problems:** Some owners have reported that their climate control systems are not working properly. This can make it difficult to maintain a comfortable temperature inside the vehicle.
    * Solution: The solution to this problem is to diagnose the cause of the climate control problem and repair it.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Overall Summary**
The 2022 Buick Envision is a comfortable and well-equipped SUV, but it has a number of potential issues that buyers should be aware of. The severity and cost of these issues can vary depending on the specific problem, but some of the more serious issues can be expensive to repair. Overall, the Envision is a solid vehicle, but buyers should carefully consider the potential risks before making a purchase.
