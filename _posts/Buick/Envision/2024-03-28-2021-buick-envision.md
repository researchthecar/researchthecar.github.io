**Introduction:**

The 2021 Buick Envision is a compact SUV with a luxurious interior and a powerful engine. It comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged inline-four engine that produces 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque, paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission. Other engine and transmission options include a 2.5-liter naturally-aspirated inline-four engine with a 6-speed automatic transmission, and a 3.6-liter V6 engine with an 8-speed automatic transmission.

The Envision has received praise for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and impressive fuel efficiency. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of off-road capability and its infotainment system's occasional glitches.

**Engine**

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Inline-Four**

* **Oil Consumption:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, requiring frequent top-ups or engine failure.
    * Solution: Engine rebuild or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $5,000+
    * Recalls: NHTSA Campaign Number 22V370000
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, unusual noises, or engine overheating.
    * Solution: Turbocharger replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattling or clunking sounds, poor handling.
    * Solution: Strut mount replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shock Absorber Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced ride comfort, excessive bouncing.
    * Solution: Shock absorber replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery can go dead even after short periods of parking.
    * Solution: Battery replacement or electrical system diagnosis.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $800
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Electrical Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Random electrical system issues such as flickering lights or inoperative power windows.
    * Solution: Electrical system diagnosis and repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door Lock Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening or closing doors, malfunctioning power locks.
    * Solution: Door lock actuator replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Tailgate Rust:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rust formation on the tailgate.
    * Solution: Tailgate replacement or rust repair.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking, rattling, or inability to open or close sunroof.
    * Solution: Sunroof panel or motor repair or replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Glitches:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Intermittent issues such as freezing, slow performance, or connectivity problems.
    * Solution: Software updates or infotainment system replacement.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $50 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 2021 Buick Envision has a number of common issues, particularly with the engine, transmission, and suspension. Some of these issues are quite severe and can be costly to fix. However, the Envision also has some good qualities, such as its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and impressive fuel efficiency. Ultimately, the decision of whether or not to buy a 2021 Buick Envision depends on the individual's priorities and willingness to accept potential problems.
