# 2024 Buick Envision Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2024 Buick Envision, buyers should weigh its stylish design and comfortable ride against a handful of reported owner concerns. While NHTSA complaints are driver reports rather than absolute proof of manufacturing flaws, reviewing these patterns helps prospective owners know what to inspect during a pre-purchase test drive.

---

## Engine

### 2.0L Turbocharged I4 Engine

The 2024 Buick Envision comes standard with a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine producing approximately 228 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. It provides adequate power for daily commuting, though buyers should verify proper engine health and service history.

**Excessive Oil Consumption**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported finding engine oil significantly low on the dipstick well before scheduled oil change intervals.
- **Solution:** Monitor oil levels regularly between changes and consult a dealer to start an oil consumption test if levels drop prematurely.

**Unexpected Engine Stall**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers experience a sudden loss of motive power and engine stalling accompanied by a Check Engine light and reduced power warnings.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician scan the powertrain control module for stored diagnostic trouble codes.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Power is routed through a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. The transmission generally delivers smooth shifts, though buyers should test all gears during a test drive.

## Exterior

### Interior Controls and Lighting

Electrical architecture manages infotainment and driver aids, requiring stable software to function reliably.

**Screen and Lighting Glitches**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers attempting to adjust settings like automatic high beams via the center console screen have experienced sudden loss of dashboard and exterior lighting functionality.
- **Solution:** Restart the vehicle to reset infotainment modules or consult a dealership for potential software patches.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Avoidance

Advanced safety features aim to protect occupants, but sensor anomalies can lead to erratic behavior.

**Unintended Automatic Braking**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may unexpectedly engage the emergency brakes or slow down while driving at higher speeds or when reversing in the dark.
- **Solution:** Check sensor alignments, keep camera and radar surfaces clean, and have dealer software calibrations updated if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 24V710000: Equipment:Other:Labels](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Envision&modelYear=2024)**
- **Issue:** As such, these vehicles fail to comply with the requirements of Federal Motor Vehicle Safety Standard number 110, "Tire Selection and Rims." Missing tire and load rating information may result in driving with improperly inflated tires or an overloaded vehicle, increasing the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will install the label, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2024 Buick Envision offers a modern compact luxury crossover experience, but prospective buyers should perform a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to fluid levels, ensure driver-assistance features operate smoothly during a test drive, and verify that all software updates have been applied.
