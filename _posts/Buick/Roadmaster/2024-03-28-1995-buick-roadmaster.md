**Introduction:**

The 1995 Buick Roadmaster was a large sedan known for its luxurious interior, spacious cabin, and powerful engine options. It came with three engine options: a 5.7L V8, a 7.4L V8, and a 6.5L turbodiesel V8. Transmission options included a 4-speed automatic and a 5-speed manual. While the Roadmaster was praised for its comfort and performance, it also had some common issues.

**Engine**

**5.7L V8**

* **Intake manifold gasket failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant or oil, overheating, poor engine performance
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Water pump failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, squealing noise
    * Solution: Replace water pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**7.4L V8**

* **Valve cover gasket leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil, burning oil smell, low oil levels
    * Solution: Replace valve cover gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-speed Automatic**

* **Transmission slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, slipping when accelerating, jerking
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Torque converter failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Shuddering when shifting gears, slipping, overheating
    * Solution: Replace torque converter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $1,200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**5-speed Manual**

* **Clutch master cylinder failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty engaging gears, leaking fluid
    * Solution: Replace clutch master cylinder
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking oil, rough ride, uneven tire wear
    * Solution: Replace struts
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Sway bar links worn:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise over bumps, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical problems, battery not charging
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty starting, clicking noise
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Door lock actuators failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Doors not locking or unlocking properly
    * Solution: Replace door lock actuators
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200 per actuator
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Radio display issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Display fading, flickering, or not working
    * Solution: Replace radio display unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1995 Buick Roadmaster is a reliable and comfortable sedan with some common issues. Engine and transmission problems can be expensive to fix, but most other issues are relatively minor and affordable. Overall, the Roadmaster is a good choice for those looking for a spacious and powerful vehicle that may require some maintenance over time.
