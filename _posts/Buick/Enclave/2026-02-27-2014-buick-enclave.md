# 2014 Buick Enclave

### Overview
The 2014 Buick Enclave is a mid-size luxury SUV known for its comfortable ride, spacious interior, and upscale features. It was generally well-received for its family-friendly attributes and smooth performance. Reliability for the 2014 model year is considered average to good, with most owners reporting satisfaction. However, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that have seen more attention from mechanics and owners. This report focuses on documented technical issues beyond routine maintenance, providing insights into potential concerns for prospective buyers or current owners.

---


## Engine

### 3.6L V6
**Timing Chain Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rattling noise from the front of the engine, especially on startup; check engine light may illuminate.
- **Solution:** Replacement of timing chain, tensioners, and guides. In some cases, variable valve timing (VVT) components may also need attention.
- **Cost:** $1,500 - $3,000

**Intake Manifold Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Coolant leaks, rough idle, potential overheating.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the intake manifold.
- **Cost:** $400 - $800


## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic (6T75)
**Transmission Slipping/Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Delayed engagement, slipping between gears, rough shifting, or failure to shift.
- **Solution:** Transmission fluid and filter change, software update, or in severe cases, transmission rebuild or replacement.
- **Cost:** $1,000 - $3,500


## Suspension

### Standard Chassis
**Strut Mounts/Bearings**
- **Symptoms:** Clunking or popping noises when turning or going over bumps, especially at lower speeds.
- **Solution:** Replacement of front strut mounts and bearings.
- **Cost:** $300 - $600


## Electrical

### Infotainment System
**Touchscreen Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Unresponsive touchscreen, freezing, or random reboots.
- **Solution:** Software update or replacement of the infotainment unit.
- **Cost:** $500 - $1,500


### Power Liftgate
**Liftgate Not Opening/Closing Properly**
- **Symptoms:** Liftgate fails to open or close automatically, or closes unexpectedly.
- **Solution:** Diagnosis and repair or replacement of liftgate motor, sensors, or control module.
- **Cost:** $400 - $900


## Exterior

### Body Panels
**No widespread issues reported.**
- **Symptoms:** N/A
- **Solution:** N/A
- **Cost:** Varies


## Interior

### Climate Control System
**Blend Door Actuator Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Inability to control temperature or direct airflow to specific vents.
- **Solution:** Replacement of the blend door actuator(s).
- **Cost:** $200 - $500


--- 
### Summary Verdict
The 2014 Buick Enclave offers a comfortable and spacious experience, with its primary powertrain being the 3.6L V6. While generally reliable, potential buyers should be aware of common issues such as timing chain problems and intake manifold leaks, which can be costly to repair. Transmission issues like slipping or hesitation are also noted, requiring attention. Electrical components, particularly the infotainment system and power liftgate, can sometimes present glitches. The suspension may require attention to strut mounts. Overall, the Enclave is a solid choice for families seeking luxury and space, but it's advisable to factor in potential repair costs for these known issues.
