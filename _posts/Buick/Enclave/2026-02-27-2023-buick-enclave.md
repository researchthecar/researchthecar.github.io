# 2023 Buick Enclave Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2023 Buick Enclave is a spacious three-row midsize crossover known for its smooth ride and family-friendly cabin. While generally comfortable and capable, used car buyers should be aware of select owner-reported issues involving exterior lighting, infotainment operation, seat belt mechanisms, and minor electrical components. As with any modern used vehicle, inspecting these specific areas during a pre-purchase test drive can help ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Powered by a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 310 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, providing confident acceleration for daily driving and highway passing. This naturally aspirated powerplant has a generally solid mechanical reputation, though routine fluid maintenance remains essential.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission available in both front-wheel drive and all-wheel drive configurations. It delivers smooth, seamless gear shifts designed to optimize highway fuel economy and low-end responsiveness.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

Stopping power is generally reliable, though minor squeals or acoustic complaints can occur under normal operating conditions.

**Brake Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have noted loud squeaking noises originating from the brake system during normal application.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for premature wear, glazing, or debris, and replace components as needed.

## Electrical

### Infotainment System

The electronics suite offers modern connectivity features, though software glitches can occasionally disrupt infotainment and smartphone integration.

**Screen Freezes and Reboots**
- **Symptoms:** The central infotainment display may fail to show backup camera feeds or navigation, or spontaneously freeze and reboot while driving.
- **Solution:** Often resolved by software updates performed at a dealership or resetting the infotainment system.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting

Exterior lighting systems generally perform well, though isolated premature LED headlight failures have been reported by owners.

**Headlight Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers report that individual LED headlight assemblies stop illuminating, typically affecting one side and creating visibility hazards at night.
- **Solution:** Requires replacement of the sealed LED headlight assembly, which may involve substantial out-of-pocket costs if out of warranty.
- **Cost:** ~$1,450

## Interior

### Sunroof and Drains

Exterior body panels fit well, but vehicles equipped with a panoramic sunroof require occasional clearing of drain tubes to prevent water intrusion.

**Sunroof Water Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Clogged or faulty sunroof drain tubes can cause water to leak into the headliner, potentially disrupting the rearview mirror electronics and wiring harnesses.
- **Solution:** Clear or repair sunroof drain tubes and inspect affected interior wiring and headliner fabric for moisture damage.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Occupant safety systems are robust, but buyers should physically test all seat belt buckles and retractors, particularly in the second and third rows.

**Retractor and Cable Issues**
- **Symptoms:** Rear or passenger seat belt retractors fail to release smoothly, or external floor anchor cables break, preventing occupants from buckling securely.
- **Solution:** Inspection and replacement of the faulty seat belt assembly or retractor mechanism by a certified technician.

## Summary Verdict

The 2023 Buick Enclave remains a compelling choice for shoppers seeking a comfortable, quiet, and well-appointed midsize SUV. While owner complaints highlight a few specific areas—such as expensive LED headlight replacements, occasional infotainment quirks, and rare seat belt mechanism snags—these do not point to widespread systemic powertrain failure. Buyers should thoroughly test all electronic features, check lighting functions, and inspect interior safety restraints before finalizing their purchase.
