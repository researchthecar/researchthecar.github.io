### Summary Verdict
The 2021 Buick Enclave generally presents a solid reliability record, with its core powertrain components (3.6L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission) showing no widespread issues. The suspension and exterior/interior elements also appear to be robust. However, some owners have encountered issues with the infotainment system, specifically screen freezing or unresponsiveness, and intermittent battery drain. These electrical gremlins, while not affecting every vehicle, are the most notable areas for potential concern. Addressing these issues typically involves software updates or component diagnosis and potential replacement. Overall, the Enclave is a reliable choice for families, provided potential buyers are aware of and prepared for these specific electrical system quirks.
