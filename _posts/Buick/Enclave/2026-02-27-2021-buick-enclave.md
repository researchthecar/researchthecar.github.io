# 2021 Buick Enclave Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2021 Buick Enclave, buyers will find a spacious, comfortable three-row midsize crossover powered by a capable V6 engine. While it offers strong family-friendly utility and a smooth highway ride, prospective owners should review common owner-reported grievances. Particular attention should be paid to reports regarding electrical liftgate anomalies and early-mileage brake friction sounds.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 SIDI VVT

The 2021 Buick Enclave comes standard with a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 310 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, paired with a smooth-shifting automatic transmission. This powertrain provides confident acceleration for daily driving and highway passing, though buyers should watch for occasional reports of exhaust component wear.

**Catalytic Converter and Exhaust Failures**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported premature failures of the catalytic converters, repeated exhaust flex pipe damage, and sections of the tailpipe loosening or detaching.
- **Solution:** Inspect the exhaust system and catalytic converters for signs of excessive wear, rattling, or previous welding repairs; replace damaged components as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A 9-speed automatic transmission routes power to either the front wheels or an available all-wheel-drive system, offering seamless gear selection and respectable highway efficiency.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The braking system generally provides adequate stopping power for a large crossover, though early-mileage noise complaints warrant a thorough pre-purchase brake inspection.

**Premature Brake Grinding and Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice a grinding noise and unusual pedal feel from the front brakes even at very low vehicle mileage.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake pads and rotors for premature wear or glazing, and replace components if scoring or abnormal friction is found.

## Exterior

### Liftgate and Trunk Latch

Electrical complaints center heavily around convenience features, particularly erratic tailgate and door lock behaviors.

**Random Liftgate Opening**
- **Symptoms:** The rear liftgate or trunk hatch unexpectedly pops open on its own while parked or, in some cases, while driving.
- **Solution:** Have a dealer or qualified technician inspect the tailgate latch assembly, body control module, and exterior sensors for electrical faults or software glitches.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally functions as intended, though passenger seatbelt latch mechanisms require physical verification during a test drive.

**Passenger Seatbelt Latch Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The passenger-side seatbelt buckle fails to latch smoothly on the first try, occasionally triggering dashboard warning lights for the airbag and seatbelt system.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seatbelt buckle assembly for internal mechanical binding or foreign debris, and replace the buckle receiver if necessary.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V472000: Air Bags:Sensor:Frontal Impact](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** During service by dealers, certain vehicles may have received incorrect air bag calibration software that may affect frontal airbag deployment. Air bags that do not deploy as intended can increase the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will update the air bag calibration software, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V210000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2021)**
- **Issue:** The harness connector to the left or right side roof rail air bag may have incompatible electrical terminals, which could prevent the air bags from deploying in a crash. Roof rail air bags that do not deploy as intended increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the harness connector to the roof rail air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2021 Buick Enclave remains an appealing choice for families seeking generous cargo room, passenger comfort, and a compliant ride. While major mechanical failures are infrequent, used car shoppers should perform a careful inspection of the rear liftgate electronics, exhaust components, and brake wear surfaces to ensure trouble-free ownership.
