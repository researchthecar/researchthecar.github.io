**Introduction:** The 2023 Buick Enclave is a midsize luxury SUV that offers a spacious interior, comfortable ride, and a powerful engine. It's available with two engine options: a 3.6-liter V6 that produces 310 horsepower and 268 lb-ft of torque, and a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder that produces 230 horsepower and 258 lb-ft of torque. Both engines are paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission. The Enclave is known for its reliability, but there have been some reports of issues with certain components.

**Engine**
**3.6-liter V6**
- **Intake manifold valve sticking**
    - Description/Symptoms: The intake manifold valve can stick, causing the engine to run rough and stall.
    - Solution: Replace the intake manifold valve.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Oil leaks from valve cover gaskets**
    - Description/Symptoms: The valve cover gaskets can leak oil, causing a burning smell and smoke from the engine compartment.
    - Solution: Replace the valve cover gaskets.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    - Recalls: None Found

**2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**
- **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**
**9-Speed Automatic**
- **Shifting problems**
    - Description/Symptoms: The transmission may shift hard or hesitate to shift gears.
    - Solution: Reprogram the transmission control module or replace the transmission.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,500+
    - Recalls: NHTSA ID: 22V225000 (Shift lever indicator may not match actual gear selected)

**Suspension**

- **Strut mounts failing**
    - Description/Symptoms: The strut mounts may fail, causing a clunking noise when driving over bumps.
    - Solution: Replace the strut mounts.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Sway bar links wearing out**
    - Description/Symptoms: The sway bar links may wear out, causing a rattling noise when driving over bumps.
    - Solution: Replace the sway bar links.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

- **Battery draining**
    - Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, leaving the vehicle unable to start.
    - Solution: Test the alternator and battery, and replace if necessary.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $1,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Electrical short in wiring harness**
    - Description/Symptoms: An electrical short in the wiring harness can cause various electrical components to malfunction.
    - Solution: Repair or replace the damaged wiring harness.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $2,000+
    - Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

- **Tailgate struts failing**
    - Description/Symptoms: The tailgate struts may fail, causing the tailgate to fall when opened.
    - Solution: Replace the tailgate struts.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Antenna mast breaking**
    - Description/Symptoms: The antenna mast may break, causing poor radio reception.
    - Solution: Replace the antenna mast.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200
    - Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

- **Infotainment system freezing**
    - Description/Symptoms: The infotainment system may freeze or crash, making it difficult to use navigation, audio, and other features.
    - Solution: Update the software or replace the infotainment unit.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
    - Recalls: None Found

- **Heated seats not working**
    - Description/Symptoms: The heated seats may not work properly or may not heat up at all.
    - Solution: Replace the heated seat element or wiring harness.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found

**Other**

- **Brake pads wearing prematurely**
    - Description/Symptoms: The brake pads may wear prematurely, requiring more frequent replacement.
    - Solution: Replace the brake pads and inspect the brake system for any underlying issues.
    - Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500
    - Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

Overall, the 2023 Buick Enclave is a reliable vehicle, but there have been some reports of issues with certain components. The most severe issues, such as transmission shifting problems and electrical shorts, can be costly to repair.
