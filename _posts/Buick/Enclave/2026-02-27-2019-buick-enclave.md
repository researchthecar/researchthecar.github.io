# 2019 Buick Enclave Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2019 Buick Enclave is a spacious and comfortable three-row crossover suited for family transport, offering ample cargo room and a smooth highway ride. However, prospective buyers should review owner-reported complaints regarding electrical issues, transmission shudder, and occasional engine driveability concerns. Performing a thorough pre-purchase inspection focused on the powertrain and electronics can help identify potential hidden issues.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

The 3.6-liter V6 engine produces approximately 310 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, providing strong acceleration for this large crossover. Overall, the engine is generally robust, though sporadic owner reports point to electrical or sensor interruptions leading to sudden stalls.

**Unexpected Engine Stalling**
- **Symptoms:** Vehicles may unexpectedly lose power while driving at moderate speeds, accompanied by loss of power steering and erratic electrical accessory behavior.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the electrical system, battery connections, and engine control modules for faults.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 9-speed automatic transmission is paired with front-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations to optimize fuel economy and highway cruising. While generally smooth, some units develop torque converter shudder or harsh engagement that warrants a test drive prior to purchase.

**Transmission Shudder and Shifting Hesitation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a noticeable shudder or vibration at low speeds, rough gear shifts, or hesitation during acceleration.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition; software updates or torque converter service may be required depending on wear.

## Electrical

### Audio and Infotainment Module

The electrical system powers extensive cabin amenities and advanced driver aids, though occasional module failures and sensor glitches have been noted by owners.

**Amplifier Failure from Condensation**
- **Symptoms:** Audio system speakers stop functioning completely due to moisture or HVAC condensation entering the amplifier module.
- **Solution:** Inspect the module for water intrusion and replace or relocate the affected unit as necessary.

## Safety

### Seat Belts

Safety equipment generally performs well, but individual hardware components like seat belt buckles should be physically tested for secure engagement.

**Seat Belt Latch Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The seat belt fails to click or lock securely into the buckle mechanism during fastening.
- **Solution:** Inspect the seat belt assembly and replace the faulty buckle mechanism if internal latch components fail.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V668000: Power Train:Automatic Transmission](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2019)**
- **Issue:** Missing bolts on the start-stop accumulator endcap could result in a transmission oil leak and may progress to a loss of propulsion, increasing the risk of a crash. A transmission fluid leak in the presence of an ignition source may increase the risk of fire.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the start-stop transmission accumulator and replace it if any bolts are missing, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2019 Buick Enclave delivers family-friendly utility, quiet comfort, and strong V6 performance, but buyers should remain vigilant about potential transmission shudder and electrical complaints. A comprehensive pre-purchase inspection emphasizing transmission health and electronic module integrity is strongly recommended before finalizing a purchase.
