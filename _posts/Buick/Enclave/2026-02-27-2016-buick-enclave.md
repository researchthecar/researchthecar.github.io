# 2016 Buick Enclave Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Buick Enclave is a large, comfortable crossover SUV known for its generous passenger space and smooth highway ride. However, prospective buyers should carefully evaluate its mechanical and electrical history, as owner reports highlight recurring issues ranging from transmission shifting anomalies to steering fluid leaks and interior water intrusion.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing around 288 horsepower, the Enclave offers strong acceleration for its size. While generally capable, owners have reported intermittent power loss and stalling concerns.

**Engine Stalling and Power Loss**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may lose power, sputter, or stall unexpectedly, particularly when accelerating from a stop or driving uphill.
- **Solution:** Diagnose fuel delivery, throttle body, and ignition components; replace faulty sensors or wiring harnesses as needed.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The 6-speed automatic transmission pairs with the V6 engine in front-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations. It delivers smooth cruising power, though older units can develop shifting delays and shuddering.

**Rough Shifting and Gear Sticking**
- **Symptoms:** The transmission may shudder or shake when shifting gears, skip gears unexpectedly, or hang up in a single gear.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition, update powertrain control modules, or overhaul internal components and torque converters if mechanical wear is present.

## Steering

### Power Steering System

The steering system provides adequate feedback for a large crossover, but hose and seal degradation can lead to fluid leaks and heavy steering feel.

**Power Steering Fluid Leaks and Stiff Steering**
- **Symptoms:** Loud whirring or squealing noises when turning the steering wheel, accompanied by heavy steering resistance at low speeds due to fluid loss.
- **Solution:** Inspect power steering lines, seals, and pump for leaks, then replace worn hoses and refill fluid.

## Interior

### Cabin Electronics and Fuse Box

Electrical accessories and onboard tech generally function well, but cabin water leaks can compromise under-dash wiring and fuse panels.

**Water Intrusion into Interior Fuse Box**
- **Symptoms:** Water leaks under the dashboard on the driver or passenger side during heavy rain, soaking carpet padding and causing erratic electrical behavior.
- **Solution:** Clear clogged cowl drains or sunroof drains and inspect weatherstripping to prevent water from entering the cabin fuse block.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V782000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag inflator may explode during deployment, due to a manufacturing defect. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V833000: Seats](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** If the seat frame is not welded in the correct places, the seat may not perform as intended, and in the event of a crash, the seat occupant could be at an increased risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect the third row left side lower seat frame, replacing it as necessary, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 15V609000: Visibility:Windshield Wiper/Washer:Motor](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A wiper motor that overheats increases the risk of a fire.
- **What to do:** GM has notified owners, and dealers will replace the windshield wiper motor cover assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V334000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag inflator may explode during deployment, due to a manufacturing defect. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Buick Enclave remains an appealing choice for families needing three rows of seating and a plush ride. Used car shoppers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection that checks transmission shift quality, power steering lines, and the condition of the interior footwells for signs of water intrusion.
