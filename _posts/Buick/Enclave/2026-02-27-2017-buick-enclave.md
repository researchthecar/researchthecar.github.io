# 2017 Buick Enclave Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2017 Buick Enclave offers a spacious and comfortable three-row cabin, smooth ride quality, and family-friendly versatility. While it remains a popular choice for larger families on the used market, buyers should carefully review maintenance records and watch out for recurring component complaints related to powertrain operation, safety systems, and electrical accessories.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 288 horsepower, this powertrain provides strong acceleration for the large crossover. Regular fluid maintenance and timely spark plug changes are critical for long-term reliability.

**Check Engine Light and Drivetrain Shudder**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle may experience unexpected vibrations, hesitation, or stall while driving, accompanied by an illuminated check engine or electronic stability control warning light.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic pull diagnostic trouble codes and inspect ignition components, sensors, or fuel delivery systems.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The six-speed automatic transmission comes paired with front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive. Smooth operation is vital, so a pre-purchase test drive focusing on gear transitions is highly recommended.

**Shudder and Harsh Shifting**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a shudder or shaking sensation when the transmission shifts between gears, particularly around lower speeds or moderate RPM ranges.
- **Solution:** Inspect transmission fluid condition, check for updated powertrain control module calibrations, or consult a specialist for internal component wear.

## Electrical

### Lighting and Battery Systems

Electrical accessories require standard upkeep, though specific complaints note diminished night visibility and occasional battery performance issues.

**Headlight Dimming and Battery Drain**
- **Symptoms:** Headlights may experience reduced illumination over time, or the vehicle may fail to start due to insufficient battery cranking power.
- **Solution:** Test the battery and charging system output, and inspect headlight assemblies for wear or bulb degradation.

## Exterior

### Sunroof and Visibility

Exterior body components are generally durable, but panoramic or standard sunroof glass panels warrant a careful inspection before purchase.

**Sunroof Shattering**
- **Symptoms:** The front sunroof glass may fracture or shatter unexpectedly while driving, producing a loud noise and scattering glass fragments.
- **Solution:** Inspect the sunroof assembly for any pre-existing stress cracks and replace damaged glass panels immediately.

## Safety

### Air Bags

Safety system complaints primarily center around warning indicators and coordination delays for manufacturer recall remedies.

**Air Bag Warning Light and Recall Delays**
- **Symptoms:** The airbag warning light may illuminate on the dashboard, or owners may experience prolonged waiting periods for replacement parts related to active safety recalls.
- **Solution:** Check the NHTSA database with your specific VIN to verify open recalls and contact an authorized dealership to schedule necessary repairs as parts become available.

---

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V782000: Air Bags:Frontal](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag inflator may explode during deployment, due to a manufacturing defect. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V246000: Air Bags:Critical Fasteners](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The side curtain air bags may not be properly attached. Unsecured side curtain air bags may not perform as intended, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will inspect and, if necessary, realign or replace the weld nuts, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 23V334000: Air Bags:Frontal:Driver Side:Inflator Module](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2017)**
- **Issue:** The driver's air bag inflator may explode during deployment, due to a manufacturing defect. An inflator explosion may result in sharp metal fragments striking the driver or other occupants, resulting in serious injury or death.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the driver's air bag module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Buick Enclave is a capable family hauler that delivers comfort and generous cargo space, but used buyers should proceed with a thorough inspection. Pay close attention to powertrain shift quality, verify that all safety recalls have been completed by a dealership, and check the electrical and sunroof components to ensure trouble-free ownership.
