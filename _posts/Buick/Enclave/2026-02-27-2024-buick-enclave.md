# 2024 Buick Enclave Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2024 Buick Enclave represents a spacious and comfortable three-row crossover choice for families. As buyers explore this model on the used market, reviewing early owner reports can help highlight initial areas to watch, keeping in mind that these are preliminary observations rather than systemic flaws.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 310 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, providing smooth acceleration for daily driving and highway cruising. This tried-and-true powerplant offers reliable performance for buyers seeking traditional V6 capability in a large crossover.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Features a 9-speed automatic transmission paired with either front-wheel drive or all-wheel drive, delivering seamless gear changes to optimize fuel efficiency. This unit generally offers dependable operation for everyday family transport.

## Steering

### Steering Column and Pump

Steering components generally feel responsive, though isolated noises have been reported.

**Steering Wheel Whining Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a whining noise originating from the steering wheel area while the vehicle is stationary or in motion during turns.
- **Solution:** Inspect the steering components and power assist system to diagnose the source of the noise.

## Electrical

### Infotainment Control Module

Owner feedback points to occasional electrical glitches affecting cabin tech and convenience features.

**Infotainment Screen Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The radio receiver and display screen may experience a complete loss of power, resulting in a blank screen and no audio output.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified technician inspect the infotainment control module and software for potential resets or component replacement.

## Exterior

### Power Mirrors

Exterior accessories and power features require standard inspection during a test drive.

**Unintended Mirror Retraction**
- **Symptoms:** Side mirrors may unexpectedly retract inward toward the windows while driving at highway speeds without driver input.
- **Solution:** Consult a dealer or technician to check the mirror control module and wiring connections.
