# 2022 Buick Enclave Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2022 Buick Enclave offers families a spacious, comfortable ride with a refined cabin and a standard V6 engine. As a relatively modern used SUV, it generally provides a smooth ownership experience, though buyers should review owner-reported concerns regarding minor electrical quirks, exhaust components, and braking system behavior during test drives.

---

## Engine

### 3.6L V6 Engine

Equipped with a 3.6-liter V6 engine producing approximately 310 horsepower and 266 lb-ft of torque, providing confident acceleration. This powerplant is generally robust, though early owner feedback highlights occasional reports concerning exhaust system leaks near mounting brackets.

### Exhaust System

The exhaust configuration routes gases from the engine block rearward, but some owners have noted durability issues with mounting brackets and pipe joints.

**Exhaust Pipe and Bracket Leaks**
- **Symptoms:** Owners have reported exhaust leaks developing near the front mounting bracket or crossmember, resulting in abnormal noise or exhaust fumes.
- **Solution:** Inspect the exhaust manifold, piping, and mounting brackets for cracks or leaks, and replace damaged gaskets or sections as necessary.

## Transmission

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

Paired with a 9-speed automatic transmission available in front-wheel or all-wheel drive configurations, delivering smooth highway cruising. This transmission generally operates reliably when properly maintained.

## Brakes

### Service Brakes

The hydraulic braking system provides adequate stopping power for this large crossover, but isolated owner reports suggest monitoring for booster or pad wear.

**Brake Booster Failure and Premature Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may experience a hard brake pedal upon startup, reduced braking responsiveness, or premature grinding noises from the pads and rotors.
- **Solution:** Have a qualified mechanic inspect the brake booster, pads, and rotors, and replace worn friction components or faulty vacuum/hydraulic boosters.

## Electrical

### Electrical and Lighting System

Electrical architecture controls numerous convenience and safety features, with occasional owner complaints pointing to intermittent lighting and liftgate glitches.

**Lighting and Tailgate Malfunctions**
- **Symptoms:** Daytime running lights or exterior headlights may intermittently fail, and the power liftgate can occasionally open randomly or fail to respond to switch inputs.
- **Solution:** Inspect lighting modules, wiring harnesses, and liftgate actuators for faults or loose connections at a certified repair shop.

## Exterior

### Windshield and Sunroof

Exterior glass components generally withstand normal weather, though rare complaints mention nighttime glare anomalies and sunroof noises.

**Windshield Optical Distortion and Sunroof Noise**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice shadows or light duplication through the windshield when high beams reflect off signs at night, or hear unexpected popping noises from the sunroof.
- **Solution:** Check the windshield for optical anomalies or alignment, and inspect the sunroof glass and seals for proper tension and fitment.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 22V210000: Air Bags:Side/Window:Curtain](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Enclave&modelYear=2022)**
- **Issue:** The harness connector to the left or right side roof rail air bag may have incompatible electrical terminals, which could prevent the air bags from deploying in a crash. Roof rail air bags that do not deploy as intended increase the risk of injury in a crash.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the harness connector to the roof rail air bags, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2022 Buick Enclave is a comfortable and spacious three-row crossover that makes for an appealing family vehicle. While most systems operate reliably, prospective buyers should thoroughly check the brakes, exhaust components, and electrical accessories like the liftgate and exterior lighting during a pre-purchase inspection to ensure everything functions properly.
