# 2022 Buick Enclave

### Overview
The 2022 Buick Enclave is a three-row mid-size SUV that offers a comfortable and spacious interior, making it a popular choice for families. It is generally considered a reliable vehicle, with a focus on smooth ride quality and a quiet cabin. The Enclave is equipped with a robust V6 engine and a nine-speed automatic transmission, providing adequate power for daily driving and highway cruising. Buick's reputation for building comfortable and well-appointed vehicles is evident in the Enclave's design and features. While most owners report a positive ownership experience, like any vehicle, there are specific areas that may warrant attention over time.

### Summary Verdict
The 2022 Buick Enclave generally presents a strong reliability profile, particularly concerning its powertrain (3.6L V6 engine and 9-speed automatic transmission) and core chassis components. Most owners experience a smooth and trouble-free ownership. The primary areas where minor issues have been noted are within the electrical system, specifically occasional infotainment glitches that are often resolved with software updates, and in rare cases, premature battery failure. Exterior and interior components are largely free from widespread complaints. Overall, the Enclave is a dependable choice for those seeking a comfortable and spacious family SUV, with potential for minor electrical quirks being the most common, albeit usually manageable, concerns.
