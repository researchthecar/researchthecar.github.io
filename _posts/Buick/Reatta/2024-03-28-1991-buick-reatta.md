**Introduction:**

The 1991 Buick Reatta was a luxury coupe that offered a smooth and refined driving experience. It was available with two engine options: a 3.8L V6 and a 3.8L supercharged V6. The V6 produced 170 horsepower and 240 lb-ft of torque, while the supercharged V6 produced 205 horsepower and 275 lb-ft of torque. Both engines were paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

Owners of the Reatta praised its comfortable ride, stylish design, and responsive handling. However, the vehicle has also been plagued by several common issues.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant leaks, engine overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace the intake manifold gaskets
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**3.8L Supercharged V6**

* **Power Steering Pump Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power steering assist, whining noise from the pump
    * Solution: Replace the power steering pump
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
    * **Slipping Transmission:**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission gears slipping, difficulty shifting
        * Solution: Rebuild or replace the transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,500 - $4,000
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking noise when turning, loose handling
    * Solution: Replace the control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, warning light on the dashboard
    * Solution: Replace the alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Motor Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Headlights not working, flickering headlights
    * Solution: Replace the headlight motors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the dashboard, affecting the appearance
    * Solution: Repair the dashboard or replace with a new one
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1991 Buick Reatta is generally a reliable vehicle, but it has some common issues that owners should be aware of. The most severe issues include transmission slippage and alternator failure, which can be costly to repair. However, the rest of the issues are relatively minor and can be fixed for a reasonable cost. Overall, the Reatta is a comfortable and stylish car that offers a good driving experience despite its potential issues.
