The 1990 Buick Reatta is a luxury coupe that was produced for a single generation from 1988 to 1991. It is powered by a 3.8-liter V6 engine that produces 165 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, and it is paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. The Reatta was praised for its stylish design and comfortable interior, but it was criticized for its lack of performance and relatively high price.

**Engine**

**3.8-Liter V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the intake manifold gasket, engine overheating
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leaking from the head gasket, engine overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $1,500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Timing Chain Tensioner Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Rattle or ticking noise from the engine, reduced engine power
    * Solution: Replace timing chain tensioner
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $600 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Solenoid Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping or not shifting properly, check engine light illuminated
    * Solution: Replace transmission solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Transmission Fluid Leak**
    * Description/Symptoms: Transmission fluid leaking from the transmission pan or lines, low transmission fluid level
    * Solution: Fix leak and refill transmission fluid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 1990 Buick Reatta is a stylish and comfortable luxury coupe, but it is known for having several common issues. The most significant issues include intake manifold gasket failure, head gasket failure, and transmission solenoid failure. These issues can be expensive to repair, and they can also lead to more serious problems if they are not addressed promptly. Overall, the Reatta is a reliable vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues that it may have so that you can budget for repairs and maintenance.
