**1995 Buick Riviera**

The 1995 Buick Riviera is an American luxury coupe that was offered with a 3.8L V6 engine and a 4-speed automatic transmission. The 3.8L V6 engine produces 170 horsepower and 220 pound-feet of torque.

Some of the things that people like about the 1995 Buick Riviera include its stylish design, comfortable interior, and powerful engine. However, the Riviera also has some drawbacks, such as its poor fuel economy and limited cargo space.
