The 2014 Buick Regal is a midsize sedan available with three engine options: a 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder, a 2.4-liter four-cylinder, and a 3.6-liter V6. Transmission options include a six-speed automatic and a nine-speed automatic. The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder produces 259 horsepower and 295 lb-ft of torque, while the 2.4-liter four-cylinder produces 182 horsepower and 172 lb-ft of torque. The 3.6-liter V6 produces 303 horsepower and 264 lb-ft of torque.

Critics praised the 2014 Buick Regal for its comfortable ride, spacious interior and powerful engines. However, the vehicle was also criticized for its lack of interior quality in some areas, as well as its poor fuel economy.

**Engine**

* **2.0-liter Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Over time, carbon deposits can build up on the intake valves, which can lead to reduced engine performance, fuel efficiency, and increased emissions.
        * Solution: The intake valves must be cleaned to remove the carbon buildup. This is typically done by using a chemical cleaner and a specialized tool.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The timing chain can fail, which can cause serious engine damage. Symptoms of a failing timing chain include a rattling noise from the engine, reduced engine performance, and a check engine light.
        * Solution: The timing chain must be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

* **2.4-liter Four-Cylinder**

    * **No Issues Found**

* **3.6-liter V6**

    * **Intake Valve Carbon Buildup**
        * Description/Symptoms: Same as for the 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder.
        * Solution: Same as for the 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: Same as for the 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder.
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Piston Ring Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: The piston rings can fail, which can lead to excessive oil consumption and reduced engine power. Symptoms of piston ring failure include blue smoke from the exhaust, a drop in oil level, and a loss of power.
        * Solution: The piston rings must be replaced.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: The battery may drain prematurely, causing the vehicle to have difficulty starting.
    * Solution: The battery may need to be replaced or the electrical system may need to be checked for parasitic draw.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: The alternator may fail, which can cause the battery to drain and the vehicle to lose power. Symptoms of alternator failure include a dim or flickering dashboard lights, a growling noise from the alternator, or a battery warning light.
    * Solution: The alternator must be replaced.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2014 Buick Regal has received mixed reviews from owners. While the vehicle offers a comfortable ride, spacious interior, and powerful engines, it has also been criticized for its lack of interior quality in some areas, as well as its poor fuel economy. The vehicle has also experienced a number of issues, including engine problems, transmission problems, electrical problems, and interior problems. The severity of the issues varies, and the cost to fix them can range from a few hundred dollars to several thousand dollars. Overall, the 2014 Buick Regal is a decent vehicle, but it is important to be aware of the potential issues that it may have before making a purchase.
