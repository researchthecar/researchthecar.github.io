# 2016 Buick Regal Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2016 Buick Regal delivers a comfortable ride, upscale cabin features, and responsive handling, making it an appealing option in the used midsize sedan market. Buyers considering this model should weigh its refined road manners against a few documented owner concerns regarding electrical accessories, steering functionality, and exterior lighting.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

The base 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces 182 horsepower, offering adequate everyday performance for daily commuting. While generally serviceable, some owners have reported intermittent drivability issues and engine rough running requiring professional diagnosis.

**Engine Performance and Sputtering**
- **Symptoms:** Illumination of the check engine light accompanied by engine sputtering and sudden loss of power while driving at moderate speeds.
- **Solution:** Have a certified mechanic diagnose fault codes, inspect the fuel and ignition systems, and replace failing components as necessary.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The available 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates 259 horsepower, providing significantly stronger acceleration and passing power. Buyers should review maintenance records carefully, as turbocharged models demand strict oil change intervals to maintain long-term reliability.

**Internal Engine and Turbocharger Wear**
- **Symptoms:** Unexpected engine stalling, dashboard warning lights, and metallic debris contaminating the oil system leading to turbocharger or engine failure.
- **Solution:** Inspect engine oil condition during pre-purchase evaluations and perform thorough mechanical inspections if unusual noises or performance drops occur.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The standard six-speed automatic transmission channels power smoothly to the front wheels or available all-wheel-drive systems. It operates reliably under normal driving conditions, provided routine fluid changes are performed.

## Steering

### Electric Power Steering System

The electronic power steering setup provides light and direct handling feel, but potential buyers should thoroughly test the steering response during a pre-purchase inspection to rule out assist failures or unusual noises.

**Steering Assist Loss and Binding**
- **Symptoms:** Intermittent loss of power steering assist making the steering wheel heavy and difficult to turn, or sudden binding and pulling during operation.
- **Solution:** Inspect the electric power steering rack, motor, and associated wiring harnesses for faults; replace defective steering assemblies if necessary.

**Steering Column Noises**
- **Symptoms:** Grinding or clicking noises emanating from the steering wheel area when executing turns during wet weather.
- **Solution:** Lubricate or replace worn steering column components or intermediate shafts as diagnosed by a qualified technician.

## Exterior

### Exterior Headlamp Assemblies

Electrical accessories and lighting systems generally function well, though headlight housing seals can degrade and allow moisture intrusion over time.

**Headlight Condensation and Dimming**
- **Symptoms:** Accumulation of moisture and condensation inside the front headlight housings, reducing night-time illumination and forward visibility.
- **Solution:** Seal or replace the affected headlight assemblies to restore proper light output and clarity.

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 16V108000: Steering:Electric Power Assist System](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Regal&modelYear=2016)**
- **Issue:** A loss of power steering assist may increase the risk of a crash.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the electric belt drive rack and pinion steering gear assembly, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

---

## Summary Verdict

The 2016 Buick Regal is a comfortable and well-equipped used sedan option, but shoppers should prioritize a thorough pre-purchase inspection. Particular attention should be paid to the electronic power steering system, headlight housing condition, and engine health—especially on turbocharged variants—to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.
