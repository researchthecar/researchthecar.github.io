**Introduction**

The 2017 Buick Regal is a stylish midsize sedan that offers a blend of performance, comfort, and advanced technology. It comes with a standard 260-horsepower 3.6-liter V6 engine paired with an eight-speed automatic transmission, and an optional 310-horsepower turbocharged 2.0-liter four-cylinder engine paired with a nine-speed automatic transmission. The Regal has received positive reviews for its well-equipped interior, spacious cabin, and impressive handling. However, it has also been criticized for its firm ride and limited rear-seat legroom.

**Engine**

* **3.6L V6**
    * **Intake Valve Sticking**
        * Description/Symptoms: Decreased engine performance, misfiring, rough idle
        * Solution: Replace intake valves
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
    * **Timing Chain Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Engine noise, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
        * Solution: Replace timing chain
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **2.0L Turbocharged I4**
    * **Turbocharger Failure**
        * Description/Symptoms: Reduced engine power, whistling noise, increased fuel consumption
        * Solution: Replace turbocharger
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Eight-Speed Automatic**
    * **Shifting Hesitation**
        * Description/Symptoms: Delayed or rough shifting between gears
        * Solution: Update transmission software, replace solenoids
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Nine-Speed Automatic**
    * **Transmission Slipping**
        * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power
        * Solution: Replace transmission fluid, overhaul transmission
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $3,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Control Arm Bushings**
    * **Premature Wear**
        * Description/Symptoms: Clunking noise when driving over bumps, poor handling
        * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies prematurely, difficulty starting the vehicle
    * Solution: Inspect electrical system for parasitic drains, replace battery
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
        * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen freezing, navigation system errors, audio issues
    * Solution: Software update, replace infotainment unit
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
        * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Assembly Condensation**
    * Description/Symptoms: Moisture accumulation inside the headlight housing
    * Solution: Replace headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Seat Heater Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Seat heaters fail to warm up or heat unevenly
    * Solution: Replace seat heater elements
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Rear Seat Occupant Detection Issue**
    * Description/Symptoms: Airbag light illuminates even when there is no one in the rear seat
    * Solution: Software update, replace seat occupancy sensor
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
        * Recalls: NHTSA ID: 17V-395

**Summary**

The 2017 Buick Regal generally has minor to moderate issues that can be costly to fix. Engine and transmission problems, particularly with the 3.6L V6 and eight-speed automatic transmission, appear to be the most severe and expensive to address. Electrical and interior issues, while not as common, can also be inconvenient and costly to resolve. Suspension issues should be addressed promptly to maintain optimal handling and comfort. Overall, the 2017 Buick Regal has potential issues that should be considered before purchasing.
