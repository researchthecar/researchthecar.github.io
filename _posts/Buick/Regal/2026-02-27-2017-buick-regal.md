# 2017 Buick Regal Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Buick Regal, buyers will find a stylish and comfortable midsize sedan that offers athletic handling, particularly in turbo and GS trims. While many owners enjoy its quiet ride and feature-rich cabin, prospective buyers should review specific mechanical areas, notably concerning braking system reports and powertrain options. A careful pre-purchase inspection can help identify potential issues before making a purchase.

---

## Engine

### 2.4L 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.4-liter naturally aspirated four-cylinder engine produces around 182 horsepower and is paired with a front-wheel-drive system. It offers adequate efficiency for daily commuting, though it lacks the spirited acceleration of the available turbo options.

### 2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder Engine

The 2.0-liter turbocharged four-cylinder engine generates approximately 259 horsepower and delivers punchy performance. It is commonly found on higher trims and the sportier GS variant, making it a favorite for buyers seeking extra passing power.

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The six-speed automatic transmission delivers smooth gear changes across front-wheel-drive and all-wheel-drive configurations. Routine fluid maintenance helps ensure long-term reliability for this gearbox.

### 6-Speed Manual Transmission

A six-speed manual transmission was selectively available on specific turbocharged trims for enthusiasts desiring a more connected driving experience. It requires standard clutch upkeep depending on driving habits.

## Brakes

### Brake Vacuum Pump & Hydraulic System

Reports concerning the hydraulic brake system and vacuum pump failures highlight critical stopping concerns for some owners.

**Brake Pedal Stiffness and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers have reported instances where the brake pedal becomes extremely firm or rigid, significantly increasing stopping distances and reducing braking effectiveness.
- **Solution:** Inspect the vacuum pump and brake booster assembly; replace faulty vacuum pumps or related components as necessary.

## Electrical

### Infotainment & Camera Module

Advanced safety and driver-assistance features provide added security, but electronic module glitches can occasionally disable peripheral camera functions.

**Rearview Camera Failure**
- **Symptoms:** The rearview camera fails to activate when shifting into reverse, leaving the display blank without warning lights.
- **Solution:** Diagnose the human interface module or camera wiring, and replace faulty electronic modules as needed.

## Exterior

### Exterior Lighting Housing

Lighting components generally operate well, though headlight housing seals should be checked for moisture ingress.

**Headlight Moisture Accumulation**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers notice condensation or moisture building up inside the headlight housings, which can potentially damage bulbs, ballasts, or wiring over time.
- **Solution:** Inspect headlight seals and housing cracks; replace the affected headlight assembly if moisture intrusion persists.

## Summary Verdict

The 2017 Buick Regal provides a refined driving dynamic and comfortable interior, making it an appealing used sedan option. While major powertrain configurations are generally dependable, buyers should pay close attention to the braking system—particularly verifying proper vacuum pump operation—and test all electrical accessories and rearview camera functions during a pre-purchase inspection.
