**Introduction:**

The 1990 Buick Regal is a mid-size sedan that was popular for its stylish design and comfortable interior. It was offered with a choice of two engine options: a 3.8L V6 and a 5.0L V8. The 3.8L V6 produced 165 horsepower and 210 lb-ft of torque, while the 5.0L V8 produced 170 horsepower and 245 lb-ft of torque. Both engines were mated to a 4-speed automatic transmission.

People liked the Regal for its spacious interior, smooth ride, and powerful engines. However, it was also known for having some reliability issues.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, rough idle
Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**5.0L V8**

* **Head Gasket Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking coolant, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
Solution: Replace head gasket
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,000 - $2,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission:**
* **Slipping Gears:**
Description/Symptoms: Transmission slipping out of gear, difficulty shifting
Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Front Struts:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking fluid, clunking noises, rough ride
Solution: Replace front struts
Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600+
Recalls: None Found

* **Rear Shocks:**
Description/Symptoms: Leaking fluid, clunking noises, rough ride
Solution: Replace rear shocks
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Battery not charging, dim headlights, flickering dash lights
Solution: Replace alternator
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
Recalls: None Found

* **Starter Failure:**
Description/Symptoms: Engine won't start, clicking noises when trying to start
Solution: Replace starter
Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Window Motors:**
Description/Symptoms: Windows not working properly, slow or noisy operation
Solution: Replace window motor
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

* **Door Handles:**
Description/Symptoms: Door handles breaking or becoming difficult to operate
Solution: Replace door handles
Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracking:**
Description/Symptoms: Cracks appearing on the dashboard
Solution: Replace dashboard
Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
Recalls: None Found

* **Seat Controls:**
Description/Symptoms: Seat controls not working properly, seats not adjusting properly
Solution: Replace seat controls or seat motors
Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
Recalls: None Found

**Summary:**

The 1990 Buick Regal had a number of issues, including engine, transmission, suspension, electrical, and interior problems. The most severe and expensive issues were head gasket failure, transmission failure, and intake manifold gasket failure. However, many of the issues were relatively minor and inexpensive to fix. Overall, the Regal was a reliable vehicle but it did have some potential problems that buyers should be aware of.
