**Introduction:**

The 2001 Buick Regal is a mid-size sedan known for its comfortable ride and spacious interior. It offers two engine options, a 3.1L V6 and a 3.8L V6, paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission.

**Engine**

**3.1L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine coolant leaks, overheating, misfires
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $800 - $1,200
    * Recalls: #02V242000
* **Head Gasket Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Engine coolant leaks, overheating, white smoke from exhaust
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $3,000

**3.8L V6**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:**
    * Symptoms: Delayed shifts, shuddering during acceleration
    * Solution: Repair or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $2,000 - $4,000

**Suspension**

* **Front Suspension Clunking:**
    * Symptoms: Noises when driving over bumps or turning
    * Solution: Replace sway bar links or bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Rear Suspension Sagging:**
    * Symptoms: Lower ride height, poor handling
    * Solution: Replace rear coil springs or shocks
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Dimming headlights, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800
* **Battery Discharge:**
    * Symptoms: Difficulty starting, electrical system malfunctions
    * Solution: Replace battery or check for electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Switch Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Headlights not working or turning off intermittently
    * Solution: Replace headlight switch
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
* **Sunroof Seal Leakage:**
    * Symptoms: Leaking water into interior
    * Solution: Replace sunroof seal
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cracks:**
    * Symptoms: Cracks appearing on dashboard
    * Solution: Replacement or repair of dashboard
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,200+
* **Seat Heater Failure:**
    * Symptoms: Seat heaters not working
    * Solution: Repair or replace seat heater module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400

**Summary:**

The 2001 Buick Regal generally has moderate reliability, though there are a few significant issues that can be expensive to repair, such as intake manifold gasket failure and head gasket failure. Other issues, like suspension clunking and electrical problems, are generally less severe and cost less to fix.
