**Introduction**

The 1995 Buick Park Avenue is a full-size luxury sedan known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. It is equipped with a 3.8L V6 engine producing 180 horsepower and 225 lb-ft of torque. The Park Avenue is offered with a 4-speed automatic transmission. While it is generally well-received, some common issues have been reported by owners.

**Engine**

**3.8L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure:** Leaks coolant and oil, causing overheating and engine damage. $500 - $1000+
* **Water Pump Failure:** Leaks coolant, leading to overheating. $200 - $400
* **Power Steering Pump Failure:** Causes difficulty steering. $400 - $600

**Transmission**

**4-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slipping:** Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power. $1000 - $2000+
* **Torque Converter Failure:** Stalling, shuddering, or slipping transmission. $600 - $1200+

**Suspension**

* **Control Arm Bushings Wear:** Causes clunking and rattling noises. $200 - $400
* **Sway Bar Links Wear:** Causes knocking or clunking sounds. $100 - $200
* **Strut or Shock Absorber Failure:** Leaking fluid, reduced ride comfort. $400 - $800 per pair

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure:** Charges battery, vehicle may stall. $400 - $600
* **Starter Failure:** Difficulty starting engine. $300 - $500
* **Headlight Switch Failure:** Headlights not working. $100 - $250

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Snapping:** Handle becomes loose or breaks. $100 - $300
* **Trunk Lid Corrosion:** Rust or pitting on trunk lid. $200 - $600
* **Mirror Adjustment Failure:** Mirror not adjusting properly. $100 - $300

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Crack:** Cracking on dashboard due to UV exposure. No fix available.
* **Climate Control Panel Failure:** Controls not working properly. $200 - $400

**Summary**

The 1995 Buick Park Avenue has some common issues to be aware of, including engine gasket failures, transmission problems, suspension wear, and electrical malfunctions. The severity of these issues varies, with some being relatively minor and others requiring more costly repairs. Overall, maintenance and repairs can be more expensive than average for this vehicle.
