**Introduction**

The 1992 Buick Park Avenue is a full-size luxury sedan powered by a 3.8L V6 engine paired with a 4-speed automatic transmission. Known for its spacious and comfortable interior, smooth ride, and powerful engine, the Park Avenue has been praised for its reliability and value. However, it's not without its reported issues.

**Engine**

* **No Issues Found**

**Transmission**

* **4-Speed Automatic Transmission**
    * **Hesitation or Jerking during Shifts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission shifts roughly or hesitates between gears, particularly when cold.
        * Solution: Service the transmission, including fluid and filter change.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $350+
    * **Delayed Upshifts or Downshifts**
        * Description/Symptoms: Transmission takes longer than usual to shift gears, leading to hesitation or loss of power.
        * Solution: Replace transmission solenoid or speed sensor.
        * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $500+

**Suspension**

* **Strut Mount Bearing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noises such as clunking or grinding while driving over bumps or turning.
    * Solution: Replace strut mount bearings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400+
* **Control Arm Bushings Wearing Out**
    * Description/Symptoms: Squeaking or rattling noises when going over bumps or turning.
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+

**Electrical**

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming headlights, slow cranking, or battery not charging.
    * Solution: Replace alternator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $250 - $550+
* **Electrical System Problems**
    * Description/Symptoms: Various electrical issues, such as intermittent malfunctions of accessories like windows, locks, or lights.
    * Solution: Diagnose and repair specific electrical component or harness issues.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $800+ depending on the issue

**Exterior**

* **Door Handle Actuator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty opening the door, particularly in cold weather.
    * Solution: Replace door handle actuator.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $300+

**Interior**

* **Dashboard Cluster Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inaccurate or flickering gauges, dim or no backlighting.
    * Solution: Repair or replace the instrument cluster.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $150 - $600+
* **Climate Control Fan Motor Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Reduced or no airflow from the heating or air conditioning system.
    * Solution: Replace the blower motor.
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $350+

**Summary**

Overall, the 1992 Buick Park Avenue has a reputation for being a reliable vehicle. However, it does have some reported issues that can be costly to fix, such as transmission hesitation and electrical problems. The severity and cost of these issues should be considered when purchasing or maintaining a used Park Avenue.
