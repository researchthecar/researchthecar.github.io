## Introduction

The 2003 Buick Park Avenue is a full-size luxury sedan known for its spacious interior, comfortable ride, and powerful engine. It is available with a choice of two engine options and two transmission options. The standard engine is a 3.8-liter V6 producing 205 horsepower, while the optional engine is a 4.6-liter V8 producing 275 horsepower. Both engines are mated to a standard four-speed automatic transmission.

Owners appreciate the Park Avenue's roomy interior, plush seats, and quiet ride. However, it has received criticism for its sluggish performance and outdated technology.

### Engine

**3.8L V6**

* **Intake Manifold Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leak, engine overheating, rough idle
    * Solution: Replace intake manifold gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Head Gasket Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Coolant or oil leak, engine overheating, loss of power
    * Solution: Replace head gasket
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $2,500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**4.6L V8**

* **No Issues Found**

### Transmission

**Four-Speed Automatic**

* **Transmission Slippage**
    * Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, loss of power, shuddering
    * Solution: Rebuild or replace transmission
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,200 - $3,000+
    * Recalls: None Found

### Suspension

* **Front Control Arm Bushing Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Clunking or rattling noise when turning or driving over bumps
    * Solution: Replace control arm bushings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

### Electrical

* **Alternator Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Dimming lights, electrical problems, battery discharge
    * Solution: Replace alternator
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $600
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Starter Failure**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to start the engine
    * Solution: Replace starter
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500
    * Recalls: None Found

### Exterior

* **Headlamp Assembly Fogging**
    * Description/Symptoms: Condensation or fog buildup inside the headlamp assembly
    * Solution: Replace headlamp assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $400
    * Recalls: None Found

### Interior

* **Dashboard Cracking**
    * Description/Symptoms: Cracks on the plastic dashboard
    * Solution: Replacement not typically recommended; cover dashboard with a dash mat
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: N/A
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunction**
    * Description/Symptoms: Inability to control temperature or airflow
    * Solution: Replace climate control module
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000
    * Recalls: None Found

### Summary

The 2003 Buick Park Avenue has several potential issues, ranging from minor and affordable to major and expensive. The most severe and costly issues include engine gasket failures, transmission slippage, and suspension failures. However, many of these issues are not common and can be prevented through regular maintenance and timely repairs. Overall, the Park Avenue is a reliable and comfortable sedan, but it is important to be aware of its potential issues before making a purchase decision.
