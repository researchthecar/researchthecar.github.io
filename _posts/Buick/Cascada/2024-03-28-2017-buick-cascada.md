**Introduction:**

The 2017 Buick Cascada is a compact convertible that offers a stylish and luxurious driving experience. It is powered by a standard 2.0L turbocharged four-cylinder engine paired with a six-speed automatic transmission. The Cascada has received praise for its responsive handling, comfortable interior, and eye-catching design. However, like all vehicles, it has its fair share of reported issues.

**Engine**

* **2.0L Turbocharged Four-Cylinder**

**Engine Misfire:**
* Description/Symptoms: Rough idling, loss of power, illuminated check engine light
* Solution: Replace ignition coil or spark plugs
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $300+
* Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

* **Six-Speed Automatic**

**Transmission Slipping:**
* Description/Symptoms: Difficulty shifting gears, delayed acceleration, shuddering during gear changes
* Solution: Replace or rebuild transmission
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $1,500 - $3,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

**Front Suspension Strut Failure:**
* Description/Symptoms: Knocking or rattling noise, uneven tire wear, poor handling
* Solution: Replace front suspension struts
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,000+
* Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

**Battery Drain:**
* Description/Symptoms: Dead battery after sitting for a period of time, difficulty starting
* Solution: Replace battery or investigate electrical system for parasitic drain
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $500+
* Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

**Fog Light Condensation:**
* Description/Symptoms: Buildup of moisture inside fog light housing
* Solution: Replace or reseal fog light housing or vent line
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $100 - $200+
* Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

**Infotainment System Malfunction:**
* Description/Symptoms: Touchscreen unresponsive, system freezing or displaying incorrect information
* Solution: Update software or replace infotainment unit
* Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1,500+
* Recalls: 19V330000 (Software Update)

**Summary:**

The 2017 Buick Cascada has a mix of minor and potentially more serious issues. While engine misfires and transmission slipping can be significant concerns, the other issues are relatively common in vehicles of this age and type. Repair costs can vary significantly depending on the severity of the issue and the location of the repair.
