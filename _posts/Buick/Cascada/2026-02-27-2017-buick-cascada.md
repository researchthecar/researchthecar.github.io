# 2017 Buick Cascada Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

When shopping for a used 2017 Buick Cascada, buyers will find a stylish and comfortable convertible cruiser designed for relaxed open-air driving. While generally offering solid mechanical foundations, used-car shoppers should review known owner complaints regarding specific engine accessories and body hardware to ensure a trouble-free ownership experience.

---

## Engine

### 1.6L Turbocharged Inline-4

The 1.6-liter turbocharged inline-4 engine produces approximately 200 horsepower and 207 lb-ft of torque, providing adequate power for casual cruising. Buyers should inspect the engine bay for common accessory seal and PCV system wear during a pre-purchase evaluation.

**PCV Valve and Camshaft Cover Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Drivers may notice a high-pitched whistling or squealing noise from the engine bay, accompanied by a rough idle and an illuminated check engine light.
- **Solution:** Replace the integrated PCV valve and camshaft cover assembly.
- **Cost:** $2,000

## Transmission

### 6-Speed Automatic Transmission

The conventional 6-speed automatic transmission routes power to the front wheels, offering smooth gear shifts suited for a touring convertible. This transmission generally maintains a dependable reputation with few widespread owner complaints.

## Exterior

### Convertible Top Rear Window

The power-folding soft top mechanism generally operates smoothly, though the rear window bonding requires close inspection during a used vehicle inspection.

**Rear Glass Separation**
- **Symptoms:** The glass rear window begins to delaminate or separate from the surrounding canvas convertible top material, usually starting at the bottom corners.
- **Solution:** Repair or replace the convertible top assembly, as temporary adhesive fixes often fail to provide a permanent seal.

## Safety

### Forward Collision Alert

Cabin electronics and driver assistance features add modern convenience, but prospective buyers should verify that all active safety systems function correctly during a test drive.

**Collision Warning Inoperable**
- **Symptoms:** The front collision alert system fails to illuminate visual warnings or sound alerts when approaching preceding vehicles too quickly.
- **Solution:** Diagnose sensor alignment and system modules at a qualified service center.
