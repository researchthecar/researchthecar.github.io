**Introduction**

The 2017 Buick Verano is a compact sedan known for its comfortable ride and stylish design. It's available with various engine and transmission options, including a 2.4L 4-cylinder engine paired with a 6-speed automatic transmission or a 2.0L turbocharged 4-cylinder engine with a 6-speed automatic or a CVT. The Verano has received praise for its spacious interior, user-friendly infotainment system, and comfortable seats. However, it has also been criticized for its lack of performance, especially with the smaller 2.4L engine.

**Engine**

**2.4L 4-Cylinder**
* **Piston Ring Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Excessive oil consumption, blue smoke from the exhaust, decreased engine power
    * Solution: Replace the piston rings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1200 - $1800+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Intake Valve Timing Solenoid Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Check engine light, rough idle, loss of engine power
    * Solution: Replace the intake valve timing solenoid
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**2.0L Turbocharged 4-Cylinder**
* **Turbocharger Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of acceleration, blue smoke from the exhaust, loud or rattling noises from the turbocharger
    * Solution: Replace the turbocharger
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Fuel Injector Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Engine misfires, rough idle, loss of fuel economy
    * Solution: Replace the faulty fuel injectors
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $400 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Transmission**

**6-Speed Automatic**
* **Transmission Fluid Leaks:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Leaking fluid from the transmission, difficulty shifting gears
    * Solution: Replace or repair the leaking part
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $700+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Shifting Problems:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Hard or delayed shifting, slipping gears
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty transmission components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1000 - $1800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**CVT**
* **Belt Slipping:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Loss of power, hesitation during acceleration, grinding noises from the transmission
    * Solution: Replace the CVT belt and pulleys
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $1500 - $2500+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Suspension**

* **Strut Bearing Failure:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Noisy suspension, clunking or knocking sounds, reduced handling
    * Solution: Replace the strut bearings
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $600+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Electrical**

* **Battery Drain:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Battery dies frequently, electrical components malfunctioning
    * Solution: Find and repair the electrical drain
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Infotainment System Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Screen freezing, navigation not working, audio cutting out
    * Solution: Software update or replacing the infotainment system
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $300 - $1000+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Exterior**

* **Headlight Condensation:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Water accumulates inside the headlight housing
    * Solution: Replace the headlight assembly
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Interior**

* **Sunroof Issues:**
    * Description/Symptoms: Sunroof leaks, rattling noises, difficulty opening or closing
    * Solution: Repair or replace the sunroof components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $500 - $1500+
    * Recalls: None Found
* **Climate Control Malfunctions:**
    * Description/Symptoms: AC not blowing cold air, heater not working, fan noise
    * Solution: Repair or replace the faulty climate control components
    * Estimated Cost to Fix: $200 - $800+
    * Recalls: None Found

**Summary**

The 2017 Buick Verano has several reported issues, most notably with the engine, transmission, suspension, and electrical system. The severity of these issues can vary, with some being more costly to fix than others. However, overall, the Verano is a reliable vehicle that offers a comfortable and stylish driving experience.
