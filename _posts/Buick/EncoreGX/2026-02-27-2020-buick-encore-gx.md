# 2020 Buick Encore GX Reliability & Common Issues

## Overview

The 2020 Buick Encore GX offers buyers a stylish subcompact crossover option with flexible cargo space and modern technology. However, prospective buyers should review recurring owner complaints carefully. Common trouble spots involve electrical and drivetrain glitches, particularly relating to gear shifter sensors, alongside isolated reports concerning braking feel and reduced engine power.

---

## Engine

### 1.2L Turbocharged I3

A 1.2-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine producing around 137 horsepower, typically paired with a CVT in front-wheel drive configurations. It provides adequate efficiency for daily city commuting.

### 1.3L Turbocharged I3

A larger 1.3-liter turbocharged three-cylinder engine generating approximately 155 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque. It offers a bit more pep on highways and is available with both front-wheel and all-wheel drive.

**Engine Power Reduction**
- **Symptoms:** Illuminated check engine light and sudden 'Engine Power Reduced' warning message accompanied by a complete loss of throttle response while driving.
- **Solution:** Diagnostic testing by a dealer or qualified mechanic to identify faulty throttle bodies, wiring harnesses, or sensor failures, followed by component replacement.

## Transmission

### Continuously Variable Transmission (CVT)

A belt-driven continuously variable transmission utilized on front-wheel drive models to maximize fuel economy. Regular maintenance is key to long-term reliability.

### 9-Speed Automatic Transmission

A traditional 9-speed planetary automatic transmission paired exclusively with all-wheel drive configurations. It provides conventional step-gearing for smooth highway cruising.

**Transmission Failure / Shifter Malfunction**
- **Symptoms:** The vehicle fails to connect properly with the engine, or the shifter fails to register the 'Park' position, leading to warning messages, inability to shut off the vehicle, and occasional lurching.
- **Solution:** Requires professional inspection of the transmission assembly or shifter mechanism; out-of-warranty repairs can be exceptionally costly if internal mechanical failure occurs.
- **Cost:** $14,000

## Brakes

### Hydraulic Brake System

Standard hydraulic disc brakes equipped with anti-lock braking systems to provide dependable stopping power under normal conditions.

**Brake Pedal Stiffness and Failure**
- **Symptoms:** Brake pedal feels spongy or feels locked in place when depressed, resulting in extended stopping distances or a failure to stop effectively.
- **Solution:** Inspect brake booster, hydraulic lines, brake fluid condition, and master cylinder; replace worn or defective hydraulic components.

## Electrical

### Shifter Microswitch / Park Sensor

Electrical sensors and body control modules responsible for cabin electronics, gear position detection, and ignition control.

**'Shift to Park' Error Message**
- **Symptoms:** A persistent dashboard warning telling the driver to 'Shift to Park' even when the gear selector is already in park, preventing the ignition from turning off and the doors from locking.
- **Solution:** Replace the faulty shifter assembly or internal microswitch responsible for detecting the park position.
- **Cost:** $630.14

## Recall Check

Recalls are listed separately because used vehicles may already have had recall work completed. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 20V588000: Service Brakes](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Encore+GX&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** Sensor connection contamination in the electronic brake boost system may cause an interruption of communication between the sensor and the brake boost system. If communication with the sensor is interrupted, the electronic brake boost assist could be lost.
- **What to do:** GM will notify owners, and dealers will replace the electronic brake boost module, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

**[Recall 21V440000: Equipment:Mechanical:Accessory Scissor/Screw/Bottle Jack/Lift:Stand](https://api.nhtsa.gov/recalls/recallsByVehicle?make=Buick&model=Encore+GX&modelYear=2020)**
- **Issue:** A fractured jack can cause the vehicle to collapse, increasing the risk of injury.
- **What to do:** Dealers will replace the jack, free of charge. Verify completion by VIN before purchase.

## Summary Verdict

The 2020 Buick Encore GX presents a mixed reliability picture for used car shoppers. While it offers appealing styling and versatile packaging, buyers should thoroughly test all electronic functions and the gear shifter during a pre-purchase inspection. Pay close attention to whether the vehicle properly recognizes the 'Park' position and check for any history of transmission or brake complaints.
